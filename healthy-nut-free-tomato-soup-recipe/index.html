Healthy Nut-Free Tomato Soup Recipe


Looking for a delicious and healthy tomato soup recipe that is nut-free? This recipe is perfect for you! Packed with rich flavors and wholesome ingredients, this tomato soup is not only satisfying but also suitable for those with nut allergies. Whether you’re looking for a comforting meal or a quick lunch option, this recipe will surely become a favorite in your household. So, let’s dive in and discover how to make this delightful and nutritious nut-free tomato soup!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fresh tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • ½ cup coconut milk (optional, for added creaminess)
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the chopped tomatoes, carrots, celery, and red bell pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Sprinkle in the dried basil, dried oregano, paprika, salt, and black pepper. These herbs and spices will enhance the flavor of the soup and give it a delightful aroma.

  4. Pour in the vegetable broth, ensuring all the vegetables are fully submerged. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for approximately 20 minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and create a delicious base for the soup.

  5. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully puree the soup until smooth and creamy. If using a regular blender, blend the soup in batches to avoid any accidents. The smooth texture will make this soup a delight to savor.

  6. If desired, add the coconut milk to the soup for an extra touch of creaminess. Stir well to incorporate the milk evenly.

  7. Return the soup to the heat and simmer for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  8. Remove the pot from the heat and ladle the soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and added freshness.

  9. Serve the nut-free tomato soup hot and enjoy its comforting flavors. Pair it with a side of crusty bread or a light salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

This healthy nut-free tomato soup recipe is a fantastic option for those with nut allergies or anyone looking for a delicious and nutritious meal. With its rich flavors and creamy texture, this soup is sure to please even the pickiest eaters. Plus, it’s quick and easy to make, perfect for a busy weeknight dinner or a lazy weekend lunch. So, next time you’re craving some comforting tomato soup, give this nut-free recipe a try. Your taste buds and your health will thank you!

Note: This recipe can easily be customized to suit individual preferences. Feel free to add your favorite herbs and spices or adjust the seasoning according to your taste. Experiment with different toppings or garnishes to make the soup even more exciting. Remember, cooking is all about creativity and making dishes that bring joy to your palate. Enjoy!
