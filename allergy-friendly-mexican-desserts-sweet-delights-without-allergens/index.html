Allergy-Friendly Mexican Desserts: Sweet Delights without Allergens


Are you a dessert lover with allergies? Do you often find yourself unable to enjoy the delicious sweets that everyone else seems to indulge in? Well, worry no more! In this blog post, we will explore a range of allergy-friendly Mexican desserts that will satisfy your sweet tooth without triggering any allergens. So, let’s dive right in and discover these delightful treats!

1. Vegan Tres Leches Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 1 cup cane sugar
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the almond milk, coconut milk, cane sugar, vegetable oil, apple cider vinegar, and vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring well until fully combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Remove from the oven and let the cake cool completely before serving. You can also refrigerate it for a few hours to enhance the texture.

2. Dairy-Free Churros

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cane sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the water, vegetable oil, and salt. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the flour. Stir vigorously until the mixture forms a ball.
  3. In a shallow bowl, mix together the cane sugar and ground cinnamon.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep skillet or pot over medium-high heat.
  5. Spoon the churro dough into a piping bag fitted with a star tip.
  6. Carefully pipe 4-inch strips of dough into the hot oil, cutting them with scissors. Fry until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  7. Remove the churros from the oil and drain on a paper towel. Roll them in the cinnamon-sugar mixture while still warm.
  8. Serve the churros immediately with a side of dairy-free chocolate sauce or caramel sauce for dipping.

3. Gluten-Free Mexican Hot Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup cane sugar
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup dairy-free chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the almond flour, cocoa powder, cane sugar, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, cayenne pepper (if using), and salt. Stir until well combined.
  3. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough and roll them into balls. Place them on the prepared baking sheet, leaving a few inches of space between each cookie.
  4. If desired, press a few dairy-free chocolate chips onto the top of each cookie.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set and the centers are slightly soft.
  6. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Conclusion

With these allergy-friendly Mexican dessert recipes, you can now indulge in the sweet delights of Mexican cuisine, even if you have allergies. Whether you’re vegan, dairy-free, or gluten-free, there’s a delicious treat for everyone. So go ahead and satisfy your cravings without any worries. Enjoy these delightful desserts and share them with your loved ones!

Remember, always check the ingredients and labels of the products you use to ensure they are safe for your specific allergies. Happy baking and enjoy your allergy-friendly Mexican desserts!
