Greek Olive Bread: A Savory Delight from the Mediterranean


Welcome to our cooking blog, where we explore the diverse and delicious world of culinary delights. Today, we bring you a recipe that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean - Greek Olive Bread. This savory delight is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a casual brunch with friends or a cozy family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Add the warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Stir until the dough starts to come together.
  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Punch down the dough to release any air bubbles. Turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and knead in the chopped olives, crumbled feta cheese, dried oregano, and chopped fresh rosemary until well incorporated.
  7. Shape the dough into a round loaf and place it on the prepared baking sheet. Cover it with the kitchen towel and let it rise for another 30 minutes.
  8. Using a sharp knife, score the top of the bread with a few diagonal slashes. This will help the bread expand while baking.
  9. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until it turns golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  10. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

Greek Olive Bread pairs beautifully with a variety of dishes. Here are a few ideas to inspire you:

  • Serve it alongside a Mediterranean-inspired salad with fresh tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and a tangy vinaigrette.
  • Toast a slice of the bread and top it with a dollop of creamy tzatziki sauce, sliced cucumbers, and a sprinkle of fresh dill.
  • Make a delicious sandwich by layering thinly sliced roasted chicken, lettuce, tomatoes, and a smear of hummus between two slices of the bread.

Conclusion

Indulge in the flavors of the Mediterranean with this delectable Greek Olive Bread. The combination of briny Kalamata olives, tangy feta cheese, and aromatic herbs will transport you to the sun-drenched shores of Greece. Enjoy this savory delight with your loved ones and create memories that will last a lifetime.
