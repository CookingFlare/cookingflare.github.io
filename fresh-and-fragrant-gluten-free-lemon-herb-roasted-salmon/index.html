Fresh and Fragrant: Gluten-Free Lemon Herb Roasted Salmon


If you’re a seafood lover like me, you’re in for a treat with this Fresh and Fragrant Gluten-Free Lemon Herb Roasted Salmon recipe. Bursting with zesty flavors and aromatic herbs, this dish is not only delicious but also gluten-free, making it a perfect option for those with dietary restrictions. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make! Let’s dive right into the recipe.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, finely chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. This flavorful marinade will infuse the salmon with a burst of tangy and herby goodness.

  3. Place the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet, skin side down. Pour the marinade over the salmon, making sure to coat each fillet evenly.

  4. Let the salmon marinate for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to penetrate the fish.

  5. Once marinated, transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and roast the salmon for approximately 12-15 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork. Cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of your salmon fillets, so keep a close eye on them to avoid overcooking.

  6. Once the salmon is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a couple of minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute, ensuring a moist and tender final result.

  7. Serve the Fresh and Fragrant Gluten-Free Lemon Herb Roasted Salmon hot, garnished with fresh parsley for an added touch of freshness. This dish pairs beautifully with a side of steamed vegetables or a light salad.

Final Thoughts

This Gluten-Free Lemon Herb Roasted Salmon recipe is a fantastic option for a healthy and flavorful meal. The combination of zesty lemon, aromatic herbs, and tender salmon creates a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply looking for a delicious seafood dish, this recipe is sure to impress. Give it a try and savor the freshness and fragrance of this delightful dish!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation, so feel free to customize this recipe to your liking. You can add your favorite herbs, spices, or even a squeeze of fresh orange juice for a citrusy twist. Don’t be afraid to get creative in the kitchen and let your taste buds guide you. Enjoy!
