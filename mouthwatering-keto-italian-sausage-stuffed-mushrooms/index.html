Mouthwatering Keto Italian Sausage Stuffed Mushrooms


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly appetizer, these Italian sausage stuffed mushrooms are sure to impress. Packed with savory flavors and wholesome ingredients, this recipe is perfect for anyone following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking to enjoy a flavorful, guilt-free treat. With a combination of Italian sausage, cream cheese, and aromatic seasonings, these stuffed mushrooms are a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 24 large white mushrooms
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Clean the mushrooms with a damp paper towel to remove any dirt. Carefully remove the stems from the mushrooms and set aside.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the Italian sausage until it’s no longer pink, breaking it into crumbles as it cooks. Drain any excess fat from the skillet.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked sausage, softened cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix until all the ingredients are well combined.
  5. Take each mushroom cap and fill it generously with the sausage and cheese mixture, mounding it slightly.
  6. Place the stuffed mushrooms on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden brown.
  7. Once done, remove the stuffed mushrooms from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes.
  8. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These mouthwatering Italian sausage stuffed mushrooms are versatile and can be served in various ways. They make for a delightful appetizer at parties or gatherings, and they can also be enjoyed as a flavorful side dish alongside your favorite main course. For a complete keto-friendly meal, pair these stuffed mushrooms with a fresh salad or roasted vegetables for a satisfying and well-balanced dining experience.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 2 stuffed mushrooms
  • Calories: 180
  • Total Fat: 14g
  • Saturated Fat: 6g
  • Cholesterol: 35mg
  • Sodium: 360mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 3g
  • Dietary Fiber: 1g
  • Sugars: 1g
  • Protein: 10g

Final Thoughts

These keto Italian sausage stuffed mushrooms are a delightful way to indulge in a savory and satisfying appetizer while staying true to your low-carb lifestyle. The combination of flavorful Italian sausage, creamy cheese, and aromatic herbs creates a culinary experience that’s as delicious as it is guilt-free. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply treating yourself to a special snack, these stuffed mushrooms are a must-try for anyone who appreciates the rich and comforting flavors of Italian cuisine. Enjoy!
