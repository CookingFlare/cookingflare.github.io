Bake Away: Spring Breads and Pastries to Enjoy


Fruit blossoms emerge, the air becomes lighter, and the first green shoots appear on the trees. Spring has arrived, and with it comes the perfect time for baking. This season brings an array of flavors, colors, and fragrances into your kitchen. From soft and buttery croissants to scrumptious fruit-filled pastries, you’ll find endless delight in these delectable spring breads and pastries.

Morning Sunshine: Lemon Poppy Seed Bread

Who doesn’t love the sunshine-y flavor of lemon poppy seed bread? Fresh lemon juice and zesty lemon oil give this bread its signature tanginess, while poppy seeds create a tender crumb. Incorporate the zest of one lemon into your batter for extra punch:

  • Ingredients *
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon poppy seeds
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk

Rainy Day Bliss: Rustic Sourdough Bread

Perfect for a rainy day spent at home, this rustic sourdough recipe will fill your kitchen with an enticing aroma. The tangy starter enhances the flavor, while the hearty crust holds up to generous schmears of butter or jams:

  • Ingredients *
  • 500g bread flour
  • 100g rye flour
  • 200g active sourdough starter
  • 500ml lukewarm water
  • 10g salt

Sweet Treats: Raspberry Almond Croissants

Consider this delicacy a perfect croissant upgrade. Rich and tender croissants are filled with a luscious raspberry jam and almond filling. Fresh raspberries dotted on top add a delightful surprise with each bite:

  • Ingredients *
  • 1 croissant dough recipe
  • ¼ cup raspberry jam
  • ¼ cup almond filling
  • Fresh raspberries

Spring Flavors: Strawberry Orange danishes

These handheld treats are bursting with spring flavors. Incorporating fresh orange juice and zest into the pastry, while a strawberry glaze brings sweetness to every bite:

  • Ingredients *
  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 1 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon Grand Marnier orange liqueur
  • ½ cup confectioners’ sugar

Indulgence: Lemon Blueberry Tart

Lemon and blueberry are a match made in heaven in this decadent tart. The bright, zesty crust pairs perfectly with the sweet and tangy blueberry filling, providing just the right amount of sweetness:

  • Ingredients *
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cuber size
  • 2 tablespoons confectioner’s sugar
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice

Baking away the winter blues, let the warmth of your oven fill your home with joy. Embrace the flavors of spring by incorporating fresh ingredients and bright, sunny flavors into your bread and pastries. Your taste buds will thank you for this delightful seasonal treat.

Indulge in Spring with These Recipes

As the days become longer, and the world awakens in a riot of color, let these spring breads and pastries be a celebration of the season. From lemon poppy seed bread to rustic sourdough bread, there’s something to satisfy every palate. So, preheat your oven and gather your ingredients – springtime is the perfect time to whip up something delicious.
