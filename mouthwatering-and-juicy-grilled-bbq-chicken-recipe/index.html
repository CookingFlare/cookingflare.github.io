Mouthwatering and Juicy: Grilled BBQ Chicken Recipe


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of sizzling, juicy BBQ chicken on a warm summer evening. The smoky, charred flavor combined with the tangy sweetness of BBQ sauce is a match made in culinary heaven. If you’re looking to elevate your grilling game and delight your taste buds, this mouthwatering and juicy grilled BBQ chicken recipe is just what you need. Let’s dive into the details of this irresistible dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by patting the chicken pieces dry with paper towels to ensure a crispy skin when grilled. Place the chicken in a large bowl and drizzle with olive oil, ensuring each piece is evenly coated. Season the chicken generously with paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper, rubbing the seasonings into the meat for maximum flavor.

Step 2: Fire up the Grill

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat, aiming for a temperature of around 375°F (190°C). Clean and oil the grates to prevent the chicken from sticking.

Step 3: Grill the Chicken

Carefully place the seasoned chicken pieces on the preheated grill, skin side down. Grill for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until the skin is golden brown and has those perfect grill marks. Keep an eye on the chicken to prevent flare-ups and ensure even cooking.

Step 4: Glaze with BBQ Sauce

Once the chicken is almost cooked through, brush each piece generously with your favorite BBQ sauce. Allow the sauce to caramelize and form a delicious glaze as the chicken continues to cook. Be sure to turn the chicken frequently to prevent the sauce from burning.

Step 5: Check for Doneness

Using a meat thermometer, check the internal temperature of the chicken. It should register at least 165°F (74°C) to ensure it’s safe to eat. Once the chicken is cooked through and the BBQ sauce has formed a sticky glaze, remove it from the grill and place it on a serving platter.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Sprinkle the grilled BBQ chicken with fresh chopped parsley for a burst of color and an added layer of flavor. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving to allow the juices to redistribute, ensuring maximum juiciness.

Enjoy the Culinary Delight

Now, take a moment to appreciate the masterpiece you’ve created. The smoky aroma, the succulent meat, and the caramelized BBQ sauce come together to create a dish that’s nothing short of extraordinary. Pair this grilled BBQ chicken with your favorite sides, such as corn on the cob, coleslaw, or a fresh green salad, and get ready to savor every mouthwatering bite.

Final Thoughts

With this recipe in your culinary arsenal, you’re well on your way to becoming the ultimate grill master. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a taste of summer, this grilled BBQ chicken is sure to impress. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and indulge in the simple yet irresistible pleasure of perfectly grilled, juicy BBQ chicken. Cheers to good food, great company, and unforgettable flavors!
