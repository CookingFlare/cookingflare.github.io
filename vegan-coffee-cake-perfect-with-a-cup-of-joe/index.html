Vegan Coffee Cake: Perfect with a Cup of Joe


Coffee and cake are a match made in heaven, and what better way to enjoy this classic combination than with a vegan twist? In this recipe, we’ll be creating a delicious Vegan Coffee Cake that is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and pair perfectly with a steaming cup of joe. So grab your apron and let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the streusel topping:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the brewed coffee, almond milk, vegetable oil, apple cider vinegar, and vanilla extract. Mix well.

  4. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan, spreading it out evenly.

  6. To make the streusel topping, combine the flour, brown sugar, and chilled vegan butter in a small bowl. Use your fingers or a fork to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until it resembles coarse crumbs. If desired, mix in the chopped walnuts for added texture and flavor.

  7. Sprinkle the streusel topping evenly over the cake batter.

  8. Place the cake pan in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  9. Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Slice the Vegan Coffee Cake and serve it alongside a steaming cup of your favorite coffee. The moist and tender cake, with its delicate coffee flavor and sweet streusel topping, is a true delight for any coffee lover.

Tips for Success

  • Make sure to cool the brewed coffee before adding it to the batter. This helps prevent the cake from becoming dense.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of coffee to vary the flavor profile of your cake. Try a bold espresso or a smooth medium roast for different taste sensations.

  • If you don’t have almond milk on hand, you can use any other non-dairy milk of your choice, such as soy milk or oat milk.

  • For a more indulgent treat, serve the Vegan Coffee Cake with a dollop of vegan whipped cream or a drizzle of dairy-free caramel sauce.

With its moist crumb, irresistible streusel topping, and subtle coffee undertones, this Vegan Coffee Cake is the perfect accompaniment to your morning coffee routine or any time you crave a sweet, comforting treat. Give this recipe a try and let its delicious flavors awaken your taste buds. Enjoy!
