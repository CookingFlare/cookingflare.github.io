10 Easy Grilled Sandwiches for a Perfect 4th of July BBQ


The 4th of July is a time to celebrate independence with family, friends, and, of course, delicious food. If you’re planning a BBQ to mark the occasion, grilled sandwiches are an excellent choice for both ease and flavor. These versatile and satisfying dishes can be prepared in advance, cooked on the grill, and enjoyed by everyone. Whether you’re hosting a casual cookout or a more festive gathering, these 10 easy grilled sandwiches will elevate your BBQ spread and bring joy to your guests.

Here are 10 mouthwatering grilled sandwiches to make your 4th of July BBQ unforgettable. From classic combinations to creative twists, you’ll find something for everyone.

1. Classic Grilled Cheese with a Twist

There’s no better way to start than with a timeless favorite – the classic grilled cheese. But let’s take it up a notch for your 4th of July BBQ by adding some extra ingredients that’ll make it stand out.

Ingredients:

  • Sourdough bread
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Garlic butter for grilling

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Spread garlic butter generously on the outsides of your bread slices.
  3. Layer the cheese, basil, and tomato between the slices of bread.
  4. Grill the sandwich for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy the perfect blend of gooey cheese, aromatic basil, and juicy tomatoes.

This grilled cheese with fresh basil and tomatoes will bring a burst of flavor to your BBQ table and is always a crowd-pleaser.

2. BBQ Pulled Pork Sandwiches

If you’re looking for a hearty and smoky sandwich to serve at your 4th of July BBQ, pulled pork is a fantastic option. This sandwich brings together the perfect combination of savory pulled pork, tangy barbecue sauce, and crunchy coleslaw.

Ingredients:

  • Brioche buns
  • Pulled pork (pre-cooked or from your favorite BBQ joint)
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Coleslaw mix (or homemade coleslaw)
  • Pickles (optional)

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Warm the brioche buns on the grill, cut side down, for 1-2 minutes until toasted.
  3. Layer the pulled pork on the bottom half of each bun.
  4. Drizzle with barbecue sauce and top with a generous serving of coleslaw.
  5. Add pickles if desired and cover with the top bun.
  6. Grill the sandwich for another 2-3 minutes, pressing down gently to allow the flavors to meld.

The smoky, juicy pulled pork paired with the sweet and tangy barbecue sauce creates a flavor explosion that will keep your guests coming back for more.

3. Grilled Veggie Delight Sandwich

For your vegetarian guests or those looking for something light yet delicious, a grilled veggie sandwich is a fantastic choice. Packed with fresh vegetables and grilled to perfection, this sandwich is a healthy and flavorful option.

Ingredients:

  • Ciabatta or baguette bread
  • Zucchini, sliced
  • Bell peppers, sliced
  • Red onion, sliced
  • Eggplant, sliced
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Toss the zucchini, bell peppers, red onion, and eggplant with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the vegetables for about 4-5 minutes on each side until tender and lightly charred.
  4. Slice the ciabatta or baguette into sandwich-sized pieces and toast them lightly on the grill.
  5. Layer the grilled vegetables and mozzarella on the bread, drizzling with balsamic glaze.
  6. Grill the sandwich for 2-3 minutes, pressing down slightly until the cheese melts.

This grilled veggie delight sandwich is a great way to enjoy the flavors of summer while keeping things fresh and light.

4. Turkey and Cranberry Grilled Sandwich

If you’re in the mood for something a bit more festive, a turkey and cranberry grilled sandwich is a perfect fit for your 4th of July BBQ. The savory turkey combined with the tartness of cranberry sauce creates a flavorful and unique sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat bread
  • Sliced turkey breast
  • Cream cheese
  • Cranberry sauce
  • Spinach leaves
  • Dijon mustard

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on both slices of bread.
  3. Add a layer of turkey slices, followed by a spoonful of cranberry sauce.
  4. Add a few spinach leaves for a fresh, green crunch, and drizzle with Dijon mustard.
  5. Grill the sandwich on each side for about 4 minutes, until golden brown and crispy.

This turkey and cranberry sandwich will surprise your guests with its unexpected combination of flavors and textures, making it an excellent addition to your BBQ spread.

5. Chicken Caesar Grilled Sandwich

For a satisfying yet light option, the Chicken Caesar grilled sandwich brings the classic Caesar salad into sandwich form. It’s a great way to enjoy the flavors of a beloved salad while keeping things portable and easy to eat.

Ingredients:

  • Focaccia bread
  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded
  • Croutons, crushed

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Spread Caesar dressing on the inside of both slices of focaccia bread.
  3. Layer the grilled chicken, romaine lettuce, and shredded Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add a sprinkle of crushed croutons for crunch.
  5. Grill the sandwich for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and crispy.

This Chicken Caesar sandwich is the perfect option for a lighter yet satisfying choice at your BBQ.

6. Caprese Grilled Sandwich

Inspired by the classic Italian Caprese salad, this sandwich is a simple yet flavorful option for your BBQ spread. The combination of fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil is always a hit, and grilling it adds a delicious twist.

Ingredients:

  • French baguette or rustic bread
  • Fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Drizzle olive oil on the outside of the bread slices.
  3. Layer the fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil leaves between the bread slices.
  4. Grill the sandwich for 3-4 minutes on each side until crispy and golden.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

The combination of fresh ingredients and the smoky flavor from the grill makes this Caprese sandwich a delightful addition to your 4th of July BBQ.

7. Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Grilled Sandwich

For those craving something hearty and comforting, the bacon, egg, and cheese grilled sandwich is the way to go. It’s the perfect balance of salty, savory, and cheesy goodness that will make everyone’s taste buds dance.

Ingredients:

  • White or whole-grain bread
  • Bacon strips, cooked
  • Eggs, scrambled
  • Cheddar cheese, sliced
  • Butter

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Butter both slices of bread and place a slice of cheese on each piece.
  3. Add a few crispy bacon strips and scrambled eggs on top of the cheese.
  4. Grill the sandwich for 3-4 minutes on each side until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted.

This indulgent sandwich will definitely be a crowd favorite at your 4th of July BBQ.

8. Philly Cheesesteak Grilled Sandwich

A true American classic, the Philly cheesesteak grilled sandwich is always a winner at any BBQ. The combination of tender beef, melted cheese, and sautéed vegetables makes this sandwich hearty and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • Hoagie rolls
  • Thinly sliced ribeye steak
  • Bell peppers, sliced
  • Onions, sliced
  • Provolone cheese
  • Olive oil

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Sauté the bell peppers and onions with olive oil until tender, about 5 minutes.
  3. Grill the ribeye steak slices until cooked to your desired doneness.
  4. Place the steak, sautéed vegetables, and provolone cheese on the hoagie rolls.
  5. Grill the sandwich for 2-3 minutes on each side until the cheese is melted and the bread is toasted.

This Philly cheesesteak sandwich will bring bold flavors and a satisfying meal to your BBQ guests.

9. Grilled Ham and Swiss with Honey Mustard

A classic ham and cheese sandwich gets a delicious upgrade with a bit of honey mustard, making it the perfect addition to your BBQ menu. The sweetness of the honey mustard pairs perfectly with the savory ham and creamy Swiss cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Rye bread
  • Sliced ham
  • Swiss cheese
  • Honey mustard
  • Butter

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Butter the outside of the rye bread and spread honey mustard on the inside.
  3. Layer the ham and Swiss cheese on the bread.
  4. Grill the sandwich for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden and crispy.

This sweet and savory grilled sandwich will add a delightful flavor to your 4th of July celebration.

10. Grilled Shrimp Po’ Boy Sandwich

For a seafood twist, the shrimp po’ boy grilled sandwich is an excellent choice. This sandwich is packed with juicy shrimp, crispy lettuce, and tangy sauce, all grilled to perfection for a flavorful bite.

Ingredients:

  • French baguette or hoagie rolls
  • Shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Remoulade sauce or tartar sauce
  • Olive oil

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Toss the shrimp with olive oil and seasonings of your choice (garlic, paprika, cayenne, etc.).
  3. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until cooked through.
  4. Layer the shrimp, lettuce, tomato slices, and a drizzle of remoulade or tartar sauce in the bread.
  5. Grill the sandwich for 2-3 minutes, pressing gently to meld the flavors.

This shrimp po’ boy will bring a taste of the Gulf Coast to your BBQ, offering a refreshing change from the usual fare.

Conclusion

These 10 easy grilled sandwiches are the perfect addition to your 4th of July BBQ. Whether you’re craving something classic, indulgent, or light, these sandwiches offer a variety of flavors and options that will satisfy every palate. Grilled sandwiches are a great way to impress your guests with minimal effort, and they pair perfectly with other BBQ favorites like coleslaw, potato salad, and grilled veggies.

So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to serve up some delicious sandwiches that will make this 4th of July celebration truly special!
