Pasta with Creamy Tomato Basil Sauce: A Comforting Classic


There’s something undeniably comforting about a steaming plate of pasta coated in a rich and creamy tomato basil sauce. This classic dish has been a favorite for generations, and for good reason. The combination of tangy tomatoes, aromatic basil, and velvety cream creates a symphony of flavors that is both satisfying and indulgent. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a hearty meal, this pasta with creamy tomato basil sauce recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta (such as penne, fettuccine, or spaghetti)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving 1/2 cup of the cooking water.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant. Be careful not to let it brown.

  3. Add the Tomatoes: Pour in the crushed tomatoes and sugar, stirring to combine. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken slightly.

  4. Create the Creamy Base: Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream, stirring gently to incorporate it into the tomato sauce. Let the sauce simmer for an additional 5-7 minutes, allowing it to thicken further.

  5. Combine the Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the sauce, tossing to coat the pasta evenly. If the sauce seems too thick, add some of the reserved pasta cooking water to loosen it up.

  6. Finish with Parmesan: Stir in the freshly grated Parmesan cheese, allowing it to melt into the sauce and create a luscious, creamy texture.

  7. Season and Serve: Season the pasta with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a burst of herbal aroma and a pop of color.

Tips for Perfection

  • Use high-quality ingredients, such as San Marzano tomatoes, for the best flavor.
  • Don’t skimp on the fresh basil - it adds a bright, herbaceous note to the dish.
  • For a touch of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.

Why We Love It

Pasta with creamy tomato basil sauce is a timeless classic that never fails to delight. The rich, velvety sauce clings to the pasta, creating a luxurious texture that is pure comfort on a plate. The marriage of sweet tomatoes, pungent garlic, and fragrant basil is a match made in culinary heaven. Whether you’re serving this dish for a cozy family dinner or a gathering with friends, it’s sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

In Conclusion

This recipe for pasta with creamy tomato basil sauce is a celebration of simple, wholesome ingredients that come together to create a dish that is greater than the sum of its parts. It’s a reminder that sometimes, the most satisfying meals are the ones that harken back to tradition and evoke a sense of warmth and contentment. So, the next time you’re in need of a little culinary comfort, reach for this recipe and let the magic of creamy tomato basil sauce work its charms.
