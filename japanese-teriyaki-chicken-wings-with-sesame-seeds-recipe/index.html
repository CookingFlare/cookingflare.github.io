Japanese Teriyaki Chicken Wings with Sesame Seeds Recipe


If you’re a fan of Japanese cuisine, then you’ll absolutely love this delicious and flavorful recipe for Teriyaki Chicken Wings with Sesame Seeds. These wings are sticky, sweet, and savory, making them perfect for a quick and easy dinner or as an appetizer for your next gathering. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, mirin, sake, brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger until well combined. This will be our teriyaki sauce.

  3. Place the chicken wings in a large ziplock bag and pour half of the teriyaki sauce over them. Close the bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes (or up to overnight for maximum flavor).

  4. Remove the chicken wings from the ziplock bag and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Reserve the marinade for later use.

  5. Bake the chicken wings in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until they are cooked through and crispy.

  6. While the wings are baking, pour the reserved marinade into a small saucepan and bring it to a boil over medium heat. In a separate small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Gradually add the slurry to the boiling marinade, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens to your desired consistency.

  7. Once the wings are done baking, remove them from the oven and brush them with the thickened teriyaki sauce. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.

  8. Return the wings to the oven for an additional 5 minutes to allow the sauce to caramelize and the sesame seeds to toast slightly.

  9. Remove the wings from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Teriyaki Chicken Wings with Sesame Seeds are incredibly versatile and can be served in various ways. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  • Serve the wings as an appetizer with a side of steamed rice and a drizzle of extra teriyaki sauce.
  • Enjoy the wings as a main course, alongside a fresh green salad and some roasted vegetables.
  • Turn the wings into a party snack by placing toothpicks in each wing and arranging them on a platter.

No matter how you choose to serve them, these Teriyaki Chicken Wings with Sesame Seeds are sure to please your taste buds and impress your guests. The sticky and flavorful teriyaki sauce combined with the nutty sesame seeds creates a winning combination that will have everyone asking for seconds.

So, what are you waiting for? Get into your kitchen, gather the ingredients, and start cooking up a batch of these delicious Japanese Teriyaki Chicken Wings with Sesame Seeds. Enjoy!

Note: This recipe serves 4-6 people as an appetizer or 2-4 people as a main course.
