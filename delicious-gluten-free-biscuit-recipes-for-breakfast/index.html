Delicious Gluten-Free Biscuit Recipes for Breakfast


Are you tired of the same old breakfast routine? Looking for a delicious and gluten-free option to start your day? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some mouthwatering gluten-free biscuit recipes that are perfect for breakfast. These recipes are not only easy to make but also packed with flavor, making them a great way to kick-start your day on a tasty note. So, let’s get started!

1. Almond Flour Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the almond flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold butter cubes to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and then add them to the flour mixture. Stir until well combined.
  5. Scoop out about 1/4 cup of the dough and shape it into a biscuit. Place it onto the prepared baking sheet. Repeat with the remaining dough.
  6. Bake the biscuits for 15-18 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

2. Coconut Flour Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 large eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the coconut flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then add the melted butter. Mix well.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until thoroughly combined.
  5. Let the dough sit for a few minutes to allow the coconut flour to absorb the liquid.
  6. Scoop out about 1/4 cup of the dough and shape it into a biscuit. Place it onto the prepared baking sheet. Repeat with the remaining dough.
  7. Bake the biscuits for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  8. Allow them to cool slightly before serving.

3. Oat Flour Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups oat flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any other non-dairy milk)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the oat flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold butter cubes to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually pour in the almond milk and stir until the dough comes together.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to about 1/2 inch thick. Use a biscuit cutter to cut out biscuits and place them onto the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake the biscuits for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  7. Allow them to cool slightly before serving.

Conclusion

There you have it - three delicious gluten-free biscuit recipes that are perfect for breakfast. Whether you prefer almond flour, coconut flour, or oat flour, these recipes offer a variety of flavors and textures to satisfy your morning cravings. Experiment with different toppings like honey, jam, or even a smear of nut butter to make these biscuits even more enticing. So, go ahead and give these recipes a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Note: These recipes are gluten-free, but as with any dietary restrictions or allergies, it’s important to check all ingredients for potential cross-contamination or allergens.

Happy baking and enjoy your gluten-free breakfast biscuits!
