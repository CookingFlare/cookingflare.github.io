Delicious Slow Cooker Creamy Mushroom Chicken Recipe


If you’re in the mood for a comforting and flavorful meal that practically cooks itself, then this Slow Cooker Creamy Mushroom Chicken recipe is just what you need. Packed with tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and a luscious creamy sauce, this dish is a surefire way to impress your family or guests. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make and requires minimal effort. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe and get ready to tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 oz. cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat the olive oil and brown the chicken on both sides until golden. This step helps to seal in the juices and adds extra flavor to the dish.

  2. Once the chicken is browned, transfer it to the slow cooker. In the same skillet, add the sliced mushrooms and chopped onion. Sauté until the mushrooms are golden and the onions are translucent. Add the minced garlic and continue to cook for another minute, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Transfer the mushroom and onion mixture to the slow cooker, arranging it around the chicken breasts.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, dried thyme, dried rosemary, and paprika. Pour this flavorful mixture over the chicken and mushrooms in the slow cooker.

  5. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked, carefully remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a plate. In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and cornstarch until smooth. Stir this creamy mixture into the slow cooker, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.

  7. Return the chicken to the slow cooker, coating it with the creamy mushroom sauce. Let it simmer for an additional 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  8. Serve the Slow Cooker Creamy Mushroom Chicken over cooked pasta, rice, or mashed potatoes, garnished with fresh parsley for a burst of color and freshness.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra depth of flavor, you can add a splash of white wine to the mushroom and onion mixture before transferring it to the slow cooker.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can increase the amount of cornstarch to achieve the desired consistency.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite herbs or spices to complement the rich and creamy sauce.

Conclusion

This Slow Cooker Creamy Mushroom Chicken recipe is a true crowd-pleaser that will have everyone asking for seconds. The convenience of using a slow cooker makes it an ideal choice for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. With its rich and comforting flavors, this dish is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a cozy family dinner or a casual gathering with friends. Give this recipe a try and savor the irresistible combination of tender chicken, savory mushrooms, and a luxuriously creamy sauce.

So, what are you waiting for? Dust off that slow cooker and get ready to indulge in a truly satisfying meal that will have everyone coming back for more. Enjoy!
