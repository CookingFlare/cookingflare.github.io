Classic Croissants Ingredients: 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour 1/4 cup granulated sugar 1 teaspoon salt 1 1/2 cups unsalted butter, chilled 1/4 cup warm water 1/4 cup milk 1 packet instant yeast 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash) Instructions: In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and salt. Cut 1/2 cup of butter into the mixture until crumbly. Dissolve the yeast in warm water and add it to the flour mixture. Stir in the milk until the dough comes together. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead until smooth. Shape it into a square, wrap it in plastic, and refrigerate for 30 minutes. Roll the dough into a 12-inch square. Place the remaining butter in the center and fold the dough over it, sealing the edges. Roll the dough into a 20x10-inch rectangle, then fold it into thirds. Chill for 30 minutes. Repeat this process twice. Roll the dough into a 20x10-inch rectangle one last time. Cut into triangles, then roll each triangle into a croissant shape. Place the croissants on a baking sheet, cover, and let rise for 1-2 hours. Preheat the oven to 425°F. Brush the croissants with egg wash and bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
