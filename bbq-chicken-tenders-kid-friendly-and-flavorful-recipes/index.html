BBQ Chicken Tenders: Kid-Friendly and Flavorful Recipes


If you’re looking for a delicious and kid-friendly meal that’s sure to please even the pickiest eaters, look no further than BBQ chicken tenders. These flavorful and tender chicken strips are the perfect balance of sweet and savory, making them a hit with both kids and adults. Whether you’re planning a family dinner or a fun weekend barbecue, BBQ chicken tenders are a versatile and easy-to-make option that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

To make the most mouthwatering BBQ chicken tenders, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of chicken tenders
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill: Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. This will ensure that your chicken tenders cook evenly and develop a delicious charred exterior.

  2. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken tenders with salt and pepper. This simple step will add a burst of flavor to the chicken and create a perfect balance with the sweetness of the BBQ sauce.

  3. Make the BBQ Glaze: In a small bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, honey, garlic powder, onion powder, and smoked paprika. This glaze will be the secret to achieving that irresistible sticky-sweet coating on the chicken tenders.

  4. Grill the Chicken: Place the seasoned chicken tenders on the preheated grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side. As the chicken begins to cook through, brush the BBQ glaze generously over each tender, allowing it to caramelize and create a mouthwatering crust.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken is fully cooked and has developed a beautiful caramelized exterior, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, serve the BBQ chicken tenders with a side of your favorite dipping sauce and watch them disappear in no time.

Tips for Success

  • Marinate for Extra Flavor: For an extra flavor boost, marinate the chicken tenders in the BBQ glaze for 1-2 hours before grilling. This will infuse the meat with even more delicious flavor.

  • Temperature Control: To ensure that the chicken cooks evenly, use a meat thermometer to check for doneness. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C) before the chicken is safe to eat.

  • Get Creative with Dipping Sauces: While BBQ chicken tenders are delicious on their own, they’re also fantastic with a variety of dipping sauces. Try serving them with ranch dressing, honey mustard, or even a zesty chipotle mayo for a fun twist.

Conclusion

BBQ chicken tenders are a fantastic addition to your recipe repertoire, offering a simple yet satisfying meal option that’s perfect for kids and adults alike. With their irresistible combination of smoky, sweet, and savory flavors, these tender and juicy chicken strips are guaranteed to become a family favorite. Whether you’re firing up the grill for a summer barbecue or simply looking for a quick and delicious weeknight dinner, BBQ chicken tenders are a versatile and crowd-pleasing option that will never disappoint. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and get ready to enjoy a mouthwatering meal that’s as easy to make as it is to devour!
