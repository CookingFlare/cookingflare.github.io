Zesty Mexican Grilled Chicken: A Flavorful BBQ Option


If you’re craving a burst of zesty, smoky flavors this summer, look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for Zesty Mexican Grilled Chicken. This dish is a perfect addition to any BBQ or outdoor gathering, impressing your guests with its vibrant colors and bold flavors. Let’s dive into the details of this tantalizing recipe and get ready to elevate your grilling game!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Zest and juice of 2 limes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, cumin, chili powder, paprika, dried oregano, cayenne pepper, lime zest, and lime juice. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring it’s evenly coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to infuse the chicken.

  3. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Make sure the grates are clean and lightly oiled to prevent sticking.

  4. Grill the Chicken: Remove the chicken from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off. Place the chicken on the preheated grill and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C). The chicken should develop beautiful grill marks and a smoky aroma.

  5. Rest and Garnish: Once the chicken is cooked through, transfer it to a serving platter and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Slice the grilled chicken and serve it alongside your favorite BBQ sides, such as Mexican street corn, grilled vegetables, or a refreshing avocado salad.

Tips for Success:

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the marinade.
  • If you prefer bone-in chicken, you can use chicken thighs or drumsticks with this marinade for equally delicious results.
  • Make sure to let the chicken rest after grilling to allow the juices to redistribute, ensuring a moist and flavorful bite.

Final Thoughts:

Zesty Mexican Grilled Chicken is a celebration of vibrant spices and bold flavors, making it a standout dish at any BBQ gathering. From the zing of lime to the warmth of cumin and chili powder, every bite is a fiesta for the taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving a taste of Mexico, this recipe is sure to become a crowd favorite. So fire up the grill, embrace the sizzle, and savor the flavors of this zesty BBQ option.
