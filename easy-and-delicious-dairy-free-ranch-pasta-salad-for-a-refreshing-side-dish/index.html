Easy and Delicious Dairy-Free Ranch Pasta Salad for a Refreshing Side Dish


Are you looking for a simple yet flavorful side dish to accompany your summer meals? Look no further than this easy and delicious dairy-free ranch pasta salad! Packed with vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, this refreshing salad is the perfect addition to any picnic, barbecue, or potluck. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make, making it a go-to dish for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta (such as rotini or penne)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

For the Dairy-Free Ranch Dressing:

  • 1 cup dairy-free mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain the pasta and rinse it under cold water to stop the cooking process. Transfer the pasta to a large mixing bowl.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables: Add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, black olives, and fresh parsley to the bowl with the cooked pasta. Gently toss the ingredients together to combine.

  3. Make the Dairy-Free Ranch Dressing: In a separate bowl, whisk together the dairy-free mayonnaise, dairy-free sour cream, apple cider vinegar, fresh chives, fresh dill, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper until smooth and well combined.

  4. Combine and Chill: Pour the dairy-free ranch dressing over the pasta and vegetables. Use a spatula to gently fold the dressing into the salad until everything is evenly coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once chilled, give the salad a final toss to recombine the ingredients. Transfer it to a serving dish and garnish with additional fresh herbs, if desired. Serve the dairy-free ranch pasta salad alongside your favorite grilled dishes or enjoy it as a standalone meal.

Tips for Customization

  • Add Protein: For a heartier version of this salad, consider adding grilled chicken, chickpeas, or tofu for an extra boost of protein.
  • Boost the Veggies: Feel free to incorporate your favorite vegetables such as bell peppers, broccoli, or even roasted corn for added color and texture.
  • Make It Gluten-Free: Simply swap the regular pasta for gluten-free pasta to accommodate gluten sensitivities or dietary preferences.

Final Thoughts

This dairy-free ranch pasta salad is a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that’s sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a light and satisfying meal, this salad delivers on both flavor and convenience. Embrace the simplicity of this recipe and let its zesty ranch flavors elevate your dining experience. Cheers to enjoying the vibrant tastes of summer with this delightful side dish!
