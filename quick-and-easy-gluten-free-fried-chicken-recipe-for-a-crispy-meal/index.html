Quick and Easy Gluten-Free Fried Chicken Recipe for a Crispy Meal


Are you craving crispy, golden fried chicken but need a gluten-free option? Look no further! This quick and easy gluten-free fried chicken recipe will satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. With a few simple ingredients and easy steps, you can whip up a delicious meal that everyone will love. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to enjoy some mouthwatering gluten-free fried chicken!

Ingredients

  • 6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by rinsing the chicken breasts and patting them dry with paper towels. Cut each chicken breast into smaller, equally sized pieces, ensuring they cook evenly and quickly.

  2. Create the Coating: In a shallow dish, combine the gluten-free all-purpose flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix the ingredients together thoroughly to create the seasoned coating for the chicken.

  3. Whisk the Eggs: In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. This will serve as the binding agent for the seasoned coating.

  4. Coat the Chicken: Dip each piece of chicken into the egg mixture, allowing any excess to drip off. Then, coat the chicken in the seasoned flour mixture, ensuring it is evenly coated on all sides.

  5. Heat the Oil: In a large skillet or frying pan, heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. To test if the oil is hot enough for frying, drop a small amount of the seasoned flour mixture into the oil. If it sizzles and bubbles, the oil is ready.

  6. Fry the Chicken: Carefully place the coated chicken pieces into the hot oil, being mindful not to overcrowd the pan. Fry the chicken for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. You may need to fry the chicken in batches to avoid overcrowding the pan.

  7. Drain and Serve: Once the chicken is golden and crispy, use tongs to transfer it to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil. Allow the chicken to rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve your gluten-free fried chicken with your favorite dipping sauces, such as honey mustard, barbecue sauce, or ranch dressing. Pair it with a side of coleslaw, mashed potatoes, or a fresh garden salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

There you have it - a quick and easy gluten-free fried chicken recipe that’s sure to become a family favorite. With its crispy exterior and tender, juicy interior, this gluten-free fried chicken is a delightful treat for any occasion. Whether it’s a weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering, this recipe is guaranteed to impress. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of gluten-free fried chicken!
