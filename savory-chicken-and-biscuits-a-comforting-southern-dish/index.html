Savory Chicken and Biscuits: A Comforting Southern Dish


There’s nothing quite like a comforting Southern dish to warm your heart and soul. And when it comes to comfort food, Savory Chicken and Biscuits takes the crown. This classic dish combines tender chicken in a rich, flavorful sauce with fluffy, buttery biscuits on top. It’s a match made in culinary heaven!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks of celery, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the biscuits:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 3/4 cup whole milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large, oven-safe skillet or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant.

  3. Add the chicken thighs to the skillet and cook until they are no longer pink. Season with salt, pepper, dried thyme, and dried rosemary.

  4. Sprinkle the all-purpose flour over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly. This will help thicken the sauce.

  5. Slowly pour in the chicken broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.

  6. Stir in the frozen peas and whole milk. Let the mixture simmer for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. While the chicken and sauce are simmering, prepare the biscuits. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the cold, cubed butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  8. Gradually add the whole milk to the flour mixture, stirring until a soft dough forms. Be careful not to overmix.

  9. Drop spoonfuls of the biscuit dough onto the chicken and sauce mixture. Spread them out evenly, leaving some space in between for the biscuits to expand.

  10. Transfer the skillet or Dutch oven to the preheated oven. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the biscuits are golden brown and cooked through.

  11. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. The sauce will thicken slightly as it cools, making it even more delicious.

  12. Spoon the savory chicken and biscuits onto plates, making sure to get a generous serving of both the chicken and the biscuits. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors of the South.

Conclusion

Savory Chicken and Biscuits is a timeless Southern dish that brings warmth and comfort to any mealtime. The tender chicken, flavorful sauce, and fluffy biscuits create a symphony of flavors that will have you coming back for seconds. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in this comforting classic. Your taste buds will thank you!
