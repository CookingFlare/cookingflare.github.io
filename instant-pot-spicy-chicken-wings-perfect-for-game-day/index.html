Instant Pot Spicy Chicken Wings: Perfect for Game Day


When it comes to Game Day snacks, nothing beats a plate of crispy, flavorful chicken wings. Whether you’re watching the big game with friends or just indulging in a little weekend treat, chicken wings are always a crowd-pleaser. And when you make them in an Instant Pot, you can achieve a tender, juicy texture while still getting that irresistible crispy exterior — all in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional methods.

In this post, we’ll walk you through how to make deliciously spicy chicken wings using your Instant Pot. These wings are packed with flavor, featuring a perfect balance of heat, tang, and savory goodness. And the best part? You don’t have to spend hours in the kitchen to make them.

Let’s dive in and discover how you can create the perfect Instant Pot spicy chicken wings for your next Game Day party or any occasion that calls for mouthwatering appetizers.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Chicken Wings?

Before we jump into the recipe itself, you might be wondering: why should I use an Instant Pot to cook my wings instead of traditional methods like baking, frying, or grilling? Here are a few reasons why Instant Pot chicken wings are the way to go:

1. Quick and Easy

One of the biggest benefits of using an Instant Pot is the speed. It cuts down the cooking time significantly compared to other methods. Typically, you would have to bake chicken wings for up to 45 minutes or deep fry them for several minutes. With the Instant Pot, you can cook your wings in just 15 minutes, plus a few extra minutes for crisping under the broiler or air fryer lid if you choose.

2. Juicy and Tender Wings

An Instant Pot uses pressure cooking to seal in the moisture, ensuring that your chicken wings are tender and juicy every time. This method helps break down the connective tissues, resulting in wings that are fall-off-the-bone delicious.

3. No Mess

Unlike frying chicken wings, which creates a greasy mess, cooking wings in an Instant Pot requires little to no oil. This makes cleanup much easier and keeps your kitchen less greasy. Plus, the Instant Pot helps lock in flavors without the need for excessive sauces or marinades.

4. Versatile

With an Instant Pot, you can choose from multiple cooking options. Want your wings tender and flavorful? Use the pressure cooker function. Want that crispy finish? You can quickly finish them off in the broiler or with the Instant Pot’s air fryer lid (if you have one).

Ingredients You’ll Need

Making these Instant Pot spicy chicken wings is simple, and you likely have most of the ingredients in your kitchen already. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Chicken Wings:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings (you can use fresh or frozen wings)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (or your preferred oil)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your preferred spice level)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth or water

For the Spicy Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup hot sauce (such as Frank’s RedHot or your favorite brand)
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar (for a touch of sweetness)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (for tang)
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • A pinch of salt and black pepper (to taste)

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Instant Pot Spicy Chicken Wings

Now that you’ve gathered all the ingredients, it’s time to start cooking. Follow these steps to make your wings:

Step 1: Prep the Chicken Wings

First, if you haven’t already, pat the chicken wings dry with paper towels. This helps remove excess moisture and ensures a crispier finish. Place the wings in a large bowl, then drizzle with olive oil.

Step 2: Season the Wings

Add the garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper to the wings. Toss the wings in the seasoning mix until they are evenly coated. This step will give your wings a flavorful kick right from the start.

Step 3: Set Up the Instant Pot

Place the trivet in the bottom of your Instant Pot. Pour in the chicken broth or water. This will help create steam and pressure to cook the wings perfectly. You can also add a small amount of the spicy sauce if you want to infuse more flavor during cooking.

Step 4: Pressure Cook the Wings

Arrange the seasoned chicken wings on the trivet inside the Instant Pot. Secure the lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Set the Instant Pot to Manual or Pressure Cook mode on high for 10 minutes. If you’re using frozen wings, you’ll need to increase the cooking time to 15 minutes.

Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 5 minutes before performing a quick release to let out any remaining pressure. This helps prevent the wings from becoming dry.

Step 5: Crisping the Wings (Optional, but Recommended)

While the wings are tender and juicy straight from the Instant Pot, they won’t have that crispy exterior just yet. To get that signature crispiness, you have two options:

  • Broiler Method: Preheat your oven’s broiler to high. Carefully remove the wings from the Instant Pot and transfer them to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Place the baking sheet under the broiler for 3-5 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the wings are golden brown and crispy.

  • Air Fryer Lid Method: If your Instant Pot has an air fryer lid, place the wings back into the pot, secure the lid, and set the air fryer to 400°F. Air fry for 5-7 minutes, flipping halfway through, to achieve a crispy finish.

Step 6: Prepare the Spicy Sauce

While the wings are crisping up, it’s time to prepare the spicy sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the hot sauce, melted butter, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally until the sauce is smooth and well combined. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.

Step 7: Toss the Wings in Sauce

Once the wings are crispy, transfer them to a large bowl. Pour the spicy sauce over the wings and toss to coat evenly. If you like extra heat, feel free to add more hot sauce or cayenne pepper at this stage.

Step 8: Serve and Enjoy

Serve the wings immediately, garnished with chopped green onions or cilantro if desired. You can also provide dipping sauces like ranch or blue cheese for added flavor.

Tips for the Best Instant Pot Spicy Chicken Wings

While this recipe is pretty straightforward, here are some additional tips to ensure your wings turn out perfectly every time:

  • Adjust Spice Levels: You can modify the amount of cayenne pepper or hot sauce to match your heat tolerance. If you like things extra spicy, consider adding some diced jalapeños to the sauce or sprinkling chili flakes over the wings.

  • Double the Recipe: If you’re cooking for a crowd, you can easily double or even triple the recipe. Just make sure not to overcrowd the Instant Pot. You may need to cook the wings in batches to ensure even cooking.

  • Use a Homemade Hot Sauce: If you want to take your wings to the next level, consider making your own hot sauce blend using fresh ingredients like garlic, shallots, or smoked peppers.

  • Meal Prep Option: If you’re prepping for Game Day or another occasion, you can cook the wings in advance and refrigerate them. Reheat them in the Instant Pot on the sauté setting for a few minutes, or crisp them up again in the air fryer or oven.

Pairing Your Wings

While the wings themselves are delicious, you can take the flavor experience even further by pairing them with some tasty sides and drinks. Here are a few ideas:

Sides:

  • Crispy French Fries: The classic side that pairs perfectly with spicy chicken wings. Make them in the oven, air fryer, or even the Instant Pot.
  • Coleslaw: A tangy and creamy coleslaw provides a cool contrast to the heat of the wings.
  • Buffalo Cauliflower: For a healthier alternative, try making spicy buffalo cauliflower bites.
  • Vegetable Platter: Serve the wings with a fresh vegetable platter featuring carrots, celery, and cucumber. These add a refreshing crunch to the meal.

Drinks:

  • Beer: A cold beer is a classic beverage choice to enjoy with spicy wings. Try a light lager or an IPA to complement the heat.
  • Iced Tea: If you’re looking for a non-alcoholic option, a tall glass of sweet iced tea is perfect for balancing out the spice.
  • Lemonade: The tangy sweetness of lemonade pairs wonderfully with the spiciness of the wings.

Final Thoughts

Instant Pot spicy chicken wings are the ultimate Game Day snack, offering a delicious balance of heat, flavor, and convenience. With just a few simple steps, you can create wings that are tender, juicy, and crispy, all while saving time and reducing cleanup. Whether you’re hosting a watch party or just craving a savory treat, these wings are sure to impress.

Give this recipe a try the next time you want to serve something special at your gathering. You won’t be disappointed!
