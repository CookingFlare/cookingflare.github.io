Easy and Delicious Rice Dish Recipes for Students


Are you a student looking for quick and easy meal ideas that won’t break the bank? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some mouthwatering rice dish recipes that are perfect for busy students. These recipes are not only delicious but also budget-friendly and require minimal cooking skills. Let’s dive in!

1. One-Pot Chicken and Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of long-grain rice
  • 1 chicken breast, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat some oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  2. Add the onion, bell pepper, and garlic to the pot, and sauté until they soften.
  3. Stir in the rice, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper, ensuring the rice is well coated with the spices.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  5. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.

2. Veggie Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked rice (preferably leftover rice)
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat some oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry until they are tender-crisp.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until they are fully cooked.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir-fry everything together, breaking up any clumps of rice.
  5. Drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice, and season with salt and pepper. Stir-fry for a few more minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.

3. Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Arborio rice
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the vegetable broth over low heat and keep it warm.
  2. In a separate large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent.
  3. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.
  4. Stir in the Arborio rice and cook for a minute or two, ensuring the rice is well coated with the butter and vegetable mixture.
  5. Begin adding the warm vegetable broth to the pot, one ladleful at a time, while stirring constantly. Allow each ladleful of broth to be absorbed before adding the next.
  6. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and cooked al dente, which should take around 20-25 minutes.
  7. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve the risotto hot, garnished with chopped parsley.

Conclusion

These easy and delicious rice dish recipes are perfect for students who are short on time and money. They are not only simple to make but also packed with flavor. Whether you’re craving a hearty one-pot meal, a quick fried rice fix, or a creamy risotto, these recipes have got you covered. So, grab your apron and get cooking!
