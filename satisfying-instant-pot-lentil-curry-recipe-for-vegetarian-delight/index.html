Satisfying Instant Pot Lentil Curry Recipe for Vegetarian Delight


If you’re looking for a hearty and flavorful vegetarian dish that’s easy to prepare, look no further than this Satisfying Instant Pot Lentil Curry recipe. Packed with protein-rich lentils, a medley of aromatic spices, and creamy coconut milk, this dish is a true delight for the senses. The best part? It can be made in a fraction of the time using the ever-convenient Instant Pot. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a cooking novice, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of dried brown lentils
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 3 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan bread for serving

Instructions:

  1. Start by rinsing the lentils under cold water and set them aside.
  2. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” function. Heat the olive oil and add the chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the onions are soft and translucent.
  3. Add the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, paprika, and garam masala to the pot. Stir well to coat the aromatics with the spices.
  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes and stir, allowing the mixture to simmer for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add the rinsed lentils, vegetable broth, coconut milk, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Stir to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Secure the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 12 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully switching the valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.
  8. Carefully open the lid and give the curry a good stir. If the consistency is too thick, you can add a splash of water to reach your desired thickness.
  9. Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  10. Serve the Instant Pot lentil curry over a bed of fluffy rice or alongside warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness.

Why We Love This Recipe:

This Instant Pot lentil curry recipe is a game-changer for busy weeknights when you crave a nutritious and satisfying meal. The Instant Pot not only speeds up the cooking process but also allows the flavors to meld together beautifully, resulting in a rich and aromatic curry that tastes like it’s been simmering for hours.

Lentils are a fantastic source of plant-based protein and fiber, making this dish not only delicious but also incredibly nourishing. The blend of spices adds depth and warmth to the curry, while the creamy coconut milk brings a luxurious texture that will have you coming back for seconds.

What’s more, this recipe is highly versatile. You can customize the level of heat by adjusting the amount of spices, and you can also add your favorite vegetables to make it even more wholesome.

So, whether you’re a devoted vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more meatless meals into your diet, this Instant Pot lentil curry is a must-try. It’s a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone at the table satisfied and asking for the recipe.

Give it a try and experience the magic of a satisfying, aromatic, and comforting lentil curry that’s perfect for any occasion.
