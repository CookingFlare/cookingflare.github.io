BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders: Mini Sandwich Recipes for BBQ Parties


If you’re looking for the perfect dish to serve at your next BBQ party, look no further than these mouthwatering BBQ pulled chicken sliders. These mini sandwiches are not only delicious but also easy to make, making them the ideal choice for feeding a crowd. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or a casual get-together with friends, these BBQ pulled chicken sliders are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

To make these delectable BBQ pulled chicken sliders, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns
  • Coleslaw (optional, for serving)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder. Place the seasoned chicken in the slow cooker.

Step 2: Add the BBQ Sauce Mixture

In a bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, chicken broth, brown sugar, and cayenne pepper. Pour the mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker, ensuring that the chicken is well coated.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken

Cover the slow cooker and cook the chicken on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

Step 4: Shred the Chicken

Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, mixing it with the sauce.

Step 5: Assemble the Sliders

To assemble the BBQ pulled chicken sliders, place a generous amount of the pulled chicken onto the bottom half of each slider bun. Top with coleslaw if desired, then cover with the top half of the bun.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Serve the sliders on a platter and watch as your guests dig in with delight. These mini sandwiches are perfect for any BBQ party, and the tender, flavorful chicken combined with the soft buns is a winning combination.

Tips for Success

  • To save time, you can use a store-bought rotisserie chicken and mix it with your favorite BBQ sauce instead of cooking the chicken from scratch.
  • For added flavor, consider toasting the slider buns before assembling the sandwiches.
  • Don’t be afraid to get creative with your toppings. In addition to coleslaw, you can add pickles, sliced red onions, or even a drizzle of extra BBQ sauce for a personalized touch.

Final Thoughts

BBQ pulled chicken sliders are a fantastic addition to any BBQ party menu. Their small size makes them easy to handle and perfect for mingling, while their big flavor ensures that they’ll be a crowd favorite. Whether you serve them as the main dish or as part of a larger spread, these mini sandwiches are sure to be a hit. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to enjoy these irresistible BBQ pulled chicken sliders at your next outdoor gathering!
