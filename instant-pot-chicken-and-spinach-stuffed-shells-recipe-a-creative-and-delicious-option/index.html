Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Shells Recipe: A Creative and Delicious Option


Are you tired of the same old stuffed shells recipe? Look no further! This Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Shells recipe is a creative and delicious twist on the classic dish. The addition of chicken and spinach adds a boost of protein and flavor to this comforting pasta dish. And the best part? It’s quick and easy to make in your Instant Pot!

##Ingredients

  • 12 jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into small pieces
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth

##Instructions

  1. Preheat the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  2. Add the chicken to the pot and cook until browned, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.
  3. Add the spinach to the pot and cook until wilted, about 1-2 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes (if desired).
  4. In a large bowl, combine the cooked chicken, wilted spinach, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, beaten egg, dried basil, and dried oregano. Mix well to combine.
  5. Stuff each pasta shell with the chicken and spinach mixture, placing them in a single layer in the Instant Pot.
  6. Pour the marinara sauce and chicken broth over the stuffed shells, making sure they are completely covered.
  7. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to “sealing”. Press the “manual” or “pressure cook” button and set the cooking time to 10 minutes at high pressure.
  8. Let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  9. Open the lid and check that the shells are cooked through and the filling is heated through. If necessary, cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  10. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and basil leaves if desired.

##Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe more substantial, add some cooked ground beef or sausage to the chicken and spinach mixture.
  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the chicken with cooked mushrooms or eggplant.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, add 1/4 cup heavy cream or half-and-half to the marinara sauce.
  • This recipe freezes well, so feel free to make a batch and freeze for later use.

##Conclusion

This Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Shells recipe is a creative and delicious twist on the classic dish. The addition of chicken and spinach adds a boost of protein and flavor to this comforting pasta dish. And the best part? It’s quick and easy to make in your Instant Pot! With its creamy sauce and flavorful filling, this recipe is sure to become a family favorite. Give it a try and enjoy!
