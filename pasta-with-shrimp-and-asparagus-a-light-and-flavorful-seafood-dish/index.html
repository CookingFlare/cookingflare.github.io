Pasta with Shrimp and Asparagus: A Light and Flavorful Seafood Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and light seafood dish that’s perfect for any occasion, look no further than this delightful Pasta with Shrimp and Asparagus recipe. This dish combines the succulent taste of shrimp with the fresh, earthy flavor of asparagus, all tossed together with perfectly cooked pasta. It’s a dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends, and it’s surprisingly easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound of asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 12 ounces of pasta (linguine or fettuccine work well)
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of dry white wine
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp and Asparagus: In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the asparagus for 2-3 minutes until crisp-tender. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the asparagus to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Drain and set aside. In the same pot of boiling water, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same skillet, add the remaining olive oil and minced garlic. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Pour in the white wine and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until it reduces slightly. Add the lemon zest and juice to the skillet, then return the cooked shrimp and asparagus to the pan. Toss everything together until well combined.

  4. Combine the Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the shrimp and asparagus. Toss everything together until the pasta is coated in the flavorful sauce and the ingredients are well combined.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the pasta with shrimp and asparagus to a large serving dish. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and an extra squeeze of lemon juice for an added burst of freshness.

This Pasta with Shrimp and Asparagus recipe is a perfect balance of lightness and flavor, making it an ideal dish for a cozy dinner at home or a special gathering with loved ones. The tender shrimp, crisp asparagus, and zesty lemon create a harmonious blend of flavors that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or just looking to try something new, this recipe is a must-try.

In conclusion, this Pasta with Shrimp and Asparagus recipe is a delightful combination of fresh ingredients that come together to create a light and flavorful seafood dish. It’s a versatile recipe that can be enjoyed as a quick weeknight meal or as part of a more elaborate dinner spread. Give it a try and savor the delightful taste of shrimp and asparagus combined with perfectly cooked pasta.
