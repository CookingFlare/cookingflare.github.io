Love in Every Bite: Heart-Shaped Ravioli Recipe for Valentine's Day


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than by cooking a delicious and romantic meal for your loved one? This year, why not surprise them with a homemade heart-shaped ravioli dish that will leave them speechless? In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of creating these adorable and tasty heart-shaped ravioli that are sure to win over any heart.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 cup marinara sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pasta Dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Create a well in the center of the flour mixture.
    • Crack the eggs into the well and add the olive oil. Gently beat the eggs and oil together with a fork, gradually incorporating the flour from the sides of the well.
    • Once the dough starts to come together, knead it on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, nutmeg, black pepper, chopped basil, and parsley. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Roll Out the Dough:

    • After the dough has rested, divide it into two equal portions. Take one portion and flatten it with your hands.
    • Using a rolling pin, roll out the dough into a thin sheet, about 1/8 inch thick. Make sure to dust the surface and the dough with flour to prevent sticking.

  4. Shape the Ravioli:

    • Using a heart-shaped cookie cutter, cut out heart shapes from the rolled-out dough. Place the hearts on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    • Spoon about a teaspoon of the ricotta filling onto the center of half of the heart shapes. Leave a small border around the edges to seal the ravioli.
    • Place another heart shape on top of each filled heart, pressing down gently around the edges to seal the ravioli. Use a fork to crimp the edges for a decorative touch.

  5. Cook the Ravioli:

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Carefully drop the ravioli into the boiling water and cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until they float to the surface.
    • Using a slotted spoon, transfer the cooked ravioli to a plate.

  6. Serve:

    • Plate the heart-shaped ravioli and drizzle with your favorite marinara sauce. Garnish with some additional grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves for an extra pop of flavor and color.

Now, all that’s left to do is to set the table, light some candles, and enjoy a romantic and delicious Valentine’s Day dinner with your special someone. Remember, every bite of these heart-shaped ravioli is filled with love. Happy cooking and happy Valentine’s Day!

Note: You can customize this recipe by adding different fillings such as spinach and ricotta or butternut squash and sage. Get creative and have fun experimenting with different flavors to make this dish truly your own.
