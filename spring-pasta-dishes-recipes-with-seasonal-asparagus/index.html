Spring Pasta Dishes: Recipes with Seasonal Asparagus


Spring is the perfect time to indulge in fresh and vibrant flavors, and what better way to celebrate the season than with delicious pasta dishes featuring seasonal asparagus? Asparagus is a versatile vegetable that adds a unique touch to any dish, and its tender and crisp texture pairs perfectly with pasta. In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering recipes that showcase the best of spring flavors.

1. Creamy Asparagus and Lemon Pasta

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 20 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the asparagus to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir in the lemon zest and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss until well coated with the creamy sauce.
  6. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.

2. Lemon Garlic Shrimp and Asparagus Linguine

Preparation time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 15 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces linguine pasta
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the linguine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side until pink and cooked through.
  4. Add the asparagus to the skillet and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until tender.
  5. Toss the cooked linguine with the shrimp, asparagus, lemon zest, and lemon juice until well combined.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste and garnish with fresh parsley.

3. Roasted Asparagus and Tomato Penne

Preparation time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 25 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Toss the asparagus, cherry tomatoes, minced garlic, olive oil, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  4. Cook the penne according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a large skillet, combine the roasted asparagus and tomatoes with the cooked penne. Toss until well combined.
  6. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.

Enjoy the vibrant flavors of spring with these delightful asparagus pasta dishes. Whether you prefer a creamy and indulgent option or a light and refreshing seafood dish, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. These recipes are quick and easy to make, making them perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. So grab some fresh asparagus and get cooking!
