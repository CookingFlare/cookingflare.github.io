Spicy Buffalo Chicken Dip Stuffed Bread: A Game Day Winner


Are you ready to elevate your game day spread with a show-stopping appetizer that will have everyone coming back for more? Look no further than this Spicy Buffalo Chicken Dip Stuffed Bread. It’s the perfect combination of spicy, cheesy, and savory flavors, all conveniently packed into a delicious loaf of bread. Whether you’re hosting a game day party or simply craving a flavorful snack, this recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

For the Buffalo Chicken Dip:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Stuffed Bread:

  • 1 large loaf of French bread
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions
  • Additional hot sauce for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large bowl, mix together the shredded chicken, hot sauce, cream cheese, sour cream, cheddar cheese, blue cheese dressing, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside.

  3. Using a sharp knife, carefully slice the top off the loaf of French bread. Hollow out the center of the loaf, leaving about a 1-inch border around the edges.

  4. Brush the inside of the bread with the melted butter, then sprinkle with garlic powder and dried parsley.

  5. Spoon the prepared buffalo chicken dip into the hollowed-out bread, spreading it evenly.

  6. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the top of the dip, then place the bread top back on.

  7. Wrap the stuffed bread in aluminum foil and place it on a baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.

  8. Remove the foil from the bread and continue baking for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Once the bread is out of the oven, sprinkle the sliced green onions over the top and drizzle with additional hot sauce for an extra kick.

  10. Allow the stuffed bread to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Spicy Buffalo Chicken Dip Stuffed Bread pairs perfectly with crisp celery sticks, carrot sticks, and your favorite dipping sauces. It’s also fantastic alongside a refreshing cucumber and tomato salad for a well-rounded game day feast.

Make It Your Own

Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. Add extra hot sauce for an even spicier kick, or mix in some diced jalapeños for added heat and texture. For a milder version, reduce the amount of hot sauce and consider using a milder cheese in place of the blue cheese.

Conclusion

With its irresistible combination of spicy buffalo chicken dip and warm, crusty bread, this recipe is a guaranteed game day winner. Whether you’re cheering on your favorite team or hosting a casual get-together, this Spicy Buffalo Chicken Dip Stuffed Bread is sure to become a crowd favorite. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress your guests with this delicious and hearty appetizer.
