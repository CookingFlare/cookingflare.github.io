Indulgent Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Bars


If there’s one thing that can instantly lift your spirits, it’s a delightful treat that combines the rich, nutty flavor of peanut butter with the indulgent sweetness of chocolate. In this recipe, we’re going to create a batch of irresistible Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Bars that will leave you and your loved ones craving for more. These bars are perfect for a cozy evening in, a potluck with friends, or simply as a well-deserved indulgence after a long day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the creamy peanut butter, melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar. Mix until smooth and well combined.

  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the peanut butter mixture, and beat until the mixture is light and fluffy.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the peanut butter mixture, stirring until just combined.

  5. Gently fold in the chocolate chips, reserving a handful to sprinkle on top of the batter.

  6. Transfer the batter to the prepared baking pan, spreading it out evenly with a spatula. Sprinkle the reserved chocolate chips on top.

  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  8. Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack. Once cooled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the bars out of the pan and onto a cutting board. Slice into squares or bars of your desired size.

Serving Suggestions

These Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Bars are best enjoyed with a glass of cold milk or a piping hot cup of coffee. They also make a delightful addition to a dessert platter at a party or a picnic. For an extra indulgent treat, warm the bars slightly in the microwave and top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Storage

Store any leftover bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. If you want to keep them for longer, you can freeze the bars in a single layer, then transfer them to a freezer-safe bag or container for up to 3 months.

Final Thoughts

The combination of creamy peanut butter and luscious chocolate chips in these bars is a match made in dessert heaven. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your collection. So why not treat yourself and your loved ones to a batch of these indulgent Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Bars? After all, life is too short to say no to a little sweetness now and then. Enjoy!
