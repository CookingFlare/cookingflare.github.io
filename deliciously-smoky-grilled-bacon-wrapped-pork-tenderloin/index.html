Deliciously Smoky: Grilled Bacon-Wrapped Pork Tenderloin

3 min · 510 words

There’s something undeniably satisfying about the combination of juicy pork, crispy bacon, and the smoky flavor of the grill. If you’re looking for a show-stopping dish to impress your guests at your next barbecue, look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for grilled bacon-wrapped pork tenderloin.

Ingredients

  • 2 pork tenderloins
  • 1 pound of bacon
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pork Tenderloin

Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the pork tenderloins by trimming off any excess fat and silver skin. Season the tenderloins with salt and pepper, ensuring that they are evenly coated.

Step 2: Wrap in Bacon

Next, take the strips of bacon and wrap them around the seasoned pork tenderloins, securing them with toothpicks as needed. The bacon not only adds a delicious smoky flavor but also helps to keep the pork tender and juicy during grilling.

Step 3: Prepare the Marinade

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, and fresh rosemary. This aromatic marinade will infuse the pork with a rich, herbaceous flavor that complements the smokiness of the bacon.

Step 4: Marinate the Pork

Place the bacon-wrapped pork tenderloins in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, ensuring that they are coated evenly. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and allow the pork to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours for maximum flavor infusion.

Step 5: Grill to Perfection

Once the pork has finished marinating, it’s time to grill! Place the bacon-wrapped pork tenderloins on the preheated grill and cook for 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally, until the bacon is crispy and the pork reaches an internal temperature of 145°F.

Step 6: Rest and Serve

Remove the pork tenderloins from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before removing the toothpicks and slicing them into medallions. The result? Tender, juicy pork with a crispy, smoky bacon crust that is sure to impress even the most discerning barbecue enthusiasts.

Serving Suggestions

This grilled bacon-wrapped pork tenderloin pairs perfectly with a variety of sides, from grilled vegetables to creamy mashed potatoes. For a burst of freshness, consider serving it with a simple green salad dressed with a zesty vinaigrette. And of course, no barbecue is complete without a refreshing beverage – whether it’s a crisp white wine, a cold beer, or a fruity lemonade, the choice is yours!

Final Thoughts

With its irresistible combination of savory pork, smoky bacon, and aromatic marinade, this grilled bacon-wrapped pork tenderloin is a surefire way to elevate your next barbecue to new heights. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving a delicious and impressive meal, this recipe is guaranteed to be a hit. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to savor every delectable bite of this mouthwatering masterpiece. Happy grilling!
