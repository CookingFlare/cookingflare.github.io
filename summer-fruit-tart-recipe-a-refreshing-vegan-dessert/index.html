Summer Fruit Tart Recipe: A Refreshing Vegan Dessert


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in refreshing and fruity desserts. And what better way to satisfy your sweet tooth than with a delicious Summer Fruit Tart? This vegan dessert is not only incredibly tasty but also easy to make. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of almond flour
  • ½ cup of coconut flour
  • ¼ cup of maple syrup
  • ¼ cup of coconut oil, melted
  • A pinch of salt

For the filling:

  • 1 can of coconut cream, chilled overnight
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the topping:

  • A variety of fresh summer fruits such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and sliced peaches

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, coconut flour, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and a pinch of salt. Mix until well combined and the mixture resembles crumbly dough.

  3. Press the dough into a tart pan, making sure to evenly distribute it along the bottom and up the sides. Use a fork to prick the bottom of the crust to prevent it from puffing up during baking.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  5. While the crust is cooling, prepare the filling. Take the chilled can of coconut cream and carefully scoop out the thick cream that has solidified at the top. Place it in a mixing bowl, leaving behind any liquid at the bottom.

  6. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the coconut cream. Using an electric mixer, beat the mixture until it becomes light and fluffy, resembling whipped cream.

  7. Once the crust has cooled, spread the coconut cream filling evenly over the crust, leaving a small border around the edges.

  8. Now, it’s time to decorate your tart with the fresh summer fruits. Arrange the sliced peaches, strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries on top of the coconut cream filling in any pattern you like. Get creative and make it as visually appealing as possible!

  9. Place the tart in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together and the filling to set.

  10. When you’re ready to serve, remove the tart from the refrigerator and slice it into pieces. Enjoy the refreshing and fruity flavors of this vegan summer dessert!

Tips and Variations

  • If you’re not a fan of almond flour, you can substitute it with oat flour or a gluten-free all-purpose flour blend.
  • Feel free to experiment with different fruits depending on what’s in season. Mangoes, kiwis, and blackberries would make great additions to your tart.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can drizzle some melted dark chocolate over the top of the tart before serving.
  • If you want to add some crunch to your tart, sprinkle some toasted coconut flakes or chopped nuts on top of the filling.

Final Thoughts

This Summer Fruit Tart is a delightful vegan dessert that will impress your family and friends. With its buttery crust, creamy coconut filling, and vibrant assortment of fresh fruits, it’s the perfect treat to enjoy on a warm summer day. Give this recipe a try and let the flavors of summer awaken your taste buds!
