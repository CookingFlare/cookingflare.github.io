Flavorful Instant Pot Indian Recipes to Spice Up Your Kitchen


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, you know that the flavors and aromas of these dishes are truly something special. The rich spices, vibrant colors, and diverse range of ingredients make Indian food a feast for the senses. And what better way to bring these flavors into your kitchen than with the help of an Instant Pot? In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious and flavorful Instant Pot Indian recipes that will surely spice up your cooking routine.

1. Butter Chicken

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Total Time: 30 minutes

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a classic Indian dish that is loved by many. It’s a creamy and flavorful curry that pairs perfectly with naan or rice. Here’s how you can make it in your Instant Pot:

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and melt the butter.
  2. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the spices (garam masala, turmeric powder, and paprika) and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree, chicken, and salt. Stir well to combine.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual high pressure for 8 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick pressure release and carefully open the lid.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream and let it simmer for a few minutes to thicken the sauce.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice.

2. Chana Masala

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Total Time: 35 minutes

Chana Masala, also known as Chickpea Curry, is a popular vegetarian dish that is full of flavor and protein. It’s perfect for those looking to add more plant-based meals to their diet. Here’s how you can make it in your Instant Pot:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas (or 1 can, drained and rinsed)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a splash of oil.
  2. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the spices (garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and red chili powder) and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree, cooked chickpeas, and salt. Stir well to combine.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual high pressure for 10 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then do a quick pressure release.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or roti.

3. Vegetable Biryani

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Total Time: 35 minutes

Biryani is a fragrant rice dish that is often served on special occasions. It’s a flavorful combination of rice, vegetables, and aromatic spices. Here’s how you can make Vegetable Biryani in your Instant Pot:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and mint, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a splash of oil.
  2. Add the cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds.
  3. Add the sliced onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onion is golden brown.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and sauté for a couple of minutes.
  5. Add the spices (biryani masala, turmeric powder, and red chili powder) and sauté for another minute.
  6. Add the soaked and drained basmati rice, yogurt, and salt. Stir well to combine.
  7. Add 2.5 cups of water and give it a gentle stir.
  8. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual high pressure for 5 minutes.
  9. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then do a quick pressure release.
  10. Fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with fresh cilantro and mint. Serve hot.

Conclusion

With these flavorful Instant Pot Indian recipes, you can bring the authentic taste of India right into your kitchen. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out, these recipes are sure to impress your family and friends. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to embark on a culinary journey filled with bold flavors and aromatic spices. Happy cooking!
