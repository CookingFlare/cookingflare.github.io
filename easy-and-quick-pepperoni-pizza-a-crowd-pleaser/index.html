Easy and Quick Pepperoni Pizza: A Crowd Pleaser


Who doesn’t love a delicious, piping hot pepperoni pizza? It’s a classic crowd-pleaser that never fails to satisfy cravings. Whether you’re hosting a game night, having a casual dinner with friends, or simply treating yourself to a tasty meal, this easy and quick pepperoni pizza recipe will surely hit the spot. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can have a homemade pizza that rivals your favorite pizzeria in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Olive oil, for brushing

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating to ensure a crispy crust.

  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness. If you prefer a thin crust, roll it out thinner; if you like a thicker crust, leave it a bit thicker.

  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a baking sheet or pizza peel lined with parchment paper. If you’re using a pizza stone, transfer the dough directly onto the preheated stone.

  4. Brush the surface of the dough lightly with olive oil. This will help prevent the sauce from making the crust soggy.

  5. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Make sure to cover the entire surface for that gooey, cheesy goodness.

  7. Arrange the sliced pepperoni on top of the cheese. Feel free to add as much or as little as you like. If you’re a fan of extra pepperoni, go ahead and load it up!

  8. Sprinkle the dried oregano evenly over the toppings. This will add a touch of herbaceous flavor to the pizza.

  9. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden brown.

  10. Once the pizza is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. This will allow the cheese to set slightly, making it easier to slice.

  11. If desired, garnish with fresh basil leaves and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for an extra kick of flavor.

  12. Slice the pizza into wedges or squares and serve hot. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Customize your pizza by adding your favorite toppings such as mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, or onions.
  • For a healthier option, use whole wheat pizza dough and opt for low-fat cheese.
  • If you prefer a spicier pizza, use spicy pepperoni or add some jalapeño slices.
  • To make the pizza dough from scratch, combine 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast, 1 teaspoon sugar, 3/4 cup warm water, and 2 cups all-purpose flour in a bowl. Let the dough rise for about an hour before rolling it out.

Now that you have this easy and quick pepperoni pizza recipe in your arsenal, you can satisfy your pizza cravings anytime. Whether you’re feeding a hungry crowd or simply treating yourself to a delicious meal, this crowd-pleaser is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, preheat that oven, and get ready to indulge in a slice of cheesy goodness. Enjoy!
