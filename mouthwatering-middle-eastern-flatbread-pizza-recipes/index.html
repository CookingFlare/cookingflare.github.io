Mouthwatering Middle Eastern Flatbread Pizza Recipes


When it comes to comfort food, pizzas reign supreme across the globe, but have you ever tried a Middle Eastern twist on this classic dish? With their rich flavors, unique ingredients, and delightful textures, Middle Eastern flatbread pizzas are a culinary adventure waiting to be explored. In this blog post, we’ll dive into some mouthwatering recipes that elevate traditional pizzas to new heights using flatbreads like pita, lavash, and manakish. Whether you’re hosting a dinner or looking for a quick and satisfying meal, these recipes are sure to impress!

The Base: Understanding Flatbreads

Before we jump into the recipes, let’s talk about the star of the show: the flatbread. Middle Eastern flatbreads come in various forms, each with its own unique texture and flavor profile. Here are a few popular types:

  • Pita Bread: Soft and pocket-like, perfect for stuffing or as a base for toppings.
  • Lavash: A thin, flexible flatbread that can be rolled or folded.
  • Manakish: A traditional Lebanese flatbread, often topped with za’atar or cheese.

These flatbreads serve as the perfect canvas for an array of toppings, allowing you to create pizzas that are both delicious and visually appealing.

Recipe 1: Za’atar Chicken Pita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 large pita breads
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons za’atar spice blend
  • 1 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Prepare the Chicken: In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with za’atar, a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. This will infuse the chicken with aromatic flavors.

  3. Assemble the Pita Pizza: Place the pita breads on a baking sheet. Spread the seasoned chicken evenly over each pita. Add the halved cherry tomatoes and sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top.

  4. Bake: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is slightly golden and the pita is crispy.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Remove from the oven, top with fresh parsley, and serve with a dollop of plain yogurt on the side. Enjoy this flavorful Za’atar Chicken Pita Pizza warm!

Recipe 2: Spicy Lamb Lavash Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 large lavash breads
  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Lamb: In a skillet over medium heat, sauté the chopped onion until translucent. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute. Then, add the ground lamb, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Cook until the lamb is browned and cooked through.

  2. Preheat the Oven: While the lamb is cooking, preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  3. Assemble the Lavash Pizza: Lay the lavash breads flat on a baking sheet. Spread the cooked lamb mixture evenly over the lavash. Top with sliced red bell peppers and sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over everything.

  4. Bake: Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for about 8-10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh mint leaves. Slice into pieces and serve hot!

Recipe 3: Mediterranean Veggie Manakish

Ingredients

  • 2 manakish breads (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1 cup bell peppers, diced (red, yellow, and green)
  • 1 cup cucumbers, diced
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Spread the Hummus: Place the manakish breads on a baking sheet. Generously spread hummus on each flatbread, covering it entirely.

  3. Top with Vegetables: Arrange the diced bell peppers, cucumbers, olives, red onion, and cherry tomatoes on top of the hummus. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper. Drizzle with olive oil for added flavor.

  4. Bake: Bake the manakish in the oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are crispy and the vegetables are slightly tender.

  5. Serve: Slice the Mediterranean Veggie Manakish into wedges and serve warm as a delicious appetizer or light meal.

Recipe 4: Roasted Eggplant and Tomato Pita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 large pita breads
  • 1 medium eggplant, diced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Roast the Vegetables: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). On a baking sheet, toss the diced eggplant and halved cherry tomatoes with olive oil, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Roast in the oven for about 20 minutes, or until the eggplant is tender and golden.

  2. Assemble the Pita Pizza: While the vegetables are roasting, place the pita breads on another baking sheet. Once the vegetables are done, distribute them evenly over the pitas. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.

  3. Bake: Return the baking sheet to the oven and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  4. Garnish and Serve: Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil, and serve hot. This Roasted Eggplant and Tomato Pita Pizza is a healthy and delicious option!

Recipe 5: Sweet and Savory Fig and Goat Cheese Lavash Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 large lavash breads
  • 1 cup fresh figs, sliced
  • 1 cup goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup balsamic glaze
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • Fresh arugula for garnish
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Assemble the Lavash Pizza: Place the lavash breads on a baking sheet. Arrange the sliced figs evenly over the lavash, then sprinkle with crumbled goat cheese and chopped walnuts.

  3. Bake: Drizzle a little olive oil over the top and season with salt and pepper. Bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is warmed and the lavash is crispy.

  4. Drizzle and Serve: Remove from the oven and drizzle with balsamic glaze. Garnish with fresh arugula before slicing and serving. This Sweet and Savory Fig and Goat Cheese Lavash Pizza is perfect for a sophisticated appetizer!

Conclusion

Middle Eastern flatbread pizzas offer a vibrant and diverse way to enjoy the beloved classic. With their unique bases and delicious toppings, these recipes bring together the best of both worlds—comfort food meets exotic flavors. Whether you’re in the mood for something meaty, veggie-packed, or sweet, there’s a flatbread pizza here for everyone. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and prepare to impress your family and friends with these mouthwatering creations! Enjoy the journey of flavors and the joy of sharing a meal that’s as delightful as it is delicious.
