Chicken and Broccoli Pasta Bake: A Wholesome and Delicious Meal


If you’re looking for a comforting and satisfying meal that the whole family will love, look no further than this Chicken and Broccoli Pasta Bake recipe. This dish is a delicious combination of tender chicken, vibrant broccoli, al dente pasta, and a creamy, cheesy sauce, all baked to perfection. It’s a one-pan wonder that’s easy to prepare and is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

To make this Chicken and Broccoli Pasta Bake, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 12 oz (340g) of penne pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Ingredients

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until just al dente. While the pasta is cooking, blanch the broccoli florets in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then drain and set aside.

Step 2: Cooking the Chicken

In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken breasts to the skillet and season with salt, pepper, oregano, and thyme. Cook until the chicken is golden brown and fully cooked. Remove the cooked chicken from the skillet and set aside.

Step 3: Making the Sauce

In the same skillet, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Sprinkle the flour over the garlic and oil, stirring constantly to make a roux. Slowly pour in the milk and chicken broth, stirring continuously until the sauce thickens.

Step 4: Assembling the Pasta Bake

Add the cooked pasta, blanched broccoli, and cooked chicken to the skillet with the creamy sauce. Stir everything together until well combined. Finally, add the shredded cheddar cheese and half of the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until the cheeses are melted and the sauce is creamy. If desired, add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat.

Step 5: Baking the Dish

Transfer the mixture to a greased 9x13-inch baking dish and sprinkle the remaining Parmesan cheese over the top. Cover the dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

Step 6: Serving and Enjoying

Once the Chicken and Broccoli Pasta Bake is golden and bubbling, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Serve generous portions onto plates and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley, if desired. This dish pairs beautifully with a crisp green salad or some garlic bread on the side.

Conclusion

This Chicken and Broccoli Pasta Bake is a versatile and satisfying meal that’s perfect for busy weeknights or special occasions. With its hearty combination of protein, vegetables, and comforting flavors, it’s a dish that’s sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. Plus, the leftovers (if there are any) make for a delicious next-day lunch. So, give this recipe a try and savor the wholesome goodness of this delightful pasta bake.
