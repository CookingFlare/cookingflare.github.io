Fudgy Black Bean Brownie Bars: A Healthy Twist

3 min · 494 words

If you’re a fan of brownies but want to indulge in a healthier version, then look no further! These Fudgy Black Bean Brownie Bars are a delightful twist on the classic brownie, offering a rich and decadent treat with a surprising secret ingredient - black beans. Yes, you read that right – black beans! Before you raise an eyebrow, let me assure you that these brownie bars are incredibly fudgy, chocolatey, and utterly delicious. Plus, they pack a nutritional punch, making them a guilt-free pleasure.

Ingredients

For this wholesome twist on brownie bars, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant coffee (optional, but enhances the chocolate flavor)
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/3 cup chopped walnuts (optional, for added crunch)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8 inch baking pan and set it aside.

  2. In a food processor, combine the black beans, eggs, melted coconut oil, cocoa powder, salt, vanilla extract, granulated sugar, and instant coffee (if using). Blend until smooth and creamy.

  3. Stir in the semi-sweet chocolate chips and chopped walnuts, if desired, and mix until well combined.

  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges start to pull away from the pan and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  6. Allow the brownie bars to cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares.

Health Benefits

Now, let’s talk about the benefits of using black beans in this recipe. Black beans are an excellent source of plant-based protein and fiber, making them a nutritious addition to any dessert. They also contain essential vitamins and minerals, such as folate, iron, and magnesium. By incorporating black beans into these brownie bars, you’re boosting their nutritional value without compromising on taste or texture.

Serving Suggestions

These Fudgy Black Bean Brownie Bars are delightful on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a dollop of Greek yogurt or a sprinkle of powdered sugar. They also pair wonderfully with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a decadent treat. Whether enjoyed as a midday snack or a dessert after dinner, these brownie bars are sure to satisfy your sweet cravings.

Conclusion

In conclusion, these Fudgy Black Bean Brownie Bars are a game-changer for anyone seeking a healthier alternative to traditional brownies. With their rich, fudgy texture and deep chocolate flavor, they are a delightful treat for both kids and adults. The addition of black beans not only enhances the nutritional profile of these brownie bars but also adds to their decadent texture. So, go ahead and surprise your taste buds with this guilt-free indulgence – after all, who said healthy can’t be delicious?
