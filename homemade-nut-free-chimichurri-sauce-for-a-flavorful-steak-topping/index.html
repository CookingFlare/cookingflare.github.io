Homemade Nut-Free Chimichurri Sauce for a Flavorful Steak Topping


If you’re a fan of grilling and love a good steak, then you’re in for a treat. This homemade nut-free chimichurri sauce is the perfect way to elevate your steak game and impress your guests. The fresh and vibrant flavors of this sauce will take your taste buds on a culinary adventure, and the best part is that it’s quick and easy to make. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or just craving a delicious steak dinner, this chimichurri sauce is a must-have in your recipe arsenal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prep the Herbs: Start by finely chopping the fresh parsley and cilantro. The key to a great chimichurri sauce is using fresh herbs, so take the time to finely chop them to release their flavors.

  2. Mix the Ingredients: In a medium bowl, combine the chopped parsley, cilantro, minced garlic, extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, fresh lemon juice, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes. Stir the mixture until everything is well combined.

  3. Season to Perfection: Season the chimichurri sauce with salt and black pepper to taste. Give it a quick taste test and adjust the seasoning according to your preference. Remember, you can always add more salt and pepper, but you can’t take it away, so start with a little and add more as needed.

  4. Let it Rest: Once the chimichurri sauce is mixed and seasoned to your liking, let it sit at room temperature for at least 20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: This nut-free chimichurri sauce is perfect for drizzling over a juicy grilled steak. It adds a burst of freshness and tanginess to the rich flavors of the meat, creating a harmonious balance that will leave your taste buds singing. You can also use it as a marinade for the steak before grilling, or even as a dipping sauce for roasted vegetables.

Why Nut-Free?

You might be wondering why this chimichurri sauce is labeled as nut-free. Traditionally, chimichurri sauce contains nuts, but this recipe omits them, making it safe for those with nut allergies to enjoy. By using a combination of fresh herbs, garlic, olive oil, and vinegar, this nut-free version captures the essence of the classic chimichurri sauce without compromising on flavor.

Final Thoughts

Homemade nut-free chimichurri sauce is a versatile and delicious condiment that can take your steak dinners to the next level. With its bright and zesty flavors, it’s a perfect complement to the richness of a perfectly cooked steak. This recipe is not only nut-free but also gluten-free and vegan, making it a crowd-pleaser for various dietary preferences. So, the next time you fire up the grill, whip up a batch of this chimichurri sauce and get ready to savor the delicious results.

Whether you’re a seasoned grill master or just starting out, this homemade nut-free chimichurri sauce will undoubtedly become a staple in your culinary repertoire. So, grab your ingredients, mix up a batch, and get ready to elevate your steak game with this flavorful and vibrant sauce. Cheers to delicious meals and happy grilling!
