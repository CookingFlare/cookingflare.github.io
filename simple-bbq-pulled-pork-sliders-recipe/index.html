Simple BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders Recipe


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing dish to serve at your next gathering, these simple BBQ pulled pork sliders are sure to be a hit. Whether it’s a summer barbecue, game day party, or casual get-together, these sliders are easy to make and packed with flavor. The tender, succulent pulled pork combined with the sweet and tangy BBQ sauce, all sandwiched between soft, buttery slider buns, creates a winning combination that will have everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

For the Pulled Pork:

  • 4-5 pound pork shoulder or butt
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chicken broth

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sliders:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • Sliced pickles (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork: In a small bowl, mix together the garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cumin, black pepper, and salt. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork shoulder, ensuring it’s evenly coated. Place the seasoned pork in the slow cooker and pour the chicken broth around it. Cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 5-6 hours, until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  2. Make the BBQ Sauce: While the pork is cooking, prepare the BBQ sauce. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, honey, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat and let it cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.

  3. Shred the Pork: Once the pork is done cooking, remove it from the slow cooker and place it on a cutting board. Using two forks, shred the pork into bite-sized pieces. Place the shredded pork back into the slow cooker and pour the BBQ sauce over it. Stir to coat the pork evenly with the sauce.

  4. Assemble the Sliders: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Slice the slider buns in half and place the bottom halves in a baking dish. Brush the melted butter over the buns. Toast them in the oven for 5 minutes, or until they’re lightly golden. Remove from the oven and spoon the saucy pulled pork onto the bottom buns. If desired, add a slice of pickle for a tangy kick. Place the top buns on the pork and press down gently.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: These simple BBQ pulled pork sliders are ready to be served. Arrange them on a platter and watch as they disappear within minutes. They pair perfectly with coleslaw, potato salad, or a fresh green salad for a complete meal.

Tips for Success

  • For extra flavor, you can add a splash of apple juice or apple cider to the slow cooker when cooking the pork.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use a store-bought BBQ sauce instead of making it from scratch.
  • Leftover pulled pork can be refrigerated for up to 3 days or frozen for later use. Just reheat it gently in a saucepan or in the microwave when you’re ready to enjoy it again.

With its tender, juicy pork and irresistible BBQ flavor, these simple pulled pork sliders are a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. They’re easy to prepare, versatile, and perfect for feeding a hungry group. So, the next time you’re hosting a gathering, consider whipping up a batch of these delicious sliders and watch as they become the star of the party.
