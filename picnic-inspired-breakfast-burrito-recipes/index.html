Picnic-Inspired Breakfast Burrito Recipes


When it comes to breakfast on the go, there’s nothing quite like a delicious and satisfying breakfast burrito. And what better way to enjoy a breakfast burrito than by taking it on a picnic? Whether you’re heading to the park, the beach, or simply out into your own backyard, these picnic-inspired breakfast burrito recipes are the perfect way to start your day off right.

1. Classic Bacon and Egg Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, crack the eggs and scramble them until fully cooked. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or over an open flame.
  4. To assemble the burritos, place a quarter of the scrambled eggs, 2 slices of bacon, and a handful of shredded cheddar cheese in the center of each tortilla. Fold in the sides and roll up tightly.
  5. Wrap each burrito in foil and pack them in your picnic basket or cooler. They can be served warm or at room temperature.

2. Veggie Lover’s Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 cup diced mushrooms
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the diced bell peppers, onion, and mushrooms. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the beaten eggs to the skillet and scramble them with the vegetables. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or over an open flame.
  4. To assemble the burritos, place a quarter of the scrambled eggs and vegetable mixture, and a sprinkle of shredded Monterey Jack cheese in the center of each tortilla. Fold in the sides and roll up tightly.
  5. Wrap each burrito in foil and pack them in your picnic basket or cooler. They can be served warm or at room temperature.

3. Southwest Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 small red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small jalapeño, seeded and diced
  • 1 cup cooked black beans
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: salsa, sour cream, avocado

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion, bell peppers, and jalapeño. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the cooked black beans and beaten eggs to the skillet. Scramble everything together until the eggs are fully cooked. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or over an open flame.
  4. To assemble the burritos, place a quarter of the scrambled eggs and vegetable mixture, and a sprinkle of shredded pepper jack cheese in the center of each tortilla. Fold in the sides and roll up tightly.
  5. Wrap each burrito in foil and pack them in your picnic basket or cooler. Serve with your favorite toppings, such as salsa, sour cream, or avocado.

These picnic-inspired breakfast burrito recipes are not only delicious and satisfying but also convenient for enjoying on the go. With a variety of flavors and ingredients, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So pack up your picnic basket, grab a blanket, and start your day with a delicious breakfast burrito in the great outdoors. Happy picnicking!
