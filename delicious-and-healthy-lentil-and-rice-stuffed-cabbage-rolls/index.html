Delicious and Healthy Lentil and Rice Stuffed Cabbage Rolls


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy plant-based meal? Look no further! These Lentil and Rice Stuffed Cabbage Rolls are packed with flavor, nutrients, and are sure to satisfy your taste buds. This recipe combines tender cabbage leaves, flavorful lentils, and aromatic spices to create a dish that is both hearty and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cabbage
  • 1 cup of brown rice
  • 1 cup of green lentils
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Start by boiling a large pot of water. Carefully remove the outer leaves of the cabbage, making sure to keep them intact. Blanch the cabbage leaves in the boiling water for a few minutes until they become tender. Remove the leaves from the water and set them aside to cool.

  2. In a separate pot, cook the brown rice according to the package instructions. In another pot, cook the green lentils until they are tender but still hold their shape.

  3. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant. Add the grated carrot and diced bell pepper, and cook for a few more minutes until they soften.

  4. Drain the cooked lentils and add them to the skillet along with the cooked brown rice. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Season with dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  6. Take a cabbage leaf and place a spoonful of the lentil and rice mixture in the center. Roll the leaf tightly, tucking in the sides as you go. Repeat this step with the remaining cabbage leaves and filling.

  7. In a baking dish, spread a layer of crushed tomatoes mixed with tomato paste. Place the stuffed cabbage rolls on top of the tomato sauce, seam side down. Pour the remaining tomato sauce over the cabbage rolls, covering them completely.

  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the cabbage rolls are heated through and the flavors have melded together.

  9. Serve the Lentil and Rice Stuffed Cabbage Rolls hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired. These rolls make a perfect main dish or a satisfying side to accompany your favorite salad or roasted vegetables.

Conclusion

These Delicious and Healthy Lentil and Rice Stuffed Cabbage Rolls are a wonderful addition to your plant-based recipe collection. Packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, this dish is not only tasty but also nourishing for your body. The combination of tender cabbage leaves, flavorful lentils, and aromatic spices creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re following a plant-based diet or simply looking for a wholesome meal, these cabbage rolls are sure to impress. Give them a try and experience the joy of healthy and delicious cooking!
