Tasty Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sliders Recipe


If you’re looking for a mouthwatering dish that’s easy to make and perfect for feeding a crowd, look no further than these Tasty Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sliders. This recipe is a game-changer for busy weeknights, casual gatherings, or even game day parties. The beauty of this dish lies in its simplicity and the incredible flavors it delivers. With just a few simple ingredients and your trusty slow cooker, you can create a dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Slider buns
  • Coleslaw (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Start by placing the chicken breasts in the slow cooker. In a separate bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, chicken broth, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and salt.

  2. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken, ensuring that the chicken is evenly coated. Gently toss the chicken in the sauce to make sure it’s well covered.

  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook the chicken on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours. The chicken is ready when it easily shreds with a fork.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, allowing it to soak up all the delicious sauce.

  5. To assemble the sliders, place a generous amount of the BBQ chicken onto each slider bun. If desired, top the chicken with a dollop of coleslaw for some extra crunch and freshness.

  6. Serve the sliders with your favorite side dishes, such as potato salad, corn on the cob, or a fresh green salad.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a dash of hot sauce or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes to the sauce mixture.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use store-bought rotisserie chicken and mix it with the BBQ sauce for a quicker version of this recipe.
  • Leftover BBQ chicken can be used in tacos, quesadillas, or even on top of a loaded baked potato for a delicious twist.

Enjoy the Flavors of Slow Cooking

The beauty of using a slow cooker for this recipe is that it allows the flavors to meld together and develop into a rich, savory, and slightly sweet BBQ sauce that perfectly coats the tender shredded chicken. The result is a dish that’s bursting with flavor and incredibly versatile. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together or simply craving a fuss-free dinner, these Tasty Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sliders are sure to be a hit.

So, fire up your slow cooker, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy the delicious aromas that will fill your kitchen as this mouthwatering dish slowly cooks to perfection. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a fantastic way to create a memorable meal with minimal effort. Give it a try and savor every bite of these irresistible BBQ chicken sliders!
