15-Minute Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus Recipe


If you’re in the mood for a quick and delicious seafood dish, look no further than this 15-minute garlic butter shrimp and asparagus recipe. Not only is this dish bursting with flavor, but it’s also incredibly easy to prepare, making it the perfect option for a busy weeknight dinner or a casual get-together with friends. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bunch of fresh asparagus, trimmed
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp and Asparagus: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning them with salt, pepper, and paprika. Set aside. Meanwhile, trim the asparagus spears and cut them into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Sauté the Asparagus: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the asparagus for 5-6 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp. Remove the asparagus from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Cook the Shrimp: Using the same skillet, add the remaining olive oil and butter. Once the butter has melted, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Then, add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until they are pink and opaque.

  4. Combine and Serve: Once the shrimp are cooked through, return the asparagus to the skillet and toss everything together for a minute to warm through. Adjust the seasoning if needed. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve the garlic butter shrimp and asparagus hot.

Tips for Success:

  • Fresh Ingredients: Using fresh shrimp and crisp asparagus will elevate the flavors of this dish.
  • Don’t Overcook: Shrimp cook quickly, so be sure not to overcook them to maintain their tender texture.
  • Customize: Feel free to add a squeeze of lemon juice or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a zesty kick.

Final Thoughts:

This 15-minute garlic butter shrimp and asparagus recipe is a testament to the fact that you don’t need to spend hours in the kitchen to create a mouthwatering meal. The combination of succulent shrimp, vibrant asparagus, and flavorful garlic butter makes for a dish that is sure to impress. Whether you’re a seafood lover or just looking for a fuss-free dinner option, this recipe is bound to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy!

With the savory aroma of garlic and butter wafting from the skillet, this quick and easy recipe promises a delightful dining experience without the hassle. The marriage of succulent shrimp and tender-crisp asparagus is a match made in culinary heaven, and the simplicity of the preparation only adds to its allure. So, whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a satisfying meal, give this 15-minute garlic butter shrimp and asparagus recipe a try and savor the flavors of a gourmet dish in the comfort of your own home.
