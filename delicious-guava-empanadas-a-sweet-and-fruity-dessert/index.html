Delicious Guava Empanadas: A Sweet and Fruity Dessert


If you’re craving a delightful treat that blends sweetness with a hint of tropical flair, look no further than Guava Empanadas. These delicious pastries offer a perfect balance of flaky pastry crust and a luscious guava filling that will transport your taste buds to sunny shores and lazy afternoons.

Ingredients

To create these scrumptious Guava Empanadas, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • For the Dough:

    • All-purpose flour
    • Unsalted butter
    • Granulated sugar
    • Salt
    • Cold water

  • For the Guava Filling:

    • Guava paste
    • Cream cheese (optional for a creamy texture)
    • Ground cinnamon (optional for added flavor)

  • For Finishing:

    • Egg (for egg wash)
    • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Cut the cold butter into small cubes and add it to the flour mixture.

  2. Cut in the Butter: Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Add Water: Gradually add cold water, a tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.

  4. Form a Dough Ball: Gather the dough into a ball, flatten it into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Prepare the Guava Filling

  1. Prepare the Guava Paste: Cut the guava paste into small cubes for easier melting.

  2. Melt the Guava Paste: In a small saucepan over low heat, melt the guava paste until it becomes smooth and spreadable. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.

  3. Optional: Add Cream Cheese and Cinnamon: For a creamier filling, stir in cream cheese until fully incorporated. Add ground cinnamon to taste for a touch of warmth.

Step 3: Assemble the Empanadas

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Roll Out the Dough: On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to about 1/8 inch thickness. Use a round cutter or a small bowl to cut out circles of dough, approximately 4-5 inches in diameter.

  3. Fill the Empanadas: Place a spoonful of the guava filling in the center of each dough circle. Fold the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape and press the edges firmly to seal. You can use a fork to crimp the edges for a decorative touch.

  4. Brush with Egg Wash: In a small bowl, beat an egg with a tablespoon of water to create an egg wash. Brush the tops of the empanadas with the egg wash for a golden finish.

Step 4: Bake and Serve

  1. Bake the Empanadas: Place the assembled empanadas on the prepared baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

  2. Cool and Dust: Remove from the oven and let the empanadas cool on a wire rack. Once cooled, dust with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness.

  3. Serve and Enjoy: Guava Empanadas are best enjoyed warm or at room temperature. They pair wonderfully with a cup of coffee or as a delightful dessert after a flavorful meal.

Tips and Variations

  • Variation: Experiment with different fillings such as pineapple, mango, or even Nutella for a decadent twist.
  • Storage: Store leftover empanadas in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. Reheat briefly in the oven to restore crispness.

Conclusion

Guava Empanadas are not just a dessert; they’re a culinary journey to the tropics. With their buttery crust and sweet guava filling, these empanadas are sure to become a favorite among family and friends. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself, indulge in the irresistible flavors of these homemade delights. Embrace the simplicity of creating something special in your kitchen and savor every bite of these delicious Guava Empanadas.
