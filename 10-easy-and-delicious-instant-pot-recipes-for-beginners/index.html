10 Easy and Delicious Instant Pot Recipes for Beginners


Cooking with an Instant Pot can be a game-changer in the kitchen, especially for beginners. This versatile appliance can help you whip up delicious meals in no time, with minimal effort. Whether you’re a busy professional, a student, or just someone who wants to simplify their cooking routine, these 10 easy and delicious Instant Pot recipes are perfect for you.

1. Creamy Chicken Alfredo

Creamy, comforting, and oh-so-satisfying, this Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo is a crowd-pleaser. Simply sauté some chicken, onions, and garlic, then add pasta, broth, and cream. Pressure cook for a few minutes, and voila! You have a restaurant-quality dinner on your plate.

2. Beef Stew

There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of beef stew on a chilly evening. With your Instant Pot, you can achieve tender and flavorful beef in a fraction of the time. Throw in some carrots, potatoes, and spices, and let the pressure cooker work its magic. In just under an hour, you’ll have a hearty and delicious stew ready to be devoured.

3. Spicy Chili

If you’re a fan of bold flavors and a little heat, this Instant Pot Spicy Chili will hit the spot. Simply brown some ground beef, onions, and peppers, then add tomatoes, beans, and spices. Pressure cook for a short while, and you’ll have a spicy and comforting chili perfect for game nights or cozy evenings at home.

4. Creamy Tomato Soup

There’s nothing quite like a bowl of tomato soup on a rainy day. With your Instant Pot, you can achieve a creamy and flavorful tomato soup without the hassle. Sauté some onions and garlic, add tomatoes, broth, and cream, and pressure cook for a few minutes. Blend it all together, and you’ll have a velvety smooth soup that’s perfect for dunking grilled cheese sandwiches.

5. Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon

Who said you can’t make gourmet seafood dishes in your Instant Pot? This Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon is not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. Simply season your salmon fillets with lemon, garlic, and butter, then pressure cook for a few minutes. The result? Tender, flaky salmon that’s bursting with flavor.

6. Pulled Pork Sandwiches

Craving some juicy pulled pork sandwiches? Your Instant Pot can make it happen. Simply season your pork shoulder with your favorite spices, add some broth, and pressure cook until it’s fall-apart tender. Shred the pork, mix it with your favorite barbecue sauce, and serve it on a toasted bun. It’s the perfect recipe for a summer barbecue or a casual dinner with friends.

7. Vegetable Risotto

Risotto may seem intimidating, but with your Instant Pot, it’s a breeze. Sauté some onions, garlic, and vegetables, then add Arborio rice, broth, and seasonings. Pressure cook for a short while, and you’ll have a creamy and comforting vegetable risotto that’s sure to impress.

8. Chicken Noodle Soup

When you’re feeling under the weather or simply in need of some comfort food, nothing beats a bowl of homemade chicken noodle soup. With your Instant Pot, you can have it ready in no time. Simply add chicken, noodles, vegetables, and broth, and pressure cook for a short while. You’ll have a steaming bowl of goodness to soothe your soul.

9. Teriyaki Chicken

Forget takeout, because your Instant Pot can make tender and delicious teriyaki chicken right in your own kitchen. Sauté some chicken, onions, and garlic, then add soy sauce, honey, and ginger. Pressure cook for a few minutes, and you’ll have a flavorful and sticky teriyaki chicken that pairs perfectly with steamed rice or stir-fried vegetables.

10. Chocolate Lava Cake

Yes, you read that right. Your Instant Pot can even make decadent desserts like this Chocolate Lava Cake. Mix together some cake batter, pour it into ramekins, and pressure cook for a short while. The result? A warm and gooey chocolate lava cake that’s sure to impress your guests or satisfy your sweet tooth.

So there you have it - 10 easy and delicious Instant Pot recipes for beginners. With these recipes, you’ll be able to create mouthwatering meals with minimal effort. So fire up your Instant Pot and get cooking!
