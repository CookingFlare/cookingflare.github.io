3 Ways with Cheese: Flavorsome Cheese Sauces to Spruce Up Your Pizza Game


Who would have thought that the humble cheese sauce could transform your ordinary pizza into a savory masterpiece? By experimenting with various cheeses and cooking techniques, you can elevate your pizza to new heights of culinary delight. Below, we’ve curated three irresistible cheese sauce recipes that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

1. Creamy Roasted Garlic Cheese Sauce

The comforting and rich flavor of roasted garlic gives this cheese sauce a decadent personality - perfect for a pizza topped with earthy, robust ingredients such as mushrooms, onions, and rosemary.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves roasted garlic (instructions below)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Roast the garlic:

  • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Cut the top off a head of garlic, exposing the individual cloves.
  • Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  • Wrap the garlic head in aluminum foil and roast in the oven for 40-45 minutes, until tender and lightly golden.
  • Allow garlic to cool before using.

To make the cheese sauce:

  • In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter.
  • Add the roasted garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes to release its aromatic flavors.
  • Gradually whisk in the heavy cream, bringing it to a gentle simmer.
  • Reduce heat to low and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese, a handful at a time, making sure each addition melts before adding more.
  • Season with salt and pepper; stir until smooth and velvety.
  • Serve your creamy roasted-garlic cheese sauce generously on your pizza for an irresistible blend of flavors that simply cannot be ignored.

2. Brie and Blue Cheese Sauce

For the adventurous pizza lover, this Brie and Blue Cheese Sauce is the perfect balance of sweet, creamy, and tangy. Pair it with toppings like gala apples, caramelized onions, and thinly sliced prosciutto for a gourmet experience.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1 (8-ounce) wheel of soft Brie, rind removed and cheese cubed
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme, leaves stripped (plus extra for garnish)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

To make the cheese sauce:

  • In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  • Stir in the blue cheese and cook until it starts to melt, 2-3 minutes.
  • Lower heat to low and whisk in the Brie cheese, blue cheese, and heavy cream until a smooth, creamy sauce forms.
  • Remove from heat and stir in the fresh thyme leaves.
  • Season with salt and black pepper.
  • Spread this decadent Brie and Blue Cheese Sauce on your pizza crust and bake as desired, then garnish with extra thyme leaves.

3. Tangy Gochujang Cheese Sauce

Are you a spice lover with a penchant for bold flavors? Then the spicy-savory combination of gochujang and cheese in this sauce will have your taste buds singing with delight. Perfect for a pizza topped with spicy Italian sausage, red onions, or even pineapple, this sauce will give your creation a fiery kick.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang paste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt, to taste

To make the cheese sauce:

  • In a small pan over medium heat, melt the butter.
  • Whisk in the gochujang paste, letting it cook for 1 minute to release its flavors and scent.
  • Gradually whisk in the milk until smooth.
  • Reduce heat to low and stir in the shredded mozzarella and cheddar cheese, a handful at a time. Stir continuously until smooth.
  • Season with a pinch of salt.
  • Slather the Tangy Gochujang Cheese Sauce on your pizza for a delightful explosion of flavors that helps elevate your ordinary pizza to extraordinary.

The possibilities are truly endless when it comes to experimenting with cheese sauces. These three flavorsome recipes provide a starting point for your culinary adventures, allowing you to create gourmet pizzas right at home. Now, who’s ready to pull out their pizza stone and try not one, but all three sauces? Happy cooking!
