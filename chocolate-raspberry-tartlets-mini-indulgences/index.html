Chocolate Raspberry Tartlets: Mini Indulgences


If you’re looking for a decadent dessert that’s sure to impress, look no further than these Chocolate Raspberry Tartlets. These mini indulgences are the perfect combination of rich, chocolatey goodness and the bright, tangy flavor of fresh raspberries. They make for a stunning addition to any dessert table and are guaranteed to be a hit with friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Tart Shells:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2-3 tablespoons cold water

For the Chocolate Filling:

  • 6 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the Raspberry Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup raspberry jam
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Make the Tart Shells

  1. In a food processor, combine the flour, cocoa powder, butter, and sugar. Pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Add the egg yolk and pulse again.
  3. With the processor running, add the cold water, one tablespoon at a time, until the dough comes together.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently. Shape it into a ball, then flatten it into a disk. Wrap the disk in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Roll out the chilled dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/8 inch thickness. Use a round cookie cutter to cut out circles of dough, then press the dough circles into the wells of a mini tart pan.
  7. Prick the bottoms of the tart shells with a fork. Line the shells with parchment paper and fill with pie weights or dried beans.
  8. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment paper and weights and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the shells are set. Let them cool completely.

Step 2: Prepare the Chocolate Filling

  1. Place the chopped chocolate in a heatproof bowl.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.
  3. Pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate and let it sit for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Gently whisk the mixture until the chocolate is completely melted and the ganache is smooth.
  5. Whisk in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
  6. Spoon the chocolate filling into the cooled tart shells and refrigerate while you prepare the raspberry topping.

Step 3: Add the Raspberry Topping

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the raspberry jam over low heat until it becomes more liquid and easier to spread.
  2. Arrange the fresh raspberries on top of the chocolate filling in a circular pattern.
  3. Using a pastry brush, gently brush the raspberries with the warmed raspberry jam to create a shiny glaze.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.

These Chocolate Raspberry Tartlets are best served chilled, allowing the flavors to meld together beautifully. They are a delightful treat for any occasion, from elegant dinner parties to casual gatherings with loved ones. The contrast of the velvety chocolate filling and the burst of fresh raspberry flavor is truly sensational. Plus, their petite size makes them perfect for portion control, although you may find it hard to resist having just one!

So, the next time you’re craving a sophisticated dessert that’s easy to make and utterly delicious, give these Chocolate Raspberry Tartlets a try. They’re a delightful way to treat yourself and your loved ones to something truly special.

Enjoy!
