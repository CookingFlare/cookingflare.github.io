Vegan Apple Cinnamon Rolls: A Cinnamony and Apple-Filled Breakfast


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying breakfast that will fill your home with the comforting aroma of cinnamon and apples? Look no further than these Vegan Apple Cinnamon Rolls! These fluffy, cinnamon-spiced rolls are filled with a sweet and tangy apple filling, making them the perfect way to start your day.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup almond milk, warmed
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, melted

For the apple filling:

  • 2 medium apples, peeled, cored, and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the cinnamon sugar topping:

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well.

  2. Gradually add the warmed almond milk and melted vegan butter to the dry ingredients. Stir until a soft dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  5. While the dough is rising, prepare the apple filling. In a small bowl, combine the chopped apples, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon. Mix well and set aside.

  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer it back to the floured surface and roll it out into a rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.

  7. Spread the apple filling evenly over the rolled-out dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  8. Starting from the long side, tightly roll the dough into a log. Pinch the seam to seal.

  9. Using a sharp knife, cut the rolled dough into 12 equal-sized slices. Place the slices in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each roll.

  10. Cover the baking dish with a clean kitchen towel and let it rise for another 30 minutes.

  11. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  12. In a small bowl, combine the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon for the cinnamon sugar topping.

  13. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture over the risen rolls, making sure to coat each one evenly.

  14. Bake the rolls in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  15. While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, almond milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  16. Once the rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the glaze over the warm rolls.

  17. Serve the Vegan Apple Cinnamon Rolls warm and enjoy!

Conclusion

These Vegan Apple Cinnamon Rolls are a delightful twist on the classic cinnamon roll recipe. The combination of warm cinnamon, sweet apples, and fluffy dough creates a breakfast treat that is sure to impress. Whether you’re serving them to guests or enjoying them on a lazy Sunday morning, these rolls are guaranteed to be a hit. So go ahead, indulge in these cinnamony and apple-filled delights and start your day off on a delicious note!
