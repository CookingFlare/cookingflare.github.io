Slow Cooker Burritos: Effortless and Delicious


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine and love the convenience of using a slow cooker, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful recipe for slow cooker burritos. Not only are slow cooker burritos incredibly easy to prepare, but they also result in a mouthwatering, flavorful meal that the whole family will love. Let’s dive into the details of how to create these effortless and delicious slow cooker burritos.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup of frozen corn
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups of shredded cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Sour cream and salsa, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Begin by placing the chicken thighs in the bottom of the slow cooker.

  2. Add the black beans, frozen corn, diced onion, red bell pepper, jalapeno pepper, and minced garlic to the slow cooker.

  3. In a small bowl, mix together the chili powder, cumin, paprika, and dried oregano. Sprinkle this seasoning mixture over the ingredients in the slow cooker.

  4. Pour the chicken broth over the ingredients, ensuring that everything is well combined.

  5. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

  6. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and fully cooked.

  7. Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, combining it with the other ingredients to create a flavorful filling.

  8. Warm the flour tortillas in the microwave or on a skillet.

  9. Spoon the chicken and vegetable filling onto each tortilla, then sprinkle with shredded cheese and a little fresh cilantro.

  10. Roll up the tortillas to form burritos and place them seam-side down in a baking dish.

  11. If desired, sprinkle additional cheese on top of the burritos.

  12. Bake the burritos in a preheated oven at 350°F for about 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the burritos are heated through.

  13. Serve the slow cooker burritos with a dollop of sour cream, a side of salsa, and enjoy the delicious flavors!

Tips:

  • You can customize the filling by adding other ingredients such as diced tomatoes, green chilies, or even cooked rice.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, consider adding a dash of hot sauce or extra jalapenos to the filling.
  • These slow cooker burritos make for fantastic leftovers, so don’t hesitate to make a larger batch and enjoy them for lunch the next day.

By following this simple and hassle-free recipe, you can savor the delightful flavors of slow cooker burritos without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether it’s a busy weeknight or a relaxed weekend, these burritos are sure to become a family favorite. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to indulge in these effortless and delicious slow cooker burritos!
