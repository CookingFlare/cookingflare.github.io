Satisfying Thai Drunken Noodle Chicken Stir Fry: A Spicy Favorite


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and crave a dish that will make your taste buds dance, then you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m going to share with you a recipe for Satisfying Thai Drunken Noodle Chicken Stir Fry, a spicy favorite that will transport you straight to the vibrant streets of Thailand.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of wide rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes (adjust according to your spice tolerance)
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

Now that we have our ingredients ready, let’s dive into the cooking process:

  1. Begin by cooking the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, rinse them under cold water to prevent sticking and set them aside.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sliced onion, and sauté until fragrant and golden.

  3. Next, add the sliced bell peppers, julienned carrot, and broccoli florets to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender yet still crisp.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the thinly sliced chicken breast to the other side. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink and is cooked through.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, and chili flakes. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables, tossing everything together to coat evenly.

  6. Add the cooked rice noodles to the skillet and gently toss them with the chicken and vegetables, ensuring that everything is well combined.

  7. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the noodles to soak up the sauce.

  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh basil leaves. The aroma of the basil will add an extra layer of freshness to the dish.

  9. Serve the Satisfying Thai Drunken Noodle Chicken Stir Fry hot, with lime wedges on the side. Squeeze some lime juice over the dish before devouring it, as it adds a tangy twist that balances out the spiciness.

Enjoy the Flavors of Thailand!

There you have it, a recipe for Satisfying Thai Drunken Noodle Chicken Stir Fry that will satisfy your cravings for bold and spicy flavors. This dish is not only bursting with vibrant colors but also offers a delightful combination of textures, from the chewy rice noodles to the tender chicken and crisp vegetables.

Feel free to experiment with the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the spice level to suit your taste buds. That’s the beauty of cooking; you can make it your own!

So, gather your ingredients, fire up your stove, and transport yourself to the bustling streets of Thailand with this delicious Thai Drunken Noodle Chicken Stir Fry. Your taste buds will thank you!
