Comforting Winter Creamy Chicken and Rice Casserole


Winter is the perfect season for cozy, comforting meals that warm you from the inside out. And what better way to embrace the chilly weather than with a creamy, hearty casserole? This Comforting Winter Creamy Chicken and Rice Casserole is the ultimate comfort food, combining tender chicken, fluffy rice, and a creamy sauce that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re looking for a family-friendly weeknight dinner or a dish to impress your guests, this casserole is sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the onion and garlic mixture, stirring constantly for about 1 minute to cook out the raw flour taste.

  4. Slowly whisk in the chicken broth and whole milk, ensuring there are no lumps. Continue stirring until the mixture thickens and comes to a gentle boil.

  5. Stir in the dried thyme, dried parsley, salt, and pepper. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

  6. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked shredded chicken, cooked rice, frozen peas, and diced carrots. Pour the creamy sauce over the mixture and gently fold everything together until well combined.

  7. Transfer the mixture to the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  8. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbling and the top is golden brown.

  9. Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and make it easier to handle.

  10. Serve the Comforting Winter Creamy Chicken and Rice Casserole hot, and enjoy the comforting flavors with your loved ones.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add your favorite vegetables to the casserole. Broccoli, corn, or mushrooms would make delicious additions.
  • If you prefer a different protein, such as turkey or ham, you can easily substitute it for the chicken.
  • For a cheesy twist, sprinkle grated cheddar or mozzarella cheese on top of the casserole during the last 10 minutes of baking.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

With its creamy texture, tender chicken, and comforting flavors, this Winter Creamy Chicken and Rice Casserole is the perfect dish to warm you up on a cold winter night. It’s easy to make, versatile, and sure to become a family favorite. So, grab your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a deliciously comforting meal.
