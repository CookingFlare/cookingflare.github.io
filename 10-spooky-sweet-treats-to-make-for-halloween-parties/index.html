10 Spooky Sweet Treats to Make for Halloween Parties


As the leaves turn and the air grows crisp, Halloween creeps around the corner, bringing with it a season of ghoulish delights and spooky celebrations. While costumes and decorations set the mood, it’s the sweet treats that truly steal the show at Halloween parties. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply want to indulge in some festive fun, these ten spooky sweet treats will enchant your guests and satisfy their sweet tooth. Get ready to whip up some frightfully delicious bites!

1. Witch’s Hat Cookies

These enchanting cookies are not only adorable but also easy to make. Using chocolate cookies as the base, you can create the perfect witch’s hat by combining a Hershey’s Kiss with a chocolate wafer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of chocolate wafer cookies
  • 1 package of Hershey’s Kisses
  • Orange icing or melted chocolate for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) if you plan to bake the cookies from scratch.
  2. If using store-bought cookies, skip to the next step.
  3. Place the chocolate wafers upside down on a baking sheet.
  4. Melt some chocolate and place a dollop in the center of each wafer.
  5. Stick a Hershey’s Kiss on top of the chocolate dollop.
  6. Use orange icing to add a decorative band around the base of the Kiss to resemble a witch’s hat.
  7. Let them cool and set before serving.

2. Mummy Brownies

These spooky brownies are sure to be a crowd-pleaser. The rich chocolate flavor, combined with the fun mummy decorations, makes them a perfect treat for Halloween.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of brownie mix (plus eggs, oil, and water as instructed)
  • White chocolate melts
  • Candy eyes

Instructions:

  1. Prepare and bake the brownies according to the package instructions.
  2. Once cooled, cut the brownies into rectangles.
  3. Melt the white chocolate melts in a microwave-safe bowl.
  4. Dip a fork into the melted chocolate and drizzle it over the brownies to create a mummy wrap effect.
  5. Place two candy eyes on each brownie before the chocolate sets.
  6. Allow the chocolate to harden before serving.

3. Ghostly Cupcakes

Delight your guests with these ghostly cupcakes that are both fun to make and delicious to eat. Topped with fluffy white frosting, they’ll look like a gathering of friendly spirits.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of vanilla cake mix (plus eggs, oil, and water as instructed)
  • White frosting
  • Mini chocolate chips
  • Marshmallows

Instructions:

  1. Prepare and bake your vanilla cupcakes according to the package instructions.
  2. Once cooled, frost the cupcakes with a generous amount of white frosting.
  3. Cut marshmallows in half and place them on top of the frosting to create a ghost shape.
  4. Use mini chocolate chips to create eyes on each ghost.
  5. Serve these spooky treats with a side of Halloween-themed decorations.

4. Spider Web Dip

Not exactly a sweet treat, but this spooky dip is a perfect addition to your dessert table. This fun and interactive dish will have guests coming back for more!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of black olives, sliced
  • Tortilla chips for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese and sour cream until smooth.
  2. Spread the mixture onto a serving platter.
  3. Create a spider web pattern with sour cream by drizzling it over the top and using a toothpick to pull it into a web shape.
  4. Place sliced black olives in the center as spiders.
  5. Serve with tortilla chips for dipping.

5. Pumpkin Spice Cake Pops

These delightful cake pops are a perfect blend of seasonal spices and sweetness. They’re also easy to make and perfect for sharing!

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of pumpkin spice cake mix (plus eggs, oil, and water as instructed)
  • Cream cheese frosting
  • Candy melts
  • Sprinkles or Halloween-themed decorations

Instructions:

  1. Prepare and bake the pumpkin spice cake according to the package instructions.
  2. Once cooled, crumble the cake into a large bowl and mix in cream cheese frosting until combined.
  3. Roll the mixture into small balls and place them on a baking sheet.
  4. Freeze the cake balls for about 30 minutes.
  5. Melt your candy melts and dip each cake pop stick into the candy before inserting them into the cake balls.
  6. Dip the entire cake pop into the melted candy and decorate with sprinkles.
  7. Allow them to set before serving.

6. Monster Rice Krispie Treats

These colorful and gooey treats are a classic that everyone loves. With a little creativity, you can turn them into delightful monsters!

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 package (10 oz) of marshmallows
  • 6 cups of Rice Krispies cereal
  • Food coloring (green, purple, etc.)
  • Candy eyes and other decorations

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over low heat.
  2. Add the marshmallows and stir until completely melted.
  3. Divide the mixture into separate bowls and add food coloring to each.
  4. Mix in the Rice Krispies until well coated.
  5. Press the mixture into a greased pan and let cool.
  6. Once set, cut into squares and decorate with candy eyes and other fun toppings to create your monsters.

7. Chocolate-Dipped Pretzel Broomsticks

These sweet and salty treats are a fun twist on traditional pretzels. They’re easy to make and perfect for snacking during the festivities.

Ingredients:

  • Pretzel rods
  • Milk or dark chocolate, melted
  • Mini Reese’s Cups
  • Candy corn for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Dip each pretzel rod into the melted chocolate, covering about two-thirds of the rod.
  2. Allow excess chocolate to drip off before placing them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. While the chocolate is still wet, gently press a mini Reese’s Cup onto the end of each pretzel to resemble a broomstick.
  4. Use candy corn to create the bristles by pressing them into the chocolate.
  5. Let them set before serving.

8. Graveyard Pudding Cups

These spooky pudding cups are a hit with kids and adults alike. They’re simple to assemble and offer a delightful treat that looks eerily delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boxes of instant chocolate pudding
  • 4 cups of milk
  • Crushed Oreo cookies
  • Gummy worms
  • Edible tombstone decorations

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the instant pudding according to package instructions using milk.
  2. In clear cups, layer chocolate pudding and crushed Oreo cookies to create a dirt-like effect.
  3. Top with gummy worms and place edible tombstones into the pudding.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator before serving to keep them fresh and cool.

9. Vampire Blood Jello Shots

Add a little adult flair to your Halloween party with these fun and spooky jello shots. They’re perfect for a more grown-up gathering!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cherry-flavored gelatin
  • 1 cup of boiling water
  • 1 cup of vodka
  • Gummy vampire fangs for decoration

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, dissolve the cherry-flavored gelatin in boiling water.
  2. Once dissolved, add in the vodka and stir well.
  3. Pour the mixture into shot glasses and refrigerate until set.
  4. Once firm, place gummy vampire fangs on the edges of the glasses for a creepy touch.

10. Candy Corn Bark

This no-bake treat is both visually appealing and delicious. It’s a sweet way to incorporate the iconic Halloween candy into your dessert spread.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of white chocolate melts
  • 1 cup of candy corn
  • 1 cup of chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Melt the white chocolate melts in a microwave-safe bowl, stirring until smooth.
  2. Pour the melted chocolate onto a lined baking sheet and spread it out evenly.
  3. Immediately sprinkle candy corn and chopped nuts over the melted chocolate.
  4. Refrigerate until set, then break it into pieces for serving.

Conclusion

These ten spooky sweet treats will make your Halloween party unforgettable! From witch’s hat cookies to monster rice krispie treats, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Not only will these delightful recipes impress your guests, but they will also create lasting memories of fun and laughter. So gather your ingredients, summon your inner baker, and get ready to create a Halloween celebration filled with sweetness and spookiness. Happy Haunting!
