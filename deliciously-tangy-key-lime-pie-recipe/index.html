Deliciously Tangy Key Lime Pie Recipe


Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Chill Time: 4 hours | Total Time: 4 hours and 30 minutes | Yield: 8 servings

Key Lime Pie is a classic dessert that never fails to impress. With its tangy and creamy filling, nestled in a buttery graham cracker crust, it’s the perfect balance of sweet and tart. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a refreshing treat, this Deliciously Tangy Key Lime Pie recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the filling:

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed key lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon key lime zest

For the whipped cream topping (optional):

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated with the butter.

  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie dish, forming a firm and even crust. Use the back of a spoon or a measuring cup to firmly press the crumbs into place.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until lightly golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  5. In a large bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sweetened condensed milk until well combined.

  6. Gradually add the key lime juice and zest to the egg yolk mixture, whisking continuously until smooth and creamy.

  7. Pour the filling into the cooled graham cracker crust, spreading it out evenly.

  8. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are slightly golden.

  9. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool to room temperature. Then, refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until fully chilled and set.

  10. If desired, prepare the whipped cream topping by whipping the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract together until soft peaks form. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled pie just before serving.

  11. Slice and serve your Deliciously Tangy Key Lime Pie, garnishing with extra lime zest if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try using a combination of key lime and regular lime juice for a more complex flavor profile.

  • If you can’t find key limes, regular limes can be used as a substitute. Just make sure to adjust the amount of juice and zest accordingly, as the flavor may be slightly different.

  • To make the pie even more refreshing, serve it with a dollop of lightly sweetened whipped cream and a sprig of fresh mint.

  • For a gluten-free version, simply substitute gluten-free graham cracker crumbs for the regular ones. The taste and texture will remain just as delicious!

  • This Key Lime Pie can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Just make sure to cover it tightly with plastic wrap to prevent any odors from seeping in.

Conclusion

Indulge in the zesty and creamy goodness of this Deliciously Tangy Key Lime Pie. With its simple yet sensational flavors, it’s the perfect dessert for any occasion. Whether you’re a fan of citrusy treats or just looking to satisfy your sweet tooth, this recipe is a must-try. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and create a slice of tangy perfection that will leave everyone craving for more. Enjoy!
