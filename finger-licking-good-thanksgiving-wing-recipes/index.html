Finger-Licking Good Thanksgiving Wing Recipes


Thanksgiving is a time for family, gratitude, and of course, delicious food. While the traditional turkey takes center stage on many tables, why not shake things up this year with some mouthwatering Thanksgiving wing recipes? Whether you’re hosting a small gathering or a larger feast, these finger-licking good wing recipes are sure to impress your guests and add a flavorful twist to your holiday spread.

Crispy Garlic Parmesan Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the grated Parmesan, minced garlic, melted butter, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pat the chicken wings dry with a paper towel, then toss them in the Parmesan mixture until fully coated.
  4. Place the wings on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until they are golden and crispy.
  5. Serve hot and watch them disappear in minutes!

These crispy garlic Parmesan wings are a delightful mix of savory Parmesan, aromatic garlic, and crispy chicken. They’re perfect for Thanksgiving appetizers or as a flavorful addition to your main course.

Sticky Honey Sriracha Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup Sriracha sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the honey, Sriracha sauce, soy sauce, minced garlic, and rice vinegar.
  3. Pat the chicken wings dry with a paper towel, then toss them in the honey Sriracha mixture until well coated.
  4. Place the wings on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 40-45 minutes, turning them halfway through and brushing with the remaining sauce.
  5. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and green onions before serving.

These sticky honey Sriracha wings strike the perfect balance between sweet and spicy, making them a crowd-pleaser for any Thanksgiving gathering.

Smoky Maple Bacon Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the maple syrup, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss the chicken wings in the maple syrup mixture until coated, then let them marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. Grill the wings for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are caramelized and cooked through.
  5. Sprinkle the cooked bacon crumbles over the wings before serving.

These smoky maple bacon wings are a delightful combination of sweet maple, smoky bacon, and juicy chicken, making them a standout dish for your Thanksgiving feast.

Conclusion

This Thanksgiving, elevate your culinary game with these irresistible wing recipes that are bound to impress your guests. Whether you’re a fan of savory Parmesan, sticky honey Sriracha, or smoky maple bacon, there’s a wing recipe here to satisfy every palate. So, roll up your sleeves, get cooking, and prepare for a Thanksgiving feast that’s sure to be remembered for years to come. Happy cooking and happy Thanksgiving!
