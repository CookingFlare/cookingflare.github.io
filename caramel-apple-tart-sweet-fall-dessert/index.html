Caramel Apple Tart: Sweet Fall Dessert


Autumn is the perfect time for reviving old memories, spending quality time with family, and indulging in delicious desserts. One such dessert that brings a nostalgic feeling with each bite is the Caramel Apple Tart. This scrumptious treat is an exquisite combination of apples and caramel, embodying the essence of the fall season. So, why not try your hand at it this season?

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons ice water
  • 6 medium apples, sliced and peeled
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon chopped candied ginger (optional)

Instructions

Let’s embark on this delightful journey of creating a Caramel Apple Tart. Please follow the steps below:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Begin by preparing your oven for the perfect baking temperature.

  2. Prepare the crust: Mix together 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup unsalted butter, 2 tablespoons granulated sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon salt in a large bowl. Incorporate 3 tablespoons ice water into the mixture, combining it thoroughly.

  3. Form the tart shell: Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least an hour. Once cooled and firm, unwrap, and roll it onto your flattened rolling pin. Gently lift the dough, positioning it over a 9-inch fluted tart pan. Carefully press the edges of the dough evenly into the tart pan.

  4. Par-bake the crust: Place the tart pan on a baking sheet and fill it withpie weights or uncooked rice to prevent the crust from shrinking during baking. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15 minutes. After the 15 minutes, remove the pie weights and bake for an additional 7-10 minutes to get a golden brown crust. Allow your tart shell to cool completely.

  5. Prepare the filling: While your tart shell cools, prepare the apples and caramel filling. Mix together 1 cup packed light brown sugar, 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 cup unsalted butter in a medium bowl. Gently stir in 6 medium apples, peeled and thinly sliced.

  6. Assemble the tart: Once your tart shell has cooled, add the caramel apple filling. Drizzle 1 tablespoon heavy cream over the filling, and optionally sprinkle chopped candied ginger on top.

  7. Finish baking and cooling: Place the filled tart back in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for 30-35 minutes. The tart is ready when the apples are tender, and the caramel fills are golden brown. Allow the tart to cool for at least 45 minutes before slicing and serving to capture the desired texture.

That’s it! Enjoy your homemade Caramel Apple Tart, shared with loved ones, and let the autumn spirit wash over you. This dessert is not just about the taste but the cherished memories created while baking.

Make sure to store any leftovers covered in the refrigerator. Bon Appétit!
