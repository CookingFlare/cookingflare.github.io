Tasty Grilled Tuna Nicoise Salad: A Fresh and Healthy Dish


As the weather starts to warm up, our taste buds begin to crave lighter and fresher dishes. One of the most popular and refreshing salads during the summer is the Nicoise salad, which originated from Nice, France. This salad is a symphony of flavors and textures, featuring grilled tuna, cherry tomatoes, and a variety of greens. In this recipe, we will add a twist to the classic Nicoise salad by incorporating grilled tuna, creating a dish that is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 4 tuna steaks (6 oz each)
  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into 1/2-inch thick rounds
  • 2 large red bell peppers, sliced into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 large zucchinis, sliced into 1/2-inch thick rounds
  • 1 large red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups mixed greens (arugula, spinach, and lettuce)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup canned black olives, pitted
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Grill the tuna: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Season the tuna steaks with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Grill the tuna for 3-4 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. Remove the tuna from the grill and set it aside to rest.
  2. Grill the vegetables: Brush the eggplant, bell peppers, and zucchinis with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Grill the vegetables for 3-4 minutes per side, or until they are tender and lightly charred. Remove the vegetables from the grill and set them aside with the tuna.
  3. Prepare the salad: In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, black olives, and feta cheese.
  4. Assemble the salad: Slice the grilled tuna into thin strips and add it to the salad bowl. Top the salad with the grilled eggplant, bell peppers, and zucchinis. Sprinkle the salad with chopped parsley and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  5. Serve and enjoy: Serve the salad immediately, garnished with a lemon wedge and a side of crusty bread if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a more intense flavor, marinate the tuna in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, and herbs such as thyme and rosemary for at least 30 minutes before grilling.
  • Substitute the feta cheese with goat cheese or ricotta cheese for a different flavor profile.
  • Add some crunch to the salad by topping it with toasted almonds or pistachios.
  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the tuna with grilled portobello mushrooms or eggplant.

Conclusion

The Tasty Grilled Tuna Nicoise Salad is a perfect dish for a light and refreshing lunch or dinner. The combination of grilled tuna, cherry tomatoes, and mixed greens creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave you craving for more. With its simplicity and versatility, this salad is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a summer barbecue or a quick weeknight dinner. So go ahead, give it a try, and experience the taste of the Mediterranean!
