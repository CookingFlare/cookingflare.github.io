Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir-Fry: A Flavorful Pork Dish


When it comes to bold flavors, few dishes can compete with Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir-Fry. This aromatic and vibrant dish, known in Thailand as “Pad Krapow Moo,” showcases the perfect balance of spicy, savory, and slightly sweet notes. With its tantalizing combination of fresh ingredients and herbs, this stir-fry is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a quick and easy meal to whip up any night of the week.

A Brief History of Thai Basil Pork

Thai cuisine is celebrated for its incredible diversity, and the use of fresh herbs and spices is at the heart of its appeal. The star ingredient in this dish is Thai basil, which differs from the sweet basil commonly found in Western cooking. Thai basil, with its distinct anise-like flavor and slightly peppery notes, is a staple in many Thai recipes. It adds a unique depth to the dish that is both fragrant and flavorful.

Pork is a preferred protein in many Thai dishes, and its ability to absorb flavors makes it an ideal choice for stir-frying. The combination of ground pork, Thai basil, and a medley of sauces creates a harmonious dish that is both satisfying and comforting.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make a delicious Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir-Fry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

For the Pork Stir-Fry

  • 1 pound (450g) ground pork
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves (fresh)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (or any neutral oil)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2-3 Thai bird’s eye chilies (sliced, adjust to taste)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 small onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice (freshly squeezed)

For Serving

  • Cooked jasmine rice (or your preferred rice)
  • Lime wedges (for garnish)
  • Sliced cucumbers (optional)

Preparation Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Before you start cooking, it’s important to prep all your ingredients. This dish comes together quickly, so having everything ready will ensure a smooth cooking process.

  • Chop the vegetables: Slice the bell pepper, onion, and chilies. Mince the garlic and set aside.
  • Wash the Thai basil: Rinse the basil leaves under cold water and pat them dry with a kitchen towel.

Step 2: Make the Stir-Fry Sauce

In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Mix well until the sugar is dissolved. This sauce will be the foundation of the dish, providing a rich umami flavor and a touch of sweetness.

Step 3: Cook the Pork

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sliced chilies, stirring for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Increase the heat to high and add the ground pork. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, breaking it apart with a spatula until it’s browned and cooked through.
  4. Once the pork is ready, add the sliced onion and bell pepper. Stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

Step 4: Add the Sauce and Basil

Pour the prepared stir-fry sauce over the cooked pork and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly.

  • Add the Thai basil: Once the pork is well-coated in sauce, add the fresh Thai basil leaves and lime juice. Stir-fry for another minute until the basil wilts and becomes fragrant.

Step 5: Serve

Remove the pan from the heat and serve the Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir-Fry over a bed of jasmine rice. Garnish with lime wedges and sliced cucumbers for a refreshing contrast to the dish’s bold flavors.

Tips for Perfecting Your Stir-Fry

  • Use fresh ingredients: The quality of your ingredients will significantly impact the taste of the dish. Fresh Thai basil and quality pork will elevate your stir-fry.
  • Adjust the spice level: If you’re sensitive to spice, reduce the number of bird’s eye chilies or remove the seeds before slicing.
  • Experiment with vegetables: Feel free to add other vegetables like green beans, carrots, or snap peas for extra nutrition and color.
  • Make it your own: This recipe is versatile! You can substitute ground pork with chicken, beef, or tofu for a vegetarian option.

Nutritional Benefits of Thai Basil Pork Stir-Fry

Not only is Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir-Fry delicious, but it also offers several nutritional benefits:

  • High in protein: Ground pork is a great source of protein, essential for muscle growth and repair.
  • Rich in vitamins: Thai basil is loaded with vitamins A, C, and K, which are important for immune function, skin health, and bone health.
  • Low in carbs: When served with vegetables and rice, this dish can be a satisfying low-carb option if you opt for cauliflower rice.

Conclusion

Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir-Fry is a culinary delight that brings the vibrant flavors of Thailand to your kitchen. Its simple preparation and bold taste make it an ideal dish for busy weeknights or when you want to impress guests with your cooking skills. With the combination of fresh ingredients, aromatic herbs, and savory sauces, this dish is sure to become a staple in your home.

Next time you crave something spicy and flavorful, try making this Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir-Fry. Your taste buds will thank you! Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a kitchen novice, this dish is sure to satisfy and impress. Enjoy the journey of cooking and the delightful flavors that come with it!
