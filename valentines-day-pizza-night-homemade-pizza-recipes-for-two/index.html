Valentine's Day Pizza Night: Homemade Pizza Recipes for Two


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a cozy pizza night at home? Skip the crowded restaurants and long waits this year, and instead, treat your loved one to a delicious homemade pizza made with love. In this blog post, we’ll share three mouthwatering pizza recipes that are perfect for a romantic evening for two.

1. Classic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 store-bought pizza dough or homemade dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Arrange the mozzarella slices on top of the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle some torn basil leaves over the cheese.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza, and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

2. BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 store-bought pizza dough or homemade dough
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread the barbecue sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Scatter the shredded chicken and sliced red onion over the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves, slice, and serve.

3. Pear and Gorgonzola Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 store-bought pizza dough or homemade dough
  • 1 ripe pear, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled gorgonzola cheese
  • Handful of arugula
  • Balsamic glaze (store-bought or homemade)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Arrange the pear slices evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled gorgonzola cheese on top of the pears.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Top with a handful of fresh arugula and drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  10. Slice and serve.

Conclusion

There you have it - three delicious homemade pizza recipes that are perfect for a cozy Valentine’s Day pizza night at home. Whether you prefer the classic Margherita, the tangy BBQ chicken, or the unique combination of pear and gorgonzola, these pizzas are sure to impress your loved one. So put on some romantic music, open a bottle of wine, and enjoy a memorable evening together with these mouthwatering creations. Happy Valentine’s Day!
