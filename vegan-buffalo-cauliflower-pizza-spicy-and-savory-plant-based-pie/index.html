Vegan Buffalo Cauliflower Pizza: Spicy and Savory Plant-Based Pie


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying pizza but want to opt for a plant-based option? Look no further than this Vegan Buffalo Cauliflower Pizza recipe. Packed with bold flavors and a spicy kick, this plant-based pie will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

For the cauliflower crust:

  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 flax eggs (2 tablespoons ground flaxseed mixed with 6 tablespoons water)

For the buffalo sauce:

  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup

For the toppings:

  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. Start by making the cauliflower crust. Cut the cauliflower into florets and pulse in a food processor until it resembles rice. Transfer the cauliflower rice to a microwave-safe bowl and cook on high for 5 minutes. Allow it to cool slightly before proceeding.

  3. Place the cooked cauliflower rice in a clean kitchen towel or cheesecloth and wring out as much moisture as possible. This step is crucial to achieving a crispy crust.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the wrung cauliflower rice, almond flour, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and flax eggs. Mix until well combined.

  5. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and transfer the cauliflower mixture onto it. Spread it out evenly, forming a round pizza crust shape. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and crispy.

  6. While the crust is baking, prepare the buffalo sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the hot sauce, melted vegan butter, and maple syrup until well combined.

  7. Once the crust is ready, remove it from the oven and brush the buffalo sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the vegan mozzarella cheese, diced red onion, and diced celery on top.

  8. Return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and garnish with fresh cilantro. Allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

  10. Enjoy your Vegan Buffalo Cauliflower Pizza while it’s still warm and the flavors are at their peak. Serve it as a main dish or alongside a crisp salad for a complete meal.

This Vegan Buffalo Cauliflower Pizza is a great way to satisfy your pizza cravings while enjoying a plant-based meal. The cauliflower crust provides a deliciously crispy base, while the buffalo sauce adds a spicy and tangy flavor. Top it off with vegan mozzarella, red onion, and celery for an extra kick of freshness. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is a winner. Give it a try and let your taste buds be amazed!
