Tantalizing Tarragon: Recipes for Culinary Excellence

Tarragon, with its unique flavor profile that’s reminiscent of anise and a hint of sweetness, is a versatile herb that can elevate the taste of various dishes. Its delicate, slightly bittersweet taste makes it a popular choice in French cuisine, but its uses extend far beyond that. In this blog post, we’ll explore some tantalizing tarragon recipes that showcase the culinary excellence of this remarkable herb. Tarragon Chicken Salad Ingredients 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1/2 cup mayonnaise 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh tarragon 1/4 cup finely chopped celery 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Poach the chicken breasts in simmering water until cooked through. Let them cool, then chop into bite-sized pieces. In a bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, tarragon, celery, and red onion. Mix well. Add the chopped chicken to the bowl and toss to coat with the dressing. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the tarragon chicken salad on a bed of fresh greens or as a sandwich filling.
