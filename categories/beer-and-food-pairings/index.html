Crispy Zucchini Fries with a Citrusy Lager Ingredients: 2 medium zucchinis 1 cup breadcrumbs 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1 teaspoon garlic powder Salt and pepper to taste Cooking spray Instructions: Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Cut the zucchinis into fry-shaped strips. In a bowl, mix breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Dip each zucchini strip into the breadcrumb mixture, ensuring it’s coated evenly. Place the coated zucchinis on the baking sheet and spray them with cooking spray. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the fries are crispy and golden brown. Pair these crispy zucchini fries with a citrusy lager to refresh your palate.
