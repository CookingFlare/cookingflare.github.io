Simple and Easy No-Bake Peanut Butter Bars


If you’re a fan of peanut butter and looking for a quick and delicious treat, then you’re in the right place. These no-bake peanut butter bars are a simple and satisfying snack that requires minimal effort and time. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, need a lunchbox addition, or simply craving a sweet and nutty indulgence, these bars are the perfect solution. Let’s dive into the easy-to-follow recipe and indulge in the delightful taste of these no-bake peanut butter bars.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (240g) creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) honey
  • 1/2 cup (120g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups (200g) graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 1/2 cups (180g) confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 cup (180g) semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) creamy peanut butter (for the chocolate topping)

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Base

In a large mixing bowl, combine the creamy peanut butter, honey, and melted unsalted butter. Stir the mixture until it’s well combined and smooth.

Step 2: Add Graham Cracker Crumbs and Sugar

Once the peanut butter mixture is smooth, add the graham cracker crumbs and confectioners’ sugar. Mix everything together until the ingredients are fully incorporated, forming a thick and slightly crumbly dough.

Step 3: Press into Pan

Take a 9x9 inch (23x23cm) square baking pan and line it with parchment paper, leaving some overhang on the sides for easy removal. Transfer the peanut butter mixture into the prepared pan and press it down firmly and evenly using the back of a spoon or your hands.

Step 4: Prepare the Chocolate Topping

In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the semisweet chocolate chips and creamy peanut butter. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the mixture is melted and smooth.

Step 5: Spread the Chocolate Topping

Pour the melted chocolate mixture over the pressed peanut butter base in the pan. Use a spatula to spread the chocolate evenly over the top, covering the entire surface of the peanut butter base.

Step 6: Chill and Serve

Place the pan in the refrigerator and allow the bars to chill for at least 2 hours, or until the chocolate topping is set. Once set, use the overhanging parchment paper to lift the bars out of the pan. Place them on a cutting board and slice into desired sizes.

Enjoy Your No-Bake Peanut Butter Bars!

These simple and easy no-bake peanut butter bars are a delightful treat for any occasion. The creamy peanut butter base combined with the rich chocolate topping creates a flavor combination that’s sure to please any peanut butter lover. Whether you’re making these bars for yourself, your family, or a gathering with friends, they are guaranteed to be a hit. Plus, the fact that they require no baking makes them a convenient option for satisfying your sweet tooth without spending hours in the kitchen. So, whip up a batch of these delicious bars and enjoy the delightful taste of peanut butter and chocolate in every bite!
