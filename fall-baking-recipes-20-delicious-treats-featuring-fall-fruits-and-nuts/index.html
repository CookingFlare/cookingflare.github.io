Fall Baking Recipes: 20 Delicious Treats Featuring Fall Fruits and Nuts


As the leaves turn vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow, and the air becomes crisp and cool, it’s the perfect time to indulge in some fall baking. Fall fruits and nuts are at their peak during this season, offering a wide range of flavors and textures that can elevate your baked treats to a whole new level. In this blog post, we’ve curated a collection of 20 delicious fall baking recipes that showcase the best of these autumnal ingredients. From apple and pear desserts to pecan and walnut delights, these treats will surely satisfy your sweet tooth and bring warmth to your kitchen. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Apple Cinnamon Muffins

Start your fall baking journey with these moist and flavorful apple cinnamon muffins. Bursting with chunks of juicy apples and infused with aromatic cinnamon, these muffins are the epitome of fall comfort.

2. Pumpkin Spice Bread

Nothing says fall quite like pumpkin spice. This easy-to-make pumpkin spice bread is a crowd-pleaser, with its tender crumb and fragrant blend of cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves.

3. Pear and Almond Tart

Indulge in the delicate sweetness of pears with this elegant pear and almond tart. The buttery crust, luscious almond cream, and juicy slices of ripe pear create a harmonious symphony of flavors.

4. Walnut Brownies

Take your brownies to the next level by incorporating crunchy walnuts. These walnut brownies strike the perfect balance between fudgy and chewy, with the added nuttiness of the walnuts adding a delightful texture.

5. Caramel Apple Crisp

Celebrate the flavors of fall with this classic caramel apple crisp. The combination of tart apples, gooey caramel, and a buttery crumble topping is simply irresistible.

6. Maple Pecan Pie

A staple of fall baking, pecan pie gets a twist with the addition of maple syrup. This rich and indulgent dessert features a buttery crust filled with a sweet and nutty pecan filling, enhanced by the deep flavors of maple.

7. Fig and Pistachio Biscotti

For a sophisticated treat, try these fig and pistachio biscotti. The crunchy biscotti are studded with dried figs and pistachios, creating a delightful contrast of textures and flavors.

8. Cranberry Orange Scones

Tangy cranberries and zesty oranges come together in these delightful cranberry orange scones. Perfect for a cozy breakfast or afternoon tea, these scones are tender, flaky, and bursting with fruity goodness.

9. Spiced Apple Cake

Warm spices like cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg infuse this spiced apple cake with the essence of fall. With its moist crumb and layers of tender apples, it’s a cake that will keep you coming back for more.

10. Hazelnut Linzer Cookies

These hazelnut linzer cookies are a treat for both the eyes and the taste buds. The buttery cookies sandwich a layer of raspberry jam, while the toasted hazelnuts add a delightful crunch.

11. Butternut Squash Bread Pudding

Utilize the flavors of fall’s favorite squash in this savory and comforting butternut squash bread pudding. Creamy butternut squash, crusty bread, and a hint of sage make this dish a perfect side for any fall feast.

12. Chestnut Chocolate Cake

Indulge in the rich and nutty flavors of chestnuts with this decadent chestnut chocolate cake. The moist chocolate cake layers are complemented by a velvety chestnut frosting, creating a dessert that is pure indulgence.

13. Cinnamon Roasted Pear Galette

Wrap slices of cinnamon-roasted pears in a rustic galette crust, and you have a simple yet stunning fall dessert. The caramelized pears and buttery crust make this galette a delightful treat.

14. Sweet Potato Pecan Bars

Combine the earthy sweetness of sweet potatoes with the crunch of pecans in these delicious sweet potato pecan bars. The creamy sweet potato filling and the buttery pecan streusel create a heavenly combination.

15. Apple Cider Doughnuts

No fall baking list is complete without apple cider doughnuts. These fluffy and moist doughnuts are infused with apple cider and coated in a cinnamon sugar blend, making them the perfect treat to enjoy with a cup of hot cider.

16. Maple Walnut Cupcakes

Maple and walnuts are a match made in fall heaven, and these maple walnut cupcakes celebrate that perfect pairing. The cupcakes are moist and tender, topped with a creamy maple frosting and a sprinkling of crunchy walnuts.

17. Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars

Give your classic cheesecake a fall twist with these luscious pumpkin cheesecake bars. The creamy pumpkin filling, spiced with cinnamon and nutmeg, sits atop a buttery graham cracker crust, creating a dessert that is both creamy and comforting.

18. Caramel Pecan Rolls

Indulge in gooey caramel and crunchy pecans with these irresistible caramel pecan rolls. The soft and fluffy rolls are swirled with a caramel pecan filling and topped with a generous drizzle of caramel sauce.

19. Ginger Pear Upside-Down Cake

Combining the warmth of ginger with the sweetness of pears, this ginger pear upside-down cake is a delightful treat. The caramelized pears and the spiced ginger cake create a perfect balance of flavors.

20. Cranberry Walnut Bread

End your fall baking journey with this cranberry walnut bread. Packed with tart cranberries and crunchy walnuts, this bread is perfect for toasting and slathering with butter for a cozy breakfast or afternoon snack.

Fall is a season that brings us an abundance of delicious fruits and nuts, and these 20 fall baking recipes allow you to fully embrace the flavors of the season. From apple and pear desserts to pecan and walnut delights, these treats will fill your home with delightful aromas and satisfy your cravings for all things fall. So, put on your apron, preheat the oven, and let the baking begin!
