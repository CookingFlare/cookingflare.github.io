Hearty Dairy-Free Lentil Shepherd's Pie Recipe


Are you looking for a hearty and wholesome dish that’s both comforting and nutritious? Look no further than this delightful Dairy-Free Lentil Shepherd’s Pie. Packed with protein-rich lentils, colorful vegetables, and flavorful herbs, this recipe is a vegan twist on the classic shepherd’s pie that will satisfy even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients

For the Lentil Filling:

  • 1 cup of brown lentils, rinsed
  • 3 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Mashed Potato Topping:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons of dairy-free margarine
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Lentil Filling

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed lentils and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender but not mushy. Drain any excess liquid and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté for 3-4 minutes until translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the diced carrots and celery to the skillet, and cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in the cooked lentils, frozen peas, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 2: Make the Mashed Potato Topping

  1. Place the peeled and cubed potatoes in a large pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are fork-tender.
  2. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Add the almond milk, dairy-free margarine, salt, and pepper. Mash the potatoes until smooth and creamy. Adjust seasoning to taste.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Transfer the lentil filling to a large oven-safe dish and spread it out evenly.
  3. Spoon the mashed potato topping over the lentil filling, spreading it out with a spatula.
  4. Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the mashed potato topping is golden and the filling is bubbling around the edges.
  5. Remove the shepherd’s pie from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestion

This Dairy-Free Lentil Shepherd’s Pie pairs beautifully with a crisp green salad or steamed vegetables. It’s a perfect dish for cozy family dinners, potlucks, or meal prepping for the week ahead. The leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days and reheated for a quick and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

This Dairy-Free Lentil Shepherd’s Pie is a wholesome and satisfying dish that’s sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Whether you follow a plant-based diet or simply enjoy exploring new culinary horizons, this recipe offers a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will leave you craving more. Give it a try and savor the heartiness of this dairy-free twist on a beloved classic!
