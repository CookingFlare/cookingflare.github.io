BBQ Chickpea Wraps: A Vegan BBQ Option


There’s something incredibly satisfying about a good BBQ. The smoky scent that drifts through the air, the sound of sizzling meat, and the tasty grilled vegetables. But what if you’re looking for a vegan alternative to traditional BBQ? Look no further than the BBQ chickpea wraps – a delicious, protein-packed option that will leave your taste buds dancing!

Chickpeas are a staple in vegan cuisine and have an adaptability that makes them perfect for grilling. With a texture that easily absorbs flavors, chickpeas are the ideal ingredient to vamp up your BBQ experience. Not only do they have a mild, nutty flavor that’s easy to work with, they’re also a great source of plant-based protein.

First Things First: Prepping the Chickpeas

Your journey to a perfect BBQ chickpea wrap starts with prepping your tasty chickpeas. Drain a can of chickpeas, rinse them well and pat dry. This step is crucial as it ensures the chickpeas hold their shape on the grill.

For a simple marinade, mix together 2 tablespoons of olive oil, the juice of half a lemon, 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, and a dash of smoked paprika. Add salt and pepper to taste. Pour the marinade over the chickpeas, cover, and let them sit for at least an hour. This process allows the flavors to really sink into the chickpeas, ensuring they’re flavorful and tender on the inside.

Grilling the Chickpeas

Preheat your grill, then thread the chickpeas onto skewers. Place the skewers on the grill and let them cook for about 5 minutes, turning frequently until they’re tender and slightly charred. Be cautious not to overcook the chickpeas, as they might become too hard and lose their appealing texture.

Preparing the Wraps

While your chickpeas are grilling, prepare your wraps. Warm up your choice of vegan flatbreads on the grill, and spread a layer of dairy-free cream cheese.

Creating Your Wrap

Off the skewers and onto the wrap. Place the grilled chickpeas right on top of the cream cheese. Load up your wrap with an assortment of toppings: fresh greens, avocado slices, cucumber, sour kraut or sautéed mushrooms - the options are endless!

Although everyone has unique preferences when it comes to BBQ chickpea wraps, this basic guide should be a starting point. The best part about these vegan BBQ wraps is their adaptability. Feel free to experiment with your own marinade, add your preferred veggies and enjoy the smoky flavors of this plant-based alternative that the grill has to offer.

Bring life to your next BBQ with these tasty BBQ chickpea wraps, and your vegan and non-vegan friends alike will be clamoring for more. So, get cooking!

Remember, regardless if you’re a full-time vegetarian or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your lifestyle, BBQ chickpea wraps are a fantastic place to start. Not only are they impressive, but they’re also protein-rich and customizable to your liking.sofar, get creative and have a great time grilling. Happy cookin'!
