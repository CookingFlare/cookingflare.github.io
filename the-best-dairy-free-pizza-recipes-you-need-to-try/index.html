The Best Dairy-Free Pizza Recipes You Need to Try


Pizza is a beloved dish worldwide, with its gooey cheese and flavorful toppings stealing hearts at every gathering. But what if you’re lactose intolerant, vegan, or simply looking to cut back on dairy? Fear not! There’s a universe of delicious dairy-free pizza options just waiting to be explored. In this post, we will dive into some of the best dairy-free pizza recipes that are not just alternatives but standouts in their own right. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with these creative and mouthwatering dairy-free pizzas.

Why Choose Dairy-Free?

Before we jump into the recipes, let’s discuss the benefits of going dairy-free. Many people are opting for dairy-free diets for various reasons, including lactose intolerance, veganism, or simply a desire to eat healthier. Dairy-free options can be lower in calories and saturated fats while still providing a satisfying meal. Additionally, there are numerous plant-based cheeses and toppings available that can bring richness and flavor to your pizza without the dairy.

1. Classic Dairy-Free Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 3/4 cup warm water
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • For the Toppings:

    • 1/2 cup dairy-free pizza sauce
    • 1 cup dairy-free mozzarella cheese (store-bought or homemade)
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Olive oil for drizzling
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Make the Dough: In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt. Gradually add warm water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms. Knead for about 5 minutes until smooth. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes in a warm place.

  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.

  3. Shape the Pizza: Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer it to a pizza peel or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  4. Add the Sauce and Cheese: Spread the dairy-free pizza sauce evenly over the crust. Sprinkle with dairy-free mozzarella cheese and season with salt and pepper.

  5. Bake: If using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the pizza onto the stone. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Slice and serve hot!

2. Dairy-Free Pesto Veggie Pizza

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:

    • 2 cups whole wheat flour
    • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 3/4 cup warm water
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • For the Toppings:

    • 1/2 cup dairy-free pesto (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
    • 1 cup spinach
    • 1/2 cup dairy-free feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a bowl, mix whole wheat flour, instant yeast, and salt. Gradually add warm water and olive oil, kneading until smooth. Allow the dough to rise for 40 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape the Pizza: Roll out the dough to your preferred thickness and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

  4. Add the Pesto and Veggies: Spread the dairy-free pesto over the crust. Top with cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, spinach, and sprinkle dairy-free feta cheese.

  5. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and toppings are tender.

  6. Slice and Serve: Allow to cool slightly before slicing. Enjoy your colorful veggie pizza!

3. Spicy BBQ Chickpea Pizza

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:

    • 1 1/2 cups gluten-free flour (or all-purpose)
    • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) yeast
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 3/4 cup warm water

  • For the Toppings:

    • 1/2 cup dairy-free BBQ sauce
    • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
    • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
    • 1 cup dairy-free mozzarella cheese
    • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Make the Dough: Combine the gluten-free flour, yeast, garlic powder, and salt in a bowl. Gradually add warm water and olive oil to form a dough. Let rise for 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Oven: Set your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape the Pizza: Roll out the dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.

  4. Add BBQ Sauce and Toppings: Spread the dairy-free BBQ sauce over the crust. Top with chickpeas, red onion, and dairy-free mozzarella cheese.

  5. Bake: Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle with fresh cilantro before serving.

4. Mediterranean Hummus Pizza

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 3/4 cup warm water
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • For the Toppings:

    • 1/2 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered
    • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
    • 1/2 cucumber, diced
    • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: Combine flour, yeast, and salt in a bowl. Add warm water and olive oil, kneading until smooth. Let rise for 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape the Pizza: Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

  4. Add Hummus and Toppings: Spread hummus over the crust. Top with cherry tomatoes, olives, and cucumber.

  5. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden.

  6. Garnish and Enjoy: Garnish with fresh parsley before slicing.

5. Loaded Dairy-Free Nacho Pizza

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 3/4 cup warm water
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • For the Toppings:

    • 1/2 cup dairy-free nacho cheese (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1 cup black beans, drained
    • 1 cup corn
    • 1/2 jalapeño, sliced
    • Fresh cilantro for garnish
    • Salsa for serving

Instructions

  1. Make the Dough: Combine flour, yeast, and salt in a bowl. Add warm water and olive oil to form a dough. Let rise for 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape the Pizza: Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

  4. Add Nacho Cheese and Toppings: Spread dairy-free nacho cheese over the crust. Top with black beans, corn, and jalapeño slices.

  5. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with salsa.

Conclusion

Dairy-free pizza doesn’t mean sacrificing flavor or satisfaction. With these delightful recipes, you can enjoy the classic comfort of pizza while embracing a dairy-free lifestyle. Whether you prefer classic Margherita, veggie-loaded options, or spicy BBQ, there’s a perfect dairy-free pizza for everyone. So gather your ingredients, invite some friends over, and make pizza night an unforgettable experience!

Feel free to experiment with different sauces, toppings, and cheeses to create your own signature dairy-free pizza. The possibilities are endless, and the results are always delicious. Happy pizza making!
