Wholesome Roasted Butternut Squash Soup: A Warming Starter


There’s nothing quite like a bowl of warm and comforting soup to soothe the soul, especially during the chilly autumn and winter months. And when it comes to wholesome and delicious soups, few can compare to the richness and flavor of a Roasted Butternut Squash Soup. This velvety smooth soup is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse, packed with essential vitamins and minerals. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure as we delve into the world of this hearty and nourishing soup.

Ingredients

To make this Wholesome Roasted Butternut Squash Soup, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 large butternut squash, halved and seeds removed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1 apple, peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the halved butternut squash on a baking sheet, cut side up. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the oven for about 45 minutes, or until the squash is tender and caramelized.

  2. While the squash is roasting, heat a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  3. Add the chopped carrot and apple to the pot, and cook for an additional 5 minutes, until they begin to soften.

  4. Once the roasted butternut squash is ready, scoop out the flesh and add it to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Pour in the vegetable broth, ensuring that the squash and other ingredients are fully submerged. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Now it’s time to add the spices. Sprinkle in the ground cumin, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg. Stir well to distribute the flavors evenly.

  7. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth and velvety in texture. If using a regular blender, be sure to let the soup cool slightly before blending and blend in batches if necessary.

  8. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper, as desired.

  9. Serve the Wholesome Roasted Butternut Squash Soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.
