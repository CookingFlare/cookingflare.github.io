Buttery Garlic Herb Baked Scallops - Simply Divine


If you’re a seafood lover, you’re in for a treat with this exquisite recipe for Buttery Garlic Herb Baked Scallops. This dish is a celebration of flavors and textures that will elevate any meal to a whole new level. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special homemade meal, these scallops are guaranteed to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh sea scallops
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, sliced for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the fresh sea scallops in a single layer in a baking dish.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and thyme leaves. Drizzle the herb butter mixture over the scallops, ensuring that each one is generously coated.

  3. In a separate bowl, mix the breadcrumbs with a pinch of salt and pepper. Sprinkle the breadcrumb mixture evenly over the scallops, adding a delightful crunch to the dish.

  4. Place the lemon slices on top of the scallops for a burst of citrus flavor and a visually stunning presentation.

  5. Bake the scallops in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are opaque and firm to the touch. Be careful not to overcook them, as scallops can become tough if left in the oven for too long.

  6. Once baked to perfection, remove the scallops from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to meld together, creating a truly harmonious dish.

  7. Serve the Buttery Garlic Herb Baked Scallops hot, garnished with additional fresh herbs and lemon wedges if desired. The aroma alone will entice your guests, and the taste will leave them craving for more.

Why Buttery Garlic Herb Baked Scallops?

This recipe is a testament to the delicate balance of flavors and textures. The richness of the butter, the aroma of the garlic, and the freshness of the herbs all come together to create a dish that is nothing short of divine. The gentle baking process allows the scallops to retain their natural sweetness while absorbing the savory essence of the herb butter.

Not only is this dish a sensory delight, but it also offers a myriad of health benefits. Scallops are an excellent source of lean protein, low in fat, and high in essential nutrients such as vitamin B12 and omega-3 fatty acids. By choosing to bake the scallops rather than frying them, you can indulge in a luxurious meal without compromising on nutrition.

Pairing Suggestions:

To complement the Buttery Garlic Herb Baked Scallops, consider serving them with a side of al dente asparagus spears or a light, refreshing salad dressed with a citrus vinaigrette. For a heartier meal, buttery mashed potatoes or a bed of fluffy quinoa make excellent accompaniments.

As for beverages, a crisp white wine such as a Chardonnay or Sauvignon Blanc will accentuate the delicate flavors of the scallops. Alternatively, a sparkling water infused with a hint of fresh lime can provide a cleansing palate cleanser between bites.

In Conclusion:

Buttery Garlic Herb Baked Scallops is a culinary masterpiece that is both elegant and effortless. It’s a dish that embodies the essence of fine dining while being accessible for home cooks of all skill levels. With its irresistible aroma, delectable taste, and nutritional value, this recipe is a true gem in the realm of seafood delicacies.

So, whether you’re looking to impress your guests or simply indulge in a luxurious meal, give this recipe a try and experience the sheer delight of Buttery Garlic Herb Baked Scallops. Your taste buds will thank you, and you’ll find yourself returning to this recipe time and time again.
