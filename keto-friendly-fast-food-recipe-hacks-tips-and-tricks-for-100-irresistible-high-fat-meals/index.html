Keto Friendly Fast Food: Recipe Hacks, Tips, and Tricks for 100 Irresistible High-Fat Meals


Are you craving for fast food but worried about slowing down your ketogenic diet progress? Fear not! Today, we’ll dig into the world of tasty, high-fat, and low-carb fast food recipes that everyone can enjoy without breaking a sweat, literally! Get ready to indulge guilt-free in these delectable delights.

1. Cheesy Bacon Deviled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 3 slices of crispy bacon, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil eggs for about 12 minutes, then chill in an ice-water bath. Once cool, halve the eggs and remove yolks.
  2. In a bowl, mix the egg yolks, heavy cream, and shredded cheddar. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Fill the egg white halves with the yolk mixture, then top with chopped bacon. Baked or air-fried to crisp up the bacon pieces if desired.

2. Avocado Bacon Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 lb of 85/15 ground beef
  • 2 bacon slices
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
  • 1 large lettuce leaf
  • 1 onion, caramelized
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook ground beef patties and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Once the patties are done, place a slice of cheddar cheese on top and let it melt.
  3. Cook the bacon until crispy and chop into bits.
  4. Assemble the burger by placing the cheeseburger on the lettuce leaf, followed by avocado slices, bacon bits, and caramelized onions.

3. Bacon Jalapeno Poppers

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, halved and seeds removed
  • 1/2 cup of cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix the cream cheese and cheddar cheese together.
  3. Stuff each jalapeno half with the cheese mixture, and sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top.
  4. Wrap each jalapeno with a half slice of bacon and season with a pinch of salt.
  5. Place on a lined baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy.

4. Greek Yogurt Ranch

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of finely chopped scallions
  • 2 tbsp of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp of dried dill
  • 1 tsp of dried parsley
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor or blender, combine Greek yogurt, chopped scallions, lemon juice, dill, parsley, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Blend until fully combined and the mixture reaches a smooth consistency. If the yogurt is too think, add water, one teaspoon at a time, until desired consistency is achieved.

5. Guacamole Deviled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream
  • 1 tsp of chili powder
  • Salt and lime juice to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil eggs for about 12 minutes, then chill in an ice-water bath. Once cool, halve the eggs and remove yolks.
  2. In a bowl, mash the egg yolks, avocado, and sour cream together. Add chili powder, salt, and lime juice to taste.
  3. Fill the egg white halves with the yolk mixture, then sprinkle with extra chili powder or a sprinkle of cilantro if desired.

6. Keto Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 lb of fettuccine noodles (substituted for zucchini noodles)
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of Parmesan cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook diced chicken breast and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Cook fettuccine noodles in accordance to the package.
  3. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream and melt the Parmesan cheese in the mixture, stirring continuously.
  4. Once the cheese is fully incorporated into the heavy cream, pour the sauce over the cooked chicken and noodles and mix well.

The possibilities are endless when it comes to recreating your fast food favorites in a keto-compliant manner. Don’t hesitate to be creative and experiment with different ingredients! Most importantly, always keep a low-carb diet enjoyable by indulging in these delicious keto fast food recipes. Happy cooking!
