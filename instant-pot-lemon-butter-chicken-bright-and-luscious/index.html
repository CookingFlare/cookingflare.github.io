Instant Pot Lemon Butter Chicken: Bright and Luscious


Are you looking for a quick and delicious chicken recipe that will brighten up your dinner table? Look no further! This Instant Pot Lemon Butter Chicken is bursting with flavor and will leave your taste buds dancing with joy. With just a few simple ingredients and the magic of the Instant Pot, you can have a mouthwatering meal on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Rub the seasoning onto the chicken, ensuring it is evenly coated.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Once hot, add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  3. Place the seasoned chicken breasts into the Instant Pot and sear them on both sides until golden brown. This will help seal in the juices and add extra flavor to the dish.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Give everything a quick stir to combine the flavors.

  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes before carefully performing a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  7. Remove the chicken breasts from the Instant Pot and set them aside on a plate. Cover with foil to keep them warm.

  8. Switch the Instant Pot back to sauté mode and add the butter. Stir until melted and incorporated into the sauce.

  9. Allow the sauce to simmer and thicken for a couple of minutes. This will intensify the flavors and create a velvety texture.

  10. Return the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot and coat them with the luscious lemon butter sauce. Spoon the sauce over the chicken to ensure it is fully immersed in the flavors.

  11. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  12. Serve your Instant Pot Lemon Butter Chicken with your favorite side dishes, such as steamed vegetables or fluffy rice. The vibrant flavors of the lemon and butter will complement any accompaniment perfectly.

Conclusion

This Instant Pot Lemon Butter Chicken recipe is a true winner when it comes to brightening up your dinner routine. The tangy lemon and rich butter create a harmonious symphony of flavors, while the Instant Pot ensures a quick and effortless cooking process. With just a handful of ingredients, you can whip up this luscious chicken dish that will impress your family and friends.

So, why wait? Dust off your Instant Pot and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and your dinner table will be graced with a bright and luscious delight. Enjoy!
