Delightful Low Carb Parmesan Crusted Pork Chops Recipe


If you’re on a low-carb diet and craving something delicious, look no further! This delightful low carb Parmesan crusted pork chops recipe is a game-changer. Not only are these pork chops tender and juicy, but they are also packed with flavor thanks to the crunchy Parmesan crust that adds an irresistible texture. Perfect for dinner parties or a cozy family meal, these pork chops are sure to impress!

Ingredients

To prepare these delightful low carb Parmesan crusted pork chops, gather the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless pork chops (about 1 inch thick)
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup almond flour (or coconut flour for a nut-free option)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or melted butter for frying
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Ingredient Notes:

  • Pork Chops: Choose thick, bone-in or boneless pork chops for the best results. Thickness ensures they stay juicy during cooking.
  • Cheese: Use freshly grated Parmesan cheese for maximum flavor and a better crust.
  • Flours: Almond flour keeps the recipe low-carb, but if you have a nut allergy, coconut flour is a great alternative.

Cooking Instructions

Follow these simple steps to create your delightful low carb Parmesan crusted pork chops:

Step 1: Prepare the Pork Chops

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure even cooking.
  2. Pat Dry: Use paper towels to pat the pork chops dry. This helps the coating adhere better and ensures a nice sear.
  3. Season: Sprinkle salt and black pepper on both sides of the pork chops. This enhances the overall flavor.

Step 2: Create the Coating

  1. Prepare the Breading Station: In one shallow dish, combine the grated Parmesan cheese, almond flour, garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  2. Egg Wash: In another shallow dish, beat the eggs until well combined.

Step 3: Coat the Pork Chops

  1. Dredge: Take each pork chop and dip it into the egg wash, ensuring it’s fully coated.
  2. Coat: Next, press the pork chop into the Parmesan mixture, covering it completely. Make sure to press down to create an even coating. Repeat this for each chop.

Step 4: Cook the Pork Chops

  1. Heat the Oil: In a large oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil or butter over medium-high heat.
  2. Sear the Chops: Once the oil is hot, carefully place the coated pork chops in the skillet. Sear for about 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  3. Bake: Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven. Bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the pork chops reaches 145°F (63°C). Use a meat thermometer for accuracy.

Step 5: Rest and Serve

  1. Rest: After removing the skillet from the oven, let the pork chops rest for 5 minutes. This helps the juices redistribute, ensuring a juicy chop.
  2. Garnish: If desired, sprinkle fresh parsley over the top before serving.

Tips for Perfect Pork Chops

  • Choose the Right Cut: Opt for thicker cuts of pork chops. Thin chops tend to dry out quickly.
  • Don’t Skip the Resting Time: Allowing the chops to rest after cooking is crucial for juicy results.
  • Check Temperature: Always use a meat thermometer to avoid overcooking. Pork should be cooked to an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C).
  • Variations: Feel free to add your favorite herbs and spices to the Parmesan coating for a personalized touch.

Serving Suggestions

These delightful low carb Parmesan crusted pork chops are versatile and pair beautifully with a variety of side dishes. Here are some serving ideas to elevate your meal:

  • Roasted Vegetables: Serve with a side of roasted broccoli, Brussels sprouts, or asparagus for a low-carb option.
  • Salad: A fresh garden salad with a tangy vinaigrette complements the richness of the pork chops perfectly.
  • Cauliflower Mash: Replace traditional mashed potatoes with creamy cauliflower mash for a delicious and satisfying side.
  • Zoodles: Spiralized zucchini cooked lightly in olive oil makes a great low-carb pasta alternative.

Nutritional Information

While the exact nutritional content may vary based on the ingredients used, here’s a general breakdown for one serving of delightful low carb Parmesan crusted pork chops (assuming 4 servings total):

  • Calories: Approximately 350
  • Protein: 35g
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Fiber: 1g

This makes the recipe a low-carb, high-protein option that fits perfectly within a keto or low-carb diet.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this delightful low carb Parmesan crusted pork chops recipe is not only easy to make, but it’s also a fantastic way to enjoy a delicious meal without the excess carbs. With a few simple ingredients and straightforward cooking steps, you can impress your family and friends with a dish that is both satisfying and healthy. So, roll up your sleeves and enjoy this delectable pork chop recipe that’s sure to become a favorite in your household!

Give this recipe a try and watch as it becomes a staple in your low carb meal rotation. Happy cooking!
