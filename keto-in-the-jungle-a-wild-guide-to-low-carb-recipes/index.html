Keto in the Jungle: A Wild Guide to Low-Carb Recipes


Welcome to the Keto in the Jungle, where we take you on a tantalizing journey into the heart of low-carb cooking. Brace yourself for an extraordinary culinary expedition into the wilderness of delicious, nourishing meals, highlighting the undeniable charm of ketogenic eating.

1. Rumble in the Jungle - Jungle Chicken Stir-Fry

Prepare yourself for the Rumble in the Jungle as we feast on the King of our keto jungle: the succulent Jungle Chicken Stir-Fry.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless and skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium-sized onion, chopped
  • 1 medium-sized zucchini, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 cup broccoli, divided
  • Soy sauce (optional: low sodium and high-quality)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the coconut oil on a pan over medium heat. Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, and your preferred herbs. Add them to the pan and cook until browned on both sides and cooked through. Set aside to rest.
  2. In the same pan, add the garlic and ginger and sauté for a minute before adding the sliced bell pepper and onions. Cook for approximately 3 minutes until they become tender.
  3. Introduce the zucchini and cook for about 2 minutes. Add half of the broccoli florets and stir until combined with the other vegetables.
  4. Slice the rested chicken into bite-sized strips and add them to the pan. Cook for about 2 minutes and then season to taste with soy sauce (if using). Plate the rest of the broccoli on the top of the stir-fry.
  5. For a fuller experience, serve the Jungle Chicken Stir-Fry over a bed of shredded cabbage.

2. Saber-tooth Sardine Cakes

A classic favorite made wild, Saber-tooth Sardine Cakes will have your taste buds singing with delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of sardines in olive oil
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Open the can of sardines, drain, and mash them in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add coconut flour, eggs, onion, parsley, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Combine all ingredients until you reach a consistency that resembles thick pancake batter.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Using your hands or a spoon, form the sardine mixture into small, flat patties.
  4. Cook the sardine cakes for about 3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  5. Enjoy your Saber-tooth Sardine Cakes with freshly squeezed lemon juice for an unforgettable wild experience.

3. Prehistoric Pomegranate Salad

Step out of your cave and try this Prehistoric Pomegranate Salad to keep your tummy from rumbling!

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 pomegranate, seeds removed (about 1 cup)
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup raw nuts (almonds, walnuts, or macadamia), chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • Olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, pomegranate seeds, avocado slices, and chopped nuts.
  2. In a separate small container, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil mixture over the salad, then add the crumbled feta cheese on top.
  4. Toss the salad lightly to ensure all ingredients are evenly coated. Enjoy!

Journey on through the wilderness of flavors and prove that eating properly doesn’t mean missing out on taste. Bon appétit!
