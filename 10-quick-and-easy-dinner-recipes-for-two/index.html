10 Quick and Easy Dinner Recipes for Two


Finding simple, delicious dinner ideas that work for just two people can be a challenge. Whether you’re cooking with your partner, a roommate, or hosting a friend, these recipes are designed to take the stress out of meal prep while delivering plenty of flavor. The best part? They’re easy to whip up, require simple ingredients, and won’t leave you with an overwhelming number of leftovers. Let’s dive into these 10 quick and easy dinner recipes for two!

1. Garlic Butter Shrimp and Zucchini Noodles

Looking for a low-carb, high-protein meal bursting with flavor? This dish combines succulent shrimp with garlic-infused butter and “zoodles” for a healthy yet satisfying dinner.

  • Ingredients: Shrimp, garlic, butter, zucchini, olive oil, lemon, salt, pepper.
  • Instructions: Sauté shrimp in butter and minced garlic until cooked. Spiralize zucchini into noodles, lightly toss in olive oil, and mix with the garlicky shrimp. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top, and season as desired.

This one-pan recipe is ready in under 20 minutes and feels like something you’d order at a fancy restaurant, minus the hefty price tag.

2. Caprese Stuffed Chicken Breasts

This is an elegant meal that’s as simple to prepare as it is impressive to serve. Juicy chicken breasts are stuffed with mozzarella, tomatoes, and fresh basil, then baked to perfection.

  • Ingredients: Chicken breasts, fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, fresh basil leaves, balsamic glaze, olive oil, salt, pepper.
  • Instructions: Slice each chicken breast to create a pocket. Stuff with mozzarella, basil, and halved cherry tomatoes. Secure with toothpicks, lightly drizzle olive oil, and season. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes. Finish with balsamic glaze before serving.

The combination of melted cheese and sweet, tangy glaze is irresistible.

3. Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

This takeout favorite can be simplified and scaled down for just two people, and it’s quicker than waiting for delivery!

  • Ingredients: Beef strips, broccoli florets, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, sesame oil, cornstarch, rice (optional).
  • Instructions: Heat sesame oil in a skillet or wok, then cook beef strips until browned. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir-fry steamed broccoli until tender-crisp. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce and cornstarch, then pour over the beef and veggies. Serve alone or over rice.

Customize the spice level by adding a pinch of red chili flakes or sriracha.

4. Creamy Spinach and Mushroom Pasta

This decadent pasta dish feels indulgent without being heavy. Perfect for those nights where you just need a comforting bowl of pasta.

  • Ingredients: Pasta of choice, fresh spinach, mushrooms, heavy cream, garlic, Parmesan cheese, butter, salt, pepper.
  • Instructions: Cook pasta to al dente. In a large pan, sauté mushrooms in butter until golden, then add spinach and garlic. Stir in heavy cream and grated Parmesan, adding cooked pasta to the pan. Toss until well combined.

A sprinkle of extra Parmesan on top adds the perfect finishing touch.

5. Sheet Pan Lemon Herb Salmon and Asparagus

Few things are as satisfying as a quick sheet pan dinner. This healthy option pairs flaky salmon with crisp-tender asparagus for a nutritious, hassle-free meal.

  • Ingredients: Salmon fillets, asparagus, olive oil, lemon, garlic powder, dill, salt, pepper.
  • Instructions: Arrange salmon and asparagus on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with garlic powder, dill, salt, and pepper. Place lemon slices over the salmon. Bake at 400°F (205°C) for 15-20 minutes.

Cleanup is a breeze, and the flavors are bright and refreshing.

6. BBQ Chicken Flatbread Pizzas

Transform flatbreads into a quick pizza night for two! This BBQ chicken version is sweet, smoky, cheesy, and utterly satisfying.

  • Ingredients: Flatbreads, cooked chicken, BBQ sauce, shredded mozzarella, red onion, cilantro.
  • Instructions: Spread BBQ sauce over each flatbread, then top with shredded chicken, mozzarella, and sliced red onion. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 10-12 minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Pair this with a simple side salad for a balanced meal.

7. Turkey Taco Lettuce Wraps

Light, flavorful, and high-protein, these lettuce wraps are perfect for a quick weeknight dinner with a fun DIY element.

  • Ingredients: Ground turkey, taco seasoning, lettuce leaves, tomatoes, shredded cheese, avocado, lime.
  • Instructions: Cook turkey with taco seasoning until fully browned. Spoon into crisp lettuce leaves and top with diced tomatoes, cheese, and avocado slices. Squeeze lime juice over the wraps before enjoying.

Serve with extra lime wedges for a burst of zing.

8. Sweet and Spicy Honey Sriracha Chicken Thighs

Get ready to balance heat, sweetness, and savory goodness in this simple yet bold chicken dish.

  • Ingredients: Chicken thighs, honey, sriracha, soy sauce, garlic, olive oil.
  • Instructions: Whisk honey, sriracha, soy sauce, and minced garlic together. Sear chicken thighs in olive oil until golden brown, then pour the sauce over and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.

Pair with steamed rice or roasted vegetables for a complete meal.

9. Vegetarian Buddha Bowls

For a wholesome, plant-based option, Buddha bowls are endlessly customizable, colorful, and pack a nutritional punch.

  • Ingredients: Quinoa (or rice), roasted sweet potatoes, chickpeas, spinach, avocado, tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt.
  • Instructions: Cook quinoa and roast sweet potato chunks until tender. Assemble by layering quinoa, sweet potatoes, chickpeas, fresh spinach, and avocado slices. Drizzle a simple dressing made of tahini, lemon juice, and olive oil.

This recipe is as Instagram-worthy as it is delicious.

10. Steak and Balsamic Glazed Brussels Sprouts

Elevate your typical steak dinner with caramelized Brussels sprouts dressed in a tangy balsamic reduction.

  • Ingredients: Steak cuts of choice, Brussels sprouts, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt, pepper.
  • Instructions: Cook steak to your desired level of doneness and set aside. Halve Brussels sprouts and sauté in olive oil until crispy and golden. Mix balsamic vinegar and honey, pour over sprouts, and heat until slightly thickened. Serve alongside the steak.

Both hearty and satisfying, this meal is perfect for a date night at home.

Final Thoughts

Cooking for two doesn’t have to mean cutting your recipes in half or dealing with excessive leftovers. These 10 quick and easy dinner recipes are designed with simplicity and flavor in mind, ensuring you’ll spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying your meal. From light and healthy options to indulgent comfort foods, there’s something here for every taste. So, grab your ingredients, get cooking, and make dinner time a relaxing and delicious part of your day!
