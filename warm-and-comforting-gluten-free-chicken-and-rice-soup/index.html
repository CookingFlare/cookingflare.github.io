Warm and Comforting: Gluten-Free Chicken and Rice Soup


Soup is the ultimate comfort food, and there’s nothing quite like a warm and hearty bowl of chicken and rice soup to soothe the soul. Whether you’re looking for a delicious meal to warm you up on a chilly day or a nourishing dish to help you recover from a cold, this gluten-free chicken and rice soup is the perfect choice. Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, this recipe is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup brown rice, cooked
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, and celery, and sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, and bay leaf to the pot. Stir well to combine and let the herbs release their flavors for about 1 minute.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the soup to a simmer. Allow it to cook for about 10 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  4. Add the shredded chicken and cooked rice to the pot. Stir well to combine and let the soup simmer for an additional 10 minutes to ensure all the ingredients are heated through.

  5. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to taste as you go and adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

  6. Once the soup is ready, remove the bay leaf and discard it. Ladle the warm and comforting chicken and rice soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley.

  7. Serve the soup hot and enjoy the comforting flavors and nourishing goodness.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a slurry made from 2 tablespoons of cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons of water. Stir it into the soup during step 4 and let it simmer for a few more minutes until the soup thickens.

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables. Peas, corn, or even spinach would be great additions to this soup.

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add a squeeze of lemon juice or a splash of apple cider vinegar to brighten up the soup.

  • This soup is perfect for meal prep. You can make a big batch and freeze it in individual portions for easy reheating later.

  • If you don’t have cooked chicken on hand, you can poach chicken breasts in the broth before shredding them. Simply add the chicken breasts to the simmering broth and cook until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

Enjoy the comforting and nourishing goodness of this gluten-free chicken and rice soup. It’s the perfect dish to warm you up on a cold day or to bring comfort when you need it most. With its simple yet delicious flavors, this soup is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So grab a bowl, cozy up, and savor every spoonful of this warm and comforting delight!
