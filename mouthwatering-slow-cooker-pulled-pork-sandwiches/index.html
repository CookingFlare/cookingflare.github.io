Mouthwatering Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Sandwiches


There’s nothing quite like sinking your teeth into a mouthwatering pulled pork sandwich. The tender, juicy meat, combined with the rich flavors of the slow-cooked spices, creates a culinary experience that is hard to resist. And the best part? You can easily make this delicious dish at home using your trusty slow cooker. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover the secrets behind these irresistible Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds pork shoulder, boneless
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 8 hamburger buns

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the paprika, brown sugar, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. This homemade spice rub will give your pork shoulder an incredible depth of flavor.

  2. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork shoulder, making sure to coat it evenly. This step is crucial for infusing the meat with the aromatic spices.

  3. Place the seasoned pork shoulder in your slow cooker, and pour in the barbecue sauce, apple cider vinegar, and chicken broth. These liquid ingredients will keep the meat moist and tender throughout the cooking process.

  4. Set your slow cooker to low heat and cook the pork shoulder for 8-10 hours, or until the meat is fork-tender and easily falls apart. This slow cooking method allows the flavors to meld together and creates a melt-in-your-mouth texture.

  5. Once the pork shoulder is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a cutting board. Use two forks to shred the meat into bite-sized pieces. The tender strands of pork will practically fall apart with minimal effort.

  6. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker, and mix it with the remaining juices and sauce. This step ensures that every bite of the sandwich is coated in the delicious barbecue flavors.

  7. Toast the hamburger buns until they are lightly golden and crispy. This will add a satisfying crunch to the sandwich.

  8. Assemble your ultimate pulled pork sandwich by placing a generous amount of the shredded pork onto the bottom half of each bun. Top it off with a dollop of barbecue sauce and your favorite coleslaw for some extra crunch and tanginess.

  9. Finally, crown your creation with the top half of the bun, and take a moment to admire your masterpiece. The combination of flavors and textures is sure to make your taste buds dance with delight.

Now, it’s time to grab that sandwich with both hands, take a big bite, and savor the explosion of flavors in your mouth. The slow-cooked, succulent pulled pork, paired with the sweetness of the barbecue sauce and the crunch of the coleslaw, creates a symphony of taste that will leave you craving for more.

So, gather your ingredients, fire up your slow cooker, and prepare to impress your friends and family with these irresistible Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Sandwiches. They are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a casual backyard barbecue or a cozy night in. Enjoy!
