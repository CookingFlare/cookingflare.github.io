Homemade Relishes: Elevate Your BBQ with Canned Goodies


Are you tired of the same old store-bought relishes that lack that homemade touch? If you’re looking to take your BBQ to the next level, homemade relishes are the way to go. Not only are they bursting with flavor, but they also add a personal touch to your dishes that can’t be replicated with store-bought varieties. In this blog post, we’ll explore the art of making homemade relishes and how you can use them to elevate your BBQ game.

The Art of Homemade Relishes

Homemade relishes are a wonderful way to add a burst of flavor and texture to your BBQ dishes. Whether you prefer the tangy zing of a pickle relish or the sweet and spicy kick of a pepper relish, making your own relishes allows you to customize the flavors to suit your taste preferences. By using fresh, quality ingredients, you can ensure that your relishes are not only delicious but also free from any artificial additives or preservatives.

Sweet and Tangy Pickle Relish

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups finely chopped cucumbers
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 cup finely chopped red bell peppers
  • 1 cup finely chopped green bell peppers
  • 1 1/2 cups white vinegar
  • 2 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
  • 1 tablespoon celery seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup pickling salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cucumbers, onions, and bell peppers.
  2. In a separate saucepan, combine the vinegar, sugar, mustard seeds, celery seeds, turmeric, and red pepper flakes. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Pour the hot liquid over the cucumber mixture and let it sit for 2 hours.
  4. After 2 hours, drain the liquid from the cucumber mixture.
  5. In a large pot, bring the pickling salt and 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the cucumber mixture and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Pack the relish into hot, sterilized jars, leaving 1/4 inch of headspace. Remove any air bubbles and seal the jars.
  7. Process the jars in a boiling water bath for 10 minutes.

Spicy Pepper Relish

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups diced red bell peppers
  • 2 cups diced green bell peppers
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 1/2 cups apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, combine the bell peppers, onion, vinegar, sugar, mustard seeds, salt, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Once the relish has thickened, remove it from the heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  4. Transfer the relish to sterilized jars and store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

Elevating Your BBQ with Homemade Relishes

Now that you have your homemade relishes ready, it’s time to take your BBQ to the next level. Here are a few creative ways to use your relishes to enhance your BBQ dishes:

  • Burger Toppings: Add a spoonful of sweet pickle relish to your burgers for a burst of tangy flavor, or spice things up with a dollop of spicy pepper relish.

  • Hot Dog Condiments: Elevate your hot dogs by topping them with a combination of mustard and sweet pickle relish, or go for a fiery kick with a generous serving of spicy pepper relish.

  • Sandwich Fillings: Use your homemade relishes as a flavorful addition to sandwiches and wraps. The sweet and tangy pickle relish pairs perfectly with ham and cheese, while the spicy pepper relish adds a zesty kick to roast beef sandwiches.

  • Grilled Meat Glazes: Brush your grilled meats with a mixture of your homemade relish and BBQ sauce for a flavor-packed glaze that will take your BBQ to the next level.

With these homemade relishes in your culinary arsenal, you’ll be well-equipped to bring a burst of flavor and creativity to your next BBQ gathering. Experiment with different flavor combinations and let your homemade relishes take center stage at your next outdoor feast.

So, why settle for store-bought relishes when you can create your own flavor-packed canned goodies? Elevate your BBQ with these homemade relishes and watch your dishes transform into culinary masterpieces.
