Peach Raspberry Crumble Bars: A Perfect Combination of Sweet and Tart


If you’re a fan of fruity desserts that perfectly balance sweetness and tartness, then you’re in for a treat with these Peach Raspberry Crumble Bars! These delightful bars are bursting with the flavors of fresh peaches and tangy raspberries, all wrapped up in a buttery crumble crust. They make for a perfect summer dessert or a delicious treat any time of the year.

Ingredients

For the crust and crumble topping:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the filling:

  • 2 cups fresh peaches, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt for the crust and crumble topping. Add in the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Set aside about 1 ½ cups of the crumb mixture for the topping. Add the beaten egg and vanilla extract to the remaining mixture and mix until it comes together to form a dough.

  4. Press the dough evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan to form the crust. Bake for 10 minutes to partially set the crust.

  5. Meanwhile, in a separate bowl, combine the sliced peaches, raspberries, sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice for the filling. Gently toss until the fruit is coated evenly.

  6. Remove the crust from the oven and spread the peach-raspberry filling evenly over the partially baked crust. Sprinkle the reserved crumb mixture over the top.

  7. Return the pan to the oven and bake for an additional 30-35 minutes, or until the crumble topping is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

  8. Once done, remove the pan from the oven and let it cool completely in the pan on a wire rack. This will allow the bars to set properly.

  9. Once cooled, lift the bars out of the pan using the parchment paper overhang and transfer them to a cutting board. Cut into squares or bars of your desired size.

  10. Serve the Peach Raspberry Crumble Bars as they are or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra touch of indulgence. Enjoy!

These Peach Raspberry Crumble Bars are a delightful combination of sweet and tart flavors that will have your taste buds dancing with joy. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a fruity dessert, these bars are sure to be a hit. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try. Your family and friends will thank you for it!

Note: Feel free to experiment with different fruits and berries to create your own unique variations of these crumble bars. You can also add a sprinkle of cinnamon or a dash of nutmeg to the crumb mixture for an extra flavor boost. Let your creativity run wild!
