Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi: A Fast and Elegant Seafood Dish


If you’re looking for a quick, elegant, and utterly delicious seafood dish, then you’re in for a treat with this Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi recipe. This classic Italian-American dish is a perfect combination of succulent shrimp, garlic, butter, and a splash of white wine, all coming together to create a dish that’s bursting with flavor. Whether you’re planning a special dinner for two or just want to treat yourself to something special, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 8 ounces linguine or your choice of pasta, cooked according to package instructions

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning them with a pinch of salt and black pepper.

  2. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once done, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set aside.

  3. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  4. Deglaze the Pan: Pour in the white wine and lemon juice, using a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Let the mixture simmer for 2-3 minutes until it reduces slightly.

  5. Combine the Shrimp and Sauce: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, and toss them in the garlic butter sauce until they are well coated. Stir in the chopped parsley for a fresh, herby flavor.

  6. Serve: To serve, place a portion of cooked pasta on each plate and top with the garlic butter shrimp scampi. Garnish with additional parsley if desired.

Tips and Tricks

  • Quality Ingredients: For the best flavor, use high-quality shrimp, fresh garlic, and real butter.
  • Don’t Overcook the Shrimp: Shrimp cook very quickly, so be sure to watch them closely to avoid overcooking.
  • Variations: Feel free to customize this recipe by adding additional ingredients such as cherry tomatoes, spinach, or shaved Parmesan cheese.

Final Thoughts

Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a light lunch, an impressive dinner, or even as a luxurious appetizer. The combination of succulent shrimp, aromatic garlic, and rich butter creates a dish that’s both comforting and indulgent. Serve this alongside a crisp white wine and some crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce, and you have a meal that’s sure to leave a lasting impression. So, the next time you’re craving a taste of the sea, give this recipe a try and savor the flavors of Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi. Cheers to good food and good company!
