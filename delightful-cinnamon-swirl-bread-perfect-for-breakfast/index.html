Delightful Cinnamon Swirl Bread: Perfect for Breakfast


There’s nothing quite like waking up to the aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through the house. And when that bread is a warm and fragrant cinnamon swirl, breakfast becomes an occasion to savor. Today, I’m going to share with you my recipe for Delightful Cinnamon Swirl Bread, a perfect treat to start your day off right.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering bread, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the cinnamon swirl filling:

  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Give it a good stir to ensure all the dry ingredients are well mixed.

  2. In a separate bowl, warm the milk slightly and add the melted butter. Stir until the butter is fully incorporated.

  3. In another small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for a few minutes until it becomes frothy.

  4. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the milk mixture, dissolved yeast, eggs, and vanilla extract. Mix everything together until a sticky dough forms.

  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Turn it out onto a floured surface and roll it into a rectangle, about 12x16 inches.

  8. Spread the softened butter over the entire surface of the dough. In a small bowl, mix together the cinnamon and sugar for the filling, and sprinkle it evenly over the buttered dough.

  9. Starting from one of the shorter sides, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Pinch the seam to seal it.

  10. Place the rolled dough into a greased loaf pan, cover it with the kitchen towel, and let it rise for another 30-45 minutes, until it has risen slightly above the rim of the pan.

  11. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the bread for 30-35 minutes, or until it is golden brown on top and sounds hollow when tapped.

  12. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This Delightful Cinnamon Swirl Bread is perfect on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with a smear of cream cheese or your favorite fruit preserves. It also makes a delicious French toast or bread pudding.

Final Thoughts

There’s something about the combination of cinnamon and freshly baked bread that just warms the soul. This Delightful Cinnamon Swirl Bread is a wonderful way to start your day, and it’s sure to become a favorite in your household. So, set your alarm a little earlier, gather your ingredients, and let the comforting aroma of cinnamon fill your kitchen. Trust me, it’s worth it. Enjoy!
