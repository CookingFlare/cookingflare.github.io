Picnic Chicken Recipes: Finger-Lickin' Good


Planning a picnic? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some finger-lickin’ good picnic chicken recipes that are sure to make your outdoor gathering a hit. Whether you’re heading to the park, beach, or even just your backyard, these delicious chicken dishes are perfect for enjoying under the sun.

1. Tangy BBQ Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces and thread them onto skewers.
  3. In a bowl, combine the BBQ sauce, honey, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Brush the sauce mixture onto the chicken skewers, making sure to coat them evenly.
  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve with extra BBQ sauce for dipping.

2. Crispy Fried Chicken Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 brioche buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and pickles for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken thighs in a bowl and pour the buttermilk over them. Let them marinate for at least 1 hour, or overnight if possible.
  2. In a shallow dish, combine the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the chicken thighs from the buttermilk, allowing any excess to drip off, then dredge them in the flour mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
  5. Carefully place the chicken thighs in the hot oil and fry for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Remove the chicken from the oil and place on a wire rack to drain any excess oil.
  7. Toast the brioche buns lightly and assemble the sandwiches by placing a piece of crispy fried chicken on the bottom bun, topped with lettuce, tomato, and pickles. Serve immediately.

3. Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large resealable bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight if possible.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  4. Remove the chicken drumsticks from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off, and place them on the preheated grill.
  5. Grill the drumsticks for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and have a nice charred exterior.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

Conclusion

These picnic chicken recipes are sure to impress your family and friends at your next outdoor gathering. From tangy BBQ chicken skewers to crispy fried chicken sandwiches and zesty grilled drumsticks, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So pack up your picnic basket, grab a blanket, and get ready to indulge in some finger-lickin’ good chicken dishes. Happy picnicking!
