Indulge in Authentic Chinese Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry


Who doesn’t love the savory and aromatic flavors of a well-prepared Chinese stir-fry? Today, we’re going to delve into the art of creating an authentic Chinese Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry that will transport your taste buds straight to the bustling streets of Beijing. This iconic dish is not only delicious but also quick and easy to prepare, making it a perfect choice for a satisfying weeknight meal. So, roll up your sleeves, sharpen your knives, and let’s embark on a culinary journey to master the art of Chinese stir-fry!

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1 pound (450g) flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 3 tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine or dry sherry
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 1 pound (450g) fresh broccoli, cut into florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 3 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 2 teaspoons water (for thickening)

Instructions

Step 1: Marinating the Beef

  1. In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced flank steak with the light soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, cornstarch, sesame oil, sugar, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure the beef is evenly coated. Let the beef marinate for at least 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse and tenderize the meat.

Step 2: Blanching the Broccoli

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and blanch the broccoli florets for 2-3 minutes until they are bright green and slightly tender. Quickly transfer the blanched broccoli to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process and preserve its vibrant color. Drain the broccoli and set aside.

Step 3: Stir-Frying

  1. Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil and swirl to coat the wok.
  2. Add the marinated beef to the wok in a single layer, allowing it to sear for 1-2 minutes without stirring to develop a caramelized crust. Flip the beef slices and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until they are just cooked through. Transfer the beef to a plate and set aside.
  3. In the same wok, add the remaining vegetable oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Stir-fry for 30 seconds until aromatic.
  4. Add the blanched broccoli florets to the wok and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes, allowing them to absorb the flavors of the garlic and ginger.
  5. Return the seared beef to the wok and toss everything together, ensuring the beef and broccoli are well combined.
  6. In a small bowl, mix the beef broth, oyster sauce, and dark soy sauce. Pour the sauce into the wok and bring it to a simmer.
  7. In a separate bowl, mix the cornstarch with water to create a slurry. Gradually add the slurry to the wok, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens and coats the beef and broccoli evenly.

Step 4: Serving

  1. Once the sauce has thickened and the beef and broccoli are well-coated, remove the wok from the heat.
  2. Transfer the Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry to a serving platter and serve hot, accompanied by steamed white rice or noodles.

Tips for Perfection

  • Slice the Beef Thinly: Slicing the beef against the grain ensures tenderness and allows it to cook quickly, resulting in juicy and succulent slices.
  • Maintain High Heat: Stir-frying requires high heat to create that characteristic smoky flavor and to lock in the natural juices of the ingredients.
  • Prepare Ingredients in Advance: The key to successful stir-frying is having all your ingredients prepped and ready to go, as the cooking process is fast-paced.

Final Thoughts

Indulging in the delightful flavors of an Authentic Chinese Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is a culinary experience that never fails to delight. The tender, savory beef combined with vibrant, crisp broccoli, and the rich, umami-laden sauce is a symphony of flavors that captures the essence of Chinese cuisine. With this recipe in your repertoire, you’ll be able to recreate the magic of a Chinese stir-fry right in your own kitchen, impressing friends and family with your culinary prowess. So, gather your ingredients, unleash your inner chef, and savor the goodness of this timeless Chinese classic!
