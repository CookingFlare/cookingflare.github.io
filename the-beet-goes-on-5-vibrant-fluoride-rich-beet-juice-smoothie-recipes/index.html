The Beet Goes On: 5 Vibrant Fluoride-rich Beet Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Beets have long been forgotten vegetables in the kitchen, often relegated to festive holiday side dishes or an occasional wedge garnishing salads. Lately, however, beets have been making headlines—they are full of intrinsic nutrients and have an abundance of health benefits. One such vital nutrient is fluoride, which not only strengthens teeth and bones but also is great for our nervous system and skin. Hence, adding a few golden beets, red beets, or even yellow beets to your diet can give you an edge on dental and overall health. How about we kick off this enticing fall color palette flavor explosion featuring awesome beet juice and smoothie recipes perfect to tantalize your senses and ignite your creativity?

Beet, Carrot, and Ginger Juice

This recipe is perfect when you want to wake up your senses and uplift your energy levels. The combination of beet, carrot, and ginger, a potent blend that’s not only vibrant in color but also gooey in taste.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized beetroots, peeled
  • 3 medium-sized carrots, peeled
  • ½ inch ginger root

Instructions:

  1. Wash and peel your beetroots, carrots, and ginger.
  2. Cut the beetroots and carrots into pieces that easily fit your juicer.
  3. Feed the vegetables and ginger through the juicer and collect the juice in a glass.
  4. Stir well and drink fresh for the best taste and nutrition. Optionally, you can add lemon juice or a bit of honey if you prefer a sweeter taste.

Golden Beet Carrot Smoothie

Golden beets are sweeter and less earthy than red beets, making them an excellent choice for smoothies. Paired with carrot and apple, it creates a mellow and sweet flavor that will have you coming back for more.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium golden beets, peeled and chopped
  • 2 medium-sized carrots, chopped
  • 1 medium-sized apple, cored
  • ½ cup plain Greek yoghurt
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Wash and peel the beets and carrots, then chop them into smaller pieces.
  2. Add the chopped beets, carrots, apple, and Greek yoghurt to a blender.
  3. If you prefer a sweeter taste, add 1 teaspoon of honey.
  4. Blend until smooth. Adjust the consistency with more yoghurt or water if needed. Serve chilled.

Beet, Spinach, and Banana Smoothie

This is for you if you crave a taste of a tropical sunrise with a touch of earthiness. This concoction blends beets, banana, and spinach into a nourishing and filling concoction, perfect for starting your day on the right note.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized beetroots, peeled
  • 2-3 ripe bananas
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Peel the beets and banana, then remove the stems from spinach.
  2. Add the beets, banana, and spinach into the blender.
  3. Pour in the almond milk and vanilla extract, then blend until smooth.
  4. Adjust sweetness with a bit of honey if needed, and serve chilled.

Red Beet Citrus Juice

A splash of vibrant red beets mixed with citrus fruits creates a refreshing and invigorating juice you’d never want to miss out on. This recipe packs a punch of antioxidants and fiber, providing unmatched health benefits.

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium-sized red beetroots, peeled
  • 1 orange, peeled
  • 1 carrot, peeled
  • ½ red apple
  • 1 inch ginger, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Wash and peel the beetroots, orange, carrot, and red apple. Mince or chop the ginger.
  2. Chop the beetroots, carrot, and apple into pieces that fit through your juicer.
  3. Feed the vegetables and apple through the juicer, followed by ginger and finish with the orange.
  4. Stir well and enjoy fresh for maximized nutrients.

Beet, Pear, and Ginger Smoothie

If you want a livelier and more vibrant smoothie that’s both satisfying and refreshing, this recipe is a must-try. With a sweet and spicy blend, it’ll be an instant hit for breakfast, snack or even dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized beetroots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 medium-sized pear, cored
  • 1-inch ginger, chopped
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Wash and peel the beetroots, pear, and ginger.
  2. Add the beetroots, pear, and ginger into the blender.
  3. Pour in the almond milk and cinnamon, then blend until smooth.
  4. Adjust the sweetness with more almond milk or a bit of honey if desired. Serve chilled.

Embark on a journey of flavor and nourishment with these beet juice and smoothie recipes. Be thrilled by the range of colors, texture, and taste, enlivening your palate and providing your body with a vital sum of nutrients. Cheers to more vibrant health!
