Mouthwatering Slow Cooker Chicken and Black Bean Chili Recipe

Are you looking for a hearty and flavorful meal that practically cooks itself? Look no further than this mouthwatering slow cooker chicken and black bean chili recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and a perfect blend of spices, this chili is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Ingredients 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes 1 cup chicken broth 1 onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 bell pepper, diced 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced 2 teaspoons chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon oregano Salt and pepper to taste Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado, fresh cilantro Instructions Begin by preparing the ingredients. Rinse and drain the black beans, dice the onion, bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper, and mince the garlic. Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker. Add the black beans, diced tomatoes, chicken broth, diced onion, minced garlic, bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper to the slow cooker. Sprinkle the chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper over the ingredients in the slow cooker. Gently stir the ingredients to combine the spices and distribute the vegetables evenly. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, or until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork. Once the chicken is fully cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker and stir it into the chili. Taste the chili and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Serve the slow cooker chicken and black bean chili hot, and garnish with your choice of shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado, and fresh cilantro. Why Slow Cooker Chicken and Black Bean Chili? Comfort Food Classics: Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese Recipes

Are you ready for a cozy and comforting meal that will satisfy all your cravings? Look no further than these slow cooker mac and cheese recipes! Made with love and packed full of cheesy goodness, these dishes are sure to become your go-to comfort food classics. Creamy Classic Mac and Cheese Ingredients: 1 pound elbow macaroni 4 cups shredded cheddar cheese 2 cups milk 1 cup heavy cream 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted 1/4 cup all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon black pepper Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the cooked macaroni and shredded cheddar cheese, stirring until well mixed. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, heavy cream, melted butter, flour, salt, and black pepper until smooth. Pour the milk mixture over the macaroni and cheese in the slow cooker, stirring gently to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours, or until the cheese is melted and the mixture is hot and bubbly. Stir well before serving, and enjoy the creamy, cheesy goodness! Bacon and Jalapeno Mac and Cheese Ingredients: 1 pound elbow macaroni 4 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese 1 cup milk 1/2 cup heavy cream 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted 1/4 cup all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon black pepper 8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled 2 jalapenos, seeded and diced Instructions: Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside. In a slow cooker, combine the cooked macaroni, shredded cheddar cheese, milk, heavy cream, melted butter, flour, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine. Add the crumbled bacon and diced jalapenos to the slow cooker, stirring gently to distribute them evenly throughout the mixture. Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours, or until the cheese is melted and the mixture is hot and bubbly. Give it a good stir before serving, and get ready to indulge in the perfect balance of smoky bacon and spicy jalapeno flavors! Three-Cheese Mac and Cheese Ingredients: 1 pound elbow macaroni 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese 1 cup shredded Gruyere cheese 2 cups milk 1/2 cup heavy cream 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted 1/4 cup all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon black pepper Instructions: Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside. In a slow cooker, combine the cooked macaroni, shredded cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, Gruyere cheese, milk, heavy cream, melted butter, flour, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours, or until the cheese is melted and the mixture is hot and bubbly. Give it a good stir before serving, and savor the delightful blend of three different cheeses that will melt in your mouth! Tips for Perfect Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese: Use elbow macaroni for the best texture, as it holds the cheese sauce well. Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese to customize the flavors to your liking. If the mac and cheese is too thick, add a little more milk or cream to thin it out. To add a crispy crust to your mac and cheese, transfer it to a baking dish and broil it in the oven for a few minutes before serving. These slow cooker mac and cheese recipes are the epitome of comfort food. They are easy to make, require minimal effort, and will leave you feeling warm and satisfied. Slow Cooker Vegetarian Chili Mac Recipes for a Comforting Meal

Are you in the mood for a hearty and comforting meal that’s also vegetarian-friendly? Look no further than these delicious slow cooker vegetarian chili mac recipes. Packed with flavor, protein, and wholesome ingredients, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings while providing a nutritious meal. Recipe 1: Classic Vegetarian Chili Mac Ingredients: 1 cup dried macaroni 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, rinsed and drained 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes 1 onion, diced 1 bell pepper, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tablespoons chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper, to taste Optional toppings: shredded cheese, chopped cilantro, sour cream Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, onion, bell pepper, garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. Just before serving, stir the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker. Allow it to heat through for 10-15 minutes. Serve hot with your choice of optional toppings such as shredded cheese, chopped cilantro, or sour cream. Recipe 2: Creamy Butternut Squash Chili Mac Ingredients: 1 cup dried macaroni 2 cups butternut squash, peeled and diced 1 can (15 ounces) white beans, rinsed and drained 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes 1 onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika 1/2 cup vegetable broth Salt and pepper, to taste Optional toppings: grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the butternut squash, white beans, diced tomatoes, onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the butternut squash is tender. Using an immersion blender or a countertop blender, puree the mixture until smooth and creamy. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. Just before serving, stir the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker. Allow it to heat through for 10-15 minutes. Serve hot with optional toppings such as grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley. Recipe 3: Spicy Lentil Chili Mac Ingredients: 1 cup dried macaroni 1 cup dried lentils 1 can (15 ounces) crushed tomatoes 1 onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika 3 cups vegetable broth Salt and pepper, to taste Optional toppings: sliced green onions, hot sauce Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the lentils, crushed tomatoes, onion, garlic, jalapeno pepper, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the lentils are tender. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. Just before serving, stir the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker. Allow it to heat through for 10-15 minutes. Serve hot with optional toppings such as sliced green onions and hot sauce. These slow cooker vegetarian chili mac recipes are a perfect combination of comfort and nutrition.
