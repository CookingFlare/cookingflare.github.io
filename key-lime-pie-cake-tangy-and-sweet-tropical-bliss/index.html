Key Lime Pie Cake: Tangy and Sweet Tropical Bliss


Are you ready to embark on a journey to tropical bliss? Look no further than this Key Lime Pie Cake recipe. With its tangy and sweet flavors, this cake will transport you to a sunny paradise with just one bite. Get your taste buds ready for a tropical adventure!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon key lime zest
  • 1/4 cup key lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the key lime frosting:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup key lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon key lime zest

For the key lime curd:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup key lime juice
  • 3 large egg yolks, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon key lime zest

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans. Set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, softened butter, buttermilk, eggs, vanilla extract, key lime zest, key lime juice, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes until the batter is smooth and well-combined.

  3. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  4. While the cakes are baking, prepare the key lime curd. In a saucepan, whisk together the granulated sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually whisk in the water and key lime juice until smooth. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and boils. Boil for 1 minute, then remove from heat.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk the beaten egg yolks. Gradually whisk in about half of the hot mixture. Return the egg mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the butter and key lime zest until smooth. Transfer the curd to a bowl, cover, and refrigerate until chilled.

  6. Once the cakes are baked, remove them from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then, transfer the cakes to a wire rack to cool completely.

  7. While the cakes are cooling, prepare the key lime frosting. In a chilled mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Gradually add the powdered sugar, key lime juice, and key lime zest. Continue beating until stiff peaks form.

  8. Once the cakes are completely cooled, place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread a generous layer of key lime curd on top. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the entire cake with the key lime frosting.

  9. For an extra touch, garnish the cake with additional key lime zest or lime slices. Refrigerate the cake for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.

  10. Slice the cake and indulge in the tangy and sweet tropical bliss of this Key Lime Pie Cake. Each bite will transport you to a sunny paradise, making it the perfect treat for any occasion.

Conclusion

This Key Lime Pie Cake is a delightful combination of tangy key lime flavors and sweet cake. Its tropical bliss will transport you to a sunny paradise with each bite. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply satisfying your sweet tooth, this cake is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and embark on a culinary adventure to tropical bliss today!
