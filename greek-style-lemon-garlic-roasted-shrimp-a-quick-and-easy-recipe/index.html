Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp: A Quick and Easy Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick, flavorful, and easy-to-make dish that will transport you to the sunny coasts of Greece, then this Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp recipe is perfect for you. Bursting with the vibrant flavors of lemon, garlic, and Mediterranean herbs, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the shrimp, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, oregano, thyme, salt, and black pepper. Toss everything together until the shrimp are evenly coated with the marinade.
  3. Spread the marinated shrimp in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Roast the shrimp in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until they are pink and cooked through. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp to keep them tender and juicy.
  5. Once done, remove the shrimp from the oven and garnish with freshly chopped parsley.
  6. Serve the Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp immediately, either as an appetizer or a main dish. You can pair it with a refreshing Greek salad, some crusty bread, or over a bed of fluffy couscous.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp recipe is a testament to the simplicity and elegance of Mediterranean cuisine. The combination of zesty lemon, pungent garlic, and aromatic herbs creates a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Moreover, the quick and easy preparation makes it an ideal choice for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or just looking to expand your culinary horizons, this dish is a must-try.

Nutritional Benefits

Aside from being incredibly delicious, this dish offers nutritional benefits as well. Shrimp is an excellent source of lean protein and is rich in essential nutrients such as selenium, vitamin B12, and omega-3 fatty acids. The use of heart-healthy olive oil and antioxidant-packed herbs further enhances the nutritional profile of this dish. Combined with the fresh burst of citrus from the lemon, this recipe not only delights the palate but also nourishes the body.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp recipe is a shining example of how a few simple ingredients can come together to create a dish that is greater than the sum of its parts. Whether you’re a novice cook or a seasoned chef, this recipe offers a delightful culinary experience that captures the essence of Greek coastal cuisine. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and embark on a flavorful journey to the Mediterranean with this quick and easy recipe. Buon appetito!
