Healthy Instant Pot Lentil Soup Recipes for a Nutritious Meal


In today’s fast-paced world, finding quick and healthy meal options is essential. One dish that ticks all the boxes is the Instant Pot lentil soup. Not only is it packed with nutrients, but it’s also incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re a busy professional or a parent looking for a wholesome meal for your family, these healthy Instant Pot lentil soup recipes will become your go-to option.

Recipe 1: Hearty Lentil and Vegetable Soup

This recipe combines the goodness of lentils with a medley of fresh vegetables, creating a hearty and nutritious soup. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of dried lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and drain.
  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a tablespoon of olive oil.
  3. Sauté the onions, carrots, celery, bell pepper, and garlic until they soften.
  4. Add the lentils, vegetable broth, cumin, and paprika to the Instant Pot.
  5. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick release.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

Recipe 2: Spicy Red Lentil Curry Soup

If you’re a fan of bold flavors and a little heat, this spicy red lentil curry soup is perfect for you. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of red lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the red lentils under cold water and drain.
  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a tablespoon of olive oil.
  3. Sauté the onions and garlic until they become translucent.
  4. Add the curry powder, turmeric, and cayenne pepper to the Instant Pot and stir for a minute to release the flavors.
  5. Add the red lentils, coconut milk, and vegetable broth to the Instant Pot.
  6. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 8 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick release.
  8. Stir in the lemon juice.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Recipe 3: Moroccan Lentil Soup

Transport your taste buds to the vibrant flavors of Morocco with this delicious and aromatic lentil soup. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of brown lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the brown lentils under cold water and drain.
  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a tablespoon of olive oil.
  3. Sauté the onions, carrots, celery, and garlic until they soften.
  4. Add the cumin, coriander, and cinnamon to the Instant Pot and stir for a minute to release their fragrant flavors.
  5. Add the lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth to the Instant Pot.
  6. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 15 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick release.
  8. Stir in the lemon juice.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Conclusion

These healthy Instant Pot lentil soup recipes are not only nourishing but also incredibly versatile. Feel free to experiment with different vegetables, herbs, and spices to suit your taste preferences. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can enjoy a wholesome and nutritious meal without spending hours in the kitchen. So, grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the goodness of these delicious lentil soups!
