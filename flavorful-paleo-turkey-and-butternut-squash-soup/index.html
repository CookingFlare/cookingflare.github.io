Flavorful Paleo Turkey and Butternut Squash Soup


When the weather turns cooler, there’s nothing quite as comforting and satisfying as a warm bowl of soup. This Flavorful Paleo Turkey and Butternut Squash Soup is not only delicious but also packed with wholesome ingredients that will nourish your body and soul. Perfect for those following a Paleo lifestyle or anyone looking for a hearty, nutrient-dense meal, this soup combines the rich flavors of roasted turkey, sweet butternut squash, and aromatic herbs and spices. Let’s dive into how to create this delightful soup that will warm you from the inside out.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 4 cups chicken or turkey broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground sage
  • 1 teaspoon ground thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

1. Prep the Ingredients:

  • Turkey: In a large soup pot or Dutch oven, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground turkey and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks. Once cooked through, remove the turkey from the pot and set aside.

  • Butternut Squash: In the same pot, add another tablespoon of olive oil if needed. Add the diced butternut squash and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it starts to soften. Remove the squash from the pot and set aside with the turkey.

2. Saute the Aromatics:

  • Onion and Garlic: Add the diced onion to the pot and sauté for 3-4 minutes until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  • Bell Pepper: Stir in the diced red bell pepper and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until it begins to soften.

3. Combine Ingredients:

  • Spices: Sprinkle the ground sage, thyme, and cinnamon over the sautéed vegetables. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices.

  • Return Turkey and Squash: Add the cooked ground turkey and butternut squash back into the pot with the vegetables. Stir everything together to combine evenly.

4. Add Liquid Ingredients:

  • Broth and Coconut Milk: Pour in the chicken or turkey broth and coconut milk. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  • Seasoning: Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the soup to a boil over medium-high heat.

5. Simmer and Serve:

  • Simmer: Once the soup reaches a boil, reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let the soup simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the butternut squash is tender.

  • Adjust Seasoning: Taste the soup and adjust seasoning if needed with more salt and pepper.

  • Serve: Ladle the hot soup into bowls. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of freshness and color.

Tips for Success:

  • Consistency: If you prefer a thicker soup, use an immersion blender to blend a portion of the soup once the butternut squash is tender. This will help thicken the broth naturally.

  • Make Ahead: This soup tastes even better the next day as the flavors have more time to meld together. Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days.

  • Variations: Feel free to customize this soup to your liking by adding in extra vegetables such as spinach or kale, or experimenting with different herbs and spices.

Nutritional Benefits:

This Paleo Turkey and Butternut Squash Soup is not only comforting but also nutritionally dense:

  • Turkey: A lean source of protein that supports muscle growth and repair.

  • Butternut Squash: Rich in vitamins A and C, as well as fiber, promoting immune function and digestive health.

  • Coconut Milk: Provides healthy fats that aid in nutrient absorption and support brain health.

In Conclusion:

Warm up this season with a bowl of this Flavorful Paleo Turkey and Butternut Squash Soup. Whether you’re enjoying it as a cozy dinner at home or packing it for lunch, this soup is sure to satisfy your taste buds while nourishing your body with wholesome ingredients. Embrace the simplicity and robust flavors of this Paleo-friendly dish, and share the joy of good food with your loved ones. Enjoy every spoonful of this comforting soup, knowing that you’re fueling your body with ingredients that support your health and well-being. Cheers to delicious, nutritious meals that bring warmth and happiness to your table!
