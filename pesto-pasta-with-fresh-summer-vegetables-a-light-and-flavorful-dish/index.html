Pesto Pasta with Fresh Summer Vegetables: A Light and Flavorful Dish


As the summer heat rolls in, it’s time to embrace light and refreshing meals that are bursting with flavor. One dish that perfectly captures the essence of summer is Pesto Pasta with Fresh Summer Vegetables. This delightful recipe combines the vibrant flavors of homemade pesto with a medley of seasonal vegetables, creating a dish that is both satisfying and wholesome.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of your favorite pasta (linguine or fusilli work well)
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it is al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the pesto. In a food processor or blender, combine the fresh basil leaves, grated Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, garlic cloves, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Pulse until the ingredients are well combined.

  3. With the food processor or blender running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the pesto reaches a smooth and creamy consistency. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  4. In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the cherry tomatoes, zucchini, yellow squash, bell peppers, and red onion. Sauté the vegetables for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Pour the pesto over the pasta and vegetables, tossing everything together until well coated.

  6. Cook the pesto pasta and vegetables for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve the Pesto Pasta with Fresh Summer Vegetables in individual bowls, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Tips and Variations

  • For a protein-packed dish, add grilled chicken or shrimp to the pasta.
  • Feel free to experiment with different vegetables based on what’s in season. Asparagus, broccoli, or snap peas would all make great additions.
  • If you prefer a creamier pesto sauce, add a tablespoon or two of Greek yogurt or heavy cream to the pesto mixture.
  • Sprinkle some extra Parmesan cheese on top before serving for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

Pesto Pasta with Fresh Summer Vegetables is the epitome of a light and flavorful summer dish. The homemade pesto brings a vibrant green color and a burst of freshness, while the assortment of summer vegetables adds a delightful crunch. This recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize it based on your preferences and the ingredients you have on hand. So, grab your favorite pasta, a bunch of fresh basil, and an assortment of colorful vegetables, and get ready to savor the taste of summer with this delightful dish.
