Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice Bowl


If you’re craving a burst of flavors in a single bowl, look no further than this Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice Bowl recipe. Packed with vibrant ingredients and bold spices, this dish is sure to tantalize your taste buds and satisfy your hunger. Whether you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is a must-try!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, cook the rice according to the package instructions, using vegetable broth instead of water. This will infuse the rice with extra flavor.

  2. While the rice is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  3. Add the diced red bell pepper and minced jalapeno pepper to the skillet, and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the peppers start to soften.

  4. Stir in the black beans and corn kernels, followed by the cumin powder, paprika, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure that the spices coat all the ingredients evenly.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and add it to the skillet with the vegetable and spice mixture. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  7. Squeeze the juice of one lime over the rice bowl and give it a final stir.

  8. Serve the Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice Bowl in bowls, garnished with freshly chopped cilantro.

Customize Your Bowl

Feel free to get creative and customize your Mexican Rice Bowl to suit your taste preferences. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Add grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a protein-packed version.
  • Top the bowl with sliced avocado or guacamole for a creamy and refreshing twist.
  • Sprinkle some crumbled feta or grated cheddar cheese on top for a cheesy indulgence.
  • Drizzle some hot sauce or salsa over the bowl for an extra kick of heat.
  • Serve the rice bowl with a side of tortilla chips or warm flour tortillas for a complete meal.

Final Thoughts

This Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice Bowl is a versatile and satisfying dish that brings together the vibrant flavors of Mexican cuisine. With its combination of spices, vegetables, and rice, it offers a balance of heat, tanginess, and freshness. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a crowd-pleasing party dish, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a bowl full of deliciousness!
