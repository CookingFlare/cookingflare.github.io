Tasty and Easy Stir Fry Recipes with Chili Paste


Are you a fan of spicy and flavorful dishes? Look no further because we have some delicious stir fry recipes that are not only tasty but also incredibly easy to make. In this blog post, we will explore the world of stir fry and how using chili paste can take your dishes to a whole new level.

Why Stir Fry?

Stir frying is a cooking technique that originated in China and has become popular all over the world. It involves quickly frying ingredients in a hot pan or wok, resulting in a dish that is bursting with flavors and retains the vibrant colors and textures of the ingredients. Stir fry is also a great way to incorporate a variety of vegetables and proteins into your diet, making it a healthy and satisfying choice.

The Magic of Chili Paste

Chili paste is a versatile ingredient that adds a kick of heat and depth of flavor to any dish. Made from a combination of chili peppers, garlic, oil, and other spices, chili paste can be used in a wide range of recipes. It can be found in different variations, such as Thai chili paste or Korean gochujang, each offering a unique taste profile. When it comes to stir fry, chili paste is a game-changer, elevating the dish to a whole new level of deliciousness.

Recipe 1: Spicy Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of chili paste
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of snow peas
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, marinate the shrimp with chili paste, minced garlic, salt, and pepper for about 15 minutes.
  2. Heat a wok or a large skillet over high heat and add sesame oil.
  3. Add the marinated shrimp to the hot pan and stir fry for about 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add the sliced bell peppers and snow peas. Stir fry for about 2-3 minutes until they are slightly tender.
  5. Return the shrimp to the pan and add soy sauce. Stir fry for another minute to combine the flavors.
  6. Serve the spicy garlic shrimp stir fry over cooked rice and enjoy!

Recipe 2: Sweet and Spicy Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of chili paste
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • Cooked noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together chili paste, honey, and soy sauce.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the sliced chicken breast to the hot pan and stir fry until cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  5. In the same pan, add the sliced red onion, zucchini, carrot, and broccoli. Stir fry for about 4-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and pour the chili paste mixture over the ingredients. Stir fry for another minute to coat everything evenly.
  7. Serve the sweet and spicy chicken stir fry over cooked noodles and enjoy!

Recipe 3: Vegetarian Tofu and Veggie Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of chili paste
  • 2 tablespoons of hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine chili paste, hoisin sauce, and soy sauce.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the cubed tofu to the hot pan and stir fry until golden brown, about 5 minutes. Remove the tofu from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add the sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, and snap peas. Stir fry for about 4-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Return the cooked tofu to the pan and pour the chili paste mixture over the ingredients. Stir fry for another minute to coat everything evenly.
  6. Serve the vegetarian tofu and veggie stir fry over cooked rice and enjoy!

Conclusion

Stir fry is a quick and versatile cooking technique that allows you to create delicious and flavorful dishes in no time. By using chili paste, you can add a spicy kick and depth of flavor to your stir fry recipes. Whether you prefer seafood, chicken, or vegetarian options, there is a stir fry recipe for everyone. So, grab your wok and chili paste, and start creating your own tasty and easy stir fry dishes today!
