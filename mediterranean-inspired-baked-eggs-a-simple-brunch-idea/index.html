Mediterranean-Inspired Baked Eggs: A Simple Brunch Idea


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying brunch idea that’s easy to make and bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than Mediterranean-inspired baked eggs! This simple yet impressive dish is perfect for a leisurely weekend brunch or even a quick weekday breakfast. With a combination of eggs, tomatoes, feta cheese, and a variety of aromatic herbs and spices, this recipe will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for greasing the baking dish
  • Crusty bread or pita for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a baking dish with olive oil to prevent the eggs from sticking.

  2. In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, minced garlic, paprika, dried oregano, chopped parsley, and chopped basil. Season the mixture with salt and black pepper to taste, and then transfer it to the prepared baking dish.

  3. Create little wells in the tomato mixture and crack the eggs into each well, taking care not to break the yolks. This will give the dish a rustic and visually appealing look.

  4. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese evenly over the top of the tomato and egg mixture.

  5. Carefully transfer the baking dish to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still slightly runny. Keep an eye on the eggs to ensure they don’t overcook.

  6. Once the eggs are cooked to your liking, remove the baking dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  7. Serve the Mediterranean-inspired baked eggs straight from the baking dish, along with some crusty bread or warm pita to soak up the flavorful tomato sauce and runny egg yolks.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of hot sauce before baking the eggs.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices such as thyme, rosemary, or cumin to customize the flavor profile to your liking.
  • If you’re a fan of spicy food, you can also add some chopped jalapeños or a dash of cayenne pepper to the tomato mixture for a fiery twist.

Mediterranean-inspired baked eggs are not only a feast for the senses but also a nutritious and protein-packed meal that will keep you fueled throughout the day. Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering with friends or simply treating yourself to a special breakfast, this dish is sure to impress with its vibrant colors and rich, comforting flavors.

So, the next time you’re craving a taste of the Mediterranean, whip up a batch of these irresistible baked eggs and savor the warmth and wholesomeness of a Mediterranean-inspired brunch right in your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
