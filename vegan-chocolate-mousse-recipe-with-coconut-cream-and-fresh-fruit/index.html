Vegan Chocolate Mousse Recipe with Coconut Cream and Fresh Fruit


Who doesn’t love a rich, creamy dessert that’s also guilt-free? This Vegan Chocolate Mousse with Coconut Cream and Fresh Fruit is a delightful treat that will satisfy your sweet tooth while still being dairy-free and vegan-friendly. The combination of velvety chocolate mousse, luscious coconut cream, and vibrant fresh fruit creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will impress even the most discerning dessert connoisseurs. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this decadent yet wholesome dessert.

Ingredients:

For the Vegan Chocolate Mousse:

  • 1 can (14 oz) of full-fat coconut milk
  • 8 oz dairy-free dark chocolate, chopped
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

For the Coconut Whipped Cream:

  • 1 can (14 oz) of full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 1-2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For Serving:

  • Assorted fresh fruits such as strawberries, raspberries, and blueberries
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

Vegan Chocolate Mousse:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the coconut milk over medium heat until it starts to simmer.
  2. Place the chopped dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl.
  3. Pour the hot coconut milk over the chocolate and let it sit for a minute to soften the chocolate.
  4. Gently whisk the mixture until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.
  5. Stir in the maple syrup, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until well combined.
  6. Transfer the chocolate mousse into individual serving glasses or bowls.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours to set.

Coconut Whipped Cream:

  1. Open the can of refrigerated coconut milk and scoop out the solid coconut cream that has risen to the top, leaving the coconut water behind.
  2. Place the coconut cream in a mixing bowl and add powdered sugar and vanilla extract.
  3. Using a hand mixer, whip the coconut cream until it becomes light and fluffy.

Serving:

  1. Once the chocolate mousse has set, spoon a dollop of coconut whipped cream on top of each serving.
  2. Garnish with fresh fruits and mint leaves.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy the luxurious flavors of this vegan dessert.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a twist, you can add a hint of orange zest to the chocolate mousse for a citrusy undertone.
  • Experiment with different combinations of fresh fruits to complement the chocolate mousse, such as sliced bananas, kiwi, or blackberries.
  • If you prefer a sweeter mousse, you can increase the amount of maple syrup to suit your taste.

Final Thoughts:

This Vegan Chocolate Mousse with Coconut Cream and Fresh Fruit is a testament to the fact that vegan desserts can be just as indulgent and satisfying as their traditional counterparts. The creamy, luscious texture of the chocolate mousse paired with the airy coconut whipped cream and the burst of freshness from the fruits creates a dessert experience that’s truly unforgettable. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your repertoire of vegan delights.

So there you have it, a delectable vegan dessert that’s as stunning to look at as it is to savor. Embrace the richness of chocolate, the creaminess of coconut, and the vibrancy of fresh fruit in every spoonful, and let this Vegan Chocolate Mousse with Coconut Cream and Fresh Fruit elevate your dessert game to a whole new level. Cheers to decadent, cruelty-free indulgence!
