Gluten-Free Ice Cream and Sorbet Recipes for Summer


Are you ready to cool down this summer with some delightful and refreshing gluten-free frozen treats? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of mouth-watering ice cream and sorbet recipes that are not only perfect for hot summer days but are also gluten-free, so everyone can indulge without worry. Let’s dive into these delectable recipes that will surely satisfy your sweet cravings!

Creamy Coconut Milk Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together coconut milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. Once churned, transfer the ice cream to a container and freeze for a few hours until firm.
  4. Scoop and enjoy the creamy goodness of this coconut milk ice cream!

Refreshing Watermelon Sorbet

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of cubed seedless watermelon
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lime juice

Instructions:

  1. Place the cubed watermelon on a baking sheet and freeze until solid.
  2. In a blender, combine the frozen watermelon, honey, and lime juice.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a freezer-safe container and freeze for a couple of hours.
  5. Serve this light and refreshing watermelon sorbet on a hot summer day for a burst of flavor.

Tangy Lemon Sorbet

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup of water
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine water and sugar. Heat until the sugar dissolves to create a simple syrup.
  2. Let the syrup cool, then add lemon juice and zest. Mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn until it reaches a sorbet consistency.
  4. Freeze for a few hours before serving. The zesty lemon sorbet is perfect for a palate-cleansing dessert.

Creamy Banana Nice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe bananas, sliced and frozen
  • 1/4 cup of almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, blend the frozen banana slices until they resemble a creamy texture.
  2. Add almond milk and vanilla extract, then blend again until smooth.
  3. Serve immediately for a soft-serve consistency or freeze for a firmer texture.
  4. This guilt-free banana nice cream is a healthy and delicious alternative to traditional ice cream.

Whether you’re craving a rich and creamy coconut milk ice cream, a light and fruity watermelon sorbet, a tangy lemon sorbet, or a guilt-free banana nice cream, these gluten-free frozen treats are sure to elevate your summer dessert game. Try out these recipes to stay cool, refreshed, and satisfied during the sunny days ahead. Happy summer indulging!

Remember to experiment with different flavors and ingredients to customize these recipes to your liking. Feel free to share your creations with friends and family for a delightful summer treat that everyone can enjoy. Cheers to a season filled with delicious gluten-free frozen delights!
