Luscious Italian Lemon Ricotta Cake Recipe - Sweet Citrus Indulgence


If you’re a fan of Italian desserts or simply have a sweet tooth, then this luscious Italian Lemon Ricotta Cake is a must-try. This delightful cake is a perfect balance of sweetness and citrusy tang, making it a refreshing treat for any occasion. The addition of ricotta cheese gives it a moist and tender texture, elevating it to a whole new level of indulgence. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a slice of something special, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan and set it aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the ricotta cheese, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared springform pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Bake for 50-55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then carefully remove the sides of the springform pan and transfer the cake to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. While the cake is cooling, prepare the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, fresh lemon juice, and lemon zest until smooth.

  10. Once the cake has completely cooled, drizzle the glaze over the top of the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.

  11. Slice and serve the cake, garnished with additional lemon zest if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This Italian Lemon Ricotta Cake is best enjoyed with a cup of freshly brewed coffee or a zesty lemon tea. The light and moist texture of the cake pairs perfectly with the bright citrus flavors, making it an ideal dessert for spring and summer gatherings. For an extra indulgent treat, serve each slice with a dollop of freshly whipped cream or a scoop of lemon sorbet.

Conclusion

With its irresistible combination of sweet, tangy, and creamy flavors, this Italian Lemon Ricotta Cake is a true citrusy indulgence. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to a slice of this delightful Italian dessert. Buon appetito!
