Vegetarian Dan Dan Noodles: A Spicy and Satisfying Dish


If you’re a fan of spicy and flavorful Asian cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this Vegetarian Dan Dan Noodles recipe! This dish originates from the Sichuan province of China and is known for its bold and fiery flavors. Traditionally made with ground pork, we’ve put a vegetarian twist on this classic dish, making it suitable for veggie lovers everywhere.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dried Chinese egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons black vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons chili oil
  • 1 tablespoon Sichuan peppercorns, toasted and ground
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped roasted peanuts
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Chinese egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, black vinegar, tahini, chili oil, and ground Sichuan peppercorns. Set the sauce aside.

  4. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes until they start to soften. Then, add the shredded cabbage and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  5. Pour the vegetable broth into the skillet and bring it to a simmer. Let it cook for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  6. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet and toss them with the vegetables and broth. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and divide the noodles among serving bowls. Drizzle each bowl with the prepared sauce, ensuring that each serving is generously coated.

  8. Garnish the noodles with chopped green onions, roasted peanuts, and fresh cilantro for an added burst of flavor and texture.

Serving Suggestions

Serve these Vegetarian Dan Dan Noodles hot and enjoy the explosion of flavors! The combination of the spicy sauce, tender noodles, and crunchy vegetables creates a truly satisfying and mouthwatering dish.

For an extra kick, you can sprinkle some additional ground Sichuan peppercorns on top before serving. If you prefer a milder version, feel free to adjust the amount of chili oil according to your taste.

This dish pairs well with a side of steamed or stir-fried vegetables, such as bok choy or broccoli. You can also enjoy it on its own as a filling and flavorful meal.

Final Thoughts

Vegetarian Dan Dan Noodles are a delightful and spicy addition to your recipe repertoire. With its vibrant flavors and satisfying texture, this dish is sure to please both vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. The combination of the umami-rich sauce, chewy noodles, and crunchy vegetables creates a symphony of taste in every bite.

So next time you’re craving something spicy and satisfying, give this Vegetarian Dan Dan Noodles recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed!

(Note: This recipe serves 2-3 people. Feel free to adjust the quantities accordingly to serve more or fewer portions.)
