Seafood Fritters Recipe with Shrimp and Vegetables


Are you craving a dish that’s bursting with flavor and packed with seafood goodness? Look no further than these delectable seafood fritters with shrimp and vegetables! This recipe combines the succulent taste of shrimp with a medley of fresh vegetables, all bound together in a light and crispy batter. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking to treat yourself to a special homemade meal, these seafood fritters are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the seafood fritters, you will need:

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup of finely chopped bell peppers (red and green)
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped red onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

1. Prepare the Shrimp and Vegetables

Begin by finely chopping the shrimp into small pieces and set aside. In a large bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk and eggs until well combined.

Add the chopped shrimp, corn kernels, bell peppers, red onion, garlic, green onions, and parsley to the flour mixture. Gently fold the ingredients together until the shrimp and vegetables are evenly coated with the flour mixture.

2. Form the Fritters

In a deep skillet or frying pan, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Using a spoon, drop spoonfuls of the shrimp and vegetable mixture into the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Flatten the fritters slightly with the back of the spoon to ensure even cooking.

Fry the fritters for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Once cooked, remove the fritters from the oil and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

3. Serve and Enjoy

These seafood fritters are best served hot and crispy, accompanied by your favorite dipping sauce or a zesty squeeze of lemon. They make a delightful appetizer for a gathering or a satisfying main course when paired with a fresh salad or steamed vegetables.

Conclusion

With their irresistible combination of tender shrimp, vibrant vegetables, and a crispy golden exterior, these seafood fritters with shrimp and vegetables are a true delight for the taste buds. The next time you’re in the mood for a seafood treat, give this recipe a try and savor the wonderful flavors that come together in each delightful bite. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or simply looking to expand your culinary horizons, these fritters are sure to become a beloved addition to your recipe collection. Enjoy!
