Sous Vide Buffalo Chicken Wings: Spicy and Succulent


There’s nothing quite like sinking your teeth into a plate of perfectly cooked buffalo chicken wings. The combination of spicy sauce and tender meat is simply irresistible. If you’re looking to take your buffalo wings game to the next level, then it’s time to embrace the magic of sous vide cooking. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create the most mouthwatering, spicy, and succulent sous vide buffalo chicken wings that will have your taste buds dancing with delight.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup of hot sauce
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 teaspoons of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your water bath to 165°F (74°C). If you don’t have a sous vide machine, you can set up a large pot of water on the stove and use a thermometer to monitor the temperature.

  2. In a bowl, whisk together the hot sauce, melted butter, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined. This will be the marinade for your chicken wings.

  3. Place the chicken wings in a ziplock bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag, ensuring all the wings are coated in the marinade. Let the wings marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, but overnight is even better for maximum flavor infusion.

  4. Once the wings have marinated, remove them from the bag and pat them dry with a paper towel. This will help achieve a crispy skin when we finish cooking them.

  5. Now it’s time to sous vide! Place the wings in a single layer in a vacuum-sealed bag or a ziplock bag, making sure they are not overcrowded. Remove as much air as possible from the bag using the water displacement method or a vacuum sealer.

  6. Gently lower the bag of wings into the preheated water bath, ensuring they are fully submerged. Cook the wings for 2 hours to develop that incredible tenderness and flavor.

  7. While the wings are cooking, preheat your grill or broiler to high heat. We’re going to finish the wings off with a nice char and crispiness.

  8. After the wings have finished cooking in the sous vide bath, remove them from the bag and pat them dry again. This step is crucial to ensure a crispy skin.

  9. Brush the wings with a little bit of oil to prevent sticking and place them on the hot grill or under the broiler. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, until the skin is crispy and slightly charred.

  10. Once the wings are nicely charred, remove them from the grill or broiler and place them in a large bowl. Pour the remaining marinade over the wings and toss to coat evenly.

  11. Serve the sous vide buffalo chicken wings hot with your favorite dipping sauce, celery sticks, and carrot sticks on the side. These wings are perfect for game days, parties, or any occasion that calls for finger-licking-good food.

Conclusion

Sous vide buffalo chicken wings take the classic game day snack to a whole new level. The sous vide cooking method ensures that the wings are tender and juicy on the inside, while finishing them off on the grill or under the broiler adds that irresistible crispy texture. The spicy and succulent flavor of the buffalo sauce will leave you wanting more with every bite. So fire up your sous vide machine, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress your friends and family with the best buffalo chicken wings they’ve ever had. Enjoy!
