One Pot Creamy Cajun Pasta


If you’re a fan of flavorful, creamy pasta dishes but dread the thought of cleaning multiple pots and pans, then this One-Pot Creamy Cajun Pasta recipe is about to become your new best friend. Not only is it incredibly delicious, but it also requires minimal cleanup, making it the perfect dish for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. This recipe combines the bold flavors of Cajun seasoning with the creamy goodness of a rich pasta sauce, all cooked in a single pot for maximum convenience.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) of fettuccine pasta
  • 1 pound (450g) of chicken breast, sliced into strips
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups (475ml) of chicken broth
  • 1 cup (240ml) of heavy cream
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) of diced tomatoes, drained
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Begin by slicing the chicken breast into strips and seasoning them with Cajun seasoning. Slice the bell peppers, chop the onion, and mince the garlic. Drain the diced tomatoes and set aside.

  2. Sear the Chicken: In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken strips and cook until they are no longer pink on the outside, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  3. Sauté the Vegetables: In the same pot, add the sliced bell peppers, chopped onion, and minced garlic. Sauté the vegetables until they are tender and fragrant, approximately 5-6 minutes.

  4. Cook the Pasta: Add the fettuccine pasta to the pot, along with the seared chicken. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, then add the drained diced tomatoes. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Simmer to Perfection: Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cover the pot and let the pasta cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the pasta is al dente and the sauce has thickened, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  6. Season and Serve: Once the pasta is cooked to perfection, season the dish with salt and black pepper to taste. Give it one final stir to ensure all the flavors are well combined. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

And there you have it - a steaming pot of One-Pot Creamy Cajun Pasta that’s ready to be enjoyed! Serve it up in deep bowls, and don’t forget to sprinkle a little extra Parmesan cheese on top for an added touch of indulgence.

Final Thoughts:

This One-Pot Creamy Cajun Pasta recipe is a true testament to the magic of one-pot cooking. Not only does it deliver on flavor, but it also minimizes the post-dinner cleanup, leaving you with more time to savor every satisfying bite. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a comforting meal for yourself, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. So, grab a pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to experience the creamy, spicy bliss of this delightful pasta dish. Cheers to the joy of one-pot wonders!
