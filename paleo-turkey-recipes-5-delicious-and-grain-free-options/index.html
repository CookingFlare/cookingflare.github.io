Paleo Turkey Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


Looking for some delicious and healthy Paleo turkey recipes? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore five mouthwatering recipes that are not only packed with flavor but also grain-free. These recipes are perfect for those following the Paleo diet or anyone looking for a nutritious and satisfying meal. So, let’s get started!

1. Herb-Roasted Turkey Breast

Ingredients:

  • 1 turkey breast (about 3-4 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, rosemary, thyme, sage, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the herb mixture all over the turkey breast, ensuring it is well coated.
  4. Place the turkey breast on a roasting pan and roast for about 1 hour or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. Let the turkey breast rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy!

2. Turkey and Butternut Squash Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 butternut squash, peeled and diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  2. Add the ground turkey and cook until browned.
  3. Add the butternut squash, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 30 minutes or until the butternut squash is tender.
  5. Serve hot and garnish with your favorite toppings such as avocado slices or fresh cilantro.

3. Turkey Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup water chestnuts, chopped
  • Lettuce leaves for wrapping

Instructions:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the ground turkey. Cook until browned.
  2. Add the coconut aminos, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic. Stir well to combine.
  3. Add the mushrooms and water chestnuts and cook for another 5 minutes or until the mushrooms are tender.
  4. Spoon the turkey mixture onto lettuce leaves and wrap them up. Serve and enjoy!

4. Turkey and Sweet Potato Hash

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  2. Add the ground turkey and cook until browned.
  3. Add the sweet potatoes, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  4. Cover the skillet and cook for about 15 minutes or until the sweet potatoes are tender.
  5. Serve hot and garnish with fresh parsley.

5. Turkey and Kale Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers, halved and seeds removed
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups kale, chopped
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the bell pepper halves on a baking sheet.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground turkey, onion, and garlic until the turkey is browned.
  4. Add the kale, diced tomatoes, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Spoon the turkey mixture into the bell pepper halves.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25 minutes or until the peppers are tender.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

These five Paleo turkey recipes are not only delicious but also grain-free, making them a perfect choice for a healthy and satisfying meal. Whether you’re following the Paleo diet or simply looking for flavorful dishes, these recipes are sure to please your taste buds. Give them a try and savor the goodness of Paleo cooking!
