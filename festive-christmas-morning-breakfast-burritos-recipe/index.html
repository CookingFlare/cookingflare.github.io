Festive Christmas Morning Breakfast Burritos Recipe


Christmas morning is a time for joy, laughter, and of course, delicious food. As you gather around the tree, unwrapping presents and sharing holiday cheer, one thing that can elevate the festive spirit is a hearty breakfast. This year, surprise your loved ones with a mouthwatering batch of Festive Christmas Morning Breakfast Burritos. These burritos are not only easy to make but are also packed with flavors that will make your holiday breakfast unforgettable.

Why Breakfast Burritos?

Breakfast burritos are the perfect combination of convenience and taste. They can be prepared ahead of time, allowing you more time to enjoy the festivities. Plus, they are versatile; you can adjust the ingredients to suit your family’s preferences or dietary restrictions. Whether you prefer a vegetarian option, a meat lover’s delight, or a spicy kick, these burritos can cater to all.

Ingredients

To create these festive breakfast burritos, gather the following ingredients:

For the Burrito Filling:

  • 8 large flour tortillas (10-inch)
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup cooked breakfast sausage (crumbled)
  • 1 cup diced cooked bacon
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers (red and green for a festive touch)
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

For the Salsa (optional):

  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime (juiced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Serving:

  • Sour cream
  • Avocado slices
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Filling

  1. Heat the Olive Oil: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onions and bell peppers, sautéing until they are soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.

  2. Cook the Eggs: In a bowl, whisk the eggs, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture into the skillet with the sautéed onions and peppers. Stir continuously until the eggs are scrambled and fully cooked. Remove from heat.

  3. Add Meats and Cheese: Fold in the crumbled sausage, diced bacon, and shredded cheddar cheese into the egg mixture. Stir until the cheese melts and everything is well combined.

Step 2: Assemble the Burritos

  1. Prepare the Tortillas: Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet for about 30 seconds on each side to make them more pliable.

  2. Fill the Tortillas: Lay a tortilla flat on a clean surface. Spoon a generous amount of the egg mixture onto the center of the tortilla, approximately 1/3 cup.

  3. Fold and Roll: Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling and then roll it from the bottom to create a burrito shape. Make sure to wrap it tightly to prevent any filling from escaping. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling.

Step 3: Make the Salsa (Optional)

  1. Mix the Ingredients: In a medium bowl, combine diced tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Adjust the seasoning to taste. This fresh salsa will add a zesty kick to your burritos.

Step 4: Serve

  1. Warm the Burritos: For a crispy exterior, you can toast the assembled burritos in a skillet over medium heat for about 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.

  2. Garnish and Enjoy: Serve the breakfast burritos warm with a side of fresh salsa, sour cream, avocado slices, and hot sauce, if desired. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a festive touch.

Tips for the Perfect Christmas Morning Breakfast Burritos

  • Make Ahead: You can prepare the filling a day prior and store it in the refrigerator. In the morning, just warm it up and assemble your burritos.
  • Customize Your Fillings: Feel free to add ingredients like spinach, mushrooms, or different kinds of cheese to suit your family’s taste.
  • Freezing: If you want to enjoy these burritos later, wrap them tightly in foil or plastic wrap and freeze. To reheat, simply microwave or bake until heated through.

Pairing Suggestions

These festive breakfast burritos pair wonderfully with various holiday drinks. Consider serving them with:

  • Hot Chocolate: A rich, creamy hot chocolate topped with whipped cream and a sprinkle of peppermint is a delightful complement.
  • Eggnog: This classic holiday drink adds a festive cheer to your breakfast.
  • Mimosas: For a bubbly brunch feel, mix orange juice with sparkling wine for a refreshing drink.

Conclusion

This Christmas, elevate your morning celebration with these delightful breakfast burritos. Their combination of savory flavors and festive colors will not only satisfy your taste buds but also fill your home with warmth and holiday spirit. Whether enjoyed by the fireplace or around the tree, these burritos will surely become a cherished tradition in your Christmas morning routine.

Make sure to share this recipe with family and friends, so they can join in on the fun. Happy holidays and happy cooking! Enjoy every bite of your Festive Christmas Morning Breakfast Burritos.
