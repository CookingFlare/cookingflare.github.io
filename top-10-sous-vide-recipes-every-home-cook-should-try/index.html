Top 10 Sous Vide Recipes Every Home Cook Should Try


Sous vide cooking has revolutionized the way we approach meal preparation, offering precision and consistency that traditional methods struggle to match. This technique, which involves cooking food in a vacuum-sealed bag submerged in a water bath at a controlled temperature, allows home cooks to achieve restaurant-quality results with ease. Whether you’re a seasoned sous vide enthusiast or just starting, here are the top ten sous vide recipes that every home cook should try.

1. Perfectly Cooked Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks (1.5 inches thick)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme
  • Olive oil or butter

Instructions:

  1. Season the Steaks: Generously season both sides of the ribeye steaks with salt and pepper. Add a sprig of rosemary or thyme for added flavor.
  2. Vacuum Seal: Place the seasoned steaks in a vacuum-seal bag. If you have a sous vide machine with a water displacement method, you can use a Ziploc bag instead.
  3. Set the Temperature: Preheat your sous vide water bath to your desired doneness (130°F for medium-rare, 140°F for medium).
  4. Cook the Steaks: Submerge the vacuum-sealed bag in the water bath and cook for 1 to 3 hours.
  5. Sear for Flavor: Once cooked, remove the steaks and pat them dry. Heat olive oil or butter in a skillet over high heat and sear each side for about 1 minute until a crust forms.
  6. Serve: Let the steaks rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

2. Sous Vide Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Season the Chicken: Rub the chicken breasts with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Seal and Sous Vide: Place the chicken in a vacuum-sealed bag with olive oil and seal it. Preheat your sous vide bath to 140°F for tender, juicy chicken.
  3. Cooking Time: Submerge the bag and cook for 1.5 to 2 hours.
  4. Sear: After cooking, remove the chicken from the bag and pat dry. Sear in a hot pan for about 1 minute on each side.
  5. Serve: Slice and serve over a salad or with your favorite sides.

3. Flavored Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pork tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, mix soy sauce, maple syrup, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Seal the Pork: Place the pork tenderloin in a vacuum bag with the marinade and seal.
  3. Sous Vide Temperature: Set your sous vide bath to 140°F and cook for 2 to 3 hours.
  4. Sear and Serve: After cooking, sear the pork in a hot pan for 2 minutes on each side. Let rest for a couple of minutes before slicing.

4. Creamy Sous Vide Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional toppings: chives, cheese, or smoked salmon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Sous Vide: Set your sous vide water bath to 167°F.
  2. Cook the Eggs: Place the eggs (in their shells) directly into the water bath and cook for 13 minutes.
  3. Serve with Style: Remove the eggs, crack them open into a bowl, season with salt and pepper, and add your favorite toppings.

5. Sous Vide Carrots with Honey Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Carrots: Place the carrot sticks in a vacuum-seal bag with honey, butter, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sous Vide Cooking: Set your sous vide to 183°F and cook for 1 hour.
  3. Serve: Toss the carrots in the bag to coat with the glaze and serve warm.

6. Sous Vide Salmon with Dill

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Season the Salmon: Sprinkle the salmon fillets with salt and pepper, and place a sprig of dill on each.
  2. Seal and Cook: Drizzle with olive oil, seal in a bag, and sous vide at 125°F for 45 minutes.
  3. Serve: Gently remove from the bag and serve with lemon wedges.

7. Sous Vide Vegetables Medley

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Place all vegetables in a vacuum bag with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sous Vide Technique: Set your sous vide to 183°F and cook for 45 minutes.
  3. Serve: Toss the vegetables for even distribution of flavors and serve as a side dish.

8. Sous Vide Lamb Chops

Ingredients:

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh rosemary

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Lamb: Rub lamb chops with olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, and rosemary.
  2. Seal and Cook: Vacuum seal the chops and sous vide at 130°F for 2 hours.
  3. Sear and Serve: Sear in a hot skillet for about 2 minutes per side and serve with a side of mint sauce.

9. Sous Vide Apples for Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium apples, peeled and cored
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Apples: Place the apples in a vacuum bag with brown sugar, cinnamon, and butter.
  2. Sous Vide Cooking: Set your sous vide to 190°F and cook for 1 hour.
  3. Serve: Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or as a topping for pancakes.

10. Sous Vide Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup dark chocolate, chopped
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup flour

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Batter: Melt chocolate and butter together. In a bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until combined. Mix in flour, then fold in the melted chocolate.
  2. Portion and Seal: Pour the batter into small mason jars and seal tightly.
  3. Sous Vide Temperature: Set the sous vide to 176°F and cook for 1 hour.
  4. Serve: Allow to cool slightly before opening and serve warm, optionally with whipped cream.

Conclusion

Sous vide cooking opens up a world of culinary possibilities for home cooks, allowing you to create dishes that are not only delicious but also perfectly cooked every time. From perfectly seared steaks to decadent desserts, these top ten sous vide recipes promise to elevate your cooking game and impress your family and friends. So, dust off that sous vide machine and get ready to explore the endless flavors and textures that await you in the sous vide world! Enjoy your cooking adventure!
