Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice with Pork


Mexican cuisine is renowned for its bold flavors and vibrant spices that tantalize the taste buds. One dish that embodies this culinary tradition perfectly is Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice with Pork. This hearty and satisfying meal combines tender pieces of pork with fragrant rice, tomatoes, and a medley of spices that create a symphony of flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork shoulder, diced
  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced (any color)
  • 2 jalapeño peppers, seeded and minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Pork

  1. Heat a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add a tablespoon of oil.
  2. Season the diced pork shoulder with salt and pepper. Add the pork to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the pork from the skillet and set aside.

2. Sauté Aromatics

  1. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of oil if needed. Sauté the chopped onion, garlic, bell pepper, and jalapeños until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes with their juices and tomato paste. Stir to combine.

3. Cook the Rice

  1. Stir in the long-grain rice, chili powder, cumin powder, and paprika. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently, until the rice is lightly toasted.
  2. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.

4. Combine and Simmer

  1. Return the browned pork shoulder to the skillet. Stir everything together well.
  2. Reduce the heat to low, cover with a lid, and simmer gently for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

5. Garnish and Serve

  1. Once the rice is cooked through, taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  2. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  3. Fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with freshly chopped cilantro.
  4. Serve hot with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrusy flavor.

Tips for Success

  • Choosing Pork: Pork shoulder is ideal for this dish as it becomes tender and flavorful when simmered with the rice.
  • Spice Level: Adjust the amount of jalapeños and chili powder to suit your spice preference.
  • Tomato Paste: Tomato paste adds richness and depth to the dish, enhancing the overall flavor profile.
  • Resting Time: Allowing the rice to rest after cooking ensures a fluffy texture and allows the flavors to develop fully.

Variations

  • Vegetarian Option: Replace the pork with black beans or tofu for a meatless version.
  • Additions: Customize with corn kernels, diced carrots, or black olives for additional texture and flavor.
  • Toppings: Serve with avocado slices, sour cream, or shredded cheese for extra indulgence.

Enjoy Your Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice with Pork!

This Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice with Pork recipe is perfect for family dinners, gatherings with friends, or any occasion where you want to impress with robust flavors and comforting aromas. It’s a complete meal in itself, combining protein, grains, and vegetables into one delicious dish. Embrace the spirit of Mexican cuisine with this easy-to-make recipe that will surely become a favorite at your table. Bon appétit!
