Savory and Satisfying: Gluten-Free Turkey Meatballs


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional meatballs, then look no further! These Savory and Satisfying Gluten-Free Turkey Meatballs are packed with flavor and are perfect for those following a gluten-free diet. Made with lean turkey meat and a blend of aromatic herbs and spices, these meatballs are sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup gluten-free bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground turkey, gluten-free bread crumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Add the lightly beaten egg to the mixture and continue to mix until the egg is fully combined.

  3. Using clean hands, shape the mixture into small meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter. Place the meatballs on a parchment-lined baking sheet and set aside.

  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  5. In a large non-stick skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the meatballs to the skillet, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Cook the meatballs in batches if necessary.

  6. Cook the meatballs for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are browned and cooked through. Transfer the cooked meatballs to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

  7. Once all the meatballs are cooked, transfer them to a baking dish and place them in the preheated oven. Bake for an additional 10-12 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through and reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

  8. Remove the meatballs from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

  9. Serve the Savory and Satisfying Gluten-Free Turkey Meatballs with your favorite gluten-free pasta or zucchini noodles and top with marinara sauce or pesto for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, try adding some finely chopped sun-dried tomatoes or roasted red peppers to the meatball mixture.
  • If you don’t have gluten-free bread crumbs on hand, you can easily make your own by toasting gluten-free bread and pulsing it in a food processor until it reaches a breadcrumb consistency.
  • These meatballs can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for up to 3 months. Simply reheat them in the oven or microwave before serving.

Enjoy these Savory and Satisfying Gluten-Free Turkey Meatballs as a delicious and wholesome alternative to traditional meatballs. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply looking for a healthier option, these meatballs are sure to satisfy your cravings. Serve them with your favorite pasta or enjoy them on their own for a tasty and nutritious meal.
