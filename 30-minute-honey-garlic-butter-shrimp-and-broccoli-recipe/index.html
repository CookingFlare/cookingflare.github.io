30-Minute Honey Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that’s bursting with flavor, this 30-minute honey garlic butter shrimp and broccoli recipe is just what you need. It’s a perfect combination of succulent shrimp, crisp-tender broccoli, and a sweet and savory sauce that will have your taste buds dancing. Plus, it’s a breeze to make, making it an ideal choice for busy weeknights when you want something satisfying but don’t have a lot of time to spare.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cups of broccoli florets
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Cooked rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning with salt and pepper.

  2. Cook the Broccoli: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add a tablespoon of butter and the broccoli florets. Sauté for 4-5 minutes until the broccoli is crisp-tender. Remove the broccoli from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. Cook the Shrimp: In the same skillet, add the remaining butter and minced garlic. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until the garlic is fragrant. Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.

  4. Make the Sauce: While the shrimp is cooking, mix the honey, soy sauce, and lemon juice in a small bowl. Once the shrimp is cooked, pour the honey mixture over the shrimp in the skillet. Toss the shrimp in the sauce until they are well coated. If you like a bit of heat, sprinkle in some red pepper flakes at this point.

  5. Add the Broccoli: Return the sautéed broccoli to the skillet and toss everything together, ensuring the shrimp and broccoli are evenly coated with the honey garlic butter sauce.

  6. Serve: Once everything is heated through, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the honey garlic butter shrimp and broccoli over cooked rice or quinoa.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a low-carb option, serve the shrimp and broccoli over cauliflower rice.
  • You can customize the sauce by adding a touch of sesame oil for an extra depth of flavor.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables like bell peppers or snap peas for a colorful and nutritious twist.

Conclusion:

This 30-minute honey garlic butter shrimp and broccoli recipe is a game-changer when it comes to quick and flavorful meals. It’s a versatile dish that you can easily modify to suit your taste preferences, and it’s a great way to incorporate more seafood and veggies into your diet. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress with its vibrant colors, delightful flavors, and the ease with which it comes together. So, the next time you’re in need of a fuss-free meal that doesn’t compromise on taste, give this recipe a try and savor every delectable bite!
