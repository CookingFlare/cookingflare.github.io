Bold and Spicy: Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry Recipe


Are you a fan of bold and spicy flavors? If so, you’re in for a treat with this Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry recipe. Bursting with vibrant spices and savory ingredients, this dish is a perfect balance of heat and flavor. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To begin this culinary adventure, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of pork loin, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, minced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

Now, let’s dive into the steps to create this mouthwatering Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry:

  1. Marinate the Pork: In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced pork loin with 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In another bowl, mix the remaining 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, gochujang, sesame oil, and honey. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Sear the Pork: Heat a large skillet or wok over high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated pork and stir-fry until it’s cooked through and slightly caramelized. Remove the pork from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and ginger. Sauté for a minute until fragrant. Then, add the sliced onion and bell peppers, and stir-fry until they are slightly tender but still crisp.

  5. Combine Everything: Return the cooked pork to the skillet and pour the prepared sauce over the ingredients. Toss everything together until the pork and vegetables are evenly coated with the spicy sauce.

  6. Add the Finishing Touch: Sprinkle the chopped green onions over the stir-fry and give it a final toss to incorporate the flavors.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry to a serving dish and pair it with steamed rice. Garnish with additional green onions if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or Korean red pepper powder to the stir-fry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this recipe based on your preferences. Broccoli, mushrooms, or carrots would make delightful additions.
  • If you prefer a milder version of this dish, adjust the amount of gochujang to suit your spice tolerance.

Final Thoughts

This Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry recipe is a testament to the vibrant and robust flavors of Korean cuisine. The combination of tender pork, crisp vegetables, and the fiery gochujang sauce creates a harmony that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re preparing a weeknight meal or hosting a dinner party, this dish is guaranteed to leave a lasting impression on your guests.

So, gather your ingredients, embrace the bold and spicy flavors, and embark on a culinary journey that will surely delight your senses. Cheers to creating a masterpiece in your kitchen with this delectable Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry!
