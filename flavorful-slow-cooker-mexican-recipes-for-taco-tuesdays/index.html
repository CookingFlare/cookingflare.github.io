Flavorful Slow Cooker Mexican Recipes for Taco Tuesdays


Are you a fan of Taco Tuesdays? If so, you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some delicious and flavorful slow cooker Mexican recipes that are perfect for Taco Tuesdays. These recipes are not only easy to make, but they also pack a punch of flavor that will leave your taste buds craving for more. So, grab your slow cooker and let’s get cooking!

1. Slow Cooker Shredded Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the chopped onion, minced garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, and oregano. Sprinkle this mixture over the chicken.
  3. Pour the chicken broth into the slow cooker.
  4. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds.
  5. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  6. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and mix it with the cooking liquid.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the shredded chicken in warm tortillas with your favorite toppings such as salsa, guacamole, and shredded cheese.

2. Slow Cooker Beef Barbacoa

Ingredients:

  • 3 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup beef broth

Instructions:

  1. Place the beef chuck roast in the slow cooker.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the chopped onion, minced garlic, chipotle peppers, lime juice, apple cider vinegar, cumin, oregano, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Spread this mixture over the beef.
  3. Pour the beef broth into the slow cooker.
  4. Cook on low heat for 8-10 hours or on high heat for 4-6 hours, until the beef is tender and easily shreds.
  5. Remove the beef from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  6. Return the shredded beef to the slow cooker and mix it with the cooking liquid.
  7. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve the beef barbacoa in warm tortillas with your favorite toppings such as diced onions, cilantro, and lime wedges.

3. Slow Cooker Vegetarian Black Bean Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup vegetable broth

Instructions:

  1. Place the black beans, chopped onion, minced garlic, chopped bell pepper, minced jalapeno pepper, chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and black pepper in the slow cooker.
  2. Pour the vegetable broth into the slow cooker.
  3. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the flavors are well combined.
  4. Mash the black beans slightly with a fork to create a creamy texture.
  5. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve the vegetarian black bean mixture in warm tortillas with your favorite toppings such as diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, and sour cream.

Conclusion

Taco Tuesdays just got a whole lot tastier with these flavorful slow cooker Mexican recipes. Whether you’re a meat lover or a vegetarian, there’s a recipe for everyone to enjoy. So, dust off your slow cooker and start cooking up a storm. Your taste buds will thank you!
