Delicious Sous Vide Chicken Breast: A Step-by-Step Recipe


Are you looking for a foolproof way to achieve perfectly cooked chicken breast every time? Look no further than sous vide cooking. This technique, which involves sealing the chicken in an airtight bag and cooking it in a water bath at a precise temperature, ensures tender and juicy results that are hard to achieve through traditional cooking methods. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of preparing delicious sous vide chicken breast that will impress even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat the sous vide water bath to 145°F (63°C). This temperature will result in tender and juicy chicken breast with a slight hint of pink in the center.

Step 2: Season the chicken breasts generously with salt and pepper on both sides. This will enhance the flavor of the meat.

Step 3: Place each chicken breast in a separate vacuum-sealed bag. Add a sprig of thyme and half of the minced garlic to each bag. Drizzle with olive oil to keep the chicken moist during the cooking process.

Step 4: Seal the bags using a vacuum sealer, ensuring there is no air left inside. If you don’t have a vacuum sealer, you can use a ziplock bag and the water displacement method. Simply place the chicken and other ingredients in the bag, seal it most of the way, then slowly lower it into a sink or large container filled with water, allowing the water to push the air out of the bag. Once the air is removed, seal the bag completely.

Step 5: Gently place the sealed bags of chicken breast into the preheated water bath. Make sure the bags are fully submerged. Use a clip or weight to keep the bags from floating.

Step 6: Cook the chicken breast in the sous vide water bath for 1 to 1.5 hours. This longer cooking time allows the meat to reach the desired temperature evenly.

Step 7: Once the cooking time is complete, carefully remove the bags from the water bath. The chicken breasts may appear pale at this stage, but don’t worry, we’ll add some color in the next step.

Step 8: Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a tablespoon of olive oil. Remove the chicken breasts from the bags and pat them dry with a paper towel. Sear the chicken in the hot skillet for about 1 minute on each side, or until a golden-brown crust forms.

Step 9: Transfer the seared chicken breasts to a cutting board and let them rest for a few minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a moist and flavorful final result.

Step 10: Slice the chicken breast against the grain into thin, even slices. This will result in tender and easy-to-eat pieces.

Step 11: Serve the delicious sous vide chicken breast with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh salad. The possibilities are endless!

Enjoy the restaurant-quality chicken breast that you’ve prepared in the comfort of your own kitchen. The sous vide cooking method guarantees tender and succulent results every time. Impress your family and friends with this simple yet elegant dish that will leave them asking for seconds. Happy cooking!

Sous vide cooking has revolutionized the way we approach cooking meat, and this delicious sous vide chicken breast recipe is a prime example of that. By following these step-by-step instructions, you can achieve perfectly cooked chicken breast that is tender, juicy, and bursting with flavor. Give it a try and elevate your culinary skills to new heights.
