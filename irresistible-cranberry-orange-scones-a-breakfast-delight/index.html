Irresistible Cranberry Orange Scones: A Breakfast Delight


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked scones wafting through the kitchen in the morning. The combination of tart cranberries and zesty orange in these irresistible scones is a surefire way to start your day on a delicious note. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this simple yet indulgent recipe is bound to become a breakfast favorite. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s dive into the delightful world of cranberry orange scones.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed orange juice

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat and Prepare

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and orange zest. Add the cold butter cubes to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

Step 3: Add the Cranberries

Gently fold the dried cranberries into the flour and butter mixture until evenly distributed.

Step 4: Combine the Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, egg, vanilla extract, and orange juice until well combined.

Step 5: Bring it All Together

Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour the wet mixture into it. Use a fork to gently mix the ingredients until a shaggy dough forms.

Step 6: Shape the Scones

Transfer the dough to a floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together. Pat the dough into a circle that’s about 1 inch thick.

Step 7: Cut and Bake

Using a sharp knife or a bench scraper, cut the dough into 8 wedges. Transfer the scones to the prepared baking sheet and brush the tops with a little cream for a golden finish.

Step 8: Bake to Perfection

Bake the scones in the preheated oven for 15-18 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

Step 9: Enjoy and Indulge

Allow the scones to cool on a wire rack for a few minutes before serving. These cranberry orange scones are best enjoyed warm, perhaps with a dollop of clotted cream and a drizzle of honey. The combination of tangy cranberries, citrusy orange, and buttery scone is simply irresistible.

Final Thoughts

These cranberry orange scones are a delightful treat that perfectly balances sweet and tangy flavors. Whether you’re hosting a brunch or simply craving a special breakfast, these scones are sure to impress. The best part? They can be made in advance and frozen, so you can enjoy a freshly baked scone anytime you please. So, next time you’re in the mood for a breakfast delight, give these irresistible cranberry orange scones a try and savor the simple pleasures of homemade baking.
