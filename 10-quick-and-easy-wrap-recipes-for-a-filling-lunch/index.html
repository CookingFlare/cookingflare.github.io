10 Quick and Easy Wrap Recipes for a Filling Lunch


Are you tired of the same old boring lunch options? Look no further! In this post, I will share with you 10 quick and easy wrap recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings and keep you feeling full throughout the day. These wraps are not only delicious but also packed with wholesome ingredients that will provide you with the energy you need to power through your afternoon. So, let’s get started!

1. Greek Chicken Wrap

This Mediterranean-inspired wrap is a perfect combination of flavors. Start by marinating grilled chicken breast in a mixture of lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and oregano. Once cooked, slice the chicken and place it on a whole wheat wrap. Top with chopped cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, feta cheese, and a drizzle of tzatziki sauce. Roll it up tightly and enjoy!

2. Veggie Hummus Wrap

For a vegetarian option, try this flavorful wrap filled with fresh veggies and creamy hummus. Spread a generous amount of hummus on a spinach wrap and layer with sliced cucumbers, bell peppers, carrots, and avocado. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and black pepper for extra flavor. Roll it up and savor the deliciousness!

3. Buffalo Chicken Wrap

If you’re a fan of spicy food, this wrap is for you. Toss cooked chicken breast in buffalo sauce and place it on a tortilla wrap. Add shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced celery, and crumbled blue cheese. Drizzle with ranch dressing for a creamy finish. Roll it up tightly and brace yourself for a burst of fiery flavor!

4. BLT Wrap

Classic flavors come together in this timeless wrap. Start with a whole wheat wrap and spread a layer of mayo or aioli. Layer with crispy bacon, sliced tomatoes, and crunchy lettuce. For an extra kick, you can add sliced avocado or a sprinkle of black pepper. Roll it up and enjoy the simplicity of this delicious creation!

5. Thai Peanut Wrap

For those who love the unique flavors of Thai cuisine, this wrap is a must-try. Spread peanut sauce on a tortilla wrap and top with cooked chicken or tofu, shredded cabbage, sliced bell peppers, and chopped cilantro. Add a squeeze of lime juice for a tangy twist. Roll it up and transport yourself to the streets of Bangkok!

6. BBQ Pulled Pork Wrap

Indulge in the smoky goodness of BBQ pulled pork in this mouthwatering wrap. Start with a tortilla wrap and layer with tender pulled pork, tangy BBQ sauce, sliced red onions, and coleslaw. Roll it up tightly and sink your teeth into the perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors!

7. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Take a trip to the Mediterranean with this vibrant and nutritious wrap. Spread a layer of hummus on a whole wheat wrap and top with sliced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, and a sprinkle of dried oregano. Roll it up and savor the taste of the Mediterranean sun!

8. Italian Caprese Wrap

Capture the essence of Italy with this fresh and flavorful wrap. Start with a spinach wrap and layer with sliced mozzarella cheese, ripe tomatoes, fresh basil leaves, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. Sprinkle with salt and black pepper to taste. Roll it up and revel in the simplicity of Italian cuisine!

9. Smoked Salmon Wrap

Indulge in the rich and smoky flavors of this gourmet wrap. Spread cream cheese on a tortilla wrap and layer with smoked salmon, sliced red onions, capers, and fresh dill. Squeeze some lemon juice for a burst of citrusy goodness. Roll it up and treat yourself to a taste of luxury!

10. Mexican Fiesta Wrap

Spice up your lunchtime with this fiesta-inspired wrap. Start with a tortilla wrap and spread a layer of refried beans. Top with cooked ground beef or shredded chicken, diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, sliced jalapeños, and grated cheddar cheese. Add a dollop of sour cream for a creamy finish. Roll it up and let the flavors dance on your taste buds!

There you have it – 10 quick and easy wrap recipes that are guaranteed to make your lunchtime more exciting and satisfying. Whether you’re a meat lover, a vegetarian, or an adventurous eater, there’s a wrap for everyone on this list. So, grab your favorite ingredients and get rolling!
