Vegan Pantry Pick-Me-Ups: 30 Quick & Hearty Recipes to Revitalize Your Meals


In the hustle and bustle of everyday life, it can be tough to find time for a sit-down meal. With a well-stocked vegan pantry, you can create quick and hearty dishes that will nourish your body and revive your meals. Below are 30 mouthwatering recipes to help you feel revitalized and satisfied any day of the week.

1. Spicy Mango Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 small red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine ingredients in a medium bowl, gently tossing to mix.
  2. Serve immediately with tortilla chips or use as a topping for tacos.

2. Chickpea & Sweet Potato Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C).
  2. Toss chickpeas and sweet potato with olive oil, cumin, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet and roast for 25 minutes, until potato is tender and chickpeas are golden.
  3. Allow to cool slightly before adding red onion. Toss to combine and serve.

3. Lentil and Vegetable Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 head cauliflower, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1/4 cup peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté onions in olive oil over medium heat until soft and translucent.
  2. Add in curry powder and cumin, cooking for 2 minutes to release flavors.
  3. Add lentils, coconut milk, vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes, stirring to combine.
  4. Add vegetables and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until lentils and vegetables are cooked through and tender. Season with salt and pepper.

4. Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and set aside.
  3. Meanwhile, halve peppers lengthwise and remove seeds.
  4. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil over medium heat until soft.
  5. Add quinoa, black beans, corn, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to the onion mixture, stirring until combined.
  6. Stuff each pepper with the quinoa mixture and place in a baking dish.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes, or until peppers are tender.

These tantalizing recipes will have you feeling revitalized in no time, with minimal effort and a fully-stocked vegan pantry. From spicy salsa to hearty curry, you’re sure to find something that suits your fancy. Enjoy, and happy cooking!
