Savory Sausage and Apple Stuffing: A Must-Have for Thanksgiving


Thanksgiving is a time for coming together with loved ones and savoring delicious, comforting dishes. One such dish that holds a special place on the Thanksgiving table is the savory sausage and apple stuffing. This classic side dish combines the rich flavors of sausage, the sweetness of apples, and the earthiness of herbs to create a mouthwatering accompaniment to your turkey. In this blog post, we’ll dive into the art of crafting the perfect savory sausage and apple stuffing that will have your guests reaching for second helpings.

Ingredients

To start off, let’s gather the essential ingredients for this delectable stuffing:

  • 1 pound of ground sausage
  • 1 loaf of day-old bread, cubed
  • 2 apples, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh sage, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preparation: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.

  2. Cook the Sausage: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground sausage until it’s browned and no longer pink. Break it up into crumbles as it cooks. Once done, remove the sausage from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same skillet, melt the butter and add the diced onion, celery, and garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant.

  4. Combine Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked sausage, sautéed vegetables, diced apples, fresh herbs, and cubed bread. Toss everything together until well mixed.

  5. Add Liquid: In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and broth. Pour this mixture over the stuffing and gently toss to ensure even distribution of the liquid.

  6. Bake: Transfer the stuffing mixture into the prepared baking dish. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the top is golden brown and crispy.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the stuffing is done, allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving. Pair it with your Thanksgiving turkey and watch as your guests delight in the harmonious flavors of the savory sausage and apple stuffing.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian twist, omit the sausage and use a mix of wild mushrooms for a robust, earthy flavor.
  • Experiment with different types of bread, such as sourdough or cornbread, to add a unique texture and taste to your stuffing.
  • If you prefer a bit of sweetness, consider adding a handful of dried cranberries or apricots to the mix.

The Perfect Thanksgiving Side

Savory sausage and apple stuffing is a timeless Thanksgiving classic that never fails to impress. The combination of savory sausage, sweet apples, and aromatic herbs creates a symphony of flavors that perfectly complements the star of the show – the Thanksgiving turkey. Whether you’re hosting a large gathering or having an intimate feast, this stuffing is a must-have for your holiday table. So, this Thanksgiving, treat your loved ones to the warmth and comfort of this irresistible dish and create lasting memories around the dinner table. Happy cooking, and happy Thanksgiving!
