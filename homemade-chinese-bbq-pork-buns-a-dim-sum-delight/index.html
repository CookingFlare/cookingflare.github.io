Homemade Chinese BBQ Pork Buns: A Dim Sum Delight


If you’re a fan of dim sum, then you’ve probably tasted the deliciousness of Chinese BBQ pork buns. These fluffy, steamed buns filled with sweet and savory BBQ pork are a true delight for the taste buds. And the best part? You can easily make them at home! In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the process of creating your own homemade Chinese BBQ pork buns that are sure to impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup lukewarm water

For the BBQ pork filling:

  • 1 pound BBQ pork, diced
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon five-spice powder

For the glaze:

  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Slowly add the lukewarm water to the dry ingredients while stirring continuously. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. While the dough is rising, prepare the BBQ pork filling. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the diced BBQ pork until it starts to brown.
  6. In a small bowl, mix together the hoisin sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, sesame oil, and five-spice powder. Add this mixture to the skillet with the pork and stir until well coated. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
  7. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down to release the air. Transfer it back to the floured surface and divide it into 12 equal pieces.
  8. Take one piece of dough and flatten it into a small circle with your hands or a rolling pin. Spoon about 1 tablespoon of the BBQ pork filling into the center of the dough circle.
  9. Gather the edges of the dough and pinch them together to seal the filling inside. Repeat this process with the remaining dough pieces and filling.
  10. Place the filled buns on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving some space between them to allow for rising. Cover with a damp cloth and let them rest for about 15 minutes.
  11. Preheat your steamer. Once the buns have rested, brush the tops with the beaten egg to give them a shiny glaze.
  12. Steam the buns for 15-20 minutes, or until they are puffed up and cooked through.
  13. Remove the buns from the steamer and let them cool slightly before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Chinese BBQ pork buns are perfect as a snack, appetizer, or even a main course. Serve them with a side of soy sauce, chili oil, or your favorite dipping sauce. They can be enjoyed hot or at room temperature.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have instant yeast, you can use active dry yeast instead. Just make sure to activate it in lukewarm water before adding it to the dry ingredients.
  • If you prefer a more pronounced BBQ flavor, you can marinate the pork in the sauce mixture for a few hours or overnight before cooking it.
  • You can also add some diced green onions or cilantro to the filling for an extra burst of freshness.
  • Leftover BBQ pork buns can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Just reheat them in a steamer or microwave before serving.

Now that you have the recipe for homemade Chinese BBQ pork buns, it’s time to get cooking! Gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and soon you’ll be enjoying these delightful dim sum treats in the comfort of your own home. So go ahead, indulge in the flavors of Chinese cuisine and impress your loved ones with your culinary skills. Happy cooking!
