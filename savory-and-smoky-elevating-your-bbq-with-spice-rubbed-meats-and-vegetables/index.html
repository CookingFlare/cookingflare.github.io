Savory and Smoky: Elevating Your BBQ with Spice-rubbed Meats and Vegetables


Barbecue season is upon us, and what better way to impress your guests than with savory and smoky spice-rubbed meats and vegetables? Elevate your BBQ game with these flavorful recipes that will leave your taste buds craving for more. From juicy and tender ribs to grilled vegetables bursting with flavor, these recipes will take your BBQ experience to the next level.

Spice-Rubbed Ribs

Ingredients:

  • 2 racks of baby back ribs
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the paprika, brown sugar, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Rub the spice mixture all over the racks of ribs, making sure to coat them evenly.
  4. Place the ribs on the grill and cook for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the meat is tender and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. Remove the ribs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Cut between the bones and serve with your favorite BBQ sauce.

Smoky Grilled Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, toss together the sliced bell peppers, zucchini, yellow squash, and red onion.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil over the vegetables and sprinkle with smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and black pepper. Toss to coat evenly.
  4. Place the vegetables on the grill and cook for about 8-10 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly charred, turning occasionally.
  5. Remove the vegetables from the grill and serve as a side dish or as a topping for your spice-rubbed meats.

Spice-Rubbed Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt.
  3. Add the chicken cubes to the bowl and toss to coat them evenly with the spice mixture.
  4. Thread the chicken cubes onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  5. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred, turning occasionally.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve with a side of your favorite dipping sauce.

Conclusion

With these savory and smoky spice-rubbed meats and vegetables, your BBQ will become an unforgettable culinary experience. The combination of spices and grill marks will tantalize your senses and keep your guests coming back for more. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and enjoy the flavors of summer with these delicious recipes. Happy grilling!
