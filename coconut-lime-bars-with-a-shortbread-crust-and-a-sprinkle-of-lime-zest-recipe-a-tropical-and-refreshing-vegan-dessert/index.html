Coconut Lime Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Sprinkle of Lime Zest Recipe: A Tropical and Refreshing Vegan Dessert


Coconut Lime Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Sprinkle of Lime Zest Recipe: A Tropical and Refreshing Vegan Dessert

Coconut Lime Bars are the perfect combination of tropical flavors and sweet indulgence. With a buttery shortbread crust, a creamy coconut lime filling, and a sprinkle of fresh lime zest, these bars will transport you to a sunny beach with every bite. The best part? They’re completely vegan!

Ingredients

For the Shortbread Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • ½ cup vegan butter, chilled and cubed

For the Coconut Lime Filling:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • ½ cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon lime zest
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

For the Lime Zest Topping:

  • Fresh lime zest, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and powdered sugar for the shortbread crust. Add the chilled vegan butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or your hands to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until you achieve a crumbly texture.

  3. Press the shortbread crust mixture into the bottom of the greased baking dish, ensuring an even layer. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 15 minutes or until lightly golden brown.

  4. While the crust is baking, prepare the coconut lime filling. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the coconut milk, lime juice, lime zest, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook the mixture, stirring constantly, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

  5. Once the crust is done baking, remove it from the oven and pour the coconut lime filling over the hot crust. Smooth the top with a spatula to ensure an even layer.

  6. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are golden brown.

  7. Remove the baking dish from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature. Once cooled, refrigerate the bars for at least 2 hours, or overnight, to allow them to set.

  8. When ready to serve, sprinkle the bars with fresh lime zest for an extra burst of tangy flavor and a pop of vibrant color.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra tropical twist, you can add shredded coconut to the shortbread crust or sprinkle it on top of the lime filling before baking.

  • If you prefer a sweeter dessert, you can increase the amount of granulated sugar in the coconut lime filling to suit your taste.

  • To make these bars gluten-free, you can substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend of your choice.

  • These coconut lime bars are best enjoyed chilled, so make sure to keep them refrigerated until serving.

  • Serve these bars as a refreshing dessert at summer parties, picnics, or any occasion where you want to impress your guests with a taste of the tropics.

Coconut Lime Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Sprinkle of Lime Zest are a delightful vegan dessert that captures the essence of a tropical paradise. The rich coconut flavor combined with the tangy lime creates a refreshing treat that will transport you to sunny shores with each bite. Enjoy the taste of summer all year round with these indulgent bars!
