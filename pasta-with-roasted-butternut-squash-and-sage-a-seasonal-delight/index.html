Pasta with Roasted Butternut Squash and Sage: A Seasonal Delight


As the leaves start to turn golden and the air becomes crisp, it’s the perfect time to indulge in the rich and comforting flavors of fall. One dish that captures the essence of the season is pasta with roasted butternut squash and sage. This delightful recipe brings together the sweetness of roasted squash, the earthy aroma of sage, and the hearty goodness of pasta to create a truly satisfying meal.

Ingredients

To start off this culinary journey, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces of your favorite pasta (penne or farfalle work well)
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh sage leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Roast the Butternut Squash

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the diced butternut squash on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Toss to coat evenly, then spread the squash out in a single layer. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the squash is tender and caramelized around the edges.

Step 2: Cook the Pasta

While the butternut squash is roasting, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, or until fragrant. Be careful not to let it brown. Next, add the roasted butternut squash to the skillet and mash it with a fork or potato masher to create a rough puree. Pour in the broth and let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 4: Finish the Dish

Once the sauce has thickened slightly, stir in the heavy cream and add the cooked pasta to the skillet. Toss everything together until the pasta is well coated with the creamy butternut squash sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, you can add a splash of pasta cooking water to loosen it up.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

To elevate the dish, tear a few fresh sage leaves and scatter them over the pasta. The aromatic herb will infuse the dish with its distinctive flavor. Finally, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta just before serving for a savory finish.

Enjoy the Seasonal Flavors

Pasta with roasted butternut squash and sage is a celebration of autumn on a plate. The tender sweetness of the squash, the earthy fragrance of sage, and the creamy decadence of the sauce come together to create a dish that is both comforting and elegant. Whether you’re hosting a fall dinner party or simply craving a taste of the season, this recipe is sure to delight your taste buds and warm your soul.

So, embrace the fall season with this delightful pasta dish, and let the flavors of roasted butternut squash and sage transport you to a cozy autumn evening.

In conclusion, Pasta with Roasted Butternut Squash and Sage is a delightful recipe that captures the essence of the fall season. The combination of sweet roasted squash, fragrant sage, and creamy sauce makes this dish a comforting and satisfying choice for any autumn occasion. Try it out and experience the magic of seasonal flavors in every bite!
