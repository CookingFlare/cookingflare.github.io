Spiced Dairy-Free Cinnamon Rolls Recipe


If you’re a fan of cinnamon rolls but prefer to avoid dairy, then this spiced dairy-free cinnamon rolls recipe is perfect for you! These rolls are soft, fluffy, and packed with warm cinnamon and aromatic spices. The best part? They’re completely dairy-free, making them suitable for those with dietary restrictions or preferences. So, let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to indulge in a delicious treat!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • ¼ cup coconut oil, melted
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the filling:

  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil, softened

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the almond milk, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Mix well.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until a dough forms. If the dough is too sticky, add a little more flour, one tablespoon at a time, until it becomes manageable.

  5. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and knead it gently for a few minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  6. Roll out the dough into a rectangular shape, about ¼ inch thick.

  7. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves for the filling.

  8. Spread the softened coconut oil evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  9. Sprinkle the sugar and spice mixture over the coconut oil, ensuring it covers the entire surface.

  10. Starting from one of the longer sides, tightly roll the dough into a log shape.

  11. Using a sharp knife, slice the log into 12 equal-sized rolls.

  12. Place the rolls in the prepared baking dish, leaving a little space between each roll to allow them to expand.

  13. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the rolls are golden brown and cooked through.

  14. While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, almond milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  15. Once the rolls are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  16. Drizzle the glaze over the warm cinnamon rolls, allowing it to seep into the crevices.

  17. Serve the spiced dairy-free cinnamon rolls warm and enjoy!

These spiced dairy-free cinnamon rolls are a delightful treat that can be enjoyed any time of the day. The combination of warm cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves creates a comforting aroma and flavor that will surely satisfy your cravings. Plus, with the substitution of dairy-free ingredients, you can indulge in these delicious rolls without any worries. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to bake a batch of these mouthwatering cinnamon rolls. Trust us, they won’t disappoint!
