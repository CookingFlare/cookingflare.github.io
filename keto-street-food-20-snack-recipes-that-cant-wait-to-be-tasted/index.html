Keto Street Food: 20 Snack Recipes That Can't Wait to Be Tasted


Are you ready to tantalize your taste buds with some of the most exquisite and delectable keto street food recipes? With the rise in popularity of the ketogenic diet, more and more people are looking for easy, tasty, and satisfying low-carb snacks to enjoy on the go. Here are 20 mouth-watering recipes that you simply can’t wait to taste!

  1. Keto Egg Rolls

    Craving a crunchy and savory bite? These keto egg rolls are sure to satisfy! Start by filling a lettuce leaf with equal parts cream cheese and diced green chilies. Add shredded chicken, and for texture, include chopped bell peppers and shredded carrots. Wrap it up, and secure with a toothpick. Dip it in sugar-free salsa for that extra kick!

  2. Keto Chocolate Chip Cookies

    Don’t miss out on dessert just because you’re on a keto diet! Whip up a batch of low-carb chocolate chip cookies by combining almond flour, butter, vanilla extract, eggs, and erythritol (a sugar alcohol that doesn’t spike blood sugar). Add chunky, sugar-free chocolate chips and bake for 12-15 minutes at 350°F. Let them cool, and enjoy!

  3. Keto Jalapeño Poppers

    Forget about ordering from the appetizer menu – DIY your jalapeño poppers with these keto-friendly recipes! Simply hollow out the peppers and fill them with a combination of cream cheese, cheddar cheese, and diced bacon. Wrap with thin bacon slices, and secure with toothpicks. Bake at 400°F for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon reaches desired crispiness.

  4. Crispy Avocado Fries

    For a crispy and crunchy snack on the keto diet, try these incredible crispy avocado fries. Slice avocados into fries, then coat them in a mixture of almond flour, crushed pork rinds, and spices. Fry them in hot oil for 3-4 minutes until golden brown, and serve with a side of garlic aioli or ranch dressing.

  5. Keto Chicken Satay Skewers

    Go for a spin on the exotic side with these delightful chicken satay skewers. Marinate chicken throughout with coconut aminos, ground ginger, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes. Thread marinated chicken onto skewers and bake at 400°F for 15-20 minutes, and serve with a side of keto peanut sauce.

  6. Keto Stuffed Mushrooms

    Impress your dinner guests by serving these fancy yet effortless stuffed mushrooms! Remove the stems of fresh mushrooms and mix them with butter, garlic, and herbs until well combined. Fill the mushroom caps generously, sprinkle them with parmesan cheese, and bake at 375°F for 20 minutes.

  7. Cheesy Broccoli Soup

    Who says you can’t enjoy soup on keto? Take on this comforting and cheesy broccoli soup. Simply chop up broccoli and sauté it in butter, then blend the mixture with heavy cream and a dash of garlic powder. Season it with salt and pepper to taste, and enjoy this creamy delight!

  8. Low-Carb Quesadillas

    Enjoy this crowd-pleasing classic with these low-carb recipes! Use a large leaf of lettuce as your tortilla and fill it with a generous amount of shredded cheese and your favorite toppings (e.g., salsa, cooked chicken, sautéed peppers). Warm it up in a skillet and watch it melt together!

  9. Keto Fried Pickles

    Feeling adventurous? Spice up snack time with these zesty fried pickles. Dip pickle spears in a mixture of egg and almond flour, then coat them in crushed pork rinds. Fry in hot oil for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown, and serve with a side of ranch dressing.

  10. Keto Hummus and Veggies

    No need to miss out on all things Mediterranean – make this tasty hummus and veggie platter. Blend together tahini, lemon juice, garlic cloves, olive oil, and chickpea flour to make your hummus. Serve it with an assortment of sliced low-carb veggies, like bell peppers, cucumbers, and jicama.

  11. Sugar-Free Fruit Roll-Ups

    Want something sweet and portable? These sugar-free fruit roll-ups are perfect for busy lives! Blend together your favorite berries, line a baking sheet with parchment paper, and spread the mixture onto it evenly. Dehydrate it at 145°F for 6 hours or until dry and leathery, then roll up!

  12. Keto Cauliflower Wings

    A twist on a classic, this low-carb take on a popular dish will be a hit at your next game night or party. Toss bite-sized cauliflower florets in butter, garlic, and parmesan cheese, then bake at 425°F for 20-25 minutes. For extra flavor, mix in your favorite hot sauce.

  13. Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries

    For a lighter, veggie-packed snack, try these baked parmesan zucchini fries. Slice zucchinis into fries and toss them with an egg wash and grated parmesan cheese. Place on a lined baking sheet and bake at 425°F for 20-25 minutes, then broil them for an extra crispy texture.

  14. Keto Chia Pudding

    Silky, sweet, and lusciously creamy, chia pudding is a dessert you’ll want to explore. Combine unsweetened almond milk and vanilla chia seeds in a jar, give it a good stir, and let it set overnight in the refrigerator.In the morning, top it with your favorite keto-friendly fruits and nuts for a delightful breakfast or snack.

  15. Keto Lactose-Free Mac and Cheese

    Cheese lovers, rejoice! Indulge in this luscious keto take on a classic comfort food. Whisk together heavy cream, grated cheese, nutritional yeast, and mustard powder. Pour the mixture onto low-carb noodles or zucchini noodles, and bake at 350°F for 20 minutes.

  16. Keto Vegetable Spring Rolls

    Enjoy the delightful crunch of spring rolls without the carb load! Fill blanched and thinly-sliced vegetables into rice paper wraps, and roll them tightly. Serve them with a side of peanut sauce or a spicy mayo for an extra kick.

  17. Savory Keto Fat Bombs

    Want to feel fully satisfied without consuming carbs? Try savory fat bombs! Combine cream cheese, shredded cheese, and bacon bits, and form them into small balls. Roll them in Everything Bagel seasoning or crushed pork rinds, and refrigerate until set.

  18. Cheesy Keto Nachos

    Taco Tuesdays can still happen on keto – by trying these delicious low-carb nachos! Use a generous amount of Chipotle Chicken Salad on a large leaf of lettuce, and top it off with cheese, jalapeños, diced tomatoes, and sour cream, then torch it lightly until bubbly.

  19. Keto Falafels

    Bring a taste of the Middle East to your kitchen – all without the carbs! Pulse together canned chickpeas (and liquid), chopped herbs, and spices in a food processor. Shape spoonfuls of the mixture into balls and bake at 375°F for 30 minutes. Serve with a side of cucumber, tomato, and avocado salad.

  20. Keto Pesto Stuffed Tomatoes

    Fresh, savory, and packed with flavor, this delightful recipe will turn a simple tomato into an exquisite delicacy. Preheat oven to 400°F and scoop out the center of your favorite variety of tomato. Combine the discarded pulp with softened cream cheese, grated parmesan cheese, and homemade or store-bought pesto. Stuff the tomato with the mixture, and place on a lined baking sheet. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and the tomatoes are tender.

These 20 scrumptious keto street food recipes are perfect for satisfying cravings and giving your taste buds the excitement they deserve. From savory dishes to sweet treats, there’s no need to feel deprived of delicious variety on a keto diet. So, grab your ingredients and start snacking your way through this tantalizing list today! Happy cooking!
