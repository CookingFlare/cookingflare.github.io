Refreshing Nut-Free Watermelon Salad Recipe

3 min · 583 words

Summer is the perfect time to enjoy light, refreshing dishes that are both satisfying and nutritious. One of the most delightful options is a watermelon salad. This vibrant dish combines the sweetness of watermelon with tangy and savory elements, creating a symphony of flavors that is sure to please your taste buds. Best of all, this recipe is nut-free, making it suitable for those with nut allergies. Let’s dive into how to prepare this delicious and refreshing watermelon salad.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 small seedless watermelon, cubed (about 8 cups)
  • 1 cup of cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup of red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh mint leaves, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup of feta cheese, crumbled (optional)

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice (about 1 lime)
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Watermelon

  1. Wash the watermelon thoroughly under running water.
  2. Cut the watermelon into quarters. Using a sharp knife, remove the rind and cut the flesh into bite-sized cubes.
  3. Place the watermelon cubes in a large mixing bowl.

Step 2: Prepare the Vegetables and Herbs

  1. Wash and dice the cucumber into small pieces. Add it to the bowl with the watermelon.
  2. Thinly slice the red onion and add it to the bowl.
  3. Roughly chop the fresh mint and basil leaves and add them to the bowl. These herbs will add a refreshing and aromatic touch to the salad.

Step 3: Prepare the Dressing

  1. In a small bowl, combine the extra virgin olive oil, lime juice, and honey.
  2. Whisk the ingredients together until well combined.
  3. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the sweetness or acidity as needed by adding more honey or lime juice.

Step 4: Assemble the Salad

  1. Pour the dressing over the watermelon mixture.
  2. Gently toss the ingredients together to ensure the watermelon, cucumber, red onion, mint, and basil are evenly coated with the dressing.
  3. If using, sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top of the salad. The feta adds a creamy, salty contrast to the sweet watermelon.

Step 5: Chill and Serve

  1. Cover the salad with plastic wrap or transfer it to an airtight container.
  2. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to meld together beautifully.
  3. Serve chilled as a refreshing side dish or appetizer at your next summer gathering.

Tips and Variations

  • Vegan Option: Omit the feta cheese or use a plant-based feta alternative.
  • Extra Crunch: Add some diced bell peppers or radishes for additional texture and flavor.
  • Herb Variations: Experiment with other herbs like cilantro or parsley for a different twist.
  • Make Ahead: This salad can be prepped a few hours in advance and kept refrigerated until ready to serve.

Nutritional Information

Here’s a general overview per serving (assuming 6 servings):

  • Calories: ~150
  • Protein: 2g
  • Carbohydrates: 20g
  • Fat: 7g
  • Fiber: 2g
  • Sugar: 15g

Why This Salad Works

The combination of sweet watermelon, crisp cucumber, and aromatic herbs creates a harmonious blend of flavors that is both refreshing and satisfying. The lime-honey dressing adds a zesty sweetness that ties everything together, while the optional feta cheese provides a creamy, salty contrast that enhances the overall taste experience.

Perfect for Any Occasion

Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, attending a potluck, or simply looking for a light meal on a hot day, this watermelon salad is a versatile and crowd-pleasing option. It’s quick and easy to prepare, making it a convenient choice for busy days. Plus, its vibrant colors and fresh ingredients make it a visually appealing addition to any table.

Conclusion

This refreshing nut-free watermelon salad is a delightful way to enjoy the flavors of summer. With its combination of juicy watermelon, crunchy cucumber, tangy red onion, and fragrant herbs, all brought together by a simple yet flavorful dressing, it’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Give it a try at your next gathering and watch it disappear in no time. Enjoy the taste of summer with this delicious and healthy salad!
