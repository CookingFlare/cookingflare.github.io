Tasty Baked Omelette Recipes to Feed a Crowd


If you’re looking for a delicious and convenient way to feed a large group of people, baked omelettes are the perfect solution. Not only are they easy to make, but they can be customized to suit a variety of tastes, making them a crowd-pleaser for any occasion. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering baked omelette recipes that are sure to impress your guests and leave them coming back for more.

Classic Baked Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 12 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined.
  3. Stir in the cheese, ham, bell peppers, onions, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the omelette is set and lightly golden on top.
  5. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

This classic baked omelette is a timeless crowd-pleaser that combines fluffy eggs with savory fillings. It’s perfect for brunch, lunch, or even dinner, and can be served with a side of toast or a fresh salad.

Mediterranean Baked Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 10 eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/4 cup diced sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream until smooth.
  3. Stir in the feta cheese, spinach, sun-dried tomatoes, black olives, basil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the omelette is puffed up and golden.
  5. Allow it to cool slightly before cutting into squares and serving.

This Mediterranean-inspired baked omelette is a flavorful and colorful option that brings the taste of the Mediterranean right to your table. It’s a great choice for a special brunch or a light dinner gathering.

Tex-Mex Baked Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup cooked and crumbled chorizo
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well blended.
  3. Stir in the cooked chorizo, bell peppers, tomatoes, pepper jack cheese, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the omelette is set and the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  5. Let it cool for a few minutes before cutting into squares and serving.

This Tex-Mex baked omelette is a zesty and satisfying dish that brings the flavors of the Southwest to your table. It’s perfect for a festive brunch or a casual dinner with friends and family.

Conclusion

Baked omelettes are a versatile and convenient option for feeding a crowd, and these recipes are just the beginning. Feel free to get creative and customize your baked omelette with your favorite fillings and flavors. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, a lunch gathering, or a dinner party, these tasty baked omelette recipes are sure to be a hit with your guests. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to impress everyone with your culinary skills!
