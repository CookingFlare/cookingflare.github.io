Candy Corn Rice Krispies Treats: A Festive Halloween Dessert Recipe


As the spooky season approaches, our homes are filled with the sweet aroma of Halloween treats. Among the many festive desserts, Rice Krispies treats are a classic favorite that can be easily customized to fit the theme. In this recipe, we’ll be making Candy Corn Rice Krispies treats that are sure to delight both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rice Krispies cereal
  • 1 cup marshmallows
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter or margarine
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup yellow candy coating or melted white chocolate
  • 1/2 cup orange candy coating or melted orange candy melts
  • 1/4 cup red candy coating or melted red candy melts
  • 1 tablespoon shortening (such as Crisco)
  • Candy corn for decoration (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Rice Krispies mixture: In a large pot or saucepan, melt the butter or margarine over low heat. Add the marshmallows and stir until they are completely melted and the mixture is smooth. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  2. Add the Rice Krispies cereal: Quickly add the Rice Krispies cereal to the pot and stir until the cereal is evenly coated with the marshmallow mixture.
  3. Cool the mixture: Let the mixture cool slightly until it is still warm but not hot. This is important, as you want the mixture to be cool enough to handle but still warm enough to shape easily.
  4. Shape the treats: Use a greased spatula or your hands to shape the mixture into desired shapes. You can make squares, rectangles, or even use a cookie cutter to create fun shapes.
  5. Color the treats: To create the candy corn effect, you’ll need to color the treats with yellow, orange, and red candy coating or melted candy melts. You can use a spatula or your hands to apply the color.
  6. Shape the candy corn stripes: Use a spatula or your hands to shape the colored treats into long, thin strips. You can also use a knife or a cookie cutter to create fun shapes.
  7. Assemble the treats: To assemble the Candy Corn Rice Krispies treats, you’ll need to stack the colored strips on top of each other, creating a candy corn effect. You can use a dab of shortening to hold the strips in place.
  8. Decorate with candy corn (optional): If desired, you can decorate the treats with candy corn pieces or sprinkles to add an extra touch of festivity.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the treats more festive, you can use edible markers or candy melts to draw on jack-o’-lantern faces or other Halloween-themed designs.
  • If you find that the colored candy coating or melted candy melts are too sticky, you can try mixing in a small amount of shortening to thin them out.
  • To make the treats more durable, you can press them into a greased pan or a silicone mat to help them hold their shape.

Conclusion

Candy Corn Rice Krispies treats are a fun and easy Halloween dessert recipe that’s perfect for parties, trick-or-treaters, or just a fun treat for the family. With their colorful candy corn stripes and festive shape, they’re sure to be a hit with both kids and adults alike. So why not give them a try this Halloween season?
