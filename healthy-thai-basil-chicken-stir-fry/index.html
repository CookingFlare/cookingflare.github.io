Healthy Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry


Enjoy the vibrant flavors of Thailand with this delicious and healthy Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry recipe. Packed with aromatic herbs, savory chicken, and colorful vegetables, this dish is a perfect balance of flavors and textures that will delight your taste buds. Plus, it’s quick and easy to make, making it an ideal choice for busy weeknights when you want a nutritious meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken breast with soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Mix well and let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes.

  2. Stir Fry: Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over high heat. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  3. Cook the Chicken: Add the marinated chicken to the pan and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.

  4. Add Vegetables: Toss in the sliced red and yellow bell peppers, and onion. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  5. Finish with Basil: Add fresh basil leaves to the pan and stir-fry for another minute until the basil is wilted.

  6. Serve: Remove from heat and serve the Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry hot over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with extra basil leaves for an added burst of flavor.

  7. Enjoy: Dive into this delightful dish and savor the explosion of Thai flavors in every bite. The combination of tender chicken, crunchy vegetables, and aromatic basil is sure to leave you craving more.

Health Benefits:

  • Protein: Chicken is a great source of lean protein, essential for muscle growth and repair.
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Bell peppers are rich in vitamins A and C, while garlic offers various health benefits, including boosting the immune system.
  • Antioxidants: Fresh basil is known for its antioxidant properties, helping to protect cells from damage and reduce inflammation.

Final Thoughts:

This Healthy Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry recipe is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nutritious meal option that you can enjoy guilt-free. With its colorful array of ingredients and aromatic flavors, this dish is a surefire way to add a touch of Thai cuisine to your dining table. Try it out for your next family dinner or gathering and watch as everyone falls in love with this captivating dish!

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and having fun in the kitchen. So, don’t be afraid to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. Whether you like it spicy, extra garlicky, or loaded with veggies, feel free to make it your own. Happy cooking and bon appétit!
