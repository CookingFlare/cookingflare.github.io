Budget-Friendly Indian Salad Recipes: Spicy and Affordable


As the world becomes increasingly health-conscious, salads have become a staple in many cuisines. Indian cuisine, in particular, offers a wide variety of salad recipes that are not only delicious but also budget-friendly. In this post, we will explore some of the most popular and spicy Indian salad recipes that won’t break the bank.

1. Rajasthani Kadhi Salad

Rajasthani Kadhi is a popular Indian dish made with yogurt, gram flour, and spices. In this salad recipe, we will use the same ingredients to create a refreshing and spicy salad.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked gram flour
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1-2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1-2 teaspoons green chili sauce
  • 2-3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the cooked gram flour, yogurt, cilantro, mint leaves, onions, tomatoes, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, and salt.
  2. Squeeze the lemon juice and green chili sauce over the salad and mix well.
  3. Drizzle the vegetable oil over the salad and serve immediately.

2. Mumbai-Style Papaya Salad

Papaya is a popular fruit in India, and when combined with spices and herbs, it makes for a delicious and healthy salad. This Mumbai-style papaya salad is a twist on the classic fruit salad.

3. South Indian Cucumber Salad

Conclusion

Indian salads are not only delicious but also budget-friendly. These four salad recipes are a great way to incorporate the flavors of India into your meal routine. From the spicy kick of the Rajasthani Kadhi Salad to the refreshing twist of the South Indian Cucumber Salad, there’s something for everyone. So next time you’re looking for a healthy and affordable meal option, give one of these Indian salad recipes a try!
