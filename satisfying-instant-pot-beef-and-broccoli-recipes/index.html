Satisfying Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Recipes


If you’re looking for a quick, nutritious, and utterly satisfying meal, beef and broccoli is a timeless choice that checks all the boxes. With the advent of the Instant Pot, preparing this classic dish has become simpler and faster than ever. This blog post will guide you through several delectable Instant Pot beef and broccoli recipes, ensuring that dinner is not only effortless but bursting with flavor.

The Allure of Instant Pot Cooking

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we approach cooking. With its ability to pressure cook, slow cook, sauté, and steam, it has become a staple in many kitchens. The convenience of this multi-cooker allows you to create dishes that are usually time-consuming in mere minutes. For a dish like beef and broccoli, which traditionally requires careful timing to keep the beef tender and the broccoli crisp, the Instant Pot shines.

Why Beef and Broccoli?

Beef and broccoli is a beloved dish that originates from Chinese cuisine, combining tender beef slices with vibrant green broccoli florets in a savory sauce. The dish is not only delicious but also offers a balance of protein and vegetables, making it a healthy choice. Plus, it’s easily customizable, allowing you to adjust flavors and ingredients according to your preferences.

Essential Ingredients

Before diving into the recipes, let’s gather the essential ingredients. While the classic dish features a few key components, you can also add a personal twist by incorporating additional ingredients.

Core Ingredients:

  • Beef: Flank steak is a popular choice due to its tenderness, but sirloin or ribeye can also work well.
  • Broccoli: Fresh broccoli florets provide both color and nutrition.
  • Soy Sauce: A foundational flavor that adds umami and depth to the dish.
  • Garlic and Ginger: These aromatics are crucial for enhancing the overall flavor profile.
  • Beef Broth: Helps to create a flavorful base for the sauce.
  • Cornstarch: Used to thicken the sauce for a satisfying consistency.

Optional Add-ins:

  • Bell Peppers: For added sweetness and color.
  • Carrots: For a touch of sweetness and crunch.
  • Sesame Oil: A drizzle at the end adds a nutty flavor.
  • Red Pepper Flakes: For those who enjoy a bit of heat.

Classic Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb flank steak, sliced thinly against the grain
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prep the Beef: Slice the flank steak against the grain into thin strips. This helps to ensure tenderness.

  2. Sauté: Turn on the Instant Pot and set it to the sauté function. Add the vegetable oil, and once hot, add the sliced beef. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.

  3. Build the Sauce: In the same pot, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the soy sauce, beef broth, and brown sugar, stirring to combine.

  4. Pressure Cook: Return the beef to the pot, ensuring it’s coated with the sauce. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.

  5. Add Broccoli: When the cooking time is up, carefully release the pressure. Open the lid and add the broccoli florets. Stir to combine, then close the lid for an additional 2 minutes on high pressure.

  6. Thicken the Sauce: Release the pressure again and remove the lid. Stir in the cornstarch mixture and let it cook for another minute until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Serve: Serve the beef and broccoli hot over cooked rice, drizzling with sesame oil if desired.

Sweet and Spicy Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli

For those who love a bit of heat, this version adds a sweet and spicy kick to the traditional recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb sirloin steak, sliced thinly
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Cooked rice for serving
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Steak: Slice the sirloin steak thinly, against the grain.

  2. Sauté: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add the vegetable oil, then the steak. Cook for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove and set aside.

  3. Create the Sauce: Add garlic and ginger to the pot, sautéing for about 30 seconds. Stir in the soy sauce, beef broth, honey, and sriracha.

  4. Pressure Cook: Return the beef to the pot, then seal the lid and set to high pressure for 10 minutes.

  5. Broccoli Time: Once done, quickly release the pressure and add the broccoli. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for an additional 2 minutes.

  6. Thicken and Serve: Release pressure, add cornstarch mixture, and let it cook for another minute. Serve over rice, garnished with sesame seeds.

Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli with Bell Peppers

This recipe adds bell peppers for extra color and flavor, making it a vibrant dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ribeye steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced (red, yellow, or green)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Slice the Meat: Thinly slice the ribeye steak against the grain.

  2. Sauté the Beef: In the Instant Pot, set the sauté function and add the vegetable oil. When hot, add the beef and sauté for 2-3 minutes. Remove from the pot.

  3. Sauté Aromatics: Add garlic and ginger to the pot, cooking until fragrant. Stir in the soy sauce, beef broth, and brown sugar.

  4. Pressure Cook: Add the beef back to the pot and close the lid. Set to high pressure for 10 minutes.

  5. Add Veggies: After the timer goes off, quickly release the pressure and add broccoli and bell pepper slices. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 2 more minutes.

  6. Thicken and Serve: Release pressure, mix in the cornstarch slurry, and let it thicken. Serve over rice.

Tips for Perfecting Your Beef and Broccoli

  1. Cut Against the Grain: Always slice your beef against the grain to ensure tenderness.

  2. Marinate for Flavor: If time permits, marinate your beef in soy sauce, garlic, and ginger for an hour or overnight for enhanced flavor.

  3. Don’t Overcook the Broccoli: To maintain its vibrant color and crunch, keep a close eye on the cooking time for the broccoli.

  4. Adjust the Sauce: Feel free to tweak the sauce ingredients based on your taste preferences. If you like it sweeter, add more brown sugar or honey; if you prefer it saltier, increase the soy sauce.

  5. Cooked Rice is Key: Serve your beef and broccoli over freshly cooked rice. The rice soaks up the delicious sauce, making every bite satisfying.

Conclusion

Instant Pot beef and broccoli recipes are a game-changer for busy weekdays or any time you want a quick and satisfying meal. The ease of preparation, combined with the ability to lock in flavors, makes this dish a favorite among many. By experimenting with different variations and ingredients, you can keep this meal exciting and new. Whether you stick to the classic recipe or opt for something with a twist, you’re guaranteed a delicious plate that’s sure to please everyone at the table.

So, gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to indulge in this mouthwatering dish that brings the essence of restaurant dining right into your home. Happy cooking!
