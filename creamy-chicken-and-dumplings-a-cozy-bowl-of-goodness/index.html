Creamy Chicken and Dumplings: A Cozy Bowl of Goodness


Are you craving a comforting meal that will warm your heart and soul? Look no further than creamy chicken and dumplings! This classic dish is the epitome of comfort food, with tender chicken, fluffy dumplings, and a creamy broth that will make you feel like you’re wrapped in a cozy blanket.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the dumplings:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, sliced carrots, and celery. Cook until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the minced garlic, dried thyme, and bay leaf to the pot. Stir to combine and cook for another minute.

  3. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then add them to the pot. Cook until the chicken is browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, making sure it covers the chicken completely. Bring the broth to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  5. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the dumplings. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the milk, stirring until a sticky dough forms.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.

  7. Increase the heat to medium-high and bring the broth back to a boil. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling dough into the boiling broth, spacing them evenly. Cover the pot and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the dumplings are cooked through and puffy.

  8. Stir in the heavy cream and season the broth with additional salt and pepper to taste. Remove the bay leaf.

  9. Serve the creamy chicken and dumplings hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy the warm and comforting flavors!

Tips and Variations

  • To add more flavor to the dish, you can sauté the vegetables in butter instead of olive oil.

  • Feel free to add other vegetables like peas or corn to the pot for extra color and texture.

  • For a healthier version, you can use boneless, skinless chicken thighs instead of chicken breasts.

  • If you prefer a thicker broth, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a little water and stir it into the pot during the last few minutes of cooking.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop or in the microwave, adding a splash of broth or water to loosen the consistency if needed.

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with creamy chicken and dumplings. This cozy bowl of goodness will warm you from the inside out, making it the perfect meal for chilly days or when you simply need a little pick-me-up. Give this recipe a try and let the creamy broth, tender chicken, and fluffy dumplings transport you to food heaven!
