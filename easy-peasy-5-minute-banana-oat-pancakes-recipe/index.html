Easy Peasy: 5-Minute Banana Oat Pancakes Recipe


Are you looking for a quick and delicious breakfast option that will satisfy your cravings and keep you energized throughout the day? Look no further than this Easy Peasy 5-Minute Banana Oat Pancakes recipe! These pancakes are not only incredibly tasty but also packed with nutritious ingredients that will fuel your body and leave you feeling satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk (any type you prefer)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Cooking oil or butter for greasing the pan

Instructions

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the ripe banana, rolled oats, eggs, milk, vanilla extract, cinnamon (if using), baking powder, and salt. Blend until smooth and well combined. The batter should have a thick, but pourable consistency.

  2. Heat a non-stick frying pan or skillet over medium heat. You can lightly grease the pan with cooking oil or butter to prevent the pancakes from sticking.

  3. Once the pan is hot, pour approximately 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the pan for each pancake. You can make them smaller or larger, depending on your preference.

  4. Cook the pancakes for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. You will notice small bubbles forming on the surface of the pancakes, indicating that they are ready to be flipped.

  5. Carefully flip the pancakes using a spatula and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes on the other side.

  6. Once cooked, transfer the pancakes to a plate and repeat the process with the remaining batter until it is all used.

  7. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, maple syrup, honey, yogurt, or nut butter. Get creative and experiment with different combinations to find your perfect pancake topping!

Tips and Variations

  • To add a little extra flavor and texture, you can mix in some chopped nuts, chocolate chips, or shredded coconut into the batter before cooking the pancakes.

  • If you prefer a gluten-free version of these pancakes, make sure to use certified gluten-free oats.

  • These pancakes can be made ahead of time and stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat them in a toaster or microwave before serving.

  • If you have any leftover pancakes, you can freeze them for later. Just place them in a freezer bag or container and store them for up to 2 months. To reheat, simply pop them in the toaster or microwave until warmed through.

Final Thoughts

With just a handful of ingredients and a few minutes of your time, you can enjoy a stack of fluffy and nutritious pancakes. This Easy Peasy 5-Minute Banana Oat Pancakes recipe is perfect for busy mornings or lazy weekends when you want a delicious breakfast without spending hours in the kitchen.

These pancakes are not only a crowd-pleaser but also a great way to sneak in some extra nutrients. The combination of ripe banana, oats, and eggs provides a good balance of carbohydrates, protein, and fiber, making them a wholesome and filling choice.

So, the next time you’re craving pancakes, give this recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed!
