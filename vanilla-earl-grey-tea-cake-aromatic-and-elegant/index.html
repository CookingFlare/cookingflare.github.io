Vanilla Earl Grey Tea Cake: Aromatic and Elegant


Delight your taste buds with a touch of elegance and the enchanting aroma of the popular floral and citrusy blend - the Vanilla Earl Grey Tea Cake. Still craving for traditional cakes? Fear not, for this fusion recipe is a perfect balance of both worlds - classic flavors and modern creativity. This aromatic and elegant cake is bound to become a staple in your dessert repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Cake:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Half a teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 3 tablespoons loose Earl Grey tea leaves or 3 Earl Grey tea bags
For the Syrup:
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup Earl Grey tea, brewed and cooled
For the Glaze:
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Earl Grey tea, brewed and cooled
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Preheat and Prepare

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease and flour two 7-inch round cake pans.
  2. Brew a cup of Earl Grey tea using loose leaves or tea bags, and let it cool to room temperature. Strain the tea leaves and reserve the tea.

Mix the Dry Ingredients

  1. In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

Cream the Butter and Sugar

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter and sugar using an electric mixer on medium speed until pale and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition to fully incorporate.

Incorporate the Wet Ingredients

  1. Add the vanilla extract and mix well. Stir in the reserved cooled Earl Grey tea, ensuring it’s combines thoroughly.
  2. Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture, alternating with the buttermilk. Mix on low speed or using a spatula until you obtain a smooth batter.
  3. Finally, gently fold in the loose tea leaves to infuse the batter with additional Earl Grey flavor.

Bake the Cakes

  1. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans.
  2. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool for a few minutes in the pans, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Prepare the Syrup

  1. While the cakes are cooling, whisk together the granulated sugar and cooled Earl Grey tea in a small saucepan. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  2. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and let it cool.

Assemble the Cake

  1. Brush the tops of the cakes with the Earl Grey syrup, ensuring an even layer seeps in.
  2. For the glaze, whisk together the powdered sugar, cooled Earl Grey tea, and vanilla extract. Drizzle the glaze over the cakes, letting it slowly cascade down the sides.

Serve and Savor

  1. Slice, serve, and savor the aromatic and elegant Vanilla Earl Grey Tea Cake. Sit back, and let your guests marvel at this superb blend of delicate flavors.

A true poetic confluence of contrasting but harmonizing flavors, our Vanilla Earl Grey Tea Cake shall transport you to a blissful escape with each bite. As the Earl Grey fragrance gracefully dances with the sweet vanilla, let your heart and taste buds enchant in this culinary wonderland.
