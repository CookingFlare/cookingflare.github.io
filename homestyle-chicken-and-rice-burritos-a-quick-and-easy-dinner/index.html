Homestyle Chicken and Rice Burritos: A Quick and Easy Dinner


Looking for a delicious and satisfying dinner that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further than these homestyle chicken and rice burritos! Packed with flavor, protein, and wholesome ingredients, these burritos are sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight or hosting a casual get-together with friends, this recipe is a winner. Let’s dive into the details of creating these mouthwatering burritos without further ado!

Ingredients

For the Chicken and Rice Filling:

  • 2 cups of cooked white rice
  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breast, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup of corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime

For the Assembly:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Sliced avocado
  • Chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken and Rice Filling:

    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken, chopped onion, and minced garlic. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink and the onion is softened.
    • Add the ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir to coat the chicken and onion evenly with the spices.
    • Stir in the diced tomatoes, black beans, and corn kernels. Cook for a few minutes until the mixture is heated through.
    • Add the cooked white rice to the skillet and gently fold it into the chicken and vegetable mixture.
    • Squeeze the lime juice over the filling and sprinkle the chopped cilantro on top. Give it a final stir, then remove the skillet from the heat.

  2. Assemble the Burritos:

    • Lay a large flour tortilla on a clean surface. Spoon a generous portion of the chicken and rice filling onto the center of the tortilla.
    • Sprinkle a handful of shredded cheese over the filling.
    • Add a dollop of sour cream, a spoonful of salsa, a few slices of avocado, and a sprinkle of chopped cilantro.
    • Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up into a burrito.

  3. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Place the assembled burritos on a hot skillet or griddle, seam side down. Cook for a few minutes on each side until they are golden brown and the cheese is melted.
    • Transfer the burritos to a serving plate and garnish with extra salsa, sour cream, and avocado slices if desired.
    • Serve the burritos hot and enjoy every delicious bite!

Tips and Variations

  • For a time-saving option, you can use leftover cooked chicken or rotisserie chicken in place of cooking the chicken from scratch.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese such as pepper jack or queso fresco for a unique flavor twist.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a dash of hot sauce or sprinkle some chopped jalapenos into the filling mixture.

Final Thoughts

These homestyle chicken and rice burritos are a fantastic way to bring the flavors of a comforting home-cooked meal to the convenience of a handheld wrap. With a balance of protein, carbs, and veggies all wrapped up in a tasty tortilla, this recipe is a winner for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. So, next time you’re craving a quick and easy dinner, give these delicious burritos a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
