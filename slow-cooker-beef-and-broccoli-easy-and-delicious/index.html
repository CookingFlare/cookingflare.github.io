Slow Cooker Beef and Broccoli: Easy and Delicious


If you’re a fan of Chinese takeout, then you’re going to love this slow cooker beef and broccoli recipe. It’s a fantastic way to enjoy the flavors of your favorite dish in the comfort of your own home, and the best part is that it requires minimal effort. The slow cooker does all the work for you, infusing the beef and broccoli with mouthwatering flavors while you go about your day. Let’s dive into the details of this easy and delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds flank steak, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the beef broth, soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, and minced garlic. This savory-sweet marinade is the key to infusing the beef with irresistible flavor.

  2. Place the sliced flank steak in the slow cooker, and pour the marinade over the top. Gently toss to ensure the beef is well-coated. Set the slow cooker to low heat, cover, and let it cook for 4-5 hours. The low and slow cooking process will result in tender, melt-in-your-mouth beef.

  3. About 30 minutes before the beef is done, add the broccoli florets to the slow cooker. This allows the broccoli to soak up the delicious sauce and become tender-crisp.

  4. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with a few tablespoons of the cooking liquid from the slow cooker to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the slow cooker, and let the mixture thicken for about 15 minutes.

  5. Once the beef and broccoli are done, serve them over a bed of cooked rice or noodles. The tender beef, vibrant broccoli, and rich sauce are a match made in culinary heaven.

  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for a pop of color and added flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the marinade.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can increase the amount of cornstarch in the slurry.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding other vegetables such as bell peppers, carrots, or snap peas.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This slow cooker beef and broccoli recipe is a game-changer for busy individuals who crave a homemade meal without the fuss. The convenience of the slow cooker allows you to set it and forget it, returning to a kitchen filled with the tantalizing aroma of beef and broccoli. Plus, the tender beef and vibrant broccoli coated in a savory, slightly sweet sauce make for a truly satisfying meal.

Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight dinner or hosting a casual gathering, this recipe is sure to impress. It’s a crowd-pleaser that brings the comforting flavors of Chinese takeout to your dinner table with minimal effort.

So, the next time you’re craving the classic combination of beef and broccoli, let your slow cooker work its magic and treat yourself to this easy and delicious meal. It’s a foolproof way to enjoy a homemade version of a beloved dish, and once you try it, you’ll find yourself turning to this recipe time and time again. Cheers to simple, flavorful cooking!
