Wholesome and Nourishing Middle Eastern Chicken Soups


Middle Eastern cuisine is known for its bold flavors, aromatic spices, and hearty dishes. One particular category that stands out is the Middle Eastern chicken soups. These soups are not only delicious but also incredibly nourishing, making them the perfect choice for a wholesome meal. In this blog post, we will explore three different Middle Eastern chicken soup recipes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds and warm your soul.

1. Lebanese Chicken Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 cup of vermicelli pasta
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, and sauté until they become translucent.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  3. Add the carrots, celery, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to coat the chicken and vegetables with the spices.
  4. Pour in enough water to cover the chicken and vegetables, and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred the meat using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  6. Add the vermicelli pasta to the pot and cook until it is tender.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve the Lebanese chicken soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

2. Moroccan Chicken Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup of chickpeas, soaked overnight and cooked
  • 1 cup of green lentils, rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  3. Add the tomatoes, chickpeas, lentils, turmeric, cumin, cinnamon, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Pour in enough water to cover the chicken and vegetables, and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred the meat using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve the Moroccan chicken soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

3. Turkish Chicken and Rice Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of long-grain rice
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, and sauté until they become soft and golden.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  3. Add the rice, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to coat the rice and chicken with the spices.
  4. Pour in enough water to cover the chicken and rice, and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred the meat using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve the Turkish chicken and rice soup hot, garnished with fresh dill.

These wholesome and nourishing Middle Eastern chicken soups are not only flavorsome but also easy to prepare. They are perfect for those cold winter nights or when you’re in need of a comforting meal. Each soup has its unique blend of spices and ingredients that will transport you to the vibrant streets of the Middle East. So why not give these recipes a try and indulge in the goodness of Middle Eastern cuisine? Your taste buds will thank you!
