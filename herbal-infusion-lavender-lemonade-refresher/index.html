Herbal Infusion: Lavender Lemonade Refresher


Welcome to another delightful culinary journey! Today, we’re going to learn how to make a soothing, refreshing and utterly rejuvenating Lavender Lemonade Refresher. If you’ve ever been on the prowl for a unique twist on a classic summertime treat, then you’re going to love this herbal infusion recipe.

Our lavender lemonade refresher is easy to prepare, and you’ll be amazed at how the uplifting aroma of lavender dance magically in your glass! The fusion of tart lemon and calming lavender is an unexpected yet delightful taste experience. A refreshing study break, a rejuvenating pre-workout sip, or that perfect pick-me-up at the end of a long day.

To achieve a revitalizing herbal infusion, start by gathering a handful of ingredients from your kitchen and garden:

  • 2 cups fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried lavender
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Now let’s get cooking!

  1. Making the lavender sugar syrup:

    First things first, we’re going to create our signature lavender sugar syrup. Combine 1 cup of sugar and 1 cup of water in a medium saucepan. Stir over low-medium heat until the sugar dissolves. Once the mixture starts boiling, remove from heat and stir in 1 tablespoon of dried lavender. To fully infuse the lavender aroma and flavor, let it sit for about 30 minutes.

  2. Preparing the lemon juice:

    While your lavender sugar syrup infuses, prepare your lemon juice. Squeeze 2 cups of fresh lemon juice to use in our refresher, and don’t discard those lemon rinds. They come in handy in our next step.

  3. Lavender Lemonade Simple Syrup:

    Here comes the interesting part! Strain the lavender-infused sugar syrup, and retain the lavender sugar for garnishing later. Add the lemon rinds to the syrup, followed by 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract. Stir to combine and then let it steep for 30 more minutes. Feel free to also stick your nose in the pot and take in the calming fragrance of the lavender!

  4. Assembling the Lavender Lemonade Refresher:

    The hard part is over! Now, it’s a simple blend. For every 2 cups of lavender-lemon rind steeped syrup, mix with 2 cups of fresh lemon juice. Dilute it with 3-4 cups of water depending on how concentrated you like your lemonade. Give it a gentle stir and sieve out the lemon rinds.

And voilà! Your sparkling lavender lemonade refresher is ready to serve!

Garnish with a sprinkle of the lavender sugar and a couple of fresh lemon slices. For that extra flair, choose a glass filled with ice and experiment with garnishing options like fresh raspberries, lavender sprigs, or a twist of lemon zest.

Make this herbal infusion your own by adding other botanicals like thyme, mint, or even rose petals! Just remember, less is more when combining botanicals. The final aroma and flavor should harmonize without overpowering each other.

Take a break from the regular, and welcome a new refreshing beverage into your life! Your lemonade will never be the same again. Happy cooking!
