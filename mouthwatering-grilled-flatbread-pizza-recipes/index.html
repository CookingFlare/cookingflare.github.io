Mouthwatering Grilled Flatbread Pizza Recipes


When it comes to outdoor cooking, few things can rival the deliciousness of grilled flatbread pizza. The char from the grill combined with your favorite toppings creates a symphony of flavors that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, enjoying a cozy family night, or looking to impress friends with your culinary skills, grilled flatbread pizzas are a fantastic choice. In this blog post, we’ll dive into a variety of mouthwatering recipes that will elevate your pizza game, along with tips and tricks for achieving the perfect grilled flatbread pizza.

The Art of Flatbread Pizza

What is Flatbread Pizza?

Flatbread pizza is a versatile dish that uses flatbread as its base instead of traditional pizza dough. This allows for a quicker preparation time and a lighter, crispier texture. Flatbreads can range from pita to naan, or you can even make your own from scratch. The beauty of flatbread pizza lies in its adaptability—toppings can be as simple or as elaborate as your taste buds desire.

Why Grill Flatbread Pizza?

Grilling flatbread pizza infuses a unique smoky flavor that baking in an oven simply can’t replicate. The high heat from the grill creates a crisp crust while keeping the toppings perfectly cooked. Plus, grilling is a social event in itself, making it a great way to enjoy cooking with friends and family.

Essential Ingredients for Grilled Flatbread Pizza

Before diving into the recipes, let’s outline some essential ingredients you’ll need:

Flatbreads

  • Store-Bought Options: Pita, naan, or lavash are readily available and work beautifully.
  • Homemade Flatbreads: For the adventurous, making your own flatbreads can be a rewarding experience. A basic recipe includes flour, water, olive oil, and salt.

Toppings

  • Sauces: Traditional tomato sauce, pesto, barbecue sauce, or olive oil and garlic.
  • Cheeses: Mozzarella is a classic choice, but feel free to experiment with goat cheese, feta, or even a sprinkle of parmesan.
  • Vegetables: Fresh tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, spinach, mushrooms, and zucchini are all excellent options.
  • Proteins: Grilled chicken, pepperoni, sausage, or even chickpeas for a vegetarian option.
  • Herbs and Spices: Fresh basil, oregano, red pepper flakes, and arugula can add depth to your pizzas.

Grilled Flatbread Pizza Recipes

1. Classic Margherita Flatbread Pizza

This timeless recipe highlights the simplicity of fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of naan or pita bread
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill: Heat your grill to medium-high.
  2. Prepare the Flatbreads: Brush one side of the flatbreads with olive oil.
  3. Grill the Flatbreads: Place the oiled side down on the grill for about 2 minutes or until grill marks appear.
  4. Add Toppings: Flip the flatbreads and spread a layer of tomato sauce. Add mozzarella slices and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Grill Until Cheese Melts: Close the grill lid and cook for another 3-5 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Finish with Basil: Remove from the grill, top with fresh basil, and drizzle with olive oil before slicing.

2. BBQ Chicken Flatbread Pizza

This recipe combines smokiness from the grill with sweet and tangy BBQ sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of flatbread (pita or naan)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill: Heat your grill to medium-high.
  2. Mix Chicken and BBQ Sauce: In a bowl, combine shredded chicken and BBQ sauce until well coated.
  3. Prepare the Flatbreads: Brush the flatbreads lightly with olive oil.
  4. Grill the Flatbreads: Place the flatbreads on the grill for 2 minutes.
  5. Add Toppings: Remove from the grill, then spread the BBQ chicken mixture evenly. Top with cheddar cheese and red onion slices.
  6. Grill Until Cheese is Melty: Return to the grill for 3-5 minutes until the cheese is melted and the edges are crispy.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Top with fresh cilantro and enjoy!

3. Mediterranean Veggie Flatbread Pizza

A vibrant and fresh option loaded with vegetables and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of flatbread (pita or lavash)
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • Black olives, pitted and sliced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill: Heat your grill to medium-high.
  2. Spread Hummus: On one side of each flatbread, spread a generous layer of hummus.
  3. Grill the Flatbreads: Place the flatbreads hummus-side down on the grill for 2 minutes.
  4. Add Toppings: Remove from the grill and layer cherry tomatoes, cucumber, feta, and olives on top.
  5. Grill Again: Return to the grill for an additional 2-3 minutes to warm the toppings and slightly melt the feta.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle with fresh parsley before slicing and serving.

4. Spicy Pepperoni and Jalapeño Flatbread Pizza

For those who love a kick of heat, this recipe is not to be missed.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of flatbread (pita or naan)
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 fresh jalapeño, thinly sliced
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill: Heat your grill to medium-high.
  2. Grill the Flatbreads: Brush one side of each flatbread with olive oil and grill for 2 minutes.
  3. Add Sauce and Toppings: Flip the flatbreads, spread marinara sauce, and top with mozzarella, pepperoni, and jalapeño slices.
  4. Grill Until Cheese Melts: Close the grill lid and cook for another 3-5 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  5. Serve with Spice: Optionally, sprinkle crushed red pepper flakes before slicing and serving.

5. Fig and Prosciutto Flatbread Pizza

This gourmet option combines sweet and savory flavors for a sophisticated twist.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of flatbread (pita or naan)
  • 1/2 cup fig jam
  • 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
  • 4 oz prosciutto, thinly sliced
  • Fresh arugula
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill: Heat your grill to medium-high.
  2. Spread Fig Jam: On each flatbread, spread a layer of fig jam.
  3. Grill the Flatbreads: Grill the flatbreads for about 2 minutes until slightly charred.
  4. Add Cheese and Prosciutto: Remove from the grill and sprinkle goat cheese and prosciutto over the jam.
  5. Grill Again: Return to the grill for another 2-3 minutes to warm the toppings.
  6. Garnish with Arugula: Top with fresh arugula and drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

Tips for Perfect Grilled Flatbread Pizza

  • Preheat the Grill: Always preheat your grill to ensure even cooking and prevent sticking.
  • Use a Pizza Stone: If you have one, using a pizza stone can help evenly distribute heat and prevent the flatbreads from burning.
  • Don’t Overload: While it’s tempting to pile on toppings, too many can make the flatbread soggy. Aim for a balanced topping-to-crust ratio.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Don’t be afraid to mix and match ingredients. Try different cheeses, sauces, and toppings based on your preferences.
  • Timing is Key: Keep an eye on your pizza while grilling to avoid burning. Each grill is different, so adjust cooking times as necessary.

Conclusion

Grilled flatbread pizza is a culinary adventure that invites creativity and experimentation. With a plethora of topping combinations and the smoky flavor from the grill, it’s a dish that everyone can enjoy. Whether you’re in the mood for something traditional like a Margherita or something unique like fig and prosciutto, these recipes will surely tantalize your taste buds. So fire up that grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to enjoy some mouthwatering grilled flatbread pizzas!
