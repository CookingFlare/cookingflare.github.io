Irresistible Bananas Foster Pancakes


Who can resist the heavenly combination of bananas, caramel, and fluffy pancakes? Today, I’m excited to share with you a recipe for Irresistible Bananas Foster Pancakes that will take your breakfast game to a whole new level. These pancakes are decadent, flavorful, and guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. So, let’s dive right in and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Bananas Foster Topping:

  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough pancakes.

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Once hot, ladle about 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown. Repeat with the remaining batter.

  5. While the pancakes are cooking, prepare the Bananas Foster topping. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sliced bananas and cook for 1-2 minutes, until slightly softened.

  6. Sprinkle the brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and salt over the bananas. Stir until the sugar has melted and the bananas are coated in the caramel sauce.

  7. Slowly pour in the heavy cream and stir until the sauce thickens slightly. This should take about 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.

  8. To serve, stack the pancakes on a plate and generously spoon the Bananas Foster topping over them. The warm caramel sauce will seep into the pancakes, adding a delightful sweetness with every bite.

  9. Optional: If you want to take these pancakes to the next level, you can add a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of chopped nuts on top. It’s all about indulgence!

Conclusion

There you have it—Irresistible Bananas Foster Pancakes that will make your taste buds dance with joy. The combination of fluffy pancakes and rich, caramelized bananas is simply irresistible. Whether you’re looking to impress guests at a brunch or treat yourself to a special breakfast, these pancakes are sure to be a hit.

So, the next time you have some ripe bananas on hand, don’t let them go to waste. Instead, whip up a batch of these mouthwatering pancakes and indulge in a truly delightful breakfast experience. Trust me, you won’t regret it!

Happy cooking and bon appétit!
