Decadent Tiramisu Layer Cake Recipe: A Classic Italian Indulgence


If you’re a fan of the classic Italian dessert, Tiramisu, and you also love indulging in a slice of moist, flavorful cake, then this Decadent Tiramisu Layer Cake recipe is the perfect fusion of two beloved treats. This delightful dessert combines the rich flavors of coffee, cocoa, and mascarpone cheese into a luxurious layer cake that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee

For the Tiramisu Filling:

  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream

For the Coffee Soaking Syrup:

  • 1/2 cup brewed strong coffee
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur (optional)

For the Topping:

  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate shavings
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cake Layers

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour three 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the buttermilk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Stir in the hot coffee until the batter is smooth.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 2: Prepare the Tiramisu Filling

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat the mascarpone cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until well combined.

Step 3: Prepare the Coffee Soaking Syrup

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the brewed coffee and granulated sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Remove the syrup from the heat and stir in the coffee liqueur, if using. Allow the syrup to cool to room temperature.

Step 4: Assemble the Cake

  1. Place one cake layer on a serving plate and poke holes in the top using a fork.
  2. Brush the cake layer with a generous amount of the coffee soaking syrup.
  3. Spread half of the Tiramisu filling over the soaked cake layer.
  4. Repeat the process with the second cake layer, syrup, and remaining Tiramisu filling.
  5. Top with the final cake layer and brush with the remaining coffee soaking syrup.
  6. Refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 5: Add the Topping

  1. Before serving, sprinkle the top of the cake with chocolate shavings and dust with cocoa powder.

Enjoy Your Decadent Tiramisu Layer Cake!

This Decadent Tiramisu Layer Cake is a show-stopping dessert that’s perfect for special occasions or any time you want to treat yourself to a little slice of indulgence. The combination of moist, tender cake layers, creamy Tiramisu filling, and a hint of coffee and cocoa makes this cake a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy your cravings for a classic Italian indulgence. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to embark on a delightful baking adventure with this Decadent Tiramisu Layer Cake recipe!
