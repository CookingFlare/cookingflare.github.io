Slow Cooker Creamy Tomato Basil Soup: A Classic Favorite


As the weather starts to cool down, there’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting bowl of soup to soothe the soul. And what better option than a classic tomato basil soup? This slow cooker recipe is a staple for a reason - it’s easy to make, packed with flavor, and can be customized to suit your taste. In this post, we’ll dive into the world of slow cooker cooking and explore the magic of creamy tomato basil soup.

The Power of Slow Cooking

Slow cooking is a game-changer for home cooks. With minimal effort, you can transform a handful of ingredients into a rich, flavorful dish that’s perfect for a chilly evening. And the best part? You can let the slow cooker do all the work while you’re busy with other tasks.

In this recipe, we’ll rely on the slow cooker’s ability to break down the tough fibers of the tomatoes and tenderize the basil, resulting in a silky-smooth soup that’s bursting with flavor. So, what are you waiting for? Let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chopped fresh tomatoes (or 1 can of diced tomatoes)
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add aromatics: Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes and broth: Add the chopped tomatoes, chicken broth, and dried oregano to the skillet. Stir to combine, then bring the mixture to a simmer.
  4. Transfer to slow cooker: Transfer the tomato mixture to a slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
  5. Blend the soup: Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup to a blender and blend in batches, then return it to the slow cooker.
  6. Add cream and basil: Stir in the heavy cream and chopped fresh basil. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve and enjoy: Ladle the soup into bowls and top with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired. Serve warm and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a creamier soup, add more heavy cream or substitute with half-and-half.
  • To add some heat, throw in a diced jalapeno or red pepper flakes.
  • Experiment with different types of tomatoes, such as cherry or grape tomatoes, for a pop of color and unique flavor.
  • Make ahead: Prepare the soup up to the blending step, then refrigerate or freeze until ready to serve.

Conclusion

There you have it - a classic slow cooker creamy tomato basil soup recipe that’s sure to become a staple in your household. With its rich, velvety texture and burst of fresh basil flavor, this soup is the perfect comfort food for any occasion. So next time you’re in the mood for something warm and cozy, give this recipe a try and experience the magic of slow cooker cooking for yourself.
