Paleo Breakfast Burrito with Scrambled Eggs and Sausage: A Filling and Healthy Option


When it comes to breakfast, many of us are looking for a meal that is not only delicious but also filling and healthy. As part of a paleo diet, it can be challenging to find options that align with our dietary restrictions. That’s why we’ve created this recipe for a paleo breakfast burrito with scrambled eggs and sausage. This dish is not only a tasty and satisfying breakfast option but also packed with nutrients and free from grains, dairy, and processed foods.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 pound paleo sausage (made from pork, turkey, or chicken)
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 paleo tortillas (made from cassava flour or coconut flour)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the coconut oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced bell peppers and onions and cook until they are tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic to the skillet and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Add the paleo sausage to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Pour the eggs over the sausage and vegetable mixture in the skillet. Scramble the eggs until they are cooked through.
  6. Warm the paleo tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  7. Assemble the burritos by spooning the scrambled egg and sausage mixture onto a tortilla, followed by any additional toppings you like (such as diced tomatoes, avocado, or salsa).

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe more substantial, add some diced cooked bacon or ham.
  • For an extra boost of protein, add some diced cooked chicken or steak.
  • If you prefer a spicier breakfast, add some diced jalapeños or serrano peppers to the skillet with the sausage and vegetables.
  • Experiment with different seasonings, such as cumin, chili powder, or smoked paprika, to add some extra flavor to the dish.

The Benefits of Paleo Breakfast Burritos

This paleo breakfast burrito with scrambled eggs and sausage is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. The eggs provide a boost of protein, while the sausage is a good source of iron and B vitamins. The bell peppers and onions add some extra fiber and vitamins, while the garlic provides some antibacterial properties. The paleo tortillas are free from grains and processed ingredients, making this dish a great option for those with dietary restrictions.

Conclusion

A paleo breakfast burrito with scrambled eggs and sausage is a filling and healthy option for breakfast. With its combination of protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates, this dish is sure to keep you satisfied until lunchtime. And with its versatility, you can customize it to your liking with your favorite toppings and seasonings. Try this recipe out and enjoy a delicious and nutritious breakfast that aligns with your paleo diet.
