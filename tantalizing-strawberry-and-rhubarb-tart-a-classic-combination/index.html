Tantalizing Strawberry and Rhubarb Tart: A Classic Combination


If you’re a fan of classic desserts that perfectly balance sweet and tangy flavors, then this tantalizing strawberry and rhubarb tart is just the recipe for you. With its delicate buttery crust and the vibrant combination of strawberries and rhubarb, this tart is sure to be a showstopper at any gathering. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and discover the magic of this classic dessert!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 cups fresh rhubarb, chopped into 1-inch pieces
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, butter, and granulated sugar. Using your fingers or a pastry cutter, blend the ingredients together until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Add the egg yolk to the mixture and mix until the dough comes together. If the dough seems too dry, add a tablespoon of cold water at a time until it reaches the right consistency.

  4. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead it gently for a minute. Then, shape it into a disk and wrap it in plastic wrap. Refrigerate the dough for at least 30 minutes.

  5. While the dough is chilling, prepare the filling. In a medium saucepan, combine the strawberries, rhubarb, granulated sugar, cornstarch, vanilla extract, and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens and the fruit softens slightly. This should take about 10-12 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  6. Take the chilled dough out of the refrigerator and roll it out on a lightly floured surface, forming a circle that’s about 12 inches in diameter.

  7. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to a 9-inch tart pan, pressing it gently against the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edges.

  8. Pour the cooled strawberry and rhubarb filling into the tart shell, spreading it evenly.

  9. Place the tart pan on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust turns golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

  10. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack. Once cooled, you can dust the top with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness.

  11. Slice and serve this tantalizing strawberry and rhubarb tart as is or with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Conclusion

With its buttery crust and the winning combination of strawberries and rhubarb, this classic tart is a delightful treat that will leave your taste buds craving more. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to indulge in a delicious dessert, this recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and get ready to enjoy the tantalizing flavors of this strawberry and rhubarb tart. Bon appétit!
