Apple Cinnamon Pancakes: A Simple and Delicious Fall Breakfast


Fall is here, and what better way to embrace the season than with a warm and comforting breakfast? These Apple Cinnamon Pancakes are the perfect treat to start your day on a sweet note. Packed with the flavors of juicy apples and aromatic cinnamon, these pancakes will delight your taste buds and fill your kitchen with the comforting scent of fall.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 medium apple, peeled, cored, and finely chopped
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon. Ensure that all the dry ingredients are well combined.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter until well blended.

  3. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough pancakes.

  4. Gently fold in the chopped apple into the pancake batter. The apple adds a delightful texture and bursts of flavor in every bite.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray.

  6. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, pour the batter onto the skillet, spreading it slightly to form a round pancake shape. Repeat this step for each pancake, ensuring to leave enough space between them for easy flipping.

  7. Cook the pancakes for about 2-3 minutes, or until small bubbles form on the surface. This indicates that the pancakes are ready to be flipped.

  8. Carefully flip the pancakes with a spatula and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  9. Remove the pancakes from the skillet and keep them warm while you cook the remaining batter.

  10. Serve the Apple Cinnamon Pancakes warm with a drizzle of maple syrup. The combination of the fluffy pancakes, tender apples, and sweet maple syrup is simply irresistible.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgence, top your pancakes with a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of powdered sugar.
  • If you prefer a more pronounced cinnamon flavor, you can increase the amount of ground cinnamon to your liking.
  • Feel free to add a handful of chopped walnuts or pecans to the pancake batter for a delightful crunch.
  • For a gluten-free version, you can substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend.
  • Experiment with different types of apples to find your favorite flavor combination. Granny Smith apples add a tartness, while Honeycrisp or Fuji apples lend a natural sweetness.

These Apple Cinnamon Pancakes are a delightful way to celebrate the flavors of fall. Whether you enjoy them on a lazy weekend morning or as a special treat during the week, they are sure to bring joy to your breakfast table. So, grab your apron and get ready to indulge in this simple and delicious fall breakfast. Happy pancake making!
