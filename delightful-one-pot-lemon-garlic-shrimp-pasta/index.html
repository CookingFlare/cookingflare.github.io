Delightful One Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

3 min · 542 words

If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that’s bursting with flavor, then look no further than this delightful one pot lemon garlic shrimp pasta recipe. This dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want something satisfying but don’t want to spend hours in the kitchen. The combination of succulent shrimp, tangy lemon, and aromatic garlic creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving for more. Plus, the best part is that it can all be made in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz of linguine or spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by preparing all your ingredients. Mince the garlic, halve the cherry tomatoes, juice and zest the lemon, and chop the parsley. This will make the cooking process smooth and efficient.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil and butter over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the pot and set them aside.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic as it can turn bitter.

  4. Add the Pasta and Broth: Add the linguine or spaghetti to the pot, followed by the chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Let the pasta cook for about 9-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it is al dente and most of the broth has been absorbed.

  5. Combine the Flavors: Once the pasta is cooked, add the cherry tomatoes, lemon juice, and lemon zest to the pot. Stir to combine all the flavors, then add the cooked shrimp back into the pot.

  6. Finish with Parmesan and Parsley: Sprinkle the freshly grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and shrimp, and gently toss everything together. Let the cheese melt and the flavors meld together. Finally, sprinkle the chopped parsley over the dish to add a fresh, herby note.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once everything is well combined and heated through, your one pot lemon garlic shrimp pasta is ready to be served. Dish it up into bowls, garnish with extra Parmesan and parsley if desired, and enjoy the delightful flavors!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes when sautéing the garlic.
  • Feel free to substitute the shrimp with chicken or even scallops for a different protein option.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can use whole wheat or gluten-free pasta, and reduce the amount of Parmesan and butter.

This delightful one pot lemon garlic shrimp pasta is a fantastic dish that combines the freshness of lemon, the savory richness of garlic, and the succulent taste of shrimp into one harmonious meal. It’s a versatile recipe that can be easily customized to suit your preferences, and it’s sure to become a go-to favorite for any pasta lover. So, the next time you’re craving a satisfying and flavorful dinner without the hassle of multiple pots and pans, give this recipe a try and savor every delightful bite!
