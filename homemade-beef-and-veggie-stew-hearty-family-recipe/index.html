Homemade Beef and Veggie Stew: Hearty Family Recipe


There’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting bowl of homemade beef and veggie stew to bring the family together. This classic dish is not only delicious and satisfying but also packed with wholesome ingredients that make it a nutritious choice for any meal. Whether you’re looking for a hearty dinner to enjoy on a chilly evening or a dish to impress your loved ones, this homemade beef and veggie stew is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

The Ingredients You’ll Need

To create this flavorful stew, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 4 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 large potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 6 cups of beef broth
  • 1 cup of red wine (optional)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 teaspoons of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Sear the Beef: Start by heating the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the beef stew meat and cook until browned on all sides. This step helps to seal in the flavor of the beef and create a rich base for the stew.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot with the seared beef. Saute until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant, infusing the beef with savory flavors.

  3. Add the Vegetables: Toss in the sliced carrots, celery, and diced potatoes. These veggies not only add vibrant colors to the stew but also contribute a wonderful depth of flavor.

  4. Pour in the Liquid: Pour in the beef broth and red wine, if using. The combination of these liquids forms the flavorful broth that will simmer with the beef and vegetables, creating a luscious stew.

  5. Season and Simmer: Add the bay leaves, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Bring the stew to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.

  6. Add the Peas: In the last 10 minutes of cooking, stir in the frozen peas to add a pop of sweetness and vibrant green color to the stew.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the stew is ready, ladle it into bowls and serve piping hot. The tender beef, flavorful broth, and hearty vegetables make this dish a complete meal that’s perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Tips for Success

  • For a deeper flavor, consider browning the beef in batches to avoid overcrowding the pot, which can lead to steaming rather than searing.
  • If you prefer a thicker stew, you can toss the beef pieces in flour before searing them, or create a slurry with cornstarch and water to thicken the stew towards the end of cooking.
  • This stew can be made in advance and tastes even better the next day as the flavors have time to meld together.

In Conclusion

Homemade beef and veggie stew is a timeless recipe that brings warmth and comfort to any table. With its rich, savory flavors and hearty ingredients, it’s a dish that’s perfect for family gatherings, cozy nights in, or any occasion that calls for a satisfying meal. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to create a memorable culinary experience with this delightful and wholesome stew.
