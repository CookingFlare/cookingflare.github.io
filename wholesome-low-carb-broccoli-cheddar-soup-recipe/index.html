Wholesome Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup Recipe


When the chill of winter settles in, there’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of soup to comfort the soul. If you’re on a low-carb diet or just looking for a healthier alternative to traditional creamy soups, this Wholesome Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup is the perfect solution. Packed with nutrition, flavor, and comfort, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Why Choose Low Carb?

Low-carb diets have gained popularity due to their effectiveness in promoting weight loss and improving overall health. By reducing carbohydrates, your body shifts into a state of ketosis, where it burns fat for energy rather than relying on glucose. This can help stabilize blood sugar levels and reduce hunger pangs. Plus, with the right ingredients, you can enjoy all the creaminess and flavor of your favorite comfort foods without the guilt.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make this deliciously creamy low carb broccoli cheddar soup, gather the following ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth or chicken broth (low sodium)
  • 1 cup heavy cream or coconut cream for a dairy-free option
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese (plus extra for garnish)
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or butter
  • Optional: A pinch of crushed red pepper flakes for a kick

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Broccoli

Start by washing your fresh broccoli thoroughly. Cut the broccoli into small florets, ensuring they’re bite-sized for easy blending later on. The stems can also be used; just peel the tough outer layer and chop them into smaller pieces.

Step 2: Sauté Aromatics

In a large pot, heat the olive oil or butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, stirring constantly to avoid burning.

Step 3: Cook the Broccoli

Add the broccoli florets and chopped stems to the pot. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth, and bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a simmer and cover the pot. Let it cook for about 10-15 minutes until the broccoli is tender.

Step 4: Blend the Soup

Once the broccoli is tender, it’s time to blend the soup. You can use an immersion blender directly in the pot for convenience or carefully transfer the soup in batches to a traditional blender. Blend until smooth, but leave some small chunks for texture if you prefer a chunkier soup.

Step 5: Add Cream and Cheese

Return the blended soup to the pot (if you used a traditional blender) and stir in the heavy cream or coconut cream. Cook over low heat, allowing the soup to warm up again, but do not let it boil. Gradually add the shredded cheddar cheese, stirring until it’s melted and fully incorporated. This will give your soup a rich, creamy texture.

Step 6: Season to Taste

At this point, season your soup with onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. If you enjoy a little heat, add a pinch of crushed red pepper flakes. Stir to combine all the flavors, then taste and adjust seasoning as needed.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Ladle the hot soup into bowls and garnish with additional shredded cheddar cheese on top. Pair it with a low-carb bread or a fresh salad for a complete meal. This wholesome broccoli cheddar soup is perfect for lunch or dinner and even makes a fantastic meal prep option.

Nutritional Benefits

This Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup is not only delicious but also packed with nutritional benefits. Let’s take a closer look at some of the key ingredients:

Broccoli

Broccoli is a cruciferous vegetable rich in vitamins K and C, fiber, and antioxidants. It supports heart health, aids digestion, and is known for its cancer-fighting properties. Plus, it’s low in carbs, making it a perfect choice for any low-carb diet.

Cheddar Cheese

Cheddar cheese is packed with protein and calcium, which are essential for healthy bones and muscles. It also adds a rich flavor to the soup, making it satisfying and delicious. By using sharp cheddar, you can enjoy a stronger flavor without needing to add too much.

Heavy Cream

Heavy cream contributes to the soup’s creamy texture and rich flavor. While it does contain fats, these are healthy fats that can aid in maintaining energy levels and keeping you full longer. If you’re looking for a dairy-free option, coconut cream provides a similar texture and richness.

Storage and Reheating Tips

Leftover soup can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. To reheat, simply pour it into a pot and warm over low heat until heated through. If the soup thickens upon sitting, you can add a splash of broth or cream to loosen it up.

Freezing the Soup

If you want to make a larger batch or save some for later, this soup freezes well. After it has cooled completely, transfer it to freezer-safe containers and store it in the freezer for up to 3 months. When you’re ready to enjoy it, simply thaw it in the refrigerator overnight and reheat as mentioned above.

Variations to Try

While the classic broccoli cheddar combination is a winner, you can easily customize your soup by adding different ingredients. Here are a few ideas:

  • Add Protein: For an extra boost, consider adding cooked chicken, turkey, or crumbled bacon to the soup.
  • Spice It Up: Experiment with different spices or herbs, such as thyme, rosemary, or cayenne pepper.
  • Change the Cheese: Try different types of cheese like gouda, mozzarella, or a spicy pepper jack for a unique twist.
  • Vegetable Boost: Incorporate other low-carb vegetables like cauliflower, zucchini, or spinach for added nutrition.

Conclusion

This Wholesome Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup is a delightful way to enjoy a comforting dish without compromising your health goals. With its creamy texture and rich flavor, it’s a perfect meal for any time of the year. Whether you’re warming up on a cold day or looking for a nutritious option, this soup is sure to satisfy.

Get creative with your ingredients, and don’t hesitate to make it your own! So grab your pot, gather your ingredients, and start cooking up this deliciously healthy soup today. Your taste buds will thank you!
