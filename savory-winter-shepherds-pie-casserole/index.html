Savory Winter Shepherd's Pie Casserole


Welcome to the ultimate comfort food experience! This savory winter shepherd’s pie casserole is a hearty and delicious dish that’s perfect for cozy nights in or for entertaining family and friends. Packed with flavorful ingredients and topped with creamy mashed potatoes, this classic recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this mouthwatering winter delight!

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 1 pound ground lamb or beef
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Mashed Potato Topping:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a large skillet, brown the ground lamb or beef over medium heat until fully cooked. Remove any excess fat.
    • Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery to the skillet and sauté until the vegetables are tender.
    • Stir in the minced garlic, frozen peas, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, and dried thyme. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
    • Pour in the beef or vegetable broth and simmer the mixture for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  2. Make the Mashed Potato Topping:

    • While the filling is simmering, place the peeled and cubed potatoes in a large pot of salted water. Bring to a boil and cook until the potatoes are tender.
    • Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Add the milk, butter, salt, and pepper. Mash the potatoes until smooth and creamy.

  3. Assemble the Casserole:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Transfer the prepared filling to a greased casserole dish, spreading it out evenly.
    • Spoon the mashed potatoes over the filling, using a fork to create a decorative pattern on top.

  4. Bake and Serve:

    • Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the mashed potato topping is golden and the filling is bubbling around the edges.
    • Allow the casserole to cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh herbs, if desired, and enjoy the comforting flavors of this savory winter shepherd’s pie casserole.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian version, substitute the ground meat with lentils or your favorite plant-based protein.
  • Experiment with different herbs and seasonings to customize the flavors of the filling.
  • Add a layer of grated cheese on top of the mashed potatoes before baking for an extra indulgent twist.

Conclusion

This savory winter shepherd’s pie casserole is a timeless recipe that brings warmth and satisfaction to the dining table. Whether you’re seeking a nourishing family meal or a dish to impress guests, this comforting casserole is a winner. Embrace the aromas and flavors of the season with this delightful creation, and make it a staple in your winter recipe collection. We hope you enjoy preparing and savoring this hearty dish as much as we do!
