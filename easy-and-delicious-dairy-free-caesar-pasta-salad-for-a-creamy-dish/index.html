Easy and Delicious Dairy-Free Caesar Pasta Salad for a Creamy Dish


Looking for a creamy and flavorful dish that’s dairy-free and easy to make? This Dairy-Free Caesar Pasta Salad is the answer! It’s a delightful fusion of classic Caesar salad flavors with the heartiness of pasta, making it a perfect dish for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, a potluck, or just craving a satisfying meal, this recipe is sure to become a go-to favorite.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 pound (450g) penne pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion

For the Caesar Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup raw cashews, soaked in hot water for 1 hour
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon capers
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For Garnish:

  • Vegan parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the pasta and rinse it under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  2. Prepare the Dressing: In a blender, combine the soaked cashews, minced garlic, nutritional yeast, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, capers, salt, black pepper, water, and olive oil. Blend until smooth and creamy. If the dressing is too thick, add a little more water until desired consistency is reached.

  3. Assemble the Salad: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, black olives, chopped parsley, and red onion. Pour the Caesar dressing over the pasta and toss until everything is well coated.

  4. Chill and Garnish: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Before serving, sprinkle with vegan parmesan cheese (if using) and freshly ground black pepper.

Serving Suggestion

This Dairy-Free Caesar Pasta Salad is perfect for serving as a main dish or as a side alongside grilled vegetables or protein. It also makes a fantastic addition to any picnic or potluck spread. The creamy texture and rich flavors will satisfy even the most discerning palates, and the best part is, it’s entirely dairy-free!

Conclusion

With this simple and delicious recipe, you can enjoy the creamy goodness of a Caesar salad without any dairy. The combination of tangy, garlicky dressing and the satisfying texture of pasta creates a winning dish that’s sure to impress. Feel free to customize the salad with your favorite add-ins such as grilled chicken, roasted chickpeas, or sun-dried tomatoes. Embrace the versatility of this recipe and make it your own!

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a creamy and satisfying dish, give this Dairy-Free Caesar Pasta Salad a try. It’s a delightful twist on a classic favorite that will leave everyone coming back for more. Enjoy!
