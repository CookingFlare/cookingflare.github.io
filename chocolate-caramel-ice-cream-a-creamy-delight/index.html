Chocolate Caramel Ice Cream: A Creamy Delight


Who doesn’t love ice cream? It’s the perfect treat to cool down on a hot summer day or indulge in any time of the year. And when it comes to ice cream flavors, chocolate and caramel are a match made in heaven. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a lusciously creamy Chocolate Caramel Ice Cream that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup caramel sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and cocoa powder. Place the saucepan over medium heat and whisk until the sugar and cocoa powder have dissolved completely.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until they become smooth and pale yellow. Slowly pour the hot cream mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly to prevent the eggs from curdling.

  3. Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring continuously, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This should take about 10-12 minutes. Be careful not to let the mixture boil.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the caramel sauce, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix well until everything is fully incorporated.

  5. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap, making sure the wrap touches the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld together and the custard to chill completely.

  6. Once the custard is chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes around 20-25 minutes, but follow the guidelines provided by your machine.

  7. While the ice cream is churning, prepare an airtight container by drizzling some additional caramel sauce on the bottom. This will create a delicious caramel ripple throughout the ice cream.

  8. Once the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to the prepared container, layering it with more caramel sauce as you go. Swirl the caramel sauce gently using a knife or a skewer to create beautiful caramel ribbons.

  9. Cover the container tightly and freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours, or until it hardens to your desired consistency.

  10. Serve the Chocolate Caramel Ice Cream in bowls or cones, and if you’re feeling extra indulgent, top it with some whipped cream and a drizzle of caramel sauce.

Enjoy the Creamy Delight!

There you have it, a heavenly Chocolate Caramel Ice Cream recipe that will satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your taste buds. The combination of rich chocolate and gooey caramel creates a truly decadent treat that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you enjoy it on its own or pair it with your favorite desserts, this creamy delight is sure to become a family favorite. So grab your ice cream maker and get ready to indulge in pure bliss!
