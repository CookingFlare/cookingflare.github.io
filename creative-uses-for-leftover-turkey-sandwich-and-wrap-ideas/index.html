Creative Uses for Leftover Turkey: Sandwich and Wrap Ideas


As the holiday season comes to a close, many of us find ourselves with an abundance of leftover turkey. While turkey sandwiches are a classic go-to, there are countless creative ways to repurpose this delicious protein into exciting meals. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of sandwich and wrap ideas that will not only help you use up that leftover turkey but also tantalize your taste buds. Let’s dive into the world of turkey creativity!

1. Turkey Avocado Club Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 4 ounces of leftover turkey, sliced
  • 1 ripe avocado, mashed
  • 2 slices of crispy bacon
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast the whole grain bread until golden brown.
  2. Spread the mashed avocado on one slice of bread and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Layer the turkey slices on top of the avocado.
  4. Add crispy bacon, lettuce, and tomato slices.
  5. Top with the second slice of bread, slice in half, and enjoy!

This sandwich combines the creaminess of avocado with the savory flavors of turkey and bacon, creating a satisfying meal that’s perfect for lunch or a light dinner.

2. Turkey and Cranberry Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla or wrap
  • 4 ounces of leftover turkey, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons of cranberry sauce
  • 1 cup of mixed greens
  • 1 tablespoon of cream cheese or goat cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread the cream cheese or goat cheese evenly over the tortilla.
  2. Layer the shredded turkey on top, followed by the cranberry sauce.
  3. Add a handful of mixed greens and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Roll the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Slice in half and serve.

This wrap is a delightful combination of sweet and savory, making it a perfect option for a quick lunch or a picnic treat.

3. Turkey Pesto Panini

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of ciabatta or focaccia bread
  • 4 ounces of leftover turkey, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of pesto
  • 1 slice of mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a panini press or grill pan.
  2. Spread pesto on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Layer the turkey and mozzarella cheese on one slice, then add fresh basil leaves.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread, pesto side down.
  5. Brush the outside of the sandwich with olive oil and grill until golden brown and the cheese is melted.

This panini is packed with flavor and is a fantastic way to elevate your leftover turkey into a gourmet meal.

4. Turkey Caesar Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla or wrap
  • 4 ounces of leftover turkey, sliced
  • 1 cup of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of Caesar dressing
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, turkey, Caesar dressing, and Parmesan cheese.
  2. Toss until everything is well coated.
  3. Place the mixture in the center of the tortilla and add croutons if desired.
  4. Roll the tortilla tightly and slice in half.

This wrap brings the classic flavors of a Caesar salad into a portable format, making it a great option for lunch on the go.

5. Turkey and Hummus Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain or pita bread
  • 4 ounces of leftover turkey, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of hummus
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Sliced red onion
  • Spinach or arugula
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread hummus on one slice of bread.
  2. Layer the turkey slices on top, followed by cucumber, red onion, and spinach.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then top with the second slice of bread.
  4. Cut in half and serve.

This sandwich is not only healthy but also packed with flavor, making it a great choice for a nutritious lunch.

6. Turkey and Apple Slaw Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla or wrap
  • 4 ounces of leftover turkey, shredded
  • 1 cup of coleslaw mix
  • 1 apple, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinaigrette
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the coleslaw mix, shredded turkey, apple slices, and apple cider vinaigrette.
  2. Toss until well mixed and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the mixture in the center of the tortilla and roll tightly.
