Savory Nut-Free Pesto Pizza Recipe


If you’re a pizza lover looking for a unique and flavorful twist on the classic Italian dish, then this savory nut-free pesto pizza recipe is just what you need. Pesto pizza is a delightful combination of fresh flavors, and by making it nut-free, you can enjoy it without worrying about any nut allergies. This recipe is perfect for a cozy night in, a family gathering, or a casual dinner with friends.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

For the Nut-Free Pesto:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Toppings:

  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ cup black olives, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Making the Nut-Free Pesto:

  1. In a food processor, combine the fresh basil leaves and garlic. Pulse until finely chopped.

  2. Add the grated Parmesan cheese, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the food processor. Blend until the ingredients are well combined and form a smooth paste.

Preparing the Pizza Dough:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm water, instant yeast, olive oil, salt, and sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes until the yeast activates and the mixture becomes frothy.

  2. Gradually add the all-purpose flour to the mixture, stirring until a dough forms.

  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.

  4. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).

  5. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired pizza crust thickness.

Assembling the Pizza:

  1. Place the rolled-out pizza dough on a pizza stone or a baking sheet.

  2. Spread a generous amount of the nut-free pesto over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.

  3. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the pesto.

  4. Arrange the halved cherry tomatoes and sliced black olives on top of the cheese.

  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  6. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  7. Garnish the pizza with fresh basil leaves before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This nut-free pesto pizza pairs wonderfully with a crisp green salad dressed with balsamic vinaigrette. You can also customize the toppings to suit your taste, adding grilled chicken, roasted vegetables, or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for an extra kick.

Enjoy this delicious and nut-free twist on traditional pesto pizza, and savor the vibrant flavors with every bite!

This savory nut-free pesto pizza recipe is a delightful way to enjoy the flavors of homemade pesto without the worry of nut allergies. The nut-free pesto, paired with the fresh toppings, creates a pizza that is both vibrant and satisfying. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a pizza enthusiast looking to try something new, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.
