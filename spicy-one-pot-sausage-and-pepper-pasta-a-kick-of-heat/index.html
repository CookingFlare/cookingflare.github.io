Spicy One-Pot Sausage and Pepper Pasta: A Kick of Heat


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and easy cleanup, then this Spicy One-Pot Sausage and Pepper Pasta recipe is a game-changer. This dish is a delightful combination of spicy Italian sausage, vibrant bell peppers, and al dente pasta, all cooked together in one pot to infuse every bite with a symphony of flavors. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a satisfying meal for yourself, this recipe is a winner. Let’s dive into the details and spice up your culinary repertoire!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spicy Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 12 ounces penne pasta
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Sausage: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the spicy Italian sausage, breaking it into smaller pieces with a wooden spoon, and cook until browned and cooked through.

  2. Add Aromatics and Peppers: Push the sausage to one side of the pot and add the sliced onion to the other side. Cook for a few minutes until the onion starts to soften, then add the minced garlic, red and yellow bell peppers, red pepper flakes, and Italian seasoning. Stir everything together and cook for another 3-4 minutes until the peppers begin to soften.

  3. Simmer with Tomatoes: Pour in the diced tomatoes and chicken broth, and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to cook for a couple of minutes, then add the penne pasta, ensuring it’s submerged in the liquid. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

  4. Cook and Garnish: Cover the pot and let the pasta simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is al dente and the liquid has reduced to a creamy sauce. If needed, add a splash of water during the cooking process to prevent the pasta from sticking to the bottom. Once the pasta is cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Enjoy the Heat

This Spicy One-Pot Sausage and Pepper Pasta is a fantastic dish that brings the heat and flavor to the forefront of your mealtime. The combination of spicy sausage, vibrant peppers, and zesty tomatoes creates a symphony of flavors that will have your taste buds dancing. Plus, the convenience of cooking everything in one pot means less time spent on cleanup and more time savoring every delicious bite.

So, whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or hosting a casual gathering, this recipe is sure to impress. The kick of heat from the spicy sausage and red pepper flakes adds a delightful warmth to the dish, making it a comforting and satisfying choice for any occasion.

Give this recipe a try and experience the joy of a flavorful, one-pot meal that will become a staple in your recipe collection. Cheers to good food, good company, and the irresistible allure of a little heat in every bite!
