Savory and Sweet Cornbread Recipes for a Southern Picnic

Are you planning a delightful Southern picnic and in need of some crowd-pleasing recipes? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore two delicious cornbread recipes—savory and sweet—that are sure to impress your guests. So, grab your apron and get ready to whip up some mouthwatering treats. Savory Cornbread Let’s start with a classic savory cornbread recipe that perfectly complements any picnic spread. Here’s what you’ll need: Ingredients: 1 cup cornmeal 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 cup buttermilk 1/4 cup vegetable oil 2 tablespoons honey 2 eggs 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese 1/4 cup chopped green onions Instructions: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and grease a 9-inch square baking pan. In a large bowl, combine the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, vegetable oil, honey, and eggs. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Fold in the grated cheddar cheese and chopped green onions. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cornbread to cool for a few minutes before cutting it into squares. Serve warm and enjoy the irresistible, savory flavor.
