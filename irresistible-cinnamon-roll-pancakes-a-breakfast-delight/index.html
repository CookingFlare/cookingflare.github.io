Irresistible Cinnamon Roll Pancakes: A Breakfast Delight


Craving a decadent and indulgent breakfast? Look no further than these irresistible Cinnamon Roll Pancakes! This delightful twist on a classic breakfast favorite will have your taste buds dancing with joy. With their fluffy texture, sweet cinnamon swirls, and gooey cream cheese glaze, these pancakes are the ultimate breakfast treat.

Ingredients

For the Pancakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Cinnamon Swirl:

  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ¼ cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

For the Cream Cheese Glaze:

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

  4. In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon for the cinnamon swirl.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Pour ¼ cup of pancake batter onto the skillet and spread it out into a circle.

  6. Drizzle about a tablespoon of the cinnamon swirl mixture onto the pancake batter and use a toothpick or the tip of a knife to swirl it into the batter.

  7. Cook the pancake for about 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles form on the surface. Flip the pancake and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until golden brown.

  8. Repeat steps 5-7 for the remaining batter and cinnamon swirl mixture, adjusting the heat as needed to prevent burning.

  9. While the pancakes are cooking, prepare the cream cheese glaze. In a bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth. Add the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract, and continue to beat until well combined and creamy.

  10. Serve the warm cinnamon roll pancakes with a generous drizzle of the cream cheese glaze.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the pancakes even more indulgent, sprinkle some chopped pecans or walnuts onto the cinnamon swirl before cooking.
  • For a fruity twist, add a handful of blueberries or sliced strawberries to the pancake batter before cooking.
  • If you prefer a stronger cinnamon flavor, increase the amount of ground cinnamon in the cinnamon swirl mixture.
  • These pancakes are best enjoyed fresh, but you can store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Simply reheat them in the microwave or toaster before serving.

Now that you have this incredible recipe for Cinnamon Roll Pancakes, it’s time to treat yourself and your loved ones to a truly unforgettable breakfast experience. Indulge in the fluffy pancakes, the sweet cinnamon swirls, and the creamy glaze – it’s a breakfast delight that will leave you craving more. Enjoy!
