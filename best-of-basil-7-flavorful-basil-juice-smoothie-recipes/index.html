Best of Basil: 7 Flavorful Basil Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Indulge your senses in the fresh, sweet, and slightly peppery notes of basil as we bring you seven tantalizing recipes that elevate the humble leafy green into a delicious culinary star. Basil is not only a versatile herb with diverse applications but also a powerhouse of nutrients, making it perfect for juices and smoothies. Take a breather from the greens and dive into the energizing world of basil-based concoctions.

1. Nutritious Green Basil Smoothie

Restore balance with this nutrient-rich concoction that brings together the classic combination of basil, spinach, and avocado. Juice of half a lemon introduces a citrusy zing while four tablespoons of chia seeds provide an omega-3 fatty acid boost.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 4 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk

Instructions:

  1. Begin by rinsing the spinach and basil leaves.
  2. In a large blender, combine the spinach, basil, avocado, lemon juice, and chia seeds, followed by unsweetened almond milk. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Adjust the consistency by adding more almond milk or blending in ice if desired. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.

2. Basil-Blueberry Burst

Harness the sweet yet tangy flavor of berries and basil in this invigorating smoothie. Fresh blueberries, honeydew melon, a touch of agave nectar, and ice serve as the ideal texture-supporting elements for basil’s accommodating personality.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 honeydew melon, cubed
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons agave nectar
  • Ice to thicken

Instructions:

  1. Quickly rinse the fresh basil leaves to remove dirt and impurities.
  2. Combine the blueberries, honeydew melon, basil, and agave nectar in your blender. Blend on high for 60 seconds until everything is thoroughly incorporated and smooth.
  3. If you prefer a thicker texture, add a handful of ice and continue blending until smooth.

3. Basil-Banana Blended Brilliance

This recipe embraces the all-American classic combination of bananas and basil, putting a twist on a traditional smoothie. A drizzle of almond butter and coconut milk assure that this green smoothie is nutritious without sacrificing flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, peeled and sliced
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 can light coconut milk
  • Ice, optional

Instructions:

  1. Thoroughly wash the basil leaves before blending.
  2. Add the bananas, basil leaves, almond butter, and coconut milk to your blender. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. If you love a frosty texture, feel free to throw in some ice and blend until well combined.

4. Green Ginger Basil Glow

Spice up your morning with the zesty combination of fresh ginger and basil in this youthful elixir. The sweet aspire for balance as pineapple chunks, unsweetened coconut milk, and lime juice join the party.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the spinach and basil thoroughly.
  2. Combine all ingredients in the blender and blend until completely smooth.
  3. Adjust to taste with additional lime juice if needed.

5. Basil-Cilantro Cleanse

Lend yourself a hand with this refreshing cleanse. Refresh both senses and cleanse your palate with this delicious combination of basil, cilantro, ginger, and lime. Apple and cucumber add a touch of sweetness and a cool finish to this invigorating concoction.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1/2 bunch fresh cilantro
  • 1-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 apple, cored
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and sliced

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the spinach, basil, and cilantro leaves.
  2. Add all ingredients to the blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Adjust the flavor with more lime juice if desired.

6. Basil-Mint Marvel

Cool down with this layered fusion of fresh basil, mint, orange juice, and a hint of vanilla. This smoothie is infused with layers of bright flavors, but it’s the honey and a touch of cinnamon that bring the ingredients into a harmonious interaction.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh basil
  • 2 cups fresh mint
  • 1 orange, peeled and separated into segments
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk

Instructions:

  1. Loosely pack the basil and mint leaves and rinse well.
  2. In a blender, combine the basil, mint, orange segments, water, honey, cinnamon, and almond milk. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and adjust the sweetness, if necessary, with extra honey.

7. Holy Basil Wellness Warrior

Invigorate your senses with holy basil, also known as tulsi, as it teams up with strawberry and pear to create an unparalleled medley of flavors. Coconut water and ice bring the refreshing elements, elevating this well-being elixir.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh holy basil (tulsi) leaves
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 pear, cored and sliced
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Pick through the holy basil leaves and rinse thoroughly.
  2. Combine the holy basil, strawberries, pear, and coconut water in your blender. Blend until smooth.
  3. For a thicker texture, feel free to add more ice and blend until well combined.

Let your culinary adventure begin by incorporating any of these fresh basil juice and smoothie recipes into your daily routine. Boost your health with basil’s flavorful versatility and experience a symphony of taste in every sip of your newly discovered green drinks. Enjoy these refreshing twists and celebrate the green goodness that awaits.
