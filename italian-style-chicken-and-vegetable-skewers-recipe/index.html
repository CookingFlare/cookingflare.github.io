Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Skewers Recipe


There’s something truly delightful about sinking your teeth into a tender, flavorful skewer of Italian-style chicken and vegetables. This recipe brings together the vibrant flavors of Italy with succulent chicken and a medley of colorful vegetables. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply looking for a quick and delicious weeknight meal, these skewers are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 8 cherry tomatoes
  • 8 button mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for maximum flavor.

  4. While the chicken is marinating, prepare your vegetables. Thread the chicken, bell peppers, red onion, cherry tomatoes, and mushrooms onto skewers, alternating between the different ingredients.

  5. Once the grill is hot, place the skewers directly on the grill grates. Cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

  7. Serve the Italian-style chicken and vegetable skewers with a side of freshly cooked pasta or a crisp green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • If you’re short on time, you can use store-bought Italian dressing as a quick and easy marinade for the chicken.

  • Feel free to customize the vegetables used in this recipe. Zucchini, eggplant, or even cherry tomatoes would make great additions to the skewers.

  • For an extra kick of flavor, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the skewers just before serving.

  • Don’t have a grill? No problem! You can also cook these skewers in the oven under the broiler for about 10-12 minutes, turning halfway through.

  • Leftover skewers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat them in the oven or microwave before enjoying.

Final Thoughts

Italian-style chicken and vegetable skewers are a fantastic option for those seeking a taste of Italy in a simple yet satisfying dish. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful vegetables, and fragrant herbs creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you straight to the streets of Italy. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or enjoying a quiet evening at home, these skewers are sure to be a hit. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a taste of Italy right in your own backyard. Buon appetito!
