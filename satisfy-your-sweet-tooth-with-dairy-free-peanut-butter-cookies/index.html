Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Dairy-Free Peanut Butter Cookies


Are you craving something sweet but want to stick to a dairy-free diet? Look no further! These dairy-free peanut butter cookies are the perfect solution to satisfy your sweet tooth without compromising on flavor. Not only are these cookies delicious, but they are also incredibly easy to make. Whether you have a dairy allergy, are lactose intolerant, or simply prefer to avoid dairy, these cookies are a delightful treat for everyone to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 flax egg (1 tablespoon ground flaxseed + 3 tablespoons water)
  • Additional sugar for rolling (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2: Prepare the Flax Egg

In a small bowl, mix together 1 tablespoon of ground flaxseed and 3 tablespoons of water. Let the mixture sit for a few minutes to thicken and form a gel-like consistency. This will act as a binding agent in place of the traditional egg.

Step 3: Combine the Ingredients

In a mixing bowl, combine the creamy peanut butter, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, baking powder, salt, and the prepared flax egg. Mix the ingredients until well combined and a thick cookie dough forms.

Step 4: Form the Cookie Dough Balls

Using a spoon or cookie scoop, portion the dough and roll it into 1-inch balls. If desired, roll the dough balls in additional sugar for a sweet and slightly crunchy exterior.

Step 5: Place the Dough Balls on the Baking Sheet

Arrange the dough balls on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie to allow for spreading during baking.

Step 6: Create the Classic Peanut Butter Cookie Crosshatch

Using a fork, gently press down on each dough ball to create the classic crosshatch pattern on the cookies. This not only adds a decorative touch but also helps the cookies bake evenly.

Step 7: Bake to Perfection

Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. Keep in mind that the cookies will continue to firm up as they cool, so it’s okay if they seem slightly soft when you take them out of the oven.

Step 8: Cool and Enjoy

Once the cookies are done baking, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, indulge in the irresistible aroma and taste of these dairy-free peanut butter cookies!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, you can mix in dairy-free chocolate chips or chopped nuts to the cookie dough.
  • If you prefer a softer texture, reduce the baking time slightly and enjoy a more chewy cookie.
  • These cookies are perfect for sharing with friends and family, and they make a delightful addition to any gathering or celebration.

Conclusion

These dairy-free peanut butter cookies are a delightful treat that proves you don’t need dairy to enjoy a scrumptious dessert. With just a few simple ingredients and easy steps, you can whip up a batch of these delectable cookies to satisfy your sweet cravings. Whether you follow a dairy-free lifestyle or simply love the rich flavor of peanut butter, these cookies are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So go ahead, preheat your oven, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the irresistible joy of baking and savoring these delightful dairy-free peanut butter cookies!
