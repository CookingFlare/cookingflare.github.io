Flavorful Nut-Free Thai Curry Recipe

5 min · 886 words

Are you looking for a delicious and exotic dish that is not only packed with flavors but also nut-free? Look no further! This Flavorful Nut-Free Thai Curry Recipe is a perfect choice for those who have nut allergies or simply want to enjoy a delicious Thai curry without the addition of nuts. With its vibrant colors, aromatic spices, and rich flavors, this curry will surely become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons Thai red curry paste
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.

  2. Add the Thai red curry paste to the skillet and stir-fry for another minute, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Add the sliced bell peppers to the skillet and cook for about 5 minutes, until they start to soften.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, vegetable broth, soy sauce, lime juice, brown sugar, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt, and pepper.

  5. Pour the coconut milk mixture into the skillet with the bell peppers and stir well to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to develop and the vegetables to become tender.

  6. Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning if needed. If you prefer a spicier curry, you can add more red curry paste.

  7. Serve the Flavorful Nut-Free Thai Curry over a bed of cooked rice and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.

Enjoy this mouthwatering nut-free Thai curry with your loved ones. The combination of aromatic spices, creamy coconut milk, and colorful bell peppers creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you straight to the streets of Thailand. Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or protein options such as tofu, chicken, or shrimp.

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and making the dish your own. So go ahead, indulge in the world of flavors, and savor every bite of this Flavorful Nut-Free Thai Curry!

Note: If you have any allergies or dietary restrictions, make sure to check the ingredients in the curry paste and other condiments to ensure they are suitable for your needs.
