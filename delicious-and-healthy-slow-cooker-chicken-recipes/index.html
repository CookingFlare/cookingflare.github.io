Delicious and Healthy Slow Cooker Chicken Recipes


If you’re looking for easy, delicious, and healthy chicken recipes, look no further than your slow cooker. Slow cookers are a fantastic tool for creating flavorful meals with minimal effort. Plus, they allow you to infuse your chicken with mouthwatering flavors while keeping it tender and juicy. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of delicious and healthy slow cooker chicken recipes that are sure to impress your taste buds.

Lemon Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  4. Cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. Serve the lemon garlic chicken with your favorite side dishes and enjoy!

Teriyaki Slow Cooker Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken thighs in the slow cooker.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, and grated ginger.
  3. Pour the teriyaki sauce over the chicken thighs.
  4. Cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the cornstarch and water until well combined.
  6. Stir the cornstarch mixture into the slow cooker, and cook on high heat for an additional 30 minutes or until the sauce thickens.
  7. Serve the teriyaki chicken over steamed rice, garnished with sesame seeds and green onions.

BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Slider buns
  • Coleslaw for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the barbecue sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, and garlic powder.
  3. Pour the barbecue sauce mixture over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are well coated.
  4. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or until the chicken is tender and easily shreds.
  5. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  6. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and stir it into the sauce.
  7. Serve the BBQ pulled chicken on slider buns, topped with coleslaw if desired.

Conclusion

These delicious and healthy slow cooker chicken recipes are perfect for busy individuals who still want to enjoy a homemade meal. With minimal effort, you can create flavorful and tender chicken dishes that will satisfy your cravings. Whether you prefer tangy lemon garlic chicken, savory teriyaki chicken, or mouthwatering BBQ pulled chicken, your slow cooker will become your new favorite kitchen companion. So go ahead, give these recipes a try, and delight in the convenience and deliciousness of slow cooker chicken. Enjoy!
