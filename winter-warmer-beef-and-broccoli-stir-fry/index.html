Winter Warmer Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry


There’s nothing quite like a hearty stir-fry to warm you up on a chilly winter evening. This Winter Warmer Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is a perfect combination of savory beef, crisp broccoli, and a flavorful stir-fry sauce that will leave you feeling cozy and satisfied. Let’s dive into this delicious recipe that will surely become a favorite in your winter meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of beef broth
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the stir-fry sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the low-sodium soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, cornstarch, and beef broth. Set the sauce aside.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the thinly sliced flank steak to the skillet and cook until it’s browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté for about 30 seconds, or until fragrant.

  4. Add the broccoli florets to the skillet and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until they are bright green and slightly tender.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the skillet and pour the stir-fry sauce over the beef and broccoli. Stir everything together and let it simmer for a few minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and coat the beef and broccoli.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened to your liking, remove the skillet from the heat.

  7. Serve the Winter Warmer Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry over cooked rice and garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions for an extra pop of flavor and color.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes to the stir-fry sauce, or include sliced red chili peppers when stir-frying the beef and broccoli.
  • To make the dish even heartier, consider adding sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, or snap peas to the stir-fry.

Enjoy the Cozy Flavors of Winter

This Winter Warmer Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is a delightful way to infuse warmth and flavor into the cold winter months. The tender beef, crisp broccoli, and savory stir-fry sauce come together to create a comforting and satisfying dish that you’ll want to make again and again. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this stir-fry is sure to impress with its delicious flavors and cozy appeal.

So, the next time you’re craving a winter warmer that’s both nutritious and delicious, give this recipe a try. Let the aromas of garlic, ginger, and savory beef fill your kitchen as you whip up this delightful stir-fry. It’s a delightful way to spice up your winter meal repertoire and bring a touch of warmth to the season. Happy cooking!
