Memorable Mango: 9 Refreshing Mango Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Indulge in the juicy, sweet, and heavenly flavors of mangoes this season with these remarkable mango juice and smoothie recipes. Mangoes, often referred to as ’the king of fruits,’ provide a delicious burst of sunshine and are perfect for refreshing your taste buds. From a classic mango lassi to an exotic mango-sorbet, these 9 refreshing concoctions will inspire your inner chef and create exciting conversations around your table. So, gather your ripe mangoes and let’s turn this tropical fruit into a tantalizing treat!

1. Classic Mango Lassi

India’s cherished tradition of blending yogurt and fruits is carried through in its beloved Mango Lassi. This tangy beverage is perfect to rejuvenate your senses after a heavy meal or to start off your day on a healthy note.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add yogurt, diced cucumber, mango, and honey to a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Extend the magic by blending in your choice of ice cubes until you reach your desired consistency.
  3. Please taste test and season with additional honey (if required) for a touch of sweetness.
  4. Serve chilled in glasses garnished with extra mango chunks.

2. Mango Berry Smoothie

An enticing symphony of zesty mango and luscious berries, this antioxidant powerhouse smoothie will keep you energized throughout the day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup non-dairy milk (almond, coconut, oat)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Blitz mango, mixed berries, banana, non-dairy milk, honey, mint leaves, and ice cubes together in a blender until you achieve the desired consistency.
  2. Filter the smoothie using a fine sieve if you prefer a clear texture, without the chunks.
  3. Serve immediately and relish your refreshing drink.

3. Mango Pineapple Blast

Get your tropical tastebuds dancing with this invigorating twist on mango juice. Sweet mango and tangy pineapple pair together splendidly in this tart and sweet scoop.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh pineapple chunks (frozen or fresh)
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons triple sec (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Combine mangoes, pineapple chunks, orange juice, and triple sec (should you choose to add it) in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Top up with ice cubes and blend again or stir in separately to reach your desired texture.
  3. Pour into glasses and garnish using pineapple wedges or a fresh mint sprig.

4. Mango Ginger Cooler

A hit with both kids and adults, this mango and ginger cooler will leave you with a delightful tingle in your mouth. The ginger in this concoction provides a mild spicy kick, perfect to chase away those cold afternoons.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 cups cold water
  • A squirt of honey or agave syrup (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add cubed mango, grated ginger, lemon juice, and water to a blender, and blitz until well mixed.
  2. Taste test the mixture, and if a little extra sweetness is what you seek, feel free to add honey or agave syrup.
  3. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before serving in glasses garnished with a lemon twist.

5. Mango Coconut Smoothie Bowl

This recipe for Mango Coconut Smoothie Bowl is one of the most enjoyable and Instagram-worthy ways to savor mangoes. Delight in this colorful bowl filled with tropical fruits and crunchy toppings making for a perfect breakfast or dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and cubed
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Toppings: shredded coconut, fresh fruits, chia seeds, and nuts

Instructions:

  1. Add mangoes, banana, coconut milk, honey, and a handful of ice cubes to a blender and blend until you reach a smooth and creamy texture.
  2. Spoon the concoction into a bowl, and arrange your toppings artistically.
  3. Dive in and enjoy this blissful tropical treat!

6. Mango Saffron Popsicles

Icy, refreshing, and fragrant, these Mango Saffron Popsicles are the epitome of summer imprisonment. Effortlessly elevate your homemade popsicles with the magical touch of saffron for an unforgettable treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 tablespoons caster sugar
  • A pinch of saffron strands
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Popsicle molds or ice-cream cones

Instructions:

  1. Let the saffron soak in boiling water for 10 minutes. Strain and set aside to cool.
  2. Meanwhile, blitz mangoes, sugar, and the saffron-infused water in a blender until smooth.
  3. Mix in the cold water and whiz once more for a uniform texture.
  4. Pour the mixture into popsicle molds and freeze for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  5. Unmold and indulge in this enchanting popsicle sensation.

7. Mango Banana Green Smoothie

Blitz together mangoes, bananas, and hearty greens in this delightful, nutrient-packed smoothie. Filled with anti-oxidant rich ingredients, it’s a complete package—no excuses needed for such a ‘green-deal.’

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and cubed
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup loosely packed spinach or kale
  • 1/2 cup non-dairy milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • A handful of ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Pop the mango, banana, spinach or kale, and non-dairy milk into a blender. Blend until you achieve a smooth mixture.
  2. Stir in honey, mix thoroughly, and taste to see if you need more sweetness.
  3. Lastly, toss in the ice cubes and blend once more for the perfect frosty-smooth texture.
  4. Pour and sip your way to health!

8. Mango Strawberry-Limeade

This family-friendly Mango Strawberry Limeade is a breeze to prepare and it takes a maximum of five minutes. It boasts a delectable combination of sweet, sour, and tangy—in one brilliant glass.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 1/2 cups cold water
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Instructions:

  1. Combine mangoes, strawberries, lime juice, water, and honey in a blender, and process until the fruits are finely chopped.
  2. Pass the mixture through a sieve to extract the juice, discarding the pulp.
  3. Taste the juice and adjust the sweetness as per your preference by adding honey.
  4. Pour the concoction into glasses, add ice cubes, and sip your way to sunshine!

9. Mango Tamarind Cooler

A traditional mango cooler zinged up with tamarind pulp, this riot of flavors will leave you craving for more. A delightful twist of sour meets sweet, making it a true gratification of your tastebuds.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind pulp (from a jar or fresh fruit)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Begin by dissolving tamarind pulp in a little water. Adjust the concentration according to your liking.
  2. Blend the mangoes, tamarind water, honey, and ice cubes together in a blender.
  3. Serve chilled to provide a burst of fruity and sour masala experience!

The irresistible, tender succulence of mangoes brings life to these refreshing mango juice and smoothie recipes. Create your own tropical paradise, one mango-infused sip at a time, and invite the sun to linger a little longer. Peak your creativity by experimenting with multi-layered smoothies, frozen treats, or invigorating cocktails for a complete mango indulgence!
