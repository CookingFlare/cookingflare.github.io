A Globetrotter's Gourmet: American, Italian, Greek... Pizza Styles from Every Corner of the World


It’s no secret that pizza is a universally beloved dish, thoughtfully crafted from simplicity and prosperity. The development of this dish has transcended borders and culture, adopting and combining styles, flavors, and ingredients as it traveled around the world. As diverse as the corners of our beautiful planet, let’s explore some fascinating variations of this alluring comfort food to satisfy every culinary craving!

  1. American: Beat the Heat- Fresh Summertime Pizza

There’s just something about American-style pizza that whispers freedom and carefreeness. This style is all about indulgence, built on a foundation of a light, crispy crust loaded with gooey layers of cheese, succulent meats, and fresh vegetables.

To prepare this ‘beachy’ summer pizza, here’s a delectable recipe for you to try at home:

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Crispy bacon
  • Jalapeño peppers
  • Sliced avocado
  • Fresh cilantro leaves

Instructions:

  1. Roll out your pizza dough to your desired thickness.

  2. Spread a thick layer of tomato sauce. Add a generous heap of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.

  3. Decorate your creation with crispy bacon pieces, fresh jalapeño slices, and sliced avocado.

  4. Slide the masterpiece into a preheated oven at 425°F and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the crust turns golden brown and bubbly.

  5. Sprinkle fresh cilantro on top and slice into wedges for your loved ones to devour.

  6. Italian: Artisanal Thin Crust Roman-Style Pizza

Let us dive into the origin of pizza with this classic Roman-Style Pizza. Thin, crispy, and packed with robust flavors, this delicacy relies on the freshest ingredients for a burst of taste.

Here’s an authentic recipe to try at your very own Roman dinner party:

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Fresh garlic cloves
  • Dried oregano
  • High-quality extra virgin olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Roll out the pizza dough until it becomes very thin.

  2. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can manage.

  3. Mix the tomato sauce with minced garlic, dried oregano, and a pinch of salt. Taste and adjust the flavors as needed.

  4. Spread the sauce evenly onto the crust, leaving a small border for the edges.

  5. Top with thin slices of Pecorino Romano cheese.

  6. Transfer the pizza onto a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet and drizzle generously with high-quality extra virgin olive oil. Bake for 10-12 minutes at a high temperature, or until the crust is crispy and golden.

  7. Slice your artisanal Roman-Style pizza into wedges and serve piping hot.

  8. Greek: Zesty Mediterranean-inspired Veggie Delight

Who would have thought Greek cuisine would have its hands on a pizza? Do not be fooled by the simplicity, this Greek-influenced pie is a diverse burst of flavors that will keep you coming back for more.

Not at all a standard recipe, here’s a mouth-watering, Mediterranean-inspired veggie delight:

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Crumbled feta cheese
  • Kalamata olives
  • Marinated artichoke hearts
  • Sliced red onions
  • Fresh Greek oregano

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F and roll out your pizza dough to your desired thickness.
  2. Spread a layer of tomato sauce onto the dough.
  3. Decorate your creation with crumbled feta cheese, earthy marinated artichoke hearts, tasty Kalamata olives, and sweet-sliced red onions.
  4. Sprinkle fresh Greek oregano on top before baking.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust turns golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Slice into wedges and serve hot with a refreshing glass of ice-cold Ouzo.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a slice of heaven, don’t hesitate to explore different international flavors. With the perfect combination of tradition, creativity, and love, each style can easily become your new go-to. Go ahead, travel the globe on a pizza experience, and let these heavenly pies awaken your taste buds!
