Indian-Inspired Curry Recipes for Cooking for Two


If you’re a fan of rich, aromatic flavors and cozy meals, Indian-inspired curry recipes are a perfect choice for cooking for two. From creamy butter chicken to spicy vegetable korma, the world of Indian curries offers a delightful array of options to explore. Whether you’re new to Indian cooking or a seasoned pro, these recipes will guide you through creating delicious, restaurant-quality curries in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked basmati rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and place in a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine yogurt, garlic, ginger, garam masala, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder. Pour the mixture over the chicken, ensuring it’s evenly coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  3. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  4. Stir in the tomato puree and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Add the heavy cream and stir until the sauce is heated through.
  6. Season with salt and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with basmati rice.

Vegetable Korma

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as carrots, peas, cauliflower, and potatoes)
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped cashews for garnish
  • Cooked naan bread for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the ground coriander, cumin, turmeric, and garam masala, and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and coat them with the spice mixture.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and yogurt. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and garnish with chopped cashews. Serve with warm naan bread.

Conclusion

These Indian-inspired curry recipes are perfect for cooking for two, offering a burst of flavors that will transport you to the streets of Delhi or the shores of Goa. Experiment with different combinations of spices and ingredients to create your own signature curry dishes. Whether you’re a meat lover or a vegetarian, there’s a curry recipe waiting for you to discover. So, grab your apron and get ready to embark on a culinary journey through the vibrant world of Indian cuisine!
