BBQ Chicken Quesadilla Wraps: Easy and Flavorful Recipes


Are you looking for a quick and delicious meal that will satisfy your cravings? Look no further than BBQ Chicken Quesadilla Wraps! These easy and flavorful recipes are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a casual weeknight dinner or a weekend get-together with friends. The combination of tender chicken, tangy barbecue sauce, melty cheese, and the crispy tortilla shell will have your taste buds dancing with delight. So, let’s dive right into the mouthwatering recipes!

Recipe 1: Classic BBQ Chicken Quesadilla Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Olive oil (for cooking)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then grill them for about 6-8 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  3. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, shred the chicken using two forks.
  4. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with the barbecue sauce until well coated.
  5. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Place one tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle half of the shredded cheese evenly over one half of the tortilla.
  6. Spread half of the barbecue chicken mixture over the cheese, then sprinkle with diced red onion and chopped cilantro.
  7. Fold the tortilla in half to cover the filling, pressing it gently with a spatula.
  8. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy and golden brown.
  9. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients to make the second quesadilla wrap.
  10. Cut each quesadilla wrap into wedges and serve hot.

Recipe 3: Honey Mustard BBQ Chicken Quesadilla Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup honey mustard sauce
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Olive oil (for cooking)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then grill them for about 6-8 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  3. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, shred the chicken using two forks.
  4. In a bowl, mix the honey mustard sauce and barbecue sauce until well combined. Add the shredded chicken to the sauce and toss until evenly coated.
  5. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Place one tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle half of the shredded cheese evenly over one half of the tortilla.
  6. Spread half of the honey mustard barbecue chicken mixture over the cheese, then sprinkle with sliced red bell pepper and chopped parsley.
  7. Fold the tortilla in half to cover the filling, pressing it gently with a spatula.
  8. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy and golden brown.
  9. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients to make the second quesadilla wrap.
  10. Cut each quesadilla wrap into wedges and serve hot.

These BBQ Chicken Quesadilla Wraps are versatile and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese, sauces, and toppings to create your own signature flavor. Whether you’re hosting a summer BBQ or simply craving a comforting meal, these easy and flavorful recipes are sure to impress. Enjoy!
