Savory Coconut Curry Shrimp - Aromatic and Delicious


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and exotic spices, then you’re in for a treat with this savory coconut curry shrimp recipe. Bursting with aromatic herbs, creamy coconut milk, and succulent shrimp, this dish is a celebration of vibrant and tantalizing flavors that will transport your taste buds to the tropics. Whether you’re an experienced home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is sure to impress and delight. So, let’s dive into the tantalizing world of savory coconut curry shrimp!

Ingredients

To create this delectable dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of palm sugar or brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Shrimp

Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning them with a pinch of salt. Set the seasoned shrimp aside while you prepare the curry base.

Step 2: Prepare the Curry Base

In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and bell peppers to the skillet and sauté until they begin to soften, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet, stirring constantly for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Step 3: Create the Curry Sauce

Push the sautéed vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the red curry paste to the cleared space. Cook the curry paste for about 1 minute, stirring constantly to release its aroma. Then, gradually stir in the coconut milk, chicken or vegetable broth, fish sauce, and palm sugar. Allow the mixture to come to a gentle simmer.

Step 4: Cook the Shrimp

Once the curry sauce is simmering, add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet, ensuring they are evenly distributed in the sauce. Cook the shrimp for 3-4 minutes, or until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become tough.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the shrimp are cooked through, remove the skillet from the heat. Taste the curry sauce and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Serve the savory coconut curry shrimp over a bed of fragrant jasmine rice, garnished with fresh cilantro for a burst of color and flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add sliced red chili peppers or a sprinkle of chili flakes to the curry sauce.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, allow the curry to simmer for a few additional minutes to reduce and thicken.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables such as snow peas, bamboo shoots, or baby corn.

Final Thoughts

Serving up a steaming bowl of savory coconut curry shrimp is sure to impress your friends and family with its vibrant flavors and exotic aromas. This dish is perfect for a cozy dinner at home or as part of a celebratory feast. The combination of creamy coconut milk, fragrant herbs, and succulent shrimp creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you to the bustling markets of Southeast Asia. So, roll up your sleeves and embark on a culinary adventure with this aromatic and delicious savory coconut curry shrimp recipe. Happy cooking!
