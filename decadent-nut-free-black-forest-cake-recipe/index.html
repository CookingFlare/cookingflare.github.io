Decadent Nut-Free Black Forest Cake Recipe


Are you a fan of indulgent desserts that are both delicious and allergy-friendly? If so, you’re in for a treat with this Decadent Nut-Free Black Forest Cake recipe. This classic German dessert is known for its rich chocolate flavor, tart cherries, and luscious whipped cream. And the best part? This version is completely nut-free, making it suitable for those with nut allergies or dietary restrictions. So, let’s get started and whip up this heavenly delight!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the cherry filling:

  • 2 cans (20 ounces each) pitted cherries in juice
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the whipped cream:

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • ¼ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.

  3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the boiling water to the batter, stirring continuously. The batter will be thin, but that’s okay.

  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  6. While the cakes are baking, prepare the cherry filling. Drain the cherries, reserving the juice. In a saucepan, combine the cherry juice, sugar, cornstarch, and vanilla extract. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and stir in the cherries. Let the filling cool completely.

  7. Once the cakes have cooled, carefully remove them from the pans. Place one cake layer on a serving plate or cake stand.

  8. Spread a generous amount of cherry filling over the top of the cake layer. Place the second cake layer on top and press gently.

  9. In a chilled mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract. Continue to whip until stiff peaks form.

  10. Frost the top and sides of the cake with the whipped cream. You can be as creative as you like with the frosting, whether it’s smooth or swirled.

  11. For an extra touch, you can garnish the cake with chocolate shavings or fresh cherries.

  12. Refrigerate the cake for at least 2 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

  13. Slice and enjoy this decadent nut-free Black Forest cake with your loved ones!

Conclusion

This Decadent Nut-Free Black Forest Cake recipe is a true crowd-pleaser, perfect for any special occasion or simply as a delightful treat. With its moist chocolate layers, tangy cherry filling, and fluffy whipped cream, it’s a dessert that will leave everyone craving for more. And the fact that it’s nut-free ensures that everyone can enjoy a slice without worry. So, don your apron and get ready to impress your family and friends with this scrumptious creation. Happy baking!
