Crispy Tofu with Black Bean Sauce: A Flavorful Vegan Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying vegan dish that’s packed with flavor, look no further than this Crispy Tofu with Black Bean Sauce recipe. This dish is a perfect balance of crispy tofu and savory black bean sauce that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. Whether you’re a vegan, a tofu lover, or just someone looking to expand your culinary horizons, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crispy Tofu:

  • 1 block of extra-firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Black Bean Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons black bean sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth

For Serving:

  • Steamed rice
  • Sliced green onions for garnish
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crispy Tofu

  1. Begin by draining the tofu and pressing out the excess water. Once the tofu is well-drained, cut it into cubes of your desired size.

  2. In a shallow dish, combine the cornstarch, soy sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss the tofu cubes in the mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  3. Heat a generous amount of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the tofu cubes in a single layer. Fry the tofu until it is golden brown and crispy on all sides, then transfer it to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

Step 2: Prepare the Black Bean Sauce

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the black bean sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. In a separate bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in the vegetable broth.

  2. Heat a small amount of vegetable oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the black bean sauce mixture and bring it to a simmer.

  3. Gradually pour in the vegetable broth and cornstarch mixture, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens. Remove the sauce from the heat and set it aside.

Step 3: Combine the Tofu and Black Bean Sauce

  1. Once the tofu and black bean sauce are prepared, carefully toss the crispy tofu cubes in the black bean sauce until they are well coated.

  2. Serve the crispy tofu and black bean sauce over steamed rice, and garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds for an extra burst of flavor and visual appeal.

Conclusion

This Crispy Tofu with Black Bean Sauce recipe is a delightful combination of textures and flavors that is sure to become a favorite in your vegan recipe repertoire. The crispy tofu provides a satisfying crunch, while the savory black bean sauce adds a depth of umami flavor that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re enjoying this dish as a weeknight meal or serving it to guests, it’s bound to impress with its rich and satisfying taste.

So go ahead, give this recipe a try and experience the delightful marriage of crispy tofu and flavorful black bean sauce in every mouthwatering bite!
