Flavor-packed One Pot Mexican Rice and Corn


If you’re in the mood for a dish that is bursting with flavor and offers the convenience of a one-pot meal, then this Mexican rice and corn recipe is just what you need. Not only is it easy to make, but it’s also a great way to add some excitement to your dinner table. Let’s dive right into this delicious and satisfying recipe that’s sure to become a family favorite!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 3/4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by gathering all the ingredients and having them measured, chopped, and ready to go. This will make the cooking process smooth and enjoyable.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: In a large, deep skillet or pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté for a few minutes until it becomes translucent. Then, add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Rice and Spices: Stir in the long-grain white rice and let it toast for a couple of minutes, stirring occasionally. This will enhance the nutty flavor of the rice. Next, sprinkle in the chili powder, ground cumin, and paprika, stirring well to coat the rice and infuse it with the aromatic spices.

  4. Incorporate the Veggies and Tomatoes: Add the diced red bell pepper and corn kernels to the pot, stirring to combine with the rice and spices. Then, pour in the canned diced tomatoes (undrained) and vegetable broth. Give everything a good stir and bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  5. Simmer and Cook: Once the mixture is boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice simmer for about 20-25 minutes. It’s important to resist the temptation to lift the lid during this time, as the steam trapped inside is what will cook the rice to perfection.

  6. Fluff and Garnish: After 20-25 minutes, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for an additional 5 minutes. Then, uncover the pot, fluff the rice with a fork, and taste for seasoning, adding salt and pepper as needed. Finally, garnish the flavorful Mexican rice and corn with fresh cilantro leaves for a burst of vibrant color and an extra layer of fresh flavor.

This one-pot wonder is not only a fantastic side dish for any Mexican-inspired meal, but it can also stand alone as a vegetarian main course. The combination of tender rice, sweet corn, and aromatic spices will have everyone coming back for seconds. Plus, the convenience of preparing it all in one pot means minimal cleanup, making it a win-win all around.

So, the next time you’re craving a dish that’s brimming with the vibrant flavors of Mexico, give this easy and delicious recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed!

Enjoy your flavor-packed one pot Mexican rice and corn – Buen provecho!
