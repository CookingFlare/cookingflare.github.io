Mouthwatering Instant Pot Pot Roast Recipe for Sunday Supper


Who doesn’t love a delicious pot roast for Sunday supper? It’s a classic dish that brings comfort and warmth to the table. And with the help of the Instant Pot, you can have a mouthwatering pot roast ready in no time. So let’s dive into this easy and flavorful recipe that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the olive oil and heat it up.

  2. Season the beef chuck roast generously with salt and pepper on all sides. Once the Instant Pot is hot, sear the roast on each side until it develops a nice brown crust. This step will help seal in the flavors and juices.

  3. Remove the roast from the Instant Pot and set it aside. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, carrots, and celery to the pot. Sauté them for a few minutes until they start to soften.

  4. Pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using) to deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. This will add depth of flavor to the pot roast.

  5. Stir in the tomato paste and Worcestershire sauce until well combined. Place the seared roast back into the Instant Pot, along with the fresh thyme and rosemary sprigs.

  6. Close the lid of the Instant Pot, making sure the valve is set to the sealing position. Select the “Meat/Stew” function and set the timer for 60 minutes on high pressure.

  7. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes. Then, carefully do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Remove the pot roast from the Instant Pot and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. This will help the juices redistribute throughout the meat, resulting in a tender and juicy roast.

  9. Serve the pot roast with the vegetables and a ladle of the flavorful cooking liquid. You can also strain the liquid and thicken it with a cornstarch slurry for a rich gravy.

Tips and Variations

  • For a deeper flavor, marinate the beef chuck roast in a mixture of Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, garlic, and herbs for a few hours or overnight before cooking.
  • Feel free to add more vegetables, such as potatoes, parsnips, or mushrooms, to the Instant Pot for a heartier meal.
  • If you prefer a thicker gravy, remove the roast and vegetables from the Instant Pot, then select the sauté function and let the cooking liquid reduce until desired thickness.
  • Leftover pot roast makes a great filling for sandwiches or can be repurposed into delicious tacos or shepherd’s pie.

So there you have it, a mouthwatering Instant Pot pot roast recipe perfect for Sunday supper. The tender and flavorful meat, combined with the aromatic vegetables and savory gravy, will make this dish a family favorite. Give it a try and let the Instant Pot work its magic in your kitchen. Enjoy!
