Mexican Chicken Posole Verde: A Tangy and Spicy Soup


If you’re looking for a vibrant and flavorful dish to brighten up your dinner table, look no further than this zesty Mexican Chicken Posole Verde recipe. Posole, a traditional Mexican soup, is known for its rich, comforting flavors and its ability to bring people together. This tangy and spicy version is a real crowd-pleaser, perfect for festive gatherings or cozy family dinners.

The Essence of Posole Verde

Posole, also known as pozole, is a traditional Mexican soup or stew that typically features hominy, a type of dried corn kernel that has been treated with an alkali. The dish is known for its bold flavors, often incorporating a variety of herbs, spices, and meats. Posole is a beloved dish in Mexican cuisine, often enjoyed during celebrations and special occasions.

Posole comes in different variations, depending on the region and the cook’s preferences. This recipe focuses on Posole Verde, which stands out for its vibrant green color and tangy, spicy flavors. The key to this dish is the use of green ingredients, such as tomatillos, green chilies, and cilantro, which give the soup its distinct color and taste.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Posole Verde:

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) of hominy, drained and rinsed
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pound fresh tomatillos, husked and rinsed
  • 2 poblano chilies, seeded and chopped
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Garnishes: sliced radishes, shredded cabbage, lime wedges, avocado, and tortilla chips

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the chicken. Season the chicken breasts with cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Grill or pan-sear the chicken until cooked through, then shred it and set aside.

  2. In a large pot, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant and translucent.

  3. Add the drained hominy to the pot and stir to combine with the onions and garlic.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a simmer.

  5. While the broth is simmering, prepare the verde sauce. In a blender, combine the tomatillos, poblano chilies, jalapeño pepper, cilantro, dried oregano, ground cumin, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.

  6. Pour the verde sauce into the pot with the simmering broth and hominy. Stir well to incorporate the sauce into the broth.

  7. Let the posole simmer for about 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  8. Once the posole is ready, ladle it into bowls and top with the shredded chicken, sliced radishes, shredded cabbage, a squeeze of lime juice, avocado slices, and a handful of tortilla chips for added crunch.

Serving Suggestions

This tangy and spicy Mexican Chicken Posole Verde is best enjoyed with warm corn tortillas or a side of Mexican rice. The vibrant flavors of the posole pair perfectly with the earthy sweetness of the hominy and the tangy kick of the verde sauce. This dish is a celebration of traditional Mexican flavors and is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Final Thoughts

Mexican Chicken Posole Verde is a dish that embodies the essence of Mexican cuisine – bold, vibrant, and comforting. The tangy and spicy flavors of this soup make it a standout dish for any occasion, from casual weeknight dinners to festive gatherings with friends and family. Embrace the flavors of Mexico with this delightful and aromatic posole verde recipe, and let it transport you to the heart of Mexican culinary tradition.
