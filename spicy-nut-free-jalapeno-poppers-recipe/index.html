Spicy Nut-Free Jalapeno Poppers Recipe


Are you a fan of spicy food? Do you love the heat of jalapeno peppers? If so, then this Spicy Nut-Free Jalapeno Poppers recipe is perfect for you! These poppers are not only delicious but also nut-free, making them a great option for those with allergies or dietary restrictions.

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh jalapeno peppers
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced green onions
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.

  2. Next, prepare the jalapeno peppers. Slice each pepper in half lengthwise and remove the seeds and membranes. This step is crucial if you want to reduce the heat level of your poppers. If you prefer them extra spicy, you can leave some of the seeds and membranes intact.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, diced green onions, garlic powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  4. Spoon the cheese mixture into the hollowed-out jalapeno pepper halves. Make sure to evenly distribute the filling among all the peppers.

  5. In separate bowls, place the all-purpose flour and beaten eggs. Dip each filled jalapeno pepper into the flour, coating it completely, then dip it into the beaten eggs, making sure it is well coated.

  6. Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the coated jalapeno poppers until they turn golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side. Transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

  7. Once all the poppers are fried, transfer them to the prepared baking sheet. Bake them in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Remove the poppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Be cautious when biting into these spicy treats, as the inside may still be hot.

Serving Suggestions

These Spicy Nut-Free Jalapeno Poppers are best served warm, making them a perfect appetizer for gatherings, game nights, or even as a snack while watching your favorite movie. You can serve them as is, or pair them with a dipping sauce of your choice, such as ranch dressing or a cool yogurt-based sauce to balance out the heat.

Final Thoughts

This Spicy Nut-Free Jalapeno Poppers recipe is a crowd-pleaser that combines the heat of jalapeno peppers with a creamy and flavorful cheese filling. The combination of spices adds an extra kick to each bite, making it a must-try for spice enthusiasts. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a delicious snack, these poppers are sure to satisfy your taste buds. Give this recipe a try and enjoy the fiery goodness!
