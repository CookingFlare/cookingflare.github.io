Easy Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe


If you’re looking for a comforting and hearty meal that practically cooks itself, then this easy slow cooker chicken and dumplings recipe is just what you need. Slow cooker recipes are a lifesaver for busy days, and this one is no exception. It’s the perfect dish to warm you up on a chilly evening or to satisfy your craving for a home-cooked meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Let’s dive into the details of this soul-soothing dish that will have your family asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 4 cups of low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 cup of diced celery
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 package of refrigerated biscuit dough

Instructions

  1. Begin by placing the chicken thighs in the slow cooker. Add the chicken broth, water, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Give everything a gentle stir to combine the ingredients.

  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-7 hours, or until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  3. Once the chicken is fully cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and flour until smooth. Stir the mixture into the slow cooker, along with the frozen peas. Cover and cook on high for an additional 30 minutes, or until the mixture thickens slightly.

  5. While the chicken mixture is simmering, cut the biscuit dough into small pieces. Gently place the dough pieces on top of the chicken mixture in the slow cooker. Cover and continue cooking on high for an additional 1 hour, or until the dumplings are cooked through.

  6. Once the dumplings are cooked, give the mixture a final stir to ensure everything is well combined. Adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  7. Serve the chicken and dumplings warm, and enjoy the heartwarming flavors of this classic comfort food.

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can sauté the onions and garlic before adding them to the slow cooker.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite herbs and spices.
  • If you prefer a thicker consistency, you can add more flour to the heavy cream mixture.
  • This recipe can easily be adapted to include your favorite vegetables such as corn, green beans, or mushrooms.

Final Thoughts

This easy slow cooker chicken and dumplings recipe is a testament to the beauty of simple, comforting meals. The tender chicken, flavorful broth, and fluffy dumplings come together to create a dish that will warm both your body and soul. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a taste of home, this recipe is sure to become a regular in your meal rotation. So, dust off your slow cooker and get ready to savor the delightful flavors of this timeless classic.
