Knead Not, Relax and Roll: The Best of Chinese Rolled Meat Recipes


In today’s fast-paced world, meal prepping and cooking have taken on a whole new meaning. Who wouldn’t want to enjoy a delicious, satisfying meal while also taking a break from the daily hustle? Welcome to the world of Chinese rolled meat recipes – an indulgent twist on traditional Chinese cooking that will make your taste buds sing and your stress melt away! Read on as we explore some of our favorite dishes that showcase these mouth-watering, hassle-free delights.

1. Scallion Encrusted Beef Rolls

Starting off with a balanced meal, these Scallion Encrusted Beef Rolls feature vibrant, aromatic scallions, tender slices of beef, and fresh cilantro. The perfect combination of flavors and textures will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup finely chopped scallions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine cornstarch and soy sauce to make a slurry, then whisk in sesame oil. Set aside.
  2. Coat the beef slices with the slurry mixture, ensuring even coverage. Set aside to marinate for about 15 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, breadcrumbs, scallions, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat, and add 1 tablespoon of oil.
  5. Dredge the marinated beef slices in the scallion and breadcrumb mixture, then place them carefully into the hot skillet.
  6. Cook for about 3 minutes on each side, until golden and crispy. Remove from heat and serve immediately.

2. Ginger-Lemongrass Prawn Rolls

Upgrade your seafood game with this zesty Ginger-Lemongrass Prawn Roll recipe. The aromatic blend of ginger and lemongrass perfectly balances the sweetness of the prawns, while the crispy coating takes this dish to new heights.

Ingredients:

  • 20 large prawns, deveined and butterflied
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped lemongrass
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine cornstarch and soy sauce, then whisk in vegetable oil, grated ginger, and chopped lemongrass. Set aside.
  2. Butterfly the prawns and marinate them in the mixture for about 15 minutes.
  3. In another bowl, mix together the flour, breadcrumbs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat, and add 1 tablespoon of oil.
  5. Coat the prawns in the flour and breadcrumb mixture, then pan-fry for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels and serve immediately.

3. Cumin-Lime Chicken Rolls

Bringing a touch of South American flair to the table, these Cumin-Lime Chicken Rolls blend the savory flavor of cumin with the refreshing taste of lime. A must-try for all you spice lovers out there!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2/3 cup cornflakes, crushed
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Pound the chicken breasts to an even thickness, then slice them into long strips.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together lime juice, vegetable oil, cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Marinate the chicken in the mixture for about 15 minutes.
  4. In another bowl, combine crushed cornflakes and chopped cilantro.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium heat, and add 1 tablespoon of oil.
  6. Coat the marinated chicken in the cornflake mixture, then pan-fry for about 3 minutes on each side, until golden and crispy. Serve immediately.

And there you have it – three sensational rolled meat recipes that can work wonders for your taste buds and your stress levels! Next time you’re in the mood for something delightfully different, give these dishes a go. Trust us, your chopsticks will thank you.
