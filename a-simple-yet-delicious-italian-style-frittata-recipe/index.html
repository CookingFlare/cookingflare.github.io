A Simple yet Delicious Italian-Style Frittata Recipe


Are you looking for a quick, easy, and delicious breakfast option? Look no further than this Italian-style frittata recipe! With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a flavorful and satisfying meal that will leave you craving for more. Let’s dive right in and discover how to make this mouthwatering frittata.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and black pepper until well combined. Set aside.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 2 minutes.

  3. Add the red bell pepper to the skillet and cook for an additional 2 minutes, until the pepper has softened slightly.

  4. Stir in the baby spinach and cook until wilted, about 1 minute.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and pour the egg mixture into the skillet, making sure it covers all the vegetables evenly. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese on top.

  6. Cover the skillet with a lid and let the frittata cook for about 10 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.

  7. Remove the lid and place the skillet under the broiler for 2-3 minutes, or until the top of the frittata is golden brown and fully set.

  8. Carefully remove the skillet from the oven and let the frittata cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

  9. Cut the frittata into wedges and serve warm. Enjoy this delightful Italian-style frittata as a delicious breakfast or brunch option!

Why Choose a Frittata?

Frittatas are a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or even dinner. They are incredibly easy to make and can be customized with a variety of ingredients to suit your taste. Plus, they are a great way to use up leftover vegetables or cheese that you have in your fridge.

Health Benefits of a Frittata

Not only is this Italian-style frittata recipe delicious, but it also packs a nutritional punch. Eggs are a great source of protein, vitamins, and minerals, while the vegetables add fiber and essential nutrients. By incorporating spinach and bell peppers into your frittata, you’re boosting your intake of vitamins A and C, as well as antioxidants.

Tips for a Perfect Frittata

  • Be sure to cook the vegetables before adding the egg mixture to ensure they are soft and flavorful.
  • Use a non-stick skillet to prevent the frittata from sticking to the pan.
  • Don’t overcook the frittata. The center should be slightly jiggly when you remove it from the heat, as it will continue to cook through residual heat.
  • Feel free to experiment with different ingredients such as mushrooms, zucchini, or even cooked bacon for added flavor.

Conclusion

There you have it, a simple yet delicious Italian-style frittata recipe that will impress your taste buds. With its versatility and incredible flavors, this frittata is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting brunch or looking for a quick and nutritious breakfast option, this recipe has got you covered. Give it a try and enjoy the delightful flavors of Italy in the comfort of your own home. Buon appetito!
