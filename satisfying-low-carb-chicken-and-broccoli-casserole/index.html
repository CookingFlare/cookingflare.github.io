Satisfying Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole

3 min · 547 words

If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying low carb meal that the whole family will love, look no further than this Satisfying Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole recipe. Packed with protein and loaded with flavor, this casserole is the perfect dish for those following a low carb lifestyle or simply looking to incorporate more wholesome ingredients into their meals.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large pot of boiling water, cook the broccoli florets until tender-crisp, about 3-4 minutes. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a separate pot, poach the chicken breasts in water or chicken broth until cooked through, about 15-20 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and let it cool slightly before shredding it with two forks.

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cooked broccoli, cream cheese, sour cream, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  5. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese and grated Parmesan cheese on top.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the casserole is heated through.

  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve hot and enjoy!

Why Choose Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole?

Not only is this Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole a delicious and satisfying meal, but it also offers several health benefits. Here are some reasons why you should give this recipe a try:

1. Low Carb Goodness

By using chicken and broccoli as the main ingredients, this casserole is naturally low in carbohydrates. It’s a perfect option for those following a low carb or keto lifestyle, as it can help you maintain stable blood sugar levels and support weight loss goals.

2. Protein-Packed

Chicken is an excellent source of lean protein, which is essential for muscle growth, repair, and overall health. By incorporating chicken into this casserole, you’ll be fueling your body with the nutrients it needs to thrive.

3. Nutrient-Dense Broccoli

Broccoli is a nutritional powerhouse, packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. It adds a vibrant green color to the casserole and provides fiber, which aids in digestion and helps you feel fuller for longer.

4. Creamy and Cheesy

The combination of cream cheese, sour cream, and shredded cheddar cheese gives this casserole a creamy and cheesy texture that is simply irresistible. It adds richness and flavor to every bite, making it a crowd-pleaser for both kids and adults.

5. Easy to Make and Meal Prep

This casserole is not only delicious, but it’s also easy to make and perfect for meal prep. You can assemble it ahead of time and refrigerate it until you’re ready to bake it. It’s a convenient option for busy weekdays or when you need to feed a crowd.

Final Thoughts

This Satisfying Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is a versatile and nutritious dish that will please even the pickiest eaters. With its low carb content, high protein ingredients, and delicious flavors, it’s a winning recipe for anyone looking to enjoy a satisfying meal without compromising their health goals. Give it a try and enjoy the goodness it brings to your table!
