Keto Cereal: Nourishing Low-Carb Choices for Your First Meal


Commence your day on the appropriate foot with this scrumptious keto-pleasant cereal recipe. Ketogenic cereal is a mouthwatering and scrumptious dish that provides you all the delectable tastes of breakfast cereal with out the undesirable carbs. With nutrient-dense materials corresponding to nuts, seeds, and wholesome fats, this keto cereal will maintain you full and glad all morning lengthy.

Elements

  • 1 cup combined nuts (pecans, almonds, walnuts, and so forth.)
  • 1 cup mixed seeds (pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, chia seeds, and so forth.)
  • 1/2 cup shredded unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 1/4 cup sugar-free dried cranberries or chopped dried mango
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup crushed walnuts or different nuts
  • 2 tablespoons crushed cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a big bowl, mix the nuts, seeds, unsweetened coconut flakes, sugar-free dried cranberries or chopped dried mango, shredded coconut, and crushed walnuts or different nuts together with the cinnamon.
  3. In a small saucepan, warmth the coconut oil and heavy cream over low warmth till the oil is fully melted.
  4. Stir within the apple cider vinegar, combining effectively.
  5. Pour the melted coconut oil combination over the dry elements and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Distribute the keto cereal combination over two parchment-lined baking sheets and unfold out in a single layer.
  7. Bake within the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or till golden brown and crispy. Watch carefully, as it’s possible you’ll want to regulate the cooking time relying in your oven. Toss midway by means of cooking to make sure all sides brown evenly.
  8. Take away from the oven after baking and let the cereal chips cool utterly.
  9. As soon as cooled, break the cereal chips into bowl-sized servings, or retailer in an hermetic container for later use.

Now, your keto-pleasant breakfast cereal is prepared to take pleasure in. Top it with almond milk, unsweetened coconut milk, or your favourite nutritious yogurt for a major meal you may look ahead to each day. Don’t overlook, you can also make a giant batch of keto cereal for the week, and retailer it safely in airtight containers to maintain freshness. This versatile recipe is absolutely customizable, so you may make it uniquely yours by incorporating your favourite keto-friendly add-ins like chopped darkish chocolate or additional cinnamon.
