Mouthwatering Breakfast Pizza Muffin Recipes for a Tasty Treat


Are you tired of the same old breakfast routine? Spice up your mornings with these mouthwatering breakfast pizza muffin recipes! These delicious muffins combine the flavors of everyone’s favorite breakfast and pizza, creating a delightful treat that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re looking for a quick grab-and-go breakfast or a leisurely brunch option, these recipes have got you covered. So, let’s dive into the world of breakfast pizza muffins and discover the perfect recipe for your next morning delight!

1. Cheesy Bacon and Egg Breakfast Pizza Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a muffin tin.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in the shredded cheddar cheese, crumbled bacon, beaten eggs, and chopped green onions.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about 2/3 full.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the muffins cool for a few minutes before removing them from the tin. Enjoy!

Serve these cheesy bacon and egg breakfast pizza muffins warm for a satisfying start to your day!

2. Veggie Lover’s Breakfast Pizza Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped spinach
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a muffin tin.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the whole wheat flour, baking powder, salt, dried oregano, and dried basil.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and olive oil.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in the diced bell peppers, diced tomatoes, sliced black olives, chopped spinach, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about 2/3 full.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the muffins to cool for a few minutes before removing them from the tin. Enjoy!

These veggie lover’s breakfast pizza muffins are packed with nutritious ingredients, making them a guilt-free indulgence!

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms

Instructions:

  5. Fold in the crumbled sausage, sliced mushrooms, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about 2/3 full.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the muffins cool for a few minutes before removing them from the tin. Enjoy!

These sausage and mushroom breakfast pizza muffins are bursting with savory flavors that will satisfy your cravings.
