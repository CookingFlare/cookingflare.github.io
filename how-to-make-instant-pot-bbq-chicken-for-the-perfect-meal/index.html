How to Make Instant Pot BBQ Chicken for the Perfect Meal


The Instant Pot has transformed the way we cook, simplifying complex recipes and bringing flavor-packed meals to the table faster than ever before. One dish that the Instant Pot does exceptionally well is BBQ chicken. Whether you’re planning a casual weeknight dinner or hosting a backyard get-together, this tender, juicy, and flavorful BBQ chicken is sure to be a hit. Follow this easy step-by-step guide to create a meal that’s bursting with smoky-sweet BBQ goodness, all with minimal effort.

Why Choose the Instant Pot for BBQ Chicken?

The Instant Pot combines pressure cooking with steam retention to infuse your chicken with flavor while keeping it moist and tender. This eliminates the risk of dryness often associated with cooking chicken, particularly when preparing lean cuts like chicken breasts. It also significantly reduces the cooking time compared to grilling or slow roasting, making it ideal for busy schedules.

BBQ chicken in the Instant Pot not only tastes amazing but also allows the seasonings and sauces to penetrate the meat deeply. You’ll be amazed at how every bite delivers a perfectly balanced mix of tangy, smoky, and sweet flavors.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Before diving into the recipe, gather all the ingredients to save time during the cooking process. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Chicken:

  • 2 pounds of chicken (boneless and skinless chicken breasts or thighs; either work well)
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (choose your favorite brand or make your own for a personalized flavor profile)
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth (for added moisture and depth)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (to sear the chicken for locking in flavors)

Dry Seasoning Mix:

  • 1 teaspoon paprika (for a hint of smokiness)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder (adds a robust, savory note)
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder (for depth of flavor)
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust based on your spice tolerance)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (to enhance the overall taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (for a mild kick)

Optional Garnishes:

  • Fresh parsley, minced
  • Squeeze of lime juice
  • A sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat

For Serving:

  • Hamburger buns, sliders, or dinner rolls (for BBQ chicken sandwiches)
  • Coleslaw or potato salad (classic BBQ sides)
  • Fresh vegetables like corn on the cob or steamed broccoli

Step-by-Step Instructions for the Perfect BBQ Chicken

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by patting your chicken breasts or thighs dry with paper towels. This ensures the seasoning sticks well to the surface. Next, create the dry seasoning mix by combining paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl. Generously rub the seasoning all over the chicken pieces, coating them evenly.

Step 2: Sear for Added Flavor

Set your Instant Pot to ‘Sauté’ mode and let it heat up for a minute or two before adding the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the seasoned chicken into the pot. Sear for about 2-3 minutes per side until a light golden crust starts to form. This step locks in flavor and creates a nice texture.

Step 3: Add the Liquid Ingredients

After searing the chicken, turn off the ‘Sauté’ mode. Pour in the chicken broth, which prevents the Instant Pot from triggering its ‘Burn’ warning and helps to keep the chicken moist. Next, coat the seared chicken with about ¾ cup of BBQ sauce, saving the remaining BBQ sauce for glazing after cooking. Spread the sauce evenly over the chicken to ensure full coverage.

Step 4: Pressure Cook to Perfection

Secure the lid onto the Instant Pot and ensure the pressure release valve is set to ‘Sealing.’ Choose the ‘Pressure Cook’ or ‘Manual’ function, and set the timer for 10 minutes if using boneless chicken breasts or 12 minutes for thicker cuts like chicken thighs. When the timer goes off, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for about 5 minutes before switching the pressure valve to ‘Venting.’

Step 5: Shred or Serve Whole

Once the pressure has fully released, carefully open the lid. At this point, your chicken will be cooked to perfection—juicy, tender, and infused with BBQ flavor. If you prefer shredded BBQ chicken, use two forks to pull the chicken apart directly inside the pot. Leave the Instant Pot on ‘Keep Warm’ mode to retain the heat. Alternatively, serve the chicken whole for a plate presentation that combines rustic charm with bold flavors.

Enhance the Flavor: Glaze for the Final Touch

If you want your BBQ chicken to have that irresistible caramelized finish, a quick glaze is the way to go. Preheat your oven’s broiler, place the cooked chicken (or shredded portions) on a baking sheet, and brush each piece with the remaining BBQ sauce. Broil for about 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce becomes slightly sticky and caramelized. This step adds an extra layer of smoky sweetness, giving your BBQ chicken a true fire-grilled feel.

Tips for Perfecting Your Instant Pot BBQ Chicken

  1. Choose the Right BBQ Sauce: Whether you love smoky, spicy, or sweet BBQ flavors, the sauce you choose plays a key role in the final taste. Experiment with homemade sauces for a unique twist.

  2. Don’t Skip the Searing: Although technically optional, searing enhances the flavor and texture of the chicken, making it worth the extra few minutes.

  3. Check for Doneness: If you’re new to pressure cooking, use a meat thermometer to ensure the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F, which is considered safe for consumption.

  4. Customize the Seasonings: Feel free to tweak the seasoning blend to suit your flavor preferences. Add cayenne for more heat or brown sugar for extra sweetness.

  5. Double the Recipe for Leftovers: BBQ chicken reheats well, so consider making a larger batch. Use leftovers in sandwiches, salads, wraps, or tacos for quick meals throughout the week.

Creative Ways to Serve Instant Pot BBQ Chicken

Sandwiches or Sliders

Pile shredded BBQ chicken onto a toasted bun and add a dollop of creamy coleslaw for a Southern-inspired meal. The combination of smoky chicken and tangy slaw creates the ultimate comfort food experience.

Over Rice or Quinoa

Serve the chicken over a bed of fluffy white rice or nutrient-packed quinoa for a balanced meal. Drizzle some of the extra sauce from the Instant Pot over the grains for added flavor.

With Grilled Vegetables

Pair your BBQ chicken with grilled or roasted veggies like zucchini, bell peppers, and asparagus for a colorful plate loaded with freshness and flavor.

Loaded Baked Potatoes

Stuff shredded BBQ chicken into baked potatoes or sweet potatoes, then top with sour cream, shredded cheese, and green onions for a hearty dish that feels like a complete treat.

Wrap-Up

Instant Pot BBQ chicken is a game-changer, offering a quick, easy, and flavorful way to enjoy a classic dish. Whether you’re cooking for family or friends, this recipe delivers restaurant-quality results with minimal time and effort. By combining perfectly seasoned chicken with the magic of pressure cooking, you’ll create a meal that’s guaranteed to impress every time.

So grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the smoky, sweet perfection of homemade BBQ chicken. Your taste buds—and your guests—will thank you!
