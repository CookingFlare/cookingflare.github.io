Satisfying One-Pot Mediterranean Couscous Recipe


Are you looking for a quick, easy, and flavorful dinner option that can be prepared in one pot? Look no further than this satisfying Mediterranean couscous recipe. This one-pot wonder is a fusion of the best of Mediterranean cuisine, combining the tender goodness of chicken, the sweetness of vegetables, and the comfort of creamy sauce, all wrapped up in a bed of fluffy couscous. This recipe is perfect for a busy weeknight dinner or a weekend lunch gathering, and it's sure to become a staple in your household. With only a few ingredients and minimal prep time required, you'll be enjoying a delicious homemade meal in no time. ** Servings: 4-6 people** Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Total Time: 35 minutes Ingredients:

1 cup couscous 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces 1 cup mixed bell peppers (green, red, yellow) 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 2 cups chicken broth 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)

Instructions:

Heat oil and cook onion: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and chicken: Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute, until fragrant. Then, add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Add vegetables and spices: Add the mixed bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, oregano, paprika, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, until the vegetables begin to soften. Add couscous and chicken broth: Stir in the couscous and add the chicken broth to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Cook couscous: Simmer the couscous for 10-12 minutes, or until it has absorbed most of the liquid and is tender. Fluff the couscous with a fork to separate the grains. Stir in parsley and feta (if using): Once the couscous is cooked, stir in the chopped parsley and crumbled feta cheese (if using). Serve: Serve the Mediterranean couscous hot, garnished with additional parsley and feta cheese if desired.

Tips and Variations:

For a protein-packed variation, add 1/2 cup crumbled cooked bacon or 1/4 cup chopped cooked chorizo to the pot in step 3. If using feta cheese, reduce the amount to 1-2 tablespoons if you prefer a lighter cheese flavor. For a vegetarian version, omit the chicken and add 1 cup cooked chickpeas, 1 cup diced zucchini, or 1 cup diced eggplant to the pot in step 3. Experiment with different spice blends, such as cumin, coriander, or cyprus seasoning, to give the dish an extra boost of flavor.

Nutrition Information (per serving):

Calories: 520 Protein: 42g Fat: 22g Saturated Fat: 4g Cholesterol: 80mg Carbohydrates: 40g Fiber: 4g Sugar: 6g Sodium: 400mg

Indulge in this mouthwatering, one-pot Mediterranean couscous recipe and experience the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean cuisine in the comfort of your own home.
