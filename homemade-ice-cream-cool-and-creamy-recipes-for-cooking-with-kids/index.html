Homemade Ice Cream: Cool and Creamy Recipes for Cooking with Kids


Who doesn’t love ice cream? It’s the perfect treat on a hot summer day or even as a sweet indulgence any time of the year. And what could be better than making your own homemade ice cream with your kids? Not only is it a fun and delicious activity, but it also allows you to control the ingredients and flavors. In this blog post, we’ll share with you some cool and creamy homemade ice cream recipes that you can make with your little ones.

Classic Vanilla Ice Cream

Let’s start with a classic favorite, vanilla ice cream. This recipe is simple yet oh-so-delicious. You’ll need:

  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract

In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. Whisk until the sugar has dissolved completely. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. Once it reaches the desired consistency, transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm. Serve and enjoy!

Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Ice Cream

Next up, we have a crowd-pleaser: chocolate chip cookie dough ice cream. Your kids will love finding delicious chunks of cookie dough in every bite. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the cookie dough:

  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of mini chocolate chips

For the ice cream base:

  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract

Start by making the cookie dough. In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add the milk and vanilla extract and mix well. Gradually add the flour and mix until combined. Stir in the mini chocolate chips. Roll the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Freeze the dough balls for at least 30 minutes.

Meanwhile, prepare the ice cream base. In a separate mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract until well combined. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. During the last few minutes of churning, add the frozen cookie dough balls. Once the ice cream is ready, transfer it to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm. Serve and enjoy the delightful combination of chocolate chip cookie dough and creamy ice cream!

Strawberry Banana Frozen Yogurt

For a healthier option, try making strawberry banana frozen yogurt. This recipe is packed with fruity goodness and is perfect for those who are looking for a lighter treat. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 cups of frozen strawberries
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup of Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract

In a blender or food processor, combine the frozen strawberries, ripe bananas, Greek yogurt, honey, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy. If you prefer a chunkier texture, you can pulse the mixture a few times to leave small strawberry and banana pieces. Transfer the mixture to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm. Serve and enjoy this refreshing and guilt-free frozen yogurt!

Conclusion

Making homemade ice cream with your kids is not only a tasty endeavor but also a wonderful bonding activity. These cool and creamy recipes are just the tip of the iceberg - there are endless flavor combinations and creative toppings you can try. So grab your little ones, put on your aprons, and let the ice cream-making adventure begin!
