Healthy Paleo Chicken and Mango Lettuce Wraps


Lettuce wraps are a refreshing and light option for a healthy meal, and when you combine them with the vibrant flavors of chicken and mango, you get a dish that’s not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. This recipe for Healthy Paleo Chicken and Mango Lettuce Wraps is a perfect choice for those looking for a satisfying, yet light and wholesome meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter lettuce leaves

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the coconut aminos, olive oil, garlic, ginger, and honey. Add the sliced chicken to the bowl and toss to coat. Let the chicken marinate for 15-20 minutes.

  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the marinated chicken and cook for 5-6 minutes, or until fully cooked through.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the diced mango, red bell pepper, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Toss gently to mix everything together.

  4. To assemble the lettuce wraps, take a piece of butter lettuce and spoon some of the cooked chicken onto it. Top with the mango salsa and wrap the lettuce around the filling.

  5. Serve the lettuce wraps with any remaining mango salsa on the side for dipping.

Health Benefits

This recipe is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse. Here’s why:

Chicken

Chicken is a great source of lean protein, making it an excellent choice for those following a paleo diet. It’s also rich in essential nutrients such as vitamin B, which is important for energy production, and minerals like selenium, which supports thyroid function.

Mango

Mangoes are not only deliciously sweet but also packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. They are a great source of vitamin C, which supports immune function, and vitamin A, which is important for eye health.

Lettuce

Using lettuce leaves as wraps instead of traditional tortillas or bread is a fantastic way to reduce carb intake and increase your vegetable consumption. Lettuce is low in calories and high in water content, making it a hydrating and nutritious choice.

Coconut Aminos

Coconut aminos are a paleo-friendly alternative to soy sauce, and they provide a savory, umami flavor to the dish without the use of soy or gluten.

Conclusion

These Healthy Paleo Chicken and Mango Lettuce Wraps are a delightful combination of flavors and textures, all while being packed with nutrients. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking for a light and refreshing meal, these lettuce wraps are sure to become a favorite. Enjoy the vibrant colors and fresh taste of this dish, and delight in the knowledge that you’re nourishing your body with wholesome, natural ingredients.
