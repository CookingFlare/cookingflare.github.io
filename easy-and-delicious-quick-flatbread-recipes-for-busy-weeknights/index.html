Easy and Delicious: Quick Flatbread Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen after a long day at work? Do you crave a quick and tasty meal that doesn’t compromise on flavor? Look no further! In this blog post, we bring you a collection of easy and delicious flatbread recipes that are perfect for busy weeknights. With minimal preparation and cook time, these recipes will save you time without sacrificing taste. Let’s dive in and discover the world of quick and mouthwatering flatbreads!

1. Margherita Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made flatbread
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the flatbread on a baking sheet and brush it lightly with olive oil.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the flatbread.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Arrange the cherry tomato halves on the flatbread.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and garnish with torn basil leaves.
  9. Slice and serve hot.

2. Mediterranean Chicken Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made flatbread
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the flatbread on a baking sheet.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the mixture over the flatbread, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  5. Spread the shredded chicken over the flatbread.
  6. Top with Kalamata olives, feta cheese, red onion, and sun-dried tomatoes.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the toppings are heated through and the flatbread is crispy.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Cut into slices and serve.

3. BBQ Pulled Pork Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made flatbread
  • 1 cup cooked pulled pork
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup pickles, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the flatbread on a baking sheet.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the pulled pork with BBQ sauce until evenly coated.
  4. Spread the BBQ pulled pork over the flatbread.
  5. Top with red onion, pickles, and cheddar cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and garnish with chopped cilantro leaves.
  8. Slice and serve hot.

4. Veggie Pesto Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made flatbread
  • 1/4 cup basil pesto
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the flatbread on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread the basil pesto evenly over the flatbread.
  4. Arrange the cherry tomatoes, zucchini, red bell pepper, and red onion on top.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the vegetables.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the flatbread is crispy and the vegetables are tender.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Slice and serve.

These flatbread recipes are incredibly versatile, allowing you to experiment with different toppings and flavors. Whether you’re a fan of classic Margherita or prefer something more adventurous like BBQ pulled pork, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, the next time you’re short on time but still want a delicious homemade meal, give these quick flatbread recipes a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
