Grilled Desserts: Sweet Treats from the BBQ


Who says the BBQ is just for savory dishes? Grilling isn’t just for burgers and steaks; it’s also a fantastic way to add a smoky, caramelized flavor to your favorite desserts. If you’re looking to impress your guests or just satisfy your sweet tooth in a unique way, grilled desserts are the perfect choice. From fruit skewers to decadent cakes, here are some mouthwatering grilled dessert recipes that will elevate your BBQ experience.

Grilled Fruit Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Assorted fruits such as pineapple chunks, strawberries, and mango slices
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon
  • Wooden skewers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Thread the fruit onto the wooden skewers, alternating the different fruits.
  3. Drizzle the skewers with honey and sprinkle with a touch of cinnamon.
  4. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until the fruits are slightly caramelized.
  5. Serve the grilled fruit skewers with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful and light dessert.

Grilled Peach Melba

Ingredients:

  • Fresh peaches, halved and pitted
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Raspberry sauce
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the peach halves on the grill, cut side down, and cook for 3-4 minutes until grill marks appear.
  3. Remove the peaches from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes.
  4. Serve the grilled peaches with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, drizzle with raspberry sauce, and garnish with fresh mint leaves for a classic and elegant dessert.

Grilled Pound Cake with Berries

Ingredients:

  • Slices of pound cake
  • Mixed berries such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries
  • Whipped cream
  • Honey

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Place the slices of pound cake on the grill and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until grill marks appear.
  3. Remove the grilled pound cake from the grill and let it cool slightly.
  4. Top the grilled pound cake with mixed berries, a dollop of whipped cream, and a drizzle of honey for a delightful and indulgent dessert.

Grilled Banana S’mores

Ingredients:

  • Ripe bananas
  • Chocolate chips
  • Mini marshmallows
  • Aluminum foil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Leaving the skin on, make a lengthwise slit in each banana, making sure not to cut all the way through.
  3. Stuff the bananas with chocolate chips and mini marshmallows, then wrap them in aluminum foil.
  4. Place the foil-wrapped bananas on the grill and cook for 5-7 minutes until the chocolate and marshmallows are melted and the bananas are soft.
  5. Carefully unwrap the bananas and serve them as a fun and delicious grilled dessert.

Grilled desserts are a fantastic way to add a unique twist to your BBQ gatherings. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply enjoying a casual evening in the backyard, these sweet treats from the BBQ will surely impress your guests and delight your taste buds. So, fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in the delightful world of grilled desserts!
