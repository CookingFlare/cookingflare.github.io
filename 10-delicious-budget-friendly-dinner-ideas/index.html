10 Delicious Budget-Friendly Dinner Ideas


Cooking dinner doesn’t have to break the bank. With a little creativity and some smart shopping, you can whip up a delicious meal that’s easy on the wallet. In this post, we’ll share 10 budget-friendly dinner ideas that are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters.

1. Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

This classic Italian dish is a staple for a reason. Spaghetti is a cheap and versatile pasta that pairs perfectly with a rich meat sauce made from ground beef, onions, garlic, and canned tomatoes. Serve with some crusty bread and a green salad for a complete meal.

  • Cost: $3.50 per serving
  • Ingredients: spaghetti, ground beef, onions, garlic, canned tomatoes, tomato paste, olive oil, salt, pepper, bread, salad greens

2. Black Bean and Sweet Potato Enchiladas

These flavorful enchiladas are a vegetarian’s dream come true. Simply sauté some onions, garlic, and sweet potatoes, then add canned black beans and enchilada sauce. Roll up the mixture in tortillas, top with cheese and bake until golden brown.

  • Cost: $2.50 per serving
  • Ingredients: sweet potatoes, onions, garlic, canned black beans, enchilada sauce, tortillas, cheese, olive oil, cumin, chili powder

3. Baked Chicken Thighs with Roasted Vegetables

Who says chicken has to be expensive? Boneless, skinless chicken thighs are a affordable and tender option that pairs perfectly with a variety of roasted vegetables. Simply season the chicken with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs, then bake in the oven with some carrots, Brussels sprouts, and red potatoes.

  • Cost: $2.25 per serving
  • Ingredients: chicken thighs, carrots, Brussels sprouts, red potatoes, olive oil, salt, pepper, herbs

4. Lentil Soup

This hearty soup is a staple of many cuisines around the world. Simply sauté some onions, garlic, and carrots, then add lentils, broth, and diced tomatoes. Simmer until the lentils are tender, then season with salt and pepper to taste.

  • Cost: $1.50 per serving
  • Ingredients: lentils, onions, garlic, carrots, broth, diced tomatoes, olive oil, salt, pepper

5. Quesadillas with Beans and Cheese

These cheesy quesadillas are a fun and easy dinner option that’s perfect for a weeknight meal. Simply sauté some onions and garlic, then add canned beans and shredded cheese. Place the mixture inside tortillas and cook in a skillet until the cheese is melted and the tortillas are crispy.

  • Cost: $2.25 per serving
  • Ingredients: tortillas, canned beans, cheese, onions, garlic, olive oil, cumin, chili powder

6. Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Tomato Soup

This classic comfort food combo is a budget-friendly option that’s sure to please. Simply butter two slices of bread, place cheese in between, and grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is toasted. Serve with a bowl of homemade tomato soup for a complete meal.

  • Cost: $2.00 per serving
  • Ingredients: bread, cheese, butter, tomato soup, can of crushed tomatoes, onion, garlic, olive oil

7. Chicken and Rice Casserole

This comforting casserole is a staple of many family dinners. Simply sauté some onions and garlic, then add cooked chicken, rice, canned soup, and frozen peas. Mix everything together, then top with cheese and bake until golden brown.

  • Cost: $2.50 per serving
  • Ingredients: cooked chicken, rice, onions, garlic, canned soup, frozen peas, cheese, olive oil, salt, pepper

8. Roasted Vegetable and Bean Tacos

These flavorful tacos are a fun and easy dinner option that’s perfect for a weeknight meal. Simply roast some vegetables like broccoli, cauliflower, and carrots, then add canned beans, taco seasoning, and shredded cheese. Serve in tacos shells with your favorite toppings.

  • Cost: $2.25 per serving
  • Ingredients: vegetables, canned beans, taco seasoning, cheese, tacos shells, olive oil, cumin, chili powder

9. Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

This quick and easy stir-fry is a budget-friendly option that’s perfect for a weeknight meal. Simply sauté some onions and garlic, then add cooked chicken, frozen vegetables, and soy sauce. Serve over rice or noodles for a complete meal.

  • Cost: $2.25 per serving
  • Ingredients: cooked chicken, onions, garlic, frozen vegetables, soy sauce, rice or noodles, olive oil, salt, pepper

10. Baked Potatoes with Toppings

These baked potatoes are a fun and easy dinner option that’s perfect for a weeknight meal. Simply bake some potatoes in the oven, then top with cheese, sour cream, and canned beans. Add some diced onions and salsa for extra flavor.

  • Cost: $1.75 per serving
  • Ingredients: potatoes, cheese, sour cream, canned beans, onions, salsa, olive oil, salt, pepper

These 10 budget-friendly dinner ideas are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. With a little creativity and some smart shopping, you can whip up a delicious meal that’s easy on the wallet. So next time you’re planning dinner, give one of these recipes a try!
