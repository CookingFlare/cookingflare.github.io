Instant Pot Smothered Pork Chops: Southern Comfort


If you’re craving a taste of the South, then these Instant Pot smothered pork chops are just what you need. This classic Southern comfort food is a hearty and flavorful dish that is sure to satisfy your soul and your appetite. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have these tender pork chops and rich, savory gravy on the table in no time. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let’s dive into the delicious world of Southern cooking.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Season the Pork Chops: Start by seasoning the pork chops generously with salt and black pepper. This will help to enhance the flavor of the meat as it cooks.

  2. Sear the Pork Chops: Turn on the sauté function on your Instant Pot and heat the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the pork chops and sear them for 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a golden-brown crust. This step is crucial for locking in the juices and creating depth of flavor.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: Add the sliced onion and minced garlic to the Instant Pot and sauté for a few minutes until they start to soften and become fragrant.

  4. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the chicken broth and use a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. This will infuse the gravy with rich, caramelized flavors.

  5. Season and Pressure Cook: Stir in the Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, dried thyme, and cayenne pepper. Place the lid on the Instant Pot, set the valve to sealing, and pressure cook on high for 10 minutes. Once done, allow a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before quick releasing any remaining pressure.

  6. Create the Gravy: Carefully remove the pork chops from the Instant Pot and set them aside. In a small bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and all-purpose flour to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the liquid in the pot and turn on the sauté function. Cook for a few minutes, stirring constantly, until the gravy thickens.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Return the pork chops to the Instant Pot, spoon the rich gravy over the top, and garnish with chopped fresh parsley. These smothered pork chops are best served hot, alongside your favorite Southern sides like mashed potatoes, collard greens, or cornbread.

The Comfort of Southern Flavors

The beauty of this Instant Pot smothered pork chops recipe lies in its ability to capture the essence of Southern comfort food with minimal effort. The Instant Pot works its magic to tenderize the pork chops while infusing them with the aromatic flavors of onions, garlic, and a medley of spices. The result is a dish that embodies the warmth and hospitality of Southern cuisine, inviting you to savor each bite and revel in its soul-soothing goodness.

Embracing the Instant Pot

The Instant Pot has become a staple in many kitchens for its convenience and efficiency. It’s a versatile tool that allows you to create complex and robust flavors in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional cooking methods. With this smothered pork chops recipe, the Instant Pot streamlines the cooking process, ensuring that you can indulge in a Southern-inspired meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re a fan of Southern cuisine or simply looking to expand your Instant Pot repertoire, these smothered pork chops are a must-try. The combination of tender pork, flavorful gravy, and a touch of Southern charm makes this dish a standout choice for any occasion. So, fire up your Instant Pot, channel your inner Southern chef, and treat yourself to a comforting meal that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of the South.
