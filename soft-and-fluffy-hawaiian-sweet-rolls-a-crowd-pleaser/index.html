Soft and Fluffy Hawaiian Sweet Rolls: A Crowd-Pleaser


If there’s one thing that can instantly elevate any meal, it’s a batch of homemade Hawaiian sweet rolls. These soft, fluffy, and slightly sweet rolls are a crowd-pleaser, perfect for any occasion, from casual family dinners to holiday feasts. In this blog post, I’ll share with you my tried and tested recipe for making these delightful rolls from scratch.

Ingredients

To start off, let’s gather all the ingredients we’ll need:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup honey

Instructions

Step 1: Activate the Yeast

In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm milk and a tablespoon of the granulated sugar. Sprinkle the yeast over the mixture and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.

Step 2: Mix the Wet Ingredients

Once the yeast is activated, add the melted butter, eggs, pineapple juice, and honey to the bowl. Mix everything together until well combined.

Step 3: Combine the Dry Ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, remaining sugar, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet ingredients, stirring until a soft dough forms.

Step 4: Knead the Dough

Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, you can add a little more flour, a tablespoon at a time.

Step 5: Let the Dough Rise

Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

Step 6: Shape the Rolls

After the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into equal-sized pieces. Shape each piece into a ball and place them in a greased baking dish, making sure to leave a little space between each roll.

Step 7: Let the Rolls Rise Again

Cover the rolls with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes, or until they have doubled in size.

Step 8: Bake to Perfection

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Once the rolls have risen, bake them for 20-25 minutes, or until they turn golden brown on top. You can brush the tops with melted butter after they come out of the oven for an extra touch of richness.

Enjoy!

Once the rolls are baked to perfection, allow them to cool for a few minutes before serving. These Soft and Fluffy Hawaiian Sweet Rolls are best enjoyed warm, either on their own or alongside your favorite dishes. The slightly sweet flavor and pillowy texture of these rolls will surely make them a hit at your next gathering.

So there you have it, a simple and delightful recipe for making Soft and Fluffy Hawaiian Sweet Rolls from scratch. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these rolls are sure to impress. Give this recipe a try and watch as these crowd-pleasers disappear from the table in no time!
