Spicy and Tangy Thai Shrimp Lettuce Wraps


If you’re looking for a flavorful and refreshing dish that’s perfect for a light lunch or a healthy appetizer, then these Spicy and Tangy Thai Shrimp Lettuce Wraps are just what you need. Packed with vibrant colors and bold flavors, these lettuce wraps are a delightful combination of spicy, tangy, and savory tastes that will tantalize your taste buds. Plus, they’re incredibly easy to make, making them an ideal choice for busy weekdays or relaxed weekends.

Ingredients

To create these mouthwatering Spicy and Tangy Thai Shrimp Lettuce Wraps, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup of shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of chopped peanuts
  • 1 head of butter lettuce, leaves separated
  • For the sauce:
    • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
    • 2 tablespoons of fresh lime juice
    • 1 tablespoon of honey
    • 1 teaspoon of chili garlic sauce
    • 1 teaspoon of grated fresh ginger
    • 1/2 teaspoon of sesame oil

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Shrimp

Start by heating the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Then, add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

Step 2: Make the Sauce

In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, fresh lime juice, honey, chili garlic sauce, grated ginger, and sesame oil until well combined. This zesty and flavorful sauce will be the perfect complement to the shrimp and vegetables.

Step 3: Cook the Vegetables

Using the same skillet, add the diced red bell pepper and shredded carrots. Sauté the vegetables for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp. Then, return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and pour the prepared sauce over the shrimp and vegetables. Stir everything together and let it cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 4: Assemble the Lettuce Wraps

Once the shrimp and vegetables are cooked to perfection, it’s time to assemble the lettuce wraps. Take a leaf of butter lettuce and spoon a generous portion of the shrimp and vegetable mixture onto the center of the leaf. Sprinkle some chopped fresh cilantro and peanuts on top for an extra burst of flavor and texture.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Arrange the assembled lettuce wraps on a platter and serve them with extra lime wedges on the side for a tangy kick. These Spicy and Tangy Thai Shrimp Lettuce Wraps are best enjoyed fresh, so dig in and savor the delightful combination of flavors with each bite.

Conclusion

These Spicy and Tangy Thai Shrimp Lettuce Wraps are a culinary delight that brings together the bold and vibrant flavors of Thailand in a light and refreshing dish. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy meal or a flavorful appetizer to impress your guests, these lettuce wraps are sure to be a hit. The combination of spicy shrimp, crisp vegetables, and zesty sauce wrapped in delicate butter lettuce leaves creates a harmonious blend of textures and flavors that will leave you craving for more. So, why not give this recipe a try and bring a taste of Thailand to your table? Your taste buds will thank you!
