Irresistible Nut-Free Apple Pie Recipe


If you’re a fan of apple pie but have a nut allergy or simply prefer to skip the nuts, this irresistible nut-free apple pie recipe is perfect for you! With a buttery crust and a deliciously spiced apple filling, this pie will have your taste buds dancing with joy. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 6-7 medium-sized apples (choose a mix of tart and sweet varieties), peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

For the topping:

  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar for the crust. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, mixing with a fork until the dough comes together. Divide the dough into two equal parts and shape each into a disk. Wrap them in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. While the dough is chilling, prepare the filling. In a separate bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.

  5. Roll out one disk of dough on a lightly floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Transfer the dough to the pie dish and gently press it into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edges.

  6. Pour the apple filling into the prepared crust and drizzle the melted butter over the top.

  7. In a small bowl, combine the flour and sugar for the topping. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle the crumb topping evenly over the apple filling.

  8. Roll out the second disk of dough and cut it into thin strips. Create a lattice pattern on top of the pie by weaving the strips over and under each other. Trim the excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie.

  9. Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

  10. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for at least 1 hour before serving. This allows the filling to set and makes for easier slicing.

  11. Serve the nut-free apple pie warm or at room temperature, and if desired, with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of fresh whipped cream.

Now you can enjoy a slice of this irresistible nut-free apple pie! The buttery crust, perfectly spiced apple filling, and delicious crumb topping make this pie a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re serving it at a family gathering, a holiday celebration, or simply as a sweet treat for yourself, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, indulge in the flavors of fall with this delightful dessert!
