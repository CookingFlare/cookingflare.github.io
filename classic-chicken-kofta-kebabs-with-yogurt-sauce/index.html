Classic Chicken Kofta Kebabs with Yogurt Sauce


Kofta kebabs, a staple in Middle Eastern and South Asian cuisine, are a delicious and versatile dish made from ground meat mixed with a variety of herbs and spices. Among the most popular variations is the chicken kofta kebab, which offers a leaner alternative to the traditional lamb or beef versions while maintaining the juicy, flavorful essence that makes koftas so beloved. Served with a refreshing yogurt sauce, these chicken kofta kebabs make a perfect dish for a cozy dinner, a summer barbecue, or even a party appetizer. In this post, we’ll walk you through the process of making these flavorful chicken koftas from scratch, paired with a tangy yogurt sauce that perfectly complements the richness of the grilled meat.

What Are Kofta Kebabs?

Kofta kebabs are essentially spiced meatballs that are shaped into patties or logs and skewered for grilling. The meat is typically mixed with onions, herbs, and spices, which infuse the kebabs with deep, aromatic flavors. While the exact origins of kofta can be traced to Middle Eastern and South Asian regions, variations of these savory kebabs can be found in cuisines all over the world, including Greece, Turkey, Iran, and India.

In the case of chicken kofta kebabs, ground chicken is the base. It’s light, tender, and pairs wonderfully with the seasoning mix that creates the distinct flavor profile. These koftas are then grilled or baked, giving them that delicious smoky char and juicy texture on the inside.

Why Chicken?

Chicken kofta kebabs are a great alternative to the more traditional beef or lamb versions. Chicken is leaner and offers a lighter taste, making it a fantastic option for anyone looking to enjoy koftas without feeling weighed down. The subtle taste of chicken also allows the spices and herbs used in the kebab mixture to truly shine. Moreover, chicken koftas are quicker to cook, making them an ideal option for busy weeknights or impromptu gatherings.

Ingredients for Chicken Kofta Kebabs

To make these delicious kebabs, you’ll need the following ingredients:

For the Koftas:

  • 500 grams ground chicken – Make sure to use chicken with a bit of fat, as this will help the koftas remain moist and juicy when grilled.
  • 1 small onion – Finely chopped to add flavor and moisture to the kebabs.
  • 2 cloves garlic – Minced for an aromatic kick.
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley – Chopped, adds a refreshing herbaceous flavor.
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro – Chopped, another herb that brightens the flavor of the kebabs.
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin – Gives the kebabs their earthy, slightly smoky flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander – Adds a citrusy undertone that complements the other spices.
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon – A warm, sweet spice that balances the savoriness of the other seasonings.
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric – Adds color and a mild, earthy taste.
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder – For a hint of heat; you can adjust this based on your spice tolerance.
  • Salt and pepper to taste – Enhances the flavors of the meat and spices.
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs – To bind the mixture together, giving the koftas the right texture.
  • 1 egg – Acts as a binding agent, helping the koftas hold their shape while cooking.
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil – Helps in binding and adds moisture to the mixture.

For the Yogurt Sauce:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt – Greek yogurt works great here, as it’s thick and creamy.
  • 1 tablespoon fresh mint – Finely chopped, gives the sauce a refreshing flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin – Adds a smoky flavor that complements the grilled koftas.
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice – For a touch of acidity that brightens the sauce.
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder – For an extra layer of flavor.
  • Salt to taste – Enhances the flavors of the yogurt sauce.
  • A pinch of cayenne pepper (optional) – Adds a little extra kick.

Making the Chicken Kofta Kebabs

Now that we have all the ingredients ready, let’s dive into the process of making the chicken kofta kebabs. The method is straightforward, but it requires a little bit of time and attention to detail to ensure the koftas are tender and flavorful.

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Mixture

Start by placing the ground chicken in a large mixing bowl. Add the finely chopped onion, garlic, parsley, and cilantro. The combination of herbs and onions will give the koftas a burst of flavor. Then, add the spices: cumin, coriander, cinnamon, turmeric, and red chili powder. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Finally, mix in the breadcrumbs and the egg, which will help bind the mixture together and give it the right texture.

Use your hands to combine all the ingredients thoroughly. Don’t overmix, as that can make the mixture dense. You want the mixture to be well combined but still light and airy. If the mixture feels too wet, add a little more breadcrumbs until it reaches a consistency that holds its shape.

Step 2: Shape the Koftas

Once the mixture is ready, it’s time to shape the kebabs. Take a small amount of the chicken mixture and roll it into a ball. Then, gently shape the ball into an oval or log shape. You can also mold them around skewers for easier grilling, or simply form them into patties if you prefer not to use skewers.

If you’re using skewers, make sure to wet them with water beforehand to prevent the chicken from sticking. The skewers also help ensure even cooking, so they are a great option when grilling.

Place the shaped koftas on a plate or tray and refrigerate them for at least 30 minutes. This will help them firm up and hold their shape better while cooking.

Step 3: Grill the Koftas

Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grill grates or pan to prevent sticking. If you’re using skewers, place the skewers on the grill and cook the koftas for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken is fully cooked and has reached an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

If you’re cooking the koftas in a pan, cook them for about 5-6 minutes per side, ensuring they are golden brown on the outside and cooked through on the inside.

Step 4: Prepare the Yogurt Sauce

While the koftas are grilling, it’s time to make the yogurt sauce. In a small bowl, combine the yogurt with the finely chopped mint, cumin, lemon juice, garlic powder, and a pinch of cayenne pepper (if using). Stir everything together until smooth, and taste for seasoning. Adjust the salt or add more lemon juice to your liking. The yogurt sauce should be creamy, refreshing, and slightly tangy, providing the perfect contrast to the savory koftas.

Step 5: Serve

Once the koftas are cooked through and golden brown, remove them from the grill or pan and let them rest for a few minutes. This helps lock in the juices and keeps them tender. Serve the chicken kofta kebabs with the cool, tangy yogurt sauce on the side for dipping.

These koftas go wonderfully with a side of warm naan, pita bread, or a fresh salad. You can also serve them with rice pilaf or roasted vegetables for a complete meal.

Tips for Perfect Chicken Kofta Kebabs

  • Don’t overwork the meat mixture: Overmixing the chicken can result in dense, tough koftas. Mix the ingredients gently and just until they are combined.
  • Use ground chicken with some fat: Chicken breast is lean but can dry out when grilled. A combination of chicken breast and thigh or chicken with a little fat ensures juicy koftas.
  • Refrigerate before grilling: Chilling the koftas before grilling helps them hold their shape and prevents them from falling apart.
  • Customize the spices: Feel free to adjust the seasoning to suit your taste. You can add more heat with chili powder or a different spice like smoked paprika for an added smoky flavor.

Conclusion

Classic chicken kofta kebabs with yogurt sauce are an incredibly satisfying and flavorful dish that will impress your family and friends. With their tender texture, vibrant spices, and refreshing yogurt dip, they’re the perfect balance of savory and cool. Whether you’re grilling on a summer evening or preparing a weeknight dinner, these koftas are sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. With a few simple ingredients and some easy steps, you can create a truly delicious and memorable meal.

Enjoy your homemade chicken kofta kebabs, and don’t forget to pair them with a chilled glass of your favorite drink for the ultimate dining experience.
