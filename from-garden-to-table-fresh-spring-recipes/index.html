From Garden to Table: Fresh Spring Recipes


Welcome to our culinary paradise where we celebrate the vibrant flavors of spring with freshest ingredients straight from the garden to your table. Spring is a time of rejuvenation when nature comes alive, and our plates reflect the bounty of the season. In this collection of recipes, we embrace the essence of spring with dishes that are light, flavorful, and bursting with colorful goodness.

Fresh Herb Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb pasta of your choice

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine basil, parsley, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while pulsing until smooth.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  5. Toss cooked pasta with the fresh herb pesto.
  6. Serve warm and garnish with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Spring Vegetable Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup asparagus, chopped
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat vegetable broth and keep it simmering.
  2. In a separate pan, sauté onion and garlic until translucent.
  3. Add Arborio rice and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in white wine and stir until absorbed.
  5. Gradually add hot broth, stirring continuously until rice is creamy.
  6. Stir in asparagus and peas, cooking until tender.
  7. Remove from heat and stir in Parmesan cheese.
  8. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh mint leaves.

Strawberry Spinach Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • Balsamic vinaigrette dressing

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, toss baby spinach with sliced strawberries.
  2. Sprinkle feta cheese and sliced almonds on top.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette dressing and toss to combine.
  4. Serve immediately as a refreshing side salad.

Lemon Blueberry Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 lemon (zest and juice)
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs, lemon zest, and lemon juice.
  4. Mix in flour, baking powder, and salt alternately with milk.
  5. Gently fold in blueberries.
  6. Pour batter into the prepared pan and bake for 40-45 minutes.
  7. Let the cake cool before slicing and serving.

Enjoy these delightful spring recipes that bring the freshness of the season to your table. Embrace the flavors of nature’s bounty and savor every bite. Happy cooking!
