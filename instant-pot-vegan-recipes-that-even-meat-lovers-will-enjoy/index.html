Instant Pot Vegan Recipes That Even Meat Lovers Will Enjoy


Are you a meat lover looking to explore the world of vegan cuisine? Or maybe you’re already a vegan, but you want to impress your carnivorous friends with delicious plant-based dishes. Look no further because we’ve got you covered! In this blog post, we will share some mouthwatering Instant Pot vegan recipes that are sure to satisfy even the most dedicated meat eaters.

1. Lentil and Vegetable Stew

This hearty stew is packed with flavor and nutrients. Start by sautéing onions, garlic, and carrots in the Instant Pot until they become tender. Then add a mix of lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and your favorite herbs and spices. Lock the lid in place, set the Instant Pot to high pressure, and let it cook for about 15 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, release the pressure and serve this comforting stew with a side of crusty bread. It’s the perfect dish for a cozy night in!

2. BBQ Jackfruit Sandwiches

Jackfruit is a magical fruit that can mimic the texture and taste of pulled pork when cooked. To make these BBQ jackfruit sandwiches, simply combine canned jackfruit, BBQ sauce, onion powder, garlic powder, and a touch of maple syrup in the Instant Pot. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes, then use a fork to shred the jackfruit. Serve the flavorful mixture on a bun with some coleslaw for a delicious and satisfying sandwich that will have even the most die-hard meat lover asking for seconds.

3. Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Who says you need dairy to make a creamy and indulgent risotto? This Instant Pot vegan version will prove them wrong. Sauté mushrooms, onions, and garlic in the Instant Pot until they release their juices and become golden brown. Add arborio rice, vegetable broth, and a splash of white wine. Lock the lid, cook on high pressure for 6 minutes, and let the pressure release naturally. Stir in some nutritional yeast and plant-based butter for that velvety texture and cheesy flavor. Garnish with fresh herbs and enjoy a decadent and satisfying meal.

4. Spicy Vegan Chili

This Instant Pot vegan chili is loaded with protein and spices that will satisfy any meat lover’s craving for a hearty and flavorful dish. Start by sautéing onions, garlic, bell peppers, and jalapeños in the Instant Pot. Add a mix of kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and a blend of chili powder, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Cook on high pressure for 12 minutes, then let the pressure release naturally. Serve this spicy chili with a dollop of vegan sour cream and some cornbread for a complete meal that will leave everyone wanting more.

5. Vegan Pho

Pho, a traditional Vietnamese noodle soup, is usually made with beef or chicken broth. But this vegan version is just as fragrant and delicious. In the Instant Pot, combine vegetable broth, ginger, star anise, cinnamon, cloves, and soy sauce. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes to infuse the flavors. Meanwhile, prepare your rice noodles and a mix of fresh herbs and vegetables like bean sprouts, cilantro, and lime wedges. Once the broth is ready, strain it and pour it over the noodles and veggies. Sprinkle with some sliced tofu or mushrooms for added protein. This vegan pho is a comforting and satisfying dish that will transport you to the streets of Vietnam.

With these Instant Pot vegan recipes, you can show your meat-loving friends that plant-based meals can be just as delicious and satisfying as their animal-based counterparts. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just dipping your toes into the world of vegan cuisine, these recipes are sure to impress and leave everyone wanting more. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to embark on a flavorful and cruelty-free culinary adventure!
