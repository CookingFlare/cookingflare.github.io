Easy Slow Cooker Chicken Cacciatore Recipe


If you’re on the hunt for a delicious, comforting meal that practically cooks itself, look no further than this Easy Slow Cooker Chicken Cacciatore recipe. This Italian classic is packed with flavors, making it a perfect dish for family dinners, meal prep, or a cozy night in. With minimal effort required and the convenience of a slow cooker, you’ll find this recipe to be both practical and satisfying.

What is Chicken Cacciatore?

Chicken Cacciatore, or “hunter’s chicken,” is a traditional Italian dish that features chicken simmered in a rich tomato sauce with a medley of vegetables and herbs. The beauty of this dish lies in its versatility; you can customize it with your favorite ingredients while maintaining its classic flavor profile. The slow cooker method allows the chicken to become incredibly tender and infused with the delicious sauce, making each bite a mouthwatering experience.

Why Use a Slow Cooker?

Using a slow cooker for Chicken Cacciatore offers several advantages:

  • Hands-Free Cooking: Once you’ve prepped your ingredients and set them in the slow cooker, you can go about your day without worrying about dinner.
  • Enhanced Flavors: Slow cooking allows the flavors of the herbs, spices, and vegetables to meld together beautifully, resulting in a rich and complex sauce.
  • Tender Meat: The low and slow cooking process breaks down the chicken, making it tender and juicy, perfect for shredding or serving whole.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To create this easy slow cooker Chicken Cacciatore, gather the following ingredients:

  • Chicken: Use bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs or breasts for the best flavor and moisture.
  • Bell Peppers: Red and green bell peppers add sweetness and color.
  • Onion: A medium-sized onion provides a strong flavor base.
  • Garlic: Fresh garlic cloves will add a fragrant punch.
  • Canned Tomatoes: Use crushed or diced tomatoes for the sauce.
  • Chicken Broth: This adds depth to the sauce.
  • Olives: Black olives lend a briny flavor that complements the dish.
  • Herbs: Fresh or dried basil, oregano, and thyme are essential for that authentic Italian taste.
  • Salt and Pepper: For seasoning.
  • Red Wine (optional): A splash of red wine enhances the sauce’s richness.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by seasoning the chicken pieces generously with salt and pepper. This enhances the flavor right from the start. If you prefer, you can sear the chicken in a skillet over medium-high heat for a few minutes on each side until golden brown. This step is optional but adds an extra layer of flavor.

Step 2: Chop the Vegetables

While the chicken is cooking, chop the bell peppers, onion, and garlic. Aim for roughly equal-sized pieces to ensure they cook evenly. The vibrant colors of the bell peppers will not only make the dish visually appealing but also add a sweet, crunchy texture.

Step 3: Combine Ingredients in the Slow Cooker

In your slow cooker, layer the chopped vegetables at the bottom. This will create a flavorful bed for the chicken. Place the seasoned chicken on top of the vegetables. Pour the canned tomatoes and chicken broth over the chicken, followed by the olives and herbs. If you’re using red wine, add it now for that extra depth of flavor. Stir gently to combine, ensuring the chicken is mostly submerged in the liquid.

Step 4: Slow Cook

Cover the slow cooker and set it to low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours. The slow cooking will allow the chicken to absorb all the wonderful flavors of the sauce. You’ll know it’s done when the chicken is tender and easily pulls apart with a fork.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once it’s cooked, shred the chicken if desired, or serve the pieces whole over a bed of pasta, rice, or polenta. Spoon the rich sauce and vegetables over the top, and garnish with fresh basil or parsley for added freshness.

Tips for the Perfect Chicken Cacciatore

  • Customization: Feel free to add other vegetables such as mushrooms, zucchini, or carrots to the mix. Each addition will enhance the flavor and nutrition of the dish.
  • Make It Spicy: If you enjoy a bit of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • Leftovers: This dish makes fantastic leftovers. The flavors deepen even more after a day in the fridge. Just reheat gently on the stove or in the microwave.

Pairing Suggestions

To round out your meal, consider serving Chicken Cacciatore with:

  • Crusty Bread: Perfect for soaking up the delicious sauce.
  • Salad: A simple green salad with a light vinaigrette complements the richness of the dish.
  • Wine: A nice red wine, such as Chianti or Merlot, pairs beautifully with this Italian classic.

Storage and Reheating

If you have any leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. You can also freeze Chicken Cacciatore for up to 3 months. To reheat, simply thaw in the refrigerator overnight and warm on the stove over low heat, adding a splash of chicken broth if the sauce is too thick.

Conclusion

This Easy Slow Cooker Chicken Cacciatore is a must-try for any home cook looking to create a flavorful, comforting meal with minimal effort. With its rich sauce, tender chicken, and colorful vegetables, it’s sure to become a family favorite. The best part? You can set it and forget it, allowing you to enjoy your day while the slow cooker does all the work. Try it today and savor the flavors of Italy in your very own kitchen!
