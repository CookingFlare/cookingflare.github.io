Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Recipe: A Chinese-Style Stir-Fry


Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, making it possible to prepare a wide range of dishes with ease and convenience. One of the most popular and beloved recipes in the Instant Pot community is the beef and broccoli stir-fry. This classic Chinese dish is a staple in many Asian households, and with the Instant Pot, it’s easier than ever to make it at home. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a delicious and authentic Chinese-style beef and broccoli stir-fry using your Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef strips (sirloin or ribeye work well)
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Scallions, chopped (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Beef

In a large bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and cornstarch. Add the beef strips and toss to coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours.

Step 2: Cook the Beef

Remove the beef from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Press the “Saute” button on the Instant Pot and heat the pot until it reads “HOT”. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil and swirl it around. Add the beef and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.

Step 3: Cook the Broccoli

Add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil to the pot. Add the broccoli and cook until it starts to soften, about 2-3 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for an additional minute, until the broccoli is tender but still crisp.

Step 4: Add the Beef Broth and Simmer

Add the beef broth, soy sauce, and sesame oil to the pot. Stir to combine, then add the cooked beef back to the pot. Close the lid and make sure the valve is set to “SEALING”. Press the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” button and set the cooking time to 5 minutes at high pressure.

Step 5: Quick Release and Serve

When the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure. Open the lid and stir the beef and broccoli to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped scallions (if desired) and serve hot over rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations

  • For a more intense flavor, use bone broth instead of beef broth.
  • Add some heat to your stir-fry by incorporating red pepper flakes or sriracha.
  • Substitute beef with chicken or pork for a different protein option.
  • Add some crunch to your stir-fry by incorporating chopped nuts or sesame seeds.
  • Experiment with different seasonings, such as five-spice powder or hoisin sauce, to give your stir-fry a unique flavor.

Conclusion

This Instant Pot beef and broccoli recipe is a delicious and easy way to make a classic Chinese dish at home. With its tender beef, crisp broccoli, and savory sauce, it’s a crowd-pleaser that’s sure to become a staple in your household. Whether you’re a busy professional or a busy parent, the Instant Pot makes it easy to cook a healthy and delicious meal in no time. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and experience the convenience and flavor of Instant Pot cooking for yourself!
