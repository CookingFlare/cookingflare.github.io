Tasty Tres Leches Cake: A Decadent Mexican Dessert


If you’re a fan of luscious, indulgent desserts, then you’re in for a treat with this Tres Leches Cake recipe. Originating from Mexico, this delectable dessert is a favorite at celebrations and gatherings, and it’s not hard to see why. The combination of light sponge cake soaked in three different kinds of milk and topped with fluffy whipped cream makes for a truly heavenly treat. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through the steps to create your own Tres Leches Cake that will impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 5 large eggs, separated
  • 1 cup granulated sugar, divided
  • 1/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Three Milks Mixture:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 ounces) evaporated milk
  • 1/4 cup whole milk

For the Whipped Cream Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.

  3. In another bowl, beat the egg yolks with 3/4 cup of sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in the milk and vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites until soft peaks form. Gradually add the remaining 1/4 cup of sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form.

  5. Gently fold the egg yolk mixture into the egg whites until just combined. Then, fold in the flour mixture until no streaks remain.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top with a spatula. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. While the cake is baking, prepare the three milks mixture by combining the sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and whole milk in a bowl.

  8. Once the cake is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes. Then, poke the surface of the cake with a fork or skewer all over.

  9. Slowly pour the three milks mixture over the cake, allowing it to soak in as much as possible. Cover the cake with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.

  10. Before serving, make the whipped cream topping by beating the heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form. Spread the whipped cream over the top of the chilled cake.

Serving

Slice the Tres Leches Cake into squares and serve chilled. The cake will be moist, creamy, and utterly irresistible.

Conclusion

Tres Leches Cake is a delightful dessert that is sure to captivate your taste buds with its rich, creamy texture and sweet flavor. Whether you’re hosting a fiesta or simply craving a special treat, this Mexican delicacy is a must-try. With this easy-to-follow recipe, you can bring a taste of Mexico to your own kitchen and delight your loved ones with this decadent delight. So, what are you waiting for? Get ready to dive into a slice of Tres Leches Cake and experience a little piece of dessert paradise!
