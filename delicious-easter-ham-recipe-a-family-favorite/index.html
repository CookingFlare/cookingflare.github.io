Delicious Easter Ham Recipe: A Family Favorite


Easter is a special time of year when families gather to celebrate and create lasting memories. And what better way to bring everyone together than with a mouthwatering Easter ham? This delicious recipe has been a family favorite for generations, and it’s sure to become a beloved tradition in your home too.

Ingredients

  • 1 bone-in ham, approximately 8-10 pounds
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C).

  2. Remove the ham from its packaging and place it on a large roasting pan, preferably with a rack. This will allow the heat to circulate evenly around the ham, ensuring a tender and juicy result.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar, honey, Dijon mustard, ground cloves, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. Using a basting brush, generously coat the entire surface of the ham with the prepared glaze. Make sure to get into all the nooks and crannies for maximum flavor.

  5. Cover the ham loosely with aluminum foil, making sure to leave some space for the heat to circulate. This will help prevent the ham from drying out while it cooks.

  6. Place the ham in the preheated oven and roast for approximately 15 minutes per pound. For an 8-10 pound ham, this will be around 2 to 2.5 hours.

  7. After the first hour of cooking, remove the foil and baste the ham with the glaze again. This will add an extra layer of flavor and give the ham a beautiful caramelized finish.

  8. Continue roasting the ham, basting every 30 minutes, until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C). Use a meat thermometer to check for doneness.

  9. Once the ham is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it rest for about 15 minutes before carving. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a tender and succulent ham.

  10. Carve the ham into thick slices and serve with your favorite Easter side dishes. Whether it’s creamy mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or a fresh salad, this ham will be the star of the meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of flavor, you can stud the ham with whole cloves before applying the glaze. This will infuse the meat with a subtle, aromatic taste.

  • If you prefer a sweeter glaze, you can add a tablespoon or two of maple syrup to the mixture. This will give the ham a delightful maple flavor that pairs perfectly with the savory meat.

  • Leftover ham can be used in a variety of dishes, such as sandwiches, soups, and casseroles. Don’t let any of this delicious ham go to waste!

  • Feel free to experiment with different spices and herbs to personalize the glaze. Adding a hint of cayenne pepper or smoked paprika can give the ham a spicy kick, while fresh herbs like rosemary or thyme can add a fragrant touch.

Conclusion

This Delicious Easter Ham Recipe is a true family favorite that will make your Easter celebration unforgettable. The combination of sweet and savory flavors, coupled with the tender and juicy meat, will have everyone coming back for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a large gathering or enjoying an intimate meal with loved ones, this ham is sure to be a hit. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create a culinary masterpiece that will be cherished for years to come. Happy Easter and happy cooking!
