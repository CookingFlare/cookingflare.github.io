Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction and Roasted Vegetables and Garlic Mashed Potatoes and Rosemary and Thyme: A Valentine's Day Main Course Recipe


Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction and Roasted Vegetables and Garlic Mashed Potatoes and Rosemary and Thyme: A Valentine’s Day Main Course Recipe

Valentine’s Day is a special occasion to celebrate love and romance, and what better way to do so than by preparing a delicious and indulgent meal for your significant other? This recipe for Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction, Roasted Vegetables, and Garlic Mashed Potatoes is the perfect main course to impress your loved one on this special day. The combination of tender filet mignon, rich red wine reduction, flavorful roasted vegetables, and creamy garlic mashed potatoes seasoned with rosemary and thyme is sure to set the stage for a memorable and romantic evening.

Ingredients:

For the Filet Mignon:

  • 2 filet mignon steaks
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 3 cloves of garlic, crushed

For the Red Wine Reduction:

  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and black pepper

For the Roasted Vegetables:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as carrots, bell peppers, and zucchini), cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

For the Garlic Mashed Potatoes:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Fresh rosemary and thyme for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Filet Mignon:

    • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Season the filet mignon steaks generously with salt and black pepper.
    • In a large oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over high heat. Add the steaks and sear for 2 minutes on each side until browned.
    • Add the butter, thyme, and crushed garlic to the skillet. Transfer the skillet to the oven and roast for 5-7 minutes for medium-rare doneness. Remove from the oven and let the steaks rest for 5 minutes before serving.

  2. Make the Red Wine Reduction:

    • In a saucepan, combine the red wine, beef broth, balsamic vinegar, and honey. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer until the liquid is reduced by half. Season with salt and black pepper.

  3. Roast the Vegetables:

    • Toss the mixed vegetables with olive oil, salt, black pepper, and dried thyme. Spread them out on a baking sheet and roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes until tender and caramelized.

  4. Prepare the Garlic Mashed Potatoes:

    • Boil the potatoes in a large pot of salted water until tender. Drain and return the potatoes to the pot.
    • In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream, butter, and minced garlic until the butter is melted. Pour the mixture over the potatoes and mash until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and black pepper.

  5. Assemble the Dish:

    • Place the filet mignon on a serving plate and drizzle with the red wine reduction. Serve the roasted vegetables and garlic mashed potatoes on the side. Garnish the potatoes with fresh rosemary and thyme for an elegant touch.

This Valentine’s Day, treat your loved one to a restaurant-quality meal with this exquisite Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction, Roasted Vegetables, and Garlic Mashed Potatoes. The tender and succulent filet mignon paired with the rich red wine reduction, flavorful roasted vegetables, and creamy garlic mashed potatoes will surely make for a romantic and unforgettable dining experience. Enjoy this special meal with a glass of your favorite wine and create cherished memories with your significant other. Cheers to love, romance, and good food!
