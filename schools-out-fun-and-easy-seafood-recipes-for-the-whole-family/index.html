School's Out: Fun and Easy Seafood Recipes for the Whole Family


Summer is fast approaching, and for many families, this means a break from the daily school routine. With more time on hand and the sunny weather to enjoy, it’s the perfect opportunity to gather your loved ones for a delicious seafood feast. In this blog post, we’ve compiled a selection of fun and easy seafood recipes to excite your taste buds, while also ensuring that they are beginner-friendly and suitable for the whole family.

1. Grilled Shrimp Skewers

An all-time favorite, this delicious dish requires minimal preparation time and offers a healthy, tasty treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper
  • Rosemary sprigs (for garnish)
  • Wooden skewers (soaked in water for at least 30 minutes)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and mix well, making sure each piece is well coated.

  3. Thread the shrimp onto the soaked skewers and discard the marinade.

  4. Preheat your grill on medium heat, and then place the skewers on the grill, cooking for 2-3 minutes each side until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.

  5. Garnish with fresh rosemary sprigs and serve immediately.

2. Fish Tacos with Mango-Avocado Salsa

Spicy, crunchy, and perfectly balanced, these fish tacos are sure to be a hit at your next backyard BBQ.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lbs white fish (such as tilapia or cod), cut into 1-inch strips
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • Mango-Avocado Salsa (recipe follows)
  • Sour cream, shredded cabbage, and lime wedges for serving

Mango-Avocado Salsa Ingredients:

  • 1 mango, peeled and diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. In a bowl, mix the olive oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Add the fish strips to the bowl and coat with the seasoning.

  3. Transfer the fish onto a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes until cooked through.

  4. While the fish is baking, prepare the Mango-Avocado Salsa by combining all its ingredients in a medium bowl and stirring well.

  5. Warm the tortillas on a griddle or in the microwave, then serve them with the cooked fish and Mango-Avocado Salsa. Complete the plate with a dollop of sour cream, shredded cabbage, and a wedge of lime.

3. Lemon Garlic Butter Crab Linguine

A classic Italian meal with a seafood twist, this dish is perfect for an elegant family dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz linguine pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 8 oz cream cheese, cubed
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 lb crab meat
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water, following the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta water before draining.

  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat, then add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.

  3. Pour the white wine into the pan, allowing it to reduce by half.

  4. Add the chicken broth, cream cheese, and unsalted butter, stirring until the cream cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth.

  5. Stir in the lemon juice and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Add the cooked crab meat and pasta to the sauce, combining well. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water to achieve the desired consistency.

  7. Serve the Lemon Garlic Butter Crab Linguine drizzled with extra virgin olive oil, garnished with chopped parsley.

With these fun and easy seafood recipes, you can spend your school-free days creating joyful memories with your loved ones, all while enjoying your favorite ocean’s bounty. Happy cooking!
