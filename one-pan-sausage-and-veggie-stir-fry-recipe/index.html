One-Pan Sausage and Veggie Stir-Fry Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious weeknight dinner that’s packed with flavor and healthy ingredients, then this one-pan sausage and veggie stir-fry is the perfect recipe for you. Not only is it easy to make, but it’s also a great way to get a variety of colorful vegetables into your diet. This stir-fry is a fantastic option for those busy evenings when you want a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 Italian sausages, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by slicing the Italian sausages, bell peppers, and onion. Mince the garlic cloves and set all the vegetables aside. This preparation will help streamline the cooking process.

  2. Sear the Sausages: In a large pan or wok, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced sausages to the pan and cook them until they are browned on all sides. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Once the sausages are cooked, transfer them to a plate and set them aside.

  3. Sauté the Veggies: In the same pan, add a bit more olive oil if needed. Sauté the sliced bell peppers, onion, snap peas, and broccoli florets until they are tender-crisp, stirring frequently. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Once the vegetables are cooked to your liking, add the minced garlic to the pan and cook for an additional 30 seconds. Then, return the cooked sausages to the pan.

  5. Add the Sauces: In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Pour this mixture over the sausage and vegetable mixture in the pan. Toss everything together until the sauce is evenly distributed and the ingredients are well coated. Allow the stir-fry to cook for another 1-2 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  6. Serve: Once everything is heated through and well combined, remove the pan from the heat. Season the stir-fry with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the sausage and veggie stir-fry over cooked rice or quinoa for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips for Success

  • Use any combination of your favorite vegetables in this stir-fry. Feel free to experiment with different flavors and textures to suit your preferences.
  • For a spicy kick, add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a dash of Sriracha sauce to the stir-fry.
  • To make this recipe even more wholesome, consider using turkey or chicken sausages instead of pork.

This one-pan sausage and veggie stir-fry is a versatile dish that can be customized to fit your taste and dietary preferences. It’s a great way to incorporate a variety of vegetables into your meals while enjoying the savory goodness of sausages. Plus, with only one pan to clean up, this recipe is a time-saving and efficient option for any night of the week.

Give this recipe a try and let the vibrant colors and flavors of this stir-fry brighten up your dinner table!
