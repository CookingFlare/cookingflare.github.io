Flavorful and Fragrant Stir Fry Recipes with Basil


When it comes to quick and delicious meals, stir-fries are an unbeatable option. They are not only versatile but also incredibly flavorful, especially when you add the aromatic touch of basil. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering stir-fry recipes that feature basil as a star ingredient. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out in the kitchen, these recipes are sure to elevate your culinary skills and impress your taste buds.

Basil Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil.
  2. Season the chicken slices with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat and add some oil.
  4. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add the chicken slices and cook until they are no longer pink.
  6. Toss in the bell pepper and snap peas, and stir-fry until they are tender-crisp.
  7. Pour in the sauce mixture and stir to coat the chicken and vegetables.
  8. Turn off the heat and stir in the fresh basil leaves.
  9. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice and garnish with additional basil leaves.

Spicy Basil Tofu Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, cut into cubes
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Cooked noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, rice vinegar, Sriracha sauce, and brown sugar.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the tofu cubes and cook until they are golden brown on all sides.
  4. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set it aside.
  5. In the same skillet, heat the remaining oil and sauté the green onions and red chili until fragrant.
  6. Add the mushrooms and broccoli, and stir-fry until they are tender.
  7. Return the tofu to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture.
  8. Toss everything together until well coated.
  9. Turn off the heat and stir in the fresh basil leaves.
  10. Serve the spicy basil tofu stir-fry over cooked noodles.

Shrimp and Basil Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the fish sauce, lime juice, and brown sugar.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  3. Add the shrimp and stir-fry until they are pink and opaque.
  4. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.
  5. In the same skillet, heat the remaining oil and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
  6. Add the bell pepper and snow peas, and stir-fry until they are crisp-tender.
  7. Return the shrimp to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture.
  8. Toss everything together until heated through.
  9. Turn off the heat and stir in the fresh basil leaves.
  10. Serve the shrimp and basil stir-fry over cooked jasmine rice.

Conclusion

Stir-fries are not only convenient but also an excellent way to incorporate fresh herbs like basil into your cooking. The fragrant and slightly peppery flavor of basil adds a delightful dimension to these quick and easy stir-fry recipes. Whether you choose chicken, tofu, or shrimp as your main protein, these dishes are guaranteed to satisfy your cravings for a flavorful and aromatic meal. So, grab your wok and get ready to create some truly sensational stir-fries with the vibrant essence of basil!
