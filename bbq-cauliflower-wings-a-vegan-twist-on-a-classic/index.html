BBQ Cauliflower Wings: A Vegan Twist on a Classic


Who says vegetarians can’t indulge in a tasty plate of wings? We’re here to introduce you to the delightful world of BBQ cauliflower wings, a plant-based spin on the classic party snack. These mouthwatering morsels are not only packed with nutrition, but they are also guaranteed to satisfy even the most stubborn meat-lovers.

The Secret to the Best BBQ Cauliflower Wings

The success of your BBQ cauliflower wings relies on two crucial factors: the right preparation of the cauliflower and an irresistible barbeque sauce.

Preparing the Cauliflower

Start by removing the outer leaves from a large head of cauliflower. Slice it into florets and rinse thoroughly. Pat them dry with a paper towel to ensure a good crust develops during the cooking process.

Preparing the BBQ Sauce

Bring a mix of smokey, spicy, and tangy flavors by combining ketchup, apple cider vinegar, deliciously smoky paprika, spicy cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and a dash of salt in a bowl. Stir well to get a perfect barbecue sauce texture.

The Step-by-Step Process to Achieve Perfection

Now that we’ve got our ingredients ready, let’s dive into the process step-by-step:

  1. Bread the Cauliflower: Place the florets into a large bowl, and pour the barbeque sauce to evenly coat them. Then, add some all-purpose flour, onion powder, and other seasonings of your choice, such as garlic powder or black pepper.

  2. Deep Fry: Heat up some vegetable oil in a deep-fryer, wok or a sturdy pot until it reaches a temperature of around 375°F. Fry the cauliflower bits for about 5-7 minutes per batch until golden brown. Remember to separate them gently so they don’t stick together.

  3. Bake ‘Em: Alternatively, you can also bake the florets at 425°F for 20 to 25 minutes until crispy and tender. Ensure to flip them halfway through for even browning.

  4. BBQ Them: Lastly, place the fried/baked cauliflower pieces into a large bowl with your favorite barbeque sauce. Toss them gently to ensure full coverage. You can even add more spices or zest up the sauce with fresh lime juice or a touch of honey for a sweet kick.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Place the coated cauliflower wings on a big platter and let your taste buds do the groovin’. Sprinkle with some green onions or sesame seeds for that added sparkle.

Making BBQ Cauliflower Wings: A Closer Look to Veganizing the Classic

The process may seem challenging, but it’s worth it. The satisfaction comes from the aromatic tanginess married with the underlying bitterness of the vegetable. It draws an ideal balance of flavors and testifies to the fact that you don’t need meat to savor succulence.

Let’s Get Cooking!

Fire up your barbeque, call your friends over, and embark on this journey of transforming a classic into something plant-based and delicious. And remember, it’s always better to make extra – you never know how many people will be asking for seconds!

Continue experimenting with seasonings, baking methods, sauces, and dips, and you might end up with your new go-to party appetizer. No one can resist a perfectly fried or baked, delectable BBQ cauli-wings!
