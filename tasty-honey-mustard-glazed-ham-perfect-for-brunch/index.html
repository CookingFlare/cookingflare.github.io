Tasty Honey Mustard Glazed Ham: Perfect for Brunch


There’s something about a beautifully glazed ham that makes any meal feel special, and when it comes to brunch, it’s the perfect centerpiece. This Tasty Honey Mustard Glazed Ham recipe is a surefire way to impress your guests and elevate your brunch spread. The combination of sweet honey and tangy mustard creates a mouthwatering glaze that caramelizes beautifully on the ham, resulting in a flavor-packed dish that’s bound to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 bone-in ham, about 8-10 pounds
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ham: Preheat your oven to 325°F. Place the ham on a cutting board and score the surface with a sharp knife in a crisscross pattern, about 1/4 inch deep. This will help the glaze penetrate the meat and add a beautiful presentation.

  2. Make the Glaze: In a small saucepan, combine the honey, Dijon mustard, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, ground cloves, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved and the glaze has slightly thickened.

  3. Glaze the Ham: Place the scored ham in a roasting pan and brush a generous amount of the honey mustard glaze all over the surface of the ham, making sure to get into the scored lines. Reserve some of the glaze for basting during the cooking process.

  4. Bake the Ham: Cover the ham loosely with foil and bake for about 15-18 minutes per pound, or until the internal temperature reaches 140°F, basting with the remaining glaze every 30 minutes. During the last 10-15 minutes of cooking, remove the foil to allow the glaze to caramelize and create a beautiful golden-brown crust.

  5. Rest and Serve: Once the ham is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 15-20 minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a moist and flavorful ham. Slice and serve the honey mustard glazed ham as the star of your brunch table.

Tips and Variations

  • Make Ahead: This ham can be prepared a day ahead and reheated before serving, making it a convenient option for entertaining.
  • Flavor Variations: Feel free to customize the glaze by adding a touch of heat with some red pepper flakes or a hint of citrus with orange zest.
  • Leftover Ideas: Use any leftover ham to make delicious sandwiches, omelets, or salads.

Whether you’re hosting a special occasion brunch or simply want to elevate your weekend meal, this Tasty Honey Mustard Glazed Ham is a versatile and impressive dish that’s sure to delight your guests. The combination of sweet and savory flavors, coupled with the stunning presentation, makes it a standout addition to any brunch menu. So, gather your loved ones, set the table, and get ready to savor every mouthwatering bite of this delectable honey mustard glazed ham.
