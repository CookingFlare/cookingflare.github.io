Hearty Low Carb Beef and Mushroom Skillet


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying low carb meal that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further than this hearty beef and mushroom skillet recipe. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is perfect for those following a low carb or ketogenic diet. Plus, it’s so delicious that even carb-lovers will be coming back for seconds!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up into small crumbles with a spoon.

  2. Once the beef is cooked, add the mushrooms, onion, and garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and the mushrooms have released their liquid.

  3. Sprinkle the dried thyme, dried rosemary, paprika, salt, and pepper over the beef and mushroom mixture. Stir well to combine and allow the spices to become fragrant.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the beef broth, tomato paste, and Worcestershire sauce. Pour the mixture into the skillet and stir to coat the beef and mushrooms.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the skillet for 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly and the flavors have melded together.

  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Add more salt and pepper to your liking.

  7. Serve the hearty low carb beef and mushroom skillet hot, garnished with fresh parsley. This dish pairs well with cauliflower rice or a side salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This hearty low carb beef and mushroom skillet is not only delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrients. The combination of ground beef and mushrooms provides a good dose of protein, while the spices add depth and flavor to the dish. Plus, it’s a one-pan meal, making cleanup a breeze.

The low carb nature of this recipe makes it a great option for those following a low carb or ketogenic diet. It’s also a fantastic way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet, as the mushrooms and onions add fiber and nutrients without adding a lot of carbs.

Not only is this dish satisfying and nutritious, but it’s also incredibly versatile. Feel free to customize it to your liking by adding additional vegetables such as bell peppers or zucchini. You can also experiment with different herbs and spices to suit your taste preferences.

So, whether you’re looking for a quick and easy weeknight dinner or a dish to impress your friends at a dinner party, this hearty low carb beef and mushroom skillet is sure to please. Give it a try and let your taste buds thank you!

Note: This recipe serves approximately 4 people, but can easily be doubled or halved to suit your needs.
