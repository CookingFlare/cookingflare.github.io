Elevated Baked Ziti: Comfort Food Refined


Are you looking for a delicious and comforting meal that will leave your taste buds singing? Look no further than this elevated baked ziti recipe. Baked ziti is a classic Italian-American dish that has been a favorite for generations, and this recipe takes it to a whole new level. With a few simple tweaks and the addition of some unexpected ingredients, you can transform this humble dish into a gourmet experience that will delight your family and friends.

Ingredients

To start your journey to elevated baked ziti, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ziti pasta
  • 1 pound of Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 cup of diced onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prep: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook the ziti according to the package instructions until al dente. While the pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the Italian sausage. Cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned. Add the diced onion and garlic, and cook until softened.

  2. Sauce Simmer: Pour in the whole peeled tomatoes and tomato sauce, then add the dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Creamy Addition: Stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese, letting the sauce simmer for another 5 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  4. Combine and Layer: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked ziti and the sauce, tossing until the pasta is well coated. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, layer half of the ziti mixture, followed by a sprinkling of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layering process, finishing with a generous layer of mozzarella on top.

  5. Bake and Garnish: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly. Let the ziti rest for a few minutes, then garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Elevate Your Experience

This elevated baked ziti is a masterpiece of flavors and textures. The addition of heavy cream brings a luxurious creaminess to the dish, while the Italian sausage infuses it with a rich, savory depth. The layers of gooey mozzarella cheese add a delightful stringiness to each bite, making it a true comfort food experience.

Pair your elevated baked ziti with a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite red wine for a complete and satisfying meal. The leftovers, if there are any, make for a fantastic next-day lunch that will have your colleagues eyeing your lunch with envy.

Conclusion

Elevated baked ziti takes a beloved classic and elevates it to a whole new level of sophistication. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to savor the comforting, refined flavors of this elevated baked ziti. Happy cooking!
