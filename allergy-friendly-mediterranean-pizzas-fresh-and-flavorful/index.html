Allergy-Friendly Mediterranean Pizzas: Fresh and Flavorful


Are you craving a taste of the Mediterranean but dealing with food allergies or sensitivities? Fear not! In this recipe, we’re going to take the classic flavors of the Mediterranean and transform them into allergy-friendly pizzas that are not only fresh and flavorful but also suitable for a range of dietary needs. Whether you’re allergic to gluten, dairy, or nuts, or simply looking for a healthier pizza alternative, these allergy-friendly Mediterranean pizzas will satisfy your cravings and delight your taste buds.

Ingredients:

Pizza Dough:

  • 2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Toppings:

  • 1/2 cup dairy-free pesto
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 cup artichoke hearts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup dairy-free mozzarella-style cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough:

    • In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
    • In a separate large mixing bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour, xanthan gum, and salt. Create a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.
    • Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes, adding more flour if the dough is too sticky. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for 1 hour in a warm place.

  2. Preheat the Oven and Prepare Toppings:

    • Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
    • While the dough is rising, prepare the toppings by slicing the cherry tomatoes, olives, artichoke hearts, and red onion. Set aside.

  3. Assemble the Pizzas:

    • Once the dough has risen, divide it into two equal portions. Roll out each portion into a 10-inch round on a piece of parchment paper.
    • Spread a layer of dairy-free pesto on each pizza base, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle dairy-free mozzarella-style cheese over the pesto, followed by the cherry tomatoes, olives, artichoke hearts, and red onion.

  4. Bake and Garnish:

    • Carefully transfer the pizzas on parchment paper onto baking sheets or pizza stones. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
    • Once out of the oven, garnish the pizzas with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil, if desired.

Enjoy the Mediterranean Flavors:

These allergy-friendly Mediterranean pizzas are not only visually appealing but also bursting with fresh and vibrant flavors. The combination of savory dairy-free pesto, tangy Kalamata olives, and juicy cherry tomatoes will transport you to the sun-soaked shores of the Mediterranean with every bite. The best part? You can customize these pizzas to suit your dietary needs, making them a versatile and crowd-pleasing option for gatherings and family dinners.

So, the next time you’re yearning for a taste of the Mediterranean, whip up these allergy-friendly pizzas and savor the delicious flavors without any worries about food allergies or dietary restrictions. Buon appetito!
