Grilled Veggie Wrap: A Burst of Summer Flavors


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy delicious, healthy meals. And what better way to celebrate the season than with a Grilled Veggie Wrap? Packed with vibrant colors, fresh flavors, and nutritious ingredients, this wrap is a true burst of summer on your plate. Whether you’re a vegetarian, a health-conscious eater, or simply looking to add more variety to your meals, this recipe is sure to please your taste buds and satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

To make the Grilled Veggie Wrap, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced lengthwise
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced lengthwise
  • 1 red bell pepper, deseeded and sliced into strips
  • 1 red onion, sliced into rings
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the zucchini, yellow squash, red bell pepper, and red onion with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Make sure the vegetables are evenly coated.
  3. Place the vegetables on the grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until they are tender and slightly charred. Remove from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  4. While the vegetables are cooling, warm the whole wheat tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side. This will make them more pliable and easier to wrap.
  5. Once the vegetables are cooled, spread 1/4 cup of hummus onto each tortilla, leaving a small border around the edges.
  6. Layer the grilled vegetables evenly on top of the hummus, followed by a handful of baby spinach leaves and a sprinkle of crumbled feta cheese.
  7. Carefully roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Cut the wrap in half diagonally for easier handling.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy the burst of summer flavors in every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • You can add additional veggies to the mix, such as eggplant or mushrooms, to further enhance the flavor and texture of the wrap.
  • For a burst of freshness, squeeze a bit of lemon juice over the grilled vegetables before assembling the wrap.
  • If you prefer a creamier texture, you can add a dollop of Greek yogurt or tzatziki sauce to the wrap.
  • This recipe is versatile and can be customized to suit your preferences. Feel free to experiment with different herbs, spices, or condiments to create your own unique twist on the Grilled Veggie Wrap.

Final Thoughts

The Grilled Veggie Wrap is a delightful combination of smoky grilled vegetables, creamy hummus, and tangy feta cheese, all wrapped up in a soft whole wheat tortilla. It’s a simple yet satisfying meal that captures the essence of summer on a plate. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply looking for a quick and healthy lunch option, this recipe is sure to impress. So fire up the grill, gather your favorite veggies, and get ready to indulge in a burst of summer flavors with this delicious Grilled Veggie Wrap.
