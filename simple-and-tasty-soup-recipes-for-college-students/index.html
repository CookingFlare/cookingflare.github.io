Simple and Tasty Soup Recipes for College Students


As a college student, finding quick and easy meal options can be a challenge. With limited time and resources, cooking can often take a backseat. However, with these simple and tasty soup recipes, you can whip up a delicious and nutritious meal in no time.

1. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and minced garlic. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  3. Using an immersion blender or regular blender, blend the soup until smooth.
  4. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream and chopped basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Heat the soup over low heat for an additional 5 minutes, or until warmed through.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh basil leaves.

2. Chicken Noodle Soup

Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 30 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of egg noodles
  • 1 carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, carrot, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the shredded chicken and chicken broth to the pot. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and add the egg noodles. Let the soup simmer for 15 minutes, or until the noodles are cooked.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy this comforting and classic soup.

3. Spicy Black Bean Soup

Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 25 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and jalapeño pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are softened.
  2. Add the black beans, vegetable broth, cumin, and chili powder to the pot. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. Using an immersion blender or regular blender, blend half of the soup until smooth. Return it to the pot and stir to combine.
  5. Add the lime juice and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Heat the soup over low heat for an additional 5 minutes, or until warmed through.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with a squeeze of lime juice and a dollop of sour cream, if desired.

Conclusion

These simple and tasty soup recipes are perfect for college students looking for quick and easy meal options. With just a few ingredients and minimal cooking time, you can enjoy a delicious and satisfying bowl of soup. Whether you’re craving a creamy tomato basil soup, a classic chicken noodle soup, or a spicy black bean soup, these recipes have got you covered. So, grab your ingredients and get cooking!
