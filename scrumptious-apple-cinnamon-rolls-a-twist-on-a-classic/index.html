Scrumptious Apple Cinnamon Rolls: A Twist on a Classic


There’s something undeniably comforting about the aroma of warm cinnamon and fresh apples wafting through the air. It’s a scent that instantly transports us to cozy kitchens and happy memories. Today, I’m going to share with you a delightful twist on a classic recipe – Scrumptious Apple Cinnamon Rolls. These gooey, sweet treats are sure to become a favorite in your household, perfect for lazy weekend mornings or special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup milk, warmed
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, granulated sugar, salt, and instant yeast. Mix well.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warmed milk, melted butter, and eggs until well combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms. If the dough is too sticky, gradually add a little more flour until it becomes manageable.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.

  6. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling. In a small bowl, combine the chopped apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Mix well.

  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air. Roll it out into a large rectangle, approximately 1/4 inch thick.

  8. Spread the softened butter evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  9. Sprinkle the apple filling evenly over the buttered dough, pressing it down gently.

  10. Starting from one of the long sides, tightly roll the dough into a log. Pinch the edges to seal.

  11. Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 12 equal-sized rolls.

  12. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, leaving a small gap between each roll. Cover and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.

  13. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  14. Bake the rolls for 20-25 minutes or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  15. While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  16. Once the rolls are out of the oven, let them cool for a few minutes before drizzling the glaze over the top.

  17. Serve warm and enjoy!

These Scrumptious Apple Cinnamon Rolls are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven when they are still warm and gooey. The combination of tender dough, sweet apple filling, and fragrant cinnamon is simply irresistible. Whether you’re serving them for breakfast, brunch, or dessert, these rolls are guaranteed to bring smiles to everyone’s faces.

So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to indulge in a delightful twist on a classic recipe. Your taste buds will thank you!
