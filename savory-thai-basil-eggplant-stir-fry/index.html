Savory Thai Basil Eggplant Stir Fry


Are you ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will tantalize your taste buds with vibrant flavors and aromatic spices? Look no further than this delectable Savory Thai Basil Eggplant Stir Fry recipe. Bursting with the goodness of fresh vegetables, fragrant Thai basil, and a perfect balance of sweet and savory sauces, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Let’s dive right into the recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized eggplants, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Handful of fresh Thai basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Eggplant: Start by cutting the eggplants into bite-sized pieces. Place them in a bowl of salted water for about 10-15 minutes to remove any bitterness. Rinse and pat dry with a paper towel.

  2. Stir Fry: Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  3. Add Vegetables: Add the sliced onion, red bell pepper, and yellow bell pepper to the pan. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened but still crisp.

  4. Cook Eggplant: Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and add a little more oil if needed. Add the eggplant pieces and cook until they are tender and slightly golden brown.

  5. Sauce Preparation: In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sugar, and chili flakes. Pour this sauce over the cooked vegetables and eggplant.

  6. Combine and Simmer: Toss everything together to ensure the sauce coats all the vegetables evenly. Let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Add Thai Basil: Once the vegetables are cooked to your liking, add a generous handful of fresh Thai basil leaves. Stir well to incorporate the basil into the dish.

  8. Final Touch: Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, or more chili flakes if desired.

  9. Serve: Remove from heat and serve the savory Thai basil eggplant stir fry hot over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with extra Thai basil leaves for a burst of freshness.

Enjoy the Flavorful Delight!

This Savory Thai Basil Eggplant Stir Fry is a delightful combination of textures and flavors that will surely impress your family and friends. The tender eggplant, crunchy bell peppers, and aromatic Thai basil come together beautifully in a savory sauce that is simply irresistible. Whether you are a vegetarian looking for a satisfying meal or a food enthusiast eager to explore new culinary horizons, this recipe is a must-try. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your wok, and get ready to savor a taste of Thailand right in your own kitchen!
