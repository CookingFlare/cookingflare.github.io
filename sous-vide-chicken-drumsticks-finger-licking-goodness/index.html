Sous Vide Chicken Drumsticks: Finger-Licking Goodness


If you’re a fan of chicken drumsticks, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to dive into the world of sous vide cooking and explore the finger-licking goodness that comes with it. Sous vide is a cooking technique that involves sealing food in a plastic bag and cooking it in a water bath at a precise temperature for an extended period. This method ensures that the chicken drumsticks are cooked evenly and retain their natural flavors and juices.

Ingredients

To get started, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 165°F (74°C).

  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. This will create a flavorful marinade for the chicken drumsticks.

  3. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large resealable bag and add the marinade. Make sure the drumsticks are well-coated with the marinade. Seal the bag, removing as much air as possible.

  4. Carefully lower the bag into the preheated water bath, ensuring that the drumsticks are fully submerged. Cook the chicken drumsticks for 2 hours.

  5. After the cooking time is up, remove the bag from the water bath and carefully take out the drumsticks. They might be quite tender, so handle them gently.

  6. Preheat a grill or a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat. Brush the drumsticks with a little olive oil and cook them for about 2 minutes on each side, or until they develop a nice char and crispy skin.

  7. Once cooked, remove the drumsticks from the grill or skillet and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These sous vide chicken drumsticks are incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve the drumsticks alongside a fresh green salad for a light and refreshing meal.

  • Pair them with your favorite dipping sauce, such as barbecue, honey mustard, or buffalo sauce, for an extra burst of flavor.

  • Turn them into a hearty chicken drumstick sandwich by placing the cooked drumsticks on a toasted bun with some lettuce, tomato, and your favorite condiments.

  • Add them to a platter of finger foods for your next gathering or party. They’re sure to be a crowd-pleaser!

Final Thoughts

Sous vide chicken drumsticks are a game-changer when it comes to cooking chicken. The precise temperature control ensures that the meat is tender, juicy, and bursting with flavor. Plus, the finishing touch of grilling or searing adds a delightful crispy texture that will have you coming back for seconds.

So why not give sous vide chicken drumsticks a try? Whether you’re cooking for yourself or hosting a gathering, this recipe is sure to impress. Get ready to indulge in finger-licking goodness like never before!
