Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Salmon: A Quick and Easy Seafood Recipe


Salmon is a versatile and delicious fish that can be prepared in numerous ways. If you’re looking for a quick and easy seafood recipe that will impress your dinner guests, look no further than Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Salmon. This mouthwatering dish combines the bright flavors of lemon and garlic with tender, flaky salmon to create a meal that is both satisfying and refreshing.

Ingredients

To make Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Salmon, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh salmon fillets
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.

  3. Place the salmon fillets in a baking dish, skin side down. Pour the marinade over the salmon, ensuring that it is evenly coated. Let the salmon marinate for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Once the salmon has marinated, transfer the baking dish to the preheated oven. Roast the salmon for approximately 12-15 minutes, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. The salmon should be opaque and flake easily with a fork.

  5. Remove the salmon from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more flavorful and tender fish.

  6. Serve the Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Salmon with your favorite side dishes. It pairs well with roasted vegetables, a fresh salad, or a side of quinoa.

Tips and Variations

  • For added freshness, garnish the roasted salmon with a sprinkle of freshly chopped parsley or dill before serving.

  • If you prefer a stronger garlic flavor, increase the amount of minced garlic in the marinade. You can also add a pinch of crushed red pepper flakes for a subtle kick of heat.

  • Experiment with different citrus fruits to create unique flavor profiles. Try using lime or orange instead of lemon for a twist on this classic recipe.

  • For a complete Greek-inspired meal, serve the salmon with a side of tzatziki sauce, pita bread, and a Greek salad.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Enjoy the chilled salmon over a bed of mixed greens for a refreshing lunch option.

Final Thoughts

Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Salmon is a quick and easy seafood recipe that is perfect for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. The combination of tangy lemon, aromatic garlic, and tender salmon creates a dish that is bursting with flavor. Whether you’re a seafood lover or just looking to incorporate more healthy proteins into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Give it a try and enjoy a taste of the Mediterranean!
