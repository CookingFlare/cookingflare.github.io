Quick and Easy Breakfast Burrito Recipes for Novice Cooks


Are you a novice cook looking for a delicious and satisfying breakfast option? Look no further than breakfast burritos! These versatile and easy-to-make meals are not only tasty but also a great way to start your day on the right foot. In this blog post, we will share three quick and easy breakfast burrito recipes that are perfect for novice cooks. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Classic Bacon and Egg Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 4 strips of bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • Optional toppings: salsa, sour cream, avocado

Instructions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the same skillet used for bacon and cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally until the eggs are scrambled and cooked through.
  4. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave for a few seconds.
  5. Place a quarter of the scrambled eggs, bacon, and shredded cheese on each tortilla.
  6. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly to form a burrito.
  7. Optional: Serve with salsa, sour cream, or avocado on top.

2. Veggie and Cheese Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1/2 cup of diced onions
  • 1/2 cup of diced mushrooms
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Optional toppings: salsa, sour cream, guacamole

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms to the skillet and sauté until they are tender.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Push the veggies to one side of the skillet and pour the egg mixture into the other side. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are scrambled and cooked through.
  5. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave for a few seconds.
  6. Place a quarter of the scrambled eggs, sautéed veggies, and shredded cheese on each tortilla.
  7. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly to form a burrito.
  8. Optional: Serve with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole on top.

3. Ham and Cheese Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of ham
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Optional toppings: ketchup, mustard, pickles, lettuce

Instructions:

  1. Place a slice of ham on each flour tortilla.
  2. Sprinkle a quarter of the shredded cheese on top of each slice of ham.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave for a few seconds.
  4. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly to form a burrito.
  5. Optional: Serve with ketchup, mustard, pickles, or lettuce on top.

These three quick and easy breakfast burrito recipes are perfect for novice cooks who are looking for a delicious and satisfying morning meal. Whether you prefer a classic bacon and egg burrito, a veggie and cheese burrito, or a ham and cheese burrito, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, get into the kitchen and start cooking up a breakfast burrito that will kickstart your day with a burst of flavor!
