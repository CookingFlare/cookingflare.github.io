Delicious and Easy Instant Pot Cheesecake Recipes


Who doesn’t love a rich and creamy cheesecake? The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, and now it’s time to put it to work creating delicious and easy cheesecakes. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering Instant Pot cheesecake recipes that are sure to impress your family and friends. So, grab your Instant Pot and let’s get started!

Classic New York Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until well combined.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in sour cream until just combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared crust. Cover the pan with aluminum foil.
  5. Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot. Place a trivet in the pot and carefully lower the springform pan onto the trivet.
  6. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to Manual mode for 30 minutes on high pressure.
  7. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then release any remaining pressure manually.
  8. Carefully remove the cheesecake from the Instant Pot and let it cool completely before refrigerating for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Chocolate Swirl Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips, melted

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine chocolate cookie crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until well combined.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Pour half of the batter into the prepared crust. Drizzle melted chocolate over the batter. Pour the remaining batter on top.
  5. Use a knife or toothpick to create a swirl pattern in the batter.
  6. Follow the same cooking instructions as the Classic New York Cheesecake recipe.
  7. Once the cheesecake is cooled and refrigerated, drizzle melted chocolate over the top for an extra touch of decadence.

Lemon Blueberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until well combined.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in lemon zest.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared crust.
  6. Follow the same cooking instructions as the Classic New York Cheesecake recipe.
  7. Once the cheesecake is cooled and refrigerated, garnish with additional fresh blueberries and a sprinkle of lemon zest for a burst of color and flavor.

Conclusion

These Instant Pot cheesecake recipes are just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to the possibilities with this versatile kitchen appliance. With a little creativity and some quality ingredients, you can create decadent and irresistible cheesecakes in no time. So, grab your Instant Pot and start experimenting with flavors and textures. Your taste buds will thank you!
