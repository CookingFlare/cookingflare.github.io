Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Dairy-Free Snickerdoodle Cookies


If you’re on the lookout for a scrumptious treat that’s both dairy-free and irresistibly delicious, look no further than these delightful Dairy-Free Snickerdoodle Cookies. With their soft, chewy texture and delightful cinnamon-sugar coating, these cookies are sure to become a staple in your baking repertoire. Whether you’re lactose intolerant, following a dairy-free diet, or simply looking to switch things up in the kitchen, these cookies are the perfect solution to satisfy your sweet cravings.

Ingredients

For the Cookie Dough:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup dairy-free butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 flax eggs (2 tablespoons ground flaxseed mixed with 5 tablespoons water)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Cinnamon-Sugar Coating:

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Cookie Dough: In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, cream of tartar, and salt. In a separate large bowl, cream together the dairy-free butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the flax eggs and vanilla extract, mixing until well combined. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a smooth dough forms.

  3. Form the Cookies: In a small bowl, combine the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon for the coating. Using your hands, roll the cookie dough into 1-inch balls, then roll each ball in the cinnamon-sugar mixture until evenly coated. Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.

  4. Bake the Cookies: Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake the cookies for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are set but the centers are still soft. Remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  5. Enjoy: Once the cookies have cooled, indulge in the irresistible aroma and taste of these dairy-free snickerdoodle cookies. They’re perfect for sharing with friends and family, or for enjoying as a special treat all to yourself.

Why Dairy-Free Snickerdoodle Cookies?

These dairy-free snickerdoodle cookies are a wonderful alternative to traditional snickerdoodles, offering a delectable flavor and texture without the use of dairy products. By using simple and readily available dairy-free ingredients, you can enjoy the classic taste of snickerdoodles while accommodating dietary restrictions or preferences.

Conclusion

In conclusion, these Dairy-Free Snickerdoodle Cookies are a fantastic addition to any baker’s collection of recipes. Not only are they dairy-free, but they are also incredibly delicious and easy to make. Whether you have a dairy allergy, intolerance, or simply want to explore dairy-free baking, these cookies are a must-try. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to delight your taste buds with these delightful dairy-free snickerdoodle cookies. Happy baking!
