Vegan Jackfruit Tacos: Flavorful and Meaty Plant-Based Alternative


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying plant-based meal that will have even the most devoted carnivores asking for seconds? Look no further than these Vegan Jackfruit Tacos! Packed with flavor and texture, these tacos are the perfect alternative to traditional meat-based tacos. And the best part? They’re incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of young jackfruit in brine or water, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 8-10 small corn tortillas
  • Your favorite taco toppings (e.g., avocado, salsa, cilantro, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the jackfruit. Using your hands or a fork, shred the jackfruit into smaller pieces, resembling pulled pork or shredded chicken.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  3. Add the shredded jackfruit to the skillet and cook for a couple of minutes to allow the flavors to combine.

  4. In a small bowl, mix together the chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Sprinkle the spice mixture over the jackfruit, ensuring that it coats all the pieces evenly.

  5. Pour in the vegetable broth, tomato paste, soy sauce, maple syrup, and lime juice. Stir well to combine, making sure the jackfruit is coated in the sauce.

  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let the jackfruit simmer for about 15-20 minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the jackfruit to soften.

  7. While the jackfruit is simmering, warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat. Cook each tortilla for about 30 seconds on each side until warm and pliable.

  8. Once the jackfruit has finished simmering, remove the skillet from the heat and use a fork to further shred the jackfruit, if desired. This will give it an even more meat-like texture.

  9. Assemble your tacos by placing a spoonful of the jackfruit mixture onto each warm tortilla. Top with your favorite taco toppings, such as sliced avocado, salsa, or fresh cilantro.

  10. Serve the Vegan Jackfruit Tacos immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors and textures in every bite!

Conclusion

These Vegan Jackfruit Tacos are a game-changer for anyone looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet. With their meaty texture and bold flavors, they’re sure to satisfy even the most discerning taste buds. Plus, they’re incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize them with your favorite toppings and seasonings.

So, why not give these Vegan Jackfruit Tacos a try? They’re a flavorful and meaty plant-based alternative that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a vegan, vegetarian, or just looking to switch up your taco game, these tacos are sure to become a new favorite in your household. Enjoy!
