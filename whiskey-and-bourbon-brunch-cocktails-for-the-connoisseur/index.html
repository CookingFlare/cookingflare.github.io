Whiskey and Bourbon Brunch Cocktails for the Connoisseur


Are you ready to elevate your brunch experience to a whole new level? Look no further than these exquisite whiskey and bourbon cocktails that are sure to impress even the most discerning connoisseur. Whether you’re hosting a brunch party or simply want to treat yourself to a luxurious morning libation, these cocktails will add a touch of sophistication and indulgence to your weekend routine. So, grab your favorite bottle of whiskey or bourbon and let’s get mixing!

1. Classic Old Fashioned Mimosa

Preparation time: 5 minutes | Serves: 1

If you’re a fan of both mimosas and old fashioneds, why not combine the two for the ultimate brunch cocktail? This Classic Old Fashioned Mimosa is a delightful blend of whiskey, orange juice, and a hint of bitters that will awaken your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 shot of whiskey
  • 1/2 cup of chilled orange juice
  • 2 dashes of Angostura bitters
  • Orange slice, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a champagne flute halfway with chilled orange juice.
  2. Add the whiskey and bitters to the flute.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with an orange slice.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

2. Bourbon Maple Bacon Bloody Mary

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Serves: 1

For those who prefer a savory twist to their brunch cocktails, this Bourbon Maple Bacon Bloody Mary is the perfect choice. The smoky flavor of the bourbon pairs beautifully with the tangy tomato juice, while the maple syrup adds a touch of sweetness. And let’s not forget about the crispy bacon garnish that takes this cocktail to a whole new level of deliciousness.

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots of bourbon
  • 1 cup of tomato juice
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup
  • 1/2 tablespoon of hot sauce
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Crispy bacon, for garnish
  • Celery stalk, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine the bourbon, tomato juice, Worcestershire sauce, maple syrup, hot sauce, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shake well to combine.
  3. Fill a glass with ice and strain the cocktail into the glass.
  4. Garnish with a crispy bacon strip and a celery stalk.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

3. Whiskey Peach Bellini

Preparation time: 5 minutes | Serves: 1

For a refreshing and fruity brunch cocktail, look no further than this Whiskey Peach Bellini. The combination of whiskey, peach puree, and sparkling wine creates a delightful and bubbly concoction that will transport you to a sunny patio overlooking a peach orchard.

Ingredients:

  • 1 shot of whiskey
  • 1/2 cup of peach puree
  • Sparkling wine, to top
  • Peach slice, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a champagne flute, add the whiskey and peach puree.
  2. Stir gently to combine.
  3. Top with sparkling wine.
  4. Garnish with a peach slice.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

4. Smoked Maple Old Fashioned

Preparation time: 5 minutes | Serves: 1

If you’re a fan of smoky flavors, this Smoked Maple Old Fashioned will be your new go-to brunch cocktail. The combination of bourbon, maple syrup, and a touch of smoked salt creates a rich and complex drink that will leave you craving another sip.

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots of bourbon
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup
  • 2 dashes of Angostura bitters
  • Pinch of smoked salt
  • Orange peel, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing glass, combine the bourbon, maple syrup, bitters, and smoked salt.
  2. Stir well to dissolve the maple syrup and salt.
  3. Fill a rocks glass with ice and strain the cocktail into the glass.
  4. Garnish with an orange peel.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

5. Bourbon Coffee Flip

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Serves: 1

For those who can’t start their day without a cup of coffee, this Bourbon Coffee Flip is the perfect brunch cocktail. The combination of bourbon, coffee liqueur, and a creamy egg yolk creates a velvety smooth drink that will give you the energy boost you need to conquer the day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots of bourbon
  • 1 shot of coffee liqueur
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 tablespoon of maple syrup
  • Freshly grated nutmeg, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine the bourbon, coffee liqueur, egg yolk, and maple syrup.
  2. Shake vigorously to combine and froth the egg yolk.
  3. Fill a glass with ice and strain the cocktail into the glass.
  4. Garnish with freshly grated nutmeg.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

Elevate Your Brunch Experience

With these whiskey and bourbon brunch cocktails, you can take your weekend brunch to new heights of indulgence and sophistication. From the classic Old Fashioned Mimosa to the smoky and savory Bourbon Maple Bacon Bloody Mary, each cocktail offers a unique flavor profile that will impress even the most discerning connoisseur. So, gather your ingredients, shake or stir to perfection, and savor every sip of these delightful libations. Cheers to a brunch experience like no other!
