How to Make the Perfect Chicken Tikka Masala: A Step-by-Step Guide


Chicken Tikka Masala is a classic Indian dish that has gained popularity all over the world. It’s a rich and flavorful curry made with succulent chicken pieces marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture and cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce. If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine or looking to explore new flavors, this recipe is a must-try. In this step-by-step guide, I’ll walk you through the process of making the perfect Chicken Tikka Masala right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 medium-sized onions, finely chopped
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 can (400 grams) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Mix well to form a smooth marinade.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and coat them well. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight in the refrigerator. This step ensures that the chicken absorbs all the flavors of the marinade.
  3. After marinating, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the marinated chicken pieces on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Set aside.
  4. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and cook until they turn golden brown. This may take around 8-10 minutes.
  5. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies to the pan. Sauté for another minute until the raw smell disappears.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and add the garam masala and paprika to the pan. Stir well to combine the spices with the onion mixture.
  7. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. This helps to cook the tomatoes and blend the flavors together.
  8. Add the baked chicken pieces to the pan and mix well with the tomato sauce. Allow the chicken to simmer in the sauce for 10 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  9. Finally, pour in the heavy cream and season with salt to taste. Stir well and let the Chicken Tikka Masala simmer for another 5 minutes.
  10. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.

Serving Suggestions

Chicken Tikka Masala pairs well with steamed basmati rice or naan bread. You can also serve it with a side of cucumber raita or a simple green salad for a refreshing contrast. Don’t forget to squeeze some lemon juice over the curry before serving to enhance the flavors.

Tips and Variations

  • For a healthier version, you can use Greek yogurt instead of regular yogurt and substitute heavy cream with coconut milk.
  • If you prefer a spicier curry, you can increase the amount of chili powder or add a pinch of cayenne pepper.
  • Feel free to adjust the consistency of the sauce by adding more or less heavy cream or crushed tomatoes.
  • To make it a complete meal, you can add vegetables like bell peppers, peas, or potatoes to the curry. Simply sauté them before adding the chicken.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop or in the microwave before serving.

Now that you have the recipe for the perfect Chicken Tikka Masala, it’s time to roll up your sleeves and get cooking. Don’t be intimidated by the long list of ingredients or the multiple steps involved. The effort will be well worth it once you take a bite of the deliciously aromatic and creamy curry. So gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and enjoy the flavors of India right in your own kitchen. Happy cooking!
