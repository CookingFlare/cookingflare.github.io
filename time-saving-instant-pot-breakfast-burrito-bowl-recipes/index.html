Time-Saving Instant Pot Breakfast Burrito Bowl Recipes


Breakfast is often considered the most important meal of the day, but with our busy lives, it can sometimes feel like a challenge to prepare a nutritious and satisfying meal in the morning. That’s where the Instant Pot comes to the rescue! With its ability to cook meals quickly and efficiently, you can whip up delicious breakfast burrito bowls in no time. This blog post will guide you through some easy and time-saving Instant Pot breakfast burrito bowl recipes that are perfect for busy mornings.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Breakfast?

The Instant Pot is a versatile kitchen appliance that combines multiple cooking functions into one device. It can pressure cook, slow cook, sauté, steam, and even make yogurt. This multifunctionality allows you to prepare a variety of dishes quickly, making it a perfect choice for breakfast. Here are some reasons why you should consider using an Instant Pot for your breakfast burrito bowls:

  1. Speed: The pressure cooking feature significantly reduces cooking time compared to traditional stovetop methods.
  2. Convenience: You can set it and forget it, allowing you to multitask while your breakfast cooks.
  3. Flavor: The sealed environment of the Instant Pot helps to lock in flavors and moisture, resulting in a tastier dish.
  4. Versatility: You can customize your burrito bowls with different ingredients to suit your taste preferences or dietary needs.

Essential Ingredients for Breakfast Burrito Bowls

Before diving into the recipes, let’s take a look at some essential ingredients you can include in your breakfast burrito bowls. Feel free to mix and match based on your preferences:

  • Base: Cooked rice, quinoa, or cauliflower rice
  • Proteins: Eggs, cooked sausage, beans, or cheese
  • Vegetables: Bell peppers, onions, spinach, tomatoes, or avocado
  • Spices: Cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, or salt and pepper
  • Toppings: Salsa, guacamole, sour cream, or fresh cilantro

Classic Breakfast Burrito Bowl Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice (or quinoa)
  • 1 cup vegetable broth or water
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup cooked sausage (chopped)
  • 1 bell pepper (diced)
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: salsa, avocado, cheese, and cilantro

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Base: In the Instant Pot, combine the brown rice and vegetable broth. Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 22 minutes. Once done, allow for a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.

  2. Sauté the Vegetables: Open the lid and set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add a splash of oil, then sauté the diced onion and bell pepper until they are soft, about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Add the Sausage and Spices: Stir in the cooked sausage, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2 minutes until heated through.

  4. Cook the Eggs: In a bowl, whisk the eggs. Push the sausage and vegetable mixture to one side of the pot, pour the eggs in, and scramble them until fully cooked.

  5. Assemble the Bowl: Fluff the rice and divide it among bowls. Top with the sausage and egg mixture, and add your favorite toppings like salsa, avocado, cheese, and cilantro.

Southwest Veggie Breakfast Burrito Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cup vegetable broth or water
  • 1 can black beans (rinsed and drained)
  • 1 cup corn (frozen or canned)
  • 1 red bell pepper (diced)
  • 1 zucchini (diced)
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: lime wedges, avocado, and fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Cook the Quinoa: Place quinoa and vegetable broth in the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 1 minute, then allow a natural release for 10 minutes.

  2. Add the Veggies: After the quinoa has finished cooking, add the black beans, corn, bell pepper, and zucchini to the pot. Sprinkle in the smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir gently to combine.

  3. Heat Through: Close the lid and set to sauté mode for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are heated through.

  4. Serve: Fluff the quinoa and divide it into bowls. Top with lime wedges, avocado, and fresh cilantro for a refreshing finish.

Spicy Chorizo Breakfast Burrito Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 pound chorizo sausage (removed from casing)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, green onions, and sour cream

Instructions

  1. Cook the Rice: Combine white rice and chicken broth in the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 4 minutes, then allow a natural release for 10 minutes.

  2. Cook the Chorizo: Open the lid and switch to sauté mode. Add the chorizo and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Drain some grease if necessary.

  3. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Stir in the diced tomatoes, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and salt. Cook for an additional 3 minutes until heated through.

  4. Scramble the Eggs: In a small bowl, beat the eggs. Push the chorizo mixture to one side, add the beaten eggs to the other side, and scramble until cooked through.

  5. Assemble Your Bowl: Fluff the rice and divide it among serving bowls. Top with the chorizo and egg mixture, and add your favorite toppings like cheese, green onions, and sour cream.

Make-Ahead Breakfast Burrito Bowls

One of the best features of these breakfast burrito bowls is their adaptability. You can prepare a large batch ahead of time and store them in the refrigerator for easy breakfasts throughout the week. Simply follow these steps:

  1. Cook in Bulk: Use any of the above recipes to create a larger batch. Double or triple the ingredients as needed.

  2. Storage: Allow the bowls to cool completely before storing them in airtight containers. They can typically last up to 4-5 days in the refrigerator.

  3. Reheating: When ready to eat, simply microwave for 1-2 minutes or until heated through. Add fresh toppings like avocado or salsa right before serving for an extra touch.

Tips for Perfect Breakfast Burrito Bowls

  • Choose Quality Ingredients: Fresh, organic, and locally sourced ingredients will enhance the flavor and nutritional value of your bowls.
  • Don’t Skimp on Spices: A well-seasoned dish elevates the entire meal. Don’t be afraid to experiment with various spices to find your perfect flavor profile.
  • Mix and Match: Feel free to customize your burrito bowls with whatever ingredients you have on hand. This is a great way to use up leftovers or avoid food waste.
  • Prep Ahead: Spend a little time on the weekend prepping ingredients, like chopping vegetables or cooking grains, to save time during busy mornings.

Conclusion

Time-saving Instant Pot breakfast burrito bowls are a fantastic way to enjoy a hearty breakfast without spending too much time in the kitchen. With a variety of ingredients and flavors to choose from, you can create a nutritious meal that suits your taste. Whether you prefer classic ingredients or want to mix it up with veggies and spices, these recipes are sure to become a staple in your morning routine.

Start your day off right with these delicious and easy-to-make breakfast burrito bowls, and embrace the convenience and versatility of the Instant Pot. Happy cooking!
