Wholesome and Filling Rice and Lentil Soup


There’s nothing quite as comforting as a hot bowl of soup on a chilly day. And when it comes to wholesome and filling soups, it’s hard to beat the classic combination of rice and lentils. This hearty soup is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients that will keep you satisfied for hours. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make! So let’s dive right into the recipe:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 cup green or brown lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice and lentils under cold water until the water runs clear. Set aside.

  2. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 5 minutes, until the vegetables are softened.

  3. Add the rinsed rice and lentils to the pot, along with the cumin, turmeric, and paprika. Stir well to coat the rice and lentils with the spices.

  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the rice and lentils are tender.

  5. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. You can adjust the seasoning based on your preferences.

  6. Serve the rice and lentil soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley. You can also add a squeeze of lemon juice for an extra burst of flavor.

Why You’ll Love This Soup

This rice and lentil soup is not only incredibly delicious but also packed with nutrition. Let’s take a closer look at some of the benefits you’ll enjoy with each spoonful:

1. High in Fiber

Both lentils and brown rice are excellent sources of dietary fiber. Fiber is essential for maintaining a healthy digestive system and can help regulate blood sugar levels. It also keeps you feeling fuller for longer, making this soup a great option for those looking to manage their weight.

2. Plant-Based Protein

Lentils are a fantastic plant-based source of protein. They contain all the essential amino acids your body needs, making them a complete protein source. Combined with the protein found in brown rice, this soup provides a satisfying and nutritious meal.

3. Rich in Vitamins and Minerals

This soup is also packed with essential vitamins and minerals. Carrots and celery add vitamins A and K, while lentils provide iron and folate. The combination of these ingredients ensures you’re getting a wide range of nutrients in every bowl.

4. Easy and Affordable

Not only is this soup delicious and nutritious, but it’s also incredibly easy to make. With simple ingredients that are readily available, you can whip up a pot of this wholesome soup in no time. Plus, it’s a budget-friendly option that won’t break the bank.

So the next time you’re in need of a comforting and nourishing meal, give this rice and lentil soup a try. It’s a hearty and filling option that will warm you up from the inside out. Enjoy!
