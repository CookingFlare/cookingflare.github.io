Breakfast Burrito Casserole: A Crowd-Pleasing Brunch Idea


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy brunch idea that will please a crowd, look no further than this Breakfast Burrito Casserole. Packed with all your favorite breakfast ingredients, this casserole is sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re hosting a brunch party or simply want to enjoy a lazy Sunday morning with your loved ones, this recipe is the perfect choice.

Ingredients

  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 8 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup diced onions
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Salsa, sour cream, and avocado for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray or butter.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cumin until well combined.

  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of oil. Sauté the diced bell peppers and onions until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for an additional 2 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

  4. Place 4 tortillas in the bottom of the prepared baking dish, overlapping them as needed to cover the entire surface. Tear the remaining 4 tortillas into small pieces and scatter them over the bottom layer.

  5. Spread the cooked vegetable mixture evenly over the tortillas. Sprinkle half of the crumbled bacon and half of the shredded cheese on top.

  6. Pour the egg mixture over the layers in the baking dish, making sure to cover everything evenly. Use a spoon to gently press down on the mixture to ensure the tortillas soak up the egg.

  7. Sprinkle the remaining bacon and cheese on top of the casserole. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.

  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the casserole is set and golden brown on top.

  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle with chopped fresh cilantro, if desired.

  10. Serve the Breakfast Burrito Casserole warm with salsa, sour cream, and avocado on the side. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite breakfast ingredients such as cooked sausage, ham, or even vegetarian options like sautéed mushrooms or spinach.
  • Make it ahead: You can prepare this casserole the night before and refrigerate it overnight. Just cover it tightly with plastic wrap and bake it in the morning as directed.
  • Leftovers: This casserole can be refrigerated for up to 3 days. Simply reheat it in the oven or microwave for a quick and easy breakfast.

With its flavorful combination of eggs, cheese, bacon, and veggies, this Breakfast Burrito Casserole is a winning brunch idea that will leave your guests asking for seconds. The best part is that it’s simple to make and can be easily customized to suit your taste. So go ahead, gather your loved ones, and enjoy a crowd-pleasing brunch that will make your weekend mornings extra special.
