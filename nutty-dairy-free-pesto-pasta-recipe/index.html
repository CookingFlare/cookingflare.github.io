Nutty Dairy-Free Pesto Pasta Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy dairy-free pasta dish, this Nutty Dairy-Free Pesto Pasta recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. Packed with flavor and simple to make, this recipe is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a leisurely weekend meal. The combination of fresh ingredients and flavorful seasonings creates a delightful pesto sauce that pairs perfectly with your favorite pasta. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup raw cashews
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces gluten-free pasta of your choice
  • Optional: cherry tomatoes, pine nuts, or vegan parmesan for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pesto Sauce: In a food processor, combine the fresh basil leaves, raw cashews, garlic, and nutritional yeast. Pulse the ingredients until they are finely chopped and well combined. While the food processor is running, slowly pour in the extra virgin olive oil and lemon juice. Continue processing until the mixture reaches a smooth and creamy consistency. Add salt and pepper to taste, and set the pesto sauce aside.

  2. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the gluten-free pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and return it to the pot.

  3. Combine the Pasta and Pesto: Pour the prepared pesto sauce over the cooked pasta in the pot. Gently toss the pasta until it is evenly coated with the pesto sauce.

  4. Serve and Garnish: Divide the nutty dairy-free pesto pasta among serving plates. If desired, garnish with halved cherry tomatoes, pine nuts, or a sprinkle of vegan parmesan for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For a nut-free version, you can substitute the raw cashews with toasted sunflower seeds or pumpkin seeds to achieve a similar creamy texture and nutty flavor.
  • If you prefer a thinner pesto sauce, you can adjust the consistency by adding a bit more olive oil or a splash of water while blending the ingredients.
  • To add a protein boost to the dish, consider incorporating grilled tofu, roasted chickpeas, or sautéed mushrooms into the pasta.

Final Thoughts

This Nutty Dairy-Free Pesto Pasta recipe offers a delightful twist on the classic pesto pasta, without the need for dairy products. Whether you’re following a dairy-free lifestyle or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is a fantastic addition to your culinary repertoire. The creamy texture of the pesto, combined with the nutty undertones, creates a satisfying and flavorful dish that will leave you wanting more. Enjoy this pasta dish as a standalone meal or pair it with a fresh green salad for a well-rounded dining experience.

Give this recipe a try and savor the delightful blend of flavors in every bite! Happy cooking!
