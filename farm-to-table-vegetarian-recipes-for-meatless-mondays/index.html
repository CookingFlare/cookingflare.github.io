Farm to Table Vegetarian Recipes for Meatless Mondays


Welcome to our farm to table vegetarian recipes for Meatless Mondays! This weekly tradition has gained popularity as people become more conscious of their health and the environment. Whether you’re a committed vegetarian or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, Meatless Mondays are a great way to start the week off on a healthy note. We’ve put together a collection of delicious and nutritious vegetarian recipes that celebrate the fresh flavors of farm-fresh produce. Let’s dive in!

Hearty Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onion, carrots, and celery until they begin to soften.
  2. Add the garlic, cumin, and smoked paprika, and cook for another minute.
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth, lentils, and diced tomatoes. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Roasted Vegetable Buddha Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tahini dressing for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss the sweet potato, zucchini, and red bell pepper with olive oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the vegetables on a baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and slightly caramelized.
  4. Assemble the bowls with cooked quinoa, roasted vegetables, and chickpeas. Drizzle with tahini dressing before serving.

Fresh Tomato and Basil Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta of your choice
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until they begin to break down.
  4. Toss in the cooked pasta and fresh basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot, with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

These farm to table vegetarian recipes are perfect for Meatless Mondays, or any day of the week when you’re craving a wholesome, plant-based meal. By using fresh, seasonal produce, you’re not only nourishing your body but also supporting local farmers and reducing your carbon footprint. We hope you enjoy these recipes and feel inspired to explore the delicious world of vegetarian cooking!
