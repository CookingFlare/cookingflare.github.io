Instant Pot Vegetable Curry Recipe: A Flavorful and Healthy Option


Looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s quick and easy to prepare? Look no further than this Instant Pot Vegetable Curry recipe! Packed with vibrant flavors and nutritious vegetables, this curry is a wonderful option for vegetarians and vegans alike. Plus, the convenience of using an Instant Pot makes the cooking process a breeze. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful dish in no time!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups of mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, carrots, peas, and cauliflower)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan bread for serving

Instructions

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add the coconut oil. Once the oil is hot, add the chopped onion and sauté for a few minutes until it becomes translucent.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot, stirring for about a minute until fragrant.

  3. Sprinkle the curry powder, ground cumin, and ground turmeric over the onion mixture. Stir well to coat the onions and spices evenly.

  4. Pour in the coconut milk and diced tomatoes, stirring to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Add the mixed vegetables to the pot, ensuring they are well coated with the flavorful sauce.

  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick pressure release and carefully open the lid.

  8. Season the curry with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.

  9. Serve the vegetable curry over cooked rice or accompanied by warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness.

Why Instant Pot Vegetable Curry?

This Instant Pot Vegetable Curry recipe is a fantastic choice for several reasons. Firstly, it’s incredibly convenient to prepare, thanks to the efficiency of the Instant Pot. The pressure cooking method allows the flavors to meld together beautifully in a fraction of the time it would take on the stovetop.

Moreover, the array of vegetables used in this recipe provides a plethora of nutrients, making it a wholesome and nourishing meal. You can customize the vegetable selection based on your preferences or what’s available in your kitchen, adding flexibility to the recipe.

Additionally, this curry is a fantastic option for those following vegetarian or vegan diets. It’s hearty, satisfying, and bursting with flavor, proving that plant-based meals can be just as indulgent as their meat-based counterparts.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Instant Pot Vegetable Curry recipe offers a delightful combination of convenience, healthfulness, and flavor. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice cook, this recipe is sure to become a regular feature in your meal rotation. Consider trying it out for your next family dinner or gathering with friends. With its vibrant colors and aromatic spices, it’s bound to be a crowd-pleaser. Enjoy the delightful experience of cooking and savoring this flavorful and healthy vegetable curry!
