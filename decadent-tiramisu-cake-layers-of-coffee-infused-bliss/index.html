Decadent Tiramisu Cake: Layers of Coffee-Infused Bliss


If you’re a fan of the classic Italian dessert, Tiramisu, then get ready to take your love for this indulgent treat to a whole new level with our Decadent Tiramisu Cake! This cake is a true masterpiece, combining layers of coffee-infused bliss with rich mascarpone cream and a hint of chocolate. It’s the perfect dessert for any occasion, whether you’re celebrating a special milestone or simply treating yourself to a sweet delight.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled

For the mascarpone cream:

  • 3 cups mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

For the coffee syrup:

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

For the cocoa dusting:

  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour three 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk until well combined.

  3. Add the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix on medium speed until the batter is smooth and well incorporated.

  4. Gradually add the cooled brewed coffee to the batter, mixing on low speed until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula.

  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  7. While the cakes are baking, prepare the mascarpone cream. In a mixing bowl, beat the mascarpone cheese, heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. Set aside.

  8. Once the cakes are baked, remove them from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then, transfer the cakes to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. While the cakes are cooling, prepare the coffee syrup. In a small saucepan, combine the brewed coffee and granulated sugar. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring until the sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  10. Once the cakes have cooled, use a long serrated knife to level the tops if needed. Place one cake layer on a serving plate and brush it generously with the coffee syrup.

  11. Spread a generous layer of mascarpone cream over the soaked cake layer. Repeat this process with the remaining cake layers, ensuring each layer is brushed with the coffee syrup and topped with mascarpone cream.

  12. After the final layer of mascarpone cream, dust the top of the cake with cocoa powder using a fine mesh sieve.

  13. Refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the cake to set.

  14. Serve chilled and enjoy each decadent slice of coffee-infused bliss!

Conclusion

Indulge in the velvety layers of our Decadent Tiramisu Cake, where the flavors of coffee, mascarpone cream, and chocolate come together to create a truly unforgettable dessert experience. This cake is perfect for impressing guests at parties or simply treating yourself to a slice of heaven. So go ahead, take your taste buds on a journey and savor the blissful essence of Tiramisu in this delightful cake creation.
