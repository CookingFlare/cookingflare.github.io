Quick and Easy Gluten-Free Pancake Recipe for a Delicious Breakfast


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy breakfast option that is both quick and easy to make? Look no further than this gluten-free pancake recipe! Whether you have dietary restrictions or simply want to try something new, these pancakes are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gluten-free flour blend
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil (melted)
  • Maple syrup, fresh fruits, or your favorite toppings

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free flour blend, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, apple cider vinegar, vanilla extract, and melted coconut oil. This combination will act as the wet ingredients for the pancake batter.

  3. Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense pancakes.

  4. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. You can lightly grease the surface with coconut oil or cooking spray if desired, although it’s not necessary.

  5. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, scoop the pancake batter onto the heated skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles start to form on the surface. This is an indication that the pancakes are ready to flip.

  6. Carefully flip the pancakes using a spatula, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.

  7. Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate, and continue cooking the remaining batter in batches until all the pancakes are done.

  8. Serve the gluten-free pancakes warm with your favorite toppings. Drizzle with maple syrup, add fresh fruits like berries or sliced bananas, or get creative with yogurt, nut butter, or a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Enjoy your delicious and fluffy gluten-free pancakes for a delightful breakfast treat that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized for the day ahead!

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a gluten-free flour blend, you can make your own by combining equal parts of rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour. This will give the pancakes a light and fluffy texture.
  • Feel free to customize your pancakes by adding extras like chocolate chips, blueberries, or chopped nuts to the batter before cooking.
  • For a protein boost, you can add a scoop of your favorite protein powder to the batter. Just be sure to adjust the liquid ingredients accordingly.
  • Leftover pancakes can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat them in a toaster or microwave before serving.

There you have it – a quick and easy gluten-free pancake recipe that will make your mornings brighter and more delicious. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply want to try something new, these pancakes are a fantastic option. So go ahead, whip up a batch, and indulge in a delightful breakfast that will leave you feeling satisfied and ready to tackle the day!
