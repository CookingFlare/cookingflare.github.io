Penne alla Vodka with Chicken: A Perfect Combination of Creamy and Savory


Penne alla Vodka with Chicken is a classic Italian dish that brings together the richness of creamy pasta and the savory flavors of chicken. This indulgent recipe is perfect for those looking for a hearty and satisfying meal. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a delicious dinner, this dish is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup vodka
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the penne pasta according to the package instructions. Make sure to cook it until al dente, as it will continue to cook in the sauce later. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook them until they are golden brown on both sides and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté them until they become translucent and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  4. Pour in the vodka and cook for another 2 minutes, allowing the alcohol to burn off. Add the tomato puree to the skillet and stir well to combine. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, slice the cooked chicken breasts into thin strips. Once the sauce has thickened slightly, add the sliced chicken to the skillet and stir to coat it with the sauce.

  6. Pour in the heavy cream and continue to cook for another 5 minutes, until the sauce is creamy and smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Add the cooked penne pasta to the skillet and toss it with the sauce until every piece of pasta is coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the pasta to absorb some of the flavors.

  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the Penne alla Vodka with Chicken with fresh basil leaves. Serve hot and enjoy!

Final Thoughts

Penne alla Vodka with Chicken is a delightful combination of creamy and savory flavors that will have your taste buds dancing with joy. The rich and velvety sauce, paired with tender chicken and perfectly cooked penne pasta, creates a dish that is both comforting and indulgent.

This recipe is versatile and can be easily customized to suit your preferences. You can add some crushed red pepper flakes for a hint of spiciness or sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese on top for an extra touch of decadence. Serve it with a side salad or some garlic bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a delicious and comforting pasta dish, give Penne alla Vodka with Chicken a try. Your family and friends will be impressed by your culinary skills, and you’ll be rewarded with a meal that is sure to become a favorite in your household. Enjoy!
