The Best Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Kabobs Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make dish that will impress your friends and family at your next barbecue? Look no further! In this recipe, we’re going to show you how to make the best Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs that will leave everyone craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 red bell peppers, cut into chunks
  • 2 yellow bell peppers, cut into chunks
  • 1 large red onion, cut into chunks
  • 16 cherry tomatoes
  • 8 large button mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon dried Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes to prevent them from burning.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, dried Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. This will be our flavorful marinade.

  3. Place the chicken pieces in a large zip-top bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken and toss to coat evenly. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours for maximum flavor.

  4. While the chicken is marinating, prepare the vegetables. Thread the chicken, bell peppers, onion, cherry tomatoes, and mushrooms onto the skewers in an alternating pattern.

  5. Once the grill is hot, place the kabobs on the grill grates. Cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.

  6. Remove the kabobs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure a juicy and flavorful bite.

  7. Serve the Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs with a side of your favorite dipping sauce or a fresh salad. They are perfect for a summer barbecue, a casual weeknight dinner, or even as a meal prep option.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize the kabobs with your favorite vegetables. Zucchini, eggplant, or even pineapple chunks would make great additions.
  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook the kabobs in the oven. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  • Make sure to soak wooden skewers in water before using them to prevent them from burning on the grill.
  • You can also use metal skewers for this recipe if you prefer.

Get ready to savor the flavors of Italy with these mouthwatering Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs. The combination of tender chicken, colorful vegetables, and aromatic Italian seasoning will transport you to the streets of Rome with every bite. So fire up your grill, gather your friends and family, and enjoy a delicious meal together. Buon appetito!
