Hearty Paleo Chicken and Spinach Casserole Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious dish that fits perfectly into your paleo lifestyle, look no further. This hearty chicken and spinach casserole is not only packed with flavor but also bursting with nutrients. It’s an ideal meal for busy weeknights or a satisfying dinner that can be enjoyed by the whole family. Let’s dive right into this wonderful recipe.

Ingredients

For the Chicken and Spinach Casserole

  • 3 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded or diced
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1/2 cup almond flour (optional, for a slightly thicker consistency)

For the Topping

  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

Preparing the Chicken and Spinach Casserole

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Prepare the Chicken: If you haven’t already cooked your chicken, start by cooking it until it’s no longer pink inside. You can bake, grill, or boil it according to your preference. Once cooked, shred or dice the chicken and set it aside.

  3. Sauté the Vegetables: In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  4. Add the Spinach: Gradually add the chopped spinach to the skillet, stirring until it’s wilted and well combined with the onions and garlic. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  5. Combine Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked chicken, sautéed spinach mixture, coconut milk, chicken broth, nutritional yeast, sea salt, black pepper, paprika, dried thyme, oregano, basil, and ground mustard. If you prefer a thicker casserole, stir in 1/2 cup of almond flour at this stage.

  6. Transfer to Baking Dish: Pour the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

Preparing the Topping

  1. Mix the Topping Ingredients: In a small bowl, combine the almond flour, shredded coconut, olive oil, and sea salt. Mix until the ingredients are well incorporated and have a crumbly texture.

  2. Sprinkle the Topping: Evenly sprinkle the topping mixture over the casserole.

Baking

  1. Bake the Casserole: Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the casserole is bubbly around the edges.

  2. Let it Rest: Remove the casserole from the oven and let it rest for about 10 minutes before serving. This helps the dish set and makes it easier to serve.

Serving Suggestions

This hearty paleo chicken and spinach casserole pairs wonderfully with a fresh green salad or roasted vegetables. It’s also great as a standalone dish due to its rich flavors and satisfying ingredients. For those who enjoy a little extra heat, consider adding a sprinkle of red pepper flakes on top before serving.

Storage and Reheating

If you have leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. To reheat, either use the microwave or place the casserole in an oven-safe dish and warm it in the oven at 350°F (175°C) until heated through.

Nutritional Benefits

This casserole is not only delicious but also brimming with health benefits:

  • Chicken: A lean source of protein that helps build and repair tissues.
  • Spinach: Rich in vitamins A, C, and K, as well as iron and calcium, spinach is a powerhouse of nutrients.
  • Coconut Milk: Provides healthy fats that are essential for hormone production and brain function.
  • Nutritional Yeast: Adds a cheesy flavor while providing B-vitamins that are crucial for energy metabolism.

Tips and Variations

  • Vegetable Variations: Feel free to mix in other vegetables such as kale, mushrooms, or bell peppers for added flavor and nutrition.
  • Herb Substitutions: Fresh herbs can be used in place of dried ones for a more vibrant taste. Use three times the amount of fresh herbs compared to dried.
  • Dairy-Free Cheese: If you’re missing the traditional cheesy casserole, consider adding a dairy-free cheese alternative to the mix for a gooey texture.

Final Thoughts

This hearty paleo chicken and spinach casserole is a testament to how eating healthy doesn’t have to mean sacrificing flavor. It’s a versatile and comforting dish that brings together the best of wholesome ingredients, making it perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking to incorporate more nutritious meals into your routine, this casserole is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Enjoy the robust flavors and nourishing goodness of this paleo-friendly dish, and don’t hesitate to experiment with ingredients to make it your own. Happy cooking!
