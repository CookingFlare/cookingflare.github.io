Mediterranean-inspired Quinoa Salad with Lemon and Feta


Are you looking for a light, refreshing, and nutritious dish that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than this delightful Mediterranean-inspired quinoa salad with lemon and feta. Packed with protein, fiber, and a spectrum of vibrant flavors, this salad is a celebration of the fresh and wholesome ingredients that characterize the Mediterranean diet.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 English cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Quinoa: Rinse the quinoa under cold water. In a medium saucepan, bring the water or vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed. Once done, fluff the quinoa with a fork and let it cool to room temperature.

  2. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, honey, minced garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

  3. Assemble the Salad: In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, parsley, and mint.

  4. Add the Dressing: Pour the dressing over the salad and gently toss everything together until well combined.

  5. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Before serving, give the salad a gentle toss and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

Why This Salad?

Nutritious and Satisfying

Quinoa is a complete protein, making this salad a satisfying and nutritious option for vegetarians and vegans. The combination of quinoa, feta cheese, and olives provides a good dose of healthy fats, protein, and fiber, while the fresh vegetables add a spectrum of vitamins and minerals.

Bursting with Mediterranean Flavors

The lemony dressing, fragrant herbs, and tangy feta cheese bring the flavors of the Mediterranean to life in this salad. Each bite is a delightful medley of citrusy, herby, and briny notes that will transport you to the sun-soaked shores of the Mediterranean.

Versatile and Customizable

This salad serves as a fantastic standalone dish for a light lunch or dinner, but it also makes a wonderful side dish for grilled meats, seafood, or roasted vegetables. You can also customize it by adding your favorite Mediterranean ingredients such as artichoke hearts, roasted red peppers, or even grilled eggplant.

Meal Prep Friendly

This salad holds up well in the refrigerator, making it an excellent option for meal prep. You can easily make a batch at the beginning of the week and enjoy it for several days, either as a side dish or a light main course.

A Celebration of Fresh Ingredients

By using fresh, seasonal, and wholesome ingredients, this salad embodies the heart of the Mediterranean diet - a celebration of simple, flavorful, and nourishing foods that are as good for the body as they are for the soul.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re a fan of Mediterranean cuisine or simply looking for a delicious and nutritious salad to add to your repertoire, this Mediterranean-inspired quinoa salad with lemon and feta is sure to become a favorite. Its vibrant flavors, nutrient-packed ingredients, and versatile nature make it a standout dish that’s perfect for any occasion. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the Mediterranean spirit, and savor every refreshing bite of this delightful salad!
