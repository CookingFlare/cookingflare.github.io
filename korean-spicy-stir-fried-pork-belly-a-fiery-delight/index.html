Korean Spicy Stir-Fried Pork Belly: A Fiery Delight


If you’re a fan of bold and spicy flavors, then Korean Spicy Stir-Fried Pork Belly, also known as Jeyuk Bokkeum, is a dish you must try. This fiery delight combines tender slices of pork belly with a spicy marinade, creating a mouthwatering dish that will leave you craving for more. In this blog post, we’ll explore the key ingredients, cooking techniques, and serving suggestions for this popular Korean dish.

Ingredients

To make Korean Spicy Stir-Fried Pork Belly, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 800g / 1.76 pounds Korean pork belly slices (Samgyeopsal), cut into bite-size pieces [1]
  • Marinade: gochujang (Korean red chili paste), soy sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, sugar, sesame oil
  • Vegetables: onion, green onions, carrots, cabbage (optional)
  • Cooking oil for stir-frying

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the marinade ingredients: gochujang, soy sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, sugar, and sesame oil. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade [2].
  2. Add the pork belly slices to the marinade and mix until the meat is well coated. Allow the pork to marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to penetrate the meat [2].
  3. Heat a large frying pan or wok over medium-high heat and add a small amount of cooking oil.
  4. Add the marinated pork belly to the hot pan and stir-fry for about 5 minutes or until the meat is cooked through and slightly caramelized.
  5. If desired, add the vegetables such as onions, green onions, carrots, and cabbage to the pan and continue stir-frying until the vegetables are cooked but still crisp [2].
  6. Serve the Korean Spicy Stir-Fried Pork Belly immediately with steamed rice or as a filling for lettuce wraps [3].

Tips and Variations

  • Pork belly is the traditional cut of meat used in this dish, but you can also use pork shoulder or tenderloin as a leaner alternative [4].
  • Adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less gochujang according to your taste preferences.
  • For added flavor, you can garnish the dish with sliced shallots and sesame seeds.
  • Feel free to experiment with additional vegetables such as bell peppers or mushrooms to add more texture and flavor to the stir-fry [2].

Conclusion

Korean Spicy Stir-Fried Pork Belly, or Jeyuk Bokkeum, is a delicious and fiery dish that showcases the bold flavors of Korean cuisine. With its tender pork belly slices and spicy marinade, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings for a flavorful and satisfying meal. Whether you enjoy it with steamed rice or as a filling for lettuce wraps, this dish is a must-try for any fan of Korean cuisine.

So why not spice up your next meal with this Korean Spicy Stir-Fried Pork Belly recipe? Gather the ingredients, follow the simple steps, and get ready to indulge in a fiery delight that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight!
