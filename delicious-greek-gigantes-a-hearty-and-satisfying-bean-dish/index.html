Delicious Greek Gigantes: A Hearty and Satisfying Bean Dish


If you’re looking for a hearty, flavorful, and satisfying dish that’s packed with protein and fiber, look no further than Greek Gigantes. This traditional Greek recipe features giant beans, known as “gigantes,” slow-cooked in a rich tomato sauce until tender and infused with aromatic herbs and spices. Whether you’re a fan of Mediterranean cuisine or simply looking to explore new flavors, Greek Gigantes are sure to delight your taste buds and leave you feeling nourished and satisfied.

Ingredients

To prepare this delicious Greek dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 500g dried gigantes beans
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 large carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Gigantes Beans

  1. Rinse the dried gigantes beans under cold water and place them in a large bowl.
  2. Cover the beans with plenty of water and let them soak overnight or for at least 8 hours to soften.

Step 2: Cook the Gigantes Beans

  1. After soaking, drain the beans and transfer them to a large pot.
  2. Cover the beans with fresh water and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the beans for 45-60 minutes, or until they are tender but still hold their shape.
  4. Once cooked, drain the beans and set them aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Tomato Sauce

  1. In a separate large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion, garlic, carrots, and celery, and sauté until the vegetables are soft and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, dried oregano, dried thyme, bay leaves, salt, and pepper.
  4. Allow the sauce to simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.

Step 4: Combine the Beans and Sauce

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Place the cooked gigantes beans in a large baking dish.
  3. Pour the tomato sauce over the beans, ensuring they are fully coated.
  4. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 45-60 minutes, or until the beans are tender and the sauce is bubbling.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Remove the baking dish from the oven and let it cool slightly.
  2. Discard the bay leaves and give the gigantes beans a gentle stir.
  3. Serve the Greek Gigantes hot, garnished with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and a sprinkle of fresh chopped parsley.
  4. Enjoy this hearty and satisfying bean dish with crusty bread or a side of Greek salad for a complete Mediterranean meal.

Final Thoughts

Greek Gigantes are a true celebration of simple, wholesome ingredients and time-honored cooking techniques. This dish not only offers a delicious dining experience but also provides a healthy dose of plant-based protein and dietary fiber. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply treating yourself to a nourishing meal, Greek Gigantes are a wonderful addition to any table. Embrace the flavors of Greece and savor every bite of this timeless and comforting bean dish.
