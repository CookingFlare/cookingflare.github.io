Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute. Season the shrimp with salt and pepper, then add them to the skillet. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the skillet from the heat and squeeze the juice of one lemon over the shrimp. Sprinkle the lemon zest and chopped parsley on top for added freshness. Serve the lemon garlic shrimp hot and enjoy!
