Gourmet Smoked Turkey Club Sandwich: A Classic with a Twist


If you’re a fan of classic club sandwiches, get ready to take your taste buds on a gourmet adventure with our Smoked Turkey Club Sandwich. This upgraded version of the traditional club sandwich is bursting with flavors and features a delicious twist that will leave you craving for more. With a careful combination of premium ingredients, this sandwich is a true delight for all the senses.

Ingredients

To create this gourmet masterpiece, you will need:

  • 8 slices of artisan bread (such as ciabatta or sourdough)
  • 1 pound of smoked turkey breast, thinly sliced
  • 8 slices of crispy bacon
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by toasting the artisan bread slices to perfection. You want them to be golden brown and slightly crispy. Set them aside to cool slightly.

  2. In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise and Dijon mustard together. This tangy and creamy spread will add a burst of flavor to your sandwich.

  3. Take four of the bread slices and spread the mayo-mustard mixture evenly on one side of each slice.

  4. Layer the smoked turkey on top of the mayo-mustard spread. Be generous with the amount of turkey you add; after all, it’s the star of the show!

  5. Next, add a layer of crispy bacon on top of the turkey. The smoky and salty flavors of the bacon will complement the turkey perfectly.

  6. Now it’s time to add some freshness to the sandwich. Layer the tomato slices, lettuce leaves, and avocado slices on top of the bacon. These ingredients will bring a burst of color and a delightful crunch to every bite.

  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to go easy on the salt since the bacon already adds a salty kick to the sandwich.

  8. Top your masterpiece with the remaining four slices of bread. Press gently to ensure all the ingredients are held together.

  9. Using a sharp knife, cut the sandwich diagonally into two halves. This not only makes it easier to hold and eat but also adds an elegant touch to the presentation.

  10. Serve your Gourmet Smoked Turkey Club Sandwich with a side of crispy fries, a fresh salad, or some homemade potato chips. The choice is yours!

The Twist: Cranberry Aioli

Now, here comes the twist that takes this classic sandwich to a whole new level: Cranberry Aioli. This tangy and slightly sweet sauce adds an unexpected burst of flavor that perfectly complements the smoky turkey and bacon. To make the Cranberry Aioli, simply mix 1/4 cup of cranberry sauce with 1/2 cup of mayonnaise. Spread it on one side of the bread before layering the ingredients for an unforgettable taste experience.

Conclusion

The Gourmet Smoked Turkey Club Sandwich is a delicious twist on a classic favorite. With the perfect combination of smoky turkey, crispy bacon, fresh veggies, and the delightful Cranberry Aioli, this sandwich is a feast for both the eyes and the taste buds. Whether you’re enjoying it for lunch or serving it at a dinner party, this gourmet creation is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, get creative in the kitchen, and indulge in the ultimate club sandwich experience.
