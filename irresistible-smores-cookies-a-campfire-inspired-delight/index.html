Irresistible S'mores Cookies: A Campfire-Inspired Delight


Who doesn’t love the classic combination of graham crackers, marshmallows, and chocolate? S’mores are a beloved treat that reminds us of cozy campfire nights and childhood memories. Today, we’re taking this campfire favorite and giving it a delicious twist with our recipe for Irresistible S’mores Cookies. These cookies capture all the flavors of traditional s’mores in a convenient and portable form. Get ready to indulge in a campfire-inspired delight!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, graham cracker crumbs, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Fold in the semi-sweet chocolate chips and mini marshmallows, distributing them evenly throughout the dough.

  6. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may appear slightly undercooked, but they will firm up as the cookies cool.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, serve and enjoy these irresistible S’mores Cookies with a glass of milk or a cup of hot cocoa. These cookies are perfect for sharing with friends and family, or for indulging in a sweet treat on your own.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra gooey and marshmallow-filled center, press a few additional mini marshmallows into the tops of the cookie dough before baking.
  • If you prefer a crunchier texture, bake the cookies for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  • Experiment with different types of chocolate, such as milk chocolate or dark chocolate, to customize the flavor of your cookies.
  • To enhance the campfire experience, try adding a sprinkle of crushed graham crackers on top of each cookie before baking.
  • These cookies can be stored in an airtight container for up to 5 days. If desired, you can warm them in the microwave for a few seconds to enjoy that freshly baked taste.

Conclusion

With their irresistible combination of graham crackers, chocolate, and marshmallows, these S’mores Cookies bring the essence of a campfire treat into your kitchen. Whether you’re reminiscing about past camping adventures or simply craving a delicious cookie, this recipe is a must-try. Indulge in the flavors of summer and enjoy the sweet nostalgia of s’mores all year round. Happy baking!
