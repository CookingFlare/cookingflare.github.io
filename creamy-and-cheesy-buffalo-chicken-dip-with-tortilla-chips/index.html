Creamy and Cheesy Buffalo Chicken Dip with Tortilla Chips


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer that combines spicy buffalo chicken with creamy, cheesy goodness, then this Creamy and Cheesy Buffalo Chicken Dip with Tortilla Chips recipe is a must-try! Packed with bold flavors and a satisfying crunch, this dip is perfect for game nights, parties, or any gathering where you want to impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced green onions
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked chicken and buffalo sauce. Stir until the chicken is well coated with the sauce.

  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the cream cheese and sour cream until smooth and well combined. This will serve as the creamy base of the dip.

  4. Add the shredded cheddar cheese, crumbled blue cheese, diced green onions, diced celery, and diced red bell pepper to the cream cheese mixture. Stir until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  5. Gently fold in the buffalo chicken mixture into the creamy base. Be careful not to overmix, as you want to maintain some texture and chunkiness.

  6. Transfer the dip to a baking dish and spread it out evenly. For a crispy topping, sprinkle some extra shredded cheddar cheese on top.

  7. Bake the dip in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Keep an eye on it to prevent it from burning.

  8. Once it’s done, remove the dip from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the dip to thicken slightly.

  9. Serve the Creamy and Cheesy Buffalo Chicken Dip with Tortilla Chips alongside a bowl of fresh tortilla chips. The crispiness of the chips pairs perfectly with the creamy dip, creating a delightful contrast of textures.

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize the level of spiciness by adjusting the amount of buffalo sauce you use. If you prefer a milder dip, reduce the amount of sauce. For a spicier kick, add a bit more.
  • If you’re not a fan of blue cheese, you can substitute it with additional shredded cheddar cheese or another type of cheese that you enjoy.
  • To save time, you can use rotisserie chicken or leftover cooked chicken for this recipe. Simply shred the chicken and proceed with the instructions.
  • If you want to add a touch of freshness, garnish the dip with some freshly chopped cilantro or parsley before serving.

Enjoy the Creamy and Cheesy Buffalo Chicken Dip with Tortilla Chips as a crowd-pleasing appetizer that will have everyone coming back for more. This delicious and satisfying dip is sure to be a hit at any gathering, and it’s so easy to make. So go ahead and indulge in the bold flavors and creamy goodness of this irresistible dip!
