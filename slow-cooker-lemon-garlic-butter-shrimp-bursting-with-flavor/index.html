Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp: Bursting with Flavor


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free seafood recipe that will impress your family and friends? Look no further! This Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe is the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings for a burst of flavor without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 teaspoon of dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, dried parsley, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure all the flavors are evenly distributed.

  2. Place the peeled and deveined shrimp into the slow cooker. Pour the lemon garlic butter mixture over the shrimp, making sure they are fully coated.

  3. Set the slow cooker to low heat and cook for 2-3 hours, or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking.

  4. Once the shrimp are cooked, remove them from the slow cooker and transfer to a serving plate. Drizzle any remaining sauce from the slow cooker over the shrimp.

  5. Garnish with fresh parsley for an added pop of color and freshness.

  6. Serve the Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp with your favorite side dishes, such as steamed rice, pasta, or a fresh salad. The shrimp can also be enjoyed on its own as a flavorful appetizer.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a splash of white wine or a sprinkle of Old Bay seasoning to the lemon garlic butter mixture before pouring it over the shrimp.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water and add it to the slow cooker during the last 30 minutes of cooking. Stir well to thicken the sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite herbs or spices. Fresh basil or dill would be a great addition to enhance the overall taste.
  • To make this recipe spicier, increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a dash of hot sauce to the lemon garlic butter mixture.

Why Choose Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp?

This recipe offers a unique twist on traditional shrimp dishes by using the slow cooker to infuse the shrimp with a rich and tangy flavor. The combination of garlic, lemon, and butter creates a mouthwatering sauce that perfectly complements the tender and succulent shrimp.

Not only is this recipe bursting with flavor, but it’s also incredibly convenient. The slow cooker does all the work for you, allowing you to enjoy a delicious homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen. It’s the perfect recipe to impress your guests at a dinner party or to treat yourself to a flavorful and hassle-free weeknight dinner.

So, why wait? Give this Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe a try and experience the incredible flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more.
