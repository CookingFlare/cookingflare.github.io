Yummy Paleo Eggplant Lasagna Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy twist on traditional lasagna, then this Yummy Paleo Eggplant Lasagna recipe is for you. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is perfect for those following a Paleo or low-carb diet. The best part? It’s easy to make and will satisfy your cravings without the guilt.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cups dairy-free cheese, shredded (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the eggplants lengthwise into thin strips, about 1/4 inch thick. Place the slices on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and sprinkle them with salt. Let them sit for about 10 minutes to release excess moisture. Pat them dry with a paper towel.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the almond flour, nutritional yeast, chopped fresh basil, and chopped fresh parsley. This mixture will be used as a substitute for traditional lasagna noodles.
  6. Begin layering the lasagna by spreading a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange a single layer of eggplant slices on top of the sauce, followed by a layer of the almond flour mixture. Repeat these layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of sauce on top.
  7. If desired, sprinkle the dairy-free cheese on top of the lasagna.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the lasagna to set and make it easier to slice into individual portions.
  10. Serve your Yummy Paleo Eggplant Lasagna with a side salad or steamed vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

This Yummy Paleo Eggplant Lasagna recipe is a healthy and flavorful alternative to traditional lasagna. By using eggplant slices instead of pasta and a combination of almond flour and nutritional yeast as a substitute for cheese, you can enjoy all the deliciousness of lasagna while sticking to your Paleo or low-carb diet. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress. Give it a try and let us know what you think!
