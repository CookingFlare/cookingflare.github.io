Citrus Sips: Beer Pairings with Citrus-Infused Dishes


The smell of citrus and the taste of well-crafted beer are two of life’s greatest simple pleasures. When combined, these simple flavors can add a burst of refreshment to any meal. In this post, we’ll explore some of the most popular citrus-infused dishes that perfectly complement different types of beer.

Sparkling Beer and Citrus Salad

One classic combination that never fails to impress is a crisp, sparkling beer with a light citrus salad. The slight bitterness of the beer contrasts with the sweetness of the citrus, making it a perfect choice for those who prefer a touch of tartness to their dishes.

In this recipe, a bright and tangy grapefruit salad complements a clean, refreshing lager. The citrus-infused dressing pairs wonderfully with the light, crisp notes of the beer. Be sure to use a high-quality lager with a mild flavor profile; this will allow the citrus elements to shine through.

IPA and Orange-Infused Risotto

The robust, hoppy flavors of an India Pale Ale (IPA) are a perfect match for rich, cream-based sauces and dishes. When you add a splash of citrus, such as orange or tangerine zest, the dish takes on a depth that complements the beer.

For this dish, try making an orange-infused risotto, where the flavors of the dish elevate the hoppy flavors in the beer. Keep the IPA cold while cooking; this will allow you to taste its flavors more clearly as you enjoy your meal.

Grapefruit Beer-Can Chicken

Beer-can chicken is a fun and easy dish that’s sure to impress your friends and family. The grapefruit version adds a citrus kick to a timeless favorite.

To prepare this dish, use a whole chicken and place it over a can of grapefruit-infused beer. The citrus pairs wonderfully with the smoky flavors of a grilled chicken, making for a perfect summer meal. Be sure to marinate the chicken in a citrus-based marinade before grilling, which will add an extra layer of flavor.

Lemon Beer Cichetti

Cichetti, or Italian tapas, are small plates that pack a big punch of flavor. When paired with a lemon-infused beer, these dishes create a bright and zesty meal.

Try serving small plates of crab croquettes with a delicious citrus aioli sauce. Pair the dish with a light, lemon-infused beer. The tangy notes of the citrus-infused beer will cut through the richness of the crab, making for a delicious and refreshing snack or appetizer.

Orange-Yuzu Fish Tacos

Fish tacos are a favorite for many, and when you add yuzu, a Japanese citrus fruit, their flavor profiles take on a whole new dimension. The unique tang of yuzu pairs well with a tangy white beer, such as a Belgian wit or a gose.

Make sure to use a sustainably-sourced white fish, like cod or mahi mahi, in your tacos. The creamy citrus slaw that tops the fish adds a beautiful brightness to these tacos, which pairs perfectly with the light and refreshing beer.

In conclusion, beer and citrus dishes are a match made in heaven. From a simple citrus salad to a delicious yuzu fish taco, these dishes and their accompanying beers prove that there’s always room for creativity in the world of cuisine. So next time you’re planning a meal or just enjoying an ice-cold beer, don’t be afraid to think outside the box and explore the flavor world of citrus-infused dishes. Enjoy!
