Flavorful Breakfast Enchilada Casserole Ideas for a Crowd


Are you tired of serving the same old breakfast dishes when you have guests over? If you’re looking to impress a crowd with a delicious and flavorful breakfast, then look no further than these amazing Breakfast Enchilada Casserole ideas. Packed with mouthwatering flavors and easy to make, these casseroles are perfect for feeding a crowd and will leave everyone asking for seconds.

1. Cheesy Bacon and Egg Enchilada Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 12 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Cook the bacon until crispy, then crumble it into small pieces.
  4. Add the crumbled bacon, shredded cheddar cheese, shredded Monterey Jack cheese, diced bell peppers, and chopped cilantro to the egg mixture. Mix well.
  5. Place a tortilla in the prepared baking dish, then pour some of the egg mixture over it. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and egg mixture, layering them in the dish.
  6. Pour the enchilada sauce evenly over the top of the casserole.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.

2. Sausage and Vegetable Enchilada Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground breakfast sausage
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1 cup diced zucchini
  • 1 cup diced mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the sausage over medium heat until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the diced onion, bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms, garlic, cumin, and chili powder. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  5. Place a tortilla in the prepared baking dish, then spread some of the cooked sausage and vegetable mixture over it. Pour some of the egg mixture on top. Repeat with the remaining tortillas, sausage and vegetable mixture, and egg mixture, layering them in the dish.
  6. Pour the enchilada sauce evenly over the top of the casserole.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded Monterey Jack cheese over the enchilada sauce.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving. Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro.

3. Vegetarian Black Bean and Spinach Enchilada Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1 cup diced zucchini
  • 1 cup diced mushrooms
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 4 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the diced bell pepper, zucchini, mushrooms, cumin, and chili powder to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in the black beans and spinach leaves. Cook until the spinach wilts.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  6. Place a tortilla in the prepared baking dish, then spread some of the black bean and spinach mixture over it. Pour some of the egg mixture on top. Repeat with the remaining tortillas, black bean and spinach mixture, and egg mixture, layering them in the dish.
  7. Pour the enchilada sauce evenly over the top of the casserole.
  8. Sprinkle the shredded Monterey Jack cheese over the enchilada sauce.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving. Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro.

These flavorful Breakfast Enchilada Casserole ideas are sure to be a hit at your next breakfast gathering. Whether you prefer a meaty version or a vegetarian option, these casseroles will satisfy everyone’s taste buds. Serve them with a side of salsa, sour cream, or guacamole for an extra burst of flavor. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to impress your guests with these delicious and crowd-pleasing breakfast dishes. Enjoy!
