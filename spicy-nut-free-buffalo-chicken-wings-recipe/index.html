Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Chicken Wings Recipe


Who doesn’t love a plate of spicy buffalo chicken wings? They’re the perfect appetizer for game nights, parties, or even just a casual night in with friends. But what if you have a nut allergy? Don’t worry, I’ve got you covered with this nut-free buffalo chicken wings recipe that packs a punch in both flavor and spice.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings, separated into drumettes and flats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (such as Frank’s RedHot)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure the spices are evenly distributed.

  3. Pat the chicken wings dry with a paper towel, then add them to the bowl with the flour mixture. Toss the wings until they are fully coated with the flour mixture.

  4. Place the coated wings on the prepared baking sheet, making sure they are evenly spaced apart. This will allow them to crisp up nicely in the oven.

  5. Bake the wings in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Flip the wings halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  6. While the wings are baking, prepare the buffalo sauce. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Once melted, add the hot sauce, honey, and apple cider vinegar. Stir well until everything is combined and heated through.

  7. Once the wings are cooked, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Pour the buffalo sauce over the wings and toss them gently to coat each wing evenly.

  8. Serve the spicy nut-free buffalo chicken wings with your favorite dipping sauce, such as ranch dressing or blue cheese dressing. Garnish with celery sticks and carrot sticks for a refreshing crunch.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer your wings even spicier, increase the amount of cayenne pepper in the flour mixture or add a few dashes of hot sauce to the buffalo sauce.

  • For a tangier flavor, add a squeeze of fresh lime juice to the buffalo sauce before tossing the wings.

  • If you’re short on time, you can also fry the wings instead of baking them. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C) and fry the wings for about 10-12 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Make sure to drain the excess oil on a paper towel before tossing them in the buffalo sauce.

  • Feel free to experiment with different hot sauces to find the one that suits your taste buds best. Some popular alternatives to Frank’s RedHot include Sriracha, Tabasco, or your favorite homemade hot sauce.

So there you have it, a delicious and nut-free version of classic buffalo chicken wings. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving some spicy goodness, these wings are sure to hit the spot. Enjoy!
