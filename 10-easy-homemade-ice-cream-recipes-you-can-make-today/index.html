10 Easy Homemade Ice Cream Recipes You Can Make Today


As the sun shines brightly and the temperature soars, there’s nothing quite like a scoop of homemade ice cream to cool you down. Making ice cream at home is not only a delightful experience, but it also allows you to tailor flavors to your liking and control the ingredients. Whether you have an ice cream maker or prefer no-churn methods, here are ten easy homemade ice cream recipes that you can whip up today.

1. Classic Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions, usually about 20-25 minutes.
  3. Transfer the churned ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tips:

For a richer flavor, you can steep a vanilla bean in the milk and cream mixture for 30 minutes before whisking in the sugar.

2. Chocolate Fudge Swirl Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate fudge sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the cream, milk, sugar, and cocoa powder over medium heat. Stir until the sugar and cocoa are fully dissolved.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Let the mixture cool to room temperature.
  3. Pour the cooled mixture into the ice cream maker and churn as directed.
  4. In the last few minutes of churning, drizzle in the chocolate fudge sauce to create swirls.
  5. Freeze for at least 4 hours before indulging.

Tips:

For extra chocolatey goodness, add chocolate chips to the mixture during the last few minutes of churning.

3. Strawberry-Basil Sorbet

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the strawberries, sugar, basil, lemon juice, and salt. Blend until smooth.
  2. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve to remove seeds.
  3. Transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  4. Freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tips:

For a refreshing twist, try adding a splash of balsamic vinegar to the strawberry mixture before blending.

4. Mango Coconut Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the diced mangoes, coconut milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. Freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tips:

For added texture, fold in some toasted coconut flakes during the last few minutes of churning.

5. Coffee Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, milk, sugar, brewed coffee, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. Freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tips:

For an extra caffeine kick, use espresso instead of brewed coffee.

6. Peanut Butter Cup Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chopped peanut butter cups

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, milk, sugar, peanut butter, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. In the last few minutes of churning, fold in the chopped peanut butter cups.
  4. Freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tips:

For a crunchy texture, add crushed peanuts to the mixture before freezing.

7. Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • A few drops of green food coloring (optional)
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, milk, sugar, peppermint extract, and food coloring until well combined.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn as directed.
  3. In the last few minutes of churning, add the chocolate chips.
  4. Freeze for at least 4 hours before enjoying.

Tips:

For a stronger mint flavor, use fresh mint leaves in the cream mixture and strain before churning.

8. Cookies and Cream Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 15 Oreo cookies, crushed

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the heavy cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. In the last few minutes of churning, fold in the crushed Oreo cookies.
  4. Freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tips:

For a fun twist, try using different flavored cookies like chocolate chip or peanut butter cookies.

9. Lavender Honey Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons dried lavender flowers
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream and milk over medium heat. Add the honey and lavender flowers and stir until combined.
  2. Remove from heat and let steep for 30 minutes. Strain the mixture to remove the lavender.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  4. Freeze for at least 4 hours before indulging.

Tips:

Lavender can be quite potent, so adjust the quantity to your taste preference.

10. Banana Split Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • Chocolate syrup and maraschino cherries for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, heavy cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. If desired, fold in the chopped nuts during the last few minutes of churning.
  4. Freeze for at least 4 hours before serving, topped with chocolate syrup and cherries.

Tips:

You can layer this ice cream with fresh banana slices and whipped cream for a true banana split experience.

Conclusion

With these ten easy homemade ice cream recipes, your summer days are bound to be sweeter and cooler. Whether you’re in the mood for something classic like vanilla or adventurous with lavender honey, there’s a recipe here for everyone. Gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy the creamy, dreamy world of homemade ice cream. Happy churning!
