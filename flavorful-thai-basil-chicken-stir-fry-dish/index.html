Flavorful Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry Dish


Are you craving a delicious and aromatic Thai dish that you can whip up quickly at home? Look no further than this Flavorful Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry! Packed with bold flavors, tender chicken, and fresh herbs, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create this mouthwatering dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by slicing the chicken breast thinly, mincing the garlic, slicing the red chili, bell pepper, and onion, and picking the fresh basil leaves. Having all the ingredients ready will make the cooking process smooth.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken with soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, sugar, and black pepper. Let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse into the chicken.

  3. Heat the Pan: Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and sliced red chili. Stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.

  4. Cook the Chicken: Add the marinated chicken to the pan and stir-fry until it is almost cooked through. This should take about 5-6 minutes.

  5. Add Vegetables: Toss in the sliced bell pepper and onion. Continue stir-frying for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  6. Finish with Basil: Add the fresh basil leaves to the pan and stir well to combine. The aroma of the basil will infuse into the dish, giving it an authentic Thai flavor.

  7. Taste and Adjust: Taste the stir fry and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can add more soy sauce, fish sauce, or sugar according to your preference.

  8. Serve Hot: Once everything is cooked to perfection, transfer the Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry to a serving dish. It pairs wonderfully with steamed jasmine rice or noodles.

Enjoy Your Flavorful Creation!

Congratulations! You have successfully prepared a delectable Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry that is bursting with flavor and ready to impress your taste buds. This dish is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with friends. Experiment with the spice levels and ingredients to make it your own signature dish. Serve hot and enjoy the harmonious blend of savory, sweet, and herbal notes in every bite.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a taste of Thailand, why not try your hand at cooking this easy and satisfying Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry? Your culinary skills are sure to shine as you savor the delightful flavors of this aromatic dish. Let the magic of Thai cuisine transport you to the bustling streets of Bangkok without leaving the comfort of your home. Happy cooking and even happier eating!
