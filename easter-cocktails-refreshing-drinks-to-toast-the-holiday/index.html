Easter Cocktails: Refreshing Drinks to Toast the Holiday


Spring is in the air, and Easter is just around the corner! It’s the perfect time to gather with loved ones and celebrate the joy of the season. And what better way to toast to the holiday than with a refreshing cocktail in hand? In this post, we’ll explore some delightful Easter cocktails that will add a festive touch to your celebrations. So, let’s dive in and discover these tantalizing drink recipes!

1. Bunny Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz gin
  • 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz simple syrup
  • 3 oz club soda
  • Mint leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup.
  2. Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for about 10 seconds.
  3. Strain the mixture into a chilled glass filled with ice.
  4. Top with club soda and garnish with mint leaves.
  5. Serve and enjoy the refreshing Bunny Fizz!

2. Carrot Crush

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz carrot juice
  • 1.5 oz vodka
  • 0.5 oz orange liqueur
  • 0.5 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange slice, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine carrot juice, vodka, orange liqueur, lemon juice, and honey.
  2. Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for about 15 seconds.
  3. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice cubes.
  4. Garnish with an orange slice.
  5. Sip on the refreshing Carrot Crush and enjoy the vibrant flavors!

3. Peep-tini

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz marshmallow vodka
  • 1 oz white chocolate liqueur
  • 1 oz coconut cream
  • Colored sugar, for rimming
  • Marshmallow Peeps, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rim a martini glass with colored sugar by dipping the rim in water and then into the sugar.
  2. In a cocktail shaker, combine marshmallow vodka, white chocolate liqueur, and coconut cream.
  3. Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for about 20 seconds.
  4. Strain the mixture into the prepared martini glass.
  5. Garnish with a Marshmallow Peep.
  6. Savor the sweet and creamy Peep-tini!

4. Spring Spritzer

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz elderflower liqueur
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 4 oz sparkling water
  • Cucumber slices, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a glass, combine elderflower liqueur and lime juice.
  2. Fill the glass with ice and top with sparkling water.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with cucumber slices and lime wedges.
  5. Sip on the light and refreshing Spring Spritzer!

5. Jelly Bean Punch

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pineapple juice
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 cup lemon-lime soda
  • 0.5 cup grenadine syrup
  • Assorted jelly beans, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine pineapple juice, orange juice, lemon-lime soda, and grenadine syrup.
  2. Stir well to combine.
  3. Fill glasses with ice and pour the punch over the ice.
  4. Garnish with assorted jelly beans.
  5. Raise a glass of Jelly Bean Punch and enjoy the fruity and fun flavors!

These Easter cocktails are sure to add a splash of excitement to your holiday celebrations. Whether you prefer the zesty Bunny Fizz, the vibrant Carrot Crush, the sweet Peep-tini, the light Spring Spritzer, or the fruity Jelly Bean Punch, there’s a drink for everyone to enjoy. So, raise your glass, toast to the holiday, and savor these refreshing concoctions. Happy Easter!
