Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Chunk Cookies: Irresistible and Chunky


Who can resist the allure of a warm, chewy cookie fresh from the oven? The combination of peanut butter and chocolate is a match made in heaven, and when you add chunky texture to the mix, you get a cookie that’s truly irresistible. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make vegan peanut butter chocolate chunk cookies that are not only delicious but also easy to make and perfect for any occasion.

The Ingredients

To make these scrumptious cookies, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter (creamy or crunchy, whichever you prefer)
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg replacement (such as flax or chia eggs or mashed banana)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semisweet vegan chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped peanuts or peanut butter chips (optional)

The Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone mat.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the peanut butter and vegan butter until smooth and creamy.
  3. Gradually add the granulated sugar and brown sugar to the peanut butter mixture, beating until well combined.
  4. Add the egg replacement, vanilla extract, and mix until smooth.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the peanut butter mixture and mix until a dough forms.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips and chopped peanuts or peanut butter chips, if using.
  7. Scoop tablespoon-sized balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  9. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips and Variations

  • To make these cookies even chunkier, you can add an extra 1/2 cup of chocolate chips or chopped nuts to the dough.
  • If you prefer a crisper cookie, bake for 15-18 minutes. If you prefer a chewier cookie, bake for 10-12 minutes.
  • You can also add a sprinkle of sea salt or a pinch of cinnamon to the cookies before baking for extra flavor.
  • To make these cookies ahead of time, you can bake them and store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. Freeze for up to 2 months.

Conclusion

These vegan peanut butter chocolate chunk cookies are the perfect treat for anyone with a sweet tooth. With their chewy texture and chunky chocolate chips, they’re sure to be a hit with anyone. Whether you’re looking for a special dessert to serve at a dinner party or just a quick snack to satisfy your cravings, these cookies are sure to please. So go ahead, get baking, and enjoy the delicious flavors of peanut butter and chocolate!
