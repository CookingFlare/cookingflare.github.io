Homemade Chocolate Ice Cream: Creamy and Rich, Just Like Grandma's


There’s something truly special about homemade ice cream, especially when it’s a rich and creamy chocolate flavor that takes you back to your childhood. With this recipe, you can recreate the nostalgic delight of Grandma’s homemade chocolate ice cream right in your own kitchen. This recipe is simple, yet it yields a decadent treat that will have your family and friends coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 6 ounces bittersweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and half of the sugar. Place over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture is hot but not boiling.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the remaining sugar, cocoa powder, and egg yolks until well combined.

  3. Gradually pour the hot cream mixture into the bowl with the cocoa and egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly to temper the eggs.

  4. Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and place over medium heat. Stir constantly until the mixture thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon.

  5. Place the chopped chocolate in a large bowl. Pour the hot cream mixture over the chocolate and let it sit for a minute to melt the chocolate. Then, whisk until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth.

  6. Stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.

  7. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, ensuring that the plastic wrap touches the surface of the mixture to prevent a skin from forming. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.

  8. Once the mixture is thoroughly chilled, pour it into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.

  9. Transfer the churned ice cream to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until the ice cream is firm.

Serving Suggestions

This homemade chocolate ice cream is perfect on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with a drizzle of warm chocolate fudge sauce, a dollop of fresh whipped cream, and a sprinkle of chopped nuts or chocolate shavings. You can also use it to make a decadent ice cream sandwich with your favorite cookies.

Tips

  • For an extra indulgent treat, fold in some chopped chocolate pieces or chocolate chips into the ice cream just before transferring it to the freezer.
  • To enhance the chocolate flavor, consider using Dutch-processed cocoa powder for a deeper, richer taste.

Conclusion

Homemade chocolate ice cream is a timeless classic that never fails to delight. With this recipe, you can capture the essence of old-fashioned, creamy chocolate ice cream just like Grandma used to make. Whether enjoyed on a hot summer day or as a comforting dessert year-round, this homemade chocolate ice cream is sure to become a beloved favorite in your household.

So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to savor the rich and velvety goodness of this homemade chocolate ice cream. Your taste buds will thank you!
