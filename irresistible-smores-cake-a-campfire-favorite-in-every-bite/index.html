Irresistible S'mores Cake: A Campfire Favorite in Every Bite


Are you ready to take your love for s’mores to the next level? Look no further than this decadent and irresistible S’mores Cake recipe. Combining the classic flavors of graham crackers, rich chocolate, and gooey marshmallows, this cake is a showstopper that will leave your taste buds begging for more. Whether you’re reminiscing about campfire nights or simply craving a truly indulgent dessert, this S’mores Cake is guaranteed to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water

For the Marshmallow Frosting:

  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract

For the Graham Cracker Crumble:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  4. Stir in the hot water, and mix until the batter is smooth.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Marshmallow Frosting

  1. In a heatproof bowl, combine the egg whites, sugar, and cream of tartar. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water, ensuring that the bottom of the bowl does not touch the water.
  2. Whisk the mixture constantly until the sugar has dissolved and the egg whites are warm to the touch.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment. Beat on high speed until stiff, glossy peaks form.
  4. Add the vanilla extract and mix until combined.

Step 3: Prepare the Graham Cracker Crumble

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated.
  3. Spread the mixture onto a baking sheet and bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until golden brown. Allow the crumble to cool completely.

Step 4: Assemble the Cake

  1. Place one of the cake layers on a serving plate. Spread a layer of marshmallow frosting over the top.
  2. Sprinkle a generous amount of graham cracker crumble over the frosting.
  3. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the entire cake with the remaining marshmallow frosting.
  4. Finish by sprinkling the remaining graham cracker crumble over the top of the cake.

Serving and Enjoying

Slice into this masterpiece of a cake and savor the irresistible combination of flavors and textures. Whether you’re enjoying it at a summer barbecue, a birthday celebration, or simply as a delightful treat, this S’mores Cake is sure to be a hit with everyone who takes a bite.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, preheat the oven, and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will transport you back to your favorite campfire memories. This S’mores Cake is not just a dessert; it’s an experience that will leave a lasting impression on all who indulge in its sweet, nostalgic charm.
