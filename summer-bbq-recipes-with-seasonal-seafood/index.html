Summer BBQ: Recipes with Seasonal Seafood


As the temperature rises and the sun stays out longer, it’s the perfect time to fire up the grill and indulge in some delicious seafood BBQ recipes. Summer brings a bounty of fresh, seasonal seafood that’s just waiting to be transformed into mouthwatering dishes. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ or simply craving some ocean-inspired flavors, these recipes will elevate your summer gatherings to a whole new level. So, let’s dive into the world of Summer BBQ with a focus on seasonal seafood!

Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked skewers.
  5. Grill the shrimp skewers for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they turn pink and opaque.
  6. Serve the grilled lemon garlic shrimp skewers hot, garnished with fresh parsley and lemon wedges.

Smoky Grilled Salmon Steaks

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon steaks
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and black pepper.
  2. Place the salmon steaks in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Let them marinate for 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  4. Remove the salmon steaks from the marinade and place them on the grill.
  5. Grill the salmon for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Garnish the smoky grilled salmon steaks with fresh dill before serving.

Grilled Lobster Tails with Herb Butter

Ingredients:

  • 4 lobster tails, thawed if frozen
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Using kitchen shears, cut the top of the lobster tails down the center to expose the meat.
  2. In a bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, parsley, chives, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the lobster tails with the herb butter mixture.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Place the lobster tails on the grill, shell side down, and grill for 5-6 minutes.
  6. Turn the lobster tails over and continue grilling for another 4-5 minutes, or until the meat is opaque and slightly charred.
  7. Serve the grilled lobster tails with additional herb butter on the side.

Grilled Garlic Butter Scallops

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh scallops
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Pat the scallops dry with paper towels and season with salt and pepper.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and lemon juice.
  3. Brush the scallops with the garlic butter mixture.
  4. Preheat the grill to high heat.
  5. Place the scallops on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they develop grill marks and are opaque in the center.
  6. Transfer the grilled garlic butter scallops to a serving platter and drizzle with any remaining butter mixture.

Conclusion

With these tantalizing recipes, you can turn your Summer BBQ into a seafood extravaganza. From succulent shrimp skewers to smoky grilled salmon, and from luscious lobster tails to buttery garlic scallops, there’s something for every seafood lover. Make the most of the season’s fresh catch and impress your guests with these irresistible dishes that celebrate the flavors of summer.
