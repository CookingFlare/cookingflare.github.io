Tasty and Easy-to-Make Chicken Recipes for Beginners


If you’re a beginner in the kitchen and looking for some delicious and straightforward chicken recipes, you’ve come to the right place! Chicken is a versatile protein that can be prepared in countless ways, making it perfect for those who are just starting their culinary journey. In this blog post, we’ll explore three mouthwatering chicken recipes that are not only tasty but also easy to make. So, let’s get cooking!

1. Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, approximately 4-5 pounds
  • 2 lemons
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Rinse the chicken and pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Rub the chicken with olive oil, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  5. Squeeze the juice of one lemon over the chicken, inside and out.
  6. Sprinkle the herb mixture over the chicken, making sure to coat both sides.
  7. Cut the second lemon into slices and place them inside the chicken cavity.
  8. Place the chicken on a baking sheet or in a roasting pan.
  9. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  10. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before carving.

Serve this delicious Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken with your favorite roasted vegetables and enjoy the flavorful and tender meat.

2. Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • ½ cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat the olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the skillet to release any browned bits.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream and grated parmesan cheese until well combined. Simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  7. Return the chicken breasts to the skillet and spoon the sauce over them.
  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the chicken to absorb the flavors.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken with pasta or steamed vegetables for a delightful meal.

3. Baked Honey Mustard Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • ¼ cup of Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken thighs on the prepared baking sheet and brush the honey mustard mixture over them, ensuring they are well coated.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  5. Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serve this Baked Honey Mustard Chicken with a side of roasted potatoes and a fresh salad for a delightful and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

Cooking delicious chicken recipes doesn’t have to be complicated, especially for beginners. With these three easy-to-make recipes, you can impress your family and friends with your culinary skills. From the zesty flavors of Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken to the creamy goodness of Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken and the sweet tanginess of Baked Honey Mustard Chicken, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So, put on your apron, grab your ingredients, and let’s get cooking!
