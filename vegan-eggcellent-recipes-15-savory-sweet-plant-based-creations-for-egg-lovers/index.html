Vegan 'Egg'cellent Recipes: 15 Savory & Sweet Plant-Based Creations for Egg Lovers


Looking for delicious vegan recipes that don’t skimp on the flavor? Dive into our egg-cellent collection of savory and sweet plant-based dishes, guaranteed to satisfy even the most dedicated egg lovers.

  1. Vegan Scramble

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp black salt (Kala namak)
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Drain the tofu and crumble it into a mixing bowl.

  2. Add the nutritional yeast, turmeric, black salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and mix thoroughly.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the bell pepper and onion, cook until softened.

  4. Add the tofu mixture to the pan, stirring occasionally for about 10 minutes. Season with black pepper and salt. Enjoy your vegan scramble on toast, in a burrito, or however you prefer!

  5. Chickpea Flour Omelette (Eggless Omelette)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp black salt
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp nutritional yeast

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the chickpea flour and water until smooth.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour the batter into the pan, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  3. Add the turmeric, black salt, onion powder, garlic powder, and nutritional yeast on top of the batter.

  4. Cook for about 3 minutes, then flip the omelette if necessary.

  5. Cook for another 2 minutes on the other side. Fold and serve!

  6. Vegan Frittata

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu
  • 1/3 cup vegan cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp fresh herbs (chives, parsley, or basil)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes

Instructions:

  1. Drain and press the tofu, then crumble it into a mixing bowl.

  2. Add the vegan cream cheese, fresh herbs, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  3. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the bell pepper, onion, and sauté until softened.

  4. Add the tofu mixture to the pan, spreading it evenly.

  5. Place the tomatoes on top, and cover with a lid to let it steam for about 20 minutes until the frittata is set.

  6. Serve hot, sliced like a pizza!

  7. Vegan Fried “Eggs”

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups vegan milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Drain and press the tofu to remove excess water. Cut into 1-inch thick slices.

  2. In a shallow dish, mix together the flour and almond milk. Season with salt and pepper.

  3. In another shallow dish, place the panko breadcrumbs.

  4. Dip each tofu slice into the milk mixture, then coat with the breadcrumbs.

  5. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep frying pan to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (180 degrees Celsius).

  6. Fry the tofu slices for about 3 minutes on each side, until golden brown. Drain on a wire rack or paper towels.

  7. Serve with your favorite accompaniments, like ketchup, vegan mayo, or on top of greens!

  8. Vegan Mayo

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup raw cashews, soaked for at least 2 hours
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Drain the cashews and place them in a high-speed blender.

  2. Add the vegetable oil, white wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper.

  3. Blend on high speed for about 3 minutes until smooth and creamy.

  4. Transfer the vegan mayo to a jar and refrigerate for at least one hour to allow flavors to develop.

  5. Vegan Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 unbaked vegan pie crust
  • 1 block firm tofu
  • 1/2 cup vegan cream cheese
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme or rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (spinach, mushrooms, tomatoes, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).

  2. Drain and press the tofu, then crumble it into a blender.

  3. Add the vegan cream cheese, fresh thyme or rosemary, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.

  4. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, bell pepper, and sauté until softened.

  5. Add the remaining vegetables and cook for another 5 minutes.

  6. Pour the tofu mixture into the pre-made pie crust, add the cooked vegetables, and mix well.

  7. Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until the quiche is set and lightly browned on top. Cool slightly before slicing and serving.

  8. Vegan Mac and “Cheese”

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups vegan cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp mustard powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black salt
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 oz (225g) elbow macaroni, cooked according to package instructions

Instructions:

  1. In a small pot, whisk together the almond milk and cornstarch until smooth.

  2. Add 1 cup of vegan cheddar cheese, mustard powder, garlic powder, black salt, salt, and pepper. Heat over medium heat until the cheese melts, stirring constantly.

  3. Add the remaining vegan cheddar cheese and stir until melted.

  4. Pour the cheese sauce over the cooked macaroni and stir well, until all of the macaroni is coated.

  5. Vegan Caesar Salad with Homemade Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegan croutons
  • 1 cup chickpea croutons
  • Vegan Caesar Salad Dressing

Vegan Caesar Salad Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup raw cashews, soaked for at least 2 hours
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegan parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Make the vegan Caesar salad dressing by first draining and blending the soaked cashews with the olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and water until smooth and creamy.

  2. Add the vegan parmesan cheese and blend until combined.

  3. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, vegan croutons, and chickpea croutons.

  4. Drizzle the dressing on top and toss well to coat.

  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your hearty, flavor-packed vegan Caesar salad!

  6. Vegan Deviled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 14 oz (400g) extra-firm tofu
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped vegan bacon
  • 1/4 cup chopped pickles
  • 1/4 cup chopped chives

Instructions:

  1. Drain the tofu, then crumble it into a mixing bowl.

  2. Add the vegan mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and white vinegar. Mix well.

  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Stir in the chopped vegan bacon, pickles, and chives.

  5. Scoop the mixture into Deviled egg cups or onto a serving plate, and enjoy!

  6. Vegan Quiche Lorraine

Ingredients:

  • 1 unbaked vegan pie crust
  • 14 oz (400g) mushrooms, chopped
  • 1/2 cup vegan cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme or 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).

  2. In a large pan, heat 1 tablespoon of water over medium heat. Add the chopped mushrooms and sauté until they release their water and it has evaporated, for about 5 minutes.

  3. In a blender, combine the vegan cream cheese, almond milk, cornstarch, fresh thyme or dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.

  4. Pour the cream cheese mixture into the sautéed mushrooms, mix well, and cook for another 3 minutes.

  5. Pour the mushroom and cream cheese mixture into the pre-made pie crust, and bake for 35-40 minutes, until the quiche is golden brown and set.

  6. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

  7. Vegan Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 1 box vegan Ladyfingers
  • 1 cup cold, strong brewed coffee
  • 2 tbsp rum or coffee liqueur, optional
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Vegan Mascarpone:
  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews, soaked for at least 2 hours
  • 1 block firm silken tofu
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee

Instructions:

  1. For the vegan mascarpone, blend all ingredients together, until smooth and creamy.

  2. In a shallow dish, combine the coffee and optional rum or coffee liqueur.

  3. Dip each vegan Ladyfinger on both sides in the coffee mixture, and then arrange in the bottom of a glass dish.

  4. Spread half of the vegan mascarpone mixture over the soaked Ladyfingers.

  5. Repeat with another layer of dipped Ladyfingers, and finish with the remaining vegan mascarpone mixture.

  6. Dust the top with cocoa powder.

  7. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving, but ideally overnight, to allow the flavors to develop.

  8. Vegan Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews, soaked for at least 2 hours
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup cold, unsweetened almond milk
  • Pro tip: For a more egg-like texture, add 1 tbsp cornstarch while soaking the cashews.

Instructions:

  1. Drain and rinse the soaked cashews, then transfer them to a high-speed blender.

  2. Add the cocoa powder, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract.

  3. Pour in the cold almond milk and blend until smooth and creamy.

  4. For a firmer texture, chill the mousse in the refrigerator for at least an hour before serving.

  5. Vegan Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 unbaked vegan pie crust
  • Vegan Lemon Filling:
  • 1/2 cup tahini, well-stirred
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup water
  • Vegan Meringue:
  • 2/3 cup aquafaba (the liquid from a can of chickpeas)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

Instructions:

  1. Make the vegan lemon filling by combining the tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, cornstarch, and water in a saucepan.

  2. Whisk the mixture continually over medium heat, until it thickens and boils. Take off the heat and let it cool.

  3. Make the vegan meringue by whipping the aquafaba in a stand mixer or using a hand mixer until foamy.

  4. Gradually whisk in the sugar, one tablespoon at a time, making sure each addition is fully incorporated before adding the next.

  5. Continue whipping until stiff peaks form.

  6. Pour the lemon filling into the pre-made pie crust, and spread the vegan meringue on top.

  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes at 350 degrees Fahrenheit (180 degrees Celsius), until the meringue is lightly browned.

  8. Allow the pie to cool before serving.

  9. Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup hazelnuts, soaked for 4 hours then roasted
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter or coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup coconut sugar
  • 2 flax eggs (2 tbsp ground flaxseed mixed with 6 tbsp water)
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (180 degrees Celsius). Grease and flour a 9-inch cake pan.

  2. Roast and then skin the hazelnuts, then blend them into a meal in a food processor.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, add the hazelnut meal, all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.

  4. In another bowl, whisk together the melted vegan butter or coconut oil, coconut sugar, flax eggs, almond milk, and vanilla extract.

  5. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients, and mix until smooth.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan, and bake for 35-40 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.

  7. Allow the cake to cool before slicing and serving.

  8. Vegan Baked Apples with Cinnamon-Streusel Topping

Ingredients:

  • 4 large apples (Gala,Fuji, or Honeycrisp)
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup coconut sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup orange juice or water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).
  2. Core the apples, leaving a hole large enough to hold approximately 1/4 cup of filling.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the melted vegan butter, all-purpose flour, rolled oats, coconut sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Stuff each apple with the cinnamon-streusel mixture.
  5. Pour the orange juice or water into a baking dish, and place the stuffed apples inside.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes, until the apples are soft and the topping is golden brown.
