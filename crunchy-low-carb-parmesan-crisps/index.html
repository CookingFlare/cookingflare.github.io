Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Crisps


If you’re looking for a simple and delicious snack that’s packed with flavor and low in carbs, then these Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Crisps are the perfect option for you. With just a few ingredients and a quick baking process, you’ll have a crispy and satisfying snack that will satisfy your cravings without derailing your healthy eating goals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, dried basil, dried oregano, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure that the spices are evenly distributed throughout the cheese.

  3. Take small spoonfuls of the Parmesan mixture and drop them onto the prepared baking sheet. Use the back of the spoon to spread and flatten the cheese into thin circles. Leave some space between each circle as the cheese will spread slightly during baking.

  4. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 5-7 minutes, or until the edges of the crisps are golden brown and the centers are crisp.

  5. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the crisps cool completely before removing them from the parchment paper. This will allow them to harden and become even crunchier.

  6. Once cooled, carefully lift each Parmesan crisp from the parchment paper and transfer them to a plate or airtight container for storage. They can be enjoyed immediately or kept for future snacking.

Serving Suggestions

These Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Crisps are incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed on their own or paired with other foods. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve them as an appetizer at your next gathering, accompanied by a selection of dips such as guacamole, salsa, or hummus.
  • Crumble them over salads to add a burst of flavor and texture.
  • Use them as a gluten-free alternative to traditional breadcrumbs in recipes such as chicken parmesan or eggplant parmigiana.
  • Enjoy them as a guilt-free snack during movie nights or when you’re craving something crunchy.

Nutritional Information

These Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Crisps are not only tasty but also a healthier alternative to traditional potato chips or processed snacks. Here’s the approximate nutritional information per serving (4 crisps):

  • Calories: 80
  • Fat: 5g
  • Carbohydrates: 1g
  • Protein: 8g

Please note that these values may vary depending on the brand of Parmesan cheese used and the size of the crisps.

Conclusion

Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Crisps are a simple, delicious, and low-carb snack that will satisfy your cravings without compromising your healthy eating goals. With just a handful of ingredients and a quick baking process, you can enjoy these crispy delights in no time. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a guilt-free snack, these Parmesan crisps are sure to impress. Give them a try and experience the perfect combination of crunch and flavor!
