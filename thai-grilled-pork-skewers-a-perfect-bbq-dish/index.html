Thai Grilled Pork Skewers: A Perfect BBQ Dish


Are you ready to take your BBQ game to the next level? Look no further than these mouthwatering Thai Grilled Pork Skewers. Bursting with flavors and grilled to perfection, these skewers are guaranteed to be a hit at your next backyard gathering. So fire up the grill and get ready to tantalize your taste buds with this delicious Thai-inspired dish.

Ingredients

To make these succulent Thai Grilled Pork Skewers, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds of pork shoulder, cut into thin strips
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the soy sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, vegetable oil, minced garlic, chopped lemongrass, fresh cilantro, ground coriander, ground cumin, and ground black pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.

  2. Add the pork strips to the marinade and toss until they are fully coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for the best flavor.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using a charcoal grill, make sure the coals are evenly spread and glowing red.

  4. Thread the marinated pork strips onto the soaked bamboo skewers, making sure to leave some space between each piece to ensure even cooking.

  5. Grill the skewers over direct heat for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until the pork is cooked through and has a nice charred exterior. Be sure to turn the skewers occasionally to prevent burning.

  6. Once the pork is cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy meat.

  7. Serve the Thai Grilled Pork Skewers hot off the grill, garnished with some fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime juice. These skewers pair perfectly with steamed rice, a side of tangy Thai dipping sauce, and some grilled vegetables.

Tips and Tricks

  • If you can’t find pork shoulder, you can also use pork tenderloin or boneless pork chops for this recipe. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly, as they may cook faster.

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding some chopped Thai bird’s eye chili or red pepper flakes to the marinade. This will give your skewers a spicy kick that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

  • Soaking the bamboo skewers in water before grilling helps prevent them from burning or charring too quickly. This ensures that your skewers cook evenly and that the pork stays securely in place.

  • If you’re looking to add some variety to your BBQ spread, try experimenting with different types of marinades. You can swap out the pork for chicken, beef, or even tofu and use a variety of Thai-inspired marinades to create a diverse and exciting skewer lineup.

So there you have it – a simple and delicious recipe for Thai Grilled Pork Skewers that is sure to impress your family and friends. With its bold flavors and tender meat, this dish will transport you straight to the vibrant streets of Thailand. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and get ready to savor the taste of summer with this perfect BBQ dish. Enjoy!
