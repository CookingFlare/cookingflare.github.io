Easy Vegan and Vegetarian Soups for Cold Weather


As the weather turns colder, there’s nothing more comforting than a warm bowl of soup. Whether you’re a vegan, vegetarian, or simply looking to add more plant-based meals to your diet, soups are an excellent way to nourish your body while warming up from the inside out. In this post, we’ll explore some delicious and easy vegan and vegetarian soups perfect for cold weather.

Why Soup is Perfect for Cold Weather

Soup is the ideal dish to enjoy during the chillier months for several reasons. It’s not only warming but also incredibly versatile. Whether you want something hearty and filling or light and refreshing, soups can meet any craving. Plus, they’re a great way to use seasonal produce, ensuring you’re eating fresh ingredients that are both nutritious and comforting.

When making vegan and vegetarian soups, the focus is often on plant-based ingredients like legumes, grains, vegetables, and aromatic herbs and spices. These ingredients can create rich, savory broths that are both satisfying and easy to prepare.

Benefits of Vegan and Vegetarian Soups

  1. Nutrient-Rich: Vegan and vegetarian soups are often packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, as they focus on plant-based ingredients.
  2. Low in Calories: Many plant-based soups are low in calories, making them an excellent choice for those looking to eat light without sacrificing flavor.
  3. High in Fiber: The abundance of vegetables, beans, and grains in these soups makes them high in fiber, which is essential for digestion and keeping you full.
  4. Customizable: You can easily adjust the flavors, textures, and ingredients in your soups, making them as simple or complex as you desire.

Now, let’s dive into some of the best and easiest vegan and vegetarian soup recipes for cold weather.

1. Classic Vegan Lentil Soup

Lentils are a staple in plant-based cooking due to their high protein content and versatility. This vegan lentil soup is hearty, nutritious, and easy to prepare. It’s a filling meal that’s perfect for a cold day, packed with warming spices and earthy flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the carrots and celery, cooking for another 3-4 minutes.
  3. Stir in the cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Add the lentils, tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let the soup simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

This vegan lentil soup is an all-time favorite that offers warmth and comfort with every spoonful.

2. Creamy Vegan Tomato Soup

There’s something about tomato soup that just hits the spot, especially when paired with a grilled cheese sandwich. This creamy vegan tomato soup swaps out dairy for coconut milk, providing a rich, smooth texture without the need for heavy cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes (14.5 oz each)
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing for 5-7 minutes, until softened and fragrant.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, coconut milk, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Let the soup cook for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Use an immersion blender or regular blender to puree the soup until smooth and creamy.
  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.

This creamy vegan tomato soup is a simple yet satisfying choice for those chilly evenings. It’s comforting and full of flavor, making it a perfect cold-weather meal.

3. Butternut Squash Soup

Butternut squash is a classic fall vegetable that pairs wonderfully with warming spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. This vegan butternut squash soup is both velvety and aromatic, creating a cozy bowl of soup that will make you feel like you’re wrapped in a blanket.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (optional for extra creaminess)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh thyme for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the butternut squash cubes on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 25-30 minutes, or until the squash is tender and lightly caramelized.
  2. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing for about 5 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the ginger, cinnamon, and nutmeg, letting the spices bloom for another minute.
  4. Add the roasted butternut squash and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes to blend the flavors.
  5. Use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  6. If you want a creamier texture, add coconut milk and stir to combine.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve garnished with fresh thyme.

This butternut squash soup is the perfect way to embrace the flavors of the season while staying cozy and nourished.

4. Vegan Mushroom Soup

Mushrooms are a great addition to soups, providing an earthy depth of flavor. This vegan mushroom soup is rich, savory, and incredibly comforting. It’s an excellent way to showcase the natural umami flavor of mushrooms.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound mixed mushrooms (cremini, shiitake, and button), sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme (or 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream (or cashew cream)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and sauté for about 10 minutes until they release their moisture and become golden brown.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, cooking for another 5-7 minutes until softened and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the thyme, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Add the coconut cream or cashew cream to the pot and stir to combine. Continue cooking for another 5 minutes to heat through.
  5. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup to your desired consistency. For a chunkier texture, blend half of the soup and leave the rest intact.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve with fresh parsley.

This vegan mushroom soup is a flavorful, earthy dish that is both nourishing and satisfying.

5. Spicy Vegan Black Bean Soup

For those who like a little heat, a spicy vegan black bean soup is a great option. This recipe packs a punch with spices like cumin, chili powder, and smoked paprika, all while maintaining the rich, savory flavor of black beans.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for extra heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, cooking for 5-7 minutes until softened.
  2. Stir in the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper. Let the spices cook for another minute to bloom.
  3. Add the black beans, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. For a smoother texture, blend part of the soup using an immersion blender.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

This spicy black bean soup is perfect for anyone who loves bold flavors and a little heat in their meals.

Conclusion

As the cold weather sets in, these easy vegan and vegetarian soups are the perfect way to stay cozy and nourished. They offer a variety of flavors, from hearty lentil soups to creamy tomato-based options, each one packed with nutrients and delicious ingredients. Whether you’re looking for a light and simple meal or something more filling, these soups are sure to satisfy. So, grab your favorite pot, get cooking, and enjoy the warmth and comfort these soups bring to your home during the cold months.
