Fluffy Vanilla Cupcakes with Fresh Berries: A Light and Summery Dessert


Welcome to my delightful recipe for Fluffy Vanilla Cupcakes with Fresh Berries! This light and summery dessert is perfect for those warm, sunny days when you’re craving something sweet but not overly heavy. The combination of fluffy vanilla cupcakes and vibrant fresh berries creates a burst of flavors that will leave you wanting more. So, let’s get right into it and whip up these delectable treats!

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk

For the fresh berry topping:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries

For the vanilla frosting:

  • 1 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense cupcakes.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  8. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and allow them to cool in the tin for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the vanilla frosting. In a medium-sized bowl, cream together the powdered sugar, softened butter, vanilla extract, and milk until smooth and creamy.

  10. Once the cupcakes have cooled, generously frost each one with the vanilla frosting using a piping bag or a butter knife.

  11. Now, it’s time for the grand finale! Decorate your fluffy vanilla cupcakes with a beautiful assortment of fresh berries. Place a few slices of strawberries, a sprinkle of blueberries, and a raspberry on top of each cupcake.

  12. Serve these delightful treats immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve. They are best enjoyed within a day or two to ensure the ultimate freshness.

Conclusion

There you have it, a recipe for Fluffy Vanilla Cupcakes with Fresh Berries that will surely satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your friends and family. These light, fluffy cupcakes paired with the vibrant burst of fresh berries are the perfect summertime dessert. So, go ahead and indulge in this delightful treat and let the flavors transport you to a sunny paradise. Enjoy!
