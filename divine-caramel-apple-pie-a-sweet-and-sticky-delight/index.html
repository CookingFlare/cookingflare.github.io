Divine Caramel Apple Pie: A Sweet and Sticky Delight


If you’re a fan of classic apple pie with a sweet, sticky twist, then this divine caramel apple pie recipe is just what you need. This delightful dessert combines the comforting flavors of warm, tender apples with the rich, buttery sweetness of caramel, all nestled in a flaky, golden crust. Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a comforting treat, this caramel apple pie is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into cubes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 6-7 large Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup homemade or store-bought caramel sauce

For the Topping:

  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Coarse sugar for sprinkling

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add the chilled butter cubes to the flour mixture.
  3. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Slowly drizzle in the ice water, a little at a time, and mix until the dough just comes together.
  5. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with the granulated sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, flour, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  2. Set the apple mixture aside and let it sit for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 3: Assemble the Pie

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out one of the chilled dough disks on a lightly floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. Gently transfer the rolled-out dough to the pie dish, trimming any excess overhang.
  4. Pour the caramel sauce over the bottom of the pie crust, spreading it evenly.
  5. Spoon the prepared apple filling over the caramel layer.

Step 4: Add the Top Crust

  1. Roll out the second chilled dough disk and place it over the apple filling.
  2. Trim any excess overhang and crimp the edges of the crust to seal.

Step 5: Bake

  1. Brush the top crust with the beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse sugar.
  2. Using a sharp knife, make a few small vents in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  3. Place the pie on a baking sheet and bake for 45-55 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

Step 6: Serve

  1. Allow the pie to cool for at least 1 hour before slicing to allow the filling to set.
  2. Serve slices of this divine caramel apple pie warm, with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream, if desired.

Conclusion

This divine caramel apple pie is a celebration of autumn flavors and the perfect way to indulge in the sweet, sticky goodness of caramel and tender, spiced apples. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of pie-making, this recipe is a wonderful way to create a memorable dessert that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every delightful bite of this sweet and sticky delight. Happy baking!
