Delicious Mini Quiches with Spinach and Feta: Perfect for Brunch


If you’re looking for a delightful dish to elevate your brunch game, look no further than these scrumptious mini quiches with spinach and feta. These little bites are bursting with flavor and are perfect for serving at any brunch gathering. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste. Whether you’re hosting a brunch for friends or simply want to treat yourself to something special, these mini quiches are sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup of ice water
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

Prepare the Crust:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the chilled, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, a little at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overwork the dough. Form the dough into a ball, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Make the Filling:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened and translucent.
  3. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk. Season with salt and pepper.

Assemble the Mini Quiches:

  1. Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface to about 1/8 inch thickness. Use a round cookie cutter or a glass to cut out circles that will fit into the cups of a mini muffin tin.
  2. Gently press the dough circles into the greased mini muffin tin, making sure to form a small crust at the bottom.
  3. Divide the sautéed spinach and onion mixture among the dough-lined muffin cups. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top.
  4. Carefully pour the egg and milk mixture over the spinach and feta in each muffin cup, filling almost to the top.

Bake and Serve:

  1. Place the mini quiches in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.
  2. Remove the mini quiches from the oven and let them cool in the muffin tin for a few minutes. Use a butter knife to gently loosen the quiches from the tin and transfer them to a serving platter.
  3. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm or at room temperature.

These mini quiches with spinach and feta are a delightful addition to any brunch spread. They are perfect for serving alongside a fresh fruit salad, crispy bacon, or a colorful array of other brunch favorites. The combination of flaky crust, savory filling, and tangy feta cheese makes these little quiches utterly irresistible.

So, next time you’re planning a brunch get-together or simply want to enjoy a special weekend treat, give these mini quiches a try. They are sure to become a new brunch favorite!
