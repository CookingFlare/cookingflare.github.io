Spicy and Fragrant: Korean Spicy Pork Belly Stir-Fry


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and spicy dishes, then you’re in for a treat with this Korean Spicy Pork Belly Stir-Fry recipe. Bursting with the perfect blend of heat and savory goodness, this dish is a must-try for anyone who loves Korean cuisine or wants to explore new and exciting flavors. The combination of tender pork belly, aromatic spices, and vibrant vegetables makes this stir-fry a standout dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let’s dive into the tantalizing world of Korean cooking.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of pork belly, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, grated
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon of gochugaru (Korean red pepper flakes)
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of toasted sesame seeds
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pork Belly: Start by slicing the pork belly into thin strips. This will help the meat absorb all the wonderful flavors of the marinade and cook quickly in the stir-fry.

  2. Create the Marinade: In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, gochujang, gochugaru, sugar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and sesame oil. Mix the marinade until the sugar has dissolved, and the ingredients are well incorporated.

  3. Marinate the Pork: Place the sliced pork belly in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over the meat, ensuring that each piece is well coated. Cover the dish and let the pork marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse the meat.

  4. Sear the Pork: Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated pork belly and cook until it’s caramelized and slightly crispy on the edges. This step will give the pork a rich, savory flavor that will elevate the entire dish.

  5. Add the Aromatics: Once the pork is cooked to perfection, add the thinly sliced onion to the skillet and stir-fry until it becomes translucent and fragrant. This will infuse the dish with a sweet and savory aroma that will make your mouth water.

  6. Bring It All Together: Next, toss in the chopped green onions and continue to stir-fry for a minute, allowing the flavors to meld together beautifully.

  7. Finish and Serve: Sprinkle the stir-fry with toasted sesame seeds and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper for an extra kick. Serve the Korean Spicy Pork Belly Stir-Fry over steamed rice and garnish with additional green onions for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra spicy kick, adjust the amount of gochugaru according to your heat preference.
  • Use a well-seasoned cast-iron skillet or a non-stick wok to prevent the pork belly from sticking during the cooking process.
  • Pair this dish with traditional Korean side dishes like kimchi and pickled vegetables for a complete and authentic dining experience.

Now that you have this tantalizing recipe in your cooking arsenal, you can impress your loved ones with the bold and vibrant flavors of Korean cuisine. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a delicious homemade meal, this Korean Spicy Pork Belly Stir-Fry is a surefire way to elevate your culinary skills and delight your taste buds. So, roll up your sleeves, embrace the aromatic spices, and savor every mouthful of this spicy and fragrant delight!
