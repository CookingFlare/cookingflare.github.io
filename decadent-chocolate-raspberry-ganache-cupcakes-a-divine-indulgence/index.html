Decadent Chocolate Raspberry Ganache Cupcakes: A Divine Indulgence


Indulge your taste buds with our heavenly Decadent Chocolate Raspberry Ganache Cupcakes. These rich and moist chocolate cupcakes are filled with a luscious raspberry ganache and topped with a smooth chocolate ganache frosting. With each bite, you’ll experience a tantalizing blend of flavors that will transport you to dessert paradise.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup boiling water
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup packed light brown sugar

For the Raspberry Ganache Filling:

  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 6 ounces dark chocolate, chopped

For the Chocolate Ganache Frosting:

  • 8 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder. Set aside.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the boiling water and cocoa powder, mixing until smooth. Then, add the buttermilk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract. Whisk until well combined.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the granulated sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Gradually add the wet ingredients, mixing well after each addition.

  5. Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to dense cupcakes.

  6. Fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with the batter. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely before filling and frosting.

  7. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the raspberry ganache filling. In a small saucepan, combine the raspberries, granulated sugar, and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the raspberries break down and release their juices.

  8. Remove the saucepan from heat and strain the raspberry mixture through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the seeds. Return the strained raspberry puree to the saucepan and add the heavy cream. Heat over medium heat until it begins to simmer.

  9. Place the chopped dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl and pour the hot raspberry cream mixture over it. Let it sit for a minute to allow the chocolate to melt, then stir until smooth and well combined. Set aside to cool slightly.

  10. Once the cupcakes have cooled, use a small knife or cupcake corer to remove a small portion from the center of each cupcake. Fill the holes with the raspberry ganache filling, being careful not to overfill.

  11. To prepare the chocolate ganache frosting, place the chopped semi-sweet chocolate in a heatproof bowl. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to boil. Pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit for a minute. Stir until the chocolate has melted and the mixture is smooth. Add the softened butter and stir until well incorporated.

  12. Allow the ganache frosting to cool for a few minutes, then frost the filled cupcakes using a piping bag or a spoon. You can get as creative as you like with the frosting, making swirls or peaks to add an elegant touch.

  13. Once frosted, refrigerate the cupcakes for at least 30 minutes to allow the ganache to set.

  14. Serve these Decadent Chocolate Raspberry Ganache Cupcakes at your next special occasion or simply treat yourself to a divine indulgence. The combination of rich chocolate, tangy raspberry, and velvety ganache is bound to leave your taste buds dancing with delight.

Enjoy the heavenly goodness of these cupcakes, and don’t forget to share them with your loved ones. They’ll be thanking you for this divine indulgence!
