Sous Vide Pork Belly: Perfectly Crispy and Tender

4 min · 694 words

If you’re a fan of succulent, melt-in-your-mouth pork belly, then you’re in for a treat. Sous vide cooking is a game-changer when it comes to achieving the perfect texture and flavor, and when it’s applied to pork belly, the results are nothing short of exceptional. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the process of preparing sous vide pork belly that’s both perfectly crispy on the outside and tender on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs (900g) pork belly, skin on
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp Chinese five-spice powder
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Pork Belly

  1. Start by preparing the pork belly. If the skin is not already scored, use a sharp knife to make shallow cuts across the skin, ensuring not to pierce the meat. This will help the skin crisp up during the final cooking stage.

  2. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, honey, Chinese five-spice powder, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. This will be the marinade for the pork belly.

  3. Place the pork belly in a large resealable bag and pour the marinade over the pork, ensuring it’s evenly coated. Seal the bag, removing as much air as possible.

Step 2: Sous Vide Cooking

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 167°F (75°C).

  2. Once the water has reached the desired temperature, carefully lower the sealed bag of pork belly into the water bath. Make sure the pork is fully submerged.

  3. Cook the pork belly in the water bath for 12 hours. This extended cooking time will allow the fat to render and the meat to become incredibly tender.

Step 3: Finishing the Pork Belly

  1. After 12 hours, carefully remove the bag from the water bath and take out the pork belly. Discard the marinade.

  2. Place the pork belly on a wire rack set over a baking sheet. Pat the skin dry with paper towels.

  3. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). Once the oven is hot, place the pork belly in the oven and roast for 30-40 minutes, or until the skin is crispy and golden brown.

  4. Once the skin has reached the desired crispiness, remove the pork belly from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into serving portions.

Serving Suggestions

This sous vide pork belly pairs beautifully with a variety of side dishes. Consider serving it with steamed jasmine rice, stir-fried vegetables, or a fresh cucumber salad for a balanced and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

Sous vide pork belly is a luxurious and indulgent dish that’s sure to impress. The slow cooking process ensures that the meat is tender and flavorful, while the final crisping step adds a delightful crunch to every bite. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this sous vide pork belly recipe is guaranteed to elevate any dining experience. So, fire up your sous vide machine, and get ready to savor the irresistible combination of perfectly crispy and tender pork belly.
