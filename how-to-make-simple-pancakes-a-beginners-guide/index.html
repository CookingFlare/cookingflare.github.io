How to Make Simple Pancakes: A Beginner's Guide


Pancakes are a classic breakfast favorite that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. Whether you’re a busy parent looking for a quick and easy breakfast option, or a college student seeking a comforting meal after a late night, pancakes are a great choice. In this guide, we’ll show you how to make simple pancakes that are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters.

Ingredients

To make simple pancakes, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • Flavorings such as vanilla extract or chocolate chips (optional)

Equipment

You’ll also need the following equipment to make simple pancakes:

  • A medium-sized bowl
  • A whisk
  • A measuring cup
  • A non-stick skillet or griddle
  • A spatula

Instructions

Step 1: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt until well combined. This will help to remove any lumps and ensure that the ingredients are evenly mixed.

Step 2: Mix the Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, milk, egg, and melted butter until well combined. This will help to create a smooth batter.

Step 3: Combine the Wet and Dry Ingredients

Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. The batter should still be slightly lumpy.

Step 4: Add Flavorings (Optional)

If you want to add some extra flavor to your pancakes, now is the time to do it. You can add a teaspoon of vanilla extract or a handful of chocolate chips to the batter.

Step 5: Cook the Pancakes

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Grease the pan with a small amount of butter or cooking spray. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, scoop the batter onto the pan. Cook the pancake for 2-3 minutes, until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges start to dry. Flip the pancake and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until golden brown.

Step 6: Serve

Serve the pancakes hot with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, butter, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.

Tips and Variations

  • Use fresh ingredients: Fresh eggs, milk, and butter will give your pancakes a richer flavor and better texture.
  • Don’t overmix the batter: Overmixing can result in tough, dense pancakes. Stop mixing as soon as the ingredients are just combined.
  • Experiment with flavors: Try adding different flavorings, such as cinnamon, nutmeg, or lemon zest, to create unique and delicious pancake flavors.
  • Make ahead: You can make the batter ahead of time and store it in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. Simply cook the pancakes when you’re ready.

Conclusion

Making simple pancakes is a breeze, and with these easy-to-follow steps, you’ll be enjoying a stack of fluffy, delicious pancakes in no time. Whether you’re a beginner in the kitchen or a seasoned pro, this guide has everything you need to get started. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the perfect breakfast!
