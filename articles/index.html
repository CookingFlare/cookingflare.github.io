Mouthwatering Slow Cooker Chicken and Black Bean Chili Recipe

Are you looking for a hearty and flavorful meal that practically cooks itself? Look no further than this mouthwatering slow cooker chicken and black bean chili recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and a perfect blend of spices, this chili is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Ingredients 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes 1 cup chicken broth 1 onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 bell pepper, diced 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced 2 teaspoons chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon oregano Salt and pepper to taste Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado, fresh cilantro Instructions Begin by preparing the ingredients. Rinse and drain the black beans, dice the onion, bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper, and mince the garlic. Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker. Add the black beans, diced tomatoes, chicken broth, diced onion, minced garlic, bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper to the slow cooker. Sprinkle the chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper over the ingredients in the slow cooker. Gently stir the ingredients to combine the spices and distribute the vegetables evenly. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, or until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork. Once the chicken is fully cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker and stir it into the chili. Taste the chili and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Serve the slow cooker chicken and black bean chili hot, and garnish with your choice of shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado, and fresh cilantro. Why Slow Cooker Chicken and Black Bean Chili?
