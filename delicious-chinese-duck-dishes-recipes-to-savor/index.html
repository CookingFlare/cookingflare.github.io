Delicious Chinese Duck Dishes: Recipes to Savor


Duck dishes have always held a special place in Chinese cuisine. The succulent meat, crispy skin, and rich flavors make them a favorite among food lovers. In this article, we will explore some delicious Chinese duck recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. From classic dishes to modern twists, these recipes are guaranteed to impress your friends and family.

1. Peking Duck: A Classic Delight

Peking Duck is perhaps the most famous Chinese duck dish, known for its crispy skin and tender meat. Here’s how you can recreate this classic delight at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole duck
  • 1 tablespoon five-spice powder
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • Pancakes, cucumber, and scallions for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Rinse the duck thoroughly and pat it dry.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the five-spice powder, honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and hoisin sauce.

  4. Rub the duck with the marinade, making sure to coat it evenly.

  5. Place the duck on a rack in a roasting pan and roast for about 2 hours, or until the skin is crispy and the meat is tender.

  6. While the duck is roasting, prepare the pancakes according to the package instructions.

  7. Once the duck is ready, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.

  8. Slice the duck and serve it with the pancakes, cucumber, and scallions.

  9. To eat, spread some hoisin sauce on a pancake, add a slice of duck, cucumber, and scallions, then roll it up. Enjoy!

2. Crispy Duck Salad: A Refreshing Twist

Looking for a lighter and refreshing way to enjoy duck? Try this crispy duck salad that combines the rich flavors of duck with the freshness of greens.

Ingredients:

  • 2 duck breasts
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Score the duck breasts, season them with salt and pepper, and place them skin side down in the skillet.

  3. Cook the duck for about 6-8 minutes, or until the skin is crispy and browned. Flip the duck and cook for another 4-5 minutes, or until cooked to your desired doneness.

  4. Remove the duck from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice it thinly.

  5. In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, cilantro, and mint.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and honey.

  7. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to coat.

  8. Arrange the sliced duck on top of the salad and serve immediately.

3. Duck Fried Rice: A Flavorful Delight

Turn leftover duck into a delicious and satisfying meal with this flavorful duck fried rice recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked white rice, chilled
  • 1 cup shredded duck meat
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots, thawed
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.

  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until fragrant and slightly softened.

  3. Push the onion and garlic to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.

  4. Add the shredded duck meat, peas and carrots to the skillet and stir-fry for a few minutes.

  5. Add the chilled rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps with a spatula.

  6. Drizzle the soy sauce and oyster sauce over the rice, and stir-fry until everything is well combined and heated through.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the chopped green onions.

  8. Serve the duck fried rice hot and enjoy!

Duck dishes are a true delight in Chinese cuisine. Whether you prefer the classic Peking Duck, a refreshing salad, or a flavorful fried rice, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So go ahead, savor the flavors of Chinese duck dishes in the comfort of your own home and impress your loved ones with these delicious creations!
