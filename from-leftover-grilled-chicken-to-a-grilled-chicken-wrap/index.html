From Leftover Grilled Chicken to a Grilled Chicken Wrap


Leftover grilled chicken is not just a meal gone by; it’s a golden opportunity to create something fresh, delicious, and exciting! If you find yourself with extra grilled chicken from a barbecue or a weeknight dinner, don’t let it go to waste. Transform those leftovers into a mouthwatering grilled chicken wrap that’s perfect for lunch, dinner, or even a snack. This guide will take you through the process step-by-step, ensuring your wrap is bursting with flavor and nutrients.

Why Grilled Chicken Wraps?

Grilled chicken wraps are not only an easy way to use up leftovers, but they also offer a multitude of benefits:

  1. Quick and Easy: With pre-cooked grilled chicken, you can whip up a satisfying meal in just a few minutes.
  2. Healthy: Packed with protein, vegetables, and whole grains, these wraps can be tailored to meet your dietary preferences.
  3. Versatile: You can customize your wrap with various sauces, veggies, and toppings to suit your taste.
  4. Portable: They make for a perfect on-the-go meal that you can enjoy at work, school, or picnics.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To create the ultimate grilled chicken wrap, gather the following ingredients:

For the Wrap

  • 2 cups of leftover grilled chicken: Shredded or diced, depending on your preference.
  • 4 large tortillas: Whole wheat or spinach tortillas are great for added nutrition.
  • 1 cup of fresh greens: Romaine lettuce or spinach work well.
  • 1 medium tomato: Diced or sliced.
  • 1/2 cucumber: Sliced thinly or diced.
  • 1/2 bell pepper: Sliced (any color you prefer).
  • 1/4 red onion: Thinly sliced.
  • 1 avocado: Sliced or mashed.
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese: Cheddar, mozzarella, or a cheese blend.

For the Sauce

  • 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt: A healthier alternative to sour cream.
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise: Optional for creaminess.
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard: Adds a tangy flavor.
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice: Freshly squeezed for brightness.
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder: For an added kick.
  • Salt and pepper to taste: Enhances the overall flavor.

Optional Add-ins

  • Hot sauce: For those who like it spicy.
  • Fresh herbs: Cilantro or parsley for extra freshness.
  • Chickpeas or black beans: For added protein and fiber.

How to Prepare Your Grilled Chicken Wrap

Step 1: Prepare the Sauce

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise (if using), Dijon mustard, lime juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. If you like a bit of heat, add a few drops of hot sauce.

Step 2: Prepare the Vegetables

  1. Wash and slice your vegetables: lettuce, tomato, cucumber, bell pepper, and red onion.
  2. If you’re using avocado, slice it just before assembling the wrap to keep it fresh.

Step 3: Heat the Tortillas

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet for about 30 seconds on each side, just until warm and pliable. This step makes rolling the wrap easier and enhances the tortilla’s flavor.
  3. Repeat this process for the remaining tortillas.

Step 4: Assemble the Wrap

  1. Lay a warm tortilla flat on a clean surface or a cutting board.
  2. Spread a generous tablespoon or two of the prepared sauce across the center of the tortilla.
  3. Layer the shredded grilled chicken on top of the sauce, followed by the fresh greens, diced tomato, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, and avocado.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the top.

Step 5: Roll It Up

  1. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward over the filling.
  2. Starting from the bottom, roll the tortilla tightly from the edge closest to you. Use your fingers to tuck the filling in as you roll.
  3. Continue rolling until the wrap is fully rolled up. If you like, you can secure it with a toothpick or wrap it in foil to hold it together.

Step 6: Slice and Serve

  1. For easier eating, slice the wrap in half diagonally.
  2. Serve immediately with a side of chips, a fresh salad, or your favorite dipping sauce.

Storing Leftover Wraps

If you have any leftover wraps, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to two days. To reheat, place the wrap in a skillet over medium heat for a few minutes on each side or use a microwave for about 30 seconds.

Tips for the Perfect Grilled Chicken Wrap

  • Mix and Match: Feel free to use any vegetables or sauces you have on hand. Grilled zucchini, roasted red peppers, or even coleslaw can be delicious additions.
  • Spice It Up: Add spices like chili powder, cumin, or Italian seasoning to the chicken before grilling for a flavor boost.
  • Meal Prep: Prepare several wraps in advance for easy grab-and-go lunches throughout the week. Just wrap them tightly in plastic wrap and store them in the fridge.
  • Grill the Chicken: If you don’t have leftover grilled chicken, you can easily grill fresh chicken breasts. Season them with your favorite spices, grill until cooked through, and let them rest before slicing or shredding.

Creative Variations

Mediterranean Grilled Chicken Wrap

  • Substitute the sauce with tzatziki sauce.
  • Add kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh dill.
  • Use pita bread instead of tortillas for a different texture.

Southwest Grilled Chicken Wrap

  • Add black beans, corn, and diced jalapeños.
  • Use a chipotle ranch dressing for a smoky flavor.
  • Top with fresh cilantro and avocado slices.

Asian-Inspired Grilled Chicken Wrap

  • Use hoisin sauce or a sesame dressing in place of the yogurt sauce.
  • Add shredded carrots, bean sprouts, and sliced green onions.
  • Serve with a side of soy sauce or spicy Sriracha.

Conclusion

Transforming leftover grilled chicken into a delicious grilled chicken wrap is a simple, fulfilling process that allows you to be creative in the kitchen. With endless combinations of ingredients and sauces, you can enjoy a different flavor profile every time you make it. Not only is it a quick meal option, but it also helps reduce food waste while keeping your palate excited. So, the next time you fire up the grill, plan for leftovers and get ready to wrap up something delicious! Enjoy your culinary adventure and savor every bite of your homemade grilled chicken wrap!
