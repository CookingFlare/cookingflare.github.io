Irresistible Raspberry Swirl Bread: Sweet and Tart


If you’re a fan of sweet and tart flavors, then this recipe for Irresistible Raspberry Swirl Bread is right up your alley. This bread is a perfect combination of soft, fluffy dough and a tangy raspberry filling that will leave you craving for more. With its vibrant colors and delightful taste, it’s sure to be a hit at any breakfast or brunch gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined.
  3. Add the softened butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense bread.
  6. In a small bowl, gently mash the fresh raspberries with a fork until they are broken down but still have some texture.
  7. Pour half of the bread batter into the prepared loaf pan and spread it evenly.
  8. Spoon the mashed raspberries over the batter in the pan, leaving a small border around the edges.
  9. Pour the remaining batter over the raspberries and use a knife or skewer to gently swirl the two together. Don’t overmix, as you want to create a marbled effect.
  10. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  11. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  12. Once the bread has cooled, dust the top with powdered sugar for a finishing touch.

Serving Suggestions

This Irresistible Raspberry Swirl Bread is delicious on its own, but if you want to take it to the next level, here are a few serving suggestions:

  • Serve it warm with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful dessert.
  • Spread a slice with cream cheese or butter for a simple, yet satisfying breakfast.
  • Toast a slice and enjoy it with your favorite cup of tea or coffee for a cozy afternoon treat.

Storage

To keep your Raspberry Swirl Bread fresh, store it in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. You can also freeze individual slices for up to a month, simply thaw them at room temperature or pop them in the toaster for a quick and easy snack.

So why wait? Give this Irresistible Raspberry Swirl Bread recipe a try and indulge in the sweet and tart flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy. Happy baking!
