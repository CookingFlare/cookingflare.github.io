Instant Pot Breakfast Recipes: Start Your Day Right


Breakfast is often regarded as the most important meal of the day, but with hectic mornings, many of us struggle to find time to prepare a nourishing meal. Fortunately, the Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we approach breakfast, making it easier than ever to whip up delicious, hearty, and healthy meals in no time. Whether you’re rushing out the door or looking for a weekend brunch option, these Instant Pot breakfast recipes will help you start your day on the right note.

Why Choose the Instant Pot for Breakfast?

The Instant Pot has gained immense popularity over the years due to its versatility, speed, and ability to simplify complex cooking processes. When it comes to breakfast, the Instant Pot is especially helpful because it allows you to prepare meals quickly without compromising on flavor or nutritional value.

  1. Time-Saving: The Instant Pot cuts down cooking time significantly. What would normally take hours in the oven or on the stove can be cooked in a fraction of the time. It’s perfect for busy mornings or lazy weekends when you want a filling breakfast without the long wait.

  2. Minimal Effort: With the Instant Pot, you simply add your ingredients, select the appropriate settings, and let it do the rest. No need for constant stirring or monitoring.

  3. Nutrient Preservation: Pressure cooking helps retain nutrients better than traditional cooking methods, allowing you to serve a wholesome breakfast.

  4. Hands-Off Cooking: You can set your Instant Pot, walk away, and come back to a perfectly cooked breakfast. This is ideal for those with limited time but still want a fresh, homemade meal.

Now, let’s dive into some of the best Instant Pot breakfast recipes that will make your mornings brighter and more delicious.

1. Instant Pot Steel-Cut Oats

Steel-cut oats are a nutritious and filling breakfast option, but they typically take a while to cook. With the Instant Pot, you can have creamy steel-cut oats ready in just a few minutes, without needing to stand over the stove.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup steel-cut oats
  • 4 cups water or milk (or a combination)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Toppings: fresh berries, nuts, maple syrup, cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Add the steel-cut oats, water (or milk), and salt to the Instant Pot.
  2. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural release of pressure for about 10 minutes, then carefully release the remaining pressure.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract, if desired, and serve with your favorite toppings such as fresh berries, nuts, or a drizzle of maple syrup.

This recipe is incredibly versatile, so you can experiment with different flavors by adding spices like cinnamon or cardamom, or even mix in some dried fruits like raisins or cranberries.

2. Instant Pot Scrambled Eggs

If you’re craving fluffy scrambled eggs without the mess or fuss, the Instant Pot has you covered. This method allows you to make eggs in bulk for a family or meal prep for the week.

Ingredients:

  • 12 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • Optional: shredded cheese, chopped herbs, diced vegetables, or cooked bacon

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the butter or olive oil to the Instant Pot, and set it to the sauté function. Once the butter is melted and the pot is hot, pour the egg mixture into the pot.
  3. Stir constantly to prevent the eggs from sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  4. Once the eggs are cooked to your desired consistency, turn off the Instant Pot and add any optional ingredients like cheese, herbs, or vegetables. Stir everything together until well combined.
  5. Serve immediately, and enjoy the creamy, fluffy eggs!

This recipe is perfect for a quick breakfast or brunch, and you can customize it with your favorite ingredients to create endless variations.

3. Instant Pot Breakfast Burrito

When you’re in the mood for a savory and filling breakfast, a breakfast burrito is a great option. The Instant Pot makes it easy to cook the ingredients for your burrito and wrap it up in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound breakfast sausage (or bacon)
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 8 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Use the sauté function on the Instant Pot to cook the breakfast sausage until browned. Remove the sausage and set it aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté the onions and bell peppers until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the beaten eggs to the pot and cook until they are scrambled and fully cooked through, about 3-5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stir in the cooked sausage and cheese, allowing the cheese to melt into the egg mixture.
  5. Warm the flour tortillas in the microwave or on a skillet. Then, scoop the egg mixture onto each tortilla and roll them up into a burrito.
  6. Serve immediately, or wrap in foil for a grab-and-go breakfast.

Breakfast burritos are a fantastic option for meal prepping, as you can easily make a batch and store them in the fridge for a quick breakfast throughout the week.

4. Instant Pot French Toast Casserole

French toast casserole is a comforting breakfast that’s perfect for special occasions or a slow weekend morning. With the Instant Pot, you can make a French toast casserole that’s fluffy, golden, and delicious in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices of thick bread (preferably day-old)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: powdered sugar, more maple syrup, fresh berries for topping

Instructions:

  1. Grease a 7-inch round cake pan or an Instant Pot-safe dish.
  2. Tear the bread slices into pieces and place them into the prepared pan.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla extract. Pour the egg mixture over the bread, making sure all the pieces are soaked.
  4. Cover the pan with foil and place it on the trivet inside the Instant Pot.
  5. Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot’s base, then seal the lid and set the pot to cook on high pressure for 20 minutes.
  6. Allow a natural release of pressure for 10 minutes, then carefully release the remaining pressure.
  7. Remove the casserole from the pot and top with powdered sugar, extra maple syrup, and fresh berries.

This Instant Pot French toast casserole is perfect for serving a crowd, and it’s a great way to use up leftover bread while making a comforting breakfast that feels special.

5. Instant Pot Quinoa Porridge

Quinoa is often thought of as a savory grain, but it makes a fantastic base for a breakfast porridge. It’s packed with protein and fiber, making it a nutritious option to start your day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or milk (or a combination)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Toppings: fresh fruit, nuts, seeds, shredded coconut

Instructions:

  1. Add the quinoa, water (or milk), and salt to the Instant Pot.
  2. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 1 minute.
  3. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural release of pressure for about 5 minutes, then carefully release any remaining pressure.
  4. Stir in honey or maple syrup, and cinnamon. Adjust the sweetness to your liking.
  5. Serve with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, nuts, seeds, or a sprinkle of shredded coconut.

This quinoa porridge is a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional oatmeal and makes for a filling, satisfying breakfast.

6. Instant Pot Banana Nut Bread

Banana nut bread is a classic breakfast treat, and with the Instant Pot, you can have it on the table without having to preheat the oven. This moist and flavorful banana bread is sure to become a staple in your breakfast rotation.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter or oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions:

  1. Grease a 7-inch round cake pan or an Instant Pot-safe pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the mashed bananas, eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. In another bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until combined.
  4. Gently fold in the chopped walnuts or pecans.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and cover it with foil.
  6. Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot’s base, then place the pan on the trivet inside the pot.
  7. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 40 minutes.
  8. Allow a natural release of pressure for 10 minutes, then carefully release the remaining pressure.
  9. Let the bread cool for a few minutes before removing it from the pan.

This Instant Pot banana nut bread is perfect for a quick breakfast or an afternoon snack, and it’s an excellent way to use up ripe bananas.

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot is an excellent tool for making quick, delicious, and nutritious breakfast options. From steel-cut oats to breakfast burritos, the possibilities are endless. These recipes will help you save time in the kitchen while still enjoying hearty, homemade meals that will fuel you throughout the day. Whether you’re looking for something sweet or savory, the Instant Pot has the versatility to create a breakfast that suits every taste. So, grab your Instant Pot and start cooking up a storm!
