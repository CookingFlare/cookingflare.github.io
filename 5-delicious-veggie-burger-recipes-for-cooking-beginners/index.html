5 Delicious Veggie Burger Recipes for Cooking Beginners


In recent years, veggie burgers have become increasingly popular, offering a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional beef burgers. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming for cooking beginners to decide which recipe to try. In this article, we will explore five mouth-watering veggie burger recipes that are easy to make and perfect for cooking novices.

Recipe 1: Black Bean and Sweet Potato Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 medium sweet potato, cooked and mashed
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mash the black beans using a fork or a potato masher.
  3. Add the mashed sweet potato, oats, breadcrumbs, cheese, olive oil, onion, garlic, cumin, salt, and pepper to the bowl.
  4. Mix everything well until a dough forms.
  5. Divide the dough into 4-6 portions, depending on the desired patty size.
  6. Shape each portion into a ball and flatten it slightly into a patty shape.
  7. Place the patties on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Serve the burgers on a toasted bun with your favorite toppings.
