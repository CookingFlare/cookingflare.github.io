Decadent Nut-Free Chocolate Cupcake Recipe


If you’re a chocolate lover with a nut allergy, you know the struggle of finding delicious desserts that don’t contain nuts. But fear not, because I have the perfect recipe for you - Decadent Nut-Free Chocolate Cupcakes! These moist and rich cupcakes are so indulgent, you won’t even miss the nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups whole milk
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cocoa powder, and granulated sugar until well combined.

  3. Add the vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until the batter is smooth and well incorporated.

  4. Gradually add the milk to the batter, mixing well after each addition. The batter will be quite thick at this stage.

  5. Slowly pour in the boiling water, stirring continuously. The batter will become thin, but that’s completely normal.

  6. Using a spoon or a scoop, fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with the batter.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  8. Once baked, remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the tin for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Frosting options

Now that you have your beautifully baked nut-free chocolate cupcakes, it’s time to add the finishing touch - the frosting! Here are a few frosting options to choose from:

Classic Chocolate Buttercream Frosting

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

In a large mixing bowl, cream the softened butter until smooth. Gradually add the powdered sugar and cocoa powder, alternating with the milk and vanilla extract. Mix until the frosting is light and fluffy. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, pipe or spread the frosting on top of each cupcake.

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

In a medium-sized bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and butter until smooth. Gradually add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, mixing until the frosting is creamy and well combined. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, pipe or spread the cream cheese frosting on top.

Whipped Ganache Frosting

  • 8 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Place the chopped dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to simmer. Pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate and let it sit for a minute. Then whisk until the mixture is smooth and shiny. Allow the ganache to cool to room temperature, then whip it until it thickens and becomes fluffy. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, pipe or spread the whipped ganache frosting on top.

Enjoy your Decadent Nut-Free Chocolate Cupcakes!

These nut-free chocolate cupcakes are a true delight for chocolate enthusiasts with nut allergies. With their moist texture and rich flavor, they are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether you choose to frost them with classic chocolate buttercream, creamy cream cheese frosting, or whipped ganache, these cupcakes are guaranteed to be a hit at any gathering. So go ahead and indulge in these decadent treats - no nuts required!
