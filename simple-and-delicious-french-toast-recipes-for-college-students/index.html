Simple and Delicious French Toast Recipes for College Students


Are you a college student looking for quick and easy breakfast options to start your day off right? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some simple and delicious French toast recipes that are perfect for busy college students. These recipes are not only easy to make but also require minimal ingredients, making them budget-friendly too. So, let’s get started!

Classic French Toast

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Butter for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a pat of butter to melt.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly.
  4. Place the dipped bread onto the heated skillet and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  5. Repeat the process until all the bread slices are cooked.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruits, or powdered sugar.

Nutella-Stuffed French Toast

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons Nutella
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Butter for frying

Instructions:

  1. Spread Nutella evenly on two slices of bread and cover with the remaining two slices to make sandwiches.
  2. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a pat of butter to melt.
  4. Dip each Nutella sandwich into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly.
  5. Place the dipped sandwiches onto the heated skillet and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with a dusting of powdered sugar or a drizzle of maple syrup.

Cinnamon Sugar French Toast Sticks

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • Butter for frying

Instructions:

  1. Cut each slice of bread into 3-4 sticks.
  2. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, cinnamon, and sugar.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a pat of butter to melt.
  4. Dip each bread stick into the egg mixture, making sure to coat all sides.
  5. Place the dipped sticks onto the heated skillet and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar or your favorite dipping sauce.

Savory French Toast with Bacon and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of cooked bacon
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese of your choice
  • Butter for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a pat of butter to melt.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly.
  4. Place the dipped bread onto the heated skillet and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  5. While the French toast is cooking, place a slice of cooked bacon and some shredded cheese on two slices of bread.
  6. Top with the remaining two slices of French toast to make sandwiches.
  7. Cook the sandwiches on the skillet for an additional 1-2 minutes on each side, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve hot with ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce.

Whether you prefer the classic version or want to experiment with different flavors, these French toast recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. They require minimal time and effort, making them perfect for busy college students. So, go ahead and give them a try. Your mornings will never be the same again!
