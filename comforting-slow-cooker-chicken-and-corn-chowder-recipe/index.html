Comforting Slow Cooker Chicken and Corn Chowder Recipe


Are you looking for a hearty and comforting meal that practically cooks itself? Look no further than this slow cooker chicken and corn chowder recipe. Packed with tender chicken, sweet corn, and flavorful seasonings, this chowder is the perfect dish to warm you up on a chilly evening. Plus, the convenience of using a slow cooker means you can set it and forget it, allowing the flavors to meld together while you go about your day. Let’s dive into the details of this soul-soothing recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 3 cups frozen corn kernels
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 medium potatoes, diced
  • 2 cups half-and-half
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Slow Cooker: Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker. Add the chicken broth, frozen corn, diced onion, minced garlic, and diced potatoes.

  2. Seasoning: Sprinkle the dried thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper over the ingredients in the slow cooker. Stir gently to combine the seasonings with the other ingredients.

  3. Cook: Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-7 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and the potatoes are tender.

  4. Shred the Chicken: Once the cooking time is complete, remove the chicken breasts from the slow cooker and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  5. Finish the Chowder: Stir in the half-and-half, allowing it to heat through for about 15-20 minutes. Adjust the seasoning with additional salt and pepper if needed.

  6. Serve: Ladle the creamy chicken and corn chowder into bowls and garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a smoky flavor, you can add a small amount of crumbled bacon to the chowder.
  • If you prefer a thicker chowder, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of water and stir it into the chowder during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  • Consider adding a dash of cayenne pepper for a subtle kick of heat.

Final Thoughts:

This slow cooker chicken and corn chowder is a versatile and satisfying dish that is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a warm bowl of homemade goodness, this recipe delivers on flavor, convenience, and comfort. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to savor the delicious aroma of this irresistible chowder as it simmers to perfection. Enjoy!

With its creamy texture and rich flavors, this comforting slow cooker chicken and corn chowder is a crowd-pleaser that’s easy to make and even easier to enjoy. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is a wonderful introduction to the world of slow cooker meals. So, gather your ingredients, set your slow cooker, and get ready to delight your taste buds with this soul-warming chowder.
