Themed Suppers: Inspiring Little Cooks with Valrhona Chocolate, Hanukkah & Whoopi Goldberg


Why settle for a regular meal when you can take your dinner party to the next level with a themed supper? This season, ignite the creativity in your little cooks by combining three exciting themes - Valrhona Chocolate, Hanukkah, and Whoopi Goldberg. Let the chocolate magic, inspirational tales, and traditional customs come together in an unforgettable feast.

Valrhona Chocolate: A Match Made in Heaven

Imagine the look of delight on your children’s faces as they learn about the world-renowned Valrhona chocolate. This French chocolate company prides itself on quality and craftsmanship, creating an allure to the finest Belgian chocolate confectionery.

For our chocolate-themed supper, gather ingredients to make a delicious and simple batch of chocolate truffles. Opt for Valrhona chocolates, such as their classic Blanc or Noir varieties, for exquisite flavor.

Chocolate Truffles

Materials:

  • 250 g Valrhona Blanc chocolate
  • 150 g heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • Cocoa powder or ground nuts for rolling
  • Tsp of powdered sugar or honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the cream in a saucepan over medium heat until it starts steaming.
  2. Place the Valrhona Blanc chocolate and butter in a large heatproof bowl, then pour the heated cream over the chocolate and butter.
  3. Let it sit for a minute, then stir until smooth and glossy.
  4. Cover and refrigerate until firm, about 2 hours.
  5. Scoop spoonfuls of the truffle mixture, roll into balls, then roll in cocoa powder or ground nuts, also adding a touch of powdered sugar or honey if desired.
  6. Serve chilled, allowing the flavors to develop before your excited little chefs dig in.

Hanukkah: Celebrating the Festival of Lights

Hanukkah, also known as the Festival of Lights, is an eight-day Jewish holiday that commemorates the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem. One of the most iconic Hanukkah traditions is the lighting of the menorah, alongside various food-inspired customs.

Among the traditional Hanukkah treats, none are as well-known as latkes, or potato pancakes. For a tasty twist, incorporate Valrhona chocolate into this mouthwatering treat. Try a new recipe: Chocolate Latkes!

Chocolate Latkes

Materials:

  • 2 tablespoons of Valrhona cocoa powder
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and grated
  • 1 medium onion, peeled and grated
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Coconut or vegetable oil for frying
  • Mini chocolate chips or chocolate shavings to serve

Instructions:

  1. Mix together the Valrhona cocoa powder and grated potato and onion in a colander to remove excess moisture, then place the mixture in a medium bowl.
  2. Add the egg, a pinch of salt, and pepper, and mix well to combine.
  3. Heat about ¼ inch of coconut or vegetable oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Spoon the potato mixture into loosely packed tablespoon-sized portions into the hot oil, using the back of the spoon to flatten the latkes.
  5. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until browned on one side, then flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes, until browned on the other side.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve warm atop mini chocolate chips or chocolate shavings, for a rich and delightful twist on a classic Hanukkah dish.

Whoopi Goldberg: Empower Their Palates with Inspiration

Whoopi Goldberg, a talented actress, comedian, and author, has inspired countless individuals with her strength, authenticity, and diversity. What better way to introduce her impact than with a funky and fabulous dinner?

Serve up a dish inspired by her unique flair and exotic tastes as you bond over her tales of strength and determination. Try this savory and fruity fusion for a fun and unique dinner: Whoopi’s Tropical Chicken.

Whoopi’s Tropical Chicken

Materials:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup Valrhona cocoa powder
  • 1 cup mango chutney
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice

Instructions:

  1. Mix together the cocoa powder, mango chutney, and pineapple juice in a blender until well combined.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish, then pour the cocoa-mango-chutney mixture over the chicken, ensuring each piece is well-coated.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to marinate.
  4. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high.
  5. Cook the marinated chicken for about 7 minutes per side, or until no longer pink.
  6. Slice and garnish with freshly chopped cilantro and serve with steamed jasmine rice for a sweet, daring, and Whoopi-ful feast!

As you bring these unique themes together, you’ll amplify your little cooks’ creative juices and ignite their gastronomic senses. Not only will they learn the ins and outs of different cultures and foods, but they’ll also be inspired by the remarkable stories and achievements of Whoopi Goldberg. This themed supper experience will be an unforgettable adventure, sparking a life-long love of food exploration and diversity.
