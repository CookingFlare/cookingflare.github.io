Greek Chicken Wrap: A Taste of the Mediterranean

Welcome to the flavorful world of Greek cuisine! Today, we’re diving into the delightful realm of Greek Chicken Wraps, a dish that perfectly encapsulates the fresh and vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a curious beginner, this recipe is sure to tantalize your taste buds and transport you to the sun-kissed shores of Greece. Ingredients: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil 3 cloves of garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried thyme Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup plain Greek yogurt 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice 1 cucumber, diced 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced 4 large whole wheat wraps 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cup crumbled feta cheese Fresh lettuce leaves Instructions: Marinating the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the extra virgin olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them, ensuring the chicken is well-coated. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse. Grilling the Chicken: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the marinated chicken from the bag and discard the excess marinade. Grill the chicken breasts for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through and have beautiful grill marks. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips. Assembling the Wraps: In a small bowl, mix the Greek yogurt with the freshly squeezed lemon juice to create a tangy dressing. Lay out the whole wheat wraps and spread a generous dollop of the yogurt dressing in the center of each wrap. Arrange the sliced grilled chicken on top of the dressing, followed by the diced cucumber, thinly sliced red onion, halved cherry tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, and fresh lettuce leaves. Gently fold in the sides of the wraps and then roll them up tightly, securing them with toothpicks if needed. Serving the Wraps: Once assembled, slice the wraps diagonally to showcase the colorful and delicious filling. Serve the Greek Chicken Wraps alongside a side salad, a refreshing tzatziki dip, or some crispy oven-baked fries for a complete Mediterranean feast. The Flavors of Greece in Every Bite With its succulent grilled chicken, creamy yogurt dressing, and a medley of fresh vegetables and tangy feta cheese, the Greek Chicken Wrap is a celebration of Mediterranean flavors. This versatile dish is perfect for a leisurely weekend lunch, a quick weeknight dinner, or even as a delightful addition to a picnic basket. ...
