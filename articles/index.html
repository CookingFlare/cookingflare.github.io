Savory Sweet Potato and Kale Breakfast Bake Ingredients: 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and grated 2 cups chopped kale 1 red bell pepper, diced 1 small onion, finely chopped 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon smoked paprika Salt and pepper to taste 6 eggs 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk Chopped fresh parsley for garnish Instructions: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with olive oil. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and bell pepper, and sauté until softened. Add the grated sweet potatoes and kale to the skillet. Cook until the sweet potatoes are tender and the kale is wilted. Season with garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and almond milk. Transfer the sweet potato and kale mixture to the greased baking dish. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the eggs are set. Sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley before serving. This savory breakfast bake is a delightful blend of sweet potatoes, hearty kale, and vibrant bell peppers.
