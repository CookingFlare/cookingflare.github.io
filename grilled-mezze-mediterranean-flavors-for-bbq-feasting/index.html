Grilled Mezze: Mediterranean Flavors for BBQ Feasting


When it comes to hosting a Mediterranean-inspired BBQ feast, Grilled Mezze is the perfect way to bring the flavors of the Mediterranean to your backyard. Mezze, which means “small dishes” in Arabic, is a collection of appetizers and small plates that are perfect for sharing and grazing. Grilling these mezze dishes adds a smoky depth of flavor that elevates the entire experience. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of Grilled Mezze dishes that will take your BBQ feast to the next level.

Grilled Halloumi Skewers

Halloumi, a semi-hard, brined cheese from Cyprus, is a staple in Mediterranean cuisine. Grilling halloumi adds a beautiful char and depth of flavor to this salty, savory cheese. To make Grilled Halloumi Skewers, simply cube the halloumi and thread it onto skewers. Brush the skewers with a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, and a touch of honey before grilling until golden and crisp. Serve the skewers with a drizzle of honey and a sprinkle of fresh herbs for a simple, yet impressive, mezze dish.

Grilled Eggplant with Tahini

Eggplant, with its meaty texture and ability to soak up flavors, is a perfect candidate for the grill. To make Grilled Eggplant with Tahini, slice the eggplant into rounds, brush with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper before grilling until tender and charred. Drizzle the grilled eggplant with a generous amount of creamy tahini sauce, made with tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and a touch of water to achieve the perfect drizzling consistency. Garnish with a sprinkle of sumac and chopped parsley for a burst of color and flavor.

Grilled Lamb Kofta

Kofta, seasoned ground meat shaped onto skewers, is a classic dish found throughout the Mediterranean and the Middle East. To make Grilled Lamb Kofta, combine ground lamb with a blend of aromatic spices, such as cumin, coriander, and paprika, along with finely chopped onion, garlic, and parsley. Form the mixture into elongated patties and thread them onto skewers before grilling until perfectly charred and cooked through. Serve the Grilled Lamb Kofta with a side of creamy tzatziki and warm pita bread for a satisfying and flavorful mezze experience.

Grilled Stuffed Peppers

Stuffed peppers are a beloved dish in Mediterranean cuisine, and grilling them adds a delightful smokiness to the dish. To make Grilled Stuffed Peppers, hollow out bell peppers and stuff them with a mixture of cooked rice, pine nuts, raisins, and a blend of aromatic spices. Grill the stuffed peppers until the peppers are tender and the filling is heated through. The sweetness of the grilled peppers pairs beautifully with the savory, spiced filling, creating a mezze dish that is as visually stunning as it is delicious.

Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp

Shrimp, when grilled with a simple marinade of lemon, garlic, and olive oil, becomes a show-stopping mezze dish that’s perfect for any BBQ feast. To make Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp, marinate large shrimp in a mixture of olive oil, minced garlic, lemon zest, and a pinch of red pepper flakes before grilling until pink and slightly charred. Serve the Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp with wedges of lemon and a sprinkle of fresh parsley for a bright and flavorful addition to your mezze spread.

Grilled Pita Bread with Dips

No mezze feast is complete without warm, pillowy pita bread served alongside an array of flavorful dips. To make Grilled Pita Bread, simply brush store-bought or homemade pita bread with olive oil before grilling until warm and slightly charred. Serve the Grilled Pita Bread with an assortment of dips, such as hummus, baba ganoush, and muhammara, for a mezze experience that is sure to impress your guests.

Conclusion

Grilled Mezze offers a delightful array of flavors and textures that are perfect for a Mediterranean-inspired BBQ feast. From the smoky char of grilled halloumi to the aromatic spices of lamb kofta, each dish brings a unique element to the mezze spread. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply enjoying a casual evening with loved ones, Grilled Mezze is a versatile and flavorful addition to any BBQ menu. Embrace the spirit of sharing and grazing with these Grilled Mezze dishes, and transport your guests to the sun-drenched shores of the Mediterranean with every bite.
