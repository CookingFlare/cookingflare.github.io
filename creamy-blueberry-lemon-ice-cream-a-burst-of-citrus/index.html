Creamy Blueberry Lemon Ice Cream: A Burst of Citrus


If you’re looking for a refreshing and delightful dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth, look no further than this Creamy Blueberry Lemon Ice Cream recipe. This homemade treat combines the tartness of fresh blueberries with the zesty citrus flavor of lemons to create a delightful burst of flavor in every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Blueberry Puree:

    • In a saucepan, combine the fresh blueberries and 1/2 cup of sugar over medium heat.
    • Cook the blueberries, stirring occasionally, until they begin to break down and release their juices, about 8-10 minutes.
    • Once the blueberries have softened, remove the saucepan from the heat and allow the mixture to cool slightly.
    • Transfer the blueberry mixture to a blender and puree until smooth. Set aside to cool completely.

  2. Make the Ice Cream Base:

    • In a separate saucepan, heat the heavy cream, whole milk, and the remaining 1/2 cup of sugar over medium heat. Stir the mixture occasionally until it begins to simmer, then remove it from the heat.

  3. Prepare the Egg Mixture:

    • In a mixing bowl, whisk the egg yolks until they become slightly lighter in color.
    • Slowly pour the warm cream mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly to temper the eggs.

  4. Cook the Custard:

    • Transfer the egg and cream mixture back to the saucepan and place it over medium heat.
    • Stir the mixture constantly until it thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon, about 5-7 minutes.
    • Remove the custard from the heat and strain it through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any lumps.

  5. Combine the Flavors:

    • Stir the vanilla extract, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt into the custard.
    • Gently fold in the cooled blueberry puree, swirling it through the custard to create a marbled effect.

  6. Chill and Churn:

    • Cover the ice cream base with plastic wrap, ensuring that the wrap touches the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming.
    • Refrigerate the mixture for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to chill thoroughly.
    • Once chilled, pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  7. Freeze and Enjoy:

    • Transfer the churned ice cream to a freezer-safe container, swirling in any remaining blueberry puree for extra flavor.
    • Freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours or until firm before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this Creamy Blueberry Lemon Ice Cream in chilled bowls or cones, garnished with fresh blueberries and a twist of lemon for a visually appealing and delicious dessert. This delightful treat is perfect for hot summer days or as a sweet ending to a special meal.

Final Thoughts

The marriage of sweet blueberries and tangy lemon in this Creamy Blueberry Lemon Ice Cream is a true celebration of summer flavors. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a cool and creamy dessert, this recipe is sure to impress with its vibrant taste and luscious texture. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your ingredients, and treat yourself to a homemade delight that will leave your taste buds singing!
