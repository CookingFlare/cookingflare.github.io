Irresistible Peanut Butter Cupcakes: A Sweet and Salty Delight


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of sweet and salty, then these Irresistible Peanut Butter Cupcakes are just what you need! With a moist and fluffy peanut butter cake base, topped with a creamy peanut butter frosting and a sprinkle of sea salt, these cupcakes are guaranteed to satisfy all your cravings. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself, these cupcakes are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt

For the frosting:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • Sea salt, for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, creamy peanut butter, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the peanut butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full. Tap the pan gently on the counter to remove any air bubbles.

  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely before frosting.

  7. To make the frosting, beat the softened butter and creamy peanut butter together until smooth and creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing until well combined. Add the milk, one tablespoon at a time, until the desired consistency is reached.

  8. Once the cupcakes are cooled, frost them generously with the peanut butter frosting. For an extra touch of indulgence, sprinkle a pinch of sea salt on top of each cupcake.

  9. Serve and enjoy! These cupcakes are best enjoyed fresh, but can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.

Final Thoughts

These Irresistible Peanut Butter Cupcakes are a delightful blend of sweet and salty flavors, perfect for any occasion. The moist and fluffy peanut butter cake pairs perfectly with the creamy frosting, and the sprinkle of sea salt adds a unique twist that will keep you coming back for more. So, why wait? Treat yourself and your loved ones to these mouthwatering cupcakes today!

Remember, baking is all about experimenting and making the recipe your own. Feel free to add your own personal touch by incorporating chopped peanuts or a drizzle of melted chocolate on top. Get creative and have fun in the kitchen – the possibilities are endless!

Enjoy the irresistible combination of peanut butter, sweetness, and a hint of salt with these delectable cupcakes. Happy baking!
