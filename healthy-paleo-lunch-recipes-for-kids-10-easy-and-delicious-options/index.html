Healthy Paleo Lunch Recipes for Kids: 10 Easy and Delicious Options


Finding healthy and delicious lunch options for kids can be a challenge, especially when you’re looking for recipes that are paleo-friendly. The paleo diet focuses on consuming whole, unprocessed foods that our ancestors would have eaten. It excludes grains, dairy, and processed sugars, making it a great option for those looking for a clean and nutritious way of eating.

In this blog post, we will share 10 easy and delicious paleo lunch recipes that are perfect for kids. These recipes are not only nutritious but also kid-approved, ensuring that your little ones will enjoy every bite while getting the essential nutrients they need.

1. Turkey and Avocado Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Turkey slices
  • Avocado
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Cucumber slices

Instructions:

  1. Lay a lettuce leaf flat on a plate.
  2. Place a turkey slice on top of the lettuce leaf.
  3. Spread avocado on the turkey slice.
  4. Add tomato and cucumber slices on top.
  5. Roll up the lettuce wrap and secure with a toothpick.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.

2. Chicken and Vegetable Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • Bell peppers, cut into chunks
  • Zucchini, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes

Instructions:

  1. Thread the chicken, bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes onto skewers.
  2. Grill the skewers until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  3. Serve with a side of paleo-friendly dipping sauce.

3. Sweet Potato Sliders

Ingredients:

  • Sweet potatoes, sliced into rounds
  • Ground beef or turkey
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion slices (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the sweet potato rounds on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the ground beef or turkey in a skillet until browned.
  5. Assemble the sliders by placing a lettuce leaf, a tomato slice, and a red onion slice (if using) on top of a sweet potato round.
  6. Top with a spoonful of cooked ground beef or turkey.
  7. Place another sweet potato round on top to complete the slider.

4. Tuna Salad Lettuce Cups

Ingredients:

  • Canned tuna, drained
  • Paleo-friendly mayonnaise
  • Diced celery
  • Diced red onion
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the canned tuna, paleo-friendly mayonnaise, diced celery, diced red onion, and fresh lemon juice.
  2. Spoon the tuna salad into lettuce leaves, creating cups.
  3. Serve chilled.

5. Egg Muffins

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Chopped vegetables (bell peppers, spinach, mushrooms, etc.)
  • Diced cooked bacon or ham (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs.
  3. Stir in the chopped vegetables and diced cooked bacon or ham (if using).
  4. Pour the mixture into greased muffin cups, filling each cup about two-thirds full.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the egg muffins are set and lightly golden.

6. Veggie Sushi Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Nori sheets
  • Cauliflower rice
  • Sliced avocado
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Carrot matchsticks

Instructions:

  1. Lay a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat or a clean kitchen towel.
  2. Spread a thin layer of cauliflower rice over the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  3. Place sliced avocado, sliced cucumber, and carrot matchsticks on top of the cauliflower rice.
  4. Roll the nori tightly, using the sushi mat or kitchen towel to help.
  5. Slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.

7. Chicken and Vegetable Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Ground chicken
  • Chopped vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, onions, etc.)
  • Coconut aminos
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground chicken until browned.
  2. Add the chopped vegetables and cook until tender.
  3. Stir in coconut aminos to taste.
  4. Spoon the chicken and vegetable mixture into lettuce leaves, creating wraps.

8. Mini Meatball Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef or turkey
  • Almond flour
  • Chopped herbs (parsley, basil, etc.)
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Wooden skewers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the ground beef or turkey, almond flour, chopped herbs, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Shape the mixture into mini meatballs.
  4. Thread the mini meatballs onto wooden skewers.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through.

9. Zucchini Noodles with Meat Sauce

Ingredients:

  • Zucchini, spiralized into noodles
  • Ground beef or turkey
  • Paleo-friendly tomato sauce
  • Chopped onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or turkey until browned.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the paleo-friendly tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Toss the zucchini noodles in the sauce until heated through.

10. Baked Chicken Nuggets

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast, cut into nugget-sized pieces
  • Almond flour
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Avocado oil or olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a shallow bowl, mix together the almond flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip each chicken nugget into the almond flour mixture, coating evenly.
  4. Place the coated chicken nuggets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Drizzle the chicken nuggets with avocado oil or olive oil.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and golden brown.

These 10 healthy paleo lunch recipes for kids are not only easy to make but also packed with nutrients that will keep your little ones energized throughout the day. Give them a try and watch your kids enjoy their delicious and wholesome meals!
