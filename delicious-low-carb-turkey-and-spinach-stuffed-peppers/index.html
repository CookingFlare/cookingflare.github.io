Delicious Low Carb Turkey and Spinach Stuffed Peppers


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy low-carb meal that’s both satisfying and easy to prepare? Look no further than these mouthwatering turkey and spinach stuffed peppers! Packed with flavor and nutrients, this recipe will become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Rinse the peppers under cold water to remove any remaining seeds. Place the peppers in a baking dish, standing upright.

  3. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until translucent and fragrant.

  4. Add the ground turkey to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat from the skillet.

  5. Stir in the chopped spinach, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Cook for an additional 2 minutes until the spinach wilts.

  6. Remove the skillet from heat and stir in 1/2 cup of marinara sauce. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  7. Spoon the turkey and spinach mixture evenly into each bell pepper, packing it tightly. Top each pepper with a spoonful of the remaining marinara sauce.

  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.

  9. Remove the foil and sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the peppers. Return the dish to the oven and bake for another 10 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  10. Remove the stuffed peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

These low carb turkey and spinach stuffed peppers are not only delicious, but they’re also a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. The combination of flavors from the ground turkey, spinach, and aromatic herbs creates a satisfying meal that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

You can serve these stuffed peppers as a main course, or pair them with a fresh salad for a lighter meal. They also make a fantastic meal prep option, as they can be stored in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before enjoying.

So why wait? Head to your kitchen and whip up a batch of these delicious low carb turkey and spinach stuffed peppers. Your taste buds and your waistline will thank you!

Remember to experiment with different spices and vegetables to personalize the recipe to your liking. Enjoy!
