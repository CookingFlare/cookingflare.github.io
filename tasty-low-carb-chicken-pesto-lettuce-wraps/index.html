Tasty Low Carb Chicken Pesto Lettuce Wraps


If you’re looking for a delicious, low-carb meal that’s bursting with flavor, these chicken pesto lettuce wraps are the perfect choice. Packed with protein and healthy fats, this recipe is not only satisfying but also incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply want to enjoy a light and refreshing meal, these lettuce wraps are sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup basil pesto
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 1 head of butter lettuce, leaves separated
  • Optional: cherry tomatoes, sliced avocado, or extra Parmesan for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and season with salt and pepper. Cook until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.

  2. Once the chicken is cooked, reduce the heat to low and add the basil pesto to the skillet. Stir the pesto into the chicken until it is well coated. Allow the chicken to simmer in the pesto for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. While the chicken is simmering, toast the pine nuts in a dry skillet over medium heat until they are lightly golden and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes. Be sure to keep an eye on them, as they can burn quickly. Once toasted, remove the pine nuts from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Once the chicken is fully coated in the pesto and heated through, remove the skillet from the heat. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and toasted pine nuts until everything is well combined.

  5. To assemble the lettuce wraps, take a leaf of butter lettuce and spoon a generous portion of the chicken pesto mixture into the center. If desired, top with cherry tomatoes, sliced avocado, or an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

  6. Serve the lettuce wraps immediately, and enjoy the fresh, vibrant flavors of this low-carb delight.

Nutritional Information

These chicken pesto lettuce wraps are not only delicious but also nutritious. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and plenty of flavor, they make a fantastic low-carb meal option. Here’s a rough estimate of the nutritional content per serving:

  • Calories: 300
  • Protein: 25g
  • Fat: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 6g
  • Fiber: 2g

Note: Nutritional information may vary depending on specific ingredients and portion sizes.

Final Thoughts

These tasty low-carb chicken pesto lettuce wraps are a wonderful option for a light and satisfying meal. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch, a flavorful appetizer, or a healthy dinner option, these wraps have it all. Their versatility makes them a great choice for any occasion, and their vibrant flavors are sure to please your taste buds.

So, next time you’re craving something fresh, flavorful, and low in carbs, give these chicken pesto lettuce wraps a try. They’re easy to make, delicious to eat, and a fantastic addition to any healthy eating plan.

Enjoy!
