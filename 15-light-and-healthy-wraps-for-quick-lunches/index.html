15 Light and Healthy Wraps for Quick Lunches


Whether you’re at work, home, or on the go, finding a quick and healthy lunch option can often feel like a challenge. Enter the versatile wrap! These delightful parcels are not only easy to prepare but also customizable to suit your taste and dietary preferences. In this post, we will explore 15 light and healthy wraps that can elevate your lunch game and keep you energized throughout the day. From fresh vegetables to lean proteins, these wraps are packed with nutrients and flavor. Letâ€™s dive in!

1. Mediterranean Chickpea Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat wrap
  • 1 cup canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumbers
  • 1/2 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons hummus
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mash the chickpeas lightly with a fork.
  2. Add cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, parsley, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Spread hummus on the wrap, layer the chickpea mixture, and roll tightly.
  4. Cut in half and enjoy!

2. Turkey and Spinach Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Spinach wrap
  • 4 oz sliced turkey breast
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 tablespoon mustard or your favorite spread

Instructions:

  1. Spread mustard on the spinach wrap.
  2. Layer turkey, spinach, avocado, and carrots.
  3. Roll tightly, slice, and serve.

3. Quinoa and Black Bean Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole grain wrap
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup shredded lettuce
  • Lime juice

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine quinoa, black beans, corn, salsa, and lime juice.
  2. Spread the mixture on the wrap, add shredded lettuce, and roll it up.
  3. Cut in half and enjoy this protein-packed meal.

4. Caprese Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Tomato basil wrap
  • 4 oz grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/2 cup arugula
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Lay the grilled chicken slices on the wrap.
  2. Top with mozzarella, arugula, balsamic glaze, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roll tightly and slice for a refreshing meal.

5. Veggie Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat wrap
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers (mixed colors)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cucumber, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Spread hummus on the wrap generously.
  2. Layer bell peppers, cabbage, carrots, and cucumber.
  3. Roll up tightly, slice, and savor the crunch!

6. Thai Peanut Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Rice paper wrap
  • 4 oz grilled chicken, sliced
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup red cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons Thai peanut sauce
  • Fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice paper wraps in warm water until pliable.
  2. Layer chicken, carrots, cucumbers, red cabbage, and cilantro on the wrap.
  3. Drizzle peanut sauce, roll tightly, and enjoy a burst of flavors.

7. Tuna Salad Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat wrap
  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt or mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix tuna with Greek yogurt, celery, and red onion.
  2. Spread the mixture on the wrap and add lettuce leaves.
  3. Roll tightly and cut in half for a satisfying lunch.

8. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Spinach wrap
  • 1/2 cup roasted sweet potato, cubed
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • Fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Layer roasted sweet potato, black beans, avocado, salsa, and cilantro on the wrap.
  2. Roll tightly, slice, and indulge in this nutrient-rich wrap.

9. Shrimp and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat wrap
  • 4 oz cooked shrimp, chilled
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • Lime juice, salt, and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange shrimp, avocado, lettuce, and tomatoes on the wrap.
  2. Drizzle lime juice, sprinkle salt and pepper, and roll tightly.
  3. Slice and enjoy a refreshing seafood option.

10. Egg Salad Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole grain wrap
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt or mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1/4 cup diced pickles
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix eggs, Greek yogurt, mustard, and pickles.
  2. Spread the egg salad on the wrap and top with lettuce.
  3. Roll up tightly and enjoy this classic dish with a twist.

11. BBQ Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat wrap
  • 4 oz shredded cooked chicken
  • 2 tablespoons BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup coleslaw mix
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken and BBQ sauce.
  2. Spread on the wrap, add coleslaw mix, and pickles if desired.
  3. Roll tightly, slice, and relish this smoky delight.

12. Falafel Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole grain wrap
  • 4-5 falafel balls, cooked
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup tzatziki sauce
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. Mash or lightly crush falafel balls in a bowl.
  2. Place on the wrap, add tomatoes, cucumbers, tzatziki, and lettuce.
  3. Roll tightly, slice, and savor the Mediterranean flavors.

13. Apple and Almond Butter Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat wrap
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
  • 1/2 apple, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Handful of spinach (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread almond butter on the wrap, layer apple slices, and sprinkle cinnamon.
  2. Add spinach for an extra crunch if desired.
  3. Roll tightly, slice, and enjoy a sweet yet healthy treat.

14. Grilled Vegetable Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Tomato basil wrap
  • 1/2 cup grilled zucchini, bell peppers, and eggplant
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons pesto

Instructions:

  1. Spread pesto on the wrap and layer the grilled vegetables.
  2. Top with feta cheese and roll tightly.
  3. Slice and indulge in this colorful and flavorful wrap.

15. Spicy Lentil Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat wrap
  • 1/2 cup cooked lentils
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce (adjust to taste)
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine lentils, tomatoes, onions, and hot sauce.
  2. Spread the mixture on the wrap and add lettuce.
  3. Roll tightly, slice, and enjoy the spicy kick!

Conclusion

Wraps are an incredibly versatile and nutritious option for quick lunches. They can be filled with a variety of ingredients, allowing you to cater to your personal taste and dietary needs. The 15 light and healthy wraps shared above are not only easy to prepare but also packed with protein, fiber, and essential vitamins. With a little creativity, you can mix and match ingredients to create your own signature wraps. So, the next time you’re looking for a healthy lunch, reach for a wrap and enjoy the convenience and flavor they provide!
