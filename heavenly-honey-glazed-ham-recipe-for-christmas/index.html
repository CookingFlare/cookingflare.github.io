Heavenly Honey Glazed Ham Recipe for Christmas


The holiday season is all about gathering with loved ones and indulging in delicious, festive meals. One dish that never fails to impress during Christmas dinner is a succulent honey glazed ham. The perfect combination of sweet and savory flavors makes this dish a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a cooking novice, this heavenly honey glazed ham recipe is sure to elevate your holiday feast to a whole new level.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (7-8 pound) bone-in, fully cooked ham
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preparation: Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C). Place the ham on a cutting board and carefully score the skin in a diamond pattern using a sharp knife. This will allow the glaze to penetrate the meat and create a beautiful presentation.

  2. Glaze Preparation: In a small saucepan, combine the honey, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, ground cloves, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture is smooth and the sugar has dissolved. Set the glaze aside.

  3. Glazing the Ham: Place the scored ham in a roasting pan and brush a generous amount of the honey glaze all over the surface of the ham, ensuring it gets into the scored cuts. Cover the ham loosely with foil and bake for 1 hour.

  4. Basting: After the first hour, remove the foil and baste the ham with the pan juices and additional glaze. Continue to bake, basting every 15 minutes, for another 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the ham is beautifully caramelized and registers an internal temperature of 140°F (60°C).

  5. Resting and Serving: Once the ham is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 15-20 minutes before carving. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a moist and flavorful ham. Carve the ham and serve it with your favorite holiday sides.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra layer of flavor, consider adding a splash of apple juice or bourbon to the glaze.
  • If your ham is larger or smaller than the specified size, adjust the cooking time accordingly. A meat thermometer is your best friend in ensuring the ham is cooked just right.
  • Leftover honey glazed ham makes fantastic sandwiches or can be used to flavor soups and stews, so be sure to save any extras!

In Conclusion:

This heavenly honey glazed ham recipe is a surefire way to make your Christmas dinner a memorable and mouthwatering experience for all. The combination of tender, juicy ham and the rich, sweet glaze is simply irresistible. Whether you’re serving a small family gathering or hosting a festive feast, this recipe is bound to be a hit. Embrace the holiday spirit and treat your loved ones to a mouthwatering honey glazed ham that will have them coming back for seconds, and maybe even thirds! Happy cooking, and Merry Christmas!
