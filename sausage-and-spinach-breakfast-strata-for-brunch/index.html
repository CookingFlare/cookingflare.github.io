Sausage and Spinach Breakfast Strata for Brunch


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying dish to serve at your next brunch gathering? Look no further than this delightful Sausage and Spinach Breakfast Strata recipe. This savory casserole is the perfect combination of fluffy eggs, crusty bread, flavorful sausage, and vibrant spinach, all baked to golden perfection. Not only is it incredibly tasty, but it’s also a convenient make-ahead dish, allowing you to spend more time with your guests and less time in the kitchen. So, let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cups fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 8 cups day-old bread, cut into cubes
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 10 large eggs
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat, breaking it into crumbles with a spatula as it browns. Once the sausage is fully cooked, add the chopped onion to the skillet and sauté until it becomes translucent. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted. Remove the skillet from the heat and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, Dijon mustard, salt, black pepper, and nutmeg until well combined.

  3. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish and place half of the bread cubes in the bottom of the dish. Top with half of the sausage and spinach mixture, followed by half of the shredded cheddar cheese. Repeat with another layer of the remaining bread cubes, sausage and spinach mixture, and cheddar cheese.

  4. Slowly pour the egg mixture over the layered ingredients in the baking dish, ensuring that the bread is evenly soaked. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the bread to absorb the egg mixture.

  5. When you’re ready to bake the strata, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Remove the plastic wrap from the baking dish and bake the strata for 45-55 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the eggs are set.

  6. Once baked, allow the strata to cool for a few minutes before slicing it into generous portions. Serve this delectable dish alongside a fresh fruit salad and a pitcher of mimosas for an unforgettable brunch experience.

Why Sausage and Spinach Breakfast Strata?

The Sausage and Spinach Breakfast Strata is a fantastic choice for a brunch gathering for several reasons. Firstly, it’s a make-ahead dish, meaning you can prepare it the night before and simply bake it in the morning, allowing you to spend more time with your guests. Additionally, the combination of savory Italian sausage, earthy spinach, and rich cheddar cheese creates a flavor profile that will leave your guests craving for more. Moreover, this recipe is incredibly versatile, as you can easily customize it by adding different types of cheese, vegetables, or even substituting the sausage with bacon or ham to suit your preferences.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Sausage and Spinach Breakfast Strata is a delightful and practical dish that is sure to impress your guests at any brunch gathering. Its simple preparation, rich flavors, and make-ahead convenience make it a must-have recipe in your culinary repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a casual weekend brunch or a special holiday gathering, this savory strata will undoubtedly become a favorite among your friends and family. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try, and get ready to elevate your brunch game to a whole new level!
