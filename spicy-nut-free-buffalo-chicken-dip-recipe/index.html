Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe


If you’re a fan of spicy, creamy, and absolutely delicious appetizers, then this Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Chicken Dip is the perfect recipe for you. Whether you’re hosting a game day gathering, a casual get-together with friends, or simply craving a flavor-packed snack, this dip is sure to be a hit. The best part? It’s nut-free, making it suitable for those with nut allergies or dietary restrictions. So, let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this crowd-pleasing dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced green onions
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cream cheese, sour cream, buffalo sauce, ranch dressing, half of the shredded cheddar cheese, diced green onions, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  3. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  4. Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheddar cheese on top of the mixture.

  5. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the dip is hot and bubbly, and the cheese on top is melted and slightly golden.

  6. Once done, remove the dip from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  7. Garnish with additional diced green onions, a drizzle of buffalo sauce, or a dollop of ranch dressing if desired.

  8. Serve the Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Chicken Dip with your favorite dippers such as tortilla chips, celery sticks, carrot sticks, or crackers.

Flavor Burst in Every Bite

This Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Chicken Dip is a flavor explosion with every bite. The creamy texture of the dip combined with the bold flavors of buffalo sauce and ranch dressing creates a harmonious blend that tantalizes the taste buds. The tender, shredded chicken adds a satisfying element to the dish, making it hearty and irresistible.

Allergy-Friendly and Crowd-Pleasing

One of the standout features of this recipe is that it’s nut-free, making it a safe option for individuals with nut allergies. By using alternative ingredients without compromising on flavor, this dip ensures that everyone can indulge in its deliciousness without worry.
