Mouthwatering One Pot Korean BBQ Chicken


If you’re a fan of Korean cuisine and love the rich, bold flavors of BBQ chicken, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering one-pot Korean BBQ chicken recipe. This dish is a perfect blend of savory, sweet, and spicy flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. The best part? It’s all made in one pot, making it a convenient and easy meal to prepare. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with this delectable Korean BBQ chicken recipe.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

For the Chicken:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken drumsticks

For the One Pot:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, brown sugar, gochujang, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, rice vinegar, and black pepper. Mix well until the sugar has dissolved.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken thighs and drumsticks in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure it’s evenly coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to infuse.

  3. Sear the Chicken: Heat the vegetable oil in a large, deep skillet or pot over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, allowing any excess marinade to drip off. Sear the chicken in the hot oil until golden brown on all sides, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  4. Cook the Vegetables: In the same pot, add the sliced onion and bell peppers. Sauté for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are slightly softened.

  5. Simmer with the Chicken: Return the seared chicken to the pot, nestling it among the sautéed vegetables. Pour any remaining marinade over the chicken. Cover the pot and reduce the heat to medium-low. Let the chicken and vegetables simmer in the marinade for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Once the chicken is cooked through, garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds. Serve the Korean BBQ chicken over steamed rice, allowing the flavors to meld together for a truly satisfying meal.

Conclusion

This one-pot Korean BBQ chicken recipe is a delightful fusion of flavors that will transport your taste buds to Korea. The savory and slightly sweet marinade paired with tender, succulent chicken creates a truly unforgettable dining experience. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or a gathering with friends, this dish is sure to impress. Embrace the convenience of a one-pot meal without compromising on flavor with this tantalizing Korean BBQ chicken recipe. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to savor the rich and aromatic essence of Korean cuisine right in your own kitchen. Enjoy!

With the delectable blend of savory, sweet, and spicy flavors, this one-pot Korean BBQ chicken recipe is sure to become a family favorite. The convenience of cooking everything in one pot makes it an ideal choice for busy weeknights, while the bold and aromatic flavors make it a standout dish for special occasions. So, roll up your sleeves and embark on a culinary journey as you prepare this mouthwatering Korean BBQ chicken.
