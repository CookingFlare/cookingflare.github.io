Chocolate Cherry Cookies: A Sweet and Tangy Combo


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of chocolate and cherries, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful recipe for Chocolate Cherry Cookies. These cookies are a perfect blend of rich, indulgent chocolate and the sweet, tangy flavor of cherries, creating a truly irresistible treat for any occasion. Whether you’re baking for a special gathering or simply treating yourself to a homemade delight, these cookies are sure to impress with their unique flavor profile and soft, chewy texture.

Ingredients

Before you dive into the baking process, let’s gather all the essential ingredients for these delectable Chocolate Cherry Cookies. You’ll need:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup dried cherries, chopped

Instructions

Now that you have everything ready, it’s time to start baking these delightful cookies. Here’s a step-by-step guide to creating your own batch of Chocolate Cherry Cookies:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until well combined.

  6. Fold in the semi-sweet chocolate chips and chopped dried cherries until evenly distributed throughout the cookie dough.

  7. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.

  8. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set but the centers are still soft.

  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once cooled, serve and enjoy these delightful Chocolate Cherry Cookies with a glass of milk or your favorite hot beverage.

Serving Suggestions

These Chocolate Cherry Cookies are not only a delicious treat on their own but also pair wonderfully with a variety of accompaniments. Consider serving them alongside a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an indulgent dessert, or packing them in a lunchbox for a delightful midday treat. The sweet and tangy flavors of the cherries perfectly complement the rich chocolate, making these cookies a versatile and irresistible addition to any occasion.

Conclusion

With their rich chocolate flavor and bursts of sweet-tart cherries, these Chocolate Cherry Cookies are a delightful twist on the classic chocolate chip cookie. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting to explore the world of homemade treats, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your collection. So, gather your ingredients, preheat the oven, and get ready to indulge in the delightful combination of chocolate and cherries with every bite of these irresistible cookies. Happy baking!
