Vegan Peanut Butter Brownies: Rich and Fudgy


If you’re looking for a decadent, melt-in-your-mouth dessert that’s both indulgent and satisfying, look no further than these Vegan Peanut Butter Brownies. With a rich, fudgy texture and a delightful peanut butter twist, these brownies are the perfect balance of sweetness and nuttiness. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan baker or just exploring plant-based treats, this recipe will quickly become a favorite in your kitchen.

Why Choose Vegan Brownies?

Traditional brownies are often made with eggs, dairy, and butter, which means they’re off-limits for anyone following a plant-based or vegan lifestyle. But don’t worry—going vegan doesn’t mean sacrificing flavor. In fact, when you remove the dairy and eggs from brownies, you’re left with a base that allows the chocolate and other ingredients to truly shine. Vegan baking is all about creativity and using plant-based substitutes that provide the same satisfying texture and taste.

These vegan peanut butter brownies are rich, moist, and packed with flavor, thanks to the perfect combination of cocoa powder, peanut butter, and vegan-friendly alternatives. They’re also incredibly easy to make and come together in just one bowl. Let’s dive into the ingredients and the simple steps required to create these indulgent brownies.

Ingredients for Vegan Peanut Butter Brownies

For the Brownie Batter:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour: This forms the base of the brownie and gives it structure. You can substitute with whole wheat flour or gluten-free flour if needed.
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder: Use unsweetened cocoa powder to provide that deep, rich chocolate flavor.
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder: This helps the brownies rise slightly, giving them a light and airy texture without being overly cakey.
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt: Just a pinch to balance out the sweetness and enhance the chocolate flavor.
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter: Choose a creamy or slightly chunky peanut butter for that nutty richness that pairs so well with chocolate.
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar or maple syrup: Either of these natural sweeteners provides a deep sweetness with a hint of caramel undertones, perfect for these brownies.
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce: This replaces eggs, providing moisture and binding the ingredients together while keeping the brownies tender and soft.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract: For that warm, fragrant flavor that enhances the overall taste.
  • 1/4 cup non-dairy milk (almond milk, oat milk, or coconut milk): To help bring the batter together and make it smooth and creamy.

For the Peanut Butter Swirl:

  • 1/4 cup peanut butter: You’ll need some extra peanut butter to create a swirl in the brownies for an added creamy, nutty element.
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup: A little sweetness to balance the peanut butter’s natural richness.

How to Make Vegan Peanut Butter Brownies

Step 1: Preheat and Prepare Your Pan

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8x8-inch square baking pan with parchment paper, or lightly grease the pan with coconut oil. This will ensure your brownies come out of the pan easily without sticking.

Step 2: Make the Brownie Batter

In a large mixing bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. This ensures that all the dry ingredients are evenly combined and helps prevent clumps in the brownie batter.

Next, add the peanut butter, coconut sugar (or maple syrup), applesauce, vanilla extract, and non-dairy milk to the dry ingredients. Use a whisk or a spatula to mix everything together until you have a smooth, thick batter. The consistency should be thick, but it should still be easy to spread into the baking pan.

Step 3: Prepare the Peanut Butter Swirl

In a small bowl, combine the peanut butter and maple syrup. If the peanut butter is too thick, you can gently warm it in the microwave for about 15-20 seconds to make it easier to stir. Once the peanut butter and syrup are well combined, set it aside for swirling.

Step 4: Assemble the Brownies

Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly with a spatula. Then, spoon the peanut butter mixture on top of the brownie batter in dollops. Use a knife or a skewer to swirl the peanut butter into the brownie batter. Don’t worry about making it perfect—just create a marbled effect, as it will look stunning when baked.

Step 5: Bake the Brownies

Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake the brownies for 20-25 minutes. Keep an eye on them—baking times can vary depending on your oven. You’ll know the brownies are done when the edges are set, but the center is still slightly soft. For that perfect fudgy texture, don’t overbake them.

Once they’re done, remove the pan from the oven and let the brownies cool in the pan for at least 15 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely. These brownies are best enjoyed when they’re fully cooled, as this allows them to set properly and become more fudgy.

Step 6: Slice and Enjoy!

Once the brownies are completely cool, slice them into squares. You’ll see that the peanut butter swirl has created a beautiful marbled pattern on top of each brownie, making them as visually stunning as they are delicious. These brownies are rich, fudgy, and full of flavor. Serve them with a glass of non-dairy milk for the ultimate treat.

Tips for the Best Vegan Peanut Butter Brownies

  • Don’t skip the peanut butter swirl! The peanut butter adds richness and a beautiful texture to the brownies. It’s not just for decoration—it’s the secret to the perfect bite.
  • Adjust the sweetness: If you like your brownies on the sweeter side, feel free to add a little more maple syrup or coconut sugar to the batter.
  • Storage: These brownies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 4-5 days. If you want to keep them fresh for longer, you can also refrigerate them. They will stay good for up to a week in the fridge.
  • Freezing: These brownies freeze well! Wrap individual brownies tightly in plastic wrap and store them in a freezer-safe container or bag. They can be frozen for up to 2 months. To thaw, simply leave them at room temperature for 20-30 minutes.

Variations of Vegan Peanut Butter Brownies

While the classic peanut butter swirl is delicious, you can get creative with these brownies by adding different ingredients or flavors. Here are some fun variations to try:

1. Chocolate Chips: Add a handful of dairy-free chocolate chips to the batter for extra chocolatey goodness. You can fold them into the batter or sprinkle them on top before baking.

2. Crunchy Peanut Butter: For added texture, use crunchy peanut butter in both the batter and the swirl. The little peanut chunks will add a delightful bite.

3. Banana: Mash a ripe banana and replace some of the applesauce with it. The banana will add natural sweetness and moisture, creating a unique flavor profile.

4. Coconut Flakes: Add unsweetened shredded coconut to the batter for a tropical twist. The coconut will pair beautifully with the peanut butter and chocolate.

5. Caramel Drizzle: Drizzle some vegan caramel sauce over the brownies just before serving. The caramel adds another layer of richness and pairs wonderfully with the chocolate and peanut butter.

The Benefits of Going Vegan

Baking vegan treats like these peanut butter brownies isn’t just about delicious flavor—it’s also a step towards a more sustainable and ethical lifestyle. Vegan baking eliminates the use of animal products, which helps reduce the environmental impact of food production. Additionally, plant-based ingredients can provide various health benefits, such as lower cholesterol levels, improved digestion, and better overall health.

By making small changes, like opting for vegan recipes, you’re not only making healthier choices for yourself, but you’re also contributing to a more compassionate and eco-friendly world.

Conclusion

These Vegan Peanut Butter Brownies are the ultimate dessert for anyone who loves rich, fudgy, and indulgent sweets. Whether you’re vegan or not, you’ll find it hard to resist the chocolate and peanut butter combination that makes this treat so irresistible. Easy to make and full of flavor, they’re perfect for any occasion, from casual afternoon snacks to special celebrations.

Next time you’re in the mood for a decadent dessert, skip the store-bought options and bake these brownies instead. Trust us—you won’t be disappointed!
