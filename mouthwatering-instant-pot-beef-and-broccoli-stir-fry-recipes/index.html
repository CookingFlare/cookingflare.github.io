Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Recipes


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that is quick and easy to make? Look no further than these mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipes. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have a flavorful and nutritious meal on the table in no time. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Recipe 1: Classic Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, cornstarch, and water. Set aside.
  2. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper.
  3. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil.
  4. Add the beef slices to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside.
  5. Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until slightly tender.
  6. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and pour in the soy sauce mixture.
  7. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position.
  8. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure.
  9. Serve the beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice and enjoy!

Recipe 3: Teriyaki Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
