Mouthwatering Thai Red Curry Stir Fry: A Spicy Delight


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and enjoy a good kick of spiciness, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering Thai Red Curry Stir Fry recipe. Packed with bold flavors and vibrant colors, this dish is a delightful combination of tender vegetables, succulent chicken, and aromatic spices. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or new to Thai cooking, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and elevate your culinary skills.

Ingredients

For the Red Curry Paste:

  • 5-6 dried red chilies, seeds removed
  • 1 shallot, roughly chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass, thinly sliced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of galangal, peeled and chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of shrimp paste
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

For the Stir Fry:

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of palm sugar
  • Fresh Thai basil leaves, for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice, to serve

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Red Curry Paste:

    • In a mortar and pestle, pound the dried red chilies, shallot, garlic, lemongrass, galangal, ground coriander, ground cumin, shrimp paste, and lime zest into a smooth paste.
    • Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add the red curry paste to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.

  2. Cook the Chicken:

    • Push the paste to one side of the pan and add another 2 tablespoons of oil to the empty space.
    • Add the sliced chicken breast and stir-fry until cooked through.

  3. Add the Vegetables:

    • Toss in the julienned red and yellow bell peppers, snap peas, and sliced carrots.
    • Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.

  4. Simmer with Coconut Milk:

    • Pour in the coconut milk, fish sauce, and palm sugar.
    • Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
    • Let the curry simmer for 5-7 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

  5. Finish and Serve:

    • Garnish with fresh Thai basil leaves for an aromatic touch.
    • Serve the Thai Red Curry Stir Fry hot with steamed jasmine rice.

Tips for Success

  • To make this dish vegetarian or vegan, you can substitute the chicken with tofu or an assortment of colorful vegetables like eggplant, zucchini, and bamboo shoots.
  • Adjust the amount of red chilies in the curry paste to suit your preferred level of spiciness.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a dash of lime juice and sprinkle some chopped cilantro before serving.

Final Thoughts

This Thai Red Curry Stir Fry is a celebration of bold flavors and exotic aromas that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand. The rich and creamy coconut milk, combined with the fiery red curry paste, creates a harmonious blend that is both comforting and exhilarating. Whether you’re preparing it for a cozy dinner at home or a gathering with friends, this recipe is a surefire way to impress and delight. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure with this spicy delight from the heart of Thailand.
