BBQ Chicken Salad: Light and Refreshing Fourth of July Recipe

3 min · 552 words

When it comes to summertime gatherings, there’s nothing quite like a good old-fashioned barbecue. The smell of sizzling meat on the grill, the laughter of friends and family, and the anticipation of a delicious meal—it all comes together to create a truly memorable experience. And what better way to celebrate the Fourth of July than with a light and refreshing BBQ Chicken Salad?

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup corn kernels, cooked
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)

BBQ Chicken Marinade

  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

BBQ Chicken Salad Dressing

  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons barbecue sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together all the ingredients for the BBQ chicken marinade. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken, ensuring it is well coated. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  2. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.

  3. While the chicken is resting, prepare the BBQ chicken salad dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, barbecue sauce, honey, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

  4. In a large serving bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, corn kernels, avocado slices, red onion, and cilantro. Toss gently to mix everything together.

  5. Slice the grilled chicken breasts into thin strips and add them to the salad bowl. Drizzle the BBQ chicken salad dressing over the top and toss again to evenly coat the ingredients.

  6. If desired, sprinkle crumbled feta cheese on top for an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Serve the BBQ Chicken Salad immediately and enjoy the vibrant colors, crisp textures, and tangy flavors that come together in this light and refreshing meal.

Final Thoughts

This BBQ Chicken Salad is the perfect dish to celebrate Independence Day with family and friends. It not only showcases the flavors of summer but also provides a healthy and satisfying option for those looking to indulge without feeling weighed down. The combination of smoky grilled chicken, crisp vegetables, creamy avocado, and tangy barbecue dressing creates a harmony of flavors that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and make this light and refreshing BBQ Chicken Salad the star of your Fourth of July feast. Let the flavors of summer shine through and create memories that will last a lifetime. Happy Independence Day!
