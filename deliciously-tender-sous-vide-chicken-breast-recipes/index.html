Deliciously Tender Sous Vide Chicken Breast Recipes

4 min · 694 words

Sous vide cooking has revolutionized the way we prepare chicken breast, resulting in incredibly tender and juicy meat. The precise temperature control of sous vide ensures that the chicken is perfectly cooked every time, without any risk of overcooking or drying out. In this blog post, we will explore three delicious sous vide chicken breast recipes that are guaranteed to impress your family and friends.

Recipe 1: Lemon Herb Sous Vide Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 145°F (63°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, dried thyme, and dried rosemary.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, and lemon juice.
  4. Place the seasoned chicken breasts in a ziplock bag and pour the lemon herb marinade over them.
  5. Seal the bag using the water displacement method, making sure to remove as much air as possible.
  6. Submerge the bag in the preheated water bath and cook for 1.5 to 2 hours.
  7. Once cooked, remove the chicken breasts from the bag and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  8. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and sear the chicken breasts for 1-2 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  9. Serve the lemon herb sous vide chicken breast with your favorite side dish and enjoy!

Recipe 2: Teriyaki Glazed Sous Vide Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)
  • Sliced green onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 150°F (65.5°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger to make the teriyaki glaze.
  3. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then place them in a ziplock bag.
  4. Pour the teriyaki glaze over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are fully coated.
  5. Seal the bag using the water displacement method and place it in the preheated water bath.
  6. Cook the chicken breasts for 1.5 to 2 hours.
  7. Once cooked, remove the chicken breasts from the bag and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  8. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat and sear the chicken breasts for 1-2 minutes on each side to caramelize the teriyaki glaze.
  9. Slice the teriyaki glazed sous vide chicken breast and garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions if desired. Serve with steamed rice or stir-fried vegetables.

Recipe 3: Buffalo Sous Vide Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Blue cheese dressing for serving (optional)
  • Celery sticks for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 150°F (65.5°C).
  2. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over low heat.
  3. Stir in the hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  4. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then place them in a ziplock bag.
  5. Pour the buffalo sauce over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are fully coated.
  6. Seal the bag using the water displacement method and place it in the preheated water bath.
  7. Cook the chicken breasts for 1.5 to 2 hours.
  8. Once cooked, remove the chicken breasts from the bag and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  9. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat and sear the chicken breasts for 1-2 minutes on each side to caramelize the buffalo sauce.
  10. Slice the buffalo sous vide chicken breast and serve with blue cheese dressing and celery sticks for a classic buffalo chicken experience.

With these three delicious sous vide chicken breast recipes, you can elevate your cooking game and impress your loved ones with tender and flavorful chicken. Experiment with different marinades and sauces to create your own signature sous vide chicken dishes. Enjoy the journey of exploring the endless possibilities of sous vide cooking!
