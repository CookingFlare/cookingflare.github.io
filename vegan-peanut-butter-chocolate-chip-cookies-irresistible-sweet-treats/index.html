Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies: Irresistible Sweet Treats


Who doesn’t love a delicious cookie? And when it comes to cookies, there’s something truly special about the combination of peanut butter and chocolate chips. But what if you’re following a vegan diet? Can you still indulge in these delightful treats? The answer is a resounding yes! In this recipe, I’ll show you how to make vegan peanut butter chocolate chip cookies that are so irresistible, you won’t even miss the butter or eggs.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter, melted coconut oil, and brown sugar. Mix until well combined and smooth.

  3. Add the almond milk and vanilla extract to the bowl and mix again until everything is fully incorporated.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing well after each addition. The dough should come together and be slightly sticky.

  6. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  8. Gently press down on each cookie with a fork to create a crisscross pattern.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.

  10. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  11. Once cooled, these vegan peanut butter chocolate chip cookies are ready to be enjoyed! Serve them with a tall glass of almond milk for the ultimate sweet treat.

Tips and Variations

  • For extra crunch, you can add 1/2 cup of chopped roasted peanuts to the cookie dough.
  • If you prefer a chewier texture, slightly underbake the cookies and let them cool completely on the baking sheet.
  • Feel free to use any type of non-dairy milk in this recipe. Almond milk, soy milk, or oat milk all work well.
  • If you’re not a fan of chocolate, you can substitute the chocolate chips with dried fruit, such as raisins or cranberries.

These vegan peanut butter chocolate chip cookies are incredibly delicious and easy to make. They’re the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth, whether you’re following a vegan diet or not. So go ahead, indulge in these irresistible sweet treats and share them with your loved ones. They won’t believe they’re vegan!

Remember to store any leftovers in an airtight container to keep them fresh for several days. But let’s be honest, with cookies this good, they probably won’t last that long.

Enjoy and happy baking!
