10 Cookie Recipes You Can Make with Pantry Staples


Cookies have a magical way of bringing joy into our lives. Whether they’re warm and gooey fresh from the oven or crisp and crumbly, cookies are a delightful treat that can satisfy any sweet tooth. But what if you’re in the middle of a baking spree and realize you’re out of the usual ingredients? Fear not! With a few pantry staples, you can whip up a variety of scrumptious cookies. Here are ten easy cookie recipes that use ingredients you likely already have on hand.

1. Classic Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined.
  6. Roll the dough into balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, flattening them slightly.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.
  8. Allow to cool on a wire rack and enjoy!

2. Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter (creamy or chunky)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the peanut butter, brown sugar, egg, baking soda, and salt until smooth.
  3. Scoop tablespoon-sized balls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  4. Flatten each ball with a fork, creating a crisscross pattern.
  5. Bake for 8-10 minutes until set.
  6. Let them cool and indulge in the rich, nutty flavor!

3. Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, cream the butter and sugars together until fluffy.
  3. Beat in the egg.
  4. In another bowl, mix the flour, oats, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, then fold in the raisins.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Let cool on a wire rack before savoring!

4. No-Bake Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 cups quick oats
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine cocoa powder, sugar, milk, and butter.
  2. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, and let boil for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the oats, vanilla, and salt until well mixed.
  4. Drop spoonfuls onto wax paper and allow to cool and set.
  5. Enjoy these chewy, chocolatey delights!

5. Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream the butter with both sugars until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs and vanilla until incorporated.
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients and fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by tablespoons onto a baking sheet and bake for 9-11 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool before enjoying the gooey goodness!

6. Cinnamon Snickerdoodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cream of tartar, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream together butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla, mixing well.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture.
  5. In a small bowl, mix cinnamon and sugar. Roll dough into balls and coat in the cinnamon sugar mixture.
  6. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes.
  7. Let cool and enjoy the sweet, cinnamon flavor!

7. Almond Joy Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine shredded coconut, almond butter, honey, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. Mix until well combined.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the mixture onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.
  6. Allow to cool before indulging in these sweet treats!

8. Lemon Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, cream together butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Beat in the egg, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
  4. In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Cool on a wire rack and enjoy the refreshing lemon flavor!

9. Pumpkin Spice Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together pumpkin puree, butter, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the egg, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  4. In another bowl, combine baking powder and flour, then gradually add to the wet mixture.
  5. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Let cool before enjoying the cozy flavors of fall!

10. Chocolate Hazelnut Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup hazelnut flour (or ground hazelnuts)
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. In another bowl, combine hazelnut flour, all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto a prepared baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool and savor the nutty chocolate flavor!

Conclusion

Baking cookies using pantry staples can be a fun and rewarding experience. These ten recipes offer a variety of flavors and textures, ensuring there’s something for everyone. Remember, the joy of baking comes not only from the delicious results but also from the creativity and love you put into each batch. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the cookie magic begin!
