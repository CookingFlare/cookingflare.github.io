The Last Word: A Unique and Complex Classic Cocktail


When it comes to classic cocktails, The Last Word stands out as a unique and complex concoction that has stood the test of time. This pre-prohibition era cocktail has experienced a resurgence in popularity in recent years, thanks to its intriguing combination of flavors and its perfect balance of sweet, tart, and herbal notes. In this post, we’ll delve into the history of The Last Word, explore its key ingredients, and provide a step-by-step guide to crafting this timeless libation.

History of The Last Word

The Last Word is believed to have originated at the Detroit Athletic Club around the 1920s, during the Prohibition era. Despite falling into obscurity for several decades, this cocktail was rediscovered in the early 2000s and has since become a beloved classic in the world of mixology. Its revival can be attributed to the cocktail renaissance and the growing interest in pre-prohibition era libations.

Key Ingredients

The beauty of The Last Word lies in its simplicity, yet complexity. This cocktail calls for equal parts of four distinct ingredients, resulting in a harmonious blend of flavors. Here are the key components of The Last Word:

  • Gin: The backbone of The Last Word, gin provides a botanical and juniper-forward base that complements the other ingredients.

  • Green Chartreuse: This herbal liqueur adds a layer of complexity with its blend of 130 different herbs, resulting in a subtly sweet and spicy undertone.

  • Maraschino Liqueur: Made from Marasca cherries, this liqueur contributes a delicate sweetness and a hint of almond flavor to the cocktail.

  • Fresh Lime Juice: The citrusy punch of fresh lime juice provides the perfect balance to the herbal and sweet elements, adding a bright and refreshing note to the mix.

Crafting The Last Word

Now that we’ve explored the history and ingredients of The Last Word, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of creating this exquisite cocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Green Chartreuse
  • 1 oz Maraschino Liqueur
  • 1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • Ice
  • Cocktail shaker
  • Strainer
  • Coupe glass

Instructions:

  1. Fill the cocktail shaker with ice to chill the ingredients.

  2. Pour the gin, Green Chartreuse, Maraschino Liqueur, and fresh lime juice into the shaker.

  3. Vigorously shake the mixture for about 15 seconds to ensure proper dilution and integration of flavors.

  4. Place the strainer over the shaker and strain the cocktail into a chilled coupe glass.

  5. Optionally, garnish with a twist of lime or a Luxardo cherry for an elegant finishing touch.

  6. Sip and savor the balanced symphony of flavors in this timeless classic.

Conclusion

The Last Word is a testament to the artistry of cocktail crafting, showcasing how a few simple ingredients can come together to create something truly extraordinary. Whether you’re an avid cocktail enthusiast or a newcomer to mixology, The Last Word is sure to captivate your taste buds with its intricate blend of botanical, herbal, and citrus flavors. Embrace the legacy of this classic cocktail and savor the experience of crafting and enjoying The Last Word. Cheers to the timeless allure of The Last Word!
