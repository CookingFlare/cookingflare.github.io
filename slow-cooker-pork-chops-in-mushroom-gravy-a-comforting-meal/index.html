Slow Cooker Pork Chops in Mushroom Gravy: A Comforting Meal


Slow cooker meals have a magical appeal—they fill your home with enticing aromas and deliver delicious, tender dishes with minimal effort. One such comforting dish is Slow Cooker Pork Chops in Mushroom Gravy. This recipe not only promises succulent pork chops but also a rich, flavorful gravy that pairs perfectly with mashed potatoes or rice. Let’s dive into how you can create this hearty meal in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 thick-cut pork chops
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork Chops:

    • Season the pork chops generously with salt and pepper.
    • In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat the olive oil. Sear the pork chops until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side. Transfer the pork chops to the slow cooker.

  2. Saute the Aromatics:

    • In the same skillet, add the sliced onions and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
    • Add the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Transfer the onions and garlic to the slow cooker, arranging them around the pork chops.

  3. Make the Mushroom Gravy:

    • In a bowl, mix together the cream of mushroom soup, chicken broth, Worcestershire sauce, dried thyme, and dried rosemary until well combined.
    • Pour the mushroom soup mixture over the pork chops and onions in the slow cooker. Add the sliced mushrooms on top.

  4. Slow Cook:

    • Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the pork chops are tender and fully cooked.

  5. Serve:

    • Once done, carefully remove the pork chops from the slow cooker and place them on a serving platter.
    • Spoon the mushroom gravy over the pork chops and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

  6. Enjoy:

    • Serve the Slow Cooker Pork Chops in Mushroom Gravy hot, alongside mashed potatoes, rice, or your favorite side dish. The tender pork chops and flavorful gravy are sure to be a hit at your dinner table.

Tips for Success

  • Choosing Pork Chops: Opt for thick-cut pork chops as they hold up better to slow cooking without drying out.
  • Searing for Flavor: Searing the pork chops before adding them to the slow cooker enhances their flavor and texture.
  • Adjusting Seasonings: Feel free to adjust the herbs and seasonings to suit your taste preferences.
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup: Use a good quality cream of mushroom soup for the best flavor. You can also use homemade mushroom soup if preferred.

Why Choose Slow Cooking?

Slow cooking is not just convenient; it’s a method that allows flavors to meld together beautifully over time. The low, slow heat tenderizes the pork chops, while the mushroom gravy develops a depth of flavor that’s hard to achieve with quick cooking methods. This makes it an ideal dish for busy days when you want a satisfying meal ready and waiting for you at dinner time.

Make It Your Own

Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your family’s tastes. You can add extra vegetables like carrots or celery to the slow cooker for added nutrition and flavor. Experiment with different herbs or even substitute the pork chops with chicken thighs for a variation. The beauty of slow cooker meals lies in their adaptability and the ease with which you can create delicious, comforting dishes with minimal fuss.

Final Thoughts

Slow Cooker Pork Chops in Mushroom Gravy is more than just a meal; it’s a comforting experience that brings warmth and satisfaction to the table. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a cozy gathering, this dish is sure to impress. With simple ingredients and the convenience of a slow cooker, you can enjoy tender pork chops bathed in a rich mushroom gravy any day of the week.

Try this recipe soon and savor the joy of slow-cooked goodness—it’s a culinary delight that never fails to please. Cheers to hearty meals and happy moments around the dinner table!
