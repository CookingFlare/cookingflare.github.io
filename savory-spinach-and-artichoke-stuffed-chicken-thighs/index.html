Savory Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Chicken Thighs


If you’re looking for a delicious and impressive dish to serve at your next dinner party or family gathering, look no further than these savory spinach and artichoke stuffed chicken thighs. This recipe takes the classic flavors of spinach and artichoke dip and elevates them to create a mouthwatering main course that’s sure to impress your guests. The combination of tender chicken, creamy filling, and flavorful seasoning makes this dish a standout winner.

Ingredients

  • 8 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1 cup chopped artichoke hearts
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Filling

Start by preparing the filling for the chicken thighs. In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, chopped artichoke hearts, cream cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Mix everything together until well combined, and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 2: Prepare the Chicken Thighs

Lay the chicken thighs flat on a clean surface, and season both sides with salt and pepper. Using a sharp knife, carefully make a slit in the center of each chicken thigh to create a pocket for the filling. Be sure not to cut all the way through.

Step 3: Stuff the Chicken Thighs

Using a spoon, carefully stuff each chicken thigh with a generous amount of the spinach and artichoke filling. Be sure to press the filling into the pockets to ensure that each thigh is evenly stuffed.

Step 4: Sear the Chicken Thighs

In a large skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the stuffed chicken thighs to the skillet, and sear them for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they develop a golden-brown crust.

Step 5: Bake the Chicken Thighs

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Transfer the seared chicken thighs to a baking dish, and place them in the preheated oven. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the filling is hot and bubbly.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the chicken thighs are cooked through, remove them from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes. Then, serve the savory spinach and artichoke stuffed chicken thighs with your choice of sides, such as roasted vegetables, rice, or a fresh salad.

Conclusion

These savory spinach and artichoke stuffed chicken thighs are a delightful combination of flavors and textures. The creamy spinach and artichoke filling adds a luxurious touch to the tender chicken, making it a dish that’s perfect for special occasions or everyday indulgence. Whether you’re cooking for family, friends, or just for yourself, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, get into the kitchen, and treat yourself to this delicious and satisfying dish!
