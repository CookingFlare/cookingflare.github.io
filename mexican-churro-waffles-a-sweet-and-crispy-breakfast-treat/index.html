Mexican Churro Waffles: A Sweet and Crispy Breakfast Treat


If you’re looking to add a touch of Mexican flair to your breakfast routine, look no further than Mexican Churro Waffles. These delightful treats combine the classic flavors of churros with the crispy texture of waffles, creating a mouthwatering breakfast experience that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To make these delectable Mexican Churro Waffles, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cooking spray or additional melted butter for greasing the waffle iron

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon. Make sure all the dry ingredients are well combined.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract until the mixture is smooth and well blended.

  3. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

  4. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions. Once heated, lightly grease it with cooking spray or melted butter.

  5. Pour a generous amount of batter onto the center of the waffle iron, spreading it evenly using a spatula or the back of a spoon. Close the waffle iron and cook the waffle until golden brown and crispy, usually about 3-5 minutes.

  6. Carefully remove the cooked waffle from the iron and place it on a wire rack to cool slightly. Repeat the process with the remaining batter until all the waffles are cooked.

  7. While the waffles are still warm, you can sprinkle them with a mixture of cinnamon and sugar for an extra touch of sweetness and that classic churro flavor. Alternatively, you can serve them with a side of warm chocolate sauce or dulce de leche for dipping.

  8. Serve the Mexican Churro Waffles immediately, while they are still warm and crispy. They are best enjoyed fresh off the waffle iron, but you can also store any leftovers in an airtight container and reheat them in a toaster or oven for a quick and delicious breakfast on the go.

Final Thoughts

Mexican Churro Waffles are a delightful twist on the traditional waffle recipe that will transport you to the streets of Mexico with their sweet and crispy goodness. Whether you’re enjoying them on a lazy Sunday morning or serving them at a brunch gathering, these waffles are sure to impress. So grab your waffle iron and get ready to indulge in a truly unique breakfast treat that combines the best of both worlds – churros and waffles!

Note: Remember to be creative with your toppings and feel free to experiment with different flavors. You can try adding a drizzle of caramel sauce, a dollop of whipped cream, or even some fresh berries to take your Mexican Churro Waffles to the next level. The possibilities are endless, so have fun and enjoy!
