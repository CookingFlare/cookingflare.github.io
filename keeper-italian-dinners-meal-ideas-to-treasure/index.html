Keeper Italian Dinners: Meal Ideas to Treasure


Treat your family to a satisfying and authentic Italian meal with these keeper recipes. From hearty pasta dishes to mouth-watering meat options, these Italian dinner ideas are sure to become a beloved staple in your home cooking repertoire.

1. Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz canned tomatoes, crushed
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • Spaghetti pasta

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned.
  3. Drain Fat and set beef aside.
  4. In the same skillet, sauté onion and garlic until translucent.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, and red wine (if using) to the skillet. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  6. Combine cooked beef and sauce, then serve over your cooked spaghetti.

2. Chicken Cacciatore

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup homemade or store-bought marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, mince, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare a breading station by mixing flour and 1/4 teaspoon salt in a shallow dish. In another shallow dish, combine beaten egg and Parmesan.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat, and dredge chicken in the flour mixture. Dredge in the egg mixture, and then fry chicken in hot oil until golden brown on both sides.
  3. In another skillet, cook garlic over medium heat until fragrant. Add marinara sauce and chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer and add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Place fried chicken over the sauce, spooning sauce over the chicken. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

3. Eggplant Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium eggplants, sliced into rounds
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups Italian breadcrumbs
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Non-stick spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and coat eggplant slices with flour. Then, dip in beaten eggs, and finally in breadcrumbs.
  2. Spray both sides of each coated eggplant slice with non-stick spray, and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes, flipping once halfway through, until the eggplant is tender and breadcrumbs are golden brown.
  4. Spoon warm marinara sauce over the bottom of an 8x8 baking dish, and then place a layer of eggplant slices over the sauce.
  5. Scatter shredded mozzarella cheese over the eggplant, and continue layering with more sauce and eggplant, finishing with a final layer of sauce and a generous amount of shredded mozzarella.
  6. Bake the casserole for 20 more minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Allow the dish to cool for a few minutes before serving.

These keeper Italian dinner recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and become the cherished meals at your dinner table. As you learn and perfect these authentic Italian dishes, enjoy sitting down with your friends and family, creating memories to treasure. Buon Appetito!
