Savory Breakfast Recipes and the Wines That Go With Them


When it comes to breakfast, we often think of sweet options like pancakes, waffles, and pastries. However, savory breakfast dishes can be just as satisfying and delicious, especially when paired with the right wine. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering savory breakfast recipes and the perfect wines to complement them. Whether you’re hosting a brunch or simply want to elevate your breakfast game, these recipes and wine pairings are sure to impress.

Spinach and Feta Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the pie crust in a pie dish and crimp the edges.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the spinach, feta cheese, and red onion to the egg mixture and stir to combine.
  5. Pour the mixture into the pie crust.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the quiche is set and golden brown.

Wine Pairing: A crisp and refreshing Sauvignon Blanc complements the creamy texture of the quiche and enhances the flavors of the spinach and feta. The wine’s citrus notes provide a delightful contrast to the savory elements of the dish.

Smoked Salmon and Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, mashed
  • 4 oz smoked salmon
  • 1 tablespoon capers
  • Fresh dill for garnish
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions:

  1. Toast the whole grain bread until golden and crispy.
  2. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto each slice of toast.
  3. Divide the smoked salmon among the toasts, placing it on top of the avocado.
  4. Sprinkle capers over the salmon and garnish with fresh dill.
  5. Serve with a wedge of lemon for squeezing over the toast before eating.

Wine Pairing: A dry Rosé wine is an excellent choice to accompany the rich, buttery texture of the avocado and the smoky, salty flavor of the salmon. The wine’s acidity and fruitiness provide a lovely balance to the dish, making it a delightful breakfast option.

Mushroom and Gruyère Frittata

Ingredients:

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup Gruyère cheese, grated
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the broiler in the oven.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat an ovenproof skillet over medium heat and add the mushrooms, cooking until they are golden brown and tender.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the mushrooms and cook for 3-4 minutes, lifting the edges to allow the uncooked eggs to flow underneath.
  5. Sprinkle the Gruyère cheese and fresh thyme over the top of the frittata.
  6. Place the skillet under the broiler for 3-4 minutes or until the frittata is set and the cheese is golden and bubbly.

Wine Pairing: A light-bodied Pinot Noir complements the earthy flavors of the mushrooms and the richness of the Gruyère cheese. The wine’s soft tannins and red fruit notes enhance the savory elements of the frittata, making it a delightful pairing for a leisurely breakfast.

Conclusion

Savory breakfast recipes offer a delightful departure from the ordinary, and when paired with the right wines, they become a truly extraordinary dining experience. Whether you’re a brunch enthusiast or simply enjoy starting your day with a culinary adventure, these recipes and wine pairings are certain to elevate your breakfast game. So, why not indulge in a leisurely morning meal with a glass of perfectly paired wine and savor the delightful flavors that await? Cheers to a savory start to the day!
