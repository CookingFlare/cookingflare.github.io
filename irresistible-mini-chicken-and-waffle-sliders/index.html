Irresistible Mini Chicken and Waffle Sliders


If you’re a fan of the sweet and savory combination, then you’re in for a treat with these irresistible mini chicken and waffle sliders. This delightful dish brings together the crispy, tender goodness of fried chicken with the soft, fluffy texture of waffles, creating a mouthwatering medley of flavors and textures that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Waffles:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • Cooking spray

For the Assembly:

  • Maple syrup
  • Honey mustard sauce
  • Pickles

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into small, bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place the chicken pieces in a bowl and pour the buttermilk over them. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes to overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, salt, pepper, and paprika.
  4. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep skillet or fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Dredge the marinated chicken pieces in the seasoned flour mixture, making sure to coat them evenly.
  6. Fry the chicken in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 6-8 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

Step 2: Make the Waffles

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and then stir in the milk and melted butter.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Lightly coat the waffle iron with cooking spray.
  6. Pour the batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden brown and crisp.

Step 3: Assemble the Sliders

  1. Cut the waffles into small, slider-sized squares.
  2. Place a piece of fried chicken on top of a waffle square.
  3. Drizzle with maple syrup and honey mustard sauce.
  4. Top with another waffle square and secure with a toothpick.
  5. Garnish with a pickle on top.

Serving Suggestions

These mini chicken and waffle sliders are perfect for brunch, game day gatherings, or any casual get-together. Serve them with extra maple syrup and honey mustard sauce on the side for dipping.

Conclusion

These mini chicken and waffle sliders are a delightful twist on the classic comfort food. The combination of crispy, juicy chicken and fluffy waffles, along with the sweet and savory toppings, creates a flavor explosion that is hard to resist. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking to indulge in a fun and delicious meal, these sliders are sure to be a hit with everyone. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to enjoy the irresistible goodness of these mini chicken and waffle sliders!
