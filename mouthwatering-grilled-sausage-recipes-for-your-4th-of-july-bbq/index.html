Mouthwatering Grilled Sausage Recipes for Your 4th of July BBQ


The 4th of July is synonymous with fireworks, family gatherings, and, of course, delicious food. What better way to celebrate than by firing up the grill and making some mouthwatering sausages? Whether you’re a grilling novice or a seasoned pro, sausages are the perfect dish for your BBQ because they are easy to prepare, flavorful, and always a crowd-pleaser. In this post, we’ll walk you through some fantastic grilled sausage recipes that will make your 4th of July BBQ unforgettable.

Why Sausages are Perfect for Grilling

Before diving into the recipes, let’s take a moment to understand why sausages make such a great choice for grilling. They are incredibly versatile, coming in various flavors, textures, and types. From classic pork sausages to spicy chicken and turkey varieties, there’s something for everyone. The best part? Sausages cook quickly and evenly on the grill, making them an ideal option for feeding a crowd.

Grilling sausages also allows you to infuse them with smoky, charred flavors that enhance their natural taste. Whether you’re serving them in a bun or alongside grilled veggies, sausages never disappoint. Let’s explore some exciting ways to serve them at your 4th of July celebration!

1. Classic Grilled Bratwurst

Ingredients:

  • 4 bratwurst sausages
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 hoagie rolls
  • Mustard and sauerkraut for serving

Directions:

  1. Preheat the Grill: Set your grill to medium heat. Brush the grates with olive oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Cook the Bratwurst: Place the bratwurst sausages on the grill, turning them occasionally for even cooking. Grill for about 15-20 minutes or until they reach an internal temperature of 160°F.
  3. Sauté the Veggies: While the bratwurst cooks, melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and bell peppers, sautéing them until they soften and caramelize, about 10 minutes.
  4. Assemble the Sandwiches: Once the bratwurst are fully cooked, place them on the hoagie rolls. Top with the sautéed onions and bell peppers. Add mustard and sauerkraut for extra flavor.
  5. Serve and Enjoy: Serve your bratwurst hot off the grill with your favorite BBQ sides.

The rich, savory flavor of bratwurst combined with the sweet and tangy onions and peppers creates a perfect combination for any BBQ. The addition of mustard and sauerkraut brings a classic touch that will remind your guests of a traditional German feast.

2. Spicy Chicken Sausage with Grilled Pineapple

Ingredients:

  • 4 spicy chicken sausages
  • 1 fresh pineapple, peeled and cut into rings
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges for serving

Directions:

  1. Prepare the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium heat. Brush the pineapple rings with a little olive oil and season with chili powder and smoked paprika.
  2. Grill the Sausages: Place the spicy chicken sausages on the grill. Cook them for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking. The sausages should have an internal temperature of 165°F.
  3. Grill the Pineapple: While the sausages are cooking, grill the pineapple rings for about 2-3 minutes per side until they are lightly charred.
  4. Assemble and Serve: Once everything is cooked, place the sausages on a platter. Top with the grilled pineapple rings and sprinkle with fresh cilantro. Serve with lime wedges for an added zesty kick.

The spicy chicken sausages pair wonderfully with the sweetness of the grilled pineapple. This recipe is a perfect balance of savory, sweet, and spicy, making it a must-try for your 4th of July BBQ.

3. Italian Sausage with Roasted Red Peppers and Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 4 Italian sausages (sweet or spicy)
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup pesto sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 4 Italian rolls or sub buns

Directions:

  1. Grill the Sausages: Preheat the grill to medium heat. Grill the Italian sausages for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally until they are fully cooked.
  2. Roast the Red Peppers: While the sausages are grilling, place the red peppers on the grill. Turn them every few minutes until they are charred on all sides. Once done, remove from the grill and let them cool. Peel off the skins and remove the seeds.
  3. Assemble the Sandwiches: Slice the roasted red peppers and set them aside. Once the sausages are cooked, place them in the Italian rolls. Top with the roasted red peppers and drizzle pesto sauce over the sausages.
  4. Serve: Serve your Italian sausage sandwiches with a side of crispy fries or a fresh salad.

This combination of juicy Italian sausages, smoky roasted peppers, and fragrant pesto is sure to impress your guests. It’s a bold and flavorful option for your BBQ spread.

4. Grilled Sausages with Beer and Mustard Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 4 pork sausages (or any variety of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup beer (lager or pale ale)
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Directions:

  1. Prepare the Glaze: In a small saucepan, combine the beer, Dijon mustard, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, and butter. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring frequently. Let it cook for about 5-7 minutes until the glaze thickens slightly.
  2. Grill the Sausages: Preheat the grill to medium heat. Grill the sausages for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until they reach an internal temperature of 160°F.
  3. Glaze the Sausages: During the last few minutes of grilling, brush the sausages with the beer and mustard glaze, allowing the sauce to caramelize and coat the sausages.
  4. Serve: Once the sausages are done, remove them from the grill and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Serve with a side of roasted potatoes or grilled vegetables.

The beer and mustard glaze gives these sausages a rich, tangy flavor with a touch of sweetness. It’s a simple yet delicious recipe that will elevate any BBQ spread.

5. Veggie Sausage with Zesty Lemon and Herb Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 4 vegetarian sausages (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Grill the Veggie Sausages: Preheat the grill to medium heat. Grill the vegetarian sausages for about 10 minutes, turning occasionally until heated through and lightly browned.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, honey, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Serve: Once the veggie sausages are grilled, drizzle the zesty lemon and herb sauce over them. Serve with a light cucumber salad or grilled asparagus for a fresh and healthy BBQ option.

This veggie sausage recipe is light, fresh, and full of flavor. The lemon and herb sauce adds a refreshing zing that complements the savory sausages perfectly.

Grilled Sausages: A 4th of July Tradition

No matter which sausage recipe you choose, grilling sausages is a fantastic way to celebrate the 4th of July. They are simple, flavorful, and perfect for sharing with friends and family. Whether you’re serving bratwurst with grilled onions, spicy chicken sausages with pineapple, or even a vegetarian option, your guests are sure to be impressed.

Be sure to complement your sausages with classic BBQ sides like potato salad, coleslaw, and baked beans. And don’t forget the drinks! A cold beer or refreshing lemonade pairs perfectly with grilled sausages.

Tips for Grilling Perfect Sausages

  • Don’t Overcook: Sausages are best when cooked to the right internal temperature. Use a meat thermometer to ensure your sausages are cooked through but not dried out.
  • Grill with Indirect Heat: For sausages with a thicker casing, consider using indirect heat for part of the cooking process to avoid burning the outside while ensuring the inside is fully cooked.
  • Use a Flavorful Marinade: For added depth of flavor, marinate your sausages for an hour or two before grilling. This helps infuse the meat with additional spices and herbs.
  • Let Them Rest: Once your sausages are done, let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute and ensures a moist bite.

Conclusion

Sausages are a classic choice for any BBQ, and with these grilled sausage recipes, your 4th of July celebration will be a hit. From classic bratwurst to spicy chicken sausage with grilled pineapple, there are endless ways to make your sausages stand out. With just a few simple ingredients and a hot grill, you can create a memorable BBQ feast that everyone will enjoy.

So fire up the grill, invite your friends and family, and get ready for a delicious 4th of July BBQ filled with mouthwatering grilled sausages!
