Tasty Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy low-carb dish, then you’re in for a treat! This Tasty Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus recipe is not only easy to make but also bursting with flavor. The combination of succulent shrimp, crisp asparagus, and the zesty flavors of lemon and garlic make this dish a winner for any occasion. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply want a light and satisfying meal, this recipe has got you covered.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound of fresh asparagus, trimmed
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp and Asparagus: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning them with salt, pepper, and paprika. Set them aside while you prepare the asparagus.

  2. Cook the Asparagus: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the trimmed asparagus spears to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the asparagus is tender but still crisp. Once cooked, transfer the asparagus to a plate and cover to keep warm.

  3. Cook the Shrimp: In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the minced garlic and crushed red pepper flakes, cooking for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.

  4. Add the Lemon Sauce: Once the shrimp are cooked, pour in the fresh lemon juice and chicken broth. Stir the sauce and let it simmer for a couple of minutes until it slightly thickens.

  5. Combine and Serve: Return the cooked asparagus to the skillet, tossing it with the lemon garlic butter sauce and shrimp. Ensure everything is well coated with the flavorful sauce. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 280
  • Total Fat: 14g
  • Cholesterol: 260mg
  • Sodium: 680mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 7g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Protein: 30g

This Tasty Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus recipe serves 4 and can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a side salad or a serving of cauliflower rice for a complete low-carb meal. It’s perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or for entertaining guests. The vibrant colors, bold flavors, and healthy ingredients make it a standout dish that’s sure to impress.

So, the next time you’re craving a delicious and nutritious meal, give this recipe a try. It’s a fantastic way to enjoy the flavors of shrimp and asparagus while keeping your carb intake in check. With just a few simple ingredients and easy steps, you’ll have a restaurant-worthy dish right in your own kitchen. Enjoy!
