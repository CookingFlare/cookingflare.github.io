Fresh and Flavorful: Pico de Gallo Recipe


If you’re looking for a simple yet delicious recipe that packs a flavorful punch, look no further than homemade pico de gallo! Originating in Mexican cuisine, pico de gallo, also known as “salsa fresca,” is a colorful and tasty addition to your dining table. The recipe is beginner-friendly and yields a loyal following once you master the art of making it.

What Is Pico de Gallo?

Pico de gallo is a fresh and crunchy tomato-based salsa, made with fresh ingredients such as tomatoes, onions, jalapeño peppers, and fresh cilantro. It is a versatile condiment which is perfect as a topping for tortilla chips, grilled fish or poultry, a fresh accompaniment to breakfast burritos or a topping for your favorite Mexican dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium-sized tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1-2 jalapeno peppers, de-seeded and diced (adjust the number to your taste, I like 2 for an extra kick)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice (be sure to use pure lime juice, not lime juice with additives)
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, start by adding the tomatoes and onion. Mix well.

  2. Next, add the jalapeño peppers and cilantro, stirring them into the mixture.

  3. Add the fresh lime juice and incorporate everything thoroughly.

  4. Finally, sprinkle in the salt and ground black pepper.

  5. Blend everything thoroughly, combining all ingredients well. It’s best to let the pico de gallo rest for a few hours before serving. This allows all the flavors to meld together.

Once you’ve mastered this recipe, feel free to experiment! Try adding diced cucumbers, bell peppers, or even mangoes for a tropical twist. You can adjust the heat level by adding or decreasing the jalapeño amount or trying different peppers altogether.

Why Make Homemade Pico de Gallo?

While it’s easy to buy pre-made pico de gallo at the grocery store, making it at home offers several benefits.

First, homemade pico de gallo tastes better! This homemade version offers more control over the ingredients, from the combination of peppers to the choice of fresh produce.

Another reason to create your pico de gallo is to avoid additives and preservatives found in store-bought versions. This homemade version is full of wholesome, fresh vegetables, and you control every element that goes in it!

Healthy Seasonal Eating with Pico de Gallo

Tomatoes—the star ingredient in pico de gallo—are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants. Moreover, adding ingredients such as cilantro and jalapeño peppers offers a wide array of beneficial nutrients. Making pico de gallo offers an impressive, farm-to-table eating experience while utilizing seasonal ingredients like tomatoes and peppers.

No More Boring Dishes!

Pico de gallo adds spice, tang, and crunch to any dish. From scrambled eggs to grilled meats, you’ll find it adds an element of flavor and excitement you never knew your dishes were missing. Give this easy and flavorful recipe a try and make your meals more delicious than ever!
