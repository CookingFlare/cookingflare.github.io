Vegan Raspberry Coconut Cupcakes: A Tropical Twist


If you’re looking for a delightful treat that combines the sweetness of raspberries with the tropical flavor of coconut, then these Vegan Raspberry Coconut Cupcakes are just what you need. This recipe is a perfect fusion of tangy raspberries and creamy coconut, creating a mouthwatering dessert that’s not only delicious but also vegan-friendly. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan baker or just starting out on your plant-based baking journey, these cupcakes are sure to impress your taste buds and your guests.

Ingredients:

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries

For the Coconut Frosting:

  • 1 can of coconut cream, refrigerated overnight
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

For the Raspberry Drizzle:

  • 1/2 cup fresh raspberries
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.

  2. Prepare the Batter: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt. In a separate bowl, combine the coconut milk, coconut oil, apple cider vinegar, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until well combined. Gently fold in the fresh raspberries.

  3. Fill the Cupcake Liners: Divide the batter evenly among the lined cupcake tin, filling each liner about 2/3 full.

  4. Bake the Cupcakes: Place the cupcake tin in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Once baked, remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool completely.

  5. Prepare the Coconut Frosting: While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the coconut frosting. Scoop the solidified coconut cream from the refrigerated can into a mixing bowl, leaving behind any liquid. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, then whip the mixture until smooth and creamy.

  6. Frost the Cupcakes: Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, pipe or spread the coconut frosting onto the cupcakes using a spatula or piping bag.

  7. Make the Raspberry Drizzle: In a small saucepan, combine the fresh raspberries, maple syrup, and cornstarch. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the raspberries break down and the mixture thickens to a drizzling consistency.

  8. Drizzle and Serve: Drizzle the raspberry mixture over the frosted cupcakes, allowing it to cascade down the sides for a visually appealing finish.

Serving Suggestions:

These Vegan Raspberry Coconut Cupcakes are best enjoyed fresh but can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Serve them at your next gathering or simply indulge in them as a delightful afternoon snack. The combination of tart raspberries and creamy coconut is sure to transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise.

Final Thoughts:

With their vibrant flavors and moist, tender crumb, these Vegan Raspberry Coconut Cupcakes are a delightful departure from traditional cupcakes. By using plant-based ingredients, you can enjoy a guilt-free indulgence that’s as delicious as it is compassionate. Whether you’re vegan, experimenting with vegan baking, or simply love the flavors of raspberry and coconut, this recipe is a must-try. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to embark on a tropical baking adventure with these delectable vegan treats!
