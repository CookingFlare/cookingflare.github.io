Satisfying Gluten-Free Pasta Salad Recipes


When it comes to summer picnics or light and refreshing meals, pasta salads are always a crowd-pleaser. But what if you or someone you know follows a gluten-free diet? Don’t worry, because we’ve got you covered! In this post, we will share some delicious and satisfying gluten-free pasta salad recipes that will leave everyone asking for seconds. So let’s dive right in!

1. Greek Quinoa Pasta Salad

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free quinoa pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the quinoa pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and parsley.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss to combine.
  4. Let the salad sit in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!

2. Caprese Gluten-Free Pasta Salad

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free fusilli pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella balls, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fusilli pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella balls, and basil leaves.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss to combine.
  4. Let the salad sit in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!

3. Thai Peanut Gluten-Free Pasta Salad

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free spaghetti
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons gluten-free soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked spaghetti, shredded carrots, red bell pepper, scallions, cilantro, and chopped peanuts.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the sesame oil, soy sauce, lime juice, honey, peanut butter, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss to combine.
  4. Let the salad sit in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Conclusion

There you have it – three delicious and satisfying gluten-free pasta salad recipes that are sure to please even the pickiest eaters. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply looking for a light and refreshing meal, these recipes are a must-try. So go ahead, whip up one of these pasta salads, and enjoy a flavorful and gluten-free dish. Bon appétit!
