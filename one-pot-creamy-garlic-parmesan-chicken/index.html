One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup, then you’re in for a treat with this One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken recipe. This dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to whip up something special without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of tender chicken, rich and creamy sauce, and savory garlic and parmesan flavors will have your taste buds singing with delight. So, let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.

  2. In a large, deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the seasoned chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside on a plate.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and let it simmer for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Next, add the heavy cream, grated parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning to the skillet, stirring until the cheese has melted and the sauce has thickened slightly.

  6. Return the cooked chicken breasts to the skillet, coating them with the creamy garlic parmesan sauce. Let the chicken simmer in the sauce for a few minutes to soak up all the flavors.

  7. Once the chicken is heated through and the sauce has reached your desired consistency, remove the skillet from the heat.

  8. Garnish the dish with fresh chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  9. Serve the One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken hot, accompanied by your favorite side dishes such as pasta, rice, or a crisp green salad.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by incorporating your favorite herbs and spices, such as thyme, oregano, or basil.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can simmer it for a bit longer until it reaches the desired consistency.

Final Thoughts

This One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken recipe is a true winner when it comes to simplicity and flavor. With just one pot, you can create a restaurant-quality dish that will impress your family and friends. The creamy garlic parmesan sauce perfectly complements the tender chicken, making every bite a delight for the senses. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a comforting meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your skillet, and get ready to savor the irresistible taste of this delightful dish!
