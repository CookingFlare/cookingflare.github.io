Savory and Satisfying Sausage and Mushroom Penne: A Hearty Meal


Are you tired of the same old pasta dishes? Looking for something hearty, flavorful, and oh-so-satisfying? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll be combining the rich flavors of sausage and mushrooms with penne pasta to create a dish that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of penne pasta
  • 1 pound of Italian sausage, casing removed
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Break the sausage into small pieces as it cooks.
  3. Remove the cooked sausage from the skillet and set aside. Leave about 1 tablespoon of the rendered fat in the skillet.
  4. In the same skillet, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.
  5. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they have softened and released their moisture.
  6. Return the cooked sausage to the skillet and mix well with the onion, garlic, and mushrooms.
  7. Pour in the diced tomatoes and chicken broth. Stir in the dried oregano, basil, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  9. Add the cooked penne pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until the pasta is well coated with the sauce.
  10. Serve the sausage and mushroom penne in bowls, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.

Enjoy the Flavorful Delight!

This savory and satisfying sausage and mushroom penne dish is perfect for those cozy nights when you’re craving a hearty meal. The combination of the Italian sausage, earthy mushrooms, and aromatic herbs creates a symphony of flavors that will transport your taste buds to a whole new level.

The penne pasta provides the perfect vehicle to soak up all the delicious sauce, ensuring that every bite is bursting with flavor. The addition of grated Parmesan cheese adds a touch of creaminess and a hint of saltiness, while the fresh basil leaves bring a refreshing note to the dish.

Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party with friends, this recipe is sure to impress. Serve it with a side of garlic bread and a crisp salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and flavorful pasta dish, give this sausage and mushroom penne a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember to share this recipe with your fellow food enthusiasts and spread the joy of a hearty, savory meal. Happy cooking!
