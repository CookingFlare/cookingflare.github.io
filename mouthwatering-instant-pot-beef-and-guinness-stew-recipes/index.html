Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Guinness Stew Recipes


If you’re craving a hearty and flavorful dish that will warm your soul, look no further than these mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Guinness Stew recipes. Whether you’re a busy professional or a home cook looking for a delicious and convenient meal, these recipes are sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Instant Pot Beef and Guinness Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup Guinness beer
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and heat the olive oil. Add the beef cubes and brown them on all sides. Remove the beef and set it aside.

  2. Add the chopped onion to the Instant Pot and sauté until it becomes translucent. Add the garlic, carrots, and celery, and cook for a few minutes.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir to coat. This will help thicken the stew.

  4. Pour in the beef broth and Guinness beer, and stir to deglaze the bottom of the pot. Add the tomato paste, bay leaves, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Return the beef to the pot and give everything a good stir.

  5. Close the Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 35 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before doing a quick release.

  6. Open the Instant Pot and give the stew a final stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve the stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Instant Pot Beef and Guinness Stew with Root Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 4 parsnips, sliced
  • 2 turnips, diced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup Guinness beer
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh chives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and heat the vegetable oil. Brown the beef cubes in batches, then remove them and set aside.

  2. Add the chopped onion to the pot and sauté until it softens. Stir in the garlic, carrots, parsnips, and turnips, and cook for a few minutes.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir to combine. This will help thicken the stew as it cooks.

  4. Pour in the beef broth and Guinness beer, and stir to deglaze the bottom of the pot. Add the tomato paste, bay leaves, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Return the beef to the pot and give everything a good stir.

  5. Close the Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 40 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before doing a quick release.

  6. Open the Instant Pot and give the stew a final stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve the stew hot, garnished with chopped fresh chives.

Both of these Instant Pot Beef and Guinness Stew recipes are perfect for cozy evenings or entertaining guests. The rich flavors of the beef, vegetables, and Guinness beer come together beautifully in the Instant Pot, resulting in a dish that is both comforting and impressive. Whether you choose the classic version or the one with root vegetables, these recipes are sure to please the palate and become a favorite in your home.

So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy the delightful aroma and incredible taste of these mouthwatering Beef and Guinness Stew recipes. Your family and friends will thank you, and your taste buds will be in for a treat!
