Savory Keto Chicken Alfredo Spaghetti Squash Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly meal, look no further. This Savory Keto Chicken Alfredo Spaghetti Squash recipe is a flavorful twist on a classic dish, and it’s perfect for those following a low-carb or ketogenic lifestyle. With the creamy richness of alfredo sauce, the tenderness of chicken, and the lightness of spaghetti squash, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe rotation.

Ingredients

For the Spaghetti Squash:

  • 1 medium spaghetti squash
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

For the Chicken Alfredo:

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Spaghetti Squash: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut the spaghetti squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds. Drizzle the cut sides with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Place the squash halves cut-side down on a baking sheet and roast for 40-50 minutes, or until the flesh is tender and easily shreds into spaghetti-like strands with a fork.

  2. Cook the Chicken: While the squash is roasting, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Make the Alfredo Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Combine and Serve: Once the spaghetti squash is done, use a fork to scrape the flesh into spaghetti-like strands. Add the cooked chicken to the skillet with the alfredo sauce and stir to combine. Place a generous serving of spaghetti squash on a plate and top with the chicken alfredo mixture. Garnish with fresh parsley and additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Nutritional Information

This Savory Keto Chicken Alfredo Spaghetti Squash dish is not only delicious but also keto-friendly. Here’s the approximate nutritional breakdown per serving:

  • Calories: 480
  • Fat: 35g
  • Protein: 28g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fiber: 2g
  • Net Carbs: 8g

Conclusion

With its rich, creamy sauce, tender chicken, and flavorful spaghetti squash, this Savory Keto Chicken Alfredo dish is a winning combination for anyone following a keto lifestyle. It’s a satisfying and indulgent meal that will leave you feeling completely satisfied without the guilt of high-carb pasta. Whether you’re new to keto or a seasoned pro, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to enjoy a comforting and flavorful meal while staying on track with their low-carb goals. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every bite of this delectable keto-friendly dish.
