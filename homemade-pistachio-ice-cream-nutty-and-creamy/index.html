Homemade Pistachio Ice Cream: Nutty and Creamy


If you’re a fan of creamy, nutty desserts, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to share with you a delicious recipe for homemade pistachio ice cream. This frozen delight is packed with the rich flavor of pistachios and has a velvety smooth texture that will leave you craving for more. So, let’s dive right in and learn how to make this delectable dessert!

Ingredients

To make this homemade pistachio ice cream, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of shelled pistachios
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Start by toasting the pistachios. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Spread the pistachios on a baking sheet and toast them for about 8-10 minutes until they become fragrant and slightly golden. Let them cool completely.

  2. Once the pistachios are cool, transfer them to a food processor or blender and pulse until they are finely ground. You can leave some larger pieces if you prefer a chunkier texture.

  3. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, milk, and half of the sugar. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring occasionally.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and the remaining sugar until the mixture becomes pale and creamy.

  5. Slowly pour the hot cream mixture into the bowl with the egg yolks, whisking constantly to temper the eggs and prevent them from curdling.

  6. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of the spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  7. Remove the saucepan from heat and strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl to remove any lumps.

  8. Stir in the vanilla extract, ground pistachios, and a pinch of salt. Mix well until everything is fully incorporated.

  9. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, ensuring it touches the surface of the mixture to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate the mixture for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld together.

  10. Once chilled, transfer the pistachio ice cream base to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  11. Transfer the ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for an additional 4-6 hours, or until firm.

  12. When you’re ready to serve, scoop the homemade pistachio ice cream into bowls or cones and garnish with a sprinkle of crushed pistachios for an extra crunch.

And there you have it – a luscious, homemade pistachio ice cream that’s nutty, creamy, and utterly delicious! This frozen treat is perfect for hot summer days or anytime you’re craving a refreshing dessert. Serve it on its own or pair it with your favorite desserts like warm brownies or freshly baked cookies for an indulgent treat. Enjoy!

*Note: Remember to adjust the recipe if you have any dietary restrictions or preferences. You can use alternative milk or sugar substitutes to suit your needs.
