Crunchy Low Carb Zucchini Fries


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional french fries, look no further than these crunchy low carb zucchini fries. This recipe takes the humble zucchini to a whole new level, transforming it into a crispy, flavorful snack that’s perfect for dipping or enjoying on its own. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking to incorporate more veggies into your meals, these zucchini fries are sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium zucchinis
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Wash the zucchinis and cut them into fry-shaped sticks, about 1/2 inch wide and 3 inches long.

  3. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs together.

  4. In another shallow bowl, combine the almond flour, parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.

  5. Dip each zucchini stick into the egg mixture, allowing any excess to drip off, then coat it in the almond flour mixture, pressing gently to adhere. Place the coated zucchini stick on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat until all zucchini sticks are coated.

  6. Bake the zucchini fries in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are golden and crispy.

  7. Once done, remove the zucchini fries from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

These crunchy low carb zucchini fries are delicious on their own, but they also pair perfectly with your favorite dipping sauces. Try serving them with marinara sauce, ranch dressing, or a creamy garlic aioli for a flavorful twist. You can also sprinkle some fresh parsley or grated parmesan cheese on top for an extra burst of flavor.

Nutritional Information:

One of the best things about these zucchini fries is that they are not only delicious but also low in carbs and packed with nutrients. Zucchinis are rich in antioxidants, vitamin C, and fiber, making them a great addition to any well-balanced diet. By using almond flour instead of traditional breadcrumbs, this recipe keeps the carb count low while adding a nutty flavor and extra crunch.

Final Thoughts:

Crunchy low carb zucchini fries are a versatile and satisfying snack that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re serving them as an appetizer at a party or enjoying them as a healthier side dish with your favorite meal, these zucchini fries are sure to be a hit. Experiment with different seasonings and dipping sauces to customize the flavor to your liking. With their crispy exterior and tender interior, these zucchini fries are a delightful way to enjoy a classic snack while staying true to your health goals. Give this recipe a try and discover a new way to love zucchinis!
