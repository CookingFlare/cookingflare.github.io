Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wings: Spicy and Tangy


If you’re a fan of spicy and tangy flavors, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to dive into the world of Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wings. This classic dish is a favorite at parties, game nights, and family gatherings, and for a good reason – it’s irresistibly delicious. The best part? You can easily make these mouthwatering wings in your slow cooker, allowing the flavors to meld together perfectly while you attend to other tasks.

Ingredients

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to whip up these delectable Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wings:

  • 3 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1 cup of hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of honey (optional, for a touch of sweetness)
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing, for serving
  • Celery sticks, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preparation: Begin by preparing your slow cooker. If desired, you can line the bottom with aluminum foil for easier cleanup. Then, place the chicken wings in the slow cooker.

  2. Sauce: In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the hot sauce, unsalted butter, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, salt, and honey (if using). Stir the mixture until the butter is melted and the ingredients are well combined.

  3. Pour and Coat: Pour the sauce over the chicken wings in the slow cooker, ensuring that the wings are evenly coated.

  4. Cooking: Cover the slow cooker and cook the wings on low heat for 4-5 hours, or on high heat for 2-3 hours. The wings should be tender and fully cooked.

  5. Broiling (Optional): For an extra crispy finish, preheat your oven to a high broil. Carefully transfer the cooked wings to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, then broil them for a few minutes until they achieve the desired level of crispiness.

  6. Serve: Once the wings are ready, serve them hot with a side of ranch or blue cheese dressing, and celery sticks for a refreshing crunch.

Tips and Variations

  • Spice Level: Adjust the amount of hot sauce to suit your spice preference. You can also add a pinch of cayenne pepper for an extra kick.
  • Honey: The touch of honey in the sauce can help balance out the heat, but feel free to omit it if you prefer a more traditional buffalo flavor.
  • Garnish: Sprinkle some chopped fresh parsley or green onions on top of the wings for a pop of color and freshness.

Conclusion

There you have it – a simple yet incredibly flavorful recipe for Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wings. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a delicious snack, these wings are sure to be a hit. The slow cooker method ensures that the chicken becomes tender and juicy, while the bold flavors of the buffalo sauce shine through with every bite. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy some seriously tasty wings! Cheers to good food and great company!
