10 Delicious Fall Recipes for Your Instant Pot


As the leaves turn and the air becomes crisp, fall brings with it a bounty of flavors that are perfect for cozy meals. The Instant Pot, a versatile kitchen appliance, makes it easier than ever to whip up hearty dishes that celebrate the season. From comforting soups to savory stews, here are ten delicious fall recipes that you can prepare in your Instant Pot, ensuring you savor every moment of this beautiful season.

1. Instant Pot Pumpkin Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add a drizzle of olive oil and sauté the chopped onion until translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  2. Add Ingredients: Stir in the pumpkin puree, vegetable broth, coconut milk, ginger, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Release Pressure: Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release.
  5. Blend: Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Adjust seasoning as needed and serve warm.

This creamy pumpkin soup is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, making it a perfect starter for your fall meals.

2. Instant Pot Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 3 potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Beef: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add olive oil and brown the beef cubes in batches. Remove and set aside.
  2. Cook the Vegetables: In the same pot, add the onion and garlic, sautéing until fragrant. Stir in the tomato paste, thyme, and rosemary.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Return the beef to the pot, add the carrots, potatoes, and beef broth. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  5. Release Pressure: Allow for a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.

This beef stew is a classic fall dish that warms the soul and fills the belly, perfect for chilly evenings.

3. Instant Pot Apple Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Apples: Place the sliced apples in the bottom of the Instant Pot. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
  2. Make the Crisp Topping: In a bowl, combine oats, brown sugar, flour, and melted butter. Mix until crumbly.
  3. Layer the Topping: Spread the oat mixture over the apples in the Instant Pot.
  4. Add Water: Pour the water around the edges of the pot to create steam.
  5. Pressure Cook: Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  6. Release Pressure: Perform a quick release and serve warm, ideally with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

This apple crisp is a delightful dessert that captures the essence of fall, making it a must-try for any gathering.

4. Instant Pot Butternut Squash Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Base: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add olive oil, then sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add Rice and Squash: Stir in the Arborio rice and diced butternut squash, cooking for 2-3 minutes.
