Hearty One Pot Beef and Barley Soup


If you’re looking for a comforting and hearty meal that’s easy to make, look no further than this delicious one pot beef and barley soup. Packed with tender beef, wholesome barley, and flavorful vegetables, this soup is a perfect choice for a cozy family dinner or a satisfying meal on a chilly day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds stew beef, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup pearl barley
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Brown the Beef: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef and cook until browned on all sides. This step adds depth of flavor to the soup.

  2. Add Aromatics: Once the beef is browned, add the diced onion, sliced carrots, and celery to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.

  3. Combine Ingredients: Pour in the beef broth and diced tomatoes. Stir in the pearl barley, bay leaves, and dried thyme. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 1 1/2 hours, or until the beef is tender and the barley is cooked through.

  4. Serve: Once the soup is ready, remove the bay leaves and adjust the seasoning if needed. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a richer flavor, you can sear the beef in the pot before starting the soup. This adds a caramelized crust to the meat, enhancing the overall taste of the dish.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in the soup based on your preferences. You can add potatoes, parsnips, or even mushrooms for a different twist.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a slurry of flour and water during the simmering process to give the soup more body.

Why You’ll Love This Soup:

This beef and barley soup is a complete meal in a bowl. It’s not only delicious and hearty, but it’s also nutritious and filling. The combination of tender beef, chewy barley, and vibrant vegetables creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will satisfy your cravings and warm your soul.

Additionally, this recipe is perfect for busy days when you want a wholesome meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The one pot method makes for easy cleanup, and the leftovers taste even better the next day.

So, whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a comforting bowl of soup, this one pot beef and barley soup is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Enjoy the warmth and goodness of this flavorful and nourishing dish!
