Quick and Easy Minestrone Soup Recipes for a Nutritious Meal


Minestrone soup is a classic Italian dish that is not only delicious but also packed with nutrition. It’s a hearty and comforting soup that can be enjoyed as a complete meal on its own. With its vibrant colors and rich flavors, it’s no wonder that minestrone soup has become a favorite for many. In this blog post, we will explore some quick and easy minestrone soup recipes that you can whip up in no time for a nutritious and satisfying meal.

Classic Minestrone Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup small pasta (e.g., macaroni or ditalini)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the carrots, celery, and zucchini to the pot. Cook for about 5 minutes, until the vegetables start to soften.
  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes.
  4. Add the kidney beans and pasta to the pot. Cook until the pasta is al dente, according to the package instructions.
  5. Stir in the dried basil and oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
