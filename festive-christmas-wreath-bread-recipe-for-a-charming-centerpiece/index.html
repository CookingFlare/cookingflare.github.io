Festive Christmas Wreath Bread Recipe for a Charming Centerpiece


The holiday season is a time for festive cheer, family gatherings, and of course, delicious food. If you’re looking for a show-stopping centerpiece for your Christmas table, look no further than this delightful Festive Christmas Wreath Bread recipe. Not only is it a feast for the eyes, but it’s also a culinary delight that will have your guests coming back for more.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup raisins

For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warm milk, melted butter, and eggs.

  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until well combined.

  4. Add the remaining 2 cups of flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until the dough comes together.

  5. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic.

  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  7. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling by combining the brown sugar, cinnamon, pecans, and raisins in a small bowl.

  8. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down and roll it out into a large rectangle.

  9. Spread the filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  10. Starting from one long edge, roll the dough into a tight log.

  11. Join the two ends of the log to form a circle and pinch the ends together to seal.

  12. Using a sharp knife, make cuts around the circle at 1-inch intervals, leaving about 1/2 inch at the center uncut.

  13. Gently twist each section of dough so that the filling is visible.

  14. Place the wreath on a parchment-lined baking sheet and let it rise for an additional 30 minutes.

  15. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

  16. Bake the wreath for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.

  17. While the wreath is baking, prepare the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  18. Once the wreath is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  19. Drizzle the glaze over the warm wreath.

  20. Allow the wreath to cool slightly before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Festive Christmas Wreath Bread makes a stunning centerpiece for your holiday table. Serve it alongside a steaming cup of hot cocoa or a fragrant spiced cider for a truly festive experience. The wreath can be pulled apart and shared with loved ones, adding a sense of warmth and togetherness to your Christmas celebration.

Conclusion

This Festive Christmas Wreath Bread recipe is not only a delightful treat for the taste buds but also a charming centerpiece that will captivate your guests. With its warm, comforting flavors and impressive presentation, this wreath is sure to become a beloved tradition in your holiday repertoire. Embrace the joy of the season and bring a touch of magic to your table with this beautiful and delicious creation. Happy baking and happy holidays!
