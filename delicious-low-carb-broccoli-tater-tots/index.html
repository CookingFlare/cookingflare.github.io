Delicious Low Carb Broccoli Tater Tots


As the world continues to shift towards a healthier lifestyle, many of us are on the lookout for recipes that are not only delicious but also low in carbs. One of the most popular snacks that often gets a bad rap is the humble tater tot. But fear not, dear readers, for we have a game-changing recipe that will revolutionize the way you think about tater tots. Introducing our Low Carb Broccoli Tater Tots, a snack that is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients.

The Problem with Traditional Tater Tots

Traditional tater tots are a staple in many households, but they are often high in carbs and calories. A single serving of regular tater tots can contain up to 30 grams of carbs, which can be a major setback for those following a low-carb diet. Additionally, they are often deep-fried, which adds to the calorie count and makes them a less-than-ideal snack option.

The Solution: Low Carb Broccoli Tater Tots

Our Low Carb Broccoli Tater Tots are a game-changer. By using cauliflower instead of potatoes, we are able to significantly reduce the carb count while still maintaining the same crispy texture and delicious flavor as traditional tater tots. And to take it to the next level, we add in some steamed broccoli florets to give it an extra nutritional boost.

The Recipe

To make our Low Carb Broccoli Tater Tots, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup of almond flour
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Rinse the cauliflower and remove the leaves and stem. Cut it into florets and pulse in a food processor until it resembles rice.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cauliflower “rice” with the broccoli florets, almond flour, Parmesan cheese, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix well until everything is fully incorporated.
  4. Using your hands, shape the mixture into small tots. You should end up with around 20-25 tots.
  5. Place the tots on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle with olive oil and toss to coat.
  6. Bake the tots in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the tots from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Serve hot and enjoy!

The Benefits

Our Low Carb Broccoli Tater Tots are not only delicious, but they also offer a range of health benefits. By using cauliflower instead of potatoes, we are able to reduce the carb count and increase the fiber content. The broccoli adds an extra boost of vitamins and antioxidants, making this snack a nutritious and guilt-free option.

Tips and Variations

  • To make these tots more crispy, try baking them for an additional 5-10 minutes. Just keep an eye on them to make sure they don’t burn.
  • If you want to add some extra flavor, try sprinkling some grated cheddar cheese on top of the tots before baking.
  • These tots are perfect for snacking on their own, but they also make a great side dish for your favorite meals. Try serving them with a homemade chicken parmesan or a hearty beef stew.

Conclusion

Our Low Carb Broccoli Tater Tots are a game-changing recipe that is sure to become a staple in your household. With their crispy texture and delicious flavor, they are the perfect snack for anyone looking to reduce their carb intake. And with the added nutritional benefits of broccoli, you can feel good about what you’re putting in your body. So go ahead, give them a try, and experience the delicious taste of a guilt-free snack.
