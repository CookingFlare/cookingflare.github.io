Satisfying Instant Pot Beef and Potato Stew Recipes


Are you in the mood for a hearty and comforting meal that will warm you up from the inside out? Look no further than these satisfying Instant Pot beef and potato stew recipes. Packed with tender beef, flavorful vegetables, and creamy potatoes, these stews are the perfect comfort food for any occasion. Plus, with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have a delicious meal on the table in no time.

Classic Beef and Potato Stew

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 35 minutes
Total Time: 50 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds stew beef, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the stew beef and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and set aside.
  2. Add the onion and garlic to the Instant Pot and sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the carrots, celery, and potatoes to the Instant Pot and stir to combine.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the beef broth, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, and rosemary. Pour the mixture into the Instant Pot.
  5. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure.
  8. Carefully remove the lid of the Instant Pot and give the stew a stir. Season with additional salt and pepper if needed.
  9. Serve the stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

Spicy Beef and Potato Stew

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 35 minutes
Total Time: 55 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds stew beef, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 jalapeños, seeded and minced
  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the vegetable oil. Add the stew beef and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and set aside.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, bell pepper, and jalapeños to the Instant Pot and sauté until softened.
  3. Add the potatoes to the Instant Pot and stir to combine.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the beef broth, tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, and paprika. Pour the mixture into the Instant Pot.
  5. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure.
  8. Carefully remove the lid of the Instant Pot and give the stew a stir. Season with additional salt and pepper if needed.
  9. Serve the stew hot, garnished with sliced jalapeños if desired.

Conclusion

These satisfying Instant Pot beef and potato stew recipes are sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Whether you prefer the classic version or want to add a spicy kick to your stew, the Instant Pot makes it easy to create delicious and comforting meals in no time. So grab your Instant Pot and get cooking!
