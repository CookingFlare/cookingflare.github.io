BBQ Chicken Salad: Grilled Perfection in a Bowl


As the sun beats down and the days grow longer, our taste buds crave something refreshing, something that screams “summer!” And what better way to satisfy that craving than with a delicious BBQ chicken salad? In this recipe, we’ll show you how to elevate the classic salad game by adding smoky grilled chicken, crunchy vegetables, and a tangy dressing that will leave you wanting more.

The Perfect Grilled Chicken

Before we dive into the salad itself, let’s talk about the star of the show: the grilled chicken. For this recipe, we’ll be using boneless, skinless chicken breasts, which are perfect for grilling. To achieve that perfect char, make sure to preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Season the chicken with a mixture of salt, pepper, and your favorite spices, and let it cook for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.

The Salad

Now that our chicken is cooked to perfection, it’s time to build the salad. In a large bowl, combine the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed greens (arugula, spinach, lettuce)
  • 1 cup cooked and diced grilled chicken
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber slices
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup croutons (homemade or store-bought)

The Dressing

The dressing is where the magic happens. In a small bowl, whisk together the following ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. You can adjust the amount of dressing to your liking, but we promise you won’t be disappointed.

The Finishing Touches

To take this salad to the next level, add some crunchy elements like chopped pecans or pistachios. You can also sprinkle some crumbled bacon or diced avocado for extra flavor and texture. And don’t forget to garnish with some fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley.

The Verdict

This BBQ chicken salad is the perfect combination of smoky, tangy, and crunchy. The grilled chicken adds a depth of flavor that’s hard to beat, while the mixed greens and vegetables provide a refreshing contrast. And let’s not forget the dressing – it’s the glue that holds everything together. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy lunch or a light and satisfying dinner, this salad is sure to please.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this salad more substantial, add some cooked quinoa or brown rice.
  • Swap out the chicken for grilled shrimp or salmon for a seafood twist.
  • Add some diced bell peppers or red onion for extra flavor and texture.
  • Use different types of cheese, like cheddar or parmesan, for a unique flavor profile.

In conclusion, this BBQ chicken salad is a summer staple that’s sure to become a family favorite. With its perfect balance of flavors and textures, it’s the perfect way to beat the heat and satisfy your cravings. So go ahead, fire up the grill, and get ready to indulge in a salad that’s truly grilled perfection in a bowl.
