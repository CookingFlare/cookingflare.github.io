Decadent S'mores Brownies: A Campfire Classic Reinvented


Who doesn’t love the classic campfire treat, s’mores? The combination of gooey marshmallows, rich chocolate, and crunchy graham crackers is a match made in heaven. But what if we told you that you can take this beloved treat to the next level by turning it into a decadent brownie? That’s right, folks! We’re talking about Decadent S’mores Brownies, a game-changing dessert that will satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you wanting more.

The Recipe

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s talk about what makes these brownies so special. We’re using high-quality dark chocolate, which provides a deep, rich flavor that’s perfect for s’mores. We’re also using a combination of brown sugar and granulated sugar to give the brownies a caramel-like flavor. And of course, we can’t forget the marshmallows – we’re using large marshmallows that are perfectly toasted to create a gooey, marshmallow-filled center.

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 and 1/2 sticks of unsalted butter, plus more for greasing the pan
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of marshmallows
  • 1 cup of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

Now that we have our ingredients, let’s get started! Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease an 8-inch square baking pan with butter and line it with parchment paper.

In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, salt, and cocoa powder. Set aside.

In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to cream together the butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.

Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave in 30-second increments, stirring between each interval, until smooth. Allow the chocolate to cool slightly.

Add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients and stir until just combined. Pour in the melted chocolate and stir until smooth.

Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

While the brownies are baking, prepare the marshmallow topping. Cut the marshmallows in half and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake in the oven for 2-3 minutes or until toasted to your liking.

Once the brownies are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool completely. Cut into squares and top each square with a toasted marshmallow, a piece of graham cracker, and a sprinkle of granulated sugar.

Tips and Variations

  • To take these brownies to the next level, try adding a scoop of ice cream or whipped cream on top.
  • If you want to make these brownies more festive, try using colored marshmallows or graham crackers.
  • You can also customize the recipe by using different types of chocolate chips or adding nuts or caramel sauce.

Conclusion

And there you have it, folks – Decadent S’mores Brownies that are sure to impress your friends and family. With their gooey marshmallow centers, crunchy graham cracker crusts, and rich chocolate flavors, these brownies are the perfect dessert for any occasion. So go ahead, give them a try, and experience the magic of s’mores like never before.
