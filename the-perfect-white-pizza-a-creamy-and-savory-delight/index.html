The Perfect White Pizza: A Creamy and Savory Delight


Are you tired of the same old tomato-based pizza? Looking for a change from the traditional red sauce? Look no further than the perfect white pizza! This creamy and savory delight is a delicious twist on the classic Italian favorite. With a rich and flavorful white sauce, topped with a variety of mouthwatering ingredients, this pizza is sure to become a new favorite in your household.

Ingredients

For the White Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pizza:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup artichoke hearts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the White Sauce:

    • In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
    • Sprinkle the flour over the butter and garlic, whisking constantly to create a roux. Cook the roux for 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.
    • Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Cook the sauce, stirring frequently, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
    • Stir in the dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Remove the sauce from the heat and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Pizza:

    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
    • Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet or a pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
    • Spread the white sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the white sauce, followed by dollops of ricotta cheese.
    • Scatter the chopped spinach, artichoke hearts, and sun-dried tomatoes over the cheese.
    • Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the pizza.

  3. Bake the Pizza:

    • If using a pizza stone, carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated stone in the oven. If using a baking sheet, place the sheet in the oven.
    • Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves and slice into wedges. Serve and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a boost of flavor, consider adding a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of balsamic glaze over the finished pizza.
  • Experiment with different toppings such as caramelized onions, roasted garlic, or prosciutto for a unique twist.
  • If you’re short on time, feel free to use store-bought Alfredo sauce as a quick alternative to making the white sauce from scratch.

Conclusion

The perfect white pizza is a delightful departure from the ordinary, offering a creamy and satisfying flavor experience that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a pizza night with friends or simply craving something different, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to indulge in a slice of pure pizza perfection!
