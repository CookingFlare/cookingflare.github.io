Summer Picnic Sandwiches That Will Steal the Show


Summer is here, and that means it’s time to get outdoors and enjoy the sun, the fresh air, and of course, some delicious food. When it comes to picnics, one of the most beloved options for a meal on the go is the sandwich. Whether you’re packing lunch for a family gathering, a romantic getaway, or just a fun day at the park, the right sandwiches can elevate your picnic experience.

But not all sandwiches are created equal. If you want to really steal the show at your next summer picnic, you’ll need a blend of flavors, textures, and ingredients that are as fresh as the season itself. Forget the traditional PB&J or basic turkey sandwiches – this summer, it’s all about getting creative and bringing a burst of flavor to your picnic basket. In this post, we’ll share some mouthwatering and unique sandwich ideas that are sure to be the stars of your picnic.

1. Grilled Chicken and Avocado Caesar Sandwiches

For a sandwich that combines classic flavors with a modern twist, the grilled chicken and avocado Caesar sandwich is a winner. Grilled chicken provides lean protein, while the creamy avocado adds a smooth richness that complements the crunchy texture of Romaine lettuce. A drizzle of Caesar dressing and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese bring everything together for a mouthwatering flavor explosion.

Ingredients:

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 2 cups Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 slices of sourdough or ciabatta bread
  • Olive oil for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill pan or outdoor grill. Brush the chicken breasts with olive oil and grill them until fully cooked (about 6-7 minutes per side).
  2. While the chicken is cooking, slice the avocado and chop the Romaine lettuce.
  3. Once the chicken is cooked, slice it into thin strips.
  4. Toast the slices of bread on the grill until golden brown.
  5. To assemble, spread a thin layer of Caesar dressing on one side of each slice of bread.
  6. Layer the grilled chicken, avocado slices, and lettuce on the bread.
  7. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and top with the second slice of bread. Slice into halves or quarters for easy serving.

This sandwich is fresh, light, and full of flavor, making it perfect for a summer picnic.

2. Caprese Sandwich with Pesto

When you think of summer, one of the first flavor combinations that come to mind is the classic Caprese salad. Tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil all shine in this simple yet elegant sandwich. The addition of pesto sauce takes this sandwich to a whole new level, adding a delightful herby flavor that will have everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette or focaccia bread
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons pesto sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Slice the baguette or focaccia into 4 pieces, and toast lightly on a grill or in a pan.
  2. Spread a thin layer of pesto sauce on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Layer the tomato slices, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves on two slices of bread.
  4. Drizzle a little olive oil over the toppings and top with the remaining bread slices.
  5. Slice the sandwich into halves or quarters.

This sandwich is packed with fresh, vibrant flavors that scream summer and is sure to be a picnic favorite.

3. Turkey, Cranberry, and Brie Sandwich

If you’re looking for a combination of sweet, savory, and creamy, the turkey, cranberry, and brie sandwich will steal the show. The turkey provides a lean protein base, while the brie cheese adds a luxurious creamy texture. The cranberry sauce gives the sandwich a delightful sweetness that complements the savory elements perfectly.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of whole-grain or multigrain bread
  • 8 ounces sliced turkey breast
  • 4 slices of brie cheese
  • 1/4 cup cranberry sauce (preferably homemade)
  • Arugula or spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. Spread a thin layer of cranberry sauce on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Layer the sliced turkey breast and brie cheese on two slices of bread.
  3. Add a handful of arugula or spinach for a fresh, peppery bite.
  4. Top with the remaining slices of bread to form the sandwiches.
  5. Slice into halves or quarters for easy sharing.

This sandwich combines familiar flavors with a gourmet twist, making it a great option for those who like to add a little sophistication to their picnics.

4. Veggie and Hummus Wraps

For a vegetarian-friendly option, try these vibrant veggie and hummus wraps. Packed with fiber and vitamins, this sandwich is not only healthy but also bursting with fresh flavors. The creamy hummus serves as the perfect spread to hold together the colorful mix of veggies, offering a satisfying crunch with every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 4 whole wheat tortillas
  • 1/2 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 2 large handfuls of spinach or mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots

Instructions:

  1. Lay out the tortillas on a flat surface.
  2. Spread a generous layer of hummus on each tortilla.
  3. Layer the sliced cucumber, bell pepper, avocado, spinach, and shredded carrots on top of the hummus.
  4. Roll up the tortillas tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
  5. Slice each wrap into halves or quarters.

These wraps are light, fresh, and packed with nutrients, making them a perfect addition to your picnic spread.

5. BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

If you’re looking for something hearty that will satisfy a crowd, these BBQ pulled pork sliders are a must-try. The tender, flavorful pulled pork combined with tangy BBQ sauce, and piled high on soft slider buns, is a picnic crowd-pleaser. They’re easy to handle, making them ideal for picnics, and you can make them ahead of time for ultimate convenience.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of pork shoulder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups BBQ sauce (your favorite kind)
  • 12 slider buns
  • 1/2 cup coleslaw (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Slow cook the pork shoulder with chopped onions and BBQ sauce in a slow cooker on low for 6-8 hours, until the meat is tender and easily pulls apart.
  2. Once cooked, shred the pork using two forks.
  3. Toast the slider buns lightly to prevent them from getting soggy.
  4. Pile the pulled pork onto the buns and top with coleslaw for added crunch and flavor.
  5. Serve as individual sliders or as a platter for a more casual picnic-style serving.

These sliders are smoky, savory, and totally satisfying – the perfect comfort food for a summer picnic.

6. Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Bagels

For a picnic with a little bit of a luxe twist, smoked salmon and cream cheese bagels are the perfect choice. The combination of creamy cheese, salty salmon, and the crunch of fresh veggies creates a flavor-packed sandwich that feels indulgent yet light enough for a warm summer day.

Ingredients:

  • 4 whole grain bagels, sliced
  • 4 ounces smoked salmon
  • 4 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 1 small cucumber, sliced thinly
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bagels lightly.
  2. Spread a generous amount of cream cheese on both halves of each bagel.
  3. Layer with smoked salmon, cucumber slices, and a sprinkle of fresh dill.
  4. Top with the second half of the bagel and serve.

This sophisticated sandwich is perfect for picnics by the water or a brunch-inspired outdoor gathering.

7. Mediterranean Veggie Pita Pockets

For a Mediterranean-inspired option that’s full of flavor, try these veggie pita pockets. Packed with a variety of fresh vegetables, creamy tzatziki sauce, and a sprinkle of feta cheese, these pita pockets are both healthy and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 4 whole wheat pita pockets
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup tzatziki sauce

Instructions:

  1. Slice the pita pockets in half and carefully open them to create pockets.
  2. Fill each pita with diced cucumber, tomato, red onion, and olives.
  3. Top with crumbled feta cheese and a drizzle of tzatziki sauce.
  4. Serve immediately, or wrap in foil to keep fresh for your picnic.

These pita pockets are light, flavorful, and make a refreshing addition to your picnic basket.

Conclusion

Whether you’re hosting a large family picnic, a small gathering, or just enjoying a relaxing day outdoors, the sandwiches you choose can make or break the meal. By mixing classic flavors with creative twists, these summer picnic sandwiches will undoubtedly steal the show. From grilled chicken and avocado Caesar sandwiches to Mediterranean veggie pita pockets, each option offers something unique and delicious for your summer outings. So, pack your picnic basket with these show-stopping sandwiches and enjoy a memorable meal under the summer sun!
