Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto: A Seasonal Delight


Risotto is a classic Italian dish that never fails to impress with its creamy texture and rich flavors. And when combined with the earthy sweetness of butternut squash and the fragrant aroma of sage, it becomes a seasonal delight that will dazzle your taste buds. This Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto recipe is perfect for those cozy autumn evenings when you crave a comforting yet elegant meal. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced into small cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage leaves, finely chopped
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the diced butternut squash on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and toss to coat evenly. Roast in the oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the squash is tender and slightly caramelized.

  2. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the Arborio rice to the saucepan and stir well to coat the grains with the oil. Toast the rice for a couple of minutes, stirring constantly, until it becomes slightly translucent around the edges.

  4. Pour in the white wine and stir until it is absorbed by the rice. This step adds a subtle tang and depth of flavor to the dish.

  5. Now, it’s time to start adding the vegetable or chicken broth. Begin by adding one cup of broth to the saucepan and stir continuously until the liquid is absorbed. This process allows the rice to release its starches, giving the risotto its creamy consistency. Repeat this step, adding one cup of broth at a time, and stirring until each addition is absorbed. It may take approximately 20-25 minutes for the rice to cook completely.

  6. Once the rice is cooked al dente (with a slight firmness to the bite), stir in the roasted butternut squash and chopped sage leaves. The vibrant orange color of the squash will beautifully complement the risotto.

  7. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. The cheese will melt into the risotto, adding a rich creaminess to the dish.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and give the risotto a final gentle stir to combine all the flavors.

  9. Serve the Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto immediately, garnished with a few fresh sage leaves for an extra touch of elegance.

Time to Indulge!

Now that you have prepared this delectable Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto, it’s time to savor every spoonful of this creamy and aromatic delight. The sweetness of the roasted butternut squash, the earthy fragrance of sage, and the comforting creaminess of the risotto combine to create a truly unforgettable dish. Whether you’re enjoying it as a main course or serving it as a side dish, this seasonal risotto will impress your family and friends with its elegance and flavor.

Remember, risotto is best enjoyed immediately after cooking, as it tends to lose its creamy texture upon reheating. So gather your loved ones, pour yourself a glass of wine, and indulge in this seasonal delight. Buon appetito!
