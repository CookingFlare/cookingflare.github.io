Caramel Apple Hand Pies: A Portable Fall Dessert


There’s something magical about the arrival of fall. The crisp air, the vibrant colors, and of course, the delicious flavors that come with the season. And what better way to celebrate the arrival of autumn than with a mouth-watering treat like Caramel Apple Hand Pies?

These handheld delights are the perfect combination of sweet and tart, with a buttery crust that melts in your mouth. The warm, gooey caramel paired with the tender apples will transport you straight to a cozy fall day. Plus, the portable nature of these pies makes them ideal for on-the-go snacking or sharing with friends and family.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 4 cups apples, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

For the caramel sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Gradually add the ice water, a few tablespoons at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.

  3. Divide the dough in half and shape each half into a disc. Wrap them in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Make the filling

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the diced apples, granulated sugar, brown sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, and flour. Toss gently until the apples are evenly coated.

Step 3: Prepare the caramel sauce

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the granulated sugar over medium-high heat until it melts and turns a deep amber color. Stir occasionally to ensure even melting.

  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and carefully add the cubed butter. Stir until the butter is melted and well combined.

  3. Slowly pour in the heavy cream while stirring constantly. Be cautious as the mixture will bubble vigorously.

  4. Stir in the salt until the caramel sauce is smooth and creamy. Set aside to cool slightly.

Step 4: Assemble the hand pies

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disc of dough to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Use a round cookie cutter or a glass to cut out circles of dough, approximately 4 inches in diameter.

  3. Place a spoonful of the apple filling onto the center of each dough circle. Drizzle a teaspoon or two of caramel sauce over the filling.

  4. Fold the dough circle in half, enclosing the filling, and press the edges together to seal. You can use a fork to create a decorative edge if desired.

  5. Repeat the process with the remaining dough and filling.

Step 5: Bake and enjoy!

  1. Place the hand pies on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the tops with beaten egg for a golden finish.

  2. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

  3. Allow the hand pies to cool on a wire rack for a few minutes before serving. Serve warm and enjoy the delectable combination of caramel, apples, and buttery crust.

Whether you’re planning a fall picnic, a cozy gathering with loved ones, or simply craving a taste of autumn, these Caramel Apple Hand Pies are sure to satisfy. The combination of flavors and the convenience of being able to enjoy them on the go make them a must-try dessert this fall season. So go ahead, indulge in the flavors of fall and savor every bite of these delightful treats!
