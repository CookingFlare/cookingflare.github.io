Satisfying Keto Bacon-Wrapped Cheese Stuffed Meatloaf Recipe


Are you ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will tantalize your taste buds and keep you in line with your keto lifestyle? This Satisfying Keto Bacon-Wrapped Cheese Stuffed Meatloaf is not just a meal; it’s a celebration of flavor, texture, and nourishment. Imagine a succulent meatloaf, generously wrapped in crispy bacon, with a gooey, cheesy center that oozes flavor with every slice. Let’s dive into this delightful recipe and discover how to create a dish that is both satisfying and keto-friendly.

Ingredients

To make this delicious meatloaf, you’ll need the following ingredients:

For the Meatloaf

  • 2 lbs ground beef (preferably grass-fed)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or cheese of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (or coconut aminos for a keto-friendly option)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

For the Bacon-Wrapped Layer

  • 10-12 slices of bacon
  • 1/4 cup sugar-free barbecue sauce (optional, for glaze)

Equipment Needed

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Baking sheet or loaf pan
  • Parchment paper or aluminum foil (optional for easy cleanup)
  • Meat thermometer
  • Sharp knife for slicing

Preparation Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that your meatloaf cooks evenly and retains its juiciness.

Step 2: Prepare the Meat Mixture

In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, shredded cheese, almond flour, eggs, chopped onion, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, Italian seasoning, smoked paprika, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix everything together until fully combined, but be careful not to overmix, as this can make the meatloaf tough.

Step 3: Form the Meatloaf

On a piece of parchment paper or directly on your baking sheet, shape the meat mixture into a rectangular loaf. The size should be roughly 10 inches long and 5 inches wide. Make a well in the center of the meatloaf to hold the cheese filling.

Step 4: Add the Cheese Filling

Spoon the remaining shredded cheese into the well you created in the meatloaf. Gently fold the sides of the meatloaf over to encase the cheese, ensuring it is completely hidden inside.

Step 5: Wrap with Bacon

Now comes the best part—wrapping the meatloaf in bacon! Lay the bacon slices over the top of the meatloaf, slightly overlapping each slice to create a beautiful bacon blanket. If you like, you can weave the bacon for an added touch of elegance.

Step 6: Glaze (Optional)

For an extra layer of flavor, brush the top of the bacon-wrapped meatloaf with sugar-free barbecue sauce. This will caramelize nicely in the oven, giving your meatloaf a delightful glaze.

Step 7: Bake

Transfer your meatloaf to the preheated oven and bake for about 60-75 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (70°C). Cooking times may vary depending on the size of your meatloaf, so it’s essential to use a meat thermometer to ensure it’s cooked thoroughly.

Step 8: Rest and Serve

Once your meatloaf is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, making for a moist and flavorful slice. When ready, slice the meatloaf and serve it warm. Consider pairing it with a fresh green salad or roasted vegetables for a complete meal.

Cooking Tips

  • Cheese Options: Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese for the stuffing. Cream cheese, mozzarella, or pepper jack can add unique flavors.
  • Bacon Choices: Use thick-cut bacon for a heartier texture. If you’re feeling adventurous, try flavored bacon, such as peppered or maple for added taste.
  • Make Ahead: This meatloaf can be prepared in advance and stored in the fridge for up to 24 hours before baking. Just wrap it tightly in plastic wrap until you’re ready to cook.
  • Freezing: Leftovers can be frozen in individual portions. Simply wrap slices in foil and place them in a freezer-safe bag. When you’re ready to eat, thaw overnight in the fridge and reheat in the oven.

Nutritional Information

While precise nutritional values may vary based on specific brands and measurements, this Satisfying Keto Bacon-Wrapped Cheese Stuffed Meatloaf typically contains:

  • Calories: Approximately 400-500 per serving
  • Protein: 30-35g
  • Fat: 30-35g
  • Carbohydrates: 3-5g (net carbs)
  • Fiber: 1-2g

This makes it an excellent choice for anyone following a ketogenic diet, providing plenty of protein and healthy fats while keeping carbs to a minimum.

Conclusion

This Satisfying Keto Bacon-Wrapped Cheese Stuffed Meatloaf is more than just a recipe; it’s a dish that brings comfort and joy to your dining table. It’s perfect for family dinners, meal prep, or even a special occasion where you want to impress your guests. With its flavorful ingredients and mouthwatering presentation, this meatloaf is guaranteed to become a favorite in your household.

So, whether you’re a seasoned keto enthusiast or just starting on your low-carb journey, this meatloaf is a must-try. Enjoy the rich flavors, the satisfying texture, and the joy of knowing you’re nourishing your body with wholesome ingredients. Happy cooking!
