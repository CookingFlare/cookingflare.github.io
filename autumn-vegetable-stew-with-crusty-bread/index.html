Autumn Vegetable Stew with Crusty Bread


As the crisp autumn air rolls in, there’s nothing quite like cozying up with a warm, hearty bowl of vegetable stew paired with freshly baked crusty bread. This comforting dish not only warms the soul but also celebrates the vibrant flavors of the season. Let’s dive into how you can create your own delicious Autumn Vegetable Stew with Crusty Bread right in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

For the Autumn Vegetable Stew:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

For the Crusty Bread:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water

Instructions:

Making the Autumn Vegetable Stew:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, sautéing until fragrant.
  2. Add in the carrots, celery, and sweet potato. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  3. Stir in the dried thyme, rosemary, and paprika, ensuring the vegetables are well coated with the herbs.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and diced tomatoes. Bring the stew to a gentle simmer.
  5. Add the white beans and butternut squash to the pot. Let the stew simmer for about 20-25 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.
  7. Serve the Autumn Vegetable Stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Baking the Crusty Bread:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt, and instant yeast together.
  2. Gradually add the warm water, stirring until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and let the dough rise for about 3 hours until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Place a dutch oven or baking dish with a lid into the oven while it preheats.
  5. Once the dough has risen, carefully transfer it to the preheated dutch oven. Cover with the lid and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove the lid and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the bread is golden brown and crusty.
  7. Let the bread cool slightly before slicing and serving alongside the Autumn Vegetable Stew.

Enjoy the Flavors of Fall

There you have it – a delightful Autumn Vegetable Stew paired with homemade Crusty Bread to elevate your autumn dining experience. The combination of hearty vegetables, aromatic herbs, and crusty bread makes this dish a perfect choice for chilly evenings or gatherings with loved ones. Embrace the essence of the season with this comforting and nourishing recipe. Happy cooking!

Remember, the key to a successful stew lies in the slow simmering of flavors and the love you pour into your cooking. So, gather your ingredients, set your pot abubble, and savor the taste of autumn with every spoonful.
