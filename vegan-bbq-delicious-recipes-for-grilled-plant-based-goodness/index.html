Vegan BBQ: Delicious Recipes for Grilled Plant-Based Goodness


Are you ready to fire up the grill and indulge in some mouthwatering vegan BBQ? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share with you some delectable recipes that will satisfy your cravings for grilled plant-based goodness. Get ready to impress your friends and family with these flavorful and satisfying dishes!

Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burger

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushroom caps
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tomato slices
  • 4 tablespoons vegan mayonnaise
  • 4 tablespoons barbecue sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the portobello mushroom caps with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the mushroom caps for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until tender.
  4. While the mushrooms are grilling, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill.
  5. Assemble the burgers by spreading vegan mayonnaise on the bottom bun, followed by a lettuce leaf, tomato slice, grilled mushroom cap, and red onion.
  6. Drizzle barbecue sauce on top of the mushroom cap and cover with the top bun.
  7. Serve the burgers with your favorite side dish and enjoy!

Grilled Vegetable Skewers

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 zucchinis, sliced into rounds
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into squares
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into squares
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 8 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating between zucchini, bell peppers, red onion, and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Brush the vegetable skewers with the marinade mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  5. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and serve them as a tasty side dish or as a colorful addition to any salad.

Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon

Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 8 minutes | Total Time: 13 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe pineapple, peeled and cored
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Vegan vanilla ice cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the pineapple into slices, about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together maple syrup and ground cinnamon.
  4. Brush the pineapple slices with the maple syrup and cinnamon mixture.
  5. Grill the pineapple slices for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they have nice grill marks.
  6. Remove the pineapple slices from the grill and serve them warm.
  7. If desired, top with a scoop of vegan vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat!

Now you’re all set to host a vegan BBQ party and impress your guests with these delicious and satisfying grilled plant-based recipes. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to incorporate more plant-based options into your diet, these recipes will surely satisfy your cravings. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and enjoy the smoky flavors of vegan BBQ goodness!
