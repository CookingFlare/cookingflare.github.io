Hearty Keto Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly meal, look no further than this hearty beef and broccoli stir-fry recipe. Packed with flavor and low in carbs, this dish is perfect for anyone following a ketogenic diet or simply looking for a healthy, flavorful meal. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce (or coconut aminos for a gluten-free option)
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of xanthan gum (or arrowroot powder for a non-keto option)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes for a spicy kick

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by slicing the flank steak into thin strips. Cut the broccoli into small florets, and mince the garlic. Grate the fresh ginger, and set all the ingredients aside for easy access during cooking.

  2. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the sliced beef, minced garlic, grated ginger, and soy sauce. Toss the ingredients together to ensure the beef is evenly coated with the marinade. Let it sit for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  3. Cook the Beef: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef to the skillet. Cook the beef for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it is browned and cooked to your desired level of doneness. Once cooked, remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Cook the Broccoli: In the same skillet, add the broccoli florets and sauté them for 2-3 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp. Season the broccoli with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Combine the Ingredients: Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the broccoli. Stir in the sesame oil and sprinkle the xanthan gum over the ingredients. Toss everything together to ensure the beef and broccoli are evenly coated with the flavorful sauce. If you prefer a spicy kick, sprinkle in some red pepper flakes at this stage.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once everything is heated through and well combined, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the hearty keto beef and broccoli stir-fry in bowls, and garnish with additional red pepper flakes or sesame seeds if desired. This dish pairs well with cauliflower rice or shirataki noodles for a complete keto-friendly meal.

Final Thoughts

This hearty keto beef and broccoli stir-fry is a fantastic option for a quick and satisfying meal that’s packed with flavor and nutrients. It’s a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste preferences, making it a go-to recipe for busy weeknights or leisurely weekends. Whether you’re following a ketogenic diet or simply looking to add more low-carb options to your meal rotation, this stir-fry is sure to become a household favorite.

Give this recipe a try, and savor the delightful combination of tender beef, vibrant broccoli, and savory seasonings. You’ll love how easy it is to prepare and how incredibly delicious it tastes. Enjoy every flavorful bite and feel good about nourishing your body with wholesome, keto-friendly ingredients. Bon appétit!
