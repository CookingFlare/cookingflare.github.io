Savory Sausage and Peppers: Easy Weeknight Dinner Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious, satisfying, and easy dinner recipe that can be whipped up in no time? Look no further than this savory sausage and peppers dish. Packed with flavor and simple to prepare, this recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to put a delicious meal on the table without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 Italian sausages
  • 2 bell peppers (red, yellow, or green), sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prep the Ingredients: Start by slicing the bell peppers and onions, mincing the garlic, and gathering all the other ingredients.

  2. Cook the Sausages: In a large skillet over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the sausages to the skillet and cook, turning occasionally, until they are browned on all sides and cooked through. This should take about 10-12 minutes. Once the sausages are cooked, transfer them to a plate and set aside.

  3. Sauté the Peppers and Onions: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Add the sliced peppers and onions to the skillet and sauté until they are softened and slightly caramelized, about 8-10 minutes.

  4. Add the Garlic and Seasonings: Once the peppers and onions are cooked, add the minced garlic, dried oregano, and dried basil to the skillet. Sauté for an additional 1-2 minutes, until the garlic is fragrant and the seasonings are well incorporated.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the sausages to the skillet with the peppers and onions. Add the can of diced tomatoes, including the juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir everything together and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve: Once the sausage and peppers are cooked and the flavors have melded, remove the skillet from the heat. Transfer the sausage and peppers to a serving dish and garnish with fresh parsley.

This savory sausage and peppers dish is perfect on its own or served over cooked pasta or with crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce. It’s a versatile and satisfying meal that the whole family will love.

Why This Recipe Works

This recipe is a winner for so many reasons. First and foremost, it’s incredibly easy to make, requiring just a handful of simple ingredients and minimal prep work. The combination of sweet bell peppers, savory Italian sausages, and fragrant seasonings creates a flavor-packed dish that is sure to satisfy even the pickiest eaters.

Additionally, this recipe is highly customizable. You can use your favorite type of Italian sausage, whether it’s mild or spicy, and mix and match different colored bell peppers for a visually stunning presentation.

Not only is this dish delicious, but it’s also a great option for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. It’s naturally gluten-free and can be enjoyed on its own or paired with your favorite low-carb side dish.

Finally, this recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to get a wholesome and tasty meal on the table without spending hours in the kitchen. With minimal prep and cook time, this savory sausage and peppers dish is a real time-saver.

So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and satisfying dinner, give this savory sausage and peppers recipe a try. It’s sure to become a staple in your weeknight dinner rotation!
