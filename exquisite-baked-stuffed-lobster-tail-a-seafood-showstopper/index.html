Exquisite Baked Stuffed Lobster Tail: A Seafood Showstopper


If you’re in the mood for an elegant and mouthwatering seafood dish that will impress your guests, look no further than the Exquisite Baked Stuffed Lobster Tail. This showstopper recipe combines the delicate flavors of fresh lobster with a rich and flavorful stuffing, resulting in a dish that is as visually stunning as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 large lobster tails
  • 1 cup cooked and chopped shrimp
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Start by preparing the lobster tails. Using a sharp pair of kitchen shears, carefully cut through the top of the shell down the center, exposing the meat. Be sure to leave the tail intact.

  3. Gently lift the lobster meat out of the shell, keeping it attached at the tail end. Place the meat on top of the shell, creating a “butterfly” effect.

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked and chopped shrimp, breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped fresh parsley, melted butter, minced garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  5. Spoon the stuffing mixture generously onto the lobster meat, dividing it equally between the two tails. Press the stuffing down gently to ensure it sticks to the meat.

  6. Place the stuffed lobster tails on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for approximately 15-20 minutes, or until the lobster meat is opaque and the stuffing is golden brown.

  7. Once cooked, remove the lobster tails from the oven and allow them to rest for a few minutes before serving. This will ensure that the flavors have time to meld together and the meat remains tender and juicy.

  8. Serve the Exquisite Baked Stuffed Lobster Tails as a main course, accompanied by your favorite side dishes such as roasted vegetables or a light salad. Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon wedges for an extra touch of elegance.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, drizzle melted butter or a creamy lobster sauce over the baked stuffed lobster tails just before serving.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to personalize the stuffing mixture. Dill, chives, or even a pinch of cayenne pepper can add an extra kick of flavor.
  • If you don’t have access to fresh lobster tails, you can use frozen ones as well. Just be sure to thaw them completely before preparing the recipe.

The Exquisite Baked Stuffed Lobster Tail is a seafood dish that is sure to impress even the most discerning palates. With its stunning presentation and sensational flavors, it is perfect for special occasions or whenever you want to treat yourself to something truly extraordinary. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and get ready to experience seafood perfection like never before!

Enjoy!

Please note that the Exquisite Baked Stuffed Lobster Tail recipe is intended for informational purposes only. Consuming raw or undercooked seafood may increase your risk of foodborne illness. When preparing seafood dishes, always ensure that the ingredients are fresh and properly cooked.
