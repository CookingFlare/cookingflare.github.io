Slow Cooker Teriyaki Chicken: A Sweet and Savory Delight


If you’re a fan of Asian cuisine and love the convenience of slow cooker meals, then this recipe for Slow Cooker Teriyaki Chicken is a must-try! The combination of sweet and savory flavors will tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more. Plus, the slow cooker does all the work, making it a perfect dish for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Optional garnish: sesame seeds, green onions

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, brown sugar, rice vinegar, minced garlic, and grated ginger. This flavorful marinade will be the secret to the mouthwatering teriyaki sauce.

  2. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker and pour the teriyaki marinade over them. Ensure the chicken is well-coated with the sauce. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes or overnight for maximum flavor infusion.

  3. Once marinated, set the slow cooker to low heat and cook the chicken for 4-5 hours or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C). The slow cooking process allows the chicken to become tender and absorb all the delicious flavors of the teriyaki sauce.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and set it aside. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water to create a slurry. This will help thicken the teriyaki sauce.

  5. Pour the remaining liquid from the slow cooker into a saucepan and bring it to a boil over medium heat. Gradually add the cornstarch slurry while continuously whisking, until the sauce thickens to your desired consistency. This step ensures that the sauce coats the chicken perfectly.

  6. Shred the cooked chicken using two forks or slice it into bite-sized pieces, depending on your preference. Return the chicken to the slow cooker and pour the thickened teriyaki sauce over it. Stir well to make sure every piece of chicken is coated in the luscious sauce.

  7. Continue cooking on low heat for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together and the chicken to soak up the sauce.

  8. Serve your Slow Cooker Teriyaki Chicken over a bed of steamed rice or alongside some stir-fried vegetables for a complete meal. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped green onions for an added touch of freshness and crunch.

Now, sit back, relax, and enjoy the delectable flavors of this Slow Cooker Teriyaki Chicken. The tender chicken, combined with the sweet and savory teriyaki sauce, creates a taste sensation that will have your family and friends begging for seconds. Plus, the convenience of the slow cooker means you can spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying your meal. So go ahead and give this recipe a try - your taste buds will thank you!

Note: Don’t forget to adjust the cooking time if using bone-in chicken or if your slow cooker runs hotter or cooler than average. Ensure the chicken reaches a safe internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) before consuming.
