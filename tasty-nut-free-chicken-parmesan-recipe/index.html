Tasty Nut-Free Chicken Parmesan Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and nut-free twist on the classic Chicken Parmesan, you’ve come to the right place. This recipe is perfect for those with nut allergies or anyone looking for a flavorful and satisfying meal. With a crispy, golden crust and a rich tomato sauce, this Nut-Free Chicken Parmesan is sure to become a family favorite. Let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In three separate shallow dishes, prepare your breading station. In the first dish, place the all-purpose flour. In the second dish, beat the eggs. In the third dish, combine the panko breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.

  3. Working with one chicken breast at a time, dredge it in the flour, shaking off any excess. Then dip it into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off. Finally, coat the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere. Place the breaded chicken on the prepared baking sheet.

  4. Once all the chicken breasts are breaded, spray the tops lightly with cooking spray. This will help them turn golden and crispy in the oven. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the coating is golden brown and crispy.

  5. While the chicken is baking, heat the marinara sauce in a saucepan over medium heat.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the oven and spoon a generous amount of marinara sauce over each breast. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top.

  7. Return the chicken to the oven and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Nut-Free Chicken Parmesan is delicious served over a bed of cooked spaghetti or alongside a crisp green salad. The crunchy coating and gooey cheese pair perfectly with the savory marinara sauce, creating a truly satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

With its nut-free twist and irresistible flavors, this Chicken Parmesan recipe is a must-try for anyone who loves a good Italian-inspired meal. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a gathering with friends, this dish is sure to impress. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of this nut-free take on a beloved classic!
