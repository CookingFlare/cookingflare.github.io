Slow Cooker Family Meals: Set It and Forget It Recipes


Slow cookers have become a staple in many households, and for good reason. They’re convenient, easy to use, and can produce delicious meals with minimal effort. If you’re looking for a way to simplify your meal planning and cooking routine, a slow cooker is definitely worth considering. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best slow cooker family meals that are perfect for busy families on-the-go.

Chicken Tacos

One of the most versatile slow cooker recipes is chicken tacos. Simply place boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced tomatoes, onions, garlic, and taco seasoning in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 6-8 hours, then shred the chicken and serve with tortillas, shredded cheese, and your favorite toppings.

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 6-8 tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Optional toppings: diced avocado, sour cream, salsa, cilantro

Beef Stew

Beef stew is a classic comfort food that’s easy to make in the slow cooker. Simply brown the beef in a skillet, then add it to the slow cooker with some vegetables and broth. Cook on low for 8-10 hours, then serve hot with crusty bread.

  • 2 lbs beef stew meat
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Crusty bread, for serving

Chicken Fajitas

Another great slow cooker recipe is chicken fajitas. Simply place boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced bell peppers, onions, and fajita seasoning in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 6-8 hours, then serve with tortillas, shredded cheese, and your favorite toppings.

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 packet fajita seasoning
  • 6-8 tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Optional toppings: diced avocado, sour cream, salsa, cilantro

Mac and Cheese

Macaroni and cheese is a comforting, crowd-pleasing dish that’s easy to make in the slow cooker. Simply cook the macaroni according to the package instructions, then mix with shredded cheese, milk, and butter. Add it to the slow cooker with some diced ham or bacon, and cook on low for 2-3 hours.

  • 1 pound macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup diced ham or bacon
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Chicken and Rice

Chicken and rice is a simple, one-pot meal that’s perfect for busy families. Simply place boneless, skinless chicken breasts, uncooked rice, chicken broth, and diced vegetables in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 6-8 hours, then serve hot.

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Conclusion

Slow cookers are a great way to simplify your meal planning and cooking routine. With these set-it-and-forget-it recipes, you can enjoy delicious, homemade meals without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re looking for a comforting stew, a flavorful taco recipe, or a simple chicken dish, a slow cooker is the perfect solution. So why not give one of these recipes a try today? Your family will thank you!
