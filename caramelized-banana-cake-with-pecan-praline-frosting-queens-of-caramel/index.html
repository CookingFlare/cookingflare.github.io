Caramelized Banana Cake with Pecan Praline Frosting: Queens of Caramel


If you’re looking for a dessert that’s sure to make you feel regal and royal, then you’ve come to the right place! Today, we’re diving into the world of caramel with a special dessert that is as rich in flavor as it is in texture. Behold the Caramelized Banana Cake with Pecan Praline Frosting: a sumptuous medley of sweet bananas, crunchy pecans, and a sinfully smooth caramel-infused frosting that’ll have you feeling like royalty in no time. So, let’s gather our ingredients and get ready to embark on this mouth-watering journey.

Ingredients: For the Cake:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream

For the Pecan Praline Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans

To Start:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease two 9-inch cake pans with butter or cooking spray. Line the bottoms with parchment paper and set aside.

The Cake:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, mash the bananas until smooth. Add the softened butter and sugar, then cream together until light and fluffy. This helps to incorporate air into your batter, which creates a moist and tender cake.
  2. Add the eggs, one at a time, to the banana mixture, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract to add a touch of sweetness and character.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry mixture to the banana mixture, alternating with the buttermilk and heavy cream. Mix until just combined.
  4. Distribute the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and smooth the tops with a spatula.

Caramelizing the Bananas:

  1. Place the two pans on a middle rack in the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 5-10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

The Pecan Praline Frosting:

  1. While the cakes are cooling, let’s make that glorious frosting! In a medium saucepan, combine the butter and sugar. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the sugar has melted and the mixture reaches a caramel color. This will be unlike the typical caramel you’re used to, as we’re making a frosting instead of a pourable caramel sauce.
  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and whisk in the heavy cream and vanilla extract. The mixture will bubble and steam - don’t worry, that’s perfectly normal. Allow the frosting to cool and thicken slightly, giving it a few stirs along the way.
  3. Once the frosting has reached the desired consistency, stir in the chopped pecans, giving the cake a lovely crunch and adding that extra touch of pecan-y goodness.

Assembly:

  1. Once your cakes have cooled, place one on a serving plate and spread a generous layer of the pecan praline frosting over the top. Then place the second cake layer on top, and frost the entire cake with the remaining frosting.
  2. Garnish with extra chopped pecans, if desired, and let the layers soak up the fabulous flavors of the frosting.

The Fruit of the Caramelized Banana Cake:

  1. Slowly slice into the cake and indulge in the layers of moist banana cake, smothered in a rich and velvety pecan praline frosting. The caramelized bananas invite a burst of natural sweetness while the pecan praline frosting adds a pleasant crunchy contrast. Each bite will make you feel like royalty, as this dessert deserves a crown.

This Caramelized Banana Cake with Pecan Praline Frosting is a classic dessert that calls for no celebration to be complete. It is indeed a regal treat that showcases the queen of all sweeteners: caramel. So, don our chefs’ coats, grab those ingredients, and join us in crafting this luxurious dessert. Prepare to be swept away to the glamorous, golden realm of caramel, where every bite will make you feel like a queen.

Happy baking, and treat yourself like royalty!
