One-Pot Chicken Parmesan Pasta: A Family-Friendly Favorite


Are you looking for a delicious and easy dinner recipe that the whole family will love? Look no further than this One-Pot Chicken Parmesan Pasta! It’s a comforting and satisfying dish that brings together the classic flavors of chicken parmesan and hearty pasta in one simple pot. Plus, it’s a breeze to make and requires minimal cleanup, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 1/2 cups of chicken broth
  • 8 ounces of penne pasta
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Cook the chicken until it’s golden brown on all sides, then remove it from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and chicken broth, then bring the mixture to a simmer.

  3. Stir in the penne pasta, making sure it’s evenly distributed in the pot. Cover and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the pasta is tender, stirring occasionally.

  4. Once the pasta is cooked, return the cooked chicken to the pot and stir to combine. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the top, followed by the shredded mozzarella cheese.

  5. Cover the pot and let it sit for a couple of minutes, allowing the cheese to melt and the flavors to meld together.

  6. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley before serving.

Why One-Pot Chicken Parmesan Pasta?

This recipe is a game-changer for several reasons. First and foremost, it’s incredibly convenient. By cooking everything in one pot, you not only save time on cleanup but also allow the flavors to meld together beautifully. The result? A rich and hearty pasta dish that tastes like it’s been simmering for hours.

Secondly, it’s a family-friendly meal that appeals to both kids and adults. The familiar flavors of chicken parmesan combined with the comforting nature of pasta make it a hit with picky eaters and discerning palates alike. Plus, who can resist the gooey, melted cheese on top?

Additionally, this recipe is highly customizable. You can easily add in extra veggies like spinach, bell peppers, or mushrooms to up the nutritional content, or swap out the penne for your favorite pasta shape. The possibilities are endless, making it a versatile go-to for any night of the week.

In Conclusion

One-Pot Chicken Parmesan Pasta is a true crowd-pleaser. It’s a stress-free, flavorful, and satisfying dish that ticks all the boxes for a family-friendly meal. Whether you’re a seasoned home chef or a kitchen novice, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, grab a pot, and get ready to enjoy a comforting and delicious dinner that will have everyone asking for seconds!
