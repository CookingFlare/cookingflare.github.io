Instant Pot Soups and Stews: Hearty Recipes for Any Season


The comforting allure of soups and stews has a way of transcending seasonal boundaries. Whether it’s a chilly winter evening requiring a piping hot bowl of goodness or a cool spring night craving something hearty but not too heavy, soups and stews are as versatile as they are nourishing. With the magic of the Instant Pot, these recipes become not only flavorful but also incredibly easy and time-efficient to prepare. Here’s a curated collection of ideas that will inspire you to get creative in the kitchen with soups and stews that will satisfy every craving throughout the year.

Why the Instant Pot is a Game-Changer for Soups and Stews

The Instant Pot is more than just a trendy appliance; it’s a powerhouse for home cooks, especially when it comes to soups and stews. Its pressure-cooking function significantly reduces the cooking time needed to extract deep flavors from ingredients, making complex-tasting soups achievable on a busy weeknight. Additionally, it doubles as a sauté pan, crockpot, and slow cooker, so you can sear, simmer, and tenderize all in one pot. Minimal cleanup is just the cherry on top.

Another notable advantage of using an Instant Pot is its ability to maintain the natural nutrients of the ingredients, thanks to its sealed cooking environment. Whether you’re cooking with hearty vegetables, quality proteins, or lentils and beans, you can trust the Instant Pot to bring out the best in every ingredient.

Versatile Soup and Stew Recipes for Any Season

Here’s a diverse selection of Instant Pot soup and stew recipes that are brimming with flavor. These recipes cater to a variety of taste preferences and dietary needs, ensuring you’ll always have something delicious to serve, no matter the time of year.

1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

A timeless favorite that brings warmth to everyone around the table, this version gets an Instant Pot upgrade for maximum flavor in minimal time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 medium carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups wide egg noodles
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric (optional, for color and added health benefits)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add a drizzle of olive oil, and sauté the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery for about 3-5 minutes until softened.
  2. Add the chicken breasts, chicken broth, dried thyme, and turmeric. Lock the lid and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook (manual) on high for 8 minutes.
  3. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then remove the chicken breasts. Shred the chicken using two forks.
  4. Switch to sauté mode again and add the egg noodles. Cook the noodles for about 5-7 minutes or until tender.
  5. Return the shredded chicken to the pot, season with salt and pepper, and stir to combine. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

2. Vegetarian Lentil Stew

Packed with plant-based protein and infused with rich spices, this lentil stew is nutritious, filling, and perfect for meal prepping.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 2 medium carrots, chopped
  • 1 14-ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a splash of olive oil. Sauté the onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced potato, carrots, tomatoes, lentils, vegetable broth, cumin, smoked paprika, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook (manual) on high for 12 minutes.
  4. Let the pressure release naturally for 5-10 minutes. Open the lid, stir in the lemon juice, and adjust seasoning as necessary. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

3. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Indulge in a velvety, rich tomato soup that’s perfect for pairing with a buttery grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 28-ounce cans of whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or coconut cream for a dairy-free option
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil or a handful of fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Start by sautéing the onion and garlic in butter or oil in the Instant Pot on sauté mode until soft and fragrant.
  2. Add the whole tomatoes, broth, and dried basil (if using). Close the lid and pressure cook (manual) on high for 10 minutes.
  3. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth. Stir in the cream and fresh basil if using. Adjust salt and pepper to taste. Serve warm.

4. Beef and Barley Stew

Hearty and full of umami, this beef and barley stew is a comforting classic perfect for cooler days.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds beef stew meat, cubed
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 medium carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1/2 cup pearl barley
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. On sauté mode, brown the beef cubes in a little oil until seared on all sides, then remove and set aside. Sauté the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery in the same pot for 3-5 minutes.
  2. Add the tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, and bay leaves. Stir to combine, then pour in the beef broth, barley, and return the beef cubes to the pot.
  3. Lock the lid and cook on pressure cook (manual) on high for 20 minutes. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning, remove the bay leaves, and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

5. Coconut Curry Sweet Potato Soup

This vibrant, slightly spicy soup is perfect for those seeking something creamy and exotic yet healthy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, diced
  • 1 14-ounce can coconut milk
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and roasted peanuts for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Using sauté mode, heat the coconut oil and sauté the onion, garlic, and ginger until softened.
  2. Stir in the red curry paste and turmeric until fragrant, then add the sweet potatoes, coconut milk, and vegetable broth.
  3. Seal the lid and pressure cook (manual) on high for 10 minutes. Allow a natural pressure release for 5 minutes before venting the rest.
  4. Blend the soup until smooth, stir in lime juice, and adjust salt if needed. Garnish with fresh cilantro and peanuts.

Tips for Success with Instant Pot Soups and Stews

  • Layer Your Flavors: Start by sautéing aromatics like onion, garlic, and spices to build a rich flavor base.
  • Adjust Consistency: For thicker stews, use less broth or blend a portion of the soup and return it to the pot.
  • Double the Batch: Soups and stews freeze beautifully, so consider making extra for future meals.
  • Experiment with Herbs: Fresh herbs like parsley, thyme, basil, and cilantro can elevate your soups to restaurant quality.

Cooking soups and stews in your Instant Pot is a wonderful way to embrace a variety of flavors and textures that change with the seasons. These recipes not only guarantee satisfaction but also deliver wholesome meals with minimal effort. Whether you’re a fan of classic chicken noodle soup or something adventurous like coconut curry, the Instant Pot has you covered, one ladle-full at a time.
