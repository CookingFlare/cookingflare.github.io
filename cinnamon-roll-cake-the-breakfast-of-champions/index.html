Cinnamon Roll Cake: The Breakfast of Champions


Is there anything more comforting and alluring than the warm, sweet aroma of cinnamon wafting through the kitchen in the early morning hours? The delightful scent of cinnamon roll cake popping from the oven sends a thrill down the spine, awakening the senses and readying the taste buds for the delectable experience that lies ahead.

This indulgent and scrumptious cinnamon roll cake transplant the rich and gooey flavors of the classic cinnamon roll into a convenient and versatile cake format. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or dessert, it’s the ultimate fusion of decadence and ease. Follow this luscious recipe to turn your morning or afternoon routine into a delightful, scrumptious affair.

The Secret Ingredients

Creating that perfectly balanced mixture of tanginess and sweetness, while maintaining that pillowy-soft texture, calls for a mixture of ingredients that perform harmoniously in your kitchen:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder and soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Caramel icing (homemade or store-bought)
  • Optional: Sprinkle of textured sugar or flaked almonds for the finishing touch

The Stepwise Recipe

Part 1: Cinnamon Sugar Swirl

This mixture acts as your cinnamon roll cake’sогendo (the gooey center that contrasts the sweetness with an earthy cinnamon tang) that holds both the cake and its signature indulgence together:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the two sugars (granulated and brown) with the cinnamon. The mixture should be clumpy and well-combined, resembling the sugar-cinnamon blend of a classic cinnamon roll.
  2. Save this mixture aside until you’re ready to assemble your cake.

Part 2: The Cake Batter

A tender, fluffy cake batter is fundamental to any great cinnamon roll-inspired dessert. Time to get your mixing tools ready!

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a 9 x 13" baking dish generously with softened butter.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat together the softened butter (you can microwave it uncovered for about 20-40 seconds), granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy and smooth.

  3. Gradually mix in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring both implementation and full integration into the batter before proceeding to add the next one.

  4. Stir in the vanilla extract, and the Greek yogurt, giving it a good whisk until smooth.

  5. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually incorporate the dry mixture into the wet ingredients, whisking consistently to avoid lumps.

Part 3: Assembly Time

Time to bring together all the elements with finesse for the cinnamon roll cake of your dreams:

  1. Spread half of the cake batter at the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Pat down with a spatula to ensure an even flat layer.

  2. Drizzle the cinnamon sugar swirl mixture evenly over the batter.

  3. Pour the remaining batter in dollops across the top, and then gently spread it to create a swirly, marbled pattern with a salad spinner or toothpick.

  4. Bake the cinnamon roll cake in preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

  5. Allow the cake to cool for about 15-20 minutes before drizzling it with caramel icing.

The Finishing Touch

Treat yourself to a slice of sinfully delicious cinnamon roll cake and discover why it justly holds the title, “Breakfast of Champions.” Add a flourish of textured sugar or flaked almonds on top for a delectable crunch that contrasts the pillowy texture beneath.

Serve the cake warm or at room temperature, dusted with powdered sugar or decorated further with whipped cream or fruit—let your creativity determine the final finishing touches.

Share the Love

Traditional cinnamon rolls require an excess of time and strenuous effort, but with this cinnamon roll cake recipe, you’re only a few steps away from unctuous bliss—a morning or afternoon ritual worth cherishing with friends and family. Happy baking and enjoy your culinary labor of love!
