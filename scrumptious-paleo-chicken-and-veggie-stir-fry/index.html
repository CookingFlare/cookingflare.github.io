Scrumptious Paleo Chicken and Veggie Stir-Fry


Are you looking for a healthy and delicious dinner option that is quick and easy to make? Look no further than this Scrumptious Paleo Chicken and Veggie Stir-Fry recipe! Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you feeling satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, zucchini, carrot, and broccoli. Cook for 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet and cook for an additional 1 minute.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the coconut aminos, fish sauce, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the skillet and stir to combine.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the Scrumptious Paleo Chicken and Veggie Stir-Fry hot, and garnish with fresh cilantro or green onions if desired.

Why Choose Paleo?

The Paleo diet has gained popularity in recent years due to its focus on whole, unprocessed foods that were available to our ancestors. By eliminating grains, dairy, and processed sugars, the Paleo diet aims to mimic the eating habits of our hunter-gatherer ancestors, which can lead to numerous health benefits.

This Scrumptious Paleo Chicken and Veggie Stir-Fry is a perfect example of a Paleo-friendly meal. By using lean chicken breast and a variety of fresh vegetables, this stir-fry is packed with protein, fiber, vitamins, and minerals. The flavorful sauce, made with coconut aminos and fish sauce, adds a delicious umami taste without any added sugars or preservatives.

Health Benefits of Chicken and Veggies

Chicken is an excellent source of lean protein, which is essential for building and repairing tissues, supporting immune function, and maintaining healthy hair, skin, and nails. It is also low in fat and calories, making it a great choice for weight management.

The colorful assortment of vegetables in this stir-fry provides a wide range of vitamins and minerals. Bell peppers are rich in vitamin C, which supports immune function and collagen production. Zucchini and broccoli are high in fiber, which aids digestion and helps maintain a healthy weight. Carrots are packed with beta-carotene, a powerful antioxidant that promotes healthy vision and skin.

Tips for Success

  • To save time, you can prep the vegetables and chicken ahead of time and store them in separate containers in the refrigerator.
  • Feel free to customize this stir-fry by adding or substituting your favorite vegetables. You can also adjust the seasonings to suit your taste preferences.
  • For a complete meal, serve the stir-fry over cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles.

So there you have it – a Scrumptious Paleo Chicken and Veggie Stir-Fry recipe that is not only delicious but also nutritious. Give it a try and enjoy a flavorful and healthy meal that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized.
