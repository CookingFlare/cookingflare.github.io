South African Sosaties with Marinated Meat and Skewers Recipe


Are you ready to take your taste buds on a journey to South Africa? Look no further than this delicious and authentic recipe for South African Sosaties with Marinated Meat and Skewers. Sosaties are a traditional South African dish that combines tender marinated meat with a flavorful marinade, resulting in a mouthwatering culinary experience. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover the secrets behind this tantalizing dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon ground paprika
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped onion, minced garlic, ground coriander, ground cumin, ground turmeric, ground paprika, ground cinnamon, brown sugar, plain yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  2. Add the bite-sized pieces of lamb to the marinade and toss until each piece is well coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the meat to tenderize.

  3. Once marinated, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated lamb onto the soaked wooden skewers, ensuring each piece is secure.

  4. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked to your desired level of doneness. The lamb should be slightly charred on the outside and juicy and tender on the inside.

  5. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more flavorful and succulent bite.

  6. Serve the South African Sosaties with Marinated Meat and Skewers hot off the grill, accompanied by a side of fragrant basmati rice or a fresh salad. The combination of the tender, spiced lamb and the vibrant flavors of the marinade will surely transport you to the heart of South Africa.

Conclusion

South African Sosaties with Marinated Meat and Skewers are a true delight for any food lover. The combination of aromatic spices, tender lamb, and a flavorful marinade creates a dish that is both exotic and comforting. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of South Africa, this recipe is sure to impress. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and prepare to savor the flavors of this remarkable dish. Enjoy!

Note: Sosaties can also be made with chicken or beef, depending on your preference. Feel free to explore different meat options and experiment with the marinade to create your own unique twist on this traditional South African dish.
