Homemade Chocolate Covered Peanut Butter Balls


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of chocolate and peanut butter, then you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m going to share with you a delicious recipe for Homemade Chocolate Covered Peanut Butter Balls. These bite-sized treats are perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving something sweet.

Ingredients

To make these delectable Peanut Butter Balls, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable shortening

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter, powdered sugar, softened butter, and vanilla extract. Using a spoon or electric mixer, mix until all the ingredients are well combined and form a smooth dough-like consistency.

  2. Once the mixture is ready, take small portions and roll them into bite-sized balls. Place the peanut butter balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and then transfer the sheet to the refrigerator. Let the balls chill for about 30 minutes to firm up.

  3. While the peanut butter balls are chilling, prepare the chocolate coating. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the semi-sweet chocolate chips and vegetable shortening. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.

  4. Once the chocolate is ready, remove the peanut butter balls from the refrigerator. Using a fork or toothpick, dip each ball into the melted chocolate, ensuring it is fully coated. Allow any excess chocolate to drip off, and then place the coated ball back onto the parchment-lined baking sheet.

  5. Repeat the process until all the peanut butter balls are coated in chocolate. If desired, you can sprinkle some crushed peanuts or colored sprinkles on top of the chocolate coating for added texture and decoration.

  6. Once all the balls are coated, return the baking sheet to the refrigerator and let them set for at least an hour, or until the chocolate coating is firm and hardened.

  7. Finally, remove the Peanut Butter Balls from the refrigerator and transfer them to an airtight container. They can be stored at room temperature for up to a week, but they are best enjoyed when chilled.

Serving Suggestions

These Homemade Chocolate Covered Peanut Butter Balls make a delightful treat on their own, but they can also be served alongside a cup of hot cocoa or as a sweet addition to an ice cream sundae. They are perfect for parties, potlucks, or as a homemade gift for friends and family.

Conclusion

Now that you have the recipe for Homemade Chocolate Covered Peanut Butter Balls, it’s time to get into the kitchen and start creating these irresistible treats. With just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of time, you can enjoy the perfect blend of creamy peanut butter and rich chocolate in every bite. So go ahead, indulge your sweet tooth and savor the deliciousness!
