Low-Alcohol Brunch Cocktails for a Light and Lively Morning


Are you tired of the same old mimosas and Bloody Marys at brunch? If you’re looking for a lighter and more refreshing option to start your morning, then low-alcohol brunch cocktails are the way to go. These delightful concoctions are perfect for those who want to enjoy a drink without feeling weighed down. So, grab your shaker and let’s dive into some delicious and easy-to-make low-alcohol brunch cocktails!

1. Sparkling Grapefruit Spritzer

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces grapefruit juice
  • 2 ounces sparkling water
  • 1 ounce elderflower syrup
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add grapefruit juice and elderflower syrup.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Top with sparkling water.
  5. Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  6. Enjoy the refreshing and citrusy flavors of this delightful spritzer!

2. Cucumber Lime Cooler

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 ounces cucumber-infused vodka
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce agave syrup
  • Soda water
  • Cucumber slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add cucumber-infused vodka, lime juice, and agave syrup.
  3. Stir well to combine.
  4. Top with soda water.
  5. Garnish with cucumber slices.
  6. Sip on this cool and invigorating cocktail that will leave you feeling refreshed and ready for the day!

3. Lavender Lemon Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 ounces lavender-infused gin
  • 1/2 ounce lemon juice
  • 1/2 ounce honey syrup
  • Club soda
  • Fresh lavender sprig for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add lavender-infused gin, lemon juice, and honey syrup.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Top with club soda.
  5. Garnish with a fresh lavender sprig.
  6. Indulge in the floral and citrusy notes of this elegant and aromatic cocktail!

4. Pineapple Coconut Mojito

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 ounces white rum
  • 1 ounce pineapple juice
  • 1/2 ounce coconut cream
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Pineapple wedge for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, muddle fresh mint leaves.
  2. Add white rum, pineapple juice, coconut cream, and lime juice.
  3. Shake vigorously with ice.
  4. Strain into a glass filled with ice cubes.
  5. Garnish with a pineapple wedge.
  6. Transport yourself to a tropical paradise with this delightful and creamy mojito!

5. Rosemary Grapefruit Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 ounces rosemary-infused vodka
  • 1 ounce grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup
  • Club soda
  • Fresh rosemary sprig for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add rosemary-infused vodka, grapefruit juice, and simple syrup.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Top with club soda.
  5. Garnish with a fresh rosemary sprig.
  6. Savor the herbal and tangy flavors of this unique and effervescent cocktail!

Enjoy a light and lively morning with these low-alcohol brunch cocktails that are sure to awaken your taste buds and set the tone for a wonderful day ahead. Whether you prefer citrusy spritzers or herbal fizzes, there’s a cocktail here for everyone. So, gather your friends, whip up a few of these delightful concoctions, and toast to a fabulous brunch experience that won’t leave you feeling weighed down. Cheers!
