Vegetarian One Pot Chickpea Curry Recipe


Looking for a hearty and flavorful vegetarian meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup? Look no further than this delicious one-pot chickpea curry recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of aromatic spices, this dish is a celebration of bold flavors and wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re a devoted vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this chickpea curry is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 ½ tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1 can (14 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 2 cans (14 oz each) of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, diced carrots, and chopped red bell pepper to the pot. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Stir in the curry powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, and turmeric, coating the vegetables with the aromatic spices.

  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes, coconut milk, drained chickpeas, and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low and let the curry simmer for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the vegetables to soften.

  6. Season the chickpea curry with salt and pepper to taste. For added heat, you can also incorporate a pinch of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes.

  7. Once the curry has thickened to your desired consistency, remove it from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Ladle the chickpea curry into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve the curry alongside fluffy basmati rice or warm naan bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra burst of freshness, squeeze a bit of lime juice over the finished curry before serving.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this recipe based on your preferences or what you have on hand. Sweet potatoes, spinach, or cauliflower would all be wonderful additions.
  • If you’re a fan of heat, consider adding a finely chopped fresh chili pepper or a spoonful of spicy harissa paste to the curry.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, allowing the flavors to further develop over time.

Enjoy Your Culinary Adventure!

There you have it – a simple and satisfying one-pot chickpea curry that’s perfect for a cozy weeknight dinner or a leisurely weekend meal. This versatile dish is not only delicious and nutritious but also incredibly convenient, requiring minimal cleanup thanks to its one-pot preparation. Embrace the vibrant flavors of this vegetarian curry and make it your own by experimenting with different spices and additions. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is a delightful introduction to the world of vegetarian cooking. So, gather your ingredients, unleash your inner culinary creativity, and savor every spoonful of this comforting chickpea curry. Happy cooking!
