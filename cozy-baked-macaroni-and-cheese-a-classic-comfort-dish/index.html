Cozy Baked Macaroni and Cheese: A Classic Comfort Dish


When it comes to classic comfort food, few dishes can rival the timeless appeal of baked macaroni and cheese. The combination of tender macaroni, rich cheese sauce, and a golden, crispy topping is a surefire way to warm the soul and satisfy the taste buds. Whether it’s a chilly winter evening or a cozy family gathering, baked macaroni and cheese never fails to bring joy and contentment to the table. In this blog post, we’ll explore the art of creating the perfect cozy baked macaroni and cheese, from selecting the right ingredients to achieving that irresistible golden crust.

Ingredients

For the Macaroni and Cheese:

  • 16 oz (450g) elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup (60g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups (950ml) whole milk
  • 2 cups (200g) sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup (100g) Gruyère cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup (100g) Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup (100g) Panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp (30g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup (25g) Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Fresh parsley, finely chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions, but slightly undercook it as it will continue to cook in the oven. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk constantly for about 2 minutes to make a roux.

  4. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly until the mixture is smooth and thickened.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and add the cheddar, Gruyère, and Parmesan cheese, stirring until melted and smooth.

  6. Stir in the Dijon mustard, paprika, salt, and pepper. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  7. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir until well coated. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish.

  8. In a small bowl, combine the Panko breadcrumbs, melted butter, and grated Parmesan cheese. Sprinkle the mixture evenly over the macaroni and cheese.

  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden and the cheese is bubbling around the edges.

  10. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley and enjoy the creamy, indulgent goodness of cozy baked macaroni and cheese.

Tips for Success

  • Use a combination of cheeses for depth of flavor and a smooth, creamy texture.
  • Don’t overcook the macaroni before baking, as it will continue to soften in the oven.
  • Experiment with different toppings such as crispy bacon or caramelized onions for added flavor and texture.

Conclusion

Cozy baked macaroni and cheese is a beloved classic that never goes out of style. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply indulging in some self-care, this comforting dish is bound to bring smiles all around. With its creamy, cheesy goodness and irresistible golden crust, it’s no wonder that baked macaroni and cheese holds a special place in the hearts of food lovers everywhere. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of bubbling cheese and pasta fill your kitchen as you embark on a culinary journey to cozy comfort and satisfaction.
