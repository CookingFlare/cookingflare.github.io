Savory Tomato and Basil Tart: A Taste of Summer


As the days grow longer and the sun shines brighter, it’s the perfect time to savor the flavors of summer. One dish that encapsulates the essence of the season is the savory tomato and basil tart. This delightful tart combines the juicy sweetness of ripe tomatoes with the aromatic freshness of basil, all encased in a buttery, flaky crust. Whether you’re hosting a garden party or simply craving a taste of summer, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 3-4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the chilled, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or fork to mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Form the dough into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  3. Roll Out the Dough:

    • On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle large enough to fit into a 9-inch tart pan. Carefully transfer the dough to the tart pan, pressing it into the bottom and sides. Trim off any excess dough.

  4. Blind Bake the Crust:

    • Line the crust with parchment paper and fill it with pie weights or dried beans. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment paper and weights and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside.

  5. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, minced garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Spread this mixture evenly over the bottom of the baked tart crust.

  6. Assemble the Tart:

    • Arrange the sliced tomatoes over the mayonnaise mixture. Sprinkle the chopped basil over the tomatoes, then top with the grated mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

  7. Bake the Tart:

    • Place the assembled tart in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is golden brown and bubbly.

  8. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Allow the tart to cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with additional fresh basil leaves if desired, and serve warm.

Tips for Success

  • Use ripe, flavorful tomatoes for the best taste.
  • Don’t skip the blind baking step for the crust to ensure a crisp, flaky base.
  • Experiment with different cheese varieties for a unique flavor twist.

Final Thoughts

The savory tomato and basil tart is a celebration of the vibrant, sun-ripened flavors of summer. With its buttery crust, juicy tomatoes, and aromatic basil, this tart is a true delight for the senses. Whether enjoyed as a light lunch, a flavorful appetizer, or a stunning addition to a summer gathering, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the simple, yet exquisite, pleasures of the season.

So, embrace the essence of summer with this delightful tomato and basil tart, and let its flavors transport you to a sun-drenched garden in the heart of the season. Cheers to savoring the tastes of summer!
