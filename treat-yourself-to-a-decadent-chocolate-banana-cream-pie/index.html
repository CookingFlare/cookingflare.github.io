Treat Yourself to a Decadent Chocolate Banana Cream Pie


Who doesn’t love a rich and indulgent dessert that combines the irresistible flavors of chocolate and banana? If you’re a fan of both, then this recipe is a must-try for you. A chocolate banana cream pie is the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your friends and family. Get ready to embark on a culinary adventure and dive into the world of decadence with this mouthwatering dessert.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons of melted butter
  • ¼ cup of granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 2 large ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 ½ cups of whole milk
  • ½ cup of heavy cream
  • ⅓ cup of granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

For the chocolate ganache:

  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 8 ounces of semisweet chocolate, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). In a mixing bowl, combine the chocolate cookie crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Press the mixture into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie dish. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  2. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the mashed bananas, whole milk, heavy cream, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. This should take about 8-10 minutes.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks. Gradually add a small amount of the hot milk mixture to the egg yolks, whisking constantly. This will temper the eggs and prevent them from curdling. Pour the tempered egg mixture back into the saucepan and continue cooking for another 2 minutes, stirring constantly.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the unsalted butter and vanilla extract until well combined. Allow the filling to cool slightly, then pour it into the prepared crust. Smooth the top with a spatula and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until set.

  5. While the pie is chilling, prepare the chocolate ganache. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Remove from heat and add the finely chopped semisweet chocolate. Let it sit for a minute to allow the chocolate to melt, then stir until smooth and glossy.

  6. Pour the chocolate ganache over the chilled pie, spreading it evenly to cover the entire surface. Return the pie to the refrigerator and let it chill for another 1-2 hours, or until the ganache is set.

  7. Once the pie is fully chilled and set, it’s ready to be served. Slice it into generous portions and garnish with whipped cream or fresh banana slices, if desired. Each bite will be a heavenly combination of creamy banana filling and rich chocolate crust.

Conclusion

Indulge yourself in the ultimate dessert experience with this decadent chocolate banana cream pie. The combination of velvety smooth banana filling, a chocolate cookie crust, and a luscious chocolate ganache topping will have your taste buds dancing with joy. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a little slice of heaven, this pie is sure to impress. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and create a masterpiece that will leave everyone craving for more. Enjoy!
