Savory Herb-Roasted Cornish Hens: Individual Holiday Entrees


The holiday season is upon us, and what better way to celebrate than with a show-stopping main course that is both delicious and impressive? Forget traditional turkey or ham, and instead, let’s explore the world of Cornish hens. These individual-sized poultry delights are the perfect centerpiece for any festive gathering. In this recipe, we’ll take it up a notch by infusing them with a savory blend of herbs and roasting them to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cornish hens, about 1.5 pounds each
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 lemons, sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, thyme leaves, sage, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  3. Remove the giblets from the Cornish hens and pat them dry with paper towels. Place the hens on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  4. Carefully loosen the skin of each Cornish hen, being careful not to tear it. Take about a tablespoon of the herb butter mixture and spread it evenly under the skin of each hen, massaging it gently to ensure it covers the entire breast area.

  5. Rub the remaining herb butter mixture all over the outside of the hens, making sure to coat them evenly.

  6. Stuff the cavity of each hen with a couple of lemon slices for added flavor and moisture.

  7. Place the baking sheet with the hens in the preheated oven and roast for about 50-60 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) when measured with a meat thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh.

  8. Once cooked, remove the hens from the oven and let them rest for about 10 minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute and ensures a moist and tender result.

  9. Serve the savory herb-roasted Cornish hens as individual entrees, garnished with fresh herbs and lemon slices for an added touch of elegance.

Tips and Variations

  • For a crispy skin, you can increase the oven temperature to 425°F (220°C) for the last 5-10 minutes of roasting.

  • If you prefer a citrusy flavor, you can squeeze some lemon juice over the hens before roasting or brush them with a lemon-herb glaze during the last few minutes of cooking.

  • To enhance the herbaceous flavor even further, you can add some fresh thyme and rosemary sprigs to the cavity of each hen before roasting.

  • This recipe can easily be adjusted to accommodate a larger or smaller number of hens. Simply adjust the quantities of the ingredients accordingly.

  • If you have any leftovers, you can use the meat from the roasted hens in sandwiches, salads, or even stir-fries for a delicious next-day meal.

So there you have it, a delectable recipe for savory herb-roasted Cornish hens that will undoubtedly impress your guests this holiday season. The combination of aromatic herbs, tender meat, and crispy skin makes this dish a true showstopper. Give it a try and elevate your holiday feast to new heights!
