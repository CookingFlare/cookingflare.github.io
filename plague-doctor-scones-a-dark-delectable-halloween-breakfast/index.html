Plague Doctor Scones: A Dark & Delectable Halloween Breakfast


Halloween is a time for tricks, treats, and all things spicy and eerie. This year, why not surprise your family and friends with something dark, different, and decidedly spooky for breakfast? Say hello to the delectable creation that is Plague Doctor Scones – flaky, fragrant, and frightfully intriguing!

Imagine the face of your loved ones when you bring a mysterious, savory scone to the breakfast table. Be it on a chill October morning or a frightful Halloween night, Plague Doctor Scones are sure to raise a few eyebrows and leave everyone craving for more. So, pull on your apron, ignite the oven, and let’s dive into the dark yet delightful world of this Halloween-inspired scone!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 cup unsweetened yogurt or buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 2 tablespoons dried basil
  • 2 tablespoons dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Optional: black food coloring
  • For the glaze: 1 cup powdered sugar + 2 tablespoons water

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (approximately 200°C) and lining a baking sheet with parchment paper. This will ensure your scones bake evenly and don’t stick to the sheet!

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. The dry ingredients will serve as the foundation of our plague doctor-inspired scones – dark and mysterious, just like their namesake! Then, add the cold cubed butter into the flour mixture and use your fingers to break it into pea-sized pieces. This will create a delightful, flaky texture!

Step 2: Combine the Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together the yogurt or buttermilk, eggs, and dark brown sugar. This combination of ingredients gives the scones a lovely tang with a hint of sweetness.

Step 3: Mix the Dough

Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to over-mix, as this will cause your scones to become tough. A slightly lumpy dough is perfect for these eerie scones!

For an extra spooky touch, you can add a couple of drops of black food coloring to the dough and stir gently until the desired darkness is achieved. While not necessary, this black hue will make your finished scones look eerily authentic.

Step 4: Shape the Scones

On a floured surface, gently pat the dough into a circle that’s about 1-inch thick. Using a floured round cookie cutter or the rim of a glass, cut out your scone shapes. You can recombine the dough scraps and repeat the process, but be careful not to overwork the dough. Place the scones on the prepared baking sheet and brush the tops lightly with beaten egg for a shiny finish.

Step 5: Decorate with Plague Doctor Accoutrements

To make our scones even more reminiscent of the Plague Doctors, sprinkle the top of each scone with a combination of dried parsley, basil, thyme, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder. These herbs and spices emulate the beak-like masks once worn by plague doctors and add an addictive depth of savory, earthy flavor.

Step 6: Bake and Glaze

Pop the scones into the oven and bake for 18-20 minutes or until the bottoms are golden brown. Rotate the baking sheet halfway through for even baking. While they are baking, mix up the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar and water in a small bowl. Once the scones are out of the oven and have slightly cooled, drizzle the glaze over the top for a ghoulish finish.

Your spooky Plague Doctor Scones are now ready to serve! Slice into the flaky, fragrant, and frighteningly delicious scones and enjoy this dark and delectable creation that will undoubtedly be a hit at your Halloween party.

Remember, make this recipe your own by experimenting with different flavor combinations or even adding a few slices of juicy, dark plum at the base of each scone. The spooky season is all about having fun and letting your creativity shine! Now that you have the sinister secrets to creating the ultimate Plague Doctor Scones, delight your friends and family with this hauntingly scrumptious treat.

Happy haunting in the kitchen!
