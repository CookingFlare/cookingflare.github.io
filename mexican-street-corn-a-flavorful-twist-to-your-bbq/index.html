Mexican Street Corn: A Flavorful Twist to Your BBQ


If you’re looking to level up your barbecue game, consider adding Mexican Street Corn (Elote) to your menu. This delicious and vibrant dish will transform your barbecued offerings and keep your guests coming back for more.

What Is Mexican Street Corn?

Mexican Street Corn (Elote) is a popular Mexican street food that elevates the standard corn-on-the-cob experience. It’s made by grilling fresh corn and topping it with a homemade chili-lime blend. The combination of smoky, sweet, and spicy flavors creates an irresistible and unique dish that is perfect for barbecues and social gatherings.

Ingredients

To make Mexican Street Corn, you’ll need:

  • 8-10 fresh ears of corn
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated cotija cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons canned chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Butter, for grilling
  • Optional toppings: diced jalapenos, lime wedges, and additional cotija cheese

Method

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Shuck the corn and remove all the silk. Then, lightly coat each ear of corn with butter.
  3. Place the corn on the grill and cook for about 10-15 minutes, rotating frequently to ensure even browning. The corn should be tender yet still slightly firm.
  4. While the corn is grilling, blend together the mayonnaise, sour cream, cotija cheese, cilantro, chipotle peppers, chili powder, garlic powder, lime juice, salt, and black pepper in a medium-sized bowl.
  5. Once the corn is done grilling, remove it from the heat and let it cool slightly. This will make it more comfortable to handle and prevent any burnt tongues!
  6. One by one, dip each ear of corn into the prepared sauce, ensuring complete coverage. Garnish the coated corn with additional toppings, such as diced jalapenos, lime wedges, and extra cotija cheese.
  7. Serve immediately with extra sauce on the side for added flavor.

Taste the Difference

Mexican Street Corn offers a unique and irresistible flavor that will elevate your barbecue offerings. The combination of smoky, sweet, and spicy elements, as well as the accompanying tangy sauce, will keep your guests craving more.

Variations

For a playful twist, try adding some heat by including diced jalapenos or substituting chipotle peppers with serrano or habanero peppers. Want a creamier texture? Use a combination of sour cream and Greek yogurt instead of plain sour cream. And for vegan friends, tofu corn or even cauliflower steaks can be a delicious alternative.

Conclusion:

Mexican Street Corn is the perfect way to jazz up your barbecue routine and please your guests. Not only is it honestly delicious, but it’s also a fun and interactive way to customize their own toppings and make their perfect corn-on-the-cob. So get grilling and experience the vibrant world of Mexican cuisine through this amazing dish!
