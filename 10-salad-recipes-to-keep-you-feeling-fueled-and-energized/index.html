10 Salad Recipes to Keep You Feeling Fueled and Energized


Are you tired of the same old boring salads? Do you want to add some excitement and flavor to your meals? Look no further! In this blog post, we have curated a list of 10 delicious salad recipes that will keep you feeling fueled and energized throughout the day. These salads are packed with nutrients, bursting with flavors, and are perfect for any occasion. So, without further ado, let’s dive into these amazing salad recipes!

1. Greek Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and Kalamata olives.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, chopped dill, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese on top.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!

2. Asian Sesame Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, shredded chicken, carrots, cucumber, red bell pepper, and green onions.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together sesame seeds, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and sesame oil.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Garnish with additional sesame seeds.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

3. Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, parsley, and mint.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Let the salad marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!

4. Southwest Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced avocado
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, black beans, corn kernels, red bell pepper, avocado, and cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

5. Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella balls
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella balls, and chopped basil.
  2. Drizzle balsamic glaze and olive oil over the salad.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

6. Thai Beef Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine cooked beef, mixed greens, cucumber, red onion, and cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, fish sauce, sugar, and chili flakes.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

7. Berry Spinach Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine fresh spinach, sliced strawberries, blueberries, crumbled goat cheese, and chopped walnuts.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and honey.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

8. Mexican Street Corn Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups grilled corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine grilled corn kernels, crumbled cotija cheese, and chopped cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, lime juice, chili powder, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

9. Spinach and Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine baby spinach, cooked quinoa, sliced strawberries, and crumbled feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, honey, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

10. Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Caesar dressing
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chopped romaine lettuce and grated Parmesan cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together Caesar dressing, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

These 10 salad recipes are not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients to keep you feeling fueled and energized throughout the day. Whether you’re looking for a light lunch, a refreshing dinner, or a side dish to complement your main course, these salads have got you covered. So, go ahead and try these recipes and elevate your salad game to a whole new level! Enjoy!
