Flavorful Mexican Street Corn Cups: A Fiesta in Every Bite


Are you ready to elevate your next fiesta with a mouthwatering dish that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than these delightful Mexican Street Corn Cups! This recipe takes the beloved Mexican street corn, or elote, to a whole new level by transforming it into convenient, individual servings. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, a Cinco de Mayo celebration, or simply craving a zesty snack, these corn cups are guaranteed to steal the show.

Ingredients

  • 6 ears of fresh corn, husks removed
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cotija cheese, crumbled
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 small cups, such as shot glasses or mini mason jars

Instructions

Step 1: Grilling the Corn

Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. Place the ears of corn directly on the grill and cook, turning occasionally, until the kernels are charred in spots, about 10-12 minutes. Once done, remove the corn from the grill and allow it to cool slightly.

Step 2: Preparing the Sauce

In a mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, cilantro, half of the crumbled cotija cheese, chili powder, cayenne pepper, minced garlic, and lime juice. Stir the ingredients until well combined. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3: Cutting the Corn Kernels

Once the grilled corn has cooled, stand each ear on its wide end and use a sharp knife to carefully cut the kernels from the cob.

Step 4: Assembling the Corn Cups

Divide the corn kernels evenly among the small cups. Spoon a generous amount of the prepared sauce over the corn in each cup, ensuring it’s well coated. Sprinkle the remaining cotija cheese on top of each cup for an extra burst of flavor.

Step 5: Serving and Enjoying

These Flavorful Mexican Street Corn Cups are best served immediately. Garnish with additional cilantro and a sprinkle of chili powder for a vibrant presentation. Your guests can simply grab a cup and enjoy the fiesta of flavors with every bite!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Convenience: The individual serving cups make these corn cups perfect for gatherings, eliminating the mess of traditional elote while preserving the authentic flavors.
  • Bold Flavors: From the charred sweetness of the corn to the tangy, creamy sauce, each bite offers a symphony of flavors that will keep your taste buds dancing.
  • Versatility: These corn cups are a versatile dish that can be served as an appetizer, a side dish, or even a standalone snack.

So, what are you waiting for? Add a vibrant touch to your next gathering with these Flavorful Mexican Street Corn Cups. Your guests will be raving about this innovative twist on a classic favorite, and you’ll be hailed as the culinary hero of the party. Get ready to savor the essence of a Mexican street fiesta in every delectable bite!
