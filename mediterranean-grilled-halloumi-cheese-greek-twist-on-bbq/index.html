Mediterranean Grilled Halloumi Cheese: Greek Twist on BBQ


If you’re looking to elevate your summer grilling game and infuse your meals with bold Mediterranean flavors, look no further than Halloumi cheese. Often hailed as the perfect grilling cheese, Halloumi’s firm texture and slightly salty, savory taste make it an excellent choice for those seeking a new twist on traditional barbecue fare.

In this post, we’ll dive deep into how to prepare Mediterranean Grilled Halloumi Cheese with a Greek flair. This dish is not only mouth-watering but also a great way to incorporate healthy, fresh ingredients into your cooking routine. Whether you’re a seasoned griller or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is simple, satisfying, and guaranteed to be a crowd-pleaser.

What is Halloumi Cheese?

Halloumi cheese hails from Cyprus, with a long-standing tradition in Greek and Mediterranean cuisine. This semi-hard, unripened cheese is made from a mixture of goat’s and sheep’s milk, though some variations may include cow’s milk. Halloumi stands out for its high melting point, which allows it to be grilled or fried without losing its shape. This unique property makes it an ideal addition to BBQs, offering a deliciously crispy exterior while maintaining a tender interior.

The taste of Halloumi is slightly tangy and savory, with a salty edge. Its dense texture makes it perfect for pairing with fresh vegetables, drizzling with olive oil, or seasoning with herbs. Whether served as an appetizer, a side dish, or the main feature of your grilling platter, Halloumi is versatile and delicious.

Why Grilled Halloumi Cheese is Perfect for BBQ

Barbecuing Halloumi cheese is a culinary treat for many reasons:

  1. Texture: Halloumi doesn’t melt like most cheeses, so it holds up beautifully on the grill. The result is a crispy, golden crust that contrasts perfectly with its tender, slightly chewy center.

  2. Quick & Easy: Unlike many grilled proteins or vegetables, Halloumi cooks quickly—usually within just a few minutes on each side. This makes it a time-saving option for grilling lovers.

  3. Flavor Absorption: Halloumi cheese’s ability to absorb the flavors of marinades and seasonings means you can customize it to fit any taste profile. From herby Mediterranean flavors to spicy or citrusy notes, the options are limitless.

  4. Diet-Friendly: Halloumi is an excellent source of protein and calcium, making it a great option for vegetarians, or anyone looking for an alternative to meat. It’s also relatively high in fat, so it provides a satisfying meal on its own or when paired with salads, grains, and vegetables.

The Mediterranean Flavor Profile: Greek Twist on Grilled Halloumi

The Mediterranean diet is known for its emphasis on fresh, healthy ingredients like olive oil, herbs, vegetables, and lean proteins. By incorporating a Greek twist into your grilled Halloumi cheese recipe, you’ll add layers of rich flavors that are both satisfying and vibrant.

Some signature Greek ingredients that pair perfectly with Halloumi include:

  • Extra virgin olive oil: A staple in Mediterranean cooking, olive oil enhances the richness of Halloumi cheese while adding its own distinctive fruity and peppery notes.
  • Lemon: The acidity of lemon balances the saltiness of Halloumi and brightens the overall flavor of the dish.
  • Oregano: A quintessential herb in Greek cuisine, oregano offers an earthy and slightly spicy flavor that complements the creamy texture of Halloumi.
  • Garlic: A touch of garlic infuses the dish with aromatic warmth, making it an irresistible treat.
  • Fresh vegetables: Pairing Halloumi with fresh, seasonal vegetables like tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and bell peppers adds crunch, color, and nutrition.

Now that we understand the Mediterranean influence, let’s dive into the steps for preparing this delightful Greek-inspired BBQ dish.

Ingredients You’ll Need

For this recipe, you’ll need the following:

  • 250g of Halloumi cheese (about one block)
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano (or fresh if you have it)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Zest of half a lemon
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (mint or parsley) for garnish
  • Optional: Fresh tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions for serving

Instructions for Grilled Halloumi Cheese

1. Preheat the Grill

Before you begin, it’s important to preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Whether you’re using a charcoal or gas grill, the key is to achieve a consistent and evenly distributed heat, which ensures that the Halloumi will cook evenly.

2. Prepare the Halloumi

Cut the Halloumi into thick slices (about 1/2 inch each). The thickness of the slices is important because it allows the cheese to hold up on the grill without falling apart. Thicker slices also provide a satisfying contrast between the crispy exterior and tender interior.

3. Marinate the Halloumi

In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, and oregano. Whisk together until well combined. This marinade will infuse the Halloumi with flavor while adding a bit of moisture to help prevent the cheese from drying out on the grill.

Place the Halloumi slices into a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Make sure each slice is coated evenly. Allow the cheese to marinate for at least 10-15 minutes. If you have more time, marinating for up to 30 minutes will deepen the flavors.

4. Grill the Halloumi

Once the grill is preheated, lightly grease the grill grates with some olive oil to prevent the Halloumi from sticking. Place the Halloumi slices directly on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy. You’ll know it’s ready when you see beautiful grill marks and the cheese has developed a crispy, golden crust.

If you’re grilling vegetables alongside your Halloumi, place them on the grill at the same time and cook until they’re tender and charred.

5. Serve & Garnish

Once the Halloumi is perfectly grilled, remove it from the grill and place it on a serving platter. Garnish with freshly chopped mint or parsley for a pop of color and freshness. You can also serve the cheese with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to brighten the flavors.

For a complete Mediterranean meal, serve the grilled Halloumi alongside fresh Greek salads, pita bread, hummus, or roasted vegetables. The smoky flavor of the cheese pairs beautifully with the crunch and freshness of the veggies.

Variations & Tips for the Perfect Grilled Halloumi

While the recipe we’ve shared is delicious on its own, there are plenty of ways to put your own spin on it:

  1. Add Spice: If you like heat, sprinkle a bit of crushed red pepper flakes over the Halloumi before grilling. The spicy kick complements the richness of the cheese perfectly.

  2. Herb Infusion: You can experiment with different herbs in the marinade. Try using thyme, rosemary, or basil for an extra layer of Mediterranean flavor.

  3. Grilled Vegetables: Add grilled zucchini, eggplant, or bell peppers to your plate for a truly Mediterranean experience. You can even toss the grilled vegetables with a drizzle of balsamic vinegar and olive oil for an added burst of flavor.

  4. Halloumi Skewers: For a fun twist, skewer cubes of Halloumi, cherry tomatoes, and bell peppers for a Mediterranean kabob. Grill them as you would the Halloumi slices and serve them with your favorite dipping sauce or yogurt.

  5. Sweet & Savory: Try pairing your grilled Halloumi with some honey or balsamic glaze. The sweet and savory combination will be a surprising yet delightful contrast to the salty cheese.

Health Benefits of Halloumi Cheese

In addition to being incredibly delicious, Halloumi offers a variety of nutritional benefits:

  • High in Protein: Halloumi is an excellent source of protein, making it a great choice for vegetarians or anyone looking to reduce their meat consumption while still maintaining a balanced diet.
  • Rich in Calcium: Like many cheeses, Halloumi is rich in calcium, which supports bone health and can help prevent osteoporosis.
  • Contains Healthy Fats: While Halloumi does contain saturated fats, it also provides healthy monounsaturated fats from olive oil (if used in marinades), which support cardiovascular health.

Conclusion

Grilled Halloumi cheese with a Greek twist is a delicious, easy-to-make dish that brings the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean to your BBQ. With its unique texture, ability to absorb flavors, and versatility, Halloumi is the perfect cheese for grilling season. Whether you’re preparing it for a casual weekend cookout, a dinner party, or as a wholesome weeknight meal, this recipe is sure to impress your guests and satisfy your taste buds.

So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and enjoy a fresh and flavorful Greek-inspired meal that’s bound to become a staple in your summer recipe rotation!
