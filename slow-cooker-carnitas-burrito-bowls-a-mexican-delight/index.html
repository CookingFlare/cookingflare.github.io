Slow Cooker Carnitas Burrito Bowls: A Mexican Delight


Are you craving a flavorful and satisfying Mexican dish? Look no further than these Slow Cooker Carnitas Burrito Bowls! Packed with tender and juicy carnitas, fresh ingredients, and bold flavors, these burrito bowls are a true delight for your taste buds. The best part? You can prepare them effortlessly using your slow cooker. So, let’s dive into the recipe and create a Mexican culinary masterpiece!

Ingredients

For the Carnitas:

  • 2 pounds pork shoulder, trimmed and cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Juice of 2 oranges
  • Juice of 2 limes

For the Burrito Bowls:

  • Cooked white or brown rice
  • Black beans, drained and rinsed
  • Sliced avocado
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Chopped red onion
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Sour cream
  • Sliced jalapenos (optional)
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the pork shoulder, onion, garlic, cumin, oregano, chili powder, salt, black pepper, chicken broth, orange juice, and lime juice. Mix well to ensure the pork is evenly coated with the spices.

  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 8-10 hours or on high heat for 4-6 hours, until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  3. Once the pork is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Set aside.

  4. Strain the cooking liquid from the slow cooker into a saucepan. Bring it to a boil over medium-high heat and cook until it reduces and thickens slightly, about 10 minutes. This will become a flavorful sauce for your carnitas.

  5. In the meantime, prepare the burrito bowl ingredients. Cook the rice according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse the black beans. Slice the avocado, chop the tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro. Set aside.

  6. Assemble your burrito bowls by starting with a base of cooked rice. Top it with a generous portion of the shredded carnitas, black beans, avocado slices, chopped tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro. Drizzle the reduced cooking liquid over the top.

  7. Finish off your burrito bowls with a dollop of sour cream, sliced jalapenos if you like some heat, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice for that extra zing.

  8. Serve your Slow Cooker Carnitas Burrito Bowls immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors in every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • To add a smoky flavor to your carnitas, you can sear the shredded meat in a hot skillet for a few minutes before serving.

  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add diced green chilies or a dash of hot sauce to the slow cooker along with the other ingredients.

  • Customize your burrito bowls by adding your favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, salsa, guacamole, or pickled jalapenos.

  • Leftover carnitas can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. They can be reheated in the microwave or on the stovetop.

So there you have it, a tantalizing recipe for Slow Cooker Carnitas Burrito Bowls that will transport you straight to the vibrant streets of Mexico. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights or casual gatherings with friends and family. Give it a try and let your taste buds dance to the rhythm of Mexican cuisine!
