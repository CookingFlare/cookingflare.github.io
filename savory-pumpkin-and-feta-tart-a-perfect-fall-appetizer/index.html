Savory Pumpkin and Feta Tart: A Perfect Fall Appetizer


Who says pumpkins are just for carving and pumpkin spice lattes? This fall, let’s take our love for pumpkins to the next level with a delicious and savory pumpkin and feta tart. Perfect as an appetizer for any autumn gathering, this recipe combines the earthy sweetness of pumpkin with the tangy creaminess of feta cheese, all nestled in a flaky pastry crust. Get ready to impress your guests with this unique and flavorful dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water
  • 1 small sugar pumpkin, peeled, seeded, and cut into thin slices
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix. Form the dough into a ball, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. While the dough is chilling, prepare the pumpkin. In a large bowl, toss the pumpkin slices with olive oil, dried thyme, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well-coated.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and cook the pumpkin slices for 2-3 minutes on each side until they begin to soften. Remove from heat and set aside.
  6. Remove the chilled dough from the refrigerator and roll it out on a lightly floured surface into a circle or rectangle, depending on your tart pan shape.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a tart pan, pressing it gently into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough.
  8. Sprinkle half of the crumbled feta cheese over the bottom of the tart crust. Arrange the cooked pumpkin slices on top, overlapping them slightly.
  9. Sprinkle the remaining feta cheese over the pumpkin slices and drizzle with a little olive oil. Top with chopped fresh parsley.
  10. Place the tart on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the pumpkin is tender.
  11. Remove from the oven and allow the tart to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This savory pumpkin and feta tart is best served warm or at room temperature. It makes a fantastic appetizer for a fall dinner party or a delightful addition to a cozy brunch. Pair it with a crisp green salad or a side of roasted vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal. The combination of the creamy feta cheese and the tender pumpkin slices is simply irresistible.

Conclusion

Embrace the flavors of fall with this savory pumpkin and feta tart. It’s an easy and impressive appetizer that will wow your guests and leave them craving for more. The delicate balance of sweet pumpkin, tangy feta cheese, and buttery pastry crust is a true taste sensation. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and your guests will be begging for the recipe! Enjoy the autumn season with this delicious and unique fall appetizer.
