Delicious Vegan Cookie Recipes for Every Occasion


Vegan cookies have come a long way! Gone are the days when vegan treats were bland and boring. Today, we celebrate the rich flavors, delightful textures, and endless variety of vegan cookies that can satisfy any sweet tooth. Whether you’re baking for a party, a family gathering, or just a cozy night in, we have a collection of delicious vegan cookie recipes perfect for every occasion.

The Foundations of Vegan Baking

Before diving into the recipes, let’s quickly touch on some essential ingredients that make vegan cookies not only possible but incredibly tasty.

Key Ingredients

  • Flours: All-purpose flour is a staple, but you can also experiment with whole wheat flour, almond flour, or oat flour for different textures and flavors.
  • Sweeteners: Traditional sugar works, but coconut sugar, maple syrup, and agave nectar can add unique flavors and are often healthier alternatives.
  • Fats: Instead of butter, use coconut oil, vegan butter, or nut butters. These fats add moisture and richness to your cookies.
  • Binders: Flaxseed meal or chia seeds mixed with water can replace eggs and create that necessary binding quality in your dough.
  • Leavening Agents: Baking soda and baking powder are essential to give your cookies that perfect rise.

Classic Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut oil (solid, not melted)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the solid coconut oil and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the almond milk and vanilla extract, mixing until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined.
  6. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden.
  9. Let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

These cookies are soft, chewy, and loaded with chocolate chips—perfect for any chocolate lover!

Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the peanut butter, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Stir in the rolled oats, baking soda, and salt until all ingredients are well combined.
  4. If you’re using chocolate chips, fold them into the dough.
  5. Drop tablespoon-sized portions onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  6. Flatten each cookie slightly with a fork.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are lightly browned.
  8. Allow to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

These cookies are hearty and satisfying, making them a great snack for any time of day!

Double Chocolate Vegan Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil (melted)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the melted coconut oil and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Add the almond milk and vanilla extract, mixing until well blended.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined.
  6. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are firm.
  9. Cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

Rich and indulgent, these double chocolate cookies are a must-try for anyone who loves a good chocolate fix!

Coconut Macaroons

Ingredients

  • 2 cups unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate (for drizzling, optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the shredded coconut, almond flour, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.
  3. Using your hands, form small balls (about 1 inch) and place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until the tops are golden brown.
  5. Let cool completely. If desired, melt the vegan chocolate and drizzle it over the cooled macaroons.

These coconut macaroons are chewy, sweet, and perfect for any occasion, especially tropical-themed parties or summer gatherings.

Snickerdoodle Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut oil (solid)
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon (for rolling)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the solid coconut oil and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Beat in the almond milk and vanilla extract.
  4. In another bowl, combine the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture until combined.
  6. In a small bowl, mix the cinnamon and 1/4 cup sugar for rolling.
  7. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough, roll them into balls, and then roll in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  8. Place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  9. Allow to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

These snickerdoodles are soft, sweet, and have that signature cinnamon flavor that makes them irresistible!

Almond Joy Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the almond flour, shredded coconut, maple syrup, almond milk, and vanilla extract until combined.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chips and chopped almonds.
  4. Drop tablespoons of dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden.
  6. Allow to cool before serving.

These cookies pack all the flavors of an Almond Joy candy bar in a wholesome, vegan package!

Tips for Perfect Vegan Cookies

  1. Don’t Overmix: When combining your wet and dry ingredients, mix until just combined to ensure your cookies remain tender.
  2. Chill the Dough: If you want thicker cookies, chill the dough for at least 30 minutes before baking.
  3. Experiment with Mix-Ins: Add your favorite nuts, dried fruits, or seeds for a personal touch.
  4. Monitor Baking Time: Every oven is different, so keep an eye on your cookies to prevent overbaking.

Conclusion

Vegan baking can be a delightful adventure filled with creativity and flavor. These delicious vegan cookie recipes cater to every occasion, ensuring that those following a plant-based diet or simply looking for healthier options can indulge in sweet treats without compromise. From classic chocolate chip cookies to tropical coconut delights, there’s something here for everyone. So gather your ingredients, unleash your inner baker, and enjoy the scrumptious world of vegan cookies!
