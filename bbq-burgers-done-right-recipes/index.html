BBQ Burgers Done Right: Recipes


Who doesn’t love the mouthwatering aroma and satisfying taste of a perfectly cooked hamburger on the grill? Transform your backyard barbecue into a feast to remember with these extraordinary burger recipes that will have your family and friends begging for seconds.

  1. The Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • All-purpose flour for dusting
  • 4 sesame seed hamburger buns
  • Butter for toasting buns
  • 4 slices of your preferred cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion, for topping
  • Homemade or store-bought burger sauce

Instructions: A. In a medium-sized bowl, mix the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, Worcestershire sauce, and salt and pepper to taste. Be careful not to over-mix the ingredients. B. Divide the mixture into four equal portions and shape into round patties. C. Dust the patties with flour on both sides to prevent sticking to the grill. D. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 160°F on a meat thermometer. E.Toast the buns on the grill or at home using butter. Place a slice of cheese on the cooked patties to melt. F. Assemble the burgers with your choice of toppings and enjoy your delicious Classic Cheeseburger.

  1. Smoky Jalapeño Popper Burgers

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  • 1/2 cup diced jalapeño peppers
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons barbecue sauce
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Cleverly crafted jalapeño popper-stuffed burgers
  • 4 brioche buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion

Instructions: A. Preheat oven to 375°F. B. Mix ground beef, jalapeño peppers, onion, cheddar cheese, barbecue sauce, salt, and pepper in a bowl. C. Form the mixture into eight burgers, each containing one stuffed jalapeño pepper. D. Heat a large, oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for a few minutes on both sides until nicely browned. E. Place the skillet into the oven and bake for roughly 10 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F on a meat thermometer. F. Top with your preferred toppings and serve with a side salad for a complete meal.

  1. Greek-Style Lamb Burgers

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground lamb
  • 1/2 cup chopped kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 slider buns
  • Tzatziki sauce, lettuce, cucumber slices for topping

Instructions: A. Mix ground lamb, kalamata olives, chopped onion, feta cheese, mint, minced garlic, salt, and pepper in a bowl. B. Divide the mixture into eight portions and form into patties, about 2 tablespoons each. C. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F on a meat thermometer. D. Assemble the burgers with tzatziki sauce, lettuce, and cucumber slices.

Now, gather your family and friends for the ultimate backyard barbecue experience. After you’ve tried these delightful burger recipes, you’ll never want to settle for just an ordinary burger again. Munch away and indulge in your new favorite burger creations!
