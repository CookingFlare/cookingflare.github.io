Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Tart: A Decadent Delight


If you’re looking for a show-stopping dessert that’s not only delicious but also vegan, then look no further than this Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Tart. This indulgent treat combines the rich flavors of chocolate and toasted hazelnuts in a creamy, dreamy filling, all nestled in a crisp, buttery crust. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving something sweet, this tart is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of coconut oil, solid but not cold
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • A pinch of salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups of raw hazelnuts
  • 1 1/2 cups of full-fat coconut milk
  • 12 ounces of dairy-free dark chocolate, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a food processor, combine the flour, coconut oil, maple syrup, and salt. Pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs and starts to come together.

  3. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom. Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork.

  4. Bake the crust for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown. Let it cool completely before adding the filling.

Step 2: Toast the Hazelnuts

  1. Spread the hazelnuts in a single layer on a baking sheet.

  2. Toast the hazelnuts in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the skins are blistered and the nuts are fragrant.

  3. Transfer the hot hazelnuts to a clean kitchen towel and rub them to remove the skins. Don’t worry if some skin remains.

  4. Allow the hazelnuts to cool completely.

Step 3: Make the Filling

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the coconut milk over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.

  2. Place the chopped chocolate in a heatproof bowl. Pour the hot coconut milk over the chocolate and let it sit for 1-2 minutes to melt the chocolate.

  3. Gently whisk the chocolate and coconut milk together until smooth and well combined.

  4. Add the maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt to the chocolate mixture. Stir until everything is fully incorporated.

  5. Coarsely chop the toasted hazelnuts and fold them into the chocolate filling.

Step 4: Assemble the Tart

  1. Pour the chocolate hazelnut filling into the cooled tart crust.

  2. Smooth the top with a spatula and refrigerate the tart for at least 4 hours, or until set.

  3. Before serving, you can garnish the tart with additional chopped hazelnuts or a sprinkle of cocoa powder, if desired.

Serving and Storing

This Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Tart is best served chilled. It pairs beautifully with a dollop of dairy-free whipped cream or a scoop of vegan vanilla ice cream. Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator, covered, for up to 5 days. Simply bring the tart to room temperature for a few minutes before serving to allow the filling to soften slightly.

Final Thoughts

Indulging in a slice of this Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Tart is an experience to savor. The contrast between the crunchy, nutty crust and the silky, chocolatey filling creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave you craving more. Whether you follow a vegan lifestyle or simply appreciate a delectable dessert, this tart is a must-try. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to create a decadent delight that’s as impressive as it is irresistible.
