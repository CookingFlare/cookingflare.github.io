Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Tart: Decadent and Nutty


Are you in the mood for a rich, indulgent dessert that combines the deep flavors of chocolate with the crunch of hazelnuts? If so, this vegan chocolate hazelnut tart is a must-try. It’s the perfect treat for any occasion, from holiday feasts to casual gatherings with friends. The best part? It’s completely plant-based, making it suitable for those following a vegan lifestyle while still offering all the richness and decadence you’d expect from a classic chocolate dessert.

In this blog post, we’ll guide you step-by-step through the process of making this luscious tart. We’ll explore the ingredients, preparation tips, and techniques needed to create a dessert that will not only satisfy your sweet tooth but also impress anyone who gets a bite. Whether you’re new to vegan baking or a seasoned pro, this tart will quickly become a favorite in your dessert repertoire.

Why Choose a Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Tart?

A vegan chocolate hazelnut tart is a wonderful choice for several reasons:

  • Dairy-Free and Egg-Free: Perfect for vegans or anyone with lactose intolerance, this recipe uses plant-based alternatives to create the same creamy, rich texture you’d find in a traditional chocolate tart.
  • Nutty and Chocolatey: The combination of hazelnuts and chocolate is a match made in heaven. The nutty flavor of the hazelnuts complements the deep, rich taste of chocolate, making every bite an irresistible experience.
  • Gluten-Free Option: With a few tweaks to the crust, this recipe can also be made gluten-free, allowing more people to enjoy it.
  • Simple Ingredients: You don’t need any hard-to-find or expensive ingredients. Everything you need for this vegan chocolate hazelnut tart is easy to find in most grocery stores.
  • Impressive Yet Easy to Make: While the tart looks and tastes incredibly fancy, it’s surprisingly easy to make. With just a few simple steps, you’ll have a dessert that looks as stunning as it tastes.

The Ingredients: A Perfect Balance of Rich and Nutty

Creating the best vegan chocolate hazelnut tart requires high-quality ingredients that balance both the richness of chocolate and the earthiness of hazelnuts. Here’s a breakdown of everything you’ll need:

For the Crust:

  • Almond flour: Almond flour gives the crust a nutty, slightly sweet flavor that complements the hazelnuts and chocolate. It’s also naturally gluten-free.
  • Oats: For texture and bulk, oats help hold the crust together while providing a hearty, wholesome taste.
  • Coconut oil: Coconut oil helps bind the crust ingredients together and provides a subtle coconut flavor, which enhances the overall depth of the tart.
  • Maple syrup: Maple syrup acts as a natural sweetener for the crust, giving it a gentle sweetness that’s not overwhelming.
  • Pinch of salt: A little salt balances out the sweetness and enhances the other flavors.

For the Filling:

  • Dark chocolate: The star of the tart. Choose a high-quality dark chocolate with at least 70% cocoa to ensure a rich, bittersweet flavor.
  • Coconut milk: Full-fat coconut milk creates a creamy, smooth texture for the filling while keeping it dairy-free.
  • Hazelnut butter: Hazelnut butter is key for adding that nutty flavor to the filling. You can easily find it in most grocery stores, or you can make your own by blending roasted hazelnuts in a food processor.
  • Vanilla extract: A dash of vanilla extract rounds out the flavors of the filling and adds an extra layer of depth.
  • Maple syrup: A little maple syrup helps sweeten the filling without overpowering the natural chocolate and hazelnut flavors.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt helps intensify the chocolatey goodness and balances the sweetness of the maple syrup.

For Garnishing:

  • Roasted hazelnuts: To top the tart, use whole roasted hazelnuts. These add both crunch and a nutty aroma that complements the filling beautifully.
  • Shaved chocolate or cocoa powder: A sprinkle of shaved dark chocolate or a light dusting of cocoa powder gives the tart a sophisticated, finished look.

The Process: Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a tart pan with a removable bottom (about 9 inches in diameter) or line it with parchment paper.

  2. In a food processor, combine the almond flour, oats, and salt. Pulse a few times to combine.

  3. Add the coconut oil and maple syrup to the dry ingredients. Pulse until the mixture starts to come together and forms a dough-like consistency. If it’s too dry, add a teaspoon of water at a time until the dough sticks together.

  4. Transfer the dough to the prepared tart pan. Press the dough evenly into the bottom and up the sides of the pan. Make sure it’s compact, so the crust holds together well when baked.

  5. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 10–12 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove it from the oven and allow it to cool completely before filling.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. While the crust cools, begin preparing the chocolate filling. In a heatproof bowl, chop the dark chocolate into small pieces.

  2. Heat the coconut milk in a saucepan over medium heat until it just begins to simmer. Remove it from the heat and pour it over the chopped chocolate. Let it sit for a minute or two to melt the chocolate.

  3. After the chocolate has melted, stir the mixture gently to combine. The chocolate should become smooth and glossy.

  4. Add the hazelnut butter, vanilla extract, and maple syrup to the chocolate mixture. Stir until everything is well incorporated. Taste the mixture and adjust the sweetness if needed.

  5. Add a pinch of salt to enhance the flavor of the chocolate. Stir well.

Step 3: Assemble the Tart

  1. Once the crust has cooled, pour the chocolate-hazelnut filling into the tart crust. Use a spatula to smooth the top and ensure an even layer.

  2. Refrigerate the tart for at least 3 hours, or until the filling is firm and set. If you’re in a hurry, you can place it in the freezer for 1 hour to speed up the process.

Step 4: Garnish and Serve

  1. After the tart has set, remove it from the fridge and top it with roasted hazelnuts and a sprinkle of shaved chocolate or cocoa powder for an extra touch of elegance.

  2. Slice the tart and serve chilled. It’s best enjoyed after allowing it to sit at room temperature for about 10 minutes, as this enhances the flavors and makes it easier to slice.

Tips for Making the Perfect Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Tart

  • Use high-quality chocolate: The chocolate is the star of this tart, so opt for a high-quality dark chocolate with at least 70% cocoa for the best flavor.
  • Make it gluten-free: To make this tart gluten-free, simply swap out the oats for gluten-free oats or use a gluten-free flour blend in place of the almond flour.
  • Experiment with flavors: Feel free to get creative with the flavor profile. You can add a dash of cinnamon, a pinch of sea salt, or even some orange zest to the filling for a unique twist.
  • Hazelnut substitute: If you don’t have hazelnut butter or want a different flavor, try substituting it with almond butter or cashew butter.
  • Top with other nuts: While roasted hazelnuts are the traditional topping, you can also use chopped almonds, pistachios, or walnuts for a different texture and flavor.
  • Make it ahead of time: This tart can be made a day or two in advance, making it perfect for parties or gatherings. Simply store it in an airtight container in the fridge until ready to serve.

Why You’ll Love This Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Tart

This vegan chocolate hazelnut tart is the ultimate dessert for chocolate lovers. With its creamy filling, nutty crust, and rich chocolate flavor, it’s sure to satisfy even the most discerning dessert connoisseur. It’s perfect for celebrations, dinner parties, or simply as an indulgent treat after a long week.

By making this tart vegan-friendly, you’re not only offering a delicious dessert but also catering to a variety of dietary preferences. Whether you’re vegan, gluten-free, or just someone who loves a good chocolate dessert, this tart will undoubtedly become a favorite.

So, gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and treat yourself to a slice of pure indulgence. Your taste buds will thank you!
