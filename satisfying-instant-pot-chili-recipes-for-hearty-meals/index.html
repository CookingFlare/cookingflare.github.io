Satisfying Instant Pot Chili Recipes for Hearty Meals


Are you craving a warm and satisfying meal that’s packed with flavor? Look no further than these mouthwatering Instant Pot chili recipes! Whether you’re a meat lover, vegetarian, or looking for a twist on the classic recipe, we have something for everyone. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have a delicious chili on the table in no time. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Beef Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and brown the ground beef until cooked through.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, and bell pepper to the pot and sauté until softened.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before quick-releasing any remaining pressure.
  6. Give the chili a good stir and serve hot with your favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped green onions.

2. Vegetarian Quinoa Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can corn, drained
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook the onion, garlic, and bell pepper until softened.
  2. Add the quinoa, diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to the pot.
  3. Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 8 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes before quick-releasing any remaining pressure.
  6. Give the chili a good stir and serve hot with a dollop of Greek yogurt and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro.

3. White Chicken Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced green chilies
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lime juice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook the chicken, onion, and garlic until the chicken is browned.
  2. Add the white beans, diced green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper to the pot.
  3. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes before quick-releasing any remaining pressure.
  5. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the chili before serving to add a burst of tangy flavor.

Conclusion

With these satisfying Instant Pot chili recipes, you can easily whip up a hearty and flavorful meal for yourself and your loved ones. Whether you prefer the classic beef chili, a vegetarian quinoa chili, or a white chicken chili, your Instant Pot will help you achieve delicious results in a fraction of the time. So fire up that Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy these comforting chili recipes. Happy cooking!

Note: The Instant Pot cooking times mentioned in this post are based on general guidelines. Please refer to your specific Instant Pot model’s manual for precise cooking instructions.
