Buttery Croissant Rolls: Flaky and Delicious


If you’re looking for a delightful baking project that will fill your kitchen with the irresistible aroma of freshly baked goods, then making Buttery Croissant Rolls is the perfect choice. These flaky and buttery delights are a true indulgence, ideal for a weekend breakfast or a special treat any time of the day. Let’s dive into the step-by-step process of creating these heavenly croissant rolls right in your own kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 ¼ cups warm whole milk
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups unsalted butter, cold
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, combine the warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.

  2. Mix the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt. Cut 1 cup of cold butter into small cubes and mix it into the dry ingredients. Pour in the yeast mixture and knead until a smooth dough forms.

  3. Create the Butter Block: On a sheet of parchment paper, place the remaining ½ cup of cold butter between two sheets. Use a rolling pin to flatten the butter into a square about ½ inch thick. Refrigerate for 15 minutes.

  4. Fold the Dough: Roll out the dough into a rectangle on a floured surface. Place the chilled butter block in the center and fold the dough over it like an envelope. Roll out the dough again and fold it in thirds. Repeat this process twice more, then refrigerate for 30 minutes.

  5. Shape the Croissants: Roll out the dough into a large rectangle. Cut it into triangles and roll each triangle from the base to the tip to form the croissant shape. Place the croissants on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  6. Proof and Bake: Cover the croissants with a kitchen towel and let them proof at room temperature for 1-2 hours until they double in size. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Brush the croissants with the beaten egg wash and bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.

  7. Enjoy the Flaky Goodness: Once baked to perfection, let the croissant rolls cool slightly before serving. These Buttery Croissant Rolls are best enjoyed fresh and warm, with a cup of your favorite coffee or tea.

Indulge in the buttery, flaky layers of these homemade croissant rolls that will surely impress your family and friends. Whether you savor them plain or fill them with your favorite ingredients like ham and cheese, these delicious treats are a labor of love that will elevate your baking skills to new heights. Happy baking!

Remember, the key to achieving the perfect Buttery Croissant Rolls lies in the precise balance of butter, patience during the folding process, and allowing the dough to rest and rise adequately. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to embark on a culinary journey that will reward you with heavenly pastries that melt in your mouth.
