Tangy Key Lime Pie: A Refreshing Dessert Delight


If you’re a fan of tangy and refreshing desserts, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to share with you a recipe for Tangy Key Lime Pie, a delightful dessert that will leave your taste buds craving for more. This easy-to-make pie is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a family gathering or a casual get-together with friends. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

To create this tangy and zesty Key Lime Pie, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup fresh key lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon key lime zest
  • Whipped cream, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated.

  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie dish, creating a firm and even crust. You can use the back of a spoon or a measuring cup to help press the crumbs into place.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, and key lime zest until well combined.

  6. Pour the mixture into the cooled graham cracker crust, spreading it evenly.

  7. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the filling is set. You can check the doneness by gently shaking the pie. If the center jiggles slightly, it’s ready to be removed from the oven.

  8. Once baked, remove the pie from the oven and let it cool to room temperature. Then, refrigerate it for at least 2 hours to allow it to set completely.

  9. Before serving, you can garnish the pie with a dollop of whipped cream, if desired. This will add a creamy element to balance the tanginess of the key lime filling.

  10. Slice the pie into wedges and serve chilled. Each bite will be a burst of tangy lime flavor, perfectly complemented by the sweet and crumbly graham cracker crust.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra tangy flavor, you can use a combination of key lime juice and regular lime juice in the filling.
  • If you can’t find key limes, regular limes will work just as well.
  • If you prefer a sweeter pie, you can increase the amount of sugar in the filling. However, keep in mind that key lime pie is traditionally on the tart side.
  • To make the graham cracker crust even more decadent, you can add a tablespoon of melted white chocolate to the mixture before pressing it into the pie dish.

So there you have it - a simple yet delicious recipe for Tangy Key Lime Pie. This dessert is perfect for summer, bringing a refreshing burst of flavor to your table. Whether you’re a fan of tangy desserts or just looking for something different to try, this pie is sure to impress. Give it a go and let us know what you think!
