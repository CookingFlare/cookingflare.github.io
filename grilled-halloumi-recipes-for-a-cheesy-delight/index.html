Grilled Halloumi: Recipes for a Cheesy Delight


Grilled halloumi is a true delight for cheese lovers. With its firm texture and unique taste, halloumi is an excellent choice for grilling. Whether you’re a vegetarian looking for a delicious protein-packed option or simply a cheese enthusiast, these grilled halloumi recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. From salads to sandwiches, let’s explore the versatility of this cheese and discover new ways to enjoy it!

Grilled Halloumi Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of halloumi cheese
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the halloumi cheese into thick slices.
  3. Grill the halloumi slices for about 2 minutes on each side, until they develop grill marks.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  7. Place the grilled halloumi slices on top of the salad.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Grilled Halloumi Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of halloumi cheese
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the halloumi cheese into cubes.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the halloumi cubes, red and yellow bell pepper chunks, zucchini slices, and red onion chunks.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to make the marinade.
  5. Pour the marinade over the halloumi and vegetables, and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Thread the halloumi cubes and vegetables onto skewers, alternating between them.
  7. Grill the skewers for about 3-4 minutes on each side, until the halloumi is golden brown and the vegetables are tender.
  8. Remove from the grill and serve hot.

Grilled Halloumi Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of halloumi cheese
  • 4 ciabatta rolls
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons pesto
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the halloumi cheese into thick slices.
  3. Grill the halloumi slices for about 2 minutes on each side, until they develop grill marks.
  4. Slice the ciabatta rolls in half and lightly toast them on the grill.
  5. Spread pesto on one side of each ciabatta roll.
  6. Layer the grilled halloumi slices, avocado slices, tomato slices, and fresh basil leaves on top of the pesto.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the fillings and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Close the sandwiches and press them lightly.
  9. Grill the sandwiches for an additional 2 minutes on each side, until the cheese melts and the bread turns crispy.
  10. Remove from the grill, slice in half, and serve warm.

Grilled halloumi opens up a world of cheesy possibilities. Whether you prefer it in a refreshing salad, on skewers with vegetables, or in a mouthwatering sandwich, halloumi never fails to impress. Its unique ability to hold its shape when grilled makes it an excellent addition to any meal. So, fire up the grill and indulge in the cheesy delight that is grilled halloumi!
