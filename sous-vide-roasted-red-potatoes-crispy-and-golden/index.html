Sous Vide Roasted Red Potatoes: Crispy and Golden


Potatoes are a versatile and beloved ingredient that can be prepared in countless ways. One of the most delicious and foolproof methods is by using the sous vide cooking technique. Sous vide ensures that the potatoes are evenly cooked and infused with flavor, resulting in crispy and golden roasted red potatoes that will leave you craving for more. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the steps to achieve perfection with your sous vide roasted red potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of red potatoes, washed and quartered
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your water bath to 185°F (85°C).

  2. In a large bowl, toss the quartered red potatoes with olive oil, minced garlic, and chopped rosemary. Make sure the potatoes are well coated with the mixture.

  3. Transfer the seasoned potatoes to a vacuum-sealable bag. Seal the bag using a vacuum sealer, ensuring all the air is removed. If you don’t have a vacuum sealer, you can use the water displacement method by partially sealing the bag and slowly submerging it in water, allowing the water pressure to push out the air. Once the air is removed, fully seal the bag.

  4. Place the sealed bag of potatoes into the preheated water bath, making sure it is fully submerged. Cook the potatoes for 1 hour to 1 hour and 30 minutes. This cooking time will depend on the size of your potatoes and your desired level of tenderness.

  5. While the potatoes are cooking, preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  6. Once the sous vide cooking time is complete, carefully remove the bag from the water bath. Take the potatoes out of the bag and pat them dry with a paper towel.

  7. Heat a large skillet or cast-iron pan over medium-high heat. Add a tablespoon of olive oil to the pan and let it heat up.

  8. Place the potatoes in the hot skillet, making sure they are in a single layer. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the potatoes develop a golden crust. Flip the potatoes and repeat the process on the other side.

  9. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and roast the potatoes for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until they are crispy and golden brown.

  10. Remove the skillet from the oven and season the potatoes with salt and pepper to taste. Mix them gently to ensure the seasoning is evenly distributed.

  11. Serve the sous vide roasted red potatoes hot as a delightful side dish to accompany your favorite main course.

Conclusion

With the sous vide cooking technique, you can achieve perfectly cooked red potatoes that are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. This recipe showcases the simplicity and effectiveness of sous vide cooking, allowing you to elevate your roasted red potatoes to a new level of deliciousness. Whether you’re serving them alongside a juicy steak or as a standalone snack, these crispy and golden roasted red potatoes are sure to impress. So, fire up your sous vide machine and get ready to enjoy a delightful and flavorful potato experience like never before!
