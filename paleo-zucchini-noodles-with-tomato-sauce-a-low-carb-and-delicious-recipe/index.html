Paleo Zucchini Noodles with Tomato Sauce: A Low-Carb and Delicious Recipe


In today’s fast-paced world, finding healthy and delicious meal options can be a challenge. With the rise of low-carb diets and the increasing popularity of zucchini noodles, it’s no surprise that many of us are looking for creative ways to incorporate these nutritious ingredients into our daily meals. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make Paleo Zucchini Noodles with Tomato Sauce, a dish that’s not only low in carbs but also packed with flavor and nutrients.

The Benefits of Zucchini Noodles

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a moment to talk about the benefits of zucchini noodles. Also known as “zoodles,” these spiralized noodles are a game-changer for anyone looking to reduce their carb intake. With only 2.5 grams of carbs per 100g serving, zucchini noodles are an excellent substitute for traditional pasta. They’re also rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making them a nutritious addition to any meal.

The Recipe

To make Paleo Zucchini Noodles with Tomato Sauce, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups fresh tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Spiralize the Zucchinis: Use a spiralizer or a vegetable peeler to create long, thin noodles from the zucchinis. Set aside.
  2. Heat the Olive Oil: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Sauté the Onion and Garlic: Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and sauté until the onion is translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the Tomatoes and Broth: Add the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth to the skillet, stirring to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Add the Oregano and Basil: Stir in the dried oregano and basil.
  6. Season with Salt and Pepper: Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Add the Zucchini Noodles: Add the zucchini noodles to the skillet, tossing to combine with the tomato sauce.
  8. Cook the Noodles: Cook the zucchini noodles for 3-5 minutes, or until they’re tender but still crisp.
  9. Serve: Serve the Paleo Zucchini Noodles with Tomato Sauce hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some protein to the dish, toss in some cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, add a tablespoon or two of coconut cream or heavy cream.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to give the sauce a unique flavor.

Conclusion

Paleo Zucchini Noodles with Tomato Sauce is a delicious and nutritious meal option that’s perfect for anyone looking to reduce their carb intake. With its rich flavor and creamy texture, this dish is sure to become a staple in your household. So go ahead, give it a try, and experience the benefits of zucchini noodles for yourself!
