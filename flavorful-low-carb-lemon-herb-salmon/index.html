Flavorful Low Carb Lemon Herb Salmon


Salmon is a versatile and delicious fish that is packed with healthy omega-3 fatty acids. It’s a favorite among seafood lovers, and for good reason. Today, I want to share with you a mouthwatering recipe for Flavorful Low Carb Lemon Herb Salmon. This dish is not only incredibly tasty but also low in carbohydrates, making it a perfect option for those following a low-carb or keto diet. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or foil for easy cleanup.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped parsley, dill, thyme, salt, and pepper. This flavorful marinade will infuse the salmon with a burst of fresh and fragrant flavors.

  3. Place the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet, skin-side down. Pour the marinade over the salmon, making sure to coat each fillet evenly. Let the salmon marinate for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to penetrate the fish.

  4. Once marinated, transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven. Bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until it flakes easily with a fork. Cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the fillets, so keep an eye on them to avoid overcooking.

  5. Once the salmon is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the fish to firm up slightly.

  6. Serve the Flavorful Low Carb Lemon Herb Salmon with your favorite side dishes. It pairs wonderfully with a fresh green salad, roasted vegetables, or cauliflower rice for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra zesty kick, you can add some lemon zest to the marinade. This will intensify the lemon flavor and add a lovely aroma to the dish.
  • If you prefer a milder garlic flavor, you can reduce the amount of minced garlic or omit it altogether.
  • Feel free to experiment with other fresh herbs like basil, oregano, or rosemary. Each herb will bring its own unique flavor profile to the dish, allowing you to customize it to your liking.
  • To ensure the salmon cooks evenly, choose fillets that are similar in size and thickness. This will help prevent some pieces from being overcooked while others are undercooked.

Conclusion

Flavorful Low Carb Lemon Herb Salmon is a delightful and healthy dish that will satisfy your taste buds while keeping your carb intake in check. The combination of fresh lemon, fragrant herbs, and succulent salmon creates a symphony of flavors that is sure to impress. Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking for a delicious seafood recipe, this dish is a winner. Give it a try and let your taste buds do the happy dance!
