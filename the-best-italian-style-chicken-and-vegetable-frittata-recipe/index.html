The Best Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Frittata Recipe


Welcome to another mouthwatering recipe on our cooking blog! Today, we are going to explore the delightful flavors of the Best Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Frittata. This easy-to-make dish is perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even a light dinner. Packed with protein and loaded with colorful vegetables, this frittata is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. So, let’s dive right in and learn how to create this Italian-inspired masterpiece!

Ingredients

For this recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the chicken breasts on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through. Once done, let it cool for a few minutes and then shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  2. In a large oven-safe skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced bell peppers, zucchini, onion, and garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, approximately 5-7 minutes.

  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.

  4. Pour the egg mixture over the sautéed vegetables in the skillet. Stir everything gently to ensure the vegetables are evenly distributed throughout the skillet.

  5. Add the shredded chicken on top of the egg and vegetable mixture, lightly pressing it into the skillet.

  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the frittata is set in the center and golden brown around the edges.

  7. Once cooked, remove the skillet from the oven and let the frittata cool for a few minutes. Slice it into wedges and serve warm.

Serving Suggestions

  • This Italian-style chicken and vegetable frittata is incredibly versatile. It can be enjoyed on its own, as a main dish, or served with a side salad for a complete meal.
  • For added flavor, top the frittata wedges with a dollop of creamy pesto sauce or a sprinkle of freshly chopped herbs like basil or parsley.
  • This frittata also makes a great addition to brunch spreads or potluck gatherings. Cut it into small squares for bite-sized appetizers.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You have just learned how to make the Best Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Frittata. With its vibrant colors and delightful flavors, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a gathering, this frittata is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone satisfied. So, grab your skillet and get ready to indulge in this Italian-inspired culinary delight!
