Savory Slow Cooker Paleo Beef Stew Recipe


If you’re in the mood for a hearty and comforting meal that’s also paleo-friendly, look no further than this savory slow cooker paleo beef stew recipe. Packed with tender chunks of beef, an array of colorful vegetables, and a rich, flavorful broth, this dish is sure to become a staple in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs grass-fed beef stew meat, cut into chunks
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons arrowroot powder (optional, for thickening)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Begin by prepping all the ingredients – chop the onion, garlic, carrots, celery, sweet potatoes, and mushrooms. This will make the assembly process smoother.

  2. Brown the Beef: Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add a splash of oil and brown the beef stew meat on all sides. This step adds depth of flavor to the stew.

  3. Assemble the Stew: Transfer the browned beef to the slow cooker. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, sliced carrots, chopped celery, cubed sweet potatoes, and sliced mushrooms to the slow cooker. Sprinkle in the dried thyme and rosemary, then season with salt and pepper.

  4. Add the Broth: Pour the beef broth over the ingredients in the slow cooker. The broth should fully cover the ingredients. If needed, add a little water to ensure everything is submerged.

  5. Cook Slow and Low: Set the slow cooker to low heat and cook the stew for 7-8 hours, or until the beef is fork-tender and the vegetables are soft and flavorful.

  6. Thicken (Optional): If you prefer a thicker stew, mix the arrowroot powder with a little water to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the stew during the last 30 minutes of cooking to thicken the broth.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the stew is ready, give it a gentle stir and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Ladle the hot stew into bowls and savor the robust flavors and comforting aroma.

Why This Recipe Works

This slow cooker paleo beef stew recipe offers a multitude of benefits. First and foremost, it’s incredibly convenient. With minimal hands-on time, the slow cooker does the heavy lifting, allowing you to come home to a warm, nourishing meal. Additionally, the use of grass-fed beef ensures a higher nutrient content and a richer flavor profile. The combination of vibrant vegetables not only adds color to the dish but also contributes an array of vitamins and minerals. Moreover, the paleo-friendly nature of this recipe means it’s free from gluten, grains, and dairy, making it suitable for individuals following a paleo lifestyle.

Final Thoughts

With its tender beef, robust vegetables, and aromatic herbs, this savory slow cooker paleo beef stew is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re meal prepping for the week or hosting a cozy dinner gathering, this recipe is a stellar choice. The comforting aroma that fills your home as it simmers in the slow cooker is just a prelude to the deliciousness that awaits. Give this recipe a try and experience the warmth and satisfaction of a nourishing paleo meal.
