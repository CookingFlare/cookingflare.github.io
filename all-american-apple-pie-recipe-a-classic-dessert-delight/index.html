All-American Apple Pie Recipe: A Classic Dessert Delight


There’s nothing quite as American as a homemade apple pie. With its flaky crust, sweet and tangy filling, and comforting aroma, it’s a dessert that has stood the test of time. Whether it’s a family gathering, a holiday celebration, or simply a cozy evening at home, an All-American Apple Pie is sure to bring joy to any occasion. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of creating this classic dessert from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water
  • 8 cups tart apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing with a fork until the dough comes together. Divide the dough in half and shape each half into a disk. Wrap them in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. While the dough is chilling, prepare the apple filling. In a separate bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, all-purpose flour, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.

  5. Roll out one of the dough disks on a floured surface into a circle large enough to fit your pie dish. Gently transfer the rolled-out dough to the dish and press it into the bottom and sides.

  6. Pour the apple filling into the pie crust, spreading it evenly. Dot the top with the melted butter.

  7. Roll out the second dough disk and carefully place it on top of the pie. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie. Cut a few slits on the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.

  8. Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

  9. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool for at least 2 hours before serving. This will allow the filling to set and make for easier slicing.

Serve and Enjoy!

Now that your All-American Apple Pie is ready, it’s time to savor the delicious flavors and share it with your loved ones. Serve it warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence. The combination of the buttery crust and the sweet, spiced apple filling is simply irresistible.

Remember, this recipe is just a starting point. Feel free to experiment with different varieties of apples or add a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar on top for an extra crunch. The joy of baking is in the creativity and personal touch you bring to each dish.

So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and embark on a delightful journey of making your own All-American Apple Pie. It’s a timeless dessert that brings people together and creates lasting memories. Happy baking!
