Slow Cooker Soup Recipes for Effortless Cooking


Are you looking for a way to prepare delicious and comforting soups without spending hours in the kitchen? Look no further than your trusty slow cooker! With its convenience and versatility, the slow cooker is the perfect tool for creating hearty and flavorful soups with minimal effort. In this blog post, we will share some mouthwatering slow cooker soup recipes that will make your taste buds dance and your cooking experience a breeze.

Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Preparation time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 6-8 hours

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, minced garlic, and chopped basil leaves.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 4 hours.
  4. After the cooking time, use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and let it heat through for another 10 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Enjoy!

Chicken Noodle Soup

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 6-8 hours

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 stalks of celery, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of egg noodles

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, chicken broth, dried thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper in the slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 4 hours.
  3. Remove the chicken breasts from the slow cooker and shred them using two forks.
  4. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.
  5. Add the egg noodles and cook on high for an additional 30 minutes or until the noodles are tender.
  6. Remove the bay leaf and serve hot. Enjoy!

Butternut Squash Soup

Preparation time: 20 minutes Cooking time: 4-6 hours

Ingredients:

  • 1 butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 apple, peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the butternut squash, apple, onion, vegetable broth, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or on high for 3 hours, until the squash is tender.
  3. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream, if desired, and let it heat through for another 10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot and sprinkle with a pinch of ground cinnamon for garnish. Enjoy!

Conclusion

With these slow cooker soup recipes, you can enjoy delicious and satisfying meals without spending hours in the kitchen. The convenience of the slow cooker allows you to set it and forget it, while still achieving mouthwatering results. Whether you’re craving a creamy tomato basil soup, a classic chicken noodle soup, or a comforting butternut squash soup, these recipes are sure to impress. Give them a try and indulge in the effortless joy of slow cooker cooking!
