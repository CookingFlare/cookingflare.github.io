How to Make Delicious Almond Joy Cookies


If you’re a fan of Almond Joy candy bars, you’re going to love these Almond Joy cookies. Imagine the creamy, sweet chocolate flavor of an Almond Joy, combined with the texture and fun of a chewy cookie. It’s a match made in dessert heaven. These cookies are a treat that everyone will enjoy, whether you’re serving them at a party, bringing them to a bake sale, or simply making them as a special snack at home.

In this blog post, we’ll walk you through everything you need to know to make these delicious cookies from scratch, including the ingredients, instructions, and some helpful tips for ensuring your cookies turn out perfectly every time.

The Perfect Almond Joy Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

Before you begin, make sure you have the following ingredients on hand:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened to room temperature)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • Whole almonds (for topping)

Equipment

To get started, gather the following tools:

  • Mixing bowls (large and medium)
  • Electric mixer or whisk
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Cookie sheets or baking trays
  • Parchment paper or silicone baking mats
  • Cooling racks

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Your Baking Sheets

Start by preparing your baking sheets. Line them with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat. This will help ensure that your cookies don’t stick to the pan and make clean-up much easier.

Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients

In a medium-sized bowl, combine your all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Use a whisk or spoon to stir them together and ensure they are evenly distributed. This will help to ensure that your cookies have the perfect texture when baked.

Step 3: Cream Butter and Sugars

In a large bowl, use an electric mixer (or a whisk if you prefer) to cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar. Beat them together for about 2 minutes or until the mixture is light and fluffy. This step is important because the creaming process helps to incorporate air into the dough, giving your cookies a soft and chewy texture.

Step 4: Add the Egg and Vanilla Extract

Next, add the egg and vanilla extract to the butter and sugar mixture. Beat until well combined. Make sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl to ensure that everything is evenly incorporated.

Step 5: Gradually Add Dry Ingredients

Now, slowly add the dry flour mixture into the wet ingredients. Mix on low speed until the dough begins to come together. It’s important to add the dry ingredients gradually to avoid flour spilling out and to prevent overmixing the dough, which can lead to dense cookies.

Step 6: Add the Coconut and Almonds

Once the flour mixture is fully incorporated, fold in the shredded coconut and chopped almonds. These ingredients are the signature additions that give this cookie its Almond Joy flavor. You can also add a bit of extra coconut if you prefer a more coconut-heavy flavor. Mix until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

Step 7: Fold in the Chocolate Chips

Now, it’s time to add the chocolate chips. We recommend using a mix of semisweet chocolate chips and milk chocolate chips for the perfect balance of sweetness. Gently fold the chocolate chips into the dough using a spatula or spoon. You want to make sure that the chips are evenly spread throughout the dough, but be careful not to overmix.

Step 8: Scoop the Dough

Using a tablespoon or cookie scoop, scoop out the dough and place it onto your prepared baking sheets. Make sure to space the cookie dough balls at least 2 inches apart, as they will spread slightly while baking. Each cookie should be about 1 to 1 1/2 inches in diameter.

Step 9: Add the Topping

To make your cookies look even more like Almond Joy candy bars, top each cookie with a whole almond. Gently press the almond into the center of the cookie dough ball before baking.

Step 10: Bake the Cookies

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Once it’s heated, place your baking sheets in the oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers are just set. Keep an eye on them as oven temperatures can vary, and you don’t want to over-bake these cookies. They should still be soft and chewy in the center.

Step 11: Cool the Cookies

Once the cookies are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheets for about 5 minutes. This will help them set before transferring them to a cooling rack. After they’ve cooled slightly, move the cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips for Perfect Almond Joy Cookies

  1. Use Softened Butter: It’s essential to use softened butter for the best texture. Cold butter doesn’t mix well with the sugars, resulting in dense cookies.

  2. Don’t Overmix the Dough: Overmixing the dough can cause the cookies to become tough. Mix until the ingredients are just combined to keep the texture light and chewy.

  3. Chill the Dough (Optional): If you have time, you can chill the dough in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before baking. This can help the cookies keep their shape and prevent them from spreading too much while baking.

  4. Customize the Nuts: If you prefer, you can swap out the chopped almonds for other types of nuts like pecans or walnuts. If you’re not a fan of nuts, you can leave them out entirely.

  5. Check for Doneness: Cookies continue to cook on the baking sheet after they are removed from the oven, so be sure not to overbake them. They should still be soft in the middle when you take them out.

  6. Store the Cookies Properly: Once your cookies have cooled, store them in an airtight container at room temperature. They should stay fresh for about 4-5 days. If you want to keep them longer, you can freeze them for up to 3 months.

How to Make These Cookies Gluten-Free

If you or someone you know needs a gluten-free version of this recipe, you can make a simple substitution. Instead of all-purpose flour, use a 1:1 gluten-free flour blend. Make sure the blend you choose contains xanthan gum or guar gum, which helps give the cookies structure.

Vegan Version of Almond Joy Cookies

For a vegan version of these Almond Joy cookies, you’ll need to make a few modifications:

  • Replace the egg with a flaxseed egg (mix 1 tablespoon of ground flaxseed with 3 tablespoons of water and let it sit for 5 minutes).
  • Use non-dairy butter (such as vegan margarine) or coconut oil.
  • Make sure the chocolate chips are dairy-free.

The result will be just as delicious and satisfying as the original recipe!

If you’re looking for a fun and unique twist on classic chocolate chip cookies, this recipe will be your new favorite. The perfect balance of sweet and salty makes them irresistible, and the best part is they are simple to make with ingredients you likely already have in your kitchen.

Final Thoughts
