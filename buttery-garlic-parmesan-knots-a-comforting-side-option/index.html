Buttery Garlic Parmesan Knots: A Comforting Side Option


If you’re looking for a versatile and delicious side dish to accompany your meals, look no further than Buttery Garlic Parmesan Knots. These delectable knots are not only easy to make but also pack a flavorful punch that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Olive oil, for brushing

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well.

  2. Gradually add the warm water to the dry ingredients, stirring until a soft dough forms. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  3. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  4. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  5. Punch down the dough and transfer it to a lightly floured surface. Divide the dough into 16 equal pieces and roll each piece into a rope approximately 8 inches long.

  6. Tie each rope into a knot, tucking the ends underneath. Place the knots on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving some space between them.

  7. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter and minced garlic. Brush the knots with the garlic butter mixture, making sure to coat them evenly.

  8. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, and a pinch of salt over the knots, pressing it gently to adhere.

  9. Bake the knots in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  10. Remove the knots from the oven and brush them with a little more melted butter. Allow them to cool slightly before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Buttery Garlic Parmesan Knots are perfect on their own as a snack or appetizer, but they also make a great accompaniment to a variety of main dishes. Serve them alongside a bowl of pasta, a hearty soup, or even a juicy steak. Their buttery, garlicky flavor pairs well with many different flavors and will elevate any meal.

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize the flavors by adding different herbs or spices to the garlic butter mixture. Try adding dried oregano, crushed red pepper flakes, or even a sprinkle of paprika for an extra kick.

  • If you prefer a softer texture, brush the knots with melted butter immediately after they come out of the oven. This will keep them moist and tender.

  • For a cheesy twist, you can also add a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella or cheddar cheese on top of the Parmesan before baking.

  • Leftover knots can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. Reheat them in the oven or toaster oven for a few minutes to restore their freshness.

So there you have it – a simple and mouthwatering recipe for Buttery Garlic Parmesan Knots. Whether you’re serving them as a side dish or enjoying them as a snack, these knots are sure to delight your taste buds. Give them a try and let the comforting aroma of garlic and Parmesan fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
