Homemade Doughnut Recipes That Rival the Bakery


Who can resist the temptation of a freshly made doughnut? The aroma of doughnuts frying in hot oil, the slight crisp as you bite into the golden exterior, and the soft, pillowy interior that melts in your mouth – pure bliss! While store-bought doughnuts are convenient, there’s something truly special about making them from scratch at home. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering homemade doughnut recipes that will rival those from the bakery.

Classic Glazed Doughnuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) instant yeast
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • Oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add eggs, sugar, flour, salt, and butter. Mix until a sticky dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth. Let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll out the dough and cut into doughnut shapes. Let them rise for 30 minutes.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and fry the doughnuts until golden brown.
  6. Dip the cooled doughnuts in a glaze made of powdered sugar and milk.

Chocolate-Filled Doughnuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Chocolate hazelnut spread for filling
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Warm milk and mix in yeast and sugar. Let it proof for 10 minutes.
  2. Add eggs, butter, flour, and salt. Knead until smooth and elastic.
  3. Let the dough rise until doubled in size, then punch it down.
  4. Roll out the dough, cut into rounds, and fill each with chocolate spread.
  5. Seal the dough tightly around the filling and let the doughnuts rise again.
  6. Fry until puffed and golden, then dust with powdered sugar.

Cinnamon Sugar Doughnuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) instant yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • Butter, melted, for brushing

Instructions:

  1. Combine warm milk, yeast, and sugar. Let it foam for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix in eggs, butter, flour, and salt to form a dough. Knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise, then roll and cut into doughnut shapes.
  4. Fry until golden brown, then immediately toss in a mixture of sugar and cinnamon.
  5. Brush with melted butter for extra richness.

Homemade doughnuts are a labor of love, but the end result is absolutely worth it. The joy of biting into a warm, freshly made doughnut that you crafted with your own hands is incomparable. Whether you opt for the classic glazed, indulgent chocolate-filled, or comforting cinnamon sugar, each bite is a celebration of flavor and texture. So, roll up your sleeves, heat up that oil, and embark on a doughnut-making adventure in your own kitchen!

Remember, the secret ingredient is always a sprinkle of love. Happy baking!
