Delicious One-Pot Rice Pilaf Recipe

3 min · 637 words

Looking for a quick, easy, and flavorful meal that requires minimal clean-up? Look no further than this delicious one-pot rice pilaf recipe. This dish combines fragrant basmati rice, aromatic spices, and tender vegetables, all cooked together in one pot to create a mouthwatering meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a go-to favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3 1/2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Saute the Aromatics: In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until soft and translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the Spices: Stir in the ground cumin, coriander, and turmeric, and cook for a minute to toast the spices and release their flavors.

  3. Add the Rice: Add the basmati rice to the pot and stir to coat the grains with the aromatic mixture.

  4. Add the Liquid: Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth, season with salt and pepper, and bring the mixture to a boil.

  5. Simmer: Once the liquid is boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and let the rice simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the liquid has been absorbed and the rice is tender.

  6. Add the Vegetables: After the rice has cooked for about 10 minutes, gently fold in the diced carrot, red bell pepper, and frozen peas. Cover the pot and allow the vegetables to steam along with the rice until they are tender.

  7. Fluff and Serve: Once the rice and vegetables are cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for a few minutes. Then, fluff the rice with a fork to separate the grains and distribute the vegetables evenly throughout.

  8. Garnish and Enjoy: Transfer the rice pilaf to a serving dish, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot as a flavorful and satisfying main course or as a side dish to complement your favorite protein.

Tips for Success

  • Use Quality Rice: Opt for high-quality basmati rice for the best flavor and texture in your pilaf.
  • Customize: Feel free to customize the vegetables in this recipe based on your preferences or what’s in season.
  • Add Protein: For a heartier meal, consider adding cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the pilaf.
  • Make It Vegan: Use vegetable broth and skip the optional protein for a delicious vegan option.

This one-pot rice pilaf is a versatile and comforting dish that’s perfect for busy weeknights, special gatherings, or anytime you’re craving a flavorful meal with minimal clean-up. The fragrant spices, tender vegetables, and fluffy rice come together in perfect harmony, making this recipe a true crowd-pleaser. Give it a try and savor the delightful flavors of this easy and delicious one-pot rice pilaf!
