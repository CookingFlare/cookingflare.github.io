Savory Steak Fajita Wrap: A Tex-Mex Favorite


Fajitas, a staple of Tex-Mex cuisine, have been a crowd-pleaser for decades. The sizzling combination of savory steak, crispy vegetables, and warm flour tortillas is a match made in heaven. In this recipe, we’ll take the classic fajita formula and wrap it up in a neat, portable package – the savory steak fajita wrap.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced (optional)
  • Sour cream, salsa, and shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Steak: In a large ziplock bag, combine lime juice, garlic, oregano, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Add the flank steak, turning to coat evenly. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables: Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until caramelized and golden brown, about 8-10 minutes. Add the sliced bell peppers and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender, about 5 minutes. Remove the vegetables from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Cook the Steak: Remove the steak from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil to the skillet and cook the steak, stirring occasionally, until cooked to desired level of doneness, about 5-7 minutes per side. Transfer the steak to a cutting board and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing into thin strips.
  4. Assemble the Wrap: Warm the flour tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds. Spoon some of the cooked vegetables onto the center of each tortilla, followed by a few slices of the steak. Top with diced tomato, chopped cilantro, and sliced jalapeño. If using, add sliced avocado, sour cream, salsa, and shredded cheese.
  5. Fold and Serve: Fold the bottom edge of the tortilla up over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll the wrap up tightly. Repeat with the remaining ingredients. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, add a sprinkle of fajita seasoning or chili powder to the vegetables and steak.
  • Swap the flank steak for skirt steak or ribeye for a different texture and flavor.
  • Add some heat to the wrap by using hot sauce or diced green chilies.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as cheddar or pepper jack, for added flavor.

Conclusion

The savory steak fajita wrap is a delicious and satisfying twist on the classic fajita recipe. With its tender steak, crispy vegetables, and warm flour tortilla, it’s a perfect meal for any occasion. Whether you’re a Tex-Mex aficionado or just looking for a new recipe to try, this wrap is sure to please. So go ahead, get creative, and wrap up your taste buds with this savory steak fajita wrap!
