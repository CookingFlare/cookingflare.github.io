Simple Pesto Shrimp and Asparagus Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick, delicious, and healthy meal, look no further than this simple pesto shrimp and asparagus recipe. This dish is perfect for a busy weeknight or a relaxed weekend dinner. The combination of succulent shrimp, vibrant asparagus, and flavorful pesto creates a symphony of flavors that will delight your taste buds. Let’s dive into the details of this easy and satisfying recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bunch of fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of your favorite pesto sauce
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • Freshly grated parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Cooked pasta or crusty bread for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp and Asparagus: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning them with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  2. Cook the Asparagus: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and the minced garlic. Sauté the garlic for about 30 seconds until it becomes fragrant. Then, add the asparagus to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the asparagus is tender-crisp.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet with the asparagus. Pour the pesto sauce over the shrimp and asparagus, and gently toss everything together until the shrimp and asparagus are evenly coated with the pesto. Allow the mixture to heat through for 1-2 minutes, stirring gently.

  4. Finish and Serve: Drizzle the lemon juice over the pesto shrimp and asparagus, and give it a final toss. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Transfer the dish to a serving platter or individual plates. Sprinkle freshly grated parmesan cheese on top for an extra burst of flavor. Serve the pesto shrimp and asparagus with your choice of cooked pasta or crusty bread, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a lighter version, you can use zucchini noodles or cauliflower rice instead of pasta.
  • Feel free to add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can mix the pesto with a bit of heavy cream or coconut milk before adding it to the shrimp and asparagus.

In Conclusion

This simple pesto shrimp and asparagus recipe is a testament to the fact that a few quality ingredients and minimal effort can result in a truly satisfying meal. Whether you’re a seafood lover, a fan of vibrant vegetables, or someone who appreciates the convenience of a one-pan dish, this recipe has something for everyone. The marriage of succulent shrimp, crisp asparagus, and the bold flavors of pesto make this dish a standout. So, the next time you’re pondering what to make for dinner, consider treating yourself to this delightful and effortless creation. Enjoy!
