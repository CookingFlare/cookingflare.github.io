Pumpkin Spice Mug Cake: A Quick and Easy Dessert Recipe


Craving a delicious dessert but don’t have the time or energy to whip up something elaborate? Look no further than this delightful Pumpkin Spice Mug Cake recipe! In just a matter of minutes, you can treat yourself to a warm, moist, and perfectly spiced cake that’s ideal for satisfying those sweet cravings. This single-serving dessert is not only easy to make but also a great way to celebrate the cozy flavors of fall anytime of the year.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons pumpkin puree
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Mug: Start by selecting a microwave-safe mug to ensure your cake cooks evenly. Lightly grease the inside of the mug with a bit of oil or cooking spray.

  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a small bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, brown sugar, baking powder, and pumpkin pie spice. Stir well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Add the Wet Ingredients: Once the dry ingredients are mixed, pour in the milk, pumpkin puree, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Stir the mixture until it forms a smooth batter. Make sure to scrape the bottom and sides of the bowl to fully incorporate all the ingredients.

  4. Pour the Batter: Transfer the batter into the prepared mug, ensuring that it fills no more than halfway to allow room for the cake to rise as it cooks.

  5. Microwave the Cake: Place the mug in the microwave and cook on high for about 1 minute and 30 seconds to 2 minutes. Keep an eye on the cake as cooking times may vary depending on your microwave’s wattage. The cake is ready when the top is set and springs back lightly to the touch.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Carefully remove the mug from the microwave (it will be hot!), and let the cake cool for a minute or two before diving in. You can enjoy the cake as is or add a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of powdered sugar for an extra touch of indulgence.

Tips and Variations:

  • Spice it Up: Feel free to adjust the amount of pumpkin pie spice to suit your taste preferences. You can even add a pinch of cinnamon, nutmeg, or cloves for a custom spice blend.
  • Chocolate Twist: For a decadent variation, mix in a tablespoon of chocolate chips or chunks into the batter before microwaving. The melty chocolate adds a delightful contrast to the warm spiced cake.
  • Nutty Addition: If you’re a fan of nuts, consider folding in a tablespoon of chopped pecans or walnuts for a delightful crunch in every bite.

Final Thoughts

The Pumpkin Spice Mug Cake is a fantastic way to indulge in the comforting flavors of fall in a convenient single-serving portion. Whether you’re looking for a quick dessert fix after a long day or want to treat yourself to a cozy evening treat, this recipe is sure to hit the spot. Don’t hesitate to experiment with different spices and mix-ins to customize the cake to your liking. So, next time you’re in the mood for a simple yet satisfying dessert, give this Pumpkin Spice Mug Cake a try and savor the delightful flavors in every spoonful!
