Leftover BBQ Bonanza: 10 Smoky Recipes for Extra Grilled Goodness


Summer is in full swing, and that means it’s BBQ season! There’s nothing quite like the smell of sizzling meat on the grill, the sound of laughter and chatter in the backyard, and the taste of smoky goodness on your plate. But what happens when you have leftovers from your BBQ feast? Don’t let them go to waste! In this post, we’ll show you 10 creative and delicious ways to transform your leftover BBQ into mouthwatering dishes that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

1. BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos

Take your leftover pulled pork and turn it into a taco fiesta! Warm up some corn tortillas, fill them with the tender pulled pork, and top with your favorite toppings like shredded cheese, diced onions, and a dollop of tangy BBQ sauce. Serve with a side of coleslaw for a refreshing crunch.

2. BBQ Chicken Pizza

Who says pizza can’t have a BBQ twist? Grab some store-bought pizza dough or make your own if you’re feeling adventurous. Spread a layer of BBQ sauce on the dough, top with shredded BBQ chicken, sliced red onions, and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese. Bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling. Slice, serve, and enjoy!

3. BBQ Brisket Grilled Cheese

Elevate your grilled cheese game with leftover BBQ brisket. Butter two slices of bread, spread some BBQ sauce on the inside, and layer on the tender brisket and your choice of cheese. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown. Cut into triangles and savor the gooey, smoky goodness.

4. BBQ Beef Nachos

Take your leftover BBQ beef and turn it into a crowd-pleasing plate of nachos. Layer tortilla chips on a baking sheet, top with shredded BBQ beef, black beans, diced tomatoes, jalapenos, and a generous sprinkle of shredded cheese. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve with your favorite salsa and guacamole for an irresistible snack.

5. BBQ Stuffed Bell Peppers

Give your bell peppers a smoky makeover with leftover BBQ meat. Slice the tops off the peppers and remove the seeds. Fill each pepper with a mixture of BBQ meat, cooked rice, diced onions, and shredded cheese. Bake in the oven until the peppers are tender and the cheese is melted and golden. Serve as a main dish or a side for a flavorful and colorful meal.

6. BBQ Pork Mac and Cheese

Upgrade your mac and cheese game by adding some BBQ pork to the mix. Cook your favorite macaroni according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside. In a separate pan, heat up some leftover BBQ pork and mix it into a creamy cheese sauce. Combine the sauce with the cooked macaroni, transfer to a baking dish, and top with breadcrumbs. Bake until golden and bubbly. Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with a smoky twist.

7. BBQ Chicken Salad

Turn your leftover BBQ chicken into a refreshing and satisfying salad. Start with a bed of mixed greens, then add sliced BBQ chicken, cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, and crumbled feta cheese. Drizzle with a tangy vinaigrette or your favorite BBQ sauce. Toss everything together and enjoy a light and flavorful meal.

8. BBQ Beef Quesadillas

Give your quesadillas a BBQ twist by using leftover BBQ beef. Fill a tortilla with a generous amount of shredded beef, sliced bell peppers, onions, and a sprinkle of shredded cheese. Fold the tortilla in half and grill it on a skillet until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy. Cut into wedges and serve with a side of sour cream or guacamole.

9. BBQ Meatball Sliders

Transform your leftover BBQ meatballs into irresistible sliders. Warm up some slider buns, then fill them with BBQ meatballs, a slice of cheese, and a drizzle of BBQ sauce. Toast the sliders in the oven until the cheese is melted and the buns are slightly crispy. Serve as a delicious appetizer or a satisfying main dish.

10. BBQ Pork Fried Rice

Take your leftover BBQ pork and turn it into a flavorful fried rice. Heat up some oil in a wok or a large skillet, then add cooked rice, diced vegetables, and chopped BBQ pork. Stir-fry everything together, adding soy sauce and any other desired seasonings. Finish off with a sprinkle of green onions for a burst of freshness. Serve as a delicious and easy weeknight meal.

Don’t let your leftover BBQ go to waste. With these 10 smoky recipes, you can transform your extra grilled goodness into a whole new culinary experience. From tacos and pizzas to salads and sliders, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So fire up that grill, invite your friends and family over, and get ready for a BBQ bonanza like no other!
