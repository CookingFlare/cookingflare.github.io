Easy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Skillet Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that’s quick and easy to prepare? Look no further than this Easy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Skillet recipe! Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, this dish is perfect for anyone following a paleo lifestyle or simply looking for a satisfying, healthy meal. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering dish in just a few simple steps.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound grass-fed ground beef
  • 1 small head of cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Avocado oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, add a drizzle of avocado oil. Once the oil is heated, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  2. Add the ground beef to the skillet, breaking it apart with a spatula. Cook the beef until it’s browned and no longer pink, stirring occasionally to ensure even cooking.

  3. Once the beef is cooked through, add the thinly sliced cabbage to the skillet. Stir the cabbage into the beef mixture, allowing it to wilt and cook down slightly.

  4. Season the beef and cabbage mixture with paprika, dried oregano, ground cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir well to evenly distribute the seasonings and ensure that the flavors are incorporated throughout.

  5. Continue cooking the beef and cabbage mixture for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cabbage is tender but still retains a bit of crunch.

  6. Once the cabbage is cooked to your desired texture, remove the skillet from the heat. Taste the dish and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  7. Garnish the Easy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Skillet with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  8. Serve the dish hot, portioning it into individual bowls or plates. Enjoy the delicious combination of savory beef and tender cabbage, all seasoned to perfection with aromatic spices!

Benefits of Paleo Eating

This Easy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Skillet recipe not only tantalizes the taste buds but also aligns with the principles of paleo eating. By focusing on whole, unprocessed foods, the paleo diet aims to mimic the dietary patterns of our ancestors and promote overall health and well-being. Incorporating nutrient-dense ingredients like grass-fed beef, cabbage, and aromatic spices, this recipe offers a wide array of benefits, including:

  • High protein content from the grass-fed beef, which supports muscle health and helps keep you feeling full and satisfied.
  • Abundant fiber from the cabbage, aiding in digestion and contributing to a healthy gut microbiome.
  • Essential vitamins and minerals from the combination of ingredients, supporting overall immune function and vitality.
  • A flavorful and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed by the whole family, making it easier to stick to a wholesome, paleo lifestyle.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Easy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Skillet recipe is a fantastic addition to your culinary repertoire. Not only is it simple to prepare and bursting with flavor, but it also aligns with the principles of paleo eating, promoting health and vitality from the inside out. Whether you’re new to paleo cooking or a seasoned pro, this recipe is sure to become a household favorite. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to create a wholesome and delicious meal that’s perfect for any occasion!
