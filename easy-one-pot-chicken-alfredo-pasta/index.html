Easy One Pot Chicken Alfredo Pasta


Are you looking for a quick and delicious weeknight meal that requires minimal effort and clean-up? Look no further than this Easy One Pot Chicken Alfredo Pasta recipe! With just a few simple ingredients and one pot, you can have a comforting and creamy pasta dish on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the minced garlic, dried Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. Bring the broth to a boil.

  4. Break the fettuccine pasta in half and add it to the pot. Stir well to ensure the pasta is submerged in the broth. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cover the pot. Cook for about 10 minutes, or until the pasta is al dente, stirring occasionally.

  5. Once the pasta is cooked, stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has thickened and the cheese has melted.

  6. Return the cooked chicken to the pot and stir to combine. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, until the chicken is heated through.

  7. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the sauce to thicken further.

  8. Serve the One Pot Chicken Alfredo Pasta in bowls, garnished with freshly chopped parsley. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add some sautéed mushrooms, chopped sun-dried tomatoes, or cooked bacon to the pasta.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can use half-and-half or milk instead of heavy cream. Just keep in mind that the sauce will be less rich and creamy.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of pasta, such as penne or bowtie, if you don’t have fettuccine on hand.
  • If you’re a fan of spice, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to give the dish a kick.

Conclusion

This Easy One Pot Chicken Alfredo Pasta recipe is a true lifesaver for busy days when you crave a comforting and satisfying meal. With its creamy sauce, tender chicken, and perfectly cooked pasta, it’s a dish that will please the whole family. Plus, the best part is that it requires only one pot, which means less time spent on cleaning up afterward. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try – you won’t be disappointed!
