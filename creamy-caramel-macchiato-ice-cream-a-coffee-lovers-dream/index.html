Creamy Caramel Macchiato Ice Cream: A Coffee Lover's Dream

3 min · 532 words

If you’re a coffee lover and an ice cream aficionado, then this recipe is perfect for you. Creamy Caramel Macchiato Ice Cream combines the rich and bold flavors of coffee with the smooth and decadent texture of ice cream. It’s a match made in heaven that will surely satisfy your cravings for both caffeine and frozen treats.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup caramel sauce
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large egg yolks

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, caramel sauce, instant coffee granules, vanilla extract, and salt. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir until the mixture is well combined and heated through. Avoid boiling the mixture.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until they are pale and slightly thickened. Slowly pour about 1 cup of the hot cream mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly to prevent the eggs from curdling.

  3. Pour the egg yolk mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining cream mixture. Place the saucepan back over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl. This step ensures a smooth and creamy texture for your ice cream. Discard any solids left behind in the sieve.

  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, making sure the wrap touches the surface of the mixture to prevent a skin from forming. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.

  6. Once the mixture is thoroughly chilled, transfer it to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes, or until the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  7. Transfer the churned ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for an additional 4 hours or until firm. This step allows the ice cream to set and develop its flavors.

  8. Serve the Creamy Caramel Macchiato Ice Cream in bowls or cones, and if desired, drizzle with extra caramel sauce for an added touch of indulgence. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of coffee flavor, you can add a shot of espresso to the mixture before chilling it in the refrigerator.
  • If you prefer a stronger caramel taste, you can increase the amount of caramel sauce in the recipe.
  • Feel free to customize your ice cream by adding mix-ins such as crushed cookies, chocolate chips, or chopped nuts during the last few minutes of churning.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can still make this recipe. After straining the mixture in step 4, pour it into a lidded container and freeze for about 1 hour. Remove from the freezer and vigorously whisk the mixture to break up any ice crystals. Repeat this process every 30 minutes for about 3-4 hours, or until the ice cream reaches the desired consistency.

Now, sit back, relax, and savor the delightful combination of creamy caramel and robust coffee in every spoonful of this luscious Caramel Macchiato Ice Cream. It’s a coffee lover’s dream come true!
