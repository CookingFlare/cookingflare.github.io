Indulgent Nutella French Toast Recipes for a Decadent Brunch

Who doesn’t love a delicious and indulgent brunch? And when it comes to brunch, French toast is an absolute classic. But why settle for ordinary French toast when you can take it to a whole new level of decadence with the addition of Nutella? In this post, we’ll explore three mouthwatering Nutella French toast recipes that will make your brunch a truly indulgent affair. Classic Nutella French Toast Ingredients: 4 slices of thick bread (brioche or challah work best) 2 eggs 1/2 cup of milk 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract Nutella Butter, for frying Powdered sugar, for dusting Instructions: In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Spread Nutella generously on two slices of bread, then sandwich them with the remaining two slices. Heat a knob of butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Dip the Nutella sandwiches into the egg mixture, ensuring they are fully coated on both sides. Place the soaked sandwiches in the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side. Repeat with the remaining sandwiches. Serve the Nutella French toast warm, dusted with powdered sugar. Nutella-Stuffed French Toast Roll-Ups Ingredients: 8 slices of bread Nutella 2 eggs 1/2 cup of milk 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract Butter, for frying Maple syrup, for serving Instructions: Trim the crusts off the bread slices and flatten them with a rolling pin. Spread Nutella on each slice of bread, leaving a small border around the edges. Roll up each slice tightly and set aside. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Heat a knob of butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Dip each Nutella roll-up into the egg mixture, ensuring they are fully coated. Place the roll-ups in the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side. Serve the Nutella French toast roll-ups warm, drizzled with maple syrup. Nutella-Stuffed French Toast Casserole Ingredients: 1 loaf of French bread, cut into cubes Nutella 6 eggs 2 cups of milk 1/4 cup of granulated sugar 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract Butter, for greasing Maple syrup, for serving Instructions: Grease a baking dish with butter and set aside. Spread Nutella on half of the bread cubes and place them in the greased baking dish. Top with the remaining bread cubes. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. Pour the egg mixture over the bread cubes, ensuring they are evenly coated. Cover the baking dish with foil and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the Nutella French toast casserole, covered, for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until golden brown and set. Serve the Nutella French toast casserole warm, with a drizzle of maple syrup. Whether you choose the classic Nutella French toast, the irresistible Nutella-stuffed roll-ups, or the comforting Nutella French toast casserole, each bite will be a delightfully indulgent experience. Healthy Veggie Breakfast Burrito Recipes for a Nutrient-Packed Start

Breakfast is often hailed as the most important meal of the day, and for good reason. It provides us with the energy and nutrients we need to kickstart our day. If you’re looking for a healthy and delicious way to power up your mornings, then look no further than these nutrient-packed veggie breakfast burrito recipes. Spinach and Mushroom Breakfast Burrito Ingredients: 1 whole wheat tortilla 2 eggs, beaten 1 cup spinach, chopped 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced 1/4 cup red bell pepper, diced 2 tablespoons feta cheese, crumbled Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with cooking spray. Add the mushrooms and red bell pepper to the skillet and sauté for 3-4 minutes until softened. Add the spinach to the skillet and cook for an additional 2 minutes until wilted. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and scramble until fully cooked. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Warm the tortilla in a separate skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make it pliable. Place the scrambled eggs and vegetable mixture onto the tortilla. Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Slice in half diagonally and serve warm. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Breakfast Burrito Ingredients: 1 whole wheat tortilla 1 small sweet potato, diced 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained 1/4 cup red onion, diced 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped 1/2 teaspoon cumin 1/4 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the diced sweet potato with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Spread the sweet potato evenly on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender. In a separate skillet, sauté the black beans and red onion until heated through. Warm the tortilla in a separate skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make it pliable. Place the roasted sweet potato, black bean mixture, and fresh cilantro onto the tortilla. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Slice in half diagonally and serve warm. Tofu and Veggie Breakfast Burrito Ingredients: 1 whole wheat tortilla 1/2 cup firm tofu, crumbled 1/4 cup red bell pepper, diced 1/4 cup zucchini, diced 1/4 cup yellow squash, diced 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast 1/2 teaspoon turmeric 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with cooking spray. Add the crumbled tofu, red bell pepper, zucchini, and yellow squash to the skillet and sauté for 5-6 minutes until vegetables are tender. Sprinkle turmeric, garlic powder, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper over the mixture and stir well to combine. Warm the tortilla in a separate skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make it pliable. Place the tofu and veggie mixture onto the tortilla. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Slice in half diagonally and serve warm. These veggie breakfast burrito recipes are not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients to start your day off right.
