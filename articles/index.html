Slow Cooker Mexican Shredded Chicken: Versatile and Delicious

Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make recipe that can be used in a variety of dishes? Look no further than slow cooker Mexican shredded chicken! This recipe is a game-changer for anyone who loves Mexican cuisine, as it can be used to make tacos, burritos, quesadillas, and more. Ingredients 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 can of diced tomatoes with green chilies 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed 1 can of diced green chilies 1 tablespoon of olive oil 1 teaspoon of cumin 1 teaspoon of chili powder Salt and pepper, to taste 1 cup of chicken broth Optional: jalapenos, sour cream, shredded cheese, and cilantro for topping Instructions Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker. In a separate bowl, mix together the diced tomatoes with green chilies, black beans, diced green chilies, olive oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the chicken breasts in the slow cooker. Pour in the chicken broth and stir to combine. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours. When the chicken is cooked through and shreds easily with a fork, it is done. Shred the chicken with two forks and return it to the slow cooker. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve the chicken hot, garnished with your desired toppings. Uses for Slow Cooker Mexican Shredded Chicken This recipe is incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of dishes.
