Bacon and Potato Flatbread: A Hearty Combination


If you’re looking for a hearty, satisfying meal that’s easy to make and bursting with flavor, look no further than bacon and potato flatbread. This delicious dish combines the smoky, savory goodness of bacon with the comforting taste of potatoes, all nestled on a crispy, golden flatbread. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together, planning a cozy night in, or simply craving a comforting meal, this bacon and potato flatbread is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

For the Flatbread:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Topping:

  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 2 large potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh rosemary for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Flatbread Dough:

    • In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt.
    • Gradually add the warm water and olive oil, mixing until the dough comes together.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes, then place it in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  3. Cook the Bacon and Potatoes:

    • In a skillet over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until it’s crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside, leaving the bacon drippings in the pan.
    • In the same skillet, add the thinly sliced potatoes, onions, and minced garlic. Cook until the potatoes are tender and golden brown, then remove from heat and set aside.

  4. Roll Out the Flatbread:

    • Once the dough has rested, divide it into two equal portions.
    • On a floured surface, roll out each portion of dough into a thin, oval shape.

  5. Assemble the Flatbread:

    • Transfer the rolled-out dough onto the prepared baking sheet.
    • Spread the cooked potatoes and onions evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle the crispy bacon and shredded mozzarella cheese over the potatoes and onions.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Bake to Perfection:

    • Place the assembled flatbreads in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
    • Once out of the oven, garnish with fresh rosemary for a burst of herbal aroma.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Slice the flatbread into portions and serve it hot, allowing the irresistible aroma to tantalize your taste buds.

The Perfect Pairing

This bacon and potato flatbread pairs beautifully with a crisp, refreshing salad, adding a delightful contrast to the hearty flavors of the flatbread. A simple garden salad with a zesty vinaigrette or a classic Caesar salad can complement the richness of the flatbread, creating a well-rounded and satisfying meal.

A Versatile Delight

One of the best things about bacon and potato flatbread is its versatility. You can customize the toppings to suit your preferences, adding extra veggies, different cheeses, or even a sprinkle of your favorite herbs. This dish is a canvas for creativity, allowing you to experiment with flavors and textures to create your signature flatbread masterpiece.

Final Thoughts

With its irresistible combination of smoky bacon, tender potatoes, and gooey cheese, this bacon and potato flatbread is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re serving it at a gathering or enjoying it as a weeknight treat, this dish is bound to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. The next time you’re craving a comforting, delicious meal, give this bacon and potato flatbread a try and savor every delightful bite.
