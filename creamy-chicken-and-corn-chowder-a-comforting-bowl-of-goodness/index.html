Creamy Chicken and Corn Chowder: A Comforting Bowl of Goodness


There’s nothing quite like a warm, creamy bowl of chicken and corn chowder to lift your spirits and satisfy your soul. This comforting soup is a perfect blend of savory chicken, sweet corn, and rich, velvety broth. Whether you’re looking for a hearty meal on a chilly evening or a dish to impress your guests, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Let’s dive into the tantalizing world of creamy chicken and corn chowder and learn how to create this bowl of goodness from scratch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 slices of bacon, diced
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups frozen corn kernels
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives, finely chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Chicken: Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large pot, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat and cook the chicken until golden brown on both sides. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside to cool. Once cooled, shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Render the Bacon: In the same pot, add the diced bacon and cook until it turns crispy and releases its flavorful fat. Remove the bacon from the pot and set it aside, leaving the bacon fat in the pot.

  3. Saute the Aromatics: Add the butter to the pot with the bacon fat and melt it over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, sautéing until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  4. Create the Base: Sprinkle the flour over the onion and garlic, stirring continuously to create a roux. Cook the roux for a few minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste.

  5. Add the Liquids: Gradually pour in the chicken broth and whole milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, allowing it to thicken slightly.

  6. Combine the Ingredients: Add the shredded chicken, crispy bacon, frozen corn kernels, diced potatoes, dried thyme, and paprika to the pot. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Simmer to Perfection: Let the chowder simmer gently for about 20-25 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the flavors have melded together beautifully.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the creamy chicken and corn chowder into bowls, garnishing each serving with a sprinkle of fresh chives for a burst of color and flavor.

Tips for Success:

  • To save time, you can use pre-cooked rotisserie chicken and store-bought bacon bits.
  • For extra richness, substitute some of the whole milk with heavy cream.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices, such as fresh thyme or smoked paprika, to elevate the flavors of your chowder.

Creamy chicken and corn chowder is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Whether you prefer a thicker consistency or a lighter broth, this recipe can easily adapt to your desires. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the warmth of the kitchen, and embark on a culinary journey that culminates in a steaming bowl of creamy chicken and corn chowder. Let the aroma fill your home and the first spoonful warm your heart.
