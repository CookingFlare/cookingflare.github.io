Succulent Grilled Halibut with Herb Butter


There’s nothing quite like the taste of succulent grilled halibut with herb butter. This dish is a perfect choice for a light summer meal or a special dinner. The delicate flavor of the halibut pairs beautifully with the richness of the herb butter, creating a mouthwatering combination that will leave your taste buds singing. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a cooking novice, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 4 halibut fillets
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, finely chopped
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Herb Butter: In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, dill, chives, and lemon zest. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined. Spoon the herb butter onto a sheet of plastic wrap and roll it into a log. Place the log in the refrigerator to chill while you prepare the halibut.

  2. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Brush the halibut fillets with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.

  3. Grill the Halibut: Place the halibut fillets on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork. Cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the fillets, so keep a close eye on them to prevent overcooking.

  4. Serve with Herb Butter: Once the halibut is cooked, remove it from the grill and transfer it to a serving platter. Remove the herb butter from the refrigerator and slice it into rounds. Place a slice of herb butter on top of each halibut fillet and allow it to melt slightly before serving.

  5. Garnish and Enjoy: Garnish the grilled halibut with a sprinkle of fresh herbs and a wedge of lemon. Serve immediately and savor the irresistible flavors of this succulent dish.

Tips for Success

  • Fresh is Best: When it comes to the herbs for the butter, opt for fresh herbs for the best flavor. Dried herbs can be used in a pinch, but fresh herbs will take this dish to the next level.

  • Don’t Overcook: Halibut can become dry and tough if overcooked, so be mindful of the cooking time. It’s better to undercook slightly and allow the residual heat to finish the cooking process.

  • Customize the Butter: Feel free to customize the herb butter to your liking. Add a pinch of cayenne for a spicy kick, or a squeeze of fresh lemon juice for a tangy twist.

This succulent grilled halibut with herb butter is a delightful dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. The light, flaky texture of the halibut combined with the rich, aromatic herb butter creates a harmony of flavors that will leave everyone wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this recipe is a winner. Enjoy!
