Get Saucy: 5 Lip-Smacking Marinades for BBQ Wings


When it comes to hosting the ultimate barbecue, nothing quite hits the spot like a plate of perfectly grilled, saucy BBQ wings. Whether you’re planning a summer cookout or just craving some finger-licking goodness, having a repertoire of tantalizing marinades at your disposal can take your BBQ wings to a whole new level. So, get ready to fire up the grill and get saucy with these 5 lip-smacking marinades that will have your guests coming back for seconds and thirds!

Classic BBQ

Let’s start with a timeless favorite: the classic BBQ marinade. This tried-and-true marinade is a crowd-pleaser, featuring a blend of ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and a hint of smoky paprika. The sweetness of the brown sugar balances perfectly with the tangy ketchup, while the Worcestershire sauce adds depth and complexity to the flavor profile. For an extra kick, a dash of hot sauce can be added to elevate this classic marinade to new heights.

Honey Mustard

For those who prefer a tangy and sweet flavor profile, the honey mustard marinade is a must-try. This marinade combines the richness of honey with the zing of Dijon mustard, creating a delectable coating for your BBQ wings. Add a splash of apple cider vinegar for a touch of acidity, and you’ve got a marinade that strikes the perfect balance between sweet and savory. The honey mustard marinade is a definite crowd-pleaser and is sure to be a hit at any barbecue gathering.

Teriyaki Glaze

Transport your taste buds to the Far East with a tantalizing teriyaki glaze for your BBQ wings. This marinade features a harmonious blend of soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger, resulting in a sticky, savory-sweet glaze that caramelizes beautifully on the grill. The umami-rich flavors of the soy sauce complement the sweetness of the brown sugar, while the garlic and ginger add a depth of flavor that is simply irresistible. For a touch of heat, a sprinkle of red pepper flakes can be added to give this teriyaki glaze an extra kick.

Lemon Pepper

If you’re in the mood for something bright and zesty, look no further than the lemon pepper marinade. This citrus-infused marinade combines the boldness of black pepper with the refreshing tang of lemon, creating a flavor combination that is both vibrant and tantalizing. The acidity of the lemon helps tenderize the meat while infusing it with a burst of fresh flavor, making it the perfect choice for those hot summer days when you crave something light and refreshing.

Spicy Buffalo

Last but certainly not least, we have the fiery and bold spicy buffalo marinade. This bold concoction combines hot sauce, butter, and a hint of vinegar, resulting in a marinade that packs a punch. The spicy buffalo marinade is not for the faint of heart, but for those who love a bit of heat, it’s an absolute winner. The rich, buttery texture combined with the fiery kick of the hot sauce creates a mouthwatering coating that will have you reaching for a cold drink – and another wing – in no time.

No matter which marinade you choose, one thing is for certain: your BBQ wings are about to reach new heights of flavor. So, fire up the grill, get saucy, and prepare to impress your guests with these 5 lip-smacking marinades that will elevate your BBQ game to a whole new level. Happy grilling!
