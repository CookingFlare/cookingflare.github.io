Delicious and Creamy Mushroom and Brie Soup Recipes


If you’re a fan of rich, velvety soups, then you’re in for a treat with these delicious and creamy mushroom and Brie soup recipes. Perfect for a cozy night in or to impress guests at a dinner party, these recipes are sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. The earthy flavor of mushrooms combined with the luxurious creaminess of Brie cheese makes for a soup that is both comforting and indulgent.

Classic Creamy Mushroom and Brie Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 8 ounces of Brie cheese, rind removed and cubed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onions and sauté until they are soft and translucent.

  2. Add the garlic and sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until the mushrooms are tender and have released their juices.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the mushroom mixture and stir to combine. Cook for a few minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste.

  4. Slowly pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the soup to thicken.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir in the Brie cheese until it melts and the soup becomes creamy and smooth.

  6. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

Vegan Creamy Mushroom and Brie Soup

For those who prefer a plant-based option, this vegan creamy mushroom and Brie soup is just as luscious and satisfying as the classic version.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of mixed mushrooms (such as cremini, shiitake, and oyster), sliced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of canned coconut milk
  • 8 ounces of vegan Brie cheese, rind removed and cubed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and sauté until they are soft and translucent.

  2. Add the garlic and sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until the mushrooms are tender and have released their juices.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the mushroom mixture and stir to combine. Cook for a few minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste.

  4. Slowly pour in the vegetable broth, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the soup to thicken.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the canned coconut milk. Stir in the vegan Brie cheese until it melts and the soup becomes creamy and smooth.

  6. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with chopped fresh chives.

Final Thoughts

These creamy mushroom and Brie soup recipes are a celebration of the wonderful combination of earthy mushrooms and creamy Brie cheese. Whether you choose the classic or vegan version, these soups are guaranteed to warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds. Serve them with crusty bread or a simple green salad for a complete and utterly delightful meal. Enjoy the luxurious flavors and comforting warmth of these decadent soups!
