Classic Tater Tot Casserole: A Nostalgic Comfort Food Recipe


There are some recipes that just never go out of style, and the classic tater tot casserole is definitely one of them. This hearty, comforting dish has been a staple in many households for decades, and for good reason. It’s a simple yet delicious combination of ground beef, creamy soup, cheese, and of course, crispy tater tots. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a dish to bring to a potluck, this tater tot casserole is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (10.75 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package (32 ounces) frozen tater tots

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef and onion over medium heat until the beef is no longer pink. Drain any excess grease.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the cream of mushroom soup, milk, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the ground beef mixture in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  5. Pour the soup mixture over the beef.
  6. Sprinkle 1 cup of the shredded cheddar cheese over the soup mixture.
  7. Arrange the tater tots in a single layer on top of the cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove the casserole from the oven and sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese over the top.
  10. Return the casserole to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the tater tots are golden brown and crispy.

Serving Suggestions

This tater tot casserole is a complete meal in itself, but you can always add a side salad or steamed vegetables for a pop of color and extra nutrients. It’s also a great dish to bring to a potluck or family gathering, as it’s sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Why We Love It

Tater tot casserole is the epitome of comfort food. It’s warm, hearty, and oh-so-satisfying. The combination of crispy tater tots, savory ground beef, and creamy soup is pure nostalgia on a plate. It’s the kind of dish that brings back memories of family dinners and simpler times. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make, which is always a win in our book.

Make It Your Own

While the classic tater tot casserole is a beloved recipe as it is, there are endless ways to customize it to your liking. Add some diced bell peppers or mushrooms to the ground beef mixture for extra flavor and nutrients. You can also swap out the cream of mushroom soup for cream of chicken or cream of celery, depending on your preferences. For a spicy twist, mix in some diced jalapeños or sprinkle some cayenne pepper over the top before baking. The possibilities are endless!

In Conclusion

Classic tater tot casserole is a timeless dish that continues to bring joy to both kids and adults alike. Its simplicity, heartiness, and nostalgic appeal make it a favorite in many households. Whether you’re cooking for your family or bringing a dish to share with friends, this tater tot casserole is sure to be a hit. So, give this recipe a try and savor the comforting flavors of this beloved classic.
