Instant Pot Chicken Marsala: A Flavorful Italian-Inspired Recipe


If you’re craving a dish that brings together tender chicken, rich mushrooms, and a flavorful Marsala wine sauce, then Chicken Marsala is the answer. This classic Italian dish is traditionally made by sautéing chicken breasts and simmering them in a delicious Marsala wine sauce with mushrooms. While the stovetop version of this recipe is a fan favorite, using the Instant Pot makes it even easier to prepare without sacrificing the authentic flavors. This Instant Pot Chicken Marsala recipe is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion meal.

What Is Chicken Marsala?

Chicken Marsala is a traditional Italian-American dish that features tender chicken breasts cooked in a sauce made with Marsala wine, a sweet or dry fortified wine from Sicily. The dish typically includes mushrooms, which absorb the rich flavors of the sauce, making every bite a delightful experience. The combination of earthy mushrooms, savory chicken, and the sweet notes of the Marsala wine creates a perfect harmony that is both hearty and comforting.

Why Make Chicken Marsala in the Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot is a fantastic tool for making Chicken Marsala because it speeds up the cooking process while retaining all the flavors and tenderness. Traditionally, Chicken Marsala requires sautéing the chicken, browning it, and simmering the sauce. This often takes some time, especially when cooking the chicken in batches. With the Instant Pot, everything comes together in one pot, and the pressure cooking process infuses the flavors deeply into the chicken and mushrooms. Not only does this method save you time, but it also results in a dish that’s rich, flavorful, and succulent.

Ingredients for Instant Pot Chicken Marsala

To make Instant Pot Chicken Marsala, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • Chicken Breasts: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts work best for this recipe as they cook quickly and stay tender in the Instant Pot. You can also use thighs if preferred.
  • Marsala Wine: A key component of this dish, Marsala wine gives the sauce its signature flavor. You can use either sweet or dry Marsala wine, depending on your preference.
  • Mushrooms: Sliced cremini or white mushrooms are ideal for this recipe. They soak up the flavors of the sauce and become tender and delicious during cooking.
  • Chicken Broth: Adds depth to the sauce and helps create a smooth, velvety texture.
  • Butter: Adds richness and helps to sauté the chicken, creating a crispy crust before pressure cooking.
  • Olive Oil: Used for sautéing the chicken before pressure cooking.
  • Garlic: Freshly minced garlic adds an aromatic base to the sauce.
  • Shallots: These contribute a mild, slightly sweet flavor to the sauce.
  • Flour: A light dusting of flour on the chicken helps create a golden-brown crust and thickens the sauce slightly.
  • Thyme: Fresh thyme gives the dish a wonderful herbal note, complementing the Marsala wine’s richness.
  • Salt and Pepper: Essential seasonings to bring all the flavors together.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Instant Pot Chicken Marsala

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by preparing the chicken breasts. If they’re very thick, slice them in half horizontally to create thinner pieces that cook more evenly and quickly. Pat the chicken breasts dry with paper towels, then lightly season them with salt and pepper on both sides. Next, dredge each piece of chicken in flour, shaking off any excess.

Step 2: Sauté the Chicken

Set your Instant Pot to the sauté setting. Add a tablespoon of olive oil and a tablespoon of butter to the pot. Once the butter has melted and the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts in batches, ensuring that each piece has enough space to brown evenly. Sauté the chicken for 2-3 minutes per side until it’s golden brown. You’re not cooking it through at this point—just giving it a nice sear. After browning, remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and set it aside.

Step 3: Sauté the Aromatics

Add the remaining tablespoon of butter to the Instant Pot. Add the minced garlic and chopped shallots. Sauté for about 1-2 minutes until they become fragrant and softened. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

Step 4: Add the Mushrooms

Toss in the sliced mushrooms and cook them for 4-5 minutes until they release their moisture and begin to brown. The mushrooms will absorb the butter and aromatics, giving the dish a deep, savory flavor.

Step 5: Deglaze the Pot

Once the mushrooms are browned, it’s time to deglaze the pot. Pour in the Marsala wine and chicken broth, using a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. These bits are full of flavor, and scraping them off ensures your sauce will have an extra depth of taste.

Step 6: Add the Chicken Back to the Pot

Return the browned chicken breasts to the Instant Pot, placing them on top of the mushroom mixture. Add a few sprigs of fresh thyme on top for an herbal flavor boost. Close the lid and ensure the vent is set to the sealing position.

Step 7: Pressure Cook the Chicken Marsala

Set the Instant Pot to manual or pressure cook on high for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 5 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure. Carefully open the lid.

Step 8: Thicken the Sauce (Optional)

If the sauce looks too thin for your liking, you can thicken it by turning the Instant Pot back to the sauté setting. Mix a teaspoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of water to create a slurry, then stir this into the sauce. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes until it thickens to your desired consistency.

Step 9: Serve the Dish

Once the sauce has reached the right thickness, taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if necessary. Plate the chicken breasts and spoon the mushroom sauce over the top. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley or more thyme if desired.

Tips for Making Perfect Instant Pot Chicken Marsala

  • Chicken Thighs vs. Chicken Breasts: While this recipe uses chicken breasts, chicken thighs work wonderfully in Chicken Marsala. They are more forgiving, stay tender, and can handle a bit more cooking time without drying out.
  • Marsala Wine Selection: Sweet Marsala will give the sauce a richer, slightly sweeter flavor, while dry Marsala provides a more savory taste. Choose according to your personal preference, but both will yield a delicious result.
  • Adding More Vegetables: You can easily make this recipe your own by adding other vegetables like onions, carrots, or peas. Just sauté them with the mushrooms or add them to the sauce for extra flavor.
  • Searing the Chicken: Don’t skip the searing step! Browning the chicken not only enhances the flavor but also creates a nice texture that elevates the dish.
  • Make It a Meal: Serve your Chicken Marsala with a side of mashed potatoes, rice, or pasta to soak up all the delicious sauce. A simple green salad with a tangy vinaigrette complements the richness of the dish perfectly.

Why You’ll Love Instant Pot Chicken Marsala

  1. Quick and Easy: The Instant Pot cuts down on cooking time significantly while still producing a rich, flavorful meal.
  2. Tender Chicken: The pressure cooking process locks in moisture, making the chicken incredibly tender and juicy.
  3. Rich and Savory Sauce: The Marsala wine sauce is deeply flavorful, with earthy mushrooms and a smooth, velvety texture.
  4. One-Pot Meal: The beauty of the Instant Pot is that everything cooks in one pot, making cleanup a breeze.

Final Thoughts

Instant Pot Chicken Marsala is a fantastic way to enjoy a classic Italian dish without the long cooking times. The flavors of Marsala wine, mushrooms, and tender chicken come together in a beautifully rich and comforting sauce that is sure to impress. Whether you’re making this for a special dinner or a weeknight meal, this recipe is a surefire way to elevate your dinner table. The Instant Pot takes all the effort out of cooking while ensuring that you still get all the authentic flavors and textures you love in a Chicken Marsala dish.

Give this recipe a try, and you’ll have a new favorite dish that you can prepare any time you want a comforting, flavorful meal.
