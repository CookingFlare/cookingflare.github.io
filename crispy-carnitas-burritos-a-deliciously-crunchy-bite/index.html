Crispy Carnitas Burritos: A Deliciously Crunchy Bite


Craving a mouthwatering and satisfying meal that combines savory flavors with a satisfying crunch? Look no further than Crispy Carnitas Burritos! These delectable creations are packed with tender and flavorful carnitas, wrapped in a warm tortilla, and crisped to perfection. With a combination of textures and flavors that will leave you craving for more, these burritos are perfect for any meal of the day.

Ingredients

To make these Crispy Carnitas Burritos, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of pork shoulder, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, guacamole, salsa, chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the pork shoulder pieces and brown them on all sides until they are crispy and golden brown. This step will take about 10 minutes. Remove the pork from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the cumin powder, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to coat the onion and garlic mixture with the spices.

  4. Return the browned pork shoulder to the skillet and pour in the chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer, covered, for about 2 hours or until the pork is tender and easily shreds apart.

  5. Once the pork is cooked, use two forks to shred it into smaller pieces. Increase the heat to medium-high and cook the shredded pork for an additional 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it becomes crispy and caramelized.

  6. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds until they are pliable.

  7. To assemble the Crispy Carnitas Burritos, spoon a generous amount of the crispy pork onto the center of each tortilla. Add any optional toppings you desire, such as shredded cheese, sour cream, guacamole, salsa, or chopped cilantro.

  8. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up tightly from one end to the other, creating a burrito shape.

  9. If desired, you can crisp up the burritos by heating a clean skillet over medium heat and cooking each burrito for a few minutes on each side until they are golden brown and crispy.

  10. Serve the Crispy Carnitas Burritos immediately, and enjoy the delicious combination of tender and flavorful carnitas, wrapped in a crispy tortilla.

Conclusion

Crispy Carnitas Burritos are a delightful and satisfying meal that brings together the comforting flavors of tender pork shoulder and the satisfying crunch of a crispy tortilla shell. With the option to customize with your favorite toppings, these burritos are versatile and can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. So, next time you’re in the mood for a deliciously crunchy bite, give these Crispy Carnitas Burritos a try and savor every flavorful and satisfying moment.
