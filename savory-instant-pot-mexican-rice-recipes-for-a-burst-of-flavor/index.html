Savory Instant Pot Mexican Rice Recipes for a Burst of Flavor


If you’re looking for a delicious and flavorful dish to add to your repertoire, you’re in the right place. Mexican rice, also known as “arroz rojo,” is a staple in Mexican cuisine, and it’s a versatile and savory dish that pairs perfectly with a variety of main courses or can stand alone as a satisfying meal. In this blog post, we’ll explore how to create mouthwatering Mexican rice using the Instant Pot, a handy kitchen appliance that streamlines the cooking process and locks in the vibrant flavors of the ingredients.

Instant Pot Mexican Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (10 oz) enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Start by rinsing the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This helps remove excess starch and prevents the rice from becoming sticky.

  2. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” function. Once hot, add the vegetable oil and chopped onions. Sauté the onions until they become translucent, then add the minced garlic and sauté for an additional 30 seconds.

  3. Add the rinsed rice to the Instant Pot, stirring constantly to coat the grains with the oil and onions. Toast the rice for a couple of minutes until it turns a light golden color.

  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes, enchilada sauce, and chicken or vegetable broth. Stir in the ground cumin, chili powder, and a pinch of salt and pepper. If you’re adding peas and carrots, this is the time to mix them in.

  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, ensuring the steam release valve is set to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 3 minutes, then allow a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully releasing any remaining pressure.

  6. Once the pressure has released, carefully remove the lid and fluff the rice with a fork. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  7. Serve the Instant Pot Mexican rice garnished with freshly chopped cilantro for a burst of freshness and color.

Instant Pot Mexican Rice Variations

1. Vegetarian Option:

For a vegetarian twist, swap the chicken broth for vegetable broth and add in extra veggies such as bell peppers, corn, or zucchini to amp up the nutritional value and add a pop of color to your dish.

2. Spicy Kick:

If you enjoy a bit of heat, consider adding a diced jalapeño or a pinch of red pepper flakes to the rice mixture for a spicy kick that will tantalize your taste buds.

3. Protein Boost:

To turn this side dish into a complete meal, stir in some cooked black beans, shredded chicken, or seasoned ground beef after the rice has finished cooking. This will elevate the dish to a hearty and protein-packed one-pot meal.

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, and it’s particularly adept at bringing out the rich and bold flavors of Mexican cuisine. Whether you’re serving up a family dinner or preparing a festive fiesta, this Instant Pot Mexican rice recipe is sure to be a hit. The combination of aromatic spices, tangy tomatoes, and fluffy rice will transport your taste buds south of the border for a culinary adventure. So, fire up your Instant Pot and embark on a flavor-packed journey with this savory Mexican rice recipe!
