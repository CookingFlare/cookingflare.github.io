A French Twist on Brunch: Croque Madame Waffles


Brunch is a beloved tradition that brings together the best of breakfast and lunch. And what better way to elevate your brunch game than with a French twist? Introducing Croque Madame Waffles – a delightful fusion of the classic Croque Madame sandwich and the beloved breakfast staple, waffles. This dish is guaranteed to impress your guests and leave them craving for more. So, let’s dive right into this delectable recipe!

Ingredients

For the waffles:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

For the béchamel sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the filling:

  • 8 slices of ham
  • 4 slices of Swiss cheese

For the topping:

  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, milk, and melted butter. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough waffles.

  3. Scoop the batter onto the preheated waffle iron, using about 1/2 cup of batter for each waffle. Close the lid and cook until golden brown and crisp. This usually takes about 4-5 minutes, but cooking times may vary depending on your waffle iron. Repeat this step until all the batter is used.

  4. While the waffles are cooking, let’s prepare the béchamel sauce. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the flour and whisk continuously for about 1 minute, until the mixture is smooth and bubbly. Gradually whisk in the milk, salt, nutmeg, and black pepper. Cook the sauce, stirring constantly, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon. Remove from heat and set aside.

  5. To assemble the Croque Madame Waffles, place a slice of ham on top of each waffle. Add a slice of Swiss cheese on top of the ham. Place the waffles on a baking sheet and broil on high for 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. While the waffles are in the oven, fry the eggs in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Once the waffles are out of the oven, carefully transfer each waffle onto a plate. Top each waffle with a fried egg and drizzle with the prepared béchamel sauce. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for an extra touch of freshness.

  8. Serve the Croque Madame Waffles immediately and enjoy the perfect combination of crispy waffles, savory ham, melted cheese, and creamy béchamel sauce.

Conclusion

Croque Madame Waffles are a delightful twist on the classic brunch dish. The combination of the crispy waffles, savory ham, melted Swiss cheese, and creamy béchamel sauce is a flavor explosion that will leave your taste buds begging for more. Whether you’re hosting a brunch for friends or simply treating yourself to a special weekend breakfast, these waffles are sure to impress. So, next time you’re in the mood for a French twist on brunch, give these Croque Madame Waffles a try. Bon appétit!
