Vegan Strawberry Rhubarb Crisp: Sweet and Tart


If you’re looking for a delightful vegan dessert that perfectly balances sweet and tart flavors, look no further than this Vegan Strawberry Rhubarb Crisp recipe. This dessert is a celebration of seasonal fruits, combining the sweetness of ripe strawberries with the tangy bite of fresh rhubarb. The best part? It’s entirely plant-based, making it a guilt-free indulgence for vegans and non-vegans alike.

Ingredients

For the Filling

  • 4 cups sliced rhubarb
  • 4 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the Crisp Topping

  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Filling: In a large bowl, combine the sliced rhubarb, sliced strawberries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, vanilla extract, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Gently toss the mixture until the fruit is evenly coated with the sugar and cornstarch. Transfer the filling to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out into an even layer.

  3. Make the Crisp Topping: In another bowl, mix together the rolled oats, all-purpose flour, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and salt. Pour the melted coconut oil over the dry ingredients and stir until the mixture is evenly moistened and clumps together.

  4. Assemble and Bake: Sprinkle the crisp topping over the fruit filling, covering it completely. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the filling is bubbly, and the topping is golden brown.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the Vegan Strawberry Rhubarb Crisp is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Serve the crisp warm, optionally topped with a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream or a dollop of coconut whipped cream.

Why You’ll Love This Vegan Strawberry Rhubarb Crisp

Seasonal Delight

This recipe takes full advantage of seasonal produce, allowing you to enjoy the natural flavors of ripe strawberries and fresh rhubarb at their peak.

Healthier Indulgence

By using wholesome ingredients and avoiding dairy and eggs, this vegan dessert offers a healthier approach to indulging in a sweet treat.

Simple and Versatile

With minimal ingredients and straightforward instructions, this recipe is accessible to both beginner and experienced bakers. Plus, you can easily customize the crisp by adding nuts or adjusting the sweetness to suit your taste.

Crowd-Pleaser

Whether you’re serving a family dinner or hosting a gathering, this Vegan Strawberry Rhubarb Crisp is sure to impress and satisfy everyone, regardless of their dietary preferences.

So, next time you’re craving a luscious dessert that embraces the flavors of the season, give this Vegan Strawberry Rhubarb Crisp a try. It’s a delightful way to savor the sweetness of summer while staying true to your vegan lifestyle.
