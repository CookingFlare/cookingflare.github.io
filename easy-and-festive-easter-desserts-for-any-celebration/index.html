Easy and Festive Easter Desserts for Any Celebration


As the air turns warmer and the blooms burst with color, we know spring is right around the corner. One of the most anticipated holidays of the season is just a few short weeks away - Easter! With its focus on families, friends, and the ever-popular egg hunt, what better way to celebrate than with some scrumptious desserts to delight everyone?

Here are a few homemade options that are simple to prepare, yet elegant and festive enough for any Easter gathering.

1. Chocolate-Covered Marshmallow Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Graham cracker crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons melted butter
  • 36 large marshmallows
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips
  • Food coloring (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter; press onto the bottom of a foil-lined pan.
  2. Melt chocolate chips in a double boiler or microwave. Let cool slightly.
  3. Divide marshmallows among 36 mini muffin liners. Use food coloring to create pastel colors if desired.
  4. Dip each marshmallow, partially, into the melted chocolate. Place onto the crumb crust.
  5. Repeat dipping process; sprinkle with remaining chocolate.
  6. Refrigerate until firm. Enjoy these delectable chocolate-covered marshmallow eggs!

2. Easter Cookie Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 box devil’s food cake mix
  • 1 box French vanilla cake mix
  • 1 container chocolate frosting
  • Easter M&M candies

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the devil’s food cake mix according to the instructions on the box.
  2. Prepare the French vanilla cake mix, but substitute the water called for with milk to create a thicker, richer texture.
  3. Bake both cakes and let them cool completely.
  4. Spread half of the chocolate frosting onto the bottom cake layer.
  5. Place the second cake layer on top of the frosting; spread remaining frosting around the sides of the cake.
  6. Place Easter M&M candies around the base of the cake, creating a border.
  7. Simply stack your cookie cake with more joyful M&Ms as decoration!

3. Easter Bunny Bark

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup Easter candy (peeps or other favorites)
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut
  • Food coloring (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Melt white chocolate chips in a double boiler or microwave. Let cool slightly.
  2. Color the melted chocolate with food coloring if desired. Spread onto a flat surface, such as a cutting board or baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle shredded coconut on top of the chocolate while it’s still wet.
  4. Press desired Easter candy lightly into the mixture.
  5. Let the chocolate set completely in the refrigerator.
  6. Break into small pieces and serve these delightful Easter bunny bark treats!

These desserts are a delightful way to celebrate the Easter holiday. From the whimsical chocolate-covered marshmallow eggs to the layered and mossy-topped cookie cake, to the playful bunny bark - every selection adds a creative touch to any celebration. Now, go ahead and unleash your creativity in making these enticing treats for family, friends, and loved ones this Easter season!
