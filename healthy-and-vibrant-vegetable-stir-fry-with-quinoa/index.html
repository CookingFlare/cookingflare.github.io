Healthy and Vibrant Vegetable Stir Fry with Quinoa


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized, look no further than this Healthy and Vibrant Vegetable Stir Fry with Quinoa recipe. Packed with colorful veggies and protein-rich quinoa, this stir fry is not only a feast for the eyes but also for the taste buds. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by rinsing the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is fluffy and the water has been absorbed. Set aside.

  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic and sauté until fragrant and slightly softened, about 2 minutes.

  3. Add the sliced bell peppers, zucchini, carrot, broccoli, snap peas, and mushrooms to the skillet. Stir fry the vegetables for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp. You want to retain some of their natural crunch for added texture.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the skillet and toss to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Finally, add the cooked quinoa to the skillet and gently stir to combine everything. Cook for another 2 minutes, until the quinoa is heated through.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve the Healthy and Vibrant Vegetable Stir Fry with Quinoa hot and enjoy!

Health Benefits

This Vegetable Stir Fry with Quinoa is not only a tasty and satisfying meal, but it’s also incredibly healthy. Here are some of the key benefits:

  • Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids that our bodies need. It’s also high in fiber and packed with vitamins and minerals.
  • The colorful array of vegetables provides a wide range of nutrients, including vitamins A, C, and K, as well as antioxidants that help protect against cell damage.
  • The low-sodium soy sauce provides a savory flavor without the excessive salt, making it a heart-healthy choice.
  • The use of olive oil and sesame oil adds healthy fats, which are essential for brain health and nutrient absorption.

Conclusion

This Healthy and Vibrant Vegetable Stir Fry with Quinoa is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nourishing meal that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized. With its vibrant colors, delicious flavors, and health benefits, it’s a dish that can be enjoyed by vegans, vegetarians, and meat-eaters alike. So why not give it a try and add some vibrancy to your mealtime?
