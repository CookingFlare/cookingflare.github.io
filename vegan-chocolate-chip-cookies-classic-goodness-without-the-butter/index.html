Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies: Classic Goodness without the Butter


Who doesn’t love a warm, gooey chocolate chip cookie fresh from the oven? The nostalgic aroma, the soft texture, and the melty chocolate chips – it’s a treat that never goes out of style. And what if I told you that you can enjoy all of that goodness in a vegan version, without sacrificing flavor or texture? Yes, you heard it right! In this recipe, we’re going to create the most delightful, mouth-watering Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies that even non-vegans will love.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Additional Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Combine the Wet Ingredients: In another large bowl, mix the melted coconut oil and coconut sugar until well combined. Add the almond milk and vanilla extract, and continue to mix until smooth.

  4. Combine the Mixtures: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a dough forms. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Add Chocolate Chips: Gently fold in the vegan chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the cookie dough.

  6. Form Cookie Dough: Using a cookie scoop or a spoon, portion the dough into balls and place them onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space between each cookie.

  7. Bake to Perfection: Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. Keep in mind that the cookies will continue to set as they cool.

  8. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Why Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies?

Now, you might be wondering, “Why should I make vegan chocolate chip cookies when I can just make the classic version with butter?” Well, let me tell you – these vegan cookies are not only kinder to animals and the environment, but they are also incredibly delicious and satisfying. The coconut oil gives them a rich, buttery flavor without the need for dairy, and the coconut sugar adds a subtle caramel undertone that complements the chocolate chips perfectly.

Tips for Perfect Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Use Melted Coconut Oil: Make sure your coconut oil is melted but not hot. This will ensure that the cookie dough comes together smoothly.

  • Don’t Overmix: Overmixing the dough can result in tough cookies. Mix until the ingredients are just combined.

  • Choose Quality Chocolate Chips: Opt for high-quality vegan chocolate chips for the best flavor and texture.

  • Let Them Cool: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet before transferring them to a wire rack. This helps them set and achieve the perfect texture.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, these Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies are a delightful alternative to the classic buttery version. They are easy to make, require simple ingredients, and are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth while sticking to a vegan diet. Whether you’re vegan, dairy-free, or simply curious about trying something new, these cookies are sure to impress. So, go ahead, preheat that oven, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to a batch of these delectable vegan delights. Happy baking!
