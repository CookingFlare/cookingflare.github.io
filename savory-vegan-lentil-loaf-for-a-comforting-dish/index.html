Savory Vegan Lentil Loaf for a Comforting Dish


Are you craving a comforting and hearty dish that’s also vegan-friendly? Look no further than this delicious Savory Vegan Lentil Loaf recipe! Packed with flavor and nutrients, this lentil loaf will satisfy your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied. Whether you’re a vegan looking for a new go-to recipe or simply want to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this savory lentil loaf is a perfect choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils, cooked
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce or tamari
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons water (flax egg)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a loaf pan with oil or cooking spray.

  2. In a large skillet, sauté the onion and garlic over medium heat until translucent and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the grated carrot, celery, red bell pepper, and mushrooms to the skillet. Cook for another 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked lentils, sautéed vegetables, rolled oats, breadcrumbs, tomato paste, soy sauce or tamari, nutritional yeast, flax egg, dried thyme, dried oregano, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  5. Transfer the lentil loaf mixture into the greased loaf pan, pressing it down firmly to ensure it holds its shape.

  6. Bake the lentil loaf in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and firm to the touch.

  7. Remove the lentil loaf from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring it to a serving plate.

  8. Slice the lentil loaf into thick slices and serve it warm with your favorite sides, such as mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or a fresh salad.

Enjoy Your Savory Vegan Lentil Loaf

This Savory Vegan Lentil Loaf is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for lunch, dinner, or even as leftovers the next day. It’s packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, making it a healthy and satisfying option for vegans and non-vegans alike. The combination of lentils, vegetables, herbs, and spices creates a flavorful loaf that will please your taste buds and leave you feeling nourished.

Feel free to experiment with different herbs, spices, or vegetables to customize the lentil loaf to your liking. You can also get creative with the leftovers by using them in sandwiches, wraps, or even crumbled over salads. The possibilities are endless!

So why wait? Give this Savory Vegan Lentil Loaf a try and discover the deliciousness of plant-based comfort food. Your taste buds and body will thank you!
