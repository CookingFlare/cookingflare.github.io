Sweet and Sour Tofu with Pineapple


Craving a delicious and colorful dish that combines sweet, sour, and savory flavors in one satisfying bite? Look no further! This Sweet and Sour Tofu with Pineapple recipe is a delightful twist on a classic Asian-inspired dish. Perfect for both vegans and non-vegans alike, this dish is sure to impress your taste buds and your guests. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 1 cup of pineapple chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable broth
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons of water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prep the Tofu: Start by draining the tofu and pressing out excess water. Cut the tofu into cubes and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix together the vegetable broth, soy sauce, rice vinegar, ketchup, brown sugar, and the cornstarch-water mixture. This will be your sweet and sour sauce.

  3. Stir-Fry Tofu: In a large pan or wok, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until it turns golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the pan and set it aside.

  4. Sauté Vegetables: In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Sauté the garlic, ginger, onion, and bell peppers until they are slightly softened.

  5. Combine Ingredients: Once the vegetables are cooked, add the pineapple chunks and the cooked tofu back to the pan.

  6. Add the Sauce: Pour the sweet and sour sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Stir gently to coat everything in the sauce. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.

  7. Adjust Seasoning: Taste the dish and add salt and pepper as needed. You can also adjust the sweetness or sourness by adding more sugar or vinegar according to your preference.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Garnish the dish with chopped green onions and serve the Sweet and Sour Tofu with Pineapple hot over a bed of steamed rice.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra kick, add a dash of chili sauce or red pepper flakes.
  • Customize the vegetables according to your preference. You can add carrots, snow peas, or broccoli.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, increase the amount of cornstarch in the recipe.
  • Feel free to experiment with different proteins like tempeh or seitan instead of tofu.

Now that you have this delectable Sweet and Sour Tofu with Pineapple recipe in your arsenal, you can impress your family and friends with a vibrant and flavorful dish that is both satisfying and nutritious. Whether you are a tofu enthusiast or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is a winner. Enjoy your culinary adventure in the comfort of your kitchen! Happy cooking!
