All-Time Favorite: Homemade Chicken Pot Pie Recipe


There’s nothing quite like the comforting aroma of a homemade chicken pot pie wafting through the kitchen. This classic dish has been a favorite for generations, and for good reason. The buttery crust, creamy filling, and hearty chunks of chicken make it the ultimate comfort food. Whether you’re hosting a family dinner or simply craving a cozy meal, this homemade chicken pot pie recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken broth
  • 2/3 cup milk

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  2. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or fork until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, tossing with a fork until the dough holds together.
  4. Divide the dough in half, shape into two discs, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a large saucepan, sauté the onion in butter until tender.
  2. Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, and celery seed until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the chicken broth and milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and bubbles.
  4. Add the cooked chicken and peas and carrots, then remove from heat.

Step 3: Assemble the Pie

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out one disc of dough to fit a 9-inch pie plate.
  3. Pour the chicken mixture into the crust.
  4. Roll out the remaining dough and place it over the filling. Trim, seal, and flute the edges.
  5. Cut slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.

Step 4: Bake

  1. Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips.
  2. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  3. Let the pie stand for 5-10 minutes before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • For a golden and flaky crust, brush the top of the pie with an egg wash before baking.
  • Feel free to add your favorite herbs and spices to the filling for extra flavor.
  • Substitute the chicken with turkey for a delicious twist on this classic recipe.

Enjoy Your Homemade Chicken Pot Pie!

There’s something truly special about sitting down to a steaming slice of homemade chicken pot pie. The warmth and richness of the filling, paired with the buttery, flaky crust, create a dish that’s both comforting and satisfying. Whether it’s a chilly winter evening or a leisurely Sunday dinner, this timeless recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the homemade goodness of a classic chicken pot pie.
