Savory Picnic Tarts for a Flavorful Bite


Are you tired of the same old sandwiches for your picnic lunches? Looking for a delicious and portable option that will leave you craving more? Look no further! These savory picnic tarts are the perfect solution for a flavorful bite on the go. Packed with mouthwatering ingredients and bursting with flavor, these tarts are sure to impress your taste buds and your fellow picnickers.

Ingredients

For the tart crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Tart Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingertips until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.
  3. Shape the dough into a ball, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.
  3. Stir in the chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the filling cool slightly. Stir in the crumbled feta cheese.

Step 3: Assemble the Tarts

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled tart dough to about 1/8 inch thickness. Cut out circles using a round cookie cutter or a glass.
  3. Place the circles of dough into greased tart pans or a muffin tin, pressing gently to line the sides and bottom.
  4. Spoon the filling into each tart shell, filling them about 3/4 of the way.
  5. Fold any excess dough over the edges of the tarts or use a second round of dough to cover the filling, creating a rustic look.
  6. Bake the tarts in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is heated through.

Step 4: Enjoy!

Once the tarts are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before removing them from the pans. These savory picnic tarts are best enjoyed warm or at room temperature. They make a perfect addition to any picnic spread or as a standalone meal. The combination of flaky crust, savory filling, and the tanginess of feta cheese is simply irresistible.

So, the next time you plan a picnic, ditch the sandwiches and give these savory picnic tarts a try. With their delicious flavors and easy portability, they are sure to become your go-to picnic treat. Enjoy the outdoors while savoring every bite of these delectable tarts. Happy picnicking!

Note: Feel free to experiment with the filling by adding your favorite herbs, spices, or even different types of cheese to customize the tarts to your liking.
