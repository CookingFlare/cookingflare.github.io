Satisfying One Pot Creamy Spinach Pasta


Are you in the mood for a comforting and satisfying meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup? Look no further than this delicious one pot creamy spinach pasta recipe! This dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want something hearty and flavorful without spending hours in the kitchen. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak some extra greens into your diet, making it a hit with both kids and adults. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this creamy, flavorful pasta dish in just one pot.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz (340g) of fettuccine or your favorite pasta
  • 4 cups (960ml) of vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup (240ml) of heavy cream
  • 3 cups (90g) of fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup (100g) of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a spicy kick)

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the Pasta and Broth: Once the aromatics are soft, add the fettuccine or pasta of your choice to the pot. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth, ensuring that the pasta is mostly submerged. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  3. Simmer and Stir: Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the pasta cook in the broth, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking. Cook for about 10-12 minutes or until the pasta is al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed.

  4. Add the Cream and Spinach: Pour in the heavy cream and add the chopped spinach to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Let the spinach wilt and the sauce thicken for a few minutes.

  5. Season and Serve: Sprinkle in the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until it melts and creates a luscious, creamy sauce. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired. Remove the pot from the heat.

  6. Garnish and Enjoy: Let the pasta sit for a few minutes to allow the sauce to thicken further. Serve the creamy spinach pasta in individual bowls, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh herbs if desired.

Tips for Success:

  • Use fresh spinach for the best flavor and texture, but frozen spinach can be substituted if needed.
  • Feel free to add cooked chicken, shrimp, or crispy bacon for extra protein and flavor.
  • If the sauce is too thick, simply stir in a splash of broth or cream to reach your desired consistency.
  • Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, though it’s best enjoyed fresh.

In Conclusion:

This one pot creamy spinach pasta is a versatile and satisfying dish that’s sure to become a staple in your recipe repertoire. Whether you’re cooking for a quick family dinner or hosting a casual gathering with friends, this recipe is a winner. With its creamy, indulgent sauce and vibrant green spinach, it’s a feast for both the eyes and the taste buds. So, next time you’re craving a comforting pasta dish, give this recipe a try and savor the delicious simplicity of one pot cooking. Happy cooking!
