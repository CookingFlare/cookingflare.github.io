Lemon Herb Baked Cod: A Simple and Healthy Recipe


Cod is a versatile and mild-flavored fish that lends itself beautifully to various cooking methods. One of the easiest and healthiest ways to prepare cod is by baking it with a flavorful lemon herb marinade. This recipe not only enhances the natural taste of the fish but also keeps it moist and tender, making it a perfect choice for a quick and nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 cod fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Prepare the Marinade

Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and chopped dill. This marinade will infuse the cod with vibrant flavors and ensure it stays moist during baking.

2. Season the Cod

Place the cod fillets on a parchment-lined baking sheet or in a baking dish. Season each fillet generously with salt and pepper on both sides. This step ensures that the fish is well-seasoned and enhances its natural taste.

3. Apply the Marinade

Using a brush or spoon, evenly coat each cod fillet with the prepared lemon herb marinade. Make sure to cover the fish thoroughly, ensuring that the flavors penetrate into the flesh. Reserve a small amount of marinade for basting later.

4. Bake the Cod

Place the baking sheet or dish with the cod into the preheated oven. Bake for 12-15 minutes, depending on the thickness of the fillets. The cod is done when it flakes easily with a fork and reaches an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C).

5. Baste and Serve

Halfway through baking, carefully open the oven and baste the cod with the reserved marinade. This extra step adds an additional layer of flavor and helps keep the fish moist. Once fully cooked, remove the cod from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve the lemon herb baked cod hot, garnished with additional fresh herbs if desired.
  • Pair it with a side of steamed vegetables, such as asparagus or green beans, to complete the meal.
  • For a heartier dish, serve the cod over a bed of quinoa or couscous, drizzled with a bit of lemon juice and olive oil.

Nutritional Benefits

Cod is rich in high-quality protein and low in fat, making it an excellent choice for a healthy diet. It’s also a good source of vitamins and minerals, including vitamin B12, phosphorus, and selenium. The lemon and herbs not only enhance the flavor but also add antioxidants and beneficial phytonutrients.

Final Thoughts

Lemon herb baked cod is a simple yet elegant dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion, this recipe is a perfect go-to option. With minimal preparation and a short cooking time, you can enjoy delicious, tender cod with bright citrus and herb flavors. Plus, it’s a healthy choice that fits well into a balanced diet.

Try this lemon herb baked cod recipe soon, and discover just how easy it is to create a gourmet meal at home with wholesome ingredients. Enjoy the flavors of fresh herbs and zesty lemon while savoring the delicate taste of perfectly baked cod. Happy cooking!
