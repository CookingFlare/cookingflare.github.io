One-Bite Cookie Dough Filled Cupcakes: Cookie and Cake Mashup


If you’re a true cookie or cake lover, you’ll surely adore this incredible hybrid dessert: the one-bite cookie dough filled cupcake. This tantalizing treat is not just a delightful fusion of two classic desserts, but a timeless indulgence for your taste buds. Crafted with love and adorned with a burst of cookie dough goodness, these cupcakes are sure to satiate your sweet cravings and elevate your baking game. Jump right in, and let’s dive into the whimsical world of the one-bite cookie dough filled cupcake - a unique creation that brings together the softness of a cupcake with the rich, creamy flavors of cookie dough.

Ingredients

Cupcake:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking power
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Cookie Dough Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter (or your preferred nut butter)
  • 1 large egg

Instructions

Preparing the Cupcake Base

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a 24-cavity cupcake tin with paper liners.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy using either a hand mixer or stand mixer.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, while beating continuously. Give your mixture a scraping down between each addition to ensure full incorporation.

  4. Stir in the vanilla extract, followed by the milk, baking powder, and baking soda.

  5. Gradually add the flour to the batter, combining gently until fully incorporated.

  6. Fill each cupcake liner with equal amounts of batter (around 3/4 full) and smooth the top using a spatula or the back of a spoon.

Baking Cupcakes

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for about 16-18 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  2. Once done, remove cupcakes from the oven and place them on a cooling rack. Allow them to cool completely before proceeding.

Making the Cookie Dough Filling

  1. In a separate mixing bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until the mixture is smooth and creamy.

  2. Stir in the salt, followed by the flour, peanut butter, and egg. Mix until you have a uniform cookie dough.

Assembling the Cupcakes

  1. Take a large baking tip or a piping bag fitted with a round tip (about 1/2 inch diameter), and fill it with the cookie dough filling.

  2. Gently make a small hole in the center of the cupcake using an apple corer or a small knife.

  3. Insert the piping bag into the hole, and gently squeeze the cookie dough filling into the cupcake until it starts to overflow slightly.

  4. If desired, top the cupcakes with frosting or simply dust with powdered sugar for an elegant and effortless finish.

Storing the Cupcakes

Store the cupcakes in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to one week. Feel free to enjoy your One-Bite Cookie Dough Filled Cupcakes as-is, or pair them with a scoop of your favorite ice cream for an extra indulgent treat!

Prepare to be blown away by the delightful combination of moist, indulgent cupcakes and the irresistible cookie dough filling. These one-bite wonders are guaranteed to make you the talk of any gathering - happy baking!
