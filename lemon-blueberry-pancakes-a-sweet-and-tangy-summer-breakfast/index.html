Lemon Blueberry Pancakes: A Sweet and Tangy Summer Breakfast


There’s something about the combination of sweet blueberries and zesty lemon that screams summer. And what better way to start a sunny day than with a stack of fluffy Lemon Blueberry Pancakes? This delightful breakfast treat is bursting with fresh flavors and is the perfect way to kick off a lazy weekend morning or a special brunch with loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Maple syrup for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and white sugar.

  2. In a separate bowl, combine the milk, egg, melted butter, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Stir until well combined.

  3. Gently fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, being careful not to overmix. A few lumps are okay – they’ll make your pancakes extra fluffy!

  4. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to break them.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.

  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.

  7. Repeat with the remaining batter, adding more butter or oil to the skillet as needed.

  8. Serve the pancakes warm, stacked high and drizzled with maple syrup.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of lemon flavor, consider adding a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to the pancake batter just before cooking.
  • If fresh blueberries are not in season, frozen blueberries can be used as a substitute.
  • For a touch of indulgence, sprinkle some powdered sugar over the pancakes before serving.
  • These pancakes are also delicious with a dollop of whipped cream or a side of crispy bacon.

Why Lemon Blueberry Pancakes?

Lemon Blueberry Pancakes are the epitome of a summer breakfast. The bright, citrusy notes of lemon perfectly complement the juicy, sweet burst of blueberries, creating a flavor combination that is both refreshing and indulgent. The fluffy texture of the pancakes provides the perfect canvas for the vibrant flavors to shine through, making each bite a delightful explosion of taste.

Moreover, these pancakes are incredibly versatile. Whether you’re hosting a brunch for friends, treating your family to a special breakfast, or simply wanting to elevate your weekend morning routine, Lemon Blueberry Pancakes are a surefire way to bring a smile to everyone’s face.

In addition to their delicious taste, these pancakes are also a feast for the eyes. The vibrant blueberries speckled throughout the golden pancakes add a pop of color that is as visually appealing as it is appetizing.

In Conclusion

Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a novice in the kitchen, Lemon Blueberry Pancakes are a delightful addition to your breakfast repertoire. With their sunny flavors and easy preparation, they are the perfect way to celebrate the joys of summer and create lasting memories with loved ones. So, dust off your griddle, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor a sweet and tangy breakfast experience that will leave you craving more.
