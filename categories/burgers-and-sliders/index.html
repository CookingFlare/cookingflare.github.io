Beyond Beef: Creative Bison Burger Recipes to Impress

If you’re looking to add a unique and flavorful twist to your summer grilling menu, bison burgers are the way to go. Beyond Beef, known for its delicious plant-based products, offers a delectable ground bison option that’s perfect for creating mouthwatering burgers. In this blog post, we’ll explore some creative and impressive bison burger recipes that are sure to elevate your next cookout. 1. Classic Bison Burger with Caramelized Onions Ingredients: 1 pound Beyond Beef ground bison 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 large onion, thinly sliced Salt and pepper to taste 4 burger buns Your favorite burger toppings (lettuce, tomato, cheese, etc.) Instructions: Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat and add the sliced onions. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the onions are soft and golden brown. While the onions are caramelizing, divide the ground bison into four equal portions and form them into burger patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the bison patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute or two. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled bison patties on the toasted buns, topping them with the caramelized onions, and adding your favorite burger fixings. 2. Spicy Southwestern Bison Burger Ingredients: 1 pound Beyond Beef ground bison 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper Salt and pepper to taste 4 burger buns Pepper jack cheese, sliced Sliced avocado Salsa Instructions: In a bowl, combine the ground bison, chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix until the spices are evenly distributed throughout the meat. Form the seasoned bison into four patties and grill them over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes on each side. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of pepper jack cheese on each patty to melt. Toast the burger buns on the grill. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled bison patties on the buns, topping them with sliced avocado and a spoonful of salsa. 3. Teriyaki Pineapple Bison Burger Ingredients: 1 pound Beyond Beef ground bison 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce 4 pineapple slices 4 burger buns Red onion, thinly sliced Lettuce Instructions: Pour the teriyaki sauce over the ground bison and mix until well combined. Form the meat into four patties. Grill the bison patties over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes on each side. During the last minute of grilling, place a pineapple slice on each patty to caramelize. Toast the burger buns on the grill. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled bison patties on the buns, topping them with the caramelized pineapple slices, red onion, and lettuce. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a gourmet burger at home, Beyond Beef ground bison is a versatile and flavorful option that’s sure to satisfy. Tantalizing Teriyaki Burger Recipes for Asian-Inspired Flavors

If you’re a fan of burgers and Asian cuisine, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll explore some tantalizing teriyaki burger recipes that will surely satisfy your cravings for Asian-inspired flavors. These burgers are packed with umami goodness and a perfect blend of sweet and savory flavors. So let’s dive right in! Classic Teriyaki Burger Ingredients: 1 pound ground beef 1/4 cup soy sauce 2 tablespoons brown sugar 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon grated ginger 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 4 burger buns Lettuce, tomato, and onion for garnish Instructions: In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Mix well until the sugar has dissolved. In a separate bowl, gently mix the ground beef with the teriyaki sauce. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough burgers. Divide the mixture into four equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on each bun, and top with lettuce, tomato, and onion. Serve hot and enjoy! Teriyaki Chicken Burger Ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce 1 tablespoon honey 1 tablespoon sesame oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon grated ginger 4 burger buns Lettuce, cucumber, and mayonnaise for garnish Instructions: In a bowl, combine the teriyaki sauce, honey, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Mix well. Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish and pour the teriyaki marinade over them. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Cook the chicken breasts for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster. Assemble the burgers by placing a sliced chicken breast on each bun, and top with lettuce, cucumber, and mayonnaise. Serve hot and enjoy! Teriyaki Portobello Mushroom Burger Ingredients: 4 Portobello mushroom caps 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon grated ginger 4 burger buns Swiss cheese, avocado, and sprouts for garnish Instructions: In a bowl, combine the teriyaki sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Mix well. Place the mushroom caps in a shallow dish and pour the teriyaki marinade over them. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Cook the mushroom caps for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are tender. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster. Assemble the burgers by placing a mushroom cap on each bun, and top with Swiss cheese, avocado, and sprouts. Serve hot and enjoy! Whether you prefer beef, chicken, or vegetarian options, there’s a teriyaki burger to suit every palate. Mediterranean Lamb Sliders: Bursting with Flavor

If you’re looking for a fresh and flavorful twist on classic sliders, these Mediterranean Lamb Sliders are sure to tantalize your taste buds. Bursting with the vibrant and aromatic flavors of the Mediterranean, these sliders are the perfect addition to any gathering, whether it’s a casual weeknight dinner or a festive backyard barbecue. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create these mouthwatering delights! Ingredients For the Lamb Patties: 1 pound ground lamb 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon Salt and pepper to taste For the Tzatziki Sauce: 1 cup Greek yogurt 1/2 cucumber, peeled, seeded, and finely grated 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped 1 clove garlic, minced 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil Salt and pepper to taste For Assembly: Mini slider buns Sliced tomatoes Sliced red onion Fresh lettuce leaves Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Lamb Patties In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground lamb, finely chopped red onion, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, coriander, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Gently mix the ingredients until well combined, being careful not to overwork the meat. Divide the mixture into small portions and shape them into mini patties, slightly larger than the size of the slider buns. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the lamb patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness. Step 2: Prepare the Tzatziki Sauce In a medium bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, grated cucumber, chopped dill, minced garlic, extra virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper. Stir the ingredients until well combined, and adjust the seasoning to taste. Step 3: Assemble the Sliders Slice the mini slider buns in half and lightly toast them on the grill or in the oven. Spread a generous dollop of the prepared tzatziki sauce on the bottom half of each slider bun. Place a cooked lamb patty on top of the tzatziki sauce. Add a slice of tomato, a few rings of red onion, and a fresh lettuce leaf. Top the sliders with the remaining half of the buns and secure them with toothpicks if needed. Step 4: Serve and Enjoy Plate the Mediterranean Lamb Sliders and serve them alongside your favorite sides, such as a crisp Greek salad, roasted vegetables, or seasoned fries.
