One-Pot Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta: A Spicy and Satisfying Meal


Are you looking for a quick and flavorful meal that will satisfy your cravings? Look no further than this One-Pot Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta recipe. Packed with spicy Cajun flavors, succulent shrimp, and savory sausage, this dish is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. And the best part? It can be made in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 12 ounces penne pasta
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the sausage slices and cook until browned. Remove the sausage from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant.

  3. Add the shrimp to the pot and cook until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the pot and set aside.

  4. Pour the chicken broth, diced tomatoes, and heavy cream into the pot. Stir in the Cajun seasoning, paprika, dried thyme, dried oregano, cayenne pepper (if desired), salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil.

  5. Once the mixture is boiling, add the penne pasta. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  6. When the pasta is cooked al dente, add the cooked sausage and shrimp back to the pot. Stir everything together, ensuring that the pasta is well-coated in the sauce.

  7. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. The sauce will thicken slightly as it cools.

  8. Serve the One-Pot Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta in bowls, garnished with freshly chopped parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier dish, increase the amount of Cajun seasoning and cayenne pepper. Adjust the heat level to your liking.

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, such as mushrooms, zucchini, or spinach. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

  • For a lighter version of this dish, substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half or whole milk. The sauce will be slightly less rich but still delicious.

  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved to suit your needs. Just adjust the ingredient quantities accordingly.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop until heated through.

Enjoy the bold and satisfying flavors of this One-Pot Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta. With its spicy kick and convenient one-pot preparation, it’s a recipe that will quickly become a favorite in your kitchen. So grab your pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a delicious and hassle-free meal!
