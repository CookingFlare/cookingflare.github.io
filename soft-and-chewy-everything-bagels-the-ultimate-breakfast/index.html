Soft and Chewy Everything Bagels: The Ultimate Breakfast


There’s something about a fresh, soft, and chewy everything bagel that just screams “ultimate breakfast.” The combination of savory and nutty flavors from the everything seasoning, paired with the satisfying chew of a perfectly baked bagel, makes it a morning staple that’s hard to resist. So, if you’re ready to take your breakfast game to the next level, it’s time to roll up your sleeves and get baking. In this recipe, I’ll walk you through the steps to create your own batch of soft and chewy everything bagels that will rival those from your favorite bakery.

Ingredients

For the Bagels:

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Everything Seasoning:

  • 2 tablespoons poppy seeds
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons dried minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons dried minced onion
  • 1 tablespoon coarse salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Create a well in the center and pour in the warm water and olive oil. Use a wooden spoon to mix the ingredients until a shaggy dough forms.

  2. Knead the Dough: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment for this step.

  3. First Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  4. Shape the Bagels: Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 8 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a smooth ball, then use your thumb to poke a hole through the center, creating the classic bagel shape. Place the shaped bagels on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  5. Second Rise: Cover the shaped bagels with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 20-30 minutes.

  6. Preheat the Oven: While the bagels are rising, preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and bring a large pot of water to a boil.

  7. Boil the Bagels: Once the water is boiling, carefully lower the bagels, 2-3 at a time, into the pot. Boil for 1 minute on each side, then remove them with a slotted spoon and return them to the baking sheet.

  8. Add the Everything Seasoning: In a small bowl, mix together the poppy seeds, sesame seeds, dried minced garlic, dried minced onion, and coarse salt. Sprinkle the seasoning generously over the boiled bagels.

  9. Bake the Bagels: Place the seasoned bagels in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.

  10. Enjoy: Allow the bagels to cool for a few minutes before slicing and toasting them. Serve with your favorite cream cheese or smoked salmon for a truly satisfying breakfast experience.

There you have it - homemade soft and chewy everything bagels that will elevate your breakfast game. With a little time and effort, you can enjoy the delicious aroma and taste of freshly baked bagels right in your own kitchen. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a beginner, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. So, go ahead, give it a try, and savor the ultimate breakfast experience.
