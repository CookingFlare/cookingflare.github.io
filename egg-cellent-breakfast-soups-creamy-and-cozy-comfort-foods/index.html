Egg-cellent Breakfast Soups: Creamy and Cozy Comfort Foods


Bursting with flavor, these breakfast soups are both comforting and nourishing. Made with an array of ingredients, you can quickly whip up a hearty and healthy meal perfect for any day of the week. Let’s dive into some delicious and easy-to-make egg-based breakfast soups.

Creamy Egg Drop Soup

This simple yet satisfying soup combines the flavors of scrambled eggs with a dash of green onion and a hint of soy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped (white and light green parts only)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 3/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring the vegetable or chicken broth to a gentle simmer.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sauté the green onions and mushrooms for about 3 minutes.
  3. Pour the hot broth into the skillet, stirring constantly.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and let simmer for about 2 minutes.
  5. Gradually pour the beaten eggs into the simmering soup, stirring gently to form ribbons.
  6. Remove the soup from the heat and stir in the soy sauce.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. Serve hot with freshlysteamed rice.

Scrambled Egg and Potato Soup

A comforting soup with the flavors of Ireland, scrambled eggs, potatoes, and bacon come together in a perfect medley.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 ounces thick-cut bacon, diced
  • 4 eggs
  • 6 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Salt and ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cover the cubed potatoes with cold water and bring to a boil. Cook for 10-15 minutes or until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Fry the bacon until crispy.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and then add the milk.
  4. In a saucepan, combine the flour and 1/2 cup of the hot milk mixture, stirring until smooth.
  5. Gradually pour the remaining milk mixture into the saucepan, stirring constantly.
  6. Cook the milk mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it starts to thicken. Add the cooked potatoes.
  7. Stir in the crispy bacon and season the soup with salt and pepper. Serve hot.

Tomato and Spinach Egg Frittata Soup

A delectable soup combining the flavors of tomato, spinach, and egg into a delightful frittata.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves, chopped
  • 2 cups canned diced tomatoes
  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute.
  2. Stir in the chopped spinach and canned diced tomatoes, cooking for about 5 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese together.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pot, stirring constantly to combine.
  5. Cook over low heat until the eggs are almost set but still slightly wet in the center. Do not stir.
  6. Remove the soup from the heat and let it sit for a minute or two. The residual heat will finish cooking the eggs.
  7. Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste. Serve hot with crusty bread.

With these delightfully versatile egg-based breakfast soups, you can kick-start your morning with a blast of flavor and nutrition. Perfect for any time of the year, let these soups nourish and invigorate your morning routine. Enjoy!
