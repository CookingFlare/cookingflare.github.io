Quick and Easy: 5 Sandwich Recipes for a Busy Weeknight Dinner


Finding time to prepare a wholesome meal after a long day can be a challenge. That’s where quick and easy sandwich recipes come to the rescue! Not only are sandwiches versatile and delicious, but they can also be a lifesaver on busy weeknights. Here are five mouthwatering sandwich recipes that are perfect for a satisfying weeknight dinner.

1. Caprese Panini

Ingredients:

  • Ciabatta bread
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a panini press or a grill pan.
  2. Slice the ciabatta bread and brush the outer sides with olive oil.
  3. Layer the mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil leaves on the bread.
  4. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Place the sandwiches in the panini press or grill pan and cook until the bread is golden and the cheese is melted.

2. BBQ Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Cooked shredded chicken
  • BBQ sauce
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Coleslaw mix
  • Hamburger buns

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce.
  2. Toast the hamburger buns.
  3. Place the BBQ chicken on the bottom half of the bun.
  4. Top with red onion and coleslaw mix.
  5. Cover with the top half of the bun and serve.

3. Veggie Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat wraps
  • Hummus
  • Cucumber, thinly sliced
  • Bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • Shredded carrots
  • Baby spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. Lay out the whole wheat wraps on a clean surface.
  2. Spread a generous layer of hummus over the wraps.
  3. Arrange the cucumber, bell peppers, shredded carrots, and spinach leaves in the center of the wraps.
  4. Fold the sides of the wrap and roll it tightly.

4. Turkey Pesto Panini

Ingredients:

  • Sourdough bread
  • Sliced turkey
  • Pesto sauce
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Provolone cheese
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Spread pesto on one side of each slice of sourdough bread.
  2. Layer the turkey, sun-dried tomatoes, and provolone cheese between the bread slices.
  3. Spread butter on the outer sides of the sandwich.
  4. Grill the sandwich in a panini press or a grill pan until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown.

5. Tuna Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Canned tuna, drained
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • Celery, finely chopped
  • Red onion, finely chopped
  • Lettuce
  • Whole grain bread

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the canned tuna with mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, celery, and red onion.
  2. Toast the whole grain bread, if desired.
  3. Place the tuna salad on a slice of bread and top with lettuce.
  4. Cover with another slice of bread and enjoy!

These quick and easy sandwich recipes are not only a time-saver but also a delicious way to enjoy a satisfying meal on a busy weeknight. Whether you’re in the mood for a warm and toasty panini or a refreshing veggie wrap, these recipes have got you covered. So, the next time you’re short on time but still want a fantastic dinner, consider whipping up one of these delightful sandwiches. Happy cooking!
