Vegan Cinnamon Rolls: Soft, Sweet, and Gooey


When you crave a warm, comforting treat that fills your kitchen with irresistible aromas, vegan cinnamon rolls are the answer. These soft, sweet, and gooey rolls are everything you want in a dessert or breakfast option. Whether you are vegan or simply looking for a plant-based alternative to traditional cinnamon rolls, this recipe offers a delightful twist without compromising on flavor or texture. Imagine biting into a tender, perfectly swirled roll filled with a cinnamon-sugar mixture, all topped with a luscious vegan glaze.

Here’s how to make these heavenly cinnamon rolls from scratch, ensuring every bite is a melt-in-your-mouth experience.

Ingredients for Vegan Cinnamon Rolls

Before diving into the step-by-step instructions, it’s important to gather the necessary ingredients for these cinnamon rolls. The following list contains ingredients that are both easy to find and essential for making the perfect batch of vegan cinnamon rolls.

For the dough:

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour: This forms the structure of the cinnamon rolls. You can substitute with whole wheat flour for a denser, heartier texture, though the rolls will be a little less soft.
  • 1 packet (or 2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast: This is key for making the dough rise and achieving that light, fluffy texture.
  • ½ cup warm almond milk (or any plant-based milk): You need the milk to be warm but not hot to activate the yeast.
  • ¼ cup melted coconut oil: This helps keep the rolls moist and tender. If you prefer, you can use olive oil or vegetable oil as an alternative.
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar: This provides a touch of sweetness to balance the cinnamon filling.
  • 1 teaspoon salt: A pinch of salt enhances the flavor and balances the sweetness.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract: For a subtle flavor boost.
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar: This reacts with the baking soda to help make the dough soft and tender.

For the cinnamon filling:

  • ½ cup light brown sugar: Brown sugar gives the filling a warm, molasses-like flavor that complements the cinnamon.
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon: The star of the filling, providing the signature warm, spicy flavor that cinnamon rolls are known for.
  • ¼ cup melted coconut oil: This helps bind the cinnamon-sugar mixture and ensures a smooth spread.
  • Pinch of salt: Balances the sweetness of the filling and enhances the flavor.

For the vegan glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar: This creates the perfect glaze that’s sweet and smooth, adding the finishing touch to your cinnamon rolls.
  • 1 tablespoon almond milk: This thins the powdered sugar and helps achieve a smooth, pourable consistency.
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract: A bit of vanilla enhances the glaze and complements the cinnamon flavor.

How to Make Vegan Cinnamon Rolls: Step-by-Step Guide

Making these vegan cinnamon rolls requires a bit of patience, but the process is simple, and the results are absolutely worth the effort. Here’s a detailed guide to help you make these delicious rolls from scratch.

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

Start by activating the yeast. In a small bowl, combine the warm almond milk and granulated sugar, then sprinkle the active dry yeast over the top. Stir gently and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes, or until the mixture becomes foamy. This shows that the yeast is active and ready to work its magic.

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Once the yeast mixture is foamy, pour it into the flour mixture, along with the melted coconut oil, apple cider vinegar, and vanilla extract. Mix the ingredients until a dough forms.

Now, transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, add a little more flour, one tablespoon at a time. If it’s too dry, add a small amount of warm water.

Once the dough is kneaded, place it into a lightly oiled bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Let the dough rise for about 1-1.5 hours, or until it has doubled in size. This is the time when your kitchen will start to smell amazing!

Step 2: Roll Out the Dough

After the dough has risen, punch it down to release any trapped air bubbles. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and roll it out into a large rectangle, about 16 inches long and 12 inches wide. Try to keep the edges even so that the cinnamon rolls cook uniformly.

Step 3: Make the Cinnamon-Sugar Filling

In a medium bowl, mix the brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Next, spread the melted coconut oil evenly over the surface of the dough. Once the dough is coated, sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly across the entire surface, leaving a small border along the edges.

Step 4: Shape the Cinnamon Rolls

Carefully roll the dough into a tight log starting from one of the longer edges. Make sure the roll is tight enough so that the rolls don’t unravel while baking, but not so tight that it’s difficult to cut.

Once the dough is rolled, use a sharp knife or dental floss to slice it into 12 equal pieces. If you prefer larger or smaller rolls, adjust the number of pieces accordingly.

Step 5: Arrange and Rise Again

Place the cinnamon rolls in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish, ensuring there is some space between each roll. Cover the pan with a kitchen towel and let the rolls rise again for about 30-45 minutes, or until they have puffed up and are touching each other.

Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) while the rolls are rising. This ensures that the rolls will bake evenly.

Step 6: Bake the Cinnamon Rolls

After the second rise, place the cinnamon rolls in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Keep an eye on them towards the end, as ovens can vary in temperature.

Once the rolls are baked, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool for about 10 minutes before glazing.

Step 7: Make the Vegan Glaze

While the cinnamon rolls are cooling, whisk together the powdered sugar, almond milk, and vanilla extract in a small bowl until smooth. If you prefer a thicker glaze, add more powdered sugar. If you like it thinner, add a little more almond milk.

Drizzle the glaze generously over the warm cinnamon rolls, allowing it to melt into the cracks and crevices. The glaze will firm up slightly as it cools, leaving you with a sweet, glossy finish.

Step 8: Serve and Enjoy!

Once glazed, your vegan cinnamon rolls are ready to be served. These rolls are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, but they can also be stored in an airtight container for up to 3 days. Simply reheat them in the microwave or oven before serving.

Tips for Perfect Vegan Cinnamon Rolls

  • Ensure your yeast is active: It’s important to make sure your yeast is fresh and activated before proceeding. If the yeast doesn’t foam after mixing with the warm milk and sugar, it may be expired, and you’ll need to start with fresh yeast.
  • Customize the filling: You can add raisins, chopped nuts, or even a sprinkle of cocoa powder to the cinnamon-sugar filling for extra flavor and texture.
  • Use a serrated knife: When slicing the rolled dough, a serrated knife works best to prevent squashing the rolls.
  • Make ahead option: You can prepare the dough and filling the night before, then refrigerate it overnight. In the morning, just let the rolls come to room temperature, let them rise for about 30 minutes, and bake as directed.

Why These Vegan Cinnamon Rolls Are Special

Vegan cinnamon rolls are not just an ethical choice—they’re also incredibly delicious. By replacing dairy and eggs with plant-based ingredients, you end up with a lighter, slightly more delicate dough. The coconut oil provides a hint of richness without being overpowering, and the cinnamon-sugar filling is just as indulgent as the traditional version. The addition of a sweet, creamy glaze ties everything together, creating a mouthwatering treat that will satisfy your sweet tooth.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a first-timer, these vegan cinnamon rolls are sure to impress. The best part? They are completely customizable, so you can experiment with different fillings, glazes, and toppings to make them your own.

These soft, sweet, and gooey vegan cinnamon rolls are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or any special occasion. The next time you’re looking to impress your friends or indulge yourself with a decadent treat, look no further than this recipe.

Happy baking, and enjoy every delicious bite!
