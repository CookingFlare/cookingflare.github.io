Korean Soy Garlic Chicken Wings: A Finger-Licking Goodness


If you’re a fan of finger-licking chicken wings, then you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m going to share with you a delicious and flavorful recipe for Korean Soy Garlic Chicken Wings. These wings are packed with a perfect balance of savory, sweet, and garlicky flavors that will have you coming back for more.

Ingredients

To make these mouthwatering Korean Soy Garlic Chicken Wings, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped green onions, for garnish
  • Toasted sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the soy sauce, honey, brown sugar, minced garlic, sesame oil, rice vinegar, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using). Mix well to combine all the flavors.

  2. Add the chicken wings to the marinade and toss until they are evenly coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the wings marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight if possible. This will allow the flavors to penetrate the meat and make the wings even more delicious.

  3. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Set aside.

  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to 350°F (175°C). If you don’t have a deep fryer, you can use a deep skillet with enough oil to fully submerge the wings.

  5. Remove the chicken wings from the marinade, shaking off any excess. Season the wings with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Carefully place the wings in the hot oil, frying them in batches to avoid overcrowding the pot. Fry the wings for about 8-10 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Make sure to flip them halfway through to ensure even cooking.

  7. Once the wings are cooked, transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to remove any excess oil.

  8. In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.

  9. Pour the marinade into the skillet and bring it to a simmer. Cook for about 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  10. Add the fried chicken wings to the skillet and toss them in the sauce until they are fully coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the wings to absorb the flavors of the sauce.

  11. Remove the wings from the skillet and transfer them to a serving platter. Garnish with chopped green onions and toasted sesame seeds for an extra burst of flavor and visual appeal.

  12. Serve the Korean Soy Garlic Chicken Wings hot and enjoy the finger-licking goodness! These wings make a perfect appetizer for parties or a delicious main dish for a casual dinner.

Conclusion

There you have it – a recipe for Korean Soy Garlic Chicken Wings that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy! The combination of soy sauce, honey, garlic, and other aromatic ingredients creates a harmonious blend of flavors that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a delightful snack, these wings are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, gather your ingredients, get cooking, and prepare yourself for a finger-licking good time!
