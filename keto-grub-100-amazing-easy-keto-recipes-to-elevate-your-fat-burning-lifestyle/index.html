Keto Grub: 100 Amazing, Easy Keto Recipes to Elevate Your Fat-Burning Lifestyle


Are you looking to revamp your diet and increase the amount of fat you’re burning? Then you’ve come to the right place! In this blog post, we’re diving headfirst into the world of Keto Grub - a collection of 100 amazing, easy keto recipes to elevate your fat-burning lifestyle. Whether you’re a seasoned Keto veteran or just starting your low-carb journey, these recipes will have your taste buds dancing while helping you reach your health goals.

Without further ado, let’s jump right into these delectable dishes!

  1. Cheesy Avocado Crack Dip

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup sliced green onion
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Set your oven to broil on low heat.

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients and mix until smooth.

  3. Scoop the mixture onto a baking sheet and spread into a single layer.

  4. Broil for 5-7 minutes or until the cheese is melted and slightly browned.

  5. Serve immediately with sliced veggies for dipping.

  6. Bacon Cheddar Deviled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 6 slices of bacon, crisply cooked and crumbled
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Slice hard-boiled eggs in half and carefully remove the yolks into a mixing bowl.

  2. Add mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, shredded cheddar cheese, crumbled bacon, salt, and pepper into the bowl with the egg yolks.

  3. Mix until smooth and fully combined.

  4. Use a spoon to scoop the egg yolk mixture back into the egg whites, dividing it evenly.

  5. Sprinkle the top of each deviled egg with the reserved shredded cheddar cheese and chopped bacon, then bake at 350°F for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and slightly browned.

  6. Cucumber Kyoto Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 small English cucumber, sliced
  • 6 cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • 6 small mozzarella balls, halved
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.

  2. Grill the halved mozzarella balls until slightly charred on both sides, then set aside.

  3. To assemble the salad, layer cucumber slices, cherry tomatoes, and grilled mozzarella balls on a serving platter.

  4. Drizzle with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then top with fresh basil slices.

  5. Coconut Flour Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 3 tablespoons swerve or erythritol sweetener
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup coconut flour
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips or Lily’s chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the melted coconut oil, sweetener, and vanilla extract. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients, including the coconut flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Slowly incorporate the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, stirring until fully combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Using a cookie scoop or spoon, portion out the dough into individual cookies and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden brown.
  7. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

As you can see, the Keto diet doesn’t mean compromising flavor. Our collection above brings a mix of classic and innovative dishes, all while keeping you within your low-carb limits. Make your next meal a winning experience by diving into the world of Keto Grub and making one of these fantastic and easy recipes today!
