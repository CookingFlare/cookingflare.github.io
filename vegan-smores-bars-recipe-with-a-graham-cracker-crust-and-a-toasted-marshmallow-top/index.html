Vegan S'mores Bars Recipe with a Graham Cracker Crust and a Toasted Marshmallow Top


S’mores are a classic campfire treat loved by many. But what if you’re vegan and can’t enjoy the traditional version? Well, fear not! I have the perfect solution for you – Vegan S’mores Bars with a delicious Graham Cracker Crust and a Toasted Marshmallow Top. These bars are not only cruelty-free but also incredibly easy to make. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the Graham Cracker Crust:

  • 2 cups vegan graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted vegan butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the Marshmallow Top:

  • 1 cup aquafaba (the liquid from a can of chickpeas)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Filling:

  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Graham Cracker Crust

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the vegan graham cracker crumbs, melted vegan butter, and granulated sugar. Mix well until the mixture resembles wet sand.
  3. Press the crust mixture into the bottom of a greased 9x9-inch baking dish, using the back of a spoon to create an even layer.
  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

Step 2: Make the Marshmallow Top

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the aquafaba, granulated sugar, and cream of tartar. Using an electric mixer, beat the mixture on high speed until stiff peaks form. This usually takes about 10-15 minutes.
  2. Once the aquafaba mixture is thick and glossy, gently fold in the vanilla extract.
  3. Spread the marshmallow mixture evenly over the cooled graham cracker crust, using a spatula to create peaks and swirls.

Step 3: Toast the Marshmallow Top

  1. Preheat your oven’s broiler to high.
  2. Place the baking dish with the marshmallow top under the broiler, about 6 inches away from the heat source.
  3. Watch closely as the marshmallow top begins to brown and bubble. This should take only a few minutes, so be careful not to let it burn.
  4. Once the marshmallow top is golden brown and toasted to your liking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

Step 4: Prepare the Chocolate Filling

  1. In a heatproof bowl, combine the vegan chocolate chips and coconut cream.
  2. Microwave the mixture in 30-second intervals, stirring well after each interval, until the chocolate is melted and smooth. Alternatively, you can melt the chocolate in a double boiler.
  3. Pour the melted chocolate mixture over the toasted marshmallow top, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

Step 5: Chill and Serve

  1. Place the baking dish in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until the chocolate filling is set.
  2. Once the bars are chilled and set, cut them into squares or rectangles using a sharp knife.
  3. Serve the Vegan S’mores Bars immediately and enjoy!

Conclusion

These Vegan S’mores Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust and a Toasted Marshmallow Top are the perfect sweet treat for any occasion. Whether you’re vegan or not, you won’t be able to resist these gooey, chocolatey, and delicious bars. So, gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and indulge in this delightful vegan twist on a classic favorite. Happy baking!
