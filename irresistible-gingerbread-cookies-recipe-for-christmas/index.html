Irresistible Gingerbread Cookies Recipe for Christmas


Are you looking for the perfect treat to delight your taste buds this Christmas season? Look no further! Our irresistible gingerbread cookies are just what you need to spread holiday cheer. These cookies are soft, chewy, and full of warm, spicy flavors that will transport you to a winter wonderland.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add the molasses, egg, and vanilla extract, and mix until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a soft dough forms. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Divide the dough into two equal portions and shape each portion into a disc. Wrap them in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  6. Once chilled, remove the dough from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for 10 minutes to soften slightly.

  7. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disc of dough to a thickness of about 1/4 inch. Use cookie cutters of your choice to cut out shapes from the dough. Transfer the cutouts to the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 1 inch of space between each cookie.

  8. Gather the scraps of dough and repeat the rolling and cutting process until all the dough is used.

  9. Bake the cookies for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are set and lightly golden. The centers may still be slightly soft, but they will firm up as they cool.

  10. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  11. Once cooled, you can decorate the cookies with royal icing, sprinkles, or simply enjoy them as they are.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of sweetness, you can dust the cookies with powdered sugar before serving.
  • If you prefer a spicier flavor, you can increase the amount of ground ginger or cinnamon in the recipe.
  • These gingerbread cookies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week.

Conclusion

Now that you have this irresistible gingerbread cookies recipe, you can make your Christmas celebrations even more special. Whether you’re baking them for yourself, your family, or to give as gifts, these delicious cookies are sure to bring joy and warmth to everyone’s hearts. So, put on your apron, preheat the oven, and let the aroma of freshly baked gingerbread fill your home this holiday season. Happy baking and Merry Christmas!
