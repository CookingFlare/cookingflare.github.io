One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp


If you’re a seafood lover, then this recipe is a must-try! One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp is a delicious and satisfying dish that brings together the flavors of garlic, parmesan, and succulent shrimp in a creamy sauce. The best part? It’s all cooked in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze. So, let’s dive right into this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. In the same skillet, pour in the chicken broth, heavy cream, dried oregano, and dried basil. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Stir well to combine.

  4. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Gradually whisk in the grated parmesan cheese, stirring constantly until it’s fully melted and the sauce becomes thick and creamy.

  6. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss them gently in the sauce, ensuring they are evenly coated. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to warm the shrimp through.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish with fresh parsley for an added burst of freshness.

  8. Serve the One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp immediately with your favorite pasta, rice, or crusty bread. The creamy sauce pairs perfectly with any of these options, allowing you to savor every delectable bite.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicy kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the sauce while it’s simmering.
  • For a healthier twist, substitute the heavy cream with Greek yogurt or a lighter cream alternative.
  • Feel free to add some veggies to the dish for extra color and flavor. Sautéed spinach, cherry tomatoes, or roasted bell peppers would be great additions.
  • If you’re not a fan of shrimp, you can easily swap it out for chicken or even scallops. Adjust the cooking time accordingly to ensure they are fully cooked through.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop or in the microwave before serving.

Final Thoughts

One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp is a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that combines the richness of garlic, the creaminess of parmesan, and the succulence of shrimp. With just a few simple ingredients and one pot, you can create a flavorful and satisfying meal that will leave everyone wanting more. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab your skillet and give it a try – you won’t be disappointed!
