Dairy-Free Chocolate Pudding Pie: A Rich and Indulgent Dessert


Indulge your taste buds with this decadent Dairy-Free Chocolate Pudding Pie recipe. Perfect for those with lactose intolerance or anyone looking to enjoy a rich and creamy dessert without the dairy, this pie is a must-try treat. With a smooth chocolate filling and a crispy crust, it’s a dessert that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of chocolate sandwich cookie crumbs (about 18 cookies)
  • 6 tablespoons of melted dairy-free butter

For the filling:

  • ⅔ cup of granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • ¼ cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt
  • 3 cups of unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup of dairy-free semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the topping:

  • ½ cup of dairy-free whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the chocolate sandwich cookie crumbs and melted dairy-free butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated.

  3. Press the cookie mixture into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie dish, forming an even crust. Place the crust in the preheated oven and bake for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  4. In a saucepan, whisk together the granulated sugar, cornstarch, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually whisk in the almond milk until the mixture is smooth.

  5. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. Continue cooking and stirring for an additional 2 minutes. Remove from heat.

  6. Add the dairy-free chocolate chips to the hot pudding mixture and stir until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  7. Pour the chocolate filling into the cooled pie crust, spreading it evenly. Smooth the top with a spatula.

  8. Cover the pie with plastic wrap, ensuring that it touches the surface of the pudding to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until set.

  9. Once the pie is set, remove it from the refrigerator. If desired, top with dairy-free whipped cream and chocolate shavings for an extra touch of indulgence.

  10. Slice and serve chilled. Enjoy the creamy and luscious Dairy-Free Chocolate Pudding Pie!

Tips and Variations

  • For a gluten-free option, use gluten-free chocolate sandwich cookies for the crust.
  • You can substitute other non-dairy milks, such as soy milk or coconut milk, for the almond milk.
  • Feel free to experiment with different flavors by adding a teaspoon of instant coffee or a tablespoon of almond extract to the filling mixture.
  • If you prefer a firmer texture, increase the amount of cornstarch to ⅓ cup.
  • This pie can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Now you have a delicious and dairy-free dessert option to satisfy your chocolate cravings. This Dairy-Free Chocolate Pudding Pie is a delightful treat that will leave you wanting more. Give it a try and let your taste buds indulge in its rich and creamy goodness. Enjoy!
