Refreshing and Citrusy Lime Cilantro Rice


If you’re looking for a side dish that adds a burst of refreshing flavors to your meal, look no further than this delicious lime cilantro rice recipe. With its zesty citrus notes and fragrant cilantro, this rice dish is the perfect accompaniment to any main course. Whether you’re serving it alongside grilled fish, roasted chicken, or even as a standalone vegetarian option, this lime cilantro rice is sure to impress your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro leaves

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly under cold water to remove excess starch. This will help to ensure that the rice cooks up light and fluffy.

  2. In a medium-sized saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the rinsed rice and stir it around for a minute or two until it becomes slightly toasted and fragrant.

  3. Pour in the water and add the salt. Give everything a good stir, then bring the mixture to a boil.

  4. Once the water is boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the saucepan with a tight-fitting lid. Allow the rice to simmer gently for about 15-20 minutes, or until all the water has been absorbed and the rice is tender.

  5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let it sit, covered, for an additional 5 minutes. This will allow the rice to steam and fluff up even more.

  6. While the rice is resting, squeeze the juice of two limes into a small bowl. Add the lime zest and chopped cilantro, then stir everything together to combine.

  7. Once the rice has finished resting, fluff it up with a fork to loosen the grains. Pour the lime cilantro mixture over the rice and gently fold it in until well incorporated.

  8. Taste the rice and adjust the seasoning if necessary. You can add a pinch of salt or a squeeze of lime juice to brighten up the flavors even more.

  9. Serve the lime cilantro rice as a side dish or as a bed for your favorite protein. It pairs beautifully with grilled shrimp, chicken, or roasted vegetables.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a finely chopped jalapeno pepper to the lime cilantro mixture.
  • If you prefer a more pronounced cilantro flavor, increase the amount of fresh cilantro leaves to 1/2 cup.
  • To make this recipe vegan-friendly, use vegetable broth instead of water.
  • Leftover lime cilantro rice can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. It can be reheated in the microwave or on the stovetop with a little bit of water to prevent it from drying out.

With its bright and vibrant flavors, this lime cilantro rice is a versatile and refreshing side dish that will elevate any meal. The tangy lime juice and fragrant cilantro add a burst of freshness that perfectly complements a wide range of main courses. Whether you’re planning a casual weeknight dinner or a special gathering with friends and family, this recipe is sure to impress. So why not give it a try and add some zing to your next meal?
