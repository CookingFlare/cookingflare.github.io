The Classic Old Fashioned Donuts: A Boozy Breakfast Treat


There’s something undeniably comforting about sinking your teeth into a perfectly crafted old fashioned donut. The crisp, golden exterior gives way to a soft, cake-like interior, and the flavor is a delightful mix of sweetness and subtle hints of nutmeg and vanilla. Now, imagine taking that classic treat to the next level by infusing it with a touch of bourbon. That’s right – we’re talking about boozy old fashioned donuts. These delectable treats are a perfect blend of sophistication and indulgence, making them an ideal addition to any brunch or breakfast spread.

Ingredients

For the donuts:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons bourbon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • Oil for frying

For the glaze:

  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup bourbon
  • 2 tablespoons whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, nutmeg, and salt.

  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, then add the sour cream, milk, bourbon, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, then add the melted butter. Stir until just combined, being careful not to overmix.

  4. Roll the dough out on a floured surface to about 1/2 inch thickness. Use a donut cutter to cut out the donuts, then use a smaller cutter to punch out the holes in the center.

  5. Heat the oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pot to 375°F. Carefully add the donuts to the hot oil, frying in batches for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. Transfer to a wire rack to drain and cool.

  6. While the donuts are cooling, prepare the glaze. In a medium bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, bourbon, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  7. Once the donuts have cooled slightly, dip each one into the glaze, allowing any excess to drip off. Place the glazed donuts back on the wire rack to set.

  8. Serve and enjoy!

The Perfect Pairing

These boozy old fashioned donuts are a delightful treat on their own, but they pair exceptionally well with a cup of freshly brewed coffee or a glass of cold milk. The subtle warmth of the bourbon complements the sweetness of the donuts, creating a harmonious flavor experience.

The Final Word

Whether you’re hosting a brunch with friends or simply want to elevate your breakfast routine, these boozy old fashioned donuts are sure to impress. The infusion of bourbon adds a sophisticated twist to a beloved classic, making them a unique and memorable addition to any morning spread.

So, why not add a touch of indulgence to your breakfast table and whip up a batch of these delightful treats? Your taste buds will thank you, and your guests will be begging for the recipe. Cheers to boozy breakfast delights!
