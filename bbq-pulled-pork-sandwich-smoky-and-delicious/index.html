BBQ Pulled Pork Sandwich: Smoky and Delicious


If you’re a fan of tender, succulent meat smothered in smoky, tangy barbecue sauce and sandwiched between soft, pillowy buns, then this BBQ pulled pork sandwich recipe is just for you. Whether you’re hosting a summer BBQ, planning a family picnic, or simply craving a hearty and satisfying meal, this classic dish is sure to hit the spot. With a few simple ingredients and a bit of patience, you can create a mouthwatering pulled pork sandwich that will have everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 pounds pork shoulder or pork butt
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 1/2 cups barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hamburger buns or sandwich rolls

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pork

Begin by seasoning the pork shoulder with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and chili powder. Place the chopped onions and minced garlic in the bottom of a slow cooker and lay the seasoned pork on top. Pour the broth over the pork, cover, and cook on low for 8-10 hours or until the meat is tender and easily falls apart with a fork.

Step 2: Shred the Pork

Once the pork is cooked, carefully transfer it to a large cutting board or dish. Using two forks, shred the meat into bite-sized pieces, discarding any large pieces of fat.

Step 3: Make the Barbecue Sauce

In a saucepan, combine the barbecue sauce, brown sugar, cumin, and a pinch of salt. Warm the sauce over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it’s heated through.

Step 4: Combine the Pork and Sauce

Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and pour the warm barbecue sauce over the meat. Gently stir to ensure the pork is evenly coated with the sauce. Cover and let it cook for an additional 30 minutes on low to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 5: Assemble the Sandwiches

To assemble the sandwiches, lightly toast the buns or rolls to add a bit of texture and warmth. Spoon a generous portion of the saucy pulled pork onto the bottom half of each bun. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.

Serving Suggestions

This smoky and delicious BBQ pulled pork sandwich pairs perfectly with classic picnic sides such as coleslaw, potato salad, or corn on the cob. For a refreshing contrast to the rich and savory flavors of the sandwich, consider serving it with a crisp, tangy cucumber salad or a light, fresh fruit salad.

Final Thoughts

There’s something undeniably comforting about the aroma of slow-cooked pork infused with the flavors of smoky barbecue sauce. This BBQ pulled pork sandwich recipe is a celebration of simple, wholesome ingredients and the joy of sharing a satisfying meal with friends and family. So, fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and savor every bite of this smoky and delicious creation. Cheers to good food and even better company!
