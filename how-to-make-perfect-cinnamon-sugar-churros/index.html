How to Make Perfect Cinnamon Sugar Churros


Churros are a beloved treat that have taken the culinary world by storm. With their crispy exterior and soft, doughy inside, churros are the perfect combination of textures. Coated in a generous layer of cinnamon sugar, they are often served with a warm cup of chocolate sauce for dipping. Whether you’re enjoying them at a fair, a street vendor, or in the comfort of your own kitchen, nothing beats the satisfaction of biting into a freshly made churro. In this post, we’re going to explore how to make perfect cinnamon sugar churros that will impress your friends and family.

The History of Churros

Before diving into the recipe, let’s take a brief look at the history of churros. Originating from Spain, churros have a long-standing tradition in Spanish and Latin American cuisine. They were initially made by shepherds who needed a simple, portable snack that could be cooked over an open fire. The dough is traditionally piped through a star-shaped nozzle, giving churros their distinct ridged appearance.

Over the years, churros have evolved, and different variations have emerged, such as filled churros and variations topped with chocolate or caramel sauces. However, the classic cinnamon sugar churro remains a favorite among many, and for good reason.

Ingredients for Perfect Cinnamon Sugar Churros

To create the perfect cinnamon sugar churros, you’ll need the following ingredients:

For the churro dough:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (plus more for frying)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg

For the cinnamon sugar coating:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

For the dipping sauce (optional):

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Equipment Needed

Before we start cooking, gather the following equipment to make the process smoother:

  • A medium saucepan
  • A whisk or wooden spoon
  • A piping bag fitted with a star-shaped nozzle
  • A deep frying pan or Dutch oven
  • A candy thermometer (optional but recommended)
  • A large plate or baking sheet lined with paper towels
  • A shallow dish for the cinnamon sugar coating

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Cinnamon Sugar Churros

Step 1: Prepare the Churro Dough

  1. Heat the Ingredients: In a medium saucepan, combine the water, granulated sugar, salt, and vegetable oil. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat.

  2. Add the Flour: Once the mixture reaches a rolling boil, remove it from the heat and quickly stir in the all-purpose flour using a wooden spoon or whisk. Mix until the dough comes together and pulls away from the sides of the pan.

  3. Incorporate Vanilla and Egg: Allow the dough to cool for a few minutes, then add the vanilla extract. Crack the egg into the dough and mix well until the egg is fully incorporated and the dough is smooth.

Step 2: Prepare the Cinnamon Sugar Coating

  1. Mix Sugar and Cinnamon: In a shallow dish, combine the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon. Set aside for later use.

Step 3: Heat the Oil for Frying

  1. Heat the Oil: In a deep frying pan or Dutch oven, pour in about 2 inches of vegetable oil. Heat the oil to 350°F (175°C). If you have a candy thermometer, use it to ensure the oil is at the right temperature for frying.

Step 4: Pipe the Churros

  1. Transfer the Dough: Once the oil is heated, transfer the churro dough to a piping bag fitted with a star-shaped nozzle.

  2. Pipe the Churros: Carefully pipe strips of dough into the hot oil, cutting them to your desired length (typically about 6 inches). Avoid overcrowding the pan; cook in batches if necessary.

Step 5: Fry the Churros

  1. Fry Until Golden Brown: Fry the churros for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until they are golden brown and crispy. Use tongs to turn them over halfway through the cooking process.

  2. Drain Excess Oil: Once cooked, remove the churros from the oil and place them on a plate or baking sheet lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

Step 6: Coat the Churros

  1. Coat in Cinnamon Sugar: While the churros are still warm, roll them in the cinnamon sugar mixture until they are fully coated. This step adds that signature sweetness and flavor that makes churros so irresistible.

Step 7: Prepare the Dipping Sauce (Optional)

  1. Heat the Cream: In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it is just about to boil.

  2. Melt the Chocolate: Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the semi-sweet chocolate chips. Let it sit for a minute, then stir until the chocolate is fully melted and smooth. Add the vanilla extract for extra flavor.

  3. Serve Warm: Pour the chocolate sauce into a small bowl for dipping.

Serving Suggestions

Now that your perfect cinnamon sugar churros are ready, it’s time to serve them! Arrange the churros on a serving platter and place the chocolate dipping sauce alongside. You can also serve them with a dusting of powdered sugar for an elegant touch. These churros are best enjoyed fresh, but if you have leftovers, store them in an airtight container to maintain their crispiness.

Tips for Perfect Churros Every Time

  • Don’t Overcrowd the Pan: Fry churros in small batches to ensure they cook evenly and maintain their shape.
  • Control the Oil Temperature: If the oil temperature is too low, the churros will absorb excess oil and become greasy. If it’s too high, they may burn before cooking through.
  • Experiment with Flavors: While cinnamon sugar is a classic coating, consider experimenting with other flavors, such as cocoa powder for chocolate churros or adding a pinch of nutmeg for a unique twist.

Conclusion

Making perfect cinnamon sugar churros at home is a rewarding experience that brings the delightful taste of this classic treat right to your kitchen. With the right ingredients and a bit of patience, you can create churros that are crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside, and absolutely delicious. Whether enjoyed on their own or paired with a rich chocolate dipping sauce, these churros are sure to become a favorite in your household. So gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and indulge in the sweet satisfaction of homemade churros. Happy cooking!
