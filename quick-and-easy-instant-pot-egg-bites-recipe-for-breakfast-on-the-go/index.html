Quick and Easy Instant Pot Egg Bites Recipe for Breakfast On-the-Go


Are you always rushing in the morning, trying to squeeze in a healthy breakfast before heading out the door? Look no further, because I have the perfect solution for you - Quick and Easy Instant Pot Egg Bites! These fluffy and delicious bites are not only packed with protein, but they can also be prepared in advance, making them the ideal on-the-go breakfast option.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your favorite cheese)
  • 1/4 cup diced vegetables (bell peppers, spinach, mushrooms, or any veggies you like)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until well beaten. Add in the milk and continue whisking until fully combined.

  2. Stir in the shredded cheese and diced vegetables. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.

  3. Lightly grease the silicone egg bite mold or individual silicone muffin cups with cooking spray. This will prevent the egg bites from sticking.

  4. Pour the egg mixture evenly into the prepared mold or cups, filling each one about 3/4 full. Make sure to leave some space for the egg bites to expand during cooking.

  5. Place the trivet into the inner pot of your Instant Pot and add 1 cup of water. Carefully place the filled egg bite mold or cups on top of the trivet.

  6. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Steam” function and set the cooking time to 8 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for about 5 minutes. Then, carefully switch the valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Remove the lid and carefully take out the egg bite mold or cups using oven mitts or tongs. Let them cool for a few minutes before removing the egg bites from the mold or cups.

  9. Serve the Instant Pot Egg Bites warm or let them cool completely before storing them in an airtight container in the refrigerator. They can be reheated in the microwave for a quick and convenient breakfast on busy mornings.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize your Instant Pot Egg Bites by adding different ingredients such as cooked bacon, diced ham, or even a sprinkle of herbs like chives or parsley.
  • If you don’t have an Instant Pot, you can still make these delicious egg bites using a regular oven. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake the egg bites for about 15-20 minutes or until they are set in the center.
  • These egg bites can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 5 days, making them perfect for meal prep. Simply reheat them in the microwave for a quick and nutritious breakfast.

So there you have it - a Quick and Easy Instant Pot Egg Bites Recipe that will revolutionize your breakfast routine. With just a few simple ingredients and the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can enjoy a delicious and filling meal on-the-go. Give it a try and let me know how you like it!
