Grilled Eggplant Parmesan: A Vegetarian Twist on a Classic


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, chances are you’ve had Eggplant Parmesan at least once in your life. This classic dish features breaded and fried eggplant slices layered with tomato sauce and melted cheese, resulting in a rich and flavorful meal. However, for those looking to explore vegetarian options or simply reduce their meat consumption, I present to you a delicious twist on this traditional recipe: Grilled Eggplant Parmesan.

The Beauty of Grilled Eggplant

Grilling the eggplant slices instead of frying them not only reduces the calorie and fat content of the dish but also adds a delightful smoky flavor. The grill marks give the eggplant a visually appealing touch, making it a perfect dish to showcase at your next summer gathering or dinner party.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 2 cups of your favorite tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. Slice the eggplants into 1/2-inch thick rounds. Sprinkle salt on both sides of the slices and let them sit for about 15 minutes. This will help draw out excess moisture and bitterness from the eggplants. Pat them dry with a paper towel before grilling.

  3. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic and olive oil. Brush both sides of the eggplant slices with the garlic-infused olive oil. This will add a subtle garlic flavor to the dish.

  4. Place the eggplant slices on the preheated grill and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they are tender and have grill marks. Be sure to keep an eye on them to prevent burning.

  5. Remove the grilled eggplant slices from the grill and let them cool slightly. Meanwhile, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom. Arrange a layer of grilled eggplant slices on top, followed by another layer of tomato sauce, a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella cheese, and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese. Repeat this process until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of cheese on top.

  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for another 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Once the Grilled Eggplant Parmesan is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle fresh basil on top for a burst of freshness and aroma.

  9. Serve the Grilled Eggplant Parmesan hot, alongside a fresh green salad and some crusty bread. Enjoy the combination of smoky grilled eggplant, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese.

Conclusion

Grilled Eggplant Parmesan offers a vegetarian twist on a beloved classic. By grilling the eggplant slices instead of frying them, you not only create a healthier version of the dish but also add a delightful smoky flavor. The combination of tender eggplant, tangy tomato sauce, and melty cheese will leave you craving for more. So next time you’re in the mood for a comforting Italian meal, give this recipe a try and impress your friends and family with this flavorful twist on Eggplant Parmesan.
