5 Dairy-Free Pasta Recipes for Quick Weeknight Dinners


When it comes to preparing quick weeknight dinners, pasta is often a go-to choice for many households. It’s versatile, easy to cook, and can be paired with a variety of flavors. For those looking to avoid dairy, whether due to dietary restrictions or personal preferences, there are plenty of delicious options that don’t compromise on taste. In this blog post, we’ll explore five mouthwatering dairy-free pasta recipes that are perfect for busy weeknights. From creamy sauces to zesty flavors, these dishes will have your family coming back for seconds!

1. Creamy Avocado Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces of your favorite pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne)
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Begin by boiling a large pot of salted water. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the rest and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce: In a blender or food processor, combine the avocados, minced garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil. Blend until smooth. If the mixture is too thick, gradually add reserved pasta water until you reach your desired consistency.

  3. Combine: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta with the avocado sauce. Toss until the pasta is evenly coated. Add salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Garnish: Fold in the fresh basil and cherry tomatoes if using. Serve immediately and enjoy the creamy goodness!

Why You’ll Love It:

This creamy avocado pasta is not only dairy-free but also packed with healthy fats and nutrients. The vibrant flavors make it a favorite among both kids and adults, and it can be prepared in less than 30 minutes!

2. Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces of linguine or spaghetti
  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of the cooking water before draining.

  2. Sauté Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink, about 2-3 minutes per side.

  3. Add Flavor: Stir in the lemon zest, lemon juice, and red pepper flakes. Combine the shrimp with the drained pasta, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a light sauce.

  4. Season and Serve: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Why You’ll Love It:

This lemon garlic shrimp pasta is a light and flavorful dish that comes together in a flash. The zing from the lemon and the kick from the red pepper flakes elevate the taste, making it a delightful weeknight meal.

3. Spinach and Tomato Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces of pasta (fusilli or penne work well)
  • 2 cups of fresh spinach
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of walnuts or pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup of nutritional yeast (for a cheesy flavor)
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil a pot of salted water and cook the pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Pesto: In a food processor, combine spinach, cherry tomatoes, nuts, nutritional yeast, garlic, and olive oil. Blend until smooth, adding a little water if necessary to reach your desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper.

  3. Combine: Toss the cooked pasta with the spinach pesto until well coated.

  4. Serve: Serve immediately, garnished with additional cherry tomatoes if desired.

Why You’ll Love It:

This dish is a vibrant take on classic pesto, utilizing fresh ingredients for a nutritious meal. The nutritional yeast provides a cheesy flavor without the dairy, making it a perfect choice for those on a dairy-free diet.

4. One-Pot Veggie Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces of pasta (any shape)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Vegetables: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, sautéing until fragrant. Add the bell pepper and zucchini, cooking until tender.

  2. Add Remaining Ingredients: Stir in the diced tomatoes (with their juices), vegetable broth, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.

  3. Cook the Pasta: Add the pasta to the pot, cover, and reduce heat to a simmer. Cook for about 10-12 minutes, or until the pasta is al dente and the liquid has mostly been absorbed, stirring occasionally.

  4. Serve: Remove from heat and garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Why You’ll Love It:

This one-pot veggie pasta is a true time-saver, minimizing cleanup while maximizing flavor. Packed with vegetables, it’s a wholesome option that’s both satisfying and easy to prepare.

5. Spicy Peanut Noodle Salad

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces of rice noodles or any preferred pasta
  • 1/4 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce or tamari
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup or honey (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sriracha or chili paste (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup of shredded carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of green onions, chopped
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Noodles: Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to cool.

  2. Make the Sauce: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, maple syrup, sesame oil, and sriracha until smooth.

  3. Combine: In a large bowl, toss the cooled noodles with the sauce. Add in the shredded carrots and sliced bell pepper, mixing well.

  4. Serve: Garnish with green onions and cilantro before serving.

Why You’ll Love It:

This spicy peanut noodle salad is a fun and flavorful dish that’s perfect for those who enjoy a bit of heat. It can be served warm or cold, making it an ideal option for meal prep or a quick dinner.

Each of these dairy-free pasta recipes showcases how easy it is to create delicious meals without compromising on flavor. They are ideal for quick weeknight dinners, allowing you to enjoy nutritious and satisfying meals with minimal effort. Whether you’re a busy parent, a student, or anyone in between, these recipes are sure to become staples in your weeknight cooking routine. Enjoy the vibrant flavors and healthy ingredients that these dishes offer and make your weeknight dinners something to look forward to!
