Easy One-Pan Greek Chicken and Rice Recipe


Are you looking for a quick and delicious meal that embodies the vibrant flavors of Greece? Look no further! This Easy One-Pan Greek Chicken and Rice recipe is not only simple to prepare but also offers a delightful combination of savory spices, tender chicken, and fluffy rice, making it a perfect weeknight dinner option. Let’s dive into the ingredients, preparation method, and some tips to make this dish a hit at your dinner table.

Ingredients

To create this flavorful Greek dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • For the Chicken:

    • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs (or breasts)
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Juice of 1 lemon
    • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

  • For the Rice:

    • 1 cup long-grain rice (such as basmati or jasmine)
    • 2 cups chicken broth (or water)
    • 1 medium onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 red bell pepper, diced
    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1/2 cup black olives, pitted and sliced (Kalamata olives work great)
    • Feta cheese for topping (optional)

Preparation Steps

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

Start by marinating the chicken to infuse it with flavor. In a mixing bowl, combine the olive oil, dried oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Add the chicken, ensuring each piece is well-coated with the marinade. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes. If you have the time, marinate it for a few hours or overnight in the refrigerator for extra flavor.

Step 2: Sauté the Vegetables

In a large skillet or a deep pan, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, sautéing until they become translucent and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes. Then, add the diced red bell pepper and continue to cook for another 2-3 minutes until softened.

Step 3: Brown the Chicken

Push the sautéed vegetables to one side of the pan and add the marinated chicken to the other side. Cook the chicken for about 5-7 minutes on each side until it is golden brown. This not only locks in the juices but also adds a beautiful color to the dish.

Step 4: Incorporate the Rice

Once the chicken is browned, add the rice to the pan, stirring it in with the vegetables. This step allows the rice to soak up all the flavors from the chicken and vegetables. Next, pour in the chicken broth, ensuring that the rice is fully submerged. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

Step 5: Add Herbs and Tomatoes

Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and add the halved cherry tomatoes, dried thyme, and paprika. Give everything a good stir to combine. Cover the pan with a lid and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and has absorbed most of the liquid.

Step 6: Finish with Olives and Feta

After the rice is cooked, remove the lid and gently fluff the rice with a fork. Stir in the sliced black olives. If you’re a fan of feta cheese, crumble some on top for a creamy, tangy finish. You can also sprinkle fresh parsley for an added touch of color and flavor.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Serve the Easy One-Pan Greek Chicken and Rice warm, garnished with extra lemon wedges and fresh herbs if desired. This dish pairs wonderfully with a simple side salad or some warm pita bread.

Tips for the Perfect One-Pan Greek Chicken and Rice

  • Choosing the Right Rice: Basmati or jasmine rice works best for this recipe due to their aromatic qualities. Avoid using sticky rice, as it won’t provide the desired texture.

  • Adjusting the Spice Level: If you enjoy a little heat, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes to the marinade or during the cooking process.

  • Meal Prep Friendly: This dish is great for meal prepping! You can store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3-4 days. Reheat gently in the microwave or on the stovetop.

  • Vegetarian Option: For a vegetarian twist, replace the chicken with chickpeas or a mix of your favorite vegetables. Ensure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

  • Fresh Herbs: Fresh herbs can elevate the flavors. Consider adding fresh dill or mint when serving for a refreshing touch.

  • Make it Your Own: Feel free to customize this dish with your favorite vegetables. Spinach, zucchini, or artichokes can all be great additions.

Nutritional Benefits

This Easy One-Pan Greek Chicken and Rice recipe not only satisfies your taste buds but also provides a balanced meal. Chicken is a great source of protein, while the rice offers carbohydrates to fuel your day. The addition of vegetables enriches the dish with vitamins and minerals. Feta cheese adds calcium and flavor, making this meal both nutritious and delicious.

Conclusion

Bringing the flavors of Greece into your kitchen has never been easier with this Easy One-Pan Greek Chicken and Rice recipe. It’s a comforting, hearty meal that requires minimal cleanup, making it ideal for busy weeknights or cozy family dinners. With its vibrant colors and rich flavors, this dish is sure to impress family and friends alike.

Try it out for your next meal, and don’t be surprised if it becomes a regular favorite in your household. Happy cooking!
