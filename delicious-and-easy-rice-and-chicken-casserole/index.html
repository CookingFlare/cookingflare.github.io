Delicious and Easy Rice and Chicken Casserole


Are you looking for a comforting and delicious meal that’s easy to make? Look no further than this mouthwatering Rice and Chicken Casserole recipe! Packed with flavor and simple to prepare, this dish is perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or meal prepping for the week. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delectable dish in just a few easy steps.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of long-grain white rice
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of grated cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cook the Chicken: In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until it’s no longer pink. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced red bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant.

  4. Add the Rice and Seasonings: Stir in the long-grain white rice, paprika, dried thyme, and dried oregano. Cook for a few minutes, allowing the rice to absorb the flavors of the seasonings.

  5. Combine the Ingredients: Transfer the cooked chicken back to the skillet with the rice and vegetables. Add the frozen peas and pour in the chicken broth. Stir everything together and bring the mixture to a simmer.

  6. Transfer to a Casserole Dish: Once the mixture is simmering, transfer everything to a greased casserole dish. Spread the mixture evenly in the dish.

  7. Bake to Perfection: Cover the casserole dish with aluminum foil and place it in the preheated oven. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.

  8. Add Cheese and Garnish: Remove the foil from the casserole dish and sprinkle the grated cheddar cheese over the top. Return the dish to the oven and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Serve and Enjoy: Once the casserole is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve this delicious Rice and Chicken Casserole to your eagerly awaiting guests or family members.

Tips and Variations:

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables such as carrots, corn, or broccoli.
  • For a spicy kick, add a dash of cayenne pepper or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.
  • Substitute the chicken with turkey or even tofu for a vegetarian option.
  • This casserole can be made ahead of time and refrigerated, making it a convenient option for meal prep.

With its delightful blend of tender chicken, flavorful rice, and comforting seasonings, this Rice and Chicken Casserole is sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a cozy homemade meal, this recipe is a winner. Give it a try and savor the irresistible flavors of this delightful dish!
