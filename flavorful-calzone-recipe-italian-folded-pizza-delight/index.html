Flavorful Calzone Recipe - Italian Folded Pizza Delight


There’s something undeniably satisfying about biting into a warm, flavorful calzone. This Italian folded pizza delight is a versatile dish that allows you to experiment with various fillings and flavors. Whether you’re a meat lover, a vegetarian, or a fan of bold and spicy flavors, the calzone has something for everyone. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create a delicious and satisfying calzone that will tantalize your taste buds and impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni or cooked sausage
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Mix the ingredients until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan cheeses.
  2. Add the pepperoni or cooked sausage, marinara sauce, basil, olives, red bell pepper, onion, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix everything until well combined.

Step 3: Assemble the Calzone

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 4 equal portions.
  3. Roll out each portion into a circle, about 8-10 inches in diameter.
  4. Spoon the filling onto one half of each circle, leaving a border around the edge.
  5. Fold the other half of the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape. Press the edges firmly to seal.
  6. Place the calzones on the prepared baking sheet and brush the tops with olive oil.

Step 4: Bake the Calzones

  1. Bake the calzones in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  2. Remove the calzones from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy Your Flavorful Calzones!

Now that you’ve mastered the art of making delicious calzones, it’s time to enjoy the fruits of your labor. Serve the calzones with extra marinara sauce for dipping and a crisp, refreshing salad on the side. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner with friends or simply treating yourself to a comforting meal, these flavorful calzones are sure to be a hit. Experiment with different fillings and get creative with your flavors – the possibilities are endless!

So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the authentic flavors of Italy with this delightful calzone recipe. Buon appetito!
