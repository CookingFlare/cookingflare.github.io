Savory Cranberry Apple Walnut Salad for Thanksgiving


As Thanksgiving approaches, it’s time to start planning a delicious and memorable menu for the festive occasion. While traditional dishes like turkey and mashed potatoes often steal the spotlight, a well-crafted salad can add a refreshing and vibrant touch to the table. This Savory Cranberry Apple Walnut Salad is the perfect addition to your Thanksgiving feast, offering a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will leave your guests craving more.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 2 medium apples, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup walnuts, toasted
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dressing

In a small bowl, whisk together the apple cider vinegar, olive oil, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set the dressing aside to allow the flavors to meld while you prepare the salad ingredients.

Step 2: Toast the Walnuts

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Spread the walnuts in a single layer on a baking sheet and toast them for 8-10 minutes, or until they become fragrant. Keep a close eye on them to prevent burning. Once toasted, remove the walnuts from the oven and allow them to cool.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, dried cranberries, sliced apples, toasted walnuts, crumbled feta cheese, and red onion. Gently toss the ingredients together to distribute them evenly.

Step 4: Dress the Salad

Drizzle the prepared dressing over the salad, starting with a small amount and adding more as needed. Toss the salad gently to coat the ingredients with the dressing, being careful not to overdress the greens.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Transfer the Savory Cranberry Apple Walnut Salad to a serving dish or individual plates and serve immediately. The combination of sweet cranberries, crisp apples, crunchy walnuts, and tangy feta cheese, all dressed in the flavorful vinaigrette, is sure to impress your Thanksgiving guests.

Conclusion

This Savory Cranberry Apple Walnut Salad is a delightful and refreshing addition to your Thanksgiving menu. Its vibrant colors, contrasting textures, and harmonious flavors make it a standout dish that complements the richness of the traditional holiday fare. Whether served as a starter or a side, this salad is bound to become a new favorite at your Thanksgiving gatherings. Embrace the spirit of the season and treat your loved ones to this irresistible culinary creation. Happy Thanksgiving!
