Picnic Pies: Savory and Sweet Delights


Are you planning a delightful picnic in the great outdoors? Look no further than these delicious picnic pies that are sure to impress your friends and family. Packed with savory and sweet fillings, these handheld treats are perfect for a sunny day out. Whether you prefer a hearty meat pie or a fruity dessert pie, we have you covered. So grab your picnic blanket, gather your loved ones, and get ready to indulge in these scrumptious delights.

Savory Pies

1. Classic Chicken and Mushroom Pie

This classic crowd-pleaser is a must-have for any picnic. Tender chunks of chicken and earthy mushrooms are cooked in a creamy sauce, then encased in a buttery pastry crust. The combination of flavors in this pie is simply irresistible.

2. Beef and Guinness Pie

For a rich and hearty option, try this Beef and Guinness pie. Slow-cooked beef is simmered in a luscious Guinness gravy, resulting in a melt-in-your-mouth filling. The robust flavors of the beef and the dark stout make this pie a true showstopper.

3. Spinach and Feta Pie

Looking for a vegetarian option? This Spinach and Feta pie is packed with nutritious greens and tangy feta cheese. The combination of flavors is fresh and vibrant, making it a perfect choice for those who prefer a lighter savory option.

4. Mini Quiches

Mini quiches are a versatile choice that allows you to experiment with different fillings. From bacon and cheese to spinach and ricotta, the possibilities are endless. These bite-sized treats are easy to eat on the go and are sure to be a hit at your picnic.

Sweet Pies

1. Fresh Strawberry Pie

Celebrate the season with a fresh strawberry pie. Juicy strawberries are piled high in a flaky pastry crust, creating a vibrant and delectable dessert. Top it off with a dollop of whipped cream for a touch of indulgence.

2. Apple and Cinnamon Pie

Nothing beats the classic combination of apples and cinnamon. This pie features sweet and tart apples, spiced with cinnamon and baked to perfection. The aroma of this pie baking in the oven will have everyone eagerly awaiting dessert time.

3. Mixed Berry Hand Pies

These handheld pies are bursting with the flavors of summer. A medley of mixed berries, such as blueberries, raspberries, and blackberries, are encased in a buttery pastry shell. These portable treats are perfect for picnics and can be enjoyed without the need for utensils.

4. Chocolate Pecan Pie Bars

Indulge your sweet tooth with these decadent chocolate pecan pie bars. A buttery shortbread crust is topped with a rich and gooey mixture of chocolate and pecans. These bars are a chocolate lover’s dream and are guaranteed to satisfy any dessert craving.

Conclusion

Picnic pies are a delightful addition to any outdoor gathering. Whether you prefer savory or sweet, there are endless possibilities for creating delicious handheld pies. From classic combinations to inventive flavor combinations, these pies are sure to impress and satisfy. So, pack your picnic basket with these savory and sweet delights, and enjoy a memorable picnic with your loved ones.
