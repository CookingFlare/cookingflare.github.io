Top 10 Italian Dishes Every Food Lover Should Know How to Cook


Italian cuisine is world-renowned for its rich flavors, mouthwatering aromas, and exotic ingredients. Home cooks everywhere dream of mastering the art of Italian cooking. Keep reading to learn about the top 10 Italian dishes that you must know how to cook.

1. Spaghetti alla Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish features spaghetti tossed in a sauce made of eggs, cheese, bacon, and black pepper. Its rich flavors will satisfy even the heartiest of appetites. To make carbonara, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions, sauté some diced pancetta, and combine the cooked pasta with beaten eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and freshly ground black pepper.

2. Risotto alla Milanese

This creamy rice dish is a staple in Italian cuisine, especially in the Lombardy region. The secret to a delicious Milanese Risotto lies in the preparation, so ensure you use a variety of Arborio rice. To make this dish, sauté onions, garlic, and white wine, add the rice, and stir continuously as you add warm broth bit by bit. Once the rice is cooked, stir in grated Parmesan cheese and prepare to indulge.

3. Margherita Pizza

Pizza Margherita is a simple yet iconic Italian dish revered for its elegance and simplicity. To prepare this pizza, combine fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, a drizzle of olive oil, and fresh basil leaves on a thin crust to create a classic Italian feast.

4. Lasagne al Forno

Lasagne al Forno is a hearty, delectable pasta dish perfect for feeding a crowd. Layer fresh lasagna sheets, a blend of sautéed ground beef and tomato sauce, and creamy ricotta cheese to create a pantry-friendly meal that is sure to impress your loved ones.

5. Osso Buco alla Milanese

Osso Buco alla Milanense is an iconic braised veal shank dish from Milan, Italy. Cook veal shanks with tomatoes, broth, and a hint of lemon zest for a hearty, comforting meal loaded with rich flavor.

6. Parmesan Crisps with Antipasto

Create a delightful appetizer perfect for any gathering by combining crunchy Parmesan Crisps with colorful medleys of antipasto. Pesto, roasted red peppers, and sun-dried tomatoes will add an Italian flair to any event.

7. Tiramisu

This classic Italian dessert is sure to leave your guests begging for more. To make Tiramisu, combine cooled espresso-laced savory mascarpone cheese with a dusting of cocoa powder to create a light, creamy, and authentic Italian experience.

8. Bruschetta

This classic Italian appetizer features garlic toast topped with diced tomatoes, fresh basil, and a drizzle of olive oil. Serve it as an appetizer at your next gathering and enjoy the delightful flavors of Italy.

9. Tagliatelle with Porcini Mushroom Sauce

Create a luxurious, satisfying, and delicious meal with fresh tagliatelle pasta tossed in a hearty porcini mushroom sauce. Sauté the mushrooms until golden brown, combine with freshly grated Parmesan cheese, and serve over al dente tagliatelle pasta for a delicious and authentic Italian experience.

10. Saltless Olive Oil Bread

For a truly unique bread, try making a saltless olive oil bread and serve it alongside your favorite Italian dishes. The olive oil provides a beautiful flavor that complements any Italian meal perfectly while creating a memorable experience for all.

With these top 10 Italian dishes, you’ll be ahead of the curve and mastering classic Italian cuisine in no time. Buon Appetito!
