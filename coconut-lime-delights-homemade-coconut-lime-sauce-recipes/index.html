Coconut Lime Delights: Homemade Coconut Lime Sauce Recipes


Coconut and lime - a taste combination that is both refreshing and irresistible. Sometimes, all it takes is the perfect blending of flavors to turn an ordinary meal into an extraordinary one. In this guide, we’ll uncover some delectable homemade coconut lime sauce recipes perfect for enhancing the taste of your favorite dishes.

Creamy Coconut Lime Sauce

First up is our Creamy Coconut Lime Sauce. The combination of tangy lime and creamy coconut results in a delicious sauce that is perfect for pasta, fish, chicken, and even wok dishes.

Ingredients:

  • ⅓ cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 cup water
  • ¼ cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons finely grated lime zest
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon corn starch

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine coconut, water, lime juice, honey, lime zest, salt, and cornstarch. Mix until evenly combined.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly. When the mixture begins to simmer, reduce heat to low.
  3. Let the sauce simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens and the flavors have melded together.
  4. Remove from heat and serve alongside your favorite dish.

Citrus Coconut Lime Sauce

Our Citrus Coconut Lime Sauce adds a burst of flavor to your dishes while providing a beautiful contrast to your plate. This sauce is terrific for drizzling over grilled fish, roasted chicken, or even stirred into your rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh orange juice
  • ½ cup water
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine orange juice, water, coconut, lime juice, lemon juice, vanilla extract, ground ginger, cumin, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Once the mixture begins to simmer, reduce heat to low.
  3. Let the sauce simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the coconut is tender and flavors have melded.
  4. Remove from heat and adjust seasonings to taste. Drizzle over your preferred dish.

No-Churn Coconut Lime Ice Cream Sauce

Keep your summer sweet with a cool and delicious No-Churn Coconut Lime Ice Cream Sauce. This homemade sauce is perfect for drizzling over ice cream cones, waffles, or pancakes.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • ¾ cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon lime zest

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, lime juice, and coconut milk. Heat over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar dissolves.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  3. Slowly pour the warmed coconut mixture into the whipped cream, stirring gently until combined.
  4. Stir in the lime zest until evenly distributed.
  5. Cover and refrigerate until chilled, usually a few hours or overnight.
  6. Serve as desired over ice cream, waffles, or pancakes.
