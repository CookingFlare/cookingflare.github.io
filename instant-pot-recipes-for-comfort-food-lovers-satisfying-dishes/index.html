Instant Pot Recipes for Comfort Food Lovers: Satisfying Dishes


When life demands ease and heartwarming meals, the Instant Pot steps in as the unsung hero of the kitchen. This multi-functional appliance transforms traditionally time-consuming comfort foods into quick, flavorful dishes with minimal effort. Whether you’re seeking creamy soups, tender roasts, or savory stews, these Instant Pot recipes are designed to bring cozy, satisfying vibes to your dining table. Let’s dive into a world of delicious comfort food that’s made effortlessly.

1. Creamy Chicken and Wild Rice Soup

Nothing says comfort quite like a warm bowl of soup, and this creamy chicken and wild rice dish combines tender chicken with nutty wild rice and an indulgent broth.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup wild rice (uncooked)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 5 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode, and melt the butter. Add onion, garlic, carrots, and celery, cooking for 3–5 minutes until vegetables are softened.
  2. Turn off “Sauté” mode and add chicken breasts, wild rice, chicken broth, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Close the lid, set the valve to seal, and cook on “Manual” high pressure for 20 minutes.
  4. Once done, perform a quick release. Remove the chicken, shred it, and return it to the pot.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and allow the soup to warm for a few minutes on “Sauté” mode. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve hot with a slice of crusty bread or crackers for the ultimate comfort experience.

2. Instant Pot Beef Stroganoff

Beef stroganoff delivers tender beef, silky noodles, and a creamy mushroom sauce that’s pure indulgence. Cooking it in the Instant Pot reduces hours of stovetop simmering to mere minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 8 ounces egg noodles
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode, and add olive oil. Season the beef with salt and pepper, then sear it in batches until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add the onion, mushrooms, and garlic to the pot. Cook until softened, about 4–5 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle flour over the vegetables, stirring to coat evenly. Slowly pour in the beef broth while stirring to avoid lumps.
  4. Add the seared beef back to the pot along with Worcestershire sauce. Close the lid, set the valve to seal, and pressure cook on “Manual” high pressure for 20 minutes.
  5. Perform a quick release. Open the pot and stir in the egg noodles, ensuring they’re submerged in the liquid. Pressure cook again for 5 minutes.
  6. Once finished, do another quick release. Stir in sour cream until the sauce is creamy and smooth. Garnish with parsley, if desired, and serve immediately.

3. Classic Pot Roast with Vegetables

A pot roast is the pinnacle of comfort food. Tender beef that falls apart at the touch of a fork, paired with perfectly cooked vegetables, brings Sunday dinner vibes any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 3-pound chuck roast
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 4 carrots, cut into chunks
  • 3 cups baby potatoes
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch (optional, for thickening)

Instructions

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode, and heat the vegetable oil. Season the roast generously with salt and pepper, then sear on all sides to develop a golden-brown crust. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add onion and garlic to the pot, stirring for 2–3 minutes until fragrant. Stir in the tomato paste, then add the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, rosemary, and thyme. Scrape the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits.
  3. Return the roast to the pot and add carrots and potatoes. Close the lid, set the valve to seal, and cook on “Manual” high pressure for 60 minutes.
  4. Allow for a 10-minute natural release, then perform a quick release. Remove the roast and vegetables.
  5. Optional: For a thicker gravy, turn the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and stir in a cornstarch slurry (2 tablespoons water + 2 tablespoons cornstarch) until thickened.
  6. Slice the beef, serve alongside the vegetables, and spoon the gravy over everything.

4. Instant Pot Mac and Cheese

Mac and cheese is the ultimate crowd-pleaser. This creamy, comforting dish requires only a handful of ingredients and zero baking.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Add macaroni, water, butter, garlic powder, and mustard powder to the Instant Pot. Stir to combine.
  2. Close the lid, set the valve to seal, and pressure cook on “Manual” high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. Perform a quick release once the cooking cycle is complete. Open the lid and stir.
  4. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Pour in the heavy cream and stir in the cheeses gradually until melted and creamy.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with a sprinkle of black pepper or herbs for a comforting, cheesy indulgence.

5. Chicken and Dumplings

Effortless chicken and dumplings in an Instant Pot bring a rich, aromatic, and supremely satisfying dish that warms you from the inside out.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup frozen peas (optional)

For the Dumplings:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • ¾ cup milk

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Heat the olive oil, then add chicken thighs, searing on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add the onion, carrots, and celery, sautéing for 4–5 minutes. Stir in the garlic powder, thyme, and broth.
  3. Return the chicken to the pot. Close the lid, set the valve to seal, and pressure cook on “Manual” high pressure for 10 minutes. Perform a quick release.
  4. While the soup is cooking, prepare the dumplings by mixing flour, baking powder, salt, butter, and milk in a bowl until a sticky dough forms.
  5. Open the lid, shred the chicken with two forks, and stir in the shredded chicken and peas if using.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dumpling dough onto the surface of the soup. Close the lid, set the valve to venting, and simmer on “Sauté” mode for 10 minutes, allowing dumplings to cook through.
  7. Serve hot, and enjoy a spoonful of heaven in every bite.

These Instant Pot recipes are perfect for anyone who craves hearty, homestyle meals without spending hours in the kitchen. The next time you’re in need of comfort on a plate, let your Instant Pot do the work. With these dishes, the hardest part will be deciding which one to make first. Happy cooking!
