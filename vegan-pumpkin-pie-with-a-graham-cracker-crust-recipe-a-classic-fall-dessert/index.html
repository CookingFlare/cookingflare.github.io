Vegan Pumpkin Pie with a Graham Cracker Crust Recipe: A Classic Fall Dessert


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in the warm, comforting flavors of pumpkin. And what better way to do so than with a delicious vegan pumpkin pie? This recipe combines the familiar taste of pumpkin with a crunchy graham cracker crust, creating a dessert that is both nostalgic and satisfying. Whether you follow a vegan lifestyle or simply want to try something new, this vegan pumpkin pie is sure to become a fall favorite.

Ingredients

For the Graham Cracker Crust:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup

For the Pumpkin Filling:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted vegan butter, and maple syrup. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated.

  3. Press the graham cracker mixture into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie dish, forming an even crust. Use the back of a spoon or a measuring cup to firmly press the crumbs into place.

  4. Place the crust in the preheated oven and bake for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and let it cool while you prepare the pumpkin filling.

  5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, coconut milk, maple syrup, cornstarch, vanilla extract, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt. Stir until all the ingredients are well combined and smooth.

  6. Pour the pumpkin filling into the cooled graham cracker crust, spreading it evenly.

  7. Place the pie back in the oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the filling is set and slightly firm to the touch.

  8. Once the pie is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature. This will allow the pie to set further.

  9. Once cooled, refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to ensure it is fully set.

  10. Serve chilled, and for an extra touch, top with vegan whipped cream or a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Tips and Variations

  • For a gluten-free version, use gluten-free graham crackers or gluten-free cookies for the crust.

  • Feel free to add your own twist to the pie by incorporating extra spices such as cloves or allspice.

  • If you prefer a sweeter pie, you can increase the amount of maple syrup or add a few tablespoons of brown sugar to the pumpkin filling.

  • Want to make it extra festive? Decorate the top of the pie with cinnamon-dusted vegan pastry leaves or a sprinkle of crushed pecans.

Conclusion

This vegan pumpkin pie with a graham cracker crust is the perfect dessert to celebrate the flavors of fall. The smooth and creamy pumpkin filling pairs beautifully with the crunchy and slightly sweet graham cracker crust, creating a harmonious combination of textures and tastes. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply craving a comforting treat, this vegan pumpkin pie is sure to impress. Enjoy a slice of this classic fall dessert and savor the deliciousness of the season.
