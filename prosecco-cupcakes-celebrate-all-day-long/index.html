Prosecco Cupcakes: Celebrate All Day Long!


Ah, Prosecco, that magical elixir of bubbly joy! Who wouldn’t want to incorporate the delightful taste of this sparkling wine into all aspects of their lives, especially their desserts? Embrace the fizzy goodness of Prosecco in a cupcake that is as delicious as it is celebratory!

These Prosecco Cupcakes are perfect for livening up any occasion, from a casual get-together with friends to a grand celebration. With every bite, you’ll feel like you’re toasting to joy and merriment. So, grab your apron, preheat the oven, and let’s get baking!

Key Ingredients

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a look at the essential elements that give these Prosecco Cupcakes their unique flavor:

  1. Prosecco: Of course, we can’t make Prosecco Cupcakes without the star ingredient! Choose a dry Prosecco for a subtle yet refreshing taste, balancing well with the sweetness of the cupcakes.
  2. Vanilla Extract: A classic baking ingredient that provides depth to the cupcakes’ flavor.
  3. Fresh Lemon Juice and Zest: Adding a citrusy twist to complement the Prosecco’s notes.
  4. Butter: For a rich and moist texture.
  5. Sugar: Balance the flavors in our cupcakes.
  6. Flour: The base of our cupcakes.
  7. Eggs: Gives structure and binds all the ingredients together.

The Recipe

Now that you’re familiar with the main ingredients, it’s time to get started with the recipe. Before we begin, make sure to preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare 12 muffin cups with liners.

For the Cupcakes

  1. In a saucepan, melt 1 cup (225g) of unsalted butter over low heat. Keep a close watch as you don’t want the butter to scorch.
  2. Once melted, pour in ½ cup (120ml) of Prosecco and let it simmer. Stir the mixture until combined.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 cups (250g) of all-purpose flour, 1½ cups (300g) of sugar, and 2 teaspoons of baking powder.
  4. To the melted butter mixture, add 2 large eggs, 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract, 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice, and 1 tablespoon of lemon zest. Whisk everything together until well combined.
  5. Carefully pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until fully incorporated.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each about ⅔ full.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  8. Let the cupcakes cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

For the Prosecco Buttercream Frosting

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat 1 cup (225g) of softened unsalted butter until creamy.
  2. Add 4 cups of powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract, and 2-3 tablespoons of Prosecco. Mix until well combined.
  3. If the frosting is too thick, gradually add more Prosecco to thin it out and achieve your desired consistency.

Assembling the Prosecco Cupcakes

  1. Once the cupcakes have cooled, generously frost each one with a swirl of Prosecco Buttercream Frosting.
  2. For an extra touch of sweetness and to keep the frosting in place, you can lightly dust the frosted cupcakes with powdered sugar.

Your Prosecco Cupcakes are now ready to be enjoyed! Serve them on a festive platter, perhaps accompanied by some champagne strawberries or edible flowers to elevate the presentation. Cheers to endless celebration with every single bite!

Keep the party going with these delightful Prosecco Cupcakes! The sweet and bubbly taste of Prosecco infuses every crumb of this delectable treat, perfect for injecting a touch of sparkle into your day. So, whether you’re celebrating a birthday, an anniversary, or just a fabulous Friday night, these cupcakes are sure to make every moment unforgettable. Enjoy the fizz and the flavor!
