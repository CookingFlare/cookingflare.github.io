Satisfying Savory Pie Recipes for Hearty Family Meals


Note: This blog post contains a collection of delicious savory pie recipes that are perfect for satisfying and hearty family meals. Each recipe is packed with flavor and will surely become a family favorite. So, let’s dive right in and discover these mouthwatering dishes!

1. Classic Chicken Pot Pie

This classic chicken pot pie recipe is a comforting and filling dish that is loved by everyone. Made with tender chicken, mixed vegetables, and a creamy sauce, it’s the perfect savory pie to warm your heart and satisfy your taste buds. The flaky crust adds a delightful crunch to every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 prepared pie crust

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the chicken broth and milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add the cooked chicken and frozen mixed vegetables. Season with salt and pepper and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  5. Pour the filling into a prepared pie crust and cover with another pie crust.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  7. Allow to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

2. Beef and Mushroom Pie

Indulge in the rich flavors of this beef and mushroom pie. The tender chunks of beef, sautéed mushrooms, and aromatic herbs create a hearty filling that will leave you craving for more. The buttery pastry crust is the perfect complement to this savory delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef, cubed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 prepared pie crust

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the beef cubes and cook until browned on all sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the mushrooms, onions, and garlic until softened.
  4. Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Gradually add the beef broth and red wine (if using), stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
  6. Return the beef to the skillet and add thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Pour the filling into a prepared pie crust and cover with another pie crust.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  9. Allow to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

3. Spinach and Feta Pie

For a vegetarian option, try this delightful spinach and feta pie. The combination of sautéed spinach, tangy feta cheese, and aromatic herbs creates a burst of flavors in every bite. The flaky phyllo pastry adds a satisfying crunch to this savory pie.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spinach, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 sheets phyllo pastry
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the onions and garlic until softened.
  3. Add the chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  4. In a bowl, mix together the feta cheese, ricotta cheese, beaten eggs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Stir in the sautéed spinach mixture until well combined.
  6. Layer 4 sheets of phyllo pastry in a greased pie dish, brushing each layer with melted butter.
  7. Pour the spinach and cheese filling into the pie dish.
  8. Layer the remaining 4 sheets of phyllo pastry on top, again brushing each layer with melted butter.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the phyllo pastry is golden brown.
  10. Allow to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

Note: Feel free to experiment with different fillings and crusts to create your own unique savory pie recipes. Whether it’s a meat lover’s delight or a vegetarian’s dream, savory pies are a versatile and satisfying option for hearty family meals. So gather your loved ones, share a meal, and savor the deliciousness of these savory pie recipes!
