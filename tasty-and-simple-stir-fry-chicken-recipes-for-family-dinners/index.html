Tasty and Simple Stir Fry Chicken Recipes for Family Dinners


Are you tired of cooking the same old chicken dishes for your family dinners? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore some delicious and easy stir fry chicken recipes that are sure to please everyone at the table. Stir fry dishes are not only quick to prepare, but they are also packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients. So, let’s dive right in and discover these tasty recipes that will leave your family asking for seconds!

1. Sweet and Sour Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, ketchup, honey, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the bell pepper and onion. Cook until they begin to soften, about 3 minutes.
  6. Add the pineapple chunks and cooked chicken back into the skillet.
  7. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  9. Serve the sweet and sour chicken stir fry over steamed rice or noodles.

2. Teriyaki Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the broccoli, carrot, and bell pepper. Cook until they begin to soften, about 3 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked chicken back into the skillet.
  7. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  9. Serve the teriyaki chicken stir fry over steamed rice or noodles.

3. Garlic Ginger Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups snap peas
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the snap peas and bell pepper. Cook until they begin to soften, about 3 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked chicken back into the skillet.
  7. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  9. Serve the garlic ginger chicken stir fry over steamed rice or noodles.

There you have it - three delicious and simple stir fry chicken recipes that are perfect for family dinners. These recipes are versatile, so feel free to add your favorite vegetables or adjust the seasonings to suit your taste. Get creative in the kitchen and enjoy the mouthwatering flavors of these stir fry dishes with your loved ones. Happy cooking!
