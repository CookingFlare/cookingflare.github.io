Delightful and Decadent Nutella Swirl Bread


If you’re a fan of Nutella and love freshly baked bread, then this recipe is a match made in heaven for you. Get ready to indulge in the delightful and decadent Nutella swirl bread that will surely satisfy your sweet tooth cravings. This recipe combines the rich and creamy goodness of Nutella with the soft and fluffy texture of homemade bread, creating a heavenly treat that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm milk (110°F/43°C)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup Nutella

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, active dry yeast, and salt. Mix well to ensure that all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the warm milk and melted butter. Using a wooden spoon or your hands, mix the ingredients until a sticky dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough feels too sticky, add a little more flour as needed.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Allow the dough to rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  5. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer the dough back onto the floured surface and roll it out into a rectangle, approximately 1/4-inch thick.

  6. Spread the Nutella evenly over the rolled-out dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Gently roll the dough into a tight log, starting from one of the longer sides.

  7. Carefully transfer the rolled dough into a greased loaf pan, making sure the seam side is facing down. Cover the pan with the kitchen towel and let it rise for another 30 minutes.

  8. Meanwhile, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  9. Once the dough has risen for the second time, place the loaf pan in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped.

  10. Remove the bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then, transfer it onto a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This delightful Nutella swirl bread is perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a sweet treat any time of the day. Serve it warm or at room temperature, and if you want to take it up a notch, spread a little extra Nutella on each slice. You can also pair it with a cup of coffee or a glass of milk for a satisfying snack.

Storage Tips

To keep your Nutella swirl bread fresh, store it in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. If you want to enjoy it for longer, you can freeze the bread in individual slices and thaw them as needed. Just make sure to wrap each slice tightly in plastic wrap before freezing.

Conclusion

Indulge in the delightful and decadent Nutella swirl bread that will transport your taste buds to a world of pure bliss. This recipe combines the irresistible flavors of Nutella with the comforting goodness of homemade bread. Whether you’re a Nutella lover or simply looking for a delicious treat, this bread is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to bake a loaf of pure deliciousness. Enjoy!
