Indulge in Dairy-Free Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of chocolate and peanut butter but are looking for a dairy-free alternative, then you’re in for a treat! These dairy-free chocolate peanut butter cups are decadent, creamy, and utterly delicious. Whether you have dietary restrictions or simply want to try something new, these homemade treats are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dairy-free chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Mini muffin liners

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chocolate

Start by melting the dairy-free chocolate chips and 1 tablespoon of coconut oil in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat the mixture in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is smooth and fully melted. Be careful not to overheat the chocolate, as it can become clumpy.

Step 2: Line the Muffin Tin

Place mini muffin liners in a mini muffin tin. This will make it easier to remove the chocolate peanut butter cups once they are set.

Step 3: Layer the Chocolate

Spoon a small amount of the melted chocolate into the bottom of each muffin liner, using about half of the chocolate mixture. Tap the tin on the counter to smooth out the chocolate and remove any air bubbles. Place the tin in the freezer for 10 minutes to set the chocolate.

Step 4: Prepare the Peanut Butter Filling

While the chocolate is setting, prepare the peanut butter filling. In a separate bowl, mix the creamy peanut butter, 1 tablespoon of coconut oil, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until smooth and well combined.

Step 5: Add the Peanut Butter Filling

Remove the muffin tin from the freezer and spoon a small amount of the peanut butter mixture on top of the set chocolate layer. Use a small spoon to gently spread the peanut butter to the edges, leaving a small border of chocolate around the sides. Return the tin to the freezer for another 10 minutes to set the peanut butter filling.

Step 6: Complete with Chocolate

After the peanut butter filling has set, remove the tin from the freezer and spoon the remaining melted chocolate on top of the peanut butter layer, covering it completely. Tap the tin on the counter again to smooth out the chocolate and remove any air bubbles.

Step 7: Final Setting

Place the tin back in the freezer for a final 10-15 minutes, or until the chocolate is completely set.

Step 8: Enjoy!

Once the chocolate peanut butter cups are fully set, remove them from the muffin tin and enjoy! Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Final Thoughts

These dairy-free chocolate peanut butter cups are a delightful indulgence for anyone looking to enjoy a sweet treat without dairy. Whether you’re making them for yourself, your family, or a special occasion, these homemade goodies are bound to impress. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and savor the rich combination of dairy-free chocolate and creamy peanut butter in every bite!
