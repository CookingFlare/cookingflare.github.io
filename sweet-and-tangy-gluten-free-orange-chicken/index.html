Sweet and Tangy: Gluten-Free Orange Chicken


Are you craving a delicious and flavorful chicken dish that’s both sweet and tangy? Look no further! Our Gluten-Free Orange Chicken recipe is here to satisfy your taste buds and leave you wanting more. Not only is this dish incredibly tasty, but it’s also gluten-free, making it suitable for those with dietary restrictions. So let’s dive right into this mouth-watering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup gluten-free soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the gluten-free soy sauce, orange juice, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes. This flavorful marinade will be the key to the sweet and tangy taste of our gluten-free orange chicken.

  2. Place the chicken pieces in a shallow dish or a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Make sure all the chicken pieces are evenly coated. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes. This will give the flavors a chance to infuse into the meat and make it even more delicious.

  3. While the chicken is marinating, prepare the sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water until the cornstarch is fully dissolved. This mixture will help thicken the sauce and give it a smooth texture.

  4. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken pieces (discard any excess marinade) and cook until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes per side.

  5. Reduce the heat to medium and pour the prepared sauce over the cooked chicken. Stir gently to coat the chicken with the sauce. Continue cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and reached your desired consistency.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the gluten-free orange chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the chicken to become even more tender.

  7. Serve the Sweet and Tangy Gluten-Free Orange Chicken over steamed rice or quinoa, and garnish with green onions or sesame seeds for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.

Conclusion

There you have it – a delicious and gluten-free twist on the classic orange chicken! This recipe combines the perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors, making it a crowd-pleaser for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a flavorful meal, this Gluten-Free Orange Chicken will not disappoint.

Feel free to get creative and customize this recipe to suit your taste. You can add your favorite vegetables such as bell peppers or broccoli for a more colorful and nutritious meal. Don’t forget to share this recipe with your friends and family so they can also enjoy this delightful dish.

So why wait? Get cooking and indulge in the delightful flavors of our Sweet and Tangy Gluten-Free Orange Chicken today!
