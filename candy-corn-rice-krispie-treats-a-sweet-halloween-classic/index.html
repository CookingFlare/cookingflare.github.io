Candy Corn Rice Krispie Treats: A Sweet Halloween Classic


As the spooky season approaches, it’s time to start thinking about fun and festive treats to enjoy with family and friends. One classic treat that never fails to delight is the Candy Corn Rice Krispie Treat. This delightful twist on the traditional Rice Krispie Treat not only captures the spirit of Halloween but also brings a burst of color and sweetness to your table. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through the simple steps to create this delicious and visually appealing Halloween classic.

Ingredients

For this recipe, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 6 cups of crispy rice cereal
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 package (10 ounces) of marshmallows
  • Yellow and orange food coloring
  • White chocolate chips
  • Candy corn for decoration

Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Add the marshmallows and stir until completely melted.

  2. Once the marshmallows are melted, remove the saucepan from the heat. Add a few drops of yellow food coloring to achieve the desired shade of yellow for the bottom layer of the Candy Corn Rice Krispie Treats. Stir until the color is evenly distributed.

  3. Add the crispy rice cereal to the marshmallow mixture and stir until well combined.

  4. Press the yellow mixture into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, spreading it evenly to create the bottom layer of the Candy Corn Rice Krispie Treats. Allow it to cool and set for about 10 minutes.

  5. In a separate saucepan, melt the white chocolate chips over low heat. Once melted, pour the white chocolate over the set yellow layer and spread it evenly to create the middle layer of the Candy Corn Rice Krispie Treats. Allow it to cool and set for about 10 minutes.

  6. Repeat step 2, this time adding orange food coloring to achieve the desired shade of orange for the top layer of the Candy Corn Rice Krispie Treats. Spread the orange mixture evenly over the set white chocolate layer. Allow it to cool and set for about 10 minutes.

  7. Once the mixture has set, cut the Candy Corn Rice Krispie Treats into squares using a sharp knife.

  8. To add a festive touch, place a candy corn on top of each treat for decoration.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a gooier texture, use a little less crispy rice cereal.
  • To prevent sticking, grease the knife with butter before cutting the treats.
  • Experiment with different food coloring ratios to achieve the perfect shades of yellow and orange.

Conclusion

The Candy Corn Rice Krispie Treats are a delightful addition to any Halloween gathering. Not only are they visually stunning, but they also offer a delicious blend of marshmallow, crispy rice, and white chocolate flavors. Whether you’re hosting a Halloween party or simply want to indulge in a festive sweet treat, these colorful and easy-to-make treats are sure to be a hit. Enjoy the process of creating these delightful Halloween classics and share the sweetness with your loved ones this spooky season!
