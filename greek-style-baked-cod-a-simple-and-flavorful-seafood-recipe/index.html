Greek-style Baked Cod: A Simple and Flavorful Seafood Recipe


If you’re a fan of seafood and Mediterranean flavors, then Greek-style Baked Cod is a dish that you absolutely must try. This recipe combines the light and flaky texture of cod with the bold and vibrant flavors of Greece, resulting in a dish that is both simple to prepare and incredibly delicious. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a flavorful and healthy meal, this Greek-style Baked Cod is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 4 fresh cod fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crumbled feta cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes, Kalamata olives, parsley, oregano, and thyme to the skillet. Stir the mixture well and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Place the cod fillets in a baking dish, ensuring they are in a single layer. Pour the tomato and olive mixture over the cod, making sure to cover the fish evenly with the flavorful sauce.
  5. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cod is cooked through and easily flakes with a fork.
  6. Once the cod is done, remove the foil and sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the top of the fillets.
  7. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until the feta cheese is slightly melted and golden brown.
  8. Serve the Greek-style Baked Cod hot, garnished with additional fresh parsley if desired.

Why You’ll Love Greek-style Baked Cod

The beauty of this recipe lies in its simplicity and the way it brings together the classic flavors of Greece. The combination of ripe tomatoes, briny Kalamata olives, fragrant herbs, and tangy feta cheese creates a symphony of flavors that perfectly complement the mild taste of the cod. This dish is not only a treat for your taste buds, but it’s also a nutritious option for a well-balanced meal.

Health Benefits of Cod

Cod is an excellent source of high-quality protein and is low in fat, making it a great choice for those looking to maintain a healthy diet. It is also a good source of vitamins and minerals, particularly vitamin B12, which is essential for a healthy nervous system, and omega-3 fatty acids, which are known for their heart-healthy benefits. By incorporating cod into your diet, you can enjoy a delicious meal while also reaping the nutritional rewards that this fish has to offer.

Pairing Suggestions

Greek-style Baked Cod pairs beautifully with a variety of side dishes. For a complete Mediterranean-inspired meal, consider serving it with a simple Greek salad, roasted vegetables, or a side of lemon-infused rice. A glass of crisp white wine, such as Assyrtiko or Sauvignon Blanc, can further enhance the dining experience and complement the dish’s vibrant flavors.

In Conclusion

Greek-style Baked Cod is a testament to the idea that sometimes, the simplest recipes are the most satisfying. With just a handful of ingredients and minimal effort, you can create a dish that is bursting with flavor and reminiscent of the sun-drenched shores of Greece. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or simply someone who appreciates a well-prepared meal, this recipe is certain to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, prepare your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the delightful taste of Greek-style Baked Cod.
