Easy Bruschetta Chicken Bites: A Simple and Delicious Snack


Looking for a quick and tasty snack that will impress your guests or satisfy your own cravings? Look no further than these Easy Bruschetta Chicken Bites! This recipe takes the classic flavors of bruschetta – juicy tomatoes, fragrant basil, and zesty garlic – and combines them with tender, succulent chicken for a mouthwatering bite-sized treat. Whether you’re hosting a party, need a simple appetizer, or just want to indulge in a flavorful snack, these Bruschetta Chicken Bites are sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

For the Bruschetta Topping:

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Chicken Bites:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the diced cherry tomatoes, chopped basil, minced garlic, extra virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine and set aside to let the flavors meld.

  3. Season the bite-sized chicken pieces with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.

  4. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken pieces and cook until golden brown on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.

  5. Transfer the browned chicken pieces to the prepared baking sheet and top each piece with a spoonful of the prepared bruschetta topping.

  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the bruschetta topping.

  7. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Remove from the oven and let the Bruschetta Chicken Bites cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Easy Bruschetta Chicken Bites are versatile and can be served in various ways. You can arrange them on a platter with toothpicks for a party-friendly finger food, serve them alongside a fresh green salad for a light and satisfying meal, or even pair them with some crusty bread to create a more substantial bruschetta-inspired dish.

Final Thoughts

With just a handful of simple ingredients and minimal prep time, these Easy Bruschetta Chicken Bites are a fantastic way to elevate your snacking game. The vibrant flavors of the bruschetta topping combined with the tender, juicy chicken create a winning combination that’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking for a delicious, hassle-free snack that doesn’t skimp on flavor. Enjoy!

Embrace the simplicity and deliciousness of these Easy Bruschetta Chicken Bites, and elevate your snacking game with this mouthwatering recipe. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a flavorful treat, these bite-sized delights are sure to impress.
