Soup Stories: Soup Cooking for Two Recipes

4 min · 715 words

Welcome to Soup Stories, where we explore the art of soup cooking for two! Soup is a versatile and comforting dish that can be enjoyed all year round. Whether you’re looking for a light and refreshing starter or a hearty meal, we’ve got you covered. In this blog post, we’ll share three delicious soup recipes that are perfect for a cozy dinner for two. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes (including the juice) and vegetable broth to the saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and chopped basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Bring the soup back to a simmer and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with a sprinkle of fresh basil on top.

2. Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup egg noodles
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, sliced carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the chicken broth, shredded chicken, egg noodles, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes or until the noodles are cooked.
  3. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  4. Ladle the soup into bowls and serve hot.

3. Spicy Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the cubed butternut squash on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Toss to coat evenly. Roast in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until tender and lightly browned.
  2. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Add the roasted butternut squash, curry powder, ground cumin, cayenne pepper (if using), and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If the soup is too thick, add more vegetable broth or water to reach the desired consistency.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

We hope you enjoy these delicious soup recipes for two! They are perfect for a cozy night in or a romantic dinner. Feel free to experiment with different ingredients and spices to make them your own. Don’t forget to share your soup stories with us in the comments below. Happy cooking!
