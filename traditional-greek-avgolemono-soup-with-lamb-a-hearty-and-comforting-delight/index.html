Traditional Greek Avgolemono Soup with Lamb: A Hearty and Comforting Delight


Are you searching for a delicious and satisfying soup to warm your soul? Look no further than the traditional Greek Avgolemono soup with lamb. This hearty and comforting delight is perfect for those chilly evenings or when you simply crave a taste of Greece. With its rich flavors and creamy texture, it’s sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

To make this traditional Greek Avgolemono soup with lamb, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of orzo pasta
  • 3 eggs
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the lamb and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the lamb from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  3. Add the diced carrots and celery to the pot and cook for a few minutes until they begin to soften.

  4. Now, it’s time to add the lamb back to the pot. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the soup is simmering, cook the orzo pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set it aside.

  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and lemon juice until well combined. This mixture will give the soup its signature creamy texture and tangy flavor.

  7. Slowly pour a ladleful of the hot soup into the egg and lemon mixture, whisking constantly. This will temper the eggs and prevent them from curdling when added to the hot soup.

  8. Gradually pour the egg and lemon mixture back into the pot, stirring gently to incorporate it into the soup. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the soup to thicken slightly.

  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to adjust the seasoning based on your preferences.

  10. Ladle the traditional Greek Avgolemono soup into bowls and garnish with fresh dill. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors of Greece.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a bay leaf and a sprinkle of dried oregano to the soup while it simmers.
  • If lamb isn’t your preferred meat, you can substitute it with chicken or beef. The soup will still be just as delicious.
  • To make the soup more filling, you can add a handful of cooked rice or chopped potatoes along with the lamb.
  • If you’re a fan of vegetables, feel free to add peas or green beans to the soup for added color and nutrition.

Final Thoughts

The traditional Greek Avgolemono soup with lamb is a classic dish that brings warmth and comfort to any meal. Its creamy texture, tangy flavor, and tender pieces of lamb make every spoonful a delight. Whether you’re a fan of Greek cuisine or looking to try something new, this soup is sure to satisfy your cravings. So, grab your apron, gather the ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a bowl of traditional Greek goodness. Opa!
