Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Recipe: A No-Bake Treat


Who doesn’t love indulging in cookie dough? It’s a guilty pleasure for many, but what if we told you that you can enjoy a guilt-free, vegan version of this classic treat? Yes, you heard it right! Today, we’re sharing a delectable Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough recipe that’s not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. The best part? It’s a no-bake recipe, so you can satisfy your sweet cravings in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups of chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup of almond butter
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/3 cup of dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, almond butter, maple syrup, vanilla extract, salt, and baking soda. Blend the ingredients until smooth and creamy.

  2. Transfer the mixture to a mixing bowl and fold in the dairy-free chocolate chips. Make sure they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  3. Using a cookie dough scoop or a spoon, portion out the cookie dough and roll it into small, bite-sized balls.

  4. Place the cookie dough balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and chill them in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to allow them to firm up.

  5. Once chilled, your Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough is ready to be enjoyed! Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, drizzle some melted dairy-free chocolate over the chilled cookie dough balls before serving.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw cashews
  • 1/2 cup oat flour
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a high-speed blender or food processor, blend the raw cashews until they reach a smooth, creamy consistency, similar to nut butter.

  2. Add the oat flour, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and sea salt to the blended cashews. Blend or process the mixture until it comes together into a smooth, uniform dough.

  3. Gently fold in the vegan chocolate chips, making sure they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  4. Transfer the cookie dough to an airtight container and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and the texture to firm up.

  5. Scoop and serve the chilled cookie dough as a delightful, guilt-free treat. Enjoy it on its own, or get creative and use it as a topping or filling for your favorite desserts.
