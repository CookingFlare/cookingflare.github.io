The Old Fashioned 2.0: A Modern Take on a Classic Bourbon Cocktail


The Old Fashioned is a timeless classic cocktail that has been enjoyed by bourbon lovers for generations. With its simple yet sophisticated combination of bourbon, sugar, bitters, and a twist of citrus, it’s no wonder that this drink has stood the test of time. But what if we told you there’s a way to elevate this classic cocktail to a whole new level? Introducing the Old Fashioned 2.0—a modern take on the traditional recipe that adds a touch of innovation and creativity to the mix.

The Ingredients

To create this modern twist on the Old Fashioned, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of your favorite bourbon
  • 1 teaspoon of maple syrup
  • 2 dashes of orange bitters
  • 1 dash of chocolate bitters
  • 1 strip of orange zest
  • 1 maraschino cherry (optional)
  • Ice cubes

The Method

  1. Start by filling an Old Fashioned glass with ice cubes. This will chill the glass and ensure that your cocktail stays cold throughout the drinking experience.

  2. In a separate mixing glass, combine the bourbon, maple syrup, orange bitters, and chocolate bitters. Stir gently with a bar spoon until the ingredients are well mixed and the maple syrup is fully dissolved.

  3. Now, it’s time to bring the flavors together. Pour the mixture over the ice in the Old Fashioned glass, allowing the flavors to meld and the drink to chill further.

  4. Take the orange zest strip and gently squeeze it over the glass, releasing its aromatic oils. Drop the strip into the glass for an extra burst of citrus fragrance and flavor.

  5. If desired, garnish your Old Fashioned 2.0 with a maraschino cherry. The addition of this sweet and tangy garnish will add a pop of color and a hint of sweetness to the cocktail.

  6. Finally, give your drink one last gentle stir to ensure that all the flavors are evenly distributed.

The Taste

The Old Fashioned 2.0 delivers a perfect balance of traditional flavors with a contemporary twist. The maple syrup adds a subtle sweetness that complements the rich and complex notes of the bourbon. The orange and chocolate bitters provide a depth of flavor and a hint of bitterness that enhances the overall taste profile. The orange zest strip adds a refreshing citrus aroma that lingers with each sip. Together, these elements create a truly memorable drinking experience that pays homage to the classic Old Fashioned while offering a modern and innovative twist.

The Final Thoughts

The Old Fashioned 2.0 is a cocktail that embodies the spirit of innovation while honoring the timeless tradition of the Old Fashioned. By incorporating maple syrup, orange bitters, and chocolate bitters, this modern take on the classic bourbon cocktail elevates the drink to new heights. So whether you’re a bourbon enthusiast looking for a fresh take on a beloved classic or simply someone who appreciates a well-crafted cocktail, give the Old Fashioned 2.0 a try. It’s a drink that combines the best of both worlds—the familiar and the new—for a truly unforgettable drinking experience. Cheers!
