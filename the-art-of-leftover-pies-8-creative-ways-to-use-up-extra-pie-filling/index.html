The Art of Leftover Pies: 8 Creative Ways to Use Up Extra Pie Filling


Are you tired of throwing away leftover pie filling after a holiday feast or special occasion? Do you struggle to come up with creative ways to use up the extra filling before it goes bad? Look no further! In this article, we’ll explore the art of leftover pies and provide you with 8 creative ways to use up extra pie filling.

The Problem with Leftover Pie Filling

We’ve all been there - you spend hours baking the perfect pie for a special occasion, only to be left with a surplus of filling after everyone’s had their fill. It’s a frustrating and wasteful situation, especially when you know that the filling could be used in a variety of delicious ways. But fear not, dear reader, for we have some solutions for you.

1. Make a Pie-Filling Frittata

One of the easiest ways to use up leftover pie filling is to make a pie-filling frittata. Simply beat some eggs, add in the leftover filling, and cook in a skillet until the eggs are set. This is a great way to use up sweet or savory fillings, and can be served for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

2. Create a Pie-Filling Crumble

Another great way to use up leftover pie filling is to make a pie-filling crumble. Simply mix the leftover filling with some sugar, flour, and spices, then top with a crumbly mixture of oats, butter, and brown sugar. Bake until the topping is golden brown, and serve with vanilla ice cream.

3. Make a Pie-Filling Galette

A pie-filling galette is a great way to use up leftover filling and can be served as a dessert or snack. Simply mix the leftover filling with some sugar and spices, then spread on a piece of puff pastry. Fold the edges of the pastry over the filling, and bake until golden brown.

4. Use Pie Filling as a Topping for Yogurt or Oatmeal

Leftover pie filling can also be used as a topping for yogurt or oatmeal. Simply spoon the filling over the top of your breakfast, and add some granola or nuts for crunch.

5. Make a Pie-Filling Cake

If you’re looking for a creative way to use up leftover pie filling, consider making a pie-filling cake. Simply mix the leftover filling with some sugar, flour, and spices, then add to a cake recipe. Top with whipped cream or frosting, and decorate with fresh fruit.

6. Create a Pie-Filling Trifle

A pie-filling trifle is a great way to use up leftover filling and can be served as a dessert or snack. Simply layer the leftover filling with some whipped cream, cake, and fruit in a large bowl or individual glasses.

7. Make a Pie-Filling Custard

If you’re looking for a creamy and rich dessert, consider making a pie-filling custard. Simply mix the leftover filling with some heavy cream, sugar, and eggs, then cook in a saucepan until thickened. Chill before serving.

8. Use Pie Filling as a Filling for Crepes

Finally, leftover pie filling can also be used as a filling for crepes. Simply spread the filling on a crepe, then top with whipped cream or fruit. Roll up the crepe and serve as a dessert or snack.

In conclusion, there are many creative ways to use up leftover pie filling. From frittatas to trifles, there’s something for everyone. So next time you’re faced with a surplus of pie filling, don’t throw it away - get creative and make something delicious!
