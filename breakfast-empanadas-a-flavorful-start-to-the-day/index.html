Breakfast Empanadas: A Flavorful Start to the Day


Are you tired of the same old breakfast routine? Looking for a delicious and satisfying way to start your day? Look no further than breakfast empanadas! These flavorful handheld treats are not only easy to make but also packed with all the nutrients you need to kickstart your morning. In this blog post, we’ll explore the art of making breakfast empanadas and provide you with some mouthwatering recipes that are sure to become your new breakfast favorites.

The Versatility of Breakfast Empanadas

One of the best things about breakfast empanadas is their versatility. You can fill them with a wide range of delicious ingredients, allowing you to create a variety of flavors to suit your taste buds. From classic combinations like bacon and eggs to more adventurous options like spinach and feta, the possibilities are endless. Whether you prefer savory or sweet, there’s a breakfast empanada recipe out there for you.

The Perfect Empanada Dough

Before we dive into the delicious filling options, let’s talk about the star of the show: the empanada dough. The key to a perfectly flaky and tender empanada crust lies in using the right ingredients and techniques. Here’s a simple recipe for empanada dough that you can use as a base for all your breakfast creations:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup cold water

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  2. Add the cold butter to the bowl and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the cold water to the bowl, mixing with a fork until the dough comes together.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently until it forms a smooth ball.
  5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before using.

Savory Breakfast Empanadas

Now that you have your empanada dough ready, let’s explore some savory breakfast empanada recipes that are sure to satisfy your morning cravings.

Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Empanadas

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 eggs, scrambled
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Roll out the empanada dough to about 1/8-inch thickness and cut out circles using a round cookie cutter.
  3. Place a spoonful of scrambled eggs, crumbled bacon, and shredded cheese onto each dough circle.
  4. Fold the dough over the filling and seal the edges by pressing them together with a fork.
  5. Transfer the empanadas to the prepared baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Allow the empanadas to cool slightly before serving.

Spinach and Feta Empanadas

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped red onion and minced garlic to the skillet and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the crumbled feta cheese.
  5. Roll out the empanada dough and cut out circles.
  6. Spoon the spinach and feta mixture onto each dough circle, fold over, and seal the edges.
  7. Place the empanadas on a baking sheet and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

Sweet Breakfast Empanadas

If you have a sweet tooth, you’ll love these delectable sweet breakfast empanadas that are perfect for a morning treat.

Apple Cinnamon Empanadas

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups diced apples
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the diced apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, and cornstarch.
  2. Roll out the empanada dough and cut out circles.
  3. Spoon the apple mixture onto each dough circle, fold over, and seal the edges.
  4. Place the empanadas on a baking sheet and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

Chocolate-Banana Empanadas

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the mashed bananas, chocolate chips, honey, and vanilla extract.
  2. Roll out the empanada dough and cut out circles.
  3. Spoon the banana-chocolate mixture onto each dough circle, fold over, and seal the edges.
  4. Place the empanadas on a baking sheet and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

Enjoy Your Flavorful Start to the Day!

There you have it, a variety of delicious breakfast empanadas to satisfy your morning cravings. Whether you prefer savory or sweet, there’s a filling combination out there for everyone. So why not break away from the usual breakfast routine and start your day with a flavorful twist? Give these breakfast empanada recipes a try and enjoy a delicious and satisfying start to your day. Bon appétit!
