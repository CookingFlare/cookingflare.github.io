Baked Chicken and Rice: A One-Pan Comfort Meal


There’s something truly comforting about a delicious one-pan meal. The simplicity of throwing everything together and letting the oven work its magic is pure bliss. And when it comes to comfort food, it’s hard to beat the classic combination of tender chicken and fluffy rice. Today, I’m going to share with you my recipe for Baked Chicken and Rice – a one-pan wonder that will leave you satisfied and longing for seconds.

Ingredients

For this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grab a large oven-safe skillet or baking dish – this will be your one-pan wonder.

  2. Season the chicken thighs generously with salt, pepper, paprika, dried thyme, and dried oregano. Set them aside while you prepare the rest of the ingredients.

  3. In the same skillet or baking dish, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced red bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet or baking dish, creating a space for the chicken thighs. Place the seasoned chicken thighs skin-side down and sear them for about 5 minutes, or until the skin is golden brown and crispy.

  5. Once the chicken thighs are nicely seared, flip them over and add the rice, chicken broth, and diced tomatoes to the skillet or baking dish. Give everything a good stir to combine the flavors.

  6. Cover the skillet or baking dish tightly with aluminum foil or a lid if you have one. Transfer it to the preheated oven and let the magic happen. Bake for 45 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through, the rice is tender, and the flavors have melded together in perfect harmony.

  7. Once the cooking time is up, remove the skillet or baking dish from the oven. Carefully remove the foil or lid to release the mouthwatering aroma that has been trapped inside. Sprinkle some fresh parsley over the top for a pop of color and freshness.

  8. Serve the Baked Chicken and Rice straight from the skillet or baking dish, allowing everyone to help themselves to this comforting feast. The tender chicken, flavorful rice, and aromatic vegetables will make your taste buds dance with delight.

Conclusion

Baked Chicken and Rice is the epitome of a one-pan comfort meal. With its simple preparation and incredible flavors, it’s a dish that will warm your heart and nourish your soul. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab your favorite skillet or baking dish and let the oven work its magic. Get ready to indulge in a symphony of flavors – a truly comforting and satisfying meal that will leave you craving more.

Enjoy!
