Chocolate Mint Ice Cream: Refreshingly Chocolaty


Indulge in the cool and creamy goodness of homemade Chocolate Mint Ice Cream. This refreshing treat is perfect for those hot summer days or whenever you’re in need of a sweet pick-me-up. With its rich chocolate flavor and refreshing hint of mint, this ice cream is sure to become a favorite in your household. Plus, making it from scratch means you can customize the intensity of the flavors to suit your preferences. So, let’s dive right in and learn how to make this delectable dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • Green food coloring (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, milk, sugar, and salt. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it reaches a gentle simmer. Remove from heat.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks until they are pale and frothy. Slowly pour about 1/2 cup of the hot cream mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly. This will temper the eggs and prevent them from curdling.

  3. Gradually pour the egg yolk mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining cream mixture, stirring constantly. Return the saucepan to medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Do not let the mixture boil.

  4. Once thickened, remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the vanilla and peppermint extracts. If desired, add a few drops of green food coloring to achieve the classic mint color. Stir until well combined.

  5. Pour the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl to remove any lumps or cooked egg bits. Let it cool at room temperature for about 15-20 minutes, then cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until chilled.

  6. Once the mixture is chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. During the last couple of minutes of churning, add the chocolate chips and let them mix evenly throughout the ice cream.

  7. Transfer the churned ice cream into an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

  8. When you’re ready to serve, scoop the Chocolate Mint Ice Cream into bowls or cones, and savor each luscious bite!

Tips:

  • For an extra burst of minty flavor, crush a few candy canes or peppermint candies and sprinkle them on top of the ice cream before serving.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can blend the finished ice cream in a blender or food processor for a few seconds before transferring it to the freezer.
  • To make this recipe dairy-free, you can substitute the heavy cream and whole milk with coconut milk or almond milk. Just make sure to adjust the quantities accordingly.

Conclusion:

There you have it – a simple yet incredibly satisfying recipe for homemade Chocolate Mint Ice Cream. With its velvety texture and refreshing taste, this frozen treat is sure to impress family and friends alike. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with a warm brownie or a crisp waffle cone, this ice cream is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. So, why wait? Gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and embark on a delightful ice cream-making adventure that will leave you craving for more!
