Rich and Nutty Hazelnut Chocolate Cupcakes: A Decadent Treat


Indulge your taste buds in the rich and nutty goodness of Hazelnut Chocolate Cupcakes. These decadent treats are perfect for any occasion, from birthdays to afternoon tea parties. With a moist and fluffy chocolate base and a creamy hazelnut frosting, these cupcakes are sure to impress even the most discerning dessert lovers.

Ingredients:

For the cupcakes:

  • 1 and ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup strong brewed coffee, cooled

For the hazelnut frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup finely chopped toasted hazelnuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring each egg is fully incorporated before adding the next one. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Alternate adding the dry ingredients and the milk to the butter mixture, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.

  6. Stir in the cooled brewed coffee. The batter will be thin, but don’t worry, that’s perfectly normal.

  7. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling them about two-thirds full.

  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  9. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for about 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the hazelnut frosting. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.

  11. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing well after each addition.

  12. Add the heavy cream and vanilla extract and continue to beat until the frosting is light and fluffy.

  13. Gently fold in the finely chopped toasted hazelnuts.

  14. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them generously with the hazelnut frosting.

  15. For an extra touch, you can sprinkle some additional chopped hazelnuts on top of the frosting.

  16. Serve and enjoy the rich and nutty Hazelnut Chocolate Cupcakes!

These cupcakes are a true delight for all chocolate and hazelnut lovers. The combination of the moist chocolate base and the creamy hazelnut frosting creates a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. Whether you’re hosting a special occasion or simply want to treat yourself, these cupcakes are sure to impress. Give them a try and let your taste buds experience the decadence of Rich and Nutty Hazelnut Chocolate Cupcakes!
