Mediterranean-Inspired Pasta Salad Recipes for Summer


As the summer sun shines bright and the warm breeze fills the air, there’s nothing quite like a refreshing pasta salad to complement the season. In this blog post, we’ll take a journey to the Mediterranean and explore some delectable pasta salad recipes inspired by the vibrant flavors of this region. From tangy olives to ripe tomatoes, and from savory feta to aromatic herbs, these recipes will transport your taste buds to the shores of the Mediterranean.

Greek Orzo Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups orzo pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/3 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the orzo according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the orzo, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and parsley.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss gently to coat.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

Tuscan Tortellini Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb cheese tortellini
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup artichoke hearts, chopped
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chiffonade
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup balsamic vinaigrette
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the tortellini according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the tortellini, sun-dried tomatoes, artichoke hearts, roasted red peppers, pine nuts, basil, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Drizzle the balsamic vinaigrette over the pasta salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before serving.

Mediterranean Pasta Salad with Lemon-Herb Vinaigrette

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz rotini pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup English cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/3 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rotini pasta according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, black olives, parsley, mint, and feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, red wine vinegar, honey, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the lemon-herb vinaigrette over the pasta salad and toss gently to coat.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to develop.

Conclusion

These Mediterranean-inspired pasta salad recipes are bursting with fresh flavors and are perfect for any summer gathering. Whether you’re hosting a picnic, a barbecue, or simply enjoying a meal al fresco, these vibrant and satisfying salads will surely be a hit. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of the Mediterranean, and savor the taste of summer with these delightful pasta salad creations!
