Soft and Chewy Pretzel Rolls Recipe


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying bread experience? Look no further than these homemade Soft and Chewy Pretzel Rolls. These rolls are a delightful blend of softness and chewiness, with that distinct pretzel flavor that we all love. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a newbie in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests. Let’s dive into the steps to create these mouthwatering pretzel rolls!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 10 cups water
  • 2/3 cup baking soda
  • 1 large egg yolk beaten with 1 tablespoon water
  • Coarse sea salt, for sprinkling

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and salt. Sprinkle the yeast on top and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes foamy. Add the flour and melted butter, then mix until the dough comes together.

  2. Knead the Dough: Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it’s smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

  3. Shape the Rolls: Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and bring the 10 cups of water and baking soda to a boil in a large pot. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into equal-sized pieces. Roll each piece into a ball and make a deep slash on top.

  4. Pretzel Bath: Boil each dough ball in the baking soda water for about 30 seconds per side. Using a slotted spoon, remove them from the water and place them on a lined baking sheet.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Brush each roll with the egg yolk wash and sprinkle with coarse sea salt. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-14 minutes or until they turn golden brown. Let them cool slightly before serving.

  6. Enjoy the Deliciousness: Once the pretzel rolls are baked to perfection, serve them warm with your favorite dips or spreads. These Soft and Chewy Pretzel Rolls are perfect for sandwiches, burgers, or just enjoyed on their own.

Now that you have mastered the art of making Soft and Chewy Pretzel Rolls, get ready to impress your family and friends with this delightful treat. The combination of soft interior and chewy crust makes these rolls a perfect addition to any meal or gathering. Give this recipe a try and elevate your bread game to a whole new level!

Remember, the key to success with this recipe lies in patience and precision. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and let the aroma of freshly baked pretzel rolls fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
