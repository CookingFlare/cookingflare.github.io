Creative Thanksgiving Leftover Sushi Rolls


Thanksgiving is a time for indulging in delicious food and spending quality time with loved ones. But what happens when the feast is over, and you’re left with a fridge full of leftovers? Don’t let those Thanksgiving goodies go to waste! Get creative and turn them into something completely unexpected – sushi rolls!

Sushi rolls are a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste preferences. By incorporating Thanksgiving leftovers, you can add a unique twist to this classic Japanese delicacy. So, let’s dive in and explore some creative Thanksgiving leftover sushi roll ideas that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

Turkey and Cranberry Sushi Roll

Ingredients:

  • 4 nori sheets
  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 1 cup leftover roasted turkey, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cranberry sauce
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Soy sauce, for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Lay a nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat or a clean kitchen towel.
  2. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori sheet, leaving a small border at the top.
  3. Place slices of roasted turkey on top of the rice, followed by a drizzle of cranberry sauce.
  4. Add a layer of cream cheese and avocado slices.
  5. Carefully roll the sushi, using the bamboo mat or towel to help create a tight roll.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Slice the sushi rolls into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife.
  8. Serve with soy sauce for dipping and enjoy!

Sweet Potato and Green Bean Sushi Roll

Ingredients:

  • 4 nori sheets
  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 1 cup leftover roasted sweet potatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup leftover green beans
  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • Sriracha sauce, to taste
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Soy sauce, for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Lay a nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat or a clean kitchen towel.
  2. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori sheet, leaving a small border at the top.
  3. Place slices of roasted sweet potatoes and green beans on top of the rice.
  4. Drizzle with mayo and a touch of sriracha sauce for a spicy kick.
  5. Carefully roll the sushi, using the bamboo mat or towel to help create a tight roll.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top of the sushi rolls for added crunch and visual appeal.
  8. Slice the sushi rolls into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife.
  9. Serve with soy sauce for dipping and enjoy!

Stuffing and Gravy Sushi Roll

Ingredients:

  • 4 nori sheets
  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 1 cup leftover stuffing
  • 1 cup leftover turkey gravy
  • 1/4 cup crispy fried onions
  • Chives, for garnish
  • Soy sauce, for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Lay a nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat or a clean kitchen towel.
  2. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori sheet, leaving a small border at the top.
  3. Place a layer of leftover stuffing on top of the rice.
  4. Drizzle with turkey gravy and sprinkle crispy fried onions on top.
  5. Carefully roll the sushi, using the bamboo mat or towel to help create a tight roll.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Garnish the sushi rolls with chopped chives for a pop of color.
  8. Slice the sushi rolls into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife.
  9. Serve with soy sauce for dipping and enjoy!

Conclusion

Don’t let your Thanksgiving leftovers go to waste! Get creative in the kitchen and transform them into mouthwatering sushi rolls. Whether you opt for the Turkey and Cranberry, Sweet Potato and Green Bean, or the Stuffing and Gravy sushi roll, these creative combinations will take your taste buds on an unexpected journey. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your leftovers, and embark on a culinary adventure that will leave you craving more.
