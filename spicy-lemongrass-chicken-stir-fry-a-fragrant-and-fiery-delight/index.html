Spicy Lemongrass Chicken Stir Fry: A Fragrant and Fiery Delight


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and aromatic spices, then this Spicy Lemongrass Chicken Stir Fry is the perfect dish to tantalize your taste buds. This recipe combines the zesty brightness of lemongrass with the fiery kick of chili peppers, creating a harmonious balance of flavors that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this dish is sure to impress with its complex yet accessible taste profile.

Ingredients

To begin this culinary adventure, gather the following ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 3 stalks lemongrass, white parts only, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
  • 1 green chili, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Method

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

Begin by marinating the thinly sliced chicken breast with the minced garlic, chopped lemongrass, fish sauce, soy sauce, and black pepper. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 2: Prepare the Stir Fry

Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken and stir-fry until it is cooked through. Remove the chicken from the wok and set it aside.

In the same wok, add a little more oil if needed, then stir-fry the sliced onion, red and green chilies, and bell peppers until they are slightly softened but still retain their crunch.

Step 3: Bring It All Together

Return the cooked chicken to the wok and add the oyster sauce, sugar, and chicken broth. Stir-fry everything together until the sauce coats the chicken and vegetables evenly.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Once everything is cooked to perfection, transfer the Spicy Lemongrass Chicken Stir Fry to a serving dish and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and added aroma. This fragrant and fiery dish pairs beautifully with steamed jasmine rice or crispy noodles.

Final Thoughts

This Spicy Lemongrass Chicken Stir Fry is a celebration of vibrant flavors and aromatic spices that will take your taste buds on a journey. The combination of zesty lemongrass, fiery chilies, and savory chicken creates a delightful harmony that is both comforting and adventurous. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or friends, this recipe is a surefire way to impress and satisfy any craving for bold and fragrant cuisine.

So, roll up your sleeves, gather those aromatic lemongrass stalks, and embark on a culinary adventure that will bring the flavors of Southeast Asia right to your kitchen. Let the sizzle of the wok and the aroma of lemongrass transport you to a world of fragrant and fiery delight.
