Savory Chicken and Bacon Wrap: A Perfect Combination


As the sun sets and the day winds down, there’s nothing quite like curling up with a warm, satisfying meal that hits all the right spots. And what better way to do that than with a savory chicken and bacon wrap? The combination of juicy chicken, crispy bacon, and fresh vegetables wrapped up in a warm tortilla is the perfect remedy for a long day.

The Inspiration

I first tried this recipe on a whim, tossing together some leftover chicken, bacon, and veggies to create a quick and easy snack. But as I took that first bite, I knew I had stumbled upon something special. The smoky flavor of the bacon paired perfectly with the tender chicken, and the crunch of the lettuce and tomato added a delightful textural element. It was love at first bite, and I knew I had to share this recipe with the world.

The Recipe

To make this mouthwatering wrap, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomato
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large tortillas
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

The Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced chicken, crumbled bacon, shredded lettuce, diced tomato, and diced red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and dried thyme. Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture and toss to combine.
  3. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  5. Spoon about 1/2 cup of the chicken mixture onto the center of each tortilla, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
  6. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the top of the filling, if using.
  7. Fold the bottom edge of the tortilla up over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll the wrap up tightly.
  8. Repeat with the remaining ingredients to make three more wraps.

The Result

The finished wraps are a true masterpiece. The chicken and bacon are perfectly balanced, with the crunch of the lettuce and tomato adding a delightful textural element. The mayonnaise and mustard add a tangy, creamy element, while the thyme provides a subtle depth of flavor. And let’s not forget the cheese – melted and gooey, it’s the perfect finishing touch.

The Verdict

This savory chicken and bacon wrap is a game-changer. It’s the perfect combination of flavors and textures, and it’s incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy lunch or a satisfying dinner, this recipe is sure to hit the spot. So go ahead, give it a try, and experience the magic for yourself.

Tips and Variations

  • For an added kick, add some diced jalapenos or hot sauce to the filling.
  • Swap out the chicken for cooked turkey or ham for a different twist.
  • Add some crispy chopped bacon bits on top of the wrap for extra crunch.
  • Use different types of cheese, such as cheddar or Swiss, for a unique flavor.

I hope you enjoy this recipe as much as I do! Let me know in the comments if you have any questions or if you’d like any variations or substitutions. Happy cooking!
