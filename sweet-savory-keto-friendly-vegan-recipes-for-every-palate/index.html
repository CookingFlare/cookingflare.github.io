Sweet & Savory: Keto-Friendly Vegan Recipes for Every Palate


Who says being vegan and keto-friendly has to be boring or bland? These carefully crafted recipes will tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more. So, buckle up and prepare for a culinary adventure because we are about to take you on a sweet and savory journey like no other.

Keto-Friendly Vegan Sushi Rolls

Who doesn’t love sushi? The silky rice, the firmness of the fish, and the perfect balance of flavors. We’re excited to share our own low-carb, vegan twist on Japanese tradition.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cauliflower rice
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 sheets nori
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • Soy sauce (tamari for Keto)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Combine cauliflower rice and rice vinegar in a microwave-safe dish and microwave for 3 minutes. Allow it to cool.
  2. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi roller and spread a thin layer of cauliflower rice evenly across the sheet.
  3. Lay the sliced vegetables, avocado, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds over the rice.
  4. Tightly roll the bamboo roller, and with a wet knife, slice the sushi rolls to the desired thickness.
  5. You have the option to place the sushi rolls in the refrigerator for a few minutes to make them firm before serving.
  6. Serve with soy sauce (tamari for Keto) and enjoy!

Almond Joy Muffins

Fulfill your chocolate cravings the healthy way with these delectable almond joy inspired muffins.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 flax eggs (2 tbsp ground flaxseed mixed with 6 tbsp water)
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup chopped almonds
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • Optional: Dark chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with 12 muffin liners.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together almond flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate mixing bowl, combine the flax eggs, almond milk, maple syrup, and melted coconut oil. Mix well.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, folding in the chopped almonds, shredded coconut, and dark chocolate chips if using.
  5. Divide the mixture evenly among the muffin liners and bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
  6. Remove from oven, let it cool, and dig in!

Cauliflower Mac and Cheese

Who could ever forget the comforting taste of mac and cheese, the gooey and delicious cheese drawing you in, offering warmth and comfort?

Ingredients:

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 1/2 cup vegan cheese shreds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the cauliflower florets in salted water for about 8-10 minutes or until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, combine almond milk, nutritional yeast, cornstarch, turmeric, paprika, garlic powder, and black pepper. Whisk together until cornstarch is dissolved.
  3. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir the mixture constantly until it thickens. This should take about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the vegan cheese shreds (optional) and mustard powder.
  5. Toss the cauliflower in the cheese sauce and mix well.
  6. Drizzle a bit of olive oil in an oven-safe dish, then transfer the cauliflower mac and cheese to the dish and bake for 10-15 minutes at 350°F (175°C), until the top is slightly golden and bubbly.

Summing it Up

These Keto-Friendly vegan recipes will bring a whole new flavor experience to your plate. Why limit yourself when it comes to food? So, break free from dietary constraints and explore this fusion of cuisines. Sweet and savory, let your taste buds rejoice in this delightful culinary journey! Enjoy the ride!
