Grilled Caesar Wrap: A Portable Twist on a Classic


If you’re a fan of the timeless flavors of a Caesar salad, then get ready to experience a whole new level of deliciousness with our Grilled Caesar Wrap recipe. This portable twist on a classic combines the familiar taste of a Caesar salad with the convenience of a handheld wrap, making it perfect for a quick lunch or a satisfying dinner. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

For the Grilled Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Caesar Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional Ingredients:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 4 cups chopped Romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup croutons

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Grilled Chicken

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Allow the chicken to rest for a few minutes, then slice it into thin strips.

Step 2: Make the Caesar Dressing

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, minced garlic, grated Parmesan cheese, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined.

Step 3: Assemble the Grilled Caesar Wraps

  1. Lay out the flour tortillas and spread a generous amount of Caesar dressing over each one.
  2. Divide the chopped Romaine lettuce evenly among the tortillas, placing it in the center.
  3. Arrange the grilled chicken strips on top of the lettuce.
  4. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and croutons over the chicken.

Step 4: Grill the Wraps

  1. Fold the bottom edge of each tortilla up over the filling, then fold in the sides, and roll tightly to enclose the ingredients.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and place the wraps seam-side down.
  3. Grill each wrap for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.

Serving Suggestions

Once the wraps are grilled to perfection, you can slice them in half and serve with additional Caesar dressing for dipping, or enjoy them as they are. These Grilled Caesar Wraps are not only packed with flavor but also offer a satisfying crunch from the croutons and a creamy richness from the dressing. They are versatile enough to be served as a main dish or cut into smaller portions for an appetizer.

Final Thoughts

The Grilled Caesar Wrap offers a delightful marriage of classic Caesar salad ingredients in a more convenient and portable form. Whether you’re looking for a quick and flavorful meal to enjoy on the go or a creative twist on a familiar dish, this recipe is sure to become a regular favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible flavors of this delectable Grilled Caesar Wrap.
