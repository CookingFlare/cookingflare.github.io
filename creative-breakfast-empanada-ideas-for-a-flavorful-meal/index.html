Creative Breakfast Empanada Ideas for a Flavorful Meal


When it comes to breakfast, we often find ourselves reaching for the same old options day in and day out. But what if we could shake things up and add a little creativity to our morning routine? That’s where breakfast empanadas come in! These handheld delights are not only delicious but also versatile, making them the perfect canvas for a flavorful morning meal.

Empanada Dough

Before we dive into the creative breakfast empanada fillings, let’s talk about the star of the show: the empanada dough. While you can certainly make your own dough from scratch, store-bought dough works just as well and saves time. Look for pre-made empanada dough in the freezer section of your local grocery store or use refrigerated pie crust as a convenient alternative.

Scrambled Egg and Chorizo Empanadas

One of the most classic breakfast flavor combinations is scrambled eggs and chorizo. To make these savory empanadas, start by cooking chorizo in a skillet until it’s browned and crumbly. In a separate pan, scramble some eggs with a sprinkle of salt and pepper. Once the chorizo and eggs are cooked, fill each empanada dough round with a spoonful of the mixture, fold it over, and crimp the edges with a fork. After a quick bake in the oven, you’ll have a delicious breakfast empanada with a spicy kick.

Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Empanadas

For a breakfast empanada that’s sure to please a crowd, consider the classic combination of bacon, egg, and cheese. Cook crispy bacon until it’s golden and crunchy, then chop it into small pieces. Scramble some eggs and fold in shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese. Spoon the bacon and egg mixture onto the empanada dough rounds, fold, and bake until golden brown. These empanadas are the epitome of comfort food and are perfect for a leisurely weekend brunch.

Southwest Veggie Breakfast Empanadas

If you’re looking for a vegetarian option that’s still packed with flavor, consider making southwest veggie breakfast empanadas. Sauté a mix of bell peppers, onions, and black beans with a blend of cumin, chili powder, and a pinch of cayenne for a smoky, spicy flavor. Once the veggies are tender, spoon them onto the empanada dough rounds and bake until the crust is golden and flaky. These empanadas are a great way to start your day with a burst of vibrant flavors.

Sweet Cream Cheese and Berry Empanadas

For those who have a sweet tooth in the morning, sweet cream cheese and berry empanadas are the perfect treat. Mix softened cream cheese with a touch of vanilla extract and a sprinkle of sugar. Spoon the cream cheese mixture onto the empanada dough rounds, then add a spoonful of your favorite berries, such as blueberries or raspberries. Fold, crimp, and bake until the empanadas are golden and the filling is oozing with sweetness. These empanadas are a delightful way to indulge in a sweet breakfast without being overly decadent.

Conclusion

Breakfast empanadas offer a versatile and flavorful way to start your day. Whether you prefer savory or sweet, there’s a breakfast empanada to suit every palate. Experiment with different fillings and get creative in the kitchen to elevate your breakfast game. With their portability and delicious fillings, breakfast empanadas are sure to become a new favorite in your morning routine. So, next time you’re pondering what to make for breakfast, consider whipping up a batch of these delightful handheld treats.
