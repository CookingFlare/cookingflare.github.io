One Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta

If you’re a fan of spicy and creamy dishes, then this One Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta is a must-try! This dish combines tender chicken, flavorful sausage, and al dente pasta smothered in a rich and creamy Cajun sauce. The best part? It all comes together in just one pot, making clean-up a breeze. So, let’s dive right into this delicious recipe! Ingredients 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces 1 pound smoked sausage, sliced 1 onion, diced 1 red bell pepper, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 2 cups chicken broth 1 can diced tomatoes, undrained 1 cup heavy cream 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme Salt and pepper, to taste 12 ounces penne pasta Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish) Instructions Heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the chicken to the pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside. In the same pot, add the sausage and cook until browned. Remove the sausage from the pot and set aside. Add the diced onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes. Return the chicken and sausage to the pot. Add the chicken broth, diced tomatoes (with their juice), heavy cream, Cajun seasoning, paprika, oregano, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Simmer for 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Add the penne pasta to the pot and stir to combine. Cover the pot and cook for an additional 10-12 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked al dente. Remove the pot from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes to allow the sauce to thicken. Serve the One Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta garnished with fresh parsley. Tips and Variations For an extra kick of heat, add some chopped jalapenos or a dash of hot sauce to the dish. Feel free to mix up the protein by using shrimp or a combination of chicken and shrimp. If you prefer a lighter option, you can use half-and-half or milk instead of heavy cream. Just keep in mind that the sauce won’t be as rich and creamy. Don’t forget to adjust the Cajun seasoning to your taste. If you like it spicier, add more; if you prefer a milder flavor, reduce the amount. Conclusion This One Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will satisfy your cravings for a hearty and comforting meal. With just one pot and a handful of ingredients, you can whip up a delicious dinner that will impress your family and friends. So, give this recipe a try and enjoy the spicy, creamy goodness that awaits you! ...
