Elevate Your BBQ: 10 Mouthwatering Grilled Meat Recipes


BBQ season is finally here! There’s nothing quite like the sweet and smoky aroma of grilled meats wafting through the air, enticing everyone to gather ‘round the grill. But, let’s be real, not all BBQ dishes are created equal. To truly elevate your BBQ game, you need recipes that are packed with flavor, tender, and juicy. That’s why we’ve curated a list of 10 mouthwatering grilled meat recipes that are sure to impress your friends and family.

Recipe 1: Korean-Style BBQ Beef Short Ribs

  • 2 lbs beef short ribs
  • 1/4 cup Gochujang sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp crushed black pepper
  • 1/4 cup green onions, thinly sliced

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, whisk together Gochujang sauce, soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, sesame oil, and black pepper. Place short ribs in a large ziplock bag and pour marinade over them. Seal bag and massage to coat evenly. Let marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Grill ribs for 5-7 minutes per side, or until caramelized and tender. Serve with green onions and additional Gochujang sauce for dipping.

Recipe 2: Chipotle Lime Chicken Breasts

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, whisk together chipotle peppers, lime juice, olive oil, garlic, and oregano. Place chicken breasts in a large ziplock bag and pour marinade over them. Seal bag and massage to coat evenly. Let marinate for at least 30 minutes. Grill chicken for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Serve with chopped cilantro and additional lime wedges.

Recipe 3: Garlic Herb Crusted Pork Chops

  • 4 pork chops
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper. Brush both sides of pork chops with olive oil. Sprinkle garlic herb mixture evenly over both sides of pork chops. Grill pork chops for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Recipe 4: Jamaican Jerk Chicken Thighs

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup jerk seasoning
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together jerk seasoning, brown sugar, soy sauce, lime juice, olive oil, and ginger. Place chicken thighs in a large ziplock bag and pour marinade over them. Seal bag and massage to coat evenly. Let marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Grill chicken for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Serve with chopped scallions and additional jerk seasoning for serving.

Recipe 5: Bacon Wrapped Filet Mignon

  • 4 filet mignon steaks
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper. Brush both sides of filet mignon with olive oil mixture. Wrap each filet with 3 slices of bacon, securing with toothpicks if necessary. Grill filets for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked to desired level of doneness. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Recipe 6: Spicy Shrimp Skewers

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, cumin, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper. Place shrimp in a large ziplock bag and pour marinade over them. Seal bag and massage to coat evenly. Let marinate for at least 30 minutes. Grill shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until pink and cooked through. Serve with chopped cilantro and additional lime wedges.

Recipe 7: Grilled Flank Steak with Chimichurri

  • 1 flank steak
  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 cup fresh oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together parsley, oregano, garlic, red wine vinegar, and olive oil. Place flank steak in a large ziplock bag and pour marinade over it. Seal bag and massage to coat evenly. Let marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Grill flank steak for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked to desired level of doneness. Serve with additional chimichurri sauce for dipping.

Recipe 8: Grilled Lamb Chops with Lemon and Rosemary

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together lemon juice, olive oil, and rosemary. Brush both sides of lamb chops with lemon mixture. Grill lamb chops for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked to desired level of doneness. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Recipe 9: Korean-Style BBQ Pork Belly

  • 2 pounds pork belly
  • 1/4 cup Gochujang sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp crushed black pepper
  • 1/4 cup green onions, thinly sliced

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, whisk together Gochujang sauce, soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, sesame oil, and black pepper. Place pork belly in a large ziplock bag and pour marinade over it. Seal bag and massage to coat evenly. Let marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Grill pork belly for 5-7 minutes per side, or until caramelized and tender. Serve with green onions and additional Gochujang sauce for dipping.

Recipe 10: Grilled Tri-Tip with Horseradish Crust

  • 1 tri-tip roast
  • 2 tbsp horseradish
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together horseradish, Dijon mustard, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Rub horseradish crust all over tri-tip roast. Grill tri-tip for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked to desired level of doneness. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving with chopped parsley.

There you have it - 10 mouthwatering grilled meat recipes to elevate your BBQ game. Whether you’re a meat lover or just looking for some new inspiration, these recipes are sure to impress. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to indulge in some seriously delicious BBQ!
