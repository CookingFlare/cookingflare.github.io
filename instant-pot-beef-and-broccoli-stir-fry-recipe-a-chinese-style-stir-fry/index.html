Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Recipe: A Chinese-Style Stir-Fry


If you’re in the mood for a quick, flavorful, and satisfying meal, look no further than this Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry! This Chinese-style dish is a beloved classic that brings together tender beef, crisp broccoli, and a savory, umami-packed sauce. The best part? With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have this delectable stir-fry on the table in no time, making it perfect for busy weeknights or leisurely weekends.

Ingredients

For the Beef Marinade:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Shaoxing wine or dry sherry
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

For the Stir-Fry Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup beef broth

Additional Ingredients:

  • 1 pound broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Cooked white rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Beef:

    • In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced flank steak with soy sauce, Shaoxing wine or dry sherry, minced garlic, cornstarch, and sesame oil. Toss to coat the beef thoroughly, then let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Stir-Fry Sauce:

    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the low-sodium soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, grated ginger, cornstarch, and beef broth until the cornstarch is fully dissolved. Set the sauce aside.

  3. Sauté the Beef and Broccoli:

    • Select the “Sauté” function on your Instant Pot and heat the vegetable oil. Once hot, add the marinated beef slices in a single layer and cook until browned on both sides. Transfer the beef to a plate and set aside.
    • Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for a few minutes until they begin to turn bright green.

  4. Pressure Cook:

    • Return the browned beef to the Instant Pot with the sautéed broccoli. Pour the prepared stir-fry sauce over the beef and broccoli, ensuring everything is well coated.
    • Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Pressure cook on high for 8 minutes, then quick release the pressure.

  5. Thicken the Sauce:

    • Once the pressure has been released, remove the lid and select the “Sauté” function again. Allow the sauce to simmer and thicken for a few minutes, stirring occasionally, until it reaches your desired consistency.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Garnish the beef and broccoli stir-fry with sliced green onions and serve over cooked white rice. Enjoy the delightful combination of tender beef, vibrant broccoli, and rich, savory sauce!

Tips for Success

  • Slice the Beef Thinly: For the most tender results, be sure to slice the flank steak against the grain into thin strips.
  • Adjust the Seasoning: Feel free to adjust the amount of brown sugar and soy sauce in the stir-fry sauce to suit your taste preferences.
  • Serve with Rice or Noodles: This beef and broccoli stir-fry is delicious on its own, but it’s even more satisfying when served over steamed rice or noodles.

Now that you have this fantastic Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe in your arsenal, you’re well-equipped to whip up a delicious and satisfying Chinese-style meal at a moment’s notice. Enjoy the convenience and incredible flavors of this iconic dish, all thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot!
