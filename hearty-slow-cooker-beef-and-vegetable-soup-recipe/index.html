Hearty Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Soup Recipe


As the chill of winter settles in, there’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of soup to comfort the soul. This Hearty Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Soup is the perfect solution for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. With its rich flavors, tender beef, and an abundance of vegetables, it’s a meal that satisfies both the palate and the heart. Best of all, it requires minimal effort, allowing you to enjoy the delicious aroma wafting through your home while you go about your day.

Ingredients

To make this delightful soup, gather the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast (or any cut suitable for slow cooking), cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 3 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, diced (Yukon Gold or red potatoes work well)
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes, with juices
  • 6 cups beef broth (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Optional Add-Ins

Feel free to customize your soup with additional ingredients. Here are some suggestions:

  • Peas: Add a cup of frozen peas for a pop of color and sweetness.
  • Corn: A cup of frozen corn can enhance the soup’s texture and flavor.
  • Parsnips: Substitute parsnips for a unique twist on traditional flavors.
  • Mushrooms: Sautéed mushrooms can add umami depth to the soup.

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Searing the Beef

Start by searing the beef to enhance its flavor. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef cubes in batches, searing each side until browned (approximately 3-4 minutes per side). This step is optional, but it adds a delicious depth to your soup. Once browned, transfer the beef to the slow cooker.

Step 2: Sautéing the Vegetables

In the same skillet, add the diced onion, minced garlic, sliced carrots, and chopped celery. Sauté for about 5 minutes or until the vegetables begin to soften. This step not only releases the flavors of the vegetables but also deglazes the pan, capturing any browned bits left behind by the beef. Add the sautéed vegetables to the slow cooker.

Step 3: Adding Remaining Ingredients

Now that your beef and vegetables are ready, it’s time to add the remaining ingredients. Add the diced potatoes, green beans, diced tomatoes with their juices, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, dried thyme, dried oregano, bay leaf, salt, and pepper to the slow cooker. Stir everything together to combine.

Step 4: Slow Cooking

Set your slow cooker to low and allow your soup to cook for 8 hours. If you’re short on time, you can also set it to high and cook for about 4 hours. The long cooking time will allow the flavors to meld beautifully, resulting in a rich and hearty soup.

Step 5: Final Touches

Once the cooking time is up, taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Remove the bay leaf before serving. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this Hearty Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Soup with a side of crusty bread or homemade biscuits for a complete meal. The bread is perfect for dipping and soaking up the flavorful broth.

Storage

If you have leftovers, store the soup in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. The flavors will continue to develop as it sits. To reheat, simply warm it on the stovetop or in the microwave until heated through.

Freezing

This soup freezes exceptionally well. Allow it to cool completely before transferring it to freezer-safe containers. It can be frozen for up to 3 months. When you’re ready to enjoy it again, thaw overnight in the refrigerator and reheat on the stovetop.

Nutritional Benefits

This Hearty Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Soup is not only delicious but also packed with nutritional benefits. Here’s a breakdown of some key ingredients:

  • Beef: A great source of protein, iron, and essential vitamins.
  • Vegetables: Carrots, celery, and potatoes are rich in vitamins A and C, fiber, and various antioxidants.
  • Tomatoes: Packed with lycopene, tomatoes are beneficial for heart health and have anti-inflammatory properties.

Conclusion

This Hearty Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Soup recipe is a true comfort food that brings warmth and nourishment to your table. With minimal prep time and the magic of slow cooking, you’ll have a deliciously satisfying meal ready to enjoy. Perfect for family dinners or meal prep, this soup is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients, set your slow cooker, and let the wonderful aroma of homemade soup fill your home!
