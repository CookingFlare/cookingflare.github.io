Spicy Mexican Street Corn Salad: A Tangy and Crunchy Side


If you’re a fan of Mexican street food, get ready to tantalize your taste buds with this delicious and vibrant Spicy Mexican Street Corn Salad. Bursting with flavors and textures, this dish is a perfect accompaniment to any summer barbecue or gathering. Not only is it incredibly easy to make, but it also adds a zesty twist to the traditional corn on the cob. Let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 4 ears of fresh corn, husked
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cotija cheese (or feta cheese)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, let’s prepare the corn.

  2. Place the corn directly on the grill grates and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are charred and slightly softened. Remove from the grill and set aside to cool.

  3. Once the corn has cooled, carefully cut the kernels off the cob using a sharp knife. Transfer the kernels to a large mixing bowl.

  4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, lime juice, chili powder, and cayenne pepper. This creamy and tangy dressing will add a burst of flavor to the salad.

  5. Pour the dressing over the corn kernels and mix well, ensuring that every kernel is coated with the dressing.

  6. Add the crumbled cotija cheese (or feta cheese) to the corn mixture. The salty and creamy cheese adds a delightful contrast to the sweetness of the corn.

  7. Finally, sprinkle the chopped cilantro over the salad for a fresh and herbaceous note. Season with salt and pepper to taste and give everything a final toss to combine all the flavors.

  8. Serve the Spicy Mexican Street Corn Salad chilled or at room temperature. It’s the perfect side dish for grilled meats, tacos, or even as a standalone snack.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have access to a grill, you can also cook the corn in a skillet over medium-high heat until charred and cooked through.
  • For an extra kick of heat, add a finely chopped jalapeno pepper to the dressing.
  • If you’re not a fan of mayonnaise, you can substitute it with Greek yogurt for a healthier twist.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavors of the salad. Smoked paprika, garlic powder, or even a dash of hot sauce can take this dish to new heights.

So there you have it, a simple yet sensational Spicy Mexican Street Corn Salad that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Mexico. The combination of the charred corn, tangy dressing, and creamy cheese will leave you craving for more. Give this recipe a try and let the fiesta begin!

Enjoy!
