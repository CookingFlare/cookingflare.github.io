Mouthwatering Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad


  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the quinoa and reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water has been absorbed.
  2. While the quinoa is cooking, prepare the dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, parsley, mint, dill, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Once the quinoa is cooked, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  5. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss gently to coat everything evenly.
  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  7. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Before serving, give the salad a good stir to ensure that all the ingredients are well combined.
  9. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • You can add some crumbled feta cheese or sliced avocado to the salad for extra creaminess and flavor.
  • If you prefer a bit of crunch, you can add some toasted pine nuts or slivered almonds to the salad.
  • Feel free to customize the herbs according to your taste. Basil, cilantro, or tarragon would also work well in this recipe.
  • This salad makes a great side dish or a light lunch. You can also serve it as a filling for wraps or stuffed into pita bread for a quick and nutritious meal on the go.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 250
  • Fat: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Protein: 8g
  • Fiber: 6g

This Mouthwatering Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad is a refreshing and nutritious dish that is perfect for summer. The combination of tangy lemon, fragrant herbs, and wholesome quinoa creates a burst of flavors in every bite. Whether you’re looking for a light lunch or a side dish to accompany your main meal, this salad is sure to please your taste buds.

Quinoa, a protein-packed grain, serves as the base of this salad, providing a satisfying and nutritious foundation. The lemon zest and juice add a bright and zesty flavor that complements the earthy quinoa perfectly. The combination of fresh parsley, mint, and dill adds a refreshing and herbaceous twist to the salad, elevating its taste to new heights.

To complete the salad, we add juicy cherry tomatoes, crunchy cucumber, and finely chopped red onion for a burst of freshness and texture. The dressing, made with extra virgin olive oil, ties all the ingredients together, creating a harmonious blend of flavors.

This salad is incredibly versatile. You can customize it to suit your preferences by adding ingredients like crumbled feta cheese or sliced avocado for extra creaminess, or toasted pine nuts or slivered almonds for a delightful crunch. The possibilities are endless!

Not only is this Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad delicious, but it’s also packed with nutritional benefits. Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids that our bodies need. It’s also high in fiber, which aids digestion and helps keep you feeling fuller for longer. The fresh herbs in the salad provide an array of vitamins and minerals, making this dish a nutritious addition to your diet.

So, the next time you’re craving a light and refreshing salad, try this Mouthwatering Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad. It’s easy to make, bursting with flavors, and will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Enjoy!
