The Corpse Reviver No. 1: A Classic Brandy Cocktail to Wake the Dead


If you’re a fan of classic cocktails with a touch of mystery and a dash of history, then The Corpse Reviver No. 1 is the perfect concoction to add to your repertoire. This vintage favorite is shrouded in the lore of old cocktail culture, and its rich flavors and intriguing name make it a must-try for any cocktail enthusiast.

Unearthing the Origins

The Corpse Reviver No. 1 dates back to the pre-Prohibition era, where it was believed to have been created as a morning pick-me-up, or what was colloquially referred to as a “hair of the dog” remedy. Its name suggests a restorative quality, meant to revitalize and awaken the senses, much like the way a good cup of coffee jump-starts the day.

Ingredients to Summon the Spirits

To summon the spirits of this classic cocktail, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Brandy
  • 1 oz. Sweet Vermouth
  • 1 oz. Apple Brandy
  • 1 dash of Absinthe
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange twist for garnish

Crafting the Elixir

  1. Begin by filling a mixing glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour in the brandy, sweet vermouth, and apple brandy.
  3. Add a dash of absinthe to the mix.
  4. Stir the concoction until it’s well-chilled.
  5. Strain the elixir into a chilled cocktail glass.
  6. Garnish with a twist of orange to add a citrusy zing.

Unveiling the Taste

The Corpse Reviver No. 1 is a hauntingly delightful blend of brandy’s warmth, the sweet vermouth’s depth, and the apple brandy’s fruity notes. The touch of absinthe adds a subtle herbal complexity, while the orange twist garnish lends a refreshing aroma that dances on the palate. This enigmatic elixir is sure to tantalize the taste buds and leave a lasting impression.

Summoning the Spirits

When it comes to serving The Corpse Reviver No. 1, it’s best enjoyed as a pre-dinner aperitif or as a nightcap, allowing its mysterious allure to set the tone for the evening ahead. Whether you’re hosting a sophisticated soirée or simply looking to indulge in a moment of refined indulgence, this classic brandy cocktail is the perfect libation to awaken the senses and evoke the spirits of a bygone era.

Embracing the Legacy

As you savor each sip of The Corpse Reviver No. 1, you become part of a tradition that spans generations, a legacy of libations that have endured the test of time. With its bewitching charm and timeless appeal, this classic brandy cocktail invites you to embrace the mystique of mixology and revel in the art of crafting cocktails that transcend mere libations and become experiences to be savored.

In conclusion, The Corpse Reviver No. 1 is not just a cocktail; it’s a journey into the past, a tribute to the art of mixology, and an invitation to savor the allure of classic libations. So, the next time you’re in the mood for a drink that’s steeped in history and brimming with flavor, consider summoning the spirits of this classic brandy cocktail to awaken the senses and indulge in a taste of tradition. Cheers to the timeless allure of The Corpse Reviver No. 1!
