Fluffy and Light Lemon Cupcakes: Bursting with Citrus Flavor


If you’re a fan of all things citrusy and delightful, then these fluffy and light lemon cupcakes are sure to become a favorite in your baking repertoire. Bursting with zesty lemon flavor and adorned with a delicate citrus-infused frosting, these cupcakes are a delightful treat for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a springtime brunch, a summer picnic, or simply craving a bright and cheerful dessert, these lemon cupcakes are the perfect choice.

Ingredients

To begin your lemon cupcake adventure, gather the following ingredients:

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Lemon Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon zest
  • 1-2 tablespoons heavy cream (if needed for consistency)
  • Additional lemon zest for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cupcake Batter

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the lemon zest and lemon juice.
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients.
  6. Stir in the vanilla extract until the batter is smooth and well-combined.

Step 2: Baking the Cupcakes

  1. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  2. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  3. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 3: Making the Lemon Frosting

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar, mixing until smooth and creamy.
  3. Beat in the lemon juice and lemon zest until well combined.
  4. If the frosting is too thick, add a tablespoon of heavy cream at a time until the desired consistency is reached.

Step 4: Decorating the Cupcakes

  1. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, use a piping bag or a small spatula to frost the cupcakes with the lemon frosting.
  2. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh lemon zest for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

Serving and Storing

These fluffy and light lemon cupcakes are best enjoyed fresh, but they can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. The bright citrus flavor of these cupcakes makes them a delightful addition to any gathering, and they are sure to be a hit with lemon lovers of all ages.

Final Thoughts

With their fluffy texture and vibrant lemon flavor, these cupcakes are a delightful way to brighten any day. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these lemon cupcakes are a perfect opportunity to indulge in the joy of citrus-infused treats. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the sunny delight of these fluffy and light lemon cupcakes!
