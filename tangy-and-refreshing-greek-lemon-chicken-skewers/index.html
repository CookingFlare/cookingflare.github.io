Tangy and Refreshing: Greek Lemon Chicken Skewers


If you’re craving a taste of the Mediterranean, look no further than these tangy and refreshing Greek lemon chicken skewers. This classic dish is bursting with flavor, combining the zesty brightness of lemon with the savory goodness of tender chicken. Whether you’re firing up the grill for a summer barbecue or simply want to bring a taste of Greece to your kitchen, these Greek lemon chicken skewers are sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the Chicken Skewers:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

For Serving:

  • Freshly chopped parsley
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, and black pepper. This aromatic marinade will infuse the chicken with the irresistible flavors of the Mediterranean.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken chunks in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish, and pour the marinade over the chicken. Ensure that the chicken is evenly coated with the marinade. Cover the bag or dish and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or ideally overnight for the most intense flavor.

  3. Skewer the Chicken: Once the chicken has marinated to perfection, thread the marinated chicken chunks onto the soaked wooden skewers, dividing the pieces evenly among the skewers.

  4. Grill the Skewers: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Place the chicken skewers on the grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a delicious charred exterior.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. Sprinkle freshly chopped parsley over the skewers and serve with lemon wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrus freshness.

Tips for Success

  • To ensure juicy and flavorful chicken, don’t skimp on the marinating time. Allowing the chicken to marinate for an extended period will result in more tender and flavorful skewers.
  • Soaking the wooden skewers in water before threading the chicken will prevent them from burning on the grill.

Conclusion

These Greek lemon chicken skewers are a delightful combination of tangy and refreshing flavors that will transport your taste buds to the sun-drenched shores of Greece. With a simple yet vibrant marinade and the smoky char from the grill, these skewers are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a taste of the Mediterranean, these tantalizing skewers are a must-try. So, fire up the grill and get ready to savor the irresistible flavors of Greek lemon chicken skewers!
