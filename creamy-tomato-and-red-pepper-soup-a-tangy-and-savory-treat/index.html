Creamy Tomato and Red Pepper Soup: A Tangy and Savory Treat


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and velvety textures, then this Creamy Tomato and Red Pepper Soup is the perfect recipe for you. Bursting with the tanginess of tomatoes and the smoky sweetness of red peppers, this soup is a treat for the taste buds. Whether you’re looking for a comforting meal on a chilly day or a quick and easy dish to impress your guests, this recipe has got you covered.

Ingredients

  • 2 large red bell peppers
  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the whole red bell peppers on a baking sheet and roast them for 20-25 minutes, or until the skins are charred and blistered. Remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  2. While the peppers are cooling, bring a pot of water to a boil. Score a small “X” on the bottom of each tomato and blanch them in the boiling water for about 30 seconds. Remove them from the water and transfer them to a bowl of ice water to cool. This will make it easier to peel the tomatoes later.

  3. Once the peppers have cooled, peel off the charred skins and remove the seeds and stems. Chop the peppers into smaller pieces and set them aside.

  4. Peel the tomatoes, remove the cores, and roughly chop them. Set them aside as well.

  5. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.

  6. Add the roasted red peppers and chopped tomatoes to the pot. Stir everything together and let it cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  7. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and cooked through.

  8. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth and creamy. If using a regular blender, be sure to blend in batches and be cautious of the hot liquid.

  9. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the soup simmer for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to come together.

  10. Ladle the creamy tomato and red pepper soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor and a touch of elegance.

Enjoy the Tangy and Savory Delight!

Now that your Creamy Tomato and Red Pepper Soup is ready, it’s time to savor the tangy and savory flavors. This soup pairs beautifully with a crusty baguette or a grilled cheese sandwich. It’s also a versatile base for adding your own twist, such as a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese or a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

This recipe is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Tomatoes are rich in vitamins A and C, while red bell peppers provide a healthy dose of antioxidants. With each spoonful, you’ll be indulging in a bowl of goodness that nourishes both your body and soul.

So, whether you’re seeking comfort or hosting a dinner party, this Creamy Tomato and Red Pepper Soup is sure to impress. Give it a try and let its tangy and savory flavors transport you to a culinary paradise.
