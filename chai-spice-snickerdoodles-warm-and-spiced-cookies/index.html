Chai Spice Snickerdoodles: Warm and Spiced Cookies


If you’re a fan of chai tea and love the comforting flavors of warm spices, then you’re in for a treat with these Chai Spice Snickerdoodles! This recipe takes the classic snickerdoodle cookie to a whole new level by infusing it with the aromatic and cozy flavors of chai. Whether you’re looking for a delightful treat to enjoy with a cup of tea or want to impress your friends and family with a unique cookie, these Chai Spice Snickerdoodles are sure to become a new favorite in your baking repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Cookie Dough:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Chai Spice Blend:

  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • ½ teaspoon ground allspice
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

For Rolling the Cookies:

  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Make the Chai Spice Blend: In a small bowl, whisk together the ground cinnamon, ground ginger, ground cardamom, ground allspice, ground cloves, and ground black pepper. Set aside.

  3. Prepare the Cookie Dough: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until well combined. Finally, stir in 2 teaspoons of the chai spice blend, reserving the remaining blend for rolling the cookies.

  4. Roll the Cookie Dough: In a small bowl, mix together the ¼ cup of granulated sugar and 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon. Using a cookie scoop or spoon, portion the dough and roll it into balls. Roll each ball in the cinnamon-sugar mixture until evenly coated.

  5. Bake the Cookies: Place the coated cookie dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set but the centers are still soft. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  6. Enjoy: Once the cookies have cooled, grab a cup of chai tea or your favorite beverage, and indulge in the warm and spiced goodness of these Chai Spice Snickerdoodles!

Tips for Success

  • Room Temperature Butter: Make sure your butter is at room temperature for easy creaming with the sugar. This helps create a light and tender cookie.

  • Chilling the Dough: If you prefer a thicker cookie, you can chill the cookie dough for 30 minutes to an hour before rolling and baking.

  • Even Rolling: To ensure even baking, try to roll the cookie dough into uniform-sized balls.

The Perfect Pairing

These Chai Spice Snickerdoodles are a delightful companion to a warm cup of chai tea or a creamy latte. The cozy blend of spices in the cookies complements the aromatic flavors of the tea, creating a comforting experience with every bite.

Final Thoughts

With their irresistible aroma and comforting flavors, Chai Spice Snickerdoodles are a delightful twist on the classic snickerdoodle cookie. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply craving a cozy treat, these warm and spiced cookies are sure to bring joy to everyone who enjoys them. Embrace the inviting allure of chai spices and elevate your cookie game with this unique and flavorful recipe.
