Easy Greek Spanakopita Triangles: A Mediterranean Delight


Greek cuisine is known for its vibrant flavors and healthy ingredients, and one dish that perfectly embodies these qualities is the delicious Spanakopita. These Easy Greek Spanakopita Triangles are a delightful twist on the traditional recipe, making them a perfect appetizer or snack for any occasion. With their flaky phyllo dough crust and savory spinach and feta filling, these triangles are sure to please even the pickiest eaters.

Ingredients

To make these Easy Greek Spanakopita Triangles, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 package of phyllo dough, thawed
  • 2 pounds of fresh spinach, washed and chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.

  3. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and allow it to cool slightly.

  4. Once the spinach mixture has cooled, transfer it to a large mixing bowl. Add the crumbled feta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, fresh dill, fresh parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  5. Unroll the thawed phyllo dough and cut it into strips about 3 inches wide. Brush each strip lightly with olive oil.

  6. Place a spoonful of the spinach and cheese mixture on the bottom corner of each phyllo strip. Fold the strip diagonally to form a triangle, and continue folding in this manner until you reach the end of the strip.

  7. Place the filled triangles onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving a small gap between each one.

  8. Brush the tops of the triangles with a little more olive oil to ensure they turn golden brown and crispy in the oven.

  9. Bake the Spanakopita Triangles for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and flaky.

  10. Remove from the oven and allow them to cool slightly before serving. These triangles can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

These Easy Greek Spanakopita Triangles are delicious on their own, but they can also be served with a side of tzatziki sauce or a simple Greek salad for a complete Mediterranean meal. They make a great appetizer for parties or a tasty snack for a cozy night in.

Conclusion

With their delicate layers of phyllo dough and flavorful spinach and feta filling, these Easy Greek Spanakopita Triangles are a Mediterranean delight that will transport your taste buds straight to Greece. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a taste of the Mediterranean, these triangles are a must-try recipe. So grab your apron and get ready to impress your friends and family with this easy and delicious Greek dish. Enjoy!
