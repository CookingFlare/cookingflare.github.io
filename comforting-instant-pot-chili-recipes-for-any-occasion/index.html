Comforting Instant Pot Chili Recipes for Any Occasion


Chili is a versatile and comforting dish that can be enjoyed all year round. Whether you’re hosting a game night with friends or craving a hearty meal after a long day, these Instant Pot chili recipes are sure to hit the spot. The Instant Pot not only cuts down the cooking time but also infuses the flavors into the ingredients, creating a rich and satisfying dish. So, grab your Instant Pot and let’s dive into these comforting chili recipes!

Classic Beef Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat some oil. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Remove any excess fat.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, and bell pepper. Sauté until softened.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual mode for 15 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before quick-releasing the remaining pressure.
  6. Give the chili a good stir and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve hot with your favorite toppings like shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped green onions.

Vegetarian Quinoa Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (4 ounces) green chilies
  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil. Add the onion, garlic, bell pepper, and zucchini. Sauté until softened.
  2. Stir in the diced tomatoes, black beans, kidney beans, green chilies, quinoa, vegetable broth, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual mode for 8 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before quick-releasing the remaining pressure.
  5. Give the chili a good stir and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve hot with a dollop of Greek yogurt and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro.

White Chicken Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (4 ounces) green chilies
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat some oil. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  2. Add the onion and garlic. Sauté until softened.
  3. Stir in the white beans, green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, dried oregano, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual mode for 10 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before quick-releasing the remaining pressure.
  6. Shred the chicken using two forks and give the chili a good stir. Serve hot with a squeeze of fresh lime juice and a sprinkle of chopped fresh cilantro.

Conclusion

These Instant Pot chili recipes are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a meat lover or prefer a vegetarian option, there’s a chili recipe here for you. The Instant Pot makes cooking these comforting dishes a breeze, allowing you to enjoy a delicious meal in no time. So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to savor the comforting flavors of these chili recipes.
