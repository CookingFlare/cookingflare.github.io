Satisfying Low Carb Cheeseburger Casserole


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb meal that will remind you of your favorite cheeseburger, without all the guilt? Look no further! This Satisfying Low Carb Cheeseburger Casserole is the answer to your cravings. Packed with savory ground beef, gooey melted cheese, and classic burger toppings, this casserole will become a staple in your weekly meal rotation. Plus, it’s easy to make and perfect for meal prep, making it an ideal choice for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup sugar-free ketchup
  • 1/4 cup mustard
  • 1/4 cup dill pickles, chopped
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Lettuce, tomato, and additional pickles for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until the meat is no longer pink and the onions are translucent. Drain any excess fat from the skillet.

  3. Season the beef mixture with salt, pepper, paprika, and dried oregano. Stir in the sugar-free ketchup, mustard, and chopped dill pickles. Spread the mixture evenly into the prepared baking dish.

  4. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the beef mixture.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, almond flour, and baking powder until smooth. Pour this mixture over the beef and cheese in the baking dish.

  6. Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the center is set.

  7. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Serve with shredded lettuce, sliced tomatoes, and additional pickles if desired.

Why You’ll Love This Casserole

This Satisfying Low Carb Cheeseburger Casserole is not only a delicious and comforting meal, but it’s also a great option for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. By replacing the traditional burger bun with a flavorful egg and cheese mixture, you get all the classic cheeseburger flavors without the extra carbs.

The combination of savory ground beef, tangy pickles, and melty cheese creates a mouthwatering experience that will satisfy your cravings without derailing your healthy eating goals. Plus, this casserole is versatile – you can customize it with your favorite burger toppings like crispy bacon, avocado, or jalapenos to make it your own.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Satisfying Low Carb Cheeseburger Casserole is a winning dish for anyone looking to enjoy the flavors of a cheeseburger in a healthier, low-carb way. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or meal prepping for the week, this casserole is sure to become a crowd-pleaser. Give it a try and savor the comforting taste of a cheeseburger without the guilt!
