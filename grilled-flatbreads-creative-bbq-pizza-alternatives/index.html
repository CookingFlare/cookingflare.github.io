Grilled Flatbreads: Creative BBQ Pizza Alternatives


Flatbreads have been a staple in many cuisines for centuries, and their versatility has made them a favorite among foodies and home cooks alike. When it comes to grilling, flatbreads take on a whole new level of flavor and texture, making them an excellent alternative to traditional pizza. In this post, we’ll explore the art of grilling flatbreads and offer some creative BBQ pizza alternatives that are sure to impress your family and friends.

The Basics of Grilling Flatbreads

Before we dive into the creative recipes, let’s start with the basics. Grilling flatbreads requires a few simple ingredients and some basic cooking skills. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Flatbreads (homemade or store-bought)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional toppings (see below)

To grill flatbreads, simply brush them with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and place them on a preheated grill or grill pan. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they’re crispy and slightly charred.

Classic Grilled Flatbread Toppings

While flatbreads can be enjoyed on their own, they’re even more delicious with some creative toppings. Here are some classic combinations that never go out of style:

  • Garlic and herbs: Mix minced garlic with chopped fresh herbs like parsley, basil, or thyme. Spread the mixture on the flatbread before grilling.
  • Cheese and meats: Top flatbreads with shredded mozzarella, parmesan, or feta cheese, and add sliced meats like prosciutto, salami, or ham.
  • Fresh vegetables: Add some color and crunch with sliced bell peppers, zucchini, or cherry tomatoes. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar for added flavor.
  • Spreads and dips: Try using hummus, guacamole, or tzatziki sauce as a topping. You can also add some crumbled feta cheese or chopped fresh herbs for added flavor.

Creative BBQ Pizza Alternatives

Now that we’ve covered the basics, let’s get creative with some BBQ-inspired flatbread recipes. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  • Grilled Chicken and Pineapple Flatbread: Top grilled flatbread with sliced grilled chicken, caramelized pineapple, red onion, cilantro, and a drizzle of BBQ sauce.
  • Spicy Shrimp and Bacon Flatbread: Combine grilled shrimp, crispy bacon, diced jalapeños, and a sprinkle of cilantro. Drizzle with a spicy BBQ sauce for added heat.
  • Caramelized Onion and Blue Cheese Flatbread: Top grilled flatbread with caramelized onions, crumbled blue cheese, and a sprinkle of thyme. Drizzle with a balsamic glaze for added sweetness.
  • Korean-Style BBQ Pork Flatbread: Marinate pork shoulder in a Korean-inspired BBQ sauce and grill until caramelized. Top flatbread with the pork, sliced green onions, and a sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds.

Tips and Variations

  • For a crisper crust, try grilling flatbreads at a higher temperature (around 425°F) for a shorter amount of time (2-3 minutes per side).
  • Experiment with different types of flatbreads, such as naan, pita, or lavash, for unique textures and flavors.
  • Add some crunch by topping flatbreads with toasted nuts or seeds, like almonds or sesame seeds.
  • Try using different cheeses, such as goat cheese or feta, for added flavor and texture.
  • Experiment with different BBQ sauces and marinades to find your favorite flavors.

Conclusion

Grilled flatbreads are a game-changer for anyone looking for a creative alternative to traditional pizza. With their crispy crusts and endless topping possibilities, they’re the perfect canvas for your favorite flavors. Whether you’re a BBQ enthusiast or just looking for a new way to enjoy your favorite foods, grilled flatbreads are sure to impress. So go ahead, get creative, and fire up your grill!
