Mediterranean-Inspired Savory Pancake Recipes


Whether you’re a fan of breakfast for dinner or simply looking to expand your culinary horizons, Mediterranean-inspired savory pancake recipes are a delicious and versatile option to consider. These pancakes are bursting with flavors from the Mediterranean region, combining fresh ingredients, aromatic herbs, and savory fillings. In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering recipes that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean.

Recipe 1: Spinach and Feta Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and olive oil.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Gently fold in the chopped spinach, feta cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, and fresh dill.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with olive oil.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with a dollop of Greek yogurt and a sprinkle of fresh dill.

Recipe 2: Mediterranean Vegetable Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 small zucchini, grated
  • 1 small red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the chickpea flour, baking powder, salt, cumin, smoked paprika, and dried oregano.
  2. Gradually add the water, whisking until a smooth batter forms.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the sliced red onion, grated zucchini, and sliced red bell pepper. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Stir the cooked vegetables and sliced black olives into the batter.
  6. Heat another tablespoon of olive oil in the skillet and pour 1/4 cup of the batter for each pancake.
  7. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with a side of tzatziki sauce and a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Recipe 3: Mediterranean Shrimp and Herb Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, dried oregano, and black pepper.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and olive oil.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. In a skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat.
  5. Add the shrimp and minced garlic, cooking until the shrimp turns pink, about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Stir in the chopped basil, parsley, mint, and lemon juice.
  7. Gently fold the shrimp mixture into the pancake batter.
  8. Heat another tablespoon of olive oil in the skillet and pour 1/4 cup of the batter for each pancake.
  9. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  10. Serve the pancakes warm with a squeeze of lemon juice and a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

These Mediterranean-inspired savory pancake recipes are perfect for brunch, lunch, or even a light dinner. They offer a delightful blend of flavors and textures, showcasing the vibrant and healthy cuisine of the Mediterranean region. So, get your spatula ready and indulge in these delicious creations that will transport you to the sun-kissed shores of the Mediterranean. Bon appétit!
