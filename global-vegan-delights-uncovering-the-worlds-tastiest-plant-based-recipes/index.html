Global Vegan Delights: Uncovering the World's Tastiest Plant-Based Recipes


Welcome, food enthusiasts! Are you ready to embark on a culinary journey like no other? This time, we’re not just exploring the vast spectrum of scrumptious flavors from around the globe but experiencing them from a brand new perspective- the vegan way! That’s right. Delve into the vibrant and ever-growing world of flavorsome plant-based recipes! It’s never too late to dish out some incredibly delectable meals without a hint of meat or dairy. Today, we’ll be uncovering some of the world’s tastiest vegan delicacies – from the shores of Thailand to the bustling streets of India, and beyond. Fasten your seatbelts as we set sail on this gastronomic adventure!

Thailand: Sweet and Spicy Pumpkin Red Curry

Thailand is renowned for its exotic and diverse culinary landscape. Let’s begin our journey with this exotic pumpkin red curry dish – one that promises to be a firm favorite on your go-to vegan recipes list.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs pumpkin, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 4 tbsp red curry paste
  • 2 cans coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp tamari or soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp chopped basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp squeezed lime juice

Instructions

  1. Begin by heating coconut oil in a large pan over medium heat, and sauté your onion slices for approximately 5 minutes until they become translucent.
  2. Toss in your minced garlic, ginger, and red curry paste. Stir well to combine and cook for another 2-3 minutes until you smell that gorgeous aromatic fragrance of ingrained Thai spices.
  3. Then, pour in the can of coconut milk and mix well to create a creamy, luscious sauce. Add your pumpkin and vegetable broth followed by tamari or soy sauce, maple syrup, and cornstarch.
  4. Allow your curry to simmer for roughly 15-20 minutes, or until your pumpkin is cooked through and tender while the sauce thickens.
  5. Finally, stir in your freshly chopped basil leaves and lime juice, and voilà! Your delectable vegan Thai pumpkin curry is ready to be served.

India: Chickpea Masala with Fragrant Basmati Rice

We can never forget India’s glorious and soulful vegan meals that put forth the perfect balance of flavors. Enjoy your cooking adventure with its popular chickpea masala dish partnered with aromatic fragrant basmati rice.

Ingredients

Chickpea Masala:

  • 2 cups chickpeas, cooked
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped fresh coriander

Basmati Rice:

  • 1 cup basmati rice, washed
  • 2 cups water
  • ½ tsp salt

Instructions

Chickpea Masala

  1. Begin by heating the coconut oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add your chopped onion and cook until softened.
  2. Stir in your minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another minute. Next, throw in garam masala, cumin, and turmeric, followed by the diced tomatoes. Bring the entire mixture to a simmer and cook for around 8-10 minutes.
  3. Plop in the cooked chickpeas, and let it simmer for another few minutes before seasoning your dish with lemon juice, salt, and fresh coriander. Go ahead and serve with your fragrant basmati rice.

Basmati Rice

  1. First, bring a large pot of water and salt to a boil. Then add your basmati rice, reduce heat to low, and cover with a lid. Let your rice cook for 15-20 minutes or until all the water is absorbed.
  2. Once done, fluff the rice with a fork, and keep it covered to preserve its steamy warmth.
