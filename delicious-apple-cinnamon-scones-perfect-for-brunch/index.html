Delicious Apple Cinnamon Scones: Perfect for Brunch


When it comes to a delicious and comforting brunch, there’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked apple cinnamon scones wafting through the air. These delightful treats are not only a joy to make but also a delightful addition to any brunch spread. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply want to treat yourself to something special, these apple cinnamon scones are sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup diced apples (peeled and cored)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Apples: In a small bowl, toss the diced apples with lemon juice, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon. Set aside while you prepare the scone dough.

  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  3. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon.

  4. Incorporate the Butter: Add the cold, diced butter to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  5. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and vanilla extract.

  6. Bring the Dough Together: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently mix until the dough begins to come together. Be careful not to overmix.

  7. Add the Apples: Gently fold in the prepared apples until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  8. Shape the Scones: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat it into a circle about 1 inch thick. Using a sharp knife, cut the circle into 8 wedges.

  9. Bake the Scones: Transfer the scones to the prepared baking sheet and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  10. Serve and Enjoy: Allow the scones to cool slightly before serving. They are best enjoyed warm, perhaps with a dollop of clotted cream or a drizzle of honey.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent touch, consider adding a simple glaze made with powdered sugar and a splash of milk to drizzle over the cooled scones.
  • Feel free to customize these scones by adding chopped nuts, such as pecans or walnuts, to the dough for some added crunch.
  • If you prefer a more pronounced cinnamon flavor, increase the amount of ground cinnamon in the dough or sprinkle a little extra on top before baking.

Conclusion

These apple cinnamon scones are a delightful way to elevate your brunch experience. The combination of tender, buttery scone with sweet, fragrant apples and warm cinnamon is simply irresistible. Whether you’re enjoying them with a cup of coffee or as part of a larger brunch spread, these scones are sure to become a favorite. So, the next time you’re in the mood for a special treat, consider whipping up a batch of these delicious apple cinnamon scones and savoring the simple pleasure of homemade baking.
