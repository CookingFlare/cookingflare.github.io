Quick and Easy Margherita Chicken Skewers Recipe


Are you looking for a quick and easy recipe that will impress your guests? Look no further than these delicious Margherita Chicken Skewers! Packed with flavor and ready in no time, they are the perfect addition to any summer barbecue or weeknight dinner. Let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, cut into small cubes
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces, approximately 1-inch cubes. Place them in a large bowl.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Pour the marinade over the chicken and toss until all the pieces are well coated. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  4. Thread the marinated chicken, cherry tomatoes, and mozzarella cubes onto skewers, alternating between the ingredients.

  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning them occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the tomatoes are slightly charred.

  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute and the flavors to intensify.

  7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve immediately. These skewers are perfect on their own or served with a side of fresh salad or grilled vegetables.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook these skewers in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for approximately 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. You can add other vegetables like bell peppers or red onions to the skewers for added color and flavor.

  • For a vegetarian option, you can substitute the chicken with tofu or halloumi cheese. The marinade and grilling process will still give you a delicious result.

  • If you want to add a little kick to the skewers, you can sprinkle some red pepper flakes or drizzle some hot sauce over the finished dish.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. They can be enjoyed cold or reheated in the microwave or oven.

Conclusion

These Quick and Easy Margherita Chicken Skewers are a crowd-pleasing dish that will elevate your next meal. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can create a flavorful and satisfying dish that everyone will love. Whether you’re hosting a barbecue or looking for a quick and delicious weeknight dinner, these skewers are sure to impress. Give them a try and let us know what you think! Enjoy!
