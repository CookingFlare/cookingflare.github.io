Sizzling Sichuan Noodles Stir Fry: A Spicy and Satisfying Meal


If you’re a fan of bold, spicy flavors, then Sizzling Sichuan Noodles Stir Fry is the perfect dish for you. This iconic Chinese dish is known for its fiery heat and complex, aromatic flavors. The combination of tender noodles, vibrant vegetables, and a sizzling, spicy sauce makes for a truly satisfying meal that will tantalize your taste buds. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of creating this delectable dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Stir Fry Sauce:

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sichuan peppercorns, toasted and ground
  • 1 tbsp chili oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth

For the Stir Fry:

  • 8 oz dried egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp peanut or vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snow peas, trimmed
  • 1 cup shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bamboo shoots, sliced
  • 1/2 cup water chestnuts, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Crushed peanuts for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Noodles:

    • Cook the dried egg noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Stir Fry Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, hoisin sauce, oyster sauce, ground Sichuan peppercorns, chili oil, and sugar. Set aside.

  3. Stir Fry the Vegetables:

    • Heat the peanut or vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Add the sliced red bell pepper, julienned carrot, snow peas, shiitake mushrooms, bamboo shoots, and water chestnuts. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Combine and Finish:

    • Add the cooked noodles to the wok or skillet with the stir-fried vegetables. Pour the stir fry sauce over the noodles and vegetables.
    • Toss everything together until the noodles are well coated with the sauce and the vegetables are evenly distributed.
    • Add the bean sprouts, green onions, and cilantro to the noodles, and toss to combine.

  5. Serve:

    • Transfer the Sizzling Sichuan Noodles Stir Fry to a serving dish. Garnish with crushed peanuts for added texture and flavor, if desired.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a vegetarian version of this dish, simply substitute the chicken broth with vegetable broth and omit the oyster sauce.
  • Sichuan peppercorns are known for their unique numbing and tingling sensation. If you prefer a milder heat, reduce the amount of Sichuan peppercorns in the stir fry sauce.
  • Customize the vegetables in this stir fry based on your preferences. You can add or substitute with baby corn, bok choy, or any other fresh vegetables you love.

Final Thoughts

Sizzling Sichuan Noodles Stir Fry is a dish that embodies the vibrant and dynamic flavors of Sichuan cuisine. The combination of heat from the chili oil, the numbing sensation from the Sichuan peppercorns, and the umami-rich sauce creates a truly unforgettable dining experience. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or looking to expand your culinary horizons, this dish is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a flavorful journey with this Sizzling Sichuan Noodles Stir Fry. Your taste buds will thank you!
