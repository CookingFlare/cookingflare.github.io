Tasty Mini Taco Pizzas: Perfect for Game Night


If you’re a fan of both tacos and pizza, then you’re in for a treat with this recipe. These tasty mini taco pizzas are the perfect snack for game night or any casual get-together. They’re easy to make, delicious, and guaranteed to be a hit with your friends and family. So, let’s dive right in and learn how to make these mouthwatering treats!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1 can of refrigerated pizza dough
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of sliced black olives
  • Sour cream, for garnish
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.

  3. Add the taco seasoning to the cooked beef according to the packet instructions. Stir well to combine and let it simmer for a few minutes until the flavors meld together.

  4. Unroll the refrigerated pizza dough onto a greased baking sheet. Using a knife or a pizza cutter, cut the dough into smaller, bite-sized pieces. You can shape them into mini circles or any other shape you prefer.

  5. Bake the pizza dough according to the package instructions until it turns golden brown. This should take about 10-12 minutes.

  6. Once the pizza dough is baked, remove it from the oven and let it cool slightly.

  7. Spread a spoonful of the seasoned ground beef onto each mini pizza crust.

  8. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese on top of the beef. Be generous with the cheese; it adds a delicious gooeyness to the pizzas.

  9. Next, add diced tomatoes, red onions, and sliced black olives as toppings. Feel free to customize the toppings according to your preferences. You can even add jalapeños for an extra kick!

  10. Place the topped mini pizzas back into the oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and the toppings are heated through.

  11. Once the pizzas are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  12. Serve the mini taco pizzas warm and garnish with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro.

Enjoy Your Game Night Delight!

These tasty mini taco pizzas are a fantastic addition to any game night spread. They’re fun, flavorful, and easy to eat with your hands, making them the perfect finger food for cheering on your favorite team or battling it out in a board game tournament. The combination of the seasoned ground beef, melted cheese, and fresh toppings creates a mouthwatering explosion of flavors in every bite.

So, the next time you’re hosting a game night or simply craving a delicious snack, give these mini taco pizzas a try. Your friends and family will thank you, and you’ll become the ultimate game night hero!
