Tasty Nut-Free Guacamole Recipe


Are you a guacamole lover but have to avoid nuts? Look no further! This nut-free guacamole recipe is the perfect solution for those with nut allergies or sensitivities. Packed with flavor and creamy texture, this guacamole is sure to become a favorite in your household. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make, so you can enjoy this delicious dip anytime you want.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small red onion, finely diced
  • 2 tomatoes, seeded and diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, finely minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Begin by halving the avocados and removing the pits. Scoop out the flesh into a mixing bowl.

  2. Using a fork, mash the avocados to your desired consistency. Some people prefer chunky guacamole, while others like it smooth. It’s really up to you!

  3. Add the diced red onion, tomatoes, minced jalapeño, chopped cilantro, and minced garlic to the bowl with the mashed avocados.

  4. Squeeze the juice of one lime into the bowl, ensuring that the seeds are caught by your hand or a strainer.

  5. Sprinkle in the cumin, salt, and pepper to taste.

  6. Gently fold all the ingredients together until well combined.

  7. Taste the guacamole and adjust the seasonings as needed. You may want to add more lime juice, salt, or pepper to suit your personal preference.

  8. Once you’re happy with the flavor, cover the guacamole with plastic wrap, ensuring that the wrap touches the surface of the guacamole to prevent browning.

  9. Refrigerate the guacamole for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  10. When you’re ready to serve, give the guacamole a quick stir and transfer it to a serving bowl.

  11. Pair this nut-free guacamole with your favorite tortilla chips, use it as a topping for tacos, or spread it on sandwiches for a burst of fresh flavor.

Why Nut-Free Guacamole?

While traditional guacamole recipes often include ingredients like nuts or nut-based seasonings, this nut-free version ensures that everyone can enjoy this beloved dip without worry. Nut allergies are quite common, and it can be challenging to find nut-free recipes that are both safe and delicious. This guacamole recipe provides a solution for individuals and families who need to avoid nuts without sacrificing flavor.

Health Benefits of Avocados

Apart from being a creamy and delicious addition to any dish, avocados offer a range of health benefits. They are packed with heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, which can help lower bad cholesterol levels and reduce the risk of heart disease. Additionally, avocados are a great source of fiber, potassium, and vitamins C, K, and E. Including avocados in your diet can support overall health and well-being.

Final Thoughts

This nut-free guacamole recipe is a versatile and crowd-pleasing option for gatherings, parties, or simply a cozy night in. Its vibrant colors and zesty flavors make it a standout dish that will leave your taste buds satisfied. Whether you’re a guacamole aficionado or trying it for the first time, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients and whip up a batch of this delectable guacamole to share with friends and family. Enjoy!

Remember, the key to a great guacamole is using perfectly ripe avocados and balancing the flavors to your liking. Feel free to customize this recipe by adding extra heat with more jalapeños or incorporating additional fresh ingredients like diced mango or pineapple for a unique twist. The possibilities are endless, so have fun experimenting and making this nut-free guacamole your own!
