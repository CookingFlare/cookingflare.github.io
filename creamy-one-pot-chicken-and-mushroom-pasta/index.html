Creamy One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Pasta


There’s something incredibly satisfying about a creamy, indulgent pasta dish, especially when it’s packed with savory chicken and earthy mushrooms. This one-pot recipe is not only a time-saver, but it’s also a flavor-packed delight that will have your family and friends coming back for seconds. Let’s dive into the delicious world of Creamy One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 12 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • Grated parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.

  3. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until they are softened and have released their juices.

  4. Sprinkle in the dried thyme, oregano, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir the seasonings into the onion and mushroom mixture.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, then add the fettuccine pasta, making sure it’s submerged in the liquid. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Let the pasta cook for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it’s tender and the sauce has thickened.

  6. Once the pasta is cooked, return the browned chicken to the pot and stir it into the creamy pasta mixture. Let it simmer for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve the Creamy One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Pasta hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley and a generous sprinkle of grated parmesan cheese.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes or a splash of white wine to the sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the mushrooms used in this recipe. Try a mix of cremini, shiitake, and button mushrooms for a variety of textures and flavors.
  • If you’re looking to make this dish a bit lighter, you can substitute half-and-half for the heavy cream, though the sauce won’t be quite as rich.

The Magic of One Pot Meals

One pot meals are a busy cook’s best friend. They not only save time on cleanup, but they also allow flavors to meld together beautifully as everything cooks in one vessel. This Creamy One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Pasta is a prime example of the magic of one pot meals. The pasta cooks directly in the creamy sauce, absorbing all the delicious flavors of the chicken, mushrooms, and herbs as it simmers to perfection.

Final Thoughts

Creamy One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Pasta is a comfort food classic that’s perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions. It’s a crowd-pleaser that doesn’t require hours of preparation or multiple pots and pans to clean up afterward. With its creamy, savory goodness, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe rotation.

So, the next time you’re craving a hearty, satisfying meal that comes together with minimal effort, give this Creamy One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Pasta a try. It’s a delightful combination of creamy, dreamy, and oh-so-delicious flavors that will leave everyone at the table asking for more.
