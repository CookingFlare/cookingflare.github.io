Vegan Delights: Wholesome and Plant-Based Rice and Grain Recipes


Welcome to the world of vegan cooking, where delicious flavors and wholesome ingredients come together to create mouthwatering dishes. In this blog post, we’re going to explore the wonderful world of rice and grain recipes that are not only vegan but also packed with nutrients and flavor. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup white wine (optional)
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.
  3. Add the Arborio rice to the pan and stir until coated in the oil.
  4. Pour in the white wine and let it simmer until it has evaporated.
  5. Gradually add the vegetable broth, stirring frequently, until the rice is cooked and creamy.
  6. Stir in the nutritional yeast, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with additional parsley if desired.

2. Spicy Chickpea Curry with Quinoa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the chickpeas, curry powder, cumin, paprika, and turmeric to the skillet. Cook for a few minutes until the chickpeas are coated in the spices.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for 10-15 minutes until the flavors have melded together.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the spicy chickpea curry over a bed of quinoa and garnish with fresh cilantro.

3. Vegetable Sushi Rolls with Brown Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups brown rice, cooked and cooled
  • 4 nori sheets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • Soy sauce for dipping
  • Pickled ginger for serving

Instructions:

  1. Lay a nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat or a clean kitchen towel.
  2. Spread a thin layer of brown rice over the nori sheet, leaving about an inch at the top.
  3. Arrange the carrot, cucumber, avocado, and red bell pepper on top of the rice.
  4. Using the sushi mat or towel, tightly roll the nori sheet into a cylinder.
  5. Repeat with the remaining nori sheets and fillings.
  6. Slice the sushi rolls into bite-sized pieces.
  7. Serve with soy sauce for dipping and pickled ginger on the side.

These vegan rice and grain recipes are just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to the delightful possibilities of plant-based cooking. Feel free to experiment with different ingredients and flavors to make these recipes your own. Enjoy the journey of creating delicious and nutritious meals that are good for you and the planet. Happy cooking!
