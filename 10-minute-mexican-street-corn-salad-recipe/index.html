10-Minute Mexican Street Corn Salad Recipe


Are you craving a delicious and refreshing salad that captures the vibrant flavors of Mexican street food? Look no further than this 10-Minute Mexican Street Corn Salad recipe. With its combination of sweet corn, tangy lime, and spicy chili, this salad is sure to delight your taste buds and impress your friends and family. And the best part? It takes just 10 minutes to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, red onion, cilantro, jalapeno, garlic, lime juice, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are well combined.

  2. Add the cooked corn kernels to the bowl and toss gently until they are fully coated with the dressing. Make sure to mix carefully to avoid breaking the corn kernels.

  3. Once the corn is evenly coated, taste the salad and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can add more lime juice, chili powder, or salt according to your taste preferences.

  4. Transfer the salad to a serving dish and garnish with additional chopped cilantro if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy the vibrant flavors of this Mexican street corn salad.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a smokier flavor, you can grill the corn before adding it to the salad. Simply brush the corn with olive oil and grill it over medium heat until it is slightly charred. Let it cool before removing the kernels and proceeding with the recipe.

  • For a creamier texture, you can add crumbled cotija cheese or feta cheese to the salad. The salty and tangy flavor of the cheese will complement the sweet corn and add an extra layer of richness.

  • If you want to make the salad a bit more substantial, you can add diced avocado or cooked black beans. These additions will not only enhance the flavor but also provide additional nutrients and protein.

  • Feel free to adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less jalapeno. If you’re not a fan of spicy food, you can omit the jalapeno entirely and still enjoy the delicious flavors of this salad.

  • This Mexican street corn salad is perfect as a side dish for grilled meats or as a topping for tacos, nachos, or quesadillas. Get creative and use it in your favorite Mexican-inspired dishes.

Conclusion

In just 10 minutes, you can create a vibrant and flavorful Mexican street corn salad that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Mexico. With its combination of sweet corn, tangy lime, and spicy chili, this salad is a true explosion of flavors. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a quick and delicious salad, this recipe is sure to impress. So grab your ingredients and get ready to enjoy the taste of Mexico in every bite!
