Homemade English Muffins: Perfect for Breakfast


Are you tired of store-bought English muffins that lack that homemade touch? Why not try making your own delicious Homemade English Muffins? These fluffy, golden-brown delights are perfect for a cozy breakfast or brunch. With this simple recipe, you can enjoy the satisfaction of baking your own English muffins from scratch. Let’s dive right in!

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Cornmeal for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  2. Mix the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the yeast mixture, warm milk, melted butter, salt, and 2 cups of flour. Gradually add the remaining flour until the dough comes together.

  3. Knead the Dough: Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 8-10 minutes until it is smooth and elastic.

  4. First Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours until it has doubled in size.

  5. Shape the Muffins: Punch down the dough and roll it out to about 1/2 inch thickness. Use a round cutter to cut out the muffins. Dust them with cornmeal and let them rest for another 30 minutes.

  6. Cook the Muffins: Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Cook the muffins for about 5-7 minutes on each side until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  7. Enjoy: Let the muffins cool slightly before splitting them with a fork. Toast them to perfection and top with your favorite spreads or toppings.

Tips for Success:

  • Make sure your yeast is fresh for the best rise.
  • Don’t skip the cornmeal dusting—it adds a lovely texture to the muffins.
  • Adjust the cooking time to achieve your desired level of doneness.

Homemade English Muffins are a delightful treat that will elevate your breakfast game. Whether topped with melted butter, jam, or your favorite breakfast sandwich ingredients, these muffins are sure to impress. So why not take a morning to indulge in the pleasure of freshly baked goodness? Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, the key to a perfect batch of Homemade English Muffins is patience and attention to detail. Treat yourself to the warm, comforting aroma of freshly baked bread and savor each bite of these delectable treats. Start your day right with a breakfast that is made with love and care. Happy baking!
