Easy One-Pan Paleo Lemon Garlic Chicken


Cooking a delicious and healthy meal doesn’t have to be complicated. This Easy One-Pan Paleo Lemon Garlic Chicken recipe is not only simple to make but also bursting with flavor. Perfect for those following a paleo diet or anyone looking for a quick, nutritious dinner option, this dish combines tender chicken thighs with zesty lemon and savory garlic, all cooked in one pan for easy cleanup.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Pat the chicken thighs dry with paper towels and season both sides generously with salt and pepper.

  2. Sear the Chicken: In a large skillet or frying pan, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken thighs to the pan and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

  3. Make the Lemon Garlic Sauce: In the same pan, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  4. Combine Ingredients: Add lemon juice, lemon zest, dried oregano, and dried thyme to the pan. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  5. Simmer: Return the chicken thighs to the pan, spooning some of the lemon garlic sauce over each piece. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the chicken simmer in the sauce for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve: Remove the pan from heat. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  7. Enjoy: Serve the One-Pan Paleo Lemon Garlic Chicken hot, accompanied by your favorite side dishes such as steamed vegetables, cauliflower rice, or a crisp green salad.

Tips for Success

  • Chicken Thighs: Using boneless, skinless chicken thighs ensures tender and juicy meat. You can also use chicken breasts if preferred, adjusting the cooking time accordingly to ensure they are cooked through.

  • Fresh Ingredients: While dried herbs work well in this recipe, you can also use fresh herbs for a more vibrant flavor. Simply double the amount if substituting fresh for dried.

  • Customization: Feel free to customize the dish by adding additional vegetables such as cherry tomatoes, spinach, or bell peppers during the cooking process for added nutrition and flavor.

  • Storage: Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop or in the microwave until warmed through.

This Easy One-Pan Paleo Lemon Garlic Chicken recipe is sure to become a staple in your weeknight dinner rotation. With minimal prep and cleanup, you can enjoy a satisfying meal that’s both healthy and delicious. Whether you’re following a paleo lifestyle or simply love flavorful, fuss-free cooking, this dish ticks all the boxes. Treat yourself to a taste of citrusy garlic goodness tonight!
