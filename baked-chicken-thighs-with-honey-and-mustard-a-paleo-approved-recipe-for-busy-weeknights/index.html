Baked Chicken Thighs with Honey and Mustard: A Paleo-Approved Recipe for Busy Weeknights


Are you tired of the same old boring chicken recipes? Do you struggle to find healthy and delicious options that fit your busy schedule? Look no further! Today, we’re going to share a mouth-watering recipe for baked chicken thighs with honey and mustard that’s perfect for a quick and easy dinner. This paleo-approved recipe is not only flavorful but also packed with nutrients and can be prepared in under 30 minutes.

The Magic of Honey and Mustard

The combination of honey and mustard may seem unusual, but trust us, it’s a game-changer. The sweetness of the honey balances out the tanginess of the mustard, creating a harmonious flavor profile that’s both sweet and savory. This duo is perfect for chicken, as it adds a depth of flavor that’s hard to achieve with traditional seasonings.

The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together honey, mustard, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Place the chicken thighs in a large bowl and brush the honey-mustard mixture evenly over both sides of the chicken.
  4. Drizzle the olive oil over the chicken and toss to coat.
  5. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and arrange the chicken thighs in a single layer.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  7. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • For a crispy skin, bake the chicken thighs for an additional 5-10 minutes. Keep an eye on them to prevent burning.
  • Substitute the thyme with rosemary or oregano for a different flavor profile.
  • Add some heat by sprinkling a pinch of red pepper flakes over the chicken before baking.
  • Serve with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables, quinoa, or a simple green salad.

Why This Recipe Works

This recipe is a winner for several reasons:

  • It’s incredibly easy to prepare, making it perfect for busy weeknights.
  • The honey and mustard add a depth of flavor that’s hard to achieve with traditional seasonings.
  • The chicken thighs are tender and juicy, with a crispy skin that’s sure to satisfy.
  • This recipe is paleo-approved, making it a great option for those following a paleo diet.

Conclusion

Baked chicken thighs with honey and mustard are a game-changer for anyone looking for a quick and easy dinner that’s packed with flavor. This paleo-approved recipe is perfect for busy weeknights, and the combination of honey and mustard is sure to become a favorite. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the delicious results!
