Savory Pancake Bites: 8 Appetizer Recipes to Try


Savory pancake bites are a delightful twist on traditional pancake recipes. These bite-sized treats are perfect for snacking, entertaining, or as a quick and easy appetizer for your next gathering. In this article, we’ll explore 8 unique savory pancake bite recipes that are sure to impress your guests.

Recipe 1: Spinach and Feta Savory Pancake Bites

Combine 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 cup chopped fresh spinach, 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a bowl. Mix well. Add 1 cup buttermilk and mix until a smooth batter forms. Drop tablespoonfuls of the batter onto a hot skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Recipe 2: Caramelized Onion and Rosemary Savory Pancake Bites

Combine 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 cup caramelized onions, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a bowl. Mix well. Add 1 cup buttermilk and mix until a smooth batter forms. Drop tablespoonfuls of the batter onto a hot skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Recipe 3: Mushroom and Cheddar Savory Pancake Bites

Combine 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 cup sautéed mushrooms, 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a bowl. Mix well. Add 1 cup buttermilk and mix until a smooth batter forms. Drop tablespoonfuls of the batter onto a hot skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Recipe 4: Bacon and Chive Savory Pancake Bites

Combine 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 4 slices of cooked bacon, chopped, 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a bowl. Mix well. Add 1 cup buttermilk and mix until a smooth batter forms. Drop tablespoonfuls of the batter onto a hot skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Recipe 5: Sun-dried Tomato and Basil Savory Pancake Bites

Combine 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a bowl. Mix well. Add 1 cup buttermilk and mix until a smooth batter forms. Drop tablespoonfuls of the batter onto a hot skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Recipe 6: Broccoli and Cheddar Savory Pancake Bites

Combine 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 cup steamed broccoli florets, 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a bowl. Mix well. Add 1 cup buttermilk and mix until a smooth batter forms. Drop tablespoonfuls of the batter onto a hot skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Recipe 7: Capers and Feta Savory Pancake Bites

Combine 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/4 cup chopped capers, 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a bowl. Mix well. Add 1 cup buttermilk and mix until a smooth batter forms. Drop tablespoonfuls of the batter onto a hot skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Recipe 8: Roasted Garlic and Rosemary Savory Pancake Bites

Combine 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 cloves roasted garlic, minced, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a bowl. Mix well. Add 1 cup buttermilk and mix until a smooth batter forms. Drop tablespoonfuls of the batter onto a hot skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Savory pancake bites are a versatile and delicious addition to any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a party, attending a potluck, or just looking for a quick and easy snack, these recipes are sure to impress. With their crispy exterior and fluffy interior, savory pancake bites are the perfect combination of flavors and textures. So go ahead, get creative, and start experimenting with different ingredients and flavor combinations to create your own unique savory pancake bite recipes!
