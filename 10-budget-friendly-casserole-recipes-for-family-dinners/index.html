10 Budget-Friendly Casserole Recipes for Family Dinners


Casseroles are a fantastic option for family dinners, as they are not only delicious but also budget-friendly. These one-dish wonders are easy to make, require minimal ingredients, and can feed a crowd. Whether you’re feeding a big family or looking for leftovers to enjoy throughout the week, these 10 budget-friendly casserole recipes are sure to please everyone at the dinner table.

1. Classic Tuna Casserole

This timeless classic is a crowd-pleaser that won’t break the bank. Combine canned tuna, egg noodles, cream of mushroom soup, peas, and shredded cheese in a casserole dish. Top it off with crushed potato chips for a crispy finish. Bake until golden and bubbly, and you’ll have a comforting meal that’s ready in no time.

2. Cheesy Chicken and Rice Casserole

This hearty casserole is a surefire way to satisfy hungry tummies. Mix cooked chicken, cooked rice, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, and shredded cheddar cheese in a baking dish. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and you’ll have a creamy and cheesy dish that will have your family asking for seconds.

3. Mexican Taco Casserole

Spice up your dinner with this flavorful Mexican-inspired casserole. Layer ground beef seasoned with taco seasoning, crushed tortilla chips, black beans, corn, salsa, and shredded Mexican cheese in a baking dish. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and serve with your favorite toppings like sour cream, guacamole, and chopped cilantro.

4. Vegetable Lasagna Casserole

Who says casseroles can’t be healthy? This vegetarian-friendly option is packed with nutritious veggies and cheesy goodness. Layer lasagna noodles, spinach, zucchini, mushrooms, marinara sauce, and a mixture of ricotta and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish. Bake until the cheese is golden and bubbly, and you’ll have a flavorful and satisfying meal.

5. Broccoli and Cheese Casserole

This casserole is a fantastic way to sneak some vegetables into your family’s diet. Combine cooked broccoli florets, cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, shredded cheddar cheese, and crushed Ritz crackers in a baking dish. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and enjoy a creamy and cheesy dish that will have even the pickiest eaters asking for seconds.

6. Shepherd’s Pie Casserole

This classic comfort food is not only delicious but also budget-friendly. Brown ground beef with onions and garlic, and mix in frozen mixed vegetables and tomato sauce. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish, and top it off with mashed potatoes. Bake until the mashed potatoes are golden and crispy, and you’ll have a hearty and satisfying meal that’s perfect for a family dinner.

7. Baked Ziti Casserole

This Italian-inspired casserole is a crowd-pleaser that won’t break the bank. Combine cooked ziti pasta, marinara sauce, ground beef or sausage, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese in a baking dish. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and enjoy a cheesy and flavorful dish that will transport you straight to Italy.

8. Ham and Potato Casserole

Got leftover ham? Turn it into a delicious casserole that the whole family will love. Combine cooked diced ham, sliced potatoes, cream of mushroom soup, shredded cheddar cheese, and diced onions in a baking dish. Bake until the potatoes are tender and the cheese is melted and bubbly, and you’ll have a hearty and comforting meal that’s perfect for a cozy family dinner.

9. Eggplant Parmesan Casserole

This vegetarian casserole is a great way to enjoy the flavors of eggplant Parmesan without all the fuss. Layer sliced eggplant, marinara sauce, shredded mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese in a baking dish. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and you’ll have a flavorful and satisfying dish that’s perfect for a meatless Monday dinner.

10. Cornbread Casserole

End your family dinner on a sweet note with this delicious cornbread casserole. Combine canned cream-style corn, cornbread mix, sour cream, melted butter, and shredded cheddar cheese in a baking dish. Bake until the cornbread is golden and crispy, and enjoy a sweet and savory dish that’s perfect for any occasion.

These 10 budget-friendly casserole recipes are not only delicious but also easy on the wallet. Whether you’re cooking for a large family or looking for leftovers to enjoy throughout the week, these recipes are sure to please. So, gather your ingredients, preheat the oven, and get ready to enjoy a comforting and satisfying family dinner with these tasty casseroles.
