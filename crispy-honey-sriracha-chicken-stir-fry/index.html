Crispy Honey Sriracha Chicken Stir Fry


Are you craving a delicious and flavorful dish that combines the perfect balance of sweet, spicy, and savory flavors? Look no further than this mouthwatering Crispy Honey Sriracha Chicken Stir Fry recipe! This dish is a delightful fusion of crispy chicken coated in a sticky honey sriracha sauce, paired with colorful stir-fried vegetables. It’s a surefire way to impress your family and friends with your culinary skills!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Sesame seeds and chopped cilantro for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Coat and Fry the Chicken:

    • In a bowl, coat the chicken pieces with cornstarch until evenly coated.
    • Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add the chicken pieces in a single layer and cook until golden and crispy, about 4-5 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, sriracha sauce, and minced garlic.

  3. Stir-Fry the Vegetables:

    • In the same skillet, add a little more oil if needed.
    • Add the sliced bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots.
    • Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  4. Combine Everything Together:

    • Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables.
    • Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken and vegetables.
    • Toss everything together until well coated and heated through.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Garnish with chopped green onions, sesame seeds, and cilantro.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Serve the Crispy Honey Sriracha Chicken Stir Fry hot over steamed rice or noodles.
