One-Pot Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta: A Mediterranean Twist


If you’re craving a taste of the Mediterranean without the hassle of multiple pots and pans, then this One-Pot Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta is the answer to your culinary dreams. With the perfect blend of creamy, tangy, and savory flavors, this dish brings together the richness of sun-dried tomatoes, the succulence of chicken, and the comforting allure of pasta in one delightful pot. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring a taste of the Mediterranean right into your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup (75g) sun-dried tomatoes, drained of oil and chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 2 1/2 cups (600ml) chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups (375ml) half and half (or heavy cream for a richer sauce)
  • 8 ounces (225g) penne pasta
  • 1/2 cup (50g) freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are golden brown and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Stir in the chopped sun-dried tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, and red pepper flakes. Cook for another 2 minutes to release the flavors.

  3. Add the chicken broth and half and half (or heavy cream) to the skillet. Stir well to combine with the sun-dried tomato mixture. Bring the liquid to a simmer.

  4. Once the liquid is simmering, add the penne pasta to the skillet. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the pasta cook in the creamy sauce, stirring occasionally. Allow the pasta to simmer in the sauce until it is al dente and the sauce has thickened, which should take about 12-15 minutes.

  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it is melted and fully incorporated into the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, stirring to combine with the pasta and sauce.

  7. Garnish with freshly chopped basil or parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This One-Pot Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta is a complete meal in itself, but you can elevate the experience by serving it with a simple mixed green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette. A glass of crisp white wine, such as a Pinot Grigio or Sauvignon Blanc, would complement the flavors of the dish beautifully.

Conclusion

With its creamy texture, vibrant flavors, and the convenience of only using one pot, this recipe is a testament to the delightful simplicity of Mediterranean-inspired cuisine. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or entertaining guests, this One-Pot Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta is sure to impress. Embrace the flavors of the Mediterranean and indulge in a truly satisfying and hassle-free culinary experience.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients and let the aroma of this Mediterranean-inspired dish transport you to a sun-drenched Italian villa, right in the comfort of your own home.

Enjoy!
