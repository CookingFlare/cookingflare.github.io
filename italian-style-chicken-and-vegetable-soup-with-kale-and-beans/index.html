Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Soup with Kale and Beans


When it comes to hearty and wholesome meals, Italian cuisine never fails to deliver. This Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup with kale and beans is a comforting dish that brings together the vibrant flavors of fresh vegetables, tender chicken, and hearty beans. Whether you’re looking for a nourishing weeknight dinner or a satisfying lunch option, this soup is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 3 cups chopped kale
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté the vegetables until they begin to soften, about 5 minutes.

  3. Stir in the dried oregano, basil, and crushed red pepper flakes, and cook for another minute to release their flavors.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and diced tomatoes, and bring the mixture to a simmer.

  5. Add the browned chicken back into the pot, along with the cannellini beans. Let the soup simmer for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Stir in the chopped kale and cook for an additional 5 minutes until the kale is wilted and tender.

  7. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Ladle the Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup into bowls and serve with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top, if desired.

Why You’ll Love It

This Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup is a celebration of fresh, flavorful ingredients that come together to create a nourishing and satisfying meal. The tender chicken, vibrant vegetables, and robust beans are complemented by the earthy richness of kale, making each spoonful a delight for the senses.

Not only is this soup a delicious addition to your table, but it’s also a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your preferences. Feel free to add other vegetables such as zucchini or bell peppers, or swap the kale for spinach for a different flavor profile. Additionally, this soup can easily be made in large batches and frozen for later enjoyment, making it a convenient option for meal prep.

With its comforting aroma and robust flavors, this Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup with kale and beans is a testament to the timeless appeal of Italian cooking. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a wonderful way to explore the vibrant and comforting flavors of Italian cuisine.

So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the heartwarming goodness of this Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup. Buon appetito!
