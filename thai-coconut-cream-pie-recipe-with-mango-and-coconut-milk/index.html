Thai Coconut Cream Pie Recipe with Mango and Coconut Milk


If you’re a fan of exotic flavors and creamy desserts, then you’re in for a treat with this Thai-inspired coconut cream pie recipe. This delightful pie combines the rich, velvety texture of coconut milk with the sweet and tangy flavor of ripe mango, creating a dessert that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of something tropical, this pie is sure to impress. So, let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this heavenly dessert.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup of coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Toasted coconut flakes for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly moistened.
  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish. Use the back of a spoon to firmly press the crumbs into place.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and allow it to cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, sweetened condensed milk, and coconut cream. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture is heated through.
  2. In a small bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in water to create a slurry.
  3. Gradually whisk the cornstarch slurry into the coconut milk mixture. Continue to cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens to a pudding-like consistency.
  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Allow the coconut filling to cool for 5 minutes.
  5. Gently fold the diced mango into the coconut filling, ensuring it’s evenly distributed.

Step 3: Assemble the Pie

  1. Pour the mango-infused coconut filling into the cooled graham cracker crust, spreading it out evenly.
  2. Cover the pie with plastic wrap, ensuring the wrap touches the surface of the filling to prevent a skin from forming.
  3. Refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is set.

Step 4: Prepare the Topping

  1. In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  2. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled pie filling, creating decorative swirls with a spatula.
  3. Sprinkle toasted coconut flakes over the top of the pie for a delightful crunch and added coconut flavor.

Serving and Enjoying

Once the pie has been assembled, it’s ready to be enjoyed. With its luscious coconut and mango flavors, this pie is a refreshing dessert that’s perfect for warm summer days or as a sweet finish to a Thai-inspired meal. The creamy coconut filling, the sweetness of the mango, and the buttery graham cracker crust come together to create a harmonious blend of tropical flavors that will transport your taste buds to paradise.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of pie-making, this Thai coconut cream pie with mango and coconut milk is a delightful recipe to add to your repertoire. It’s a unique twist on a classic dessert that’s sure to impress your family and friends. So, why not give this recipe a try and indulge in a slice of tropical paradise right at home?
