Easy One-Pan Mexican Chicken and Rice


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal that’s bursting with flavor, then this Easy One-Pan Mexican Chicken and Rice recipe is just what you need. It’s a fantastic dish that brings together tender chicken, savory rice, and vibrant Mexican flavors in one pan, making it a perfect choice for a quick and satisfying dinner. Whether you’re a busy parent, a college student, or someone who simply loves convenient yet tasty meals, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. In a large skillet or frying pan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and fully cooked. Once done, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the diced onion, red bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté the vegetables for a few minutes until they are softened and fragrant.

  3. Stir in the long-grain rice, diced tomatoes (with their juices), chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, and paprika. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  4. Once the mixture is boiling, reduce the heat to low. Place the cooked chicken breasts on top of the rice and tomato mixture in the skillet. Cover the skillet with a lid and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the liquid.

  5. Once the rice is cooked and the chicken is heated through, remove the skillet from the heat. Sprinkle fresh cilantro over the dish for a burst of freshness and color.

  6. Serve the Mexican Chicken and Rice directly from the skillet, allowing everyone to dig in and enjoy the delicious flavors.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Easy One-Pan Mexican Chicken and Rice recipe is a game-changer for several reasons. Firstly, it’s incredibly convenient. By cooking everything in one pan, you minimize the clean-up time, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Secondly, the flavors in this dish are simply irresistible. The combination of tender chicken, aromatic spices, and hearty rice creates a mouthwatering experience that’s hard to resist.

Moreover, this recipe is highly versatile. You can customize it to suit your preferences by adding ingredients such as black beans, corn, or jalapeños for an extra kick. It’s also a great dish for meal prepping, as it reheats beautifully and can be enjoyed as leftovers for lunch the next day.

The Easy One-Pan Mexican Chicken and Rice is a fantastic way to bring the vibrant and bold flavors of Mexican cuisine to your table without spending hours in the kitchen. It’s a crowd-pleaser that’s bound to impress your family and friends.

So, next time you’re craving a delicious and stress-free meal, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful flavors of Mexico right in your own kitchen.

Enjoy!
