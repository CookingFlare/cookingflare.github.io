Pasta with Creamy Lemon Herb Shrimp: A Tangy and Savory Seafood Delight


If you’re a seafood lover, you’re in for a treat with this Pasta with Creamy Lemon Herb Shrimp recipe. It’s a delightful combination of tangy lemon, aromatic herbs, and succulent shrimp, all brought together in a creamy sauce that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just craving a comforting meal, this dish is sure to impress. So, let’s dive into the details and bring this savory seafood delight to your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

  • 12 oz (340g) linguine or fettuccine
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

For the Creamy Lemon Herb Shrimp:

  • 1 lb (450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) chicken broth
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pasta:

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the pasta water, and toss it with a drizzle of olive oil to prevent sticking. Set aside.

  2. Cook the Shrimp:

    • Season the shrimp with salt and black pepper.
    • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
    • Add the shrimp in a single layer and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until pink and opaque.
    • Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Creamy Lemon Herb Sauce:

    • In the same skillet, add a bit more olive oil if needed and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
    • Pour in the chicken broth and let it simmer for a minute, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.
    • Reduce the heat and stir in the heavy cream, lemon zest, lemon juice, oregano, and thyme. Let the sauce simmer and thicken for a few minutes.

  4. Combine the Pasta and Shrimp:

    • Add the cooked shrimp back to the skillet and toss to coat in the creamy sauce.
    • Gently fold in the cooked pasta, adding some of the reserved pasta water as needed to loosen the sauce and coat the pasta evenly.
    • Season with additional salt and black pepper to taste.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Transfer the pasta to a serving dish and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.
    • Serve hot and savor the delightful combination of flavors in each creamy, tangy, and savory bite.

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize this recipe by adding a bit of red pepper flakes for a subtle kick of heat or incorporating your favorite fresh herbs, such as basil or dill, to enhance the flavor profile.
  • For a lighter version, you can substitute half-and-half for the heavy cream, although the sauce may not be as rich and creamy.

Final Thoughts

Pasta with Creamy Lemon Herb Shrimp is a perfect marriage of flavors, textures, and aromas that will elevate your dining experience. From the zing of lemon to the earthy notes of the herbs and the succulence of the shrimp, every element plays a crucial role in creating a truly memorable dish. So, the next time you’re in the mood for a seafood delight, consider whipping up this recipe and delighting your senses with a burst of tangy and savory goodness.
