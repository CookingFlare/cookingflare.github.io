Greek Style Roast Chicken: A Festive Main Course


Looking for a show-stopping main course for your next special occasion? Look no further than this Greek Style Roast Chicken recipe. Bursting with flavors of the Mediterranean, this dish combines succulent roast chicken with aromatic herbs and zesty lemon. It’s the perfect centerpiece for any festive gathering, impressing your guests with its vibrant flavors and delightful presentation.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (approximately 4-5 pounds)
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Mix well to create a flavorful herb paste.

  3. Place the whole chicken on a clean cutting board and pat it dry with paper towels. This will help the skin crisp up during roasting.

  4. Carefully loosen the skin from the meat of the chicken by sliding your fingers gently between the two. Be careful not to tear the skin.

  5. Spread the herb paste evenly under the chicken skin, ensuring it covers as much of the meat as possible. This will infuse the chicken with amazing flavors as it roasts.

  6. Season the chicken generously with salt and pepper, both inside and out.

  7. Stuff the cavity of the chicken with the thinly sliced lemon. This will add a tangy brightness to the dish.

  8. Place the seasoned and stuffed chicken on a roasting rack in a large roasting pan.

  9. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) when tested with a meat thermometer.

  10. Baste the chicken with the pan juices every 30 minutes to keep it moist and flavorful.

  11. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before carving. This allows the juices to redistribute and ensures a tender and juicy roast.

  12. Carve the chicken into serving pieces and arrange them on a platter. Garnish with fresh herbs, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This Greek Style Roast Chicken pairs beautifully with a variety of side dishes. Here are a few suggestions to complete your festive meal:

  • Lemon and Herb Roasted Potatoes: Toss baby potatoes with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and fresh herbs. Roast until golden and crispy.

  • Greek Salad: Combine fresh tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese. Dress with olive oil, lemon juice, and oregano.

  • Tzatziki Sauce: Serve a refreshing bowl of tzatziki on the side. This creamy yogurt and cucumber dip adds a cool and tangy element to the meal.

Conclusion

With its tantalizing flavors and eye-catching presentation, this Greek Style Roast Chicken is sure to become a favorite at your festive gatherings. The combination of aromatic herbs, zesty lemon, and succulent roast chicken creates a truly memorable main course. So gather your loved ones, raise a glass of wine, and savor the delicious tastes of Greece with this festive dish. Opa!
