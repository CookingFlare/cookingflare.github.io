Tara's Treats: The Witch's Brew (Meringue Cookies)


Ah, Halloween – a season that both children and adults alike look forward to with great anticipation. The eerie moonlight, the thrilling costumes, and undoubtedly, the delectable treats! This year, why not try something extra special? Something that’ll whisk you away to a forest of mystery and magic? How about whipping up a batch of Tara’s Treats: The Witch’s Brew Meringue Cookies? These delightful confections are sure to bewitch everyone who bites into them. So, let’s put our cauldron aside and venture into the kitchen to conjure up these enchanting cookies, shall we?

Mystical Ingredients:

Before we get our whisking arms a-churn, let’s gather the ingredients needed to brew this bewitching concoction.

  • 3 large egg whites
  • 1/8 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • Optional:Food coloring (green, for a witchy touch)

Now, let the spellbinding journey begin!

Magical Steps:

Step 1: Preparing the Magical Mix Separate the egg whites from the yolks and place them in a large, clean, and grease-free bowl. Add the cream of tartar to the egg whites, stirring gently to combine. Why the witches’ brew needs a dollop of cream of tartar, you ask? Keep reading.

Using a handheld or stand mixer, beat the egg whites and cream of tartar on medium speed until they become foamy.

Step 2: Invoking the Sugar Spirit Gradually add the white granulated sugar to the foamy egg whites, one tablespoon at a time, and continue beating on medium speed. This step will transform your mixture into a stiff and glossy meringue, looking like the silkiest of spirit perched atop a cloud.

Step 3: Incorporating the Essence Now, it’s time to infuse your meringue with a touch of the mysterious. Add a drop or two of food coloring (for that bewitching green hue) and your trusty teaspoon of pure vanilla extract to the meringue. Can you taste the enchantment brewing, my fellow witch?

Step 4: The Big Whisk Using a clean whisk, gently fold the cookies into the desired shape. Surely, your meringue might now resemble a splendid cauldron. Gently place them onto parchment-lined cookie sheets, just like casting a spell to let your cookies bake without a hitch.

Step 5: The Cauldron Swirls Gently swirl the cookies with a spoon, piping tip or even a finger-tipped ink dot (told you it’s a mystery) to create magical designs, symbolic of the witchy forest coming alive.

Step 6: Into the Oven they Go! Preheat your oven to a witchy 200°F (100°C) and place your trays inside. Carefully, as these mystical cookies have to bake undisturbed for two mind-twisting hours or until they become firm and dry.

Step 7: The Bewitching Unveiling Once you’ve reached the intended bewitching hour, switch off the oven and allow the meringue cookies to sit inside with the door propped open for another hour. This precaution ensures your cookies retain their enchantment: crisp on the outside, and soft as whispers on the inside.

The Witch’s Brew: Tantalizing Tally-Ho Toppers!

For that lilac touch of whimsy and witchcraft, you may garnish your cookies with edible glitter, crushed candy, or zealously sprinkle them with a dusting of cocoa powder.

And there you have it! Tara’s Treats: The Witch’s Brew Meringue Cookies! As you cutely package these treats into little cauldrons or decorate your serving platter, the sense of enchantment and creativity that went into crafting these is unparalleled.

So, conjure up your cauldron of cookies this Halloween, and let the inquisitive eyes of costumed trick-or-treaters be bewitched by a spellbinding taste of Tara’s Treats.

As they disappear into their own kooky costumes, think back upon the magical moments in the making of The Witch’s Brew Meringue Cookies – and may your Halloween be one of unending enchantment, with every crispy, scrumptious bite!
