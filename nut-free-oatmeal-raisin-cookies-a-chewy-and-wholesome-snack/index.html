Nut-Free Oatmeal Raisin Cookies: A Chewy and Wholesome Snack


If you’re on the lookout for a delicious and healthy snack that’s both nut-free and packed with flavor, then look no further than these Nut-Free Oatmeal Raisin Cookies! These chewy and wholesome treats are perfect for anyone with nut allergies or dietary restrictions, and they are bound to satisfy your sweet tooth without compromising on taste.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to whip up a batch of these delightful oatmeal raisin cookies:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the rolled oats, all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, ground cinnamon, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. You can use an electric mixer or do it by hand with a wooden spoon.

  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the butter and sugar mixture, and mix until well combined.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet mixture, stirring until just incorporated. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

  6. Gently fold in the raisins until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Using a tablespoon or a cookie scoop, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  8. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for approximately 10-12 minutes, or until they are lightly golden around the edges.

  9. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once cooled, these nut-free oatmeal raisin cookies are ready to be enjoyed! Store them in an airtight container to maintain their freshness.

Why Choose Nut-Free Oatmeal Raisin Cookies?

  1. Allergy-Friendly: These cookies are perfect for individuals with nut allergies or dietary restrictions, as they are completely nut-free.

  2. Wholesome Ingredients: Made with rolled oats and raisins, these cookies are a healthier alternative to traditional store-bought treats.

  3. Chewy Texture: The combination of rolled oats and raisins creates a delightful chewiness that is sure to satisfy your cravings.

  4. Versatile Snack: These cookies are not only great for snacking on their own, but they can also be enjoyed with a glass of milk or crumbled over yogurt for added texture and flavor.

So, the next time you’re in need of a nut-free and delicious snack, give these Nut-Free Oatmeal Raisin Cookies a try. With their wholesome ingredients and chewy texture, they are sure to become a family favorite!
