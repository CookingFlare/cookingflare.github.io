Keto Yoga: Beautiful & Vibrant Low-Carb Recipes for the Health Enthusiast


Have you ever felt overwhelmed by the abundance of unhealthy snacks available when you’re in a rush or craving something delicious? Have you tried countless diets and exercises with absolutely no rewarding results? Fret not, because today we are going to embark on a delightful culinary journey we lovingly call Keto Yoga! This wonderful fusion of the ketogenic diet and yoga poses allows your body to reap the benefits of both worlds. Don’t worry if you think you can’t stomach the restrictive nature of a low-carb diet; our beautiful and vibrant low-carb recipes are too tasty to resist and will replace your sweet cravings with savory sensations.

To kickstart our Keto Yoga adventure, let’s dive into our first creation: the mouthwatering Shawarma Bowl. Packed with scrumptious marinated chicken, zesty cucumber-zucchini noodles, creamy cauliflower tzatziki, and the refreshing touch of mint and parsley, our Shawarma Bowl is genuinely a feast on a plate. Let’s prepare our powerhouse of nutrients today!

Shawarma Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 zucchini, spiraled into noodles
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice or riced cauliflower (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon chopped dill
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley and mint, chopped

Instructions:

  1. To prepare the chicken, whisk together olive oil, garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper in a shallow dish. Place the chicken in the mixture and marinate for 1 hour.

  2. Preheat a grill or large skillet over medium heat. Cook the marinated chicken for 6-7 minutes per side or until fully cooked. When done, slice the chicken into thin strips and set it aside.

  3. In a bowl, mix together the spiralized zucchini, cucumber, olive oil, salt, and pepper for the zesty noodles, and set it aside.

  4. Prepare the cauliflower tzatziki by combining cauliflower rice, Greek yogurt, chopped cucumber, garlic, dill, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a food processor or blender. Pulse until you have a smooth, delicious mixture. Refrigerate until ready for use.

  5. Assemble your Shawarma Bowl by placing the zesty cucumber-zucchini noodles at the bottom, followed by the sliced marinated chicken, and a generous dollop of the creamy cauliflower tzatziki. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and mint.
