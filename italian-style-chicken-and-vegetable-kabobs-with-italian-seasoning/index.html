Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Kabobs with Italian Seasoning


If you’re looking for a delicious and flavorful dish that’s perfect for summer grilling, these Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs with Italian seasoning are just what you need. Packed with vibrant colors and bold flavors, these kabobs are sure to impress your family and friends at your next backyard barbecue.

Ingredients

For the chicken marinade:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the kabobs:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 8-10 cherry tomatoes

For the Italian seasoning:

  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, dried Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper to make the chicken marinade.

  2. Place the chicken pieces in a Ziploc bag or a bowl and pour the marinade over them. Toss to coat the chicken evenly. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours for maximum flavor.

  3. While the chicken is marinating, prepare the Italian seasoning by combining all the dried herbs and spices together in a small bowl. Set aside.

  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  5. Thread the marinated chicken, bell peppers, red onion, zucchini, yellow squash, and cherry tomatoes onto skewers, alternating between the different ingredients. This will create a beautiful and colorful presentation.

  6. Sprinkle the kabobs generously with the homemade Italian seasoning, making sure to coat all sides of the chicken and vegetables. The aromatic blend of herbs and spices will elevate the flavors of the kabobs.

  7. Place the kabobs on the preheated grill and cook for about 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

  8. Once cooked, remove the kabobs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute, ensuring moist and succulent chicken.

  9. Serve the Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs hot off the grill, garnished with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of freshness. These kabobs are delicious on their own, or you can serve them with a side of couscous or a fresh green salad.

Enjoy the explosion of flavors in every bite! These Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs are a fantastic addition to your summer grilling repertoire. Impress your guests with a dish that combines the vibrant colors of the Mediterranean with the aromatic blend of Italian herbs and spices. Fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in a taste of Italy right in your own backyard.
