Flavorful Low Carb Greek Chicken Salad


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s easy to make and perfect for a summer day? Look no further than this Flavorful Low Carb Greek Chicken Salad recipe! Packed with fresh ingredients and bursting with Mediterranean flavors, this salad is a crowd-pleaser that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized. Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply want to enjoy a light and flavorful dish, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

Start by marinating the chicken breasts with dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes to infuse it with the aromatic flavors of the herbs.

Step 2: Grill the Chicken

Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the marinated chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through and have a nice char on the outside. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

In a large mixing bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, thinly sliced red onion, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese. Toss the ingredients together gently to distribute the flavors evenly.

Step 4: Prepare the Dressing

In a small bowl, whisk together 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar, a pinch of dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create a simple and tangy dressing.

Step 5: Bring It All Together

Add the mixed salad greens to the bowl of vegetables and feta cheese. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss everything together until the ingredients are well coated with the dressing.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Divide the salad onto serving plates and top each portion with the sliced grilled chicken. Garnish with an extra sprinkle of feta cheese and a few Kalamata olives for an extra burst of flavor. Serve immediately and enjoy the delightful combination of fresh, vibrant ingredients.

Nutritional Information

This Flavorful Low Carb Greek Chicken Salad is not only delicious but also nutritious. Packed with lean protein, healthy fats, and a variety of vitamins and minerals from the vegetables, this salad makes for a well-rounded meal that’s low in carbohydrates and high in flavor.

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Fiber: 4g

Final Thoughts

This Flavorful Low Carb Greek Chicken Salad is a perfect example of how healthy eating can be both satisfying and delicious. Whether you’re preparing it for a quick weeknight dinner or serving it at a summer gathering, this salad is sure to impress. The combination of tender grilled chicken, crisp vegetables, creamy feta cheese, and tangy dressing creates a symphony of flavors that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean. So, next time you’re craving a light and refreshing meal, give this recipe a try and savor the vibrant taste of Greece right in your own kitchen!
