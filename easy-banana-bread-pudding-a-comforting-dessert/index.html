Easy Banana Bread Pudding: A Comforting Dessert


Are you a fan of comfort food? Do you have a sweet tooth that craves indulgent desserts? If so, then you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m going to share with you a recipe for Easy Banana Bread Pudding, a dessert that will warm your heart and satisfy your cravings. This delightful combination of moist banana bread and rich custard is the perfect way to end any meal.

Ingredients

To make this scrumptious Banana Bread Pudding, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 cups of day-old banana bread, cubed
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray to prevent sticking.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, milk, heavy cream, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and salt. Whisk until well combined and the sugar has dissolved.

  3. Add the cubed banana bread to the mixing bowl and gently fold it into the custard mixture, ensuring that all the bread pieces are coated evenly.

  4. Allow the mixture to sit for about 10 minutes, allowing the bread to absorb the custard.

  5. Transfer the banana bread and custard mixture into the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the pudding is set. You can test the doneness by inserting a toothpick into the center of the pudding – if it comes out clean, it’s ready!

  7. Once the Banana Bread Pudding is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Serve warm and enjoy the comforting flavors of this delightful dessert. You can choose to serve it as is or add a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent touch.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some crunch to your Banana Bread Pudding, you can sprinkle some chopped nuts, such as walnuts or pecans, on top before baking.

  • For a burst of fruity goodness, you can add a handful of raisins or dried cranberries to the custard mixture.

  • If you have some leftover caramel sauce or chocolate sauce, drizzle it over the warm banana bread pudding before serving for an extra decadent treat.

  • This Banana Bread Pudding can be stored in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat it in the microwave or oven before serving.

Conclusion

Banana Bread Pudding is a timeless dessert that combines the familiar flavors of banana bread with the creamy richness of custard. It’s a dessert that brings comfort and indulgence to the table, making it perfect for special occasions or simply for satisfying your sweet cravings. With its easy preparation and delicious taste, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. So why wait? Gather the ingredients and give this Easy Banana Bread Pudding a try – you won’t be disappointed!
