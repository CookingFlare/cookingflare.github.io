Mouthwatering Low Carb Avocado Egg Salad


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb meal that’s packed with flavor and nutrients? Look no further than this mouthwatering low carb avocado egg salad recipe. This dish is not only easy to make but also incredibly versatile, making it perfect for a quick lunch, a light dinner, or as a side dish for a summer BBQ. Let’s dive into the recipe and uncover the secrets to making this delightful dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Eggs: Start by boiling the eggs until they are hard-boiled. Once cooled, peel and chop them into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Combine the Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, gently combine the chopped eggs, diced avocados, red onion, celery, and fresh cilantro.

  3. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and fresh lemon juice until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Mix It All Together: Pour the dressing over the avocado and egg mixture, gently tossing to coat everything evenly. Be careful not to mash the avocados completely, as you want to maintain some texture.

  5. Chill and Serve: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Once chilled, give the salad a final gentle toss and serve.

Serving Suggestions:

This low carb avocado egg salad is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • As a Salad: Serve the avocado egg salad over a bed of fresh mixed greens for a light and refreshing salad.
  • In Lettuce Wraps: Spoon the salad into large lettuce leaves and roll them up for a satisfying low-carb wrap.
  • On Whole Grain Toast: Spread the avocado egg salad on whole grain toast for a quick and nutritious breakfast or snack.
  • With Grilled Proteins: Pair the salad with grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a complete and wholesome meal.

Nutritional Benefits:

This avocado egg salad is not only delicious but also offers a range of nutritional benefits. Avocados are rich in heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, fiber, and various vitamins and minerals. Eggs are a great source of high-quality protein, essential amino acids, and important nutrients like vitamin D and choline. Together, they make a nutrient-dense and satisfying combination that’s perfect for anyone looking to maintain a low-carb lifestyle.

Final Thoughts:

Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a new way to enjoy avocados and eggs, this mouthwatering low carb avocado egg salad is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. It’s easy to make, packed with flavor, and offers a satisfying combination of textures and nutrients. Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite herbs, spices, or additional vegetables to make it your own. Enjoy this delightful dish and savor the goodness of wholesome ingredients in every bite!
