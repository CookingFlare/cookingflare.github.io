Crispy Fried Pickles: A Unique and Delicious Snack


If you’re a fan of pickles and love a good crunch, then crispy fried pickles might just become your new favorite snack. This unique twist on a classic pickle brings together the tangy flavor of pickles with a crispy, golden coating that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a game day gathering, planning a movie night in, or just craving a tasty snack, these crispy fried pickles are sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

To make crispy fried pickles, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • Dill pickles, sliced into thin rounds
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Buttermilk
  • Egg
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pickles

Start by draining the pickle slices on a paper towel to remove excess moisture. This step is crucial to ensure that the coating adheres well to the pickles and results in a crispy texture.

Step 2: Prepare the Coating

In a shallow dish, combine the all-purpose flour, cornmeal, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. This flavorful coating will give the pickles that irresistible crunch and zesty flavor.

Step 3: Dip and Coat

In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk and egg. Dip each pickle slice into the buttermilk and egg mixture, allowing any excess to drip off. Then, coat the pickle slice in the seasoned flour and cornmeal mixture, ensuring that it’s evenly coated on all sides.

Step 4: Fry to Perfection

In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Carefully place the coated pickle slices into the hot oil, being mindful not to overcrowd the skillet. Fry the pickles until they are golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side. Once fried to perfection, remove the pickles from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Arrange the crispy fried pickles on a serving platter and pair them with your favorite dipping sauce. Whether you prefer a classic ranch dressing, a zesty chipotle mayo, or a tangy dill sauce, the crispy fried pickles are the perfect canvas for your favorite dipping accompaniment. Serve them hot and watch them disappear in no time.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a pinch of cayenne pepper to the seasoned flour and cornmeal mixture.
  • Experiment with different types of pickles, such as bread and butter pickles or spicy dill pickles, to create unique flavor combinations.
  • If you’re looking to lighten up the recipe, try baking the coated pickle slices in the oven instead of frying them. Simply place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake at 425°F until golden and crispy.

Crispy fried pickles are a crowd-pleasing snack that’s easy to make and impossible to resist. Whether you’re enjoying them as an appetizer, a side dish, or a late-night snack, these golden, crunchy bites of tangy goodness are sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients, heat up the oil, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible delight of crispy fried pickles. Cheers to snacking in style!
