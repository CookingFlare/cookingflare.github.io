Delicious Picnic Stuffed Zucchini Recipes


Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make dish that’s perfect for your next picnic? Look no further than these mouthwatering stuffed zucchinis! Packed with flavor and nutrients, these recipes will surely impress your friends and family. Whether you’re a vegetarian or a meat lover, there’s a stuffed zucchini recipe here for everyone. So grab your picnic blanket and let’s get cooking!

Classic Italian Stuffed Zucchini

If you’re a fan of Italian flavors, this classic stuffed zucchini recipe is a must-try. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 1 cup Italian sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds to create a hollow cavity.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the cooked Italian sausage, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, basil, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff each zucchini half with the mixture, pressing it down gently.
  5. Place the stuffed zucchinis on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchinis are tender and the filling is golden brown.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Mediterranean Quinoa Stuffed Zucchini

For a lighter and vegetarian option, try this Mediterranean-inspired quinoa stuffed zucchini. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds to create a hollow cavity.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the cooked quinoa, feta cheese, Kalamata olives, sun-dried tomatoes, parsley, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff each zucchini half with the mixture, pressing it down gently.
  5. Place the stuffed zucchinis on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchinis are tender and the filling is slightly golden.
  6. Serve warm or at room temperature for a refreshing and nutritious picnic treat.

Tex-Mex Stuffed Zucchini Boats

If you’re craving some Tex-Mex flavors, these stuffed zucchini boats will hit the spot. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 1 cup ground beef or turkey, cooked and seasoned with taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds to create a hollow cavity.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the cooked ground beef or turkey, black beans, corn kernels, bell peppers, cilantro, salsa, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff each zucchini half with the mixture, pressing it down gently.
  5. Place the stuffed zucchini boats on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchinis are tender and the filling is heated through.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the top and return to the oven for an additional 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve hot with a dollop of sour cream and some extra salsa for a Tex-Mex fiesta!

These stuffed zucchini recipes are not only delicious but also versatile. Feel free to experiment with different fillings and seasonings to suit your taste. Whether you’re planning a picnic or simply looking for a satisfying meal, these stuffed zucchinis will surely become a family favorite. So grab your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a flavorful and healthy treat!
