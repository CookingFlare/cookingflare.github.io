Wholesome Oatmeal Pancakes Recipe for a Healthy Christmas Breakfast


‘Tis the season for delicious breakfasts that warm the heart and nourish the soul. As Christmas morning approaches, what better way to start the day than with a stack of wholesome oatmeal pancakes? This recipe is not only a treat for the taste buds but also a nutritious choice for a joyful and healthy holiday breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Cooking spray or butter for greasing the pan

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the rolled oats and buttermilk. Let the mixture soak for at least 15 minutes, allowing the oats to soften and absorb the buttermilk.

  2. After the oats have soaked, add the eggs, honey, and vanilla extract to the bowl. Whisk the ingredients together until well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, sift together the whole wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the oat mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter, as this can result in tough pancakes.

  4. Heat a non-stick griddle or skillet over medium heat and lightly coat it with cooking spray or butter.

  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until the edges begin to look set and bubbles form on the surface, then flip the pancakes and cook until golden brown on the other side.

  6. Continue this process until all the batter has been used, keeping the cooked pancakes warm in a low oven while you finish cooking the rest.

  7. Serve the oatmeal pancakes warm, topped with your favorite fruits, nuts, yogurt, or a drizzle of pure maple syrup.

Nutritional Benefits:

These wholesome oatmeal pancakes are not only delicious but also pack a nutritional punch. Rolled oats are a great source of fiber, which can help keep you feeling full and satisfied throughout the morning. The whole wheat flour adds additional fiber and nutrients, while the eggs provide a good source of protein. By using honey as a natural sweetener, you can indulge in a sweet treat without the guilt of refined sugars.

Make It Festive:

To add a festive touch to your Christmas breakfast, consider incorporating seasonal fruits such as cranberries, pomegranate seeds, or sliced persimmons into the pancake batter. You can also sprinkle a hint of nutmeg or ginger into the batter for a warm and cozy holiday flavor.

Conclusion:

This Wholesome Oatmeal Pancakes Recipe is a delightful way to celebrate Christmas morning with a healthy and satisfying breakfast. Whether you’re enjoying a quiet morning with loved ones or hosting a festive brunch, these pancakes are sure to bring joy to the table. So, this holiday season, treat yourself and your loved ones to a stack of nourishing oatmeal pancakes and savor the simple pleasures of a healthy and happy Christmas breakfast.
