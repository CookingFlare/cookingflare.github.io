Savory Thanksgiving Ham Glaze Recipes for a Flavorful Feast


Thanksgiving is just around the corner, and what better way to impress your guests than with a deliciously glazed ham as the centerpiece of your feast? A well-glazed ham not only adds a touch of elegance to your table but also infuses your Thanksgiving meal with amazing flavors. In this blog post, we will explore three savory Thanksgiving ham glaze recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave your guests craving for more.

1. Maple Mustard Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the maple syrup, Dijon mustard, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, cinnamon, and cloves.
  3. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the brown sugar has dissolved and the glaze has thickened slightly.
  4. Place the ham on a rack in a roasting pan and brush the glaze generously over the entire surface of the ham.
  5. Bake the ham in the preheated oven, basting with the glaze every 20 minutes, until the internal temperature reaches 140°F (60°C), about 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
  6. Remove the ham from the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.

2. Orange Honey Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the orange juice, honey, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, orange zest, and ground ginger.
  3. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the honey has dissolved and the glaze has thickened slightly.
  4. Place the ham on a rack in a roasting pan and brush the glaze generously over the entire surface of the ham.
  5. Bake the ham in the preheated oven, basting with the glaze every 20 minutes, until the internal temperature reaches 140°F (60°C), about 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
  6. Remove the ham from the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.

3. Balsamic Cranberry Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cranberry sauce
  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the cranberry sauce, balsamic vinegar, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, dried thyme, and black pepper.
  3. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the brown sugar has dissolved and the glaze has thickened slightly.
  4. Place the ham on a rack in a roasting pan and brush the glaze generously over the entire surface of the ham.
  5. Bake the ham in the preheated oven, basting with the glaze every 20 minutes, until the internal temperature reaches 140°F (60°C), about 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
  6. Remove the ham from the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.

These savory Thanksgiving ham glaze recipes are sure to elevate your holiday meal to new heights. Whether you prefer the sweet and tangy combination of maple mustard, the citrusy notes of orange honey, or the delightful blend of balsamic cranberry, each glaze will add a burst of flavor to your ham. So, go ahead and impress your guests with these delectable glaze recipes. Happy Thanksgiving!
