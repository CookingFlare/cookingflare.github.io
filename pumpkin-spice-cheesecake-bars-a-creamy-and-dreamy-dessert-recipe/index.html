Pumpkin Spice Cheesecake Bars: A Creamy and Dreamy Dessert Recipe


As the leaves start to change colors and the crisp autumn air sets in, our thoughts turn to warm, comforting desserts that evoke the cozy feeling of a crackling fire on a chilly evening. What better way to satisfy our sweet tooth than with a creamy and dreamy Pumpkin Spice Cheesecake Bar? These bars are a masterclass in textures, with a crumbly oat and brown sugar crust giving way to a silky-smooth cheesecake filling infused with the unmistakable flavors of pumpkin, cinnamon, and nutmeg.

The Ingredients

To make these Pumpkin Spice Cheesecake Bars, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Salt, to taste

The Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Line an 8-inch square baking dish with parchment paper or aluminum foil, leaving some overhang for easy removal.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, oats, brown sugar, and granulated sugar. Add the melted butter and stir until the mixture forms a crumbly mixture. Press the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  3. Bake the crust for 20-25 minutes, or until it’s lightly golden brown. Let it cool completely.
  4. In a large bowl, beat the eggs until they’re light and fluffy. Add the pumpkin puree, cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger, and beat until well combined.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and granulated sugar until the sugar is dissolved. Beat in the cream cheese until smooth, then beat in the vanilla extract.
  6. Add the cream cheese mixture to the pumpkin mixture, and beat until well combined. Pour the mixture over the cooled crust.
  7. Bake the bars for 35-40 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is just slightly jiggly. Let them cool completely in the pan.
  8. Once the bars have cooled, refrigerate them for at least 4 hours or overnight. Cut them into bars and serve.

Tips and Variations

  • To make these bars more festive, you can top them with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon or nutmeg.
  • If you want to make these bars more substantial, you can add some chopped nuts or chocolate chips to the crust or cheesecake mixture.
  • If you’re looking for a different flavor profile, you can substitute the pumpkin puree with butternut squash or sweet potato puree.

Conclusion

These Pumpkin Spice Cheesecake Bars are a showstopper, with their creamy, dreamy filling and crumbly, oat-based crust. Whether you’re serving them at a holiday party or just want a sweet treat to brighten up your day, these bars are sure to impress. So go ahead, get baking, and indulge in the warm, comforting flavors of the season!
