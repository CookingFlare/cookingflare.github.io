Sizzling Szechuan Beef Stir Fry


Are you craving a mouthwatering dish that combines bold flavors with a hint of spice? Look no further than this tantalizing Szechuan Beef Stir Fry recipe. Packed with umami goodness and a symphony of textures, this dish is sure to become a favorite at your dining table. Let’s dive into the world of Szechuan cuisine and learn how to whip up this delectable stir fry in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef (flank steak works best)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
  • 2 teaspoons of cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2-3 dried red chilies (adjust to taste)
  • Szechuan peppercorns, crushed
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice (to serve)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Beef:

    • Slice the beef thinly against the grain and place it in a bowl.
    • In a separate bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, hoisin sauce, cornstarch, and sugar.
    • Pour this marinade over the beef, ensuring each slice is well coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Stir Fry:

    • Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
    • Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and stir fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
    • Toss in the marinated beef and stir fry until it is browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside.

  3. Vegetable Medley:

    • In the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed.
    • Stir fry the sliced bell peppers, onion, dried red chilies, and crushed Szechuan peppercorns until the vegetables are tender yet still crisp.

  4. Bring It All Together:

    • Return the cooked beef to the wok with the vegetables.
    • Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
    • Adjust seasoning with more soy sauce or hoisin sauce if desired.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Garnish your Szechuan Beef Stir Fry with chopped green onions.
    • Serve hot over steamed rice and savor the explosion of flavors with each bite.

Tips:

  • For an extra kick of heat, you can add more dried red chilies or a drizzle of chili oil.
  • Szechuan peppercorns lend a unique numbing sensation to the dish. Use sparingly if you’re not accustomed to the flavor.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir fry based on your preferences. Broccoli, mushrooms, or snap peas make excellent additions.

There you have it – a recipe that brings the vibrant flavors of Szechuan cuisine to your dinner table. This Sizzling Szechuan Beef Stir Fry is a perfect balance of savory, sweet, and spicy notes that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Give it a try and let the aromas of this dish transport you to the bustling streets of Szechuan province. Happy cooking and even happier eating!
