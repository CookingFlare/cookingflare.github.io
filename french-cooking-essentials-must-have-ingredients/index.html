Essential Vegetables and Fruits The richness of French cuisine often comes from a well-crafted balance between the perfect vegetables and fruits selected to elevate the textures and tones of popular ingredients.

Shallots and onions: Used to add layers of flavor and aroma to countless sauces, soups, and stews. Spices and Seasonings A variety of spices and seasonings are used in French cuisine to add depth, complexity, and flavor to dishes.
