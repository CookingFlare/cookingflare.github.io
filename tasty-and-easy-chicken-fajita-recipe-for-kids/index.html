Tasty and Easy Chicken Fajita Recipe for Kids


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that your kids will love? Look no further! Our Tasty and Easy Chicken Fajita Recipe is sure to be a hit with even the pickiest eaters. Packed with flavor and nutrition, this dish is perfect for family dinners or lunchboxes. Plus, it’s incredibly simple to make! Let’s get started.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small flour tortillas

Instructions

  1. Begin by preparing your ingredients. Thinly slice the chicken breasts, bell peppers, and onion. Set them aside for later use.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the sliced chicken breasts and cook until they are no longer pink in the center. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  3. Next, add the sliced bell peppers and onion to the skillet. Sprinkle in the chili powder, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together to ensure the spices are evenly distributed among the ingredients.

  4. Continue cooking the mixture for an additional 5 minutes, or until the peppers and onions have softened slightly. Be sure to stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  5. While the chicken and vegetables cook, warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave according to the package instructions. This will ensure they are soft and pliable for folding.

  6. Once the chicken and vegetables are cooked to perfection, it’s time to assemble the fajitas! Place a spoonful of the chicken and vegetable mixture onto each tortilla, and fold it in half to create a pocket.

  7. Serve the fajitas warm and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your family’s taste preferences. You can add additional spices like oregano or cayenne pepper for an extra kick, or even swap out the chicken for shrimp or tofu for a vegetarian twist.

  • To make these fajitas even more kid-friendly, provide a variety of toppings for your little ones to choose from. Sliced avocado, shredded cheese, and sour cream are all great options to enhance the flavor and texture of the fajitas.

  • If you’re short on time, you can prepare the chicken and vegetable mixture in advance and store it in the refrigerator. Simply reheat it when you’re ready to assemble the fajitas.

  • Don’t forget to involve your kids in the cooking process! Let them help with slicing the vegetables or assembling the fajitas. It’s a great way to teach them about healthy eating and make mealtime a fun and interactive experience.

Final Thoughts

Our Tasty and Easy Chicken Fajita Recipe for Kids is a winner when it comes to satisfying both young and old taste buds. With its vibrant colors, flavorful spices, and customizable options, it’s a meal that will keep your kids excited about eating healthy. So, why not give it a try and watch as your little ones gobble up these delicious fajitas? Happy cooking!
