Mediterranean Olive Focaccia: A Taste of the Mediterranean


If you’re a fan of Mediterranean cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious recipe for Mediterranean Olive Focaccia. Bursting with flavors from the region, this savory bread is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re serving it as an appetizer, enjoying it with a bowl of soup, or using it as a base for your favorite sandwich, this focaccia is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your apron, and let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup pitted and sliced Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh rosemary
  • Coarse sea salt, for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, honey, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until the yeast becomes foamy.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until the dough comes together.

  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with plastic wrap or a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  5. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease a baking sheet or line it with parchment paper.

  6. Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to the prepared baking sheet. Gently press it out into a rectangle shape, about 1/2 inch thick.

  7. Using your fingertips, make indentations all over the surface of the dough. This will create those classic dimples that are characteristic of focaccia.

  8. Drizzle the surface of the dough with olive oil and sprinkle the sliced olives and chopped rosemary evenly over the top. Finish it off with a sprinkle of coarse sea salt.

  9. Bake the focaccia in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until it is golden brown on top and cooked through.

  10. Remove the focaccia from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing it into squares or wedges.

  11. Serve the Mediterranean Olive Focaccia warm or at room temperature. It pairs perfectly with a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil and a sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper.

Enjoy the Flavors of the Mediterranean

Mediterranean Olive Focaccia is not just a bread; it’s a taste of the Mediterranean. The combination of the soft, pillowy texture of the dough, the briny Kalamata olives, and the aromatic rosemary will transport you to the sun-soaked shores of the Mediterranean.

This versatile bread can be enjoyed on its own, dipped in a bowl of balsamic vinegar and olive oil, or used as a base for a mouthwatering sandwich. It’s a true crowd-pleaser that will impress your family and friends.

So, why not bring a taste of the Mediterranean into your kitchen? This recipe for Mediterranean Olive Focaccia is easy to make, requires simple ingredients, and is packed with flavor. Give it a try, and let the aromas and flavors of the Mediterranean fill your home. Buon appetito!
