Tasty Gluten-Free Pizza Rolls for a Quick Snack or Party Appetizer


Pizza rolls are a classic snack that everyone loves. They are bite-sized, delicious, and perfect for any occasion. However, if you’re following a gluten-free diet, finding gluten-free pizza rolls can be a challenge. But fear not! I have a fantastic recipe for Tasty Gluten-Free Pizza Rolls that will satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. Whether you’re looking for a quick snack or a party appetizer, these gluten-free pizza rolls are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup gluten-free pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced onions (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)

For the dipping sauce:

  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour blend, baking powder, salt, garlic powder, dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, xanthan gum, and black pepper.

  3. Add the olive oil to the dry ingredients and mix well. Gradually add the warm water and continue mixing until a soft dough forms.

  4. Place the dough on a lightly floured surface and knead it for a few minutes until smooth and elastic.

  5. Roll out the dough into a rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.

  6. Spread the gluten-free pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  7. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, and add any additional toppings of your choice, such as pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, or black olives.

  8. Starting from one of the long ends, tightly roll up the dough into a log.

  9. Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 1-inch thick slices, and place them on the prepared baking sheet, cut-side up.

  10. Bake the gluten-free pizza rolls in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the rolls are golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  11. While the pizza rolls are baking, prepare the dipping sauce by combining the marinara sauce, dried oregano, dried basil, and garlic powder in a small bowl. Mix well.

  12. Once the pizza rolls are ready, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  13. Serve the gluten-free pizza rolls warm with the dipping sauce on the side.

Conclusion

These Tasty Gluten-Free Pizza Rolls are a game-changer for anyone following a gluten-free diet. The flavorful dough, gooey cheese, and delicious toppings create a snack or appetizer that everyone will enjoy. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a quick snack, these gluten-free pizza rolls are the perfect solution. So go ahead, indulge in these mouthwatering treats and savor every bite!

Remember to experiment with different toppings and dipping sauces to suit your taste. Enjoy your gluten-free pizza rolls and let the compliments roll in!
