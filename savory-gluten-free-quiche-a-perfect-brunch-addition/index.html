Savory Gluten-Free Quiche: A Perfect Brunch Addition


Are you looking for a delicious and versatile dish to elevate your brunch game? Look no further than this savory gluten-free quiche recipe! Quiche is a classic French dish that has made its way into brunch menus around the world, and for good reason. It’s a crowd-pleaser that can be customized with various fillings to suit different tastes. In this recipe, we’ll be making a gluten-free quiche that’s not only flavorful and satisfying but also suitable for those with dietary restrictions. Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply want to enjoy a special homemade meal, this quiche is sure to impress.

Ingredients:

For the Gluten-Free Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Quiche Filling:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 cup shredded Gruyère cheese
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped cooked bacon or ham
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

Making the Gluten-Free Crust:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, melted butter, egg, and salt. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Press the dough into a 9-inch pie dish, making sure it’s evenly distributed along the bottom and up the sides.
  4. Bake the crust for 12-15 minutes, or until it’s set and lightly golden. Remove from the oven and set aside.

Preparing the Quiche Filling:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, pepper, and garlic powder until well combined.
  2. Stir in the shredded Gruyère cheese, chopped spinach, diced bell peppers, and chopped bacon or ham.
  3. Pour the filling into the pre-baked gluten-free crust.

Baking the Quiche:

  1. Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the center is set and the top is golden brown.
  2. Once done, remove the quiche from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  3. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.

Serving Suggestions:

This savory gluten-free quiche is best enjoyed warm, either fresh out of the oven or gently reheated. It pairs wonderfully with a crisp green salad dressed with vinaigrette for a complete brunch experience. You can also serve it alongside roasted potatoes or fresh fruit for a balanced meal. The beauty of quiche lies in its ability to be served at any temperature, making it a convenient make-ahead option for busy mornings.

Conclusion:

Serving a gluten-free quiche is a thoughtful way to accommodate guests with dietary restrictions without compromising on flavor. This recipe demonstrates that gluten-free dishes can be just as indulgent and satisfying as their traditional counterparts. Whether you’re gluten-intolerant or simply appreciate a good quiche, this savory gluten-free quiche is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor every bite of this delightful brunch addition.
