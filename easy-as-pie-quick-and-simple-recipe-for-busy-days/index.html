Easy as Pie: Quick and Simple Recipe for Busy Days


Life can get hectic, and we often find ourselves juggling multiple responsibilities, leaving little time for elaborate cooking. On those busy days, a quick and simple recipe can be a lifesaver. If you’re looking for a delicious and easy-to-make dish that will save you time without compromising on flavor, then you’re in the right place. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through a straightforward recipe for a savory pie that’s perfect for those busy days when you need a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need. The beauty of this recipe lies in its simplicity, and you’ll find that these ingredients are easy to come by. You’ll need:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 pound of ground beef (or ground turkey for a leaner option)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup of beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Instructions

Now that we have the ingredients sorted, let’s move on to the recipe itself. Follow these simple steps to create a mouthwatering pie that will leave you and your loved ones satisfied:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C) to ensure it’s ready when your pie is prepared.

  2. Prepare the Filling: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent. Next, add the ground beef (or turkey) to the skillet, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Once the meat is browned, add the frozen mixed vegetables, tomato paste, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together and let it cook for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to thaw.

  3. Add the Broth: Pour in the beef or vegetable broth, and allow the mixture to simmer for another 5-7 minutes, or until the liquid reduces slightly. The broth will infuse the filling with rich flavor and keep it moist.

  4. Prepare the Pie: While the filling simmers, place the pre-made pie crust in a pie dish, ensuring it covers the bottom and sides. Once the filling is ready, carefully spoon it into the pie crust, spreading it out evenly.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Place the pie in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust turns golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the pie is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, slice it up and serve it hot. The savory aroma and delicious taste will surely delight your taste buds.

Final Thoughts

There you have it – an easy as pie recipe for those busy days when time is of the essence. This savory pie is versatile and can be enjoyed as a family dinner, packed lunch, or even a quick weeknight meal. Its simplicity doesn’t compromise on flavor, making it a go-to recipe for anyone looking to enjoy a homemade dish without spending hours in the kitchen. So, the next time you find yourself pressed for time, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful flavors of a homemade pie without the hassle.

We hope you enjoy making and savoring this easy as pie recipe. Feel free to share your experience and any creative variations you come up with. Happy cooking!
