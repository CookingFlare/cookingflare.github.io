Coconut Lime Bars with a Shortbread Crust Recipe: A Tropical and Refreshing Vegan Dessert


If you’re looking for a delightful dessert that combines the tropical flavors of coconut and lime, then look no further! These Coconut Lime Bars with a Shortbread Crust are the perfect treat for warm summer days or any time you’re in need of a refreshing and indulgent vegan dessert.

Ingredients

For the Shortbread Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, softened

For the Coconut Lime Filling:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon lime zest
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Additional lime zest, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking pan and set it aside.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and powdered sugar for the shortbread crust. Add the softened vegan butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan.

  3. Bake the shortbread crust in the preheated oven for about 15 minutes, or until it turns golden brown around the edges. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  4. While the crust is cooling, prepare the coconut lime filling. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the coconut milk, lime juice, lime zest, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a gentle boil. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  5. Once the filling has thickened, immediately pour it over the cooled shortbread crust, spreading it evenly. Allow the bars to cool at room temperature for about 30 minutes, then transfer them to the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours, or until set.

  6. Once the bars are fully chilled and set, remove them from the refrigerator and slice them into squares or rectangles. If desired, sprinkle some additional lime zest on top for an extra burst of citrus flavor and visual appeal.

  7. Serve your Coconut Lime Bars chilled and enjoy the tropical and refreshing flavors that will transport you to a sunny beach getaway. These bars are perfect for entertaining guests or simply treating yourself to a little taste of paradise.

Tips and Variations

  • For a gluten-free version, you can substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend of your choice.
  • If you prefer a sweeter filling, you can adjust the amount of granulated sugar to suit your taste.
  • Feel free to experiment with other citrus flavors, such as lemon or orange, to create different variations of these bars.
  • These Coconut Lime Bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days, but trust us, they won’t last that long!

So there you have it, a simple and delicious recipe for Coconut Lime Bars with a Shortbread Crust. With their tropical flavors and vegan-friendly ingredients, these bars are sure to impress and satisfy everyone’s taste buds. Give them a try and let us know how they turned out! Enjoy!
