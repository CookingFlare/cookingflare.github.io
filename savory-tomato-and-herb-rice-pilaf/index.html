Savory Tomato and Herb Rice Pilaf


If you’re looking for a flavorful and satisfying side dish to complement your favorite entrees, then this savory tomato and herb rice pilaf is the perfect choice. This dish is a delightful blend of aromatic herbs, tangy tomatoes, and fluffy rice, creating a symphony of flavors that will elevate any meal. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting homemade dish, this rice pilaf is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring this delectable dish to life in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Rice: Start by rinsing the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This helps remove excess starch, resulting in fluffy, separate grains once cooked.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large skillet or pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Rice and Herbs: Stir in the rice, dried oregano, thyme, and paprika, ensuring that the rice is well coated with the aromatic mixture.

  4. Incorporate the Tomatoes: Pour in the can of diced tomatoes, including the juices, and stir to combine with the rice and herbs.

  5. Simmer and Cook: Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth and season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the liquid.

  6. Fluff and Garnish: Once the rice is cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for an additional 5 minutes. Then, fluff the rice with a fork to separate the grains. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of freshness and color.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Your savory tomato and herb rice pilaf is now ready to be served alongside your favorite main dishes. The vibrant flavors of the tomatoes and herbs combined with the fluffy texture of the rice make this pilaf a delightful addition to any meal.

Tips for Success

  • For added depth of flavor, consider using fire-roasted diced tomatoes.
  • Feel free to customize the herb blend to your preference, incorporating fresh herbs for a more vibrant taste.
  • If you prefer a richer flavor, you can substitute some of the broth with white wine for a delightful twist.

Final Thoughts

This savory tomato and herb rice pilaf is a versatile and delicious dish that pairs beautifully with a wide range of cuisines. Whether you’re serving it alongside grilled meats, roasted vegetables, or as part of a festive feast, this pilaf is sure to be a hit. The combination of robust herbs, tangy tomatoes, and tender rice creates a harmonious medley of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing. So, next time you’re in need of a standout side dish, consider bringing this delightful rice pilaf to the table for a memorable dining experience. Enjoy every aromatic bite and savor the joys of homemade cooking!
