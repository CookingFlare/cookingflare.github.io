Italian-Style Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Shells with Ricotta and Parmesan


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful Italian dish to impress your family and friends, look no further than these Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed shells with ricotta and parmesan. This recipe combines tender chicken, vibrant spinach, creamy ricotta, and savory parmesan, all nestled in jumbo pasta shells and baked to perfection. It’s a dish that’s perfect for a cozy family dinner or a gathering with loved ones. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

To make Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed shells with ricotta and parmesan, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound jumbo pasta shells
  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 15 ounces whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Pasta Shells

Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Cook the jumbo pasta shells according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the shells and set them aside to cool.

Step 2: Making the Filling

In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, chopped spinach, ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix everything together until well combined.

Step 3: Stuffing the Shells

Using a spoon, carefully fill each cooked pasta shell with the chicken and spinach filling, making sure to pack them full but not overstuffed. Place the filled shells in a baking dish, arranging them in a single layer.

Step 4: Baking the Stuffed Shells

Pour the marinara sauce over the stuffed shells, making sure to cover them evenly. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the marinara sauce.

Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

Step 5: Serving and Enjoying

Once the stuffed shells are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Serve the Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed shells with ricotta and parmesan on individual plates, garnishing with fresh basil or parsley if desired.

Conclusion

This Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed shells with ricotta and parmesan recipe is a wonderful way to bring the flavors of Italy to your dinner table. The combination of tender chicken, nutritious spinach, and creamy cheeses creates a dish that’s both comforting and elegant. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a delicious homemade meal, these stuffed shells are sure to impress. Give this recipe a try, and savor the delightful flavors of Italy right in your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
