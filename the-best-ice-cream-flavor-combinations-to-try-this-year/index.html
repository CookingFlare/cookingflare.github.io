The Best Ice Cream Flavor Combinations to Try This Year


As the warm weather rolls in, there’s nothing quite like a scoop of creamy, delicious ice cream to cool you down and satisfy your sweet tooth. While traditional flavors like chocolate and vanilla have their place, this year we’re diving into a world of adventurous ice cream flavor combinations that will tantalize your taste buds and elevate your dessert experience. Whether you prefer fruity, nutty, or spicy options, we’ve curated a list of the best ice cream flavor combinations that are sure to impress.

1. Lavender Honey and Lemon Zest

Start your ice cream journey with a refreshing twist by combining lavender honey ice cream with a hint of lemon zest. The floral notes of lavender perfectly complement the sweet, rich flavor of honey, while the lemon zest adds a zesty brightness that cuts through the creaminess. This combination is perfect for a warm summer evening.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Start with a lavender-infused ice cream base, adding honey during the churning process.
  • Flavor Boost: Incorporate freshly grated lemon zest before serving.

2. Spicy Chocolate and Sea Salt

For the chocolate lovers looking for a kick, spicy chocolate ice cream paired with a sprinkle of sea salt is a must-try. The heat from the chili powder or cayenne pepper enhances the rich chocolate flavor, while the sea salt elevates it to a whole new level. This combination is perfect for those who enjoy a little adventure in their desserts.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Use a rich chocolate ice cream base and mix in your desired amount of chili powder.
  • Finish: Top with a light sprinkle of flaky sea salt just before serving.

3. Matcha Green Tea and Coconut

Transport yourself to a tropical paradise with the unique pairing of matcha green tea and coconut ice cream. The earthy notes of matcha blend beautifully with the creamy, sweet flavor of coconut, creating a harmonious balance that is both refreshing and indulgent.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Start with a coconut ice cream base and swirl in matcha powder.
  • Texture: For added texture, mix in shredded coconut before freezing.

4. Roasted Strawberry and Balsamic Vinegar

Elevate your fruit game with the sophisticated combination of roasted strawberry ice cream drizzled with balsamic vinegar. Roasting strawberries intensifies their sweetness and adds a depth of flavor, while the balsamic vinegar introduces a tangy contrast that will leave your taste buds dancing.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Roast fresh strawberries until caramelized and blend them into a creamy ice cream base.
  • Drizzle: Serve with a drizzle of high-quality balsamic vinegar for an extra kick.

5. Maple Pecan and Bourbon

Indulge in the flavors of fall any time of year with a delightful mix of maple pecan ice cream and a splash of bourbon. The rich maple flavor is complemented by the crunch of toasted pecans and the warmth of bourbon, making this combination a perfect treat for adults.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Create a maple-flavored ice cream base and fold in roasted pecans.
  • Add Bourbon: Stir in bourbon just before freezing for a subtle kick.

6. Sweet Corn and Blueberry

This unusual pairing of sweet corn ice cream with a swirl of blueberry sauce is a delightful surprise. The creamy, sweet corn flavor evokes the essence of summer, while the tartness of blueberries adds a refreshing contrast.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Prepare a sweet corn ice cream base by pureeing fresh corn.
  • Swirl: Top with homemade blueberry sauce made from simmered blueberries and sugar.

7. Peanut Butter and Pickle

If you’re feeling adventurous, don’t miss out on the bold combination of peanut butter ice cream with pickles. This quirky mix is not for the faint of heart, but the salty, creamy peanut butter paired with the crunch and tang of dill pickles offers a surprising and addictive flavor experience.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Create a rich peanut butter ice cream base.
  • Add Pickles: Top with chopped dill pickles or serve with pickle spears on the side.

8. Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough and Mint

For a nostalgic yet refreshing twist, combine chocolate chip cookie dough ice cream with a hint of mint. The classic flavors of cookie dough are brightened by the refreshing taste of mint, creating a dessert that’s both comforting and invigorating.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Start with chocolate chip cookie dough ice cream as your base.
  • Infuse Mint: Add crushed mint leaves or mint extract for a refreshing touch.

9. Mango and Chili Lime

Capture the essence of summer with a vibrant combination of mango ice cream and a sprinkle of chili lime seasoning. The sweetness of ripe mango pairs perfectly with the heat and tang of chili and lime, delivering a burst of tropical flavor.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Blend fresh mango puree into your ice cream base.
  • Season: Just before serving, sprinkle with chili lime seasoning for an extra zing.

10. Saffron and Pistachio

For a sophisticated dessert, try the luxurious pairing of saffron and pistachio ice cream. Saffron adds a unique floral and slightly sweet note, while the nutty flavor of pistachios provides a delightful crunch.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Infuse your ice cream base with saffron threads and let them steep.
  • Mix: Fold in chopped pistachios before freezing.

11. Caramel and Sea Salt Brownie

Indulge in the rich flavors of caramel and sea salt brownie ice cream for a decadent treat. This combination brings together the chewy texture of brownies with the sweet, buttery flavor of caramel, all balanced by a hint of sea salt.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Create a brownie batter ice cream base and fold in chunks of brownies.
  • Drizzle: Swirl in caramel sauce and sprinkle with sea salt before serving.

12. Raspberry and Basil

Refresh your palate with a light and fruity combination of raspberry ice cream topped with fresh basil. The tartness of raspberries is beautifully complemented by the aromatic notes of basil, making for a refreshing summer dessert.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Blend fresh raspberries into your ice cream base.
  • Garnish: Top with fresh basil leaves just before serving.

13. Chocolate and Orange Zest

Revitalize the classic chocolate flavor with a hint of orange zest. The bright, citrusy notes of orange will enhance the richness of chocolate, making for a delightful dessert that’s perfect for any occasion.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Use a rich chocolate ice cream base.
  • Add Zest: Incorporate freshly grated orange zest during the mixing process.

14. Vanilla and Cardamom

For a warm and comforting dessert, try a blend of vanilla ice cream with a hint of cardamom. The aromatic spice adds depth to the classic flavor, creating a deliciously unique experience.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Start with a vanilla ice cream base and mix in ground cardamom.
  • Serve: Pair with a sprinkle of cinnamon for an added layer of flavor.

15. Coffee and Whiskey

Coffee lovers will savor the bold combination of coffee ice cream infused with a splash of whiskey. The rich coffee flavor is beautifully complemented by the warmth of whiskey, creating a perfect after-dinner treat.

How to Make It:

  • Base: Prepare a coffee-flavored ice cream base.
  • Infuse: Stir in whiskey just before freezing for a warm touch.

Conclusion

This year, step out of your ice cream comfort zone and try these exciting flavor combinations. From savory to sweet, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply treating yourself, these unique ice cream blends will surely impress and satisfy. So grab your ice cream maker or head to your favorite local shop and indulge in the delicious world of ice cream like never before!
