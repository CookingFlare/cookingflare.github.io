Keto-Friendly Cabbage Rolls: A Comforting and Healthy Dish


Are you following a keto diet and looking for a comforting and healthy dish to add to your meal rotation? Look no further than these keto-friendly cabbage rolls! Packed with flavor and low in carbohydrates, these cabbage rolls are the perfect option for anyone wanting a satisfying and nutritious meal. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through how to make these delicious rolls step by step.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 head of cabbage
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower rice
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup sugar-free tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Start by boiling a large pot of water. Once the water is boiling, carefully add the whole head of cabbage and let it cook for about 5 minutes. This will soften the leaves, making them easier to roll later.

  3. While the cabbage is cooking, in a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Once cooked, drain any excess fat from the skillet.

  4. Add the cauliflower rice, diced onions, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper to the skillet with the ground beef. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the onions are tender.

  5. Remove the cabbage from the pot and carefully peel off the individual leaves. Trim off the thick center rib from each leaf to make rolling easier.

  6. Place a spoonful of the ground beef mixture onto the bottom part of each cabbage leaf. Roll the leaf tightly, folding in the sides as you go. Repeat this process until all the filling is used.

  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom. Arrange the cabbage rolls on top, seam side down. Pour the remaining tomato sauce over the rolls, making sure they are fully covered.

  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.

  9. Remove the foil and sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the cabbage rolls. Return to the oven and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  10. Once done, remove the cabbage rolls from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These keto-friendly cabbage rolls are satisfying on their own, but you can also pair them with a side salad or steamed vegetables for a complete meal. They can be enjoyed fresh out of the oven or reheated for leftovers. Either way, they are sure to become a family favorite!

Conclusion

With their comforting flavors and low-carb profile, these keto-friendly cabbage rolls are a must-try for anyone following a ketogenic lifestyle. They are a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet while still enjoying a delicious and filling meal. So why not give this recipe a try and experience the comfort and health benefits of these tasty cabbage rolls? Happy cooking!
