Delightful and Decadent Cinnamon Raisin Bread


There’s something undeniably comforting and irresistible about the aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through the air. And when it comes to delightful and decadent bread, few things can compare to the heavenly combination of cinnamon and raisins. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of creating your very own batch of mouthwatering cinnamon raisin bread that is sure to delight your senses and impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Mix well to ensure even distribution of the ingredients.

  2. Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the warm milk, melted butter, and egg. Use a wooden spoon or your hands to mix everything together until a sticky dough forms.

  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. Add more flour if necessary to prevent sticking, but be careful not to add too much, as it may result in a dense loaf.

  4. Once the dough is kneaded to perfection, gently fold in the raisins, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap. Allow it to rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

  6. After the dough has risen, gently punch it down to release any trapped air. Turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and divide it in half.

  7. Roll out each portion of dough into a rectangular shape, approximately 9x12 inches. Sprinkle a generous amount of cinnamon sugar over the entire surface of each rectangle.

  8. Starting from the shorter side, tightly roll up the dough into a log, sealing the edges as you go. Repeat with the second portion of dough.

  9. Place the two cinnamon raisin bread logs into greased loaf pans, cover them, and allow them to rise for an additional 45-60 minutes, or until they have doubled in size once again.

  10. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Once the loaves have risen, bake them in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until they are golden brown and sound hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  11. Remove the loaves from the oven and let them cool in the pans for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Enjoy the Delightful and Decadent Cinnamon Raisin Bread

Now that you’ve mastered the art of making your very own cinnamon raisin bread, it’s time to indulge in its delightful flavors and decadent aroma. Whether you enjoy a slice for breakfast, as a snack, or even as part of a delicious French toast recipe, this homemade bread is sure to satisfy your cravings and bring warmth to your home.

So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and embark on this culinary adventure. In just a few simple steps, you’ll have a loaf of cinnamon raisin bread that will make you the envy of your friends and family. Happy baking!
