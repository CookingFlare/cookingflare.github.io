Allergy-Free Mediterranean Pies and Tarts: Sweet and Savory Delights


Are you ready to embark on a culinary journey through the Mediterranean with allergy-free pies and tarts? From the sun-drenched coastlines of Greece to the aromatic markets of Morocco, the Mediterranean region is renowned for its vibrant flavors and fresh ingredients. In this blog post, we’ll explore a delightful array of sweet and savory pies and tarts that are not only delicious but also free from common allergens such as gluten, dairy, and nuts. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a taste of the Mediterranean, these allergy-free recipes are sure to impress.

Savory Spinach and Feta Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup dairy-free feta cheese
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the gluten-free flour, olive oil, and water to form a dough. Divide the dough in half.
  3. Roll out one half of the dough and place it in a greased pie dish.
  4. In a skillet, sauté the chopped onion and garlic until translucent. Add the chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Spread the spinach mixture over the prepared crust and crumble the dairy-free feta cheese on top.
  6. Roll out the remaining dough and place it over the filling. Seal the edges and brush the top with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.

This savory spinach and feta pie is a classic Mediterranean dish that’s perfect for a light lunch or dinner. The combination of tangy feta and earthy spinach encased in a flaky, gluten-free crust will transport your taste buds to the shores of the Aegean Sea.

Sweet Citrus Almond Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • Zest of 2 oranges
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Sliced almonds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the almond flour, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup to form a dough.
  3. Press the dough into a greased tart pan, covering the bottom and sides evenly.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the citrus zest, orange juice, and lemon juice.
  5. Pour the citrus filling into the prepared crust and spread it out evenly.
  6. Sprinkle sliced almonds on top for a decorative touch.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the filling is set and the crust is golden.

This sweet citrus almond tart is a delightful finale to any Mediterranean-inspired meal. The nutty almond crust paired with the zesty citrus filling creates a harmonious balance of flavors that will transport you to a sun-kissed orchard in Sicily.

Conclusion

These allergy-free Mediterranean pies and tarts are a testament to the rich culinary heritage of the region. By using simple, wholesome ingredients and creative substitutions, you can savor the essence of the Mediterranean without compromising on flavor or dietary restrictions. Whether you’re catering to allergies or simply seeking a healthier alternative, these recipes offer a tantalizing glimpse into the diverse and delectable world of Mediterranean cuisine. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the aromas of the Mediterranean fill your kitchen as you embark on a culinary adventure like no other. Buon appetito!
