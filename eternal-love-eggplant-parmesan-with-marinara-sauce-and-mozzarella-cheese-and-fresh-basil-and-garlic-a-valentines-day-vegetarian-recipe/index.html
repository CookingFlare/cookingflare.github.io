Eternal Love Eggplant Parmesan with Marinara Sauce and Mozzarella Cheese and Fresh Basil and Garlic: A Valentine's Day Vegetarian Recipe


Eternal Love Eggplant Parmesan with Marinara Sauce and Mozzarella Cheese and Fresh Basil and Garlic: A Valentine’s Day Vegetarian Recipe

Eggplant Parmesan is a classic Italian dish that has been loved for generations. It’s a comforting and flavorful dish that warms the heart and satisfies the taste buds. This version of Eggplant Parmesan takes it to a whole new level with the addition of marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, and garlic. It’s a dish that is perfect for a romantic Valentine’s Day dinner, especially for those who follow a vegetarian diet.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Slice the eggplants into 1/2-inch thick rounds. Sprinkle salt on both sides of each slice and let them sit for about 30 minutes to draw out the excess moisture. This step helps to remove any bitterness from the eggplants.

  3. Rinse the eggplant slices under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels.

  4. In three separate shallow bowls, place the flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs mixed with grated Parmesan cheese.

  5. Dredge each eggplant slice in the flour, then dip it into the beaten eggs, and finally coat it with the breadcrumb mixture. Make sure each slice is evenly coated.

  6. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Fry the breaded eggplant slices in batches until golden brown on both sides. Place the cooked slices on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb any excess oil.

  7. In a separate saucepan, heat the marinara sauce over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  8. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of fried eggplant slices on top of the sauce, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.

  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  10. Once the Eggplant Parmesan is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Eternal Love Eggplant Parmesan is a rich and indulgent dish that can be served as a main course. Pair it with a side salad dressed with a light vinaigrette to balance out the flavors. A glass of red wine would also complement this dish perfectly.

Conclusion

This Valentine’s Day, surprise your loved one with this delectable Eternal Love Eggplant Parmesan. The combination of crispy breaded eggplant, tangy marinara sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and aromatic basil and garlic will surely create a culinary experience that will be cherished forever. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking for a delicious dish to celebrate the day of love, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, spread the love and savor every bite of this Eternal Love Eggplant Parmesan!
