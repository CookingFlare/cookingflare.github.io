Hearty Keto Creamy Chicken and Bacon Zoodle Recipe


Are you following a keto diet and looking for a delicious and satisfying meal? Look no further! This Hearty Keto Creamy Chicken and Bacon Zoodle Recipe is the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your low-carb lifestyle. Packed with flavor and easy to make, this recipe will become a family favorite in no time!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 medium zucchinis, spiralized into zoodles
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. Add the heavy cream, chicken broth, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine.

  4. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, use a spiralizer to make zoodles out of the zucchinis. Set aside.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened slightly, return the chicken to the skillet. Add the crumbled bacon and stir gently to coat the chicken.

  7. Add the zoodles to the skillet and toss them in the sauce until they are evenly coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the zoodles are tender but still have a slight crunch.

  8. Remove the skillet from heat and garnish with fresh parsley.

  9. Serve the Hearty Keto Creamy Chicken and Bacon Zoodle dish hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • To add some extra flavor, you can sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the dish before serving.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to tailor the dish to your taste preferences.
  • If you don’t have a spiralizer, you can use a julienne peeler to make zoodles.
  • This recipe is also great for meal prepping. Simply divide the dish into individual portions and store them in airtight containers in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Final Thoughts

The Hearty Keto Creamy Chicken and Bacon Zoodle Recipe is a fantastic option for keto dieters who want a comforting and flavorful meal. The combination of tender chicken, crispy bacon, and creamy sauce is simply irresistible. Plus, the zoodles add a refreshing and healthy twist to the dish.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a hearty and keto-friendly meal, give this recipe a try. It’s easy to make, packed with flavor, and will keep you on track with your low-carb lifestyle. Enjoy!

Note: This recipe serves 4 people. Adjust the quantities accordingly for smaller or larger servings.
