Lemon Bars Recipe: A Tangy and Sweet Vegan Dessert


Are you a fan of tangy and sweet desserts? Look no further! Today, I’m excited to share with you a delightful recipe for Lemon Bars, a vegan dessert that will surely satisfy your cravings. These bars are bursting with vibrant lemon flavor and have a perfectly balanced sweet and tangy taste. Plus, they’re incredibly easy to make! So, let’s dive right into it.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup of vegan butter, melted

For the filling:

  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1/2 cup of almond milk
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and powdered sugar for the crust. Add in the melted vegan butter and mix until you have a crumbly mixture.

  3. Press the crust mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Make sure it’s tightly packed.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 15 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside.

  5. While the crust is baking, prepare the filling. In a large bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar, all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.

  6. Add in the fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, and almond milk. Whisk until the mixture is smooth and well combined.

  7. Pour the filling mixture over the baked crust, spreading it out evenly.

  8. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set and slightly golden on top.

  9. Once done, remove the lemon bars from the oven and let them cool completely in the baking dish.

  10. Once cooled, dust the top of the bars with powdered sugar. You can use a fine-mesh sieve for a nice even dusting.

  11. Carefully lift the bars out of the baking dish using the parchment paper and transfer them to a cutting board.

  12. Cut the bars into squares or rectangles of your desired size. You can make them bite-sized or larger, depending on your preference.

  13. Serve and enjoy! These Lemon Bars are perfect for any occasion - from a casual afternoon treat to a delightful dessert for a special gathering.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra zesty flavor, you can add more lemon zest to the filling mixture.

  • If you prefer a thicker crust, you can double the crust ingredients and press the mixture more firmly into the baking dish.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of citrus fruits, such as limes or oranges, to create unique variations of these bars.

  • To make the bars even more decadent, you can drizzle them with a simple vegan glaze made from powdered sugar and lemon juice.

  • Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4-5 days. They can be enjoyed chilled or at room temperature.

So there you have it - a simple yet incredibly delicious Lemon Bars recipe that’s both tangy and sweet. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking for a delightful dessert, these bars are sure to impress. Give them a try and let your taste buds indulge in the refreshing flavors of citrusy goodness. Enjoy!
