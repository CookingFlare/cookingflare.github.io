Chocolate Orange Cake: A Citrusy Delight


If you’re a fan of the delightful combination of chocolate and orange, then this Chocolate Orange Cake recipe is bound to become one of your favorites. The zesty citrus flavor of orange perfectly complements the rich, indulgent taste of chocolate, resulting in a cake that is both refreshing and satisfying. Whether you’re hosting a special occasion or simply craving a decadent treat, this cake is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your loved ones. So, let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this citrusy delight!

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • Zest of 2 oranges

For the Chocolate Orange Ganache:

  • 8 ounces dark chocolate, finely chopped
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • Zest of 1 orange

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the eggs, orange juice, buttermilk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and orange zest until well combined.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 2: Making the Chocolate Orange Ganache

  1. Place the chopped dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to simmer.
  3. Pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate and let it sit for a minute.
  4. Gently stir the mixture until the chocolate is completely melted and the ganache is smooth.
  5. Stir in the orange zest and let the ganache cool to room temperature.

Step 3: Assembling the Cake

  1. Place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread a layer of the chocolate orange ganache over the top.
  2. Carefully place the second cake layer on top and cover the entire cake with the remaining ganache.
  3. If desired, garnish with additional orange zest for a pop of color and flavor.

Step 4: Enjoying the Citrusy Delight

Slice the cake and savor the delightful combination of rich chocolate and bright orange flavors. This cake is perfect for any occasion, from birthday celebrations to afternoon tea gatherings. Serve it with a cup of hot coffee or a refreshing glass of orange-infused iced tea for a truly indulgent experience.

Final Thoughts

The Chocolate Orange Cake is a true testament to the magic that happens when chocolate meets citrus. Its moist, tender crumb and luscious ganache make it a standout dessert that will leave a lasting impression on anyone who takes a bite. So, gather your ingredients and let the aroma of chocolate and orange fill your kitchen as you embark on creating this citrusy delight. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to elevate your baking game and bring joy to those you share it with. Happy baking!
