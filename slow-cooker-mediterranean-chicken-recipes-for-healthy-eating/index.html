Slow Cooker Mediterranean Chicken Recipes for Healthy Eating


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy way to enjoy chicken? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering slow cooker Mediterranean chicken recipes that are not only easy to make but also packed with flavor. These recipes are perfect for those who are trying to eat healthier without sacrificing taste. So, grab your slow cooker and let’s get cooking!

Recipe 1: Greek Lemon Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken breasts, making sure they are evenly coated.
  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  5. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the slow cooker and let it rest for a few minutes.
  6. Serve the chicken with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and enjoy!

Recipe 2: Moroccan Chicken Tagine

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup pitted green olives
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken thighs in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the sliced onion, minced garlic, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground cinnamon, ground ginger, and ground turmeric.
  3. Sprinkle the spice mixture over the chicken thighs, making sure they are well coated.
  4. Pour the chicken broth and diced tomatoes over the chicken.
  5. Add the green olives to the slow cooker.
  6. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  7. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and let it rest for a few minutes.
  8. Serve the chicken with a sprinkle of fresh cilantro and enjoy!

Recipe 3: Italian Herb Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the halved cherry tomatoes, sliced black olives, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, dried rosemary, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken breasts, making sure they are evenly coated.
  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  5. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and let it rest for a few minutes.
  6. Serve the chicken with a sprinkle of fresh basil and enjoy!

Conclusion

These slow cooker Mediterranean chicken recipes are a delicious and healthy way to incorporate more flavor into your meals. Whether you choose the Greek Lemon Chicken, Moroccan Chicken Tagine, or Italian Herb Chicken, you can’t go wrong. So, dust off your slow cooker and give these recipes a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and your body will appreciate the nutritious ingredients. Enjoy your healthy eating journey!
