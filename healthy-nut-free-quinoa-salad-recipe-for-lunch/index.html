Healthy Nut-Free Quinoa Salad Recipe for Lunch


Looking for a delicious and healthy lunch option that’s packed with nutrients and flavor? Look no further! This Nut-Free Quinoa Salad Recipe is the perfect choice for those with nut allergies or simply looking for a nut-free option. It’s easy to make, full of wholesome ingredients, and bursting with vibrant flavors. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the quinoa and water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover. Simmer for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, parsley, and mint. Mix well to combine.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the quinoa salad and toss to coat all the ingredients evenly.

  4. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top of the salad. Gently toss again to incorporate the cheese.

  5. Serve the Nut-Free Quinoa Salad immediately or refrigerate for a few hours to allow the flavors to meld together.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 250
  • Total Fat: 10g
  • Saturated Fat: 2g
  • Cholesterol: 8mg
  • Sodium: 200mg
  • Carbohydrates: 34g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Sugar: 4g
  • Protein: 8g

Why Choose Nut-Free Quinoa Salad?

This Nut-Free Quinoa Salad is not only delicious but also offers a range of health benefits. Let’s take a closer look at why you should consider adding this salad to your lunch rotation:

1. Nut-Free Option

For individuals with nut allergies, finding nut-free recipes can be a challenge. This Nut-Free Quinoa Salad eliminates the risk of allergic reactions while still providing a satisfying and nutritious meal.

2. High in Protein

Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids that our bodies need. This makes it an excellent choice for vegetarians and vegans looking to boost their protein intake.

3. Nutrient-Rich Ingredients

The cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, red bell peppers, and red onions in this salad are all packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. These ingredients contribute to overall health and support a strong immune system.

4. Rich in Fiber

Quinoa and vegetables are excellent sources of dietary fiber, which aids in digestion, promotes satiety, and helps maintain a healthy weight. Fiber also plays a crucial role in supporting gut health.

5. Easy to Customize

Feel free to get creative and customize this salad to your taste preferences. You can add other vegetables, herbs, or even protein sources like grilled chicken or tofu for added variety and flavor.

Conclusion

This Nut-Free Quinoa Salad Recipe is a simple yet satisfying option for a healthy lunch. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with flavors, it’s a dish that will keep you energized throughout the day. Whether you have a nut allergy or simply want to explore new salad recipes, give this one a try. Your taste buds and body will thank you!

Remember, always consult with your healthcare professional or nutritionist if you have specific dietary requirements or concerns.

Enjoy your delicious and nutritious Nut-Free Quinoa Salad!
