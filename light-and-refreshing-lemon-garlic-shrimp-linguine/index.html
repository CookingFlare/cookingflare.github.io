Light and Refreshing Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine


If you’re looking for a light and refreshing seafood pasta dish that’s bursting with flavor, then you’re in for a treat. This Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine recipe is a delightful combination of succulent shrimp, zesty lemon, aromatic garlic, and perfectly al dente linguine. It’s a dish that’s perfect for both casual weeknight dinners and special occasions, and it’s sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

For this delectable Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of linguine
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a hint of heat)

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Linguine

Begin by bringing a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the linguine and cook it according to the package instructions until it’s al dente. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and set it aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Shrimp

While the linguine is cooking, it’s time to prepare the star of the dish – the succulent shrimp. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until it becomes fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.

Next, add the shrimp to the skillet, season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes if you like a bit of heat. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once the shrimp is cooked through, remove them from the skillet and set them aside.

Step 3: Bring It All Together

In the same skillet, add the cooked linguine, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Toss everything together to coat the linguine with the flavorful garlic-infused oil and the bright, citrusy notes of lemon. Add the cooked shrimp back into the skillet and toss everything together to ensure the shrimp is evenly distributed throughout the linguine.

Step 4: Finish with Freshness

To finish off this delightful dish, sprinkle the chopped fresh parsley over the Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine. The vibrant green parsley not only adds a pop of color but also a fresh, herbaceous flavor that beautifully complements the shrimp and lemon.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once everything is well combined, it’s time to serve up this delightful Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine. Plate the pasta and shrimp, and if you like, garnish with an extra sprinkle of fresh parsley and a drizzle of good quality olive oil for a touch of richness.

Conclusion

This Light and Refreshing Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine is a celebration of fresh flavors and simple ingredients coming together to create a memorable meal. Whether you’re a seafood lover or simply enjoy a bright and zesty pasta dish, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Its elegant simplicity and vibrant flavors make it a standout choice for any occasion. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every delightful forkful of this Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine. Cheers to good food and great company!
