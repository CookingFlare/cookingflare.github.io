10 Quick and Easy Indian Recipes for Lazy Weekends


If you're looking for a way to spice up your lazy weekends, try a simple and delicious Indian meal using these quick and easy recipes. These Indian dishes not only offer a flavorful twist to your meal but also help you save time without compromising on taste. Monday Lunch - Simple Palak Paneer Begin your week with a classic Indian dish that is not only delicious but also healthy: Palak Paneer. It is a creamy spinach curry made with paneer (Indian cheese), spinach puree, and spices. Ingredients:

250 gm paneer 2 cups spinach puree 2 green chilies, slit 1 teaspoon cumin seeds 1 teaspoon garam masala 1 teaspoon lemon juice Salt to taste Cooking oil for frying

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Add spinach puree and green chilies and cook until the mixture boils. Add paneer, garam masala, and lemon juice. Season with salt as needed.

Quick Avocado Raita A quick and easy side dish for any meal is the Avocado Raita. It's a refreshing yogurt side dish that can easily be customized with mix-ins of your choice. Ingredients:

250 ml yogurt 1 ripe avocado 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds 1/2 cup cucumber, grated 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder Salt to taste

Instructions:

In a bowl, combine yogurt, grated cucumber, avocado, cumin seeds, and cumin powder. Mix well and season with salt needed.

Aloo Gobi Curry This popular North Indian curry is made using cauliflower and potatoes, making it a great side dish or a main course. Ready in less than 30 minutes, it's a great option for lazy weekends. Ingredients:

250 gm potatoes, cut into cubes 250 gm cauliflower (break into florets) 1 onion 2 cloves garlic 1 teaspoon cumin seeds 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon turmeric powder 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder 2 tablespoons ghee or oil

Instructions:

Heat ghee or oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, cumin powder, and turmeric powder. Add onion and cook until it becomes golden brown. Now, add cauliflower and potatoes, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the vegetables are soft.

Chana Masala Oats Start your day with a twist on the classic breakfast by whipping up Chana Masala Oats. This innovative and healthy breakfast provides you with a perfect dose of protein and fiber. Ingredients:

250 gm rolled oats 250 gm cooked and mashed chickpeas 2 tablespoons cumin seeds 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon red chili powder 2 tablespoons almond butter 250 ml plant-based milk Optional: Mexican nuts

Instructions:

In a bowl, combine oats, almond butter, mashed chickpeas, cumin seeds, red chili powder, and plant-based milk. Mix well and add nuts as toppings if desired.

Spinach Ricotta Stuffed Naan Recipe Elevate your snack game with this indulgent and delicious Spinach Ricotta Stuffed Naan. Add it to your comfort food repertoire. Ingredients:

250 gm naan bread 250 gm ricotta cheese 2 tablespoons spinach puree 1 teaspoon garlic, minced 1 teaspoon lemon juice Salt to taste Cooking oil for toasting

Instructions:

Spread ricotta cheese on naan bread and layer it with spinach puree, garlic, and lemon juice. Toast until crispy.

Chicken Tikka Masala Wrap Unique and delicious, this Indian twist of the chicken wrap is a perfect finger food for parties and special occasions. Ingredients:

250 gm skinless chicken breast 1 cup plain yogurt 2 tablespoons gram flour (chickpea flour) 1 tablespoon turmeric 2 tablespoons cooking oil 4 flatbread - naan

Instructions:

Marinate the chicken in yogurt, gram flour, and turmeric. Let it rest for 2-3 hours, then grill and mix with spices for serving.

Bagan Tikka Make poTCut-weekends delicious with Bagan Tikka. This smoked and flavored eggplant dish won't take long to prepare and taste great as a snack. Ingredients:

2 eggplants, cut into oval rings 2 tablespoons yogurt 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1/2 teaspoon garam masala 1 onion 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon ghee or oil

Instructions:

Grill the eggplant, then marinate it in a mixture of yogurt, cumin powder, garam masala, garlic, and salt. Let it rest for 10 minutes and serve.

Instructions:

Combine ghee, cumin seeds, chicken, tomato puree, and tomatoes. Let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes for the flavors to get intense.
