Butternut Squash and Kale Stuffed Shells: A Vegetarian Fall Delight


Are you looking for a delicious and hearty vegetarian dish to warm you up this fall? Look no further than these Butternut Squash and Kale Stuffed Shells! Packed with flavor and nutrients, this recipe is the perfect comfort food for those chilly autumn evenings. The combination of creamy butternut squash, earthy kale, and savory cheese makes every bite a delight. So, let’s dive right into this mouth-watering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 bunch of kale, stems removed and leaves chopped
  • 1 box of jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the jumbo shells according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  3. In the meantime, place the cubed butternut squash in a microwave-safe dish and cook on high for 8-10 minutes, or until tender. Alternatively, you can roast the squash in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 25-30 minutes.

  4. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes. Add the chopped kale and cook until wilted, about 5 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, dried sage, and nutmeg.

  5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked butternut squash, sautéed kale, ricotta cheese, and half of the shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  6. Grease a baking dish with olive oil. Stuff each cooked jumbo shell with a generous spoonful of the butternut squash and kale mixture and place them in the baking dish.

  7. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses over the stuffed shells.

  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Butternut Squash and Kale Stuffed Shells are incredibly satisfying on their own, but you can take them to the next level with a few extra touches. Serve them with a side of garlic bread for a comforting and filling meal. You can also garnish the shells with fresh parsley or basil for a pop of color and added freshness.

Final Thoughts

With its creamy butternut squash filling, nutritious kale, and cheesy goodness, this dish is a vegetarian delight that will impress even the most devoted meat lovers. The flavors of fall come together in perfect harmony, creating a comforting and delicious meal that is sure to become a seasonal favorite. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the goodness of Butternut Squash and Kale Stuffed Shells. Happy cooking!
