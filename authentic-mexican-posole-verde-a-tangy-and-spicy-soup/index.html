Authentic Mexican Posole Verde: A Tangy and Spicy Soup


¡Bienvenidos a todos! Today, we’re diving into the vibrant and flavorful world of Mexican cuisine with a recipe for Authentic Mexican Posole Verde. This tangy and spicy soup is a beloved dish that captures the essence of Mexican flavors in every spoonful. So, grab your apron and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure!

Ingredients

To make this delicious Posole Verde, you will need:

  • 2 pounds of pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 pound of fresh tomatillos, husks removed and rinsed
  • 2 jalapeño peppers, stemmed and seeded
  • 2 poblano peppers, roasted, peeled, and seeded
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of hominy, drained and rinsed

Instructions

  1. Start by heating the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the pork shoulder chunks and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the pork from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  3. Meanwhile, in a blender, combine the tomatillos, jalapeño peppers, roasted poblano peppers, and fresh cilantro. Blend until smooth, creating a vibrant green salsa.

  4. Add the green salsa to the pot with the onions and garlic. Stir well to combine and let it cook for a couple of minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Return the browned pork shoulder to the pot, along with the chicken broth and water. Season with dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 2 hours, or until the pork is tender and the flavors have developed.

  6. Once the pork is tender, add the drained and rinsed hominy to the pot. Stir well and let it simmer for another 15 minutes to allow the hominy to absorb the flavors of the soup.

  7. Serve the Posole Verde hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, sliced radishes, and a squeeze of lime juice. Don’t forget to have warm corn tortillas on the side to complete this authentic Mexican experience.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier soup, keep the seeds in the jalapeño peppers or add an extra pepper for an extra kick.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different proteins. You can substitute the pork shoulder with chicken, beef, or even tofu for a vegetarian twist.
  • For a heartier version of Posole Verde, you can add additional vegetables like diced zucchini or bell peppers.
  • Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors will continue to develop, making it even more delicious the next day.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You have successfully cooked up a bowl of Authentic Mexican Posole Verde. This tangy and spicy soup is a celebration of Mexican flavors and a true delight for the taste buds. The combination of tender pork, vibrant green salsa, and hearty hominy creates a satisfying and comforting dish that will transport you to the streets of Mexico.

So, gather your loved ones, share this delicious meal, and let the flavors of Mexico infuse your kitchen. ¡Buen provecho!
