Keto Comfort Classics: Revamped Low-Carb Recipes for Familiar Favorites


Are you a fans of classic recipes but following a keto diet? This post is for you! In today’s guide, we’re going to cheat on our diets just a little bit, but healthily, thanks to some incredible low-carb recipe revamps of all-time comfort foods. So lets’s dive in!

1. Low-Carb Shepherd’s Pie

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef, 1 lb
  • Onion, medium, chopped
  • Garlic, cloves, minced
  • Broccoli, 2 cups, chopped
  • Frozen cauliflower, 2 cups, riced
  • Heavy cream, 1 cup
  • Cheddar cheese, 1 cup, shredded
  • Olive oil, 2 tbsp
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Thyme, 1 tsp
  • Chili powder, 1 tsp

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and garlic. Cook until the meat is browned, and onions are softened.
  2. Add salt, pepper, and thyme to the meat mixture, and cook for an additional minute.
  3. In a separate large pan, heat 2 tbsp of olive oil, then add riced cauliflower, broccoli, and season with salt, pepper, and chili powder. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Pour the heavy cream into the vegetables, add half of the cheddar cheese, and stir well. Cook for 2-3 more minutes, until the mixture thickens slightly.
  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Transfer the meat filling into a baking dish, top it off with the creamy vegetable mixture, and sprinkle the remaining cheddar cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Let it cool for 5 minutes before serving.

2. Cinnamon Roll in a Cup

Ingredients:

  • Almond flour, 1 cup
  • Erythritol, ½ cup
  • Baking powder, 1 tsp
  • Vanilla extract, ½ tsp
  • Eggs, 2
  • Heavy cream, 6 tbsp

Filling:

  • Cream cheese, 4 oz, softened
  • Erythritol, 2 tbsp
  • Vanilla extract, ½ tsp
  • Almond flour, 1 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). In a large bowl, mix together Almond flour, Erythritol, Baking powder, and Vanilla extract. Add the eggs and heavy cream, and mix until well combined.
  2. In a greased mug or ramekin, pour ½ of the batter and microwave for 1 minute.
  3. For the filling, beat together the cream cheese, Erythritol, and vanilla extract. Add almond flour and mix until well combined. Pour the filling on top of the cooked batter and microwave for another 30 seconds until the center is cooked.
  4. Allow the mug to cool for a minute before digging in.

3. Cheesy Cauliflower Breadsticks

Ingredients:

  • Cauliflower, medium, riced
  • Eggs, 2
  • Mozzarella cheese, 1 cup, shredded
  • Basil, fresh leaves, chopped
  • Italian seasoning, ½ tsp
  • Salt, ¼ tsp

Toppings:

  • Tomato sauce, ½ cup
  • Mozzarella cheese, ¼ cup, shredded
  • Pepperoni slices, optional

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). In a microwave-safe bowl, combine riced cauliflower, eggs, mozzarella, chopped basil, Italian seasoning, and salt. Cook in the microwave for 5 minutes to soften the cauliflower, then mix everything together to form a doughy consistency.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and shape the dough into a flat rectangle, as thin or thick as desired. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until edges start to brown.
  3. Remove the breadsticks from the oven and add tomato sauce, and top with shredded mozzarella cheese and pepperoni slices (if using). Return to the oven and bake for another 6-8 minutes,until cheese is melted and bubbly.

With these revamped comfort classics, let your taste buds enjoy the goodness of your favorite keto-friendly dishes. Returning to a healthy diet doesn’t mean you have to miss out on those classic recipes we all know and love. Enjoy cooking, and stay ketogenic!
