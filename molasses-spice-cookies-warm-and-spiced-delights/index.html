Molasses Spice Cookies: Warm and Spiced Delights


If you’re looking for a cookie that embodies the cozy flavors of fall and winter, look no further than Molasses Spice Cookies. These warm and spiced delights are perfect for snuggling up by the fire with a cup of hot cocoa or sharing with loved ones during the holiday season. With their rich molasses flavor and aromatic blend of spices, these cookies are sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Additional granulated sugar, for rolling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done using an electric mixer or by hand with a wooden spoon.

  3. Add the molasses and eggs to the butter-sugar mixture, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. The molasses will give the cookies their distinct flavor, while the eggs will help bind everything together.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg. This spice blend will infuse the cookies with warmth and depth of flavor.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

  6. Shape the cookie dough into 1-inch balls. Roll each ball in additional granulated sugar to coat the exterior. Place the coated balls onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  7. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers are set. The cookies will continue to firm up as they cool, so don’t worry if they seem slightly soft when you take them out of the oven.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. This will ensure that the cookies maintain their shape and texture.

  9. Once the cookies have cooled, store them in an airtight container at room temperature. They will stay fresh for up to a week, although they are unlikely to last that long!

Serving Suggestions

Molasses Spice Cookies are delicious on their own, but they can also be paired with a variety of beverages and desserts to enhance their flavors. Here are a few serving suggestions to try:

  • Enjoy a warm cookie alongside a steaming mug of spiced chai tea for a cozy afternoon treat.
  • Create an indulgent dessert by sandwiching two cookies together with a scoop of vanilla ice cream in the middle.
  • Crumble the cookies over a bowl of creamy vanilla pudding for a delightful twist on a classic dessert.
  • Serve the cookies at your next holiday gathering alongside a selection of other festive treats for a memorable dessert spread.

Conclusion

Molasses Spice Cookies are a delightful treat that brings warmth and comfort to any occasion. With their rich molasses flavor and aromatic blend of spices, these cookies are sure to please even the most discerning palates. Whether you enjoy them on their own or incorporate them into a creative dessert, these warm and spiced delights are bound to become a favorite in your household. So, grab your apron and get baking – your taste buds will thank you!
