10-Minute Mediterranean Couscous Salad Recipe

If you’re looking for a quick and delicious salad that captures the flavors of the Mediterranean, then this 10-Minute Mediterranean Couscous Salad is the perfect recipe for you. Packed with fresh ingredients and bursting with vibrant flavors, this salad is not only easy to make but also incredibly satisfying. Ingredients 1 cup couscous 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cucumber, diced 1 red bell pepper, diced 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Cook the couscous according to the package instructions. Fluff it with a fork and let it cool for a few minutes. In a large bowl, combine the cooked couscous, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh parsley. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Pour the dressing over the couscous salad and toss gently to combine, ensuring all the ingredients are well coated. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a few hours to allow the flavors to meld together. Tips and Variations For an extra burst of flavor, you can add some crumbled feta cheese or sliced grilled chicken to the salad. If you prefer a different type of grain, you can easily substitute the couscous with quinoa or bulgur. This salad makes a perfect side dish for grilled meats or a light lunch on its own. It’s also great for meal prep, as it can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for a few days. Tasty and Nutritious Veggie Omelette: A Quick Breakfast Option

A delicious and nutritious breakfast option that can be prepared in no time is the veggie omelette. This versatile dish is packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making it an excellent choice for those looking to start their day off right. In this recipe, we will explore the benefits of using different vegetables to create a flavorful and healthy omelette. Ingredients 2 large eggs 1/2 cup diced bell peppers 1/2 cup diced onions 1/2 cup diced mushrooms 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley 1 tablespoon olive oil Salt and pepper to taste 1 tablespoon grated cheddar cheese (optional) Instructions In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs and a pinch of salt until well combined. Set aside. In a large non-stick skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes. Pour the whisked eggs over the vegetables in the skillet. Cook until the eggs start to set, about 2-3 minutes. Use a spatula to gently lift and fold the edges of the omelette towards the center. Tilt the skillet to allow the uncooked egg to flow to the edges. Once the eggs are almost set, sprinkle the chopped parsley and grated cheese (if using) over the top of the omelette. Use the spatula to fold the omelette in half. Cook for an additional 30 seconds to 1 minute, until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully cooked. Slide the omelette out of the skillet onto a plate and serve hot.
