Spicy Korean Chicken Stir Fry: A Flavorful Delight


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and vibrant spices, then you’re in for a treat with this Spicy Korean Chicken Stir Fry recipe. This dish is a perfect combination of heat, sweet, and savory, making it a crowd-pleaser for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy your taste buds. So, let’s dive in and discover the secrets to creating this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Cooked white rice, for serving
  • Toasted sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish

Instructions

Marinate the Chicken

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, gochujang, honey, sesame oil, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Mix well to create the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.

Stir Fry

  1. Heat a large wok or skillet over high heat. Add a tablespoon of oil and swirl to coat the pan.
  2. Add the marinated chicken to the hot wok or skillet and spread it out into a single layer. Let it cook without stirring for 2-3 minutes to get a nice sear on the chicken.
  3. Once the chicken is browned on one side, stir-fry it for another 2-3 minutes until it’s cooked through. Transfer the cooked chicken to a plate and set it aside.
  4. In the same wok or skillet, add a little more oil if needed. Add the sliced onion, bell peppers, and mushrooms. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the cooked chicken to the wok or skillet. Add the chopped green onions and toss everything together to combine.
  6. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to heat the chicken through and allow the flavors to meld.

Serve

  1. Serve the Spicy Korean Chicken Stir Fry over steamed white rice.
  2. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds and sliced green onions for an extra pop of flavor and color.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a sprinkle of Korean red pepper flakes or a drizzle of extra gochujang.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can swap the chicken for tofu or tempeh and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.

Conclusion

This Spicy Korean Chicken Stir Fry recipe is a fantastic way to add some excitement to your weekly meal rotation. With its bold flavors and customizable heat level, it’s a dish that’s sure to become a new favorite in your kitchen. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or a group of friends, this flavorful delight will leave everyone craving for more. So, grab your wok and get ready to experience the deliciousness of Spicy Korean Chicken Stir Fry!
