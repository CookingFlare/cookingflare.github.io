Delicious Greek Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast


If you’re looking for a flavorful and impressive dish to serve for a special dinner or a gathering with friends and family, this recipe for Greek Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast is sure to impress. The combination of tender chicken breast, savory spinach, and creamy feta cheese, all infused with the vibrant flavors of Mediterranean herbs and spices, makes this dish a culinary delight. Plus, it’s a great way to incorporate healthy greens and lean protein into your meal. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering dish step by step.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Breasts

Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). Then, using a sharp knife, carefully butterfly each chicken breast. This means slicing each breast horizontally, but not all the way through, so you can open it up like a book. Once the chicken breasts are butterflied, place them between two sheets of plastic wrap and gently pound them with a meat mallet or rolling pin to an even thickness of about 1/4 inch. Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, crumbled feta cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

Step 3: Stuff the Chicken Breasts

Divide the spinach and feta filling evenly among the butterflied chicken breasts, spreading it over one half of each breast. Be sure not to overfill the chicken, as you’ll need to be able to close it easily. Once the filling is in place, carefully fold the other half of the chicken breast over the filling to enclose it. Use toothpicks to secure the edges and keep the filling from spilling out during cooking.

Step 4: Cook the Stuffed Chicken

In a large oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the stuffed chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are nicely browned. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake the chicken for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked through and the filling is hot and bubbly.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the chicken is cooked, remove the skillet from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. Carefully remove the toothpicks from the chicken breasts and slice them into thick, beautiful pieces. Serve the Greek Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast with a side of your choice, such as a crisp salad, roasted vegetables, or a fluffy couscous.

Conclusion

With its tantalizing blend of Mediterranean flavors and succulent chicken, this Greek Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast is a show-stopping dish that will leave your guests impressed and satisfied. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to elevate your weeknight dinner, this recipe is a winner. Try it out and let the delicious aromas and flavors transport you to the sunny shores of Greece. Buon appetito!
