Spanish Inspired Roasted Red Pepper Hummus Recipe


Hummus is a beloved dish that has transcended boundaries and cultures, becoming a staple in many kitchens around the world. While traditional hummus is made primarily from chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic, there’s a world of flavor waiting to be explored with creative variations. One such delightful twist is the Spanish inspired roasted red pepper hummus. This recipe infuses the classic Middle Eastern dip with the vibrant flavors of Spain, creating a rich, smoky, and slightly sweet spread that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

To create this delicious Spanish inspired roasted red pepper hummus, gather the following ingredients:

For the Hummus Base:

  • 1 can (15 oz) of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini (sesame seed paste)
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed for desired consistency

For the Roasted Red Pepper:

  • 2 large red bell peppers
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Garnish:

  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • A drizzle of olive oil
  • Smoked paprika (for dusting)

Instructions

Step 1: Roast the Red Peppers

Start by roasting the red bell peppers to bring out their natural sweetness and enhance their flavor. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).

  1. Prepare the Peppers: Wash and dry the red bell peppers. Cut them in half lengthwise and remove the seeds and stems.

  2. Season the Peppers: Place the pepper halves on a baking sheet, cut side up. Drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with sea salt and black pepper.

  3. Roast: Roast the peppers in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the skins are charred and blistered. Remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  4. Peel the Skins: Once the peppers are cool enough to handle, peel off the charred skin. This step is optional; some like to leave the skins on for added texture, but peeling them results in a smoother hummus.

Step 2: Blend the Hummus

Now that your roasted red peppers are ready, it’s time to blend everything together to create a creamy hummus.

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a food processor, combine the drained chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, ground cumin, smoked paprika, and the peeled roasted red peppers.

  2. Blend Until Smooth: Process the mixture until it is smooth and creamy. You may need to stop the processor and scrape down the sides to ensure everything is well mixed.

  3. Adjust Consistency: If the hummus is too thick, add water one tablespoon at a time, blending until you reach your desired consistency. The hummus should be creamy but still hold its shape.

  4. Taste and Adjust Seasoning: Taste the hummus and adjust the seasoning by adding salt or more lemon juice if desired.

Step 3: Serve and Enjoy

Your Spanish inspired roasted red pepper hummus is now ready to be served! Transfer the hummus to a serving bowl.

  1. Garnish: Drizzle with a bit of extra virgin olive oil, sprinkle with smoked paprika, and garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color.

  2. Pairing Ideas: This hummus pairs beautifully with a variety of dippers and accompaniments. Serve it with warm pita bread, crispy vegetable sticks (like cucumber, carrot, or bell pepper), or even spread it on sandwiches or wraps for an extra layer of flavor.

  3. Storage: If you have leftovers (though they’re unlikely!), store the hummus in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to five days. You may need to stir it before serving as it can thicken in the fridge.

The Benefits of This Dish

Nutritional Powerhouse

This Spanish inspired roasted red pepper hummus not only tantalizes the taste buds but also offers a variety of health benefits. Chickpeas are rich in protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals. Tahini, made from sesame seeds, adds healthy fats and a unique nutty flavor. Roasted red peppers are an excellent source of vitamins A and C, which are vital for maintaining healthy skin and boosting the immune system.

Versatile and Adaptable

One of the best aspects of hummus is its versatility. While this recipe features roasted red peppers, you can experiment with a range of ingredients to suit your palate. Consider adding ingredients such as sun-dried tomatoes, olives, or even a hint of spicy harissa for a kick. The possibilities are endless!

Perfect for Any Occasion

Whether you’re hosting a party, preparing a picnic, or just looking for a healthy snack, this hummus fits the bill. It’s great for sharing, and its vibrant color and rich flavors are sure to impress your guests.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Spanish inspired roasted red pepper hummus is a delightful fusion of flavors that brings a new twist to a classic dish. With its creamy texture, smoky undertones, and a touch of sweetness, it’s a perfect addition to any appetizer spread or meal. The simplicity of the ingredients combined with the depth of flavor makes it a recipe worth trying. So gather your ingredients, get roasting, and enjoy a taste of Spain right in your home! Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this hummus is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. Happy cooking!
