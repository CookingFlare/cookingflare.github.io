Vegan Avocado Obsession: 20 Tasty Brain-Boosting Treats to Satisfy Your Cravings


Are you an avocado enthusiast searching for new ways to incorporate this nutrient-rich fruit into your diet? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ve curated 20 delectable and guilt-free vegan avocado recipes that will not only satisfy your cravings but also supercharge your brain.

1. Avocado Chocolate Mousse

Indulge in a dessert that’s both creamy and guilt-free. Using ripe avocados and cocoa powder, you’ll have a restaurant-quality mousse in the comfort of your own home.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Additional cocoa powder and sliced almonds for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Scoop the avocado flesh into a food processor.
  2. Add the almond milk, cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Portion out the mousse into serving dishes, garnish with cocoa powder and sliced almonds if desired, and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.

2. Avocado Alfredo Sauce

Say goodbye to the heavy, cream-based Alfredo sauce and say hello to a healthier, plant-based alternative. Creamy, dreamy, and perfect for your pasta nights!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add the avocado, almond milk, nutritional yeast, garlic, lemon juice, black pepper, and salt to a food processor or high-speed blender.
  2. Blend until completely smooth, adding additional almond milk if necessary to achieve the desired consistency.
  3. Use immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

3. Avocado Green Smoothie

Start your day off right with this refreshing green smoothie packed with nutrients to fuel your brain and body.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a high-speed blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately. Enjoy!

4. Avocado Chickpea Salad Sandwich

For those lasy nights or rushed mornings, whip up this tasty, nutritious sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 can chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mixed greens or lettuce leaves
  • Sliced avocado (for topping)
  • Chia seed bread (or gluten-free bread of choice)

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, mash the chickpeas with a fork or potato masher, leaving some whole for texture.
  2. Add the other ingredients to the bowl and gently mix.
  3. Lay two slices of bread on a plate and layer mixed greens, the chickpea mixture, and sliced avocado.
  4. Top with the remaining slices of bread, slice, and enjoy!

These 20 vegan avocado recipes will give your taste buds a fun and exciting adventure. With brain-boosting ingredients and delicious flavors, you’ll be dancing in the kitchen with every bite. So, put on your apron and whip up some of these amazing dishes. Happy cooking!
