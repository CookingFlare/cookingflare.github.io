Traditional Greek Avgolemono Soup with Chicken: A Comforting Delight


Comfort food has a special place in our hearts, and Traditional Greek Avgolemono Soup with Chicken is no exception. This creamy and tangy soup is a staple in Greek cuisine, loved for its comforting flavors and nourishing qualities. In this recipe, we’ll explore the step-by-step process of creating this delightful soup in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 large eggs
  • Juice of 3 lemons
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  3. Add the diced carrots and celery to the pot. Cook for a few minutes until they begin to soften.

  4. Return the browned chicken pieces to the pot. Add the bay leaf, chicken broth, and water. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 45 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the pot and set it aside to cool slightly. Shred the chicken into smaller pieces using a fork.

  6. Meanwhile, bring the soup back to a gentle simmer. Add the long-grain rice and cook until it is tender, usually around 15 minutes.

  7. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and lemon juice until well combined.

  8. Slowly add a ladleful of the hot soup broth to the egg and lemon mixture, whisking continuously. This step is crucial to avoid curdling the eggs.

  9. Gradually pour the egg and lemon mixture back into the pot, stirring continuously. Cook the soup for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  10. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Remember, the chicken broth already contains salt, so be mindful while seasoning.

  11. Remove the pot from the heat and let the soup rest for a few minutes.

  12. Serve the Traditional Greek Avgolemono Soup hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley. The soup can be enjoyed as a standalone dish or served alongside some crusty bread.

Conclusion

Traditional Greek Avgolemono Soup with Chicken is a comforting delight that brings warmth and flavor to any table. The creamy texture and tangy taste of the lemon-infused broth make it a perfect choice for a cozy meal or a comforting dish during colder months. The simple yet flavorful combination of chicken, rice, and the velvety egg-lemon sauce creates a harmonious balance that will satisfy your palate and leave you wanting more. So, don’t hesitate to try this traditional Greek recipe and experience the heartwarming comfort it brings.
