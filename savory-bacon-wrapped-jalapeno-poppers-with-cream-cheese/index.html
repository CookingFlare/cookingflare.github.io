Savory Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers with Cream Cheese


Are you ready to take your appetizer game to the next level? Look no further than these delicious and savory bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers with a creamy twist of cream cheese. This recipe combines the perfect balance of heat, smokiness, and creaminess, making it an absolute crowd-pleaser for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh jalapeno peppers
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • Toothpicks, for securing the bacon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy clean-up.

  2. Slice the jalapeno peppers in half lengthwise and remove the seeds and membranes. You can use a spoon or a small knife to gently scrape them out. Be sure to wear gloves to protect your hands from the heat of the peppers.

  3. In a bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, and smoked paprika. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. Spoon the cream cheese mixture into each jalapeno half, filling them generously. Make sure to press the filling down to ensure it stays in place.

  5. Take a slice of bacon and wrap it around each stuffed jalapeno, securing it with a toothpick. Repeat until all the jalapenos are wrapped.

  6. Place the bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers on the prepared baking sheet. Make sure to leave some space between each popper to allow even cooking.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and golden brown. Keep an eye on them, as cooking times may vary depending on your oven.

  8. Once cooked, remove the poppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. The cream cheese filling will be hot, so be cautious when taking a bite!

  9. Serve these savory bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers as a crowd-pleasing appetizer at your next gathering. They pair perfectly with a cool and refreshing dip, such as ranch or sour cream.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, try adding some shredded cheddar cheese or crumbled blue cheese to the cream cheese mixture before filling the jalapenos.
  • If you prefer a milder version, you can remove the seeds and membranes from the jalapenos more thoroughly or substitute them with mini sweet peppers.
  • To make these poppers in advance, you can assemble them up to a day ahead and refrigerate until ready to bake. Just remember to bring them to room temperature before baking to ensure even cooking.
  • If you’re grilling, these jalapeno poppers can also be cooked on a preheated grill over medium heat until the bacon is crispy. Just be sure to keep a close eye on them, as the cooking time may be shorter than in the oven.

So there you have it - a foolproof recipe for savory bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers with a creamy surprise inside. These poppers are sure to be a hit at your next gathering, whether it’s a summer barbecue or a cozy game night. Enjoy the perfect combination of smoky bacon, spicy jalapenos, and creamy cream cheese in every delicious bite!

Note: Remember to adjust the spice level according to your preference and the heat of the jalapenos. Enjoy responsibly and have fun experimenting with this versatile recipe!
