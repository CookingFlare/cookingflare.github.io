10-Minute Mexican Street Corn Dip Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious dip to serve at your next gathering, this 10-minute Mexican Street Corn Dip is the perfect choice. Packed with bold flavors and featuring the irresistible combination of corn, cheese, and spices, this dip will have your guests coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen corn, thawed
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the thawed corn, mayonnaise, sour cream, cotija cheese, cilantro, green onions, jalapeño, garlic, chili powder, cumin, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Taste and adjust the seasonings according to your preference. If you like it spicier, add more jalapeño or chili powder. If you prefer it milder, reduce the amount of jalapeño or omit it altogether.

  3. Transfer the dip to a serving bowl and garnish with additional crumbled cotija cheese, chopped cilantro, and a sprinkle of chili powder. You can also add a squeeze of lime juice for an extra burst of freshness.

  4. Serve the Mexican Street Corn Dip with tortilla chips, toasted baguette slices, or fresh vegetable sticks. The creamy and tangy flavors of the dip will pair perfectly with the crunchy texture of the chips or bread.

  5. Enjoy this delicious dip as an appetizer, snack, or even as a topping for grilled meats or tacos. Its versatility makes it a crowd-pleaser for any occasion.

Tips for Serving

  • For a smoky twist, you can grill the corn before preparing the dip. Simply brush the corn with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and grill over medium heat until charred and tender. Allow the corn to cool before adding it to the dip.

  • If you can’t find cotija cheese, you can substitute it with feta cheese. The crumbly texture and salty flavor of feta cheese work well in this recipe.

  • To make the dip ahead of time, prepare all the ingredients and store them separately. Mix everything together just before serving to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.

  • If you want to add a bit of sweetness to the dip, you can mix in some diced roasted red peppers or a teaspoon of honey.

  • Feel free to customize the spice level of the dip by adjusting the amount of jalapeño or adding a dash of hot sauce.

Final Thoughts

This 10-Minute Mexican Street Corn Dip is a true crowd-pleaser. Its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and creamy texture make it a hit at any gathering or party. Whether you’re hosting a game night, a potluck, or simply enjoying a cozy night in, this dip will take your taste buds on a journey to the streets of Mexico. So grab your favorite chips, gather your friends and family, and get ready to indulge in this mouthwatering delight.
