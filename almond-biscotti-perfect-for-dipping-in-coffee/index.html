Almond Biscotti: Perfect for Dipping in Coffee


There’s something about the combination of a crispy biscuit and a hot cup of coffee that just hits the spot. And when it comes to the perfect biscuit for dipping, almond biscotti takes the crown. These Italian delights are twice-baked, resulting in a crunchy texture that’s just begging to be dunked into a steaming mug of coffee. So, if you’re ready to elevate your coffee break to a new level of indulgence, let’s dive into the world of almond biscotti and learn how to make them from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 cup whole almonds, toasted and coarsely chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, vanilla extract, and almond extract until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the egg mixture to the dry ingredients, mixing until a dough starts to form.

  5. Fold in the chopped almonds, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  6. Divide the dough in half and shape each half into a log about 12 inches long and 2 inches wide, placing them on the prepared baking sheet.

  7. Bake for 25 minutes, or until the logs are firm to the touch and lightly golden.

  8. Remove the logs from the oven and let them cool for 15 minutes. Reduce the oven temperature to 325°F (160°C).

  9. Using a serrated knife, carefully slice the logs into 1/2-inch-thick biscotti.

  10. Place the biscotti cut side down on the baking sheet and return them to the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until they are golden and crisp.

  11. Once baked, transfer the biscotti to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

Now that you’ve mastered the art of making almond biscotti, it’s time to enjoy them to the fullest. These crunchy treats are perfect for dipping into a variety of beverages, but they truly shine when paired with a freshly brewed cup of coffee. The combination of the nutty almond flavor and the rich, aromatic coffee creates a sensory experience that’s hard to beat.

For an extra indulgent treat, consider dipping one end of the biscotti in melted dark chocolate and allowing it to set before serving. The added richness of the chocolate takes these biscotti to a whole new level of decadence.

Final Thoughts

Almond biscotti may seem like a fancy treat reserved for coffee shops, but with this recipe, you can bring the magic of these crunchy delights into your own kitchen. Whether you’re looking for a delightful accompaniment to your morning coffee or a sweet treat to enjoy with afternoon tea, almond biscotti are the perfect choice. So, grab your apron and get ready to impress your friends and family with this classic Italian biscuit that’s simply perfect for dipping in coffee. Enjoy!
