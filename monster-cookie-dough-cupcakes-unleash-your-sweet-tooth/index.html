Step 2: Mix the dry ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine your all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Start by stirring with a whisk or a fork until you have a relatively smooth mixture without any lumps.

Step 3: Cream your butter and sugars

In another mixing bowl, use an electric or stand mixer to beat the softened unsalted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar together until you achieve a light and creamy consistency.

Step 4: Combine the wet ingredients

To your creamy mixture, add the pure vanilla extract and eggs. Mix until everything is well combined. Then, turn your mixer on low and gradually add in your dry ingredients. Mix until everything is well incorporated, but don’t overdo it to avoid any dry or lumpy batter.

Step 5: Fold in the goodies

Now, it’s time to add your ‘goods’ to the dough. Fold in your mini chocolate chips and a good amount of m&m’s by hand until they are evenly distributed throughout your batter.
