Slow Cooker BBQ Recipes for Finger-Licking Goodness


If you’re a fan of finger-licking BBQ goodness but don’t have the time to tend to a grill all day, then slow cooker BBQ recipes are here to save the day! With the help of your trusty slow cooker, you can achieve tender, flavorful, and melt-in-your-mouth BBQ dishes without breaking a sweat. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering slow cooker BBQ recipes that will leave you craving for more.

1. Pulled Pork Heaven

Let’s start with a classic: pulled pork. This versatile dish is a crowd-pleaser and perfect for any occasion. To make this finger-licking goodness, you’ll need:

  • 3 to 4 pounds of pork shoulder or butt
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the pork shoulder with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, and brown sugar.
  3. Place the seasoned pork shoulder in the slow cooker and pour the BBQ sauce mixture over it.
  4. Cook on low heat for 8 to 10 hours or on high heat for 4 to 6 hours, until the pork is tender and easily shredded.
  5. Once cooked, remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  6. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and mix it with the remaining BBQ sauce.
  7. Cook for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Serve the pulled pork on a soft bun with coleslaw for a classic pulled pork sandwich or use it as a filling for tacos, enchiladas, or sliders. The possibilities are endless!

2. Smoky BBQ Ribs

Now, let’s dive into some smoky and succulent BBQ ribs. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 racks of baby back ribs
  • 1 cup of BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the racks of ribs with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce and brown sugar.
  3. Place the seasoned ribs in the slow cooker and pour the BBQ sauce mixture over them, ensuring the ribs are evenly coated.
  4. Cook on low heat for 6 to 8 hours or on high heat for 3 to 4 hours, until the meat is tender and easily falls off the bone.
  5. Once cooked, carefully remove the ribs from the slow cooker and transfer them to a baking sheet.
  6. Brush the ribs with additional BBQ sauce and broil them in the oven for 5 to 7 minutes, until they develop a caramelized crust.

Serve these smoky BBQ ribs with cornbread, baked beans, and a refreshing coleslaw for a complete BBQ feast.

3. Tangy BBQ Chicken

Last but not least, let’s explore a tangy BBQ chicken recipe that will leave your taste buds dancing. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4 to 6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, honey, Dijon mustard, and Worcestershire sauce.
  3. Place the seasoned chicken breasts in the slow cooker and pour the BBQ sauce mixture over them.
  4. Cook on low heat for 4 to 6 hours or on high heat for 2 to 3 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  6. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and mix it with the remaining BBQ sauce.
  7. Cook for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Serve the tangy BBQ chicken on a bed of fluffy rice or as a filling for sandwiches, wraps, or salads. It’s guaranteed to be a hit!

Conclusion

With these slow cooker BBQ recipes, you can achieve finger-licking goodness without the hassle of tending to a grill. Whether you’re hosting a backyard party or simply craving some BBQ goodness, these recipes will satisfy your taste buds. So dust off your slow cooker and get ready to indulge in tender, flavorful, and melt-in-your-mouth BBQ dishes. Happy cooking!

Remember, these recipes are just a starting point. Feel free to experiment with different spices, sauces, and ingredients to create your own signature slow cooker BBQ masterpieces. The possibilities are endless, and the results are guaranteed to be finger-licking good!
