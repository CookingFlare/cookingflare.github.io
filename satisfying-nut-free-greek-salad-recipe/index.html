Satisfying Nut-Free Greek Salad Recipe


Greek salads are renowned for their vibrant colors, fresh ingredients, and robust flavors that transport you to the sun-drenched shores of the Mediterranean. This Nut-Free Greek Salad recipe stays true to tradition while offering a safe and delicious option for those with nut allergies. Packed with crisp vegetables, tangy olives, creamy feta cheese, and a zesty dressing, this salad is not only nutritious but also incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 4 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

To begin, prepare all your ingredients. Dice the tomatoes, cucumber, red and yellow bell peppers into bite-sized pieces. Thinly slice the red onion, pit the Kalamata olives if necessary, and crumble the feta cheese.

Making the Salad

  1. Combine Vegetables: In a large salad bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, cucumber, red and yellow bell peppers, sliced red onion, and Kalamata olives.

  2. Add Feta Cheese: Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the vegetables. The creamy texture of the feta will complement the crispness of the vegetables.

Creating the Dressing

  1. Mix Olive Oil and Vinegar: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar until well combined. This classic dressing forms the base of the salad’s flavor profile.

  2. Seasoning: Add dried oregano, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper to the dressing mixture. Adjust seasoning according to your taste preferences. The oregano adds a Mediterranean flair, while the Dijon mustard provides a subtle tanginess.

  3. Dress the Salad: Drizzle the dressing over the salad ingredients in the bowl. Gently toss the salad using salad tongs or two large spoons to ensure that the dressing coats each ingredient evenly. Be careful not to overdress; you can always add more dressing if needed.

Serving the Salad

  1. Chill and Garnish: Once the salad is dressed, refrigerate it for about 15-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and an extra burst of freshness.

  2. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the Nut-Free Greek Salad as a refreshing side dish or a light main course. It pairs beautifully with grilled meats, seafood, or as part of a larger Mediterranean-inspired meal.

Tips for the Perfect Greek Salad

  • Fresh Ingredients: Use the freshest vegetables available for the best flavor and texture. Farmers’ markets or local produce stands are excellent sources.
  • Customization: Feel free to customize the salad to your liking. Add or omit ingredients such as green bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, or even a sprinkle of capers for added zest.
  • Make Ahead: The salad can be prepared a few hours in advance and stored in the refrigerator until ready to serve. This makes it ideal for meal prepping or entertaining guests.

Nutritional Benefits

Greek salads are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Tomatoes provide vitamin C and lycopene, while cucumbers offer hydration and fiber. Olives and olive oil contribute heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, and feta cheese adds calcium and protein. Together, these ingredients create a well-rounded dish that nourishes both body and soul.

Final Thoughts

This Nut-Free Greek Salad recipe is a testament to the simplicity and elegance of Mediterranean cuisine. Whether enjoyed on a sunny afternoon or as part of a festive gathering, its flavors will transport you to the shores of Greece. Embrace the freshness of seasonal vegetables, the tang of olives and feta, and the zing of the homemade dressing. Share this salad with friends and family, and savor the joy of wholesome, nut-free eating. Cheers to good health and delicious food!
