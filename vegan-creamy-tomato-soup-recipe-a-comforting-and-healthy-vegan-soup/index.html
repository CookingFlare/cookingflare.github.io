Vegan Creamy Tomato Soup Recipe: A Comforting and Healthy Vegan Soup


Are you looking for a soul-warming, creamy, and healthy vegan soup recipe? Look no further! This Vegan Creamy Tomato Soup recipe is a delightful blend of rich flavors and wholesome ingredients that will satisfy your comfort food cravings while nourishing your body. Whether you’re a dedicated vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, stirring frequently.

  2. Simmer the Tomatoes: Add the whole peeled tomatoes to the pot, using a wooden spoon to break them up into smaller pieces. Let the tomatoes simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Blend the Soup: Once the tomatoes have softened, carefully transfer the mixture to a blender and blend until smooth. Return the blended mixture to the pot.

  4. Add the Creaminess: Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, stirring to combine. Stir in the tomato paste, sugar, dried basil, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Simmer and Serve: Bring the soup to a gentle simmer over low heat, stirring occasionally. Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to develop. Once ready, ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Why You’ll Love This Vegan Creamy Tomato Soup:

  • Wholesome Ingredients: This recipe is packed with the goodness of whole peeled tomatoes, coconut milk, and a variety of aromatic spices, making it a nourishing meal option.
  • Creamy and Comforting: The addition of coconut milk creates a luxurious, creamy texture that is both indulgent and satisfying.
  • Versatile and Customizable: You can easily customize this recipe by adding your favorite herbs or spices to suit your taste preferences.
  • Perfect for Any Occasion: Whether you’re seeking a cozy meal on a chilly evening or a light lunch option, this soup fits the bill perfectly.

In Conclusion:

This Vegan Creamy Tomato Soup recipe offers a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will leave you feeling nourished and content. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a crusty slice of bread or a fresh salad. Embrace the comfort of a warm bowl of soup while knowing that you’re nourishing your body with wholesome, plant-based ingredients. Try this recipe today and experience the heartwarming goodness of a comforting and healthy vegan soup!
