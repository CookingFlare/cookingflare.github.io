Tasty Low Carb Stuffed Bell Peppers


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s both satisfying and low in carbs, then you’re in for a treat! Our Tasty Low Carb Stuffed Bell Peppers recipe is a perfect choice for anyone watching their carbohydrate intake but still craving a flavorful and filling dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking dish with parchment paper or lightly grease it with olive oil.

  2. Cut off the tops of the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the bell peppers upright in the prepared baking dish.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  4. Add the ground turkey to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon. Season with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.

  5. Stir in the cauliflower rice and diced tomatoes. Cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the mixture cool slightly. Then, stir in the shredded cheddar cheese and chopped fresh parsley.

  7. Spoon the turkey mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers, filling them to the top. Place the stuffed bell peppers back into the baking dish.

  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for another 10-15 minutes, or until the bell peppers are tender and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Once cooked, remove the stuffed bell peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with additional parsley, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

These Tasty Low Carb Stuffed Bell Peppers can be enjoyed on their own as a satisfying main dish, but they also pair well with a side salad or some roasted vegetables. For an extra kick of flavor, you can top the stuffed bell peppers with a dollop of Greek yogurt or a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.

Nutritional Information

Each serving of our Tasty Low Carb Stuffed Bell Peppers provides a balanced combination of protein, healthy fats, and essential vitamins and minerals. The exact nutritional values may vary depending on the specific ingredients and brands used, but here’s a general breakdown to give you an idea:

  • Calories: 250-300
  • Protein: 25-30 grams
  • Carbohydrates: 10-15 grams
  • Fat: 10-15 grams
  • Fiber: 3-5 grams

Final Thoughts

With their vibrant colors and delicious flavors, these Tasty Low Carb Stuffed Bell Peppers are a crowd-pleaser that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into your diet, this recipe is a winner. So go ahead, give it a try, and savor every bite of this healthy and mouthwatering dish!
