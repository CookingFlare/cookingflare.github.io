Delicious and Unique Olive Bread Recipes

Are you a bread lover? Do you enjoy experimenting with unique flavors and ingredients? If so, you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious and unique olive bread recipes that will surely impress your taste buds. Whether you’re new to bread making or a seasoned pro, these recipes are sure to inspire you to get creative in the kitchen. Recipe 1: Rosemary and Kalamata Olive Bread Ingredients: 3 cups all-purpose flour 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast 1 ½ teaspoons salt 1 ½ cups warm water 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped Instructions: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, and salt. Gradually add in the warm water and olive oil, stirring until a sticky dough forms. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Place the dough back into the mixing bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Punch down the risen dough and gently fold in the chopped olives and rosemary. Shape the dough into a round loaf and place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Let the dough rise for another 30 minutes. Bake the bread for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and hollow-sounding when tapped on the bottom. Allow the bread to cool before slicing and serving. Recipe 2: Sundried Tomato and Green Olive Focaccia Ingredients: 4 cups bread flour 2 teaspoons instant yeast 2 teaspoons salt 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 ¾ cups warm water ½ cup pitted green olives, sliced ½ cup sundried tomatoes, chopped Instructions: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, and salt. Gradually add in the warm water and olive oil, stirring until a sticky dough forms. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Place the dough back into the mixing bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Punch down the risen dough and gently fold in the sliced green olives and chopped sundried tomatoes. Grease a baking sheet with olive oil and transfer the dough onto it, spreading it out evenly. Drizzle some olive oil on top of the dough and use your fingertips to create dimples all over the surface. Let the dough rise for another 30 minutes. Bake the focaccia for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown. Allow the bread to cool slightly before slicing and serving. Recipe 3: Olive and Feta Cheese Breadsticks Ingredients: 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon instant yeast 1 teaspoon salt 1 cup warm water 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 cup pitted black olives, sliced ½ cup crumbled feta cheese Instructions: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, and salt. Gradually add in the warm water and olive oil, stirring until a sticky dough forms. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Place the dough back into the mixing bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Punch down the risen dough and gently fold in the sliced black olives and crumbled feta cheese. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a long, thin breadstick shape. Place the breadsticks on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Let the dough rise for another 30 minutes. Bake the breadsticks for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown. Allow the breadsticks to cool before serving. Conclusion There you have it, three delicious and unique olive bread recipes to spice up your baking adventures. Whether you prefer the savory combination of rosemary and Kalamata olives, the Mediterranean flavors of sundried tomatoes and green olives, or the tangy goodness of black olives and feta cheese, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings.
