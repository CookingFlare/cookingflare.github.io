Satisfying Dairy-Free Coconut Curry Recipe


Coconut curry is a beloved dish that tantalizes the taste buds with its rich and aromatic flavors. This dairy-free version of the classic coconut curry is a delightful option for those with lactose intolerance or anyone looking to explore the diverse world of plant-based cooking. The combination of coconut milk, flavorful spices, and an array of fresh vegetables creates a dish that is not only delicious but also satisfying and nourishing.

Ingredients

For the Curry Paste:

  • 2 shallots, roughly chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 2 red chilies, deseeded and chopped
  • 1 lemongrass stalk, tough outer layer removed and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon tomato puree
  • Zest and juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Curry:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded and sliced
  • 1 yellow pepper, deseeded and sliced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Curry Paste:

    • In a food processor, combine the shallots, garlic, ginger, red chilies, lemongrass, ground coriander, ground cumin, ground turmeric, tomato puree, lime zest, and lime juice. Blend until a smooth paste forms.

  2. Cooking the Curry:

    • Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and cook until softened.
    • Add the prepared curry paste to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly to release the flavors.
    • Stir in the sliced red and yellow peppers, green beans, broccoli florets, and snow peas. Cook for 5 minutes, allowing the vegetables to soften slightly.

  3. Adding the Liquid Ingredients:

    • Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
    • Add the soy sauce and brown sugar, then season with salt to taste. Allow the curry to simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the curry reaches the desired consistency, remove it from the heat.
    • Serve the dairy-free coconut curry over cooked rice or noodles, garnished with fresh cilantro for a burst of color and flavor.

Tips for Success

  • To make this dish even heartier, consider adding protein such as tofu, chickpeas, or seitan.
  • Experiment with different vegetable combinations based on your preferences and seasonal availability.
  • For an extra kick of heat, leave the seeds in the red chilies or add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the curry paste.

Final Thoughts

This dairy-free coconut curry recipe is a celebration of vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe invites you to explore the art of plant-based cooking while savoring a satisfying and aromatic dish. Embrace the versatility of coconut milk and fragrant spices as they come together to create a curry that is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Enjoy the process of preparing this delightful dish and savor each nourishing bite!
