Easy Homemade Cinnamon Rolls: A Breakfast Treat Worth Waking Up For


There’s nothing quite like the comforting aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls wafting through the air. These Easy Homemade Cinnamon Rolls are a breakfast treat that will have you jumping out of bed in the morning. With their soft, fluffy dough and gooey cinnamon filling, they are the perfect indulgence to start your day off right.

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well.

  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk, water, and melted butter until warm (about 110°F/43°C). Add the warm milk mixture and the egg to the dry ingredients. Stir until a soft dough forms.

  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  4. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon until well combined.

  5. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down and turn it out onto a lightly floured surface. Roll the dough into a 12x18-inch rectangle.

  6. Spread the filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Starting from one long side, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Pinch the seam to seal.

  7. Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 12 equal slices. Place the slices in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.

  8. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

  9. While the cinnamon rolls are baking, prepare the icing. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  10. Remove the cinnamon rolls from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the icing over the warm rolls.

Serving Suggestions

Serve these Easy Homemade Cinnamon Rolls warm with a steaming cup of coffee or a tall glass of milk. They are perfect for breakfast or brunch, but they can also be enjoyed as a sweet treat any time of the day.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a handful of raisins or chopped nuts to the filling before rolling up the dough.
  • If you prefer a cream cheese icing, simply replace the milk in the icing recipe with softened cream cheese.
  • These cinnamon rolls can be made ahead of time and stored in an airtight container for up to 2 days. Just warm them up in the microwave for a few seconds before serving.

So, why wait? Treat yourself and your loved ones to these Easy Homemade Cinnamon Rolls. They are sure to become a breakfast favorite that will have you looking forward to waking up in the morning. Enjoy!
