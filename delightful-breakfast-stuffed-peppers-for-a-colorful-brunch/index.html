Delightful Breakfast Stuffed Peppers for a Colorful Brunch


When it comes to brunch, we all love a dish that’s not only delicious but also visually appealing. Enter the delightful breakfast stuffed peppers – a colorful and flavorful option that will brighten up any brunch table. This recipe combines the vibrant hues of bell peppers with the rich, savory flavors of breakfast staples, creating a dish that’s as pleasing to the eyes as it is to the taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (assorted colors for a visually stunning presentation)
  • 6 eggs
  • 1/2 cup diced ham
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil spray

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Peppers

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes from the insides.
  3. Place the peppers in a baking dish, cut side up, and lightly spray the insides with cooking oil spray. This will help them cook evenly and prevent sticking.

Step 2: Filling Preparation

  1. In a skillet, sauté the diced onions until they are translucent and fragrant.
  2. Add the diced ham and cook until it’s lightly browned.
  3. Stir in the chopped spinach and diced tomatoes, cooking until the spinach wilts.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.

Step 3: Assembling the Stuffed Peppers

  1. Divide the egg mixture evenly among the bell peppers, filling each one about three-quarters full.
  2. Spoon the sautéed ham, onion, spinach, and tomato mixture on top of the eggs in each pepper.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the filling, allowing it to cascade down the sides for a beautiful presentation.

Step 4: Baking

  1. Cover the baking dish with foil and place it in the preheated oven.
  2. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the peppers are tender but still slightly crisp.

Step 5: Serving

  1. Carefully remove the stuffed peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  2. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh herbs, such as parsley or chives, for a pop of color and added freshness.
  3. Serve the breakfast stuffed peppers warm, alongside a fresh fruit salad or a slice of crusty bread for a complete and colorful brunch experience.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Colorful Presentation: The assortment of bell peppers in vibrant colors creates a visually stunning dish that’s perfect for a brunch spread.
  • Customizable: Feel free to customize the filling with your favorite breakfast ingredients, such as bacon, sausage, or different types of cheese.
  • Nutritious and Filling: Packed with protein from the eggs and ham, as well as the goodness of vegetables, these stuffed peppers make for a satisfying and wholesome brunch option.
  • Make-Ahead Friendly: You can prepare the filling ahead of time and simply assemble and bake the peppers when you’re ready to serve, making it a convenient dish for entertaining.

So, the next time you’re hosting a brunch or simply want to elevate your weekend breakfast, consider whipping up these delightful breakfast stuffed peppers. Not only are they a feast for the eyes, but they also deliver on flavor and nutritional value, making them a brunch favorite that’s sure to impress your guests. Enjoy!
