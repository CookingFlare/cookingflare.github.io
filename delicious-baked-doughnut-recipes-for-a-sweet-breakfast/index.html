Delicious Baked Doughnut Recipes for a Sweet Breakfast


Doughnuts are a classic breakfast treat that we all love. However, traditional fried doughnuts can be heavy and greasy. That’s why I’m here to share with you some delicious baked doughnut recipes that are not only healthier but also incredibly tasty. So, let’s dive right into these mouthwatering recipes that will make your breakfasts extra special!

1. Cinnamon Sugar Baked Doughnuts

Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 12 minutes | Total time: 27 minutes | Yield: 12 doughnuts

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a doughnut pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared doughnut pan, filling each cavity about two-thirds full.
  6. Bake for 12 minutes or until the doughnuts are lightly golden.
  7. Remove from the oven and let them cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack.
  8. While still warm, toss the doughnuts in a mixture of cinnamon and sugar.
  9. Serve and enjoy these delicious cinnamon sugar baked doughnuts!

2. Chocolate Glazed Baked Doughnuts

Prep time: 20 minutes | Cook time: 10 minutes | Total time: 30 minutes | Yield: 12 doughnuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3-4 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a doughnut pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, salt, granulated sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared doughnut pan, filling each cavity about two-thirds full.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the doughnuts comes out clean.
  7. Remove from the oven and let them cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack.
  8. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, cocoa powder, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  9. Dip each doughnut into the glaze and let the excess drip off.
  10. Place the glazed doughnuts on a wire rack to set.
  11. Indulge in these decadent chocolate glazed baked doughnuts!

3. Blueberry Lemon Baked Doughnuts

Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 12 minutes | Total time: 27 minutes | Yield: 12 doughnuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a doughnut pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, Greek yogurt, melted butter, eggs, vanilla extract, and lemon zest.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  6. Spoon the batter into the prepared doughnut pan, filling each cavity about two-thirds full.
  7. Bake for 12 minutes or until the doughnuts are lightly golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and let them cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack.
  9. Enjoy these delightful blueberry lemon baked doughnuts!

Conclusion

These baked doughnut recipes offer a healthier twist on the classic breakfast treat. Whether you prefer the warmth of cinnamon sugar, the richness of chocolate glaze, or the freshness of blueberry lemon, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to a sweet and delicious breakfast with these homemade baked doughnuts? Enjoy!
