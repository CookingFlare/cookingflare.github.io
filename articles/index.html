Not only is it incredibly tasty, but it’s also a breeze to make, making it the perfect meal for busy weeknights or casual gatherings with friends and family. Ingredients 6 boneless, skinless chicken thighs 12 slices of bacon 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon paprika 1 teaspoon dried thyme Salt and black pepper to taste Fresh parsley for garnish Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Season the chicken thighs with garlic powder, paprika, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper, ensuring that each piece is evenly coated with the flavorful seasoning. Carefully wrap each seasoned chicken thigh with two slices of bacon, securing them with toothpicks if necessary. In a large oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the bacon-wrapped chicken thighs to the skillet, searing them for 2-3 minutes on each side until the bacon is slightly crispy. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the bacon is golden and crispy. Once done, remove the skillet from the oven and let the chicken thighs rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and an extra burst of freshness.
