A Taste of Italy: 4 Marinades for Grilled Chicken Parmesan


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, then you’re probably familiar with the classic dish, Chicken Parmesan. This delicious and hearty meal consists of breaded and fried chicken cutlets smothered in tomato sauce, topped with melted cheese, and served over a bed of pasta. But have you ever thought about taking this dish to the next level by grilling the chicken instead? Grilled Chicken Parmesan not only adds a smoky flavor to the dish but also makes it a healthier alternative to the traditional fried version. And to make it even more exciting, we have four delicious marinade recipes that will elevate your Grilled Chicken Parmesan to new heights of flavor. Let’s dive in!

1. Classic Italian Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated.
  3. Marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard any excess marinade.
  5. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

2. Lemon Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, minced garlic, chopped parsley, chopped basil, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated.
  3. Marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard any excess marinade.
  5. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

3. Sun-Dried Tomato and Basil Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (from the sun-dried tomato jar)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Drain the sun-dried tomatoes from the oil and finely chop them.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the chopped sun-dried tomatoes, olive oil, minced garlic, chopped basil, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated.
  4. Marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard any excess marinade.
  6. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  7. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

4. Spicy Garlic and Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated.
  3. Marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard any excess marinade.
  5. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Now that you have four tantalizing marinade options, it’s time to fire up that grill and give your Chicken Parmesan a touch of smoky goodness. Whichever marinade you choose, the result will be a mouthwatering dish that will transport you straight to the heart of Italy. Serve your Grilled Chicken Parmesan with a side of pasta or a fresh green salad for a complete Italian feast. Buon appetito!
