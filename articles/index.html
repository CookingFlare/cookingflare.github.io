Easy Chicken and Rice Burrito Bowl Recipe

Looking for a quick, delicious, and satisfying meal? Look no further than this easy chicken and rice burrito bowl recipe. Packed with flavor, protein, and wholesome ingredients, this dish is perfect for a busy weeknight dinner or a relaxed weekend lunch. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to tantalize your taste buds. Ingredients 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 cup of white or brown rice 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed 1 cup of corn kernels (fresh, canned, or frozen) 1 red bell pepper, diced 1 red onion, finely chopped 1 teaspoon of chili powder 1 teaspoon of cumin 1 teaspoon of paprika Salt and pepper to taste 1 lime, juiced Fresh cilantro for garnish Optional toppings: shredded cheese, diced avocado, salsa, sour cream Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, chili powder, and cumin. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked. Once cooked, let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips. Step 2: Cook the Rice In a medium saucepan, cook the rice according to the package instructions. Fluff with a fork and set aside. Step 3: Prepare the Vegetables In the same skillet used for the chicken, add a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the diced red bell pepper and red onion until they are tender and slightly caramelized. Add the corn kernels and black beans to the skillet, and stir well to combine. Season the vegetable mixture with paprika, salt, and pepper. Step 4: Assemble the Burrito Bowls Start by dividing the cooked rice among serving bowls. Top the rice with the sautéed vegetable mixture and sliced chicken. Squeeze fresh lime juice over each bowl for a burst of citrus flavor. Garnish with fresh cilantro and any optional toppings of your choice. Enjoy Your Creation Now that you’ve completed the simple steps, it’s time to savor the fruits of your labor. The combination of tender chicken, fluffy rice, vibrant vegetables, and zesty lime juice creates a harmonious medley of flavors and textures in every bite.
