Paleo Beef and Mushroom Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


As a fan of hearty and flavorful dishes, I’m excited to share with you five mouth-watering paleo beef and mushroom recipes that are not only delicious but also grain-free and packed with nutrients. In this post, we’ll dive into the world of paleo cooking and explore the perfect combination of tender beef and earthy mushrooms.

Recipe 1: Beef and Mushroom Stroganoff

In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of coconut oil over medium-high heat. Add 1 pound of sliced beef strips and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside. Add 1 cup of sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown, about 5 minutes. Add 1 tablespoon of tomato paste, 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard, and 1/2 teaspoon of paprika. Stir well to combine. Add the cooked beef back into the skillet and stir to combine. Serve over zucchini noodles or cauliflower rice.

Recipe 2: Mushroom and Beef Meatballs

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large bowl, combine 1 pound of ground beef, 1/2 cup of finely chopped mushrooms, 1 egg, 1/4 cup of almond flour, 1/4 cup of coconut milk, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Mix well with your hands until just combined. Use your hands to shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until cooked through. Serve with a side of roasted vegetables.

Recipe 3: Beef and Mushroom Fajitas

In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of coconut oil over medium-high heat. Add 1 pound of sliced beef strips and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside. Add 1 cup of sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown, about 5 minutes. Add 1/4 cup of sliced red bell pepper and 1/4 cup of sliced yellow bell pepper. Cook for an additional 2 minutes. Add the cooked beef back into the skillet and stir to combine. Serve with a side of sautéed onions and bell peppers.

Recipe 4: Beef and Mushroom Shepherd’s Pie

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of coconut oil over medium-high heat. Add 1 pound of sliced beef strips and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside. Add 1 cup of sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown, about 5 minutes. Add 1/4 cup of beef broth and 1/4 cup of red wine. Stir well to combine. Add the cooked beef back into the skillet and stir to combine. Transfer the mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with 1 cup of mashed sweet potatoes and 1/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the sweet potatoes are golden brown.

Recipe 5: Beef and Mushroom Burgers

In a large bowl, combine 1 pound of ground beef, 1/2 cup of finely chopped mushrooms, 1 egg, 1/4 cup of almond flour, 1/4 cup of coconut milk, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Mix well with your hands until just combined. Use your hands to shape the mixture into patties, about 1/2 inch thick. Place the patties on a preheated grill or grill pan. Cook for 4-5 minutes per side or until cooked through. Serve on a bed of lettuce with your favorite toppings.

In conclusion, these five paleo beef and mushroom recipes are perfect for anyone looking to add some excitement to their meal routine. With their rich flavors and textures, they’re sure to become a staple in your household. So go ahead, get cooking, and enjoy the delicious combination of tender beef and earthy mushrooms!
