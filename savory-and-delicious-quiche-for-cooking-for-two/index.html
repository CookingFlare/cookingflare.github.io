Savory and Delicious Quiche for Cooking for Two


If you’re looking for a dish that’s both elegant and comforting, look no further than a savory and delicious quiche. This classic French dish is perfect for a cozy dinner for two, and the best part is that you can customize it with your favorite ingredients. Whether you prefer a vegetarian quiche with spinach and mushrooms or a hearty ham and cheese quiche, there’s a combination to suit every taste.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (such as Gruyere, cheddar, or Swiss)
  • 1/2 cup cooked and chopped vegetables or meat of your choice (spinach, mushrooms, ham, bacon, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Add the chilled, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Drizzle the ice water over the mixture and stir with a fork until the dough comes together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  3. Roll Out the Dough:

    • On a floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle, about 12 inches in diameter. Carefully transfer the dough to a 9-inch pie dish, gently pressing it into the bottom and up the sides. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges.

  4. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined. Stir in the shredded cheese and your choice of cooked vegetables or meat.

  5. Assemble the Quiche:

    • Pour the filling into the prepared pie crust and spread it out evenly.

  6. Bake the Quiche:

    • Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Pair it with a simple green salad or fresh fruit for a delightful meal for two.

Tips and Variations

  • Make-Ahead: You can prepare the quiche up to a day in advance and refrigerate it until ready to bake.
  • Crust Options: If you’re short on time, a store-bought pie crust works just as well.
  • Customize the Fillings: Feel free to experiment with different combinations of vegetables, cheeses, and meats to create your perfect quiche.
  • Mini Quiches: If you prefer individual servings, divide the dough and filling among smaller tart pans or ramekins for adorable mini quiches.

Quiche is a versatile and impressive dish that’s perfect for a special dinner at home. With its flaky crust and creamy, flavorful filling, it’s sure to become a favorite in your cooking repertoire. So, gather your favorite ingredients, preheat that oven, and get ready to savor the delightful flavors of a homemade quiche for two!
