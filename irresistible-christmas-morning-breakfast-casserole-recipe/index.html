Irresistible Christmas Morning Breakfast Casserole Recipe


Christmas morning is a time of joy, excitement, and anticipation. It’s a time when families come together to celebrate and create lasting memories. And what better way to kick-start the festivities than with a delicious and comforting breakfast? This Irresistible Christmas Morning Breakfast Casserole is the perfect dish to fuel your family for the day ahead.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread, crusts removed
  • 1 pound of breakfast sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of paprika
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, Dijon mustard, salt, black pepper, and paprika until well combined. Set aside.

  3. Cut the crusts off the bread slices and arrange them in a single layer at the bottom of the greased baking dish. Make sure to cover the entire surface.

  4. Sprinkle the cooked and crumbled breakfast sausage evenly over the bread slices. Follow by layering the shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheeses on top.

  5. Give the egg mixture a quick stir and then pour it evenly over the casserole, making sure to cover all the ingredients.

  6. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 20-25 minutes until the casserole is set and the top is golden brown.

  7. Once baked, remove the casserole from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  8. Serve the Irresistible Christmas Morning Breakfast Casserole warm, accompanied by freshly brewed coffee, hot chocolate, or your favorite festive drink.

Tips and Variations

  • Make it Ahead: This breakfast casserole can be prepared the night before. After assembling the casserole, cover it with foil and refrigerate overnight. In the morning, simply pop it in the oven and bake as directed. This makes it a perfect time-saving option for busy Christmas mornings.

  • Add Vegetables: Feel free to add some sautéed vegetables like bell peppers, onions, or mushrooms to the casserole for an extra burst of flavor and nutrition. Simply cook the vegetables before adding them to the casserole.

  • Experiment with Cheese: While cheddar and mozzarella cheeses work perfectly in this recipe, you can also try different combinations like Swiss, pepper jack, or even a sprinkle of Parmesan for added richness.

  • Customize with Seasonings: If you like a little kick, spice things up by adding a dash of hot sauce or crushed red pepper flakes to the egg mixture. Adjust the seasonings according to your taste preferences.

  • Serve with Sides: This casserole is a complete meal in itself, but you can always serve it with some fresh fruit salad, yogurt, or a side of crispy bacon to round out the breakfast spread.

There you have it! An irresistible Christmas morning breakfast casserole that will delight your family and make your holiday morning extra special. Make sure to give this recipe a try and let the warm, comforting aromas fill your home with holiday cheer. Merry Christmas and happy cooking!
