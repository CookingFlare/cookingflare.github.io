Tasty Paleo Beef and Asparagus Stir-Fry


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy stir-fry recipe that’s perfect for a paleo diet? Look no further! This Tasty Paleo Beef and Asparagus Stir-Fry is not only flavorful and satisfying, but it’s also packed with nutritious ingredients that will leave you feeling energized and satisfied. With a perfect balance of protein, healthy fats, and fresh vegetables, this stir-fry is a fantastic addition to your paleo recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 bunch of fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 3 tablespoons of coconut aminos
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of arrowroot powder
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Start by slicing the flank steak into thin strips, making sure to cut against the grain for maximum tenderness. Trim the asparagus and cut it into bite-sized pieces. Slice the red and yellow bell peppers into thin strips, mince the garlic, and grate the fresh ginger.

Step 2: Marinate the Beef

In a bowl, combine the sliced flank steak with the minced garlic, grated ginger, coconut aminos, and sesame oil. Toss the steak until it’s evenly coated with the marinade, then cover the bowl and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.

Step 3: Cook the Asparagus and Bell Peppers

Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add a splash of sesame oil. Once the oil is hot, add the asparagus and bell peppers to the pan. Stir-fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then transfer the cooked vegetables to a plate and set them aside.

Step 4: Cook the Beef

In the same skillet or wok, add the marinated beef and cook it for 2-3 minutes, or until it is browned and cooked to your desired level of doneness. In a small bowl, mix the arrowroot powder with a tablespoon of water to create a slurry, then pour it over the beef. Stir the beef and slurry together until the sauce thickens, then add the cooked asparagus and bell peppers back into the pan. Toss everything together until the vegetables and beef are evenly coated with the sauce.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Transfer the Tasty Paleo Beef and Asparagus Stir-Fry to a serving dish and garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds if desired. This flavorful and nutritious stir-fry is best enjoyed hot, and it pairs perfectly with cauliflower rice or a fresh green salad.

Final Thoughts

This Tasty Paleo Beef and Asparagus Stir-Fry is a wonderful option for anyone following a paleo diet or simply looking for a wholesome and satisfying meal. With its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and nutrient-dense ingredients, it’s a dish that’s sure to become a regular in your recipe rotation. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or a group of friends, this stir-fry is a crowd-pleaser that showcases the beauty of paleo-friendly cooking.

So next time you’re craving a hearty and flavorful meal, give this Tasty Paleo Beef and Asparagus Stir-Fry a try. It’s a delightful combination of tender beef, crisp asparagus, and sweet bell peppers, all brought together with a savory and umami-rich sauce. Embrace the simplicity and wholesomeness of paleo cooking with this delicious stir-fry recipe!
