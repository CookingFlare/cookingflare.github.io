Vegan Peanut Butter Cup Recipe with a Fudge Brownie Crust


Who doesn’t love the combination of peanut butter and chocolate? It’s a match made in dessert heaven! And if you’re a fan of both flavors, you’re in for a treat with this Vegan Peanut Butter Cup Recipe with a Fudge Brownie Crust. This indulgent dessert is not only delicious but also entirely plant-based, making it suitable for vegans and those with dietary restrictions. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and learn how to make these heavenly treats!

Ingredients

For the Fudge Brownie Crust:

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of melted vegan butter
  • 1/4 cup of unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the Peanut Butter Filling:

  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup of melted vegan butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Ganache Topping:

  • 1 cup of vegan chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup of coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch (23 cm) round baking pan and set it aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and salt for the fudge brownie crust.

  3. Add the melted vegan butter, unsweetened applesauce, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully combined.

  4. Transfer the brownie batter into the greased baking pan and spread it evenly to form the crust. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few crumbs. Allow the crust to cool completely.

  5. In another mixing bowl, combine the creamy peanut butter, powdered sugar, melted vegan butter, and vanilla extract. Mix until the ingredients are well incorporated and the mixture is smooth.

  6. Once the brownie crust has cooled, spread the peanut butter filling evenly over the crust.

  7. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the vegan chocolate chips and coconut milk for the chocolate ganache topping. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is fully melted and smooth.

  8. Pour the melted chocolate ganache over the peanut butter filling, spreading it evenly to cover the entire surface.

  9. Place the pan in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or until the chocolate ganache is set.

  10. Once the vegan peanut butter cups are set, remove them from the refrigerator and cut them into small squares or desired shapes.

  11. Serve and enjoy your Vegan Peanut Butter Cup with a Fudge Brownie Crust!

Conclusion

This Vegan Peanut Butter Cup Recipe with a Fudge Brownie Crust is a perfect combination of rich flavors and textures. The fudge brownie crust adds a decadent touch to the creamy peanut butter filling, while the chocolate ganache topping takes it to the next level of indulgence. Whether you’re a vegan or not, these sweet treats are bound to satisfy your dessert cravings. So give this recipe a try and let your taste buds experience the blissful harmony of peanut butter and chocolate in a vegan-friendly way!
