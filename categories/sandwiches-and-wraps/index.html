The Classic Club Wrap: A Portable Delight

Are you tired of the same old sandwiches for lunch? Looking for a quick and easy meal that you can enjoy on the go? Look no further than the Classic Club Wrap! Packed with flavor and conveniently portable, this wrap is sure to become your new favorite lunchtime companion. Ingredients To make this Classic Club Wrap, you will need the following ingredients: 1 large tortilla wrap 4 slices of cooked bacon 2 slices of roasted turkey breast 2 slices of ham 1/4 cup of shredded lettuce 1/4 cup of diced tomatoes 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Lay the tortilla wrap flat on a clean surface. Spread the mayonnaise evenly over the entire surface of the wrap. Place the bacon slices on top of the mayonnaise, followed by the turkey and ham slices. Sprinkle the shredded lettuce and diced tomatoes over the meats, ensuring an even distribution. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Starting from one end, tightly roll the wrap, ensuring that all the ingredients are securely enclosed. Slice the wrap in half diagonally, creating two halves. Serve immediately or wrap in foil for later enjoyment. Tips and Variations For added flavor, you can spread a thin layer of mustard or ranch dressing on the wrap before adding the mayonnaise. Feel free to customize your Classic Club Wrap with additional ingredients such as avocado slices, cheese, or pickles. If you prefer a warm wrap, you can heat the tortilla in a dry skillet for a few seconds on each side before assembling the ingredients. To make this recipe gluten-free, simply substitute the tortilla wrap with a gluten-free alternative. Why Choose The Classic Club Wrap?
