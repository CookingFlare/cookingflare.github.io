Classic Mexican Chicken and Corn Soup Recipe


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful soup that captures the essence of Mexican cuisine, look no further than this classic Mexican chicken and corn soup recipe. Packed with vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, this soup is a perfect balance of hearty and refreshing, making it a delightful addition to any meal.

Ingredients

To create this delightful soup, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of corn kernels, drained
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Avocado slices, for serving
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by cooking the chicken breasts. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a gentle simmer. Add the chicken breasts and let them cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until they are fully cooked. Once done, remove the chicken from the broth, shred it using two forks, and set it aside.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: In the same pot, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they turn translucent and aromatic.

  3. Add the Spices and Vegetables: Once the onions and garlic are fragrant, sprinkle in the cumin, chili powder, and paprika. Stir the spices for a minute to release their flavors. Then, add the drained corn, diced tomatoes, and green chilies. Stir the mixture well to combine the ingredients.

  4. Simmer the Soup: Pour the chicken broth (used for cooking the chicken) into the pot with the vegetable mixture. Bring the soup to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Finish the Soup: After the soup has simmered, return the shredded chicken to the pot. Add the chopped cilantro and season the soup with salt and pepper according to your taste. Let the soup simmer for a few more minutes to ensure the chicken is heated through and the flavors are well incorporated.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the soup into bowls and serve with lime wedges, avocado slices, and a side of tortilla chips. The lime wedges add a bright citrusy kick, while the creamy avocado slices complement the soup’s flavors. The tortilla chips provide a satisfying crunch, creating a delightful eating experience.

Conclusion

This classic Mexican chicken and corn soup is a celebration of vibrant flavors and heartwarming comfort. Whether you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine or simply seeking a nourishing and delicious meal, this soup is sure to impress. Its blend of tender chicken, sweet corn, aromatic spices, and zesty toppings creates a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. So, gather your ingredients and immerse yourself in the rich and soulful experience of preparing and savoring this delightful Mexican-inspired soup.
