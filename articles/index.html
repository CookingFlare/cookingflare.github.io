Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee Recipe: A French-Inspired Dish

French cuisine is renowned for its rich flavors, intricate preparations, and exquisite presentation. Today, we’re going to bring a taste of France to your table with a classic dish that’s both elegant and effortless: Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee. This heavenly recipe is a perfect blend of tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices, all cooked to perfection in the Instant Pot. Ingredients 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces 2 tablespoons butter 1 medium onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup chicken broth 1 cup heavy cream 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper, to taste Fresh parsley, chopped (optional) Instructions Press the “Saute” button on the Instant Pot and melt the butter. Add the diced onion and cook until it’s translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they’re browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside. Add the flour to the pot and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. The mixture should form a smooth paste. Gradually add the chicken broth and heavy cream, whisking continuously to prevent lumps. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until it thickens slightly. Add the thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine. Return the chicken to the pot and stir to coat with the sauce. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to “Sealing”. Press the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” button and set the cooking time to 10 minutes at high pressure. When the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure. Open the lid and check the chicken for tenderness. If it’s not cooked to your liking, close the lid and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve the Chicken Fricassee hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired. Tips and Variations For a richer sauce, use 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream or add 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. To add some color and texture, stir in 1 cup of frozen peas during the last 2 minutes of cooking. For a spicy kick, add 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes to the sauce. This recipe freezes well, so feel free to make a batch and store it in the freezer for up to 3 months. Simply thaw and reheat when needed. Conclusion Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee is a show-stopping dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends. With its tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices, it’s a perfect representation of French cuisine. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make and requires minimal effort, thanks to the Instant Pot. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and experience the magic of French cooking in the comfort of your own home. Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo Recipes

Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal to satisfy your cravings? Look no further than these quick and easy Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo recipes! With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can whip up a creamy and flavorful dish in no time. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, these recipes are sure to impress. Recipe 1: Classic Chicken Alfredo Ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup chicken broth 1 cup heavy cream 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese Salt and pepper to taste 8 ounces fettuccine pasta, cooked Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then add them to the Instant Pot. Brown the chicken on both sides. Pour in the chicken broth and secure the lid. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, use the quick release method to release the pressure. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and set aside. Switch the Instant Pot to sauté mode again and pour in the heavy cream. Bring it to a simmer. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Slice the cooked chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces and add them back to the Instant Pot. Stir to coat the chicken with the sauce. Serve the creamy chicken Alfredo over a bed of cooked fettuccine pasta. Garnish with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired. Recipe 2: Spinach and Mushroom Chicken Alfredo Ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup chicken broth 1 cup heavy cream 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese Salt and pepper to taste 8 ounces fettuccine pasta, cooked 2 cups fresh spinach 1 cup sliced mushrooms Instructions: Follow the same instructions for browning the chicken breasts as in Recipe 1. Add the minced garlic, spinach, and mushrooms to the Instant Pot after removing the chicken breasts. Sauté until the spinach has wilted and the mushrooms have softened. Proceed with the remaining steps from Recipe 1, starting with pouring in the chicken broth. Once the sauce is ready, add the cooked chicken, spinach, and mushrooms back to the Instant Pot. Stir to combine everything well. Serve the flavorful spinach and mushroom chicken Alfredo over cooked fettuccine pasta. Sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese for an extra burst of flavor. Recipe 3: Sun-Dried Tomato and Basil Chicken Alfredo Ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup chicken broth 1 cup heavy cream 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese Salt and pepper to taste 8 ounces fettuccine pasta, cooked 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped Instructions: Prepare the chicken breasts as directed in Recipe 1. After sautéing the garlic, add the sun-dried tomatoes and fresh basil to the Instant Pot. Sauté for a minute to release their flavors. Continue with the remaining steps from Recipe 1, starting with pouring in the chicken broth. Once the sauce is ready, add the cooked chicken, sun-dried tomatoes, and basil back to the Instant Pot. Stir well to incorporate all the ingredients. Serve the aromatic sun-dried tomato and basil chicken Alfredo over cooked fettuccine pasta. Top with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and fresh basil for a vibrant finish. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can enjoy a homemade Alfredo dish without spending hours in the kitchen. Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Casserole Recipes

Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free weeknight dinner? Look no further than these quick and easy Instant Pot chicken and rice casserole recipes. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have a wholesome meal on the table in no time. Whether you’re a seasoned Instant Pot user or a newbie, these recipes are sure to impress. Recipe 1: Lemon Herb Chicken and Rice Casserole Ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 cup long-grain white rice 1 ½ cups chicken broth 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon lemon zest 2 tablespoons lemon juice 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1 teaspoon dried rosemary Salt and pepper, to taste Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish Instructions: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the chicken breasts to the pot and cook until browned on both sides, about 3 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside. Add the minced garlic to the pot and sauté for 1 minute. Add the rice, chicken broth, lemon zest, lemon juice, dried thyme, and dried rosemary to the pot. Stir well to combine. Place the chicken breasts on top of the rice mixture. Close the Instant Pot lid, set the valve to sealing mode, and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure. Carefully remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Stir the shredded chicken back into the rice mixture. Serve the casserole hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley. Recipe 2: Cheesy Broccoli Chicken and Rice Casserole Ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs 1 cup long-grain white rice 1 ½ cups chicken broth 2 tablespoons butter 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 cups chopped broccoli florets 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions: Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and melt the butter. Add the chicken thighs to the pot and cook until browned on both sides, about 3 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside. Add the minced garlic to the pot and sauté for 1 minute. Add the rice, chicken broth, and chopped broccoli to the pot. Stir well to combine. Place the chicken thighs on top of the rice mixture. Close the Instant Pot lid, set the valve to sealing mode, and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure. Remove the chicken thighs from the pot and shred them using two forks. Stir the shredded chicken back into the rice mixture. Add the shredded cheddar cheese and grated Parmesan cheese to the pot. Stir until the cheese has melted and everything is well combined. Serve the casserole hot. Conclusion These quick and easy Instant Pot chicken and rice casserole recipes are perfect for busy weeknights when you want a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can have a delicious and wholesome dinner on the table in no time. So grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get cooking! ...
