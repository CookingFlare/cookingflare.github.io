Picnic Perfection: 10 Refreshing Salad Recipes for Sunny Days


Summer is the perfect time for outdoor picnics and get-togethers with family and friends. A refreshing salad can be the star of your picnic table, and these 10 recipes will keep your guests coming back for more. From fruity to tangy to vegetable-packed, there’s a salad here for every taste and dietary preference.

1. Strawberry Spinach Salad

This delightful salad combines sweet, juicy strawberries, fresh spinach leaves, and a zesty lemon-poppyseed dressing. Topped with crunchy pecans for added texture, it’s sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • Lemon-Poppyseed Dressing (see below)

Lemon-Poppyseed Dressing

  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon poppy seeds
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

2. Greek Salad

A classic favorite, Greek salad boasts a medley of fresh tomatoes, cucumbers, red onion, and Kalamata olives. Topped with crumbled feta cheese and a simple olive oil and vinegar dressing, it’s refreshing and full of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cucumbers, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, patted dry
  • 4 ounces crumbled feta cheese
  • Greek Salad Dressing (see below)

Greek Salad Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

3. Cucumber Watermelon Salad

A new take on the familiar cucumber and tomato salad, this version replaces the tomatoes with juicy watermelon. Mint, feta, and a tangy lime dressing create a delightful flavor combination.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups seedless watermelon, cubed
  • 2 cups sliced cucumbers
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • Lime Vinaigrette (see below)

Lime Vinaigrette

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

4. Grilled Corn and Black Bean Salad

This hearty salad combines grilled corn with black beans, red bell pepper, red onion, and a tangy lime-cilantro dressing. The perfect marriage of flavors, it’s a delicious vegetarian option.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups grilled corn kernels
  • 2 cups black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Lime-Cilantro Dressing (see below)

Lime-Cilantro Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

5. Blueberry Arugula Salad

A slightly sweet, slightly tangy salad, blueberries and arugula are a match made in heaven. Pecans and goat cheese make it a satisfying and flavorful dish.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups arugula
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

6. Italian Chopped Salad

A glorious mix of romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, and Italian seasoning, this salad is served with a garlic vinaigrette for extra flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Garlic Vinaigrette (see below)

Garlic Vinaigrette

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

7. Citrus Avocado Salad

The perfect refreshing salad for summer, citrus avocado salad features creamy avocado, oranges, grapefruit, and mint, all topped with a citrus that is simply divine.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, cubed
  • 1 orange, segmented
  • 1 grapefruit, segmented
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • Citrus Dressing (see below)

Citrus Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed grapefruit juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

8. Thai Coleslaw

A unique twist on traditional coleslaw, this Thai version features sweet potatoes, mango, cabbage, and a tangy lime-peanut dressing. It’s a delightful one-plate meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded sweet potato
  • 1 cup diced mango
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • Thai Lime-Peanut Dressing (see below)

Thai Lime-Peanut Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1/4 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 red chili pepper, seeded and minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

9. Watercress and Radish Salad

Bold and crunchy, this salad combines watercress, radishes, cucumber, and feta cheese, all topped with a simple apple cider vinegar dressing. It’s a delightful, peppery salad.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups watercress
  • 2 cups sliced radishes
  • 2 cups sliced cucumbers
  • 4 ounces crumbled feta cheese
  • Apple Cider Vinegar Dressing (see below)

Apple Cider Vinegar Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

10. Mango and Avocado Salad

A beautiful and flavor-packed salad, mango and avocado are paired with fresh mint and baby spinach for a refreshing, tropical-inspired dish. It’s perfect for a warm, sunny picnic day.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 2 ripe avocados, cubed
  • 2 cups diced mango
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • Avocado-Lime Dressing (see below)

Avocado-Lime Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 ripe avocado, peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

With these 10 refreshing salad recipes, you’ll be the talk of the picnic. Light, flavorful, and packed with nutrition, they’ll become your go-to dishes for sunny days. Enjoy, and happy picnicking!
