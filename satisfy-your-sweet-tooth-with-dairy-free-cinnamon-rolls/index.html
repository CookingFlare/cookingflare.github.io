Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Dairy-Free Cinnamon Rolls


Who doesn’t love the warm, gooey goodness of a freshly baked cinnamon roll? The smell alone is enough to make your mouth water. But what if you’re lactose intolerant or following a dairy-free diet? Fear not! I have the perfect recipe for you to satisfy your sweet tooth without any dairy. These dairy-free cinnamon rolls are just as delicious and indulgent as their traditional counterparts, but without the lactose. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup warm water
  • ¼ cup dairy-free milk (such as almond, coconut, or soy milk)
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil

For the filling:

  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup dairy-free butter, melted

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons dairy-free milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well.

  2. In a separate bowl, combine the warm water, dairy-free milk, and vegetable oil. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with a clean kitchen towel. Allow it to rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  5. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare a baking dish by greasing it with dairy-free butter or lining it with parchment paper.

  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer it to a lightly floured surface and roll it out into a rectangle, about ¼ inch thick.

  7. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and cinnamon for the filling. Brush the melted dairy-free butter onto the rolled-out dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over the buttered dough.

  8. Starting from one of the long sides, tightly roll the dough into a log. Use a sharp knife to cut the log into 12 equal slices.

  9. Place the cinnamon rolls in the prepared baking dish, spacing them evenly. Cover the dish with the kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes.

  10. Bake the cinnamon rolls in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  11. While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, dairy-free milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  12. Once the cinnamon rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the glaze over the warm rolls and serve.

Now it’s time to dig in and enjoy these dairy-free cinnamon rolls! The soft, fluffy dough, sweet cinnamon filling, and creamy glaze will surely satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether you’re lactose intolerant or simply looking for a dairy-free treat, these cinnamon rolls are a perfect choice. So go ahead, indulge yourself and savor every bite. Happy baking!
