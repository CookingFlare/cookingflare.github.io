1. Classic Coleslaw: The backbone of our coleslaw repertoire, this recipe combines cabbage and carrots in a creamy and tangy dressing for a truly timeless taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small head of green cabbage, shredded
  • 1 medium carrot, shredded
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup sweet pickle juice
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon celery seeds
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage and carrot.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, pickle juice, sugar, lemon juice, celery seeds, salt, and pepper until well incorporated.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage and carrot mixture, and toss until the vegetables are evenly coated.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.

2. Spicy Mango Slaw: For a refreshing twist on a traditional coleslaw, whip up this Spicy Mango Slaw that balances sweet, spicy, and tangy flavors beautifully.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small red cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 mango, peeled and diced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced red cabbage, diced mango, and thinly sliced red onion.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together lime juice, honey, chili powder, ground cumin, olive oil, salt, and pepper until emulsified.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage, mango, and onion mixture, and toss to combine.
  4. Allow the slaw to marinate for at least 30 minutes before serving to develop its full flavor.

3. Kale and Brussels Sprout Slaw: Dive headfirst into the superfood world with this Kale and Brussels Sprout Slaw, packed with fiber, protein, and vitamins.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of lacinato kale, thinly sliced
  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, thinly shredded
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced kale and shredded Brussels sprouts.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together olive oil, apple cider vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper until well-emulsified.
  3. Pour the dressing over the kale and Brussels sprouts, and toss until the veggies are evenly coated.
  4. Finish the slaw with a sprinkle of pumpkin seeds and dried cranberries before serving.
