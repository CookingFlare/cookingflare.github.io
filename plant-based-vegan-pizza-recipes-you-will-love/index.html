Plant-Based Vegan Pizza Recipes You Will Love


Pizza is one of the most beloved foods worldwide, and it’s no surprise why. Its versatility, flavor, and ability to bring people together make it an all-time favorite for casual gatherings, celebrations, and weeknight dinners. However, if you’re following a plant-based vegan lifestyle, you might think that enjoying pizza is no longer an option. Well, think again! This guide is packed with delicious, plant-based vegan pizza recipes that will blow your mind and satisfy your taste buds.

Whether you’re a long-time vegan, someone who wants to add more plant-based meals to their diet, or just looking for a fun and delicious way to enjoy pizza without the dairy or meat, these recipes have something for everyone. From classic flavors to creative twists, we’ve got a wide variety of plant-based pizza ideas to suit all tastes.

Why Choose Plant-Based Vegan Pizza?

Before we dive into the recipes, let’s take a moment to talk about why plant-based vegan pizza is a great choice.

  1. Health Benefits: Plant-based pizzas are rich in vegetables, whole grains, and plant proteins, making them more nutrient-dense than traditional pizzas. They provide essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which are important for overall health and well-being. A diet rich in plant-based foods has been linked to lower risks of heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers.

  2. Environmentally Friendly: Plant-based diets have a lower environmental impact than diets rich in animal products. By choosing plant-based pizza, you’re making a conscious decision to reduce your carbon footprint and support sustainable farming practices.

  3. Dietary Considerations: Plant-based vegan pizzas are suitable for those who follow a vegan, vegetarian, or dairy-free lifestyle. They also accommodate gluten-free, soy-free, and nut-free diets when necessary, making them a great option for individuals with food allergies or sensitivities.

  4. Delicious Flavors: Contrary to the misconception that vegan food is bland, plant-based pizzas are bursting with flavor. From fresh vegetables and herbs to creative dairy-free cheese options, vegan pizzas are just as indulgent as their traditional counterparts.

The Essential Ingredients for Vegan Pizza

Before you start creating your own plant-based pizza masterpieces, it’s important to know the essential ingredients that make vegan pizza possible:

  • Pizza Dough: While many store-bought pizza doughs are already vegan, it’s also easy to make your own. The basic ingredients for pizza dough include flour, yeast, water, olive oil, and a pinch of salt. Some recipes may include sugar to help activate the yeast.

  • Vegan Cheese: Vegan cheese has come a long way in recent years. There are many different varieties available, from cashew-based cheeses to coconut oil-based mozzarella alternatives. For a classic pizza taste, look for dairy-free mozzarella-style cheeses that melt well.

  • Sauce: The traditional pizza sauce is typically made with crushed tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and herbs like oregano and basil. You can also experiment with pesto, hummus, or even olive tapenade for a unique twist.

  • Toppings: This is where the fun begins! Vegan pizza toppings are limitless. You can go for the classic veggies like mushrooms, onions, peppers, and spinach, or get more creative with ingredients like artichokes, sun-dried tomatoes, olives, and even vegan sausage or tempeh for added protein.

Now, let’s get into some delicious plant-based vegan pizza recipes that are sure to become your new favorites.

1. Classic Margherita Vegan Pizza

A simple yet flavorful pizza, the Margherita is a classic for a reason. This plant-based version swaps traditional mozzarella with a creamy, dairy-free alternative while retaining the essential tomato and basil flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 1/2 cups vegan mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you’re using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it heats.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the vegan mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Place the pizza in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese has melted.
  6. Once the pizza is baked, remove it from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Slice and enjoy your delicious Margherita vegan pizza!

2. Mediterranean Veggie Pizza

This Mediterranean-inspired vegan pizza is loaded with fresh vegetables, olives, and a tangy hummus base for a burst of flavor. It’s the perfect combination of savory and earthy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup artichoke hearts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/4 cup vegan feta cheese (optional)
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of hummus on the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Top the pizza with cherry tomatoes, olives, red onion, artichoke hearts, and spinach.
  5. Sprinkle with vegan feta cheese, if using.
  6. Bake the pizza in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the toppings are slightly roasted.
  7. Once the pizza is done, drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Slice and serve your Mediterranean veggie pizza with a side of fresh greens for a complete meal.

3. BBQ Veggie and Tempeh Pizza

For a smoky and savory twist, this BBQ veggie and tempeh pizza is packed with protein and flavor. The BBQ sauce adds a bold, tangy kick that pairs perfectly with the grilled vegetables and tempeh.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tempeh, sliced thinly
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup vegan cheddar cheese (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of BBQ sauce over the dough.
  4. Arrange the tempeh, bell pepper, onion, and corn on top of the sauce.
  5. If desired, sprinkle with vegan cheddar cheese for extra flavor.
  6. Bake the pizza in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the toppings are slightly caramelized.
  7. Once baked, remove from the oven and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Slice and serve your BBQ veggie and tempeh pizza.

4. Pesto and Roasted Veggie Pizza

This pizza is loaded with roasted vegetables and a flavorful pesto sauce, which adds a rich and aromatic taste to the dish. You can customize the vegetables based on what you have on hand, but roasted zucchini, bell peppers, and mushrooms work especially well.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup pesto sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts (optional)
  • Vegan parmesan cheese, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of pesto sauce over the dough.
  4. Arrange the zucchini, bell pepper, mushrooms, and red onion on top.
  5. Optionally, sprinkle with pine nuts for added texture.
  6. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the vegetables are roasted.
  7. Once the pizza is ready, garnish with vegan parmesan cheese and serve.

5. Spicy Mexican-Inspired Pizza

If you love a little heat, this Mexican-inspired pizza is the perfect way to satisfy your cravings. Topped with spicy salsa, black beans, corn, and avocado, it’s a fusion of flavors that’s sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup salsa (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • Lime wedges for serving
  • Vegan sour cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of salsa over the dough.
  4. Top the pizza with black beans, corn, and red onion.
  5. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the toppings are slightly roasted.
  6. Once the pizza is done, remove from the oven and top with fresh cilantro and avocado slices.
  7. Serve with lime wedges and a dollop of vegan sour cream for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

These plant-based vegan pizza recipes prove that you don’t need to sacrifice flavor or creativity when choosing a vegan lifestyle. With a variety of fresh, wholesome ingredients and plenty of options for cheese and toppings, vegan pizza can be just as indulgent and satisfying as the traditional version. Whether you’re enjoying a classic Margherita, a Mediterranean veggie pizza, or a BBQ tempeh pizza, each of these recipes will show you how easy and delicious plant-based pizzas can be. So, gather your ingredients, preheat the oven, and get ready to enjoy a homemade, vegan pizza that will leave you coming back for more.
