Irresistible Slow Cooker Desserts for Sweet Tooths


Are you a dessert lover with a serious sweet tooth? Do you find yourself craving delectable treats that are not only delicious but also easy to make? Look no further! In this post, we will explore some irresistible slow cooker desserts that will satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. Get ready to indulge in a world of mouthwatering flavors and heavenly aromas.

Slow Cooker Chocolate Lava Cake

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 2 hours | Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Stir until well combined.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Grease the inside of your slow cooker with cooking spray.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased slow cooker.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix the boiling water with 1/4 cup of granulated sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  7. Carefully pour the water-sugar mixture over the batter in the slow cooker.
  8. Cover and cook on high for 2 hours, or until the cake is set around the edges and slightly gooey in the center.
  9. Serve warm with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Slow Cooker Caramel Apple Crisp

Preparation time: 20 minutes | Cooking time: 4 hours | Serves: 8

Ingredients:

  • 8 Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup caramel sauce

Instructions:

  1. Place the sliced apples in the slow cooker.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, oats, brown sugar, melted butter, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Mix until crumbly.
  3. Sprinkle the crumb mixture over the apples in the slow cooker.
  4. Drizzle the caramel sauce over the top.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden and crisp.
  6. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a drizzle of additional caramel sauce.

Slow Cooker Bread Pudding

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 4 hours | Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups day-old bread, cut into cubes
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place the bread cubes in the slow cooker.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, granulated sugar, brown sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  3. Pour the mixture over the bread cubes in the slow cooker.
  4. If desired, sprinkle the raisins over the top.
  5. Gently press the bread cubes down to ensure they are fully soaked in the liquid.
  6. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours, or until the bread pudding is set and golden brown on top.
  7. Serve warm with a dusting of powdered sugar or a drizzle of warm caramel sauce.

Conclusion

These irresistible slow cooker desserts are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without spending hours in the kitchen. With just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of time, you can create mouthwatering treats that will impress family and friends. So go ahead, indulge in the goodness of these desserts and let your taste buds dance with delight. Happy slow cooking!
