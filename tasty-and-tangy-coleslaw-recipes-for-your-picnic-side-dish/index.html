Tasty and Tangy Coleslaw Recipes for Your Picnic Side Dish


When it comes to picnics, barbecues, or any outdoor gathering, having the perfect side dish can take your meal to the next level. Coleslaw, with its crunchy texture and zesty flavor, is a classic choice that complements a wide range of main dishes. Whether you prefer a creamy coleslaw or a tangy vinaigrette-based version, there’s a recipe to suit every taste. In this post, we’ll explore some delicious and refreshing coleslaw recipes that will elevate your picnic experience.

Classic Creamy Coleslaw

Ingredients:

  • 1 small head of cabbage, finely shredded
  • 2 carrots, grated
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, white vinegar, Dijon mustard, and sugar until well combined.
  2. Add the shredded cabbage and grated carrots to the bowl, and toss until the vegetables are evenly coated with the dressing.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

This classic creamy coleslaw is a crowd-pleaser, with its rich and indulgent flavor. The combination of mayonnaise, vinegar, and mustard creates a creamy dressing that perfectly coats the crisp cabbage and carrots. It’s a versatile side dish that pairs well with grilled meats, sandwiches, or as a topping for tacos.

Tangy Apple Cider Vinegar Coleslaw

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 head of red cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 head of green cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon celery seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the apple cider vinegar, olive oil, honey, and celery seeds until well combined.
  2. Add the sliced cabbages, julienned carrot, and sliced red onion to the bowl, and toss until the vegetables are coated with the tangy dressing.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Chill the coleslaw in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to develop.

This tangy apple cider vinegar coleslaw offers a refreshing and vibrant twist on the traditional recipe. The combination of crisp cabbage, sweet carrot, and zesty red onion, dressed in a tangy apple cider vinegar and honey vinaigrette, creates a light and flavorful side dish that pairs perfectly with grilled fish, pulled pork, or even as a topping for burgers.

Spicy Jalapeno Coleslaw

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 head of green cabbage, shredded
  • 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, cumin, and chili powder until smooth.
  2. Add the shredded cabbage, chopped jalapenos, and sliced red bell pepper to the bowl, and toss until the vegetables are coated with the creamy and spicy dressing.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Refrigerate the coleslaw for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

For those who enjoy a bit of heat, this spicy jalapeno coleslaw is the perfect choice. The combination of crunchy cabbage, fiery jalapenos, and creamy dressing with a hint of lime and spices creates a bold and flavorful side dish that pairs excellently with grilled chicken, tacos, or as a topping for spicy pulled pork sandwiches.

Conclusion

Coleslaw is a versatile and refreshing side dish that adds a burst of flavor and texture to any picnic or outdoor meal. Whether you prefer the classic creamy version, a tangy apple cider vinegar twist, or a spicy jalapeno kick, these coleslaw recipes are sure to impress your guests and elevate your picnic experience. Experiment with different variations and find the perfect coleslaw recipe to complement your favorite summer dishes. Enjoy the crunch and tang of these tasty coleslaw recipes at your next picnic!
