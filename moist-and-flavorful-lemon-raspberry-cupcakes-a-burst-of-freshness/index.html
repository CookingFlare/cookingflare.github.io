Moist and Flavorful Lemon Raspberry Cupcakes: A Burst of Freshness


If you’re a fan of citrusy and fruity desserts, then you’re in for a treat! These Moist and Flavorful Lemon Raspberry Cupcakes are a burst of freshness in every bite. With their tangy lemon flavor and the delightful addition of juicy raspberries, these cupcakes are the perfect summer treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.

  5. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing well after each addition.

  7. Add the lemon zest and lemon juice to the batter and mix until well combined.

  8. Gently fold in the fresh raspberries, being careful not to overmix.

  9. Spoon the batter into the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  11. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and allow them to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Optional Lemon Cream Cheese Frosting

If you want to take these cupcakes to the next level, you can top them with a delicious lemon cream cheese frosting. Here’s how you can make it:

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter until smooth and creamy.

  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing well after each addition.

  3. Add the lemon juice and lemon zest to the frosting and mix until well combined.

  4. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, pipe or spread the lemon cream cheese frosting onto each cupcake.

  5. If desired, garnish with a raspberry on top of each cupcake.

Enjoy the Burst of Freshness!

These Moist and Flavorful Lemon Raspberry Cupcakes are the perfect combination of tangy and sweet, with a burst of freshness from the raspberries. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a summery treat, these cupcakes are sure to impress. So go ahead, indulge in a burst of flavor and enjoy every bite!

Remember to share this recipe with your friends and family so they can also experience the deliciousness of these Moist and Flavorful Lemon Raspberry Cupcakes. Happy baking!
