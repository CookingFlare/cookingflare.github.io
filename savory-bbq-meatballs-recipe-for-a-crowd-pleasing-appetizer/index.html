Savory BBQ Meatballs Recipe for a Crowd-Pleasing Appetizer


If you’re looking for a delicious and crowd-pleasing appetizer for your next gathering, these Savory BBQ Meatballs are sure to hit the spot! Made with a flavorful blend of ground meat, breadcrumbs, and savory seasonings, these meatballs are then coated in a tangy and smoky BBQ sauce that will leave your guests wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a party, game night, or simply craving a tasty snack, these meatballs are the perfect choice. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the BBQ sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for extra heat)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, chopped onion, minced garlic, parsley, Parmesan cheese, beaten egg, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Shape the meat mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Leave a little space between each meatball to ensure even cooking.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are cooked through and browned on the outside. Remove from the oven and set aside.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the BBQ sauce. In a medium saucepan, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper (if using). Stir well to combine.

  6. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Once the meatballs are cooked, transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Pour the BBQ sauce over the meatballs and gently toss until they are evenly coated.

  8. To serve, you can skewer the meatballs with toothpicks for easy grabbing or place them in a serving dish with extra sauce on the side for dipping. These meatballs are best enjoyed hot and fresh out of the oven!

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize the meatball mixture by using a combination of different ground meats, such as beef, pork, or even turkey, for a unique flavor profile.
  • If you prefer a spicier BBQ sauce, feel free to add more cayenne pepper or even a dash of hot sauce to amp up the heat.
  • These meatballs can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. Simply reheat them in the oven before serving.
  • Serve these meatballs as an appetizer, or turn them into a main course by serving them over cooked rice or pasta.

So there you have it – a delicious and crowd-pleasing recipe for Savory BBQ Meatballs that will surely impress your guests. The combination of the juicy meatballs and tangy BBQ sauce is simply irresistible. Give this recipe a try and watch as these meatballs disappear in no time. Enjoy!
