Savory Thanksgiving Gravy Variations


Thanksgiving dinner is never complete without a rich, flavorful gravy to drizzle over your turkey, mashed potatoes, and stuffing. While traditional gravy is a classic favorite, why not spice things up this year with some creative and savory variations? In this post, we will explore four delicious twists on Thanksgiving gravy that will impress your guests and elevate your holiday feast to the next level.

1. Mushroom and Thyme Gravy

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms (e.g., cremini, shiitake)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they are golden brown and tender.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and stir to create a roux.
  4. Slowly pour in the broth while continuously whisking to avoid lumps.
  5. Add the fresh thyme leaves, salt, and pepper. Simmer until the gravy thickens to your desired consistency.

2. Red Wine and Shallot Gravy

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped shallots and cook until soft and translucent.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the shallots and cook for a minute to remove the raw flour taste.
  4. Slowly pour in the red wine and broth, whisking constantly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Let the gravy simmer until it thickens and the alcohol cooks off.

3. Maple Bacon Gravy

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the crumbled bacon and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the bacon and whisk to combine.
  4. Slowly pour in the broth and maple syrup, stirring constantly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Cook the gravy until it thickens and the flavors meld together.

4. Herb Infused Gravy

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • Assorted fresh herbs (e.g., rosemary, sage, parsley)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add a handful of chopped fresh herbs and sauté briefly to release their flavors.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the herbs and stir to form a roux.
  4. Slowly whisk in the broth, ensuring there are no lumps.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Let the gravy simmer until it thickens and the herbs infuse their aromatic essence.

Experiment with these savory Thanksgiving gravy variations to add a delightful twist to your holiday table. Whether you opt for the earthy richness of mushroom and thyme, the luxurious depth of red wine and shallots, the sweet-savory combination of maple bacon, or the fragrant herb-infused goodness, your guests are sure to be impressed. Elevate your Thanksgiving dinner with these creative and delicious gravy options that will have everyone asking for seconds!

Enjoy the process of creating these flavorful gravies, and may your Thanksgiving celebration be filled with warmth, laughter, and culinary delights!
