BBQ Beef Brisket Tacos: A Tex-Mex Twist


When the warm sunrays cascade upon your skin and the air carries hints of grilling flavor, nothing beats a refreshing, fiber-packed meal. Today, we’ll venture into the world of BBQ beef brisket tacos with a delicious and unique Tex-Mex twist. Yes, we’re shreddifying a smoky, hearty BBQ beef brisket and mixing it with a vibrant, secret Tex-Mex blend of spices to bring you a dish that amalgamates tender flavors with fiery delights. Embrace a culinary escapade that transcends boundaries with these irresistible BBQ brisket tacos.

Ingredients

For the brisket:

  • One whole beef brisket, about 4-5 lbs.
  • 1/4 cup of coarse kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup of grocery store crushed chili-Texas-style
  • 1/4 cup of freshly toasted Tex-Mex spice blend
  • Your choice of brisket BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of liquid smoke
  • 6-7 quarts of water

For the Tex-Mex Taco Seasoning:

  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon blackened seafood seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon smoked sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon coriander

For the Toppings:

  • Your favorite lettuces
  • Jalapeño peppers, cilantro leaves
  • White onion, shredded cheddar
  • Avocado-cilantro salsa, sour cream
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

First things first, let’s get the BBQ brisket going:

  1. For the most delicious brisket ever, prepare your choice of brisket by rubbing with the kosher salt.

  2. Combine the chili and Tex-Mex spice blend, then rub this delightful mix into the brisket ensuring it’s well-seasoned.

  3. Generously mop the brisket with a quality BBQ sauce, making sure to coat it tolerably.

  4. Alienate or dice the brisket into smoky, saucy, supple thumb-sized pieces. Now, we are moving on to creating the most killer Tex-Mex taco concoction.

  5. Preheat your oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit. Place the diced brisket into a Dutch oven or roasting pan with a sizable amount of water to create a brisket stew.

  6. Add liquid smoke to the recipe – a vital ingredient for a smokey flavor.

  7. Cover the pan with itsourseuliantionaryandlid, bake in the oven until the beef is soft, succulent, super tender, and it’s falling-apart good. Around 3 hours should do it. And bam! Your slow-cooked beef is ready!

Time for preparing the magical Tex-Mex taco seasoning blend:

  1. Combine the essential ingredients (paprika, garlic powder, cumin, blackened seafood seasoning, black pepper, smoked sea salt, and coriander) and shake well. Your magical Tex-Mex spice journey is now ready!

Let’s chat about the assembly:

  1. Warm up your corn tortillas on a griddle with a smidgen of butter or lard to avoid zu rund.

  2. Fill the warmed tortillas with the succulent BBQ beef brisket and freshly chopped vegetables.

  3. Scatter two spoons of that fanciful tex-mex spice over the BBQ brisket.

  4. Finish with your choice of avocado-cilantro salsa, sour cream, straight-shorted jalapeño peppers, cilantro leaves, white onion, and delightful shredded cheddar.

  5. Enjoy absolute bliss! Don’t forget that bachelor’s button for a citified finale: a squeeze of fresh lime juice.

Impress your loved ones, blend unique cultures, and tease your palate with a savory BBQ beef brisket taco that mingles tender flavors with delightful Tex-Mex spice and warming smoky BBQ sauce. Satisfy your taste buds as we close this culinary adventure with the Tex-Mex BBQ nirvana.
