Eclairs Inspired Mini Eclairs Cake: All the Goodness with Less Mess


Have you ever wanted to savor the delicious taste of eclairs without all the fuss? Look no further! Today, we’ll be sharing a unique adaptation that combines the best of both worlds – eclairs and cake.

Introducing the mini eclairs cake, an innovative and scrumptious twist on the classic dessert that is bound to elevate your baking game. With fewer messes and less stress, you can create an impressive, show-stopping dessert that rivals even the finest French patisseries.

What You’ll Need:

For the Cake:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk

For the Pastry Cream:

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 7 ounces semi-sweet chocolate
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Directions:

1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and prepare a mini muffin tin by greasing lightly with butter or non-stick spray. Set aside.

2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, butter, eggs, vanilla extract, salt, and milk. Mix well until a smooth batter is formed. Fill each mini muffin cavity about 3/4 of the way with the batter. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Let the cakes cool in the tray for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

3. While your mini eclairs cakes are cooling, create the pastry cream. In a saucepan, combine milk, sugar, cornstarch, and vanilla extract over medium heat. Whisk constantly until the mixture thickens into a pudding-like consistency. Remove from heat and stir in the butter and salt. Pour the hot pastry cream into a mixing bowl and cover with plastic wrap, ensuring the wrap is in contact with the cream to prevent a skin from forming. Let it cool completely.

4. For the chocolate ganache, heat the heavy cream in a small saucepan until it boils. Pour the cream over the semi-sweet chocolate chunks placed in a heatproof bowl. Let it sit for about 2 minutes before stirring until smooth. Add the butter and mix well. Set aside to let it cool and thicken slightly.

5. Once everything is cooled, it’s time to assemble! Using a small pairing knife, create a small hole in the side of each mini eclairs cake. Carefully fill the cavity with the pastry cream, being cautious not to puncture the cake. Drizzle or pipe the cooled ganache over the top of the cakes and serve.

Tips:

  • For an added indulgence, incorporate a layer of whipped cream or fresh fruit in between the pastry cream and ganache.
  • To ensure the perfect ganache consistency, keep a close eye on the heat – it should not boil.
  • Swap out the vanilla extract in the pastry cream for your favorite flavored extract, like almond or lemon, to create unique flavor combinations.

The mini eclairs cake marries the delectable flavors of the classic eclair with the convenience and presentation of a cake, making it perfect for gatherings of any size. Give it a try and impress your friends and family with this delightful fusion of two timeless desserts while minimizing the mess!

Happy baking!
