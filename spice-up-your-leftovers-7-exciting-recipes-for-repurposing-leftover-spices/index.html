Spice Up Your Leftovers: 7 Exciting Recipes for Repurposing Leftover Spices


Welcome to our blog post on repurposing leftover spices! We all know how it feels to have a cabinet full of half-empty spice jars, waiting to be used. Instead of letting them collect dust, why not give your leftovers a new lease on life? In this post, we’ll share seven exciting recipes that will spice up your meals and help you make the most of those neglected spices. Let’s get started!

1. Spiced Potato Wedges

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cut the potatoes into wedges and place them in a large bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the spice mixture over the potato wedges and toss until they are evenly coated.
  5. Spread the wedges out on the baking sheet in a single layer.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the potatoes are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve hot as a delicious side dish or snack!

2. Spicy Roasted Cauliflower

Ingredients:

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the cauliflower florets with olive oil, chili powder, turmeric, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the seasoned cauliflower out on the baking sheet in a single layer.
  4. Roast for 20-25 minutes or until the cauliflower is tender and slightly charred.
  5. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.
  6. Enjoy as a spicy and flavorful side dish or add it to salads and bowls for an extra kick!

3. Moroccan Spiced Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the lentils, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, turmeric, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper to the pot.
  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 20 minutes or until the lentils are cooked through.
  5. Use an immersion blender or a countertop blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro. Enjoy this flavorful and nutritious soup!

4. Spiced Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Whipped cream, for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until hot but not boiling.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cayenne pepper (if using).
  3. Slowly add the cocoa mixture to the hot milk, whisking continuously until well combined.
  4. Continue to heat the hot chocolate until it reaches your desired temperature.
  5. Pour into mugs and top with whipped cream if desired. Sip and savor the warming flavors!

5. Spicy Tomato Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, red pepper flakes, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Taste and adjust the seasonings as needed.
  6. Use the spicy tomato sauce as a base for pasta dishes, pizzas, or as a dipping sauce for breadsticks.

6. Indian Spiced Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 tablespoon ghee or butter
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. Heat ghee or butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add the cumin seeds and cook until they start to sizzle and become fragrant.
  4. Stir in the ground coriander, ground turmeric, ground cardamom, and salt.
  5. Add the rinsed rice to the pan and stir well to coat the grains with the spices.
  6. Pour in 2 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the rice simmer for 15-20 minutes or until cooked through.
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with chopped cilantro. Serve as a flavorful side dish.

7. Spiced Yogurt Dip

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (such as mint or cilantro), for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the plain yogurt, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground turmeric, ground paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Taste and adjust the seasonings to your liking.
  3. Transfer the spiced yogurt dip to a serving bowl.
  4. Garnish with fresh herbs.
  5. Serve with vegetable sticks, pita bread, or as a condiment for grilled meats. Enjoy the creamy and tangy flavors!

That concludes our list of seven exciting recipes for repurposing leftover spices. We hope you find these ideas inspiring and that they help you add a burst of flavor to your meals. Remember, don’t let those spices go to waste – get creative and spice up your leftovers! Happy cooking!
