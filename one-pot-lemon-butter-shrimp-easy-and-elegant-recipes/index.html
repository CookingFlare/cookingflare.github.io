One-Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp: Easy and Elegant Recipes


Are you looking for a delicious and elegant dish that is easy to make and requires minimal cleanup? Look no further than this One-Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp recipe! This dish is bursting with flavor, combining the tangy freshness of lemon with the richness of butter to create a truly mouthwatering experience. Not only is it a treat for your taste buds, but it’s also a feast for the eyes with its vibrant colors and inviting aroma. Let’s dive into how you can recreate this culinary masterpiece in your own kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: Add the shrimp to the skillet in a single layer. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same skillet, pour in the chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  4. Add Butter: Once the liquid is simmering, add the butter to the skillet. Stir continuously until the butter is fully melted and the sauce is smooth and slightly thickened.

  5. Combine Ingredients: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, tossing them in the lemon butter sauce until they are well coated. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle fresh parsley over the top for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the One-Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp hot, either on its own or over a bed of pasta or rice.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a touch of freshness, squeeze extra lemon juice over the dish just before serving.
  • Add a handful of cherry tomatoes or asparagus to the skillet along with the shrimp for a colorful and nutritious twist.
  • Feel free to adjust the amount of red pepper flakes to suit your spice preference.
  • This dish pairs beautifully with a crisp white wine or a light salad.

Final Thoughts

One-Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp is the perfect recipe for those busy weeknights when you want a gourmet meal without the fuss. The combination of zesty lemon and velvety butter elevates simple shrimp into a dish that is both easy to prepare and impressive to serve. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your skillet, and get ready to delight your senses with this flavorful and elegant dish!

With this One-Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp recipe, you can bring a touch of sophistication to your dinner table with minimal effort. Enjoy the burst of flavors and the satisfaction of creating a restaurant-quality dish right in your own kitchen. Feel free to experiment with additional ingredients and make this recipe your own. Happy cooking!
