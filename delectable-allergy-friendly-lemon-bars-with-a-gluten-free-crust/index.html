Delectable Allergy-Friendly Lemon Bars with a Gluten-Free Crust


If you’re looking for a delightful dessert that’s not only delicious but also allergy-friendly, then you’re in for a treat! These delectable lemon bars with a gluten-free crust are the perfect combination of tangy and sweet, all wrapped up in a buttery, crumbly crust that’s completely gluten-free. Whether you’re catering to your own dietary restrictions or hosting guests with various food sensitivities, this recipe is sure to be a hit.

Gluten-Free Crust

Let’s start with the foundation of these irresistible lemon bars – the gluten-free crust. To create the perfect base for our lemony filling, we’ll be using a blend of gluten-free flour, almond flour, and a touch of powdered sugar for sweetness. The almond flour adds a delightful nuttiness to the crust, while the gluten-free flour ensures a tender and crumbly texture that will perfectly complement the zesty lemon filling.

To bring this crust together, simply combine the gluten-free flour, almond flour, powdered sugar, and a pinch of salt in a mixing bowl. Then, add in the cold, cubed butter and work the mixture with your fingertips until it resembles coarse crumbs. Press this mixture into the bottom of a prepared baking pan and bake until golden brown. The result? A perfectly crisp and buttery gluten-free crust that will elevate your lemon bars to the next level.

Luscious Lemon Filling

Now, let’s move on to the star of the show – the luscious lemon filling. Bursting with bright citrus flavor, this filling strikes the perfect balance between tart and sweet. The secret to achieving the ideal texture and flavor lies in the combination of freshly squeezed lemon juice, lemon zest, and just the right amount of sweetness.

To create the filling, whisk together eggs, granulated sugar, freshly squeezed lemon juice, lemon zest, and a touch of gluten-free flour until smooth. Once the gluten-free crust has cooled slightly, pour the lemon filling over the crust and return the pan to the oven. As the bars bake, the filling will set into a velvety, lemony layer that will have your taste buds dancing with delight.

Serving and Storage

Once the lemon bars have cooled to room temperature, it’s time to cut them into perfectly portioned squares and dust them with a light shower of powdered sugar. These allergy-friendly lemon bars are best enjoyed with a hot cup of tea or as a refreshing conclusion to a summertime meal.

If you find yourself with any leftovers (which is a rare occurrence with these crowd-pleasers), simply store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator. The bars will keep well for a few days, but we doubt they’ll last that long once word gets out about their irresistible flavor.

Final Thoughts

With their irresistible combination of zesty lemon filling and buttery gluten-free crust, these allergy-friendly lemon bars are a true delight for the senses. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a sweet treat, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your dessert repertoire. So, grab your apron and get ready to bake up a batch of these delectable lemon bars – your taste buds will thank you!

By incorporating a gluten-free crust and a luscious lemon filling, these allergy-friendly lemon bars offer a delightful treat for those with dietary restrictions. With a perfectly crisp crust and a velvety, tangy filling, these bars are sure to become a favorite among your family and friends. Enjoy the zingy flavor and buttery texture of these delectable lemon bars for a refreshing dessert experience!
