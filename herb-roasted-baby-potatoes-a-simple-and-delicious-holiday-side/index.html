Herb-Roasted Baby Potatoes: A Simple and Delicious Holiday Side


As the holiday season approaches, the aroma of roasting potatoes wafts through the air, evoking memories of warm gatherings and festive feasts. This year, why not elevate your holiday spread with a mouth-watering, herb-roasted baby potato dish that’s sure to impress your guests? In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the simple process of transforming humble potatoes into a show-stopping, flavorful side that’s sure to become a new holiday tradition.

The Perfect Potatoes

For this recipe, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound baby potatoes (about 1-2 inches in diameter)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary leaves
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Preparation

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone mat.

Wash the baby potatoes thoroughly and pat them dry with a paper towel. Cut them in half lengthwise, if needed, to ensure even roasting.

In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, chopped rosemary, thyme, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add the baby potatoes to the bowl and toss them gently to coat them evenly with the herb mixture.

Roasting

Spread the potatoes out in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. If using Parmesan cheese, sprinkle it evenly over the potatoes.

Roast the potatoes in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they’re tender and golden brown, flipping them halfway through the cooking time.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra boost of flavor, toss the potatoes with some chopped onion or shallot before roasting.
  • Substitute other herbs like parsley, sage, or oregano for the rosemary and thyme.
  • Add some diced bell peppers or zucchini to the potatoes for a pop of color and added nutrition.
  • For a crisper exterior, increase the oven temperature to 450°F (230°C) and roast the potatoes for an additional 5-7 minutes.

Presentation

Once the potatoes are roasted, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly. Serve them hot, garnished with additional chopped fresh herbs if desired.

This herb-roasted baby potato dish is the perfect accompaniment to your holiday feast, whether you’re serving turkey, ham, or something more exotic. Its simple, flavorful ingredients and effortless preparation make it a crowd-pleaser, and the best part? You can prepare it ahead of time, allowing you to focus on other dishes or simply enjoy the company of your loved ones.

Conclusion

Herb-roasted baby potatoes are a holiday side dish that’s sure to become a new tradition in your household. With their tender, flavorful flesh and crispy, golden exterior, they’re the perfect complement to any holiday meal. So why not give them a try this year? With this simple, delicious recipe, you’ll be well on your way to creating a memorable holiday feast that your guests will cherish for years to come.
