Comforting Instant Pot Beef Stroganoff for Cozy Evenings


If you’re looking for a hearty and comforting meal to warm you up on a cozy evening, then this Instant Pot Beef Stroganoff recipe is just what you need. This classic dish is a true crowd-pleaser, with tender beef, savory mushrooms, and a creamy sauce that will leave you coming back for seconds. The best part? It’s all made in one pot, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot. Let’s dive into the details of this soul-warming dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 ½ cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 oz egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Beef: Start by setting your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Season the beef with salt and pepper, then sear it in the Instant Pot until browned on all sides. This will help lock in the flavors.

  2. Add Aromatics: Add the chopped onions and minced garlic to the pot, and sauté for a few minutes until they soften and become fragrant.

  3. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, paprika, and thyme. Use a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. This step is crucial for adding depth of flavor to the dish.

  4. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set it to high pressure for 15 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  5. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix together the flour and water to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the beef mixture in the Instant Pot, then switch to sauté mode and let the sauce thicken for a few minutes.

  6. Cook the Noodles: While the sauce thickens, cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions in a separate pot on the stove.

  7. Finish the Dish: Once the sauce has thickened, stir in the sour cream until well combined. Add the sliced mushrooms to the Instant Pot and let them cook for a few minutes until they soften.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the beef stroganoff over the cooked egg noodles, garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra flavor boost, you can add a splash of white wine or brandy when deglazing the pot.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can increase the amount of flour in the slurry.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors will continue to develop, making it even more delicious the next day.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot Beef Stroganoff is the epitome of comfort food, perfect for those chilly evenings when you crave something soul-satisfying. The tender beef, creamy sauce, and earthy mushrooms come together to create a dish that will warm both your body and your soul. Plus, the convenience of making it all in one pot means less time spent on cleanup and more time to enjoy your meal. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to savor the comforting flavors of this classic dish. Cheers to cozy evenings and delicious meals!
