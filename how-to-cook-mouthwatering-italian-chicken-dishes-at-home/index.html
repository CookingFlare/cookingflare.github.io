How to Cook Mouthwatering Italian Chicken Dishes at Home


Welcome to a beautiful journey of Italian chicken dishes! Today, we are going to explore some delectable and mouthwatering recipes that you can easily prepare at home. Impress your family and friends with these scrumptious Italian chicken dishes that will leave them asking for more. Let’s get started!

  1. Chicken Saltimbocca

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 fresh sage leaves
  • 8 slices prosciutto
  • 8 toothpicks
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.

  2. Season chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper.

  3. Place a sage leaf and a slice of prosciutto on each chicken breast.

  4. Pin each chicken breast Closed captioning tightly with a toothpick.

  5. Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium heat; add olive oil, and then chicken breasts.

  6. Cook until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side.

  7. Transfer the skillet to the oven and cook until the chicken is done, about 15 minutes.

  8. Remove toothpicks and serve your Chicken Saltimbocca hot with a side of flair.

  9. Chicken Cacciatore

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups Italian-style canned tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup green olives, pitted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.

  2. Season chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook chicken breasts until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side.

  4. In a baking dish, combine tomatoes, chicken broth, garlic, and parsley.

  5. Add chicken breasts to the baking dish and bake for about 20 minutes.

  6. In the same skillet used for cooking the chicken, cook mushrooms and green olives until golden brown.

  7. Spoon the mushroom mixture over the chicken breasts and let it cook for a few more minutes before serving. Enjoy your Chicken Cacciatore!

  8. Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup Italian-seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 4 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 4 ounces Parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Season chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Prepare a dish of beaten eggs and another dish of flour.
  4. Coat the chicken breasts in the flour and then dip them in the beaten eggs.
  5. In a plastic bag, combine the breadcrumbs and some tasty cheese, then coat the chicken breasts delicately.
  6. Heat a large skillet over medium flame; add enough olive oil to coat bottom.
  7. Brown the chicken on both sides, then stash in a baking dish.
  8. Ladle marinara sauce over the chicken breasts; sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and bake for 20 minutes.
  9. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve hot.
