Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Risotto Recipe: A Creamy and Rich Option


Risotto is a classic Italian dish known for its creamy and rich texture. Traditionally, making risotto involves constant stirring and adding broth gradually. However, with the help of an Instant Pot, you can achieve the same delicious results with much less effort. In this recipe, we’ll be combining tender chicken and earthy mushrooms to create a flavorful and satisfying Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Risotto.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté setting and melt the butter. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onions become translucent, about 3-4 minutes [1].
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms to the Instant Pot and cook until they become tender, about 3-4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste [1].
  3. Stir in the Arborio rice, making sure each grain is coated with the butter and mushroom mixture. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to lightly toast the rice [2].
  4. Pour in the white wine and cook for a minute or two until it has evaporated, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the chicken broth to the Instant Pot and give everything a good stir. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 6 minutes [1].
  6. Once the cooking time is complete, do a quick release of the pressure. Carefully open the lid and stir in the cooked chicken and grated Parmesan cheese. The residual heat will melt the cheese and create a creamy consistency [3].
  7. Taste the risotto and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. If the risotto is too thick, you can add a little more chicken broth to achieve your desired consistency [4].
  8. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Risotto hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations:

  • For a vegetarian version, you can omit the chicken and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables like peas or spinach to the risotto for added flavor and nutrition [1].
  • If you prefer a stronger mushroom flavor, you can use a combination of different mushroom varieties, such as portobello and white mushrooms.
  • To make the risotto even creamier, you can stir in a tablespoon of cream or butter at the end [5].

Now you can enjoy a restaurant-quality Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Risotto in the comfort of your own home. This creamy and rich dish is sure to impress your family and friends. Give it a try and savor the delicious flavors!
