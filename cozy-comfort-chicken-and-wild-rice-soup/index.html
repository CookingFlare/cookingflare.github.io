Cozy Comfort: Chicken and Wild Rice Soup


There is nothing quite like curling up with a steaming bowl of Chicken and Wild Rice Soup on a chilly day, enveloped in warmth and comfort. This hearty, flavorsome soup will soon become a go-to favorite in your kitchen.

Wild Rice SoupIngredients2 tablespoons olive oil1 large onion, chopped1 carrot, chopped3 stalks celery, chopped2 cloves garlic, minced2 cups chicken broth1/3 cup wild rice1 tablespoon poultry seasoning1 teaspoon dried thyme1 teaspoon celery seed1 teaspoon garlic powder1 teaspoon paprikaSalt and pepper to taste4 cups chicken broth, divided2 cups cooked chicken, shredded

Instructions

Begin by preparing your ingredients. Chop your onion, carrot, and celery while the garlic is minced. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, carrot, and celery to the pot, cooking until the vegetables are soft, about 5 minutes.

While that is cooking, prepare your seasonings. In a small bowl, combine the wild rice, poultry seasoning, thyme, celery seed, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. This mix of spices truly brings out the savory and comforting flavors of this soup.

Add the rice mixture to the pot, stirring to combine with the vegetables. Pour in 2 cups of chicken broth and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce heat, and let simmer for about 20 minutes, until the rice is cooked and plump.

During this time, in a separate pot, heat the remaining 4 cups of chicken broth until it comes to a boil. Add your shredded cooked chicken, and allow it to warm through for about 5 minutes.

Once the rice has finished cooking, stir in the chicken and its broth into the pot with the vegetables and rice. Cook for another 10 minutes, letting the flavors meld together while the chicken warms through.

Delicious garnishes for this soup include fresh parsley or thyme, a dollop of sour cream, or crumbled crackers.
