Garlic Parmesan Dinner Rolls: A Flavorful Side Dish


If you’re looking to elevate your dinner table with a delightful side dish that bursts with flavor, look no further than Garlic Parmesan Dinner Rolls. These soft, pillowy rolls infused with the savory goodness of garlic and the richness of Parmesan cheese are sure to be a hit with your family and guests. Whether served alongside a hearty stew, a comforting bowl of soup, or as a standalone snack, these rolls are a versatile and delicious addition to any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a bowl, combine the warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until foamy.

  2. Mix the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, melted butter, minced garlic, Parmesan cheese, salt, and the activated yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.

  3. Knead the Dough: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 10-15 minutes until smooth and elastic.

  4. First Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours until doubled in size.

  5. Shape the Rolls: Punch down the risen dough and divide it into equal-sized portions. Shape each portion into a ball and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  6. Second Rise: Cover the shaped rolls with a damp towel and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes.

  7. Prepare for Baking: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Brush the risen rolls with the beaten egg wash and sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese on top.

  8. Bake: Bake the rolls in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until they are golden brown and sound hollow when tapped.

  9. Garnish and Serve: Once out of the oven, garnish the Garlic Parmesan Dinner Rolls with chopped parsley for a touch of freshness. Serve warm and enjoy the irresistible aroma and taste!

These Garlic Parmesan Dinner Rolls are a wonderful combination of soft, fluffy texture and bold, cheesy flavor. They are perfect for soaking up sauces, complementing main dishes, or simply enjoying on their own. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is sure to impress and become a staple at your dining table. Give it a try and treat your taste buds to a delectable experience!

Remember, the key to great cooking is passion and creativity. So, don’t be afraid to experiment with different herbs, cheeses, or even adding a twist of your own to make these Garlic Parmesan Dinner Rolls truly your own masterpiece. Happy baking!
