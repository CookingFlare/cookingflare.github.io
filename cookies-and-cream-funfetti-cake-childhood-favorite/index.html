Cookies and Cream Funfetti Cake: Childhood Favorite


Once upon a time, we were kids blissfully enjoying boxes of cookies n’ cream ice cream as our favorite dessert. Little did we know, we could turn that beloved flavor into a warm, gooey, and utterly delectable cake. The Cookies and Cream Funfetti Cake combines the nostalgic taste of Oreo cookies and the jubilant ambiance of sprinkles into one homemade, extra-special treat. Read on to learn how to bring this childhood favorite to life in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 20 Oreo cookies, crushed
  • ½ cup sprinkles
  • 1 cup Heavy whipping cream
  • 3 tablespoons confectioners’ sugar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 8-inch cake pans with butter or cooking spray. Set these aside while you prepare the batter.

  2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, softened butter, eggs, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix thoroughly until fully combined and a smooth dough forms.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk and vanilla extract. Gradually pour this mixture into the dry ingredients, stirring constantly to avoid any lumps.

  4. Fold in half of the crushed Oreos and half of the sprinkles. Once fully incorporated, set the batter aside.

  5. Now, for the craveworthy ‘Cookies and Cream’ twist, chop further 10 of the Oreo cookies and gently fold them into the batter. Reserving the remaining 10 cookies to use as decoration on top of the cake.

  6. Distribute the mix evenly between the prepared cake pans, gently leveling off the surface so that both cakes are the same height.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for around 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  8. During this time, prepare the frosting by whipping the heavy cream in a large bowl with an electric mixer, until it begins to thicken. Slowly pour in the confectioners’ sugar while continuing to whip until glossy peaks form.

  9. Allow the cakes to cool for at least 20 minutes before transferring them onto serving plates or cake stands. Once the cakes have cooled, spread a generous layer of whipped cream frosting on top of one of the cakes.

  10. Stack the second cake on top of the frosted first, and finish by spreading frosting all around the sides of the cake, and on top, ensuring an even distribution.

  11. Crumble the remaining 10 Oreos and sprinkle them on top, along with any remaining decorations and pastel confetti for an extra touch of nostalgia.

  12. Slice, serve, and relive the essence of childhood in every bite. Gather your friends and family for a taste of the past, and witness a cake drawing oohs and aahs, leaving them longing for more.

Conclusion:

There’s nothing like taking a step back in time with a delectable dessert. The Cookies and Cream Funfetti Cake combines both your favorite moments of giggles and cookies with the delight of a frosted cake. A delightful twist on the nostalgic treats we’ve grown up with, this dessert is guaranteed to put a smile on everyone’s faces - and specks of childhood memories in every bite. Enjoy the fluffy, luscious layers as you indulge in this thoughtfully crafted delight.
