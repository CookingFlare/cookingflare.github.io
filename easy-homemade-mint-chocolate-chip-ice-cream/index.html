Easy Homemade Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream


If you’re a fan of mint and chocolate, there’s nothing quite like the refreshing taste of homemade mint chocolate chip ice cream. This delightful frozen treat is perfect for hot summer days or any time you’re in the mood for a cool, creamy dessert. The best part? You can make it at home with just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of time. So, let’s dive into this easy and delicious homemade mint chocolate chip ice cream recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure mint extract
  • A few drops of green food coloring (optional)
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, and granulated sugar until the sugar is completely dissolved.

  2. Add the mint extract to the mixture and stir well. If you want your ice cream to have a vibrant green color, add a few drops of green food coloring and mix until the desired shade is achieved. Remember, the food coloring is optional, so feel free to skip it if you prefer a more natural look.

  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes, or until the mixture reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  4. While the ice cream is churning, chop the semisweet chocolate chips into smaller pieces. You want them to be bite-sized but not too small.

  5. Once the ice cream has reached the desired consistency, add the chopped chocolate chips and churn for an additional 2-3 minutes to mix them evenly into the ice cream.

  6. Transfer the freshly churned ice cream into a freezer-safe container, cover it with a lid or plastic wrap, and freeze for at least 4 hours to allow it to firm up.

  7. When ready to serve, scoop the mint chocolate chip ice cream into bowls or cones, and enjoy the delightful combination of cool, creamy mint with rich, chocolatey goodness.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent treat, drizzle some hot fudge or chocolate sauce over the ice cream before serving.
  • If you prefer a more natural mint flavor, you can use fresh mint leaves instead of mint extract. Simply steep a handful of mint leaves in the warm milk and cream mixture before chilling it.
  • Experiment with different types of chocolate. Try using dark chocolate or white chocolate chips for a unique twist on this classic ice cream flavor.

Final Thoughts

There’s something incredibly satisfying about making your own ice cream at home, and this easy mint chocolate chip recipe is sure to be a hit with friends and family. The refreshing mint flavor combined with the decadent chocolate chips creates a perfect balance of sweetness and freshness. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a cool dessert, this homemade mint chocolate chip ice cream is a delightful choice. So, grab your ingredients, get churning, and treat yourself to a scoop of homemade goodness.
