Taste the Mediterranean with Greek Chicken Souvlaki: A Classic Street Food


If you’re craving the flavors of the Mediterranean, there’s no better way to satisfy your taste buds than with Greek Chicken Souvlaki. This classic street food is a crowd-pleaser that combines tender marinated chicken with aromatic herbs and spices. With its vibrant flavors and easy preparation, Greek Chicken Souvlaki is the perfect dish to transport you to the sunny shores of Greece. So let’s dive into the recipe and bring a taste of the Mediterranean to your plate!

Ingredients

To create the perfect Greek Chicken Souvlaki, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by cutting the chicken into bite-sized pieces. This will ensure that the chicken cooks evenly and absorbs the flavors of the marinade.

  2. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. This aromatic marinade will infuse the chicken with the delicious taste of the Mediterranean.

  3. Place the chicken pieces into a resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish, and pour the marinade over them. Make sure all the chicken pieces are coated evenly. Seal the bag or cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  4. When you’re ready to cook the chicken, preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. If you don’t have a grill, you can also use a skillet on the stovetop.

  5. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. This will allow the chicken to cook evenly and develop a delicious charred exterior.

  6. Place the skewers on the preheated grill or grill pan and cook for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and nicely charred. Remember to rotate the skewers occasionally to ensure even cooking.

  7. Once the chicken is cooked, remove the skewers from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy chicken.

  8. Serve the Greek Chicken Souvlaki with warm pita bread and a side of tzatziki sauce. The cool and creamy tzatziki perfectly complements the flavorful chicken, creating a harmonious balance of tastes and textures.

Enjoy the Flavors of Greece

Now that you have the recipe for Greek Chicken Souvlaki, it’s time to take a bite and experience the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean. This classic street food is not only delicious but also easy to make. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of Greece, Greek Chicken Souvlaki is sure to satisfy your culinary wanderlust. So gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and transport yourself to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean with every savory bite. Opa!

Note: Greek Chicken Souvlaki is also delicious when served with a side of Greek salad or roasted vegetables. Feel free to experiment with different accompaniments to create your perfect Mediterranean meal.
