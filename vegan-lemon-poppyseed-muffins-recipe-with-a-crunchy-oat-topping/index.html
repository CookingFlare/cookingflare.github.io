Vegan Lemon Poppyseed Muffins Recipe with a Crunchy Oat Topping


If you’re looking for a delightful and wholesome treat to brighten up your day, look no further than these Vegan Lemon Poppyseed Muffins with a Crunchy Oat Topping. Bursting with zesty lemon flavor and speckled with poppyseeds, these muffins are a perfect combination of tangy and sweet. The addition of a crunchy oat topping adds a delightful textural contrast, making these muffins a true delight for your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Muffins:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons poppyseeds

For the Crunchy Oat Topping:

  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and lemon zest.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, applesauce, melted coconut oil, and almond milk.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter. Fold in the poppyseeds.

  5. In a small bowl, mix together the oats, flour, brown sugar, and melted coconut oil for the crunchy oat topping.

  6. Fill each muffin cup with the batter, about 3/4 full, and sprinkle the crunchy oat topping over each muffin.

  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  8. Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

These Vegan Lemon Poppyseed Muffins with a Crunchy Oat Topping are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a delightful afternoon snack. Pair them with a hot cup of tea or coffee for a truly indulgent treat. They also make a wonderful addition to a brunch spread or as a sweet ending to a light lunch.

Storage

Store any leftover muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, you can freeze the muffins in a freezer-safe container for up to 3 months. Simply allow them to thaw at room temperature when ready to enjoy.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Lemon Poppyseed Muffins with a Crunchy Oat Topping are a delightful way to indulge in a sweet treat while staying true to your vegan lifestyle. The bright, citrusy flavor of the lemon combined with the satisfying crunch of the oat topping makes these muffins a truly irresistible delight. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, these muffins are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to bake up a batch of these delightful vegan muffins!
