Tasty Thanksgiving Bread Bowl Recipes


The Thanksgiving holiday is a time for gathering with loved ones and indulging in delicious food. One way to elevate your Thanksgiving feast is by incorporating tasty bread bowl recipes that are sure to impress your guests. Whether you’re hosting a large gathering or having an intimate dinner with family, these creative bread bowl recipes are perfect for adding a unique twist to your Thanksgiving menu.

Savory Pumpkin Soup Bread Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 4 small pumpkins
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 small bread bowls

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut off the tops of the pumpkins and scoop out the seeds.
  3. Place the pumpkins on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until tender.
  4. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  5. Stir in the cinnamon and nutmeg, then add the vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Once the pumpkins are roasted, scoop out the flesh and add it to the pot. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Carve out the center of each bread bowl to create a hollow space.
  9. Ladle the pumpkin soup into the bread bowls and serve immediately.

Cranberry Turkey Salad Bread Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked turkey, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 small bread bowls
  • Lettuce leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped turkey, mayonnaise, dried cranberries, chopped pecans, and fresh parsley. Mix well.
  2. Season the turkey salad with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Carve out the center of each bread bowl to create a cavity for the salad.
  4. If desired, line the bread bowls with lettuce leaves.
  5. Spoon the cranberry turkey salad into the bread bowls, filling them to the top.
  6. Serve the bread bowls immediately, or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Apple Pecan Bread Pudding Bread Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cubed bread
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup diced apples
  • 4 small bread bowls

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. Add the cubed bread, chopped pecans, and diced apples to the mixture. Toss until the bread is evenly coated.
  4. Allow the mixture to sit for 10-15 minutes, letting the bread absorb the liquid.
  5. Carve out the center of each bread bowl to create a well for the bread pudding.
  6. Spoon the bread pudding mixture into the bread bowls, distributing evenly.
  7. Place the filled bread bowls on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the bread pudding is set and golden.
  8. Serve the apple pecan bread pudding bread bowls warm, optionally topped with a dollop of whipped cream.

These delightful bread bowl recipes are a fantastic way to bring a touch of creativity to your Thanksgiving celebration. Whether you opt for a savory pumpkin soup, a delightful cranberry turkey salad, or a warm apple pecan bread pudding, these recipes are sure to leave a lasting impression on your guests. Embrace the warmth and comfort of the holiday season with these delectable bread bowl creations that are as visually appealing as they are delicious.
