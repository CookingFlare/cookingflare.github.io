Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken Enchilada Recipes


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine and love the convenience of using an Instant Pot, then you’re in for a treat with these quick and easy Instant Pot chicken enchilada recipes. The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to whipping up delicious meals in a fraction of the time, and these enchilada recipes are no exception. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or meal prepping for the week, these recipes are sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Instant Pot Chicken Enchilada Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Tortilla strips or crushed tortilla chips for garnish

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts at the bottom of the Instant Pot.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the diced tomatoes, enchilada sauce, green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the chicken.
  3. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then carefully switch the valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.
  5. Open the lid and use two forks to shred the chicken directly in the Instant Pot.
  6. Stir in the shredded cheese and chopped cilantro until the cheese is melted and the soup is well combined.
  7. Serve the soup in bowls, garnished with tortilla strips or crushed tortilla chips.

Instant Pot Chicken Enchilada Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 6 corn tortillas, torn into quarters
  • Sliced green onions and chopped fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In the Instant Pot, combine the chicken, enchilada sauce, green chilies, frozen corn, black beans, cumin, chili powder, and garlic powder.
  2. Place the torn corn tortillas on top of the mixture.
  3. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is complete, do a quick pressure release.
  5. Open the lid and stir in the shredded cheese and sour cream until well combined.
  6. Serve the enchilada casserole on plates, garnished with sliced green onions and chopped fresh cilantro.

These Instant Pot chicken enchilada recipes are not only flavorful and satisfying but also incredibly simple to prepare. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can enjoy the rich and robust flavors of traditional enchiladas with a fraction of the effort. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply want to indulge in a comforting meal, these recipes are sure to impress. So, dust off your Instant Pot and get ready to savor the deliciousness of homemade chicken enchiladas in no time!
