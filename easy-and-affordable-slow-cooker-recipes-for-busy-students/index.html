Easy and Affordable Slow Cooker Recipes for Busy Students


Are you a busy student looking for quick and delicious meals that won’t break the bank? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore a collection of easy and affordable slow cooker recipes that are perfect for students on the go. With minimal prep time and a hands-off cooking approach, these recipes will save you time and money while still satisfying your taste buds. Let’s dive right in!

1. Hearty Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a slow cooker and stir well.
  2. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy a comforting bowl of vegetable soup.

2. BBQ Pulled Chicken Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • Hamburger buns

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce.
  3. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken breasts.
  4. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours.
  5. Using two forks, shred the chicken in the slow cooker.
  6. Serve the pulled chicken on hamburger buns and enjoy a flavorful BBQ sandwich.

3. Creamy Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions and drain.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until smooth.
  4. Gradually whisk in the milk and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer until thickened, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.
  7. Combine the cooked macaroni and cheese sauce in the slow cooker.
  8. Cook on low heat for 2-3 hours or on high heat for 1-2 hours.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy a creamy and cheesy mac and cheese.

4. Teriyaki Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken, broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot in the slow cooker.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, and garlic.
  3. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables.
  4. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours.
  5. Serve the teriyaki chicken stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles and enjoy a flavorful Asian-inspired meal.

5. Chunky Beef Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the beef is browned and cooked through.
  2. Drain any excess fat and transfer the mixture to the slow cooker.
  3. Add the kidney beans, diced tomatoes, beef broth, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to the slow cooker.
  4. Stir well to combine.
  5. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy a hearty bowl of beef chili.

Conclusion

With these easy and affordable slow cooker recipes, you can enjoy delicious meals without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re a busy student or just looking for convenient meal options, these recipes are sure to satisfy your hunger and your wallet. So grab your slow cooker and get cooking!
