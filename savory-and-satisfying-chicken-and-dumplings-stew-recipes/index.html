Savory and Satisfying Chicken and Dumplings Stew Recipes


Chicken and dumplings is a timeless comfort food that has warmed hearts and filled bellies for generations. This hearty dish combines tender chicken with fluffy dumplings in a rich, flavorful broth, creating a satisfying meal that’s perfect for any occasion. In this blog post, we’ll explore several variations of chicken and dumplings stew, each offering a unique twist on this classic recipe. Whether you prefer a traditional approach or something with a modern flair, you’ll find a recipe that suits your taste.

The Classic Chicken and Dumplings Stew

Ingredients

For the stew:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 lbs), cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

For the dumplings:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken Stew:

    • In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and brown them on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken and set it aside.
    • In the same pot, add the diced onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are softened. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
    • Pour in the chicken broth and stir in the thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Return the browned chicken to the pot. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

  2. Make the Dumplings:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper. In a separate bowl, mix the melted butter and milk. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
    • Once the chicken is cooked, remove the chicken pieces from the pot, shred the meat, and discard the bones. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.

  3. Cook the Dumplings:

    • Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter onto the surface of the simmering stew. Cover the pot with a lid and let the dumplings steam for about 15 minutes. Do not lift the lid during this time.
    • Once the dumplings are cooked through and fluffy, serve the chicken and dumplings stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Creamy Chicken and Dumplings with Vegetables

For those who crave a creamy twist, this recipe adds richness and flavor to the classic stew.

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (peas, corn, green beans)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the dumplings:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables:

    • Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
    • Add the mixed vegetables and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes.

  2. Build the Stew:

    • Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Add the heavy cream, shredded chicken, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well and let it simmer for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  3. Prepare the Dumplings:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, black pepper, and Parmesan cheese. Stir in the milk until just combined.
    • Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling mixture into the simmering stew. Cover the pot and let the dumplings cook for about 15 minutes without lifting the lid.

  4. Serve:

    • Once the dumplings are fluffy and cooked through, serve the creamy chicken and dumplings hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Southern Style Chicken and Dumplings

This recipe pays homage to the Southern tradition of chicken and dumplings, emphasizing rich flavors and tender textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4 lbs)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups buttermilk biscuit mix
  • 1/2 cup milk (for dumplings)
  • 1 teaspoon sage
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Chicken:

    • In a large pot, place the whole chicken and cover it with water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for about 1 hour or until the chicken is tender. Remove the chicken, reserving the broth, and let it cool before shredding the meat.

  2. Create the Stew Base:

    • In the same pot, melt the butter and add the diced onion. Sauté until translucent. Pour in the reserved chicken broth and milk, bringing it to a gentle simmer.
    • Stir in the shredded chicken, sage, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes.

  3. Prepare the Dumplings:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the biscuit mix with 1/2 cup of milk until a soft dough forms.
    • Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling mixture into the simmering stew. Cover the pot and let the dumplings steam for 10-12 minutes.

  4. Serve:

    • Once the dumplings are fluffy, serve the Southern style chicken and dumplings hot, garnished with chopped green onions.

Healthy Chicken and Dumplings with Cauliflower

For a lighter, healthier option, this recipe incorporates cauliflower to create a nutritious twist on chicken and dumplings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 head of cauliflower, chopped into florets
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the dumplings:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk or almond milk
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Cook the Chicken:

    • In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until browned. Remove the chicken and set aside.

  2. Sauté the Vegetables:

    • In the same pot, add the onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté until softened, about 5 minutes. Return the chicken to the pot.

  3. Add the Broth and Cauliflower:

    • Pour in the chicken broth and add the cauliflower florets. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for about 20 minutes until the cauliflower is tender. Add the frozen peas, thyme, salt, and pepper.

  4. Make the Dumplings:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine almond flour, coconut flour, baking powder, milk, and eggs. Mix until well combined.
    • Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling mixture into the simmering stew. Cover and let the dumplings steam for 10-12 minutes.

  5. Serve:

    • Serve the healthy chicken and dumplings hot, enjoying a nutritious twist on a classic dish.

Conclusion

Chicken and dumplings is a versatile dish that can be adapted to suit any palate, from traditional recipes that evoke nostalgia to modern interpretations that cater to health-conscious diners. Whether you choose the classic version, a creamy variant, a Southern-style dish, or a healthy option, each recipe promises to deliver comfort and satisfaction in every bite. Gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and enjoy the process of creating this comforting stew that brings family and friends together around the table.

Dive into these recipes, experiment with flavors, and make this beloved dish your own! Happy cooking!
