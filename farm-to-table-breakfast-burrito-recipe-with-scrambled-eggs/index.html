Farm-to-Table Breakfast Burrito Recipe with Scrambled Eggs


Are you looking for a delicious and wholesome breakfast option that celebrates fresh, farm-to-table ingredients? Look no further than this mouthwatering Farm-to-Table Breakfast Burrito recipe featuring fluffy scrambled eggs, vibrant vegetables, and flavorful herbs. This hearty burrito is not only a fantastic way to start your day but also a celebration of locally sourced produce and ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 4 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salsa and avocado slices (optional, for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Scrambled Eggs: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  2. Cook the Vegetables: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the diced bell peppers and onion, and sauté until they begin to soften, about 3-4 minutes. Add the cherry tomatoes and spinach, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the spinach wilts.

  3. Scramble the Eggs: Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour the egg mixture into the other side. Let it sit for a few seconds, then gently scramble the eggs until they are just set but still moist.

  4. Assemble the Burritos: Place a tortilla on a flat surface. Spoon a portion of the scrambled eggs and vegetable mixture onto the center of the tortilla. Sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese and chopped cilantro.

  5. Roll the Burritos: Fold in the sides of the tortilla, then roll it up tightly from the bottom to enclose the filling.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Repeat with the remaining ingredients. Serve the Farm-to-Table Breakfast Burritos with salsa, avocado slices, or your favorite hot sauce on the side.

Why Choose Farm-to-Table?

Farm-to-table cooking is not just a trend; it’s a commitment to freshness, sustainability, and supporting local farmers. By choosing farm-fresh ingredients for your meals, you not only enjoy superior taste and quality but also contribute to a more environmentally friendly and ethical food system.

The beauty of this Farm-to-Table Breakfast Burrito recipe lies in its simplicity and versatility. You can customize it with your favorite vegetables, herbs, or cheeses, making it a perfect dish for using up whatever produce you have on hand. Whether enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or even a quick dinner, this burrito is sure to satisfy your cravings for a hearty and nutritious meal.

So, the next time you’re craving a wholesome breakfast that celebrates the abundance of the local harvest, give this Farm-to-Table Breakfast Burrito recipe a try. Your taste buds and your community will thank you for it!

Remember, the key to a truly delicious meal is not just in the ingredients but in the love and care you put into preparing it. Happy cooking and happy eating!
