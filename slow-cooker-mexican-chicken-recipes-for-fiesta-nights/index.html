Slow Cooker Mexican Chicken Recipes for Fiesta Nights


Are you ready to spice up your fiesta nights with some delicious Mexican chicken dishes? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share with you three mouthwatering slow cooker Mexican chicken recipes that will make your taste buds dance with joy. These recipes are not only easy to make but also packed with flavor, making them perfect for any fiesta night celebration. So let’s get cooking!

1. Slow Cooker Mexican Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Tortillas, for serving
  • Your favorite taco toppings (lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker and sprinkle the taco seasoning over them.
  2. Pour the salsa, chicken broth, and lime juice over the chicken.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or until the chicken is tender and easily shreds.
  4. Shred the chicken using two forks and mix it with the sauce in the slow cooker.
  5. Serve the shredded chicken in tortillas with your favorite taco toppings.

2. Slow Cooker Mexican Chicken Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can corn, drained
  • 1 can diced green chilies
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken thighs, onion, garlic, diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, diced green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper in the slow cooker.
  2. Stir to combine all the ingredients.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  4. Remove the chicken thighs from the slow cooker and shred them using two forks.
  5. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and stir to combine.
  6. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

3. Slow Cooker Mexican Chicken Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can corn, drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can diced green chilies
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker and season them with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, diced green chilies, and salsa to the slow cooker.
  3. Stir to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  5. Remove the chicken breasts from the slow cooker and shred them using two forks.
  6. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and stir to combine.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the top.
  8. Cover and cook for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
  9. Serve the casserole hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

These slow cooker Mexican chicken recipes are sure to be a hit at your next fiesta night. Whether you choose to make tacos, soup, or casserole, the flavors will transport you to the vibrant streets of Mexico. So gather your friends and family, turn on the slow cooker, and get ready for a fiesta like no other. Enjoy!
