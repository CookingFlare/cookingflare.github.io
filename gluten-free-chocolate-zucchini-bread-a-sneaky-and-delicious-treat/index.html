Gluten-Free Chocolate Zucchini Bread: A Sneaky and Delicious Treat


Are you looking for a creative way to sneak some extra veggies into your diet? Look no further than this delectable gluten-free chocolate zucchini bread! Not only is this recipe a delightful way to enjoy a sweet treat, but it also packs a nutritious punch with the addition of zucchini. Whether you’re baking for yourself, your family, or a special occasion, this recipe is sure to impress with its rich chocolate flavor and moist, tender crumb.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups grated zucchini
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan and set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt until well combined.

  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs, coconut oil, applesauce, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Gently fold in the grated zucchini and chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Bake for 55-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Sneaky and Delicious

What makes this gluten-free chocolate zucchini bread truly special is its ability to sneak in a serving of vegetables without compromising on taste. The zucchini adds moisture and a subtle sweetness to the bread, while the cocoa powder and chocolate chips provide a rich, indulgent flavor that will have you coming back for more.

Perfect for Any Occasion

Whether you’re hosting a brunch, packing a school lunch, or simply craving a chocolatey pick-me-up, this bread is a versatile and satisfying option. It’s also a fantastic way to use up an abundance of zucchini from your garden or local farmers’ market.

Gluten-Free Goodness

By using gluten-free all-purpose flour in this recipe, you can cater to a wider range of dietary needs without sacrificing taste or texture. This makes it an ideal choice for those with gluten sensitivities or for anyone looking to reduce their gluten intake.

Conclusion

Gluten-free chocolate zucchini bread is a delightful twist on a classic treat, offering a balance of wholesome ingredients and irresistible flavor. Whether you enjoy it for breakfast, as a snack, or as a dessert, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. With its sneaky incorporation of zucchini and rich chocolatey taste, it’s a delicious way to indulge while still nourishing your body with a touch of green goodness. So, grab your apron and get ready to bake up this delightful gluten-free treat!
