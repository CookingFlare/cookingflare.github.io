Decadent Chocolate Peanut Butter Ice Cream Cake Recipe


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of chocolate and peanut butter, this decadent ice cream cake is sure to become your new favorite dessert. With layers of rich chocolate cake, creamy peanut butter ice cream, and a luscious chocolate ganache topping, this indulgent treat is perfect for any special occasion or just a sweet weekend indulgence.

Ingredients

For the Chocolate Cake:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup boiling water

For the Peanut Butter Ice Cream:

  • 2 cups creamy peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 cups heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

For the Chocolate Cake:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add the eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla extract; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour the batter into the prepared pans.
  4. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely on a wire rack.

For the Peanut Butter Ice Cream:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the peanut butter and sugar until smooth. Gradually beat in the heavy cream and vanilla until well combined.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to the manufacturer’s instructions.

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  1. In a small saucepan, bring the heavy cream to a simmer. Place the chopped chocolate in a heatproof bowl.
  2. Pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit for 2 minutes. Add the butter and stir until smooth.

Assembly

  1. Once the cake layers are completely cooled, place one layer in the bottom of a springform pan.
  2. Pour the peanut butter ice cream over the cake layer, spreading it evenly.
  3. Place the second cake layer on top of the ice cream and press down gently.
  4. Pour the chocolate ganache over the top of the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.
  5. Freeze the cake for at least 4 hours, or until the ice cream is firm.

Serving

When you’re ready to serve, remove the cake from the freezer and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes to make slicing easier. This cake is rich and indulgent, so a small slice goes a long way. Enjoy!

This Chocolate Peanut Butter Ice Cream Cake is a show-stopping dessert that’s perfect for any chocolate and peanut butter lover. Its decadent layers and rich flavors are sure to impress your friends and family. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself, this cake is guaranteed to be a hit. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to create a dessert that will be remembered long after the last crumb is gone.
