Grilled Octopus with Chimichurri Sauce Recipe


If you’re looking to impress your guests with a unique and flavorful seafood dish, look no further than this Grilled Octopus with Chimichurri Sauce recipe. Grilled octopus is a delicacy enjoyed in many coastal regions around the world, and when paired with the vibrant flavors of chimichurri sauce, it creates a truly memorable dining experience.

Ingredients

For the Octopus:

  • 2 lbs. fresh octopus, cleaned
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Chimichurri Sauce:

  • 1 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the octopus. In a large pot of boiling water, blanch the octopus three times, dunking it into the boiling water and removing it quickly. This process helps tenderize the octopus.

  2. In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper. Coat the blanched octopus with this mixture, ensuring that it’s evenly coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  3. While the octopus is marinating, prepare the chimichurri sauce. In a bowl, combine the chopped parsley, cilantro, minced garlic, olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside to let the flavors meld.

  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Grill the octopus for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until it develops a lightly charred exterior and is cooked through. Be mindful not to overcook the octopus, as it can become tough.

  5. Once the octopus is grilled to perfection, remove it from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice it into individual tentacles.

  6. Serve the grilled octopus with a generous drizzle of the chimichurri sauce on top. The bright, herbaceous flavors of the chimichurri perfectly complement the tender, smoky octopus.

Serving Suggestions

This Grilled Octopus with Chimichurri Sauce pairs beautifully with a simple arugula salad dressed with lemon vinaigrette and a side of crusty bread to soak up the flavorful chimichurri sauce. It’s a dish that’s sure to elevate any dinner gathering and leave a lasting impression on your guests.

Final Thoughts

Grilling octopus may seem intimidating at first, but with the right technique and a flavorful chimichurri sauce, it becomes an approachable and impressive dish. The combination of tender, charred octopus and zesty, herb-packed chimichurri is a match made in culinary heaven. So, the next time you’re in the mood to try something new and exciting, consider giving this Grilled Octopus with Chimichurri Sauce recipe a whirl. Your taste buds will thank you!

With its tender, smoky flavors and vibrant chimichurri sauce, this Grilled Octopus recipe is a true showstopper. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a gourmet seafood dish, this recipe is certain to impress. So, fire up the grill and get ready to savor every flavorful bite of this culinary masterpiece!
