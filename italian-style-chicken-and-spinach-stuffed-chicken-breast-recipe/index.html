Italian-Style Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and impressive dish to serve at your next dinner party or to simply elevate your weeknight dinner, this Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast recipe is a must-try. This dish combines the succulent tenderness of chicken breast with the flavorful goodness of Italian ingredients, resulting in a mouthwatering and satisfying meal that will have everyone asking for seconds. Let’s dive into the delightful world of this delectable recipe.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Breasts

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place each chicken breast between two sheets of plastic wrap and gently pound to an even thickness of about 1/2 inch. Season both sides with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

Step 3: Stuff the Chicken Breasts

  1. Spoon the spinach and cheese mixture onto one side of each chicken breast. Fold the other half of the chicken breast over the filling to create a pocket.

Step 4: Sear the Chicken

  1. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the stuffed chicken breasts for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.

Step 5: Make the Sauce and Bake

  1. In the same skillet, add crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, minced garlic, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  2. Place the skillet in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once cooked, remove the skillet from the oven. Serve the stuffed chicken breasts with a generous spoonful of the flavorful tomato sauce.

Tips and Variations

  • To add a bit of heat, consider sprinkling red pepper flakes into the spinach and cheese mixture.
  • For a gluten-free option, use gluten-free bread crumbs in the filling.

In Conclusion

This Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast recipe is a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that will undoubtedly impress your family and friends. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is approachable and rewarding. The tender, juicy chicken paired with the creamy spinach and cheese filling, all nestled in a rich tomato sauce, is a culinary experience worth savoring. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the art of Italian-inspired cooking. Buon appetito!
