Thai Coconut Curry Soup Ingredients 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk 2 cups vegetable broth 1 tablespoon red curry paste 1 tablespoon gluten-free soy sauce 1 cup mushrooms, sliced 1 cup bell peppers, sliced 1 cup broccoli florets 1 cup snap peas 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced 2 cloves garlic, minced Juice of 1 lime Fresh cilantro for garnish Instructions Sauté Aromatics: In a large pot, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add the minced ginger and garlic, sautéing for about 2 minutes until fragrant. Add Vegetables: Stir in the mushrooms, bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas. Add the red curry paste and gluten-free soy sauce, stirring until the paste is fully dissolved. Simmer: Bring the soup to a low simmer and let it cook for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. Satisfying Gluten-Free Chicken and Rice Casserole: A Comforting Dish

Are you in the mood for a delicious and comforting meal that won’t leave you feeling bloated or guilty? Look no further! Our Satisfying Gluten-Free Chicken and Rice Casserole is the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings without compromising your dietary needs. Ingredients 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded 2 cups cooked brown rice 1 cup broccoli florets 1 cup carrots, diced 1 cup peas 1 onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup chicken broth 1 cup unsweetened almond milk 2 tablespoons gluten-free all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a casserole dish with cooking spray. In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the diced carrots, broccoli florets, and peas to the skillet. Cook for 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are slightly tender. In a separate bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, almond milk, gluten-free all-purpose flour, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Ensure that the flour is fully dissolved. Pour the mixture into the skillet with the vegetables. Stir well to combine. Add the cooked chicken and brown rice to the skillet. Stir until all the ingredients are evenly coated with the sauce. Transfer the mixture to the greased casserole dish, spreading it out evenly. Cover the casserole dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbling. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Serving Suggestions This Gluten-Free Chicken and Rice Casserole is a complete meal on its own, but you can also serve it with a fresh green salad or steamed vegetables for added nutrition. Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite herbs and spices to enhance the flavor. ...
