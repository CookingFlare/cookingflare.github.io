Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to make, then this Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo recipe is for you. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have a creamy and flavorful chicken alfredo dish on the table in no time. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to enjoy a comforting homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Chicken: Start by setting your Instant Pot to the “Sauté” function. Add the olive oil, then add the chicken pieces. Sauté the chicken until it’s browned on all sides, then remove it from the Instant Pot and set it aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the minced garlic and chopped onion. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion is soft and translucent.

  3. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the chicken broth and use a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. This step will add a depth of flavor to the dish.

  4. Pressure Cook the Pasta: Break the fettuccine pasta in half and add it to the Instant Pot, making sure it’s submerged in the liquid. Cancel the “Sauté” function, then secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 6 minutes.

  5. Quick Release and Add the Chicken: Once the cooking time is up, perform a quick pressure release. Open the lid and stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Add the sautéed chicken back into the pot, then season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Let the chicken alfredo sit for a few minutes to thicken, then garnish with fresh parsley before serving. This creamy and flavorful Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo is best enjoyed hot, straight from the pot to the plate.

Tips and Variations

  • For a healthier twist, you can use whole wheat fettuccine or even zucchini noodles instead of traditional pasta.
  • Feel free to add some cooked broccoli or spinach to the dish for an extra boost of nutrients and color.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can stir in a cornstarch slurry at the end and let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce reaches your desired consistency.

Final Thoughts

This Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo recipe is a game-changer for anyone craving a comforting Italian meal without the hassle of traditional stovetop cooking. The Instant Pot streamlines the process, making it possible to have a homemade chicken alfredo on the table in a fraction of the time. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight dinner or simply craving a creamy pasta dish, this recipe is sure to become a household favorite. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy a delightful and fuss-free meal!
