Crispy Fried Pickles with Ranch Dressing: A Southern-Inspired Snack


If you’re a fan of Southern cuisine or looking to try something new, then you’re in for a treat! Crispy fried pickles with ranch dressing are the perfect snack to satisfy your cravings. This delicious combination of tangy pickles and creamy ranch dressing is a match made in culinary heaven. So, let’s dive right into this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

For the pickles:

  • 1 jar of dill pickle chips
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the ranch dressing:

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the ranch dressing. In a bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, buttermilk, fresh dill, fresh parsley, minced garlic, onion powder, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the dressing while you prepare the fried pickles.

  2. Drain the jar of dill pickle chips and pat them dry using paper towels. This step is crucial to ensure the pickles get crispy when fried.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, paprika, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful coating for the pickles.

  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to a temperature of 375°F (190°C).

  5. Take a handful of pickle chips and toss them in the flour mixture, ensuring each pickle is fully coated. Shake off any excess flour and carefully place the coated pickles in the hot oil. Fry them in small batches to maintain the oil temperature and prevent overcrowding.

  6. Fry the pickles for about 3-4 minutes, or until they turn golden brown and crispy. Use a slotted spoon or tongs to remove the pickles from the oil and transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

  7. Repeat the coating and frying process with the remaining pickle chips until all the pickles are fried to perfection.

  8. Serve the crispy fried pickles with a side of the chilled ranch dressing. The cool and creamy ranch dressing perfectly complements the crispy and tangy pickles, making each bite a burst of flavors.

Enjoy your homemade crispy fried pickles with ranch dressing as a delightful snack or as an appetizer for a gathering with friends and family. These Southern-inspired treats are sure to impress everyone with their unique taste and irresistible crunch. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in this finger-licking good snack!

Remember, this recipe is just a starting point. Feel free to experiment with different spices and seasonings to make it your own. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a tasty snack, these crispy fried pickles with ranch dressing are sure to be a hit. Happy cooking!
