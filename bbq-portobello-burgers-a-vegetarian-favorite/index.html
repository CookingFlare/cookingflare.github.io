BBQ Portobello Burgers: A Vegetarian Favorite


If you’re craving a hearty, mouthwatering meal that’s perfect for any grill session, look no further than the BBQ Portobello Burger! Tender, meaty Portobello mushrooms serve as a savory, protein-packed base that will delight even the most dedicated carnivores. Best of all, this scrumptious burger can be enjoyed by both vegetarians and meat-eaters alike, making it a fantastic option for a crowd-pleasing cookout.

why portobello mushrooms make an excellent burger patty

Perhaps you’re not familiar with wooing your guests with the appetizing taste of grilled Portobello mushrooms. These giant ‘shrooms have much going for them:

  1. Hearty Flavor: Beyond staking your claim as the master of the grill, you’ll marvel at the way the flavors meld and intensify over the flames. Just a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of salt brings out rich umami notes, begging for a classic cheese pairing or tangy BBQ sauce.
  2. High in Protein: If you’re a vegetarian, a Portobello burger packs a powerful protein punch. Whether you want to dial up your plant-based meal or just feel like a break from the typical cookout fare, mushrooms deliver.
  3. Superior Nutrients: Few foods can boast the impressive nutrient profile of Portobello mushrooms. They’re loaded with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. B grillin’ and reap the rewards of this fantastic fungi!

create the best BBQ portobello burger:

Making the ultimate BBQ Portobello Burger is a matter of assembling superb ingredients. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Portobello Patties

  • 4 Large Portobello mushrooms
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Your choice of toppings (cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, etc.)

For the Best Buns

  • 4 Soft, hearty burger buns
  • Your favorite spread (mustard, mayo, or a blend)

For the Sizzling BBQ Sauce

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika

Enough talk; let’s get grilling!

1. Prepare the Portobello Patties:

a. Clean and thoroughly dry four large Portobello mushrooms. b. Brush the tops of the mushrooms with a small amount of olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste. c. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

2. Grill the Burgers:

a. Once your grill is hot, place the mushrooms, gill-side down, directly on the grates. b. Grill for around 6 minutes, until the mushrooms have developed a nice char and released some of their moisture. c. Gently flip the mushrooms and continue cooking for another 6 minutes on the non-gill side.

3. Make the BBQ Sauce (optional):

a. While your patties are cookin’, prepare your favorite BBQ sauce by combining the ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, and smoked paprika in a small saucepan. Simmer for a few minutes until all the flavors meld. b. Feel free to adjust the sweetness or tanginess according to your liking; some folks may enjoy a spicy kick incorporated into their sauce!

4. Assemble and Serve:

a. Once mushrooms are fully cooked, remove them from the grill and place onto your choice of buns. Add your prefered toppings like cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, sliced onions, or pickles. b. Add a smear of your favorite spread (we love mustard or a mix of mayo and classic barbecue sauce) on the bun top. c. Cap off the burger with the bun top and enjoy!

5. Grill Up Some Sides:

To make it a full-blown cookout, remember, variety is the spice of life! Your barbecued portobello burgers can be accompanied by grilled vegetables, corn on the cob, a fresh cucumber salad, or a hearty plate of coleslaw.

Get ready to impress your friends and family with this smashing vegetarian burger that’s sure to be a hit at your next cookout. The BBQ Portobello Burger – a delicious excuse to get grilling and savor the smoky, savory flavor of these meaty mushrooms!
