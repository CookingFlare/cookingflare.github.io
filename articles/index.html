Healthy One-Pot Quinoa and Black Bean Chili: A Nutritious Option

Welcome to another delicious and nutritious recipe on our blog! Today, we’re excited to share with you a flavorful and easy-to-make one-pot meal: Healthy One-Pot Quinoa and Black Bean Chili. Packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, this hearty chili is perfect for those looking for a wholesome and satisfying option. Let’s dive right in! Ingredients 1 cup quinoa 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 onion, diced 3 cloves of garlic, minced 1 bell pepper, diced 2 carrots, diced 1 zucchini, diced 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce 2 tablespoons chili powder 1 tablespoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper to taste Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish) Instructions Rinse the quinoa thoroughly under cold water to remove any bitterness. Set it aside. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent. Add the diced bell pepper, carrots, and zucchini to the pot. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables start to soften. Add the rinsed black beans, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, paprika, and salt and pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Pour in the rinsed quinoa and stir again. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the quinoa is cooked and the flavors have melded together. Once the chili is ready, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Add more chili powder or salt and pepper according to your preference. Serve the Healthy One-Pot Quinoa and Black Bean Chili hot, garnished with fresh chopped cilantro.
