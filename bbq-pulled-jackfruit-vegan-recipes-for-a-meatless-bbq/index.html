BBQ Pulled Jackfruit: Vegan Recipes for a Meatless BBQ


Are you tired of the same old BBQ recipes? Looking for a delicious and satisfying vegan alternative for your next cookout? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make BBQ pulled jackfruit – a mouthwatering dish that will leave you and your guests wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of young green jackfruit in brine or water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce of your choice
  • Hamburger buns or rolls, for serving

Instructions

  1. Drain and rinse the jackfruit thoroughly. Cut off the tough core from the jackfruit pieces and discard. Shred the remaining jackfruit using your hands or a fork to resemble pulled pork.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Add the shredded jackfruit to the skillet and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the jackfruit starts to brown slightly.

  4. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt, and black pepper. Sprinkle this spice mixture over the jackfruit in the skillet and stir well to coat the jackfruit evenly.

  5. Pour the BBQ sauce over the jackfruit and stir to combine. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the jackfruit is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  6. Remove the lid and continue to cook for an additional 5-10 minutes to allow the sauce to thicken slightly.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the BBQ pulled jackfruit cool for a few minutes.

  8. Serve the BBQ pulled jackfruit on hamburger buns or rolls, and enjoy the deliciousness of this vegan twist on a classic BBQ favorite.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier BBQ pulled jackfruit, feel free to add more chili powder or a dash of cayenne pepper to the spice mixture.
  • You can customize the flavor of your BBQ sauce by choosing a sweet and smoky one, a tangy and spicy one, or even a fruity and tropical one.
  • Serve the BBQ pulled jackfruit with your favorite coleslaw, pickles, or vegan cheese for an extra layer of flavor and texture.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in a skillet or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

BBQ pulled jackfruit is a fantastic vegan alternative to traditional pulled pork. Its meaty texture and ability to soak up flavors make it the perfect ingredient for a meatless BBQ. Whether you’re a vegan, vegetarian, or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and show you that plant-based cooking can be just as delicious and satisfying. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and enjoy a mouthwatering meatless BBQ with BBQ pulled jackfruit as the star of the show!
