Scrumptious Red Velvet Cookie Recipe: A Decadent Delight


Are you craving a rich and indulgent treat that will leave your taste buds begging for more? Look no further than these Scrumptious Red Velvet Cookies! With their vibrant red color, velvety texture, and irresistible cream cheese filling, these cookies are a decadent delight that will satisfy even the most discerning sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons red food coloring
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk

Cream Cheese Filling

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the egg and mix until well combined. Stir in the red food coloring and vanilla extract, ensuring the batter has a vibrant red hue.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk. Mix until just combined, being careful not to overmix.

  5. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Use the back of a spoon to gently flatten each portion, creating a small well in the center.

  6. In a separate bowl, prepare the cream cheese filling. Beat together the softened cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  7. Spoon a small amount of the cream cheese filling into the well of each cookie, filling it to the top.

  8. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set and the tops are slightly cracked. Remove from the oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, these Scrumptious Red Velvet Cookies are ready to be enjoyed! Serve them with a glass of cold milk or a cup of hot tea for the ultimate indulgence.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of elegance, dust the tops of the cookies with powdered sugar before serving.
  • Add a sprinkle of chocolate chips or chopped nuts to the cream cheese filling for added texture and flavor.
  • Experiment with different food coloring shades to create unique and eye-catching cookie creations.
  • Store any leftover cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. They can also be frozen for longer storage.

These Scrumptious Red Velvet Cookies are the perfect treat for any occasion. Whether you’re baking them for a special celebration or simply indulging in a sweet treat, they are sure to satisfy your cravings. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to experience the decadent delight of these delicious cookies. Happy baking!
