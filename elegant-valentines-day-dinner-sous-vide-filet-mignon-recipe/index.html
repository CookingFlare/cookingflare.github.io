Elegant Valentine's Day Dinner: Sous Vide Filet Mignon Recipe


Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to impress your loved one with an exquisite and elegant dinner. If you’re looking to create a memorable dining experience, I have just the recipe for you: Sous Vide Filet Mignon. This cooking technique ensures that your steak is cooked to perfection, tender and juicy every time. So, let’s dive right into it!

Ingredients

  • 2 filet mignon steaks (1 ½ to 2 inches thick)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 4 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • Optional: Red wine reduction, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 130°F (54°C). This temperature will give you a medium-rare steak, but feel free to adjust it according to your preference.

  2. Season your filet mignon steaks generously with salt and pepper on both sides. Place each steak in a separate vacuum-sealed bag. If you don’t have a vacuum sealer, you can use a ziplock bag and remove as much air as possible by submerging it in water and sealing it just before it’s completely submerged.

  3. Carefully place the bags in the preheated water bath, ensuring that they are fully submerged. Cook the steaks for 2 to 3 hours, allowing them to reach the desired temperature and tenderness.

  4. While the steaks are cooking, prepare a cast-iron skillet or grill pan over high heat. Add the olive oil and butter, allowing the butter to melt and coat the pan evenly.

  5. Once the steaks have finished cooking, remove them from the bags and pat them dry with paper towels. This step is crucial to achieving a beautiful sear on the outside of the steak.

  6. Transfer the steaks to the hot skillet and sear them for about 1 minute on each side, or until a golden brown crust forms. During this step, you can add the thyme sprigs and crushed garlic to infuse the flavors into the steak.

  7. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing. This resting period allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a tender and flavorful steak.

  8. Serve your Sous Vide Filet Mignon with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables or creamy mashed potatoes. If you want to elevate the dish even further, drizzle a red wine reduction over the steak for a burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For a more intense flavor, you can marinate the steaks in a mixture of olive oil, minced garlic, and fresh herbs before sealing them in the bags.

  • Experiment with different seasonings and spices to create your own unique flavor profile. Consider adding a touch of smoked paprika, dried herbs, or even a hint of cayenne pepper for a slight kick.

  • If you don’t have a sous vide machine, you can achieve similar results by using a large pot filled with water and a thermometer. Monitor the temperature closely and make adjustments as needed.

  • Don’t forget to let your steaks rest before slicing. This step is essential for a tender and juicy result.

Conclusion

Preparing an elegant Valentine’s Day dinner doesn’t have to be complicated. With this Sous Vide Filet Mignon recipe, you can create a restaurant-quality dish right in the comfort of your own home. The sous vide technique ensures a perfectly cooked steak every time, allowing you to focus on creating a romantic ambiance for your special evening. So, grab your apron, get cooking, and enjoy a memorable Valentine’s Day dinner with your loved one. Cheers to love and good food!
