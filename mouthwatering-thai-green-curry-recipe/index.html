Mouthwatering Thai Green Curry Recipe


If you’re craving a burst of exotic flavors with a touch of spice, look no further than this delightful Thai Green Curry recipe. Known for its vibrant color and aromatic blend of herbs and spices, Thai Green Curry is a dish that never fails to impress. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a newbie in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to elevate your culinary skills and transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, thinly sliced
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of green curry paste
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of bamboo shoots
  • 1 cup of green beans, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • Handful of fresh Thai basil leaves
  • Sliced red chilies (optional, for extra heat)
  • Cooked jasmine rice, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Curry Base: In a large pan over medium heat, scoop out the thick cream from the top of the coconut milk can and add it to the pan. Stir in the green curry paste and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.

  2. Add Chicken: Add the sliced chicken thighs to the pan and cook until they are no longer pink on the outside.

  3. Simmer: Pour in the remaining coconut milk and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  4. Add Vegetables: Toss in the red and green bell peppers, bamboo shoots, and green beans. Let the curry simmer until the vegetables are tender but still vibrant.

  5. Season: Season the curry with fish sauce and sugar. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences.

  6. Finish: Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the fresh Thai basil leaves. If you like some extra heat, sprinkle some sliced red chilies on top.

  7. Serve: Serve the Thai Green Curry hot over steamed jasmine rice. Garnish with additional basil leaves and red chilies for a pop of color.

Tips for a Perfect Thai Green Curry:

  • For a vegetarian version, substitute the chicken with tofu or your favorite vegetables like eggplant or zucchini.
  • If you prefer a thicker curry, let it simmer uncovered for a few extra minutes to reduce the liquid.
  • Taste as you go and adjust the seasoning to achieve the perfect balance of sweet, salty, spicy, and savory flavors.
  • Fresh ingredients like Thai basil and red chilies make a significant difference in the flavor profile of the curry.

Final Thoughts

Indulge in the enticing flavors of Thailand with this Mouthwatering Thai Green Curry recipe. With its harmonious blend of creamy coconut milk, fragrant herbs, and zesty green curry paste, this dish is a true celebration of Thai cuisine. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a flavorful meal, this recipe is guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds and leave you craving more. So, gather your ingredients, unleash your inner chef, and embark on a culinary journey to the heart of Thailand with this delectable Thai Green Curry!
