Wholesome Dairy-Free Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe

If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious dairy-free option for your next family gathering or holiday feast, this Wholesome Dairy-Free Sweet Potato Casserole is the perfect choice. Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, this casserole is sure to please even the pickiest eaters. Plus, it’s easy to make and can be prepared ahead of time, making it a convenient dish for busy days. Ingredients 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed 1/2 cup coconut milk 1/4 cup maple syrup 2 tablespoons coconut oil 1 teaspoon cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg 1/4 teaspoon salt 1 cup chopped pecans 1/2 cup shredded coconut Instructions Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the sweet potato cubes in a large pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil and cook for about 15 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender. Drain the sweet potatoes and transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Add the coconut milk, maple syrup, coconut oil, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt to the sweet potatoes. Use a potato masher or an electric mixer to mash and mix the ingredients until smooth and well combined. In a separate bowl, combine the chopped pecans and shredded coconut. Grease a casserole dish with coconut oil and transfer the sweet potato mixture into the dish. Smooth the top with a spatula. Sprinkle the pecan and coconut mixture evenly over the top of the sweet potato mixture. Place the casserole dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the casserole is heated through. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Serving Suggestions This Wholesome Dairy-Free Sweet Potato Casserole is a versatile dish that can be served as a side dish for your holiday feast or as a main course for a cozy weeknight meal.
