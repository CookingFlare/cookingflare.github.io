Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipes for Classic Comfort


Are you craving a warm and comforting meal that will transport you back to your childhood? Look no further than these delicious slow cooker chicken and dumplings recipes. Made with tender chicken, fluffy dumplings, and a rich and flavorful broth, this classic comfort food is sure to satisfy your taste buds and warm your soul.

Classic Chicken and Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts, chicken broth, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper in a slow cooker. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 4-5 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  2. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, milk, and melted butter. Stir until a thick and sticky dough forms.

  4. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling dough into the slow cooker, on top of the chicken and vegetables. Cover and cook on high for an additional 1 hour, or until the dumplings are cooked through and fluffy.

  5. Remove the bay leaf from the slow cooker and season with additional salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy!

Creamy Chicken and Dumplings

If you’re looking for a creamy twist on the classic chicken and dumplings, this recipe is for you. The addition of cream cheese and heavy cream creates a luscious and indulgent dish that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken thighs, chicken broth, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper in a slow cooker. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 4-5 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  2. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  3. In a separate bowl, mix the softened cream cheese and heavy cream until smooth. Stir the cream cheese mixture into the slow cooker, combining it with the chicken and vegetables.

  4. In another mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, milk, and melted butter. Stir until a thick and sticky dough forms.

  5. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling dough into the slow cooker, on top of the chicken and creamy mixture. Cover and cook on high for an additional 1 hour, or until the dumplings are cooked through and fluffy.

  6. Remove the bay leaf from the slow cooker and season with additional salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy the creamy goodness!

Conclusion

These slow cooker chicken and dumplings recipes are the perfect way to indulge in classic comfort food without spending hours in the kitchen. The tender chicken, fluffy dumplings, and flavorful broth will warm your heart and bring a smile to your face. Whether you choose the classic version or the creamy twist, these recipes are guaranteed to become family favorites. So grab your slow cooker and get ready to enjoy a bowl of pure comfort.
