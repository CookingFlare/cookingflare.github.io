Spicy Instant Pot Thai Green Curry Chicken Recipes


If you’re looking to elevate your weeknight dinners with a punch of flavor, look no further than a Spicy Instant Pot Thai Green Curry Chicken. This dish not only tantalizes your taste buds with its complex flavors but also offers the convenience of being cooked in an Instant Pot, making it quicker and easier than traditional methods.

What Makes Thai Green Curry Special?

Thai green curry, known as “Gaeng Khiao Wan,” is a beloved dish in Thai cuisine characterized by its vibrant green color and aromatic spices. The green color comes from fresh green chilies blended with herbs like cilantro, basil, and lemongrass, creating a beautiful and fragrant base for the curry. The addition of coconut milk gives it a rich, creamy texture, while chicken adds protein to make this dish satisfying and nutritious.

Ingredients You’ll Need

For the Curry Paste

  • 3-4 green chilies (adjust based on spice preference)
  • 1 shallot, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass, finely chopped (white part only)
  • 1-2 Kaffir lime leaves (optional but recommended)
  • A handful of fresh cilantro (stems and leaves)
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar (preferably palm sugar)

For the Curry

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, or eggplant)
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice or noodles (for serving)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Green Curry Paste

  1. Blend the Ingredients: In a food processor, combine the green chilies, shallot, garlic, ginger, lemongrass, Kaffir lime leaves, cilantro, fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar. Blend until you achieve a smooth paste. If the mixture is too thick, add a splash of water to help it blend.

  2. Taste and Adjust: Taste the paste and adjust the seasoning. If you prefer more heat, add an extra chili. For a sweeter flavor, increase the sugar.

Step 2: Sauté the Chicken

  1. Set Up Your Instant Pot: Turn on your Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” function. Allow it to heat up for a few minutes.

  2. Add Oil: Pour in the vegetable oil and swirl it around to coat the bottom of the pot.

  3. Sear the Chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the pot and sauté for about 3-4 minutes until they start to brown. This step adds depth to the flavor.

Step 3: Add the Curry Paste

  1. Incorporate the Paste: Add the prepared green curry paste to the pot with the seared chicken. Stir well to ensure the chicken is coated with the paste.

  2. Cook the Mixture: Allow the paste to cook with the chicken for about 2-3 minutes, letting the flavors meld together.

Step 4: Add Liquids and Vegetables

  1. Pour in Coconut Milk and Broth: Add the can of coconut milk and chicken broth to the pot. Stir to combine everything thoroughly.

  2. Add Vegetables: Toss in your choice of mixed vegetables. The beauty of this dish is its versatility; feel free to use whatever you have on hand.

Step 5: Pressure Cook the Curry

  1. Seal the Pot: Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position.

  2. Set Cooking Time: Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” setting and set the timer for 10 minutes. The pot will take a few minutes to come to pressure before the cooking time begins.

Step 6: Release Pressure

  1. Quick Release: Once the cooking time is complete, carefully perform a quick release of the pressure by turning the valve to the venting position. Be cautious of the steam.

  2. Open the Lid: Once the pressure has fully released, open the lid. Your green curry should be bubbling and aromatic!

Step 7: Final Touches

  1. Taste and Season: Give your curry a taste. If needed, adjust the seasoning with more salt or a squeeze of lime juice for brightness.

  2. Garnish: Serve the curry hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Step 8: Serve

  1. Pair with Rice or Noodles: Spoon the spicy Thai green curry over a bed of jasmine rice or serve it with noodles for a hearty meal.

  2. Enjoy: Dive into this comforting, flavorful dish that brings a taste of Thailand straight to your kitchen!

Tips for the Best Thai Green Curry

  • Customize Your Veggies: Feel free to customize the vegetables based on what you have. Snow peas, carrots, and broccoli are wonderful additions.

  • Adjust Spice Levels: If you’re not a fan of too much heat, start with fewer chilies and gradually increase based on your tolerance.

  • Substitutions: If you’re looking for alternatives, you can easily substitute chicken with shrimp, tofu, or even beef. Just adjust the cooking times accordingly.

  • Make It Ahead: This curry tastes even better the next day! Consider making a big batch and reheating for lunch or dinner later in the week.

Nutritional Benefits

Not only is this Spicy Instant Pot Thai Green Curry Chicken delicious, but it also packs a nutritional punch. The chicken provides lean protein, while the coconut milk offers healthy fats. The herbs and vegetables contribute vitamins and minerals, making this dish both satisfying and nourishing.

Conclusion

This Spicy Instant Pot Thai Green Curry Chicken recipe is a fantastic addition to any weeknight dinner rotation. With its vibrant flavors, ease of preparation, and adaptability, it’s sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe will guide you through creating a restaurant-quality dish that you can enjoy in the comfort of your own home.

So, what are you waiting for? Grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure to Thailand with this spicy, aromatic green curry. Happy cooking!
