Comforting Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings Recipes


There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of chicken and dumplings on a chilly evening. The tender chicken, flavorful broth, and fluffy dumplings create the ultimate comfort food experience. And with the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have this classic dish on the table in no time. In this post, we’ll be sharing not just one, but three different variations of Instant Pot chicken and dumplings recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, let’s get cooking!

Classic Chicken and Dumplings

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some oil. Add the chicken thighs and cook until browned on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the diced onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the chicken broth, bay leaf, dried thyme, and the browned chicken thighs back to the pot. Give it a good stir.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual pressure cook for 10 minutes.
  6. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the dumplings. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the milk and melted butter, and stir until just combined.
  7. Once the chicken is done cooking, quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  8. Shred the chicken with two forks and return it to the pot.
  9. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter onto the chicken and broth mixture.
  10. Close the Instant Pot lid again and set it to manual pressure cook for 8 minutes.
  11. Once the dumplings are cooked, quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  12. Serve the chicken and dumplings hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

Spicy Southwest Chicken and Dumplings

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 jalapeños, seeded and minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, chili powder, and cumin.
  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some oil. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the diced onion, diced bell peppers, minced jalapeños, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the chicken broth, diced tomatoes with green chilies, and the browned chicken breasts back to the pot. Give it a good stir.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual pressure cook for 10 minutes.
  6. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the dumplings. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the milk and melted butter, and stir until just combined.
  7. Once the chicken is done cooking, quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  8. Shred the chicken with two forks and return it to the pot.
  9. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter onto the chicken and broth mixture.
  10. Close the Instant Pot lid again and set it to manual pressure cook for 8 minutes.
  11. Once the dumplings are cooked, quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  12. Serve the spicy Southwest chicken and dumplings hot, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired.

Creamy Mushroom and Leek Chicken and Dumplings

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 leeks, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some oil. Add the chicken thighs and cook until browned on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the sliced leeks, sliced mushrooms, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the chicken broth and the browned chicken thighs back to the pot. Give it a good stir.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual pressure cook for 10 minutes.
  6. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the dumplings. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the milk and melted butter, and stir until just combined.
  7. Once the chicken is done cooking, quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  8. Shred the chicken with two forks and return it to the pot.
  9. In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and all-purpose flour until smooth. Pour the mixture into the Instant Pot and stir well.
  10. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter onto the chicken and creamy broth mixture.
  11. Close the Instant Pot lid again and set it to manual pressure cook for 8 minutes.
  12. Once the dumplings are cooked, quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  13. Serve the creamy mushroom and leek chicken and dumplings hot, sprinkled with freshly ground black pepper if desired.

Whether you prefer the classic version, a spicy twist, or a creamy variation, these Instant Pot chicken and dumplings recipes are guaranteed to warm your soul. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to whip up a comforting and delicious meal that the whole family will love. Enjoy!
