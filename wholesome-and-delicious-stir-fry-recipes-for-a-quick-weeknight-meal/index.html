Wholesome and Delicious Stir-Fry Recipes for a Quick Weeknight Meal


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen after a long day at work? Look no further! Stir-fry recipes are the perfect solution for a quick and wholesome weeknight meal. Not only are they delicious, but they also offer a great way to incorporate a variety of vegetables and proteins into your diet. In this blog post, we will share three mouthwatering stir-fry recipes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds and save you time in the kitchen.

1. Teriyaki Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, honey, sesame oil, and minced garlic. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, broccoli, and snap peas. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet and pour the teriyaki sauce mixture over the ingredients. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.

2. Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, sliced into thin strips
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 1 minute, or until fragrant.
  3. Add the beef strips to the skillet and cook until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the broccoli florets and beef broth. Cover and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender-crisp.
  5. Return the beef to the skillet and pour the soy sauce mixture over the ingredients. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice.

3. Shrimp and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced zucchini
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute, or until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the sliced carrots, zucchini, mushrooms, and snow peas. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. Return the shrimp to the skillet and pour the soy sauce mixture over the ingredients. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  6. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.

There you have it - three wholesome and delicious stir-fry recipes that are perfect for a quick weeknight meal. Whether you prefer chicken, beef, or shrimp, these recipes offer a variety of flavors and textures to satisfy your cravings. So why spend hours in the kitchen when you can whip up a flavorful stir-fry in no time? Give these recipes a try and enjoy a tasty and hassle-free dinner tonight!
