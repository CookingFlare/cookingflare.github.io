Mouthwatering Mexican Flatbread Pizza Recipes


If you’re craving a delicious twist on traditional pizza, look no further than these mouthwatering Mexican flatbread pizza recipes. Packed with bold flavors and vibrant ingredients, these recipes will take your taste buds on a journey south of the border. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply want to spice up your weeknight dinner, these Mexican flatbread pizzas are sure to impress.

Recipe 1: Spicy Chicken Fajita Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made flatbread
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Place the flatbread on a baking sheet and spread the salsa evenly over the surface.

  3. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheese over the salsa.

  4. In a skillet, sauté the bell pepper and red onion until they are tender and slightly caramelized.

  5. Spread the cooked chicken evenly over the flatbread, followed by the sautéed bell pepper and red onion.

  6. Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheese over the top.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing.

  9. Serve hot, garnished with sour cream and fresh cilantro.

Recipe 2: Shrimp and Guacamole Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made flatbread
  • 1 cup cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup guacamole
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled queso fresco
  • Fresh lime wedges, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Place the flatbread on a baking sheet and spread the guacamole evenly over the surface.

  3. Arrange the cooked shrimp on top of the guacamole.

  4. Sprinkle the diced tomatoes and red onion over the shrimp.

  5. Crumble the queso fresco evenly over the top.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is lightly browned and the shrimp is heated through.

  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing.

  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh lime wedges.

Recipe 3: Vegetarian Black Bean and Corn Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made flatbread
  • 1 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Place the flatbread on a baking sheet and spread the black beans evenly over the surface.

  3. Sprinkle the corn, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, and red onion over the beans.

  4. Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the top.

  5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing.

  7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh cilantro and lime wedges.

These mouthwatering Mexican flatbread pizza recipes are easy to make and bursting with flavor. Whether you’re a meat lover, a seafood enthusiast, or a vegetarian, there’s a recipe here to suit your taste buds. So, why wait? Gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in a taste of Mexico right in your own kitchen. Enjoy!
