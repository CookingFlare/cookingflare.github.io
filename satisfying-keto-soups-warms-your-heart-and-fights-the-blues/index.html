Satisfying Keto Soups: Warms Your Heart and Fights the Blues


When the weather starts to cool, nothing beats the comfort of a steaming bowl of soup to warm your heart and chase away the blues. And when you’re following a keto diet, you might have wondered if you have to miss out on this satisfying meal. Well, wonder no more! In this blog post, we’ll share our favorite keto soup recipes that are satisfying, flavorful, and perfect for the transitional weather.

Creamy Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Nothing says comfort food like a creamy broccoli cheddar soup, and with a keto twist, you’ll feel right at home with this dish. This soup isn’t just pleasing to the palate, but it’s easy to whip up in no time for a quick dinner or lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 4 cups chopped broccoli
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese (for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and cook for 5 minutes until they’re soft.
  2. Add the chopped broccoli, chicken broth, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes or until broccoli is tender.
  3. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth. If you don’t have an immersion blender, you can transfer the soup to a traditional blender.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and shredded cheddar cheese until melted. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the soup with an extra sprinkle of grated cheddar cheese on top.

Tomato Basil Soup

If you’re a fan of the classic Italian combination of tomatoes and basil, then this tomato basil soup is sure to delight your taste buds. It’s a light yet satisfying soup that’s perfect for when you’re craving something warm and flavorful.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups diced fresh tomatoes (or 1 can of diced tomatoes)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking for about 5 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes and chicken broth, bringing the mixture to a boil. Reduce heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes.
  3. Take the pot off the heat and stir in the chopped basil leaves. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth.
  4. If desired, stir in the heavy cream for added richness. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Cauliflower Cheese Soup

For cauliflower lovers, this cauliflower cheese soup is a delightful keto-friendly dish. It features the mild and subtle flavor of cauliflower combined with a deliciously cheesy broth. It’s the perfect pick-me-up on a cold day.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chopped cauliflower
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheddar cheese (optional topping)

Instructions:

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and cook for 5 minutes until they’re soft.
  2. Add the chopped cauliflower and chicken broth, bringing the mixture to a boil. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes or until cauliflower is tender.
  3. Add the heavy cream and shredded cheddar cheese, stirring until melted and well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth. If desired, top with grated cheddar cheese before serving.

As you dive into these satisfying keto soup recipes, you might just find that low-carb eating doesn’t mean missing out on soul-soothing comfort. Experiment with different varieties and enjoy each bowl to its fullest – who knows, you might discover an all-new favorite! Let your taste buds dance to the tunes of delightful flavors and enjoy a heartwarming keto soup experience.
