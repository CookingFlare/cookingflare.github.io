Mediterranean Lamb Sliders: Bursting with Flavor

If you’re looking for a fresh and flavorful twist on classic sliders, these Mediterranean Lamb Sliders are sure to tantalize your taste buds. Bursting with the vibrant and aromatic flavors of the Mediterranean, these sliders are the perfect addition to any gathering, whether it’s a casual weeknight dinner or a festive backyard barbecue. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create these mouthwatering delights! Ingredients For the Lamb Patties: 1 pound ground lamb 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon Salt and pepper to taste For the Tzatziki Sauce: 1 cup Greek yogurt 1/2 cucumber, peeled, seeded, and finely grated 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped 1 clove garlic, minced 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil Salt and pepper to taste For Assembly: Mini slider buns Sliced tomatoes Sliced red onion Fresh lettuce leaves Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Lamb Patties In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground lamb, finely chopped red onion, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, coriander, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Gently mix the ingredients until well combined, being careful not to overwork the meat. Divide the mixture into small portions and shape them into mini patties, slightly larger than the size of the slider buns. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the lamb patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness. Step 2: Prepare the Tzatziki Sauce In a medium bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, grated cucumber, chopped dill, minced garlic, extra virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper. Stir the ingredients until well combined, and adjust the seasoning to taste. Step 3: Assemble the Sliders Slice the mini slider buns in half and lightly toast them on the grill or in the oven. Spread a generous dollop of the prepared tzatziki sauce on the bottom half of each slider bun. Place a cooked lamb patty on top of the tzatziki sauce. Add a slice of tomato, a few rings of red onion, and a fresh lettuce leaf. Top the sliders with the remaining half of the buns and secure them with toothpicks if needed. Step 4: Serve and Enjoy Plate the Mediterranean Lamb Sliders and serve them alongside your favorite sides, such as a crisp Greek salad, roasted vegetables, or seasoned fries. These sliders are best enjoyed with good company and a refreshing beverage. ...
