Indulge in the Creaminess of Middle Eastern Labneh Tart


If you’re a fan of creamy and indulgent desserts, then you’re in for a treat with this Middle Eastern Labneh Tart. Labneh, a thick and tangy yogurt cheese, is the star of this dessert, providing a luscious and velvety texture that will melt in your mouth. This recipe combines the rich flavors of labneh with a buttery crust, creating a dessert that is both elegant and comforting.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups of crushed digestive biscuits
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter

For the filling:

  • 2 cups of labneh
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of rosewater (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the topping:

  • Fresh berries of your choice
  • Honey for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a bowl, combine the crushed digestive biscuits and melted butter. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated with butter.

  3. Press the biscuit mixture into the bottom of a tart pan, spreading it out to form an even layer. Press firmly to ensure that the crust holds together.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the labneh, heavy cream, powdered sugar, rosewater (if using), and vanilla extract. Mix until the ingredients are well combined and the mixture is smooth.

  6. Pour the labneh mixture into the cooled crust, spreading it out evenly.

  7. Place the tart in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 4 hours, or overnight if possible. This will allow the labneh to set and the flavors to meld together.

  8. Once the tart has chilled, remove it from the refrigerator and decorate it with fresh berries of your choice. You can arrange them in a pattern or simply scatter them on top.

  9. Drizzle the tart with honey for a touch of sweetness and extra indulgence.

  10. Slice and serve the Middle Eastern Labneh Tart, savoring each creamy bite.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic labneh tart, you can add a layer of fruit preserves or compote between the crust and the labneh filling. This will add an extra burst of flavor and sweetness.

  • Experiment with different types of crusts to suit your taste. Graham crackers, shortbread cookies, or even a nut-based crust would all work well with the creamy labneh filling.

  • If you prefer a lighter version of this dessert, you can substitute Greek yogurt for the labneh. The result will still be delicious, but with a slightly different tang.

  • Serve the tart chilled for the best flavor and texture. The creamy labneh filling will be at its creamiest and most indulgent when cold.

Indulge in the Creaminess of Middle Eastern Labneh Tart and let its velvety smoothness transport you to a world of culinary delights. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a decadent dessert, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, take a bite, and savor the irresistible combination of creamy labneh, buttery crust, and sweet berries.
