Hearty Low Carb Beef and Cabbage Skillet


Looking for a delicious and satisfying low carb recipe that’s packed with flavor? Look no further! This Hearty Low Carb Beef and Cabbage Skillet is the perfect meal for those who want to enjoy a wholesome and nutritious dish without compromising on taste. With tender beef, nutrient-rich cabbage, and a medley of aromatic spices, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small head of cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain any excess fat, if needed.

  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the ground beef. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Add the thinly sliced cabbage to the skillet and stir well to combine. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the cabbage starts to soften.

  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes, including the juice, and tomato paste. Mix well to incorporate all the ingredients.

  5. Sprinkle in the paprika, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Give everything a good stir to evenly distribute the flavors.

  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let simmer for 15-20 minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the cabbage to become tender.

  7. Once the cabbage is cooked to your desired tenderness, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of freshness and color.

  8. Serve the Hearty Low Carb Beef and Cabbage Skillet hot, straight from the skillet. This dish can be enjoyed on its own as a complete meal or paired with a side of cauliflower rice or a crisp green salad.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Low Carb: This recipe is perfect for anyone following a low carb or keto lifestyle. It’s packed with protein and healthy fats while keeping the carb count low.

  • Nutrient-Rich: Cabbage is a cruciferous vegetable that’s loaded with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. It’s a great way to incorporate more nutrition into your diet.

  • Quick and Easy: With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep, this recipe comes together in no time. It’s perfect for busy weeknights when you want a wholesome meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

  • Versatile: Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. You can add additional vegetables like bell peppers or carrots, or spice it up with some crushed red pepper flakes for a kick of heat.

  • Leftovers: This dish tastes even better the next day, making it ideal for meal prepping. Simply store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator and reheat when ready to enjoy.

So if you’re craving a hearty and satisfying meal that won’t derail your low carb lifestyle, give this Beef and Cabbage Skillet a try. It’s a flavorful and nutritious dish that will keep you coming back for more. Enjoy!
