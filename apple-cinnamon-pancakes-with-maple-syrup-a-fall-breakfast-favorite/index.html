Apple Cinnamon Pancakes with Maple Syrup: A Fall Breakfast Favorite


Are you ready to indulge in a warm and comforting breakfast that perfectly captures the essence of fall? Look no further than these delicious Apple Cinnamon Pancakes with Maple Syrup. Packed with the flavors of juicy apples, aromatic cinnamon, and sweet maple syrup, these pancakes are sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup finely chopped apples
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon. This combination of dry ingredients will give your pancakes a fluffy texture and a delightful hint of warmth from the cinnamon.

  2. In a separate bowl, beat the egg lightly and then add the milk and melted butter. Whisk until well combined. The melted butter adds richness to the batter, while the milk provides the necessary moisture.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough pancakes. A few lumps are perfectly fine and will add to the texture of the pancakes.

  4. Gently fold in the chopped apples, making sure they are evenly distributed throughout the batter. The apples will provide a burst of natural sweetness and a pleasant crunch in every bite.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Grease the surface lightly with butter or cooking spray.

  6. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, pour the batter onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles start to form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown. Repeat with the remaining batter.

  7. Serve the Apple Cinnamon Pancakes warm, drizzled with maple syrup. The rich, amber syrup complements the flavors of the apples and cinnamon, creating a heavenly combination that will delight your taste buds.

Tips and Variations

  • To make these pancakes even more indulgent, you can add a handful of chopped walnuts or pecans to the batter. The nutty crunch will take these pancakes to a whole new level of deliciousness.

  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can blend the chopped apples into a chunky applesauce before adding them to the batter. This will distribute the apple flavor more evenly and create a softer pancake.

  • For a healthier twist, you can substitute whole wheat flour for all or part of the all-purpose flour. This will add a nutty flavor and increase the fiber content of the pancakes.

  • If you want to make these pancakes ahead of time, you can freeze them in an airtight container. When you’re ready to enjoy them, simply pop them in the toaster or microwave to reheat.

So, why wait for fall to savor the deliciousness of these Apple Cinnamon Pancakes with Maple Syrup? Whip up a batch today and treat yourself to a breakfast that will warm your soul and brighten your morning. Enjoy!
