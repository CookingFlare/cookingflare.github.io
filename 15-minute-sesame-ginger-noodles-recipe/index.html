15-Minute Sesame Ginger Noodles Recipe


If you’re a fan of quick and delicious meals, then this 15-minute sesame ginger noodles recipe is just what you need. Packed with flavor and simple to make, this dish is perfect for a busy weeknight or a lazy weekend. The combination of savory sesame and zesty ginger creates a delightful explosion of taste that will leave you craving more. Plus, with minimal cooking time, you can have this satisfying meal on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) of noodles of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup (150g) of mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, snap peas, and carrots)
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Noodles: Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, and rice vinegar. Set the mixture aside.

  3. Cook the Aromatics: In a large pan or wok, heat the sesame oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Add Vegetables: Add the mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.

  5. Combine Everything: Add the cooked noodles to the pan, followed by the prepared sauce. Toss everything together until the noodles are well coated with the sauce and the vegetables are evenly distributed.

  6. Finish and Serve: Sprinkle the sesame seeds and sliced green onions over the noodles. If you like a bit of heat, sprinkle some chili flakes as well. Serve the sesame ginger noodles hot and enjoy!

Tips for Success

  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this recipe to suit your taste. Broccoli, mushrooms, or baby corn can also be great additions.
  • If you prefer a protein-packed version, you can add some cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the dish.
  • To make this dish gluten-free, use tamari instead of soy sauce and ensure that the noodles are gluten-free as well.

Conclusion

This 15-minute sesame ginger noodles recipe is a fantastic addition to your repertoire of quick and tasty meals. The blend of sesame, ginger, and soy flavors combined with the satisfying texture of the noodles creates a dish that’s both comforting and exciting. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or friends, this recipe is sure to impress. Plus, with minimal clean-up and maximum flavor, it’s a win-win all around. Try it out and savor the delightful fusion of Asian-inspired goodness in every bite.

So, the next time you’re in need of a fast and flavorful meal, turn to this sesame ginger noodles recipe for a satisfying dining experience without the hassle. After all, great food doesn’t always have to take hours to prepare. Enjoy!
