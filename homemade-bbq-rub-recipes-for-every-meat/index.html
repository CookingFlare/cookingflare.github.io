Homemade BBQ Rub Recipes for Every Meat


Barbecue rubs are an essential component of the grilling process, adding depth of flavor and a delicious crust to your meats. They come in a myriad of varieties, from spicy to sweet, bold to mild. The following recipes for homemade BBQ rubs will transform your barbecues into Epicurean feasts. These versatile rubs are suitable for various meats, including chicken, beef, pork, and even seafood. Best of all, they require simple ingredients you likely already have at home!

Classic MBM 5-Spice Rub

This rub is a go-to option for most barbecue enthusiasts. With just five ingredients, you can shake up authentic taste every time:

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a small bowl, mixing well.
  2. Apply liberally to your preferred meat, ensuring a proper and even coating.
  3. Let the rub sit on the meat for at least an hour before placing on the grill.

Honey Mustard BBQ Rub

This sweet and tangy rub is irresistible on poultry:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup paprika
  • 1/4 cup garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup onion powder
  • 1/4 cup dried parsley
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon celery salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together apple cider vinegar, honey, and mustard until well-combined.
  3. Combine both mixtures together and coat your chicken or turkey heavily.
  4. Let the mixture sit for an hour before barbecuing.

Cajun BBQ Rub

Fire up some authentic Cajun flavor with this spice blend:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup paprika
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup onion powder
  • 2 tablespoons black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Apply generously to your meat and let it sit for at least two hours before barbecuing.
  3. Grill to perfection for the authentic Cajun experience.

Teriyaki BBQ Rub

This exotic rub brings a rich taste of the East to your barbecue:

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried ginger
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon black sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Stir the rice vinegar into the powdered mix.
  3. Coat your favorite meats liberally and let sit for at least an hour.
  4. Grill to perfection for a fantastic East-meets-West barbecue experience.

Classic BBQ Five-Legged Cow Rub

This rub is equal parts robust and unique. Give it a try for something different:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup paprika
  • 1 cup garlic powder
  • 1 cup onion powder
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cowboy beef seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Apply to meats of all kinds before barbecuing.
  3. Enjoy the robust flavor of the one-of-a-kind five-legged cow BBQ rub.

Homemade BBQ rubs are a boon to any barbecue. They invite experimentation and cater to diverse tastes. So mix up one or several of these recipes, and be prepared for the most delicious barbecues of your life. Happy grilling!
