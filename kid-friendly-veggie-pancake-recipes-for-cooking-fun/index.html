Kid-Friendly Veggie Pancake Recipes for Cooking Fun


Pancakes are a breakfast favorite in many households, but they can also be a fantastic way to sneak in some veggies for the little ones. In this blog post, we’re going to explore some creative and delicious kid-friendly veggie pancake recipes that will not only make cooking fun but also encourage your kids to enjoy their vegetables in a whole new way.

Veggie-packed Spinach Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Start by blending the fresh spinach and milk in a blender until smooth.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. Add the blended spinach mixture, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.

These vibrant green pancakes are not only visually appealing but also packed with the goodness of spinach, making them a nutritious and delicious breakfast option for kids.

Carrot Cake Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup finely grated carrots

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and brown sugar.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the buttermilk, egg, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, then fold in the grated carrots.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and scoop 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until golden brown on both sides.

These carrot cake pancakes are a delightful way to incorporate carrots into your kids’ diet while providing the cozy flavors of a classic carrot cake.

Sweet Potato Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk
  • 1 cup mashed sweet potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and brown sugar.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, mashed sweet potatoes, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until golden brown on both sides.

These sweet potato pancakes are not only naturally sweet and delicious but also provide a healthy dose of vitamin A, making them a wonderful addition to your kids’ breakfast menu.

Conclusion

Cooking with kids can be a fun and educational experience, and these veggie pancake recipes offer a great way to introduce nutritious ingredients in a format that kids will love. Experiment with different veggies and let your kids get involved in the cooking process to make it an enjoyable and memorable experience for the whole family. So, get ready to flip some veggie-packed pancakes and watch your little ones enjoy every bite!
