Savory Low Carb Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious low-carb meal that’s bursting with flavor, then this savory garlic butter shrimp and broccoli recipe is just what you need. Packed with protein and healthy fats, this dish is not only satisfying but also incredibly easy to make. The combination of succulent shrimp, tender broccoli, and a rich garlic butter sauce creates a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cups of fresh broccoli florets
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly chopped parsley for garnish
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a hint of heat)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp and Broccoli:

    • Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning them with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set them aside.

  2. Sauté the Broccoli:

    • In the same skillet, add the broccoli florets and sauté them for 3-4 minutes until they become bright green and slightly tender. Season the broccoli with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce:

    • Reduce the heat to medium, then add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.
    • Add the unsalted butter to the skillet and allow it to melt, swirling it around with the garlic to infuse the flavors.

  4. Combine and Serve:

    • Once the butter is melted, return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss them with the garlic butter sauce.
    • Stir everything together until the shrimp and broccoli are coated in the luscious garlic butter sauce.
    • Sprinkle with freshly chopped parsley and red pepper flakes if desired.
    • Serve the savory low carb garlic butter shrimp and broccoli hot, and enjoy the delightful flavors!

Nutritional Information:

  • This savory low carb garlic butter shrimp and broccoli dish is not only delicious but also nutritious. With the goodness of protein from the shrimp and the vitamins and fiber from the broccoli, it’s a well-balanced meal that fits perfectly into a low-carb lifestyle. The healthy fats from the butter and olive oil add richness to the dish, making it a satisfying and wholesome option for any mealtime.

Conclusion:

In just a few simple steps, you can create a delightful and nourishing meal that’s perfect for a busy weeknight or a leisurely weekend dinner. This savory low carb garlic butter shrimp and broccoli recipe is a testament to the fact that healthy eating doesn’t have to be bland or boring. With the right combination of ingredients and flavors, you can elevate your low-carb cooking game to new heights. So, grab your skillet and get ready to indulge in a dish that’s as wholesome as it is delicious.
