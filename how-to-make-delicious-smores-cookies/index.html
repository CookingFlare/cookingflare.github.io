How to Make Delicious S'mores Cookies


When you think of s’mores, visions of cozy campfires and gooey marshmallows sandwiched between chocolate and graham crackers might come to mind. But what if you could bring those nostalgic flavors into a cookie that combines everything you love about s’mores into a bite-sized treat? S’mores cookies are a creative twist on the traditional dessert, offering a perfect balance of crisp edges, soft centers, melted chocolate, and toasted marshmallow goodness.

In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to prepare these irresistible cookies from scratch. Whether you’re baking these for a family gathering, a picnic, or simply to satisfy a sweet craving, this recipe delivers unforgettable flavor and texture in every bite.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make these delicious s’mores cookies, you’ll need a mix of pantry staples and the key ingredients that make s’mores so iconic. Here’s what you’ll need:

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup finely crushed graham crackers (about 5 whole crackers)

Wet Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup light brown sugar (packed)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Mix-ins:

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup mini marshmallows (plus extra for topping)

Prep Work: Setting the Stage

Before diving into the recipe, preparation is key to making flawless s’mores cookies. Follow these steps to set yourself up for success:

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line two baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats.
  2. Crush the Graham Crackers: Place graham crackers in a resealable plastic bag and crush them into fine crumbs using a rolling pin. Alternatively, pulse them in a food processor for quicker results.
  3. Measure All Ingredients: Gather and measure all your ingredients. This step helps ensure you’re not scrambling for anything mid-recipe.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, and crushed graham crackers until evenly combined. This dry mixture provides the base for the cookies and ensures they’ll have the crunch and texture reminiscent of s’mores.

2. Cream Butter and Sugars

In a large mixing bowl, use a hand mixer or stand mixer to beat the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This process should take about 2-3 minutes and contributes to the cookies’ soft, chewy texture.

3. Add the Egg and Vanilla

Beat in the egg and vanilla extract to the butter-sugar mixture. Mix until fully incorporated, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed. These wet ingredients add richness and enhance the overall flavor profile.

4. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing on low speed until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can make the cookies dense rather than tender.

5. Fold in the Mix-Ins

Using a spatula, gently fold in the chocolate chips and mini marshmallows. Ensure the mix-ins are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

Pro Tip: Chill the Dough

For best results, chill the dough in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before baking. Chilling helps solidify the butter, preventing the cookies from spreading too much while baking. If you’re short on time, you can skip this step, but the texture of the finished cookie may vary slightly.

Baking the Cookies

1. Scoop and Shape

Use a cookie scoop or tablespoon to portion the dough onto the prepared baking sheets. Space the dough balls about 2 inches apart to allow room for spreading. Before placing them in the oven, press a few extra mini marshmallows on top of each dough ball for a visually appealing finish.

2. Bake

Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are golden brown and the marshmallows on top are toasted. Keep an eye on them during the last few minutes—those marshmallows can brown quickly.

3. Cool

Remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for around 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack. This step ensures they set properly without losing their delicate structure.

Serving Suggestions

S’mores cookies are best enjoyed warm, straight out of the oven, when the chocolate chips and marshmallows are perfectly gooey. However, they’re just as delightful at room temperature and make the ideal treat for any occasion. Serve them with a cold glass of milk, hot cocoa, or even over a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a decadent dessert.

Storage Tips

If you have leftovers (unlikely but possible!), store your s’mores cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. To reheat and refresh their gooey texture, pop them in the microwave for 10-15 seconds.

Customizing Your S’mores Cookies

One of the best things about making s’mores cookies is that they’re incredibly versatile. Here are some fun variations to try:

  • Add Peanut Butter: Drizzle melted peanut butter over the cookies for a sweet-savory addition.
  • Use Dark Chocolate: Swap the semi-sweet chocolate chips for dark chocolate chunks to add richness.
  • Go Nutty: Stir in chopped pecans, walnuts, or almonds for extra crunch.
  • Try a Stuffed Variation: Wrap the cookie dough around a piece of chocolate bar (like Hershey’s) for a surprise in the center.

Bringing Campfire Magic Indoors

S’mores cookies take the classic campfire treat and transform it into a portable, crowd-pleasing dessert that everyone will love. The combination of buttery cookie dough, crunchy graham cracker crumbs, rich chocolate, and gooey marshmallows creates a flavor explosion that’s impossible to resist.

Whether you’re reminiscing about summer nights or introducing a younger crowd to the joys of s’mores, this recipe is sure to spark smiles around the table. Give it a try, and prepare to fall in love with this nostalgic yet innovative take on a beloved classic!
