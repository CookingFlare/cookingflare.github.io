Hearty and Flavorful: Middle Eastern Chicken Kebab Skewers


If you’re looking to add a burst of flavor to your grilling repertoire, then look no further than these Middle Eastern Chicken Kebab Skewers. Packed with aromatic spices and tender chicken, these kebabs are the perfect addition to any summer barbecue or weeknight dinner. So fire up the grill and get ready to tantalize your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for extra heat)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, cinnamon, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.

  2. Cut the chicken breasts into 1-inch cubes and add them to the marinade. Toss the chicken until it is well-coated with the marinade. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  4. Thread the marinated chicken onto the soaked wooden skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each piece. This will ensure even cooking.

  5. Place the chicken skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred on the outside. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (75°C).

  6. Once cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes to allow the juices to redistribute. Serve the Middle Eastern Chicken Kebab Skewers hot with your favorite side dishes or pita bread.

Serving Suggestions

These Middle Eastern Chicken Kebab Skewers pair perfectly with a variety of sides and sauces. Here are a few suggestions to complete your meal:

  • Mediterranean Couscous Salad: Whip up a refreshing couscous salad with chopped tomatoes, cucumbers, parsley, lemon juice, and olive oil. The light and tangy flavors will complement the savory kebabs beautifully.

  • Garlic Yogurt Sauce: Prepare a simple yet delicious garlic yogurt sauce by combining plain Greek yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. This creamy sauce adds a cooling element to balance out the spices in the kebabs.

  • Grilled Vegetables: Take advantage of the grill and cook some vibrant vegetables alongside the chicken kebabs. Bell peppers, zucchini, and red onions work particularly well. Simply toss them in olive oil, salt, and pepper before grilling until tender and slightly charred.

Conclusion

These Middle Eastern Chicken Kebab Skewers are a fantastic way to introduce the vibrant flavors of the Middle East to your next grilling session. With their tender chicken and aromatic spices, they are sure to be a hit with family and friends. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the bold flavors of this mouthwatering dish. Enjoy!
