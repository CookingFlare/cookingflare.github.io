Mouthwatering Greek Chicken Souvlaki: A Flavorful Grilled Dish


If there’s one dish that captures the essence of Greek cuisine, it’s the mouthwatering Greek Chicken Souvlaki. This flavorful grilled dish is a staple of Greek street food and a favorite at family gatherings. The combination of tender, marinated chicken, fresh vegetables, and aromatic herbs and spices makes it a true delight for the senses.

Ingredients:

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Tzatziki Sauce:

  • 1 cup of Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cucumber, grated and drained
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For Serving:

  • Pita bread
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onions
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Add the chicken chunks to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to infuse the chicken.

  2. While the chicken is marinating, prepare the tzatziki sauce. In a medium bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, grated and drained cucumber, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, fresh dill, salt, and black pepper. Mix well and refrigerate until ready to serve.

  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken chunks onto skewers, alternating with pieces of sliced red onions and bell peppers if desired.

  4. Grill the chicken skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice charred exterior.

  5. Warm the pita bread on the grill for a minute on each side.

  6. To serve, place a few pieces of grilled chicken onto a piece of warm pita bread. Add a dollop of tzatziki sauce, sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Tips for Perfect Souvlaki:

  • For extra flavor, consider marinating the chicken overnight for a more intense taste.
  • Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes before threading the chicken to prevent them from burning on the grill.
  • If using metal skewers, be sure to handle them with care as they can get very hot on the grill.

Enjoy the Authentic Greek Experience:

The Greek Chicken Souvlaki is more than just a dish – it’s a sensory experience that transports you to the vibrant streets of Greece. Whether you’re enjoying it as a quick meal on a busy weeknight or serving it at a summer barbecue, this flavorful grilled dish never fails to impress. The tender, juicy chicken combined with the cool, refreshing tzatziki sauce and the crisp, fresh vegetables create a harmony of flavors that will have you coming back for more. So, fire up the grill and bring a taste of Greece to your table with this classic Greek Chicken Souvlaki recipe. Opa!
