Elegant Valentine's Day Dinner Party Recipes to Impress Your Guests


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and you’re planning a dinner party to impress your loved ones. You want to create a memorable evening with exquisite dishes that will leave your guests in awe. Look no further! In this article, we’ll provide you with three elegant Valentine’s Day dinner party recipes that are sure to impress.

Amuse-Bouche: Heirloom Tomato Consommé

Start your Valentine’s Day dinner party with an amuse-bouche that will awaken your guests’ taste buds. Heirloom Tomato Consommé is a delicate and flavorful soup that sets the tone for a sophisticated evening.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heirloom tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Add the diced heirloom tomatoes and cook until they start to release their juices.
  4. Add the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the consommé in demitasse cups and garnish with chopped fresh basil leaves, if desired.

Appetizer: Seared Scallops with Microgreens and Lemon-Garlic Butter

The next course is Seared Scallops with Microgreens and Lemon-Garlic Butter, a dish that combines the sweetness of scallops with the brightness of lemon and the pungency of garlic. This recipe is sure to impress your guests with its simplicity and elegance.

Ingredients:

  • 12 large scallops
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup microgreens (such as pea shoots or purslane)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a skillet over high heat.
  2. Season the scallops with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the softened butter to the skillet and let it melt.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add the scallops to the skillet and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through.
  6. Remove the scallops from the skillet and place them on a plate.
  7. Stir in the lemon juice and microgreens.
  8. Serve the scallops with the lemon-garlic butter spooned over the top.

Main Course: Filet Mignon with Roasted Garlic Mashed Potatoes and Broccolini

The main event of your Valentine’s Day dinner party is Filet Mignon with Roasted Garlic Mashed Potatoes and Broccolini. This dish is a classic combination of tender filet mignon, creamy mashed potatoes, and crunchy broccolini.

Ingredients:

  • 4 filet mignon steaks, 6 ounces each
  • 2 large garlic bulbs, separated into individual cloves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 bunch broccolini, trimmed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the garlic cloves on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Roast the garlic in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and caramelized.
  4. Remove the garlic from the oven and let it cool.
  5. In a large saucepan, combine the chicken broth and heavy cream.
  6. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until reduced by half.
  7. Add the roasted garlic to the saucepan and stir to combine.
  8. Add the filet mignon steaks to the saucepan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until cooked to desired doneness.
  9. Serve the filet mignon with the roasted garlic mashed potatoes and broccolini.

Dessert: Chocolate Lava Cake with Salted Caramel Sauce

No Valentine’s Day dinner party would be complete without a decadent dessert. Chocolate Lava Cake with Salted Caramel Sauce is a rich and indulgent treat that will satisfy your guests’ sweet tooth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces high-quality dark chocolate, broken into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup salted caramel sauce (see below for recipe)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, melt the chocolate in the microwave in 30-second increments, stirring between each interval, until smooth.
  4. Add the softened butter to the melted chocolate and stir until combined.
  5. Add the eggs one at a time, stirring well after each addition.
  6. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  7. Pour the batter into 4 ramekins or small cups.
  8. Bake the cakes for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are set and the centers are still slightly jiggly.
  9. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool for 1 minute.
  10. Serve the cakes with a scoop of salted caramel sauce.

Salted Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the heavy cream, sugar, and sea salt.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  4. Remove the sauce from the heat and stir in the butter and vanilla extract.
  5. Let the sauce cool to room temperature before serving.

Conclusion

With these elegant Valentine’s Day dinner party recipes, you’ll be sure to impress your guests with your culinary skills. From the delicate Heirloom Tomato Consommé to the rich Chocolate Lava Cake with Salted Caramel Sauce, each dish is a masterpiece of flavor and presentation. So why not give these recipes a try and make this Valentine’s Day one to remember?
