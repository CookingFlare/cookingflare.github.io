Quick and Easy Thai Peanut Curry


Craving a flavorful and comforting meal that’s quick to make? Look no further than this delicious Quick and Easy Thai Peanut Curry recipe! Packed with the perfect blend of creamy peanut butter, aromatic Thai spices, and tender vegetables, this curry is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a cooking novice, this recipe is a breeze to follow and guarantees a mouthwatering result every time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 medium carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Aromatics: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.

  2. Add Vegetables: Toss in the sliced red bell pepper, broccoli florets, and thinly sliced carrot. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: In a bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, vegetable broth, soy sauce, red curry paste, peanut butter, and brown sugar until smooth.

  4. Combine Ingredients: Pour the prepared sauce into the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Stir well to combine everything evenly.

  5. Simmer: Let the curry simmer for about 10-15 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked to your liking and the flavors have melded together.

  6. Season to Taste: Taste the curry and season with salt and pepper as needed. Adjust the spiciness or sweetness according to your preference.

  7. Serve: Serve the Thai Peanut Curry hot over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor.

  8. Enjoy: Dive into a bowl of this aromatic and creamy curry, savoring each bite of the rich and comforting flavors.

Tips for Success:

  • Feel free to customize this curry by adding your favorite protein such as chicken, tofu, or shrimp.
  • For an extra kick of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes or drizzle with sriracha sauce before serving.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat on the stovetop or in the microwave before enjoying.

With this Quick and Easy Thai Peanut Curry recipe, you can whip up a restaurant-quality dish in the comfort of your own kitchen in no time. Impress your family and friends with this flavorful and aromatic curry that’s perfect for any day of the week. Happy cooking!
