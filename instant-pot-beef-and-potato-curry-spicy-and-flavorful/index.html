Instant Pot Beef and Potato Curry: Spicy and Flavorful


Curries are a staple in many cuisines around the world, and for good reason. They offer a beautiful combination of flavors, spices, and textures that can transport you to different parts of the globe with just one bite. Today, I want to share with you a recipe for Instant Pot Beef and Potato Curry that is not only spicy and flavorful but also incredibly easy to make. Let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of beef, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by heating your Instant Pot on the Saute function. Add a little oil and saute the chopped onion until translucent.

  2. Once the onion is cooked, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Saute for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the beef to the Instant Pot and brown it on all sides. This will help seal in the flavors and give the curry a rich taste.

  4. Now, it’s time to add the spices. Add the curry powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and chili powder to the Instant Pot. Stir well to coat the beef and onions with the spices.

  5. Next, add the chopped tomatoes and beef broth to the pot. Stir everything together and season with salt to taste.

  6. Lock the lid of the Instant Pot in place and set it to Manual mode, High Pressure. Cook the curry for 20 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes. Then, carefully do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Open the Instant Pot and add the cubed potatoes and coconut milk. Give everything a good stir, making sure the potatoes are well-coated with the curry sauce.

  9. Lock the lid back in place and set the Instant Pot to Manual mode, High Pressure, for another 5 minutes.

  10. After the second cooking cycle, do a quick release of the pressure. Open the Instant Pot and give the curry a final stir.

  11. Serve the Instant Pot Beef and Potato Curry over steamed rice or with some warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder curry, adjust the amount of chili powder to suit your taste.
  • Feel free to add vegetables like carrots, peas, or bell peppers to the curry for added color and nutrition.
  • For a creamier curry, you can substitute some of the coconut milk with heavy cream or yogurt.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors will continue to develop, making it even more delicious the next day.

So there you have it, a delicious and easy recipe for Instant Pot Beef and Potato Curry. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have a flavorful curry on the table in no time. Enjoy the aromatic spices, tender beef, and creamy potatoes in every bite. Happy cooking!
