Falling in Love Fettuccine with Pesto Cream Sauce and Cherry Tomatoes and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil and Garlic: A Valentine's Day Pasta Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate love than with a delicious homemade meal? This Falling in Love Fettuccine with Pesto Cream Sauce and Cherry Tomatoes is the perfect dish to impress your special someone. The combination of flavors and textures in this pasta dish is sure to make your Valentine’s Day dinner unforgettable.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz. fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pesto: In a food processor, combine the basil, garlic, pine nuts, and Parmesan cheese. With the processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture is smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  2. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. Make the Pesto Cream Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the pesto over medium heat. Pour in the heavy cream and stir until the sauce is heated through.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet with the pesto cream sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly.

  5. Add the Cherry Tomatoes: Gently fold in the halved cherry tomatoes, allowing them to warm through but not cook completely.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the fettuccine to serving plates and garnish with additional Parmesan cheese and fresh basil, if desired.

Why This Recipe Is Perfect for Valentine’s Day:

This Falling in Love Fettuccine with Pesto Cream Sauce and Cherry Tomatoes is a celebration of love on a plate. The vibrant colors of the cherry tomatoes, the aromatic basil, and the rich, creamy pesto sauce come together to create a visually stunning and mouthwatering dish. The flavors are balanced and luxurious, making it an ideal choice for a romantic dinner for two.

Pairing Suggestions:

To complement the flavors of this pasta dish, consider serving it with a light, crisp white wine such as Pinot Grigio or Sauvignon Blanc. For a complete meal, start with a fresh green salad dressed with a simple vinaigrette and finish with a decadent chocolate dessert.

Final Thoughts:

This Valentine’s Day, take the time to create a memorable dining experience for your loved one with this Falling in Love Fettuccine. The process of preparing this dish together can be just as enjoyable as savoring the final result. Whether you’re celebrating with a partner, family, or friends, this pasta recipe is a delightful way to show your love through food.

Remember, the key to a successful Valentine’s Day meal is not just the food itself, but the love and care you put into preparing it. So, put on your favorite apron, pour a glass of wine, and let the magic of this Falling in Love Fettuccine dish bring joy to your Valentine’s Day celebration.
