Citrus Burst: Homemade Lemon Curd Recipes to Brighten Your Day


Are you looking to add a zesty and vibrant touch to your desserts or breakfast spreads? Look no further than homemade lemon curd! Bursting with the fresh and tangy flavors of lemons, this delightful spread is a versatile addition to your culinary repertoire. In this blog post, we will explore different homemade lemon curd recipes that will surely brighten your day and elevate your dishes to the next level.

Classic Lemon Curd Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4–5 lemons (for zest and juice)
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 4 large eggs

Instructions:

  1. Zest and juice the lemons, making sure to strain the juice to remove any seeds.
  2. In a saucepan, whisk together the lemon zest, lemon juice, sugar, and butter over low heat until the butter melts.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and gradually add them to the lemon mixture while whisking constantly.
  4. Cook the mixture over low heat, stirring continuously, until it thickens to a custard-like consistency.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool before transferring to a jar. Refrigerate before serving.

This classic lemon curd recipe is perfect for spreading on toast, filling tarts, or swirling into yogurt for a refreshing treat.

Vegan Lemon Curd Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4–5 lemons (for zest and juice)
  • 1 cup of coconut sugar
  • 1/2 cup of coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup of full-fat coconut milk

Instructions:

  1. Zest and juice the lemons as in the classic recipe.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the lemon zest, lemon juice, coconut sugar, coconut oil, and coconut milk. Heat over low heat until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk a small amount of the hot mixture with cornstarch to create a slurry. Add it back to the saucepan.
  4. Cook the mixture over low heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool before transferring to a jar. Chill before serving.

This vegan-friendly lemon curd is rich, creamy, and maintains that signature tanginess that will dazzle your taste buds.

Lemon Curd Tart Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-baked tart shell
  • Homemade lemon curd
  • Fresh berries for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill the pre-baked tart shell with the prepared lemon curd.
  2. Chill the tart in the refrigerator until set.
  3. Top with fresh berries like raspberries or blueberries before serving.

This lemon curd tart is a show-stopping dessert that combines the buttery crust with the luscious lemon curd for a delightful treat that is both sweet and tangy.

Whether you prefer the traditional, vegan, or tart variation, these homemade lemon curd recipes are sure to bring a burst of sunshine to your plate. Experiment with different uses for lemon curd, such as layering it in cakes, drizzling it over pancakes, or even filling macarons. Get creative in the kitchen and let the bright flavors of lemon elevate your culinary creations. Enjoy the citrusy goodness and let it brighten your day with every spoonful!

Remember, a touch of lemon can bring a burst of flavor to any dish!
