Quick and Easy: Gluten-Free Chicken Enchiladas

3 min · 493 words

If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free weeknight meal, these Quick and Easy Gluten-Free Chicken Enchiladas are just what you need. Packed with flavor and simple to make, these enchiladas are sure to become a family favorite. And the best part? They’re completely gluten-free!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup salsa verde
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • 8 gluten-free tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant, about 3 minutes.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Sprinkle this spice mixture over both sides of the chicken breasts.

  4. Move the sautéed onions and garlic to one side of the skillet, and add the seasoned chicken breasts to the other side. Cook the chicken for about 5 minutes on each side, or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center.

  5. Remove the cooked chicken from the skillet and shred it using two forks.

  6. Return the shredded chicken to the skillet and add the salsa verde. Stir well to combine, and cook for an additional 2 minutes to heat through.

  7. Spread a thin layer of salsa verde on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  8. Warm the gluten-free tortillas according to the package instructions to make them easier to roll. Spoon about 1/4 cup of the chicken mixture onto each tortilla, and roll them up tightly. Place the rolled enchiladas seam-side down in the baking dish.

  9. Pour the remaining salsa verde over the top of the enchiladas, spreading it evenly. Sprinkle the shredded Mexican blend cheese over the salsa verde.

  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  11. Remove the enchiladas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Quick and Easy Gluten-Free Chicken Enchiladas are delicious on their own, but feel free to serve them with your favorite toppings. Sour cream, guacamole, and diced tomatoes all make great additions. You can also serve them with a side of Mexican rice or a fresh green salad for a complete meal.

Conclusion

With just a handful of ingredients and minimal prep time, these Quick and Easy Gluten-Free Chicken Enchiladas make the perfect weeknight dinner. They’re packed with flavor, gluten-free, and sure to please even the pickiest eaters. Give this recipe a try and enjoy a tasty and satisfying meal in no time!

Remember, cooking should be fun and stress-free. Feel free to experiment with different fillings and toppings to make these enchiladas your own. Happy cooking!
