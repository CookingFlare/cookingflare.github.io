Light and Refreshing: Greek Watermelon and Feta Salad


When it comes to summer, there’s nothing quite like a light and refreshing salad to satisfy your cravings and keep you cool. One of my all-time favorite summer salads is the Greek Watermelon and Feta Salad. This delightful dish combines the sweetness of watermelon with the salty tang of feta cheese, creating a flavor explosion that will leave your taste buds dancing.

Ingredients

To make this delicious Greek Watermelon and Feta Salad, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 cups of cubed watermelon
  • 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Watermelon: Start by cutting the watermelon into bite-sized cubes. Make sure to remove any seeds and place the cubed watermelon in a large mixing bowl.

  2. Add the Feta: Crumble the feta cheese over the cubed watermelon. The combination of sweet watermelon and salty feta is what makes this salad so irresistible.

  3. Toss in the Fresh Mint: Add the chopped fresh mint leaves to the bowl. The mint adds a burst of freshness and complements the sweetness of the watermelon.

  4. Add the Red Onion: Thinly slice the red onion and toss it into the bowl. The sharpness of the onion provides a nice contrast to the sweetness of the watermelon and the saltiness of the feta.

  5. Drizzle with Olive Oil and Balsamic Vinegar: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Drizzle this dressing over the salad and gently toss to ensure everything is evenly coated.

  6. Season to Taste: Finally, season the salad with salt and pepper to taste. Be mindful of the salt content in the feta cheese before adding too much salt.

  7. Chill and Serve: Once the salad is well mixed, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. When you’re ready to serve, give the salad a quick toss and enjoy the refreshing combination of flavors.

Why You’ll Love This Salad

The Greek Watermelon and Feta Salad is more than just a delightful combination of flavors; it’s also a nutritional powerhouse. Watermelon is not only incredibly hydrating, but it’s also packed with vitamins A and C, making it a perfect summer fruit. Feta cheese adds a dose of calcium and protein, while the fresh mint and red onion contribute antioxidants and other essential nutrients.

This salad is also incredibly versatile. You can serve it as a side dish at a barbecue, bring it to a potluck, or enjoy it as a light lunch on a hot day. The combination of sweet, salty, and savory flavors makes it a crowd-pleaser for all occasions.

So, the next time you’re looking for a light and refreshing dish to beat the summer heat, give this Greek Watermelon and Feta Salad a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and you’ll have a new go-to recipe for all your summer gatherings.

This Greek Watermelon and Feta Salad is a refreshing and flavorful dish that’s perfect for summer. The combination of sweet watermelon, salty feta, and tangy balsamic dressing makes it a delightful addition to any meal. Plus, it’s packed with nutrients and is incredibly easy to prepare. Whether you’re hosting a barbecue or simply looking for a light lunch, this salad is sure to impress.
