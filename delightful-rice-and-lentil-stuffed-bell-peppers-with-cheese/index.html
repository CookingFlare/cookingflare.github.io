Delightful Rice and Lentil Stuffed Bell Peppers with Cheese


There’s something inherently satisfying about a dish that combines wholesome ingredients with vibrant flavors, and these Rice and Lentil Stuffed Bell Peppers with Cheese are no exception. Packed with protein-rich lentils, fluffy rice, and gooey melted cheese, they offer a delightful culinary experience that’s both comforting and nutritious.

Ingredients

To recreate this flavorful dish, gather the following ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup brown rice, cooked
  • 1 cup lentils, cooked (you can use canned lentils for convenience)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella work well)
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Bell Peppers

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes from inside. Rinse them thoroughly under cold water and set aside.

Step 2: Cook the Filling

In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, sautéing until they become translucent and fragrant.

Step 3: Add Rice and Lentils

Stir in the cooked brown rice and lentils into the skillet with the onions and garlic. Season with cumin powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly combined and heated through.

Step 4: Stuff the Peppers

Place the hollowed-out bell peppers upright in a baking dish. Carefully spoon the rice and lentil mixture into each pepper, pressing gently to pack it in. Fill each pepper to the top, ensuring they are evenly stuffed.

Step 5: Add Cheese and Bake

Sprinkle shredded cheese generously on top of each stuffed pepper, covering the filling. Cover the baking dish loosely with aluminum foil and place it in the preheated oven.

Step 6: Bake Until Golden and Tender

Bake the stuffed bell peppers for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the cheese is melted and golden brown on top.

Step 7: Garnish and Serve

Once done, remove the baking dish from the oven. Garnish the stuffed peppers with freshly chopped parsley or cilantro for a pop of color and freshness.

Enjoy Your Meal!

Serve these Rice and Lentil Stuffed Bell Peppers with Cheese hot, either as a wholesome main dish or a satisfying side. The combination of tender bell peppers, hearty rice and lentils, and melted cheese creates a harmony of flavors and textures that is sure to please everyone at the table. Whether you’re looking for a meatless Monday meal or simply craving a comforting dish packed with nutrients, these stuffed peppers deliver on all fronts.

Tips for Variation

  • Protein Boost: Add cooked ground meat or crumbled tofu to the filling mixture for an extra protein punch.
  • Vegetarian Delight: Use vegan cheese or omit the cheese altogether for a dairy-free and vegan-friendly version.
  • Spice It Up: Customize the seasoning with your favorite herbs and spices like oregano, thyme, or chili flakes for a spicier kick.

With these versatile and delicious Rice and Lentil Stuffed Bell Peppers with Cheese, you can elevate your dinner table with a dish that’s both satisfying and nutritious. Experiment with different fillings and seasonings to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a gathering, these stuffed peppers are sure to become a favorite go-to recipe.
