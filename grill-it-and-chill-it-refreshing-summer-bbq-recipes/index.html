Grill It and Chill It: Refreshing Summer BBQ Recipes


Summer is here, and that means it’s time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious BBQ recipes. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout or just looking for a tasty meal to enjoy outdoors, these refreshing summer BBQ recipes are sure to impress your family and friends. From juicy burgers to flavorful grilled vegetables, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So, grab your apron and let’s get grilling!

1. Grilled Watermelon and Feta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 small watermelon, sliced into wedges
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Handful of fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Drizzle of balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the watermelon wedges on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until grill marks appear.
  3. Remove the watermelon from the grill and let it cool slightly.
  4. Cut the watermelon into bite-sized cubes and transfer to a serving dish.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and chopped mint leaves over the watermelon.
  6. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and serve immediately.

This grilled watermelon and feta salad is a perfect combination of sweet and savory flavors. The smoky char from the grill adds a unique twist to this refreshing summer salad.

2. BBQ Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. Soak the skewers in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  3. In a bowl, combine the chicken chunks, BBQ sauce, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to coat the chicken evenly.
  4. Thread the chicken onto the skewers, leaving a small gap between each piece.
  5. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char.
  6. Remove from the grill and let the skewers rest for a few minutes before serving.

These BBQ chicken skewers are a crowd-pleaser. The tangy BBQ sauce adds a delicious flavor to the tender chicken, making it a perfect main dish for your summer BBQ.

3. Grilled Corn on the Cob with Herb Butter

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (such as parsley, basil, or chives), chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, fresh herbs, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Brush the herb butter evenly over the corn.
  4. Place the corn on the grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the corn is tender and has a charred appearance.
  5. Remove from the grill and let it cool slightly before serving.

Grilling corn on the cob brings out its natural sweetness and adds a smoky flavor. The herb butter adds a burst of freshness, making this side dish a perfect complement to any summer BBQ meal.

4. Grilled Pineapple with Coconut Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 pineapple, peeled and sliced into rings
  • Coconut oil for brushing
  • Coconut ice cream for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the pineapple rings with coconut oil on both sides.
  3. Place the pineapple rings on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until grill marks appear.
  4. Remove from the grill and let it cool slightly.
  5. Serve the grilled pineapple rings with a scoop of coconut ice cream on top.

Grilled pineapple is a delightful summer treat. The caramelized sugars from grilling bring out the pineapple’s natural sweetness, while the cool and creamy coconut ice cream adds a tropical twist to this dessert.

Conclusion

These refreshing summer BBQ recipes are perfect for outdoor gatherings, picnics, or simply enjoying a delicious meal in the sunshine. From the smoky watermelon and feta salad to the tangy BBQ chicken skewers, these recipes are sure to impress your taste buds. So, fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and indulge in these mouthwatering dishes that celebrate the flavors of summer.
