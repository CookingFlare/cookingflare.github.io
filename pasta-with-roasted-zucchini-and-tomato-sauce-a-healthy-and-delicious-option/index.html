Pasta with Roasted Zucchini and Tomato Sauce: A Healthy and Delicious Option


Are you looking for a healthy and delicious pasta dish that will satisfy your cravings without sabotaging your diet? Look no further! Our Pasta with Roasted Zucchini and Tomato Sauce recipe is the perfect option for you. Packed with flavor, nutrients, and a touch of Italian goodness, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of your favorite pasta (penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 2 medium zucchinis, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large baking dish, combine the sliced zucchini, cherry tomatoes, minced garlic, olive oil, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Toss until all the ingredients are well-coated.
  3. Spread the mixture evenly in the baking dish and roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchini is tender and the tomatoes are slightly caramelized.
  4. While the vegetables are roasting, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. Once the roasted vegetables are ready, transfer them to a blender or food processor and blend until smooth. You can adjust the consistency by adding a little water or vegetable broth if desired.
  6. In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the blended sauce to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until heated through.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  8. Serve the Pasta with Roasted Zucchini and Tomato Sauce hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Health Benefits

This Pasta with Roasted Zucchini and Tomato Sauce recipe is not only delicious but also packed with health benefits. Zucchini is a low-calorie vegetable that is rich in dietary fiber, vitamins A and C, and minerals like potassium and manganese. It adds a satisfying crunch and a subtle sweetness to the dish. Tomatoes, on the other hand, are a great source of lycopene, a powerful antioxidant that has been linked to various health benefits, including reduced risk of heart disease and certain types of cancer.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of pasta. Whole wheat or gluten-free options work well with this recipe.
  • Add some protein by tossing in cooked chicken breast, shrimp, or tofu.
  • For extra flavor, sprinkle some chopped fresh basil or parsley over the finished dish.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the roasted vegetable mixture.

So there you have it – a healthy and delicious pasta recipe that will satisfy your taste buds and nourish your body. Enjoy the flavors of Italy with this Pasta with Roasted Zucchini and Tomato Sauce dish. Buon appetito!

Note: This recipe serves 4 people and can be easily doubled or halved to suit your needs.
