Deliciously Simple: Gluten-Free Raspberry Crumble Bars


Who doesn’t love a delicious and easy-to-make dessert that can be enjoyed by everyone, regardless of dietary restrictions? These Gluten-Free Raspberry Crumble Bars are a perfect treat for those who follow a gluten-free lifestyle or simply want to try something new. With a sweet and tangy raspberry filling and a buttery crumble topping, these bars are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Plus, they are incredibly simple to make, requiring just a few basic ingredients. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the crust and crumble topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups gluten-free rolled oats
  • 1 1/4 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed

For the raspberry filling:

  • 2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking pan and set it aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, gluten-free all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the bowl and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to work it into the dry ingredients. Continue mixing until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Take 2 cups of the crumb mixture and set it aside for the topping. Press the remaining mixture evenly into the bottom of the greased baking pan to form the crust.

  5. In a separate bowl, combine the fresh raspberries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice. Gently toss the ingredients together until the raspberries are coated.

  6. Spread the raspberry filling evenly over the crust in the baking pan.

  7. Sprinkle the reserved crumb mixture over the raspberry filling, ensuring an even distribution.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the raspberry filling is bubbly.

  9. Remove from the oven and let the bars cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares.

  10. Serve and enjoy these delicious Gluten-Free Raspberry Crumble Bars with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra special treat.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to substitute the raspberries with your favorite berry, such as strawberries or blueberries. The crumble bars will still turn out delicious!
  • If you prefer a sweeter filling, you can add an additional tablespoon of sugar to the raspberry mixture.
  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Now that you have the recipe for these scrumptious Gluten-Free Raspberry Crumble Bars, it’s time to roll up your sleeves and get baking! Whether you’re planning a family gathering, a potluck, or simply want to treat yourself to a sweet indulgence, these bars are the perfect choice. They are not only gluten-free but also incredibly tasty and easy to make. So, why wait? Gather your ingredients and start baking these deliciously simple treats today!
