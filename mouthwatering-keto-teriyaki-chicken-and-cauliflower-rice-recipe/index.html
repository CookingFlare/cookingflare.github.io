Mouthwatering Keto Teriyaki Chicken and Cauliflower Rice Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy keto-friendly meal? Look no further! This mouthwatering Keto Teriyaki Chicken and Cauliflower Rice recipe is the perfect combination of savory flavors and satisfying textures. Not only is it low in carbs, but it’s also packed with protein and nutrients, making it an ideal choice for anyone following a keto lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce or coconut aminos
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp erythritol or preferred sweetener
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 tsp xanthan gum (optional, for thickening)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

1. Prepare the Cauliflower Rice

Start by washing the head of cauliflower and removing the leaves and core. Cut the cauliflower into florets and place them in a food processor. Pulse the cauliflower until it reaches a rice-like consistency.

2. Marinate the Chicken

In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce (or coconut aminos), water, apple cider vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, sweetener, and sesame oil to create the teriyaki marinade. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish and pour half of the marinade over the chicken. Reserve the other half of the marinade for later. Allow the chicken to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or ideally, overnight for maximum flavor.

3. Cook the Chicken

After marinating, remove the chicken from the refrigerator and let it come to room temperature. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat and add the chicken breasts. Cook for 6-7 minutes on each side or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside to rest.

4. Make the Teriyaki Sauce

While the chicken is resting, pour the reserved teriyaki marinade into the same skillet. Bring the marinade to a simmer over medium heat. If desired, sprinkle xanthan gum over the sauce to thicken it. Stir well until the sauce thickens slightly.

5. Prepare the Cauliflower Rice

In a separate skillet, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add the riced cauliflower and sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until it’s tender but still has a bit of crunch. Season the cauliflower rice with salt and pepper to taste.

6. Assemble and Serve

Slice the cooked chicken and drizzle the teriyaki sauce over the top. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds. Serve the teriyaki chicken alongside the cauliflower rice for a satisfying and flavorful keto-friendly meal.

Conclusion

This Keto Teriyaki Chicken and Cauliflower Rice recipe is a fantastic option for anyone looking to enjoy a delicious Asian-inspired dish while staying true to their keto lifestyle. It’s a simple yet flavorful meal that will leave your taste buds satisfied and your body fueled with wholesome ingredients. Give this recipe a try and experience the joy of indulging in a mouthwatering, guilt-free meal. Enjoy!
