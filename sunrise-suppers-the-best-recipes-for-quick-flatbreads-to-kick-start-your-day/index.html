Sunrise Suppers: The Best Recipes for Quick Flatbreads to Kick-start Your Day


Flatbreads have been an essential part of human diets for centuries. Whether you find yourself craving a savory breakfast wrap or an afternoon snack, these versatile dishes are guaranteed to satisfy. And what better way to start your day than with some freshly prepared Sunrise Suppers? Prepare to embark on a culinary adventure as we explore easy and delicious flatbread recipes that will become your new go-to options for kicking your day off on the right track.

1. Sunrise Scramble Flatbread

Who knew that flatbreads could be the perfect vehicle for your breakfast scramble? This recipe is an absolute winner when you want a substantial meal without spending too much time in the kitchen.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 flatbread
  2. 2 eggs
  3. 1/4 cup mixed bell peppers, diced
  4. 1/4 cup onions, diced
  5. 1/4 cup tomato, diced
  6. Salt and pepper to taste
  7. 1 tbsp olive oil
  8. Fresh coriander leaves, to garnish

Instructions:

  1. Start by heating your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the flatbread on a baking sheet and brush with the olive oil.
  3. In a frying pan, cook the bell peppers and onions over medium heat until the onions turn golden, then transfer them onto the flatbread.
  4. In the same frying pan, cook the scrambled eggs by beating them in a bowl and adding salt and pepper. Cook for a couple of minutes, then add them to the flatbread.
  5. Place the baking sheet with flatbread into the oven for about 10 minutes or until the flatbread becomes crispy.
  6. Once done, garnish it with fresh coriander leaves, slice, and serve hot.

2. Veggie-Packed Spinach Pesto Flatbread

An excellent choice for vegetarians and those looking for a healthy dose of greens, this recipe combines the earthy flavors of spinach pesto with an abundance of veggies.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 flatbread
  2. 1/2 cup spinach pesto
  3. 1/4 cup mixed vegetables, such as cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, and onions, sliced
  4. 1 avocado, thinly sliced
  5. Salt and pepper to taste
  6. 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread the spinach pesto evenly on the flatbread and top it with the mixed vegetables.
  3. Bake the flatbread in the oven for approximately 10-12 minutes or until the vegetables are slightly roasted.
  4. Remove from the oven and place the avocado slices over it. Season with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  5. Serve immediately.

3. Smoky Bacon & Cheddar Flatbread

Savor the flavors of velvety cheese, crispy bacon, and an herby touch of rosemary in this decadent flatbread breakfast.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 flatbread
  2. 1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  3. 4-5 slices of cooked bacon, crumbled
  4. Fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  5. Salt and pepper to taste
  6. 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Adjust your oven’s temperature to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Heavily brush your flatbread with olive oil and place it directly onto the middle rack in the oven.
  3. Bake for about 8-10 minutes or until the flatbread turns crispy and golden.
  4. Remove the flatbread from the oven, sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese, crumbled bacon, and rosemary.
  5. Return the flatbread to the oven for 2-3 minutes longer, or until the cheese begins to melt.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, slice, and serve immediately.

4. Tandoori Chicken & Mango Flatbread

For those days when you crave something out of the ordinary, this fusion flatbread is guaranteed to impress! The exciting mix of tandoori chicken and juicy mango chunks-make this a unique sunrise suppers delight.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 flatbread
  2. 1/4 cup tandoori chicken, shredded
  3. 1/4 cup fresh mango, diced
  4. A few sprigs of fresh mint, chopped
  5. Salt, pepper, and chili flakes to taste
  6. 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Begin with the flatbread brushed with olive oil, placed on a baking sheet.
  3. Cook for 8-10 minutes until crispy, then remove from the oven and layer the tandoori chicken and diced mango.
  4. Sprinkle with fresh mint, season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes, if desired.
  5. Slice and serve this mouthwatering sunrise treat.

To wrap up, we hope these four easy-to-follow recipes inspire you to enjoy a delicious, no-fuss Sunrise Supper. It’s amazing how flatbreads have become so versatile and versatile, standing as perfect companions to a wide range of breakfast and snack options. So gather your ingredients and start crafting some scrumptious and healthy Sunrise Suppers to embark on your day with full enthusiasm and flavor. Happy cooking!
