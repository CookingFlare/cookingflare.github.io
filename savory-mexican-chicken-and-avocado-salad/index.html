Savory Mexican Chicken and Avocado Salad


If you’re looking for a fresh and flavorful salad that’s anything but boring, this Savory Mexican Chicken and Avocado Salad is just what you need. Packed with vibrant colors, bold flavors, and satisfying textures, this salad is a complete meal in itself. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, preparing a quick weeknight dinner, or simply craving a healthy and delicious dish, this recipe is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the tantalizing world of Mexican flavors with this easy and satisfying salad.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, canned, or frozen)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken:

    • In a small bowl, mix the chili powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper. Rub the spice mixture evenly over the chicken breasts.
    • In a skillet over medium-high heat, warm the olive oil. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked. Once done, set aside to cool before slicing into strips.

  2. Make the Dressing:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, honey, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper until well combined. Set aside.

  3. Assemble the Salad:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the black beans, corn, diced red bell pepper, halved cherry tomatoes, chopped red onion, and fresh cilantro. Toss gently to mix.
    • Add the diced avocados and sliced chicken to the bowl, and drizzle the dressing over the top. Gently toss until everything is evenly coated with the dressing.

  4. Serve:

    • Arrange the mixed salad greens on a serving platter or individual plates. Spoon the chicken and avocado mixture over the greens, and serve immediately.

Final Thoughts

This Savory Mexican Chicken and Avocado Salad is a celebration of vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients. The tender, spiced chicken pairs beautifully with the creamy avocados, while the colorful array of vegetables adds a delightful crunch and freshness to every bite. The zesty lime dressing ties everything together with a burst of citrusy goodness. Whether you’re a devoted salad enthusiast or someone looking to incorporate more nutritious meals into your diet, this recipe is a winner.

With its combination of protein, healthy fats, and fiber-rich veggies, this salad is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nourishing meal that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized. It’s perfect for a quick lunch, a light dinner, or as a side dish for your next gathering. So, gather your freshest ingredients, embrace the vibrant flavors of Mexico, and indulge in this delightful Savory Mexican Chicken and Avocado Salad. Cheers to good food and good health!
