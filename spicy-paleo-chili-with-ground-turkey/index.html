Spicy Paleo Chili with Ground Turkey


Are you in the mood for a hearty and flavorful dish that’s not only delicious but also fits into your paleo lifestyle? Look no further than this Spicy Paleo Chili with Ground Turkey. Packed with protein, vegetables, and just the right amount of heat, this chili is a perfect option for a cozy dinner or meal prep for the week ahead. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how you can create this mouthwatering dish in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Brown the Ground Turkey: In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the ground turkey over medium heat until it’s no longer pink, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.

  2. Add the Aromatics: Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot with the turkey. Cook for a few minutes until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Incorporate the Vegetables: Stir in the diced red and green bell peppers and cook for 5-7 minutes until they begin to soften.

  4. Combine the Tomatoes and Broth: Add the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and broth to the pot, stirring to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Season the Chili: Sprinkle in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Stir well to ensure the seasonings are distributed evenly.

  6. Simmer to Perfection: Bring the chili to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chili has thickened and the flavors have melded together, it’s ready to be served. Ladle it into bowls, garnish with fresh cilantro, and savor every spicy, satisfying spoonful.

Benefits of Spicy Paleo Chili with Ground Turkey

This Spicy Paleo Chili with Ground Turkey is not only a treat for your taste buds but also offers several health benefits. Ground turkey is a lean source of protein that can support muscle health and keep you feeling full and satisfied. Additionally, the mix of vegetables and spices provides a variety of essential nutrients and antioxidants. The absence of beans and dairy in this recipe makes it a great option for those following a paleo or dairy-free diet.

Make It Your Own

One of the best things about chili is its versatility. Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your preferences. You can adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less cayenne pepper, or incorporate other vegetables such as zucchini or carrots for added texture and flavor. For those who enjoy a smoky element, consider adding a touch of smoked paprika to the mix.

In Conclusion

This Spicy Paleo Chili with Ground Turkey is a fantastic addition to your recipe collection, offering a comforting and nourishing meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply enjoying a cozy night in, this chili is sure to impress. Embrace the warmth and depth of flavor in every spoonful, and relish in the satisfaction of creating a wholesome dish that’s both delicious and nutritious.
