Homemade Spicy Sriracha Mayo Sauce for Burgers


When it comes to creating the perfect burger, there’s one element that can elevate the flavor to an entirely new level: the sauce. Sure, classic ketchup and mustard have their place, but if you’re looking for something that brings a little extra kick, a homemade spicy Sriracha mayo sauce is exactly what you need.

This creamy and spicy concoction packs the perfect punch without overpowering the other ingredients in your burger. It’s a versatile sauce that can also be used on sandwiches, wraps, fries, and even as a dipping sauce. Best of all, it’s incredibly easy to make with just a few simple ingredients that are likely already sitting in your kitchen. Let’s dive into how to create this flavor-packed Sriracha mayo sauce and make your next burger night unforgettable!

Ingredients You’ll Need

Creating the best spicy Sriracha mayo for burgers doesn’t require any exotic or hard-to-find ingredients. You probably have most of these items in your kitchen already. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Mayonnaise (1 cup): This creamy base will give the sauce its smooth texture. It acts as the perfect carrier for the heat from the Sriracha and the other flavors in the sauce.
  • Sriracha sauce (2 tablespoons): This hot chili sauce gives the mayo its signature spicy kick. The depth of flavor and the heat from the Sriracha are what make this sauce so addictive.
  • Garlic powder (1 teaspoon): A pinch of garlic powder adds a savory depth to the sauce, complementing the other bold flavors.
  • Lemon juice (1 teaspoon): A bit of acidity helps balance the richness of the mayonnaise while adding a fresh note to the sauce.
  • Honey (1 teaspoon): A small amount of honey provides a subtle sweetness that helps to cut through the spiciness of the Sriracha.
  • Rice vinegar (1 teaspoon): If you want a more tangy and zesty flavor profile, rice vinegar is a fantastic addition that pairs perfectly with the creaminess of the mayo.
  • Salt (to taste): Salt is essential to bring all the flavors together and enhance the overall taste of the sauce.
  • Optional: Paprika or cayenne pepper (for extra heat): If you prefer your sauce extra spicy, adding a pinch of paprika or cayenne pepper can dial up the heat.

Once you have all of these ingredients, you’re ready to make your spicy Sriracha mayo sauce.

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Spicy Sriracha Mayo Sauce

1. Combine the Base Ingredients

Start by placing the mayonnaise in a mixing bowl. The mayo is the foundation of your sauce and provides the creamy texture that balances the heat from the Sriracha sauce.

Next, add the Sriracha sauce to the bowl. Depending on how spicy you want the mayo, you can adjust the amount of Sriracha. If you’re a fan of spicy food, feel free to add a bit more for an extra fiery punch.

2. Add Flavor Enhancers

Now it’s time to add the garlic powder, lemon juice, and honey. The garlic powder gives the sauce a savory undertone, while the honey adds just a touch of sweetness that perfectly complements the spicy Sriracha.

The lemon juice will brighten up the sauce and help to balance the creaminess with a slight tang. For an added zing, you can also stir in a teaspoon of rice vinegar. The rice vinegar brings an extra layer of acidity that cuts through the richness of the mayo, making the sauce taste fresher.

3. Season with Salt and Optional Spices

Add a pinch of salt to bring out all the flavors in the sauce. Taste and adjust the salt level based on your preferences. If you’re someone who loves heat, now’s the time to sprinkle in some paprika or cayenne pepper. This will kick the spice level up a notch, but feel free to skip this step if you prefer a milder flavor.

4. Mix Thoroughly

Once all the ingredients are in the bowl, give everything a good mix. Use a whisk or spoon to ensure that the sauce is smooth and well-combined. You want the mayonnaise to absorb all the other ingredients evenly, creating a creamy, flavorful sauce that coats the back of a spoon.

5. Let It Chill (Optional)

While this sauce can be used immediately, letting it sit in the fridge for 30 minutes to an hour will allow the flavors to meld together even more. The waiting time is especially helpful for those who are using it on burgers, as the sauce will become thicker and even more delicious as it rests.

Tips for the Perfect Homemade Spicy Sriracha Mayo Sauce

  • Adjust the Heat: If you’re not into very spicy food, start with just 1 tablespoon of Sriracha and gradually add more until you reach the desired heat level. On the flip side, if you enjoy intense heat, don’t hesitate to use extra Sriracha or even add a dash of hot sauce to the mix.
  • Make It Your Own: This recipe is highly customizable. If you like more tang, you can up the amount of lemon juice or vinegar. If you want to add a smoky element, consider mixing in some smoked paprika.
  • Storage: You can store this sauce in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week. It should stay fresh and flavorful, so feel free to make a big batch ahead of time for multiple uses.

How to Use Spicy Sriracha Mayo on Burgers

Now that you’ve made this delicious homemade spicy Sriracha mayo sauce, you’ll want to know the best ways to use it on your burgers. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

1. Classic Burger: Spread a generous amount of the spicy mayo on your burger buns for an extra kick. The creaminess of the mayo pairs beautifully with the juicy patty, while the Sriracha gives it a satisfying heat.

2. Chicken Burgers: This sauce works wonderfully on chicken burgers too. The mild flavor of the chicken allows the sauce to really shine, creating a harmonious balance between the meat and the creamy, spicy mayo.

3. Veggie Burgers: If you’re vegetarian or simply looking for a lighter option, this sauce pairs beautifully with veggie burgers. The heat and flavor from the Sriracha mayo will elevate the flavors of the vegetables and grains, making for a delicious meat-free burger.

4. Asian-Inspired Burgers: For a fun twist, use the spicy Sriracha mayo on an Asian-inspired burger with ingredients like teriyaki sauce, grilled pineapple, and pickled vegetables. The tangy, spicy mayo will complement the sweet and savory flavors of the other ingredients perfectly.

5. Sliders: If you’re making mini sliders for a party or gathering, a dollop of spicy Sriracha mayo will take them to the next level. The small size of the sliders makes them perfect for experimenting with different flavors, and the mayo adds that extra touch of boldness that guests will love.

Other Ways to Enjoy Spicy Sriracha Mayo

While this spicy mayo sauce is undoubtedly amazing on burgers, it has many other uses in your kitchen. Here are a few ideas for how to enjoy your homemade sauce in other ways:

1. As a Dipping Sauce for Fries or Sweet Potato Fries

Serve it as a dipping sauce alongside your crispy French fries or sweet potato fries. The creamy, spicy flavor pairs incredibly well with the crispiness of the fries, making for an irresistible snack or side dish.

2. On Sandwiches and Wraps

You can spread it on sandwiches or wraps to give your lunch an exciting twist. It adds a creamy element while bringing an enjoyable heat to any type of sandwich, whether it’s chicken, turkey, or even a veggie wrap.

3. As a Sushi Condiment

If you’re a sushi lover, this spicy mayo sauce is a must-try. Drizzle it over your favorite rolls, like spicy tuna rolls or California rolls, to add a creamy and spicy kick. It’s a common topping in many sushi restaurants, and making your own version at home ensures freshness and flavor.

4. On Grilled Vegetables

This mayo sauce can also be drizzled over grilled vegetables like corn on the cob, zucchini, or bell peppers. The heat of the Sriracha adds a smoky depth to the grilled vegetables while the creaminess of the mayo balances out the charred flavors.

5. As a Topping for Tacos or Burritos

Try adding a little bit of this spicy mayo to tacos, burritos, or quesadillas for a creamy, spicy twist. Whether you’re making fish tacos, beef tacos, or a veggie burrito, this sauce will complement any filling perfectly.

Conclusion: Why Homemade Spicy Sriracha Mayo Is a Game-Changer

The homemade spicy Sriracha mayo sauce for burgers is a true game-changer in the world of sauces. It offers the perfect balance of heat, creaminess, and tang, transforming a basic burger into an extraordinary experience. By making this sauce at home, you can control the level of spiciness, tweak the flavor profile to suit your tastes, and avoid the artificial additives found in store-bought versions.

Whether you’re grilling burgers for a weekend barbecue or preparing a quick lunch, this spicy mayo sauce is the perfect condiment to elevate your meal. Try it out today and get ready to enjoy the ultimate combination of spicy, creamy, and flavorful goodness.
