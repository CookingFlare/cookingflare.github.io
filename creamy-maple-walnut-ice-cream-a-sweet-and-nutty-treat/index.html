Creamy Maple Walnut Ice Cream: A Sweet and Nutty Treat


Who doesn’t love a scoop of creamy, indulgent ice cream on a hot summer day? If you’re a fan of the classic combination of sweet maple and crunchy walnuts, then this recipe is for you. Get ready to embark on a flavor journey with our homemade Creamy Maple Walnut Ice Cream!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream and whole milk over medium heat. Heat the mixture until it begins to steam, but make sure not to let it boil.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar and egg yolks until well combined and slightly thickened.

  3. Slowly pour the hot cream mixture into the egg mixture, whisking constantly to prevent curdling. This step is called tempering and ensures a smooth and creamy base for your ice cream.

  4. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon. The mixture will gradually thicken and coat the back of the spoon. This process, known as “coating the spoon,” should take about 5-7 minutes.

  5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the pure maple syrup and vanilla extract. Allow the mixture to cool completely.

  6. Once cooled, transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes, and you should end up with a soft, creamy ice cream.

  7. Fold in the chopped walnuts during the last 5 minutes of churning. The walnuts will add a delightful crunch to every bite.

  8. Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm. This step allows the ice cream to set and develop its rich, creamy texture.

  9. When you’re ready to serve, scoop the Creamy Maple Walnut Ice Cream into bowls or cones, and savor the sweet and nutty flavors that will surely satisfy your dessert cravings.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a drizzle of warm maple syrup over the ice cream before serving.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can strain the custard base before churning to remove any potential lumps or bits of cooked egg.
  • Feel free to experiment with different nuts like pecans or almonds if you’re not a fan of walnuts. The creamy base pairs well with a variety of nutty flavors.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can still make this recipe by pouring the mixture into a shallow dish and freezing it. Just remember to stir the mixture every 30 minutes for the first few hours to break up any ice crystals and achieve a smoother texture.

Conclusion

Creamy Maple Walnut Ice Cream is a delightful treat that combines the sweetness of maple syrup with the crunch of walnuts. Whether you enjoy it on its own or as a topping for your favorite desserts, this homemade ice cream is sure to impress your family and friends. So go ahead, indulge in this sweet and nutty delight and savor the flavors of summer!
