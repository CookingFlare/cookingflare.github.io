Top 10 Best Burrito Recipes for Weeknight Dinners


Weeknight dinners can often feel like a daunting task. After a long day, the last thing you want is to spend hours in the kitchen. But what if you could whip up a meal that’s both quick and satisfying? Enter burritos – a versatile, customizable dish that can suit any palate and fit into your busy schedule. From savory meats to fresh veggies and rich fillings, burritos offer endless possibilities. Whether you’re craving something classic or want to try a new twist, here are the top 10 best burrito recipes that will transform your weeknight dinners into flavorful, hassle-free meals.

1. Classic Beef Burrito

A classic beef burrito is a staple in many households. It’s the perfect balance of seasoned beef, melty cheese, and fresh toppings, all wrapped in a soft flour tortilla. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade taco seasoning)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup refried beans
  • 1 cup cooked rice (optional)
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Salsa (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Beef: In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat. Break it apart with a spatula as it cooks. Once browned, drain excess fat.
  2. Season the Meat: Add taco seasoning and a little water (as directed on the packet) and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the beef is fully coated in the seasoning.
  3. Prepare the Fillings: In the same skillet, heat the refried beans until warm. If you’re using rice, heat it separately.
  4. Assemble the Burritos: Lay a tortilla flat and spread a spoonful of refried beans in the center. Add some seasoned beef, rice, shredded cheese, diced onions, and chopped tomatoes. Top with sour cream and salsa if desired.
  5. Roll and Serve: Fold the sides of the tortilla inwards and roll tightly from one end to the other. Serve immediately with extra salsa on the side.

2. Chicken Fajita Burrito

For those who love a little zest in their meals, the chicken fajita burrito is the way to go. With grilled chicken, peppers, and onions, this burrito is bursting with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 packet fajita seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup guacamole
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with fajita seasoning and a drizzle of olive oil. Grill or sauté until the chicken is fully cooked and no longer pink in the center, about 6-7 minutes per side. Let it rest before slicing into thin strips.
  2. Sauté the Vegetables: In the same pan, sauté the bell peppers and onions until they are softened and slightly caramelized, about 5 minutes.
  3. Assemble the Burritos: Lay a tortilla flat and spread a layer of guacamole in the center. Add the grilled chicken, sautéed peppers and onions, shredded lettuce, and cheese.
  4. Roll and Serve: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly. Serve with a squeeze of fresh lime juice for an extra zing.

3. Vegetarian Burrito

For a meatless option, this vegetarian burrito is packed with colorful vegetables and hearty beans. It’s a great way to enjoy a satisfying meal without the meat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup chopped bell peppers (mix of colors)
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or rice
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Salsa for topping

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Veggies: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the bell peppers and sauté for 3-4 minutes until softened. Season with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat the Beans and Corn: Add the black beans and corn to the skillet and cook for another 3-4 minutes until heated through.
  3. Assemble the Burritos: Lay a tortilla flat and layer with a scoop of quinoa or rice. Add the veggie and bean mixture, avocado slices, diced tomatoes, and a sprinkle of cheese.
  4. Roll and Serve: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly. Serve with a dollop of salsa and fresh cilantro.

4. Breakfast Burrito

Why wait for lunch or dinner to enjoy a burrito? This breakfast burrito is a perfect way to start your day, packed with eggs, bacon, and all the delicious fixings.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • Fresh avocado slices

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Eggs: Scramble the eggs in a pan with butter over medium heat until they are fully cooked.
  2. Prepare the Veggies: Sauté the onions and bell peppers in the same pan until softened, about 3 minutes.
  3. Assemble the Burritos: Lay a tortilla flat and layer with scrambled eggs, bacon, sautéed onions and peppers, shredded cheese, and salsa.
  4. Roll and Serve: Fold in the sides and roll up the burrito tightly. Top with fresh avocado slices for extra creaminess.

5. BBQ Chicken Burrito

This BBQ chicken burrito is a savory twist on the classic, with smoky barbecue sauce and tender chicken breast wrapped up in a warm tortilla.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup lettuce
  • 6 large flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Chicken: Grill or bake the chicken breasts until fully cooked. Once done, shred the chicken using two forks.
  2. Toss in BBQ Sauce: In a bowl, toss the shredded chicken with barbecue sauce until well coated.
  3. Assemble the Burritos: Lay a tortilla flat and spread a little BBQ sauce on it. Add the BBQ chicken, corn, green onions, and shredded cheese.
  4. Roll and Serve: Fold in the sides and roll tightly. Serve with extra barbecue sauce for dipping.

6. Fish Taco Burrito

This fish taco burrito combines the flavors of crispy fish, creamy sauce, and tangy slaw all in one burrito. It’s fresh, light, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 white fish fillets (like tilapia or cod)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 6 large flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Fish: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Season the fish fillets with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook the fish for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and crispy. Shred the fish using a fork.
  2. Make the Slaw: In a bowl, combine shredded cabbage, mayo, and lime juice. Toss to combine.
  3. Assemble the Burritos: Lay a tortilla flat and spread a spoonful of salsa. Add the shredded fish, slaw, and a drizzle of creamy sauce.
  4. Roll and Serve: Fold the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly. Serve with extra lime wedges.

7. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Burrito

Sweet potatoes and black beans are a match made in heaven. This burrito is perfect for vegetarians or anyone craving something hearty but healthy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Sweet Potatoes: Roast the diced sweet potatoes in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 minutes, or until soft and golden brown.
  2. Prepare the Beans: Heat the black beans in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add cumin, paprika, and chili powder to taste.
