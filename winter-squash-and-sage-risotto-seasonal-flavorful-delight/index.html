Winter Squash and Sage Risotto: Seasonal Flavorful Delight


Winter squash and sage risotto, a match made in heaven. The sweet, nutty flavor of roasted winter squash pairs perfectly with the earthy, savory taste of sage, all wrapped up in a creamy, comforting risotto. This dish is a true celebration of the flavors and ingredients of the season.

准备工作

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s talk about the importance of using high-quality ingredients. For this dish, you’ll need the following:

  • 1 medium-sized winter squash (such as butternut or kabocha)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth, warmed
  • 1/4 cup white wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 sprigs fresh sage, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)

煮 Risotto

To start, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut the winter squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds. Place the squash on a baking sheet, cut side up, and drizzle with olive oil. Roast in the oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the squash is tender and caramelized.

While the squash is roasting, heat the chicken broth in a separate pot and keep warm. In a large skillet, melt 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

Add the Arborio rice to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the rice is coated in the butter and slightly toasted. If using wine, add it to the skillet and cook until the liquid is almost completely absorbed.

添加 Sage 和 Squash

Add 1/2 cup of the warmed chicken broth to the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, until the liquid is mostly absorbed. Repeat this process, adding the broth in 1/2 cup increments, and waiting for it to be absorbed before adding more. This process should take about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and creamy.

When the squash is done roasting, let it cool slightly, then scoop out the flesh and add it to the skillet with the rice. Stir to combine, then add the chopped sage and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until the flavors have melded together.

Serve

Serve the risotto hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and additional chopped sage if desired. This dish is perfect for a chilly winter evening, and the flavors will only get better with time.

Tips 和 Variations

  • To make this dish more substantial, add some cooked chicken, sausage, or mushrooms to the risotto.
  • Substitute other types of winter squash, such as acorn or hubbard, for a different flavor and texture.
  • Add some dried cranberries or cherries to the risotto for a pop of color and a sweet and tangy flavor.
  • Make this dish ahead of time and refrigerate or freeze for up to 3 days. Reheat gently over low heat, adding a little more broth if necessary.

This winter squash and sage risotto is a true celebration of the flavors and ingredients of the season. With its creamy texture and rich, savory flavors, it’s a dish that’s sure to become a new favorite. So go ahead, give it a try, and let the warmth and comfort of this dish wrap you up like a cozy blanket on a chilly winter night.
