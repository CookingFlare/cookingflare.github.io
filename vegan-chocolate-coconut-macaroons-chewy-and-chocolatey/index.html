Vegan Chocolate Coconut Macaroons: Chewy and Chocolatey


If you’re a fan of coconut and chocolate, then you’re in for a treat with these vegan chocolate coconut macaroons. These delightful little morsels are not only chewy and chocolatey but also entirely plant-based, making them a guilt-free indulgence for anyone with a sweet tooth. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan cook or just dipping your toes into the world of plant-based baking, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix the Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the shredded coconut, coconut cream, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir until all the ingredients are well combined.

  3. Form the Macaroons: Using a cookie scoop or your hands, form the coconut mixture into small mounds and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Make sure to leave some space between the macaroons as they will spread slightly while baking.

  4. Bake: Bake the macaroons for 15-18 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the tops are slightly firm to the touch. Remove from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes.

  5. Melt the Chocolate: While the macaroons are cooling, melt the vegan chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until smooth and melted.

  6. Dip the Macaroons: Once the macaroons have cooled slightly, carefully dip the bottoms of each macaroon into the melted chocolate, allowing any excess chocolate to drip off. Place the dipped macaroons back on the parchment paper.

  7. Chill: Place the macaroons in the refrigerator for 10-15 minutes to allow the chocolate to set.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chocolate has set, your vegan chocolate coconut macaroons are ready to be enjoyed. Store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature or in the refrigerator.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra decadent touch, drizzle some melted chocolate over the tops of the macaroons before chilling.
  • Feel free to add a dash of almond extract to the coconut mixture for a subtle nutty flavor.
  • Experiment with different types of vegan chocolate, such as dark chocolate or even flavored chocolate, to customize the taste to your liking.

Final Thoughts

These vegan chocolate coconut macaroons are a delightful combination of chewy coconut and rich chocolate, making them a perfect treat for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, looking for a sweet snack, or simply craving a bit of indulgence, these macaroons are sure to satisfy. Plus, with their simple and wholesome ingredients, they are a guilt-free pleasure that you can feel good about enjoying.

So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to whip up a batch of these delectable vegan treats. You’ll be amazed at how such a simple recipe can yield such delicious results. Give it a try, and let these vegan chocolate coconut macaroons add a touch of sweetness to your day!
