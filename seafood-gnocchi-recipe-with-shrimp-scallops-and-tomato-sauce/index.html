Seafood Gnocchi Recipe with Shrimp, Scallops, and Tomato Sauce


If you’re a seafood lover looking for a delightful and flavorful dish to impress your guests or simply treat yourself to a gourmet meal, then this Seafood Gnocchi recipe with shrimp, scallops, and tomato sauce is just what you need. This recipe combines the rich flavors of succulent shrimp and tender scallops with pillowy gnocchi, all brought together with a luscious tomato sauce. It’s a dish that is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Gnocchi:

  • 2 pounds of potatoes
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • Salt

For the Seafood and Tomato Sauce:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound of fresh scallops
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Making the Gnocchi:

  1. Start by boiling the potatoes until they are tender. Once cooked, peel and mash them in a large bowl.
  2. Add the flour, egg, and a pinch of salt to the mashed potatoes. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. On a floured surface, roll the dough into long ropes, then cut them into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Use a fork to create ridges on the gnocchi pieces. This will help the sauce cling to the gnocchi later.
  5. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the gnocchi until they float to the surface. Remove them with a slotted spoon and set aside.

Preparing the Seafood and Tomato Sauce:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the shrimp and scallops to the skillet, cooking until they are just opaque. Remove the seafood from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Pour the white wine into the skillet, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Allow the wine to reduce by half.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet, along with salt, pepper, and torn basil leaves. Simmer the sauce for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once the sauce has thickened, return the cooked seafood to the skillet and stir gently to combine.

Bringing It All Together:

  1. Add the cooked gnocchi to the skillet with the seafood and tomato sauce. Gently toss everything together until the gnocchi is coated with the sauce and the seafood is evenly distributed.
  2. Serve the Seafood Gnocchi in individual bowls, garnished with fresh basil leaves and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.

Enjoy Your Culinary Creation!

This Seafood Gnocchi recipe with shrimp, scallops, and tomato sauce is a true celebration of flavors and textures. The tender gnocchi, succulent seafood, and aromatic tomato sauce come together to create a dish that is both comforting and elegant. Whether you’re preparing this for a special occasion or simply indulging in a luxurious meal at home, this recipe is guaranteed to leave a lasting impression. So, gather your ingredients, channel your inner chef, and treat yourself to a taste of the sea with this delightful dish. Cheers to good food and great company!
