Revamp Your Omelette Style: Parisian Langostinos Omelette Recipe


Ready to put a sophisticated spin on your morning omelette? Look no further than the elegant Parisian langostinos omelette. This mouth-watering dish has been a culinary staple in the City of Lights for decades and is now ready to steal the spotlight in your kitchen. So, dissolve your everyday omelette rut and experience a delectable culinary upgrade.

To prepare this luxurious omelette, start by gathering the following ingredients:

  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 0.5 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 10 large langostinos (spanish prawns)
  • 0.25 cup diced shallots
  • 0.25 cup chopped fresh chives
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided

Now, stand out from the ordinary by following this secret Parisian technique:

  1. Begin by gently dissolving the yolk of each egg in a mixing bowl. This creates a creamier texture for your omelette.
  2. Next, stir in the heavy cream, ensuring a smooth, velvety consistency. This will give your omelette the much-coveted silky touch of luxury.
  3. Add a dash of salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste, for that quintessential French flavor.
  4. Gently cook the langostinos in barely simmering water for about 3 minutes or until they turn pink. Drain and set them aside.
  5. In another pan, melt half the butter over medium heat. Sauté the shallots until tender and translucent.
  6. To these shallots, add the langostinos, cooking for one to two minutes until heated through.
  7. In a separate, well-seasoned, non-stick pan, distribute the remaining butter evenly. This will ensure that your omelette slides effortlessly onto your plate.
  8. Pour in the egg yolk mixture and allow it to set slightly but not fully cook. The omelette should look slightly jiggly.
  9. Vigorously mix the chives into the cooked langostinos and shallots.
  10. Distribute the langostinos mixture evenly over one half of the omelette.
  11. Fold the other half over the langostinos, effectively enclosing this delightful mixture within your omelette.
  12. Gently slide the omelette onto a plate, allowing the silky exterior to meld with the succulent filling.
  13. Your Parisian langostinos omelette is now ready to serve. Bon appétit!
