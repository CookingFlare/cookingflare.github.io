The Best Mediterranean Seafood Pasta Recipe


The Mediterranean diet is known for its emphasis on fresh produce, whole grains, and a balanced intake of protein. When it comes to cooking with seafood, the possibilities are endless, and when paired with pasta, the result is a dish that is both light and satisfying. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of creating a seafood pasta dish that captures the essence of the Mediterranean. It's a delicious combination of flavors, textures, and aromas that will transport your taste buds to the Mediterranean coast. Ingredients For this Mediterranean seafood pasta recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

12 oz pasta of your choice (such as spaghetti or linguine) 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil 1 onion, finely chopped 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup mixed seafood (such as shrimp, mussels, clams, and calamari) 1 (28-oz) can of crushed tomatoes 2 cups fish broth 1 tablespoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried thyme Salt and pepper to taste 1/4 cup white wine (optional) Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions This Mediterranean seafood pasta recipe consists of a few steps that blend together to create a delicious meal.

Prepare the seafood: Rinse the seafood under cold water and pat it dry. Cut any large shrimp into smaller pieces and set aside.

Sauté the onion and garlic: In a large skillet, heat the extra-virgin olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté for 3-4 minutes, or until softened.

Add the garlic and seafood: Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute, until fragrant. Then, add the mixed seafood and cook for 3-4 minutes, until the seafood is about half-cooked.

Add the tomatoes, broth, oregano and thyme: Pour in the crushed tomatoes, fish broth, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Stir well to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Simmer the sauce: Bring the sauce to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the seafood is cooked through.

Add the white wine (optional): If you choose to add the white wine, pour it in and stir well. The acidity will help to brighten up the flavors of the dish.

Cook the pasta: Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling water, following the package instructions for the best results. Drained and set aside.

Combine the pasta and sauce: In a large serving bowl, combine the cooked pasta, seafood sauce, and seafood. Toss well to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Serve and enjoy: Finally, serve the Mediterranean seafood pasta hot, topped with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.

Serving Suggestions This seafood pasta dish can be served as a main course for a seafood-focused dinner party or as a lighter meal for a weeknight dinner. If you prefer a little extra flavor, pair it with a side salad of mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and a drizzle of balsamic vinegar. For a Mediterranean twist on the classic dessert, try serving a lemon panna cotta with a sprinkle of lemon zest and chopped fresh mint. Tips and Variations To make this Mediterranean seafood pasta dish your own, consider these variations:

Hot peppers can add a spicy kick to this recipe. Fresh basil can add a bright pop of flavor. Grilled octopus or calamari can add a delicious smoky flavor. Pesto can be used in place of thyme, for a tangier flavor.

Conclusion The Best Mediterranean Seafood Pasta Recipe is a simple and delicious way to experience the Mediterranean coast without leaving home. It combines the delicate flavors of seafood with the richness of Mediterranean cuisine. Whether you're a pasta lover, a seafood enthusiast, or just a foodie at heart, this Mediterranean seafood pasta dish will be a recipe for success.
