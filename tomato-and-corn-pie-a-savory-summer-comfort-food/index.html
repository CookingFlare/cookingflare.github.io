Tomato and Corn Pie: A Savory Summer Comfort Food


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy the abundance of fresh produce. From juicy tomatoes to sweet corn, there’s no shortage of delicious ingredients to work with. If you’re looking for a comforting yet refreshing dish to satisfy your summer cravings, look no further than Tomato and Corn Pie. This savory pie combines the sweetness of tomatoes with the crunch of corn, all wrapped in a flaky crust. It’s the ultimate summer comfort food that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To make this delightful Tomato and Corn Pie, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch pie crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 4 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish and set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced tomatoes, corn kernels, shredded cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, chopped basil, chopped green onions, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Gently toss everything together until well mixed.

  3. Pour the tomato and corn mixture into the prepared pie crust, spreading it evenly.

  4. Place the pie dish on a baking sheet and transfer it to the preheated oven. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

  5. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. The pie can be served warm or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

Tomato and Corn Pie is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with a side salad or a scoop of creamy mashed potatoes. The flavors of the pie pair well with a refreshing glass of iced tea or a crisp white wine. It’s the perfect dish for a summer picnic, backyard barbecue, or a casual dinner with friends and family.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top of the pie before baking.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of the pie. Thyme, oregano, or smoked paprika can add a unique twist.
  • If you’re a fan of spice, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the filling mixture.
  • To make the pie gluten-free, use a gluten-free pie crust or make a crustless version by simply pouring the filling into a greased pie dish.

Conclusion

Tomato and Corn Pie is a versatile and satisfying dish that celebrates the flavors of summer. With its flaky crust, juicy tomatoes, and sweet corn, it’s a true comfort food that will transport you to warm summer days. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply looking for a delicious meal to enjoy, this savory pie is sure to impress. So go ahead, grab those ripe tomatoes and fresh corn, and get ready to savor the taste of summer with Tomato and Corn Pie!
