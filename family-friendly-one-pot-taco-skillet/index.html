Family-Friendly One Pot Taco Skillet


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious dinner that the whole family will love, look no further than this Family-Friendly One Pot Taco Skillet! This easy-to-make dish is packed with flavor, wholesome ingredients, and it all comes together in just one skillet, making cleanup a breeze. Whether you’re a busy parent or simply want to whip up a fuss-free meal, this recipe is sure to become a go-to favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, diced avocado, sour cream, chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.

  2. Add the diced onion and red bell pepper to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are softened and the beef is fully cooked.

  3. Stir in the black beans, frozen corn, diced tomatoes, rice, chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil.

  4. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let the mixture simmer for about 20 minutes or until the rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed.

  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve the taco skillet hot, with optional toppings such as shredded cheese, diced avocado, sour cream, and chopped cilantro.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

There are so many reasons to love this Family-Friendly One Pot Taco Skillet. Not only is it incredibly easy to make, but it’s also a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your family’s tastes. You can easily swap out the ground beef for ground turkey or chicken for a lighter option, or even make it vegetarian by using plant-based meat alternatives. The addition of black beans and corn not only adds a nutritional boost but also brings a delightful texture and color to the dish.

Furthermore, this recipe is a great way to get the little ones involved in the kitchen. From stirring the ingredients to choosing their favorite toppings, kids can have fun being part of the cooking process. It’s also a fantastic opportunity to introduce them to different flavors and expand their palate in a fun and enjoyable way.

In addition to being family-friendly, this taco skillet is a fantastic meal prep option. You can easily make a big batch and have delicious leftovers for the next day, saving you time and effort. Plus, with just one skillet to clean up, you’ll appreciate the simplicity of this recipe – fewer dishes means more time to spend with your loved ones.

Final Thoughts

The Family-Friendly One Pot Taco Skillet is a true winner for busy weeknights or laid-back weekends. Its vibrant colors, robust flavors, and easy preparation make it a standout dish that’s sure to bring smiles to the dinner table. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking for a satisfying meal that’s both delicious and convenient. So, gather your ingredients, grab your skillet, and get ready to savor every delightful bite of this family favorite!
