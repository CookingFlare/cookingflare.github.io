Mediterranean Chicken and Rice Skillet Dinner


As the sun sets over the Mediterranean coast, the aroma of sizzling chicken and rice wafts through the air, tantalizing the senses and beckoning you to the table. This classic combination is a staple of Mediterranean cuisine, and for good reason. The union of juicy chicken, fluffy rice, and a medley of herbs and spices creates a dish that's both satisfying and enlightening. In this recipe, we'll take you on a culinary journey through the sun-kissed Mediterranean, where warm hospitality and rich flavors meld together to create a truly unforgettable dining experience. Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces 2 cups uncooked white or brown rice 2 cups chicken broth 1/4 cup olive oil 1 large onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup sliced bell peppers (any color) 1 cup sliced mushrooms 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 2 teaspoons dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper, to taste Feta cheese, crumbled (optional)

Planning and Preparation Begin by preparing your ingredients. Rinse the chicken, pat it dry with paper towels, and season with salt and pepper. In a medium-sized skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil, then add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant. Building the Skillet Add the sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, and cherry tomatoes to the skillet, cooking until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 4-5 minutes. Stir in the oregano, thyme, and paprika. Cook for an additional minute, until the spices are fragrant. Cooking the Rice In a medium saucepan, combine the chicken broth and rice. Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to low and cover the saucepan with a tight-fitting lid. Simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until the liquid has been absorbed and the rice is tender. Assembling the Skillet Once the rice is cooked, add it to the skillet with the vegetables. Stir to combine, then add the cooked chicken back into the skillet. Final Touches Season the skillet with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, crumble feta cheese over the top of the skillet and place it under the broiler for a few minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Serving and Enjoying Transfer the Mediterranean Chicken and Rice Skillet to a large serving platter or individual plates. Garnish with fresh parsley or oregano, if desired. Serve hot, and enjoy the warm, sunshine-kissed flavors of the Mediterranean. This Mediterranean Chicken and Rice Skillet Dinner is a simple yet satisfying meal that's perfect for busy weeknights or special occasions alike. The combination of protein, carbs, and vegetables creates a well-balanced and filling dish that's sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. Whether you're a seasoned cook or a culinary newbie, this recipe is an excellent choice for those looking to explore the rich flavors and aromas of the Mediterranean. Variations and Tips For a vegetarian or vegan version of this recipe, swap the chicken with tofu, tempeh, or seitan, and substitute the chicken broth with vegetable broth. To add some crunch to your skillet, sprinkle some chopped almonds or pistachios on top before serving. If you prefer a spicier dish, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the skillet with the garlic and onion. Experiment with different types of rice, such as jasmine or Basmati, for a unique texture and flavor. Nutritional Information (per serving)

Calories: 420 Protein: 35g Fat: 12g Saturated fat: 2.5g Cholesterol: 60mg Carbohydrates: 25g Fiber: 2g Sugar: 2g Sodium: 350mg

This Mediterranean Chicken and Rice Skillet Dinner is a true culinary delight, packed with nutrients and flavor. With its rich aroma and satisfying textures, it's a dish that's sure to impress even the most discerning palates. Take a trip to the Mediterranean, without leaving your kitchen, and indulge in this hearty and flavorful recipe.
