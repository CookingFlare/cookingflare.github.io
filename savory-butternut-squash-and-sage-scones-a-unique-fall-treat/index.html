Savory Butternut Squash and Sage Scones: A Unique Fall Treat


Welcome, food enthusiasts, to another exciting recipe on our blog! Today, we’re going to take your taste buds on a flavor adventure with our savory butternut squash and sage scones. These delightful treats are perfect for the fall season, offering a unique twist on the traditional sweet scone. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 cup roasted butternut squash, mashed
  • 1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1/4 cup fresh sage leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, black pepper, and dried sage. Add the cold butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the mashed butternut squash, cheddar cheese, and fresh sage leaves. Add this mixture to the dry ingredients and gently fold everything together until just combined.

  4. Gradually pour in the milk while stirring, until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently a few times to ensure everything is fully incorporated. Pat the dough into a circle about 1-inch thick.

  6. Using a sharp knife or a biscuit cutter, cut out scones from the dough and transfer them to the prepared baking sheet. Leave some space between each scone to allow for slight expansion.

  7. Brush the tops of the scones with the beaten egg, which will give them a beautiful golden color as they bake.

  8. Bake the scones in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until they have risen and turned golden brown.

  9. Once baked, remove the scones from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack for a few minutes.

  10. Serve these savory butternut squash and sage scones warm with butter or your favorite spread. They pair wonderfully with a cup of hot soup or a cozy autumn-themed brunch.

Why You’ll Love These Scones

What makes these scones truly special is the combination of the earthy, sweet butternut squash, the aromatic sage, and the tangy cheddar cheese. The scones have a perfectly crumbly texture on the outside, while being light and fluffy on the inside. The flavors come together harmoniously, creating a delightful balance that will surely impress your taste buds.

Butternut squash is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. It is an excellent source of vitamins A and C, as well as fiber, making these scones a guilt-free indulgence. Plus, they make for a great addition to your fall recipe repertoire, whether you’re hosting a brunch or simply want to enjoy a cozy treat on a chilly afternoon.

So, what are you waiting for? Grab your apron, gather the ingredients, and let’s get baking these savory butternut squash and sage scones. Your friends and family will be amazed by this unique fall treat that combines the best flavors of the season. Happy baking!

Note: If you have any specific dietary restrictions or preferences, feel free to customize this recipe to suit your needs. You can experiment with different herbs, cheeses, or even add a hint of spice for an extra kick. The possibilities are endless, so don’t be afraid to get creative in the kitchen!
