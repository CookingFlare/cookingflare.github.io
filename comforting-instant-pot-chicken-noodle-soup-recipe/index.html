Comforting Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe


There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting bowl of chicken noodle soup to soothe the soul and lift your spirits. And with the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have this delicious and nourishing meal on the table in no time. Whether you’re feeling under the weather or simply craving some homemade goodness, this Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Soup recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 8 ounces egg noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing your ingredients. Slice the carrots, celery, and dice the onion. Mince the garlic and set everything aside.

  2. Turn on your Instant Pot and select the sauté function. Add a drizzle of oil and sauté the onions and garlic until fragrant and translucent.

  3. Add the chicken breasts to the pot and cook for a few minutes on each side until lightly browned. This will help seal in the flavors.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, followed by the carrots, celery, thyme, rosemary, and bay leaf. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  5. Lock the lid of your Instant Pot in place and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Select the manual or pressure cook setting and set the timer for 10 minutes.

  6. While the soup is cooking, prepare the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  7. Once the timer goes off, carefully release the pressure by turning the pressure release valve to the venting position. Be cautious of the hot steam.

  8. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.

  9. Add the cooked egg noodles to the soup and give everything a good stir. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  10. Allow the soup to sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can sauté the carrots and celery along with the onions and garlic before adding the chicken to the pot.

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables such as peas or corn.

  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a slurry of cornstarch and water to the pot after cooking to thicken the broth.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat on the stove or in the microwave before serving.

Conclusion

This comforting Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Soup recipe is not only incredibly delicious but also quick and easy to make. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can have a hearty and satisfying meal on the table in no time. So the next time you’re in need of some cozy comfort food, give this recipe a try. It’s sure to become a family favorite!
