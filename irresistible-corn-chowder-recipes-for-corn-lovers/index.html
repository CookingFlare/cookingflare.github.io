Irresistible Corn Chowder Recipes for Corn Lovers


If you’re a corn lover like me, you’ll appreciate the comforting and creamy goodness of corn chowder. Whether you’re looking for a classic recipe or a unique twist on this beloved dish, you’ve come to the right place. In this blog post, I’ll share three irresistible corn chowder recipes that will elevate your love for corn to a whole new level. From traditional flavors to unexpected ingredients, these recipes are sure to satisfy your corn cravings.

Classic Corn Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of fresh corn kernels
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups of diced potatoes
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onions and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the corn kernels and cook for a few minutes until they start to caramelize.
  3. Pour in the broth and add the diced potatoes. Simmer until the potatoes are tender.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and thyme, and let the chowder simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the chowder hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley.

This classic corn chowder recipe is a timeless favorite that celebrates the natural sweetness of corn. The combination of tender corn kernels, creamy potatoes, and aromatic thyme creates a symphony of flavors that will warm your soul.

Spicy Southwestern Corn Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of corn kernels
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup of diced cooked chicken (optional for non-vegetarian version)
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, sauté the red bell pepper and jalapeño until softened.
  2. Add the corn kernels, cumin, and chili powder, and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the black beans and diced chicken (if using) and let the chowder simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Remove the pot from the heat and swirl in the sour cream.
  6. Serve the spicy Southwestern corn chowder garnished with fresh cilantro.

This spicy variation of corn chowder brings a kick of heat to the table, making it a perfect choice for those who enjoy bold flavors. The combination of sweet corn, savory black beans, and fiery jalapeño creates a fiesta in every spoonful.

Coconut Curry Corn Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of corn kernels
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 cup of diced sweet potatoes
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onions and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the corn kernels and curry powder, and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, then add the diced carrots and sweet potatoes. Simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Use an immersion blender to partially blend the chowder for a creamy texture with some whole corn kernels remaining.
  5. Serve the coconut curry corn chowder hot, garnished with fresh basil.

This exotic twist on corn chowder introduces the aromatic flavors of curry and coconut, transporting your taste buds to distant lands. The marriage of sweet corn, creamy coconut milk, and fragrant curry powder creates a luxurious and satisfying dish.

Whether you prefer the familiar taste of a classic corn chowder or crave the excitement of bold and unexpected flavors, these recipes are designed to delight corn lovers of all kinds. So, grab your apron and a bag of fresh corn, and let’s embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the humble yet versatile corn in all its glory!
