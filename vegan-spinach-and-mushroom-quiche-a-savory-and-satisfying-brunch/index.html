Vegan Spinach and Mushroom Quiche: A Savory and Satisfying Brunch


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying brunch recipe that will impress even the most discerning palates? Look no further than this Vegan Spinach and Mushroom Quiche. This savory dish is packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, making it the perfect centerpiece for your next brunch gathering. Whether you’re a vegan, vegetarian, or simply looking to add more plant-based meals to your diet, this quiche is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cold vegan butter, cubed
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4-6 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cubed vegan butter and use a pastry cutter or fork to mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together.
  3. Shape the dough into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat the Oven

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 3: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.
  3. Add the chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Remove the skillet from the heat and set aside.

Step 4: Assemble the Quiche

  1. In a food processor, combine the crumbled tofu, almond milk, nutritional yeast, dried thyme, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Roll out the chilled dough on a lightly floured surface and carefully transfer it to a 9-inch pie dish. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edges.
  3. Spread the spinach and mushroom mixture evenly over the bottom of the crust.
  4. Pour the tofu mixture over the spinach and mushrooms, spreading it out to cover the entire surface.

Step 5: Bake the Quiche

  1. Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.
  2. Remove the quiche from the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Serve and Enjoy!

This Vegan Spinach and Mushroom Quiche is a delightful combination of earthy mushrooms, vibrant spinach, and creamy tofu filling, all nestled in a flaky, buttery crust. Whether you serve it for a leisurely weekend brunch or as part of a special occasion spread, this quiche is sure to be a hit with everyone at the table.
