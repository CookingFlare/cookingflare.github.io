Coconut Lime Tarts: A Tropical and Refreshing Vegan Dessert


As the weather warms up, our taste buds crave something light, refreshing, and indulgent. Coconut lime tarts fit the bill perfectly, offering a tropical escape from the ordinary. These vegan treats are not only a delight to the senses but also a great way to beat the heat and satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup ice-cold water

For the filling:

  • 1 cup full-fat coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, unsweetened shredded coconut, confectioners’ sugar, and salt. Add the vegan butter and use a pastry blender or your fingertips to work it into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add the ice-cold water, stirring with a fork until the dough comes together in a ball. Wrap it in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Cut out circles of dough using a cookie cutter or the rim of a glass. You should be able to get about 12 circles.
  4. Place the dough circles into a muffin tin, pressing them into the corners and up the sides of the cups. Trim any excess dough from the edges.
  5. In a blender or food processor, combine the coconut cream, granulated sugar, lime juice, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  6. Spoon the filling mixture into the dough-lined muffin cups, filling them about 3/4 of the way full. Sprinkle the tops with unsweetened shredded coconut.
  7. Bake the tarts for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges of the crust are golden brown. Remove them from the oven and let them cool completely in the pan.
  8. Once cooled, refrigerate the tarts for at least 2 hours or overnight to allow the filling to set.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra layer of flavor, drizzle the cooled tarts with a little maple syrup or agave nectar.
  • Substitute the lime juice with lemon or orange juice for a different twist.
  • Add a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes or chopped nuts to the filling for added texture.
  • These tarts freeze well, so feel free to make a batch and store them in an airtight container for up to 3 months. Simply thaw at room temperature or refrigerate overnight.

Conclusion

Coconut lime tarts are a refreshing and indulgent treat that’s perfect for warm weather. With their tropical flavors and creamy texture, they’re sure to become a new favorite. Whether you’re a vegan or just looking for a dairy-free dessert option, these tarts are a great choice. So go ahead, give them a try, and let the tropical flavors transport you to a state of sweet bliss.
