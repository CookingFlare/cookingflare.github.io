The Keto Diaries - Always Hungry? How to Satisfy Your Cravings with 100 Keto-Friendly Recipes


Are you always hungry or struggling to maintain a ketogenic diet? The ketogenic, or keto, diet, has taken the world by storm, promising weight loss and a host of health benefits. However, if you’re following this diet and still find yourself craving all the delicious foods you once enjoyed, it’s time to ditch those cravings for good with these 100 keto-friendly recipes.

Delight your taste buds while keeping your carb intake minimal with these mouthwatering dishes. From breakfast to dinner, and even desserts, we’ve got you covered. Ready to become a master chef and adhere to your keto lifestyle? Let’s dive in.

Breakfast

1. Fluffy Scrambled Eggs with Avocado and Feta

Give your morning routine a refreshing twist by combining protein-packed scrambled eggs, creamy avocado, and salty feta cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 ripe avocado, mashed
  • 1 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl, ensuring the yolks and whites are well combined.
  2. Melt a teaspoon of butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the beaten eggs and scramble until cooked.
  4. In a separate pan, melt a small amount of butter and add the mashed avocado. Cook until heated through.
  5. To assemble the dish, create two parts on the plate - one for the scrambled eggs and the other for the avocado.
  6. Sprinkle the feta cheese on top and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Keto Pancakes

Sink your teeth into fluffy, buttery pancakes, guilt-free with this simple keto recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 4 tbsp erythritol or sweetener of choice
  • Fresh berries, to serve (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. In a blender, combine the eggs, heavy whipping cream, vanilla, almond flour, melted butter, and sweetener. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet, creating a circular shape.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles form on the surface. Flip, and cook the other side for another 2 minutes.
  5. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  6. Serve with fresh berries, if desired.

Lunch

3. Caprese Stuffed Avocado

Give the classic caprese salad a new twist by stuffing it inside a creamy avocado.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 2 oz fresh mozzarella, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Slice the avocado in half, remove the pit, and gently scoop out some of the avocado flesh to create a well.
  2. In a bowl, combine the diced avocado flesh with the tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stuff the mixture back into the avocado halves and serve immediately.

4. Zucchini Noodle Pesto

Revamp your pasta habits with this easy-to-make, low-carb version featuring zucchini noodles and a homemade pesto sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium zucchinis, spiralized
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2/3 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the spiralized zucchini noodles in a microwave-safe dish, and microwave for 2 minutes to soften.
  2. In a food processor, combine the basil, garlic, olive oil, pine nuts, Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Blend until a smooth pesto forms.
  3. Toss the zucchini noodles in the pesto sauce until well-coated.
  4. Garnish with extra Parmesan cheese and serve.

Dinner

5. Cheeseburger Soup

Transform your classic cheeseburger and fries into a hearty soup, while keeping it keto-friendly.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped pickles
  • Fresh chives, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened.
  2. Add the beef broth, heavy cream, and ground beef. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Stir in the black pepper, cheddar cheese, and pickles. Cook until the cheese is melted.
  4. Garnish with fresh chives and serve immediately.

6. Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp

Upgrade your shrimp dish with a flavor-packed lemon garlic butter sauce that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • 12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the shrimp with salt and pepper.
  2. In a medium skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the shrimp, cooking for 2 minutes on each side until pink.
  3. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Add the lemon juice, zest, and garlic to the skillet. Cook for 2 minutes, or until fragrant.
  5. Return the shrimp to the skillet, spooning the sauce over them. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with a side of zucchini noodles or cauliflower rice for that complete keto experience.

Dessert

7. Almond Flour Chocolate Chip Cookies

Enjoy your favorite chocolate chip cookies sans the guilt, thanks to this almond flour-based version.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup erythritol or sweetener of choice
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350¬∞F (180¬∞C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine the almond flour, softened butter, sweetener, egg, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop 1 tbsp of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each cookie.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until lightly golden.
  6. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack.
