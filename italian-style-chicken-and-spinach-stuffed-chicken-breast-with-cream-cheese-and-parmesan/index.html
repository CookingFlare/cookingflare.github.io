Italian-Style Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast with Cream Cheese and Parmesan


Are you looking for a delicious and impressive dish to serve at your next dinner party? Look no further than this Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast with cream cheese and Parmesan. This flavorful recipe is sure to impress your guests and leave them asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly incorporated.

  3. Lay the chicken breasts flat on a cutting board and make a lengthwise incision in each breast, being careful not to cut all the way through. This will create a pocket for the stuffing.

  4. Stuff each chicken breast with a generous amount of the cream cheese mixture, using a spoon or your fingers to evenly distribute it. Be sure not to overstuff, as this may cause the filling to spill out while cooking.

  5. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the stuffed chicken breasts in the skillet. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is golden brown.

  6. Transfer the browned chicken breasts to a baking dish and place them in the preheated oven. Bake for approximately 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

  7. While the chicken is baking, prepare the spinach by heating a separate skillet over medium heat. Add a drizzle of olive oil and the fresh spinach leaves. Sauté until the spinach wilts, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and set aside.

  8. Once the chicken is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy chicken.

  9. To serve, place a bed of sautéed spinach on each plate and top it with a stuffed chicken breast. Garnish with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for an extra burst of flavor.

Final Thoughts

This Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast with cream cheese and Parmesan is a delightful dish that combines the flavors of Italy with a touch of elegance. The creamy filling and tender chicken make for a perfect pairing, while the sautéed spinach adds a refreshing and nutritious element to the plate.

Whether you’re looking to impress your dinner guests or simply want to enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal, this recipe is a must-try. The combination of flavors and textures will leave you craving more.

So, why wait? Gather the ingredients, follow the simple instructions, and get ready to savor a taste of Italy in the comfort of your own home. Buon appetito!
