Magical Mushroom Meals: 15 Irresistible Vegan Recipes Starring the Humble Fungi


Mushrooms are among nature’s most versatile and nourishing ingredients. These mighty microorganisms offer a distinct earthy flavor and incomparable texture that elevate any dish to gourmet status. In this post, we’ve gathered 15 vegan recipes featuring mushrooms as the star ingredient. These Magical Mushroom Meals are sure to enchant you and your dinner guests. Let’s get started on our culinary journey!

1. Roasted Garlic and Mushroom Soup

  • Ingredients: garlic, mushrooms, olive oil, vegetable broth, salt, pepper, and thyme
  • Instructions: Begin by roasting garlic and mushrooms until tender. Then, blend the ingredients with vegetable broth, olive oil, salt, pepper, and thyme until smooth. Reheat and adjust seasoning as necessary.

2. Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Pasta Shells

  • Ingredients: jumbo pasta shells, mushrooms, spinach, marinara sauce, cashew cheese, nutritional yeast, and garlic
  • Instructions: Cook pasta shells and sauté mushrooms with spinach, garlic, and a touch of nutritional yeast. Stuff each shell with the mixture and top with marinara sauce. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

3. Mushroom Risotto

  • Ingredients: Arborio rice, mushrooms, vegetable broth, onion, garlic, white wine, olive oil, and fresh herbs
  • Instructions: Sauté onion and garlic, then add rice, stirring until lightly toasted. Deglaze with white wine and gradually add broth one ladle at a time. Incorporate sautéed mushrooms and fresh herbs for the finishing touch.

4. Mushroom Lentil Shepherd’s Pie

  • Ingredients: mushrooms, lentils, carrots, onion, garlic, canned diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, potato, olive oil, and thyme
  • Instructions: Cook lentils and prepare a mashed potato topping. Then, sauté vegetables with mushrooms, herbs, and seasonings to create a savory filling. Pour the lentil mixture into a baking dish and top with mashed potatoes, baking until golden and bubbling.

5. Mushroom and Tofu Stir-Fry

  • Ingredients: firm tofu, mushrooms, carrots, bell pepper, snow peas, sauces, and rice
  • Instructions: Press and crumble tofu, then sauté with vegetables until tender. Add a variety of sauces (such as soy, oyster sauce, or vegan mushroom broth) and serve over rice for an Asian-inspired dish.

6. Vegan Mushroom Risotto Cakes

  • Ingredients: leftover mushroom risotto, breadcrumbs, vegetable stock, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, and flour
  • Instructions: Combine risotto and seasonings in a bowl, then form into patties and coat with breadcrumbs. Fry the patties in a skillet until crispy and golden.

7. Mushroom Stroganoff

  • Ingredients: mushrooms, onions, garlic, vegetable broth, cream cheese, vegan sour cream, flour, and thyme
  • Instructions: Cook the shrooms with onions and garlic, then blend with sour cream and cream cheese to create a luscious sauce. Thicken the mixture using a slurry made from flour and vegetable broth.

8. Stuffed Mushrooms

  • Ingredients: large or small mushrooms, breadcrumbs, garlic, rosemary, pine nuts, and nutritional yeast
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Carefully remove stems from mushrooms and finely chop them. Combine stems with breadcrumb mixture and stuff into caps. Bake for 20 minutes or until tender and crispy.

9. Vegan Mushroom Bourguignon

  • Ingredients: mushrooms, onions, garlic, carrots, tomato paste, red wine, vegetable broth, pearl onions, and squashed potatoes
  • Instructions: Sauté mirepoix (onions, carrots, and celery) with garlic, then add tomato paste and red wine. Eventually, mix in mushrooms, cover with broth, and simmer for approximately one hour until flavors are well blended. Pearl onions and squashed potatoes help complement the flavors of this classic dish.

10. Mushroom and Kale Quiche

  • Ingredients: pre-made or homemade pie crust, tofu, mushrooms, kale, garlic, nutritional yeast, spring onions, and vegan cheese
  • Instructions: Bake pie crust according to package instructions. Combine tofu, sautéed mushrooms, and kale in a food processor. Mix in the rest of the ingredients, then pour into the crust and bake until set.

11. Mushroom Pâté

  • Ingredients: shiitake mushrooms, walnuts, garlic, vegan mayonnaise, nutritional yeast, and lemon juice
  • Instructions: Sauté garlic and shiitakes, then mix them with walnuts, vegan mayonnaise, nutritional yeast, and lemon juice. Blend until smooth, adjusting flavors as necessary.

12. Mushroom and Brussels Sprouts Hash

  • Ingredients: Brussels sprouts, mushrooms, onion, garlic, vegan cheese, and homemade vegan bacon bits
  • Instructions: Roast Brussels sprouts first, and sauté onions and garlic. Add mushrooms and combine everything on a preheated skillet; sprinkle with vegan cheese and bacon bits before serving.

13. Vegan Mushroom Mac and Cheese

  • Ingredients: macaroni, cashews, nutritional yeast, garlic, onion, turmeric, smoked paprika, white or cremini mushrooms, and lemon juice
  • Instructions: Cook macaroni; meanwhile, mix cashews, nutritional yeast, garlic, onion, and spices in a food processor. Blend in water until a smooth consistency is achieved. Sauté mushrooms, then mix them all together. Serve immediately, or bake for a creamier texture.

14. Mushroom Polenta Casserole

  • Ingredients: mushrooms, onion, garlic, polenta, vegetable broth, and vegan cheese
  • Instructions: Cook polenta in vegetable broth, then spread it into a baking dish. Sauté mushrooms and onion, and layer everything before finishing with vegan cheese. Bake until hot and bubbly.

15. Mushroom and Seitan Stroganoff

  • Ingredients: seitan, mushrooms, onion, garlic, vegan cream cheese, vegan sour cream, flour, and thyme
  • Instructions: Cook seitan strips in a skillet and set aside. Sauté onions and garlic, then add mushrooms and thyme. Blend flour and vegetable broth, then mix in cream cheese and sour cream. Add seitan back to the skillet and stir to combine, then serve over rice or noodles.

Each of these Magical Mushroom Meals is a testament to the incredible flavors that can be unlocked with the help of humble fungi. Wasn’t that an unexpected culinary adventure? May these recipes inspire you to bring an earthy twist to your plant-based creations and share with those you cherish. Enjoy the magic of mushrooms!
