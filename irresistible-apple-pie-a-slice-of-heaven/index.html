Irresistible Apple Pie: A Slice of Heaven


There’s something undeniably comforting about the aroma of a freshly baked apple pie wafting through the kitchen. The golden, flaky crust, the sweet and tangy filling, and the warm spices all come together to create a dessert that is truly a slice of heaven. In this recipe, I’ll share with you the secrets to creating an irresistible apple pie that will have your friends and family coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 6-7 large apples, such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

For the Egg Wash:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon milk

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar. Add the cold cubed butter.
  2. Use a pastry cutter or your hands to work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together.
  4. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with lemon juice to prevent browning.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the granulated sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and cornstarch.
  3. Add the sugar mixture to the sliced apples and toss until the apples are evenly coated.

Step 3: Assemble the Pie

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disk of the chilled dough into a circle large enough to fit into a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to the pie dish and gently press it into the bottom and sides.
  4. Pour the apple filling into the prepared crust, then dot the filling with small pieces of butter.
  5. Roll out the second disk of dough and place it over the filling. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal.

Step 4: Bake the Pie

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg and milk to create the egg wash.
  2. Brush the top crust with the egg wash and sprinkle with a little sugar for a golden finish.
  3. Use a sharp knife to make a few small vents in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  4. Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake for 45-55 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

Step 5: Enjoy!

Allow the pie to cool for at least 1 hour before slicing to allow the filling to set. Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for the ultimate indulgence.

Conclusion

There’s no denying the allure of a perfectly baked apple pie. Whether it’s for a special occasion or just a cozy night in, this irresistible apple pie recipe is sure to become a favorite in your dessert repertoire. The combination of buttery crust, perfectly spiced apples, and the satisfaction of creating a homemade treat from scratch is truly a slice of heaven. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the delicious rewards of this classic dessert.
