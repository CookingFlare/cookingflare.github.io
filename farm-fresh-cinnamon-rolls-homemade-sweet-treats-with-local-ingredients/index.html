Farm Fresh Cinnamon Rolls: Homemade Sweet Treats with Local Ingredients


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls wafting through the kitchen. The combination of warm spices, soft dough, and gooey icing is enough to make anyone’s mouth water. And when those cinnamon rolls are made with farm-fresh, local ingredients, the taste is simply unparalleled. In this recipe, we’ll take you through the steps of making your own Farm Fresh Cinnamon Rolls, ensuring a delightful and satisfying treat for your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Set aside.

  2. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm milk. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  3. Pour the yeast mixture into the dry ingredients. Add the melted butter and egg. Mix until a soft dough forms.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes or until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Allow it to rise in a warm place for approximately 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  6. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon until well combined.

  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Roll it out into a rectangular shape, about 1/4 inch thick.

  8. Spread the filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  9. Starting from the long side, tightly roll the dough into a log. Cut the log into 12 equal-sized pieces.

  10. Place the cinnamon rolls in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each roll. Cover with the kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  11. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  12. Once the rolls have risen, bake them in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until they turn golden brown.

  13. While the rolls are baking, prepare the icing. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  14. Remove the cinnamon rolls from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before drizzling the icing on top.

  15. Serve the Farm Fresh Cinnamon Rolls warm and enjoy the heavenly combination of flavors and textures!

Conclusion

Making your own Farm Fresh Cinnamon Rolls allows you to savor the goodness of local ingredients while indulging in a sweet treat. The process may take a little time and effort, but the end result is definitely worth it. The soft, pillowy dough, the irresistible cinnamon filling, and the creamy icing make these cinnamon rolls a true delight for any occasion.

So, gather your farm-fresh ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to experience the homemade goodness of Farm Fresh Cinnamon Rolls. Your taste buds will thank you!
