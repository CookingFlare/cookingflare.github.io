Tempting Mexican Chocolate Churro Cupcakes: A Sweet Twist on a Classic Treat


If you’re a fan of traditional churros and the rich, decadent flavors of Mexican chocolate, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful fusion dessert. These tempting Mexican Chocolate Churro Cupcakes take the beloved churro to a whole new level by combining it with the indulgent taste of Mexican chocolate. The result is a delectable treat that perfectly balances the warm, cinnamon-infused goodness of churros with the deep, complex flavors of Mexican chocolate. Whether you’re hosting a fiesta or simply craving a unique and irresistible dessert, these cupcakes are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup boiling water

For the Churro Topping:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Mexican Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for a spicy kick)

Instructions

Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Gradually stir in the boiling water, mixing until the batter is smooth.
  5. Fill each cupcake liner about 2/3 full with the batter.
  6. Bake for 20-22 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Churro Topping:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and cinnamon for the churro topping.
  2. Once the cupcakes have cooled, brush the tops with melted butter and dip them into the cinnamon sugar mixture to coat.

Mexican Chocolate Ganache:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it begins to simmer.
  2. Place the chopped chocolate, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper (if using) in a heatproof bowl.
  3. Pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Gently whisk the mixture until the chocolate is completely melted and the ganache is smooth and glossy.
  5. Allow the ganache to cool for a few minutes before spooning it over the churro-topped cupcakes.

These Mexican Chocolate Churro Cupcakes are a delightful combination of textures and flavors, with the soft, moist cake complemented by the crunchy, sugary churro topping and the luxurious, spiced chocolate ganache. Whether you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine or simply looking for a unique and indulgent dessert to impress your friends and family, these cupcakes are a surefire hit. Serve them at your next gathering or enjoy them as a special treat for yourself – either way, they are bound to leave a lasting impression.

The fusion of Mexican chocolate and churro flavors in these cupcakes offers a delightful twist on a beloved classic, making them a standout dessert that will have everyone reaching for seconds. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the tempting allure of Mexican Chocolate Churro Cupcakes!
