Vegan Banana Pudding with Nilla Wafers Recipe: A Creamy and Delicious Dessert


If you’re craving a creamy and indulgent dessert that’s entirely plant-based, look no further than this Vegan Banana Pudding with Nilla Wafers recipe. This delightful treat is perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth while also being dairy-free and egg-free. Whether you’re a committed vegan or simply looking to explore new dessert options, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your collection.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package (about 12 ounces) Nilla Wafers
  • Fresh berries for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Banana Pudding

Start by peeling the ripe bananas and slicing them into rounds. In a medium saucepan, combine the sliced bananas and coconut milk over medium heat. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 5 minutes, until the bananas soften.

In a small bowl, whisk the cornstarch with a few tablespoons of water to create a slurry. Gradually pour the cornstarch slurry into the banana and coconut milk mixture, stirring constantly. Continue to cook the pudding until it thickens, which should take about 5-7 minutes.

Once the pudding has thickened, remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix until well combined and smooth.

Step 2: Assemble the Banana Pudding Layers

In a serving dish or individual dessert cups, begin by layering the bottom with Nilla Wafers. Spoon a generous portion of the banana pudding over the wafers, creating a smooth and even layer. Repeat this process to create multiple layers, alternating between Nilla Wafers and the creamy banana pudding.

Step 3: Chill and Serve

Once the layers are assembled, cover the dish or individual cups with plastic wrap and place them in the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours. This chilling time allows the flavors to meld together, resulting in a rich and luscious dessert.

Before serving, you can garnish the pudding with additional Nilla Wafers or fresh berries for a pop of color and extra sweetness.

Enjoy Your Vegan Delight!

This Vegan Banana Pudding with Nilla Wafers is a delightful combination of creamy, sweet, and comforting flavors that are sure to please everyone at the table. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special dessert, this recipe offers a guilt-free indulgence that’s both satisfying and delicious. The smooth texture of the banana pudding paired with the crunch of the Nilla Wafers creates a delightful contrast that will leave you craving more.

Give this recipe a try and discover just how satisfying and decadent vegan desserts can be. With its simple ingredients and straightforward preparation, this Vegan Banana Pudding with Nilla Wafers is a must-try for anyone who loves a good dessert, vegan or not. So, gather your ingredients, whip up a batch, and savor every spoonful of this luscious treat. Cheers to delightful vegan indulgence!
