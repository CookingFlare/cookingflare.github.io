A Delicious Italian-Style Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Shells Recipe


As the aroma of freshly baked pasta fills the air, the anticipation of a delicious meal builds. One of the most beloved Italian dishes is stuffed shells, and when combined with the savory flavors of chicken and spinach, it’s a match made in culinary heaven. In this recipe, we’ll explore the art of creating a mouthwatering Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed shells dish that’s sure to impress even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients

For the chicken and spinach filling:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • 1 package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano

For the shells:

  • 12 jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the chicken and spinach filling: In a medium bowl, combine the cooked chicken, spinach, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, salt, pepper, basil, and oregano. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  2. Cook the pasta shells: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta shells according to package instructions until they’re al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Assemble the stuffed shells: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the cooked pasta shells and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they’re lightly browned. This step helps prevent the shells from sticking together when they’re filled.
  4. Fill the shells: Stuff each cooked pasta shell with the chicken and spinach filling, placing them in a single layer in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Top with marinara sauce and cheese: Pour the marinara sauce over the stuffed shells, followed by the shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake until golden and bubbly: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some extra flavor, sprinkle some chopped fresh parsley or basil on top of the dish before baking.
  • For a spicy kick, add some red pepper flakes to the chicken and spinach filling.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, mix some heavy cream or Greek yogurt with the marinara sauce before pouring it over the shells.

Conclusion

Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed shells is a dish that’s sure to become a staple in your household. With its rich flavors and satisfying texture, it’s a perfect meal for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a comforting dinner or a delicious lunch, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a culinary experience that will leave you wanting more. Buon appetito!
