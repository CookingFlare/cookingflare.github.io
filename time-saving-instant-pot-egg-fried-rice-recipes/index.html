Time-Saving Instant Pot Egg Fried Rice Recipes


If you’re a fan of quick and easy meals that don’t skimp on flavor, then you’re in for a treat with these time-saving Instant Pot egg fried rice recipes. The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way many of us cook, providing a convenient and efficient way to prepare delicious meals without spending hours in the kitchen. With these recipes, you’ll be able to whip up a satisfying and flavorful egg fried rice in no time, perfect for busy weeknights or whenever you’re craving a comforting meal.

Classic Instant Pot Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of frozen peas and carrots
  • 1/2 cup of diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Start by rinsing the white rice under cold water and then draining it.
  2. In the Instant Pot, add the rinsed rice along with 2 1/4 cups of water.
  3. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to Manual mode for 3 minutes at high pressure.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, and then do a quick release of any remaining pressure.
  5. While the rice is cooking, beat the eggs in a bowl and set aside.
  6. After releasing the pressure from the Instant Pot, open the lid and fluff the rice with a fork.
  7. Turn on the sauté function and add the sesame oil, diced onion, and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion becomes translucent.
  8. Add the beaten eggs to the Instant Pot and stir continuously until they are fully cooked and scrambled.
  9. Toss in the frozen peas and carrots, and continue to sauté for a couple of minutes.
  10. Stir in the cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing well to ensure everything is evenly coated.
  11. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  12. Once everything is heated through, garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.

This classic Instant Pot egg fried rice recipe is a crowd-pleaser and makes for a satisfying meal on its own or as a side dish to accompany your favorite Asian-inspired dishes.

Vegetarian Instant Pot Egg Fried Rice

For those looking for a meatless option, this vegetarian Instant Pot egg fried rice recipe is packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of brown rice
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup of diced tofu
  • 3 tablespoons of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the brown rice under cold water and drain.
  2. In the Instant Pot, combine the rinsed brown rice with 2 1/2 cups of water.
  3. Close the lid and cook on Manual mode for 22 minutes at high pressure.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then release any remaining pressure.
  5. While the rice is cooking, scramble the eggs in a bowl and set aside.
  6. After releasing the pressure, fluff the rice with a fork and transfer it to a separate bowl.
  7. Using the sauté function, add a bit of oil to the Instant Pot and cook the diced tofu until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  8. In the same pot, add a little more oil and sauté the mixed vegetables until they are tender-crisp.
  9. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for another minute.
  10. Push the vegetables to one side of the pot and pour the beaten eggs into the other side, scrambling until cooked.
  11. Combine the cooked rice and scrambled eggs with the vegetables in the Instant Pot.
  12. Drizzle with low-sodium soy sauce and rice vinegar, tossing everything together until well combined.
  13. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.

This vegetarian Instant Pot egg fried rice is a flavorful and nutritious option that is sure to become a household favorite, even among meat lovers.

Conclusion

With these time-saving Instant Pot egg fried rice recipes, you can enjoy the classic flavors of this beloved dish with minimal effort and maximum satisfaction. Whether you opt for the classic version or the vegetarian option, these recipes are perfect for those busy days when you crave a homemade meal without the extensive prep and clean-up. So fire up your Instant Pot and treat yourself to a delicious and fulfilling meal that’s ready in no time!
