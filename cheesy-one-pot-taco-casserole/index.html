Cheesy One Pot Taco Casserole


If you’re a fan of hearty, flavorful meals that are easy to make and require minimal cleanup, then you’re in for a treat with this Cheesy One Pot Taco Casserole recipe. This dish is a delightful fusion of Mexican and American flavors, combining the zesty taste of tacos with the comforting, cheesy goodness of a casserole. Not only is it a crowd-pleaser, but it’s also a fantastic way to satisfy your craving for a delicious, fuss-free dinner. Let’s dive into the details of how to whip up this delectable Cheesy One Pot Taco Casserole in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 ½ cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sour cream (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Once the beef is browned, add the diced onion, red bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables have softened.

  2. Season and Simmer: Sprinkle the taco seasoning over the beef and vegetables, then add the diced tomatoes, corn, and black beans. Stir everything together and let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  3. Add the Rice and Cheese: Stir in the cooked rice until it’s well combined with the beef and vegetable mixture. Reduce the heat to low, then sprinkle the shredded cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese over the top.

  4. Bake to Perfection: Preheat your oven’s broiler. Transfer the skillet to the oven and broil for 3-5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Once out of the oven, garnish the Cheesy One Pot Taco Casserole with chopped cilantro. Serve hot, with a dollop of sour cream if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • Vegetarian Option: For a vegetarian twist, you can substitute the ground beef with plant-based crumbles or tofu, and use vegetable broth instead of beef broth.
  • Spice it Up: If you prefer your casserole with an extra kick, you can add some diced jalapeños or a sprinkle of cayenne pepper to the mix.
  • Tortilla Chips Topping: Before baking, consider adding a layer of crushed tortilla chips over the cheese for a crunchy, flavorful topping.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Cheesy One Pot Taco Casserole is a fantastic way to bring the flavors of a classic taco dish into a comforting casserole format. It’s a versatile recipe that allows for personalization, making it suitable for family dinners, potlucks, or casual gatherings. The best part is that it all comes together in one pot, minimizing the cleanup and maximizing the enjoyment. Give this recipe a try, and watch it become a beloved favorite in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy!
