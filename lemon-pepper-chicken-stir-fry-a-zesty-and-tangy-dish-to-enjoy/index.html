Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry: A Zesty and Tangy Dish to Enjoy


If you’re looking for a quick, flavorful, and healthy meal, look no further than this delicious Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry recipe. Packed with zesty and tangy flavors, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Whether you’re a busy professional, a parent on the go, or just someone who loves good food, this recipe is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

To start off, let’s gather the ingredients you’ll need to make this mouthwatering Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas, trimmed
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

Now that you have all the ingredients, let’s dive into the cooking process:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with a pinch of salt, black pepper, and 1 tablespoon of lemon juice. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 15 minutes to absorb the flavors.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a separate bowl, whisk together the remaining lemon juice, lemon zest, soy sauce, honey, and cornstarch. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Cook the Chicken: Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a plate and set it aside.

  4. Stir Fry the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Add the bell peppers, snap peas, broccoli, and minced garlic. Stir fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes or until they are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Allow the mixture to simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.

  6. Serve: Once the sauce has thickened to your liking, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh lemon zest for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

Enjoy Your Culinary Creation

Congratulations! You’ve just prepared a mouthwatering Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry that’s bursting with zesty and tangy flavors. This dish is not only delicious but also packed with protein and an assortment of colorful vegetables, making it a wholesome meal for you and your loved ones.

The next time you’re craving a satisfying meal that’s as easy to make as it is flavorful, remember this recipe. Whether it’s for a quick weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with friends, this Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry is sure to impress. So, grab your skillet and get ready to savor the zesty and tangy goodness of this delightful dish!
