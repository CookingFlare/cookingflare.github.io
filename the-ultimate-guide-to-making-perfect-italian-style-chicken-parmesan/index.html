The Ultimate Guide to Making Perfect Italian-Style Chicken Parmesan


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, then you’ve probably heard of Chicken Parmesan. This classic dish is a staple in many Italian-American households and is loved for its crispy chicken, gooey cheese, and rich tomato sauce. In this ultimate guide, we will take you through the step-by-step process of creating the perfect Italian-style Chicken Parmesan. So, roll up your sleeves, put on your apron, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

For the chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

For the tomato sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the assembly:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Start by preparing the tomato sauce. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. While the sauce is simmering, it’s time to bread the chicken. Set up three shallow bowls: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with a mixture of breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper.

  5. Dip each chicken breast into the flour, making sure to coat both sides evenly. Shake off any excess flour and then dip the chicken into the beaten eggs, followed by the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere the breadcrumbs to the chicken.

  6. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Fry the breaded chicken breasts for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they turn golden brown and crispy. Transfer the chicken to the prepared baking sheet.

  7. Spoon a generous amount of tomato sauce over each chicken breast, covering them completely. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.

  8. Bake the Chicken Parmesan in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the chicken is cooked through.

  9. Once out of the oven, let the Chicken Parmesan rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an extra touch of freshness.

Serving Suggestions

Chicken Parmesan is delicious on its own, but you can elevate your meal by serving it with spaghetti or linguine tossed in olive oil and garlic. A side of steamed vegetables or a simple green salad would also complement the dish perfectly.

Final Thoughts

Making perfect Italian-style Chicken Parmesan may seem intimidating at first, but with this ultimate guide, you’ll be able to create a restaurant-quality dish right in your own kitchen. Remember to take your time, follow the steps carefully, and don’t be afraid to add your own personal touch. Buon appetito!
