Lemongrass Chicken Ramen: A Fusion Noodle Bowl


If you’re a fan of both Asian and Western flavors, then this Lemongrass Chicken Ramen recipe is the perfect fusion dish for you. Combining the aromatic and citrusy notes of lemongrass with the comforting and soul-soothing goodness of ramen, this noodle bowl is a delightful blend of East meets West. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 lemongrass stalks, bruised and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 4 packs of ramen noodles
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add the lemongrass stalks and chicken broth. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 10 minutes to infuse the broth with the lemongrass flavor.

  2. While the broth is simmering, season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a separate pan, heat some oil over medium-high heat and sear the chicken breasts until they are golden brown and cooked through. Set aside to rest.

  3. In the same pan, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  4. Add the soy sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, and chili flakes to the pan. Stir well to combine the flavors.

  5. Pour the sauce mixture into the pot with the lemongrass-infused broth. Let it simmer for another 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. While the broth is simmering, cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  7. Once the broth is ready, remove the lemongrass stalks from the pot. Slice the rested chicken breasts into thin strips.

  8. To serve, divide the cooked ramen noodles among the serving bowls. Ladle the lemongrass-infused broth over the noodles, making sure to distribute the chicken evenly.

  9. Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra zing.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian version, replace the chicken broth with vegetable broth and omit the chicken. You can add tofu or your favorite vegetables instead.

  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a few slices of fresh chili pepper or a drizzle of sriracha sauce to your bowl.

  • Experiment with different toppings such as sliced scallions, bean sprouts, or a soft-boiled egg for added texture and flavor.

  • To make it a complete meal, serve the Lemongrass Chicken Ramen with a side of steamed vegetables or a refreshing cucumber salad.

Final Thoughts

Lemongrass Chicken Ramen is a unique and flavorful fusion dish that brings together the best of both worlds. The combination of lemongrass, chicken, and aromatic spices creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re a noodle lover or an adventurous foodie, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a bowl of comforting and aromatic Lemongrass Chicken Ramen!
