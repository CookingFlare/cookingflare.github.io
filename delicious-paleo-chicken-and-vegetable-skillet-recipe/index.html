Delicious Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Skillet Recipe


Are you looking for a healthy, flavorful, and easy-to-make dinner option? Look no further than this delicious Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Skillet recipe. Packed with protein, vitamins, and minerals, this dish is not only satisfying but also a perfect fit for those following a paleo lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, season the chicken strips with paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Make sure the chicken is well coated with the seasoning.

  2. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken strips and cook until browned and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes. Once done, transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Add the bell peppers, zucchini, and yellow squash. Sauté for 4-5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, add the cherry tomatoes, and stir everything together. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh parsley.

  6. Serve the Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Skillet hot and enjoy a wholesome, nutrient-packed meal that’s as delightful to the taste buds as it is to the eyes.

Why Choose This Recipe?

This Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Skillet recipe is a fantastic choice for anyone seeking a healthy, gluten-free, and dairy-free meal option. The combination of lean protein from the chicken and an assortment of vibrant, nutrient-rich vegetables makes this dish a powerhouse of essential nutrients. Moreover, the use of olive oil adds healthy fats and a rich flavor to the dish, while the seasoning blend elevates the overall taste without compromising the paleo principles.

Nutritional Benefits

This recipe is not only delectable but also offers a myriad of nutritional benefits. The chicken provides high-quality protein, essential for muscle repair and overall body function. The bell peppers, zucchini, yellow squash, and cherry tomatoes contribute an array of vitamins, antioxidants, and fiber, promoting good digestion, skin health, and immune function. Additionally, the olive oil used in this recipe offers heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, which have been associated with numerous health benefits.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Delicious Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Skillet recipe is a testament to the fact that healthy eating can indeed be delicious and satisfying. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking to incorporate more nutritious meals into your routine, this recipe is a must-try. It’s quick to make, bursting with flavor, and provides a wholesome balance of macronutrients and micronutrients. So, roll up your sleeves, gather the ingredients, and treat yourself to a nourishing culinary experience with this delightful Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Skillet. Cheers to your health and happiness!
