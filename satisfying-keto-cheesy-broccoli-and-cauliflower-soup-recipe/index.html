Satisfying Keto Cheesy Broccoli and Cauliflower Soup Recipe


Are you looking for a comforting and satisfying keto-friendly soup that’s perfect for chilly evenings or a cozy dinner at home? Look no further than this delicious and creamy keto cheesy broccoli and cauliflower soup. Packed with nutritious vegetables and rich, cheesy flavor, this soup is a must-try for anyone following a low-carb or keto lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: crispy bacon, green onions, extra shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Start by steaming the broccoli and cauliflower until they are tender but still slightly firm. This can be done on the stovetop or in the microwave. Set aside.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  3. Add the Broth: Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  4. Blend the Soup: Carefully transfer the soup to a blender or use an immersion blender to purée the mixture until smooth.

  5. Add the Cream and Cheese: Return the blended soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream and shredded cheddar cheese. Continue to cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the cheese is fully melted and the soup is creamy and well combined.

  6. Season to Perfection: Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting as needed to suit your preferences.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with your choice of optional toppings, such as crispy bacon, sliced green onions, or extra shredded cheese.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 cup
  • Calories: 280
  • Total Fat: 22g
  • Saturated Fat: 14g
  • Cholesterol: 70mg
  • Sodium: 560mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 8g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Sugars: 3g
  • Protein: 13g

Why This Soup is Perfect for Keto Dieters

This keto cheesy broccoli and cauliflower soup is a fantastic choice for those following a ketogenic diet. Broccoli and cauliflower are low in carbohydrates and high in fiber, making them excellent choices for keto-friendly recipes. The addition of heavy cream and cheese provides healthy fats while keeping the carb count low. This soup is not only delicious but also incredibly satisfying, making it easier to stick to your keto goals without feeling deprived.

Final Thoughts

This keto cheesy broccoli and cauliflower soup is a comforting and flavorful dish that’s sure to become a staple in your keto recipe collection. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or a dish to impress guests, this creamy soup is a winner. With its rich, cheesy flavor and nutritious ingredients, it’s the perfect way to warm up on a cold day while staying true to your keto lifestyle.

So, why not give this recipe a try and savor the goodness of a warm, satisfying bowl of keto cheesy broccoli and cauliflower soup? Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
