Satisfying Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan Recipes


If you’re looking for a delightful and satisfying meal that combines the classic flavors of chicken parmesan with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you’re in for a treat! This blog post explores a variety of Instant Pot chicken parmesan recipes that are not only easy to prepare but also bursting with flavor. From the traditional chicken parmesan to creative twists, these recipes are sure to impress your family and friends. Let’s dive into the delicious world of Instant Pot chicken parmesan.

What is Chicken Parmesan?

Chicken parmesan, or pollo alla parmigiana, is a beloved Italian-American dish comprising breaded chicken cutlets topped with marinara sauce and melted cheese. Traditionally, it is baked in the oven, but the Instant Pot allows for a quicker and equally delicious preparation. The result is tender, flavorful chicken enveloped in a savory sauce, making it perfect for a cozy family dinner or a festive gathering.

Why Use an Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot is a multi-functional kitchen appliance that combines the features of a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, and more. Using an Instant Pot to prepare chicken parmesan offers several benefits:

  • Speed: The pressure cooking feature significantly reduces cooking time compared to traditional methods.
  • Flavor: Cooking under pressure enhances the flavors, resulting in tender and juicy chicken.
  • Convenience: With its one-pot design, cleanup is a breeze, making it perfect for busy weeknights.

Classic Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (Italian-seasoned preferred)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh basil, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and dried oregano. This step enhances the flavor profile right from the start.

  2. Sear the Chicken: Turn the Instant Pot to the sauté function and add olive oil. Once heated, sear the chicken breasts for about 3-4 minutes on each side until they are golden brown. This step locks in the juices and adds a rich flavor.

  3. Add Marinara Sauce: Pour the marinara sauce over the seared chicken. Make sure the chicken is well coated, as this will infuse flavor during the cooking process.

  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid of the Instant Pot, ensuring the valve is set to sealing. Select the manual or pressure cook function and set the timer for 8 minutes. The Instant Pot will take a few minutes to come to pressure before the cooking time begins.

  5. Release Pressure: Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release by turning the valve to venting. Carefully open the lid when the pressure has fully released.

  6. Add Cheese: Sprinkle the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the chicken and cover the pot with the lid again. Let it sit for about 5 minutes, allowing the cheese to melt.

  7. Serve: Carefully remove the chicken from the pot and place it on a serving platter. Garnish with fresh basil if desired, and serve with pasta, salad, or crusty bread.

Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan with Spinach

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Season the Chicken: Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and dried basil.

  2. Sear in the Instant Pot: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode, add olive oil, and sear the chicken thighs for 3-4 minutes per side until browned.

  3. Layer Ingredients: After searing, layer the fresh spinach on top of the chicken, then pour the marinara sauce over everything.

  4. Pressure Cook: Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

  5. Quick Release: When the timer goes off, perform a quick release, then open the lid.

  6. Top with Cheese: Add mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top of the chicken and allow it to melt for a few minutes.

  7. Serve: Serve hot, garnished with additional spinach or basil, alongside your favorite sides.

Zesty Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Season with Spice: Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, and red pepper flakes for a zesty kick.

  2. Searing for Flavor: In the Instant Pot, heat olive oil and sear the chicken for about 4 minutes on each side.

  3. Add Marinara: Pour the marinara sauce over the chicken, ensuring it’s well-covered.

  4. Pressure Cooking: Secure the lid and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.

  5. Release & Add Cheese: After quick releasing the pressure, add mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top of the chicken.

  6. Final Touch: Let it sit with the lid on for a few minutes until the cheese melts.

  7. Plate and Serve: Serve the zesty chicken parmesan with a side of pasta or a fresh green salad.

Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan

  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh herbs and quality marinara sauce can significantly enhance the flavor of your dish.
  • Don’t Overcrowd: Ensure that the chicken pieces are not overcrowded in the pot to allow even cooking.
  • Experiment with Cheeses: Feel free to mix different types of cheese for a unique flavor profile.
  • Let it Rest: After cooking, letting the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving allows the juices to redistribute.

Conclusion

There you have it—three satisfying Instant Pot chicken parmesan recipes that are sure to become family favorites. Each recipe provides a unique twist on the classic dish while maintaining the comforting flavors we all love. The Instant Pot not only speeds up the cooking process but also enriches the flavors, making it a perfect tool for busy cooks.

Whether you prefer the classic recipe, a creamy spinach variation, or a zesty twist, these Instant Pot chicken parmesan recipes are bound to satisfy your cravings. Enjoy these delicious meals with loved ones, and don’t hesitate to get creative with your own variations. Happy cooking!
