Savory Keto Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers: A Spicy Appetizer


If you’re a fan of spicy and savory appetizers, then you’re in for a treat with this recipe for Savory Keto Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers. These delicious bites are a perfect blend of heat, creaminess, and smoky bacon flavor, making them an ideal appetizer for any occasion. Plus, they’re keto-friendly, so you can enjoy them guilt-free!

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh jalapeno peppers
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 12 slices bacon, cut in half
  • Toothpicks for securing the bacon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. Cut each jalapeno pepper in half lengthwise and remove the seeds and membranes. Be sure to wear gloves or wash your hands thoroughly after handling the peppers to avoid any discomfort.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika. Mix until well combined.

  4. Spoon the cream cheese mixture into each jalapeno half, filling them to the top. Make sure to evenly distribute the filling among all the peppers.

  5. Take one bacon slice and wrap it around each jalapeno half, securing it with a toothpick. Repeat this step for all the peppers.

  6. Place the bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers on the prepared baking sheet, spacing them out evenly.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until the bacon is crispy and the peppers are tender.

  8. Once cooked, remove the jalapeno poppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Be cautious as the filling will be hot!

  9. Serve these savory keto bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers as an appetizer at your next gathering or enjoy them as a delicious snack. They pair well with a creamy dip or ranch dressing for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, leave some of the seeds and membranes in the jalapeno peppers. Just be aware that this will make the poppers spicier.

  • If you prefer a milder version, you can use mini sweet peppers instead of jalapenos. The creaminess of the filling will balance out the sweetness of the peppers.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese for the filling. Pepper jack or smoked Gouda can add a unique flavor to these poppers.

  • If you don’t have an oven, you can also cook these on a grill over medium heat until the bacon is crispy and the peppers are tender.

  • These poppers can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator until ready to bake. Just be sure to bring them to room temperature before cooking.

Enjoy these savory keto bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers and watch them disappear in no time. They’re sure to be a hit with your friends and family, and with their keto-friendly ingredients, you can indulge without any guilt. So, spice up your appetizer game and give this recipe a try today!
