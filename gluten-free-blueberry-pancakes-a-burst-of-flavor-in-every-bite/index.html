Gluten-Free Blueberry Pancakes: A Burst of Flavor in Every Bite


Are you ready to elevate your breakfast game with a delightful stack of gluten-free blueberry pancakes? Look no further, because we’ve got the perfect recipe to satisfy your cravings for a delicious and wholesome morning treat. Whether you follow a gluten-free diet by choice or necessity, these pancakes are a delightful way to start your day on a sweet note.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Maple syrup for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Whisk the dry ingredients together until well combined.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, egg, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter; a few lumps are perfectly fine.

  4. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to crush them, to ensure each pancake is bursting with juicy blueberry goodness.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with coconut oil or non-stick cooking spray.

  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.

  7. Transfer the cooked pancakes to a warm plate and cover them to keep warm while you cook the remaining batter.

  8. Serve the gluten-free blueberry pancakes with a drizzle of maple syrup and extra fresh blueberries for a burst of flavor in every bite.

Tips for Perfect Pancakes

  • For extra fluffy pancakes, allow the batter to rest for 5-10 minutes before cooking.
  • If you prefer a thicker batter, add a little more gluten-free flour until you reach your desired consistency.
  • To keep the pancakes warm while cooking the rest, place them on a baking sheet in a 200°F oven.

The Perfect Morning Indulgence

These gluten-free blueberry pancakes are not only a treat for your taste buds but also a wholesome way to enjoy a classic breakfast favorite without gluten. The sweet bursts of juicy blueberries combined with the light and fluffy texture of the pancakes make for a truly delightful morning indulgence.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a special breakfast, whip up a batch of these gluten-free blueberry pancakes and savor the burst of flavor in every bite. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or treating your loved ones to a memorable breakfast, these pancakes are sure to be a hit. Enjoy!
