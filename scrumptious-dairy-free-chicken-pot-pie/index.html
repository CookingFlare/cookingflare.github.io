Scrumptious Dairy-Free Chicken Pot Pie


Are you craving a comforting and hearty meal that’s also dairy-free? Look no further than this scrumptious dairy-free chicken pot pie recipe! Packed with tender chicken, flavorful vegetables, and a creamy dairy-free sauce, this dish is a perfect choice for a cozy dinner with family or friends. Let’s dive into the details of creating this delectable pot pie.

Ingredients

For the Pie Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup vegetable shortening
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 cup diced potatoes
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup dairy-free butter
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Making the Pie Crust:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  2. Cut in the vegetable shortening until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add ice water, tossing with a fork until a ball forms.
  4. Divide the dough in half and shape into two disks. Wrap in plastic and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Preparing the Filling:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the diced chicken until no longer pink. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, sauté the potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, and garlic until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the dairy-free butter to the skillet and melt.
  4. Stir in the flour until well combined to create a roux.
  5. Gradually add the chicken broth and almond milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
  6. Add the cooked chicken back into the skillet and season with thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer for a few minutes until the filling is creamy and well combined.

Assembling the Pot Pie:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out one of the chilled pie crust disks to fit a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. Pour the prepared chicken and vegetable filling into the pie crust.
  4. Roll out the second pie crust disk and place it over the filling, sealing the edges and trimming any excess dough.
  5. Cut slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.

Baking the Pot Pie:

  1. Place the assembled pot pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

Serving and Enjoying

Once the pot pie is baked to perfection, allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. The creamy, dairy-free filling combined with the flaky, golden crust is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you wanting more. This scrumptious dairy-free chicken pot pie is a wonderful option for a cozy dinner on a chilly evening or a special gathering with loved ones.

Give this recipe a try and savor the delightful flavors of a classic chicken pot pie without the dairy. It’s a surefire way to please everyone at the table, dairy-free or not. Enjoy your culinary adventure as you create this comforting and delicious dish!

That’s it for our dairy-free chicken pot pie recipe! We hope you find joy in preparing and savoring this delectable dish. Remember, cooking is an art, so feel free to add your own twist to the recipe for a truly personalized experience. Happy cooking!
