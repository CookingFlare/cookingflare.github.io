Mini Sliders: Tasty and Miniature Recipes for Little Hands


If you’re looking for a fun and delicious way to satisfy your cravings, look no further than mini sliders! These bite-sized burgers are not only perfect for parties and gatherings, but they also make a fantastic snack or meal for little hands. In this post, we’ll explore some creative and mouthwatering mini slider recipes that are sure to delight both kids and adults alike.

Classic Cheeseburger Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns
  • 6 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce, tomatoes, and onions for topping
  • Condiments of your choice (ketchup, mustard, mayo)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill or stovetop griddle to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 12 small patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. As the patties cook, place the slider buns on the grill to toast lightly.
  5. Once the patties are cooked, place a slice of cheese on each patty and allow it to melt slightly.
  6. Assemble the sliders by placing the patties on the bottom buns and adding lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and condiments of your choice.
  7. Top with the remaining slider buns and serve hot.

BBQ Chicken Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 12 slider buns
  • Coleslaw for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the shredded chicken and BBQ sauce over medium heat.
  2. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is heated through and coated in the BBQ sauce.
  3. Toast the slider buns on a grill or stovetop.
  4. Assemble the sliders by placing a generous portion of BBQ chicken on the bottom buns and topping with coleslaw.
  5. Place the remaining slider buns on top and serve immediately.

Veggie Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns
  • Avocado, lettuce, and sliced cucumber for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mash the black beans with a fork or potato masher until they form a thick paste.
  2. Add the bread crumbs, bell peppers, onions, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the bowl and mix until well combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into 12 equal portions and form them into small patties.
  4. Cook the patties in a lightly oiled skillet over medium heat for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and heated through.
  5. Toast the slider buns on a grill or stovetop.
  6. Assemble the sliders by placing the black bean patties on the bottom buns and adding avocado, lettuce, and sliced cucumber.
  7. Top with the remaining slider buns and serve fresh.

Mini sliders are not only delicious but also versatile, allowing you to get creative with different ingredients and flavors. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a fun meal idea for the family, these miniature recipes are sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, get cooking, and enjoy these tasty treats with your little ones!
