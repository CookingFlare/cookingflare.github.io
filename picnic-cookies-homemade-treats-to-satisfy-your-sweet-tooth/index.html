Picnic Cookies: Homemade Treats to Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth


Picnic cookies, a classic favorite that never goes out of style. These chewy, sweet, and indulgent treats are perfect for snacking on the go, whether you’re packing a lunch for school or heading out on a family picnic. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make homemade picnic cookies that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone mat.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to cream together the butter and sugars until light and fluffy, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix in the flour mixture until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips and walnuts (if using).
  7. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden brown.
  9. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips and Variations

  • To make these cookies more picnic-perfect, try using a cookie scoop to portion out the dough. This will give you uniform cookies that are easy to pack and transport.
  • If you want to add a little extra flavor to your cookies, try substituting the vanilla extract with a different flavor, such as almond or coconut.
  • For a chocolate-only cookie, simply omit the walnuts and add an extra 1/2 cup of chocolate chips.
  • To make these cookies more festive, try adding a few drops of food coloring to the dough to give them a fun and colorful twist.

Why You’ll Love These Cookies

  • They’re easy to make: With just a few simple ingredients and a quick bake time, you can have a batch of homemade picnic cookies in no time.
  • They’re perfect for snacking: Whether you’re heading out on a picnic or just need a quick pick-me-up, these cookies are the perfect snack.
  • They’re customizable: With a variety of mix-ins and flavor options, you can make these cookies your own and tailor them to your tastes.

Conclusion

Picnic cookies are a classic favorite that never goes out of style. With their chewy texture, sweet flavor, and crunchy edges, it’s no wonder why they’re a beloved treat. By following this simple recipe, you can make your own homemade picnic cookies that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. So next time you’re packing a lunch or heading out on a family picnic, be sure to include a few of these delicious treats in your bag. Your taste buds will thank you!
