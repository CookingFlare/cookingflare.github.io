Mediterranean Lamb Sliders: Bursting with Flavor

If you’re looking for a fresh and flavorful twist on classic sliders, these Mediterranean Lamb Sliders are sure to tantalize your taste buds. Bursting with the vibrant and aromatic flavors of the Mediterranean, these sliders are the perfect addition to any gathering, whether it’s a casual weeknight dinner or a festive backyard barbecue. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create these mouthwatering delights! Ingredients For the Lamb Patties: 1 pound ground lamb 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon Salt and pepper to taste For the Tzatziki Sauce: 1 cup Greek yogurt 1/2 cucumber, peeled, seeded, and finely grated 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped 1 clove garlic, minced 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil Salt and pepper to taste For Assembly: Mini slider buns Sliced tomatoes Sliced red onion Fresh lettuce leaves Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Lamb Patties In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground lamb, finely chopped red onion, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, coriander, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Gently mix the ingredients until well combined, being careful not to overwork the meat. Divide the mixture into small portions and shape them into mini patties, slightly larger than the size of the slider buns. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the lamb patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness. Step 2: Prepare the Tzatziki Sauce In a medium bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, grated cucumber, chopped dill, minced garlic, extra virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper. Stir the ingredients until well combined, and adjust the seasoning to taste. Step 3: Assemble the Sliders Slice the mini slider buns in half and lightly toast them on the grill or in the oven. Spread a generous dollop of the prepared tzatziki sauce on the bottom half of each slider bun. Place a cooked lamb patty on top of the tzatziki sauce. Add a slice of tomato, a few rings of red onion, and a fresh lettuce leaf. Top the sliders with the remaining half of the buns and secure them with toothpicks if needed. Step 4: Serve and Enjoy Plate the Mediterranean Lamb Sliders and serve them alongside your favorite sides, such as a crisp Greek salad, roasted vegetables, or seasoned fries. Classic Bison Burger with Caramelized Onions Ingredients: 1 pound Beyond Beef ground bison 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 large onion, thinly sliced Salt and pepper to taste 4 burger buns Your favorite burger toppings (lettuce, tomato, cheese, etc.) Instructions: Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat and add the sliced onions. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the onions are soft and golden brown. While the onions are caramelizing, divide the ground bison into four equal portions and form them into burger patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the bison patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute or two. Mix until the spices are evenly distributed throughout the meat. Form the seasoned bison into four patties and grill them over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes on each side. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of pepper jack cheese on each patty to melt. Toast the burger buns on the grill. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled bison patties on the buns, topping them with sliced avocado and a spoonful of salsa. 3. Southwest Turkey Sliders: A Flavorful Twist

As the summer sun beats down, our taste buds crave something refreshing and exciting. The classic turkey slider just isn’t cutting it anymore. It’s time to shake things up with a flavorful twist that combines the comfort of turkey with the bold flavors of the Southwest. Enter the Southwest Turkey Sliders, a game-changing recipe that will elevate your backyard BBQ or casual gathering to new heights. Ingredients 1 pound ground turkey 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper 1/4 cup finely chopped yellow onion 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika Salt and pepper, to taste 4 hamburger buns Lettuce Tomato Avocado or guacamole Pepper jack cheese or crumbled queso fresco Chipotle aioli or sour cream Cilantro, chopped (optional) Instructions Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, chopped red bell pepper, yellow onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Use your hands or a wooden spoon to mix the ingredients until just combined. Be careful not to overmix. Form the turkey mixture into 8-10 patties, about 3/4 inch thick. Grill the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F. Let them rest for a few minutes before assembling the sliders. Meanwhile, toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster. Assemble the sliders by spreading a dollop of chipotle aioli or sour cream on the bottom bun, followed by a turkey patty, a slice of lettuce, a tomato slice, a slice of avocado or a spoonful of guacamole, a sprinkle of pepper jack cheese or crumbled queso fresco, and finally, the top bun. Garnish with chopped cilantro, if desired. Tips and Variations For an extra kick, add some diced jalapeños or serrano peppers to the turkey mixture. Substitute the chipotle aioli with a spicy mayo or a drizzle of hot sauce for added heat. Swap the pepper jack cheese for crumbled feta or goat cheese for a tangy twist. Add some crispy bacon or caramelized onions to the slider for added texture and flavor. Serve the Southwest Turkey Sliders with a side of spicy roasted vegetables or a refreshing fruit salad to balance out the bold flavors. Conclusion The Southwest Turkey Sliders are a flavorful twist on the classic turkey slider, combining the comfort of turkey with the bold flavors of the Southwest. With its crispy turkey patties, tangy chipotle aioli, and crunchy avocado, this recipe is sure to impress your friends and family. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ or a casual gathering, these sliders are the perfect addition to your menu. So go ahead, get creative, and give your taste buds a flavorful twist! ...
