BBQ Chicken Flatbread: Quick and Easy Recipes for Grilled Pizza

If you’re a fan of pizza and barbecue, then BBQ Chicken Flatbread is the perfect combination of flavors for you. This quick and easy recipe for grilled pizza will satisfy your cravings for smoky, tangy, and cheesy goodness. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or looking for a simple yet delicious weeknight dinner, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. Ingredients: For the Flatbread: 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon sugar 1 cup warm water 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 packet instant yeast For the Toppings: 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken 1/2 cup barbecue sauce 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Step 1: Prepare the Flatbread Dough In a large bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and instant yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy. Add the olive oil, flour, and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir until a dough starts to form. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for 1 hour. Step 2: Grill the Flatbread Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Divide the dough into two equal portions and roll each portion into a thin oval shape. Carefully place the dough on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until it puffs up and gets grill marks. Remove from the grill and set aside. Step 3: Assemble the BBQ Chicken Flatbread In a small bowl, toss the shredded chicken with the barbecue sauce until it’s evenly coated. Spread the barbecue chicken mixture evenly over the grilled flatbreads. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the chicken. Scatter the red onion slices on top of the cheese. Step 4: Grill the Pizza Return the assembled flatbreads to the grill and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes with the lid closed, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Once done, remove the flatbreads from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes. Step 5: Serve and Enjoy Sprinkle the chopped cilantro over the hot flatbreads and season with salt and pepper to taste. Slice the BBQ Chicken Flatbread into wedges and serve immediately. Tips and Variations: For a vegetarian option, you can substitute the chicken with grilled vegetables or tofu. Experiment with different types of cheese such as cheddar, gouda, or smoked provolone for added flavor. Drizzle a little extra barbecue sauce on top before serving for an extra burst of flavor. BBQ Chicken Flatbread is a fantastic way to enjoy the smoky flavors of barbecue in a fun and versatile pizza form. This recipe is perfect for sharing with friends and family at any gathering or simply for a laid-back meal at home. The combination of tender chicken, tangy barbecue sauce, and gooey cheese on a crispy grilled crust is a surefire crowd-pleaser. So fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in this delightful BBQ Chicken Flatbread! ...
