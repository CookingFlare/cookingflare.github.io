Easy Weeknight Dinner: One-Pot Chicken and Rice Recipe


Cooking a delicious and nutritious meal on a busy weeknight can sometimes feel like a daunting task. But fear not, because I have the perfect solution for you – an easy one-pot chicken and rice recipe that will have your taste buds singing and your family asking for seconds!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they turn golden brown and fragrant.

  2. Season the chicken breasts with paprika, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper. Add them to the pot and cook until they are browned on both sides, about 5 minutes per side.

  3. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside. In the same pot, add the rice and sauté for a couple of minutes until it is coated in the flavorful oil.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice simmer for about 15 minutes, or until it is cooked through and the liquid is absorbed.

  5. While the rice is cooking, shred the cooked chicken into bite-sized pieces using two forks.

  6. Once the rice is cooked, stir in the frozen peas, diced carrots, and shredded chicken. Cover the pot and let it cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

  7. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  8. Serve the one-pot chicken and rice dish hot, garnished with fresh parsley for added freshness and flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking by adding your favorite herbs and spices. You can experiment with different seasonings such as rosemary, oregano, or even curry powder to give the dish a unique twist.

  • If you prefer a creamier texture, you can stir in a dollop of sour cream or Greek yogurt at the end. This will add a rich and tangy flavor to the dish.

  • To make this recipe vegetarian-friendly, simply omit the chicken and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth. You can also add extra vegetables like bell peppers or zucchini for a colorful and nutritious twist.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat the dish in the microwave or on the stovetop for a quick and easy lunch or dinner.

This one-pot chicken and rice recipe is not only delicious and filling, but it also saves you time on cleanup since everything is cooked in a single pot. It’s the perfect solution for those busy weeknights when you want to enjoy a home-cooked meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Give it a try and let me know how it turns out!
