Transforming Leftover Turkey into a Tasty Turkey and Stuffing Wrap


Are you tired of eating the same old leftover turkey sandwich after Thanksgiving? Don’t worry, we’ve got a delicious solution for you! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to transform your leftover turkey into a mouthwatering turkey and stuffing wrap that will leave you craving for more. Let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • Leftover turkey (shredded or sliced)
  • Leftover stuffing
  • Tortillas (flour or corn, whichever you prefer)
  • Cranberry sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lettuce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the stuffing: If you have leftover stuffing, great! If not, you can quickly make some by sautéing onions and celery in a pan until they are soft and translucent. Then, mix in your favorite herbs and spices, like sage, thyme, and rosemary. Finally, add breadcrumbs and chicken broth to create a moist and flavorful stuffing.

  2. Warm the tortillas: Warm your tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for a few seconds on each side until they are pliable. This step will make them easier to wrap and prevent them from tearing.

  3. Spread a layer of cranberry sauce: Take one tortilla and spread a thin layer of cranberry sauce evenly across the surface. The sweet and tangy flavor of the cranberry sauce will complement the savory turkey and stuffing perfectly.

  4. Layer on the turkey: Place a generous amount of leftover turkey on top of the cranberry sauce. You can use shredded or sliced turkey, depending on your preference. Make sure to distribute the turkey evenly to ensure every bite is full of flavor.

  5. Add the stuffing: Now it’s time to add the star of the show - the stuffing! Spread a layer of stuffing over the turkey, covering the entire surface of the tortilla. The combination of the moist stuffing and tender turkey will create a delightful texture and taste.

  6. Season with salt and pepper: Sprinkle a pinch of salt and pepper over the stuffing to enhance the flavors even further. Be careful not to overdo it, as the leftover turkey and stuffing should already be seasoned from the previous cooking.

  7. Layer on the lettuce: Place a few leaves of fresh lettuce on top of the stuffing. The crispness of the lettuce will add a refreshing element to the wrap, balancing out the richness of the turkey and stuffing.

  8. Drizzle with mayonnaise: Finish off your wrap by drizzling a generous amount of mayonnaise over the lettuce. The creamy and tangy mayo will tie all the flavors together and give the wrap a luscious texture.

  9. Roll it up: Fold the sides of the tortilla inward, then roll it up tightly from one end to the other. Cut the wrap in half diagonally for easy handling, or leave it whole if you’re feeling extra hungry!

  10. Enjoy your Turkey and Stuffing Wrap: Now it’s time to dig in! Take a bite of your homemade turkey and stuffing wrap and savor the delicious combination of flavors. The tender turkey, savory stuffing, tangy cranberry sauce, and creamy mayo will make your taste buds dance with joy.

Conclusion

With this recipe, you can turn your leftover turkey into a mouthwatering meal that feels like a brand new creation. The combination of turkey, stuffing, cranberry sauce, and lettuce wrapped in a tortilla creates a delightful blend of flavors and textures. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy lunch idea or a satisfying dinner, this turkey and stuffing wrap is sure to hit the spot. Give it a try and let us know how it turns out for you!
