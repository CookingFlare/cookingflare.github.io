Quench the Thirst of Thyme: 5 Thyme-based Juice & Smoothie Recipes


One of the most underrated herbs when it comes to culinary delights, thyme has always managed to slip under the radar when compared to its more popular counterparts like basil and rosemary. However, the aromatic, slightly peppery flavor of thyme can add a delightful twist to an otherwise ordinary juice or smoothie, giving it an earthy freshness like never before. So, let’s delve into the magic of thyme and create some refreshing concoctions to savor and relish.

  1. Sweet Citrus Thyme Cooler This citrusy cooler is the perfect blend of tangy oranges, sweet strawberries, and earthy thyme, creating a refreshing drink that’s perfect for a lazy weekend afternoon relaxing on your porch. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups orange juice
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 4-5 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  • In a blender, combine orange juice, fresh strawberries, and thyme sprigs.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • If you prefer a sweeter taste, add honey and blend once again.
  • Pour the mixture over ice cubes and serve instantly.
  1. Earthy Green Smoothie The Earthy Green Smoothie is for all those who love leafy greens and are always on the lookout for ways to incorporate more into their diet. This smoothie uses kale, cucumber, spinach, and apple, giving it a burst of nutrients and freshness, with thyme adding a touch of earthiness to the mix. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup washed kale leaves
  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 green apple
  • 2-3 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  • In a blender, add kale leaves, cucumber, spinach, green apple, and thyme sprigs.
  • Add water, cover the blender, and blend until smooth.
  • Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
  1. Cooling Thyme & Mint Juice Looking for a drink that will refresh you on a warm summer’s day? Try this Cooling Thyme & Mint Juice. It combines the fresh, cooling flavors of mint, cucumber, lime, and thyme to create a drink that will invigorate your senses and chill you out. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • 5-6 fresh mint leaves
  • 2 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • Juice of half a lime
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  • In a blender, add cucumber, mint leaves, thyme sprigs, and lime juice.
  • Add water and blend until smooth.
  • Pour the juice into a glass and serve over ice for the ultimate in refreshment.
  1. Thyme & Pineapple Smoothie If tangy and tropical citrus flavors are your thing, the Thyme & Pineapple Smoothie will leave you wanting more. With fresh pineapple and thyme in the mix, the lack of sweetness makes it a healthier option when you don’t feel like consuming too much sugar. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1-2 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 1 cup water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  • In a blender, combine pineapple chunks and thyme sprigs.
  • Add water and if desired, a handful of ice cubes.
  • Blend until smooth and serve immediately.
  1. Sleepy-time Herbal Smoothie This Bedtime Smoothie is rich in magnesium, a mineral that is known to induce sleep, and the calming nature of thyme only adds to its benefits for a restful night’s sleep. To make this smoothie:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1-2 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 1/2 cup honey or agave nectar
  • 1/2 cup hemp seeds
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  • In a blender, combine almond milk, rolled oats, thyme sprigs, honey or agave nectar, and hemp seeds.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Add water and blend once again.
  • Serve and enjoy!

With these amazing juice and smoothie recipes, you might just find your new favorite way to incorporate more thyme into your everyday life. Experiment, innovate, and add your own personal touch to make your concoctions truly unique and memorable. Cheers to healthy living and that delightful, fragrant taste of thyme!
