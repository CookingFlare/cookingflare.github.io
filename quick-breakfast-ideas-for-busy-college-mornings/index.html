10. Protein-Packed Pancakes

Pancakes don’t have to be reserved for weekends. With a little preparation, you can enjoy a protein-packed breakfast any day of the week.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oats (blended into oat flour)
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the oats, protein powder, baking powder, milk, egg, and vanilla extract.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour in the pancake batter.
  3. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Serve with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, maple syrup, or nut butter.

Protein pancakes are a filling and nutritious option that will keep you energized throughout your day.

Final Thoughts

Being a college student doesn’t mean you have to sacrifice your health or your morning routine. With these quick breakfast ideas, you can make sure you’re eating something nutritious and satisfying without wasting time. Whether you’re preparing the night before or whipping something up in minutes, these recipes will keep you fueled for even the busiest days. Try incorporating a few of them into your routine, and you’ll notice a significant difference in your energy levels and focus!
