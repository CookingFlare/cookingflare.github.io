Magic Bar Cake: Classic Delight in a Mini Size


If you’re looking for a simple yet decadent dessert to impress your friends and family this weekend, look no further than the classic Magic Bar Cake. Renowned for its delectable combination of flavors, it has garnered a devoted following over the years. In this post, we’ll explore how to create this divine cake in mini size form, perfect for an after-dinner treat or homemade gift. So, let’s get into the kitchen and whip up some delicious memories!

Ingredients

1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 cups whole graham cracker crumbs1 cup chopped almonds1 cup unsweetened coconut flakes
1 1/2 cups sweetened flaked coconut2 cups all-purpose flour2 cups chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups brown sugar2 teaspoons baking powder1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (177°C). Grease a mini muffin tin, or line with miniature muffin liners. This will help prevent your delicious Magic Bar Cakes from sticking.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, chopped almonds, unsweetened coconut flakes, and melted butter. Mix until well incorporated. You should achieve a moist and crumbly texture. Spoon about one tablespoon of this mixture into the bottom of each mini muffin cup. Press down gently to form a firm crust.

  3. In another bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, remaining graham cracker crumbs, flour, baking powder, and salt. Once thoroughly combined, stir in the sweetened flaked coconut, brown sugar, and chocolate chips. Ensure everything is evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  4. Distribute the cake batter evenly among the mini muffin cups, filling them approximately 3/4 of the way full. The batter will create a delightfully sweet and crumbly top layer for your Mini Magic Bar Cakes.

  5. Into the preheated oven, place the mini muffin tin and bake for 20 to 25 minutes. The key to a perfect Mini Magic Bar Cake is watching closely for the edges to turn a light golden brown, which will typically indicate that they’re ready.

  6. Remove the mini muffin tin from the oven and allow your Mini Magic Bar Cakes to cool fully within the tin. This process will make it much easier to remove them without breaking.

  7. Once cooled, gently remove the Mini Magic Bar Cakes from the tin. Dust the tops lightly with powdered sugar, if desired. Serve and enjoy this delightful and nostalgic dessert with family and friends!

Why Mini Magic Bar Cake?

Creating Mini Magic Bar Cakes is a fantastic way to serving up these tasty treats, not only do they look adorable, but they are also perfect for portion control. No matter if you’re hosting a party, making gifts, or craving a sweet indulgence, these little cakes are sure to satisfy. Plus, with all those wonderful textures and flavors combined in one mouthwatering bite, the possibilities are endless!

Customizing Your Mini Magic Bar Cakes

The beauty of the Mini Magic Bar Cake format is its versatility. You can easily customize it to fit your own tastes and preferences. Why not try using different types of nuts, dried fruits, or crushed Candied Ginger for a delightful twist? Consider substituting white chocolate chips for the semi-sweet variety, or experiment with different types of graham crackers or cookies in your crust. Go ahead and make this classic dessert your own!

Conclusion

The Mini Magic Bar Cake is an affectionate nod to the classic dessert beloved by many and now finally brought to a miniature size, making it an irresistible sweet treat for any occasion. With its simple and delicious flavor combination, it is sure to become a crowd-pleaser and cherished recipe in your personal collection. Happy baking, and allow this scrumptious creation to bring smiles to your loved ones’ faces!
