Wraps for a Quick and Easy Lunch: 10 Recipes


When you’re in need of a quick and delicious lunch, wraps are the perfect solution. They’re versatile, easy to prepare, and can be packed with a variety of ingredients to suit any craving or dietary need. Whether you’re looking for something fresh and light, hearty and filling, or packed with protein, there’s a wrap recipe for you.

In this post, we’ve compiled 10 unique and delicious wrap recipes that are ideal for busy lunches. From classic combinations to creative twists, these wraps can be made ahead of time, packed for lunch, or enjoyed right away. Let’s dive in!

1. Classic Chicken Caesar Wrap

If you’re a fan of the classic Caesar salad, this wrap will satisfy your cravings with a twist. Tender grilled chicken breast, romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and a drizzle of Caesar dressing are all wrapped up in a soft flour tortilla.

Ingredients:

  • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup romaine lettuce, chopped
  • ¼ cup croutons, crushed
  • 2 tbsp Caesar dressing
  • 1 flour tortilla

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface.
  2. Layer the lettuce, grilled chicken, and crushed croutons in the center.
  3. Drizzle with Caesar dressing.
  4. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly.
  5. Cut the wrap in half and serve.

This wrap is not only filling but offers a satisfying combination of crunch, flavor, and protein, making it an excellent choice for lunch.

2. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

A vibrant, fresh wrap bursting with Mediterranean flavors. This wrap is perfect for vegetarians or anyone looking to enjoy a lighter, healthy lunch. It features hummus, roasted vegetables, and a blend of spices that’ll transport your taste buds to the Mediterranean coast.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp hummus
  • ½ cup roasted vegetables (zucchini, bell pepper, eggplant)
  • ¼ cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp olives, chopped
  • 1 large whole-wheat tortilla

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of hummus over the center of the tortilla.
  2. Add the roasted vegetables, crumbled feta cheese, and chopped olives.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly, folding in the edges as you go.
  4. Slice and serve.

This Mediterranean veggie wrap is packed with nutrients and flavors, and it’s the perfect way to enjoy a light, wholesome lunch.

3. Spicy Thai Peanut Chicken Wrap

For those who love a little heat, the Spicy Thai Peanut Chicken Wrap is the answer. This wrap combines the deliciously savory flavors of chicken, veggies, and a tangy peanut sauce with a spicy kick, all wrapped up in a soft tortilla.

Ingredients:

  • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • ½ cup shredded carrots
  • ¼ cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 flour tortilla

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, and sriracha to create the sauce.
  2. Lay the tortilla flat and spread the peanut sauce evenly over it.
  3. Add the grilled chicken, shredded carrots, and chopped cilantro.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly and cut it in half.

The combination of spicy and savory in this wrap is a winner. It’s a great option for those looking for a bit of excitement in their lunch.

4. Turkey & Avocado Wrap

This wrap is both creamy and savory, featuring tender turkey breast and creamy avocado. With the addition of fresh vegetables, it’s the perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices turkey breast
  • ½ avocado, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 tbsp mustard or mayonnaise
  • 1 whole-wheat tortilla

Instructions:

  1. Spread mustard or mayonnaise on the center of the tortilla.
  2. Layer the turkey slices, avocado, and tomato on top.
  3. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly.
  4. Slice the wrap in half and enjoy.

This turkey and avocado wrap is a simple yet satisfying option for a quick lunch that’s both healthy and filling.

5. BBQ Chicken Wrap

The smoky, tangy flavor of BBQ chicken paired with crisp lettuce and shredded cheese makes for a mouthwatering wrap. Whether you use leftover grilled chicken or cook it fresh, this wrap is always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • 1 grilled chicken breast, shredded
  • 2 tbsp BBQ sauce
  • ½ cup shredded lettuce
  • ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 flour tortilla

Instructions:

  1. Toss the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce until well coated.
  2. Lay the tortilla flat and add the BBQ chicken, shredded lettuce, and shredded cheddar cheese.
  3. Roll up the wrap tightly, ensuring all the ingredients stay inside.
  4. Cut the wrap in half and serve.

This BBQ chicken wrap is a great option for those who love bold flavors and a little bit of smokiness in their meal.

6. Tuna Salad Wrap

Tuna salad is a classic lunch favorite, and when wrapped up in a tortilla, it becomes an even more convenient and portable meal. This wrap is easy to make and packed with protein, making it a filling choice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of tuna, drained
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 hard-boiled egg, chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped celery
  • 1 flour tortilla

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the tuna, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, chopped egg, and chopped celery.
  2. Mix until everything is well incorporated.
  3. Spread the tuna salad mixture in the center of the tortilla.
  4. Roll the tortilla tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
  5. Slice and serve.

This tuna salad wrap is a quick and easy option for a protein-packed lunch that’s both satisfying and delicious.

7. Caprese Chicken Wrap

Inspired by the classic Caprese salad, this wrap features tender chicken, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze for a burst of flavor. It’s the perfect combination of fresh ingredients and savory goodness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 mozzarella ball, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • 1 flour tortilla

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla flat and layer the grilled chicken, mozzarella, tomato, and basil leaves in the center.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly, folding in the edges to keep everything secure.
  4. Cut the wrap in half and serve.

This Caprese chicken wrap is a light yet flavorful option for a lunchtime treat that’s sure to please anyone craving something fresh and satisfying.

8. Veggie & Hummus Wrap

If you’re looking for a meatless wrap that’s full of flavor, the veggie and hummus wrap is an excellent choice. Packed with crunchy vegetables and creamy hummus, it’s a quick and satisfying meal that’s also incredibly nutritious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp hummus
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • ½ bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • ¼ cup spinach or mixed greens
  • 1 whole-wheat tortilla

Instructions:

  1. Spread hummus evenly over the center of the tortilla.
  2. Layer the cucumber, bell pepper, carrot, and spinach on top.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly and slice into halves.

This veggie and hummus wrap is fresh, colorful, and perfect for those who want to enjoy a light but filling lunch.

9. Breakfast Wrap

Who says wraps are just for lunch? A breakfast wrap is a fantastic way to start your day, or even enjoy it for lunch! With scrambled eggs, bacon, and cheese, this wrap is a savory and satisfying choice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 scrambled eggs
  • 2 slices cooked bacon
  • ¼ cup shredded cheese
  • 1 flour tortilla
  • Salsa (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Scramble the eggs and cook the bacon until crispy.
  2. Lay the tortilla flat and layer the scrambled eggs, bacon, and shredded cheese in the center.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly and serve with salsa on the side for an extra kick.

This breakfast wrap is a hearty meal that’ll fuel you through the day.

10. Chicken Fajita Wrap

If you love the flavors of fajitas, then this wrap will be a hit. Tender strips of chicken, sautéed bell peppers, onions, and a sprinkle of fajita seasoning come together to create a delicious, spicy wrap.

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast, sliced into strips
  • ½ bell pepper, sliced
  • ¼ onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp fajita seasoning
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 flour tortilla

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken strips and cook until browned.
  3. Add the sliced bell pepper and onion, along with the fajita seasoning.
  4. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Spoon the chicken and vegetable mixture onto a tortilla, roll up, and serve.

The chicken fajita wrap is a flavorful, savory meal that will make you feel like you’re dining at a Mexican restaurant.

Final Thoughts

Wraps are an incredibly versatile and convenient option for lunch. Whether you’re craving something fresh, savory, spicy, or light, these 10 wrap recipes are sure to satisfy. They’re easy to make, customizable, and perfect for packing ahead of time. So the next time you’re in need of a quick and easy lunch, turn to these wraps for a satisfying meal that’s bursting with flavor.
