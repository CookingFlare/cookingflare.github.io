Satisfying Nut-Free Chicken Enchilada Recipe


  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced bell peppers. Sauté until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.

  2. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the chicken breasts. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 6-8 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and shred it using two forks.

  3. Return the shredded chicken to the skillet and add the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes with green chilies, diced green chilies, cumin, chili powder, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined. Simmer for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side, or until pliable.

  5. Spoon about 1/4 cup of the chicken mixture onto each tortilla. Roll up the tortillas and place them seam-side down in the greased baking dish.

  6. Pour any remaining chicken mixture over the enchiladas, spreading it evenly. Sprinkle the shredded Monterey Jack cheese over the top.

  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve the nut-free chicken enchiladas hot, accompanied by a side of Mexican rice and refried beans. Top with sour cream, guacamole, and extra salsa for added flavor.

Recipe Notes

  • To make this recipe gluten-free, ensure that the corn tortillas you use are certified gluten-free.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite ingredients such as sliced black olives, chopped green onions, or diced jalapeños.
  • Leftover enchiladas can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Enjoy this satisfying nut-free chicken enchilada recipe that’s packed with flavors and perfect for a weeknight dinner or a gathering with friends and family. The combination of tender shredded chicken, savory sauce, and gooey melted cheese will leave everyone craving for more. Don’t forget to customize it to your taste and enjoy the deliciousness of homemade enchiladas.
