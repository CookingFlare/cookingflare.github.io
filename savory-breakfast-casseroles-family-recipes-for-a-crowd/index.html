Savory Breakfast Casseroles: Family Recipes for a Crowd


When it comes to breakfast, few things can rival the delight of a savory breakfast casserole. Perfect for feeding a crowd, these hearty dishes combine the best of breakfast staples—eggs, meats, cheeses, and vegetables—into one glorious, easy-to-prepare meal. Whether you’re hosting a family gathering, a brunch party, or simply looking for a delicious way to start the day, breakfast casseroles are the ultimate solution.

In this post, we’ll explore a variety of savory breakfast casserole recipes that are sure to impress your family and friends. Each recipe is designed to serve a crowd, making them perfect for occasions that call for extra servings. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to whip up some comforting and satisfying breakfast delights!

The Basics of Breakfast Casseroles

Before diving into the recipes, let’s talk about what makes a breakfast casserole so special. The beauty of these dishes lies in their versatility. You can mix and match ingredients based on your preferences, dietary restrictions, and what you have on hand.

Common Ingredients

  1. Eggs: The foundation of any breakfast casserole, eggs provide structure and protein.
  2. Bread: Cubed bread or even hash browns can act as a base, absorbing flavors and adding texture.
  3. Meats: Sausage, bacon, ham, or even smoked salmon can elevate your casserole.
  4. Cheese: From cheddar to feta, cheese adds richness and flavor.
  5. Vegetables: Fresh or sautéed vegetables such as bell peppers, spinach, and onions add color and nutrients.
  6. Seasonings: Don’t forget the spices! Salt, pepper, garlic powder, and herbs can transform your dish.

Tips for Perfect Casseroles

  • Prep Ahead: Many casseroles can be prepared the night before. Assemble the dish, cover it, and refrigerate until you’re ready to bake.
  • Use a Deep Dish: A deep baking dish allows for even cooking and prevents overflow.
  • Let it Rest: Allow your casserole to sit for a few minutes after baking. This helps it set and makes serving easier.

Classic Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 pound breakfast sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 cups cubed bread (preferably stale)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prep the Baking Dish: Grease a large 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Cook the Sausage: In a skillet, cook the breakfast sausage until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Combine Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and oregano. Stir in the sausage, bread cubes, bell peppers, onion, and cheese.
  5. Bake: Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the eggs are set and the top is golden brown.
  6. Serve: Allow to cool for 5 minutes, then slice and serve warm.

Spinach and Feta Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 10 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (fresh or canned)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups cubed bread (whole grain works well)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Grease the Dish: Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Mix Eggs and Milk: In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk. Add salt, pepper, and basil.
  4. Layer Ingredients: In the baking dish, layer the cubed bread, spinach, tomatoes, and feta. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the top.
  5. Bake: Bake for 40-45 minutes or until the top is golden and the eggs are fully set.
  6. Cool and Serve: Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve warm for a delightful start to your day.

Southwest Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 pound chorizo, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 2 cups shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 4 cups tortilla chips, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the Dish: Grease a large baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Cook Chorizo: In a skillet, cook the chorizo until fully browned. Drain excess grease.
  4. Whisk the Eggs: In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  5. Layer the Casserole: In the baking dish, layer the crushed tortilla chips, chorizo, black beans, corn, tomatoes, and cheese. Pour the egg mixture over the top.
  6. Bake: Bake for 50-60 minutes or until the eggs are set and the top is slightly crispy.
  7. Slice and Enjoy: Let it cool slightly before slicing. Serve with avocado or salsa on the side.

Vegetarian Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 2 cups diced zucchini
  • 2 cups diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 cups cubed bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Grease the Baking Dish: Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Sauté Vegetables: In a skillet, sauté zucchini, bell peppers, and onion until tender. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  4. Whisk Eggs and Milk: In a large bowl, whisk together eggs and milk. Add salt and pepper.
  5. Combine Ingredients: In the baking dish, layer the cubed bread, sautéed vegetables, and mozzarella cheese. Pour the egg mixture over everything.
  6. Bake: Bake for 40-45 minutes or until the eggs are set and the top is golden.
  7. Serve: Allow to cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve warm, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.

Sweet Potato and Sausage Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 pound breakfast sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 12 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the Dish: Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Cook Sweet Potatoes: Boil or steam sweet potatoes until tender. Drain and set aside.
  4. Whisk Eggs and Milk: In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Combine Ingredients: In the baking dish, layer cooked sweet potatoes and sausage. Pour the egg mixture over the top, then sprinkle with cheese.
  6. Bake: Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the eggs are set.
  7. Cool and Serve: Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Conclusion

Savory breakfast casseroles are the ideal way to bring family and friends together at the breakfast table. With their endless variety and ease of preparation, they can be customized to suit any taste and occasion. Whether you prefer the classic flavors of eggs and sausage or the vibrant twist of a Southwest-inspired dish, there’s a breakfast casserole for everyone.

Next time you’re planning a gathering, consider one of these delicious recipes. Not only will you provide a satisfying meal that everyone will love, but you’ll also create a warm and inviting atmosphere that encourages conversation and connection. Happy cooking, and enjoy sharing these delightful breakfast casseroles with your loved ones!
