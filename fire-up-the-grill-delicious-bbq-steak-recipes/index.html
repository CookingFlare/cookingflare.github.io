Fire Up the Grill: Delicious BBQ Steak Recipes


Are you ready to tantalize your taste buds with mouth-watering, savory BBQ steak dishes? There’s no better time than now to fire up that grill and get cooking! From succulent ribeyes to tender filets, we’ve got you covered with an assortment of scrumptious recipes that will make your next BBQ a hit. Let’s dive into the delicious world of barbecue steak cooking!

  1. Classic BBQ Ribeye Steaks

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks (1 1/2 inches thick)
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a bowl, mix together barbecue sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder.

  3. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper.

  4. Brush both sides of the steaks with the barbecue sauce mixture.

  5. Grill the steaks for approximately 6 to 7 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 135°F for medium-rare.

  6. Remove from the grill and let the steaks rest for 5 minutes before serving.

  7. Drizzle extra barbecue sauce on top and enjoy!

  8. Spicy Grilled Steak with Chimichurri Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 2 filet steaks (1 1/2 inches thick)
  • Chimichurri sauce (recipe below)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Chimichurri Sauce Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.

  2. Season steaks with salt and pepper.

  3. Grill the steaks for approximately 6 to 7 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 135°F for medium-rare.

  4. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for 5 minutes.

  5. Drizzle chimichurri sauce over the steaks and serve.

  6. Smoky Marinated Flank Steak

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds flank steak
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon coarse ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, smoked paprika, black pepper, and cumin.
  2. Place the flank steak in a shallow dish or large resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over the steak, ensuring it is completely coated.
  3. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or up to 24 hours.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Remove the steak from the marinade and pat dry with paper towels.
  6. Grill the steak for approximately 4 to 5 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 145°F for medium-rare.
  7. Remove the steak from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

Fire up that grill and bring your BBQ game to the next level with these delicious steak recipes. Your loved ones will be asking for seconds! So, what are you waiting for? Grab your apron and let’s get cooking!
