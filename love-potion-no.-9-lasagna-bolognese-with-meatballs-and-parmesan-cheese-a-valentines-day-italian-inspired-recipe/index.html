Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese: A Valentine's Day Italian-Inspired Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a romantic Italian-inspired dinner? This Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese recipe is sure to cast a spell on your loved one’s taste buds. This classic Italian dish combines the rich flavors of ground beef, pork, and tomato sauce with the tender texture of meatballs and the creaminess of Parmesan cheese, all wrapped up in a delicious lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for greasing the baking dish

Instructions:

Meatballs

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, chopped onion, minced garlic, egg, breadcrumbs, and salt and pepper to taste. Mix everything together with your hands until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

Use your hands to shape the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 1/2 inches (3.8 cm) in diameter. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving about 1 inch (2.5 cm) of space between each meatball. Drizzle with a little olive oil and gently roll them around to coat evenly.

Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until cooked through and lightly browned on the outside. Remove from the oven and set aside.

Lasagna Sauce

While the meatballs are baking, heat the tomato sauce in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the chopped parsley and a pinch of salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine.

Lasagna Assembly

In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of the lasagna sauce on the bottom. Arrange 4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.

Spread half of the meatballs over the noodles, followed by half of the shredded mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with half of the grated Parmesan cheese.

Repeat the layers, starting with the lasagna noodles, then the lasagna sauce, the remaining meatballs, the remaining mozzarella cheese, and finally the remaining Parmesan cheese.

Baking and Serving

Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with additional parsley and Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Tips and Variations:

  • To make the lasagna more romantic, serve with a side of garlic bread and a green salad.
  • For an extra boost of flavor, add a sprinkle of dried basil or oregano to the meatballs before baking.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, substitute the meatballs with roasted eggplant or zucchini slices.
  • To make ahead, assemble the lasagna up to the baking step, then refrigerate or freeze until ready to bake.

This Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese is sure to be a hit on Valentine’s Day, or any day, for that matter. The combination of rich flavors and textures will leave your loved one feeling loved and satisfied. So go ahead, cast your spell with this delicious Italian-inspired recipe!
