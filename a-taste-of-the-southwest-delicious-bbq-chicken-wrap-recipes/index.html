A Taste of the Southwest: Delicious BBQ Chicken Wrap Recipes


If you’re in search of a quick, flavorful meal that captures the essence of the Southwest, look no further than BBQ chicken wraps. These delicious creations combine tender, smoky chicken with fresh vegetables and zesty sauces, all wrapped up in a soft tortilla. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or a picnic, BBQ chicken wraps deliver a satisfying combination of taste and texture. In this blog post, we’ll explore several mouth-watering BBQ chicken wrap recipes that will transport your taste buds straight to the sunny Southwest.

The Essence of BBQ Chicken Wraps

BBQ chicken wraps are versatile and can be tailored to suit any palate. The key elements include grilled or shredded chicken coated in a rich barbecue sauce, fresh vegetables for crunch, and a variety of toppings that enhance the flavor profile. Whether you prefer a classic BBQ sauce or a spicy twist, this dish is bound to impress.

Why BBQ Chicken Wraps?

  1. Quick and Easy: Wraps are among the simplest meals to prepare. They come together in minutes, making them ideal for busy weeknights.
  2. Nutritious: By incorporating fresh vegetables and lean chicken, these wraps can be both healthy and satisfying.
  3. Customizable: From the type of sauce to the choice of vegetables, BBQ chicken wraps can be personalized to meet your dietary preferences and cravings.

Let’s dive into some delicious BBQ chicken wrap recipes that embody the spirit of the Southwest!

Classic BBQ Chicken Wrap

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or diced
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce (your favorite brand or homemade)
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Optional: sliced jalapeños for heat

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: In a medium bowl, mix the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce until well coated. You can adjust the amount of sauce according to your taste preference.

  2. Assemble the Wrap: Lay a tortilla flat on a clean surface. Start by adding a layer of romaine lettuce, followed by the BBQ chicken mixture. Top with cherry tomatoes, red onion, cheddar cheese, and jalapeños if using.

  3. Wrap It Up: Fold the sides of the tortilla inward and then roll it from the bottom up to secure the filling. Repeat this process with the remaining tortillas.

  4. Serve: Slice the wraps in half and serve immediately, or wrap them in foil for a grab-and-go meal.

Spicy Southwest BBQ Chicken Wrap

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup spicy BBQ sauce
  • 4 whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup corn (canned or grilled)
  • 1/4 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup pepper jack cheese, shredded
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Combine Flavors: In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with spicy BBQ sauce, ensuring the chicken is thoroughly coated.

  2. Layer the Ingredients: On a tortilla, lay down a handful of spinach leaves. Add the spicy BBQ chicken, followed by avocado slices, corn, black beans, and pepper jack cheese.

  3. Wrap and Roll: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly. Secure with a toothpick if necessary.

  4. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle fresh cilantro on top for an added burst of flavor. Cut in half and enjoy the spicy goodness!

BBQ Chicken Caesar Wrap

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 4 large spinach tortillas
  • 1 cup romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons for crunch

Instructions

  1. Mix the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the diced chicken with BBQ sauce. Set aside.

  2. Create the Base: On each spinach tortilla, place a generous amount of romaine lettuce.

  3. Add the Chicken: Spoon the BBQ chicken on top of the lettuce. Drizzle with Caesar dressing and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and croutons.

  4. Wrap It Up: Roll the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go. Slice in half and serve with extra Caesar dressing for dipping.

BBQ Chicken and Coleslaw Wrap

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup coleslaw mix (cabbage and carrots)
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup pickles, sliced
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce.

  2. Mix the Coleslaw: In another bowl, mix the coleslaw with ranch dressing until well coated.

  3. Build the Wrap: On a tortilla, add a scoop of coleslaw followed by the BBQ chicken and pickles.

  4. Wrap and Garnish: Fold the tortilla and roll tightly. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.

BBQ Chicken Wrap with Mango Salsa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup mango salsa (diced mango, red onion, jalapeño, cilantro, lime juice)
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled

Instructions

  1. Mix the Chicken: Combine shredded chicken with BBQ sauce in a bowl.

  2. Prepare the Salsa: In a separate bowl, mix diced mango, red onion, jalapeño, cilantro, and lime juice to create a fresh salsa.

  3. Assemble the Wrap: On a corn tortilla, layer mixed greens, BBQ chicken, mango salsa, and feta cheese.

  4. Roll It Up: Fold the sides and roll tightly. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Tips for Perfect BBQ Chicken Wraps

  • Choose the Right Tortilla: Experiment with different types of tortillas such as whole wheat, spinach, or corn to find your favorite.

  • Make Ahead: Prepare the chicken in advance and store it in the refrigerator for quick assembly during busy days.

  • Grill for Flavor: For an authentic taste, grill the chicken with a bit of seasoning before shredding it for the wraps.

  • Sauce Variety: Don’t hesitate to switch up the sauces! Try using teriyaki, honey mustard, or even a spicy chipotle sauce for different flavor profiles.

Conclusion

BBQ chicken wraps are a fantastic way to enjoy a taste of the Southwest without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you prefer a classic BBQ flavor, a spicy kick, or a fresh twist with mango salsa, there’s a wrap for everyone. These recipes not only provide a delicious meal but also encourage creativity in the kitchen. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and let the flavors of the Southwest come alive in your very own BBQ chicken wraps!
