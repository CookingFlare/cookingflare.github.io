Slow Cooker Creamy Potato and Ham Soup: Warm and Satisfying


Welcome to our cozy corner of delicious recipes! Today, we’re diving into the world of comfort food with a hearty and soul-warming Slow Cooker Creamy Potato and Ham Soup. This recipe is a celebration of simple, wholesome ingredients coming together to create a symphony of flavors that will warm you from the inside out. Whether you’re looking for a comforting weeknight dinner or a dish to share with loved ones, this Slow Cooker Creamy Potato and Ham Soup is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pound russet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prep the Ingredients: Start by peeling and dicing the russet potatoes, chopping the onion, slicing the carrots and celery, and mincing the garlic. Having all the ingredients prepped and ready will make the cooking process a breeze.

  2. Combine in the Slow Cooker: In the slow cooker, combine the chicken broth, diced potatoes, diced ham, chopped onion, sliced carrots, celery, minced garlic, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Give it a gentle stir to ensure all the ingredients are well mixed.

  3. Cook to Perfection: Set the slow cooker to low and let the soup simmer for 7-8 hours, or until the potatoes are tender and the flavors have melded together beautifully.

  4. Add the Cream: Once the soup has cooked to perfection, stir in the heavy cream to add a luxurious creaminess to the soup. Season with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning according to your preference.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the Slow Cooker Creamy Potato and Ham Soup into bowls, garnish with chopped fresh parsley, and serve with your favorite crusty bread or dinner rolls. Each spoonful of this velvety soup is a delightful combination of tender potatoes, savory ham, and a medley of aromatic herbs.

Why We Love This Recipe

  • Convenience: The slow cooker does all the heavy lifting, allowing you to come home to a fragrant and comforting meal without the stress of extensive cooking.

  • Versatility: This recipe is incredibly versatile. You can customize it by adding your favorite ingredients such as corn, peas, or even a dash of hot sauce for a spicy kick.

  • Comfort in a Bowl: There’s something undeniably soothing about a steaming bowl of creamy potato and ham soup. It’s like a warm hug on a chilly day.

  • Leftover Magic: This soup tastes even better the next day, making it a wonderful option for meal prep or leftovers for the week.

In Conclusion

The Slow Cooker Creamy Potato and Ham Soup is a timeless classic that never fails to impress. Its simplicity, heartiness, and comforting nature make it a beloved dish for families and gatherings alike. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, set your slow cooker, and get ready to savor the delightful flavors of this warm and satisfying soup. Happy cooking!
