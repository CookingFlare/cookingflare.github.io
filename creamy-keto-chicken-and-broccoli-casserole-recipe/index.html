Creamy Keto Chicken and Broccoli Casserole Recipe


If you’re on a keto diet and craving a comforting, creamy casserole, then this Creamy Keto Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is a must-try! Packed with protein, healthy fats, and fiber, this dish is not only delicious but also fits perfectly into a low-carb lifestyle. The combination of tender chicken, crisp broccoli, and a rich, creamy sauce makes this casserole a satisfying and nutritious meal for the whole family.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, and paprika. In a large skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Sauté the Broccoli: In the same skillet, add the broccoli florets and sauté for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften. Remove the broccoli from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Prepare the Creamy Sauce: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Add the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, onion powder, and dried thyme. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly.

  5. Assemble the Casserole: Arrange the seared chicken breasts and sautéed broccoli in the prepared baking dish. Pour the creamy sauce over the chicken and broccoli, making sure everything is evenly coated. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top.

  6. Bake the Casserole: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is bubbly and golden.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired and enjoy your creamy keto chicken and broccoli casserole!

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 380
  • Total Fat: 22g
  • Cholesterol: 135mg
  • Sodium: 420mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 9g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 36g

This Creamy Keto Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a side salad for a complete meal. It’s a great way to incorporate healthy fats and vegetables into your diet while satisfying your cravings for a creamy, comforting casserole. Whether you’re following a keto lifestyle or simply looking for a delicious new recipe to try, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Try it out and savor the creamy goodness of this delightful keto-friendly casserole!
