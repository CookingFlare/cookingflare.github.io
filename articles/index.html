Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes: A Plant-Based Take on a Classic

If you’re looking for a hearty, satisfying, and entirely plant-based meal that will please even the most devoted meat-eaters, then look no further than these Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes. This recipe puts a delicious and nutritious spin on the classic sloppy joe, using wholesome ingredients and bold flavors to create a dish that’s perfect for weeknight dinners, potlucks, or any occasion that calls for good food and good company. Ingredients 1 cup of dried green or brown lentils 3 cups of vegetable broth 1 tablespoon of olive oil 1 onion, finely chopped 2 cloves of garlic, minced 1 bell pepper, diced 1 carrot, grated 1 can (14 oz) of crushed tomatoes 2 tablespoons of tomato paste 2 tablespoons of maple syrup 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar 1 tablespoon of soy sauce 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika 1 teaspoon of cumin 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder Salt and pepper to taste 6 hamburger buns Instructions Cooking the Lentils Rinse the lentils under cold water and drain them. In a medium saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Add the lentils to the saucepan, reduce the heat, and let them simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender but still hold their shape. Once the lentils are cooked, drain any excess liquid and set them aside. Making the Sloppy Joe Filling In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes. Add the diced bell pepper and grated carrot to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, maple syrup, apple cider vinegar, soy sauce, smoked paprika, cumin, and chili powder. Stir to combine all the ingredients. Add the cooked lentils to the skillet and stir until everything is well combined. Let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Assembling the Sloppy Joes Toast the hamburger buns until they are golden brown and slightly crispy. Spoon a generous amount of the lentil sloppy joe filling onto the bottom half of each bun. Place the top half of the bun over the filling, and serve the sloppy joes with your favorite side dish or a fresh salad. Why Choose Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes These Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes are not only delicious but also packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients.
