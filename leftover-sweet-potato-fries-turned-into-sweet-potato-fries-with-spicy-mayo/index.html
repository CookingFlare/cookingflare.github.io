Leftover Sweet Potato Fries Turned into Sweet Potato Fries with Spicy Mayo


Have you ever found yourself with a container of leftover sweet potato fries that you just didn’t know what to do with? Well, fret no more! In this recipe, I’m going to show you how to transform those leftover sweet potato fries into a mouthwatering snack that will have you coming back for more. And the best part? We’re going to kick it up a notch by adding a spicy mayo dipping sauce that will take these fries to a whole new level of deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • Leftover sweet potato fries
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Spread the leftover sweet potato fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Make sure the fries are not touching each other to ensure even crisping.

  3. Bake the fries in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes, or until they are crispy and heated through. Keep a close eye on them to prevent burning.

  4. While the fries are baking, let’s prepare the spicy mayo dipping sauce. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Sriracha sauce, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated. Adjust the amount of Sriracha sauce according to your heat tolerance.

  5. Once the sweet potato fries are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly. The cooling process will help them become even crispier.

  6. Serve the sweet potato fries alongside the spicy mayo dipping sauce. You can either drizzle the sauce over the fries or serve it on the side for dipping.

  7. Enjoy your leftover sweet potato fries turned into sweet potato fries with spicy mayo! The combination of the crispy fries with the creamy and spicy mayo is simply irresistible.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have leftover sweet potato fries, you can make a fresh batch specifically for this recipe. Simply cut sweet potatoes into fry shapes, toss them with a little olive oil, salt, and pepper, and bake them in the oven until crispy.

  • Feel free to customize the spicy mayo dipping sauce according to your taste preferences. You can add other spices such as garlic powder, paprika, or cayenne pepper for an extra kick.

  • Serve these sweet potato fries as an appetizer, a side dish, or even as a tasty snack. They pair well with burgers, sandwiches, or simply on their own.

  • If you want to make these sweet potato fries even more indulgent, you can sprinkle them with grated Parmesan cheese or chopped fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro before baking.

  • Leftover sweet potato fries can also be used as a topping for salads or turned into a delicious breakfast hash with some sautéed onions, bell peppers, and a fried egg on top.

So, the next time you find yourself with leftover sweet potato fries, don’t let them go to waste. Give them a delicious makeover with this recipe and enjoy the crispy goodness of sweet potato fries with a spicy mayo dipping sauce. It’s a simple and flavorful way to turn leftovers into something truly amazing.
