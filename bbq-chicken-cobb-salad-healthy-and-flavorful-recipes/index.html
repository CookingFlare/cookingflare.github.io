BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad: Healthy and Flavorful Recipes


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s perfect for summer? Look no further than this BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad! Packed with protein, fresh vegetables, and a burst of flavor, this salad is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a satisfying meal, this BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad is the perfect choice.

Ingredients

For the BBQ Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Salad:

  • 6 cups mixed salad greens
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 large avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled bacon
  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

For the Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the BBQ Chicken

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then brush them with olive oil.
  3. Place the chicken breasts on the grill and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through.
  4. During the last few minutes of grilling, brush the chicken with BBQ sauce, allowing it to caramelize and create a delicious glaze.
  5. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.

Step 2: Assemble the Salad

  1. In a large bowl, arrange the mixed salad greens as the base of the salad.
  2. Arrange the sliced hard-boiled eggs, cherry tomatoes, corn kernels, diced avocado, crumbled bacon, and crumbled blue cheese on top of the greens.
  3. Add the sliced BBQ chicken on the salad.

Step 3: Make the Dressing

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until smooth and well combined.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Drizzle the dressing over the BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad, or serve it on the side.
  2. Garnish the salad with chopped green onions.
  3. Toss the salad just before serving to ensure each bite is coated with the delicious dressing.

Nutritional Information

This BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad is not only bursting with flavor, but it’s also a nutritious meal option. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and an array of vitamins and minerals from the fresh vegetables, this salad is a well-rounded choice for a satisfying meal.

  • Total calories: 450
  • Protein: 30g
  • Fat: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fiber: 8g

Conclusion

There you have it – a mouthwatering BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad that’s both healthy and flavorful. Whether you’re looking to impress guests at a summer gathering or simply treat yourself to a satisfying meal, this salad is a must-try. The combination of smoky BBQ chicken, crisp vegetables, and creamy dressing creates a harmony of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing. Give this recipe a try and elevate your salad game with this BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad!
