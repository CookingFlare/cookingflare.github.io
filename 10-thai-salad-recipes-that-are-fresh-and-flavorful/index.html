10 Thai Salad Recipes That Are Fresh and Flavorful


Thai cuisine is renowned for its vibrant flavors, fresh ingredients, and harmonious balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy elements. One of the best ways to experience these delightful flavors is through Thai salads. These dishes are not only refreshing but also packed with nutrients, making them perfect for a light meal or a side dish. In this post, we will explore ten Thai salad recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds and bring a burst of freshness to your table.

1. Thai Green Papaya Salad (Som Tum)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups green papaya, shredded
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup green beans, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 Thai bird’s eye chilies, smashed
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, crushed

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded green papaya, cherry tomatoes, and green beans.
  2. In a mortar and pestle, pound the garlic and chilies together until fragrant.
  3. Add the fish sauce, lime juice, and palm sugar to the mixture, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to combine.
  5. Top with crushed peanuts before serving.

This salad is a staple in Thai cuisine, offering a delightful crunch and a perfect balance of flavors.

2. Thai Beef Salad (Yam Nua)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, grilled and sliced
  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chili flakes
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the fish sauce, lime juice, sugar, and chili flakes.
  3. Add the grilled beef slices to the salad and drizzle the dressing over the top.
  4. Toss gently to combine and garnish with fresh cilantro.

This hearty salad is perfect for a filling lunch or dinner, combining protein with fresh vegetables.

3. Thai Mango Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, julienned
  • 1 cup red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/4 cup roasted cashews, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mango, red bell pepper, red onion, mint, and cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, fish sauce, and honey.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Top with chopped roasted cashews before serving.

This salad is a delightful mix of sweet and savory, making it a perfect side dish for grilled meats.

4. Thai Cucumber Salad (Som Tum Taeng)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, crushed

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cucumber, red onion, cherry tomatoes, and cilantro.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the fish sauce, lime juice, sugar, and sesame oil.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to combine.
  4. Sprinkle with crushed peanuts before serving.

This refreshing cucumber salad is perfect for hot summer days and pairs well with spicy dishes.
