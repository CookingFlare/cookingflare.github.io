Savoury Sides: 7 Unique Flatbread Variation Recipes You Must Try


Flatbreads are tasty, versatile, and can be enjoyed in various forms across different cultures. In this blog post, we shall explore seven unique flatbread recipes that will serve as delightful savoury sides to your main courses. Made with a diverse range of ingredients, these flatbreads will create excitement in each bite, capturing different flavour profiles. So, get your apron on, roll out that dough, and be ready to embark on a culinary adventure!

1. Za’atar and Olive Oil Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon za’atar spice blend
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and yeast. Slowly mix in warm water.
  2. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes and shape it into a ball. Let it rest for 30 minutes, covered with a damp cloth.
  3. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface into a flat, circle shape.
  4. Heat half of the olive oil in a small saucepan and brush it onto the flatbread.
  5. Sprinkle za’atar over the flatbread and add a pinch of salt. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until golden.
  6. Slice and garnish with chopped parsley. Serve warm.

2. Garlic and Rosemary Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 2 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, and yeast. Slowly mix in warm water, garlic, and rosemary.
  2. Knead the dough, then set it aside for 30 minutes to rest.
  3. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough and brush it with olive oil.
  4. Add salt and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until crispy and lightly browned.
  5. Allow the flatbread to cool slightly before slicing into pieces.

3. Spinach and Feta Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, and yeast. Slowly mix in warm water, spinach, and feta.
  2. Knead the dough until well combined and let it rest, covered, for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough, brush with olive oil, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Slice and serve warm.

4. Caramelised Onion and Brie Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 2 cups thinly sliced onions
  • 1 cup brie cheese, chopped
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, and yeast. Mix in warm water.
  2. Knead the dough and let it rest, covered, for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough, top with caramelised onions, brie, and drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until the cheese has melted. Sprinkle with salt and pepper before serving.

5. Pesto and Cherry Tomato Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 2/3 cup homemade or store-bought pesto
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl, kneading until well combined. Set aside to rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough and spread pesto onto the surface.
  3. Place cherry tomatoes on the pesto-covered flatbread. Brush with olive oil and add salt and pepper.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is crispy and the tomatoes are slightly roasted.

6. Jalapeño and Cheddar Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 2 jalapeño peppers, thinly sliced (remove seeds if you prefer less heat)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine flour, sugar, and yeast in a large mixing bowl. Mix in warm water, jalapeños, and cheddar before kneading.
  2. Let dough rest for 30 minutes, covered.
  3. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough and brush with olive oil. Add salt and bake.
  4. Cook for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is crispy, and cheese is melted.

7. Sweet Potato and Sage Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 2 cups cooked, mashed sweet potato
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh sage
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, and yeast. Slowly mix in warm water, sweet potato, and sage.
  2. Knead the dough and let it rest, covered, for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough and brush with olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.

Now you have seven unique flatbread variations that can elevate any meal. These delectable savoury treats are perfect for impressing your dinner guests or simply enjoy a culinary adventure in your own home. So, roll up your sleeves and embrace the delightful world of flatbreads!
