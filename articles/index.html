Mediterranean-Inspired Breakfast Cookies: A Healthy Treat

Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious way to start your day? Look no further than these Mediterranean-inspired breakfast cookies! Packed with wholesome ingredients and flavors reminiscent of the Mediterranean region, these cookies are a delightful and healthy treat that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized. Ingredients 1 cup rolled oats 1/2 cup almond flour 1/4 cup flaxseeds 1/4 cup chopped almonds 1/4 cup dried cranberries 1 teaspoon cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon sea salt 1/4 cup honey 1/4 cup olive oil 1 teaspoon vanilla extract Zest of 1 orange Instructions Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a large bowl, combine the rolled oats, almond flour, flaxseeds, chopped almonds, dried cranberries, cinnamon, and sea salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the honey, olive oil, vanilla extract, and orange zest. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until well combined. Using a cookie scoop or spoon, portion the dough and place it onto the prepared baking sheet. Use your fingers to gently flatten each cookie. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
