Sous Vide Chicken Satay: A Flavorful and Tender Skewer


Are you looking for a unique and flavorful way to enjoy chicken skewers? Look no further than Sous Vide Chicken Satay. This cooking method ensures that the chicken is tender, juicy, and infused with the rich flavors of the marinade. Whether you’re hosting a barbecue, a dinner party, or simply craving a delicious meal, this recipe is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs (680g) chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon (15ml) ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon (5ml) curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2.5ml) turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon (1.25ml) cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon (1.25ml) cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Marinade

In a bowl, combine the coconut milk, soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, grated ginger, curry powder, turmeric, cumin, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the marinade is thoroughly combined.

Step 2: Marinate the Chicken

Place the chicken strips in a resealable plastic bag or a vacuum-sealed bag. Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring that each strip is coated evenly. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to infuse.

Step 3: Preheat the Sous Vide Water Bath

Fill a large pot or container with water and attach the sous vide precision cooker. Set the temperature to 140°F (60°C) for perfectly tender chicken.

Step 4: Cook the Chicken

Once the water reaches the desired temperature, place the sealed bag of chicken in the water bath. Cook for 1 to 2 hours, ensuring that the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 140°F (60°C) for pasteurization.

Step 5: Finish on the Grill

After cooking in the sous vide bath, remove the chicken from the bag and thread the strips onto the soaked wooden skewers. Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Grill the chicken skewers for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop a beautiful char and caramelization.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the chicken skewers are grilled to perfection, transfer them to a serving platter. Garnish with chopped cilantro, crushed peanuts, and a side of peanut dipping sauce. Serve the Sous Vide Chicken Satay skewers hot and watch them disappear within minutes!

Why Sous Vide?

Sous vide cooking is a game-changer for achieving perfectly tender and flavorful chicken. The precise temperature control ensures that the chicken is cooked to the ideal doneness without any risk of overcooking. The marinade infuses the chicken while it cooks, resulting in a depth of flavor that traditional grilling or baking methods can’t match.

Final Thoughts

Sous Vide Chicken Satay is a delightful twist on the classic skewered chicken dish. The tenderness of the chicken combined with the aromatic and savory marinade creates a truly unforgettable culinary experience. Whether you’re a sous vide enthusiast or new to this cooking technique, this recipe is a must-try. Elevate your next gathering with these succulent and flavorful chicken skewers that are bound to become a crowd favorite. Happy cooking!
