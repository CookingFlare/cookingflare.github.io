Rich and Creamy Instant Pot Tiramisu Recipe for a Decadent Dessert


Instant Pot, a kitchen appliance that has revolutionized the way we cook, has now made it possible to create a rich and creamy tiramisu dessert without any hassle. This Italian classic is a crowd-pleaser, and with this Instant Pot recipe, you can impress your family and friends with a decadent dessert that’s sure to satisfy their sweet tooth.

Ingredients:

  • 12-16 ladyfingers
  • 1 cup of strong brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter, softened
  • 8 ounces of mascarpone cheese
  • 8 ounces of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Coffee Syrup

In a small bowl, combine the brewed coffee and softened butter. Stir until the butter is fully incorporated and the mixture is smooth.

Step 2: Dip the Ladyfingers

Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee syrup for about 3-5 seconds on each side. They should be soft and pliable but not too wet.

Step 3: Prepare the Cream Mixture

In a separate bowl, combine the mascarpone cheese, heavy cream, granulated sugar, egg yolks, vanilla extract, and salt. Beat the mixture with an electric mixer until it’s smooth and creamy.

Step 4: Assemble the Tiramisu

In the Instant Pot, create a layer of ladyfingers. You can trim them to fit the bottom of the pot if necessary. Spread half of the cream mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat this process, creating another layer of ladyfingers and topping it with the remaining cream mixture.

Step 5: Cook and Press

Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to “sealing”. Press the “manual” or “pressure cook” button and set the cooking time to 5 minutes at high pressure. Let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.

Open the lid and use a spatula to press down on the tiramisu, releasing any air bubbles that may have formed.

Step 6: Chill and Serve

Let the tiramisu cool to room temperature, then cover it with plastic wrap and refrigerate it for at least 3 hours or overnight. Dust the top with cocoa powder before serving.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a stronger coffee flavor, you can use espresso instead of brewed coffee.
  • You can also add a layer of chocolate shavings or cocoa powder to the top of the tiramisu for extra flavor and texture.
  • If you prefer a lighter texture, you can use less mascarpone cheese or add more heavy cream.
  • You can also make individual servings of tiramisu in small cups or ramekins by adjusting the cooking time to 3-4 minutes.

Conclusion:

With this Rich and Creamy Instant Pot Tiramisu Recipe, you can create a decadent dessert that’s sure to impress your family and friends. The Instant Pot makes it easy to cook the tiramisu to perfection, and the result is a creamy and rich dessert that’s perfect for any occasion. So go ahead, give it a try, and indulge in the sweet and creamy goodness of this Italian classic.
