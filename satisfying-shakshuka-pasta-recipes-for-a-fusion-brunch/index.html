Satisfying Shakshuka Pasta Recipes for a Fusion Brunch


Shakshuka, a traditional Middle Eastern dish consisting of eggs poached in a flavorful tomato sauce, has become a beloved breakfast and brunch option around the world. Its rich and tangy flavors make for a satisfying meal that is both comforting and exciting. In this recipe blog post, we’re going to take Shakshuka to the next level by fusing it with another beloved dish: pasta. Get ready for a fusion brunch experience like no other!

Recipe 1: Spicy Shakshuka Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the red bell pepper, smoked paprika, cumin, and chili flakes to the skillet. Cook for another 3-4 minutes, until the pepper begins to soften.
  4. Pour in the canned chopped tomatoes and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Create four wells in the sauce and crack an egg into each well. Cover the skillet and cook for about 5 minutes, until the eggs are cooked to your desired consistency.
  6. Serve the spicy Shakshuka pasta over the cooked spaghetti. Garnish with fresh parsley and enjoy!

Recipe 2: Creamy Shakshuka Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 250g fettuccine
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200g bacon, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 100ml heavy cream
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and bacon, and sauté until the bacon is crispy and the onion is translucent.
  3. Sprinkle smoked paprika and dried oregano over the bacon mixture. Stir well to combine.
  4. Pour in the canned chopped tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Create four wells in the sauce and crack an egg into each well. Cover the skillet and cook for about 5 minutes, until the eggs are cooked to your desired consistency.
  7. Serve the creamy Shakshuka carbonara over the cooked fettuccine. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and enjoy!

Recipe 3: Mediterranean Shakshuka Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 250g penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the zucchini, red bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, dried basil, and dried oregano to the skillet. Cook for another 5-7 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with the vegetable mixture in the skillet. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Remove from heat and crumble the feta cheese over the pasta. Add the Kalamata olives and gently toss to combine.
  6. Serve the Mediterranean Shakshuka pasta salad warm or chilled. Enjoy as a refreshing and satisfying fusion brunch option!

These satisfying Shakshuka pasta recipes offer a delightful fusion of flavors, combining the best of Middle Eastern and Italian cuisines. Whether you prefer a spicy kick, a creamy indulgence, or a refreshing salad, these recipes are guaranteed to elevate your brunch experience. So gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and get ready to impress your family and friends with these delicious and unique Shakshuka pasta creations. Bon appétit!
