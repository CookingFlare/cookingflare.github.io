Delicious Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes: A Summertime Favorite


Summer is the perfect time for light and refreshing desserts, and what better way to celebrate the season than with delicious Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes? These delightful treats are a twist on the classic strawberry shortcake, combining the flavors of fresh strawberries, fluffy cake, and creamy frosting in a convenient, handheld form. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, planning a picnic in the park, or simply craving a sweet indulgence, these cupcakes are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk

For the Strawberry Filling:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

For the Whipped Cream Frosting:

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cupcakes:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper cupcake liners.
    • In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
    • In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
    • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.
    • Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.
    • Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely.

  2. Prepare the Strawberry Filling:

    • In a medium bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, granulated sugar, and lemon juice. Stir to coat the strawberries evenly and allow the mixture to macerate for at least 30 minutes.

  3. Prepare the Whipped Cream Frosting:

    • In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

  4. Assemble the Cupcakes:

    • Using a paring knife, gently remove the center of each cupcake to create a small well.
    • Spoon the macerated strawberries into the wells, allowing any excess juices to soak into the cupcakes.
    • Pipe or spread the whipped cream frosting over the filled cupcakes, creating a decorative swirl or dollop on top.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • These Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes are best enjoyed the same day they are assembled. Store any leftovers in the refrigerator.

Final Thoughts

Delicious Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes are a delightful way to savor the flavors of summer. The combination of tender cake, juicy strawberries, and billowy whipped cream is simply irresistible. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a sweet indulgence, these cupcakes are sure to bring a smile to your face. So, gather your fresh strawberries, whip up some fluffy cupcakes, and indulge in the blissful flavors of summertime. Cheers to a season filled with delicious moments and sweet treats!
