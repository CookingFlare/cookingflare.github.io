Cherry Almond Galette: A Rustic Summer Dessert


There’s something special about a rustic galette that captures the essence of summer. The free-form nature of this dessert allows the vibrant flavors of fresh cherries and the nutty richness of almonds to take center stage. This Cherry Almond Galette recipe is a delightful way to celebrate the abundance of summer fruits and create a stunning, yet effortless dessert that will impress your family and friends. So, let’s dive into the delightful world of galettes and create a memorable treat that will elevate your summer gatherings.

Ingredients

For the Galette Dough:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Cherry Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh cherries, pitted and halved
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • Zest of 1 lemon

For Assembly:

  • 1/3 cup almond flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Sliced almonds (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Galette Dough:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, almond flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Drizzle the ice water over the mixture and gently mix it with a fork until the dough begins to come together. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it a few times to bring it together into a smooth ball. Flatten the dough into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  2. Prepare the Cherry Filling:

    • In a large bowl, toss the pitted and halved cherries with granulated sugar, cornstarch, almond extract, and lemon zest until well combined. Set the cherry filling aside while you roll out the galette dough.

  3. Assemble the Galette:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    • On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled galette dough into a rough circle about 12 inches in diameter. Transfer the rolled-out dough to the prepared baking sheet.
    • Sprinkle the almond flour and granulated sugar over the center of the galette dough, leaving a 2-inch border around the edges. Arrange the prepared cherry filling over the almond flour mixture.

  4. Fold and Bake the Galette:

    • Gently fold the edges of the dough over the filling, pleating as you go to create a free-form crust. Brush the edges of the dough with the beaten egg and sprinkle the sliced almonds over the crust.
    • Bake the cherry almond galette for 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cherry filling is bubbling. Allow the galette to cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Cherry Almond Galette is best enjoyed warm, either on its own or with a dollop of vanilla ice cream or a drizzle of almond-flavored whipped cream. The combination of the sweet cherry filling and the nutty almond crust creates a delightful contrast of flavors and textures that will surely impress your guests.

Conclusion

The beauty of a galette lies in its simplicity and rustic charm, making it the perfect dessert for laid-back summer gatherings. Embrace the imperfections and allow the natural flavors of the cherries and almonds to shine through in every bite. This Cherry Almond Galette is a celebration of summer’s bounty, and it’s a delightful way to savor the season’s best fruits in a stunning, yet effortless dessert. So, go ahead, gather the freshest cherries you can find, and let the magic of this rustic galette bring joy to your summer table. Cheers to the simple pleasures of homemade desserts and the flavors of summer!
