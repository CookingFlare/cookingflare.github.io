Spanish Inspired Spinach and Manchego Empanadas Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying dish that’s perfect for any occasion, then these Spanish inspired spinach and Manchego empanadas are just what you need. Empanadas are savory pastries filled with a variety of ingredients, and this recipe puts a flavorful spin on the traditional Spanish favorite. Whether you’re hosting a party, planning a picnic, or simply craving a tasty snack, these empanadas are sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to make these mouthwatering treats!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup cold water
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 10 ounces fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup grated Manchego cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions

Making the Dough

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt together.
  2. Add the cold water and olive oil to the flour mixture.
  3. Knead the dough until it comes together and forms a smooth ball.
  4. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.

Preparing the Filling

  1. In a skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted.
  5. Season the spinach mixture with paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the filling cool.

Assembling the Empanadas

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/8 inch thickness.
  3. Using a round cutter or a small plate, cut the dough into circles.
  4. Spoon a portion of the spinach filling onto one half of each circle.
  5. Sprinkle grated Manchego cheese over the filling.
  6. Fold the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape and crimp the edges with a fork to seal.
  7. Place the empanadas on the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Brush the tops of the empanadas with the beaten egg to create a golden crust.

Baking the Empanadas

  1. Bake the empanadas in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.
  2. Remove the empanadas from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Spanish inspired spinach and Manchego empanadas are delicious on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a side of chimichurri sauce for dipping. The fresh, herby flavors of chimichurri complement the savory filling of the empanadas perfectly, adding a zesty kick to every bite.

Conclusion

With their flaky crust and flavorful filling, these Spanish inspired spinach and Manchego empanadas are a crowd-pleasing dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a taste of Spain right in your own home!
