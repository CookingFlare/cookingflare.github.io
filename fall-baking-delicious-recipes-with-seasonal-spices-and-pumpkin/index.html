Fall Baking: Delicious Recipes with Seasonal Spices and Pumpkin


Fall is in the air, and it’s time to embrace the cozy vibes of the autumn season. What better way to do that than by indulging in some delicious fall baking? With the abundance of seasonal spices and the versatility of pumpkin, there are endless possibilities to create mouthwatering treats that will warm your heart and satisfy your taste buds. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delectable fall baking recipes that are sure to impress.

1. Spiced Pumpkin Bread

Start your fall baking journey with a classic - Spiced Pumpkin Bread. This moist and flavorful bread is packed with aromatic spices and the rich taste of pumpkin. It’s the perfect treat to enjoy with a cup of hot cocoa or a pumpkin spice latte. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the pumpkin puree. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk. Stir in the vanilla extract. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan and bake for 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

2. Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes

Take your fall baking to the next level with these irresistible Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes. Combining the flavors of a classic pumpkin spice latte with a moist and tender cupcake, these treats are a true delight. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee, dissolved in 1 tablespoon hot water

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the pumpkin puree. In a small bowl, combine the milk, vanilla extract, and dissolved instant coffee. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk mixture. Pour the batter into cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely before frosting.

3. Cinnamon Sugar Pumpkin Donuts

Indulge in the ultimate fall treat with these irresistible Cinnamon Sugar Pumpkin Donuts. These fluffy and moist donuts are coated in a sweet and aromatic cinnamon sugar mixture, making them the perfect companion for a cozy evening. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger. In a separate large bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar, brown sugar, vegetable oil, eggs, pumpkin puree, buttermilk, and vanilla extract. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Spoon the batter into a greased donut pan, filling each donut mold about 2/3 full. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the donuts comes out clean. Allow the donuts to cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack. While the donuts are still warm, roll them in a mixture of cinnamon and sugar until fully coated. Serve and enjoy!

Fall baking is a wonderful way to celebrate the season and indulge in comforting flavors. Whether you’re a fan of pumpkin spice or prefer the warmth of cinnamon and nutmeg, these recipes are sure to satisfy your autumn cravings. So, put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of fall baking fill your home. Happy baking!
