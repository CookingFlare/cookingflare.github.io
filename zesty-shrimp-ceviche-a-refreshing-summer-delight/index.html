Zesty Shrimp Ceviche: A Refreshing Summer Delight


Summertime calls for light and refreshing dishes that can beat the heat and satisfy your taste buds. One such dish that perfectly fits the bill is Zesty Shrimp Ceviche. Bursting with flavors and loaded with fresh ingredients, this dish is a crowd-pleaser and a go-to for any summer gathering. In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps of creating this tantalizing dish that will surely leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of fresh lime juice
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shrimp and lime juice. Make sure the shrimp is fully submerged in the lime juice. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour. The lime juice will cook the shrimp, giving it a tender and flavorful taste.

  2. While the shrimp is marinating, prepare the rest of the ingredients. Finely chop the red onion, mince the jalapeno pepper, dice the tomatoes, cucumber, and avocado, and chop the cilantro. Set them aside in separate bowls.

  3. After the shrimp has marinated, remove it from the lime juice and discard the juice. Chop the shrimp into bite-sized pieces and transfer them to a clean bowl.

  4. Add the red onion, jalapeno pepper, tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and cilantro to the bowl with the shrimp. Gently toss the ingredients together to combine.

  5. Season the ceviche with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to start with a small amount and adjust according to your preference. The lime juice already adds a tangy flavor, so be mindful not to over-season.

  6. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the ceviche for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This will also help the ceviche to become more chilled and refreshing.

  7. When ready to serve, give the ceviche a quick stir and transfer it to a serving dish. Garnish with additional cilantro if desired.

  8. Serve the Zesty Shrimp Ceviche with tortilla chips on the side. The crispy chips provide a perfect contrast to the zesty ceviche, giving you a delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder ceviche, you can reduce the amount of jalapeno pepper or omit it altogether.
  • Feel free to add other ingredients to the ceviche, such as diced mango, pineapple, or corn, to give it a tropical twist.
  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add a splash of hot sauce or a sprinkle of chili powder to the ceviche.
  • Experiment with different types of seafood, such as scallops or white fish, to create your own unique ceviche recipe.

So there you have it, a recipe for Zesty Shrimp Ceviche that will transport you to a tropical paradise with every bite. This refreshing summer delight is perfect for picnics, barbecues, or simply enjoying a sunny day. Give it a try and let the flavors dance on your palate. Bon appétit!
