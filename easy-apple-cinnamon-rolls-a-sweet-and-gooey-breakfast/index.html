Easy Apple Cinnamon Rolls: A Sweet and Gooey Breakfast


Are you looking for a delicious and indulgent breakfast treat that will make your taste buds sing? Look no further than these Easy Apple Cinnamon Rolls! With their soft and fluffy texture, warm and comforting flavors, and gooey caramel apple filling, these cinnamon rolls are the perfect way to start your day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup water
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

For the filling:

  • 3 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and chopped into small pieces
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast. Set aside.

  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk, water, and melted butter until warm (about 110°F). Remove from heat and whisk in the egg.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  5. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling. In a medium-sized saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped apples, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon. Cook for about 5 minutes, until the apples are tender and the mixture is caramelized. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.

  6. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down to release the air. Roll it out on a lightly floured surface into a 12x18-inch rectangle.

  7. Spread the cooled apple filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Starting from one long side, tightly roll the dough into a log.

  8. Cut the log into 12 equal-sized rolls and place them in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with a kitchen towel and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.

  9. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the cinnamon rolls for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  10. While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  11. Once the cinnamon rolls are out of the oven, let them cool for a few minutes before drizzling the glaze over the top. Serve warm and enjoy!

These Easy Apple Cinnamon Rolls are a delightful combination of sweet, tart apples, warm cinnamon, and the perfect touch of gooey caramel. They are the ultimate breakfast treat that will have your family and friends coming back for seconds.

So, why wait? Surprise your loved ones with these indulgent and irresistible cinnamon rolls, and make your mornings a little sweeter. Happy baking!
