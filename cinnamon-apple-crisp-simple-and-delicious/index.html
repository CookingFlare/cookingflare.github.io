Cinnamon Apple Crisp: Simple and Delicious


Apple crisp is a classic dessert that’s cozy, warm, and packed full of apple flavor. Once you taste this cinnamon-spiced treat, you’ll surely crave more. This easy recipe is perfect for busy home cooks or anyone looking to impress their guests with minimal effort. Best of all, the ingredients are likely already in your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium apples (rely on the season’s best, and try to pick different varieties for added flavor)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cold butter, sliced (use a dairy-free substitute if you prefer)
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. First, prepare your apples. Peel, core, and thinly slice the apples and place them into a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. In another bowl, mix together the flour, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and salt. This combination serves as both the crunch and the spice you’re after. Make sure everything is combined well.
  3. Cut the cold butter into small chunks and add them to the dry mix. Using either your hands or a pastry cutter, create a coarse and crumbly mixture by working the butter into the dry ingredients.
  4. Pour the vanilla extract into the apple mixture and toss gently to coat. Grab a 9x13 baking dish and spread half of the flour mixture evenly over the bottom. Then, scoop the apples onto the mix in the dish, being careful not to knock off the bottom layer.
  5. Sprinkle the remainder of the flour mixture over the apples. Use your hands or a fork to create a “crispy peak” by pressing down gently on the top layer of flour mix. This will ensure that your crust crisps up just right during baking.
  6. Place the dish on a middle rack in the preheated oven. Bake at 375 degrees Fahrenheit for about 40–45 minutes, or until the apples are just tender, and the top has a lovely, golden-brown hue.
  7. Remove from the oven and let sit for a few minutes before serving. Don’t forget to scoop a helping of warm cinnamon-spiced apples and crunch together! This dessert can also be served with vanilla ice cream or sweet whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgent goodness.

Variations

Should you be feeling adventurous, try adding other flavors. For instance, sprinkle some freshly grated nutmeg or some ground ginger for an added depth. Substitute half of the cinnamon with cardamom for an exotic flavor.

Change things up with some chopped walnuts or chopped pecans mixed into the topping. Both options add a delightful crunch.

If you’d like to experiment with fruit variety, create a mixed-berry apple crisp by adding a cup of your favorite seasonal fruit under the apples. Capture the season by picking fresh berries in the summer or a mix of tart and sweet fruits in colder months.

Remember, cooking should be fun and exciting. Do not be afraid to experiment with this recipe, and make it your very own!

Happy cooking and indulge in comfort! Enjoy your Cinnamon Apple Crisp.
