Fire Up the Grill: Gluten-Free BBQ Chicken Skewers


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious barbecue. If you’re looking for a gluten-free option that’s sure to impress your guests, then look no further than these mouthwatering BBQ chicken skewers. Packed with flavor and easy to make, these skewers are a must-try for any barbecue enthusiast.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup gluten-free BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Cut the chicken breasts into 1-inch cubes and place them in a large bowl.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: In a separate bowl, mix the BBQ sauce, honey, olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Pour the marinade over the chicken cubes, making sure they are well-coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  3. Preheat the Grill: While the chicken is marinating, preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  4. Skewer the Chicken: Once the chicken has marinated, thread the chicken pieces onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each piece. This will ensure even cooking.

  5. Grill the Skewers: Place the chicken skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice charred exterior.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken is cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. Serve the BBQ chicken skewers with your favorite gluten-free side dishes and enjoy!

Tips for Perfect BBQ Chicken Skewers

  • Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes before threading the chicken to prevent them from burning on the grill.
  • If using metal skewers, remember to handle them with care as they can get very hot during grilling.
  • Brush any remaining marinade onto the chicken skewers while grilling for an extra burst of flavor.

Why Choose Gluten-Free BBQ Chicken Skewers?

Not only are these BBQ chicken skewers bursting with flavor, but they are also gluten-free, making them a great option for anyone with dietary restrictions. By using a high-quality gluten-free BBQ sauce, you can ensure that everyone at your barbecue can enjoy these delectable skewers without worry.

Get Creative with Your Gluten-Free BBQ Chicken Skewers

Feel free to customize your BBQ chicken skewers by adding vegetables such as bell peppers, onions, or cherry tomatoes to the skewers for added color and flavor. You can also try different gluten-free BBQ sauce variations to find your perfect combination of sweet, tangy, and smoky flavors.

Final Thoughts

Fire up the grill and treat your friends and family to these irresistible gluten-free BBQ chicken skewers. With a simple marinade and easy grilling process, you’ll have a crowd-pleasing dish that’s perfect for any summer gathering. So, grab your skewers and get ready to savor the flavors of summer with these fantastic gluten-free BBQ chicken skewers. Happy grilling!
