Zesty Mexican Chicken Enchilada Casserole: A Comforting Twist on a Classic


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine and love the comforting flavors of a classic enchilada, then you’re in for a treat with this zesty Mexican chicken enchilada casserole recipe. This dish takes the traditional elements of a chicken enchilada and transforms them into a delicious, easy-to-make casserole that’s perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers throughout the week. Packed with bold flavors, gooey cheese, and tender chicken, this dish is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

To make this zesty Mexican chicken enchilada casserole, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, sliced jalapeños, sour cream, avocado

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, red enchilada sauce, diced green chilies, ground cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Spread a small amount of the chicken mixture on the bottom of the dish.
  4. Layer 4 corn tortillas over the chicken mixture, overlapping as necessary to cover the bottom of the dish.
  5. Spread half of the remaining chicken mixture over the tortillas, then sprinkle with 1/3 of the shredded cheese.
  6. Repeat the layers with 4 more tortillas, the remaining chicken mixture, and another 1/3 of the shredded cheese.
  7. Finish with the remaining 4 tortillas and sprinkle the remaining 1/3 of the shredded cheese over the top.
  8. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle the chopped cilantro over the top.
  10. Serve the zesty Mexican chicken enchilada casserole with your choice of optional toppings, such as diced tomatoes, sliced jalapeños, sour cream, and avocado.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian option, you can substitute the chicken with black beans or roasted vegetables.
  • To make this dish spicy, use a spicy enchilada sauce and add extra diced jalapeños to the chicken mixture.
  • This casserole can be assembled ahead of time and refrigerated until ready to bake. Simply increase the baking time by 10-15 minutes if baking from cold.

Conclusion

This zesty Mexican chicken enchilada casserole is a delightful twist on a classic dish, bringing all the flavors of traditional enchiladas in an easy-to-serve casserole format. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner, a potluck, or just meal prepping for the week, this recipe is a winner. The layers of tender chicken, flavorful spices, and gooey cheese will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, gather your ingredients and give this comforting Mexican-inspired casserole a try. It’s a guaranteed crowd-pleaser!
