Delectable Chicken Pot Pie Recipes for Comforting Meals


There’s something undeniably comforting about a warm, flaky chicken pot pie straight from the oven. The combination of tender chicken, savory vegetables, and rich, creamy sauce nestled beneath a golden crust is a timeless classic that never fails to satisfy. Whether you’re looking for a traditional recipe or a creative spin on this beloved dish, we’ve got you covered with a variety of delectable chicken pot pie recipes that are perfect for cozy family dinners or entertaining guests.

Classic Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup celery, diced
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken broth
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 unbaked pie crusts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery, and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, and celery seed until well combined.
  4. Gradually stir in the chicken broth and milk, simmering and stirring constantly until the mixture has thickened.
  5. Add the cooked chicken and peas, then remove the skillet from the heat.
  6. Line a pie dish with one of the pie crusts, then pour the chicken mixture into the crust.
  7. Cover the filling with the second pie crust, sealing and crimping the edges. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.

Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 can artichoke hearts, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 unbaked pie crusts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, spinach, artichoke hearts, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, sour cream, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  3. Line a pie dish with one of the pie crusts, then pour the chicken mixture into the crust.
  4. Cover the filling with the second pie crust, sealing and crimping the edges. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

BBQ Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 unbaked pie crusts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the cooked chicken with the BBQ sauce until well coated.
  3. Stir in the corn kernels and red bell pepper.
  4. Line a pie dish with one of the pie crusts, then pour the chicken mixture into the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the filling, then cover with the second pie crust, sealing and crimping the edges. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

Whether you choose the classic recipe or opt for a more unconventional twist, these delectable chicken pot pie recipes are sure to bring warmth and satisfaction to your table. Experiment with different fillings, spices, and crust variations to create your own signature version of this timeless comfort food. Enjoy the process of baking and savor the delightful aromas that will fill your kitchen as these savory pies bake to perfection.
