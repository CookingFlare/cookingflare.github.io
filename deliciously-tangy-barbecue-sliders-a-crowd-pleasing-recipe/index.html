Deliciously Tangy Barbecue Sliders: A Crowd-Pleasing Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy crowd-pleasing recipe, then look no further than these mouthwatering Deliciously Tangy Barbecue Sliders. Perfect for parties, game nights, or just a fun family dinner, these sliders are packed with flavor and are sure to be a hit with everyone. The combination of savory pulled pork, tangy barbecue sauce, and soft slider buns creates a symphony of taste that will leave your guests craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Pulled Pork:

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth

For the Barbecue Sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional Ingredients:

  • 12 slider buns
  • Sliced pickles (optional)
  • Coleslaw (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C).

  2. In a small bowl, mix the smoked paprika, garlic powder, brown sugar, salt, and pepper. Rub this mixture all over the pork shoulder.

  3. Place the seasoned pork shoulder in a roasting pan and pour the chicken broth into the pan.

  4. Cover the pan with foil and roast in the preheated oven for 6-7 hours, or until the pork is tender and easily falls apart with a fork.

  5. While the pork is roasting, prepare the barbecue sauce. In a saucepan, combine the ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Simmer over low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened.

  6. Once the pork is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, using two forks, shred the pork into bite-sized pieces.

  7. In a large bowl, toss the shredded pork with the prepared barbecue sauce until it is well coated.

  8. To assemble the sliders, slice the slider buns in half and place a generous amount of the saucy pulled pork on the bottom half of each bun.

  9. If desired, add a slice of pickle and a spoonful of coleslaw on top of the pork.

  10. Place the top half of the bun on the fillings and secure each slider with a toothpick.

  11. Serve immediately and watch them disappear in no time!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a dash of hot sauce to the barbecue sauce.
  • You can make the pulled pork ahead of time and reheat it before assembling the sliders.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use a store-bought barbecue sauce, but making it from scratch is well worth the effort.

Conclusion

These Deliciously Tangy Barbecue Sliders are the epitome of comfort food with a delightful twist. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a hearty meal, this recipe is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. The tender, flavorful pulled pork combined with the tangy barbecue sauce and soft buns creates a symphony of flavors that will keep everyone coming back for more. So, roll up your sleeves, get cooking, and get ready to impress your guests with this crowd-pleasing recipe!
