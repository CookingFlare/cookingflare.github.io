Delicious Low Carb Cucumber Sushi Rolls


Are you a sushi lover looking for a healthier alternative to the traditional rice-filled rolls? Look no further! These delicious low carb cucumber sushi rolls are the perfect guilt-free option for sushi enthusiasts who are watching their carbohydrate intake. Packed with fresh ingredients and bursting with flavor, this recipe will satisfy your sushi cravings without compromising your healthy eating goals.

Ingredients

  • 2 large cucumbers
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 small bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 1 small bunch of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 small bunch of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of sriracha sauce (optional, for spice lovers)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the cucumber. Using a sharp knife or a mandoline slicer, carefully slice the cucumber lengthwise into thin strips. These strips will serve as the base for your sushi rolls.

  2. Next, prepare the filling. Peel and slice the avocado into thin strips. Julienne the carrot and red bell pepper into matchstick-sized pieces. Finely chop the cilantro, mint, and basil leaves.

  3. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and sriracha sauce (if using). This will be the delicious dressing for your cucumber rolls.

  4. Lay out one cucumber strip on a clean surface and place a few slices of avocado, carrot, and red bell pepper on top. Sprinkle some chopped cilantro, mint, and basil over the vegetables. Drizzle a small amount of the dressing over the filling.

  5. Carefully roll up the cucumber strip, ensuring that the filling stays in place. Repeat this process with the remaining cucumber strips and filling ingredients.

  6. Once all the rolls are prepared, serve them immediately or refrigerate for a refreshing, chilled snack.

Tips and Variations

  • Don’t limit yourself to just these ingredients! Feel free to get creative and add your favorite sushi fillings like cooked shrimp, crab meat, or thinly sliced cucumber for extra crunch.

  • If you’re not a fan of spicy food, omit the sriracha sauce or adjust the amount according to your taste preferences.

  • These low carb cucumber sushi rolls make a great appetizer for parties or a light lunch option. Serve them with a side of pickled ginger, wasabi, and extra soy sauce for dipping.

  • To make the rolls more visually appealing, feel free to use different colored vegetables like yellow bell pepper or purple cabbage.

  • If you’re planning on making these rolls ahead of time, make sure to wrap them tightly in plastic wrap or store them in an airtight container to prevent them from drying out.

So there you have it – a simple and delicious recipe for low carb cucumber sushi rolls that will satisfy your sushi cravings while keeping your carb intake in check. Give them a try and let your taste buds embark on a flavorful and healthy journey. Enjoy!
