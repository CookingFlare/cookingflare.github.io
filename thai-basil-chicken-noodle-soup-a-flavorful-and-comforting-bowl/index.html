Thai Basil Chicken Noodle Soup: A Flavorful and Comforting Bowl


There’s something undeniably satisfying about a hot bowl of soup, especially when it’s packed with delicious flavors and comforting ingredients. If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this Thai Basil Chicken Noodle Soup recipe. It’s a perfect blend of aromatic herbs, tender chicken, and slurpy noodles, all bathed in a flavorful broth.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves, roughly chopped
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth and water to a boil. Add the chicken slices and cook until they are no longer pink, about 4-5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the rice noodles and cook according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic, shallot, and red chili, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.

  4. Return the cooked chicken to the pot with the broth. Stir in the sautéed garlic, shallot, and chili mixture.

  5. Add the fish sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar to the pot. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  6. Bring the soup to a simmer and let it cook for another 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Just before serving, stir in the Thai basil leaves and green onions. Let them wilt slightly in the hot soup.

  8. Ladle the soup into bowls and serve hot with lime wedges on the side for an extra zing of citrusy freshness.

Enjoy the Flavors of Thailand

This Thai Basil Chicken Noodle Soup is a feast for the senses, with its enticing aroma, vibrant colors, and complex flavors. The combination of tender chicken, slurpy noodles, and fragrant Thai basil creates a harmonious balance that will transport you straight to the bustling streets of Bangkok.

The secret to this soup’s depth of flavor lies in the combination of fish sauce, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. These umami-rich ingredients add a savory and slightly sweet note to the broth, elevating it to a whole new level of deliciousness.

The addition of Thai basil leaves at the end adds a fresh and aromatic touch to the soup. The basil’s distinct flavor profile, with hints of anise and mint, complements the other ingredients perfectly, making each spoonful a burst of delightful flavors.

Whether you’re craving a comforting meal on a chilly evening or simply want to explore the vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine, this Thai Basil Chicken Noodle Soup is sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, gather your ingredients, don your apron, and embark on a culinary adventure that will leave you wanting more.
