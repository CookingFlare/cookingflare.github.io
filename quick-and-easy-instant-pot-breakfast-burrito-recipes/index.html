Quick and Easy Instant Pot Breakfast Burrito Recipes


Breakfast is often hailed as the most important meal of the day, yet in the hustle and bustle of daily life, it can be all too easy to skip it. This is where the Instant Pot comes to the rescue, providing a quick and easy solution for busy mornings. Breakfast burritos are not only a delicious way to start your day, but they are also incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize them with your favorite ingredients. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of quick and easy Instant Pot breakfast burrito recipes that you can whip up in no time, ensuring you fuel your body for the day ahead.

Why Use the Instant Pot for Breakfast Burritos?

The Instant Pot is a powerful kitchen tool that combines the functions of a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, and more, all in one appliance. Here are a few reasons why using an Instant Pot for breakfast burritos is a fantastic choice:

  1. Speed: The pressure cooking feature significantly reduces cooking time, allowing you to prepare your burritos in a matter of minutes.
  2. Flavor: The sealed environment of the Instant Pot locks in flavors, making your burritos tastier.
  3. Convenience: One-pot cooking means fewer dishes to clean up, making breakfast prep a breeze.
  4. Customization: You can easily adapt recipes to suit your dietary preferences or ingredient availability.

Now that we’ve established the benefits of using the Instant Pot, let’s dive into some delicious breakfast burrito recipes!

Classic Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup diced onions
  • 1 cup cooked and crumbled breakfast sausage or bacon
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Optional: salsa, avocado, or hot sauce for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Instant Pot: Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté mode. Add a splash of oil and sauté the diced onions and bell peppers until they are soft, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Mix the Eggs: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  3. Add Ingredients: Stir in the cooked breakfast sausage or bacon into the sautéed vegetables. Then, pour the egg mixture over the top and stir gently to combine.

  4. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set it to manual high pressure for 5 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then perform a quick release for any remaining pressure.

  5. Assemble the Burritos: Once the eggs are fully cooked, stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted. Spoon the egg mixture onto each flour tortilla, fold in the sides, and roll it up tightly.

  6. Serve: Enjoy your classic breakfast burrito with salsa, avocado, or your favorite hot sauce.

Veggie-Packed Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large whole wheat tortillas
  • Optional: cilantro and lime for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Veggies: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add a bit of oil and sauté the red onion until translucent, about 2-3 minutes. Then, add the chopped spinach and diced tomatoes, cooking until the spinach wilts.

  2. Whisk Eggs: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.

  3. Combine Ingredients: Pour the egg mixture over the cooked vegetables in the Instant Pot and add the black beans. Stir gently to combine.

  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set to manual high pressure for 5 minutes. After cooking, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes before a quick release.

  5. Add Cheese: Stir in the Monterey Jack cheese until melted and well combined.

  6. Wrap It Up: Spoon the mixture onto the whole wheat tortillas, fold, and roll them up. Garnish with cilantro and a squeeze of lime if desired.

Southwest Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup diced green bell peppers
  • 1 cup diced onions
  • 1 cup cooked chorizo sausage
  • 1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Optional: sour cream and jalapeños for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Chorizo: Use the sauté function on your Instant Pot to cook the chorizo until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.

  2. Cook the Vegetables: In the same pot, add the diced onions and bell peppers. Sauté until softened, about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Prepare the Eggs: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.

  4. Combine and Cook: Pour the egg mixture over the sautéed vegetables and chorizo in the Instant Pot. Close the lid and cook on manual high pressure for 5 minutes. Allow for a natural release for 5 minutes before a quick release.

  5. Melt the Cheese: Stir in the shredded pepper jack cheese until well combined and melted.

  6. Wrap and Serve: Fill the tortillas with the mixture, roll them up, and top with sour cream and jalapeños if desired.

Sweet Potato & Black Bean Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 large corn tortillas
  • Optional: avocado and salsa for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Sweet Potatoes: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a bit of oil. Add the diced sweet potatoes and sauté for about 5-7 minutes until they begin to soften.

  2. Add Beans: Stir in the black beans, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.

  3. Prepare the Egg Mixture: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until combined.

  4. Combine and Cook: Pour the egg mixture over the sweet potato and bean mixture in the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set to manual high pressure for 5 minutes. Allow a natural release for 5 minutes, then do a quick release.

  5. Add Cheese: Mix in the shredded cheddar cheese until it melts into the mixture.

  6. Assemble the Burritos: Spoon the filling into the corn tortillas, roll them up tightly, and serve with avocado and salsa.

Quick Tips for Making Breakfast Burritos

  1. Meal Prep: Prepare your fillings in advance and store them in the fridge. In the morning, all you need to do is assemble and heat your burrito.
  2. Freezing: Breakfast burritos freeze well. Wrap them individually in foil or plastic wrap, then store them in a freezer-safe bag. Heat them in the microwave or a toaster oven when you’re ready to enjoy.
  3. Customize: Feel free to swap out ingredients based on your preferences. Try adding different proteins, veggies, or cheeses for a unique twist each time.
  4. Spice It Up: If you love heat, add jalapeños, hot sauce, or spicy salsa to your burrito filling.

Conclusion

Breakfast burritos made in the Instant Pot are a game-changer for busy mornings. With just a few simple ingredients and a bit of creativity, you can whip up a delicious, hearty meal that’s packed with flavor and nutrition. Whether you prefer classic flavors, veggie-packed options, or something with a southwestern twist, there’s an Instant Pot breakfast burrito recipe for everyone. Embrace the convenience and start your day off right with these quick and easy burritos!
