Delicious Grilled Chicken Recipes for Your Picnic


Are you planning a picnic and looking for some delicious grilled chicken recipes to impress your friends and family? Look no further! Grilled chicken is a perfect addition to any picnic menu, offering a versatile and flavorful option that everyone will love. Whether you prefer zesty marinades, smoky barbecue flavors, or savory herb-infused chicken, these recipes are sure to elevate your picnic experience.

Tangy Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, thyme, and rosemary.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then place them in a resealable plastic bag.
  3. Pour the marinade over the chicken, seal the bag, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  6. Allow the chicken to rest for a few minutes before serving.

This tangy lemon herb grilled chicken is bursting with fresh flavors and is perfect for a sunny picnic day. The zesty combination of lemon, garlic, and aromatic herbs will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Smoky Barbecue Grilled Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the barbecue sauce, olive oil, smoked paprika, and garlic powder.
  2. Season the chicken chunks with salt and pepper, then thread them onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  3. Brush the chicken skewers with the barbecue sauce mixture, coating them evenly.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  5. Grill the chicken skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with the remaining barbecue sauce.

These smoky barbecue grilled chicken skewers are a crowd-pleaser, offering a perfect balance of sweet, smoky, and savory flavors. The succulent chicken thighs paired with the rich barbecue sauce create a mouthwatering combination that will be a hit at your picnic.

Honey Mustard Glazed Grilled Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, and garlic powder.
  2. Season the chicken drumsticks with salt and pepper, then brush them with the honey mustard glaze.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Grill the chicken drumsticks for 25-30 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with the remaining honey mustard glaze.

These honey mustard glazed grilled chicken drumsticks offer a delightful combination of sweet and tangy flavors that will have everyone reaching for seconds. The sticky glaze caramelizes beautifully on the grill, creating a finger-licking good experience for your picnic.

Conclusion

Grilled chicken is a versatile and delicious addition to any picnic menu, offering a wide range of flavors and cooking styles to suit every palate. Whether you prefer the zesty tang of lemon herb chicken, the smoky allure of barbecue skewers, or the sweet and savory appeal of honey mustard glazed drumsticks, these recipes are sure to elevate your outdoor dining experience. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and enjoy these delectable grilled chicken recipes at your next picnic!
