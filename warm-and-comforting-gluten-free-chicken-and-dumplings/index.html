Warm and Comforting: Gluten-Free Chicken and Dumplings


There’s something truly special about a warm, comforting bowl of chicken and dumplings. This classic dish is a favorite for many, but for those with gluten sensitivities, enjoying traditional chicken and dumplings can be a challenge. Fear not! In this recipe, we’re going to show you how to create a gluten-free version of this beloved comfort food that’s every bit as delicious and satisfying.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Stew:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Gluten-Free Dumplings:

  • 2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Chicken Stew

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion, carrots, and celery. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the chicken broth, thyme, rosemary, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  6. Let the stew simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

Step 2: Prepare the Gluten-Free Dumplings

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the gluten-free all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Pour in the milk and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

Step 3: Add the Dumplings to the Stew

  1. Once the chicken stew is ready, drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter on top of the simmering stew.
  2. Cover the pot with a lid and let the dumplings cook for 15 minutes. Avoid lifting the lid during this time to ensure the dumplings cook through.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once the dumplings are cooked through, ladle the chicken and dumplings into bowls.
  2. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Tips for Success

  • When making the gluten-free dumplings, it’s important not to overmix the batter. Overmixing can result in tough and dense dumplings.
  • Use a gluten-free all-purpose flour blend that’s specifically designed for baking to achieve the best texture for the dumplings.
  • If you prefer a thicker stew, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a little water and stir it into the simmering stew to thicken it before adding the dumplings.

Final Thoughts

This gluten-free chicken and dumplings recipe is a true crowd-pleaser, perfect for cozy family dinners or casual gatherings with friends. The warm, savory stew paired with pillowy soft dumplings is sure to bring a smile to everyone’s face, gluten-free or not. Give this recipe a try and savor the heartwarming flavors of this classic dish, reimagined for those with gluten sensitivities.
