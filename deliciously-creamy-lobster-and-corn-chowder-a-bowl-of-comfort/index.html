Deliciously Creamy Lobster and Corn Chowder: A Bowl of Comfort


There’s something undeniably comforting about a warm bowl of chowder. The creamy texture, the rich flavors, and the hearty ingredients all come together to create a dish that warms both the body and the soul. And when it comes to chowder, few variations can compare to the delectable combination of lobster and corn. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create a deliciously creamy lobster and corn chowder that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 2 lobsters (about 1 ½ pounds each)
  • 4 ears of corn, shucked and kernels removed
  • 4 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the lobsters. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil. Carefully place the lobsters into the boiling water and cook for about 8 minutes, or until they turn bright red. Remove the lobsters from the pot and set them aside to cool.

  2. Once the lobsters have cooled, remove the meat from the shells. Chop the meat into bite-sized pieces and set it aside.

  3. In a large soup pot, cook the bacon over medium heat until it becomes crispy. Remove the bacon from the pot and set it aside, leaving the bacon fat in the pot.

  4. Add the diced onion to the pot and sauté it in the bacon fat until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  5. Add the diced potatoes, corn kernels, and thyme to the pot. Stir everything together and cook for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.

  7. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree about half of the soup mixture until smooth. This will help thicken the chowder while still leaving some chunks of vegetables for texture.

  8. Stir in the heavy cream and butter, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to come together.

  9. Finally, add the chopped lobster meat to the pot and cook for a few minutes, just until the lobster is heated through.

  10. Ladle the chowder into bowls and garnish with crispy bacon bits and fresh parsley. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors of this creamy lobster and corn chowder.

Conclusion

Indulge in a bowl of deliciously creamy lobster and corn chowder for a truly comforting meal. The combination of tender lobster meat, sweet corn kernels, and creamy broth will warm your heart and satisfy your taste buds. This recipe is perfect for a cozy night in or for impressing guests with a taste of coastal comfort. So grab your pot and get cooking, because a bowl of this chowder is just a spoonful away from pure bliss.
