Pasta with Roasted Butternut Squash: A Sweet and Savory Combination


Are you tired of the same old pasta dishes? Looking for a way to elevate your weeknight dinner? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create a delicious pasta dish with a twist - Pasta with Roasted Butternut Squash. This sweet and savory combination will take your taste buds on a culinary adventure.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 8 ounces pasta of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. In a large bowl, toss the butternut squash cubes with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Make sure the squash is well coated with the seasonings.

  3. Spread the seasoned butternut squash cubes evenly on a baking sheet and roast in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the squash is tender and slightly caramelized. Make sure to toss the squash halfway through the cooking time to ensure even roasting.

  4. While the butternut squash is roasting, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  5. In a large skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the roasted butternut squash and cooked pasta to the skillet, tossing gently to combine.

  6. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and squash mixture, stirring until the cheese has melted and coated the pasta. Add salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with freshly chopped parsley.

  8. Serve hot and enjoy the delightful combination of sweet roasted butternut squash and savory pasta!

Tips and Variations

  • You can add additional ingredients to this dish to suit your taste. Try tossing in some cooked bacon or pancetta for an added smoky flavor. You can also sprinkle some toasted pine nuts or walnuts over the pasta for extra crunch.

  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can add a splash of heavy cream or a dollop of mascarpone cheese to the skillet before adding the Parmesan cheese. This will create a luscious and indulgent sauce that complements the roasted butternut squash perfectly.

  • This recipe works well with various types of pasta, such as penne, farfalle, or rigatoni. Feel free to experiment with different pasta shapes to find your favorite combination.

  • To make this dish vegan-friendly, simply omit the Parmesan cheese or substitute it with a plant-based alternative. You can also use gluten-free pasta to make it suitable for those with gluten sensitivities.

So there you have it - a simple yet flavorful pasta recipe that combines the natural sweetness of roasted butternut squash with the savory goodness of pasta. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress. Give it a try and let your taste buds rejoice in this delightful sweet and savory combination!

Remember, cooking is all about creativity, so feel free to customize this recipe to suit your preferences. Bon appétit!
