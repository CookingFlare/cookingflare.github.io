Quick and Easy Nut-Free Chicken Fajita Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner option? Look no further! This Quick and Easy Nut-Free Chicken Fajita Recipe is a crowd-pleaser that can be prepared in no time. Packed with flavors and bursting with colors, this recipe will satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 small flour tortillas

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the vegetables. Thinly slice the bell peppers and onion into long strips. Set aside.

  2. Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips. This will ensure that the chicken cooks quickly and absorbs all the flavors of the marinade.

  3. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.

  4. Add the chicken strips to the bowl and toss them in the marinade until they are well coated. Set aside for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the chicken is marinating, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the marinated chicken and cook for about 5-6 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  6. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and onion. Cook for about 4-5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  7. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables and toss everything together. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.

  8. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave according to the package instructions.

  9. Serve the chicken fajita mixture on warm tortillas and garnish with your favorite toppings such as sour cream, salsa, guacamole, or shredded cheese.

  10. Roll up the tortillas, tucking in the sides as you go, and enjoy your delicious nut-free chicken fajitas!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Quick and Easy: This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to prepare a flavorful meal without spending hours in the kitchen.
  • Nut-Free: If you or someone in your family has a nut allergy, you can rest assured knowing that this recipe is safe and delicious.
  • Versatile: Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. Add some jalapenos for an extra kick of spiciness or top your fajitas with fresh cilantro for added freshness.
  • Colorful and Healthy: The combination of vibrant bell peppers and lean chicken breast makes this dish not only visually appealing but also a nutritious option.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a quick and easy dinner, give this Nut-Free Chicken Fajita Recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and your family or guests will be impressed with your culinary skills. Enjoy the flavors, the colors, and the simplicity of this delicious meal!
