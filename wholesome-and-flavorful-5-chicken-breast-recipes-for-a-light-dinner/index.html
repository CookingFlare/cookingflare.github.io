Wholesome and Flavorful: 5 Chicken Breast Recipes for a Light Dinner


If you’re looking for a light and healthy dinner option, chicken breast is always a great choice. Not only is it lean and packed with protein, but it also serves as a blank canvas for a wide range of flavors. In this post, we’ll explore five wholesome and flavorful chicken breast recipes that are perfect for a light dinner. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 25 minutes
Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breast halves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, parsley, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in a ziplock bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Grill the chicken breasts for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  5. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve with a side of steamed vegetables or a fresh salad.

2. Balsamic Glazed Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Total Time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breast halves
  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, honey, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and pour the balsamic glaze over them. Make sure the chicken is well coated.
  4. In a separate bowl, toss the mixed vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Arrange the vegetables around the chicken in the baking dish.
  5. Roast the chicken and vegetables for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  6. Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

3. Greek Stuffed Chicken Breast

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 50 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breast halves
  • ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ¼ cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • ¼ cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F.
  2. In a bowl, combine the crumbled feta cheese, chopped Kalamata olives, sun-dried tomatoes, fresh basil, fresh parsley, salt, and pepper.
  3. Make a horizontal slit in each chicken breast to create a pocket. Be careful not to cut all the way through.
  4. Stuff each chicken breast pocket with the feta cheese mixture.
  5. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the stuffed chicken breasts for about 2-3 minutes on each side.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve with a side of Greek salad and tzatziki sauce.

4. Coconut Curry Chicken

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breast halves
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, carrots, and peas)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the curry powder to the skillet and stir for about a minute to toast the spices.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 5 minutes on each side, or until browned.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer and cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Stir in the mixed vegetables and cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Serve with steamed rice or naan bread.

5. Caprese Stuffed Chicken Breast

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Total Time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breast halves
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 4 slices tomato
  • ¼ cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F.
  2. Make a horizontal slit in each chicken breast to create a pocket. Be careful not to cut all the way through.
  3. Stuff each chicken breast pocket with a slice of mozzarella cheese, a slice of tomato, and a few fresh basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the stuffed chicken breasts for about 2-3 minutes on each side.
  5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted.
  6. Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve with a side of roasted potatoes or a fresh green salad.

Conclusion

There you have it – five wholesome and flavorful chicken breast recipes for a light dinner. Whether you’re grilling, roasting, or stuffing, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds while keeping your meal healthy and nutritious. So next time you’re in the mood for a light and delicious dinner, give one of these recipes a try. Happy cooking!
