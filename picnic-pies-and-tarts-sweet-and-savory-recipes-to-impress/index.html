Picnic Pies and Tarts: Sweet and Savory Recipes to Impress


Are you looking to elevate your picnic game with delicious and impressive pies and tarts? Look no further! We’ve got you covered with a variety of sweet and savory recipes that are perfect for any outdoor gathering. Whether you’re planning a romantic picnic for two or a fun family outing, these recipes are sure to wow your companions. So pack your picnic basket, grab a blanket, and get ready to indulge in some delectable treats!

Sweet Strawberry Galette

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 3 tablespoons ice water
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Powdered sugar, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, granulated sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter and blend until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water and mix until the dough comes together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and chill for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. In a bowl, toss the strawberries with cornstarch and brown sugar.
  5. Roll out the dough into a circle on a floured surface. Transfer to a baking sheet.
  6. Arrange the strawberry mixture in the center of the dough, leaving a border around the edges. Fold the edges of the dough over the filling.
  7. Brush the dough with the beaten egg wash.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  9. Let it cool slightly, dust with powdered sugar, and serve.

This sweet and tangy strawberry galette is a perfect picnic dessert that will impress your guests with its rustic charm and delightful flavors.

Savory Spinach and Feta Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 package frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package phyllo dough
  • ½ cup melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a skillet, sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil until softened.
  3. Add the spinach, feta cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Cook until well combined.
  4. Layer 5 sheets of phyllo dough, brushing each sheet with melted butter.
  5. Spread the spinach mixture over the phyllo dough.
  6. Fold in the edges of the dough and roll it up.
  7. Brush the top with butter and score the top with a knife.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.

This savory spinach and feta pie is a flavorful and satisfying addition to your picnic spread. The crispy phyllo dough complements the creamy spinach and cheese filling beautifully.

Conclusion

Whether you have a sweet tooth or prefer savory delights, these picnic pies and tarts are guaranteed to impress. From the fruity goodness of the strawberry galette to the savory richness of the spinach and feta pie, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So next time you head out for a picnic, be sure to bring along these delectable treats for a memorable dining experience in the great outdoors. Happy picnicking!
