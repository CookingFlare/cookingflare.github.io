Savor the Aromas of Spain: Spanish Chicken with Peppers Recipe


Are you ready to embark on a culinary journey to Spain? Get ready to savor the vibrant flavors and enticing aromas of Spanish cuisine with this mouthwatering Spanish Chicken with Peppers recipe. From the moment you start cooking, your kitchen will be filled with the delightful scents of garlic, paprika, and bell peppers. This traditional Spanish dish is a true celebration of the rich culinary heritage of Spain.

Ingredients

To make this Spanish Chicken with Peppers recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 red bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 2 yellow bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Start by heating the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken thighs to the skillet and cook for about 5 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the sliced onion and minced garlic. Sauté them for a few minutes until they become fragrant and start to soften.

  3. Next, add the sliced bell peppers to the skillet. Stir them well with the onion and garlic, and cook for about 5 minutes, or until the peppers are slightly tender.

  4. Now it’s time to add the smoked paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir the spices into the pepper mixture until everything is well combined.

  5. Return the chicken thighs to the skillet, nestling them into the pepper mixture. Pour in the chicken broth and white wine, and bring the mixture to a simmer.

  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let the chicken simmer for about 30 minutes, or until it is cooked through and tender. Make sure to stir occasionally to prevent sticking and ensure even cooking.

  7. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the skillet and transfer it to a serving plate. Spoon the peppers and sauce over the chicken thighs, and garnish with freshly chopped parsley.

  8. Serve the Spanish Chicken with Peppers alongside some crusty bread or steamed rice to soak up the delicious sauce. You can also pair it with a crisp green salad for a refreshing contrast.

Enjoy the Flavors of Spain

Congratulations! You have successfully prepared a delectable Spanish Chicken with Peppers dish that will transport you to the sun-soaked streets of Spain. The tender chicken, combined with the sweet and tangy bell peppers, infused with the smoky flavors of paprika and oregano, creates a harmonious symphony of tastes that will tantalize your taste buds.

Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this Spanish Chicken with Peppers recipe is sure to impress. So gather your loved ones, uncork a bottle of Spanish wine, and savor the aromas and flavors of Spain right in the comfort of your own home.

¡Buen provecho! (Enjoy your meal!)
