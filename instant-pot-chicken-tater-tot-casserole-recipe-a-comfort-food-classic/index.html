Instant Pot Chicken Tater Tot Casserole Recipe: A Comfort Food Classic


  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the diced chicken and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and set aside.

  2. In the same Instant Pot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 2 minutes.

  3. Add the dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper to the Instant Pot. Stir well to combine with the onion and garlic.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the cream of chicken soup, sour cream, and milk until smooth. Pour the mixture into the Instant Pot and stir to combine with the onion and herb mixture.

  5. Return the cooked chicken to the Instant Pot and add the frozen mixed vegetables. Stir well to coat everything with the soup mixture.

  6. Place the frozen tater tots on top of the chicken and vegetable mixture in an even layer. Do not stir.

  7. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.

  8. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully release the pressure using the quick release method.

  9. Open the Instant Pot and sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top of the tater tots. Close the lid and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the cheese to melt.

  10. Remove the lid and serve the Instant Pot Chicken Tater Tot Casserole hot. Enjoy!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

The Instant Pot Chicken Tater Tot Casserole is the ultimate comfort food classic that combines tender chicken, flavorful vegetables, and crispy tater tots. Here’s why you’ll love this recipe:

  1. Quick and Easy: With the help of your Instant Pot, this recipe comes together in under an hour, making it perfect for busy weeknights when you want a hearty and delicious meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

  2. Versatile: This casserole is incredibly versatile. Feel free to customize it by adding your favorite vegetables or swapping out the chicken for ground beef or turkey. The possibilities are endless!

  3. Family-Friendly: Kids and adults alike will love this comforting dish. The combination of crispy tater tots, creamy sauce, and savory chicken appeals to all taste buds.

  4. One-Pot Wonder: The Instant Pot allows you to cook the chicken, vegetables, and tater tots all in one pot, minimizing cleanup and making it a convenient option for any meal.

So, what are you waiting for? Grab your Instant Pot and whip up this delicious Chicken Tater Tot Casserole for a comforting and satisfying meal that will surely become a family favorite. Enjoy!
