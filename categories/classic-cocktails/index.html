Ingredients To create the perfect Boulevardier, you will need the following ingredients: 2 ounces bourbon or rye whiskey 1 ounce sweet vermouth 1 ounce Campari Orange peel, for garnish Instructions Fill a mixing glass with ice. Add the bourbon or rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, and Campari. Stir gently for about 30 seconds to combine the ingredients and chill the drink. Strain the mixture into a chilled glass filled with fresh ice. Garnish with a twist of orange peel for an extra touch of flavor. The History of the Boulevardier The Boulevardier cocktail has an intriguing history that dates back to the 1920s.
