Savory and Cheesy Garlic Knots: A Garlic Lover's Dream


If you’re a fan of garlic, cheese, and all things savory, then you’re in for a treat. These savory and cheesy garlic knots are a garlic lover’s dream come true. They’re perfect as a side dish for pasta, a delicious appetizer, or even as a snack on their own. The combination of fragrant garlic, gooey cheese, and soft, fluffy bread makes these knots a real crowd-pleaser. So, let’s dive into the recipe and make some mouthwatering garlic knots!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Olive oil, for brushing
  • All-purpose flour, for dusting

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Dough

Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). While the oven is heating up, prepare a baking sheet by lining it with parchment paper and set it aside. Dust a clean surface with some all-purpose flour and roll out the pizza dough into a large rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.

Step 2: Making the Garlic Butter Mixture

In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

Step 3: Assembling the Knots

Using a pizza cutter, slice the rolled-out dough into long strips, about 1 inch wide. Take each strip and tie it into a knot, tucking the ends underneath. Place the knots onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each one.

Step 4: Adding the Cheese and Garlic Butter

Brush each knot with the garlic butter mixture, making sure to coat each one generously. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the knots, allowing some to fall onto the baking sheet for crispy, cheesy edges.

Step 5: Baking the Knots

Once all the knots are assembled and coated, drizzle a little olive oil over the top for a golden finish. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the knots for 15-18 minutes, or until they are golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Step 6: Garnishing and Serving

Once out of the oven, sprinkle the freshly chopped parsley over the hot knots for a burst of color and freshness. Let the knots cool for a few minutes before serving them warm.

Final Thoughts

These savory and cheesy garlic knots are the perfect way to satisfy your garlic cravings. Whether you’re serving them alongside a hearty pasta dish or as a standalone appetizer, they are sure to be a hit. The combination of fragrant garlic, gooey cheese, and soft, fluffy bread makes these knots irresistible. So, the next time you’re in the mood for something savory and comforting, give these garlic knots a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

There you have it - a simple yet delicious recipe for savory and cheesy garlic knots. Give it a try and let us know how it turns out in the comments below. Happy cooking!
