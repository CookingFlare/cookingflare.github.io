Creamy Italian Tiramisu Cheesecake Recipe - Decadent Dessert Fusion


If you’re a fan of the classic Italian Tiramisu and the rich, creamy indulgence of cheesecake, then get ready to experience the best of both worlds with this exquisite Creamy Italian Tiramisu Cheesecake recipe. This decadent dessert fusion combines the bold flavors of espresso, cocoa, and mascarpone with the velvety smoothness of cream cheese, resulting in a dessert that’s truly a showstopper.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of chocolate cookie crumbs
  • ⅓ cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar

For the Filling:

  • 24 ounces of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • ½ cup of mascarpone cheese
  • 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of instant espresso powder
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon of coffee liqueur (optional)

For the Tiramisu Layer:

  • 1 ½ cups of strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 3 tablespoons of coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon of unsweetened cocoa powder

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • ½ teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
    • In a medium bowl, combine the chocolate cookie crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then set aside to cool.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
    • In a separate bowl, mix the sour cream, mascarpone cheese, flour, espresso powder, cocoa powder, and coffee liqueur (if using) until well combined. Gently fold this mixture into the cream cheese mixture until smooth.

  3. Assemble the Tiramisu Layer:

    • In a shallow dish, combine the brewed coffee and coffee liqueur (if using). Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture for a few seconds, then arrange them over the cooled crust in the springform pan.

  4. Layer the Cheesecake:

    • Pour half of the cream cheese filling over the ladyfingers. Smooth the top with a spatula. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining cream cheese filling.

  5. Bake and Chill:

    • Place the cheesecake in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the center is almost set. Let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door ajar for 1 hour, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.

  6. Whip the Topping:

    • In a medium bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled cheesecake.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Just before serving, dust the top of the cheesecake with cocoa powder. Slice and savor every luscious bite of this Creamy Italian Tiramisu Cheesecake.

Final Thoughts

This Creamy Italian Tiramisu Cheesecake is a delightful fusion of two beloved desserts, combining the best elements of Tiramisu and cheesecake into one heavenly treat. With its rich flavors and creamy texture, this decadent dessert is sure to impress your family and friends at any gathering or special occasion. So, indulge your senses and treat yourself to the luxurious experience of this remarkable dessert fusion.
