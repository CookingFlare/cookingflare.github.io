Delicious Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes: A Fall Favorite


When the leaves start to turn and there’s a chill in the air, it’s time to embrace all things cozy and comforting. And what better way to do that than with a delicious treat that captures the essence of fall? Enter the Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes – a delightful fusion of the beloved pumpkin spice flavor and the rich, aromatic notes of a latte. These cupcakes are the perfect way to indulge in the flavors of the season, whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a sweet treat.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons espresso or strongly brewed coffee

For the Frosting:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon instant espresso powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions

Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the pumpkin puree.
  4. In a small bowl, combine the milk and espresso or coffee.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk mixture, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Frosting:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter together until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add the powdered sugar, vanilla extract, espresso powder, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Beat until smooth and well combined.
  3. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them with the prepared frosting.

Serving and Enjoyment

These Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes are a delightful treat that can be enjoyed with a cup of coffee or tea, making them the perfect addition to any fall gathering or as a comforting indulgence on a chilly evening. The warm spices and the creamy frosting come together to create a flavor sensation that is sure to become a seasonal favorite. So, go ahead and embrace the flavors of fall with these delectable cupcakes!

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, these cupcakes are a must-try. They capture the essence of the season in every bite, and their irresistible aroma will fill your home with the comforting scents of fall. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the cozy goodness of these Delicious Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes.
