BBQ Meatball Subs: Grilled Sandwich Sensations


If you’re a fan of barbecue and crave the ultimate comfort food, then you’re in for a treat! These Grilled BBQ Meatball Subs are a flavor sensation that will leave your taste buds dancing with delight. Perfect for a summer cookout or a cozy night in, this recipe combines the smokiness of barbecue sauce with the savory goodness of meatballs, all nestled in a warm, toasted sub roll. Let’s dive into the mouthwatering details of this grilled sandwich sensation!

Ingredients:

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional:

  • 4 sub rolls
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Meatballs

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, minced garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter.

Step 2: Make the BBQ Sauce

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly, about 10 minutes.

Step 3: Grill the Meatballs

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the meatballs on the grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are browned and cooked through.
  3. Brush the meatballs with the BBQ sauce during the last few minutes of grilling, allowing the sauce to caramelize on the meatballs.

Step 4: Assemble the Subs

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Split the sub rolls lengthwise and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Place a layer of mozzarella cheese on the bottom of each sub roll.
  4. Arrange the grilled meatballs on top of the cheese, then drizzle with additional BBQ sauce.
  5. Sprinkle more mozzarella cheese on top and cover the subs with the top halves of the rolls.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the rolls are toasted.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy!

  1. Remove the subs from the oven and sprinkle with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.
  2. Serve these Grilled BBQ Meatball Subs hot and watch them disappear in no time!

Conclusion:

These Grilled BBQ Meatball Subs are a delightful twist on the classic meatball sub, elevating it to a whole new level of deliciousness. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a hearty meal, this recipe is sure to be a hit. The smoky, tangy flavor of the BBQ sauce paired with the juicy meatballs and gooey cheese creates an irresistible combination that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, fire up the grill and get ready to savor every mouthful of these Grilled BBQ Meatball Subs!
