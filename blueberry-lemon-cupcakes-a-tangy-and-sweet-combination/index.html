Blueberry Lemon Cupcakes: A Tangy and Sweet Combination


There’s something magical about the combination of blueberry and lemon. The tangy zest of lemon perfectly complements the sweet burst of blueberries, creating a flavor pairing that is simply irresistible. And what better way to enjoy this delightful duo than in the form of cupcakes? These Blueberry Lemon Cupcakes are the perfect treat for any occasion, whether it’s a birthday party, a bridal shower, or simply a weekend indulgence.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing just until combined.
  5. Gently fold in the lemon zest and blueberries, being careful not to overmix.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  8. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Lemon Cream Cheese Frosting

No cupcake is complete without a delicious frosting, and these Blueberry Lemon Cupcakes are no exception. The tangy lemon cream cheese frosting adds an extra layer of flavor that perfectly complements the cupcakes.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, beat together the cream cheese and butter until creamy and smooth.
  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, beating well after each addition.
  3. Stir in the lemon juice and lemon zest, mixing until well combined.
  4. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, frost them with the lemon cream cheese frosting using a piping bag or a spatula.
  5. For an extra touch, garnish each cupcake with a sprinkle of lemon zest.

Serving and Storage

These Blueberry Lemon Cupcakes are best enjoyed fresh, but they can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Just make sure to bring them to room temperature before serving to allow the flavors to fully develop.

So, the next time you’re craving a tangy and sweet treat, give these Blueberry Lemon Cupcakes a try. They are sure to brighten up your day and leave you wanting more. Happy baking!
