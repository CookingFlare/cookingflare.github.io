Delicious Pecan Praline Cheesecake: A Nutty Holiday Dessert


When the holiday season rolls around, it’s time to bring out the big guns in the dessert department. And what better way to impress your family and friends than with a show-stopping Pecan Praline Cheesecake? This decadent dessert combines the richness of cheesecake with the nutty goodness of pecan praline, creating a flavor explosion that will leave everyone craving for more. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the steps to create this mouthwatering masterpiece that’s perfect for any holiday gathering.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

For the Pecan Praline Topping:

  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated with the butter.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, creating an even layer.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

Step 2: Making the Cheesecake Filling

  1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract and sour cream until fully incorporated.
  4. Pour the cheesecake filling over the cooled crust, spreading it into an even layer.

Step 3: Creating the Pecan Praline Topping

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the brown sugar, heavy cream, and butter. Stir until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture is smooth.
  2. Add the chopped pecans and continue to cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and the pecans are coated, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Remove the mixture from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the pecan praline topping over the cheesecake filling, spreading it evenly.

Step 4: Baking and Chilling

  1. Place the springform pan on a baking sheet and bake the cheesecake in the preheated oven for 55-60 minutes, or until the center is set.
  2. Once done, turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour.
  3. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and run a knife around the edge of the pan to loosen the cheesecake from the sides.
  4. Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to set.

Serving

Before serving, carefully remove the sides of the springform pan and transfer the cheesecake to a serving plate. Slice and serve the pecan praline cheesecake, and watch as your guests delight in every nutty, creamy bite. This dessert is the perfect finale to any holiday meal, leaving a lasting impression on all who indulge in its heavenly flavors.

So, this holiday season, treat your loved ones to the indulgence of a Pecan Praline Cheesecake. Its rich, creamy texture and the irresistible crunch of pecan praline will make it an instant favorite at your holiday gatherings. Enjoy the joy of creating and savoring this delightful dessert that embodies the spirit of the holiday season. Cheers to happy holidays and unforgettable desserts!
