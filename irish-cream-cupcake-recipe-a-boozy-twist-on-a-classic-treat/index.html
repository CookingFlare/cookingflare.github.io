Irish Cream Cupcake Recipe: A Boozy Twist on a Classic Treat


If you’re a fan of cupcakes and love a good boozy twist, then these Irish Cream Cupcakes are sure to become your new favorite treat! With a rich and moist chocolate base, topped with a creamy Irish Cream frosting, these cupcakes are the perfect indulgence for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup Irish Cream liqueur

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate small bowl, dissolve the cocoa powder in boiling water, stirring until smooth. Set aside to cool slightly.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

  5. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  6. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, alternating with the buttermilk, beginning and ending with the flour mixture. Mix until just combined.

  7. Stir in the cooled cocoa mixture and Irish Cream liqueur until well incorporated.

  8. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  10. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

Irish Cream Frosting

No Irish Cream cupcake would be complete without a luscious and creamy Irish Cream frosting to top it off. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup Irish Cream liqueur
  • Green food coloring (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream the softened butter until smooth and fluffy.

  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, mixing well after each addition.

  3. Stir in the Irish Cream liqueur until the frosting reaches your desired consistency.

  4. If desired, add a few drops of green food coloring to give your frosting a festive touch.

  5. Once the cupcakes have cooled, frost them generously with the Irish Cream frosting.

Serving Suggestions

These Irish Cream cupcakes are delicious on their own, but if you want to take them to the next level, here are a few serving suggestions:

  • Sprinkle some chocolate shavings or cocoa powder on top of the frosting for an extra touch of decadence.
  • Serve alongside a cup of hot coffee or a glass of cold milk to balance out the richness of the cupcakes.
  • For a festive St. Patrick’s Day treat, garnish each cupcake with a shamrock-shaped cookie or a sprinkle of green sugar.

Conclusion

These Irish Cream Cupcakes are a delightful twist on a classic treat. The combination of rich chocolate and creamy Irish Cream creates a flavor explosion that is sure to impress. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in a sweet treat, these cupcakes are a must-try. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to bake up a batch of these boozy delights!
