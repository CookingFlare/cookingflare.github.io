Mouthwatering Low Carb Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and are looking for a delicious low-carb dish to add to your recipe repertoire, then this Mouthwatering Low Carb Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry is the perfect choice. Packed with flavor, tender beef, and aromatic Thai basil, this dish is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. The best part? It’s quick and easy to make, making it an ideal option for weeknight dinners or whenever you’re craving a taste of Thailand.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar substitute (such as erythritol or stevia)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 red chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the thinly sliced flank steak with soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar substitute. Allow it to marinate for at least 15 minutes.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over high heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped red chilies, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  3. Add the marinated beef to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until it’s cooked to your liking. Remove the beef from the pan and set it aside.

  4. In the same pan, add the sliced bell pepper and onion. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they begin to soften but still have a slight crunch.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the pan and toss everything together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Turn off the heat and stir in the Thai basil leaves until they wilt slightly from the residual heat.

  7. Serve the Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry immediately, garnished with extra Thai basil leaves if desired.

Why This Recipe Works

This Mouthwatering Low Carb Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry is a winning combination of tender beef, vibrant vegetables, and the unmistakable aroma of Thai basil. The key to the dish’s success lies in the balance of flavors and textures. The savory umami from the soy and oyster sauces, the subtle sweetness from the sugar substitute, and the heat from the red chilies create a symphony of tastes that will tantalize your taste buds.

Furthermore, the use of flank steak ensures that the beef is tender and cooks quickly, making this dish not only flavorful but also convenient for busy days. The addition of colorful bell peppers and onions not only adds crunch and sweetness but also provides a visual feast.

Health Benefits

In addition to its incredible flavor, this Low Carb Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry is a great option for those looking to reduce their carbohydrate intake. By omitting traditional starches like rice or noodles, this dish offers a lower-carb alternative without compromising on taste. The inclusion of lean flank steak and an array of vegetables makes it a well-rounded, nutritious choice for anyone following a low-carb or ketogenic lifestyle.

Conclusion

With its bold flavors, satisfying textures, and low-carb appeal, this Mouthwatering Low Carb Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry is a must-try for anyone seeking a taste of Thailand without the guilt. Whether you’re a fan of Thai cuisine or simply looking for a new and exciting low-carb recipe, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. So, grab your wok and get ready to experience the magic of Thai flavors right in your own home!
