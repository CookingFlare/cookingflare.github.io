Mouthwatering Keto Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Chicken Recipe


Are you tired of the same old boring chicken recipes? Look no further! Today, we’re going to dive into a mouthwatering keto creamy spinach and artichoke chicken recipe that’s sure to become a new favorite. This dish is a creative spin on the classic spinach and artichoke dip, but instead of serving it as a dip, we’re going to use it as a creamy sauce for our chicken.

The Perfect Combination

Chicken and spinach are a match made in heaven, but add in some artichoke hearts and a rich and creamy sauce, and you’ve got a dish that’s truly special. This recipe is not only delicious, but it’s also keto-friendly and packed with nutrients. The chicken provides lean protein, while the spinach and artichoke hearts add fiber and antioxidants.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, melt 1 tablespoon of butter over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes per side. Transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped artichoke hearts and cook for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Add the fresh spinach leaves to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 2-3 minutes.
  6. In a small saucepan, combine the heavy cream and grated cheddar cheese. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat and cook until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  7. Add the cooked spinach and artichoke mixture to the saucepan and stir to combine.
  8. Season the sauce with salt, pepper, and lemon zest.
  9. Add the cooked chicken breasts back to the skillet and spoon some of the creamy sauce over the top of each breast.
  10. Transfer the chicken to a baking dish and top with additional sauce and chopped parsley.
  11. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes until the sauce is bubbly and the chicken is heated through.
  12. Remove the chicken from the oven and sprinkle with chopped basil.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe more substantial, serve with a side of roasted vegetables or a salad.
  • If you prefer a lighter sauce, you can reduce the amount of heavy cream or substitute it with Greek yogurt or cottage cheese.
  • Add some diced bell peppers or onions to the skillet with the artichoke hearts for added flavor and texture.
  • Use different types of cheese, such as Parmesan or feta, for a unique flavor profile.

Conclusion

This mouthwatering keto creamy spinach and artichoke chicken recipe is a game-changer for anyone looking for a delicious and healthy dinner option. The combination of chicken, spinach, and artichoke hearts is a match made in heaven, and the creamy sauce adds a rich and indulgent touch. Whether you’re following a keto diet or just looking for a new recipe to try, this dish is sure to become a new favorite. So go ahead and give it a try – your taste buds will thank you!
