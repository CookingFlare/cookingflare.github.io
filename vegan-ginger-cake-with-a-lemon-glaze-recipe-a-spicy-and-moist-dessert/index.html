Vegan Ginger Cake with a Lemon Glaze Recipe: A Spicy and Moist Dessert


If you’re a fan of ginger and love indulging in delicious desserts, then you’re in for a treat! This Vegan Ginger Cake with a Lemon Glaze is the perfect combination of spicy and sweet, and it’s guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. The best part? It’s completely plant-based, making it a great option for vegans and those with dietary restrictions.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the lemon glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, ground ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the melted coconut oil, almond milk, brown sugar, molasses, freshly grated ginger, and vanilla extract. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  6. While the cake is baking, prepare the lemon glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and lemon zest until smooth and well combined.

  7. Once the cake is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer the cake to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Once the cake has cooled, drizzle the lemon glaze over the top, allowing it to drip down the sides. You can use a spoon or a piping bag for more precise drizzling.

  9. Allow the glaze to set for a few minutes before serving. Slice the cake and enjoy!

Serving Suggestions

This Vegan Ginger Cake with a Lemon Glaze is delicious on its own, but you can elevate it even further with some additional toppings. Serve each slice with a dollop of vegan whipped cream or a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream. You can also garnish with a sprinkle of freshly grated ginger or a dusting of powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness.

Final Thoughts

With its bold and spicy flavor, this Vegan Ginger Cake with a Lemon Glaze is sure to impress both vegans and non-vegans alike. The moist and tender texture, combined with the tangy lemon glaze, creates a harmonious balance of flavors that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to a delightful dessert, this recipe is a must-try. So go ahead and indulge in this irresistible vegan delight!
