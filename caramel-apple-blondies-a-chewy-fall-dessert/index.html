Caramel Apple Blondies: A Chewy Fall Dessert


There’s something magical about the arrival of fall - the crisp air, the vibrant colors, and of course, the delicious flavors that come along with it. One dessert that perfectly captures the essence of this season is the Caramel Apple Blondie. These chewy, gooey treats are a delightful combination of sweet caramel, tart apples, and warm spices. Whether you’re hosting a cozy gathering or simply craving a comforting treat, these blondies are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 medium apples, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1 cup caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and brown sugar until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  6. Gently fold in the diced apples, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  7. Pour half of the batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it out evenly.

  8. Drizzle half of the caramel sauce over the batter, using a knife or spatula to swirl it into the batter.

  9. Repeat with the remaining batter and caramel sauce, creating a marbled effect.

  10. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  11. Allow the blondies to cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares.

  12. Serve and enjoy! These blondies are best enjoyed warm, with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent treat, sprinkle some chopped pecans or walnuts on top of the caramel sauce before baking.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of apples, such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp, to add unique flavors and textures to your blondies.

  • If you prefer a softer texture, reduce the baking time by a few minutes. Conversely, if you prefer a firmer blondie, bake them for a few extra minutes.

  • These blondies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. To retain their freshness, you can also freeze them for up to 2 months.

Conclusion

Caramel Apple Blondies are the ultimate fall dessert, combining the classic flavors of caramel and apple with a chewy and irresistible blondie base. With their warm and comforting taste, these treats are a perfect way to celebrate the arrival of autumn. Whether you’re enjoying them on a cozy evening at home or sharing them with loved ones, these blondies are sure to become a fall favorite. So, grab your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a slice of caramel apple goodness. Happy baking!
