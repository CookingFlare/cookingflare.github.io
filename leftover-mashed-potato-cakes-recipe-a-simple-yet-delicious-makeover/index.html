Leftover Mashed Potato Cakes Recipe: A Simple yet Delicious Makeover


We all love mashed potatoes, don’t we? But sometimes, we end up with a little too much of it after a big family meal, and it sits in the fridge, waiting to be forgotten. Well, not anymore! Today, I’m going to show you how to turn those leftover mashed potatoes into a mouthwatering dish that will have everyone asking for seconds. Introducing the Leftover Mashed Potato Cakes - a simple yet delicious makeover for your neglected mashed potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups leftover mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Mixture: In a large mixing bowl, combine the leftover mashed potatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, chopped green onions, all-purpose flour, beaten egg, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  2. Form the Potato Cakes: Take a small handful of the potato mixture and shape it into a patty. Repeat this process until all the mixture has been used, making approximately 8 potato cakes.

  3. Coat with Breadcrumbs: Place the breadcrumbs on a plate and gently coat each potato cake with breadcrumbs, ensuring an even coating on all sides.

  4. Pan-Fry the Cakes: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the potato cakes in the skillet. Cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the potato cakes are golden and crispy, remove them from the skillet and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb any excess oil. Serve the potato cakes hot with a dollop of sour cream or a side of your favorite dipping sauce.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a cheesy twist, add some extra shredded cheese in the middle of each potato cake before shaping them.
  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can add in some chopped cooked bacon or diced ham to the potato mixture for an extra flavor boost.
  • These potato cakes can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for a quick and easy snack or side dish.

Leftover mashed potatoes will never be the same once you’ve tried this simple yet delicious makeover. These potato cakes are crispy on the outside, creamy on the inside, and packed with savory flavors that will leave everyone at the table wanting more. So, the next time you find yourself with leftover mashed potatoes, remember this recipe and give those potatoes the second chance they deserve!

Now, go ahead and give this Leftover Mashed Potato Cakes recipe a try, and let your taste buds thank you for the wonderful transformation!
