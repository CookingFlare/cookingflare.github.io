Decadent Christmas Cheesesteaks: Gourmet Sandwiches for a Festive Feast


‘Tis the season to indulge in delicious, comforting food, and what better way to celebrate than with Decadent Christmas Cheesesteaks? These gourmet sandwiches are a perfect addition to your festive feast, combining the rich flavors of tender steak, gooey cheese, and festive ingredients. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply want to treat yourself to something special, these cheesesteaks are sure to impress and delight.

Ingredients

For the Steak:

  • 1.5 pounds of ribeye steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder

For the Onions and Peppers:

  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 bell peppers (red and green), thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Festive Touch:

  • 1 cup of cranberry sauce
  • 8 ounces of brie cheese, sliced
  • 4 crusty hoagie rolls
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the steak. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Season the thinly sliced ribeye steak with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Add the steak to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. Once cooked, remove the steak from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. Using the same skillet, add the butter and sauté the onions and peppers over medium heat until they are caramelized and tender, about 10-15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Split the hoagie rolls and place them on a baking sheet. Toast the rolls in the oven for 5-7 minutes, or until they are slightly crispy.

  4. While the rolls are toasting, assemble the cheesesteaks. Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise on one side of each roll, and a thin layer of Dijon mustard on the other side. Place a generous portion of the cooked steak on the bottom half of each roll, followed by a scoop of the caramelized onions and peppers. Top with a few slices of brie cheese.

  5. Return the assembled cheesesteaks to the oven and bake for 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Once the cheesesteaks are out of the oven, spread a dollop of cranberry sauce on top of the melted cheese to add a festive touch.

  7. Serve the Decadent Christmas Cheesesteaks warm, alongside your favorite holiday sides and a glass of mulled wine for the perfect festive feast.

Conclusion

These Decadent Christmas Cheesesteaks are a delightful twist on the classic sandwich, combining the indulgent flavors of steak, gooey brie cheese, and sweet cranberry sauce. Whether you’re celebrating with loved ones or simply want to savor a special meal, these gourmet sandwiches are a perfect addition to your holiday menu. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the festive spirit, and treat yourself to the joy of indulgent holiday dining with these Decadent Christmas Cheesesteaks. Cheers to a delicious and delightful holiday season!
