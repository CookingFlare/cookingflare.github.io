10 Spanish Dishes to Make for a Romantic Dinner


Planning a romantic dinner and want to impress your partner with some delectable, authentic Spanish cuisine? Spanish food, with its vibrant flavors, rich history, and regional variety, is perfect for creating an unforgettable culinary experience. From tapas to hearty main dishes, Spain offers a fantastic range of recipes that can elevate any romantic meal. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply want to enjoy a cozy evening together, these 10 Spanish dishes will set the mood for a perfect night in.

1. Patatas Bravas (Fried Potatoes with Spicy Tomato Sauce)

No Spanish dinner would be complete without patatas bravas, a quintessential Spanish tapas dish. Crispy fried potatoes are served with a spicy tomato sauce, and they make for an excellent starter or side dish to set the tone for your meal. The sauce is usually made with tomatoes, garlic, onions, and a hint of cayenne pepper to add some heat. It’s a dish that is both simple and full of flavor, allowing you to dive straight into the heart of Spanish comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Fry the potato cubes until golden and crispy, around 15-20 minutes.
  3. In a separate pan, heat a tablespoon of olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato sauce, paprika, cayenne pepper, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 5 minutes.
  5. Pour the sauce over the fried potatoes and garnish with fresh parsley.

This easy-to-make dish will undoubtedly add a touch of Spanish charm to your romantic evening.

2. Tortilla Española (Spanish Omelette)

Tortilla Española is a classic Spanish dish made from eggs, potatoes, and onions. This dish is often served at room temperature, making it perfect for preparing ahead of time for your romantic dinner. The simplicity of the ingredients allows the flavors to meld beautifully, creating a rich and savory experience. The tender, slightly caramelized onions paired with the fluffy potatoes and eggs make this a heartwarming dish to share with your partner.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Add the onions and potatoes, cooking until soft, about 15 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Once the potatoes and onions are tender, drain excess oil and let the mixture cool slightly.
  4. Add the potato and onion mixture to the beaten eggs and stir gently.
  5. Pour everything back into the frying pan and cook on low heat, occasionally swirling the pan to ensure the eggs set evenly.
  6. Once the bottom is golden, flip the tortilla using a plate and cook on the other side for another 5-7 minutes.

Tortilla Española pairs wonderfully with a glass of Spanish red wine or sangria.

3. Gambas al Ajillo (Garlic Shrimp)

This dish is ideal for seafood lovers. Gambas al Ajillo is a sizzling Spanish shrimp dish infused with garlic, olive oil, and a touch of chili. It’s quick to prepare and can add a bit of drama to your romantic dinner when served sizzling hot straight from the pan. The bold flavors and aromatic garlic create an irresistible combination that will have both of you craving more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes, sautéing until the garlic is golden but not burnt.
  3. Add the shrimp to the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. Season with salt and garnish with fresh parsley.
  5. Serve immediately with some crusty bread to soak up the delicious garlic-infused oil.

This dish is perfect for sharing, and its bold flavor will definitely impress your partner.

4. Paella Valenciana (Traditional Valencian Paella)

Paella is perhaps the most famous Spanish dish worldwide, and for good reason. Originating from Valencia, this hearty one-pan rice dish is made with a combination of saffron, chicken, rabbit, and a variety of vegetables. The flavors meld together beautifully as the rice absorbs all the delicious juices from the meat and vegetables. Paella Valenciana can be an impressive and satisfying main course for your romantic dinner, offering a taste of Spanish tradition.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 lb rabbit (optional), cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups short-grain rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1/2 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Instructions:

  1. In a large paella pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Brown the chicken and rabbit pieces, then add the chopped onion, tomatoes, and bell pepper. Cook until softened.
  3. Add the rice and stir to coat in the oil and vegetables.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth, and stir in the saffron, green beans, and peas.
  5. Let the paella cook without stirring for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed.
  6. Serve with lemon wedges and a glass of white wine for the perfect finish.

The vibrant colors and rich flavors of Paella Valenciana are sure to impress your partner and create an unforgettable dinner experience.

5. Albóndigas (Spanish Meatballs in Tomato Sauce)

If you’re looking for a comforting dish that’s packed with flavor, Spanish albóndigas are a great choice. These meatballs are tender and juicy, served in a rich tomato sauce with aromatic spices. They’re perfect for dipping with crusty bread or served alongside rice. This dish is hearty yet light enough for a romantic dinner, making it a great option for an intimate meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  • Olive oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the ground meat, breadcrumbs, egg, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper. Form into small meatballs.
  2. Heat olive oil in a frying pan and cook the meatballs until browned on all sides.
  3. In a separate pan, heat the tomato sauce with paprika and cumin. Add the meatballs to the sauce and simmer for 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  4. Serve with rice or crusty bread for a comforting and flavorful meal.

6. Pisto Manchego (Spanish Ratatouille)

If you’re looking for a vegetarian option, pisto manchego is a flavorful and colorful dish made from tomatoes, peppers, zucchini, onions, and eggplant. It’s the Spanish equivalent of ratatouille, and it’s the perfect way to showcase the freshness of summer vegetables. Often served with a fried egg on top, pisto manchego can be a light and satisfying addition to your romantic dinner menu.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 eggplant, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 eggs (optional, for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, bell pepper, zucchini, and eggplant. Cook until softened, about 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, salt, and pepper, and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  4. Optionally, fry eggs in a separate pan and place one on top of each serving of pisto.
  5. Serve as a side dish or as the main event for a lighter option.

This dish is a perfect vegetarian choice to serve alongside any of the other dishes on your menu.

7. Churros with Chocolate Sauce

Finish your romantic Spanish dinner with a sweet and indulgent treat: churros with chocolate sauce. These fried dough pastries are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, dusted with sugar and cinnamon. Paired with a rich, velvety chocolate dipping sauce, churros make the perfect ending to your Spanish-inspired meal. They are fun to eat together, making them an ideal dessert for a romantic evening.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for coating)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon (for coating)

For the chocolate sauce:

  • 1 cup dark chocolate
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, salt, and olive oil. Bring to a boil and then remove from heat.
  2. Stir in the flour until the dough forms. Allow it to cool slightly, then add the egg and mix until smooth.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan or deep fryer. Pipe the dough into long strips and fry until golden brown.
  4. Mix sugar and cinnamon, then toss the churros in the mixture.
  5. For the chocolate sauce, melt dark chocolate and heavy cream in a saucepan over low heat, stirring until smooth.
  6. Serve the churros with the warm chocolate sauce for dipping.

This sweet treat is sure to leave a lasting impression.

8. Serrano Ham with Melon

Serrano ham is one of Spain’s most famous cured meats, known for its salty yet savory flavor. When paired with sweet melon, it creates a beautiful contrast that will delight your taste buds. This dish is simple yet elegant, making it perfect for a romantic appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cantaloupe melon, cut into wedges
  • 6-8 slices of serrano ham

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the melon wedges on a plate.
  2. Drape each wedge with a slice of serrano ham.
  3. Serve immediately for a refreshing, flavorful appetizer.

This dish is perfect for setting the tone for a light and delicious dinner.

9. Pulpo a la Gallega (Galician-Style Octopus)

For seafood lovers looking to try something different, pulpo a la gallega is a standout Spanish dish. Originating from Galicia, this dish features tender octopus served with boiled potatoes, paprika, and olive oil. It’s a flavorful and visually stunning dish that will definitely impress your partner with its unique and delicate flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb octopus, cleaned
  • 4 medium potatoes, boiled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil the octopus until tender, about 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  2. Slice the octopus into rounds and serve over the boiled potatoes.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with paprika, and season with salt.
  4. Serve warm for a delicious and unforgettable dish.

10. Cava Sangria

Finally, complement your Spanish dinner with a refreshing and fruity sangria made with cava, Spain’s famous sparkling wine. This twist on traditional sangria adds effervescence to the classic recipe, perfect for a romantic dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle of cava
  • 1/4 cup brandy
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup
  • 1 apple, thinly sliced
  • 1 orange, thinly sliced
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup strawberries, sliced
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the brandy, orange juice, lemon juice, and simple syrup.
  2. Add the fruit slices and stir gently.
  3. Pour in the cava just before serving and stir to combine.
  4. Serve over ice and enjoy a sparkling, refreshing end to your romantic Spanish meal.

These 10 Spanish dishes provide the perfect ingredients for an unforgettable romantic dinner. Whether you choose to go for a full tapas experience or a mix of hearty mains and desserts, Spain’s vibrant cuisine has something for every couple to enjoy. Each dish offers a unique taste of Spain and a chance to create lasting memories over a shared meal. Enjoy the flavors, the romance, and the passion that Spanish food brings to the table!
