Quick and Easy Teriyaki Chicken Stir Fry: A Family Favorite


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than this Quick and Easy Teriyaki Chicken Stir Fry recipe! Packed with flavors and loaded with nutritious ingredients, this stir fry is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, cornstarch, and water until well combined. Set aside.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute, until fragrant.

  3. Add the chicken strips to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, snap peas, broccoli florets, and sliced carrots. Cook for 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and pour the teriyaki sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly.

  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has thickened and the chicken and vegetables are fully coated.

  7. Serve the teriyaki chicken stir fry over cooked rice and garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions, if desired.

Why We Love This Recipe

  • Quick and Easy: This stir fry comes together in just under 30 minutes, making it a perfect option for busy weeknight dinners or last-minute meal plans.

  • Family Favorite: With its sweet and savory flavors, this teriyaki chicken stir fry is a hit with both kids and adults. It’s a great way to sneak in some extra veggies too!

  • Versatile: Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the sauce to your taste. You can also swap the chicken for beef, shrimp, or tofu for a different twist.

  • Healthy and Nutritious: Packed with fresh vegetables and lean protein, this stir fry is a balanced meal option that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

  • Flavorful Sauce: The homemade teriyaki sauce gives this dish its deliciously rich and tangy flavor. Plus, you can control the amount of sodium and sugar compared to store-bought sauces.

Tips for Success

  • Prep Ahead: To save time, you can slice the chicken and chop the vegetables in advance. Store them in airtight containers in the refrigerator until ready to use.

  • Cook in Batches: If your skillet or wok is not large enough to accommodate all the ingredients at once, cook the chicken and vegetables in separate batches and then combine them at the end.

  • Don’t Overcook: Be mindful not to overcook the vegetables, as they should retain a slight crunch for added texture and freshness.

  • Serve with Rice: The teriyaki chicken stir fry pairs perfectly with steamed white or brown rice. You can also serve it over noodles or quinoa for a different twist.

So there you have it – a Quick and Easy Teriyaki Chicken Stir Fry recipe that will wow your taste buds and please your entire family. Enjoy this flavorful and nutritious meal any day of the week. Happy cooking!
