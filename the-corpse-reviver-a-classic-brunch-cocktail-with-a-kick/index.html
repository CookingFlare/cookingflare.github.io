The Corpse Reviver: A Classic Brunch Cocktail with a Kick


If you’re in the mood for a brunch cocktail that will wake you up and get your day off to a roaring start, look no further than the Corpse Reviver. This classic cocktail is not for the faint of heart, as it packs quite a punch. But fear not, because it’s also incredibly delicious and will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

To make the Corpse Reviver, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 oz gin
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 1 oz Lillet Blanc
  • 1 oz Cointreau
  • A dash of absinthe

Instructions

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Add the gin, lemon juice, Lillet Blanc, and Cointreau to the shaker.
  3. Shake vigorously for about 15 seconds to ensure all the flavors are well combined.
  4. Grab a chilled cocktail glass and rinse it with a dash of absinthe. Discard any excess absinthe.
  5. Strain the cocktail from the shaker into the rinsed cocktail glass.
  6. Give it a quick stir to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  7. Garnish with a lemon twist or a cherry, if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

The History Behind the Corpse Reviver

The Corpse Reviver is one of the classic cocktails that originated during the cocktail renaissance of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. It was initially intended as a “hair of the dog” hangover cure, hence the name “Corpse Reviver.”

This drink is believed to have been first mentioned in the 1860s, but it gained popularity in the 1930s when it was featured in “The Savoy Cocktail Book” by Harry Craddock. Since then, it has become a staple in the world of mixology.

The Flavor Profile

The Corpse Reviver is a delightful combination of botanical gin, citrusy lemon juice, sweet and floral Lillet Blanc, and the orange-flavored liqueur Cointreau. The addition of a dash of absinthe adds a unique herbal note that ties all the flavors together.

The result is a cocktail that is both refreshing and invigorating. The citrusy notes from the lemon juice and the floral undertones from the Lillet Blanc create a harmonious balance, while the gin and Cointreau provide a boozy kick that will jolt your taste buds awake.

When to Enjoy the Corpse Reviver

While the Corpse Reviver is often associated with brunch, it’s a versatile cocktail that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. Its vibrant flavors and energizing qualities make it an excellent choice for any occasion where you want to indulge in a spirited libation.

Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering, looking for a pick-me-up after a long day, or simply want to impress your friends with your mixology skills, the Corpse Reviver is the perfect cocktail to have in your repertoire.

In Conclusion

The Corpse Reviver is a classic brunch cocktail with a kick that will awaken your senses and set the tone for an unforgettable day. Its bold flavors and intriguing history make it a favorite among cocktail enthusiasts around the world.

So, why not give it a try? Whip up a Corpse Reviver for yourself and experience the magic of this timeless concoction. Cheers to good times and excellent cocktails!
