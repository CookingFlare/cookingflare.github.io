Spiced Apple Cider Mocktail Recipe for Fall


As the leaves start to change color and the air becomes crisper, there’s nothing quite like sipping on a warm, spiced apple cider to embrace the coziness of fall. This Spiced Apple Cider Mocktail recipe is the perfect non-alcoholic drink to enjoy during the autumn season. It’s a delightful blend of apple cider, aromatic spices, and a touch of citrus, creating a comforting and refreshing beverage that can be enjoyed by all ages. Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering or simply want to treat yourself to a seasonal mocktail, this recipe is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of apple cider
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh apple slices and cinnamon sticks for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, pour the apple cider and add the cinnamon sticks, cloves, star anise, orange slices, lemon slices, and brown sugar. Stir gently to combine.

  2. Place the pot over medium heat and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally to let the flavors meld together.

  3. Once the cider is infused with the spices and citrus, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

  4. Strain the spiced apple cider mixture to remove the cinnamon sticks, cloves, star anise, and fruit slices, leaving behind a fragrant and flavorful liquid.

  5. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the spiced apple cider mocktail over the ice.

  6. Garnish each glass with a slice of fresh apple and a cinnamon stick for a touch of seasonal charm.

  7. Serve the mocktail immediately, and enjoy the warm, inviting flavors of fall in every sip.

Tips for Serving:

  • For an extra festive touch, rim the glasses with a mixture of cinnamon and sugar before pouring in the mocktail.
  • To make a larger batch for a gathering, simply double or triple the recipe and keep the spiced apple cider warm in a slow cooker for guests to help themselves.
  • Feel free to adjust the sweetness by adding more or less brown sugar, depending on your preference.

Why This Mocktail Is Perfect for Fall:

This Spiced Apple Cider Mocktail captures the essence of fall with its blend of comforting spices and the sweetness of apple cider. The warm aroma of cinnamon, cloves, and star anise combined with the citrusy notes from the orange and lemon creates a delightful sensory experience. The mocktail is not only a treat for the taste buds but also a beautiful addition to any autumn gathering, whether it’s a cozy night in or a festive celebration.

The versatility of this mocktail makes it an ideal choice for all occasions. It can be enjoyed by both kids and adults, making it a family-friendly beverage that everyone can appreciate. Additionally, the simplicity of the recipe allows for easy customization, so you can tailor the flavors to suit your personal preferences.

So, as the days grow shorter and the temperatures begin to drop, treat yourself and your loved ones to the comforting flavors of fall with this Spiced Apple Cider Mocktail. Cheers to the season of warmth, togetherness, and delicious mocktails!
