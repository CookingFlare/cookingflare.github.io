Summer Grilling: Recipes with Seasonal Fruits and Veggies


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy the fresh flavors of seasonal fruits and vegetables. Grilling not only adds a smoky and charred flavor to your dishes but also enhances the natural sweetness and juiciness of the produce. In this blog post, we will explore some delicious and creative recipes that incorporate seasonal fruits and veggies, taking your summer grilling game to the next level.

Grilled Peach and Arugula Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 fresh peaches, halved and pitted
  • 4 cups arugula
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the peach halves with a little olive oil, then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the peaches cut-side down on the grill and cook for about 3-4 minutes, until grill marks appear and the peaches are slightly softened.
  4. Remove the peaches from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes.
  5. In a large bowl, combine arugula, feta cheese, and walnuts.
  6. Slice the grilled peaches and add them to the bowl.
  7. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  8. Season with additional salt and pepper if desired.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy the perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors.

Grilled Veggie Skewers with Chimichurri Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 8-10 cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, minced garlic, chopped parsley, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the chimichurri sauce.
  3. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating between the different veggies.
  4. Brush the skewers with the chimichurri sauce, making sure to coat all sides.
  5. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and lightly charred.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and serve hot with extra chimichurri sauce on the side.
  7. The vibrant colors and flavors of these grilled veggie skewers will be a hit at your summer gatherings.

Grilled Watermelon and Halloumi Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cubed watermelon
  • 1 block halloumi cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lime juice, chopped mint leaves, salt, and pepper to make a marinade.
  3. Thread the watermelon cubes and halloumi cheese onto skewers, alternating between the two.
  4. Brush the skewers with the marinade, making sure all sides are coated.
  5. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, until the halloumi cheese is golden and the watermelon is slightly caramelized.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and serve immediately.
  7. The unique combination of sweet watermelon and salty halloumi cheese will leave your taste buds craving for more.

Conclusion

Summer grilling offers endless possibilities to explore the flavors of seasonal fruits and vegetables. From the smoky sweetness of grilled peaches to the vibrant colors of veggie skewers, these recipes will elevate your grilling experience. So, gather your loved ones, fire up the grill, and enjoy the bounty of summer with these delicious and healthy dishes. Happy grilling!

Note: Remember to always follow proper grilling safety guidelines and use caution when handling hot grill surfaces.
