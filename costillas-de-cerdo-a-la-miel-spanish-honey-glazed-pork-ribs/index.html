Costillas de Cerdo a la Miel: Spanish Honey-Glazed Pork Ribs


If you’re looking for a mouthwatering dish that perfectly captures the essence of Spanish cuisine, look no further than Costillas de Cerdo a la Miel, or Spanish Honey-Glazed Pork Ribs. This delectable dish combines tender pork ribs with a sweet and savory honey glaze, creating a flavor profile that will leave your taste buds singing. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a hearty meal, these Spanish pork ribs are sure to impress.

Ingredients

To make Costillas de Cerdo a la Miel, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 racks of pork ribs
  • 1 cup of honey
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Ribs

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). While the oven is heating up, prepare the pork ribs by removing the membrane from the back of the racks. This will help the ribs absorb the flavors of the marinade more effectively.

Step 2: Make the Marinade

In a mixing bowl, combine the honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Whisk the ingredients together until they are well incorporated. This rich and flavorful marinade will be the key to infusing the pork ribs with the perfect balance of sweetness and savory goodness.

Step 3: Marinate the Ribs

Place the racks of pork ribs in a large, shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, making sure to coat the ribs evenly. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and let the ribs marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or ideally overnight. This will allow the flavors to meld and infuse the meat, resulting in incredibly flavorful ribs.

Step 4: Cook the Ribs

Once the ribs have finished marinating, remove them from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature. Place the ribs on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil, and pour any remaining marinade over the top. Cover the ribs with another sheet of foil and seal the edges to create a packet.

Bake the ribs in the preheated oven for 2 to 2.5 hours, or until the meat is tender and cooked through. The low and slow cooking process will ensure that the pork ribs become tender and juicy, while allowing the honey glaze to caramelize and develop a beautiful golden hue.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the ribs are done, carefully remove them from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes. Before serving, garnish the ribs with fresh parsley to add a pop of color and a hint of freshness. Slice the racks into individual ribs and serve them alongside your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables, Spanish rice, or a crisp green salad.

Conclusion

Costillas de Cerdo a la Miel, or Spanish Honey-Glazed Pork Ribs, are a true delight for the senses. The combination of tender pork ribs and a luscious honey glaze creates a dish that is both comforting and elegant, making it perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a fan of Spanish cuisine or simply appreciate a well-executed rib dish, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the irresistible flavors of Spanish Honey-Glazed Pork Ribs. Buen provecho!
