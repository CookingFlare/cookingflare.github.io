Summer Squash and Zucchini Noodle Stir-Fry Recipe


Are you looking for a light and flavorful dish that captures the essence of summer? Look no further than this delightful Summer Squash and Zucchini Noodle Stir-Fry. Packed with vibrant colors, textures, and flavors, this recipe is not only a feast for the eyes but also a celebration of seasonal produce. Let’s dive into the world of fresh ingredients and delicious flavors with this easy-to-follow recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 2 yellow summer squash
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Vegetables:

    • Using a spiralizer, create noodles from the zucchinis and yellow squash. Set aside.
    • Thinly slice the bell peppers, onion, and garlic. Grate the ginger.

  2. Make the Stir-Fry Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes if using.

  3. Stir-Fry:

    • Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add a splash of oil.
    • Add the sliced onion and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.
    • Add the garlic and stir for another 30 seconds until fragrant.

  4. Add the Vegetables:

    • Toss in the zucchini and squash noodles. Stir well to combine with the other ingredients.

  5. Incorporate the Sauce:

    • Pour the stir-fry sauce over the vegetables. Stir continuously for 2-3 minutes until the noodles are tender yet slightly crisp.

  6. Season and Garnish:

    • Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.
    • Sprinkle sesame seeds and fresh cilantro on top for an extra burst of flavor and visual appeal.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Transfer the Summer Squash and Zucchini Noodle Stir-Fry to a serving dish.
    • Garnish with additional cilantro and sesame seeds if desired.
    • Serve hot and savor the fresh flavors of summer!

Tips for Success:

  • Don’t Overcook the Noodles: Zucchini and squash noodles can become mushy if overcooked, so keep an eye on them while stir-frying.
  • Customize Your Stir-Fry: Feel free to add protein such as tofu, chicken, or shrimp to make this dish more substantial.
  • Play with Seasonings: Experiment with different herbs and spices to tailor the flavor profile to your liking.

This Summer Squash and Zucchini Noodle Stir-Fry is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a side of rice or quinoa for a more substantial meal. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress with its fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors. Embrace the bounty of summer produce and treat yourself to a delicious and nutritious meal that celebrates the season!
