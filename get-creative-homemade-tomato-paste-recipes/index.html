Get Creative: Homemade Tomato Paste Recipes


Tomato paste is a versatile ingredient that adds depth and richness to a wide variety of dishes. While it’s readily available in stores, making your own homemade tomato paste can be a rewarding and flavorful experience. Whether you have a surplus of fresh tomatoes from your garden or want to elevate your culinary creations, here are some creative homemade tomato paste recipes to inspire your inner chef.

Classic Homemade Tomato Paste

Ingredients:

  • 6 lbs ripe tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions:

  1. Begin by washing and quartering the tomatoes, then removing the seeds.
  2. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat and sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until they’re soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the tomatoes to the pot and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the salt, sugar, oregano, basil, and bay leaf, then reduce the heat and let the mixture simmer for 2-3 hours, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once the tomatoes have reduced and thickened, remove the bay leaf and let the mixture cool.
  6. Puree the cooled tomato mixture in a food processor or blender until smooth.
  7. Transfer the puree to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and spread it into a thin, even layer.
  8. Place the baking sheet in the oven at 200°F (95°C) and let the tomato puree dry out for 6-8 hours, stirring occasionally, until it becomes a thick paste.
  9. Let the homemade tomato paste cool completely before transferring it to a jar or airtight container for storage.

Sun-Dried Tomato Paste

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs Roma tomatoes, halved lengthwise
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°F (95°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Arrange the halved Roma tomatoes on the baking sheet, cut side up, and drizzle them with olive oil.
  3. Sprinkle the minced garlic, salt, black pepper, thyme, and rosemary over the tomatoes.
  4. Place the baking sheet in the oven and let the tomatoes dry out for 8-10 hours, or until they are shriveled and slightly caramelized.
  5. Remove the tomatoes from the oven and let them cool completely.
  6. Once cooled, transfer the tomatoes to a food processor and blend them into a thick, smooth paste.
  7. Store the sun-dried tomato paste in a sealed container in the refrigerator for up to two weeks.

Spicy Tomato Paste

Ingredients:

  • 5 lbs vine-ripened tomatoes, halved
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 red chili peppers, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 250°F (120°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place the halved tomatoes on the baking sheet, cut side up, and drizzle them with olive oil.
  3. Sprinkle the chopped red chili peppers, cayenne pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper over the tomatoes.
  4. Roast the tomatoes in the oven for 3-4 hours, until they are soft and slightly charred.
  5. Remove the tomatoes from the oven and let them cool.
  6. Once cooled, transfer the tomatoes to a food processor and blend them into a fiery and flavorful tomato paste.
  7. Store the spicy tomato paste in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week.

Conclusion

Homemade tomato paste is a fantastic way to capture the vibrant flavors of fresh tomatoes and elevate your cooking to new heights. Whether you opt for the classic recipe, the intense sun-dried version, or the spicy twist, experimenting with homemade tomato paste opens up a world of culinary possibilities. From sauces and soups to marinades and spreads, the rich, concentrated taste of homemade tomato paste will undoubtedly enhance your kitchen creations. So, roll up your sleeves, embrace your creativity, and savor the delicious rewards of making your own homemade tomato paste.
