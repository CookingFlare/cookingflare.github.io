Savory Low Carb Bacon-Wrapped Brussels Sprouts


If you’re looking for a delicious and low-carb appetizer or side dish, these savory bacon-wrapped Brussels sprouts are the perfect choice. Combining the rich, smoky flavor of bacon with the earthy taste of Brussels sprouts, this recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser at any gathering. Plus, it’s easy to make and requires only a handful of ingredients, making it a convenient option for busy weeknights or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that the bacon-wrapped Brussels sprouts cook evenly and develop a delicious crispy texture.

  2. Prepare the Brussels Sprouts: In a large bowl, toss the halved Brussels sprouts with olive oil, garlic powder, and black pepper. Make sure the Brussels sprouts are well coated with the seasonings, as this will enhance their flavor during baking.

  3. Wrap the Brussels Sprouts: Take each halved Brussels sprout and wrap it with a slice of bacon, securing it with a toothpick. The bacon not only adds a savory element to the dish but also helps to keep the Brussels sprouts tender and juicy as they cook.

  4. Arrange on a Baking Sheet: Place the bacon-wrapped Brussels sprouts on a baking sheet, making sure to space them out evenly. This will allow the heat to circulate around each piece, ensuring that they cook uniformly.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake the Brussels sprouts for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the Brussels sprouts are tender. Keep an eye on them to prevent the bacon from burning.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the bacon-wrapped Brussels sprouts are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Then, carefully remove the toothpicks before serving. These savory delights are best enjoyed warm and can be served with your favorite dipping sauce or enjoyed on their own.

Nutritional Information

This savory low-carb dish is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a healthier option. Brussels sprouts are rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, while bacon adds a satisfying protein boost. With minimal carbs and plenty of flavor, these bacon-wrapped Brussels sprouts are a great choice for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle.

Conclusion

In conclusion, these savory low-carb bacon-wrapped Brussels sprouts are a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will elevate any meal. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a delicious snack, this recipe is a must-try. With just a few simple ingredients and easy preparation, it’s a hassle-free way to impress your guests or treat yourself to a tasty, guilt-free indulgence.

So, the next time you’re craving something savory and satisfying, give these bacon-wrapped Brussels sprouts a try. They’re a surefire way to add a touch of elegance and flavor to any occasion.
