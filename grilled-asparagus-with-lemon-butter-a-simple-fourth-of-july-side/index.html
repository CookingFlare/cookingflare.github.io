Grilled Asparagus with Lemon Butter: A Simple Fourth of July Side


As we gear up for the Fourth of July celebrations, it’s the perfect time to add some sizzle to your grill with a vibrant and flavorful side dish. Grilled asparagus with lemon butter is a delightful addition to any outdoor gathering, adding a pop of color and a burst of freshness to your holiday spread.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, tough ends trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Asparagus

Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, take the trimmed asparagus spears and place them in a large bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Toss the asparagus to ensure they are evenly coated with the oil and seasoning.

Step 2: Grill the Asparagus

Once the grill is hot, place the asparagus spears directly onto the grill grates. Grill for 4-5 minutes, turning occasionally, until the asparagus is tender and has developed some charred marks. Remove the grilled asparagus from the grill and transfer to a serving platter.

Step 3: Prepare the Lemon Butter

In a small saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the lemon zest and lemon juice.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Drizzle the prepared lemon butter over the grilled asparagus. The bright, citrusy flavor of the lemon butter perfectly complements the smoky char from the grill, elevating the natural sweetness of the asparagus. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Why Grilled Asparagus with Lemon Butter?

Grilled asparagus with lemon butter is a versatile and crowd-pleasing side dish that embodies the essence of summer. The smoky aroma from the grill enhances the earthy flavor of the asparagus, while the zesty lemon butter adds a tangy and creamy element that takes this dish to the next level.

This dish not only celebrates the vibrant flavors of the season but also offers a visually stunning addition to your Fourth of July spread. The bright green asparagus spears, adorned with charred grill marks, are a feast for the eyes and the palate.

Conclusion

This Fourth of July, delight your guests with the bright and bold flavors of grilled asparagus with lemon butter. This simple yet elegant side dish is a celebration of summer’s bounty and is sure to become a staple at your outdoor gatherings. Embrace the simplicity of fresh ingredients, the sizzle of the grill, and the joy of sharing delicious food with loved ones as we come together to honor this special day.
