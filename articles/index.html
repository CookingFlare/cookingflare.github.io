Mini Beef and Onion Sliders with Horseradish Sauce: A Gourmet and Impressive Appetizer

If you’re looking for a show-stopping appetizer that will impress your guests, look no further than these mini beef and onion sliders with zesty horseradish sauce. Perfect for parties, gatherings, or even a special dinner at home, these sliders are packed with flavor and are sure to be a hit with everyone who tries them. Ingredients For the Sliders: 1 pound ground beef 1 large onion, finely chopped 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon onion powder Salt and pepper to taste Slider buns For the Horseradish Sauce: 1/2 cup mayonnaise 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard 1 tablespoon lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste Optional Toppings: Sliced cheese Lettuce Tomato slices Pickles Instructions 1. Prepare the Beef and Onion Sliders In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, chopped onion, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Form the mixture into small patties, slightly smaller than the slider buns, as they will shrink during cooking. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. 2. Make the Horseradish Sauce In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, prepared horseradish, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, salt, and pepper until well combined. Adjust the seasoning to taste. 3. Assemble the Sliders Spread a dollop of the horseradish sauce on the bottom half of each slider bun. Place a cooked beef and onion patty on top of the sauce. Add any optional toppings you desire, such as sliced cheese, lettuce, tomato, or pickles. Place the top half of the slider bun on the toppings, and secure with a toothpick if needed. 4. Serve and Enjoy Arrange the mini sliders on a platter and watch them disappear as your guests savor the delightful flavors of the beef and onion, complemented by the zesty kick of the horseradish sauce. Why These Sliders Are a Must-Try These mini beef and onion sliders with horseradish sauce are the perfect combination of gourmet flavors and simple, rustic charm. The juicy, savory beef patties, combined with the sweet and tangy flavor of the caramelized onions, create a taste sensation that will leave your guests craving more.
