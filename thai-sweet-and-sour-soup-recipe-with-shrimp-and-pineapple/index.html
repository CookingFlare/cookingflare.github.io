Thai Sweet and Sour Soup Recipe with Shrimp and Pineapple


Looking for a flavorful and comforting soup that will transport your taste buds to Thailand? Look no further than this Thai Sweet and Sour Soup with Shrimp and Pineapple! This vibrant and aromatic dish is a perfect balance of sweet, sour, and spicy flavors, making it a favorite among Thai cuisine enthusiasts.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch, dissolved in 2 tablespoons of water
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Thai basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent, about 2 minutes.

  2. Add the bell peppers to the pot and cook for another 2 minutes, until slightly softened.

  3. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, fish sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and brown sugar. Stir well to combine the ingredients.

  4. Bring the soup to a simmer and let it cook for about 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Add the shrimp and pineapple chunks to the pot. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.

  6. In a small bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in water to create a slurry. Slowly pour the slurry into the soup while stirring continuously. This will help thicken the soup slightly.

  7. Continue to cook the soup for another 2 minutes, until it has thickened to your desired consistency.

  8. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the chopped cilantro leaves.

  9. Serve the Thai Sweet and Sour Soup hot, garnished with fresh Thai basil leaves and lime wedges on the side.

Enjoy the Flavors of Thailand

This Thai Sweet and Sour Soup with Shrimp and Pineapple is a delightful blend of flavors that will transport you to the bustling streets of Thailand. The combination of succulent shrimp, tangy pineapple, and aromatic herbs and spices creates a harmonious dish that is both comforting and refreshing.

The secret to this soup’s depth of flavor lies in the balance of sweet, sour, and savory elements. The sweetness of the pineapple complements the tanginess of the vinegar and lime, while the fish sauce and soy sauce add a savory umami note. The result is a tantalizing explosion of flavors in every spoonful.

Whether you’re craving a warm and comforting meal on a chilly evening or looking to impress guests with an exotic dish, this Thai Sweet and Sour Soup with Shrimp and Pineapple is sure to satisfy. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure to Thailand right in your own kitchen.
