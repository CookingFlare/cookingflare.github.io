Nut-Free Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies: A Perfect Combination of Flavors


Are you a fan of cookies but have a nut allergy? Or maybe you’re just looking for a delicious and healthy treat to satisfy your sweet tooth. Look no further! These Nut-Free Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies are the perfect combination of flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the mashed bananas, vanilla extract, and egg to the butter-sugar mixture. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing well after each addition. The dough should be slightly sticky but not wet.

  6. Gently fold in the chocolate chips until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Using a cookie scoop or a spoon, drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.

  9. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cookies have cooled, serve and enjoy!

Why Nut-Free Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies?

These Nut-Free Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies are not only delicious but also a healthier alternative to traditional cookies. By using ripe bananas, we add natural sweetness and moisture to the cookies, reducing the need for excessive amounts of sugar and fat. Additionally, these cookies are nut-free, making them safe for those with allergies or dietary restrictions.

The Perfect Combination of Flavors

The combination of bananas and chocolate chips creates a delightful flavor profile. The sweetness of the bananas complements the richness of the chocolate, resulting in a cookie that is both sweet and satisfying. Each bite is filled with the creamy texture of the banana and the burst of chocolate chips, creating a harmony of flavors that will have you reaching for another cookie.

Conclusion

If you’re looking for a nut-free and delicious treat, these Nut-Free Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies are the answer. With their perfect combination of flavors and healthier ingredients, they are a guilt-free indulgence. Whether you’re baking for yourself, your family, or a special occasion, these cookies are sure to impress. So go ahead, give them a try, and let your taste buds experience the magic of this delightful treat!
