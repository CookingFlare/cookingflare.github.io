BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Asparagus: A Savory Fourth of July Side


Welcome to our latest recipe blog post, just in time for the Fourth of July celebrations! If you’re looking for a delicious and savory side dish to complement your barbecue spread, look no further than our BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Asparagus. This dish is the perfect combination of smoky, crispy bacon and tender, flavorful asparagus, all glazed with a sweet and tangy barbecue sauce. It’s a crowd-pleaser that will have your guests coming back for more.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh asparagus spears
  • Thinly sliced bacon
  • Your favorite barbecue sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Asparagus: Start by washing and trimming the tough ends off the asparagus spears. Drizzle them with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Toss them gently to ensure they are evenly coated.

  2. Wrap with Bacon: Take a few spears of asparagus and wrap them with a slice of bacon. The bacon should securely wrap around each spear, and you can place them on a baking sheet seam-side down to prevent them from unraveling.

  3. Grill or Bake: You have the option to either grill or bake the bacon-wrapped asparagus. If grilling, preheat your grill to medium-high heat and place the asparagus directly on the grill. Cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the bacon is crispy and the asparagus is tender. If baking, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and place the asparagus on a baking sheet. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the asparagus is tender.

  4. Glaze with Barbecue Sauce: During the last few minutes of cooking, brush the bacon-wrapped asparagus with your favorite barbecue sauce. This will add a delicious sweet and tangy flavor to the dish and caramelize beautifully on the bacon.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the bacon-wrapped asparagus is cooked to perfection, carefully transfer them to a serving platter. They are best enjoyed hot, so serve them immediately and watch them disappear!

Tips and Variations:

  • For a spicy kick, consider adding a pinch of cayenne pepper or a drizzle of hot sauce to the barbecue glaze.
  • If you’re a fan of cheese, sprinkle some grated Parmesan or cheddar over the bacon-wrapped asparagus during the last few minutes of cooking for a gooey, indulgent twist.
  • Experiment with different types of bacon, such as maple or hickory-smoked, to add unique flavor profiles to the dish.

Why BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Asparagus?

This savory side dish is a perfect addition to any Fourth of July celebration or summer barbecue. The combination of crispy bacon, tender asparagus, and sticky barbecue sauce creates a medley of flavors and textures that will delight your taste buds. Not to mention, it’s a simple and impressive dish that will earn you rave reviews from your guests.

So, this Fourth of July, fire up the grill or preheat the oven, and treat your friends and family to the irresistible flavors of BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Asparagus. It’s a dish that embodies the spirit of summer and the joy of good food shared with loved ones.
