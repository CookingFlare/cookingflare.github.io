Soft and Fluffy Milk Bread Recipe


Today, I am thrilled to share a simple, yet mouth-watering recipe for Soft and Fluffy Milk Bread that is sure to become a favorite in your home. This bread is perfect for sandwiches, toast, or simple snacks, and its tender texture will leave your family asking for more.

Milk Bread is very popular in countries like Japan and Korea, where bakeries are lined with soft and fluffy loaves. Its unique taste and texture are attributed to the use of milk, which helps create an especially tender crumb. Today, you’ll learn how to make this magical bread at home.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups (375 g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons (29 g) bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 envelope active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups (360 ml) milk - at room temperature
  • 1 cup (240 ml) water - at room temperature
  • 3 tablespoons (45 g) unsalted butter - at room temperature
  • 1 large egg - at room temperature and beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Start by combining the bread flour, regular flour, sugar, and yeast in the bowl of your stand mixer or a large mixing bowl.
  2. Heat the milk and water in a small saucepan until it reaches a temperature of 40°C - 44°C (104°F - 111°F). The warmth of the liquids is important for the yeast to activate and trigger the process of fermentation.
  3. Pour the warm milk mixture into the dry ingredients and mix until fully incorporated.
  4. Add the soft and beaten egg, then mix some more to form a smooth and elastic dough.
  5. Dump the softened butter into the dough while it’s still mixing. Continue kneading for a few minutes until the butter is fully incorporated.
  6. Cover the dough and allow it to ferment for about 60-90 minutes in a warm and draft-free environment. The dough should rise and become soft and fluffy, nearly doubling its size.
  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles, then transfer it to a lightly floured surface.
  8. Divide the dough into 16 equal parts and shape them into round balls. You can also choose to make smaller pieces or a single large loaf, depending on your preference.
  9. Place the shaped dough on parchment paper-lined baking trays, allowing about 2-3 cm (1-2 inches) of space between each piece.
  10. Mist the dough with water and loosely cover them with a damp towel to prevent them from drying out. Allow them to proof for another 20-30 minutes.
  11. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) with a convection feature if you have one. This helps bake the bread evenly.
  12. Once the oven is up to temperature, bake the milk bread for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown. The loaves should sound hollow when tapped lightly on the bottom.
  13. Remove the delicious milk bread from the oven and allow them to cool in the baking trays for 5-10 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack and cool completely.
  14. Enjoy your Soft and Fluffy Milk Bread any way you like - and get ready to impress your friends and family with this easy-to-make recipe!

In summary, Milk Bread is an easy and delicious addition to your baking repertoire. Its delicate texture and incomparable taste will make this a favorite amongst any bread enthusiasts. Best of all, it is simple to make, so you can enjoy fresh, homemade bread any day of the week. Happy baking!
