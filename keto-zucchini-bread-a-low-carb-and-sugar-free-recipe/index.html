Keto Zucchini Bread: A Low-Carb and Sugar-Free Recipe


Are you following a keto diet and craving some delicious baked goods? Look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for Keto Zucchini Bread. Packed with flavor and low in carbs, this bread is the perfect guilt-free treat for any time of the day. And the best part? It’s sugar-free too!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/4 cup flaxseed meal
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup Erythritol (or your preferred keto-friendly sweetener)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups grated zucchini

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan with coconut oil or line it with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, coconut flour, flaxseed meal, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Stir well to ensure all the dry ingredients are evenly mixed.

  3. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, melted coconut oil, almond milk, Erythritol, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir until the batter is smooth and no lumps remain.

  5. Gently fold in the grated zucchini, ensuring it is evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and spread it out evenly.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Remove the bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, slice the bread and enjoy! You can store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Why Choose Keto Zucchini Bread?

Zucchini bread is a classic favorite, and this keto version is no exception. By using almond flour, coconut flour, and flaxseed meal instead of traditional wheat flour, this recipe is low in carbs and suitable for those following a keto lifestyle. The addition of grated zucchini not only adds moisture and texture but also sneaks in some extra veggies without compromising on taste.

Not only is this bread low-carb, but it’s also sugar-free. By using a keto-friendly sweetener like Erythritol, you can satisfy your sweet tooth without derailing your diet. Plus, the natural sweetness of the zucchini adds a subtle hint of flavor that pairs perfectly with the warm spices.

Enjoy Anytime, Anywhere

This Keto Zucchini Bread is versatile and can be enjoyed at any time of the day. Have a slice for breakfast, pair it with your afternoon tea, or indulge in a guilt-free dessert after dinner. You can even top it with some sugar-free cream cheese frosting for an extra special treat.

So, what are you waiting for? Whip up a batch of this delicious Keto Zucchini Bread and satisfy your cravings without compromising your health goals. It’s the perfect combination of flavor, texture, and nutrition, all in one delightful loaf. Give it a try today and enjoy a tasty treat that’s low-carb, sugar-free, and oh-so-delicious!
