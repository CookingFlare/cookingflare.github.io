Instant Pot Desserts: Sweet Treats You Can Make in Minutes


When it comes to fast and easy desserts, your Instant Pot can be one of the most powerful tools in your kitchen. Known for its versatility, this multi-cooker isn’t just about savory dinners and meal prep. It’s also a game-changer for creating sweet indulgences in a fraction of the time it would take with traditional methods. Whether you’re hosting a last-minute gathering or simply craving a quick homemade dessert, the Instant Pot offers endless possibilities for dessert lovers.

So, let’s dive right into some mouthwatering Instant Pot dessert ideas that are quick to prepare, rich in flavor, and sure to impress anyone with a sweet tooth. Here’s everything you need to know about crafting delectable desserts with the convenience of your trusty pressure cooker.

Why Use the Instant Pot for Desserts?

The Instant Pot isn’t just convenient—it also enhances flavors, retains moisture, and offers consistency when making desserts. Thanks to its even cooking style and steam-infused environment, you’ll find that your desserts are perfectly cooked without the risk of drying out, burning, or uneven textures. Plus, the reduced cooking time makes it the ultimate tool for fast sweets.

Another bonus? Making desserts in the Instant Pot allows you to free up your oven or stovetop when you’re preparing multiple dishes.

Essential Tips for Success

Before you begin crafting delicious desserts in your Instant Pot, here are a few key tips to keep in mind:

  1. Use the Right Accessories: The Instant Pot works best when you have the right tools, such as silicone muffin cups, a springform pan, or ramekins, depending on the dessert you’re preparing.

  2. Always Add Water: Keep in mind that the Instant Pot requires liquid to create pressure. For desserts, you typically place your dessert container on a trivet above water.

  3. Foil or Lid Coverage Is Key: Many desserts need to be covered tightly with aluminum foil or a silicone lid to prevent condensation from dripping onto the batter or mixture.

  4. Use the Natural Release Method: For creamy and delicate desserts, opt for natural pressure release to avoid rapid changes in temperature, which might impact the texture.

  5. Prep Ahead of Time: Some Instant Pot desserts benefit from chilling after cooking. If you’re making cheesecake or custard, allow plenty of time for the dessert to cool thoroughly.

Irresistible Instant Pot Dessert Recipes

1. Classic Instant Pot Cheesecake

When you think of rich and creamy desserts, cheesecake is often at the top of the list. Making a cheesecake in the Instant Pot is not only quick but results in an incredibly velvety texture.

Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter, then press into the bottom of a greased 7-inch springform pan to form the crust. Refrigerate while preparing the filling.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, followed by sour cream, vanilla, and flour. Mix until just combined.
  3. Pour the filling onto the crust. Cover the pan tightly with aluminum foil.
  4. Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot. Place the pan on the trivet and lower it into the pot.
  5. Cook on high pressure for 30 minutes, then allow natural pressure release.
  6. Cool at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.

2. Decadent Chocolate Lava Cakes

If you’re craving molten chocolate goodness, these Instant Pot lava cakes are a quick way to satisfy your dessert needs.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: Ice cream or whipped cream for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate chips in 30-second intervals until smooth.
  2. Whisk together eggs, egg yolks, powdered sugar, and vanilla, then stir in the melted chocolate mixture. Slowly fold in the flour until combined.
  3. Grease ramekins and pour the batter in, leaving about an inch of space at the top.
  4. Place 1 cup of water in the Instant Pot and insert the trivet. Arrange ramekins on the trivet.
  5. Cook on high pressure for 7-9 minutes, depending on how molten you want the centers. Release pressure manually.
  6. Carefully remove the ramekins, allow to cool slightly, and serve with a dollop of ice cream or whipped cream.

3. Instant Pot Rice Pudding

Creamy and comforting, rice pudding is a dessert that’s hearty, simple, and delicious. The Instant Pot makes it fuss-free and quicker than ever.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio or jasmine rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups milk
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Optional toppings: Raisins, nuts, or fresh fruit

Instructions:

  1. Combine rice and water in the Instant Pot. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes, then allow a natural pressure release.
  2. Stir in the milk, sugar, and vanilla. Use the sauté function and cook, stirring occasionally, until the pudding thickens to your desired consistency.
  3. For added flavor, mix in cinnamon or a handful of raisins during the sauté phase.
  4. Serve warm or chilled, garnished with your choice of toppings.

4. Fluffy Instant Pot Banana Bread

Banana bread may not sound like a dessert, but when it’s warm, moist, and paired with a dollop of whipped cream or chocolate sauce, it transforms into a decadent treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • Optional mix-ins: Chocolate chips or chopped nuts

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together bananas, sugar, butter, and eggs until smooth.
  2. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Stir just until combined. Fold in optional mix-ins if desired.
  3. Grease a 6-inch cake pan and pour in the batter.
  4. Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot. Place the pan on a trivet and lower it into the pot.
  5. Cook on high pressure for 50 minutes, then allow a natural pressure release.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from the pan. Slice and serve warm.

5. Instant Pot Crème Brûlée

Crème brûlée may seem fancy, but the Instant Pot allows you to make this luxurious dessert effortlessly.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar, plus extra for topping
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale. Heat cream in the microwave until warm, then slowly whisk into the egg mixture. Stir in vanilla.
  2. Strain the mixture through a fine sieve and divide it evenly into ramekins.
  3. Cover ramekins with foil and place them on the trivet in the Instant Pot with 1 cup of water below.
  4. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes, then allow a natural pressure release.
  5. Chill the custards in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours. Before serving, sprinkle sugar on top and caramelize with a kitchen torch.

Embrace the Sweet Magic of the Instant Pot

The Instant Pot is so much more than a kitchen gadget for soups and stews. By experimenting with different recipes, ingredients, and cooking methods, you’ll discover the versatility and convenience of creating desserts that taste like they took hours to prepare—but were ready in mere minutes.

Start by trying one of the recipes above, or get creative and adapt your favorite dessert to the Instant Pot. With this innovative appliance, the possibilities for sweet treats are endless. Delight your family and friends, save time in the kitchen, and most importantly, indulge in the satisfaction of homemade desserts that hit the spot every time.
