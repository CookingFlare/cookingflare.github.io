5-Ingredient Instant Pot Chili Recipe


If you’re a fan of hearty, flavorful, and easy-to-make meals, then you’re in for a treat. This 5-ingredient Instant Pot chili recipe is a game-changer for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you crave a comforting bowl of chili without spending hours in the kitchen. With minimal prep and cooking time, this recipe delivers maximum flavor and satisfaction. Let’s dive into the details of this simple yet delicious chili creation that will become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

Before we get into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the handful of ingredients that will come together to create a mouthwatering bowl of chili:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder

That’s it! Just five simple ingredients, and you’re on your way to enjoying a delightful chili experience.

Instructions

Step 1: Sauté the Ground Beef

Start by turning on your Instant Pot and selecting the “Sauté” function. Once the pot is hot, add the ground beef and cook it until it’s browned, breaking it apart with a wooden spoon as it cooks. This step will infuse the chili with rich, meaty flavors that will elevate the overall taste.

Step 2: Add the Onions and Chili Powder

Once the ground beef is browned, add the diced onions and chili powder to the pot. Sauté everything together for a few minutes until the onions are soft and translucent, and the aroma of the chili powder fills the air. This is where the magic starts to happen as the flavors mingle and intensify.

Step 3: Pressure Cook

Next, it’s time to add the diced tomatoes and kidney beans to the Instant Pot. Give everything a good stir to combine the ingredients. Then, secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” mode for 10 minutes. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before carefully doing a quick release.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Once the pressure has been released, carefully open the lid of the Instant Pot. Give the chili a gentle stir, and behold the rich, aromatic chili that has come together effortlessly. Serve the chili in bowls, and if desired, garnish with your favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, or fresh cilantro.

Tips for Customization

While the beauty of this recipe lies in its simplicity, there are endless ways to customize it to suit your taste preferences. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  • Spice It Up: If you prefer a spicier chili, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a chopped jalapeño to the mix.
  • Vegetarian Twist: For a vegetarian version, swap the ground beef for plant-based ground meat or extra beans for a protein-packed alternative.
  • Toppings Galore: Get creative with toppings! Avocado slices, diced red onions, or a squeeze of lime can take your chili to the next level.

Final Thoughts

In just a few simple steps and with minimal ingredients, you’ve created a delicious 5-ingredient Instant Pot chili that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a cozy meal at home, this recipe is a winner. The convenience of the Instant Pot combined with the rich flavors of the chili will make this dish a go-to option time and time again.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and fuss-free meal, remember this recipe and let your Instant Pot work its magic. Happy cooking and even happier eating!
