Crispy Baked Chicken Tenders: Kid-Friendly Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and kid-friendly meal that’s also easy to prepare, look no further than these crispy baked chicken tenders. This recipe is a healthier alternative to traditional fried chicken tenders, and it’s guaranteed to be a hit with the whole family. The best part? These chicken tenders are baked to golden perfection, so you can enjoy the crispy goodness without the extra oil. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken breast tenders
  • 1 cup of panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. In a shallow dish, whisk the eggs until well beaten.

  3. In another shallow dish, combine the panko breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure the seasonings are evenly distributed.

  4. Dip each chicken tender into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off, then coat it with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere. Place the coated tenders on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each piece.

  5. Once all the tenders are coated and arranged on the baking sheet, lightly spray them with cooking spray. This will help them achieve that golden crispiness in the oven.

  6. Bake the chicken tenders for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Remember to flip them halfway through the baking time to ensure even browning.

  7. Once the chicken tenders are cooked through and crispy, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These crispy baked chicken tenders are versatile and can be served with a variety of sides. Consider pairing them with some sweet potato fries, a fresh garden salad, or steamed vegetables for a well-rounded meal. For dipping, you can offer a classic ranch dressing, honey mustard, or barbecue sauce to appeal to different taste preferences.

Kid-Friendly Tips

Getting kids involved in the kitchen can make mealtime more enjoyable for the whole family. Encourage your little ones to help with the breading process by setting up an assembly line for dipping and coating the chicken tenders. This hands-on approach can spark their interest in cooking and make them more excited to try the finished dish.

Final Thoughts

These crispy baked chicken tenders are a crowd-pleaser for all ages. Whether you’re serving them for a family dinner, packing them in lunchboxes, or hosting a kids’ party, this recipe is sure to be a hit. The crunchy exterior gives way to tender, juicy chicken on the inside, making it a delightful dish that everyone will love. Plus, the fact that they are baked instead of fried adds a healthier twist without sacrificing flavor. Give this recipe a try, and watch as it becomes a regular favorite in your household!
