Deliciously Sweet Cinnamon Sugar Donut Muffins


If you’re a fan of both donuts and muffins, then this recipe is a match made in heaven for you! These Deliciously Sweet Cinnamon Sugar Donut Muffins combine the best of both worlds into a delightful treat that will satisfy your cravings. With their soft and fluffy texture, irresistible cinnamon-sugar coating, and a hint of vanilla, these muffins are sure to become a household favorite.

Ingredients

For the muffins:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buttermilk

For the cinnamon-sugar coating:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease them with cooking spray.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 3-4 minutes using an electric mixer.

  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the buttermilk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix as this can result in dense muffins.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling them about ¾ full.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  8. While the muffins are baking, prepare the cinnamon-sugar coating. In a shallow bowl, combine the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon.

  9. Once the muffins are done baking, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes.

  10. Dip each muffin into the melted butter, making sure to coat all sides. Then roll the muffin in the cinnamon-sugar mixture until fully coated.

  11. Place the coated muffins on a wire rack to cool completely.

  12. Serve and enjoy these Deliciously Sweet Cinnamon Sugar Donut Muffins with a warm cup of coffee or a glass of milk. They are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as an afternoon snack.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a teaspoon of ground nutmeg or a pinch of ground cloves to the cinnamon-sugar coating mixture.

  • If you don’t have buttermilk on hand, you can make your own by adding 1 tablespoon of lemon juice or vinegar to 1 cup of milk. Let it sit for 5 minutes before using.

  • These muffins are best enjoyed on the day they are made. However, you can store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days.

  • Feel free to get creative with the coatings! Instead of cinnamon sugar, you can try powdered sugar, a glaze, or even a chocolate ganache.

So there you have it, a recipe that combines the best of both donuts and muffins. These Deliciously Sweet Cinnamon Sugar Donut Muffins are a delightful treat that is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether you enjoy them for breakfast, brunch, or as a snack, these muffins are bound to become a family favorite. Give them a try and let us know what you think!
