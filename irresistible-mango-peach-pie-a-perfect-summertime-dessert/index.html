Irresistible Mango Peach Pie: A Perfect Summertime Dessert


The sun is shining, and the temperatures are rising. What better way to beat the heat than with a refreshing and mouthwatering dessert? Introducing the Irresistible Mango Peach Pie, a perfect summertime treat that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 cups fresh peaches, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package (2 sheets) store-bought pie crusts
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • 1 tablespoon turbinado sugar (for sprinkling)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place a baking sheet in the oven to preheat as well. This will help ensure a perfectly crisp and golden crust.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced mangoes and sliced peaches. Add the granulated sugar, cornstarch, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and salt. Gently stir until all the ingredients are well combined and the fruit is evenly coated. Set aside and let the mixture sit for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  3. Roll out one sheet of the pie crust and carefully transfer it to a 9-inch pie dish. Press the crust into the dish, ensuring that it covers the bottom and the sides completely.

  4. Pour the mango and peach mixture into the pie crust, spreading it out evenly. Be careful not to overfill the pie crust, as the fruit will release juices while baking.

  5. Roll out the second sheet of pie crust and cut it into strips. Create a lattice pattern on top of the fruit filling by weaving the strips over and under each other. Alternatively, you can cover the pie with the entire sheet of crust, cutting a few slits to allow steam to escape.

  6. Once the lattice or full crust is in place, trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie. This will not only make it look beautiful but also prevent any filling from leaking out during baking.

  7. Brush the top crust with the beaten egg wash, ensuring that it is evenly coated. Sprinkle the turbinado sugar over the top for a delightful crunch and added sweetness.

  8. Place the pie on the preheated baking sheet in the oven and bake for approximately 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly. If the crust starts to brown too quickly, cover it loosely with aluminum foil to prevent burning.

  9. Once the pie is baked to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for at least 2 hours. This will allow the filling to set, making it easier to slice and serve.

  10. Serve the Irresistible Mango Peach Pie on its own or with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence. Enjoy the explosion of flavors and textures that this delightful summertime dessert offers.

Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a sweet treat, the Irresistible Mango Peach Pie is sure to impress. The combination of juicy mangoes, luscious peaches, and a flaky crust creates a dessert that captures the essence of summer. So go ahead, embrace the flavors of the season, and treat yourself to this irresistible pie that will leave you longing for more.
