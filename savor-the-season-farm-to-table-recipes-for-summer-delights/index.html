Savor the Season: Farm to Table Recipes for Summer Delights


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in the bounty of fresh, locally grown produce. From vibrant fruits to crisp vegetables, the summer season offers an abundance of flavors that can elevate your culinary creations to new heights. Embracing the farm-to-table concept, we’ve curated a collection of recipes that celebrate the best of summer’s offerings. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or preparing a light and refreshing meal for your family, these recipes are designed to savor the season in every bite.

Grilled Peach and Burrata Salad

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes | Total Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • 8 oz. burrata cheese
  • 3 cups arugula
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the peach halves with olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of salt.
  3. Grill the peaches for 2-3 minutes on each side until they have grill marks.
  4. Arrange the arugula on a serving platter.
  5. Place the grilled peaches on top of the arugula.
  6. Tear the burrata into pieces and distribute it over the peaches and arugula.
  7. Drizzle the salad with balsamic glaze and a touch of olive oil.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy the delightful combination of sweet, smoky, and creamy flavors.

Lemon Garlic Shrimp and Zucchini Noodles

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 medium zucchinis, spiralized into noodles
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. Toss in the zucchini noodles and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until they are just tender.
  5. Drizzle the lemon juice over the shrimp and zucchini noodles.
  6. Sprinkle with lemon zest and fresh parsley.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve immediately for a light and flavorful summer meal that’s as delicious as it is healthy.

Berry Mint Sparkling Lemonade

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup simple syrup
  • 4 cups sparkling water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, muddle the mixed berries and mint leaves to release their flavors.
  2. Add the freshly squeezed lemon juice and simple syrup to the pitcher.
  3. Stir the mixture to combine the flavors.
  4. Fill the pitcher with ice cubes.
  5. Pour the sparkling water over the ice and stir gently to mix.
  6. Serve the berry mint sparkling lemonade in chilled glasses and garnish with additional berries and mint leaves.
  7. Enjoy the refreshing and effervescent drink that captures the essence of summer in every sip.

Conclusion

Embracing the farm-to-table philosophy allows us to appreciate the natural abundance of each season and create culinary masterpieces that honor the flavors of the earth. These recipes are just a glimpse into the world of summer delights that can be crafted from the freshest ingredients available. As you explore the local farmers’ markets and harvest the treasures of the season, let these recipes inspire you to savor the goodness of summer in every dish. Cheers to a season filled with delectable farm-to-table delights!
