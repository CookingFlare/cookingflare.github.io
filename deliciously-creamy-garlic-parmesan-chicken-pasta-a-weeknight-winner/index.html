Deliciously Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Pasta: A Weeknight Winner


Are you tired of the same old weeknight dinner routine? Looking for a quick and easy recipe that will satisfy your family’s taste buds? Look no further, because we have the perfect solution for you - Deliciously Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Pasta!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  4. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, and chicken broth to the skillet. Stir until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Slice the cooked chicken breasts into thin strips and add them to the skillet with the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the chicken is heated through.

  6. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet and toss until well coated in the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce.

  7. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

  8. Serve immediately and enjoy the deliciously creamy garlic Parmesan chicken pasta!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This recipe for Deliciously Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Pasta is a weeknight winner for several reasons. First, it comes together in just under 30 minutes, making it the perfect option for busy weeknights when you don’t have a lot of time to spend in the kitchen. Second, it’s incredibly flavorful and satisfying, thanks to the combination of garlic, Parmesan cheese, and tender chicken. Finally, the creamy sauce coats the fettuccine pasta perfectly, creating a comforting and indulgent dish that will have your family coming back for seconds.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add some red pepper flakes to the sauce while cooking the garlic.
  • Feel free to add some vegetables to the dish for added nutrition. Sautéed mushrooms, spinach, or roasted cherry tomatoes would all be delicious additions.
  • If you prefer a lighter version of this dish, you can substitute half-and-half or milk for the heavy cream. The sauce won’t be as rich, but it will still be creamy and delicious.
  • If you’re looking to cut down on carbs, you can substitute the fettuccine pasta with zucchini noodles or spaghetti squash.

So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and delicious weeknight dinner, give this recipe for Deliciously Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Pasta a try. With its creamy sauce, tender chicken, and perfectly cooked pasta, it’s sure to be a hit with your family. Enjoy!
