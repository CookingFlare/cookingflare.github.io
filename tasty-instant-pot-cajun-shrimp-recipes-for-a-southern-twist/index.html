Tasty Instant Pot Cajun Shrimp Recipes for a Southern Twist


When it comes to Southern cuisine, few dishes evoke the vibrant flavors and spice of Cajun cooking as well as shrimp. Shrimp is a staple along the Gulf Coast, and its versatility makes it a favorite among home cooks and professional chefs alike. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious Instant Pot Cajun shrimp recipes that are bound to impress your family and friends, while also providing you with a quick and easy way to enjoy this classic dish.

What Makes Cajun Cooking Unique?

Cajun cuisine is characterized by its bold flavors, use of fresh ingredients, and a cooking style that often incorporates one-pot meals. Originating from the Cajun people of Louisiana, this culinary tradition blends French, African, Spanish, and Native American influences, resulting in an explosion of flavor that is both comforting and exciting.

The Benefits of Using an Instant Pot

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, particularly when it comes to preparing seafood like shrimp. Not only does it significantly reduce cooking time, but it also infuses flavors in a way that traditional methods sometimes can’t achieve. Shrimp cooks quickly, and the pressure cooking method helps to retain moisture, ensuring that your dish remains juicy and tender.

Essential Ingredients for Cajun Shrimp

Before we dive into the recipes, let’s discuss some of the essential ingredients you’ll need to create that signature Cajun flavor:

  • Shrimp: Fresh or frozen shrimp works well; just ensure that they are peeled and deveined.
  • Cajun Seasoning: You can either buy pre-made Cajun seasoning or create your own using a mix of paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, thyme, and black pepper.
  • Onions and Bell Peppers: These aromatic vegetables form the base of many Cajun dishes.
  • Garlic: Fresh minced garlic adds depth and richness.
  • Tomatoes: Diced tomatoes or tomato sauce can add a touch of sweetness and acidity.
  • Broth: Chicken or seafood broth enhances the flavor profile.
  • Cream: For creamy versions, heavy cream or coconut milk can provide a rich texture.

Recipe 1: Classic Cajun Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning (or to taste)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Sauté Vegetables: Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add olive oil, then toss in the chopped onions and bell peppers. Sauté for about 3-4 minutes until they soften. Stir in the minced garlic and sauté for another minute.

  2. Add Pasta and Liquid: Pour in the penne pasta, diced tomatoes (with juices), chicken broth, and Cajun seasoning. Stir well to combine.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid of the Instant Pot, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: Once the cooking time is complete, carefully perform a quick release of the pressure.

  5. Add Shrimp and Cream: Open the lid, stir in the shrimp and heavy cream. Close the lid again and let it sit for about 5 minutes. The residual heat will cook the shrimp perfectly.

  6. Serve: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Why You’ll Love It

This classic Cajun shrimp pasta is not only quick to make but also brings a delightful kick that will tantalize your taste buds. The creaminess balances out the spices, making it a dish that everyone will enjoy.

Recipe 2: Instant Pot Cajun Shrimp and Grits

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup stone-ground grits
  • 4 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, sliced (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook Grits: In your Instant Pot, combine the grits and water or chicken broth. Set to pressure cook on high for 10 minutes. Once done, do a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.

  2. Sauté Shrimp: In a separate skillet, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent. Stir in the minced garlic and Cajun seasoning, cooking for another minute.

  3. Cook Shrimp: Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink, about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Combine: Stir the cheese into the cooked grits until melted and creamy. Fold in the sautéed shrimp mixture.

  5. Serve: Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Serve hot, garnished with sliced green onions.

Why This Recipe Works

Shrimp and grits is a Southern classic, and the Instant Pot makes this dish even easier to prepare. The creamy grits combined with the spicy shrimp create a comforting meal that’s perfect for any occasion.

Recipe 3: Spicy Cajun Shrimp Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate Shrimp: In a bowl, combine shrimp, olive oil, Cajun seasoning, and lime juice. Let it marinate for about 15 minutes.

  2. Cook Shrimp: Using the sauté function on the Instant Pot, cook the marinated shrimp for 3-4 minutes until they are pink and cooked through.

  3. Warm Tortillas: You can either warm the tortillas in a skillet or place them in the Instant Pot on the warming setting for a couple of minutes.

  4. Assemble Tacos: Place a generous amount of shrimp on each tortilla, top with shredded cabbage, avocado slices, and a dollop of sour cream.

  5. Garnish: Sprinkle with fresh cilantro before serving.

Why This Recipe Stands Out

These spicy Cajun shrimp tacos offer a fun twist on traditional taco night. They are light yet flavorful, making them a perfect option for a summer gathering or a casual weeknight dinner.

Tips for Perfect Cajun Shrimp Every Time

  1. Choose the Right Shrimp: Fresh, high-quality shrimp will always yield the best results. If using frozen shrimp, make sure to thaw them properly before cooking.

  2. Don’t Overcook: Shrimp cooks quickly, and overcooked shrimp can become rubbery. Keep an eye on them and remove from heat as soon as they turn pink.

  3. Experiment with Spices: While Cajun seasoning is key, feel free to adjust the spice levels to suit your taste. Adding hot sauce or extra cayenne pepper can amp up the heat.

  4. Serve Immediately: Cajun shrimp dishes are best served fresh and hot. If you need to prepare in advance, consider cooking the shrimp separately and adding them to the dish just before serving.

Bringing the Flavor Home

Cajun cuisine is all about bringing bold flavors to the table, and these Instant Pot shrimp recipes are perfect examples of how you can do just that. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a delicious meal, these recipes will help you embrace the spirit of Southern cooking right in your own kitchen.

So gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor the tastes of the South with these scrumptious Cajun shrimp dishes. Bon appétit!
