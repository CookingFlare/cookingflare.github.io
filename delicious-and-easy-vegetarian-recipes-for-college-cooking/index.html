Delicious and Easy Vegetarian Recipes for College Cooking


Are you a college student looking for delicious and easy vegetarian recipes that won’t break the bank? Look no further! In this blog post, we have compiled a list of mouthwatering vegetarian dishes that are perfect for college cooking. These recipes are not only budget-friendly but also quick and easy to make. So, let’s dive right in and explore these delectable vegetarian recipes that will surely satisfy your taste buds.

1. Creamy Avocado Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 200g spaghetti

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a blender or food processor, combine the avocado, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the creamy avocado sauce until well coated.
  4. Serve immediately and garnish with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.

2. Veggie Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • 2 large tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • Salsa and sour cream for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle half of the shredded cheese evenly over it.
  3. Layer the diced bell peppers, corn kernels, black beans, and red onion on top of the cheese.
  4. Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top and cover with the second tortilla.
  5. Cook for a few minutes on each side until the cheese is melted and the tortillas are crispy.
  6. Slice into wedges and serve with salsa and sour cream.

3. One-Pot Spinach and Tomato Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes (including the juice) to the pot and bring to a simmer.
  3. Break the spaghetti in half and add it to the pot, ensuring it is submerged in the liquid.
  4. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once the pasta is cooked, add the fresh spinach and stir until wilted.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.

4. Chickpea Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and golden.
  2. Add the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Cook for a minute to release the flavors.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add the rinsed and drained chickpeas to the skillet, stirring to combine.
  5. Simmer for about 10 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Serve the chickpea curry over steamed rice and garnish with fresh cilantro.

5. Caprese Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2-3 slices of fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Layer the fresh mozzarella cheese, tomato slices, and basil leaves on one slice of bread.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the filling.
  5. Top with the other slice of bread and press gently to hold everything together.
  6. Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy!

These vegetarian recipes are not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make, making them perfect for college students who are short on time and budget. Whether you’re craving pasta, a quick quesadilla, a flavorful curry, or a refreshing sandwich, these recipes have got you covered. So, go ahead and give them a try! Your taste buds will thank you.
