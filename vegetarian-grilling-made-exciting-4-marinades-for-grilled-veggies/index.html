Vegetarian Grilling Made Exciting: 4 Marinades for Grilled Veggies


Grilling is a popular method for cooking vegetables, as it brings out their natural sweetness and adds a smoky flavor. However, many people may think that grilling is only suitable for meat, but this is not the case. Vegetarian grilling can be just as delicious and satisfying, and with the right marinades, it can be truly exciting. In this post, we will explore four different marinades that can elevate your grilled vegetable game.

Lemon-Herb Marinade

The first marinade we will discuss is the lemon-herb marinade. This marinade is perfect for vegetables such as zucchini, bell peppers, and eggplant. It is made by combining lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and a variety of herbs such as basil, oregano, and thyme. The acidity of the lemon juice helps to break down the cell walls of the vegetables, making them tender and flavorful.

To make this marinade, simply combine 1/2 cup of lemon juice, 1/4 cup of olive oil, 2 cloves of garlic, minced, and 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh herbs. Mix well and brush the marinade onto the vegetables before grilling.

Balsamic Glaze Marinade

The second marinade we will discuss is the balsamic glaze marinade. This marinade is perfect for vegetables such as portobello mushrooms, asparagus, and Brussels sprouts. It is made by combining balsamic glaze, olive oil, and a variety of spices such as salt, pepper, and paprika. The sweet and tangy flavor of the balsamic glaze adds a depth of flavor to the vegetables.

To make this marinade, simply combine 1/4 cup of balsamic glaze, 1/4 cup of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of salt, 1 tablespoon of black pepper, and 1 tablespoon of paprika. Mix well and brush the marinade onto the vegetables before grilling.

Chimichurri Marinade

The third marinade we will discuss is the chimichurri marinade. This marinade is perfect for vegetables such as bell peppers, zucchini, and onions. It is made by combining parsley, oregano, garlic, red pepper flakes, red wine vinegar, and olive oil. The fresh and herby flavor of the chimichurri marinade adds a bright and refreshing taste to the vegetables.

To make this marinade, simply combine 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh oregano, 2 cloves of garlic, minced, 1 tablespoon of red pepper flakes, 2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar, and 1/4 cup of olive oil. Mix well and brush the marinade onto the vegetables before grilling.

Indian-Style Yogurt Marinade

The fourth marinade we will discuss is the Indian-style yogurt marinade. This marinade is perfect for vegetables such as cauliflower, broccoli, and carrots. It is made by combining plain yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, cumin, coriander, and cayenne pepper. The tangy and creamy flavor of the yogurt marinade adds a unique and exotic taste to the vegetables.

To make this marinade, simply combine 1/2 cup of plain yogurt, 2 tablespoons of lemon juice, 1 teaspoon of garam masala, 1 teaspoon of ground cumin, 1 teaspoon of ground coriander, and 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper. Mix well and brush the marinade onto the vegetables before grilling.

In conclusion, these four marinades offer a variety of flavors and options for vegetarian grilling. Whether you prefer a bright and herby flavor or a tangy and creamy flavor, there is a marinade on this list that is sure to satisfy your taste buds. So next time you fire up the grill, try one of these marinades and experience the excitement of vegetarian grilling.
