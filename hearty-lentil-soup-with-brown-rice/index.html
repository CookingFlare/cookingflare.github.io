Hearty Lentil Soup with Brown Rice


There’s nothing quite like a warm, hearty bowl of soup to comfort the soul, especially when it’s packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with flavor. This Hearty Lentil Soup with Brown Rice is a wholesome and satisfying dish that’s perfect for chilly evenings or anytime you crave a nourishing meal. Packed with plant-based protein, fiber, and a variety of vitamins and minerals, this soup is not only delicious but also incredibly good for you. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this comforting bowl of goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup brown rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Lentils and Rice: In a large pot, heat a little olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery, and sauté for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute.

  2. Add the Broth and Tomatoes: Pour in the vegetable broth and add the diced tomatoes, cumin, smoked paprika, and turmeric. Stir well to combine the ingredients. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20 minutes.

  3. Incorporate the Lentils and Rice: Add the rinsed and drained lentils and brown rice to the pot. Stir gently to combine everything, then cover the pot and let the soup simmer for an additional 30-35 minutes, or until the lentils and rice are tender.

  4. Season and Serve: Once the lentils and rice are cooked through, season the soup with salt and pepper according to your taste. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot.

Nutritional Benefits

This Hearty Lentil Soup with Brown Rice is not only a delicious comfort food, but it’s also incredibly nutritious. Lentils are a fantastic source of plant-based protein, fiber, and various essential nutrients such as iron, folate, and manganese. Brown rice adds a nutty flavor and provides additional fiber and minerals, making this soup a wholesome and filling meal. The combination of vegetables, herbs, and spices further enhances the nutritional profile of this dish, offering a wide array of vitamins and antioxidants.

Serving Suggestions

This soup pairs beautifully with a slice of crusty whole-grain bread or a side salad for a complete and satisfying meal. You can also customize the flavors by adding a dash of hot sauce for a spicy kick or a squeeze of fresh lemon juice for a bright, citrusy note. Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days, and the flavors tend to meld and intensify over time, making the soup even more delicious with each passing day.

Final Thoughts

When it comes to nourishing, wholesome, and comforting meals, this Hearty Lentil Soup with Brown Rice truly stands out. Whether you’re a fan of plant-based cuisine, looking to incorporate more meatless meals into your diet, or simply craving a hearty and flavorful bowl of soup, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. It’s versatile, budget-friendly, and perfect for meal prep, making it an ideal choice for busy weekdays or leisurely weekends. So, gather your ingredients, simmer a pot of this nourishing soup, and savor every spoonful of this delicious, heartwarming creation.
