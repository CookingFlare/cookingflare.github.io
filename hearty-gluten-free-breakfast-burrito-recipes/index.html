Hearty Gluten-Free Breakfast Burrito Recipes


Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and what better way to start your morning than with a hearty, gluten-free breakfast burrito? In this post, we’ll share some delicious and easy-to-make gluten-free breakfast burrito recipes that are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters.

Recipe 1: Classic Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked scrambled eggs
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage (such as chorizo or breakfast sausage)
  • 1/2 cup black beans, cooked and mashed
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large tortillas (gluten-free)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add the bell peppers and onion to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. In a separate bowl, scramble the eggs and set aside.
  4. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  5. Assemble the burritos by spooning the egg mixture, sausage mixture, and black beans onto one half of each tortilla. Fold the tortilla in half to enclose the filling.

Recipe 2: Breakfast Burrito with Avocado and Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked scrambled eggs
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage (such as chorizo or breakfast sausage)
  • 1/2 cup diced black beans
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large tortillas (gluten-free)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add the bell pepper and onion to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. In a separate bowl, scramble the eggs and set aside.
  4. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  5. Assemble the burritos by spooning the egg mixture, sausage mixture, and black beans onto one half of each tortilla. Top with diced avocado and a spoonful of salsa. Fold the tortilla in half to enclose the filling.

Recipe 3: Breakfast Burrito with Spinach and Feta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked scrambled eggs
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage (such as chorizo or breakfast sausage)
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large tortillas (gluten-free)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add the bell pepper to the skillet and cook until tender.
  3. In a separate bowl, scramble the eggs and set aside.
  4. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  5. Assemble the burritos by spooning the egg mixture, sausage mixture, and spinach onto one half of each tortilla. Top with crumbled feta cheese. Fold the tortilla in half to enclose the filling.

Recipe 4: Breakfast Burrito with Sweet Potato and Black Beans

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked scrambled eggs
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage (such as chorizo or breakfast sausage)
  • 1/2 cup cooked and mashed sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup black beans, cooked and mashed
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large tortillas (gluten-free)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add the bell pepper to the skillet and cook until tender.
  3. In a separate bowl, scramble the eggs and set aside.
  4. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  5. Assemble the burritos by spooning the egg mixture, sausage mixture, and sweet potato onto one half of each tortilla. Top with black beans. Fold the tortilla in half to enclose the filling.

These gluten-free breakfast burrito recipes are perfect for a quick and easy breakfast on-the-go. Simply wrap them in a napkin and take them with you to work or school. You can also customize the fillings to your liking, adding your favorite ingredients such as diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, or sliced jalapeños.

Whether you’re looking for a classic breakfast burrito or something a little more adventurous, these recipes are sure to please. So why not give them a try and start your day off right with a delicious gluten-free breakfast burrito?
