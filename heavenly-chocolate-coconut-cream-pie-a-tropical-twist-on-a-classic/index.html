Heavenly Chocolate Coconut Cream Pie: A Tropical Twist on a Classic


If you’re a fan of the classic chocolate cream pie and you’re craving a tropical twist, then this recipe is for you. This Heavenly Chocolate Coconut Cream Pie combines the rich, velvety texture of chocolate cream pie with the exotic flavor of coconut, creating a dessert that’s truly out of this world. The combination of chocolate and coconut is a match made in dessert heaven, and this pie is sure to become a favorite at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, a holiday feast, or simply satisfying your sweet tooth, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted

For the Coconut Cream Filling:

  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup of sweetened shredded coconut
  • ½ cup of granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Cream Filling:

  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • ½ cup of granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 6 ounces of semi-sweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the Whipped Cream Topping:

  • 1 ½ cups of heavy cream
  • ¼ cup of confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

Making the Crust:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the chocolate cookie crumbs and melted butter until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes, then let it cool completely.

Making the Coconut Cream Filling:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, whole milk, and sweetened shredded coconut. Heat over medium heat until it just starts to simmer.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and egg yolks until smooth.
  3. Gradually pour the hot milk mixture into the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the butter and vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the filling into the cooled pie crust and refrigerate while making the chocolate cream filling.

Making the Chocolate Cream Filling:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the whole milk over medium heat until it just starts to simmer.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and egg yolks until smooth.
  3. Gradually pour the hot milk mixture into the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped chocolate, butter, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  6. Pour the chocolate filling over the coconut filling in the pie crust and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until set.

Making the Whipped Cream Topping:

  1. In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream, confectioners’ sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  2. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled pie.
  3. Optional: sprinkle with toasted coconut or chocolate shavings for garnish.

Serving and Enjoying

Slice the Heavenly Chocolate Coconut Cream Pie and serve it to your delighted guests. The creamy coconut layer perfectly complements the rich chocolate layer, creating a dessert that’s decadent, tropical, and simply irresistible. This pie is the perfect ending to any meal and is guaranteed to transport your taste buds to a sunny, tropical paradise. Whether you’re indulging in a slice by yourself or sharing it with friends and family, this pie is sure to be a hit.

So, go ahead and bring a taste of the tropics to your table with this delightful twist on a classic chocolate cream pie. This Heavenly Chocolate Coconut Cream Pie is a showstopper that will leave everyone craving more. Dive into the luscious layers of chocolate and coconut, and savor every blissful bite.
