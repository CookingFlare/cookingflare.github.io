Savory Sausage and Potato Salad with Mustard Dressing


If you’re looking for a hearty and flavorful dish to add to your recipe collection, look no further than this savory sausage and potato salad with mustard dressing. This dish is perfect for a summer barbecue, picnic, or as a satisfying main course. The combination of savory sausages, tender potatoes, and tangy mustard dressing creates a mouthwatering flavor experience that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 pound of your favorite sausages (such as Italian sausage or bratwurst)
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Mustard Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup whole grain mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by boiling the diced potatoes in a pot of salted water until they are tender but still firm, about 10-12 minutes. Once they are cooked, drain the potatoes and set them aside to cool.

  2. While the potatoes are cooking, you can grill the sausages. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and grill the sausages for about 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are fully cooked and have a nice char on the outside. Once they are done, remove them from the grill and let them cool slightly before slicing them into bite-sized pieces.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled diced potatoes, sliced sausages, chopped red onion, and fresh parsley.

  4. To make the mustard dressing, whisk together the whole grain mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, and olive oil in a small bowl until well combined. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Pour the mustard dressing over the potato and sausage mixture, and gently toss everything together until it is well coated in the dressing.

  6. Once everything is mixed together, give the salad a taste and adjust the seasoning if needed with additional salt and pepper.

  7. Allow the salad to chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  8. When you’re ready to serve, give the salad a final toss and garnish with a little extra chopped parsley for a pop of color.

Serving Suggestions

This savory sausage and potato salad with mustard dressing is a versatile dish that can be served in various ways. It makes a delicious side dish to accompany grilled meats or seafood. You can also serve it as a main course for a light and satisfying meal.

This salad is perfect for picnics, potlucks, and barbecues, as it can be made ahead of time and served at room temperature. It’s also a great option for meal prep, as it can be stored in the refrigerator for a few days and enjoyed as a quick and flavorful meal throughout the week.

Conclusion

This savory sausage and potato salad with mustard dressing is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. The smoky, savory sausages pair perfectly with the tender, creamy potatoes, while the tangy mustard dressing adds a zesty kick that brings everything together. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply looking for a delicious and satisfying meal, this recipe is a must-try. Enjoy!
