Autumn Delight: Spiced Apple Cider Mocktail


Autumn is a beautiful and vibrant season that fills our lives with rich colors, flavors, and cozy, comforting moments. In this post, we’ll be exploring an irresistible concoction that will warm your heart and elevate your fall celebrations: Spiced Apple Cider Mocktail. So, grab your ingredients and let’s dive into the world of autumnal treats!

What You’ll Need

  • 6 cups water
  • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup ground ginger
  • 2 tablespoons whole cloves
  • 2 tablespoons whole allspice
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 quarts ginger ale or sparklingwater

How to Make It

Start by combining water, applesauce, lemon juice, and spices in a large pot over medium heat. Stir the mixture thoroughly to blend all the flavors together.

Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer uncovered for about an hour. Stir occasionally to prevent Burning.

After an hour, remove the pot from the heat and let it cool to room temperature. Meanwhile, prepare six glasses and fill them with ice.

Once the spiced apple mixture has cooled, strain the liquid using a fine-mesh strainer. To capture those delicious spices, give the strainer a few gentle shakes, pressing down on the fruit solids to push the liquid through.

Next, pour the spiced apple liquid over ice in glasses, stopping just shy of the brim. Top off each glass with ginger ale or sparkling water, stirring gently until fully mixed.

You could garnish each drink with a cinnamon stick or apple slice for an added touch of elegance. Cheers to a perfect autumn delight!

The Secret to a Perfect Mocktail

The secret to creating the perfect Spiced Apple Cider Mocktail lies in the combination of spices. They infuse the drink with a warm, comforting flavor that resonates with the very essence of autumn. Experiment with the amount and type of spices for your preference, but be wary of overpowering your beverage.

Including whole spices, instead of pre-ground, offers a deeper, more pronounced flavor. It also allows you to remove them before serving, ensuring a strain-free drinking experience. Remember to toast your spices gently in the pot before adding water and other ingredients. This step releases the oils locked inside the spices which vastly improves the final flavor of your drink.

Making It Your Own

Feel free to adjust the recipe to suit your preferences. Here are a few suggestions for customization:

  • Try adding a splash of vanilla extract for an extra layer of flavor.
  • For a more potent flavor, infuse the mixture with a shot of rum or brandy.
  • Warm up your Autumn Delight by serving it hot. Simply reheat the spiced apple mixture and serve alongside a Mugwort Whiskey Sour or Mulled Wine.

Autumn Delight Spiced Apple Cider Mocktail is a tasteful and cheerful drink that leaves you with a sense of accomplishment. It’s a wonderful way to celebrate the season and delight in the simple pleasures of the autumn harvest. Happy cooking, and enjoy this delightful concoction as you bring the vibrant season of autumn into your home.
