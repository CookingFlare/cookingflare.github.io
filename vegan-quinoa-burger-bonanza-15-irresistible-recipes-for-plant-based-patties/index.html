Vegan Quinoa Burger Bonanza: 15 Irresistible Recipes for Plant-Based Patties


Burgers have long been a staple of the American diet, with juicy beef patties and melted cheese atop freshly-baked buns being a given. But with the growing interest in plant-based alternatives and a focus on overall health, the vegan quinoa burger has become a popular choice for those looking to make a shift in their eating habits. This rather humble grain, packed with protein, fiber, and a myriad of vitamins and minerals, brings a unique texture and a nutty flavor to these patties that can’t be replicated. Today, we’re bringing you 15 irresistible vegan quinoa burger recipes for you to dive into on your culinary adventures.

  1. Quinoa Chickpea Veggie Burger

The first of our vegan quinoa burger recipes is quite the chameleon—packed with plenty of chickpeas and mixed vegetables, it’s fully customizable to your taste buds’ desires. Form the patties and cook them gently in coconut oil to retain the natural flavors and maintain their texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions
  • 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 2 tbsp whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

2. Quinoa Black Bean Burgers

Black beans and quinoa are magic together. Their flavors and textures complement each other so well, that it’s no surprise that these patties are elevating the standard grocery store frozen burger.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, cumin, and chili powder to taste

3. Cumin-Lime Quinoa and Veggie Burgers

How about some Southwestern flair in your burger patties? This version melds quinoa with plenty of colorful vegetables and zesty flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • Salt to taste

4. Quinoa and Kale Bacon Burger

Let’s combine two seemingly unrelated ingredients—kale and bacon—with this next burger recipe! You’ll be intrigued by the fusion of textures and spinach’s earthiness giving it an edge.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup chopped kale
  • 4 pieces of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • Salt, garlic powder, and pepper to taste

5. Quinoa and Lentil Veggie Burger

Lentils are incredibly nutritious and offer a robust, earthy taste that complements the quinoa well. This burger combines the protein power of lentils and quinoa with falafel-inspired herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup cooked lentils
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Salt, cumin, and coriander to taste

6. Quinoa and Avocado Burger

The buttery richness of avocado paired with quinoa creates a delectably creamy and satisfying patty. To elevate this burger, add your favorite toppings such as sliced radish, red onion, lettuce, and tomato.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup chopped chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

7. Breakfast Quinoa Porridge Burger

Getting creative with breakfast? This recipe blends quinoa porridge with Beyond Meat crumbles and your favorite savory toppings for a bold, protein-packed morning treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa porridge
  • 1 cup Beyond Meat crumbles
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

8. Herb and Garlic Quinoa Burgers

Tired of the same old flavors? This combination brings the pungent flavors of parsley, cilantro, and garlic for a delectable alternative.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Quinoa Mushroom Burger

Mushrooms, when cooked down, have an umami flavor that pairs wonderfully with fresh thyme and porcini powder. These earthy flavors mingle with the quinoa, making a memorable burger.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup chopped mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tbsp porcini powder
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, black pepper, and dried thyme to taste
  1. Spicy African Quinoa Veggie Burger

Experience a taste of Africa with bold spices such as smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, and curry powder. This unique burger is sure to bring a kick to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

11. Quinoa and Pumpkin Seed Burger

A vegan burger doesn’t have to be heavy; this recipe with quinoa and pumpkin seeds offers a light and crunchy texture that is still protein-packed.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tbsp water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

12. Quinoa and Chia Seed Burger

Chia seeds have exploded in popularity due to their huge nutritional value. Add them to this burger for a pop of crunch and extra fiber.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 2 tbsp ground chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

13. Quinoa Chickpea Fritter Burger

Embrace a lighter version of a traditional veggie burger with chickpeas and quinoa combined in a fritter form—perfect for warmer days.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onions
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup water or chickpea brine, as needed
  • Salt, garlic powder, and cumin to taste

14. Chestnut and Quinoa Burger

Sweet and earthy chestnuts mix with quinoa for a burger that surprises and delights! Add a dollop of tangy cranberry sauce, for an extra burst of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can chestnuts, drained and chopped
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

15. Quinoa and Smoked Walnut Burger

Lastly, combine smoky, crunchy walnuts with quinoa, and the result is a surprisingly gourmet burger. Make extra patties, as they freeze beautifully for future meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chopped smoked walnuts
  • 1/2 cup crushed crackers
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, smoked paprika, and garlic powder to taste

Now that you have these mouth-watering vegan quinoa burger recipes, go ahead and experiment with your preferred toppings and side dishes. We are excited to hear about your culinary triumphs or any tweaks you add to make these patties even more personal to your taste buds. Enjoy!
