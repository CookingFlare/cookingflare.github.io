Zesty Orange Pound Cake: A Burst of Citrus Flavor


Are you ready to tantalize your taste buds with a burst of citrusy goodness? Look no further than this delightful Zesty Orange Pound Cake recipe! This cake is a perfect blend of tangy oranges and sweet cake, creating a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re a baking enthusiast or just looking to impress your friends and family with a delectable dessert, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • Zest of 2 oranges
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons fresh orange juice
  • Zest of 1 orange

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract and orange zest.

  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients alternately with the Greek yogurt and orange juice.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 50-55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. While the cake is baking, prepare the glaze by whisking together the confectioners’ sugar, fresh orange juice, and orange zest until smooth.

  8. Once the cake is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer the cake to a wire rack placed over a baking sheet.

  9. Drizzle the orange glaze over the warm cake, allowing it to soak in and create a sweet, citrusy crust.

  10. Allow the cake to cool completely before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Zesty Orange Pound Cake is a delightful treat on its own, but you can take it to the next level with a few simple serving suggestions.

  • Serve slices of the cake with a dollop of freshly whipped cream and a sprinkle of additional orange zest for an elegant presentation.
  • Pair a slice of this cake with a cup of hot tea or coffee for a delightful afternoon treat.
  • For a decadent dessert, serve the cake alongside a scoop of vanilla ice cream and a drizzle of caramel sauce.

Conclusion

The Zesty Orange Pound Cake is a delightful dessert that brings a burst of citrus flavor to any occasion. It’s a versatile treat that can be enjoyed with a cup of tea in the afternoon or dressed up for an elegant dinner party. The bright, tangy notes of fresh orange combined with the moist, buttery cake create a truly irresistible combination. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to bake your way to citrusy bliss with this delightful recipe!
