Delicious Low Carb Portobello Mushroom Pizzas


Are you craving pizza but trying to stick to a low-carb diet? Look no further! These delicious low carb portobello mushroom pizzas are the perfect solution. They are easy to make, packed with flavor, and a healthier alternative to traditional pizza. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to enjoy a guilt-free pizza night!

Ingredients

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: sliced bell peppers, onions, olives, or your favorite pizza toppings

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with olive oil.

  2. Prepare the Portobello Mushrooms: Gently clean the portobello mushrooms with a damp cloth to remove any dirt. Carefully remove the stems from the mushrooms and use a spoon to scrape out the gills, creating a hollow space for the pizza toppings.

  3. Brush with Olive Oil: Place the prepared portobello mushrooms on the baking sheet, cap side down. Brush the tops of the mushrooms with olive oil and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  4. Add the Toppings: Spoon marinara sauce into each mushroom cap, spreading it evenly. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by the grated parmesan cheese. Add the sliced cherry tomatoes on top of the cheese, and finish with a sprinkle of fresh basil.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Place the assembled portobello mushroom pizzas in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the mushrooms are tender.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once ready, remove the portobello mushroom pizzas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Carefully transfer them to a serving plate, slice if desired, and garnish with additional fresh basil.

Tips and Variations

  • Low-Carb Crust Alternative: If you prefer a thicker crust, you can add a layer of almond flour or crushed pork rinds before adding the toppings.

  • Customize Your Toppings: Feel free to get creative with your toppings. Add some sliced bell peppers, onions, olives, or any other low-carb vegetables you enjoy.

  • Grilled Option: These portobello mushroom pizzas can also be prepared on the grill for a smoky flavor. Simply grill the mushrooms for a few minutes on each side before adding the toppings, then continue cooking until the cheese is melted.

  • Make It a Meal: Pair these delicious low carb portobello mushroom pizzas with a fresh green salad or some roasted vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal.

Enjoy this low-carb twist on a classic favorite and indulge in the flavors of pizza without the guilt. Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking for a tasty and nutritious meal, these portobello mushroom pizzas are sure to become a new go-to recipe. Happy cooking and bon appétit!
