Homemade Biscuits and Gravy: A Southern Breakfast Staple


When it comes to comforting, hearty breakfasts, there’s nothing quite like homemade biscuits and gravy. This classic Southern dish has been satisfying hungry bellies for generations, and for good reason. The warm, flaky biscuits paired with the rich, creamy gravy create a flavor combination that is hard to resist. If you’re looking to bring a taste of the South to your breakfast table, look no further. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of making homemade biscuits and gravy that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the biscuits:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 3/4 cup milk

For the gravy:

  • 1 pound breakfast sausage
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Biscuits

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the bowl. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Slowly pour in the milk, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough biscuits.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together.
  6. Roll out the dough to a thickness of about 1 inch. Using a biscuit cutter or a glass, cut out biscuits and place them on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the biscuits are golden brown and cooked through.

Step 2: Make the Gravy

  1. In a large skillet, cook the breakfast sausage over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Use a wooden spoon to break up the sausage into smaller pieces.
  2. Sprinkle the flour over the cooked sausage and stir to coat. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, allowing the flour to cook and absorb the sausage fat.
  3. Slowly pour in the milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Continue cooking and stirring until the gravy thickens to your desired consistency. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Season the gravy with salt and pepper, adjusting to taste.

Step 3: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Split the warm biscuits in half and place them on individual plates.
  2. Ladle the creamy sausage gravy over each biscuit, making sure to cover them generously.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy the deliciousness of homemade biscuits and gravy.

Conclusion

There you have it, a simple yet satisfying recipe for homemade biscuits and gravy. Whether you’re looking to start your day off right or indulge in a comforting brunch, this Southern breakfast staple is sure to hit the spot. The buttery, flaky biscuits perfectly complement the creamy, savory gravy, creating a harmonious dish that will leave your taste buds begging for more. So, why not give it a try and experience the true taste of the South? Your breakfast table will thank you.
