Creamy Broccoli Cheddar Soup Recipes for Cheese Lovers


If you’re a cheese lover, then you’re in for a treat with these delightful and creamy broccoli cheddar soup recipes. Packed with the comforting flavors of cheddar cheese and the wholesome goodness of broccoli, these recipes are perfect for warming you up on a chilly day or satisfying your craving for a rich and indulgent meal. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, these recipes are sure to become a staple in your cooking repertoire.

Classic Creamy Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.
  2. Sprinkle the flour over the onion and garlic, and stir to create a roux. Cook the roux for a few minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste.
  3. Gradually whisk in the chicken or vegetable broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Add the broccoli florets to the pot and simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.
  5. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  6. Return the soup to the pot over low heat, and stir in the milk and shredded cheddar cheese until the cheese is melted and the soup is creamy.
  7. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste, and serve hot.

Extra-Cheesy Broccoli Cheddar Soup with Bacon

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Gouda cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until it’s crispy. Remove the bacon from the pot and set it aside, leaving the bacon drippings in the pot.
  2. Add the butter to the pot with the bacon drippings and melt it over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the onion and garlic, and stir to create a roux. Cook the roux for a few minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste.
  4. Gradually whisk in the chicken or vegetable broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  5. Add the broccoli florets to the pot and simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.
  6. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  7. Return the soup to the pot over low heat, and stir in the milk, shredded cheddar cheese, and shredded Gouda cheese until the cheese is melted and the soup is creamy.
  8. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste, and serve hot, topped with the crispy bacon.

These creamy broccoli cheddar soup recipes are a celebration of all things cheesy and comforting. Whether you opt for the classic version or the extra-cheesy variation with bacon, you’re in for a delightful culinary experience. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the creamy, cheesy goodness of these delectable soup recipes.
