The Ultimate BBQ Bacon Cheeseburger Recipe


When it comes to crafting the perfect burger, few things come close to the irresistible combination of smoky BBQ sauce, crispy bacon, and juicy burger patties topped with melted cheese. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving a rich and flavorful meal, a BBQ bacon cheeseburger is guaranteed to satisfy every bite.

This recipe for the ultimate BBQ bacon cheeseburger will elevate your burger-making game, featuring layers of bold flavors, the right balance of textures, and a step-by-step guide to ensure you achieve the perfect burger every time. Let’s dig right in and create a masterpiece worthy of your grill!

Ingredients List

For the Burger Patties:

  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef – Choose 80% lean, 20% fat for maximum juiciness and flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

For the Toppings:

  • 6 slices crispy bacon – Cooked to perfection and ready to add crunch.
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese – For a gooey, melted layer that’s packed with flavor.
  • 1/3 cup BBQ sauce – Choose a smoky or tangy variety for depth.
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 large tomato, sliced into rounds
  • Leaves of fresh romaine or iceberg lettuce

For the Buns:

  • 4 brioche burger buns – Toasted lightly for added texture.
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter – Softened for spreading.

Optional Additions:

  • Pickles or jalapeños – For an extra zing.
  • Avocado slices – For creaminess.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Prepare the Bacon

Begin by cooking your bacon strips until crispy. Heat a skillet over medium heat and place the bacon slices evenly in the pan. Cook for 4-6 minutes, flipping occasionally. Once fully cooked, transfer the strips to a plate lined with paper towels to soak up any excess grease. Crispy bacon will provide the perfect crunch that makes your burger unforgettable!

2. Make the Burger Patties

To create juicy, flavorful burger patties, mix the ground beef with garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Be careful not to overmix – doing so can make your patties tough. Divide the mixture into four equal parts, and shape them into patties about ¾-inch thick. Set them aside on a tray.

3. Toast the Burger Buns

Toast burger buns lightly for added texture and flavor. Spread a thin layer of softened butter on the cut sides of the brioche buns. Heat a skillet or griddle on medium-high heat and place the buns cut-side down for 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown and slightly crisp. Set the buns aside.

4. Grill the Patties

Fire up your grill or heat a cast-iron skillet on medium-high. Once hot, add the patties and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium doneness. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of each patty and cover with a lid or foil. The cheese will melt beautifully over the meat, creating a luscious layer that ties the flavors together.

5. Assemble the Burgers

Now comes the fun part—assembling your masterpiece! Start by placing the bottom half of the toasted bun on your plate. Add a leaf of lettuce to provide a fresh, crispy base. Next, layer on a juicy tomato slice followed by your cheesy burger patty. Drizzle a generous amount of BBQ sauce over the patty before placing two strips of crispy bacon on top. Add thin slices of red onion for a bit of sharpness, and if desired, include pickles or jalapeños for an extra kick of flavor. Crown your creation with the top half of the bun.

6. Serve and Enjoy

Your ultimate BBQ bacon cheeseburger is now ready to devour! Pair it with classic sides like potato wedges, coleslaw, or a fresh green salad. Add a cold drink to the mix—whether it’s iced tea, an ice-cold beer, or a refreshing soda—and enjoy your restaurant-worthy creation, right from the comfort of your home.

Tips and Tricks for Perfect Burgers

Choosing the Right Beef

The key to a juicy burger is the fat content in your ground beef. An 80/20 ratio (80% lean meat, 20% fat) strikes the perfect balance between tenderness and flavor. If possible, opt for freshly ground beef for the best results.

Don’t Press Your Burgers!

It might be tempting to press down on your patties while cooking to “speed things up,” but this is a surefire way to squeeze out the juices that keep your burgers moist. Let your patties cook undisturbed for the best texture.

Customize Your BBQ Sauce

The BBQ sauce is the soul of your bacon cheeseburger, so feel free to experiment with flavors. A smoky sauce works wonders with grilled patties, while a tangy sauce adds a welcomed contrast to the richness of the cheese and bacon. If you’re feeling ambitious, try making your own sauce with ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and a dash of liquid smoke!

Toasting Makes a Difference

Don’t underestimate the power of a toasted bun! The slight char not only enhances the flavor but also prevents the bun from getting soggy when layered with juicy toppings.

Preheat Your Grill Properly

If you’re using a grill, make sure to preheat it thoroughly to ensure even cooking. A hot grill creates a nice sear on the outside of your patty while keeping the inside juicy.

Variations to Try

Though the classic BBQ bacon cheeseburger is hard to beat, there’s plenty of room for creativity! Here are a few variations you can experiment with:

  • The Spicy BBQ Burger: Add slices of jalapeño or a spicy BBQ sauce to give your burger a fiery kick.
  • The Cowboy Burger: Include crispy fried onions and swap cheddar for Monterey Jack cheese.
  • The Gourmet Twist: Use arugula instead of lettuce and caramelized onions for a sophisticated flavor profile.
  • The Breakfast Burger: Add a fried egg on top and use maple-glazed bacon for a morning-inspired treat.

Wrapping It Up

Few meals capture the essence of comfort food and indulgence as perfectly as a BBQ bacon cheeseburger. This ultimate recipe incorporates all the right elements—juicy beef, smoky sauce, crisp bacon, and creamy melted cheese—for a timeless burger that’s guaranteed to impress.

Perfect for grilling season or a casual weeknight meal, this BBQ bacon cheeseburger delivers a fusion of flavors that will leave everyone wanting seconds. Follow this recipe, and you’ll elevate your burger-making skills while enjoying a dish that’s hearty, flavorful, and downright irresistible. So fire up your grill, grab your favorite toppings, and get ready to savor every bite!
