10 Delicious Picnic Sandwiches Perfect for Summer


Summer is the perfect season for picnics, and what better way to enjoy a sunny day outdoors than with a selection of delicious sandwiches? Whether youâ€™re heading to the park, the beach, or simply enjoying your backyard, picnic sandwiches are easy to prepare, transport, and devour. Below are ten delightful sandwich recipes that will elevate your summer picnics, each featuring fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors. Let’s dive into these mouthwatering creations!

1. Classic Caprese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Ciabatta bread
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Ripe tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Slice the ciabatta bread horizontally and drizzle both sides with olive oil.
  2. Layer fresh mozzarella slices on the bottom half, followed by slices of ripe tomatoes.
  3. Add fresh basil leaves on top, then drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Close the sandwich and cut into manageable portions. This sandwich is best enjoyed fresh but can be wrapped tightly for a picnic.

2. Turkey and Avocado Club

Ingredients:

  • Whole grain bread
  • Sliced turkey breast
  • Avocado, mashed
  • Crisp lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. Toast the whole grain bread lightly for added crunch.
  2. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice.
  3. Layer mashed avocado, sliced turkey, lettuce, tomato, and crumbled bacon on one slice.
  4. Top with another slice of bread and repeat the layering for a double-decker effect.
  5. Finish with a final slice of bread, secure with toothpicks, and cut into quarters for easy handling.

3. Mediterranean Veggie Pita

Ingredients:

  • Pita bread
  • Hummus
  • Cucumber, diced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Kalamata olives, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Cut the pita bread in half to create pockets.
  2. Spread a generous amount of hummus inside each pita half.
  3. Fill with diced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, olives, red onion, and crumbled feta.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley for a burst of flavor.
  5. Serve chilled or at room temperature for a refreshing summer treat.

4. BBQ Chicken Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Shredded rotisserie chicken
  • BBQ sauce
  • Celery, chopped
  • Green onions, sliced
  • Greek yogurt or mayonnaise
  • Whole wheat sandwich rolls
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine shredded chicken, BBQ sauce, chopped celery, green onions, and Greek yogurt (or mayonnaise) until well mixed.
  2. Slice the whole wheat rolls and layer lettuce on the bottom half.
  3. Scoop the BBQ chicken salad generously onto the lettuce.
  4. Top with the other half of the roll and wrap for a picnic-ready meal.

5. Tuna Salad Sandwich with a Twist

Ingredients:

  • Canned tuna, drained
  • Greek yogurt
  • Dijon mustard
  • Pickles, diced
  • Celery, finely chopped
  • Whole grain bread
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix drained tuna, Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, diced pickles, and chopped celery until combined.
  2. Spread the mixture onto slices of whole grain bread.
  3. Add a layer of lettuce leaves and close the sandwich.
  4. Cut into triangles and pack for your picnic adventure.

6. Spicy Chickpea Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • Sriracha sauce
  • Avocado, mashed
  • Lime juice
  • Cilantro, chopped
  • Whole grain bread

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mash chickpeas with a fork and mix in Sriracha, mashed avocado, lime juice, and cilantro.
  2. Spread the spicy chickpea mixture onto slices of whole grain bread.
  3. Top with additional cilantro if desired and close the sandwich.
  4. This sandwich is not only spicy but also packed with protein, perfect for an energizing picnic.

7. Classic BLT Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Bacon, cooked
  • Crisp lettuce
  • Ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • Mayonnaise
  • White or whole grain bread

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice.
  3. Layer crispy bacon, lettuce, and tomato slices on one slice of bread.
  4. Top with the other slice and cut in half for easy handling.
  5. This timeless sandwich is a hit for any summer gathering.

8. Pesto Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Pesto sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Fresh spinach
  • Focaccia bread

Instructions:

  1. Slice the focaccia bread horizontally and spread pesto on both sides.
  2. Layer sliced grilled chicken, fresh mozzarella, roasted red peppers, and spinach on the bottom half.
  3. Close the sandwich and press down lightly.
  4. For a warm option, wrap in foil and heat on a grill for a few minutes before serving.

9. Hummus and Roasted Vegetable Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat wrap
  • Hummus
  • Zucchini, sliced and roasted
  • Bell peppers, sliced and roasted
  • Spinach
  • Feta cheese, crumbled

Instructions:

  1. Spread hummus evenly over the entire surface of the whole wheat wrap.
  2. Layer roasted zucchini, bell peppers, spinach, and crumbled feta cheese.
  3. Roll the wrap tightly and slice it in half for easy eating.
  4. This wrap can be made in advance and stored in the fridge until picnic time.

10. Sweet and Savory Fig Jam and Goat Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Sourdough bread
  • Fig jam
  • Goat cheese, softened
  • Arugula
  • Prosciutto (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread fig jam on one slice of sourdough bread and goat cheese on the other.
  2. Layer fresh arugula and prosciutto if using in between the slices.
  3. Close the sandwich and press down gently.
  4. Cut into halves or quarters for sharing and enjoy the unique sweet and savory combination.

Tips for Packing and Enjoying Your Picnic Sandwiches

  • Choose the Right Bread: Opt for sturdy breads like ciabatta, whole grain, or focaccia that can handle fillings without getting soggy.
  • Wrap It Up: Use parchment paper or wax paper to wrap your sandwiches, making them easy to transport and eat.
  • Keep It Cool: Use a cooler bag with ice packs to keep your sandwiches fresh and prevent spoilage, especially on hot summer days.
  • Add Fresh Fruits and Veggies: Donâ€™t forget to pack some fresh fruits or veggie sticks alongside your sandwiches for a well-rounded picnic meal.
  • Stay Hydrated: Bring along refreshing drinks to complement your delicious sandwiches, such as iced tea, lemonade, or sparkling water.

These ten picnic sandwich ideas are sure to impress your friends and family this summer. Each recipe brings a unique twist to classic flavors while ensuring ease of preparation and enjoyment outdoors. So, gather your ingredients, pack your picnic basket, and make the most of the warm summer days ahead with these scrumptious sandwiches!
