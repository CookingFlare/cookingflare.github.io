Delicious Pumpkin Bread with Cream Cheese


Are you ready to elevate your baking game with a mouthwatering treat that combines the rich flavors of pumpkin and the creamy goodness of cream cheese? Look no further because we have the perfect recipe for you! This Delicious Pumpkin Bread with Cream Cheese is sure to become a favorite in your household, whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply craving a cozy and indulgent snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon cloves
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, until well incorporated. Stir in the pumpkin puree.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in the chopped walnuts if desired.

  6. In a small bowl, beat together the cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  7. Pour half of the pumpkin bread batter into the prepared loaf pan. Spread the cream cheese mixture on top. Cover with the remaining pumpkin bread batter.

  8. Swirl a knife through the batter to create a marbled effect.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  10. Cool the pumpkin bread in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Enjoy a slice of this Delicious Pumpkin Bread with Cream Cheese with a hot cup of coffee or a steaming mug of spiced chai for the ultimate fall indulgence.

  • Top slices with a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of cinnamon for an extra special touch.

  • Wrap individual slices in plastic wrap and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days, or freeze for longer storage.

Conclusion:

Baking this Delicious Pumpkin Bread with Cream Cheese is a delightful way to embrace the flavors of the season and treat yourself to a homemade delicacy that is sure to impress. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is perfect for creating a delicious and comforting treat that will have your friends and family coming back for more. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to savor the warm, cozy goodness of this irresistible pumpkin bread. Happy baking!

Remember, patience and precision are key to baking success, so take your time and enjoy the process. Happy baking and indulging in this scrumptious treat!
