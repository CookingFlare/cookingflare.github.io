Vegan BBQ Chickpea Wraps: Tangy and Flavorful Plant-Based Lunch


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying plant-based lunch option, these Vegan BBQ Chickpea Wraps are sure to hit the spot. Packed with protein, fiber, and flavor, these wraps are perfect for a quick and easy meal that doesn’t skimp on taste. Whether you’re a committed vegan, a curious flexitarian, or just someone looking to add more plant-based meals to your diet, these wraps are a fantastic choice.

Ingredients

For the BBQ Chickpeas:

  • 2 cans of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Wraps:

  • Whole wheat or gluten-free tortillas
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Sliced red onions
  • Sliced avocado
  • Vegan ranch dressing or tahini sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a large bowl, toss the chickpeas with the olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until they are well coated.

  3. Spread the seasoned chickpeas in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are slightly crispy.

  4. While the chickpeas are roasting, warm the BBQ sauce in a small saucepan over low heat.

  5. Once the chickpeas are done, transfer them to a large bowl and pour the warm BBQ sauce over them. Gently toss the chickpeas until they are evenly coated with the sauce.

  6. To assemble the wraps, lay out the tortillas and place a handful of fresh spinach leaves in the center of each one.

  7. Spoon a generous portion of the BBQ chickpeas onto the spinach, then top with sliced red onions and avocado.

  8. Drizzle with vegan ranch dressing or tahini sauce, if desired.

  9. Fold the sides of the tortillas over the filling, then roll them up tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.

  10. Cut the wraps in half and serve immediately, or wrap them tightly in foil for an on-the-go meal.

Why You’ll Love These Wraps

The combination of the smoky, tangy BBQ chickpeas with the creamy avocado, crisp spinach, and zesty red onions creates a flavor explosion that will satisfy your taste buds and leave you feeling nourished. These wraps are not only delicious but also incredibly versatile. Feel free to customize them with your favorite veggies, add a sprinkle of vegan cheese, or swap the BBQ sauce for a spicy sriracha or a sweet teriyaki glaze.

Nutritional Benefits

Chickpeas are an excellent source of plant-based protein and fiber, making them a filling and satisfying addition to any meal. They are also rich in vitamins and minerals, including folate, iron, and manganese. Avocado adds healthy monounsaturated fats, while spinach provides a boost of vitamins A and K. This meal is not only tasty but also packed with nutrients that will keep you energized throughout the day.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan BBQ Chickpea Wraps are proof that plant-based eating can be easy, delicious, and satisfying. Whether you’re preparing a quick lunch for yourself or serving a crowd, these wraps are a fantastic choice. They are also perfect for meal prepping, as the BBQ chickpeas can be made ahead of time and stored in the fridge for up to a week. So, give these wraps a try and enjoy a flavorful, tangy, and entirely plant-based meal that will leave you feeling nourished and satisfied.
