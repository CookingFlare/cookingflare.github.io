Easy Cold Appetizers: 10 No-Cook Recipes for Hot Days


Summer is here, and the heat is unbearable. But that doesn’t mean you have to sacrifice the fun of entertaining friends and family. In fact, hot summer days are the perfect excuse to serve refreshing and light cold appetizers that will keep your guests cool and satisfied. And the best part? You don’t have to spend hours in the kitchen cooking up a storm. Here are 10 no-cook cold appetizer recipes that are easy to prepare and perfect for hot summer days.

1. Tomato and Mozzarella Salad

This classic Italian salad is a staple for a reason. Fresh tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and a drizzle of olive oil and basil come together to create a simple yet elegant appetizer. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella into thin rounds, place them on a platter, and drizzle with olive oil and basil. You can also add a sprinkle of salt and pepper to taste.

2. Cucumber and Feta Dip

This refreshing dip is perfect for hot summer days. Simply combine diced cucumber, crumbled feta cheese, and a squeeze of lemon juice in a bowl. Serve with pita chips or crackers for a crunchy snack.

3. Smoked Salmon Pâté

Smoked salmon is a staple in many Scandinavian countries, and for good reason. It’s rich, creamy, and pairs perfectly with crackers or toast. Simply combine smoked salmon, cream cheese, lemon juice, and chopped fresh dill in a bowl. Serve with crackers or toast points for a sophisticated appetizer.

4. Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Salad

This sweet and savory salad is perfect for summer. Simply combine sliced grilled peaches, prosciutto, arugula, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze in a bowl. The sweetness of the peaches pairs perfectly with the salty prosciutto and tangy arugula.

5. Spicy Mango Salsa

This spicy and sweet salsa is perfect for topping tacos or grilled meats. Simply combine diced mango, red onion, jalapeño pepper, cilantro, and lime juice in a bowl. Serve with tortilla chips or use as a topping for grilled meats.

6. Caprese Skewers

These colorful skewers are perfect for a summer party. Simply alternate cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, and fresh basil leaves on skewers. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze for a pop of color and flavor.

7. Tzatziki Sauce

This Greek yogurt-based sauce is perfect for hot summer days. Simply combine Greek yogurt, diced cucumber, garlic, lemon juice, and dill in a bowl. Serve with pita chips or as a dip for vegetables.

8. Avocado and Sprout Bruschetta

This creamy and crunchy bruschetta is perfect for a summer party. Simply mash ripe avocados and mix with chopped sprouts, lemon juice, and salt. Spread on toasted bread and top with cherry tomatoes for a delicious appetizer.

9. Roasted Red Pepper Hummus

This creamy and flavorful hummus is perfect for dipping vegetables or pita chips. Simply combine roasted red peppers, chickpeas, tahini, garlic, and lemon juice in a bowl. Serve with pita chips or vegetables for a healthy snack.

10. Watermelon and Feta Salad

This refreshing salad is perfect for hot summer days. Simply combine diced watermelon, crumbled feta cheese, mint, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze in a bowl. The sweetness of the watermelon pairs perfectly with the salty feta and tangy mint.

There you have it - 10 no-cook cold appetizer recipes that are perfect for hot summer days. Whether you’re hosting a summer party or just want a quick and easy snack, these recipes are sure to impress. So go ahead, get creative, and enjoy the warm weather with your favorite cold appetizers!
