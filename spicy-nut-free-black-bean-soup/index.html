Spicy Nut-Free Black Bean Soup


Are you looking for a hearty and flavorful soup that’s not only delicious but also nut-free? Look no further! This Spicy Nut-Free Black Bean Soup recipe is the perfect blend of bold spices, creamy black beans, and vibrant vegetables, creating a dish that’s both satisfying and nutritious. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering creation.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and red bell pepper. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are soft and fragrant.

  2. Spice it Up: Stir in the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper. Allow the spices to toast for a minute to release their flavors, creating a rich and aromatic base for the soup.

  3. Bring it Together: Add the black beans, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, and tomato paste to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Simmer and Blend: Bring the soup to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until it reaches your desired consistency. If you prefer a chunkier soup, you can skip this step or blend only a portion of the soup.

  5. Taste and Adjust: Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning as needed. You can add more cayenne pepper for extra heat or a squeeze of lime juice for a burst of freshness.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with lime wedges on the side for a zesty finish. This soup pairs wonderfully with crusty bread, tortilla chips, or a dollop of sour cream for added creaminess.

Nutritional Benefits

This Spicy Nut-Free Black Bean Soup is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a boon for your health. Black beans are a fantastic source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. They can help regulate blood sugar levels, improve digestion, and contribute to heart health. Additionally, the vibrant array of vegetables in this soup provides a spectrum of vitamins and minerals, making it a wholesome and nourishing dish for the whole family.

Final Thoughts

With its robust flavors and wholesome ingredients, this Spicy Nut-Free Black Bean Soup is a versatile addition to your recipe repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a cozy meal at home, this soup is bound to become a staple in your kitchen. Feel free to customize the spice level and add your favorite toppings to make it your own. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let the aromas of this delightful soup fill your kitchen with warmth and comfort. Cheers to good food and happy cooking!
