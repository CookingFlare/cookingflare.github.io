Delicious Instant Pot Pumpkin Cheesecake Recipes for Fall


Are you ready to indulge in the flavors of fall? Look no further than these delicious Instant Pot Pumpkin Cheesecake recipes. With their creamy texture, rich pumpkin flavor, and the convenience of the Instant Pot, these desserts are perfect for any autumn gathering or just a cozy night at home.

Classic Pumpkin Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 packages (24 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • Whipped cream, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan to form the crust.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add pumpkin puree, eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. Mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  4. Place a trivet in the Instant Pot and add 1 cup of water. Carefully lower the springform pan onto the trivet.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 35 minutes on high pressure.
  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.
  7. Carefully remove the cheesecake from the Instant Pot and let it cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Serve chilled, garnished with whipped cream.

Pumpkin Swirl Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gingersnap cookie crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 packages (24 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine gingersnap cookie crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan to form the crust.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add pumpkin puree, eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. Mix well.
  3. Pour 2/3 of the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  4. Add 1/3 cup of the remaining cream cheese mixture to a separate bowl and mix in 2 tablespoons of pumpkin puree.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of the pumpkin mixture onto the cream cheese mixture in the pan. Use a knife to swirl the two mixtures together.
  6. Place a trivet in the Instant Pot and add 1 cup of water. Carefully lower the springform pan onto the trivet.
  7. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 35 minutes on high pressure.
  8. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.
  9. Carefully remove the cheesecake from the Instant Pot and let it cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  10. Serve chilled and enjoy the beautiful swirls of pumpkin and cream cheese.

Pumpkin Pecan Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 3 packages (24 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans, for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, chopped pecans, and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan to form the crust.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add pumpkin puree, eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. Mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  4. Place a trivet in the Instant Pot and add 1 cup of water. Carefully lower the springform pan onto the trivet.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 35 minutes on high pressure.
  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.
  7. Carefully remove the cheesecake from the Instant Pot and let it cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Before serving, sprinkle the top of the cheesecake with chopped pecans for added crunch and flavor.

Whether you’re a fan of classic pumpkin cheesecake, love the swirls of pumpkin and cream cheese, or enjoy the added crunch of pecans, these Instant Pot Pumpkin Cheesecake recipes will satisfy your fall cravings. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can enjoy these delicious desserts in no time. So gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor the flavors of the season!
