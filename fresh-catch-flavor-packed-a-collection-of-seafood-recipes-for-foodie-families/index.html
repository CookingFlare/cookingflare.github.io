The Essence of Seafood

Before we delve into the delicious dishes, let’s first learn what makes seafood such a delightful and essential food group. 1. Grilled Lemongrass Shrimp Skewers

Nothing screams summer like a barbeque party and a sizzling platter of Grilled Lemongrass Shrimp Skewers. These delightful morsels are zesty, aromatic, and packed with intense flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 lemongrass stalks, outer layers removed, minced
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the shrimp, minced lemongrass, lime juice, fish sauce, oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate for 30 minutes, making sure to refrigerate.
  3. Thread the shrimp onto the skewers, being cautious not to overcrowd.
  4. Cook your skewers either on the grill or under the broiler for about 2 to 3 minutes on each side, until they just turn pink.
  5. Serve with a tangy dipping sauce of your choice and refreshed mint leaves.

2. Spaghetti with Garlic-Parmesan Crab

If you’re passionate about pasta meets seafood, Spaghetti with Garlic-Parmesan Crab will be your new favorite go-to dish. Creamy, decadent, and packing a crabby punch, it won’t take long for your family to fall in love.

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cups fresh crabmeat
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and add red pepper flakes and garlic. Cook until aromatic, about a minute.
  3. Add the milk and Parmesan cheese to the skillet and stir well.
  4. Stir in the crabmeat and cook until well combined and heated through.
  5. Add the spaghetti to the skillet and toss well, making sure to coat every strand in the delightful sauce.
  6. Adjust the salt to preference, garnish with extra Parmesan, and serve immediately.

3. Seafood Paella

Always popular, seafood paella lets you take advantage of the fusion of different seafood flavors to create a robust and satisfying feast. Inspired by the vibrant cuisine of Spain, this dish is perfect for a dinner gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cups bomba rice or calasparra rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron strands
  • 2 cups chicken or seafood broth, warm
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 1/2 cups mixed seafood (mussels, shrimp, clams)
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Over medium-high heat, saut√© the onion and garlic in olive oil in a paella pan until soft.
  2. Add the paprika, saffron, and tomato paste and stir to combine.
  3. Pour in the white wine, allowing it to simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the warm broth, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  5. Nestle the seafood into the mixture, ensuring even distribution.
  6. Scatter the diced tomatoes and peas over the seafood.
  7. Bring the heat down to low and simmer uncovered for 18-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid has been absorbed.
  8. Let it rest for 5 minutes before sprinkling the green onions and lemon zest over the dish.
  9. Squeeze a bit of lemon juice to taste before serving.
