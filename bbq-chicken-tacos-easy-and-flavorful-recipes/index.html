BBQ Chicken Tacos: Easy and Flavorful Recipes


Tacos are a beloved dish that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a tasty meal to enjoy with your family, BBQ chicken tacos are a fantastic option. With their smoky flavors and tender chicken, these tacos are sure to be a hit at any gathering. In this blog post, we’ll explore two easy and flavorful recipes for BBQ chicken tacos that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Recipe 1: Classic BBQ Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Toppings of your choice: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, grated cheese, and sour cream

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, smoked paprika, ground cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the bowl and coat them evenly with the marinade. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard any excess marinade.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F. Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
  5. Once rested, slice the chicken into thin strips.
  6. Warm the flour tortillas on the grill or stovetop until they are pliable.
  7. To assemble the tacos, place a few slices of grilled chicken onto each tortilla. Top with your desired toppings and enjoy!

Recipe 2: Spicy BBQ Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup spicy BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce (adjust according to your preference)
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • Toppings of your choice: pickled jalapenos, chopped cilantro, diced onions, crumbled queso fresco, and lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the spicy BBQ sauce, olive oil, hot sauce, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken thighs to the bowl and ensure they are well-coated with the marinade. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, allowing any excess marinade to drip off.
  4. Grill the chicken thighs for 5-6 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and have reached an internal temperature of 165°F. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
  5. Once rested, shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Heat the corn tortillas on a dry skillet or griddle until they are warm and slightly charred.
  7. To assemble the tacos, place a generous portion of the shredded chicken onto each tortilla. Top with your desired toppings and squeeze fresh lime juice over the tacos before serving.

These BBQ chicken tacos are incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize them to your liking. Feel free to experiment with different toppings, such as guacamole, salsa, or even a squeeze of lime juice. The smoky and tangy flavors of the BBQ sauce combined with the tender chicken make these tacos a crowd favorite.

Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving a delicious meal, these easy and flavorful BBQ chicken tacos are a fantastic choice. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a mouthwatering taco experience that will satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. Enjoy!
