Summer Berry Crumble Bars: A Deliciously Sweet Treat


If there’s one thing that screams summer, it’s the abundance of ripe, juicy berries. From plump strawberries to succulent blueberries and vibrant raspberries, the summer season brings with it an explosion of fruity flavors that are just begging to be turned into something delicious. That’s where these Summer Berry Crumble Bars come in.

The Perfect Balance of Sweet and Tart

These crumble bars strike the perfect balance between sweet and tart, thanks to the combination of fresh summer berries and a buttery crumbly crust. The sweetness of the berries shines through, while the crumbly oat topping adds a satisfying crunch. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, packing a picnic, or simply looking for a delightful dessert to enjoy at home, these bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients:

For the Crust and Crumble:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes

For the Berry Filling:

  • 3 cups mixed fresh berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven and Prepare the Pan:

    Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan and line it with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. Make the Crust and Crumble:

    In a large bowl, combine the flour, oats, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the cubed butter and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Set aside 1 1/2 cups of the mixture for the crumble topping. Press the remaining mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan to form the crust.

  3. Prepare the Berry Filling:

    In another bowl, gently toss the mixed berries with sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice until the berries are coated.

  4. Assemble and Bake:

    Evenly spread the berry filling over the crust. Sprinkle the reserved crumble mixture over the top of the berries. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crumble is golden brown and the berries are bubbling.

  5. Cool and Serve:

    Allow the crumble bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack. Once cooled, lift the bars out of the pan using the parchment paper overhang and cut into squares. Serve and enjoy!

Tips and Variations:

  • For a gluten-free version, use a 1-to-1 gluten-free all-purpose flour blend in place of regular flour.
  • Feel free to mix and match your favorite berries, or use just one type of berry for the filling.
  • Serve these crumble bars with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgent treat.

Final Thoughts:

These Summer Berry Crumble Bars are a celebration of the season’s finest fruits, encapsulating the essence of summer in every bite. Whether you’re baking for a crowd or simply treating yourself to a delightful homemade dessert, these bars are a must-try. With their irresistible combination of sweet, tart, and buttery flavors, they are sure to become a staple in your summer baking repertoire. So, go ahead, savor the flavors of summer with these delectable crumble bars!
