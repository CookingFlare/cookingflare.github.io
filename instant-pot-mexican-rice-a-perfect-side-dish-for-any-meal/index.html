Instant Pot Mexican Rice: A Perfect Side Dish for Any Meal


If you’re looking for a versatile and flavorful side dish to complement your favorite Mexican-inspired meals, Instant Pot Mexican Rice is the perfect choice. This dish is a delicious combination of fluffy rice, savory tomatoes, aromatic spices, and a hint of heat, all cooked to perfection in your Instant Pot. Whether you’re serving up tacos, enchiladas, or fajitas, this Mexican rice will elevate your meal to a whole new level.

Why Choose Instant Pot Mexican Rice?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, making it easier and faster to prepare delicious meals without sacrificing flavor. When it comes to making Mexican rice, the Instant Pot is a game-changer. It allows you to achieve perfectly cooked rice with a rich, savory flavor in a fraction of the time it would take on the stovetop.

Ingredients

To make Instant Pot Mexican Rice, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the oil, chopped onion, and minced garlic, and sauté for a few minutes until the onion is soft and translucent.

  2. Add the Rice and Spices: Add the long-grain white rice to the Instant Pot, stirring to coat the rice in the aromatic oil. Then, add the ground cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper, stirring well to evenly distribute the spices.

  3. Incorporate the Tomatoes and Broth: Pour in the can of diced tomatoes (undrained) and the chicken or vegetable broth, stirring to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.

  5. Natural Release: Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes. Then, carefully release any remaining pressure and remove the lid.

  6. Fluff and Garnish: Fluff the rice with a fork to separate the grains, and garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of fresh flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Instant Pot Mexican Rice is a versatile side dish that pairs well with a variety of Mexican and Tex-Mex dishes. Serve it alongside your favorite tacos, burritos, enchiladas, or fajitas for a complete and satisfying meal. It’s also a great accompaniment to grilled chicken, beef, or fish, adding a pop of color and flavor to any plate.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant colors, robust flavors, and effortless preparation, Instant Pot Mexican Rice is a must-try side dish for any lover of Mexican cuisine. Whether you’re hosting a festive gathering or simply craving a comforting meal at home, this flavorful rice dish is sure to impress. Embrace the convenience and incredible taste of this Instant Pot recipe, and elevate your next meal with a generous serving of Mexican-inspired goodness!
