5 Quick and Easy Fish Recipes for New Cooks


If you’re a new cook looking to dive into the world of seafood, you’ve come to the right place. Fish is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, making it an excellent choice for a healthy diet. These five quick and easy fish recipes are perfect for beginners, each designed to be simple, tasty, and satisfying. Whether you prefer baking, pan-searing, or grilling, there’s something here for everyone. Let’s get started!

1. Lemon Garlic Baked Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Prepare the Marinade: In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

  3. Marinate the Salmon: Place the salmon fillets in a baking dish and pour the marinade over them. Let the fish marinate for about 15 minutes for maximum flavor.

  4. Bake the Salmon: Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve with a side of steamed vegetables or rice.

Tips:

  • For added flavor, you can sprinkle some lemon zest over the salmon before baking.
  • Pair this dish with a crisp white wine for a perfect meal.

2. Quick Pan-Seared Tilapia

Ingredients:

  • 2 tilapia fillets
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Season the Fish: Pat the tilapia fillets dry with a paper towel. Season both sides generously with salt, pepper, and paprika.

  2. Heat the Pan: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.

  3. Cook the Tilapia: Once the oil is hot, add the tilapia fillets to the skillet. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the fish is golden brown and cooked through.

  4. Serve: Transfer the fillets to a plate and serve with lemon wedges for squeezing on top.

Tips:

  • Adding a sprinkle of fresh herbs, like cilantro or parsley, can elevate the dish.
  • This recipe is versatile; feel free to swap tilapia for any white fish you enjoy.

3. Easy Fish Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound white fish (like cod or halibut), cut into strips
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Lime wedges (for serving)
  • Salsa (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Fish: In a bowl, combine the cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Toss the fish strips in the spice mix until well coated.

  2. Cook the Fish: Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a little oil. Cook the fish strips for about 3-5 minutes until cooked through and slightly crispy.

  3. Warm the Tortillas: In another pan, warm the corn tortillas for about 30 seconds on each side.

  4. Assemble the Tacos: Place a few strips of fish on each tortilla. Top with shredded cabbage, avocado slices, and a squeeze of lime. Add salsa if desired.

  5. Serve: Enjoy your fish tacos with extra lime wedges on the side.

Tips:

  • Feel free to customize your toppings; add cheese, sour cream, or your favorite hot sauce.
  • These tacos are great for meal prep—just store the components separately and assemble when ready to eat.

4. One-Pan Mediterranean Cod

Ingredients:

  • 2 cod fillets
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Arrange Ingredients: In a baking dish, arrange the cod fillets in the center. Surround them with cherry tomatoes and olives.

  3. Season: Drizzle the olive oil over everything, sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper.

  4. Bake: Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cod is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.

  5. Garnish: Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Tips:

  • Serve this dish with crusty bread to soak up the delicious juices.
  • You can add other vegetables like zucchini or bell peppers for a more colorful dish.

5. Spicy Shrimp Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Cooked rice (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce and sriracha together. Set aside.

  2. Heat the Oil: In a large skillet or wok, heat the sesame oil over medium-high heat.

  3. Cook the Garlic: Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  4. Add the Shrimp and Vegetables: Add the shrimp and mixed vegetables to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Add Sauce: Pour the soy sauce mixture over the shrimp and vegetables, stirring to combine. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until everything is well coated and heated through.

  6. Serve: Serve the stir-fry over a bed of cooked rice.

Tips:

  • Feel free to experiment with different vegetables based on what you have on hand.
  • This dish is quick, making it perfect for weeknight dinners.

Conclusion

These five quick and easy fish recipes are not only simple to prepare but also bursting with flavor. As a new cook, you can confidently try your hand at these dishes, impressing your family and friends while enjoying the health benefits of fish. Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and having fun. Don’t be afraid to tweak these recipes to suit your taste or dietary needs. Happy cooking!
