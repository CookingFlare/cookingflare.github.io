BBQ Seitan Skewers: A Plant-Based BBQ Option


Marinating seitan, a wheat gluten-based meat substitute, is an excellent way to add flavor and texture to your next barbecue gathering. Known for its chewy, meat-like texture, seitan is a perfect option for vegans and vegetarians, as well as those looking to reduce their meat intake. In this post, we’ll explore how to prepare delectable BBQ seitan skewers that will delight your guests and keep them coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb seitan, cut into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup vegan Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbsp liquid smoke
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 24 wooden skewers
  • 24 cherry tomatoes
  • 8 oz vegan cheese, cut into cubes (optional)

Directions

  1. Begin by preparing the seitan cubes. Cut the seitan into small, bite-sized pieces about 1/2 inch thick. This will ensure easy skewering and even cooking.
  2. Prepare the marinade by combining barbecue sauce, vegan Worcestershire sauce, apple cider vinegar, vegetable oil, garlic, liquid smoke, smoked paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Coat the seitan cubes in the marinade, ensuring all pieces are evenly covered. Cover and refrigerate for at least four hours. Overnight is preferable for optimum flavor.
  4. Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes. This will prevent them from burning during cooking.
  5. When ready to cook, preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Take the marinated seitan out of the refrigerator, and start assembling the skewers. Thread the seitan cubes, cherry tomatoes, and vegan cheese (if using) onto the skewers.
  7. Place the skewers onto the grill, being sure to leave space between each for even cooking. Cook for about 7-10 minutes, turning occasionally, or until seitan is tender and charred to your liking.
  8. Remove the skewers from the grill and serve immediately. Trust us; they’ll be gone in a flash!

Variations

Keep your BBQ seitan skewers simple with just seitan and cherry tomatoes, or experiment with other vegetables like bell peppers, zucchini, or mushrooms. You could even add a kick to the marinade by including spicy sauces like sriracha or jalapeno slices to taste.

Why BBQ Seitan Skewers are a Perfect BBQ Option

Not only are BBQ seitan skewers an enjoyable and flavorful addition to any barbecue, but they’re also an affordable option that can easily be adjusted to suit the preferences of your guests. Moreover, they’re a versatile dish which can be easily adapted for different dietary requirements such as gluten-free or nut-free.

In summary, BBQ seitan skewers are an outstanding addition to any grill gathering. So now that you know how to create the perfect BBQ seitan skewers, impress your friends and family with your culinary prowess at your next barbecue. Happy grilling!
