Irresistible Cinnamon Roll Pancake Recipes for a Brunch Treat


If you’re looking to elevate your brunch game, there’s no better way to do it than with a batch of irresistible cinnamon roll pancakes. These fluffy, indulgent pancakes are the perfect combination of two breakfast classics, and they’re sure to impress your family and friends. With a swirl of cinnamon sugar and a drizzle of cream cheese glaze, these pancakes are as visually stunning as they are delicious. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to create a brunch masterpiece that will have everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients:

For the Pancakes:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

For the Cinnamon Swirl:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the Cream Cheese Glaze:

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3-4 tablespoons milk

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Cinnamon Swirl:

    • In a small bowl, mix the melted butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon until well combined. Transfer the mixture to a piping bag or a small plastic bag with a corner snipped off.

  2. Make the Cream Cheese Glaze:

    • In a medium bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth. Add the powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and milk, and beat until the glaze is smooth and creamy. Set aside.

  3. Prepare the Pancake Batter:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined, being careful not to overmix.

  4. Cook the Pancakes:

    • Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet. Snip the corner of the plastic bag containing the cinnamon swirl mixture and pipe a spiral onto the pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  5. Assemble the Pancakes:

    • Stack the cooked pancakes and drizzle the cream cheese glaze over the top. Serve warm and enjoy!

Tips for Success:

  • Ensure the cinnamon swirl mixture is at room temperature so that it is easy to pipe onto the pancakes.
  • Use a non-stick skillet or griddle to prevent the pancakes from sticking.
  • If you prefer a thinner glaze, add more milk to achieve the desired consistency.

Conclusion:

These cinnamon roll pancakes are a brunch treat that will take your breakfast spread to the next level. The combination of fluffy pancakes, warm cinnamon swirl, and creamy glaze is simply irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a special occasion brunch or simply want to treat yourself, these pancakes are guaranteed to be a hit. So, fire up the stove, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress with this unforgettable breakfast delight!
