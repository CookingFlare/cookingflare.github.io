Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta: A Delicious Combination


Are you craving a comforting and flavorful pasta dish that will leave you wanting more? Look no further than this Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta recipe! With its velvety sauce, tender chicken, and bursts of tangy sun-dried tomatoes, this dish is a true delight for your taste buds. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of pasta (your choice of shape)
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of sun-dried tomatoes, drained and chopped
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  4. Add the heavy cream, chicken broth, and sun-dried tomatoes to the skillet. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 5 minutes, until slightly thickened.

  5. Slice the cooked chicken breasts into thin strips and add them back to the skillet. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until the pasta is coated in the creamy sauce and the chicken is evenly distributed.

  7. Serve the Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and fresh basil, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, such as spinach, mushrooms, or roasted red peppers. Simply sauté them with the garlic before adding the cream and sun-dried tomatoes.

  • If you prefer a spicier dish, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce for a little kick.

  • For a vegetarian version, substitute the chicken with tofu or roasted chickpeas. You can also skip the chicken altogether and enjoy a delicious sun-dried tomato pasta on its own.

  • This dish reheats well, making it perfect for leftovers. Simply store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator and reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop when ready to enjoy again.

  • Pair this Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta with a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite white wine for a complete and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Pasta is a crowd-pleasing dish that combines the richness of cream, the tanginess of sun-dried tomatoes, and the heartiness of chicken. It’s a harmonious blend of flavors that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a pasta lover or simply looking for a comforting and delicious meal, this recipe is a must-try. So grab your ingredients, get cooking, and prepare to indulge in a bowl of pure pasta perfection!
