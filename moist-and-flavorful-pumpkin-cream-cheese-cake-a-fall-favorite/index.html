Moist and Flavorful Pumpkin Cream Cheese Cake: A Fall Favorite


As the leaves turn golden and the air becomes crisper, it’s the perfect time to indulge in the warm and comforting flavors of fall. One of the most beloved autumn treats is the Pumpkin Cream Cheese Cake – a delightful blend of moist pumpkin cake and luscious cream cheese filling. This recipe is a celebration of the season, combining the earthy sweetness of pumpkin with the creamy richness of cream cheese. Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering or simply craving a cozy dessert, this Pumpkin Cream Cheese Cake is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Pumpkin Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 can (15 ounces) pumpkin puree

For the Cream Cheese Filling:

  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg

For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves.

  3. In a large bowl, beat together the granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vegetable oil until well combined. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the pumpkin puree.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Do not overmix.

  5. In a separate bowl, prepare the cream cheese filling by beating the softened cream cheese, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and egg until smooth and creamy.

  6. Spread half of the pumpkin cake batter into the prepared baking pan. Carefully spread the cream cheese filling over the batter, then top with the remaining pumpkin cake batter.

  7. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the cake is set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cake to cool completely.

  8. While the cake is cooling, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a large bowl, beat together the softened butter and cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar and vanilla extract, beating until smooth and creamy.

  9. Once the cake has cooled, spread the cream cheese frosting over the top.

Serving and Storage

This Pumpkin Cream Cheese Cake is best served at room temperature. Cut into squares and enjoy with a sprinkle of cinnamon or a dollop of whipped cream. Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Conclusion

The Moist and Flavorful Pumpkin Cream Cheese Cake is a true delight for the senses, capturing the essence of fall in every bite. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is a wonderful way to embrace the seasonal flavors and create lasting memories with loved ones. So, grab your apron and let the warm aroma of pumpkin spice fill your kitchen – it’s time to bake up a taste of fall with this irresistible Pumpkin Cream Cheese Cake!
