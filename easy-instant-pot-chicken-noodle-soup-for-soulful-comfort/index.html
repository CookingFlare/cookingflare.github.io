Easy Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Soup for Soulful Comfort


There are few things in life as comforting as a steaming bowl of chicken noodle soup. It’s a classic dish that warms the soul and brings back memories of home. And with the convenience of an Instant Pot, you can enjoy all the goodness of homemade chicken noodle soup without spending hours in the kitchen. This easy Instant Pot chicken noodle soup recipe is a game-changer when it comes to quick, delicious, and soul-soothing comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 cups of chicken broth
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of egg noodles
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by gathering and preparing all the ingredients. This will make the cooking process smooth and enjoyable.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a splash of olive oil. Sauté the onions, garlic, carrots, and celery until they begin to soften and become fragrant.

  3. Add Chicken and Broth: Add the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot, followed by the chicken broth. The broth should cover the chicken entirely. This is essential for a flavorful and tender result.

  4. Season and Pressure Cook: Season the broth with thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Lock the lid in place and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook on high for 10 minutes. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before using the quick release.

  5. Shred the Chicken: Carefully remove the chicken breasts from the Instant Pot and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.

  6. Add Noodles: Switch the Instant Pot back to sauté mode and add the egg noodles. Let them cook in the soup for about 5 minutes or until they reach the desired tenderness.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the noodles are cooked, ladle the chicken noodle soup into bowls, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot. The aroma and warmth of this comforting soup are sure to lift your spirits.

Why Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Soup?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, especially when it comes to comfort foods like chicken noodle soup. With its pressure cooking function, this versatile kitchen appliance allows you to achieve rich and flavorful soup in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional methods. The result is a soulful and comforting dish that’s perfect for any day of the week.

Conclusion

Easy Instant Pot chicken noodle soup is a simple yet satisfying dish that brings warmth and comfort with every spoonful. Whether you’re feeling under the weather or simply craving a taste of home, this recipe is a go-to for soulful nourishment. Embrace the convenience of the Instant Pot and savor the heartwarming flavors of homemade chicken noodle soup. It’s a bowl of comfort that’s always ready to soothe your soul.

So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in the heartwarming goodness of this easy chicken noodle soup recipe. It’s the perfect way to nourish your body and lift your spirits with a bowl of soulful comfort.
