Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef Tacos with Homemade Salsa Recipes


If you’re craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s easy to make, look no further than these mouthwatering Instant Pot beef tacos with homemade salsa recipes. This recipe is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a casual get-together with friends and family. The combination of tender, flavorful beef and fresh, zesty salsa is sure to be a hit with everyone at the table.

Instant Pot Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into chunks
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Saute” setting. Add the olive oil and brown the beef in batches until golden brown on all sides. Remove the beef and set it aside.
  2. Add the diced onion to the Instant Pot and cook until softened, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and add the beef broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” for 45 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before using the quick release.
  5. Shred the beef using two forks and return it to the cooking liquid to keep it moist and flavorful.

Homemade Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped red onion, minced jalapeno, and chopped cilantro.
  2. Squeeze the lime juice over the mixture and toss to combine.
  3. Season the salsa with salt and pepper to taste, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Assembling the Tacos

Once the beef is ready and the salsa has had time to chill, it’s time to assemble the tacos. Warm up some corn or flour tortillas, spoon some of the tender beef onto each tortilla, and top it off with a generous helping of the homemade salsa. For an extra burst of flavor and freshness, add some sliced avocado, a dollop of sour cream, and a sprinkle of crumbled queso fresco on top.

Serve these Instant Pot beef tacos with homemade salsa alongside a side of Mexican rice, refried beans, or a simple green salad for a complete and satisfying meal. The tender, juicy beef combined with the bright, zesty flavors of the homemade salsa will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Whether you’re a seasoned cook or new to using an Instant Pot, this recipe is a surefire way to impress your taste buds and those of your loved ones. Enjoy the convenience of the Instant Pot and the freshness of homemade salsa in this mouthwatering dish that’s perfect for any occasion. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor the flavors of these delicious beef tacos with homemade salsa.
