Vegan Gluten-Free Sweetness: 25 Tantalizing Recipes for Allergy Sensitive Tummies


When you’re living with dietary restrictions or allergies, enjoying a delectable, homemade dessert can feel like a distant dream. But fear not! This guide brings you 25 tantalizing recipes that cater to both vegan and gluten-free diets. Indulge in heavenly sweets that everyone can savor, no matter their dietary limitations.

  1. Flourless Chocolate Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 1/2 cup (112g) vegan butter
  • 1 1/3 cups (160g) almond flour
  • 2/3 cup (80g) cocoa powder
  • 2/3 cup (160g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs (replaced with 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce)
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate and vegan butter in a double boiler.

  2. Allow mixture to cool slightly before combining the dry ingredients.

  3. Stir in boiling water to achieve a smooth batter.

  4. Fill cupcake liners 2/3 full and bake at 350°F (180°C) for 20 minutes.

  5. Coconut Milk Panna Cotta with Mango Coulis

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup (60g) vegan gelatin powder
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) boiling water
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) sugar syrup
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

For the Mango Coulis:

  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) sugar syrup

Instructions:

  1. Sprinkle vegan gelatin over boiling water, let sit for 5 minutes.

  2. Heat coconut milk, sugar syrup, and vanilla extract with gelatin.

  3. Pour into molds and refrigerate until firm.

  4. Peel and chop mangoes; blend with sugar syrup.

  5. Serve chilled panna cotta with mango coulis.

  6. Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) sugar
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) gentle sweetener
  • 1/8 tsp stevia powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 bunch fresh mint leaves
  • Chocolate chips (ensure they’re vegan and gluten-free)

Instructions:

  1. Mix coconut milk, sugar, gentle sweetener, stevia, and vanilla.

  2. Blend in mint leaves and chill mixture.

  3. Process using ice cream maker until thick.

  4. Gently stir in chocolate chips, then freeze.

  5. Macadamia Almond Milk Fudge

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 4 cups (640g) maple sugar
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) gentle sweetener
  • 1/4 cup (60g) coconut oil
  • 2 cups raw macadamia nuts
  • 1 cup raw almonds

Instructions:

  1. Simmer almond milk, maple sugar, and gentle sweetener.

  2. Stir in coconut oil and let cool.

  3. Blend macadamia nuts and almonds in a food processor.

  4. Combine with almond milk mixture, then pour into a lined pan.

  5. Refrigerate until set, then cut into fudge squares.

  6. Roasted Raspberry Vanilla Ripple Meringues

For the Meringues:

  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 cup (200g) caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the Raspberry Ripple:

  • 2 cups (300g) fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) sugar syrup

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°F (95°C). Whip egg whites and cream of tartar.

  2. Gradually add sugar, continuing to whip until stiff peaks form.

  3. Gently fold in vanilla extract.

  4. Shape meringues onto parchment-lined trays.

  5. Bake for 1 hour or until crisp. Turn off heat and let them dry for an hour.

  6. Blend raspberries and sugar syrup in a food processor.

  7. Gently swirl raspberry mixture into meringue halves, then return them to the oven for 15 minutes at 300°F (150°C) to rewarm.

  8. Banana Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 3/4 cup (180ml) melted coconut oil
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) maple syrup
  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup (150g) raisins or chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Combine banana, coconut oil, baking soda, and maple syrup.

  2. Stir in rolled oats, then fold in raisins or chocolate chips.

  3. Drop spoonfuls onto a parchment-lined baking sheet, then bake for 12-15 minutes.

  4. Chocolate Avocado Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe avocados
  • 1 cup (120g) cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) almond milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) agave nectar
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) gentle sweetener
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Scoop avocado flesh into a food processor.

  2. Add cocoa powder and blend until smooth.

  3. Combine almond milk, agave nectar, gentle sweetener, vanilla, and salt.

  4. Blend with avocado mixture until creamy.

  5. Chill for at least an hour before serving.

  6. Orange Cranberry Rice Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup short grain brown rice
  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) orange juice
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) sugar syrup
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup (40g) dried cranberries
  • Zest of an orange
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice in almond milk; stir in orange juice and sugar syrup.

  2. Let simmer until rice is soft and mixture is thick.

  3. Add cinnamon, cranberries, orange zest, and chia seeds.

  4. Scoop into dishes and allow it to thicken before serving.

  5. Cinnamon Walnut Pear Crumble

For the Pear Filling:

  • 4 pears, peeled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) sugar syrup
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

For the Crumble Topping:

  • 1 cup (120g) almond flour
  • 1/2 cup (60g) walnuts, finely chopped
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) maple syrup
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup (60g) vegan butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Combine pears, sugar syrup, and lemon juice in a baking dish.

  2. Mix almond flour, walnuts, maple syrup, cinnamon, and vegan butter in a separate bowl.

  3. Sprinkle the crumble topping over the pears.

  4. Bake for 40-45 minutes until the pears are tender and the crumble is golden.

  5. Spiced Sweet Potato Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (280g) mashed sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) pumpkin puree
  • 2 large eggs (replaced with 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce)
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) coconut oil, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups (180g) almond flour
  • 1/2 cup (60g) cinnamon chips
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Mix mashed sweet potato, pumpkin puree, applesauce, and coconut oil.

  2. Blend in almond flour, cinnamon chips, baking powder, cinnamon, and nutmeg.

  3. Spoon batter into greased muffin tins.

  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

  5. Vegan and Gluten-Free Berry Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 2 pints mixed berries
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) sugar syrup
  • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) coconut cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Blend berries and sugar syrup until smooth.
  2. Whip coconut cream and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  3. Fold berry mixture into whipped cream, then refrigerate for an hour.
  4. Serve chilled, garnishing with fresh berries on top.

And there you have it – 25 tantalizing vegan and gluten-free dessert recipes that will satisfy any sweet tooth craving while keeping allergies at bay. From rich chocolate delights to fruity creations, these treats have something for everyone. So, gather your ingredients, experiment in the kitchen, and enjoy guilt-free indulgence with these allergy-sensitive sweets!
