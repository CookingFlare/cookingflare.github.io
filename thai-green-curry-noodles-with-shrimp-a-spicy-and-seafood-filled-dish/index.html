Thai Green Curry Noodles with Shrimp: A Spicy and Seafood-Filled Dish


If you are a fan of Thai cuisine and love the tantalizing combination of spicy flavors and seafood, then Thai Green Curry Noodles with Shrimp is a dish that will undoubtedly captivate your taste buds. This vibrant and aromatic dish brings together the richness of coconut milk, the heat of green curry paste, the freshness of herbs, and the succulence of shrimp, all combined with tender rice noodles to create a truly unforgettable culinary experience.

Ingredients

To embark on this culinary adventure, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 8 oz of rice noodles
  • 1 lb of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 3 tbsp of Thai green curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp of fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp of brown sugar
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas, trimmed
  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 fresh limes, cut into wedges
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Rice Noodles

Start by cooking the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Once tender, drain them and set them aside.

Step 2: Sear the Shrimp

In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set them aside.

Step 3: Create the Green Curry Base

Using the same skillet, add the Thai green curry paste and stir it in the remaining oil for about a minute to release its aromatic flavors. Then, pour in the coconut milk and chicken or vegetable broth, stirring to combine. Let the mixture simmer for about 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 4: Add Flavor Enhancers

Next, add the fish sauce and brown sugar to the curry base, stirring until the sugar is dissolved. These ingredients will contribute a depth of flavor and a perfect balance of sweetness and saltiness to the dish.

Step 5: Bring It All Together

Now, it’s time to add the cooked shrimp, red bell pepper, and snap peas to the skillet. Let the mixture simmer for another 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender and the shrimp are heated through. Season with salt to taste.

Step 6: Final Touches

Once the curry is ready, stir in the fresh basil leaves and half of the chopped cilantro, reserving the rest for garnish. These herbs will infuse the dish with their vibrant aromas and fresh flavors, elevating the overall experience.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Divide the cooked rice noodles among serving bowls and ladle the flavorful green curry over the noodles. Garnish with the remaining cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side, allowing each diner to squeeze fresh lime juice over their dish to brighten the flavors.

Conclusion

Thai Green Curry Noodles with Shrimp is a dish that embodies the essence of Thai cuisine—bold, aromatic, and delightfully complex. With its spicy kick and a medley of seafood and vibrant herbs, this dish promises to transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand. Whether you’re a seasoned Thai food enthusiast or a curious culinary explorer, this recipe is sure to become a beloved addition to your repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and embark on a journey of flavors with this tantalizing Thai masterpiece!
