Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Cake: Nutella Lover's Paradise


If you’re a fan of Nutella, then get ready to indulge in a slice of heaven with this Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Cake. This cake is a Nutella lover’s paradise, combining rich chocolate flavors with the creamy goodness of Nutella and the crunch of toasted hazelnuts. It’s a showstopper dessert that will impress your guests and leave them craving for more.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Nutella frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 cup Nutella
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the chocolate ganache:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped

For the garnish:

  • 1/2 cup toasted hazelnuts, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk together until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix on medium speed for 2 minutes, until the batter is smooth.

  4. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin, but that’s okay. It will result in a moist and tender cake.

  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  6. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  7. While the cakes are cooling, prepare the Nutella frosting. In a mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar and beat until light and fluffy.

  8. Add the Nutella, heavy cream, and vanilla extract to the butter mixture. Beat on medium-high speed until well combined and smooth.

  9. Once the cakes are completely cooled, place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread a generous amount of Nutella frosting evenly over the top.

  10. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the entire cake with the remaining Nutella frosting. Use a spatula to create smooth and even layers.

  11. To make the chocolate ganache, heat the heavy cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Remove from heat and add the chopped chocolate. Let it sit for a minute, then stir until smooth and glossy.

  12. Pour the chocolate ganache over the top of the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides. Use a spatula to spread the ganache evenly over the cake.

  13. Sprinkle the chopped toasted hazelnuts over the top of the cake as a garnish. Gently press them into the ganache for them to stick.

  14. Place the cake in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour to allow the ganache to set.

  15. Once the ganache is set, slice and serve this decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Cake to your guests. Watch their faces light up with delight as they take their first bite into this Nutella lover’s paradise.

Enjoy every bite of this rich and indulgent cake, and savor the perfect balance of chocolate and hazelnut flavors. Whether it’s a special occasion or just a craving for something sweet, this Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Cake will always be a crowd-pleaser. So go ahead, treat yourself and your loved ones to this Nutella lover’s paradise. You won’t be disappointed!
