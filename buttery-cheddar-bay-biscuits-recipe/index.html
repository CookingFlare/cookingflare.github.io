Buttery Cheddar Bay Biscuits Recipe


As the scent of freshly baked biscuits wafts through your kitchen, you can’t help but feel a sense of warmth and coziness. When that aroma is combined with the savory, buttery goodness of melted cheddar cheese and the pleasing texture of flaky biscuits, your culinary creations take on a whole new level of comfort.

Buttery Cheddar Bay Biscuits are just the recipe you need to satisfy that craving for simple, delicious food. These delectable treats combine the best of homemade biscuits and cheesy goodness, making them the perfect accompaniment to any meal or a delightful snack on their own.

To create this scrumptious dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter (1 stick), chilled and cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 well-beaten egg

First, preheat your oven to 400°F (205°C). Next, line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat to prevent sticking.

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Now, cut in the chilled butter using a pastry cutter, a fork, or even your hands, until the mixture resembles coarse cornmeal.

Here’s the magic step: Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese.

In another bowl, lightly beat the egg and then gradually add the milk, stirring constantly to combine. Add this mixture to the flour mixture and stir gently until just combined.

Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface, and gently knead for a few seconds. Pat the dough until it is about 3/4 inch thick. You want the biscuits to be thick and fluffy, so handle the dough gently to retain as much air as possible.

Using a 2-inch round cookie or biscuit cutter, stamp out as many biscuits as you can. Place them on your prepared baking sheet, spacing them evenly.

For an extra touch of flavor, brush the biscuits with a little beaten egg and sprinkle with additional shredded cheddar cheese.

Finally, your Cheddar Bay Biscuits are ready for the oven. Bake for roughly 12 to 15 minutes, or until biscuits are golden brown. They should sound hollow when you tap their bottoms gently.

Remove from the oven and let the biscuits cool on the baking sheet for about 5 minutes. No need to rush – let them cool and become fluffy and tender.

Enjoy these Buttery Cheddar Bay Biscuits with some melted butter and a heartfelt conversation with loved ones. You’ve created a culinary masterpiece, and sharing it with others makes it all the more special.

This recipe yields the tastiest biscuits you’ve ever had, with a tender interior and a delightful crispiness on the outside. Cheddar Bay Biscuits are both a staple and a comfort for any occasion.

Whether you’re gathering with friends, serving a family meal, or simply treating yourself, nothing beats the combination of warm, buttery biscuits and melted cheddar cheese. With this recipe, you’ll have a go-to comfort food that will be treasured by all who taste it. So go ahead and indulge in the deliciousness of homemade Buttery Cheddar Bay Biscuits!
