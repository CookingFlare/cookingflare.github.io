Tasty Turkey and Cheese Pinwheels: Kid-Friendly Recipe


Looking for a fun and delicious recipe that your kids will love? Look no further! These tasty turkey and cheese pinwheels are not only a hit with kids, but they’re also easy to make and perfect for a quick snack or lunch. Packed with protein and flavor, these pinwheels are a great way to satisfy your little one’s hunger while keeping them happy and healthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of refrigerated crescent roll dough
  • 1/4 pound of sliced turkey
  • 1/4 pound of sliced cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • 1/2 cup of baby spinach leaves
  • 1/4 cup of roasted red peppers, drained and chopped
  • 1/4 cup of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Unroll the crescent roll dough and separate it into rectangles, pressing the perforations to seal.

  3. In a small bowl, mix the softened cream cheese with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Spread this mixture evenly over the dough rectangles.

  4. Layer the turkey, cheese, spinach leaves, and chopped roasted red peppers over the cream cheese mixture.

  5. Starting from one short side, roll up each rectangle, and then with a sharp knife, cut each roll into 4-5 slices.

  6. Place the pinwheels onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.

  7. Let the pinwheels cool for a few minutes before serving.

Why Kids Love It

These pinwheels are not only delicious but also fun to eat! The colorful layers and the spiral shape make them visually appealing, which can entice even the pickiest eaters. The combination of turkey, cheese, and veggies provides a balanced mix of flavors and textures that kids enjoy. Plus, they’re the perfect size for little hands to hold and munch on.

Why Parents Love It

Aside from being a hit with the kids, these pinwheels are a parent’s dream come true. They are quick and easy to make, making them ideal for busy days or as a last-minute snack. Additionally, by adding spinach and roasted red peppers, you can sneak in some extra veggies without any fuss. The protein from the turkey and cheese also makes this a satisfying and nutritious option for growing kids.

Serving Suggestions

These pinwheels are versatile and can be served in various ways. Pair them with a side of fresh fruit and veggies for a balanced meal, or pack them in lunchboxes for a school lunch. They also make great party appetizers or after-school snacks. The options are endless!

So, the next time you’re looking for a kid-friendly recipe that’s both tasty and easy, give these turkey and cheese pinwheels a try. They’re sure to become a regular favorite in your household. Enjoy!

In summary, these Tasty Turkey and Cheese Pinwheels are a delightful, kid-friendly recipe that’s simple to make and sure to please both kids and parents alike. With a balance of flavors and the convenience of being able to sneak in some extra veggies, these pinwheels are a win-win for everyone. Whether it’s for a quick lunch, a snack, or a party appetizer, these pinwheels are a versatile and delicious option that’s bound to become a household favorite.
