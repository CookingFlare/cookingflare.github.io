You Had Me at Mole: A Valentine's Day Chocolate Mole Recipe with Chicken and Chorizo: A Valentine's Day Main Course Recipe


Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to impress your loved one with a delicious and unique dish. And what better way to show your affection than with a rich and indulgent chocolate mole? This Valentine’s Day, treat your sweetheart to a memorable dining experience with this exquisite Chocolate Mole Recipe, featuring tender chicken and spicy chorizo.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 150g chorizo, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup toasted almonds, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Cooked rice or warm tortillas, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the chorizo and cook until crispy. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the onion and garlic. Sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  4. Add the chili powder, cumin, cinnamon, oregano, cloves, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine and cook for a minute or two to release the flavors.
  5. Add the cocoa powder and stir until incorporated.
  6. Gradually pour in the chicken broth, stirring constantly to avoid any lumps. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  7. Return the chicken and chorizo to the skillet, along with any juices that have accumulated. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  8. Remove the chicken breasts from the skillet and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the skillet.
  9. Stir in the almond butter until well combined. Cook for an additional 10 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  10. Add the raisins and toasted almonds to the skillet. Stir to distribute evenly.
  11. Serve the chocolate mole over cooked rice or with warm tortillas. Garnish with fresh cilantro.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian version, replace the chicken and chorizo with your favorite vegetables or tofu.
  • Adjust the spice level by adding more or less chili powder according to your taste.
  • If you prefer a smoother sauce, you can blend the mole in a blender or use an immersion blender before adding the shredded chicken.

Conclusion

This Valentine’s Day, surprise your loved one with a unique and unforgettable dining experience. This Chocolate Mole Recipe with Chicken and Chorizo is the perfect dish to set the mood and show your affection. The rich and complex flavors of the mole, combined with the tender chicken and spicy chorizo, will leave you and your sweetheart wanting more. Serve it over rice or with warm tortillas for an authentic Mexican feast. So, this Valentine’s Day, let the flavors of chocolate and love intertwine with this mouthwatering mole recipe.
