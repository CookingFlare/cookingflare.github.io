Creamy Keto Shrimp Scampi: A Classic Dish Made Low-Carb


Are you a fan of classic shrimp scampi but looking for a low-carb, keto-friendly version to enjoy guilt-free? Look no further! This creamy keto shrimp scampi recipe is a delightful twist on the traditional dish, featuring succulent shrimp, rich creamy sauce, and a burst of citrus flavors. Whether you’re following a ketogenic diet or simply looking to add more variety to your culinary repertoire, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite at your dinner table.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Make the Sauce: In the same skillet, pour in the white wine and chicken broth. Let it simmer for a few minutes, allowing the alcohol to cook off.

  4. Add the Creamy Element: Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Simmer the sauce gently for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  5. Combine and Serve: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss to coat them in the creamy sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle chopped parsley over the shrimp scampi.

  6. Serve: Serve the creamy keto shrimp scampi over a bed of cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles, allowing the flavors to mingle and delight your taste buds.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

  • Keto-Friendly: With the use of heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, this shrimp scampi is low in carbs and high in flavor, making it a perfect fit for a keto lifestyle.

  • Creamy Indulgence: The addition of heavy cream creates a luscious, velvety sauce that coats the shrimp beautifully, elevating the dish to a whole new level of indulgence.

  • Citrusy Freshness: The zesty lemon juice and lemon zest add a refreshing citrusy note to the dish, balancing the richness of the creamy sauce and bringing a burst of freshness to every bite.

  • Easy and Elegant: This recipe is surprisingly easy to prepare and yet impressively elegant, making it an ideal choice for both weeknight dinners and special occasions.

  • Versatile Serving Options: Whether you opt for cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles, the creamy keto shrimp scampi pairs perfectly with these low-carb alternatives, offering a satisfying meal without the guilt.

Indulge in the creamy richness of this keto shrimp scampi, and savor every bite knowing that you’re treating yourself to a delectable low-carb delight. This dish is a testament to the fact that classic flavors can be reinvented to suit a variety of dietary preferences without compromising on taste or satisfaction. So, gather your ingredients and embark on a culinary adventure that promises to tantalize your taste buds and keep you coming back for more!
