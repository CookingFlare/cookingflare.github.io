Comfort Food Makeovers: Creamy and Indulgent Macaroni Salad

Who doesn’t love a good macaroni salad? It’s a classic comfort food that brings back memories of picnics and family gatherings. But let’s be honest, traditional macaroni salads can be heavy, loaded with mayonnaise, and not the healthiest option. That’s why we’re here to give this beloved dish a makeover - a creamy and indulgent macaroni salad that will satisfy your cravings without sacrificing flavor or nutrition. Ingredients: 1 pound elbow macaroni 1 cup Greek yogurt 1/2 cup mayonnaise 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon onion powder Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup diced celery 1 cup diced red bell pepper 1/2 cup diced red onion 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley 1/2 cup chopped dill pickles 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese Instructions: Cook the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to cool. In a large bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper until well combined. Add the cooked macaroni, celery, red bell pepper, red onion, parsley, dill pickles, and feta cheese to the bowl. Gently toss to coat everything in the creamy dressing. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together. Give the macaroni salad a taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can add more salt, pepper, or any other spices you prefer. Serve chilled and enjoy! Why Choose This Macaroni Salad? This creamy and indulgent macaroni salad is a healthier alternative to traditional recipes. By replacing the majority of mayonnaise with Greek yogurt, you’re cutting down on fat and adding a protein boost. Greek yogurt also provides a tangy flavor that complements the other ingredients perfectly. ...
