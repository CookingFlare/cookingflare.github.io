Cozy Winter Cheesy Broccoli Pasta Bake


As the cold winter months roll in, there’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting meal to lift your spirits. This Cozy Winter Cheesy Broccoli Pasta Bake is the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings and bring a smile to your face. Packed with wholesome ingredients and oozing with cheesy goodness, this recipe is a delightful twist on the classic pasta bake. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering creation.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pasta (such as penne or rigatoni)
  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ cups of milk
  • 2 cups of sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • ½ cup of Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, adding the broccoli florets to the boiling water during the last 3 minutes of cooking. Drain the pasta and broccoli, then set aside.

  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, whisk in the flour and cook for about 1 minute, until it turns a light golden color.

  4. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the cheddar, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese until they melt and the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.

  6. Add the garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, thyme, and oregano to the cheese sauce, stirring to combine all the flavors.

  7. Combine the cooked pasta and broccoli with the cheese sauce, ensuring that everything is evenly coated.

  8. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  9. Sprinkle the top with paprika for an extra burst of flavor and a pop of color.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

Serving Suggestion

This Cozy Winter Cheesy Broccoli Pasta Bake is a versatile dish that can stand alone as a hearty meal or be paired with a crisp green salad for a well-rounded dinner. The creamy cheese sauce combined with the tender broccoli and perfectly cooked pasta creates a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing. Whether you’re hosting a winter gathering or simply craving some comfort food, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Conclusion

Embrace the warmth of winter with this indulgent Cozy Winter Cheesy Broccoli Pasta Bake. Its rich, cheesy goodness and wholesome ingredients make it a standout dish for any occasion. As the aromas of bubbling cheese and spices fill your kitchen, you’ll know that you’ve created a masterpiece that will bring joy to everyone who has the pleasure of tasting it. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the magic of this delightful winter comfort food.
