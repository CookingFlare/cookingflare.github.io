Tasty and Easy Baked Zucchini Fries Recipe for Kids


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy snack that your kids will love? Look no further! These tasty and easy baked zucchini fries are the perfect way to get your little ones excited about eating their veggies. Not only are they crispy and flavorful, but they are also baked, making them a healthier alternative to traditional fries. Let’s dive into this simple and delightful recipe that will have your kids asking for more!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Cut the zucchinis into thin sticks, resembling the shape and size of fries.

  3. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten.

  4. In another shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and salt.

  5. Dip each zucchini stick into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off.

  6. Roll the zucchini stick in the breadcrumb mixture, coating it evenly on all sides.

  7. Place the coated zucchini sticks on the prepared baking sheet, making sure they are evenly spaced and not touching each other.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchini fries are golden and crispy.

  9. Once done, remove from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These baked zucchini fries are delicious on their own, but they can also be paired with a variety of dipping sauces such as marinara sauce, ranch dressing, or even a simple homemade garlic aioli. You can also sprinkle some fresh chopped parsley on top for an extra burst of color and flavor.

Nutritional Benefits

Zucchinis are a fantastic source of dietary fiber, which is essential for a healthy digestive system. They also contain vitamin C, vitamin A, and potassium, making them a nutritious addition to your child’s diet. By baking the zucchini instead of frying, you are reducing the amount of unhealthy fats and calories typically found in traditional fries, making this recipe a guilt-free indulgence for both kids and adults.

Get the Kids Involved

Getting your kids involved in the kitchen is a wonderful way to encourage them to try new foods. Let them help with coating the zucchini sticks in the breadcrumb mixture or arranging them on the baking sheet. Not only will they enjoy the hands-on experience, but they will also feel a sense of accomplishment when they see the finished product.

Final Thoughts

These tasty and easy baked zucchini fries are a fantastic way to introduce more vegetables into your child’s diet while still keeping mealtime fun and enjoyable. Whether it’s for an after-school snack, a lunchbox addition, or a side dish for dinner, these zucchini fries are sure to be a hit with the whole family. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to delight your kids with this scrumptious and nutritious treat!
