The Best Spanish Cooking YouTube Channels to Follow


If you’re passionate about Spanish cuisine or simply looking to explore the vibrant flavors of Spain, YouTube is an incredible resource to learn traditional recipes, modern twists, and techniques directly from experts. Spanish cooking is a feast for the senses, with its bold use of paprika, olive oil, fresh seafood, and jamón. Whether you’re interested in prepping a silky tortilla española, simmering a rich paella, or crafting delicate churros, these Spanish cooking YouTube channels will immerse you in the culinary heart of Spain.

Each of these channels stands out for its unique approach, talented hosts, and dedication to sharing the soul of Spanish cooking. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned cook, there’s plenty to learn from each of them.

1. Spain on a Fork

When it comes to Spanish food, simplicity and flavor are key ingredients. “Spain on a Fork,” run by Albert Bevia, perfectly captures this philosophy. With concise and approachable videos, Albert expertly guides viewers through recipes that highlight iconic Spanish ingredients.

His channel covers everything from tapas to hearty stews, and he often includes helpful tips for cooking with authentic Spanish products. Whether you’re on the hunt for crisp patatas bravas or creamy arroz con leche, Albert’s step-by-step presentations make these recipes easy to replicate at home. This channel is a great starting point for anyone looking to dip their toes into Spanish culinary traditions.

Why You’ll Love This Channel:

  • Focus on authentic Spanish flavors.
  • Recipes that balance tradition with simplicity.
  • Easy-to-follow instructions perfect for home cooks.

2. Las Recetas de MJ

For those who crave both creativity and authenticity, “Las Recetas de MJ” is a treasure trove. MJ, the creator behind this channel, is not just passionate about Spanish cooking; she explores international cuisines while bringing her unique flair to every dish. Her Spanish recipes, however, are where she truly shines.

From homemade croquetas to regional specialties like fabada asturiana, MJ dives deep into the cultural significance of each dish while providing detailed instructions. Her lively personality and focus on high-quality results make this channel ideal for intermediate to advanced home chefs seeking culinary inspiration.

Why You’ll Love This Channel:

  • Extensive collection of Spanish recipes, including lesser-known regional dishes.
  • Engaging and detailed explanations of techniques and ingredients.
  • Inspirational ideas for those who love experimenting in the kitchen.

3. De la Cocina a la Mesa

“De la Cocina a la Mesa” bridges the gap between home cooking and restaurant-quality dishes. This channel knows how to serve up traditional Spanish recipes with a twist. Their approach appeals to those who love experimenting and elevating everyday meals into gourmet experiences.

The recipes cover a variety of Spanish staples, including pulpo a la gallega (Galician-style octopus), hearty caldos (broths), and luscious desserts like tarta de Santiago. With clear visuals and concise explanations, their videos add a professional edge to traditional dishes. Fans of Spanish seafood and robust flavors will appreciate the rich variety offered here.

Why You’ll Love This Channel:

  • Techniques for refining traditional Spanish dishes.
  • Gourmet takes on classic recipes.
  • High-quality production that is pleasing to watch.

4. Chef Juan Llorca

A warm and approachable cooking guide, Chef Juan Llorca specializes in Spanish recipes that are both nourishing and delicious. His channel often focuses on healthy variations of traditional dishes, making it perfect for those who want to enjoy Spanish flavors without compromising on wellness.

One of Chef Llorca’s strengths is his detailed attention to ingredient sourcing, teaching viewers how to select fresh produce and pantry staples like olive oil and saffron. His videos balance nutrition with indulgence, showcasing recipes for everything from flavorful gazpacho to clean spins on classic Spanish desserts.

Why You’ll Love This Channel:

  • Perfect for health-conscious cooks wanting Spanish food.
  • Clear, heartfelt explanations combined with accessible recipes.
  • Focus on fresh ingredients, making every dish vibrant.

5. Cocina con Carmen

“Cocina con Carmen” is like having a friendly Spanish grandmother showing you the ropes of Spanish cooking. Carmen’s channel is filled with heartwarming traditional recipes, and her nurturing attitude shines through every video.

From simple empanadas to complex cocido madrileño, her straightforward approach makes authentic Spanish cooking accessible to all. This channel emphasizes traditional flavors and methods, ideal for anyone who wants to master the classics or learn how to cook true comfort food.

Why You’ll Love This Channel:

  • Perfect for mastering heritage-rich recipes.
  • A warm, personal vibe that feels inviting and authentic.
  • Great for beginners and anyone looking to cook Spanish staples.

6. Recetas de Esbieta

Esbieta’s channel is the go-to for bakers who want a slice of Spanish culinary tradition. Her videos center on Spanish pastries and breads, from pan de pueblo to rosquillas. Whether you’re learning how to fold buttery napolitanas or perfecting the art of making ensaimadas, the visuals and instructions are captivating and clear.

While Esbieta primarily focuses on baking, she occasionally diverges into broader Spanish cuisine territory. Offering detailed insights on everything from ingredient ratios to oven temperatures, her channel is a must-watch for dessert enthusiasts and carb aficionados.

Why You’ll Love This Channel:

  • Comprehensive content on Spanish baking.
  • Recipes feature a mix of artistry and practicality.
  • Perfect for both beginner and advanced bakers.

7. La Cocina de Masito

Masito’s cooking channel is ideal for those who love down-to-earth recipes bursting with flavor. Specializing in budget-friendly Spanish dishes, “La Cocina de Masito” is filled with ideas for hearty meals that require few ingredients but deliver big results.

Popular recipes include easy Spanish stews, cost-effective appetizers, and family-style meals that stick to traditional roots. Whether it’s cooking a tortilla española with leftovers or whipping up satisfying albondigas (meatballs), Masito’s enthusiastic style makes cooking approachable for every viewer.

Why You’ll Love This Channel:

  • Budget-friendly Spanish recipes for everyday meals.
  • Warm, accessible approach to cooking.
  • Perfect for busy home cooks seeking reliable ideas.

8. El Cocinero Fiel

With its focus on building relationships through food, “El Cocinero Fiel” highlights the cultural richness behind Spanish dishes. This channel often showcases regional cooking, providing insight into how recipes differ across Spain’s diverse geography.

Specialties include seafood-packed fideuà, slow-cooked porrusalda, and short, instructional videos on tapas favorites like boquerones en vinagre. Fans of culinary history and authentic preparation methods will find this channel particularly insightful.

Why You’ll Love This Channel:

  • Great for exploring regional Spanish recipes.
  • Offers a deeper dive into the cultural background behind the dishes.
  • A unique collection of recipes with an authentic Spanish touch.

Conclusion: Dive into Spain’s Culinary World

Spanish cuisine tells a story—one of history, tradition, and passion for excellent food. By following these exceptional YouTube channels, you’ll not only master recipes but also gain a deeper understanding of Spain’s rich culinary heritage. Each channel offers unique perspectives and specialties, ensuring there’s something for every cook, whether you prefer classic techniques or modern interpretations.

Don’t just watch the videos—try the recipes! Spanish cooking is meant to be experienced, shared, and celebrated. From savory paella to sweet churros dipped in chocolate, these channels will unlock the secrets of Spain’s vibrant kitchens and inspire your culinary journey. Spain awaits, one delicious dish at a time!
