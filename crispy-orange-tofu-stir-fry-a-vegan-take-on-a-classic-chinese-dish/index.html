Crispy Orange Tofu Stir Fry: A Vegan Take on a Classic Chinese Dish


If you’re a fan of Chinese cuisine, you’ve likely come across the beloved classic, Orange Chicken. The combination of crispy, tangy, and sweet flavors is undeniably delicious. But what if you’re following a vegan lifestyle? Fear not, because today, we’re putting a vegan twist on this timeless dish with our Crispy Orange Tofu Stir Fry recipe.

Ingredients

For the Tofu:

  • 1 block of extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons agave nectar or maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon orange zest
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger

For the Stir Fry:

  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tofu:

    • In a shallow dish, mix the cornstarch, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Coat the tofu cubes in the cornstarch mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.
    • In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the coated tofu cubes in a single layer. Fry until golden and crispy, then transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

  2. Make the Sauce:

    • In a bowl, whisk together the orange juice, soy sauce, rice vinegar, agave nectar or maple syrup, cornstarch, orange zest, garlic, and ginger. Set aside.

  3. Stir Fry:

    • In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed. Add the bell pepper, broccoli, and carrots. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
    • Pour the prepared sauce over the vegetables in the skillet. Stir and cook until the sauce thickens and coats the vegetables evenly.
    • Add the crispy tofu to the skillet and gently toss to coat the tofu with the sauce and vegetables.

  4. Serve:

    • Garnish the dish with chopped green onions and serve the Crispy Orange Tofu Stir Fry over cooked rice or noodles.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Our vegan take on the classic Chinese Orange Chicken brings together the crispy texture of tofu and the vibrant flavors of the citrusy orange sauce. The crispy tofu absorbs the sweet and tangy flavors of the orange sauce, creating an explosion of taste in every bite. This dish is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a visual delight with its colorful array of stir-fried vegetables.

Final Thoughts

This Crispy Orange Tofu Stir Fry recipe is a testament to the fact that plant-based dishes can be just as satisfying and flavorful as their non-vegan counterparts. The marriage of crispy tofu, vibrant vegetables, and a zesty orange sauce creates a harmony of flavors that will leave you craving for more. So, whether you’re a dedicated vegan or simply looking to explore new culinary horizons, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Give it a try, and let the aroma of this delectable vegan dish transport you to the bustling streets of a Chinese food market. Enjoy!
