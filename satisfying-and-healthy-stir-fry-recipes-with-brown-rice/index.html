Satisfying and Healthy Stir Fry Recipes with Brown Rice


Stir-fries are a fantastic way to create a flavorful, satisfying, and healthy meal in no time. By combining fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and the nutty goodness of brown rice, you can whip up a delicious stir fry that will leave you feeling nourished and satisfied. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of stir fry recipes that are not only easy to make but also packed with nutrients and goodness. So, let’s grab our woks and get cooking!

Teriyaki Tofu Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 4 cups of cooked brown rice
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Add the cubed tofu and let it marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  2. In a wok or large skillet, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the marinated tofu and cook until golden brown. Remove from the wok and set aside.
  3. In the same wok, stir fry the bell pepper, snap peas, and carrot until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the cooked brown rice to the wok and stir in the vegetables.
  5. Return the tofu to the wok and toss everything together.
  6. Serve the stir fry over brown rice and garnish with sesame seeds.

Ginger Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of snow peas
  • 4 cups of cooked brown rice
  • Sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Add the shrimp and let it marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  2. In a wok or large skillet, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the marinated shrimp and cook until pink and opaque. Remove from the wok and set aside.
  3. In the same wok, stir fry the bell pepper, broccoli, and snow peas until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the cooked brown rice to the wok and stir in the vegetables.
  5. Return the shrimp to the wok and toss everything together.
  6. Serve the stir fry over brown rice and garnish with sliced green onions.

Vegetable Cashew Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cashews
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
  • 4 cups of cooked brown rice

Instructions:

  1. In a dry skillet, toast the cashews over medium heat until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. In a wok or large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, and stir fry for a minute.
  3. Add the bell pepper, snap peas, mushrooms, and broccoli to the wok and stir fry until they are tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce and hoisin sauce. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the wok.
  5. Add the cooked brown rice to the wok and toss everything together.
  6. Serve the stir fry over brown rice and top with the toasted cashews.

Enjoy these delicious and nutritious stir fry recipes with brown rice. They’re perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a flavorful meal prep option. Experiment with different veggies, proteins, and sauces to create your own unique stir fry masterpieces. Happy cooking!
