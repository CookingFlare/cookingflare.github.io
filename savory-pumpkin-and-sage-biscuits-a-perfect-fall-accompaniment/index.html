Savory Pumpkin and Sage Biscuits: A Perfect Fall Accompaniment


As the leaves begin to change and the air takes on a crisp edge, it’s the perfect time to embrace the flavors of fall. One delightful way to do so is by baking a batch of savory pumpkin and sage biscuits. These biscuits are the epitome of comfort food, with their warm, earthy flavors and fluffy texture. Whether you’re hosting a cozy brunch or simply craving a seasonal treat, these biscuits are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons fresh sage, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, buttermilk, fresh sage, nutmeg, and black pepper.

  5. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour the wet ingredients into the well.

  6. Gently mix the wet and dry ingredients together until a soft dough forms. Be careful not to overwork the dough, as this can make the biscuits tough.

  7. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat it into a 1-inch thickness.

  8. Use a biscuit cutter to cut out the biscuits and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

  9. Gather any remaining dough, pat it out again, and cut out more biscuits until all the dough is used.

  10. Bake the biscuits for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  11. Serve the biscuits warm, accompanied by butter or your favorite savory spread.

Why Savory Pumpkin and Sage Biscuits?

1. Flavors of Fall

Pumpkin and sage are quintessential fall ingredients, and they come together beautifully in these biscuits. The subtle sweetness of the pumpkin pairs perfectly with the earthy, aromatic flavor of sage, creating a delightful sensory experience.

2. Versatility

These biscuits are incredibly versatile and can accompany a wide range of dishes. Whether served alongside a hearty soup, as part of a brunch spread, or simply enjoyed with a smear of butter, they add a touch of warmth and comfort to any meal.

3. Easy to Make

Despite their impressive flavor, these biscuits are surprisingly easy to make. With just a handful of ingredients and a few simple steps, you can have a batch of these delectable treats ready to enjoy in no time.

4. Perfect for Entertaining

If you’re hosting a fall gathering, these biscuits are a fantastic addition to your menu. They offer a unique twist on a classic favorite and are sure to leave your guests impressed with your culinary prowess.

In Conclusion

Savory pumpkin and sage biscuits are a delightful way to welcome the flavors of fall into your kitchen. Their warm, comforting taste and fluffy texture make them a perfect accompaniment to any autumn meal. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these biscuits are a must-try for anyone who appreciates the magic of fall flavors. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the taste of the season with these delicious biscuits.

So, what are you waiting for? Embrace the flavors of fall and treat yourself to these delightful savory pumpkin and sage biscuits today!
