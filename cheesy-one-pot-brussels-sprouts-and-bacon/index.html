Cheesy One Pot Brussels Sprouts and Bacon


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy side dish to elevate your dinner, this Cheesy One Pot Brussels Sprouts and Bacon recipe is the perfect choice. Packed with flavor and simple to make, this dish will impress your family and friends. The combination of crispy bacon, tender Brussels sprouts, and gooey cheese creates a mouthwatering experience that will have everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 6 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Bacon: In a large skillet or pot, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until it’s crispy. Once done, remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside. Leave the bacon drippings in the skillet for added flavor.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same skillet with the bacon drippings, add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté them until they become fragrant and the onion turns translucent.

  3. Add the Brussels Sprouts: Add the trimmed and halved Brussels sprouts to the skillet and cook for a few minutes, allowing them to brown slightly.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, then bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Let the Brussels sprouts cook in the creamy liquid until they become tender but still retain a slight bite.

  5. Melt the Cheese: Once the Brussels sprouts are cooked to your liking, sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the top. Stir the mixture gently until the cheese melts and coats the Brussels sprouts and bacon.

  6. Season and Serve: Season the dish with salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle the crispy bacon pieces over the top, and garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  7. Enjoy: Serve the Cheesy One Pot Brussels Sprouts and Bacon hot and savor the delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Tips and Variations

  • For a smoky flavor, you can use smoked cheddar cheese instead of regular cheddar.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the finished dish.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese such as Gruyère or Parmesan for a unique twist.
  • To make this dish vegetarian, simply omit the bacon and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.

Conclusion

This Cheesy One Pot Brussels Sprouts and Bacon recipe is a fantastic way to enjoy the earthy flavor of Brussels sprouts combined with the irresistible richness of cheese and bacon. Whether you’re serving it alongside a hearty roast or as a standalone dish, this recipe is sure to be a hit. Try it out for your next gathering and watch as it disappears within minutes, leaving everyone asking for more!

So, there you have it—an indulgent yet simple dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Give this recipe a try and let the flavors speak for themselves!
