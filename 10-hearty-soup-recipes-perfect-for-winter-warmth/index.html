10 Hearty Soup Recipes Perfect for Winter Warmth


Cold winter days call for comforting soups that warm our souls and fill our stomachs. Hearty, robust, and loaded with flavors â€“ these 10 soup recipes will surely brighten up your winter dining experience.

1. Vegan Mushroom Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 4 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup milk (unsweetened soy, almond, or any non-dairy milk)
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Thyme (fresh or dried)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add diced onions and cook until they become soft and golden brown.
  2. Introduce mushrooms, and cook them for about five minutes until they become tender and aromatic.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth and milk, stirring occasionally.
  4. Season with salt, pepper and thyme. You can use fresh or dried thyme, according to your preference.
  5. Let the soup simmer for about 20 minutes to meld flavors.
  6. Serve hot with sliced bread.

2. Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup shredded chicken
  • Handful of noodles
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Thyme (fresh or dried)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Cook diced onions for about 5 minutes, until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add garlic, and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Introduce chicken broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Introduce shredded chicken and noodles; let it simmer for around 15 minutes.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and thyme.
  6. Garnish with green onions or parsley and serve hot.

3. Beef and Barley Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups cooked, cubed beef
  • 1 cup barley
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Thyme (fresh or dried)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Cook diced onions for about 5 minutes, until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Introduce beef broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Introduce cooked, cubed beef and barley; let it simmer for around 30 minutes.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and thyme.
  6. Serve hot.

4. Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup lentils
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Thyme (fresh or dried)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Cook diced onions for about 5 minutes, until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Introduce vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Introduce lentils; let it simmer for around 20-25 minutes or until lentils are cooked.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and thyme.
  6. Serve hot.

5. Roasted Red pepper Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 roasted red bell peppers
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onions and cook until they become soft and golden brown.
  2. Introduce minced garlic and the roasted red peppers. Cook for about five minutes until they are tender.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth and heavy cream.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Let the soup simmer for about 15-20 minutes to meld flavors.
  6. Serve hot with crusty bread.

6. Tomato and Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Basil (fresh or dried)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Cook diced onions for about 5 minutes, until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Introduce chopped tomatoes, vegetable broth, and Parmesan cheese. Stir well.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and basil.
  5. Let the soup simmer for about 15 minutes to meld flavors.
  6. Serve hot with crusty bread.

7. Cauliflower Cheese Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups cauliflower broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Cheddar cheese
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Cook diced onions for about 5 minutes, until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Introduce cauliflower broth and heavy cream.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Let the soup simmer for about 15 minutes to meld flavors.
  6. Stir in grated Cheddar cheese until well combined.
  7. Serve hot with crackers or bread.

8. Seafood Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups fish broth
  • 2 cups mixed seafood (shrimp, crab, mussels, clams)
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Cook diced onions for about 5 minutes, until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Introduce fish broth and let it come to a boil.
  4. Introduce mixed seafood; let it cook until the shells have turned red or opaque â€“ around 3-5 minutes depending on the seafood.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot with crusty bread.

9. Quinoa and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup chopped colorful vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, green beans)
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add diced onions and cook until they become soft and golden brown.
  2. Introduce minced garlic and cook for about a minute.
  3. Add vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Introduce cooked quinoa and mixed vegetables. Cook until vegetables are tender â€“ around 10 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot.

10. Corn Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups corn broth
  • 2 cups corn kernels
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Chives (chopped)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Cook diced onions for about 5 minutes, until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Introduce corn broth and cream. Stir well to combine.
  4. Add corn kernels and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Let the soup simmer for about 10-15 minutes to meld flavors.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with chopped chives.
