Fresh and Light Quinoa and Cucumber Salad


If you’re looking for a refreshing and nutritious dish that’s perfect for the summer, then this Fresh and Light Quinoa and Cucumber Salad is just what you need. Packed with protein, fiber, and vibrant flavors, this salad is a delightful addition to any meal. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, planning a picnic, or simply craving a healthy lunch, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds and nourish your body.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 large English cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Quinoa: Rinse the quinoa under cold water using a fine-mesh sieve. In a medium saucepan, bring the water or vegetable broth to a boil. Add the rinsed quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed and the quinoa is fluffy. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  2. Combine the Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, diced cucumber, chopped red onion, fresh parsley, fresh mint, and cherry tomatoes.

  3. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the quinoa and vegetable mixture. Gently toss to coat all the ingredients evenly.

  4. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Before serving, give the salad a gentle stir and adjust the seasoning if needed. This salad can be served chilled or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

This Fresh and Light Quinoa and Cucumber Salad is versatile and pairs well with a variety of dishes. Serve it as a side dish alongside grilled chicken, fish, or tofu for a complete and satisfying meal. You can also enjoy it on its own as a light and refreshing lunch option. For added texture and flavor, consider topping the salad with crumbled feta cheese, chopped avocado, or toasted nuts.

Health Benefits

Quinoa is a complete protein and an excellent source of fiber, making it a nutritious choice for vegetarians and vegans. Cucumbers are hydrating and low in calories, while fresh herbs like parsley and mint add a burst of freshness and contribute to overall well-being. This salad is not only delicious but also a great way to incorporate wholesome ingredients into your diet.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Fresh and Light Quinoa and Cucumber Salad is a simple yet elegant dish that celebrates the beauty of fresh, seasonal produce. Whether you’re following a healthy lifestyle or simply seeking a delightful culinary experience, this recipe is a must-try. With its vibrant colors, crisp textures, and zesty flavors, this salad is a celebration of summer on a plate. Try it out, and let it become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Cheers to good food and good health!
