Spiced Pear and Almond Frangipane Tart: A Sophisticated Dessert


As the seasons change and autumn’s warmth gives way to winter’s chill, our thoughts turn to comforting, warming desserts that evoke the coziness of the season. One such dessert is the Spiced Pear and Almond Frangipane Tart, a sophisticated and elegant treat that combines the sweetness of pears with the nutty flavor of almonds and the warmth of spices. This tart is perfect for a cold winter’s evening, served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup ice-cold water

For the frangipane:

  • 1 cup ground almonds
  • 1/2 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom

For the pear filling:

  • 2 ripe pears, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon orange liqueur (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions

Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour and confectioners’ sugar.
  3. Add the cold butter and use a pastry blender or your fingers to work it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually add the ice-cold water, stirring with a fork until the dough comes together in a ball.
  5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Frangipane

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the ground almonds, confectioners’ sugar, and softened butter.
  2. Beat in the eggs, almond extract, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cardamom until smooth.
  3. Set the frangipane aside.

Pear Filling

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced pears, granulated sugar, honey, orange liqueur (if using), cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  2. Toss the pears to coat them evenly with the sugar mixture.

Assembly and Baking

  1. Roll out the chilled crust dough on a lightly floured surface to a thickness of about 1/8 inch.
  2. Transfer the dough to a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom.
  3. Spread the frangipane evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
  4. Arrange the pear slices on top of the frangipane in a pattern that looks appealing to you.
  5. Fold the edges of the crust up over the filling, pressing gently to seal.
  6. Brush the crust with a little bit of milk or beaten egg and sprinkle with granulated sugar.
  7. Bake the tart for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is set.
  8. Allow the tart to cool completely on a wire rack.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the tart more festive, sprinkle a little bit of confectioners’ sugar on top of the crust before baking.
  • If you prefer a more intense almond flavor, you can add an additional 1/2 teaspoon of almond extract to the frangipane.
  • To make the tart more seasonal, you can add a few strips of candied citron or orange peel to the pear filling.
  • The tart can be made ahead of time and stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. Serve chilled, with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.

Conclusion

The Spiced Pear and Almond Frangipane Tart is a sophisticated and elegant dessert that is sure to impress your guests. With its combination of sweet and savory flavors, crunchy and smooth textures, and beautiful presentation, it’s the perfect treat for a special occasion or a cozy night in.
