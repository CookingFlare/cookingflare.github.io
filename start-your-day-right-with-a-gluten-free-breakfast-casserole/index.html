Start Your Day Right with a Gluten-Free Breakfast Casserole


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious way to kickstart your day? Look no further than this mouthwatering gluten-free breakfast casserole! Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, this breakfast casserole is the perfect way to fuel your morning. Whether you follow a gluten-free diet or simply love a hearty breakfast, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to whip up this delectable gluten-free breakfast casserole.

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup of milk (or dairy-free milk alternative)
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound of gluten-free breakfast sausage, crumbled and cooked
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cups of frozen hash browns
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese (or dairy-free cheese alternative)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  3. Add the cooked breakfast sausage, diced bell peppers, onion, and frozen hash browns to the bowl, and mix everything together until evenly distributed.

  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top.

  5. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the casserole is set and the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  6. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This gluten-free breakfast casserole is incredibly versatile and pairs well with a variety of sides. Consider serving it with a side of fresh fruit, avocado slices, or a dollop of Greek yogurt for added creaminess. You can also customize it with your favorite toppings such as salsa, hot sauce, or sliced avocado.

Benefits of a Gluten-Free Breakfast Casserole

Not only is this breakfast casserole incredibly tasty, but it also offers a range of health benefits. The combination of protein from the eggs and sausage, along with the nutrients from the bell peppers and hash browns, makes it a well-rounded and satisfying meal to start your day.

Additionally, by using gluten-free ingredients, this casserole is suitable for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, ensuring that everyone at the breakfast table can enjoy a hearty and wholesome meal without compromise.

Make-Ahead Option

One of the best things about this gluten-free breakfast casserole is that it can be prepared in advance, making it an ideal option for busy mornings or entertaining guests. Simply assemble the casserole the night before, cover it with foil, and refrigerate. In the morning, pop it in the oven, and voila – a hot and delicious breakfast is ready to be enjoyed without any morning rush.

Final Thoughts

With its delightful flavors, nutritional benefits, and make-ahead convenience, this gluten-free breakfast casserole is a winning choice for anyone seeking a satisfying and stress-free morning meal. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, feeding a hungry family, or simply treating yourself to a special breakfast, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So, why not give it a try and start your day right with a slice of this delectable gluten-free breakfast casserole?
