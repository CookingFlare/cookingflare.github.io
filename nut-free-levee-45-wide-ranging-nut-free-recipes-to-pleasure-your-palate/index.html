Nut-Free Levee: 45 Wide-ranging Nut-Free Recipes to Pleasure Your Palate


Food allergies have changed the way we think about cooking and meal planning. According to studies, nut allergies in particular have become quite common. This has led to an increasing demand for delicious, nut-free recipes that do not compromise on taste or texture. If you are seeking to explore the exciting realm of nut-free cooking, look no further! Our comprehensive collection of 45 nut-free recipes promises to cater to every cravings and cooking goals. With a wide selection of meals, desserts and snacks, we assure you, your palate will be nothing short of delighted.

Kick-start Your Morning with Nut-Free Delights

Who says nut-free is boring? See for yourself with these delightful morning meals.

1. Almond Milk and Berries Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 frozen banana, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • Mixed berries (frozen or fresh)
  • Optional: honey, granola

Instructions:

  1. Blend almond milk and the frozen banana until smooth.
  2. Pour the smoothie into a bowl and top generously with mixed berries and chia seeds.
  3. Sweeten with honey and sprinkle granola for an extra crunch.

2. Spiced Banana and Oat Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 cup oats (ground into a flour)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Cooking oil or butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients together until well blended.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add a small amount of oil or butter.
  3. Pour the batter in circles on the heated pan, cook until bubbles form and flip. Repeat with remaining batter.

Nut-Free Snacks You Cannot Miss

Whoever said, nutrients are only in the nuts, was utterly mistaken. Here are some delicious and nutritious treats you can enjoy.

3. Roasted Chickpeas

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and patted dry
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss chickpeas in oil, cumin, paprika, and salt.
  2. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 30 minutes, shaking the pan halfway through.

4. Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Paprika or chili powder for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Cut sweet potatoes into fry shapes.
  2. Toss with oil, salt, and place on baking sheet. Roast for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through.

Nut-Free Meals from Every Corner of the Globe

Far from being limited in taste, our nut-free recipes from all over the world carry a unique charm.

5. Vegan Mujadara

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 2 medium onions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon allspice
  • 1/2 cup fried onions (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pot over medium heat, sauté onions in oil with garlic until soft and golden.
  2. Add lentils, water, and allspice. Simmer for about 30 minutes or until lentils are cooked.
  3. Add cooked rice and mix gently. Cook for another 10 minutes, then garnish with fresh parsley and fried onions.

6. Zucchini Noodles (Zoodles) with Avocado Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 3 zucchini
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice from 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Crushed red pepper (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Use a spiralizer or a vegetable peeler to create zucchini noodles.
  2. Blend the avocado, herbs, garlic, lemon juice, oil, salt, and pepper in a food processor until smooth. If you like a bit of heat, add crushed red pepper.
  3. Toss zucchini noodles with a generous amount of the avocado pesto.

Indulge in Nut-Free Desserts

Indulgence knows no bounds even when nuts are absent.

7. Nut-Free Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Cream together butter and sugars, add the vanilla and eggs. Fold in the flour mixture and stir in the chocolate chips.
  3. Drop by spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes or until cookies are golden and set.

8. Coconut Cream Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 can (14 oz.) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • Whipped cream and extra coconut flakes for garnish

Instructions for filling:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Arrange crust in a pie plate. In saucepan, combine milk, cornstarch, salt, and sugar. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened.
  2. Stir 1/2 cup of hot mixture into beaten egg yolks. Return all to pan and stir until boiling. Cook 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla, condensed milk, and coconut. Pour into pie crust, top with coconut flakes and bake for 25 minutes or until lightly browned.

Instructions for whipped cream:

  1. In a bowl, beat whipping cream until stiff; spread over top of pie. Garnish with additional whipped cream and coconut flakes.
