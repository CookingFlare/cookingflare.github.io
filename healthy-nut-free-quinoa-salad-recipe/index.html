Healthy Nut-Free Quinoa Salad Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious salad recipe that is not only nut-free but also packed with protein and fiber? Look no further! This Healthy Nut-Free Quinoa Salad is not only easy to make but also bursting with flavor and goodness. Whether you’re preparing a quick lunch for yourself or hosting a gathering, this recipe is sure to impress even the pickiest eaters.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Quinoa: Rinse the quinoa under cold water using a fine-mesh strainer to remove any bitterness. In a saucepan, combine the quinoa and water or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed. Fluff with a fork and let it cool.

  2. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

  3. Assemble the Salad: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, parsley, and mint. Gently toss to mix the ingredients evenly.

  4. Add the Dressing: Pour the dressing over the quinoa salad and toss until everything is well coated.

  5. Final Touch: If desired, sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the salad for an extra burst of flavor.

  6. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Nutritional Benefits

This Nut-Free Quinoa Salad is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a powerhouse of nutrients. Quinoa is a complete protein, containing all nine essential amino acids, making it an excellent choice for vegetarians and vegans. Additionally, it is rich in fiber, iron, magnesium, and antioxidants. The fresh vegetables in this salad provide an array of vitamins and minerals, while the olive oil offers heart-healthy monounsaturated fats.

Customization

Feel free to customize this recipe based on your preferences and dietary restrictions. You can add or substitute ingredients such as chopped spinach, diced avocado, grilled chicken, or chickpeas to make it even more satisfying and diverse.

Wrapping Up

This Healthy Nut-Free Quinoa Salad is not only a feast for the senses but also a celebration of nutritious ingredients coming together in harmony. Whether you’re seeking a light and refreshing meal or a vibrant side dish, this recipe is a winner. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to this wholesome and flavorsome creation!
