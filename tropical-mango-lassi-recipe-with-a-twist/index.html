Tropical Mango Lassi Recipe with a Twist


If you’re a fan of the classic mango lassi, get ready to take your taste buds on a tropical adventure with this unique twist on the traditional recipe. This tropical mango lassi recipe combines the luscious sweetness of ripe mangoes with the refreshing tang of tropical fruits, resulting in a delightful beverage that’s perfect for a hot summer day or any time you’re craving a taste of the tropics.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/4 cup papaya chunks
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • Crushed ice

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the diced mangoes, plain yogurt, and coconut milk. Blend until smooth and creamy.

  2. Add the pineapple chunks, papaya chunks, honey, and ground cardamom to the blender. Blend until all the ingredients are well combined.

  3. Once the mixture is smooth and well blended, add a handful of crushed ice to the blender. Blend until the ice is completely incorporated and the lassi has a slushy consistency.

  4. Taste the lassi and adjust the sweetness by adding more honey if desired.

  5. Pour the tropical mango lassi into tall glasses and garnish with a slice of fresh mango or a sprinkle of ground cardamom for a touch of elegance.

  6. Serve the lassi immediately and enjoy the tropical flavors in every sip.

The Twist

What sets this tropical mango lassi apart from the traditional version is the addition of pineapple and papaya, which bring a bright and sunny flavor to the drink. The creamy texture of the coconut milk adds a luxurious richness, while the hint of cardamom provides a subtle warmth that enhances the overall tropical experience.

The combination of mango, pineapple, and papaya not only creates a vibrant flavor profile but also infuses the lassi with a boost of vitamins and minerals, making it a refreshing and nutritious treat.

Serving Suggestions

This tropical mango lassi is a versatile beverage that can be enjoyed on its own as a delicious and healthy snack. It also pairs beautifully with spicy dishes, such as curries or grilled meats, as the coolness of the lassi helps to balance out the heat of the spices.

For a fun twist, try freezing the lassi mixture in popsicle molds for a delightful frozen treat that’s sure to be a hit with kids and adults alike.

Final Thoughts

With its exotic blend of tropical fruits and creamy yogurt, this tropical mango lassi with a twist is a refreshing departure from the ordinary. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply want to treat yourself to something special, this recipe is sure to transport you to a sun-drenched paradise with every sip. So, go ahead, whip up a batch of this delightful lassi, and let its tropical flavors whisk you away to a blissful escape.

So there you have it, a tantalizing twist on the classic mango lassi that’s bound to become a new favorite in your recipe collection. Cheers to enjoying the flavors of the tropics in every sip!
