Weeknight Pasta Dinners for Two: Quick and Tasty


Welcome to our quick and tasty weeknight pasta dinners for two! We understand the struggle of finding time to cook a delicious and satisfying meal after a long day at work. That’s why we’ve put together a collection of simple yet flavorful pasta recipes that are perfect for those busy evenings when you just want to relax and enjoy a great meal with your partner. Let’s dive right in and explore these mouthwatering recipes together.

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Let it cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss to coat the pasta with the creamy garlic sauce.
  5. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Lemon Asparagus Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the asparagus pieces to the skillet and sauté for 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Toss in the cooked spaghetti, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Stir to combine.
  5. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and toss to coat.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy the refreshing flavors of this lemon asparagus pasta.

Spicy Arrabbiata Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until the garlic is golden and the red pepper flakes are fragrant.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and sugar. Simmer the sauce for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the cooked penne to the saucepan and toss to coat the pasta with the spicy arrabbiata sauce.
  5. Season with salt to taste.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve hot.

These delicious and easy weeknight pasta dinners for two are perfect for those moments when you want to spend more time enjoying your meal and less time in the kitchen. We hope you give these recipes a try and create memorable dining experiences with your loved one. Happy cooking!
