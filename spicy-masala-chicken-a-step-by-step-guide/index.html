Spicy Masala Chicken: A Step-by-Step Guide


In the world of Indian cuisine, there's a dish that stands out from the rest for its symphony of flavors and bold spices - the Spicy Masala Chicken. This mouth-watering delicacy is a staple in many Indian households and is often served at special occasions. The magic lies in the harmony between the succulent chicken, the aromatic spices, and the perfect balance of heat and flavor. In this article, we will take you through a step-by-step guide to preparing Spicy Masala Chicken, ensuring that you and your loved ones can relish in its savory goodness. Ingredients Before we dive into the cooking process, let's start with the essential ingredients:

1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 medium onions, thinly sliced 4 cloves of garlic, minced 2 medium tomatoes, diced 2 teaspoons ginger paste 2 teaspoons lemon juice 1 teaspoon garam masala powder 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 2 tablespoons butter Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish Chopped scallions, for garnish

Step 1: Preparing the Marinade Marination is an essential step in cooking Spicy Masala Chicken. In this step, we will mix the marinade ingredients together.

In a large bowl, combine chicken pieces, ginger paste, garam masala powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Mix the marinade ingredients together, ensuring that the chicken is evenly coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

Step 2: Searing the Chicken In this step, we will sear the marinated chicken in a pan to give it a golden-brown color.

Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off. Sear the chicken in batches, being careful not to overcrowd the pan. Cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is browned. Transfer the seared chicken to a plate and set it aside.

Step 3: Cooking the Onions and Tomatoes In this step, we will cook the onions and tomatoes together, creating a flavorful base for our dish.

Heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in the same skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and cook until they are lightly golden, stirring occasionally. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, stirring constantly. Add the diced tomatoes, ginger paste, and lemon juice. Mix the ingredients together, ensuring that the tomatoes are coated in the flavorful sauce. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce thickens.

Step 4: Combining the Chicken and Sauce In this final step, we will combine the seared chicken and the flavorful sauce, creating a symphony of flavors and textures.

Add the seared chicken to the skillet with the onion and tomato sauce. Mix the chicken and sauce together, ensuring that the chicken is coated in the flavorful sauce. Add the butter and mix until it melts, creating a creamy sauce. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the chicken is fully coated.

Step 5: Serving The final step is the most enjoyable part - serving the Spicy Masala Chicken to your loved ones.

Garnish the dish with fresh cilantro leaves and chopped scallions. Serve hot, accompanied by basmati rice, naan bread, or roti. Enjoy the rich flavors and textures of this mouth-watering dish.

Conclusion Preparing Spicy Masala Chicken is an art that requires patience, attention to detail, and a willingness to experiment. With these simple steps, you can create a dish that is sure to impress even the most discerning palates. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a newcomer to the world of Indian cuisine, this recipe is a sure-fire way to delight your taste buds. So go ahead, give it a try, and savor the magic of Spicy Masala Chicken!
