Satisfying One Pot Beef and Broccoli


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s easy to prepare and requires minimal cleanup? Look no further than this One Pot Beef and Broccoli recipe! This classic Asian-inspired dish is a crowd-pleaser and perfect for a cozy night in or a busy weeknight dinner. Packed with flavor and healthy ingredients, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your cooking repertoire.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Cooked white rice, for serving
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes. Add the sliced flank steak to the marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Coat the Beef with Cornstarch: After marinating, remove the beef from the marinade and toss it in cornstarch until fully coated.

  3. Sear the Beef: Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot or deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef in a single layer and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.

  4. Cook the Broccoli and Onion: In the same pot, add the broccoli florets and sliced onion. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the seared beef to the pot with the broccoli and onion. Pour the remaining marinade over the beef and vegetables. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Serve the One Pot Beef and Broccoli over cooked white rice. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for an added pop of color and flavor.

Tips for Success:

  • Slice the Beef Thinly: For the most tender and flavorful results, be sure to slice the flank steak against the grain into thin strips.
  • Don’t Overcook the Broccoli: To retain the vibrant green color and crisp texture of the broccoli, avoid overcooking it. It should be tender-crisp when added to the dish.
  • Adjust the Heat: If you prefer a spicier dish, increase the amount of crushed red pepper flakes in the marinade to suit your taste.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

  • Effortless Cleanup: With only one pot required for cooking, cleanup is a breeze, giving you more time to enjoy your meal and less time spent washing dishes.
  • Wholesome and Flavorful: This dish combines lean protein from the beef and nutrient-rich broccoli, all infused with a savory and slightly sweet sauce that’s sure to please your taste buds.
  • Versatile and Customizable: Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, such as bell peppers or snap peas, for a personalized twist on this classic dish.

Whether you’re a cooking novice or a seasoned chef, this One Pot Beef and Broccoli recipe is a must-try. Its simplicity, bold flavors, and wholesome ingredients make it a standout dish that’s sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor every bite of this satisfying and comforting meal!
