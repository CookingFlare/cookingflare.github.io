Healthy and Delicious Greek Yogurt Guacamole with Veggie Sticks


If you’re looking for a healthy and delicious snack that’s perfect for summer gatherings or just a midday pick-me-up, then you’re in for a treat. This Greek Yogurt Guacamole with Veggie Sticks is a refreshing twist on the classic guacamole, packed with nutrients and flavor. It’s the perfect way to satisfy your cravings while still sticking to your health goals.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Assorted veggie sticks (carrots, cucumbers, bell peppers, etc.) for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Avocados: Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

  2. Mash the Avocados: Using a fork, mash the avocados to your desired consistency. Some prefer chunky guacamole, while others like it smooth – it’s all up to you!

  3. Add the Greek Yogurt: Once the avocados are mashed, add the Greek yogurt to the bowl. This is where the magic happens – the Greek yogurt adds a creamy and tangy element to the guacamole while boosting its protein content.

  4. Mix in the Flavor: Add the red onion, tomatoes, cilantro, minced garlic, and lime juice to the bowl. Gently fold the ingredients together until everything is well combined. The colors and aromas coming together at this stage are simply irresistible.

  5. Season to Perfection: Season the guacamole with salt and pepper to taste. Don’t be shy with the seasoning – it’s what elevates the flavors and makes this guacamole truly irresistible.

  6. Chill and Serve: Cover the guacamole with plastic wrap, ensuring the wrap touches the surface of the guacamole to prevent browning. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. When you’re ready to serve, prepare an assortment of veggie sticks for dipping.

  7. Enjoy: Serve the chilled Greek Yogurt Guacamole with the colorful array of veggie sticks, and get ready to indulge in a guilt-free and satisfying snack.

Health Benefits

This Greek Yogurt Guacamole isn’t just a treat for your taste buds – it’s also packed with nutritional benefits. Avocados are rich in heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, while Greek yogurt adds a protein punch. The veggies sticks provide a crunchy and fiber-filled accompaniment that makes this snack a wholesome choice.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re hosting a summer get-together or simply craving a nutritious snack, this Healthy and Delicious Greek Yogurt Guacamole with Veggie Sticks is a winner. It’s easy to prepare, bursting with flavor, and offers a healthier twist on a beloved classic. So, next time you’re in the mood for something tasty and wholesome, whip up a batch of this guacamole and enjoy the goodness of fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors.

So, what are you waiting for? Get your ingredients ready and treat yourself to a bowl of this delightful guacamole – your taste buds and your body will thank you!
