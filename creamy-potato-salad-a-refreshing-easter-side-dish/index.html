Creamy Potato Salad: A Refreshing Easter Side Dish


Potato salad is a classic dish that graces the tables of many family gatherings and social events. With Easter just around the corner, it’s time to think about refreshing and delicious side dishes that will complement your Easter feast. Look no further than this creamy potato salad recipe that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of white wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of diced celery
  • 1/4 cup of diced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Place the cubed potatoes in a large pot and cover them with cold water. Add a pinch of salt to the water. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat and cook the potatoes until they are tender when pierced with a fork, about 10-15 minutes.

  2. While the potatoes are cooking, prepare the dressing. In a medium bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, Dijon mustard, white wine vinegar, salt, and black pepper until well combined.

  3. Once the potatoes are cooked, drain them and transfer them to a large bowl. Let them cool for a few minutes.

  4. Add the diced celery, diced red onion, chopped fresh dill, and chopped fresh parsley to the bowl with the potatoes.

  5. Pour the prepared dressing over the potatoes and gently toss everything together until the potatoes are evenly coated with the dressing and the vegetables are well distributed throughout.

  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. When ready to serve, give the potato salad a quick stir and taste for seasoning. Adjust the salt and pepper if needed.

  8. Transfer the potato salad to a serving bowl and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh dill and parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • For added texture and flavor, you can add some crispy bacon bits to the potato salad just before serving.
  • If you prefer a tangier dressing, you can increase the amount of Dijon mustard and white wine vinegar.
  • For a twist on the classic potato salad, you can add some hard-boiled eggs, chopped pickles, or even a little grated cheese.
  • Make sure to use waxy potatoes like Yukon Gold or red potatoes for this recipe as they hold their shape better when cooked.

Now that you have this creamy potato salad recipe in your arsenal, it’s time to impress your guests with a refreshing and delicious Easter side dish. The combination of creamy dressing, tender potatoes, and crunchy vegetables will make this dish a crowd favorite. Enjoy the flavors of spring with this delightful potato salad that will surely become a staple at your Easter celebrations. Happy cooking!
