Irresistible Salted Caramel Apple Crumble: A Sweet and Salty Dessert


Are you looking for a dessert that combines the comforting flavors of warm apples, sweet caramel, and buttery crumble? Look no further! Our irresistible Salted Caramel Apple Crumble is the perfect blend of sweet and salty, creating a dessert that will have your taste buds dancing with delight.

Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 4 large apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Crumble Topping:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed

For the Salted Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or non-stick cooking spray.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, all-purpose flour, ground cinnamon, and salt. Toss until the apples are evenly coated in the mixture.

  3. In a separate bowl, prepare the crumble topping by combining the all-purpose flour, rolled oats, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and salt. Mix well. Add the cold, cubed butter and use your hands or a pastry cutter to incorporate the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Spread the apple filling evenly in the greased baking dish. Sprinkle the crumble topping over the apples, covering them completely.

  5. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 40-45 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the crumble topping is golden brown.

  6. While the crumble is baking, prepare the salted caramel sauce. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water. Heat over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved. Once the mixture starts to boil, stop stirring and let it cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until it turns a deep amber color.

  7. Remove the saucepan from the heat and carefully pour in the heavy cream. Be cautious as it may splatter. Stir until the cream is fully incorporated.

  8. Return the saucepan to low heat and add the unsalted butter and sea salt. Cook, stirring constantly, until the butter has melted and the sauce is smooth and glossy.

  9. Once the crumble is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool slightly. Drizzle the warm salted caramel sauce over the crumble, allowing it to seep into the nooks and crannies.

  10. Serve the Salted Caramel Apple Crumble warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Enjoy the perfect balance of sweet and salty flavors!

Conclusion

Indulge in the irresistible combination of sweet, tender apples, buttery crumble, and rich salted caramel sauce with our Salted Caramel Apple Crumble. This dessert is the ultimate comfort food, perfect for cozy evenings or special occasions. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself, this sweet and salty dessert will leave a lasting impression. Give it a try and experience the delightful flavors for yourself. Happy baking!
