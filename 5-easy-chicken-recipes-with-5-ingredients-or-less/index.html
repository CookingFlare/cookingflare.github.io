5 Easy Chicken Recipes with 5 Ingredients or Less


Chicken is one of the most versatile proteins out there, and when you’re pressed for time or want a simple yet delicious meal, you don’t have to go overboard with ingredients. Sometimes, the best meals come from the simplest recipes, with minimal effort and maximum flavor. If you love chicken but don’t want to spend too much time in the kitchen, these five easy chicken recipes with five ingredients or less will help you get dinner on the table in no time.

1. Garlic Herb Chicken Breast

This garlic herb chicken breast recipe is perfect for a quick, flavorful dinner. It takes only a few simple ingredients but packs a punch with aromatic herbs and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Start by heating the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and dried thyme.
  3. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook for about 5-7 minutes on each side until the chicken is cooked through and golden brown.
  4. In the last minute of cooking, add the minced garlic to the skillet, stirring it around the chicken to avoid burning. Let it cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Serve the chicken hot, garnished with additional thyme if desired. It pairs perfectly with a side of vegetables or a light salad.

This simple garlic herb chicken is the epitome of easy comfort food. The thyme gives the dish a wonderful earthiness, while the garlic adds a rich depth of flavor. You can also substitute fresh herbs if you have them on hand.

2. Honey Mustard Chicken Thighs

If you love the sweet and tangy combination of honey and mustard, this honey mustard chicken thighs recipe is the perfect go-to for an easy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine the honey and Dijon mustard, stirring until smooth.
  3. Pat the chicken thighs dry with paper towels and season them with salt and pepper.
  4. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken thighs, skin side down, and cook for about 5-6 minutes until the skin is crispy and golden.
  5. Flip the chicken over, then pour the honey mustard sauce over the chicken thighs.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F (74°C).
  7. Serve the chicken with your favorite side dish, such as roasted potatoes or steamed vegetables.

The combination of honey and mustard in this recipe creates a balanced, sweet, and tangy glaze that perfectly complements the richness of the chicken thighs. This dish is incredibly easy but looks and tastes impressive enough for dinner guests.

3. Lemon Pepper Chicken Wings

Lemon pepper chicken wings are crispy, zesty, and absolutely delicious. This recipe uses minimal ingredients but results in crispy, flavorful wings that everyone will love.

Ingredients:

  • 10-12 chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon of freshly cracked black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Pat the chicken wings dry with paper towels to ensure they crisp up nicely.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the chicken wings with olive oil, lemon zest, black pepper, and salt until evenly coated.
  4. Arrange the wings in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping the wings halfway through the cooking time to ensure they are evenly crispy.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra lemon zest or a squeeze of lemon juice for an added citrus burst.

These lemon pepper chicken wings are incredibly easy to make and have a bright, zesty flavor from the fresh lemon and black pepper. They are perfect as a snack, appetizer, or even a main dish when paired with your favorite sides.

4. Crispy Baked Chicken Tenders

Who doesn’t love crispy chicken tenders? This simple recipe gives you perfectly golden, crunchy chicken tenders without the need for frying. They’re perfect for a healthy but delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken tenders
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs (use panko for extra crispiness)
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the breadcrumbs, salt, pepper, and paprika (if using) in a shallow dish and mix together.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the chicken tenders and toss them to coat evenly.
  4. Roll the chicken tenders in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing lightly to ensure the crumbs stick.
  5. Arrange the coated chicken tenders on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce, such as ketchup, honey mustard, or barbecue sauce.

These crispy baked chicken tenders are simple, tasty, and healthier than the deep-fried version. The breadcrumb coating gives them the perfect crunch, while the tender chicken inside stays juicy. They’re a family favorite and easy to make on busy nights.

5. BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

This BBQ chicken drumstick recipe is all about simplicity. With just a few ingredients, you can create a delicious, flavorful meal that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat (about 400°F or 200°C).
  2. Drizzle olive oil over the chicken drumsticks and season them with salt, pepper, and garlic powder if using.
  3. Place the drumsticks on the grill or in the oven and cook for about 25-30 minutes, turning occasionally to ensure they cook evenly and don’t burn.
  4. Brush the BBQ sauce on the drumsticks during the last 5-10 minutes of cooking, allowing it to caramelize and become sticky.
  5. Once the chicken has reached an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C), remove from heat and serve with extra BBQ sauce on the side.

These BBQ chicken drumsticks are incredibly easy to make and bursting with smoky, tangy flavors from the BBQ sauce. Whether you’re grilling outside or using your oven, these drumsticks are sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Conclusion

These five easy chicken recipes with five ingredients or less are proof that you don’t need a long list of ingredients to make a delicious meal. Whether you’re craving garlic herb chicken, crispy baked tenders, or BBQ drumsticks, each of these recipes will help you create a flavorful, satisfying dish without spending hours in the kitchen. With just a handful of pantry staples, you can make dinner stress-free and delicious. Give these recipes a try the next time you’re looking for a simple yet tasty chicken dinner. Enjoy!
