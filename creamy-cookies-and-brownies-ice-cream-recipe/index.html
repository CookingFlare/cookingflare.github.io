Creamy Cookies and Brownies Ice Cream Recipe


Are you a fan of both cookies and brownies? How about combining them into a delightful, homemade ice cream? If that sounds like something you’d love to try, then you’re in for a treat! This Creamy Cookies and Brownies Ice Cream recipe brings together the best of both worlds - rich, chocolatey brownies and the delightful crunch of cookies, all in a creamy, dreamy ice cream base.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup crumbled chocolate brownies
  • 1 cup crushed chocolate cookies
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Base: In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and half of the sugar. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it begins to simmer. Remove from the heat.

  2. Whisk the Egg Yolks: In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks with the remaining sugar until the mixture is pale and slightly thickened.

  3. Temper the Eggs: Slowly pour a small amount of the warm cream mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly. This will temper the eggs and prevent them from scrambling when added to the hot liquid.

  4. Combine and Heat: Pour the tempered egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining cream mixture. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon.

  5. Chill the Base: Remove the mixture from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Transfer the base to a bowl and cover with plastic wrap, ensuring the wrap touches the surface of the mixture. This prevents a skin from forming. Chill the base in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.

  6. Churn the Ice Cream: Once the base is thoroughly chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.

  7. Add the Goodies: During the last few minutes of churning, add in the crumbled brownies, crushed cookies, and chocolate chips. The ice cream should be thick and creamy with the goodies evenly distributed.

  8. Freeze: Transfer the churned ice cream into a freezer-safe container, smoothing the top with a spatula. Cover the surface with parchment paper or plastic wrap directly on the ice cream to prevent ice crystals from forming.

  9. Enjoy!: Freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours or until firm. When ready to indulge, scoop out generous portions of this creamy, cookies-and-brownies-filled delight and savor every spoonful.

Tips for Success

  • Make sure the base is thoroughly chilled before churning to achieve a creamy consistency.
  • Experiment with different types of cookies and brownies for unique flavor combinations.
  • For an extra indulgent treat, drizzle some warm fudge sauce over your scoops of ice cream.

With its rich, velvety texture and irresistible mix-ins, this Creamy Cookies and Brownies Ice Cream is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a decadent dessert, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to churn up a batch of this delectable homemade ice cream that perfectly marries the flavors of cookies and brownies. Your taste buds will thank you!
