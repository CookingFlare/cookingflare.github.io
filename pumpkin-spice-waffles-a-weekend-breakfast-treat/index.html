Pumpkin Spice Waffles: A Weekend Breakfast Treat


Who doesn’t love a cozy and delicious breakfast on a lazy weekend morning? If you’re in the mood for something warm, fragrant, and full of fall flavors, then look no further than these Pumpkin Spice Waffles. Made with a delightful blend of seasonal spices and creamy pumpkin puree, these waffles are the perfect way to start your day. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • ½ cup pumpkin puree
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and salt. Make sure all the dry ingredients are well combined.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, pumpkin puree, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. The pumpkin puree will give the waffles a beautiful orange color and a hint of sweetness.

  3. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, whisking gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense waffles. A few lumps in the batter are perfectly fine.

  4. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions. Once it’s hot, lightly grease the waffle plates with non-stick cooking spray or a small amount of melted butter.

  5. Pour the batter onto the preheated waffle iron, using a ladle or measuring cup to ensure even distribution. The amount of batter will depend on the size of your waffle iron. Close the lid and cook until the waffles are golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes.

  6. Carefully remove the waffles from the iron and place them on a wire rack to cool slightly. This will help them maintain their crispness.

  7. Serve the Pumpkin Spice Waffles warm with your favorite toppings. Some delicious options include a dollop of whipped cream, a drizzle of maple syrup, a sprinkle of powdered sugar, or a handful of chopped pecans for added crunch.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a teaspoon of pumpkin pie spice to the dry ingredients. This will intensify the warm and aromatic notes in the waffles.
  • If you prefer a lighter texture, you can separate the egg whites and yolks. Whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form, then gently fold them into the batter before cooking the waffles. This will result in a fluffier and airier texture.
  • Feel free to experiment with different toppings and accompaniments to make these waffles your own. Sliced bananas, a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar, or a scoop of vanilla ice cream can take these waffles to the next level.

Conclusion

Pumpkin Spice Waffles are the ultimate weekend breakfast treat. With their irresistible aroma and cozy flavors, they are sure to become a family favorite. Whether you enjoy them on a chilly autumn morning or as a special treat throughout the year, these waffles are guaranteed to bring joy and deliciousness to your table. So, grab your waffle iron, whip up a batch of these delightful waffles, and savor every bite. Happy cooking!
