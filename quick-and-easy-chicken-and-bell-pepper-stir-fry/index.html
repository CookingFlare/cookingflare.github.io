Quick and Easy Chicken and Bell Pepper Stir Fry


Looking for a simple yet flavorful dish to whip up for dinner? Look no further than this Quick and Easy Chicken and Bell Pepper Stir Fry recipe. Packed with protein, vibrant bell peppers, and a savory stir fry sauce, this dish is a surefire way to satisfy your taste buds without spending hours in the kitchen. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to elevate your weeknight dinner game!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by slicing the chicken, bell peppers, and onion. Mince the garlic and grate the ginger. Having everything prepped and ready to go will make the cooking process smooth and efficient.

  2. Make the Stir Fry Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Cook the Chicken: Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add one tablespoon of vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the sliced chicken to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper, and cook until the chicken is no longer pink and starts to brown. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Sauté the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Toss in the sliced bell peppers and onion, and sauté until they are tender yet still crisp, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Pour the prepared stir fry sauce over the chicken and vegetables, and toss everything together to ensure the sauce coats the ingredients evenly. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken and vegetables are coated in the savory stir fry sauce, it’s time to serve. Spoon the stir fry over cooked rice or noodles, and garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor and color.

Tips for Success

  • Customization: Feel free to customize the stir fry by adding your favorite vegetables such as broccoli, snap peas, or mushrooms.
  • Meal Prep: This stir fry is perfect for meal prep. Make a larger batch and portion it out for quick and convenient lunches throughout the week.
  • Protein Swap: Not a fan of chicken? You can easily substitute it with thinly sliced beef, shrimp, or tofu for a different twist.

Final Thoughts

This Quick and Easy Chicken and Bell Pepper Stir Fry is a versatile and satisfying dish that can be on your table in no time. With its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and simple preparation, it’s a recipe that’s sure to become a regular in your dinner rotation. Give it a try and savor the delightful combination of tender chicken, crisp bell peppers, and a savory stir fry sauce. Happy cooking!
