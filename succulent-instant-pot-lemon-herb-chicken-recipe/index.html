Succulent Instant Pot Lemon Herb Chicken Recipe


Are you craving a tender, juicy, and flavorful chicken dish that’s easy to make and bursting with fresh, zesty flavors? Look no further than this Succulent Instant Pot Lemon Herb Chicken recipe. With the magic of the Instant Pot, you can achieve perfectly cooked chicken infused with the bright and aromatic essence of lemon and herbs in no time. Let’s dive into the delightful world of this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and black pepper. This will lay the foundation for a well-seasoned and succulent dish.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the minced garlic, dried thyme, rosemary, and oregano. Sauté for a minute until the garlic becomes fragrant and the herbs release their flavors.

  3. Sear the Chicken: Add the seasoned chicken breasts to the Instant Pot and brown them on both sides for about 2 minutes per side. This step locks in the juices and adds a beautiful golden color to the chicken.

  4. Add the Liquid and Flavorings: Pour in the chicken broth, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and lemon zest. The combination of these ingredients will infuse the chicken with a bright and tangy flavor profile.

  5. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set it to high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release.

  6. Finish with Freshness: Carefully remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and set it aside. Stir the chopped parsley into the cooking liquid to add a final burst of freshness and color.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Slice the succulent lemon herb chicken, and drizzle it with the flavorful cooking liquid. Pair it with your favorite sides such as roasted vegetables, steamed rice, or a crisp garden salad.

Tips for Success:

  • For an extra layer of flavor, consider marinating the chicken in the lemon juice, olive oil, and herbs for 30 minutes before cooking.
  • Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences. You can enhance the citrusy notes by adding a touch of lemon zest or increase the herbal aroma with a sprinkle of fresh thyme or rosemary.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can create a slurry by mixing a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of water and adding it to the cooking liquid before simmering.

Final Thoughts:

This Succulent Instant Pot Lemon Herb Chicken recipe is a testament to the incredible convenience and flavor that the Instant Pot brings to your kitchen. It’s a perfect choice for a quick weeknight dinner or a special meal with loved ones. The harmonious blend of zesty lemon, aromatic herbs, and tender chicken will surely elevate your culinary experience. Give this recipe a try, and savor the delightful flavors that await you!

So there you have it - a delightful Succulent Instant Pot Lemon Herb Chicken recipe that’s sure to become a new favorite in your cooking repertoire. The next time you’re seeking a dish that’s both convenient and bursting with flavor, turn to your Instant Pot and let the magic unfold!
