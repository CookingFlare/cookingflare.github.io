Pumpkin Spice Latte Overnight Chia Pudding: A Fall Twist on a Classic


With fall just around the corner, it’s time to start thinking about cozy, comforting recipes that capture the essence of the season. What better way to welcome the crisp autumn air than with a delicious and nutritious Pumpkin Spice Latte Overnight Chia Pudding? This recipe puts a seasonal twist on the beloved classic, infusing it with the warm flavors of pumpkin spice and the creamy richness of a latte. Not only is this pudding a treat for your taste buds, but it’s also a powerhouse of nutrients, making it the perfect breakfast or snack to fuel your day.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chia seeds
  • 2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 2-3 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin spice blend
  • 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee or espresso
  • Whipped coconut cream (optional, for serving)
  • Chopped pecans or walnuts (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chia seeds and almond milk. Stir well to ensure the chia seeds are evenly distributed.
  2. Add the pumpkin puree, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and pumpkin spice blend to the chia seed mixture. Mix until all the ingredients are well combined.
  3. Pour in the strong brewed coffee or espresso and stir until fully incorporated.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a lid and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or preferably overnight to allow the chia seeds to absorb the liquid and form a pudding-like consistency.
  5. Once the pudding has set, give it a good stir to ensure the flavors are well blended.
  6. Serve the Pumpkin Spice Latte Overnight Chia Pudding in individual jars or bowls, topped with a dollop of whipped coconut cream and a sprinkle of chopped pecans or walnuts for an extra touch of indulgence.

Nutritional Benefits

This Pumpkin Spice Latte Overnight Chia Pudding is not only a delightful treat but also a nutritional powerhouse. Chia seeds are loaded with fiber, protein, and omega-3 fatty acids, making them a great addition to your diet. Pumpkin puree is rich in beta-carotene, a precursor to vitamin A, which is essential for eye health and immune function. The addition of coffee or espresso provides a gentle caffeine kick to help you start your day on the right foot.

Serving Suggestions

This fall-inspired chia pudding is versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. You can layer it with granola and fresh fruit to create a parfait, or use it as a creamy filling for stuffed crepes. You can even blend it into a smoothie for a delicious and nutritious twist. The possibilities are endless, so don’t be afraid to get creative with how you enjoy this seasonal delight.

Final Thoughts

Pumpkin Spice Latte Overnight Chia Pudding is the perfect way to savor the flavors of fall in a wholesome and satisfying treat. Whether you’re a pumpkin spice aficionado or just looking for a new breakfast option, this recipe is sure to become a seasonal favorite. So, embrace the autumn vibes and indulge in this delightful Pumpkin Spice Latte Overnight Chia Pudding – your taste buds and body will thank you!

I hope you find this recipe for Pumpkin Spice Latte Overnight Chia Pudding as delightful to make and enjoy as I do. It’s the perfect way to welcome the fall season with open arms and a satisfied stomach.
