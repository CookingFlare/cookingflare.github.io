Korean Army Stew: A Hearty and Delicious Recipe


Are you ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight? Look no further than Korean Army Stew, also known as Budae Jjigae. This hearty and delicious dish originated in South Korea during the Korean War when food was scarce, and inventive locals combined leftover ingredients to create a flavorful stew that would sustain them. Today, Korean Army Stew has become a popular comfort food, loved for its rich flavors and the way it brings people together.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering stew, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon of gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of minced ginger
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of sliced cabbage
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1 cup of sliced tofu
  • 1 cup of instant ramen noodles
  • 1 cup of sliced Spam or hot dogs
  • 1 cup of sliced bacon
  • 1 cup of sliced kimchi
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the chicken broth and beef broth over medium heat. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  2. Add the gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce, minced garlic, and minced ginger to the pot. Stir well to combine and allow the flavors to meld together.

  3. Toss in the sliced onion, green onions, mushrooms, cabbage, and carrots. These vegetables add a wonderful depth of flavor and texture to the stew. Let them simmer for about 10 minutes until they start to soften.

  4. Now it’s time to add the protein! Add the sliced tofu, instant ramen noodles, Spam or hot dogs, and bacon to the pot. These ingredients represent the resourcefulness of the Korean War era when people used whatever they had on hand. Stir gently to ensure everything is evenly distributed.

  5. Finally, add the star ingredient - kimchi! This fermented cabbage adds a tangy and spicy kick to the stew. Stir it in and let the flavors meld for another 5 minutes.

  6. Season the stew with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to taste as you go and adjust the seasonings according to your preference.

  7. Reduce the heat to low and let the stew simmer for another 10 minutes, allowing all the flavors to come together in perfect harmony.

  8. Once the stew is done, serve it hot in individual bowls and garnish with fresh green onions. The combination of flavors and textures in each spoonful is sure to satisfy your cravings and warm your soul.

Enjoy the Experience

Korean Army Stew is not just a meal; it’s an experience. As you gather around the table with family and friends, sharing stories and laughter, you’ll appreciate the communal nature of this dish. The vibrant colors, the enticing aroma, and the explosion of flavors will transport you to the streets of Seoul, where food is a celebration of life.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and let the magic of Korean Army Stew unfold in your kitchen. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice cook, this recipe is sure to impress and leave everyone asking for seconds. Give it a try and discover why Korean Army Stew has captured the hearts and taste buds of people all around the world.
