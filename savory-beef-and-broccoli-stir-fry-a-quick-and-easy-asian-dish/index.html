Savory Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry: A Quick and Easy Asian Dish


Stir-fries are a staple in Asian cuisine, known for their vibrant flavors and quick cooking times. One classic stir-fry combination that never fails to satisfy is beef and broccoli. This savory dish brings together tender strips of beef, crisp broccoli florets, and a delectable sauce that coats every ingredient in a burst of umami. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create a delicious beef and broccoli stir-fry that is not only easy to make but also packed with flavor.

Ingredients

To make this savory beef and broccoli stir-fry, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and sesame oil. Set aside.

  2. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the beef slices and season with salt and pepper. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, until the beef is browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Add the broccoli florets and red bell pepper slices to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the skillet and pour the sauce mixture over the ingredients. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce thickens and coats the beef and vegetables.

  6. Remove from heat and let the stir-fry sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve the savory beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add some red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce to the stir-fry.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables such as snap peas, mushrooms, or carrots to customize the dish to your liking.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can substitute the beef with tofu or tempeh.
  • To make the dish gluten-free, use tamari or a gluten-free soy sauce instead of regular soy sauce.

Conclusion

This savory beef and broccoli stir-fry is a delightful Asian dish that brings together tender beef, crisp broccoli, and a flavorful sauce. With its quick cooking time and simple ingredients, it’s a perfect go-to recipe for busy weeknight dinners or when you’re craving a taste of Asian cuisine. Whether you’re a fan of stir-fries or new to cooking Asian dishes, give this recipe a try and enjoy a delicious meal that’s both satisfying and easy to prepare.
