Sliders with a Global Twist: Recipes from Around the World


Sliders are a favorite for many—easy to eat, versatile, and incredibly satisfying. As the world of culinary arts continues to evolve, we see sliders taking on various forms inspired by global cuisines. In this blog post, we’ll explore several delicious slider recipes from different parts of the world, each with its unique flair. Whether you're hosting a gathering or simply want to indulge in a flavorful meal, these sliders will inspire you to take a trip around the globe, right from your kitchen!

  1. Asian-Inspired Teriyaki Chicken Sliders Let’s kick things off with a taste of Asia! These Teriyaki Chicken Sliders are sweet, savory, and packed with flavor, making them a crowd-pleaser. Ingredients

1 lb ground chicken 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce 1 tablespoon soy sauce 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon ginger powder Salt and pepper to taste Slider buns 1 cup purple cabbage, shredded 1/4 cup mayonnaise 1 tablespoon lime juice Sliced green onions for garnish Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

Prepare the Chicken Mixture: In a bowl, combine ground chicken, teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, garlic powder, ginger powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined. Form the Sliders: Preheat your grill or skillet over medium heat. Shape the chicken mixture into small patties (approximately 2-3 inches wide). Cook the Sliders: Grill or pan-fry the patties for about 5-7 minutes on each side until fully cooked and golden brown. Make the Slaw: In a separate bowl, mix shredded purple cabbage, mayonnaise, lime juice, and a pinch of salt. Toss until well combined. Assemble the Sliders: Spread a generous spoonful of cabbage slaw on the bottom half of each slider bun, add a chicken patty, and top with sliced green onions and sesame seeds. Cap with the top bun. Serve and Enjoy: These sliders are best served immediately, allowing the savory flavors to shine through!

  1. Mexican Street Corn Sliders Transport yourself to the vibrant streets of Mexico with these unique Mexican Street Corn Sliders, inspired by elote, a popular Mexican grilled corn dish. Ingredients

1 cup corn kernels (fresh or canned) 1/2 cup cotija cheese, crumbled 1/2 teaspoon chili powder 1 tablespoon mayonnaise 1 tablespoon sour cream 1 tablespoon lime juice 1 teaspoon garlic powder Salt to taste Slider buns Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

Prepare the Corn Mixture: In a bowl, combine corn, cotija cheese, chili powder, mayonnaise, sour cream, lime juice, garlic powder, and salt. Mix thoroughly until bubbly and creamy. Form the Sliders: Shape the corn mixture into small patties using your hands. Cook the Sliders: Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil. Cook the corn patties for about 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown. Assemble the Sliders: Place a corn patty on the bottom half of each slider bun. Top with additional cotija cheese and fresh cilantro, then cap with the top bun. Serve: These sliders pair wonderfully with a refreshing drink. Enjoy the sweet and spicy flavors of Mexico!

  1. Mediterranean Lamb Sliders with Tzatziki Sauce Transport yourself to the Mediterranean coast with these savory Lamb Sliders, complemented by a refreshing tzatziki sauce. Ingredients

1 lb ground lamb 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon fresh oregano, chopped (or 1 teaspoon dried) 1 teaspoon ground cumin Salt and pepper to taste Slider buns 1 cucumber, shredded 1 cup Greek yogurt 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 tablespoon lemon juice Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions

Prepare the Lamb Mixture: In a bowl, combine ground lamb, minced garlic, oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Form the Sliders: Shape the lamb mixture into small patties. Cook the Sliders: Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side until they reach the desired doneness. Make the Tzatziki Sauce: In a separate bowl, combine shredded cucumber, Greek yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and dill. Mix until creamy. Assemble the Sliders: Spread tzatziki sauce on the bottom half of each slider bun. Place the lamb patty on top, add more sauce if desired, and cap with the top bun. Serve: These sliders are aromatic and delicious, making them perfect for any gathering or a Mediterranean-themed dinner night.

  1. Indian-Spiced Chickpea Sliders For our vegetarian friends, we present Indian-Spiced Chickpea Sliders that pack a punch with their vibrant flavors and fragrant spices. Ingredients

1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed 1/2 onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon curry powder 1 teaspoon garam masala 1/2 teaspoon turmeric 1/2 teaspoon cumin Salt and pepper to taste 1/4 cup breadcrumbs Slider buns Fresh mint and cilantro for garnish Yogurt sauce (optional, for serving)

Instructions

Prepare the Chickpea Mixture: In a large bowl, mash chickpeas with a fork until mostly smooth. Add onion, garlic, curry powder, garam masala, turmeric, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Gradually stir in breadcrumbs until the mixture holds together. Form the Sliders: Shape the mixture into small patties. Cook the Sliders: Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly oil it. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy. Assemble the Sliders: Place a chickpea patty on each slider bun. Top with fresh mint and cilantro. Serve: For an extra layer of flavor, drizzle yogurt sauce over the sliders before capping them with the top bun.

  1. Caribbean Jerk Pork Sliders Experience the bold flavors of the Caribbean with Jerk Pork Sliders that feature a spicy and aromatic rub. Ingredients

1 lb ground pork 2 tablespoons jerk seasoning 1 tablespoon brown sugar Salt to taste Slider buns Pineapple slices (grilled) Spicy mayo (mix mayonnaise with hot sauce)

Instructions

Prepare the Pork Mixture: In a bowl, mix ground pork, jerk seasoning, brown sugar, and salt until well combined. Form the Sliders: Shape the pork mixture into small patties. Cook the Sliders: Preheat a grill or skillet. Cook the patties for about 5-6 minutes on each side until thoroughly cooked. Grill the Pineapple: Cut fresh pineapple into slices and grill until caramelized and glowing. Assemble the Sliders: Place the jerk pork patty on the bottom half of each bun, add a slice of grilled pineapple, and spread spicy mayo on top. Cap with the top bun. Serve: Bring a taste of the tropics to your table with these dance-worthy sliders that's sure to impress.

  1. Italian Caprese Sliders Last but not least, let’s make Italian Caprese Sliders that embody the classic flavors of Italy. Ingredients

Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced Ripe tomatoes, sliced Fresh basil leaves Balsamic glaze Olive oil Salt and pepper to taste Slider buns

Instructions

Prepare the Sliders: Slice your slider buns in half. Layer the Ingredients: On the bottom half, layer mozzarella cheese, a slice of tomato, and fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with Olive Oil and Balsamic Glaze: Add a light drizzle of olive oil and balsamic glaze. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cap and Serve: Place the top buns back on and serve. These sliders are a fresh, light option that captures the essence of Italian cuisine.

Conclusion From the savory Teriyaki Chicken of Asia to the refreshing Caprese sliders of Italy, these global-inspired sliders provide a delightful way to celebrate flavors from around the world. They're not just delicious; they also make for a fun culinary adventure that you can share with friends and family. Next time you're in the mood for sliders, try one of these recipes and take your taste buds on a world tour without leaving home! Happy cooking!
