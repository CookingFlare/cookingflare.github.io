Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta: A Refreshing Comfort Dish


If you’re looking for a dish that combines the zesty freshness of lemon, the savory richness of garlic, and the succulent goodness of shrimp, then look no further. This Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta recipe is a delightful blend of flavors and textures, perfect for a special dinner or a comforting meal any day of the week. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp:

    • In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat.
    • Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper.
    • Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  2. Cook the Pasta:

    • In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain the pasta and set it aside.

  3. Make the Creamy Lemon Garlic Sauce:

    • In the same skillet used to cook the shrimp, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter.
    • Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
    • Pour in the chicken broth and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
    • Reduce the heat to medium-low and stir in the heavy cream, lemon zest, and lemon juice.
    • Allow the sauce to simmer gently for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly.
    • Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and the sauce becomes creamy.
    • Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Combine and Serve:

    • Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet with the creamy lemon garlic sauce.
    • Toss the pasta gently to coat it evenly with the sauce.
    • Gently fold in the cooked shrimp, ensuring they are well incorporated into the pasta.
    • Garnish the dish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

This Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta is best enjoyed immediately, allowing the flavors to shine at their peak. The combination of the tangy lemon, robust garlic, and tender shrimp creates a symphony of flavors that is sure to impress your taste buds.

Tips and Variations

  • For a lighter version of this dish, you can use half-and-half instead of heavy cream, although the sauce will be slightly less rich.
  • Experiment with adding a touch of red pepper flakes for a subtle kick of heat, or incorporate a handful of baby spinach for some extra greens.
  • If you prefer a different type of pasta, such as linguine or spaghetti, feel free to make the substitution to suit your taste.

Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta is a versatile and satisfying dish that never fails to impress. The bold flavors and creamy texture make it a standout comfort food that is both refreshing and indulgent.

So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure with this delightful recipe. Your taste buds will thank you for the vibrant burst of flavors that await in every forkful of this Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta. Enjoy!
