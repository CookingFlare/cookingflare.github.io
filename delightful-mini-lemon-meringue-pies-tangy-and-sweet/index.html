Delightful Mini Lemon Meringue Pies: Tangy and Sweet


If you’re a fan of tangy and sweet flavors, then these delightful mini lemon meringue pies are the perfect treat for you. With their buttery crust, zesty lemon filling, and fluffy meringue topping, these bite-sized pies are a burst of sunshine in every bite. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a homemade dessert, these mini lemon meringue pies are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups water
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

For the meringue:

  • 3 large egg whites
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt for the crust. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together.

  3. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/8-inch thickness. Use a round cookie cutter or a glass to cut out circles that fit into the cups of a muffin tin. Press the dough circles into the cups, ensuring they cover the bottom and sides. Prick the bottoms with a fork to prevent air bubbles.

  4. Bake the crusts for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from the oven and let them cool completely.

  5. While the crusts are cooling, prepare the filling. In a saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually whisk in the water until the mixture is smooth. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil.

  6. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks. Gradually whisk in about 1/2 cup of the hot sugar mixture to temper the yolks. Then, pour the tempered yolks into the saucepan with the remaining sugar mixture. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in the lemon zest, lemon juice, and butter until well combined.

  7. Spoon the lemon filling into the cooled crusts, filling them about three-quarters full. Smooth the tops with a spatula or the back of a spoon.

  8. In a clean mixing bowl, beat the egg whites and cream of tartar on medium speed until foamy. Gradually add the sugar while continuing to beat until the meringue forms stiff peaks.

  9. Spoon or pipe the meringue onto the lemon filling, covering it completely and creating decorative peaks.

  10. Bake the mini pies for 10-12 minutes, or until the meringue is golden brown.

  11. Remove from the oven and let them cool completely before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These delightful mini lemon meringue pies are best enjoyed chilled. Serve them as a refreshing dessert after a summer barbecue or as a sweet treat with afternoon tea. You can also garnish them with a sprinkle of powdered sugar or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence.

Conclusion

With their tangy lemon filling, buttery crust, and fluffy meringue topping, these delightful mini lemon meringue pies are a true delight. They are the perfect combination of sweet and tangy, making them a crowd-pleasing dessert for any occasion. So, grab your apron and get ready to impress your family and friends with these irresistible mini pies. Enjoy!
