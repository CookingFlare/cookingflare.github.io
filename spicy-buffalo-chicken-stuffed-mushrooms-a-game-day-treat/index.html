Spicy Buffalo Chicken Stuffed Mushrooms: A Game Day Treat


If you’re a fan of game day snacks that pack a punch, these Spicy Buffalo Chicken Stuffed Mushrooms are sure to become a new favorite. This recipe takes the classic flavors of buffalo chicken and combines them with savory mushrooms, creating a mouthwatering appetizer that’s perfect for sharing with friends and family. Whether you’re hosting a game day get-together or simply craving a spicy treat, these stuffed mushrooms are a must-try.

Ingredients

  • 24 large white mushrooms, stems removed
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken and buffalo sauce, ensuring the chicken is well coated.
  3. In another bowl, mix together the cream cheese, blue cheese crumbles, green onions, celery, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  4. Fill each mushroom cap with a generous amount of the cream cheese mixture, creating a small well in the center.
  5. Place the stuffed mushrooms on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden and bubbly.
  6. Remove the mushrooms from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes.
  7. Top each mushroom with a spoonful of the buffalo chicken mixture, ensuring the mushrooms are generously filled.
  8. Return the mushrooms to the oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the chicken topping is heated through.
  9. Once cooked, remove the mushrooms from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.
  10. Serve the Spicy Buffalo Chicken Stuffed Mushrooms with a side of ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a sprinkle of cayenne pepper to the buffalo chicken mixture.
  • If you prefer a milder flavor, opt for a mild buffalo sauce or mix it with a bit of melted butter.
  • You can prepare the cream cheese mixture and buffalo chicken filling ahead of time and refrigerate until you’re ready to assemble and bake the mushrooms.

Why These Stuffed Mushrooms Are a Game Day Winner

The Spicy Buffalo Chicken Stuffed Mushrooms are a game day winner for several reasons. Firstly, they offer a delightful combination of spicy, tangy, and savory flavors that are sure to please any crowd. The heat from the buffalo sauce, the creaminess of the cheese filling, and the tender texture of the mushrooms create a satisfying bite that will keep your guests coming back for more.

Additionally, these stuffed mushrooms are a versatile option for entertaining. They can be served as a standalone appetizer or incorporated into a larger spread of game day treats. Their bite-sized nature makes them easy to enjoy while watching the game or socializing with friends, and their bold flavors are a welcome departure from traditional party snacks.

Moreover, the Spicy Buffalo Chicken Stuffed Mushrooms are relatively simple to prepare, making them an accessible option for home cooks of all skill levels. With just a handful of ingredients and straightforward instructions, you can whip up a batch of these delicious stuffed mushrooms in no time, leaving you free to enjoy the game and the company of your guests.

In conclusion, these Spicy Buffalo Chicken Stuffed Mushrooms are a game day treat that’s sure to impress. From their bold flavors to their easy preparation, they check all the boxes for a winning game day snack. So, next time you’re planning a gathering to watch the big game, consider adding these mouthwatering stuffed mushrooms to your menu. They’re guaranteed to be a hit!
