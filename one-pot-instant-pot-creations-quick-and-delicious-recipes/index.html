One-Pot Instant Pot Creations: Quick and Delicious Recipes


In today’s fast-paced world, finding time to prepare a delicious meal can be a challenge. That’s where the Instant Pot comes in as a lifesaver for busy individuals looking to whip up quick and flavorful dishes with minimal effort. What’s even better? One-pot recipes that save on cleanup time while packing a punch in the flavor department. Let’s dive into some mouth-watering One-Pot Instant Pot creations that will revolutionize your weeknight dinners!

Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast, diced
  • 2 cups penne pasta
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Fresh basil, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Turn your Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook the chicken until browned.
  2. Add in the onion and garlic, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and scrape any browned bits from the bottom.
  4. Add the pasta, diced tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  5. Close the lid, set to high pressure for 4 minutes, then quick release.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.
  7. Garnish with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil before serving.

Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, sliced thinly
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Green onions, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In the Instant Pot, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add the sliced flank steak and toss to coat in the sauce.
  3. Layer the broccoli and bell pepper on top.
  4. Close the lid, set to high pressure for 10 minutes, then quick release.
  5. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch with water and stir into the beef mixture to thicken the sauce.
  6. Serve over rice and garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.

Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions:

  1. In the Instant Pot, combine lentils, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, cumin, paprika, and vegetable broth.
  2. Close the lid, set to high pressure for 8 minutes, then natural release.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon juice.
