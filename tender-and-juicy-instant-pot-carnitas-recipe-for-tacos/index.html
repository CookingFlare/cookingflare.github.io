Tender and Juicy Instant Pot Carnitas Recipe for Tacos


If you’re craving the mouthwatering flavors of Mexican cuisine, then look no further than this tender and juicy Instant Pot carnitas recipe. Perfect for filling tacos, burritos, or topping salads, these succulent carnitas are a must-try for any food enthusiast. The best part? With the help of your Instant Pot, you can achieve the same melt-in-your-mouth tenderness and rich flavor traditionally attained through slow cooking, in a fraction of the time.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, sliced (seeds removed for less heat)
  • 1 orange, juiced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth

Instructions

Step 1: Sear the Pork

  • Start by seasoning the pork pieces with salt, pepper, cumin, oregano, smoked paprika, and chili powder.
  • Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and heat the vegetable oil. Once the pot is hot, add the seasoned pork pieces in batches, ensuring they are browned on all sides. This step helps to seal in the juices and adds a depth of flavor to the carnitas.

Step 2: Pressure Cook

  • Once all the pork pieces are seared, add the minced garlic, chopped onion, and sliced jalapeño to the Instant Pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the onions are translucent.
  • Pour in the freshly squeezed orange and lime juice, along with the chicken broth. Give everything a good stir to combine the flavors.
  • Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  • Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully releasing any remaining pressure.

Step 3: Crisp the Carnitas

  • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) while the carnitas are cooking in the Instant Pot.
  • Use a slotted spoon to transfer the cooked pork pieces to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Gently shred the pork using two forks, spreading it out in an even layer.
  • Drizzle some of the cooking liquid over the shredded pork to keep it moist and flavorful, then place the baking sheet in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the edges of the carnitas turn golden and crispy.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  • Once the carnitas are beautifully crispy, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly.
  • Now, it’s time to assemble your tacos! Warm up some corn tortillas and fill them with the delicious carnitas. Top with your favorite fresh toppings such as diced onions, cilantro, salsa, and a squeeze of lime juice.

Final Thoughts

With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can indulge in the rich and satisfying flavors of tender and juicy carnitas without spending hours in the kitchen. This recipe is perfect for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, and the leftovers (if there are any) can be used in a variety of dishes throughout the week. So, gather your ingredients, set up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor the irresistible taste of homemade carnitas tacos.
