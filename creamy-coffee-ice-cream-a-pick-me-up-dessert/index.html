Creamy Coffee Ice Cream: A Pick-Me-Up Dessert


There’s something undeniably luxurious about the creamy indulgence of a good ice cream, especially when it’s infused with the deep, rich flavor of coffee. Creamy coffee ice cream is the perfect pick-me-up dessert, offering a delightful combination of sweet, creamy goodness and the invigorating kick of caffeine. This recipe will guide you through the steps of creating your own batch of this delectable treat, so you can enjoy a scoop of pure bliss whenever you need a little boost.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of instant coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 5 large egg yolks

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Base: In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and instant coffee granules. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir the mixture until it is hot but not boiling. Once hot, remove the saucepan from the heat and set it aside.

  2. Whisk the Yolks: In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and granulated sugar until the mixture is pale and slightly thickened.

  3. Temper the Eggs: Gradually pour the hot cream mixture into the bowl with the egg yolks, whisking constantly to temper the eggs and prevent them from curdling.

  4. Cook the Custard: Pour the combined mixture back into the saucepan and return it to the stove over medium heat. Stir the mixture constantly until it thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. This step requires patience, as you want the custard to thicken without curdling the eggs.

  5. Strain and Chill: Once the custard has thickened, remove the saucepan from the heat and pour the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any lumps or bits of cooked egg. Stir in the vanilla extract, then cover the custard with plastic wrap, ensuring the wrap touches the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming. Chill the custard in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or until it is completely cold.

  6. Churn the Ice Cream: After the custard has chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. The ice cream should have a soft, creamy texture when done.

  7. Freeze and Enjoy: Transfer the churned ice cream to a freezer-safe container, cover it with a lid, and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until the ice cream is firm and scoopable.

Serving Suggestions

Creamy coffee ice cream is delightful on its own, but it also pairs beautifully with a variety of toppings and accompaniments. Consider serving it with a sprinkle of cocoa powder for an extra chocolatey touch, or alongside a warm slice of chocolate cake for a decadent dessert experience. For a refreshing twist, try serving the ice cream affogato-style by pouring a shot of hot espresso over a scoop of the creamy treat.

Final Thoughts

Indulging in a scoop of homemade creamy coffee ice cream is a wonderful way to treat yourself to a little moment of bliss. The rich, velvety texture and bold coffee flavor make it a dessert that’s sure to lift your spirits and satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether enjoyed on a sunny afternoon or as a delightful after-dinner treat, this pick-me-up dessert is bound to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, why not whip up a batch and savor the simple pleasure of homemade creamy coffee ice cream today?
