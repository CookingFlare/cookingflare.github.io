Hearty Instant Pot Bean Recipes for a Protein-Packed Meal


Beans are a versatile and nutritious ingredient that can add depth and flavor to any meal. Whether you’re a vegetarian looking for protein-packed options or simply want to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these hearty Instant Pot bean recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you feeling full and satisfied.

1. Mexican Black Bean Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried black beans
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and sort the black beans, removing any debris or stones.
  2. In the Instant Pot, sauté the onion, garlic, jalapeno pepper, and red bell pepper until softened.
  3. Add the black beans, diced tomatoes, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot.
  4. Add enough water to cover the beans by about an inch.
  5. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 30 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before carefully releasing any remaining pressure.
  7. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

2. Tuscan White Bean Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried cannellini beans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 teaspoons dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and sort the cannellini beans, removing any debris or stones.
  2. In the Instant Pot, sauté the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery until softened.
  3. Add the cannellini beans, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 40 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes before carefully releasing any remaining pressure.
  6. Serve the stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

3. Indian Chickpea Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried chickpeas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and sort the chickpeas, removing any debris or stones.
  2. In the Instant Pot, sauté the onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add the curry powder, turmeric, chickpeas, coconut milk, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 45 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes before carefully releasing any remaining pressure.
  6. Serve the curry hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

4. Cuban Black Beans and Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried black beans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked white rice (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and sort the black beans, removing any debris or stones.
  2. In the Instant Pot, sauté the onion, garlic, green bell pepper, and red bell pepper until softened.
  3. Add the black beans, cumin, dried oregano, bay leaf, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot.
  4. Add enough water to cover the beans by about an inch.
  5. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 30 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before carefully releasing any remaining pressure.
  7. Serve the black beans over cooked white rice.

These hearty Instant Pot bean recipes are not only delicious but also a great way to incorporate more protein into your meals. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or feeding a crowd, these recipes are sure to impress. So fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy a protein-packed meal that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.
