Thai Khao Soi with Curried Noodles and Chicken Recipe


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious and flavorful recipe for Khao Soi with Curried Noodles and Chicken. This traditional dish from Northern Thailand is known for its rich and aromatic flavors, combining tender chicken, creamy coconut milk, and fragrant spices. So let’s dive right into the recipe and discover how to create this culinary masterpiece in your own kitchen!

Ingredients

For the Khao Soi Paste:

  • 4 shallots, chopped
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce

For the Khao Soi Soup:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste (optional)
  • Salt, to taste

For the Curried Noodles:

  • 8 ounces egg noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine all the ingredients for the Khao Soi paste. Blend until smooth and set aside.

  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  3. In the same pot, add the Khao Soi paste and cook for 2-3 minutes until fragrant. Add the chicken broth, coconut milk, soy sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, and tamarind paste (if using). Stir well to combine.

  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Season with salt to taste.

  5. While the soup is simmering, prepare the curried noodles. In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the red onion, bell peppers, and carrots. Cook for 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  6. Add the cooked egg noodles to the pan with the vegetables and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the noodles are heated through.

  7. To serve, divide the curried noodles among serving bowls. Ladle the Khao Soi soup over the noodles, and top with bean sprouts and fresh cilantro for garnish.

  8. Enjoy your homemade Thai Khao Soi with Curried Noodles and Chicken! The combination of the creamy coconut milk, fragrant spices, and tender chicken will transport you to the streets of Northern Thailand with every bite.

Conclusion

Thai Khao Soi with Curried Noodles and Chicken is a dish that truly captures the essence of Thai cuisine. With its complex flavors and comforting warmth, it’s the perfect meal to enjoy on a cozy evening or to impress your friends and family at a dinner party. So next time you’re craving Thai food, why not try your hand at this authentic and delicious recipe? Your taste buds will thank you!
