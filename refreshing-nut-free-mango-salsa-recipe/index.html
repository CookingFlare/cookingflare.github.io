Refreshing Nut-Free Mango Salsa Recipe


Looking for a vibrant and flavor-packed addition to your summer menu? This refreshing nut-free mango salsa is the perfect blend of sweet, tangy, and spicy flavors that will elevate any dish. Whether you pair it with grilled chicken, fish tacos, or simply enjoy it with tortilla chips, this salsa is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful mango salsa at home.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by dicing the ripe mangoes, finely chopping the red bell pepper, and dicing the red onion. Seed and mince the jalapeno, and chop the fresh cilantro. Gather all the ingredients in separate bowls for easy assembly.

  2. Combine the Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced mangoes, chopped red bell pepper, diced red onion, minced jalapeno, and chopped cilantro. Toss the ingredients gently to ensure an even distribution.

  3. Add the Flavor: Squeeze the juice of two limes over the mango mixture. Drizzle the honey over the ingredients to add a touch of sweetness. Season the salsa with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.

  4. Mix Well: Gently mix all the ingredients together, ensuring that the flavors are well incorporated. Be mindful not to crush the mango pieces, as the salsa looks best when the mango chunks remain intact.

  5. Chill and Serve: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the mango salsa for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Once chilled, give it a gentle stir and it’s ready to serve.

Serving Suggestions:

This refreshing nut-free mango salsa is incredibly versatile and can be paired with a variety of dishes. Here are a few serving suggestions to inspire your culinary creativity:

  • Grilled Chicken: Serve the mango salsa alongside grilled chicken breasts or thighs for a burst of tropical flavor.

  • Fish Tacos: Elevate your fish tacos by adding a generous spoonful of mango salsa on top for a refreshing twist.

  • Snack Time: Simply grab your favorite tortilla chips and indulge in the sweet and tangy flavors of this delightful salsa.

  • Topping for Salads: Use the mango salsa as a vibrant topping for green salads or grain bowls for an added punch of flavor.

Final Thoughts:

This nut-free mango salsa is not only a delicious and refreshing addition to your culinary repertoire, but it also offers a burst of nutrients and vibrant colors to brighten up any meal. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this salsa is a must-try. Experiment with the spice level by adjusting the amount of jalapeno, and feel free to customize the recipe to suit your taste preferences.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a zesty and rejuvenating dish, whip up a batch of this nut-free mango salsa and savor the delightful combination of sweet mangoes, zesty lime, and spicy jalapeno. Your taste buds will thank you for it!

Feel free to experiment with this vibrant mango salsa recipe and let its refreshing flavors transport you to a tropical paradise!
