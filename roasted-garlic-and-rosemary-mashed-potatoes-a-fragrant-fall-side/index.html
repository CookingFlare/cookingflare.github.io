Roasted Garlic and Rosemary Mashed Potatoes: A Fragrant Fall Side


There’s something magical about the flavors of fall. As the weather gets cooler and the leaves start to change, our taste buds crave comforting and aromatic dishes. And what better way to capture the essence of this season than with a bowl of creamy, flavorful mashed potatoes? But not just any mashed potatoes – we’re talking about Roasted Garlic and Rosemary Mashed Potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 1 whole head of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wash and scrub the potatoes to remove any dirt.
  3. Cut the potatoes into quarters, leaving the skin on. This will give the mashed potatoes a rustic texture.
  4. Place the potato quarters in a large pot and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil over high heat and cook until the potatoes are fork-tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  5. While the potatoes are boiling, prepare the roasted garlic. Cut off the top of the head of garlic to expose the cloves. Drizzle with olive oil and wrap tightly in aluminum foil. Place in the oven and roast for 30-40 minutes, or until the cloves are soft and golden.
  6. Once the potatoes are cooked, drain them and return them to the pot.
  7. Squeeze the roasted garlic cloves out of their skins and add them to the pot with the potatoes.
  8. Add the softened butter, milk, and chopped rosemary to the pot.
  9. Using a potato masher or a fork, mash the potatoes until smooth and creamy. If you prefer a smoother texture, you can also use a hand mixer or a blender.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and give everything a final stir to incorporate all the flavors.
  11. Serve the Roasted Garlic and Rosemary Mashed Potatoes hot, garnished with a sprig of fresh rosemary for an extra touch of elegance.

Why You’ll Love These Mashed Potatoes

  1. Fragrant and Flavorful: The combination of roasted garlic and fresh rosemary adds a delightful aroma and a burst of flavor to your traditional mashed potatoes. It’s a simple yet sophisticated twist that will elevate your fall side dishes.

  2. Creamy and Comforting: These mashed potatoes are velvety smooth and oh-so-creamy. The addition of butter and milk creates a luscious texture that melts in your mouth, making each bite a comforting experience.

  3. Versatile and Crowd-Pleasing: Whether you’re serving these mashed potatoes as a side dish for a holiday feast or as a comforting weeknight dinner, they’re sure to please a crowd. Their rich and robust flavors complement a wide range of main courses.

  4. Easy and Time-Saving: With just a few simple steps, you can create a dish that looks and tastes like it took hours to prepare. The roasted garlic can be prepared in advance, saving you time on the day of cooking.

So, the next time you’re looking for a show-stopping side dish that captures the essence of fall, give these Roasted Garlic and Rosemary Mashed Potatoes a try. With their fragrant aroma, creamy texture, and incredible flavors, they’re sure to become a fall favorite in your household. Enjoy!
