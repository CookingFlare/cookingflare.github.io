Easy and Delicious Pasta Recipes for Beginner Cooks


Are you a beginner cook looking to impress your friends and family with a delicious homemade meal? Look no further than these easy and delicious pasta recipes! Pasta is a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that can be enjoyed by everyone. With these simple recipes, you’ll be able to whip up a fantastic meal in no time. So put on your apron and let’s get cooking!

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until melted and smooth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until well coated.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley.

One-Pot Tomato Basil Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of spaghetti
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the spaghetti, onion, garlic, diced tomatoes (with their juice), vegetable broth, and torn basil leaves.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is cooked and most of the liquid has been absorbed, about 10-12 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of linguine pasta
  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the linguine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Add the lemon zest and juice to the skillet, along with salt and pepper to taste. Stir until well combined.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until the shrimp and pasta are well coated.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley.

Conclusion

With these easy and delicious pasta recipes, even beginner cooks can create impressive meals that will leave everyone satisfied. Whether you prefer a creamy garlic Parmesan pasta, a one-pot tomato basil pasta, or a zesty lemon garlic shrimp pasta, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress with your newfound cooking skills. Happy cooking!
