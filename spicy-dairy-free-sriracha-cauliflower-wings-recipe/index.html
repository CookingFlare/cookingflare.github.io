Spicy Dairy-Free Sriracha Cauliflower Wings Recipe


Are you ready to take your snacking game to the next level? If you’re looking for a deliciously spicy and completely dairy-free alternative to traditional chicken wings, look no further than these Spicy Dairy-Free Sriracha Cauliflower Wings. Perfect for game day, a casual get-together, or a cozy movie night, this recipe is not only easy to make but also packed with flavor. Let’s dive into how you can whip up these delectable wings that will have everyone coming back for more!

Why Choose Cauliflower Wings?

Cauliflower has become a popular substitute for chicken wings in recent years, especially among those seeking plant-based options or those with dietary restrictions. These Spicy Dairy-Free Sriracha Cauliflower Wings offer a satisfying crunch and a spicy kick, making them an excellent choice for both vegans and non-vegans alike. Not only are they lower in calories compared to traditional wings, but they are also rich in vitamins and minerals, offering a healthier snacking alternative without sacrificing taste.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make your Spicy Dairy-Free Sriracha Cauliflower Wings, gather the following ingredients:

For the Cauliflower Wings:

  • 1 large head of cauliflower, cut into bite-sized florets
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (or a gluten-free blend)
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any dairy-free milk)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Spicy Sriracha Sauce:

  • ½ cup Sriracha sauce (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup (for a touch of sweetness)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon sesame oil for added flavor

For Garnish:

  • Fresh cilantro or parsley, chopped
  • Lime wedges

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cauliflower

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 450°F (230°C). This high temperature will help achieve that perfect crispy texture.

  2. Cut the Cauliflower: Take your large head of cauliflower and cut it into bite-sized florets. Aim for uniform pieces to ensure even cooking.

Step 2: Make the Batter

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Whisk together until well blended.

  2. Add Wet Ingredients: Gradually whisk in the almond milk until you reach a smooth, thick batter. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter. If it’s too thick, add a bit more almond milk.

Step 3: Coat the Cauliflower

  1. Dip and Coat: Take each cauliflower floret and dip it into the batter, making sure it’s fully coated. Allow any excess batter to drip off.

  2. Arrange on Baking Sheet: Place the coated florets on a parchment-lined baking sheet, ensuring they are spaced out and not touching to allow for even crisping.

Step 4: Bake the Cauliflower Wings

  1. First Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through. You want them to be golden brown and crispy.

Step 5: Prepare the Spicy Sriracha Sauce

  1. Combine Ingredients: While the cauliflower is baking, prepare the spicy Sriracha sauce. In a medium bowl, whisk together the Sriracha sauce, maple syrup, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, lime juice, and sesame oil (if using).

  2. Adjust to Taste: Taste the sauce and adjust the ingredients as needed. If you like it spicier, add more Sriracha. For a sweeter sauce, add a bit more maple syrup.

Step 6: Toss and Bake Again

  1. Coat in Sauce: Once the cauliflower wings are golden and crispy, remove them from the oven and place them in a large mixing bowl. Pour the spicy Sriracha sauce over the wings and toss until they are evenly coated.

  2. Final Bake: Return the coated wings to the baking sheet and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes. This will help caramelize the sauce and enhance the flavors.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Garnish: Once they are done baking, remove the cauliflower wings from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with chopped cilantro or parsley and serve with lime wedges on the side.

  2. Dig In: These Spicy Dairy-Free Sriracha Cauliflower Wings are perfect for dipping in your favorite vegan ranch or blue cheese dressing. Enjoy them hot and fresh out of the oven!

Storage Tips

If you have leftovers (though they are so good you might not!), store the cauliflower wings in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat them in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 10-15 minutes to restore their crispiness.

Health Benefits of Cauliflower

Cauliflower is a nutrient-dense vegetable that boasts numerous health benefits. Here are a few reasons to love this cruciferous vegetable:

  • Low in Calories: Cauliflower is low in calories, making it an excellent base for healthier recipes.
  • Rich in Vitamins: It is high in vitamins C, K, and B6, as well as folate, which are essential for overall health.
  • High in Fiber: The fiber content in cauliflower supports digestive health and can help keep you full longer.
  • Antioxidants: Cauliflower contains antioxidants that help combat inflammation and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Customization Ideas

One of the best parts about this recipe is its versatility! Here are a few ideas to customize your Spicy Dairy-Free Sriracha Cauliflower Wings:

  • Add More Heat: If you’re a spice lover, consider adding diced jalapeños or red pepper flakes to the batter or sauce for an extra kick.
  • Flavor Variations: Experiment with different sauces such as buffalo sauce, teriyaki, or a garlic herb sauce for a unique twist.
  • Extra Crispy: For an even crispier texture, try tossing the coated cauliflower in panko breadcrumbs before baking.

Conclusion

These Spicy Dairy-Free Sriracha Cauliflower Wings are sure to become a favorite in your household. They are easy to make, packed with flavor, and offer a healthier alternative to traditional wings. Whether you’re hosting a party, enjoying a night in, or simply craving something delicious, this recipe ticks all the boxes. So grab your ingredients, preheat that oven, and get ready to indulge in a mouthwatering snack that everyone will love!

Happy cooking!
