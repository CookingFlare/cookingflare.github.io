Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi: Elegant and Effortless


Are you looking for a delightful and elegant dish that can be prepared effortlessly? Look no further than Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi. This classic Italian-American dish is bursting with flavors of garlic, lemon, and succulent shrimp, all coming together in a harmonious symphony of taste. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have this impressive meal on the table in no time, making it perfect for both weeknight dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces of linguine or spaghetti
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Season the shrimp with salt and black pepper.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: Set the Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Liquid: Pour in the chicken broth, lemon juice, lemon zest, and red pepper flakes, then give everything a good stir.

  4. Cook the Pasta: Break the linguine or spaghetti in half and layer them over the shrimp mixture. Do not stir. Gently press the pasta down to ensure it is submerged in the liquid.

  5. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to Manual/Pressure Cook mode for 4 minutes. Once done, perform a quick pressure release.

  6. Add the Shrimp: Open the lid and add the seasoned shrimp to the pot, making sure they are submerged in the liquid. Put the lid back on and let it sit for 5 minutes to allow the residual heat to cook the shrimp.

  7. Serve: Carefully open the lid and give everything a gentle stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Serve the Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi over a bed of pasta, garnished with freshly chopped parsley.

Tips for Success

  • Use fresh, high-quality shrimp for the best flavor and texture.
  • Adjust the amount of red pepper flakes according to your spice preference.
  • For a touch of indulgence, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the finished dish.

Why Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to cooking this classic dish. Not only does it cut down the cooking time significantly, but it also allows the flavors to meld together beautifully, resulting in a deeply satisfying meal that tastes like it’s been simmering for hours.

The bright and zesty flavors of lemon and garlic perfectly complement the natural sweetness of the shrimp, creating a dish that is both refreshing and comforting. The addition of red pepper flakes provides a gentle heat that elevates the overall flavor profile, adding a subtle kick without overpowering the other elements.

Final Thoughts

Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi is a testament to the fact that elegance and effortlessness can indeed coexist in the kitchen. Whether you’re treating yourself to a luxurious solo dinner or impressing your guests with a stunning entrée, this dish is sure to leave a lasting impression.

So, fire up your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every delectable bite of this exquisite Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi. Bon appétit!
