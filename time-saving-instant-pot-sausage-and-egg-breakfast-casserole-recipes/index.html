Time-Saving Instant Pot Sausage and Egg Breakfast Casserole Recipes


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen preparing breakfast? Well, fret no more because I’ve got the perfect solution for you – Time-Saving Instant Pot Sausage and Egg Breakfast Casserole! This recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly convenient, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot. Whether you’re a busy parent trying to get the family ready for the day or a professional rushing off to work, this breakfast casserole will save you time and energy without compromising on flavor.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to whip up this delightful breakfast dish:

  • 1 pound of breakfast sausage
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup of diced onions
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Once it’s hot, add the breakfast sausage and cook until it’s browned and crumbled.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  3. Once the sausage is cooked, add the diced bell peppers and onions to the Instant Pot and sauté for a few minutes until they soften.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the sausage and vegetables in the Instant Pot.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top of the egg mixture.
  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 20 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before quick releasing any remaining pressure.
  7. Carefully remove the lid and behold the deliciousness that awaits you!

Serving

This breakfast casserole is perfect for feeding a hungry crowd or for meal prepping for the week ahead. Cut it into generous squares and serve with a side of fresh fruit or a simple green salad for a well-rounded breakfast.

Time-Saving Tips

One of the best things about using the Instant Pot for this recipe is that it significantly cuts down on cooking time. While the casserole is cooking in the Instant Pot, you can use that time to prepare your morning coffee, set the table, or even get a head start on other tasks for the day. It’s a true time-saving hack that will make your mornings a whole lot easier.

Conclusion

The Time-Saving Instant Pot Sausage and Egg Breakfast Casserole is a game-changer for anyone looking to enjoy a hearty and delicious breakfast without the hassle of traditional cooking methods. With just a few simple steps and the help of the Instant Pot, you can have a satisfying meal ready in no time. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try – your taste buds and your schedule will thank you!

So there you have it – a breakfast recipe that’s as convenient as it is delicious. Say goodbye to long mornings in the kitchen and hello to more time for yourself, your family, or whatever else you enjoy doing. Happy cooking!
