Tres Leches Cake Recipe: A Thirst-quenching Dessert


Are you in search of a sweet and creamy dessert that will impress your friends and family during the upcoming gatherings? Look no further! Tres Leches Cake, also known as ‘Three Milk Cake’, is bound to become your new favorite dessert. This classic Latin American recipe will tantalize your taste buds with its unique combination of flavors and textures. Let’s dive into the enticing world of Tres Leches Cake, and learn how to create this thirst-quenching dessert that will make you the talk of the town.

Ingredients

Before we get our hands dirty, let’s list down the ingredients required to bake this scrumptious treat. Gather the following items and you’ll be all set to make Tres Leches Cake.

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

To assemble:

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Whipped Cream (optional)
  • Caramel sauce (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cake Batter

To make the cake, begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.

In a large mixing bowl, sift together 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon baking powder, and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Set aside.

Now, create a small well in the middle of the dry ingredients and add 3 large eggs, 1 cup melted unsalted butter, 3/4 cup granulated sugar, and 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract. Beat until well combined.

Slowly incorporate the dry ingredients into the wet mixture, and then pour in 1 cup of sweetened condensed milk. Keep mixing until the batter is smooth and evenly mixed.

Step 2: Bake the Cake

Pour the cake batter evenly into the prepared baking pan and smooth its surface with a spatula or the back of a spoon.

Bake the cake for approximately 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.

While your Tres Leches Cake is in the oven, prepare your leches mixture by combining 1 cup sweetened condensed milk, 1/2 cup evaporated milk, and 1/2 cup heavy cream in a large bowl.

Step 3: Assemble the Cake

Allow the cake to cool to room temperature before making any cuts or punctures into its surface. Once cooled, gently pierce the top of the cake with a fork or a skewer, creating small holes throughout.

Pour the cold leches mixture evenly over the cake, ensuring that it seeps into the holes. Allow the cake to absorb the milk mixture for 30-60 minutes. You’ll notice the cake will continue to soften and become more moist.

Step 4: Add Finishing Touches

Now, it’s time to assemble your Tres Leches Cake. Decorate the top of the cake with your choice of fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries). If desired, add a dollop of whipped cream and a drizzle of caramel sauce for extra sweetness and richness.

Slice, serve, and relish this thirst-quenching dessert at your next family gathering or dinner party.

Tres Leches Cake: A Slice of Heaven

The allure of Tres Leches Cake lies in its perfect balance of sweet, rich flavors and refreshing milkiness—making it irresistible for dessert lovers. It’s a crowd-pleaser, sure to impress your guests, and a guaranteed hit wherever you serve it. Give this delectable treat a try, and share the delight of Tres Leches Cake with your loved ones.

And as you master the art of making Tres Leches Cake, feel free to experiment with different fruits, toppings, and variations. The possibilities are endless! Happy baking!
