Keto Basics

Before diving headfirst into a realm of mouth-watering recipes, let’s reinforce some foundational basics of the ketogenic diet. Understanding the underlying principles is essential to enjoy the best results and maintain a sustainable lifestyle.

Macronutrient Breakdown

High Fat – The Core of Keto

Fats are your best friends in this diet, making up 70-80% of your daily calorie intake. They provide the necessary energy required to sustain daily activities without causing blood sugar fluctuations.

Low Carbohydrates – The Despised Ingredient

Carbs are restricted to only 5% of your daily calorie needs. This means, you’ll have to steer clear of most processed foods containing refined sugars, starchy grains, and sugary foods. A typical day’s intake may clock in at around 20-50 grams of carbohydrates, focusing on leafy greens, low-carb vegetables, and nuts.

Moderate Protein – The Balancing Act

Protein makes up approximately 20% of your daily calorie intake. Ensuring the right proportion keeps your body fully operational and promotes healthy muscle growth without hampering ketosis, the state wherein your body starts burning fat for energy.

Morning Indulgence: Avocado Chocolate Pudding

Yes, you heard that right! Combining avocado and cocoa can create a delectable pudding offering a healthy calorie-dense start to your day. Full of healthy fats and antioxidants, this dessert-like breakfast will make you start your day right:

  • 2 medium-sized ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of natural peanut butter or almond butter
  • 1/4 cup of coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons of Erythritol (natural sweetener)
