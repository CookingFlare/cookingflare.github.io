Mediterranean Stuffed Tomatoes: A Fresh and Flavorful Dish


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy recipe that will transport you straight to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean? Look no further than Mediterranean Stuffed Tomatoes! Bursting with vibrant flavors and packed with nutritious ingredients, this dish is a true crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 6 large tomatoes
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Slice off the top of each tomato and carefully scoop out the pulp and seeds, creating a hollow cavity. Set aside the tomato shells.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, crumbled feta cheese, chopped Kalamata olives, fresh parsley, fresh mint, chopped red onion, minced garlic, lemon juice, extra virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  3. Stuff each tomato shell with the quinoa mixture, making sure to press it down gently to fill any gaps. Place the stuffed tomatoes in a baking dish.

  4. Bake the tomatoes in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the tomatoes are tender and the filling is heated through. The tomatoes should be slightly softened but still hold their shape.

  5. Once cooked, remove the tomatoes from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. The flavors will meld together beautifully as they cool.

  6. Serve the Mediterranean Stuffed Tomatoes as a delightful appetizer, side dish, or even a light main course. They pair perfectly with a fresh green salad or crusty bread.

Health Benefits

Not only are Mediterranean Stuffed Tomatoes bursting with flavor, but they are also incredibly nutritious. Here are a few health benefits of this delectable dish:

  • Tomatoes are rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals. They are a great source of lycopene, which has been linked to a reduced risk of certain cancers and heart disease.
  • Quinoa is a complete protein, containing all nine essential amino acids. It is also high in fiber, making it a fantastic choice for maintaining a healthy digestive system.
  • Kalamata olives are full of heart-healthy monounsaturated fats and are a great source of antioxidants.
  • Fresh herbs like parsley and mint add a burst of freshness to the dish while also providing essential vitamins and minerals.

Conclusion

Mediterranean Stuffed Tomatoes are a delightful combination of fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and health benefits. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking for a nutritious meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So why not bring a taste of the Mediterranean to your kitchen and enjoy this fresh and flavorful dish today?

Happy cooking, and bon appétit!
