Maple Pecan Sticky Buns: A Sweet Breakfast Treat


Maple Pecan Sticky Buns are a delightful indulgence that combines the rich flavors of maple syrup and crunchy pecans in a warm, gooey pastry. These sweet breakfast treats are perfect for a cozy weekend morning or a special occasion brunch. Follow this easy recipe to create your own batch of Maple Pecan Sticky Buns that will impress your family and friends.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 to 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped pecans

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the yeast mixture, warm milk, sugar, butter, salt, eggs, and 2 cups of flour. Mix well.
    • Gradually add the remaining flour until the dough comes together.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for 1 hour until doubled in size.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a medium bowl, mix the brown sugar, melted butter, and maple syrup until well combined.
    • Spread the filling mixture evenly on the bottom of a greased baking dish.
    • Sprinkle chopped pecans over the filling.

  3. Assemble the Sticky Buns:

    • Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into a rectangle on a floured surface.
    • Spread additional melted butter over the dough and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon if desired.
    • Roll up the dough tightly and cut it into 12 equal pieces.

  4. Bake the Sticky Buns:

    • Place the rolls in the prepared baking dish on top of the filling.
    • Cover and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake the sticky buns for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
    • Let them cool slightly before serving.

  5. Enjoy Your Maple Pecan Sticky Buns:

    • Serve the sticky buns warm and drizzle with extra maple syrup if desired.
    • These sweet treats are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven with a cup of hot coffee or tea.

Indulge in the irresistible flavors of Maple Pecan Sticky Buns and treat yourself to a delicious breakfast experience. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting, this recipe is sure to impress with its warm, sticky goodness. Try making these sticky buns for your next weekend brunch and watch them disappear in no time!

Remember, the key to perfect sticky buns is patience and attention to detail. The combination of maple syrup, pecans, and soft dough is a winning treat that will have everyone coming back for more. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the sweet satisfaction of homemade Maple Pecan Sticky Buns. Happy baking!
