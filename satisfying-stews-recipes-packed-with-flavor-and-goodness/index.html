Satisfying Stews: Recipes Packed with Flavor and Goodness


Stews, those comforting creations that often involve meat, vegetables, and a tasty broth, have long been a staple in kitchens around the world. From hearty beef stroganoff to light vegetable tagine, there is a stew to satisfy every palate. Below are four delectable stew recipes to try at home.

Irish Beef and Guinness Stew

Nothing says “comfort food” quite like Irish cuisine, and this hearty beef and Guinness stew is no exception. It is the perfect dish for chilly evenings when you need some serious comfort.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of beef stew meat
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 cups of chopped onions
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 cup of Guinness or any stout beer
  • 3 cups of beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 cup of chopped carrots
  • 1 cup of chopped potatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat a large pot over medium-high heat, and add the olive oil.
  2. Brown the beef on all sides, about 10 minutes.
  3. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.
  4. In the same pot, cook the onions until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  6. Pour in the Guinness, stirring to deglaze the pan.
  7. Add the beef broth, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, tomato paste, and stir until well combined.
  8. In a small bowl, dissolve the flour in the remaining Guinness, and add to the stew.
  9. Return the beef to the pot and bring to a simmer.
  10. Add the carrots and potatoes, season with salt and pepper. Cover and simmer for at least 2 hours.
  11. Serve hot with some Irish soda bread.

Tomato-Based Vegetable Stew

A satisfying vegetarian option, this vegetable stew is bursting with flavor. It’s a perfect meal for a cool day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cups of chopped onions
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 3 cups of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of chopped red and green bell peppers
  • 1 cup of chopped zucchini
  • 1 cup of chopped potatoes
  • 1 cup of chopped carrots
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth

Directions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Cook the onions, stirring occasionally, for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, sugar, salt, and pepper, stirring well to combine.
  5. Add mushrooms, red and green bell peppers, zucchini, potatoes, and carrots.
  6. Pour in the vegetable broth, stirring occasionally.
  7. Simmer for 2 hours, covered.
  8. Serve hot with crusty bread.

Fish Stew with Tomatoes and Saffron

This fish stew from the Mediterranean is both refreshing and comforting. The combination of fish, tomatoes, and saffron makes for a stunning dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cups of chopped onions
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 cups of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 teaspoon of saffron threads
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 pound white fish fillets (such as cod, hake, or halibut)
  • 2 cups of fish broth
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • 1/2 cup of chopped green onions (scallions)

Directions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Cook the onions, stirring occasionally, for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, sugar, salt, and pepper, stirring well to combine.
  5. Stir in the saffron threads.
  6. Pour in the fish broth and white wine, stirring occasionally.
  7. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to a simmer.
  8. Add the white fish fillets to the pot and simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with crusty bread and a sprinkle of chopped green onions.

Spicy Lamb and Vegetable Tagine

This Moroccan-inspired lamb and vegetable tagine is a beautiful blend of sweet and spicy flavors. It’s perfect for a cozy dinner on a chilly winter night.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of boneless lamb shoulder, cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 cups of chopped onions
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1 cup of chopped potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh cilantro (coriander leaves)

Directions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the lamb and brown on all sides, about 10 minutes.
  3. Remove the lamb from the pot and set aside.
  4. In the same pot, cook the onions until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the garlic, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, ginger, turmeric, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper, and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth.
  7. Stir in the lamb and bring to a simmer.
  8. Add the carrots, potatoes, and chickpeas, and simmer for 2 hours.
  9. Serve hot with white rice and garnish with cilantro.
