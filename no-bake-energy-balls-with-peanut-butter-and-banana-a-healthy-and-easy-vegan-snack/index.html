No-Bake Energy Balls with Peanut Butter and Banana: A Healthy and Easy Vegan Snack

3 min · 492 words

Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious snack that requires no baking? Look no further than these mouthwatering No-Bake Energy Balls with Peanut Butter and Banana! Packed with natural ingredients that provide a boost of energy, these vegan-friendly treats are not only easy to make but also incredibly satisfying. Whether you need a quick pick-me-up during the day or a post-workout snack, these energy balls are the perfect choice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter (preferably natural)
  • 1/4 cup ground flaxseed
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup or agave nectar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mashed banana, peanut butter, maple syrup (or agave nectar), and vanilla extract. Mix well until the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  2. Add the rolled oats, ground flaxseed, chia seeds, cinnamon (if using), and salt to the bowl. Stir everything together until the mixture is evenly coated.

  3. Place the bowl in the refrigerator for about 15-30 minutes. Chilling the mixture will make it easier to form into balls.

  4. Once the mixture has chilled, remove it from the refrigerator. Take about a tablespoon of the mixture and roll it between your palms to form a ball. Repeat this process with the remaining mixture.

  5. Place the energy balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. If desired, you can roll the balls in additional toppings such as shredded coconut, chopped nuts, or cocoa powder for added flavor and texture.

  6. Once all the energy balls are formed, return them to the refrigerator and let them set for at least 1 hour. This will help them firm up and hold their shape.

  7. After the energy balls have set, transfer them to an airtight container and store in the refrigerator for up to one week. Enjoy them whenever you need a quick and healthy snack!

Why Choose No-Bake Energy Balls?

These No-Bake Energy Balls with Peanut Butter and Banana are not only delicious but also offer several health benefits. Let’s take a closer look at some of the key ingredients and their nutritional advantages:

  • Bananas: Packed with natural sugars and fiber, bananas provide a quick source of energy and help to keep you feeling full for longer.

  • Peanut Butter: High in protein and healthy fats, peanut butter adds a creamy texture and a rich flavor to these energy balls. It also offers essential nutrients such as vitamin E, magnesium, and potassium.

  • Rolled Oats: A great source of complex carbohydrates and fiber, rolled oats provide sustained energy and help to regulate blood sugar levels.

  • Flaxseed and Chia Seeds: These tiny seeds are packed with omega-3 fatty acids, fiber, and antioxidants. They not only add a delightful crunch to the energy balls but also offer numerous health benefits, including improved heart health and digestion.

  • Maple Syrup or Agave Nectar: These natural sweeteners add a touch of sweetness without the refined sugars found in many store-bought snacks.

So, whether you’re looking for a quick and easy snack or a healthy treat to satisfy your sweet tooth, these No-Bake Energy Balls with Peanut Butter and Banana are the perfect choice. Whip up a batch, store them in the refrigerator, and enjoy them throughout the week. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!

Remember, these energy balls are highly customizable. Feel free to experiment with different ingredients such as dried fruits, nuts, or even a sprinkle of cocoa powder for a chocolatey twist. Get creative and make these energy balls your own! Happy snacking!
