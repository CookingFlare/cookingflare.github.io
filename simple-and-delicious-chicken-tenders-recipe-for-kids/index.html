Simple and Delicious Chicken Tenders Recipe for Kids


Who doesn’t love chicken tenders? They are crispy, flavorful, and perfect for little hands. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a fun party snack, these simple and delicious chicken tenders are sure to be a hit with kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Start by prepping your chicken tenders. Cut the chicken breasts into long, thin strips, about 1 inch wide. Set aside.

  2. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. This flavorful mixture will be the coating for your chicken tenders.

  3. In another shallow bowl, beat the eggs and milk until well combined. This will be the egg wash that helps the flour mixture stick to the chicken.

  4. Now it’s time to coat the chicken tenders. Dip each strip of chicken into the egg wash, allowing any excess to drip off, and then dredge it in the flour mixture. Make sure to press the flour mixture onto the chicken to ensure an even coating.

  5. Heat about 1/2 inch of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add a few chicken tenders at a time, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through.

  6. Transfer the cooked chicken tenders to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil. Repeat the frying process with the remaining chicken tenders.

  7. Serve the chicken tenders hot with your favorite dipping sauces. Whether it’s ketchup, barbecue sauce, or honey mustard, the choice is yours!

Tips and Variations

  • For a healthier twist, you can bake the chicken tenders instead of frying them. Place the coated chicken tenders on a greased baking sheet and bake in a preheated 400°F (200°C) oven for about 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway through.

  • Experiment with different seasonings to customize the flavor of your chicken tenders. Add some cayenne pepper for a spicy kick, or try mixing in some grated Parmesan cheese for a cheesy twist.

  • If you’re short on time, you can prepare the chicken tenders in advance and freeze them. Simply bread the chicken tenders as instructed, place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and freeze until solid. Once frozen, transfer the chicken tenders to a freezer-safe bag or container. When ready to cook, simply thaw them in the fridge overnight and follow the cooking instructions.

  • Get creative with the dipping sauces! In addition to the classic options, you can try ranch dressing, buffalo sauce, or even a homemade honey mustard sauce.

Now that you have this simple and delicious chicken tenders recipe, you’re ready to impress your family and friends with a crowd-pleasing dish. Enjoy the crispy, flavorful goodness of these homemade chicken tenders and watch as everyone asks for seconds!
