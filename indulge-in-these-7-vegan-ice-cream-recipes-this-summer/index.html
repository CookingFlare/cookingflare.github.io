Indulge in These 7 Vegan Ice Cream Recipes This Summer


As the sun blazes and temperatures rise, nothing beats the heat like a scoop of ice cream. For those who prefer a plant-based lifestyle or are simply looking to indulge in healthier yet delicious desserts, vegan ice cream is the perfect way to satisfy your sweet tooth. This summer, treat yourself to these seven delightful vegan ice cream recipes that are easy to make, refreshing, and bursting with flavor. Let’s dive into the creamy, dreamy world of vegan ice cream!

1. Creamy Coconut Mango Sorbet

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Mango: In a blender, combine the diced mangoes, coconut milk, maple syrup, lime juice, and a pinch of salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Chill the Mixture: Pour the mixture into a bowl and refrigerate for about 30 minutes to chill and thicken slightly.
  3. Freeze: Transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. If you don’t have an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into a shallow dish and freeze for about 4 hours, stirring every 30 minutes to break up ice crystals.
  4. Serve: Once fully frozen, scoop into bowls and enjoy the tropical flavors of this creamy coconut mango sorbet!

2. Chocolate Avocado Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup or agave nectar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Blend Ingredients: In a blender or food processor, combine the ripe avocados, almond milk, cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Taste and Adjust: Taste the mixture and adjust sweetness if necessary, adding more maple syrup if desired.
  3. Chill the Mixture: Transfer the mixture to a bowl and refrigerate for about 30 minutes.
  4. Freeze: Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. For a no-churn method, pour the mixture into a shallow dish and freeze, stirring every 30 minutes until firm.
  5. Serve: Scoop into bowls and relish the rich, chocolatey goodness of this creamy avocado ice cream!

3. Strawberry Banana Nice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced and frozen
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • Optional toppings: chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or fresh berries

Instructions:

  1. Blend the Ingredients: In a food processor, combine the frozen bananas, fresh strawberries, and almond milk. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as necessary.
  2. Taste and Adjust: Taste the mixture and add more strawberries or bananas as desired for flavor.
  3. Serve Immediately or Freeze: You can serve the nice cream immediately for a soft-serve consistency or transfer it to a container and freeze for 1-2 hours for a firmer texture.
  4. Add Toppings: Scoop into bowls and top with your favorite toppings for added texture and flavor!

4. Vanilla Cashew Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water for 4-6 hours
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Blend Cashews: Drain and rinse the soaked cashews. In a blender, combine the cashews, coconut milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until completely smooth and creamy.
  2. Chill the Mixture: Pour the mixture into a bowl and refrigerate for about 30 minutes.
  3. Freeze: Transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. For a no-churn option, pour the mixture into a shallow dish and freeze, stirring every 30 minutes until firm.
  4. Serve: Scoop into bowls and enjoy the classic flavors of this creamy vanilla cashew ice cream!

5. Matcha Green Tea Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons matcha green tea powder
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, almond milk, maple syrup, matcha powder, and salt until well combined.
  2. Chill the Mixture: Refrigerate the mixture for about 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and the mixture to chill.
  3. Freeze: Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. For a no-churn option, pour the mixture into a shallow dish and freeze, stirring every 30 minutes until firm.
  4. Serve: Scoop into bowls and savor the unique flavor of this refreshing matcha green tea ice cream!

6. Peanut Butter Banana Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, sliced and frozen
  • 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup (optional)
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Blend the Ingredients: In a food processor, combine the frozen banana slices, peanut butter, maple syrup (if using), and salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Taste and Adjust: Taste the mixture and adjust sweetness or peanut butter as desired.
  3. Serve Immediately or Freeze: You can serve immediately for a soft-serve texture or transfer to a container and freeze for 1-2 hours for a firmer consistency.
  4. Enjoy: Scoop into bowls and indulge in the nutty, creamy goodness of this peanut butter banana ice cream!

7. Raspberry Coconut Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen raspberries
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Blend the Ingredients: In a blender, combine the raspberries, coconut milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Strain (Optional): If you desire a smoother texture, strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the seeds.
  3. Chill the Mixture: Pour the mixture into a bowl and refrigerate for about 30 minutes.
  4. Freeze: Transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. For a no-churn option, pour the mixture into a shallow dish and freeze, stirring every 30 minutes until firm.
  5. Serve: Scoop into bowls and enjoy the vibrant flavors of this raspberry coconut ice cream!

Conclusion

These seven vegan ice cream recipes are the perfect way to cool down this summer while indulging in delicious, plant-based desserts. From creamy coconut mango sorbet to rich chocolate avocado ice cream, each recipe offers unique flavors and textures that are sure to impress. So grab your ingredients, get creative in the kitchen, and treat yourself to these delightful homemade vegan ice creams. Your taste buds will thank you! Enjoy the summer heat with these refreshing, guilt-free treats!
