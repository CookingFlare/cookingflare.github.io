Lemon Garlic Butter Marinade The classic combination of lemon and garlic never fails to tantalize the senses. To prepare this marinade, start by melting half a cup of unsalted butter in a saucepan over low heat. Add the zest of one lemon and two cloves of minced garlic to the melted butter. Stir well and let the flavors infuse for a few minutes. Once the marinade is ready, generously brush it onto the lobster meat before grilling.
