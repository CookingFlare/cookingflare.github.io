Healthy Veggie Packed Meatloaf: Family Dinner Recipe


Meatloaf is a classic comfort food that brings families together around the dinner table. It’s hearty, flavorful, and reminds us of home-cooked meals from our childhood. But what if we could take this traditional dish and make it even healthier and more nutritious? Introducing the Healthy Veggie Packed Meatloaf, a delicious twist on the traditional recipe that is sure to please both young and old alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground lean turkey or chicken
  • 1 cup grated zucchini
  • 1 cup grated carrot
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a loaf pan with non-stick cooking spray.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground turkey or chicken, grated zucchini, grated carrot, chopped onion, chopped bell pepper, minced garlic, almond flour, tomato sauce, Worcestershire sauce, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. If desired, fold in the shredded cheese to add a melty, gooey surprise to your meatloaf.

  4. Transfer the meatloaf mixture to the prepared loaf pan, pressing it down firmly to ensure it holds its shape during baking.

  5. Place the loaf pan in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 1 hour, or until the meatloaf is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).

  6. Once cooked, remove the meatloaf from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

  7. Serve the Healthy Veggie Packed Meatloaf with your favorite sides, such as mashed potatoes and steamed vegetables, for a complete and satisfying family dinner.

Why Choose Veggie Packed Meatloaf?

This Healthy Veggie Packed Meatloaf is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. By incorporating grated zucchini and carrots into the meat mixture, you’re adding an extra dose of vitamins, minerals, and fiber to your meal. These veggies also contribute to the moistness of the meatloaf, ensuring a tender and juicy texture.

Moreover, using lean turkey or chicken instead of beef reduces the saturated fat content, making this dish heart-healthy and suitable for those watching their cholesterol levels. The almond flour adds a nutty flavor and acts as a binder, replacing the need for breadcrumbs.

Make it Your Own

Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your family’s preferences. You can experiment with different vegetables like spinach, mushrooms, or even sweet potatoes. Additionally, you can play around with the herbs and spices, adding a touch of cumin or smoked paprika for extra flavor.

Conclusion

With this Healthy Veggie Packed Meatloaf recipe, you can enjoy a classic family favorite while adding a nutritious twist to your meal. It’s a great way to sneak in extra vegetables and make sure your loved ones are getting the vitamins and minerals they need. So gather your family around the table, savor the delicious flavors, and create new memories with this wholesome and satisfying dish.
