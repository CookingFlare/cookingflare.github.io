10 Quick and Easy Family-Friendly Stir-Fry Recipes


Stir-fry dishes are the epitome of convenience, flavor, and nutrition—all wrapped up in a single, colorful, and satisfying meal. Whether you are rushing through a busy weeknight, trying to get your kids to eat more vegetables, or simply looking for a quick and healthy meal, stir-fry recipes always deliver. These meals are highly adaptable and can be tailored to fit dietary preferences, ingredient availability, and family taste preferences.

In this post, we’ll explore 10 delicious and family-friendly stir-fry recipes that are quick, easy, and perfect for busy households. These recipes are packed with fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a good balance of protein, vegetables, and whole grains. Plus, they take minimal time to prepare—so you can enjoy a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen!

1. Classic Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

A timeless stir-fry that brings together the perfect mix of lean chicken, vibrant vegetables, and savory sauce. This recipe is ideal for anyone looking for a healthy and delicious meal that the whole family will enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil to make the stir-fry sauce.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken slices and stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes or until fully cooked.
  3. Add the garlic and stir-fry for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Toss in the red bell pepper, broccoli, carrot, and onion. Stir-fry for an additional 3-4 minutes, allowing the vegetables to soften but still retain their crunch.
  5. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables, stirring to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

This stir-fry is a crowd-pleaser that’s sure to become a weeknight staple!

2. Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Beef and broccoli is one of the most popular stir-fries, and for good reason. The tender beef pairs perfectly with the crisp broccoli, and the savory sauce brings everything together in harmony.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch together. Add the beef slices to the mixture and marinate for about 15 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef slices and cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing them to sear.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger to the pan, stirring for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Toss in the broccoli and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, ensuring the beef is tender and the broccoli is crisp-tender.
  5. Add a splash of water if necessary to create a light sauce.
  6. Serve with steamed rice or noodles for a complete meal.

This beef and broccoli stir-fry is a family favorite that offers rich, savory flavors that are guaranteed to please everyone at the dinner table.

3. Sweet and Sour Shrimp Stir-Fry

Shrimp stir-fries are a fantastic way to quickly prepare a protein-packed dish that’s both healthy and delicious. This sweet and sour shrimp stir-fry is a delightful mix of flavors with a vibrant sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, ketchup, vinegar, honey, and red pepper flakes to create the sweet and sour sauce.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are pink and opaque.
  3. Add the bell pepper, onion, and pineapple chunks. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour the sweet and sour sauce over the shrimp and vegetables, tossing to coat evenly.
  5. Cook for an additional 2 minutes until everything is well combined.
  6. Serve with steamed jasmine rice or rice noodles for a balanced meal.

This sweet and sour shrimp stir-fry is bursting with flavor and offers a delicious tropical twist to your stir-fry repertoire.

4. Tofu and Vegetable Stir-Fry (Vegan)

Tofu is a great plant-based protein, and when paired with a variety of vegetables, it makes a satisfying and nutritious stir-fry. This vegan-friendly stir-fry is packed with vibrant veggies and flavored with a savory sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Press the tofu to remove excess moisture, then cut it into cubes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook for 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden and crispy on all sides.
  3. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set it aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add sesame oil and sauté the garlic and ginger for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add the mushrooms, bell pepper, zucchini, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  6. Return the tofu to the pan, then add the soy sauce and hoisin sauce. Toss everything together until well coated.
  7. Serve with steamed rice or noodles for a hearty and satisfying vegan meal.

This tofu and vegetable stir-fry is a wonderful option for those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet without sacrificing flavor or texture.

5. Cashew Chicken Stir-Fry

Cashew chicken is a beloved stir-fry dish that combines tender chicken with crunchy cashews and vegetables, all brought together with a savory sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 1/2 cup cashews (roasted and unsalted)
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken and cook for 5-7 minutes, until browned and cooked through.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Toss in the red bell pepper and broccoli. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, and sesame oil. Pour this sauce over the chicken and vegetables, tossing to coat evenly.
  5. Stir in the cashews and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Serve with steamed rice or noodles for a full meal.

This cashew chicken stir-fry is a crunchy, flavorful, and satisfying dish that everyone in the family will enjoy.

6. Pork and Pineapple Stir-Fry

The combination of savory pork and sweet pineapple creates a wonderful contrast in this stir-fry, making it a hit for both adults and kids alike.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced pork and stir-fry for about 4-5 minutes until cooked through.
  2. Add the ginger, garlic, red bell pepper, and onion. Stir-fry for an additional 2 minutes.
  3. Toss in the pineapple chunks and cook for another minute.
  4. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, honey, and rice vinegar. Pour this sauce over the pork and vegetables, stirring well to coat everything.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice for a complete, balanced meal.

This pork and pineapple stir-fry offers a sweet and savory combination that will delight your taste buds and bring a fun tropical flair to your dinner table.

7. Spicy Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry

Inspired by Thai street food, this spicy basil chicken stir-fry is aromatic, zesty, and bursting with fresh flavors. The combination of basil and chili gives it a delicious kick.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1-2 Thai bird’s eye chilies, minced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground chicken and cook until browned, breaking it up into smaller pieces as it cooks.
  2. Add the garlic, chilies, red bell pepper, green beans, and onion. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in the soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice. Toss to combine.
  4. Add the fresh basil leaves and cook for another 2 minutes until they wilt.
  5. Serve with steamed jasmine rice for an authentic Thai-inspired meal.

This spicy Thai basil chicken stir-fry is perfect for those who enjoy bold, flavorful dishes with a hint of spice.

8. Veggie Stir-Fry with Brown Rice

This hearty, vegetarian stir-fry makes use of wholesome ingredients like brown rice, tofu, and a variety of vegetables, making it a filling and nourishing meal for the whole family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown rice (cooked)
  • 1 block firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Press the tofu to remove excess moisture, then cut into cubes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add tofu and cook for 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally until golden.
  3. Remove tofu from the pan and set aside. Add sesame oil to the skillet and stir-fry the mushrooms, bell pepper, carrot, and zucchini for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked brown rice and tofu to the skillet. Pour in soy sauce and rice vinegar, stirring well to combine.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy a nourishing, plant-based stir-fry.

9. Garlic Shrimp Stir-Fry with Asparagus

Garlic and shrimp go hand in hand, and when combined with fresh asparagus, it makes for a quick and elegant stir-fry that’s packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque.
  2. Add the garlic and asparagus to the skillet. Stir-fry for another 4-5 minutes until the asparagus is tender-crisp.
  3. Add soy sauce, lemon juice, and red pepper flakes. Stir to combine and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Serve with steamed rice or noodles for a complete meal.

This garlic shrimp stir-fry with asparagus is simple, quick, and full of flavor—perfect for a healthy dinner.

10. Lemon Chicken Stir-Fry

This zesty lemon chicken stir-fry brings a fresh citrus twist to the traditional chicken stir-fry. The bright lemon flavor is balanced with savory soy sauce for a vibrant, tangy dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken and cook for about 5-7 minutes until browned and cooked through.
  2. Add the onion and red bell pepper, and stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the lemon juice, lemon zest, soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil. Pour this mixture over the chicken and vegetables.
  4. Stir well and cook for an additional 2 minutes until everything is coated and heated through.
  5. Serve with steamed rice for a light yet flavorful meal.

This lemon chicken stir-fry is bright, fresh, and perfect for a light weeknight dinner.

These 10 quick and easy stir-fry recipes are ideal for busy families looking for a meal that’s delicious, nutritious, and easy to prepare. Whether you prefer chicken, beef, shrimp, or a vegetarian option, there’s a stir-fry for everyone. Enjoy these flavorful dishes and bring a variety of delicious stir-fries to your dinner table this week!
