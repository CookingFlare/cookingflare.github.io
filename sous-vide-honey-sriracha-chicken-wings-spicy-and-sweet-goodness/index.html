Sous Vide Honey Sriracha Chicken Wings: Spicy and Sweet Goodness


If you’re looking for a tantalizing twist on classic chicken wings, look no further than these Sous Vide Honey Sriracha Chicken Wings. This recipe combines the succulent tenderness of sous vide cooking with the bold flavors of honey and sriracha, resulting in a mouthwatering dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a game day gathering or simply craving a flavorful snack, these wings are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To get started, gather the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional garnish: sesame seeds and chopped green onions

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Sous Vide Bath

  1. Preheat your water bath to 165°F (74°C).
  2. While the water bath is heating, prepare the chicken wings by trimming off any excess skin and separating the drumettes from the flats.

Step 2: Marinating the Chicken Wings

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the honey, sriracha sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil until well combined.
  2. Place the chicken wings in a large resealable bag and pour the marinade over them, ensuring that the wings are evenly coated. Seal the bag, removing as much air as possible.

Step 3: Sous Vide Cooking

  1. Submerge the sealed bag of chicken wings in the preheated water bath and cook for 2 hours.
  2. Once the cooking time is up, carefully remove the bag from the water bath and transfer the wings to a bowl.

Step 4: Broiling the Wings

  1. Preheat your broiler on high.
  2. Place the wings on a lined baking sheet and broil for 5-7 minutes, flipping once halfway through, until the skin is golden and crispy.

Step 5: Serving

  1. Transfer the wings to a serving platter and sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped green onions, if desired.
  2. Serve the wings hot, and enjoy the harmonious blend of sweet and spicy flavors with each succulent bite.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra kick of heat, increase the amount of sriracha sauce to suit your preference.
  • To save time, you can prepare the marinade a day in advance and let the wings marinate overnight in the refrigerator.
  • If you prefer a stickier glaze, you can simmer the remaining marinade in a saucepan until it thickens, then brush it onto the wings before broiling.

Conclusion

These Sous Vide Honey Sriracha Chicken Wings are a delightful combination of spicy and sweet, delivering a flavor-packed punch with every bite. Whether you’re a fan of fiery heat or crave a touch of sweetness, these wings are sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. So, fire up your sous vide machine, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible goodness of these delectable chicken wings. Enjoy!
