Tasty Instant Pot Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Recipes


If you’re looking for a quick, delicious, and comforting meal, look no further than these tasty Instant Pot creamy chicken and mushroom recipes. With the magic of the Instant Pot, you can whip up a creamy, flavorful dish in no time, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Instant Pot Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Casserole

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Set the Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and heat the olive oil. Brown the chicken breasts on both sides for about 3 minutes per side. Once browned, remove the chicken and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the butter, mushrooms, and onion. Saute until the mushrooms are golden brown and the onions are translucent. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the mushroom mixture and stir to combine. This will help thicken the sauce.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and stir, scraping any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. This will add a depth of flavor to the dish.

  5. Place the chicken back into the pot, then add the dried thyme. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then do a quick pressure release.

  7. Open the lid and stir in the heavy cream. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.

  8. Serve the creamy chicken and mushroom casserole over rice or pasta, garnished with fresh parsley.

Instant Pot Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Crusty bread for serving

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper. Using the Instant Pot’s “Saute” mode, heat the olive oil and brown the chicken thighs for about 3 minutes per side. Once browned, remove the chicken and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the onion and saute until translucent. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.

  3. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their juices and start to brown.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, add the dried thyme, and return the chicken thighs to the pot.

  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then do a quick pressure release.

  7. Remove the chicken thighs from the pot and shred them using two forks.

  8. Stir in the heavy cream and return the shredded chicken to the pot. Let the soup simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

  9. Serve the creamy chicken and mushroom soup garnished with fresh parsley, accompanied by crusty bread.

These Instant Pot creamy chicken and mushroom recipes are sure to become a staple in your meal rotation. They’re easy to prepare, packed with flavor, and perfect for warming you up on a chilly day. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or a group of friends, these recipes are guaranteed to impress. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in some creamy, comforting goodness!
