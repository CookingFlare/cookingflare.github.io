Beyond Burgers: Creative Grilled Sandwich Ideas


Are you tired of the same old burger recipes? Looking for a delicious and unique twist on the classic grilled sandwich? Look no further! We have some amazing ideas using Beyond Burgers that will take your grilled sandwich game to the next level. These plant-based burgers are not only tasty but also offer a sustainable and healthier alternative to traditional meat patties. So, fire up your grill and get ready to tantalize your taste buds with these creative grilled sandwich ideas!

1. BBQ Bacon Ranch Burger

Ingredients:

  • 2 Beyond Burger patties
  • 4 slices of crispy bacon
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ranch dressing
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the Beyond Burger patties according to the package instructions.
  3. While the patties are cooking, crisp up the bacon in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Once the patties are cooked, brush them with BBQ sauce and place a slice of cheddar cheese on top of each patty.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill until lightly golden.
  6. Assemble the sandwich by placing a patty with cheese on the bottom bun, followed by two slices of bacon, a drizzle of ranch dressing, and the top bun.
  7. Repeat the process to make another delicious BBQ Bacon Ranch Burger.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Mediterranean Veggie Burger

Ingredients:

  • 2 Beyond Burger patties
  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 4 slices of red onion
  • 4 hamburger buns

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the Beyond Burger patties according to the package instructions.
  3. While the patties are cooking, prepare the toppings by dicing the cucumbers and tomatoes, and crumble the feta cheese.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill until lightly golden.
  5. Spread a generous amount of hummus on the bottom bun.
  6. Place a Beyond Burger patty on top of the hummus, followed by the diced cucumbers, tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, and a slice of red onion.
  7. Cover with the top bun and press the sandwich gently to hold everything together.
  8. Repeat the process to make another mouthwatering Mediterranean Veggie Burger.
  9. Serve immediately and savor the Mediterranean flavors!

3. Spicy Guacamole Burger

Ingredients:

  • 2 Beyond Burger patties
  • 1/2 cup guacamole
  • 4 slices of pepper jack cheese
  • 4 tablespoons pickled jalapeños
  • 4 hamburger buns

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the Beyond Burger patties according to the package instructions.
  3. While the patties are cooking, prepare the toppings by spreading a generous amount of guacamole on the bottom bun and slicing the pepper jack cheese.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill until lightly golden.
  5. Once the patties are cooked, place a slice of pepper jack cheese on top of each patty and allow it to melt slightly.
  6. Place a Beyond Burger patty on the bottom bun, followed by a tablespoon of pickled jalapeños.
  7. Cover with the top bun and press the sandwich gently to combine all the flavors.
  8. Repeat the process to make another tantalizing Spicy Guacamole Burger.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy the spicy kick!

These are just a few creative grilled sandwich ideas using Beyond Burgers. Feel free to experiment with different toppings, sauces, and buns to create your own unique combinations. The possibilities are endless! So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and let your taste buds be amazed by the deliciousness of these plant-based grilled sandwiches. Happy grilling!
