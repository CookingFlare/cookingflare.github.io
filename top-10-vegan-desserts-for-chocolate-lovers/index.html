Top 10 Vegan Desserts for Chocolate Lovers


If you’re a chocolate lover who also follows a vegan lifestyle, you might think satisfying your sweet tooth is a challenge. But fear not! The world of vegan desserts is rich and diverse, offering an abundance of delicious chocolatey treats that are not only plant-based but also incredibly satisfying. From decadent cakes to creamy mousses, this list of the top 10 vegan desserts for chocolate lovers will have you indulging without the guilt!

1. Vegan Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup silken tofu
  • 1 cup dairy-free dark chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Melt the chocolate chips in a microwave or double boiler until smooth.
  2. In a blender, combine the melted chocolate, silken tofu, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until creamy and well combined.
  3. Pour the mousse into serving dishes and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving. This dessert is rich, creamy, and perfect for a romantic evening.

2. Vegan Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine almond flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool before cutting into squares.

3. Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix almond flour, coconut flour, coconut sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together melted coconut oil, almond milk, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, then fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop tablespoons of dough onto the baking sheet, spacing them apart.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden. Let cool before enjoying these chewy delights.

4. Vegan Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together almond milk, vegetable oil, apple cider vinegar, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and bake for 30-35 minutes. Let cool before frosting.

Frosting

  • 1/2 cup dairy-free butter
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Beat dairy-free butter until creamy. Gradually add powdered sugar and cocoa powder.
  2. Add almond milk and vanilla extract until smooth. Frost the cooled cake layers generously.

5. Vegan Chocolate Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 3/4 cups almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together cocoa powder, cornstarch, sugar, and salt.
  2. Gradually stir in almond milk until well combined.
  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract. Pour into serving dishes and chill before serving.

6. Vegan Chocolate Energy Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pitted dates
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup dairy-free chocolate chips
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, pulse dates until they form a sticky paste.
  2. Add rolled oats, cocoa powder, almond butter, chocolate chips, and salt. Pulse until combined.
  3. Roll the mixture into small balls and place them on a baking sheet.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to firm up. These bites are perfect for a quick energy boost!

7. Vegan Chocolate Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine coconut milk, cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. Transfer to a container and freeze for a few hours before serving. Enjoy your creamy, chocolatey delight!

8. Vegan Chocolate Truffles

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dairy-free chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup canned coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder or crushed nuts for rolling

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat coconut milk until just boiling. Remove from heat and add chocolate chips.
  2. Stir until the chocolate is melted and smooth. Add vanilla extract and mix well.
  3. Refrigerate the mixture for about 30 minutes until firm.
  4. Scoop out small portions and roll into balls. Roll in cocoa powder or crushed nuts for coating.
  5. Chill again before serving. These truffles are a rich and satisfying treat!

9. Vegan Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and grease four ramekins.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt chocolate chips and coconut oil together.
  3. Stir in almond milk until smooth. Then mix in flour, powdered sugar, baking powder, and salt until combined.
  4. Divide the batter among the ramekins and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  5. Let cool for a minute, then invert onto plates. Enjoy the gooey center!

10. Vegan Chocolate Pecan Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made vegan pie crust
  • 1 cup pecans
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup silken tofu
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a blender, combine maple syrup, silken tofu, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until smooth.
  3. Fold in chopped pecans and pour the mixture into the pie crust.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes until the filling is set. Let cool before slicing.

Conclusion

These top 10 vegan desserts are perfect for chocolate lovers looking to indulge in sweet treats without compromising on their dietary choices. Each recipe is simple, delicious, and packed with chocolatey goodness. Whether you’re craving a rich cake, creamy mousse, or something quick like energy bites, this list has something for every occasion. So gather your ingredients, unleash your inner baker, and treat yourself to some of the best vegan chocolate desserts you’ve ever had!
