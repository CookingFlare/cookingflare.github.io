Grilled Watermelon Salad: A Refreshing Fourth of July Side


Summer is in full swing, and what better way to celebrate the Fourth of July than with a refreshing and vibrant Grilled Watermelon Salad? This unique twist on a classic fruit salad will have your taste buds dancing with delight. Bursting with juicy watermelon, tangy feta cheese, crisp cucumber, and a zesty lime dressing, this salad is the perfect addition to your holiday spread.

Ingredients

  • 1 small seedless watermelon
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the watermelon by cutting it into thick, round slices. Leave the rind intact for grilling.

  2. Place the watermelon slices directly on the grill grates. Grill for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until you see nice grill marks forming. Remove the grilled watermelon from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes.

  3. While the watermelon is cooling, peel the cucumber and slice it into thin rounds. Set aside.

  4. Once the watermelon has cooled slightly, cut it into bite-sized cubes, removing the rind as you go. Transfer the cubed watermelon to a large bowl.

  5. Add the cucumber slices, crumbled feta cheese, mint leaves, and basil leaves to the bowl with the watermelon.

  6. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.

  7. Pour the dressing over the watermelon salad and gently toss to combine, ensuring that all the ingredients are evenly coated.

  8. Transfer the salad to a serving dish and garnish with a few sprigs of fresh mint and basil leaves for an extra pop of color.

  9. Serve the Grilled Watermelon Salad immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors in every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • To take this salad to the next level, consider adding some grilled shrimp or chicken for a protein-packed meal.
  • If you’re not a fan of feta cheese, try substituting it with crumbled goat cheese or fresh mozzarella.
  • For a little extra crunch, sprinkle some toasted pine nuts or chopped walnuts over the salad.
  • Don’t have a grill? No problem! You can achieve similar results by using a grill pan or even a regular frying pan on the stovetop.
  • Feeling adventurous? Try adding a drizzle of balsamic glaze or a sprinkle of chili flakes for an extra kick.

Final Thoughts

Grilled Watermelon Salad is the epitome of summer eating. It’s light, refreshing, and bursting with seasonal flavors. By grilling the watermelon, you add a subtle smokiness that enhances the natural sweetness of the fruit. The combination of juicy watermelon, tangy feta cheese, cool cucumber, and aromatic herbs creates a harmonious balance of flavors that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re hosting a Fourth of July barbecue or simply looking for a delicious and healthy side dish, this Grilled Watermelon Salad is sure to impress. So fire up the grill and get ready to savor the taste of summer!
