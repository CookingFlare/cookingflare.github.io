Savory Keto Meatballs in Marinara Sauce: A Family Favorite


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly meal that the whole family will love, look no further than these savory keto meatballs in marinara sauce. Packed with flavor and low in carbs, these meatballs are perfect for anyone following a keto lifestyle or simply looking to enjoy a healthier, low-carb dinner option. The best part? They’re so tasty that even the pickiest eaters won’t be able to resist!

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Marinara Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meatballs:

    • In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, almond flour, Parmesan cheese, parsley, egg, garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Use your hands to mix everything together until well combined.
    • Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter. You should get around 20 meatballs from the mixture.

  2. Cook the Meatballs:

    • Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add a bit of olive oil. Once the skillet is hot, add the meatballs in a single layer, being careful not to overcrowd the pan. You may need to cook them in batches.
    • Cook the meatballs for about 8-10 minutes, turning them occasionally, until they are browned on all sides and cooked through. Remove the meatballs from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Make the Marinara Sauce:

    • In the same skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
    • Pour in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine, then bring the sauce to a simmer.
    • Once the sauce is simmering, return the meatballs to the skillet, spooning some of the sauce over the top of each one. Let the meatballs simmer in the sauce for an additional 10-15 minutes.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the meatballs are heated through and the flavors have melded together, it’s time to serve! Plate the meatballs and spoon some of the marinara sauce on top. Optionally, sprinkle with a bit of fresh parsley or grated Parmesan cheese for an extra pop of flavor.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 4 meatballs with sauce
  • Calories: 315
  • Total Fat: 22g
  • Saturated Fat: 8g
  • Cholesterol: 105mg
  • Sodium: 420mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 4g
  • Protein: 24g

Final Thoughts

These savory keto meatballs in marinara sauce are a surefire way to please the whole family while staying true to your keto lifestyle. The combination of tender, flavorful meatballs and rich, tangy marinara sauce is a match made in culinary heaven. Plus, with the added bonus of being low in carbs, you can indulge in this meal without any guilt. So, next time you’re planning a family dinner or a special gathering, consider adding this recipe to your menu. It’s a crowd-pleaser that’s as enjoyable to make as it is to eat!
