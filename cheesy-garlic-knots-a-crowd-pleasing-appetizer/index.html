Cheesy Garlic Knots: A Crowd-Pleasing Appetizer


Cheesy Garlic Knots are the perfect appetizer to impress your friends and family. Not only are they absolutely mouth-watering, but they’re also simple to make. This recipe is ideal for parties, game nights, or any gathering where you need a decadent, satisfying snack. In this guide, you’ll learn how to create this addictive appetizer and impress your guests with ease.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for brushing

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix thoroughly.

This step creates the perfect canvas for our cheesy garlic knots. The sugar and yeast play a crucial role in the rising dough, which ensures a soft and tender final product.

  1. In another bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, and chopped parsley.

This garlic butter mixture infuses the knots with an aromatic, nutty flavor. We’ll use it later to help our knots achieve that irresistible cheesy, garlicky coating.

  1. Slowly incorporate the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until a dough forms.

It’s crucial to slowly incorporate the dry ingredients to avoid overworking the dough. If the mix appears too dry, add small amounts of water until the dough comes together. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 4 minutes, or until it is smooth and elastic.

  1. Allow the dough to rise in a well-oiled bowl for about an hour.

This step is crucial for the dough to fully develop its yeasty, delicious flavor. Cover the bowl with a clean towel and let it sit in a warm place while it rises.

  1. Punch down the dough and roll it into small, round portions.

Once the dough has risen, punch it down and form it into small, round portions. Roll each portion into a tight knot. This technique ensures that your cheesy garlic knots stay tender from edge to edge.

  1. Roll each knot in the garlic butter mixture and place them onto a baking sheet.

This crucial step imparts that aromatic, garlicky flavor we’re aiming for! Make sure to coat each knot thoroughly in the garlic butter mixture.

  1. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for about 15-20 minutes or until the knots are golden brown.

These delicious cheesy garlic knots should be cooked just until they’re golden brown, with a tempting crunch.

  1. Once done, take them out of the oven and brush with extra-virgin olive oil.

This final touch adds that perfect layer of richness to the knots, enhancing the overall flavor of our appetizer.

  1. Serve immediately and enjoy.

Cheesy Garlic Knots are best enjoyed right away, so serve them hot and watch as they disappear in no time!

In conclusion, Cheesy Garlic Knots are a surefire way to wow your guests at any gathering. This delicious and satisfying appetizer is easier to make than you might think. So, bring these knots to your next party and watch as your friends and family generations eagerly devour them!
