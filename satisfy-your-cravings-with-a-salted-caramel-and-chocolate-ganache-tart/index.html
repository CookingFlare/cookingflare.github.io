Satisfy Your Cravings with a Salted Caramel and Chocolate Ganache Tart

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups (155g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon (13g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) ice water

For the Salted Caramel Filling:

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) water
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup (57g) unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • 8 ounces (227g) semisweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

For the Crust:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Using a pastry cutter or two forks, cut in the chilled butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, mixing with a fork until the dough starts to come together.
  4. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead briefly until it forms a smooth ball.
  5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  6. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to a 12-inch circle.
  7. Transfer the dough to a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom, pressing it into the bottom and up the sides.
  8. Trim the excess dough from the edges and prick the bottom with a fork.
  9. Refrigerate the tart shell for 30 minutes.
  10. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  11. Line the tart shell with parchment paper or foil and fill with pie weights or dried beans.
  12. Bake for 20 minutes, then remove the weights and parchment paper.
  13. Continue baking for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  14. Remove from the oven and let cool completely on a wire rack.

For the Salted Caramel Filling:

  1. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water.
  2. Cook over medium heat, swirling the pan occasionally, until the sugar dissolves and turns a deep amber color.
  3. Remove from heat and carefully pour in the heavy cream (the mixture will bubble up).
  4. Whisk until the mixture is smooth and the caramel has dissolved.
  5. Add the unsalted butter and whisk until melted and combined.
  6. Stir in the vanilla extract and sea salt.
  7. Pour the salted caramel filling into the cooled tart shell and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until set.

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.
  2. Remove from heat and add the finely chopped semisweet chocolate.
  3. Let stand for 2-3 minutes, then whisk until the mixture is smooth and the chocolate is completely melted.
  4. Stir in the sea salt.
  5. Allow the ganache to cool and thicken slightly, about 10-15 minutes.
  6. Pour the ganache over the chilled salted caramel filling, spreading it evenly with an offset spatula.
  7. Refrigerate the tart for at least 2 hours, or until the ganache is set.

Serving and Storing

This salted caramel and chocolate ganache tart is best served chilled or at room temperature. Slice it into wedges and enjoy the perfect balance of flavors with every bite. If you have any leftovers, store the tart in the refrigerator, covered with plastic wrap or aluminum foil, for up to 5 days.
