Grilled Pita Pockets: Portable and Delicious


Who doesn’t love a tasty and convenient meal that you can take on the go? That’s where grilled pita pockets come in! These delicious handheld delights are not only easy to make but also incredibly versatile. From savory fillings to sweet treats, the possibilities are endless. So, let’s dive into the world of grilled pita pockets and discover some scrumptious recipes that will leave you craving more.

The Perfect Grilled Pita Pocket

Before we delve into the mouthwatering fillings, let’s start with the basics - the perfect grilled pita pocket. The key to achieving that irresistible grilled flavor lies in the preparation. Here’s a step-by-step guide to grilling the perfect pita pocket:

  1. Choose Your Pita: Opt for fresh, high-quality pita bread. Whole wheat or white, it’s entirely up to you.

  2. Preheat Your Grill: Whether you’re using a gas or charcoal grill, make sure it’s preheated to medium-high heat. This will ensure that your pita pockets cook evenly and develop those beautiful grill marks.

  3. Brush with Olive Oil: Lightly brush both sides of the pita bread with olive oil. This will prevent sticking and add a delightful flavor.

  4. Grill to Perfection: Place the oiled pita bread directly on the grill grates. Cook for about 1-2 minutes on each side until it becomes slightly crispy and has those irresistible grill marks.

  5. Remove and Rest: Once your pita pockets are grilled to perfection, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a minute or two. This will allow them to cool slightly and become more pliable for filling.

Now that we have the grilled pita pockets ready, let’s explore some delectable fillings that will elevate your taste buds to new heights.

Savory Grilled Pita Pocket Fillings

  1. Greek-inspired Chicken: Marinate chicken breast in a mixture of lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Grill the chicken until cooked through, then slice it into thin strips. Fill your pita pocket with the grilled chicken, sliced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onions, feta cheese, and a dollop of tzatziki sauce. This Mediterranean-inspired delight is bursting with flavors.

  2. Mexican Fiesta: Cook ground beef with your favorite taco seasoning until browned and flavorful. Fill your pita pocket with the seasoned beef, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, shredded cheese, and a drizzle of spicy salsa. This zesty combination will transport your taste buds to the streets of Mexico.

  3. Mediterranean Veggie: Grill slices of eggplant, bell peppers, and zucchini until tender. Fill your pita pocket with the grilled veggies, crumbled goat cheese, fresh basil leaves, and a sprinkle of balsamic glaze. This vegetarian delight is not only colorful but also bursting with Mediterranean flavors.

Sweet Grilled Pita Pocket Treats

  1. Nutella and Banana: Spread a generous layer of Nutella on the inside of your grilled pita pocket. Slice a ripe banana and place the slices inside the pocket. For an extra touch of indulgence, sprinkle some crushed nuts on top. This sweet treat is perfect for satisfying your chocolate cravings.

  2. Grilled Fruit Delight: Grill slices of pineapple, peaches, and mango until they develop those beautiful grill marks. Fill your pita pocket with the grilled fruits, a dollop of Greek yogurt, and a drizzle of honey. This refreshing and fruity dessert is a delightful way to end any meal.

The Possibilities are Endless

Grilled pita pockets are a culinary canvas waiting to be filled with your favorite ingredients. Whether you prefer savory or sweet, there’s a grilled pita pocket recipe for everyone. Get creative, experiment with flavors, and enjoy the portability and deliciousness of these handheld wonders.

So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and satisfying meal, don’t hesitate to whip up some grilled pita pockets. They’re easy to make, packed with flavor, and perfect for any occasion. Get grilling and discover the endless possibilities that await you!
