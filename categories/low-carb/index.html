Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Make sure to coat both sides evenly. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Once cooked, remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. While the chicken is resting, prepare the salad by combining the mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese in a large bowl. Slice the grilled chicken breasts into thin strips and add them to the salad bowl. Toss the salad gently to combine all the ingredients and distribute the flavors evenly.
