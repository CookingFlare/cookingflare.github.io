Flavorful and Fragrant Vietnamese Pho Stew Recipes


Pho, the beloved Vietnamese noodle soup, is a dish that captivates the senses with its aromatic broth and tender ingredients. This comforting bowl of goodness has gained popularity worldwide for its rich flavors and versatility. In this blog post, we will explore some delicious and authentic Vietnamese Pho stew recipes that will transport you to the bustling streets of Vietnam.

Classic Beef Pho

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef bones
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 3-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 4 star anise pods
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 6 cloves
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, bean sprouts, and lime wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the beef bones on a baking sheet and roast for 30 minutes until browned.
  2. In a large pot, add the roasted bones, onion, ginger, star anise, cinnamon sticks, cloves, coriander seeds, fennel seeds, sugar, fish sauce, salt, and beef broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours.
  3. While the broth simmers, cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Strain the broth through a fine-mesh sieve, discarding the solids. Return the broth to the pot and bring it back to a simmer.
  5. Divide the cooked rice noodles among serving bowls. Top with the thinly sliced beef sirloin.
  6. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles and beef, ensuring they are fully submerged. The hot broth will cook the beef slices.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro, bean sprouts, and lime wedges.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy the flavorful and fragrant beef pho stew!

Chicken Pho with a Twist

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 onions, halved
  • 4-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 4 star anise pods
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 6 cloves
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • Fresh herbs such as Thai basil, mint, and cilantro for garnish
  • Sliced jalapeños and lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken in a large pot and add the onions, ginger, star anise, cinnamon sticks, cloves, coriander seeds, fennel seeds, sugar, fish sauce, salt, and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 1 hour until the chicken is cooked through.
  2. Remove the chicken from the pot and let it cool. Once cooled, shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces and set aside.
  3. Strain the broth through a fine-mesh sieve, discarding the solids. Return the broth to the pot and bring it back to a simmer.
  4. Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Divide the cooked rice noodles among serving bowls. Top with the shredded chicken.
  6. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles and chicken.
  7. Garnish with fresh herbs such as Thai basil, mint, and cilantro.
  8. Serve hot with sliced jalapeños and lime wedges on the side for an extra kick of flavor.

Vegetarian Pho Delight

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, halved
  • 4-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 4 star anise pods
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 6 cloves
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 8 cups vegetable broth
  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • Assorted vegetables such as bok choy, mushrooms, and bean sprouts for garnish
  • Fresh cilantro and lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, add the onion, ginger, star anise, cinnamon sticks, cloves, coriander seeds, fennel seeds, sugar, soy sauce, salt, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour.
  2. While the broth simmers, cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prep your choice of vegetables by washing and slicing them.
  4. Strain the broth through a fine-mesh sieve, discarding the solids. Return the broth to the pot and bring it back to a simmer.
  5. Divide the cooked rice noodles among serving bowls. Top with the assorted vegetables.
  6. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles and vegetables.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and lime wedges.
  8. Serve hot and savor the delightful flavors of the vegetarian pho stew!

Conclusion

Pho is an incredibly versatile dish that can be customized to suit various dietary preferences. Whether you prefer the rich flavors of beef, the comforting taste of chicken, or the freshness of vegetables, there’s a pho recipe for everyone. These flavorful and fragrant Vietnamese Pho stew recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings and transport you to the vibrant streets of Vietnam. So gather your ingredients, embrace the aromas, and dive into a bowl of pho that will warm your heart and tantalize your taste buds. Enjoy!
