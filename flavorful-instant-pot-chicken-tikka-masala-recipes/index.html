Flavorful Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala Recipes


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, you’ve likely indulged in the rich and aromatic flavors of Chicken Tikka Masala. This classic dish is a favorite in Indian restaurants around the world, and for good reason. The tender, marinated chicken combined with a creamy and spiced tomato-based sauce creates a symphony of flavors that is simply irresistible. While traditional preparation methods can be time-consuming, I’m excited to share with you a convenient and delicious way to make Chicken Tikka Masala at home using an Instant Pot.

Instant Pot: A Time-Saving Wonder

The Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking by significantly reducing cooking times without compromising on flavor. This electric pressure cooker is a game-changer for busy individuals who still crave homemade, flavorful meals. By harnessing the power of pressure and steam, the Instant Pot allows you to achieve succulent and tender dishes in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional cooking methods.

A Flavorful Twist on Chicken Tikka Masala

The Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala recipe I’m about to share with you encapsulates the essence of this beloved dish while streamlining the cooking process. By infusing the chicken with a blend of spices and then pressure cooking it with a luscious tomato and yogurt-based sauce, you’ll achieve a restaurant-quality result right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs (680g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup (240g) plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon (15ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon (15g) grated fresh ginger
  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup (240ml) chicken broth
  • 2 teaspoons (10g) ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons (10g) paprika
  • 2 teaspoons (10g) garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon (5g) turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon (5g) ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon (5g) ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon (5g) cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with the Greek yogurt, cumin, paprika, garam masala, turmeric, coriander, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure the chicken is evenly coated. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or ideally overnight for the best flavor.

  2. Set the Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger, and sauté until the onions are soft and translucent.

  3. Add the marinated chicken to the Instant Pot and cook until the chicken is lightly browned on all sides.

  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and chicken broth, then give everything a good stir, ensuring to scrape any browned bits from the bottom of the pot to prevent burning.

  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, set the vent to the sealing position, and pressure cook on high for 10 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then carefully perform a quick pressure release.

  7. Stir in the heavy cream and let the chicken tikka masala sit for a few minutes to thicken up. If the sauce needs further thickening, you can set the Instant Pot to the sauté function for a few minutes, stirring occasionally.

  8. Serve the Chicken Tikka Masala over steamed basmati rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Final Thoughts

The beauty of this Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala recipe lies in its ability to deliver a deeply flavorful and aromatic dish in a fraction of the time it would traditionally take. The tender, marinated chicken combined with the rich and creamy sauce makes for a truly satisfying meal that will have your taste buds singing with joy. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a cooking novice, this recipe is sure to impress and become a go-to favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, fire up your Instant Pot and embark on a culinary journey to savor the vibrant flavors of Chicken Tikka Masala from the comfort of your home.

I hope you enjoy creating and savoring this delightful Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala recipe as much as I do. Happy cooking!
