Thai Khao Soi Recipe with Curried Noodles and Chicken


If you’re craving a taste of Thailand in your own kitchen, look no further than this authentic and aromatic Thai Khao Soi recipe. This dish, featuring curried noodles and tender chicken, is a symphony of flavors and textures that will transport you to the bustling streets of Chiang Mai. Let’s dive into the delightful world of Thai cuisine and learn how to create this culinary masterpiece right in your own home.

Ingredients

For the Khao Soi Paste:

  • 2 shallots, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2-inch piece of ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of shrimp paste
  • 2-3 dried red chilies, soaked in hot water

For the Khao Soi Soup:

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons of Khao Soi paste
  • 2 cans of coconut milk
  • 4 cups of chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon of palm sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass, bruised
  • 8 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on

For the Curried Noodles:

  • 400g of egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons of yellow curry paste
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of palm sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • Sliced shallots, lime wedges, and cilantro for garnish

Instructions

Making the Khao Soi Paste:

  1. In a dry pan, toast the coriander seeds until fragrant, then grind them into a fine powder.
  2. In a blender, combine the toasted coriander, shallots, garlic, ginger, turmeric, curry powder, shrimp paste, and soaked red chilies. Blend until a smooth paste forms.

Cooking the Khao Soi Soup:

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the Khao Soi paste and cook for a few minutes until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken stock, then add the palm sugar, fish sauce, and bruised lemongrass. Bring the soup to a gentle simmer.
  3. Add the chicken thighs to the pot and simmer for 30-40 minutes until the chicken is tender and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred the meat. Discard the bones and return the shredded chicken to the soup.

Preparing the Curried Noodles:

  1. Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the yellow curry paste and cook for a minute to release its aromas.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk, soy sauce, palm sugar, and fish sauce. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Add the cooked egg noodles to the curry sauce and toss to coat evenly.

Serving the Khao Soi:

  1. Ladle the fragrant Khao Soi soup into bowls, then top each serving with a generous portion of curried noodles.
  2. Garnish with sliced shallots, lime wedges, and fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness and color.
  3. Serve the Khao Soi hot and enjoy the rich, complex flavors of this traditional Thai dish.

Conclusion

The Thai Khao Soi recipe with curried noodles and chicken is a delightful harmony of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors that will captivate your taste buds. With its vibrant colors and aromatic aromas, this dish is a true celebration of Thai cuisine. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or an adventurous beginner, this recipe is sure to impress and transport you to the bustling streets of Thailand. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the exotic, and embark on a culinary journey with this remarkable Khao Soi dish.
