Indulge in Authentic Chinese Beef with Chinese Broccoli (Gai Lan)


If you’re looking to add a touch of authenticity to your home cooking, then you’re in for a treat. Today, we’re diving into the world of traditional Chinese cuisine with a mouthwatering recipe for Chinese Beef with Chinese Broccoli, also known as Gai Lan. This dish is a delightful combination of tender beef, vibrant green Chinese broccoli, and a savory sauce that will transport your taste buds straight to the streets of Beijing.

Ingredients

For the Beef Marinade:

  • 1 pound (450g) flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Chinese rice wine or dry sherry
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 1 pound (450g) Chinese broccoli (Gai Lan), cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) beef broth or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 2 teaspoons water
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions

Marinating the Beef:

  1. In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced flank steak with soy sauce, oyster sauce, Chinese rice wine, cornstarch, sesame oil, sugar, and black pepper. Toss to coat the beef evenly, then let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Stir-Frying the Dish:

  1. Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil and swirl to coat the pan.
  2. Add the marinated beef to the wok in a single layer, allowing it to sear without overcrowding the pan. Cook for about 1-2 minutes on each side until the beef is browned but still slightly pink in the center. Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside.
  3. In the same wok, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil. Stir in the minced garlic and ginger, and cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the Chinese broccoli to the wok and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until the greens begin to wilt.
  5. Pour in the beef or chicken broth, oyster sauce, and soy sauce. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then return the cooked beef to the wok, combining all the ingredients.
  6. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with water to create a slurry. Pour the slurry into the wok, stirring continuously until the sauce thickens and coats the beef and Chinese broccoli evenly.
  7. Once the sauce has thickened, remove the wok from the heat.

Serving the Dish:

  1. Transfer the Chinese Beef with Chinese Broccoli to a serving plate and serve it alongside steamed rice for a complete and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

Indulging in Authentic Chinese Beef with Chinese Broccoli (Gai Lan) is a delightful experience that brings the rich flavors of Chinese cuisine into your own kitchen. The tender beef, vibrant greens, and savory sauce make this dish a true standout. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just beginning your culinary adventures, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your wok, and transport your taste buds to the heart of China with this authentic and delicious dish. Enjoy!
