Mouthwatering Maple Pecan Bars: A Sweet Fall Treat


When the leaves start to change and the air becomes crisp, there’s nothing quite like the warm, comforting flavors of fall. And what better way to embrace the season than with a batch of mouthwatering maple pecan bars? These delightful treats are a perfect blend of sweet, nutty, and buttery flavors, making them an ideal companion to a cozy evening by the fireplace or a gathering with friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup confectioners’ sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup pure maple syrup
  • ¾ cup packed light brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup heavy cream
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cups pecan halves, roughly chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
    • In a food processor, combine the flour, confectioners’ sugar, and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Press the mixture into the prepared baking pan and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until lightly golden.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a saucepan, combine the maple syrup, brown sugar, heavy cream, and butter. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture comes to a boil.
    • Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the chopped pecans. Let the mixture cool for 10 minutes.

  3. Assemble the Bars:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, vanilla extract, salt, and flour. Gradually pour the maple pecan mixture into the bowl, stirring until well combined.
    • Pour the filling over the baked crust and return the pan to the oven. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set.

  4. Cool and Serve:

    • Allow the maple pecan bars to cool completely in the pan before cutting them into squares. These bars are best served at room temperature and can be stored in an airtight container for up to a week.

Why You’ll Love These Bars

The combination of rich, buttery crust and the sweet, nutty filling makes these maple pecan bars a true delight for the taste buds. The use of pure maple syrup adds a depth of flavor that perfectly complements the crunchy pecans, while the buttery crust provides the ideal base for this indulgent treat.

These bars are versatile and can be enjoyed as a dessert, a sweet snack, or even as a decadent addition to a brunch spread. Their irresistible aroma and beautiful golden-brown color also make them a stunning centerpiece for any fall gathering or holiday celebration.

Embrace the Flavors of Fall

As the season changes and the air becomes cooler, take the opportunity to indulge in the delightful flavors of fall with these mouthwatering maple pecan bars. Whether you’re baking them for a special occasion or simply to treat yourself, these bars are sure to become a beloved favorite in your repertoire of fall recipes. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the sweet aroma of maple and pecans fill your kitchen with warmth and comfort.
