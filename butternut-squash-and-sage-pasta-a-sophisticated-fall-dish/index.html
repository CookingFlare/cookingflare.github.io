Butternut Squash and Sage Pasta: A Sophisticated Fall Dish


As the leaves start to turn vibrant shades of red and orange, and the air becomes crisp and cool, it’s time to embrace the flavors of fall. What better way to do that than with a sophisticated and comforting dish like Butternut Squash and Sage Pasta? This recipe combines the earthy sweetness of butternut squash with the warm, aromatic notes of sage, creating a delightful harmony of flavors that will leave you craving more. Let’s dive into the culinary journey of creating this exquisite dish.

Ingredients

For the Butternut Squash Sauce:

  • 1 medium-sized butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pasta:

  • 1 lb. fettuccine or your preferred pasta
  • Salt for boiling water

For the Sage Butter:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 8-10 fresh sage leaves
  • Salt to taste

For Garnish (optional):

  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

Step 1: Roasting the Butternut Squash

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the diced butternut squash on a baking sheet, drizzle with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the squash is tender and caramelized.

Step 2: Making the Butternut Squash Sauce

In a large saucepan, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute. Stir in the roasted butternut squash, ground nutmeg, and red pepper flakes. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to cook for 10-15 minutes, then remove from heat.

Step 3: Cooking the Pasta

While the butternut squash sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the cooking water.

Step 4: Preparing the Sage Butter

In a small saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Add the fresh sage leaves and cook until the butter turns golden brown and the sage leaves are crispy. Season with a pinch of salt.

Step 5: Bringing It All Together

Toss the cooked pasta with the butternut squash sauce, adding a little bit of the reserved cooking water if needed to loosen the sauce. Drizzle the sage butter over the pasta and gently toss to coat.

Step 6: Serving and Garnishing

Divide the Butternut Squash and Sage Pasta among serving plates. If desired, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper on top for an extra layer of flavor.

Conclusion

With its rich and velvety butternut squash sauce, fragrant sage butter, and perfectly cooked pasta, this dish is a true celebration of fall flavors. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or treating yourself to a cozy night in, this sophisticated Butternut Squash and Sage Pasta is sure to impress and satisfy. So, embrace the season and indulge in this culinary masterpiece that captures the essence of autumn in every bite.
