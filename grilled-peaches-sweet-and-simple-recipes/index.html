Grilled Peaches: Sweet and Simple Recipes


Who can resist the sweet and juicy goodness of grilled peaches? These summertime treats are not only delicious but also incredibly versatile. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy dessert or a unique twist to your savory dish, grilled peaches are the way to go. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering recipes that showcase the natural sweetness of peaches and take your summer grilling to a whole new level.

Grilled Peach Salad with Feta and Arugula

This refreshing salad combines the smoky flavor of grilled peaches with the peppery taste of arugula and the tanginess of feta cheese. It’s a perfect balance of flavors that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • 4 cups arugula
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the peach halves with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  3. Place the peaches on the grill, cut side down, and cook for about 3-4 minutes or until grill marks appear.
  4. Flip the peaches and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove the peaches from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  6. In a large bowl, combine the arugula, feta cheese, and chopped mint.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  9. Slice the grilled peaches and arrange them on top of the salad.
  10. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Pizza

If you’re a fan of sweet and savory flavor combinations, this pizza recipe is a must-try. The sweetness of the grilled peaches pairs perfectly with the salty prosciutto, creating a flavor explosion in every bite. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 2 ripe peaches, thinly sliced
  • 4 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, torn into small pieces
  • 4 slices prosciutto, torn into smaller pieces
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Brush one side of the dough with olive oil and place it on the grill, oiled side down.
  4. Cook for about 2-3 minutes or until the bottom of the dough is golden brown and has grill marks.
  5. Flip the dough and quickly arrange the sliced peaches, mozzarella cheese, and prosciutto on top.
  6. Close the grill lid and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes or until the cheese has melted and the bottom of the crust is crispy.
  7. Remove the pizza from the grill and let it cool slightly.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil.
  9. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy the delicious combination of flavors!

Grilled Peach and Honey Yogurt Parfait

If you’re in the mood for something sweet and indulgent, this grilled peach and honey yogurt parfait is the perfect dessert. It’s a simple yet elegant dish that will impress your guests and satisfy your sweet tooth. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 4 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the peach halves with olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of salt.
  3. Place the peaches on the grill, cut side down, and cook for about 3-4 minutes or until grill marks appear.
  4. Flip the peaches and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove the peaches from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  6. In serving glasses or bowls, layer Greek yogurt, grilled peaches, honey, and granola.
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a drizzle of honey and a sprinkle of granola on top.
  8. Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.
  9. Serve immediately and indulge in this delightful and healthy dessert.

Grilled peaches are a summer delight that can be enjoyed in various ways. Whether you prefer them in a salad, on a pizza, or in a dessert, these recipes are guaranteed to satisfy your cravings and impress your friends and family. So fire up the grill, grab some fresh peaches, and get ready to experience the sweet and simple pleasure of grilled peaches. Happy grilling!
