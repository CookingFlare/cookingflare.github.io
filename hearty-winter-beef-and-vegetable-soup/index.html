Hearty Winter Beef and Vegetable Soup


As the cold winter days approach, there’s nothing quite as satisfying as a warm, hearty bowl of beef and vegetable soup. This classic dish is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients and flavors that will warm you from the inside out. In this blog post, I’ll share with you my favorite recipe for Hearty Winter Beef and Vegetable Soup, along with some tips and tricks to make it even more delightful.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef stew meat, cut into small cubes
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup of green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups of beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef stew meat and cook until browned on all sides. This will take about 5-7 minutes.

  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion is soft and translucent.

  3. Stir in the carrots, celery, potatoes, and green beans. Cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the vegetables to slightly soften.

  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes and beef broth. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the beef is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.

  5. Add the dried thyme and rosemary to the pot, then season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

  6. Once the soup is ready, ladle it into bowls and serve hot. You can garnish with some fresh parsley for an extra touch of flavor.

Tips and Variations:

  • To add more depth of flavor, consider browning the beef in batches before cooking the onion and garlic.
  • For a heartier texture, you can add barley or quinoa to the soup.
  • If you prefer a thicker consistency, mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a little water and stir it into the soup during the last few minutes of cooking.

Conclusion:

This Hearty Winter Beef and Vegetable Soup is a true winter comfort food that will warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds. It’s a versatile dish that you can customize to your liking, and it’s perfect for batch cooking and freezing for later use. Whether you’re hosting a family dinner or simply craving a cozy meal, this soup is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients, prepare your pot, and let the aroma of this delicious soup fill your kitchen on those chilly winter days. Enjoy!

With its rich flavors and comforting warmth, this Hearty Winter Beef and Vegetable Soup is a must-try for anyone looking to enjoy a nourishing and satisfying meal during the colder months. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a bowl of homemade goodness, this recipe is sure to hit the spot.
