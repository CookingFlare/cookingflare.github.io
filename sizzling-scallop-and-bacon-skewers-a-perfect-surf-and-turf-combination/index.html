Sizzling Scallop and Bacon Skewers: A Perfect Surf and Turf Combination


Who doesn’t love a perfect surf and turf combination? The marriage of succulent seafood and flavorful meat is a match made in heaven. In this recipe, we take it up a notch by combining plump scallops with crispy bacon to create sizzling skewers that will tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

To make these delectable scallop and bacon skewers, you will need:

  • Fresh sea scallops (about 12 to 16 pieces)
  • Thick-cut bacon slices (about 8 to 10 slices)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon wedges, for serving
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you don’t have a grill, you can also use a grill pan on the stovetop.

  2. Rinse the scallops under cold water and pat them dry using a paper towel. This will ensure that they cook evenly and develop a nice sear.

  3. Cut each bacon slice in half, creating shorter strips that are easier to handle. Wrap each scallop with a slice of bacon and secure it with a toothpick. Repeat this process until all the scallops are wrapped.

  4. Drizzle a bit of olive oil over the skewers and season them with salt and pepper. This will enhance the flavors and help the bacon crisp up nicely.

  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill or grill pan. Cook for about 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until the bacon is crispy and the scallops are opaque and firm to the touch. Be careful not to overcook them, as they can become tough and rubbery.

  6. Once cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a couple of minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure maximum flavor.

  7. Serve the sizzling scallop and bacon skewers on a platter, garnished with fresh parsley and accompanied by lemon wedges for squeezing. The acidity of the lemon will cut through the richness of the dish and provide a refreshing contrast.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, try marinating the scallops in a mixture of olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and herbs before wrapping them in bacon. This will infuse them with additional aromas and make them even more irresistible.

  • If you prefer a smoky flavor, you can also use smoked bacon instead of regular bacon. The smokiness will add depth to the dish and complement the sweetness of the scallops.

  • Feel free to get creative with your skewers by adding other ingredients like cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, or even pineapple chunks. The combination of sweet and savory will take these skewers to a whole new level.

  • If you’re serving these as an appetizer or party food, consider making smaller bite-sized skewers. They will be easier to handle and perfect for sharing.

  • Don’t forget to soak your wooden skewers in water for about 30 minutes before using them. This will prevent them from burning on the grill.

Conclusion

These sizzling scallop and bacon skewers are the epitome of a perfect surf and turf combination. The tender scallops and crispy bacon create a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a special treat, these skewers are sure to impress. So fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in a mouthwatering dish that brings together the best of both land and sea.
