Delicious Dishes: Cooking with Fresh Mint


Mint is an aromatic herb known for its refreshing and vibrant flavor. While commonly used as a garnish or in beverages, this versatile herb can also take center stage in your cooking. In this blog post, we will explore the delightful world of cooking with fresh mint and discover how it can elevate your dishes to new heights. So, let’s dive right in!

Minty Starters

1. Minty Pea Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen peas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the peas and vegetable broth, then bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for about 10 minutes, or until the peas are tender.
  4. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  6. Stir in the chopped mint leaves and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with a sprig of fresh mint.

2. Watermelon Mint Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups diced watermelon
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, torn
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced watermelon, feta cheese, and torn mint leaves.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the watermelon mixture and gently toss to combine.
  4. Let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy the burst of sweetness and freshness in every bite.

Minty Mains

1. Grilled Lamb Chops with Mint Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chopped mint leaves, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the lamb chops with the mint sauce on both sides.
  4. Place the chops on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  5. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  6. Serve the grilled lamb chops with a drizzle of mint sauce on top and enjoy the tender and flavorful meat.

2. Minty Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, diced cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, and chopped mint leaves.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss to combine.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve chilled and savor the refreshing combination of flavors in every forkful.

Minty Desserts

1. Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the heavy cream, whole milk, and granulated sugar over medium heat until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract, peppermint extract, and chopped mint leaves.
  3. Cover and let it steep for about 30 minutes.
  4. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the mint leaves.
  5. Refrigerate the mixture until completely chilled.
  6. Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  7. During the last few minutes of churning, add the chocolate chips.
  8. Transfer the ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for a few hours to firm up.
  9. Scoop and serve the creamy and refreshing mint chocolate chip ice cream.

2. Minty Lemon Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, softened butter, and powdered sugar. Mix until crumbly.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking dish and bake for 15 minutes, or until lightly golden.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, granulated sugar, all-purpose flour, lemon juice, and chopped mint leaves.
  5. Pour the mixture over the baked crust and return to the oven.
  6. Bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the filling is set.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.
  8. Dust with powdered sugar and cut into bars.
  9. Indulge in the zesty and minty goodness of these delightful lemon bars.

Conclusion

Fresh mint is a wonderful herb that can transform your dishes with its vibrant and refreshing flavor. From starters to mains, and even desserts, there are countless ways to incorporate mint into your cooking. So, don’t hesitate to experiment and let your taste buds be delighted by the magic of fresh mint. Enjoy the burst of flavor and elevate your dishes to a whole new level with this versatile herb. Happy cooking!
