Perfectly Spiced Chai Snickerdoodle Cookie Recipe


If you’re a fan of cozy flavors and comforting treats, then this Perfectly Spiced Chai Snickerdoodle Cookie recipe is just what you need. With a delightful blend of chai spices and the classic snickerdoodle texture, these cookies are the perfect indulgence for any occasion. Get ready to experience a burst of warm flavors and a hint of nostalgia with every bite!

Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Chai Spice Blend

  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This process can be easily achieved using an electric mixer on medium speed for about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as it can result in a tougher cookie texture. The dough should be soft and slightly sticky.

  6. In a small bowl, combine the chai spice blend ingredients: ground cinnamon, ground ginger, ground cardamom, ground cloves, and ground nutmeg. Mix well.

  7. Shape the cookie dough into 1-inch balls and roll each ball in the chai spice blend until fully coated. Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Feel free to flatten the dough balls slightly with the bottom of a glass for a flatter cookie.

  8. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may appear slightly soft, but they will firm up as the cookies cool.

  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once cooled, store the cookies in an airtight container to maintain their freshness. They can be enjoyed for up to a week, if they last that long!

Serving Suggestions

These Perfectly Spiced Chai Snickerdoodle Cookies pair wonderfully with a hot cup of chai tea or a steaming mug of coffee. They are also a delightful treat to share with friends and family during cozy gatherings or as a homemade gift for special occasions.

So, go ahead and indulge in the comforting flavors of chai spice and the classic snickerdoodle cookie. This recipe is a surefire way to satisfy your sweet tooth while adding a touch of warmth and nostalgia to your day. Happy baking!

Note: Remember to adjust the baking time accordingly if you prefer your cookies softer or crisper.
