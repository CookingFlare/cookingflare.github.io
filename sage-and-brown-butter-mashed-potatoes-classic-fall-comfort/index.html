Sage and Brown Butter Mashed Potatoes: Classic Fall Comfort


As the leaves start to change color and the air becomes crisp, there’s nothing quite like indulging in comforting, hearty dishes that warm the soul. One such dish that perfectly encapsulates the essence of fall is Sage and Brown Butter Mashed Potatoes. This classic side dish is elevated with the earthy aroma of sage and the nutty richness of brown butter, making it a perfect addition to any autumn meal. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply craving a cozy night in, these mashed potatoes are sure to become a beloved staple in your fall recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup fresh sage leaves, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the Potatoes: Place the potato chunks in a large pot and cover them with cold water. Add a generous pinch of salt to the water. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat and let the potatoes simmer until they are fork-tender, about 15-20 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Brown Butter: While the potatoes are cooking, melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Once the butter has melted, continue to cook it, swirling the pan occasionally, until it turns a deep golden brown and gives off a nutty aroma. Be careful not to let it burn. Add the chopped sage to the browned butter and let it sizzle for a minute or two, infusing the butter with the fragrant herb.

  3. Mash the Potatoes: Once the potatoes are tender, drain them and return them to the pot. Using a potato masher or a ricer, mash the potatoes until they are smooth and free of lumps. Pour the brown butter and sage mixture over the mashed potatoes, and then add the milk. Continue to mash and mix until the ingredients are fully incorporated. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the mashed potatoes to a serving bowl and garnish with a few extra sage leaves for a pop of color. These Sage and Brown Butter Mashed Potatoes are the perfect complement to roasted meats, hearty stews, or even enjoyed on their own as a comforting dish.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra indulgent touch, consider adding a splash of heavy cream along with the milk for an even creamier texture.
  • If you prefer a smoother consistency, use a hand mixer to blend the potatoes and butter together.
  • Don’t skimp on the sage – its aromatic flavor is what sets these mashed potatoes apart from the ordinary.

Embracing Fall Flavors

The combination of fragrant sage and rich brown butter brings a depth of flavor to these mashed potatoes that is undeniably autumnal. The warm, comforting notes of this dish make it a perfect addition to any fall menu, whether you’re hosting a festive gathering or simply craving a taste of the season. Serve these Sage and Brown Butter Mashed Potatoes alongside your favorite fall dishes and savor the cozy comfort they bring with each spoonful.

From the creamy texture to the aromatic infusion of sage and brown butter, these mashed potatoes are a celebration of classic fall flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just beginning your culinary journey, this recipe is a wonderful way to embrace the joys of the autumn season through the delights of delicious, homemade comfort food.
