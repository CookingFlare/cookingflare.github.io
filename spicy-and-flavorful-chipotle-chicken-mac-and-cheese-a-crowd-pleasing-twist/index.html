Spicy and Flavorful Chipotle Chicken Mac and Cheese: A Crowd-Pleasing Twist


Are you tired of the same old mac and cheese recipe? Looking for a way to spice up your dinner routine? Well, look no further because we have the perfect recipe for you! Introducing our Spicy and Flavorful Chipotle Chicken Mac and Cheese – a crowd-pleasing twist on a classic comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of macaroni
  • 2 cups of shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of adobo sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Add the minced chipotle pepper and adobo sauce to the skillet. Stir well to combine with the onion and garlic.

  4. Sprinkle the flour over the mixture in the skillet and stir until all the ingredients are coated.

  5. Slowly pour in the milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Continue to cook and stir until the mixture thickens.

  6. Reduce the heat to low and add the shredded cheddar and pepper jack cheeses to the skillet. Stir until the cheeses have melted and the sauce is smooth.

  7. Add the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir to combine with the cheese sauce.

  8. Season the mixture with smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasonings according to your preference for spiciness.

  9. Add the cooked macaroni to the skillet and toss until the pasta is evenly coated with the cheese and chicken mixture.

  10. Serve the Spicy and Flavorful Chipotle Chicken Mac and Cheese hot, garnished with chopped fresh cilantro if desired.

Why You’ll Love It

This Spicy and Flavorful Chipotle Chicken Mac and Cheese recipe is the perfect combination of creamy, cheesy goodness and a kick of heat. The chipotle peppers add a smoky and spicy flavor that elevates the traditional mac and cheese to a whole new level. The shredded chicken adds protein and makes this dish a complete meal that will satisfy even the heartiest appetites.

Not only is this recipe delicious, but it’s also incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of prep work, you can have a crowd-pleasing twist on mac and cheese on the table in no time. It’s the perfect dish to serve at family gatherings, potlucks, or any time you want to impress your guests with a unique and flavorful comfort food.

So, why settle for plain old mac and cheese when you can take it up a notch with this Spicy and Flavorful Chipotle Chicken Mac and Cheese recipe? Give it a try and get ready to wow your taste buds and impress your loved ones with this delicious twist on a classic favorite. Enjoy!
