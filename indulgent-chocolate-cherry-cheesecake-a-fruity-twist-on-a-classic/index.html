Indulgent Chocolate Cherry Cheesecake: A Fruity Twist on a Classic


If you’re a fan of classic cheesecake with a fruity twist, then this indulgent chocolate cherry cheesecake recipe is about to become your new favorite dessert. The combination of rich chocolate, creamy cheesecake, and sweet cherries creates a decadent treat that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a luxurious dessert, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Filling:

  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup dark chocolate, melted

Topping:

  • 1 can cherry pie filling
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Fresh cherries for garnish (optional)

Instructions

1. Preparing the Crust

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • In a bowl, combine the chocolate cookie crumbs, sugar, and melted butter.
  • Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, creating an even layer.
  • Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool.

2. Making the Filling

  • In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth.
  • Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  • Stir in the vanilla extract, sour cream, flour, and cocoa powder until fully incorporated.
  • Gently fold in the melted dark chocolate until the filling is smooth and uniform.

3. Assembling the Cheesecake

  • Pour the filling over the cooled crust, spreading it evenly.
  • Tap the pan on the counter to remove any air bubbles.
  • Place the springform pan on a large piece of aluminum foil and wrap the foil around the bottom and sides to prevent any water from leaking into the pan during the baking process.

4. Baking the Cheesecake

  • Place the wrapped springform pan into a larger baking dish and fill the outer dish with hot water, creating a water bath for the cheesecake.
  • Bake the cheesecake in the water bath for 55-60 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center still jiggles slightly.
  • Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door slightly ajar for one hour.
  • Remove the cheesecake from the oven and run a knife around the edge of the pan to loosen the cheesecake. Allow it to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

5. Adding the Topping

  • Once the cheesecake is thoroughly chilled, carefully remove it from the springform pan.
  • Spread the cherry pie filling over the top of the cheesecake, creating an even layer of sweet cherries.
  • For an extra touch, add dollops of whipped cream and fresh cherries as garnish.

Enjoying the Indulgence

This indulgent chocolate cherry cheesecake is best enjoyed chilled, allowing the flavors to meld together for a truly heavenly experience. Each bite offers a delightful contrast of creamy cheesecake, rich chocolate, and sweet cherries, making it the perfect dessert for those who appreciate a fruity twist on a classic favorite.

Whether you’re serving it at a celebration or simply treating yourself to a luxurious dessert, this recipe is guaranteed to be a crowd-pleaser. Embrace the indulgence and savor every luscious bite of this delightful chocolate cherry cheesecake.
