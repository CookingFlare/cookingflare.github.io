Vegan Peanut Butter Cups: A Nutty and Chocolatey Delight


Who doesn’t love the combination of peanut butter and chocolate? It’s a match made in heaven, and when you make it vegan, it becomes even more heavenly. In this blog post, we’ll be sharing a delicious recipe for Vegan Peanut Butter Cups that are not only cruelty-free but also incredibly easy to make. So let’s dive right into it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of vegan chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Start by melting the chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl. You can do this by microwaving them in 30-second intervals, stirring in between until completely melted. Alternatively, you can melt the chocolate using a double boiler on the stovetop.

  2. Once the chocolate is melted, line a muffin tin with paper or silicone cupcake liners. Spoon about a tablespoon of melted chocolate into each liner, spreading it evenly to form the base of the peanut butter cups. Place the muffin tin in the freezer for about 10 minutes to allow the chocolate to set.

  3. While the chocolate is setting, prepare the peanut butter filling. In a separate bowl, combine the creamy peanut butter, coconut oil, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. Once the chocolate bases have set, remove the muffin tin from the freezer. Spoon about a teaspoon of the peanut butter filling onto each chocolate base, making sure to leave some space around the edges.

  5. After adding the peanut butter filling, cover it with another layer of melted chocolate. Use a spoon to carefully spread the chocolate over the peanut butter, ensuring that it covers the filling completely.

  6. Return the muffin tin to the freezer and let the peanut butter cups set for at least 1 hour, or until firm.

  7. Once the peanut butter cups are fully set, remove them from the muffin tin and store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator. They can be enjoyed immediately or kept for up to 2 weeks.

Serving Suggestions

Vegan Peanut Butter Cups are a versatile treat that can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to get your creative juices flowing:

  • Serve them as a dessert after a delicious vegan meal.
  • Chop them into small pieces and add them as a topping to your favorite vegan ice cream.
  • Use them as a filling for vegan cupcakes or brownies.
  • Gift them to your vegan friends and family for a sweet and thoughtful gesture.

Conclusion

These Vegan Peanut Butter Cups are the perfect treat for all the chocolate and peanut butter lovers out there. They are easy to make, require minimal ingredients, and are completely cruelty-free. So why not give them a try and indulge in a nutty and chocolatey delight? Trust me, your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, creativity is key in the kitchen, so feel free to experiment with different flavors and variations. Whether you choose to add a sprinkle of sea salt on top or mix in some crushed pretzels for a salty-sweet twist, the possibilities are endless.

Now it’s time to put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create some delicious Vegan Peanut Butter Cups. Happy cooking!
