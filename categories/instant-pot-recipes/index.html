Flavorful Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Soup Recipes

In this blog post, we will explore some delicious and flavorful Instant Pot chicken noodle soup recipes that are perfect for those cozy evenings or when you need a comforting bowl of soup. The Instant Pot is a versatile and convenient kitchen appliance that allows you to cook meals quickly and with minimal effort. Let’s dive into these mouthwatering recipes! Classic Chicken Noodle Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts 4 carrots, peeled and sliced 3 stalks celery, sliced 1 medium onion, chopped 4 cloves garlic, minced 8 cups chicken broth 2 cups egg noodles 1 teaspoon dried thyme Salt and pepper, to taste Fresh parsley, for garnish Instructions: Place the chicken breasts, carrots, celery, onion, and garlic in the Instant Pot. Pour in the chicken broth and season with dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes, followed by a quick release. Carefully remove the chicken breasts and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and add the egg noodles. Close the lid again and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 3 minutes, followed by a quick release. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley. Thai Coconut Curry Chicken Noodle Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs 1 can coconut milk 4 cups chicken broth 2 tablespoons red curry paste 2 tablespoons fish sauce 2 tablespoons lime juice 2 teaspoons brown sugar 4 ounces rice noodles 1 red bell pepper, sliced 1 cup snap peas Fresh cilantro, for garnish Lime wedges, for serving Instructions: Add the chicken thighs, coconut milk, chicken broth, red curry paste, fish sauce, lime juice, and brown sugar to the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes, followed by a natural release. Remove the chicken thighs from the Instant Pot and shred them. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and add the rice noodles, red bell pepper, and snap peas. Close the lid again and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 3 minutes, followed by a quick release. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and lime wedges. Lemon Chicken Orzo Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs 4 cups chicken broth 1 cup orzo pasta 1 lemon, zest and juice 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes Salt and pepper, to taste Fresh dill, for garnish Instructions: Place the chicken thighs, chicken broth, orzo pasta, lemon zest, lemon juice, dried oregano, dried thyme, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper in the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes, followed by a natural release. Remove the chicken thighs from the Instant Pot and shred them. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and stir well. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh dill. Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew Recipe: A Hearty and Flavorful Option

There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting stew to satisfy your cravings on a chilly day. And when it comes to convenience and flavor, it’s hard to beat the Instant Pot. This versatile kitchen appliance allows you to create delicious meals in a fraction of the time, and today, we have a recipe that will surely become a favorite in your household: Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew. Ingredients 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 large onions, thinly sliced 3 cloves garlic, minced 4 carrots, peeled and sliced 3 celery stalks, diced 1 pound potatoes, cubed 4 cups beef broth 1 cup red wine (optional) 2 tablespoons tomato paste 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon dried thyme 1 tablespoon dried rosemary Salt and pepper, to taste 2 tablespoons olive oil Instructions Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the olive oil and allow it to heat up. Once the oil is hot, add the beef stew meat and brown it on all sides. This step will help seal in the flavor and keep the meat tender. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and set it aside. Add the onions and garlic to the pot and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant. Next, add the carrots, celery, and potatoes to the pot, and give them a quick stir to combine everything. Return the beef to the Instant Pot, along with any accumulated juices. Pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using), and stir in the tomato paste and Worcestershire sauce. Add the dried thyme and rosemary, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Give everything a good stir to ensure the flavors are well-distributed. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, making sure the valve is set to the sealing position. Select the “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 30 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes, and then carefully perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure. Give the stew a gentle stir, and if desired, you can thicken the sauce by selecting the “Sauté” function and simmering the stew for a few minutes until it reaches your desired consistency. Serve the Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. Pair it with crusty bread or fluffy mashed potatoes for a complete and satisfying meal.
