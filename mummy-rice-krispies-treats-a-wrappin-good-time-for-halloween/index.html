Mummy Rice Krispies Treats: A Wrappin' Good Time for Halloween


Halloween has to be one of the most anticipated days of the year, and one of the most festive ways to celebrate is by creating spooky and fun treats with loved ones. This season, inspire some frightful fun with a delightful twist on a classic recipe for Mummy Rice Krispies Treats. Make a lasting impression on your little goblins and ghouls with these ooey-gooey wrapped morsels, dusted in powdered sugar to resemble bandaged mummies. Prepare yourself for a wrappin’ good time.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal
  • 4 cups mini marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Green food coloring
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • Wax paper or parchment paper

Instructions

  1. First, line a baking sheet with wax or parchment paper and set it aside.

  2. In a large pot, melt the unsalted butter over low heat, followed by the mini marshmallows. Stir constantly until the marshmallows are fully melted and create a smooth, gooey mixture. Be cautious not to burn the mixture.

  3. Add the ground cinnamon and vanilla extract to the melted marshmallow mixture, stirring until combined.

  4. Divide the Rice Krispies cereal into two large mixing bowls.

  5. Add green food coloring to one of the bowls and mix until the cereal is evenly coated in a vibrant green color. This will be used as the mummy’s bandages.

  6. Grab a spoonful of the green Rice Krispies mixture and form it into a oval shape, slightly larger than a golf ball. Lay it flat on the prepared baking sheet.

  7. Grab a spoonful of the regular Rice Krispies mixture and form it into a slightly smaller oval shape, placing it in the center of the green cereal.

  8. Gently press the two halves together, shaping them into the body of the mummy.

  9. Repeat the process for the remaining of the mixture, spacing them evenly apart on the baking sheet.

  10. Let the Mummy Rice Krispies Treats chill and harden for at least an hour.

  11. Once the treats have hardened, prepare the powdered sugar coating. In a shallow dish, mix together the powdered sugar and a few drops of green food coloring, if desired.

  12. One by one, gently roll the chilled treats in the powdered sugar, ensuring they’re evenly coated in a faux bandage pattern.

  13. Lastly, use twisted pieces of mini licorice to create twisted bandages for a more realistic mummy look. Place two licorice strands across the top of each mummy and continue weaving them back and forth until all the Rice Krispies Treats are complete.

  14. Serve and enjoy these adorable Mummy Rice Krispies Treats with your friends, family or to treat ghouls at a Halloween gathering.

These Mummy Rice Krispies Treats make the perfect bite-sized snack, packed with the warm flavors of fall and decked out with a hauntingly adorable exterior. Scare up some festive fun, and get your hands on all the gooey goodness by working your mummy magic in the kitchen this Halloween season. Happy haunting!
