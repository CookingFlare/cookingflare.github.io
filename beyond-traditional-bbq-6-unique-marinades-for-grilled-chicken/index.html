Beyond Traditional BBQ: 6 Unique Marinades for Grilled Chicken


Are you tired of the same old barbecue sauce for your grilled chicken? It’s time to think beyond the traditional and explore the world of unique marinades that will take your grilled chicken to the next level. In this post, we will share six mouthwatering marinade recipes that will leave your taste buds begging for more. From zesty citrus to savory Asian-inspired flavors, these marinades are sure to impress your family and friends at your next cookout.

1. Tangy Lemon Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, thyme, and rosemary.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over it.
  4. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  5. Grill the chicken over medium heat until cooked through, about 6-8 minutes per side.

The tangy lemon juice combined with the aromatic herbs creates a refreshing and flavorful marinade that pairs perfectly with grilled chicken.

2. Spicy Honey Lime Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to your heat preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, lime juice, soy sauce, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it, making sure to coat each piece evenly.
  3. Let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the chicken for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

The combination of sweet honey, tangy lime, and spicy chili creates a tantalizing marinade that adds a kick to your grilled chicken.

3. Garlic and Herb Yogurt Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, minced garlic, parsley, dill, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken to the bowl and toss until each piece is coated with the marinade.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for a stronger flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  5. Grill the chicken for about 8-10 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

The creamy yogurt combined with the garlic and herbs creates a flavorful and tenderizing marinade that will make your grilled chicken irresistibly delicious.

4. Teriyaki Pineapple Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, pineapple juice, brown sugar, minced garlic, grated ginger, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  2. Pour the marinade over the chicken in a resealable plastic bag.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for a stronger flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

The sweet and tangy flavors of pineapple combined with the umami of teriyaki create a tropical twist on traditional grilled chicken.

5. Moroccan Spice Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it, making sure to coat each piece evenly.
  3. Let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

The warm and aromatic spices of Morocco infuse the chicken with a rich and exotic flavor that will transport you to the bustling streets of Marrakech.

6. Maple Mustard Glaze Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, maple syrup, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over it.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  5. Grill the chicken for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

The combination of tangy mustard, sweet maple syrup, and smoky paprika creates a glaze that caramelizes beautifully on the grill, giving the chicken a mouthwatering flavor.

Conclusion

Don’t limit yourself to traditional barbecue sauce when it comes to grilling chicken. These unique marinades will elevate your grilled chicken game and impress your guests with their distinctive flavors. From the tangy lemon herb marinade to the spicy honey lime marinade, there’s a marinade for every palate. So fire up the grill, get creative, and enjoy the deliciousness of these beyond traditional BBQ marinades for grilled chicken.
