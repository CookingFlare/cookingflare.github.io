Spicy Instant Pot Jambalaya Recipe for a Taste of New Orleans


If you’re craving a taste of the vibrant and spicy flavors of New Orleans, look no further than this Spicy Instant Pot Jambalaya recipe. This iconic Creole dish is a celebration of bold spices, tender meats, and a melody of vegetables, all cooked to perfection in one pot. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can enjoy this mouthwatering jambalaya in a fraction of the time it would traditionally take. So, let’s dive into the world of Louisiana cuisine and bring the spirit of the French Quarter into your kitchen.

Ingredients

To start your culinary journey to New Orleans, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of Andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of long-grain white rice
  • 1.5 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 teaspoons of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Sear the Meats

Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and heat the olive oil. Add the sliced Andouille sausage and cook until browned. Remove the sausage and set it aside. Next, add the diced chicken thighs to the Instant Pot and cook until they are lightly browned. Remove the chicken and set it aside with the sausage.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

In the same pot, add the chopped onion, bell pepper, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté the vegetables until they are softened and aromatic, stirring occasionally.

Step 3: Add the Remaining Ingredients

Once the vegetables are tender, stir in the diced tomatoes, long-grain rice, chicken broth, Cajun seasoning, dried thyme, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Return the cooked sausage and chicken to the pot, and give everything a good stir to combine the flavors.

Step 4: Pressure Cook

Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then carefully release any remaining pressure.

Step 5: Serve and Garnish

Open the lid of the Instant Pot and give the jambalaya a gentle stir. The rice should be tender, and the meats and vegetables should be beautifully incorporated. Spoon the jambalaya into serving bowls and garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and a hint of freshness.

Enjoy the Flavors of New Orleans

Serve this Spicy Instant Pot Jambalaya with a side of cornbread or a fresh green salad for a complete and satisfying meal. The smoky and spicy flavors combined with the heartiness of the meats and rice will transport your taste buds to the heart of the Big Easy. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a comforting and flavorful dinner, this jambalaya is sure to impress.

So, embrace the spirit of New Orleans in your own kitchen with this Spicy Instant Pot Jambalaya recipe, and savor every bite of this classic Creole dish. Let the tantalizing aromas and robust flavors whisk you away to the bustling streets and lively music of the French Quarter, all from the comfort of your home.
