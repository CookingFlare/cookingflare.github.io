Savory Garlic Ginger Beef Stir Fry


Are you looking to add a burst of flavor to your weeknight dinner routine? Look no further than this delicious and savory garlic ginger beef stir fry recipe! Packed with bold Asian-inspired flavors, tender beef, and crunchy vegetables, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Plus, it’s quick and easy to make, perfect for those busy evenings when you want a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of thinly sliced beef (such as flank steak or sirloin)
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, and cornstarch. Add the thinly sliced beef to the marinade, making sure it’s well coated. Let it marinate for at least 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a separate bowl, mix the beef broth with a tablespoon of cornstarch to create a slurry. This will help thicken the sauce later.

  3. Stir Fry: Heat a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl to coat the pan. Once hot, add the marinated beef (reserving any excess marinade) and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the pan and set aside.

  4. Cook the Vegetables: In the same pan, add the sliced bell peppers and onion. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften but still have a bit of crunch.

  5. Combine: Return the cooked beef to the pan with the vegetables. Pour in the remaining marinade and the beef broth slurry. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Finish and Serve: Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened, and the beef and vegetables are cooked through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Serve: Serve the savory garlic ginger beef stir fry hot over a bed of steamed rice. Garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor and enjoy!

Tips:

  • Slice the beef thinly: This ensures that the beef cooks quickly and remains tender.
  • Customize your veggies: Feel free to add your favorite vegetables like broccoli, snap peas, or mushrooms to make this dish your own.
  • Adjust the sauce: If you prefer a sweeter or spicier stir fry, play around with the ingredients like adding a dash of honey or chili sauce to suit your taste.

With its savory garlic, zesty ginger, and tender beef, this stir fry is a complete meal that satisfies your cravings for Asian-inspired flavors. It’s a versatile dish that you can easily customize to suit your preferences. So, next time you’re craving a quick and flavorful meal, give this garlic ginger beef stir fry a try!
