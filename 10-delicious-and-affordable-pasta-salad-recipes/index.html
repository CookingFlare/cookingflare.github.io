10 Delicious and Affordable Pasta Salad Recipes


Pasta salads are a delightful side dish or an easy main course that can fit any occasion. From backyard barbecues to weeknight meals, they offer a perfect blend of flavors in every bite. The best part? You don’t need to spend a fortune to whip up a flavorful pasta salad. Whether you’re looking for something light and zesty or hearty and rich, these ten recipes deliver delicious options that are both affordable and satisfying.

1. Classic Italian Pasta Salad

This vibrant dish is a crowd-pleaser with its colorful array of ingredients and bold flavor profile. It’s also highly adaptable to what you have in your pantry.

Ingredients:

  • Rotini pasta
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Black olives, sliced
  • Cucumber, diced
  • Red onion, finely chopped
  • Mozzarella cheese cubes
  • Pepperoni slices (optional)
  • Italian dressing

Cook the pasta until it’s al dente, then rinse under cold water. Combine the vegetables, cheese, and pasta in a large bowl. Drizzle with Italian dressing and toss until everything is evenly coated. Refrigerate for at least an hour to let the flavors meld together.

2. Creamy Dill Pasta Salad

This creamy and tangy pasta salad is a perfect choice for dill lovers. It’s refreshing, delicious, and surprisingly light on the budget.

Ingredients:

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Mayonnaise
  • Greek yogurt
  • Fresh dill, chopped
  • Lemon juice
  • Pickles, diced
  • Red bell pepper, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Whisk together mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, lemon juice, and fresh dill in a bowl. Cook the macaroni, rinse it with cold water, and combine it with the dressing, pickles, and red bell pepper. Season with salt and pepper, and let it chill before serving.

3. Mediterranean Chickpea Pasta Salad

Packed with wholesome ingredients, this Mediterranean-inspired recipe is protein-rich and incredibly tasty.

Ingredients:

  • Penne pasta
  • Canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • Kalamata olives, halved
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Dried oregano

Cook the penne, toss with the chickpeas, vegetables, and feta. Drizzle olive oil and fresh lemon juice over the salad, then sprinkle oregano. It’s a tangy, filling dish that’s perfect as a light lunch or dinner.

4. Southwest Pasta Salad

Bring the flavors of the Southwest to your table with this zesty pasta salad that’s perfect for spice lovers.

Ingredients:

  • Bowtie pasta
  • Canned black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Corn kernels
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Diced avocado (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime juice
  • Ranch dressing
  • Taco seasoning

Cook the bowtie pasta, rinse with cold water, and mix it with beans, corn, cheese, cilantro, and ranch dressing combined with a dash of taco seasoning. Add diced avocado just before serving for extra creaminess.

5. Veggie-Packed Garden Salad

This recipe is a great way to use up seasonal vegetables without breaking the bank.

Ingredients:

  • Fusilli pasta
  • Zucchini, diced
  • Broccoli florets
  • Carrots, shredded
  • Red onion, diced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder

Cook the pasta, blanch the broccoli, and combine all the vegetables in a bowl. Whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, and garlic powder, and toss with the pasta and vegetables. This salad is healthy, light, and perfect for meal prep.

6. Tuna and Lemon Pasta Salad

Canned tuna is one of the most affordable sources of protein, and adding it to pasta salad creates a delightful, hearty meal.

Ingredients:

  • Shell pasta
  • Canned tuna, drained
  • Lemon zest and juice
  • Capers
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Cook the pasta and mix it with the tuna, parsley, capers, olive oil, and a squeeze of lemon. The tangy flavors pair beautifully with the tuna, creating a simple but satisfying dish.

7. Pesto Pasta Salad

Pesto adds such a rich and aromatic flavor, transforming any pasta into a delicious dish.

Ingredients:

  • Rotini pasta
  • Store-bought basil pesto (or homemade)
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh spinach, chopped
  • Parmesan cheese, grated

After cooking the pasta, combine it with the pesto while it’s still warm so it absorbs the flavors. Add the spinach, tomatoes, and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese. Serve warm or chilled for two different flavor experiences.

8. Greek-Inspired Orzo Salad

This refreshing salad features orzo pasta and classic Greek flavors, perfect for summer picnics.

Ingredients:

  • Orzo pasta
  • Cucumber, diced
  • Kalamata olives, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Red onion, diced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Fresh oregano
  • Olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar

Cook the orzo and rinse it under cold water. Mix with the vegetables and feta. Whisk together olive oil, oregano, and red wine vinegar, then toss it all together for a quick and healthy dish.

9. BBQ Ranch Chicken Pasta Salad

For a heartier option, this BBQ ranch chicken pasta salad combines smoky, tangy, and creamy flavors kids and adults alike will love.

Ingredients:

  • Penne pasta
  • Shredded rotisserie chicken
  • Corn kernels
  • Black beans
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Ranch dressing
  • BBQ sauce

Cook the penne, then mix it with shredded chicken, beans, corn, and cheese. Blend equal parts BBQ sauce and ranch dressing, and toss everything together for a filling, flavorful salad packed with proteins.

10. Caprese Pasta Salad

Bring the classic Caprese salad flavors to your pasta bowl with this light and elegant recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Bowtie pasta
  • Fresh mozzarella balls
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil

Cook the pasta and let it cool. Combine the mozzarella balls, cherry tomatoes, and pasta, then drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze. Garnish with fresh basil for a beautiful presentation that tastes just as good as it looks.

With these ten affordable pasta salad recipes, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Whether you’re looking for a quick meal, a side dish for a party, or an easy potluck option, these salads are sure to please the palate without stretching your budget.
