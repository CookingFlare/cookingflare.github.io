Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta: A Taste of the Mediterranean


If you’re looking for a dish that embodies the fresh and vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean, look no further than Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta. This recipe combines the succulent taste of shrimp with the zesty tang of lemon and the aromatic allure of garlic, all brought together with al dente pasta. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply craving a taste of the seaside, this dish is sure to transport your taste buds to the shores of the Mediterranean.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of linguine or spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Shrimp

Begin by seasoning the shrimp with salt and black pepper. In a large skillet over medium heat, add 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

Step 2: Cook the Pasta

In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it reaches al dente perfection. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

Step 3: Create the Lemon Garlic Sauce

Using the same skillet used for the shrimp, add the remaining olive oil and minced garlic. Sauté the garlic until it becomes fragrant, being careful not to let it brown. Add the red pepper flakes, lemon zest, and lemon juice to the skillet. Let the sauce simmer for a couple of minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 4: Bring It All Together

Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the lemon garlic sauce, tossing it gently to coat the pasta evenly. Gently fold in the cooked shrimp, ensuring that they are coated with the delightful lemony sauce.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

Once the shrimp and pasta are combined, sprinkle the dish with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of color and herbaceous flavor. Serve the Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta in individual plates or a large serving dish, and enjoy the taste of the Mediterranean right at your table.

Pairing Suggestion:

To elevate the experience, pair this delectable Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta with a crisp white wine such as Pinot Grigio or Sauvignon Blanc. The bright acidity of the wine will complement the citrusy notes in the dish, creating a harmonious culinary symphony on your palate.

Final Thoughts:

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta is a celebration of Mediterranean flavors that can transport you to the sun-drenched coastlines with every bite. The succulent shrimp, vibrant lemon, and aromatic garlic come together to create a dish that is both comforting and invigorating. Whether you’re enjoying a cozy dinner at home or hosting a gathering with loved ones, this recipe is a delightful way to savor the essence of the Mediterranean.

So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of the Mediterranean, and embark on a culinary journey with this Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta that promises to tantalize your senses and delight your guests. Buon appetito!
