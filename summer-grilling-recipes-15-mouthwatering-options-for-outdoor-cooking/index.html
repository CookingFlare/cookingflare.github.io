Summer Grilling Recipes: 15 Mouthwatering Options for Outdoor Cooking


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy the great outdoors with friends and family. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or just looking to elevate your weeknight dinners, grilling offers a delicious and healthy way to prepare food. From succulent meats to vibrant veggies, the options for outdoor cooking are endless. To help you make the most of the season, we’ve curated 15 mouthwatering grilling recipes that are sure to impress your guests and tantalize your taste buds.

1. Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken

Marinated in a zesty blend of lemon, garlic, and fresh herbs, this grilled chicken recipe is bursting with flavor. The charred, juicy chicken pairs perfectly with a side of grilled vegetables or a fresh summer salad.

2. Smoky BBQ Ribs

Achieving the perfect smoky flavor and tender texture, these BBQ ribs are a true crowd-pleaser. Slathered in a tangy barbecue sauce and slow-cooked on the grill, they’re an indulgent treat for any summer gathering.

3. Grilled Shrimp Skewers

Light and vibrant, these grilled shrimp skewers are a delightful appetizer or main course. The combination of succulent shrimp, colorful bell peppers, and zesty marinade is a feast for the senses.

4. Portobello Mushroom Burgers

For a vegetarian option that doesn’t skimp on taste, try these portobello mushroom burgers. Marinated in balsamic vinaigrette and grilled to perfection, they’re a hearty and satisfying alternative to traditional beef burgers.

5. Grilled Pineapple Salsa

Elevate your grilled dishes with a refreshing and tangy grilled pineapple salsa. This vibrant accompaniment adds a burst of tropical flavor to any meal and is the perfect topping for grilled fish or chicken.

6. Tandoori Grilled Lamb Chops

Infused with aromatic Indian spices, these tandoori grilled lamb chops are a culinary delight. The tender, flavorful meat is a standout dish for any summer feast.

7. Grilled Corn on the Cob with Chili Lime Butter

A classic summer staple, grilled corn on the cob is taken to the next level with a zesty chili lime butter. This crowd-pleasing side dish is a hit at any barbecue or picnic.

8. Teriyaki Glazed Grilled Salmon

Sweet, savory, and utterly delectable, this teriyaki glazed grilled salmon is a must-try for seafood lovers. The caramelized glaze adds a delightful twist to this summer grilling favorite.

9. Grilled Caesar Salad

Give your traditional Caesar salad a smoky twist by grilling the lettuce. The charred romaine adds a depth of flavor that perfectly complements the creamy dressing and crunchy croutons.

10. Grilled Vegetable Platter

A colorful array of grilled vegetables is a stunning addition to any outdoor gathering. From vibrant bell peppers to tender asparagus, this versatile dish celebrates the best of summer produce.

11. Hawaiian Grilled Chicken Skewers

Transport your taste buds to the tropics with these Hawaiian grilled chicken skewers. The sweet and tangy marinade combined with juicy chicken and fresh veggies is a delightful summer treat.

12. Grilled Stuffed Peppers

Packed with a flavorful mixture of rice, beans, and spices, these grilled stuffed peppers are a mouthwatering vegetarian option. The charred peppers add a smoky depth to the dish.

13. Grilled Flat Iron Steak with Chimichurri Sauce

Tender and full of flavor, grilled flat iron steak is complemented perfectly by the zesty, herbaceous chimichurri sauce. This dish is a true showstopper for any outdoor gathering.

14. Grilled Peach and Burrata Salad

Indulge in the sweet and savory combination of grilled peaches and creamy burrata cheese. Topped with a drizzle of balsamic glaze, this elegant salad is a delightful addition to any summer meal.

15. Grilled Banana S’mores

Put a creative spin on the classic s’mores by grilling bananas alongside the marshmallows and chocolate. The caramelized bananas add a delicious twist to this beloved dessert.

We hope these 15 mouthwatering summer grilling recipes inspire you to get creative with your outdoor cooking. Whether you’re a seasoned grill master or just starting out, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and savor the flavors of summer with these delectable recipes!
