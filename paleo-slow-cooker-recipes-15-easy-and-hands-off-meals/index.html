Paleo Slow Cooker Recipes: 15 Easy and Hands-Off Meals


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen preparing meals? Do you want to follow a Paleo diet but don’t have the time to cook elaborate meals? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share 15 easy and hands-off Paleo slow cooker recipes that will save you time and effort in the kitchen while still allowing you to enjoy delicious and nutritious meals.

1. Slow Cooker BBQ Pulled Pork

This mouthwatering recipe requires just a few simple ingredients: pork shoulder, barbecue sauce, and spices. Let the slow cooker work its magic for 8 hours on low heat, and you’ll have tender and flavorful pulled pork that pairs perfectly with cauliflower rice or lettuce wraps.

2. Paleo Beef Stew

Warm up on a cold winter’s day with this hearty beef stew. Simply combine beef, carrots, onions, garlic, and your favorite Paleo-friendly broth in the slow cooker. Let it simmer for 6-8 hours, and you’ll have a comforting and nourishing meal that will satisfy your cravings.

3. Lemon Herb Chicken

Infuse your chicken with the bright flavors of lemon and herbs. Place chicken breasts or thighs in the slow cooker, along with fresh herbs like thyme, rosemary, and parsley. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top, season with salt and pepper, and let it cook for 4-6 hours. The result? Tender and flavorful chicken that pairs well with roasted vegetables.

4. Slow Cooker Chili

Who doesn’t love a good bowl of chili? This recipe is packed with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, peppers, and a blend of spices. Let it simmer in the slow cooker for several hours, and you’ll have a warm and comforting meal that is perfect for any occasion.

5. Moroccan Lamb Stew

Transport yourself to exotic flavors with this Moroccan-inspired lamb stew. Combine lamb, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and cinnamon in the slow cooker. Let it cook on low heat for 6-8 hours, and you’ll be rewarded with a fragrant and tender stew that will tantalize your taste buds.

6. Creamy Paleo Chicken Tikka Masala

Craving Indian cuisine? This recipe is a healthier take on the classic Chicken Tikka Masala. Marinate chicken in a mixture of coconut milk, spices, and lemon juice, then let it cook in the slow cooker with a creamy tomato-based sauce. Serve it over cauliflower rice for a satisfying and flavorful meal.

7. Slow Cooker Balsamic Pot Roast

Tender pot roast infused with the tangy flavors of balsamic vinegar is a surefire way to please your taste buds. Combine beef roast, onions, garlic, carrots, and a balsamic vinegar-based sauce in the slow cooker. Let it cook on low heat for 8 hours, and you’ll have a melt-in-your-mouth pot roast that will impress your family and friends.

8. Paleo Chicken Fajitas

Enjoy the flavors of Tex-Mex cuisine with these Paleo-friendly chicken fajitas. Simply combine chicken, bell peppers, onions, and a blend of spices in the slow cooker. Let it cook for a few hours, and you’ll have tender and flavorful chicken that is perfect for stuffing into lettuce wraps or serving with cauliflower tortillas.

9. Slow Cooker Coconut Curry

Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of this slow cooker coconut curry. Combine chicken or beef, coconut milk, curry paste, and vegetables in the slow cooker. Let it simmer for several hours, and you’ll have a creamy and fragrant curry that pairs perfectly with cauliflower rice.

10. Paleo Pot Roast Soup

Turn your pot roast leftovers into a comforting and delicious soup. Simply combine shredded pot roast, beef broth, carrots, celery, and spices in the slow cooker. Let it simmer for a few hours, and you’ll have a hearty and flavorful soup that will warm you up on a chilly day.

11. Slow Cooker Pork Carnitas

Craving Mexican flavors? These slow cooker pork carnitas are a crowd-pleaser. Simply season pork shoulder with spices, onions, and garlic, then let it cook on low heat for 8 hours. Shred the meat and broil it for a few minutes to get crispy edges. Serve it in lettuce wraps or with cauliflower rice for a satisfying meal.

12. Paleo Beef and Broccoli

Skip the takeout and make your own Paleo-friendly beef and broccoli at home. Combine beef, broccoli florets, garlic, ginger, and a savory sauce in the slow cooker. Let it cook for a few hours, and you’ll have a flavorful and healthy version of this classic dish.

13. Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Salmon

Yes, you can even cook fish in the slow cooker! Place salmon fillets on a bed of sliced lemons and garlic, season with salt and pepper, and let it cook on low heat for 2-3 hours. The result? Moist and flavorful salmon that is perfect for a light and refreshing meal.

14. Paleo Buffalo Chicken Dip

Looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer? This Paleo buffalo chicken dip is a winner. Combine shredded chicken, hot sauce, coconut cream, and spices in the slow cooker. Let it cook for a few hours, and you’ll have a creamy and spicy dip that pairs well with vegetable sticks or Paleo-friendly crackers.

15. Slow Cooker Apple Cinnamon Pork Chops

End your meal on a sweet note with these apple cinnamon pork chops. Season pork chops with cinnamon and place them in the slow cooker with sliced apples and onions. Let it cook on low heat for 4-6 hours, and you’ll have tender and flavorful pork chops with a hint of sweetness.

There you have it – 15 easy and hands-off Paleo slow cooker recipes that will simplify your meal prep and allow you to enjoy delicious and nutritious meals without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re following a Paleo diet or simply looking for convenient and flavorful recipes, these dishes are sure to satisfy your cravings. Give them a try and let the slow cooker do the work for you!
