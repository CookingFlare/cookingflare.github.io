Chocolate Covered Cinnamon Bears: A Fun and Spicy Treat


If you’re searching for a delightful treat that marries the warmth of cinnamon with the indulgence of chocolate, look no further than chocolate covered cinnamon bears. This fun and spicy confection not only offers a unique flavor combination but also serves as a fantastic snack for parties, movie nights, or just a cozy evening at home. In this post, we’ll explore the history of cinnamon bears, the process of making them, and how you can elevate your chocolate covered cinnamon bears to gourmet status.

The Sweet Origins of Cinnamon Bears

Cinnamon bears have a rich history that dates back to the early 20th century. Originating in the United States, these gummy candies were inspired by the spicy warmth of cinnamon, a flavor that has been cherished for centuries. While traditional cinnamon bears are chewy and sweet, the addition of chocolate takes this beloved treat to a whole new level. The combination of the soft, spicy bear with a rich chocolate coating creates an explosion of flavors that is simply irresistible.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To create your own chocolate covered cinnamon bears, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cinnamon bears: These can be found at most candy stores or online. If you prefer, you can make your own using a gelatin-based recipe.
  • 1 cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips: You can also use milk or dark chocolate depending on your preference.
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut oil: This helps to thin the chocolate, making it easier to coat the bears.
  • Optional toppings: Consider using crushed nuts, sprinkles, or sea salt to add an extra layer of flavor and texture.

Preparing the Chocolate

Before you dive into the chocolate coating process, it’s important to properly melt your chocolate. Here’s how to do it safely:

  1. Double Boiler Method: Fill a saucepan with water and bring it to a simmer. Place a heatproof bowl on top, ensuring it doesn’t touch the water. Add your chocolate chips and coconut oil to the bowl. Stir continuously until completely melted and smooth.

  2. Microwave Method: Place your chocolate chips and coconut oil in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat in 30-second increments, stirring in between, until the chocolate is fully melted and smooth.

Regardless of the method you choose, be sure to monitor your chocolate closely to avoid burning it.

Coating the Cinnamon Bears

Once your chocolate is melted, it’s time to coat the cinnamon bears!

  1. Prepare Your Workspace: Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. This will prevent the chocolate covered bears from sticking and make cleanup easier.

  2. Coat the Bears: Using a fork or a toothpick, dip each cinnamon bear into the melted chocolate, ensuring it is fully coated. Allow any excess chocolate to drip off before placing it on the prepared baking sheet.

  3. Chill: Once all the bears are coated, place the baking sheet in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes or until the chocolate has hardened.

Elevating Your Chocolate Covered Cinnamon Bears

While chocolate covered cinnamon bears are delicious on their own, you can easily elevate them with a few simple additions. Here are some creative ideas to take your treats to the next level:

1. Spicy Chocolate

For those who love a bit of heat, consider adding cayenne pepper or chili powder to your melted chocolate. Start with a small pinch and adjust to your taste. The result will be a delightful spicy kick that complements the cinnamon flavor.

2. Flavor Infusions

Try infusing your chocolate with flavors such as vanilla extract, orange zest, or even espresso powder. Each of these additions will create a more complex flavor profile that enhances the overall experience.

3. Dipping Variations

Experiment with different types of chocolate! White chocolate, dark chocolate, or even flavored chocolates (like mint or raspberry) can create a unique twist on the classic chocolate covered cinnamon bears.

Serving Suggestions

Now that your chocolate covered cinnamon bears are ready, it’s time to enjoy them! Here are some serving suggestions:

  • Party Treats: Serve them in a decorative bowl at your next gathering. They make great conversation starters and are sure to impress your guests.

  • Gift Giving: Package your chocolate covered cinnamon bears in clear bags tied with a festive ribbon. They make fantastic gifts for friends, family, or coworkers.

  • Movie Night Snack: Pair them with popcorn for a fun movie night treat. The combination of crunchy and chewy textures will be a hit!

Storage Tips

To keep your chocolate covered cinnamon bears fresh, store them in an airtight container at room temperature. They can last for up to two weeks, but we doubt they’ll last that long once you start snacking!

If you live in a hot climate, consider refrigerating them to prevent melting. Just be sure to let them come to room temperature before serving for the best flavor and texture.

Conclusion

Chocolate covered cinnamon bears are a fun and spicy treat that everyone will love. They’re easy to make, versatile, and perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re making them for a party, a gift, or just for yourself, this delightful combination of flavors is sure to become a favorite. So why not whip up a batch today? You’ll be amazed at how quickly they disappear!

Final Thoughts

As you explore the world of chocolate covered cinnamon bears, let your creativity run wild. Each batch can be a new adventure in flavor and texture. Don’t hesitate to experiment with different ingredients and toppings to find your perfect combination. Happy snacking!
