Tasty Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup: Warm and Satisfying


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful soup that’s not only delicious but also fits into your keto lifestyle, then you’re in for a treat! This Tasty Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup is the perfect combination of warm, creamy, and satisfying, making it an ideal choice for a cozy night in or a hearty meal any time of the year. Packed with wholesome ingredients and rich flavors, this soup will surely become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to prepare this delectable Tasty Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup:

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: crispy bacon bits for topping

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Broccoli

Start by washing the broccoli florets thoroughly and cutting them into small, bite-sized pieces. Set the prepared broccoli aside for later use.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

In a large pot over medium heat, melt the butter and add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté the aromatics until they become fragrant and the onion turns translucent, stirring occasionally to prevent burning.

Step 3: Add the Broth and Broccoli

Once the aromatics are ready, pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and add the prepared broccoli to the pot. Allow the mixture to come to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the broccoli is tender.

Step 4: Blend the Soup

Once the broccoli is cooked to perfection, it’s time to blend the soup to achieve a smooth and creamy texture. You can use an immersion blender directly in the pot, or carefully transfer the soup in batches to a countertop blender to puree until smooth.

Step 5: Add the Cream and Cheese

Return the blended soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream. Let the soup simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Then, gradually add the shredded cheddar cheese while stirring continuously until the cheese is fully melted and the soup is smooth and creamy.

Step 6: Season and Serve

Finally, season the Tasty Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, top each serving with crispy bacon bits for an extra crunch and savory flavor. Ladle the warm soup into bowls, and enjoy the rich and satisfying goodness with your loved ones.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 cup
  • Calories: 280
  • Total Fat: 22g
  • Saturated Fat: 14g
  • Cholesterol: 75mg
  • Sodium: 600mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 9g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Sugars: 3g
  • Protein: 12g

Final Thoughts

This Tasty Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup is a wonderful way to indulge in a creamy and cheesy delight while staying true to your keto lifestyle. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or hosting a gathering, this soup is sure to impress even the most discerning palates. With its velvety texture and robust flavor, it’s a comforting dish that’s perfect for any occasion. So why not gather the ingredients and treat yourself to this warm and satisfying keto-friendly delight today?
