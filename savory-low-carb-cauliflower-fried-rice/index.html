Savory Low Carb Cauliflower Fried Rice


Are you looking for a healthy and delicious alternative to traditional fried rice? Look no further! Our Savory Low Carb Cauliflower Fried Rice is the perfect dish for those watching their carb intake, without compromising on flavor. Packed with fresh vegetables and protein, this recipe is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower, grated
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Begin by preparing the cauliflower. Remove the leaves and cut the head into florets. Using a food processor or a grater, grate the cauliflower until it resembles rice. Set aside.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for a few minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until they are fully cooked, then mix them with the vegetables.

  5. Add the grated cauliflower to the skillet and stir-fry for about 5 minutes, until it is tender but not mushy.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the low sodium soy sauce and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the cauliflower mixture and toss to coat evenly.

  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  8. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions.

  9. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize this recipe by adding your favorite protein, such as grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu. Simply cook the protein separately and add it to the fried rice at the end.

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add a teaspoon of grated ginger or a dash of sriracha sauce to the sauce mixture.

  • Feel free to experiment with different vegetables based on your preference or what you have on hand. Broccoli, bell peppers, and snap peas are all great additions.

  • This recipe can easily be doubled or tripled to feed a crowd. Just make sure to adjust the cooking times accordingly.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in a skillet or microwave before serving.

Why Choose Low Carb Cauliflower Fried Rice?

Low Carb Cauliflower Fried Rice is an excellent option for those following a low-carb or keto diet. By substituting cauliflower for rice, you significantly reduce the carb content of the dish while still enjoying a satisfying and flavorful meal. Cauliflower is also rich in vitamins C and K, as well as fiber, making it a nutritious choice for any diet.

Not only is this recipe low in carbs, but it is also packed with vegetables, adding essential vitamins and minerals to your meal. The combination of flavors from the sautéed onions, garlic, and soy sauce creates a savory and satisfying dish that will leave you coming back for seconds.

So why not give our Savory Low Carb Cauliflower Fried Rice a try? It’s a healthy twist on a classic dish that is sure to please both your taste buds and your waistline. Enjoy!
