Seafood Tacos Recipe with Grilled Shrimp and Salsa


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy the flavors of the ocean. Seafood tacos are a delicious and refreshing twist on traditional tacos, and with this recipe, you’ll be able to create mouth-watering dishes that will impress your friends and family. In this post, we’ll explore a seafood tacos recipe that features grilled shrimp, succulent and flavorful salsa, and a crunchy slaw made with red cabbage and cilantro.

Ingredients

For the grilled shrimp:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the salsa:

  • 1 cup diced fresh tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup diced fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the slaw:

  • 1 head red cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the tacos:

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • Sliced radishes, lime wedges, and cilantro leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Grill the Shrimp: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, garlic, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Brush the mixture evenly onto both sides of the shrimp. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they’re pink and cooked through.
  2. Prepare the Salsa: In a medium bowl, combine diced tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, and jalapeño pepper. Stir well to combine and adjust seasoning as needed.
  3. Make the Slaw: In a large bowl, combine thinly sliced red cabbage, chopped cilantro, and lime juice. Toss well to combine and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Assemble the Tacos: Warm the corn tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds. Assemble the tacos by placing a few grilled shrimp onto a warmed tortilla, followed by a spoonful of salsa, a sprinkle of slaw, and a sprinkle of cilantro.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy slaw, try adding a pinch of salt and a tablespoon of apple cider vinegar to the slaw mixture.
  • Experiment with different types of seafood, such as cod or scallops, for a unique flavor profile.
  • Add some heat to your tacos by incorporating diced jalapeños or serrano peppers into the salsa.
  • Serve with your favorite toppings, such as diced avocado, sour cream, or crumbled queso fresco.

Conclusion

Seafood tacos are a delicious and refreshing twist on traditional tacos, and with this recipe, you’ll be able to create mouth-watering dishes that will impress your friends and family. By combining grilled shrimp, succulent salsa, and crunchy slaw, you’ll have a flavor profile that’s both classic and innovative. So next time you’re looking for a new recipe to try, give this seafood tacos recipe a shot and enjoy the taste of the ocean in every bite!
