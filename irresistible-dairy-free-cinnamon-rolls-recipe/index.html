Irresistible Dairy-Free Cinnamon Rolls Recipe


Cinnamon rolls are a classic comfort food, loved by many for their soft, fluffy texture and deliciously sweet, spicy filling. Whether you’re starting your day with a cozy breakfast or indulging in an afternoon treat, these cinnamon rolls are the perfect choice. And if you’re following a dairy-free lifestyle or have a dairy intolerance, don’t worry – this recipe is made with ingredients that make it just as indulgent and tasty as the traditional version.

In this blog post, I’m going to walk you through the steps to create dairy-free cinnamon rolls that are warm, gooey, and irresistibly good. These cinnamon rolls are made with simple, wholesome ingredients, and the best part? They are easy to make, so you’ll have fresh, homemade cinnamon rolls ready to enjoy in no time. Let’s dive into this scrumptious recipe and make your kitchen smell like heaven!

Ingredients for Dairy-Free Cinnamon Rolls

Before we get started, gather all the ingredients for both the dough and the filling. You likely already have most of them in your kitchen.

For the Dough:

  • 1 cup warm dairy-free milk (such as almond milk, oat milk, or coconut milk)
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast (or one packet)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup coconut oil (melted) or your preferred dairy-free butter
  • ¼ cup maple syrup (or other sweeteners such as agave)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed (optional, for added texture and moisture)

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar or brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup dairy-free butter (such as coconut oil or margarine)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup

For the Dairy-Free Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar (or powdered coconut sugar for a refined-sugar-free option)
  • 2 tablespoons dairy-free milk (almond milk works great)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Now that you have your ingredients ready, let’s get started on making these irresistible dairy-free cinnamon rolls!

Step-by-Step Instructions for Making Dairy-Free Cinnamon Rolls

Step 1: Prepare the Yeast Mixture

Start by preparing the yeast mixture, which will help the dough rise and create that light, fluffy texture we all love in cinnamon rolls. In a small bowl, combine the warm dairy-free milk (make sure it’s not too hot or too cold, about 110°F/45°C is ideal), sugar, and active dry yeast. Stir to dissolve the yeast, then let the mixture sit for about 5-10 minutes. You’ll notice the yeast start to bubble and foam, signaling that it’s activated and ready to go.

Step 2: Make the Dough

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Once the yeast mixture is ready, pour it into the flour along with the melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract. If you’re using flaxseed, add it now. Mix the ingredients together until the dough starts to form.

Once the dough starts coming together, transfer it to a lightly floured surface and knead it for about 5-7 minutes. The dough should be smooth and elastic. If it feels too sticky, add a little more flour, a tablespoon at a time. Be careful not to add too much flour, or you’ll end up with dense rolls. When the dough is ready, shape it into a ball and place it back in the bowl.

Step 3: Let the Dough Rise

Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1 to 1.5 hours. The dough should double in size. If you’re in a hurry, you can preheat the oven to 200°F (93°C) for a couple of minutes, turn it off, and place the dough inside to rise.

Step 4: Roll Out the Dough

Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles, then transfer it to a lightly floured surface. Roll the dough out into a rectangle, about 16x12 inches (40x30 cm). Try to keep the edges straight to ensure even rolls.

Step 5: Prepare the Filling

In a small bowl, mix together the coconut sugar (or brown sugar) and ground cinnamon. Then, melt the dairy-free butter (or coconut oil) and drizzle it evenly over the rolled-out dough. Spread the melted butter over the entire surface of the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture generously over the butter.

Drizzle the maple syrup over the top of the sugar and cinnamon for an extra layer of flavor. Now, you’re ready to roll up your dough!

Step 6: Roll Up the Dough

Starting from the long edge of the rectangle, carefully roll up the dough tightly, making sure to keep the filling in place. When you reach the end, pinch the seam to seal it. Using a sharp knife or dental floss, slice the roll into 12 even pieces. These will be your individual cinnamon rolls.

Step 7: Arrange the Rolls and Let Them Rise

Place the sliced cinnamon rolls in a greased 9x13-inch baking pan, arranging them so they are close together but not touching. This will help them rise up instead of spreading out as they bake. Cover the pan with a clean kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for an additional 30-45 minutes. They should puff up and fill the pan.

Step 8: Bake the Cinnamon Rolls

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Once the rolls have risen, place the pan in the oven and bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until the cinnamon rolls are golden brown on top and cooked through. The edges should be slightly crispy, while the center remains soft and gooey.

While the rolls are baking, you can prepare the dairy-free glaze.

Step 9: Prepare the Dairy-Free Glaze

In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, dairy-free milk, and vanilla extract. If you prefer a thicker glaze, add more powdered sugar. If you’d like a thinner glaze, add a little more dairy-free milk.

Once the cinnamon rolls are out of the oven, let them cool for about 5 minutes. Then, drizzle the glaze over the warm rolls, allowing it to melt into the cracks and crevices. The combination of warm, gooey cinnamon rolls and the sweet glaze will make your taste buds dance with delight.

Step 10: Serve and Enjoy

Your dairy-free cinnamon rolls are now ready to be enjoyed! Serve them warm with a cup of your favorite dairy-free coffee or tea, or indulge in them on their own. These rolls are perfect for any occasion – from a casual weekend brunch to a festive holiday breakfast.

Tips for Perfect Dairy-Free Cinnamon Rolls

  • Flour Alternatives: If you need to make this recipe gluten-free, try using a gluten-free all-purpose flour blend. Be sure to use one that includes xanthan gum or another binder to help the dough hold together.

  • Make Ahead Option: You can prepare the dough the night before and let it rise in the refrigerator overnight. In the morning, take it out, let it come to room temperature, and proceed with the remaining steps.

  • Adding Nuts: For a bit of crunch and extra flavor, add chopped pecans, walnuts, or almonds to the cinnamon-sugar filling before rolling up the dough.

  • Freezing: These cinnamon rolls freeze beautifully. After baking, let them cool completely and freeze them in an airtight container. When you’re ready to enjoy, simply reheat them in the oven or microwave, then top with the glaze.

Conclusion

These dairy-free cinnamon rolls are the perfect combination of soft, fluffy dough and a rich, spiced filling. With just the right amount of sweetness and that signature gooey texture, they’re sure to become a favorite in your home. Plus, they’re versatile and can be customized to suit your dietary preferences, whether you’re avoiding dairy or simply seeking a lighter version of this classic treat.

Whether you’re making these rolls for a special occasion or just because you need a little indulgence, this recipe is sure to deliver. So, roll up your sleeves, get in the kitchen, and bake up a batch of these irresistible dairy-free cinnamon rolls today! You’ll be glad you did.

Happy baking!
