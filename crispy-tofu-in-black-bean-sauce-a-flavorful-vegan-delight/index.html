Crispy Tofu in Black Bean Sauce: A Flavorful Vegan Delight

3 min · 574 words

If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying vegan dish that’s packed with flavor, look no further than Crispy Tofu in Black Bean Sauce. This dish is a delightful combination of crispy tofu, savory black bean sauce, and a variety of aromatic herbs and spices. Whether you’re a long-time vegan, a tofu enthusiast, or just someone looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Crispy Tofu:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, pressed and cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Black Bean Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons fermented black beans, rinsed and mashed
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch, dissolved in 2 tablespoons water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Serving:

  • Cooked white rice
  • Sliced green onions for garnish
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crispy Tofu

  1. Begin by pressing the block of firm tofu to remove excess moisture. Once pressed, cut the tofu into bite-sized cubes.

  2. In a shallow dish, combine the cornstarch, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Toss the tofu cubes in the cornstarch mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  3. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the coated tofu cubes in a single layer. Fry the tofu until golden and crispy on all sides, then transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 2: Prepare the Black Bean Sauce

  1. In the same skillet, remove any excess oil, leaving about a tablespoon in the pan. Add the minced garlic, ginger, and mashed fermented black beans. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the diced red bell pepper and chopped green onions to the skillet, and cook until the vegetables are slightly softened.

  3. Pour in the vegetable broth, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sugar. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  4. Stir in the cornstarch mixture, allowing the sauce to thicken. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3: Assemble and Serve

  1. Once the black bean sauce has thickened to your desired consistency, add the crispy tofu to the skillet, gently tossing to coat the tofu in the flavorful sauce.

  2. Serve the Crispy Tofu in Black Bean Sauce over a bed of cooked white rice. Garnish with sliced green onions and a sprinkle of sesame seeds for added flavor and visual appeal.

Conclusion

Crispy Tofu in Black Bean Sauce is a wonderful way to enjoy the versatility of tofu in a vibrant and flavorful vegan dish. The crispy texture of the tofu paired with the savory and umami-rich black bean sauce creates a delightful sensory experience with every bite. Whether you’re preparing a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. Give it a try and discover the joy of plant-based cooking with this delightful vegan delight!
