Creamy Coconut Lime Bars: A Tropical Twist on Classic Bars


If you’re a fan of tropical flavors and creamy desserts, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful recipe for Creamy Coconut Lime Bars. These bars are a perfect fusion of tangy lime and luscious coconut, creating a refreshing and indulgent dessert that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, craving a taste of the tropics, or simply looking for a new dessert to add to your repertoire, these bars are the perfect choice.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • ⅓ cup of granulated sugar
  • ⅓ cup of melted butter

For the filling:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • ½ cup of fresh lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons of lime zest
  • 4 large egg yolks

For the topping (optional):

  • Whipped cream
  • Lime slices
  • Shredded coconut

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking pan, creating an even layer. Bake for 10 minutes, then set aside to cool.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, coconut milk, lime juice, lime zest, and egg yolks until smooth and well combined.
  2. Pour the filling over the cooled crust, spreading it into an even layer.

Step 3: Bake and Chill

  1. Bake the bars for 15-20 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
  2. Remove the bars from the oven and let them cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until completely chilled and set.

Step 4: Add Toppings (optional)

  1. Once the bars are chilled, cut them into squares and serve with a dollop of whipped cream, a sprinkle of shredded coconut, and a slice of lime for garnish.

Serving and Storing

These Creamy Coconut Lime Bars are best served chilled, making them a refreshing dessert option for warm weather. The combination of creamy coconut and zesty lime creates a tropical flavor explosion that’s sure to delight your taste buds. These bars can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, allowing you to enjoy a taste of the tropics whenever the craving strikes.

Final Thoughts

Creamy Coconut Lime Bars offer a delightful tropical twist on the classic dessert bars, making them a standout addition to any gathering or simply as a treat for yourself. The creamy, tangy, and refreshing flavors make these bars a crowd-pleaser, and the simple preparation ensures that you can enjoy a taste of the tropics with minimal effort. Whether you’re a fan of coconut, lime, or simply love trying new dessert recipes, these bars are a must-try for any dessert enthusiast.

So, why not transport yourself to a tropical paradise with a batch of these Creamy Coconut Lime Bars? Whether you’re lounging by the pool, hosting a summer barbecue, or simply craving a taste of the tropics, these bars are the perfect way to indulge in a little slice of paradise. Enjoy!
