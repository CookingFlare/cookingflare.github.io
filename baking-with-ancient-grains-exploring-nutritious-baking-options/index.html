Baking with Ancient Grains: Exploring Nutritious Baking Options


In recent years, there has been a growing interest in baking with ancient grains due to their nutritional benefits and unique flavors. Ancient grains are minimally processed and have been cultivated for centuries, making them a wholesome choice for baking enthusiasts looking to experiment with different ingredients. In this blog post, we will delve into the world of ancient grains and explore some delicious and nutritious baking options that you can try at home.

The Beauty of Ancient Grains

Ancient grains, such as spelt, amaranth, teff, and quinoa, offer a variety of health benefits compared to modern wheat flour. These grains are rich in fiber, protein, vitamins, and minerals, making them a great addition to any baked goods. Not only do they provide a nutritional boost, but they also add depth of flavor and texture to your recipes, creating a more wholesome and satisfying baking experience.

Delicious Ancient Grain Recipes

1. Spelt Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups spelt flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix the spelt flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the mashed bananas, honey, coconut oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the banana bread to cool before slicing and serving.

2. Amaranth Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup amaranth flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the amaranth flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream the coconut sugar and coconut oil until smooth. Add the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until well combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  6. Allow the cookies to cool before enjoying with a glass of milk or a cup of tea.

Embrace Ancient Grains in Your Baking

By incorporating ancient grains into your baking repertoire, you not only elevate the nutritional value of your creations but also open yourself up to a world of exciting flavors and textures. Whether you are making bread, cookies, muffins, or pancakes, experimenting with ancient grains can transform your baked goods into wholesome treats that nourish both the body and soul.

So why not embark on a culinary journey with ancient grains and discover the endless possibilities they offer for nutritious and delicious baking? Your taste buds and your health will thank you for embracing these time-honored ingredients in your kitchen.

Happy baking!

In this blog post, we explored the wonderful world of baking with ancient grains, discovering the nutritious and delicious options they bring to the table. From spelt banana bread to amaranth chocolate chip cookies, there are endless ways to incorporate these wholesome grains into your baking endeavors. Start your culinary adventure today and enjoy the benefits of ancient grains in every bite!
