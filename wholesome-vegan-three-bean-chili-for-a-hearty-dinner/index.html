Wholesome Vegan Three Bean Chili for a Hearty Dinner


Looking for a satisfying and wholesome vegan dinner option? Look no further than this delicious and hearty three bean chili recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of flavorful spices, this chili is sure to become a crowd favorite. Whether you’re a dedicated vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is a winner.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped (optional for spice lovers)
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado, cilantro, vegan sour cream, diced green onions

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced bell peppers. Sauté for 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are softened.

  2. Add the jalapeno pepper, carrots, and zucchini to the pot. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Add the drained and rinsed kidney beans, black beans, and pinto beans to the pot, along with the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine.

  4. Sprinkle in the chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to evenly distribute the spices throughout the chili.

  5. Bring the chili to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and simmer for 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. After 30 minutes, taste the chili and adjust the seasonings to your preference. If you prefer a spicier chili, add more chili powder or a dash of cayenne pepper.

  7. Serve the wholesome vegan three bean chili hot, garnished with your favorite toppings such as avocado, cilantro, vegan sour cream, or diced green onions.

Why Choose Vegan?

While traditional chili often relies on meat for its heartiness, this vegan version proves that plant-based meals can be just as satisfying and delicious. By using a combination of kidney beans, black beans, and pinto beans, you’re getting a hefty dose of protein and fiber. These beans are also rich in essential minerals like iron, magnesium, and potassium.

The assortment of vegetables in this recipe adds extra nutrients and flavor, making it a well-rounded meal. Plus, the spices like chili powder, cumin, and smoked paprika give this chili a robust and smoky flavor that will have your taste buds dancing.

Conclusion

This wholesome vegan three bean chili is the perfect dish to warm you up on a chilly evening or to serve to friends and family at gatherings. It’s easy to make, packed with nutritious ingredients, and bursting with flavor. Give it a try and let this vegan chili become a staple in your dinner rotation. Enjoy!
