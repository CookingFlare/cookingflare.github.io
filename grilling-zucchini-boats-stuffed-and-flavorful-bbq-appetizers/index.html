Grilling Zucchini Boats: Stuffed and Flavorful BBQ Appetizers


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy appetizer to kick off your next BBQ party? Look no further than grilled zucchini boats! These stuffed and flavorful treats are not only easy to make but also a crowd-pleaser. The best part? They are packed with nutrients and bursting with mouthwatering flavors. So, fire up your grill and get ready to impress your guests with these zucchini boats!

Ingredients

  • 4 large zucchinis
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers (red, yellow, and green)
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. Slice the zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the center to create a hollow boat shape. Save the scooped-out flesh for later use.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, diced bell peppers, diced tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, chopped fresh basil, minced garlic, and the scooped-out zucchini flesh. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Drizzle the olive oil over the zucchini boats and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Spoon the quinoa mixture into the hollowed-out zucchini boats, filling them generously.

  6. Place the stuffed zucchini boats on the preheated grill. Cook for about 10-12 minutes, or until the zucchini is tender and the filling is heated through.

  7. Remove the zucchini boats from the grill and let them cool slightly before serving.

  8. Garnish with additional chopped basil, if desired, and serve hot.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to get creative with the fillings! You can add cooked ground meat, such as beef or turkey, for an extra protein boost. Or, for a vegetarian option, add some sautéed mushrooms or spinach.

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also bake the zucchini boats in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchini is tender.

  • These zucchini boats make a great appetizer, but they can also be served as a light main course. Simply pair them with a side salad or some grilled vegetables for a complete meal.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat them in the oven or microwave before serving.

Health Benefits of Zucchini

Zucchini is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. It is low in calories and high in fiber, making it a great choice for weight management. It is also a good source of vitamins A, C, and K, as well as minerals like potassium and manganese. Additionally, zucchini is rich in antioxidants, which help protect your body against free radicals and promote overall health.

Conclusion

Grilled zucchini boats are a fantastic way to enjoy the flavors of summer while keeping your meals healthy and satisfying. With their vibrant colors, flavorful fillings, and easy preparation, they are sure to become a favorite at your next BBQ party. So, fire up the grill and get ready to impress your guests with these stuffed and flavorful zucchini boats!
