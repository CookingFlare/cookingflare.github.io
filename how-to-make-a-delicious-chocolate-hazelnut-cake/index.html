How to Make a Delicious Chocolate Hazelnut Cake


Chocolate and hazelnut is a timeless combination. The deep, rich flavor of chocolate perfectly complements the earthy, nutty taste of hazelnuts, creating a flavor profile that has become beloved by many. If you’re looking to bake something extraordinary for a special occasion or just want to indulge in a sweet treat, a chocolate hazelnut cake is the perfect choice. This cake is rich, moist, and packed with flavor, offering the best of both worlds with its smooth chocolate base and crunchy hazelnut accents.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is straightforward, and the result is nothing short of impressive. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to create a decadent chocolate hazelnut cake from scratch.

Ingredients You Will Need

For the Cake

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 cup roasted hazelnuts (finely chopped)
  • ½ cup dark chocolate chips (optional, for an extra chocolatey layer)

For the Hazelnut Chocolate Ganache

  • 200g dark chocolate (about 70% cocoa)
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ½ cup ground hazelnuts (you can buy them pre-ground or grind them yourself in a food processor)

For Decoration

  • Whole hazelnuts (for topping)
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder (for garnish)

Equipment You’ll Need

  • Two 9-inch round cake pans
  • Electric mixer or whisk
  • Large mixing bowls
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Spatula
  • Cooling rack

Now that you have all your ingredients and tools ready, let’s get started!

Preparing the Cake Layers

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that your cake bakes evenly once the batter is ready. Grease and line two 9-inch round cake pans with parchment paper to prevent the cake from sticking.

Step 2: Mixing Dry Ingredients

In a large bowl, sift together the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Sifting is essential as it prevents lumps and helps evenly distribute the cocoa powder and leavening agents throughout the flour.

Step 3: Adding Wet Ingredients

To the dry ingredients, add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Using an electric mixer or a whisk, mix until the batter is smooth and well combined. You’ll notice the batter is fairly thick at this stage, which is normal.

Step 4: Incorporating Hot Water

Next, slowly add the hot water to the batter, mixing continuously. This step makes the batter thinner, which will help in achieving a moist and tender cake. Don’t worry if the batter looks a bit runny at this point – this is expected and will lead to a light texture once baked.

Step 5: Adding Hazelnuts and Chocolate Chips

Fold in the finely chopped roasted hazelnuts and dark chocolate chips (if using) using a spatula. The chopped hazelnuts will add a delightful crunch to the cake, while the chocolate chips will melt into pockets of gooey chocolate throughout.

Step 6: Baking the Cake

Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and smooth the tops with a spatula. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean. Be sure to check the cakes a few minutes before the recommended time to prevent overbaking.

Once baked, remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Afterward, turn them out onto a cooling rack to cool completely.

Making the Hazelnut Chocolate Ganache

While your cakes are cooling, it’s the perfect time to prepare the rich and glossy hazelnut chocolate ganache. This ganache will be the key to elevating the flavor of the cake, providing both a silky smooth finish and an intense chocolate-hazelnut flavor.

Step 1: Heating the Cream

In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat. Stir occasionally until it’s just about to boil, but do not let it come to a full boil.

Step 2: Melting the Chocolate

Once the cream is heated, pour it over the chopped dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl. Let the mixture sit for a minute or two to allow the heat to melt the chocolate. Afterward, stir until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture becomes smooth.

Step 3: Adding Butter and Hazelnuts

Stir in the unsalted butter until it’s fully incorporated into the ganache. Finally, add the ground hazelnuts to the ganache and stir well. This step infuses the ganache with a delightful nutty flavor that pairs beautifully with the chocolate. Allow the ganache to cool for 15-20 minutes, so it thickens slightly and becomes spreadable.

Assembling the Cake

Now that both the cake layers and ganache are ready, it’s time to assemble your chocolate hazelnut cake.

Step 1: Layering the Cake

Place one of the cooled cake layers on a serving platter or cake stand. Using a spatula, spread a generous layer of hazelnut chocolate ganache over the top of the first layer. Be sure to spread it evenly, reaching all the way to the edges.

Step 2: Adding the Second Layer

Carefully place the second cake layer on top of the ganache. Gently press down to ensure the layers stick together. At this point, you can apply a thin crumb coat of ganache to the entire cake, which will help seal in any loose crumbs. Let this layer chill in the fridge for about 15 minutes to set before applying the final layer of ganache.

Step 3: Covering with Ganache

Once the crumb coat has set, apply the remaining ganache evenly over the entire cake, smoothing it out with a spatula for a polished look. If you like a more rustic finish, feel free to leave the ganache slightly swirled.

Decorating the Cake

To make your chocolate hazelnut cake truly special, decorating it is the final touch. Here are a few decoration ideas to take your cake to the next level:

Step 1: Adding Whole Hazelnuts

Gently place whole roasted hazelnuts around the top edge of the cake for a classic look. You can also scatter some on top of the cake for added texture and flavor.

Step 2: Chocolate Shavings or Cocoa Powder

For an extra touch of elegance, sprinkle some finely grated chocolate shavings on top of the ganache. Alternatively, sift a small amount of cocoa powder over the cake to give it a sophisticated finish.

Step 3: Optional Garnish

If you want to go all out, you can drizzle some extra ganache over the top or add a few more chopped hazelnuts. Some people like to add a few decorative touches, like gold leaf or edible flowers, to make the cake even more festive.

Tips for a Perfect Chocolate Hazelnut Cake

  • Quality Ingredients Matter: Since the main flavors of this cake come from chocolate and hazelnuts, choose high-quality chocolate and fresh hazelnuts. Look for chocolate with at least 70% cocoa content for a richer taste.

  • Properly Toast Hazelnuts: If you’re toasting your own hazelnuts, ensure they are toasted lightly to bring out their flavor. Toasting also makes the skins easier to remove.

  • Let the Cake Cool Completely: Before frosting and assembling the cake, make sure the layers are completely cool. If they are still warm, the ganache may melt and slide off.

  • Make Ahead: This cake can be made in advance. Simply store the cake layers separately in an airtight container for up to 2 days before assembling. The ganache can also be prepared ahead of time and stored in the fridge, though it will need to be reheated before use.

Serving the Cake

When you’re ready to serve the cake, cut it into slices and enjoy the perfect balance of moist cake, creamy ganache, and crunchy hazelnuts. This cake is perfect for birthdays, holidays, or any occasion that calls for a show-stopping dessert. Pair it with a cup of coffee or a glass of dessert wine, and you’re in for a treat!

Conclusion

Making a chocolate hazelnut cake from scratch may seem like a daunting task, but with the right recipe and ingredients, it’s a rewarding experience that results in an absolutely delicious dessert. The cake itself is moist, fluffy, and full of flavor, while the hazelnut chocolate ganache adds a luxurious and decadent touch. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in a rich dessert, this chocolate hazelnut cake will surely impress everyone who tastes it. Happy baking!
