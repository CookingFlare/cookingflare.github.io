Add the minced garlic, olive oil, maple syrup, cumin, salt, and black pepper to the bowl, and mix until well combined. Gradually add the water to the mixture, stirring continuously until the dressing reaches your desired consistency. Add more water if you prefer a thinner dressing. Taste the dressing and adjust the seasoning to your liking. You can add more lemon juice for extra tanginess, maple syrup for sweetness, or salt and pepper for additional flavor.
