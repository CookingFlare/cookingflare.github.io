Pumpkin Spice Granola Bars: A Nutritious Fall Snack


The arrival of fall brings with it a craving for warm, comforting flavors, and what better way to satisfy that craving than with homemade pumpkin spice granola bars? These nutritious treats are not only delicious but also packed with wholesome ingredients that will keep you energized throughout the day. Whether you’re looking for a convenient breakfast option, a midday pick-me-up, or a satisfying dessert, these pumpkin spice granola bars are the perfect choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the oats, chopped almonds, pumpkin seeds, and dried cranberries. Stir to mix evenly.

  3. In a small saucepan, heat the honey, pumpkin puree, and coconut oil over low heat, stirring until the mixture is smooth and well combined. Remove from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, cloves, and salt.

  4. Pour the wet mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until everything is well coated.

  5. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish, pressing it down firmly and evenly with a spatula or the back of a spoon.

  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.

  7. Allow the granola bars to cool completely in the dish before using the parchment overhang to lift them out and transfer to a cutting board.

  8. Using a sharp knife, cut the cooled granola mixture into bars of your desired size.

  9. Store the pumpkin spice granola bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, or in the refrigerator for longer shelf life.

Nutritional Benefits

These pumpkin spice granola bars are not only a delicious treat but also offer a range of nutritional benefits. The oats provide a good source of fiber and complex carbohydrates, which can help keep you feeling full and satisfied. Almonds and pumpkin seeds add a dose of healthy fats, protein, and essential minerals such as magnesium and zinc. The dried cranberries contribute a touch of sweetness and a boost of antioxidants. The addition of pumpkin puree not only infuses the bars with that quintessential fall flavor but also delivers a dose of vitamin A and fiber.

Serving Suggestions

Enjoy these pumpkin spice granola bars as a quick breakfast on the go, a post-workout snack, or a delightful addition to your fall-themed dessert spread. Pair them with a warm cup of coffee or a spiced chai latte for the ultimate autumn indulgence.

Conclusion

With their warm, comforting flavors and wholesome ingredients, these pumpkin spice granola bars are the perfect way to embrace the spirit of fall. Whether you’re a fan of all things pumpkin spice or simply looking for a nutritious snack to fuel your day, these homemade granola bars are sure to hit the spot. Give this recipe a try and savor the taste of autumn in every bite!
