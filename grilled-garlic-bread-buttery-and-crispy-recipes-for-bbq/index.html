Grilled Garlic Bread: Buttery and Crispy Recipes for BBQ


Grilled garlic bread is the perfect accompaniment to any BBQ. The combination of buttery, crispy bread and the rich flavors of garlic make it a crowd-pleaser that will have your guests coming back for seconds. In this blog post, we will explore different recipes for grilled garlic bread that will take your BBQ to the next level. So fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in some mouthwatering goodness!

Recipe 1: Classic Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of French bread
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, softened butter, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  3. Slice the French bread lengthwise, creating two large halves.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut sides of the bread.
  5. Place the bread on the grill, cut side down, and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Flip the bread over and grill for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Remove the grilled garlic bread from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes.
  8. Slice the bread into smaller pieces and serve hot.

Recipe 2: Cheesy Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of Italian bread
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, softened butter, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  3. Slice the Italian bread lengthwise, creating two large halves.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut sides of the bread.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the garlic butter mixture.
  6. Place the bread on the grill, cut side down, and cook for about 4-5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove the grilled garlic bread from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes.
  8. Slice the bread into smaller pieces and serve hot.

Recipe 3: Herb-infused Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of ciabatta bread
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh oregano, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, softened butter, chopped basil, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  3. Slice the ciabatta bread lengthwise, creating two large halves.
  4. Spread the herb-infused garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut sides of the bread.
  5. Place the bread on the grill, cut side down, and cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Flip the bread over and grill for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Remove the grilled garlic bread from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes.
  8. Slice the bread into smaller pieces and serve hot.

Conclusion

Grilled garlic bread is a simple yet satisfying addition to any BBQ menu. Whether you prefer the classic version, the cheesy variation, or the herb-infused twist, these recipes will leave your taste buds craving for more. The buttery and crispy texture, combined with the robust flavors of garlic and other ingredients, make grilled garlic bread an irresistible treat. So the next time you fire up the grill, don’t forget to include this delectable side dish. Your guests will thank you!
