Mouthwatering Wild Rice Salad with Cranberries and Almonds


If you’re looking for a healthy and flavorful salad that will tantalize your taste buds, look no further than this mouthwatering wild rice salad with cranberries and almonds. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with vibrant flavors, this salad is a perfect addition to any meal or a satisfying main course on its own.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wild rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the wild rice under cold water to remove any impurities. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed rice and water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 45-50 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, apple cider vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper to make the dressing. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked wild rice, dried cranberries, sliced almonds, chopped red onion, parsley, and mint. Toss gently to combine.

  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss again to coat all the ingredients evenly.

  5. Allow the salad to sit at room temperature for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This step is crucial as it enhances the taste of the salad.

  6. Once the salad has marinated, give it a final toss before serving. You can serve it chilled or at room temperature, depending on your preference.

  7. Transfer the salad to a serving bowl or individual plates and garnish with some extra chopped mint and sliced almonds for an extra pop of color and crunch.

This wild rice salad is not only visually appealing but also bursting with flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. The nutty and chewy wild rice pairs perfectly with the sweetness of the dried cranberries, while the sliced almonds add a delightful crunch to every bite. The combination of fresh herbs like parsley and mint adds a refreshing element that complements the other ingredients perfectly.

Not only is this salad delicious, but it’s also incredibly nutritious. Wild rice is a great source of fiber and essential minerals, while cranberries are packed with antioxidants. The almonds provide healthy fats and protein, making this salad a well-rounded meal option.

Whether you’re looking for a side dish to accompany your main course or a light and satisfying lunch, this mouthwatering wild rice salad with cranberries and almonds is a must-try. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed all year round and is sure to impress your family and guests with its vibrant flavors and colorful presentation.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather the ingredients and get ready to indulge in this delightful wild rice salad that will have you coming back for seconds. Enjoy!
