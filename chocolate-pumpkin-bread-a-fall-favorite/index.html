Chocolate Pumpkin Bread: A Fall Favorite


There’s something magical about the arrival of fall - the crisp air, the vibrant leaves, and the enticing aroma of pumpkin spice. It’s a season that beckons us to indulge in comforting treats, and what better way to embrace the cozy vibes of autumn than with a slice of delectable Chocolate Pumpkin Bread? This moist, flavorful bread is a delightful fusion of rich chocolate and earthy pumpkin, making it the perfect accompaniment to a steaming cup of coffee on a chilly fall morning. In this blog post, I’ll walk you through a scrumptious Chocolate Pumpkin Bread recipe that will surely become a staple in your fall baking repertoire.

Ingredients

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of canned pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract

Dry Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of pumpkin pie spice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, granulated sugar, brown sugar, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and pumpkin pie spice.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  6. Bake for 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the bread comes out clean.

  7. Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

This Chocolate Pumpkin Bread is wonderful on its own, but you can take it to the next level by adding a sprinkle of powdered sugar on top for a touch of sweetness. If you’re feeling indulgent, a dollop of freshly whipped cream or a drizzle of chocolate ganache will elevate this treat to a decadent dessert status. Pair a slice with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an irresistible autumn-inspired sundae.

Storage

To keep your Chocolate Pumpkin Bread fresh, store it in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, wrap the bread tightly in plastic wrap and aluminum foil, then freeze for up to 3 months.

Conclusion

As the leaves turn golden and the air becomes brisk, the allure of fall baking beckons. Whether you’re planning a cozy brunch or simply want to treat yourself to a delightful homemade snack, this Chocolate Pumpkin Bread is a must-try. The marriage of rich cocoa and velvety pumpkin creates a symphony of flavors that captures the essence of autumn. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the comforting aroma of Chocolate Pumpkin Bread fill your kitchen, welcoming the splendor of fall with every heavenly bite.
