Yummy Low Carb Spaghetti Squash Shrimp Scampi


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy low-carb dish that doesn’t compromise on flavor, then this Yummy Low Carb Spaghetti Squash Shrimp Scampi recipe is perfect for you. With the combination of tender shrimp, garlic-infused butter, and the unique texture of spaghetti squash, this dish is sure to impress your taste buds. Plus, it’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized spaghetti squash
  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut the spaghetti squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds. Place the squash halves cut-side down on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for about 40-45 minutes, or until the flesh is tender and easily comes apart with a fork. Let it cool slightly.

  2. While the spaghetti squash is baking, prepare the shrimp scampi. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook them as they can become rubbery.

  4. Once the shrimp are cooked, remove them from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the olive oil and lemon juice. Use a spatula to scrape any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. This will add extra flavor to the sauce.

  5. Using a fork, scrape the flesh of the baked spaghetti squash into the skillet with the lemon and olive oil mixture. Toss the spaghetti squash in the sauce until well coated.

  6. Add the cooked shrimp back into the skillet with the spaghetti squash. Gently toss everything together until the shrimp and spaghetti squash are evenly distributed.

  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley for added freshness and visual appeal.

  8. Serve the Yummy Low Carb Spaghetti Squash Shrimp Scampi immediately while it’s still warm. Enjoy the delicious flavors and the guilt-free satisfaction of a low-carb meal.

Tips:

  • To save time, you can prepare the spaghetti squash ahead of time and store it in the refrigerator until you’re ready to make the scampi. Simply reheat it in the skillet with the sauce and shrimp.
  • Feel free to add your own twist to this recipe by incorporating other vegetables such as cherry tomatoes, spinach, or bell peppers.
  • If you prefer a spicier version, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the garlic butter sauce.

Final Thoughts:

This Yummy Low Carb Spaghetti Squash Shrimp Scampi recipe is a delightful and healthy option for those looking to cut down on carbs without sacrificing taste. The combination of tender shrimp, garlic-infused butter, and the lightness of spaghetti squash creates a harmonious blend of flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is easy to follow and will surely impress your friends and family. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of this low-carb masterpiece!
