Simple and Delicious Banana Bread Recipe


Who doesn’t love a warm and moist slice of banana bread? Whether you enjoy it for breakfast, as a snack, or even as a dessert, this classic treat never fails to satisfy. Today, I’m sharing with you a simple and delicious banana bread recipe that will have your taste buds begging for more. So, let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, melted butter, and sugar. Mix well until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the banana mixture. Stir until everything is well incorporated.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture, stirring until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the sour cream, being careful not to overmix. The sour cream will add a wonderful tang and moisture to the bread.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it evenly.
  7. Bake for about 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The baking time may vary, so keep an eye on it after the 50-minute mark.
  8. Once baked, remove the banana bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This banana bread is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level by adding a few extra toppings or serving it with your favorite accompaniments. Here are a few suggestions to get you started:

  • Spread a layer of cream cheese or Nutella on each slice for a creamy and indulgent treat.
  • Toast the slices and serve them with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon for an extra touch of sweetness.
  • Add a handful of chocolate chips or chopped nuts to the batter before baking for added texture and flavor.

Storage Tips

If you manage to resist devouring the entire loaf in one sitting, here’s how you can store your banana bread for later enjoyment:

  • Wrap the cooled banana bread tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil to prevent it from drying out.
  • Store it at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.
  • To freeze the banana bread, wrap it in plastic wrap and then place it in a freezer-safe bag or container. It will keep well for up to 3 months. Thaw it overnight in the refrigerator before serving.

Final Thoughts

There you have it! A simple and delicious banana bread recipe that is sure to become a favorite in your household. The combination of ripe bananas, butter, and a few staple ingredients creates a moist and flavorful bread that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you enjoy it plain or get creative with toppings, this banana bread is bound to satisfy your cravings. So, grab those ripe bananas and start baking. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, baking is all about experimentation, so feel free to add your own personal touch to this recipe. Whether it’s a sprinkle of cinnamon or a handful of your favorite nuts, don’t be afraid to get creative and make this recipe your own. Enjoy!
