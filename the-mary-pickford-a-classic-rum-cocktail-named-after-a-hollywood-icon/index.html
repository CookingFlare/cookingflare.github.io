The Mary Pickford: A Classic Rum Cocktail Named After a Hollywood Icon


The world of cocktails is filled with vibrant and intriguing concoctions, each with its own unique history and flavor profile. One such classic cocktail is the Mary Pickford, a delightful rum-based drink that pays homage to the legendary Hollywood icon of the same name. With its combination of rum, pineapple juice, grenadine, and maraschino liqueur, the Mary Pickford is a cocktail that will transport you to the golden age of cinema with every sip.

The Origins of the Mary Pickford Cocktail

Named after the renowned actress Mary Pickford, who was one of the most popular stars of the silent film era, this cocktail was created in the 1920s as a tribute to her success and glamour. Mary Pickford was not only a talented actress but also a trailblazer in the film industry, co-founding the United Artists studio alongside Charlie Chaplin, Douglas Fairbanks, and D.W. Griffith.

The cocktail was reportedly invented by a bartender at the Hotel Nacional de Cuba, where Mary Pickford and her husband, Douglas Fairbanks, frequented during their visits to Havana. The drink gained popularity and spread throughout the United States, becoming a classic cocktail that is still enjoyed today.

The Ingredients

To make a Mary Pickford cocktail, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of white rum
  • 2 ounces of pineapple juice
  • 1 teaspoon of grenadine
  • 1 teaspoon of maraschino liqueur

The Preparation

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.
  2. Add the white rum, pineapple juice, grenadine, and maraschino liqueur to the shaker.
  3. Shake vigorously for about 15 seconds to ensure all the ingredients are well mixed and chilled.
  4. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass.
  5. Optionally, garnish the cocktail with a maraschino cherry or a pineapple wedge for a touch of elegance.

The Taste

The Mary Pickford cocktail is a delightful balance of sweet and tart flavors. The rum provides a smooth and mellow base, while the pineapple juice adds a refreshing tropical note. The grenadine adds a touch of sweetness, and the maraschino liqueur lends a subtle floral undertone. The combination of these flavors creates a cocktail that is both sophisticated and easy to drink.

Serving Suggestions

The Mary Pickford cocktail is best enjoyed on a warm summer evening, either as an aperitif or as a refreshing companion to a leisurely brunch. Its tropical flavors make it a great choice for beach parties or backyard gatherings. Serve it in a chilled cocktail glass to enhance its elegance and visual appeal.

In Conclusion

The Mary Pickford cocktail is a classic drink that not only pays homage to a Hollywood icon but also captures the essence of an era long gone. Its combination of rum, pineapple juice, grenadine, and maraschino liqueur creates a harmonious blend of flavors that is sure to please even the most discerning cocktail connoisseur. So why not raise a glass to Mary Pickford and experience the taste of Hollywood’s golden age with this delightful cocktail? Cheers!
