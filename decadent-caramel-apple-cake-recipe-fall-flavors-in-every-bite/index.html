Decadent Caramel Apple Cake Recipe: Fall Flavors in Every Bite


Are you looking for a show-stopping dessert that perfectly captures the essence of fall? Look no further than this Decadent Caramel Apple Cake. With its rich caramel flavor, tender apple chunks, and warm spices, this cake is the ultimate indulgence for the autumn season. Each bite is like a taste of pure bliss, making it the perfect dessert for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 cups peeled, cored, and chopped apples (about 2 medium-sized apples)
  • 1 cup caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. Mix well to ensure that all the dry ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Add the softened butter, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and eggs to the dry mixture. Using an electric mixer, beat the ingredients together on medium speed until well combined and the batter is smooth and creamy.

  4. Gently fold in the chopped apples, ensuring that they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  5. Pour half of the batter into the prepared cake pan and spread it evenly. Drizzle half of the caramel sauce over the batter, allowing it to swirl into the cake.

  6. Pour the remaining batter over the caramel layer and spread it evenly. Drizzle the remaining caramel sauce on top, once again allowing it to swirl into the cake.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once the cake has cooled, you can serve it as is or top it with additional caramel sauce for an extra decadent touch. Enjoy!
