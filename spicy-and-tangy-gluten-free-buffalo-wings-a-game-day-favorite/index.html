Spicy and Tangy Gluten-Free Buffalo Wings: A Game Day Favorite


Welcome to our kitchen, where we’re turning up the heat with a gluten-free twist on a game day classic: Spicy and Tangy Buffalo Wings! Whether you’re hosting a game day gathering or just craving a deliciously fiery snack, these wings are sure to hit the spot. This recipe is not only gluten-free but also packed with flavor and perfect for sharing with friends and family. Let’s dive into the details and get ready to elevate your game day spread with these irresistible buffalo wings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs of chicken wings
  • 1 cup of gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup of hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Wings

  • Start by rinsing the chicken wings and patting them dry with paper towels. This will help the seasoning and coating stick to the wings better.

Step 2: Season and Coat

  • In a large bowl, combine the gluten-free all-purpose flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Toss the chicken wings in the flour mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.

Step 3: Fry the Wings

  • In a large, deep skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the coated wings in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Fry the wings until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes per batch. Transfer the cooked wings to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 4: Prepare the Sauce

  • In a small saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over low heat. Once melted, whisk in the hot sauce and honey. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.

Step 5: Toss and Serve

  • In a large mixing bowl, toss the fried wings in the prepared spicy and tangy buffalo sauce until they are evenly coated. The aroma will be irresistible! Transfer the wings to a serving platter and garnish with your favorite fresh herbs, such as chopped cilantro or parsley.

Now, dig in and enjoy the bold flavors of these gluten-free buffalo wings! Whether you serve them with a side of cooling ranch dressing, crisp celery sticks, or your favorite game day snacks, these wings are bound to be the star of the show.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the buffalo sauce.
  • If you prefer a milder version, reduce the amount of cayenne pepper in the coating and sauce.
  • To make these wings dairy-free, substitute the unsalted butter with a dairy-free alternative, such as vegan butter or margarine.
  • Experiment with different gluten-free flour blends for a unique texture and flavor.

These Spicy and Tangy Gluten-Free Buffalo Wings are a game day favorite for a reason. They’re crispy, flavorful, and undeniably addictive. Whether you’re cheering for your favorite team or simply indulging in a delicious treat, these wings are sure to make any occasion a memorable one. So, roll up your sleeves, get cooking, and let the tantalizing aroma of these wings set the stage for an unforgettable game day experience!
