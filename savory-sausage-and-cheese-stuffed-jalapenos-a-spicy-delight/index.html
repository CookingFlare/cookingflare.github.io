Savory Sausage and Cheese Stuffed Jalapenos: A Spicy Delight


When it comes to spicy food, many of us crave that fiery kick that sets our taste buds ablaze. And what better way to satisfy this craving than with a dish that combines the spiciness of jalapenos with the savory flavors of sausage and cheese? In this recipe, we’ll explore the world of Savory Sausage and Cheese Stuffed Jalapenos, a dish that’s sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 12 large jalapenos
  • 1 pound of savory sausage (such as andouille or chorizo)
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions

To begin, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). While the oven is heating up, prepare the jalapenos by cutting off the stems and scooping out the seeds and membranes. This will help to reduce the heat level of the peppers and make them more manageable to stuff.

Next, cook the savory sausage in a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Once the sausage is fully cooked, drain the excess fat and set it aside.

In a separate bowl, mix together the shredded cheddar cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and chopped cilantro. This will be the filling for our stuffed jalapenos.

To assemble the dish, stuff each jalapeno pepper with the sausage and cheese mixture, making sure to pack it in tightly. Place the stuffed jalapenos on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving about 1 inch of space between each pepper.

Drizzle the olive oil over the jalapenos and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. Dot the top of each pepper with butter.

Baking

Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. You may need to rotate the baking sheet halfway through the cooking time to ensure even cooking.

Serving

Once the jalapenos are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Serve warm, garnished with additional cilantro if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this dish more substantial, serve the stuffed jalapenos as an appetizer or side dish with a hearty main course, such as grilled meats or roasted vegetables.
  • For an added crunch, top the stuffed jalapenos with crispy breadcrumbs or crushed crackers before baking.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as mozzarella or feta, for a unique flavor profile.
  • If you prefer a milder heat level, use only half of the jalapeno peppers or substitute in some milder peppers, such as Anaheim or bell peppers.

Conclusion

Savory Sausage and Cheese Stuffed Jalapenos are a delicious and spicy twist on traditional stuffed peppers. With their bold flavors and satisfying crunch, these peppers are sure to become a new favorite in your household. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or just looking for a unique appetizer, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, give it a try, and ignite your taste buds with this spicy delight!
