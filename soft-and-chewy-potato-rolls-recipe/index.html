Soft and Chewy Potato Rolls Recipe


Are you tired of the same old boring dinner rolls? Elevate your meal with these irresistible, soft and chewy potato rolls that will have both you and your family reaching for more. Made from scratch with simple ingredients, these delicious potato rolls make the perfect accompaniment to any meal, from hearty beef stews to savory poultry dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm water (100-110°F)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1/4 cup potato flakes
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s review some helpful tips for success:

  1. Use a digital kitchen scale: Measuring flour by volume can lead to inconsistent results. A kitchen scale ensures accurate proportions, leading to perfectly soft and chewy rolls every time.
  2. Allow the dough to rise in a warm place: Yeast is sensitive to temperature. Keeping the dough in a warm environment will aid in the fermentation process, producing a well-risen, fluffy roll.
  3. Avoid overworking the dough: For the most tender potato rolls, gently knead and handle the dough. Avoid adding more flour than necessary, as it can make the rolls tough.

Ready to get started? Here’s the full recipe:

In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Allow the mixture to sit for about 5 minutes, or until the yeast is frothy.

Meanwhile, in a medium-sized bowl, mix together 2 cups of flour, potato flakes, salt, and 1/4 cup of vegetable oil. Once the yeast mixture is ready, add this to the dry ingredients and mix thoroughly.

Slowly add the beaten egg and mix until a dough forms. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for approximately 10 minutes, or until it is smooth and elastic. Avoid adding too much extra flour to the dough – this will make the rolls tough.

Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it with a damp towel, and allow it to rise in a warm area for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 12 equal portions. Shape each portion into a smooth ball, and place them on a greased baking sheet or parchment paper.

Allow the rolls to rise for another 30 minutes, or until they are puffing up and nearly doubled in size.

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake the rolls for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown.

Remove the rolls from the oven and allow them to cool on a wire rack before serving. Enjoy your soft and chewy potato rolls with your favorite meal – you may never return to store-bought rolls again!

And there you have it, the ultimate recipe for your new favorite dinner rolls. The perfect marriage of classic flavors with a comforting texture, these potato rolls are sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So why not surprise your family tonight with a warm batch of homemade potato rolls? They won’t be able to resist reaching for more.
