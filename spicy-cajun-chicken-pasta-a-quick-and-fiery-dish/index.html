Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta: A Quick and Fiery Dish


Are you ready to add some spice to your life? Look no further than this delicious Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta recipe! Bursting with bold flavors and a fiery kick, this dish is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just treating yourself to a flavorful meal, this quick and easy recipe is guaranteed to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of penne pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes, drained
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a bowl, toss the chicken breast pieces with Cajun seasoning until evenly coated.

  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.

  5. Pour in the heavy cream and chicken broth, stirring to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for 2 minutes.

  6. Stir in the diced tomatoes and season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue to simmer for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Add the cooked penne pasta and cooked chicken to the skillet, tossing to coat everything in the creamy Cajun sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat through.

  8. Serve the Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta hot, garnished with fresh parsley for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a dash of hot sauce or red pepper flakes to the Cajun seasoning.
  • Feel free to customize the dish by adding your favorite vegetables such as mushrooms, zucchini, or spinach.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half or whole milk.
  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved to accommodate your serving needs.

Enjoy the Fiery Flavors!

Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta is a dish that combines the bold flavors of Cajun seasoning with the creaminess of a rich sauce. The heat from the spices is perfectly balanced by the coolness of the cream, creating a harmony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. This quick and easy recipe is perfect for busy weeknights or when you’re in the mood for a delicious and satisfying meal.

So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to enjoy a plateful of Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta. With its fiery flavors and quick preparation, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household. Give it a try and let your taste buds embark on a flavorful adventure!
