Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Chicken and Rice


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal that the whole family will love, this Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Chicken and Rice recipe is the perfect solution. Not only is it incredibly flavorful, but it’s also a one-pot wonder, which means minimal cleanup – a win-win situation for any home cook. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 ¼ cups chicken broth
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and paprika. In a large, deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked through. Once done, transfer the chicken to a plate and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Garlic and Rice: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the rice and stir to coat it with the remaining oil and garlic. Cook the rice for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Simmer the Rice: Pour in the chicken broth and add the dried thyme. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the skillet and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the liquid.

  4. Combine the Chicken and Rice: Once the rice is cooked, return the chicken to the skillet, nestling it into the rice. Add any juices from the resting plate as well. Cut the butter into small pieces and dot it over the chicken and rice.

  5. Finish and Serve: Cover the skillet again and cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the chicken is heated through and the butter has melted. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley before serving.

Tips for Success:

  • Use a skillet with a tight-fitting lid to ensure the rice cooks evenly.
  • For added flavor, consider using low-sodium chicken broth and adjusting the salt to taste.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the finished dish.

Enjoy the Delightful Dish:

This Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Chicken and Rice is a comforting and satisfying meal that’s perfect for any day of the week. The combination of tender chicken, fragrant garlic, and buttery rice creates a symphony of flavors that will leave everyone at the table asking for seconds. Plus, with the simplicity of cooking everything in one pot, cleanup is a breeze. So, if you’re in need of a reliable and delectable recipe to add to your repertoire, give this one a try – it’s sure to become a family favorite!

In conclusion, this Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Chicken and Rice recipe offers a delightful balance of flavors and a convenient cooking process that’s bound to make your mealtime a joy. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this dish is a fantastic addition to your cooking repertoire. Give it a go and savor the delicious results!
