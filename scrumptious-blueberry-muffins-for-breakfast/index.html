Scrumptious Blueberry Muffins for Breakfast


There’s nothing quite like waking up to the smell of freshly baked blueberry muffins wafting through the air. These little pockets of goodness are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even a delightful snack. If you’re a fan of blueberries and enjoy baking, this recipe is a must-try. Let’s dive into the delightful world of baking and create some scrumptious blueberry muffins that will leave everyone craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/2 cups fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preparation: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease the cups with butter.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the melted butter, eggs, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  4. Combine Mixtures: Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl of dry ingredients and gently fold them together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Add Blueberries: Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to break them apart.

  6. Fill Muffin Cups: Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each about two-thirds full.

  7. Bake: Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, these scrumptious blueberry muffins are ready to be enjoyed.

Tips and Variations

  • Frozen Blueberries: If fresh blueberries are not in season, feel free to use frozen blueberries. Just be sure to gently fold them into the batter while they are still frozen to prevent them from bleeding into the batter.

  • Additional Flavors: For a twist, consider adding a hint of lemon zest to the batter for a refreshing citrusy note that complements the blueberries beautifully.

  • Storage: These muffins can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week. They also freeze well for up to 3 months, making them a convenient make-ahead breakfast option.

  • Serve with: These muffins pair perfectly with a hot cup of coffee or a cold glass of milk for a delightful breakfast treat.

Final Thoughts

These scrumptious blueberry muffins are a delightful addition to any breakfast or brunch table. The burst of juicy blueberries combined with the tender crumb of the muffin is simply irresistible. Whether you’re baking for yourself, your family, or a gathering of friends, these muffins are sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of freshly baked blueberry muffins fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
