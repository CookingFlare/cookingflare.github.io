Transform Your Cooking Skills: Brilliant Time-Saving Chinese Cooking Hacks


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen, trying to perfect that authentic Chinese dish? If you’re on the lookout for time-saving Chinese cooking hacks that won’t compromise on flavor, we’ve got you covered! In this blog post, we will introduce you to various innovative techniques that will bring more convenience and efficiency to your cooking adventure. Prepare to witness culinary magic!

1. Master the One-Pan Sauce

Oh, the joy of cleaning up after a meal - not! That’s why we can’t overstate the importance of one-pan cooking. The principle? Cook everything on a single pan to save time and energy. To make a dynamite pan-seared chicken with oyster sauce, simply combine soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, sugar, and cornstarch. Pan-sear your chicken, add in the sauce mixture, and let it cook down. Voila! A delectable, efficient meal is served!

2. The Garlic Chopper

Feeling overwhelmed by the prospect of finely chopping a pound of garlic? Enter the garlic chopper! This nifty little device will have your garlic minced in seconds, saving you tons of time. Trust us, your peeling, chopping, and mincing days are officially over.

3. Steam Fish like a Pro

Cooking fish might seem complicated, but this mouthwatering Asian classic is simpler than you think. Find a whole fish, preferably with scales removed, and add a mix of ginger, garlic, green onions, and soy sauce. Instead of slaving over a hot stove, stuff the fish with more aromatics and place it in a steamer. Steam for about 15 minutes, and let the fragrant mixture lend its flavor to the fish. Remove from the steamer, pan-sear, and serve with a sprinkle of sesame oil for a restaurant-quality dish.

4. Invest in a Wok

If you haven’t already, it’s time toadd a wok to your cooking arsenal. A wok can make a world of difference in cooking Chinese cuisine, capable of sautéing, steaming, and deep-frying your favorite dishes. Master the art of the “wok hay,” or breathing of the wok, which brings out exciting flavors in your cooking.

5. Quick-Cook Vegetables

Chinese cooking relies heavily on vegetables, so you’ll undoubtedly be using a lot of them. To save time, focus on quick-cooking techniques, like stir-frying or blanching, making sure to have everything prepped and in easy reach beforehand. To optimize these techniques, cook your vegetables in high heat and small batches to ensure crispiness and flavor.

6. Time-Saving Marinades

Marinades are crucial for locking in flavors, but finding the one can be tricky. Marinating beef in a simple mixture of Shaoxing wine, soy sauce, and sugar not only tenderizes the meat but also elevates its overall taste and appeal. Speed up the process by using a vacuum sealer, which helps you marinate meats in half the time!

7. Shortcut With Convenience Ingredients

Don’t be ashamed - embrace the convenience! Store-bought ingredients like oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and soy sauce will save you plenty of time and energy in the kitchen. Use them to your advantage when whipping up those scrumptious dishes without sacrificing flavor.

8. Advanced Knife Skills

Time is money, and nothing’s more true when prepping in the kitchen. Develop your knife skills to slice and dice faster and with more accuracy. Master chopping, dicing, and mincing, and learn the art of the julienne cut, giving you a professional upper hand in the kitchen.

9. The Soft-Boiled Egg Trick

Impress your dinner guests with the perfectly soft-boiled egg without exhausting your egg supply. Fill a pot with water and bring it to a rolling boil, then turn off the heat, cover the pot, and let the eggs sit for about 7 minutes. This foolproof method will have your eggs cooked to perfection every time.

10. Freezing Prepped Ingredients

Life can get hectic, and sometimes prepping days in advance is necessary. Prepare your ingredients in advance and freeze them in an airtight container or zip-lock bag to keep your meal prep fresher and easier. Pro tip: freeze herbs in ice cube trays filled with water or oil for quick and easy defrosting.

Uncover the world of Chinese cooking with these time-saving hacks. From sauce elimination toingredient shortcuts, these brilliant tips grant you the freedom to concentrate on what truly matters - bringing together delicious flavors on your plate. Now, go ahead and transform your cooking skills - it’s wok time!
