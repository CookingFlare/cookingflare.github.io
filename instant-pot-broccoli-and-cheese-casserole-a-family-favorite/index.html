Instant Pot Broccoli and Cheese Casserole: A Family Favorite


If you’re looking for an easy, quick, and comforting dish to serve for dinner, look no further than this Instant Pot Broccoli and Cheese Casserole. Packed with tender broccoli, creamy cheese, and a delightful crunch on top, this dish is sure to become a staple in your family’s dinner rotation. Not only is it incredibly delicious, but it’s also quick and easy to make, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.

Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight or hosting a casual family gathering, this casserole checks all the boxes: it’s simple to prepare, versatile, and loved by both kids and adults alike. Let’s dive into why this Instant Pot broccoli and cheese casserole is such a hit and how you can make it your own.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Casseroles?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to preparing family-friendly meals. Traditional casseroles often take time to bake in the oven, but with the Instant Pot, you can cut down on cooking time significantly while still achieving that delicious, comforting flavor. The pressure cooking function allows all the ingredients to meld together beautifully, ensuring that every bite is filled with rich flavors and tender textures. Plus, you don’t have to worry about constantly monitoring the stovetop or oven. With just a few simple steps, your meal is ready in no time.

Another great feature of cooking with an Instant Pot is the ability to set it and forget it. Once you’ve added all your ingredients and sealed the lid, you can walk away and let the Instant Pot work its magic. No need for stirring or adjusting the heat—just set the timer, and you’ll be left with a perfectly cooked casserole every time.

Key Ingredients for Broccoli and Cheese Casserole

This Instant Pot broccoli and cheese casserole is incredibly versatile, but there are a few key ingredients that bring the dish together:

1. Fresh Broccoli

The star of this casserole is undoubtedly the broccoli. Fresh broccoli florets provide a perfect balance of texture and flavor. You’ll want to cut the broccoli into bite-sized pieces, allowing it to cook evenly and mix well with the other ingredients.

2. Cheese

Cheese is what makes this casserole so rich and comforting. A combination of sharp cheddar cheese and creamy mozzarella cheese creates the perfect cheesy base for the casserole. You can experiment with different types of cheese depending on your preference, such as adding Monterey Jack for extra meltiness or a little Parmesan for a more complex flavor.

3. Cream of Mushroom Soup (or Alternative)

To give the casserole its creamy texture, you’ll need a can of cream of mushroom soup, or you can opt for a homemade version if you prefer. This ingredient helps bind everything together while adding a savory depth to the dish. If you’re looking for a non-creamy alternative, you can replace it with a dairy-free or gluten-free version.

4. Seasonings and Spices

A good casserole needs the right seasonings to elevate the flavors. Basic ingredients like garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper bring out the natural flavors of the broccoli and cheese. For some extra flair, consider adding a pinch of paprika or a dash of cayenne pepper for a hint of heat.

5. Crunchy Topping (Optional)

While the casserole itself is already delicious, you can make it even better by adding a crunchy topping. A mixture of breadcrumbs and melted butter creates a golden, crispy crust that adds texture and flavor contrast to the creamy casserole. You can also sprinkle on some grated Parmesan cheese for an added savory touch.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Instant Pot Broccoli and Cheese Casserole

1. Prepare the Broccoli

Begin by preparing your broccoli. Wash the broccoli thoroughly, then cut it into bite-sized florets. Make sure the florets are small enough to cook evenly but still big enough to provide that satisfying texture. If you prefer, you can blanch the broccoli beforehand to ensure it’s tender, but the Instant Pot will cook it perfectly on its own without the extra step.

2. Layer the Ingredients in the Instant Pot

Next, you’ll layer the ingredients in the Instant Pot. Start by adding your prepared broccoli to the Instant Pot’s inner pot. Then, pour in the can of cream of mushroom soup, followed by a generous amount of both shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheese. Season the mixture with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper to taste. Mix everything together to evenly coat the broccoli in the cheesy, creamy sauce.

3. Set the Instant Pot to Cook

Secure the lid on your Instant Pot, making sure the valve is set to the “sealing” position. Set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 3 minutes. This short cooking time ensures that the broccoli retains its bright green color and crisp-tender texture. While the Instant Pot is cooking, you can prepare the topping if you plan to add one.

4. Release the Pressure

Once the cooking time is complete, allow the Instant Pot to release pressure naturally for 5 minutes before performing a quick release. This helps the casserole finish cooking and prevents any unwanted splattering when you open the lid.

5. Add the Crunchy Topping (Optional)

If you’re adding a crunchy topping, now is the time to do it. In a separate bowl, combine breadcrumbs and melted butter, and then sprinkle this mixture over the top of your casserole. You can also add a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese for an extra savory kick. If you prefer a more indulgent topping, feel free to add more shredded cheese for a gooey, melty finish.

6. Serve and Enjoy!

Once your casserole is ready, carefully remove the lid and give everything a gentle stir to combine. Serve it up warm, and watch as your family digs in. This Instant Pot broccoli and cheese casserole pairs wonderfully with roasted chicken, grilled steak, or even as a stand-alone dish for a meatless meal. Leftovers can be stored in the fridge for a few days, and it reheats beautifully in the microwave or oven.

Tips for the Best Broccoli and Cheese Casserole

1. Customize the Cheese Blend

While sharp cheddar and mozzarella are a classic combination, don’t be afraid to get creative with your cheese selection. For a richer flavor, try adding gouda, Gruyère, or even a bit of blue cheese. The possibilities are endless, and experimenting with different cheese blends can take this casserole to a whole new level.

2. Add Protein

If you’re looking to turn this casserole into a more hearty, protein-packed meal, consider adding cooked chicken, turkey, or sausage. Shredded rotisserie chicken works great, as it’s easy to incorporate and adds extra flavor to the dish. Simply stir it into the casserole mixture before cooking, and you’ll have a complete meal in one pot.

3. Use Fresh or Frozen Broccoli

Fresh broccoli is ideal for this recipe, but if you’re in a pinch, frozen broccoli will work as well. Just be sure to thaw the broccoli completely before adding it to the Instant Pot, as excess water from frozen broccoli can make the casserole too watery.

4. Experiment with Different Cream Soups

Although cream of mushroom soup is a classic choice, feel free to switch things up by using other varieties like cream of chicken or even cream of broccoli soup for an extra layer of flavor. Just be sure to choose a soup that complements the broccoli and cheese without overpowering it.

5. Make It Ahead of Time

If you’re planning to serve this casserole for a busy weeknight or a special gathering, you can prepare it ahead of time. Simply layer the ingredients in the Instant Pot insert and refrigerate it for up to 24 hours. When you’re ready to cook, just pop the insert into the Instant Pot and follow the instructions as usual.

Why This Instant Pot Broccoli and Cheese Casserole is a Family Favorite

There are a few key reasons why this casserole is beloved by so many families:

  • Quick and Easy: With just a few simple ingredients and the magic of the Instant Pot, you can have a delicious casserole ready in under 30 minutes.
  • Crowd-Pleasing: Whether you have picky eaters or a family of diverse tastes, everyone will love the creamy cheese and tender broccoli in this casserole.
  • Versatile: This recipe is highly customizable to suit your family’s preferences, from changing up the cheeses to adding proteins or extra veggies.
  • Comfort Food: There’s something inherently comforting about a cheesy casserole, and this one does not disappoint. It’s perfect for cozy family dinners or when you want to indulge in something warm and satisfying.

Conclusion

This Instant Pot broccoli and cheese casserole is an easy, delicious, and comforting dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner, a holiday meal, or a special gathering, it’s a guaranteed hit with both kids and adults alike. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can create a flavorful, cheesy, and creamy casserole that comes together in no time.

So, next time you’re craving a simple yet indulgent dinner, give this Instant Pot broccoli and cheese casserole a try. It’s sure to become a family favorite that you’ll make over and over again. Enjoy!
