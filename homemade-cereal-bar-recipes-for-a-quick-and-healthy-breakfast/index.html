Homemade Cereal Bar Recipes for a Quick and Healthy Breakfast


Are you tired of reaching for store-bought cereal bars that are packed with artificial ingredients and preservatives? If so, it’s time to try making your own homemade cereal bars. Not only are they healthier, but they are also incredibly easy to make. In this blog post, we will share with you three delicious homemade cereal bar recipes that are perfect for a quick and healthy breakfast. Let’s get started!

1. Nutty Granola Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds, walnuts, or cashews)
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup dried fruits (such as raisins, cranberries, or apricots)
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the rolled oats, chopped nuts, dried fruits, and dark chocolate chips (if using).
  3. In a small saucepan, heat the honey and almond butter over low heat until melted and well combined. Stir in the vanilla extract and cinnamon.
  4. Pour the honey mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and press it down firmly with the back of a spoon or your hands.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown around the edges.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely before cutting into bars.
  8. Store in an airtight container for up to one week.

2. Peanut Butter and Banana Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups crispy rice cereal
  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Line a baking dish with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the crispy rice cereal and oats.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, heat the peanut butter and honey for 30 seconds, or until melted. Stir in the mashed banana and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the peanut butter mixture over the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  5. Fold in the chopped peanuts and mini chocolate chips (if using).
  6. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and press it down firmly.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until firm.
  8. Cut into bars and store in the refrigerator for up to one week.

3. Chocolate Coconut Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups crispy rice cereal
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Line a baking dish with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the crispy rice cereal and shredded coconut.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat the almond butter, honey, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and salt over low heat until melted and well combined.
  4. Pour the almond butter mixture over the dry ingredients and mix until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Fold in the chopped almonds.
  6. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and press it down firmly.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until firm.
  8. Cut into bars and store in the refrigerator for up to one week.

Now that you have three amazing homemade cereal bar recipes, you can say goodbye to store-bought options. These bars are not only packed with wholesome ingredients but are also incredibly delicious. So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and healthy breakfast, grab one of these homemade cereal bars and start your day right! Enjoy!
