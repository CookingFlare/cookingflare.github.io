Cranberry Orange Bread: A Citrusy Delight


Cranberry Orange Bread is a delightful and refreshing homemade bread that is perfect for breakfast or as a dessert. Its incredible combination of flavors makes it loved by many. If you have never tried making it at home, today is your lucky day. You couldn’t be more prepared for this as I’ve spent hours crafting this guide to ensure you get that perfect Cranberry Orange Bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • ⅓ cup of fresh orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon grated orange zest
  • 1 cup dried cranberries

Instructions

Step 1: Preparation

Before we dive into our recipe, make sure you have gathered all your ingredients. Keep in mind that ingredients at room temperature work best. Make sure to let your butter and egg come to room temperature for a smoother result. Also, the zest should be prepared ahead of time too since we only need a small amount; you can freeze the rest.

Step 2: Mix the dry ingredients

In a medium bowl, mix together 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour, ½ teaspoon baking soda, and ⅛ teaspoon salt. Set aside.

Step 3: Cream the butter and sugar

Using a stand mixer or handheld mixer, cream together ½ cup of unsalted butter, and ¾ cup granulated sugar until it’s light and fluffy. This step is important as it ensures you have a light and airy bread.

Step 4: Add the egg

Add the large room temperature egg into the butter and sugar mixture and continue to beat. This will ensure a smoother consistency.

Step 5: Add the wet ingredients

Slowly add ⅓ cup of fresh orange juice and 1 teaspoon of grated orange zest into the mixture. Continue to blend until everything is well combined.

Step 6: Fold in the dried cranberries

With a gentle hand, fold in 1 cup of the dried cranberries. This ensures that all the bread has a balanced amount of cranberries throughout.

Step 7: Combine wet and dry ingredients

Combine the dry ingredients and wet ingredients gradually. Be careful not to overmix your bread which will create a tougher bread texture.

Step 8: Prepare the loaf pan

Grease an 8 ½ inch x 4 ½ inch loaf pan with butter. This prevents any sticking to the pan during baking.

Step 9: Pour and Bake

Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan evenly and bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for about 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. The Cranberry Orange Bread is done when the center is set and doesn’t jiggle.

Step 10: Cool and Enjoy

Allow the bread to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely. Cut into slices and serve your delightful Cranberry Orange Bread. This is sure to vanish quickly at breakfast time or will be gone soon at a dessert table. If you’d like, you can also pass the bread as a unique addition to a festive holiday meal.

It’s amazing to see how a simple recipe can create a burst of flavor. Enjoy the pleasant combination of citrusy and sweet flavors in every bite of your homemade Cranberry Orange Bread. This bread surely is a star on its own, like a true Citrusy Delight!
