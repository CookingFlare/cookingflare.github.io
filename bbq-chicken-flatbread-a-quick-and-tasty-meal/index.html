BBQ Chicken Flatbread: A Quick and Tasty Meal


Have you ever ended up with unexpected guests on a sunny afternoon? Or, do you simply want a quick and easy meal without spending hours in the kitchen? Fear not, for I’ve got the perfect solution for you – BBQ Chicken Flatbread!

Ingredients

  • 1 refrigerated pizza dough (approx. 12 inches)
  • 1 cup barbeque sauce
  • 1 cup diced chicken, cooked
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Your favorite BBQ spices
  • Cooking oil or cooking spray

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius), to ensure even cooking. This will prevent your flatbread from being overcooked or burnt.
  2. Prepare the pizza dough: Gently roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired flatbread thickness. Make sure to evenly spread the dough and avoid any thin spots. The dough should be large enough to accommodate the bounty of toppings.
  3. Lightly coat the rolled-out dough with cooking oil or use cooking spray to further prevent sticking. This will promote even cooking, ensuring no parts of your flatbread get burnt.
  4. Cook the ground chicken in a pan on the stovetop until it’s fully cooked. Avoid overcooking as this can lead to a dry flatbread topping. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and BBQ spices.
  5. Mix the BBQ sauce, cooked chicken, onion, and minced garlic in a bowl. The addition of the minced garlic and onion provides additional depth to the dish, making it taste more savory and flavorful.
  6. Spread the BBQ mixture evenly over the flattened dough, leaving a slim border around the edges. This ensures even distribution of ingredients and prevents the dough from becoming soggy.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese generously over the BBQ sauce mixture. Cheese adds creaminess and richness to your BBQ Chicken Flatbread, making it a true comfort food.
  8. Place the pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Rotate your baking sheet halfway through to achieve even baking.
  9. Remove the flatbread from the oven and allow it to cool for a couple of minutes. Cutting into the hot flatbread can result in a mushy, gooey mess. It’s best to let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
  10. Slice the BBQ Chicken Flatbread into wedges or squares. As it’s a one-pan meal, it’s a great opportunity to engage the whole family in the cutting and serving process.

BBQ Chicken Flatbread is a scrumptious and stress-free meal. With minimal effort, you’ll have a delicious homemade dinner that will leave your unexpected guests pleasantly surprised and your stomach happily full. Plus, it’s versatile – you can double the recipe and freeze leftovers for a convenient future meal, or switch up the toppings to cater to your preferences. Just remember that the secret to success lies in proper preparation and even cooking to avoid burnt and overcooked spots.

Don’t let your next impromptu visit become a culinary nightmare. Keep some pizza dough on hand and prepare for those unexpected guests with your own signature BBQ Chicken Flatbread! Bon appétit!
