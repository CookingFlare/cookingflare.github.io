Vegan Rice Krispie Treats: Easy and Delicious


When it comes to sweet treats, few things match the nostalgic joy brought by a batch of Rice Krispie Treats. They’re gooey, crispy, and brimming with childhood memories. But what about those following a plant-based lifestyle? Can vegans enjoy this classic dessert without compromising their diet? The answer is a resounding yes! Vegan Rice Krispie Treats are not only possible but also incredibly simple and irresistibly delicious. This recipe shines as a quick snack or an after-dinner delight you’ll make over and over again.

What Makes Rice Krispie Treats Non-Vegan?

Rice Krispie Treats are traditionally made using marshmallows and butter, two ingredients that typically prevent them from being vegan-friendly. Marshmallows often contain gelatin, a product derived from animal sources. Meanwhile, butter comes from dairy, making it non-vegan as well. Luckily, there are plenty of plant-based substitutes that perfectly replicate the taste and texture of these ingredients without any animal products.

Key Ingredients for Vegan Rice Krispie Treats

Creating vegan Rice Krispie Treats starts with finding the right alternatives for your ingredients. Here’s what you’ll need:

Vegan Marshmallows

The cornerstone of Rice Krispie Treats is marshmallows, providing the gooey sweetness. Opt for vegan marshmallows made with tapioca starch instead of gelatin. Brands like Dandies or Trader Joe’s offer fantastic options that work perfectly in this recipe.

Plant-Based Butter

To achieve that classic richness, use a vegan butter or plant-based margarine. There are many brands available, such as Earth Balance or Miyoko’s Creamery, that mimic the creamy consistency and flavor of traditional butter.

Crisped Rice Cereal

Good news: most puffed rice cereals, like classic Rice Krispies, are vegan-friendly. However, always check the label for hidden animal-derived ingredients, such as vitamin D3 or honey. Brands like Nature’s Path offer explicitly vegan options.

How to Make Vegan Rice Krispie Treats

This recipe requires minimal ingredients, short preparation time, and only one saucepan. Whether you’re serving them to guests, family, or enjoying them solo, these vegan Rice Krispie Treats are sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of puffed rice cereal (Rice Krispies or a vegan equivalent)
  • 4 cups of vegan marshmallows
  • 3 tablespoons of plant-based butter or margarine
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional for added flavor)
  • A pinch of sea salt (optional for balancing sweetness)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Your Pan
    Begin by greasing a 9x13-inch baking pan with a small amount of plant-based butter or margarine. This ensures that your treats won’t stick when it’s time to serve. If you prefer easy cleanup, you can line the pan with parchment paper instead.

  2. Melt the Butter
    In a large saucepan over medium heat, melt the plant-based butter. Stir occasionally to prevent it from burning.

  3. Add the Marshmallows
    Reduce the heat slightly before adding vegan marshmallows to the saucepan. Stir continuously as the marshmallows melt into a gooey consistency. This step may take a few minutes, so be patient. Once melted, add the vanilla extract and pinch of sea salt if desired.

  4. Mix in the Cereal
    Remove the saucepan from heat and pour the crisped rice cereal into the melted marshmallow mixture. Use a spatula or wooden spoon to gently combine, ensuring all the cereal is evenly coated with the marshmallow mixture.

  5. Spread into the Pan
    Transfer the mixture into your prepared baking pan. Use the back of your spatula or lightly greased hands to press the mixture evenly into the pan. Do not press too hard; packing the treats too tightly can make them overly dense.

  6. Let Them Cool
    Allow the Rice Krispie Treats to cool at room temperature for about 30 minutes. Once set, cut them into squares or rectangles using a sharp knife. Enjoy your vegan creation!

Tips for Perfect Vegan Rice Krispie Treats

While this recipe is simple, here are a few tips to ensure it comes out perfectly every time:

  • Work Quickly: Melted marshmallow tends to set quickly, so mix the cereal in promptly and efficiently.
  • Customize Your Recipe: Add your own spin by folding in vegan chocolate chips, dried fruit, or a sprinkle of cinnamon for extra flavor.
  • Store Properly: Keep your treats fresh by storing them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days.

Why Vegan Rice Krispie Treats Are a Winner

Vegan Rice Krispie Treats are not only delicious but also a fantastic option for anyone with dietary restrictions. They’re naturally gluten-free (when using gluten-free puffed rice cereal), and free from dairy or other animal products. Whether you’re new to veganism or a seasoned plant-based eater, these easy treats are a fun recipe to add to your repertoire.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use coconut oil instead of plant-based butter?

Absolutely! Coconut oil works well as a butter substitute. However, it does have a distinct flavor, so use refined coconut oil for a neutral taste.

Are vegan marshmallows harder to melt?

Not necessarily! Vegan marshmallows melt just as easily as traditional ones; it just requires some patience and consistent stirring.

Can I make these treats nut-free?

This recipe is naturally nut-free unless your puffed rice cereal or vegan marshmallows contain traces of nuts. Always check packaging labels if you’re cooking for someone with allergies.

Can I freeze Vegan Rice Krispie Treats?

Yes! Freeze them in an airtight container for up to one month. Let them thaw at room temperature before serving.

Final Thoughts

Vegan Rice Krispie Treats prove that indulging in dessert doesn’t mean compromising your values or dietary needs. With just a few simple swaps, this plant-based version of a beloved classic is easy to whip up and satisfies the sweet tooth of vegans and non-vegans alike. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, needing a make-ahead snack, or just craving something to enjoy while binge-watching your favorite show, these treats fit the bill perfectly.

Make a batch to share with friends and family—if you can bring yourself to part with them! Once you take a bite of these gooey, crispy squares, it’s hard to stop at just one.
