Instant Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Hash Recipe: A Crispy and Delicious Breakfast Option


Are you tired of the same old boring breakfast routine? Do you crave a dish that is both crispy and delicious, with a hint of sweetness? Look no further! Today, we’re going to explore the world of Instant Pot cooking and create a mouth-watering Chicken and Sweet Potato Hash recipe that will become your new go-to breakfast option.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Sweet Potatoes

In the Instant Pot, combine the diced sweet potatoes, 1 cup of water, and 1/2 tsp salt. Close the lid and make sure the valve is set to “sealing”. Press the “manual” or “pressure cook” button and set the cooking time to 5 minutes at high pressure. Let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken and Onions

In the same Instant Pot, add the chicken, onions, garlic, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir to combine. Add the olive oil, chicken broth, and water. Close the lid and make sure the valve is set to “sealing”. Press the “manual” or “pressure cook” button and set the cooking time to 8 minutes at high pressure. Let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.

Step 3: Mash the Sweet Potatoes

Using a fork or potato masher, mash the cooked sweet potatoes in a large bowl until they’re smooth and creamy. Set aside.

Step 4: Crisp the Chicken and Onions

Using a spatula, carefully remove the cooked chicken and onions from the Instant Pot and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Broil in the oven for 5-7 minutes, or until the chicken is crispy and golden brown.

Step 5: Assemble the Hash

In a large skillet, combine the mashed sweet potatoes, crispy chicken, and shredded cheese (if using). Stir to combine and cook over medium heat for 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the hash is heated through.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy hash, add a beaten egg to the skillet with the sweet potatoes and chicken and scramble it in.
  • Substitute the chicken with cooked sausage or bacon for added flavor.
  • Add some diced bell peppers or mushrooms to the skillet with the onions for added color and texture.
  • Serve the hash with a fried egg on top or a dollop of sour cream for added richness.

Conclusion

And there you have it - a delicious and crispy Instant Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Hash recipe that’s perfect for breakfast or brunch. With its sweet and savory flavors, this dish is sure to become a crowd-pleaser. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the perfect breakfast option for any day of the week!
