Guest Blog: Top Pizza and Flatbread Recipes from Around the World


In the realm of handcrafted pizzas and flatbreads, it is hard not to fall in love with their versatility and rich history. From the earliest forms of flatbreads to the modern day artisanal pizzas, these delectable dishes continue to thrive in every corner of the world. Let us explore a smorgasbord of international pizza and flatbread recipes that promise to elevate your culinary expertise to the next level.

Summertime Sardinian Flatbreads (Carta di Musica)

The sun-kissed island of Sardinia unveils a culinary gem known as Carta di Musica, a simple yet scrumptious flatbread that pairs perfectly with fresh, summertime produce. Gather your ingredients and prepare:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • Olive oil for brushing

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add the warm water and yeast into the mixture, stirring to create a smooth dough.
  3. Knead and set aside to rise for about 45 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls, similar in size to a tennis ball.
  5. Roll each ball out into a thin disk, approximately 6 inches in diameter.
  6. Use a fork to prick several holes in each disk and brush with olive oil.
  7. Bake at 450째F for roughly 8-10 minutes, until lightly golden.

Serve warm with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper, alongside an assortment of tomatoes, olives, and fresh garden herbs.

Indonesian Rijsttafel-inspired Pizza

The artfully curated experience known as Rijsttafel hails from Indonesia, showcasing a vibrant platter of side dishes to be shared amongst family and friends. Let us pay tribute to this remarkable tradition by crafting an Indonesian Rijsttafel-inspired pizza:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of strong bread flour
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1.5 tsp instant yeast
  • 1.5 tsp sugar
  • 1.25 cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 oz coconut curry sauce
  • 4 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 oz peeled and diced shrimp
  • 4 oz crumbled fried shallots
  • 4 oz sliced Indonesian-style beef satay

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, salt, instant yeast, and sugar.
  2. Gradually add the warm water and olive oil, incorporating into the mixture to create a smooth, elastic dough.
  3. Knead and let the dough rise for 45 minutes.
  4. Preheat your pizza oven or a traditional oven to 500째F.
  5. Roll out the pizza dough to your preferred thickness and spread a generous layer of coconut curry sauce.
  6. Top with shredded mozzarella, shrimp, fried shallots, and sliced satay.
  7. Bake for approximately 8-10 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Slice into bite-sized portions and serve a steaming bowl of sambal oelek on the side for an authentic touch.

Greek Pita with Strained Yogurt and Herbs

Embrace the sun-drenched flavors of Greece with a traditional Pita, lathered in tangy, strained yogurt and fragrant herbs. You’ll find:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • Olive oil for brushing

Strained Yogurt & Herb Topping:

  • 16 oz strained Greek yogurt
  • 1 chopped cucumber (peeled and seeded)
  • 1 clove finely minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp chopped dill
  • 1 tbsp chopped mint
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine flour, salt, sugar, warm water, and yeast into a mixing bowl. Stir to create a smooth dough.
  2. Knead and let it rise for approximately 40 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into balls and roll out into thin, 6-inch disks.
  4. Prick several holes with a fork and brush each disk with olive oil.
  5. Bake at 450째F for 8-10 minutes.
  6. While the pitas are baking, whisk together the strained yogurt, chopped cucumber, minced garlic, dill, and mint.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Split each freshly baked pita and generously slather the inside with the strained yogurt and herb mixture. This dish can be served on its own or stuffed with your favorite proteins like slow-cooked lamb or salmon.

Mexican Pizza de Hoja

Let Mexico’s vibrant colors and flavors influence your next pizza creation with the Mexican Pizza de Hoja:

Ingredients:

  • 1 recipe for pre-made or purchased Mexican flour tortillas
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup leftover slow-cooked chili con carne
  • 1 cup of crumbled queso fresco
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 avocado, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F.
  2. Spread a layer of chili con carne on each tortilla, leaving a thin border around the edges.
  3. Sprinkle a generous layer of shredded mozzarella on top.
  4. Add crumbled queso fresco, then garnish with fresh cilantro and chopped avocado.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for around 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Slice into warm wedges and delight in this savory and satisfying creation.
