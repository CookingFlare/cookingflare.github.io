Beef and Vegetable Soup: A Hearty Comfort with a Nutritious Twist


There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting bowl of soup, especially when it’s packed with hearty beef and nutritious vegetables. This Beef and Vegetable Soup recipe is the perfect blend of flavors and textures, making it a satisfying meal on its own or a delicious starter for any gathering. So, grab your apron, sharpen your knife, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef stew meat and cook until browned on all sides. This will help develop a rich flavor in the soup.

  2. Once the beef is browned, remove it from the pot and set it aside. In the same pot, add the diced onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the minced garlic to the pot and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent it from burning.

  4. Return the beef to the pot and pour in the beef broth and diced tomatoes. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Add the diced potatoes, green beans, corn kernels, dried thyme, and dried rosemary to the pot. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Give everything a good stir, then bring the soup to a boil.

  6. Once the soup is boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the beef is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.

  7. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed. If the soup is too thick, you can add some water or additional beef broth to thin it out.

  8. Ladle the Beef and Vegetable Soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot and enjoy!

The Nutritious Twist

While this Beef and Vegetable Soup is undeniably delicious, it also offers a nutritious twist. By incorporating a variety of vegetables, you’re not only adding vibrant colors to your meal but also boosting its nutritional value. Carrots, celery, green beans, and corn provide essential vitamins and minerals, while the diced tomatoes add a burst of tangy flavor and antioxidants. Plus, the lean beef stew meat offers a good source of protein to keep you satisfied.

Conclusion

Beef and Vegetable Soup is a classic comfort food that never fails to warm the soul. With its tender beef, flavorful broth, and a medley of vegetables, this soup is a perfect balance of taste and nutrition. Whether you’re looking to cozy up on a chilly day or impress your friends and family with a nourishing meal, this recipe is a winner. So, grab a spoon, savor each spoonful, and let the comforting flavors whisk you away to a place of pure culinary bliss.
