Low-Calorie Weight Watchers Cabbage Soup


There’s something undeniably comforting about a warm bowl of soup, especially when it’s packed with flavor, easy to make, and incredibly nutritious. This low-calorie Weight Watchers cabbage soup is all that and more. Whether you’re looking to shed a few pounds, maintain a healthy diet, or simply enjoy a guilt-free yet satisfying meal, this recipe checks all the right boxes.

Cabbage soup has gained a reputation as a go-to meal for those seeking a light but filling dish. Its versatility, simplicity, and affordability make it a favorite for home cooks everywhere. The best part? This particular recipe is both low in calories and high in nutritional value, making it a perfect choice for anyone focusing on balanced eating habits. So, roll up your sleeves, grab a chef’s knife, and get ready to prepare a pot of this delightful cabbage soup.

Why This Cabbage Soup is the Ultimate Healthy Meal

At its core, cabbage soup is a medley of nutrient-dense vegetables simmered in a flavorful broth. It is naturally low in fat and calories, making it a Weight Watchers-approved meal. The star ingredient, cabbage, is a powerhouse of vitamins C and K and is high in fiber, which aids digestion and promotes a feeling of fullness.

Additionally, the inclusion of other vegetables like carrots, celery, and tomatoes enhances the flavor and nutritional profile without adding unnecessary calories. Each ingredient plays a role in creating a harmonious blend of taste and health benefits.

This soup is also highly adaptable, allowing you to tailor it to your preferences while keeping it nutritious. Want to add in some leafy greens, fresh herbs, or even a dash of spice? Go right ahead! Consider this recipe your foundation for endless culinary creativity.

Ingredients You’ll Need to Prepare Low-Calorie Cabbage Soup

One of the best aspects of this recipe is that it calls for simple, everyday ingredients. Many of these are pantry staples, so you likely have most of them on hand. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 medium head of green cabbage: Chopped into bite-sized pieces.
  • 2 medium carrots: Peeled and sliced.
  • 2 stalks of celery: Diced.
  • 1 medium onion: Finely chopped.
  • 3 cloves of garlic: Minced.
  • 1 large bell pepper (any color): Chopped.
  • 1 can (14-ounce) of diced tomatoes: With their juices (use no-salt-added for a lighter option).
  • 4 cups of low-sodium vegetable broth: To keep the soup heart-healthy.
  • 2 cups of water: For the right consistency.
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme: Adds a warm, earthy flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano: For a touch of herbal complexity.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika: Optional but recommended for its subtle smoky hint.
  • Salt and black pepper: To taste.
  • Juice of 1/2 a lemon: For a bright, tangy finish.
  • Fresh parsley: Optional for garnish.

These ingredients are the cornerstone of this recipe, but feel free to make substitutions that fit your tastes or dietary preferences.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Making Weight Watchers Cabbage Soup

Now that you’ve gathered all your ingredients, let’s dive into the step-by-step process to create this wholesome and delicious cabbage soup.

Step 1: Prepare Your Vegetables

Proper preparation is key to ensuring a smooth cooking process. Begin by thoroughly washing all your vegetables. Chop the cabbage, slice the carrots, dice the celery and onion, mince the garlic, and chop the bell pepper. Having everything prepped and ready will make cooking this soup much more efficient.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

In a large soup pot or Dutch oven, heat a splash of olive oil or non-stick cooking spray over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot and sauté for about 2–3 minutes, or until they become soft and fragrant. This simple step lays a flavorful foundation for your soup.

Step 3: Add the Vegetables

Next, add the sliced carrots, diced celery, and chopped bell pepper to the pot. Stir for another 3–4 minutes to soften the veggies slightly. This step also deepens their flavor.

Step 4: Stir in the Spices

Once the vegetables have softened, sprinkle in the dried thyme, oregano, smoked paprika (if using), salt, and black pepper. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices. This evenly distributes the seasoning and infuses the soup with herbal, earthy goodness.

Step 5: Add the Cabbage and Liquids

Now it’s time to incorporate the star of the show: cabbage. Add the chopped cabbage to the pot and pour in the vegetable broth along with water. Stir to combine and bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

Step 6: Include the Diced Tomatoes

Add the can of diced tomatoes, including their juices, to the pot. Stir to mix everything together. At this point, your soup will start to take on a vibrant color and hearty texture.

Step 7: Simmer to Perfection

Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for 30–40 minutes. Stir occasionally to ensure all the vegetables cook evenly. The cabbage will become tender, and the flavors will meld beautifully as the soup simmers.

Step 8: Final Touches

Once the vegetables are tender and the broth is full of flavor, squeeze in the juice of half a lemon. This adds a bright, tangy finish that balances the richness of the soup. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.

Step 9: Serve and Enjoy

Ladle the cabbage soup into bowls and garnish with freshly chopped parsley, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy this wholesome, satisfying dish that’s as healthy as it is delicious.

Tips for Making the Best Cabbage Soup

  1. Choose Fresh Ingredients: Fresh vegetables are crucial to achieving the best flavor and texture in this soup.
  2. Customize the Flavor Profile: If you prefer a spicier soup, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce.
  3. Add Protein if Desired: To make this soup more filling, you can add lean protein such as shredded chicken breast or canned beans.
  4. Store for Later: This cabbage soup stores well in the refrigerator for up to 4 days or in the freezer for up to 3 months. Reheat on the stovetop or in the microwave for a quick, nutritious meal.

Health Benefits of This Low-Calorie Cabbage Soup

This cabbage soup isn’t just delicious—it also offers a plethora of health benefits:

  • Weight Management: Its low-calorie content makes it ideal for those following a weight-loss plan.
  • Rich in Fiber: With cabbage, carrots, and celery, this soup is an excellent source of dietary fiber, supporting digestion and satiety.
  • Packed with Vitamins: Cabbage alone is rich in vitamins C and K, while the other vegetables add even more nutrients to the mix.
  • Heart-Friendly: Using low-sodium broth and avoiding heavy creams keeps this soup light and heart-healthy.

Conclusion

This low-calorie Weight Watchers cabbage soup is a fantastic addition to any meal plan. It’s not only easy to whip up but also incredibly versatile and packed with nutrients. Whether you’re meal-prepping for the week, staying mindful of your health, or simply seeking a warming dish for a cozy night in, this soup has you covered.

Try it out and see how satisfying healthy eating can be. It’s proof that nutritious food can be as comforting and enjoyable as any indulgent dish. So, grab a bowl, savor every flavor-packed spoonful, and relish the fact that you’re nourishing your body while satisfying your taste buds.
