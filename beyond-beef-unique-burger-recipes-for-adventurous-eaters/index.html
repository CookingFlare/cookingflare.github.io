Beyond Beef: Unique Burger Recipes for Adventurous Eaters


Are you tired of the same old beef burgers? Do you crave something more exciting and adventurous? Look no further! Beyond Beef offers a game-changing alternative to traditional beef patties, and we’re here to show you how to take your burger game to the next level with these unique recipes.

The Power of Plant-Based Protein

Beyond Beef is made from plant-based ingredients, carefully crafted to mimic the taste and texture of beef. This innovative product is perfect for vegans, vegetarians, and anyone looking for a meat-free option that’s both delicious and sustainable. With its rich, meaty flavor and tender texture, Beyond Beef is the perfect substitute for traditional beef in your favorite burger recipes.

Recipe 1: Korean BBQ Burger

  • 1 Beyond Beef patty
  • 1 tablespoon Gochujang sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup crispy kimchi slaw
  • 1 sesame seed bun

Grill the Beyond Beef patty and top it with a drizzle of Gochujang sauce, soy sauce, brown sugar, and rice vinegar. Add a sprinkle of chopped green onions and a dollop of crispy kimchi slaw for a flavorful and spicy kick.

Recipe 2: Mediterranean Mash-Up Burger

  • 1 Beyond Beef patty
  • 1 tablespoon hummus
  • 1 tablespoon feta cheese crumbles
  • 1/4 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup artichoke hearts
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 sesame seed bun

Grill the Beyond Beef patty and top it with a dollop of hummus, crumbles of feta cheese, and a sprinkle of chopped Kalamata olives, artichoke hearts, and sun-dried tomatoes. This burger is a flavorful and refreshing twist on traditional Mediterranean cuisine.

Recipe 3: Spicy Southwestern Burger

  • 1 Beyond Beef patty
  • 1 tablespoon chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sour cream
  • 1/4 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapeños
  • 1 sesame seed bun

Grill the Beyond Beef patty and top it with a drizzle of chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, a dollop of sour cream, and a sprinkle of shredded pepper jack cheese, chopped cilantro, and sliced jalapeños. This burger is a spicy and satisfying twist on traditional Southwestern cuisine.

Recipe 4: Indian-Style Butter Chicken Burger

  • 1 Beyond Beef patty
  • 1 tablespoon butter chicken sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup crumbled paneer cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1 sesame seed bun

Grill the Beyond Beef patty and top it with a drizzle of butter chicken sauce, a sprinkle of chopped cilantro, and a dollop of crumbled paneer cheese. Add a slice of cucumber for a refreshing and cooling contrast to the rich flavors of the burger.

Conclusion

With these unique and adventurous recipes, you’ll never have to settle for the same old beef burgers again. Beyond Beef offers a world of possibilities for creative and delicious burgers that are sure to impress even the most discerning palates. So go ahead, get creative, and take your burger game to the next level with these Beyond Beef recipes!
