Delightful Apple Cider Donuts: A Sweet Holiday Breakfast Treat


As the holiday season approaches, the aroma of freshly baked donuts wafting from the kitchen is a surefire way to get everyone in the mood for celebration. And what better way to indulge in this festive treat than with a batch of delightful apple cider donuts? These sweet and savory pastries are the perfect way to start your holiday morning, and with this recipe, you can easily impress your family and friends with your baking skills.

Ingredients

To make these scrumptious donuts, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup apple cider
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting

Instructions

Step 1: Make the Donut Dough

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and yeast. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until a smooth batter forms. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the dough rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

Step 2: Prepare the Apple Cider Glaze

While the dough is rising, prepare the apple cider glaze by whisking together the apple cider and vanilla extract in a small bowl. Set aside.

Step 3: Fry the Donuts

Heat about 2-3 inches of vegetable oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat until it reaches 350°F. Using a cookie scoop or a spoon, drop the dough into the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Fry the donuts for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Use a slotted spoon to remove the donuts from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

Step 4: Glaze the Donuts

Dip the warm donuts into the apple cider glaze, coating them evenly. Place the glazed donuts on a wire rack set over a piece of parchment paper or a silicone mat.

Step 5: Dust with Confectioners’ Sugar

Once the donuts have cooled slightly, dust them with confectioners’ sugar.

Tips and Variations

  • To make these donuts extra special, try adding a few drops of cinnamon or nutmeg to the batter for an extra boost of flavor.
  • If you want to make these donuts ahead of time, you can fry and glaze them, then store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. Simply dust with confectioners’ sugar before serving.
  • To make these donuts more festive, try using red or green food coloring to dye the apple cider glaze.

Conclusion

These delightful apple cider donuts are the perfect treat to start your holiday morning. With their sweet and savory flavors, they are sure to delight both kids and adults alike. And with this recipe, you can easily impress your family and friends with your baking skills. So go ahead, give these donuts a try, and make this holiday season one to remember!
