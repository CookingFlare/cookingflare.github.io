Soft and Buttery Crescent Rolls: A Versatile Favorite


Who doesn’t love the smell of freshly baked bread wafting through the house? One of my all-time favorite bread recipes is the classic Soft and Buttery Crescent Rolls. These delightful rolls are not only incredibly soft and buttery, but they are also incredibly versatile. Whether you enjoy them as a side with your favorite soup or salad, use them as a base for mini sandwiches, or simply enjoy them on their own, these rolls are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

To make these Soft and Buttery Crescent Rolls, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, instant yeast, and salt. Mix well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. In a separate smaller bowl, whisk together the melted butter, warm milk, and eggs until well combined.

  3. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the wet ingredients. Using a wooden spoon or your hands, mix until a sticky dough forms.

  4. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough back into the mixing bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Allow it to rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Turn it out onto a floured surface and divide it into 16 equal pieces.

  7. Take one piece of dough and roll it into a long rope shape. Starting from one end, roll the dough up into a crescent shape. Repeat with the remaining pieces of dough.

  8. Place the shaped crescent rolls onto a greased baking sheet, leaving some space between each roll. Cover them with a kitchen towel and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.

  9. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Once the rolls have finished their final rise, bake them in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.

  10. Remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can brush the rolls with melted butter and sprinkle them with garlic powder or your favorite herbs before baking.
  • If you prefer a sweeter roll, you can add a tablespoon of honey or maple syrup to the dough mixture.

Conclusion

Soft and Buttery Crescent Rolls are a versatile favorite that can elevate any meal. With their soft, pillowy texture and buttery flavor, they are sure to become a staple in your recipe repertoire. Whether you enjoy them as a side dish, use them as a base for mini sandwiches, or simply savor them on their own, these rolls are a true delight.

So, why not roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and bake a batch of these irresistible Soft and Buttery Crescent Rolls? Your taste buds will thank you!
