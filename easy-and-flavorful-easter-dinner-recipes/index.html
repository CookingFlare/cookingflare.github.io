Easy and Flavorful Easter Dinner Recipes


Are you looking to impress your guests this Easter with a delicious and stress-free dinner? Look no further! We’ve curated a selection of easy and flavorful Easter dinner recipes that will have everyone at your table asking for seconds. From succulent mains to delightful sides and decadent desserts, this menu has everything you need to create a memorable Easter feast.

Herb-Crusted Roast Lamb

Ingredients:

  • 1 5-pound boneless leg of lamb
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, pepper, and olive oil to create a paste.
  3. Rub the paste all over the lamb, ensuring it’s evenly coated.
  4. Place the lamb in a roasting pan and roast for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C) for medium-rare.
  5. Let the lamb rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Lemon Herb Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh dill, chopped
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, toss the potatoes with olive oil, parsley, dill, lemon zest, salt, and pepper until well coated.
  3. Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 30-35 minutes, or until golden and crispy.

Honey Glazed Carrots

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of baby carrots
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the carrots, honey, butter, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook over medium heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the carrots are tender and glazed.
  3. Sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving.

Raspberry Lemon Cheesecake Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter, melted
  • 16 ounces of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup of raspberry preserves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan and bake for 10 minutes.
  4. In another bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  5. Add the eggs, lemon zest, and lemon juice, and mix until well combined.
  6. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the baked crust.
  7. Dollop raspberry preserves on top and swirl with a knife.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until set.
  9. Let it cool before chilling in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
  10. Cut into bars and serve.

With these easy and flavorful Easter dinner recipes, you’ll spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying the company of your loved ones. Whether you’re hosting a small gathering or a big family dinner, these dishes are sure to impress. Happy Easter and happy cooking!
