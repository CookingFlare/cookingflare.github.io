Healthy and Delicious: Quinoa Stuffed Peppers Recipe


Looking for a healthy and delicious meal that is packed with nutrients and flavor? Look no further than this Quinoa Stuffed Peppers recipe! These vibrant and colorful bell peppers are filled with a protein-rich quinoa and vegetable mixture, making it a perfect option for a satisfying and nutritious meal. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  4. Add the cooked quinoa, black beans, and corn kernels to the skillet. Stir well to combine.

  5. Sprinkle in the cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until the spices are evenly distributed.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the mixture cool slightly.

  7. Spoon the quinoa and vegetable mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers, packing it tightly.

  8. Place the stuffed peppers on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and slightly charred.

  9. Once cooked, remove the peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

These Quinoa Stuffed Peppers can be enjoyed as a main course or as a side dish. They are delicious on their own, but you can also pair them with a fresh green salad or some crusty bread for a complete meal. They are perfect for lunch or dinner and can even be prepared ahead of time and reheated for a quick and easy meal during the week.

Nutritional Information

Each serving of this Quinoa Stuffed Peppers recipe contains approximately:

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fat: 4g
  • Fiber: 8g

Conclusion

With its vibrant colors, robust flavors, and nutritious ingredients, this Quinoa Stuffed Peppers recipe is a winner in both taste and health. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed by everyone, regardless of their dietary preferences. So, why not give it a try and add a healthy twist to your next meal? Your taste buds and your body will thank you!

Remember to experiment with different spices and toppings to make this recipe your own. Enjoy the process of cooking and savoring this delicious and nutritious meal. Happy cooking!
