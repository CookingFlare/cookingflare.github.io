Hearty and Comforting: Chicken and Rice Casserole


There’s nothing quite like a hearty casserole to warm you up from the inside out, and when it comes to comfort food, chicken and rice casserole takes the crown. This classic dish is a staple in many households, and for good reason. It’s easy to make, incredibly versatile, and oh-so-satisfying. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd, meal prepping for the week, or simply craving a cozy dinner, this chicken and rice casserole recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

To start off, let’s gather our ingredients:

  • 2 cups of long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup of diced onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat and Prepare

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set it aside.

Step 2: Cook the Rice

In a large saucepan, bring the chicken broth to a boil. Stir in the rice, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed. Fluff the rice with a fork and transfer it to the prepared baking dish.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken and Vegetables

In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until it’s no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

In the same skillet, add the diced carrots, peas, and onion. Sauté until the vegetables are tender. Add the minced garlic, dried thyme, and dried oregano, and cook for an additional minute.

Step 4: Make the Creamy Sauce

Sprinkle the flour over the vegetable mixture and stir to combine. Gradually pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in 1 1/2 cups of the shredded cheddar cheese until melted.

Step 5: Assemble the Casserole

Pour the creamy vegetable mixture over the cooked rice in the baking dish and gently stir to combine. Arrange the cooked chicken on top of the rice mixture.

Step 6: Bake and Serve

Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese over the casserole. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

Garnish the casserole with chopped fresh parsley before serving.

Conclusion

This chicken and rice casserole is a true crowd-pleaser, and it’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of tender chicken, fluffy rice, and creamy sauce is simply irresistible. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak in some veggies for the picky eaters in your life. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner, a potluck, or just craving some comfort food, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire.

So, next time you’re in need of a comforting and satisfying dish, give this chicken and rice casserole a try. It’s a timeless classic that never disappoints.
