Dutch Pancakes and Savory Pies


Are you tired of the same old brunch options? Looking for something unique and flavorful to impress your guests? Well, look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore the delightful world of Dutch Pancakes and Savory Pies. These two dishes may seem simple, but they pack a punch when it comes to taste and versatility. So, let’s dive right in and discover how you can create a memorable brunch experience with these Dutch classics.

Dutch Pancakes: Fluffy Delights That Will Leave You Craving for More

Dutch Pancakes, also known as “Pannenkoeken,” are a popular dish in the Netherlands. Unlike their American counterparts, Dutch Pancakes are thinner and larger, almost resembling crepes. But don’t be fooled by their appearance, as these pancakes are incredibly versatile. You can enjoy them sweet or savory, making them a great choice for any occasion.

Classic Sweet Dutch Pancakes

To start, let’s explore the classic sweet Dutch Pancakes. They are incredibly easy to make and require just a few basic ingredients. Here’s how you can whip up a batch of fluffy delights:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter, for greasing the pan

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, whisking constantly until a smooth batter forms.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and melt a small pat of butter.
  5. Pour a ladleful of batter into the skillet and swirl it around to evenly distribute the batter.
  6. Cook the pancake for about 2-3 minutes until the edges start to brown and the center is set.
  7. Flip the pancake and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more butter to the skillet as needed.

Once your Dutch Pancakes are ready, it’s time to get creative with the toppings. You can go traditional and serve them with a sprinkling of powdered sugar and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Or, you can experiment with various fruits, syrups, and even whipped cream. The possibilities are endless!

Savory Dutch Pancakes: A Twist That Will Delight Your Taste Buds

Now that we’ve satisfied our sweet tooth, let’s delve into the world of savory Dutch Pancakes. These pancakes are perfect for a hearty brunch or a light dinner. Here’s a delicious recipe that combines the richness of cheese and the savory goodness of bacon:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded Gouda cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked and crumbled bacon
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Butter, for greasing the pan

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as the classic sweet Dutch Pancakes for preparing the batter.
  2. Stir in the shredded Gouda cheese, crumbled bacon, and chopped green onions into the batter.
  3. Season the batter with salt and pepper, according to your taste.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and melt a small pat of butter.
  5. Pour a ladleful of batter into the skillet and swirl it around to evenly distribute the batter.
  6. Cook the pancake for about 2-3 minutes until the edges start to brown and the center is set.
  7. Flip the pancake and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more butter to the skillet as needed.

Once cooked, these savory Dutch Pancakes are best enjoyed with a dollop of sour cream or a drizzle of tangy hollandaise sauce. Serve them alongside a fresh green salad, and you have a complete and satisfying meal.

Savory Pies: A Delightful Combination of Flavors and Textures

Moving on from Dutch Pancakes, let’s explore another Dutch favorite: Savory Pies. These pies are a true comfort food, with a crispy crust and a delicious filling that can be customized to your liking. Here’s a recipe for a classic Dutch Savory Pie that will surely impress your family and friends:

Ingredients:

  • 2 sheets of prepared puff pastry
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, carrot, peas, and dried thyme to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are softened.
  4. Sprinkle the flour over the mixture and stir well to combine.
  5. Stir in the tomato paste and beef broth, and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook the mixture for a few more minutes until it thickens slightly.
  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the mixture cool.
  8. Roll out one sheet of puff pastry and line a pie dish with it.
  9. Spoon the cooled meat mixture into the pie dish and spread it evenly.
  10. Roll out the second sheet of puff pastry and place it on top of the filling.
  11. Trim any excess pastry and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  12. Brush the top of the pie with the beaten egg wash.
  13. Use a sharp knife to make a few small slits on the top of the pie to allow steam to escape.
  14. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and crisp.

Serve this delicious Dutch Savory Pie hot, with a side of fresh salad or steamed vegetables. It’s a hearty and satisfying meal that will leave everyone asking for seconds.

Conclusion

Dutch Pancakes and Savory Pies are not only delicious but also a great way to explore the rich culinary traditions of the Netherlands. Whether you prefer sweet or savory, these dishes offer endless possibilities for customization and experimentation. So, the next time you’re planning a brunch or a cozy dinner, consider adding a touch of Dutch flair to your menu. Trust us, your taste buds will thank you!

Now that you have these fantastic recipes in your arsenal, it’s time to get cooking. Gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and let the magic unfold in your kitchen. Enjoy the process and savor the flavors of these Dutch classics. Happy cooking!
