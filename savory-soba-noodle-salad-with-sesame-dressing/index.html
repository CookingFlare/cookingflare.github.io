Savory Soba Noodle Salad with Sesame Dressing


If you’re looking for a refreshing and satisfying dish that’s perfect for warm weather, look no further than this savory soba noodle salad with sesame dressing. This Japanese-inspired salad is a delightful blend of flavors and textures, making it a crowd-pleaser for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer BBQ or simply craving a light and flavorful meal, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 8 ounces of soba noodles
  • 1 cup of shredded carrots
  • 1 cup of thinly sliced cucumber
  • 1 cup of edamame, shelled
  • 1/4 cup of chopped scallions
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of toasted sesame seeds

For the Sesame Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Cook the Soba Noodles:

    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil and add the soba noodles. Cook according to the package instructions, then drain and rinse the noodles under cold water. This will stop the cooking process and prevent them from sticking together.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables:

    • In a large bowl, combine the shredded carrots, sliced cucumber, edamame, chopped scallions, and cilantro. Toss the vegetables together to mix them evenly.

  3. Make the Sesame Dressing:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, honey, minced garlic, grated ginger, and sesame seeds. This flavorful dressing will elevate the entire salad with its umami-rich taste.

  4. Assemble the Salad:

    • Add the cooked and cooled soba noodles to the bowl of vegetables. Pour the sesame dressing over the noodles and vegetables, then gently toss everything together until well combined.

  5. Chill and Garnish:

    • Cover the salad and let it chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, sprinkle the toasted sesame seeds on top for a delightful crunch and extra nuttiness.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once chilled, give the salad a final toss and transfer it to a serving dish. This savory soba noodle salad with sesame dressing is ready to be enjoyed as a main dish or a delightful side to complement any meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a heartier version of this salad, add grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a protein boost.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable mix based on your preferences. Thinly sliced bell peppers, snap peas, or shredded cabbage would all make excellent additions.
  • If you’re a fan of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the salad for a spicy kick.

Final Thoughts

This savory soba noodle salad with sesame dressing is a celebration of vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients. The nuttiness of the soba noodles, combined with the freshness of the vegetables and the richness of the sesame dressing, creates a harmonious dish that’s as pleasing to the palate as it is to the eye. Whether you’re a fan of Japanese cuisine or simply love exploring new flavors, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the art of noodle salad making, and delight in every forkful of this delightful creation. Cheers to good food and happy cooking!
