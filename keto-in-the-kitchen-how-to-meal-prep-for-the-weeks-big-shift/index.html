Keto in the Kitchen: How to Meal Prep for the Week's Big Shift


Meal prepping is a fantastic way to save time, stay organized, and achieve your health goals – especially when transitioning to a Keto diet. This week’s meal prep plan is designed to optimize your macros, reduce cravings, and keep you feeling energetic and satiated. Let’s dive right in!

Day 1: Fluffy Keto Pancakes

Start your week off right with creamy, fluffy pancakes that are packed with fiber and healthy fats. With just a few key ingredients, you’ll create a delicious breakfast that’ll leave you feeling full and energized.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Butter or ghee, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the almond flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, cream, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  3. Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry, stirring well to combine.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and melt a small amount of butter or ghee.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter into the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the edges begin to crisp.
  6. Flip pancake and cook an additional 1-2 minutes before removing from heat.
  7. Repeat with remaining batter.

Day 2: Creamy Chicken Bacon Chowder

Keep the momentum going with this warming and cozy chicken bacon chowder. Perfect to reheat for lunch, this recipe is packed full of low-carb vegetables and protein.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 lb chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 lb bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups cauliflower, chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp chicken bouillon powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  2. Remove the chicken and set aside, then add the bacon, onion, and garlic to the pot. Cook until softened.
  3. Return the chicken to the pot and add the cauliflower, chicken broth, and bouillon powder.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until cauliflower is tender.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and cook an additional 5 minutes before removing from heat.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste and garnish with chopped green onions.

Day 3: Keto Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken

Channel your inner Julia Child with this elegant and savory spin on a classic. Stuffed with fresh spinach and tangy feta, this dish combines the best aspects of Mediterranean cooking with tried-and-true Keto principles.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut a pocket into each chicken breast, being careful not to cut all the way through.
  3. Stuff each chicken breast with the spinach and feta, then close with toothpicks.
  4. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  5. Brush the chicken with the oil mixture and cook for 25-30 minutes, or until juices run clear.
  6. Remove toothpicks and serve.

Day 4: Avocado Chicken Salad Lettuce Wraps

Pack yourself a refreshing and delish lettuce wrap to munch on during your busy day. You’ll thank yourself later for preparing this easy and on-the-go salad.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Large lettuce leaves, for wrapping

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, avocado, onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stir well to combine and adjust seasonings as needed.
  3. Spoon mixture into lettuce leaves and wrap.

Day 5: Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon

Upgrade your side dish routine with this stupendous and savory combination of crispy bacon, tender Brussels sprouts, and a hint of garlic. Oh, YUM!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 6 slices bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with foil or parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the Brussels sprouts, bacon, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture evenly onto the baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until Brussels sprouts are tender and bacon is crispy.

Day 6: Easy Keto Alfredo

Comfort food doesn’t stop at the door of Keto. South your posture cravings and streamline your dinner with a simplified, delectable Alfredo recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 oz zoodles (zucchini noodles)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir well and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until slightly thickened.
  4. Toss zoodles in Alfredo sauce and serve hot.

Day 7: Lemon Blueberry Muffins

Indulge your sweet tooth without compromising your dietary goals. These blissful Blueberry Muffins arrange the perfect blend of flavors and textures to awaken your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup erythritol
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with papers.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the almond flour, erythritol, baking powder, and cinnamon.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, butter, cream, and vanilla.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into the dry and stir well to combine.
  5. Gently fold in the blueberries, lemon zest, and juice.
  6. Fill muffin papers 2/3 full and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden and firm to the touch.

Congratulations – you’ve successfully covered all your bases for the week by getting your Keto meal prep on point! Remember that meal prepping is simply a tool to make your journey easier and more enjoyable. Feel free to tweak this plan, add, or subtract recipes as needed. Have fun in the kitchen and enjoy your well-deserved success!
