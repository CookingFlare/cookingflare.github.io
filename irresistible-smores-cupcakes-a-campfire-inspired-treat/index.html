Irresistible S'mores Cupcakes: A Campfire-Inspired Treat


If you love the classic campfire treat of gooey marshmallows, rich chocolate, and crunchy graham crackers, then you’re in for a real treat with these irresistible S’mores Cupcakes. This delightful twist on the traditional s’mores brings all the flavors of a campfire favorite into a handheld, delectable dessert. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ, a birthday party, or simply craving a sweet indulgence, these cupcakes are sure to impress and satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Marshmallow Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 7 oz marshmallow fluff

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 8 oz semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream

For the Topping:

  • Graham cracker crumbs
  • Mini marshmallows

Instructions

Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a cupcake tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Stir in the boiling water, the batter will be thin but that’s okay.
  5. Fill each cupcake liner about 2/3 full with batter.
  6. Bake for 20-22 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely before filling and frosting.

Marshmallow Filling:

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar and vanilla extract, beating until smooth.
  3. Add the marshmallow fluff and beat until well combined.

Chocolate Ganache:

  1. Place the chopped chocolate in a heatproof bowl.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.
  3. Pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate and let it sit for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Gently whisk the mixture until smooth and shiny.

Assembly:

  1. Using a cupcake corer or a sharp knife, remove the center of each cupcake and fill it with the marshmallow filling.
  2. Dip the top of each cupcake into the chocolate ganache, allowing any excess to drip off.
  3. Sprinkle graham cracker crumbs on top of the ganache, then place a few mini marshmallows on top.
  4. For an extra touch, you can lightly toast the marshmallows with a kitchen torch.

Serving and Storage

These S’mores Cupcakes are best enjoyed the day they are made, but you can store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. Be sure to let them come to room temperature before serving for the best taste and texture.

Conclusion

These irresistible S’mores Cupcakes are a delightful way to capture the essence of a campfire treat in a convenient and delicious dessert. Whether you’re reminiscing about summer nights by the fire or simply craving a sweet and satisfying treat, these cupcakes are sure to be a hit. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible flavors of S’mores Cupcakes.
