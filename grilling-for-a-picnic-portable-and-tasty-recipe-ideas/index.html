Grilling for a Picnic: Portable and Tasty Recipe Ideas


Summer is here, and that means it’s time for picnics in the great outdoors! One of the best ways to enjoy a picnic is to fire up the grill and cook up some delicious food on the go. In this blog post, we’ll explore some portable and tasty recipe ideas for grilling at your next picnic.

1. Grilled Chicken Skewers

Grilled chicken skewers are a classic picnic favorite that are easy to prepare and always a hit. Start by marinating chicken breast pieces in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and your favorite herbs and spices. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers and grill them until they are cooked through and slightly charred. Serve them with a refreshing cucumber and yogurt dip for a cool and tangy accompaniment.

2. Grilled Vegetable Kebabs

For the vegetarians in your group, grilled vegetable kebabs are a fantastic option. Cut a variety of colorful vegetables such as bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms, and cherry tomatoes into bite-sized pieces. Toss them with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper. Skewer the vegetables and grill them until they are tender and slightly caramelized. The smoky flavors of the grill will enhance the natural sweetness of the vegetables, creating a mouthwatering dish.

3. Grilled Shrimp Tacos

If you’re looking for a portable and flavorful option, grilled shrimp tacos are the way to go. Season jumbo shrimp with a blend of spices, including chili powder, cumin, paprika, and garlic powder. Grill the shrimp until they are pink and slightly charred. Warm up some tortillas on the grill, and assemble the tacos with the grilled shrimp, fresh avocado slices, shredded lettuce, and a drizzle of tangy lime crema. These tacos are sure to be a crowd-pleaser!

4. Grilled Fruit Skewers

Don’t forget dessert! Grilled fruit skewers are a delightful and healthy way to end your picnic meal. Choose your favorite fruits such as pineapple chunks, watermelon cubes, and peach slices. Thread them onto skewers and grill them until they have grill marks and are slightly caramelized. The heat of the grill will intensify the natural sweetness of the fruits, creating a delicious and refreshing dessert. Serve them with a dollop of Greek yogurt or a sprinkle of cinnamon for an extra touch of flavor.

5. Grilled Halloumi and Vegetable Wraps

For a hearty and satisfying option, try grilled halloumi and vegetable wraps. Slice halloumi cheese and marinate it in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, and chopped fresh herbs. Grill the marinated halloumi cheese along with a selection of your favorite vegetables such as eggplant, bell peppers, and red onions. Once everything is cooked to perfection, assemble the wraps with a smear of hummus, grilled vegetables, and the grilled halloumi. These wraps are a great choice for a filling and delicious picnic meal.

Remember to pack your grilling essentials such as charcoal, lighter fluid, and grilling utensils. Bring along some extra marinades and sauces for added flavor options. With these portable and tasty grilling recipe ideas, you’re all set to have a memorable picnic with family and friends. Happy grilling and happy picnicking!
