Savory One-Pot Beef and Noodle Skillet: A Comforting Classic


There’s something incredibly satisfying about a one-pot meal. It’s convenient, easy to clean up, and packed with flavor. And when it comes to comfort food, few dishes can beat the classic combination of beef and noodles. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create a delicious and hearty Savory One-Pot Beef and Noodle Skillet that will warm your soul and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 8 ounces wide egg noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef stew meat and cook until browned on all sides. This will help develop a rich flavor in the dish. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant. Then, add the sliced carrots, celery, and mushrooms. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the tomato paste to the empty space. Cook the tomato paste for a minute or two to enhance its flavor. Then, mix it with the vegetables.

  4. Return the browned beef to the skillet and stir well to combine with the vegetables and tomato paste. Pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using). The red wine adds a depth of flavor, but you can omit it if you prefer.

  5. Add the dried thyme, dried rosemary, and bay leaf to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Give everything a good stir and bring the mixture to a boil.

  6. Once the mixture is boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the skillet. Simmer for about 1 hour, or until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  7. While the beef is simmering, cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  8. Once the beef is tender, remove the bay leaf from the skillet. Add the cooked egg noodles to the skillet and toss to coat them in the flavorful sauce.

  9. Serve the Savory One-Pot Beef and Noodle Skillet hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley. The combination of tender beef, savory vegetables, and perfectly cooked noodles will surely satisfy your cravings for a comforting, classic dish.

Conclusion

This Savory One-Pot Beef and Noodle Skillet is a true comfort food classic that brings together the richness of beef, the earthiness of mushrooms, and the heartiness of egg noodles. The flavors meld together in one skillet, creating a dish that is both satisfying and easy to prepare. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. Give it a try and let the comforting flavors warm your soul.
