Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shells: An Irresistible Weeknight Dinner


Are you looking for a delicious and comforting meal that’s quick and easy to make on a busy weeknight? Look no further than this creamy garlic parmesan shells recipe. It’s a perfect combination of creamy, cheesy, and garlicky flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak some veggies into your meal, making it a wholesome dinner option for the whole family.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to make this irresistible creamy garlic parmesan shells:

  • 1 pound medium pasta shells
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Start by cooking the pasta shells according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant. Sprinkle the flour over the garlic and butter, and whisk constantly for 1-2 minutes to create a roux.

  3. Add the Milk: Slowly pour in the whole milk while continuing to whisk, ensuring there are no lumps. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

  4. Season and Cheese: Season the sauce with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Then, stir in the grated parmesan and shredded mozzarella, allowing the cheese to melt and the sauce to become creamy.

  5. Add the Veggies and Pasta: Once the sauce is creamy, add the chopped spinach and halved cherry tomatoes to the skillet. Let them cook for a couple of minutes until the spinach wilts slightly. Then, add the cooked pasta shells to the skillet and gently toss to coat everything in the creamy sauce.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the creamy garlic parmesan shells to a serving dish, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot.

Tips for Perfection

  • Don’t Overcook the Pasta: Be sure to cook the pasta shells al dente, so they hold their shape and texture when mixed with the creamy sauce.

  • Fresh Ingredients: Using fresh garlic, spinach, and cherry tomatoes will enhance the flavors of the dish, giving it a vibrant and wholesome taste.

  • Customize with Protein: If you want to add some protein, consider tossing in some grilled chicken or shrimp for a heartier meal.

Final Thoughts

Creamy garlic parmesan shells are a wonderful option for a satisfying weeknight dinner. The creamy sauce combined with the savory garlic and parmesan flavors creates a dish that’s both comforting and indulgent. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a casual dinner with friends, this recipe is sure to impress. So, next time you’re craving a creamy, cheesy delight, give this recipe a try and enjoy the irresistible flavors of this delightful dish!
