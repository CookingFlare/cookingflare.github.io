Easy and Creamy Cheddar Grits with Bacon


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful dish that’s perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even dinner, then you’re in for a treat! This recipe for easy and creamy cheddar grits with bacon is a true crowd-pleaser. The creamy texture of the grits combined with the rich, sharp flavor of cheddar cheese and the savory goodness of bacon makes this dish a standout favorite. Whether you’re a grits enthusiast or new to the world of this Southern staple, this recipe is sure to win you over.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of stone-ground grits
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped scallions or chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by cooking the bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until it’s crisp and golden brown. Once done, transfer the bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to drain the excess fat. Set it aside for later use.

  2. In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a boil. Once boiling, gradually whisk in the stone-ground grits. Reduce the heat to low and let the grits simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 20-25 minutes or until they are thick and creamy.

  3. Stir in the whole milk, shredded sharp cheddar cheese, and unsalted butter into the cooked grits. Continue to stir until the cheese has melted, and the mixture is smooth and creamy.

  4. Season the cheddar grits with salt and pepper to taste, keeping in mind that the bacon will also add some saltiness to the dish. Feel free to adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

  5. Once the grits are ready, spoon them into serving bowls, and top each portion with the crispy bacon pieces. If desired, garnish with chopped scallions or chives for a pop of freshness and color.

  6. Serve the easy and creamy cheddar grits with bacon while they’re still warm and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra flavor boost, consider adding a pinch of cayenne pepper or a dash of hot sauce to the grits for a subtle kick.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version of this dish, simply omit the bacon and sauté some finely diced onions and bell peppers in a little olive oil until they’re tender and caramelized. Use this mixture as a flavorful topping for the cheddar grits.

Final Thoughts

Easy and creamy cheddar grits with bacon are a simple yet indulgent dish that brings together the heartiness of grits, the richness of cheddar cheese, and the irresistible appeal of crispy bacon. This recipe is a wonderful way to elevate a humble ingredient into a standout dish that’s sure to impress. Whether you serve it as a comforting breakfast, a satisfying brunch, or a delightful side dish, these cheddar grits with bacon are bound to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, get cooking, and savor every spoonful of this delicious creation!
