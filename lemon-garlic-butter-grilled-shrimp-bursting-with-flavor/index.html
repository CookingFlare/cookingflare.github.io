Lemon Garlic Butter Grilled Shrimp: Bursting with Flavor


Grilled shrimp is a favorite for many seafood lovers, and when you combine it with the irresistible flavors of lemon, garlic, and butter, you have a dish that is truly bursting with flavor. This recipe is simple yet elegant, making it perfect for a casual backyard barbecue or a special dinner with friends. So fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using a gas grill, preheat it for about 10 minutes. If you’re using a charcoal grill, make sure the coals are fully lit and covered with gray ash.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon juice, melted butter, olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  3. Thread the shrimp onto skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each shrimp. This will allow them to cook evenly on the grill.

  4. Brush the garlic butter mixture onto both sides of the shrimp skewers, making sure to coat them thoroughly. This will infuse the shrimp with the delicious flavors of garlic, lemon, and butter.

  5. Place the shrimp skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook them, as they can become tough and rubbery.

  6. Once the shrimp are cooked through, remove them from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter. Sprinkle fresh parsley over the top for a touch of color and freshness.

  7. Serve the lemon garlic butter grilled shrimp hot, alongside your favorite side dishes. They pair well with a crisp green salad, grilled vegetables, or even a simple pasta dish. The possibilities are endless!

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have access to a grill, you can also cook these shrimp skewers on a stovetop grill pan or even in the oven under the broiler. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

  • Feel free to customize the flavors by adding a pinch of chili flakes or a sprinkle of your favorite herbs and spices. The lemon garlic butter is a versatile base that can be easily adapted to suit your taste preferences.

  • For an extra burst of freshness, squeeze a little extra lemon juice over the cooked shrimp just before serving. This will enhance the citrus flavor and brighten up the dish.

  • If you’re serving these shrimp as an appetizer, you can leave them on the skewers for easy handling. Otherwise, feel free to remove them from the skewers and serve them as a main course.

  • Leftover grilled shrimp can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. They can be enjoyed cold or reheated gently in a skillet or microwave.

So there you have it - a simple yet sensational recipe for lemon garlic butter grilled shrimp. With just a few ingredients and a hot grill, you can create a dish that is bursting with flavor and sure to impress your guests. So go ahead, give it a try, and let your taste buds dance with delight!
