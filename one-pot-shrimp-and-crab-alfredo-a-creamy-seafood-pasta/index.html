One-Pot Shrimp and Crab Alfredo: A Creamy Seafood Pasta


If you’re a seafood lover and a fan of creamy pasta dishes, then you’re in for a treat with this One-Pot Shrimp and Crab Alfredo recipe. This dish is a delightful combination of succulent shrimp, tender crab meat, and a rich, velvety Alfredo sauce, all cooked together in one pot for easy preparation and minimal cleanup. Not only is it a flavorful and satisfying meal, but it’s also a perfect dish to impress your family and friends at gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

To create this indulgent seafood pasta, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 ounces of lump crab meat
  • 12 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prep the Ingredients

Before you start cooking, ensure that all the ingredients are prepped and ready to go. Thaw the shrimp if frozen, peel and devein them, and set them aside. Drain the lump crab meat and gently pick through it to remove any shell fragments. Mince the garlic, finely chop the onion, and grate the Parmesan cheese if not using pre-grated.

Step 2: Cook the Seafood

In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion, and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Next, add the shrimp to the pot and cook until they turn pink and opaque, then remove them from the pot and set them aside. In the same pot, add the lump crab meat and gently warm it through, being careful not to break up the crab meat too much. Once heated, remove the crab meat from the pot and set it aside with the shrimp.

Step 3: Prepare the Alfredo Sauce

Using the same pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until the cheese has fully melted and the sauce has thickened. If desired, add a teaspoon of red pepper flakes to the sauce for a hint of heat. Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste.

Step 4: Cook the Pasta

Add the fettuccine pasta to the pot with the Alfredo sauce, ensuring that the pasta is fully submerged in the sauce. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, stirring occasionally to prevent it from sticking to the bottom of the pot. Once the pasta is al dente and has absorbed the creamy sauce, return the cooked shrimp and crab meat to the pot, gently folding them into the pasta and sauce.

Step 5: Serve and Garnish

Once the seafood pasta is ready, remove the pot from the heat. Serve the One-Pot Shrimp and Crab Alfredo in individual bowls, garnishing each serving with a sprinkle of fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness. Pair this delectable dish with a crisp white wine or a side of garlic bread for a complete dining experience.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this One-Pot Shrimp and Crab Alfredo recipe is a fantastic way to indulge in the luxurious flavors of seafood and creamy Alfredo sauce without the hassle of multiple pots and pans. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a comforting and satisfying meal, this dish is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied. Give this recipe a try and experience the magic of a creamy seafood pasta made easy in just one pot!
