Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan Recipes for Italian Comfort


If you’re craving the classic flavors of Italy but don’t have the time to stand over a stove, then slow cooker chicken parmesan is the perfect solution. This dish takes the traditional flavors of chicken parmesan and transforms them into a convenient and comforting slow-cooked meal. The tender chicken, rich tomato sauce, and gooey cheese come together to create a truly satisfying Italian comfort food experience. In this blog post, we’ll explore two mouth-watering slow cooker chicken parmesan recipes that will bring the taste of Italy right to your kitchen.

Classic Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, dried basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Dredge each chicken breast in the breadcrumb mixture, ensuring that it is evenly coated.
  3. Place the coated chicken breasts in the slow cooker and pour the marinara sauce over them.
  4. Cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the chicken during the last 15 minutes of cooking.
  6. Once the cheese is melted and bubbly, garnish with fresh basil and serve over cooked pasta or with crusty bread.

This classic slow cooker chicken parmesan recipe captures the essence of the beloved Italian dish while offering the convenience of a slow-cooked meal. The chicken becomes incredibly tender, absorbing the flavors of the marinara sauce and herbs, while the melted mozzarella adds a delightful richness to each bite.

Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 cup of panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the diced chicken, panko breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Transfer the chicken mixture to the slow cooker and pour the marinara sauce over it.
  3. Cook on low for 4-5 hours or on high for 2-3 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and the casserole is bubbling.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the casserole and cook for an additional 15-20 minutes, until the cheese is melted and golden.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and serve over cooked spaghetti or alongside a fresh green salad.

This slow cooker chicken parmesan casserole offers a delightful twist on the traditional dish, turning it into a comforting and easy-to-prepare meal. The diced chicken ensures that each bite is packed with flavor, while the golden, bubbly cheese adds a satisfying finishing touch to this Italian-inspired creation.

Whether you opt for the classic slow cooker chicken parmesan or the convenient casserole version, these recipes are sure to bring the warmth and comfort of Italian cuisine to your table. By utilizing the slow cooker, you can savor the flavors of this beloved dish without spending hours in the kitchen. Try these recipes for a taste of Italy that’s both convenient and deeply satisfying. Buon appetito!
