Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Bark Cheesecake: A Holiday Showstopper


The holiday season is the perfect time to indulge in decadent desserts, and what better way to impress your friends and family than with a showstopping Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Bark Cheesecake? This luscious dessert combines the rich flavors of chocolate and the refreshing zing of peppermint, making it the ultimate treat for the festive season.

Ingredients

For the Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake Filling

  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

For the Chocolate Peppermint Bark

  • 12 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp peppermint extract
  • 6 candy canes, crushed

For the Topping

  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup crushed candy canes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, mix the chocolate cookie crumbs, sugar, and melted butter until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then set aside to cool.

  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add the vanilla extract and beat in the eggs, one at a time. Mix in the sour cream, flour, and heavy cream until the batter is smooth and creamy.

  4. Pour the cheesecake filling over the cooled crust. Smooth the top with a spatula and tap the pan on the counter to release any air bubbles. Place the pan on a large piece of foil and fold the foil up the sides of the pan. This will prevent any water from leaking into the cheesecake during the water bath.

  5. Place the springform pan into a larger pan and pour hot water into the larger pan until it reaches halfway up the sides of the springform pan. This will create a water bath for the cheesecake, ensuring a moist and creamy texture.

  6. Bake the cheesecake in the preheated oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes. After the baking time, turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door closed for 1 hour. This gradual cooling process will help prevent the cheesecake from cracking.

  7. While the cheesecake is cooling, prepare the chocolate peppermint bark. Melt the dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of simmering water. Stir in the peppermint extract and half of the crushed candy canes. Spread the chocolate mixture onto a parchment-lined baking sheet and sprinkle the remaining crushed candy canes on top. Refrigerate until set, then break into pieces.

  8. For the topping, heat the semi-sweet chocolate chips and heavy cream in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of simmering water, stirring until smooth. Pour the chocolate ganache over the cooled cheesecake and spread it evenly. Place the peppermint bark pieces on top and refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to set.

  9. When ready to serve, carefully release the cheesecake from the springform pan and place it on a serving plate. Sprinkle the crushed candy canes over the top for an extra festive touch.

This Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Bark Cheesecake is a true holiday showstopper, combining the richness of chocolate and the refreshing burst of peppermint in every luscious bite. Whether you’re hosting a festive gathering or simply want to treat your loved ones to something special, this indulgent dessert is sure to be a hit. Enjoy the joyous holiday season with this delightful creation that will leave everyone coming back for more!
