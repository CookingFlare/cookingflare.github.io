Sous Vide Lemon Butter Scallops: A Refreshing Seafood Dish


If you’re a fan of seafood, you’re in for a treat with this delightful recipe for Sous Vide Lemon Butter Scallops. This dish combines the delicate flavors of perfectly cooked scallops with the zesty freshness of lemon and the richness of butter, resulting in a culinary masterpiece that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight.

Ingredients

  • Fresh scallops
  • Unsalted butter
  • Fresh lemon
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Sous Vide Water Bath: Fill a large pot or container with water and set your sous vide precision cooker to 122°F (50°C).

  2. Prepare the Scallops: Rinse the fresh scallops under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel. Season the scallops with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Vacuum Seal the Scallops: Place the seasoned scallops in a single layer in a vacuum-sealable bag. Add a few small cubes of unsalted butter to the bag, and then vacuum seal it, ensuring a tight seal to prevent any water from entering during the cooking process.

  4. Sous Vide Cooking: Once the water bath has reached the set temperature, carefully lower the sealed bag of scallops into the water. Cook the scallops for 30-40 minutes, allowing them to gently cook to perfection.

  5. Prepare the Lemon Butter Sauce: While the scallops are cooking, melt a generous amount of unsalted butter in a small saucepan over low heat. Squeeze the juice of a fresh lemon into the melted butter, stirring gently to combine. Allow the flavors to meld together, creating a luscious lemon butter sauce.

  6. Searing the Scallops: After the sous vide cooking time is complete, carefully remove the bag of scallops from the water bath. Gently pat the scallops dry with a paper towel. Heat a skillet over high heat and add a small amount of unsalted butter. Once the butter is hot and foaming, sear the scallops for 1-2 minutes on each side until they develop a golden crust.

  7. Plating: Arrange the perfectly seared scallops on a serving plate and drizzle the luscious lemon butter sauce over them. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Your Sous Vide Lemon Butter Scallops are now ready to be served! Pair them with a crisp white wine and your favorite side dishes for a complete and satisfying meal.

Why Sous Vide?

Sous vide cooking is a technique that involves cooking food in a precisely controlled water bath, resulting in consistently perfect results. When it comes to delicate seafood like scallops, sous vide ensures that the natural flavors and textures are preserved, yielding tender, succulent morsels every time. The gentle cooking process also allows the scallops to absorb the flavors of the butter and lemon, creating a harmonious marriage of tastes in every bite.

Final Thoughts

Sous Vide Lemon Butter Scallops are a true celebration of the ocean’s bounty, bringing together the sweetness of scallops, the tang of lemon, and the richness of butter in a single dish. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this recipe is sure to impress and delight. So, don your apron, fire up your sous vide precision cooker, and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will elevate your love for seafood to new heights. Cheers to good food and great company!
