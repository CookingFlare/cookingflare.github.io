Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo Recipes


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal to satisfy your cravings? Look no further than these quick and easy Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo recipes! With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can whip up a creamy and flavorful dish in no time. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, these recipes are sure to impress.

Recipe 1: Classic Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta, cooked

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then add them to the Instant Pot. Brown the chicken on both sides.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and secure the lid. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, use the quick release method to release the pressure. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and set aside.
  5. Switch the Instant Pot to sauté mode again and pour in the heavy cream. Bring it to a simmer.
  6. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Slice the cooked chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces and add them back to the Instant Pot. Stir to coat the chicken with the sauce.
  8. Serve the creamy chicken Alfredo over a bed of cooked fettuccine pasta. Garnish with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Recipe 2: Spinach and Mushroom Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta, cooked
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions for browning the chicken breasts as in Recipe 1.
  2. Add the minced garlic, spinach, and mushrooms to the Instant Pot after removing the chicken breasts. Sauté until the spinach has wilted and the mushrooms have softened.
  3. Proceed with the remaining steps from Recipe 1, starting with pouring in the chicken broth.
  4. Once the sauce is ready, add the cooked chicken, spinach, and mushrooms back to the Instant Pot. Stir to combine everything well.
  5. Serve the flavorful spinach and mushroom chicken Alfredo over cooked fettuccine pasta. Sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese for an extra burst of flavor.

Recipe 3: Sun-Dried Tomato and Basil Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta, cooked
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the chicken breasts as directed in Recipe 1.
  2. After sautéing the garlic, add the sun-dried tomatoes and fresh basil to the Instant Pot. Sauté for a minute to release their flavors.
  3. Continue with the remaining steps from Recipe 1, starting with pouring in the chicken broth.
  4. Once the sauce is ready, add the cooked chicken, sun-dried tomatoes, and basil back to the Instant Pot. Stir well to incorporate all the ingredients.
  5. Serve the aromatic sun-dried tomato and basil chicken Alfredo over cooked fettuccine pasta. Top with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and fresh basil for a vibrant finish.

Conclusion

These quick and easy Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo recipes are perfect for busy weeknights or any time you’re craving a comforting meal. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can enjoy a homemade Alfredo dish without spending hours in the kitchen. Feel free to customize these recipes by adding your favorite ingredients or experimenting with different flavors. Give them a try and let your taste buds be delighted by the creamy and savory goodness of these dishes.
