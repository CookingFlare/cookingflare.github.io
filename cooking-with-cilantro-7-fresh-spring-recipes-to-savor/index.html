Cooking with Cilantro: 7 Fresh Spring Recipes to Savor


As the days grow warmer and the flowers bloom, spring brings a vibrant array of fresh ingredients to our kitchens. One herb that shines particularly bright during this season is cilantro. Known for its bright flavor and fragrant aroma, cilantro can elevate any dish, adding a refreshing twist to your meals. In this blog post, we will explore seven delightful spring recipes that incorporate cilantro, allowing you to savor the season while enjoying the herbâ€™s unique taste.

Why Cook with Cilantro?

Cilantro, also known as coriander in some parts of the world, is a versatile herb that is used in various cuisines. Its leaves add a burst of freshness to dishes, while the seeds (coriander) have a warm, spicy flavor. Cilantro is rich in vitamins A, C, and K, and also contains antioxidants that can help combat inflammation. This makes it not only a delicious addition to your meals but a nutritious one too!

1. Cilantro Lime Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Corn tortillas
  • Toppings: diced tomatoes, avocado, red onion, and lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, lime juice, cumin, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken breasts and marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken for about 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes.

  3. Prepare the Tacos: Slice the chicken into strips. Warm the corn tortillas in a pan or microwave. Fill each tortilla with chicken, cilantro, and your desired toppings.

  4. Serve: Add extra lime juice over the tacos for an extra zing and enjoy the fresh flavors!

2. Cilantro Pesto Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup walnuts or pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 8 ounces pasta (your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes and mozzarella balls for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil water in a large pot and cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside to cool.

  2. Make the Pesto: In a food processor, combine cilantro, walnuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese. Pulse until finely chopped. Gradually add olive oil while processing until a smooth pesto forms. Season with salt and pepper.

  3. Combine: In a large bowl, mix the cooled pasta with the cilantro pesto. Toss until the pasta is evenly coated.

  4. Garnish and Serve: Add cherry tomatoes and mozzarella balls. Serve chilled or at room temperature for a refreshing spring dish.

3. Grilled Shrimp and Cilantro Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers (if wooden, soak in water for 30 minutes)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Shrimp: In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, garlic, cilantro, salt, and pepper. Add shrimp and let marinate for at least 15 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Skewers: Thread shrimp onto skewers, leaving space between each one.

  3. Grill: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Grill the shrimp skewers for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until they turn pink and opaque.

  4. Serve: Serve the skewers with extra cilantro and lime wedges for a delicious appetizer or main dish.

4. Cilantro and Avocado Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a medium bowl, gently mix together diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro.

  2. Dress the Salsa: Drizzle lime juice over the mixture and add salt to taste. Gently toss to combine, being careful not to mash the avocados.

  3. Serve: This refreshing salsa pairs perfectly with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos and grilled meats.

5. Cilantro Lime Quinoa Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook Quinoa: In a medium saucepan, combine quinoa and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is fluffy and liquid is absorbed. Fluff with a fork.

  2. Mix Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, red bell pepper, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Toss well to combine.

  3. Serve: Serve warm or chilled for a nutritious and filling meal.

6. Cilantro Garlic Butter Grilled Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, halved
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Vegetables: In a large bowl, combine zucchini, bell pepper, and mushrooms.

  2. Make Garlic Butter: In a separate bowl, mix melted butter, minced garlic, cilantro, salt, and pepper. Pour over the vegetables and toss to coat.

  3. Grill Vegetables: Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium heat. Place the vegetables in a grill basket and grill for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until tender and slightly charred.

  4. Serve: Enjoy these flavorful vegetables as a side dish with your favorite protein.

7. Cilantro Mint Chutney

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1-2 green chilies (adjust to taste)
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Blend Ingredients: In a blender, combine cilantro, mint, green chilies, lime juice, sugar, and salt. Blend until smooth, adding a little water if necessary to achieve the desired consistency.

  2. Serve: This chutney makes a great dipping sauce for snacks or a zesty spread for sandwiches and wraps.

Conclusion

Cilantro is an herb that brings brightness and freshness to a variety of dishes, making it an ideal choice for spring cooking. From zesty tacos to refreshing salads and vibrant salsas, the recipes explored in this post showcase the versatility of cilantro in your culinary adventures. As you experiment with these dishes, you’ll not only enjoy the unique flavor of cilantro but also celebrate the incredible bounty of spring produce. So gather your ingredients, get into the kitchen, and savor the season with these delightful cilantro-infused recipes!
