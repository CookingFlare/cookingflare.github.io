Mouthwatering Breakfast Pizza Recipes to Start Your Day Right


There’s something undeniably satisfying about breakfast pizzas. The combination of crispy crust, gooey cheese, and flavorful toppings makes for a delicious start to the day. Whether you’re a fan of classic breakfast flavors or looking to experiment with new ingredients, these mouthwatering breakfast pizza recipes are sure to kickstart your morning on the right note.

Classic Bacon and Egg Breakfast Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh chives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet, then brush the surface with olive oil.
  4. Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy, then chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  5. Crack the eggs onto the pizza dough, evenly spacing them apart.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the entire pizza.
  7. Add the chopped bacon on top of the cheese.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  10. Garnish with fresh chives before slicing and serving.

Veggie Delight Breakfast Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet, then brush the surface with olive oil.
  4. Scatter the chopped spinach, sliced mushrooms, and diced bell peppers over the pizza dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the veggies.
  6. Create small wells in the cheese and veggies for the eggs.
  7. Crack the eggs into the wells.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley before slicing and serving.

Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Breakfast Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • 4 oz. smoked salmon
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1/4 cup capers
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet, then brush the surface with olive oil.
  4. Spread the cream cheese evenly over the pizza dough.
  5. Tear the smoked salmon into small pieces and distribute them over the cream cheese.
  6. Scatter the sliced red onions and capers over the salmon.
  7. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden.
  8. Garnish with fresh dill before slicing and serving.

Whether you prefer a classic bacon and egg combination, a veggie-packed delight, or a luxurious smoked salmon and cream cheese option, breakfast pizzas offer a delightful way to start your day. Get creative with your toppings and enjoy a delicious slice that’s sure to satisfy your morning cravings.
