Delicious Turkey Slider Recipes for a Twist on Tradition


If you’re looking to put a unique spin on your next gathering or simply want to enjoy a delicious meal with a twist, turkey sliders are a fantastic option. These mini sandwiches are not only cute and fun to eat, but they also offer a lighter alternative to traditional beef sliders. Whether you’re hosting a party, planning a family meal, or just craving something different, these delicious turkey slider recipes are sure to impress. Let’s dive into some mouthwatering recipes that will elevate your slider game to a whole new level!

Classic Turkey and Cranberry Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns
  • 1/2 cup cranberry sauce
  • 6 slices of provolone cheese
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined, then form the mixture into small patties.
  3. Cook the turkey patties in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked.
  4. Split the slider buns and place the bottom halves in a baking dish. Spread cranberry sauce over the bottom halves, then place a slice of provolone cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add a cooked turkey patty on each bun, then cover with the top halves of the buns.
  6. In a small bowl, mix the melted butter, Dijon mustard, and honey. Brush this mixture over the tops of the buns.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the sliders are heated through.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy these delightful sliders!

Spicy BBQ Turkey Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • Lettuce and tomato slices for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, red bell pepper, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well and form the mixture into small patties.
  3. Grill the turkey patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  4. Split the slider buns and spread mayonnaise on the bottom halves. Place a lettuce leaf and a tomato slice on each bottom bun.
  5. Add a cooked turkey patty on top of the veggies, then drizzle BBQ sauce over the patties.
  6. Cover with the top halves of the buns and serve these spicy sliders hot off the grill!

Mediterranean Turkey Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh spinach
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns
  • Tzatziki sauce
  • Sliced cucumber and red onion for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the ground turkey, feta cheese, spinach, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Form the mixture into small patties.
  3. Bake the turkey patties for 15-20 minutes, or until fully cooked.
  4. Split the slider buns and spread a generous amount of tzatziki sauce on the bottom halves. Place a couple of cucumber slices and red onion on each bottom bun.
  5. Add a cooked turkey patty on top of the veggies, then cover with the top halves of the buns.
  6. Serve these Mediterranean-inspired sliders warm and enjoy the burst of flavors!

Conclusion

These delightful turkey slider recipes are perfect for any occasion and will undoubtedly add a twist of excitement to your menu. Whether you opt for the classic combination of turkey and cranberry, the fiery kick of spicy BBQ, or the Mediterranean flair, these sliders are sure to impress your guests and tantalize your taste buds. Get creative with your toppings and sauces to tailor these recipes to your preferences. So, the next time you’re in the mood for something different, whip up a batch of these scrumptious turkey sliders and savor the deliciousness!
