Nutritious Vegan Sweet Potato and Black Bean Enchiladas


Enchiladas are a beloved dish in Mexican cuisine, and this vegan twist on the classic recipe is sure to tantalize your taste buds. Packed with nutritious sweet potatoes and protein-rich black beans, these enchiladas are a satisfying and flavorful meal that will leave you feeling energized and satisfied. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan cook or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Enchilada Sauce:

  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt to taste

For the Assembly:

  • 10-12 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup dairy-free shredded cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Filling:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the diced sweet potatoes on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 20-25 minutes or until tender.
    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
    • Add the black beans, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and roasted sweet potatoes to the skillet. Stir to combine and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Enchilada Sauce:

    • In a saucepan, combine the vegetable broth, tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Assemble the Enchiladas:

    • Spread a thin layer of the enchilada sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Warm the corn tortillas in the microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable. Spoon the sweet potato and black bean filling onto each tortilla, roll it up, and place it seam-side down in the baking dish.
    • Once all the tortillas are filled and arranged in the baking dish, pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the top. Sprinkle with dairy-free shredded cheese.

  4. Bake and Serve:

    • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    • Garnish with chopped cilantro before serving.

Nutritional Benefits

These vegan sweet potato and black bean enchiladas are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Sweet potatoes are an excellent source of vitamin A, fiber, and antioxidants, while black beans provide a healthy dose of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential minerals. This combination makes for a well-rounded, satisfying meal that supports overall health and well-being.

Serving Suggestions

Serve these enchiladas with a side of fresh guacamole, salsa, and a crisp green salad for a complete and balanced meal. The creamy richness of the guacamole and the acidity of the salsa complement the heartiness of the enchiladas beautifully, while the fresh salad adds a refreshing crunch to the meal.

Final Thoughts

These Nutritious Vegan Sweet Potato and Black Bean Enchiladas are a celebration of vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply preparing a weeknight dinner, this recipe is a fantastic choice for those looking to savor the goodness of plant-based eating without sacrificing on taste. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and prepare to delight your senses with this delightful vegan enchilada dish.
