Baking with Whole Grains: Healthier Options for Your Baked Treats


When it comes to indulging in scrumptious baked treats, there’s nothing like the aroma of freshly-baked goods wafting through your kitchen. But, what if you could enhance the nutritional value and healthiness of these irresistible delights, without compromising on taste? Introducing the wonders of whole grains – a perfect addition to your baking routine!

Whole grains, such as oats, barley, and quinoa, not only provide vitamins, minerals, and fiber, but also promote digestive health and satiety. Incorporating these incredible grains into your baked recipes can revolutionize your culinary journey. In this post, we’ll uncover some of the delectable and easy-to-make whole grain baked recipes to satisfy your cravings while nourishing your body.

  1. Whole Grain Chocolate Chip Cookies:

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups whole grain flour (such as whole wheat, spelt, or oat flour)
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup walnuts or pecans (optional)
  • 1 generous teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Directions: Combine the whole grain flour, baking soda, and salt. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Stir in the chocolate chips and nuts (if using). Gradually add the flour mixture, followed by the vanilla extract. Chill the dough for at least an hour, then bake at 375°F (190°C) for approximately 12 minutes. A sweet, chocolatey delight – now with added whole grain goodness!

  1. Quinoa and Pecan Bran Muffins:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa flour
  • 1/2 cup bran flakes
  • 1/2 cup pecans, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: Fresh fruit or berries for garnishing

Directions: Mix the quinoa flour, bran flakes, pecans, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. In a separate bowl, combine the applesauce, yogurt, and sugar. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until smooth. Fill greased muffin tins 3/4 full and bake at 375°F (190°C) for approximately 20 minutes. These cornucopia of flavors are perfect for a healthy breakfast treat or a snack on-the-go!

  1. Wholesome Banana Nut Bread:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats, ground into a coarse flour
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup mashed ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: Walnuts or pecans for topping

Directions: Combine the oat flour, almond flour, baking soda, and cinnamon. Mix the mashed banana, applesauce, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until just combined. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan and sprinkle with nuts (if using). Bake at 350°F (175°C) for approximately 60 minutes. Let the bread cool, slice, and enjoy the wholesome flavors of bananas and nuts, guilt-free!

Revolutionize Your Baking with Whole Grains

Incorporating whole grains into your baking endeavors is an exciting and nutritious way to elevate your favorite baked treats. Experiment with different types of grains and adapt the recipes to your taste preferences. Not only will your palate thank you, but your body will too! So, get your whole grain baking supplies ready and embark on this delicious and healthy culinary journey.
