Healthy Pesto Pasta: Quick and Nutritious Recipes for College Students

Are you a busy college student looking for a quick and nutritious meal that won’t break the bank? Look no further than this healthy pesto pasta recipe! Packed with flavor, vitamins, and minerals, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you energized throughout the day. Plus, it’s super easy to make, perfect for those hectic study nights. Let’s get cooking! Ingredients 8 ounces of whole wheat pasta 2 cups of fresh basil leaves 1/4 cup of pine nuts 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese 2 cloves of garlic 1/2 cup of extra virgin olive oil Salt and pepper to taste Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, sliced black olives, roasted vegetables Instructions Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Make sure to cook it al dente for the perfect texture. Drain and set aside. In a food processor or blender, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped. Gradually add the olive oil while the blender is running, until the mixture reaches a smooth and creamy consistency. Transfer the pesto sauce to a large mixing bowl. Add the cooked pasta to the bowl and toss until the pasta is evenly coated with the pesto sauce. If desired, add optional toppings such as cherry tomatoes, sliced black olives, or roasted vegetables for extra flavor and nutrition. Serve immediately and enjoy! Why Choose Healthy Pesto Pasta?
