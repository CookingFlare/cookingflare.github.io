Wholesome and Filling Rice and Lentil Burrito Bowl


Looking for a nutritious yet satisfying meal that’s easy to prepare? Look no further than this wholesome rice and lentil burrito bowl recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and vibrant flavors, it’s perfect for a quick lunch or dinner option that will keep you energized throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • For the Rice and Lentils:

    • 1 cup brown rice
    • 1/2 cup brown lentils
    • 2 cups vegetable broth
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  • For the Black Bean Corn Salsa:

    • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
    • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
    • 1 red bell pepper, diced
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
    • Juice of 1 lime
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  • For the Avocado Cream:

    • 1 ripe avocado
    • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
    • Juice of 1 lime
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Optional Garnishes:

    • Sliced jalapeños
    • Sliced radishes
    • Crumbled queso fresco
    • Tortilla chips

Instructions

1. Cook the Rice and Lentils:

  • In a medium saucepan, combine the brown rice, lentils, vegetable broth, cumin powder, and chili powder.
  • Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30-35 minutes or until the rice and lentils are tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.

2. Prepare the Black Bean Corn Salsa:

  • In a large bowl, combine the black beans, corn kernels, red onion, red bell pepper, cilantro, and lime juice.
  • Toss gently to mix, then season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Set aside to allow the flavors to meld while you prepare the avocado cream.

3. Make the Avocado Cream:

  • In a blender or food processor, combine the avocado, Greek yogurt, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy. Add a tablespoon of water if needed to reach your desired consistency.

4. Assemble the Burrito Bowls:

  • Divide the cooked rice and lentil mixture evenly among serving bowls.
  • Top each bowl with a generous scoop of the black bean corn salsa.
  • Drizzle with the avocado cream sauce.

5. Garnish and Serve:

  • Garnish with sliced jalapeños, radishes, crumbled queso fresco, and tortilla chips if desired.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your wholesome and filling rice and lentil burrito bowl!

Tips for Variations:

  • Protein Boost: Add grilled chicken, tofu, or shrimp for an extra protein punch.
  • Vegetarian Option: Substitute the Greek yogurt in the avocado cream with a dairy-free alternative to keep it vegan.
  • Spicy Kick: Mix some chipotle powder or hot sauce into the avocado cream for a spicier flavor profile.

Final Thoughts

This rice and lentil burrito bowl is not only delicious and filling but also versatile and customizable to suit your taste preferences. Whether you’re meal prepping for the week or whipping up a quick dinner after a long day, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Enjoy the wholesome goodness of whole grains, legumes, and fresh vegetables in every satisfying bite!

Give this recipe a try and discover how easy it is to create a nutritious meal that doesn’t compromise on flavor. Perfect for any occasion, from family dinners to casual lunches, this burrito bowl will satisfy your cravings and keep you fueled for whatever comes next. Bon appétit!
