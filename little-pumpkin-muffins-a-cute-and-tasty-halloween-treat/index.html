Little Pumpkin Muffins: A Cute and Tasty Halloween Treat


Are you looking for a fun and spooky treat to bake for your little ones this Halloween? Look no further than these adorable Little Pumpkin Muffins! Bursting with pumpkin, spice, and everything nice, these muffins are sure to be a hit at your Halloween party or spooky snacks night. So, let’s don our aprons and get ready to make some witching hour treats!

Ingredients

For the Little Pumpkin Muffins, you’ll need the following:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree (fresh or canned)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips (optional, but highly recommended)
  • Cooking spray (for greasing)

Equipment

To create these spook-tacular muffins, we’ll need:

  • Electric mixer
  • Whisk
  • 12-count muffin tin
  • Spatula
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Parchment paper (optional)
  • Mixing bowls
  • Oven

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and spray your muffin tin with cooking spray, or line it with parchment liners to ensure easy removal.

  2. Begin by combining the dry ingredients in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Add 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour, 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Mix until well combined.

  3. In a separate, larger mixing bowl, combine the wet ingredients. Add 1 cup pumpkin puree, 1/2 cup vegetable oil, 1/2 cup granulated sugar, 1 large egg, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1/2 cup milk. Using an electric mixer, beat all the ingredients together until they are well-incorporated and smooth. Be sure to scrape down the sides of the mixing bowl to ensure there are no hidden lumps!

  4. Once your wet ingredients are combined, slowly incorporate the dry ingredients by pouring the dry mixture into the wet mixture in three parts. Using a spatula, gently fold the ingredients together until you have a smooth and slightly thick batter.

  5. Add the mini chocolate chips at this stage, if you choose to include them. Fold them into the batter until evenly distributed.

  6. At this point, get ready to fill your muffin tin. Using a cookie scoop or spoon, divide the batter amongst the 12 cups. Each cup should be filled about 3/4 of the way to the top. Optionally, you can sprinkle a couple of extra chocolate chips on top for some added oomph.

  7. Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and allow the Little Pumpkin Muffins to bake for 18-20 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center of one of the muffins comes out clean and free from wet batter.

  8. When the muffins are done, allow them to cool in the tin for about 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. This will allow them to settle and become perfectly round and firm.

  9. Now it’s time to eat your creations! Serve your Little Pumpkin Muffins to your Halloween guests with a smile, and take delight in the oohs and ahhs that follow.

And that, my friends, is how you create delightful Little Pumpkin Muffins - perfect for spooking up your Halloween feast! Your littles will love these, and you’ll have a great opportunity to get into the Halloween spirit while sharing a delicious treat. Enjoy, happy baking, and happy haunting!
