Ingredients 1 cup mayonnaise 2 tablespoons ketchup 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish 1 tablespoon finely minced onion 1 tablespoon white vinegar 1/2 teaspoon sugar 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce Salt and pepper to taste Instructions In a medium-sized bowl, combine the mayonnaise, ketchup, sweet pickle relish, minced onion, white vinegar, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir the ingredients together until they are well combined and the dressing is smooth and creamy. Taste the dressing and adjust the seasonings to your preference. If you like it sweeter, add a little more sugar. If you like it tangier, add a splash more vinegar. Once you are satisfied with the taste, transfer the dressing to a jar or airtight container and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve the Homemade Thousand Island Dressing chilled and drizzle it over your favorite salads, sandwiches, or use it as a dip for vegetables.
