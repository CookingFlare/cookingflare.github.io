Vegetarian Pad Thai Stir Fry: A Colorful Twist on a Thai Favorite


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and looking for a delicious vegetarian option, then this Vegetarian Pad Thai Stir Fry recipe is for you! Bursting with vibrant colors and bold flavors, this dish puts a unique twist on the traditional Pad Thai by using plant-based ingredients. Let’s dive right in and discover how to create this mouthwatering masterpiece.

Ingredients

To make this Vegetarian Pad Thai Stir Fry, you will need:

  • 8 ounces of rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of thinly sliced bell peppers (assorted colors for added vibrancy)
  • 1 cup of shredded carrots
  • 1 cup of bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup of chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup of chopped peanuts
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Sauce

For the sauce, you will need:

  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of sriracha (adjust the amount to your desired level of spiciness)

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together all the sauce ingredients until well combined. Set aside.

  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the broccoli florets, bell peppers, and shredded carrots. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the cooked rice noodles to the other side. Pour the sauce over the noodles and toss everything together until well coated.

  5. Add the bean sprouts and chopped green onions to the skillet and continue stir-frying for another 2-3 minutes until the bean sprouts are slightly wilted.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the Vegetarian Pad Thai Stir Fry with chopped peanuts, fresh cilantro, and lime wedges for an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Serve hot and enjoy this colorful twist on a Thai favorite!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add more vegetables to this stir fry, such as snap peas, mushrooms, or zucchini, to suit your personal preferences.
  • For a protein-packed version, add tofu, tempeh, or edamame to the stir fry.
  • If you’re not a fan of peanuts, you can substitute them with cashews or almonds for a different nutty crunch.
  • Customize the level of spiciness by adjusting the amount of sriracha in the sauce. If you prefer a milder flavor, you can omit it altogether.
  • Make it gluten-free by using gluten-free soy sauce and ensuring that all other ingredients are gluten-free as well.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant array of colors and mouthwatering flavors, this Vegetarian Pad Thai Stir Fry is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to explore new culinary horizons, this dish offers a delightful twist on the classic Thai favorite. So grab your wok, gather your ingredients, and get ready to embark on a flavorful journey that will leave your taste buds begging for more!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation and making the dish your own. Don’t be afraid to add your personal touch and adjust the ingredients according to your taste preferences. Happy cooking!
