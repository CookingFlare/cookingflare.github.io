Vegan Homecoming Heroes: 15 Crowd-Pleasing Main Dishes to Welcome the Fam


Welcome back, beloved family and friends! There’s nothing quite like reuniting with loved ones over a scrumptious meal. As the ultimate vegan chef, I’ve curated an array of drool-worthy main dishes sure to impress and fill your heart and bellies. Gather ‘round and sharpen your knives because this collection of plant-based recipes can’t be missed.

  1. ‘Taco ‘Bout Delicious Vegan Tacos

Kick off this culinary journey with a Virgin-Approved fusion of flavors in these delightfully vibrant Vegan Tacos. Enthralling a medley of spices, your chosen filling - black beans, quinoa, tofu, or portobello mushrooms - awaits your composition expertise. Top it off with a tantalizing blend of rejuvenating avocado, zesty salsa, and fresh cilantro for the ultimate flavor explosion. It’s not a party without the magic ingredient - corn tortillas, naturally!

  1. Soul-Satisfying Vegan Chili

A tried and true crowd-pleaser, this recipe for the Heartfelt Vegan Chili is perfect to warm your soul on chilly reunions. With a savory medley of ingredients, including kidney beans, sweet potatoes, and bell peppers, this dish is spiced with a gentle touch. Cornbread muffins are an exceptional addition to the meal, providing a delicious and delectable contrast to each savory bite.

  1. Tempting Vegan Shepherd’s Pie

Indulge in the rich, savory enchantments of the Alluring Vegan Shepherd’s Pie. Comforting potatoes cascade over a medley of gently simmered lentils, carrots, and peas to deliver an exquisite interplay of flavors and textures. This dish is a guaranteed heartwarmer, thawing even the iciest of personalities with its velvety embrace.

  1. Zesty Vegan Jambalaya

Experience an intoxicating brew of flavors with the Vivacious Vegan Jambalaya. Combining the hearty goodness of chopped vegetables, with the zesty zing of spices, this dish makes perfect use of plant proteins like tofu or tempeh, searing and simmering them in a fragrant mixture of onions, garlic, and bell peppers with rice. The vibrant and tantalizing aroma of this dish will have everyone begging for seconds!

  1. Veggie-Packed Vegan Lasagna

Usher in elegance and tradition with the Vegan Veggie Lasagna. Celebrating the finest veggies like zucchinis, eggplants, squash, and spinach, this dish is complemented by a rich layer of herbed tofu ricotta and utterly irresistible tomato-based sauce. Prepared to be swept away by the memories this scrumptious lasagna will create, as you ta-daah! It harmoniously unites delectable flavors and unforgettable aromas to tantalize your guests.

  1. Veggie-Stuffed Vegan Sweet Potatoes

The Divine Vegan Sweet Potato Stuffs showcase immense satisfaction and flavor, each better than its predecessor. The humble and reliable sweet potato serves as the perfect vessel for embracing black beans, quinoa, and a rainbow of colorful veggies. A drizzle of zesty lime and smoky spices elevates these stuffed sweet potatoes to heavenly heights.

  1. Flavorful Vegan Burrito Bowl

Delight your senses with the Enthralling Vegan Burrito Bowl, a crowd-pleaser that captures the essence of a burrito in a convenient dish. Let the colors, textures, and earthy flavors unfold with every bite, generously portioned with a bed of rice, black beans, avocado, and pico de gallo. Top it off with the magic touch of guacamole and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro.

  1. Umami Vegan Sushi

Ignite the passion for Japanese cuisine with the Umami Vegan Sushi. With its flaky, textured nori wrapping encasing a luscious mTamago-filled rice, you’ll transport your diners to the heart of Japan. Innovative and informative, learn the methods of rolling sushi with this exquisite dish.

  1. Hemp Heart “Pulled” BBQ Portobello Mushrooms

Going meatless has never been tastier, thanks to the mouthwatering Hemp Heart BBQ Portobello Mushrooms. Tender and succulent, these marinated portobello mushrooms are dressed in a tangy yet smoky BBQ sauce. Impress your guests with a perfectly plant-based recipe that is sure to have them feeling grateful for your culinary prowess.

  1. Vegan Cajun Fried “Chicken”

A beloved comfort food transforms into a satisfying vegan treat with the Vegan Cajun Fried “Chicken,” a crispy and spicy dish that is certain to tickle your taste buds, leaving you and your guests craving more. Partnered with a cooling, creamy dill sauce, these fried “chicken” bites are a delightful and enticing addition to your vegan gatherings.

  1. Spicy Vegan Coconut Curry

The mouthwatering aroma of the Spicy Vegan Coconut Curry will set your family and friends begging for spoonfuls of this luscious creation. A winning combination of herbs, spices, and coconut milk creates a sumptuous sauce to warm the heart. The tender potatoes and carrots, combined with the firm blocks of tofu, make this meal as satisfying as it is delicious.

  1. Zucchini “Spaghetti” with Avocado Pesto

Prepare to indulge in a healthier, plant-based take on an Italian classic with the Zucchini “Spaghetti” with Avocado Pesto. Spiralized zucchini noodles are coated with a heartwarming, protein-packed avocado-based pesto sauce, ensuring a light, vibrant, and dreamy feast for your eagerly awaiting guests.

  1. Vegan Chickpea “Tuna”

Bring back the nostalgia of the classic childhood sandwiches with the delightful Vegan Chickpea “Tuna”. Savory, umami-rich chickpeas seasoned to perfection, bound together with a creamy vegan mayo, make for an irresistibly delicious replacement for traditional “tuna” sandwiches. This is an unsurpassable mix of nostalgic and novel flavors that all will adore.

  1. Chipotle Vegan Mac and Cheese

Travel down memory lane with the irresistible Chipotle Vegan Mac and Cheese. Channeling the warmth and indulgence of your favorite childhood dish, this grown-up version spices up traditional mac and cheese with smoky, spicy chipotle peppers in a creamy blend. Get ready to be spooned up with every last bit of crave-worthy goodness.

  1. Dates-Soaked Vegan Meatballs

Preserve the joy of an Italian feast with the heartwarming Dates-Soaked Vegan Meatballs. These black bean-based balls boast a secret force – sweet, sticky dates – that bind and suffuse their intrigue into every bit. Rich in a hearty tomato sauce and crafted with the supreme (p)artistry, these meatballs will leave hearts singing and palates teeming.

Bravo, my fellow chefs! Embrace these irresistible recipes in hosting the most memorable homecoming feast. As your family and friends arrive, these delectable delicacies will surely make their hearts sing like the choir they do. May every scrumptious bite reveal love, warmth, and cherished connections as you devise your culinary menu. Here’s to homecomings, heavenly aromas, and a hearty welcome to your fam!
