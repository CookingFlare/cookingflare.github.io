Baking with Fresh Plums: Tart and Sweet Goodness


Are you ready to embark on a delicious baking adventure? Look no further than the delightful world of fresh plums. These juicy fruits bring a unique combination of tartness and sweetness to any dish, making them an excellent choice for your next baking project. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering recipes that showcase the irresistible flavors of fresh plums. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s get started!

Plum Crumble Bars

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Total Time: 55 minutes | Yield: 12 bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh plums, pitted and diced
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingertips until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and vanilla extract. Add the egg mixture to the flour mixture and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Press two-thirds of the dough evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan to form the crust.
  5. In another bowl, combine the diced plums, brown sugar, and cornstarch. Gently toss until the plums are coated.
  6. Spread the plum mixture evenly over the crust in the baking pan.
  7. Crumble the remaining dough evenly over the plum mixture.
  8. Bake for 40 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the plums are bubbling. Allow to cool completely before cutting into bars.

Plum Galette

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Total Time: 50 minutes | Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 4-5 tablespoons ice water
  • 4 cups fresh plums, pitted and sliced
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Coarse sugar (for sprinkling)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingertips until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the sliced plums, granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Gently toss until the plums are coated.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle about 12 inches in diameter.
  6. Transfer the rolled-out dough to the prepared baking sheet.
  7. Arrange the plum mixture in the center of the dough, leaving about 2 inches of dough around the edges.
  8. Fold the edges of the dough over the plums, creating a rustic, free-form tart.
  9. Brush the edges of the dough with the beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse sugar.
  10. Bake for 30 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the plums are tender.
  11. Allow the galette to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy it warm or at room temperature with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Plum Upside-Down Cake

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Total Time: 1 hour 5 minutes | Yield: 8-10 servings

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 4 cups fresh plums, pitted and sliced
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter and brown sugar. Pour the mixture into the greased cake pan, spreading it evenly.
  3. Arrange the sliced plums on top of the butter and sugar mixture, creating a single layer.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. In a separate large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  6. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  7. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  8. Pour the batter over the plums in the cake pan, spreading it evenly.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before inverting it onto a serving plate. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Conclusion

Baking with fresh plums opens up a world of tantalizing flavors and textures. Whether you choose to make Plum Crumble Bars, a Plum Galette, or a Plum Upside-Down Cake, these recipes are sure to impress your family and friends. The tartness of plums combined with their natural sweetness creates a delightful balance that will leave you craving more. So, don’t wait any longer – head to your nearest farmers’ market or grocery store and grab a batch of fresh plums to embark on a baking adventure filled with tart and sweet goodness!
