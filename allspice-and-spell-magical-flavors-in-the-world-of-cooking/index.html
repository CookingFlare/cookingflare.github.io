The Alluring Allspice

Allspice, also known as pimenta, is a small, round, dried berry that borrows flavors from cloves, cinnamon, and nutmeg. This beloved spice originates on the Caribbean island of Jamaica, where it has been cultivated for centuries. In fact, the Aztecs and Mayans cherished the fruit from which allspice derives from, using it for medicinal and culinary purposes.

Its name elucidates its distinct, multifaceted flavor, much like a witch’s_spell. Allspice continues to bewitch palates worldwide and remains a fundamental ingredient in countless recipes. From warming and aromatic dishes to sweet and sumptuous treats, allspice adapts to any culinary concoction with grace and finesse.

Enchanting Allspice: Sweet Spells

1. Allspice Poached Pears

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe but firm pears (preferably Bosc)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 2 lemon slices
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves
  • A pinch of allspice

Instructions:

  1. Peel pears, leaving the stalks intact.
  2. Place the pears in a pot with water, lemon slices, cinnamon stick, cloves, allspice, and 2 tablespoons of sugar.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat.
  4. Skim off any foam and reduce heat to a simmer.
  5. Cook pears for about 20-25 minutes until they become tender.
  6. Remove pears and set aside.
  7. Increase the heat to medium and let the poaching liquid reduce to a syrupy consistency.
  8. Strain the syrup and serve with the pears.
  9. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and an allspice berry.

2. Allspice Apple Cake

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of peeled and chopped apples
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of ground allspice
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (180째C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix chopped apples with 1/2 cup of sugar and 1 teaspoon of allspice. Set aside to let the flavors meld.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, the remaining sugar, and cinnamon.
  4. Whisk in eggs and oil into the dry ingredients.
  5. Add the spiced apples and mix until just incorporated.
  6. Fold in the chopped walnuts.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before enjoying.

1. Allspice Rubbed Roasted Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 tablespoon of ground allspice
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425째F (220째C) and pat the chicken dry with paper towels.
  2. In a bowl, combine allspice, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the chicken with olive oil, then evenly coat with the spice mixture.
  4. Place the chicken in a roasting pan and bake for about 1 hour or until fully cooked.
