BBQ Chicken Tacos: Grilled Southwestern Flavors


Welcome to our flavorful journey into the world of BBQ chicken tacos infused with the bold and vibrant tastes of the Southwest. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create mouthwatering BBQ chicken tacos that are grilled to perfection and bursting with the rich, smoky flavors of the grill. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of the Southwest, these BBQ chicken tacos are sure to become a new favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

For the BBQ Chicken:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Taco Assembly:

  • 12 small flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving
  • Additional BBQ sauce for drizzling

Instructions

Step 1: Marinating the Chicken

  1. In a bowl, combine the BBQ sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a large resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over the chicken. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken, ensuring that each piece is thoroughly coated. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or ideally overnight, to allow the flavors to infuse.

Step 2: Grilling the Chicken

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, allowing any excess marinade to drip off.
  2. Place the chicken thighs on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has beautiful grill marks. Brush the chicken with additional BBQ sauce during the last few minutes of grilling for an extra burst of flavor. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.

Step 3: Assembling the BBQ Chicken Tacos

  1. Warm the tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side, until they are soft and pliable.
  2. To assemble the tacos, place a few strips of the grilled BBQ chicken onto each tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, diced red onion, and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro.
  3. Squeeze a lime wedge over each taco for a pop of citrusy freshness, and drizzle with additional BBQ sauce for an extra burst of flavor.

Serving Suggestions

These BBQ chicken tacos are perfect for a casual gathering or a family dinner. Serve them alongside a refreshing avocado and corn salad, or pair them with a side of grilled corn on the cob for a complete Southwestern-inspired feast. Don’t forget to have extra lime wedges and BBQ sauce on hand for those who want to customize their tacos to their liking.

Conclusion

Grilling up these BBQ chicken tacos with the flavors of the Southwest is a delightful way to infuse your summer gatherings with a touch of smoky, tangy goodness. The tender, juicy chicken paired with the vibrant toppings and warm tortillas creates a harmonious symphony of flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds singing. Whether you’re a seasoned grill master or a novice in the art of BBQ, these tacos are an approachable and delicious addition to any cookout. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and savor the irresistible allure of these BBQ chicken tacos with grilled southwestern flavors. Happy grilling!
