Zesty Lemon Coconut Bars: A Tangy and Creamy Treat


Do you crave the perfect balance between tangy and creamy flavors? Look no further than these Zesty Lemon Coconut Bars! Bursting with vibrant lemon zest and complemented by the rich creaminess of coconut, these bars are a delightful treat for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply satisfying your sweet tooth, these bars will surely impress. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • ½ cup of melted unsalted butter
  • ¼ cup of granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 2 cups of sweetened shredded coconut
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups of granulated sugar
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • ½ cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ cup of coconut milk
  • Powdered sugar for dusting (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving a slight overhang for easy removal later.

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar for the crust. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated with the butter and sugar. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan to form an even layer.

  3. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly while you prepare the filling.

  4. In a large bowl, whisk together the sweetened shredded coconut, all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt for the filling. Set aside.

  5. In another bowl, beat the eggs and granulated sugar together until well combined. Add the lemon zest, lemon juice, and coconut milk. Mix until smooth.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  7. Pour the filling over the cooled crust, spreading it evenly. Tap the pan gently on the counter to remove any air bubbles.

  8. Bake the bars for approximately 30-35 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the center is set. The bars may still jiggle slightly, but they will firm up as they cool.

  9. Remove the pan from the oven and let the bars cool completely in the pan on a wire rack. Once cooled, refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld together.

  10. Once chilled, lift the bars out of the pan using the parchment paper overhang. Cut into squares or bars of your desired size. If desired, dust with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness.

  11. Serve these Zesty Lemon Coconut Bars chilled and enjoy the tangy and creamy flavors melting in your mouth. These bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days, if they last that long!

Note: For a more intense coconut flavor, you can toast the shredded coconut before adding it to the filling. Simply spread the coconut on a baking sheet and bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 5-7 minutes, or until lightly golden brown.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a tangy and creamy treat, give these Zesty Lemon Coconut Bars a try. With their vibrant flavors and velvety texture, they are sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. Enjoy!
