Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies: A Classic Combo


There’s something undeniably comforting about the combination of peanut butter and chocolate. The rich, nutty flavor of peanut butter paired with the sweetness of chocolate creates a classic flavor profile that appeals to many. And when you take that winning combination and transform it into a vegan-friendly treat, you’ve got a recipe for success. In this blog post, we’re going to explore the world of Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies – a delightful twist on a beloved classic.

The Magic of Vegan Baking

Vegan baking has come a long way in recent years, and it’s no longer a challenge to create delicious, plant-based treats. With the right ingredients and techniques, you can achieve the perfect texture and flavor without using any animal products. When it comes to cookies, the absence of eggs and dairy is not a barrier to achieving a soft, chewy, and flavorful result. In fact, vegan baking often introduces unique and innovative flavors that can elevate traditional recipes.

The Key Ingredients

Peanut Butter

The star of the show, peanut butter brings a rich and nutty flavor to these cookies. Opt for natural peanut butter without added sugar or oil for a purer taste.

Dairy-Free Chocolate Chips

To achieve that classic chocolate chip cookie experience, look for high-quality dairy-free chocolate chips. Many brands offer vegan-friendly options that are just as delicious as their dairy counterparts.

Flaxseed Meal

Flaxseed meal acts as a binding agent in place of eggs, helping to hold the cookies together while adding a nutritional boost.

Coconut Oil

Coconut oil serves as the fat component in this recipe, lending a subtle tropical note to the cookies while ensuring they stay moist and tender.

Pure Vanilla Extract

A touch of vanilla extract enhances the overall flavor profile and adds depth to the cookies.

All-Purpose Flour

For structure and texture, all-purpose flour is the go-to choice for these cookies. If you prefer a gluten-free option, you can use a gluten-free flour blend instead.

Baking Soda

Baking soda helps the cookies rise and contributes to their soft, chewy texture.

Sea Salt

A pinch of sea salt balances the sweetness and enhances the flavors of the peanut butter and chocolate.

The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond or oat milk)
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons flaxseed meal
  • 1 1/2 cups dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted coconut oil and brown sugar until well combined.
  3. Add the peanut butter, non-dairy milk, and vanilla extract to the bowl, and mix until smooth and creamy.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, sea salt, and flaxseed meal.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until a dough forms.
  6. Gently fold in the dairy-free chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.
  7. Using a cookie scoop or spoon, portion the dough into balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Flatten each ball slightly with the back of a fork, creating a crisscross pattern on the top of each cookie.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  10. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving and Storage

These Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies are best enjoyed fresh from the oven, with a tall glass of non-dairy milk for dunking. However, if you have leftovers, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. You can also freeze the cookies for longer storage – just be sure to thaw them at room temperature before serving.

In Conclusion

Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies are a delightful way to indulge in a classic flavor combination while adhering to a plant-based lifestyle. With the right ingredients and a little bit of love, you can create a batch of cookies that are sure to satisfy any sweet tooth. Whether you’re vegan or simply looking to try something new, these cookies are a testament to the fact that plant-based baking can be just as delicious and indulgent as traditional baking. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the irresistible blend of peanut butter and chocolate in every bite!
