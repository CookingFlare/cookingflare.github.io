Greek Yogurt Cheesecake: A Creamy and Indulgent Dessert


Are you looking for a delicious and healthier alternative to traditional cheesecake? Look no further than this irresistible Greek yogurt cheesecake recipe. This creamy and indulgent dessert will delight your taste buds while offering a lighter option for those mindful of their health. Made with rich Greek yogurt, this cheesecake is a delightful blend of tangy and sweet flavors that will leave you craving for more. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delectable treat in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling:

  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, creating an even layer.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool.

Step 2: Making the Filling

  1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add the vanilla extract and beat until well incorporated.
  3. Mix in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring each egg is fully combined before adding the next.
  4. Gently fold in the Greek yogurt and flour until the mixture is smooth and uniform.
  5. Pour the filling over the cooled crust.

Step 3: Baking the Cheesecake

  1. Place the springform pan on a baking sheet to catch any potential leaks.
  2. Bake the cheesecake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center still has a slight jiggle.
  3. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes.

Step 4: Creating the Topping

  1. In a small bowl, mix the sour cream, sugar, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  2. Gently spread the topping over the surface of the cheesecake.

Step 5: Chilling and Serving

  1. Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow it to set.
  2. Once chilled, carefully remove the cheesecake from the springform pan.
  3. Slice the cheesecake and serve it chilled, savoring each creamy and luscious bite.

Enjoy the Creamy Bliss

This Greek yogurt cheesecake is a delightful marriage of velvety textures and delightful flavors. Whether you’re hosting a special occasion or simply craving a luxurious dessert, this recipe is sure to impress. Embrace the tangy richness of Greek yogurt combined with the decadence of a classic cheesecake, and treat yourself to a slice of creamy bliss. Share this recipe with friends and family, and let them indulge in the irresistible allure of Greek yogurt cheesecake. Happy baking and even happier indulging!
