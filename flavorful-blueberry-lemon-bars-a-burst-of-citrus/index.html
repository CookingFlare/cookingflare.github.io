Flavorful Blueberry Lemon Bars: A Burst of Citrus


If you’re a fan of the delightful combination of sweet and tangy flavors, then you’re in for a treat with these Flavorful Blueberry Lemon Bars. This recipe is a burst of citrusy goodness that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. The buttery crust, the zesty lemon filling, and the burst of juicy blueberries come together to create a dessert that’s both refreshing and indulgent. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a delightful treat, these Blueberry Lemon Bars are the perfect choice.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed

For the Filling:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

For the Topping:

  • Additional confectioners’ sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
    • In a food processor, combine the flour and confectioners’ sugar. Add the chilled butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until lightly golden.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, granulated sugar, and flour until well combined.
    • Stir in the lemon juice and lemon zest until smooth.
    • Gently fold in the fresh blueberries.

  3. Assemble and Bake:

    • Pour the lemon-blueberry filling over the baked crust.
    • Return the pan to the oven and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are lightly golden.

  4. Cool and Serve:

    • Allow the Blueberry Lemon Bars to cool completely in the pan.
    • Once cooled, dust the top with confectioners’ sugar.
    • Cut into squares and serve.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of citrus, add a teaspoon of lemon extract to the filling.
  • You can use frozen blueberries if fresh ones are not available.
  • Serve the bars with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful twist.

The Perfect Summer Dessert

These Flavorful Blueberry Lemon Bars are a celebration of summer flavors. The tartness of the lemons combined with the sweetness of the blueberries creates a harmonious blend that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re enjoying them at a picnic, a barbecue, or a cozy evening at home, these bars are sure to impress. The buttery crust adds a satisfying crunch to every bite, making this dessert a true delight for the senses.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a refreshing and vibrant dessert, give these Blueberry Lemon Bars a try. You’ll find yourself transported to a sunny orchard with every citrusy, blueberry-filled bite. Enjoy the burst of citrus, savor the sweetness, and indulge in the delightful flavors of summer.
