Savory Cornbread and Sausage Stuffing Recipe for a Hearty Thanksgiving


Thanksgiving is a time for coming together, sharing gratitude, and indulging in delicious food. And what better dish to grace your Thanksgiving table than a mouthwatering Savory Cornbread and Sausage Stuffing? This recipe combines the comforting flavors of cornbread, savory sausage, aromatic herbs, and wholesome ingredients to create a dish that will have your guests reaching for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground sausage
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups diced onions
  • 2 cups diced celery
  • 4 cups crumbled cornbread
  • 4 cups cubed white bread
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 large eggs, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground sausage over medium heat until it is no longer pink. Remove the sausage from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. In the same skillet, melt the butter and sauté the onions and celery until they are soft and translucent.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the crumbled cornbread, cubed white bread, cooked sausage, sautéed onions and celery, parsley, sage, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Gently toss the ingredients until they are well combined.
  5. Gradually pour the chicken or vegetable broth over the stuffing mixture, ensuring that it is evenly moistened. Add the beaten eggs and stir until everything is well incorporated.
  6. Transfer the stuffing mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.
  7. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 40 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and crispy.
  8. Once done, remove the stuffing from the oven and let it sit for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Savory Cornbread and Sausage Stuffing makes a delightful addition to any Thanksgiving spread. Serve it alongside roast turkey, cranberry sauce, mashed potatoes, and roasted vegetables for a complete and satisfying holiday meal. The savory flavors and comforting texture of this stuffing are sure to be a hit with your family and friends.

Make-Ahead Tips

To make your Thanksgiving day less hectic, you can prepare the stuffing a day in advance. Simply follow the recipe until step 6, cover the baking dish with plastic wrap, and refrigerate it overnight. On Thanksgiving day, allow the stuffing to come to room temperature while the oven preheats, then bake it according to the instructions.

Conclusion

This Savory Cornbread and Sausage Stuffing recipe is a true crowd-pleaser, bringing warmth and flavor to your Thanksgiving feast. Whether you’re hosting a large gathering or enjoying an intimate meal with loved ones, this hearty and comforting dish is a perfect way to celebrate the spirit of Thanksgiving. Enjoy the joy of cooking and sharing a meal with those you cherish, and may your holiday be filled with love, laughter, and delicious food. Happy Thanksgiving!
