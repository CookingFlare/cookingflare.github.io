Irresistible Christmas Morning Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

Are you ready to make your Christmas morning extra special? Look no further than these irresistible cinnamon rolls that will fill your home with the warm and comforting aroma of cinnamon and sugar. These fluffy and gooey treats are perfect for starting your day off in the most delicious way possible. So, grab your apron and let’s get baking! Ingredients 4 cups all-purpose flour 1/4 cup granulated sugar 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast 1 teaspoon salt 1 cup milk, warmed 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted 2 large eggs For the Filling 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened 1 cup brown sugar 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon For the Cream Cheese Frosting 4 ounces cream cheese, softened 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened 1 cup powdered sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract Instructions In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warmed milk, melted butter, and eggs. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick. Spread the softened butter evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the buttered dough. Starting from one long edge, tightly roll up the dough into a log shape. Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 12 equal-sized rolls. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each roll. Cover the rolls with a clean kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Once the rolls have risen, bake them in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown. While the rolls are baking, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and butter until creamy. Gradually add in the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and continue beating until smooth and well combined. Once the rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Spread the cream cheese frosting over the warm cinnamon rolls, allowing it to melt and ooze into all the nooks and crannies. Serve the rolls warm and enjoy the gooey, cinnamon-spiced goodness! Tips and Variations For an extra indulgent twist, sprinkle chopped pecans or walnuts over the filling before rolling up the dough. You can make the dough the night before and let it rise in the refrigerator overnight. In the morning, simply roll out the dough, assemble the rolls, and bake as directed. If you prefer a thicker frosting, add more powdered sugar until you reach your desired consistency. Now, all that’s left to do is to gather your loved ones around the table, pour some hot coffee or cocoa, and savor the heavenly delight of these irresistible Christmas morning cinnamon rolls. The combination of soft dough, sweet cinnamon filling, and creamy frosting is sure to make this holiday season extra special. Enjoy and Merry Christmas! ...
