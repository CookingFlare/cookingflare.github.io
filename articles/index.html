Deliciously Spiced Indian Butter Chicken: A Flavorful Curry Dish

If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this deliciously spiced Indian Butter Chicken recipe. Bursting with flavors and aromas, this flavorful curry dish is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure! Ingredients 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter) 1 large onion, finely chopped 3 cloves of garlic, minced 1-inch piece of ginger, grated 2 teaspoons garam masala 1 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon paprika 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference) 1 cup plain yogurt 1 cup tomato puree 1 cup heavy cream Salt to taste Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish Instructions In a large skillet or pan, heat the ghee over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet and cook for another minute, stirring constantly to avoid burning. In a small bowl, mix together the garam masala, turmeric powder, paprika, cumin powder, coriander powder, and chili powder. This blend of spices will give your Butter Chicken its rich and aromatic flavor. Add the spice mixture to the skillet and cook for a minute, stirring well to coat the onions, garlic, and ginger with the spices. Now, it’s time to add the chicken to the skillet. Cook the chicken until it is lightly browned on all sides. Reduce the heat to low and add the plain yogurt and tomato puree to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and coat the chicken with the creamy sauce. Cover the skillet and let the dish simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the chicken to become tender. After 20 minutes, uncover the skillet and stir in the heavy cream. Season with salt to taste and let the curry simmer for another 5 minutes, until it thickens slightly. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the Butter Chicken with fresh cilantro leaves for an added burst of freshness and aroma. Serve the Deliciously Spiced Indian Butter Chicken hot with steamed basmati rice or naan bread. The creamy and flavorful curry pairs perfectly with these traditional Indian accompaniments.
