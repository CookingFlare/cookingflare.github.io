One Pot Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta


There’s nothing quite like a hearty, comforting pasta dish to satisfy your cravings, and this One Pot Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta is the perfect recipe to add to your repertoire. This dish combines tender chicken, flavorful sun-dried tomatoes, and a creamy sauce to create a meal that’s both indulgent and easy to make. The best part? It all comes together in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to indulge in a delicious Italian-inspired meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, drained and chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Chicken: In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and season with salt and pepper. Sear the chicken until golden brown on all sides, then remove from the pot and set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the minced garlic and sun-dried tomatoes. Sauté for a few minutes until the garlic is fragrant and the tomatoes have softened.

  3. Add the Herbs and Pasta: Stir in the dried oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes. Add the uncooked penne pasta to the pot, followed by the chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  4. Simmer: Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the pasta simmer in the creamy sauce, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking. Cook for about 15-20 minutes or until the pasta is al dente and the sauce has thickened.

  5. Finish and Serve: Once the pasta is cooked through, add the seared chicken back into the pot. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and coats the pasta. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley before serving.

Tips and Tricks

  • Creamy Consistency: If you prefer a thicker sauce, let the pasta simmer for a few extra minutes until it reaches your desired consistency.
  • Customization: Feel free to add spinach or chopped kale to the dish for an extra dose of greens and nutrients.
  • Leftovers: This One Pot Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta makes excellent leftovers. Store any extra portions in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Final Thoughts

This One Pot Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta is a true crowd-pleaser. It’s a versatile dish that’s perfect for a cozy family dinner or a casual gathering with friends. The combination of creamy sauce, tender chicken, and flavorful sun-dried tomatoes creates a symphony of flavors that will leave everyone at the table wanting more. Plus, with the convenience of only using one pot, cleanup is minimal, allowing you to savor the meal without the hassle of a sink full of dishes. So, the next time you’re craving a comforting pasta dish, give this recipe a try and transport your taste buds to the sun-soaked hills of Tuscany. Buon appetito!
