BBQ Chicken Skewers: Quick and Easy Recipes


There’s nothing quite like the sizzle of barbecue on a sunny day. And when it comes to easy and delicious BBQ recipes, chicken skewers are a crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout or simply craving a flavorful meal, these BBQ chicken skewers will not disappoint. So fire up the grill and get ready for a mouthwatering adventure!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken breast, cut into bite-sized cubes
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (optional, for those who like a spicy kick)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. This marinade will infuse the chicken with incredible flavor.

  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and toss until they are well-coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you don’t have a grill, you can also use a grill pan on your stovetop.

  4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto the soaked wooden skewers, leaving a small gap between each piece. This will ensure even cooking and prevent the chicken from sticking to the grill.

  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has beautiful grill marks. Remember to rotate the skewers occasionally for even cooking.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a couple of minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy chicken.

  7. Serve the BBQ chicken skewers hot with your favorite dipping sauce or alongside a fresh salad and some grilled vegetables. The smoky flavor and tender meat will make your taste buds dance with delight.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer boneless chicken thighs instead of chicken breast, feel free to swap them out in this recipe. The dark meat will add an extra depth of flavor to your skewers.

  • Don’t limit yourself to just chicken! You can also use this marinade and grilling technique for other meats like pork or beef, or even for a vegetarian option with tofu or vegetables.

  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the marinade. Add a squeeze of lemon juice for a tangy twist or sprinkle some chili flakes for an extra kick.

  • Soaking the wooden skewers in water before grilling will prevent them from burning. If you prefer metal skewers, you can skip this step.

  • To make cleanup easier, line your grill with aluminum foil or use a grill basket to prevent the chicken from sticking.

  • If you’re hosting a BBQ party, consider serving these chicken skewers as an appetizer or part of a larger spread. They are great for sharing and will surely impress your guests.

Now that you have this quick and easy BBQ chicken skewers recipe in your arsenal, you’re ready to become the grill master of the summer. Enjoy the smoky aroma, the juicy chicken, and the compliments from your friends and family. Happy grilling!
