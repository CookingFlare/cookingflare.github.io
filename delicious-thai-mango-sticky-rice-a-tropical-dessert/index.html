Delicious Thai Mango Sticky Rice: A Tropical Dessert


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering dessert recipe. Thai Mango Sticky Rice is a delightful combination of fragrant sticky rice, fresh ripe mangoes, and a sweet coconut sauce. The contrasting flavors and textures make it a truly irresistible treat that will transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sticky rice
  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Toasted sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the sticky rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This step is important to remove any excess starch.
  2. Soak the rice in water for at least 4 hours or overnight. This will help soften the rice and ensure a perfect texture.
  3. After soaking, drain the rice and transfer it to a steamer. Steam the rice for about 20-25 minutes, or until it becomes tender and translucent.
  4. While the rice is steaming, prepare the coconut sauce. In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Heat the mixture over medium heat until the sugar dissolves, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once the sugar has dissolved, reduce the heat to low and simmer the coconut sauce for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  6. Remove the rice from the steamer and transfer it to a bowl. Pour half of the coconut sauce over the rice and gently mix it together, ensuring that each grain is coated with the sauce.
  7. Let the rice sit for 15-20 minutes, allowing it to absorb the flavors of the coconut sauce.
  8. While the rice is resting, peel and slice the ripe mangoes into thin strips or bite-sized pieces.
  9. To serve, place a generous portion of the sticky rice on a plate and top it with the sliced mangoes. Drizzle some of the remaining coconut sauce over the mangoes.
  10. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds for an added crunch and visual appeal.
  11. Enjoy your Thai Mango Sticky Rice immediately, savoring the blend of sweet mangoes, creamy coconut sauce, and sticky rice.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, you can add a scoop of coconut ice cream on top of the sticky rice and mangoes.
  • If you prefer a sweeter dessert, you can increase the amount of sugar in the coconut sauce according to your taste.
  • Don’t forget to use ripe, juicy mangoes for the best flavor. Look for mangoes that are slightly soft to the touch and have a fragrant aroma.
  • To enhance the presentation, you can shape the sticky rice into small balls or use a mold to create beautiful shapes before serving.
  • If you’re feeling adventurous, try adding a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes or a drizzle of caramel sauce for an extra layer of flavor.

Thai Mango Sticky Rice is not only a delicious dessert but also a wonderful way to experience the exotic flavors of Thailand. It’s a perfect treat for hot summer days or any time you’re craving something sweet and refreshing. Give this recipe a try and let your taste buds embark on a tropical journey. Enjoy!
