'Tis the Season Peppermint Mocha Funfetti Cake: Holiday Cheer


It’s that time of the year again: the air is chilly, the decorations are up, and the irresistible aroma of freshly brewed coffee fills the room. It can only mean one thing – the holidays are here! This season, as you plan your festive gatherings, why not try something different? Create a Tis the Season Peppermint Mocha Funfetti Cake, a sweet treat that combines the mouthwatering flavors of peppermint and mocha, while adding a delightful dash of festive sprinkles.

To begin, preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease two 8-inch cake pans with butter and dust them with flour. This will ensure that your cake doesn’t stick to the sides of the pan as it bakes.

Ingredients:

Funfetti Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ⅔ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp distilled white vinegar
  • ½ cup strong brewed coffee

Peppermint Mocha Frosting:

  • 1 ½ cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 5 cups powdered sugar
  • ¼ cup strong brewed coffee
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 oz finely crushed peppermint candies
  • 1 tsp peppermint extract

Sprinkle Topping:

  • ¼ cup rainbow sprinkles

Now, it’s time to assemble the cake! First, prepare the funfetti cake batter. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, vanilla extract, vinegar, and brewed coffee. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry, mixing until just combined.

Divide the batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans. Bake in the preheated oven for around 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.

While the cakes are cooling, it’s time to prepare the peppermint mocha frosting. Beat the softened butter in a stand mixer on medium speed until creamy and smooth. Gradually add in the powdered sugar, coffee, cocoa powder, heavy cream, crushed peppermint candies, and peppermint extract. Continue mixing on medium speed until the frosting reaches a smooth and spreadable consistency.

Once the cakes have cooled, place one layer on a serving plate and spread a generous amount of peppermint mocha frosting over the top. Place the second layer on top and cover it with the remaining frosting, ensuring the cake is uniformly frosted. For an added burst of color and festive flair, sprinkle the rainbow sprinkles over the top and sides of the cake.

Serve your Tis the Season Peppermint Mocha Funfetti Cake on a cold, wintery day, and enjoy the delights of the holiday season with family and friends. This delightful treat not only warms the heart but also entertains the tastebuds with its unique combination of flavors.

Merry Christmas and happy baking!
