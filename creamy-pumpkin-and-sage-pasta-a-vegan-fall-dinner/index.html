Creamy Pumpkin and Sage Pasta: A Vegan Fall Dinner


Fall is here, and it’s time to embrace the flavors of the season! This Creamy Pumpkin and Sage Pasta recipe is the perfect way to celebrate the arrival of autumn. With its velvety pumpkin sauce and aromatic sage, this vegan pasta dish is a comforting and delicious dinner option. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring the taste of fall to your table.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) pasta (your choice of shape)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup unsweetened plant-based milk (such as almond or oat milk)
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp dried sage
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh sage leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  3. Add the pumpkin puree to the skillet and stir well to combine with the onion and garlic. Cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Gradually add the plant-based milk to the skillet, stirring constantly to incorporate it into the pumpkin mixture. Continue to cook for another 5 minutes, until the sauce becomes creamy and smooth.

  5. Stir in the nutritional yeast and dried sage, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss gently to coat the pasta with the creamy pumpkin sauce.

  7. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the pasta to absorb the flavors of the sauce.

  8. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh sage leaves.

  9. Serve hot and enjoy this comforting vegan fall dinner!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can roast the pumpkin before pureeing it. Simply cut the pumpkin into chunks, drizzle with olive oil, and roast in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for 30-40 minutes, or until tender. Then, puree the roasted pumpkin and proceed with the recipe.

  • If you prefer a thinner sauce, you can add more plant-based milk to achieve the desired consistency.

  • Feel free to add your favorite vegetables to the pasta for added texture and nutrition. Sautéed mushrooms, roasted Brussels sprouts, or steamed broccoli would all be delicious additions.

  • To make the dish gluten-free, simply use gluten-free pasta.

  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved to suit your needs.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop or in the microwave before serving.

In Conclusion

This Creamy Pumpkin and Sage Pasta is the ultimate vegan fall dinner that will satisfy your cravings for comfort food. With its rich and creamy pumpkin sauce, fragrant sage, and perfectly cooked pasta, it’s a dish that will warm your soul and bring the flavors of autumn to your table. Whether you’re vegan or simply looking for a delicious and wholesome meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and let’s dive into the flavors of fall with this delightful pasta dish. Enjoy!
