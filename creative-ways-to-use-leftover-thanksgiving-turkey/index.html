Creative Ways to Use Leftover Thanksgiving Turkey


Welcome to our culinary adventure where we explore creative and delicious ways to make the most out of your leftover Thanksgiving turkey. After the festive feast, it’s common to find yourself with a surplus of turkey. Rather than reheating the same meal, let’s transform those leftovers into exciting new dishes that will tantalize your taste buds. From hearty soups to flavorful sandwiches, we’ve got you covered with these innovative recipes:

1. Turkey Cranberry Panini

Ingredients:

  • Sliced leftover turkey
  • Cranberry sauce
  • Sliced cheese (such as brie or cheddar)
  • Sourdough bread
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Spread cranberry sauce on one side of the sourdough bread.
  2. Layer sliced turkey and cheese on top of the cranberry sauce.
  3. Top with another slice of bread.
  4. Spread butter on the outside of the sandwich.
  5. Grill the panini until the bread is crispy and the cheese is melted.

2. Turkey and Wild Rice Soup

Ingredients:

  • Leftover turkey, shredded
  • Cooked wild rice
  • Carrots, celery, and onions (diced)
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Heavy cream
  • Fresh herbs (such as thyme and rosemary)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, sauté the diced vegetables until soft.
  2. Add the shredded turkey, cooked wild rice, and broth to the pot.
  3. Simmer until the flavors meld together.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and fresh herbs.
  5. Season with salt and pepper before serving.

3. Turkey Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Leftover turkey, thinly sliced
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Lettuce
  • Tortillas
  • Ranch dressing

Instructions:

  1. Lay out a tortilla and spread ranch dressing on it.
  2. Layer slices of turkey, avocado, tomato, and lettuce on top.
  3. Roll up the tortilla tightly.
  4. Slice the wrap in half for easy serving.

4. Turkey Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Leftover turkey, cubed
  • Cooked pasta of your choice
  • Pesto sauce
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté cherry tomatoes in olive oil.
  3. Add cubed turkey to the pan to warm through.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with pesto sauce.
  5. Mix in the turkey and tomato mixture.
  6. Serve topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

5. Turkey Cranberry Salad

Ingredients:

  • Leftover turkey, shredded
  • Mixed greens
  • Dried cranberries
  • Toasted pecans
  • Feta cheese
  • Balsamic vinaigrette

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shredded turkey, mixed greens, cranberries, pecans, and feta cheese.
  2. Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette over the salad.
  3. Toss gently to mix all the ingredients.
  4. Enjoy a refreshing and satisfying salad.

There you have it – a collection of inventive recipes to elevate your leftover Thanksgiving turkey into new culinary delights. Whether you’re craving a comforting soup or a zesty wrap, these ideas will breathe new life into your post-holiday meals. Get creative in the kitchen and savor the flavors of these delightful dishes made with love from your Thanksgiving leftovers. Happy cooking!
