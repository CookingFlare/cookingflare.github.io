10 Mouthwatering Wrap Recipes for a Gluten-Free Feast


If you’re on a gluten-free diet or simply looking to switch up your meals, wraps are a versatile and satisfying option. From savory to sweet, there’s a wrap recipe for every craving. Here are 10 mouthwatering wrap recipes that are not only delicious but also entirely gluten-free.

1. Grilled Chicken Caesar Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free wrap
  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Lay out the gluten-free wrap and place the grilled chicken breast in the center.
  2. Add a handful of fresh, crispy romaine lettuce on top of the chicken.
  3. Drizzle with gluten-free Caesar dressing and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Roll up the wrap tightly and slice it in half.

Enjoy the classic flavors of a Caesar salad in a convenient wrap form.

2. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free wrap
  • Hummus
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of hummus onto the gluten-free wrap.
  2. Layer the sliced cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and Kalamata olives on top of the hummus.
  3. Crumble feta cheese over the veggies.
  4. Roll up the wrap and secure it with a toothpick if needed.

This colorful and flavorful wrap is a taste of the Mediterranean in every bite.

3. Thai Peanut Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free wrap
  • Shredded cooked chicken
  • Shredded carrots
  • Sliced red bell pepper
  • Chopped peanuts
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Thai peanut sauce

Instructions:

  1. Place the shredded cooked chicken in the center of the gluten-free wrap.
  2. Add a handful of shredded carrots and sliced red bell pepper on top of the chicken.
  3. Sprinkle with chopped peanuts and fresh cilantro.
  4. Drizzle with Thai peanut sauce before rolling up the wrap.

The fusion of savory, sweet, and nutty flavors makes this wrap a delightful gluten-free option.

4. Caprese Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free wrap
  • Fresh mozzarella slices
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Layer the fresh mozzarella slices, sliced tomatoes, and fresh basil leaves on the gluten-free wrap.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze for a burst of tangy sweetness.
  3. Roll up the wrap and cut it into pinwheels for a visually stunning appetizer.

This Italian-inspired wrap brings together the classic flavors of Caprese salad in a handheld form.

5. Breakfast Burrito Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free wrap
  • Scrambled eggs
  • Cooked breakfast sausage or bacon
  • Diced bell peppers
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salsa

Instructions:

  1. Fill the gluten-free wrap with scrambled eggs, cooked breakfast sausage or bacon, and diced bell peppers.
  2. Sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese and add a dollop of salsa.
  3. Fold in the sides of the wrap and roll it up into a hearty breakfast burrito.

Start your day with a protein-packed and gluten-free breakfast that’s perfect for on-the-go mornings.

6. Teriyaki Tofu Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free wrap
  • Baked or pan-fried tofu slices
  • Sliced avocado
  • Shredded carrots
  • Cooked quinoa
  • Teriyaki sauce

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the baked or pan-fried tofu slices in the center of the gluten-free wrap.
  2. Add sliced avocado, shredded carrots, and cooked quinoa on top of the tofu.
  3. Drizzle with teriyaki sauce and wrap up the flavorsome filling.

This vegan-friendly wrap offers a delightful combination of textures and umami flavors.

7. Smoked Salmon and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free wrap
  • Smoked salmon
  • Sliced avocado
  • Cream cheese
  • Red onion slices
  • Fresh dill

Instructions:

  1. Spread a layer of cream cheese onto the gluten-free wrap.
  2. Layer the smoked salmon, sliced avocado, red onion slices, and fresh dill on top of the cream cheese.
  3. Roll up the wrap and slice it into elegant pinwheels for an impressive appetizer.

Indulge in the luxurious combination of smoked salmon and creamy avocado, all wrapped up in gluten-free goodness.

8. BBQ Pulled Pork Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free wrap
  • Slow-cooked pulled pork
  • Coleslaw
  • Pickles
  • BBQ sauce
