Satisfying Low Carb Taco Stuffed Avocados


If you’re looking for a delicious, low-carb twist on traditional tacos, then you’re in for a treat with these satisfying low carb taco stuffed avocados. Packed with flavor and healthy fats, this recipe is not only a feast for your taste buds but also a nutritious option for those following a low-carb lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe avocados
  • 1 pound ground turkey or beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, hot sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Avocados:

    • Slice the avocados in half and remove the pits. Using a spoon, carefully scoop out some of the flesh to create a larger hollow for the filling. Place the scooped avocado in a bowl and mash it with a fork.

  2. Cook the Meat:

    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
    • Add the ground turkey or beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
    • Stir in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few minutes until the meat is well coated with the spices.

  3. Assemble the Filling:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked meat, mashed avocado, diced cherry tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice. Mix until all the ingredients are well combined.

  4. Stuff the Avocados:

    • Spoon the taco filling into the hollowed-out avocados, dividing it evenly among them.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Top the stuffed avocados with your choice of optional toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, or hot sauce.

  6. Optional Baking Step:

    • If desired, place the stuffed avocados in a baking dish and bake at 350°F for 5-10 minutes until heated through.

Why You’ll Love These Taco Stuffed Avocados

  • Low-Carb Goodness: Avocados are packed with healthy fats and are naturally low in carbs, making them an excellent choice for a low-carb diet.
  • Versatile: You can customize the filling with your favorite taco ingredients, making it a versatile dish that suits your taste preferences.
  • Nutrient-Rich: This recipe is loaded with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients from the avocado and other fresh ingredients.
  • Satisfying: The combination of creamy avocado and flavorful taco filling is incredibly satisfying, leaving you feeling full and content.

Final Thoughts

These satisfying low carb taco stuffed avocados are a delightful way to enjoy the flavors of tacos while keeping your carb intake in check. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a crowd-pleasing party appetizer, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab some ripe avocados and get ready to indulge in a delicious and nutritious culinary experience!
