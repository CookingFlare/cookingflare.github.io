Tasty Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick, delicious, and healthy meal that’s bursting with flavor, then this Tasty Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken recipe is just what you need. The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, and this recipe takes full advantage of its speedy cooking capabilities to produce tender, juicy chicken infused with the zesty tang of lemon and the aromatic notes of garlic. This dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion, and it’s sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. This will ensure that the chicken is well seasoned and flavorful.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts and sear them for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown. Searing the chicken will lock in the juices and add depth of flavor to the dish.

  3. Add the minced garlic, dried oregano, and dried thyme to the Instant Pot, and saute for another 1-2 minutes, until the garlic is fragrant and the herbs are toasted.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Give everything a good stir to ensure that the flavors are well combined.

  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then do a quick release to let out any remaining pressure.

  7. Carefully remove the chicken breasts from the Instant Pot and transfer them to a serving platter. Tent them with foil to keep warm.

  8. Set the Instant Pot back to “Saute” mode and let the sauce simmer for a few minutes to reduce and thicken slightly. This will intensify the flavors and create a luscious sauce for the chicken.

  9. Once the sauce has thickened, pour it over the chicken breasts and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

Serving Suggestions:

This Tasty Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken pairs beautifully with a side of fluffy rice, creamy mashed potatoes, or a crisp green salad. The bright, citrusy flavors of the chicken make it a versatile dish that can be enjoyed with a variety of accompaniments. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a satisfying meal, this recipe is sure to impress.

Final Thoughts:

The Tasty Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken recipe is a testament to the power of the Instant Pot to produce succulent, flavorful meals in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional cooking methods. The combination of zesty lemon, pungent garlic, and aromatic herbs creates a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more. So, fire up your Instant Pot and give this recipe a try – you won’t be disappointed!
