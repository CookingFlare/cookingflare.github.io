Irresistible Cinnamon Roll Recipe: Perfect for Brunch


Are you looking for the perfect brunch treat to impress your family and friends? Look no further! These irresistible cinnamon rolls are sure to steal the show and leave everyone craving for more. With their soft, fluffy texture and the warm, comforting aroma of cinnamon, these rolls are a brunch staple that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup water
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

For the filling:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the cream cheese glaze:

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast. Mix well.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk and water until warm (around 110°F). Pour the warm liquid over the dry ingredients.
  3. Add the melted butter and egg to the bowl. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  6. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon until well combined.
  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a rectangular shape, about ¼-inch thick.
  8. Spread the filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  9. Starting from one of the long sides, tightly roll up the dough into a log.
  10. Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 12 equal-sized rolls.
  11. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, cover with a kitchen towel, and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
  12. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) while the rolls are rising.
  13. Once the rolls have risen, bake them in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.
  14. While the rolls are baking, prepare the cream cheese glaze. In a medium bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and continue beating until smooth.
  15. Remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before drizzling the cream cheese glaze over the top.
  16. Serve warm and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra flavor boost, add a handful of chopped nuts or raisins to the filling before rolling up the dough.
  • If you prefer a less sweet glaze, you can reduce the amount of powdered sugar in the cream cheese glaze or omit it altogether.
  • These cinnamon rolls are best when served fresh and warm. However, you can store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. Just reheat them in the microwave for a few seconds before serving.
