Salad Spotlight: Highlighting a Different Ingredient in Every Recipe

Salads are a versatile and refreshing addition to any meal. They can be a simple side dish or a satisfying main course, depending on the ingredients used. In this Salad Spotlight series, we will be exploring the vast world of salads by highlighting a different ingredient in every recipe. Today, we shine the spotlight on the humble cucumber. Cucumber and Tomato Salad Ingredients: 2 cucumbers, thinly sliced 2 large tomatoes, diced 1 red onion, thinly sliced 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled Juice of 1 lemon 2 tablespoons olive oil Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: In a large bowl, combine the sliced cucumbers, diced tomatoes, and sliced red onion. Add the chopped parsley and mint to the bowl, and gently toss to combine. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the cucumber and tomato mixture, and toss until well coated. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top, and give it a final toss. Let the salad sit for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve the cucumber and tomato salad chilled, and enjoy! Health Benefits of Cucumbers Cucumbers, with their high water content and refreshing crunch, are a fantastic addition to any salad. Not only do they add a refreshing element, but they also offer numerous health benefits. Cucumbers are low in calories and packed with essential vitamins and minerals.
