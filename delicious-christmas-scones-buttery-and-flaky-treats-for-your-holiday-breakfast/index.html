Delicious Christmas Scones: Buttery and Flaky Treats for Your Holiday Breakfast


The holiday season is a time for warmth, joy, and delightful treats. What better way to start your Christmas morning than with a batch of delicious Christmas scones? These buttery and flaky treats are the perfect addition to your holiday breakfast spread, and they are sure to impress your family and friends. In this blog post, I’ll share with you my favorite recipe for Christmas scones, along with some tips and tricks to make them extra special.

Ingredients

For the Scones:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream

For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. Add the cold butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Stir in the dried cranberries and white chocolate chips.

  5. In a separate bowl, mix the vanilla extract and heavy cream. Pour this mixture into the dry ingredients and gently stir until the dough comes together.

  6. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently a few times until it holds together.

  7. Pat the dough into a circle about 1 inch thick. Use a sharp knife to cut the circle into 8 wedges.

  8. Transfer the scones to the prepared baking sheet and bake for 15-18 minutes, or until they are golden brown.

  9. While the scones are cooling, prepare the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract. Drizzle the glaze over the cooled scones.

Tips for Perfect Christmas Scones

  • Keep it Cold: It’s essential to keep the butter and cream cold throughout the process to ensure the scones turn out light and flaky.

  • Don’t Overwork the Dough: Once the wet and dry ingredients are combined, avoid over-kneading the dough. Overworking can result in tough scones.

  • Customize the Add-Ins: While this recipe calls for cranberries and white chocolate, feel free to get creative with your add-ins. Dried cherries, chopped nuts, or even citrus zest can add a unique twist to your scones.

  • Glazing Technique: For a professional touch, use a piping bag or a zip-lock bag with the corner snipped off to drizzle the glaze over the scones.

Serving Suggestions

These Christmas scones are best served fresh from the oven with a steaming cup of coffee or tea. They also make a delightful addition to a holiday brunch or tea party. If you’re feeling extra indulgent, serve them with clotted cream and your favorite jam for a truly luxurious treat.

Conclusion

There’s something truly special about the aroma of freshly baked scones wafting through the house during the holiday season. With their buttery, flaky texture and delightful bursts of cranberries and white chocolate, these Christmas scones are sure to become a new holiday tradition in your home. So, this Christmas, treat yourself and your loved ones to these delightful homemade scones and make your holiday breakfast extra special.
