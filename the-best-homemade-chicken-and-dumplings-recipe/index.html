The Best Homemade Chicken and Dumplings Recipe


Chicken and dumplings - a classic comfort food dish that warms the heart and soul. There’s nothing quite like a steaming hot bowl of tender chicken and fluffy, pillowy dumplings to cure what ails you. And the best part? It’s incredibly easy to make at home, with just a few simple ingredients and some basic cooking techniques.

开始

To start, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil, for greasing the pot

Now, let’s get started!

做鸡肉

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

做云豆

In the same pot, add the diced onion, minced garlic, sliced carrots, and sliced celery. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.

做浓缩汁

Add the chicken broth, thyme, basil, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine, then bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced slightly and the flavors have melded together.

做云豆的面团

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, eggs, and salt. Gradually add the warm milk, stirring with a fork until the dough comes together in a sticky ball.

做云豆

Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes, until the dough is smooth and elastic. Cut the dough into small pieces and roll each piece into a ball. Flatten each ball slightly into a disk shape.

做云豆的烹饪

When the chicken broth has finished simmering, add the dumplings to the pot. Cover the pot and cook for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the dumplings are cooked through and fluffy.

组合

Once the dumplings are cooked, add the browned chicken back to the pot and stir to combine. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh herbs if desired.

And that’s it! A delicious, comforting bowl of homemade chicken and dumplings, made with love and care. This recipe is perfect for a chilly winter’s night, or anytime you need a little pick-me-up. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the warm, fuzzy feelings that come with a hearty, homemade meal.

###Tips和变通

  • Use leftover chicken or turkey for added convenience.
  • Substitute vegetable broth for a vegetarian option.
  • Add some diced bell peppers or mushrooms for added flavor and nutrition.
  • Serve with a side of crusty bread or a green salad for a well-rounded meal.
