Homemade Strawberry Shortcake: A Sweet Summertime Treat


Welcome to our kitchen! Today, we’re going to share with you a delightful recipe that perfectly captures the essence of summertime: Homemade Strawberry Shortcake. This classic dessert is a crowd-pleaser, combining fresh strawberries, fluffy cake, and creamy whipped cream. Get ready to indulge in this sweet treat that will surely become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

For the shortcake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the strawberry filling:

  • 4 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the whipped cream:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Prepare the shortcake: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Create a well in the center of the mixture and pour in the milk and vanilla extract. Stir until just combined, being careful not to overmix. The dough should be slightly sticky.

  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together. Pat the dough into a 1-inch thick rectangle and cut it into 6 equal-sized squares.

  4. Place the shortcake squares on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from the oven and let them cool completely.

  5. Prepare the strawberry filling: In a medium bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, granulated sugar, and lemon juice. Stir gently to coat the strawberries in the sugar and lemon juice mixture. Allow the strawberries to macerate for at least 30 minutes to release their natural juices.

  6. Prepare the whipped cream: In a chilled mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form. Be careful not to overbeat, as it can result in a grainy texture.

  7. Assemble the strawberry shortcake: Slice each cooled shortcake square in half horizontally. Place the bottom half onto a plate or serving dish. Spoon a generous amount of the macerated strawberries onto the bottom half of the shortcake. Top with a dollop of whipped cream. Place the other half of the shortcake on top and repeat the process.

  8. Garnish and serve: Finish each strawberry shortcake with a final dollop of whipped cream and a fresh strawberry on top. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try using different fruits like blueberries, raspberries, or peaches. The possibilities are endless!
  • If you prefer a sweeter shortcake, consider sprinkling a little powdered sugar on top of each square before baking.
  • To save time, you can prepare the shortcakes and whipped cream in advance. When ready to serve, simply assemble the strawberry shortcakes and garnish.
  • For an extra special touch, drizzle some melted chocolate over the whipped cream for an added decadence.

Homemade Strawberry Shortcake is the epitome of a light and refreshing summer dessert. The combination of the sweet strawberries, tender shortcake, and fluffy whipped cream creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply want to treat yourself, this timeless dessert is sure to impress. So go ahead, dive into this luscious creation and let the summertime vibes take over!
