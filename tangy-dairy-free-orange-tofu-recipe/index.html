Tangy Dairy-Free Orange Tofu Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy dairy-free recipe that’s bursting with flavor, then you’re in for a treat. This tangy dairy-free orange tofu recipe is a perfect combination of zesty orange flavors, savory tofu, and a delightful blend of spices. Not only is this dish a delight for your taste buds, but it’s also packed with protein and nutrients, making it a wholesome addition to your meal rotation.

Ingredients

For this tangy dairy-free orange tofu recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 block of extra-firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • Sliced green onions for garnish
  • Cooked rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Tofu

Begin by draining the tofu and pressing it to remove excess moisture. Once drained, cut the tofu into bite-sized cubes and toss them in cornstarch until evenly coated.

Step 2: Pan-Fry the Tofu

In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the coated tofu cubes and cook until they are golden and crispy on all sides. This usually takes about 8-10 minutes. Once done, transfer the tofu to a plate lined with paper towels to absorb any excess oil.

Step 3: Prepare the Orange Sauce

In the same skillet, combine the orange juice, soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, minced garlic, grated ginger, red pepper flakes, and orange zest. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken slightly.

Step 4: Combine and Serve

Return the cooked tofu to the skillet with the orange sauce, tossing gently to coat the tofu evenly. Let it simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the tofu to absorb the flavors of the sauce.

Once the tofu is coated with the tangy orange sauce, remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the dish with sliced green onions. Serve the tangy dairy-free orange tofu over cooked rice or quinoa, and enjoy a burst of citrusy goodness with every bite.

Nutritional Benefits

This tangy dairy-free orange tofu recipe not only excites the palate but also offers several nutritional benefits. Tofu is an excellent source of plant-based protein and is rich in essential amino acids. The orange juice provides a boost of vitamin C, while the ginger and garlic contribute their immune-boosting properties. Additionally, this recipe is naturally dairy-free, making it suitable for individuals with lactose intolerance or those following a dairy-free diet.

Final Thoughts

The tangy dairy-free orange tofu recipe is a vibrant and flavorful dish that’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Whether you’re a seasoned tofu enthusiast or new to cooking with plant-based ingredients, this recipe offers a delightful fusion of sweet, savory, and tangy flavors that will leave you craving for more. Try it out for your next meal and delight in the delightful combination of zesty orange and savory tofu.
