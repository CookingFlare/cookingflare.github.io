Speedy Pizza Recipes: Quick Meals for Pizza Night


When it comes to mealtime, there's nothing quite like the convenience and excitement of pizza night. But who says pizza can't be quick and easy to make? In this post, we'll explore a range of speedy pizza recipes that are perfect for busy home cooks who want to wow their families and friends without sacrificing flavor or time. Classic Margherita Pizza in 15 Minutes First up, we have the classic Italian favorite - the Margherita pizza. This timeless recipe requires just a few key ingredients, and can be cooked in the blink of an eye. Here's what you'll need:

1 lb pizza dough (homemade or store-bought) 1 cup crushed tomatoes 2 tbsp olive oil 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves Salt and pepper, to taste

Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Roll out the dough to a circle or rectangle shape, whichever suits your preference. Spread a thin layer of olive oil over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Spoon the crushed tomatoes over the dough, followed by a layer of sliced mozzarella cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer the pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with fresh basil leaves before slicing and serving. Pesto Chicken Pizza in 20 Minutes For a flavorful twist on the classic Margherita, try this pesto chicken pizza recipe.

1 lb pizza dough (homemade or store-bought) 1 cup pesto sauce 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese 1/4 cup diced chicken breast, cooked 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves Salt and pepper, to taste

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough to a circle or rectangle shape. Spread a thin layer of pesto sauce over the dough, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese and shredded parmesan cheese. Top with diced chicken breast and sprinkle with chopped fresh basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer the pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. BBQ Chicken Pizza in 20 Minutes This BBQ chicken pizza recipe is perfect for a quick and satisfying meal that's full of flavor.

1 lb pizza dough (homemade or store-bought) 1 cup BBQ sauce 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1/2 cup diced cooked chicken breast 1/4 cup chopped red onion 1/4 cup chopped cilantro leaves Salt and pepper, to taste

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough to a circle or rectangle shape. Spread a thin layer of BBQ sauce over the dough, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese and shredded cheddar cheese. Top with diced cooked chicken breast, chopped red onion, and chopped cilantro leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer the pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Quick Vegetable Pizza in 20 Minutes This veggie-packed pizza is perfect for vegetarians and vegans alike.

1 lb pizza dough (homemade or store-bought) 1 cup tomato sauce 8 oz shredded vegan mozzarella cheese 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, zucchini, onion, and mushrooms) 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves Salt and pepper, to taste

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough to a circle or rectangle shape. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, followed by a layer of shredded vegan mozzarella cheese and mixed vegetables. Sprinkle with chopped fresh basil leaves and season with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer the pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Calzone in 15 Minutes For a fun twist on traditional pizza, try making a calzone. This folded pizza is filled with cheese, vegetables, and meat for a satisfying meal that's perfect for kids and adults alike.

1 lb pizza dough (homemade or store-bought) 1/2 cup shredded ricotta cheese 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1 cup mixed shredded vegetables (such as bell peppers, onion, and mushrooms) 1/2 cup shredded meat (such as ground beef, Italian sausage, or chicken)

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the dough to a square shape. In a bowl, mix together shredded ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, and shredded cheddar cheese. Divide the dough in half and roll out each half to a circle. Place a quarter of the cheese mixture in the center of each circle, followed by a quarter of the shredded vegetables and shredded meat. Fold the dough in half to enclose the filling and press the edges to seal. Use a fork to crimp the edges. Transfer the calzone to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Tips for Perfect Pizza Night While these recipes are quick and easy to make, there are a few tips to keep in mind to ensure the perfect pizza night:

Use a pizza stone or baking sheet to achieve a crispy crust. Use a variety of toppings to suit your preferences and dietary needs. Keep things simple by starting with a store-bought pizza dough or making your own dough ahead of time. Experiment with new recipes and flavors to keep your pizza nights exciting and creative. Don't be afraid to get creative and try new toppings, cheeses, and sauces to find your new favorite combinations.

With these speedy pizza recipes and helpful tips, you're ready to create a pizza night that's sure to impress and satisfy your family and friends. Whether you're a seasoned cook or a novice in the kitchen, the options are endless and the fun is just a roll away.
