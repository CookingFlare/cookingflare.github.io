Crispy Honey Garlic Pork Stir Fry: A Sweet and Sticky Delight


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further than this Crispy Honey Garlic Pork Stir Fry recipe! This dish combines the savory goodness of crispy pork with the sweet and sticky flavors of honey and garlic, creating a mouthwatering combination that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Pork:

  • 1 pound of pork tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

For the Stir Fry Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of water

For the Stir Fry:

  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork:

    • In a bowl, combine the sliced pork with cornstarch, salt, and black pepper. Toss until the pork is evenly coated.
    • In a large skillet or wok, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the pork to the skillet in a single layer and cook until golden and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove the pork from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Make the Stir Fry Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, rice vinegar, sesame oil, cornstarch, and water until well combined.

  3. Stir Fry the Vegetables:

    • In the same skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion to the skillet and cook, stirring frequently, until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Combine and Simmer:

    • Return the crispy pork to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the stir fry sauce over the pork and vegetables, tossing to coat everything evenly. Allow the sauce to simmer and thicken, about 2-3 minutes.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the sauce has thickened, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the dish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions. Serve the Crispy Honey Garlic Pork Stir Fry over steamed rice or noodles, and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the stir fry sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir fry based on your preferences. Broccoli, snap peas, or carrots would all be great additions.
  • If you prefer a leaner option, you can use pork loin instead of pork tenderloin.

Final Thoughts

This Crispy Honey Garlic Pork Stir Fry is a delightful combination of crispy, tender pork and a sweet and savory sauce that will tantalize your taste buds. It’s a versatile dish that can be easily adapted to suit your preferences, making it a perfect addition to your weekly meal rotation. Whether you’re a cooking enthusiast or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is approachable and guaranteed to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to create a sweet and sticky masterpiece that’s sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection!
