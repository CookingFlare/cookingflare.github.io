Savory Quiches for Two: Brunch Delights


Are you looking to impress your loved one with a delicious and elegant brunch? Look no further than these savory quiches for two! Quiches are a versatile and satisfying dish that can be enjoyed any time of the day, but they truly shine as a centerpiece for a cozy brunch. With their buttery crusts and creamy fillings, these individual-sized quiches are sure to delight your taste buds and make your brunch experience unforgettable.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Gruyere, or Swiss)
  • 1/4 cup diced cooked bacon or ham
  • 1/4 cup diced vegetables (spinach, mushrooms, bell peppers, or onions)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease two mini quiche pans or ramekins.

  2. Roll out the pre-made pie crust on a lightly floured surface. Using a cookie cutter or a glass, cut out two circles of dough that are slightly larger than the diameter of your quiche pans or ramekins.

  3. Carefully press the dough circles into the pans or ramekins, making sure to cover the bottom and sides evenly. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edges.

  4. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, and milk until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheese and half of the diced bacon or ham onto the bottom of each quiche crust. Add the diced vegetables on top.

  6. Pour the egg mixture evenly into each quiche pan or ramekin, ensuring that the fillings are covered.

  7. Place the quiches on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the quiches are set and the crust is golden brown.

  8. Remove the quiches from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. The quiches can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature.

  9. Serve these delightful savory quiches alongside a fresh salad or some roasted potatoes for a complete brunch experience. You can also pair them with a mimosa or a cup of your favorite hot beverage.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different fillings to customize your quiches. Try using smoked salmon, asparagus, or feta cheese for a unique twist.
  • If you don’t have mini quiche pans or ramekins, you can use a regular pie dish and adjust the baking time accordingly.
  • Make these quiches ahead of time by preparing them the night before and refrigerating them overnight. Just pop them in the oven in the morning for a hassle-free brunch.
  • Don’t be afraid to get creative with the presentation. Garnish your quiches with fresh herbs, cherry tomatoes, or a drizzle of balsamic glaze for an extra touch of elegance.

There you have it – a simple yet delicious recipe for savory quiches for two that will elevate your brunch to new heights. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself and your loved one, these quiches are the perfect way to start your day. So, put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the flavors of a homemade brunch delight!
