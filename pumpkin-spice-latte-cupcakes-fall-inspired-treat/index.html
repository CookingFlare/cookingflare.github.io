Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes: Fall-Inspired Treat


Ah, the beauty of fall – crisp air, vibrant foliage, and pumpkin spice in all its glory. One of the best ways to celebrate the arrival of the season is by baking seasonal goodies, and there’s no treat more fitting than pumpkin spice latte cupcakes! These deliciously moist cupcakes are a delightful twist on the classic drink that we all continue to love, and for good reason. This post will walk you through the process of creating these delightful fall-inspired treats in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line two 12-cup muffin tins with cupcake liners.

  2. In a large bowl, beat the sugar and softened butter until creamy and light.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time to the mixture, mixing well after each addition.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, spices, and salt. You will create the magic of autumn with these ingredients!

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk, and mix until just combined.

  6. In another bowl, whisk together the melted butter, vegetable oil, pumpkin puree, coffee, and buttermilk. Add this to the flour mixture, making sure everything is well combined.

  7. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.

  8. Bake for 18-22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  9. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely in the pan.

  10. For the frosting, beat the cream cheese and vanilla extract until smooth. Gradually add the powdered sugar, spices, and mix until light and fluffy.

  11. Pipe the frosting onto the cooled cupcakes and enjoy an irresistible taste of fall!

The delightful combination of flavors – the subtle sweetness of the pumpkin, the rich aroma of the spices, the richness of the coffee, and the tanginess of the cream cheese frosting – are a surefire way to transport you right into the heart of fall.

As you bite into these cupcakes, relish the feeling of the changing seasons. Indulge in the textures and tastes that bring back memories of cozy knit sweaters, crackling fires, and the unmistakable scent of pumpkin spice in the air. These Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes are a perfect treat to not only delight your taste buds but also immeraged the warm feeling of the season.

Now, shut the door to your kitchen, put on your favorite fall-themed socks and enjoy the fruits of your labor in the heartwarming essence of fall! Happy baking, and may your cupcake endeavor be filled with the aroma of pumpkin spice and everything nice!
