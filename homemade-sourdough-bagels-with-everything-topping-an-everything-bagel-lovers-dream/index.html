Homemade Sourdough Bagels with Everything Topping: An Everything Bagel Lover's Dream


Are you a fan of the classic everything bagel? The delicious combination of poppy seeds, sesame seeds, onion, garlic, and salt creates a flavor explosion that’s hard to resist. What if I told you that you could take that beloved flavor and combine it with the chewy, tangy goodness of a sourdough bagel? Yes, you heard that right! In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the process of making homemade sourdough bagels with a delightful everything topping that will elevate your bagel game to a whole new level.

Ingredients

For the Sourdough Starter:

  • 1/2 cup of active sourdough starter
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar

For the Bagel Dough:

  • 3 1/2 cups of bread flour
  • 1 cup of active sourdough starter
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 cup of warm water

For the Everything Topping:

  • 2 tablespoons of poppy seeds
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of dried minced onion
  • 1 tablespoon of dried minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of coarse salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Sourdough Starter

  1. In a glass or plastic container, combine 1/2 cup of active sourdough starter, 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup of water, and 1 teaspoon of sugar. Mix well, cover loosely, and let it sit at room temperature for 12 hours, until bubbly and active.

Step 2: Make the Bagel Dough

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 1/2 cups of bread flour, 1 cup of active sourdough starter, 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt, 1 tablespoon of sugar, and 1 cup of warm water. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  2. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 10-12 minutes, until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  3. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a kitchen towel, and let it rise at room temperature for 4-6 hours, or until doubled in size.

Step 3: Shape and Boil the Bagels

  1. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down and divide it into 8 equal pieces. Shape each piece into a ball, then use your thumb to poke a hole through the center to form a bagel shape.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Boil the bagels, 2-3 at a time, for 1-2 minutes per side.
  3. Remove the boiled bagels from the water and place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

Step 4: Prepare the Everything Topping and Bake

  1. In a small bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of poppy seeds, 2 tablespoons of sesame seeds, 1 tablespoon of dried minced onion, 1 tablespoon of dried minced garlic, and 1 tablespoon of coarse salt.
  2. Sprinkle the everything topping generously over the boiled bagels.
  3. Bake the bagels in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

Enjoy Your Homemade Sourdough Bagels!

Once you pull these golden, aromatic sourdough bagels out of the oven, you’ll know that your efforts have paid off. The chewy texture and tangy flavor of the sourdough combined with the irresistible everything topping make these bagels a true delight for any bagel enthusiast. Whether you enjoy them plain, with cream cheese, or as the base for a breakfast sandwich, these homemade sourdough bagels are sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So, roll up your sleeves, get your sourdough starter ready, and embark on a bagel-making adventure that will leave you with a batch of delicious, homemade everything bagels that will rival any bakery’s offerings.
