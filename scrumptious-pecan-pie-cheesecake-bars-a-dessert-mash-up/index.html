Scrumptious Pecan Pie Cheesecake Bars: A Dessert Mash-Up


Are you ready to take your dessert game to the next level? Look no further than these delectable Scrumptious Pecan Pie Cheesecake Bars! This dessert mash-up combines the rich, buttery goodness of pecan pie with the creamy indulgence of cheesecake, resulting in a treat that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Pecan Pie Layer:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup dark corn syrup
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups pecans, chopped

For the Cheesecake Layer:

  • 16 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Crust:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then set aside to cool.

Pecan Pie Layer:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the granulated sugar, corn syrup, and melted butter. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar is dissolved. Remove from the heat and let the mixture cool for 5 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the beaten eggs and vanilla extract. Gradually pour the sugar mixture into the egg mixture, whisking constantly. Stir in the chopped pecans. Pour the pecan pie filling over the cooled crust.

Cheesecake Layer:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract. Pour the cheesecake mixture over the pecan pie filling.

Baking:

  1. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature.
  2. Once cooled, refrigerate the bars for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to set.

Serving

Once the Scrumptious Pecan Pie Cheesecake Bars are fully set, use the parchment paper overhang to lift them out of the pan. Cut into squares and serve. These bars are best enjoyed chilled and can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Conclusion

These Scrumptious Pecan Pie Cheesecake Bars are a delightful combination of two beloved desserts, and they are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself, this dessert mash-up is guaranteed to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create a memorable sweet treat that will have everyone asking for the recipe. Enjoy!
