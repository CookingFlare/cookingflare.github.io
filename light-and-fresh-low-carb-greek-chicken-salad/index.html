Light and Fresh Low Carb Greek Chicken Salad


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s bursting with flavor and easy to make? Look no further than this Light and Fresh Low Carb Greek Chicken Salad! Packed with lean protein, crisp vegetables, and zesty Mediterranean flavors, this salad is the perfect dish for a light lunch or dinner. Plus, it’s low in carbs, making it a great option for anyone watching their carb intake.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with dried oregano, thyme, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until cooked through. Once done, set the chicken aside to cool.

  2. While the chicken is cooling, prepare the salad by combining the diced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, Kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, and chopped parsley in a large bowl.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, and lemon juice to create the dressing.

  4. Once the chicken has cooled, slice it into strips and add it to the salad. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and chicken, then gently toss everything together until well combined.

  5. Serve the Greek chicken salad immediately, or refrigerate it for an hour to allow the flavors to meld together even more.

Why You’ll Love This Salad

This Light and Fresh Low Carb Greek Chicken Salad is not only delicious and satisfying, but it’s also packed with nutritious ingredients. The lean protein from the chicken, the vibrant vegetables, and the heart-healthy olive oil make this salad a wholesome choice for any meal. The Mediterranean-inspired flavors of feta, olives, and herbs add a burst of freshness that will have your taste buds singing.

Nutritional Benefits

This salad is not only low in carbs, but it’s also rich in essential nutrients. The chicken provides a lean source of protein, while the vegetables offer a plethora of vitamins and minerals. The olive oil and feta cheese contribute healthy fats, making this salad a well-rounded and nourishing option.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re following a low carb diet or simply looking for a light and flavorful meal, this Light and Fresh Low Carb Greek Chicken Salad is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. It’s simple to make, bursting with Mediterranean flavors, and perfect for a quick and healthy meal any day of the week. Enjoy the fresh and vibrant taste of Greece right in your own kitchen with this delightful salad!
