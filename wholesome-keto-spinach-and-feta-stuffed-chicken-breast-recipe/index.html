Wholesome Keto Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe


When it comes to delicious, nutritious meals that fit into a ketogenic lifestyle, few dishes can compete with a well-prepared stuffed chicken breast. Today, we’re diving into a recipe that combines the rich flavors of spinach and feta cheese, creating a dish that is not only satisfying but also packed with protein and healthy fats. This Wholesome Keto Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion, and it’s sure to impress everyone at your table.

Why Choose This Recipe?

This recipe is designed for those who are following a ketogenic diet, which emphasizes low-carb, high-fat food. The combination of tender chicken, savory spinach, and tangy feta cheese provides a wonderful flavor profile while keeping the net carbs low. Not only is this meal healthy, but it’s also incredibly easy to prepare. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

To make the Wholesome Keto Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast, you’ll need the following ingredients:

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 6 ounces each)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (or avocado oil)

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a kick)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon fresh basil, chopped (or 1/2 teaspoon dried basil)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For Garnish:

  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for serving)
  • Lemon wedges (optional)

Kitchen Tools You’ll Need

  • Sharp knife
  • Cutting board
  • Mixing bowl
  • Skillet or frying pan
  • Toothpicks or kitchen twine
  • Oven-safe baking dish
  • Meat mallet or rolling pin (optional, for flattening chicken)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Filling

  1. Sauté the Spinach: In a skillet over medium heat, add a splash of olive oil. Once hot, add the chopped spinach and sauté for about 2-3 minutes, or until it’s wilted. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

  2. Mix the Filling: In a mixing bowl, combine the sautéed spinach, crumbled feta cheese, softened cream cheese, minced garlic, red pepper flakes (if using), lemon juice, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir until well combined.

Step 2: Prepare the Chicken Breasts

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Flatten the Chicken: Place each chicken breast between two pieces of plastic wrap or parchment paper. Using a meat mallet or rolling pin, gently pound the chicken to about 1/2 inch thick. This ensures even cooking and makes it easier to stuff.

  3. Season the Chicken: Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

Step 3: Stuff the Chicken

  1. Fill the Chicken: Take a generous amount of the spinach and feta filling and place it in the center of each chicken breast.

  2. Roll and Secure: Carefully roll the chicken around the filling, starting from one side to the other. Use toothpicks or kitchen twine to secure the ends, ensuring the filling doesn’t escape during cooking.

Step 4: Cook the Chicken

  1. Sear the Chicken: In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the stuffed chicken breasts and sear for about 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown.

  2. Bake the Chicken: Transfer the seared chicken breasts to an oven-safe baking dish. Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Rest the Chicken: Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for about 5 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, making the chicken juicy and tender.

  2. Garnish and Serve: Before serving, remove the toothpicks or twine. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips for Success

  • Choosing Chicken: Look for chicken breasts that are uniform in size to ensure even cooking. If you prefer, you can also use chicken thighs for a richer flavor.

  • Prep Ahead: This dish can be prepped ahead of time. Stuff the chicken breasts and store them in the fridge until you’re ready to cook. Just add a few extra minutes to the cooking time if they’re cold from the fridge.

  • Customize the Filling: Feel free to get creative with the filling! You can add sun-dried tomatoes, olives, or even artichokes for an extra flavor boost.

Nutritional Information

This Wholesome Keto Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast is not just delicious; it’s also packed with nutrients. Here’s a breakdown of the approximate nutritional values per serving (based on one stuffed chicken breast):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 40g
  • Total Fat: 20g
  • Saturated Fat: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Fiber: 1g
  • Net Carbs: 3g

This recipe is an excellent choice for those on a ketogenic diet, providing high protein and healthy fats while remaining low in carbohydrates.

Conclusion

The Wholesome Keto Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast is a delightful dish that satisfies both the palate and the dietary needs of those following a low-carb lifestyle. With its vibrant flavors and appealing presentation, it’s a perfect meal for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for your family, entertaining guests, or simply treating yourself, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

So, roll up your sleeves and give this recipe a try! Enjoy every bite of this healthy and tasty dish, and relish the knowledge that you’re nourishing your body with wholesome ingredients. Happy cooking!
