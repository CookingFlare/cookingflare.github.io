Flavorful Dairy-Free Teriyaki Tofu Stir-Fry


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy dinner option that is both dairy-free and packed with flavor? Look no further than this amazing recipe for Flavorful Dairy-Free Teriyaki Tofu Stir-Fry! This dish is a perfect combination of tender tofu, crisp vegetables, and a tangy teriyaki sauce that will leave your taste buds begging for more. Plus, it’s super easy to make and can be ready in under 30 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium-sized carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger

Teriyaki Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of water

Instructions:

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the cornstarch and soy sauce. Add the cubed tofu and gently toss until each piece is coated in the mixture. Set aside.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together all the ingredients for the teriyaki sauce until well combined. Set aside.

  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the tofu and cook until golden brown and crispy on all sides. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the sesame oil and sauté the garlic and ginger until fragrant, which should take about 1-2 minutes.

  5. Add the sliced bell peppers, carrot, broccoli, and snap peas to the skillet. Stir-fry the vegetables for about 5 minutes or until they are tender-crisp.

  6. Return the tofu to the skillet and pour in the teriyaki sauce. Stir everything together until the tofu and vegetables are evenly coated in the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.

  7. Remove the skillet from heat and serve the Flavorful Dairy-Free Teriyaki Tofu Stir-Fry over a bed of steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with some sliced green onions or sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor.

Tips:

  • Make sure to press the tofu to remove any excess moisture before cubing it. This will help it achieve a crispy texture when cooked.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir-fry to your liking. You can add or substitute any of your favorite vegetables such as mushrooms, zucchini, or snow peas.
  • If you prefer a spicier stir-fry, add a teaspoon of sriracha or chili flakes to the teriyaki sauce.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in a skillet or microwave before serving.

So there you have it - a delicious and dairy-free teriyaki tofu stir-fry that will satisfy both your taste buds and your desire for a healthy meal. This recipe is not only packed with flavor, but it’s also easy to make and perfect for busy weeknights. Give it a try and let us know what you think! Enjoy!
