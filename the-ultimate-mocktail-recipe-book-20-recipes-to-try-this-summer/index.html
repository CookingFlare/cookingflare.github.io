The Ultimate Mocktail Recipe Book: 20+ Recipes to Try This Summer


Summer is finally here, and what better way to beat the heat than with refreshing, fruity, and bubbly mocktails? Whether you’re a health-conscious individual, a non-drinker, or simply looking for a fun twist on traditional cocktails, mocktails are the perfect solution. In this ultimate mocktail recipe book, we’ve curated 20+ recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds and become your new summer obsession.

Classic Spritzer

  • 2 cups sparkling water
  • 1 cup mixed berries (such as raspberries, blueberries, and blackberries)
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Combine sparkling water, mixed berries, lemon juice, and honey in a large pitcher. Stir well and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

Cucumber Lime Refresher

  • 2 cups sparkling water
  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Combine sparkling water, cucumber slices, lime juice, and honey in a large pitcher. Stir well and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

Virgin Mojito

  • 2 cups sparkling water
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Muddle mint leaves and sugar in a large pitcher. Add lime juice and stir well. Add sparkling water and stir gently. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

Spicy Pineapple Ginger Fizz

  • 2 cups sparkling water
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed ginger juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1-2 dashes cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh pineapple wedges and sprigs of fresh ginger for garnish

Combine pineapple juice, ginger juice, and honey in a large pitcher. Stir well and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. Add cayenne pepper to taste, if desired. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh pineapple wedges and sprigs of fresh ginger.

Virgin Daiquiri

  • 2 cups sparkling water
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup
  • 1/4 cup crushed ice
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Combine lime juice, simple syrup, and crushed ice in a blender. Blend until smooth and pour into glasses. Top with sparkling water and garnish with fresh mint leaves.
