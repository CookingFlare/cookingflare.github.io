The Hanky Panky 4.0: A Modern Twist on a Classic Gin Cocktail


Are you a fan of classic gin cocktails but also love to experiment with new flavors? Then, The Hanky Panky 4.0 is the perfect drink for you! This modern twist on the classic Hanky Panky cocktail takes the original recipe to a whole new level with a tantalizing blend of flavors that will surely impress your guests. Whether you’re hosting a sophisticated soiree or simply relaxing at home, The Hanky Panky 4.0 is a versatile cocktail that suits any occasion.

The History of The Hanky Panky Cocktail

Before we delve into the recipe for The Hanky Panky 4.0, let’s take a quick look at the history of the original Hanky Panky cocktail. This classic drink was created by Ada Coleman, the head bartender at the American Bar in the Savoy Hotel, London, during the early 20th century. The Hanky Panky cocktail quickly gained popularity for its perfect balance of gin, sweet vermouth, and Fernet-Branca, a complex and aromatic Italian amaro.

The Ingredients

To create The Hanky Panky 4.0, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 ½ oz. London dry gin
  • 1 ½ oz. sweet vermouth
  • ¼ oz. Fernet-Branca
  • 1 orange peel for garnish
  • 2-3 dashes of orange bitters
  • Ice cubes
  • Cocktail shaker
  • Mixing glass

The Recipe

  1. Start by filling a mixing glass with ice cubes.

  2. Pour the London dry gin, sweet vermouth, and Fernet-Branca into the mixing glass.

  3. Add 2-3 dashes of orange bitters for an extra layer of flavor.

  4. Stir the ingredients well for about 20-30 seconds to ensure they are properly chilled and blended.

  5. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass.

  6. Express the oils from the orange peel over the drink by holding it over the glass and giving it a gentle twist. This will release the citrusy aroma, adding a delightful sensory element to the cocktail.

  7. Drop the orange peel into the glass as a garnish.

The Modern Twist

Now, here comes the modern twist to elevate this classic cocktail to The Hanky Panky 4.0. In this updated version, we infuse the gin with a hint of lavender to bring a subtle floral note to the drink. To achieve this, simply add a small amount of dried lavender to the gin and let it steep for 24 hours before using it in the cocktail. This infusion process adds a delicate and fragrant dimension to the cocktail, making it a truly unique and sophisticated libation.

Serving Suggestions

The Hanky Panky 4.0 is best served in a chilled coupe glass to enhance its elegant presentation. You can also serve it over a large ice cube in a rocks glass for a contemporary twist. Pair this delightful cocktail with light appetizers such as bruschetta, smoked salmon, or a cheese platter to complement its complex flavors.

In Conclusion

The Hanky Panky 4.0 is a delightful fusion of tradition and innovation, offering a refreshing take on a beloved classic. With its intriguing blend of flavors and aromatic profile, this modern twist on the original Hanky Panky cocktail is sure to captivate the senses and leave a lasting impression on your guests. So, gather your ingredients, mix up a batch of The Hanky Panky 4.0, and raise a toast to the art of mixology! Cheers!
