Citrus Burst: Orange Walnut Salad Recipe


Looking for a refreshing and vibrant salad to brighten up your day? This Citrus Burst: Orange Walnut Salad is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will tantalize your taste buds. Packed with the goodness of juicy oranges, crunchy walnuts, and a zesty dressing, this salad is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a healthy and flavorful meal, this citrus-infused salad is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 4 large navel oranges, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, roughly chopped
  • 6 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Oranges: Start by peeling the oranges and slicing them into rounds or segments. Set aside.

  2. Toast the Walnuts: In a dry skillet over medium heat, toast the walnuts for 2-3 minutes until they become fragrant. Be sure to stir them frequently to prevent burning. Once toasted, remove from heat and let them cool.

  3. Assemble the Salad: In a large bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, sliced red onion, and the prepared oranges. Toss gently to mix the ingredients evenly.

  4. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, fresh orange juice, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  5. Dress the Salad: Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat the ingredients with the flavorful dressing.

  6. Add the Walnuts and Feta: Sprinkle the toasted walnuts and crumbled feta cheese over the dressed salad.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the salad to a serving platter or individual plates and serve immediately. The vibrant colors and fresh flavors of this salad make it a delightful addition to any meal.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 280
  • Total Fat: 20g
  • Saturated Fat: 3g
  • Cholesterol: 5mg
  • Sodium: 150mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Dietary Fiber: 6g
  • Sugars: 18g
  • Protein: 5g

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of freshness, add a handful of pomegranate arils to the salad.
  • Substitute the feta cheese with goat cheese for a tangy twist.
  • To make the salad more filling, add grilled chicken or shrimp on top.

This Citrus Burst: Orange Walnut Salad is a celebration of vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients. The juicy sweetness of the oranges, the earthy crunch of walnuts, and the tangy creaminess of the feta cheese come together to create a symphony of taste in every bite. Whether you’re a fan of citrusy delights or simply looking for a nutritious and delicious salad, this recipe is a must-try. Enjoy the refreshing zing of citrus with every forkful, and let this salad brighten up your day!

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, whip up this delightful salad, and savor the citrus burst with every mouthful. Bon appétit!
