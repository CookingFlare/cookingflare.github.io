Hello Spring: Lemon Blueberry Springtime Bundt Cake


Spring is the perfect time to indulge in the fresh flavors of the season, and what better way to celebrate than with a delightful Lemon Blueberry Bundt Cake? This springtime treat combines the zesty tang of lemon with the sweet burst of blueberries, creating a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds tingling. Whether you’re hosting a spring brunch or simply craving a slice of something special, this Lemon Blueberry Bundt Cake is sure to brighten your day.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cups fresh blueberries, tossed in 1 tablespoon of flour

For the Glaze:

  • 1 1/2 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 3-4 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and generously grease a 10-inch bundt pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the sour cream. Mix until just combined.

  5. Gently fold in the lemon zest, lemon juice, and the flour-coated blueberries. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared bundt pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Tap the pan on the counter to remove any air bubbles.

  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then carefully invert it onto a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. While the cake is cooling, prepare the glaze by whisking together the confectioners’ sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest until smooth.

  10. Once the cake has cooled, drizzle the glaze over the top, allowing it to drip down the sides.

  11. Slice, serve, and enjoy the fresh, zesty flavors of spring!

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra burst of lemon flavor, you can add a few drops of lemon extract to the batter.
  • To prevent the blueberries from sinking to the bottom of the cake, toss them in a tablespoon of flour before folding them into the batter.
  • If you don’t have a bundt pan, you can use a 9x13-inch baking pan and adjust the baking time accordingly.

Embrace Springtime Flavors

This Lemon Blueberry Bundt Cake is a celebration of spring in every bite. The tangy lemon and juicy blueberries come together to create a refreshing and vibrant dessert that’s perfect for any springtime occasion. Whether you’re hosting a garden party, a Mother’s Day brunch, or simply want to treat yourself to something special, this cake is sure to bring a smile to everyone’s face. So, embrace the flavors of spring and indulge in a slice of this delightful Lemon Blueberry Bundt Cake.
