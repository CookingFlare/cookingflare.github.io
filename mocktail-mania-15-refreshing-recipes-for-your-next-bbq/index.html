Mocktail Mania: 15 Refreshing Recipes for Your Next BBQ


When it comes to a summer BBQ, the drinks are almost as important as the food. Gone are the days of sugary soda and bland water. The modern BBQ requires a drink that’s just as refreshing and exciting as the dishes being served. Enter the world of mocktails - a refreshing alternative to traditional cocktails that’s perfect for the whole family.

In this post, we’ll explore 15 of the most refreshing mocktail recipes that are sure to be a hit at your next BBQ. From fruity and bubbly to sweet and tangy, there’s a mocktail on this list for everyone.

1. Cucumber Lime Refresher

  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Combine water, lime juice, and simple syrup in a large pitcher. Add sliced cucumber and stir to combine. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving. Serve over ice with a sprig of fresh mint.

2. Virgin Mojito

  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup
  • 1/4 cup crushed ice
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • Soda water

Muddle fresh mint leaves and crushed ice in a large pitcher. Add water, lime juice, and simple syrup. Stir to combine. Top with soda water and serve over ice with additional fresh mint leaves.

3. Cranberry Spritzer

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cranberry juice
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup
  • 1/4 cup sparkling water
  • Fresh cranberries for garnish

Combine water, cranberry juice, and simple syrup in a large pitcher. Stir to combine. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving. Serve over ice with a splash of sparkling water and garnish with fresh cranberries.
