Savory and Nutty Farro Pilaf with Roasted Vegetables


Are you looking for a wholesome and flavorful dish that celebrates the goodness of whole grains and roasted vegetables? Look no further than this delectable Savory and Nutty Farro Pilaf with Roasted Vegetables recipe. Packed with earthy flavors, hearty textures, and a burst of colorful veggies, this dish is a celebration of wholesome, nutritious ingredients that come together to create a truly satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup farro
  • 2 1/2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Farro: In a medium saucepan, combine the farro and vegetable or chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the farro is tender and the liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and set aside.

  2. Roast the Vegetables: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large bowl, toss the chopped red onion, garlic, zucchini, bell peppers, and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread the vegetables in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until they are tender and lightly caramelized.

  3. Combine and Serve: In a large serving bowl, combine the cooked farro, roasted vegetables, chopped parsley, and basil. Toss gently to mix everything together. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. Serve the farro pilaf warm, garnished with an extra sprinkle of fresh herbs if desired.

Why Farro Pilaf?

Farro, an ancient grain with a nutty flavor and satisfying chewy texture, serves as the perfect base for this pilaf. Not only does it provide a hearty and nutritious foundation for the dish, but it also offers a host of health benefits. Farro is an excellent source of fiber, protein, and nutrients like magnesium and iron, making it a wholesome choice for those seeking a balanced and nourishing meal.

The Magic of Roasted Vegetables

The roasted vegetables in this recipe add a delightful depth of flavor and a burst of vibrant colors to the dish. The caramelization that occurs during roasting brings out the natural sweetness of the vegetables, while also imparting a slightly smoky and savory essence. Not to mention, roasted vegetables are rich in essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making them a valuable addition to any meal.

Versatility and Adaptability

One of the beauties of this farro pilaf recipe is its versatility. Feel free to customize the vegetable selection based on what’s in season or what you have on hand. For a touch of indulgence, consider adding roasted butternut squash, sweet potatoes, or even a handful of toasted nuts for an extra crunch. The possibilities are endless, allowing you to tailor this dish to your personal preferences and the availability of ingredients.

A Complete Meal

Whether you serve it as a standalone vegetarian entrée or as a hearty side dish alongside grilled chicken or fish, this Savory and Nutty Farro Pilaf with Roasted Vegetables is a complete meal that satisfies both the palate and the body. With its robust flavors, nutrient-packed ingredients, and simple yet elegant preparation, this dish is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire.

So, the next time you’re craving a wholesome and satisfying meal, consider whipping up this delightful farro pilaf. It’s a celebration of the natural goodness of whole grains and fresh vegetables, brought together in a symphony of flavors that will delight your senses and nourish your body. Enjoy!
