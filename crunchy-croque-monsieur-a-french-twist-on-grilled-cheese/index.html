Crunchy Croque Monsieur: A French Twist on Grilled Cheese


If you’re a fan of grilled cheese, then get ready to take your love for this classic comfort food to the next level with the French twist of Crunchy Croque Monsieur. This indulgent, crispy, and oh-so-satisfying sandwich is a delightful marriage of ham, cheese, and creamy béchamel sauce, all nestled between slices of crusty bread and grilled to golden perfection.

The Secret to a Perfect Crunchy Croque Monsieur

The secret to achieving the perfect Crunchy Croque Monsieur lies in the layers of flavor and texture. The combination of smoky ham, rich Gruyère cheese, and velvety béchamel sauce provides a symphony of flavors, while the crunch of the grilled bread adds a delightful contrast.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Here’s what you’ll need to create this mouthwatering Crunchy Croque Monsieur:

  • 8 slices of crusty bread (sourdough or country bread work well)
  • 8 slices of smoked ham
  • 2 cups of grated Gruyère cheese
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon of freshly grated nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Béchamel Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the flour, and whisk constantly until the mixture turns a light golden color.
  3. Gradually add the milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
  4. Cook the sauce for a few minutes until it thickens, then add the nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Set the sauce aside.

Step 2: Assemble the Sandwiches

  1. Place 4 slices of bread on a work surface and divide the grated Gruyère cheese equally among them.
  2. Top the cheese with the smoked ham, then spoon the béchamel sauce over the ham.
  3. Place the remaining 4 slices of bread on top to form sandwiches.

Step 3: Grill the Sandwiches

  1. In a large skillet, melt a tablespoon of butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the sandwiches to the skillet and cook until golden and crispy on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Once the sandwiches are golden and crunchy, remove them from the skillet and let them cool for a minute before slicing them in half. The gooey cheese, savory ham, and creamy béchamel sauce will create an irresistible aroma that will have your taste buds tingling with excitement.

The Perfect Pairing

Pair your Crunchy Croque Monsieur with a simple mixed green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette for a well-rounded and satisfying meal. The freshness of the salad provides a delightful contrast to the richness of the sandwich, making it a perfect combination.

Final Thoughts

The Crunchy Croque Monsieur is a delightful departure from the traditional grilled cheese, offering a symphony of flavors and textures that will transport you to the charming cafes of Paris with every bite. Whether you’re looking to elevate your brunch game or simply indulge in a comforting meal, this French twist on a classic is sure to impress. So, don your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible crunch of this delectable sandwich.

Bon appétit!
