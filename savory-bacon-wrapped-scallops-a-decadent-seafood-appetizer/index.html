Savory Bacon-Wrapped Scallops: A Decadent Seafood Appetizer


If you’re a seafood lover looking for a delicious and impressive appetizer to serve at your next dinner party, look no further than savory bacon-wrapped scallops. This mouthwatering dish combines the delicate sweetness of succulent scallops with the smoky, salty goodness of crispy bacon. It’s a match made in culinary heaven!

Ingredients

To make this indulgent seafood appetizer, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • Fresh scallops (about 1 pound)
  • Thinly sliced bacon (about 12 slices)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Start by preparing your scallops. If they are still attached to their shells, gently remove them and discard the shells. Rinse the scallops under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel. This step ensures that your scallops are clean and ready to be cooked.

  3. Season the scallops with a pinch of salt and pepper. Be sure to evenly coat each scallop to enhance their natural flavors.

  4. Take a slice of bacon and wrap it around one scallop, securing it in place with a toothpick. Repeat this process for the remaining scallops, ensuring that each one is fully wrapped in bacon. Bacon not only adds a rich and savory taste but also helps to keep the scallops moist during the cooking process.

  5. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Place the bacon-wrapped scallops in the skillet, making sure they are not too crowded. Cook them for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bacon is crispy and golden brown.

  6. Transfer the cooked scallops to the prepared baking sheet and place them in the preheated oven. Let them bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the scallops are cooked through and tender.

  7. Once cooked, remove the toothpicks from the scallops and transfer them to a serving platter. You can garnish them with fresh herbs like parsley or chives for an added touch of freshness.

Serving Suggestions

Savory bacon-wrapped scallops are best served hot and fresh out of the oven. They make a delightful appetizer for any occasion, from casual gatherings to formal dinner parties. You can serve them as they are or pair them with a tangy dipping sauce like a lemon aioli or a spicy chipotle mayo.

For a complete meal, consider serving these delectable treats alongside a refreshing salad or some roasted vegetables. The combination of flavors and textures will surely impress your guests and leave them craving for more.

Conclusion

Savory bacon-wrapped scallops are a true delight for seafood enthusiasts. With their irresistible combination of tender scallops and crispy bacon, they are sure to be a hit at any gathering. This indulgent appetizer is not only easy to make but also delivers on flavor and presentation.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a decadent seafood treat, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and your guests will be left wanting more of these delightful bacon-wrapped scallops. Enjoy!
