BBQ Beef Brisket: Slow-Cooked and Flavorful Recipes


Are you craving a mouthwatering BBQ beef brisket that’s packed with flavor and falls apart with every bite? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore some incredible slow-cooked recipes that will make your taste buds dance. From traditional Texas-style to tangy and sweet variations, these recipes are sure to impress your family and friends at your next barbecue.

Classic Texas-Style BBQ Beef Brisket

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 12-14 hours Total Time: 12-14 hours and 10 minutes Servings: 8-10

Ingredients

  • 1 whole beef brisket (approximately 10 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Wood chips for smoking (hickory or oak)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your smoker to 225°F (107°C) and add wood chips for smoking.
  2. In a small bowl, combine kosher salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, cumin, and brown sugar. Mix well to create a dry rub.
  3. Trim the excess fat from the brisket, leaving a thin layer for added flavor and moisture.
  4. Rub the dry rub generously all over the brisket, making sure to cover every inch.
  5. Place the brisket on the smoker, fat side up, and close the lid. Let it cook for 12-14 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 195°F (90°C).
  6. Once the brisket is cooked, remove it from the smoker and let it rest for 30 minutes before slicing.
  7. Slice the brisket against the grain and serve with your favorite BBQ sauce.

Tangy and Sweet BBQ Beef Brisket

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 6-8 hours Total Time: 6-8 hours and 15 minutes Servings: 6-8

Ingredients

  • 1 whole beef brisket (approximately 6 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup apple juice
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon liquid smoke

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 275°F (135°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine kosher salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, and brown sugar. Mix well to create a dry rub.
  3. Trim the excess fat from the brisket, leaving a thin layer for added flavor and moisture.
  4. Rub the dry rub generously all over the brisket, making sure to cover every inch.
  5. Place the brisket in a roasting pan and pour apple juice over it.
  6. Cover the pan tightly with aluminum foil and place it in the preheated oven. Let it cook for 6-8 hours, or until the meat is tender and easily pulls apart.
  7. While the brisket is cooking, prepare the BBQ sauce. In a saucepan, combine ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, and liquid smoke. Simmer over low heat for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Once the brisket is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes before slicing.
  9. Slice the brisket against the grain and serve with the tangy and sweet BBQ sauce.

Conclusion

BBQ beef brisket is a true delicacy that requires time, patience, and the right combination of flavors. Whether you prefer the classic Texas-style or a tangy and sweet variation, these slow-cooked recipes are guaranteed to satisfy your BBQ cravings. So fire up your smoker or preheat your oven, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in tender, flavorful, and unforgettable BBQ beef brisket. Your taste buds will thank you!

Enjoy and happy cooking!
