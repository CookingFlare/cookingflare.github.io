Vegan Chocolate Dipped Strawberry Recipe with Fresh Fruit and a Coconut Cream


If you’re on the hunt for a delightful vegan dessert that satisfies your sweet tooth while still feeling indulgent, look no further than this Vegan Chocolate Dipped Strawberry recipe. It combines the natural sweetness of fresh strawberries with rich, creamy chocolate and a luscious coconut cream that will leave your taste buds dancing in delight. This simple yet elegant treat is perfect for special occasions, romantic evenings, or just a sweet snack to brighten your day.

Why Choose Vegan Chocolate Dipped Strawberries?

Vegan desserts often get a bad rap for being bland or lacking in richness. However, this recipe proves that you can enjoy a decadent dessert without any animal products. Strawberries are naturally sweet, low in calories, and packed with nutrients, making them a fantastic base for any dessert. When dipped in a rich vegan chocolate coating and topped with creamy coconut, they transform into a luxurious treat that everyone will love.

Ingredients You’ll Need

For the Chocolate Dipped Strawberries:

  • Fresh Strawberries: 1 pound (450g), preferably organic
  • Vegan Dark Chocolate: 8 ounces (226g), chopped (choose a high-quality brand for the best flavor)
  • Coconut Oil: 1 tablespoon (optional, for a smoother chocolate consistency)

For the Coconut Cream:

  • Coconut Cream: 1 can (13.5 ounces or 400ml), refrigerated overnight
  • Maple Syrup: 2 tablespoons, for sweetness
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon, for flavor
  • Pinch of Sea Salt: enhances the overall flavor

Equipment Needed

  • Baking sheet
  • Parchment paper
  • Heatproof bowl
  • Saucepan (if using the double boiler method)
  • Whisk or electric mixer (for whipping coconut cream)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Strawberries

Start by washing your fresh strawberries gently under cold water. Pat them dry with a clean towel, ensuring they are completely dry before dipping. This is important because excess moisture can prevent the chocolate from adhering properly to the fruit. Leave the green stems on for a beautiful presentation and easier handling.

Step 2: Melt the Vegan Dark Chocolate

There are two methods to melt the chocolate: using a microwave or a double boiler.

Microwave Method:

  1. Place the chopped vegan dark chocolate and coconut oil (if using) in a microwave-safe bowl.
  2. Heat in 30-second intervals, stirring in between until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.

Double Boiler Method:

  1. Fill a saucepan with a couple of inches of water and bring it to a gentle simmer.
  2. Place a heatproof bowl over the saucepan, ensuring it doesn’t touch the water.
  3. Add the chopped chocolate and coconut oil to the bowl, stirring until melted and smooth.

Step 3: Dip the Strawberries

Once the chocolate is melted, it’s time to dip the strawberries. Hold each strawberry by the green stem and dip it into the melted chocolate, ensuring it’s fully coated. Allow any excess chocolate to drip off before placing the dipped strawberry on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Repeat this process for all the strawberries.

Step 4: Allow to Set

Once all strawberries are dipped, place the baking sheet in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes or until the chocolate has fully set. This step is crucial as it gives the chocolate a nice firm texture.

Step 5: Prepare the Coconut Cream

While the chocolate is setting, prepare the coconut cream. Remove the can of coconut cream from the refrigerator and carefully scoop out the solidified cream into a mixing bowl, avoiding the liquid at the bottom. This liquid can be saved for smoothies or other recipes.

  1. Use a whisk or an electric mixer to whip the coconut cream until it’s light and fluffy.
  2. Add the maple syrup, vanilla extract, and a pinch of sea salt. Continue to whip until well combined and smooth. Taste and adjust sweetness if desired.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the chocolate has set on the strawberries, it’s time to serve! Arrange the chocolate-dipped strawberries on a serving platter, and serve with a bowl of the whipped coconut cream for dipping. This presentation not only looks stunning but also adds an interactive element to your dessert experience.

Tips for the Perfect Chocolate Dipped Strawberries

  • Choose Quality Ingredients: The flavor of your chocolate and strawberries will shine through, so opt for high-quality ingredients.
  • Don’t Rush the Setting: Make sure to allow the chocolate to set completely; this will enhance the eating experience.
  • Experiment with Toppings: Get creative! You can sprinkle crushed nuts, shredded coconut, or even a drizzle of white vegan chocolate over the dipped strawberries before the chocolate sets.

Storage Instructions

If you have any leftovers (which is rare, but can happen!), store the chocolate-dipped strawberries in an airtight container in the refrigerator. They are best enjoyed within 24 hours, as the strawberries may start to release moisture over time.

Conclusion

Vegan chocolate-dipped strawberries with coconut cream are a delicious, guilt-free dessert that’s easy to make and sure to impress. Whether you’re treating yourself or sharing with loved ones, this recipe is a fantastic choice for any occasion. Enjoy the burst of fresh fruit paired with rich chocolate and creamy coconut that creates a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. Indulge in this decadent delight today, and make every moment a little sweeter!
