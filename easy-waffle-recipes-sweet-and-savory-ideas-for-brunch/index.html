Easy Waffle Recipes: Sweet and Savory Ideas for Brunch


Variety is the spice of life, and when it comes to waffles, the possibilities are endless. If you’re looking to elevate your brunch game, waffles provide the ultimate blank canvas for culinary creativity. Their golden, crispy exterior and soft, fluffy interior can adapt to both sweet and savory flavors, making them a versatile option for breakfast, lunch, or brunch spreads. Whether you’re planning a family gathering or just spoiling yourself with a special weekend treat, these easy waffle recipes will tempt your taste buds and impress your guests.

Let’s dive into some inspired waffle ideas—both sweet and savory—that are delicious, easy to prepare, and perfect for any brunch menu.

Sweet Waffle Recipes

1. Classic Buttermilk Waffles

The quintessential waffle recipe never goes out of style. These buttermilk waffles have a light, tangy flavor that pairs beautifully with sweet toppings. Perfect for purists, they serve as a great starting point for experimenting with other flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and combine them with the buttermilk and melted butter.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring just until combined. Avoid overmixing.
  5. Pour an appropriate amount of batter onto the waffle iron and cook until the waffles are golden and crisp.
  6. Serve with maple syrup, whipped cream, and fresh berries for the ultimate sweet experience.

2. Cinnamon Roll Waffles

Transform your brunch spread with these decadent cinnamon roll-inspired waffles. They’re a perfect crossover of two breakfast classics, delivering gooey cinnamon sweetness in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups waffle batter (use the buttermilk waffle recipe as your base)
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and melted butter to create the cinnamon filling.
  3. Pour a small amount of waffle batter onto the waffle iron, then swirl in the cinnamon filling before adding more batter to cover.
  4. Cook until golden brown.
  5. Drizzle with cream cheese glaze or powdered sugar icing for an indulgent finish.

3. Banana Nut Waffles

For a naturally sweet and hearty option, banana nut waffles are unbeatable. The bananas add a tender texture, while chopped nuts bring in crunch and a hint of nutty richness.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups waffle batter (use any basic waffle recipe)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine mashed bananas, eggs, waffle batter, and vanilla extract.
  2. Gently fold in the chopped nuts.
  3. Ladle the batter onto a preheated waffle iron and cook until golden.
  4. Top with sliced bananas, more nuts, and a drizzle of honey or maple syrup.

Savory Waffle Recipes

1. Cheddar and Chive Waffles

If you’re a fan of cheesy, savory flavors, these cheddar and chive waffles are a must-try. They pair beautifully with fried eggs, crispy bacon, or even a dollop of sour cream.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and pepper.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, and melted butter, then combine with the dry ingredients.
  4. Fold in the shredded cheddar cheese and chopped chives.
  5. Cook the batter in your waffle iron until golden and crisp.
  6. Serve with savory toppings of your choice, such as sautéed mushrooms or avocado slices.

2. Bacon and Cornmeal Waffles

With a crispy cornmeal texture and smoky bits of bacon mixed in, these waffles bring robust flavors to the table. They’re perfect for topping with fried chicken or a sunny-side-up egg.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ¾ cups buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ½ cup cooked and crumbled bacon

Instructions:

  1. Mix together all-purpose flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs, buttermilk, and melted butter together in another bowl and pour into the dry ingredients, stirring until combined.
  3. Fold in the crumbled bacon.
  4. Cook on a preheated waffle iron until crisp and golden.
  5. Serve with a drizzle of hot sauce or maple syrup for a sweet-salty contrast.

3. Herbed Potato Waffles

Potato waffles are the savory vehicle you never knew you needed. Packed with fresh herbs, these waffles are a great substitute for toast or hashbrowns in a brunch setting.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes (prepared ahead or use leftover mashed potatoes)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill

Instructions:

  1. Combine mashed potatoes, flour, baking powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, egg, and milk in a large bowl.
  2. Stir in chopped parsley and dill until evenly distributed.
  3. Add the mixture to your waffle iron and cook until edges are crisp and golden brown.
  4. Serve with sour cream, smoked salmon, or even a poached egg on top.

Tips for Perfect Waffles Every Time

  1. Preheat Your Waffle Iron: Always make sure it’s preheated before adding batter to ensure even cooking.
  2. Don’t Overmix the Batter: A lumpy batter is better than overworked batter, which can result in dense waffles.
  3. Use Nonstick Spray or Butter: To prevent sticking, lightly grease your waffle iron before each batch.
  4. Let Them Cool on a Rack: For ultimate crispiness, place cooked waffles on a wire rack instead of piling them on a plate. This prevents sogginess.

With these sweet and savory waffle recipes in your repertoire, your brunches are sure to become legendary. Whether you’re topping your waffles with syrup and fruit or layering them with cheese and herbs, they’re guaranteed to be a hit. Brunch doesn’t have to be complicated—just grab your waffle iron, experiment with different ingredients, and enjoy the delightful mix of textures and flavors waffles have to offer!
