One-Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Pasta: A Quick and Easy Dinner


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner recipe that will satisfy your cravings without spending hours in the kitchen? Well, look no further! This one-pot creamy tomato basil chicken pasta is the perfect solution. With its rich flavors and creamy texture, it’s bound to become a favorite in your household. And the best part? It’s quick and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 12 ounces penne pasta
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the minced garlic and chopped onion. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and chicken broth. Stir well to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  4. Add the dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Return the chicken to the pot and add the penne pasta. Stir everything together until the pasta is fully coated in the sauce.

  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked al dente and the chicken is tender.

  7. Pour in the heavy cream and stir until the sauce becomes creamy and well-combined.

  8. Remove from heat and let the pasta rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the sauce to thicken slightly.

  9. Serve the one-pot creamy tomato basil chicken pasta hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. You can add some vegetables like spinach or bell peppers for an added nutritional boost.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes or chili powder to the sauce.
  • Don’t limit yourself to just chicken! This recipe works well with other proteins like shrimp or even sausage.
  • If you’re not a fan of penne pasta, you can use any other type of pasta you have on hand. Fusilli, rigatoni, or even spaghetti will work just as well.

In Conclusion

This one-pot creamy tomato basil chicken pasta is the perfect dish for those busy weeknights when you want something comforting and delicious without spending hours in the kitchen. With its simple ingredients and easy preparation, it’s a recipe that will quickly become a go-to in your meal rotation. So, gather your ingredients, grab a pot, and get ready to enjoy a quick and satisfying dinner that will leave everyone asking for seconds!
