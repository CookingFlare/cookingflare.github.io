Savory Summer Picnic Quiche Recipes for Brunch Outdoors


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy a leisurely brunch outdoors. And what better way to indulge in the fresh flavors of the season than with a delicious savory quiche? Whether you’re planning a picnic in the park or a lazy brunch in your backyard, these savory summer picnic quiche recipes are sure to impress your friends and family.

1. Spinach and Feta Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out the pie crust and press it into a greased 9-inch pie dish.
  3. In a skillet, sauté the spinach, red bell pepper, and red onion until the spinach wilts.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread the sautéed vegetables evenly over the pie crust, then sprinkle the feta cheese on top.
  6. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables and cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes or until the quiche is set and golden brown on top.
  8. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

2. Tomato and Basil Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Press the pie crust into a greased 9-inch pie dish.
  3. Arrange the halved cherry tomatoes evenly over the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese and chopped basil on top of the tomatoes.
  5. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour the egg mixture over the tomatoes, cheese, and basil.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until the quiche is set and lightly browned.
  8. Let the quiche cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

3. Bacon and Cheddar Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup half-and-half
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Press the pie crust into a greased 9-inch pie dish.
  3. Sprinkle the crumbled bacon, cheddar cheese, and chopped green onions evenly over the crust.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, half-and-half, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the bacon, cheese, and green onions.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes or until the quiche is set and the top is golden brown.
  7. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

These savory summer picnic quiche recipes are not only delicious but also incredibly versatile. Feel free to experiment with different combinations of vegetables, cheeses, and herbs to suit your taste. Serve them alongside a fresh salad or some crusty bread for a complete and satisfying brunch outdoors. So grab a blanket, gather your loved ones, and enjoy the flavors of summer with these delightful quiches. Bon appétit!
