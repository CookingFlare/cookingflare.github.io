Satisfying Low Carb Chicken Enchilada Stuffed Peppers


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb meal, then you’re in for a treat with these Chicken Enchilada Stuffed Peppers. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this recipe is a perfect way to enjoy the bold flavors of enchiladas without the guilt of high carbs. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a tasty and healthy meal, these stuffed peppers are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Prepare the Peppers: Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the peppers in a baking dish, cut side up.

  3. Cook the Chicken: In a large skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and sauté until softened. Add the shredded chicken, enchilada sauce, black beans, corn, cumin, and chili powder. Stir well to combine and let the mixture simmer for 5-7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Stuff the Peppers: Spoon the chicken and vegetable mixture into the prepared bell peppers, pressing down gently to fill them completely.

  5. Bake: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.

  6. Add Cheese: Remove the foil and sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the stuffed peppers. Return to the oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  7. Serve: Once the peppers are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

These Chicken Enchilada Stuffed Peppers are a fantastic way to enjoy the flavors of traditional enchiladas while keeping your carb intake in check. The combination of tender chicken, savory enchilada sauce, and melted cheese nestled inside sweet bell peppers creates a mouthwatering dish that’s as visually appealing as it is delicious.

This recipe is also incredibly versatile. You can customize the filling to suit your taste by adding ingredients such as diced tomatoes, jalapeños, or even cauliflower rice for an extra boost of veggies. Additionally, by using bell peppers as the base instead of tortillas, you’re adding a healthy dose of vitamins and antioxidants to your meal.

Not only is this dish perfect for a cozy family dinner, but it also makes for a great meal prep option. You can easily double the recipe and store the leftovers for a quick and satisfying lunch throughout the week.

In Conclusion

These Satisfying Low Carb Chicken Enchilada Stuffed Peppers are a must-try for anyone looking to enjoy the robust flavors of enchiladas in a healthier, low-carb package. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this recipe is sure to impress with its combination of vibrant colors, bold flavors, and nutritious ingredients. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to savor every bite of these delightful stuffed peppers!

Remember, cooking is all about enjoying the process and sharing the love through delicious meals. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the aromas of this delightful dish fill your kitchen with warmth and satisfaction. Cheers to good food and good company!
