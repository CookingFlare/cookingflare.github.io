Homemade Garlic Knots with Parmesan: A Flavorful Appetizer


When it comes to appetizers, we often opt for something light and easy to munch on. But, why settle for something bland when you can create a dish that’s both flavorful and visually appealing? Today, we’re going to share a recipe for homemade garlic knots with Parmesan that’s sure to impress your guests. These knots are soft, buttery, and packed with a punch of garlic and Parmesan cheese. They’re the perfect accompaniment to your favorite soups, salads, or main courses.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

To start, combine the warm water and yeast in a large mixing bowl. Let it sit for 5-7 minutes until the yeast is activated and frothy. Add the olive oil, salt, sugar, and 2 cups of flour to the bowl. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.

Gradually add the remaining 2 cups of flour, one cup at a time, until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. You can use a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment or knead the dough by hand.

Once the dough is ready, transfer it to a lightly floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Divide the dough into 8-10 equal pieces and roll each piece into a long rope. Tie each rope into a knot and place them on the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 1 inch of space between each knot.

In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic and grated Parmesan cheese. Brush the tops of the knots with the garlic-Parmesan mixture and then brush with the beaten egg. Sprinkle with chopped parsley.

Bake the knots for 15-20 minutes, or until they’re golden brown. Remove them from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the garlic knots more crispy, brush them with a little bit of water and then sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese before baking.
  • You can also add some chopped fresh herbs like rosemary or thyme to the garlic-Parmesan mixture for added flavor.
  • If you want to make the garlic knots ahead of time, you can prepare the dough and let it rise overnight in the refrigerator. Simply shape the dough into knots and bake them in the morning.

Conclusion

Homemade garlic knots with Parmesan are a flavorful and impressive appetizer that’s sure to please any crowd. With their soft, buttery texture and savory garlic and Parmesan flavors, they’re the perfect addition to any meal. So, next time you’re looking for a delicious and easy-to-make appetizer, give this recipe a try. Your guests will thank you!
