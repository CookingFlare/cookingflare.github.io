Succulent Garlic Butter Baked Scallops: A Seafood Delicacy


If you’re a seafood lover, you’re in for a treat with this succulent and flavorful recipe for Garlic Butter Baked Scallops. Scallops, known for their delicate taste and tender texture, are elevated to a whole new level with the addition of garlic-infused butter and a hint of lemon. This dish is perfect for a special occasion or a luxurious weeknight meal that will impress your family and friends. Without further ado, let’s dive into the details of creating this delectable seafood delicacy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh sea scallops
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that the scallops bake evenly and develop a beautiful golden crust.

  2. Prepare the Scallops: Rinse the sea scallops under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels. It’s important to remove any excess moisture to allow the scallops to sear and bake properly.

  3. Create the Garlic Butter Mixture: In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a fragrant and flavorful garlic butter mixture.

  4. Coat the Scallops: Dip each scallop into the garlic butter mixture, ensuring that they are evenly coated. Then, roll the scallops in a mixture of breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese, pressing gently to adhere the coating to the scallops.

  5. Arrange the Scallops: Place the coated scallops in a single layer in a baking dish or a cast-iron skillet. Ensure that there is some space between each scallop to allow for even cooking.

  6. Bake to Perfection: Place the baking dish or skillet in the preheated oven and bake the scallops for 12-15 minutes, or until they are opaque and the topping is golden brown. Be careful not to overcook the scallops, as they can become tough and rubbery.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the scallops are baked to perfection, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with a sprinkle of freshly chopped parsley and a drizzle of fresh lemon juice. Serve the succulent garlic butter baked scallops hot, and savor the irresistible blend of flavors with each bite.

Tips for Success:

  • Selecting Fresh Scallops: When purchasing scallops, look for fresh, dry-packed scallops rather than wet-packed ones, as they tend to have a better flavor and texture.

  • Avoid Overcrowding: Make sure not to overcrowd the baking dish or skillet when arranging the scallops, as this can prevent them from developing a golden crust.

  • Adjusting Seasonings: Feel free to adjust the amount of garlic, butter, or lemon juice according to your personal taste preferences.

  • Pairing Suggestions: These succulent garlic butter baked scallops pair wonderfully with a side of wild rice, steamed vegetables, or a crisp green salad for a well-rounded meal.

In Conclusion:

Succulent Garlic Butter Baked Scallops are a true celebration of the sea, bringing together the natural sweetness of scallops with the richness of garlic-infused butter. This recipe is a testament to the fact that sometimes, the most delicious dishes are also the simplest. Whether you’re a seasoned seafood enthusiast or a newcomer to the world of scallops, this recipe is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you craving more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey that will undoubtedly earn you rave reviews. Cheers to indulging in the luxurious flavors of succulent garlic butter baked scallops!
