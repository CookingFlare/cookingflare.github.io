A Simple yet Delicious Italian-Style Roasted Vegetable Soup


When it comes to comfort food, few things can rival the warmth and satisfaction of a hearty bowl of soup. And if you’re looking for a flavorful and healthy option, Italian-style roasted vegetable soup is the perfect choice. This simple yet delicious soup is packed with the goodness of roasted vegetables, fragrant herbs, and rich tomato broth, making it a comforting and nourishing meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes, halved
  • 2 red bell peppers, seeded and halved
  • 2 zucchinis, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Roast the Vegetables: Place the halved tomatoes, red bell peppers, zucchinis, and chopped onions on the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized.

  3. Prepare the Soup Base: In a large pot, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the vegetable broth and canned diced tomatoes. Stir in the dried oregano and basil. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  4. Blend the Soup: Once the roasted vegetables are ready, carefully transfer them to the pot with the simmering broth. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. If you don’t have an immersion blender, you can use a regular blender, working in batches if necessary.

  5. Simmer and Season: Allow the soup to simmer for 10-15 minutes to let the flavors meld together. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the hot soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves. You can also drizzle a little extra virgin olive oil on top for an added touch of richness. This soup pairs beautifully with crusty bread or a simple green salad.

The Beauty of Roasted Vegetables

The secret to the depth of flavor in this Italian-style roasted vegetable soup lies in the process of roasting the vegetables. Roasting not only caramelizes the natural sugars in the vegetables, bringing out their inherent sweetness, but it also adds a rich, smoky complexity to the soup. The combination of tomatoes, bell peppers, zucchinis, and onions creates a symphony of flavors that is both comforting and vibrant.

A Versatile and Nutritious Dish

What’s wonderful about this soup is its versatility. You can easily customize it by adding other vegetables such as carrots, celery, or even butternut squash to the roasting mix. Additionally, the soup is naturally vegan and gluten-free, making it a fantastic option for those with dietary restrictions or preferences.

Final Thoughts

Italian-style roasted vegetable soup is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients and the art of simple, yet delicious cooking. Whether you’re looking for a satisfying weeknight dinner or a cozy meal to share with loved ones, this soup is sure to delight your taste buds and warm your soul. So, gather your favorite vegetables, embrace the magic of roasting, and savor the comforting flavors of this Italian-inspired masterpiece. Buon appetito!
