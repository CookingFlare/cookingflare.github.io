Leftover Roasted Vegetables Turned into a Hearty Vegetable Stew


We’ve all been there – a fridge full of leftover roasted vegetables from last night’s dinner, and you’re wondering what to do with them. Well, fear not, because I’ve got the perfect solution for you. Turn those leftover roasted vegetables into a hearty and delicious vegetable stew that will warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • Leftover roasted vegetables (any combination you have on hand – think potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, zucchini, and onions)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Base: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  2. Add Roasted Vegetables: Chop the leftover roasted vegetables into bite-sized pieces if they’re not already. Add them to the pot and stir to combine with the onions and garlic.

  3. Enhance the Flavor: Pour in the can of diced tomatoes and vegetable broth. Stir in the dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Bring the stew to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Adjust Seasoning: Taste the stew and adjust the seasoning if necessary. If you prefer a thicker stew, you can mash some of the vegetables with a fork or use an immersion blender to blend a portion of the stew.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the vegetable stew into bowls, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve with crusty bread or a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.

Why This Recipe Works

This vegetable stew is a fantastic way to breathe new life into leftover roasted vegetables. The roasting process already intensifies the flavors of the vegetables, and when they’re simmered in a rich tomato-based broth with aromatic herbs, the result is a comforting and hearty stew that’s perfect for a cozy dinner.

Not only is this recipe a great way to reduce food waste, but it’s also incredibly versatile. You can use any combination of roasted vegetables, so feel free to get creative and use whatever you have on hand. The addition of the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth creates a robust base for the stew, while the herbs add depth and complexity to the flavor profile.

This vegetable stew is not only a delicious and satisfying meal, but it’s also a nutritious one. Packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber from the assortment of vegetables, it’s a wholesome option for anyone looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet.

So, the next time you find yourself staring at a container of leftover roasted vegetables, remember that you can easily transform them into a delightful vegetable stew that’s both comforting and nourishing.

Give this recipe a try, and let those leftover roasted vegetables shine in a whole new light!
