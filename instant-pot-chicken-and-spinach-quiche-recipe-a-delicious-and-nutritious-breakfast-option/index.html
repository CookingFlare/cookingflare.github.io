Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Quiche Recipe: A Delicious and Nutritious Breakfast Option


Quiche is a classic breakfast dish that combines a flaky crust with a savory filling. It’s a versatile recipe that can be customized with various ingredients to suit your taste. Today, we’re going to share a delicious and nutritious variation of quiche made with chicken and spinach, cooked in an Instant Pot. This Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Quiche recipe is not only easy to make but also packed with flavor and nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper until well combined. Set aside.

  2. Grease a round baking dish that fits inside your Instant Pot. You can use a 7-inch round dish for this recipe.

  3. Layer the cooked chicken, spinach, and shredded cheddar cheese in the greased baking dish.

  4. Pour the egg mixture over the chicken, spinach, and cheese, ensuring that all the ingredients are evenly covered.

  5. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, creating a loose tent to allow for expansion during cooking.

  6. Pour 1 cup of water into the Instant Pot insert and place the trivet inside.

  7. Carefully lower the baking dish onto the trivet inside the Instant Pot.

  8. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position.

  9. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the cooking time to 25 minutes on high pressure.

  10. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then carefully switch the valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  11. Remove the baking dish from the Instant Pot using oven mitts or tongs, as it will be hot.

  12. Remove the foil and let the quiche cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Quiche is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or even as a light lunch or dinner. Pair it with a fresh green salad or some roasted potatoes for a complete meal. You can also serve it with a dollop of sour cream or a side of salsa for added flavor.

Nutritional Information

This Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Quiche is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Here’s a breakdown of the nutritional information per serving:

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 18g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fiber: 2g

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Quiche is a simple yet satisfying breakfast option that combines the goodness of protein-rich chicken, nutrient-dense spinach, and creamy cheddar cheese. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, this recipe can be prepared in no time, making it an ideal choice for busy mornings or lazy weekend brunches. Give it a try and enjoy a delicious and nutritious start to your day!
