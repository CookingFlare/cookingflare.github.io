Cherry Gazette: 7 Cherry-licious Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Summer has arrived, and the cherry season is in full swing. Cherries are not only delightfully sweet and bursting with flavor, but they also offer a tasty source of nutrition like vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants. In this edition of the Cherry Gazette, I’ll share 7 charming cherry juice and smoothie recipes to help you satisfy your sweet cravings and indulge in this stunning fruit. Read on for these refreshing, quick, and easy-to-make treats that will leave you wanting more!

#1 Cherry Vanilla Sunrise Smoothie

Bring the sunshine indoors with this vibrant and refreshing Cherry Vanilla Sunrise Smoothie. The natural vanilla flavor combined with tart cherries creates the perfect symphony of tastes sure to bring a smile to your face.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup low-fat milk
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen whole tart cherries, pitted
  • 1/2 cup vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the drink into a tall glass, garnish with a cherry on a toothpick or skewer, and enjoy!

#2 Cherry Almond Green Smoothie

Detoxify your palate with the Cherry Almond Green Smoothie that complements the natural sweetness of cherries with almond butter and a squeeze of lime. Rich in antioxidants, this smoothie is the perfect power meal to start your day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen whole tart cherries, pitted
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes
  • Juice of 1/2 lime

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and slightly thick.
  3. Pour into a tall glass, and admire the vibrant greens that await your first sip.

#3 Cherry-Berry Blitz Smoothie

Say hello to summertime with this Cherry-Berry Blitz Smoothie, a delightful fusion of tart cherries, mixed berries, and a touch of vanilla protein powder. This refreshing beverage is perfect for that post-gym pick-me-up or a mid-afternoon slushie on a warm afternoon.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup non-dairy milk (almond, coconut, or soy)
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen whole tart cherries, pitted
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and thick.
  3. Pour into a tall glass and enjoy the simultaneous tang and sweetness of this Cherry-Berry Blitz Smoothie.

#4 Cherry Lemonade Slush

For a grown-up twist on classic lemonade, try this refreshing Cherry Lemonade Slush. Tart cherries and additional lemon juice lend a vibrant burst of flavor, while the vodka adds a subtle kick to create a fruity cocktail for the summer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh or frozen whole tart cherries, pitted
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup vodka (optional)
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients, except for the ice cubes, to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again until the consistency of a slushy is reached.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve with a cherry on a skewer. Adjust the amount of vodka to your preference.

#5 Cherry Banana Berry Blast Smoothie

Creamy, dreamy, and undeniably delicious, the Cherry Banana Berry Blast Smoothie is a must-try for any smoothie fan. Packed with fiber, potassium, and antioxidants, this smoothie is a powerhouse of nutrition to energize any day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen whole tart cherries, pitted
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a tall glass and marvel at the intense color and beautiful flavors of this explosion in a glass.

#6 Cherry Pineapple Refresher

Made with just four ingredients, the Cherry Pineapple Refresher could not be simpler or more delightful. The tangy pineapple and sweet cherries harmonize to create a refreshing and energizing drink that’s perfect for a warm day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh or frozen whole tart cherries, pitted
  • 1 cup diced fresh pineapple
  • 1/2 cup non-dairy milk (almond, coconut, or soy)
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and reach a slushy consistency.
  3. Pour into a glass, and sip on this Cherry Pineapple Refresher that’s so perfect for the season.

#7 Cherry Ginger Turmeric Juice

The combination of tart cherries, ginger, and turmeric creates a zesty and invigorating Cherry Ginger Turmeric Juice. Powerful anti-inflammatory properties, combined with the sweet and tangy flavors, make this juice an absolute game-changer for your health and wellbeing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh or frozen whole tart cherries, pitted
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients (except for the honey) to a juicer and extract the juice.
  2. Stir in honey until dissolved.
  3. Pour the juice into a glass and savor the exotic and invigorating blend.

Conclusion

Here you have it, friends—the Cherry Gazette’s top 7 cherry recipes that are sure to become new summer favorites. Whether you prefer a frosty smoothie or a tangy juice, these recipes all highlight the unique, beautiful flavor of cherries. So go ahead, experiment, and enjoy these cherry-licious concoctions to spend a delightful and healthy summer filled with cherries. Cheers!
