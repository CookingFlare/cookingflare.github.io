Cinnamon Churro Cupcakes: Spicy, Sugary Delights

Who says cupcakes can’t be adventurous? Take your taste buds on a fun-filled journey with cinnamon churro cupcakes – a delightful fusion of sweet and spicy flavors. These scrumptious delights are perfect for any occasion, from tea parties to festive celebrations. Get ready to whip up a batch and let your taste buds dance the night away! Ingredients: 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened 1 cup granulated sugar 2 large eggs 2 teaspoons vanilla extract 1/2 cup milk 1/2 cup brewed coffee, cooled 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1 cup sour cream 1 cup cinnamon sugar (1:1 blend of granulated sugar and ground cinnamon) 1/2 cup unsalted butter 1/2 cup heavy cream 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips Parchment paper or cupcake liners Whipped cream (optional for topping) Instructions: Prepare the Cupcakes: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners or parchment paper. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside. In a larger bowl, cream the softened unsalted butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes. Add eggs, one at a time, fully incorporating each one. Mix in vanilla extract. Gradually add flour mixture to wet ingredients, alternating with milk and coffee, starting and ending with the flour mixture. Stir in ground cinnamon. Mix until well combined. Gently fold in the sour cream until the batter is smooth. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 of the way full. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Allow cupcakes to cool completely on a wire rack. Prepare the Cinnamon Churro Frosting: In a small saucepan, melt 1/2 cup of unsalted butter over medium heat. Whisk in 1/2 cup of heavy cream, 1 cup of granulated sugar, and 1 cup of ground cinnamon. Stir until the mixture comes to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 3-4 minutes, until slightly thickened. Remove from heat and set aside. Mix in 1 cup of chocolate chips into the hot cinnamon sugar mixture, stirring until melted and well combined. Allow the frosting to cool to room temperature, and then chill in the refrigerator for at least 20 minutes to thicken. Once chilled, gently whip the frosting with a hand mixer to create a smooth and spreadable consistency. Assemble the Cinnamon Churro Cupcakes: Using a small paring knife or an apple corer, remove a small center piece from each cupcake to create a little well for the frosting. Fill each well with a generous spoonful of the whipped cinnamon frosting. Drizzle with additional chilled frosting for extra sweetness and churro flair. Top each cupcake with a generous dollop of whipped cream, if desired. Dust with extra ground cinnamon for that final churro touch. Serve these scrumptious cinnamon churro cupcakes at your next gathering and watch as they disappear quickly. Enjoy the warm embrace of cozy cinnamon and the delicate sweetness of churros in bite-sized form – a delightful treat that’s sure to leave everyone wanting more! ...
