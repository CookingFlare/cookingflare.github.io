Delicious Peanut Sauce Noodle Stir Fry: A Thai-Inspired Dish


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this delectable recipe for Peanut Sauce Noodle Stir Fry. This dish combines the rich flavors of peanut sauce with the satisfying texture of noodles and an array of fresh vegetables. It’s a perfect balance of savory, sweet, and nutty flavors, making it an instant favorite for anyone who tries it.

Ingredients:

For the Peanut Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup warm water

For the Stir Fry:

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Crushed peanuts for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Peanut Sauce:

    • In a medium bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes. Gradually add warm water, stirring until the sauce reaches a smooth and pourable consistency. Set aside.

  2. Cook the Rice Noodles:

    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the rice noodles and cook according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse the noodles under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  3. Stir Fry the Vegetables:

    • In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced red and yellow bell peppers, snap peas, and shredded carrots. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Combine the Noodles and Vegetables:

    • Add the cooked rice noodles to the skillet with the stir-fried vegetables. Pour the prepared peanut sauce over the noodles and vegetables. Toss gently to coat everything in the flavorful sauce.

  5. Finish and Serve:

    • Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle the chopped green onions and cilantro over the stir-fry. Toss once more to distribute the herbs. Transfer the peanut sauce noodle stir fry to serving plates and garnish with crushed peanuts for an extra crunch.

Tips for Success:

  • To add a protein boost to this dish, consider tossing in some cooked and diced chicken, shrimp, or tofu.
  • Adjust the level of spice in the peanut sauce by increasing or decreasing the amount of red pepper flakes to suit your taste preferences.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable selection based on what is in season or your personal favorites.

In Conclusion:

This Peanut Sauce Noodle Stir Fry is a delightful and satisfying dish that brings the exotic flavors of Thai cuisine right to your dinner table. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out on your culinary adventures, this recipe is sure to impress with its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and irresistible aroma. Give it a try, and you’ll soon find yourself coming back to it time and time again for a taste of Thailand in the comfort of your own home. Enjoy!
