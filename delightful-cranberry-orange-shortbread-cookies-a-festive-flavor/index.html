Delightful Cranberry Orange Shortbread Cookies: A Festive Flavor


The holiday season is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a batch of delightful Cranberry Orange Shortbread Cookies? These festive treats are a perfect combination of tangy cranberries and zesty orange, creating a flavor explosion that will leave your taste buds craving for more. The best part? They are incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

To make these delicious Cranberry Orange Shortbread Cookies, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • Zest of one orange
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour and powdered sugar. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy and smooth. Add the orange zest, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, mixing until a dough forms. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

  5. Gently fold in the dried cranberries, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  6. Divide the dough in half and shape each half into a log, about 2 inches in diameter. Wrap the logs tightly in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or until firm.

  7. Once the dough is firm, remove it from the refrigerator and slice it into 1/4 inch thick rounds. Place the rounds onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.

  8. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. Keep a close eye on them, as shortbread cookies can quickly go from perfectly golden to overbaked.

  9. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cookies have cooled, store them in an airtight container to maintain their freshness.

Serving Suggestions

These delightful Cranberry Orange Shortbread Cookies are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a holiday gathering, a tea party, or simply a sweet treat to enjoy with a cup of coffee. They also make fantastic gifts, especially when packaged in a festive tin or wrapped in a beautiful ribbon.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a drizzle of melted white chocolate or a sprinkle of orange zest on top of the cookies after they have cooled.
  • If you prefer a sweeter cookie, you can roll the dough logs in granulated sugar before refrigerating them.
  • Experiment with different dried fruits, such as cherries or blueberries, to create unique flavor combinations.

So go ahead and indulge in the delightful goodness of Cranberry Orange Shortbread Cookies. With their festive flavor and buttery texture, they are sure to become a favorite in your household. Happy baking!
