Juicy Fourth of July BBQ Steak Recipes for Meat Lovers


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to mark the occasion than with a sizzling BBQ feast? For meat lovers, nothing quite hits the spot like a perfectly grilled steak. Whether you prefer a tender filet mignon, a marbled ribeye, or a hearty T-bone, we’ve got you covered with some mouthwatering steak recipes that will take your Fourth of July BBQ to the next level.

Classic Grilled Ribeye Steak

Ingredients:

  • 4 ribeye steaks (1 inch thick)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to high heat.
  2. Brush the steaks with olive oil and season generously with salt, pepper, and minced garlic.
  3. Place the steaks on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or until the desired doneness is reached.
  4. During the last 2 minutes of cooking, place a sprig of rosemary on each steak to infuse it with a fragrant aroma.
  5. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Pro Tips: For an extra flavor boost, you can marinate the steaks in your favorite steak seasoning or marinade for a few hours before grilling.

Smoky BBQ T-Bone Steak

Ingredients:

  • 4 T-bone steaks
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, Worcestershire sauce, honey, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  2. Place the T-bone steaks in a large dish and pour the marinade over them, ensuring they are evenly coated. Let them marinate for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the steaks for 5-6 minutes on each side, basting with the leftover marinade until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Let the steaks rest for a few minutes before serving.

Pro Tips: For an extra smoky flavor, add a handful of soaked wood chips to your grill.

Garlic Butter Filet Mignon

Ingredients:

  • 4 filet mignon steaks
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the filet mignon steaks with salt and pepper.
  2. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the fresh thyme to the garlic butter and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  4. Preheat the grill to high heat.
  5. Grill the steaks for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, brushing them with the garlic butter mixture during grilling.
  6. Let the steaks rest for a few minutes before serving.

Pro Tips: For an extra indulgent touch, top the grilled filet mignon with a dollop of garlic butter before serving.
