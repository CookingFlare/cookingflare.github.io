Satisfying Nut-Free Trail Mix Recipe for Outdoor Adventures


Are you planning your next outdoor adventure and looking for a healthy and satisfying snack to fuel your journey? Look no further! This nut-free trail mix recipe is the perfect solution for all your snacking needs. Packed with a variety of flavors and textures, this trail mix will keep you energized and satisfied on the trail. Whether you’re hiking, camping, or simply enjoying a day outdoors, this nut-free trail mix is the ideal companion for your adventures.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of pretzels
  • 2 cups of popcorn
  • 1 cup of pumpkin seeds
  • 1 cup of sunflower seeds
  • 1 cup of dried cranberries
  • 1 cup of dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup of coconut flakes
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the pretzels, popcorn, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, dried cranberries, dark chocolate chips, and coconut flakes. Toss the ingredients together until well mixed.

  3. In a small saucepan, heat the honey and coconut oil over low heat, stirring until well combined. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract, cinnamon, and sea salt.

  4. Pour the honey mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until everything is evenly coated.

  5. Spread the mixture in an even layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the mixture is golden and fragrant.

  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  8. Once cool, break the trail mix into smaller clusters and transfer to an airtight container for storage.

Why This Trail Mix is Perfect for Outdoor Adventures

Nut-Free and Allergy-Friendly

This trail mix is completely nut-free, making it a safe and delicious option for those with nut allergies. The combination of pretzels, popcorn, seeds, and dried fruit provides a satisfying crunch and sweetness without the use of any nuts.

Balanced Nutrients for Sustained Energy

Pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds are rich in essential nutrients such as protein, healthy fats, and fiber, which are essential for sustaining energy levels during outdoor activities. The addition of dark chocolate chips provides a touch of indulgence while still offering some additional energy-boosting benefits.

Customizable and Versatile

One of the best things about this trail mix recipe is its versatility. Feel free to customize it to your liking by adding your favorite ingredients such as dried apricots, raisins, or even a sprinkle of your favorite spices. The possibilities are endless, allowing you to create a trail mix that perfectly suits your taste preferences.

Easy to Pack and Carry

This trail mix is lightweight and easy to pack, making it a convenient snack to take on any outdoor adventure. Whether you’re hitting the trails for a day hike or embarking on a multi-day camping trip, this nut-free trail mix can be portioned into resealable bags for quick and easy snacking on the go.

Delicious and Satisfying

The combination of sweet, salty, and crunchy elements in this trail mix is sure to satisfy your snack cravings while providing the sustained energy you need for your outdoor activities. The hint of honey and cinnamon adds a delightful touch of sweetness, making this trail mix a truly irresistible treat.

Conclusion

Next time you’re gearing up for an outdoor adventure, don’t forget to prepare a batch of this nut-free trail mix. Not only is it a delicious and satisfying snack, but it’s also allergy-friendly, nutrient-packed, and incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re exploring the great outdoors or simply need a wholesome snack for your everyday activities, this trail mix is a must-have companion. So, grab your ingredients, mix up a batch, and get ready to fuel your adventures with this irresistible nut-free trail mix!
