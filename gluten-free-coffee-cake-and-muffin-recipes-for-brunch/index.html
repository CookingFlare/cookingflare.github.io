Gluten-Free Coffee Cake and Muffin Recipes for Brunch


When it comes to hosting a delightful brunch, having a selection of gluten-free baked goods is a fantastic way to cater to all your guests’ dietary needs. Gluten-free coffee cakes and muffins are not only delicious but also perfect for morning gatherings. Whether you’re looking for a treat to enjoy with your coffee or a sweet addition to your brunch spread, these recipes will surely impress your guests. Let’s dive into the world of gluten-free baking with these scrumptious recipes!

Gluten-Free Coffee Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • Cinnamon for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a round cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the Greek yogurt.
  6. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  8. Sprinkle sliced almonds over the batter and dust with cinnamon.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Allow the cake to cool before serving. Enjoy a slice with your favorite hot beverage!

Gluten-Free Muffin Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup gluten-free oats
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup fresh raspberries
  • Sliced almonds for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a bowl, combine the almond flour, oats, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix the mashed bananas, honey, melted coconut oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the fresh raspberries.
  6. Divide the batter among the muffin cups and top with sliced almonds.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the muffins cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. These muffins are perfect for a grab-and-go brunch option or a sweet treat any time of the day!

Whether you prefer the moist and crumbly texture of coffee cake or the convenient and portable nature of muffins, these gluten-free recipes are sure to be a hit at your next brunch. Experiment with different fruits, nuts, and spices to customize these recipes to your liking. Serve them alongside a hot cup of coffee or tea, and watch as your guests indulge in these delectable treats. Happy baking and brunching!
