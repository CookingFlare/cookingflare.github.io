The Perfect Veggie Patty: Homemade Recipes You'll Love


Are you tired of searching for the perfect veggie patty recipe? Look no further! In this post, we’ll share some delicious homemade veggie patty recipes that are guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these recipes are for you!

1. Classic Chickpea Patty

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mash the chickpeas with a fork until they are mostly smooth.
  2. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, breadcrumbs, parsley, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into patties of your desired size.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a little oil.
  5. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

2. Quinoa and Black Bean Patty

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mash the black beans with a fork until they are mostly smooth.
  2. Add the cooked quinoa, chopped onion, minced garlic, cilantro, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into patties of your desired size.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a little oil.
  5. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are crispy on the outside and heated through.

3. Sweet Potato and Lentil Patty

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1 medium sweet potato, cooked and mashed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked lentils, mashed sweet potato, chopped onion, minced garlic, parsley, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into patties of your desired size.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a little oil.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

Conclusion

There you have it! Three delicious veggie patty recipes that are easy to make and packed with flavor. Whether you choose the classic chickpea patty, the quinoa and black bean patty, or the sweet potato and lentil patty, you’re sure to enjoy a satisfying and nutritious meal. Feel free to customize these recipes with your favorite spices and toppings, and don’t forget to serve them with your preferred bun and condiments. Get creative and make these veggie patties your own!
