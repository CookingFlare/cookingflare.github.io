Instant Pot Chili Recipes: Hearty and Spicy Comfort Food


When the aroma of perfectly seasoned chili fills your kitchen, it’s hard not to feel instantly wrapped in warmth and comfort. Chili is that quintessential dish capable of lifting your spirits on a chilly winter night, fueling you after a long day, or impressing guests with spicy, bold flavors. Cooking chili in an Instant Pot adds another layer of convenience to its already shining reputation—it transforms this hearty classic into a quick, no-fuss, and deeply flavorful meal.

In this post, we’re taking a deep dive into Instant Pot chili recipes. Whether you’re a fan of traditional beef chili, adventurous turkey versions, or meatless wonders, you’ll find a recipe here that will satisfy your chili cravings. Plus, these recipes are easy to customize, so you can tailor them to suit your heat tolerance and personal preferences.

Why Use an Instant Pot for Chili?

Before diving into the recipes, let’s talk about why the Instant Pot is an ideal tool for cooking chili. Traditionally, chili benefits from long, slow cooking times to build flavors, tenderize the meat, and thicken the sauce. But with an Instant Pot, those hours of slow cooking are reduced to mere minutes, all while achieving the same depth of flavor.

Here are the top reasons why you’ll love using the Instant Pot for your chili recipes:

  1. Time Saving: Pressure cooking accelerates the cooking process without compromising flavor.
  2. Hands-Off Cooking: Once you set the cooking time, there’s no need to hover over the stove.
  3. Enhanced Flavor: The pressurized environment locks in moisture and creates a richness in flavor that rivals traditional methods.
  4. One-Pot Wonder: You can sauté, pressure cook, and simmer all in the same pot, leaving you with less cleanup.

Now, let’s jump into some of the best Instant Pot chili recipes that are both quick to make and packed with bold, hearty flavors.

Classic Instant Pot Beef Chili

This is your go-to recipe for the ultimate classic chili experience. The secret lies in balancing beautifully browned ground beef, bold spices, and a hint of acidity to create that iconic chili flavor. And the best part? This recipe can be on your table in under 45 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef (80/20 ground chuck works best)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 (14-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (14-ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (15-ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Turn your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil.
  2. Add the ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned, breaking up the meat as it cooks. Once done, remove any excess grease.
  3. Add the diced onion and garlic to the pot and cook for 2-3 minutes until soft and fragrant.
  4. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, oregano, and cayenne pepper. Let the spices toast for about 30 seconds to release their fragrance.
  5. Add the tomato paste and stir to coat the beef and onions.
  6. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, kidney beans, and beef broth. Stir everything to combine.
  7. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to Manual or Pressure Cook on high for 15 minutes.
  8. Once the cooking cycle is complete, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes before performing a quick release.
  9. Stir the chili, taste, and adjust seasonings if needed. Serve hot with your favorite toppings, such as shredded cheese, sour cream, green onions, or jalapeños.

Instant Pot Turkey Chili

For a leaner take on chili, turkey is an excellent substitute for ground beef. This chili is packed with protein and vegetables, making it both hearty and healthy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound lean ground turkey
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon (optional, for added warmth)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 (14-ounce) can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 (14-ounce) can white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (14-ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil.
  2. Add ground turkey and cook until browned, breaking it up into crumbles as it cooks. Season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Stir in the onion, bell pepper, and garlic, cooking for 2–3 minutes until softened.
  4. Mix in the chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and cinnamon, toasting the spices for a burst of flavor.
  5. Add the tomato paste and stir well.
  6. Pour in the fire-roasted diced tomatoes, both types of beans, and chicken broth.
  7. Secure the lid and set the cooker to Manual or Pressure Cook on high for 12 minutes.
  8. Allow a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before performing a quick release.
  9. Give the chili a good stir, adjust seasonings as necessary, and serve with your favorite toppings.

Vegetarian Instant Pot Chili

For those skipping the meat, this vegetarian version is just as hearty and satisfying. With beans, vegetables, and bold seasoning, you won’t miss the meat one bit.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (14-ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (14-ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (14-ounce) can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil on the sauté setting of your Instant Pot.
  2. Add the onion, bell peppers, and carrots, cooking until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the garlic, chili powder, smoked paprika, cumin, and coriander. Toast the spices for 1 minute.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, black beans, kidney beans, pinto beans, vegetable broth, and corn. Stir to combine.
  5. Seal the Instant Pot lid and set to Manual or Pressure Cook on high for 10 minutes.
  6. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before performing a quick release.
  7. Stir and adjust seasoning as desired. Serve with cilantro, avocado slices, or a dollop of Greek yogurt.

Chili Topping Ideas

No chili is complete without toppings. Here are some ideas to elevate your meal:

  • Shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
  • A dollop of sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Chopped green onions or fresh cilantro
  • Sliced avocado or guacamole
  • Crushed tortilla chips
  • A squeeze of lime juice for brightness

Storing and Reheating Chili

One of the best things about chili is how well it stores and reheats. In fact, many people claim that chili tastes better the next day after the flavors have melded together.

  • Refrigerate: Let the chili cool completely, then store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
  • Freeze: Transfer cooled chili to freezer-safe containers or bags, leaving some room for expansion, and freeze for up to 3 months.
  • Reheat: Warm your chili on the stovetop over medium heat or in the microwave for a quick meal. Add a splash of water or broth if it has thickened too much during storage.

In Conclusion

Instant Pot chili recipes are a game-changer for anyone looking to whip up a quick, hearty, and flavorful meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re a traditional beef lover, prefer a leaner turkey version, or need a meat-free alternative, there’s an Instant Pot chili recipe here for everyone to enjoy. Packed with wholesome ingredients and rich flavors, these chilis are perfect for everything from weeknight dinners to game-day spreads. So grab your spoon, sprinkle on your favorite toppings, and savor every cozy, spicy bite!
