Creamy and Cheesy Baked Mac and Cheese: A Classic Comfort Dish


There’s nothing quite like a piping hot dish of creamy and cheesy baked mac and cheese to warm the soul. This classic comfort dish has been a favorite for generations, and for good reason. The combination of tender macaroni, rich cheese sauce, and a golden, crispy topping is a surefire way to bring joy to the table. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just craving some nostalgic comfort food, this recipe is a winner.

Ingredients

For the mac and cheese:

  • 1 pound (16 oz) elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Gruyere cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the topping:

  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, whisk in the flour to create a roux. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly.

  4. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Cook the sauce until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the cheddar, mozzarella, and Gruyere cheese until melted and smooth. Add the garlic powder, salt, and pepper, adjusting to taste.

  6. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce, stirring until well coated. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish.

  7. In a small bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, melted butter, and Parmesan cheese. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the mac and cheese.

  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

  9. Let the mac and cheese cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.

Tips for Success

  • Use a combination of cheeses for a rich and complex flavor profile.
  • For extra creaminess, you can add a splash of heavy cream to the cheese sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the topping with different herbs or additional cheese for a unique twist.

Serving Suggestions

Creamy and cheesy baked mac and cheese pairs perfectly with a crisp green salad or steamed vegetables for a well-rounded meal. It’s also a fantastic side dish for barbecues, potlucks, and holiday gatherings. For a comforting solo indulgence, simply grab a fork and savor each creamy, cheesy bite.

Conclusion

Creamy and cheesy baked mac and cheese is a timeless dish that never fails to satisfy. With its luscious, velvety cheese sauce and irresistible golden crust, it’s a beloved classic for good reason. Whether you’re seeking a crowd-pleasing main course or a cozy weeknight dinner, this recipe is a guaranteed winner. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to experience the ultimate comfort food bliss.
