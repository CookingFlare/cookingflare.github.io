Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells: A Cheesy and Indulgent Pasta Bake


If you’re searching for a dish that combines comfort, indulgence, and a touch of gourmet flair, look no further than Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells. This recipe is a delightful twist on traditional pasta bakes, featuring large pasta shells generously filled with a creamy chicken Alfredo mixture, topped with gooey melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or meal prep, this dish is sure to impress with its rich flavors and satisfying textures. Let’s dive into the world of cheesy, creamy goodness that is Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells!

The Allure of Stuffed Pasta

Stuffed pasta has long been a favorite in many cuisines around the world. From classic Italian cannelloni to ricotta-filled manicotti, there’s something undeniably satisfying about biting into a pasta shell brimming with delicious filling. Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells take this concept to the next level, combining the beloved flavors of chicken Alfredo with the comfort of baked pasta. This dish not only offers a hearty meal but also serves as a canvas for your culinary creativity.

Why Choose Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells?

1. Comfort Food at Its Best

Chicken Alfredo is a classic comfort food dish that warms the soul. When you stuff it into pasta shells and bake it to perfection, you elevate the experience. The creamy Alfredo sauce enveloping tender chicken and pasta is a dish that feels like a hug on a plate.

2. Customization Galore

One of the best aspects of Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells is the versatility it offers. You can easily modify the recipe to suit your taste preferences. Want to add vegetables? Spinach, mushrooms, or roasted bell peppers can enhance both flavor and nutrition. Prefer a different protein? Swap the chicken for shrimp or even a meat substitute for a vegetarian option.

3. Make-Ahead Magic

This dish is perfect for meal prep or hosting. You can prepare the stuffed shells ahead of time, refrigerate them, and bake them just before serving. This not only saves time but also allows the flavors to meld together beautifully.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To create this cheesy and indulgent Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells, gather the following ingredients:

For the Stuffed Shells:

  • 20-24 large pasta shells: The perfect vessel for holding all the delicious filling.
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded: Use rotisserie chicken for convenience or cook your own.
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese: Adds creaminess and richness to the filling.
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded: For that gooey, melty topping.
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese, grated: A must for flavor and a touch of sophistication.
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder: For an aromatic kick.
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning: Adds depth and an herby note.
  • Salt and pepper to taste: Essential for bringing out the flavors.

For the Alfredo Sauce:

  • 1 cup heavy cream: The base of a rich and creamy sauce.
  • ½ cup butter: For a luxurious texture.
  • 2 cups Parmesan cheese, grated: For flavor and thickness.
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced: Fresh garlic enhances the overall taste.
  • Salt and pepper to taste: Essential for seasoning.

For Topping:

  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded: More cheesy goodness for the top.
  • Optional fresh parsley, chopped: For garnish and a pop of color.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pasta Shells

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the large pasta shells and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water to prevent sticking. Set aside on a baking sheet to cool.

Step 2: Make the Alfredo Sauce

  1. Melt the Butter: In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the Cream: Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Stir occasionally, allowing the cream to thicken slightly.

  3. Incorporate the Cheese: Gradually add in the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Filling

  1. Mix the Filling: In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

Step 4: Assemble the Stuffed Shells

  1. Fill the Shells: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread a thin layer of Alfredo sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Stuff the Shells: Carefully stuff each pasta shell with the chicken mixture and place them in the baking dish, open side up.

  3. Top with Sauce: Once all the shells are filled, pour the remaining Alfredo sauce over the stuffed shells, ensuring they are well-covered.

  4. Add More Cheese: Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese generously over the top.

Step 5: Bake to Perfection

  1. Bake: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

  2. Garnish and Serve: Once baked, remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired, then serve warm.

Tips for the Perfect Stuffed Shells

  • Don’t Overcook the Pasta: When cooking the shells, aim for al dente. They will continue to cook in the oven, and you don’t want them to become mushy.

  • Experiment with Flavors: Feel free to add your favorite herbs or spices to the filling or sauce. A pinch of nutmeg in the Alfredo sauce can add a delightful depth of flavor.

  • Make it Ahead: You can prepare the entire dish ahead of time and refrigerate it unbaked. Just increase the baking time by about 10-15 minutes if baking from cold.

Pairing Suggestions

Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells are a meal in themselves, but if you want to round out your dining experience, consider pairing them with:

  • Garlic Bread: A classic side that complements the rich flavors of the dish.
  • Caesar Salad: The crispness of romaine and the tangy dressing provide a refreshing contrast.
  • Roasted Vegetables: Seasonal roasted veggies add a healthy touch and vibrant colors to your plate.

Storing and Reheating Leftovers

Should you have leftovers (which is unlikely given how delicious this dish is!), store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. To reheat, simply pop them in the oven at 350°F (175°C) until warmed through, or microwave individual servings for convenience.

Conclusion

Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells are more than just a meal; they embody the essence of comfort food and culinary creativity. This indulgent pasta bake is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser that can be enjoyed on any occasion, from a cozy family dinner to a festive gathering with friends. With easy preparation, customizable ingredients, and rich, cheesy goodness, you’ll find yourself reaching for seconds (or thirds!). So why wait? Gather your ingredients and treat yourself to this deliciously cheesy delight. Your taste buds will thank you!
