Apple Cinnamon French Toast Casserole: A Perfect Brunch Dish

3 min · 536 words

Are you ready to elevate your brunch game with a delightful and comforting dish that is sure to impress your guests? Look no further than this Apple Cinnamon French Toast Casserole. This delicious recipe combines the warmth of cinnamon, the sweetness of apples, and the richness of French toast into a delightful casserole that is perfect for any brunch occasion.

Ingredients

To get started, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of French bread, cut into cubes
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 2 apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of cold butter, cut into small pieces
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Place the cubed French bread in the prepared baking dish.

  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, whole milk, heavy cream, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, and nutmeg. Pour this mixture over the bread, making sure to coat all the pieces evenly. Press the bread down with a spatula to ensure it is well-soaked.

  4. Arrange the apple slices over the top of the bread mixture, then sprinkle the remaining teaspoon of cinnamon over the apples.

  5. In a separate bowl, combine the brown sugar, flour, and cold butter. Use a pastry cutter or your fingers to mix the ingredients together until crumbly. Sprinkle this streusel mixture evenly over the apples.

  6. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the casserole is set and the top is golden brown.

  7. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Cut into squares, drizzle with maple syrup, and serve warm.

Why It’s Perfect for Brunch

This Apple Cinnamon French Toast Casserole is the perfect dish for brunch for several reasons. First, it can be prepared ahead of time, allowing you to spend more time with your guests rather than slaving away in the kitchen. Simply assemble the casserole the night before, cover it, and refrigerate. In the morning, all you have to do is pop it in the oven and let the delicious aroma fill your home.

Second, this casserole is a crowd-pleaser. The combination of warm, comforting flavors and the sweet aroma of cinnamon and apples will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Finally, this dish is versatile. While it’s a standout on its own, you can also serve it with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a truly indulgent treat.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Apple Cinnamon French Toast Casserole is a brunch game-changer. With its ease of preparation, crowd-pleasing flavors, and versatility, it’s sure to become a staple in your entertaining repertoire. So, the next time you’re planning a brunch gathering, give this recipe a try and watch as it becomes the star of the table.
