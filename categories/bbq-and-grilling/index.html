BBQ Chicken Flatbread: Quick and Flavorful Recipes

Are you looking for a quick and flavorful meal that will satisfy your cravings for BBQ chicken? Look no further! This BBQ chicken flatbread recipe is the perfect combination of smoky, tangy, and savory flavors, all packed into a convenient and easy-to-make flatbread. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner with friends or craving a delicious homemade meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your collection. Ingredients For the Flatbread: 2 cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 cup Greek yogurt 2 tablespoons olive oil For the BBQ Chicken Topping: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 cup BBQ sauce 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Step 1: Preparing the Flatbread In a large bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the Greek yogurt and olive oil to the flour mixture. Mix until a dough forms. On a lightly floured surface, knead the dough for a few minutes until smooth. Divide the dough into two equal portions and roll each portion into a thin oval shape to form the flatbreads. Step 2: Cooking the Chicken Preheat the grill or a grill pan over medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips. Step 3: Assembling the Flatbread Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Place the prepared flatbreads on a baking sheet. Spread a layer of BBQ sauce over each flatbread, leaving a small border around the edges. Arrange the sliced chicken and red onion over the BBQ sauce. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the top of each flatbread. Step 4: Baking the Flatbread Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges of the flatbread are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly. Remove the flatbreads from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle the chopped cilantro over the hot flatbreads for a burst of freshness. Serving Suggestions This BBQ chicken flatbread is best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, with a side of crisp, mixed greens or a refreshing cucumber and tomato salad. For a delightful contrast, pair it with a glass of chilled lemonade or your favorite craft beer. ...

BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad: Healthy and Flavorful Recipes

Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s perfect for summer? Look no further than this BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad! Packed with protein, fresh vegetables, and a burst of flavor, this salad is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a satisfying meal, this BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad is the perfect choice. Ingredients For the BBQ Chicken: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1/2 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce 1 tablespoon olive oil Salt and pepper to taste For the Salad: 6 cups mixed salad greens 4 hard-boiled eggs, sliced 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cup corn kernels 1 large avocado, diced 1/2 cup crumbled bacon 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese 1/4 cup chopped green onions For the Dressing: 1/2 cup Greek yogurt 2 tablespoons mayonnaise 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar 1 tablespoon honey 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Step 1: Prepare the BBQ Chicken Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then brush them with olive oil. Place the chicken breasts on the grill and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through. During the last few minutes of grilling, brush the chicken with BBQ sauce, allowing it to caramelize and create a delicious glaze. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips. Step 2: Assemble the Salad In a large bowl, arrange the mixed salad greens as the base of the salad. Arrange the sliced hard-boiled eggs, cherry tomatoes, corn kernels, diced avocado, crumbled bacon, and crumbled blue cheese on top of the greens. Add the sliced BBQ chicken on the salad. Step 3: Make the Dressing In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until smooth and well combined. Step 4: Serve and Enjoy Drizzle the dressing over the BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad, or serve it on the side. Garnish the salad with chopped green onions. Toss the salad just before serving to ensure each bite is coated with the delicious dressing. Nutritional Information This BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad is not only bursting with flavor, but it’s also a nutritious meal option. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and an array of vitamins and minerals from the fresh vegetables, this salad is a well-rounded choice for a satisfying meal. ...

Grilling Scallops: Elegant Recipes for Seafood Lovers

If you’re a seafood enthusiast, then grilling scallops is a culinary delight you won’t want to miss. Scallops, with their delicate flavor and tender texture, are perfect for grilling, and they make an elegant addition to any meal. In this blog post, we’ll explore some exquisite recipes for grilling scallops that will elevate your seafood game and impress your guests. Grilled Scallops with Lemon and Herb Butter Ingredients: Fresh large sea scallops 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened Zest of 1 lemon 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, lemon zest, chopped parsley, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined. Pat the scallops dry with paper towels and season them with salt and pepper. Place the scallops on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are opaque and have grill marks. Once the scallops are cooked, transfer them to a serving platter and top each one with a generous dollop of the lemon and herb butter mixture. Serve immediately and enjoy the succulent flavors of the grilled scallops with the zesty, herb-infused butter. Grilled Scallop and Mango Skewers Ingredients: Fresh large sea scallops Ripe mango, cut into chunks 1/4 cup olive oil 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice 1 teaspoon honey 1 teaspoon ground cumin Salt and pepper to taste Fresh cilantro, for garnish Instructions: Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill. Thread the scallops and mango chunks onto the skewers, alternating between the two. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, honey, ground cumin, salt, and pepper to create a marinade. Brush the marinade over the scallop and mango skewers, ensuring they are well coated. Preheat the grill to medium heat and place the skewers on the grill. Grill for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the scallops are opaque and the mango is lightly caramelized. Once done, remove the skewers from the grill and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Grilled Scallops with Bacon and Balsamic Glaze Ingredients: Fresh large sea scallops Thinly sliced bacon Balsamic glaze Salt and pepper to taste Toothpicks Instructions: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Wrap each scallop with a slice of bacon and secure it with a toothpick. Season the bacon-wrapped scallops with salt and pepper. Place the scallops on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the bacon is crispy and the scallops are cooked through. Once done, transfer the scallops to a serving platter and drizzle them with balsamic glaze for a delightful sweet and tangy finish. Grilling scallops opens up a world of culinary possibilities, allowing you to experiment with different flavors and textures. Whether you prefer the simplicity of lemon and herb butter, the exotic combination of scallops and mango, or the indulgent pairing of bacon and balsamic glaze, these recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and impress your guests.
