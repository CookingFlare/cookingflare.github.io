Protein-Packed Quinoa and Black Bean Wrap


Looking for a nutritious and satisfying meal that’s easy to make and full of flavor? Look no further than this Protein-Packed Quinoa and Black Bean Wrap recipe. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this wrap is sure to become a favorite. Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of delicious ingredients, it’s perfect for lunch, dinner, or even as a hearty snack.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to make this delicious wrap:

  • Quinoa: 1 cup, rinsed
  • Black Beans: 1 can (15 ounces), drained and rinsed
  • Red Bell Pepper: 1 large, diced
  • Red Onion: 1/2 medium, finely chopped
  • Corn: 1/2 cup (fresh or frozen)
  • Fresh Cilantro: 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Lime Juice: From 1 lime
  • Cumin: 1 teaspoon, ground
  • Chili Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon, or to taste
  • Black Pepper: 1/4 teaspoon, freshly ground
  • Tortillas: 4 large (whole wheat or gluten-free)

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Quinoa

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa with 2 cups of water.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to low.
  3. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa has absorbed all the water and is tender.
  4. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and set aside to cool.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, black beans, diced red bell pepper, chopped red onion, corn, and chopped cilantro.
  2. Drizzle the lime juice over the mixture and sprinkle with ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Stir well to combine, ensuring the spices are evenly distributed.

Step 3: Assemble the Wraps

  1. Warm the tortillas slightly in a dry skillet or microwave to make them easier to fold.
  2. Spoon a generous portion of the quinoa and black bean mixture onto each tortilla, spreading it out evenly.
  3. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and then roll it up tightly, starting from the bottom.
  4. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Optionally, you can warm the assembled wraps in a skillet for a minute on each side to crisp them up slightly.
  2. Cut each wrap in half diagonally and serve immediately.
  3. These wraps can also be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Enjoy them cold or gently reheated.

Tips for Success

  • Customize: Feel free to customize these wraps with your favorite toppings such as avocado slices, shredded lettuce, or a dollop of Greek yogurt.
  • Make Ahead: Prepare the filling in advance and assemble the wraps just before serving for a quick meal.
  • Nutritional Boost: Add spinach or kale to the filling for an extra nutritional boost.

Why Quinoa and Black Beans?

Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids our bodies need. Paired with black beans, which are also rich in protein and fiber, this wrap provides a balanced and satisfying meal. The addition of fresh vegetables and spices not only enhances the flavor but also boosts the nutritional content, making it a wholesome choice for any mealtime.

Final Thoughts

This Protein-Packed Quinoa and Black Bean Wrap recipe is not only nutritious but also delicious and versatile. Whether you enjoy it for lunch, dinner, or even as a portable snack, it’s sure to keep you satisfied and energized. Experiment with different variations to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Embrace the goodness of plant-based ingredients with this easy-to-make wrap that’s perfect for any occasion. Enjoy!
