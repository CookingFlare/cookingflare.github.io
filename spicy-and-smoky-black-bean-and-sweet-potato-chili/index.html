Spicy and Smoky Black Bean and Sweet Potato Chili


If you’re looking for a hearty and flavorful dish to warm you up on a chilly evening, look no further than this Spicy and Smoky Black Bean and Sweet Potato Chili recipe. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bold flavors, this chili is a sure crowd-pleaser. Plus, it’s a great option for vegetarians and meat-lovers alike. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delicious and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic, red bell pepper, and jalapeño pepper, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  2. Stir in the diced sweet potatoes, cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally to coat the sweet potatoes with the spices.

  3. Add the black beans, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender.

  4. Season the chili with salt and pepper to taste. If you prefer a thicker consistency, use a potato masher to lightly mash some of the sweet potatoes and black beans.

  5. Once the chili is ready, ladle it into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with lime wedges on the side for a burst of citrus flavor.

Tips and Substitutions

  • For an extra smoky flavor, you can add a splash of liquid smoke to the chili.
  • If you prefer a meaty texture, you can add plant-based ground meat or crumbled tofu to the chili.
  • Feel free to customize the level of spiciness by adjusting the amount of cayenne pepper or adding a dash of hot sauce.

Final Thoughts

This Spicy and Smoky Black Bean and Sweet Potato Chili is a wonderful dish for cozy gatherings, weeknight dinners, or meal prepping for the week ahead. It’s not only delicious and satisfying but also rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants. Whether you’re a fan of spicy foods or simply enjoy a comforting bowl of chili, this recipe is a must-try.

So, gather your ingredients, embrace the aromas of the spices, and savor every spoonful of this delightful chili. Your taste buds and your body will thank you for it. Enjoy!

This recipe is a delightful blend of flavors and textures, bringing together the natural sweetness of sweet potatoes, the earthy richness of black beans, and the smoky heat of spices. It’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. If you have any feedback or your own variations of this recipe, feel free to share them in the comments below!
