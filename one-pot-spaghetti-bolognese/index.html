One Pot Spaghetti Bolognese


Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make meal that the whole family will love? Look no further than one pot spaghetti bolognese! This classic Italian dish is a go-to favorite for many, and for good reason. It’s hearty, flavorful, and best of all, it can be made in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze. In this blog post, I’ll walk you through a step-by-step guide to creating a mouthwatering one pot spaghetti bolognese that will have everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely grated
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Brown the Ground Beef: Heat a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until it is browned and cooked through. Remove any excess fat from the pot.

  2. Add Aromatics: Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, grated carrot, and chopped celery to the pot with the ground beef. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Combine Ingredients: Pour in the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Cook the Spaghetti: Break the spaghetti in half and add it to the pot. Use tongs to gently push the spaghetti into the liquid. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to medium-low. Cover the pot and cook, stirring occasionally, until the spaghetti is al dente and the sauce has thickened, about 15-20 minutes.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Once the spaghetti is cooked to your liking, remove the pot from the heat. Serve the one pot spaghetti bolognese in individual bowls, garnished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.

Tips for Success:

  • To add a depth of flavor, consider adding a splash of red wine when you add the crushed tomatoes.
  • Don’t be afraid to customize the recipe with your favorite vegetables or herbs. Mushrooms, bell peppers, or a dash of red pepper flakes can all add a unique twist to the dish.

Why One Pot Spaghetti Bolognese?

The beauty of one pot spaghetti bolognese lies in its simplicity. By cooking everything in one pot, the flavors meld together beautifully, creating a rich and satisfying meal. The convenience of using just one pot also means less time spent on cleanup, making it an ideal choice for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

This dish is also incredibly versatile. You can easily adjust the ingredients to suit your preferences or dietary restrictions. For a vegetarian version, simply swap the ground beef for plant-based crumbles or lentils. Additionally, it’s a great way to sneak in extra servings of vegetables, making it a hit with kids and adults alike.

Final Thoughts

One pot spaghetti bolognese is a timeless classic that never fails to disappoint. Whether you’re a cooking novice or a seasoned pro, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Not only does it deliver on flavor, but it also offers the convenience of minimal cleanup. So, the next time you’re craving a comforting and satisfying meal, give this one pot spaghetti bolognese a try. You won’t be disappointed!
