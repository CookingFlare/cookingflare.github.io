Hearty Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts Casserole


Are you tired of the same old boring side dishes? Do you crave something hearty, comforting, and packed with flavor? Look no further! Today, we’re going to dive into the world of keto cooking and create a show-stopping Hearty Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts Casserole that will become a staple in your household.

The Power of Brussels Sprouts

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s talk about the star of the show: Brussels sprouts. These tiny, green cruciferous vegetables are often misunderstood and underappreciated. But trust us, they’re a game-changer when it comes to adding texture, flavor, and nutrition to your meals. Rich in vitamins C and K, fiber, and antioxidants, Brussels sprouts are a nutritional powerhouse that will keep you full and satisfied.

The Magic of Keto

For those new to the keto lifestyle, let’s quickly cover the basics. Keto, short for ketogenic, is a high-fat, low-carb diet that focuses on inducing a metabolic state called ketosis. When your body is in ketosis, it burns fat for energy instead of carbohydrates, resulting in rapid weight loss, improved blood sugar control, and increased energy levels. The key to a successful keto diet is to keep your macronutrient ratios in check: 70-80% fat, 15-20% protein, and 5-10% carbohydrates.

The Recipe

With that said, let’s get to the recipe! This Hearty Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts Casserole is a masterclass in balancing flavors and textures. With a rich, creamy cheese sauce, crispy bacon, and tender Brussels sprouts, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 6 slices of bacon, diced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the diced bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the unsalted butter and cook until melted. Add the halved Brussels sprouts and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until tender and caramelized. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked Brussels sprouts, crispy bacon, grated cheddar cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, heavy cream, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish and top with additional grated cheese if desired.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe more substantial, add some cooked ground beef or chicken to the mixture.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, add some diced onions or shallots to the skillet with the Brussels sprouts.
  • If you’re not a fan of heavy cream, substitute with Greek yogurt or sour cream for a lower-carb option.
  • This recipe freezes beautifully! Simply thaw overnight in the refrigerator and reheat in the oven until warmed through.

Conclusion

And there you have it, folks! A Hearty Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts Casserole that’s sure to become a staple in your keto kitchen. With its rich flavors, tender Brussels sprouts, and crispy bacon, this dish is the perfect combination of comfort and nutrition. So go ahead, get cooking, and indulge in the creamy, cheesy goodness that awaits!
