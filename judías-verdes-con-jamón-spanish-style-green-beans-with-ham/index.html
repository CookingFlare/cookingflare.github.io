Judías Verdes con Jamón: Spanish-Style Green Beans with Ham


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious side dish that captures the essence of Spanish cuisine, look no further than Judías Verdes con Jamón. This traditional Spanish recipe combines tender green beans with the rich and savory flavors of jamón, creating a dish that is both satisfying and full of Mediterranean flair.

Ingredients

To make Judías Verdes con Jamón, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh green beans, washed and trimmed
  • 4 ounces of jamón (Spanish cured ham), diced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Here’s how you can prepare this delightful Spanish-style green beans with ham dish:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced jamón and cook until it becomes crispy and golden brown. Remove the jamón from the skillet and set it aside for later.

  2. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Add the trimmed green beans to the skillet and stir well to coat them with the onion and garlic mixture. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Reduce the heat to low and cover the skillet. Allow the green beans to cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until they become tender but still retain a slight crunch.

  5. Once the green beans are cooked to your desired tenderness, remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle the crispy jamón over the top. Give it a gentle toss to combine all the flavors.

  6. Serve the Judías Verdes con Jamón hot as a side dish alongside your favorite Spanish main course. This dish pairs exceptionally well with grilled meats, roasted chicken, or even a simple Spanish omelette.

The Flavorful Combination

The marriage of tender green beans and savory jamón creates a delightful contrast of textures and flavors in this Spanish-style dish. The green beans provide a fresh and vibrant crunch, while the jamón adds a rich and smoky undertone that elevates the dish to new heights.

The use of jamón, a staple in Spanish cuisine, infuses the green beans with a distinct umami flavor that is hard to resist. The saltiness of the ham perfectly balances the natural sweetness of the green beans, creating a harmonious combination that will have your taste buds singing.

Nutritional Benefits

In addition to its delicious taste, Judías Verdes con Jamón also offers numerous nutritional benefits. Green beans are a great source of vitamins A, C, and K, as well as dietary fiber. They are also low in calories and fat, making them an excellent choice for those seeking a healthy and balanced diet.

The addition of jamón adds a protein boost to this dish, making it a satisfying and wholesome option for both meat lovers and vegetarians alike. The combination of protein and fiber in this recipe will help keep you feeling full and satisfied, making it an ideal side dish for any meal.

Conclusion

Judías Verdes con Jamón is a classic Spanish recipe that showcases the best of Mediterranean flavors. With its simple yet delicious combination of green beans and jamón, this dish is a perfect addition to any meal. Whether you’re planning a festive Spanish feast or simply want to add a touch of Spanish flair to your dinner table, this recipe is sure to impress.

So next time you’re in the mood for a side dish that is both flavorful and nutritious, give Judías Verdes con Jamón a try. Your taste buds will thank you for it!
