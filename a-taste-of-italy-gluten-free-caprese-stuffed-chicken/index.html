A Taste of Italy: Gluten-Free Caprese Stuffed Chicken


Are you looking for a delicious Italian-inspired dish that is both gluten-free and packed with flavor? Look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for Caprese Stuffed Chicken. With its combination of juicy chicken breast, fresh tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and aromatic basil, this dish is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Balsamic glaze, for drizzling (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Prepare a baking dish by lining it with parchment paper or lightly greasing it.

  2. Lay the chicken breasts flat on a clean surface. Using a sharp knife, carefully cut a pocket into the side of each chicken breast, being careful not to cut all the way through. This pocket will be used to hold the Caprese filling.

  3. Season the inside of each chicken breast with salt and pepper. Stuff each pocket with a slice of tomato, a slice of mozzarella cheese, and a few basil leaves. Press the edges of the chicken breast together to seal the pocket.

  4. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the stuffed chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. This step will help to lock in the flavors and create a crispy exterior.

  5. Transfer the seared chicken breasts to the prepared baking dish and place them in the preheated oven. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

  6. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure a moist and tender chicken.

  7. To serve, place a stuffed chicken breast on each plate. Drizzle with balsamic glaze, if desired, for an extra burst of flavor. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a touch of freshness.

  8. Enjoy your gluten-free Caprese Stuffed Chicken with a side of roasted vegetables, a crisp salad, or even some garlic bread for a complete Italian-inspired meal.

Conclusion

There you have it – a simple yet delicious recipe for Caprese Stuffed Chicken that will transport you to the beautiful flavors of Italy. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply looking for a flavorful meal, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings. With its combination of juicy chicken, fresh tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil, it’s a true taste of Italy that you can enjoy in the comfort of your own home. So why wait? Gather your ingredients, get cooking, and indulge in a culinary journey that will leave your taste buds singing. Buon appetito!
