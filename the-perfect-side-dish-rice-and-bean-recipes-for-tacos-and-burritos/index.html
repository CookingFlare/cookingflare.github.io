The Perfect Side Dish: Rice and Bean Recipes for Tacos and Burritos


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, you know that tacos and burritos are incomplete without the perfect side dish. Rice and beans are classic accompaniments that not only complement the flavors of these dishes but also add a wholesome touch to your meal. Whether you prefer a simple side of rice and beans or want to elevate it with additional flavors, we’ve got you covered with some delicious recipes that are sure to become your new favorites.

Classic Spanish Rice and Beans

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until they turn translucent.
  2. Add the rice to the pan and stir to coat it with the oil. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in the broth, add the cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for 20 minutes or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and gently fold in the black beans.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

This classic Spanish rice and beans recipe is simple yet flavorful, making it a perfect side dish for both tacos and burritos. The combination of fluffy rice, savory beans, and aromatic spices will enhance the overall dining experience.

Cilantro Lime Rice with Pinto Beans

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can (15 oz) pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the rice and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 18-20 minutes or until the rice is tender.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the cooked rice, pinto beans, chopped cilantro, lime juice, and salt.
  4. Gently toss the ingredients together until well combined.
  5. Serve the cilantro lime rice with pinto beans alongside your favorite tacos or burritos.

The zesty flavors of cilantro and lime add a refreshing twist to the traditional rice and beans, making this side dish a delightful addition to your Mexican-inspired meal.

Spicy Mexican Red Rice and Black Bean Medley

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped scallions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  2. Stir in the rice and cook for 2-3 minutes until lightly toasted.
  3. Pour in the broth, add the diced tomatoes with green chilies, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes or until the rice is cooked.
  4. Once the rice is done, gently fold in the black beans and mix until heated through.
  5. Garnish the spicy Mexican red rice and black bean medley with chopped scallions before serving.

This vibrant and spicy rice and bean dish is a perfect way to add a kick to your tacos and burritos, adding depth of flavor and a hint of heat to your meal.

Conclusion

Rice and beans are versatile, nutritious, and delicious, making them the perfect side dish for any Mexican-inspired meal. Whether you prefer the classic combination or want to experiment with different flavors, these recipes offer a variety of options to suit your taste. Elevate your tacos and burritos with these delightful rice and bean dishes, and take your culinary journey to the next level!
