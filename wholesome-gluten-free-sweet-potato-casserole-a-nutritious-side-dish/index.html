Wholesome Gluten-Free Sweet Potato Casserole: A Nutritious Side Dish


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious side dish that will impress your guests and satisfy your taste buds? Look no further than this wholesome gluten-free sweet potato casserole! Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, this dish is not only a crowd-pleaser but also a healthy addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup coconut sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with coconut oil.

  2. Place the diced sweet potatoes in a large pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil and cook until the sweet potatoes are fork-tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain the sweet potatoes and return them to the pot.

  3. Add the coconut oil, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, almond milk, vanilla extract, and salt to the pot with the sweet potatoes. Mash the sweet potatoes until smooth and well combined.

  4. Transfer the sweet potato mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  5. In a small bowl, mix together the chopped pecans and coconut sugar. Sprinkle this mixture over the top of the sweet potato mixture.

  6. Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the casserole is heated through.

  7. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Nutritional Benefits

This wholesome gluten-free sweet potato casserole is not only delicious but also packed with nutritional benefits. Sweet potatoes are a great source of fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making them a nutritious addition to your diet. The use of coconut oil and almond milk adds healthy fats and richness to the dish, while the pecans provide a dose of heart-healthy fats and protein. This casserole is also naturally sweetened with maple syrup and coconut sugar, making it a healthier alternative to traditional sweet treats.

Serving Suggestions

This casserole is a versatile dish that can be served as a side with roasted chicken, grilled fish, or as a part of a vegetarian feast. It’s perfect for holiday gatherings, potlucks, or simply as a comforting side for a family dinner. The warm spices and natural sweetness make it a hit with both kids and adults.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this wholesome gluten-free sweet potato casserole is a fantastic addition to any meal. It’s a nourishing, flavorful, and satisfying dish that will leave everyone asking for seconds. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply looking for a wholesome side dish, this casserole is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.
