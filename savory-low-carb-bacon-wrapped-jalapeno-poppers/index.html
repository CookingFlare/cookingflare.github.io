Savory Low Carb Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers


If you’re looking for a mouthwatering appetizer that’s sure to impress your guests, look no further than these savory low carb bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers. This recipe takes the classic jalapeno popper to a whole new level by adding the irresistible flavor of crispy bacon and a delicious low-carb twist. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a flavorful snack, these poppers are a must-try.

Ingredients

For the Jalapeno Poppers:

  • 12 fresh jalapeno peppers
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slices of bacon, cut in half

For the Dipping Sauce (Optional):

  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Slice each jalapeno pepper in half lengthwise and remove the seeds and membranes. This step will help reduce the heat of the jalapenos, making them more palatable for those who are sensitive to spice.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and season with salt and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  4. Fill each jalapeno half with the cream cheese mixture, using a spoon to press it in firmly.

  5. Wrap each stuffed jalapeno half with a half-slice of bacon, ensuring that the bacon is securely wrapped around the pepper. Place the bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers on the prepared baking sheet, seam side down.

  6. Bake the poppers in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the jalapenos are tender.

  7. While the poppers are baking, you can prepare the optional dipping sauce. Simply combine the sour cream, mayonnaise, chopped cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Mix well and refrigerate until ready to serve.

  8. Once the poppers are done, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes before serving.

These savory low carb bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers are best served warm, alongside the optional dipping sauce. The combination of creamy cheese, spicy jalapeno, and crispy bacon is truly irresistible. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer, these poppers are sure to be a hit at any gathering.

Conclusion

In conclusion, these savory low carb bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers are a delicious and satisfying treat that’s perfect for any occasion. With their bold flavors and low-carb profile, they make for an excellent appetizer or snack that will leave everyone coming back for more. Give this recipe a try and watch as these irresistible poppers become a staple in your entertaining repertoire. Enjoy!
