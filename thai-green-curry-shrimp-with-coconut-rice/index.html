Thai Green Curry Shrimp with Coconut Rice


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering recipe for Thai Green Curry Shrimp with Coconut Rice. This dish is a perfect blend of aromatic herbs, spicy green curry, succulent shrimp, and creamy coconut rice that will transport your taste buds to the streets of Bangkok. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your family and friends with its exotic flavors and vibrant colors.

Ingredients

For the Thai Green Curry Shrimp:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of green curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

For the Coconut Rice:

  • 1 cup of jasmine rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

For Serving:

  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Coconut Rice

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the jasmine rice, coconut milk, water, and salt.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  4. Fluff the rice with a fork and keep it warm until ready to serve.

Step 2: Cook the Thai Green Curry Shrimp

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the green curry paste and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and broth, and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  4. Add the sliced red bell pepper, onion, and minced garlic to the skillet, and cook for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.
  5. Stir in the fish sauce and brown sugar, and let the flavors meld for another 2 minutes.
  6. Gently add the shrimp to the curry sauce and cook for 4-5 minutes, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.
  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the curry with fresh basil leaves.

Step 3: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Divide the coconut rice among serving plates or bowls.
  2. Ladle the fragrant Thai Green Curry Shrimp over the coconut rice.
  3. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve with lime wedges on the side.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a few slices of Thai bird’s eye chilies to the curry.
  • Customize the vegetables in the curry by adding snow peas, bamboo shoots, or baby corn for a delightful twist.

Conclusion

Thai Green Curry Shrimp with Coconut Rice is a delightful dish that brings together the vibrant flavors of Thailand in a single, satisfying meal. The aromatic green curry, tender shrimp, and creamy coconut rice create a harmony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to a special meal, this recipe is a surefire way to impress and indulge in the exotic flavors of Thai cuisine. So, roll up your sleeves, gather the ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey to the heart of Thailand with this sensational dish!
