Creamy Lobster and Shrimp Ravioli: A Decadent Italian Delight


If you’re looking to impress your dinner guests with a luxurious and indulgent Italian dish, look no further than creamy lobster and shrimp ravioli. This decadent recipe combines the delicate flavors of seafood with a rich and creamy sauce, creating a meal that is truly unforgettable. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s dive into the world of homemade ravioli!

Ingredients

For the Ravioli Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 pound lobster meat, cooked and chopped
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Creamy Sauce:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, eggs, olive oil, and salt for the ravioli dough. Mix until the dough comes together, then knead it on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Meanwhile, prepare the filling by combining the cooked lobster meat, shrimp, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, parsley, lemon zest, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Once the dough has rested, roll it out using a pasta machine or a rolling pin until it’s thin enough to be filled. Cut the dough into small squares, about 2 inches in size.

  4. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough square. Fold the square over to form a triangle, then press the edges together to seal the ravioli. Repeat this process until all the dough and filling have been used.

  5. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Carefully drop the ravioli into the boiling water and cook for about 3-4 minutes until they float to the surface. Remove the ravioli from the water using a slotted spoon and set them aside.

  6. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and the sauce thickens slightly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. To serve, place a few ravioli on each plate and drizzle the creamy sauce over them. Garnish with chopped fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor. Serve immediately and enjoy the decadent Italian delight you’ve created!

Conclusion

Creamy lobster and shrimp ravioli is a dish that combines the elegance of Italian cuisine with the richness of seafood. The delicate flavors of the lobster and shrimp filling, paired with the creamy sauce, create a symphony of taste that will leave your taste buds dancing. So, why wait? Gather the ingredients, follow the steps, and treat yourself to this decadent delight. Your dinner guests will be impressed, and you’ll have a new favorite recipe up your sleeve. Buon appetito!
