Hearty and Delicious Beef Stroganoff Soup Recipes


If you’re a fan of hearty and flavorful soups, then you’re in for a treat with these delectable beef stroganoff soup recipes. Packed with tender beef, savory mushrooms, and a rich, creamy broth, these soups are the perfect comfort food for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy meal at home, these recipes are sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let’s dive into the world of beef stroganoff soups.

Classic Beef Stroganoff Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced beef and cook until browned. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and garlic. Cook until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until they are tender and golden brown.
  4. Sprinkle the flour and paprika over the mushrooms, and stir to coat.
  5. Gradually pour in the beef broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  6. Return the cooked beef to the pot and bring the soup to a simmer. Let it cook for 15-20 minutes.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk the sour cream until smooth. Gradually add a ladle of the hot soup to the sour cream, whisking constantly to temper it.
  8. Pour the tempered sour cream into the pot of soup, stirring constantly.
  9. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Creamy Mushroom Beef Stroganoff Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cups sliced button mushrooms
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the cubed beef and cook until browned. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.
  2. Add the diced onion to the pot and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their juices and become golden brown.
  4. Return the cooked beef to the pot and pour in the beef broth.
  5. Bring the soup to a simmer and let it cook for 1-2 hours, until the beef is tender.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and thyme. Let the soup simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes.
  7. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with chopped chives before serving.

Conclusion

There you have it – two mouthwatering beef stroganoff soup recipes that are guaranteed to warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds. Whether you opt for the classic version with sour cream or the creamy mushroom variation, these soups are a delightful twist on the traditional beef stroganoff dish. So, next time you’re craving a comforting and indulgent meal, give these recipes a try and savor the delightful flavors of beef stroganoff in soup form!
