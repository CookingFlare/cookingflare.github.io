Delightful Picnic Dessert Bars for a Sweet Summer Treat


Are you planning a picnic this summer? If so, you’ll definitely want to bring along a sweet treat that’s easy to transport and even more delightful to devour. Look no further than these mouthwatering picnic dessert bars that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Packed with flavor and bursting with summer vibes, these bars are the perfect addition to any outdoor gathering. So grab your picnic blanket, gather your friends and loved ones, and get ready to enjoy a sweet summer treat like no other!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • 1 cup dried fruits (such as cranberries, apricots, or cherries)
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated with butter.

  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom of the lined baking dish, creating an even layer. Use the back of a spoon or your hands to press the mixture firmly.

  4. Pour the sweetened condensed milk evenly over the graham cracker crust.

  5. Sprinkle the chocolate chips, shredded coconut, chopped nuts (if using), dried fruits, and mini marshmallows over the sweetened condensed milk layer.

  6. Drizzle the vanilla extract over the top for an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the middle is set.

  8. Remove from the oven and let the bars cool completely in the baking dish.

  9. Once cooled, lift the bars out of the dish using the parchment paper and transfer to a cutting board. Cut into squares or rectangles of your desired size.

  10. Pack the bars in an airtight container or wrap individually in plastic wrap for easy transport to your picnic.

Serving Suggestions

These delightful picnic dessert bars are perfect on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream. The combination of the sweet, chewy bars and the creamy, cool topping is simply irresistible. You can also sprinkle some powdered sugar on top for an extra touch of sweetness.

Conclusion

Picnics are all about enjoying the great outdoors and indulging in delicious food. These delightful picnic dessert bars are the epitome of a sweet summer treat that will leave everyone wanting more. With their irresistible combination of graham cracker crust, sweetened condensed milk, chocolate chips, coconut, nuts, dried fruits, and marshmallows, these bars are sure to be a hit at your next picnic. So go ahead and whip up a batch of these scrumptious bars, pack them up, and head outside for a picnic adventure filled with sweet memories.
