Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipes for Game Day


When it comes to game day snacks, nothing quite hits the spot like a warm, spicy, and creamy buffalo chicken dip. It’s the perfect blend of flavors that pairs well with chips, veggies, or even as a hearty topping for baked potatoes. With the rise of the Instant Pot, preparing this crowd-pleaser has never been easier or faster. In this post, we’ll explore some tantalizing spicy Instant Pot buffalo chicken dip recipes that are sure to impress your friends and family during the big game.

The Appeal of Buffalo Chicken Dip

Buffalo chicken dip is a beloved classic, known for its spicy kick and creamy texture. The combination of shredded chicken, tangy buffalo sauce, creamy cheese, and a touch of ranch dressing creates a flavor profile that is irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a watch party or heading to a friend’s house, this dip is an essential addition to your game day spread.

Why Use an Instant Pot?

Using an Instant Pot to prepare buffalo chicken dip has several advantages:

  1. Speed: The Instant Pot significantly reduces cooking time. You can have your dip ready in under 30 minutes.
  2. Flavor: The pressure cooking process locks in flavors and keeps the chicken tender and juicy.
  3. Ease of Use: With just a few ingredients and minimal prep time, the Instant Pot makes preparing this dish a breeze.
  4. Versatility: You can adjust the heat level to suit your taste, making it customizable for everyone at your gathering.

Essential Ingredients

Before diving into the recipes, let’s take a look at the essential ingredients you’ll need:

  • Chicken: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs work best.
  • Buffalo Sauce: Choose your favorite brand or make your own for a personal touch.
  • Cream Cheese: This adds creaminess and richness to the dip.
  • Ranch or Blue Cheese Dressing: For that classic flavor, you can use either dressing depending on your preference.
  • Shredded Cheese: Cheddar or a blend of cheeses adds a delicious cheesy layer.
  • Green Onions: For garnish and an added layer of flavor.

Classic Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts in the Instant Pot. Pour the buffalo sauce over the chicken, ensuring they are well coated.

  2. Cook the Chicken: Seal the Instant Pot lid and set it to manual high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural release for 5 minutes, then perform a quick release.

  3. Shred the Chicken: Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.

  4. Add the Cream Cheese: Add the softened cream cheese, ranch dressing, and half of the shredded cheddar cheese to the pot. Stir until well combined.

  5. Heat Through: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook for about 5-10 minutes, stirring frequently until the mixture is heated through and creamy.

  6. Finish with Cheese: Sprinkle the remaining cheddar cheese on top and let it melt for a couple of minutes.

  7. Serve: Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot with tortilla chips, celery sticks, or carrot sticks.

Spicy Jalapeño Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Dip

For those who love an extra kick, this spicy jalapeño version will take your buffalo chicken dip to the next level.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup ranch or blue cheese dressing
  • 1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup pickled jalapeños, chopped (adjust according to heat preference)
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Place the chicken thighs in the Instant Pot and pour the buffalo sauce over them.

  2. Cook the Chicken: Seal the lid and set to manual high pressure for 10 minutes. Allow for a natural release for 5 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.

  3. Shred the Chicken: Remove the chicken and shred it with forks, then return it to the pot.

  4. Add the Cream Cheese and Jalapeños: Stir in the cream cheese, ranch or blue cheese dressing, and chopped jalapeños until everything combines.

  5. Melt the Cheese: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add the shredded pepper jack cheese, stirring until melted and creamy.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Top with green onions before serving. This dip pairs wonderfully with tortilla chips or fresh vegetables.

Vegan Instant Pot Buffalo Cauliflower Dip

Not everyone may be a fan of chicken, and that’s perfectly okay! This vegan buffalo cauliflower dip is a fantastic alternative that everyone can enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 cup cashew cream (soaked cashews blended with water)
  • 1 cup vegan ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Cauliflower: Add the cauliflower florets and buffalo sauce to the Instant Pot. Seal the lid and cook on manual high pressure for 3 minutes. Quick release the pressure.

  2. Blend the Dip: Transfer the cauliflower and sauce to a blender. Add the cashew cream, vegan ranch dressing, and nutritional yeast. Blend until smooth and creamy.

  3. Heat through: Pour the mixture back into the Instant Pot to warm it up on sauté mode for about 5 minutes.

  4. Serve: Garnish with green onions and serve with an assortment of dippables like tortilla chips, celery, and carrots.

Tips for the Perfect Buffalo Chicken Dip

  • Choose Quality Ingredients: The taste of your dip will largely depend on the quality of the chicken, cheese, and sauces you use.
  • Adjust the Spice Level: If you prefer a milder dip, reduce the amount of buffalo sauce or replace it with a milder hot sauce.
  • Make It Ahead: You can prepare the dip in advance. Just reheat it in the Instant Pot on the sauté setting before serving.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Feel free to add extras like diced tomatoes, black beans, or even jalapeños for added flavor and texture.

Serving Suggestions

  • Dippers: Serve your buffalo chicken dip with a variety of dippers, including tortilla chips, sliced baguette, celery sticks, carrot sticks, or even pretzel bites.
  • Make It a Meal: Transform this dip into a meal by serving it over rice or in a baked potato.
  • Pair with Drinks: Buffalo chicken dip pairs well with a variety of beverages. Try it with beer, soda, or even a tangy lemonade to balance the heat.

Conclusion

Spicy Instant Pot buffalo chicken dip is a game day essential that is sure to be a hit with your guests. With its easy preparation and delicious flavor, it’s a recipe you’ll want to make time and time again. Whether you choose the classic version, a spicy jalapeño twist, or a vegan alternative, each dip offers a unique take on this beloved snack. So gather your friends, fire up the Instant Pot, and enjoy the game with this mouthwatering dip that everyone will love!

With the right ingredients and a little bit of creativity, your game day spread will be the talk of the town. Happy cooking!
