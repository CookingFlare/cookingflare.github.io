One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken and Broccoli


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup, then this One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken and Broccoli recipe is just what you need. This dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to put a wholesome and flavorful meal on the table without spending hours in the kitchen. It’s a comforting combination of tender chicken, vibrant broccoli, and a rich, creamy sauce that’s infused with garlic and Parmesan cheese. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups half and half
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.

  2. In a large skillet or pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Once done, transfer the chicken to a plate and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and allow it to simmer for a couple of minutes, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.

  5. Reduce the heat to medium and stir in the half and half, grated Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning. Let the sauce simmer gently, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened, add the broccoli florets to the skillet and stir to combine. Allow the broccoli to simmer in the sauce for about 5 minutes, or until it is tender-crisp.

  7. Return the cooked chicken breasts to the skillet, nestling them into the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce and broccoli. Let everything simmer together for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  8. Garnish the dish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken and Broccoli is a complete meal on its own, but you can also serve it with cooked pasta, rice, or crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce. A simple side salad or steamed vegetables would also complement this dish beautifully.

Final Thoughts

With its creamy, indulgent sauce and wholesome ingredients, this one pot meal is sure to become a family favorite. The combination of garlic, Parmesan, and tender chicken creates a dish that’s both comforting and elegant. Plus, the added broccoli brings a pop of color and freshness to the plate. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a satisfying dinner, this recipe is a must-try. Enjoy the delightful flavors and the convenience of having only one pot to clean up after cooking this scrumptious meal.

So there you have it – a simple and delightful One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken and Broccoli recipe that’s bound to impress. Give it a try and savor the wonderful flavors of this comforting dish!
