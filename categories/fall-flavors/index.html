Roasted Cauliflower with Turmeric and Cumin: A Flavorful Fall Side

There’s something about the crispness in the air during fall that makes us crave warm and comforting dishes. And what better way to embrace the flavors of the season than with a delicious and nutritious side dish? Enter roasted cauliflower with turmeric and cumin – a flavorful combination that will leave your taste buds singing. Ingredients 1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon turmeric 1 teaspoon cumin 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper Fresh cilantro, for garnish (optional) Instructions Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a large bowl, combine the cauliflower florets, olive oil, turmeric, cumin, salt, and black pepper. Toss gently until the cauliflower is evenly coated with the spices and oil. Transfer the cauliflower to the prepared baking sheet, spreading it out in a single layer. This will ensure even roasting and maximize the flavor. Roast the cauliflower in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until it is golden brown and tender. Be sure to give the cauliflower a gentle toss halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning. Once roasted, remove the cauliflower from the oven and let it cool slightly. Garnish with fresh cilantro, if desired, for an added burst of freshness. Serve the roasted cauliflower as a side dish alongside your favorite protein or as a flavorful addition to salads, grain bowls, or wraps. Health Benefits Not only is roasted cauliflower with turmeric and cumin a delicious fall side dish, but it also packs a nutritional punch. Cauliflower is a cruciferous vegetable that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. It is known for its anti-inflammatory properties and is a great source of antioxidants. Turmeric, on the other hand, contains curcumin, a compound with powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Apple Cinnamon Quinoa Porridge: A Nutritious Fall Breakfast

Fall is the perfect season to cozy up with a warm and comforting breakfast that nourishes your body and satisfies your taste buds. If you’re tired of the same old oatmeal, it’s time to try something new and exciting – Apple Cinnamon Quinoa Porridge. Packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, this delightful dish will keep you energized and ready to take on the day. Ingredients 1 cup quinoa 2 cups almond milk (or your preferred plant-based milk) 1 apple, diced 1/4 cup raisins 1/4 cup chopped walnuts 1 tablespoon maple syrup 1 teaspoon cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Pinch of salt Instructions Rinse the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. Drain well. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa, almond milk, diced apple, raisins, chopped walnuts, maple syrup, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the saucepan with a lid. Simmer the mixture for about 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid has been absorbed. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Once the quinoa is tender and the porridge has thickened, remove the saucepan from the heat. Let the porridge sit for a few minutes to cool slightly before serving. The porridge will thicken further as it cools. Serve the Apple Cinnamon Quinoa Porridge warm in bowls. You can top it with additional diced apple, a sprinkle of cinnamon, and a drizzle of maple syrup for extra flavor. Enjoy your nutritious fall breakfast!
