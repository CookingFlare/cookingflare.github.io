Grilling Beyond Meat: Unique Recipe Ideas


If you’re a fan of plant-based alternatives and love the smoky flavors of grilling, then you’re in luck! Beyond Meat, the popular plant-based meat substitute, is not only delicious but also perfect for grilling. In this blog post, we’ll explore some unique recipe ideas that will take your grilling game to the next level. So fire up your grill and get ready for some mouthwatering dishes!

1. Beyond Meat Skewers with Chimichurri Sauce

Skewers are a classic grilling favorite, and they work wonderfully with Beyond Meat. Start by cutting the Beyond Meat into bite-sized pieces and marinating them in a mixture of olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and your favorite herbs and spices. Thread the marinated Beyond Meat pieces onto skewers, alternating with colorful vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and cherry tomatoes. Grill the skewers over medium-high heat for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally until they are charred and cooked through. Serve these delicious skewers with a vibrant chimichurri sauce for a burst of flavor.

2. Grilled Beyond Meat Burger with Avocado Salsa

Who doesn’t love a good burger? Take your burger game to new heights by grilling a Beyond Meat patty and topping it with a refreshing avocado salsa. Start by seasoning your Beyond Meat patty with salt, pepper, and your favorite spices. Grill the patty over medium heat for about 4-5 minutes on each side until it’s nicely charred and cooked through. While the patty is grilling, prepare the avocado salsa by mashing ripe avocados with lime juice, chopped red onions, diced tomatoes, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper. Toast the burger buns on the grill and assemble your burger with the grilled patty and a generous dollop of the avocado salsa. This juicy and flavorful burger will leave you craving for more!

3. Grilled Beyond Meat Tacos with Mango Salsa

Tacos are always a crowd-pleaser, and when you combine them with the smoky goodness of grilled Beyond Meat, you have a winning combination. Start by grilling the Beyond Meat crumbles in a grill basket or on a piece of foil over medium-high heat until they are nicely browned and heated through. While the crumbles are grilling, prepare a vibrant mango salsa by combining diced mangoes, red onions, jalapenos, cilantro, lime juice, and a pinch of salt. Warm up your tortillas on the grill and assemble your tacos with the grilled Beyond Meat crumbles and a generous spoonful of the mango salsa. These tacos are bursting with flavors and are perfect for a summer cookout!

4. Grilled Beyond Meat Kebabs with Peanut Satay Sauce

If you’re a fan of bold and exotic flavors, then these grilled Beyond Meat kebabs with peanut satay sauce are for you. Start by marinating the Beyond Meat strips in a mixture of soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and a touch of sweetness like maple syrup or honey. Thread the marinated Beyond Meat strips onto skewers, alternating with colorful vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers, and red onions. Grill the kebabs over medium-high heat for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally until they are nicely charred and cooked through. While the kebabs are grilling, prepare a creamy peanut satay sauce by combining peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, garlic, and a bit of chili paste. Serve these flavorful kebabs with the peanut satay sauce and watch them disappear in no time!

Conclusion

Grilling Beyond Meat opens up a world of possibilities for delicious, plant-based meals. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based options into your diet, these unique recipe ideas will satisfy your cravings and impress your taste buds. So next time you fire up the grill, give these recipes a try and let the flavors of Beyond Meat shine! Happy grilling!
