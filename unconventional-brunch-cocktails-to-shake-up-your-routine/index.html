Unconventional Brunch Cocktails to Shake Up Your Routine


Who says brunch has to be all about mimosas and Bloody Marys? While those classic brunch cocktails have their place, why not shake up your routine with some unconventional and exciting drink options? Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or just looking to elevate your own weekend morning, these unique brunch cocktails are sure to impress your guests and tantalize your taste buds.

1. Spicy Mango Michelada

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle of light Mexican beer
  • 1 cup of mango juice
  • 2 tablespoons of hot sauce
  • Lime wedges
  • Tajin seasoning for rimming the glass
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Rim a glass with Tajin seasoning.
  2. Fill the glass with ice and add the mango juice, hot sauce, and a squeeze of lime.
  3. Slowly pour the beer into the glass and stir gently to mix the flavors.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge and enjoy!

The Spicy Mango Michelada is a refreshing and unexpected twist on the classic Michelada. The sweetness of the mango juice balances out the heat from the hot sauce, creating a perfect blend of flavors that will wake up your taste buds and leave you craving more.

2. Orange Turmeric Mimosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle of champagne
  • 1 cup of freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • Orange slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pitcher, combine the orange juice and ground turmeric.
  2. Pour the champagne into the pitcher and gently stir to mix.
  3. Pour the Orange Turmeric Mimosa into champagne flutes and garnish with an orange slice.

The Orange Turmeric Mimosa not only adds a vibrant pop of color to your brunch table but also brings a boost of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties thanks to the turmeric. This unconventional mimosa is a healthy and flavorful twist on the traditional recipe.

3. Cucumber Elderflower Spritz

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz gin
  • 1 oz elderflower liqueur
  • 2 cucumber slices
  • 4 oz sparkling water
  • Ice
  • Fresh mint for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the cucumber slices.
  2. Add the gin and elderflower liqueur to the shaker and fill it with ice.
  3. Shake well and strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Top with sparkling water and garnish with fresh mint.

The Cucumber Elderflower Spritz is a light and refreshing cocktail that combines the crispness of cucumber with the delicate floral notes of elderflower. This sophisticated drink is perfect for a brunch on a warm summer day and is guaranteed to impress your guests with its unique and elegant flavor profile.

4. Pineapple Jalapeño Margarita

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 oz pineapple juice
  • 2-3 slices of fresh jalapeño
  • Salt for rimming the glass
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Rim a glass with salt.
  2. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the jalapeño slices with the lime juice.
  3. Add the tequila, triple sec, and pineapple juice to the shaker and fill it with ice.
  4. Shake well and strain the mixture into the prepared glass filled with ice.

The Pineapple Jalapeño Margarita strikes the perfect balance between sweet and spicy, making it an exciting and unconventional addition to your brunch cocktail lineup. The heat from the jalapeño pairs beautifully with the sweetness of the pineapple, creating a margarita that is both refreshing and unexpected.

5. Lavender Lemon Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz lavender syrup
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Soda water
  • Lavender sprig for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine the vodka, lavender syrup, and lemon juice.
  2. Fill the shaker with ice and shake well.
  3. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Top with soda water and garnish with a lavender sprig.

The Lavender Lemon Fizz is a beautifully fragrant and elegant cocktail that brings a touch of sophistication to your brunch table. The floral notes of the lavender syrup pair harmoniously with the zesty lemon, creating a drink that is both visually stunning and delightfully refreshing.

With these unconventional brunch cocktails, you have the opportunity to elevate your brunch experience and impress your guests with unexpected flavor combinations and creative twists on classic recipes. So, why not shake up your routine and add a splash of excitement to your next brunch gathering? Cheers to delicious and unconventional brunch cocktails!
