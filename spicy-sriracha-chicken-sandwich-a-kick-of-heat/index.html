Spicy Sriracha Chicken Sandwich: A Kick of Heat


Are you ready to spice up your sandwich game? Look no further than this Spicy Sriracha Chicken Sandwich recipe! It’s a flavor-packed, mouthwatering delight that will leave your taste buds tingling with excitement. With the perfect balance of heat and flavor, this sandwich is a surefire way to add some sizzle to your mealtime. Let’s dive into the details of this tantalizing recipe.

Ingredients

For the Sriracha Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup Sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Sandwich Assembly:

  • 4 sandwich buns
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Sriracha sauce
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tomato slices
  • Sliced red onion (optional)
  • Sliced pickles (optional)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, mix the buttermilk, Sriracha sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken breasts to the mixture, ensuring they are well coated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or ideally, marinate overnight for maximum flavor.

  2. Prepare the Sriracha Mayo: In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise and Sriracha sauce. Adjust the amount of Sriracha to your preferred level of spiciness. Set aside in the refrigerator until ready to assemble the sandwiches.

  3. Coat and Fry the Chicken: In a shallow dish, season the flour with salt and pepper. Dredge each marinated chicken breast in the seasoned flour, shaking off any excess. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken breasts for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Once done, transfer the chicken to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

  4. Assemble the Sandwiches: To assemble each sandwich, spread a generous amount of the Sriracha mayo on the bottom half of the bun. Place a lettuce leaf on top of the mayo, followed by a crispy Sriracha chicken breast. Add a tomato slice, and if desired, top with sliced red onion and pickles. Complete the sandwich with the top half of the bun.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once assembled, these Spicy Sriracha Chicken Sandwiches are ready to be devoured! Serve them alongside your favorite side dishes or enjoy them on their own for a satisfying meal.

Tips for Customization

  • For an extra kick of heat, consider adding a slice of pepper jack cheese to the sandwich.
  • Experiment with different types of buns, such as brioche or ciabatta, to change up the texture of the sandwich.
  • If you prefer a lighter alternative, you can grill the marinated chicken breasts instead of frying them for a healthier twist.

Conclusion

There you have it – a tantalizing recipe for Spicy Sriracha Chicken Sandwiches that will elevate your sandwich game to a whole new level. Whether you’re a spice enthusiast or simply looking to add some zest to your meals, this recipe is a must-try. The combination of crispy, flavorful chicken and the zing of Sriracha mayo creates a harmony of flavors that will leave you craving more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure that’s packed with a kick of heat!
