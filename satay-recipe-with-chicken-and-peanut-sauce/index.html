Satay Recipe with Chicken and Peanut Sauce


Are you ready to tantalize your taste buds with a burst of flavors? Look no further than this delightful Satay recipe with tender chicken and a rich, creamy peanut sauce. Satay is a popular Southeast Asian dish known for its aromatic spices and succulent meat, often served as an appetizer or main course. Let’s dive into the art of creating this mouthwatering dish that will surely impress your family and friends.

Ingredients:

For the Chicken Satay:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

For the Peanut Sauce:

  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional, for a hint of heat)

Instructions:

Marinating the Chicken:

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, ground turmeric, ground coriander, ground cumin, and vegetable oil. Mix well to form a smooth marinade.
  2. Add the chicken strips to the marinade, ensuring they are evenly coated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to infuse into the meat.

Preparing the Peanut Sauce:

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the peanut butter, soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, grated ginger, minced garlic, and water. Stir continuously until the sauce is smooth and heated through. If desired, add a pinch of crushed red pepper flakes for a subtle kick. Set aside.

Skewering and Grilling the Chicken:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Thread the marinated chicken strips onto the soaked bamboo skewers, ensuring an even distribution of meat on each skewer.
  3. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has beautiful grill marks.

Serving the Satay:

  1. Arrange the grilled chicken satay on a serving platter and accompany with the luscious peanut sauce.
  2. Garnish with chopped cilantro, sliced green onions, and a sprinkle of crushed peanuts for an extra layer of texture and flavor.

Enjoy the Culinary Adventure:

Indulge in the sensational combination of tender, marinated chicken with the velvety smoothness of the peanut sauce. The enticing aroma of the spices and the charred perfection of the grilled satay will transport your senses to the bustling streets of Southeast Asia. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a culinary adventure, this Satay recipe is sure to leave a lasting impression.

So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and embark on a journey of flavors with this exquisite Chicken Satay and Peanut Sauce recipe. Let the fusion of savory, sweet, and aromatic elements elevate your dining experience to new heights. Cheers to creating unforgettable moments around the table with this extraordinary dish!
