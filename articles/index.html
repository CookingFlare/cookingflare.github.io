Flavorful Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Soup Recipes

In this blog post, we will explore some delicious and flavorful Instant Pot chicken noodle soup recipes that are perfect for those cozy evenings or when you need a comforting bowl of soup. The Instant Pot is a versatile and convenient kitchen appliance that allows you to cook meals quickly and with minimal effort. Let’s dive into these mouthwatering recipes! Classic Chicken Noodle Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts 4 carrots, peeled and sliced 3 stalks celery, sliced 1 medium onion, chopped 4 cloves garlic, minced 8 cups chicken broth 2 cups egg noodles 1 teaspoon dried thyme Salt and pepper, to taste Fresh parsley, for garnish Instructions: Place the chicken breasts, carrots, celery, onion, and garlic in the Instant Pot. Pour in the chicken broth and season with dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes, followed by a quick release. Carefully remove the chicken breasts and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and add the egg noodles. Close the lid again and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 3 minutes, followed by a quick release. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley. Thai Coconut Curry Chicken Noodle Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs 1 can coconut milk 4 cups chicken broth 2 tablespoons red curry paste 2 tablespoons fish sauce 2 tablespoons lime juice 2 teaspoons brown sugar 4 ounces rice noodles 1 red bell pepper, sliced 1 cup snap peas Fresh cilantro, for garnish Lime wedges, for serving Instructions: Add the chicken thighs, coconut milk, chicken broth, red curry paste, fish sauce, lime juice, and brown sugar to the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes, followed by a natural release. Remove the chicken thighs from the Instant Pot and shred them. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and add the rice noodles, red bell pepper, and snap peas. Close the lid again and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 3 minutes, followed by a quick release. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and lime wedges. Lemon Chicken Orzo Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs 4 cups chicken broth 1 cup orzo pasta 1 lemon, zest and juice 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes Salt and pepper, to taste Fresh dill, for garnish Instructions: Place the chicken thighs, chicken broth, orzo pasta, lemon zest, lemon juice, dried oregano, dried thyme, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper in the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes, followed by a natural release. Remove the chicken thighs from the Instant Pot and shred them. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and stir well. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh dill.
