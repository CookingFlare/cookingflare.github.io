Delicious Mediterranean Pesto Recipes to Elevate Your Cooking


If you’re a fan of Mediterranean cuisine, you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious Mediterranean pesto recipes that will take your cooking to a whole new level. Pesto, a versatile sauce made with fresh herbs, nuts, cheese, and olive oil, is a staple in Mediterranean cooking. It adds a burst of vibrant flavor to any dish and can be used in a variety of ways. So, let’s dive in and discover some mouthwatering pesto recipes!

Classic Basil Pesto

Let’s start with the classic basil pesto, a timeless favorite. To make this delicious sauce, you’ll need:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while the food processor is running until the mixture is well combined and forms a smooth paste.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Enjoy this classic basil pesto tossed with hot pasta, spread on a crusty baguette, or as a dip for fresh vegetables. The possibilities are endless!

Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto

For a twist on the classic, try making sun-dried tomato pesto. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes (in oil), drained
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup toasted almonds
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the sun-dried tomatoes, basil leaves, toasted almonds, garlic, and Parmesan cheese. Pulse until well combined.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while the food processor is running until the mixture forms a smooth paste.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

This sun-dried tomato pesto is perfect for spreading on sandwiches, mixing into cooked grains, or using as a topping for grilled chicken or fish.

Spinach and Walnut Pesto

If you’re looking for a nutritious twist, try making spinach and walnut pesto. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/2 cup walnuts
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the spinach leaves, walnuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese. Pulse until well combined.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while the food processor is running until the mixture forms a smooth paste.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

This vibrant green pesto is perfect for tossing with pasta, spreading on grilled vegetables, or using as a dip for crunchy breadsticks.

Roasted Red Pepper Pesto

For a burst of smoky flavor, try making roasted red pepper pesto. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 roasted red peppers, peeled and seeded
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup toasted almonds
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the roasted red peppers, basil leaves, toasted almonds, garlic, and Parmesan cheese. Pulse until well combined.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while the food processor is running until the mixture forms a smooth paste.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

This roasted red pepper pesto is perfect for spreading on grilled bread, tossing with roasted vegetables, or using as a sauce for homemade pizza.

Wrapping Up

There you have it – four delicious Mediterranean pesto recipes to elevate your cooking! Whether you stick with the classic basil pesto or venture into new flavors like sun-dried tomato, spinach and walnut, or roasted red pepper, these pesto recipes will add a burst of Mediterranean goodness to your dishes. So, grab your food processor and get ready to take your culinary skills to new heights. Bon appétit!
