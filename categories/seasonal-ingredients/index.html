Peach and Prosciutto Flatbread: A Sweet and Savory Summer Meal

Welcome to our culinary corner, where we bring you delightful recipes to tantalize your taste buds. Today, we’re diving into the world of sweet and savory flavors with a delectable Peach and Prosciutto Flatbread. This dish is perfect for those warm summer days when you crave something light, yet bursting with flavor. Let’s get started on this mouthwatering journey! Ingredients For the Flatbread: 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon salt 1 cup warm water 2 tablespoons olive oil For the Toppings: 2 ripe peaches, thinly sliced 6 slices of prosciutto 1 cup fresh arugula 1 cup crumbled feta cheese Balsamic glaze for drizzling Olive oil for brushing Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Flatbread Dough In a large bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Gradually add the warm water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rest for 30 minutes. Step 2: Roll Out the Dough Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). After the dough has rested, divide it into two equal parts. Roll out each portion of the dough on a lightly floured surface to form two flatbreads of your desired shape and thickness. Step 3: Assemble the Flatbreads Place the flatbreads on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Lightly brush the surface of each flatbread with olive oil. Arrange the thinly sliced peaches and prosciutto on top of the flatbreads. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the peaches and prosciutto. Drizzle a little olive oil over the toppings. Step 4: Bake to Perfection Place the assembled flatbreads in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the toppings are caramelized. Once baked, remove the flatbreads from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Step 5: Add the Finishing Touches Scatter the fresh arugula over the warm flatbreads. Finish by drizzling a generous amount of balsamic glaze over the top. Serving Suggestion Slice the Peach and Prosciutto Flatbread into wedges and serve immediately. The combination of sweet, juicy peaches, salty prosciutto, tangy feta, and peppery arugula will create a flavor explosion in every bite. This flatbread pairs beautifully with a crisp, chilled glass of white wine or a refreshing summer cocktail. ...
