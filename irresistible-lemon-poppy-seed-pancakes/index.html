Irresistible Lemon Poppy Seed Pancakes


Pancakes are a classic breakfast staple that never fails to bring comfort and satisfaction. And what better way to elevate this beloved dish than with the refreshing zing of lemon and the delightful crunch of poppy seeds? Today, I’ll share with you my recipe for Irresistible Lemon Poppy Seed Pancakes that will surely brighten up your mornings and leave your taste buds begging for more!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  2. In another bowl, combine the buttermilk, milk, eggs, melted butter, lemon zest, lemon juice, and poppy seeds. Whisk until well blended.

  3. Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour the wet mixture into it. Gently stir until just combined, being careful not to overmix. A few lumps in the batter are perfectly fine.

  4. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with cooking spray or a small amount of butter.

  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.

  6. Transfer the cooked pancakes to a warm plate and cover with a clean kitchen towel to keep them warm while you cook the remaining batter.

  7. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings. Fresh berries, a drizzle of maple syrup, or a dollop of whipped cream are all excellent choices!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra lemony twist, you can prepare a simple lemon glaze by whisking together powdered sugar and lemon juice. Drizzle the glaze over the pancakes just before serving for an added burst of flavor.

  • If you prefer a lighter texture, you can substitute half of the all-purpose flour with cake flour.

  • To make these pancakes even more indulgent, add a handful of white chocolate chips to the batter. The combination of lemon, poppy seeds, and white chocolate is simply divine!

  • If you don’t have buttermilk on hand, you can easily make a substitute by adding 1 tablespoon of lemon juice or vinegar to 1 cup of milk. Let it sit for 5 minutes before using.

  • Leftover pancakes can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. To reheat, simply pop them in the toaster or warm them in a skillet over low heat.

So there you have it, a recipe for Irresistible Lemon Poppy Seed Pancakes that will undoubtedly become a new favorite in your breakfast repertoire. The tangy lemon flavor and the subtle crunch of the poppy seeds add a delightful twist to the classic pancake experience. Whether you enjoy them with a cup of coffee or as a treat for a lazy weekend brunch, these pancakes are sure to bring a smile to your face. Give them a try and let me know how you like them!
