The Best Spanish Cooking Magazines to Subscribe To


Spanish cuisine is celebrated worldwide for its unique blend of rich flavors, traditional techniques, and regional diversity. It offers a culinary spectrum, from comforting classics like patatas bravas and paella to more daring innovations by chefs leading the modern Spanish food revolution. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or simply someone who loves discovering delectable recipes from Spain, subscribing to the right cooking magazines can open up a treasure trove of artistry, inspiration, and authentic knowledge.

This curated list dives into the best Spanish cooking magazines available today. These magazines not only showcase recipes but also tell the stories behind the dishes and the culture that shapes Spanish gastronomy. Whether you’re looking to expand your repertoire, learn about the latest culinary trends in Spain, or simply immerse yourself in food culture from the Iberian Peninsula, these subscriptions will be worth every page.

1. España Gastronómica

One of the leading names in Spanish food publishing, España Gastronómica covers the vibrant Spanish food scene from every angle. This magazine is perfect for anyone keen to learn not only recipes but also the history, traditions, and innovations behind them. It features articles about renowned chefs, restaurant reviews, and insights into local ingredients distinctive to Spain’s various regions.

The magazine is well-known for its recipes that are easy to follow yet deeply authentic. Whether you’re tackling a simple tortilla española or exploring intricate dishes like suquet de peix (Catalan fish stew), these pages are guaranteed to be your guide. Additionally, the magazine often dives into wine pairings, an essential part of Spanish meals and celebrations, helping readers understand the perfect Rioja or Albariño to complement their dishes.

2. El Arte de Cocinar

For fans of gourmet cooking, El Arte de Cocinar is an excellent choice. This magazine is geared toward those who enjoy high-end cuisine and the artistry that goes into creating it. It includes elaborate recipes often inspired by avant-garde Spanish chefs such as Ferran Adrià and Joan Roca while still celebrating the simplicity of classic dishes.

Beyond recipes, this magazine delves into food techniques and plating aesthetics that elevate cooking from mere sustenance to a form of art. With contributions from some of Spain’s most celebrated culinary figures, readers gain exclusive insights into Michelin-star kitchens and rising culinary trends across the country.

Its seasonal cooking section is particularly popular, highlighting ingredients available during each time of year and showcasing how these can be integrated into stunning dishes. For anyone looking to explore culinary creativity at its finest, this is a must-have subscription.

3. Saber Cocinar

Saber Cocinar translates to “Know How to Cook,” and true to its name, this magazine is an accessible guide for anyone looking to master Spanish cooking, regardless of their skill level. It serves as an excellent resource for home cooks who want to build their confidence in the kitchen while learning straightforward recipes that hit the mark on flavor.

This magazine stands out for its step-by-step tutorials, taking readers through essential Spanish dishes such as gazpacho, croquetas, and chorizo-stuffed peppers. The approachable style makes intimidating recipes easy to understand, encouraging even beginners to try their hand at iconic Spanish dishes.

In addition to recipes, readers appreciate the magazine’s tips for kitchen efficiency, including advice on pantry staples, ingredient substitutions, and tricks for making complex dishes simpler without compromising on authenticity. For fans of Spanish home cooking, this subscription is indispensable.

4. Cocina XXI

Cocina XXI (Kitchen 21st Century) is a magazine for the modern food lover. Focused on the new wave of Spanish cuisine, this publication is your go-to source for innovative recipes, emerging trends, and unconventional techniques rooted in Spain’s rich food heritage.

The magazine frequently reviews the most cutting-edge restaurants in Spain, offering readers travel inspiration while spotlighting the creative minds rewriting culinary rules. From molecular gastronomy to fusion recipes blending traditional Spanish flavors with global influences, Cocina XXI is a playground for curious cooks and adventurous eaters.

It also dedicates a section to sustainable cooking practices, including how to shop locally, reduce food waste, and source eco-friendly ingredients while cooking the dishes that define modern Spanish gastronomy. Perfect for environmentally-conscious foodies, this magazine strikes the balance between indulgence and responsibility.

5. Revista Tapas

For fans of tapas culture, Revista Tapas is an absolute gem. This magazine celebrates Spain’s iconic tradition of small plates, offering an array of recipes designed for sharing with family and friends. With a focus on simple preparations and exceptional flavors, it perfectly captures the spirit of Spanish dining.

Each issue is brimming with tapas recipes, from classics like gambas al ajillo (garlic shrimp) to creative takes on pintxos and other bite-sized gastronomic delights. The magazine also highlights wine, sherry, and cocktail pairings that enhance the tapas experience, creating memorable food moments for gatherings and celebrations.

What sets Revista Tapas apart is its deep dive into the cultural significance of tapas. It often features profiles of the best tapas bars across Spain, offering a virtual tour of the establishments defining this beloved culinary experience.

6. Cocina de Hoy

Cocina de Hoy (Today’s Cuisine) is a magazine celebrating current trends while keeping one foot firmly grounded in tradition. A favorite of busy home cooks, it emphasizes approachable recipes that require minimal prep without sacrificing authenticity in taste.

Readers appreciate its focus on seasonal ingredients and quick recipes to help prepare meals after a busy workday or in preparation for gatherings. With sections devoted to family-friendly meals, quick tapas ideas, and simple desserts, its content is highly practical but still deeply enjoyable.

Every issue of Cocina de Hoy includes cooking tips, budget-friendly advice, and ideas for meal planning. It’s an excellent subscription for anyone looking to dive into Spanish cooking amid a busy lifestyle while embracing its various techniques and flavors.

Why Subscribe to a Spanish Cooking Magazine?

Spanish cooking magazines are more than recipe collections—they’re cultural touchstones. Through these publications, you gain unique insight into Spain’s rich gastronomic heritage. From celebrated traditions to cutting-edge innovations, these magazines provide endless inspiration for anyone passionate about Spanish cuisine.

Subscribing to one (or several) of these culinary magazines opens the door to an immersive learning experience. You will not only cook amazing dishes but also discover new ingredients, appreciate regional differences, and fall in love with the culture tied to each recipe.

Whether you’re preparing bocadillos for a casual lunch, experimenting with paella for a festive occasion, or sharpening your culinary skills with avant-garde creations, these magazines empower you to embrace Spain’s world-class culinary landscape in the comfort of your kitchen.

Dive into flavors that range from the fiery heat of Andalusian spices to the umami depths of Basque seafood. Explore the art of harmonizing food and wine that the Spaniards have perfected over centuries. Transport yourself to sun-soaked plazas, bustling markets, and quiet countryside farms, all through the pages of these vibrant publications.

A subscription to the right Spanish cooking magazine is more than an investment in recipes—it’s a commitment to exploring a way of life where food takes center stage. Prepare for a culinary journey that will inspire and delight with every whisk, taste, and bite.
