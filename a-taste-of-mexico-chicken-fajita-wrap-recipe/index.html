A Taste of Mexico: Chicken Fajita Wrap Recipe


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this delicious and easy-to-make Chicken Fajita Wrap recipe. Packed with vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, this dish is perfect for a quick lunch, a casual dinner, or even a fun party spread. Let’s dive into the tantalizing world of Mexican flavors and create a mouthwatering dish that will surely impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Fajita Vegetables:

  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Avocado Cream:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt to taste

Additional Ingredients:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Grated cheddar cheese
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

  1. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the marinade, making sure they are evenly coated.
  3. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

Step 2: Prepare the Fajita Vegetables

  1. In a separate bowl, combine the sliced bell peppers, onion, olive oil, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss the vegetables until they are well coated with the seasoning.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken and Vegetables

  1. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken breasts for 6-7 minutes on each side until they are fully cooked and have beautiful grill marks. Remove from the pan and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  3. In the same pan, add the seasoned fajita vegetables and cook for 5-6 minutes until they are tender and slightly charred. Remove from the pan and set aside.

Step 4: Make the Avocado Cream

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the ripe avocado, sour cream, lime juice, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.

Step 5: Assemble the Chicken Fajita Wraps

  1. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry pan or microwave for a few seconds.
  2. Spread a generous spoonful of the avocado cream on each tortilla.
  3. Arrange the sliced chicken and fajita vegetables on top of the avocado cream.
  4. Sprinkle with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and grated cheddar cheese.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side.

Now, sit back and enjoy the burst of flavors as you savor every bite of these delightful Chicken Fajita Wraps. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or simply craving a taste of Mexico, this recipe is a surefire way to transport your taste buds to the sunny streets of Mexico City. Buena provecho!
