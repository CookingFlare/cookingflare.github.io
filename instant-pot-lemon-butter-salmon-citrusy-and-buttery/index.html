Instant Pot Lemon Butter Salmon: Citrusy and Buttery


If you’re a seafood lover, you’re in for a treat with this Instant Pot Lemon Butter Salmon recipe. Bursting with a citrusy and buttery flavor, this dish will surely become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make in your Instant Pot, saving you time and effort in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the salmon fillets with salt and pepper on both sides. This will enhance the flavor of the fish and ensure a well-seasoned final dish.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and melt the butter. Once the butter is melted, add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. The aroma of garlic will fill your kitchen, creating anticipation for the delicious meal ahead.

  3. Carefully place the seasoned salmon fillets into the Instant Pot, skin side down. Arrange the lemon slices on top of the fillets, creating a beautiful citrus layer that will infuse the salmon with a refreshing tang.

  4. Sprinkle the dried dill evenly over the salmon and cover the Instant Pot with its lid. Make sure the venting knob is set to sealing to create the necessary pressure for cooking.

  5. Set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 4 minutes. The salmon will cook quickly and retain its tenderness, ensuring a moist and flavorful result.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes before carefully releasing the remaining pressure. This will ensure that the flavors have fully developed and the salmon is cooked to perfection.

  7. Carefully remove the salmon fillets from the Instant Pot and transfer them to a serving dish. The salmon will be delicate, so handle it with care to prevent it from falling apart.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot Lemon Butter Salmon hot, garnished with fresh dill or parsley if desired. The combination of the citrusy lemon and the rich buttery flavor will tantalize your taste buds, creating a truly unforgettable dining experience.

Tips and Variations

  • For a burst of freshness, squeeze some lemon juice over the salmon just before serving. This will enhance the citrus flavor and add an extra zing to the dish.

  • If you prefer a stronger garlic flavor, feel free to increase the amount of minced garlic in the recipe. Adjust the quantity to suit your personal taste preferences.

  • To make this dish even more nutritious, serve it with a side of steamed vegetables or a crisp green salad. The vibrant colors and flavors will complement the salmon beautifully.

  • If you don’t have an Instant Pot, you can still make this recipe using a traditional stovetop method. Simply follow the same steps, but use a skillet instead of the Instant Pot. Cook the salmon over medium heat, covered, for about 5-6 minutes per side or until it flakes easily with a fork.

With its vibrant citrusy notes and indulgent buttery taste, Instant Pot Lemon Butter Salmon is a dish that will impress both your family and guests. The convenience of the Instant Pot makes it a breeze to prepare, while the mouthwatering flavors will make it a regular on your dining table. Give this recipe a try and prepare to be amazed by the delightful combination of flavors in every bite. Enjoy!
