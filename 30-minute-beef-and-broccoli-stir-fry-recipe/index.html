30-Minute Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick, delicious, and healthy meal that you can whip up in no time, then this 30-Minute Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is the perfect recipe for you. Packed with flavor, tender beef, and crisp broccoli, this dish is a crowd-pleaser and a great option for a busy weeknight dinner. Plus, it’s a fantastic way to get your protein and veggies in one dish, making it a well-rounded meal for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by thinly slicing the flank steak against the grain to ensure tenderness. Cut the broccoli into florets and mince the garlic. Grate the fresh ginger and set all the ingredients aside for easy access during the cooking process.

  2. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and red pepper flakes. This will be the flavorful sauce that brings the entire dish together.

  3. Cook the Beef: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced beef and cook for 2-3 minutes until browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Cook the Broccoli: In the same skillet, add the garlic and ginger, and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the broccoli florets and cook for 3-4 minutes until they are bright green and tender-crisp.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the broccoli. Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and broccoli, stirring to coat everything evenly. Let the mixture simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the beef and broccoli.

  6. Serve: Once the beef and broccoli are coated in the flavorful sauce and heated through, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice and garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For added crunch, toss in some sliced water chestnuts or cashews during the last minute of cooking.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, increase the amount of cornstarch in the sauce mixture.
  • Feel free to customize the protein by using chicken, shrimp, or tofu instead of beef.
  • To make the dish spicy, increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a drizzle of sriracha.

Enjoy this 30-Minute Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry as a satisfying and flavorful meal that’s quick and easy to prepare. It’s a versatile recipe that you can adapt based on your preferences, making it a go-to dish for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or entertaining guests, this stir-fry is sure to impress with its vibrant flavors and tender beef.
