Delicious and Healthy Stir Fry Recipes with Cauliflower Rice


Stir fry recipes are a great way to incorporate healthy ingredients into your meals while still enjoying delicious flavors and textures. If you’re looking for a low-carb alternative to traditional rice, cauliflower rice is the perfect substitute. Packed with nutrients and easy to prepare, cauliflower rice adds a unique twist to your stir fry dishes. In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering stir fry recipes that feature cauliflower rice as the star ingredient.

1. Teriyaki Chicken Stir Fry with Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and minced ginger. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and broccoli florets. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the cauliflower rice to the other side. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cauliflower rice is heated through.
  5. Pour the teriyaki sauce over the vegetables and cauliflower rice. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and toss everything together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve the stir fry hot, garnished with chopped green onions.

2. Shrimp and Vegetable Stir Fry with Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas, trimmed
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers and snap peas. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the cauliflower rice to the other side. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cauliflower rice is heated through.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and cauliflower rice. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  6. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss everything together.
  7. Serve the stir fry hot, garnished with chopped cilantro.

3. Tofu and Mushroom Stir Fry with Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms (shiitake or cremini)
  • 1 cup sliced bok choy
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • Chopped scallions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, toss the cubed tofu with cornstarch until evenly coated.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu and cook until crispy and golden brown, about 4-5 minutes per side. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and ginger. Sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced mushrooms and bok choy to the skillet. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the cauliflower rice to the other side. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cauliflower rice is heated through.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and rice vinegar. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and cauliflower rice. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  7. Return the cooked tofu to the skillet and toss everything together.
  8. Serve the stir fry hot, garnished with chopped scallions.

Enjoy these delicious and healthy stir fry recipes with cauliflower rice as a nutritious and satisfying alternative to traditional rice. Experiment with different vegetables and protein options to create your own unique stir fry creations. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a flavorful meal, these recipes are sure to impress.
