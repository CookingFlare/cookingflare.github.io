Savory Nut-Free Meatball Recipe

3 min · 583 words

Meatballs are a classic comfort food that can be enjoyed in various ways. Whether you pair them with pasta, serve them as appetizers, or enjoy them as a main course, meatballs are always a crowd-pleaser. However, for those with nut allergies, finding a nut-free meatball recipe can be a challenge. But worry not! I have a delicious and savory nut-free meatball recipe that will satisfy your cravings without compromising on flavor. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, chopped fresh parsley, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the milk and beaten egg. Pour the milk and egg mixture into the meat mixture and mix until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined. The milk and egg act as binders, helping to hold the meatballs together.

  3. Once the mixture is well-mixed, use your hands to shape it into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, ensuring they are evenly spaced.

  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). While the oven is preheating, let the meatballs rest for about 10 minutes. This allows the flavors to meld together and the meatballs to set slightly.

  5. Once the oven is preheated, bake the meatballs for 20-25 minutes or until they are cooked through and golden brown on the outside. To ensure even cooking, you can rotate the baking sheet halfway through the cooking time.

  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. You can serve these nut-free meatballs with your favorite sauce, whether it’s marinara, barbecue, or a tangy sweet and sour sauce.

  7. Enjoy your savory nut-free meatballs as an appetizer, with pasta, or as a main course with a side of mashed potatoes and vegetables. These meatballs are versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways, making them a perfect addition to your meal rotation.

Conclusion

These savory nut-free meatballs are a delicious and allergy-friendly option for meatball lovers. Packed with flavor from the herbs and spices, these meatballs will leave you satisfied and craving for more. Plus, with the absence of nuts, you can enjoy them without worrying about any allergies. Give this recipe a try, and I’m sure it will become a family favorite in no time!

So there you have it, a simple and tasty recipe for nut-free meatballs. Enjoy the cooking process, savor the flavors, and make your mealtime a memorable one!
