Delicious and Festive: Christmas Sugar Cookies Recipe


‘Tis the season to be jolly, and what better way to spread holiday cheer than with some delicious Christmas sugar cookies? This classic recipe is a festive favorite, perfect for gifting, decorating, and of course, enjoying with loved ones. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Step 1: Cream the Butter and Sugar

In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This step is crucial for achieving the perfect texture in your sugar cookies.

Step 2: Add Flavor Enhancers

Once the butter and sugar are well combined, add the vanilla extract and almond extract. These flavor enhancers add a delightful aroma and taste to the cookies, making them truly irresistible.

Step 3: Incorporate the Egg

Beat in the egg until fully incorporated. The egg not only acts as a binding agent but also contributes to the rich, buttery flavor of the cookies.

Step 4: Sift the Dry Ingredients

In a separate bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Sifting the dry ingredients ensures that the cookies have a light and airy texture.

Step 5: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until a soft dough forms. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

Step 6: Chill the Dough

Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and chill it in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour. Chilling the dough allows the flavors to meld and makes it easier to roll out.

Step 7: Roll and Cut

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to about 1/4 inch thickness. Use festive cookie cutters to cut out shapes and place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

Step 8: Bake to Perfection

Bake the cookies for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. Keep a close eye on them as they can quickly go from perfectly golden to overdone.

Step 9: Decorate and Enjoy

Once the cookies are cooled, it’s time for the fun part – decorating! Use royal icing, sprinkles, and edible glitter to adorn the cookies with festive designs. Let your creativity shine and make each cookie a work of art.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a twist, add a pinch of cinnamon or nutmeg to the dough for a warm, spicy flavor.
  • To make the cookies extra festive, use red and green food coloring in the icing.
  • Store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, or freeze them for longer shelf life.

These Christmas sugar cookies are not just a treat for the taste buds but also a delightful activity for the whole family. Whether you’re hosting a cookie decorating party or simply enjoying a cozy night in, these cookies are sure to bring joy and warmth to your holiday season. Happy baking and Merry Christmas!
