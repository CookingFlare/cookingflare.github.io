Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust and a Sprinkle of Peanut Butter Chips Recipe: A No-Bake Twist on the Classic Sandwich


If you’re a fan of the classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich, then you’re in for a treat with this no-bake twist on the timeless favorite. These Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust and a Sprinkle of Peanut Butter Chips are a delightful combination of sweet, nutty, and fruity flavors, all wrapped up in a convenient and easy-to-make bar.

Ingredients:

For the Crust:

  • 2 cups vegan graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup

For the Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup your favorite vegan jelly or jam

For the Topping:

  • 1/2 cup vegan peanut butter chips

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup.
  2. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a parchment-lined 9x9 inch baking dish. Place the dish in the refrigerator to chill while preparing the filling.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the creamy peanut butter, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.
  2. Pour the peanut butter filling over the chilled crust and spread it evenly with a spatula.
  3. Gently warm the vegan jelly or jam in a small saucepan over low heat until it becomes easier to drizzle. Drizzle the jelly or jam over the peanut butter filling in a decorative pattern.

Step 3: Add the Topping

  1. Sprinkle the vegan peanut butter chips over the top of the bars, gently pressing them into the filling.
  2. Place the bars in the refrigerator and chill for at least 2 hours, or until set.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once the bars are firm, remove them from the baking dish using the parchment paper overhang.
  2. Cut the bars into squares or rectangles and serve. Store any leftovers in the refrigerator for up to one week.

Why You’ll Love These Bars

These Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars are a delightful no-bake treat that captures the essence of the beloved peanut butter and jelly sandwich in a convenient bar form. The creamy peanut butter filling pairs perfectly with the fruity sweetness of the jelly, all nestled on a buttery graham cracker crust. The sprinkle of peanut butter chips adds a delightful crunch and extra burst of peanut butter flavor. Plus, being vegan, these bars are suitable for a wide range of dietary preferences.

Whether you’re looking for a simple dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth or a crowd-pleasing treat for a gathering, these bars are sure to impress. They’re easy to make, require minimal time in the kitchen, and offer a nostalgic twist on a classic flavor combination.

So, give this recipe a try and indulge in the irresistible blend of peanut butter, jelly, and a hint of graham cracker goodness. These Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust and a Sprinkle of Peanut Butter Chips are a delightful no-bake creation that’s bound to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.
