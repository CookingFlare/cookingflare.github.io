Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice with Beef


Are you craving a flavorful and satisfying meal that’s easy to prepare? Look no further than this Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice with Beef recipe! Packed with bold spices, tender beef, and zesty flavors, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner or simply want to treat yourself to a delicious homemade meal, this recipe is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 cup of long-grain white rice
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or frying pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned and crumbled. Drain any excess fat and set the beef aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté the vegetables until they are soft and fragrant.

  3. Stir in the rice, diced tomatoes (with their juices), beef broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a boil.

  4. Once the mixture is boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the skillet. Allow the rice to simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until it is tender and has absorbed the liquid.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the skillet and stir it into the rice mixture. Let it simmer for an additional 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes to allow the rice to steam and become fluffy.

  7. Before serving, fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a diced jalapeño or a pinch of red pepper flakes to the rice mixture.
  • If you prefer a milder version, you can substitute the diced tomatoes with mild green chilies.
  • This dish pairs wonderfully with a side of creamy guacamole or a refreshing tomato and avocado salad.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, such as corn, peas, or carrots.

Final Thoughts

This Spicy and Tangy Mexican Rice with Beef is a delightful combination of savory, spicy, and tangy flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. The tender beef, aromatic spices, and vibrant colors make this dish as visually appealing as it is delicious. Whether you’re an experienced home cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is simple to follow and yields impressive results.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and hearty meal, give this recipe a try. It’s a one-pan wonder that will bring the vibrant flavors of Mexico right to your table. Enjoy!
