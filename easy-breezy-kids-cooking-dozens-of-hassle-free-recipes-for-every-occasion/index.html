Tips for Easy Breezy Kids’ Cooking

  • Never exclude children from adult cooking prep – let them explore and learn
  • Select ingredients and experiment with flavors that cater to their palate
  • Encourage the use of kitchen tools, such as measuring cups, cookie cutters, and playful gadgets
  • Establish safety rules and let them know why each guideline is essential in simple terms
  • Be patient but firm, guide them when needed, and most importantly, have fun!
