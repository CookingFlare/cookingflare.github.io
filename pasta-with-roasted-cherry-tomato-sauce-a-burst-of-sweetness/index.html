Pasta with Roasted Cherry Tomato Sauce: A Burst of Sweetness


Are you looking for a simple yet delicious pasta recipe that will leave your taste buds craving for more? Look no further! This Pasta with Roasted Cherry Tomato Sauce is the perfect dish to satisfy your pasta cravings and tantalize your palate with a burst of sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta of your choice (penne, spaghetti, or linguine)
  • 2 pints cherry tomatoes
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large baking dish, toss the cherry tomatoes with minced garlic, olive oil, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Ensure that all the tomatoes are evenly coated with the seasonings.
  3. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and roast the tomatoes for about 20-25 minutes, or until they start to burst and release their juices. This roasting process will intensify the natural sweetness of the tomatoes, creating a flavorful sauce.
  4. While the tomatoes are roasting, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Remember to generously salt the boiling water to enhance the flavor of the pasta.
  5. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and set it aside.
  6. Remove the roasted tomatoes from the oven and gently mash them with a fork to release their juices and create a chunky sauce. The burst tomatoes should have a thick, saucy consistency.
  7. In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the roasted tomato sauce to the skillet and stir it well to combine.
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it gently to coat the pasta evenly with the roasted tomato sauce.
  9. Cook the pasta and sauce together for about 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  10. Serve the Pasta with Roasted Cherry Tomato Sauce hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add some red pepper flakes to the roasted tomato sauce during the cooking process.
  • If you prefer a smoother sauce, you can blend the roasted tomatoes in a food processor or blender before adding them to the skillet.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of pasta to suit your preferences. Fusilli, rigatoni, or farfalle would all work wonderfully with the roasted cherry tomato sauce.
  • To make this dish more substantial, you can add some grilled chicken or sautéed shrimp to the pasta before tossing it with the sauce.
  • This pasta also pairs well with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese or a drizzle of balsamic glaze for an extra layer of flavor.

So there you have it - a simple yet incredibly flavorful Pasta with Roasted Cherry Tomato Sauce recipe that will take your pasta nights to a whole new level. The burst of sweetness from the roasted tomatoes combined with the aromatic garlic and herbs will leave you craving for more. Give this recipe a try and let your taste buds dance with joy!
