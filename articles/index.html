Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Dairy-Free Apple Crisp

Are you craving a delicious dessert that is both sweet and comforting? Look no further than this dairy-free apple crisp recipe! Made with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavors, this dessert is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth without compromising your dietary preferences. Ingredients: 4 large apples, peeled and sliced 1 cup rolled oats 1/2 cup almond flour 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted 1/4 cup maple syrup 1/4 cup chopped walnuts 2 teaspoons cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg Pinch of salt Instructions: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a baking dish. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, cinnamon, nutmeg, and a pinch of salt. Toss until the apples are evenly coated with the spices. Transfer the spiced apples to the greased baking dish, spreading them out evenly. In the same bowl, combine the rolled oats, almond flour, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, chopped walnuts, and a pinch of salt. Mix until the ingredients are well combined and form a crumbly texture. Sprinkle the oat and almond mixture evenly over the apples in the baking dish, covering them completely. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown. Remove the apple crisp from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve warm and enjoy on its own or with a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream for an extra treat! Tips and Variations: Feel free to experiment with different types of apples for varying flavors and textures. Granny Smith apples are tart and hold their shape well, while Honeycrisp or Pink Lady apples provide a sweeter taste. If you prefer a crunchier topping, you can add more chopped walnuts or sprinkle some granola over the apple mixture before adding the crumb topping. For a touch of indulgence, drizzle some dairy-free caramel sauce over the warm apple crisp before serving. Leftovers can be refrigerated for up to 3 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before enjoying. Health Benefits: This dairy-free apple crisp is not only a delicious dessert but also a healthier alternative to traditional recipes. By substituting dairy products with almond flour and coconut oil, you can enjoy a lighter option without sacrificing taste. Apples are packed with fiber and essential nutrients, making this dessert a guilt-free treat. The combination of oats, nuts, and spices adds a delightful crunch and enhances the overall flavor profile. ...
