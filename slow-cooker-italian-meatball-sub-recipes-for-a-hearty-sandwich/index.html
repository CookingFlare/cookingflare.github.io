Slow Cooker Italian Meatball Sub Recipes for a Hearty Sandwich


If you’re looking for a delicious and hearty sandwich that will satisfy your cravings, look no further than the Slow Cooker Italian Meatball Sub. This classic Italian-American dish is a comfort food favorite, featuring tender, flavorful meatballs smothered in rich marinara sauce and topped with melted cheese, all nestled in a warm, crusty sub roll. The best part? You can easily make it in your slow cooker, allowing the flavors to meld together while you go about your day. Let’s dive into the art of crafting the perfect Slow Cooker Italian Meatball Sub.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 24 ounces marinara sauce
  • 4-6 sub rolls
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Meatballs

In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, and egg. Use your hands to mix the ingredients until well combined. Roll the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.

Step 2: Brown the Meatballs

Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and lightly coat it with oil. Once the skillet is hot, add the meatballs and brown on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. This step adds depth of flavor to the meatballs and helps them hold their shape during the slow cooking process.

Step 3: Slow Cook the Meatballs

Pour half of the marinara sauce into the bottom of the slow cooker. Add the browned meatballs on top of the sauce. Pour the remaining marinara sauce over the meatballs, ensuring they are fully covered. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours, allowing the meatballs to become tender and soak up the flavors of the sauce.

Step 4: Assemble the Subs

When the meatballs are ready, preheat your oven to 375°F. Slice the sub rolls lengthwise, leaving one edge attached. Place the rolls on a baking sheet and fill each with the cooked meatballs and sauce. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of each sub.

Step 5: Bake the Subs

Bake the assembled meatball subs in the oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the rolls are warmed through.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Once out of the oven, garnish the meatball subs with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the subs hot and enjoy the melty, saucy goodness of these Slow Cooker Italian Meatball Subs.

Tips for Success:

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes to the meatball mixture.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can use meatless meatballs and still achieve a delicious result.
  • Don’t skimp on the quality of your marinara sauce – it’s a key component of the dish, so opt for a flavorful, high-quality brand.

Final Thoughts

The Slow Cooker Italian Meatball Sub is a timeless crowd-pleaser that brings together the comforting flavors of Italian cuisine in a convenient sandwich form. Whether you’re preparing a family dinner, hosting a casual gathering, or simply craving a satisfying meal, these meatball subs are sure to hit the spot. So, roll up your sleeves and let your slow cooker do the work while you anticipate the delightful aroma and flavors that await. Buon appetito!
