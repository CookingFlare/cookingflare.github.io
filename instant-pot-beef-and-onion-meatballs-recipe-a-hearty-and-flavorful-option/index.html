Instant Pot Beef and Onion Meatballs Recipe: A Hearty and Flavorful Option


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that’s easy to prepare, look no further than this Instant Pot Beef and Onion Meatballs recipe. These tender and flavorful meatballs are cooked to perfection in a fraction of the time thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup marinara sauce

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, onion, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, egg, garlic, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Using your hands, shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. You should be able to make around 20 meatballs.

  3. Preheat your Instant Pot by selecting the Saute function. Once hot, add a drizzle of oil to the pot. Working in batches, add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides. This step helps to seal in the flavors and adds a nice texture to the meatballs.

  4. Once all the meatballs are browned, remove them from the Instant Pot and set them aside. Pour in the beef broth and use a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. This will add extra flavor to the sauce.

  5. Return the meatballs to the Instant Pot and pour in the marinara sauce. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  6. Lock the lid in place and set the Instant Pot to the Manual/Pressure Cook function. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

  7. After the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Carefully remove the lid and give the meatballs a gentle stir. Serve them hot with your favorite pasta or on a bed of fluffy mashed potatoes. Garnish with additional grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic meatball flavor, try adding a teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce or a dash of hot sauce to the meatball mixture.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can use ground turkey or chicken instead of beef.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of your meatballs. Basil, thyme, or even a pinch of red pepper flakes can add a delicious kick.
  • If you have any leftovers, these meatballs can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. They also freeze well, so you can make a big batch and save some for later.

Enjoy the rich and comforting flavors of these Instant Pot Beef and Onion Meatballs. They make a perfect weeknight dinner or a crowd-pleasing option for your next gathering. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you’ll have a hearty and flavorful meal on the table in no time.
