Refreshing Nut-Free Cucumber Salad Recipe


Are you looking for a light and refreshing salad to enjoy on a hot summer day? Look no further! This nut-free cucumber salad is the perfect combination of crisp cucumbers, tangy dressing, and a hint of fresh herbs. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply want a healthy side dish, this recipe is sure to impress. Plus, it’s nut-free, making it a great option for those with nut allergies or sensitivities. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful and easy-to-make cucumber salad.

Ingredients

  • 3 large cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced cucumbers and red onion.

  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the white vinegar, olive oil, honey, and a pinch of salt and pepper until well combined.

  3. Pour the dressing over the cucumbers and onions, tossing gently to coat.

  4. Add the chopped dill and parsley to the salad, and gently toss to distribute the herbs evenly.

  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  6. Before serving, taste the salad and adjust the seasoning if necessary with more salt and pepper.

  7. Serve the nut-free cucumber salad chilled, and enjoy the crisp, refreshing flavors!

Tips and Variations

  • For a tangier flavor, you can add a splash of lemon juice to the dressing.
  • If you prefer a sweeter taste, increase the amount of honey in the dressing.
  • Feel free to customize the herbs to your liking. Mint and cilantro can also be great additions to this salad.
  • This salad pairs wonderfully with grilled chicken, fish, or as a topping for tacos.

Health Benefits

Cucumbers are not only hydrating but also low in calories, making them a great addition to a healthy diet. They are also a good source of vitamins and minerals, including vitamin K and potassium. The fresh herbs in this salad add an extra layer of flavor while providing antioxidants and other nutrients.

Final Thoughts

This nut-free cucumber salad is a versatile and satisfying dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Its simplicity makes it an ideal choice for a quick weeknight dinner or an elegant addition to a special meal. The combination of crisp cucumbers, zesty dressing, and aromatic herbs creates a burst of flavors that will leave you feeling refreshed and satisfied. So, the next time you’re in need of a light and delightful salad, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

With its crisp cucumbers, tangy dressing, and fresh herbs, this nut-free cucumber salad recipe is a delightful addition to any meal.
