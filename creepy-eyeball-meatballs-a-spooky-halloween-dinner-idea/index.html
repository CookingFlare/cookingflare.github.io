Creepy Eyeball Meatballs: A Spooky Halloween Dinner Idea


Halloween is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a spooky and delicious dinner? Creepy Eyeball Meatballs are the perfect dish to serve at your Halloween party or to enjoy with your family. These eerie meatballs are guaranteed to be a hit, and their ghoulish appearance will add an extra element of fun to your Halloween festivities. So, let’s gather our ingredients and get cooking!

Ingredients

For the meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the eyeballs:

  • 1 mozzarella cheese ball
  • Black olives

For the sauce:

  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine all the meatball ingredients: ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, milk, chopped onion, minced garlic, egg, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  3. Shape the meat mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place the meatballs on the prepared baking sheet, spacing them out evenly.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are cooked through and browned on the outside.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the eyeballs. Cut the mozzarella cheese ball into small cubes, about 1/2 inch in size. Slice the black olives into thin rounds.

  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool slightly. With a small spoon or your finger, create a small indentation in the center of each meatball.

  7. Place a cube of mozzarella cheese into the indentation of each meatball, and top it with a slice of black olive. Press the olive slice down slightly to resemble an eyeball.

  8. In a saucepan, combine the tomato sauce, dried basil, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Heat the sauce over medium heat until it simmers.

  9. Gently add the meatballs to the simmering sauce, making sure they are fully submerged. Reduce the heat to low and let the meatballs simmer in the sauce for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  10. Serve the Creepy Eyeball Meatballs with a side of spaghetti or as a spooky appetizer. The meatballs will be tender, flavorful, and their ghoulish appearance will surely impress your guests.

Enjoy the creepy deliciousness of these Eyeball Meatballs, and have a spooktacular Halloween!

Note: If you want to make this dish even creepier, you can add some food coloring to the mozzarella cheese before shaping it into eyeballs. Use red for bloodshot eyes or green for a more supernatural look. Get creative and have fun with it!

Remember, Halloween is all about embracing the spooky and having a good time. These Creepy Eyeball Meatballs will add a touch of frightful fun to your Halloween dinner and leave your guests craving for more. So, get into the Halloween spirit and give this recipe a try. Happy haunting and happy cooking!
