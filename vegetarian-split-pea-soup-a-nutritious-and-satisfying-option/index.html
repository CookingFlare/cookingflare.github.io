Vegetarian Split Pea Soup: A Nutritious and Satisfying Option

3 min · 467 words

Are you in search of a hearty, nutritious, and vegetarian-friendly soup to warm your soul? Look no further than this delicious Vegetarian Split Pea Soup recipe! Bursting with flavor and packed with wholesome ingredients, this soup is not only satisfying but also incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe will surely become a staple in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

To make this comforting soup, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried split peas
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the split peas under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery, and sauté until they begin to soften, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the pot and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Add the split peas, vegetable broth, water, bay leaf, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let it simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the split peas are tender and cooked through.
  6. Remove the bay leaf from the pot and discard.
  7. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth and creamy. Be careful when blending hot liquids, as they can splatter. If using a regular blender, blend in batches and allow the soup to cool slightly before blending.
  8. Return the soup to the pot and heat over low heat until warmed through.
  9. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  10. Serve the Vegetarian Split Pea Soup hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley for an added burst of freshness.

Nutritional Benefits

Not only is this Vegetarian Split Pea Soup incredibly delicious, but it also offers a multitude of nutritional benefits. Split peas are an excellent source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals. They are also low in fat and cholesterol-free, making them a fantastic option for those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet. Additionally, the carrots and celery in this soup provide a dose of vitamins A and C, while the garlic adds a delightful flavor and potential immune-boosting properties.

Final Thoughts

Vegetarian Split Pea Soup is a versatile and nutritious option that can be enjoyed as a satisfying meal on its own or paired with a crusty slice of bread for a heartier experience. It’s perfect for those chilly winter evenings or when you’re in need of a comforting bowl of goodness. Plus, it’s a great way to incorporate more plant-based meals into your weekly menu.

So, why not give this recipe a try? Your taste buds and body will thank you for it! Enjoy the warmth and nourishment of Vegetarian Split Pea Soup, and savor every spoonful of this delightful culinary creation.
