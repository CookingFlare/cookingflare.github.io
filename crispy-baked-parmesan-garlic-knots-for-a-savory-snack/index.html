Crispy Baked Parmesan Garlic Knots for a Savory Snack


If you’re looking for a delicious and savory snack to satisfy your cravings, look no further than these crispy baked parmesan garlic knots. These little bites of goodness are perfect for serving at parties, as an appetizer, or simply as a tasty treat to enjoy on a cozy night in. The best part? They’re baked, not fried, so you can indulge without the guilt. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to tantalize your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (about 3 1/2 cups) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Add the warm water and olive oil, and mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  2. Shape the Knots: After the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 16 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a rope about 10 inches long, then tie it into a knot. Place the knots on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, cover with a kitchen towel, and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  3. Bake the Knots: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Once the knots have finished rising, bake them for 15-18 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

  4. Prepare the Garlic Parmesan Coating: While the knots are baking, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant. Remove from heat and stir in the grated parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.

  5. Coat the Knots: When the knots are done baking, remove them from the oven and immediately brush them with the garlic parmesan mixture. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt to taste.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: These crispy baked parmesan garlic knots are best served warm. They pair perfectly with marinara sauce for dipping or as a side to your favorite Italian dishes. Enjoy every savory, garlicky bite!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, sprinkle the knots with a bit of red pepper flakes before baking.
  • Experiment with different herbs and cheeses to create your own unique twist on this classic recipe.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use store-bought pizza dough to simplify the preparation process.

There you have it – a simple yet irresistible recipe for crispy baked parmesan garlic knots. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself, these savory snacks are sure to be a hit. Plus, the aroma of freshly baked garlic and parmesan will fill your kitchen and have everyone eagerly awaiting a taste. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s get baking!
