The Best Instant Pot Desserts You Need to Try


When it comes to quick, delicious, and effortless desserts, the Instant Pot has become a game-changer. Whether you’re craving something rich and chocolatey, fruity and light, or creamy and indulgent, there’s an Instant Pot dessert for every mood. The pressure cooker not only cuts down on cooking time but also locks in flavors, creating mouthwatering treats that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. In this guide, we’ll take you through some of the best Instant Pot desserts you need to try.

1. Instant Pot Chocolate Lava Cake

If you’re a fan of molten chocolate cakes, then the Instant Pot chocolate lava cake is an absolute must-try. With its gooey, warm center and rich chocolate flavor, this dessert is perfect for impressing guests or treating yourself after a long day.

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz dark chocolate (roughly chopped)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Cocoa powder (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the chocolate mixture: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and dark chocolate together, stirring every 30 seconds until smooth.
  2. Combine the ingredients: Once the chocolate mixture is cool enough to handle, whisk in the powdered sugar, eggs, flour, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.
  3. Prepare ramekins: Grease 4 ramekins and dust them lightly with cocoa powder. This step will help your cakes release easily after cooking.
  4. Fill the ramekins: Spoon the batter into the prepared ramekins, filling each one about halfway.
  5. Pressure cook: Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot’s inner pot. Place the ramekins on a trivet and lower them into the pot. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  6. Release and serve: Once the cooking cycle completes, perform a quick release. Let the cakes sit for a minute before carefully removing them from the Instant Pot. Serve warm, and enjoy the oozing chocolate center!

2. Instant Pot Cheesecake

Cheesecake is one of those timeless desserts that always wins over a crowd. The Instant Pot makes creating a perfect cheesecake easier and faster than the traditional method, and the result is a creamy, velvety dessert that will have everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1 1/2 cups cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust: Combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter in a bowl. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust. Set aside.
  2. Make the filling: In a separate bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth. Add the sour cream, sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, and flour, and mix until everything is well incorporated.
  3. Assemble the cheesecake: Pour the cheesecake mixture over the crust in the springform pan. Tap the pan gently to release any air bubbles.
  4. Pressure cook: Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot. Place the springform pan on a trivet, and lower it into the pot. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  5. Release and chill: After the cooking cycle finishes, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 10 minutes before performing a quick release. Once the cheesecake is cool enough, remove it from the pot and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight for the best texture.
  6. Serve: Serve chilled, with a drizzle of fruit sauce or whipped cream if desired.

3. Instant Pot Rice Pudding

Rice pudding is a comforting dessert that brings nostalgia and warmth with every bite. The Instant Pot reduces the cooking time, making it perfect for busy weeknights when you crave a cozy treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice (or any short-grain rice)
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: Raisins or dried fruit for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Combine the ingredients: Add the rice, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt to the Instant Pot and stir well.
  2. Pressure cook: Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to cook on high pressure for 20 minutes.
  3. Release and stir: After the cooking cycle is complete, let the Instant Pot naturally release for 10 minutes before performing a quick release. Open the lid and stir the pudding. It will be creamy and slightly thickened.
  4. Serve: Let the rice pudding cool to your desired temperature and garnish with raisins or dried fruit if desired. For an extra touch, sprinkle with more cinnamon before serving.

4. Instant Pot Apple Crisp

Apple crisp is a fall favorite, but you can enjoy this dessert year-round with the help of the Instant Pot. This version gives you all the cozy flavors of baked apples and a crunchy topping without having to turn on your oven.

Ingredients:

  • 6 medium apples (peeled and sliced)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup butter (cubed)
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the apples: Place the sliced apples in the Instant Pot. Add the brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Stir to coat the apples evenly.
  2. Make the topping: In a separate bowl, mix the rolled oats, flour, butter, and salt until the mixture becomes crumbly.
  3. Assemble the crisp: Sprinkle the oat mixture evenly over the apples in the Instant Pot.
  4. Pressure cook: Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  5. Release and serve: Perform a quick release once the cooking cycle is complete. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream for an extra indulgence.

5. Instant Pot Banana Bread

Banana bread is a beloved dessert that combines the sweetness of ripe bananas with a soft, moist texture. Using the Instant Pot ensures that the bread cooks evenly and remains perfectly moist.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Optional: Chopped walnuts or chocolate chips for extra flavor

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the batter: In a bowl, mash the bananas and mix them with the eggs, sugar, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
  2. Combine dry ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  3. Mix the batter: Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. If desired, fold in chopped walnuts or chocolate chips.
  4. Prepare the pan: Grease a 7-inch cake pan or a loaf pan that fits in your Instant Pot. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Pressure cook: Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot. Place the pan on a trivet, lower it into the pot, and close the lid. Set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 40 minutes.
  6. Release and serve: Once the cooking cycle completes, let the Instant Pot naturally release for 10 minutes. Then perform a quick release. Remove the banana bread from the Instant Pot and allow it to cool before slicing.

6. Instant Pot Chocolate Cheesecake

For chocolate lovers, a rich and creamy chocolate cheesecake made in the Instant Pot is a must-try. With a chocolate crust and a smooth, velvety filling, this dessert is the epitome of indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup melted chocolate (cooled)

Instructions:

  1. Make the crust: Combine the chocolate graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
  2. Prepare the filling: Beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add the sugar, cocoa powder, eggs, vanilla extract, heavy cream, and melted chocolate. Beat until well combined.
  3. Assemble the cheesecake: Pour the cheesecake filling over the crust in the springform pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  4. Pressure cook: Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot. Place the springform pan on a trivet and lower it into the pot. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  5. Release and chill: Let the Instant Pot naturally release pressure for 10 minutes. Then, do a quick release. Chill the cheesecake in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.

Conclusion

From decadent chocolate lava cakes to creamy cheesecakes and fruity crisps, the Instant Pot has revolutionized dessert-making by providing faster, more convenient alternatives without compromising on flavor or texture. These recipes are just a starting point—feel free to experiment with different flavors and ingredients to make them your own. Whether you’re a seasoned Instant Pot user or just getting started, these desserts will certainly inspire you to get creative in the kitchen!
