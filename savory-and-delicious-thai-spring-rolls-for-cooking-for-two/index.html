Savory and Delicious Thai Spring Rolls for Cooking for Two


If you’re looking for a fresh and flavorful dish to enjoy with your significant other, Thai spring rolls are the perfect choice. These savory delights are not only delicious but also fun to make together. Packed with vibrant colors and a variety of textures, Thai spring rolls are an excellent option for a light and satisfying meal. So, let’s dive into the world of Thai cuisine and learn how to prepare these delectable treats for two.

Ingredients

For the Spring Rolls:

  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • 1 cup cooked vermicelli rice noodles
  • 12 large shrimp, peeled, deveined, and cooked
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup julienned carrots
  • 1 cup julienned cucumber
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped roasted peanuts

For the Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce (optional for a spicy kick)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by cooking the vermicelli rice noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside. Cook the shrimp in boiling water for 3-4 minutes until they turn pink, then remove and let them cool. Julienne the carrots and cucumber, and chop the mint and cilantro leaves. Have all the ingredients ready and within reach for easy assembly.

  2. Soak the Rice Paper Wrappers: Fill a shallow dish with warm water. Working with one wrapper at a time, dip it into the water for a few seconds until it softens. Place it on a clean, damp kitchen towel.

  3. Assemble the Spring Rolls: Lay out the softened rice paper wrapper and start by placing a small handful of vermicelli noodles in the center. Add a few shrimp, lettuce, carrots, cucumber, mint, cilantro, and a sprinkle of chopped roasted peanuts.

  4. Roll the Spring Rolls: Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling, then gently roll it up, making sure the filling is tightly packed. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling.

  5. Prepare the Dipping Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the hoisin sauce, peanut butter, lime juice, minced garlic, soy sauce, and sriracha sauce (if using). Adjust the flavors to your liking by adding more lime juice or sriracha for a tangy or spicy kick.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the spring rolls are assembled, serve them with the dipping sauce on the side. These spring rolls are best enjoyed fresh, so make sure to indulge in them right away.

Tips for Success

  • Keep the rice paper wrappers moist and pliable by working on a damp kitchen towel to prevent them from tearing.
  • Customize the filling according to your preferences by adding ingredients such as cooked chicken, tofu, or a variety of fresh herbs.

Final Thoughts

Thai spring rolls are not only a delightful dish to share with a loved one but also a great way to experiment with Thai flavors and textures. The combination of fresh ingredients and vibrant colors makes these spring rolls a feast for the eyes and the taste buds. So, grab your partner and embark on a culinary adventure with this simple and satisfying recipe for two. Enjoy the process of creating these savory and delicious Thai spring rolls together, and savor every delightful bite. Cheers to good food and great company!
