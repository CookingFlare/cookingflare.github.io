Spicy Low Carb Jalapeno Popper Stuffed Pork Chops


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and hearty meals, then these Spicy Low Carb Jalapeno Popper Stuffed Pork Chops are sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection. This dish combines the succulent taste of juicy pork chops with the spicy kick of jalapeno poppers, creating a mouthwatering experience that’s perfect for a special dinner or a weekend indulgence.

Ingredients

For the Pork Chops:

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Jalapeno Popper Filling:

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2-3 jalapenos, finely chopped (seeds removed for less heat)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Spicy Breadcrumb Topping:

  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a bowl, mix together all the ingredients for the jalapeno popper filling until well combined.

  3. Using a sharp knife, carefully cut a pocket into each pork chop, being careful not to cut all the way through.

  4. Stuff each pork chop with the jalapeno popper filling, dividing it evenly among the chops. Use toothpicks to secure the openings if necessary.

  5. Season the stuffed pork chops with salt and pepper.

  6. In a separate bowl, combine the almond flour, parmesan cheese, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper for the spicy breadcrumb topping.

  7. Dip each stuffed pork chop into the melted butter, then coat it with the spicy breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

  8. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Sear the pork chops for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown.

  9. Transfer the seared pork chops to a baking dish and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the pork reaches an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C).

  10. Once cooked, remove the toothpicks from the pork chops and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Spicy Low Carb Jalapeno Popper Stuffed Pork Chops are delicious on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a side of steamed vegetables or a crisp green salad. The creamy and spicy flavors of the jalapeno popper filling perfectly complement the savory taste of the pork, making every bite a delight for your taste buds.

Final Thoughts

With their zesty flavors and low-carb profile, these Spicy Low Carb Jalapeno Popper Stuffed Pork Chops are a fantastic choice for anyone following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. They’re also a great way to spice up your dinner routine and impress your family and friends with a dish that’s both indulgent and satisfying. So, the next time you’re craving a hearty and flavorful meal, give this recipe a try and enjoy the delicious fusion of juicy pork and spicy jalapeno poppers.
