Fall Harvest Stuffed Acorn Squash


Welcome to our cozy kitchen, where we’re embracing the delightful flavors of fall with a scrumptious recipe for Fall Harvest Stuffed Acorn Squash. This hearty and wholesome dish is perfect for those chilly evenings when you crave something comforting yet nutritious. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe that celebrates the bountiful harvest season.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized acorn squash
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 small apple, diced
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the Squash: Cut the acorn squash in half and scoop out the seeds. Place the squash halves on a baking sheet, cut side up, and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the oven for about 30-40 minutes or until the flesh is tender.
  3. Prepare the Filling: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant. Add the carrot, celery, and apple, and cook until slightly softened.
  4. Combine Ingredients: Stir in the dried cranberries, chopped pecans, sage, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Add the cooked quinoa and vegetable broth, and mix everything together until well combined.
  5. Stuff the Squash: Once the acorn squash halves are tender, remove them from the oven. Fill each squash half with the quinoa stuffing mixture.
  6. Bake Again: Place the stuffed squash back in the oven and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the filling is heated through and the flavors meld together.
  7. Serve and Enjoy: Garnish the stuffed acorn squash with fresh parsley and serve warm. This dish pairs beautifully with a crisp green salad or roasted vegetables.

Why We Love This Recipe:

  • Seasonal Delight: Fall harvest ingredients come together to create a flavorful and festive dish.
  • Nutritious and Filling: Packed with wholesome ingredients like quinoa, nuts, and veggies, this recipe is both satisfying and nourishing.
  • Versatile and Customizable: Feel free to add your favorite herbs, spices, or additional veggies to personalize this dish to your taste preferences.
  • Perfect for Entertaining: Impress your guests with this elegant yet straightforward dish that showcases the best of autumn produce.

Embrace the spirit of the season with this Fall Harvest Stuffed Acorn Squash recipe that brings warmth and flavor to your table. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply treating yourself to a cozy evening in, this dish is sure to delight your taste buds and fill your home with the delightful aromas of fall.
