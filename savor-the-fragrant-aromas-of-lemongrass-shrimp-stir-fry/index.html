Savor the Fragrant Aromas of Lemongrass Shrimp Stir Fry


There’s something truly magical about the combination of lemongrass and shrimp in a stir fry. The zesty, citrusy fragrance of lemongrass perfectly complements the succulent, tender shrimp, creating a dish that’s bursting with flavor and aroma. If you’re a fan of vibrant, aromatic dishes that transport you to far-off places with every bite, then this Lemongrass Shrimp Stir Fry recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1 stalk of lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined

For the Stir Fry:

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • Cooked jasmine rice, for serving
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. Marinating the Shrimp:

    • In a bowl, combine the chopped lemongrass, minced garlic, fish sauce, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, and chili flakes.
    • Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the marinade, ensuring that each shrimp is coated evenly.
    • Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse the shrimp.

  2. Stir Frying the Aromatics:

    • Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add the sliced red and yellow bell peppers, along with the onion, to the wok.
    • Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened but still crisp.

  3. Adding the Shrimp:

    • Push the vegetables to the side of the wok to create space in the center.
    • Add the marinated shrimp to the center of the wok, spreading them out into a single layer.
    • Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.

  4. Finishing Touches:

    • Toss in the snap peas and stir-fry for an additional 1-2 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
    • Remove the wok from the heat and give everything a good toss to combine the flavors.

  5. Serving:

    • Serve the fragrant lemongrass shrimp stir fry over a bed of jasmine rice.
    • Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

The beauty of this Lemongrass Shrimp Stir Fry lies in its simplicity and depth of flavor. The marinade infuses the shrimp with the bright, citrusy notes of lemongrass, the umami richness of fish sauce, and the subtle sweetness of honey. Paired with the crisp, colorful bell peppers, onions, and snap peas, every bite is a harmonious blend of textures and tastes.

Additionally, this recipe is incredibly versatile. You can easily customize it by adding your favorite vegetables such as broccoli, mushrooms, or water chestnuts. For a vegetarian twist, you can swap the shrimp for tofu or tempeh and still achieve a dish that’s bursting with flavor.

Not to mention, lemongrass is known for its aromatic and therapeutic properties. The fragrant aroma that fills your kitchen as you cook this dish is truly invigorating, creating a sensory experience that goes beyond just the taste.

So, if you’re ready to embark on a culinary journey that tantalizes the senses and transports you to the vibrant streets of Southeast Asia, grab your wok and whip up this Lemongrass Shrimp Stir Fry. With its enticing aroma and exquisite flavors, it’s a dish that’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire.
