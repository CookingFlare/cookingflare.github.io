Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Shrimp and Rice


If you’re in need of a quick, delicious, and satisfying meal that requires minimal effort and cleanup, then this Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Shrimp and Rice recipe is the perfect solution. With just a handful of simple ingredients and a single pot, you can create a flavorful dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to enjoy a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Begin by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning them with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  2. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large pot or deep skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the seasoned shrimp to the pot and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the pot and set them aside.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pot, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the minced garlic, paprika, and dried thyme, and sauté for 1-2 minutes until the garlic is fragrant.

  4. Add the Rice: Pour the white rice into the pot and stir it to coat it with the butter and aromatic mixture.

  5. Cook the Rice: Pour the chicken or vegetable broth into the pot and bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until it is tender and the liquid is absorbed.

  6. Add the Shrimp: Once the rice is cooked, gently stir in the cooked shrimp, ensuring that it is evenly distributed throughout the rice.

  7. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle the dish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added flavor. Serve the Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Shrimp and Rice hot, and enjoy!

This delightful one-pot meal is a fantastic way to enjoy the succulent flavor of shrimp combined with the comforting goodness of buttery rice. The garlic, paprika, and thyme add a depth of flavor that elevates the dish, making it a standout option for a cozy dinner at home.

The beauty of this recipe lies in its simplicity and versatility. You can easily customize it by adding your favorite vegetables, such as peas, bell peppers, or spinach, to the pot during the final minutes of cooking. Additionally, you can adjust the seasoning to suit your taste preferences, making it a dish that can cater to a variety of flavor profiles.

Not only is this Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Shrimp and Rice recipe a breeze to make, but it also minimizes the cleanup, as it requires only one pot for the entire cooking process. This means less time spent washing dishes and more time to savor the delightful flavors of this dish.

Whether you’re a seafood lover, a fan of easy one-pot meals, or simply someone who appreciates a delicious homemade dinner, this recipe is bound to become a regular addition to your culinary repertoire. It’s a wonderful choice for a stress-free meal that doesn’t compromise on taste or satisfaction.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a fuss-free and delightful meal, consider whipping up this Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Shrimp and Rice. It’s a surefire way to indulge in a delightful dining experience without the hassle of complex cooking techniques or extensive cleanup. Enjoy every flavorful bite and savor the simplicity and deliciousness of this fantastic dish.
