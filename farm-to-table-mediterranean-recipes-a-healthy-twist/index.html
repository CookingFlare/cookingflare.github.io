Farm to Table Mediterranean Recipes: A Healthy Twist


Welcome to our blog, where we explore delicious and nutritious recipes inspired by the Mediterranean diet. In today’s post, we’re taking a farm-to-table approach and putting a healthy twist on traditional Mediterranean dishes. So let’s dive right in and discover some mouthwatering recipes that will tantalize your taste buds and nourish your body.

Recipe 1: Roasted Vegetable Quinoa Salad

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 25 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and drain well.
  3. In a medium saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  4. In the meantime, place the diced peppers, zucchini, eggplant, and red onion on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat evenly.
  5. Roast the vegetables in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until they are tender and lightly browned.
  6. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa and roasted vegetables. Toss gently to mix.
  7. Serve the salad warm or chilled, garnished with fresh parsley.

Recipe 2: Lemon Herb Baked Salmon

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 20 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, chopped dill, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the salmon fillets in a baking dish and pour the lemon herb mixture over them. Make sure the fillets are evenly coated.
  4. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until they are cooked through and flake easily with a fork.
  5. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  6. Serve the lemon herb baked salmon with lemon wedges on the side.

Recipe 3: Greek Style Stuffed Bell Peppers

Preparation time: 20 minutes | Cooking time: 40 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh mint, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut off the tops of the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the bell peppers in a baking dish.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, Kalamata olives, fresh parsley, fresh mint, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  4. Stuff the bell peppers with the quinoa mixture, pressing it down gently to fill the peppers completely.
  5. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for about 30-35 minutes or until the bell peppers are tender.
  6. Remove from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Conclusion

These farm-to-table Mediterranean recipes provide a healthy twist to traditional dishes, combining fresh ingredients with vibrant flavors. Whether you’re looking for a light and refreshing salad, a flavorful seafood option, or a hearty stuffed vegetable dish, these recipes have got you covered. So, grab your apron, head to your local farmer’s market, and let the Mediterranean flavors transport you to a world of delicious and nutritious cuisine. Bon appétit!
