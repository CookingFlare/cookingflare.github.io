Chicken and Avocado Tacos: Creamy and Delicious


Tacos are a beloved dish that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. They are versatile, allowing for endless combinations of fillings and toppings. Today, I want to share with you a recipe for Chicken and Avocado Tacos that are not only creamy and delicious but also incredibly easy to make. So, let’s dive right into it!

Ingredients

For the chicken marinade:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the avocado cream:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the taco assembly:

  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lime juice, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the marinade, making sure they are well coated. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken breasts for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and have reached an internal temperature of 165°F.
  4. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.
  5. While the chicken is resting, prepare the avocado cream. In a small bowl, mash the ripe avocado until smooth. Add the sour cream, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  6. To assemble the tacos, warm the tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side.
  7. Spread a generous amount of the avocado cream onto each tortilla.
  8. Top with a handful of shredded lettuce, a few slices of grilled chicken, diced tomatoes, and chopped cilantro.
  9. Squeeze some fresh lime juice over the taco filling for an extra burst of flavor.
  10. Serve the Chicken and Avocado Tacos immediately, with extra lime wedges on the side for squeezing.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier version, feel free to add some sliced jalapeños or a sprinkle of chili powder to the avocado cream.
  • For a vegetarian option, you can substitute the chicken with grilled vegetables like bell peppers, zucchini, and onions.
  • Experiment with different toppings such as crumbled feta cheese, sliced black olives, or a drizzle of hot sauce to add your own personal touch.
  • If you want to make the tacos gluten-free, use corn tortillas instead of flour tortillas.

Conclusion

Chicken and Avocado Tacos are a perfect combination of flavors and textures. The creamy avocado cream pairs beautifully with the grilled chicken, creating a delicious and satisfying meal. Whether you’re hosting a Taco Tuesday gathering or simply looking to spice up your weeknight dinner routine, these tacos are sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and get ready to indulge in a plate full of creamy and delicious Chicken and Avocado Tacos!
