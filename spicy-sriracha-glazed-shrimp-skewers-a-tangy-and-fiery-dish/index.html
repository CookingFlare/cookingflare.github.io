Spicy Sriracha Glazed Shrimp Skewers: A Tangy and Fiery Dish


Are you a seafood lover looking to spice up your next barbecue? Look no further than these Spicy Sriracha Glazed Shrimp Skewers! Bursting with flavor and heat, these tangy and fiery shrimp skewers are sure to impress your guests and leave them craving for more. So, fire up the grill and get ready to tantalize your taste buds with this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of Sriracha sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the Sriracha sauce, honey, soy sauce, lime juice, minced garlic, grated ginger, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a spicy glaze for the shrimp.

  2. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using a charcoal grill, make sure the coals are hot and glowing.

  3. Thread the jumbo shrimp onto the soaked wooden skewers, leaving a small gap between each shrimp to ensure even cooking.

  4. Brush the shrimp skewers generously with the spicy glaze, coating them on all sides. Reserve some of the glaze for basting during grilling.

  5. Place the shrimp skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque. Baste the shrimp with the reserved glaze every time you flip them.

  6. Once the shrimp are cooked through and glazed to perfection, remove them from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter. Garnish with fresh cilantro or chopped green onions for an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Serve the Spicy Sriracha Glazed Shrimp Skewers immediately with a side of steamed rice or a crisp garden salad. The combination of the tangy glaze and succulent shrimp will leave your taste buds dancing with delight.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder heat, reduce the amount of Sriracha sauce in the glaze. You can always adjust the spiciness to suit your personal preference.
  • For an added twist, try marinating the shrimp in the glaze for 30 minutes before grilling. This will infuse the shrimp with even more flavor.
  • Feel free to experiment with different seasonings and spices. Adding a pinch of cayenne pepper or a dash of hot sauce can take the heat to a whole new level.
  • If you don’t have access to a grill, you can also cook these shrimp skewers on a stovetop grill pan or under the broiler in your oven. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

There you have it - a simple yet explosive recipe for Spicy Sriracha Glazed Shrimp Skewers. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a spicy seafood dish, these tangy and fiery shrimp skewers are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and get ready to indulge in a flavor-packed culinary adventure. Enjoy!
