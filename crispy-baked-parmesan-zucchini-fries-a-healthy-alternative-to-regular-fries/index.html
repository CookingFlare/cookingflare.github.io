Crispy Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries: A Healthy Alternative to Regular Fries


Are you craving a delicious snack but trying to stay healthy at the same time? Look no further than these Crispy Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries! This recipe offers a fantastic alternative to traditional fries, giving you all the crispy goodness you love without the guilt. Made with fresh zucchini and a flavorful Parmesan coating, these fries are sure to become a new favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 2 large zucchinis
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it to prevent sticking.

  2. Wash the zucchinis and cut off the ends. Slice them into thin strips, resembling the shape of fries. Make sure to remove any excess moisture by patting them dry with a paper towel.

  3. In a shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to evenly distribute the flavors.

  4. Dip each zucchini strip into the beaten eggs, making sure to coat it completely. Then, roll the strip in the breadcrumb mixture until it is fully coated.

  5. Place the coated zucchini fries onto the prepared baking sheet, making sure to leave a bit of space between each one. This will allow them to bake evenly and become crispy.

  6. Bake the fries in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until they turn golden brown and crispy. Flip them halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  7. Once the zucchini fries are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow them to firm up and reach their maximum crispiness.

  8. Serve your Crispy Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries with your favorite dipping sauce, such as marinara sauce, ranch dressing, or even a homemade garlic aioli. Get creative and experiment with different flavors to find your perfect match!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or chili powder to the breadcrumb mixture.
  • If you prefer a gluten-free version, substitute the breadcrumbs with almond flour or gluten-free breadcrumbs.
  • Feel free to customize your zucchini fries by adding other seasonings or herbs to the breadcrumb mixture. Some great options include dried basil, oregano, or even a sprinkle of grated lemon zest.
  • To achieve maximum crispiness, make sure to space out the zucchini fries on the baking sheet. Crowding them together may result in a softer texture.
  • These zucchini fries are best enjoyed immediately after baking. However, if you have leftovers, you can reheat them in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for a few minutes to regain their crispiness.

Now that you have this amazing recipe in your arsenal, you can satisfy your cravings for crispy fries without sacrificing your health goals. These Crispy Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries are not only delicious but also a fantastic way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. Give them a try and prepare to be amazed by their addictive flavor and crunchiness. Enjoy!
