Hearty Vegetable and Tofu Pad Thai


Pad Thai is a classic and beloved Thai dish known for its perfect balance of sweet, sour, and savory flavors. In this recipe, we elevate the traditional Pad Thai by incorporating an abundance of hearty vegetables and protein-packed tofu. This Hearty Vegetable and Tofu Pad Thai is not only delicious but also a nutritious twist on a familiar favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 1 block of firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup snow peas
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup peanuts, crushed
  • Lime wedges for serving

For the sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Rice Noodles:

    • Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, tamarind paste, rice vinegar, brown sugar, and Sriracha sauce. Set aside.

  3. Cook the Tofu:

    • In a large pan, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium heat.
    • Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the pan and set aside.

  4. Stir-Fry Vegetables:

    • In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil.
    • Add garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
    • Add the bell pepper, carrot, zucchini, broccoli, and snow peas. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine Everything:

    • Return the tofu to the pan with the vegetables.
    • Add the cooked noodles and the prepared sauce. Toss everything together to coat evenly.

  6. Finish and Serve:

    • Stir in chopped green onions and half of the crushed peanuts.
    • Cook for another minute until everything is heated through.
    • Serve the Hearty Vegetable and Tofu Pad Thai hot, garnished with the remaining crushed peanuts and lime wedges.

Enjoy the Burst of Flavors!

This Hearty Vegetable and Tofu Pad Thai recipe offers a delightful combination of textures and flavors. The tender-crisp vegetables, savory tofu, and flavorful sauce create a dish that is both satisfying and nutritious.
