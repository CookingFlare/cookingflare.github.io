Delicious and Easy One Pot Pasta Recipe


Welcome to our blog, where we bring you delicious and easy recipes that will satisfy your taste buds while minimizing your time in the kitchen. Today, we’re excited to share with you a mouthwatering one pot pasta recipe that will leave you craving for more. This recipe is not only convenient and time-saving, but it also delivers on flavor and texture. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, dried basil, and dried oregano to the pot. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Add the pasta to the pot, making sure it is fully submerged in the liquid. If needed, break the pasta in half to fit the pot. Give it a gentle stir to prevent sticking.

  5. Cover the pot and let the pasta cook on low heat for the recommended cooking time stated on the pasta package. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking.

  6. Once the pasta is cooked al dente and the liquid has been absorbed, remove the pot from the heat. If the pasta is still a bit undercooked, you can add a splash of hot water and continue cooking for a few more minutes.

  7. Season the pasta with salt and pepper to taste. Give it a final stir to distribute the flavors evenly.

  8. Serve the one pot pasta in bowls, garnished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves. The cheese will melt slightly on the hot pasta, adding a creamy and savory touch.

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables such as spinach, bell peppers, or mushrooms. Simply sauté them along with the onion and garlic before adding the remaining ingredients.
  • For a protein-packed meal, you can add cooked chicken, shrimp, or sausage to the pasta.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, sprinkle some red pepper flakes into the pot during the cooking process.
  • This one pot pasta recipe is versatile and forgiving, so feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to suit your taste.

Enjoy the simplicity and deliciousness of this one pot pasta recipe, where all the flavors meld together beautifully in a single pot. The convenience of cooking everything in one pot not only saves time on cleanup but also allows the pasta to absorb all the wonderful flavors from the sauce. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Go ahead and give it a try, and let us know how it turns out! Happy cooking!
