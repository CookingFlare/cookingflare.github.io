Spicy and Fragrant: Thai Red Curry Beef Recipe


If you’re craving a dish that’s bursting with bold flavors and exotic aromas, look no further than this Thai Red Curry Beef recipe. This dish is a harmonious blend of tender beef, vibrant vegetables, and a rich, aromatic red curry sauce that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Bangkok. The combination of spicy, sweet, and savory flavors makes this dish an absolute delight for anyone looking to explore the vibrant world of Thai cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3-4 tbsp Thai red curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • Fresh Thai basil leaves
  • Cooked jasmine rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Begin by heating the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the Thai red curry paste to the skillet and stir-fry for a minute or two until it becomes fragrant.

  2. Next, add the thinly sliced beef to the skillet and stir-fry until it’s cooked to your desired level of doneness.

  3. Once the beef is cooked, pour in the coconut milk and beef broth, and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and bamboo shoots to the skillet, and continue to simmer for another 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Stir in the fish sauce and brown sugar, adjusting the quantities to taste. These ingredients will add the perfect balance of salty, sweet, and savory flavors to the dish.

  6. Just before serving, toss in a generous handful of fresh Thai basil leaves to infuse the curry with their unmistakable aroma and flavor.

  7. Serve the Thai red curry beef over a bed of steamed jasmine rice, allowing the fragrant sauce to soak into the fluffy grains.

Tips and Serving Suggestions

  • For an extra kick of heat, consider adding a few slices of fresh red chili to the curry while it simmers.
  • If you’re vegetarian, tofu can be used as a delicious alternative to beef in this recipe.
  • Garnish the finished dish with a sprinkle of chopped cilantro and a wedge of lime for a burst of freshness.

Embrace the Flavors of Thailand

This Thai Red Curry Beef recipe is a celebration of the bold, aromatic, and spicy flavors that define Thai cuisine. The combination of tender beef, vibrant vegetables, and the complex layers of the red curry sauce make this dish a true standout. Whether you’re a seasoned Thai food enthusiast or a newcomer to this culinary tradition, this recipe is sure to captivate your senses and leave you craving more.

So, gather your ingredients, fire up your skillet, and immerse yourself in the intoxicating world of Thai cooking with this unforgettable red curry beef dish. From the first whiff of fragrant spices to the last satisfying bite, this recipe is a journey to Thailand that you can savor in the comfort of your own kitchen.
