Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wings Recipe for Game Day


Are you ready to take your game day snacks to the next level? Look no further than these mouthwatering Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wings! These crispy, tangy, and spicy wings are the perfect addition to any game day spread. Whether you’re hosting a party or just enjoying a casual night in with friends, these wings are sure to be a hit. So grab your favorite team’s jersey, fire up the TV, and get ready to indulge in some seriously delicious wings.

Ingredients

To make these Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wings, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup of hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper. Mix well to evenly distribute the spices.

  2. Rinse the chicken wings under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel. Place the wings in the bowl with the flour mixture and toss to coat them thoroughly.

  3. In a deep skillet or Dutch oven, heat about 2 inches of vegetable oil over medium-high heat until it reaches 375°F (190°C). Carefully add the coated chicken wings to the hot oil, working in batches if necessary to avoid overcrowding the pan.

  4. Fry the wings for about 10-12 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Use a slotted spoon or tongs to transfer the wings to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the hot sauce and melted butter until well combined. This will be your spicy buffalo sauce.

  6. Once all the chicken wings are fried and drained, transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Pour the spicy buffalo sauce over the wings and toss them gently to coat them evenly.

  7. Serve the Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wings immediately with your favorite dipping sauce, such as ranch or blue cheese dressing. Garnish with celery sticks and carrot sticks for a refreshing crunch.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy coating, you can double-dip the chicken wings in the flour mixture before frying. Simply repeat steps 2 and 3 for a thicker and crunchier crust.

  • Adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less cayenne pepper to the flour mixture. If you prefer milder wings, reduce the amount of cayenne pepper, or omit it altogether.

  • If you don’t have a deep skillet or Dutch oven, you can also bake the chicken wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and bake the wings for about 45-50 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are crispy and cooked through. Then toss them in the spicy buffalo sauce.

Conclusion

These Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wings are the ultimate game day treat. The crispy exterior gives way to tender and juicy meat, while the tangy and spicy buffalo sauce adds a kick that will keep you coming back for more. Serve them up with your favorite cold beverage and watch as they disappear within minutes. So, gather your friends, turn on the game, and enjoy these irresistible wings that are sure to be the MVP of any game day spread.
