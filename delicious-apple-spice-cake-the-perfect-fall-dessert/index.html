Delicious Apple Spice Cake: The Perfect Fall Dessert


When the crisp air of fall sets in and leaves start to turn vibrant shades of red and gold, there’s nothing quite like the comforting aroma of freshly baked apple spice cake wafting through the house. This delectable dessert is the epitome of fall, capturing the essence of the season with its warm spices and tender chunks of juicy apples. Whether you’re hosting a cozy gathering or simply craving a sweet treat, this apple spice cake is sure to delight your taste buds and fill your home with the scents of autumn.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 cup grated apple (about 2 medium apples)
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done using an electric mixer or by hand using a wooden spoon.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the applesauce. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing just until combined.

  6. Gently fold in the grated apple and chopped walnuts, if desired. Be careful not to overmix; you want the apple and nuts to be evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and spread it evenly.

  8. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The cake should be golden brown and slightly pulled away from the edges of the pan.

  9. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cake has cooled, you can enjoy it as is or frost it with your favorite cream cheese frosting for an extra touch of indulgence. Dust with a sprinkle of cinnamon for a beautiful finishing touch.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this scrumptious apple spice cake as a delightful dessert after a hearty fall meal. It pairs wonderfully with a dollop of freshly whipped cream or a scoop of creamy vanilla ice cream. For an extra special treat, drizzle warm caramel sauce over the cake slices before serving.

Conclusion

Indulge in the flavors of fall with this delicious apple spice cake. With its moist texture, fragrant spices, and bursts of juicy apple, it’s the perfect dessert to celebrate the beauty of autumn. Whether you’re enjoying it with loved ones or savoring a slice on a cozy evening, this cake will warm your heart and satisfy your sweet tooth. So go ahead, bake this delightful treat and embrace the joys of the season.
