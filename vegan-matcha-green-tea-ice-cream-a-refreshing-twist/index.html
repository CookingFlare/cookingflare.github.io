Vegan Matcha Green Tea Ice Cream: A Refreshing Twist


As the weather starts to warm up, our cravings for cold and creamy treats begin to grow. But what if we told you there’s a way to satisfy your sweet tooth without sacrificing your dietary preferences? Enter vegan matcha green tea ice cream, a refreshing twist on a classic dessert that’s perfect for hot summer days.

The Power of Matcha

Matcha, the finely ground green tea leaves, has been a staple in Japanese culture for centuries. Not only does it offer a boost of antioxidants and energy, but it also adds a distinct flavor and vibrant green color to any dish. In the case of vegan matcha green tea ice cream, the matcha powder infuses the ice cream with a subtle bitterness and a hint of grassy notes, balancing out the sweetness of the coconut milk.

The Magic of Coconut Milk

Coconut milk is a game-changer in the world of vegan ice cream. With its rich, creamy texture and subtle coconut flavor, it’s the perfect substitute for traditional dairy. In this recipe, the coconut milk is blended with the matcha powder, maple syrup, and a touch of lemon juice to create a smooth and silky ice cream.

The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 can full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup matcha powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon xanthan gum (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the coconut milk, maple syrup, matcha powder, lemon juice, and salt. Whisk until the matcha powder is fully incorporated.
  2. Add the water to the saucepan and whisk until the mixture is smooth.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and xanthan gum (if using).
  4. Gradually add the cornstarch mixture to the saucepan, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
  5. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a boil.
  6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  7. Cover the saucepan with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  8. Once the mixture is chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  9. Once the ice cream is churned, transfer it to an airtight container and store it in the freezer for at least 2 hours to set.

Tips and Variations

  • For a creamier ice cream, add 1/4 cup of cashew cream or soaked and blended cashews to the mixture before churning.
  • Experiment with different flavor combinations by adding a pinch of cinnamon, a drizzle of honey, or a sprinkle of sesame seeds to the ice cream.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can also freeze the mixture in a shallow metal pan and blend it in a food processor once it’s frozen solid.

Conclusion

Vegan matcha green tea ice cream is a refreshing twist on a classic dessert that’s perfect for hot summer days. With its creamy texture, subtle bitterness, and hint of grassy notes, it’s a treat that’s sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you feeling energized and refreshed. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and experience the magic of matcha for yourself.
