Say Cheese! 7 Irresistible Recipes for Leftover Cheese


When it comes to leftover cheese, many of us are tempted to simply wrap it up and store it in the fridge for another day. But why not get creative and turn it into something truly special? In this post, we’ll explore 7 irresistible recipes that will make you fall in love with leftover cheese all over again.

Recipe 1: Cheesy Frittata

Who says frittatas are only for breakfast? This recipe is perfect for brunch or even dinner. Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large bowl, whisk together 6 eggs, 1/2 cup grated leftover cheese (such as cheddar or mozzarella), 1/2 cup diced bell peppers, and 1/2 cup diced onions. Pour the mixture into a greased 9-inch (23cm) pie plate and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the eggs are set. Slice and serve.

Recipe 2: Cheese and Chive Mashed Potatoes

Mashed potatoes are a classic comfort food, and adding leftover cheese takes them to a whole new level. Boil 2-3 large potatoes until they’re tender, then drain and mash with 1/4 cup grated leftover cheese, 1 tablespoon butter, and 1 tablespoon chopped chives. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Recipe 3: Cheesy Spinach and Artichoke Dip

This addictive dip is perfect for snacking or entertaining. In a large bowl, combine 1 (14.5 oz) can of artichoke hearts, 1 package of frozen chopped spinach, 1 cup grated leftover cheese (such as Parmesan or cheddar), 1/2 cup mayonnaise, and 1/2 cup sour cream. Mix well and serve with tortilla chips or crackers.

Recipe 4: Cheesy Broccoli Soup

This creamy soup is a great way to use up leftover cheese and broccoli. In a large pot, sauté 2 tablespoons butter and 1 onion until softened, then add 4 cups broccoli florets and 2 cups chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the broccoli is tender. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup, then stir in 1/2 cup grated leftover cheese and 1/2 cup heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Recipe 5: Cheesy Stuffed Peppers

These colorful peppers are a fun and easy way to use up leftover cheese. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the tops off 4 large bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. In a large bowl, combine 1 cup cooked rice, 1 cup grated leftover cheese, 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes, and 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley. Stuff each pepper with the mixture and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.

Recipe 6: Cheesy Garlic Bread

Who doesn’t love garlic bread? This recipe is a simple way to use up leftover cheese and is perfect for snacking or as a side dish. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Slice a baguette into 1-inch (2.5cm) thick slices and place on a baking sheet. Sprinkle each slice with 1/4 cup grated leftover cheese and 1 clove garlic, minced. Drizzle with olive oil and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Recipe 7: Cheesy Zucchini Boats

These flavorful zucchini boats are a great way to use up leftover cheese and zucchini. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut 4 large zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds and pulp. In a large bowl, combine 1 cup grated leftover cheese, 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley, and 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil. Stuff each zucchini boat with the mixture and place on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchinis are tender.

There you have it - 7 irresistible recipes that will make you fall in love with leftover cheese all over again. Whether you’re a cheese lover or just looking for some creative ways to use up your leftovers, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So go ahead, get creative, and say cheese!
