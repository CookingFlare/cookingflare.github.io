Picnic-Inspired Spring Roll Recipes for a Light Meal


Spring rolls are a delightful and refreshing dish that is perfect for picnics. The combination of fresh vegetables, herbs, and protein wrapped in a light rice paper wrapper makes for a satisfying and healthy meal. In this blog post, we will share three picnic-inspired spring roll recipes that are easy to prepare, packed with flavor, and suitable for a light meal.

Recipe 1: Thai Chicken Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Peanut sauce, for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Fill a shallow dish with warm water. Dip one rice paper wrapper into the water for a few seconds until it softens. Place it on a clean surface.

  2. Arrange a small amount of shredded chicken, carrots, cucumber, bell pepper, cilantro, and mint leaves in the center of the rice paper wrapper.

  3. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling and then roll it up tightly, similar to a burrito.

  4. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.

  5. Serve the Thai chicken spring rolls with peanut sauce for dipping.

Recipe 2: Mediterranean Veggie Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh parsley leaves
  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Tzatziki sauce, for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Dip one rice paper wrapper into warm water until it softens. Place it on a clean surface.

  2. Spread a thin layer of hummus across the center of the rice paper wrapper.

  3. Arrange the carrot, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, olives, feta cheese, and parsley leaves on top of the hummus.

  4. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling and then roll it up tightly.

  5. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.

  6. Serve the Mediterranean veggie spring rolls with tzatziki sauce for dipping.

Recipe 3: Asian Shrimp Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Hoisin sauce, for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Dip one rice paper wrapper into warm water until it softens. Place it on a clean surface.

  2. Arrange a small amount of shrimp, carrots, cucumber, cilantro, and mint leaves in the center of the rice paper wrapper.

  3. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling and then roll it up tightly.

  4. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.

  5. Serve the Asian shrimp spring rolls with hoisin sauce for dipping.

Conclusion

These picnic-inspired spring roll recipes are not only delicious but also perfect for a light and refreshing meal. Whether you prefer the flavors of Thai cuisine, Mediterranean ingredients, or Asian-inspired fillings, these recipes offer a variety of options to suit your taste. Enjoy these spring rolls on your next picnic or as a healthy snack any time of the day. So grab a pack of rice paper wrappers and get rolling!
