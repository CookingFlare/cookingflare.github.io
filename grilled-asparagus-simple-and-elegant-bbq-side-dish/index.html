Grilled Asparagus: Simple and Elegant BBQ Side Dish


As the summer season rolls in, it’s time to fire up the grill and enjoy the smoky flavors of barbecued dishes. While meat often takes center stage at a barbecue, let’s not forget about the delicious and nutritious vegetable options that can elevate any BBQ spread. One such vegetable that deserves the spotlight is asparagus. Grilled asparagus is a simple and elegant BBQ side dish that adds a burst of freshness and vibrant color to your meal. So, let’s dive into how to prepare this delightful dish!

Ingredients

To make grilled asparagus, you will need:

  • Fresh asparagus spears
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. This will ensure that the asparagus cooks evenly and develops a delicious char.

  2. Rinse the asparagus spears under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel. Trim off the tough ends of the spears by snapping them off or cutting about an inch from the bottom.

  3. Place the asparagus spears in a shallow dish and drizzle them with olive oil. Use your hands or a brush to evenly coat the spears with oil.

  4. Season the asparagus with salt and pepper, according to your taste preferences. For an extra kick, you can also sprinkle some garlic powder or lemon zest.

  5. Carefully lay the asparagus spears on the preheated grill. Make sure they are arranged in a single layer to ensure even cooking.

  6. Grill the asparagus for about 4-6 minutes, turning them occasionally to prevent burning. The cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the spears. You want the asparagus to be tender yet still slightly crisp.

  7. Once the asparagus is cooked to your desired doneness, remove them from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter.

  8. Serve the grilled asparagus immediately as a side dish to complement your BBQ feast. They pair well with grilled meats, seafood, or even as a standalone appetizer.

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can squeeze fresh lemon juice over the grilled asparagus just before serving. The tanginess of the lemon perfectly balances the smoky flavors from the grill.

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese or crumbled feta cheese over the grilled asparagus for a creamy and savory twist.

  • Grilled asparagus can also be used as a topping for salads, pasta dishes, or as a filling for sandwiches and wraps. Get creative with how you incorporate this versatile vegetable into your meals!

Why Grilled Asparagus?

Grilled asparagus not only tastes delicious but also offers numerous health benefits. Asparagus is a nutrient-rich vegetable that is low in calories and high in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. It is a great source of folate, vitamin K, vitamin C, and vitamin A. Additionally, asparagus is packed with antioxidants that contribute to overall well-being.

The grilling process enhances the natural flavors of asparagus while adding a smoky and slightly charred taste. The result is a side dish that is both elegant and simple, perfect for any BBQ gathering.

Final Thoughts

Grilled asparagus is a wonderful addition to any barbecue spread. Its simplicity allows the natural flavors to shine through, while the grilling process adds a delightful smokiness. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving a healthy and delicious side dish, give grilled asparagus a try. You won’t be disappointed by its simplicity and elegance!

So, fire up your grill, grab some fresh asparagus, and indulge in the flavors of this fantastic BBQ side dish. Enjoy!
