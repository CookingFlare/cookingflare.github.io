Air Fryer Pizza Toss: Quick, Easy and Delicious Multi-layered Loaves for Busy Weeknights


Skip the takeout and whip up a flavorsome meal with our Air Fryer Pizza Toss recipe. This culinary triumph is perfect for those hectic weeknights when you’re in need of a speedy satisfaction. Dedicatedly concocted with a fusion of versatile ingredients, the Air Fryer Pizza Toss delivers a concise cooking procedure with sumptuous results. Buckle up and be prepared to delight in the scrumptious and satisfying culmination, in the form of multi-layered loaves that are bound to become a favorite in your culinary arsenal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups bread flour
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • ½ cup lukewarm water
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • ½ cup sliced mushrooms
  • ½ cup cooked ground beef (optional)

Preparation Methods:

  1. Whisk the lukewarm water and sugar in a mixing bowl to dissolve the sugar. Allow the mixture to sit for a couple of minutes until it becomes foamy.

  2. Add the yeast to the water mixture and let it stand for about 5 minutes.

  3. While the yeast is activating, mix the flour and salt in another bowl.

  4. Gradually pour the flour mixture into the yeast mix, stirring until a soft dough forms.

  5. On a lightly floured surface, knead the dough for approximately 5-8 minutes or until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  6. Lightly oil a bowl and place the kneaded dough inside. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and allow it to rise in a warm place for about 1 hour.

  7. Preheat your air fryer to 400°F (200°C).

  8. While your dough is resting, prepare the toppings: combine the marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, and ground beef (if using) in a bowl and mix together.

  9. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into eight equal pieces.

  10. Roll each piece into a small ball and flatten them into circles, about ½-inch in thickness.

  11. Place each flattened circle on a prepared surface and spoon 1 to 2 tablespoons of the topping mixture in the center of each.

  12. Gather the edges of the dough circle together, pinching them to seal and encase the toppings in the middle.

  13. Lightly coat the air fryer basket with oil spray or line it with parchment paper.

  14. Carefully place 4 of the formed loaves into the air fryer basket, creating space between each loaf to prevent them from sticking together.

  15. Cook the Air Fryer Pizza Toss for about 10 minutes or until the loaves are golden brown and the cheese has melted.

  16. Remove the loaves from the basket and repeat the process with the remaining 4 loaves, making sure to reheat the air fryer to 400°F (200°C) before starting the second batch.

  17. Serve the Air Fryer Pizza Toss warm, garnished with chopped parsley or red pepper flakes, alongside your favorite dipping sauces, and enjoy your mouthwatering creation!

Conclusion:

In an age where simplicity and taste merge, the Air Fryer Pizza Toss is not only absolutely delicious but also a testament to your multifaceted cooking prowess. This appetizing innovation will most certainly find its place in the hearts of your friends and family, as it does in your recipe repertoire. Bon appétit!
