Savory Low Carb Bacon-Wrapped Chicken Skewers


When it comes to delicious and satisfying dishes, bacon-wrapped chicken skewers are hard to beat. Not only are they packed with flavor, but they are also low in carbs, making them a perfect choice for those following a low-carb or keto diet. The combination of juicy chicken and crispy bacon is a match made in heaven, and the addition of savory spices takes these skewers to a whole new level. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply looking for a quick and easy weeknight dinner, these savory low carb bacon-wrapped chicken skewers are sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful spice blend.

  3. Take a slice of bacon and wrap it around a cube of chicken. Thread the bacon-wrapped chicken onto a wooden skewer. Repeat this process until all the chicken is used.

  4. Sprinkle the spice blend over the bacon-wrapped chicken skewers, making sure to coat them evenly.

  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the bacon is crispy and the chicken is cooked through.

  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure maximum flavor.

  7. Serve the savory low carb bacon-wrapped chicken skewers hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook these skewers in the oven. Simply preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and bake the skewers on a lined baking sheet for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the chicken is cooked through.

  • Feel free to customize the spice blend according to your taste preferences. You can add a pinch of cayenne pepper for some heat or a sprinkle of smoked paprika for a smoky flavor.

  • These bacon-wrapped chicken skewers can be served as an appetizer, a main dish, or even as a party snack. They are versatile and sure to be a hit at any gathering.

  • If you’re not following a low-carb diet, you can serve these skewers with a side of roasted vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Enjoy the deliciousness of these savory low carb bacon-wrapped chicken skewers and savor the flavors of juicy chicken and crispy bacon. This recipe is a crowd-pleaser and a great choice for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, these skewers will surely impress. So fire up the grill or preheat the oven, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a mouthwatering dish that is both low carb and incredibly delicious!
