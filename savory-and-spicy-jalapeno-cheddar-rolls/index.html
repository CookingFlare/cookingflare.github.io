Savory and Spicy Jalapeno Cheddar Rolls


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and a touch of heat, then you’re in for a treat with this recipe for Savory and Spicy Jalapeno Cheddar Rolls. These delectable rolls are perfect for brunch, picnics, or even as a side dish for your favorite soup or salad. Packed with the perfect combination of sharp cheddar cheese and spicy jalapenos, these rolls are a guaranteed crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet of instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup pickled jalapenos, chopped
  • 1/4 cup green onions, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until the mixture becomes foamy.
    • Add the melted butter, eggs, and salt to the yeast mixture and whisk until well combined.
    • Gradually add the flour and mix until a dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a medium bowl, combine the shredded cheddar cheese, chopped jalapenos, green onions, softened butter, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined. Set aside.

  3. Assemble the Rolls:

    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and transfer it to a lightly floured surface.
    • Roll the dough into a 16x20 inch rectangle.
    • Spread the prepared filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Starting from one of the longer edges, tightly roll the dough into a log. Pinch the seam to seal.

  4. Bake the Rolls:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 12 equal-sized rolls.
    • Place the rolls in a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, cover with a kitchen towel, and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.
    • Bake the rolls for 20-25 minutes or until they are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once baked, remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
    • Serve the warm Savory and Spicy Jalapeno Cheddar Rolls on their own or with a side of your favorite dipping sauce.

These rolls are best enjoyed fresh, but you can store any leftovers in an airtight container and reheat them in the oven for a few minutes to enjoy the gooey cheese and flavorful jalapenos all over again.

Conclusion

Savory and Spicy Jalapeno Cheddar Rolls are a delightful twist on classic dinner rolls. The combination of sharp cheddar cheese and zesty jalapenos creates a burst of flavor with every bite. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving something savory and spicy, these rolls are sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to bake up a batch of these delicious rolls for your next meal!
