Instant Pot Vegan Recipes: Plant-Based Meals for Everyone


The Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking, making it faster and more convenient to prepare delicious meals without sacrificing flavor or nutrition. For those embracing a plant-based diet, it’s an exceptional tool for creating vegan dishes that are hearty, wholesome, and packed with flavor. Whether you’re new to vegan cooking or a seasoned pro looking to shake up your meal prep routine, the Instant Pot is the perfect companion to create easy-to-make, nutrient-dense recipes for breakfasts, lunches, dinners, and even desserts.

In this post, we’ll dive into a variety of vegan recipes that are sure to please everyone at the table—even non-vegans. From creamy soups to protein-packed mains, and even a decadent dessert or two, these Instant Pot vegan recipes offer something for every occasion.

One-Pot Vegan Chili: A Comforting Classic

No list of Instant Pot vegan recipes would be complete without a robust chili. Packed with protein-rich beans, hearty vegetables, and a medley of spices, this dish is perfect for meal prep or cozy weeknight dinners.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional for sautéing)
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional for heat)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté mode and heat the olive oil.
  2. Add the onion and garlic; cook for 2–3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the bell pepper and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients, stirring well to combine.
  5. Switch the Instant Pot to the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” mode and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  6. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before performing a quick release.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped cilantro, a squeeze of lime, or vegan sour cream.

This chili is a satisfying, protein-rich dish that can be enjoyed as a main course or served atop rice or baked sweet potatoes.

Creamy Vegan Mushroom Risotto

Risotto has a reputation for being labor-intensive, but your Instant Pot makes this creamy, luxurious dish a breeze. Perfect for date nights or small dinner parties, this vegan mushroom risotto is rich in umami goodness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp vegan butter or olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 2 1/2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms (cremini or button mushrooms work well)
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened plant-based milk (optional for extra creaminess)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and melt the vegan butter or heat the oil.
  2. Stir in the onion and garlic, cooking for 3–4 minutes until softened.
  3. Add the Arborio rice and toast it for about 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. If using wine, deglaze the pot by adding it and stirring until absorbed.
  5. Turn off sauté mode and add the vegetable broth and mushrooms, stirring well.
  6. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” for 6 minutes.
  7. After cooking, do a quick release of pressure.
  8. Stir in nutritional yeast and plant-based milk, if using. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.

The result is an indulgently creamy risotto with minimal stirring and maximum flavor.

Lentil and Sweet Potato Curry

For a meal that’s loaded with nutrients and bold spices, this lentil and sweet potato curry is a winner. It’s hearty, warming, and filled with vibrant colors and textures.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp coconut oil or vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp red chili flakes (optional for spice)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in the Instant Pot using sauté mode. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, cooking until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the sweet potatoes, lentils, coconut milk, vegetable broth, and spices.
  3. Cancel sauté mode and set the Instant Pot to “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then perform a quick release.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve over rice or with naan.

This curry is not only deeply satisfying but also nutrient-packed, making it ideal for a quick, healthy dinner.

Instant Pot Vegan Mac and Cheese

This creamy, luscious dish is comfort food at its best—all while being completely vegan! The cheesy sauce is made with wholesome, plant-based ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked for 4 hours or boiled for 10 minutes
  • 1 cup unsweetened plant-based milk
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add macaroni and water or vegetable broth to the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set to “Pressure Cook” for 4 minutes.
  2. While the pasta cooks, blend soaked cashews, plant-based milk, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a high-speed blender until smooth.
  3. Perform a quick release on the Instant Pot and drain any excess liquid from the pasta.
  4. Stir in the cashew sauce until the pasta is evenly coated.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy the nostalgic flavors of this plant-based mac and cheese.

The creamy sauce is velvety and packed with cheesy flavor, satisfying vegans and non-vegans alike.

Sweet and Sticky Vegan Rice Pudding

Finish your meal on a sweet note with this comforting dessert that’s quick to prepare and absolutely delightful. This vegan rice pudding is rich, creamy, and perfectly spiced.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio or jasmine rice
  • 2 cups canned coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup or agave nectar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • A pinch of salt
  • Raisins or chopped nuts for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add rice, coconut milk, water, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and vanilla extract to the Instant Pot.
  2. Secure the lid and set to “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” for 15 minutes.
  3. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then perform a quick release.
  4. Stir the pudding to combine the flavors, adding additional sweetener if desired.
  5. Serve warm, topped with raisins, chopped nuts, or a sprinkle of cinnamon.

This rice pudding is the perfect blend of creamy and sweet, making it a family favorite.

These Instant Pot vegan recipes showcase how versatile and simple plant-based cooking can be. Whether you’re preparing a quick weeknight meal or impressing guests with a gourmet dish, the Instant Pot can help you create nourishing, flavorful, vegan food in no time at all.
