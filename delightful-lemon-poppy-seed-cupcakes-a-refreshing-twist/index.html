Delightful Lemon Poppy Seed Cupcakes: A Refreshing Twist


Are you tired of the same old cupcake flavors? Looking for a refreshing twist to satisfy your sweet tooth? Look no further! These Delightful Lemon Poppy Seed Cupcakes are just what you need. Bursting with tangy lemon flavor and a subtle crunch from the poppy seeds, these cupcakes are a delightful treat for any occasion. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons poppy seeds

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, making sure to fully incorporate each egg before adding the next one.

  5. Add the lemon zest and lemon juice to the butter-sugar mixture, and mix until well combined.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cupcake.

  7. Gently fold in the poppy seeds, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  8. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  10. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and allow them to cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

Optional Lemon Cream Cheese Frosting

If you want to take these cupcakes to the next level, consider adding a delicious lemon cream cheese frosting. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

  • 8 oz (225g) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, beat together the cream cheese and butter until smooth and creamy.

  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing well after each addition.

  3. Add the lemon juice and lemon zest, and beat until fully incorporated.

  4. Once the cupcakes have cooled, generously frost each cupcake with the lemon cream cheese frosting.

Enjoy Your Delightful Lemon Poppy Seed Cupcakes!

These Delightful Lemon Poppy Seed Cupcakes are sure to be a hit at your next gathering or as a special treat for yourself. The tangy lemon flavor combined with the crunch of the poppy seeds creates a unique and refreshing twist on a classic cupcake. So, grab your apron and get baking – your taste buds will thank you!
