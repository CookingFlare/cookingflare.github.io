Mini Lemon Meringue Pies: Adorable and Delicious


Are you looking for a delightful dessert that is both adorable and delicious? Look no further than these Mini Lemon Meringue Pies! These bite-sized treats are perfect for any occasion, from afternoon tea parties to fancy dinner gatherings. With their tangy lemon filling and fluffy meringue topping, these pies are sure to impress your guests and leave them wanting more.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

For the lemon filling:

  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 cups water

For the meringue topping:

  • 3 large egg whites
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the mixture into the bottom of a mini pie pan or a regular muffin tin lined with cupcake liners. Make sure to press the crust firmly to create a solid base.

  3. Bake the crust for about 8 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  4. In a saucepan, whisk together the lemon juice, lemon zest, granulated sugar, egg yolks, cornstarch, and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. Continue cooking for an additional minute, then remove from heat. The lemon filling should be smooth and glossy.

  5. Pour the lemon filling into the cooled crusts, dividing it evenly among them. Allow the pies to cool for about 30 minutes at room temperature, then transfer them to the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours, or until set.

  6. In a clean, dry mixing bowl, beat the egg whites and cream of tartar on high speed until soft peaks form. Gradually add the granulated sugar, a tablespoon at a time, while continuing to beat until stiff peaks form and the meringue becomes glossy.

  7. Remove the chilled pies from the refrigerator and top each one with a generous dollop of meringue. Use the back of a spoon to create decorative peaks.

  8. Preheat your oven’s broiler on high. Place the pies on a baking sheet and carefully transfer them to the oven. Broil for about 1-2 minutes, or until the meringue turns golden brown. Keep a close eye on them to prevent burning.

  9. Once the meringue is golden brown, remove the pies from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.

These Mini Lemon Meringue Pies are the perfect combination of sweet and tangy flavors. The crisp graham cracker crust provides a delightful contrast to the smooth and creamy lemon filling. And let’s not forget about the light and airy meringue topping that adds a touch of elegance to these adorable little treats.

So, whether you’re hosting a tea party or simply craving a delicious dessert, give these Mini Lemon Meringue Pies a try. They are sure to impress your guests and leave them asking for the recipe. Enjoy!
