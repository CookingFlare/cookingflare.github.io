How to Make Spring Rolls: 5 Fresh and Flavorful Recipes


Spring rolls are a popular and delicious dish that originated in East Asia. These light and refreshing rolls are filled with a variety of fresh ingredients and wrapped in a delicate rice paper wrapper. They are perfect as an appetizer, a light meal, or a healthy snack. In this blog post, we will share with you five different spring roll recipes that are not only easy to make but also bursting with flavor. So, let’s get started!

1. Traditional Vegetable Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • 1 cup of shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup of julienned carrots
  • 1 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup of bean sprouts
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Soy sauce or peanut sauce for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Prepare a clean and flat surface for rolling the spring rolls.
  2. Dip one rice paper wrapper into a bowl of warm water for a few seconds until it becomes pliable.
  3. Place the wet rice paper wrapper onto the prepared surface.
  4. Arrange a small amount of shredded cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, bean sprouts, mint leaves, and cilantro leaves in the center of the rice paper wrapper.
  5. Fold the sides of the rice paper wrapper over the filling, then roll it tightly from the bottom to the top, just like rolling a burrito.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Serve the spring rolls with soy sauce or peanut sauce for dipping.

2. Spicy Shrimp Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Cooked and peeled shrimp
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup of julienned cucumbers
  • 1 cup of sliced avocado
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Sriracha sauce or sweet chili sauce for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same steps as the Traditional Vegetable Spring Rolls to prepare the rice paper wrappers.
  2. Place a cooked shrimp in the center of the rice paper wrapper.
  3. Add shredded lettuce, julienned cucumbers, sliced avocado, and cilantro leaves on top of the shrimp.
  4. Roll the spring roll tightly, starting from the bottom.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  6. Serve the spring rolls with sriracha sauce or sweet chili sauce for dipping.

3. Chicken and Mango Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup of julienned mango
  • 1 cup of julienned red bell peppers
  • 1 cup of sliced cucumber
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Hoisin sauce or sweet and sour sauce for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the rice paper wrappers as mentioned above.
  2. Place a small amount of shredded chicken in the center of the rice paper wrapper.
  3. Add julienned mango, red bell peppers, sliced cucumber, and basil leaves on top of the chicken.
  4. Roll the spring roll tightly, starting from the bottom.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  6. Serve the spring rolls with hoisin sauce or sweet and sour sauce for dipping.

4. Tofu and Vegetable Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Firm tofu, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 cup of julienned carrots
  • 1 cup of julienned cucumbers
  • 1 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Soy sauce or sesame ginger sauce for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same steps as the Traditional Vegetable Spring Rolls to prepare the rice paper wrappers.
  2. Place a slice of tofu in the center of the rice paper wrapper.
  3. Add julienned carrots, cucumbers, bell peppers, mint leaves, and cilantro leaves on top of the tofu.
  4. Roll the spring roll tightly, starting from the bottom.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  6. Serve the spring rolls with soy sauce or sesame ginger sauce for dipping.

5. Smoked Salmon Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Smoked salmon slices
  • 1 cup of mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup of sliced cucumbers
  • 1 cup of sliced avocado
  • Fresh dill leaves
  • Lemon wedges for squeezing

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the rice paper wrappers as mentioned above.
  2. Place a slice of smoked salmon in the center of the rice paper wrapper.
  3. Add mixed salad greens, sliced cucumbers, sliced avocado, and dill leaves on top of the salmon.
  4. Squeeze a lemon wedge over the filling for added flavor.
  5. Roll the spring roll tightly, starting from the bottom.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Serve the spring rolls with additional lemon wedges on the side.

Now that you have learned how to make five different spring roll recipes, you can impress your family and friends with these fresh and flavorful delights. Feel free to experiment with different fillings and dipping sauces to create your own variations. Enjoy the burst of flavors in every bite and have fun rolling your way to a delicious meal!
