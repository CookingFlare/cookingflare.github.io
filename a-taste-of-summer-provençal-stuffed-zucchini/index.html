A Taste of Summer: Provençal Stuffed Zucchini


Summertime is all about vibrant flavors and fresh produce. And what better way to celebrate the season than with a delicious and healthy dish like Provençal Stuffed Zucchini? This recipe combines the rich flavors of the Mediterranean with the lightness of zucchini, creating a perfect dish for those warm summer evenings. So let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized zucchini
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the zucchini in half lengthwise and scoop out the flesh, leaving a 1/4-inch border. Set the zucchini shells aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the scooped-out zucchini flesh, cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, garlic, basil, parsley, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  4. Stuff each zucchini shell with the quinoa mixture, pressing it down gently to ensure it fills the cavity.
  5. Place the stuffed zucchini on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchini is tender and the filling is golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and let the stuffed zucchini cool for a few minutes.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Add a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top of the stuffed zucchini before baking for an extra burst of flavor.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices, such as oregano or thyme, to give the dish your own unique twist.
  • If you’re not a fan of quinoa, you can substitute it with cooked rice or couscous.
  • For a heartier meal, serve the stuffed zucchini with a side of mixed greens or a fresh salad.

Health Benefits

Not only is this Provençal Stuffed Zucchini dish bursting with flavor, but it’s also packed with nutritional goodness. Zucchini is low in calories and high in fiber, making it a great choice for those looking to maintain a healthy weight. Quinoa, on the other hand, is a complete protein and a good source of essential amino acids, making it a perfect choice for vegetarians and vegans. The combination of fresh herbs and vegetables adds an array of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to the dish, promoting overall health and well-being.

Conclusion

Provençal Stuffed Zucchini is a delightful dish that captures the essence of summer in every bite. The flavors of the Mediterranean come together beautifully, creating a light and satisfying meal that will leave you feeling nourished and refreshed. So why not take advantage of the abundance of zucchini during the summer months and give this recipe a try? Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember to experiment with different variations and make this recipe your own. And most importantly, enjoy the process of cooking and savor the taste of summer with every mouthful. Bon appétit!
