Creamy One Pot Chicken and Asparagus Pasta


Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make dinner recipe that will satisfy your taste buds? Look no further! This Creamy One Pot Chicken and Asparagus Pasta recipe is the perfect choice for a quick and flavorful meal. With tender chicken, crisp asparagus, and a creamy sauce, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides, about 5-6 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic. Add the asparagus pieces and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they are bright green and slightly tender.

  3. Meanwhile, cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  4. Once the asparagus is cooked, add the chicken broth to the skillet and bring it to a simmer. Use a spatula or wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. This will add extra flavor to the sauce.

  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Continue to stir until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Slice the cooked chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces and add them back to the skillet. Stir in the cooked penne pasta and toss everything together until well coated in the creamy sauce.

  7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot and enjoy!

This Creamy One Pot Chicken and Asparagus Pasta recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients and a single skillet, you can have a satisfying meal on the table in no time. The creamy sauce adds a luxurious touch to the dish, making it perfect for special occasions or a comforting weeknight dinner.

The combination of tender chicken, crisp asparagus, and al dente penne pasta creates a delightful texture that will keep you coming back for more. The flavors of the garlic, Parmesan cheese, and chicken broth blend together beautifully, resulting in a dish that is rich and flavorful.

This recipe is also versatile and allows for customization. Feel free to add your favorite herbs or spices to elevate the flavors even further. You can also substitute the penne pasta with any other pasta shape of your choice.

Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a dinner party, this Creamy One Pot Chicken and Asparagus Pasta recipe is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. Give it a try and let your taste buds indulge in the creamy goodness!
