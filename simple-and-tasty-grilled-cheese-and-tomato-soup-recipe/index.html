Simple and Tasty: Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup Recipe


If you’re looking for a comforting meal that’s perfect for any day of the week, then you’re in luck! This grilled cheese and tomato soup recipe is a classic combination that never fails to satisfy. With its warm and gooey cheese sandwich paired with a rich and flavorful tomato soup, it’s a match made in food heaven. So, let’s get started and indulge in this simple yet tasty dish!

Ingredients

For the Grilled Cheese Sandwich:

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread (white, whole wheat, or sourdough)
  • 2-3 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Butter, softened

For the Tomato Soup:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)

Instructions

Grilled Cheese Sandwich:

  1. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Place one slice, buttered side down, onto the skillet.
  4. Add the cheese slices on top of the bread.
  5. Place the second bread slice on top, buttered side up.
  6. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom bread slice is golden brown.
  7. Flip the sandwich and cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown.
  8. Remove from the skillet and let it cool for a minute before slicing.

Tomato Soup:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, vegetable broth, sugar, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove the pot from the heat and let it cool slightly.
  6. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully puree the soup until smooth.
  7. Return the pot to low heat and stir in the heavy cream (if using). Heat the soup until warmed through.
  8. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

Serving Suggestions

Serve the grilled cheese sandwich alongside a bowl of piping hot tomato soup. The crispy and cheesy sandwich can be dunked into the creamy and tangy soup for an explosion of flavors. It’s the ultimate comfort food combination that will warm your soul on a chilly day.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different types of cheese for your grilled cheese sandwich. Try using Swiss, provolone, or even a combination of different cheeses to add more depth of flavor.
  • Add a slice of ham or bacon to your grilled cheese sandwich for an extra protein kick.
  • If you prefer a chunkier tomato soup, you can skip the blending step and leave it as is.
  • Garnish your tomato soup with fresh basil or a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for an extra touch of elegance.

Final Thoughts

This simple and tasty grilled cheese and tomato soup recipe is a classic that never goes out of style. Its comforting flavors and easy preparation make it a go-to meal for both kids and adults alike. So, the next time you’re craving something warm and delicious, give this recipe a try and savor every bite. Enjoy!
