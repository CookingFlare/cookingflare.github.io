Savory Everything Bagel Breakfast Wraps


Breakfast is often hailed as the most important meal of the day, and for good reason! It sets the tone for your entire day, fueling your body and mind for whatever lies ahead. If you’re tired of the same old breakfast routine, allow me to introduce you to a delightful twist on morning fare: Savory Everything Bagel Breakfast Wraps. These wraps are not only delicious but also incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize them to suit your taste preferences. Let’s dive into the world of flavors and textures that this breakfast wrap brings to the table!

Why Everything Bagels?

Everything bagels are a popular choice for breakfast enthusiasts who enjoy a burst of flavor with every bite. Topped with a delightful blend of poppy seeds, sesame seeds, garlic, onion, and salt, these bagels provide a savory base for our breakfast wraps. Incorporating the essence of an everything bagel into a wrap elevates the breakfast experience, making it both satisfying and exciting.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Creating your own Savory Everything Bagel Breakfast Wraps is simple and requires minimal ingredients. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Wraps:

  • 4 large flour tortillas (or everything bagel-flavored wraps for extra flavor)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your choice)
  • 1 cup cooked and crumbled breakfast sausage (or diced ham, bacon, or a meat alternative)
  • 1 cup fresh spinach (or any leafy green)
  • 1 tablespoon cream cheese (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons everything bagel seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Serving:

  • Sliced avocado
  • Fresh salsa or pico de gallo
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Step-by-Step Preparation

1. Prepare Your Ingredients

Before you start cooking, gather all your ingredients to make the process smooth and efficient. You can easily customize your wraps by adding your favorite veggies, proteins, or spices. For a healthier version, you might want to include diced bell peppers, mushrooms, or even some chopped tomatoes.

2. Scramble the Eggs

In a medium skillet, heat a splash of oil or a pat of butter over medium heat. Crack the eggs into a bowl, season with salt and pepper, and whisk until fully combined. Pour the mixture into the heated skillet.

Stir occasionally, allowing the eggs to cook evenly. Remove them from the heat when they are still slightly undercooked, as they will continue to cook in the residual heat.

3. Cook the Sausage

In the same skillet, add the crumbled breakfast sausage. Cook it until browned and crispy, about 5-7 minutes. If you’re using bacon, ensure it’s crispy and well-cooked before adding it to the wraps.

4. Combine the Ingredients

Once the sausage is cooked, lower the heat and add the scrambled eggs back into the skillet. Stir in the shredded cheese and toss in the spinach. The residual heat will wilt the spinach and melt the cheese, creating a creamy and flavorful filling.

5. Assemble the Wraps

Lay out your tortillas on a clean, flat surface. If you’re using cream cheese, spread a layer on each tortilla to add creaminess and flavor.

Spoon an equal amount of the egg and sausage mixture onto the center of each tortilla. Sprinkle a generous amount of everything bagel seasoning on top, which will enhance the flavor profile of the wraps.

6. Roll the Wraps

To roll your wraps, fold in the sides of the tortilla and then roll from the bottom up to encase the filling. Make sure to wrap them tightly to prevent any spillage.

7. Toast the Wraps (Optional)

For an extra crunch, you can toast your assembled wraps. Heat a skillet over medium heat, and place the wraps seam-side down. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy. This step adds a delightful texture that complements the savory filling.

8. Serve and Enjoy!

Slice the breakfast wraps in half for easier handling, and serve them warm. For an extra touch, garnish with slices of avocado, fresh salsa, or a drizzle of hot sauce. Enjoy with a cup of coffee or your favorite morning beverage, and relish the deliciousness of your creation.

Customization Ideas

The beauty of Savory Everything Bagel Breakfast Wraps is their versatility. Here are a few ideas to customize your wraps further:

  • Veggie Delight: Add diced bell peppers, onions, or mushrooms to the egg mixture for a hearty veggie-packed breakfast.
  • Spicy Kick: Incorporate jalapeños or spicy sausage for those who enjoy a little heat in their morning routine.
  • Fresh Herbs: Toss in some chopped fresh herbs like chives, parsley, or cilantro for a burst of freshness.
  • Different Cheeses: Experiment with different types of cheese, such as feta, pepper jack, or goat cheese, to find your perfect flavor combination.

Nutritional Benefits

These breakfast wraps are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Eggs provide high-quality protein and essential vitamins, while the addition of spinach adds iron and vitamins A and C. The choice of toppings and fillings allows you to incorporate healthy fats and fiber, making these wraps a balanced breakfast option.

Meal Prep and Storage

Savory Everything Bagel Breakfast Wraps are perfect for meal prep! You can make a batch ahead of time and store them in the refrigerator for up to three days. To reheat, simply pop them in the microwave for a minute or two, or reheat in a skillet for that freshly toasted flavor.

Final Thoughts

Savory Everything Bagel Breakfast Wraps are a fantastic way to revitalize your breakfast routine. They offer a delicious blend of flavors and textures that will leave you satisfied and energized. The best part? They are easy to make, customizable, and perfect for busy mornings or leisurely brunches. So, gather your ingredients, unleash your creativity, and enjoy these mouthwatering wraps that will undoubtedly become a favorite in your breakfast repertoire. Happy cooking!
