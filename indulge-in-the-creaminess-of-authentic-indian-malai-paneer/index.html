Indulge in the Creaminess of Authentic Indian Malai Paneer


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, then you must have come across the delightful dish called Malai Paneer. This creamy and rich delicacy is a favorite among vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. Made with fresh paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and a luscious blend of spices, Malai Paneer is a dish that will transport you straight to the streets of India. So, why not indulge in the creaminess of authentic Indian Malai Paneer right in your own kitchen?

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 200 grams of paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons of ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 teaspoons of ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1/4 cup of cashews, soaked in water
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions

Now that you have all the ingredients ready, let’s dive into the process of making this delectable dish:

  1. Start by heating the ghee in a pan over medium heat. Once the ghee is hot, add the finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

  2. Next, add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.

  3. Now, it’s time to add the tomato puree and cook it until the oil separates from the mixture. This will take around 5-7 minutes.

  4. While the tomato puree is cooking, drain the soaked cashews and blend them into a smooth paste using a blender or food processor.

  5. Once the tomato puree is cooked, add the cashew paste and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring continuously.

  6. Now, it’s time to add the spices. Add the cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix everything together and cook for a minute to allow the flavors to blend.

  7. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream. Stir well to combine the cream with the spice mixture.

  8. Gently add the paneer cubes to the mixture and stir carefully to coat the paneer with the creamy sauce. Be gentle to avoid breaking the paneer cubes.

  9. Cover the pan and let the paneer simmer in the sauce for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  10. Finally, sprinkle the garam masala over the dish and give it a good stir.

  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.

Enjoy the Creamy Delight

There you have it - a creamy and indulgent dish that is sure to satisfy your taste buds. The richness of the heavy cream, the creaminess of the paneer, and the aromatic blend of spices make Malai Paneer a true delight. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is easy to follow and will surely impress your guests.

So, why wait? Indulge in the creaminess of authentic Indian Malai Paneer and experience the flavors of India in every bite. Happy cooking!
