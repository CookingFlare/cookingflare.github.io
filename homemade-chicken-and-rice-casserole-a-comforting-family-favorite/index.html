Homemade Chicken and Rice Casserole: A Comforting Family Favorite


There’s something special about a homemade chicken and rice casserole that brings warmth and comfort to the table. This classic dish is a crowd-pleaser and perfect for family gatherings, potlucks, or cozy weeknight dinners. The combination of tender chicken, fluffy rice, and savory seasonings creates a hearty and satisfying meal that’s sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. In this blog post, we’ll explore how to make a delicious homemade chicken and rice casserole from scratch, and we’ll share some tips for customizing the recipe to suit your family’s tastes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, then add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute.

  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook until browned on both sides, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the rice and stir to coat it with the remaining oil and flavorful bits from the chicken. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the rice starts to turn golden.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth, dried thyme, dried oregano, paprika, black pepper, and salt. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat, cover the skillet, and cook for 15 minutes, or until the rice is almost tender.

  6. While the rice is cooking, dice the cooked chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  7. Once the rice is almost tender, stir in the frozen peas, diced carrots, and sliced mushrooms. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, until the vegetables are heated through and the rice is fully cooked.

  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the diced chicken. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  9. Sprinkle the grated cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese over the top of the casserole.

  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  11. Garnish the casserole with fresh parsley before serving.

Customization Tips

  • Vegetable Variations: Feel free to customize the vegetable mix in the casserole based on your family’s preferences. Broccoli, bell peppers, or spinach can be great additions or substitutions.

  • Creamy Additions: For a creamier texture, consider stirring in a can of cream of chicken soup or a cup of sour cream before baking.

  • Flavor Enhancements: Add a kick of heat with a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce. Fresh herbs like rosemary or basil can also elevate the flavors in the dish.

  • Topping Ideas: Experiment with different cheese blends for the topping, or add a crunchy element with a layer of buttery breadcrumbs before baking.

Conclusion

Homemade chicken and rice casserole is a timeless dish that brings together simple ingredients to create a comforting and satisfying meal. Whether you’re cooking for a large gathering or simply craving a taste of home, this recipe is sure to impress. With its versatility and comforting flavors, it’s no wonder that this casserole has become a beloved family favorite for generations. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the heartwarming goodness of homemade chicken and rice casserole.
