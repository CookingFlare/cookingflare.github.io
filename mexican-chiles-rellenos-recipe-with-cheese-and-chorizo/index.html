Mexican Chiles Rellenos Recipe with Cheese and Chorizo


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious recipe for Chiles Rellenos. This classic dish combines the bold flavors of cheese and chorizo, wrapped in a crispy, golden-brown exterior. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds and impress your friends and family with this mouthwatering creation!

Ingredients

  • 6 large poblano peppers
  • 1 pound of chorizo, removed from casings
  • 1 cup of shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Preheat your broiler to high. Place the poblano peppers on a baking sheet and broil them until the skins are charred and blistered, turning them occasionally to ensure even cooking. This should take about 10 minutes. Remove the peppers from the oven and transfer them to a heatproof bowl. Cover the bowl tightly with plastic wrap and let the peppers steam for 10 minutes. This will make it easier to remove the skins.

  2. While the peppers are steaming, cook the chorizo in a skillet over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Use a wooden spoon to break up the meat into small crumbles. Once cooked, remove the chorizo from the skillet and set it aside to cool.

  3. After the peppers have steamed for 10 minutes, carefully remove the plastic wrap and peel off the charred skins. Make a small slit lengthwise in each pepper and carefully remove the seeds and membranes, taking care not to tear the peppers.

  4. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine the cooked chorizo, Monterey Jack cheese, and cheddar cheese. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  5. Stuff each pepper with the cheese and chorizo mixture, being careful not to overstuff them. Use toothpicks to secure the peppers closed, if necessary.

  6. In a shallow dish, whisk together the flour, salt, and black pepper. In a separate shallow dish, beat the eggs until well blended.

  7. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. You want enough oil to reach a depth of about 1 inch. While the oil is heating, coat each stuffed pepper in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess. Dip the coated pepper into the beaten eggs, ensuring it is well coated. Place the pepper in the hot oil and repeat this process with the remaining peppers.

  8. Fry the peppers until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides, turning them occasionally to ensure even cooking. This should take about 5 minutes per pepper. Once cooked, remove the peppers from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain off any excess oil.

  9. Serve the Chiles Rellenos hot, garnished with your favorite toppings such as salsa, sour cream, or guacamole. Enjoy the explosion of flavors with every bite!

Conclusion

Mexican Chiles Rellenos Recipe with Cheese and Chorizo is a crowd-pleasing dish that brings together the vibrant flavors of Mexico in one tantalizing package. The crispy exterior, the gooey melted cheese, and the savory chorizo filling create a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. Whether you’re hosting a fiesta or simply craving a delicious homemade meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab your apron and get ready to embark on a culinary journey that will transport your taste buds south of the border!
