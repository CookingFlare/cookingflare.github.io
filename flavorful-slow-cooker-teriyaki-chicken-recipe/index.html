Flavorful Slow Cooker Teriyaki Chicken Recipe


Looking for a delicious and easy dinner recipe that will impress your family and friends? Look no further than this flavorful Slow Cooker Teriyaki Chicken recipe. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal effort, you can create a mouthwatering dish that is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 green onions, sliced (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using). This flavorful marinade will be the key to your delicious teriyaki chicken.

  2. Place the chicken thighs in the slow cooker and pour the marinade over them. Make sure all the chicken pieces are well-coated. Cover the slow cooker and set it to cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or on high heat for 2-3 hours. The slow cooking process will ensure that the chicken is tender and infused with the teriyaki flavors.

  3. Once the chicken is fully cooked and tender, remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a plate. Shred the chicken using two forks. Meanwhile, pour the remaining liquid from the slow cooker into a saucepan.

  4. In a separate small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water together to create a slurry. Gradually whisk the slurry into the liquid in the saucepan. Cook the sauce over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens to your desired consistency. This will create a delicious teriyaki sauce to coat the shredded chicken.

  5. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and pour the thickened teriyaki sauce over it. Stir to combine, ensuring that all the chicken is coated in the sauce. Cover and let it cook on low heat for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve the flavorful slow cooker teriyaki chicken over a bed of cooked rice. Garnish with sliced green onions for a pop of freshness and added visual appeal. The tender and juicy chicken, combined with the sweet and savory teriyaki sauce, will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Tips and Variations

  • For extra flavor, you can add vegetables such as bell peppers, carrots, or broccoli to the slow cooker along with the chicken. Simply chop them into bite-sized pieces and cook them with the chicken.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can increase the amount of cornstarch in the slurry. Add it gradually until you achieve your desired thickness.
  • Feel free to customize the level of spiciness by adjusting the amount of red pepper flakes. If you prefer a milder flavor, you can omit them altogether.
  • This recipe is perfect for meal prepping. You can make a big batch of the slow cooker teriyaki chicken and portion it out for easy lunches or dinners throughout the week.

Enjoy the flavors of this flavorful Slow Cooker Teriyaki Chicken recipe that will have your taste buds dancing with delight. It’s a simple yet satisfying dish that will impress even the toughest critics. Give it a try and let the slow cooker do all the work while you enjoy a delicious meal with your loved ones.
