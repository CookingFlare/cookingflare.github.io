Grilled Salmon Burger Creations for Seafood Lovers


salmon burgers are a game-changer for seafood enthusiasts. The combination of juicy salmon, crispy bun, and creamy toppings is a match made in heaven. In this post, we’ll explore some innovative grilled salmon burger creations that will elevate your seafood game.

Grilled Salmon Burger with Spicy Mango Salsa

The sweetness of the mango pairs perfectly with the spicy kick of the jalapeño, creating a flavor combination that will leave you wanting more. To make this burger, start by grilling the salmon patties until they’re cooked through, then top them with a slice of mango, a spoonful of spicy mango salsa, and a sprinkle of cilantro.

  • 4 salmon fillets, skin removed
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Cilantro leaves for garnish

Grilled Salmon Burger with Lemon-Dill Sauce

Brighten up your summer with the zesty flavor of lemon and the freshness of dill. This burger is perfect for a light and refreshing meal. To make the lemon-dill sauce, combine 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt, 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill, and 1 minced garlic clove in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  • 4 salmon fillets, skin removed
  • 1/4 cup lemon-dill sauce (see above)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves for garnish
  • Tomato slices for garnish

Grilled Salmon Burger with Avocado and Bacon

The creamy avocado and crispy bacon add a rich and satisfying element to this burger. To make the bacon, cook 6 slices of bacon in a pan until crispy, then chop into small pieces. To assemble the burger, place a cooked salmon patty on a hamburger bun, top with a slice of avocado, a few pieces of crispy bacon, and a sprinkle of red onion.

  • 4 salmon fillets, skin removed
  • 2 ripe avocados, sliced
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Red onion slices for garnish

Grilled Salmon Burger with Pineapple and Teriyaki Glaze

Sweet and savory, this burger is a perfect combination of flavors. To make the teriyaki glaze, combine 1/4 cup soy sauce, 1/4 cup honey, 2 tablespoons rice vinegar, and 1 tablespoon grated ginger in a bowl. Brush the glaze over the salmon patties during the last minute of grilling.

  • 4 salmon fillets, skin removed
  • 1 ripe pineapple, sliced
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki glaze (see above)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Green onion slices for garnish

Tips and Variations

  • For a more intense flavor, use a cedar plank or a piece of aluminum foil with holes punched in it to grill the salmon. This will infuse the fish with a smoky flavor.
  • Swap out the hamburger buns for a bed of mixed greens or a slice of whole grain bread for a healthier option.
  • Add some crunch to your burger by topping with chopped nuts or crispy fried onions.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as feta or goat cheese, for a unique flavor combination.

With these grilled salmon burger creations, you’ll be the envy of all your seafood-loving friends. Whether you’re in the mood for something spicy, sweet, or savory, there’s a burger on this list that’s sure to satisfy your cravings. So go ahead, get creative, and grill up some salmon burgers that will make your taste buds dance!
