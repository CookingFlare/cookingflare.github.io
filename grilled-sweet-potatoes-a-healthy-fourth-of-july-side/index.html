Grilled Sweet Potatoes: A Healthy Fourth of July Side


As the Fourth of July approaches, it’s time to start planning the perfect menu for your holiday gathering. While hamburgers and hot dogs often take center stage, it’s essential to have a variety of sides to complement the main dishes. One dish that never fails to impress is grilled sweet potatoes. Not only are they delicious, but they also add a nutritious and colorful element to your spread. Here’s a simple and delightful grilled sweet potatoes recipe that will elevate your Fourth of July celebration.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Optional: fresh herbs for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sweet Potatoes: Wash the sweet potatoes thoroughly and pat them dry. Cut each sweet potato into 1/4-inch slices, ensuring they are of uniform thickness to promote even grilling.

  2. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat, around 375-400°F (190-200°C). If using a gas grill, preheat with the lid closed for 10-15 minutes. For a charcoal grill, distribute the charcoal evenly and allow it to heat for about 15 minutes.

  3. Season the Sweet Potatoes: In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, salt, black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder. Brush both sides of the sweet potato slices with the seasoned olive oil mixture.

  4. Grill the Sweet Potatoes: Carefully place the seasoned sweet potato slices on the preheated grill. Close the lid and grill for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are tender and have beautiful grill marks.

  5. Serve: Once the sweet potatoes are grilled to perfection, transfer them to a serving platter. If desired, sprinkle them with fresh herbs such as parsley or chives for an extra burst of flavor and color.

Why Grilled Sweet Potatoes?

Grilled sweet potatoes are the perfect addition to your Fourth of July menu for several reasons. Not only do they provide a healthier alternative to traditional sides, but they also offer a delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors. Additionally, sweet potatoes are packed with essential nutrients such as fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, making them a nutritious choice for your holiday gathering.

Pairing Suggestions

Pair your grilled sweet potatoes with a variety of dipping sauces or condiments to elevate their flavors. Consider serving them with a zesty cilantro lime dipping sauce, a tangy garlic aioli, or a smoky chipotle mayo. These sauces complement the natural sweetness of the potatoes and add an extra layer of deliciousness to the dish.

Conclusion

This Fourth of July, take your side dish game to the next level with these mouthwatering grilled sweet potatoes. Not only are they simple to prepare, but they also offer a healthy and flavorful option for your guests to enjoy. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or a festive picnic, grilled sweet potatoes are sure to be a hit at your Independence Day celebration. So fire up the grill, grab some sweet potatoes, and get ready to impress your friends and family with this delectable and nutritious side dish. Happy Fourth of July!
