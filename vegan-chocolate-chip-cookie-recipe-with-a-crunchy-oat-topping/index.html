Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe with a Crunchy Oat Topping


If you’re looking for a delicious vegan treat that will satisfy your sweet tooth, look no further! These Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies with a Crunchy Oat Topping are the perfect combination of chewy, chocolatey goodness and a crispy oat topping that adds a delightful crunch. Whether you’re a vegan, have dietary restrictions, or simply love a good cookie, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

For the Cookies:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup coconut oil, melted
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 flax egg (1 tbsp ground flaxseed + 3 tbsp water)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

For the Crunchy Oat Topping:

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil, melted
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a separate large bowl, mix the melted coconut oil, brown sugar, granulated sugar, flax egg, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a smooth cookie dough forms.

  5. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  6. In another small bowl, prepare the crunchy oat topping by mixing together the rolled oats, melted coconut oil, brown sugar, and cinnamon.

  7. Using a cookie scoop or a spoon, portion the cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.

  8. Gently press a small amount of the crunchy oat topping onto the top of each cookie, pressing it down slightly to adhere.

  9. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.

  10. Once baked, remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  11. Enjoy your delicious Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies with a Crunchy Oat Topping with a glass of almond milk or your favorite dairy-free beverage!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra flavor kick, consider adding a teaspoon of instant espresso powder to the cookie dough for a subtle coffee undertone.

  • If you prefer a softer oat topping, mix in a tablespoon of maple syrup to the oat mixture before topping the cookies.

  • Experiment with different types of vegan chocolate chips, such as dark chocolate or white chocolate, to customize the flavor of your cookies.

  • To make these cookies gluten-free, simply substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies with a Crunchy Oat Topping are a delightful vegan twist on the classic chocolate chip cookie. The combination of the chewy cookie base and the crispy oat topping creates a textural sensation that will have you reaching for seconds. Whether you’re baking for yourself, family, or friends, these cookies are sure to be a hit. So, preheat your oven, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in these irresistible vegan treats!
