Autumn Harvest Recipes: 20 Delicious and Easy Options for Thanksgiving and Beyond


Autumn is a time of abundance, where the bountiful harvest brings an array of vibrant colors and flavors to our tables. With Thanksgiving just around the corner, it’s the perfect opportunity to celebrate the season and indulge in delicious meals that showcase the best of autumn produce. In this blog post, we’ve curated 20 mouthwatering recipes that will not only impress your guests on Thanksgiving but will also become your go-to options for any autumn gathering.

1. Butternut Squash Soup

Creamy, velvety, and bursting with the flavors of roasted butternut squash, this soup is the epitome of comfort. It’s the perfect way to start your Thanksgiving feast or to warm up on a chilly autumn evening.

2. Roasted Turkey with Cranberry Glaze

No Thanksgiving is complete without a succulent turkey centerpiece. This recipe takes it up a notch with a tangy cranberry glaze that adds a delightful sweetness to the perfectly roasted bird.

3. Sweet Potato Casserole

A classic Thanksgiving side dish, this sweet potato casserole is a crowd-pleaser. Topped with a crunchy pecan streusel, it strikes the perfect balance between sweet and savory.

4. Apple Cider-Glazed Brussels Sprouts

Transform humble Brussels sprouts into a show-stopping side dish with a sticky apple cider glaze. The natural sweetness of the apple cider complements the earthy flavors of the Brussels sprouts, creating a harmonious blend of tastes.

5. Maple-Glazed Carrots

Enhance the natural sweetness of autumn carrots with a luscious maple glaze. This simple yet elegant side dish is a great way to incorporate seasonal flavors into your Thanksgiving spread.

6. Harvest Salad with Pomegranate Dressing

Add a burst of freshness to your Thanksgiving table with a vibrant harvest salad. Tossed with a tangy pomegranate dressing, this salad is a medley of flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

7. Pumpkin Ravioli with Sage Brown Butter

Elevate your pasta game with homemade pumpkin ravioli. Served in a decadent sage brown butter sauce, this dish is a perfect combination of autumnal flavors and comforting richness.

8. Cranberry-Stuffed Acorn Squash

Celebrate the season with this stunning stuffed acorn squash. Filled with a savory cranberry and herb stuffing, it’s a beautiful and delicious centerpiece for any autumn gathering.

9. Caramelized Onion and Mushroom Tart

Impress your guests with this elegant caramelized onion and mushroom tart. The flaky crust, creamy filling, and earthy flavors make it a perfect appetizer or side dish for your Thanksgiving feast.

10. Roasted Beet and Goat Cheese Salad

Bring the colors of autumn to your table with this vibrant roasted beet and goat cheese salad. The earthy beets, creamy goat cheese, and crunchy walnuts create a delightful combination of flavors and textures.

11. Apple Cinnamon Pork Chops

Elevate your pork chops with the flavors of autumn. This recipe combines juicy pork chops with sweet apples and warm cinnamon for a comforting and satisfying main course.

12. Pumpkin Bread Pudding

Put a seasonal twist on a classic dessert with this pumpkin bread pudding. Rich, creamy, and packed with warm spices, it’s the perfect sweet ending to your Thanksgiving meal.

13. Caramel Apple Cheesecake Bars

Indulge your sweet tooth with these decadent caramel apple cheesecake bars. With a buttery crust, creamy cheesecake filling, and gooey caramel apple topping, they’re a deliciously addictive treat.

14. Spiced Pear Tart

Capture the essence of autumn with this spiced pear tart. The sweet and juicy pears are complemented by warm spices and a buttery crust, making it a truly irresistible dessert.

15. Cranberry Orange Bread

Bake up a batch of this moist and flavorful cranberry orange bread. Bursting with tart cranberries and zesty orange, it’s a delightful treat for breakfast or a snack during the holiday season.

16. Pumpkin Spice Latte

No autumn recipe roundup is complete without a pumpkin spice latte. Skip the coffee shop and make your own version at home, complete with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

17. Roasted Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Take your mashed potatoes to the next level with the rich and savory flavors of roasted garlic. Creamy, buttery, and with a hint of garlic, these mashed potatoes are a true crowd-pleaser.

18. Maple Glazed Ham

For those who prefer ham over turkey, this maple-glazed ham is a showstopper. The sweet and smoky glaze adds a wonderful depth of flavor to the succulent ham.

19. Apple Cinnamon Crumble Bars

Enjoy the flavors of apple pie in a convenient and portable form with these apple cinnamon crumble bars. With a buttery crust, sweet apple filling, and a crunchy crumble topping, they’re perfect for snacking or dessert.

20. Pumpkin Spice Pancakes

Start your Thanksgiving morning with a stack of fluffy pumpkin spice pancakes. Topped with maple syrup and a dollop of whipped cream, they’re a delicious way to kick off the holiday festivities.

These 20 autumn harvest recipes are just a taste of the culinary delights that await you this Thanksgiving and throughout the autumn season. Whether you’re hosting a grand feast or enjoying a cozy meal at home, these dishes will bring warmth, flavor, and joy to your table. So gather your loved ones, embrace the spirit of the season, and savor the delicious flavors of autumn. Happy Thanksgiving and happy cooking!
