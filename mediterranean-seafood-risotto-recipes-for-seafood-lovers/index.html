Mediterranean Seafood Risotto Recipes for Seafood Lovers


If you’re a seafood lover, you’re in for a treat with these delectable Mediterranean seafood risotto recipes. The combination of creamy risotto and fresh seafood, infused with Mediterranean flavors, creates a mouthwatering experience that’s perfect for a special dinner or a gathering with friends and family. Let’s dive into the world of Mediterranean flavors and tantalizing seafood!

Classic Mediterranean Seafood Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups seafood or chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 pound mixed seafood (shrimp, scallops, mussels)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until translucent.
  2. Add the Arborio rice to the pan, stirring to coat the grains with the oil. Toast the rice for a couple of minutes until it becomes slightly translucent.
  3. Pour in the white wine and allow it to evaporate, infusing the rice with its flavor.
  4. Begin adding the seafood or chicken broth, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously and allowing the rice to absorb the broth before adding more.
  5. When the rice is almost cooked, add the mixed seafood to the pan, along with the cherry tomatoes and fresh basil. Continue stirring gently until the seafood is cooked through and the rice is creamy.
  6. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve the Mediterranean seafood risotto hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups seafood or chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until fragrant.
  2. Add the Arborio rice to the pot, stirring to coat the grains with the oil. Toast the rice for a couple of minutes until it becomes slightly translucent.
  3. Pour in the white wine and let it simmer until it has been absorbed by the rice.
  4. Begin adding the seafood or chicken broth, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously and allowing the rice to absorb the broth before adding more.
  5. In a separate pan, heat some olive oil and cook the shrimp until they turn pink and opaque.
  6. When the rice is almost cooked, stir in the cooked shrimp, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Continue cooking until the rice is creamy and the shrimp are heated through.
  7. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish the lemon garlic shrimp risotto with freshly chopped parsley before serving.

Tips for Perfect Mediterranean Seafood Risotto

  • Use high-quality Arborio rice for the creamiest texture.
  • Maintain a gentle simmer while adding broth to the rice, allowing it to absorb the liquid gradually.
  • Experiment with different seafood combinations to create your own unique version of Mediterranean seafood risotto.
  • Fresh herbs and citrus zest add brightness and depth of flavor to the risotto.

Whether you choose the classic Mediterranean seafood risotto or the zesty lemon garlic shrimp risotto, these recipes are sure to impress seafood lovers with their rich flavors and creamy textures. Elevate your culinary skills and treat yourself to a taste of the Mediterranean with these delightful seafood risotto recipes!
