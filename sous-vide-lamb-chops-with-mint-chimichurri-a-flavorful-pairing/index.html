Sous Vide Lamb Chops with Mint Chimichurri: A Flavorful Pairing


Are you ready to take your culinary skills to the next level? Look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for Sous Vide Lamb Chops with Mint Chimichurri. This flavorful pairing is sure to impress your guests and leave them craving for more. So, let’s dive right into it!

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Mint Chimichurri:

  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup fresh parsley leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 shallot
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 130°F (54°C). This temperature will result in medium-rare lamb chops that are tender and juicy.

  2. Season the lamb chops generously with salt and pepper. This will enhance the flavors as they cook.

  3. Place the seasoned lamb chops in a vacuum-sealed bag, drizzle with olive oil, and seal the bag using a vacuum sealer or the water displacement method.

  4. Submerge the sealed bag in the preheated water bath and cook for 2 hours. This slow cooking method will ensure that the lamb chops are evenly cooked and retain their moisture.

  5. While the lamb chops are cooking, let’s prepare the mint chimichurri. In a food processor, combine the mint leaves, parsley leaves, garlic, shallot, red wine vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Pulse until the ingredients are well combined but still slightly chunky.

  6. Once the lamb chops have finished cooking, remove them from the water bath and pat them dry using a paper towel. This step will help achieve a nice sear on the outside.

  7. Preheat a grill or skillet over high heat. Sear the lamb chops for 1-2 minutes on each side until they develop a golden brown crust. This will add a delicious charred flavor to the dish.

  8. Remove the lamb chops from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes. This rest period allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more tender and flavorful final dish.

  9. Serve the sous vide lamb chops with a generous drizzle of the mint chimichurri sauce on top. The refreshing combination of mint, parsley, and garlic will complement the rich flavors of the lamb perfectly.

Tips and Variations

  • For a more intense flavor, you can marinate the lamb chops in the mint chimichurri sauce for a few hours before cooking them sous vide.

  • If you don’t have a sous vide machine, you can still achieve delicious results by using a digital thermometer to monitor the internal temperature of the lamb chops while cooking them in the oven. Aim for an internal temperature of 130°F (54°C) for medium-rare.

  • Feel free to customize the chimichurri sauce by adding other herbs or spices like cilantro, oregano, or crushed red pepper flakes. Make it your own!

  • This recipe pairs well with roasted vegetables, creamy mashed potatoes, or a light and refreshing salad.

Now that you have the recipe for Sous Vide Lamb Chops with Mint Chimichurri, it’s time to unleash your culinary prowess and create a dish that will leave everyone in awe. Enjoy the tender, juicy lamb chops and the vibrant flavors of the mint chimichurri. Bon appétit!
