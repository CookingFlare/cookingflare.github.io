Savory Low Carb Veggie Packed Quiche

4 min · 685 words

If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy dish that’s perfect for any time of the day, then you’ve come to the right place. This savory low carb veggie packed quiche is a fantastic way to enjoy a flavorful meal while keeping your carb intake in check. Packed with nutritious vegetables and mouthwatering flavors, this quiche is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or a blend)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • In a bowl, mix the almond flour, melted butter, and salt until well combined.
    • Press the mixture into a 9-inch pie dish, covering the bottom and sides evenly.
    • Bake the crust for 10-12 minutes, or until lightly golden. Set aside to cool.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add the diced onion and cook until translucent.
    • Add the spinach, cherry tomatoes, mushrooms, and bell pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
    • Remove the skillet from the heat and let the vegetables cool slightly.

  3. Assemble the Quiche:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, garlic powder, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
    • Spread the cooked vegetables evenly over the pre-baked crust.
    • Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the vegetables.
    • Carefully pour the egg mixture over the vegetables and cheese.

  4. Bake the Quiche:

    • Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the center is set and the top is golden brown.
    • Remove the quiche from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This savory low carb veggie packed quiche can be enjoyed hot, warm, or even at room temperature. It’s a versatile dish that can be served for breakfast, brunch, lunch, or dinner. Pair it with a fresh green salad, roasted vegetables, or a side of fresh fruit for a complete and satisfying meal.

  • Saturated Fat: 12g
  • Cholesterol: 230mg
  • Sodium: 320mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 15g

Conclusion

This savory low carb veggie packed quiche is not only delicious, but it’s also a great way to incorporate an array of nutrient-packed vegetables into your diet. Whether you’re following a low carb lifestyle or simply looking for a flavorful and satisfying meal, this quiche is a winner. Experiment with different vegetable combinations and cheese options to create your own signature quiche. Enjoy the goodness of vegetables in every bite with this delightful and easy-to-make dish!
