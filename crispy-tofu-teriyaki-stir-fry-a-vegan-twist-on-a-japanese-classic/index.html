Crispy Tofu Teriyaki Stir Fry: A Vegan Twist on a Japanese Classic


If you’re a fan of Japanese cuisine but are looking for a vegan twist, then you’re in for a treat with this Crispy Tofu Teriyaki Stir Fry recipe. This dish combines the umami flavors of teriyaki sauce with the satisfying crunch of crispy tofu, all mixed with a colorful array of stir-fried vegetables. It’s a delightful dish that’s not only delicious but also packed with nutrients.

Ingredients

For the Crispy Tofu:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

For the Teriyaki Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

For the Stir Fry:

  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crispy Tofu:

    • In a bowl, combine the cornstarch, soy sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Toss the cubed tofu in the mixture until well coated.
    • Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until golden brown and crispy on all sides. Remove from the pan and set aside.

  2. Make the Teriyaki Sauce:

    • In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, mirin, water, maple syrup, cornstarch, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook until the sauce thickens.

  3. Stir Fry the Vegetables:

    • In the same pan used for the tofu, add a little more oil if needed. Sauté the bell pepper, broccoli, carrot, and snap peas until they are tender-crisp.

  4. Combine and Serve:

    • Add the crispy tofu back into the pan with the vegetables.
    • Pour the teriyaki sauce over the tofu and vegetables, tossing gently to coat everything in the sauce.
    • Serve the crispy tofu teriyaki stir fry over cooked rice or noodles, and garnish with chopped green onions.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the stir fry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences or what you have on hand. Mushrooms, zucchini, or snow peas would all be great additions.
  • If you’re not a fan of tofu, you can substitute it with tempeh or seitan for a different texture and flavor.

The Vegan Twist

This vegan version of the classic teriyaki stir fry offers a healthier and cruelty-free take on a beloved Japanese dish. Tofu, the star of this recipe, provides a good source of plant-based protein and is an excellent canvas for absorbing the flavors of the teriyaki sauce. The colorful assortment of vegetables not only adds vibrancy to the dish but also contributes various vitamins and minerals, making it a well-rounded meal.

Final Thoughts

Crispy Tofu Teriyaki Stir Fry is a delightful way to enjoy the flavors of Japan in a vegan-friendly dish. Whether you’re a dedicated vegan, someone looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, or simply a lover of Japanese cuisine, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds. It’s a perfect balance of savory, sweet, and umami flavors, all coming together in a colorful and satisfying stir fry.

So, the next time you’re craving Japanese flavors with a vegan twist, give this Crispy Tofu Teriyaki Stir Fry a try and indulge in a healthier, compassionate, and utterly delicious meal.
