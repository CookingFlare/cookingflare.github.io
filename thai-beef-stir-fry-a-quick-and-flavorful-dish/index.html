Thai Beef Stir-Fry: A Quick and Flavorful Dish


Thai cuisine is known for its bold flavors, aromatic herbs, and vibrant colors, and this Thai Beef Stir-Fry recipe is no exception. With tender slices of beef, crisp vegetables, and a savory-sweet sauce, this dish is a perfect balance of textures and flavors. Plus, it’s quick and easy to make, making it an ideal choice for a busy weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with friends. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this delicious Thai Beef Stir-Fry in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef steak (such as sirloin or flank), thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 cup of sugar snap peas, trimmed
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, cornstarch, and water. Set the sauce aside.

  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat. Add the thinly sliced beef to the wok in a single layer and let it sear for 1-2 minutes without stirring to develop a nice caramelization. Then, stir-fry the beef for another 1-2 minutes until it’s just cooked through. Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside.

  3. In the same wok, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and sliced onion, and stir-fry for about a minute until fragrant.

  4. Add the red bell pepper, sugar snap peas, and carrot to the wok, and continue to stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the wok and pour the prepared sauce over the beef and vegetables. Toss everything together until the sauce evenly coats the beef and vegetables, and the mixture is heated through.

  6. Remove the wok from the heat and garnish the Thai Beef Stir-Fry with fresh cilantro leaves.

  7. Serve the flavorful Thai Beef Stir-Fry over steamed jasmine rice and enjoy the burst of flavors in every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add sliced red chilies or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes to the stir-fry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this dish based on your preferences. Broccoli, snow peas, or baby corn would all be great additions or substitutions.
  • To make this dish gluten-free, use tamari instead of soy sauce and ensure the oyster sauce is gluten-free.

Conclusion

This Thai Beef Stir-Fry recipe is a wonderful way to bring the vibrant and enticing flavors of Thai cuisine into your home kitchen. With its tender beef, crisp vegetables, and a luscious savory sauce, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. It’s a quick and flavorful option for those busy days when you crave something delicious and satisfying. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your wok, and treat yourself to a delightful Thai Beef Stir-Fry experience.

Whether you’re new to Thai cooking or a seasoned pro, this recipe is a great addition to your culinary arsenal. Enjoy the process of creating this dish, and savor every mouthful of its delightful flavors. Happy cooking!
