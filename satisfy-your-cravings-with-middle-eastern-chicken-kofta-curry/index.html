Satisfy Your Cravings with Middle Eastern Chicken Kofta Curry


Are you in the mood for a flavorful and satisfying meal? Look no further than Middle Eastern Chicken Kofta Curry! This delicious dish combines tender chicken koftas with a rich and aromatic curry sauce. It’s the perfect recipe to satisfy your cravings for bold flavors and exotic spices.

Ingredients

For the chicken koftas:

  • 500g ground chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the curry sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground chicken, chopped onion, minced garlic, parsley, cilantro, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Take a small portion of the chicken mixture and shape it into a small cylindrical kofta. Repeat until all the mixture is used. You should have around 12 koftas.

  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another minute.

  4. Add the tomato paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground turmeric, ground cinnamon, and cayenne pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine the spices with the onion mixture.

  5. Pour in the coconut milk and bring the sauce to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and let it cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Gently place the chicken koftas into the simmering sauce, making sure they are fully submerged. Cover the skillet and let the koftas cook for 15-20 minutes until they are cooked through.

  7. Once the koftas are cooked, remove the skillet from the heat and let it cool slightly. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

  8. Serve the Middle Eastern Chicken Kofta Curry with steamed basmati rice or warm naan bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

With its tantalizing blend of spices and tender chicken koftas, Middle Eastern Chicken Kofta Curry is a dish that will surely satisfy your cravings for bold flavors and exotic cuisine. The rich and aromatic curry sauce complements the succulent koftas perfectly, creating a harmonious and memorable dining experience. Whether you’re a fan of Middle Eastern cuisine or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is guaranteed to impress. So why wait? Gather your ingredients and embark on a flavorful journey to the Middle East with this delicious chicken kofta curry!
