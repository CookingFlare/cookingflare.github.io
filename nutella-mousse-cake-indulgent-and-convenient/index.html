Nutella Mousse Cake: Indulgent and Convenient


Are you craving a delightful and delicious dessert but don’t want to spend hours slaving away in the kitchen? Fear not because today I invite you to indulge in the world of Nutella Mousse Cake – a heavenly combination of smooth, creamy mousse with a velvety, chocolatey base and Nutella swirl that will have you swooning with every spoonful. So, let’s get started with this easy-to-follow recipe and create something truly magical in the comfort of your own home. It’s indulgent, elegant, and oh-so-convenient!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup digestive biscuits, finely crushed
  • 150 grams butter, melted
  • 500 grams Nutella
  • 500 ml heavy cream
  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

Base:

  1. Begin by preparing the base of your cake. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the crushed digestive biscuits and melted butter until well mixed.
  2. Using a 9" springform cake pan, press the mixture evenly into the bottom and set aside. While it’s impossible to underplay the importance of a good base, this simple yet delicious one is the perfect canvas for the Nutella mousse to shine.

Nutella Mousse:

  1. In a separate mixing bowl, add the Nutella and slightly warm it up in the microwave for about 30 seconds. This makes it easier to mix until smooth and creamy.
  2. Now, whip the heavy cream in a large bowl until it forms stiff peaks. To achieve the perfect mousse-like consistency, gently blend in about 1/3 of the whipped cream into the Nutella mixture to lighten it up. Followed by carefully folding in the remaining cream.
  3. For the egg whites, make sure that your bowl and whisk are completely grease-free to avoid any separation. Beat the egg whites with salt and sugar until soft peaks form. Slowly add in the vanilla extract and continue to beat until the egg whites are stiff but not dry.
  4. To create our elegant Nutella mousse, gently fold in a third of the egg white mixture into the Nutella and cream mixture. And then follow up by carefully folding in the remaining egg whites until fully incorporated. Take your time with this step to avoid deflating the mixture.

Creating the Cake:

  1. Gently pour the Nutella mousse onto the base and tap the pan lightly to release any air bubbles. Smooth out the top of the mousse with a spatula and sprinkle with a handful of crushed digestive biscuits (optional) for some added crunch.
  2. Wrap the cake tin’s bottom with aluminum foil, ensuring it reaches about an inch up the sides. This prevents any leaks during the baking process, and you’ll thank yourself later.
  3. There’s no need to bake this cake in a preheated oven, simply placing it in the refrigerator for about 4-6 hours or overnight will give it that perfect, firm-to-the-touch texture.

Serving:

  1. Once your Nutella mousse cake has set, it’s time to reveal the masterpiece you’ve created! Run a knife around the edge of the cake to loosen it, then gently remove the springform pan’s side.
  2. Serve your cake chilled with a drizzle of Nutella, a dollop of whipped cream, or a handful of fresh berries for a touch of color and added sweetness.

And there you have it, a heavenly Nutella Mousse Cake that not only satisfies your craving but also brings oohs and aahs from your dinner guests. It’s a recipe anyone can tackle in their own kitchen with the minimal effort to yield the maximum indulgence. So what are you waiting for?
