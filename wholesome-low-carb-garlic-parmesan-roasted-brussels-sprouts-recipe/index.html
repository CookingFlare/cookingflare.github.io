Wholesome Low Carb Garlic Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy side dish to accompany your meals? Look no further! This Wholesome Low Carb Garlic Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts recipe is the perfect combination of flavor and nutrition. Brussels sprouts are not only low in carbs but also packed with vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants, making them an excellent choice for a healthy diet.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the trimmed and halved Brussels sprouts with olive oil, minced garlic, and a generous sprinkle of salt and pepper. Toss until the Brussels sprouts are evenly coated with the oil and seasonings.

  3. Spread the Brussels sprouts in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet, ensuring that they have some space between them. This allows them to roast evenly and develop a delicious caramelized exterior.

  4. Roast the Brussels sprouts in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until they are tender on the inside and crispy on the outside. Be sure to toss them halfway through the roasting time to ensure even cooking.

  5. Once the Brussels sprouts are roasted to perfection, remove them from the oven and transfer them back to the mixing bowl.

  6. While the Brussels sprouts are still warm, sprinkle them with the grated Parmesan cheese and gently toss to coat them evenly. The residual heat from the sprouts will melt the cheese slightly, creating a savory and nutty flavor that complements the roasted garlic.

  7. Transfer the Garlic Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts to a serving dish and garnish with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious, wholesome flavors!

Nutritional Benefits:

Brussels sprouts are a nutritional powerhouse, providing a rich source of vitamin C, vitamin K, fiber, and antioxidants. The addition of olive oil contributes heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, while garlic offers immune-boosting properties. The Parmesan cheese not only enhances the flavor but also provides a good dose of calcium and protein.

Serving Suggestions:

This delightful side dish pairs well with a variety of main courses. Serve it alongside roasted chicken, grilled salmon, or even as part of a vegetarian bowl. The crispy, savory Brussels sprouts are sure to be a hit at any meal.

Conclusion:

With its delightful combination of flavors, nutritional benefits, and ease of preparation, this Wholesome Low Carb Garlic Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts recipe is a must-try for anyone seeking a healthy and delicious addition to their meals. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress and satisfy.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the wholesome goodness of this delectable Brussels sprouts recipe!
