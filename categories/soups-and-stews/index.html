Creamy Avocado and Lime Soup: A Refreshing and Healthy Choice

Avocados are not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients. They are a great source of healthy fats, fiber, and vitamins. One fantastic way to incorporate avocados into your diet is by making a creamy and refreshing avocado and lime soup. This soup is not only easy to make but also incredibly delicious. So, let’s get started! Ingredients 2 ripe avocados 2 limes 1 small onion, finely chopped 2 cloves of garlic, minced 2 cups vegetable broth 1 cup plain Greek yogurt Fresh cilantro, for garnish Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Start by scooping out the flesh of the avocados into a blender or food processor. Squeeze the juice of the limes and add it to the blender. In a medium-sized pot, sauté the finely chopped onion and minced garlic until they become translucent and fragrant. Add the sautéed onion and garlic to the blender with the avocado and lime juice. Pour in the vegetable broth and blend until smooth and creamy. Transfer the mixture back to the pot and heat it over medium heat. Once heated through, remove the pot from the heat and stir in the Greek yogurt. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the creamy avocado and lime soup hot or chilled, depending on your preference. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor.
