Tuscan Ribeye Steak with Rosemary Potatoes: A Satisfying Italian Meal


There’s something undeniably satisfying about a perfectly cooked ribeye steak. Add in the flavors of Tuscany and a side of crispy rosemary potatoes, and you’ve got yourself a truly indulgent Italian meal that’s sure to impress. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of preparing a succulent Tuscan ribeye steak paired with fragrant rosemary potatoes. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a delicious homemade meal, this dish is a definite winner.

Ingredients

For the Ribeye Steak:

  • 2 16-ounce ribeye steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Rosemary Potatoes:

  • 4 large potatoes, washed and diced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Prepare the Ribeye Steak:

  1. Preheat the Grill: Brush the ribeye steaks with olive oil and season generously with minced garlic, fresh rosemary, salt, and pepper. Allow the steaks to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill the Steaks: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Place the marinated ribeye steaks on the grill and cook for approximately 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time to your desired level of doneness.
  3. Rest and Serve: Once cooked to perfection, remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for 5 minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a tender and flavorful steak.

Prepare the Rosemary Potatoes:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Toss the Potatoes: In a large bowl, toss the diced potatoes with olive oil, chopped rosemary, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  3. Bake the Potatoes: Spread the seasoned potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy, turning them halfway through the cooking time for even browning.

Serving Suggestion

Once the ribeye steaks are sliced and the rosemary potatoes are golden and crispy, plate them together for a stunning presentation. The rich, juicy steak paired with the aromatic and crispy potatoes creates a harmony of flavors and textures that will tantalize your taste buds. Pair this dish with a glass of your favorite Italian red wine, and you’ve got yourself a complete Tuscan dining experience right in your own home.

Final Thoughts

The Tuscan ribeye steak with rosemary potatoes is a dish that celebrates the robust and comforting flavors of Italian cuisine. The simplicity of the ingredients allows the natural flavors to shine, making it a perfect choice for both special occasions and casual gatherings. From the juicy tenderness of the steak to the crispy perfection of the potatoes, this meal is a true testament to the joy of cooking and savoring delicious food.

So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and get ready to savor the delightful flavors of Tuscany with this impressive yet approachable recipe. Buon appetito!
