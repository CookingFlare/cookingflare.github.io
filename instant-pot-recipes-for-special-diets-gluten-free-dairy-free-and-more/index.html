Instant Pot Recipes for Special Diets: Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free, and More


The Instant Pot has revolutionized modern cooking, making meal preparation faster and more convenient than ever. But beyond the time saved, this versatile kitchen appliance can also help us easily adapt recipes for specific dietary needs. Whether you’re gluten-free, dairy-free, or adhering to another special diet, the Instant Pot can be your best ally in creating delicious, satisfying meals that align with your health goals. In this guide, we’ll explore a variety of recipes tailored to these dietary preferences, along with tips and tricks to get the most out of your Instant Pot.

Why the Instant Pot is Perfect for Special Diets

One of the reasons the Instant Pot is a game-changer for those on special diets is its ability to lock in flavor while maintaining the integrity of your ingredients. Because it uses pressure cooking, steaming, sautéing, and more, you can create dishes that are healthier by default, skipping unnecessary additives or preservatives often found in pre-packaged foods. Beyond versatility, the Instant Pot reduces the hassle of watching over the stove, so you can focus on exploring new recipes that align with your dietary restrictions.

When you’re dealing with special diets like gluten-free or dairy-free, finding substitutes and creating flavors that emulate traditional dishes can often be time-consuming. The Instant Pot simplifies this process, letting you confidently prepare meals that nourish and delight without compromise. Let’s dive into some mouthwatering recipes that prove you don’t have to sacrifice flavor for dietary compliance.

Gluten-Free Instant Pot Recipes

1. Hearty Chicken and Wild Rice Soup

This gluten-free, comforting meal is perfect for a cozy lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup wild rice (confirm it’s gluten-free!)
  • 2 cups unsweetened chicken broth
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper

Instructions:

  1. Turn your Instant Pot to the sauté mode. Add a drizzle of olive oil, onions, and garlic. Cook until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken breast and sauté lightly for 3-4 minutes, just until the edges begin to brown.
  3. Combine the wild rice, carrots, celery, thyme, salt, and pepper into the pot. Pour in the chicken broth.
  4. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Pressure Cook” mode on high for 25 minutes.
  5. Once the timer goes off, allow a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before quick-releasing any remaining pressure.
  6. Stir in the coconut milk and taste for seasoning. The soup should be creamy and flavorful.

This gluten-free soup is filling, nutritious, and packed with protein and wholesome veggies.

2. Beef and Broccoli Stir-fry

Skip the takeout and indulge in a homemade, gluten-free classic.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup gluten-free soy sauce or tamari
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch or arrowroot powder
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Cooked rice or cauliflower rice (optional, for serving)

Instructions:

  1. On sauté mode, brown the sliced beef in sesame oil until both sides are lightly browned. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, ginger, honey, rice vinegar, and cornstarch. Add the mix to the Instant Pot along with the minced garlic.
  3. Return the beef to the pot and stir well. Close the lid, set the Instant Pot to “Pressure Cook” on high, and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Manually release the pressure. Stir in broccoli florets, close the lid again, and set to “Steam” mode for 3-5 minutes until the broccoli is tender.
  5. Serve over rice or a low-carb option like cauliflower rice.

Dairy-Free Instant Pot Recipes

3. Coconut Curry Lentils

This vegan and dairy-free recipe is packed with bold flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils (red or green)
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn on the sauté mode and add a small amount of oil, onions, and garlic. Cook until fragrant.
  2. Add curry powder, turmeric, and cumin, stirring well to bloom the spices.
  3. Pour in the lentils, vegetable broth, and tomatoes. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Seal the lid and set to “Pressure Cook” on high for 15 minutes.
  5. Once finished, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 10 minutes before quick-releasing any remaining pressure.
  6. Stir in coconut milk, adjust seasoning if needed, and serve topped with fresh cilantro.

4. Dairy-Free Mashed Potatoes

These creamy mashed potatoes are so good, you won’t even miss the butter.

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Add the peeled potatoes and enough water to cover them into the Instant Pot. Cook on “Pressure Cook” mode for 10 minutes.
  2. Quick-release the pressure and drain the potatoes.
  3. Return the potatoes to the pot and mash directly with almond milk, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Serve hot with a sprinkle of parsley on top.

Versatile Instant Pot Recipes for Other Special Diets

5. Low-Carb Cauliflower Risotto

Ideal for keto or low-carb lifestyles, this creamy risotto swaps rice for cauliflower.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cauliflower rice (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • ½ cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot on sauté mode. Add olive oil, onions, and garlic, cooking until soft.
  2. Add cauliflower rice, seasoning, and vegetable broth. Stir well and cancel sauté mode.
  3. Seal the lid and set to “Pressure Cook” on high for 3 minutes.
  4. Release the pressure manually and stir in nutritional yeast for a cheesy, dairy-free finish.

6. Paleo-Friendly Braised Short Ribs

Perfect for paleo practitioners, these ribs are bursting with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds short ribs
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Rub the short ribs with smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. On the sauté mode, sear the ribs on all sides until browned. Set aside.
  3. Add onions and garlic to the pot. Once softened, deglaze with apple cider vinegar.
  4. Add beef broth and return the ribs to the pot.
  5. Set to “Pressure Cook” on high for 35 minutes, then allow a natural release for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve alone or with steamed vegetables for a wholesome, paleo-friendly meal.

Tips for Cooking Special Diets in the Instant Pot

  1. Meal Prepping Made Easy: Batch-cook staples like rice, beans, or gluten-free pasta in your Instant Pot for the week.
  2. Mind the Labels: Always check product labels to ensure they comply with dietary restrictions.
  3. Use Fresh Ingredients: The Instant Pot enhances the natural flavors of foods, so fresh ingredients make a huge difference.
  4. Experiment with Substitutes: Coconut milk, almond flour, and tamari are just a few staple substitutes to have on hand for various diets.

With these Instant Pot recipes in your arsenal, accommodating gluten-free, dairy-free, or other dietary preferences doesn’t have to feel like a challenge. Explore, experiment, and savor the flavors of meals designed to fit your lifestyle while offering maximum taste and nutrition.
