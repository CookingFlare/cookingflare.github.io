Quick and Easy Mediterranean Baked Ziti


Are you tired of the same old pasta recipes? Do you want to add a taste of the Mediterranean to your dinner table? Look no further than this quick and easy Mediterranean Baked Ziti recipe. This delightful dish is a perfect blend of Mediterranean flavors, perfectly balanced between tangy, sweet, salty, and savory. The Perfect Combination of Flavors Mediterranean cuisine is all about combining simple, fresh ingredients to create complex and delicious flavors. In this recipe, we're using a combination of classic Mediterranean ingredients such as feta cheese, kalamata olives, artichoke hearts, and sun-dried tomatoes to create a flavor profile that's both familiar and exciting. The Recipe Here's what you'll need:

1 pound ziti pasta 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup artichoke hearts, canned 1 cup kalamata olives, pitted 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste

Now, let's get started! Cooking the Pasta Start by boiling a large pot of salted water and cooking the ziti pasta according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta water before draining the ziti. Making the Sauce While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until it's translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, until fragrant. Add the artichoke hearts, kalamata olives, sun-dried tomatoes, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, until the vegetables are tender. Stirring in the Cheese Add the crumbled feta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and reserved pasta water to the skillet. Stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy. Assembling the Baked Ziti In a large bowl, combine the cooked ziti pasta and the Mediterranean sauce. Mix until the pasta is well coated. Transfer the pasta mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with additional feta cheese and grated Parmesan cheese. Baking to Perfection Bake the Mediterranean Baked Ziti in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 20-25 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Enjoy Your Delicious Dish! This Quick and Easy Mediterranean Baked Ziti recipe is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. It's a versatile dish that's sure to impress your family and friends. Simply serve with a side salad or garlic bread and enjoy the incredible Mediterranean flavors! Tips and Variations

Use fresh vegetables: Substitute the canned artichoke hearts with fresh artichokes and the kalamata olives with fresh olives for a more authentic Mediterranean flavor. Add protein: Add cooked chicken or ground beef to the Mediterranean sauce for a hearty twist. Spice it up: Add a pinch of red pepper flakes to give the dish a spicy kick.

Conclusion In conclusion, this Quick and Easy Mediterranean Baked Ziti recipe is a delicious and refreshing twist on traditional pasta dishes. With its combination of tangy, sweet, salty, and savory flavors, it's sure to become a new favorite at your dinner table. So, go ahead and give it a try – your taste buds will thank you!
