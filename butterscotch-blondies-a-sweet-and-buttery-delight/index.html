Butterscotch Blondies: A Sweet and Buttery Delight


If you’re a fan of rich, buttery desserts, then butterscotch blondies are a must-try treat for you. These delightful bars are a perfect blend of sweetness and buttery flavor, making them a favorite for both kids and adults. Whether you’re looking for a simple dessert to enjoy with your afternoon tea or a crowd-pleasing treat for a special occasion, butterscotch blondies are sure to impress. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of creating these delectable blondies from scratch, ensuring that you achieve the perfect balance of sweetness, chewiness, and butterscotch flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butterscotch chips
  • Optional: 1/2 cup chopped pecans or walnuts for added crunch

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat and Prepare

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Grease and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

Step 2: Mix the Wet Ingredients

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and packed brown sugar. Stir until well combined.
  • Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  • Stir in the vanilla extract until the mixture is smooth and glossy.

Step 3: Incorporate the Dry Ingredients

  • In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

Step 4: Add Butterscotch and Nuts

  • Gently fold in the butterscotch chips until evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  • If desired, fold in the chopped nuts for an extra layer of texture and flavor.

Step 5: Bake to Perfection

  • Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it out evenly with a spatula.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

Step 6: Cool and Serve

  • Allow the butterscotch blondies to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.
  • Once cooled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the blondies out of the pan and transfer them to a cutting board.
  • Cut into squares or bars of your desired size and serve.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, drizzle melted butterscotch or caramel sauce over the cooled blondies before serving.
  • Experiment with different mix-ins such as chocolate chips, toffee bits, or dried fruit to customize the blondies to your liking.
  • Store any leftover blondies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3-4 days, or freeze for longer preservation.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out in the kitchen, this butterscotch blondies recipe is a delightful way to satisfy your sweet tooth. The rich butterscotch flavor combined with the chewy texture of the blondies makes for an irresistible treat that’s perfect for sharing with family and friends. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the sweet and buttery delight of homemade butterscotch blondies!
