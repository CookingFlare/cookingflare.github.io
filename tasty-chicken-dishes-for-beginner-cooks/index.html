Tasty Chicken Dishes for Beginner Cooks


Cooking chicken can be both exciting and intimidating, especially for beginner cooks. The good news is that there are plenty of delicious and easy chicken dishes that even the most novice cook can master. In this blog post, we will explore some tasty chicken recipes that are perfect for those just starting their culinary journey.

1. Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken

Roasting a whole chicken may seem like a daunting task, but this lemon garlic roasted chicken recipe is foolproof and bursting with flavor. To prepare this dish, you will need:

  • One whole chicken
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

To start, preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Place the chicken in a roasting pan and rub the lemon garlic mixture all over the chicken, ensuring it is evenly coated. Roast the chicken for about 1 hour or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Let it rest for a few minutes before carving. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!

2. Chicken Stir-Fry

Stir-frying is a quick and easy cooking method that can result in a flavorful and nutritious meal. For a simple chicken stir-fry, gather the following ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

In a bowl, marinate the sliced chicken breasts with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil for 15 minutes. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir-fry until cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside. In the same pan, add the bell pepper, carrot, broccoli, and minced garlic. Stir-fry for about 3 minutes or until the vegetables are crisp-tender. Return the chicken to the pan and season with salt and pepper. Stir-fry for an additional minute and serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

3. Baked Parmesan Crusted Chicken

If you’re craving a crispy and flavorful chicken dish, this baked Parmesan crusted chicken recipe is perfect for you. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). In a shallow dish, combine the grated Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Dip each chicken breast into the beaten eggs, then coat it with the Parmesan mixture, pressing gently to adhere. Place the coated chicken breasts on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the crust is golden brown. Serve with a side salad or roasted vegetables for a complete meal.

Conclusion

Chicken is a versatile and delicious protein that can be prepared in countless ways. These three recipes are perfect for beginner cooks looking to explore the world of chicken dishes. Whether you choose to roast, stir-fry, or bake, these recipes are sure to impress your taste buds and boost your confidence in the kitchen. So grab your apron, prepare your ingredients, and get ready to create some tasty chicken dishes that will leave you wanting more!
