One-Pot Wonder: Easy Chicken Alfredo for Beginners


If you’re a fan of creamy pasta dishes but dread the thought of a sink full of dishes, then this one-pot chicken alfredo recipe is about to become your new best friend. Not only is it incredibly delicious, but it’s also a breeze to make and requires minimal cleanup. Perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends, this easy chicken alfredo recipe is sure to become a staple in your cooking repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Chicken: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are golden brown on all sides, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. Sauté the Garlic: In the same pot, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds, or until fragrant.

  3. Cook the Pasta: Add the fettuccine pasta to the pot, followed by the chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook the pasta, stirring occasionally, until it is al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed, about 12-15 minutes.

  4. Add the Chicken and Cream: Once the pasta is cooked, return the seared chicken to the pot. Pour in the heavy cream and stir everything together. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

  5. Finish with Parmesan: Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it is fully melted and the sauce is smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Remove the pot from the heat and let the chicken alfredo stand for a few minutes to thicken further. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Tips for Success

  • Use a wide pot or Dutch oven to ensure the pasta cooks evenly and absorbs the flavors of the dish.
  • For a flavor boost, consider adding a pinch of nutmeg or a dash of red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • If you’re looking to add some greens to the dish, toss in a handful of spinach leaves just before serving.

Final Thoughts

This one-pot chicken alfredo recipe is a game-changer for anyone who loves indulgent pasta dishes but doesn’t want the hassle of multiple pots and pans. With its rich and creamy sauce, tender bites of chicken, and perfectly cooked fettuccine, this dish is a true crowd-pleaser. Plus, the fact that it all comes together in a single pot means that cleanup is a breeze. Whether you’re a beginner in the kitchen or a seasoned home cook looking for a fuss-free meal, this easy chicken alfredo is sure to satisfy your cravings with minimal effort.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting and satisfying meal, give this one-pot chicken alfredo recipe a try. It’s a delicious and convenient way to elevate your pasta game without the extra cleanup. Happy cooking!
