Coconut Mango Rice Pudding: A Tropical Dessert Delight


If you’re searching for a dessert that brings the flavors of the tropics right to your table, look no further than this delightful Coconut Mango Rice Pudding recipe. This creamy, aromatic dessert is a perfect fusion of sweet mango, creamy coconut, and comforting rice. Whether you’re hosting a summer get-together or simply craving a taste of the exotic, this recipe is sure to transport your taste buds to a sun-soaked paradise.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut (optional, for garnish)
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

Step 1: Cooking the Rice

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the jasmine rice and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed.

Step 2: Preparing the Coconut Mango Mixture

  1. In a separate saucepan, combine the coconut milk, sugar, diced mango, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally.

Step 3: Combining the Rice and Coconut Mango Mixture

  1. Once the rice is cooked, add it to the coconut mango mixture.
  2. Stir gently to combine, allowing the rice to absorb the creamy coconut and mango flavors.

Step 4: Chilling and Serving

  1. Transfer the coconut mango rice pudding to a serving bowl or individual dessert cups.
  2. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to meld and the pudding to set.
  3. When ready to serve, garnish with a sprinkle of shredded coconut and a few fresh mint leaves for a pop of color and texture.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra layer of indulgence, consider adding a splash of coconut rum to the coconut mango mixture.
  • Experiment with different types of rice for varying textures and flavors. Basmati rice can lend a fragrant aroma, while short-grain rice offers a creamier consistency.
  • If fresh mango is not available, frozen mango chunks can be used as a convenient alternative.

The Taste of the Tropics

With its luscious blend of coconut, mango, and fragrant jasmine rice, Coconut Mango Rice Pudding is a dessert that captures the essence of a tropical paradise. The creamy texture and sweet, fruity flavors make it an irresistible treat for any occasion. Whether you’re savoring a leisurely summer evening or simply longing for a taste of the exotic, this delightful dessert is sure to transport you to a world of sun-drenched beaches and swaying palm trees. Enjoy the taste of the tropics with every spoonful of this Coconut Mango Rice Pudding!
