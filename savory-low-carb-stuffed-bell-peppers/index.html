Savory Low Carb Stuffed Bell Peppers


If you’re looking for a delicious, low-carb meal that’s both satisfying and easy to make, then you’ve come to the right place. These savory low carb stuffed bell peppers are not only a feast for the eyes but also a delight for your taste buds. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this recipe is perfect for anyone looking to enjoy a healthy, yet indulgent meal.

Ingredients

For the Stuffed Bell Peppers:

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

For the Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Bell Peppers:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the bell peppers in a baking dish.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground turkey until no longer pink. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent.
    • Stir in the cauliflower rice, diced tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
    • Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in half of the shredded mozzarella cheese.

  3. Stuff the Bell Peppers:

    • Spoon the turkey and cauliflower rice mixture into the prepared bell peppers, dividing it equally among them. Top each pepper with the remaining mozzarella cheese.

  4. Prepare the Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, mix together the crushed tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Pour the sauce over and around the stuffed bell peppers in the baking dish.

  5. Bake:

    • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Then, uncover the dish and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  6. Serve:

    • Once out of the oven, allow the stuffed bell peppers to cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Low Carb Goodness: By using cauliflower rice instead of traditional grains, these stuffed bell peppers are low in carbs and high in fiber, making them a perfect choice for anyone following a low-carb or keto lifestyle.
  • Packed with Protein: The ground turkey provides a lean source of protein, helping you feel satisfied and nourished.
  • Flavorful and Satisfying: The combination of herbs, tomatoes, and melted mozzarella cheese creates a mouthwatering experience that will have you coming back for more.

Whether you’re looking for a new way to enjoy bell peppers or simply want to add a healthy and delicious dish to your recipe repertoire, these savory low carb stuffed bell peppers are sure to become a favorite. Enjoy them for a cozy family dinner or impress your guests with this flavorful, colorful creation.
