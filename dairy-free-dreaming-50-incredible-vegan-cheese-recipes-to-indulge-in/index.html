Dairy-Free Dreaming: 50 Incredible Vegan Cheese Recipes to Indulge In


Are you ready to dive into a world of dairy-free delight? Say hello to your new favorite cookbook - 50 incredible vegan cheese recipes that will leave you craving more! These plant-based, dairy-free cheese recipes are not only easy to make but guaranteed to impress even the pickiest of cheese lovers.

As we embark on this dairy-free dreaming journey, let’s OSS (Oh So Satisfying) our taste buds with these 50 vegan cheese recipes. Unleash your inner culinary genius and create cheese galore from the safety of your own kitchen. We’ve got everything from delectable cheeses for spreading on crackers to to-die-for melty cheeses perfect for fancy grilled cheese sandwiches.

Let’s take a look at some truly mouthwatering vegan cheese recipes:

1. Vegan Cream Cheese

Just because you’re going dairy-free doesn’t mean you have to sacrifice that irresistible cream cheese taste. Thanks to this recipe, you can now indulge in a protein-packed, silky smooth vegan cream cheese. The only thing better than our Creamy Vegan Cream Cheese? The guilt-free factor! Made from cashews, lemon juice, and apple cider vinegar, this cream cheese is all the rage.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked for at least 4 hours and drained
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon apple sauce
  • Pinch of sea salt

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine all ingredients and process until completely smooth. This might take a while, but be patient!
  2. Transfer your dreamy vegan cream cheese mixture into a container, cover, and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  3. Voilà! Swoop up some crackers, spread that delight, and savor every second of it!

2. Smoked Gouda

Does the thought of Smoked Gouda bring a smile to your face? Us too! Luxuriate in the smoky flavors of this exceptional vegan Gouda alternative. The combination of walnuts and nutritional yeast creates the umami flavor our taste buds are loving.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw walnuts, soaked for at least 4 hours and drained
  • 2 tablespoons filtered water
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, blend all ingredients into a smooth, spreadable consistency. If too thick, thin with a little more filtered water or add more nutritional yeast if it’s too thin.
  2. Pop this luscious mixture into a container, chill, and wait patiently (just a few hours) for it to transform into a sliceable “cheese.”
  3. Grab some baguette slices for savoring this vegan Smoked Gouda masterpiece.

3. Vegan Camembert

I know, I know. Camembert is non-negotiable for many cheese-lovers. Allow us to innovate your world with our luscious, rich Vegan Camembert that ticks all the right boxes. Cashews and coconut oil whisk away the tension, while the tangy flavor comes from kombu seaweed.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews, soaked for 4 hours, and drained
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 tablespoon kombu seaweed (crumbled)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon agar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a high-speed blender or food processor, blend the cashews, coconut oil, seaweed, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, agar powder, sea salt, and black pepper until completely smooth.
  2. Pour this divine mixture into a lined 8-inch cake pan and refrigerate for 1 hour or until solid.
  3. At this point, your Vegan Camembert is ready to be sliced and enjoyed!

4. Vegan Blue Cheese

Indulge in a world where convincing vegan Blue Cheese exists. Caressed by the umami powers of walnuts and blue algae, this vegan version of the classic Blue Cheese will have you questioning your reality.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw walnuts, soaked for at least 4 hours and drained
  • ½ cup rejuvelac (vegan dairy-free probiotic beverage)
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 2 teaspoons barley or other blue algae
  • 2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Blend all the ingredients together in a food processor or high-speed blender until smooth and creamy.
  2. Store this one in a jar in the fridge, and you’ll have a Blue Cheese experience ready to be slathered onto salads or enjoyed with your fave cocktails and wines.
