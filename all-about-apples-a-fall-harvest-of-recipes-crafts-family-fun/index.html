All About Apples: A Fall Harvest of Recipes, Crafts, & Family Fun


As the leaves turn amber, orange, and red, and crisp autumn air fills our lungs, it’s time to welcome the fall season with open arms. One of the most beloved and versatile fruits of the harvest is the apple. This revered fruit offers endless opportunities for seasoning our culinary creations and serves as the inspiration for numerous creative endeavors. Let’s delve into some delightful ways to embrace the beauty and flavor of apples this fall with recipes, crafts, and plenty of family fun!

Delightful Apple-centric Recipes

From appetizers to desserts, nothing pairs better with the fall season than the humble apple. Here are some innovative apples recipes that showcase the delicious versatility of this fruit:

Apple Cinnamon Overnight Oats

Prepare for a full day of apple-inspired goodness with this scrumptious breakfast treat that’s both nutritious and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup milk (or non-dairy alternative)
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 small apple, diced
  • Honey or maple syrup for drizzling (optional)

Directions:

  1. Blend oats, milk, yogurt, and cinnamon in a blender until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour the mixture into a mason jar or Tupperware container, then layer diced apples on top.
  3. Refrigerate overnight.
  4. In the morning, give it a good stir, adjust sweetness with a drizzle of honey or maple syrup, and enjoy!

Slow Cooker Apple Cider

Transform your home into the scents of fall with this warm and comforting apple cider recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 gallons apple juice or cider
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar (packed)
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Directions:

  1. Combine the apple juice and brown sugar in the slow cooker.
  2. Mix in the cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and salt.
  3. Cover and cook on HIGH for 2 to 4 hours, SKIMMING THE FROTH THAT RISES TO THE TOP.
  4. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve and serve warm!

Salted Caramel Apple Nachos

Create a fun, interactive dessert that’s perfect for the entire family with these crowd-pleasing apple nachos.

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium apples (sliced into wedges)
  • 1/2 cup caramel sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pecans)
  • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips
  • Sea salt flakes

Directions:

  1. Place apple wedges in a single layer on a plate.
  2. Drizzle with caramel sauce.
  3. Sprinkle with nuts, chocolate chips, and a touch of sea salt.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Creative Apple-centric Crafts

Apples aren’t just delicious, they can also be a source of endless creativity. Here are some crafty ways you can make the most out of this natural beauty:

Apple Painting

Add a touch of autumn to your walls with this easy and vibrant apple painting project.

Materials:

  • Acrylic paint in various shades of red, green, and yellow
  • Paintbrushes
  • Canvas or poster board
  • Paper towels

Directions:

  1. Sketch a basic apple outline on the canvas.
  2. Begin by applying the darkest shades first, using red and green to create depth.
  3. Dip the paintbrush into lighter shades of red and yellow, apply them over the darker ones.
  4. Drybrush or blend the colors for a more seamless finish.
  5. Display your stunning apple painting to brighten up your space!

DIY Apple Wreath

A decorative wreath add luscious charm to your front door or dining room for the season. Here’s how you can make it:

Materials:

  • A foam wreath base
  • 2 to 3 yards of burlap ribbon
  • 1 yard of raffia
  • 1 yard of apple garland
  • Hot glue gun

Directions:

  1. Secure your burlap ribbon to the wreath base, wrapping it completely around the foam.
  2. Cut the raffia into six-inch segments and intertwine them with the burlap ribbon.
  3. Begin winding the apple garland to the base, along with the raffia in a random pattern.
  4. Overlap the apples and raffia to create fullness and secure with hot glue when necessary.
  5. Give your wreath a rustic charm and hang it up to welcome guests with a cozy fall feeling!

Embracing Fall Family Fun with Apples

Make the most of the apple harvest with these fun fall activities that encourage bonding and create lasting memories with the entire family:

Apple-themed Scavenger Hunt

Turn a day at the orchard into an adventure with an apple-themed scavenger hunt.

Materials needed:

  • Paper and pens
  • Apples of various colors and sizes
  • A bag or basket to collect the items

Scavenger hunt list:

  1. Find an apple that’s as big as your palm.
  2. Spot five apples with the exact same coloring.
  3. Pick an apple that looks like a smiley face from above.
  4. Collect a pair of apples with leaves attached to them, but they must be different leaves.
  5. Find the greenest apple in the orchard.
  6. Pick an apple that rolls when you give it a gentle kick.
  7. Locate an apple with a marking like a scar.

Apple Picking Relay Race

Host an apple picking relay race to make the day’s harvest even more fun and enjoyable.

Materials:

  • Two baskets per team
  • Apples or another equivalent to place in the baskets
  • A designated voting zone (table or balance beam to stand on)
  • A 10-meter starting line

Rules:

  1. Divide your apple lovers into two teams of equal size.
  2. Set up two starting lines 10 meters apart, with a single basket placed at the end of each line.
  3. Place an equal number of apples in each basket.
  4. On the signal, team members race to the first basket, pick an apple, and race back to their teammates, transferring the apple to their basket.
  5. Once the first team member reaches the basket, the second team member takes their place, and the race begins anew.
  6. The winning team is the one that first fills their basket with apples with each apple remaining upright without tipping over in the voting zone.

Embrace the bounty of the fall season by incorporating apples into your meals, crafts, and activities. With so many delightful possibilities, you and your loved ones will find endless ways to indulge in the beauty and flavor of this simple, yet enchanting fruit.

Cheers to a season well spent with apples by our side, showering us with boundless inspiration in the culinary arts, crafts, and family fun!
