Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup Recipe for Vegetarian Comfort

There’s nothing quite as comforting as a warm bowl of soup on a chilly day. And when it comes to vegetarian comfort food, this Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup is a winner. Packed with flavor, protein, and nutrients, this recipe will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make in your Instant Pot, saving you time and effort in the kitchen. Ingredients 1 pound dried black beans, rinsed and soaked overnight 1 onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 bell pepper, diced 2 carrots, diced 2 stalks celery, diced 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced 1 tablespoon cumin 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon smoked paprika 1 teaspoon oregano 4 cups vegetable broth 2 cups water Salt and pepper, to taste Optional toppings: chopped cilantro, diced avocado, sour cream, lime wedges Instructions Drain and rinse the soaked black beans. Set aside. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and heat a tablespoon of oil. Add the diced onion, garlic, bell pepper, carrots, celery, and jalapeno pepper. Sauté for 5 minutes until the vegetables are softened. Add the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and oregano to the Instant Pot. Stir to coat the vegetables in the spices. Pour in the vegetable broth and water, followed by the soaked black beans. Give everything a good stir. Secure the lid on your Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 25 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes, then carefully quick-release any remaining pressure. Use an immersion blender to partially blend the soup, leaving some whole beans for texture. If you don’t have an immersion blender, carefully transfer a portion of the soup to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot. Taste the soup and season with salt and pepper as needed. Ladle the soup into bowls and top with your favorite garnishes, such as chopped cilantro, diced avocado, sour cream, and a squeeze of lime juice. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors of this Instant Pot Black Bean Soup! Why We Love This Recipe This Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup is a vegetarian’s dream come true. Not only does it offer a hearty and filling meal, but it’s also packed with plant-based protein from the black beans. Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Curry Recipe: A Flavorful and Healthy Option

In this blog post, we are going to share with you an amazing Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Curry recipe that will tantalize your taste buds and keep you feeling satisfied. Ingredients 1 cup of lentils (any variety) 1 onion, diced 2 cloves of garlic, minced 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated 1 tablespoon of curry powder 1 teaspoon of cumin 1 teaspoon of turmeric 1 teaspoon of paprika 1 teaspoon of salt 1 can of diced tomatoes 2 cups of vegetable broth 2 cups of mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, peas, etc.) 1 can of coconut milk Fresh cilantro, for garnish Instructions Rinse the lentils under cold water and set aside. Delicious Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas Recipe for Southwestern Flavor

Looking for a delicious and easy dinner recipe that will transport you to the flavors of the Southwest? Look no further than this mouthwatering Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas recipe. Packed with bold flavors and cooked to perfection in your trusty Instant Pot, these enchiladas will have your taste buds dancing with joy. So, put on your apron and let’s get cooking! Ingredients 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 small onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1 cup chicken broth 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 8 flour tortillas 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, sour cream Instructions Start by setting your Instant Pot to the “Saute” function. Add the olive oil and heat it up. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot and saute until the onion is translucent and fragrant. In a small bowl, combine the ground cumin, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well. Season the chicken breasts with half of the spice mixture, making sure to coat both sides. Add the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are browned. Pour the chicken broth, red enchilada sauce, and diced green chilies into the pot, along with the remaining spice mixture. Stir well to combine. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then carefully perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Take a tortilla and spoon some of the enchilada sauce mixture onto it. Add a portion of the shredded chicken and a sprinkle of shredded cheese. Roll the tortilla tightly and place it seam-side down in a baking dish. Repeat with the remaining tortillas. Pour any remaining enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas and sprinkle with the remaining shredded cheese. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. After 20 minutes, remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove the enchiladas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas with your favorite toppings, such as diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream. Enjoy the Flavors of the Southwest There you have it, a delicious Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas recipe that is sure to satisfy your cravings for Southwestern flavors. With its tender shredded chicken, flavorful sauce, and gooey melted cheese, these enchiladas are a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in this irresistible dish. Happy cooking! ...
