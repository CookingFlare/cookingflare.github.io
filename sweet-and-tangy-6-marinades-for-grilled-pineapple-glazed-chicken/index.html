Sweet and Tangy: 6 Marinades for Grilled Pineapple Glazed Chicken


Are you looking for a delicious and flavorful dish to spice up your summer grilling? Look no further! In this recipe blog post, we will explore six mouthwatering marinades that will take your grilled pineapple glazed chicken to a whole new level. These marinades are sweet, tangy, and packed with delightful flavors that will leave you craving for more. So fire up your grill, grab your apron, and let’s get cooking!

1. Pineapple Teriyaki Marinade

This marinade combines the sweetness of fresh pineapple with the savory flavors of teriyaki sauce. The tanginess of the pineapple perfectly complements the juicy grilled chicken. To prepare this marinade, simply blend fresh pineapple chunks, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and a touch of honey. Marinate the chicken for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to infuse. Grill until the chicken is golden brown and serve with a side of grilled pineapple slices for an extra burst of tropical goodness.

2. Honey Mustard Glaze

For those who appreciate the classic combination of sweet and tangy, this honey mustard glaze is a winner. In a bowl, whisk together Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Coat the chicken in the glaze and let it marinate for at least 1 hour. Grill until the chicken is cooked through, and the glaze forms a beautiful caramelized crust. Serve with a fresh green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

3. Citrus and Herb Marinade

If you’re a fan of zesty flavors, this citrus and herb marinade will tickle your taste buds. Combine the juice of fresh oranges, lemons, and limes with minced garlic, chopped fresh herbs like rosemary and thyme, and a drizzle of olive oil. Marinate the chicken for at least 4 hours to allow the flavors to meld. Grill until the chicken is tender and fragrant. The citrusy marinade adds a refreshing twist to the grilled pineapple glazed chicken, making it perfect for a summer barbecue.

4. Spicy Mango Marinade

For those who like a little kick in their dishes, this spicy mango marinade will not disappoint. In a blender, puree ripe mangoes, jalapeños (seeds removed for a milder version), lime juice, honey, and a pinch of cayenne pepper. Marinate the chicken for at least 3 hours to let the flavors infuse. Grill until the chicken is cooked through, and the spicy mango marinade creates a delightful glaze. Serve with a side of grilled pineapple for a tropical explosion of flavors.

5. Balsamic and Brown Sugar Glaze

This balsamic and brown sugar glaze is a perfect balance of tangy and sweet. In a saucepan, combine balsamic vinegar, brown sugar, minced garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Simmer until the mixture thickens into a glaze-like consistency. Brush the glaze onto the chicken and let it marinate for at least 2 hours. Grill until the chicken is nicely charred and the glaze caramelizes. The balsamic and brown sugar glaze adds a rich, complex flavor profile to the grilled pineapple glazed chicken, making it a crowd-pleaser.

6. Asian Ginger Soy Marinade

Last but not least, this Asian-inspired marinade is a symphony of flavors. Mix soy sauce, grated ginger, minced garlic, sesame oil, and a splash of rice vinegar in a bowl. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour to allow the marinade to penetrate the meat. Grill until the chicken is tender and juicy, with a hint of smokiness from the grill. Serve with a side of grilled pineapple and steamed rice for a complete and satisfying meal.

There you have it – six irresistible marinades that will elevate your grilled pineapple glazed chicken to new heights. Whether you prefer a sweet and tangy fusion or a spicy twist, these marinades offer something for every palate. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and indulge in the mouthwatering flavors of these delicious marinades. Happy grilling!
