Flavorful Low Carb Chicken Alfredo Zoodle Bowl


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy low-carb meal that is packed with flavor? Look no further than this Flavorful Low Carb Chicken Alfredo Zoodle Bowl recipe! This dish combines tender chicken, zucchini noodles, and a creamy alfredo sauce for a satisfying and nutritious meal that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 medium zucchini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the zucchini noodles, also known as “zoodles.” To do this, use a spiralizer or a vegetable peeler to create long, thin strands of zucchini. Set aside.

  2. Heat one tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and dried Italian seasoning. Cook the chicken breasts in the skillet until they are no longer pink in the center, about 6-8 minutes per side. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and minced garlic. Cook the garlic for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.

  4. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream to the skillet. Stir well to combine with the garlic, and then gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese. Continue stirring until the cheese has melted and the sauce has thickened, about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, use a fork to shred the cooked chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened, add the shredded chicken to the skillet and stir to coat it with the alfredo sauce. Allow the chicken to heat through for about 2-3 minutes.

  7. Finally, add the zucchini noodles to the skillet and toss them with the chicken and sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the zucchini noodles have softened slightly but still retain some crunch.

  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and divide the zoodle bowl into individual serving bowls. Garnish with additional grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This Flavorful Low Carb Chicken Alfredo Zoodle Bowl is a complete meal on its own, but you can also pair it with a side salad or some roasted vegetables for added nutrition. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for lunch or dinner, and it’s perfect for those following a low-carb or keto diet.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 420
  • Fat: 32g
  • Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Protein: 28g

Conclusion

With its combination of tender chicken, zucchini noodles, and creamy alfredo sauce, this Flavorful Low Carb Chicken Alfredo Zoodle Bowl is a winner when it comes to taste and health. It’s a low-carb option that doesn’t sacrifice flavor, making it a perfect choice for anyone looking to indulge in a satisfying meal without the guilt. Give this recipe a try and discover a new favorite in your low-carb cooking repertoire!
