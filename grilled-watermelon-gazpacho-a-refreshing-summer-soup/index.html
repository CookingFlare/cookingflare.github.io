Grilled Watermelon Gazpacho: A Refreshing Summer Soup


Who says soup is only for the cold winter months? With summer in full swing, it’s time to explore refreshing and light options that will cool you down on those hot days. Look no further than Grilled Watermelon Gazpacho, a unique twist on the classic Spanish chilled soup. This recipe combines the sweetness of watermelon with the smoky flavors of the grill, resulting in a truly tantalizing dish that will leave your taste buds dancing.

Ingredients

  • 1 small watermelon, sliced into rounds
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Place the watermelon rounds directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2 minutes on each side, until grill marks appear. Remove from the grill and let cool.

  2. Once the watermelon has cooled, remove the rind and chop the grilled flesh into small cubes. Set aside a few cubes for garnish.

  3. In a blender, combine the grilled watermelon cubes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, garlic, olive oil, red wine vinegar, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy.

  4. Transfer the gazpacho to a bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. When ready to serve, ladle the chilled soup into bowls and garnish with the reserved grilled watermelon cubes and fresh mint leaves. Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing flavors of summer!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a dash of hot sauce or a sprinkle of cayenne pepper to the gazpacho.
  • If you prefer a chunkier soup, reserve some of the diced vegetables before blending and stir them in before serving.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile. Basil, cilantro, or even a touch of ginger can add an exciting twist.
  • This gazpacho can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Just give it a good stir before serving.

The Perfect Summer Soup

Grilled Watermelon Gazpacho is the ultimate summer soup that combines the freshness of watermelon with the smoky flavors of the grill. The result is a delightful and refreshing dish that will keep you cool during those scorching summer days. With its vibrant colors and unique blend of flavors, this gazpacho is sure to impress friends and family at your next summer gathering. So fire up that grill, grab some watermelon, and get ready to indulge in this deliciously different soup!

Remember to experiment with different ingredients and flavors to make this recipe your own. Don’t be afraid to get creative and add your personal touch. Enjoy the flavors of summer with Grilled Watermelon Gazpacho, and let your taste buds savor the unique combination of summer sweetness and smoky goodness.
