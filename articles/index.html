Creamy Mushroom and Spinach Pasta Ingredients: 8 oz. whole wheat pasta 1 tbsp olive oil 2 garlic cloves, minced 8 oz. sliced mushrooms 2 cups fresh spinach 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth 1/2 cup low-fat cream cheese Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute. Add the mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they are tender and browned. Stir in the spinach and cook until wilted. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat and stir in the cream cheese until it melts and forms a creamy sauce. Season with salt and pepper. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until the pasta is coated with the creamy mushroom and spinach sauce. Serve hot and enjoy!
