Soul-Soothing Mac and Cheese Recipe for a Cozy Night In


There’s something about a warm, creamy bowl of mac and cheese that just seems to wrap you up in a big, comforting hug. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a satisfying dish for a cozy night in, this soul-soothing mac and cheese recipe is sure to hit the spot. With a perfect balance of cheesy goodness, a hint of spice, and a crunchy breadcrumb topping, it’s the ultimate comfort food that will have you coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Mac and Cheese:

  • 16 oz (450g) elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup (60g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups (960ml) whole milk
  • 2 cups (200g) sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup (100g) Gruyère cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup (100g) panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup (25g) Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Macaroni: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. Make the Cheese Sauce: In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, whisk in the flour to create a roux. Cook the roux for a few minutes, stirring constantly, until it turns a light golden color. Gradually whisk in the milk, and continue cooking and stirring until the mixture thickens and comes to a gentle boil.

  4. Add the Cheeses and Seasonings: Reduce the heat to low and stir in the shredded cheddar and Gruyère cheeses, mustard powder, and cayenne pepper. Keep stirring until the cheeses are melted and the sauce is smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Combine the Macaroni and Cheese Sauce: Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir until well combined. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  6. Prepare the Topping: In a small bowl, combine the panko breadcrumbs, melted butter, and grated Parmesan cheese. Sprinkle the breadcrumb mixture evenly over the mac and cheese.

  7. Bake and Garnish: Bake the mac and cheese in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly. Once out of the oven, let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.

Tips for a Perfect Mac and Cheese

  • For an extra creamy mac and cheese, use whole milk and high-quality cheeses.
  • Don’t overcook the pasta before baking, as it will continue to cook in the oven.
  • Feel free to customize your mac and cheese with add-ins such as crispy bacon, caramelized onions, or diced tomatoes.

Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just looking to indulge in a comforting meal for one, this soul-soothing mac and cheese is the answer. With its creamy, cheesy goodness and a perfect golden crust, it’s a timeless classic that never fails to bring joy to the table. So, grab a fork, cozy up on the couch, and savor every bite of this ultimate comfort food.
