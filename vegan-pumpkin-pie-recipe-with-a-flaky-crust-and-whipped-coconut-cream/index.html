Vegan Pumpkin Pie Recipe with a Flaky Crust and Whipped Coconut Cream


Welcome to our vegan pumpkin pie recipe! If you’re a fan of traditional pumpkin pie but prefer a plant-based version, you’re in for a treat. This recipe combines the warm flavors of pumpkin, cinnamon, and nutmeg with a flaky crust and a luscious whipped coconut cream topping. Whether you’re vegan or not, this pie is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 4-6 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Whipped Coconut Cream:

  • 1 can full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Crust:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the vegan butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.
  3. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Filling:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, coconut milk, maple syrup, coconut sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and salt until well combined.
  3. Take the chilled dough out of the refrigerator and roll it out on a lightly floured surface until it’s about 1/8-inch thick.
  4. Carefully transfer the crust to a 9-inch pie dish and press it gently into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges.
  5. Pour the pumpkin filling into the prepared crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  6. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.
  7. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack.

Whipped Coconut Cream:

  1. While the pie is cooling, prepare the whipped coconut cream. Open the can of refrigerated coconut milk and scoop out the solid coconut cream into a mixing bowl, leaving the liquid behind.
  2. Using an electric mixer, beat the coconut cream on high speed for a few minutes until it becomes light and fluffy.
  3. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract and continue beating until well combined.
  4. Once the pie has cooled, spread the whipped coconut cream over the top.
  5. Slice, serve, and enjoy!

Conclusion

There you have it! A delicious vegan pumpkin pie with a flaky crust and a heavenly whipped coconut cream topping. This recipe is a perfect dessert for Thanksgiving, holiday gatherings, or any time you’re craving a comforting slice of pie. The combination of warm spices and creamy coconut will surely satisfy your taste buds. So go ahead and give this recipe a try, and let us know how it turned out for you. Happy baking!
