10-Minute Greek Quinoa Stuffed Peppers Recipe


Are you looking for a quick and healthy meal option that’s packed with flavor? Look no further than this 10-Minute Greek Quinoa Stuffed Peppers recipe! These colorful and tasty peppers are filled with a delicious mixture of quinoa, fresh vegetables, and feta cheese, creating a satisfying dish that is perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the bell peppers. Cut off the tops and remove the seeds and membranes. Rinse the peppers under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh parsley. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss gently to coat everything with the flavorful dressing.

  4. Spoon the quinoa mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers, pressing it down gently to ensure a tight fit. Fill each pepper to the top, leaving a small amount of space for the mixture to expand as it cooks.

  5. Place the filled peppers on a baking sheet and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 10 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and slightly charred.

  6. Once the peppers are cooked, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. The delicious aroma of the Greek flavors will surely make your mouth water!

  7. Serve the 10-Minute Greek Quinoa Stuffed Peppers as a main course or as a side dish. They pair well with a fresh green salad or some steamed vegetables for a complete and nutritious meal.

Enjoy a Flavorful and Healthy Dish

These 10-Minute Greek Quinoa Stuffed Peppers are not only quick and easy to make, but they are also packed with nutritional value. Quinoa is a great source of protein, while the fresh vegetables add vitamins and minerals to the dish. The feta cheese provides a creamy and tangy element that balances the flavors perfectly.

With just a few simple ingredients and minimal cooking time, this recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to whip up a delicious and satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Plus, the vibrant colors of the peppers make this dish visually appealing, making it a great option for dinner parties or potlucks.

So, why not give this 10-Minute Greek Quinoa Stuffed Peppers recipe a try? You’ll be amazed at how easy it is to create such a flavorful and nutritious dish in just a matter of minutes. Enjoy the Mediterranean flavors and the satisfaction of a wholesome meal that will leave you feeling energized and satisfied.
