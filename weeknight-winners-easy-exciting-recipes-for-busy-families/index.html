Weeknight Winners: Easy, Exciting Recipes for Busy Families


Balancing life’s demands can be tough, but there’s no reason the simple yet scrumptious weeknight dinner shouldn’t be on your side. Enter Weeknight Winners: a collection of easy, exciting, and absolutely delicious recipes designed for you and your busy family. From one-pot wonders to no-fuss baking, we’ve got you covered with a variety of dishes that will keep your family smiling around the table. Time to say goodbye to dinner woes and hello to effortless weeknight victories.

1. Quick and Hearty Chicken Parmesan

What’s not to love about a classic dish that’s just as versatile as it is quick to make? The familiar gooey cheese combined with the seasoned crispy breadcrumbs perfectly complements the tender chicken breasts that require zero pounding or marinating — result! Make this Winning Weeknight dish in less than 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to season
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs mixed with 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 24-ounce jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Set up a breading station with flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumb mix.
  3. Dip each chicken breast in flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs, and arrange them in a baking dish.
  4. Pour marinara sauce over the chicken, covering it to about half of its height. The remaining sauce can be saved for another use.
  5. Sprinkle cheese generously over the chicken and sauce.
  6. Bake at 400°F for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

2. Simmered Sizzling Shakshuka

Give your family the taste of a vibrant and flavorful Mediterranean evening right in your kitchen. You can made this mouthwatering Shakshuka dish in less than half an hour — talk about a show-stopping dinner that’s all about minimal effort.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 red bell peppers, diced
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to season
  • 2 cans (14.5-ounce) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups shredded spinach
  • Fresh parsley for garnishing
  • Crusty bread for dipping

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, bell peppers, and jalapeño, cooking until softened, approximately 7 minutes.
  2. Stir in ground cumin, smoked paprika, and cayenne (if using) to create a blissful spice blend.
  3. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper, then pour in the diced tomatoes and let it simmer for about 10 minutes or until slightly thickened.
  4. Preheat your broiler to high. Carefully make an indention in the tomato sauce and crack in the eggs, covering them well.
  5. Sprinkle shredded spinach over the sauce.
  6. Place the skillet under the broiler for 3-5 minutes, or until the egg whites are set and the yolks are still runny. Watch it as broilers can be unpredictable.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with crusty bread for scooping.

3. Flavorful Five-Spice Pork Tenderloin with Honey Glaze

Bring an explosion of flavors using five-spice powder, combined with a dashes of honey for a delightful glaze that’ll make your taste buds dance. This recipe gives your pork tenderloin a gourmet flair, all while taking less than an hour from start to finish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons five-spice powder
  • Salt and pepper to season
  • 1 1 1/2-pound pork tenderloin
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • Fresh thyme sprigs for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. In a small skillet, melt the butter over low heat. Stir in honey, increase heat, and let boil for 2 minutes. Set aside.
  3. Season the pork with five-spice, salt, and pepper.
  4. In an oven-safe skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add seasoned pork and cook until well browned on all sides, approximately 3 minutes per side.
  5. Transfer skillet to the preheated oven and roast until internal temperature reaches 145°F, approximately 20-25 minutes. Remove pork from oven and cover with foil, allowing it to rest for about 10 minutes.
  6. Slice into medallions and drizzle with honey glaze.
  7. Garnish with fresh thyme sprigs before serving.

By now, you’re probably salivating over these Weeknight Winners. It’s no longer goodbye, dinner worries; it’s hello, tasteful triumphs. Kick your weeknight meals up a notch, and soon enough, you’ll be a busy family’s go-to dinner hero. So fire up the stove, gather your ingredients — and most importantly — enjoy the incredible flavors that await you. Happy cooking!
