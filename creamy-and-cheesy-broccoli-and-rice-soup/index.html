Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli and Rice Soup


When the weather turns chilly and you’re craving something comforting, there’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of soup to soothe the soul. One such soup that ticks all the boxes for comfort, flavor, and nutrition is the Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli and Rice Soup. This hearty and rich dish combines the wholesome goodness of broccoli with the satisfying texture of rice, all enveloped in a luscious cheesy broth. Perfect for a family dinner or meal prep, this soup will quickly become a staple in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Soup:

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups fresh broccoli florets, chopped
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Optional Garnishes:

  • Additional shredded cheddar cheese
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • Croutons

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

  1. Chop the onion finely and mince the garlic cloves.
  2. Cut the broccoli florets into small, bite-sized pieces.
  3. Shred the cheddar cheese if not pre-shredded.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

  1. In a large soup pot, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté for about 5 minutes, or until soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Step 3: Cook the Broccoli and Rice

  1. Add the chopped broccoli florets to the pot and stir to combine with the onions and garlic.
  2. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, add the long-grain white rice and reduce the heat to a simmer.
  4. Cover the pot and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the broccoli is cooked through.

Step 4: Make the Soup Creamy

  1. Add the whole milk to the pot, stirring to combine.
  2. Gradually stir in the shredded cheddar cheese, one handful at a time, allowing each addition to melt completely before adding more.
  3. Add the dried thyme and dried basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 5: Blend for Creaminess (Optional)

  1. For a creamier texture, use an immersion blender to blend a portion of the soup directly in the pot. Alternatively, transfer about half of the soup to a blender, blend until smooth, and then return it to the pot.
  2. Stir well to combine the blended soup with the remaining chunky soup.

Step 6: Final Adjustments

  1. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
  2. If the soup is too thick, add a splash of milk or broth to reach your desired consistency.

Step 7: Serve and Garnish

  1. Ladle the creamy and cheesy broccoli and rice soup into bowls.
  2. Garnish with additional shredded cheddar cheese, chopped fresh parsley, or croutons, if desired.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting, rich flavors of this delicious soup!

Tips for Making the Perfect Broccoli and Rice Soup

Choose Fresh Ingredients

Using fresh broccoli will give your soup a vibrant color and fresh flavor. If fresh broccoli isn’t available, frozen broccoli can be used in a pinch.

Cheese Matters

Opt for a good-quality cheddar cheese for the best flavor. Sharp cheddar provides a stronger cheese taste, while mild cheddar gives a more subtle flavor. Feel free to mix cheeses like cheddar and Monterey Jack for a unique twist.

Control the Consistency

The soup’s thickness can be easily adjusted. For a thicker soup, use less broth or add more rice. For a thinner consistency, add more broth or milk.

Blend to Your Liking

Blending a portion of the soup creates a creamy texture while still leaving some chunks of broccoli and rice for a delightful contrast. Adjust the amount you blend based on your preference.

Reheating Tips

When reheating leftovers, do so gently over low heat to prevent the cheese from separating. Adding a little extra milk while reheating can help maintain a creamy consistency.

Nutritional Benefits

Packed with Nutrients

Broccoli is a nutritional powerhouse, rich in vitamins C and K, fiber, and various antioxidants. Incorporating it into a creamy and cheesy soup makes it both delicious and nutritious.

Balanced Meal

This soup combines vegetables, protein, and carbohydrates, making it a balanced meal. The rice provides carbs and fiber, while the cheese and milk add protein and calcium.

Comfort in a Bowl

Not only is this soup tasty, but it also delivers comfort in every spoonful. The creamy texture and cheesy flavor make it a hit with both kids and adults.

Variations to Try

Add Protein

For a more filling soup, consider adding cooked chicken, ham, or bacon. Simply stir in your protein of choice during the final stages of cooking.

Spice It Up

If you enjoy a bit of heat, add some red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the soup. This can provide a nice contrast to the creamy, cheesy base.

Swap the Rice

While long-grain white rice is traditional, feel free to experiment with other grains like brown rice, quinoa, or even cauliflower rice for a lower-carb option.

Dairy-Free Option

To make this soup dairy-free, substitute the milk with a plant-based alternative like almond or oat milk, and use a dairy-free cheese substitute.

Conclusion

Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli and Rice Soup is more than just a meal; it’s a bowl of comfort that brings warmth and satisfaction on any day. With its rich flavors, nourishing ingredients, and easy preparation, this soup is perfect for feeding a hungry family or enjoying as leftovers throughout the week. Whether you’re looking for a hearty lunch, a cozy dinner, or a dish to impress guests, this soup is sure to please. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a delightful bowl of Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli and Rice Soup!
