Homemade Salted Caramel Pretzel Ice Cream: A Sweet and Salty Combo


If you’re a fan of the sweet and salty flavor combination, then homemade salted caramel pretzel ice cream is a must-try treat. This delightful dessert brings together the richness of caramel, the crunch of pretzels, and the creaminess of ice cream for a truly indulgent experience. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through a step-by-step recipe to create this delectable homemade ice cream in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to make your own batch of salted caramel pretzel ice cream:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup salted caramel sauce
  • 1 cup chopped pretzels

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Base

In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream and milk over medium heat until it just begins to simmer. In a separate saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water over medium-high heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves. Let the mixture cook, without stirring, until it turns a deep amber color. This will take about 5-7 minutes.

Step 2: Create the Caramel Base

Once the sugar mixture achieves the desired color, remove it from the heat and gradually whisk in the warm cream and milk mixture. Be cautious, as the mixture will bubble up. Return the combined mixture to medium heat and cook until smooth.

Step 3: Temper the Eggs

In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks. Gradually pour about 1/2 cup of the hot cream mixture into the yolks, whisking constantly. This process, known as tempering, will prevent the eggs from scrambling when added to the hot mixture.

Step 4: Combine the Mixtures

Pour the tempered egg mixture into the saucepan with the remaining cream mixture. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes.

Step 5: Add Flavor

Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Then pour the base through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl to strain out any lumps. Let the mixture cool to room temperature before covering it and refrigerating it for at least 4 hours, or overnight.

Step 6: Churn the Ice Cream

Once the base is thoroughly chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. As the ice cream begins to set, slowly drizzle in the salted caramel sauce and add the chopped pretzels. Continue churning until the ice cream reaches a firm, yet creamy consistency.

Step 7: Freeze and Serve

Transfer the churned ice cream into a freezer-safe container, swirling in additional salted caramel sauce and pretzel pieces as desired. Cover the container and freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours, or until it reaches the desired firmness.

Enjoy the Sweet and Salty Indulgence

Once the ice cream has had time to set, scoop it into bowls or cones and savor the delightful combination of sweet, creamy caramel and crunchy, salty pretzels. This homemade salted caramel pretzel ice cream is perfect for a summer treat or any time you’re craving a unique and decadent dessert.

Whether you’re a seasoned ice cream enthusiast or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress with its irresistible blend of flavors and the satisfaction of creating your own frozen masterpiece. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the sweet and salty delight of homemade salted caramel pretzel ice cream.
