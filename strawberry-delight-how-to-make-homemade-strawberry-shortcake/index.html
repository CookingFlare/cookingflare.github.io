Strawberry Delight: How to Make Homemade Strawberry Shortcake


Are you ready to indulge in a delightful summer treat that will leave your taste buds begging for more? Look no further than this recipe for Homemade Strawberry Shortcake. With its light and fluffy cake, sweet and juicy strawberries, and creamy whipped topping, this dessert is a true delight for the senses. So, roll up your sleeves, put on your apron, and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the strawberries:

  • 4 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the whipped topping:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Make a well in the center of the mixture and pour in the milk and vanilla extract. Stir until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently for a minute or two, until it comes together.
  6. Roll the dough out to a thickness of about 1/2 inch. Using a round biscuit cutter or a drinking glass, cut out circles of dough and place them onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake the shortcakes for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown. Remove from the oven and let them cool completely.

Step 2: Preparing the Strawberries

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the sliced strawberries and sugar. Toss gently to coat the strawberries in the sugar.
  2. Let the strawberries sit for at least 15 minutes, allowing them to release their juices and become even more deliciously sweet.

Step 3: Making the Whipped Topping

  1. In a chilled mixing bowl, pour in the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract.
  2. Using an electric mixer or a whisk, beat the cream until stiff peaks form. Be careful not to overbeat, as the cream may turn into butter.

Step 4: Assembling the Strawberry Shortcake

  1. Slice each cooled shortcake in half horizontally.
  2. Place the bottom half of a shortcake on a serving plate and spoon a generous amount of the strawberry mixture on top.
  3. Add a dollop of whipped cream on the strawberries, then place the top half of the shortcake on top.
  4. Repeat this process with the remaining shortcakes.
  5. Finish each serving with a final dollop of whipped cream and a strawberry on top for garnish.

Now, sit back, relax, and enjoy every luscious bite of your Homemade Strawberry Shortcake. The combination of the tender cake, juicy strawberries, and creamy whipped topping is simply irresistible. Whether you’re serving it at a summer picnic, a family gathering, or simply as a special treat for yourself, this dessert is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. So go ahead, savor the flavors of summer with this delightful recipe!
