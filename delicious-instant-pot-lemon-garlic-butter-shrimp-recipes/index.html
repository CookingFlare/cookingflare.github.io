Delicious Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp Recipes


Shrimp, a beloved seafood delicacy, is often celebrated for its versatility and quick cooking time. When paired with the zesty brightness of lemon and the rich, savory flavor of garlic butter, shrimp transforms into a dish that is both gourmet and comforting. One of the best ways to prepare this mouthwatering combination is by using an Instant Pot, which not only speeds up the cooking process but also locks in flavors and moisture. In this blog post, we will explore several variations of Instant Pot lemon garlic butter shrimp recipes that are sure to impress your family and friends.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Shrimp?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer in the kitchen, especially for seafood lovers. Here’s why:

  1. Speed: Shrimp cooks quickly, and the Instant Pot can prepare it even faster. You can have a delicious meal ready in under 30 minutes.
  2. Flavor Infusion: The pressure cooking process allows the flavors of lemon, garlic, and butter to penetrate the shrimp deeply, resulting in an incredibly flavorful dish.
  3. Convenience: With just one pot, cleanup is a breeze. You can sauté, pressure cook, and keep your meal warm all in the Instant Pot.
  4. Nutritional Benefits: Cooking shrimp in an Instant Pot preserves its nutrients, giving you a healthy meal option without sacrificing taste.

Essential Ingredients

Before diving into the recipes, let’s gather the essential ingredients you’ll need:

  • Shrimp: Use fresh or frozen shrimp, preferably peeled and deveined for convenience.
  • Butter: Unsalted butter is ideal, as it allows you to control the saltiness of the dish.
  • Garlic: Fresh garlic cloves are recommended for the best flavor, but you can use minced garlic if pressed for time.
  • Lemon: Fresh lemon juice and lemon zest will brighten the dish and enhance the overall taste.
  • Chicken broth: This adds depth to the flavor. You can substitute vegetable broth for a lighter version.
  • Seasonings: Salt, black pepper, red pepper flakes (for a kick), and fresh herbs like parsley or thyme can elevate your dish further.

Classic Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Garlic: Begin by setting your Instant Pot to the ‘Sauté’ mode. Add the butter, allowing it to melt, then add the minced garlic. Sauté for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.

  2. Add Broth and Shrimp: Pour in the chicken broth, scraping any browned bits off the bottom of the pot. Add the shrimp, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. If you enjoy some heat, sprinkle in the red pepper flakes.

  3. Pressure Cook: Seal the Instant Pot lid and set it to ‘Pressure Cook’ on high for 2 minutes. The shrimp will cook quickly, so keep an eye on the time.

  4. Release Pressure: Once the cooking time is up, perform a quick release of the pressure. Carefully open the lid, and give the shrimp a gentle stir to combine with the flavorful broth.

  5. Serve: Transfer the shrimp to a serving bowl, drizzle with additional melted butter if desired, and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot over rice, pasta, or with crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce.

Creamy Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces pasta (linguine or fettuccine)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: In the Instant Pot, add 4 cups of water and bring to a boil using the ‘Sauté’ function. Add the pasta and cook for 6 minutes on high pressure. Quick release the pressure and drain the pasta, setting it aside.

  2. Sauté Garlic: In the same pot, melt the butter and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.

  3. Add Broth and Shrimp: Pour in the chicken broth, then add the shrimp, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Cook on high pressure for 2 minutes, then quick release.

  4. Make it Creamy: Stir in the heavy cream, mixing well until combined. Add the cooked pasta back to the pot and toss everything together until creamy and well coated.

  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh basil and additional lemon zest. Serve immediately for a comforting, creamy dish that’s sure to please!

Spicy Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Corn or flour tortillas
  • Toppings: diced avocado, cilantro, lime wedges, shredded cabbage

Instructions

  1. Sauté Garlic: Set the Instant Pot to ‘Sauté’ and melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.

  2. Prepare Shrimp: Add chicken broth, shrimp, lemon juice, lemon zest, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 2 minutes. Quick release the pressure.

  4. Assemble Tacos: Warm the tortillas in a separate skillet. Fill each tortilla with shrimp and top with avocado, cilantro, and shredded cabbage.

  5. Serve: Serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Shrimp

  1. Avoid Overcooking: Shrimp cooks quickly, so it’s important to keep an eye on the timing. Two minutes under pressure is usually sufficient.
  2. Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh shrimp and garlic will always yield the best flavor. If using frozen shrimp, ensure they are thawed before cooking.
  3. Experiment with Flavors: Feel free to add your favorite spices, herbs, or even vegetables to customize your dish.

Conclusion

Instant Pot lemon garlic butter shrimp recipes are not only delicious but also incredibly versatile. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic preparation, a creamy pasta dish, or a spicy taco, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, you’ll impress everyone at your dinner table. So grab your Instant Pot, and let the culinary adventure begin! Enjoy your meal and the delightful burst of flavors that comes with each bite!

Happy cooking!
