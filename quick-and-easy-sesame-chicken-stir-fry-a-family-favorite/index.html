Quick and Easy Sesame Chicken Stir Fry: A Family Favorite


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that you can whip up in no time? Look no further than this Quick and Easy Sesame Chicken Stir Fry recipe. Packed with flavor and ready in under 30 minutes, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the sliced chicken with soy sauce and sesame oil. Let it marinate for 15 minutes while you prepare the other ingredients.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and onion. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the minced garlic and ginger to the other side. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch, chicken broth, honey, and rice vinegar until smooth. Pour the mixture into the skillet and bring it to a simmer. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens.

  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and toss it with the vegetables and sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until everything is well coated and heated through.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle the sesame seeds over the stir fry. Give it a final toss to distribute the sesame seeds evenly.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  9. Serve the Quick and Easy Sesame Chicken Stir Fry over steamed rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of Sriracha sauce to the stir fry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this recipe based on your family’s preferences. Broccoli, snap peas, or mushrooms would be great additions.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can substitute tofu or tempeh for the chicken.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in a skillet or microwave before serving.

Enjoy the delicious flavors of this Quick and Easy Sesame Chicken Stir Fry. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed any night of the week. Whether you’re a busy parent looking for a quick dinner solution or just someone who loves a flavorful stir fry, this recipe is sure to please your taste buds.
