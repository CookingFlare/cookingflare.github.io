Pumpkin Spice Muffins Recipe: A Fall-Inspired Vegan Treat


As the leaves turn vibrant shades of red and gold and the air becomes crisp, it’s the perfect time to indulge in the warm, comforting flavors of fall. One of the most beloved autumn treats is the pumpkin spice muffin - a delightful blend of aromatic spices and the earthy sweetness of pumpkin. In this vegan recipe, we’ll show you how to create these delectable muffins using wholesome, plant-based ingredients that will satisfy your cravings while nourishing your body.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Optional Add-Ins:

  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or lightly grease it.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients - flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, cloves, and salt. Mix well to ensure even distribution of the spices.

  3. In another bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients - pumpkin puree, applesauce, melted coconut oil, almond milk, and vanilla extract.

  4. Gently fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, being careful not to overmix. If desired, gently fold in the optional add-ins such as chopped nuts or vegan chocolate chips.

  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 3/4 full.

  6. Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  7. Once baked, remove the muffins from the oven and allow them to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Serve the pumpkin spice muffins warm or at room temperature, and enjoy the delightful flavors of fall in every bite!

Tips and Serving Suggestions

  • For an extra indulgent touch, drizzle the muffins with a simple glaze made from powdered sugar and a splash of almond milk.
  • These muffins pair beautifully with a steaming cup of chai tea or a creamy pumpkin spice latte.
  • Store any leftover muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or freeze them for longer storage.

Final Thoughts

These pumpkin spice muffins are a delightful way to celebrate the flavors of fall while embracing a vegan lifestyle. Whether you’re gathering with loved ones for a cozy brunch or simply treating yourself to a sweet indulgence, these muffins are sure to evoke the warmth and comfort of the autumn season. With their aromatic spices and tender crumb, they are a true testament to the magic of plant-based baking. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let the enticing aroma of pumpkin spice fill your kitchen as you embark on this delightful baking adventure. Happy baking!
