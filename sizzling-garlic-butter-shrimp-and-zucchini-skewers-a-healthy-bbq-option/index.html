Sizzling Garlic Butter Shrimp and Zucchini Skewers: A Healthy BBQ Option


If you’re looking for a healthy and flavorful option for your next barbecue, look no further than these sizzling garlic butter shrimp and zucchini skewers. This recipe combines the succulent taste of shrimp with the freshness of zucchini, all infused with the rich flavors of garlic and butter. It’s a dish that’s not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, making it a perfect choice for those looking to maintain a balanced diet without sacrificing taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3-4 medium zucchinis, cut into chunks
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, olive oil, and chopped parsley. This will be the flavorful marinade for your shrimp and zucchini skewers.

  2. Thread the shrimp and zucchini chunks onto the wooden skewers, alternating between the two ingredients. This not only creates a visually appealing skewer but also ensures that the shrimp and zucchini cook evenly on the grill.

  3. Once all the skewers are assembled, generously brush the garlic butter marinade over each skewer, making sure to coat the shrimp and zucchini thoroughly.

  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate to prevent the skewers from sticking.

  5. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the shrimp is opaque and the zucchini is tender, with a slight char.

  6. As the skewers cook, continue to brush them with the remaining garlic butter marinade to enhance the flavors and keep the ingredients moist.

  7. Once the shrimp is cooked through and the zucchini is tender, carefully remove the skewers from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter.

  8. Serve the sizzling garlic butter shrimp and zucchini skewers immediately, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and an extra drizzle of the garlic butter marinade for added flavor.

Health Benefits

This recipe not only tantalizes the taste buds but also offers a range of health benefits. Shrimp is an excellent source of protein, low in calories, and provides a good dose of omega-3 fatty acids. Zucchini, on the other hand, is rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber, making it a great addition to any diet. The use of garlic and olive oil further enhances the health profile of this dish, as they are known for their anti-inflammatory and heart-healthy properties.

Conclusion

Sizzling garlic butter shrimp and zucchini skewers are a delightful and nutritious option for your next barbecue. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a flavorful meal, this recipe is sure to impress. The combination of succulent shrimp, fresh zucchini, and the rich flavors of garlic and butter make this dish a standout on any BBQ menu. Plus, with the added health benefits, you can indulge in this delicious meal without any guilt. So fire up the grill and treat yourself to these mouthwatering skewers – your taste buds and your body will thank you!
