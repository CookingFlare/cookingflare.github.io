Creamy Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings Recipe


If you’re craving a comforting, hearty meal that’s ready in a fraction of the time it would take to make on the stovetop, then this Creamy Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings recipe is just what you need. This dish is the epitome of comfort food, with tender chicken, flavorful vegetables, and pillowy dumplings all swimming in a rich, creamy broth. The best part? It’s all made in one pot, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Dumplings

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken thighs with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to “Saute” and melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Once hot, sear the chicken thighs on both sides until golden brown, then remove them from the pot and set aside.

  2. Add the remaining tablespoon of butter to the pot and sauté the onion, carrots, and celery until they begin to soften, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic, thyme, and rosemary, and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth, then return the chicken thighs to the pot. Lock the lid in place and set the Instant Pot to cook at high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, perform a quick release of the pressure.

  4. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the dumpling dough. In a bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir in the milk and parsley until just combined.

  5. Once the pressure is released from the Instant Pot, carefully remove the chicken and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot, then stir in the heavy cream.

  6. Using a spoon or a cookie scoop, drop spoonfuls of the dumpling dough on top of the chicken mixture in the Instant Pot.

  7. Lock the lid back in place and cook at high pressure for 5 minutes. Once again, perform a quick release of the pressure.

  8. Gently stir the dumplings into the creamy chicken mixture, allowing them to cook for an additional 5 minutes in the residual heat of the Instant Pot.

  9. Serve the creamy Instant Pot chicken and dumplings in bowls, garnished with extra chopped parsley for a pop of color.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a time-saving shortcut, you can use pre-made biscuit dough for the dumplings instead of making them from scratch.
  • If you prefer a thicker broth, you can make a slurry with a couple of tablespoons of cornstarch and cold water, then stir it into the chicken mixture before adding the dumplings.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The dumplings may absorb some of the liquid as they sit, so you can add a splash of chicken broth when reheating to loosen the mixture.

With this Creamy Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings recipe, you can enjoy the flavors of a slow-simmered, comforting meal without spending hours in the kitchen. It’s the perfect dish for a cozy night in or for impressing guests with your culinary prowess. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in a bowl of creamy, soul-warming goodness!
