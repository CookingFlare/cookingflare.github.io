Homemade Chicken and Biscuits: A Classic Comfort Food Recipe


When it comes to comfort food, few dishes evoke the same sense of warmth and nostalgia as homemade chicken and biscuits. This classic American recipe has been a staple in many households for generations, and for good reason. The combination of tender, juicy chicken and flaky, buttery biscuits is a match made in heaven. In this post, we’ll explore the secrets to making the perfect homemade chicken and biscuits, and why it’s a dish that’s sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need. You may already have some of these pantry staples on hand, but if not, they’re easily found at your local grocery store.

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup cold butter, cut into small pieces

The Chicken

The first step in making homemade chicken and biscuits is to prepare the chicken. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using). Add the chicken pieces and toss to coat, making sure they’re evenly coated with the spice mixture.

Next, heat the butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

The Biscuits

While the chicken is cooking, it’s time to start on the biscuits. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Cut the cold butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

Add the heavy cream to the bowl and stir until the dough comes together in a shaggy mass. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead it a few times until it comes together into a cohesive mass.

Assembling the Dish

Now it’s time to assemble the dish. Add the cooked chicken back into the skillet and stir in the softened butter until it’s fully incorporated. Then, add the biscuit dough on top of the chicken and spread it out evenly.

Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 minutes, or until the biscuits are golden brown and the chicken is hot and bubbly.

Serving

Once the dish is out of the oven, remove it from the skillet and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, slice the biscuits and serve them alongside the chicken. You can also serve the dish with a side of mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or a simple green salad.

Tips and Variations

Here are a few tips and variations to keep in mind when making homemade chicken and biscuits:

  • To make the dish more substantial, add some diced vegetables (such as carrots, celery, and onions) to the skillet with the chicken.
  • For an extra crispy biscuit topping, brush the biscuits with a little bit of melted butter before baking.
  • To make the dish more spicy, add some diced jalapenos or serrano peppers to the skillet with the chicken.
  • To make the dish more comforting, serve it with a side of warm, comforting soup (such as chicken noodle or creamy tomato).

Conclusion

Homemade chicken and biscuits is a classic comfort food recipe that’s sure to become a family favorite. With its tender, juicy chicken and flaky, buttery biscuits, it’s a dish that’s sure to satisfy even the heartiest of appetites. Whether you’re serving it as a main course or as a side dish, this recipe is sure to please. So go ahead, give it a try, and experience the comfort and joy of homemade chicken and biscuits for yourself.
