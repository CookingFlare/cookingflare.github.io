Delicious Nut-Free Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

3 min · 583 words

There’s something undeniably comforting about a warm, freshly baked chocolate chip cookie. The aroma that fills the kitchen, the gooey chocolate chips, and the soft, chewy texture – it’s a timeless delight that brings joy to people of all ages. However, for those with nut allergies, enjoying this classic treat can be a challenge. That’s why I’m excited to share with you my nut-free chocolate chip cookies recipe. These cookies are not only safe for nut-allergy sufferers, but they are also irresistibly delicious, making them a perfect addition to any cookie jar.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Cream the butter and sugars: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the eggs and vanilla: Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Combine the dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.

  5. Mix the wet and dry ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until well combined.

  6. Fold in the chocolate chips: Gently fold in the semisweet chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Scoop the cookie dough: Using a cookie scoop or a spoon, drop rounded balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.

  8. Bake to perfection: Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still look slightly underdone, but they will continue to set as they cool.

  9. Cool and enjoy: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Then, it’s time to indulge in these delectable nut-free chocolate chip cookies!

Why You’ll Love These Cookies:

  • Nut-Free: This recipe is perfect for individuals with nut allergies or those baking for someone with a nut allergy. You can enjoy these cookies without any worry.

  • Classic Flavor: Despite being nut-free, these cookies maintain the classic chocolate chip cookie flavor and texture that everyone loves.

  • Family-Friendly: Whether for school lunches, bake sales, or family gatherings, these nut-free chocolate chip cookies are a crowd-pleaser for all ages.

  • Customizable: Feel free to add your own twist by incorporating white chocolate chips, dried fruits, or even a sprinkle of sea salt on top before baking.

  • Perfect for Any Occasion: These cookies are versatile and can be enjoyed as a sweet treat on any occasion.

So, the next time you’re craving a batch of homemade chocolate chip cookies, give this nut-free recipe a try. It’s a simple yet delightful twist on a beloved classic, and I’m sure it will become a staple in your recipe collection. Happy baking!
