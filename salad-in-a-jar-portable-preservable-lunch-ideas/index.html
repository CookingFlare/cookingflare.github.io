Salad in a Jar: Portable & Preservable Lunch Ideas


Having a healthy and delicious lunch on-the-go shouldn’t feel like a burden. That’s where salad in a jar comes in, offering a convenient and creative solution to your daily meal preparation. With many different combinations to choose from, salads in a jar are not only visually appealing but also very versatile, making it easier for you to pack in your favorite ingredients. Let’s dive into some amazing recipes for a delightful and well-balanced lunch experience.

Classic Caesar Salad in a Jar

This enduring classic never goes out of style and is the perfect starting point for any salad enthusiast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup small pasta (e.g., orzo, farfalle)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken breast, cubed
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

Dressing:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a small pot, mix all dressing ingredients, whisking well to incorporate. Set aside.
  3. Layer the following in a 16-ounce jar: pasta, tomatoes, chicken, Parmaesan, and croutons.
  4. Shake well before consuming, and pour dressing over salad. Mix thoroughly.

Mediterranean Quinoa Salad in a Jar

This hearty and filling salad is not only delicious, but it also offers a variety of mouth-watering flavors and textures.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup diced feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint

Dressing:

  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions; let cool.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together all dressing ingredients.
  3. In a large bowl, combine quinoa and dressing, and mix well to coat.
  4. In a 16-ounce jar, start layering: cucumber, bell pepper, onion, quinoa salad, tomatoes, chickpeas, feta, and mint.
  5. Shake well before serving.

Asian Slaw Salad in a Jar

If you’re on the lookout for an exciting lunch option, this Asian-inspired salad in a jar is perfect for satisfying your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup quinoa noodles
  • 1/2 cup seasoned rice vinegar

Dressing:

  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together all dressing ingredients.
  2. In a 16-ounce jar, layer cabbage, carrots, bell pepper, green onions, and quinoa noodles.
  3. Pour dressing over layered ingredients.
  4. Shake well before serving, ensuring all ingredients are evenly coated in the dressing.

There you have it - three exciting salad in a jar recipes that make for delightful and convenient lunches on-the-go. By storing the salad dressed, it not only helps to keep everything in place but also infuses the ingredients with the flavors required for a sensational dining experience. Feel free to experiment with different combinations of favorite ingredients, dressings, and add-ins to create your very own personalized salad in a jar. Happy eating!
