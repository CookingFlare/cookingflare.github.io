Mouthwatering BBQ Chicken Recipes for Summer Grilling


Summer is finally here, and it’s the perfect time to break out the grill and fire up the BBQ. There’s nothing quite like the smell of sizzling meat and the taste of a perfectly grilled chicken to evoke the feeling of a warm summer day. In this post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering BBQ chicken recipes that are sure to become your new summer staples.

Classic BBQ Chicken Recipe

This classic BBQ chicken recipe is a timeless favorite that’s easy to make and always a crowd-pleaser. Marinate chicken breasts in a mixture of olive oil, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper for at least 30 minutes. Then, grill the chicken over medium heat for 5-7 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.

While the chicken is grilling, prepare the BBQ sauce by combining ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, and a pinch of salt and pepper in a small saucepan. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly. Brush the BBQ sauce onto the chicken during the last 2-3 minutes of grilling.

Spicy BBQ Chicken Recipe

For those who like a little heat in their BBQ, this spicy BBQ chicken recipe is a must-try. Marinate chicken breasts in a mixture of olive oil, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, cumin, chili powder, and a pinch of salt and pepper for at least 30 minutes. Then, grill the chicken over medium heat for 5-7 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.

While the chicken is grilling, prepare the spicy BBQ sauce by combining ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, cumin, chili powder, and a pinch of salt and pepper in a small saucepan. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly. Brush the spicy BBQ sauce onto the chicken during the last 2-3 minutes of grilling.

Honey BBQ Chicken Recipe

For a sweet and tangy twist on traditional BBQ, try this honey BBQ chicken recipe. Marinate chicken breasts in a mixture of olive oil, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, honey, garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper for at least 30 minutes. Then, grill the chicken over medium heat for 5-7 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.

While the chicken is grilling, prepare the honey BBQ sauce by combining ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, honey, and a pinch of salt and pepper in a small saucepan. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly. Brush the honey BBQ sauce onto the chicken during the last 2-3 minutes of grilling.

Tips and Variations

  • For a crispy exterior and juicy interior, make sure to pat the chicken dry with paper towels before grilling.
  • To add some extra flavor to your BBQ chicken, try using different types of wood chips or chunks to smoke the chicken while it’s grilling.
  • For a vegetarian twist on BBQ chicken, try substituting the chicken breasts with portobello mushrooms or eggplant slices.
  • To make the BBQ sauce ahead of time, simply store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

With these mouthwatering BBQ chicken recipes, you’ll be well on your way to becoming the king or queen of the grill. Whether you prefer classic, spicy, or sweet, there’s a BBQ chicken recipe here for everyone. So fire up the grill, grab some friends and family, and get ready to enjoy the perfect summer meal.
