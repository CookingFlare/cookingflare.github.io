Zesty Mexican Shrimp Cocktail: A Fresh and Flavorful Seafood Delight


If you’re a fan of seafood, then you’re in for a treat with this zesty Mexican shrimp cocktail recipe. Bursting with bold flavors and fresh ingredients, this dish is perfect for those hot summer days when you’re craving something light and refreshing. Whether you’re hosting a party or just looking for a satisfying snack, this shrimp cocktail is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of finely chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons of ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons of hot sauce (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips for serving

Instructions

  1. Start by boiling a pot of water and adding a pinch of salt. Once the water is boiling, add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp as they can become tough. Once cooked, drain the shrimp and set aside to cool.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, ketchup, and hot sauce. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  3. Once the shrimp has cooled, add them to the bowl and gently toss until they are evenly coated with the tomato mixture. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This step is crucial for achieving the perfect balance of flavors in your shrimp cocktail.

  5. Just before serving, gently fold in the diced avocado. Be careful not to overmix as you want to maintain the integrity of the avocado chunks.

  6. Serve the zesty Mexican shrimp cocktail chilled in individual glasses or bowls, garnished with a sprig of cilantro. Pair it with some crispy tortilla chips for a delightful crunch.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a few dashes of your favorite hot sauce or some finely chopped jalapenos.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding other ingredients like diced cucumber, bell peppers, or even mango for a touch of sweetness.
  • If you prefer a milder flavor, reduce the amount of hot sauce or omit it altogether.
  • For a gluten-free option, serve the shrimp cocktail with gluten-free tortilla chips or lettuce cups instead.

This zesty Mexican shrimp cocktail is not only visually appealing but also packed with vibrant flavors. The combination of juicy tomatoes, tangy lime juice, and succulent shrimp creates a refreshing and satisfying dish that will keep you coming back for more. So, gather your ingredients, prepare this flavorful seafood delight, and get ready to be transported to the sunny beaches of Mexico with every bite. Enjoy!
