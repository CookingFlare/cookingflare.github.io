Instant Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp: Bursting with Flavor


Are you looking for a quick and delicious seafood dish that will impress your family and friends? Look no further than this Instant Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp recipe! Bursting with flavor and cooked to perfection in the Instant Pot, this dish is a true crowd-pleaser. The combination of zesty lemon, savory butter, and succulent shrimp creates a culinary experience that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Plus, with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have this impressive meal on the table in no time! Let’s dive into the details of this delectable Instant Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the shrimp with salt and black pepper. This will ensure that the shrimp are well-seasoned and flavorful.

  2. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” function. Melt the butter in the Instant Pot and add the minced garlic. Sauté the garlic until it becomes fragrant, which should take about 1-2 minutes.

  3. Once the garlic is fragrant, add the seasoned shrimp to the Instant Pot. Sauté the shrimp for 2-3 minutes, or until they turn pink and opaque.

  4. Next, add the chicken or vegetable broth to the Instant Pot. This will create a flavorful base for the shrimp to cook in.

  5. Pour in the freshly squeezed lemon juice and add the lemon zest, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes. These ingredients will infuse the shrimp with a bright, zesty flavor that is simply irresistible.

  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook the shrimp on high pressure for 1 minute.

  7. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release to release the pressure from the Instant Pot. Carefully open the lid and marvel at the perfectly cooked Lemon Butter Shrimp.

  8. Serve the shrimp immediately, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh chopped parsley. The vibrant green color of the parsley will add a pop of freshness to the dish and complement the flavors beautifully.

Serving Suggestions

This Instant Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp is incredibly versatile and can be served in a variety of ways. You can enjoy it over a bed of fluffy white rice, tossed with al dente pasta, or alongside a crisp, green salad. The bright and tangy flavors of the shrimp make it an ideal pairing for a refreshing glass of white wine, such as a Sauvignon Blanc or Pinot Grigio.

Conclusion

With its vibrant flavors and effortless preparation, this Instant Pot Lemon Butter Shrimp recipe is a must-try for any seafood enthusiast. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a gourmet meal on a busy weeknight, this dish delivers on both taste and convenience. The next time you’re in the mood for a culinary adventure, fire up your Instant Pot and treat yourself to a delightful serving of Lemon Butter Shrimp. Your taste buds will thank you!
