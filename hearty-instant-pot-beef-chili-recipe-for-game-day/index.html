Hearty Instant Pot Beef Chili Recipe for Game Day


Are you ready to elevate your game day spread with a hearty and flavorful dish that will have everyone cheering? Look no further than this Instant Pot Beef Chili recipe! Packed with tender beef, savory spices, and a touch of heat, this chili is the perfect comfort food to enjoy while watching the big game with friends and family. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to score big with your game day menu!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped cilantro, sliced jalapeños

Instructions

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” function. Once hot, add the olive oil and sear the beef cubes in batches until browned on all sides. Transfer the seared beef to a plate and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, garlic, and bell peppers. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables soften and become aromatic.

  3. Add the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, beef broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper to the pot. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  4. Return the seared beef cubes and any accumulated juices to the pot. Stir to incorporate the beef with the rest of the ingredients.

  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully switching the valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  6. Once the pressure has been released, carefully open the lid. Give the chili a good stir and season with salt and black pepper to taste.

  7. Serve the Instant Pot Beef Chili in bowls, and let your guests customize their bowls with their favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped cilantro, and sliced jalapeños.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra depth of flavor, consider adding a splash of dark beer or coffee to the chili during the cooking process.
  • This chili can be made ahead of time and tastes even better the next day, making it a convenient option for game day entertaining.
  • Feel free to adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less cayenne pepper according to your preference.

Conclusion

With its rich flavors and satisfying heartiness, this Instant Pot Beef Chili is sure to become a game day favorite in your household. Whether you’re hosting a crowd or simply enjoying a cozy night in, this recipe delivers on both taste and convenience. So, fire up your Instant Pot, prepare a batch of this chili, and get ready to savor every delicious spoonful as you root for your favorite team on game day!
