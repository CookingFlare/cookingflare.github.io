Chocolate Coconut Bliss Balls: A Healthy and Energizing Snack


Are you looking for a quick and nutritious snack that will satisfy your sweet tooth and keep you energized throughout the day? Look no further! These Chocolate Coconut Bliss Balls are the perfect solution. Packed with wholesome ingredients, these little balls of goodness are not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Medjool dates, pitted
  • 1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup raw almonds
  • 1/4 cup raw cacao powder
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of sea salt

Instructions

  1. In a food processor, combine the pitted Medjool dates, unsweetened shredded coconut, raw almonds, raw cacao powder, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and a pinch of sea salt. Pulse until the mixture comes together and forms a sticky dough-like consistency.

  2. Once the mixture is well combined, use your hands to roll the dough into bite-sized balls. If the mixture is too sticky, you can wet your hands with a little bit of water to make the rolling process easier.

  3. Place the rolled bliss balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow them to firm up.

  4. After chilling, the bliss balls are ready to be enjoyed! Store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week, or freeze them for longer storage.

Health Benefits

Not only are these Chocolate Coconut Bliss Balls incredibly tasty, but they also offer a range of health benefits. Let’s take a look at some of the key ingredients and their nutritional value:

  • Medjool dates: These sweet and sticky fruits are a great source of natural sugars, fiber, and essential minerals like potassium and magnesium. They provide a quick energy boost and help support healthy digestion.

  • Unsweetened shredded coconut: Rich in healthy fats, fiber, and essential minerals, shredded coconut adds a tropical flavor to these bliss balls. It also provides medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs), which are easily absorbed and utilized by the body for energy.

  • Raw almonds: Almonds are packed with protein, healthy fats, and essential nutrients like vitamin E and magnesium. They provide a satisfying crunch and help keep you feeling full and satisfied.

  • Raw cacao powder: Cacao powder is made from cold-pressed cacao beans, which are rich in antioxidants and mood-boosting compounds. It adds a rich chocolate flavor to these bliss balls without the added sugar and artificial ingredients found in conventional chocolate.

  • Coconut oil: This healthy fat is known for its many benefits, including supporting brain health, boosting metabolism, and promoting healthy skin. It also helps bind the bliss ball mixture together and adds a subtle coconut flavor.

Conclusion

These Chocolate Coconut Bliss Balls are not only a delicious treat but also a nutritious snack option that will keep you fueled throughout the day. Whether you enjoy them as a mid-afternoon pick-me-up or a post-workout snack, these bliss balls are sure to satisfy your cravings and provide a boost of energy. Give them a try and let us know what you think! Enjoy!
