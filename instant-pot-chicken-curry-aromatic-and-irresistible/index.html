Instant Pot Chicken Curry: Aromatic and Irresistible


Curry lovers, rejoice! Today, I bring you a recipe that will transport your taste buds to the exotic flavors of India. Get ready to indulge in the aromatic and irresistible Instant Pot Chicken Curry. This dish is not only packed with bold flavors but also incredibly easy to make, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot. So, let’s dive right in and explore the steps to create this mouthwatering masterpiece.

Ingredients

To create this tantalizing Instant Pot Chicken Curry, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon of salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnishing

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the oil. Once the oil is hot, add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. This will take about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot and sauté for another minute until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. Now, it’s time to add the chicken. Place the chicken pieces in the pot and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides. This will take approximately 5 minutes.

  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the curry powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Sprinkle this spice mixture over the chicken and stir well to coat the chicken evenly.

  5. Pour in the pureed tomatoes and salt, and give everything a good stir. Allow the mixture to simmer for a couple of minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  6. Next, it’s time to add the star ingredient – coconut milk. Pour in the coconut milk and give the curry a gentle stir to combine all the ingredients.

  7. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure valve to sealing. Select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” mode and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

  8. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, and then carefully perform a quick release by turning the pressure valve to venting.

  9. Open the Instant Pot and give the curry a good stir. If you prefer a thicker consistency, you can simmer the curry for a few minutes on the sauté mode until it reaches your desired thickness.

  10. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Curry hot, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves. This delectable dish pairs perfectly with steamed rice or warm naan bread.

Final Thoughts

There you have it – an aromatic and irresistible Instant Pot Chicken Curry that will take your taste buds on a culinary adventure. The combination of bold spices, tender chicken, and creamy coconut milk creates a harmonious symphony of flavors that will leave you craving for more.

The convenience of the Instant Pot makes this recipe a breeze to prepare, allowing you to enjoy the rich flavors of a slow-cooked curry in a fraction of the time. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor the taste of India in the comfort of your own home.

Remember, cooking is an art, and you have the creative freedom to adjust the spices and seasonings according to your personal taste. Feel free to experiment and make this Instant Pot Chicken Curry truly your own. Happy cooking!
