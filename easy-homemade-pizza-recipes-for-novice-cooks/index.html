Easy Homemade Pizza Recipes for Novice Cooks


Are you a pizza lover who is craving a delicious homemade pizza but doesn’t know where to start? Look no further! In this blog post, I will share with you some easy homemade pizza recipes that are perfect for novice cooks. Whether you’re a beginner in the kitchen or just looking for a quick and tasty meal, these recipes will have you whipping up mouthwatering pizzas in no time.

Classic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with extra virgin olive oil.
  10. Slice and serve!

BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh cilantro leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the barbecue sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded chicken and red onion over the sauce.
  6. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves, if desired.
  10. Slice and serve!

Veggie Supreme Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted vegetables (such as bell peppers, mushrooms, onions, olives, etc.)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the assorted vegetables on top of the cheese.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.
  10. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  11. Slice and serve!

Conclusion

Making homemade pizza doesn’t have to be intimidating, especially for novice cooks. With these easy recipes, you can enjoy the satisfaction of creating your own delicious pizzas right in your own kitchen. Experiment with different toppings and flavors to customize your pizzas to your liking. So, put on your apron, preheat that oven, and get ready to indulge in the cheesy goodness of homemade pizza!
