Easy Mediterranean Vegetable Stir-Fry Recipe


If you're looking for a quick, flavorful, and healthy meal option that can be prepared in just 20 minutes, look no further than this Mediterranean vegetable stir-fry recipe. This dish is perfect for vegetarians and vegans alike, and can be customized to suit your dietary preferences. Ingredients To make this recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

2 tablespoons olive oil 1 small onion, sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 red bell pepper, sliced 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced 1 small eggplant, sliced 1 cup chopped fresh tomatoes 1 cup cooked chickpeas 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley 1 tablespoon lemon juice 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional) Feta cheese (optional)

Equipment You will need the following equipment to make this recipe:

Large skillet or wok Cutting board Knife Measuring cups and spoons Wooden spoon

Instructions To make this recipe, follow these steps: Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients Start by preparing the ingredients. Rinse the sliced vegetables under cold water to remove any dirt or debris. Pat the vegetables dry with a paper towel to prevent them from steaming instead of sautéing. Step 2: Heat the Olive Oil Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the sliced onion and sauté until it is translucent. Step 3: Add the Garlic and Bell Peppers Add the minced garlic and sliced bell peppers to the skillet. Sauté for 2-3 minutes, or until the bell peppers are tender. Step 4: Add the Eggplant Add the sliced eggplant to the skillet and sauté for 2-3 minutes, or until it is tender. Step 5: Add the Tomatoes and Chickpeas Add the chopped fresh tomatoes and cooked chickpeas to the skillet. Stir to combine with the eggplant and bell peppers. Step 6: Season the Dish Season the dish with salt, pepper, and dried oregano. If you like a little heat, add the cayenne pepper. Stir well to combine. Step 7: Add the Lemon Juice and Parsley Stir in the lemon juice and chopped fresh parsley. Step 8: Serve the Dish Serve the Mediterranean vegetable stir-fry hot, garnished with crumbled feta cheese if desired. Serving Suggestions This recipe is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a quick lunch. You can serve it as is, or add it to a bed of quinoa or couscous for a filling meal. The combination of vegetables and chickpeas makes this dish a great source of protein and fiber. Nutrition Information This recipe is a nutrient-dense meal option that is low in calories and high in flavor. The olive oil provides healthy fats, while the tomatoes and bell peppers provide vitamin C and lycopene. The chickpeas provide protein and fiber, making this dish a great option for vegetarians and vegans. Tips and Variations To make this dish more substantial, you can add cooked lamb or chicken to it. You can also add other vegetables such as zucchini or mushrooms to the dish. For a spicy kick, add more cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes. For a tangy twist, add a tablespoon of freshly squeezed lemon juice. Conclusion This Mediterranean vegetable stir-fry recipe is a delicious and nutritious meal option that can be prepared in just 20 minutes. With the combination of vegetables, chickpeas, and olive oil, this dish is a great way to add some flavor and nutrients to your meal.   • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small eggplant, sliced
  • 1 cup chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Feta cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the ingredients.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Sauté the onion, garlic, and bell peppers until the bell peppers are tender.
  4. Add the eggplant and sauté until it is tender.
  5. Add the tomatoes and chickpeas and stir to combine.
  6. Season the dish with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
  7. Stir in the lemon juice and chopped parsley.
  8. Serve the dish hot, garnished with crumbled feta cheese if desired.

Serving Suggestions:

Serve the dish as is, or add it to a bed of quinoa or couscous for a filling meal.

Nutrition Information:

This recipe is a nutrient-dense meal option that is low in calories and high in flavor.

Tips and Variations:

Add cooked lamb or chicken to make the dish more substantial. Add other vegetables such as zucchini or mushrooms for more flavor and nutrients.
