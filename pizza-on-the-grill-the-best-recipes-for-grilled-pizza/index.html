Pizza on the Grill: The Best Recipes for Grilled Pizza


Grilled pizza has taken the culinary world by storm. It brings a fresh twist to a classic favorite, combining smoky flavors with crispy, golden crusts. There’s something incredibly satisfying about preparing pizza on the grill, whether you’re hosting a summer party, a casual family dinner, or just craving something different. Grilled pizza is easy to make, incredibly customizable, and a fun cooking experience that everyone can enjoy.

In this post, we’ll dive deep into the best recipes for grilled pizza, share tips for perfecting your grilled pie, and explore different flavor combinations that will elevate your pizza game.

Why Grill Pizza?

Before we jump into the recipes, let’s first explore why grilling pizza is so popular and how it changes the way we enjoy this beloved dish.

1. Unique Flavor

Grilling pizza imparts a smoky, charred flavor that a traditional oven simply can’t replicate. The heat of the grill sears the crust, creating a crispy, slightly smoky outer layer while keeping the inside soft and chewy.

2. Quick Cooking Time

Grilled pizza cooks faster than baked pizza. Since the grill reaches higher temperatures than most ovens, it cooks the pizza in just a few minutes. This means less waiting around and more time enjoying your meal.

3. Fun and Interactive

Grilling pizza is an interactive cooking experience. It’s perfect for gatherings where everyone can top their pizza however they like. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or a pizza-making party, grilling pizza is a fun and engaging activity.

4. No Need for a Pizza Stone

Traditional oven-baked pizzas often require a pizza stone or steel to achieve the desired crispiness. With grilling, you can skip the stone and still achieve a perfectly crisp crust.

Now that we know why grilled pizza is so great, let’s dive into the best recipes to try on your grill.

Basic Grilled Pizza Dough Recipe

Grilled pizza starts with the right dough. This basic pizza dough recipe will serve as your foundation for a variety of pizza combinations. It’s easy to prepare, and with just a few ingredients, you’ll have a dough that can be grilled to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F)
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour (plus extra for dusting)
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Stir gently and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.

  2. Mix the Dough: In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture along with the olive oil. Stir with a wooden spoon until a dough begins to form.

  3. Knead the Dough: Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 8-10 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. Add a little flour if the dough is too sticky.

  4. First Rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

  5. Prepare for Grilling: Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 2-3 portions, depending on how large you want your pizzas. Roll each portion into a ball and let them rest for 10-15 minutes.

Now you’re ready to grill! Make sure your grill is preheated to a medium-high heat. You can use a gas grill or charcoal grill; both work well for pizza.

Tips for Grilling Pizza

Before we dive into the different pizza recipes, here are a few tips to ensure your grilled pizza turns out perfectly every time:

  • Use a pizza peel or flat spatula: A pizza peel helps you transfer the pizza onto the grill without it sticking or losing its shape. If you don’t have a pizza peel, you can use a flat spatula or even a baking sheet as a makeshift solution.

  • Preheat the grill: Ensure that your grill is preheated and very hot before placing the pizza on it. A hot grill will give you the crispy, charred crust you’re looking for.

  • Don’t overload with toppings: Grilled pizza has a delicate balance. If you overload it with too many toppings, the crust might not cook evenly. Keep toppings light and ensure everything is sliced thin to cook quickly on the grill.

  • Use indirect heat: If you’re worried about burning the pizza, you can grill it using indirect heat. For a gas grill, simply turn one burner off and place the pizza on the side that is off. On a charcoal grill, push the coals to one side and cook the pizza on the other side.

Now that you have the basics, let’s explore some mouth-watering grilled pizza recipes!

1. Margherita Grilled Pizza

This classic pizza is a fan favorite and perfect for grilling. The fresh, tangy tomato sauce combined with creamy mozzarella and fresh basil leaves creates a delicious, simple pie that’s packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 batch of basic pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: Roll out the dough to your desired thickness and shape.

  2. Grill the Pizza Crust: Place the dough on the preheated grill. Close the lid and cook for about 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden and crispy. Flip the dough and cook for another 1-2 minutes.

  3. Add Toppings: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the grilled crust. Top with slices of mozzarella cheese, salt, and pepper to taste.

  4. Finish Grilling: Close the lid and continue grilling for another 3-4 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  5. Garnish: Once off the grill, add fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil. Slice and serve!

2. BBQ Chicken Grilled Pizza

If you’re craving a smoky, savory pizza, this BBQ chicken pizza will satisfy your taste buds. The combination of tender grilled chicken, tangy barbecue sauce, and melted cheese makes for an irresistible pizza.

Ingredients:

  • 1 batch of basic pizza dough
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • ½ cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup gouda cheese, shredded
  • Cilantro (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: Roll out the dough into a large circle or rectangle, depending on your grill size.

  2. Grill the Crust: Place the dough on the preheated grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until it starts to brown. Flip the dough over.

  3. Prepare the Toppings: In a bowl, toss the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce.

  4. Top the Pizza: Spread a thin layer of BBQ sauce on the grilled crust. Add the BBQ chicken, red onion slices, and a mixture of mozzarella and gouda cheese.

  5. Finish Grilling: Close the grill lid and cook for 4-5 minutes until the cheese is melted and the toppings are sizzling. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of flavor.

3. Veggie Lovers Grilled Pizza

For those who prefer a veggie-filled pizza, this grilled veggie pizza is the perfect choice. Loaded with fresh vegetables like bell peppers, mushrooms, zucchini, and spinach, this pizza is both colorful and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 batch of basic pizza dough
  • ½ cup marinara sauce or pesto sauce
  • 1 cup bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: Roll the dough into a circle or rectangle.

  2. Grill the Crust: Place the dough on the grill, cooking it for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden.

  3. Prepare the Veggies: Sauté the bell peppers, zucchini, and mushrooms in a skillet with olive oil over medium heat for about 5 minutes. You want the vegetables to soften a little but still maintain their texture.

  4. Top the Pizza: Spread a thin layer of marinara or pesto sauce on the grilled crust. Add the sautéed vegetables, fresh spinach, and shredded mozzarella cheese.

  5. Finish Grilling: Close the grill lid and cook for another 3-4 minutes, until the cheese melts and everything is heated through.

4. Pepperoni and Mushroom Grilled Pizza

For those who love a meat-filled pizza, pepperoni and mushroom grilled pizza is a great choice. The spicy pepperoni and earthy mushrooms complement each other perfectly on the smoky grilled crust.

Ingredients:

  • 1 batch of basic pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 ½ cups mozzarella cheese, shredded

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: Roll out the dough to your desired size.

  2. Grill the Crust: Place the dough on the grill, cooking it for about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Top the Pizza: Spread tomato sauce over the grilled crust, then add the pepperoni, mushrooms, and shredded mozzarella cheese.

  4. Finish Grilling: Close the lid and cook for 3-4 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Conclusion

Grilled pizza is a fun and flavorful way to enjoy this beloved dish. Whether you’re making a classic Margherita, a smoky BBQ chicken pie, or a veggie-packed delight, grilling your pizza adds a unique twist that will impress your family and friends. So fire up the grill, grab your favorite toppings, and enjoy the delicious, smoky goodness of grilled pizza!

Happy grilling!
