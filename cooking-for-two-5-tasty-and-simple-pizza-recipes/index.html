Cooking for Two: 5 Tasty and Simple Pizza Recipes


Whether you’re cooking for a romantic date night or simply enjoying a cozy dinner at home with a friend, homemade pizza is always a great idea. Not only are pizzas easy to prepare, but they also offer an excellent opportunity to get creative and try out different combinations of flavors. If you’ve ever wondered how to make pizza that’s tailored for two people, look no further. Below, we’ll explore five mouthwatering pizza recipes that are simple, flavorful, and perfectly portioned for a pair.

1. Classic Margherita Pizza for Two

Known for its simple yet delicious ingredients, Margherita pizza is always a hit. This recipe honors the traditional preparation while scaling it down for two.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ball of pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 large fresh mozzarella ball, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle, about 10-12 inches wide.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly across the dough, leaving about half an inch as a border.
  4. Add slices of fresh mozzarella on top, spacing them evenly.
  5. Bake the pizza on the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet for 8-10 minutes or until the crust is golden and crispy.
  6. Remove from the oven, top with fresh basil leaves, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle a pinch of salt.

This pizza is light, flavorful, and perfect for a quiet evening in.

2. BBQ Chicken Pizza

This sweet-and-savory pizza recipe is ideal for those who love barbecue flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ball of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C) with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a 10-12 inch round base.
  3. Spread a thin layer of barbecue sauce, reserving a little for the chicken.
  4. Toss the shredded chicken in the remaining sauce and distribute it evenly over the pizza.
  5. Scatter the red onion slices across the top, followed by the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake the pizza for 10 minutes or until the crust is crisp and the cheese is bubbling.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired, and serve immediately.

The tangy barbecue sauce combined with melted cheese and tender chicken creates an irresistible flavor you’ll both love.

3. Veggie Lover’s Delight

For vegetarians—or simply veggie enthusiasts—this pizza is colorful, nutrient-packed, and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ball of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • Handful of baby spinach leaves
  • Dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to your desired size.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly across the surface.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese as the base layer.
  5. Distribute the vegetables evenly: cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and spinach leaves.
  6. Finish with a light sprinkle of dried oregano.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the edges of the crust are golden and the cheese is melted.
  8. Slice and enjoy a vibrant and flavorful pizza packed with fresh produce.

4. Pepperoni and Jalapeño Lovers’ Pizza

If you’re craving something a bit spicy, this bold pizza is for you.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ball of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 15 slices pepperoni
  • 1 fresh jalapeño, thinly sliced
  • Red chili flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C) with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a 10-12 inch base.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Layer on the shredded mozzarella cheese, pepperoni slices, and jalapeño slices.
  5. Bake the pizza for 8-10 minutes, or until the crust is brown and crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbling.
  6. Sprinkle red chili flakes on top for an extra kick, if desired.

The combination of spicy toppings and rich pepperoni makes every bite exciting.

5. White Garden Pizza

For a unique twist, this white pizza skips the traditional tomato sauce in favor of creamy ricotta and garlic flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ball of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup artichoke hearts, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C) and heat a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a round or oval shape.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the ricotta mixture evenly across the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella on top, followed by the thinly sliced zucchini and artichoke hearts.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil over the veggies.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove and serve immediately for a creamy, garlicky pizza experience.

Tips for Perfect Pizza Every Time:

  1. Use High Heat: Always preheat your oven to its highest temperature for the crispiest crust.
  2. Invest in a Pizza Stone or Steel: These tools help mimic a pizzeria oven by evenly distributing heat.
  3. Keep Ingredients Fresh: Fresh cheeses, veggies, and sauces elevate the flavor of even the simplest recipes.
  4. Don’t Overload: Less is more. Too many toppings can make your pizza soggy.

Experimenting with these five fun and easy pizza recipes is a great way to enjoy quality time in the kitchen for two. Whether you have a penchant for classic combinations or love exploring unconventional flavors, these ideas will help you create memorable, shareable meals that hit the spot. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and prepare to impress your dining companion with some homemade pizza magic!
