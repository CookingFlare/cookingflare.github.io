Best of Bananas: 10 Creamy Banana Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Bananas, those sweet and versatile fruits, are truly a gift to any kitchen, especially if you’re looking to whip up some delicious and comforting smoothies or juices. Not only are these ten creamy banana juice and smoothie recipes perfect for any time of the day, but they’re also incredibly simple to prepare – plus, they pack a nutritious punch that can do wonders for your overall health!

So, let’s get started on this delightful banana journey and explore our top picks!

1. Classic Banana Smoothie

Who could forget the all-time favorite banana smoothie? This tried-and-true combination of bananas, milk, honey, and vanilla extract makes for a delightful breakfast or an after-dinner treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine all ingredients until the mixture turns smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass, serve immediately, and enjoy!

2. Banana Berry Smoothie

If you’re looking to add a twist to your usual banana smoothie, try this Banana Berry Smoothie. The addition of fresh berries, spinach, and almond milk makes it a perfect health booster.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon of honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Adjust the sweetness by adding honey if desired.
  3. Sip and savor the delectable blend of flavors!

3. Creamy Banana Almond Butter Smoothie

Spread the love with this creamy concoction – an absolute treat for almond butter enthusiasts. Feel free to substitute almond butter with any nut or seed butter of your choice for a delightful twist.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 tablespoons of almond butter
  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Whip all the ingredients together until you achieve a smooth finish.
  2. Let the flavors meld magically in your mouth!

4. Banana Pineapple Juice

Try this refreshing Banana Pineapple Juice for a tropical spin. The blend of ripe bananas, fresh pineapple, ginger, and lime juice creates a guaranteed burst of sunshine in a glass!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup of chopped fresh pineapple
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 cup of water

Instructions:

  1. Blend together the bananas, pineapple, ginger, and lime juice until fully combined.
  2. Add water to reach your desired consistency, and blend again.
  3. Enjoy a taste of the tropics at any time!

5. Chocolate Banana Smoothie

Indulge in guilt-free decadence with this chocolate banana smoothie. Made with ripe bananas, cocoa powder, peanut butter, and almond milk, it comes together for the perfect lazy weekend treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Combine banana, cocoa powder, peanut butter, almond milk, and vanilla extract in a blender.
  2. Blend well until fully emulsified and smooth.
  3. Scoop up your dessert disguised as breakfast!

6. Banana Carrot Juice

Don’t knock it ’till you’ve tried it! This surprisingly refreshing banana carrot juice takes advantage of the natural sweetness of bananas and carrots, while also packing a nutritional punch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 1 cup of water

Instructions:

  1. Peel and chop the carrot and add it to the blender.
  2. Add bananas and water, blend until smooth.
  3. Serve and taste the unexpected goodness!

7. Frozen Banana Peanut Butter Smoothie

Prepare to fall in love with this frozen banana peanut butter smoothie. Creamy, satisfying, and easy to make – it’ll be your go-to recipe in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas (frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine frozen bananas, peanut butter, almond milk, honey, and vanilla.
  2. Blend all the ingredients until the smoothie reaches a glamorous consistency.
  3. Enjoy the heavenly taste!

8. Banana Orange Juice

Bright, zesty, and invigorating, this banana orange juice will make you feel like you’re on a beach vacation, no matter where you are.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 medium oranges
  • 1 cup of water

Instructions:

  1. Juice the oranges and add the juice to the blender, followed by bananas and water.
  2. Blend until it’s smooth and enjoy!
  3. Sunshine in a glass!

9. Banana Avocado Green Smoothie

Super green and super creamy, the combination of bananas, avocado, kale, and almond milk is a match made in heaven. Packed with nutrients, this smoothie is a fantastic way to fuel your body and mind!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 cup of packed kale leaves
  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon of honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine banana, avocado, kale, almond milk, and honey.
  2. Whisk it all up and grab a spoon; breakfast is served!
  3. Health never tasted so good.

10. Banana Coconut Milk Smoothie

For a refreshing departure from the usual, try this Banana Coconut Milk Smoothie. Surprisingly light and creamy, this recipe plays up the natural sweetness of the banana while delivering a pleasant coconut flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup of coconut milk (from a carton)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Simply blend banana, coconut milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass, sip, and savor!

Now that you have ten amazing banana juice and smoothie recipes in your repertoire, you’re ready to impress your family and friends with your newfound smoothie and juice crafting skills! But the best part – they’re not only easy to make but also ensure you’re keeping your well-being in check. So, don’t hesitate to experiment and customize these recipes as you see fit. Cheers to creativity and deliciousness!

Remember, these creamy concoctions are only the beginning. Who knows what other fantastic juice and smoothie discoveries await you! Happy blending!
