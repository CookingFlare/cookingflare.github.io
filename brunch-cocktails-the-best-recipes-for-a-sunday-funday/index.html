Brunch Cocktails: The Best Recipes for a Sunday Funday


Brunch is not just a meal; it’s a celebration of the weekend, a chance to unwind, and an opportunity to gather with friends and family. What makes brunch even more delightful? Cocktails, of course! A well-crafted brunch cocktail can elevate your Sunday Funday from ordinary to extraordinary. In this blog post, we’ll dive into some of the best brunch cocktail recipes that will impress your guests and leave everyone feeling festive.

The Classic Mimosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 part champagne or sparkling wine
  • 1 part fresh orange juice
  • Fresh orange slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Chill the Ingredients: Ensure your champagne and orange juice are well chilled.
  2. Combine: In a champagne flute, pour in the orange juice first, followed by the champagne.
  3. Garnish: Add a fresh orange slice to the rim of the glass.
  4. Serve: Enjoy immediately and toast to a beautiful brunch!

The Mimosa is a staple in brunch cocktails. Its refreshing taste and bubbly nature make it a perfect complement to a variety of brunch dishes. Whether you’re serving pancakes, eggs benedict, or a fruit salad, this drink pairs well.

Bloody Mary: A Savory Favorite

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 oz vodka
  • 3 oz tomato juice
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • 2 dashes of Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 dashes of hot sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Celery stalk and lemon wedge for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix: In a shaker, combine vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shake: Fill the shaker with ice and shake gently to combine.
  3. Strain: Pour the mixture into a tall glass filled with ice.
  4. Garnish: Add a celery stalk and a lemon wedge to the rim.
  5. Serve: Sip slowly and savor the complex flavors!

The Bloody Mary is known for its savory profile and is often touted as the perfect hangover remedy. With endless garnish possibilities—from pickles to olives to even bacon—this cocktail is as versatile as it is delicious.

Bellini: A Fruity Twist

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz peach puree
  • 4 oz prosecco
  • Fresh peach slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Peach Puree: Blend ripe peaches until smooth. Strain if you prefer a silkier texture.
  2. Combine: In a champagne flute, add the peach puree first, then gently pour in the prosecco.
  3. Garnish: Top with a slice of fresh peach.
  4. Serve: Enjoy this fruity delight on a sunny afternoon!

The Bellini is a delightful Italian cocktail that brings the essence of summer to your brunch table. Its sweet, fruity flavor and effervescence make it an excellent choice for warm-weather gatherings.

Spicy Paloma: A Zesty Kick

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz agave syrup
  • Club soda
  • Salt for rimming the glass
  • Grapefruit slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Glass: Rim your glass with salt by running a lime wedge around the edge and dipping it in salt.
  2. Mix: In a shaker, combine tequila, grapefruit juice, lime juice, and agave syrup. Shake well.
  3. Pour: Fill the prepared glass with ice and strain the mixture over it. Top with club soda.
  4. Garnish: Add a slice of grapefruit.
  5. Serve: Enjoy the refreshing zing!

The Spicy Paloma is a fantastic way to spice up your brunch. The combination of tequila and grapefruit juice creates a refreshing yet bold cocktail that’s sure to awaken your taste buds.

Lavender Collins: A Floral Delight

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • 1 oz lavender syrup
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • Club soda
  • Lavender sprig for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix: In a shaker, combine gin, lavender syrup, and lemon juice. Shake well with ice.
  2. Pour: Strain into a tall glass filled with ice and top with club soda.
  3. Garnish: Add a sprig of lavender for a beautiful presentation.
  4. Serve: This cocktail is perfect for those who appreciate floral flavors.

The Lavender Collins offers a unique twist on the classic Tom Collins. The floral notes of lavender pair beautifully with gin, creating a refreshing and aromatic drink that’s perfect for spring and summer brunches.

Espresso Martini: For Coffee Lovers

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz coffee liqueur
  • 1 oz freshly brewed espresso
  • Coffee beans for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Espresso: Brew a fresh shot of espresso and let it cool slightly.
  2. Mix: In a shaker, combine vodka, coffee liqueur, and espresso. Shake vigorously with ice.
  3. Strain: Pour the mixture into a chilled martini glass.
  4. Garnish: Top with a few coffee beans.
  5. Serve: Enjoy this energizing cocktail!

The Espresso Martini is perfect for brunches that extend into the afternoon. It’s a delightful way to combine your love for coffee with the excitement of a cocktail. This drink will keep the energy flowing and spirits high!

Ginger Mojito: A Refreshing Twist on a Classic

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz ginger syrup
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Club soda
  • Lime wedge and mint sprig for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Muddle: In a glass, muddle the mint leaves and lime juice to release the mint’s aroma.
  2. Combine: Add the ginger syrup and rum, then fill the glass with ice.
  3. Top: Fill with club soda and stir gently.
  4. Garnish: Add a lime wedge and a sprig of mint.
  5. Serve: Sip on this refreshing cocktail!

The Ginger Mojito is a zesty and invigorating drink that adds a twist to the traditional mojito. The ginger syrup gives it a distinctive flavor that sets it apart from the crowd.

The Tropical Mimosa: A Paradise in a Glass

Ingredients:

  • 1 part champagne or sparkling wine
  • 1 part pineapple juice
  • 1 part coconut water
  • Pineapple slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Chill the Ingredients: Ensure all ingredients are cold.
  2. Combine: In a glass, pour the pineapple juice and coconut water, followed by the champagne.
  3. Garnish: Add a slice of pineapple for a tropical touch.
  4. Serve: Enjoy this refreshing cocktail on a sunny brunch day!

The Tropical Mimosa transports you straight to the beach. The combination of pineapple juice and coconut water gives a refreshing, tropical twist to the classic mimosa.

Conclusion: Cheers to Sunday Funday!

Brunch cocktails are an essential part of any Sunday Funday. They add a festive touch to your gathering and can be customized to suit any palate. From the classic Mimosa to the bold Spicy Paloma, these cocktails are sure to impress your guests and make your brunch memorable.

Experiment with these recipes, mix and match ingredients, and don’t hesitate to create your own signature brunch cocktail. Cheers to a delightful Sunday filled with great company, delicious food, and refreshing drinks!
