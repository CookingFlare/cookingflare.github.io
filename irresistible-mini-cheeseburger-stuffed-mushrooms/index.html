Irresistible Mini Cheeseburger Stuffed Mushrooms


If you’re a fan of burgers and mushrooms, then you’re in for a real treat with this recipe. These mini cheeseburger stuffed mushrooms are a delightful twist on the classic cheeseburger, offering a savory and satisfying appetizer or snack that’s sure to impress your guests. The juicy, flavorful combination of ground beef, cheese, and mushrooms creates a mouthwatering bite-sized dish that will leave everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 24 large white mushrooms
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup mustard
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.

  2. Clean the mushrooms with a damp paper towel to remove any dirt. Remove the stems and use a small spoon to gently scrape out the gills to create a hollow space for the filling.

  3. In a skillet over medium heat, heat the vegetable oil. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until they become soft and translucent.

  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until it is browned and fully cooked. Drain any excess fat and return the skillet to the heat.

  5. Season the beef with salt, pepper, and Worcestershire sauce. Stir well to combine, then remove the skillet from the heat.

  6. In a small bowl, mix together the ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise to create a special cheeseburger sauce.

  7. Place the hollowed-out mushrooms on the prepared baking sheet. Spoon a small amount of the cheeseburger sauce into each mushroom, then fill them generously with the seasoned ground beef mixture.

  8. Top each mushroom with a generous sprinkle of shredded cheddar cheese.

  9. Bake the stuffed mushrooms in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the mushrooms are tender.

  10. Once they are ready, remove the mushrooms from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These mini cheeseburger stuffed mushrooms are best enjoyed hot and fresh from the oven. They make a fantastic appetizer for parties, game nights, or any occasion where you want to impress your guests with a unique and delicious snack. You can also serve them as a fun and flavorful addition to your next barbecue or potluck.

Pair these irresistible mini cheeseburger stuffed mushrooms with your favorite dipping sauces, such as ranch dressing, barbecue sauce, or even more of the cheeseburger sauce for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

With their savory filling and delightful presentation, these mini cheeseburger stuffed mushrooms are a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. The combination of juicy ground beef, gooey cheese, and earthy mushrooms creates a flavor explosion that will have everyone reaching for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a creative twist on a classic dish, these irresistible stuffed mushrooms are the perfect choice for any occasion. Enjoy the delicious fusion of flavors and textures in every bite!
