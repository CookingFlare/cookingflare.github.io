Delightful Dip: Artichoke and Spinach Springtime Spread


Springtime calls for fresh, vibrant flavors that awaken the senses and elevate any gathering. This Artichoke and Spinach Springtime Spread is a delightful dip that embodies the essence of the season. Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, this recipe is a must-try for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1 cup frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 French baguette, sliced and toasted (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped artichoke hearts, drained spinach, grated Parmesan cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  3. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  4. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the dip is hot and bubbly, and the top is golden brown.

  5. Once done, remove the dip from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  6. Serve the Artichoke and Spinach Springtime Spread warm, alongside the toasted French baguette slices.

Tips and Variations

  • For added freshness, consider adding a squeeze of lemon zest to the dip before baking.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, sprinkle some additional red pepper flakes on top of the dip before serving.
  • This dip pairs beautifully with an assortment of fresh vegetables for a colorful and healthy option.
