Quick and Easy Beef and Snow Pea Stir Fry


If you’re looking for a delicious and quick meal to whip up after a long day, this Beef and Snow Pea Stir Fry recipe is just what you need. Packed with flavor, vibrant colors, and a satisfying crunch, this stir fry is sure to become a regular in your weeknight dinner rotation. The best part? It takes just a few simple ingredients and minimal time to prepare, making it a perfect option for busy individuals and families.

Ingredients

For the Stir Fry:

  • 1 pound (450g) beef steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cups (200g) snow peas, ends trimmed
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Beef: In a bowl, combine the beef slices with soy sauce, cornstarch, and a pinch of black pepper. Toss to coat the beef evenly and set aside to marinate while you prepare the vegetables.

  2. Heat the Wok: In a wok or large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until it’s browned. Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside on a plate.

  3. Cook the Vegetables: In the same wok, add a little more oil if needed, then add the snow peas, red bell pepper, and yellow bell pepper. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  4. Combine and Flavor: Push the vegetables to the side of the wok and add the minced garlic and ginger to the center. Stir for 30 seconds until fragrant, then combine with the vegetables. Return the cooked beef to the wok and stir to combine everything.

  5. Add the Sauces: Pour in the oyster sauce and a splash of sesame oil. Toss everything together until well coated and heated through. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

  6. Serve: Once the stir fry is ready, remove it from the heat and serve immediately over cooked rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations

  • To add a touch of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes or drizzle with a bit of chili oil.
  • For a vegetarian version, substitute the beef with tofu or your favorite meat alternatives.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables to your liking. Broccoli, snap peas, or baby corn would all be excellent additions to this stir fry.
  • For a gluten-free option, use tamari instead of soy sauce and ensure the oyster sauce is gluten-free as well.

Final Thoughts

This Quick and Easy Beef and Snow Pea Stir Fry is not only a delightful dish to prepare, but it’s also a versatile one. With a beautifully balanced combination of flavors and a colorful presentation, it’s a perfect choice for a speedy weeknight dinner or a relaxed weekend lunch. Plus, the simplicity of the ingredients and the speed of preparation make it an ideal choice for those times when you need a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. So, the next time you find yourself craving a flavorful and fuss-free meal, give this stir fry a try and savor the delightful results!
