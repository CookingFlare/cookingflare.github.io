Beef and Mushroom Pie: A Savory Comfort Delight


If you’re looking for a hearty and comforting dish that will satisfy your cravings, look no further than this delicious Beef and Mushroom Pie. Packed with savory flavors and a buttery, flaky crust, this pie is the epitome of comfort food. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy meal at home, this recipe is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the details of creating this savory delight!

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 1 ½ pounds of beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh thyme and rosemary, chopped

For the Crust:

  • 2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, cold and diced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 6-8 tablespoons of ice water

Instructions

Making the Filling:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef cubes and brown them on all sides. Once browned, remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and garlic. Sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the mushrooms, carrots, and celery to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are slightly softened.
  4. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir to coat evenly.
  5. Gradually pour in the beef broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Add the tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper, and herbs.
  6. Return the browned beef to the skillet. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook until the beef is tender and the sauce has thickened.

Preparing the Crust:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold, diced butter.
  2. Use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together.
  4. Divide the dough into two equal portions and shape them into discs. Wrap them in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Assembling and Baking the Pie:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out one disc of the chilled dough on a floured surface to fit the bottom of a pie dish. Place it in the dish and trim any excess dough.
  3. Pour the beef and mushroom filling into the pie crust.
  4. Roll out the second disc of dough and place it over the filling. Trim and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  5. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.
  6. Optional: Brush the top crust with an egg wash for a golden finish.
  7. Bake the pie for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  8. Allow the pie to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Beef and Mushroom Pie pairs beautifully with a crisp green salad or steamed vegetables. For a comforting meal, serve it alongside creamy mashed potatoes or a side of buttery peas. A glass of full-bodied red wine or a refreshing iced tea complements the rich flavors of the pie perfectly.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Beef and Mushroom Pie is a true delight for anyone seeking a comforting and satisfying meal. With its tender beef, earthy mushrooms, and flaky crust, it’s a dish that brings warmth and flavor to any occasion. Whether enjoyed with family or shared with friends, this savory pie is a timeless classic that never fails to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey that will reward you with a delicious, homemade Beef and Mushroom Pie.
