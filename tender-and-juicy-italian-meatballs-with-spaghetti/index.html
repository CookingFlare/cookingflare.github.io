Tender and Juicy Italian Meatballs with Spaghetti


Mamma mia! Are you ready for a mouthwatering Italian feast? Today, I’m going to share with you a beloved recipe for tender and juicy Italian meatballs with spaghetti that will transport you straight to the streets of Italy. Get your aprons on and let’s dive into this delicious culinary adventure!

Ingredients

For the meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil

For the sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For serving:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, minced garlic, finely chopped onion, egg, salt, black pepper, dried oregano, and dried basil. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about the size of a golf ball. You should be able to make around 20 meatballs. Set them aside on a plate.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  4. Carefully add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the flavors. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15 minutes, allowing it to thicken.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  6. In the meantime, gently add the meatballs to the simmering sauce. Cover the skillet and let them cook for about 15 minutes, or until they are cooked through and juicy.

  7. Once the meatballs are cooked, remove them from the sauce and set them aside on a plate.

  8. To serve, place a generous portion of spaghetti on a plate or in a bowl. Top it with a ladleful of the sauce. Arrange a few meatballs on top of the spaghetti and drizzle some extra sauce over them.

  9. Garnish the dish with grated Parmesan cheese and torn fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor and freshness.

Buon appetito! You’re now ready to savor your tender and juicy Italian meatballs with spaghetti. This classic dish is perfect for a cozy family dinner or a gathering with friends. Serve it with a side of garlic bread and a crisp salad for a complete Italian experience.

Remember, cooking is all about love and creativity. Don’t be afraid to add your personal touch to this recipe. Whether it’s a sprinkle of chili flakes for a spicy kick or a handful of chopped fresh herbs for added freshness, make it your own and enjoy every bite!

So, roll up your sleeves and get cooking. Your taste buds will thank you for this delightful Italian feast. Happy cooking!
