Italian-Style Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta with Spinach and Feta


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering recipe for Italian-style chicken and mushroom creamy pasta with spinach and feta. This dish combines the richness of creamy pasta with the savory goodness of chicken and mushrooms, all brought together with the freshness of spinach and the tangy flavor of feta cheese. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just craving a comforting meal, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of chicken breast, sliced into strips
  • 8 oz of cremini or button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup of feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta (penne or fettuccine works well)
  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Chicken: In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Season the chicken strips with salt and pepper, then add them to the skillet. Cook the chicken until it’s golden brown and cooked through, then remove it from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. Saute the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Saute the chopped onion and minced garlic until they are soft and fragrant. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are golden brown and tender.

  4. Bring It All Together: Pour the chicken broth into the skillet and stir to deglaze the pan, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Add the heavy cream and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

  5. Add the Spinach and Feta: Once the sauce has thickened, add the fresh spinach leaves to the skillet. Let them wilt into the sauce, then stir in the crumbled feta cheese until it’s well combined.

  6. Combine with Pasta: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it with the creamy chicken and mushroom sauce until everything is well coated. Allow the pasta to heat through in the sauce for a couple of minutes.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the creamy pasta to serving plates, top with the cooked chicken strips, and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve immediately and savor the delicious flavors of this Italian-inspired dish.

Tips:

  • For a lighter version, you can use half-and-half or whole milk instead of heavy cream, although the sauce won’t be as rich.
  • Feel free to add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat, or a squeeze of lemon juice for a hint of brightness.

Final Thoughts:

This Italian-style chicken and mushroom creamy pasta with spinach and feta is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will surely delight your senses. The creamy sauce, tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and vibrant spinach come together to create a truly satisfying dish that’s perfect for a cozy dinner at home or for entertaining guests. So, roll up your sleeves, get your ingredients ready, and prepare to indulge in a bowl of pure comfort and deliciousness. Buon Appetito!
