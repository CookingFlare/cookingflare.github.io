Savory Garlic and Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts for Christmas


Are you looking for a delicious side dish to impress your guests this Christmas? Look no further! This Savory Garlic and Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts recipe is the perfect addition to your holiday feast. With its crispy texture, bold flavors, and festive presentation, it’s bound to be a crowd pleaser. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the Brussels sprouts, olive oil, minced garlic, grated Parmesan cheese, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Toss until the Brussels sprouts are evenly coated with the mixture.

  3. Spread the Brussels sprouts in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Make sure they are not overcrowded to ensure even roasting.

  4. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until the Brussels sprouts are tender and golden brown. Make sure to stir the Brussels sprouts halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  5. Once the Brussels sprouts are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a serving dish.

  6. Serve the Savory Garlic and Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts immediately while they’re still hot. The combination of the crispy texture, garlicky flavor, and cheesy goodness will have your guests coming back for seconds!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add some extra crunch by sprinkling toasted pine nuts or chopped walnuts over the roasted Brussels sprouts before serving.
  • For a touch of freshness, squeeze some lemon juice over the roasted Brussels sprouts just before serving.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the seasoning mixture.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile to your liking. Some great options include rosemary, paprika, or even a dash of balsamic vinegar.

Conclusion

This Savory Garlic and Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts recipe is a simple yet elegant side dish that will elevate your Christmas dinner to another level. The combination of the nutty Brussels sprouts, aromatic garlic, and savory Parmesan cheese creates a medley of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a cozy family gathering or a festive holiday party, this dish is sure to impress. Give it a try this Christmas and watch as your guests fall in love with these crispy, flavorful Brussels sprouts. Happy cooking and Merry Christmas!
