Delicious Chinese Pork Belly: Recipes to Indulge In


If you’re a fan of succulent, melt-in-your-mouth meat, then Chinese pork belly should be at the top of your must-try list. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, these delicious recipes will have you indulging in flavors that will transport you straight to the bustling streets of China. From crispy crackling to tender meat, there’s a recipe for every palate. So grab your apron and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure!

1. Classic Chinese Roast Pork Belly

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (900g) pork belly, skin-on
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine
  • 1 tablespoon five-spice powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Score the pork belly skin with a sharp knife, being careful not to cut into the meat.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, honey, Shaoxing wine, five-spice powder, salt, and white pepper.
  4. Rub the marinade all over the pork belly, making sure to get it into the scored skin.
  5. Place the pork belly on a wire rack set inside a roasting pan, with the skin side up.
  6. Roast in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, then reduce the heat to 350°F (180°C) and continue roasting for another 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until the pork is cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve with steamed rice or noodles.

2. Braised Chinese Pork Belly with Soy Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (900g) pork belly, skin-on
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-inch piece ginger, sliced
  • 2 star anise
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the water, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, star anise, and cinnamon sticks. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add the pork belly to the pot, making sure it is fully submerged in the liquid.
  3. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 2-3 hours, or until the pork is tender.
  4. Remove the pork belly from the pot and let it cool slightly. Then, slice it into bite-sized pieces.
  5. Strain the braising liquid and return it to the pot. Bring it to a boil and cook until it has reduced and thickened slightly.
  6. Add the sliced pork belly back into the pot and toss to coat it in the sauce.
  7. Serve the braised pork belly over steamed rice, garnished with chopped green onions.

3. Crispy Chinese Pork Belly Bites

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (900g) pork belly, skin-on
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon Chinese five-spice powder
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Score the pork belly skin with a sharp knife, being careful not to cut into the meat.
  3. Rub the vegetable oil all over the pork belly, making sure to get it into the scored skin.
  4. Season the pork belly generously with salt, Chinese five-spice powder, and cornstarch.
  5. Place the pork belly on a wire rack set inside a baking sheet, with the skin side up.
  6. Roast in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the skin is crispy and golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing into bite-sized pieces.
  8. Serve the crispy pork belly bites as an appetizer or as part of a larger meal.

Conclusion

Chinese pork belly offers a tantalizing combination of flavors and textures that will leave you craving more. Whether you prefer it roasted, braised, or crispy, these recipes provide a delicious and indulgent experience that will transport you to the heart of Chinese cuisine. So, gather your ingredients and let your taste buds embark on a culinary journey they won’t soon forget. Enjoy!
