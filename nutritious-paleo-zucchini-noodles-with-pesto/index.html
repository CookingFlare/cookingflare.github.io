Nutritious Paleo Zucchini Noodles with Pesto


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that’s also paleo-friendly? Look no further! These zucchini noodles with pesto are a perfect choice for a healthy and satisfying dinner. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is a great way to enjoy a comforting pasta-like meal while staying true to your paleo lifestyle. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: grilled chicken or shrimp for added protein

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Zucchini Noodles

Using a spiralizer, create zucchini noodles by spiralizing the zucchinis. If you don’t have a spiralizer, you can use a vegetable peeler to create long, thin strips resembling noodles. Set the zucchini noodles aside while you prepare the pesto.

Step 2: Make the Pesto

In a food processor, combine the fresh basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, and nutritional yeast. Pulse the ingredients until they are finely chopped. While the food processor is running, slowly pour in the olive oil until the mixture is well combined and forms a smooth pesto. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3: Combine and Serve

In a large pan, heat a small amount of olive oil over medium heat. Add the zucchini noodles and sauté for 2-3 minutes, just until they are heated through. Be careful not to overcook the noodles, as they will become soggy.

Once the zucchini noodles are heated, add the prepared pesto to the pan and toss the noodles until they are evenly coated with the pesto. If desired, you can add grilled chicken or shrimp at this stage for extra protein.

Divide the zucchini noodles with pesto into serving bowls and garnish with additional pine nuts or fresh basil leaves for a beautiful presentation.

Nutritional Benefits

This paleo zucchini noodle dish offers a wealth of nutritional benefits. Zucchini is low in calories and high in fiber, making it a great choice for weight management and digestive health. It also provides essential vitamins and minerals, including vitamin C and potassium.

The pesto in this recipe adds a dose of healthy fats from the olive oil and pine nuts, as well as the antibacterial properties of fresh garlic. The nutritional yeast not only contributes a cheesy flavor to the pesto but also provides a source of B vitamins, making this dish a well-rounded and nourishing option for your paleo lifestyle.

Final Thoughts

Nutritious, flavorful, and easy to prepare, these paleo zucchini noodles with pesto are a must-try for anyone looking to incorporate more wholesome, paleo-friendly meals into their diet. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply seeking a lighter alternative to traditional pasta, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings while keeping your health goals on track.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a comforting meal, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of fresh zucchini noodles and vibrant pesto. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
