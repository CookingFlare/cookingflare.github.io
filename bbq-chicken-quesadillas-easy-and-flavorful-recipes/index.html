BBQ Chicken Quesadillas: Easy and Flavorful Recipes

3 min · 568 words

If you’re craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s both easy to make and bursting with flavor, look no further than BBQ Chicken Quesadillas. This simple yet mouthwatering recipe combines the smoky goodness of barbecue chicken with the cheesy goodness of quesadillas, resulting in a meal that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

To make these BBQ Chicken Quesadillas, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Olive oil, for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the cooked and shredded chicken with the barbecue sauce. Make sure the chicken is evenly coated with the sauce.

  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the sliced red onion and bell pepper. Sauté them until they are softened and slightly caramelized, about 5 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add another drizzle of olive oil and place one flour tortilla in the pan. Spread a layer of the barbecue chicken mixture evenly over the tortilla. Top with a handful of the sautéed red onion and bell pepper mixture, followed by a generous sprinkle of shredded cheddar cheese. Finally, sprinkle some chopped cilantro over the top.

  4. Place another flour tortilla on top to create a sandwich-like structure. Press down gently with a spatula to help the quesadilla hold together.

  5. Cook the quesadilla for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the tortilla is golden brown and crispy, and the cheese is melted.

  6. Remove the quesadilla from the skillet and transfer it to a cutting board. Let it cool for a minute before slicing it into wedges.

  7. Repeat the process with the remaining tortillas and fillings until all the quesadillas are cooked.

Serving Suggestions

BBQ Chicken Quesadillas are incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed on their own or served with a variety of side dishes. Here are a few serving suggestions to enhance your meal:

  • Fresh Guacamole: Whip up a batch of creamy guacamole to serve alongside your quesadillas. The coolness of the guacamole pairs perfectly with the smoky flavors of the chicken.

  • Salsa and Sour Cream: Add a dollop of salsa and sour cream to your plate for an extra burst of flavor and creaminess.

  • Side Salad: Serve the quesadillas with a simple side salad dressed with a tangy vinaigrette. The crispness of the salad will complement the richness of the quesadillas.

  • Mexican Rice: Complete the meal with a side of flavorful Mexican rice. The combination of the rice and quesadillas creates a well-rounded and satisfying dish.

Conclusion

BBQ Chicken Quesadillas are the perfect meal for those who crave a balance of smoky, cheesy, and flavorful goodness. With just a few simple ingredients and easy steps, you can create a delicious and satisfying dish that will impress your family and friends. Whether enjoyed as a main course or as a party appetizer, these quesadillas are sure to be a hit. So fire up the grill, grab your tortillas, and get ready to indulge in BBQ Chicken Quesadillas that are easy to make and bursting with flavor.
