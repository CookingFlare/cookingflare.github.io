Spicy Vegan Burger

如果您喜欢Spicy Food，可以尝试Spicy Vegan Burger。这个版本使用edamame和roasted red pepper来增加Spicy Flavor。

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked edamame
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped roasted red pepper
  • 1 minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Vegan Panko Breading
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 Vegan Buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and avocado for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mash the edamame using a fork.
  3. Add the cooked brown rice, chopped onion, roasted red pepper, garlic, olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Mix well.
  4. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty.
  5. Place the patties on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. In a small bowl, mix the Vegan Panko Breading and water. Brush the mixture evenly onto the patties.
  7. Bake the patties for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the Vegan Buns and adding lettuce, tomato, and avocado for topping.

Mushroom Vegan Burger

如果您喜欢Mushroom的味道，可以尝试Mushroom Vegan Burger。这个版本使用dried mushrooms和Worcestershire Sauce来增加Mushroom Flavor。

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped mushroom
  • 1 minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Vegan Panko Breading
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 Vegan Buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and avocado for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked brown rice, chopped onion, chopped mushroom, garlic, olive oil, thyme, rosemary, Worcestershire Sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty.
  4. Place the patties on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the Vegan Panko Breading and water. Brush the mixture evenly onto the patties.
  6. Bake the patties for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the Vegan Buns and adding lettuce, tomato, and avocado for topping.
