Delicious Cinnamon Apple Rolls: A Sweet and Spiced Treat


If there’s one thing that can fill a home with the aroma of comfort and warmth, it’s the scent of freshly baked cinnamon apple rolls. These delightful treats are a perfect combination of sweet, spiced apples and soft, fluffy dough, making them a beloved favorite for breakfast, brunch, or any time of day. In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the steps to create your own batch of delicious cinnamon apple rolls that will have your family and friends coming back for more.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened

For the Filling:

  • 4 large apples, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 1/2 cups of flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
  2. In a separate microwave-safe bowl, heat the water, milk, and butter until warm (about 120-130°F).
  3. Add the liquid mixture to the dry ingredients and stir to combine.
  4. Add the eggs and enough of the remaining flour to form a soft dough.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 6-8 minutes.
  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, turning once to grease the top. Cover and let it rise in a warm place until doubled, about 1 hour.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the diced apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the apples are tender, about 10 minutes.
  3. Remove from the heat and let the mixture cool to room temperature.

Step 3: Assemble the Rolls

  1. Punch the dough down and turn it onto a lightly floured surface.
  2. Roll the dough into a 16x12-inch rectangle.
  3. Brush the dough with the melted butter and spread the apple filling evenly over the dough.
  4. Starting with a long side, roll up the dough jelly-roll style; pinch the seam to seal.
  5. Cut the roll into 12 slices and place them in a greased 13x9-inch baking pan.
  6. Cover and let the rolls rise until doubled, about 30 minutes.

Step 4: Bake the Rolls

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Bake the rolls for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
  3. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly.

Step 5: Prepare the Glaze

  1. In a small bowl, combine the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. Drizzle the glaze over the warm rolls and serve.

Enjoy Your Delicious Cinnamon Apple Rolls!

These cinnamon apple rolls are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, with a warm cup of coffee or a cold glass of milk. The soft, buttery dough paired with the sweet and spiced apple filling creates a flavor combination that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re baking these rolls for a special occasion or just to treat yourself, they are sure to become a beloved addition to your baking repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to savor the delightful aroma and flavors of these delicious cinnamon apple rolls!
