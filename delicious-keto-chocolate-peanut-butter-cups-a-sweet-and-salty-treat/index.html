Delicious Keto Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups: A Sweet and Salty Treat


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of chocolate and peanut butter, then these delicious keto chocolate peanut butter cups are about to become your new obsession. Not only are they incredibly easy to make, but they’re also low in carbs and sugar, making them the perfect guilt-free indulgence for anyone following a ketogenic lifestyle.

Ingredients

For the Chocolate Coating:

  • 1 cup of sugar-free dark chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil

For the Peanut Butter Filling:

  • 1/2 cup of natural peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered erythritol
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chocolate Coating:

    • In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the sugar-free dark chocolate chips and coconut oil. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.

  2. Prepare the Peanut Butter Filling:

    • In a separate bowl, mix together the natural peanut butter, powdered erythritol, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until well combined.

  3. Assemble the Peanut Butter Cups:

    • Line a mini muffin tin with paper or silicone cupcake liners.
    • Spoon a small amount of the melted chocolate into the bottom of each liner, ensuring that the bottom is evenly covered.
    • Place the muffin tin in the freezer for 5 minutes to allow the chocolate to set.

  4. Add the Peanut Butter Filling:

    • Once the chocolate has set, remove the muffin tin from the freezer.
    • Spoon a small amount of the peanut butter filling on top of the chocolate layer in each cup, pressing it down gently to flatten.

  5. Complete with Chocolate Coating:

    • Pour the remaining melted chocolate over the peanut butter filling in each cup, making sure to cover it completely.

  6. Chill and Serve:

    • Return the muffin tin to the freezer and chill the peanut butter cups for at least 30 minutes, or until the chocolate is firm.
    • Once set, remove the peanut butter cups from the liners and store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator until ready to serve.

Nutritional Information

  • Servings: 12
  • Serving Size: 1 peanut butter cup
  • Calories: 115kcal
  • Total Fat: 10g
  • Saturated Fat: 5g
  • Cholesterol: 0mg
  • Sodium: 50mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Sugars: 0g
  • Protein: 2g

Final Thoughts

These keto chocolate peanut butter cups are the perfect blend of sweet and salty, making them an irresistible treat for anyone looking to satisfy their cravings while staying on track with their keto diet. Whether you enjoy them as a mid-afternoon pick-me-up or a post-dinner dessert, these homemade treats are sure to become a favorite in your keto recipe collection.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients and get ready to whip up a batch of these delectable keto chocolate peanut butter cups. Once you take that first indulgent bite, you’ll wonder how you ever lived without them!
