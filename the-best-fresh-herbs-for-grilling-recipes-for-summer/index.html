The Best Fresh Herbs for Grilling: Recipes for Summer


As summer approaches, it’s time to fire up the grill and embrace the vibrant flavors of the season. One of the best ways to enhance your grilled dishes is by incorporating fresh herbs. Not only do they add depth and complexity to your meals, but they also bring a burst of freshness that perfectly complements the smoky essence of grilled ingredients. In this guide, we’ll delve into the best fresh herbs for grilling and provide you with delicious recipes that will elevate your summer cookouts.

Why Use Fresh Herbs for Grilling?

Fresh herbs are a game-changer in the grilling world. They offer a multitude of benefits:

  1. Flavor Enhancement: Fresh herbs can drastically transform the flavor profile of your grilled dishes. They can provide a hint of sweetness, a peppery bite, or even an earthy undertone.

  2. Aromatics: The aroma of fresh herbs mingling with grilled food creates an inviting and mouthwatering experience for you and your guests.

  3. Health Benefits: Many herbs are packed with antioxidants and essential nutrients. Incorporating them into your meals can contribute to a healthier diet.

  4. Versatility: Fresh herbs can be used in marinades, rubs, dressings, and even as garnishes, making them a versatile addition to your grilling repertoire.

Now, let’s explore some of the best fresh herbs for grilling and how to use them in your summer recipes.

1. Basil

Flavor Profile:

Basil is a sweet and fragrant herb that is a staple in Mediterranean cuisine. Its aromatic leaves are perfect for adding a fresh and vibrant flavor to grilled dishes.

Recipe: Grilled Basil Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chopped basil, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a zip-top bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for more flavor).
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the leftover marinade.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  5. Serve with a fresh basil salad on the side.

2. Rosemary

Flavor Profile:

Rosemary has a strong, woodsy flavor that can stand up to the high heat of grilling. Its needle-like leaves add a distinct aroma and taste to meats and vegetables alike.

Recipe: Grilled Rosemary Lamb Chops

Ingredients:

  • 8 lamb chops
  • 4 sprigs of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the chopped rosemary, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rub the mixture over the lamb chops and let them marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Grill the lamb chops for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare.
  4. Let the chops rest for a few minutes before serving. Pair with grilled vegetables for a complete meal.

3. Cilantro

Flavor Profile:

Cilantro adds a bright, citrusy note to grilled dishes. It’s especially popular in Latin American and Asian cuisines, making it a versatile herb for various recipes.

Recipe: Grilled Cilantro Lime Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the cilantro, lime juice, minced garlic, chili powder, and salt.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade and let it sit for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the shrimp onto skewers.
  4. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.
  5. Serve with extra lime wedges and a side of rice.

4. Thyme

Flavor Profile:

Thyme is a subtle herb with earthy and minty notes. It pairs beautifully with roasted meats and vegetables, making it a perfect addition to grilled dishes.

Recipe: Grilled Thyme Vegetable Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the zucchini, bell pepper, onion, and cherry tomatoes.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil, add chopped thyme, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat the vegetables evenly.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium heat and thread the vegetables onto skewers.
  4. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  5. Serve as a side dish or over a bed of greens.

5. Oregano

Flavor Profile:

Oregano is known for its robust and slightly bitter flavor. It’s often used in Italian and Mediterranean dishes, making it ideal for grilled meats and pizzas.

Recipe: Grilled Oregano Marinated Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • 1/4 cup fresh oregano, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine chopped oregano, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rub the mixture over the steaks and let them marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill to high heat. Grill the steaks for about 5-7 minutes on each side for medium-rare.
  4. Let the steaks rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve with grilled asparagus.

6. Dill

Flavor Profile:

Dill has a fresh, slightly tangy flavor that works wonderfully with seafood and vegetables. It’s an excellent herb for summer grilling, giving your dishes a light and refreshing taste.

Recipe: Grilled Dill Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the chopped dill, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Let them marinate for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium heat. Grill the salmon for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  4. Serve with a side of grilled corn on the cob.

7. Mint

Flavor Profile:

Mint is a refreshing herb that adds a sweet and cooling flavor to grilled dishes. It’s perfect for adding a twist to meats and salads during the hot summer months.

Recipe: Grilled Mint Lamb Burgers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns and toppings of your choice

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine ground lamb, chopped mint, minced garlic, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Form the mixture into patties.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Grill the lamb burgers for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium.
  4. Assemble the burgers with your favorite toppings and serve.

Grilling Tips with Fresh Herbs

  • Use Fresh Herbs: Always opt for fresh herbs instead of dried ones when grilling. Fresh herbs have a more potent flavor and aroma.

  • Timing: Add delicate herbs like basil and mint towards the end of grilling to preserve their flavor. Heartier herbs like rosemary and thyme can be added earlier.

  • Herb Oil: Create a herb-infused oil by blending fresh herbs with olive oil. Brush it over your grilled meats and vegetables for added flavor.

  • Chopping: Chop herbs just before use to maximize their flavor. The oils in the herbs are most potent right after they are cut.

  • Storage: Store fresh herbs in a damp paper towel inside a plastic bag in the refrigerator to keep them fresh for longer.

Conclusion

Fresh herbs are an essential component of summer grilling, bringing flavor, health benefits, and vibrant aromas to your outdoor meals. Whether you’re grilling meats, seafood, or vegetables, these herbs will elevate your dishes and impress your guests. Try the recipes above and experiment with different combinations of herbs to create your unique grilling masterpieces. Enjoy the warm summer nights filled with delicious food and great company!
