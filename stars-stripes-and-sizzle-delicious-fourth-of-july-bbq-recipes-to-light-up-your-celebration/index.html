Stars, Stripes, and Sizzle: Delicious Fourth of July BBQ Recipes to Light Up Your Celebration


The Fourth of July, a day to celebrate American independence, is just around the corner. What better way to commemorate this special occasion than with a mouth-watering BBQ feast? In this post, we’ll share some mouth-watering Fourth of July BBQ recipes that will light up your celebration and satisfy your guests’ taste buds.

Appetizers

Red, White, and Blue Sliders

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped white onion
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Blue cheese crumbles
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Form beef mixture into patties and grill for 4-5 minutes per side. Assemble sliders with beef, lettuce, tomato, and blue cheese crumbles.

Star-Spangled Deviled Eggs

  • 6 large eggs, hard-boiled and peeled
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Paprika
  • Chopped chives

Cut eggs in half lengthwise and carefully remove yolks. Mash yolks with mayonnaise, mustard, and horseradish. Season with salt and pepper. Spoon yolk mixture into egg whites and garnish with paprika and chives.

Main Course

Grilled American Cheeseburgers

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 8 ounces ground beef
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Form beef mixture into patties and grill for 4-5 minutes per side. Assemble burgers with beef, American cheese, lettuce, tomato, and pickles.

Sizzlin’ Sausage Skillet

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Provolone cheese slices

Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Grill sausage and cook for 5-6 minutes per side. In a large skillet, cook onion, garlic, and bell pepper in olive oil until tender. Add sausage to skillet and simmer for 2-3 minutes. Serve sausage with provolone cheese slices on hamburger buns.

Sides

Yankee Doodle Dandy Potato Salad

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Boil potatoes until tender. Drain and chill. In a large bowl, combine mayonnaise, mustard, and vinegar. Add parsley and season with salt and pepper. Fold in potatoes until coated.

All-American Corn on the Cob

  • 4 ears corn, husked and silked
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cooked bacon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat grill to medium heat. Grill corn for 10-12 minutes, turning frequently. Brush with butter and sprinkle with cheddar cheese and crumbled bacon.

Desserts

Red, White, and Blue Trifle

  • 1 package vanilla cake mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 cup vanilla ice cream

Bake cake according to package instructions. Cut into small cubes. In a large bowl, combine milk, butter, and heavy cream. Dip cake cubes into milk mixture, then layer in a large trifle dish with blueberries, strawberries, and vanilla ice cream.

Firework Fudge Brownies

  • 1 and 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, plus more for greasing
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Grease an 8-inch square baking pan. In a large bowl, combine butter, sugar, eggs, and cocoa powder. Stir in flour and salt. Melt chocolate chips and stir into batter. Fold in walnuts. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.

Conclusion

These delicious Fourth of July BBQ recipes are sure to light up your celebration and satisfy your guests’ taste buds. From appetizers to desserts, we’ve got you covered with a variety of mouth-watering dishes that are perfect for the occasion. So, fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to enjoy a star-spangled Fourth of July celebration!
