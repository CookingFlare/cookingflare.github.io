Spicy and Satisfying Allergy-Safe Chicken Curry Recipe


Are you tired of boring and bland chicken dishes? Do you have dietary restrictions or allergies that make it difficult to find flavorful recipes? Look no further! This Spicy and Satisfying Allergy-Safe Chicken Curry Recipe is here to satisfy your taste buds and cater to your dietary needs.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan. Cook for another minute until fragrant.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the curry powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, paprika, and chili powder. Mix well to create a spice blend.

  4. Add the spice blend to the pan and stir it into the onion mixture. Allow the spices to cook for a minute to release their flavors.

  5. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are browned on all sides.

  6. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir in the tomato paste until it is well incorporated.

  7. Reduce the heat to low and let the curry simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  8. Taste the curry and season with salt, if needed.

  9. Serve the Spicy and Satisfying Allergy-Safe Chicken Curry over steamed rice or with naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for added freshness and flavor.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Allergy-Safe: This chicken curry recipe is free from common allergens such as dairy, nuts, and gluten, making it suitable for those with dietary restrictions or allergies.

  • Spicy and Flavorful: The combination of aromatic spices gives this curry a kick of heat and a burst of flavor. Adjust the spice level to your preference and enjoy the bold flavors.

  • Satisfying and Filling: The tender chicken and creamy coconut milk create a rich and satisfying curry that will leave you feeling full and satisfied.

  • Easy to Make: With simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, this recipe is perfect for both novice and experienced cooks. It’s a great option for a quick and delicious weeknight meal.

  • Versatile: Feel free to customize this recipe by adding vegetables or adjusting the spice blend to suit your taste. Make it your own and explore different variations of this allergy-safe chicken curry.

So, if you’re looking for a delicious and allergy-safe chicken curry recipe that doesn’t compromise on flavor, give this Spicy and Satisfying Allergy-Safe Chicken Curry Recipe a try. It’s a guaranteed crowd-pleaser that will spice up your dinner table. Enjoy!
