Sizzling Summer Picnic BBQ Chicken Recipes


As summer rolls in, the allure of outdoor picnics beckons food lovers everywhere. The sun-drenched days and warm evenings create the perfect backdrop for a delightful BBQ chicken feast. With the right recipes, you can transform simple chicken into sizzling sensations that will have friends and family craving more. This blog post is dedicated to bringing you mouthwatering BBQ chicken recipes that are perfect for summer picnics. Let’s fire up those grills and dive into some delicious culinary creations!

The Perfect BBQ Chicken Marinade

Before we jump into the recipes, let’s talk about the secret to juicy, flavorful BBQ chicken: the marinade. A good marinade not only enhances the chicken’s natural flavors but also helps to tenderize the meat. Here’s a simple recipe for a classic BBQ chicken marinade that you can use for any of the recipes below.

Ingredients for BBQ Chicken Marinade

  • 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)

Instructions for Marinade

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together all the ingredients until well combined.
  2. Place your chicken in a resealable plastic bag or a large bowl.
  3. Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring it’s well coated.
  4. Seal the bag or cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight for maximum flavor.

Recipe 1: Classic BBQ Grilled Chicken

Nothing beats the classic BBQ grilled chicken, and it’s incredibly easy to make. This recipe will give you tender chicken with a smoky flavor that’s perfect for any picnic.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts or thighs (bone-in or boneless)
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Remove the chicken from the marinade and let any excess marinade drip off.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  5. Brush the BBQ sauce over the chicken during the last few minutes of grilling for a sticky, caramelized finish.
  6. Remove from the grill and let rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this delicious grilled chicken with classic picnic sides like coleslaw, potato salad, or corn on the cob for a complete summer meal.

Recipe 2: Honey Mustard BBQ Chicken Skewers

If you’re looking for something a little different, these honey mustard BBQ chicken skewers are a fantastic option. They’re easy to eat and perfect for sharing!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/3 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers (soaked in water if wooden)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the honey, Dijon mustard, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and toss to coat. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken onto the skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has nice grill marks.
  6. Serve hot with additional honey mustard sauce for dipping.

Pairing Ideas

These skewers pair beautifully with a fresh green salad or grilled vegetables, making them a light and satisfying picnic option.

Recipe 3: Spicy Chipotle BBQ Chicken

For those who like a bit of heat, this spicy chipotle BBQ chicken recipe will definitely hit the spot. The smoky chipotle flavor combined with the sweetness of BBQ sauce creates a perfect balance.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on preferred)
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chipotle in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, chipotle in adobo, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Coat the chicken thighs with the spicy sauce and let them marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  4. Place the chicken on the grill, skin-side down, and cook for about 10 minutes.
  5. Flip the chicken and continue grilling for another 10-15 minutes, basting with leftover sauce, until fully cooked.
  6. Let rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this spicy chicken with cooling sides like creamy coleslaw or a refreshing cucumber salad to balance the heat.

Recipe 4: BBQ Chicken Salad

If you want to keep things light, a BBQ chicken salad is a fantastic way to enjoy those summer flavors without the heaviness of traditional BBQ dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts, sliced
  • 4 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, romaine)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup corn (canned or grilled)
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or pepper jack)
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce, for drizzling
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing, for drizzling

Instructions

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, tomatoes, corn, black beans, red onion, and cheese.
  2. Top with sliced grilled chicken.
  3. Drizzle with BBQ sauce and ranch dressing just before serving. Toss gently to combine.

Tips for a Great Salad

Feel free to add more toppings like avocado, crispy bacon bits, or croutons for extra flavor and crunch.

Recipe 5: BBQ Chicken Pizza

Take your summer picnic to the next level with BBQ chicken pizza. This recipe is a crowd-pleaser and can easily be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven according to the pizza crust instructions.
  2. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with 1/4 cup of BBQ sauce.
  3. Spread the remaining BBQ sauce evenly over the pizza crust.
  4. Top with BBQ chicken, red onion, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake according to the crust instructions, usually around 12-15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  6. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh cilantro before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this pizza with a side of fresh fruit or a simple green salad for a complete meal.

Conclusion

With these sizzling summer picnic BBQ chicken recipes, you’re all set to impress your guests at your next outdoor gathering. From classic grilled chicken to fun BBQ chicken pizza, these dishes will bring flavors that everyone will love. Remember, the key to great BBQ chicken lies in the marinade, so don’t skip that step!

As you enjoy your summer picnics, feel free to experiment with different marinades, sauces, and sides to make each gathering unique. Happy grilling, and may your summer be filled with delicious meals and cherished memories!
