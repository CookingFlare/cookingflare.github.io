Chicken Parmesan Casserole: A Tasty Twist on a Comfort Classic


If you’re a fan of traditional chicken parmesan, you’re going to love this delicious twist on the classic dish. Chicken Parmesan Casserole takes all the flavors of the beloved Italian favorite and transforms them into a hearty and satisfying casserole that’s perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as a cozy family dinner. This recipe brings together tender chicken, savory marinara sauce, gooey cheese, and zesty herbs for a mouthwatering meal that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray and set it aside.

  2. Begin by preparing the chicken. Place the chicken breasts on a cutting board and use a sharp knife to carefully slice each one in half horizontally, creating two thin cutlets from each breast. Season the chicken cutlets on both sides with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.

  3. In a large skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the seasoned chicken cutlets and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. In a small bowl, combine the Panko breadcrumbs with the grated Parmesan cheese, mixing well to ensure the cheese is evenly distributed throughout the breadcrumbs.

  5. To assemble the casserole, spread half of the marinara sauce in an even layer on the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Arrange the cooked chicken cutlets on top of the sauce, then pour the remaining marinara sauce over the chicken.

  6. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the chicken and marinara, then top with the breadcrumb and Parmesan mixture, spreading it evenly across the top of the casserole.

  7. Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the breadcrumbs are golden brown.

  8. Once the casserole is finished baking, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped fresh basil for a pop of color and flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Chicken Parmesan Casserole pairs perfectly with a side of spaghetti or your favorite pasta. The rich, tomato-based sauce and cheesy topping make it an ideal match for al dente noodles. For a complete meal, serve with a crisp green salad dressed with balsamic vinaigrette for a refreshing contrast to the indulgent casserole.

Final Thoughts

This Chicken Parmesan Casserole recipe offers all the flavors you love in a traditional chicken parmesan, but in a convenient casserole form that’s easy to prepare and even easier to enjoy. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a cozy and comforting meal, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. With its simple preparation and irresistible taste, this casserole is a delicious twist on a classic that will have everyone asking for seconds. Try it out and savor the delightful flavors of this comforting Italian-inspired dish!
