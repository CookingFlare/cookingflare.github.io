Sicilian Arancini with Meat Sauce: A Flavorful Italian Street Snack


If you’ve ever strolled through the streets of Sicily, you’ve likely been tantalized by the aromas of delicious, sizzling street food. One such delight is the Sicilian arancini, a beloved Italian snack that has captured the hearts and taste buds of food enthusiasts around the world. These golden, crispy orbs of creamy risotto, stuffed with a savory meat sauce and cheese, are a true delight for the senses. In this blog post, we’re going to explore the art of making Sicilian arancini with meat sauce from scratch, so you can bring a taste of Sicily to your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Risotto:

  • Arborio rice
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Onion
  • White wine
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

For the Meat Sauce:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Red wine
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper

For the Assembly:

  • Mozzarella cheese, cut into small cubes
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

Making the Risotto:

  1. Begin by making a creamy risotto base. Sauté finely chopped onions in a pan until translucent, then add the Arborio rice and stir until the grains are coated with the oil.
  2. Pour in a splash of white wine and allow it to evaporate, then gradually add the chicken or vegetable broth, stirring constantly until the rice is cooked and the mixture is creamy.
  3. Remove the risotto from the heat and stir in grated Parmesan cheese, butter, salt, and pepper. Let it cool completely.

Preparing the Meat Sauce:

  1. In a separate pan, cook the ground beef with finely chopped onions and minced garlic until browned.
  2. Add the tomato sauce, red wine, and torn basil leaves, then simmer the sauce until it thickens. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Assembling the Arancini:

  1. Take a small handful of the cooled risotto and flatten it in the palm of your hand. Place a cube of mozzarella in the center and a spoonful of the meat sauce on top.
  2. Carefully encase the filling with the risotto, shaping it into a smooth ball.
  3. Roll the arancini first in flour, then dip them in beaten eggs, and finally coat them in breadcrumbs, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  4. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep pan or fryer and carefully fry the arancini until they are golden brown and crispy. Drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.

Serving and Enjoying:

Serve the Sicilian arancini with meat sauce piping hot, allowing the melted mozzarella and rich, savory filling to delight your taste buds. These delightful morsels are perfect for sharing with friends and family, or for indulging in as a delightful snack.

Conclusion

Sicilian arancini with meat sauce is a truly special dish that embodies the rich flavors and culinary traditions of Italy. By mastering this recipe, you can bring the vibrant spirit of Sicilian street food into your own home, impressing your loved ones with a taste of la dolce vita. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a delicious culinary journey to create these golden, crispy orbs of delight. Buon appetito!
