Gooey Caramel Cookie Bars: A Sweet Indulgence


If you’re a fan of gooey, sticky, and utterly indulgent treats, then you’re in for a real sweet delight with these Gooey Caramel Cookie Bars. These delectable bars are a perfect combination of chewy caramel, buttery cookie, and a hint of salt that will have your taste buds singing with joy. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, in need of a pick-me-up, or simply craving something sweet, these bars are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

For the Cookie Base:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

For the Caramel Layer:

  • 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup corn syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cookie Base

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, and then mix in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
  6. Press the cookie dough evenly into the prepared baking pan and set aside.

Step 2: Making the Caramel Layer

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the sweetened condensed milk, heavy cream, granulated sugar, unsalted butter, corn syrup, and salt over medium heat.
  2. Stir the mixture constantly until the sugar has dissolved and the butter has melted.
  3. Once the mixture comes to a gentle boil, insert a candy thermometer and continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the temperature reaches 235°F (113°C).
  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the caramel over the cookie base, spreading it out evenly with a spatula.

Step 3: Baking and Serving

  1. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the center is set.
  2. Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan before lifting them out using the parchment paper overhang.
  3. Once cooled, cut the bars into squares or rectangles, and serve them on a platter for a delightful indulgence.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra indulgent touch, sprinkle a pinch of flaky sea salt over the caramel layer before baking for a sweet and salty contrast.
  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days, or refrigerated for up to a week for a cool, chewy treat.

Final Thoughts

These Gooey Caramel Cookie Bars are the epitome of sweet indulgence. With a buttery cookie base and a luscious caramel layer, they are guaranteed to become a favorite in your baking repertoire. Whether you’re sharing them with friends and family or simply treating yourself, these bars are sure to bring smiles and satisfied taste buds. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the gooey goodness of these irresistible treats!
