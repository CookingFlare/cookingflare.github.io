Thai Mango Sticky Rice: Sweet and Creamy Dessert Recipes


Craving a delicious and exotic dessert? Look no further than Thai Mango Sticky Rice! This mouthwatering combination of sweet and creamy flavors will transport your taste buds to the tropical paradise of Thailand. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of making this delectable dessert from scratch. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

To create the perfect Thai Mango Sticky Rice, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of sticky rice (also known as glutinous rice)
  • 1 ripe mango, sliced
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Soak the sticky rice: Start by rinsing the sticky rice thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear. Then, place the rice in a bowl and cover it with water. Let it soak for at least 4 hours or overnight. This process will ensure that the rice becomes soft and sticky when cooked.

  2. Prepare the steamer: Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil. Once the water is boiling, place a steamer basket lined with cheesecloth or a thin kitchen towel on top of the pot. Make sure the water does not touch the bottom of the basket.

  3. Cook the sticky rice: Drain the soaked rice and transfer it to the steamer basket. Cover the pot with a lid and steam the rice for about 25-30 minutes, or until it becomes tender and translucent. Make sure to check the rice occasionally to prevent it from overcooking.

  4. Prepare the coconut sauce: While the rice is steaming, it’s time to prepare the coconut sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir until the sugar dissolves completely. Remove the saucepan from the heat and set it aside to cool.

  5. Serve and enjoy: Once the sticky rice is cooked, transfer it to a serving plate. Pour the coconut sauce over the rice, allowing it to soak in and enhance its flavor. Arrange the sliced mango on top of the rice, and sprinkle with sesame seeds for an extra touch of crunch and visual appeal.

  6. Indulge in Thai Mango Sticky Rice: Your Thai Mango Sticky Rice is now ready to be enjoyed! Take a bite and savor the harmonious blend of sweet mango, creamy coconut sauce, and sticky rice. The combination of textures and flavors will surely satisfy your dessert cravings and transport you to the vibrant streets of Thailand.

Tips and Variations

  • For a creamier coconut sauce, use full-fat coconut milk instead of the light version.
  • If you prefer a sweeter dessert, you can increase the amount of sugar in the coconut sauce to your liking.
  • Feel free to get creative with toppings! Try adding toasted coconut flakes, crushed peanuts, or a drizzle of condensed milk for an extra touch of indulgence.
  • If mangoes are not in season, you can substitute them with other tropical fruits like pineapple or papaya.

Now that you have mastered the art of making Thai Mango Sticky Rice, surprise your family and friends with this exotic dessert. It’s a delightful treat that will leave everyone craving for more. So go ahead, embrace the flavors of Thailand, and satisfy your sweet tooth with this divine creation. Happy cooking!
