Delicious and Healthy Wrap Ideas for Packed Lunches


When it comes to packed lunches, wraps are a versatile and convenient option. They are easy to prepare, portable, and can be filled with a variety of delicious and healthy ingredients. Whether you’re packing lunch for work, school, or a day out, these wrap ideas are sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you energized throughout the day. Let’s dive into some creative and mouthwatering wrap recipes that will elevate your packed lunch game!

Mediterranean Chickpea Wrap

Prep Time: 15 minutes Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large whole wheat wraps

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mash the chickpeas with a fork until slightly chunky.
  2. Add cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and parsley to the bowl.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the mixture and toss to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Divide the chickpea mixture evenly between the two wraps.
  6. Roll up the wraps tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
  7. Cut the wraps in half and secure with toothpicks if needed.

This Mediterranean Chickpea Wrap is a refreshing and protein-packed option for a packed lunch. The combination of chickpeas, fresh vegetables, and zesty dressing brings the flavors of the Mediterranean to your lunch break.

Thai Peanut Tofu Wrap

Prep Time: 20 minutes Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz firm tofu, drained and sliced into strips
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 large whole grain wraps
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and ginger to make the sauce.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, lightly brown the tofu strips on each side.
  3. Place the tofu in the sauce and toss to coat.
  4. Lay out the wraps and divide the tofu, shredded cabbage, and julienned carrot between them.
  5. Drizzle any remaining sauce over the fillings.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
  7. Roll up the wraps and slice in half before packing.

The Thai Peanut Tofu Wrap offers a delightful blend of savory, sweet, and nutty flavors. It’s a great way to incorporate plant-based protein into your lunch and satisfy your craving for Thai-inspired cuisine.

Grilled Veggie and Hummus Wrap

Prep Time: 25 minutes Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced lengthwise
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and quartered
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced into rounds
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tbsp hummus
  • 2 large spinach wraps
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Brush zucchini, red bell pepper, and red onion with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the vegetables until tender and slightly charred, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Spread 2 tablespoons of hummus onto each wrap.
  5. Arrange grilled vegetables and baby spinach on the wraps.
  6. Roll up the wraps, tucking in the ends, and slice in half.

The Grilled Veggie and Hummus Wrap is a celebration of smoky grilled vegetables paired with creamy hummus. It’s a fantastic way to enjoy a variety of colorful veggies in a convenient handheld package.

Conclusion

Packed lunches don’t have to be boring or unhealthy. With these delicious and healthy wrap ideas, you can look forward to a satisfying and flavorful midday meal. Experiment with different fillings, sauces, and wraps to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Whether you’re a fan of Mediterranean flavors, crave Thai-inspired dishes, or love grilled vegetables, there’s a wrap recipe here for you. Say goodbye to uninspired lunches and hello to a new world of packed lunch possibilities!
