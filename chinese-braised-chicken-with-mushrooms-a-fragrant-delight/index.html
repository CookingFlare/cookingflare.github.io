Chinese Braised Chicken with Mushrooms: A Fragrant Delight


Braised chicken is a classic Chinese dish that combines succulent chicken pieces with earthy mushrooms in a rich and flavorful sauce. This recipe for Chinese Braised Chicken with Mushrooms will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of China, where aromatic spices and ingredients come together to create a truly satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Spring onions, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large, deep pan over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are golden brown on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

  2. In the same pan, add the garlic and ginger. Sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the mushrooms and cook until they have softened and released their juices.

  3. Return the chicken to the pan and add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, Shaoxing wine (if using), and sugar. Stir well to coat the chicken and mushrooms with the sauce.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, ensuring that the liquid covers the chicken and mushrooms. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pan and let it simmer gently for about 45 minutes, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  5. Remove the lid and increase the heat to medium-high. Allow the sauce to reduce and thicken slightly, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened, taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. Remove from heat and garnish with spring onions.

  7. Serve the Chinese Braised Chicken with Mushrooms hot with steamed rice or noodles. The tender chicken, flavorful mushrooms, and fragrant sauce will delight your senses and leave you craving for more.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a few slices of chili pepper or a teaspoon of chili flakes to the sauce.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of mushrooms, such as shiitake or oyster mushrooms, to add depth and variety to the dish.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water and add it to the pan during the last few minutes of cooking. Stir well until the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency.
  • This dish can also be prepared in a slow cooker. Simply follow the instructions until step 4, then transfer everything to a slow cooker and cook on low for 4-6 hours or on high for 2-3 hours.

Final Thoughts

Chinese Braised Chicken with Mushrooms is a dish that embodies the essence of Chinese cuisine. The combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and aromatic spices creates a delightful harmony of flavors. Whether you’re serving it to family and friends or enjoying it on a cozy night in, this dish is sure to impress. So go ahead, grab your apron, and embark on a culinary adventure with this fragrant delight.

Note: All cooking times may vary depending on the size of your chicken pieces and the heat of your stove. Adjust accordingly to ensure the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
