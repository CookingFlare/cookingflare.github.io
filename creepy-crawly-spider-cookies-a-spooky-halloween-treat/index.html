Creepy Crawly Spider Cookies: A Spooky Halloween Treat


It’s that time of the year again - Halloween! And what better way to celebrate this spooky season than by whipping up some delightfully eerie treats? If you’re looking for a fun and creepy addition to your Halloween spread, look no further than these Creepy Crawly Spider Cookies. These cookies are not only delicious but also a perfect way to add some frightful fun to your Halloween party or gathering.

Ingredients

For the Cookies:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

For the Spider Decorations:

  • 1 cup of milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 24 small chocolate candies (such as M&M’s)
  • 48 small edible candy eyes

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cookie Dough

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and light-brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add salt, vanilla, and eggs to the butter-sugar mixture and beat until well combined.
  5. Gradually stir in the flour mixture until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

Step 2: Baking the Cookies

  1. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets, about 2 inches apart.
  2. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden but the centers are still soft.
  3. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 3: Decorating the Cookies

  1. Melt the milk chocolate chips in the microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval until smooth.
  2. Dip the top of each cookie into the melted chocolate, allowing any excess to drip off.
  3. Place the dipped cookies back onto the wire rack to set.

Step 4: Creating the Spider Decorations

  1. Melt the semisweet chocolate chips in the microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval until smooth.
  2. Transfer the melted chocolate to a piping bag or a zip-top plastic bag with a tiny corner snipped off.
  3. Pipe eight legs onto each cookie by squeezing the melted chocolate out of the bag in a zig-zag motion.
  4. While the chocolate is still wet, place two small chocolate candies as the spider’s body and attach two edible candy eyes.

Serving and Storing

Once the cookies are fully set, serve them at your Halloween gathering and watch as your guests marvel at these delightfully creepy treats. These cookies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, ensuring that you can enjoy the spooky fun even after Halloween.

Conclusion

These Creepy Crawly Spider Cookies are not only a delicious sweet treat but also a fantastic way to add a touch of spookiness to your Halloween celebrations. Whether you’re hosting a Halloween party or simply want to enjoy some eerie delights with your loved ones, these cookies are sure to impress. So, get into the Halloween spirit and give these spider cookies a try for a hair-raisingly good time!
