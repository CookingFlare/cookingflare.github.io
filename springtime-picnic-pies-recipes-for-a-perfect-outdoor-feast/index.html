Springtime Picnic Pies: Recipes for a Perfect Outdoor Feast


Spring is in the air, and what better way to celebrate the season than with a delightful outdoor picnic? Whether you’re planning a romantic outing for two or a fun gathering with friends and family, picnic pies are the perfect addition to your springtime spread. These savory and sweet pies are not only delicious but also portable, making them ideal for a picnic feast. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering recipes for springtime picnic pies that are sure to impress your fellow picnic-goers.

Savory Spinach and Feta Picnic Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of store-bought puff pastry
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a pie dish with the puff pastry.
  2. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat and sauté the onion and garlic until softened.
  3. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  4. In a bowl, combine the spinach mixture, feta cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and beaten eggs. Mix well.
  5. Pour the spinach and feta filling into the prepared pie dish and fold over any excess pastry.
  6. Brush the top of the pie with a little beaten egg for a golden finish.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until the pastry is golden brown and the filling is set.
  8. Allow the pie to cool slightly before slicing and serving.

This savory spinach and feta picnic pie is a delightful combination of flaky pastry and a flavorful, cheesy filling. It’s perfect for a light lunch or as part of a picnic spread.

Sweet Mixed Berry Hand Pies

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups mixed fresh berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 package of refrigerated pie crusts
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Coarse sugar for sprinkling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, gently toss together the mixed berries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Roll out the pie crusts on a lightly floured surface and use a round cutter to cut out circles.
  4. Spoon a generous amount of the berry filling onto one half of each pie crust circle, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Fold the other half of the pie crust over the filling and crimp the edges with a fork to seal.
  6. Place the hand pies on the prepared baking sheet, brush the tops with beaten egg, and sprinkle with coarse sugar.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the hand pies are golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  8. Allow the hand pies to cool slightly before serving.

These sweet mixed berry hand pies are a delightful treat for a springtime picnic. The burst of fresh berry flavors encased in flaky pastry is sure to be a hit with everyone.

Conclusion

Springtime picnic pies are versatile, delicious, and easy to transport, making them the perfect addition to any outdoor gathering. Whether you prefer a savory or sweet option, these recipes are bound to elevate your picnic experience. So, pack up your picnic basket, grab a blanket, and head outdoors to enjoy the beautiful weather and these delectable springtime picnic pies!
