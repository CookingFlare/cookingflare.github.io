Baked Ziti: A Cheesy and Comforting Pasta Casserole


Are you craving a hearty and delicious pasta dish that will warm your soul? Look no further than Baked Ziti! This cheesy and comforting pasta casserole is the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings and impress your family and friends. With its layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese, Baked Ziti is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone wanting more.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering Baked Ziti, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound ziti pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef (or you can use ground turkey or sausage for a twist)
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cook the ziti pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they are fragrant and translucent.

  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until it’s browned and cooked through. If you’re using ground turkey or sausage, follow the same steps.

  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the tomato meat sauce. Add half of the cooked ziti pasta on top, followed by half of the remaining sauce. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese and half of the Parmesan cheese over the sauce.

  7. Repeat the layering process with the remaining ziti pasta, sauce, and cheeses.

  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Once the Baked Ziti is golden and bubbling, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

Baked Ziti is a versatile dish that can be served as a main course or as a side dish alongside a fresh salad or garlic bread. It pairs well with a glass of red wine, such as Chianti or Sangiovese. The leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, making it a great option for meal prep or quick weeknight dinners.

Final Thoughts

Baked Ziti is a classic Italian-American dish that brings comfort and satisfaction to the table. Its layers of pasta, robust tomato sauce, and melty cheese create a symphony of flavors that will have everyone asking for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy meal, this cheesy and comforting pasta casserole is sure to impress. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the deliciousness of Baked Ziti!
