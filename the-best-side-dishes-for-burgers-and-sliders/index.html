The Best Side Dishes for Burgers and Sliders


Burgers and sliders are classic comfort foods that are loved by many. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, a casual dinner party, or simply craving a delicious meal, burgers and sliders are always a crowd-pleaser. While the star of the show is undoubtedly the juicy patty or the flavorful mini-sandwich, the perfect side dish can elevate the entire dining experience. Here are some fantastic side dish ideas to complement your burgers and sliders and take your meal to the next level.

1. Crispy Garlic Parmesan Fries

Who can resist a plate of golden, crispy fries? Elevate this classic side dish by tossing the fries in a blend of garlic powder, grated Parmesan cheese, and fresh parsley. The flavorful combination of garlic and Parmesan will add a gourmet touch to your meal, making it a perfect accompaniment to any burger or slider.

2. Tangy Coleslaw

The creamy and tangy nature of coleslaw makes it an ideal side dish to serve alongside burgers and sliders. The refreshing crunch of shredded cabbage and carrots combined with a tangy dressing provides a delightful contrast to the savory flavors of the main dish. For a twist, consider adding a hint of apple cider vinegar or a sprinkle of celery seeds to your coleslaw for an extra layer of flavor.

3. Loaded Potato Salad

A well-made potato salad is a timeless side dish that pairs beautifully with burgers and sliders. For a loaded version, mix in crispy bacon, sharp cheddar cheese, and finely chopped scallions with the creamy potato salad base. The smoky, savory, and creamy elements in this dish perfectly complement the rich flavors of a burger or a slider.

4. Grilled Corn on the Cob

Grilled corn on the cob is a quintessential summer side dish that pairs wonderfully with burgers and sliders. Brushed with a garlic herb butter or sprinkled with chili-lime seasoning, the sweet and smoky flavors of the grilled corn add a delightful touch to your meal. The charred kernels provide a satisfying crunch, and the natural sweetness of the corn balances the savory components of your main dish.

5. Balsamic Glazed Grilled Vegetables

For a lighter and more health-conscious side dish option, consider serving balsamic glazed grilled vegetables alongside your burgers and sliders. Toss an assortment of colorful vegetables such as zucchini, bell peppers, and mushrooms in a balsamic glaze before grilling them to perfection. The sweet and tangy flavors of the balsamic glaze complement the smokiness of the grilled vegetables, offering a refreshing and vibrant addition to your meal.

6. Sweet Potato Fries with Spicy Aioli

Swap traditional fries for sweet potato fries for a healthier and more flavorful alternative. The natural sweetness of the sweet potatoes pairs beautifully with a creamy and spicy aioli dipping sauce. The hint of heat from the aioli adds a kick to the meal, creating a delightful contrast to the sweetness of the fries and the savory flavors of the burgers or sliders.

7. Caprese Salad

A refreshing and light option, a classic Caprese salad is a perfect side dish to serve alongside burgers and sliders, especially during the warmer months. Slices of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil drizzled with balsamic glaze create a vibrant and flavorful accompaniment to the heartiness of a burger or slider. The combination of flavors and textures in this salad offers a delightful contrast to the main dish.

Conclusion

Pairing the right side dishes with your burgers and sliders can truly enhance the dining experience, offering a variety of flavors, textures, and colors to complement the main attraction. Whether you prefer classic comfort foods or lighter, more refreshing options, there’s a side dish out there to perfectly complement your burgers and sliders. Experiment with different combinations to find the perfect pairing that suits your taste and impresses your guests at your next gathering. Happy cooking!
