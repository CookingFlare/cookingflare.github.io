Homemade Cookies and Cream Brownie Ice Cream: A Decadent Dessert


Indulge in the rich and creamy goodness of homemade Cookies and Cream Brownie Ice Cream. This decadent dessert combines the classic flavors of cookies and cream with chunks of fudgy brownies for a treat that will satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 10 Oreo cookies, crushed
  • 1 cup brownie chunks (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the heavy cream and whole milk over medium heat. Heat the mixture until it reaches a gentle simmer, stirring occasionally to prevent scorching.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar and egg yolks until well combined. Gradually pour the hot cream mixture into the sugar and egg yolks, whisking constantly to prevent the eggs from curdling.

  3. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld.

  5. Once the ice cream base has chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes.

  6. While the ice cream is churning, prepare the mix-ins. Crush the Oreo cookies into small pieces and chop the brownie chunks into bite-sized pieces.

  7. In the last few minutes of churning, add the crushed Oreo cookies and brownie chunks to the ice cream maker. Let the machine mix them in evenly.

  8. Once the ice cream has reached the desired consistency, transfer it to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.

  9. When you’re ready to enjoy, scoop the Cookies and Cream Brownie Ice Cream into bowls or cones and savor the rich combination of creamy vanilla ice cream, crunchy Oreo cookies, and fudgy brownie chunks.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent touch, drizzle some hot fudge or chocolate sauce over your serving of Cookies and Cream Brownie Ice Cream.
  • Feel free to experiment with different mix-ins. You can replace the Oreo cookies with your favorite cookie variety or add nuts, chocolate chips, or caramel swirls for added texture and flavor.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can still make this recipe by pouring the chilled ice cream base into a shallow dish and freezing it. Every 30 minutes, remove the dish from the freezer and vigorously stir the mixture to break up any ice crystals. Repeat this process for about 3-4 hours or until the ice cream is smooth and creamy.

Conclusion

Homemade Cookies and Cream Brownie Ice Cream is a delightful dessert that brings together the best of both worlds – the classic combination of cookies and cream and the rich indulgence of fudgy brownies. With its creamy texture and delightful mix-ins, this ice cream is sure to be a hit at any gathering or simply as a treat for yourself. So grab your ingredients and get ready to embark on a sweet journey of flavors and smiles with this decadent dessert!
