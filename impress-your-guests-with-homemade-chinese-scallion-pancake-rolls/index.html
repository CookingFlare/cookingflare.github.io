Impress Your Guests with Homemade Chinese Scallion Pancake Rolls


If you’re looking for a unique and impressive dish to serve at your next gathering, homemade Chinese scallion pancake rolls are sure to delight your guests. This traditional Chinese appetizer is not only delicious but also visually stunning, making it the perfect choice for entertaining. In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the process of creating these flavorful and crispy pancake rolls step by step, so you can impress your guests with your culinary skills.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup boiling water
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup finely chopped scallions (green onions)
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Soy sauce for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Slowly add the boiling water while stirring with chopsticks or a wooden spoon.
    • Once the water is incorporated, add the cold water and knead the mixture into a smooth dough. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Roll out the Dough:

    • After the dough has rested, divide it into 4 equal portions. Take one portion and roll it into a thin, even rectangle on a lightly floured surface.
    • Brush the surface of the dough with a thin layer of sesame oil, then sprinkle a generous amount of chopped scallions over the oiled surface.

  3. Form the Pancake Rolls:

    • Starting from one end, carefully roll the dough into a long cylinder. Coil the cylinder into a spiral shape, then gently flatten it with your hands to create a pancake-like disc.
    • Repeat this process with the remaining portions of dough, sesame oil, and scallions.

  4. Pan-Fry the Pancake Rolls:

    • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a tablespoon of vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, carefully place a pancake roll in the skillet and cook until golden brown and crispy on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.
    • Repeat this process with the remaining pancake rolls, adding more oil to the skillet as needed.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the pancake rolls are golden and crispy, transfer them to a cutting board and cut them into wedges. Serve the scallion pancake rolls hot with a side of soy sauce for dipping.

Tips for Success:

  • To achieve a flaky and crispy texture, be sure to roll the dough as thinly as possible without tearing it.
  • Don’t skimp on the scallions – the more, the better! Their fresh, savory flavor is the star of this dish.
  • If you’re short on time, you can prepare the dough in advance and store it in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours before rolling and frying the pancake rolls.

By following this recipe, you’ll create scallion pancake rolls that are not only delicious but also a visual masterpiece. Your guests will be amazed by the layers of savory scallions and the crispy texture of the pancake rolls. So, next time you’re hosting a dinner party or gathering, impress your guests with these homemade Chinese scallion pancake rolls – they’re sure to be a hit!
