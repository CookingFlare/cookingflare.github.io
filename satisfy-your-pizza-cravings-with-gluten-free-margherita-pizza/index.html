Satisfy Your Pizza Cravings with Gluten-Free Margherita Pizza

3 min · 493 words

Are you a pizza lover who also happens to follow a gluten-free diet? Look no further! In this recipe, we will show you how to make a delicious and satisfying gluten-free Margherita pizza that will leave you craving for more. With a crispy gluten-free crust, tangy tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves, this pizza is a true crowd-pleaser. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • For the crust:

    • 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
    • 1 teaspoon xanthan gum (if not already included in the flour blend)
    • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup warm water
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • For the sauce:

    • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  • For the toppings:

    • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
    • Fresh basil leaves, torn

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free flour blend, xanthan gum (if needed), yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well.

  3. Gradually add warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Stir until a soft dough forms. If the dough seems too dry, add a little more water, one tablespoon at a time.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough back into the mixing bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Allow the dough to rise in a warm place for about 30 minutes or until it doubles in size.

  6. While the dough is rising, prepare the pizza sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook over medium heat for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  7. Once the dough has risen, transfer it onto a piece of parchment paper. Roll out the dough into your desired pizza shape.

  8. Carefully transfer the parchment paper with the rolled-out dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.

  9. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  10. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.

  11. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  12. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  13. Garnish the pizza with torn basil leaves.

  14. Slice the pizza and serve hot.

Enjoy Your Gluten-Free Margherita Pizza!

There you have it – a scrumptious gluten-free Margherita pizza that will satisfy your cravings without compromising your dietary needs. Whether you’re hosting a pizza night or simply treating yourself, this pizza is a winner. The combination of the crispy crust, tangy sauce, ooey-gooey cheese, and fragrant basil creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and indulge in a slice of pizza perfection!
