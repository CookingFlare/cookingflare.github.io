Homemade Banana Nut Cookie Recipe: A Nutty Twist


If you’re a fan of classic banana bread and love the nutty goodness of cookies, then this Homemade Banana Nut Cookie recipe is just what you need. With a delightful balance of sweetness and crunch, these cookies are the perfect treat for any occasion. Plus, they’re incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the mashed bananas, eggs, and vanilla extract to the butter and sugar mixture. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Fold in the chopped walnuts, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the cookie dough.

  6. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  7. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until they are golden brown around the edges.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cookies cool for a few minutes on the sheet before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once the cookies have cooled, indulge in their delicious nutty flavor and enjoy!

Variations

Feel free to get creative with this recipe and add your own twist! Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  • Swap the walnuts for pecans or almonds for a different flavor profile.
  • Add a teaspoon of cinnamon or nutmeg to the dough to enhance the warm, comforting flavors.
  • Mix in some chocolate chips or dried cranberries for an extra burst of sweetness.

Serving Suggestions

These Homemade Banana Nut Cookies are perfect on their own, but they also pair well with a cup of coffee or a glass of milk. They make a great snack for picnics, potlucks, or any gathering with friends and family.

Storage Tips

To keep your cookies fresh, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. If you want to enjoy them for longer, you can freeze the cookies for up to 2 months. Just make sure to let them thaw at room temperature before serving.

Conclusion

With this Homemade Banana Nut Cookie recipe, you can transform your ripe bananas into a delectable treat that will satisfy your cookie cravings. The combination of the sweet bananas and crunchy walnuts provides a delightful contrast of flavors and textures. So, why not give this recipe a try and experience the nutty twist for yourself? Happy baking!
