Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Skewers: A Sweet and Salty Snack for Summer


Are you looking for a simple yet impressive summer snack that perfectly combines the sweetness of ripe peaches with the savory richness of prosciutto? Look no further than these delicious Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Skewers! This delightful dish is not only easy to prepare but also a crowd-pleaser at any summer gathering. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a flavorful snack, these skewers are sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s gather the essential ingredients. For this recipe, you will need:

  • Ripe peaches
  • Prosciutto slices
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

To begin, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is warming up, wash the peaches thoroughly and cut them into wedges, removing the pits. Each peach wedge should be large enough to accommodate wrapping with a slice of prosciutto.

Once the peaches are prepped, carefully slice the prosciutto into thin strips. It’s important to ensure that the prosciutto slices are long enough to wrap around the peach wedges securely.

Next, take a fresh basil leaf and place it on top of a peach wedge. Then, wrap a strip of prosciutto around the peach and basil, securing them together. Repeat this process for the remaining peach wedges, prosciutto, and basil leaves.

Grilling

Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over the assembled skewers, and lightly season them with salt and pepper. The olive oil will help prevent the skewers from sticking to the grill and add a subtle richness to the dish.

Carefully place the skewers on the preheated grill, allowing them to cook for approximately 2-3 minutes on each side. The goal is to achieve beautifully caramelized grill marks on the peaches and for the prosciutto to become slightly crispy.

Serving

Once the skewers are grilled to perfection, carefully remove them from the heat and transfer them to a serving platter. Before serving, drizzle a generous amount of balsamic glaze over the skewers. The sweet and tangy flavor of the balsamic glaze will complement the grilled peaches and prosciutto, elevating the overall taste of the dish.

These Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Skewers are best enjoyed immediately while the peaches are still warm and the prosciutto is at its crispiest. The combination of sweet, juicy peaches with the salty, savory prosciutto is a delightful contrast of flavors that is sure to tantalize your taste buds.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Skewers are a delightful and unexpected treat for any summer occasion. This simple yet elegant dish beautifully captures the essence of summer with its sweet and salty flavor profile. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together or simply want to savor a delicious snack, these skewers are bound to be a hit.

So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself and your loved ones to this irresistible summer snack. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, these Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Skewers are sure to become a staple in your summer recipe repertoire. Cheers to savoring the flavors of the season!

Enjoy!
