Butternut Squash and Sage Pasta: A Creamy Fall Delight


Fall is the perfect time to cozy up with a warm and comforting meal, and nothing embodies the flavors of the season quite like butternut squash and sage. This delicious pasta dish combines the smooth and creamy texture of roasted butternut squash with the earthy aroma of fresh sage, creating a delightful combination that will leave your taste buds craving more.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized butternut squash
  • 8 ounces of pasta (your choice of shape)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • A handful of fresh sage leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut the butternut squash in half lengthwise and remove the seeds. Place the squash, cut side up, on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Roast in the oven for about 45 minutes, or until the squash is tender and golden brown.

  2. While the squash is roasting, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.

  4. Scoop out the roasted butternut squash flesh and add it to the skillet with the onion and garlic. Stir well to combine and cook for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Using a blender or food processor, puree the squash mixture until smooth. Return the puree to the skillet and add the vegetable broth, heavy cream, and grated Parmesan cheese. Stir well to combine and bring the sauce to a simmer. Cook for about 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  6. While the sauce is simmering, heat a small skillet over medium heat. Add the fresh sage leaves and cook for about 2 minutes on each side, or until they are crispy and fragrant. Remove the sage leaves from the skillet and set them aside.

  7. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the sauce and toss until the pasta is coated evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the pasta is heated through.

  8. Serve the butternut squash and sage pasta in bowls, garnished with the crispy sage leaves. Add a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top for an extra burst of flavor.

Enjoy the Flavors of Fall

This creamy butternut squash and sage pasta is the perfect dish to enjoy during the autumn season. The rich and velvety sauce, combined with the earthy aroma of sage, creates a comforting meal that will warm both your body and your soul. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress.

So grab a fork, dig in, and savor the flavors of fall with this delightful pasta dish. It’s an easy and delicious way to celebrate the season and indulge in a creamy, comforting meal that will leave you longing for more.

Bon appétit!
