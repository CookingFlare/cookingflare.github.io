Creamy Chicken Pot Pie Soup: Comfort Food at Its Best


There’s something about a warm bowl of soup that brings comfort and coziness to our hearts. And when that soup is a creamy chicken pot pie soup, it takes comfort food to a whole new level. This recipe combines all the flavors of a classic pot pie, but in a comforting and soul-soothing soup form. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and golden brown.

  2. Add the diced carrots and celery to the pot, and continue cooking for another 5 minutes, until they begin to soften.

  3. Sprinkle the dried thyme, dried parsley, salt, and black pepper over the vegetables, and stir well to combine.

  4. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables, and stir until it coats them evenly. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, stirring constantly, to cook off the raw flour taste.

  5. Slowly pour in the chicken broth, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 10 minutes, until the soup thickens slightly.

  6. Add the shredded chicken, frozen peas, and frozen corn to the pot. Stir well, and let the soup simmer for another 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Pour in the heavy cream, and stir until it’s fully incorporated into the soup. Let it simmer for an additional 5 minutes to heat through.

  8. While the soup is simmering, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). On a lightly floured surface, roll out the puff pastry sheet until it’s about 1/4 inch thick. Cut it into small squares or shapes of your choice.

  9. Place the puff pastry squares on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and bake them in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes, or until they turn golden brown and crispy.

  10. Ladle the creamy chicken pot pie soup into bowls, and top each serving with a handful of the baked puff pastry squares. Garnish with fresh parsley for an extra pop of color and flavor.

Now, take a moment to savor the aroma of this comforting soup before diving in. The creamy texture, the savory chicken, and the heartiness of the vegetables will warm your soul with every spoonful. It’s the perfect dish to enjoy on a chilly evening or whenever you need a little taste of home.

So, next time you’re craving the flavors of a classic pot pie but want something a bit easier to make, give this creamy chicken pot pie soup a try. It’s comfort food at its best, and it’s sure to become a favorite in your household. Enjoy!
