Instant Pot Vegetable and Cheese Quesadilla Recipe: A Delicious and Nutritious Option


Are you looking for a quick and delicious meal that’s packed with veggies and cheesy goodness? Look no further than this Instant Pot Vegetable and Cheese Quesadilla recipe. It’s a nutritious option that’s perfect for a busy weeknight or a leisurely weekend brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salsa, sour cream, and guacamole for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” setting. Add the olive oil and allow it to heat up.

  2. Add the sliced red and green bell peppers, red onion, and mushrooms to the Instant Pot. Sauté for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender.

  3. Sprinkle the ground cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper over the vegetables. Stir to combine, allowing the spices to toast for about a minute.

  4. Turn off the Instant Pot and remove the vegetable mixture from the pot. Wipe the pot clean if necessary.

  5. Place one tortilla on a clean work surface. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of the shredded cheese evenly over the tortilla. Spoon a quarter of the sautéed vegetable mixture over half of the tortilla. Sprinkle with a tablespoon of chopped cilantro. Fold the tortilla in half to enclose the filling.

  6. Repeat the process with the remaining tortillas, cheese, vegetable mixture, and cilantro.

  7. Place a trivet in the Instant Pot and add 1 cup of water. Carefully place the folded quesadillas on the trivet, stacking them if necessary.

  8. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 5 minutes. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes before performing a quick pressure release.

  9. Carefully remove the quesadillas from the Instant Pot using tongs. Allow them to cool for a few minutes before slicing into wedges.

  10. Serve the quesadillas with salsa, sour cream, and guacamole if desired.

Nutritional Benefits

This Instant Pot Vegetable and Cheese Quesadilla recipe is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. The combination of colorful bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms provides an array of vitamins and antioxidants. Additionally, cheese offers a good source of protein and calcium. This recipe is a great way to sneak in extra servings of vegetables and calcium-rich dairy into your diet.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant flavors and nutrient-packed ingredients, this Instant Pot Vegetable and Cheese Quesadilla recipe is a fantastic addition to your culinary repertoire. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or a group of friends, this dish is sure to impress. The convenience of the Instant Pot makes it a breeze to whip up this mouthwatering meal in no time. So, next time you’re craving a satisfying and wholesome dish, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of vegetables and cheese in every bite!
