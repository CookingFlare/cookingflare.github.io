How to Bake Classic American Coffee Cake: Recipe Inside


Coffee cake is a beloved staple in American kitchens, known for its rich flavor, soft texture, and delightful aroma that fills the home while baking. This versatile treat is perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a delightful accompaniment to your afternoon coffee. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of baking a classic American coffee cake that is sure to impress your family and friends.

What is Coffee Cake?

Coffee cake is a type of cake that is traditionally served with coffee. It is characterized by its moist crumb and often features a crumbly streusel topping. Unlike many cakes, coffee cake does not typically contain coffee as an ingredient; instead, it is designed to complement the beverage. This cake can be made in various flavors, with cinnamon being the most popular. The beauty of a classic American coffee cake lies in its simplicity and the balance of flavors.

Ingredients You Will Need

To create a delicious classic American coffee cake, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Cake:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream or plain yogurt

For the Streusel Topping:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ½ cup chopped nuts (optional)

For Serving:

  • Powdered sugar for dusting (optional)

Equipment You’ll Need

Before you start baking, make sure you have the following equipment:

  • 9x13 inch baking pan
  • Mixing bowls
  • Electric mixer or whisk
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Rubber spatula
  • Cooling rack

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that your coffee cake bakes evenly and rises properly.

Step 2: Prepare the Baking Pan

Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan with butter or non-stick cooking spray. For added assurance, you can also line the bottom with parchment paper.

Step 3: Make the Streusel Topping

In a medium bowl, combine 1 cup of all-purpose flour, ½ cup of packed brown sugar, and 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon. Mix these dry ingredients thoroughly. Next, pour in ½ cup of melted unsalted butter and stir until the mixture is crumbly. If you’re using nuts, fold them in now. Set the streusel topping aside while you prepare the cake batter.

Step 4: Prepare the Cake Batter

In a large mixing bowl, cream together 1 cup of softened unsalted butter, 1 cup of granulated sugar, and ½ cup of packed brown sugar using an electric mixer on medium speed. Beat until the mixture is light and fluffy, which should take about 3-5 minutes.

Next, add in the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Then, stir in 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.

In a separate bowl, whisk together 2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour, 1 ½ teaspoons of baking powder, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, and ½ teaspoon of salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the creamed butter mixture, alternating with 1 cup of sour cream or plain yogurt. Begin and end with the flour mixture. Mix until just combined; be careful not to overmix, as this can affect the cake’s texture.

Step 5: Assemble the Cake

Pour half of the cake batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it evenly. Sprinkle half of the streusel topping over the batter. Then, add the remaining batter and spread it gently on top. Finally, sprinkle the rest of the streusel topping over the top layer of batter.

Step 6: Bake the Coffee Cake

Place the baking pan in the preheated oven and bake for 40-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The top should be golden brown and slightly firm to the touch.

Step 7: Cool the Cake

Once baked, remove the coffee cake from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for about 10-15 minutes. Then, transfer the cake to a wire cooling rack to cool completely.

Step 8: Serve and Enjoy

Once cooled, you can dust the top of the cake with powdered sugar if desired. Slice and serve your classic American coffee cake alongside a steaming cup of coffee or tea. It’s perfect for breakfast, brunch, or any time you want a delicious treat.

Tips for the Perfect Coffee Cake

  1. Use Room Temperature Ingredients: Make sure your butter, eggs, and sour cream are at room temperature. This helps create a smoother batter and ensures even mixing.

  2. Don’t Overmix: Overmixing the batter can lead to a dense cake. Mix just until combined for a light and fluffy texture.

  3. Experiment with Flavors: While cinnamon is traditional, feel free to add your twist. Try adding lemon zest for a citrusy flavor or swapping out some of the flour for cocoa powder for a chocolate coffee cake.

  4. Storage: If you have leftovers, store the coffee cake in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. You can also freeze slices for up to three months.

  5. Serving Suggestions: Pair your coffee cake with fresh fruit, whipped cream, or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an indulgent dessert.

Conclusion

Baking a classic American coffee cake is a rewarding experience that fills your home with warmth and delicious aromas. This recipe is not only simple to follow but also adaptable to your taste preferences. Whether you enjoy it as a morning treat or a delightful dessert, this coffee cake is sure to please. So gather your ingredients, preheat that oven, and get ready to enjoy a slice of homemade goodness that will surely become a favorite in your household! Happy baking!
