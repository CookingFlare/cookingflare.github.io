BBQ Shrimp Tacos: Quick and Tasty Recipes for Taco Night


Taco night just got a whole lot tastier with this quick and easy BBQ shrimp tacos recipe. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a flavorful meal, these tacos are sure to become a new family favorite. The combination of succulent shrimp, zesty BBQ sauce, and fresh toppings creates a tantalizing flavor profile that will leave your taste buds craving more. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe and get ready to elevate your taco night experience.

Ingredients

For the Shrimp:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce

For the Tacos:

  • 8 small flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a large bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, minced garlic, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Ensure that the shrimp are evenly coated with the seasonings.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, pour the BBQ sauce over the shrimp and stir to coat them evenly. Allow the shrimp to simmer in the BBQ sauce for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  3. Warm the Tortillas: While the shrimp is cooking, warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave until they are soft and pliable.

  4. Assemble the Tacos: To assemble the tacos, place a generous portion of the BBQ shrimp onto each tortilla. Top the shrimp with shredded cabbage, diced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, and a few slices of avocado. Squeeze a lime wedge over each taco for an added burst of citrus flavor.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Arrange the assembled tacos on a platter and serve them alongside any additional toppings or sides of your choice.

Tips for Success

  • Use fresh shrimp for the best flavor and texture.
  • Experiment with different BBQ sauce varieties to find your favorite flavor profile.
  • Customize the taco toppings to suit your preferences, such as adding a dollop of sour cream or a sprinkle of crumbled cheese.

Conclusion

These BBQ shrimp tacos are a delightful fusion of smoky, savory, and fresh flavors that will take your taco night to the next level. With minimal prep and cook time, this recipe is perfect for busy weeknights or leisurely gatherings. The succulent shrimp, paired with the vibrant toppings, creates a symphony of flavors that will leave your family and guests impressed. Embrace the simplicity and bold flavors of these BBQ shrimp tacos, and get ready to savor every bite.

So, why wait? Gather the ingredients, fire up the skillet, and treat yourself to a memorable taco night with these irresistible BBQ shrimp tacos. Your taste buds will thank you!
