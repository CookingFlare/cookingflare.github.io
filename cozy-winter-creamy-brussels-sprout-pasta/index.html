Cozy Winter Creamy Brussels Sprout Pasta


When the winter chill sets in, there’s nothing quite like a comforting, creamy pasta dish to warm you up from the inside out. This Cozy Winter Creamy Brussels Sprout Pasta is the perfect blend of hearty flavors and indulgent creaminess, making it an ideal choice for a cozy night in. The star ingredient, Brussels sprouts, adds a delightful crunch and earthy flavor to the dish, while the creamy sauce brings everything together in a luscious embrace. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring some warmth to your winter evenings.

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

  • 1 pound of fettuccine pasta
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt for boiling water

For the Creamy Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Brussels Sprouts:

  • 1 pound of Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Brussels Sprouts:

    • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Toss the halved Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
    • Spread the Brussels sprouts on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden and crispy on the edges.

  2. Cook the Pasta:

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add the fettuccine pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain the pasta and toss with a tablespoon of olive oil to prevent sticking.

  3. Prepare the Creamy Sauce:

    • In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Add the minced garlic and chopped shallot, sautéing until fragrant and translucent.
    • Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
    • Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, allowing it to melt and thicken the sauce.
    • Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste.

  4. Combine and Serve:

    • Add the roasted Brussels sprouts to the creamy sauce, tossing to coat them evenly.
    • Gently fold in the cooked fettuccine pasta, ensuring that it is well coated with the creamy Brussels sprout mixture.
    • Serve the Cozy Winter Creamy Brussels Sprout Pasta in warm bowls, garnishing with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and freshly cracked black pepper.

Enjoy the Cozy Comfort

This delightful Cozy Winter Creamy Brussels Sprout Pasta is a celebration of seasonal flavors and comforting textures. The tender fettuccine pasta, coated in a velvety Parmesan cream sauce and studded with roasted Brussels sprouts, creates a symphony of taste and satisfaction. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a special dish for a gathering of loved ones, this recipe is sure to bring warmth and joy to the table. Embrace the winter season with a bowl of this creamy indulgence, and let the cozy comfort envelop you in every savory bite.

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the magic of winter with this Cozy Winter Creamy Brussels Sprout Pasta. Happy cooking!
