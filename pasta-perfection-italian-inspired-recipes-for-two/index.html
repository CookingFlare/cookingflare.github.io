Pasta Perfection: Italian-inspired Recipes for Two


Are you looking to impress your significant other with a romantic dinner at home? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering Italian-inspired pasta recipes that are perfect for two.

1. Creamy Carbonara

Preparation time: 20 minutes | Cooking time: 15 minutes

Indulge in the creamy goodness of Carbonara, a classic Italian dish that never fails to please. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta, diced
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti, reserving a small amount of the cooking water.
  5. Immediately transfer the drained spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Stir well to coat the pasta.
  6. Pour in the egg yolk mixture and toss quickly to combine, ensuring the eggs don’t scramble.
  7. If the mixture seems too dry, add a splash of the reserved cooking water to loosen it up.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy!

2. Caprese Pasta Salad

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 10 minutes

If you’re looking for a lighter option, this Caprese Pasta Salad is the perfect choice. Bursting with fresh flavors, it’s a great dish for a warm summer evening.

Ingredients:

  • 250g fusilli pasta
  • 200g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 200g mozzarella pearls
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fusilli pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and let cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooled pasta, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella pearls, and torn basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad, and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss gently to combine all the ingredients, ensuring everything is evenly coated.
  5. Let the salad sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve chilled or at room temperature. Enjoy the refreshing taste of summer!

3. Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells

Preparation time: 30 minutes | Cooking time: 25 minutes

Looking for a comforting and hearty meal? These Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 18 jumbo pasta shells
  • 250g frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook the jumbo pasta shells according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, combine the thawed and squeezed dry spinach, ricotta cheese, and beaten egg. Mix well.
  4. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  5. Stuff each cooked pasta shell with the spinach and ricotta mixture, and arrange them in the baking dish.
  6. Pour the remaining marinara sauce over the stuffed shells, and sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors!

Conclusion

Italian cuisine has a way of bringing people together, and these Pasta Perfection recipes are no exception. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply want to enjoy a delicious meal with your loved one, these Italian-inspired dishes are sure to impress. So grab your apron, get cooking, and savor the flavors of Italy!
