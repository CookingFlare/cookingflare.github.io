Instant Pot Seafood Pasta: A Delicious Dish in Minutes


For seafood lovers and pasta aficionados, there’s nothing better than a dish that combines the rich flavors of the ocean with the comforting texture of pasta. What’s even better? Preparing this indulgent delight in an Instant Pot to save both time and effort without compromising taste. Instant Pot Seafood Pasta is the perfect recipe to elevate your meal game, whether you’re hosting a dinner party or craving something sophisticated after a long day. With minimal prep and a quick cook time, this dish delivers exquisite results in mere minutes.

Why Choose an Instant Pot for Seafood Pasta?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking, offering speed, convenience, and versatility in one device. When it comes to cooking delicate seafood and pasta, the pressure cooking functionality ensures that they’re perfectly tender and infused with all the flavors of your sauce. Moreover, the sauté function allows you to build layers of flavors directly in the pot, making it the ideal one-stop solution for creating a mouthwatering seafood pasta dish from start to finish.

This method reduces the chance of overcooked seafood or mushy pasta, which can be common pitfalls with traditional stovetop recipes. The Instant Pot locks in moisture and boosts flavor profiles, amplifying the natural sweetness of seafood while blending it with seasonings, aromatics, and your choice of sauce.

Ingredients You’ll Need

The beauty of this recipe lies in its adaptability. Feel free to adjust the ingredients to suit your personal tastes or accommodate what’s available in your pantry. Below is a list of ingredients that make up the perfect Instant Pot Seafood Pasta:

Main Ingredients:

  • Seafood mix (shrimp, scallops, mussels, or calamari — about 1 pound)
  • Pasta (spaghetti, linguine, or penne — 8 ounces, broken in half for easier fitting)
  • Water or seafood stock (2 cups)
  • Juicy diced tomatoes (1 cup)
  • Tomato sauce (1 cup, or a marinara of your choice)

Aromatics:

  • Garlic (4 cloves, minced)
  • Shallots or onion (1 medium, diced)
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Seasonings and Spices:

  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
  • Red chili flakes (optional, for a kick of heat)
  • Paprika (1/2 teaspoon, to add depth)
  • Italian seasoning or dried oregano (1 teaspoon)

Additional Flavor Enhancers:

  • Olive oil (2 tablespoons, for sautéing)
  • White wine (1/2 cup, optional, but adds richness to the dish)
  • Lemon juice (zest and juice of 1 lemon)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)

Step-by-Step Directions

1. Prep the Ingredients

Begin by rinsing the seafood mix thoroughly under cold water to remove any grit or impurities. Dry with a paper towel. Chop the garlic, and dice the shallot or onion. If needed, break pasta in half so it fits easily in the Instant Pot.

2. Sauté for Flavor

Set your Instant Pot to Sauté mode and let it heat up for a few seconds. Add olive oil, then sauté the garlic and shallot until fragrant, about 1–2 minutes. Watch closely to ensure the garlic doesn’t burn, as this could impart a bitter taste.

Next, deglaze the pot with white wine. Stir, scraping up any flavorful browned bits stuck to the bottom. This step adds an incredible depth of flavor to your sauce.

3. Add the Liquid Base

Pour in the seafood stock (or water) and stir in the tomato sauce and diced tomatoes. Add the seasonings: salt, pepper, chili flakes, paprika, and Italian seasoning. Mix everything well to combine.

4. Layer the Pasta

Carefully add your pasta to the pot, ensuring it is submerged in the liquid but not overly stirred. This step is key to ensuring the pasta cooks evenly while absorbing the rich tomato-based flavor.

5. Cook Under Pressure

Seal the Instant Pot lid and set to High Pressure for 5 minutes if using thin pasta (like spaghetti or linguine), and 6–7 minutes for thicker options (like penne). The pressure cooking time allows for perfectly al dente noodles without the need for constant supervision.

Once the cooking time is complete, use the Quick Release function to release the pressure. Carefully remove the lid and give the dish a gentle stir.

6. Add Seafood

Switch back to Sauté mode and add the seafood mix. Stir gently, allowing the seafood to simmer in the hot sauce for about 3–4 minutes or until cooked through. Shrimp should turn pink, scallops should become opaque, and mussels should open up.

If using frozen seafood, ensure it’s thawed beforehand for even cooking. Avoid overcooking, as seafood tends to turn rubbery if left in high heat for too long.

7. Finish with Fresh Ingredients

Turn off the Instant Pot and stir in fresh lemon juice and zest for brightness. Garnish with chopped parsley for a fresh, earthy flavor and grated Parmesan for creaminess (optional but highly recommended). Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning as needed.

Serving Suggestions

Once your tantalizing Instant Pot Seafood Pasta is ready, it’s time to serve! Plate the pasta and top it with an extra sprinkle of fresh parsley or basil for visual appeal and aroma. Pair this dish with crusty garlic bread or a refreshing green salad for a complete meal. If you enjoy pairing wine with seafood, a glass of crisp Sauvignon Blanc or a buttery Chardonnay will beautifully complement the dish’s flavors.

Tips for Success

To get the most out of your Instant Pot Seafood Pasta, consider these helpful tips:

  • Use high-quality seafood: Fresh seafood will deliver the best flavor, but frozen can also work well if properly thawed.
  • Choose the right pasta: Stick with pasta shapes that hold sauce well, like linguine or penne.
  • Don’t skimp on deglazing: Deglazing the pot enhances every layer of flavor in your sauce.
  • Avoid overcrowding: Ensure the Instant Pot isn’t overfilled to allow proper cooking.
  • Timing is key: Quick-release the pressure immediately when the cook time ends to prevent overcooking.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Not only is this Instant Pot Seafood Pasta quick and easy to prepare, but it also offers the perfect balance of decadence and simplicity. The natural sweetness of seafood combined with the richness of the tomato-based sauce creates a harmonious flavor profile that’s hard to resist. Whether you’re looking to impress guests or upgrade your weekly dinner rotation, this recipe is guaranteed to be one you’ll turn to again and again.

With its easy cleanup and straightforward process, the Instant Pot becomes your secret weapon for creating restaurant-quality meals in a fraction of the time. So go ahead—grab your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and dive into the delightful world of seafood pasta made in minutes. Bon appétit!
