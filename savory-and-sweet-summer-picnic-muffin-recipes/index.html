Savory and Sweet Summer Picnic Muffin Recipes


Summer picnics are all about enjoying the great outdoors with delicious food, and what better way to elevate your picnic spread than with a variety of savory and sweet muffins? These portable treats are easy to pack and even easier to enjoy, making them the perfect addition to any picnic basket. Whether you’re a fan of savory flavors or have a sweet tooth, we’ve got you covered with these mouthwatering muffin recipes that are sure to impress your fellow picnickers.

Savory Spinach and Feta Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the melted butter, eggs, and milk.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the chopped spinach, feta cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about two-thirds full.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

These savory spinach and feta muffins are packed with flavor and make for a delightful addition to any picnic. The combination of fresh spinach and tangy feta cheese creates a savory muffin that pairs perfectly with a variety of picnic spreads.

Sweet Blueberry Lemon Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh blueberries
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk, and mix until just combined.
  6. Gently fold in the blueberries, lemon zest, and lemon juice.
  7. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about two-thirds full.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the muffins are lightly golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

These sweet blueberry lemon muffins are bursting with fresh fruit flavor and are the perfect sweet treat for a summer picnic. The combination of juicy blueberries and zesty lemon creates a delightful flavor that will have everyone reaching for seconds.

Tips for Packing Muffins for a Picnic

When packing muffins for a picnic, it’s essential to ensure they stay fresh and intact during transport. Here are a few tips to help you pack your savory and sweet muffins for a successful picnic:

  • Use a sturdy, airtight container to store the muffins and prevent them from getting squished.
  • Layer the muffins with parchment paper to prevent sticking and maintain their shape.
  • If you’re packing both savory and sweet muffins, consider using separate containers to prevent flavor transfer.
  • Keep the muffins in a cool, shaded spot to prevent them from getting too warm and losing their freshness.

With these savory and sweet muffin recipes in your repertoire, you’re all set to impress your picnic companions with a delightful array of homemade treats. So pack your picnic basket, grab a blanket, and head outdoors to enjoy the sunshine and these delicious muffins!
