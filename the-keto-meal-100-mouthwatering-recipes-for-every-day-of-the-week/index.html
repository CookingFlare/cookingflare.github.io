The Keto Meal: 100 Mouthwatering Recipes for Every Day of the Week


Embarking on a ketogenic diet can leave you wondering what to cook, but don’t worry. While many might think that the ketogenic diet has strict restrictions, it’s actually full of creative and delicious possibilities. With “The Keto Meal: 100 Mouthwatering Recipes for Every Day of the Week,” not only can you find inspiration for an entire week’s worth of mouthwatering meals but also enjoy the boundless creativity of a low-carb, high-fat lifestyle.

Monday: The Classic BLT Sandwich

To begin our week of keto-friendly meals, let’s switch it up with a beloved classic – the BLT sandwich, only keto-ified!

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 slices of Thick-Cut Bacon, nitrate-free
    • 2 Thick Slices of Heirloom Tomato
    • 2 Leaf Lettuce, Large Leaves
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 1 Avocado, sliced
    • 2 slices of Cheddar Cheese
    • 2 Soft Keto Buns, see recipe below

  • Instructions:

    1. Cook the bacon until crispy, once done, place on a paper towel to drain excess grease.
    2. For soft keto buns, mix almond flour, shredded mozzarella, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Microwave for 1 minute or until the dough forms a ball. Let it cool, slice it down the middle, and toast.
    3. Assemble the sandwich by layering two slices of cheddar cheese, crispy bacon, slices of tomato, leaf lettuce, avocado, and season with salt and pepper. Put the second slice of keto bun on top and enjoy.

Tuesday: Zucchini Noodles (Zoodles) with Pesto

A versatile dish: Zucchini noodles or ‘zoodles’ provide a delicious alternative to traditional pasta. Here’s a simple and tasty recipe to include in your breakfast or dinner meals on your keto diet

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 Medium Zucchinis)
    • Olive oil
    • Salt
    • 1/2 Cup of Homemade Pesto Sauce
    • Parmigiano Reggiano, for garnishing
    • Crushed Red Pepper, optional

  • Instructions:

    1. Use a spiralizer or a julienne peeler to make ribbons of zucchini. Place them in a colander to let the water drain.
    2. Salt your zucchini noodles to draw out excess water, let them rest for 10 minutes, and pat dry with a paper towel.
    3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add zucchini noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes; cook longer for a more tender zucchini.
    4. Stir in your homemade pesto sauce. Cook for another minute, then remove from heat and top it off with Parmigiano Reggiano and crushed red pepper if you like it spicy.

Wednesday: Keto BBQ Pulled Pork with Slaw

Who said lavish gatherings are off-limits on keto? Indulge in the heartiness of BBQ pulled pork, delightfully paired with a tangy and refreshing cabbage slaw for a glorious meal with friends and family.

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 lbs Pork Shoulder
    • 1/2 Cup Salted Butter
    • 1/2 Cup Onion, Finely Chopped
    • 1/4 Cup Garlic, Minced
    • 1/2 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar
    • 1/2 Cup Water
    • 1/2 Cup Liquid Honey
    • 1 Cup Barbecue Sauce
    • 4 Cups Green Cabbage, Shredded
    • 2 Carrots, Grated

  • Instructions:

    1. Season the pork shoulder generously with salt and pepper. Heat a tablespoon of butter in a large oven-safe pot over high heat. Sear the pork on all sides until golden.
    2. Remove the pork and add the remaining butter, chopped onions, and minced garlic; cook till the onions give off their moisture and begin to caramelize.
    3. Return the pork to the pot, pour in the apple cider vinegar, water, and liquid honey. Bring to a gentle simmer, then cover and place in the preheated oven.
    4. Cook for 3-3.5 hours until the pork is tender and can be easily pulled apart. Shred the meat and drizzle with remaining barbecue sauce.
    5. For the slaw, toss shredded cabbage and grated carrots with your favorite keto-friendly dressing. Combine with pulled pork for a perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Thursday: Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast

This delicious chicken breast is packed with nutrient-dense spinach and rich, creamy feta cheese, making for a wholesome yet delightful meal.

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 Whole Chicken Breast
    • 2 Lb. Fresh Spinach
    • 1/2 Cup Feta Cheese, Crumbled
    • 1/4 Lob of Cream Cheese
    • 1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
    • Salt and Pepper, to taste

  • Instructions:

    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Blanch the spinach in boiling water for about a minute, drain and chop finely.
    2. Mix the spinach, crumbled feta, cream cheese, and garlic powder in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
    3. Carefully slice the chicken breast horizontally, open it up like a book, and stuff the spinach mix into the middle. Close the chicken around the filling and season both sides with salt and pepper.
    4. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a pan and sear the chicken until golden on both sides. Then transfer it to the oven to cook for about 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
    5. Let cool and slice, to serve.

Friday: Zucchini Bread Muffins

Satisfy your sweet tooth with these Keto Zucchini Bread Muffins – a guilt-free dessert that doesn’t sacrifice flavor

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 Large Eggs
    • 3/4 Cup Granulated Erythritol
    • 1/2 Cup Coconut Oil, Melting
    • 1 3/4 Cup Almond Flour
    • 1/2 Cup Unsweetened Applesauce
    • 1/2 Cup Shredded Zucchini
    • 1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
    • 1/2 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
    • 1/2 teaspoon Ground Nutmeg
    • Pinch of Salt

  • Instructions:

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (177°C) and line a muffin tin with paper muffin cups.
    2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs and then add the erythritol, melted coconut oil, and unsweetened applesauce. Mix well.
    3. In a separate bowl, mix together the almond flour, shredded zucchini, baking soda, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and pinch of salt.
    4. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Mix well.
    5. Spoon the batter into the muffin cups filling almost to the top.
    6. Cook for 20-25 mins until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the muffins to cool before enjoying.

Saturday: Almond Flour Pancakes

Satisfy your weekend cravings with a stack of delicious almond flour pancakes that fit perfectly into your keto meal plan

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 cup Almond Flour
    • 1 Tbsp Baking Powder
    • 1/2 tsp Salt
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
    • 2 Tbsp Coconut Oil or Butter, melted

  • Instructions:

    1. In a bowl, mix the almond flour, baking powder, and salt to form a uniform mixture.
    2. Create a well in the center and add the eggs, heavy cream, and melted coconut oil or butter. Mix until smooth.
    3. Preheat a large non-stick skillet over medium-low heat. Use a ladle to pour the batter onto the skillet, making your desired pancake size.
    4. Cook pancakes for about 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Serve immediately or keep warm in the oven at a low temperature.
    5. You can top it with sugar-free syrup or make a delicious fruit sauce with berries keto style.

Sunday: Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

A cozy meal for a Sunday evening, the Creamy Tomato Basil Soup will warm you up and delight your taste buds.

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 tbsp Olive oil
    • 1 Small Onion, chopped
    • 2 Stalks Celery, chopped
    • 2 Carrots, chopped
    • 2 Cloves Garlic, minced
    • 1 can Diced Tomatoes
    • 2 cups Chicken Broth
    • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
    • 1/4 cup Fresh Basil
    • Salt and Pepper to taste
    • Optional: Gruyere Cheese or Keto Bread for topping

  • Instructions:

    1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion, celery, and carrots. Cook until they’re tender, around 5 minutes.
    2. Add minced garlic, and cook for a minute until fragrant.
    3. Stir in diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and salt and pepper. Bring to boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for 20 minutes.
    4. Remove from heat, stir in the heavy cream and fresh basil. Blend the mixture using an immersion blender or by transferring the soup to a large food processor.
    5. Serve hot, topped with shredded Gruyere cheese or toasted keto bread cubes.

With a variety of flavors and textures, “The Keto Meal: 100 Mouthwatering Recipes for Every Day of the Week” ensures that you’ll never be bored or restricted while enjoying the life-changing benefits of the ketogenic diet. Happy cooking!
