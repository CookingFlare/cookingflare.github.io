BBQ Chicken Pizza: A Crowd-Pleasing Recipe


Few dishes can boast the scrumptious appeal of BBQ chicken pizza. This delicious meal gathers diverse flavors and textures to amaze your family and friends during gatherings or relaxed, home-cooked dinner nights. Let’s dive into the scrumptious details and create a perfect and irresistible BBQ chicken pizza.

Ingredients

To bring together this fabulous explosion of flavors, you’ll need the following:

  • For the Dough:

    • 3 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 cup warm water
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • For the BBQ Chicken Topping:

    • 1 medium rotisserie chicken, cooled and deboned
    • 1 cup barbecue sauce
    • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
    • 1/4 cup red bell pepper, diced
    • 2 tablespoons jalapeno peppers, diced
    • Salt and Pepper, to taste

  • Toppings:

    • Fresh cilantro, chopped
    • Additional jalapeno peppers (optional)

Perfecting the Dough

First, combine the yeast, sugar, and warm water in a bowl. Let the mixture sit for about 10 minutes, allowing the yeast to activate and froth. In a separate big bowl, combine the flour and salt. Gradually add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix thoroughly until you have a unified, slightly sticky dough.

On a floured surface, knead the dough rigorously for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place it in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for one hour. After the dough has risen, punch it down and let it cool.

Preparing the BBQ Chicken Topping

While the dough is cooling, start preparing your toppings. Begin by deboning the cooled rotisserie chicken and tearing the chicken meat into chewable chunks.

Next, mix the barbecue sauce with the chicken in a bowl. Combine the diced red onion, red bell pepper, and jalapeno peppers to the chicken mixture. Make sure to season everything generously with salt and pepper.

Assembling the Pizza

Once your dough and toppings are ready, preheat your oven to 425°F (219°C). If you have a baking stone, place it in the oven while preheating.

Gently, roll out your dough on a floured surface until it reaches your desired thickness. Then transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza peel, leaving enough space for the toppings.

Add the BBQ chicken mixture, generously spreading it across your dough. Evenly sprinkle the shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheeses over the chicken mixture.

Bake your exquisite BBQ chicken pizza in the preheated oven for approximately 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. If you don’t have a baking stone, move the pizza to the topmost rack to ensure even baking.

Finalizing the Dish

Remove your divine BBQ chicken pizza from the oven and let it cool for approximately 5 minutes. This short cooling period brings all flavors together and makes slicing a breeze.

Finally, garnish your pizza with fresh cilantro and extra jalapeno peppers if you desire. Slice your BBQ chicken pizza into tasty squares, and enjoy this irresistible crowd-pleaser!

And there you have it – a delectable BBQ chicken pizza that can make any meal feel like a party. Serve it with a crisp salad and your favorite beverage for an unbeatable home-cooked meal.
