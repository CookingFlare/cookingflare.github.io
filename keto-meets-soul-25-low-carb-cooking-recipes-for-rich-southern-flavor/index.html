Keto Meets Soul: 25 Low-Carb Cooking recipes for Rich Southern Flavor


Prep your tastebuds for a delicious journey as we explore 25 scrumptious, low-carb recipes with rich southern flavors.   1. Keto Southern Jalapeño Hush Puppies

These crunchy delights obtain their texture from almond flour and are topped with a kick of jalapeño heat. Enjoy this southern classic without all the carbs.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup shredded zucchini
  • ½ cup sliced jalapeños
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooking oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. Squeeze zucchini to remove excess moisture.

  2. Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl, then form into small patties.

  3. Heat cooking oil to 375°F (190°C).

  4. Carefully drop patties into oil and fry for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown.

  5. Serve hot with your favorite dip.

  6. Low-Carb Southern Fried Chicken

This traditional fried chicken recipe gets a low-carb makeover using almond flour, rather than the typical dense breading. The result? Crispy, crunchy, and scrumptious fried chicken without that guilt trip after.

Ingredients:

  • 4 whole chicken legs (drumsticks and thighs)
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • Cooking oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. Cut a slit into the thickest part of the chicken legs.

  2. In a shallow bowl whisk the eggs.

  3. In another shallow bowl, mix the almond flour with the salt, paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper.

  4. Dip the chicken legs into the egg wash then coat with almond flour mixture.

  5. Heat cooking oil in a large skillet and fry the chicken until cooked through and golden brown, approximately 10-15 minutes per side.

  6. Remove chicken from oil and place on a paper towel-lined plate to remove excess oil.

  7. Southern Keto Cucumber Salad

Refreshingly cool and delightfully low-carb, this cucumber salad is a must-try for any southern cookout or family dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cucumber, red onion, dill, salt, and pepper.

  2. Drizzle lemon juice over the cucumber mixture and gently toss to combine.

  3. Allow the salad to sit for at least an hour before serving, so flavors can mesh together.

  4. Keto Crab Cake Sloppy Joes

Yes, you read that right! southern sloppy joes made with crab – a keto twist on the southern classic. These crab cakes are packed with flavor without the heaviness of traditional breaded versions.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lump crab meat, drained
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp red onion, finely diced
  • 1 tbsp Old Bay seasoning
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooking oil, for frying
  • Keto-friendly buns, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients until fully mixed.

  2. Form crab mixture into 4 equal-sized cakes.

  3. Heat cooking oil in a large skillet over medium heat and cook cakes for about 5 minutes per side, until crispy and golden.

  4. Place crab cakes on keto-friendly buns and enjoy!

  5. Low-Carb Pimento Cheese Deviled Eggs

A fun twist on southern deviled eggs - elevated by incorporating pimento cheese, the south’s beloved spread. Impress your friends with this delectable appetizer!

Ingredients:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, peeled
  • 4 tbsp pimento cheese
  • 1 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp chives, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Halve hard-boiled eggs lengthwise; remove yolks and place in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the pimento cheese, cream cheese, and mayonnaise to the yolk mixture, and mash together with a fork.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Spoon the yolk mixture evenly into the egg whites, then top each with a sprinkle of chopped chives.

And there you have it, 5 deliciously low-carb recipes that offer southern flavors while fitting within the ketogenic diet. Keto never tasted so good! Now go ahead, put that non-stick frying pan to use, and wow your family and friends with these delightful low-carb dishes. Happy cooking!
