Vegan Chocolate Chip Muffins with a Caramel Drizzle and a Sprinkle of Chocolate Chips Recipe: A Soft and Chewy Treat


Vegan Chocolate Chip Muffins with a Caramel Drizzle and a Sprinkle of Chocolate Chips Recipe: A Soft and Chewy Treat

Who doesn’t love a warm, freshly baked muffin straight out of the oven? And when it’s vegan, chocolatey, and topped with a decadent caramel drizzle and a sprinkle of chocolate chips, it becomes a treat that is hard to resist. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the steps to create these soft and chewy vegan chocolate chip muffins that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease it lightly with oil.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the almond milk, maple syrup, coconut sugar, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Whisk until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense muffins.

  5. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips, reserving a handful for sprinkling on top of the muffins.

  6. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 3/4 full. Top each muffin with a few reserved chocolate chips.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  8. Remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Caramel Drizzle:

To take these vegan chocolate chip muffins to the next level, let’s make a simple caramel drizzle to drizzle over the top.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the coconut cream, maple syrup, and salt. Heat over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  2. Remove from heat and let the caramel cool for a few minutes. It will thicken further as it cools.

  3. Drizzle the caramel over the cooled muffins, allowing it to drip down the sides.

Serving Suggestions:

These vegan chocolate chip muffins with a caramel drizzle and a sprinkle of chocolate chips are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as an afternoon pick-me-up. Serve them with a cup of your favorite hot beverage for a delightful treat.

Storage:

Store any leftover muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. If you prefer them warm, simply pop them in the microwave for a few seconds before enjoying.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in these soft and chewy vegan chocolate chip muffins. With their delightful combination of chocolate, caramel, and a sprinkle of chocolate chips, they are sure to become a favorite among vegans and non-vegans alike. Enjoy!
