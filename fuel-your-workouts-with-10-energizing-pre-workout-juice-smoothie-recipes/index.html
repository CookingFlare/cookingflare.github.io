Fuel Your Workouts with 10 Energizing Pre-Workout Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Gearing up for a workout? Fuel your bod with these delicious and energy-boosting juices and smoothies before you hit the gym or the track.

1. Green Power Blast

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Blend together the avocado and banana until smooth.
  2. Add the spinach and blend until combined.
  3. Gradually add almond milk until the desired consistency is reached.
  4. Stir in chia seeds and honey, and serve.

2. Berry Blast Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Blend mixed berries and banana together until smooth.
  2. Add almond butter, almond milk, and Greek yogurt.
  3. Mix well, and sweeten with honey to taste.

3. Matcha Power-Up

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon matcha powder
  • 1/2 sweet banana
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Mix matcha powder with the almond milk until well combined.
  2. Add the banana and blend until smooth.
  3. Stir in chia seeds and honey.

4. Tropical Mango Madness

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup diced mango
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Place mango and pineapple chunks in a blender.
  2. Pour the almond milk and Greek yogurt in and blend until smooth.
  3. Add honey, and blend again to combine.

5. Citrus Charge

Ingredients:

  • 1 Orange
  • 1 cup carrot juice
  • 1 tablespoon flaxseeds
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Blend the orange and carrot juice together.
  2. Add flaxseeds, honey, and ice cubes for a refreshing treat.
  3. Blend again until thoroughly mixed and chilled.

6. Pineapple-Papaya Punch

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup papaya chunks
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Toss pineapple chunks, papaya chunks, and coconut water in a blender.
  2. Mix well and add honey.
  3. Stir in ice cubes for an extra chill.

7. Matcha Madness

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 teaspoons matcha powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Slightly warm the unsweetened almond milk and pour it over the matcha powder.
  2. Let it sit for 1-2 minutes to help the matcha powder mix well.
  3. Blend with the banana, honey, and ice until smooth.

8. Berry Beet Booster

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup beetroots
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Blend mixed berries and beetroots until smooth.
  2. Add almond milk and chia seeds.
  3. Whisk in honey before serving.

9. Carrot Spice Twist

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup carrot juice
  • 1/2 cup apple juice
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Mix carrot juice and apple juice together in a blender.
  2. Add turmeric and cinnamon, blending until frothy.
  3. Stir in honey and ice for a spiced treat.

10. Green Grape Fruit Smash

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green grapes
  • 1/2 orange
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine green grapes and half the orange.
  2. Blend until smooth and pour into a serving glass.
  3. Mix lemon juice and honey, then top the grape mixture with this mixture.

Now that you have these revitalizing pre-workout recipes, you’ll be energized and ready for any challenge that awaits you in the gym! Enjoy the delicious and nourishing drinks, and let’s hit the workout together!
