Classic Cocktails for the Holidays: Festive Recipes to Celebrate


The holiday season is upon us, and what better way to celebrate than with a classic cocktail that evokes the warmth and joy of the season? From timeless favorites to modern twists, these festive recipes are sure to delight your guests and leave them feeling merry and bright.

The Classic Manhattan

A staple of holiday gatherings, the Manhattan is a timeless cocktail that pairs perfectly with the festive atmosphere. Made with whiskey, vermouth, and bitters, this classic cocktail is a masterclass in balance and complexity.

  • 2 ounces whiskey
  • 1 ounce vermouth
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • Cherry garnish

Combine whiskey, vermouth, and bitters in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until chilled, then strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a cherry and serve.

The Sparkling Cranberry Spritzer

Add a touch of sparkle to your holiday gathering with this refreshing and fruity cocktail. Made with cranberry juice, prosecco, and a splash of lime juice, this spritzer is the perfect drink to toast the season.

  • 2 ounces cranberry juice
  • 4 ounces prosecco
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice
  • Lime wheel garnish

Combine cranberry juice and lime juice in a glass filled with ice. Top with prosecco and stir gently. Garnish with a lime wheel and serve.

The Spiced Apple Cider Mule

This twist on the classic Moscow Mule adds a warm and spicy kick to the traditional recipe. Made with apple cider, ginger beer, and a splash of bourbon, this cocktail is a perfect fit for the holiday season.

  • 2 ounces bourbon
  • 4 ounces apple cider
  • 2 ounces ginger beer
  • Lime wheel garnish

Combine bourbon, apple cider, and ginger beer in a copper mug filled with ice. Stir gently and garnish with a lime wheel. Serve with a cinnamon stick garnish for added festive flair.

The Eggnog White Russian

Eggnog is a holiday staple, and this creamy cocktail is a perfect way to enjoy it. Made with eggnog, vodka, and coffee liqueur, this White Russian is a rich and indulgent treat.

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 2 ounces eggnog
  • 1 ounce coffee liqueur
  • Nutmeg garnish

Combine vodka, eggnog, and coffee liqueur in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until chilled, then strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a sprinkle of nutmeg and serve.

The Mulled Wine Sangria

This festive twist on traditional sangria adds a warm and spicy kick to the classic recipe. Made with mulled wine, orange juice, and a splash of brandy, this cocktail is a perfect fit for the holiday season.

  • 2 ounces mulled wine
  • 2 ounces orange juice
  • 1 ounce brandy
  • Orange slice garnish

Combine mulled wine, orange juice, and brandy in a large pitcher. Stir gently and serve in glasses over ice. Garnish with an orange slice and serve with a cinnamon stick garnish for added festive flair.

The Gingerbread Martini

This festive martini is a perfect fit for the holiday season. Made with gingerbread-flavored liqueur, vodka, and a splash of cream, this cocktail is a sweet and indulgent treat.

  • 2 ounces gingerbread-flavored liqueur
  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1/2 ounce cream
  • Whipped cream garnish

Combine gingerbread-flavored liqueur, vodka, and cream in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until chilled, then strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with whipped cream and serve.

These classic cocktails are sure to be a hit at your holiday gathering. Whether you’re looking for a timeless favorite or a modern twist, there’s something for everyone on this list. So grab your mixing glass, shake (or stir) things up, and get ready to celebrate the holiday season in style!
