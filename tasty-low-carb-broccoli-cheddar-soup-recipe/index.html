Tasty Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy soup recipe that will warm your soul without compromising your low-carb diet? Look no further, because this Tasty Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup Recipe is here to satisfy your cravings and keep you on track with your nutritional goals. Packed with the goodness of fresh broccoli and the creamy richness of cheddar cheese, this soup is a comforting and guilt-free treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional garnish: extra shredded cheddar cheese, crispy bacon bits, or chopped chives

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Broccoli: Start by washing the broccoli florets thoroughly and cutting them into small pieces. Set them aside for later.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot over medium heat, melt the butter and add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  3. Cook the Broccoli: Add the prepared broccoli florets to the pot and stir them with the sautéed aromatics. Let the broccoli cook for about 5 minutes, allowing it to soften slightly.

  4. Add the Broth: Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, covering the broccoli and aromatics. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.

  5. Blend the Soup: Once the broccoli is cooked, use an immersion blender or transfer the mixture to a blender to purée the soup until smooth. Be cautious when blending hot liquids.

  6. Add the Cream and Cheese: Return the pot to low heat and stir in the heavy cream. Gradually add the shredded cheddar cheese, stirring continuously until the cheese is melted and the soup is creamy.

  7. Season to Perfection: Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning according to your preference.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the warm and velvety soup into bowls, and if desired, garnish with extra shredded cheddar cheese, crispy bacon bits, or chopped chives for an extra flavor boost.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 cup
  • Calories: 280
  • Total Fat: 22g
  • Saturated Fat: 14g
  • Cholesterol: 75mg
  • Sodium: 580mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 9g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Sugars: 3g
  • Protein: 12g

Final Thoughts

This Tasty Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup Recipe is a fantastic way to indulge in a creamy and satisfying soup while maintaining a low-carb lifestyle. Whether you’re looking for a comforting meal on a chilly evening or a flavorful dish to impress your friends and family, this recipe has got you covered. With its simple preparation and delightful flavors, it’s sure to become a regular on your dinner table. So, go ahead and whip up a batch of this delectable soup to treat your taste buds and nourish your body at the same time!
