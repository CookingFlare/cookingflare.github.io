Decadent Chocolate Yule Log: A Showstopper Holiday Dessert


The holiday season is upon us, and with it, the pressure to create a showstopping dessert that will impress our loved ones. Look no further than the Decadent Chocolate Yule Log, a rich and decadent masterpiece that is sure to become a new family tradition.

The Perfect Combination

The Yule Log, a classic French dessert, typically consists of a genoise or sponge cake filled with buttercream and coated in chocolate. However, to take it to the next level, we’re going to add some extra ingredients to create a truly decadent treat. We’ll be using high-quality dark chocolate, rich caramel, and crunchy hazelnuts to create a dessert that is both elegant and indulgent.

The Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup (85g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) whole milk, at room temperature
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (120g) dark chocolate chips, at least 70% cocoa solids
  • 1/2 cup (120g) caramel sauce
  • 1/2 cup (60g) chopped hazelnuts
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting

The Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 10x15-inch (25x38cm) jelly roll pan and line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and whisk until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the cake is set and springs back when touched.
  7. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 5 minutes.
  8. Dust the cake with confectioners’ sugar and invert onto a wire rack to cool completely.

The Assembly

  1. Once the cake is completely cool, use a serrated knife to cut off the edges and create a smooth surface.
  2. Melt the chocolate chips in a double boiler or in the microwave in 30-second increments, stirring between each interval until smooth.
  3. Spread the melted chocolate over the top and sides of the cake, making sure to cover it evenly.
  4. Drizzle the caramel sauce over the top of the cake, allowing it to drizzle down the sides.
  5. Sprinkle the chopped hazelnuts over the top of the cake, pressing them gently into the chocolate.
  6. Place the cake on a serving plate or cake stand and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to set the chocolate.

The Finishing Touches

  1. Just before serving, use a serrated knife to create a rustic, bark-like texture on the outside of the cake.
  2. Dust the top of the cake with confectioners’ sugar and serve chilled.

Conclusion

The Decadent Chocolate Yule Log is a showstopping dessert that is sure to impress your holiday guests. With its rich, velvety chocolate and crunchy hazelnuts, it’s the perfect treat to enjoy during the holiday season. So why not give it a try and make it a new family tradition?
