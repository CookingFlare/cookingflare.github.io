Wholesome Paleo Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that’s both satisfying and healthy, then you’re in for a treat with this Wholesome Paleo Stuffed Bell Peppers recipe. Packed with flavorful ingredients and a perfect balance of protein, healthy fats, and fiber, these stuffed bell peppers are a crowd-pleaser and an ideal addition to your paleo recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Stuffed Bell Peppers:

  • 4 large bell peppers, any color
  • 1 pound ground turkey or chicken
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Prepare the bell peppers by cutting off the tops and removing the seeds and membranes. Place the peppers in a baking dish and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground turkey or chicken until it’s no longer pink. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent.

  4. Stir in the cauliflower rice, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few minutes until the cauliflower rice is tender.

  5. Meanwhile, in a separate saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, olive oil, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  6. Once the turkey or chicken and cauliflower rice mixture is ready, spoon it into the prepared bell peppers, filling them to the top.

  7. Pour the tomato sauce over the stuffed bell peppers, covering them evenly.

  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.

  9. Once the stuffed bell peppers are cooked, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Wholesome Paleo Stuffed Bell Peppers are versatile and can be served in various ways. You can enjoy them as a main dish alongside a fresh salad, or pair them with cauliflower rice for a complete paleo meal. If you’re not strictly following a paleo diet, feel free to sprinkle some grated cheese on top before baking for an extra indulgent touch.

Nutritional Benefits

This recipe is not only delicious but also nutritionally dense. Bell peppers are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, while the lean ground turkey or chicken provides high-quality protein. The cauliflower rice adds a serving of vegetables and fiber, making this dish a wholesome choice for anyone looking to eat healthily without sacrificing flavor.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re following a paleo lifestyle or simply looking to incorporate more nutritious recipes into your meal rotation, these Wholesome Paleo Stuffed Bell Peppers are sure to become a favorite. The combination of vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients makes this dish a winner for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. Plus, the leftovers are just as delicious, making it a perfect make-ahead option for busy days.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and nourishing meal, give this recipe a try and savor the wholesome goodness of these Paleo Stuffed Bell Peppers.
