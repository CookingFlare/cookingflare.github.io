Satisfying Philly Cheesesteak Stuffed Peppers: A Low-Carb Option


If you’re a fan of Philly cheesesteak sandwiches but want a low-carb alternative, then look no further! These satisfying Philly cheesesteak stuffed peppers are a delicious and healthy twist on the classic sandwich. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound thinly sliced beef (such as ribeye or sirloin)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 4 slices of provolone cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the peppers in a baking dish, standing upright.

  3. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the thinly sliced beef and cook until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the onion, garlic, and green bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the skillet and mix well with the vegetables. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Spoon the beef and vegetable mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers, filling them to the top.

  7. Top each stuffed pepper with a slice of provolone cheese.

  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 5 minutes to allow the cheese to brown slightly.

  10. Carefully remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

These Philly cheesesteak stuffed peppers make for a satisfying and low-carb meal that is sure to impress. The combination of tender beef, sautéed vegetables, and melted provolone cheese is simply irresistible. Plus, the bell peppers add a fresh and vibrant element to the dish.

Not only are these stuffed peppers delicious, but they are also packed with nutrients. Bell peppers are a great source of vitamins A and C, as well as fiber. The lean beef provides protein and essential minerals, making this dish a well-rounded option for those looking to maintain a healthy and balanced diet.

Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking. You can add mushrooms, jalapeños, or any other vegetables that you enjoy. You can also experiment with different types of cheese, such as Swiss or cheddar, to add your own twist.

Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking for a tasty and nutritious meal, these Philly cheesesteak stuffed peppers are a winner. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and savor the flavors of a classic Philly cheesesteak in a healthier and more wholesome way.

Remember, you can always adjust the quantities and ingredients to suit your preferences. Enjoy this delicious low-carb option and share it with family and friends. Happy cooking!
