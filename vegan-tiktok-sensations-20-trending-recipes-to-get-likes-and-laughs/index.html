Vegan TikTok Sensations: 20 Trending Recipes to Get Likes and Laughs


Who doesn’t love a good vegan recipe? Not only are they animal-friendly, but they’re also healthier and packed with flavor. In recent years, vegan TikTok sensations have taken the platform by storm, sharing their innovative and exciting plant-based recipes with millions of viewers worldwide. In this article, dive into 20 trending recipes that not only gain you likes and laughs on TikTok but also contribute to a more sustainable and compassionate world.

  1. Chickpea Flour Omelette

Start your day with an eye-catching Chickpea Flour Omelette recipe. This protein-packed vegan breakfast alternative is not only delicious but also simple to make. This recipe showcases how far veganism has come in providing exciting and high-protein alternatives to meat-based food choices.

  1. Lentil Bolognese

A twist on a classic, Lentil Bolognese will quickly become a staple in your vegan recipe collection. This satisfying dish boasts a similar texture and flavor profile as traditional Bolognese but with a healthier, plant-based twist. Your followers will surely appreciate this festive plant-based twist to an Italian classic.

  1. Matcha Magic Chocolate Truffles

Make the dessert lover’s taste buds sing with these Matcha Magic Chocolate Truffles. These vegan truffles are infused with the earthy flavor of matcha and coated in rich dark chocolate. These indulgent treats are perfect for sharing, especially when paired with a warm cup of tea.

  1. Vegan Banana Split

Are you looking for an Instagram-worthy dessert? Look no further than this Vegan Banana Split recipe. This nostalgic dessert is brought to life with vegan ice cream, whipped coconut cream, and a bevy of toppings, while maintaining the same sweetness and richness as the classic version.

  1. Cauliflower Fried Rice

Expand your TikTok following with this popular Cauliflower Fried Rice recipe. This low-carb and gluten-free twist on a classic dish surprises even the most skeptical non-vegans. It provides a surplus of flavor and texture without the use of oil or soy sauce, making it the perfect economical and healthy meal.

  1. Vegan Sushi Rolls

Surprisingly simple to make, Vegan Sushi Rolls will have your followers demanding more. This avocado-based sushi recipe showcases restaurants’ mobile cooking capabilities right in their kitchen. Roll out all your sushi desires with minimal effort and mouthwatering results.

  1. Tofu Scramble

In need of an easy and tasty breakfast dish? This Tofu Scramble is just the recipe you need. This satisfying, protein-packed meal proves that one doesn’t miss out on anything––even their morning scrambled eggs.

  1. Vegan Irish Cream Liqueur

Skip the store-bought stuff and make your own Vegan Irish Cream Liqueur. Made from plant-based milk, cocoa, and liqueur of your choice, this recipe is surprisingly simple. Impress your friends at your next St. Patrick’s Day celebration or anytime you want to indulge.

  1. Vegan Loaded Nachos

Who doesn’t love Nachos? This recipe for Vegan Loaded Nachos will have your entire TikTok community coming back for more. Hearty, flavorful, and a total crowd-pleaser, this dish might just be the star of your next game day gathering.

  1. Broccoli and Cheddar Soup

Bring the warm comfort of this Broccoli and Cheddar Soup to your followers. Satisfy any craving with this cream-filled soup, all the while feeling guilt-free. By using a cashew-based cream sauce, this dish comes together quickly without sacrificing flavor or texture.

  1. Cinnamon Roll Pancakes

Achieve the best of two worlds with this Cinnamon Roll Pancake recipe. Tender, fluffy pancakes are infused with warm cinnamon and sweet icing for a breakfast that can’t be beaten. Feel the warmth of comfort food in every bite, all while sticking to your vegan lifestyle.

  1. Vegan Snickers Bar

Did you say Snickers? Add some excitement to your dessert collection with these Vegan Snickers Bars! Toss ignorance to the wind by trying this plant-based twist inspired by the classic candy bar. All your sweet cravings will be satisfied.

  1. Black Bean Brownies

Excite your followers with these Black Bean Brownies. This secret-ingredient-enriched dessert maintains its fudgy texture and rich taste while providing an extra boost of fiber and protein. Revamp your no-bake dessert recipe collection with this genius vegan recipe.

  1. Vegan Macarons

Refine your baking skills and elevate your snacks game with these Vegan Macarons. Gently colored and customized, these macarons are worth a thousand likes and laughs, with flavors ranging from pistachio to lavender.

  1. Vegan Ice Cream Sandwiches

Incorporate this timeless dessert into your vegan repertoire with Vegan Ice Cream Sandwiches. Perfect for summer gatherings, this simple recipe combines two beloved vegan desserts: cookies and ice cream—resulting in a fun, mouthwatering creation.

  1. Vegan Spinach and Artichoke Dip

Unite your household around the TV with Vegan Spinach and Artichoke Dip. A savory plant-based dip ideal for any sporty gathering. The flavor combination is as close to nature as it gets, packing a massive punch of tangy, cheesy goodness.

  1. Vegan Chocolate Almond Milk

Who knew that almond milk could be taken up a notch in this Vegan Chocolate Almond Milk recipe? Wake up your tastebuds with this deceptively simple recipe. Perfect for the morning or an afternoon pick-me-up, your followers will never go back to store-bought stuff again.

  1. Vegan Lemon Bars

Brighten your day with Vegan Lemon Bars. Impress your friends with the perfect balance of sweet and tangy, with a homemade lemon curd that will leave them grinning from ear to ear.

  1. Vegan Sausage Rolls

Bring back pub memories with these Vegan Sausage Rolls. With golden-baked, flaky pastry and a flavorful sausage filling, they will make any foodie’s heart sing.

  1. Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Spread

This Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Spread stores well and comes together with effortless whizzing in your blender or food processor. Spread this on toast, fill crepes, or even drizzle over ice cream.

There you have it–20 vegan TikTok sensation recipes your followers will find irresistible. From savory dishes to delectable desserts, you’ll effortlessly win over your audience and their taste buds. Ready? It’s time to cook and create some TikTok inspiration. Happy posting!
