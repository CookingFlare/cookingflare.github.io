Homemade Nut-Free Hummus for a Healthy and Flavorful Dip


If you’re looking for a nutritious and delicious dip that’s free from nuts, then this homemade nut-free hummus recipe is just what you need. Hummus is a versatile spread made from chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, and various seasonings. It’s not only a healthy snack option, but it’s also a great addition to sandwiches, wraps, and as a dip for fresh vegetables. This nut-free version of hummus is perfect for those with nut allergies or anyone looking for a flavorful, plant-based dip. Let’s dive into the recipe and explore how to make this delightful and healthy homemade nut-free hummus.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3 tablespoons of tahini
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-4 tablespoons of water
  • Optional garnish: a drizzle of olive oil, a sprinkle of paprika, and fresh herbs

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chickpeas: Start by draining and rinsing the chickpeas thoroughly. This helps to remove excess sodium and gives the hummus a smoother texture.

  2. Combine Ingredients: In a food processor, add the chickpeas, tahini, minced garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, ground cumin, paprika, and a pinch of salt.

  3. Blend: Process the ingredients until they form a thick and creamy paste. You may need to scrape down the sides of the food processor to ensure all the ingredients are well combined.

  4. Adjust Consistency: If the hummus seems too thick, add 2-4 tablespoons of water, one at a time, until you reach your desired consistency.

  5. Taste and Season: Taste the hummus and adjust the seasoning to your preference. You can add more salt, lemon juice, or cumin as needed.

  6. Serve: Transfer the nut-free hummus to a serving bowl. To garnish, drizzle a little olive oil over the top, sprinkle with paprika, and add some fresh herbs for a pop of color and flavor.

  7. Enjoy: Your homemade nut-free hummus is ready to be enjoyed! Serve it with a variety of dippable options such as carrot sticks, cucumber slices, bell pepper strips, or pita bread.

Benefits of Nut-Free Hummus:

  • Allergy-Friendly: This nut-free hummus is perfect for individuals with nut allergies or those who prefer to avoid nuts in their diet.
  • High in Protein: Chickpeas are a great source of plant-based protein, making this hummus an excellent snack for vegetarians and vegans.
  • Heart-Healthy: Olive oil, a key ingredient in hummus, is known for its heart-healthy benefits, and when combined with chickpeas, it makes for a nutritious dip.

Conclusion:

Homemade nut-free hummus is a fantastic addition to any healthy eating plan. It’s easy to make, versatile, and packed with nutritious ingredients. Whether you’re hosting a party, packing a lunch, or simply looking for a wholesome snack, this nut-free hummus is sure to please. Feel free to experiment with additional seasonings and garnishes to suit your taste preferences. Give this recipe a try and enjoy the creamy, flavorful goodness of homemade nut-free hummus!
