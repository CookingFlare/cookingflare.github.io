Delicious Thanksgiving Sweet Potato Casserole: A Crowd Favorite


When it comes to Thanksgiving, there are certain dishes that are simply non-negotiable. One of these is the classic sweet potato casserole. This mouthwatering dish has been a staple on Thanksgiving tables for generations, and for good reason. It perfectly balances the sweetness of the potatoes with a delicious, crunchy topping, making it a crowd-pleaser every time. If you’re looking to add a new twist to your Thanksgiving menu, or if you simply want to perfect this beloved dish, look no further. This recipe for Delicious Thanksgiving Sweet Potato Casserole is sure to become a new favorite at your holiday table.

Ingredients

For the Sweet Potato Base:

  • 5 large sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Pecan Streusel Topping:

  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Sweet Potatoes

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Wash the sweet potatoes thoroughly and prick them with a fork.
  • Place the sweet potatoes on a baking sheet and bake for 45-60 minutes, or until they are tender.
  • Once cooked, allow the sweet potatoes to cool, then peel off the skins and place the flesh in a large mixing bowl.

Step 2: Making the Sweet Potato Base

  • Mash the sweet potatoes until they are smooth and free of lumps.
  • Add the melted butter, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, milk, and salt to the bowl with the sweet potatoes.
  • Mix all the ingredients together until well combined.

Step 3: Assembling the Casserole

  • Transfer the sweet potato mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish and spread it out evenly.

Step 4: Creating the Pecan Streusel Topping

  • In a separate bowl, combine the chopped pecans, flour, brown sugar, and melted butter.
  • Mix until the ingredients come together and form a crumbly texture.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

  • Sprinkle the pecan streusel topping over the sweet potato mixture in an even layer.

Step 6: Baking the Casserole

  • Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the topping is golden and the sweet potato base is set.

Step 7: Serving

  • Allow the casserole to cool for a few minutes before serving.
  • This sweet potato casserole is best served warm and makes a delightful addition to any Thanksgiving feast.

Tips for Success

  • For a smoother sweet potato base, use a hand mixer or stand mixer to combine the ingredients.
  • If you prefer a sweeter casserole, you can adjust the amount of brown sugar to taste.
  • Make sure to keep an eye on the pecan streusel topping while baking to prevent it from burning.

Conclusion

Delicious Thanksgiving Sweet Potato Casserole is a classic dish that never fails to impress. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is a must-try for the holiday season. The creamy sweet potato base combined with the crunchy, nutty topping is a match made in Thanksgiving heaven. So, why not add a new twist to your Thanksgiving table this year with this crowd-favorite sweet potato casserole? It’s sure to become a beloved tradition for years to come. Happy cooking, and happy Thanksgiving!
