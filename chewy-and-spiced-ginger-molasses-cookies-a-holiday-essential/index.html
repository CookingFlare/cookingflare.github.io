Chewy and Spiced Ginger Molasses Cookies: A Holiday Essential

3 min · 496 words

The holiday season is a time for cozy gatherings, festive decorations, and, of course, delightful treats. One classic holiday essential that never fails to bring warmth and joy to the table is the chewy and spiced ginger molasses cookies. These cookies are a delightful combination of rich molasses, warm spices, and a chewy texture that makes them an absolute crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re hosting a holiday cookie exchange or simply looking to add a touch of nostalgia to your seasonal baking, these ginger molasses cookies are a must-try.

Ingredients

To create these delectable cookies, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar (for rolling)

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Dough

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, ground ginger, baking soda, ground cinnamon, ground cloves, and salt. Set this dry ingredient mixture aside.

  2. In a separate large bowl, using an electric mixer, beat the softened butter and 1 cup of granulated sugar until the mixture becomes light and fluffy.

  3. Add the egg and molasses to the butter-sugar mixture, and continue beating until well combined.

  4. Gradually stir in the dry ingredient mixture until a smooth dough forms.

Step 2: Shaping the Cookies

  1. Cover the dough and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow it to firm up.

  2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  3. Using a cookie scoop or a spoon, form the chilled dough into small balls, each about 1 inch in diameter.

  4. Roll the dough balls in the remaining 1/3 cup of granulated sugar to coat them evenly.

  5. Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each for spreading.

Step 3: Baking the Cookies

  1. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are set but the centers are still soft.

  2. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving and Storing

These chewy and spiced ginger molasses cookies are best enjoyed with a glass of milk, a cup of hot cocoa, or a festive mug of spiced tea. Once cooled, store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to one week. They can also be frozen for longer storage, and will thaw beautifully when brought back to room temperature.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the aroma of ginger and molasses wafting through the house as these cookies bake is a true hallmark of the holiday season. Whether you’re sharing them with loved ones or savoring them with a quiet moment to yourself, these chewy and spiced ginger molasses cookies are sure to infuse your holidays with a comforting and nostalgic spirit. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the magic of these holiday essentials unfold in your kitchen. Happy baking, and happy holidays!
