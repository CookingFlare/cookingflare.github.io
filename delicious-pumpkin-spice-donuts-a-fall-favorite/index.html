Delicious Pumpkin Spice Donuts: A Fall Favorite


Fall is here, and what better way to embrace the season than with a batch of delicious pumpkin spice donuts? These delightful treats are the perfect combination of warm spices, rich pumpkin flavor, and a soft, fluffy texture. Whether you’re enjoying them with a cup of hot coffee on a crisp morning or serving them at a cozy gathering, these pumpkin spice donuts are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup pumpkin puree
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a donut pan.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, pumpkin pie spice, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In another bowl, combine the granulated sugar, brown sugar, pumpkin puree, eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Whisk until well combined and smooth.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense donuts.

  5. Spoon the batter into a piping bag or a zip-top bag with one corner snipped off. Pipe the batter evenly into the prepared donut pan, filling each cavity about 2/3 full.

  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into a donut comes out clean.

  7. Remove the donuts from the oven and let them cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Once the donuts are completely cooled, you can enjoy them as is or add a delicious glaze or dusting of powdered sugar for extra sweetness. Feel free to get creative with toppings like cinnamon sugar, cream cheese frosting, or even a drizzle of caramel.

  9. Serve the pumpkin spice donuts with a hot cup of coffee or apple cider, and savor the flavors of fall.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have pumpkin pie spice on hand, you can make your own by combining 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger, 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg, and a pinch of ground cloves.

  • For a healthier twist, you can replace some or all of the all-purpose flour with whole wheat flour or a gluten-free flour blend.

  • Add some texture and flavor by tossing the baked donuts in a mixture of cinnamon and sugar while they’re still warm.

  • If you don’t have a donut pan, you can use a muffin tin instead. Simply shape small balls of dough and place them in the muffin cups. Adjust the baking time accordingly.

  • These pumpkin spice donuts can also be frozen for later enjoyment. Just make sure to cool them completely before storing them in an airtight container or freezer bag.

Conclusion

Delicious pumpkin spice donuts are a fall favorite for a reason. They capture the essence of the season with their warm, comforting flavors and soft, pillowy texture. Whether you’re a fan of pumpkin spice everything or just looking for a delightful treat to enjoy during the fall months, these donuts are sure to satisfy. So grab your apron, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in this autumnal delight. Happy baking!
