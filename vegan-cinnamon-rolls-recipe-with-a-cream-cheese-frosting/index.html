Vegan Cinnamon Rolls Recipe with a Cream Cheese Frosting


Welcome to my vegan kitchen! Today, I’m going to share with you a mouthwatering recipe for vegan cinnamon rolls with a luscious cream cheese frosting. These sweet treats are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or any time you’re craving a deliciously indulgent dessert. The best part? They’re entirely plant-based, so you can enjoy them guilt-free!

Ingredients

For the cinnamon rolls:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 3 tbsp vegan butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the filling:

  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 1/2 cup vegan cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well.

  2. Heat the almond milk in a small saucepan over low heat until warm. Pour the warm milk, melted vegan butter, and vanilla extract into the dry ingredients. Stir until a sticky dough forms.

  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Allow it to rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  4. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and transfer it to a lightly floured surface. Roll it out into a rectangular shape, about 1/4 inch thick.

  5. Spread the softened vegan butter evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the brown sugar and cinnamon on top of the butter.

  6. Starting from one of the long sides, tightly roll the dough into a log shape. Use a sharp knife to cut the log into 12 equal-sized rolls.

  7. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each one. Cover the dish with a kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for an additional 30 minutes.

  8. Meanwhile, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  9. Once the rolls have risen, bake them in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

  10. While the rolls are baking, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a mixing bowl, beat the vegan cream cheese, softened vegan butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  11. Once the cinnamon rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the cream cheese frosting over the warm rolls.

  12. Serve the vegan cinnamon rolls fresh and enjoy every delicious bite!

Conclusion

These vegan cinnamon rolls with cream cheese frosting are the epitome of indulgence. The soft and fluffy rolls, infused with sweet cinnamon and slathered with creamy frosting, will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking for a healthier alternative to traditional cinnamon rolls, this recipe is a must-try. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a guilt-free treat that will satisfy your sweet tooth. Enjoy!
