Grilled Flatbreads: Creative Toppings for BBQ Pizza Nights


When it comes to summer nights, there’s nothing quite like firing up the grill and enjoying a delicious meal outdoors. One of the best ways to elevate your BBQ game is by making grilled flatbread pizzas with a variety of creative toppings. Not only are they easy to prepare, but they also offer a fun and customizable dining experience for family and friends. In this blog post, we’ll explore some exciting grilled flatbread pizza topping ideas that will take your BBQ nights to the next level.

The Perfect Grilled Flatbread

Before we dive into the toppings, let’s start with the foundation of our grilled flatbread pizzas. You can either make your own flatbread dough or pick up pre-made flatbread from the store for convenience. If you’re feeling adventurous, try experimenting with different types of flatbread such as whole wheat, garlic-herb, or even gluten-free options to cater to everyone’s dietary preferences.

To prepare the flatbread for grilling, brush it with a light coating of olive oil and sprinkle a pinch of sea salt for added flavor. This will help create a crispy and delicious base for your pizza toppings.

Creative Toppings

1. Mediterranean Delight

  • Start with a layer of hummus as the base.
  • Add sliced cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, red onion, and crumbled feta cheese.
  • Finish with a drizzle of balsamic glaze and a sprinkle of fresh basil.

2. BBQ Chicken Fiesta

  • Spread a layer of tangy BBQ sauce on the flatbread.
  • Top with shredded grilled chicken, red onions, bell peppers, and a blend of mozzarella and cheddar cheese.
  • After grilling, garnish with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime for a burst of flavor.

3. Veggie Lovers’ Dream

  • Brush the flatbread with garlic-infused olive oil.
  • Arrange a colorful mix of thinly sliced zucchini, bell peppers, mushrooms, and red onion.
  • Sprinkle with Italian seasoning and a generous amount of shredded Parmesan cheese before grilling.

4. Smoky Pesto Delight

  • Smear a generous amount of basil pesto on the flatbread.
  • Layer on sun-dried tomatoes, artichoke hearts, roasted garlic, and fresh mozzarella.
  • Once grilled, finish with a sprinkle of pine nuts for added texture.

Grilling Tips

To achieve the perfect grilled flatbread pizza, it’s essential to keep a few things in mind. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates to prevent sticking. Place the prepared flatbread directly on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the crust is crispy and the toppings are heated through. Keep a close eye on the flatbread to avoid burning, and consider using a pizza stone for even cooking.

Final Thoughts

Grilled flatbread pizzas with creative toppings are a fantastic way to bring a gourmet touch to your BBQ gatherings. Whether you’re catering to meat lovers, vegetarians, or anyone in between, the customizable nature of these pizzas ensures there’s something for everyone to enjoy. The next time you’re planning a BBQ night, consider adding grilled flatbread pizzas to the menu for a memorable and delicious experience.

We hope these topping ideas inspire you to get creative with your grilled flatbread pizzas, and we can’t wait to hear about your favorite combinations. Happy grilling!
