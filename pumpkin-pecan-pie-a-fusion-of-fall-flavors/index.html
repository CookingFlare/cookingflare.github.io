Pumpkin Pecan Pie: A Fusion of Fall Flavors


Indulge in the cozy flavors of fall with this delectable Pumpkin Pecan Pie recipe. The warm spices of pumpkin pie meet the rich, nutty goodness of pecan pie, creating a fusion of flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply craving a sweet treat, this pie is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s dive into this delightful dessert.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 4-6 tablespoons ice water

For the filling:

  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon cloves
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • ¾ cup heavy cream

For the pecan topping:

  • 1 cup pecans, roughly chopped
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together. Form the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 3: Roll out the Crust

On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Carefully transfer the dough to the dish, gently pressing it into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges as desired.

Step 4: Prepare the Filling

In a medium mixing bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, cloves, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Finally, stir in the heavy cream until the filling is smooth and creamy.

Step 5: Pour the Filling into the Crust

Pour the pumpkin filling into the prepared crust, spreading it evenly.

Step 6: Prepare the Pecan Topping

In a small bowl, mix together the chopped pecans, brown sugar, and melted butter until the pecans are coated.

Step 7: Add the Pecan Topping to the Pie

Sprinkle the pecan topping evenly over the pumpkin filling, covering the entire surface.

Step 8: Bake the Pie

Place the pie in the preheated oven and bake for about 45-50 minutes or until the center is set and the crust is golden brown. If the crust starts to brown too quickly, cover it with aluminum foil.

Step 9: Cool and Serve

Once baked, remove the pie from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack. This will allow the filling to set. Serve the Pumpkin Pecan Pie at room temperature or chilled, and top it with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra touch of indulgence.

Enjoy the perfect combination of pumpkin and pecan flavors in every bite of this heavenly pie. The velvety smoothness of the pumpkin filling combined with the crunchy, caramelized pecan topping is pure bliss. With its warm spices and rich textures, this Pumpkin Pecan Pie is the epitome of fall comfort food. So, gather your loved ones, savor the aroma of autumn, and delight in this delicious fusion of flavors. Happy baking!
