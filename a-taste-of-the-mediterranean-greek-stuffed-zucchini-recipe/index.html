A Taste of the Mediterranean: Greek Stuffed Zucchini Recipe


When it comes to Mediterranean cuisine, the flavors and ingredients used create a culinary experience that is both vibrant and wholesome. One dish that perfectly captures the essence of Mediterranean cooking is the Greek stuffed zucchini. This delightful recipe combines fresh zucchinis, savory ground meat, aromatic herbs, and tangy feta cheese to create a dish that is as delicious as it is satisfying. Whether you’re looking to impress your dinner guests or simply indulge in a taste of the Mediterranean at home, this Greek stuffed zucchini recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Stuffed Zucchini:

  • 6 medium-sized zucchinis
  • 1 pound ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Zucchinis:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cut the tops off the zucchinis and carefully scoop out the flesh, leaving about 1/4 inch of flesh to create a shell. Chop the scooped-out zucchini flesh and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened.
    • Add the ground meat to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
    • Stir in the chopped zucchini flesh and cook for a few minutes until softened.
    • Remove from heat and stir in the cooked rice, chopped parsley, chopped dill, crumbled feta cheese, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Stuff the Zucchinis:

    • Fill the hollowed-out zucchinis with the prepared filling, mounding it on top. Place the stuffed zucchinis in a baking dish.

  4. Prepare the Tomato Sauce:

    • In a separate saucepan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened.
    • Stir in the diced tomatoes and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer the sauce for 5-7 minutes.

  5. Bake the Stuffed Zucchinis:

    • Pour the prepared tomato sauce over the stuffed zucchinis in the baking dish. Cover the dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the zucchinis are tender and the filling is cooked through, remove them from the oven. Serve the Greek stuffed zucchinis hot, garnished with additional chopped parsley and a sprinkle of crumbled feta cheese.

A Taste of the Mediterranean at Home

This Greek stuffed zucchini recipe is a celebration of the vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients that are characteristic of Mediterranean cuisine. The combination of tender zucchinis, savory meat, fragrant herbs, and tangy feta cheese creates a dish that is both comforting and exotic. Whether enjoyed as a main course or as part of a Mediterranean-inspired feast, these stuffed zucchinis are a delicious way to bring a taste of the Mediterranean into your home.

So, the next time you’re craving a culinary adventure, consider trying your hand at this Greek stuffed zucchini recipe. With its rich flavors and satisfying appeal, it’s a dish that is sure to transport you to the sun-kissed shores of the Mediterranean with every delightful bite.
