Irresistible Salted Caramel Brownies: A Sweet and Salty Treat


If you’re a fan of the delightful combination of sweet and salty flavors, then these irresistible salted caramel brownies are bound to become your new favorite treat. With a rich, fudgy brownie base and a decadent layer of gooey salted caramel, these brownies are the perfect indulgence for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a gathering with friends or simply treating yourself to a cozy night in, these brownies are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Brownie Base:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Salted Caramel Layer:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Brownie Base

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in 30-second intervals until fully melted.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and granulated sugar. Mix well.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the butter mixture, and beat until well combined.
  5. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until the batter is smooth.
  7. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.

Step 2: Make the Salted Caramel

  1. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, heat the granulated sugar over medium heat. Stir constantly with a high-heat resistant rubber spatula or wooden spoon.
  2. As the sugar melts, it will clump, but keep stirring. Be careful not to burn the sugar.
  3. Once the sugar has completely melted and turned amber in color, add the butter. The mixture will bubble rapidly.
  4. Stir the butter into the caramel until it is completely melted, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Slowly drizzle in the heavy cream while stirring constantly. Allow the mixture to boil for 1 minute.
  6. Remove the caramel from the heat and stir in the sea salt.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake

  1. Pour the salted caramel over the brownie batter in the pan, spreading it into an even layer.
  2. Use a knife to swirl the caramel into the brownie batter, creating a marbled effect.
  3. Bake the brownies in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  4. Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

These irresistible salted caramel brownies are best enjoyed with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and an extra drizzle of salted caramel sauce. They also pair beautifully with a hot cup of coffee or a glass of cold milk.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, these brownies are a fantastic way to elevate your dessert game. The combination of rich chocolate, luscious caramel, and a touch of salt creates a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every delectable bite of these irresistible salted caramel brownies.
