Vegan Rice Krispy Treats Recipe with a Marshmallow and Coconut Milk


If you’re craving a sweet and crunchy treat that’s vegan-friendly, look no further than these delightful Vegan Rice Krispy Treats with a twist of marshmallow and coconut milk. This recipe puts a plant-based spin on the classic Rice Krispy Treats, offering a delightful combination of chewy, gooey, and crispy textures that will satisfy your sweet tooth without any animal products. Plus, the addition of coconut milk adds a subtle tropical flavor that takes these treats to the next level. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to indulge in a guilt-free delight!

Ingredients:

  • 5 cups of vegan-friendly crispy rice cereal
  • 1 package (10 ounces) of vegan marshmallows
  • 1/4 cup of coconut milk
  • 3 tablespoons of vegan butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pan: Start by greasing a 9x13-inch baking pan with a light coating of coconut oil or vegan butter, ensuring that the entire surface is well-coated to prevent the treats from sticking.

  2. Melt the Marshmallows: In a large saucepan, melt the vegan butter over low heat. Once the butter has melted, add the vegan marshmallows and stir continuously until they have completely melted and formed a smooth, gooey mixture. This process may take a few minutes, so be patient and keep stirring to prevent the marshmallows from burning.

  3. Add Coconut Milk and Vanilla: Once the marshmallows have melted, pour in the coconut milk and vanilla extract, stirring gently to combine all the ingredients. The coconut milk will add a creamy texture and a hint of tropical flavor to the mixture, enhancing the overall taste of the treats.

  4. Incorporate the Crispy Rice Cereal: Remove the saucepan from the heat and quickly add the crispy rice cereal to the marshmallow mixture. Using a spatula, gently fold the cereal into the marshmallow mixture until it is evenly coated and well combined. Work swiftly to ensure that the cereal is coated before the marshmallow mixture begins to set.

  5. Press Into the Pan: Transfer the mixture into the prepared baking pan and, using the spatula or a piece of parchment paper, press it down firmly and evenly to create a smooth, compact layer of treats. This step is crucial for ensuring that the treats hold together well once they have set.

  6. Allow to Set: Let the treats cool and set at room temperature for at least 30 minutes. Once they have firmed up, you can cut them into squares or rectangles of your desired size using a sharp knife.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Your Vegan Rice Krispy Treats with Marshmallow and Coconut Milk are now ready to be served and enjoyed! These delectable treats are perfect for snacking, dessert, or even as a sweet addition to a lunchbox or picnic spread.

Final Thoughts:

These Vegan Rice Krispy Treats with Marshmallow and Coconut Milk are a delightful twist on a beloved classic, offering a cruelty-free and delicious option for those who follow a vegan lifestyle or simply enjoy plant-based treats. The addition of coconut milk brings a subtle richness and tropical flavor to the treats, elevating them to an entirely new level of indulgence. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, preparing a special treat for your family, or simply craving a sweet pick-me-up, these vegan-friendly treats are sure to be a hit. Try out this recipe and share the joy of these delightful treats with your loved ones!
