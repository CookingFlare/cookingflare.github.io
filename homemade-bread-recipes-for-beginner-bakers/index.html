Homemade Bread Recipes for Beginner Bakers


Are you a beginner baker looking to try your hand at making homemade bread? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share three delicious and foolproof bread recipes that are perfect for beginners. From classic white bread to flavorful herb bread, these recipes will have you baking bread like a pro in no time. So put on your apron, dust off your rolling pin, and let’s get started!

1. Classic White Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Gradually add the warm water and vegetable oil to the dry ingredients. Mix well until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Punch down the dough to release any air bubbles, then shape it into a loaf and place it in a greased loaf pan.
  6. Cover the pan with the kitchen towel again and let the dough rise for another 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  8. Bake the bread for 25-30 minutes, or until it turns golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  9. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack before slicing.

2. Cheesy Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of French bread
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the French bread horizontally, without cutting all the way through.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, and chopped parsley. Mix well.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly between the slices of bread.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the bread.
  6. Wrap the bread in aluminum foil and place it on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.

3. Rosemary Olive Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1/2 cup pitted and chopped Kalamata olives

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Gradually add the warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Mix well until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Punch down the dough to release any air bubbles, then knead in the chopped rosemary and olives.
  6. Shape the dough into a round loaf and place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Cover the loaf with the kitchen towel again and let it rise for another 30 minutes.
  8. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  9. Bake the bread for 25-30 minutes, or until it turns golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  10. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack before serving.

There you have it - three easy and delicious homemade bread recipes for beginner bakers. Whether you prefer a classic white bread, a cheesy garlic bread, or a flavorful rosemary olive bread, these recipes will help you master the art of bread baking in no time. So roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and enjoy the wonderful aroma of freshly baked bread filling your kitchen. Happy baking!
