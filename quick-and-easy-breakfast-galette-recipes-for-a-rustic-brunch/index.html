Quick and Easy Breakfast Galette Recipes for a Rustic Brunch


Are you looking for a delicious and rustic brunch idea? Look no further than these quick and easy breakfast galette recipes! Galettes are a versatile dish that can be enjoyed any time of the day, but they are especially perfect for a leisurely weekend brunch. With their flaky crust and flavorful fillings, these galettes are sure to impress your family and friends. So roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s get cooking!

Savory Mushroom and Spinach Galette

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded Gruyere cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their liquid and start to brown, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the spinach and garlic to the skillet and cook until the spinach wilts, about 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Roll out the pie crust on a lightly floured surface. Transfer it to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Spread the mushroom and spinach mixture evenly over the pie crust, leaving a border around the edges.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded Gruyere cheese over the filling.
  7. Fold the edges of the pie crust over the filling, pleating as necessary.
  8. Brush the edges of the galette with the beaten egg to give it a golden brown color.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and crispy.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.

Sweet Berry and Cream Cheese Galette

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  3. In a separate bowl, toss the berries with lemon juice and cornstarch until well coated.
  4. Roll out the pie crust on a lightly floured surface. Transfer it to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly over the pie crust, leaving a border around the edges.
  6. Arrange the berries on top of the cream cheese mixture.
  7. Fold the edges of the pie crust over the filling, pleating as necessary.
  8. Brush the edges of the galette with the beaten egg to give it a golden brown color.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the berries are bubbling.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before dusting with powdered sugar.

Get Creative with Fillings!

One of the great things about galettes is that you can get creative with the fillings. Don’t be afraid to experiment and try new combinations. Here are some ideas to get you started:

  • Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Galette: Cook bacon until crispy, scramble eggs, and sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese.
  • Apple and Cinnamon Galette: Thinly slice apples, toss with cinnamon and sugar, and sprinkle with chopped walnuts.
  • Tomato, Basil, and Mozzarella Galette: Layer sliced tomatoes, fresh basil leaves, and mozzarella cheese for a savory and refreshing galette.

No matter which filling you choose, the key is to have fun and enjoy the process. Galettes are meant to be rustic and imperfect, so embrace their charming imperfections. Serve these delightful galettes at your next brunch gathering, and watch as your guests are blown away by your culinary prowess. Bon appétit!
