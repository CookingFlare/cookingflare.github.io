Instant Pot Lemon Butter Scallops: Succulent and Buttery


When it comes to elegant seafood dishes that are both quick and easy to prepare, Instant Pot Lemon Butter Scallops stand out as a top choice. This recipe delivers perfectly cooked scallops bathed in a luscious lemon butter sauce, making it ideal for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. The Instant Pot not only speeds up the cooking process but also ensures that the scallops remain tender and juicy. Let’s dive into this delightful recipe and discover how to make these succulent and buttery scallops in your Instant Pot.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 lb large sea scallops: Fresh or frozen (thawed) scallops work best.
  • 2 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing.
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter: Divided.
  • 3 cloves garlic: Minced.
  • Juice of 1 lemon: Freshly squeezed.
  • Zest of 1 lemon: Adds a bright, citrusy flavor.
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine: Optional, for deglazing the pot.
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth: Low sodium preferred.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley: Chopped, for garnish.
  • Lemon wedges: For serving.

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Scallops

Start by preparing your scallops. If you’re using frozen scallops, make sure they are fully thawed and then pat them dry with paper towels. This step is crucial as it ensures the scallops will sear properly and develop a beautiful golden crust.

Step 2: Sauté the Scallops

Set your Instant Pot to the sauté mode and add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, place the scallops in the pot in a single layer. You may need to do this in batches to avoid overcrowding. Season the scallops with salt and pepper and sear each side for about 2-3 minutes until they develop a nice golden-brown crust. Remove the scallops from the pot and set them aside on a plate.

Step 3: Prepare the Lemon Butter Sauce

In the same pot, add 2 tablespoons of the unsalted butter. Once melted, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. If you’re using white wine, pour it in now to deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Allow the wine to reduce by half, which should take about 2 minutes. Then, add the chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest, stirring to combine all the flavors.

Step 4: Pressure Cook

Return the scallops to the pot, nestling them into the sauce. Secure the lid on your Instant Pot and ensure the vent is set to sealing. Select the manual or pressure cook mode and set the timer for 1 minute. Due to the nature of scallops, they cook very quickly, and this short cooking time is sufficient.

Step 5: Quick Release

Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick release to let out the steam. Carefully open the lid and check the scallops for doneness. They should be opaque and slightly firm to the touch.

Step 6: Finish the Sauce

Remove the scallops from the pot and place them on a serving platter. Switch the Instant Pot back to sauté mode. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter to the sauce and stir until melted and well combined. This will thicken the sauce slightly and give it a rich, buttery texture.

Step 7: Serve

Pour the lemon butter sauce over the scallops and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Serve immediately with lemon wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrus flavor. This dish pairs wonderfully with a side of steamed vegetables, rice, or a fresh green salad.

Tips for Perfect Lemon Butter Scallops

  1. Pat Dry the Scallops: Ensuring the scallops are dry before searing is key to getting that perfect golden crust.
  2. Don’t Overcrowd the Pot: Sear the scallops in batches if necessary to avoid steaming them instead of searing.
  3. Use Fresh Lemon: Freshly squeezed lemon juice and zest provide a much brighter and more vibrant flavor than bottled lemon juice.
  4. Wine Option: While optional, using white wine for deglazing adds depth to the sauce. Choose a dry white wine like Sauvignon Blanc or Pinot Grigio for the best results.
  5. Quick Cooking Time: Scallops cook very quickly, so be mindful of the short cooking time to avoid overcooking and ending up with rubbery scallops.

Why Use an Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot is a versatile kitchen appliance that simplifies the cooking process while delivering consistent results. Here are some reasons why using an Instant Pot is advantageous for this scallop recipe:

  • Speed: The Instant Pot significantly reduces the cooking time, allowing you to prepare a gourmet meal in minutes.
  • Flavor: The pressure cooking method helps infuse the scallops with the rich flavors of the lemon butter sauce.
  • Convenience: With the sauté function, you can sear the scallops and make the sauce all in one pot, minimizing cleanup.

Variations

This lemon butter scallop recipe is highly adaptable. Here are some variations to suit different tastes and dietary preferences:

  • Herb Infusion: Add herbs like thyme or rosemary to the sauce for an aromatic twist.
  • Spicy Kick: Incorporate a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce for a spicy version.
  • Creamy Sauce: Stir in a splash of heavy cream at the end for a richer, creamier sauce.
  • Vegetarian Option: Substitute the scallops with large mushrooms or tofu slices for a vegetarian-friendly dish.
  • Gluten-Free: Ensure that all ingredients, including the chicken broth and wine, are gluten-free if you have dietary restrictions.

Pairing Suggestions

When serving Instant Pot Lemon Butter Scallops, consider these delicious side dishes and beverages to complement the meal:

  • Side Dishes: Steamed asparagus, garlic mashed potatoes, quinoa, or a crisp Caesar salad.
  • Beverages: A chilled glass of dry white wine such as Chardonnay or Sauvignon Blanc, sparkling water with a twist of lemon, or a light beer.

Storing and Reheating

If you have any leftovers, store the scallops and sauce in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. To reheat, gently warm the scallops in a skillet over medium-low heat, adding a bit of extra butter or broth if the sauce has thickened too much. Avoid microwaving, as it can make the scallops rubbery.

Conclusion

Instant Pot Lemon Butter Scallops are a delightful dish that combines the elegance of scallops with the simplicity of the Instant Pot. The result is a succulent, buttery, and citrusy delight that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking to impress guests or simply want to treat yourself to a delicious meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a gourmet seafood experience right at home. Happy cooking!
