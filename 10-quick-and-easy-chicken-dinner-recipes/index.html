10 Quick and Easy Chicken Dinner Recipes


Chicken is a versatile, delicious, and affordable protein that can easily be transformed into a variety of dishes. Whether you’re a beginner cook or a seasoned pro, these 10 quick and easy chicken dinner recipes will satisfy your hunger and delight your taste buds. From one-pan meals to flavorful stir-fries, we’ve got you covered with meals that come together in no time.

1. One-Pan Baked Lemon Garlic Chicken

This dish is a simple, yet flavorful option for a quick chicken dinner. With just a few ingredients, you can have a perfectly roasted chicken dinner ready in about 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken breasts on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Pour the lemon-garlic mixture over the chicken, ensuring it’s evenly coated.
  5. Roast for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
  6. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve with roasted vegetables or a side salad.

This one-pan meal is perfect for busy weeknights and requires minimal cleanup!

2. Chicken Stir-Fry with Veggies

If you’re looking for a quick and healthy chicken dinner, stir-fries are a go-to option. Packed with vegetables and lean chicken, this dish is both satisfying and nutritious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Directions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, honey, and rice vinegar to make the stir-fry sauce.
  2. Heat olive oil and sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken slices to the pan and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken and set it aside. In the same pan, add the vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, until they are tender but still crisp.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan and pour the sauce over the mixture. Stir to coat and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve the chicken stir-fry over a bed of steamed rice.

This chicken stir-fry is a fantastic way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet while keeping dinner quick and flavorful.

3. Chicken Alfredo Pasta

A creamy, indulgent pasta dish that’s surprisingly easy to make. This chicken alfredo is a classic that the whole family will love.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Directions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to the package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken cubes to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook until browned and cooked through, about 7-10 minutes.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in the Parmesan cheese and cook until the sauce thickens, about 3-5 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine, ensuring the pasta is well coated in the creamy sauce.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.

This creamy chicken alfredo is a comforting dinner that’s both rich and satisfying, yet incredibly easy to prepare.

4. Chicken Fajitas

For a Mexican-inspired chicken dinner that’s full of flavor, chicken fajitas are a crowd-pleaser. With tender chicken, bell peppers, and onions, these fajitas are perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fajita seasoning
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Salsa, sour cream, and guacamole for serving

Directions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes.
  3. Add the bell peppers and onions to the skillet and sprinkle with fajita seasoning. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave.
  5. Serve the chicken and vegetable mixture in the warm tortillas with your favorite toppings like salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.

Chicken fajitas are a great option for an easy, flavorful dinner, and they’re fun to assemble with all the tasty toppings.

5. Sheet Pan Chicken and Vegetables

If you love minimal prep and cleanup, this sheet pan chicken and vegetable recipe is perfect. With just one pan to clean, you’ll have a delicious, well-rounded meal in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 cup carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the chicken thighs, potatoes, and carrots on a large sheet pan.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss everything to coat evenly.
  4. Roast for 35-40 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy a fuss-free dinner!

This sheet pan meal is the ultimate in convenience, offering a hearty, balanced meal without the mess.

6. Chicken Caesar Salad

For a light yet satisfying dinner, this chicken Caesar salad is a quick and tasty option. The combination of crispy lettuce, grilled chicken, and creamy Caesar dressing is simply irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, grilled and sliced
  • 4 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing

Directions:

  1. Grill the chicken breasts over medium heat until fully cooked, about 6-8 minutes per side. Slice into strips.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the chopped lettuce with Caesar dressing until well coated.
  3. Top the salad with the sliced chicken, grated Parmesan, and croutons.
  4. Serve immediately.

This Caesar salad with grilled chicken is light yet filling, perfect for those who want a quick dinner without compromising on flavor.

7. Chicken and Rice Casserole

A comforting, one-pot meal that combines tender chicken with seasoned rice and vegetables. This chicken and rice casserole is an easy dinner option that’s filling and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large ovenproof skillet, cook the diced chicken over medium heat until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add the chopped onion and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until softened.
  4. Stir in the rice, chicken broth, peas and carrots, garlic powder, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then cover with a lid and transfer to the oven.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.

This casserole is a no-fuss dinner that combines chicken and rice in a hearty and comforting way.

8. Chicken Parmesan

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian dish that is both crispy and cheesy, and it can be made quickly for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, pounded thin
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix the breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese in a shallow dish.
  3. Dredge the chicken breasts in the breadcrumb mixture, coating both sides evenly.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the chicken breasts for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
  5. Transfer the chicken to a baking dish and top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Serve your chicken Parmesan with spaghetti or a side of garlic bread for a satisfying Italian-inspired dinner.

9. Grilled Chicken with Avocado Salsa

For a fresh and healthy chicken dinner, grilled chicken paired with a zesty avocado salsa is a perfect choice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Rub the chicken breasts with olive oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for 5-7 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  4. While the chicken is grilling, prepare the avocado salsa by combining the diced avocado, red onion, lime juice, and cilantro in a bowl.
  5. Serve the grilled chicken topped with the fresh avocado salsa.

This dish is light, flavorful, and perfect for a quick dinner that feels like a treat.

10. Chicken Tacos

Chicken tacos are a fast, customizable, and flavorful option for a weeknight dinner. You can use any toppings you like to create your perfect taco.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salsa and sour cream for serving

Directions:

  1. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  2. Heat the shredded chicken in a skillet over medium heat, adding a pinch of salt and pepper for seasoning.
  3. Assemble the tacos by placing some shredded chicken on each tortilla and topping with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and any other desired toppings.
  4. Serve with salsa and sour cream on the side.

These chicken tacos are quick to prepare and allow for endless flavor combinations, making them a perfect meal for any night.

Each of these quick and easy chicken dinner recipes will make mealtime stress-free while still delivering delicious results. From creamy pastas to fresh salads and hearty casseroles, there’s a recipe for every taste and occasion. Happy cooking!
