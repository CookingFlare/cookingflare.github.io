Slow Cooker Magic: Easy and Flavorful Taco and Burrito Fillings


Are you ready to elevate your taco and burrito game to a whole new level? Say goodbye to the hassle of standing over a stove for hours, because we’re about to dive into the world of slow cooker magic! With these easy and flavorful taco and burrito fillings, you’ll be able to create mouthwatering dishes with minimal effort. Let’s get started!

Slow Cooker Shredded Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix the chicken broth, chopped onion, minced garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  5. Once the chicken is tender and cooked through, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, allowing it to soak up all the flavorful juices.

Serving Suggestion:

  • Use the slow cooker shredded chicken as a filling for tacos, burritos, enchiladas, or even as a topping for salads.

Slow Cooker Barbacoa Beef

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs beef chuck roast, cut into large chunks
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the beef chunks in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, combine the apple cider vinegar, lime juice, minced garlic, chopped chipotle peppers, cumin, oregano, cloves, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the beef in the slow cooker.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-6 hours, until the beef is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

Serving Suggestion:

  • Enjoy the slow cooker barbacoa beef in tacos, burritos, rice bowls, or as a filling for quesadillas.

Slow Cooker Black Bean and Corn Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine the black beans, frozen corn, diced bell pepper, chopped red onion, minced jalapeño, and chopped cilantro.
  2. Drizzle the lime juice and olive oil over the mixture.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then gently stir to combine.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 2-3 hours, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Serving Suggestion:

  • Serve the slow cooker black bean and corn salsa as a topping for tacos, burrito bowls, or as a side dish for grilled meats.

Conclusion

There you have it – easy and flavorful taco and burrito fillings that are sure to impress your family and friends. The magic of the slow cooker allows you to achieve rich, complex flavors with minimal hands-on time. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner or simply craving a delicious homemade meal, these slow cooker recipes are the perfect solution. So, dust off your slow cooker and get ready to enjoy the convenience and deliciousness of these mouthwatering taco and burrito fillings. Happy cooking!
