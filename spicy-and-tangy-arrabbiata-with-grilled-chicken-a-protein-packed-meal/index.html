Spicy and Tangy Arrabbiata with Grilled Chicken: A Protein-Packed Meal


Arrabbiata sauce is a classic Italian pasta sauce known for its spicy kick and vibrant flavors. In this recipe, we elevate the traditional Arrabbiata by pairing it with succulent grilled chicken, creating a protein-packed meal that satisfies both taste and nutrition. This dish is perfect for those looking to add a bit of heat to their dinner repertoire while enjoying a wholesome, balanced meal.

Ingredients

For the Grilled Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Arrabbiata Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)

For Serving:

  • 1 lb spaghetti or pasta of your choice
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

Grilled Chicken:

  1. Preparation: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well. Place chicken breasts in the marinade, turning to coat evenly. Let it marinate for at least 15-20 minutes.

  3. Grill the Chicken: Grill the chicken breasts for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked (internal temperature of 165°F or 74°C). Cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts. Once cooked, remove from grill and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.

  4. Slice and Set Aside: Slice the grilled chicken breasts into thin strips or bite-sized pieces. Set aside while you prepare the Arrabbiata sauce and pasta.

Arrabbiata Sauce:

  1. Saute Garlic and Red Pepper Flakes: In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes. Saute for 1-2 minutes until garlic is fragrant and slightly golden.

  2. Add Crushed Tomatoes and Seasonings: Pour in the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine. Bring the sauce to a simmer.

  3. Simmer and Adjust Seasonings: Reduce heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly and the flavors meld together. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.

Pasta:

  1. Cook Pasta: While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain well.

  2. Combine Sauce and Pasta: Add the cooked and drained pasta to the skillet with the Arrabbiata sauce. Toss gently to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.

Assembly:

  1. Serve: Divide the Arrabbiata pasta among serving plates. Top each portion with slices of grilled chicken. Garnish with chopped fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

  2. Enjoy: Serve immediately while hot. This dish pairs well with a side salad or garlic bread for a complete meal.

Tips for Success:

  • Adjust Spice Level: If you prefer a milder sauce, reduce the amount of red pepper flakes. For extra heat, add more to suit your taste.

  • Grilling Chicken: Ensure your grill is properly preheated and clean for best results. You can also use a grill pan on the stovetop if preferred.

  • Fresh Ingredients: Using fresh garlic and herbs enhances the flavors of the dish. Don’t hesitate to add extra basil or parsley for additional freshness.

  • Storage: Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop or microwave before serving.

Why Choose Spicy Arrabbiata with Grilled Chicken?

This dish not only packs a punch in flavor but also offers a balanced meal option. Grilled chicken is lean and rich in protein, essential for muscle repair and overall health. The Arrabbiata sauce, with its tomatoes and herbs, provides vitamins and antioxidants. Together, they create a satisfying dish that fuels your body and delights your taste buds.

Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or a special occasion, Spicy Arrabbiata with Grilled Chicken is sure to impress. It combines the warmth of Italian cuisine with the heartiness of grilled chicken, making it a versatile and delicious choice for any mealtime. Try it tonight and experience the perfect blend of spice, tang, and wholesome goodness!
