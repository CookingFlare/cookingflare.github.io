Tips for Making Perfect Cookies When making cookies, keep in mind the following general guidelines:

Use room temperature ingredients. Don't overmix the dough. Chill the dough for at least 30 minutes. Scoop the cookies into equal-sized balls. Bake the cookies at the correct temperature and for the right amount of time.

Perfect Valentine's Day Cookies Recipe Here's a simple recipe for chocolate-peanut butter cookies that's perfect for Valentine's Day. Ingredients:

1 cup high-quality all-purpose flour 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened 1/2 cup pure peanut butter 1/2 cup granulated sugar 1/4 cup brown sugar 2 large eggs 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup chocolate chips Chopped peanuts (optional)

Instructions:

Sift the flour, baking powder, and salt into a separate bowl. Cream the butter and peanut butter until light and fluffy. Add the sugars and mix until combined. Beat in the eggs and vanilla extract. Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined. Stir in the chocolate chips and peanuts (if using). Scoop the dough into equal-sized balls and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake at 375°F for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
