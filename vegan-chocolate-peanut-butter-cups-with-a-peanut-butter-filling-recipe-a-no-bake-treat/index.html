Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups with a Peanut Butter Filling Recipe: A No-Bake Treat


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of chocolate and peanut butter, then you’re in for a treat. These Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups with a Peanut Butter Filling are not only delicious, but they are also incredibly easy to make. Best of all, they require no baking!

Ingredients

For the chocolate shell:

  • 1 cup of vegan chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil

For the peanut butter filling:

  • 1/2 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the vegan chocolate chips and coconut oil together. Start with 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the mixture is smooth and fully melted.

  2. Line a muffin tin with paper liners. Spoon a small amount of the melted chocolate mixture into the bottom of each liner, spreading it out to create an even layer. Place the muffin tin in the freezer for about 10 minutes to set the chocolate.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the peanut butter, coconut oil, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir until well combined and smooth.

  4. Remove the muffin tin from the freezer and spoon a small amount of the peanut butter filling onto the center of each chocolate layer. Make sure not to fill the cups to the top, as you will need to add another layer of chocolate on top.

  5. Once the peanut butter filling is in place, spoon another layer of the melted chocolate mixture on top, covering the filling completely. Use a spoon to smooth out the chocolate and ensure it reaches the edges of the liners.

  6. Return the muffin tin to the freezer and let the peanut butter cups set for at least 30 minutes, or until the chocolate is firm.

  7. Once the chocolate is fully set, remove the peanut butter cups from the muffin tin and store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

  8. Serve chilled and enjoy the perfect combination of creamy peanut butter and rich chocolate in every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • For a crunchy twist, you can add crushed peanuts on top of the peanut butter filling before adding the final layer of chocolate.
  • If you prefer a sweeter filling, you can increase the amount of maple syrup or add a touch of powdered sugar to the peanut butter mixture.
  • Experiment with different types of chocolate for the shell, such as dark chocolate or white chocolate, to create unique flavor profiles.

These Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups with a Peanut Butter Filling are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving more. They are perfect for parties, potlucks, or simply as a tasty treat to enjoy on your own. Plus, being vegan, they are suitable for those with dietary restrictions or preferences. Give this no-bake recipe a try and indulge in a guilt-free dessert that is both delicious and nutritious!
