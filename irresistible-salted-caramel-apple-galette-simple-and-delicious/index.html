Irresistible Salted Caramel Apple Galette: Simple and Delicious


If you’re in the mood for a dessert that combines the sweet and tart flavors of apples with the rich, buttery taste of caramel, then look no further than this Irresistible Salted Caramel Apple Galette. This delicious treat is not only simple to make but also a guaranteed crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

For the pastry:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 4-6 tablespoons ice water

For the filling:

  • 4 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the salted caramel sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt for the pastry. Add the cold cubed butter and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to cut it into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing gently with a fork until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix. Form the dough into a ball, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. While the dough is chilling, prepare the filling. In a separate bowl, toss together the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, salt, and lemon juice until well coated. Set aside.

  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a rough circle, about 12 inches in diameter. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  6. Arrange the apple slices in the center of the dough, leaving a 2-inch border around the edges. Fold the edges of the dough over the apples, pleating as you go, to create a rustic crust.

  7. Place the galette in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the apples are tender.

  8. While the galette is baking, prepare the salted caramel sauce. In a medium-sized saucepan, heat the granulated sugar over medium heat, stirring constantly until it melts and turns amber in color. Add the butter and whisk until melted and well combined. Slowly pour in the heavy cream, whisking continuously. Finally, stir in the sea salt until fully incorporated. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.

  9. Once the galette is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before drizzling the salted caramel sauce over the top. Serve warm or at room temperature.

The combination of the flaky pastry, tender apples, and decadent salted caramel sauce makes this Irresistible Salted Caramel Apple Galette a dessert that will impress even the most discerning taste buds. Whether you’re serving it at a dinner party or enjoying it as a sweet treat for yourself, this simple and delicious galette is sure to become a favorite. Enjoy!
