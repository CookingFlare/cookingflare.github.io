Chocolate Swirl Bread: A Decadent Dessert or Breakfast Treat


If you’re a fan of chocolate and bread, then you’re in for a real treat with this Chocolate Swirl Bread recipe. This decadent delight is perfect for indulging your sweet tooth and makes for a delicious dessert or a special breakfast treat. With a soft and fluffy bread filled with rich chocolate swirls, this recipe is sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the dry ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk until warm. Remove from heat and add the melted butter. Stir until well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and vanilla extract together. Slowly add the egg mixture to the milk and butter mixture, stirring continuously.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients. Mix until a soft dough forms.

  5. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic.

  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with a clean kitchen towel. Allow it to rise in a warm place for approximately 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and turn it out onto a floured surface. Roll it into a rectangle shape, approximately 12x16 inches.

  8. Sprinkle the chocolate chips evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  9. Starting from one of the long sides, tightly roll the dough into a log, jelly-roll style. Pinch the edges to seal.

  10. Place the rolled dough into a greased loaf pan, seam side down. Cover with a kitchen towel and let it rise for another 30 minutes.

  11. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  12. Once the dough has risen, bake it in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped.

  13. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Enjoy this Chocolate Swirl Bread on its own, or spread some butter or Nutella for an extra indulgence. It’s perfect for a cozy breakfast or as a dessert after a satisfying meal. This recipe is versatile and can be customized by adding nuts or even a touch of cinnamon to the chocolate filling. So, go ahead and satisfy your chocolate cravings with this delightful treat!

Note:

The Chocolate Swirl Bread can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. To keep it fresh for longer, you can also freeze it wrapped tightly in plastic wrap and placed in a freezer-safe bag. Simply thaw it at room temperature when ready to enjoy again.
