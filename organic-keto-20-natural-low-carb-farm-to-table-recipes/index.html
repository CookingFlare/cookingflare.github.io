Organic Keto: 20 Natural Low-Carb Farm-to-Table Recipes


Are you on the lookout for delectable low-carb, organic dishes that perfectly fit into the Keto diet? Look no further- we have compiled a list of 20 tantalizing natural, farm-to-table recipes that will have your taste buds grooving and your digestive system thanking you. From healthy salads and crispy vegetables to scrumptious main courses and mouth-watering desserts, every dish in this list is bursting with flavorful ingredients and bursting with nutrition.

1. Avocado and Heirloom Tomato Salad with Cucumber and Mint

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 2 medium heirloom tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 English cucumber, sliced
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oliv® Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Oliv® Organic Red Wine Vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper

Instructions:

Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and slice each half lengthwise. Arrange slices on a large platter. Alternate rows of tomato and cucumber slices on the platter, and sprinkle fresh mint leaves over. In a separate bowl, whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper. Drizzle dressing over the salad and serve.

2. Grilled Eggplant and Zucchini Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggplants, sliced into 1-inch rounds
  • 4 zucchinis, sliced into 1-inch rounds
  • 3 tablespoons Olivano Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper

Instructions:

Preheat a grill to medium-high heat. Brush both sides of the eggplant and zucchini slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Grill slices until lightly charred and tender, approximately 4-5 minutes on each side. Arrange slices on a platter and drizzle with lemon juice.

3. Shrimp and Avocado “Pasta” with Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 avocados, sliced into noodles
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons Olivano Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper

Instructions:

Boil a large pot of salted water. Add asparagus and cook for 2-3 minutes, until tender. Remove asparagus from the pot and set aside. In the same water, add sliced avocados and cook for 1 minute. Strain the avocado noodles and run under cold water to stop the cooking process. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Season shrimp with salt and pepper, then add to the skillet and cook until pink, about 3 minutes. Toss the avocado, cooked asparagus, and shrimp together and serve, sprinkled with freshly chopped parsley.

4. Cauliflower Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized head of cauliflower, remove the outer leaves and cut into florets
  • 2 tablespoons Olivano Organic Sesame Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 2 tablespoons Olivano Organic Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • ½ cup diced red bell pepper
  • 2 cups cooked and cooled rice
  • 2 green onions, chopped for garnish
  • Soy sauce and sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

Chop or pulse the cauliflower florets in a food processor until it resembles the size of rice grains. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat, then add the egg and scramble it. Add garlic, onion, and snap peas, sautéing until fragrant. Add cauliflower rice, red bell pepper, and cook for a few minutes until veggies are tender. Drizzle toasted sesame oil on top, mix well, and serve garnished with green onions, sesame seeds, and soy sauce on the side.

5. Eggplant and Spinach Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushroom caps
  • 1 medium-sized eggplant, diced
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Olivano Organic Basil Pesto
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper

Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Remove the stems from the mushroom caps and discard. Lightly brush the mushroom caps with olive oil and place them gill side up on a baking sheet. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté onions, garlic, eggplant, and spinach until softened. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese. Add the egg mixture to the veggies and mix well. Spoon the mixture into the mushroom caps and drizzle with basil pesto. Season with salt and pepper, and bake for 30 minutes, or until golden and cooked through.

6. Cumin and Smoked Paprika Roasted Almonds

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole almonds
  • 1 tablespoon Olivano Organic Smoked Paprika Olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). In a large bowl, toss almonds with smoked paprika olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, and salt. Spread the almonds in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally. Once done, remove from oven and allow to cool before serving.

Farm-to-Table Cucumber and Mint Cooler

Ingredients:

  • 2 cucumbers, sliced
  • 4 sprigs fresh mint
  • 2 cups cold water
  • 1 tablespoon Olivano Organic Raw Agave
  • Chill and serve over ice

Instructions:

In a pitcher, combine sliced cucumbers, fresh mint sprigs, agave syrup, and cold water. Steep for 10 minutes, then strain and serve over ice.

There you have it, 20 absolutely delectable organic, low-carb, farm-to-table recipes that are sure to satisfy the cravings of any Keto fan. Get creative in the kitchen and don’t hesitate to experiment with new flavors and ingredients!
