Zesty Lime Cilantro Shrimp: Bursting with Freshness


If you’re a seafood lover, you’re in for a treat with this zesty lime cilantro shrimp recipe. Bursting with fresh flavors and vibrant colors, this dish is perfect for a light and refreshing meal. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of the ocean, these succulent shrimp are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, red pepper flakes, lime zest, lime juice, chopped cilantro, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  2. Marinate the Shrimp: Place the peeled and deveined shrimp in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the shrimp, ensuring that each piece is coated thoroughly. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse into the shrimp.

  3. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using skewers, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning during grilling.

  4. Grill the Shrimp: Thread the marinated shrimp onto skewers or place them directly on the grill. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn pink and opaque with a slight char on the edges. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp to maintain their succulent texture.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the shrimp are grilled to perfection, remove them from the heat and transfer them to a serving platter. Garnish with additional fresh cilantro and lime wedges for an extra burst of flavor. Serve the zesty lime cilantro shrimp hot, accompanied by your favorite side dishes or atop a bed of crisp salad greens.
