Delicious Summer Picnic Dessert Bar Recipes for Everyone


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy the great outdoors, and what better way to do that than with a delightful picnic? Whether you’re planning a family outing, a romantic date, or a fun gathering with friends, a picnic dessert bar is a fantastic addition to your spread. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering and easy-to-make dessert bar recipes that are perfect for a summer picnic. From fruity delights to decadent treats, there’s something for everyone to enjoy!

Lemon Blueberry Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x9 inch baking pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine 1 cup of flour, softened butter, and powdered sugar. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. Bake the crust for 20 minutes in the preheated oven.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, granulated sugar, 2 tablespoons of flour, baking powder, salt, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
  5. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries.
  6. Pour the mixture over the baked crust.
  7. Bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
  8. Allow the bars to cool before cutting into squares and serving.

These lemon blueberry bars are a delightful combination of tangy lemon and sweet blueberries, making them a perfect addition to any summer picnic dessert bar.

Salted Caramel Pretzel Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pretzel crumbs
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup caramel sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon flaky sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the pretzel crumbs, melted butter, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes, then allow it to cool slightly.
  5. Pour the caramel sauce over the pretzel crust and spread it evenly.
  6. Sprinkle the flaky sea salt over the caramel layer.
  7. Chill the bars in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before cutting into squares and serving.

The combination of crunchy pretzels, rich caramel, and a touch of sea salt makes these bars a crowd-pleaser at any summer picnic.

Tropical Fruit Oat Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups mixed tropical fruits (such as mango, pineapple, and papaya), diced
  • 1/2 cup sweetened shredded coconut

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the oats, flour, brown sugar, and baking soda.
  3. Stir in the melted butter until the mixture is crumbly.
  4. Press half of the oat mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  5. Spread the mixed tropical fruits over the crust, then sprinkle the shredded coconut on top.
  6. Crumble the remaining oat mixture over the fruit and coconut layer.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
  8. Allow the bars to cool before cutting into bars and serving.

These tropical fruit oat bars are a delightful and wholesome addition to any summer picnic dessert bar, offering a taste of the tropics in every bite.

Conclusion

These delicious dessert bar recipes are sure to elevate your summer picnic experience. Whether you prefer the zesty combination of lemon and blueberry, the indulgent flavors of salted caramel and pretzel, or the tropical sweetness of mixed fruits and oats, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So, pack up your picnic basket, head outdoors, and savor these delightful treats with your loved ones. Happy picnicking!
