Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the cubed sweet potatoes to the skillet and cook for 10-12 minutes, or until they start to soften. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking. Once the sweet potatoes are slightly tender, add the diced red and green bell peppers, as well as the finely chopped red onion. Continue cooking for an additional 5-6 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked through and slightly caramelized. Sprinkle the paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper over the sweet potato mixture. Stir well to evenly coat the vegetables with the spices. Cook for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Remove the skillet from heat and transfer the sweet potato hash to a serving dish. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of freshness and color. Serve the Egg-Free Sweet Potato Hash hot and enjoy this flavorful and nutritious breakfast!
