The Secret to Making Crispy Thai Fish Cakes


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re probably familiar with the delightful flavors and textures that it offers. One dish that perfectly encapsulates the essence of Thai cooking is the crispy Thai fish cake. These little patties are bursting with flavor and have a satisfying crunch that keeps you coming back for more.

So, what’s the secret to making these fish cakes crispy? It all comes down to a few key techniques and ingredients that I’m about to share with you. Get ready to take your taste buds on a journey to Thailand!

Ingredients

To make these crispy Thai fish cakes, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 500g white fish fillets (such as cod or haddock)
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves, finely chopped
  • 2 green beans, finely sliced
  • 2 spring onions, finely chopped
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by cutting the fish fillets into small pieces and placing them in a food processor. Pulse until the fish is minced but still has some texture.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the minced fish, red curry paste, fish sauce, cornstarch, beaten egg, kaffir lime leaves, green beans, and spring onions. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Take a small handful of the mixture and shape it into a patty using your hands. Repeat this process until all the mixture is used.

  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or wok over medium-high heat. The oil should be hot enough to sizzle when you add the fish cakes.

  5. Carefully place the fish cakes into the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Fry them for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn golden brown and crispy.

  6. Once cooked, remove the fish cakes from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

  7. Serve the crispy Thai fish cakes hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and accompanied by lime wedges for squeezing over the top. These fish cakes are delicious on their own, but you can also serve them with sweet chili sauce or a tangy cucumber relish for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • If you’re unable to find kaffir lime leaves, you can substitute them with lime zest for a similar citrusy flavor.
  • Feel free to adjust the amount of red curry paste and fish sauce to suit your taste preferences. If you like it spicier, add more curry paste, and if you prefer it milder, reduce the amount.
  • For a healthier alternative, you can bake the fish cakes instead of frying them. Simply preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and place the patties on a lined baking sheet. Bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until they are cooked through and golden brown.

Now that you know the secret to making crispy Thai fish cakes, it’s time to put your culinary skills to the test. These flavorful patties are perfect for a quick and satisfying meal or as an appetizer to impress your guests. So, gather your ingredients, get cooking, and enjoy the mouthwatering taste of Thailand right in your own kitchen. Happy cooking!
