Decadent Oreo Cheesecake Bars: A Cookies and Cream Heaven


If you’re a fan of the classic cookies and cream flavor, then you’re in for a treat with these Decadent Oreo Cheesecake Bars. This recipe combines the richness of cheesecake with the irresistible crunch of Oreo cookies, resulting in a dessert that’s truly a cookies and cream heaven. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, celebrating a special occasion, or simply indulging in a sweet craving, these Oreo Cheesecake Bars are sure to impress and delight.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 24 Oreo cookies
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 16 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 12 Oreo cookies, roughly chopped

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 6 Oreo cookies, crushed

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. Prepare the Crust: In a food processor, pulse the Oreo cookies until they turn into fine crumbs. Add the melted butter and pulse again until well combined. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside.

  3. Make the Cheesecake Filling: In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract and sour cream until fully incorporated. Gently fold in the chopped Oreo cookies. Pour the cheesecake filling over the prepared crust.

  4. Bake and Cool: Bake the cheesecake bars for 35-40 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly. Remove from the oven and allow the bars to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

  5. Prepare the Topping: In a medium bowl, whip the heavy cream and powdered sugar until stiff peaks form. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled cheesecake bars. Sprinkle the crushed Oreo cookies on top.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Using the parchment paper overhang, lift the cheesecake bars out of the pan and onto a cutting board. Slice into bars and serve.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent touch, drizzle the cheesecake bars with warm chocolate ganache before serving.
  • Experiment with different flavors of Oreo cookies, such as mint or peanut butter, to customize the bars to your preference.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can blend the Oreo cookies for the filling into a finer consistency.

These Decadent Oreo Cheesecake Bars are a delightful fusion of velvety cheesecake and the timeless appeal of Oreo cookies. With a buttery Oreo crust, a creamy cheesecake filling studded with cookie pieces, and a luscious whipped cream topping, every bite is a symphony of flavors and textures. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is approachable and guaranteed to earn you rave reviews. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a journey to create your very own cookies and cream heaven!
