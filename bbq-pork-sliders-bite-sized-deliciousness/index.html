BBQ Pork Sliders: Bite-Sized Deliciousness


Who doesn’t love a good barbecue? Infusing succulent pork with a lip-smacking sauce brings out the perfect blend of sweet and smoky flavors. When served on a soft, warm slider bun, it transports us to a laid-back, backyard cookout atmosphere. These BBQ pork sliders are not only the epitome of comfort food but also the quintessential dish for any gathering. So, let’s dive into the delightful world of these mouthwatering bites.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless pork shoulder
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Slider buns, warmed
  • Pickles, for serving (optional)

Method:

  1. Begin by seasoning the pork shoulder with salt and pepper. In a large bowl or resealable plastic bag, combine the pork, 1/2 cup barbecue sauce, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, and smoked paprika. Mix well to ensure the pork is evenly coated.

  2. Cover the bowl and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least two hours, allowing the flavors to meld and penetrate the meat.

  3. Preheat your grill or smoker to a consistent temperature of around 250°F (121°C).

  4. Remove the seasoned pork from the refrigerator and allow it to rest for 10-15 minutes before grilling. This helps achieve a more even cook and prevents the exterior from seizing too quickly.

  5. Place the pork on the grill and cook it slowly over indirect heat, aiming for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 190°F (88°C) on an instant-read thermometer. Your BBQ pork shoulder will be tender and juicy.

  6. As the pork cooks, warm your slider buns and prepare the remaining barbecue sauce. If desired, slice the pickles and set them aside.

  7. Once the pork shoulder reaches the desired internal temperature, remove it from the grill and let it rest for about 20 minutes. During this time, the juices will redistribute within the meat, ensuring a fantastic mouthfeel.

  8. Using a sharp knife or food processor, shred the pork and discard any excess fat or connective tissue.

  9. In a small saucepan, reheat the reserved barbecue sauce over low heat until it’s just warm. If the sauce seems a bit thin, melt in some additional brown sugar to achieve a thicker consistency.

  10. To assemble, slather a generous amount of warmed barbecue sauce on the interior of each slider bun. Add shredded BBQ pork, and garnish with sliced pickles, if you wish.

  11. Serve these delightful BBQ pork sliders immediately while still warm. Enjoy them with friends and family, savoring each bite.

Dishing Out the Charm

BBQ pork sliders are a versatile dish that caters to everyone. Whether for game day, a graduation party, or just a casual cookout, these sliders will steal the show. The key to exceptional BBQ pork sliders lies in the marination, slow cooking, and careful shredding of the pork. Put on your apron and get ready to impress your circles with this winning dish!
