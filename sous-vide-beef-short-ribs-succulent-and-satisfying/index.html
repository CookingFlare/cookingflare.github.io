Sous Vide Beef Short Ribs: Succulent and Satisfying


If you’re a fan of melt-in-your-mouth, succulent beef, then sous vide beef short ribs are a must-try. This cooking method is a game-changer when it comes to achieving the perfect texture and flavor for beef short ribs. The sous vide technique involves cooking food in vacuum-sealed bags in a water bath at a precise temperature, ensuring that the meat is evenly cooked to the desired doneness. In this blog post, we’ll explore the step-by-step process of preparing sous vide beef short ribs, along with some flavorful seasoning and serving suggestions.

Ingredients:

  • 4 beef short ribs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • 2 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

Step 1: Preparing the Beef Short Ribs

  • Start by seasoning the beef short ribs generously with salt and pepper. Make sure to coat all sides of the ribs evenly.
  • Divide the minced garlic, fresh rosemary, and fresh thyme equally among the beef short ribs, gently pressing them onto the surface of the meat.
  • Place each seasoned beef short rib into a separate vacuum-sealed bag, ensuring that the garlic and herbs are distributed evenly.

Step 2: Sous Vide Cooking

  • Preheat your sous vide water bath to 135°F (57°C) for medium-rare doneness, or adjust the temperature based on your preferred level of doneness.
  • Once the water bath reaches the desired temperature, carefully submerge the sealed beef short rib bags into the water, ensuring that they are fully submerged.
  • Cook the beef short ribs in the sous vide water bath for 48 hours, allowing the meat to tenderize and develop rich flavors.

Step 3: Finishing the Short Ribs

  • After 48 hours, carefully remove the sous vide bags from the water bath and gently pat the beef short ribs dry with paper towels.
  • Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat and add olive oil and butter.
  • Once the skillet is hot, sear the beef short ribs for 1-2 minutes on each side until a golden crust forms, enhancing the flavor and texture of the meat.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Serve the succulent sous vide beef short ribs with your choice of sides, such as creamy mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or a fresh garden salad.
  • Drizzle any remaining juices from the sous vide bags over the beef short ribs for added flavor.
  • Garnish with fresh herbs or a sprinkle of flaky sea salt before serving.

Enjoy Your Sous Vide Beef Short Ribs!

Sous vide beef short ribs are a true delight for beef lovers, offering unparalleled tenderness and flavor. The slow cooking process allows the meat to reach the perfect level of doneness while retaining its natural juices and richness. Whether you’re hosting a special dinner or simply indulging in a gourmet meal at home, these succulent and satisfying beef short ribs are sure to impress your guests and elevate your culinary skills.

So, fire up your sous vide machine, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure with this delectable sous vide beef short ribs recipe. Your taste buds will thank you for the unforgettable experience of savoring perfectly cooked, succulent beef short ribs. Cheers to the art of sous vide cooking and the joy of creating memorable dining experiences!
