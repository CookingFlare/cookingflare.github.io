So, if you’re ready to embark on a culinary adventure, let’s dive into the world of tropical banana bread! Ingredients Before we start, let’s gather our ingredients: 3 ripe bananas, mashed 1/3 cup melted coconut oil 3/4 cup brown sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 2 eggs 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup crushed pineapple, drained 1/2 cup shredded coconut 1/2 cup chopped macadamia nuts Instructions Step 1: Preparing the Batter Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, melted coconut oil, brown sugar, and vanilla extract. Add the eggs to the bowl and mix until well combined. Step 2: Mixing the Dry Ingredients In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Step 3: Adding the Tropical Flair Gently fold in the crushed pineapple, shredded coconut, and chopped macadamia nuts. Be careful not to overmix the batter, as it can result in a tough banana bread. Step 4: Baking the Bread Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and spread it evenly. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Step 5: Enjoying the Fruits of Your Labor Once baked, remove the banana bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
