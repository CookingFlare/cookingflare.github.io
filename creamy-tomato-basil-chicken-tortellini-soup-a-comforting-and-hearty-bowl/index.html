Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Tortellini Soup: A Comforting and Hearty Bowl


There’s something truly special about a steaming bowl of soup that warms you from the inside out, especially when it’s packed with comforting flavors and hearty ingredients. This Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Tortellini Soup is a perfect example of a dish that delivers on both flavor and comfort. With tender chicken, cheesy tortellini, and a luscious creamy tomato basil broth, this soup is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 pound cheese tortellini
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper, then add them to the pot. Cook until golden brown and cooked through, then transfer to a plate and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, and red pepper flakes, and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 15 minutes.

  4. Using an immersion blender or a countertop blender, puree the soup until smooth. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream and chopped basil. Bring the soup back to a simmer.

  5. Add the cheese tortellini to the pot and cook according to the package instructions, usually about 7-9 minutes.

  6. Once the tortellini is cooked, return the chicken to the pot and let it warm through for a few minutes. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Serve the Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Tortellini Soup hot, garnished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and additional basil if desired.

Why You’ll Love This Soup

This Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Tortellini Soup is a wonderful combination of flavors and textures. The tender chicken adds protein and heartiness to the soup, while the cheese tortellini brings a comforting, carb-filled element. The creamy tomato basil broth ties everything together with its rich and flavorful base, making each spoonful a delight.

This soup is also incredibly versatile. You can easily customize it by adding your favorite vegetables such as spinach, kale, or carrots. You could also swap out the chicken for Italian sausage if you’re looking for a different flavor profile. The possibilities are endless, allowing you to make this soup your own.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re looking for a cozy meal on a chilly evening or a dish to impress your dinner guests, this Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Tortellini Soup fits the bill. It’s a comforting and hearty bowl that’s simple to make yet tastes like it came from a gourmet kitchen. So, grab a spoon, ladle yourself a generous serving, and savor the warmth and flavor of this delightful soup.
