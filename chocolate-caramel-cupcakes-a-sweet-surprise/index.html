Chocolate Caramel Cupcakes: A Sweet Surprise


If there’s one thing that can instantly put a smile on anyone’s face, it’s a delicious cupcake. And when that cupcake is a chocolate caramel delight, it becomes a sweet surprise that will leave everyone craving for more. Today, I’m going to share with you a mouthwatering recipe for Chocolate Caramel Cupcakes that will surely become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup boiling water

For the caramel filling:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup water
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the chocolate ganache:

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a cupcake pan with paper liners.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Make sure all the dry ingredients are well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  4. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Slowly pour in the boiling water and stir until the batter is smooth. The batter will be thin, but that’s what will give the cupcakes their moist and tender texture.

  6. Fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with the batter. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  7. While the cupcakes are cooling, let’s prepare the caramel filling. In a medium saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved. Increase the heat to high and let the mixture come to a boil.

  8. Cook the sugar mixture without stirring until it turns a deep amber color. This will take about 5-7 minutes. Remove the saucepan from the heat and carefully add the heavy cream, butter, and vanilla extract. Be cautious as the mixture will bubble up.

  9. Stir the caramel mixture until smooth and well combined. Let it cool slightly before transferring it to a piping bag fitted with a round tip.

  10. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, use a knife or cupcake corer to create a small hole in the center of each cupcake. Fill the holes with the caramel filling, making sure not to overfill.

  11. Now, it’s time to make the chocolate ganache. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the chocolate chips and heavy cream. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate has melted and the mixture is smooth.

  12. Let the ganache cool for a few minutes, then drizzle it over the cupcakes, allowing it to drip down the sides.

  13. Finally, let the cupcakes set for about 30 minutes to allow the ganache to firm up. Serve and enjoy!

Conclusion

These Chocolate Caramel Cupcakes are the perfect combination of rich chocolate, gooey caramel, and creamy ganache. They are a delightful treat that will satisfy any sweet tooth. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply indulging in a sweet surprise, these cupcakes are guaranteed to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to embark on a baking adventure that will leave you and your loved ones craving for more. Happy baking!
