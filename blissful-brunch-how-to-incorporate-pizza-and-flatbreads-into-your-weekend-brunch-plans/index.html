Blissful Brunch: How to Incorporate Pizza and Flatbreads into Your Weekend Brunch Plans


Are you seeking a unique, savory way to spice up your weekend brunch routine? Look no further! Embrace the art of mingling pizza and flatbreads into your brunch plans. This delightful fusion of breakfast and dinner will undoubtedly give your gathering a distinctive appeal. Here, we explore an eclectic array of recipes and tips that’ll uplift your brunch game and leave your guests begging for more.

Classic Pizza Brunch

Kick off your brunch with a classic Margherita pizza. This time-honored combination of tomatoes, mozzarella, fresh basil, and a drizzle of olive oil never fails to satisfy.

  • Pizza Dough

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour

  • 1 tsp active dry yeast

  • 2 ml sugar

  • 1 cup warm water

  • 1 tsp salt

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • Tomato Sauce

  • 3 tbsp olive oil

  • 1 small onion, finely diced

  • 28 oz canned whole peeled tomatoes, crushed by hand

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • Salt and pepper, to taste

  • Fresh basil leaves, torn

  • Toppings

  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced

  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Begin with the dough. Combine flours, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Gradually whisk in warm water and olive oil until a soft, sticky dough forms. Knead for 5-7 minutes, until smooth. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  2. In another saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions, and sauté for 5 minutes. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, garlic, salt, and pepper. Simmer, uncovered, for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Punch down the dough and roll it out into 14-inch circles. Spread the tomato sauce evenly onto the crust, then add mozzarella slices and torn basil leaves.
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and slightly brown. Slice, garnish with additional fresh basil, and serve!

Scrumptious Sausage & Egg Pizza

Featuring a delightful combination of sweet and spicy sausage, runny yolks, and gooey cheese, this pizza will undoubtedly be the most talked-about dish at your brunch.

  • Pre-baked Pizza Crust

  • Store-bought or homemade dough

  • Cooked and crumbled breakfast sausage

  • Dried oregano and basil

  • Salt and black pepper

  • Egg & Cheese

  • 4 large eggs

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Bake the pizza dough pre-baked for about 10 minutes until lightly browned.
  2. Sprinkle the cooked sausage, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper even over the pre-baked crust.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the sausage.
  4. Return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 12-15 minutes or until the eggs are set and cheese is golden and bubbly. Slice and serve hot.

Delectable Apple Cinnamon Flatbread

This gourmet flatbread combines the classic fall flavors of apple and cinnamon, making it an irresistibly indulgent brunch must-try.

  • Pizza Dough

  • Store-bought or homemade dough

  • Butter, for brushing

  • Apple Cinnamon Topping

  • 1 small apple, thinly sliced

  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

  • 1 tsp cinnamon

  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the pizza dough into a 14-inch circle. Brush the dough lightly with butter.
  2. Combine apple slices, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a medium bowl. Arrange the apple slices evenly atop the dough.
  3. Drizzle with melted butter, then bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden-brown and apples are tender. Slice the flatbread into wedges, and serve immediately.
