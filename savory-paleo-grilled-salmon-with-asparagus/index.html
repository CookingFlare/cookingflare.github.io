Savory Paleo Grilled Salmon with Asparagus


There’s nothing quite like the smoky, charred flavors that come from grilling, especially when it comes to seafood. If you’re a fan of paleo cooking and love the taste of fresh salmon, then you’re in for a treat with this savory grilled salmon recipe. Paired with tender asparagus spears, this dish is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Get ready to fire up the grill and impress your taste buds with this flavorful paleo delight!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, tough ends trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Fresh lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using a gas grill, preheat it for about 10 minutes. If you’re using a charcoal grill, light the charcoal and let it burn until the coals are covered in white ash.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, smoked paprika, sea salt, and black pepper. Stir well to create a flavorful marinade for the salmon.

  3. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish or a resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the salmon, making sure each fillet is well-coated. Allow the salmon to marinate for at least 15 minutes, or up to 30 minutes if you have the time.

  4. While the salmon is marinating, prepare the asparagus. Toss the trimmed asparagus spears with a drizzle of olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of sea salt and black pepper.

  5. Once the grill is preheated, carefully place the salmon fillets on the grill grates, skin side down. Close the lid and let the salmon cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the flesh is opaque and flakes easily with a fork. Cooking times may vary depending on the thickness of your salmon fillets, so keep an eye on them to prevent overcooking.

  6. While the salmon is cooking, place the asparagus spears on the grill. Cook for about 3-4 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are tender and slightly charred.

  7. Once the salmon and asparagus are cooked, remove them from the grill. Serve the grilled salmon on a platter, garnished with fresh lemon wedges. Arrange the grilled asparagus alongside the salmon for a beautiful presentation.

  8. Enjoy your savory paleo grilled salmon with asparagus immediately. The smoky flavors of the grilled salmon combined with the tender, charred asparagus make for a truly mouthwatering dish. Serve it as a main course for a special dinner, or make it a part of your weekly meal prep for a healthy and satisfying lunch option.

Final Thoughts

Grilling salmon brings out its natural flavors, and when combined with the earthiness of asparagus, it creates a delightful and satisfying paleo-friendly meal. This recipe is not only delicious but also packed with omega-3 fatty acids, essential vitamins, and minerals. Whether you’re a seasoned paleo enthusiast or just looking to try something new, this savory paleo grilled salmon with asparagus is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a healthy and mouthwatering meal!
