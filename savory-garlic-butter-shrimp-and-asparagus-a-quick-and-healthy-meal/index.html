Savory Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus: A Quick and Healthy Meal


Are you looking for a quick and healthy meal that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than this delicious recipe for Savory Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus. Packed with protein and nutrients, this dish is not only satisfying but also incredibly easy to prepare. So let’s dive right in and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 425°F (220°C). This will ensure that your shrimp and asparagus cook evenly and develop a delicious golden crust.

  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes. Be careful not to burn the garlic as it can turn bitter.

  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. The cooking time may vary depending on the size of your shrimp, so keep a close eye on them to avoid overcooking.

  4. While the shrimp are cooking, prepare the asparagus. Place the trimmed asparagus on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss to coat evenly.

  5. Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and roast the asparagus for 10-12 minutes, or until they are tender yet still crisp. Cooking times may vary depending on the thickness of the asparagus spears, so adjust accordingly.

  6. Once the shrimp and asparagus are cooked to perfection, it’s time to bring it all together. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the shrimp and toss gently to coat. This citrusy touch adds a refreshing zing to the dish.

  7. Serve the garlic butter shrimp alongside the roasted asparagus for a complete and satisfying meal. The vibrant colors and enticing aroma will surely impress your family and friends.

Health Benefits

Not only is this dish incredibly delicious, but it’s also packed with health benefits. Shrimp is an excellent source of lean protein, low in calories, and rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which are beneficial for heart health. Asparagus, on the other hand, is loaded with vitamins, minerals, and fiber, making it a great addition to any balanced diet.

Final Thoughts

Savory Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus is a quick and healthy meal that will satisfy your taste buds and nourish your body. With its simple preparation and fantastic flavors, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights or when you’re craving something delicious but don’t want to spend hours in the kitchen. So why not give it a try and enjoy a wholesome meal that’s both satisfying and nutritious? Bon appétit!

Remember to adjust the recipe according to your personal preferences and dietary restrictions. Feel free to experiment with additional seasonings or spices to customize the flavors to your liking. Enjoy!
