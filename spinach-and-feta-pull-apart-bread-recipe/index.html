Spinach and Feta Pull-Apart Bread Recipe


What could be better than a warm, tender, and scrumptious piece of bread? How about a rich and flavorful Spinach and Feta Pull-Apart Bread that will surely amaze you with its unique blend of ingredients? This bread combines two heavenly flavors, spinach and feta cheese, to create a truly mouthwatering treat.

For this recipe, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 egg yolk + 1 tablespoon water (for egg wash)
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Before you start, understand and let the ingredients come to room temperature.

First, in a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well with a whisk to ensure all ingredients are evenly distributed. In a separate bowl, combine the warm water and melted butter, then pour this mixture into the dry ingredients. Mix using a wooden spoon or your hands until you achieve a soft and slightly sticky dough. Remember not to overmix the dough.

Next, fold in the chopped spinach leaves and crumbled feta cheese. Take care not to overmix as this may cause the cheese to become tough. Instead, gently mix the ingredients just enough to incorporate them evenly throughout the dough.

Now it’s time to knead the dough. Dust your work surface lightly with flour and turn out the dough onto it. Knead by hand or with a dough hook attachment on your mixer for about 5-8 minutes. The dough should be smooth, elastic, and not sticky.

Place the dough in a well-oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

While your dough is rising, preheat your oven to 375˚F (190˚C). If using a standard loaf pan, grease it with butter or non-stick cooking spray. Alternatively, you can use a disposable aluminum loaf pan for easy clean-up.

Once the dough has risen, punch down the dough and turn it onto a floured surface. Divide the dough into equal portions, and then shape them into a round or oblong shape, depending on your pan size.

Place the bread in the prepared pan, leaving space between each piece to allow for expansion during baking. Brush the top of the bread with the egg yolk mixture for a beautiful and shiny finish.

Sprinkle the freshly grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the bread.

Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown and sound hollow when tapped. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serve this delicious Spinach and Feta Pull-Apart Bread warm with a cooling dip or simply enjoy it on its own. This bread contains a perfect balance of flavors, textures, and is a showstopper that will impress your family and friends.

Just try not to eat the entire loaf in one sitting.

Feel free to experiment with other cheese and herb combinations or even replacing the spinach with your choice of ingredients. Baking should be an avenue for creativity, so have fun and enjoy!
