Coconut Shrimp Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised and chopped
  • 4 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 green onions, sliced (for garnish)
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a small skillet, toast the cumin and coriander seeds over medium heat until fragrant, about 2-3 minutes. Transfer to a spice grinder or mortar and pestle, and grind into a powder.

  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the red curry paste and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly, until fragrant.

  3. Add the coconut milk, broth, fish sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and the ground cumin and coriander powder. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  4. Add the shrimp, bell pepper, and mushrooms to the curry sauce. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the shrimp are opaque and cooked through.

  5. Remove the lemongrass stalks and kaffir lime leaves from the curry.

  6. Garnish with sliced green onions and fresh cilantro.

  7. Serve hot over steamed jasmine rice or with your choice of accompaniments.
