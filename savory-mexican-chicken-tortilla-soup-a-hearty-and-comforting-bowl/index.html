Savory Mexican Chicken Tortilla Soup: A Hearty and Comforting Bowl


When it comes to comfort food, few dishes can rival the hearty and soul-warming qualities of a bowl of Mexican chicken tortilla soup. This savory delight combines the vibrant flavors of Mexico with the comforting goodness of a warm and satisfying soup. Packed with tender chicken, flavorful spices, and crispy tortilla strips, this recipe is a true crowd-pleaser. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your apron, and let’s dive into the world of Mexican cuisine with this delicious and comforting bowl of goodness.

Ingredients

To create the perfect Mexican chicken tortilla soup, you’ll need:

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of cooked shredded chicken
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of frozen corn kernels
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Tortilla strips, for topping

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, jalapeno pepper, and red bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  2. Stir in the ground cumin, chili powder, and dried oregano. Cook for another minute to toast the spices and enhance their flavors.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth and diced tomatoes, including their juices. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Add the cooked shredded chicken to the pot and season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the chicken to absorb the flavors of the soup.

  5. Stir in the frozen corn kernels and black beans. Cook for another 5 minutes until the corn is heated through and the beans are tender.

  6. Remove the pot from the heat and garnish the soup with freshly chopped cilantro. Serve hot with lime wedges and a generous handful of tortilla strips on top.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, leave the seeds in the jalapeno pepper or add a dash of hot sauce to the soup.
  • If you prefer a thicker consistency, you can blend a portion of the soup using an immersion blender or regular blender before adding the shredded chicken, corn, and beans.
  • Feel free to customize the toppings to your liking. Sour cream, avocado slices, and shredded cheese are all delicious additions to this already flavorful soup.

Conclusion

Savory Mexican chicken tortilla soup is a true crowd-pleaser that brings the vibrant flavors of Mexico to your bowl. With its combination of tender chicken, aromatic spices, and crispy tortilla strips, this hearty and comforting dish is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a comforting meal on a chilly evening or hosting a gathering with friends, this recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, follow the easy steps, and enjoy the flavors of Mexico in every spoonful of this delicious soup.
