Instant Pot Beef and Barley Soup: Hearty and Nourishing


When it comes to comforting and nourishing meals, few things hit the spot quite like a bowl of hearty beef and barley soup. This Instant Pot recipe takes a classic favorite and streamlines it for a quick and easy weeknight dinner. Packed with tender beef, wholesome barley, and flavorful vegetables, this soup is the perfect way to warm up on a chilly evening.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup pearl barley
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Beef: Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add a little oil to the pot and brown the beef cubes in batches. This helps to develop flavor and ensure that the meat is tender.

  2. Add Aromatics and Vegetables: Once the beef is browned, add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and minced garlic to the Instant Pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften and become fragrant.

  3. Add Barley and Seasonings: Stir in the pearl barley, bay leaves, dried thyme, and season with salt and pepper. Give everything a good stir to combine the flavors.

  4. Pressure Cook: Pour in the beef broth, close the Instant Pot lid, and set the valve to the sealing position. Pressure cook on high for 20 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for an additional 10 minutes.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the pressure has released, carefully open the Instant Pot lid. Give the soup a good stir, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Ladle the beef and barley soup into bowls, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve with crusty bread on the side.

Tips for Success

  • Choose the Right Cut of Beef: For this soup, beef chuck is a great choice as it becomes tender and flavorful when cooked under pressure.

  • Don’t Skip the Sauté Step: While it may be tempting to skip the browning of the beef, taking the time to do so adds a depth of flavor that is well worth the effort.

  • Adjust the Veggies: Feel free to customize the vegetables in this soup to your liking. If you prefer more onions or extra carrots, go ahead and tailor the recipe to suit your taste.

  • Storage and Reheating: This soup keeps well in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat it on the stove or in the microwave for a quick and satisfying meal.

In Conclusion

This Instant Pot beef and barley soup is a wonderful way to enjoy a nourishing and comforting meal with minimal effort. The tender beef, hearty barley, and aromatic vegetables come together in a rich and flavorful broth that is sure to warm you from the inside out. Whether you’re cooking for your family or simply craving a bowl of homemade goodness, this recipe is a winner. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor a bowl of this hearty and nourishing soup.
