Simple and Tasty Fried Rice Recipes for Cooking Newbies


Are you new to cooking and looking for a simple and delicious recipe to start with? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be sharing some easy-to-follow fried rice recipes that are perfect for cooking newbies. Fried rice is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste preferences, and it’s also a great way to use up leftover rice and veggies. So, let’s dive right into the recipes!

Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan and sauté until they become fragrant and slightly translucent.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for a few minutes until they are tender.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and add the cooked rice to the other side. Break up any clumps of rice with a spatula.
  5. Pour the soy sauce over the rice and mix well to evenly distribute the flavors.
  6. Stir-fry the rice and vegetables together for a few minutes, until everything is well combined and heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade vegetable fried rice!

Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté until it becomes fragrant.
  3. Add the cooked chicken and mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Push the chicken and vegetables to one side of the pan and add the cooked rice to the other side. Break up any clumps of rice with a spatula.
  5. Pour the soy sauce over the rice and mix well to evenly distribute the flavors.
  6. Stir-fry the rice, chicken, and vegetables together for a few minutes, until everything is well combined and heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade chicken fried rice!

Shrimp Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté until it becomes fragrant.
  3. Add the cooked shrimp and mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for a few minutes until the shrimp is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  4. Push the shrimp and vegetables to one side of the pan and add the cooked rice to the other side. Break up any clumps of rice with a spatula.
  5. Pour the soy sauce over the rice and mix well to evenly distribute the flavors.
  6. Stir-fry the rice, shrimp, and vegetables together for a few minutes, until everything is well combined and heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade shrimp fried rice!

Conclusion

Fried rice is a fantastic dish for cooking newbies. With just a few simple ingredients and some basic cooking techniques, you can create a delicious and satisfying meal. Whether you prefer a vegetarian version with mixed vegetables or a protein-packed option with chicken or shrimp, these recipes are sure to impress your taste buds. So, grab your ingredients, heat up your pan, and get ready to enjoy some tasty homemade fried rice!
