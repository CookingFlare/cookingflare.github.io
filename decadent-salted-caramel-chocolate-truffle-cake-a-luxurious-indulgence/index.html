Decadent Salted Caramel Chocolate Truffle Cake: A Luxurious Indulgence


If you’re a fan of the rich and velvety combination of chocolate and caramel, then this Decadent Salted Caramel Chocolate Truffle Cake is a must-try for you. With its luscious layers of moist chocolate cake, creamy salted caramel filling, and smooth chocolate ganache, this cake is the epitome of indulgence.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Salted Caramel Filling:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 12 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.

  3. Add the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix on medium speed until well combined.

  4. Reduce the speed to low and slowly add the boiling water to the batter. Mix until just combined. The batter will be thin, but that’s okay.

  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  7. While the cakes are baking, prepare the salted caramel filling. In a medium saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture comes to a boil.

  8. Continue cooking, without stirring, until the mixture turns a deep amber color. This should take about 6 to 8 minutes.

  9. Remove the saucepan from the heat and carefully whisk in the heavy cream. Be cautious as the mixture will bubble vigorously.

  10. Add the butter and sea salt to the caramel mixture, stirring until smooth. Set aside to cool completely.

  11. Once the cakes have cooled, carefully remove them from the pans. Place one cake layer on a serving platter.

  12. Spread a generous amount of the salted caramel filling over the top of the cake layer.

  13. Place the second cake layer on top of the caramel filling, creating a sandwich.

  14. In a medium saucepan, heat the heavy cream for the chocolate ganache over medium heat until it starts to simmer. Remove from the heat.

  15. Add the finely chopped chocolate to the hot cream and let it sit for a minute to melt. Stir until smooth and glossy.

  16. Pour the chocolate ganache over the top of the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.

  17. Use a spatula to smooth the ganache and create a luxurious finish.

  18. Place the cake in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until the ganache has set.

  19. Before serving, sprinkle a pinch of sea salt on top of the cake for an extra touch of indulgence.

  20. Slice and serve this Decadent Salted Caramel Chocolate Truffle Cake to impress your guests or to treat yourself to a moment of pure bliss.

Enjoy every bite of this luxurious indulgence! The combination of the moist chocolate cake, rich salted caramel filling, and smooth chocolate ganache will transport your taste buds to a realm of pure decadence. Whether it’s a special occasion or simply a desire for something extraordinary, this cake is sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. So go ahead, take a leap into the world of indulgence and let this Salted Caramel Chocolate Truffle Cake be your guide.
