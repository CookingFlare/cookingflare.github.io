Tasty Nut-Free Pumpkin Spice Latte Recipe


Nothing says fall quite like a warm and comforting pumpkin spice latte. But what if you have a nut allergy and can’t enjoy the traditional version? Don’t worry, because I’ve got you covered with this delicious and nut-free pumpkin spice latte recipe. It’s the perfect blend of cozy flavors that will make you feel like you’re sipping autumn in a cup.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/2 cup of pumpkin puree
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of brewed coffee or espresso
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Ground cinnamon (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the milk, pumpkin puree, sugar, pumpkin pie spice, and vanilla extract. Place the saucepan over medium heat and whisk until the mixture is well combined and heated through. Be careful not to let it boil.

  2. While the milk mixture is heating, brew your coffee or espresso using your preferred method. Set it aside.

  3. Once the milk mixture is warmed, remove it from the heat and carefully pour it into a blender. Blend on high speed for about 30 seconds to create a frothy texture.

  4. Divide the coffee or espresso between two mugs. Slowly pour the frothy milk mixture over the coffee, dividing it equally between the mugs.

  5. If desired, top each mug with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of ground cinnamon for an extra touch of indulgence.

  6. Grab a cozy blanket, curl up on the couch, and enjoy your homemade nut-free pumpkin spice latte!

Note: Feel free to adjust the sweetness and spice level to suit your taste. You can also experiment with different types of milk, such as almond, oat, or soy, depending on your dietary preferences.

Why We Love This Recipe

This nut-free pumpkin spice latte recipe is not only delicious, but it’s also allergy-friendly, making it accessible to those who have nut allergies or sensitivities. By using pumpkin puree and pumpkin pie spice, we achieve that signature fall flavor without relying on any nuts or nut-based ingredients.

The combination of creamy milk, aromatic spices, and the subtle sweetness of pumpkin creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will warm your soul on chilly autumn days. Plus, the frothy texture adds a touch of decadence to your morning or afternoon coffee routine.

Another reason to love this recipe is its simplicity. With just a handful of ingredients and a few easy steps, you can whip up a homemade pumpkin spice latte that rivals any café version. Plus, you have the freedom to customize the sweetness and spice level to your liking, ensuring that every sip is tailored to your preferences.

So, whether you have a nut allergy, dietary restrictions, or simply want to enjoy a homemade pumpkin spice latte without the hassle of going out, this recipe is a winner. It’s the perfect way to savor the flavors of fall and indulge in a cozy beverage without any worries.

Give this nut-free pumpkin spice latte recipe a try, and let the warm and comforting flavors transport you to a pumpkin-filled paradise. Cheers to autumn and all the delicious treats it brings!
