Creamy Keto Chicken and Bacon Alfredo: A Rich and Satisfying Dish


Are you following a keto diet and craving a rich and satisfying meal? Look no further than this Creamy Keto Chicken and Bacon Alfredo recipe! Packed with flavor and low in carbs, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings without throwing you off track. Let’s dive right in and discover how to create this mouthwatering delight!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 slices of bacon, cooked until crispy
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken breasts until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside to rest.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream to the skillet. Stir well, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Simmer for about 2 minutes until the cream has slightly thickened.

  4. Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet, stirring constantly until fully melted and incorporated into the sauce.

  5. Crumble the crispy bacon into the sauce, reserving a few pieces for garnish. Stir in the Italian seasoning and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Return the cooked chicken breasts to the skillet, turning them to coat them evenly with the creamy sauce. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes until the chicken is heated through.

  7. Remove the chicken from the skillet and serve it hot, garnished with the remaining bacon crumbles and fresh parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • If you want to add some extra flavor to the dish, try adding some sautéed mushrooms or spinach to the sauce.
  • For a lighter version, you can substitute half-and-half or unsweetened almond milk for the heavy cream.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of the dish to your liking.
  • This recipe pairs well with zucchini noodles or cauliflower rice for a complete keto-friendly meal.

Conclusion

There you have it – a creamy and indulgent Creamy Keto Chicken and Bacon Alfredo recipe that is sure to satisfy your taste buds while keeping you on track with your keto diet. With its rich flavors and satisfying ingredients, this dish is perfect for a cozy night in or for impressing your guests at a dinner party. Give it a try and experience the delight of a guilt-free, low-carb Alfredo dish like never before!

Remember, the key to a successful keto lifestyle is finding delicious recipes that fit your dietary goals. This Creamy Keto Chicken and Bacon Alfredo recipe is just one example of the many tasty and satisfying meals you can enjoy while staying in ketosis. So, get cooking and bon appétit!
