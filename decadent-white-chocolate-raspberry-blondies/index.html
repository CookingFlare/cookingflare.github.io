Decadent White Chocolate Raspberry Blondies


If you’re a fan of the sweet, tangy flavor combination of white chocolate and raspberry, then these Decadent White Chocolate Raspberry Blondies are sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection. These indulgent blondies are perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a gathering with friends or simply treating yourself to a delightful dessert. The soft, chewy texture of the blondies combined with the bursts of tart raspberry and creamy white chocolate make for an irresistible treat that will have everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and brown sugar until well combined.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the butter mixture, and whisk until smooth.
  4. Gently fold in the flour and salt until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.
  5. Stir in the white chocolate chips, reserving a handful to sprinkle on top of the batter.
  6. Carefully fold in the fresh raspberries, being mindful not to crush them too much.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it out evenly with a spatula.
  8. Sprinkle the reserved white chocolate chips on top of the batter.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  10. Allow the blondies to cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares.

Serving Suggestions

These Decadent White Chocolate Raspberry Blondies are delicious on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and a drizzle of raspberry sauce. They also make a delightful addition to any dessert platter when entertaining guests, or as a sweet surprise in packed lunches or picnics. Enjoy them with a cup of your favorite tea or coffee for a delightful afternoon treat.

Storage

To keep your blondies fresh, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. If you want to enjoy them for longer, they can be stored in the refrigerator for up to a week. You can also freeze them for up to 3 months by wrapping individual blondie squares in plastic wrap and placing them in a freezer-safe bag.

Final Thoughts

These Decadent White Chocolate Raspberry Blondies are a delightful twist on the classic blondie recipe, and they’re sure to be a hit with anyone who tries them. The combination of sweet white chocolate and tart raspberries creates a flavor experience that is truly indulgent. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is straightforward and rewarding. So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to a batch of these irresistible blondies? Happy baking!
