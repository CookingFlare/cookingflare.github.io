Grill & Chill: BBQ Recipes for Your Ultimate Summer Picnic


Summer is the perfect time to embrace the great outdoors, soak up the sunshine, and indulge in delicious BBQ flavors. Whether you’re hosting a backyard gathering or heading out for a picnic in the park, nothing beats the smoky goodness of grilled dishes paired with refreshing sides. To help you elevate your summer picnic experience, here are some mouthwatering BBQ recipes that will make your Grill & Chill gathering a hit!

Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine lemon juice, zest, garlic, olive oil, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add chicken chunks to the marinade and coat evenly. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers.
  4. Grill the skewers over medium-high heat for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally until cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with a side of grilled vegetables or a fresh salad.

Smoky BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Slider buns
  • Coleslaw for topping

Instructions:

  1. Season the pork shoulder with salt and pepper and place it in a slow cooker.
  2. Add sliced onions, minced garlic, BBQ sauce, and chicken broth to the slow cooker.
  3. Cook on low for 8 hours until the pork is tender and easy to shred.
  4. Remove the pork, shred it using two forks, and mix it with the cooking juices.
  5. Fill slider buns with the pulled pork mixture and top with coleslaw.
  6. Enjoy these flavorful sliders at your picnic!

Grilled Corn on the Cob with Chili Lime Butter

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 4 tbsp butter, softened
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine softened butter, chili powder, lime zest, and salt.
  2. Brush the chili lime butter onto each ear of corn.
  3. Grill the corn over medium heat, turning occasionally, for about 10 minutes or until charred.
  4. Serve hot with extra chili lime butter on the side for a burst of flavor.

Grilled Peach and Burrata Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 oz burrata cheese
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Brush peach halves with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill the peaches, cut side down, for 3-4 minutes until grill marks appear.
  3. Arrange grilled peaches on a platter with torn burrata cheese and basil leaves.
  4. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and a sprinkle of salt.
  5. This sweet and savory salad is a delightful addition to your picnic spread.

Conclusion

With these delectable BBQ recipes, your summer picnic will be a memorable feast for all. From juicy grilled chicken skewers to smoky pulled pork sliders and flavorful sides, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So fire up the grill, invite your loved ones, and savor the flavors of summer with these Grill & Chill recipes. Happy grilling!

Let the vibrant flavors and aromas of these BBQ recipes transport you to a culinary paradise at your next summer picnic. Remember, good food shared with good company makes for the best memories. Enjoy the sunshine, the laughter, and the delicious eats. Happy grilling and chilling!
