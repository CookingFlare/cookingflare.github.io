Vegan Panna Cotta Recipe: An Italian-Inspired Dessert


Are you looking for a delicious and elegant dessert that’s not only vegan but also full of flavor? Look no further than this Vegan Panna Cotta recipe! Panna Cotta, which translates to “cooked cream” in Italian, is a classic Italian dessert known for its creamy texture and delicate sweetness. In this vegan version, we’ll be using coconut milk and agar agar to achieve that same luxurious consistency without any animal products. Let’s dive into the recipe and create a decadent treat that will impress vegans and non-vegans alike.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (27 oz) of full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of agar agar powder
  • Fresh berries or fruit compote for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Coconut Milk Mixture

    • In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, sugar, and vanilla extract.
    • Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir the mixture until the sugar has dissolved completely.
    • Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, stirring occasionally to prevent the coconut milk from sticking to the bottom of the pan.

  2. Activate the Agar Agar

    • In a small bowl, combine the agar agar powder with 1/4 cup of water. Let it sit for 5 minutes to allow the agar agar to activate.

  3. Incorporate the Agar Agar

    • Once the coconut milk mixture is simmering, pour in the agar agar mixture while continuously whisking to ensure it’s fully incorporated.

  4. Simmer and Stir

    • Allow the mixture to simmer for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent lumps from forming.

  5. Pour Into Molds

    • Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the mixture cool for a few minutes.
    • Pour the mixture into individual ramekins or molds, filling them to the top.

  6. Chill and Set

    • Place the filled ramekins in the refrigerator and let the panna cotta set for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.

  7. Serve and Garnish

    • Once the panna cotta is set, carefully run a knife around the edges of each mold and invert the panna cotta onto serving plates.
    • Garnish with fresh berries or a fruit compote of your choice.

Tips for Success

  • Use full-fat coconut milk for the richest and creamiest texture.
  • Agar agar is a vegan alternative to gelatin and is essential for setting the panna cotta. Be sure to follow the instructions for activating and incorporating it properly.
  • Allow the panna cotta to chill for the recommended time to ensure it sets properly and has the perfect consistency.

Final Thoughts

This Vegan Panna Cotta recipe offers a delightful twist on a traditional Italian dessert, showcasing that vegan cooking can be just as indulgent and satisfying. Whether you’re serving it at a dinner party or treating yourself to a special dessert, this creamy and luscious panna cotta is sure to captivate the taste buds of everyone who tries it. Enjoy the luxurious flavors and textures of this Italian-inspired treat, guilt-free and purely delightful. Buon appetito!
