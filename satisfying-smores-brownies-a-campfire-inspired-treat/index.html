Satisfying S'mores Brownies: A Campfire-Inspired Treat


When it comes to desserts that evoke nostalgia and warmth, few things can compare to the classic combination of s’mores. The gooey marshmallows, rich chocolate, and crunchy graham crackers create an irresistible treat that transports us to campfires under starlit skies. But what if you could capture that campfire magic in a brownie? Enter the S’mores Brownies—a delightful mashup that combines the fudgy richness of brownies with the iconic flavor profile of s’mores.

In this blog post, we’ll take you through an easy yet satisfying recipe for S’mores Brownies that will satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your friends and family. Perfect for gatherings, parties, or just a cozy night in, these decadent brownies will become a staple in your dessert repertoire.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the steps, let’s gather our ingredients. Here’s what you’ll need to make these heavenly S’mores Brownies:

For the Brownie Base:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

For the S’mores Layer:

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs (about 8 full sheets)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (semi-sweet or milk chocolate)
  • 1/2 cup additional mini marshmallows for topping

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup chocolate chips (for drizzling)
  • 1/2 cup crushed graham crackers

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that we have our ingredients ready, let’s jump right into the preparation.

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that your brownies will bake evenly and come out perfectly fudgy.

Step 2: Prepare the Brownie Batter

  1. Melt the Butter: In a medium saucepan, melt the 1 cup of unsalted butter over low heat. Once melted, remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

  2. Mix Sugar and Eggs: In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and 2 cups of granulated sugar. Mix well until fully combined. Add the eggs one by one, mixing thoroughly after each addition. Finally, stir in the vanilla extract.

  3. Combine Dry Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, unsweetened cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder. Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; you want your brownies to remain fudgy.

Step 3: Create the Graham Cracker Layer

  1. Prepare the Crust: In a separate bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are thoroughly coated and have a sandy texture.

  2. Layer in the Pan: Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan or line it with parchment paper. Press the graham cracker mixture into the bottom of the pan to form an even layer.

Step 4: Assemble the Brownies

  1. Layer the Brownie Batter: Pour half of the brownie batter on top of the graham cracker crust, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  2. Add the S’mores Filling: Sprinkle 1 cup of mini marshmallows and 1 cup of chocolate chips over the brownie layer. Pour the remaining brownie batter on top, spreading it gently to cover the marshmallows and chocolate.

  3. Top with More Marshmallows: Finally, sprinkle 1/2 cup of additional mini marshmallows on top of the brownie batter. This will give your brownies that classic s’mores look once baked.

Step 5: Bake the Brownies

Place the baking pan in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes. The brownies are done when a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs but not wet batter. Keep in mind that the marshmallows will puff up, creating a gooey and delightful texture.

Step 6: Prepare the Topping

While the brownies are baking, melt the additional 1 cup of chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring in between until smooth. Once the brownies are out of the oven and slightly cooled, drizzle the melted chocolate over the top. Finally, sprinkle the crushed graham crackers on top for that extra crunch.

Step 7: Cool and Serve

Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before cutting them into squares. This will make it easier to get nice, clean edges. Serve them warm or at room temperature—the choice is yours!

Tips for the Perfect S’mores Brownies

  • Use Quality Chocolate: The key to rich and delicious brownies is high-quality chocolate. Choose the type you enjoy most, whether it’s semi-sweet, milk chocolate, or even dark chocolate for a more intense flavor.

  • Don’t Overbake: For that perfect fudgy texture, keep an eye on the baking time. Remove the brownies from the oven when they’re just set in the center.

  • Customize Your Ingredients: Feel free to add your twist! You can mix in peanut butter chips, swap out the chocolate for white chocolate, or even add a layer of caramel for an extra indulgent treat.

Storing Your S’mores Brownies

If you somehow have leftovers (which is unlikely), you can store your S’mores Brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. They can also be refrigerated for up to a week. To reheat, simply pop them in the microwave for a few seconds until warm, and enjoy the gooey goodness all over again!

Conclusion

S’mores Brownies are more than just a dessert—they’re an experience that brings back memories of summer nights and campfire stories. This recipe combines the rich, fudgy goodness of brownies with the nostalgic flavors of s’mores, creating a treat that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a party, attending a potluck, or just indulging in a sweet craving, these brownies are sure to be a hit.

So, gather your ingredients, channel your inner campfire spirit, and get ready to enjoy a satisfying dessert that will leave everyone asking for seconds! Happy baking!
