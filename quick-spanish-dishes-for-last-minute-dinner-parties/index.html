Quick Spanish Dishes for Last-Minute Dinner Parties


When it comes to hosting a dinner party, the pressure can often feel overwhelming, especially when time is of the essence. If you find yourself in a pinch, don’t fret! Spanish cuisine offers a plethora of quick and delightful dishes that will not only impress your guests but also allow you to enjoy the evening without being stuck in the kitchen for hours. This guide will walk you through several quick Spanish dishes that are perfect for last-minute dinner parties, ensuring you have a memorable gathering filled with flavor and warmth.

1. Tapas: The Perfect Starter

Tapas are small Spanish dishes that are perfect for sharing, making them an ideal starter for your dinner party. They can be prepared in advance or quickly assembled right before serving. Here are a few easy tapas recipes to get you started:

Patatas Bravas

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Potatoes: Peel and dice the potatoes into bite-sized pieces. Rinse them under cold water to remove excess starch.

  2. Fry the Potatoes: Heat a generous amount of olive oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Once hot, add the diced potatoes and fry until golden brown and crispy. This should take about 10-15 minutes.

  3. Make the Sauce: While the potatoes are frying, combine the mayonnaise, ketchup, smoked paprika, and hot sauce in a small bowl. Mix well.

  4. Serve: Drain the fried potatoes on paper towels, sprinkle with salt, and serve with the spicy mayo on the side.

Chorizo al Vino

Ingredients:

  • 300g Spanish chorizo
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Slice the Chorizo: Cut the chorizo into thick slices.

  2. Cook: In a skillet, combine chorizo, minced garlic, and red wine. Simmer over medium heat for about 10 minutes until the wine reduces slightly and the chorizo is heated through.

  3. Garnish and Serve: Transfer to a serving dish, sprinkle with fresh parsley, and serve warm.

2. Main Courses That Wow

Spanish cuisine is rich with flavors, and preparing a main course doesn’t have to be time-consuming. Here are two quick and satisfying Spanish dishes that will be the highlight of your dinner party.

Spanish-Style Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 500g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh lemon for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Garlic: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant but not browned.

  2. Add Shrimp: Toss in the shrimp, paprika, and chili flakes. Cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.

  3. Serve: Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the shrimp and serve immediately with crusty bread.

Quick Paella

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 200g mixed seafood (shrimp, mussels, and squid)
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • Saffron or turmeric for color
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Vegetables: In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped bell pepper and sauté for 3-4 minutes.

  2. Add Rice and Broth: Stir in the Arborio rice and pour in the broth. Add saffron or turmeric for color. Bring to a simmer.

  3. Cook Seafood: Once the rice is nearly cooked, add the mixed seafood and peas. Cook until the seafood is done, about 5 minutes.

  4. Serve: Let the paella sit for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

3. Delicious Desserts to Conclude the Meal

No dinner party is complete without a sweet ending! Spanish desserts are often simple yet indulgent. Here are two quick recipes that will satisfy your guests’ sweet tooth.

Churros with Chocolate Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Make the Dough: In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, and salt. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat and stir in flour until a dough forms.

  2. Heat Oil: In a deep frying pan, heat oil over medium-high heat.

  3. Fry Churros: Fill a piping bag with the dough and pipe long strips into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes. Drain on paper towels.

  4. Prepare Chocolate Sauce: In a small saucepan, heat heavy cream until simmering. Pour over chopped chocolate and stir until smooth.

  5. Serve: Serve churros warm with chocolate sauce for dipping.

Flan

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Caramelize Sugar: In a saucepan, melt sugar over medium heat until it turns golden brown. Quickly pour into a flan mold or individual ramekins.

  2. Blend Ingredients: In a blender, combine eggs, sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla. Blend until smooth.

  3. Pour and Bake: Pour the mixture over the caramelized sugar in the mold. Bake in a water bath at 350°F (175°C) for about 50 minutes or until set.

  4. Chill and Serve: Once cooled, refrigerate for a few hours. To serve, invert the flan onto a plate so the caramel sauce drizzles over the top.

4. Wine Pairing Tips

To complement your delicious Spanish dishes, consider serving a selection of Spanish wines. Here are a few pairings that will elevate your dinner party experience:

  • With Tapas: A chilled Albariño pairs beautifully with the fresh flavors of seafood and light tapas.
  • With Shrimp: Enjoy your Spanish-style shrimp with a crisp Verdejo for a refreshing contrast.
  • With Paella: A robust Tempranillo or Garnacha will enhance the flavors of the saffron and seafood.
  • With Dessert: A sweet Pedro Ximénez or a sparkling Cava can provide a lovely finish to your meal.

5. Final Thoughts

Hosting a dinner party doesn’t have to be a stressful endeavor. With these quick Spanish dishes, you can create a menu that is both simple to prepare and delightful to enjoy. From tapas to main courses and irresistible desserts, Spanish cuisine offers a variety of flavors that will impress your guests and make your gathering unforgettable. So, gather your ingredients, put on some lively Spanish music, and get ready to share an evening filled with laughter, good food, and great company!

Remember, the key to a successful dinner party is to enjoy yourself and the time spent with your loved ones. Happy cooking!
