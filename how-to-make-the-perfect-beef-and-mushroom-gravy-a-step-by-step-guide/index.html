How to Make the Perfect Beef and Mushroom Gravy: A Step-by-Step Guide


Are you a fan of hearty and delicious gravy? Look no further! In this step-by-step guide, I will walk you through the process of making the perfect beef and mushroom gravy. This rich and savory gravy is the perfect accompaniment to mashed potatoes, roasted meats, and so much more. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef (such as chuck roast or sirloin), cut into small pieces
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the beef and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the onions and mushrooms. Cook until the onions are translucent and the mushrooms have released their moisture.

  3. Add the garlic to the skillet and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.

  4. Push the onions, mushrooms, and garlic to one side of the skillet, creating a space in the center. Add the flour to the center and cook for a minute, stirring constantly.

  5. Gradually pour in the beef broth, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until it thickens slightly.

  6. Return the beef to the skillet and stir to combine. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for 2-3 hours, until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  7. Stir in the heavy cream and Worcestershire sauce. Continue cooking for an additional 10 minutes, until the gravy has thickened to your desired consistency.

  8. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.

  9. Remove from heat and let the gravy cool slightly before serving. It will continue to thicken as it cools.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve the beef and mushroom gravy over mashed potatoes for a classic comfort food combination.
  • Drizzle the gravy over a juicy steak for an extra burst of flavor.
  • Use the gravy as a base for a beef pot pie or shepherd’s pie.
  • Pour the gravy over roasted vegetables for a delicious vegetarian option.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a deeper flavor, consider adding a splash of red wine to the gravy.
  • If you prefer a thicker gravy, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of cold water and stir it into the gravy during the last few minutes of cooking.
  • Leftover gravy can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Reheat it gently on the stovetop or in the microwave.

And there you have it - a simple and foolproof recipe for the perfect beef and mushroom gravy. This versatile gravy is sure to become a family favorite and elevate any meal. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress with this delicious homemade gravy. Enjoy!
