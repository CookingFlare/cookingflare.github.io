Keto Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Dip: A Low-Carb and Delicious Appetizer


Indulging in a tasty and satisfying appetizer while sticking to a low-carb diet can be a challenge. But fear not, because we have the perfect solution for you - Keto Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Dip! Packed with flavor and low in carbohydrates, this dip is a crowd-pleaser that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup of canned artichoke hearts, drained and roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup of full-fat Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, minced garlic, onion powder, dried basil, and dried oregano. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  3. Next, add the chopped spinach and artichoke hearts to the bowl. Gently fold them into the mixture until evenly distributed.

  4. Season the dip with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to go easy on the salt, as the Parmesan cheese already adds a salty flavor.

  5. Transfer the dip to a baking dish or oven-safe skillet, spreading it out evenly.

  6. Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

  7. Once done, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Serve the Keto Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Dip with low-carb options such as cucumber slices, celery sticks, or almond flour crackers. The dip can also be enjoyed with pork rinds for an extra crunch.

Tips and Variations

  • For a creamier texture, you can add 1/4 cup of cream cheese to the mixture. This will make the dip even more indulgent and satisfying.

  • If you prefer a spicier kick, consider adding a dash of hot sauce or a pinch of red pepper flakes to the dip. Adjust the amount according to your taste preferences.

  • To make this recipe dairy-free, simply swap out the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, and cheeses with their dairy-free alternatives. You can use coconut milk yogurt, vegan mayonnaise, and dairy-free cheese substitutes.

  • Don’t limit yourself to just using this dip as an appetizer. It also makes a great topping for grilled chicken or a delicious filling for stuffed mushrooms.

  • Leftover dip can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Reheat it in the oven or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

With this Keto Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Dip recipe, you can enjoy a delectable appetizer without straying from your low-carb lifestyle. The combination of fresh spinach, tangy artichoke hearts, and creamy cheeses creates a flavorful dip that will please your taste buds and keep you on track with your dietary goals. So go ahead, whip up a batch of this crowd-pleasing dip and savor every creamy, low-carb bite!

Note: Always consult with a healthcare professional before making any significant changes to your diet, especially if you have any underlying health conditions.
