Slow Cooker Comfort Desserts to Indulge In


Indulging in a warm and comforting dessert is one of life’s greatest pleasures. And what better way to satisfy your sweet tooth than with slow cooker comfort desserts? These delightful treats are not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. So grab your slow cooker and get ready to indulge in these mouthwatering desserts that will leave you wanting more.

Slow Cooker Apple Crisp

There’s nothing quite like the aroma of apples and cinnamon filling your home. This slow cooker apple crisp is the perfect dessert to enjoy on a cozy evening. Start by peeling and slicing 4-5 apples and place them in the slow cooker. In a separate bowl, mix together 1 cup of oats, 1/2 cup of flour, 1/2 cup of brown sugar, 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Cut in 1/2 cup of cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle the crumb mixture over the apples in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 4-6 hours or until the apples are tender. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a truly indulgent experience.

Slow Cooker Chocolate Lava Cake

Chocolate lovers, rejoice! This slow cooker chocolate lava cake is the ultimate indulgence. In a bowl, whisk together 1 cup of flour, 1/2 cup of sugar, 1/4 cup of cocoa powder, 2 teaspoons of baking powder, and a pinch of salt. In a separate bowl, mix together 1/2 cup of milk, 1/4 cup of melted butter, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until well combined. Pour the batter into a greased slow cooker. In a small bowl, mix together 1/2 cup of sugar and 1/4 cup of cocoa powder. Sprinkle this mixture over the batter in the slow cooker. Finally, pour 1 1/2 cups of boiling water over the top. Cover and cook on high for 2-3 hours or until the edges are set and the center is gooey. Serve warm with a dollop of whipped cream for a truly decadent treat.

Slow Cooker Bread Pudding

Bread pudding is the epitome of comfort food, and making it in a slow cooker takes it to a whole new level. Start by tearing 6 cups of day-old bread into bite-sized pieces and placing them in the slow cooker. In a bowl, whisk together 2 cups of milk, 1/2 cup of sugar, 4 eggs, 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Pour this mixture over the bread in the slow cooker, making sure all the bread is soaked. Cook on low for 4-6 hours or until the pudding is set in the center. Serve warm with a drizzle of caramel sauce for a truly comforting dessert.

Slow Cooker Berry Cobbler

If you’re a fan of berries, then this slow cooker berry cobbler is a must-try. In a bowl, mix together 4 cups of mixed berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries), 1/2 cup of sugar, 2 tablespoons of cornstarch, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Pour this mixture into the slow cooker. In a separate bowl, mix together 1 cup of flour, 1/4 cup of sugar, 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Cut in 1/4 cup of cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle the crumb mixture over the berries in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 2-3 hours or until the berries are bubbling and the topping is golden brown. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful dessert.

Conclusion

Slow cooker comfort desserts are the perfect way to indulge in something sweet and satisfying. From apple crisp to chocolate lava cake, bread pudding to berry cobbler, these recipes are guaranteed to make your taste buds sing. So dust off your slow cooker and get ready to create some mouthwatering desserts that will have everyone asking for seconds. Enjoy the comfort and joy that these desserts bring, and let the slow cooker do all the hard work for you. Happy indulging!
