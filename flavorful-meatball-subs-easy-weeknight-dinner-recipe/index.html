Flavorful Meatball Subs: Easy Weeknight Dinner Recipe


Looking for a quick and delicious weeknight dinner idea? Meatball subs are the perfect go-to meal when you need something that’s easy to make, satisfying, and bursting with flavor. This recipe takes the classic meatball sub to a whole new level, combining homemade meatballs, savory marinara sauce, and gooey melted cheese, all nestled in a warm and toasty sub roll. Whether you’re cooking for a busy family or just craving a comforting meal for yourself, these flavorful meatball subs are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Marinara Sauce:

  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional Ingredients:

  • Sub rolls
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Meatballs

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix until all the ingredients are well-combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.

Step 2: Cook the Meatballs

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the meatballs are cooked through and browned on the outside.

Step 3: Prepare the Marinara Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Simmer the sauce over medium heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 4: Assemble the Meatball Subs

  1. Slice the sub rolls in half lengthwise and place them on a baking sheet.
  2. Place a few meatballs on one half of each sub roll.
  3. Spoon the marinara sauce over the meatballs.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the marinara sauce.

Step 5: Bake the Subs

  1. Place the assembled subs in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  2. Remove the subs from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Serving Suggestion

Serve these flavorful meatball subs with a side of crispy garlic parmesan fries or a simple green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

With just a few simple ingredients and easy-to-follow steps, you can create a mouthwatering meal that’s perfect for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. The combination of tender meatballs, zesty marinara sauce, and melted cheese nestled in a warm sub roll is a surefire way to please everyone at the dinner table. So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and delicious dinner, give these flavorful meatball subs a try. They’re guaranteed to become a family favorite in no time!
