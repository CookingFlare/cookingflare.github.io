Gourmet Tuna Nicoise Wrap: A Sophisticated and Flavorful Option


If you’re looking for a sophisticated and flavorful option for a light meal or a picnic, the Gourmet Tuna Nicoise Wrap is an excellent choice. This delicious wrap combines the freshness of tuna, the brininess of olives, the crunch of green beans, and the creaminess of eggs and potatoes, all wrapped in a soft, fluffy tortilla. Whether you’re a fan of traditional French cuisine or simply love the combination of savory flavors, this recipe is sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Wrap:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Fresh baby spinach leaves
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves

For the Tuna Nicoise Filling:

  • Canned tuna, drained
  • Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • Green beans, blanched and chopped
  • Hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • Baby potatoes, boiled and sliced
  • Capers
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

For the Vinaigrette:

  • Dijon mustard
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vinaigrette: In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, red wine vinegar, and olive oil until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside.

  2. Assemble the Wrap: Lay a tortilla on a flat surface and arrange a handful of baby spinach leaves in the center. Top with cherry tomatoes, red onion slices, and fresh basil leaves.

  3. Prepare the Tuna Nicoise Filling: In a separate bowl, combine the drained canned tuna, chopped Kalamata olives, blanched and chopped green beans, sliced hard-boiled eggs, boiled and sliced baby potatoes, capers, and chopped fresh parsley. Drizzle the prepared vinaigrette over the filling and toss gently to coat.

  4. Fill the Wrap: Spoon the tuna Nicoise filling onto the bed of spinach leaves and other vegetables in the tortilla. Be generous with the filling, ensuring an even distribution of all the delicious ingredients.

  5. Wrap it Up: Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling and then roll it up tightly, ensuring that the ends are tucked in. Repeat the process for the remaining wraps.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once all the wraps are assembled, slice them in half and serve immediately. These wraps can also be refrigerated and enjoyed later, making them a perfect make-ahead option for picnics or packed lunches.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian version, you can substitute the tuna with chickpeas or white beans for a protein-packed alternative.
  • Add a sprinkle of crumbled feta cheese for an extra burst of flavor.
  • Experiment with different types of olives to find your preferred combination of flavors.

The Gourmet Tuna Nicoise Wrap is not only a delightful and sophisticated option for a light meal, but it also offers a convenient way to enjoy the classic flavors of a Nicoise salad in a portable wrap form. Whether you’re preparing it for a leisurely weekend lunch or a quick weekday dinner, this recipe is bound to impress your family and friends with its blend of fresh, vibrant ingredients and complex flavors. So, roll up your sleeves and dive into the world of gourmet wraps with this tantalizing recipe!
