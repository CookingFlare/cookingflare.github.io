Hearty Vegan One-Pot Wonders: 20 Supper-Saving Recipes for Lazy Weeknights


Busy weeknights demand quick and easy meals, and who says they can’t be healthy and delicious too? Say hello to hearty vegan one-pot wonders! With these 20 supper-saving recipes, you’ll save time, dirty fewer dishes, and impress your taste buds without breaking a sweat. Here’s a flavorful way to make your weeknights brighter.

  1. Vegan One-Pot Minestrone Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 2 cups diced zucchini
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (15-oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup small pasta (like small shells or medium lentils)
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook until the onions are translucent.

  2. Stir in the zucchini, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.

  3. Add the pasta or lentils, cover, and cook until tender (typically about 10-15 minutes).

  4. Stir in the spinach and let it wilt for a minute. Top with fresh basil and serve.

  5. Vegan One-Pot Chickpea Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (14-oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (15-oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • Vegan curry paste (2 tablespoons), or to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice or cauliflower rice

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the onion, garlic, ginger, and red bell pepper in oil until soft. Add the diced tomatoes, chickpeas, and curry paste. Stir well and season with salt and pepper.

  2. Add the frozen mixed vegetables and combine well. Cook until the vegetables are done.

  3. Add the cooked rice, mix well, and heat through.

  4. Vegan One-Pot Vegan Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 red bell peppers, diced
  • 2 cups diced zucchini
  • 1 (15-oz) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (15-oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (28-oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, bell peppers, and zucchini in oil until softened.

  2. Add beans, crushed tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir well.

  3. Simmer for about 25-30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender. Serve with fresh cilantro on top.

  4. Vegan One-Pot Quinoa Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 bell peppers, halved and seeded
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 (15-oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the bell peppers, cut-side up, in a large pot.

  2. Cook quinoa, vegetable broth, cumin, salt, and pepper in the pot until the quinoa is fully cooked.

  3. Stir in black beans, corn, and cilantro, mix well.

  4. Spoon the mixture into the hollowed-out peppers, and continue to cook for a few minutes until heated through. Serve with lime wedges.

  5. Vegan One-Pot Eggplant and Lentil Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large eggplant, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 (28-oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and turmeric in the oil until fragrant. Add the eggplant, salt, and pepper, and cook until browned.

  2. Stir in diced tomatoes, broth, lentils, curry powder, and garam masala. Bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer.

  3. Cook until lentils are tender and the curry has thickened. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

  4. Vegan One-Pot Spaghetti Squash Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 1 spaghetti squash
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Halve the spaghetti squash lengthwise and remove the seeds. Brush the inside with oil and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Roast the squash halves for about 45 minutes or until tender. Let cool slightly.
  3. Scoop out the flesh using a fork, it should come out in spaghetti-like strands.
  4. Heat garlic in a large pot and add the spaghetti strands, broth, and almond milk. Cook until heated through. Season with additional salt and pepper if required. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley.

Conclusion

With one-pot wonders, even the busiest kitchen can whip up flavorsome vegan meals in a breeze. Streamlining the cooking process not only minimizes the stress involved in meal prepping but also allows the love and care for each ingredient to shine. Cook, explore, and enjoy these vegan one-pot miracles, effortlessly.

Keep the warmth and scrumptiousness flowing in your kitchen, even on those peak-meal-prep days. Happy cooking!
