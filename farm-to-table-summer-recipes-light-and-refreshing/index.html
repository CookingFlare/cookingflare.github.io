Farm to Table Summer Recipes: Light and Refreshing


Summer is the perfect time to explore the abundance of fresh produce available at local farms. With the sun shining brightly and the temperatures rising, it’s important to stay cool and refreshed. What better way to do that than with light and refreshing farm-to-table recipes? In this blog post, we will share some delicious recipes that highlight the flavors of summer while keeping things healthy and vibrant. So put on your apron and let’s get cooking!

Watermelon and Feta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of diced watermelon
  • 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 cup of arugula
  • 1/4 cup of fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup of sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the watermelon, feta cheese, arugula, mint leaves, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the watermelon mixture and gently toss to combine.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy the burst of flavors!

Grilled Zucchini and Corn Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 zucchinis, sliced lengthwise
  • 2 ears of corn, husked
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the zucchini slices and corn with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the zucchini for about 2-3 minutes per side, until tender and lightly charred.
  4. Grill the corn for about 10 minutes, rotating occasionally, until lightly charred.
  5. Remove the zucchini and corn from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  6. Cut the kernels off the corn cob and place them in a bowl.
  7. Dice the grilled zucchini and add it to the bowl along with the avocado and cilantro.
  8. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  9. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss to combine.
  10. Serve as a side dish or a light lunch option.

Strawberry and Basil Lemonade

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 4 cups of cold water
  • 1/4 cup of honey or agave syrup (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place the strawberries and basil leaves in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. In a pitcher, combine the strawberry-basil mixture, lemon juice, water, and sweetener (if using).
  3. Stir well until everything is thoroughly mixed.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh basil leaves or sliced strawberries.

Grilled Peach and Burrata Crostini

Ingredients:

  • Baguette slices, toasted
  • 2 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • 4 ounces of burrata cheese
  • Honey, for drizzling
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Balsamic glaze, for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the peach halves for about 2-3 minutes per side, until grill marks appear and they are slightly softened.
  3. Remove the peaches from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  4. Slice the peaches into thin wedges.
  5. Spread a layer of burrata cheese on each toasted baguette slice.
  6. Top with grilled peach slices, drizzle with honey, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with torn basil leaves and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.
  8. Serve as an appetizer or a light summer snack.

Conclusion

These farm-to-table summer recipes are not only light and refreshing but also a celebration of the vibrant flavors that summer brings. From the juicy watermelon and feta salad to the grilled zucchini and corn salad bursting with smoky flavors, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. And let’s not forget the sweet and tangy strawberry and basil lemonade or the indulgent grilled peach and burrata crostini that will surely impress your guests. So head to your local farmer’s market, gather the freshest ingredients, and savor the taste of summer with these delightful recipes. Happy cooking!
