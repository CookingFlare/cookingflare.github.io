The Perfect Recipe for Crispy and Flavorful General Tso's Chicken


General Tso’s Chicken is a popular Chinese dish known for its crispy exterior and flavorful sauce. It’s a dish that combines sweet and spicy flavors, making it a favorite among many. Today, I’m going to share with you my secret recipe for creating the perfect General Tso’s Chicken at home. Get ready to impress your family and friends with this delicious and authentic dish!

Ingredients

For the chicken:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

For garnish:

  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cornstarch, salt, and black pepper. Toss the chicken pieces in the cornstarch mixture until well coated.

  2. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan or wok over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the chicken pieces in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Fry the chicken until golden brown and crispy, about 4-5 minutes per batch. Remove the chicken from the pan and let it drain on a paper towel-lined plate.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sriracha sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, and sesame oil until well combined. Set aside.

  4. Remove any excess oil from the pan, leaving about 1 tablespoon. Heat the pan over medium heat and add the sauce mixture. Stir the sauce constantly until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon, about 3-4 minutes.

  5. Add the fried chicken to the sauce and toss until each piece is evenly coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Remove the General Tso’s Chicken from the heat and garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds.

  7. Serve the General Tso’s Chicken hot with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy coating, you can double-coat the chicken by dipping the cornstarch-coated chicken in beaten egg and then coating it with another layer of cornstarch before frying.

  • If you prefer a milder version, reduce the amount of sriracha sauce or omit it altogether.

  • Feel free to add some vegetables to the dish for added color and nutrition. Bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots work well with the flavors of General Tso’s Chicken.

  • If you want a healthier alternative, you can bake the coated chicken in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through.

  • General Tso’s Chicken is best enjoyed immediately, as the crispy coating may become soggy over time.

Now that you have the perfect recipe for Crispy and Flavorful General Tso’s Chicken, it’s time to put your culinary skills to the test. Prepare this mouthwatering dish and savor the delicious combination of sweet and spicy flavors. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, General Tso’s Chicken is sure to be a hit at your table. Enjoy!
