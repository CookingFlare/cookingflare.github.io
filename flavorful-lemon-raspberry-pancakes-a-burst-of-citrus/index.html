Flavorful Lemon Raspberry Pancakes: A Burst of Citrus


If you’re looking for a breakfast that’s bursting with flavor and will brighten up your morning, then these Flavorful Lemon Raspberry Pancakes are just what you need. Packed with zesty lemon and juicy raspberries, these pancakes are a delightful combination of tangy and sweet. Get ready to elevate your pancake game to a whole new level!

Ingredients

To make these Flavorful Lemon Raspberry Pancakes, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Ensure all the dry ingredients are well combined.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and lemon zest. Make sure to mix until the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense pancakes.

  4. Gently fold in the fresh raspberries into the pancake batter. The raspberries will add a burst of fruity goodness to every bite.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Grease the surface with a small amount of butter or cooking spray.

  6. Using a ¼ cup measuring cup, pour the pancake batter onto the heated skillet. Cook until bubbles start to form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes or until golden brown.

  7. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more butter or cooking spray as needed.

  8. Serve the Flavorful Lemon Raspberry Pancakes warm with a drizzle of maple syrup or a dollop of whipped cream. The sweet and tangy flavors of the lemon and raspberry will dance on your taste buds, leaving you craving for more.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of citrus flavor, you can add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to the pancake batter. This will intensify the lemony goodness.

  • If you prefer a thicker pancake, you can add a little less milk to the batter. Adjust the consistency to your liking by gradually adding more flour if needed.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of berries. Blueberries or strawberries would also work well in this recipe, offering their unique flavors.

  • To keep the pancakes warm while cooking batches, preheat your oven to a low temperature (around 200°F or 93°C) and place the cooked pancakes on a baking sheet in the oven until ready to serve.

So there you have it, a recipe for Flavorful Lemon Raspberry Pancakes that will bring a burst of citrus to your breakfast table. These pancakes are perfect for lazy weekend mornings or special occasions when you want to treat yourself to something extra special. Enjoy the tangy and sweet combination of lemon and raspberries in every bite. Bon appétit!
