Satisfying Low Carb Zucchini Noodle Carbonara


If you’re on the lookout for a scrumptious yet low-carb pasta dish, look no further than this Satisfying Low Carb Zucchini Noodle Carbonara recipe. This dish is a delightful twist on the classic Italian carbonara, replacing traditional pasta with healthy zucchini noodles. Not only is it a fantastic way to sneak in some extra veggies, but it’s also a guilt-free option for those watching their carbohydrate intake. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Zucchini Noodles: Using a spiralizer, create zucchini noodles from the zucchinis. If you don’t have a spiralizer, you can use a vegetable peeler to create long, thin strips resembling noodles. Set the zucchini noodles aside on a paper towel to drain excess moisture.

  2. Cook the Bacon: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until it’s crispy and browned. Once done, transfer the bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to drain the excess fat. Leave the bacon drippings in the skillet.

  3. Whisk the Eggs and Cheese: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, and Pecorino Romano cheese until well combined. This will form a creamy, cheesy sauce for the carbonara.

  4. Cook the Zucchini Noodles: In the same skillet with the bacon drippings, add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Then, add the zucchini noodles to the skillet and toss them in the bacon drippings and garlic for about 2-3 minutes, or until they are just tender. Be careful not to overcook the zucchini noodles, as they can become soggy.

  5. Combine the Ingredients: Once the zucchini noodles are cooked, reduce the heat to low and add the cooked bacon back into the skillet. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the zucchini noodles and toss everything together using tongs. The heat from the zucchini noodles will cook the eggs and melt the cheese, creating a creamy, luscious sauce.

  6. Season and Serve: Season the carbonara with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley and an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Enjoy Your Low Carb Indulgence

This Satisfying Low Carb Zucchini Noodle Carbonara is a wonderful way to enjoy the flavors of a classic carbonara while keeping your carb intake in check. The zucchini noodles provide a refreshing and light base for the rich and creamy sauce, making it a perfect dish for warm summer evenings or cozy winter nights. Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a favorite at your dinner table.

So, next time you’re craving a comforting pasta dish without the guilt, give this Low Carb Zucchini Noodle Carbonara a try. It’s a simple, flavorful, and satisfying meal that will leave you feeling nourished and content. Bon appétit!
