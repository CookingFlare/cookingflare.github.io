Paleo Meatloaf Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


If you’re following a Paleo diet or simply looking to incorporate more grain-free options into your meals, meatloaf is a classic comfort food that can easily be adapted to fit your dietary needs. In this blog post, we’ll explore five delicious and grain-free Paleo meatloaf recipes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds. Let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Paleo Meatloaf

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well using your hands.
  3. Transfer the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet and shape it into a loaf.
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the meatloaf is cooked through and golden brown on top.
  5. Allow the meatloaf to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!

2. Bacon-Wrapped Paleo Meatloaf

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 8-10 slices of bacon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, ground beef, almond flour, tomato sauce, coconut aminos, onion, garlic, Italian seasoning, sea salt, black pepper, and egg. Mix well.
  3. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and shape it into a loaf.
  4. Lay the bacon slices over the top of the meatloaf, tucking the ends underneath.
  5. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the meatloaf is cooked through and the bacon is crispy.
  6. Allow the meatloaf to rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy!

3. Turkey and Spinach Stuffed Meatloaf

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground turkey, almond flour, marinara sauce, coconut aminos, onion, garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, sea salt, black pepper, and egg. Mix well.
  3. Divide the mixture in half and press one half into the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  4. Layer the chopped spinach over the meat mixture, leaving a border around the edges.
  5. Press the remaining meat mixture on top of the spinach, sealing the edges.
  6. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the meatloaf is cooked through.
  7. Allow the meatloaf to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!

4. Mexican-Style Paleo Meatloaf

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup diced green chilies
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well using your hands.
  3. Transfer the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet and shape it into a loaf.
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the meatloaf is cooked through and golden brown on top.
  5. Allow the meatloaf to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!

5. Asian-Inspired Paleo Meatloaf

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon Chinese five-spice powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, almond flour, coconut aminos, green onions, garlic, grated ginger, Chinese five-spice powder, sea salt, black pepper, and egg. Mix well.
  3. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and shape it into a loaf.
  4. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the meatloaf is cooked through and golden brown on top.
  5. Allow the meatloaf to rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy!

These grain-free Paleo meatloaf recipes offer a delicious and satisfying way to enjoy a classic comfort food while sticking to your dietary preferences. Whether you prefer a classic meatloaf or want to explore different flavors like bacon-wrapped, stuffed, or with a Mexican or Asian twist, there’s a recipe here to suit your taste buds. Give them a try and discover a new favorite dish that will leave you feeling nourished and satisfied. Happy cooking!
