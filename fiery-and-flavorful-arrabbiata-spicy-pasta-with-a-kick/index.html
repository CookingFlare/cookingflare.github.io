Fiery and Flavorful Arrabbiata: Spicy Pasta with a Kick


Are you ready to spice up your pasta game? Look no further than this fiery and flavorful Arrabbiata recipe! Bursting with a rich tomato sauce, infused with garlic, onions, and a generous amount of red pepper flakes, this pasta dish is guaranteed to set your taste buds on fire. Let’s dive right in and discover how to create this tantalizing dish that packs a punch.

Ingredients

To make this zesty Arrabbiata pasta, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice tolerance)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil. Add a generous amount of salt to the boiling water, followed by the penne pasta. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion to the skillet, sautéing until they turn golden and fragrant.

  3. Pour the crushed tomatoes into the skillet, followed by the dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and bring the sauce to a gentle simmer.

  4. Allow the sauce to simmer on low heat for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. This will help the flavors meld together and create a deliciously rich and robust sauce.

  5. Once the sauce has thickened and the flavors have developed, it’s time to add the cooked penne pasta. Gently toss the pasta in the sauce, ensuring that each piece is coated in the fiery goodness.

  6. Serve the Arrabbiata pasta hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. The vibrant green basil adds a refreshing touch to the dish, balancing out the spiciness with its aromatic flavor.

Serving Suggestions

This fiery Arrabbiata pasta is perfect on its own, but you can also enhance the meal with some additional ingredients or side dishes. Here are a few suggestions to consider:

  • Grated Parmesan cheese: Sprinkle some freshly grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta for a cheesy twist. The creamy and nutty flavors of Parmesan complement the spiciness of the Arrabbiata sauce beautifully.

  • Grilled chicken: Add some grilled chicken strips to the pasta for a protein-packed meal. The tender chicken pairs well with the fiery sauce, creating a satisfying and filling dish.

  • Garlic bread: Serve the Arrabbiata pasta with a side of warm garlic bread. The crispy exterior and soft, buttery center of the bread make it the perfect companion to soak up the flavorful sauce.

Final Thoughts

Arrabbiata pasta is a true delight for those who crave a spicy kick in their meals. This recipe offers a perfect balance of flavors, with the heat from the red pepper flakes complementing the tanginess of the tomatoes and the earthiness of the herbs. It’s a dish that will awaken your taste buds and leave you longing for more.

So, why wait? Gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and prepare to indulge in this fiery and flavorful Arrabbiata pasta. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress with its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and mouthwatering aroma. Enjoy the spicy journey!
