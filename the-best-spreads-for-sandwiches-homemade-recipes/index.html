The Best Spreads for Sandwiches: Homemade Recipes


When it comes to making a great sandwich, the spread is often what makes or breaks the experience. Whether you prefer classic options or something a little more adventurous, the right spread can elevate your sandwich from simple to spectacular. In this guide, we’ll explore a variety of homemade spreads that will transform your sandwiches and bring new flavors to your meals.

Why Homemade Spreads?

While store-bought spreads can be convenient, homemade spreads offer a level of customization and flavor that is unmatched. Making your own spreads allows you to control the ingredients, adjust flavors to your liking, and avoid preservatives or artificial additives. Additionally, homemade spreads can be a healthier option, as you can choose high-quality ingredients, such as fresh herbs, organic oils, and natural seasonings.

The best part is that homemade spreads are often incredibly easy to make. Most recipes require only a few ingredients and minimal preparation time. You can prepare them in bulk and store them in your refrigerator for quick access throughout the week, making them perfect for meal prepping or last-minute sandwich cravings.

Classic Sandwich Spreads

1. Classic Mayonnaise

Mayonnaise is a classic spread that is rich, creamy, and versatile. It’s perfect for pairing with virtually any sandwich, from chicken to veggies. Making your own mayonnaise at home allows you to adjust the tanginess and texture to suit your taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large egg (at room temperature)
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup light olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the egg, mustard, vinegar, and salt. Whisk until the mixture is smooth and fully combined.
  2. While continuously whisking, slowly drizzle in the oil. Start with just a few drops at a time, then increase the flow as the mixture emulsifies.
  3. Once the mixture thickens to your desired consistency, taste and adjust with more salt, pepper, or vinegar if needed.
  4. Store the mayonnaise in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

2. Butter Spread

Butter is a staple in many sandwiches, but why not give it a twist with a simple herb butter spread? Perfect for grilled sandwiches, this spread adds an extra layer of richness and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the softened butter with the fresh herbs, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stir until the mixture is well incorporated.
  3. Spread the butter generously on bread before grilling for a flavorful, crispy sandwich.

3. Cream Cheese Spread

Cream cheese is another classic that pairs wonderfully with smoked salmon, cucumbers, and bagels. For a more complex flavor, try adding herbs, garlic, or even a bit of honey for a sweet and savory touch.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese with the lemon juice, dill, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix thoroughly until smooth.
  3. Spread generously on bagels, sandwiches, or crackers. This spread works wonderfully with smoked salmon or fresh veggies.

Gourmet and Adventurous Sandwich Spreads

4. Avocado Mash Spread

For a fresh, creamy, and slightly tangy alternative to traditional spreads, try an avocado mash. This spread adds a burst of healthy fats and flavor to any sandwich. It’s particularly delicious with turkey, chicken, or even roasted vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 tablespoon lime or lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mash the avocados until smooth or slightly chunky, depending on your preference.
  2. Stir in the lime or lemon juice, red onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread generously on your sandwich bread, adding a creamy layer of flavor. Top with chicken, turkey, or your favorite fresh ingredients.

5. Hummus Spread

Hummus is a great spread for those who want a plant-based, protein-packed alternative to traditional spreads. It’s rich in flavor and can be easily customized with different herbs and spices. Hummus works great on wraps, pita sandwiches, or veggie-packed sandwiches.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Process until smooth, adding more olive oil or water to reach your desired consistency.
  3. Spread on your favorite bread or pita. Add fresh vegetables, grilled chicken, or roasted red peppers for a Mediterranean-inspired sandwich.

6. Pesto Spread

Pesto is a vibrant, herbaceous spread that brings an intense burst of flavor to sandwiches. Made with basil, garlic, pine nuts, and olive oil, pesto pairs particularly well with mozzarella, chicken, and roasted veggies. You can also experiment with different types of pesto, such as sun-dried tomato or arugula, for a unique twist.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts (or walnuts)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the basil, pine nuts, Parmesan, and garlic.
  2. Process until the mixture is finely chopped.
  3. With the food processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until smooth.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Spread generously on toasted bread or grilled sandwiches. Add fresh mozzarella, roasted chicken, or sun-dried tomatoes for a truly delicious sandwich.

Sweet and Savory Spreads

7. Honey Mustard Spread

Honey mustard is a perfect blend of sweet and tangy, and it pairs excellently with chicken, turkey, and ham sandwiches. This spread is simple to make but adds a lot of flavor to your sandwiches.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or Greek yogurt
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, and mayonnaise or yogurt.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread on bread for a sweet and tangy kick that complements your favorite deli meats.

8. Cranberry Cream Cheese Spread

For a unique and festive twist on the classic cream cheese spread, try adding cranberries and a hint of sweetness. This spread works wonderfully with turkey or chicken sandwiches, especially during the holidays.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon orange zest
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the softened cream cheese with the chopped cranberries, honey or maple syrup, orange zest, and salt.
  2. Stir until smooth and fully incorporated.
  3. Spread on your sandwich bread, and enjoy with roasted turkey, chicken, or even a veggie sandwich for a sweet and savory experience.

Vegan and Allergy-Friendly Spreads

9. Vegan Cashew Cream Spread

Cashews make an excellent base for a creamy, dairy-free spread. This spread is rich and nutty, and it works wonderfully as a substitute for cream cheese or mayonnaise in sandwiches. You can also add herbs or spices to suit your taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water for at least 4 hours
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Drain the soaked cashews and place them in a food processor.
  2. Add the water, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Process until smooth and creamy, adding more water if necessary to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Spread on your favorite bread or crackers for a creamy, dairy-free option.

10. Roasted Red Pepper Spread

Roasted red peppers bring a naturally sweet and smoky flavor to any sandwich spread. Blended with olive oil and garlic, this spread is rich and flavorful while being completely vegan.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large roasted red peppers, peeled and seeded
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the roasted red peppers, olive oil, garlic, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Spread on toasted bread, pita, or use as a dip for vegetables.

Conclusion

When it comes to creating the perfect sandwich, the spread is a key player in bringing everything together. From creamy classics like mayonnaise and butter to zesty, adventurous options like pesto and hummus, the possibilities are endless. Whether you’re preparing a simple lunch or a gourmet sandwich for a special occasion, these homemade spreads are sure to elevate your sandwich game. The best part is, once you’ve mastered these recipes, you can customize them to your liking, creating your own signature spreads that will impress every time.

Experiment with different combinations, add fresh herbs, and explore new textures to make each sandwich truly your own. With these spreads on hand, every bite of your sandwich will be a delightful experience.
