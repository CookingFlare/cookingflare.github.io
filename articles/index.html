Pasta with Creamy Lemon Herb Chicken: A Bright and Flavorful Dish

If you’re looking for a bright and flavorful dish that’s perfect for any occasion, then look no further than this Pasta with Creamy Lemon Herb Chicken recipe. This dish is an absolute crowd-pleaser, combining the zesty freshness of lemon and herbs with the rich creaminess of a classic pasta dish. It’s a delightful fusion of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing! Ingredients For the Chicken: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts Salt and pepper to taste 2 tablespoons olive oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1 teaspoon dried rosemary 1/2 cup chicken broth 1/2 cup heavy cream Zest of 1 lemon Juice of 1 lemon 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped For the Pasta: 12 oz fettuccine or your pasta of choice Salt for boiling water Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for serving Fresh parsley for garnish Instructions Step 1: Cook the Chicken Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. Step 2: Prepare the Sauce In the same skillet, add minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Let the broth simmer for 2-3 minutes. Stir in the heavy cream, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, coating it with the creamy lemon herb sauce. Sprinkle fresh parsley over the chicken. Step 3: Cook the Pasta In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the pasta water. Step 4: Combine the Pasta and Chicken Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the creamy lemon herb chicken. Toss the pasta and chicken together, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if necessary. Step 5: Serve and Enjoy Divide the pasta into serving plates. Garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley. Serve immediately and enjoy the bright and flavorful dish! Conclusion This Pasta with Creamy Lemon Herb Chicken recipe is a true celebration of fresh flavors and creamy indulgence. The zing of lemon and the fragrant herbs elevate the tender chicken and perfectly coat the pasta, creating a dish that’s both comforting and exciting. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a special meal at home, this recipe is sure to impress. Deliciously Creamy Butternut Squash and Sage Pasta: A Fall Favorite

As the leaves turn golden and the air gets crisp, it’s the perfect time to savor the flavors of fall. One dish that captures the essence of the season is the deliciously creamy butternut squash and sage pasta. This comforting and flavorful recipe is a celebration of autumn produce and will surely warm your heart and soul. Ingredients 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into 1-inch cubes 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg 3 cloves garlic, minced 1/4 cup fresh sage leaves, chopped 1/2 cup vegetable broth 1 cup heavy cream 1 pound pasta of your choice Grated Parmesan cheese for serving Instructions Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the butternut squash cubes with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Spread them in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and lightly caramelized. In a large skillet, heat the remaining olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped sage, and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Add the roasted butternut squash to the skillet and mash it with a fork or potato masher until it forms a creamy consistency. Pour in the vegetable broth and heavy cream, stirring to combine. Let the sauce simmer for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the cooking water. Toss the cooked pasta in the butternut squash sauce, adding a splash of the pasta cooking water if needed to loosen the sauce. Serve the creamy butternut squash and sage pasta in bowls, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and an extra sprinkle of chopped sage. Why This Recipe Is Perfect for Fall The creamy texture of the butternut squash sauce combined with the earthy aroma of sage creates a symphony of flavors that evokes the warmth and comfort of the fall season. Homemade Spinach and Ricotta Ravioli: A Cheesy and Flavorful Treat

There’s something truly special about homemade pasta. The taste, the texture, and the satisfaction of creating something delicious from scratch are unmatched. If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, you’ll love this recipe for homemade spinach and ricotta ravioli. It’s a dish that’s both comforting and elegant, making it perfect for a cozy dinner at home or a special occasion with loved ones. Ingredients For the Pasta Dough: 2 cups all-purpose flour 3 large eggs 1 tablespoon olive oil 1/2 teaspoon salt For the Filling: 1 1/2 cups fresh spinach, chopped 1 cup ricotta cheese 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1 clove garlic, minced Salt and pepper to taste For the Sauce: 4 tablespoons unsalted butter 3-4 fresh sage leaves 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Step 1: Make the Pasta Dough On a clean work surface, mound the flour and create a well in the center. Crack the eggs into the well, add the olive oil, and sprinkle with salt. Using a fork, gradually incorporate the flour into the eggs until a dough starts to form. Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes. Step 2: Prepare the Filling In a skillet, sauté the chopped spinach over medium heat until wilted. Remove from heat and let it cool. In a bowl, combine the cooled spinach, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined. Step 3: Roll Out the Dough After the dough has rested, cut it into four equal pieces. Working with one piece at a time, flatten the dough into a rough rectangle shape. Set a pasta machine to the widest setting and pass the dough through it. Fold the dough into thirds and pass it through the machine again. Repeat this process, gradually decreasing the thickness settings, until the dough is thin and smooth. Step 4: Assemble the Ravioli Lay the rolled-out dough on a floured surface. Place small spoonfuls of the filling about 1 inch apart on the dough. Brush water around each mound of filling. Lay another sheet of rolled-out dough over the filling and press around each mound to seal. Use a fluted pastry wheel to cut the ravioli into individual pieces. Step 5: Cook the Ravioli Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Carefully drop the ravioli into the boiling water and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they float to the surface. Remove the ravioli with a slotted spoon and transfer to a plate. Step 6: Make the Sauce In a skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sage leaves and cook until the butter is golden and the sage leaves are crisp. Remove the skillet from the heat and discard the sage leaves. Toss the cooked ravioli in the brown butter sauce and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Enjoy Your Homemade Spinach and Ricotta Ravioli! The combination of tender pasta, creamy ricotta, and earthy spinach, all enveloped in a nutty brown butter sauce, makes this homemade spinach and ricotta ravioli a true delight for the senses. Whether you’re a seasoned pasta maker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. So, roll up your sleeves, embrace the process, and treat yourself to this cheesy and flavorful Italian classic made with love from your own kitchen. Buon appetito! ...

Pasta with Roasted Cherry Tomato Sauce: A Burst of Sweetness

Are you looking for a simple yet delicious pasta recipe that will leave your taste buds craving for more? Look no further! This Pasta with Roasted Cherry Tomato Sauce is the perfect dish to satisfy your pasta cravings and tantalize your palate with a burst of sweetness. Ingredients 1 pound pasta of your choice (penne, spaghetti, or linguine) 2 pints cherry tomatoes 4 cloves of garlic, minced 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried basil Salt and pepper to taste Fresh basil leaves, for garnish Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large baking dish, toss the cherry tomatoes with minced garlic, olive oil, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Ensure that all the tomatoes are evenly coated with the seasonings. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and roast the tomatoes for about 20-25 minutes, or until they start to burst and release their juices. This roasting process will intensify the natural sweetness of the tomatoes, creating a flavorful sauce. While the tomatoes are roasting, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Remember to generously salt the boiling water to enhance the flavor of the pasta. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and set it aside. Remove the roasted tomatoes from the oven and gently mash them with a fork to release their juices and create a chunky sauce. The burst tomatoes should have a thick, saucy consistency. In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the roasted tomato sauce to the skillet and stir it well to combine. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it gently to coat the pasta evenly with the roasted tomato sauce. Cook the pasta and sauce together for about 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Serve the Pasta with Roasted Cherry Tomato Sauce hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of freshness. Tips and Variations For an extra kick of flavor, you can add some red pepper flakes to the roasted tomato sauce during the cooking process. If you prefer a smoother sauce, you can blend the roasted tomatoes in a food processor or blender before adding them to the skillet. Feel free to experiment with different types of pasta to suit your preferences. Fusilli, rigatoni, or farfalle would all work wonderfully with the roasted cherry tomato sauce. To make this dish more substantial, you can add some grilled chicken or sautéed shrimp to the pasta before tossing it with the sauce. This pasta also pairs well with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese or a drizzle of balsamic glaze for an extra layer of flavor. So there you have it - a simple yet incredibly flavorful Pasta with Roasted Cherry Tomato Sauce recipe that will take your pasta nights to a whole new level.
