BBQ Chicken Pizza: Creative and Delicious Recipes for Grilled Pizza


Are you tired of the same old pizza flavors? Looking to spice up your pizza night with something unique and mouthwatering? Look no further than BBQ Chicken Pizza! This creative twist on a classic favorite will leave your taste buds begging for more. In this post, we will share with you some delicious recipes for grilled BBQ Chicken Pizza that will take your pizza game to a whole new level.

The Perfect BBQ Chicken Pizza Crust

Before we dive into the toppings, let’s start with the foundation of any great pizza – the crust. For BBQ Chicken Pizza, we recommend a thin and crispy crust that will provide the perfect balance to the rich and flavorful toppings. Here’s a simple recipe for a homemade pizza dough:

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the dry ingredients, and mix until the dough begins to come together.
  4. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about an hour, or until it doubles in size.
  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down, and divide it into two equal portions.
  7. Roll out each portion into a thin round shape, and you’re ready to top it with delicious BBQ chicken goodness!

Tangy BBQ Chicken Topping

Now that we have our crust ready, let’s move on to the star of the show – the BBQ chicken topping. This tangy and flavorful combination of grilled chicken, BBQ sauce, and other delicious ingredients will make your taste buds dance with joy. Here’s how to prepare it:

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • ½ cup BBQ sauce
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, and grill them for about 6-8 minutes per side or until they are cooked through.
  3. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the BBQ sauce with the grilled chicken strips until they are well coated.
  5. Spread the BBQ chicken evenly over the prepared pizza crusts.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese, red onion slices, and fresh cilantro on top.
  7. Place the pizzas on a preheated grill or in the oven at 425°F (220°C) for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden brown.
  8. Remove the pizzas from the grill or oven, let them cool for a few minutes, and then slice and serve.

Creative Variations to Try

While the classic BBQ chicken pizza is absolutely delicious, why not get creative and try some unique variations? Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  1. Hawaiian BBQ Chicken Pizza: Add some sliced pineapple and ham to your BBQ chicken pizza for a tropical twist.
  2. Spicy BBQ Chicken Pizza: If you love heat, sprinkle some crushed red pepper flakes or diced jalapeños over your BBQ chicken pizza before baking.
  3. BBQ Chicken Ranch Pizza: Drizzle some ranch dressing over your BBQ chicken pizza for a creamy and tangy flavor combination.
  4. Mediterranean BBQ Chicken Pizza: Top your BBQ chicken pizza with feta cheese, black olives, and roasted red peppers for a Mediterranean flair.

The possibilities are endless when it comes to BBQ chicken pizza. Feel free to experiment with different toppings and flavors to find your perfect combination.

Conclusion

BBQ Chicken Pizza is a creative and delicious twist on traditional grilled pizza. With its tangy BBQ chicken topping and crispy crust, it’s sure to satisfy even the pickiest of eaters. Whether you stick to the classic recipe or venture into creative variations, this grilled pizza is guaranteed to become a favorite in your household. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a slice of BBQ heaven. Enjoy!
