Classic Teriyaki Marinade Ingredients: 1 cup soy sauce 1/2 cup brown sugar 1/4 cup honey 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon grated ginger 1/4 cup mirin 1/4 cup sake (or rice vinegar) Optional: red pepper flakes for a kick Instructions: In a saucepan over medium heat, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, garlic, ginger, mirin, and sake. Stir the mixture until the sugar has dissolved and the ingredients are well combined. Bring the marinade to a simmer and cook for about 10-15 minutes until it slightly thickens. Let the marinade cool completely before using it to marinate your choice of protein. This classic teriyaki marinade is perfect for chicken, beef, or tofu.
