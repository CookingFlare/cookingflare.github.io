Tasty Instant Pot Orange Chicken Recipe for a Citrus Twist


If you’re craving a zesty and flavorful dish that’s quick and easy to make, then this Instant Pot orange chicken recipe is just what you need. With a delightful citrus twist, this dish is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more. The beauty of using the Instant Pot is that it effortlessly infuses the flavors of the ingredients, resulting in a mouthwatering meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Sliced green onions and sesame seeds for garnish
  • Cooked rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a bowl, whisk together the orange juice, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes. This flavorful concoction will be the heart and soul of our orange chicken.

  2. Sauté the Chicken: Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and add a splash of oil. Once the pot is hot, add the chicken pieces and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides, ensuring they are not fully cooked through.

  3. Pressure Cook: Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken in the Instant Pot. Secure the lid and set the pot to manual high pressure for 5 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully releasing any remaining pressure.

  4. Thicken the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and stir in the slurry. Allow the sauce to simmer and thicken for a few minutes, stirring constantly until it reaches the desired consistency.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the sauce has thickened, it’s time to savor the fruits of your labor. Serve the orange chicken over a bed of fluffy rice or quinoa, garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds, and relish the delightful citrus flavors.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra burst of citrus, add some orange zest to the sauce.
  • If you prefer a bit of tanginess, increase the amount of rice vinegar.
  • Adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less red pepper flakes.
  • Feel free to substitute chicken thighs with chicken breast if you prefer.
  • For a healthier option, you can use low-carb sweeteners instead of honey.

Final Thoughts:

This Instant Pot orange chicken recipe offers a delightful blend of sweet, savory, and tangy flavors that will impress your family and friends. The convenience of using the Instant Pot makes this dish a breeze to prepare, perfect for busy weeknights or leisurely weekends. The succulent chicken, bathed in the luscious orange-infused sauce, is a true testament to the magic of simple yet carefully selected ingredients. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and indulge in this tantalizing citrus twist on a classic favorite. Happy cooking and even happier eating!
