Deliciously Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake


If you’re looking for a comforting and indulgent meal that’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this Deliciously Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake is the answer. This dish combines the rich flavors of garlic, Parmesan, and tender chicken with al dente pasta, all baked to perfection for a satisfying and flavorful experience.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of chicken breast, diced
  • 16 oz of penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until it’s al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken breast and cook until it’s no longer pink in the center, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the cooked chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  5. Reduce the heat to medium and pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly. Bring the cream to a gentle simmer.

  6. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Continue to simmer the sauce, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly, about 5-7 minutes.

  7. Add the cooked chicken and penne pasta to the skillet, tossing everything together until the chicken and pasta are well coated with the creamy Alfredo sauce.

  8. Transfer the chicken Alfredo pasta mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

  10. Once out of the oven, let the pasta bake cool for a few minutes, then garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

This Deliciously Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake is a complete meal on its own, but you can elevate it even further by serving it with a side salad or some garlic bread. The creamy and indulgent flavors pair wonderfully with a crisp, refreshing salad or warm, toasty bread.

Conclusion:

With its rich, creamy sauce, tender chicken, and perfectly cooked pasta, this Deliciously Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone at the table satisfied. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a comforting homemade meal, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures in this irresistible pasta bake!
