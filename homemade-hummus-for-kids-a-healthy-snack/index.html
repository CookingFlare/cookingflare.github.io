Homemade Hummus for Kids: A Healthy Snack


Hummus is a versatile and nutritious snack that both kids and adults love. Made from simple and wholesome ingredients, it provides a great source of protein, fiber, and healthy fats. Plus, it’s easy to make at home, allowing you to control the ingredients and customize the flavors to suit your child’s taste buds.

Why Choose Homemade Hummus?

Store-bought hummus may seem convenient, but it often contains added preservatives, artificial flavors, and high levels of sodium. By making your own hummus at home, you can ensure that your child is getting a healthier and tastier alternative.

The Basic Hummus Recipe

To make a basic hummus, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons of tahini
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a food processor or blender, combine the chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed. If the mixture is too thick, you can add a little water or extra olive oil to achieve the desired consistency.
  3. Taste the hummus and adjust the seasonings according to your child’s preferences. You can add more lemon juice for a tangier flavor, or more garlic for a stronger taste.
  4. Once the hummus is smooth and well-seasoned, transfer it to a serving bowl.
  5. Serve the hummus with a variety of dippers such as carrot sticks, cucumber slices, whole grain pita bread, or even apple slices for a sweet twist.

Fun and Healthy Variations

Now that you have the basic hummus recipe, it’s time to get creative and add some fun and healthy variations to make it even more appealing to your little ones. Here are some ideas:

1. Roasted Red Pepper Hummus

  • Add 1/2 cup of roasted red peppers to the basic hummus recipe.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • The vibrant red color and slightly sweet flavor will surely entice your kids.

2. Spinach and Artichoke Hummus

  • Add 1 cup of fresh spinach leaves and 1/2 cup of canned artichoke hearts to the basic hummus recipe.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • The added vegetables provide extra nutrients and a unique taste.

3. Peanut Butter Chocolate Hummus

  • Add 2 tablespoons of peanut butter and 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder to the basic hummus recipe.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • This sweet and nutty hummus is perfect for a dessert or a special treat.

Make It a Family Activity

Making hummus can be a fun and educational activity for the whole family. Involve your kids in the process by letting them measure and pour the ingredients, and even customize their own flavors. This way, they will develop a sense of ownership and excitement for the healthy snack they helped create.

Final Thoughts

Homemade hummus is a fantastic and nutritious snack option for kids. By making it yourself, you can ensure that your child is getting a wholesome and tasty treat without any unnecessary additives. Get creative with flavors, involve your kids in the process, and enjoy the benefits of this delicious and healthy snack.

So, what are you waiting for? Grab your ingredients, put on your apron, and let’s make some homemade hummus!
