Hearty One Pot Chicken and Dumplings


One pot meals are a lifesaver when it comes to convenience and flavor. And when it comes to comfort food, nothing beats a steaming bowl of chicken and dumplings. This hearty dish is the perfect combination of tender chicken, fluffy dumplings, and a rich, flavorful broth. It’s a classic that never fails to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and brown them on all sides. This step adds a depth of flavor to the dish.

  2. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside. In the same pot, add the diced onion, sliced carrots, and celery. Sauté them for about 5 minutes, until they start to soften.

  3. Add the minced garlic to the pot and cook for another minute, until fragrant. Return the chicken to the pot, along with any juices that have accumulated.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and water, and add the dried thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir, then cover the pot and let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  5. While the chicken is simmering, prepare the dumplings. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the milk and melted butter, and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough dumplings.

  6. After the chicken has finished cooking, remove it from the pot and shred it into bite-sized pieces using two forks. Discard the bay leaf.

  7. Return the shredded chicken to the pot and bring the broth back to a simmer. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter into the simmering broth, spacing them out evenly.

  8. Cover the pot and let the dumplings cook for about 15 minutes, or until they are cooked through and fluffy. Avoid lifting the lid during this time, as it can interfere with the cooking process.

  9. Once the dumplings are cooked, give the pot a gentle stir to combine them with the chicken and broth. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  10. Serve the chicken and dumplings hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. This dish is a complete meal on its own, but you can also serve it with a side salad or crusty bread for a heartier meal.

Final Thoughts

Hearty One Pot Chicken and Dumplings is the epitome of comfort food. With its tender chicken, fluffy dumplings, and flavorful broth, it’s a dish that warms both body and soul. Plus, the convenience of cooking everything in one pot makes it a winner in terms of simplicity and ease. Whether you’re looking for a cozy weeknight dinner or a comforting dish to serve to guests, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab your pot and get cooking – you won’t be disappointed!
