Pasta with Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce: A Tangy and Creamy Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and vibrant pasta dish to elevate your dinner table, look no further than this Pasta with Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce recipe. The combination of tangy roasted red peppers and creamy sauce will surely impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more. This dish is perfect for a cozy night in or for entertaining guests with its rich flavors and beautiful presentation.

Ingredients

For the Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce:

  • 3 large red bell peppers
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pasta:

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta (linguine, fettuccine, or penne work well)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Extra Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Roasting the Red Peppers

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the whole red bell peppers on a baking sheet and drizzle with a little olive oil.
  3. Roast the peppers in the oven for 25-30 minutes, turning them occasionally until the skins are charred and blistered.
  4. Once done, transfer the roasted peppers to a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let them steam for about 10 minutes.

Step 2: Making the Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce

  1. After steaming, peel the charred skin off the peppers and remove the stems and seeds. Cut the peppers into strips.
  2. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, sautéing until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the roasted red pepper strips to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes to meld the flavors.
  4. Transfer the pepper mixture to a blender or food processor and puree until smooth.
  5. Return the pureed mixture to the skillet over low heat. Stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and keep warm over low heat while you prepare the pasta.

Step 3: Cooking the Pasta

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the pasta water.
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the roasted red pepper cream sauce. Toss the pasta gently to coat, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh basil leaves and extra Parmesan cheese.

Serving

Serve the Pasta with Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce immediately, and enjoy the delightful combination of tangy red peppers and creamy sauce coating each strand of pasta. Pair it with a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite white wine for a complete and satisfying meal.
