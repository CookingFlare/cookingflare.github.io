Easy and Healthy Low Carb Mushroom and Spinach Skillet


In today’s fast-paced world, it’s easy to get caught up in the hustle and bustle of daily life and neglect our health and well-being. One of the simplest and most effective ways to improve our overall health is by making conscious food choices. A low-carb diet can be a great way to achieve this, as it can help to reduce inflammation, improve blood sugar control, and boost energy levels.

However, it can be challenging to find delicious and easy-to-make low-carb recipes that are also healthy and nutritious. That’s why I’m excited to share with you a recipe for an Easy and Healthy Low Carb Mushroom and Spinach Skillet. This recipe is not only low in carbs, but it’s also packed with protein, fiber, and vitamins, making it a nutritious and satisfying meal option.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cups mixed mushrooms (such as cremini, shiitake, and button), sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheddar cheese (optional)
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced onion and cook until it’s translucent and starting to caramelize, about 5 minutes.

Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning.

Add the dried thyme, salt, and black pepper to the skillet and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until the flavors have melded together and the mushrooms are tender.

Add the fresh spinach leaves to the skillet and stir until they’re wilted and heated through. If using cheese, sprinkle it over the top of the skillet and stir until it’s melted and creamy.

Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them together. Pour the eggs over the top of the mushroom and spinach mixture in the skillet. Cook until the eggs are scrambled and set, about 3-4 minutes.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe more substantial, add some cooked chicken, turkey, or tofu to the skillet along with the mushrooms and spinach.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, add 1-2 tablespoons of heavy cream or Greek yogurt to the skillet along with the cheese.
  • To make this recipe more spicy, add some red pepper flakes or diced jalapeños to the skillet along with the garlic.
  • This recipe is also delicious served with some crusty low-carb bread or a side salad.

Conclusion

This Easy and Healthy Low Carb Mushroom and Spinach Skillet is a delicious and nutritious meal option that’s perfect for a quick and easy dinner. With its low carb count and high protein and fiber content, it’s a great choice for anyone looking to improve their overall health and well-being. Plus, it’s easy to customize with your favorite ingredients and spices, making it a recipe that you’ll come back to again and again.
