Flavorful Mini Taco Cups: Perfect for Taco Tuesday


Taco Tuesday is a beloved tradition for many, and what better way to celebrate than with these flavorful mini taco cups? These bite-sized treats are not only delicious but also easy to make and perfect for entertaining. Whether you’re hosting a party or looking for a fun weeknight dinner, these mini taco cups are sure to be a hit with everyone. So, let’s dive right in and learn how to make these tasty delights!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 24 wonton wrappers
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • Sliced jalapenos (optional)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a muffin tin.

  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Drain any excess grease from the skillet.

  3. Add the taco seasoning to the cooked ground beef, along with the amount of water specified on the taco seasoning packet. Stir well to combine and simmer for a few minutes until the mixture thickens slightly.

  4. Take each wonton wrapper and press it into each muffin cup, forming a cup shape. Make sure the edges of the wonton wrappers are slightly overlapping to create a sturdy base for the filling.

  5. Spoon a tablespoon of the taco meat mixture into each wonton cup, spreading it evenly.

  6. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese on top of the taco meat in each cup.

  7. Bake the mini taco cups in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the wonton wrappers are golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Remove the muffin tin from the oven and let the taco cups cool slightly.

  9. Once the taco cups have cooled, carefully remove them from the muffin tin and place them on a serving platter.

  10. Top each mini taco cup with diced tomatoes, diced red onion, chopped cilantro, a dollop of sour cream, and sliced jalapenos if desired.

  11. Serve the flavorful mini taco cups with lime wedges on the side for an added burst of citrusy flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • Get creative with the fillings! Consider adding black beans, corn, or even grilled vegetables to the taco meat mixture for extra flavor and texture.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add some hot sauce or diced jalapenos to the taco meat mixture while cooking.
  • These mini taco cups can be easily customized to suit different dietary restrictions. Swap out the ground beef for ground turkey or chicken, and use dairy-free cheese or vegan sour cream for a delicious plant-based version.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use store-bought taco seasoning instead of making your own. Just follow the instructions on the packet and adjust the amount accordingly.

Conclusion

These flavorful mini taco cups are a fun and delicious twist on traditional tacos. With their crispy wonton shell, savory taco meat, and a variety of toppings, they are sure to be a crowd-pleaser at your next Taco Tuesday gathering. So, gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and get ready to enjoy these tasty bite-sized treats!
