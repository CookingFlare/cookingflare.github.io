Irresistible Cinnamon Rolls Recipe for Breakfast Bliss


There’s something truly magical about waking up to the aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls in the morning. The soft dough infused with sweet cinnamon filling, topped with creamy icing – it’s a breakfast treat that never fails to delight. If you’re ready to elevate your breakfast game and bring a touch of sweetness to your mornings, then this irresistible cinnamon rolls recipe is just what you need.

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:

    • 1 cup warm milk
    • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
    • 2 large eggs
    • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp salt

  • For the Filling:

    • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
    • 2 1/2 tbsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened

  • For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

    • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm milk and let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
    • Add sugar, melted butter, eggs, flour, and salt to the yeast mixture. Mix until a soft dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar and cinnamon for the filling.
    • Roll out the dough into a rectangle, spread softened butter over it, and sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly.

  3. Roll and Cut:

    • Roll up the dough tightly from the long side and cut it into 12 equal pieces.
    • Place the rolls in a greased baking pan, cover them, and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  4. Bake to Perfection:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Bake the cinnamon rolls for 25-30 minutes until golden brown and fragrant.

  5. Add the Finishing Touch:

    • While the rolls are baking, make the cream cheese frosting by beating together cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
    • Once the rolls are out of the oven, let them cool slightly before generously spreading the frosting on top.

Serving Suggestion:

Serve these irresistible cinnamon rolls warm with a cup of freshly brewed coffee or a glass of cold milk. The combination of gooey cinnamon filling and creamy frosting is sure to make these rolls a breakfast favorite in no time.

Don’t wait any longer to treat yourself to a slice of breakfast bliss with these homemade cinnamon rolls. Whether you enjoy them fresh out of the oven or save them for a special weekend treat, these delightful pastries are bound to bring joy to your mornings. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of homemade cinnamon rolls. Enjoy every bite!

Remember, the key to perfecting this recipe lies in patience and precision. Let the dough rise, embrace the aromas filling your kitchen, and indulge in the simple pleasures of a homemade breakfast delight.
