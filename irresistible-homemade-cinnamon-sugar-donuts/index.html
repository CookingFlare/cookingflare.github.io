Irresistible Homemade Cinnamon Sugar Donuts


Who can resist the warm, comforting aroma of freshly baked cinnamon sugar donuts? These delightful treats are a classic favorite for breakfast, brunch, or a sweet snack any time of the day. Making them at home is not only a fun and rewarding experience but also ensures that you get to enjoy them at their freshest. In this recipe, we’ll take you through the step-by-step process of creating your own irresistible homemade cinnamon sugar donuts that are sure to become a staple in your baking repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Donuts:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted and cooled

For the Cinnamon Sugar Coating:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Donut Batter:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, nutmeg, and cinnamon.
    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, eggs, vanilla extract, and melted butter.
    • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

  2. Fry the Donuts:

    • Heat oil in a deep fryer or heavy-bottomed pot to 350°F (175°C).
    • While the oil is heating, turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and roll it out to about 1/2 inch thickness. Use a donut cutter or two differently sized round cutters to cut out the donuts and the donut holes.
    • Carefully place the donuts into the hot oil, frying 2-3 at a time, for about 2 minutes on each side or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the donuts to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.

  3. Coat the Donuts:

    • In a shallow bowl, combine the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon for the coating.
    • While the donuts are still warm, dip them into the melted butter, then roll them in the cinnamon sugar mixture until they are well coated.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Your irresistible homemade cinnamon sugar donuts are now ready to be enjoyed! Whether served warm or at room temperature, these delicious treats are sure to be a hit with family and friends.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist, try adding a hint of ground cardamom to the donut batter for an exotic flavor.
  • To make the donuts ahead of time, store them in an airtight container at room temperature and coat them with the cinnamon sugar just before serving.
  • If you don’t have buttermilk on hand, you can make a quick substitute by adding 1 tablespoon of white vinegar or lemon juice to 1 cup of milk and letting it sit for 5 minutes.

Conclusion

Homemade cinnamon sugar donuts are a delightful indulgence that brings joy to any occasion. With this simple and satisfying recipe, you can easily recreate the magic of a bakery-fresh donut right in your own kitchen. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, these irresistible treats are a must-try. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to delight your senses with the warm, sweet, and comforting flavors of homemade cinnamon sugar donuts.
