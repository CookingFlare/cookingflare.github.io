Picnic Perfect: 10 Portable Salad Recipes for Outdoor Dining


As the sun shines brightly and the weather warms up, there’s no better way to enjoy the great outdoors than with a delightful picnic. Whether you’re lounging in the park, at the beach, or in your backyard, a well-prepared picnic can elevate your outdoor experience. One of the best ways to keep your meal fresh, healthy, and satisfying is by packing portable salads that are easy to transport and delicious to eat. In this blog post, weâ€™ll explore ten scrumptious salad recipes that are perfect for outdoor dining. These salads are not only portable but also packed with flavors and nutrients, making them ideal for a picnic spread.

1. Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine quinoa and water (or broth). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes or until quinoa is fluffy and water is absorbed. Let it cool.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, olives, feta cheese, and parsley.
  3. Once the quinoa has cooled, add it to the mixing bowl.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Pack in an airtight container and refrigerate until ready to serve.

2. Chickpea Salad with Avocado Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. In a blender, combine avocado, lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour the avocado dressing over the chickpea salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with fresh cilantro and pack in a portable container.

3. Classic Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 large heirloom tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Layer the tomato and mozzarella slices on a platter or in a container, alternating each slice.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves in between the layers.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil, then sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

4. BBQ Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup corn kernels (canned or frozen)
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken, corn, black beans, and red onion. Add BBQ sauce and mix well.
  2. In a separate bowl, arrange the mixed greens.
  3. Top the greens with the BBQ chicken mixture. Drizzle with ranch dressing before serving.
  4. Pack in a large container and keep the dressing separate until you’re ready to eat.

5. Asian Noodle Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz soba noodles
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup peanuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Cook soba noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked noodles, carrots, bell pepper, green onions, cilantro, and peanuts.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and honey. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Pack in an airtight container for your picnic.

6. Spinach and Strawberry Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinaigrette

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine spinach, strawberries, almonds, and feta cheese.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette and toss gently to coat.
  3. Pack in a container and enjoy fresh outdoors.

7. Greek Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (penne or rotini)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooled pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, onion, olives, and feta cheese.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper. Pour over the salad and toss.
  4. Pack in an airtight container and refrigerate until ready to serve.

8. Roasted Vegetable and Farro Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup farro, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400Â°F (200Â°C). Toss zucchini, bell pepper, and onion with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, cook farro in broth or water according to package instructions. Drain and let cool.
  3. In a large bowl, combine roasted vegetables, farro, basil, and balsamic vinegar. Toss to combine.
  4. Pack in a portable container for a hearty picnic salad.

9. Watermelon, Feta, and Mint Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups watermelon, cubed
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine watermelon, feta cheese, and mint leaves.
  2. Drizzle with lime juice and sprinkle with salt, tossing gently to combine.
  3. This refreshing salad is best enjoyed chilled, so pack it in a cooler for your picnic.

10. Pesto Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup pesto sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. Add pesto sauce, salt, and pepper, and toss to combine.
  3. This protein-packed salad is perfect for a filling picnic meal. Pack in an airtight container.

Conclusion

With these ten portable salad recipes, your next picnic is sure to be a hit. Each recipe is easy to prepare, travel-friendly, and packed with flavor and nutrition. Remember to keep your salads chilled in a cooler if you’re heading out on a warm day. So grab your picnic blanket, pack your favorite salads, and enjoy a delightful outdoor dining experience with family and friends. Happy picnicking!
