Irresistible Peanut Butter Blondies: Chewy and Nutty


If you’re a fan of peanut butter and crave a chewy, nutty treat, then you’re in for a delightful surprise! These irresistible peanut butter blondies are the perfect combination of sweet and salty, with a dense and fudgy texture that will have you reaching for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, treating your family, or simply indulging in a solo baking session, these blondies are sure to steal the show.

Ingredients

For the Blondies:

  • 1 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional, but highly recommended)

For the Peanut Butter Drizzle (Optional):

  • 1/4 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, microwave the peanut butter and melted butter together for about 30 seconds until smooth. Stir in the brown sugar until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the peanut butter mixture, and whisk until smooth and creamy.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. If using chocolate chips, gently fold them into the batter.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.

  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly soft. Be careful not to overbake to maintain the chewy texture.

  7. While the blondies are cooling, you can prepare the peanut butter drizzle by whisking together the peanut butter, powdered sugar, and milk until smooth and pourable. Drizzle it over the cooled blondies.

  8. Once completely cooled, lift the blondies out of the pan using the parchment paper overhang and cut them into squares.

Serving Suggestions

These irresistible peanut butter blondies are delightful on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and a drizzle of warm caramel sauce. They also pair wonderfully with a glass of cold milk or a piping hot cup of coffee.

Storage

To keep these blondies fresh and chewy, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. If you have any leftovers (which is unlikely!), you can also freeze them for up to 2 months.

Final Thoughts

The rich and nutty flavor of these peanut butter blondies, combined with their chewy texture, makes them an irresistible treat for any occasion. Whether you’re a peanut butter enthusiast or simply looking for a new go-to dessert, these blondies are sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible goodness of these chewy and nutty peanut butter blondies!
