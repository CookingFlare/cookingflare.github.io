Easy Beef Enchiladas with Green Sauce


Enchiladas are a delicious and satisfying Mexican dish that can be enjoyed by the whole family. In this recipe, we will be making Easy Beef Enchiladas with Green Sauce, a flavorful twist on the classic dish. These enchiladas are filled with seasoned ground beef and topped with a tangy and vibrant green sauce. They are easy to make and perfect for a weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering with friends. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the enchiladas:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 10 flour tortillas

For the green sauce:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeds removed (optional for extra heat)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth

For garnish:

  • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Sprinkle the cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper over the beef mixture. Stir well to combine and let the flavors meld together for a few minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

  4. To make the green sauce, combine the spinach, cilantro, jalapeño pepper (if using), garlic clove, lime juice, salt, and pepper in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth, adding the chicken broth gradually until the desired consistency is reached. Set aside.

  5. Take a flour tortilla and spoon some of the beef mixture onto the center. Roll the tortilla tightly and place it seam-side down in a greased baking dish. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and beef mixture.

  6. Pour the green sauce over the enchiladas, making sure to cover them completely. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the top.

  7. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Remove from the oven and let the enchiladas cool for a few minutes. Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro, diced tomatoes, and a dollop of sour cream before serving.

  9. Serve the Easy Beef Enchiladas with Green Sauce hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • You can substitute ground beef with ground chicken or turkey for a leaner option.
  • If you prefer a spicier sauce, leave the seeds in the jalapeño pepper or add some crushed red pepper flakes.
  • Feel free to add your favorite toppings such as sliced black olives, diced avocado, or pickled jalapeños.
  • Serve the enchiladas with a side of Mexican rice and refried beans for a complete meal.

Now you have a simple and tasty recipe for Easy Beef Enchiladas with Green Sauce. These enchiladas are sure to become a family favorite and are perfect for any occasion. Enjoy the flavors of Mexico right in your own kitchen!
