Pairing Wine with Your Favorite Pizza Recipes


Pizza is one of the most beloved dishes around the world, celebrated for its versatility and bold flavors. Whether you enjoy a classic Margherita, a loaded meat feast, or a gourmet white pizza, the right wine can elevate your dining experience to new heights. In this blog post, we’ll explore various pizza recipes and suggest the perfect wine pairings to enhance each dish. So, grab your favorite bottle and let’s dive into the delicious world of pizza and wine!

The Art of Wine Pairing

Before we jump into specific pizza recipes, it’s essential to understand the basics of wine pairing. The key principle is to find a balance between the flavors of the food and the wine. Here are some general tips to keep in mind:

  1. Match Intensity: Light wines pair well with lighter dishes, while bold wines complement richer, more robust flavors.
  2. Consider Acidity: The acidity in both wine and food can enhance their flavors. A high-acid wine can cut through the richness of a fatty dish, while a low-acid wine suits milder flavors.
  3. Think About Tannins: Tannins in red wine can clash with acidic pizza sauces. Opt for lower tannin wines with tomato-based pizzas.
  4. Play with Flavors: Think about the main ingredients in your pizza—herbs, meats, and cheeses—and select a wine that complements those flavors.

Now that we have a foundation in wine pairing, let’s explore some mouth-watering pizza recipes and their ideal wine companions.

Classic Margherita Pizza

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Arrange the mozzarella slices on top and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.

Wine Pairing

A classic Margherita pizza pairs beautifully with a light, crisp white wine such as a Sauvignon Blanc. The wine’s acidity complements the tomato sauce while its herbal notes enhance the fresh basil. For those who prefer red wine, a Chianti is an excellent choice, as its bright cherry flavors and acidity work well with the pizza’s cheese and tomato.

Pepperoni Pizza

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 6 oz pepperoni slices
  • Oregano for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly, then layer on the pepperoni slices.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted.
  6. Garnish with oregano before serving.

Wine Pairing

For pepperoni pizza, a medium-bodied red wine like a Zinfandel is a fantastic match. The wine’s fruitiness balances the spiciness of the pepperoni, while its acidity cuts through the cheese. If you prefer a white wine, a dry Riesling can also work, as its sweetness provides a nice contrast to the savory flavors.

Veggie Supreme Pizza

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • Black olives
  • Spinach
  • Italian seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and layer on the vegetables.
  5. Season with Italian seasoning.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden and bubbly.

Wine Pairing

A light Pinot Grigio or an unoaked Chardonnay pairs wonderfully with veggie supreme pizza. The wine’s crisp acidity complements the freshness of the vegetables while enhancing the overall flavors. If you prefer red, a light-bodied Grenache will also work well, as its fruitiness won’t overpower the pizza.

BBQ Chicken Pizza

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Spread barbecue sauce over the dough.
  4. Layer on the shredded chicken, mozzarella cheese, and red onion slices.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Wine Pairing

A fruity and slightly sweet wine like a Merlot pairs excellently with BBQ chicken pizza. The wine’s fruitiness complements the sweetness of the barbecue sauce. Alternatively, a hoppy IPA can also work well, as the bitterness of the beer contrasts with the sweetness of the sauce and chicken.

White Pizza

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup spinach, sautéed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Spread ricotta cheese over the dough, then layer mozzarella on top.
  4. Distribute the sautéed spinach and minced garlic evenly.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Wine Pairing

A crisp and refreshing Pinot Grigio is a perfect match for white pizza. Its acidity cuts through the richness of the cheeses and complements the flavors of the garlic and spinach. If you prefer a red, consider a light-bodied Barbera, which will not overpower the delicate flavors.

Meat Lovers Pizza

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 4 oz pepperoni slices
  • 4 oz Italian sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 oz ham, diced
  • 4 oz bacon, cooked and crumbled

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese and layer on the meats.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is crispy and the cheese is bubbly.

Wine Pairing

For a meat lovers pizza, a robust Cabernet Sauvignon is a perfect choice. Its full-bodied nature and tannins complement the richness of the meats. If you’re looking for something different, a Malbec’s dark fruit flavors and smokiness can enhance the deep flavors of the pizza.

Conclusion

Pairing wine with pizza is not only about personal preference but also about exploring flavors and enhancing your overall dining experience. Each type of pizza has its own unique flavor profile, which can be beautifully complemented by the right choice of wine. Whether you prefer a light white, a bold red, or something in between, there’s a perfect wine for every pizza recipe.

So next time you whip up a delicious pizza at home, don’t forget to choose a wine that elevates your meal. Enjoy the delightful combination of flavors, and happy pairing!
