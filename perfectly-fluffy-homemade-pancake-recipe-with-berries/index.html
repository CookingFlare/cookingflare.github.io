Perfectly Fluffy Homemade Pancake Recipe with Berries


Who doesn’t love waking up to a stack of fluffy, melt-in-your-mouth pancakes? And when those pancakes are topped with sweet and juicy berries, it’s like a little slice of breakfast heaven. Today, I’m sharing with you my recipe for the perfect homemade pancakes that are guaranteed to make your mornings a little brighter.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh berries of your choice (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to tough pancakes. It’s okay if there are a few lumps in the batter.

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. If needed, lightly grease the surface with a small amount of butter or cooking spray.

  5. For each pancake, pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet. Use the back of a spoon to spread the batter into a circular shape if needed.

  6. Cook the pancakes for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles form on the surface. Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  7. Remove the pancakes from the skillet and keep warm while you cook the remaining batter.

  8. Serve the pancakes warm, topped with a generous handful of fresh berries. You can also drizzle them with maple syrup, honey, or a dollop of whipped cream for extra indulgence.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have buttermilk on hand, you can make a simple substitute by adding 1 tablespoon of vinegar or lemon juice to 1 3/4 cups of milk. Let it sit for a few minutes before using.

  • Feel free to get creative with your pancake toppings. Alongside fresh berries, you can add sliced bananas, chopped nuts, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar.

  • To make these pancakes even fluffier, you can separate the egg yolks and whites. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, then gently fold them into the batter just before cooking.

  • If you prefer a whole grain option, you can replace half of the all-purpose flour with whole wheat flour or use a gluten-free flour blend for a gluten-free version.

  • Leftover pancakes can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat them in the toaster or microwave for a quick and delicious breakfast.

Now that you have the recipe for the perfect homemade pancakes, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking! These fluffy pancakes, bursting with the natural sweetness of fresh berries, are sure to become a breakfast favorite in your household. So whip up a batch this weekend and start your day off right. Happy pancake making!
