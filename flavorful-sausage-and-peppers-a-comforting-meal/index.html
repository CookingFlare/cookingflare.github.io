Flavorful Sausage and Peppers: A Comforting Meal


Are you craving a hearty and comforting meal that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than this delicious recipe for Sausage and Peppers. With its combination of savory Italian sausage, vibrant bell peppers, and aromatic spices, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 Italian sausages (hot or sweet, depending on your preference)
  • 2 bell peppers (red and green), sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sausages and cook until browned on all sides. This will take about 5-7 minutes. Once cooked, remove the sausages from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the sliced onions and bell peppers. Sauté them for about 5 minutes or until they become tender and slightly caramelized.

  3. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent it from burning.

  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together to combine and bring the mixture to a simmer.

  5. Return the cooked sausages to the skillet, nestling them into the sauce and vegetables. Cover the skillet with a lid and let everything simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the sausages are fully cooked and the flavors have melded together beautifully.

  6. Once the sausages are cooked through, remove them from the skillet and slice them into bite-sized pieces. Return the sliced sausages to the skillet and give everything a good stir to ensure the flavors are evenly distributed.

  7. Serve the flavorful sausage and pepper mixture over cooked pasta, rice, or on a crusty roll for a delicious sandwich. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier dish, opt for hot Italian sausages instead of sweet ones. You can also add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the skillet when sautéing the vegetables.
  • For a burst of freshness, squeeze a bit of lemon juice over the finished dish before serving.
  • If you’re following a low-carb or keto diet, skip the pasta or roll and serve the sausage and peppers on a bed of cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles instead.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of sausages and peppers to find your favorite combination. Spicy sausages work well with sweet bell peppers, while milder sausages pair nicely with a mix of colorful peppers.

Conclusion

Sausage and Peppers is a classic Italian dish that never fails to impress. With its robust flavors and comforting qualities, it’s perfect for a cozy family dinner or a gathering with friends. The combination of juicy sausages, tender bell peppers, and aromatic spices creates a symphony of taste that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, grab your skillet and get cooking, because this Flavorful Sausage and Peppers recipe is a must-try!
