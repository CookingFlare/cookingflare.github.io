10 Unique and Tasty Flatbread Pizza Topping Combinations


Are you tired of the same old pizza toppings? Do you want to try something different and exciting? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore 10 unique and tasty flatbread pizza topping combinations that will take your pizza game to the next level. From sweet and savory to spicy and tangy, these toppings will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more.

1. Fig and Prosciutto

Combine the sweetness of fresh figs with the saltiness of prosciutto for a flavor explosion. Spread a layer of fig jam on your flatbread, top it with thinly sliced prosciutto, and sprinkle some crumbled goat cheese. Bake until the edges are golden brown and the cheese has melted. Finish it off with a drizzle of balsamic glaze for the perfect balance of flavors.

2. BBQ Chicken

Indulge in the smoky and tangy flavors of BBQ chicken on your flatbread pizza. Start by spreading a layer of your favorite BBQ sauce on the flatbread. Top it with cooked and shredded chicken, red onions, and a generous amount of shredded cheddar cheese. Bake until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an added burst of freshness.

3. Mediterranean Delight

Transport yourself to the shores of the Mediterranean with this flavorful flatbread pizza. Start with a layer of homemade tzatziki sauce on your flatbread. Top it with sliced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese. Sprinkle some dried oregano and a drizzle of olive oil. Bake until the edges are crispy and the cheese has melted.

4. Pesto and Sun-Dried Tomato

Experience the vibrant flavors of pesto and sun-dried tomatoes on your flatbread pizza. Spread a generous layer of pesto sauce on the flatbread, top it with sliced sun-dried tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Sprinkle some crushed red pepper flakes if you like a little heat.

5. Pear and Gorgonzola

Combine the sweetness of ripe pears with the boldness of gorgonzola cheese for a unique and delicious flatbread pizza. Spread a layer of caramelized onions on your flatbread, top it with thinly sliced pears, crumbled gorgonzola cheese, and a sprinkle of chopped walnuts. Bake until the cheese is melted and the walnuts are toasted. Drizzle with honey for a touch of sweetness.

6. Buffalo Chicken

If you love spicy food, this flatbread pizza is for you. Start by tossing cooked and shredded chicken in buffalo sauce. Spread a layer of ranch dressing on the flatbread, top it with the buffalo chicken, sliced red onions, and a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with fresh cilantro or green onions for added freshness.

7. Caprese

Experience the classic flavors of Caprese salad on your flatbread pizza. Start with a layer of marinara sauce on the flatbread. Top it with sliced tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and torn basil leaves. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and sprinkle some salt and pepper. Bake until the cheese is melted and the tomatoes are slightly roasted.

8. Smoked Salmon and Dill

Indulge in the richness of smoked salmon and the freshness of dill on your flatbread pizza. Spread a layer of cream cheese on the flatbread, top it with smoked salmon slices, thinly sliced red onions, and a sprinkle of fresh dill. Bake until the edges are crispy and the cream cheese is slightly melted. Finish it off with a squeeze of lemon juice for a burst of acidity.

9. Indian-inspired

Take a trip to India with this flavorful flatbread pizza. Start with a layer of curry sauce on the flatbread. Top it with cooked and spiced chicken, sliced red onions, bell peppers, and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella cheese. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime juice for an extra kick.

10. S’mores

Indulge your sweet tooth with a dessert-inspired flatbread pizza. Spread a layer of Nutella on the flatbread, top it with mini marshmallows, crushed graham crackers, and a sprinkle of chocolate chips. Bake until the marshmallows are golden brown and slightly toasted. Drizzle with caramel sauce or chocolate sauce for a decadent finish.

These 10 unique and tasty flatbread pizza topping combinations are sure to impress your friends and family. Whether you’re hosting a pizza night or simply craving something different, these toppings will elevate your pizza experience. So, get creative in the kitchen and start experimenting with these delicious flavor combinations. Enjoy your culinary adventure!
