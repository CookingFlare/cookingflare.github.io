Zesty Salade Lyonnaise: A French Salad with a Twist


When it comes to classic French cuisine, Salade Lyonnaise holds a special place in the hearts of food enthusiasts. Traditionally, this salad features frisée lettuce, crispy lardons, a poached egg, and a tangy vinaigrette. While the original recipe is undeniably delicious, we’re going to put a modern twist on this French favorite to create a Zesty Salade Lyonnaise that will tantalize your taste buds and elevate your salad game to a whole new level.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 head of frisée lettuce
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped chives
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Gruyère cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Frisée: Start by thoroughly washing and drying the frisée lettuce. Tear the leaves into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large mixing bowl.

  2. Cook the Lardons: Instead of the traditional lardons, we’re going to add a twist by using crispy pancetta. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the pancetta until it’s golden and crispy. Once done, remove the pancetta from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. Poach the Eggs: Fill a large saucepan with water and bring it to a gentle simmer. Crack each egg into a small bowl or cup. Create a gentle whirlpool in the water and carefully slide the eggs into the center. Poach for 3-4 minutes for a perfectly runny yolk. Once done, remove the eggs with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to drain any excess water.

  4. Assemble the Salad: Add the halved cherry tomatoes, sliced red onion, chopped parsley, chives, and croutons to the bowl of frisée lettuce. Toss everything together gently to combine.

  5. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, minced garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  6. Bringing It All Together: Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat the ingredients with the zesty dressing. Divide the salad among serving plates and sprinkle the crispy pancetta on top. Place a poached egg on each serving and finish with a generous sprinkle of grated Gruyère cheese. Season with a pinch of salt and a crack of black pepper.

  7. Enjoy the Twist: The Zesty Salade Lyonnaise is ready to be enjoyed. The combination of the tangy dressing, the richness of the poached eggs, and the crispy texture of the pancetta will take your taste buds on a delightful journey.

The Final Word

By adding a zesty and modern twist to the classic Salade Lyonnaise, we’ve elevated this French salad to a whole new level. The vibrant flavors and textures in this dish will make it a standout addition to your culinary repertoire. Whether you’re looking for a refreshing lunch option or a unique side dish for a dinner party, the Zesty Salade Lyonnaise is sure to impress. So, venture into your kitchen, embrace the twist, and savor every bite of this delightful creation.
