Grilled Pesto Chicken: Herbaceous and Delicious


If you’re looking for a flavorful and easy grilling recipe, look no further than this grilled pesto chicken. Packed with herbaceous flavors and juicy chicken, this dish is a crowd-pleaser and perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply want to elevate your weeknight dinner, this recipe will not disappoint. The combination of fresh herbs, garlic, and parmesan cheese in the pesto marinade creates a mouthwatering experience that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pesto: In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, and parmesan cheese. Pulse the ingredients until coarsely chopped. While the food processor is running, slowly add the olive oil until the mixture is smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the freshly made pesto over the chicken, ensuring that each breast is evenly coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse into the chicken.

  3. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Make sure the grill grates are clean and lightly oiled to prevent the chicken from sticking.

  4. Grill the Chicken: Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard any excess pesto. Place the chicken on the preheated grill and cook for 6-7 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has beautiful grill marks. The cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts, so it’s essential to use a meat thermometer to ensure the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes to allow the juices to redistribute. Serve the grilled pesto chicken with your favorite sides, such as a crisp green salad, roasted vegetables, or a refreshing pasta salad.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a citrusy twist, add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to the pesto marinade.
  • If you’re a fan of heat, incorporate a pinch of red pepper flakes into the pesto for a subtle kick.
  • Substitute pine nuts with walnuts or almonds for a unique flavor profile.
  • This grilled pesto chicken is also fantastic when sliced and served over a bed of mixed greens for a light and satisfying salad.

Conclusion:

Grilled pesto chicken is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed in various settings, from casual gatherings to intimate dinners. The vibrant flavors of the pesto complement the succulent chicken, creating a memorable dining experience for everyone at the table. With simple preparation and outstanding taste, this recipe is a go-to option for anyone who appreciates the natural goodness of fresh herbs and high-quality ingredients. Elevate your grilling game with this herbaceous and delicious grilled pesto chicken recipe!
