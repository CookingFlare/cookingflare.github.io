Flavorful Olive Rosemary Focaccia: A Mediterranean Twist


Welcome to our kitchen! Today, we are going to take you on a culinary journey to the Mediterranean with our Flavorful Olive Rosemary Focaccia recipe. This delightful twist on the classic Italian bread will transport your taste buds to the sun-soaked shores of the Mediterranean Sea. With the perfect combination of olives, rosemary, and a touch of sea salt, this focaccia is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. So, let’s dive right in and discover the secrets behind this mouthwatering creation.

Ingredients

To create this flavor-packed Olive Rosemary Focaccia, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 300ml warm water
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup pitted black olives, halved
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary leaves, chopped
  • Extra olive oil and sea salt for topping

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, sugar, instant yeast, and sea salt. Mix well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. Gradually add the warm water to the dry ingredients, stirring with a wooden spoon until a sticky dough forms. Then, add the olive oil and continue to mix until the dough comes together.

  3. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and knead it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment for this step.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Allow it to rise in a warm place for approximately 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.

  5. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer it to a lightly greased baking sheet and gently press it out to form a rectangular shape, about 1 inch thick.

  6. Using your fingertips, create dimples all over the surface of the dough. This will give the focaccia its characteristic texture.

  7. Scatter the halved black olives and chopped rosemary evenly over the dough, gently pressing them into the dimples. Drizzle some olive oil over the top and sprinkle with sea salt to enhance the flavors.

  8. Cover the baking sheet with the kitchen towel again and allow the dough to rest for another 30 minutes, giving it time to rise slightly.

  9. Meanwhile, preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).

  10. Once the dough has rested, place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the focaccia turns golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  11. Remove the focaccia from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for a few minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and make it easier to slice.

  12. Serve your Flavorful Olive Rosemary Focaccia warm or at room temperature. It pairs perfectly with a drizzle of balsamic vinegar and a side of fresh Mediterranean salad.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You have successfully baked your own Flavorful Olive Rosemary Focaccia with a delightful Mediterranean twist. The combination of briny black olives, fragrant rosemary, and the pillowy texture of the bread will transport you to the sun-drenched shores of the Mediterranean with every bite. Whether you serve it as an appetizer, a side dish, or enjoy it on its own, this focaccia is sure to impress your family and friends. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and let the flavors of the Mediterranean infuse your kitchen. Bon appétit!
