Chocolate Peanut Butter Blondies: A Chewy Delight


Indulge in the mouth-watering combination of chocolate and peanut butter with these delectable Chocolate Peanut Butter Blondies. These chewy treats are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth and are sure to become a favorite among friends and family. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is simple to follow and guaranteed to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar. Stir until well combined.

  3. Add the vanilla extract and eggs to the bowl, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix as this can result in dense blondies.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.

  6. Warm the peanut butter in the microwave for about 30 seconds, or until it becomes smooth and pourable. Drizzle the peanut butter over the batter in the pan.

  7. Using a butter knife or a toothpick, swirl the peanut butter into the batter, creating a marbled effect.

  8. Sprinkle the chocolate chips evenly over the top of the batter.

  9. Place the baking pan in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the blondies are golden brown around the edges and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  10. Remove the pan from the oven and allow the blondies to cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares and serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Chocolate Peanut Butter Blondies can be enjoyed on their own, but why not take them to the next level with some delicious serving suggestions?

  • Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a decadent dessert.
  • Drizzle with melted chocolate and sprinkle with crushed peanuts for an extra touch of indulgence.
  • Pair with a glass of cold milk or a hot cup of coffee for a delightful afternoon treat.

Storage

If you have any leftovers (which is unlikely!), these blondies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, you can freeze them for up to 3 months. Simply place the squares in a freezer-safe container or bag, and thaw them at room temperature when you’re ready to enjoy.

Conclusion

With their irresistible combination of chocolate and peanut butter, these Chocolate Peanut Butter Blondies are sure to be a hit with everyone who tries them. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or just want to treat yourself, this recipe is a must-try. So grab your apron, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the chewy delight of these incredible blondies. Happy baking!
