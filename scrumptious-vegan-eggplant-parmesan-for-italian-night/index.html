Scrumptious Vegan Eggplant Parmesan for Italian Night


Welcome to our kitchen, where we’re serving up a scrumptious vegan twist on a classic Italian dish - Eggplant Parmesan! This hearty and flavorful recipe is perfect for your next Italian night, and it’s sure to please even the most discerning palates. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 2 cups of bread crumbs
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 cups of marinara sauce
  • 2 cups of vegan mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Slice the eggplants into 1/2-inch thick rounds. Place the rounds on a paper towel-lined baking sheet and sprinkle them with salt. Let them sit for 15 minutes to remove any excess moisture.

  3. In a shallow bowl, combine the bread crumbs, flour, dried oregano, dried basil, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.

  4. Dip each eggplant round into the bread crumb mixture, pressing lightly to ensure the crumbs adhere to the eggplant. Place the coated eggplant rounds onto the prepared baking sheet.

  5. Bake the eggplant rounds for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Flip them halfway through cooking to ensure even browning.

  6. While the eggplant is baking, heat the marinara sauce in a saucepan over medium heat until heated through.

  7. Once the eggplant rounds are done baking, remove them from the oven and reduce the oven temperature to 375°F (190°C).

  8. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of baked eggplant rounds on top, followed by a layer of marinara sauce and vegan mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all the eggplant rounds are used, ending with a layer of marinara sauce and vegan mozzarella cheese on top.

  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  10. Remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

  11. Serve this scrumptious vegan Eggplant Parmesan with a side of spaghetti or a green salad for a complete Italian-inspired meal. Buon appetito!

Final Thoughts

This Scrumptious Vegan Eggplant Parmesan is a delightful alternative to traditional Parmesan dishes. The crispy breaded eggplant rounds, combined with the rich marinara sauce and melty vegan mozzarella cheese, create a symphony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this dish is a must-try. So, gather your loved ones, light some candles, and enjoy a delicious Italian night with this delightful and satisfying dish. Mangia!

Note: If you have any leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. To reheat, simply place the desired portion in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 15-20 minutes, or until heated through.
