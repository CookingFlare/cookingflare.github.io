Rocky Road Brownies: A Delicious Combination of Chocolate, Marshmallows, and Nuts


If you’re a fan of indulgent desserts that combine the rich flavors of chocolate, the gooey goodness of marshmallows, and the satisfying crunch of nuts, then you’re in for a treat with this Rocky Road Brownies recipe. These decadent brownies are the perfect blend of fudgy, chewy, and crunchy, making them an irresistible delight for any chocolate lover. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply craving a sweet pick-me-up, these brownies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

For the Brownies:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Topping:

  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans work well)

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Brownie Batter

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in short intervals until fully melted.
  3. In a separate mixing bowl, combine the melted butter with granulated sugar and mix until well combined.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the butter-sugar mixture, and beat until smooth.
  5. Sift in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt, and fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

Step 2: Baking the Brownies

  1. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs. Do not overbake, as you want the brownies to be fudgy.

Step 3: Adding the Topping

  1. Once the brownies are done baking, remove them from the oven and immediately sprinkle the mini marshmallows over the top.
  2. Return the pan to the oven for 2-3 minutes to allow the marshmallows to soften and puff up.
  3. Remove the pan from the oven again and sprinkle the chocolate chips over the marshmallows.
  4. Let the residual heat from the brownies melt the chocolate chips for a minute, then use a spatula to spread the melted chocolate evenly over the marshmallows.
  5. Finally, sprinkle the chopped nuts over the melted chocolate, gently pressing them into the surface.

Step 4: Cooling and Serving

  1. Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before slicing them into squares.
  2. These Rocky Road Brownies are best enjoyed when completely cooled, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of indulgence, drizzle some caramel sauce over the finished brownies.
  • If you prefer a different nut, feel free to substitute with almonds, peanuts, or any other favorite nut.
  • To enhance the chocolate flavor, consider adding a tablespoon of espresso powder to the brownie batter.

In Conclusion

These Rocky Road Brownies are a delightful combination of textures and flavors, making them a surefire hit at any gathering or as a special treat for yourself. The fudgy, chocolatey base pairs perfectly with the gooey marshmallows and crunchy nuts, creating a sensory experience with every bite. Whether you’re an experienced baker or just starting out, this recipe is a great way to impress your friends and family with a crowd-pleasing dessert. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible decadence of Rocky Road Brownies!
