Slow Cooker Chicken Enchilada Recipes for Mexican Flair


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine but don’t always have the time to prepare elaborate meals, slow cooker chicken enchiladas are here to save the day! With their rich flavors and tender chicken, these enchiladas will transport your taste buds straight to Mexico. Plus, the convenience of using a slow cooker means you can set it and forget it, allowing you to go about your day while dinner simmers away. So, let’s dive into some delicious slow cooker chicken enchilada recipes that will add a Mexican flair to your dinner table.

Classic Slow Cooker Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) corn kernels, drained
  • 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, place the chicken breasts, diced onion, and red bell pepper.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the black beans, corn kernels, enchilada sauce, shredded cheddar cheese, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker, ensuring it covers the chicken evenly.
  4. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, shred it using two forks and mix it well with the sauce in the slow cooker.
  6. Warm the flour tortillas and spoon the chicken mixture onto each tortilla, rolling them up tightly.
  7. Place the rolled enchiladas in a baking dish and pour any remaining sauce from the slow cooker over the top.
  8. Sprinkle some shredded cheddar cheese on top.
  9. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Serve hot and garnish with fresh cilantro, sour cream, and sliced jalapeños if desired.

Green Chile Slow Cooker Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cans (4 ounces each) diced green chilies
  • 1 can (10 ounces) green enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, place the chicken thighs, diced onion, diced green chilies, green enchilada sauce, chicken broth, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  3. Once the chicken is cooked, shred it using two forks and mix it well with the sauce in the slow cooker.
  4. Warm the corn tortillas and spoon the chicken mixture onto each tortilla, rolling them up tightly.
  5. Place the rolled enchiladas in a baking dish.
  6. Sprinkle shredded Monterey Jack cheese on top.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve hot and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Vegetarian Slow Cooker Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) corn kernels, drained
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine the black beans, corn kernels, diced onion, diced red bell pepper, red enchilada sauce, shredded cheddar cheese, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Cook on low for 4-6 hours or on high for 2-3 hours, until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Warm the flour tortillas and spoon the vegetable mixture onto each tortilla, rolling them up tightly.
  4. Place the rolled enchiladas in a baking dish.
  5. Optional: Pour some extra enchilada sauce over the top.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with fresh cilantro, sour cream, and sliced jalapeños if desired.

Enjoy these delicious slow cooker chicken enchiladas with your loved ones, and let the flavors of Mexico fill your kitchen. With these easy-to-follow recipes, you can satisfy your cravings for Mexican cuisine without spending hours in the kitchen. So, get your slow cooker ready and indulge in the delightful combination of tender chicken, zesty sauce, and warm tortillas. ¡Buen provecho!
