Hearty Low Carb Coconut Curry Chicken


Are you looking for a delicious and hearty low carb meal that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than this amazing recipe for Coconut Curry Chicken. This dish is a perfect balance of creamy coconut, aromatic spices, and tender chicken, creating a meal that’s satisfying and nutritious. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply want to try something new, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet, stirring constantly for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until they are no longer pink on the outside.

  4. Sprinkle the curry powder and turmeric over the chicken, stirring to coat the pieces evenly.

  5. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth, stirring well to combine all the ingredients. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  6. Add the sliced red and yellow bell peppers to the skillet, stirring to incorporate them into the curry.

  7. Cover the skillet and let the curry simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors have melded together.

  8. Season the curry with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning as needed.

  9. Serve the Coconut Curry Chicken hot, garnished with fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness.

Nutritional Information

  • Total Fat: 18g
  • Cholesterol: 96mg
  • Sodium: 278mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 9g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 3g
  • Protein: 33g

Why This Recipe?

This Coconut Curry Chicken recipe is not only delicious but also fits perfectly into a low-carb lifestyle. The combination of protein-packed chicken, healthy fats from coconut milk, and the array of colorful vegetables makes this dish a well-rounded meal. The warm and aromatic spices like curry powder and turmeric not only add flavor but also have potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory properties.

The versatility of this dish makes it a great option for meal prepping. You can easily store it in airtight containers and reheat for a quick and satisfying meal throughout the week. Additionally, this recipe can be customized to suit various dietary preferences by adjusting the spice level or adding different vegetables like broccoli or spinach.

In Conclusion

With its rich and creamy texture, vibrant colors, and bold flavors, this Coconut Curry Chicken is a must-try for anyone looking to add more excitement to their low-carb cooking repertoire. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this dish is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor a taste of the exotic with this delightful low carb coconut curry chicken recipe!
