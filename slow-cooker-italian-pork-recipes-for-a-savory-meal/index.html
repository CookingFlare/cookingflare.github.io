Slow Cooker Italian Pork Recipes for a Savory Meal


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen preparing elaborate meals? Do you crave the rich flavors of Italian cuisine but don’t have the time to slave over the stove? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore some delicious and easy-to-make slow cooker Italian pork recipes that will satisfy your cravings and leave you with a savory meal.

1. Slow Cooker Italian Pulled Pork

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork shoulder, trimmed of excess fat
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the pork shoulder in the slow cooker and season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  2. In a bowl, mix together chicken broth, tomato sauce, balsamic vinegar, and minced garlic.
  3. Pour the mixture over the pork shoulder in the slow cooker.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 8-10 hours or on high heat for 4-5 hours, until the pork is tender and easily falls apart.
  5. Once cooked, remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  6. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and mix it with the sauce.
  7. Cook for an additional 30 minutes on low heat to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Serve the pulled pork on toasted buns or over steamed rice. Enjoy!

2. Slow Cooker Italian Pork Roast

Ingredients:

  • 3 pounds pork loin roast
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the pork loin roast in the slow cooker and season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  2. In a bowl, mix together chicken broth, red wine, olive oil, and minced garlic.
  3. Pour the mixture over the pork loin roast in the slow cooker.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the pork is cooked through and tender.
  5. Once cooked, remove the pork from the slow cooker and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  6. Serve the sliced pork roast with the sauce from the slow cooker, drizzling it over the meat. Pair it with roasted vegetables or a fresh green salad for a complete meal.

3. Slow Cooker Italian Pork Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork stew meat, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the pork stew meat in the slow cooker and season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  2. Add chopped onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery, minced garlic, diced tomatoes (with their juices), chicken broth, red wine, and tomato paste to the slow cooker.
  3. Stir everything together to combine.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the pork is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.
  5. Serve the Italian pork stew in bowls, garnished with fresh parsley. Serve with crusty bread or pasta for a hearty and delicious meal.

Conclusion

With these slow cooker Italian pork recipes, you can enjoy the flavors of Italy without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether it’s the tender and flavorful pulled pork, the juicy and succulent pork roast, or the hearty and comforting pork stew, each dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you wanting more. So dust off your slow cooker and get ready to impress your family and friends with these delightful Italian-inspired creations. Buon appetito!
