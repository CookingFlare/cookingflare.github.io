Paleo Sweet Potato Fries Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


If you’re following a Paleo diet or simply looking for a healthier alternative to regular fries, sweet potato fries are a fantastic option. Not only are they incredibly tasty, but they’re also grain-free, making them suitable for those with dietary restrictions. In this post, we’ll explore five delicious and easy-to-make Paleo sweet potato fries recipes that will satisfy your cravings without compromising your health. So grab your sweet potatoes and let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Oven-Baked Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and peel the sweet potatoes, then cut them into evenly-sized fries.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, sea salt, and black pepper.
  4. Add the sweet potato fries to the bowl and toss them until they’re well-coated with the seasoning mixture.
  5. Spread the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the fries are crispy and golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven, let them cool slightly, and serve immediately.

2. Spicy Cajun Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tablespoon of Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and peel the sweet potatoes, then cut them into fries.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the melted coconut oil, Cajun seasoning, sea salt, and cayenne pepper.
  4. Add the sweet potato fries to the bowl and toss them until they’re evenly coated with the seasoning mixture.
  5. Arrange the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the fries are crispy and lightly browned.
  7. Let the fries cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy the spicy kick!

3. Garlic Parmesan Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of ghee, melted
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and peel the sweet potatoes, then cut them into fries.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the melted ghee, minced garlic, grated Parmesan cheese, and sea salt.
  4. Add the sweet potato fries to the bowl and toss them until they’re well-coated with the garlic Parmesan mixture.
  5. Spread the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the fries are crispy and the cheese is melted and golden.
  7. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with fresh parsley, and serve immediately.

4. Rosemary and Sea Salt Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of avocado oil
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh rosemary leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and peel the sweet potatoes, then cut them into fries.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the avocado oil, chopped rosemary leaves, sea salt, and black pepper.
  4. Add the sweet potato fries to the bowl and toss them until they’re evenly coated with the rosemary-infused oil.
  5. Arrange the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the fries are crispy and lightly browned.
  7. Sprinkle with a little extra sea salt and serve immediately. Enjoy the aromatic rosemary flavor!

5. Maple Cinnamon Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil, melted
  • 2 tablespoons of pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and peel the sweet potatoes, then cut them into fries.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the melted coconut oil, maple syrup, ground cinnamon, and sea salt.
  4. Add the sweet potato fries to the bowl and toss them until they’re well-coated with the maple cinnamon mixture.
  5. Spread the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the fries are crispy and caramelized.
  7. Let the fries cool for a few minutes before serving. Indulge in the sweet and spicy combination!

These five Paleo sweet potato fries recipes offer a variety of flavors to satisfy every craving. Whether you’re a fan of classic seasonings, spicy Cajun flavors, or sweet and savory combinations, there’s a recipe here for you. So go ahead and enjoy these delicious and grain-free alternatives to regular fries. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!

Remember, creativity is encouraged with these recipes. Feel free to adjust the seasonings and experiment with different flavors to suit your personal preferences. Happy cooking and bon appétit!
