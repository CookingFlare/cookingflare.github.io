Speedy Honey Garlic Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen after a long day at work? Look no further! This Speedy Honey Garlic Chicken Stir-Fry recipe is here to save the day. With its flavorful combination of sweet honey and savory garlic, this dish is sure to please your taste buds and satisfy your hunger in no time. Plus, it’s packed with nutritious ingredients that will keep you feeling energized and ready to take on the world.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas)

Instructions

  1. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and cook until they are browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil and minced garlic. Cook for about 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, cornstarch, and water until well combined. Pour the sauce mixture into the skillet with the garlic and stir well.

  4. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and toss everything together to coat the chicken and vegetables with the sauce.

  5. Continue cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken and vegetables evenly.

  6. Remove from heat and serve hot over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with chopped green onions or sesame seeds for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the amount of honey and garlic to suit your taste preferences. You can also add some heat by including a teaspoon of red pepper flakes or Sriracha sauce.

  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the chicken with tofu or tempeh. The cooking time may vary slightly, so adjust accordingly.

  • To make it gluten-free, use tamari instead of soy sauce and ensure that all other ingredients are gluten-free as well.

  • This recipe is perfect for meal prep. Make a double batch and store the leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat on the stovetop or in the microwave for a quick and delicious meal.

Conclusion

In just a few easy steps, you can whip up this Speedy Honey Garlic Chicken Stir-Fry recipe and have a delicious and nutritious meal on the table in no time. With its flavorful sauce and tender chicken, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. So the next time you’re pressed for time but still want a homemade meal, give this recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed!
