Homemade Pies: Sweet and Savory Recipes for Two


Are you craving a delicious homemade pie but find yourself hesitating because most recipes yield large quantities? Well, worry no more! In this blog post, we will be sharing some delightful sweet and savory pie recipes specifically designed for two people. Whether you’re looking to satisfy your sweet tooth or indulge in a savory delight, we have got you covered.

Sweet Pies

1. Classic Apple Pie

There’s nothing quite like the comforting aroma of a freshly baked apple pie. This classic recipe is perfect for a cozy evening with your loved one. Start by preparing the crust using flour, salt, butter, and ice water. For the filling, toss thinly sliced apples with sugar, cinnamon, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Fill the pie crust with the apple mixture, cover it with another layer of crust, and bake until golden brown and bubbling. Serve it warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra touch of decadence.

2. Chocolate Pecan Pie

Indulge your sweet tooth with this rich and chocolatey pecan pie. Begin by making the crust using crushed graham crackers, melted butter, and a touch of sugar. For the filling, combine eggs, corn syrup, brown sugar, melted chocolate, and a generous amount of pecans. Pour the mixture into the crust and bake until set. Allow it to cool before slicing and savoring each bite of this irresistible dessert.

3. Mixed Berry Galette

If you’re a fan of rustic desserts, this mixed berry galette will surely steal your heart. To create the flaky crust, combine flour, sugar, and butter until crumbly. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet. In a bowl, mix together your favorite combination of fresh berries, a sprinkle of sugar, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Pile the berry mixture onto the center of the crust, leaving a border around the edges. Fold the edges of the crust over the berries and bake until golden brown. Serve warm with a dusting of powdered sugar.

Savory Pies

1. Chicken Pot Pie

Warm up your evenings with this comforting and savory chicken pot pie. Begin by sautéing diced chicken breast, onions, carrots, and peas in a skillet until cooked through. In a separate saucepan, melt butter and whisk in flour to create a roux. Slowly add chicken broth and milk, stirring constantly until thickened. Combine the cooked chicken and vegetables with the creamy sauce, season with salt and pepper, and transfer the filling into a prepared pie crust. Cover with another layer of crust, cut slits to allow steam to escape, and bake until golden brown and bubbling. Allow it to cool slightly before serving.

2. Spinach and Feta Pie

For a vegetarian option bursting with flavor, try this spinach and feta pie. Start by wilting fresh spinach in a pan with a drizzle of olive oil. In a separate bowl, mix together crumbled feta cheese, beaten eggs, chopped dill, and a pinch of nutmeg. Combine the cooked spinach with the feta mixture and pour into a prepared pie crust. Bake until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown. This savory pie is perfect for a light lunch or dinner.

3. Beef and Mushroom Hand Pies

If you’re in the mood for something hearty and handheld, these beef and mushroom hand pies are a must-try. Begin by browning ground beef in a skillet and then add diced onions, sliced mushrooms, and minced garlic. Season with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs. Roll out pie dough and cut it into circles. Spoon the beef and mushroom mixture onto one half of each circle, fold the dough over, and crimp the edges to seal. Bake until the crust is golden brown and enjoy these delicious handheld pies on the go.

With these sweet and savory pie recipes designed for two, you can now enjoy the pleasure of homemade pies without worrying about leftovers. Whether you have a sweet tooth or crave something savory, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings and create lasting memories. So, put on your apron, preheat your oven, and let the aroma of freshly baked pies fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
