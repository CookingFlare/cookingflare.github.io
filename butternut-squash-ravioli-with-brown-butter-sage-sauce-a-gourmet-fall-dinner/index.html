Butternut Squash Ravioli with Brown Butter Sage Sauce: A Gourmet Fall Dinner


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in hearty and flavorful dishes that warm the soul. And one dish that perfectly captures the essence of the season is Butternut Squash Ravioli with Brown Butter Sage Sauce. This gourmet recipe brings together the earthy sweetness of butternut squash, the richness of brown butter, and the aromatic flavors of sage, creating a truly unforgettable dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups roasted butternut squash puree
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 package store-bought wonton wrappers
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 8-10 fresh sage leaves
  • Additional grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the roasted butternut squash puree, ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, nutmeg, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  2. Lay out the wonton wrappers on a clean, dry surface. Spoon about a teaspoon of the butternut squash filling onto the center of each wonton wrapper. Brush the edges of the wrappers with water, then fold them over to create a triangle shape. Press the edges firmly to seal the ravioli. Repeat this process until all the filling is used.

  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Carefully drop the ravioli into the boiling water and cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until they float to the top. Remove the cooked ravioli from the pot using a slotted spoon and set aside.

  4. In a separate saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Add the fresh sage leaves and cook until the butter turns a golden brown color and the sage leaves are crispy. Be careful not to burn the butter. Remove the pan from the heat.

  5. Pour the brown butter sage sauce over the cooked ravioli, gently tossing to coat them evenly. Serve the ravioli hot, garnished with additional grated Parmesan cheese for extra flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • To save time, you can use pre-made butternut squash puree instead of roasting and pureeing the squash yourself.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, substitute vegetable broth for the chicken broth in the brown butter sage sauce.
  • For an added touch of elegance, sprinkle some toasted pine nuts or crispy pancetta over the ravioli before serving.
  • This recipe can easily be doubled or tripled to serve a larger crowd or to have leftovers for later.

Conclusion

Butternut Squash Ravioli with Brown Butter Sage Sauce is the epitome of a gourmet fall dinner. The flavors and textures come together harmoniously, creating a dish that is both comforting and impressive. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a special meal for yourself, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you longing for more. So, grab your apron, gather the ingredients, and get ready to delight in the deliciousness of this autumn-inspired masterpiece.
