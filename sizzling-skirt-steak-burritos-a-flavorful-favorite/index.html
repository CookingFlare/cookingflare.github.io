Sizzling Skirt Steak Burritos: A Flavorful Favorite


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, you’re in for a treat with these sizzling skirt steak burritos. This savory and satisfying dish is a crowd-pleaser, perfect for family dinners, casual gatherings, or even a solo indulgence. The combination of juicy skirt steak, flavorful seasonings, and fresh ingredients wrapped in a warm tortilla creates a culinary experience that’s hard to resist. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering delight.

Ingredients

For the Skirt Steak Marinade:

  • 1.5 pounds skirt steak
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Burrito Filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional Ingredients:

  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sour cream, salsa, and guacamole (optional, for serving)

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Skirt Steak

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, lime juice, minced garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the skirt steak in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over the steak, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to infuse the meat.

Step 2: Prepare the Burrito Filling

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and bell peppers, and sauté until they are tender and slightly caramelized.
  3. Season the vegetables with cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper, stirring to combine the flavors.
  4. Transfer the cooked vegetables to a plate and set aside.

Step 3: Grill the Skirt Steak

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan over high heat.
  2. Remove the skirt steak from the marinade and pat it dry with paper towels to remove excess marinade.
  3. Grill the steak for 3-4 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  4. Once cooked, transfer the steak to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it against the grain into thin strips.

Step 4: Assemble the Burritos

  1. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or in the microwave to make them pliable.
  2. In the center of each tortilla, layer some cooked rice, black beans, grilled skirt steak, sautéed vegetables, and shredded cheddar cheese.
  3. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up tightly to form a burrito.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Place the assembled burritos seam side down on a hot skillet or grill pan to lightly toast the tortilla and melt the cheese.
  2. Transfer the burritos to a serving plate and garnish with chopped cilantro.
  3. Serve with optional sides of sour cream, salsa, and guacamole for dipping or drizzling.

Conclusion

Sizzling skirt steak burritos are a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that are sure to tantalize the taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is approachable and rewarding, offering a satisfying meal that’s bursting with savory goodness. The tender, marinated skirt steak combined with the colorful medley of vegetables and warm tortilla creates a harmonious blend that will leave you craving more. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and treat yourself to the joy of savoring these delightful, sizzling skirt steak burritos!
