Vegan Apple Pie: A Traditional Dessert Made Animal-Friendly


There’s something undeniably comforting about the aroma of a freshly baked apple pie wafting through the air. The combination of tart apples, warm spices, and buttery crust is a classic that has stood the test of time. But what if you want to enjoy this traditional dessert without the use of animal products? Enter the Vegan Apple Pie – a delicious twist on a beloved classic that is both cruelty-free and incredibly satisfying. In this recipe, we will explore how to create a mouthwatering Vegan Apple Pie from scratch. So grab your apron and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup vegan margarine, cold
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice-cold water

For the filling:

  • 6-8 medium-sized apples (use Granny Smith for a tart flavor or Gala for a sweeter taste)
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves

Instructions

Preparing the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Cut the cold vegan margarine into small cubes and add it to the flour mixture.

  2. Using a pastry cutter or your hands, work the margarine into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice-cold water, one tablespoon at a time, while mixing the dough with a fork. Continue adding water until the dough comes together and can be formed into a ball.

  4. Divide the dough into two equal portions and shape them into disks. Wrap each disk in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Preparing the Filling

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Peel, core, and slice the apples into thin wedges. Place them in a large mixing bowl.

  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves.

  4. Pour the sugar mixture over the sliced apples and toss until the apples are evenly coated.

Assembling and Baking the Pie

  1. Remove one of the chilled dough disks from the refrigerator and roll it out on a lightly floured surface to fit the size of your pie dish.

  2. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to the pie dish and press it gently into the bottom and sides.

  3. Pour the apple filling into the prepared pie crust, spreading it evenly.

  4. Roll out the second chilled dough disk and place it over the apple filling. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie.

  5. Make several slits on the top crust to allow steam to escape while baking.

  6. Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake in the preheated oven for 45-55 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

  7. Once baked, remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool on a wire rack before serving.

Serving and Enjoying

Vegan Apple Pie is best enjoyed warm, topped with a scoop of vegan vanilla ice cream or a dollop of coconut whipped cream. The combination of the tender, spiced apples and flaky crust is sure to satisfy any dessert craving.

Conclusion

With this Vegan Apple Pie recipe, you can enjoy a traditional dessert without compromising your commitment to a cruelty-free lifestyle. This recipe proves that vegan baking can be just as delicious and indulgent as its non-vegan counterparts. So gather your loved ones, savor the flavors of fall, and indulge in a slice of this delectable Vegan Apple Pie. Happy baking!
