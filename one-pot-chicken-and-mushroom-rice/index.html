One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Rice


Are you looking for a simple and flavorful meal that requires minimal effort and cleanup? Look no further than this One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Rice recipe! This dish is a perfect combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and fluffy rice, all cooked in one pot for a fuss-free cooking experience. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 oz (225g) cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups (300g) long-grain white rice
  • 3 1/2 cups (875ml) chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken thighs with salt and pepper. In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken thighs and cook until they are golden brown on both sides. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté them until they are soft and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes. Then, add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are golden and slightly caramelized.

  3. Once the mushrooms have cooked down, add the rice to the pot. Stir the rice for a minute to coat it with the flavors from the pot.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and add the dried thyme. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low. Place the chicken thighs on top of the rice, cover the pot, and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the chicken is cooked through.

  5. Once the rice has absorbed all the liquid and the chicken is tender, remove the pot from the heat. Allow it to rest, covered, for a few minutes before serving.

  6. To serve, fluff the rice with a fork, and divide it among serving plates. Place a chicken thigh on top of each serving, and garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Convenience

The beauty of this One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Rice recipe lies in its simplicity and convenience. By cooking everything in one pot, you’ll save time on both prep and cleanup. This makes it an ideal dish for busy weeknights when you want a delicious homemade meal without the hassle of multiple pots and pans.

Flavorful and Nutritious

The combination of chicken, mushrooms, and rice creates a hearty and satisfying meal. The mushrooms add an earthy richness to the dish, while the chicken infuses the rice with savory flavors. Additionally, this recipe is highly adaptable, allowing you to incorporate additional vegetables or herbs to suit your taste preferences.

Family-Friendly

This recipe is a crowd-pleaser, making it perfect for family dinners or gatherings with friends. The familiar flavors and comforting nature of this dish will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Versatility

While this recipe calls for chicken thighs, you can easily substitute them with chicken breasts or even bone-in chicken pieces. You can also customize the dish by adding different types of mushrooms, such as shiitake or oyster mushrooms, to elevate the flavor profile.

In Conclusion

One Pot Chicken and Mushroom Rice is a fantastic dish that brings together the convenience of one-pot cooking and the delightful flavors of tender chicken and earthy mushrooms. Whether you’re a novice cook or a seasoned chef, this recipe is sure to become a household favorite. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of this comforting meal. Happy cooking!
