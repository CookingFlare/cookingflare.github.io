Savory BBQ Breakfast Hash: A Hearty Start to the Day


Have you ever wished for a breakfast that doesn’t conform to the traditional boundaries of morning meals? A glorious concoction that delivers bold flavors, hearty textures, and satisfies your morning cravings? Look no further! This savory BBQ breakfast hash is the perfect start to your day. It’s a little bit sweet, a little bit savory, and wholly delicious.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

From the moment you wake up to the aroma of sizzling ingredients, you’ll be fighting the clock to devour this hearty meal. Not your ordinary morning fare, this breakfast hash is:

  • Nutritious: Loaded with fresh vegetables, lean pork, and nutrient-dense potatoes.
  • Versatile: Enjoy the dish as is, or serve it as a breakfast burrito with a fluffy egg inside.
  • Customizable: Feel free to substitute meat and vegetables to your heart’s content.
  • Delicious: Need we say more? Infused with BBQ flavor, this breakfast hash is anything but boring.

It’s breakfast time with an edge! So, gather your ingredients, apron, and appetite, and let’s get cooking.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to whip up this incredible meal:

  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 2 medium-sized Russet potatoes
  • 1 pound of ground pork (or any meat of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons BBQ sauce
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these easy steps to make a mouthwatering BBQ breakfast hash:

  1. Dice the Onion and Potatoes: Once the veggies are prepped, set them aside.
  2. Cook the Potatoes: Boil the potatoes until fork-tender (about 10 minutes). Then, drain them and set them aside.
  3. Sauté the Onion: In a large skillet or cast iron pan, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened and slightly caramelized (6-8 minutes).
  4. Add the Protein: Remove the onions from heat and set them aside. In the same pan, cook the ground pork, breaking it up with a spoon or spatula. Cook until it reaches a nice brown color.
  5. Combine the Ingredients: Put the cooked onions back into the pan with the pork. Add in the potatoes, 2 tablespoons BBQ sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Mash the potatoes a little to create a chunky hash.
  6. Simmer: Turn the heat to low and allow the hash to simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent burning. If the hash becomes too dry, feel free to add some water or BBQ sauce to maintain a soft and moist texture.
  7. Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro, if you’d like. This hearty hash goes well with toast, fried eggs, or in breakfast burritos.

Now, indulge in the savory goodness you’ve created! This BBQ breakfast hash is an innovative, nutrient-dense dish that’s perfect for an exhilarating start to your day.

Remember, the beauty of this recipe lies in its adaptability. Feel free to experiment with different types of meat, vegetables, or spices. Try it out and savor the results!
