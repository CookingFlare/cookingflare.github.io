Satisfying Loaded BBQ Chicken Fries Recipe


Are you ready for a flavor explosion that will leave your taste buds craving for more? Look no further than these mouthwatering Loaded BBQ Chicken Fries! This recipe takes classic fries to a whole new level by adding tender shredded chicken, tangy barbecue sauce, and a generous amount of gooey melted cheese. Whether you’re hosting a game night, a casual get-together, or simply craving a comforting meal, these loaded fries are guaranteed to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large russet potatoes, washed and dried
  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Step 1: Preparing the Fries

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cut the potatoes into 1/4-inch thick fries, leaving the skin on for added texture.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the fries with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  4. Arrange the fries in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden and crispy.

Step 2: Assembling the Loaded BBQ Chicken Fries

  1. While the fries are baking, combine the shredded chicken and barbecue sauce in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir until the chicken is evenly coated and heated through.
  2. Once the fries are done, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a serving platter or individual plates.
  3. Spoon the warm barbecue chicken over the crispy fries, ensuring that each fry gets a generous portion of chicken.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the chicken, allowing the heat from the fries and chicken to melt the cheese.
  5. Garnish the loaded fries with diced red onion, fresh cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream.

Step 3: Serving and Enjoying

  1. Serve the Loaded BBQ Chicken Fries immediately while they’re hot and gooey.
  2. Encourage everyone to dig in and enjoy the irresistible combination of flavors and textures.
  3. Don’t be surprised if these loaded fries disappear within minutes – they’re that good!

Tips and Variations:

  • For a spicy kick, add sliced jalapeños or a drizzle of hot sauce before serving.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as Monterey Jack or pepper jack, for a unique twist.
  • If you’re short on time, use frozen fries and rotisserie chicken as a convenient shortcut.

Final Thoughts:

The beauty of Loaded BBQ Chicken Fries lies in their simplicity and versatility. Whether you’re a fan of barbecue chicken, cheesy fries, or just good food in general, this recipe has something for everyone. It’s a crowd-pleaser that can be customized to suit your taste preferences, making it an ideal choice for casual gatherings or laid-back weeknight dinners. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a plate of pure comfort and satisfaction with these Loaded BBQ Chicken Fries!
