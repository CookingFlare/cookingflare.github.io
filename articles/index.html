Savor the Flavors of Greece with Greek Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp

Are you ready to embark on a culinary journey to Greece? Get ready to savor the flavors of the Mediterranean with this mouthwatering recipe for Greek Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp. Bursting with tangy lemon, fragrant garlic, and a medley of aromatic herbs, this dish will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Greece. Ingredients 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined 4 cloves of garlic, minced 2 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil 1 lemon, juiced and zested 1 teaspoon of dried oregano 1 teaspoon of dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon of dried rosemary Salt and pepper to taste Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish) Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large mixing bowl, combine the minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, dried oregano, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the bowl, making sure they are coated evenly with the marinade. Let the shrimp marinate for at least 20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Once the shrimp has marinated, transfer them to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Arrange the shrimp in a single layer to ensure even cooking. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast the shrimp for 8-10 minutes, or until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become rubbery. Once the shrimp are cooked, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley for an extra pop of color and flavor. Serve the Greek Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp as an appetizer, main course, or even as a topping for a vibrant Greek salad. The possibilities are endless! The Taste of Greece With each bite of these succulent Greek Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp, you’ll be transported to the sun-soaked shores of Greece. The zesty lemon and fragrant garlic infuse the shrimp with a burst of freshness, while the medley of dried herbs adds depth and complexity to the dish. The roasting process intensifies the flavors, resulting in a tantalizing combination that will leave you craving more. ...
