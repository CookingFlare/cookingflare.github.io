Mummy Pizza Bites: A Quick and Easy Halloween Snack Recipe


Are you ready to spook up your Halloween party with a delicious and adorable treat? Look no further than these Mummy Pizza Bites! Perfect for kids and adults alike, these spooky snacks are not only a breeze to make but also incredibly tasty. Whether you’re hosting a Halloween bash or just looking for a fun and festive snack to enjoy with your family, these Mummy Pizza Bites are sure to be a hit. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to impress your guests with these eerie yet irresistible treats.

Ingredients

  • 1 tube of refrigerated pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced black olives
  • Sliced red bell pepper
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

  2. Unroll the refrigerated pizza dough and cut it into small squares, approximately 2x2 inches. These will be the bases for your Mummy Pizza Bites.

  3. Place the dough squares on the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each one.

  4. Spoon a small amount of pizza sauce onto each dough square and spread it out evenly, leaving a small border around the edges.

  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, covering the dough completely.

  6. Now, it’s time to create the mummy look! Take the sliced black olives and arrange them on top of the cheese to resemble the mummy’s eyes. You can get creative here and make different expressions for each mummy.

  7. Next, take the sliced red bell pepper and create the mummy’s “bandages” by laying them across the dough in a crisscross pattern, leaving space for the eyes.

  8. Lightly spray the mummy pizza bites with cooking spray. This will help the crust to turn golden brown and crispy in the oven.

  9. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.

  10. Once the Mummy Pizza Bites are out of the oven, let them cool for a few minutes before serving. These little mummies are best enjoyed warm and fresh out of the oven.

Tips and Tricks

  • If you want to make these Mummy Pizza Bites even more festive, you can use different colored bell peppers or even add other toppings like pepperoni or mushrooms.
  • Feel free to get creative with the “bandage” pattern – you can make them look neat and tidy or go for a more messy, unraveling mummy look.
  • These bites are perfect for a Halloween party, but they also make a fun and tasty after-school snack for the kids.

Conclusion

With just a few simple ingredients and a bit of creativity, you can whip up a batch of Mummy Pizza Bites that are sure to impress. Whether you’re getting into the Halloween spirit or just craving a fun and delicious snack, these little mummies are a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to delight your taste buds with these adorable and tasty Mummy Pizza Bites! Happy Halloween!
