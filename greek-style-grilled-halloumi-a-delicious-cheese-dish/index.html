Greek Style Grilled Halloumi: A Delicious Cheese Dish


Are you a cheese lover? If so, you’re in for a treat! Today, I want to share with you a mouthwatering recipe for Greek Style Grilled Halloumi. This dish is a true delight for cheese enthusiasts and will transport you to the sunny shores of Greece with every bite.

What is Halloumi?

Halloumi is a unique cheese that originates from Cyprus. It has a distinctive texture that is both firm and chewy, making it perfect for grilling. What sets Halloumi apart from other cheeses is its high melting point, allowing it to retain its shape even when exposed to heat. This makes it an excellent choice for grilling, as it develops a beautiful golden crust while maintaining its deliciously creamy interior.

The Ingredients

To make Greek Style Grilled Halloumi, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 250 grams of Halloumi cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

The Preparation

  1. Start by preheating your grill or grill pan on medium-high heat. It’s essential to ensure that the grill is hot before adding the Halloumi to achieve that perfect sear.

  2. While the grill is heating up, slice the Halloumi cheese into thick slices, about half an inch in thickness. This size ensures that the cheese holds its shape while grilling.

  3. Once the grill is hot, brush both sides of the Halloumi slices with olive oil. This step not only prevents sticking but also adds a lovely flavor to the cheese.

  4. Carefully place the Halloumi slices onto the grill and let them cook for approximately 2-3 minutes on each side. The cheese should develop a beautiful char and a crispy exterior while remaining soft and gooey on the inside.

  5. While the Halloumi is grilling, cut the lemon in half and squeeze the juice over the cheese slices. The tangy acidity of the lemon adds a refreshing zing that perfectly complements the richness of the cheese.

  6. Sprinkle some freshly ground black pepper over the grilled Halloumi to enhance its flavor further. The peppery notes will add a subtle kick to every mouthful.

  7. Once the Halloumi is perfectly grilled and seasoned, carefully transfer it to a serving platter. If desired, garnish with fresh mint leaves to add a burst of freshness and color to the dish.

Serving and Enjoying

Greek Style Grilled Halloumi is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions:

  • Serve the grilled Halloumi as an appetizer or as part of a mezze platter. Pair it with olives, roasted red peppers, and warm pita bread for a delightful Mediterranean feast.

  • Use the grilled Halloumi as a filling for sandwiches or wraps. Its unique texture and salty flavor make it an excellent substitute for meat or other cheeses.

  • Add the grilled Halloumi to salads for an extra burst of flavor and texture. Its charred exterior and creamy interior will take your salad to a whole new level.

  • Enjoy the Halloumi on its own as a snack. Its addictive taste and satisfying chewiness make it the perfect nibble to satisfy your cheese cravings.

Conclusion

Greek Style Grilled Halloumi is a cheese lover’s dream come true. Its unique texture, deliciously creamy center, and golden crust make it a true delight for the taste buds. Whether you enjoy it as an appetizer, in a sandwich, or on top of a salad, this dish will transport you to the sunny shores of Greece with its authentic flavors. So, fire up your grill and get ready to indulge in this mouthwatering cheese dish. Opa!
