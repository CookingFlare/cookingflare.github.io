Vegan S'mores Bars: A Twist on the Classic Campfire Treat


If you’re a fan of the classic campfire treat, S’mores, but follow a vegan lifestyle, then you’re in for a delicious surprise! These Vegan S’mores Bars are a delightful twist on the traditional recipe, offering all the gooey marshmallow and chocolate goodness you love, without any animal products. They’re easy to make, incredibly satisfying, and perfect for sharing with friends and family. So gather ‘round the virtual campfire, and let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups vegan graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 2 cups vegan marshmallows
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted vegan butter, and granulated sugar. Mix well until the mixture resembles coarse sand.

  3. Press the crust mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking dish, creating an even layer. Use the back of a spoon or your fingers to press it firmly.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.

  5. While the crust is cooling, prepare the filling. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the vegan marshmallows, vegan chocolate chips, almond milk, and creamy peanut butter.

  6. Microwave the filling mixture in 30-second intervals, stirring well after each interval, until the marshmallows and chocolate chips are fully melted and well combined. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  7. Pour the melted filling over the cooled crust, spreading it evenly with a spatula or the back of a spoon.

  8. Place the baking dish in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 2 hours, or until the filling is set.

  9. Once the Vegan S’mores Bars are chilled and set, remove them from the refrigerator and cut them into squares or rectangles.

  10. Serve the bars immediately and enjoy the gooey, chocolatey, and marshmallow goodness!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, drizzle some melted vegan chocolate on top of the bars before serving.

  • If you don’t have graham cracker crumbs, you can make your own by crushing vegan graham crackers in a food processor or placing them in a plastic bag and using a rolling pin to crush them.

  • Feel free to experiment with different flavors of vegan chocolate chips, such as dark chocolate or mint chocolate, to add a unique twist to the bars.

  • If you prefer a nut-free version, you can substitute the almond milk with another non-dairy milk of your choice, such as soy milk or oat milk.

  • These Vegan S’mores Bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. However, they’re so delicious that they might not last that long!

So there you have it, a vegan-friendly twist on the classic campfire treat, S’mores. These Vegan S’mores Bars are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you wanting more. Whether you’re enjoying them at a backyard barbecue or a cozy night in, they’re the perfect treat for any occasion. Give them a try and let us know what you think. Happy indulging!
