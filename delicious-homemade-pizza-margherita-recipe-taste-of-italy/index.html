Delicious Homemade Pizza Margherita Recipe - Taste of Italy


There’s something undeniably special about the taste of authentic Italian cuisine. The rich flavors, fresh ingredients, and time-honored recipes come together to create a culinary experience that is truly unmatched. One such classic dish that embodies the essence of Italian cooking is the Pizza Margherita. This simple yet exquisite pizza is a celebration of the flavors of Italy, featuring the vibrant colors of the Italian flag with its use of tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. In this blog post, we’ll take you through the steps to create your own delicious Homemade Pizza Margherita, allowing you to savor the taste of Italy from the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 3 1/2 cups (440g) of all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/3 cups (320ml) of warm water
  • 1 teaspoon of active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

For the Toppings:

  • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) of tomato passata
  • 2 cups (240g) of fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Pizza Dough

  • In a large bowl, combine the warm water and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  • Add the flour, olive oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir until a dough forms.
  • Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.

2. Preheat the Oven

  • Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can reach, typically around 500°F (260°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.

3. Shape the Dough

  • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal portions. Shape each portion into a ball and let them rest for 15 minutes.

4. Assemble the Pizza

  • Take one of the dough balls and, using your hands, gently stretch and shape it into a round disc, about 12 inches (30cm) in diameter.
  • Transfer the shaped dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet or a pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
  • Spread half of the tomato passata over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Arrange the sliced mozzarella on top of the tomato sauce.
  • Season with salt and pepper, then drizzle with a little olive oil.

5. Bake the Pizza

  • If using a pizza stone, carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated stone in the oven. If using a baking sheet, place the sheet in the oven.
  • Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

6. Garnish and Serve

  • Once the pizza is out of the oven, tear the fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the hot pizza.
  • Drizzle with a little more olive oil, slice, and serve immediately.

The Taste of Italy, Right in Your Kitchen

There you have it – your very own Homemade Pizza Margherita, straight from the heart of Italy. The simplicity of the ingredients allows the flavors to shine through, making each bite a delightful experience. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, looking for a fun family cooking activity, or simply craving a taste of Italy, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, dust off your apron, and transport yourself to the sun-soaked streets of Naples with this iconic Italian dish. Buon appetito!
