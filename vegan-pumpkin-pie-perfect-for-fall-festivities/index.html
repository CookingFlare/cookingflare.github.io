Vegan Pumpkin Pie: Perfect for Fall Festivities


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in delicious and comforting treats. And what better way to celebrate the season than with a vegan pumpkin pie? This classic dessert is a staple at fall gatherings and will surely impress your friends and family. The best part? It’s completely plant-based, making it a guilt-free indulgence for everyone. So, grab your apron and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup of melted vegan butter
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup

For the filling:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) of pumpkin puree
  • ¾ cup of coconut milk
  • ½ cup of brown sugar
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons of pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9-inch pie dish.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted vegan butter, and maple syrup. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated.

  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom and sides of the prepared pie dish, forming an even crust. Use the back of a spoon or your fingers to press firmly.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, coconut milk, brown sugar, cornstarch, pumpkin pie spice, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.

  5. Pour the filling into the prepared crust, spreading it evenly.

  6. Place the pie dish in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.

  7. Once baked, remove the pie from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack. This will allow the filling to set properly.

  8. Once cooled, refrigerate the pie for at least 2 hours to ensure it is fully chilled and set.

  9. Serve chilled and garnish with a dollop of coconut whipped cream, a sprinkle of cinnamon, or a drizzle of maple syrup, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a gluten-free version, use gluten-free graham cracker crumbs or replace them with crushed gluten-free cookies of your choice.
  • Experiment with different spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, or cloves to add a unique twist to the traditional pumpkin pie flavor.
  • If you prefer a sweeter pie, increase the amount of brown sugar or add a tablespoon or two of maple syrup to the filling mixture.
  • To make individual-sized pies, use a muffin tin and adjust the baking time accordingly.

Now that you have the perfect vegan pumpkin pie recipe, it’s time to gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and embrace the flavors of fall. Whether you’re hosting a fall-themed dinner party or simply craving a sweet treat, this vegan pumpkin pie is sure to be a hit. Enjoy the warm and comforting flavors of pumpkin, spices, and a buttery crust, all while knowing you’re indulging in a cruelty-free and plant-based dessert. Happy baking and happy fall festivities!
