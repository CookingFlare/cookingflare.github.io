Spicy Shrimp Cocktail with Mango and Avocado: A Refreshing and Healthy Snack


If you’re looking for a refreshing and healthy snack that packs a punch of flavor, look no further than this Spicy Shrimp Cocktail with Mango and Avocado recipe. This zesty dish combines succulent shrimp, juicy mango, creamy avocado, and a spicy kick that will leave your taste buds begging for more. Whether you’re hosting a summer party or simply want to treat yourself to a delicious snack, this recipe is sure to impress. So let’s dive in and learn how to make this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup of diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of freshly chopped cilantro
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked shrimp, diced mango, diced avocado, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, jalapeño pepper, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Gently toss to mix all the ingredients together.

  2. Taste the mixture and adjust the seasoning according to your preference. If you like it spicier, add more cayenne pepper or chopped jalapeño. If you prefer it milder, reduce the amount of jalapeño or omit it altogether.

  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Once chilled, give the mixture a final stir and transfer it to a serving dish.

  5. Serve the Spicy Shrimp Cocktail with Mango and Avocado with tortilla chips on the side for dipping. The crispiness of the chips complements the creamy avocado and adds a delightful crunch to each bite.

  6. Enjoy this refreshing and healthy snack with family and friends, or savor it all to yourself!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Healthy and Nutritious: Shrimp is a great source of lean protein, while mango and avocado provide essential vitamins and minerals. This dish is not only delicious but also good for your body.

  • Vibrant and Refreshing: The combination of juicy mango, creamy avocado, and zesty lime juice creates a burst of refreshing flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.

  • Versatile: This recipe can be served as an appetizer, snack, or even a light meal. It’s perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a party or enjoying a quiet night at home.

  • Easy to Make: With just a few simple steps, you can whip up this Spicy Shrimp Cocktail in no time. It requires minimal effort but delivers maximum flavor.

  • Customizable: Feel free to adjust the spice level to suit your preferences. Add more heat if you’re a spice lover, or tone it down if you prefer a milder flavor.

Final Thoughts

This Spicy Shrimp Cocktail with Mango and Avocado is a delightful combination of flavors that will take your taste buds on a culinary adventure. The heat from the jalapeño and cayenne pepper, the sweetness of the mango, and the creaminess of the avocado create a perfect balance of taste and texture. Whether you serve it as an appetizer or a snack, this dish is sure to impress your guests and leave them wanting more. So grab your ingredients, put on your chef’s hat, and get ready to indulge in this refreshing and healthy snack that will elevate your culinary repertoire. Enjoy!
