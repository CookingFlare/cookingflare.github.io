Grilling Sweet and Savory: Dessert Recipes


Who says grilling is only for savory dishes? Fire up your grill and get ready to indulge in some mouthwatering sweet and savory dessert recipes. These unique creations will impress your family and friends at your next backyard gathering. From grilled fruit skewers to decadent grilled chocolate sandwiches, this collection of recipes will take your dessert game to the next level. So, grab your apron and let’s get grilling!

Grilled Fruit Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Pineapple chunks
  • Strawberries
  • Bananas
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Thread the pineapple chunks, strawberries, and bananas onto skewers.
  3. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear.
  4. Remove the skewers from the grill and drizzle with honey.
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon over the top.
  6. Serve the grilled fruit skewers warm and enjoy the burst of flavors!

Grilled Pound Cake with Berries and Cream

Ingredients:

  • Pound cake slices
  • Fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Whipped cream
  • Mint leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Place the pound cake slices on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear.
  3. Remove the grilled pound cake slices from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  4. Top each slice with a generous amount of fresh berries.
  5. Dollop whipped cream on top of the berries.
  6. Garnish with mint leaves, if desired.
  7. Serve the grilled pound cake with berries and cream as a delightful summer dessert.

Grilled Banana S’mores

Ingredients:

  • Ripe bananas
  • Marshmallows
  • Chocolate bars
  • Graham crackers, crushed

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Slice the bananas lengthwise, leaving the skin intact.
  3. Fill the banana slices with marshmallows and chocolate bars.
  4. Wrap each banana in aluminum foil, ensuring it is tightly sealed.
  5. Place the foil-wrapped bananas on the grill and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the marshmallows are melted and gooey.
  6. Carefully remove the foil-wrapped bananas from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  7. Unwrap the foil and sprinkle crushed graham crackers over the top.
  8. Serve the grilled banana s’mores as a fun and delicious twist on the classic campfire treat.

Grilled Chocolate Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • Bread slices
  • Chocolate spread
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Spread a generous amount of chocolate spread on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Sandwich the bread slices together, chocolate side in.
  4. Butter the outer sides of the bread slices.
  5. Place the grilled chocolate sandwiches on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the bread is toasted and the chocolate is melted.
  6. Remove the grilled chocolate sandwiches from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  7. Cut the sandwiches into halves or quarters for easy serving.
  8. Serve the grilled chocolate sandwiches as a decadent treat for chocolate lovers.

Conclusion

Grilling isn’t just for burgers and steaks anymore. These sweet and savory dessert recipes will elevate your grilling experience and leave your taste buds craving for more. So, fire up your grill, gather your ingredients, and prepare to impress your guests with these delightful creations. Happy grilling and enjoy the sweet and savory goodness!
