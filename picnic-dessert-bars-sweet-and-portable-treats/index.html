Picnic Dessert Bars: Sweet and Portable Treats


Are you planning a delightful picnic under the warm sun? Don’t forget to pack some delicious treats to satisfy your sweet tooth! Picnic dessert bars are the perfect portable delights that will leave you and your loved ones craving for more. Whether it’s a family outing, a romantic date, or a gathering with friends, these sweet bars are sure to be a hit. In this blog post, we will share some mouthwatering picnic dessert bar recipes that are easy to make, travel well, and are guaranteed to impress.

Recipe 1: Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the crushed graham crackers and melted butter until well mixed. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of a greased baking dish.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the creamy peanut butter and powdered sugar until smooth. Spread this mixture evenly over the graham cracker crust.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the chocolate chips in short intervals, stirring in between, until smooth. Pour the melted chocolate over the peanut butter layer and spread it evenly.
  4. Refrigerate the bars for about 1-2 hours or until the chocolate is set. Once set, cut the bars into squares or rectangles.
  5. Pack them in an airtight container, layering them with parchment paper to prevent sticking. Enjoy these delectable chocolate peanut butter bars during your picnic!

Recipe 2: Lemon Raspberry Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen raspberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish and set it aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, powdered sugar, and softened butter. Mix until crumbly, then press the mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  3. Bake the crust for 15-20 minutes or until lightly golden. Remove from the oven and set aside.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, granulated sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest, and flour until well combined. Gently fold in the raspberries.
  5. Pour the lemon-raspberry mixture over the baked crust, spreading it evenly. Return the dish to the oven and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes or until the filling is set.
  6. Allow the bars to cool completely before cutting them into squares or rectangles. Pack them up for your picnic, and enjoy the zesty flavors of these lemon raspberry bars!

Recipe 3: S’mores Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish and set it aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the crushed graham crackers and melted butter until well combined. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  3. Sprinkle the mini marshmallows evenly over the graham cracker crust, followed by the chocolate chips.
  4. Bake the bars for 10-15 minutes or until the marshmallows are golden and puffy, and the chocolate is melted.
  5. Allow the bars to cool completely before cutting them into squares or rectangles. These gooey and irresistible s’mores bars are perfect for sharing during your picnic adventures!

Conclusion

Picnic dessert bars are the ultimate portable treats that will add a touch of sweetness to your outdoor adventures. From the indulgent chocolate peanut butter bars to the refreshing lemon raspberry bars and the nostalgic s’mores bars, these recipes offer a variety of flavors to suit every palate. Make a batch of these delightful bars, pack them up, and create lasting memories while enjoying the great outdoors. Happy picnicking and happy indulging!
