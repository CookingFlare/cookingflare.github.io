The Best Cookie Recipes for Kids' Birthday Parties


When it comes to planning a kid’s birthday party, one of the most important aspects is the food. And what’s more popular among kids than cookies? In this article, we will explore the best cookie recipes that are perfect for kids’ birthday parties.

Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Who doesn’t love a classic chocolate chip cookie? This recipe is a staple for any kid’s birthday party and is sure to be a hit with both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix in the flour mixture until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until lightly golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Sugar Cookies with Sprinkles

Who says sugar cookies have to be boring? Adding sprinkles to these classic cookies makes them perfect for a kid’s birthday party.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Colored sprinkles for decorating

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix in the flour mixture until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
  6. Divide dough into 2 equal parts and mix in a few drops of food coloring to make pink and blue dough.
  7. Roll out each half of the dough on a floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
  8. Cut into desired shapes using a cookie cutter.
  9. Place on the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 1 inch of space between each cookie.
  10. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until lightly golden brown.
  11. Remove from oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  12. Decorate with sprinkles as desired.

Peanut Butter Blossoms

These peanut butter cookies are perfect for kids who love peanut butter. The Hershey’s Kiss on top adds a delicious surprise to each cookie.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • Hershey’s Kisses, unwrapped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together peanut butter and butter until smooth.
  4. Gradually mix in sugars until well combined.
  5. Beat in egg.
  6. Gradually mix in the flour mixture until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
  7. Roll dough into balls, about 1 inch in diameter.
  8. Place on the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until lightly golden brown.
  10. Remove from oven and immediately press a Hershey’s Kiss into the center of each cookie.
  11. Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

These oatmeal raisin cookies are a healthier option for kids and are perfect for a birthday party.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, oats, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix in the flour mixture until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
  6. Stir in raisins.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until lightly golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Conclusion

These cookie recipes are sure to be a hit at any kid’s birthday party. From classic chocolate chip to oatmeal raisin, there’s something for every kid’s taste. So why not try one of these recipes out and see what your kids think?
