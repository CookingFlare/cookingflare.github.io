Yummy Keto Creamy Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy keto-friendly dish that’s quick and easy to prepare, then this Yummy Keto Creamy Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe is just what you need. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is perfect for those following a low-carb lifestyle or anyone who simply enjoys a mouthwatering seafood meal. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Begin by rinsing the shrimp under cold water and patting them dry with a paper towel. Season the shrimp with a pinch of salt and black pepper.

  2. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic. Pour in the chicken broth and allow it to simmer for a couple of minutes.

  4. Add the Cream and Flavor: Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Let the mixture simmer gently for 5-7 minutes until it begins to thicken slightly. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.

  5. Combine the Shrimp and Sauce: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss them gently in the creamy lemon garlic butter sauce, ensuring that each shrimp is coated evenly.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Once the shrimp are heated through, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the dish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 320
  • Total Fat: 22g
  • Cholesterol: 283mg
  • Sodium: 561mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Protein: 25g

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Yummy Keto Creamy Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe is a winner for several reasons. Not only is it incredibly flavorful and satisfying, but it’s also low in carbohydrates, making it an ideal choice for those following a keto lifestyle. The combination of creamy, tangy, and savory flavors in the rich sauce perfectly complements the tender, succulent shrimp, creating a dish that’s both indulgent and wholesome. Additionally, this recipe can be whipped up in under 30 minutes, making it a fantastic option for a quick weeknight dinner or a special weekend meal.

In Conclusion

Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or simply someone who appreciates a well-balanced, flavorful dish, this Yummy Keto Creamy Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. With its enticing aroma and delectable taste, this dish is bound to impress your family and friends, all while keeping you on track with your keto lifestyle.
