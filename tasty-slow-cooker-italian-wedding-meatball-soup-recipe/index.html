Tasty Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Meatball Soup Recipe


If you’re looking for a comforting and hearty meal that’s effortless to prepare, look no further than this Tasty Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Meatball Soup Recipe. Combining tender meatballs, fresh vegetables, and a rich broth, this dish is perfect for a cozy night in or when you’re entertaining guests. The best part? It simmers away in your slow cooker, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully while you go about your day.

What is Italian Wedding Soup?

Italian Wedding Soup is a traditional Italian-American dish that features a delicious combination of meatballs, greens, and pasta, all simmered in a savory broth. The name might suggest a wedding, but it actually refers to the “marriage” of flavors found in this delightful soup. While there are many variations of this dish, our slow cooker version simplifies the cooking process while still delivering the same hearty taste.

Ingredients

To make this Tasty Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Meatball Soup, gather the following ingredients:

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef (or a blend of beef and pork)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs (oregano, basil, thyme)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the Soup:

  • 6 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cups fresh spinach (or kale)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 cup small pasta (such as orzo or acini di pepe)
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Preparation Steps

1. Make the Meatballs

Start by making the meatballs. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, minced garlic, dried herbs, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

Once the mixture is ready, roll it into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. This recipe should yield approximately 20-24 meatballs.

2. Brown the Meatballs (Optional)

For an extra depth of flavor, consider browning the meatballs in a skillet over medium-high heat until they are golden brown on all sides. This step is optional, but it adds a lovely caramelized flavor to the meatballs. If you choose to skip this step, don’t worry! The meatballs will still be delicious cooked directly in the soup.

3. Prepare the Slow Cooker

In your slow cooker, add the diced onion, carrots, and celery. These vegetables will form a flavorful base for the soup. Pour the chicken broth over the vegetables, followed by the diced tomatoes (with their juices) and the dried Italian herbs.

4. Add the Meatballs

Gently place the meatballs into the slow cooker, ensuring they are submerged in the broth. This will help them cook evenly and absorb all the wonderful flavors of the soup.

5. Cook the Soup

Cover the slow cooker and set it to low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours. The long cooking time allows the flavors to deepen and the meatballs to become tender.

6. Add the Pasta and Greens

About 30 minutes before serving, stir in the pasta and fresh spinach (or kale). If you’re cooking on low, this is the time to switch to high heat to ensure the pasta cooks through in time. Allow the soup to cook until the pasta is al dente.

7. Season and Serve

Before serving, taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnishing each serving with fresh chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

1. Convenience of Slow Cooking

One of the biggest advantages of this recipe is the convenience of using a slow cooker. You can set it and forget it, allowing you to focus on other tasks while your soup simmers away.

2. Flavorful and Nutritious

This soup is packed with flavor and nutrients. The combination of fresh vegetables, lean meat, and greens makes it a wholesome choice for your family.

3. Versatile

Feel free to customize this soup to your liking. You can swap out the meat for turkey or chicken, use different types of greens, or even add beans for extra protein and fiber.

4. Perfect for Meal Prep

This soup freezes beautifully, making it an excellent option for meal prep. Portion it out into freezer-safe containers, and you’ll have delicious homemade soup ready to go for those busy weeknights.

Pairing Suggestions

To elevate your dining experience, consider pairing this Tasty Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Meatball Soup with:

  • Garlic Bread: The perfect accompaniment to soak up that delicious broth.
  • Mixed Green Salad: A refreshing side of greens to balance the hearty soup.
  • A Glass of Red Wine: A light Chianti or Sangiovese complements the flavors of the soup beautifully.

Conclusion

This Tasty Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Meatball Soup Recipe is not only a delightful dish but also a testament to the joy of cooking with loved ones. The ease of preparation, combined with the comforting flavors, makes it a must-try for any home cook. So grab your slow cooker, gather your ingredients, and indulge in a bowl of this hearty soup that’s sure to warm your soul. Whether it’s a chilly evening or a special gathering, this soup will surely impress and satisfy everyone at your table. Enjoy!
