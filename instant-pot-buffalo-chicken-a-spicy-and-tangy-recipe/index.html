Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken: A Spicy and Tangy Recipe


Are you looking for a way to spice up your cooking routine without spending hours in the kitchen? Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken is the perfect solution! This dish combines the bold kick of buffalo sauce with tender, juicy chicken to create a meal that’s versatile, packed with flavor, and easy to prepare. Whether you’re hosting game night, meal prepping for the week, or simply craving something zesty, this recipe is sure to please.

What Makes This Recipe Special?

Buffalo chicken is famous for its bold, tangy flavor and just the right amount of heat. This Instant Pot rendition takes things a step further by offering incredible convenience without compromising on taste. The Instant Pot helps lock in the moisture while infusing the chicken with deep, savory flavors, ensuring a perfectly cooked dish every time.

One of the best things about Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken is its versatility. You can serve it as a main dish, use it in wraps, spread it over pizza, pile it onto buns for sandwiches, or even toss it with pasta for a spicy twist. Moreover, the recipe is highly customizable to cater to your spiciness level preference—just adjust the amount of buffalo sauce!

Ingredients You’ll Need

Before you get started, gather all the ingredients to make this dish flavorful and unforgettable. Here’s what you need:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1 cup of buffalo sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • ½ cup of chicken broth (or water for an alternative)
  • 2 tablespoons of butter (unsalted)
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional garnish: chopped green onions, ranch or blue cheese dressing, and celery sticks

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Preparation

Start by rinsing and patting dry your chicken. If you want smaller portions or quicker cook time, feel free to cut the chicken into chunks or strips. Sprinkle your chicken with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper to ensure every bite is brimming with flavor.

2. Setting Up the Instant Pot

Set your Instant Pot to Sauté Mode. Once heated, add the butter and allow it to melt completely. Butter helps add richness to the buffalo sauce, balancing its tangy spice. Place the seasoned chicken in the pot and sear each side for 2–3 minutes. While this step is optional, it helps lock in flavors and creates a nice base for the dish.

3. Creating the Sauce Base

Pour the chicken broth into the Instant Pot, scraping the bottom to deglaze the pan and loosen up any browned bits. Browning adds depth to the final flavor of the sauce. Next, pour over the buffalo sauce. Stir gently to ensure the sauce is evenly distributed across the chicken.

4. Pressure Cooking

Lock the lid into place and set the valve to Sealing Mode. Then select the Manual Pressure Cook option and set the timer for 12 minutes (15 minutes for larger chicken pieces). The Instant Pot works its magic, infusing the chicken with bold buffalo flavors while keeping it perfectly tender.

5. Natural Release

Once the pressure cooking cycle ends, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes. Carefully switch the valve to Venting Mode to fully release any remaining steam before opening the lid. Don’t skip the natural release—it prevents the chicken from overcooking by gradually lowering the pressure inside the pot.

6. Shredding the Chicken

Using tongs, remove the chicken onto a cutting board or large plate. Use two forks to shred the chicken; it should be tender enough to pull apart effortlessly. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and stir well, ensuring the pieces get coated with the creamy buffalo sauce.

Serving Suggestions

There are endless ways to enjoy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken. Here are some ideas to spark creativity in your kitchen:

  1. Buffalo Chicken Sliders: Load the shredded buffalo chicken onto soft slider buns. Top with ranch dressing, a sprinkle of green onions, or a drizzle of extra buffalo sauce for added heat.

  2. Buffalo Chicken Wraps: Grab a flour tortilla and fill it with buffalo chicken, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheddar cheese. Fold it tightly, and you’ve got a quick and delicious meal.

  3. Game Night Nachos: Spread tortilla chips on a baking sheet, top them generously with buffalo chicken, shredded cheese, and jalapeños. Bake until the cheese is melty, then drizzle with ranch dressing.

  4. Buffalo Chicken Pasta: Toss the buffalo chicken with cooked pasta and a dollop of cream cheese or sour cream for a creamy, spicy dish. Garnish with parsley and grated parmesan.

  5. Buffalo Chicken Salad: Add the shredded chicken atop a bed of crisp romaine lettuce, celery, and carrots. Drizzle with your favorite dressing for a light yet satisfying option.

Storage and Reheating Tips

One of the greatest advantages of this buffalo chicken recipe is how well it stores. Place the cooled buffalo chicken in an airtight container and store it in the refrigerator for up to four days. You can also freeze portions for up to three months.

To reheat, simply warm the chicken on the stovetop or pop it in the microwave. Add a splash of buffalo sauce or chicken broth to restore its original moisture and flavor.

Customizations to Fit Your Taste

The beauty of this dish lies in its adaptability. Whether you’re a fan of classic buffalo spice or want to tone it down, here’s how you can tweak the recipe to fit your preferences:

  • Adjust the Heat: For a milder version, use equal parts buffalo sauce and barbecue sauce to temper the spice. If you love heat, add a dash of cayenne pepper or chili flakes to amp things up!
  • Add Sweetness: Stir in a tablespoon of honey or brown sugar for a sweet-spicy balance. This trick goes especially well if you plan to serve the chicken in wraps or sandwiches.
  • Creamy Twist: Mix a few tablespoons of cream cheese into the buffalo sauce post-cooking for a creamy buffalo flavor.

Why Use an Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot simplifies cooking by serving as your go-to pressure cooker, sauté pan, and slow cooker—all in one. It drastically reduces cooking time without sacrificing flavor and texture. For dishes like buffalo chicken, it ensures the perfect balance of tenderness and sauciness. Say goodbye to hours of cooking and dishwashing, and hello to effortless homemade meals.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken recipe is the ultimate crowd-pleaser. It combines convenience, bold flavors, and endless serving possibilities. Whether you’re a busy parent, a beginner in the kitchen, or a seasoned foodie looking for a new twist on a classic favorite, this recipe is sure to earn a place in your regular rotation.

So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy a dish that packs a punch in every bite. With minimal prep and maximum flavor, this buffalo chicken is bound to become one of your go-to recipes for busy weeknights, gatherings, and everything in between.
