Delicious Sticky Toffee Pudding for a Sweet Christmas Finale


The holiday season is a time for indulgence, and what better way to satisfy your sweet tooth than with a decadent dessert that will leave your guests in awe? This Christmas, treat yourself and your loved ones to a luscious and irresistible Sticky Toffee Pudding. This classic British dessert is a perfect finale to any festive feast, and its rich, moist texture and indulgent toffee sauce will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and let’s dive into the delightful world of Sticky Toffee Pudding!

Ingredients

For the Pudding:

  • 200g pitted dates, chopped
  • 250ml boiling water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 80g unsalted butter, softened
  • 180g brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

For the Toffee Sauce:

  • 200g brown sugar
  • 150ml heavy cream
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Pudding

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a 9-inch square baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, pour the boiling water over the chopped dates and let them sit for 5-10 minutes to soften.
  3. After the dates have softened, use a blender or food processor to puree the dates and water until smooth. Add in the vanilla extract and set aside.
  4. In a separate bowl, cream together the softened butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, until well combined.
  5. Sift the flour, baking powder, and baking soda into the wet ingredients and fold gently until just combined.
  6. Stir in the date puree until evenly distributed.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top with a spatula.

Step 2: Baking the Pudding

  1. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the pudding is firm to the touch and a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
  2. Once baked, remove the pudding from the oven and set aside to cool slightly while you prepare the toffee sauce.

Step 3: Making the Toffee Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, combine the brown sugar, heavy cream, butter, and salt. Place the pan over medium heat and stir until the sugar has dissolved and the sauce is smooth and slightly thickened.
  2. Let the toffee sauce simmer for a few minutes until it reaches a rich, caramel color.
  3. Remove the sauce from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

Step 4: Serving the Sticky Toffee Pudding

  1. Using a skewer or fork, poke several holes in the warm pudding.
  2. Pour a generous amount of the warm toffee sauce over the pudding, allowing it to seep into the holes and drizzle over the top.
  3. Serve the Sticky Toffee Pudding warm, with an extra drizzle of toffee sauce and a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream if desired.

Conclusion

There you have it – a show-stopping Sticky Toffee Pudding that will make your Christmas celebration truly memorable. The combination of moist, date-infused sponge and decadent toffee sauce is a match made in dessert heaven. This sweet and sticky delight is sure to leave everyone at the table with smiles on their faces and contented sighs. So, this holiday season, elevate your Christmas finale with this irresistible dessert that will have your guests begging for the recipe. Happy baking and Merry Christmas!
