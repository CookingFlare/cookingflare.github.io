Creamy Low Carb Chicken Alfredo


If you’re looking for a comforting and satisfying meal without the guilt, this creamy low carb chicken Alfredo recipe is just what you need. Packed with flavor and low in carbs, this dish is perfect for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. The rich and creamy Alfredo sauce paired with tender chicken creates a delightful combination that will leave you craving for more. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this delicious and healthy twist on a classic favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • 1 package of low-carb pasta of your choice (zoodles, shirataki noodles, or any other low-carb alternative)

Instructions:

  1. Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and fully cooked. Once done, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, reduce the heat to medium and add the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, cream cheese, Italian seasoning, paprika, and nutmeg. Stir the mixture continuously until the cheeses have melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy.

  4. While the sauce is simmering, prepare the low-carb pasta according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  5. Slice the cooked chicken breasts into strips and add them back to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce. Stir to coat the chicken evenly with the sauce.

  6. To serve, place a portion of the low-carb pasta on a plate and top it with the creamy chicken Alfredo. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Tips for Success:

  • To add extra flavor, consider incorporating cooked bacon or sautéed mushrooms into the Alfredo sauce.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can let it simmer for a few additional minutes to reduce and thicken.
  • Feel free to customize the dish by adding your favorite low-carb vegetables such as broccoli, spinach, or asparagus.

Final Thoughts

This creamy low carb chicken Alfredo recipe is a wonderful way to indulge in a classic Italian dish while sticking to your low-carb goals. The combination of tender chicken, creamy Alfredo sauce, and low-carb pasta creates a truly satisfying meal that will leave you feeling nourished and content. Whether you’re following a specific dietary plan or simply looking for a healthier alternative to traditional pasta dishes, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your meal rotation. Enjoy the luxurious flavors of Alfredo without the guilt, and savor every creamy, low-carb bite!
