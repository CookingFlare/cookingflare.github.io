Spice of Life: 5 Exotic Rubs for Vegetarian BBQ


When it comes to BBQ, most people immediately think of juicy steaks, succulent ribs, and sizzling sausages. But who said that BBQ can’t be just as flavorful and satisfying without meat? Vegetarian BBQ is a rising trend that allows plant-based eaters to indulge in smoky, charred goodness. And to take your veggie BBQ to the next level, we have curated five exotic rubs that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. So fire up your grills and get ready to experience the Spice of Life!

1. Mediterranean Magic Rub

This rub is inspired by the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean region. Combine two tablespoons of dried oregano, one tablespoon of dried thyme, one tablespoon of dried basil, one tablespoon of smoked paprika, one teaspoon of garlic powder, and one teaspoon of sea salt. Mix well and generously coat your vegetables with this aromatic blend before grilling. The combination of herbs and spices will transport you to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean with every bite.

2. Asian Fusion Rub

For a taste of the Far East, try our Asian Fusion Rub. In a bowl, mix together two tablespoons of soy sauce, two tablespoons of brown sugar, one tablespoon of sesame oil, one tablespoon of grated ginger, one teaspoon of Chinese five-spice powder, and one teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes. This flavorful rub pairs perfectly with tofu, mushrooms, and even grilled pineapple. Let the exotic flavors of Asia dance on your palate as you savor each bite.

3. Smoky Southwestern Rub

If you’re a fan of bold and smoky flavors, this rub is for you. Combine two tablespoons of chili powder, one tablespoon of smoked paprika, one tablespoon of cumin, one tablespoon of garlic powder, one tablespoon of onion powder, one teaspoon of cayenne pepper, and one teaspoon of sea salt. Give your veggies a generous coating of this rub, and let them bathe in the rich flavors of the Southwest. The combination of spices will create a mouthwatering smoky sensation that will leave you craving for more.

4. Middle Eastern Delight Rub

Transport yourself to the bustling markets of the Middle East with this aromatic rub. In a bowl, mix together two tablespoons of ground cumin, two tablespoons of ground coriander, one tablespoon of ground sumac, one tablespoon of smoked paprika, one teaspoon of ground cinnamon, and one teaspoon of sea salt. The fragrant blend of spices will infuse your grilled vegetables with the warm and exotic flavors of the Middle East. Get ready to embark on a culinary journey like no other.

5. Caribbean Heat Rub

For those who crave a little heat, our Caribbean Heat Rub is the perfect choice. In a bowl, combine two tablespoons of brown sugar, two tablespoons of paprika, one tablespoon of dried thyme, one tablespoon of ground allspice, one teaspoon of cayenne pepper, and one teaspoon of sea salt. This spicy rub will add a fiery kick to your grilled veggies, giving them a Caribbean twist. Let the heat take over your taste buds and transport you to the tropical paradise of the Caribbean.

Now that you have these five exotic rubs in your arsenal, it’s time to fire up the grill and let the flavors take center stage. Experiment with different combinations, adjust the spice levels to your liking, and most importantly, enjoy the journey of vegetarian BBQ. Whether you’re a dedicated herbivore or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these rubs will elevate your BBQ game and prove that vegetarian food can be just as satisfying and delicious. So gather your friends, embrace the sizzle, and let the Spice of Life guide your vegetarian BBQ adventures. Happy grilling!
