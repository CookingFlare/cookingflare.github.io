Ghostly Pasta Salad: Spooky & Delicious Halloween Lunch Idea


The leaves are turning orange and red, pumpkins are appearing everywhere, and a chill is in the air. Halloween parties and gatherings are on everyone’s minds, preparing for the scariest time of the year. As the nights grow longer, our appetites grow bigger! This year, why not scare up a deliciously spooky lunch idea, perfect for your Halloween celebrations? Here comes the Ghostly Pasta Salad; this hair-raising dish is bound to haunt your taste buds!

What You Need

Ingredients

  • 1 box of spiral pasta (about 12 ounces)
  • 1 cup cooked and crumbled Italian sausage
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup small mozzarella balls, halved
  • 1/2 cup sliced olives (black or green)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped bell pepper (any color)
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • For the hauntingly good dressing
    • 1/2 cup olive oil
    • 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
    • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
    • 2 tablespoons honey
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper, to taste

Ghostly Pasta Salad Instructions

  1. Get your inner ghoul ready and crank up the creepy playlist. First, begin by boiling a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain and set aside to cool.

  2. While the pasta is chilling, create the vengeful vinaigrette. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the olive oil, white wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, and dried oregano. Season the phantom dressing to taste with salt and pepper, and set it aside.

  3. For a spine-chilling twist, cook and crumble the Italian sausage in a skillet over medium heat. Once cooked, transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to soak up the excess grease.

  4. Time to assemble the ghostly macabre mixture! In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked and cooled spiral pasta, cooked sausage, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, sliced olives, chopped bell pepper, chopped parsley, and shredded Parmesan cheese. Toss gently until the ingredients are well combined.

  5. Lastly, pour the eerie dressing over the pasta salad and mix until evenly coated.

  6. Just before serving, add a final sprinkle of parsley on top for added color and freshness.
