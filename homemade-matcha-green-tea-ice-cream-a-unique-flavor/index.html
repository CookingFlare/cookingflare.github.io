Homemade Matcha Green Tea Ice Cream: A Unique Flavor


Are you tired of the same old ice cream flavors? Looking for something unique and refreshing to tantalize your taste buds? Look no further! In this recipe, I’ll show you how to make homemade matcha green tea ice cream – a delightful twist on a classic treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons matcha green tea powder
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream and whole milk over medium heat. Heat the mixture until it starts to steam, but make sure it doesn’t boil.

  2. While the cream and milk mixture is heating, whisk together the granulated sugar, matcha green tea powder, and egg yolks in a separate bowl. Make sure the mixture is well combined and smooth.

  3. Once the cream and milk mixture is steaming, slowly pour it into the matcha green tea mixture while whisking continuously. This will temper the eggs and prevent them from curdling.

  4. Pour the entire mixture back into the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon. Make sure to scrape the bottom of the pan to prevent any burning.

  5. Continue cooking until the mixture thickens enough to coat the back of the spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Be careful not to overcook, as the eggs may scramble.

  6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Let the mixture cool at room temperature for about 30 minutes.

  7. Once the mixture has cooled, cover it with plastic wrap, making sure the plastic wrap touches the surface of the mixture. This will prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate the mixture for at least 4 hours or overnight.

  8. After the mixture has chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes.

  9. Once the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.

  10. Serve the homemade matcha green tea ice cream in bowls or cones and enjoy the unique and refreshing flavor!

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add mix-ins such as chocolate chips, crushed cookies, or chopped nuts during the last few minutes of churning.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute half-and-half or milk for the heavy cream. However, keep in mind that the texture may be slightly less creamy.
  • Experiment with different brands of matcha green tea powder to find your preferred flavor intensity.
  • To enhance the green color of the ice cream, you can add a few drops of green food coloring if desired.
  • Serve the matcha green tea ice cream with a drizzle of honey, a sprinkling of toasted sesame seeds, or a dollop of whipped cream for an added touch of indulgence.

So, why settle for ordinary when you can indulge in the extraordinary? Treat yourself to a scoop of homemade matcha green tea ice cream and experience the unique flavor that will transport your taste buds to a whole new level of delight. Enjoy!
