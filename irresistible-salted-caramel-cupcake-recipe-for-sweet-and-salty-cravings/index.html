Irresistible Salted Caramel Cupcake Recipe for Sweet and Salty Cravings


If you’re looking for a delightful treat that perfectly balances sweet and salty flavors, look no further than these irresistible salted caramel cupcakes. With a moist, fluffy vanilla cupcake base topped with a rich salted caramel frosting, these cupcakes are sure to satisfy your cravings. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in a sweet moment, this recipe will elevate your baking game.

Ingredients

For the Vanilla Cupcakes

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup whole milk

For the Salted Caramel Sauce

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt (adjust to taste)

For the Salted Caramel Frosting

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup salted caramel sauce (cooled)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk (optional, for consistency)

Equipment Needed

  • Cupcake pan
  • Cupcake liners
  • Mixing bowls
  • Electric mixer or whisk
  • Saucepan
  • Candy thermometer (optional)
  • Piping bag and tip (for frosting)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cupcake Batter

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a cupcake pan with cupcake liners.

  2. Cream the Butter and Sugar: In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 3-4 minutes. The air you incorporate here will result in a lighter cupcake.

  3. Add Eggs and Vanilla: Beat in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring each is fully incorporated before adding the next. Add the vanilla extract and mix until combined.

  4. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. This step ensures even distribution of the leavening agent.

  5. Mix Dry and Wet Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with the whole milk. Start and end with the flour mixture. Mix until just combined; be careful not to overmix.

Step 2: Bake the Cupcakes

  1. Fill the Liners: Use a cookie scoop or spoon to fill the cupcake liners about two-thirds full with batter.

  2. Bake: Place the cupcake pan in the preheated oven and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  3. Cool: Once baked, remove the cupcakes from the oven and allow them to cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 3: Make the Salted Caramel Sauce

  1. Melt the Sugar: In a medium saucepan over medium heat, add the granulated sugar. Stir constantly until it melts and turns a deep amber color. Be careful not to burn it; this process takes about 5-10 minutes.

  2. Add Butter: Once the sugar is melted, carefully add the butter, stirring constantly until melted and combined.

  3. Add Cream and Salt: Remove from heat and slowly pour in the heavy cream, stirring until smooth. The mixture will bubble up, so be cautious. Stir in the sea salt to finish the sauce.

  4. Cool: Allow the salted caramel sauce to cool completely before using it in the frosting. You can store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

Step 4: Prepare the Salted Caramel Frosting

  1. Cream the Butter: In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened unsalted butter until creamy.

  2. Add Powdered Sugar: Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing on low speed until well combined.

  3. Incorporate Salted Caramel: Add in the cooled salted caramel sauce and vanilla extract. Beat on medium speed until fully combined and fluffy.

  4. Adjust Consistency: If the frosting is too thick, add milk one tablespoon at a time until you reach your desired consistency.

Step 5: Assemble the Cupcakes

  1. Frost the Cupcakes: Once the cupcakes are completely cool, use a piping bag fitted with your desired tip to frost the cupcakes with the salted caramel frosting.

  2. Drizzle with Salted Caramel: Drizzle additional salted caramel sauce over the frosted cupcakes for an extra touch of decadence.

  3. Garnish: For a final touch, sprinkle a pinch of flaky sea salt on top of each cupcake to enhance the sweet and salty flavor.

Tips for Perfect Salted Caramel Cupcakes

  • Room Temperature Ingredients: Ensure all your ingredients are at room temperature for better mixing and texture.
  • Don’t Overmix: Overmixing the batter can result in dense cupcakes. Mix just until combined.
  • Check for Doneness: Oven temperatures can vary; always check for doneness a few minutes before the suggested baking time.
  • Customize the Flavor: For an extra twist, consider adding a pinch of espresso powder to the cake batter to enhance the flavor profile.

Storage Suggestions

These salted caramel cupcakes can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. If you want to keep them fresh for longer, refrigerate them for up to a week. Just be sure to bring them back to room temperature before serving for the best taste and texture.

Conclusion

These irresistible salted caramel cupcakes are not just a treat for the taste buds; they also bring joy to any occasion. The combination of fluffy vanilla cake, rich salted caramel frosting, and the delightful crunch of sea salt creates a perfect harmony of flavors. Whether you’re baking for a party, a special occasion, or just to indulge in a sweet craving, this recipe will not disappoint. So gather your ingredients, turn on your oven, and get ready to create a batch of these delectable treats that will have everyone coming back for more!

Indulge in the sweet and salty magic of salted caramel cupcakes, and enjoy every bite as you savor the delightful contrast that makes these cupcakes utterly irresistible. Happy baking!
