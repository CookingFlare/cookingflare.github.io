Creamy One Pot Cajun Chicken Pasta


If you’re on the hunt for a delicious and comforting meal that’s easy to make, look no further than this Creamy One Pot Cajun Chicken Pasta recipe. This dish combines the bold flavors of Cajun seasoning with the creamy goodness of a pasta dish, all cooked in one pot for minimal cleanup. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a casual get-together, this recipe is sure to impress your guests and leave them coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 8 oz. penne pasta
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by heating the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and sprinkle them with Cajun seasoning. Cook the chicken until it’s no longer pink on the outside, then remove it from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper, and cook for a few minutes until the peppers have softened slightly.

  3. Next, add the penne pasta to the pot, followed by the chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cover the pot and let the pasta cook for about 12-15 minutes, or until it’s al dente.

  4. Once the pasta is cooked, stir in the cooked chicken, heavy cream, and grated Parmesan cheese. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few more minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. To serve, garnish the creamy Cajun chicken pasta with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a dash of hot sauce to the dish.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or switching up the type of pasta used.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors will continue to meld together, making for a delicious next-day meal.

Final Thoughts

This Creamy One Pot Cajun Chicken Pasta is a perfect harmony of flavors and textures, and it’s a testament to the beauty of one-pot meals. The creamy sauce, combined with the bold Cajun seasoning, creates a dish that’s both comforting and exciting to the taste buds. Plus, the convenience of only having one pot to clean up afterward is an added bonus.

So, whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is a must-try. Gather your ingredients, grab a large pot, and get ready to savor every bite of this flavorful and satisfying dish. It’s a guaranteed crowd-pleaser that will have everyone asking for the recipe. Enjoy!
