Decadent Christmas Desserts: 15 Recipes to Indulge In


The holiday season is upon us, and with it comes the delightful prospect of indulging in decadent Christmas desserts. These sweet treats not only satisfy our cravings but also bring joy and warmth to our gatherings. From rich cakes to luscious pies, Christmas is the perfect time to showcase your baking skills and delight your family and friends with mouthwatering desserts. Here, we’ve compiled 15 irresistible recipes that will make your holiday celebrations even more special.

1. Classic Gingerbread Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ tablespoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup molasses
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Royal icing for decoration

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and brown sugar until fluffy. Beat in the egg, molasses, and vanilla.
  3. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until combined.
  4. Divide the dough in half, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Roll out the dough to ¼ inch thick and cut into desired shapes.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes until firm. Let cool before decorating with royal icing.

2. Chocolate Peppermint Bark

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
  • 12 ounces white chocolate, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1 cup crushed candy canes

Instructions:

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the dark chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring until smooth.
  3. Pour the melted chocolate onto the prepared baking sheet and spread evenly.
  4. In a separate bowl, melt the white chocolate. Stir in peppermint extract.
  5. Drizzle the white chocolate over the dark chocolate and swirl with a knife.
  6. Sprinkle crushed candy canes on top. Refrigerate until set, then break into pieces.

3. Eggnog Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup eggnog
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press into the bottom of a springform pan.
  3. In a bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well.
  4. Stir in eggnog, vanilla, and nutmeg until combined. Pour over the crust.
  5. Bake for 1 hour or until set. Cool in the pan before removing. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours.

4. Red Velvet Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk, room temperature
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • For the frosting: 8 ounces cream cheese, softened, ½ cup unsalted butter, softened, 4 cups powdered sugar, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vanilla, and vinegar.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  5. Fill each cupcake liner ⅔ full. Bake for 20-22 minutes.
  6. For the frosting, beat cream cheese and butter until smooth. Gradually add powdered sugar and vanilla until fluffy.
  7. Frost cooled cupcakes and enjoy!

5. Buche de Noel (Yule Log Cake)

Ingredients:

  • For the sponge cake:
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • ¼ cup cocoa powder
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • ¼ teaspoon salt
    • 3 large eggs
    • ¾ cup sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • For the chocolate ganache:
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 8 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a jelly roll pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat eggs and sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in vanilla.
  4. Gradually incorporate the dry ingredients into the egg mixture.
  5. Spread the batter evenly in the pan and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  6. While the cake is baking, prepare the ganache by heating heavy cream and pouring it over chopped chocolate. Stir until smooth.
  7. Once the cake is done, roll it in a clean kitchen towel and let it cool.
  8. Unroll, spread ganache, roll back up, and frost the outside with remaining ganache. Decorate as desired.

6. Pecan Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup corn syrup
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups pecans, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Fit the pie crust into a pie pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together corn syrup, brown sugar, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Stir in the chopped pecans.
  4. Pour the filling into the pie crust and bake for 60 minutes or until set.
  5. Let cool before slicing and serving.

7. Spiced Apple Cider Donuts

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup apple cider
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a donut pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together apple cider, eggs, and melted butter.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed. Fill the donut pan.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Allow to cool before glazing or dusting with powdered sugar.

8. Chocolate Lava Cakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease ramekins with butter and dust with flour.
  2. Melt chocolate chips and butter together in a microwave-safe bowl.
  3. In another bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick and pale.
  4. Combine the chocolate mixture with the egg mixture, then fold in flour.
  5. Divide the batter among the ramekins and bake for 12-14 minutes.
  6. Let cool for 1 minute, then invert onto plates and dust with powdered sugar.

9. Peppermint Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 6 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • Whipped cream and crushed candy canes for topping

Instructions:

  1. Melt the chocolate in a bowl over simmering water. Let cool slightly.
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until thick and pale. Fold in the melted chocolate and peppermint extract.
  3. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture.
  4. Spoon into serving dishes and chill for at least 2 hours. Top with whipped cream and crushed candy canes before serving.

  • For the crust:
