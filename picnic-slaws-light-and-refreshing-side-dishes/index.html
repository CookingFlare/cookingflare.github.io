Picnic Slaws: Light and Refreshing Side Dishes


When it comes to picnics and outdoor gatherings, having the perfect side dish can make all the difference. Picnic slaws are a fantastic addition to any outdoor feast. Light, refreshing, and easy to prepare, they are the ideal accompaniment to grilled meats, sandwiches, and other picnic favorites. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious picnic slaw recipes that are sure to become staples at your outdoor gatherings.

Classic Coleslaw

Let’s start with a timeless favorite - classic coleslaw. This simple yet flavorful dish is a blend of shredded cabbage, carrots, and a creamy dressing. To elevate the flavor, consider adding a touch of Dijon mustard and apple cider vinegar to the dressing. The tangy and creamy combination is a crowd-pleaser and pairs perfectly with grilled burgers or hot dogs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small head of cabbage, shredded
  • 2 carrots, shredded
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage and carrots.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and apple cider vinegar.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage and carrots, and toss until well coated.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

Tangy Broccoli Slaw

For a refreshing twist, consider making a tangy broccoli slaw. This variation on the classic coleslaw features crisp broccoli florets, dried cranberries, and a zesty vinaigrette. The combination of sweet and tangy flavors makes this slaw a standout side dish that’s perfect for a sunny afternoon picnic.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups broccoli florets, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the broccoli florets, dried cranberries, red onion, and sunflower seeds.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, white wine vinegar, and honey.
  3. Pour the dressing over the broccoli mixture and toss until well combined.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

Spicy Mexican Slaw

For those who enjoy a bit of heat, a spicy Mexican slaw is a perfect choice. This vibrant and colorful slaw features a mix of cabbage, bell peppers, and jalapeños, all tossed in a zesty lime dressing. The spicy kick of the jalapeños combined with the freshness of the lime makes this slaw a standout addition to any picnic spread.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 head of red cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 head of green cabbage, shredded
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 jalapeños, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded red cabbage, green cabbage, bell peppers, jalapeños, and cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, honey, and ground cumin.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage mixture and toss until everything is well coated.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

Conclusion

Picnic slaws are versatile, easy to prepare, and add a burst of flavor and color to any outdoor gathering. Whether you prefer the classic coleslaw, a tangy broccoli slaw, or a spicy Mexican slaw, there’s a variation to suit every taste. Next time you’re planning a picnic or BBQ, consider adding one of these delightful slaws to your menu for a refreshing and delicious side dish that’s sure to impress your guests.
