Savory Keto Bacon-Wrapped Meatloaf: A Flavorful Twist


Meatloaf is a classic comfort food that brings back nostalgic memories of family dinners. But why stick to the traditional recipe when you can take it up a notch with a savory twist? Enter the Savory Keto Bacon-Wrapped Meatloaf. This mouthwatering dish combines the richness of bacon with the hearty flavors of a traditional meatloaf while keeping it low-carb and keto-friendly. Get ready to indulge in a flavor-packed meal that will satisfy your taste buds and keep you on track with your dietary goals.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 8-10 slices of bacon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a loaf pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, almond flour, Parmesan cheese, onion, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, black pepper, and the egg. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Take half of the bacon slices and arrange them side by side, slightly overlapping, on a clean surface. Place the meatloaf mixture on top of the bacon slices and shape it into a loaf.

  4. Carefully wrap the bacon slices around the meatloaf, ensuring that it is fully covered. Use the remaining bacon slices to cover any exposed areas.

  5. Transfer the bacon-wrapped meatloaf to the prepared loaf pan and gently press it down to ensure it holds its shape.

  6. Place the loaf pan in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 1 hour, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C) using a meat thermometer.

  7. Once cooked, remove the meatloaf from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.

  8. Serve the Savory Keto Bacon-Wrapped Meatloaf with your favorite low-carb side dishes such as cauliflower mash or a refreshing salad. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can experiment with different seasonings such as smoked paprika or cayenne pepper.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, consider adding diced jalapeños or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes to the meatloaf mixture.
  • To ensure the bacon wraps around the meatloaf securely, you can use toothpicks to hold it in place while baking, just remember to remove them before serving.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or oven when ready to enjoy.

Final Thoughts

The Savory Keto Bacon-Wrapped Meatloaf is a delicious twist on a classic dish that will satisfy your cravings for comfort food while keeping you on track with your keto lifestyle. The combination of juicy ground beef and pork, paired with the smoky and salty flavors of bacon, creates a flavor explosion that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a flavorful dinner option, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. So go ahead, give it a try, and savor every bite of this mouthwatering meal.
