Heartwarming Valentine's Day Stew: Beef Bourguignon Recipe

Valentine’s Day is a time to celebrate love and what better way to do so than with a heartwarming and delicious meal? This Beef Bourguignon recipe is the perfect dish to share with your loved one on this special day. With its rich flavors and tender beef, it’s sure to impress and create a memorable dining experience. Ingredients 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes 4 slices bacon, chopped 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 large onion, chopped 3 garlic cloves, minced 1 cup red wine 2 cups beef broth 2 tablespoons tomato paste 1 teaspoon thyme 2 bay leaves 1 pound small potatoes, halved 1/2 pound carrots, cut into 1-inch pieces Salt and pepper to taste Chopped fresh parsley for garnish Instructions Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). In a large Dutch oven, cook the bacon over medium heat until crisp. Remove the bacon and set it aside, leaving the drippings in the pot. Season the beef cubes with salt and pepper, then add them to the pot in batches, browning them on all sides. Remove the beef and set it aside. Add the chopped onion to the pot and cook until it’s softened. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute. Sprinkle the flour over the onion and garlic, stirring to coat. Cook for a few minutes to remove the raw flour taste. Pour in the red wine and beef broth, stirring to combine. Add the tomato paste, thyme, and bay leaves. Bring the mixture to a simmer. Return the beef and bacon to the pot. Cover and place it in the preheated oven for 90 minutes. After 90 minutes, add the potatoes and carrots to the pot. Cover and return it to the oven for an additional 1 hour, or until the beef is tender. Once done, remove the bay leaves and skim off any excess fat from the surface. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the Beef Bourguignon in bowls, garnished with chopped fresh parsley. Why Beef Bourguignon? Beef Bourguignon, also known as Beef Burgundy, is a classic French stew that originated in the Burgundy region of France. Its deep, rich flavors and tender beef make it a perfect choice for a special occasion like Valentine’s Day. The slow cooking process allows the flavors to meld together, creating a dish that is both comforting and luxurious. ...
