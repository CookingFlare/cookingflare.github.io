Comforting Slow Cooker Chicken and Corn Tortilla Soup Recipe


If you’re looking for a hearty and comforting meal that practically cooks itself, look no further than this slow cooker chicken and corn tortilla soup recipe. Packed with flavor, this soup is perfect for a cozy night in or for feeding a crowd. The best part? You can simply set it and forget it, letting the slow cooker work its magic while you go about your day. Let’s dive into this delicious and easy-to-make recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and diced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 6 corn tortillas, cut into strips
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Avocado slices, for serving
  • Sour cream, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker. Add the black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, onion, garlic, jalapeño, chili powder, cumin, paprika, and chicken broth. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  3. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and stir in the chopped cilantro.
  4. About 30 minutes before serving, add the corn tortilla strips to the slow cooker and stir to combine. Cover and let the soup cook for another 30 minutes to allow the tortilla strips to soften and absorb the flavors.
  5. Ladle the soup into bowls and serve with lime wedges, avocado slices, and a dollop of sour cream on top.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a spicier kick, leave the seeds in the jalapeño or add a dash of hot sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the toppings with shredded cheese, diced red onion, or a sprinkle of tortilla strips for added crunch.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can crush some of the tortilla strips into the soup to help thicken it up.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

This slow cooker chicken and corn tortilla soup is a crowd-pleaser for many reasons. Not only is it incredibly easy to prepare, but it’s also packed with wholesome ingredients and bold flavors. The slow cooking process allows the flavors to meld together, resulting in a rich and satisfying soup that’s perfect for any occasion. Plus, the versatility of the toppings means you can customize each bowl to suit your taste.

So, whether you’re planning a casual family dinner or hosting a laid-back gathering with friends, this comforting slow cooker chicken and corn tortilla soup is sure to be a hit. Set it in the morning and come home to a house filled with the enticing aroma of a delicious, homestyle meal. This recipe is a testament to the fact that sometimes, the simplest dishes are the most satisfying.
