Nut-Free Wanderlust: 45 Flavorful Nut-Free Recipes for the Travel-Loving Cook


Ah, the aroma of a well-spiced curry from Bangkok, the tangy zing of a Szechuan stir-fry from Chengdu, or the mouthwatering scent of freshly baked bread from a Parisian boulangerie - what could be more exciting than exploring the culinary gems of the world and taking those incredible flavors home? If you have nut allergies or simply avoid nuts in your diet, this doesn’t have to limit your gastronomic adventures. Embrace your inner food explorer with the following 45 nut-free recipes to ensure you have a taste of global cuisine right from your kitchen.

  1. Spicy Thai Red Curry with Tofu
  2. Mapo Tofu
  3. Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells
  4. Veggie-packed Pasta with a Roasted Tomato Sauce
  5. Moroccan Chickpea Stew
  6. Lentil Soup with Lemon & Cumin
  7. Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry
  8. Greek-inspired Eggplant and Tomato Casserole
  9. Baked Eggplant and Chickpea Pita Pizzas
  10. Edamame Succotash
  11. Sweet and Spicy Pineapple Sambal
  12. Durian-infused Coconut Pancakes
  13. Lychee Panna Cotta
  14. Fresh Strawberry Galette
  15. Clementine and Cardamom Rice Pudding
  16. Vegan Shepherd’s Pie
  17. Fig and Brie Tart with a Savormare Biscuit Crust
  18. Tarte Tatin with Caramelized Shallots and Apple
  19. Classic French Quiche Lorraine without the Crust
  20. Basque Burnt Cheesecake

When venturing into new regions of gastronomy, it is essential to find inspiration from the ingredients and flavors that define those places. The following recipes embrace the worldwide spectrum of vegetable, legume, and humble grains to bring you tangy, sweet, and spicy dishes that are sure to entice your taste buds as you travel without confines.

Arguably one of the most vibrant and richly-spiced cuisines worldwide can be found in Thailand. Capture the heart of this Southeast Asian country with a fiery Spicy Thai Red Curry with Tofu. With a base of coconut milk, red curry paste, and a medley of vegetables, tofu provides an ideal protein substitute without compromising on flavor. Delight your taste buds with the powerful combination of fragrant lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, giving you a genuine Thai experience.

Why stop at one country? Head straight to the Sichuan province of China and give yourself a culinary adventure into the world-renowned Mapo Tofu. Influenced by the fiery spice level and chewiness of the silken tofu, you’ll discover an earthy depth that comes from fermented black beans, Chili bean paste, and minced pork.

As we traverse further into Europe, welcome the tastes of sun-kissed vegetables ripe from the Italian summers with a Veggie-packed Pasta with a Roasted Tomato Sauce. Combining fresh garlic, basil, and a generous sprinkle of Parmigiano Reggiano, this dish showcases the warmth and simplicity of Italian cooking and brings the essences of Naples right to your kitchen.

For a truly enchanting dining experience, you will not need to look any further than our Moroccan Chickpea Stew, a wholesome explosion of traditional Moroccan flavors: a warm blend of spices, rich tomato sauce, and the irresistible softness of the chickpea. This heartwarming dish will awaken your senses and transport you to the bustling souks and colorful markets of Morocco.

Greater rewards await in the kitchen as you create a journey through international gastronomy. Nut-free dishes abound; with these recipes, a new world of flavors awaits you. Bon appétit!
