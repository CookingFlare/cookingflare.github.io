BBQ Shrimp Po' Boy: A Classic Sandwich Recipe with a Twist


Are you craving a taste of New Orleans? Look no further than this delicious BBQ Shrimp Po’ Boy sandwich! With its crispy shrimp, tangy BBQ sauce, and fluffy bread, this classic Southern dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds. But we’re not stopping there - we’re adding a little twist to take this sandwich to the next level. Are you ready to dive into this mouthwatering recipe?

Ingredients

For the Shrimp:

  • 1 pound fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Sandwich:

  • 4 French baguettes, sliced lengthwise
  • Lettuce, tomato, and pickles for garnish
  • Mayonnaise for spreading

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Toss the shrimp in the spice mixture until well coated.

  2. Heat vegetable oil in a deep skillet or fryer to 350°F (175°C). Carefully place the shrimp in the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the shrimp from the oil and drain on a paper towel-lined plate.

  3. In a small saucepan, combine all the BBQ sauce ingredients over medium heat. Stir well to combine and simmer for 5 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Remove from heat and set aside.

  4. To assemble the sandwiches, spread mayonnaise on both sides of the sliced baguettes. Place a generous amount of lettuce, tomato slices, and pickles on the bottom half of each baguette.

  5. Arrange the crispy shrimp on top of the garnish and drizzle with the homemade BBQ sauce. Place the top half of the baguette over the shrimp to complete the sandwich.

  6. Serve the BBQ Shrimp Po’ Boy immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors in every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier BBQ sauce, add a few dashes of hot sauce or cayenne pepper to the mixture.
  • For a healthier alternative, you can bake the shrimp instead of frying. Simply preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C), place the shrimp on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and bake for 8-10 minutes or until they are cooked through and crispy.
  • Feel free to customize your Po’ Boy sandwich with additional toppings such as sliced avocado, red onion, or a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

Now that you have the recipe for this incredible BBQ Shrimp Po’ Boy sandwich, it’s time to head to the kitchen and start cooking! Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ or simply craving a taste of the South, this sandwich will surely become a favorite. So grab your ingredients, put on some jazz music, and get ready to indulge in a true New Orleans experience. Bon appétit!
