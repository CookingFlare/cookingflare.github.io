Flavorful Paleo Grilled Shrimp with Pineapple


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy dish that’s bursting with flavor, then look no further than this recipe for Flavorful Paleo Grilled Shrimp with Pineapple. This dish is not only a feast for the taste buds but also fits perfectly into a paleo lifestyle. The combination of succulent grilled shrimp and sweet, juicy pineapple creates a harmony of flavors that will leave you craving for more. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 fresh pineapple, peeled, cored, and cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a large bowl, combine the shrimp, olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, cumin, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Toss everything together until the shrimp are evenly coated with the seasonings. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  2. Preheat the Grill: While the shrimp is marinating, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using wooden skewers, soak them in water for about 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill.

  3. Skewer the Shrimp and Pineapple: Thread the marinated shrimp and pineapple chunks onto skewers, alternating between the two. This will create beautiful and flavorful kebabs that are perfect for grilling.

  4. Grill the Kebabs: Place the shrimp and pineapple kebabs on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the shrimp are pink and opaque. The pineapple should develop grill marks and caramelize slightly, enhancing its natural sweetness.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the kebabs are cooked to perfection, remove them from the grill and place them on a serving platter. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

Why This Recipe Rocks

This Flavorful Paleo Grilled Shrimp with Pineapple recipe is a true standout for several reasons. First and foremost, it’s incredibly delicious. The combination of the smoky grilled shrimp and the sweet, charred pineapple creates a flavor profile that’s both satisfying and unexpected. This dish is also paleo-friendly, meaning it aligns with a diet rich in lean proteins, fruits, and vegetables.

Furthermore, the preparation and cooking process is simple and straightforward, making it an ideal dish for a quick weeknight meal or a weekend barbecue. The marinade infuses the shrimp with a robust blend of spices, while the natural sugars in the pineapple caramelize on the grill, adding a delightful touch of sweetness to each bite.

In conclusion, this Flavorful Paleo Grilled Shrimp with Pineapple recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to elevate their grilling game while keeping things healthy and delicious. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply love the combination of shrimp and pineapple, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. So fire up the grill and get ready to savor every mouthwatering bite of these delectable kebabs!

That’s a wrap for this recipe. We hope you enjoy making and savoring this delightful dish as much as we do. Happy grilling!
