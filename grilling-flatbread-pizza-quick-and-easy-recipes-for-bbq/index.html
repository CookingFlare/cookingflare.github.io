Grilling Flatbread Pizza: Quick and Easy Recipes for BBQ


Are you tired of the same old BBQ recipes? Looking for something quick, easy, and delicious to serve at your next backyard gathering? Look no further than grilling flatbread pizza! This versatile dish combines the smoky flavors of the grill with the fresh and vibrant toppings of a classic pizza. It’s a crowd-pleasing option that will have your guests coming back for more.

The Perfect Grilling Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Flatbread or Naan bread
  • Olive oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Make sure the grates are clean and lightly oiled to prevent sticking.

  2. Brush both sides of the flatbread or Naan bread with olive oil. This will give your pizza a crispy and flavorful crust.

  3. Place the oiled flatbread onto the preheated grill. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bread is lightly charred and crispy. Keep a close eye on it to prevent burning.

  4. Once the flatbread is grilled to perfection, remove it from the grill and place it on a clean surface.

  5. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the grilled side of the flatbread. Be generous with the sauce, but avoid overloading the pizza, as it can make the crust soggy.

  6. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce. The heat from the grill will melt the cheese to ooey-gooey perfection.

  7. Now it’s time to get creative with your toppings! Add your favorite ingredients, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, or any other toppings you love. Remember to distribute the toppings evenly for the best flavor experience.

  8. Sprinkle some fresh basil leaves over the top and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  9. Carefully place the assembled pizza back onto the preheated grill, close the lid, and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted, and the toppings are heated through.

  10. Once the cheese is bubbly and the toppings are cooked to perfection, remove the pizza from the grill.

  11. Let the pizza cool for a minute or two before slicing it into wedges. This will allow the cheese to set slightly and prevent any topping mishaps.

  12. Serve your grilled flatbread pizza hot and enjoy the burst of flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing!

Tips and Variations:

  • Experiment with different sauces and cheeses to add variety to your grilled flatbread pizza. Try pesto sauce with goat cheese or barbecue sauce with cheddar cheese for a unique twist.
  • Don’t limit yourself to traditional pizza toppings. Grilled pineapple, prosciutto, or even grilled shrimp can take your flatbread pizza to a whole new level.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use pre-made flatbread or Naan bread from your local grocery store. Just make sure to choose a sturdy and thick variety that can withstand the heat of the grill.
  • For a vegetarian option, load up your pizza with a variety of grilled vegetables. Zucchini, eggplant, and tomatoes work particularly well.

Grilling flatbread pizza is a fantastic way to elevate your BBQ game and impress your guests. With its quick and easy preparation, endless topping options, and mouthwatering flavors, it’s a surefire hit at any summer gathering. So fire up the grill, grab your favorite ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible goodness of grilled flatbread pizza!
