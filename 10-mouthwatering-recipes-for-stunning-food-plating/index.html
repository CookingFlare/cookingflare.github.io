10 Mouthwatering Recipes for Stunning Food Plating


Are you tired of serving the same old dishes on plain white plates? Elevate your culinary skills and impress your guests with these 10 mouthwatering recipes for stunning food plating. From vibrant colors to artistic arrangements, these recipes will not only tantalize your taste buds but also make your dishes Instagram-worthy. Get ready to unleash your inner chef and create stunning masterpieces on your plate!

1. Salmon Tartare with Avocado Cream

Ingredients:

  • Fresh salmon fillet
  • Avocado
  • Lemon juice
  • Red onion
  • Capers
  • Fresh dill
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Chop the salmon into small cubes and set aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper to create a smooth cream.
  3. Finely chop the red onion, capers, and fresh dill.
  4. Combine the salmon, red onion, capers, and dill in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Using a ring mold, place a layer of the avocado cream at the bottom and top with the salmon mixture.
  6. Gently remove the ring mold and garnish with a sprig of fresh dill.

2. Beetroot Carpaccio with Goat Cheese

Ingredients:

  • Fresh beetroots
  • Goat cheese
  • Walnuts
  • Arugula
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Thinly slice the beetroots using a mandoline slicer.
  2. Arrange the beetroot slices on a plate, slightly overlapping each other.
  3. Crumble the goat cheese over the beetroot slices.
  4. Sprinkle with chopped walnuts and arugula.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.

3. Caprese Salad Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Mozzarella balls
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Thread a cherry tomato, a mozzarella ball, and a fresh basil leaf onto a skewer.
  2. Repeat the process until all the ingredients are used.
  3. Arrange the skewers on a plate in a decorative pattern.
  4. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.

4. Watermelon and Feta Salad

Ingredients:

  • Watermelon
  • Feta cheese
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cut the watermelon into bite-sized cubes.
  2. Arrange the watermelon cubes on a plate.
  3. Crumble the feta cheese over the watermelon.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.

5. Ratatouille Stack

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Red bell pepper
  • Yellow bell pepper
  • Tomato
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Slice the eggplant, zucchini, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and tomato into thin rounds.
  2. Drizzle each vegetable slice with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill or roast the vegetable slices until tender.
  4. Assemble the ratatouille stack by alternating the vegetable slices and fresh basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.

6. Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Smoked salmon slices
  • Cream cheese
  • Fresh dill
  • Lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Lay out a slice of smoked salmon on a clean surface.
  2. Spread a layer of cream cheese over the salmon.
  3. Sprinkle with fresh dill and lemon zest.
  4. Roll up the salmon tightly.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  6. Slice the rolls into bite-sized pieces and arrange them on a plate.

7. Mango Salsa Stuffed Avocado

Ingredients:

  • Ripe avocados
  • Mango
  • Red onion
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime juice
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits.
  2. Dice the mango, red onion, and fresh cilantro.
  3. In a bowl, combine the mango, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spoon the mango salsa into the avocado halves.
  5. Garnish with additional cilantro leaves.

8. Chocolate Lava Cake with Raspberry Sauce

Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Raspberries
  • Powdered sugar

Instructions:

  1. Melt the dark chocolate and butter together.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and sugar.
  3. Combine the melted chocolate mixture with the egg mixture.
  4. Fold in the flour until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into greased ramekins.
  6. Bake the lava cakes in a preheated oven until the edges are set but the center is still gooey.
  7. While the cakes are baking, blend the raspberries and powdered sugar to create a smooth sauce.
  8. Once the cakes are done, invert them onto plates and drizzle with the raspberry sauce.

9. Fruit Parfait

Ingredients:

  • Greek yogurt
  • Fresh fruits (strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, etc.)
  • Granola
  • Honey

Instructions:

  1. Layer Greek yogurt, fresh fruits, and granola in a glass or bowl.
  2. Drizzle with honey.
  3. Repeat the layering process until the glass or bowl is full.
  4. Finish with a dollop of Greek yogurt and a sprinkle of granola on top.

10. Matcha Green Tea Panna Cotta

Ingredients:

  • Heavy cream
  • Sugar
  • Matcha green tea powder
  • Gelatin
  • Fresh berries for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream and sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Whisk in the matcha green tea powder until well combined.
  3. Sprinkle the gelatin over the mixture and let it bloom for a few minutes.
  4. Stir the mixture over low heat until the gelatin dissolves completely.
  5. Pour the mixture into individual serving cups or molds.
  6. Refrigerate until set, usually around 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Garnish with fresh berries before serving.

Now that you have these 10 mouthwatering recipes for stunning food plating, it’s time to get creative in the kitchen. Transform your meals into works of art and let your culinary skills shine. Experiment with different flavors, textures, and colors to create visually stunning dishes that will leave your guests in awe. Bon appétit!
