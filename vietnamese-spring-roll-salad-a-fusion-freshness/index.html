Vietnamese Spring Roll Salad: A Fusion Freshness


If you’re craving a light, fresh, and flavorful dish that’s a fusion of Vietnamese cuisine and salad goodness, then look no further than the Vietnamese Spring Roll Salad. This delightful recipe combines the vibrant flavors of traditional Vietnamese spring rolls with the crispness and healthfulness of a refreshing salad, making it a perfect choice for a light lunch, a side dish, or even a main course.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 head of green leaf lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup of bean sprouts
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup of fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup of fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 cup of chopped peanuts
  • 1/4 cup of fried shallots

For the Spring Roll Filling:

  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • 8 large cooked shrimp, peeled and halved lengthwise
  • 1 cup of cooked rice vermicelli noodles
  • 1/2 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup of fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup of fresh cilantro leaves

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 small red chili, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Salad Ingredients:

    • In a large bowl, combine the chopped lettuce, bean sprouts, julienned cucumber, julienned carrot, fresh mint leaves, and fresh cilantro leaves. Toss the ingredients together gently to mix.

  2. Prepare the Spring Roll Filling:

    • Fill a large bowl with warm water. Dip one rice paper wrapper into the water for a few seconds until it softens. Lay the wrapper flat on a clean, damp kitchen towel.
    • In the center of the wrapper, place 2 halves of the cooked shrimp, a small handful of rice vermicelli noodles, and a few leaves of basil, mint, and cilantro.
    • Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling, then tightly roll it up like a burrito. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling ingredients.

  3. Make the Dressing:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the fish sauce, lime juice, rice vinegar, sugar, minced garlic, and chopped red chili until the sugar is dissolved.

  4. Assemble the Salad:

    • Arrange the prepared spring roll salads on a serving platter or individual plates.
    • Drizzle the dressing over the salads, then sprinkle with the chopped peanuts and fried shallots.

  5. Serve and Enjoy!

    • Serve the Vietnamese Spring Roll Salad immediately, allowing everyone to drizzle extra dressing over their portions if desired.

The Fusion of Flavors

This Vietnamese Spring Roll Salad offers a delightful fusion of flavors and textures. The crispness of the fresh vegetables and the crunch of the peanuts and fried shallots provide a satisfying contrast to the soft and tender spring roll filling. The fragrant herbs and zesty dressing elevate the dish with their bright and aromatic notes, creating a harmony of tastes that will tantalize your taste buds.

Health Benefits

In addition to its delectable flavor, this salad is also a healthy choice. Packed with fiber, vitamins, and minerals from the fresh vegetables and herbs, it offers a nutritious boost to your day. The lean protein from the shrimp and the lightness of the rice paper wrappers make this salad a well-rounded and satisfying option for those looking to enjoy a wholesome meal.

Conclusion

The Vietnamese Spring Roll Salad brings together the best of both worlds – the invigorating freshness of a salad and the vibrant flavors of Vietnamese cuisine. Whether you’re a fan of Vietnamese food or simply looking for a new and exciting salad recipe, this fusion creation is sure to impress. It’s a wonderful way to enjoy the essence of spring rolls in a lighter and more refreshing form, perfect for warm weather or any time you crave a burst of freshness on your plate. So, gather your ingredients, unleash your creativity, and savor the fusion freshness of this delightful Vietnamese Spring Roll Salad!
