Spicy One Pot Cajun Shrimp and Grits


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and hearty meals, then you’re in for a treat with this Spicy One Pot Cajun Shrimp and Grits recipe. This Southern classic combines succulent shrimp, creamy grits, and a blend of spices that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy. And the best part? It all comes together in just one pot, making clean-up a breeze. So, let’s dive right into this flavorful adventure!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of stone-ground grits
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice tolerance)
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil. Gradually whisk in the grits and reduce the heat to low. Cook the grits for about 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they are creamy and tender.

  2. While the grits are cooking, heat a tablespoon of oil in a separate pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, and garlic, and sauté until they are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the shrimp to the pan and season them with Cajun seasoning, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook them, as they can become tough and rubbery.

  4. Once the shrimp are cooked, remove them from the pan and set them aside. In the same pan, melt the butter and stir in the heavy cream. Cook the cream for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

  5. Now, it’s time to bring everything together. Add the cooked shrimp to the pot of creamy grits and gently stir to combine. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.

  6. Serve the Spicy One Pot Cajun Shrimp and Grits in bowls, garnished with fresh parsley. This dish pairs well with a side of collard greens or a simple green salad.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a few dashes of hot sauce or sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the finished dish.
  • If you prefer a thicker consistency for your grits, reduce the amount of chicken broth by half.
  • Experiment with different types of seafood, such as crawfish or scallops, to create your own unique twist on this recipe.
  • To make it a complete meal, serve the Cajun Shrimp and Grits with a side of crusty bread or cornbread.

Final Thoughts

Spicy One Pot Cajun Shrimp and Grits is a comforting and flavorful dish that brings the taste of the South right to your kitchen. The combination of perfectly cooked shrimp, creamy grits, and a spicy Cajun seasoning blend will transport your taste buds to a world of culinary delight. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. So grab your apron, fire up the stove, and get ready to savor every mouthful of this delicious Southern classic. Enjoy!
