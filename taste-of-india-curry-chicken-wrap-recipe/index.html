Taste of India: Curry Chicken Wrap Recipe


If you’re looking for a flavorful and exotic dish to tantalize your taste buds, look no further than this delectable Taste of India: Curry Chicken Wrap recipe. Bursting with aromatic spices and succulent chicken, this dish is a true celebration of Indian cuisine. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a cooking novice, this recipe is sure to impress and delight your friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Curry Chicken Filling:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 can (14 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

For the Wrap:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Fresh lettuce leaves
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Greek yogurt or raita for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Curry Chicken Filling:

    • In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
    • Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet and cook for another minute until fragrant.
    • Stir in the curry powder, ground cumin, ground turmeric, paprika, and cayenne pepper, and cook for a minute to toast the spices.
    • Add the diced chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until they are no longer pink.
    • Pour in the diced tomatoes and chicken broth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors have melded together.

  2. Assemble the Curry Chicken Wraps:

    • Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable.
    • Place a generous spoonful of the curry chicken filling in the center of each tortilla.
    • Top the filling with fresh lettuce leaves, sliced cucumbers, and tomatoes.
    • Drizzle with Greek yogurt or raita for a cool and creamy contrast to the spicy curry chicken.
    • Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling and roll it up tightly to form a wrap.

  3. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Slice the wraps in half diagonally and serve them immediately, garnished with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor.
    • These Curry Chicken Wraps are perfect for a quick and satisfying lunch or dinner. They also make a delightful addition to any picnic or potluck gathering.

Conclusion

The Taste of India: Curry Chicken Wrap recipe is a delightful fusion of vibrant flavors and textures. The aromatic blend of spices combined with tender chicken, fresh vegetables, and cooling yogurt creates a truly memorable dining experience. Whether you’re craving a taste of India or simply looking to spice up your mealtime routine, these wraps are a must-try. Embrace the exotic allure of Indian cuisine with this simple yet extraordinary recipe, and transport your taste buds to a world of culinary delight.
