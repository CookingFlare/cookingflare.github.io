Italian Chicken Parmesan Recipe: A Classic Dish with a Twist


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, then you’re probably familiar with the deliciousness of Chicken Parmesan. This classic dish features breaded chicken cutlets, smothered in marinara sauce and melted cheese. It’s a crowd-pleaser that never fails to satisfy. But today, we’re going to take this beloved dish and give it a little twist to make it even more exciting and flavorful. Get ready for Italian Chicken Parmesan with a Twist!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  3. Take each chicken breast and coat it in the flour, shaking off any excess. Then dip it into the beaten eggs, making sure it’s fully coated. Finally, dredge the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the breadcrumbs adhere to the chicken.

  4. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Place the breaded chicken breasts in the skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. This step will give the chicken a nice crispy exterior.

  5. Once the chicken is nicely browned, transfer it to a baking dish. Spoon marinara sauce over each chicken breast, spreading it evenly. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce, covering the chicken completely.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  7. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and make the chicken even juicier.

  8. Serve the Italian Chicken Parmesan with a Twist hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. You can pair it with your favorite pasta or a side of garlic bread for a complete Italian feast.

The Twist: Adding Sun-Dried Tomatoes

Now, here comes the twist! To take this classic dish up a notch, we’re going to add sun-dried tomatoes to the marinara sauce. These flavorful gems bring a tangy sweetness that perfectly complements the savory chicken and cheese. Simply chop a handful of sun-dried tomatoes and stir them into the marinara sauce before spooning it over the chicken. The result? A burst of Mediterranean flavors that will make your taste buds dance with joy.

Final Thoughts

Italian Chicken Parmesan with a Twist is a delightful variation of the classic dish that will impress your family and friends. The combination of crispy breaded chicken, rich marinara sauce, melted cheese, and the tangy sweetness of sun-dried tomatoes creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving for more. So, why not give this recipe a try and experience the magic yourself? Bon appétit!
