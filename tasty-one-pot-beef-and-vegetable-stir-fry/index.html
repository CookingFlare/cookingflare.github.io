Tasty One Pot Beef and Vegetable Stir Fry


If you’re looking for a quick, delicious, and healthy meal that’s easy to prepare, then look no further than this Tasty One Pot Beef and Vegetable Stir Fry recipe. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want something hearty and satisfying without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced beef with soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Allow the beef to marinate for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables: While the beef is marinating, prepare the vegetables by slicing the onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, and julienned carrot. Wash the broccoli florets and snow peas, and set aside.

  3. Stir Fry: Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef to the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until it is browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Cook the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed, then add the sliced onion and cook for 2-3 minutes until it becomes translucent. Add the bell pepper, broccoli, snow peas, carrot, and mushrooms to the skillet and stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine and Season: Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the vegetables. Toss everything together and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve: Once the beef and vegetables are well combined and heated through, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the stir fry over cooked rice or noodles, and garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds if desired.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a spicy kick, add a dash of chili flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the stir fry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences. Add in some baby corn, water chestnuts, or snap peas for extra crunch and variety.
  • You can substitute the beef with chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a different protein option.
  • To save time, prep the vegetables and slice the beef in advance, so everything is ready to go when it’s time to cook.

This Tasty One Pot Beef and Vegetable Stir Fry is a versatile dish that’s sure to become a family favorite. It’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet while still enjoying the savory flavors of tender beef. Plus, the one-pot aspect makes for easy cleanup, leaving you with more time to savor your delicious creation.

So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and satisfying meal, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of flavors and textures that this stir fry has to offer!
