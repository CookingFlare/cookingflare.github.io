Festive Thanksgiving Waffle Recipes for a Fun Breakfast


Thanksgiving is a time for family, gratitude, and of course, delicious food. While most people think of turkey and pumpkin pie when it comes to Thanksgiving meals, why not start the day with a festive and fun breakfast? Waffles are a versatile and delightful way to kick off your Thanksgiving morning, and here are some creative and delicious recipes to make your breakfast extra special.

Pumpkin Spice Waffles

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, brown sugar, baking powder, salt, and spices.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the melted butter, milk, pumpkin puree, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. Gently fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until just combined.
  4. Preheat your waffle iron and lightly grease it with non-stick cooking spray.
  5. Pour the batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  6. Serve the waffles warm with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon for that extra festive touch.

These pumpkin spice waffles are a delightful blend of warm spices and pumpkin flavor, perfect for a cozy Thanksgiving morning.

Cranberry Orange Waffles

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1/2 cup fresh cranberries, coarsely chopped
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and orange zest.
  2. Gently fold in the chopped cranberries.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the melted butter, milk, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  5. Preheat your waffle iron and lightly grease it with non-stick cooking spray.
  6. Pour the batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  7. Serve the waffles with a drizzle of warm maple syrup and a garnish of fresh cranberries for a burst of Thanksgiving flavor.

The tangy cranberries and bright citrus flavors make these waffles a refreshing and festive addition to your Thanksgiving breakfast spread.

Apple Cinnamon Waffles

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 large apple, peeled and grated
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
  2. Stir in the grated apple until evenly distributed.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the melted butter, milk, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gently fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until just combined.
  5. Preheat your waffle iron and lightly grease it with non-stick cooking spray.
  6. Pour the batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  7. Serve the waffles with a dollop of freshly whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar for a delightful Thanksgiving treat.

The combination of sweet apples and warm cinnamon makes these waffles a comforting and delicious way to start your Thanksgiving celebration.

Whether you’re looking to add a festive touch to your Thanksgiving morning or simply want to enjoy a special breakfast with your loved ones, these Thanksgiving waffle recipes are sure to delight. So fire up that waffle iron and get ready to savor the flavors of the season!
