Satisfying and Nutritious Lentil and Kale Stew Recipes


If you’re looking for a hearty, nutritious, and flavorful meal, look no further than lentil and kale stew. This dish is not only satisfying and comforting, but it’s also packed with nutrients and is perfect for a cozy night in or a family gathering. In this blog post, I’ll share two delicious and unique recipes for lentil and kale stew that are sure to become staples in your kitchen.

Classic Lentil and Kale Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic, carrots, and celery to the pot and sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.
  3. Stir in the cumin and paprika, then add the rinsed lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth.
  4. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  5. Add the chopped kale to the pot and simmer for an additional 5 minutes until the kale is wilted.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve the stew hot, garnished with a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of fresh herbs if desired.

Spicy Coconut Lentil and Kale Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 red chili, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Coconut oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a spoonful of coconut oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped red chili to the pot and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the curry powder and turmeric, then add the rinsed red lentils, coconut milk, and vegetable broth.
  4. Bring the stew to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the chopped kale to the pot and simmer for an additional 5 minutes until the kale is tender.
  6. Season with salt to taste.
  7. Serve the stew hot, with a sprinkle of fresh cilantro and a side of warm naan bread.

Both of these lentil and kale stew recipes are not only delicious and hearty but also provide a wealth of nutrients. Lentils are an excellent source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential minerals, while kale is packed with vitamins and antioxidants. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, these recipes are easy to follow and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. So, next time you’re craving a comforting and nutritious meal, give one of these stew recipes a try and savor the wholesome goodness in every spoonful!
