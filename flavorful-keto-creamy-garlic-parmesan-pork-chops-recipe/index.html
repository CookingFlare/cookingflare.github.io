Flavorful Keto Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pork Chops Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly meal, look no further than this flavorful creamy garlic parmesan pork chops recipe. Packed with rich flavors and low in carbs, this dish is perfect for anyone following a ketogenic diet or simply looking for a scrumptious, low-carb meal option. The creamy garlic parmesan sauce perfectly complements the tender pork chops, making it a must-try for anyone who loves a good, hearty meal.

Ingredients:

For the Pork Chops:

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Pork Chops

  • Start by seasoning the pork chops with salt and pepper on both sides.
  • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  • Once the skillet is hot, add the pork chops and cook for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the pork chops from the skillet and set aside.

2. Make the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Sauce

  • In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  • Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.
  • Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer.
  • Stir in the grated parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Continue to cook the sauce, stirring frequently, until it thickens slightly.
  • Once the sauce has thickened, return the pork chops to the skillet, spooning the sauce over them.
  • Let the pork chops simmer in the sauce for a few minutes to soak up the flavors and ensure they are heated through.

3. Serve and Enjoy

  • Garnish the pork chops with fresh parsley before serving.
  • Plate the pork chops and spoon some of the creamy garlic parmesan sauce over the top.
  • Pair this dish with your favorite keto-friendly sides such as steamed vegetables or a crisp salad.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This creamy garlic parmesan pork chops recipe is a keto-friendly delight that’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. The juicy pork chops combined with the rich and creamy sauce create a dish that’s both comforting and indulgent. Plus, the ease of preparation makes it perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special weekend meal.
