Rare Roasted Red Pepper Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 red bell peppers
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove (peeled)
  • 1/2 onion (diced)
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 1 quart of vegetable broth

Instructions:

  1. Roast red bell peppers until charred. Transfer to a bowl, cover, and allow it to steam.
  2. Once cooled, peel off the skin, remove the seeds, and chop roughly.
  3. Sauté garlic and onion in olive oil until fragrant.
  4. Add roasted peppers, tomatoes, and vegetable broth to the pot.
  5. Simmer for about 20 minutes and blend until smooth. Serve hot with a drizzle of olive oil.

Quinoa Bacon Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 4-5 slices of bacon (cooked and crumbled)
  • 1 small avocado (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)

Instructions:

  1. Mix together cooked quinoa, crumbled bacon, and any remaining bacon fat.
  2. Incorporate avocado, corn, cherry tomatoes, and chopped scallions.
  3. Drizzle with your favorite dressing (nut-free, of course!) and season with salt and pepper.
