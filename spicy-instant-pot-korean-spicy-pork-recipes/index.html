Spicy Instant Pot Korean Spicy Pork Recipes


If you’re looking to spice up your dinner routine, look no further than this mouthwatering Spicy Instant Pot Korean Spicy Pork recipe! Known as Dae Ji Gukbap, this Korean dish is a flavorful combination of tender pork, aromatic spices, and a hint of sweetness that will transport you straight to the streets of Seoul. The Instant Pot makes the process incredibly easy and fast, allowing you to enjoy this delectable dish without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

To create this delicious and spicy pork dish, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Pork Marinade:

  • 2 lbs of pork shoulder or pork belly, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 2 tablespoons gochugaru (Korean red chili flakes)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

For the Cooking:

  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced (shiitake or button)
  • 1 cup water or chicken broth
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Equipment Needed

  • Instant Pot
  • Mixing bowl
  • Cutting board
  • Knife

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Pork

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a mixing bowl, combine gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, minced garlic, grated ginger, rice vinegar, and black pepper. Mix until well-combined to create a thick marinade.

  2. Marinate the Pork: Add the bite-sized pork pieces to the bowl and coat them thoroughly with the marinade. For the best flavor, let the pork marinate for at least 30 minutes, or even overnight in the refrigerator if you have time.

Step 2: Sauté the Vegetables

  1. Set Instant Pot to Sauté: Turn on your Instant Pot and set it to the sauté function. Allow it to heat up for a few minutes.

  2. Sauté the Onions: Add a splash of oil to the pot and toss in the sliced onions. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes, or until they become translucent.

  3. Add Remaining Vegetables: Next, add the sliced carrots, zucchini, and mushrooms, cooking for an additional 3-4 minutes until they begin to soften.

Step 3: Cook the Pork

  1. Add the Marinated Pork: Once the vegetables are sautéed, add the marinated pork to the Instant Pot. Stir well to ensure even distribution with the vegetables.

  2. Add Liquid: Pour in the water or chicken broth, making sure to scrape the bottom of the pot to release any flavorful bits stuck to the bottom.

  3. Set to Pressure Cook: Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to sealing. Select the pressure cook function and set the timer for 25 minutes on high pressure.

Step 4: Quick Release and Garnish

  1. Release Pressure: Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release by carefully turning the valve to venting. Be cautious of the steam!

  2. Garnish and Serve: Open the lid and give the pork a good stir. Serve it hot, garnished with chopped green onions and a sprinkle of sesame seeds. This dish pairs wonderfully with steamed rice or even on its own for a low-carb option.

Tips for Perfect Spicy Korean Pork

  • Control the Heat: Adjust the amount of gochugaru based on your spice tolerance. You can also add more honey or sugar to balance the heat if needed.
  • Vegetable Variations: Feel free to add other vegetables like bell peppers or bok choy, depending on your preference.
  • Leftovers: This dish makes excellent leftovers and can be stored in the refrigerator for up to three days. The flavors only get better with time!

Conclusion

This Spicy Instant Pot Korean Spicy Pork recipe is not just a meal; it is an experience filled with vibrant flavors and a touch of Korean culture. The Instant Pot takes the hassle out of cooking, making it easier than ever to enjoy this traditional dish at home. Whether you’re serving it for a family dinner or impressing guests, this recipe is sure to be a hit.

So grab your ingredients, fire up that Instant Pot, and get ready to indulge in a dish that tantalizes your taste buds and warms your soul. Enjoy your culinary adventure with this spicy delight!
