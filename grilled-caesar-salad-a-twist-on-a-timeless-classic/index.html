Grilled Caesar Salad: A Twist on a Timeless Classic


As the weather gets warmer, it’s the perfect time to fire up the grill and try something new. And what better way to do that than with a twist on a timeless classic? Today, we’re elevating the humble Caesar salad by giving it a smoky, charred flavor that can only come from grilling.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce hearts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Caesar dressing (homemade or store-bought)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Croutons (homemade or store-bought)
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the romaine lettuce hearts by cutting them in half lengthwise, leaving the core intact.

  2. Brush the cut sides of the lettuce hearts with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper to taste. This will help to enhance the flavors and prevent sticking on the grill.

  3. Place the lettuce hearts cut-side down on the grill grates and cook for approximately 2-3 minutes, or until they begin to char and wilt slightly. Flip the lettuce hearts over and grill for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  4. Once the lettuce hearts are nicely charred and slightly wilted, remove them from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter.

  5. Drizzle the grilled lettuce hearts with Caesar dressing, making sure to coat each piece evenly. The smoky flavor from the grill will infuse the dressing, adding another layer of complexity to the dish.

  6. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the grilled lettuce hearts, followed by a generous handful of croutons. The contrast of the crunchy croutons against the smoky lettuce will add texture and depth to each bite.

  7. Serve the grilled Caesar salad immediately, garnished with lemon wedges on the side. Squeezing fresh lemon juice over the top of the salad just before eating will brighten the flavors and provide a refreshing tang.

Tips and Variations

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can also grill some chicken or shrimp to add protein to your grilled Caesar salad. Simply season the meat with salt, pepper, and your favorite spices before grilling.

  • For a vegetarian twist, try grilling some halloumi cheese alongside the lettuce hearts. The salty, squeaky cheese develops a delicious crust when grilled and pairs perfectly with the charred lettuce.

  • Experiment with different toppings and additions to make this salad your own. Some popular options include cherry tomatoes, avocado slices, or even a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can achieve a similar effect by using a grill pan or even a cast-iron skillet on the stovetop. The key is to get that smoky, charred flavor on the lettuce.

Grilled Caesar salad is a delightful way to bring a new twist to a classic dish. The combination of the smoky, charred lettuce, creamy dressing, and crunchy croutons creates a harmony of flavors and textures that will leave you wanting more. So, fire up the grill and give this recipe a try – your taste buds will thank you!
