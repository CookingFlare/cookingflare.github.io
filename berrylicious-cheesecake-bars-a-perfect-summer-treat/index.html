Berrylicious Cheesecake Bars: A Perfect Summer Treat


If you’re looking for a refreshing and indulgent dessert to beat the summer heat, look no further than these Berrylicious Cheesecake Bars. Bursting with vibrant flavors and creamy goodness, these bars are the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply enjoying a lazy summer afternoon, these delightful bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the cheesecake filling:

  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the berry topping:

  • 2 cups fresh mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking dish, creating an even layer. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and granulated sugar together until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the graham cracker crust, spreading it out evenly with a spatula. Smooth the top with the back of a spoon.

  5. In a separate bowl, combine the fresh mixed berries, granulated sugar, and lemon juice. Gently toss the berries until they are coated in the sugar and lemon juice mixture.

  6. Carefully spoon the berry mixture over the cream cheese layer, distributing it evenly. Use a spoon to gently press the berries into the cream cheese mixture, creating a beautiful marbled effect.

  7. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.

  8. Once done, remove the baking dish from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature. Once cooled, refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the cheesecake to set.

  9. When ready to serve, carefully cut the cheesecake into bars and garnish with additional fresh berries, if desired. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a teaspoon of lemon zest to the crust mixture.
  • If you prefer a sweeter cheesecake, you can increase the amount of granulated sugar in the filling.
  • Feel free to customize the berry topping by using your favorite combination of fresh berries.
  • To achieve clean, even cuts, dip a sharp knife in hot water and wipe it dry before each slice.

These Berrylicious Cheesecake Bars are a delightful combination of creamy cheesecake, buttery crust, and juicy berries. They are the perfect balance of sweet and tangy, making them an ideal summer treat for both kids and adults alike. So, go ahead and indulge in this delicious dessert that captures the essence of summer in every bite. Enjoy!
