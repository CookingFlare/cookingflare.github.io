Wholesome Instant Pot Lentil Soup Recipes for a Nutritious Meal


Lentil soup is a staple in many households, cherished not only for its hearty flavor but also for its remarkable nutritional profile. Packed with protein, fiber, and essential vitamins, lentils are a versatile ingredient that can transform a simple soup into a wholesome meal. With the advent of the Instant Pot, preparing lentil soup has never been easier or quicker. Below, we explore several delightful lentil soup recipes that are perfect for any occasion, all cooked to perfection in your Instant Pot.

Benefits of Lentils

Before diving into the recipes, let’s take a moment to appreciate why lentils are a fantastic choice for a nutritious meal:

  1. High in Protein: Lentils are an excellent plant-based protein source, making them perfect for vegetarians and vegans.
  2. Rich in Fiber: They help promote digestive health and keep you feeling satisfied.
  3. Vitamins and Minerals: Lentils are packed with essential vitamins such as B vitamins, folate, and minerals like iron and magnesium.
  4. Low in Calories: A great option for those looking to maintain a healthy weight.

Essential Ingredients for Lentil Soup

While each recipe may vary, there are several key ingredients that often feature in lentil soup recipes. Here’s a quick rundown:

  • Lentils: Brown, green, red, or black—each type offers a unique texture and flavor.
  • Vegetables: Onions, carrots, celery, garlic, and tomatoes are common choices.
  • Broth: Vegetable or chicken broth serves as the soup’s base, adding depth to the flavor.
  • Herbs and Spices: Bay leaves, thyme, cumin, and paprika can elevate the dish.
  • Acid: A splash of lemon juice or vinegar adds brightness and balances the flavors.

Classic Instant Pot Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: Turn your Instant Pot to the sauté mode. Add olive oil and let it heat. Sauté the onion, carrots, and celery until the onion becomes translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Stir in the garlic, cumin, and smoked paprika, cooking for an additional minute until fragrant.

  2. Add Lentils and Broth: Add the lentils, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes (with their juice), and bay leaf. Stir to combine.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to sealing. Select the pressure cook setting and set the timer for 15 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes, then carefully switch the valve to venting to release any remaining pressure.

  5. Season and Serve: Remove the bay leaf, season with salt and pepper to taste, and garnish with fresh herbs if desired. Enjoy your nourishing soup!

Spicy Curried Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In the Instant Pot, set to sauté mode. Melt the coconut oil and add the onion, garlic, and ginger. Cook until the onion is soft and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Add Spices and Lentils: Stir in the curry powder and turmeric, cooking for an additional minute. Add the red lentils, coconut milk, vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes. Mix well.

  3. Pressure Cook: Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook for 10 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: After cooking, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes before switching to venting.

  5. Blend for Creaminess: For a creamier texture, use an immersion blender to blend the soup to your desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with cilantro before serving.

Mediterranean Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown or green lentils
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Feta cheese for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Base: Start by selecting the sauté mode on your Instant Pot. Heat the olive oil, then add the onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened, approximately 5 minutes. Stir in the garlic, oregano, and thyme, cooking for another minute.

  2. Combine Ingredients: Add the lentils, diced tomatoes, and broth to the pot. Stir well to combine.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: Allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes before venting.

  5. Finish and Serve: Stir in the lemon juice and season with salt and pepper. Serve with a sprinkle of feta cheese if desired. This soup pairs wonderfully with crusty bread.

Hearty Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green lentils, rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary
  • 8 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: In the Instant Pot, heat olive oil on sauté mode. Add the onion, carrots, and celery; cook until softened, around 4-5 minutes. Stir in the garlic, thyme, and rosemary, cooking for an additional minute.

  2. Add Remaining Ingredients: Add the lentils, diced potatoes, vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes. Stir to combine.

  3. Pressure Cook: Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 25 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: Allow natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then vent remaining pressure.

  5. Season and Serve: Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste. This heartwarming soup is perfect for cold days and can be stored for meal prep throughout the week.

Tips for Perfect Lentil Soup

  1. Choosing Lentils: Different lentils have varying cooking times and textures. Red lentils become mushy, while green and brown lentils hold their shape better. Choose based on your texture preference.

  2. Flavor Development: Allow the soup to sit after cooking; flavors deepen as it cools. Reheating the next day often yields an even tastier dish.

  3. Storage: Lentil soup can be stored in the refrigerator for up to five days or frozen for up to three months. Freeze in portions for easy meal prep.

  4. Add-Ins and Variations: Feel free to customize your lentil soup by adding other vegetables like spinach, kale, or bell peppers. You can also experiment with different spices and herbs to suit your taste.

Conclusion

Lentil soup is a nourishing, budget-friendly meal that can be tailored to your taste preferences. The convenience of the Instant Pot allows for quick preparation without sacrificing flavor or nutrition. Whether you prefer a classic recipe or something with a bit of spice, these wholesome lentil soup recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings and provide you with a hearty, nutritious meal. Enjoy experimenting and discovering your favorite variations as you embrace the wonderful world of lentil soups!
