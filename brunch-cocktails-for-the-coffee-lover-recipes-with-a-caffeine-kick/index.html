Brunch Cocktails for the Coffee Lover: Recipes with a Caffeine Kick


Are you a coffee aficionado who loves to indulge in a boozy brunch? If so, you’re in for a treat! We’ve curated a collection of tantalizing brunch cocktail recipes that are perfect for coffee lovers. These cocktails are designed to give you that much-needed caffeine kick while also tantalizing your taste buds. So, grab your cocktail shaker and let’s get mixing!

Espresso Martini

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz coffee liqueur
  • 1 oz freshly brewed espresso
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.
  2. Pour the vodka, coffee liqueur, espresso, and simple syrup into the shaker.
  3. Shake vigorously for about 10 seconds.
  4. Strain the mixture into a chilled martini glass.
  5. Garnish with a few coffee beans for that extra touch.

The Espresso Martini is a classic brunch cocktail that perfectly marries the bold flavors of coffee with the smoothness of vodka. It’s the ideal pick-me-up for a lazy Sunday brunch.

Mocha Mint Julep

Preparation time: 7 minutes

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • 1/2 oz chocolate liqueur
  • 1/2 oz freshly brewed coffee
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Crushed ice

Instructions

  1. In a glass, muddle a few fresh mint leaves to release the aroma.
  2. Fill the glass with crushed ice.
  3. Pour the bourbon, chocolate liqueur, and freshly brewed coffee over the ice.
  4. Stir gently to combine all the ingredients.
  5. Garnish with a sprig of mint.

The Mocha Mint Julep is a delightful twist on the classic mint julep, infusing it with the rich flavors of coffee and chocolate. It’s a refreshing and invigorating cocktail that’s perfect for a sunny brunch outdoors.

Coffee Old Fashioned

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • 1/2 oz cold brew coffee
  • 1/4 oz maple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel twist

Instructions

  1. In a mixing glass, combine the bourbon, cold brew coffee, maple syrup, and bitters.
  2. Fill the glass with ice and stir for about 30 seconds.
  3. Strain the mixture into a rocks glass filled with ice.
  4. Express the oil from the orange peel over the drink and drop it into the glass.

The Coffee Old Fashioned puts a caffeinated spin on the timeless classic, adding depth and complexity with the infusion of cold brew coffee. It’s a sophisticated cocktail that’s perfect for those who appreciate the finer things in life.

Spiked Iced Caramel Macchiato

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz rum
  • 1 oz coffee liqueur
  • 1 oz caramel syrup
  • 2 oz milk
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. In a shaker, combine the rum, coffee liqueur, caramel syrup, and milk.
  2. Shake well for about 15 seconds.
  3. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  4. Strain the mixture into the glass.

The Spiked Iced Caramel Macchiato is a luscious and indulgent cocktail that brings together the flavors of creamy caramel and rich coffee with a delightful kick of rum. It’s the ultimate treat for those with a sweet tooth.

Conclusion

These brunch cocktails for the coffee lover are sure to elevate your brunch experience to a whole new level. Whether you’re hosting a gathering with friends or simply unwinding on a lazy weekend morning, these caffeine-infused concoctions are bound to impress. So, go ahead, shake up one of these delightful cocktails and savor the harmonious blend of coffee and spirits. Cheers to indulgent brunches and unforgettable flavors!
