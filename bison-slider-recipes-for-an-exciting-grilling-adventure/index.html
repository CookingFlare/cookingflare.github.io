Bison Slider Recipes for an Exciting Grilling Adventure


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious sliders. If you’re looking to step up your grilling game and try something new, why not give bison sliders a go? Bison meat is lean, flavorful, and a healthier alternative to traditional beef sliders. In this blog post, we’ll explore some exciting bison slider recipes that will take your grilling adventure to the next level.

Bison and Mushroom Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground bison
  • 1 cup finely chopped mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Toppings of your choice (lettuce, tomato, cheese, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the ground bison, mushrooms, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into small patties, about 2-3 inches in diameter.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Place the bison patties on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. Remove the patties from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
  6. Assemble your sliders by placing a patty on each bun and adding your favorite toppings.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Southwest Bison Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground bison
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Toppings of your choice (guacamole, salsa, sour cream, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the ground bison, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into small patties.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  4. Grill the bison patties for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through.
  5. Remove the patties from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
  6. Toast the slider buns on the grill for a minute or two.
  7. Assemble your sliders by placing a patty on each bun and adding your desired toppings.
  8. Serve and enjoy the delicious Southwest flavors!

Blue Cheese Bison Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground bison
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped red onion
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Toppings of your choice (caramelized onions, arugula, bacon, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the ground bison, blue cheese, red onion, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into small patties.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Place the bison patties on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are cooked to your liking.
  5. Remove the patties from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, toast the slider buns on the grill for a minute or two.
  7. Assemble your sliders by placing a patty on each bun and adding your desired toppings.
  8. Serve and savor the bold and tangy flavors of the blue cheese!

Conclusion

Grilling bison sliders is a fantastic way to add excitement to your outdoor cooking adventures. Whether you prefer the earthy flavors of mushrooms, the boldness of Southwest spices, or the tanginess of blue cheese, these recipes offer something for everyone. So, fire up your grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a mouthwatering bison slider feast. Happy grilling!
