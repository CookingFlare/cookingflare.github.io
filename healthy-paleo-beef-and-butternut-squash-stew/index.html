Healthy Paleo Beef and Butternut Squash Stew

3 min · 532 words

Are you looking for a hearty and nutritious meal that’s also paleo-friendly? Look no further! This Healthy Paleo Beef and Butternut Squash Stew is packed with flavor, protein, and wholesome ingredients that will keep you satisfied and energized. Plus, it’s easy to make and perfect for those chilly evenings when you need something warm and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of grass-fed beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into cubes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the coconut oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the beef stew meat to the pot and brown on all sides. This will help to seal in the juices and add extra flavor to the stew.

  3. Once the beef is browned, add the butternut squash, carrots, celery, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried thyme, dried rosemary, paprika, and season with salt and pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Pour in the beef broth and bring the stew to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let the stew simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  5. After the stew has simmered, taste and adjust the seasonings as needed. You can add more salt, pepper, or herbs to suit your taste.

  6. Serve the Healthy Paleo Beef and Butternut Squash Stew hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired. This stew pairs perfectly with a side of cauliflower rice or a simple green salad.

Why Choose Paleo?

The paleo diet focuses on consuming whole, unprocessed foods that our ancestors would have eaten. It eliminates grains, legumes, dairy, and refined sugars, and instead emphasizes lean proteins, vegetables, fruits, nuts, and seeds. This Healthy Paleo Beef and Butternut Squash Stew fits perfectly into the paleo lifestyle as it incorporates nutrient-dense ingredients and avoids any processed or inflammatory foods.

Health Benefits

This stew is not only delicious but also offers a range of health benefits. Let’s take a look at some of the key ingredients and how they contribute to your well-being:

  • Grass-fed Beef: Grass-fed beef is higher in omega-3 fatty acids and lower in unhealthy fats compared to conventionally raised beef. It also contains more vitamins and minerals, including iron and zinc.

  • Butternut Squash: Butternut squash is rich in beta-carotene, a powerful antioxidant that’s converted into vitamin A in the body. It also provides fiber, potassium, and vitamin C.

  • Vegetables: Carrots and celery are excellent sources of vitamins and minerals, while onions and garlic add flavor and have immune-boosting properties.

  • Coconut Oil: Coconut oil is a healthy source of fat that contains medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs), which can boost metabolism and provide a quick source of energy.

  • Herbs and Spices: Thyme, rosemary, and paprika not only enhance the taste of the stew but also offer antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Conclusion

With its hearty ingredients and nourishing qualities, this Healthy Paleo Beef and Butternut Squash Stew is a must-try recipe for anyone following a paleo lifestyle or simply looking for a wholesome and satisfying meal. The combination of grass-fed beef, butternut squash, and flavorful herbs and spices creates a stew that is both delicious and nutritious. So grab your pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a bowl of pure comfort!
