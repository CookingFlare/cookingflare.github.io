Drool-Worthy Breakfast Grilled Cheese Sandwiches


Are you ready to take your breakfast game to a whole new level? Look no further than these drool-worthy breakfast grilled cheese sandwiches. This breakfast twist on the classic grilled cheese is a surefire way to start your day with a smile. Whether you’re a fan of savory or sweet breakfasts, there’s a delicious grilled cheese sandwich waiting for you to savor.

Classic Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of thick-cut bread
  • 8 slices of cooked bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook the bacon until crispy. Set aside on a paper towel to drain excess grease.
  2. In the same skillet, crack the eggs and cook to your desired doneness. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Place a slice of bread, buttered side down, in a separate skillet over medium heat.
  5. Layer the cooked bacon, eggs, and shredded cheddar cheese on the bread.
  6. Top with another slice of bread, buttered side up.
  7. Cook the sandwich for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Repeat with the remaining ingredients to make another sandwich.

Berry and Cream Cheese Stuffed French Toast Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of brioche bread
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Maple syrup (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. Spread the cream cheese mixture on 4 slices of brioche bread.
  3. Top the cream cheese mixture with a generous amount of mixed berries.
  4. Place the remaining slices of brioche bread on top to form sandwiches.
  5. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs and milk.
  6. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  7. Dip each sandwich into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are coated.
  8. Place the sandwiches in the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and the filling is warmed through.
  9. Drizzle with maple syrup if desired before serving.

Avocado, Tomato, and Mozzarella Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of sourdough bread.
  2. Place a slice of bread, buttered side down, in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Layer the avocado, tomato, mozzarella, and basil on the bread.
  4. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and top with another slice of bread, buttered side up.
  5. Cook the sandwich for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  6. Slice the sandwich in half and serve hot.

These drool-worthy breakfast grilled cheese sandwiches are the ultimate comfort food to kickstart your day. Whether you prefer the classic combination of bacon, egg, and cheese, a sweet and indulgent stuffed French toast variation, or a fresh and flavorful avocado, tomato, and mozzarella option, there’s a breakfast grilled cheese sandwich for every palate. Try these recipes and get ready to elevate your breakfast experience!
