A Fresh Delight: Strawberry Tart with Vanilla Cream


Are you ready to indulge in the luscious sweetness of summer? Look no further than this exquisite recipe for a Strawberry Tart with Vanilla Cream. This delightful dessert combines the vibrant flavors of fresh strawberries with the velvety richness of vanilla cream, all nestled within a buttery, flaky tart crust. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a special treat, this strawberry tart is sure to impress and delight.

Ingredients

For the Tart Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons ice water

For the Vanilla Cream:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons powdered sugar

For the Strawberry Topping:

  • 1 lb fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons strawberry jam
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tart Crust:

    • In a food processor, pulse together the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold cubed butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk and ice water. Drizzle the egg mixture into the food processor and pulse until the dough comes together.
    • Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disk. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  2. Make the Vanilla Cream:

    • In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream, vanilla extract, and powdered sugar until stiff peaks form. Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.

  3. Bake the Tart Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle slightly larger than your tart pan. Carefully transfer the dough to the pan, pressing it into the corners and trimming any excess. Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork.
    • Line the crust with parchment paper and fill with pie weights or dried beans. Bake for 15 minutes. Remove the weights and parchment paper, then bake for an additional 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Allow to cool completely.

  4. Assemble the Tart:

    • In a small saucepan, heat the strawberry jam and water over low heat until melted. Brush the bottom of the tart crust with the melted jam.
    • Spread the vanilla cream evenly over the bottom of the tart crust.
    • Arrange the sliced strawberries on top of the cream in a decorative pattern.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Garnish the tart with fresh mint leaves for a pop of color and added freshness.
    • Slice and serve this delightful strawberry tart to your guests or savor it with a loved one. Enjoy the harmonious blend of flavors and the decadent texture of this delightful dessert.

Conclusion

This Strawberry Tart with Vanilla Cream is a celebration of the season’s bounty, bringing together the natural sweetness of fresh strawberries with the indulgent creaminess of vanilla. This recipe is a testament to the beauty of simple, quality ingredients coming together to create a truly memorable dessert. Whether it’s a sunny afternoon or a special occasion, this strawberry tart is a fresh delight that’s sure to leave a lasting impression. Happy baking!
