Fresh Strawberry Tart: A Summery Dessert for Easter Brunch


The arrival of spring brings with it an abundance of fresh and vibrant produce, and there’s no better way to celebrate the season than with a delicious and refreshing dessert. This Fresh Strawberry Tart is the perfect addition to your Easter brunch table, featuring juicy strawberries nestled on a buttery crust and topped with a luscious cream filling. With its beautiful presentation and irresistible flavors, this tart is sure to impress your guests and leave them wanting more.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons ice water

For the cream filling:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup powdered sugar

For the strawberry topping:

  • 2 pints fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
  • ¼ cup strawberry jam

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and sugar. Add the cold butter cubes and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk and ice water. Gradually pour the egg mixture into the flour mixture, stirring until the dough starts to come together.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently a few times until it forms a smooth ball. Flatten the dough into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Baking the Crust

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle slightly larger than your tart pan. Carefully transfer the rolled dough to the tart pan, pressing it into the bottom and sides.
  3. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edges and use a fork to prick the bottom of the crust. This will prevent it from puffing up during baking.
  4. Line the crust with parchment paper or aluminum foil and fill it with pie weights or dried beans to keep the crust from shrinking.
  5. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. Remove the weights and parchment paper, and continue baking for an additional 5 minutes to ensure the crust is fully cooked. Set aside to cool completely.

Step 3: Making the Cream Filling

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream, vanilla extract, and powdered sugar. Using an electric mixer or a whisk, beat the mixture until stiff peaks form. Be careful not to overbeat, as it may result in a grainy texture.
  2. Once the crust has cooled, spread the cream filling evenly over the bottom of the tart shell. Make sure to create a smooth and level surface.

Step 4: Assembling the Tart

  1. Arrange the halved strawberries on top of the cream filling, starting from the outer edge and working your way towards the center. Be creative with the placement, creating a visually appealing pattern.
  2. In a small saucepan, warm the strawberry jam over low heat until it becomes liquid. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  3. Using a pastry brush, gently brush the strawberry jam over the top of the strawberries. This will give the tart a glossy finish and enhance the flavors.
  4. Refrigerate the tart for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together and the filling to set.

Step 5: Serving the Tart

  1. Carefully remove the tart from the refrigeration and transfer it to a serving platter.
  2. Slice the tart into wedges and serve it chilled. You can garnish each slice with a sprig of fresh mint for an extra touch of elegance.

Conclusion

This Fresh Strawberry Tart is a delightful dessert that captures the essence of spring and adds a burst of flavor to your Easter brunch. The buttery crust provides a perfect base for the velvety cream filling, while the tangy strawberries add a refreshing and vibrant touch. With its beautiful presentation and irresistible taste, this tart is sure to become a favorite among your family and friends. So go ahead, embrace the flavors of the season and indulge in this summery treat that will leave everyone craving for more. Enjoy!
