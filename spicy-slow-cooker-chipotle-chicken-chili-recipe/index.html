Spicy Slow Cooker Chipotle Chicken Chili Recipe


Are you looking for a hearty and flavorful dish that will warm you up on a chilly evening? Look no further than this Spicy Slow Cooker Chipotle Chicken Chili recipe. Packed with bold flavors and a hint of heat, this chili is a perfect balance of smoky chipotle, tender chicken, and an array of aromatic spices. Plus, the convenience of using a slow cooker makes it an ideal meal for busy weeknights or laid-back weekends. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this comforting dish that’s sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, finely chopped
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Begin by preparing the ingredients. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces, chop the onion, mince the garlic, and finely chop the chipotle peppers.

  2. In the slow cooker, combine the diced tomatoes, black beans, red kidney beans, chopped onion, minced garlic, and chipotle peppers. Stir to mix the ingredients evenly.

  3. Add the chicken pieces to the slow cooker, spreading them out in an even layer over the bean mixture.

  4. In a separate bowl, mix the chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Stir until the spices are well incorporated into the broth.

  5. Pour the seasoned chicken broth over the ingredients in the slow cooker, ensuring that the liquid covers the chicken and beans.

  6. Cover the slow cooker and cook the chili on low heat for 6-8 hours, or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  7. Once the chili is ready, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  8. Serve the Spicy Slow Cooker Chipotle Chicken Chili hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and accompanied by lime wedges for a burst of citrusy flavor.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the seasoning mix.
  • If you prefer a thicker chili, you can mash some of the beans with a fork or potato masher before serving.
  • This chili can be served over rice or with a side of warm cornbread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts:

This Spicy Slow Cooker Chipotle Chicken Chili recipe offers a delightful blend of smoky, spicy, and savory flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. The tender chunks of chicken, combined with the richness of the beans and the warmth of the spices, create a truly comforting dish that’s perfect for sharing with loved ones. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or simply craving a cozy meal at home, this chili is a fantastic choice that’s easy to prepare and guaranteed to impress. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of this soul-warming dish!
