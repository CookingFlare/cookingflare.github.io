Springtime Seafood Suppers: Recipes for Fresh and Flavorful Meals


Spring is the perfect time to enjoy the bounty of the sea. As the weather gets warmer and the days get longer, seafood becomes a popular choice for those looking to create fresh and flavorful meals. Whether you’re a seafood lover or just looking to incorporate more seafood into your diet, these springtime seafood supper recipes are sure to impress. From light and zesty shrimp dishes to succulent salmon creations, there’s something here for everyone.

1. Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Squeeze the lemon juice over the shrimp and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss the shrimp in the lemon garlic butter sauce until well-coated.
  6. Serve the shrimp hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

2. Grilled Salmon with Mango Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on the grill, skin-side down, and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  4. In a bowl, combine the diced mango, red onion, jalapeno, lime juice, and cilantro.
  5. Season the mango salsa with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the grilled salmon hot, topped with the mango salsa.

3. Garlic Butter Scallops with Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of scallops
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the scallops to the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Transfer the scallops to a baking sheet lined with foil.
  6. Arrange the trimmed asparagus around the scallops on the baking sheet.
  7. Drizzle the asparagus with the garlic butter from the skillet.
  8. Season the scallops and asparagus with salt and pepper.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the scallops are cooked through and the asparagus is tender.
  10. Serve the garlic butter scallops and asparagus hot, with fresh lemon wedges on the side.

4. Spicy Thai Coconut Curry Mussels

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of Thai red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the coconut milk over medium heat.
  2. Add the Thai red curry paste, fish sauce, and brown sugar to the pot and stir until well-combined.
  3. Add the sliced red bell pepper and onion to the pot and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the cleaned mussels to the pot and cover with a lid.
  5. Cook the mussels for 5-7 minutes, or until they have opened.
  6. Discard any mussels that have not opened.
  7. Serve the spicy Thai coconut curry mussels hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Conclusion

Spring is a wonderful time to indulge in the fresh flavors of seafood. Whether you prefer shrimp, salmon, scallops, or mussels, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. From tangy lemon garlic butter shrimp to spicy Thai coconut curry mussels, these springtime seafood suppers are bursting with flavor and perfect for any occasion. So go ahead, dive into these delicious recipes and enjoy the taste of spring!
