Italian Caprese Salad Recipe with Fresh Mozzarella and Tomatoes


When it comes to Italian cuisine, there are few dishes as iconic and beloved as the Caprese salad. This simple yet delicious salad celebrates the flavors of fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and fragrant basil, all brought together with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and a sprinkle of salt. It’s a dish that embodies the essence of Italian cooking – fresh, vibrant, and full of flavor. In this blog post, we’ll explore the history of the Caprese salad, the key ingredients, and of course, a step-by-step recipe to create this classic dish in your own kitchen.

The History of Caprese Salad

The Caprese salad hails from the beautiful island of Capri, located in the Gulf of Naples in Italy. The salad is said to have been created as a homage to the colors of the Italian flag, with the red of the tomatoes, the white of the mozzarella, and the green of the basil representing the tricolor. It’s a dish that has become synonymous with the Mediterranean diet, known for its emphasis on fresh, seasonal ingredients and simple preparation.

Key Ingredients

Fresh Mozzarella

The star of the Caprese salad is undoubtedly the fresh mozzarella. Made from cow’s milk, this cheese has a soft, delicate texture and a mild, milky flavor. When selecting mozzarella for your Caprese salad, look for the freshest you can find. Ideally, use mozzarella di bufala, made from buffalo milk, for an even more authentic experience.

Ripe Tomatoes

The quality of the tomatoes is crucial to the success of your Caprese salad. Look for ripe, juicy tomatoes that are bursting with flavor. Heirloom tomatoes, with their diverse colors and shapes, can add visual interest to the dish, but any ripe, in-season tomatoes will work beautifully.

Fresh Basil

Basil contributes a peppery, slightly sweet flavor and a wonderful aroma to the salad. Look for fresh basil leaves that are vibrant green and free from any blemishes. The aroma of fresh basil is a key component of the Caprese experience.

Extra Virgin Olive Oil

A drizzle of high-quality extra virgin olive oil brings richness and depth to the Caprese salad. Look for a good quality, fruity olive oil to complement the flavors of the other ingredients.

Sea Salt

A sprinkle of flaky sea salt is the finishing touch that enhances the flavors of the mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil. Use it sparingly, as a little goes a long way.

Recipe: Classic Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Flaky sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the slices of mozzarella and tomatoes on a serving platter, alternating them for visual appeal.
  2. Tuck the fresh basil leaves in between the slices of mozzarella and tomatoes.
  3. Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil over the salad, ensuring each slice is lightly coated.
  4. Sprinkle the salad with a pinch of flaky sea salt.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the fresh flavors of Italy.

The Perfect Summer Dish

The beauty of the Caprese salad lies in its simplicity. It’s a dish that allows the quality of the ingredients to shine, making it the perfect choice for showcasing the bounty of summer produce. Whether served as an appetizer, a side dish, or a light lunch, the Caprese salad is sure to evoke the sun-drenched flavors of the Mediterranean.

So, the next time you find yourself with a basket of ripe tomatoes and a bunch of fragrant basil, consider creating a classic Caprese salad. With just a few simple ingredients, you can capture the essence of Italian cuisine and delight your taste buds with the vibrant flavors of this timeless dish. Buon appetito!
