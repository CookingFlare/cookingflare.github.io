Paleo Apple Crisp: A Delicious and Healthy Dessert Option


As the crisp autumn air sets in, our taste buds begin to crave the warm, comforting flavors of the season. One classic dessert that never fails to hit the spot is apple crisp, with its tender apples, crunchy topping, and sweet aroma wafting from the oven. But what if you’re following a paleo diet, and traditional recipes loaded with refined sugar and grains are off the table? Fear not, dear dessert lovers! Today, we’ll be exploring a paleo apple crisp recipe that’s just as delicious as its traditional counterpart, but with a healthier twist.

The Magic of Paleo Apple Crisp

So, what makes paleo apple crisp so special? For starters, we’re ditching the refined sugar and relying on natural sweeteners like honey and maple syrup to add a touch of sweetness to our dessert. We’re also swapping out traditional oats for a crunchy topping made from almond flour and coconut flakes, which provide a satisfying texture without the grains. And let’s not forget the star of the show: tender, juicy apples, carefully selected to ensure maximum flavor and texture.

The Recipe

Now that we’ve got the basics covered, let’s dive into the recipe itself. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 6-8 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

To start, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, honey, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Toss until the apples are evenly coated with the sweet and spicy mixture.

In a separate bowl, mix together the almond flour, coconut flakes, and a pinch of salt. Add in the coconut oil and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

Pour the apple mixture into a 9x9-inch baking dish and top with the crumb mixture. If using walnuts, sprinkle them evenly over the top of the crumb mixture.

Baking and Enjoying

Bake the paleo apple crisp for 40-45 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve warm, topped with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream, if desired.

The Verdict

So, what’s the verdict on this paleo apple crisp recipe? In short, it’s a game-changer. The combination of tender apples, crunchy topping, and sweet aroma is a match made in heaven, and the lack of refined sugar and grains means you can indulge without guilt. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or just looking for a healthier dessert option, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite.

Conclusion

And there you have it, folks – a delicious and healthy paleo apple crisp recipe to enjoy all season long. With its natural sweeteners, grain-free topping, and tender apples, this dessert is the perfect way to satisfy your sweet tooth without compromising on health. So go ahead, give it a try, and experience the magic of paleo apple crisp for yourself.
