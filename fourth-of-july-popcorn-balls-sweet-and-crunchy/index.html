Fourth of July Popcorn Balls: Sweet and Crunchy


Are you looking for a fun and delicious treat to celebrate the Fourth of July? Look no further than these sweet and crunchy Fourth of July Popcorn Balls! These patriotic snacks are not only easy to make but also perfect for sharing with family and friends at your Independence Day gathering. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 10 cups of popped popcorn
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup of light corn syrup
  • 1/3 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Red and blue food coloring
  • Non-stick cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Prepare the popcorn by popping 10 cups of popcorn using your preferred method. Remove any unpopped kernels and set the popcorn aside in a large bowl.

  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the granulated sugar, light corn syrup, water, unsalted butter, and salt. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir continuously until the mixture comes to a boil.

  3. Once the mixture reaches a boil, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 2 minutes. Make sure to stir occasionally to prevent the mixture from burning.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Let the mixture cool for a few minutes.

  5. Divide the mixture evenly into three separate bowls. Add a few drops of red food coloring to one bowl and blue food coloring to another bowl. Leave the third bowl without any food coloring for white popcorn balls.

  6. Pour the red mixture over one-third of the popcorn and gently toss until the popcorn is evenly coated. Repeat this step with the blue mixture for another one-third of the popcorn.

  7. Spray your hands with non-stick cooking spray to prevent sticking, and then quickly shape the coated popcorn into balls. You can make them as big or as small as you like, depending on your preference.

  8. Repeat step 7 with the remaining one-third of the popcorn, leaving it uncolored for white popcorn balls.

  9. Place the popcorn balls on a parchment-lined baking sheet and let them cool completely. This will allow them to harden and hold their shape.

  10. Once the popcorn balls have cooled and hardened, they are ready to be enjoyed! Arrange them on a festive platter or wrap them individually in plastic wrap for easy sharing.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some extra crunch and flavor, consider mixing in some festive sprinkles or mini chocolate chips into the popcorn before shaping them into balls.
  • For a healthier alternative, you can use air-popped popcorn and substitute the granulated sugar with a natural sweetener like honey or maple syrup.
  • Customize your popcorn balls by adding in other mix-ins like chopped nuts, dried fruits, or even mini marshmallows for a gooey surprise.

These Fourth of July Popcorn Balls are sure to be a hit at your Independence Day celebration. Their sweet and crunchy goodness, combined with their patriotic colors, make them a festive and delicious treat for everyone to enjoy. So, gather your loved ones, put on some fireworks, and indulge in these delightful snacks that capture the spirit of the holiday. Happy Fourth of July!
