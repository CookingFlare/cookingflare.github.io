Savory French Lentil Soup Recipes for a Nutritious Meal


Are you looking for a hearty and nutritious meal that will warm your soul? Look no further than savory French lentil soup! Packed with protein, fiber, and a variety of delicious flavors, this soup is not only satisfying but also incredibly healthy. In this blog post, we will explore different French lentil soup recipes that will surely become a staple in your kitchen.

Classic French Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups French green lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the lentils, bay leaf, vegetable broth, and water to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the bay leaf before serving.

French Lentil Soup with Bacon

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups French green lentils
  • 6 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the lentils, bay leaf, chicken broth, and water to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the soup topped with the crispy bacon.

French Lentil Soup with Spinach and Lemon

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups French green lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the lentils, bay leaf, vegetable broth, and water to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  3. Stir in the chopped spinach and lemon juice. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, until the spinach is wilted.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the soup hot.

French Lentil Soup with Sausage and Herbs

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups French green lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 4 sausages, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (such as thyme, rosemary, or parsley), chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the lentils, bay leaf, vegetable broth, and water to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  3. In a separate pan, cook the sliced sausages over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir the cooked sausages and fresh herbs into the soup. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.

Enjoy the comforting and nutritious goodness of French lentil soup! These recipes are versatile and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Whether you prefer a classic version or want to experiment with different ingredients, French lentil soup is sure to become a favorite in your household. So grab a bowl, cozy up, and savor the flavors of this delicious and wholesome meal.
