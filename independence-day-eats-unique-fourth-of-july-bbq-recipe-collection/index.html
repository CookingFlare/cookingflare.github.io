Grilled Watermelon Salad with Feta and Mint

Ingredients:

  • 1 small seedless watermelon
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Watermelon: Cut the watermelon into 1-inch thick slices. Remove the rind and then cut the slices into triangles or cubes, as desired.
  2. Preheat the Grill: Heat your grill to medium-high. Brush the watermelon slices with olive oil.
  3. Grill the Watermelon: Place the watermelon slices on the grill. Grill for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until grill marks appear and the watermelon is warmed through.
  4. Assemble the Salad: In a large bowl, combine the grilled watermelon, crumbled feta, and chopped mint. Drizzle with balsamic glaze, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately as a refreshing side dish that pairs perfectly with grilled meats.

This vibrant salad combines the sweetness of grilled watermelon with the saltiness of feta and the freshness of mint, making it a unique addition to your BBQ spread.

Spicy Grilled Corn on the Cob with Lime Butter

Ingredients:

  • 6 ears of corn, husked
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Corn: Husk the corn and remove all silk. Soak the corn in cold water for 30 minutes to prevent burning on the grill.
  2. Make Lime Butter: In a bowl, mix together softened butter, lime juice, chili powder, garlic powder, and salt until well combined.
  3. Preheat the Grill: Heat your grill to medium-high.
  4. Grill the Corn: Remove the corn from the water and shake off excess. Place the corn directly on the grill and cook for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until nicely charred.
  5. Finish with Butter: Once grilled, slather the corn with lime butter and serve with lime wedges for squeezing.

This spicy twist on a classic BBQ side will have your guests reaching for seconds!

BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders with Pineapple Slaw

Ingredients:

  • 3 pounds pork shoulder
  • 2 cups BBQ sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 12 slider buns
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup diced fresh pineapple
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pork: Rub the pork shoulder with salt and pepper. Place it in a slow cooker and cover with BBQ sauce. Cook on low for 8 hours or until the pork is tender and easily shredded.
  2. Make the Slaw: In a bowl, combine shredded cabbage, shredded carrots, diced pineapple, mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside.
  3. Shred the Pork: Once cooked, shred the pork with two forks and mix it with the remaining BBQ sauce in the slow cooker.
  4. Assemble the Sliders: Toast the slider buns if desired. Place a generous amount of pulled pork on each bun and top with pineapple slaw.
  5. Serve: Serve these sliders warm for a delicious and satisfying main dish that everyone will love.

The combination of sweet and spicy flavors in these sliders makes them a standout choice for your Independence Day BBQ.

Firecracker Shrimp Tacos with Avocado Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the Shrimp: In a bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  2. Cook the Shrimp: Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and cooked through. Remove from heat.
  3. Make Avocado Cream: In a blender, combine avocado, sour cream, lime juice, and a pinch of salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Prepare the Tacos: Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over the grill. Fill each tortilla with a few shrimp and drizzle with avocado cream.
  5. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately for a zesty and fresh taco option.

These firecracker shrimp tacos are bursting with flavor and will add a festive touch to your Fourth of July celebration.

Red, White, and Blue Berry Trifle

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound strawberries, sliced
  • 1 pound blueberries
  • 1 pound raspberries
  • 1 pound pound cake, cubed
  • 2 cups whipped cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Cream: In a mixing bowl, combine whipped cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Whip until soft peaks form.
  2. Layer the Trifle: In a large trifle dish or individual cups, layer cubed pound cake, a mix of berries, and whipped cream. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with whipped cream on top.
  3. Chill and Serve: Refrigerate the trifle for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld. Serve chilled for a refreshing and festive dessert.

This eye-catching dessert not only tastes amazing but also pays homage to the colors of the American flag, making it a perfect addition to your Independence Day menu.
