Homestyle Chicken and Biscuits: A Comforting Dinner


There’s something about a homestyle meal that warms the heart and brings back memories of sitting around the dinner table with loved ones. And when it comes to comfort food, few dishes can rival the classic combination of homestyle chicken and biscuits. This timeless recipe is not only delicious but also easy to make, making it an ideal choice for a comforting dinner.

Ingredients

To start off, let’s gather the ingredients for our homestyle chicken and biscuits:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Now that we have everything we need, let’s get cooking!

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, and then place them on a baking sheet. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through. Set aside to cool slightly, and then shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  2. While the chicken is baking, let’s make the biscuits. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to cut it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk. Stir until just combined, being careful not to overmix.

  3. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together. Roll the dough out to a 1-inch thickness, and then use a biscuit cutter to cut out the biscuits. Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.

  4. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and sauté until softened and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes. Add the shredded chicken and cook for another 2 minutes.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, and bring the mixture to a simmer. Cook for 5 minutes or until the sauce has thickened slightly. Stir in the frozen peas and cook for another 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. To serve, split the biscuits in half and place them on individual plates. Spoon the creamy chicken mixture over the biscuits, and garnish with fresh herbs if desired. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors of homestyle chicken and biscuits.

Conclusion

Homestyle chicken and biscuits are a classic combination that never fails to provide comfort and warmth. With its tender shredded chicken, creamy sauce, and fluffy biscuits, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. It’s the kind of meal that brings people together and creates lasting memories. So why not treat yourself to a comforting dinner tonight? Gather the ingredients, follow the simple steps, and indulge in the deliciousness of homestyle chicken and biscuits. Your taste buds will thank you!
