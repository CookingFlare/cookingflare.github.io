Easy Homemade Rice Krispies Treats Recipe


If you’re looking for a simple, delicious, and fun treat to whip up in no time, homemade Rice Krispies treats are the way to go! Whether you’re preparing a snack for your kids, hosting a party, or just craving something sweet, these gooey, crispy bars are a crowd-pleaser that everyone will love. In this recipe, I’ll walk you through a step-by-step guide to making these delightful treats from scratch. The best part? You only need a few ingredients, and they come together in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make Rice Krispies treats at home, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 6 cups of Rice Krispies cereal
  • 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 package (10 oz) of marshmallows (or about 40 large marshmallows)
  • A pinch of salt (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)

These basic ingredients come together to create the perfect balance of sweetness and crunch. If you’re feeling adventurous, you can customize your Rice Krispies treats by adding fun ingredients like chocolate chips, sprinkles, or even peanut butter!

Equipment You’ll Need

You don’t need any fancy equipment to make Rice Krispies treats. Here’s what you’ll need to get started:

  • A large saucepan
  • A large mixing bowl
  • A spatula (preferably silicone)
  • A 9x13-inch baking dish (or a similar-sized dish)
  • Cooking spray or butter to grease the baking dish

Once you have everything in place, you’re ready to get started!

Instructions: How to Make Rice Krispies Treats

Step 1: Prepare Your Baking Dish

Before you start making your Rice Krispies treats, it’s a good idea to grease your baking dish. You can use cooking spray, butter, or even a non-stick baking spray to lightly coat the inside of your 9x13-inch dish. This will prevent the treats from sticking to the dish once they’re set and ready to cut into squares.

Step 2: Melt the Butter and Marshmallows

In a large saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Stir it gently to ensure that it melts evenly without burning. Once the butter is melted, add the marshmallows to the saucepan and stir constantly.

Keep stirring until the marshmallows have completely melted and formed a smooth, gooey mixture. You want to make sure there are no lumps or unmelted pieces of marshmallow left in the pan. If desired, you can add a pinch of salt and a teaspoon of vanilla extract to enhance the flavor of the treats, but this is completely optional.

Step 3: Mix in the Rice Krispies Cereal

Once your marshmallow mixture is nice and smooth, it’s time to add the Rice Krispies cereal. Turn off the heat and slowly pour the cereal into the marshmallow mixture. Stir everything together gently using your spatula, making sure that each piece of cereal is coated with the sticky marshmallow mixture. You want the cereal to be evenly coated, but be careful not to crush the cereal too much.

If you’re adding any extra ingredients like chocolate chips, candy, or sprinkles, now is the time to fold them in!

Step 4: Press the Mixture into the Baking Dish

Once the cereal is fully coated with the marshmallow mixture, it’s time to transfer it to your prepared baking dish. Use your spatula to spread the mixture evenly across the dish. To prevent the mixture from sticking to your spatula, you can lightly butter the spatula or use a piece of wax paper to press the mixture down.

Make sure the mixture is packed tightly into the dish. This will help the treats hold their shape once they’ve set and cooled.

Step 5: Let the Treats Cool and Set

After pressing the mixture into the baking dish, allow the Rice Krispies treats to cool and set at room temperature. This will usually take about 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the temperature of your kitchen. If you’re in a hurry, you can speed up the process by placing the dish in the fridge for about 15-20 minutes.

Once they’ve set, the treats should be firm and easy to cut into squares.

Step 6: Cut and Serve

Once the treats have cooled and set, use a sharp knife to cut them into squares or rectangles, depending on your preferred size. If the mixture sticks to your knife, you can lightly grease it with a bit of butter to make cutting easier.

Now, it’s time to serve and enjoy! These homemade Rice Krispies treats are perfect for packing in lunchboxes, bringing to a party, or enjoying as an after-school snack.

Tips and Variations for Perfect Rice Krispies Treats

Although this recipe is pretty straightforward, there are a few tips and variations you can try to make your treats even better.

1. Use Fresh Marshmallows

If possible, use fresh marshmallows for the best results. Older marshmallows can lose their fluffiness, making it harder to get that perfect gooey texture. Fresh marshmallows melt more evenly and create that soft, chewy treat we all know and love.

2. Experiment with Different Cereal Varieties

While Rice Krispies cereal is the classic choice for these treats, you can experiment with other cereals to change things up. Try using Cocoa Krispies for a chocolatey twist or Cornflakes for a slightly different texture. You can even use puffed rice or other puffed cereals for a fun spin on the traditional recipe.

3. Add Mix-ins for Extra Flavor

Feel free to add your favorite mix-ins to your Rice Krispies treats. Chocolate chips, mini M&Ms, sprinkles, or even small pieces of candy can be stirred in to create a fun, personalized treat. You could also try adding a spoonful of peanut butter for a creamy and nutty flavor.

4. Try Dipping the Treats in Chocolate

For an extra special touch, dip the cut squares of Rice Krispies treats into melted chocolate. You can use milk, dark, or white chocolate for this step. Once dipped, allow the chocolate to set before serving. This adds a delicious chocolatey coating to your treats, taking them to a whole new level of indulgence.

5. Make Them Gluten-Free

Rice Krispies treats are naturally gluten-free, but be sure to double-check that the Rice Krispies cereal you’re using is certified gluten-free. Some brands may use barley malt extract, which contains gluten, so it’s always best to look for a certified gluten-free option if you’re catering to dietary restrictions.

Storing Your Rice Krispies Treats

If you have any leftovers (though that’s unlikely!), storing your homemade Rice Krispies treats is easy. To keep them fresh:

  • Room Temperature: Store the treats in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.
  • Refrigerator: If you want to keep them fresh for longer, you can store the treats in the fridge for up to a week.
  • Freezing: You can also freeze Rice Krispies treats for up to 3 months. To freeze, wrap them tightly in plastic wrap or place them in a freezer-safe container.

When you’re ready to eat the frozen treats, simply thaw them at room temperature for a few hours.

Why You’ll Love This Easy Recipe

There’s so much to love about homemade Rice Krispies treats. They are:

  • Quick to Make: This recipe is fast, and you can have a batch of treats ready in under 30 minutes.
  • Budget-Friendly: With just a few simple ingredients, these treats are an affordable way to satisfy your sweet tooth.
  • Customizable: Whether you’re adding mix-ins, experimenting with different cereals, or dipping them in chocolate, Rice Krispies treats are versatile and easy to customize to your tastes.
  • Perfect for Any Occasion: From birthdays to family gatherings or even as a lunchbox treat, Rice Krispies treats are a hit with kids and adults alike.

Whether you’re making them for a special occasion or simply for a quick snack, this easy homemade Rice Krispies treats recipe is sure to become a go-to favorite in your kitchen. So, grab those few ingredients, fire up your stove, and get ready to enjoy a classic treat that never goes out of style!
