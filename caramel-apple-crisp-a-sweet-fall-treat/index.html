Caramel Apple Crisp: A Sweet Fall Treat


Fall is the season of cozy sweaters, colorful leaves, and, of course, delicious comfort food. One dessert that perfectly captures the essence of autumn is the classic Caramel Apple Crisp. This warm and gooey treat combines the tartness of apples with the sweetness of caramel and the crunch of a buttery oat topping. Whether you’re hosting a fall dinner party or simply craving a comforting dessert, this Caramel Apple Crisp recipe is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 6 large apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans (optional)

For the Caramel Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. In a large bowl, gently toss the sliced apples with granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice until well coated. Transfer the apple mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  3. In another bowl, mix together the oats, flour, brown sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Pour in the melted butter and stir until the mixture is crumbly. If desired, fold in the chopped pecans for an extra crunch.

  4. Sprinkle the oat topping over the apples in the baking dish, covering them completely.

  5. Bake the Caramel Apple Crisp in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apples are tender.

  6. While the crisp is baking, prepare the caramel sauce. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and stir in the brown sugar, heavy cream, and salt. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, stirring constantly, and let it bubble for 2 minutes. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.

  7. Once the Caramel Apple Crisp is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the warm caramel sauce over the top of the crisp before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Caramel Apple Crisp is best enjoyed warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The contrast of the cold ice cream against the warm, gooey apple filling is simply divine. For an extra indulgent treat, add a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon on top.

Conclusion

With its comforting flavors and irresistible aroma, Caramel Apple Crisp is a quintessential fall dessert that never fails to impress. Whether you’re baking it for a special occasion or simply to treat yourself, this recipe is a delightful way to celebrate the season. So, gather your loved ones, embrace the fall vibes, and savor every spoonful of this sweet and satisfying treat. Happy baking!
