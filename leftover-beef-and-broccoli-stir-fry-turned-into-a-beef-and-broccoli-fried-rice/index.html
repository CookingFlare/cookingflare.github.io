Leftover Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Turned into a Beef and Broccoli Fried Rice


So, you made a delicious beef and broccoli stir-fry for dinner last night, and now you have some leftovers sitting in the fridge. Instead of simply reheating the same dish, why not transform it into something new and exciting? In this recipe, we’ll show you how to turn your leftover beef and broccoli stir-fry into a flavorful and satisfying beef and broccoli fried rice. This dish is not only a great way to repurpose leftovers, but it’s also a quick and easy meal that’s perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients:

  • Leftover beef and broccoli stir-fry
  • Cooked white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Ingredients

Start by gathering your leftover beef and broccoli stir-fry and cooked white rice. If the beef and broccoli stir-fry is in large pieces, consider chopping them into smaller, bite-sized pieces to ensure even distribution throughout the fried rice.

2. Scramble the Eggs

In a large skillet or wok, heat a small amount of vegetable oil over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the skillet and scramble them with a spatula. Once the eggs are cooked through, remove them from the skillet and set them aside.

3. Heat the Skillet

Using the same skillet, add a bit more vegetable oil if needed and turn the heat to medium-high. Add the leftover beef and broccoli stir-fry to the skillet, stirring occasionally to heat it through.

4. Add the Rice

Once the beef and broccoli stir-fry is heated, add the cooked white rice to the skillet. Use a spatula to break up any clumps of rice and mix it with the beef and broccoli. Stir the ingredients together until they are well combined.

5. Season the Fried Rice

Drizzle a few dashes of soy sauce and a splash of sesame oil over the rice mixture. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue to stir the fried rice, ensuring that the seasonings are evenly distributed.

6. Incorporate the Scrambled Eggs

Add the scrambled eggs back into the skillet with the rice and beef, stirring to combine. The eggs will add a fluffy and savory element to the fried rice, complementing the flavors of the beef and broccoli.

7. Garnish and Serve

Once the fried rice is heated through and well mixed, remove the skillet from the heat. Transfer the beef and broccoli fried rice to serving plates or bowls, and garnish with chopped green onions for a pop of freshness and color.

Conclusion

Leftover beef and broccoli stir-fry has been given a new lease on life with this simple and delicious beef and broccoli fried rice recipe. By repurposing leftovers in creative ways, you can elevate your meals and reduce food waste. This dish is versatile and can be customized with additional vegetables or protein, making it a go-to recipe for using up whatever ingredients you have on hand. Whether you’re cooking for one or feeding a family, this beef and broccoli fried rice is sure to become a favorite in your meal rotation. Enjoy!
