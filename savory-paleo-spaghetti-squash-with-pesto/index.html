Savory Paleo Spaghetti Squash with Pesto


If you’re in the mood for a hearty, satisfying meal that’s also paleo-friendly, look no further than this savory spaghetti squash with pesto recipe. With its vibrant colors and flavors, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. The combination of tender spaghetti squash and vibrant, herbaceous pesto creates a dish that’s not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Plus, it’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet without sacrificing flavor. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium spaghetti squash
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast (optional, for a cheesy flavor)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Spaghetti Squash:

    • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Carefully cut the spaghetti squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds.
    • Drizzle the cut sides of the squash with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    • Place the squash halves, cut side down, on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    • Roast in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes or until the squash is tender and the flesh can be easily shredded into “spaghetti” strands with a fork.

  2. Make the Pesto:

    • While the squash is roasting, prepare the pesto.
    • In a food processor, combine the basil, pine nuts, garlic, and nutritional yeast (if using).
    • Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped.
    • With the food processor running, drizzle in the extra virgin olive oil until the pesto reaches your desired consistency.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Assemble the Dish:

    • Once the spaghetti squash is tender, use a fork to scrape the flesh into spaghetti-like strands.
    • Transfer the shredded squash to a large bowl and toss with the prepared pesto until evenly coated.
    • Serve the savory paleo spaghetti squash with a sprinkle of additional pine nuts and a few fresh basil leaves for garnish.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

This savory paleo spaghetti squash with pesto is a winning combination for several reasons. Firstly, the spaghetti squash provides a nutritious, low-carb alternative to traditional pasta, making it an excellent choice for those following a paleo lifestyle. Additionally, the homemade pesto adds a burst of fresh, herbaceous flavor that complements the mild sweetness of the squash.

Moreover, this dish is incredibly versatile. While the recipe calls for a classic basil and pine nut pesto, feel free to get creative with your pesto variations. Try using spinach or arugula in place of basil, or experiment with different nuts like almonds or walnuts. The options are endless, allowing you to personalize this dish to suit your taste preferences.

Furthermore, this recipe is a great way to introduce more plant-based meals into your diet. By incorporating nutrient-dense ingredients like spaghetti squash and fresh basil, you’re treating your body to a wealth of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. It’s a dish that not only tastes amazing but also nourishes your body from the inside out.

In conclusion, this savory paleo spaghetti squash with pesto is a delightful, nutrient-packed dish that’s perfect for a cozy weeknight dinner or a special gathering with friends and family. It’s a testament to the fact that healthy eating can be both delicious and satisfying. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every forkful of this delightful paleo creation. Cheers to good food and great company!
