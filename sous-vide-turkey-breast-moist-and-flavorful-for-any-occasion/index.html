Sous Vide Turkey Breast: Moist and Flavorful for Any Occasion


If you’re looking for a foolproof way to achieve a perfectly moist and flavorful turkey breast, then look no further than sous vide cooking. This cooking method has gained popularity for its ability to lock in juices and flavors, resulting in tender and succulent meat that’s sure to impress your guests. Whether you’re hosting a special dinner party or simply craving a delicious turkey dish, this sous vide turkey breast recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole turkey breast, boneless and skinless
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh thyme, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Sous Vide Water Bath: Fill a large container with water and preheat your sous vide machine to 145°F (63°C).

  2. Season the Turkey Breast: In a small bowl, mix the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, salt, and pepper. Rub the turkey breast with olive oil and then generously coat it with the herb mixture, ensuring that the entire surface is well seasoned.

  3. Vacuum Seal the Turkey Breast: Place the seasoned turkey breast into a vacuum-sealable bag and carefully remove the air before sealing it.

  4. Sous Vide Cooking: Gently lower the sealed turkey breast into the preheated water bath, ensuring that it is fully submerged. Cook the turkey breast for 4 hours, maintaining the water temperature at 145°F (63°C).

  5. Searing the Turkey Breast (Optional): After the turkey breast has been sous vide cooked, you can achieve a golden-brown exterior by searing it in a hot skillet with a bit of olive oil for 1-2 minutes on each side.

  6. Rest and Serve: Once the turkey breast is seared (if desired), remove it from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in an even more succulent and flavorful turkey breast. Slice the turkey breast against the grain and serve it with your favorite sides or sauces.

Tips for Success

  • Use a high-quality vacuum sealer to ensure that the turkey breast is properly sealed for sous vide cooking.
  • Experiment with different herb combinations to customize the flavor profile of the turkey breast.
  • If you prefer crispy skin, you can remove the skin from the turkey breast, season it separately, and crisp it up in the oven while the turkey is sous vide cooking.

Why Sous Vide Turkey Breast?

Sous vide cooking offers precise temperature control, ensuring that the turkey breast is cooked to perfection without the risk of overcooking. The gentle cooking process also allows the flavors from the herbs and seasonings to infuse into the meat, resulting in a turkey breast that’s incredibly moist and bursting with flavor.

Additionally, sous vide cooking is a convenient method for preparing turkey breast in advance. Once the turkey breast is cooked sous vide, it can be quickly seared and served, making it an ideal choice for busy occasions or when you want to spend more time with your guests and less time in the kitchen.

Final Thoughts

Sous vide turkey breast is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on various occasions, from holiday gatherings to casual weeknight dinners. The precise control and incredible results of sous vide cooking make this recipe a standout choice for anyone who appreciates tender, juicy, and flavorful turkey breast. Give this recipe a try, and prepare to elevate your turkey game to a whole new level!
