Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Curry Recipe: A Flavorful and Healthy Option


Are you tired of the same old recipes? Looking to spice up your dinner routine with something delicious and nutritious? Well, look no further! In this blog post, we are going to share with you an amazing Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Curry recipe that will tantalize your taste buds and keep you feeling satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of lentils (any variety)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, peas, etc.)
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and set aside.
  2. Turn on your Instant Pot and select the sauté function. Add a splash of oil and sauté the diced onion until translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot and sauté for another minute, until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, paprika, and salt. Cook for a minute to allow the spices to bloom.
  5. Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth, and give everything a good stir.
  6. Add the rinsed lentils and mixed vegetables to the pot, making sure they are well-coated with the sauce.
  7. Cancel the sauté function and secure the lid on the Instant Pot. Set the pressure release valve to sealing position.
  8. Select the manual or pressure cook function and set the timer for 12 minutes on high pressure.
  9. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully quick-release any remaining pressure.
  10. Stir in the coconut milk and let the curry sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  11. Serve the Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Curry over a bed of fluffy rice or with some warm naan bread.
  12. Garnish with fresh cilantro for added freshness and flavor.

Why Choose Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot is a versatile and time-saving kitchen appliance that allows you to prepare delicious meals in a fraction of the time compared to traditional cooking methods. This recipe showcases the convenience of the Instant Pot by combining all the ingredients into one pot and letting the pressure cooker do its magic. The result? A flavorful and hearty Lentil and Vegetable Curry that will make your taste buds dance with delight.

Health Benefits of Lentils and Vegetables

Not only is this recipe packed with flavor, but it’s also incredibly healthy. Lentils are a great source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential minerals. They are known to improve heart health, aid in digestion, and promote stable blood sugar levels. Additionally, lentils are low in fat and calories, making them an excellent choice for weight management.

The inclusion of mixed vegetables adds an array of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to the curry. Vegetables are essential for maintaining a balanced diet and supporting overall health. They provide essential nutrients, promote healthy digestion, and contribute to a strong immune system.

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Curry recipe is a perfect option for those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet or for anyone who simply enjoys a flavorful and healthy dish. With the convenience of the Instant Pot and the nutritious combination of lentils and vegetables, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So, go ahead, give it a try, and let your taste buds thank you for the explosion of flavors!
