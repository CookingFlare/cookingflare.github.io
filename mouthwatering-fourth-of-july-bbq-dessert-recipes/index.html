Mouthwatering Fourth of July BBQ Dessert Recipes


The Fourth of July is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a mouthwatering BBQ dessert spread? From classic favorites to creative twists, we’ve got you covered with these delicious dessert recipes that will surely impress your guests. So fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in these sweet treats!

Grilled Berry Skewers

If you’re looking for a simple yet delightful dessert option, these grilled berry skewers are just what you need. The combination of sweet, juicy berries and the subtle smoky flavor from the grill is a match made in heaven. Here’s how you can make them:

Ingredients:

  • Assorted berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Wooden skewers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Thread the berries onto the wooden skewers, alternating between different types of berries.
  3. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until the berries are slightly charred.
  4. Remove from the grill and serve immediately. You can serve them as is or with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence.

Grilled Watermelon with Lime and Mint

Watermelon is a classic summer fruit, and grilling it takes its sweetness to a whole new level. The addition of lime and mint adds a refreshing twist that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Here’s how you can make this unique dessert:

Ingredients:

  • Watermelon slices
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Fresh mint leaves, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the watermelon slices directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear.
  3. Remove the watermelon slices from the grill and drizzle them with fresh lime juice.
  4. Sprinkle the finely chopped mint leaves over the watermelon slices.
  5. Let the flavors meld together for a few minutes before serving. This dessert is best enjoyed chilled.

Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon and Honey

Grilled pineapple is a tropical delight that brings a burst of flavor to any barbecue. The caramelization from the grill, combined with the warmth of cinnamon and sweetness of honey, creates a truly irresistible dessert. Here’s how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • Pineapple slices
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Honey

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the pineapple slices directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear.
  3. Remove the pineapple slices from the grill and sprinkle them with ground cinnamon.
  4. Drizzle honey over the grilled pineapple slices, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  5. Let the flavors meld together for a few minutes before serving. You can serve them as is or with a scoop of coconut ice cream for a tropical twist.

Grilled Banana S’mores

S’mores are a classic campfire treat, but have you ever tried grilling your bananas before making them into s’mores? The result is a gooey, caramelized delight that takes this dessert to a whole new level. Here’s how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • Ripe bananas
  • Chocolate squares
  • Marshmallows
  • Graham crackers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Slice the bananas lengthwise, leaving the skin on.
  3. Place the banana halves, cut side down, on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until grill marks appear.
  4. Remove the bananas from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  5. Peel the bananas and place a piece of chocolate and a marshmallow on each half.
  6. Sandwich the banana halves together and wrap them tightly in aluminum foil.
  7. Place the foil-wrapped bananas back on the grill and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the marshmallows have melted.
  8. Remove from the grill and carefully unwrap the foil. Serve the grilled banana s’mores with graham crackers for dipping.

Conclusion

This Fourth of July, elevate your BBQ game with these mouthwatering dessert recipes. From grilled berry skewers to indulgent grilled banana s’mores, these desserts are sure to be a hit with your family and friends. So fire up the grill, embrace the summer flavors, and celebrate Independence Day with these delicious treats. Happy Fourth of July!
