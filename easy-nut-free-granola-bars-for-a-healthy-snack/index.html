Easy Nut-Free Granola Bars for a Healthy Snack


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious snack that is packed with flavor but free from nuts? Look no further! These Easy Nut-Free Granola Bars are the perfect solution. They are quick to make, require minimal ingredients, and are loaded with wholesome goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup crispy rice cereal
  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup dried apricots, chopped
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the oats, crispy rice cereal, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, dried cranberries, and dried apricots. Mix well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the honey, brown sugar, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture is well combined.

  3. Pour the melted butter mixture over the dry ingredients. Use a spatula or your hands to mix everything together until the dry ingredients are fully coated.

  4. Line a 9x13-inch baking dish with parchment paper. Transfer the granola mixture into the prepared dish and press it down firmly with the back of a spoon or your hands to create an even layer.

  5. Place the baking dish in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until the granola bars have set.

  6. Once the granola bars are firm, remove them from the refrigerator and lift them out of the baking dish using the parchment paper. Place the bars on a cutting board and slice them into individual bars of your desired size.

  7. Store the granola bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week. Enjoy them as a quick and nutritious snack whenever you’re on the go!

Why Choose Nut-Free Granola Bars?

Nut allergies are becoming increasingly common, making it essential to have nut-free snack options available. These Easy Nut-Free Granola Bars are not only safe for those with nut allergies, but they also offer a range of health benefits.

By incorporating oats, crispy rice cereal, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, dried cranberries, and dried apricots, these granola bars provide a good source of fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Oats are known for their heart-healthy properties, while seeds are packed with essential fatty acids and antioxidants. The dried fruits add a touch of natural sweetness and contribute to the bars’ chewy texture.

Customize Your Granola Bars

Feel free to experiment with different add-ins to create your own unique flavor combinations. Here are some ideas to get you started:

  • Swap out the dried cranberries and apricots for raisins and dried cherries.
  • Add a handful of chocolate chips or shredded coconut for a touch of indulgence.
  • Mix in a tablespoon of chia seeds or flaxseeds for an extra boost of nutrition.

The possibilities are endless, so don’t be afraid to get creative and tailor the granola bars to your taste preferences.

Conclusion

These Easy Nut-Free Granola Bars are a fantastic option for a healthy and satisfying snack. They are simple to make, require minimal ingredients, and can be customized to suit your personal preferences. Whether you have a nut allergy or simply want to enjoy a delicious and nutritious snack, give these granola bars a try. You won’t be disappointed!

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients and whip up a batch of these Easy Nut-Free Granola Bars today. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
