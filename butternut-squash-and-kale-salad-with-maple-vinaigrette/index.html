Butternut Squash and Kale Salad with Maple Vinaigrette


Are you looking for a healthy and delicious salad recipe that will satisfy your taste buds and keep you feeling energized? Look no further! This Butternut Squash and Kale Salad with Maple Vinaigrette is the perfect combination of flavors and textures, making it a standout dish for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 cups kale, stems removed and leaves torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Maple Vinaigrette

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, toss the butternut squash cubes with olive oil, salt, and pepper until evenly coated. Transfer the coated squash to a baking sheet and spread it out in a single layer.

  3. Roast the butternut squash in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until it becomes tender and slightly caramelized. Make sure to flip the cubes halfway through the cooking time for even browning.

  4. While the squash is roasting, prepare the maple vinaigrette. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside.

  5. In a large salad bowl, combine the torn kale leaves, dried cranberries, crumbled feta cheese, and chopped walnuts.

  6. Once the roasted butternut squash is ready, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Add the roasted squash to the salad bowl.

  7. Drizzle the maple vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.

  8. Allow the salad to sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This will also give the kale a chance to soften slightly.

  9. Serve the Butternut Squash and Kale Salad with Maple Vinaigrette as a side dish or a light meal on its own. Enjoy the vibrant colors, delightful textures, and the perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors.

Conclusion

This Butternut Squash and Kale Salad with Maple Vinaigrette is a true crowd-pleaser that will impress even the most discerning palates. The combination of roasted butternut squash, hearty kale, tangy cranberries, creamy feta cheese, and crunchy walnuts creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave you craving more.

Not only is this salad delicious, but it is also packed with nutrients. Kale is a superfood known for its high antioxidant content, while butternut squash provides a good source of vitamins A and C. The maple vinaigrette adds a touch of sweetness that perfectly complements the earthy flavors of the vegetables.

Whether you’re looking for a healthy lunch option, a refreshing side dish, or a vibrant addition to your dinner table, this Butternut Squash and Kale Salad with Maple Vinaigrette is sure to hit the spot. Give it a try and let your taste buds dance with delight!
