XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Chip Cookies with Walnuts and Sea Salt: A Valentine's Day Treat Recipe


Are you searching for the perfect Valentine’s Day treat to impress your loved ones? Look no further! These XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Chip Cookies with Walnuts and Sea Salt are the ultimate indulgence for this special occasion. With their rich chocolatey flavor, crunchy walnuts, and a touch of sea salt, these cookies are sure to make your sweetheart’s heart skip a beat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons xanthan gum
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • Sea salt, for sprinkling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, salt, and xanthan gum.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Fold in the chocolate chips and chopped walnuts until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  8. Gently flatten each cookie with the back of a spoon and sprinkle a pinch of sea salt on top of each flattened cookie.

  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may appear slightly undercooked, but they will firm up as they cool.

  10. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  11. Once cooled, serve these delectable XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Chip Cookies with Walnuts and Sea Salt to your loved ones and watch their faces light up with joy.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra indulgent twist, drizzle melted dark or white chocolate over the cooled cookies.
  • Replace the walnuts with your favorite nut or omit them altogether if you prefer a nut-free version.
  • Experiment with different types of chocolate chips, such as milk chocolate or dark chocolate, to create unique flavor combinations.
  • Store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to one week.

These XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Chip Cookies with Walnuts and Sea Salt are the ultimate Valentine’s Day treat. The combination of rich chocolate, crunchy walnuts, and a hint of sea salt creates a flavor explosion that will leave your taste buds begging for more. Whether you’re baking them for your significant other, your friends, or just treating yourself, these cookies are guaranteed to spread love and happiness on this special day. So go ahead, whip up a batch of these delightful cookies and let the magic of Valentine’s Day come alive in every bite. Happy baking!
