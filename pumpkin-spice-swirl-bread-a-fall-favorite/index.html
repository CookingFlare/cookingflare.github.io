Pumpkin Spice Swirl Bread: A Fall Favorite


Fall brings with it the warm hues of changing leaves, cozy evenings by the fire, and the delicious aroma of freshly baked goods. One iconic treat that perfectly captures the essence of autumn is Pumpkin Spice Swirl Bread. This delightful loaf combines the rich flavors of pumpkin, warm spices, and a sweet swirl, making it a must-have for your fall baking repertoire. Follow along to learn how to create this seasonal favorite in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

For the Bread:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Swirl:

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a separate large bowl, mix together the granulated sugar, brown sugar, and oil. Add in the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the pumpkin puree, milk, and vanilla extract.

  4. Combine Wet and Dry: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Make the Swirl: In a small bowl, mix the granulated sugar and cinnamon for the swirl.

  6. Assemble the Bread: Pour half of the batter into the prepared loaf pan. Sprinkle half of the cinnamon sugar mixture over the batter. Pour the remaining batter on top, then sprinkle the remaining cinnamon sugar on top.

  7. Swirl: Use a knife to swirl the batter and cinnamon sugar together gently.

  8. Bake: Bake the bread for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  9. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing. Serve slices slightly warm or at room temperature.

Tips and Variations:

  • Add Nuts: For extra crunch, fold in a half cup of chopped nuts like pecans or walnuts into the batter.
  • Cream Cheese Filling: Create a decadent cream cheese swirl by mixing softened cream cheese with sugar and vanilla, then layer it between the pumpkin batter.
  • Glaze: Drizzle the cooled bread with a simple glaze made of powdered sugar, milk, and a splash of vanilla extract.

Pumpkin Spice Swirl Bread is a delightful treat that embodies the flavors of fall in every bite. Whether enjoyed with a cup of coffee on a crisp morning or shared with loved ones during the holiday season, this bread is sure to become a seasonal staple in your home. Embrace the spirit of autumn and savor the warm, comforting taste of homemade Pumpkin Spice Swirl Bread. Happy baking!
