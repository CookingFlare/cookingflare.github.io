Korean Spicy Braised Tofu: A Vegan Dish Packed with Flavor


If you’re looking for a vegan dish that’s bursting with flavor, look no further than Korean Spicy Braised Tofu. This dish combines the rich and hearty flavors of tofu with the spicy kick of Korean cuisine, creating a truly unforgettable culinary experience. Whether you’re a vegan or simply someone who enjoys exploring new and exciting flavors, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

To make Korean Spicy Braised Tofu, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, cut into bite-sized cubes
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons of gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch, mixed with 2 tablespoons of water
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the red bell pepper and green onions to the pan, and continue to cook for another 2 minutes.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the gochujang, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and vegetable broth. Pour the mixture into the pan with the vegetables, and stir well to combine.

  4. Add the tofu cubes to the pan, gently stirring to coat them in the spicy sauce. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the tofu simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. In a separate bowl, mix the cornstarch with water to create a slurry. Pour the slurry into the pan, stirring continuously until the sauce thickens.

  6. Remove the pan from the heat and let the dish cool for a few minutes. The tofu will absorb even more of the flavorful sauce as it cools.

  7. Garnish the Korean Spicy Braised Tofu with a sprinkle of sesame seeds for added texture and visual appeal.

Serving Suggestions

Korean Spicy Braised Tofu is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or served with a side of steamed rice and kimchi for a complete Korean meal. The combination of spicy, savory, and slightly sweet flavors makes it a perfect accompaniment to a variety of dishes. You can also add some fresh vegetables, such as spinach or mushrooms, to further enhance the dish’s nutritional value and taste.

Final Thoughts

Korean Spicy Braised Tofu is a vegan dish that doesn’t compromise on flavor. It’s a testament to the richness and depth of Korean cuisine, showcasing how tofu can be transformed into a satisfying and mouthwatering meal. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based options into your diet, this recipe is a must-try.

So, what are you waiting for? Grab your apron and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with Korean Spicy Braised Tofu. Your taste buds will thank you, and you’ll have a newfound appreciation for the bold and vibrant flavors of Korean cuisine. Enjoy!
