One-Pot Creamy Tuscan Sausage and Tortellini: A Decadent Delight


If you’re a fan of rich and indulgent pasta dishes, then this One-Pot Creamy Tuscan Sausage and Tortellini recipe is a must-try. With the perfect combination of savory sausage, tender tortellini, and a creamy sauce infused with the flavors of Tuscany, this dish is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package (20 ounces) refrigerated tortellini
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for garnish
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the Italian sausage and cook, breaking it up into crumbles, until browned and cooked through.

  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Stir in the sun-dried tomatoes and baby spinach, allowing the spinach to wilt slightly.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to cook for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine, ensuring that all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  6. Gently add the tortellini to the pot, making sure they are submerged in the sauce. Cover the pot and cook for 10-12 minutes, or until the tortellini are tender and cooked through.

  7. Once the tortellini are cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the sauce to thicken slightly.

  8. Serve the One-Pot Creamy Tuscan Sausage and Tortellini hot, garnished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, use hot Italian sausage instead of mild.
  • To add some extra veggies, feel free to include sliced bell peppers or mushrooms.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute half-and-half or milk for the heavy cream.
  • If you’re vegetarian, you can swap out the sausage for plant-based sausage or omit it altogether and add more vegetables for a delicious meat-free option.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Conclusion

This One-Pot Creamy Tuscan Sausage and Tortellini recipe is a true delight for pasta lovers. With its rich and creamy sauce, flavorful sausage, and tender tortellini, it’s a dish that will impress both friends and family. Plus, the fact that it can be cooked in just one pot means less time spent on cleanup and more time enjoying the delicious flavors. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and treat yourself to a decadent and satisfying meal.
