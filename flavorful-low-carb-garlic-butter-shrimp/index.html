Flavorful Low Carb Garlic Butter Shrimp


If you’re looking for a quick, flavorful, and low-carb meal, then this recipe for garlic butter shrimp is a must-try. Bursting with savory garlic, aromatic herbs, and succulent shrimp, this dish is not only delicious but also easy to prepare. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply love seafood, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly squeezed lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels to remove any excess moisture.

  2. Sauté the Garlic: In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 2 tablespoons of butter with the olive oil. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.

  3. Cook the Shrimp: Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the skillet. Season the shrimp with paprika, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn pink and opaque.

  4. Add Butter and Lemon Juice: Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter to the skillet and drizzle the freshly squeezed lemon juice over the shrimp. Toss the shrimp in the garlic butter sauce until they are well-coated.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the shrimp with freshly chopped parsley.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the garlic butter shrimp to a serving platter and serve immediately. This dish pairs well with a side of zucchini noodles, cauliflower rice, or a fresh garden salad.

Nutritional Information:

  • Serving Size: 1/4 of the recipe
  • Calories: 230
  • Total Fat: 16g
  • Saturated Fat: 8g
  • Cholesterol: 240mg
  • Sodium: 580mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 2g
  • Dietary Fiber: 0g
  • Sugars: 0g
  • Protein: 20g

Final Thoughts:

This simple yet elegant dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. The combination of garlic, butter, and tender shrimp creates a symphony of flavors that will delight your taste buds. Additionally, with only 2 grams of carbohydrates per serving, this recipe is a great option for those following a low-carb lifestyle.

So, next time you’re craving a delicious seafood dish, give this flavorful low carb garlic butter shrimp a try. It’s a delightful way to indulge in a satisfying meal without compromising on taste or your health goals. Enjoy!
