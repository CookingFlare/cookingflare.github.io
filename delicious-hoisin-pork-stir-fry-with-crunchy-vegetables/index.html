Delicious Hoisin Pork Stir Fry with Crunchy Vegetables

3 min · 559 words

If you’re looking for a quick and flavorful meal that’s packed with protein and veggies, then this Hoisin Pork Stir Fry with Crunchy Vegetables is the perfect dish for you. The combination of tender pork, vibrant vegetables, and a savory hoisin sauce creates a mouthwatering stir-fry that will have your taste buds singing. Plus, it’s a great way to get your daily serving of vegetables while enjoying a delicious meal. Let’s dive into the recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork tenderloin, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas, trimmed
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the hoisin sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Mix well and set aside.

  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the pork strips and stir-fry until browned and cooked through, about 4-5 minutes. Once cooked, remove the pork from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.

  4. Add the sliced bell peppers, snow peas, broccoli, and onion to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Return the cooked pork to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the hoisin sauce mixture over the pork and vegetables. Stir-fry for an additional 1-2 minutes until everything is well coated and heated through.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Serve the Hoisin Pork Stir Fry over cooked rice and garnish with sesame seeds or sliced green onions, if desired.

Tips for Success

  • Prep Ahead: To make this stir-fry even quicker, prep your ingredients ahead of time. Slice the pork and vegetables in advance so that everything is ready to go when it’s time to cook.

  • Don’t Overcrowd the Pan: When stir-frying, it’s important not to overcrowd the pan. Cook the ingredients in batches if needed to ensure that they cook evenly and get that perfect stir-fry texture.

  • Customize Your Veggies: Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir-fry based on your preferences or what you have on hand. You can add carrots, snap peas, or water chestnuts for additional crunch and flavor.

  • Serve with Noodles: While this recipe calls for serving the stir-fry over rice, you can switch it up and serve it with cooked noodles for a delicious alternative.

Final Thoughts

This Hoisin Pork Stir Fry with Crunchy Vegetables is a fantastic way to enjoy a satisfying and flavorful meal in no time. The tender pork, vibrant vegetables, and savory hoisin sauce come together to create a dish that’s perfect for busy weeknights or leisurely weekends. Plus, it’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet without sacrificing taste. Give this recipe a try and savor every delicious bite!
