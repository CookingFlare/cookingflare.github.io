Whip Up a Quick and Easy Paleo-Friendly Stuffed Bell Pepper


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s quick and easy to prepare, then you’re in the right place! This Paleo-friendly stuffed bell pepper recipe is not only packed with flavor but also provides a nutritious option for anyone following a Paleo diet. The combination of fresh ingredients and savory seasonings makes this dish a crowd-pleaser, perfect for any occasion. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to whip up these delectable Paleo-friendly stuffed bell peppers in no time!

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Prepare the Bell Peppers: Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Rinse the peppers and set them aside.

  3. Cook the Ground Turkey: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground turkey, breaking it apart with a spoon as it browns.

  4. Add the Aromatics: Once the turkey is partially cooked, add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  5. Incorporate Cauliflower Rice: Stir in the cauliflower rice and cook for a few minutes until it starts to soften.

  6. Season the Filling: Add the diced tomatoes, paprika, oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Mix well and let the flavors meld for a few minutes.

  7. Stuff the Peppers: Place the prepared bell peppers in a baking dish. Spoon the turkey and cauliflower rice mixture into the peppers, filling them to the top.

  8. Bake to Perfection: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake the stuffed peppers in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.

  9. Garnish and Serve: Once the peppers are done, remove them from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Notes

  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the ground turkey with cooked quinoa or black beans.
  • Feel free to customize the seasonings to suit your taste preferences.
  • These stuffed bell peppers can be prepared in advance and refrigerated before baking for a convenient make-ahead meal.

Final Thoughts

This Paleo-friendly stuffed bell pepper recipe is a fantastic addition to any meal rotation. It’s not only easy to make but also incredibly versatile, allowing for various ingredient substitutions to accommodate different dietary preferences. Whether you’re following a Paleo lifestyle or simply looking for a wholesome and flavorful dish, these stuffed peppers are sure to impress. Enjoy the combination of vibrant bell peppers, savory turkey, and aromatic seasonings in this delightful Paleo-friendly creation!

So there you have it — a quick and easy Paleo-friendly stuffed bell pepper recipe that’s bound to become a family favorite. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of a wholesome meal made with love and fresh ingredients. Happy cooking!
