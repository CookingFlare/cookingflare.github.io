Homemade Cinnamon Rolls: A Breakfast Dessert Delight


Welcome to our delightful culinary adventure where we dive into the scrumptious world of homemade cinnamon rolls. This classic breakfast treat is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a heartwarming experience in the comfort of your own kitchen. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a baking journey that will fill your home with the irresistible aroma of cinnamon and sugar.

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:

    • 1 cup warm milk
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/3 cup melted butter
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 2 eggs
    • 4 cups all-purpose flour

  • For the Filling:

    • 1 cup brown sugar
    • 3 tablespoons ground cinnamon
    • 1/3 cup butter, softened

  • For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

    • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
    • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
    • 1/4 cup cream cheese
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm milk and let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
    • Add sugar, melted butter, salt, eggs, and flour to the yeast mixture. Mix until a soft dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour until doubled in size.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a small bowl, combine brown sugar and cinnamon for the filling.
    • Roll out the dough into a large rectangle and spread the softened butter over it.
    • Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly over the dough.

  3. Roll and Cut:

    • Roll the dough tightly from the long side to form a log.
    • Cut the log into 12 equal pieces using a sharp knife or floss.
    • Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, cover, and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  4. Bake to Perfection:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Bake the cinnamon rolls for 25-30 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
    • Let them cool slightly before frosting.

  5. Prepare the Cream Cheese Frosting:

    • In a mixing bowl, beat together softened butter, cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until smooth and creamy.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Drizzle the warm cinnamon rolls with the luscious cream cheese frosting.
    • Serve them fresh and enjoy the gooey, sweet goodness with a hot cup of coffee or tea.

Homemade cinnamon rolls are not just a breakfast indulgence; they are a labor of love that brings joy to those who savor them. Whether you’re baking them for a special occasion or simply to treat yourself, the heavenly combination of soft dough, aromatic cinnamon, and creamy frosting is sure to delight your senses.

So, gather your ingredients, embrace the sweet anticipation of baking, and relish every moment as you create these delightful breakfast dessert treats. From the first proof of the yeast to the last bite of a warm cinnamon roll, let the experience warm your heart and fill your home with happiness.

Enjoy the journey of baking and savoring your homemade cinnamon rolls. Happy baking!
