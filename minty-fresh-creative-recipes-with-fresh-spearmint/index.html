Minty Fresh: Creative Recipes with Fresh Spearmint


Welcome to our delightful collection of recipes that showcase the vibrant and refreshing flavors of fresh spearmint! From appetizers to desserts, we’ve got you covered with unique and inventive dishes that will leave you craving for more. Let’s dive right in!

1. Spearmint Infused Watermelon Salad

Prep time: 15 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup fresh spearmint leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the watermelon, feta cheese, red onion, and fresh spearmint leaves.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Gently toss the ingredients together until well combined.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy the refreshing combination of flavors.

2. Minty Pesto Pasta

Prep time: 20 minutes | Cook time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz spaghetti
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh spearmint leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, spearmint leaves, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped.
  4. Gradually add the olive oil while continuing to pulse until the mixture forms a smooth paste.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the minty pesto until well coated.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.

3. Grilled Lemon-Mint Chicken Skewers

Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 10 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh spearmint leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the lemon juice, olive oil, spearmint leaves, lemon zest, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss until well coated.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers.
  6. Grill the chicken skewers for about 5 minutes per side or until cooked through.
  7. Remove from the grill and let rest for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Serve alongside a fresh salad or your favorite dipping sauce.

4. Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream Sandwiches

Prep time: 20 minutes | Cook time: 10 minutes | Freeze time: 4 hours | Makes: 8 sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup fresh spearmint leaves, chopped
  • 1 pint mint chocolate chip ice cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips and fresh spearmint leaves.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are set.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool completely on a wire rack.
  10. Once the cookies have cooled, scoop a generous portion of mint chocolate chip ice cream onto the bottom side of one cookie.
  11. Place another cookie on top, bottom side down, and gently press together to form a sandwich.
  12. Repeat with the remaining cookies and ice cream.
  13. Place the ice cream sandwiches in the freezer for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  14. Indulge in these delightful frozen treats whenever you’re in need of a refreshing dessert.

Conclusion

We hope you’ve enjoyed this exploration of fresh spearmint and its incredible versatility in the kitchen. Whether you’re looking to enhance your salads, pasta dishes, grilled meats, or desserts, spearmint adds a burst of freshness that takes your culinary creations to the next level. So go ahead, get creative, and infuse your recipes with the invigorating and minty flavors of fresh spearmint!

Note: Remember to adjust the quantity of spearmint according to your personal preference, as the intensity of the flavor can vary. Enjoy experimenting and discovering your own favorite minty fresh recipes!
