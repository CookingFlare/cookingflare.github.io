Irresistible Almond Poppy Seed Bread: A Nutty Delight


Are you a fan of nutty delights? If so, then you’re in for a treat with this irresistible Almond Poppy Seed Bread recipe. This delightful bread is packed with the perfect balance of almond flavor and the subtle crunch of poppy seeds. It’s a recipe that will have your taste buds dancing with joy!

Ingredients

To make this nutty delight, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Instructions

Now that we have all the ingredients ready, let’s get started with the step-by-step instructions for making this irresistible Almond Poppy Seed Bread:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and Greek yogurt until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, until fully incorporated into the mixture.
  5. Stir in the almond extract and poppy seeds, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it out evenly.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This Almond Poppy Seed Bread is a versatile treat that can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to enhance your nutty delight experience:

  • Serve the bread as is, warm or at room temperature, for a simple and satisfying snack.
  • Toast a slice of the bread and spread some butter or cream cheese on top for a delightful breakfast or afternoon treat.
  • Pair a slice of this bread with a cup of hot tea or coffee for a cozy and comforting moment.

Storage

To ensure the freshness of your Almond Poppy Seed Bread, follow these storage tips:

  • Once completely cooled, store the bread in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.
  • For longer storage, wrap the bread tightly in plastic wrap and store it in the refrigerator for up to a week.
  • You can also freeze the bread for up to 3 months. Just make sure to wrap it tightly in plastic wrap and place it in a freezer-safe bag or container.

Conclusion

There you have it, a recipe for an irresistible Almond Poppy Seed Bread that is both delicious and nutty. With its perfect balance of almond flavor and the delightful crunch of poppy seeds, this bread is sure to be a hit with family and friends. Whether enjoyed as a simple snack or as part of a cozy breakfast, this nutty delight will leave you wanting more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather the ingredients, and indulge in the joy of baking this irresistible Almond Poppy Seed Bread!
