Instant Pot Sweet Potatoes: A Versatile Side Dish


Sweet potatoes have earned their place as one of the most beloved, versatile, and nutritious root vegetables. From their natural sweetness to their ability to pair seamlessly with both savory and sweet flavors, they’ve become a favorite ingredient in countless recipes. One of the easiest ways to prepare sweet potatoes is by using an Instant Pot, a method that cuts down on cooking time without sacrificing taste or texture. In this blog post, we’ll dive deep into the world of Instant Pot sweet potatoes, exploring their uses as a side dish, cooking tips, serving ideas, and more.

Why Sweet Potatoes?

Sweet potatoes provide an incredibly rich flavor profile, but they also rank high as a nutrient-packed superfood. They are loaded with fiber, vitamin A (in the form of beta-carotene), vitamin C, potassium, and antioxidants. Beyond their health benefits, sweet potatoes are inexpensive, widely available year-round, and come in several varieties that cater to different culinary styles.

Whether you’re preparing a simple weeknight dinner or hosting a festive gathering, sweet potatoes can effortlessly complement almost any meal. Using an Instant Pot makes preparing them not only efficient but also foolproof, ensuring they’re perfectly cooked every time.

Preparing Sweet Potatoes in the Instant Pot

Cooking sweet potatoes in an Instant Pot is one of the quickest and most consistent ways to prepare them. Gone are the days of waiting for them to roast or boil on the stovetop—this pressure cooking method achieves perfectly tender sweet potatoes in no time at all.

Ingredients:

Before you start, gather these simple ingredients:

  • Sweet potatoes (medium-sized, any variety)
  • Water (1 cup)
  • Optional salt or seasoning (if desired)

How to Cook Sweet Potatoes in the Instant Pot:

  1. Wash and Prep the Sweet Potatoes:
    Rinse your sweet potatoes thoroughly under cold water to remove dirt. There’s no need to peel them unless your recipe specifically calls for peeled sweet potatoes; their skin is nutritious and flavorful.

  2. Add Water to the Instant Pot:
    Pour one cup of water into the Instant Pot. This is essential for creating steam during pressure cooking.

  3. Place Sweet Potatoes on the Steamer Rack:
    Use a trivet or steamer rack inside the Instant Pot. Place the sweet potatoes on the rack so they’re lifted above the water. Avoid stacking them too tightly to ensure even cooking.

  4. Set the Pressure Cooker:
    Cover the Instant Pot with its lid, ensuring the pressure valve is in the sealed position. Set the Instant Pot to Manual (High Pressure) mode and adjust the cooking time depending on the size of your sweet potatoes:

    • Small sweet potatoes: 12-14 minutes
    • Medium sweet potatoes: 16-18 minutes
    • Large sweet potatoes: 20-25 minutes

  5. Natural Pressure Release:
    After the cooking cycle completes, allow the Instant Pot to release pressure naturally for about 10 minutes. Then perform a quick release to safely release any remaining steam.

  6. Check for Doneness:
    Use a fork or knife to test the softness of the sweet potatoes. If they’re not tender enough, cook them under pressure for an additional few minutes.

Serving Ideas for Instant Pot Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes are endlessly adaptable, making them an excellent base for an array of dishes. Here are some ways you can transform your Instant Pot sweet potatoes into a side dish worthy of applause.

1. Simple and Classic:

For simple yet satisfying results, slice your pressure-cooked sweet potatoes in half, sprinkle with a pinch of salt, and lightly drizzle with olive oil or melted butter. The natural sweetness of the potatoes shines through, making this the perfect pairing for grilled meats, roasted vegetables, or other hearty mains.

2. Sweet Potato Mash:

Create a silky, flavorful mashed sweet potato dish by peeling your cooked sweet potatoes and mashing them in a bowl. Incorporate ingredients like a splash of milk or cream, a hint of cinnamon, and a touch of maple syrup for a comforting side dish. Add chopped pecans or sprinkle with nutmeg for extra flair.

3. Loaded Sweet Potatoes:

Add a creative twist by loading your sweet potatoes with toppings. Cut the cooked potatoes lengthwise and fluff the interior with a fork. Add sour cream or Greek yogurt, crumbled bacon, shredded cheese, green onions, and a dash of hot sauce for a savory-loaded masterpiece.

4. Sweet Potato Cubes:

Dice your cooked sweet potatoes into bite-sized cubes and toss them with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and a pinch of sea salt. Serve them as a warm, flavorful side dish or chill them for use in salads.

5. Sweet Dessert-Inspired Side:

Leverage the natural sweetness of these potatoes by transforming them into a dessert-like side dish. Drizzle them with honey or agave syrup and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar. Top with whipped cream or yogurt to finish it off.

Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Sweet Potatoes

To ensure your sweet potatoes are irresistible every time you make them, follow these tried-and-true tips:

  • Choose the Right Size: Pick sweet potatoes of similar size to ensure consistent cooking. If you’re using smaller potatoes, adjust the cooking time accordingly.

  • Don’t Skip the Trivet: Raising the potatoes above the water ensures they steam evenly and don’t become soggy.

  • Add Flavor Before or After: If you want to infuse extra flavor directly into the potatoes, rub them with spices or seasonings before you pressure cook them. Otherwise, save the flavors for afterward when you’re ready to serve.

  • Make Them Ahead of Time: Instant Pot sweet potatoes can be made in advance and stored in the fridge for up to 3-5 days. Reheat them in the microwave or oven and save time on busy nights.

  • Experiment with Variety: Sweet potatoes come in several types, including orange-fleshed varieties, purple-skinned ones, and even white-fleshed variations. Each offers unique flavors and textures.

Why Use the Instant Pot for Sweet Potatoes?

The Instant Pot revolutionizes the way we cook sweet potatoes. Not only does it cut down on preparation and cooking time, but it also locks in nutrients and enhances natural sweetness. Traditional methods, like roasting and boiling, are still popular, but the pressure cooker consistently delivers tender, evenly cooked sweet potatoes every time. Plus, the convenience of hands-free cooking lets you focus on preparing other dishes or simply enjoy more downtime.

Pairings: What Goes Well with Sweet Potatoes?

Wondering what to serve alongside your Instant Pot sweet potatoes? Here are some pairing options to inspire your next meal:

  • Protein Pairings: Sweet potatoes complement grilled chicken, roasted pork tenderloin, baked salmon, or seared tofu.

  • Vegetable Medleys: Add sautéed asparagus, roasted Brussels sprouts, or fresh greens for a balanced spread.

  • Grains and Legumes: Serve sweet potatoes with quinoa, rice pilaf, or lentils for wholesome and hearty combinations.

  • Sauces and Condiments: Experiment with creamy sauces like aioli or tangy dressings like balsamic glaze to amplify the flavors.

Final Thoughts

Sweet potatoes cooked in the Instant Pot are truly a game-changer, turning this humble vegetable into a tender and flavorful side dish in minimal time. This cooking method offers flexibility, consistency, and unlimited creative possibilities for serving. Whether you prefer mashed, roasted, or loaded sweet potatoes, the Instant Pot has you covered.

So, what are you waiting for? Grab some sweet potatoes and your trusty Instant Pot, and start experimenting today. You might just discover your new favorite way to elevate weeknight dinners or holiday feasts with this naturally sweet, nutrient-rich side dish.
