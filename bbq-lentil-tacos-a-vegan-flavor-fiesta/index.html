BBQ Lentil Tacos: A Vegan Flavor Fiesta


Are you tired of the usual taco night routine? Ready to spice up your dinner game with a scrumptious and flavorful vegan dish?! If your answer is a loud and resounding “Yes” then you’ve come to the right place! Today, we are going to explore the mouthwatering and delightful world of BBQ lentil tacos. Never mind the meat, these vegan tacos pack a tasty gourmet punch that will have your taste buds fiesta-ing for more!

Ingredients

Before we dive into the actual cooking process, let’s take a peek at the incredible ingredients we’ll be using.

  • 1 cup of green or brown lentils
  • 2 cups of water
  • ½ cup of barbecue sauce
  • ½ cup of chili sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of celery salt
  • Salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • Turmeric, for color
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 3 medium-sized tortillas, warmed
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup of cilantro, chopped
  • Hot sauce, for serving (optional)

Cooking Process

Step 1: Prep the lentils

First, rinse the lentils under running water until the water runs clear. This simple step will remove any of those pesky residue and impurities that may come with the dried legumes.

Step 2: Cook the lentils

In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the lentils and water. Bring to a boil, then simmer over medium heat for about 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender. Drain any excess liquid.

Step 3: Whisk the sauce

In a bowl, mix together the barbecue sauce, chili sauce, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, and celery salt. You can customize the spiciness of the sauce by adding more or less of your favorite ingredients. Taste along the way and adjust as necessary! Don’t forget to season with a little bit of salt and ground black pepper for extra flavor.

Step 4: Combine the lentils and sauce

Once your lentils are cooked, stir them into the bowl with the sauce, making sure the lentils are evenly coated. Add turmeric to give the lentils a beautiful color, and season the mixture with the lime juice.

Step 5: Assemble the tacos

Now that our amazing BBQ lentil mixture is ready, let’s assemble the tacos. Start by dividing the lentils evenly between the tortillas. Top with sliced red onions, fresh cilantro, and avocado. The last step—drizzle with hot sauce for an added kick.

Conclusion

As you sit down to enjoy your BBQ lentil tacos, you’ll realize that this vegan dish is a symphony of flavors and textures. The lentils offer a satisfying, hearty texture, while the barbecue and chili sauce add just the right amount of tanginess and smokiness. And the avocado, red onion, and cilantro? A colorful and crunchy finishing touch you’ll never forget!

Switching to a meat-free diet never tasted so good with this recipe for BBQ lentil tacos. With the perfect mix of flavors and the versatility of ingredients, it’s a must-try for anyone who’s looking for some new and exciting dishes to add to their rotation. Bon appétit!
