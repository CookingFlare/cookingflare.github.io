Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Casserole: The Perfect Side Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting side dish that will impress everyone at the dinner table, look no further than this creamy and cheesy broccoli casserole. Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, this dish is the perfect way to elevate any meal. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to add a little something extra to your family dinner, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 cup of grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons of butter, melted
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a casserole dish.

  2. In a large pot of boiling water, blanch the broccoli florets for about 3-4 minutes, or until they are slightly tender. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, sour cream, mayonnaise, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  4. Add the blanched broccoli florets to the cheese mixture and gently toss until all the florets are coated.

  5. Transfer the mixture to the greased casserole dish and spread it out evenly.

  6. In a small bowl, combine the grated Parmesan cheese, breadcrumbs, and melted butter. Sprinkle this mixture over the top of the casserole.

  7. Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This creamy and cheesy broccoli casserole is incredibly versatile and pairs well with a variety of main dishes. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve it as a side dish with roasted chicken or grilled steak.
  • Pair it with a simple green salad for a light and satisfying vegetarian meal.
  • Add it to your holiday menu as a festive and flavorful side dish.
  • Pack it for lunch and enjoy it as a standalone meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add some diced cooked bacon or sautéed onions to the cheese mixture before baking.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese to create your own unique flavor profile. Gouda, Swiss, or Monterey Jack would all work well in this recipe.
  • If you prefer a crispier topping, broil the casserole for an additional 2-3 minutes at the end of the baking time.

In Conclusion

Creamy and cheesy broccoli casserole is a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone asking for seconds. With its rich and comforting flavors, it’s the perfect side dish to complement any meal. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to add a little something extra to your family dinner, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite. So go ahead, give it a try, and watch as your guests rave about this delicious and wholesome dish. Enjoy!
