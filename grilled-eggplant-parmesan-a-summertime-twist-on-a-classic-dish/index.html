Grilled Eggplant Parmesan: A Summertime Twist on a Classic Dish


When it comes to classic Italian comfort food, Eggplant Parmesan holds a special place in many hearts. The combination of crispy breaded eggplant, rich tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese is simply irresistible. However, as the summer months roll around, firing up the grill becomes a popular cooking method, and what better way to embrace the season than by giving this beloved dish a summertime twist? Grilled Eggplant Parmesan is a delightful variation that brings a smoky flavor to the table, making it a perfect addition to your summer menu.

Grilled Eggplant Slices

The first step in preparing Grilled Eggplant Parmesan is to slice the eggplant. Opt for medium-sized eggplants, as they tend to have fewer seeds and a milder flavor. Slice the eggplant into rounds, about half an inch thick. Sprinkle the slices with salt and let them sit for about 30 minutes to draw out the excess moisture. This process also helps to reduce any bitterness in the eggplant. After 30 minutes, rinse the salt off the slices and pat them dry with a paper towel.

Grilling the Eggplant

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Brush the eggplant slices with olive oil and season them with salt, pepper, and a pinch of dried oregano. Place the slices on the grill and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they develop grill marks and become tender. The smoky flavor from the grill adds a whole new dimension to the eggplant, elevating the dish to a new level of deliciousness.

Assembling the Parmesan

Once the eggplant slices are grilled to perfection, it’s time to assemble the Grilled Eggplant Parmesan. Start by spreading a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Place the grilled eggplant slices in a single layer on top of the sauce. Add another layer of marinara sauce on top of the eggplant, followed by a generous sprinkling of shredded mozzarella cheese and a dusting of Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until all the eggplant slices are used up, finishing with a generous layer of cheese on top.

The Final Touch

Place the assembled Grilled Eggplant Parmesan on the grill, close the lid, and let it cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. The heat from the grill will infuse the dish with even more smoky goodness, creating a flavor profile that is undeniably summery.

Serving Suggestions

Grilled Eggplant Parmesan is best enjoyed fresh off the grill, paired with a crisp green salad or a side of garlic bread. The smoky aroma and the gooey cheese will have everyone at the table eager to dig in.

Conclusion

Grilled Eggplant Parmesan is a delightful way to enjoy a classic Italian dish with a summertime twist. The smoky flavor from the grill adds depth to the dish, making it a perfect addition to your summer culinary repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a taste of summer, this grilled version of Eggplant Parmesan is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you longing for more.

So, fire up the grill, grab some fresh eggplants, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of Grilled Eggplant Parmesan – a perfect fusion of classic comfort food and summertime grilling magic.
