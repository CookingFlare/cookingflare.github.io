Cooking with Ancient Grains: Healthy Recipes from Around the World


Grains have been a staple in human diets for thousands of years, and ancient grains offer a unique twist to traditional recipes. These grains have stood the test of time and continue to provide us with a myriad of health benefits. In this blog post, we will explore the fascinating world of cooking with ancient grains and share some delicious and healthy recipes from around the world.

Quinoa Salad with Mediterranean Flavors

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness.
  2. In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil and add the quinoa. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the water is absorbed.
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss gently to combine.
  7. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Amaranth Porridge with Berries

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup amaranth
  • 2 cups almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pistachios)

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the almond milk to a boil.
  2. Add amaranth and reduce heat to low. Simmer for about 20 minutes or until the amaranth is tender and the mixture thickens.
  3. Stir in honey (if desired) and vanilla extract.
  4. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. Serve the amaranth porridge in bowls and top with mixed berries and chopped nuts.
  6. Drizzle with honey for extra sweetness, if desired.
  7. Enjoy a warm and comforting breakfast!

Teff Flatbread with Spicy Chickpea Curry

Ingredients: Teff Flatbread:

  • 1 cup teff flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Spicy Chickpea Curry:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions: Teff Flatbread:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine teff flour, water, and salt. Mix well until a smooth batter is formed.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and brush with olive oil.
  3. Pour 1/4 cup of the teff batter onto the skillet and spread it evenly to form a thin round flatbread.
  4. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  5. Repeat with the remaining batter to make more flatbreads.

Spicy Chickpea Curry:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and garlic, and sauté until onions are translucent.
  3. Add curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Add chickpeas and coconut milk. Simmer for about 10 minutes or until the curry thickens slightly.
  5. Season with salt to taste.
  6. Serve the spicy chickpea curry with the teff flatbread and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Conclusion

Ancient grains add a delightful twist to our everyday meals, providing us with a wide array of nutrients and flavors. Whether you’re looking for a refreshing salad, a comforting porridge, or a spicy curry, these healthy recipes from around the world will surely satisfy your taste buds and nourish your body. So, go ahead and explore the world of ancient grains in your kitchen, and enjoy the benefits of these wholesome ingredients. Bon appétit!
