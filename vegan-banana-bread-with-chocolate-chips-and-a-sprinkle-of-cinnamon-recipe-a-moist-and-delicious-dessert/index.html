Vegan Banana Bread with Chocolate Chips and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon Recipe: A Moist and Delicious Dessert


Are you in the mood for a delectable vegan dessert that will satisfy your sweet tooth? Look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for Vegan Banana Bread with Chocolate Chips and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon. Bursting with flavor and moistness, this delightful treat is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup of coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 cup of almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of vegan chocolate chips
  • A sprinkle of cinnamon for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan with coconut oil or line it with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, melted coconut oil, almond milk, vanilla extract, and brown sugar. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a denser texture.

  4. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, smoothing the top with a spatula. Sprinkle a dusting of cinnamon on top for added flavor and aesthetic appeal.

  6. Place the loaf pan in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The baking time may vary, so keep a close eye on it after the 45-minute mark.

  7. Once baked, remove the banana bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

  8. Slice the cooled banana bread into thick, luscious pieces and serve with your favorite hot beverage or enjoy it as is. The combination of sweet bananas, rich chocolate chips, and a hint of cinnamon will surely tantalize your taste buds.

Tips and Variations:

  • To add some crunch, consider incorporating a handful of chopped walnuts or pecans into the batter.
  • For a healthier twist, substitute the brown sugar with coconut sugar or maple syrup. You can also use whole wheat flour instead of all-purpose flour for added fiber.
  • If you love the taste of cinnamon, feel free to add a teaspoon of ground cinnamon into the batter for an extra burst of flavor.
  • This vegan banana bread can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. Alternatively, you can refrigerate it to prolong its freshness.

Enjoy this delightful Vegan Banana Bread with Chocolate Chips and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon as a scrumptious dessert or a satisfying snack. Its moist texture and delightful combination of flavors will leave you craving more. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in this sensational vegan treat!
