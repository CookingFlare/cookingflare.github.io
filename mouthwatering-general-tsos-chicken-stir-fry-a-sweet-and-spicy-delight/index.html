Mouthwatering General Tso's Chicken Stir Fry: A Sweet and Spicy Delight


If you’re a fan of Chinese takeout, then General Tso’s Chicken is probably a dish that’s close to your heart. The perfect combination of sweet and spicy flavors, along with the crunch of the chicken, makes it a beloved choice for many. But what if I told you that you can recreate this delectable dish right in your own kitchen? Yes, you heard that right! Today, I’m going to share with you a mouthwatering recipe for General Tso’s Chicken Stir Fry that will have your taste buds singing with joy.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine or dry sherry
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 egg white

For the General Tso’s Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes (adjust to your spice preference)

For the Stir Fry:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Marinating the Chicken:

    • In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, rice wine, sesame oil, cornstarch, and egg white. Add the chicken pieces and toss to coat. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Making the General Tso’s Sauce:

    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, and cornstarch until smooth. Set aside.

  3. Stir Frying the Chicken:

    • Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir fry until golden and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the wok and set aside.

  4. Stir Frying the Vegetables:

    • In the same wok, heat the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  5. Bringing It All Together:

    • Return the cooked chicken to the wok with the vegetables. Give the General Tso’s sauce a quick stir and then pour it over the chicken and vegetables. Toss everything together until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken and vegetables evenly.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Serve the General Tso’s Chicken Stir Fry over a bed of steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions for an extra pop of flavor.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra crispy texture, you can lightly coat the marinated chicken pieces with a thin layer of cornstarch before stir frying.
  • Adjust the level of spiciness by increasing or decreasing the amount of chili flakes in the sauce.
  • Feel free to add your favorite vegetables to the stir fry, such as water chestnuts, baby corn, or mushrooms.

General Tso’s Chicken Stir Fry is a perfect balance of sweet and spicy, and it’s a dish that never fails to impress. By following this recipe, you can enjoy the flavors of your favorite takeout dish right at home, knowing exactly what goes into your meal. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create a mouthwatering delight that will have everyone asking for seconds!
