Creamy Broccoli Cheddar Soup: A Comforting Vegetarian Option


When it comes to comforting and hearty soups, one recipe that always hits the spot is Creamy Broccoli Cheddar Soup. This vegetarian option is packed with flavor, creamy texture, and a vibrant green color that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more veggies into your diet, this soup is a delicious and satisfying choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the broccoli florets to the pot and stir them around to coat them with the butter, onion, and garlic mixture. Let them cook for a few minutes until they slightly soften.

  3. Pour in the vegetable broth, ensuring that the broccoli is completely submerged. Increase the heat to high and bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.

  4. Use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree the soup until smooth. If using a regular blender, be sure to blend in batches and exercise caution while handling hot liquids.

  5. Return the pureed soup to the pot and place it over low heat. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and well combined. This will give the soup its creamy and cheesy consistency.

  6. Slowly pour in the heavy cream, stirring continuously to incorporate it into the soup. Season with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the flavors to your preference.

  7. Allow the soup to simmer for an additional 5 minutes on low heat, allowing all the flavors to meld together.

  8. Serve the Creamy Broccoli Cheddar Soup hot, garnished with some extra shredded cheddar cheese or a sprinkle of fresh herbs for added flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For a healthier twist, you can use low-fat cheddar cheese and substitute half of the heavy cream with milk or a non-dairy alternative like almond milk.
  • Don’t discard the broccoli stems! Peel and chop them into small pieces, and add them to the soup along with the florets for added texture and nutrients.
  • If you prefer a chunkier soup, reserve some of the cooked broccoli florets before pureeing the soup. Stir them back in after blending for a more textured experience.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to enhance the flavor profile of the soup. Fresh thyme, nutmeg, or a pinch of cayenne pepper can add a delightful twist.

In Conclusion

Creamy Broccoli Cheddar Soup is a delightful and comforting vegetarian option that will warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds. With its vibrant color, creamy texture, and cheesy goodness, it’s the perfect soup to enjoy on a chilly day or whenever you’re in need of some comforting flavors. Whip up a batch of this delicious soup and indulge in its warmth and nourishment.
