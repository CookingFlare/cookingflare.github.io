Forever Yours Filet Mignon: A Valentine's Day Main Course Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to impress your loved one than with a perfectly cooked filet mignon? This Forever Yours Filet Mignon recipe is sure to set the mood for a romantic evening, and its tender and juicy flavors will leave your special someone begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 filet mignon steaks (6-8 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening the sauce)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, salt, and pepper. Brush this mixture onto both sides of the filet mignon steaks, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  3. Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add the butter and let it melt, then place the steaks in the skillet and sear them for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop a golden brown crust.

  4. Once the steaks are seared, transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and cook for an additional 6-8 minutes for medium-rare doneness. If you prefer your steak more well-done, cook for a few minutes longer.

  5. Remove the skillet from the oven and transfer the filet mignon steaks to a plate. Cover them loosely with aluminum foil and let them rest for about 5 minutes to allow the juices to redistribute.

  6. While the steaks are resting, prepare the balsamic sauce. Place the skillet back on the stovetop over medium heat. Add the balsamic vinegar and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Cook for about 1 minute, or until the vinegar has reduced slightly.

  7. Pour in the beef broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. If desired, mix the cornstarch with a little water to create a slurry, then add it to the sauce to thicken it. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened to your liking.

  8. To serve, place the filet mignon steaks on a plate and drizzle the balsamic sauce over the top. You can also serve the steaks with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes.

A Perfectly Romantic Meal

The Forever Yours Filet Mignon is not just a delicious main course, but also a symbol of your love and dedication. The tender and juicy steak, combined with the flavorful balsamic sauce, creates a dish that is sure to impress your loved one on Valentine’s Day.

Remember, the key to a perfect filet mignon is in the cooking technique. Searing the steaks before finishing them in the oven ensures a beautiful crust while keeping the inside tender and juicy. And don’t forget to let the steaks rest before serving to allow the flavors to develop fully.

So, this Valentine’s Day, treat your special someone to a memorable dining experience with the Forever Yours Filet Mignon. With its exquisite flavors and romantic ambiance, this dish is bound to make your evening one to remember. Enjoy!
