Baklava Bars: A Modern Twist on a Classic Dessert


If you’re a fan of the rich, sweet flavors of baklava but want a more convenient and portable option, then you’re in for a treat with these Baklava Bars. This modern twist on the classic dessert combines all the delicious elements of traditional baklava into a convenient bar form, making it perfect for parties, picnics, or a sweet indulgence on the go.

Ingredients

For the Nut Filling:

  • 2 cups chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup chopped pistachios
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

For the Phyllo Layers:

  • 1 package phyllo dough, thawed
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Syrup:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Nut Filling

  1. In a bowl, combine the chopped walnuts, pistachios, sugar, cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg. Mix well and set aside.

Step 2: Assemble the Phyllo Layers

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Brush a 9x13 inch baking dish with melted butter.
  3. Carefully unroll the phyllo dough and place it on a clean, dry surface. Cover the phyllo sheets with a damp kitchen towel to prevent them from drying out.
  4. Place one sheet of phyllo dough in the prepared baking dish and brush it with melted butter. Repeat this process with 6 more sheets of phyllo, brushing each sheet with butter.
  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of the nut filling over the phyllo layers.

Step 3: Repeat the Layers

  1. Place 7 more sheets of phyllo over the nut filling, brushing each sheet with butter.
  2. Add another layer of the nut filling.
  3. Continue layering the remaining phyllo sheets, brushing each with butter, and adding the nut filling in between, until all the phyllo and nut filling are used.

Step 4: Cut and Bake

  1. Using a sharp knife, carefully cut the assembled baklava into bars or squares.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the baklava bars are golden brown and crisp.

Step 5: Make the Syrup

  1. While the baklava bars are baking, combine the water, sugar, honey, cinnamon stick, and lemon juice in a saucepan.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until the syrup thickens slightly.

Step 6: Finish and Serve

  1. Once the baklava bars are done baking, remove them from the oven and immediately drizzle the hot syrup over the bars, allowing it to soak in.
  2. Allow the baklava bars to cool completely in the baking dish before serving.

Enjoy Your Modern Twist on Baklava

These Baklava Bars are a delightful fusion of the beloved flavors of traditional baklava in a convenient and portable form. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just looking to try something new, these bars are sure to impress with their layers of crispy phyllo, sweet nut filling, and fragrant syrup. So, give this recipe a try and savor the delicious marriage of tradition and modern convenience in every bite!
