Quick and Easy Dinner Recipes for Cooking Newbies


Cooking can be an intimidating task, especially for those who are new to the kitchen. But fear not, because I have some quick and easy dinner recipes that even the most inexperienced cooks can whip up effortlessly. These recipes are perfect for those busy weeknights when you want a delicious meal without spending hours in the kitchen. So grab your apron, put on some music, and let’s get cooking!

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir well to combine.
  4. Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until melted and smooth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until evenly coated with the creamy sauce.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortillas, for serving
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, salsa, shredded cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chicken slices, bell peppers, onion, olive oil, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss until well coated.
  3. Arrange the chicken and vegetables in a single layer on a sheet pan.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  6. Serve the sheet pan chicken fajitas with warm tortillas and your choice of toppings.

One-Pan Lemon Garlic Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. In an oven-safe skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Place the salmon fillets in the skillet, skin-side down.
  5. Arrange the lemon slices on top of the salmon.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked to your desired doneness.
  7. Garnish with fresh dill and serve hot.

Conclusion

Cooking doesn’t have to be difficult, and with these quick and easy dinner recipes, even cooking newbies can whip up delicious meals in no time. Whether you’re craving a creamy pasta, flavorful fajitas, or a healthy salmon dish, these recipes have got you covered. So put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress yourself and your loved ones with these simple yet tasty creations. Happy cooking!
