Homemade Cinnamon Rolls with Cream Cheese Frosting


If there’s one aroma that can instantly transport you to a cozy kitchen, it’s the scent of freshly baked cinnamon rolls. These soft, pillowy treats swirled with cinnamon and topped with luscious cream cheese frosting are a beloved classic for a reason. And the best part? Making them from scratch at home is easier than you might think. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a delightful baking adventure with this irresistible homemade cinnamon rolls recipe.

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Activate the yeast: In a large bowl, combine the warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.

  2. Prepare the dough: To the yeast mixture, add the sugar, melted butter, eggs, salt, and 4 cups of flour. Mix until a soft dough forms. Gradually add the remaining 1/2 cup of flour if the dough is too sticky.

  3. Knead the dough: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. Let it rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  5. Prepare the filling: In a small bowl, mix the softened butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon until well combined.

  6. Roll out the dough: Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a 16x21 inch rectangle.

  7. Add the filling: Spread the filling evenly over the rolled-out dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  8. Roll it up: Starting with the long edge, tightly roll the dough into a log. Cut the log into 12 equal pieces.

  9. Arrange the rolls: Place the rolls in a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover and let them rise again for about 30 minutes.

  10. Bake: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake the rolls for 20-25 minutes or until they turn golden brown.

  11. Make the frosting: While the rolls are baking, prepare the cream cheese frosting by beating together the softened butter, cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until smooth and creamy.

  12. Frost the rolls: Once the cinnamon rolls are done, generously spread the cream cheese frosting over them while they’re still warm.

  13. Enjoy: Allow the rolls to cool slightly before serving. Then, indulge in the warm, gooey goodness of your homemade cinnamon rolls with cream cheese frosting.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra flavor boost, consider adding a handful of chopped pecans or raisins to the filling before rolling up the dough.
  • If you prefer a thicker frosting, simply adjust the amount of powdered sugar to achieve your desired consistency.
  • These cinnamon rolls are best enjoyed fresh, but you can store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. Just pop them in the microwave for a few seconds to reheat and enjoy them as if they were freshly baked.

There you have it – a foolproof recipe for homemade cinnamon rolls with cream cheese frosting that’s sure to become a staple in your baking repertoire. Whether you’re treating your family to a special weekend brunch or surprising your friends with a delightful dessert, these heavenly cinnamon rolls are bound to be a hit. So, put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and let the sweet aroma of cinnamon and sugar fill your kitchen as you embark on this delightful baking adventure. Happy baking!
