One Pot Lemon Herb Shrimp and Orzo


Are you looking for a quick and flavorful meal that requires minimal effort and cleanup? Look no further than this delicious One Pot Lemon Herb Shrimp and Orzo recipe. Packed with vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want to enjoy a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of orzo pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juice and zest
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cups of vegetable or chicken broth
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot or skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the shrimp to the pot and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the pot and set them aside.

  3. In the same pot, add the orzo pasta and toast it for a couple of minutes until it turns slightly golden. This will enhance its nutty flavor.

  4. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Stir well to combine all the flavors. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot.

  5. Let the orzo cook for about 10-12 minutes, or until it becomes tender and absorbs most of the liquid. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  6. Once the orzo is cooked, return the shrimp to the pot. Add the dried oregano, dried thyme, and dried parsley. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preference.

  7. Stir everything together and let it simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  8. Remove the pot from the heat and garnish with fresh chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  9. Serve the One Pot Lemon Herb Shrimp and Orzo hot and enjoy the burst of flavors with every bite. This dish pairs well with a crisp green salad or some crusty bread.

Final Thoughts:

One Pot Lemon Herb Shrimp and Orzo is a delightful recipe that brings together the tanginess of lemon, the aromatic herbs, and the succulent shrimp. The beauty of this dish lies in its simplicity and the fact that it requires only one pot, making cleanup a breeze. The orzo pasta adds a comforting and hearty element to the dish, making it a satisfying meal for any occasion.

The flavors of this dish are versatile and can be easily customized to suit your taste. Feel free to experiment with different herbs or add some vegetables like spinach or cherry tomatoes for an extra burst of freshness. You can also adjust the amount of lemon juice and zest to your liking, depending on how bold you want the citrus flavor to be.

So, the next time you’re craving a flavorful and hassle-free meal, give this One Pot Lemon Herb Shrimp and Orzo recipe a try. It’s a guaranteed crowd-pleaser that will leave you satisfied and wanting more. Enjoy!
