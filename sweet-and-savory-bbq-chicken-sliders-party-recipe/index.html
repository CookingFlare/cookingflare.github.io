Sweet and Savory BBQ Chicken Sliders: Party Recipe


Are you looking for the perfect party recipe that will leave your guests begging for more? Look no further than these mouthwatering Sweet and Savory BBQ Chicken Sliders! Packed with flavor and easy to make, these sliders are sure to be a hit at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or a casual get-together, these sliders will steal the show.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Sliders:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 1 cup coleslaw
  • Pickles, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the barbecue sauce, honey, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, and onion powder until well combined. This will be the flavorful glaze for the chicken.

  3. Place the chicken breasts on the preheated grill and brush them generously with the barbecue glaze. Cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).

  4. While the chicken is cooking, lightly toast the slider buns on the grill for a few minutes until they are warm and slightly crispy.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, thinly slice the chicken breasts into strips.

  6. To assemble the sliders, spread a spoonful of coleslaw on the bottom half of each bun. Top with a generous portion of the sliced barbecue chicken. Add a pickle slice if desired. Finish with the top half of the bun.

  7. Serve the Sweet and Savory BBQ Chicken Sliders immediately and watch as your guests dive in with delight. These sliders are the perfect combination of sweet and savory flavors, with the tender chicken and tangy barbecue glaze complementing each other perfectly. The coleslaw adds a refreshing crunch, while the pickles provide a burst of tanginess.

Tips for Success

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook the chicken in a skillet over medium heat or bake it in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through.

  • Feel free to customize these sliders to your liking. You can add cheese, avocado, or even bacon for a twist on the classic recipe.

  • Make the coleslaw ahead of time to save prep time on the day of your party. Simply mix shredded cabbage, carrots, mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, and salt to taste. Let it sit in the refrigerator for a few hours to allow the flavors to meld together.

  • Double or triple the recipe to feed a larger crowd. These sliders are always a crowd-pleaser, so you’ll want to make sure you have enough to go around.

So, what are you waiting for? Fire up the grill and get ready to impress your guests with these Sweet and Savory BBQ Chicken Sliders. With their irresistible flavors and easy preparation, these sliders are the ultimate party recipe. Enjoy!
