Spicy Chicken Tortilla Stew: A Mexican-Inspired Dish


Are you in the mood for a hearty and flavorful Mexican-inspired dish? Look no further than this Spicy Chicken Tortilla Stew recipe! Packed with tender chicken, a medley of vegetables, and just the right amount of spice, this stew is sure to satisfy your cravings. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a gathering with friends, this dish is guaranteed to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Tortilla chips (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the onion, garlic, bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper. Cook until the vegetables are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes.

  4. Add the browned chicken back into the pot, along with the frozen corn kernels and black beans. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors have melded together.

  5. Just before serving, squeeze in the juice of one lime for a burst of freshness. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  6. Ladle the Spicy Chicken Tortilla Stew into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with tortilla chips on the side for some added crunch.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder stew, you can reduce the amount of chili powder and jalapeno pepper to suit your taste.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables such as zucchini, carrots, or diced sweet potatoes for extra flavor and nutrition.
  • For a creamier texture, you can stir in a dollop of sour cream or top each bowl with a sprinkle of shredded cheese.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors will continue to develop over time, making it even more delicious.

Now that you have the recipe for this Spicy Chicken Tortilla Stew, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking! This dish is perfect for cozy nights at home or for entertaining guests. The combination of tender chicken, vibrant vegetables, and bold spices will transport your taste buds to Mexico. So, put on your apron and let’s get started on this mouthwatering culinary adventure!
