Savory Breakfast Casserole Recipes for a Crowd


When it comes to feeding a large group of people, especially for breakfast, it can be a daunting task. But fear not, dear cooks! Savory breakfast casserole recipes are here to save the day. With its ease of preparation, versatility, and crowd-pleasing appeal, a savory breakfast casserole is the perfect solution for your next brunch gathering or family breakfast.

The Magic of Savory Breakfast Casseroles

What makes savory breakfast casseroles so special? For starters, they are incredibly easy to prepare. Simply combine your favorite ingredients, such as vegetables, meats, and cheeses, in a baking dish, and let the oven do the rest. This means you can focus on other important tasks, like getting the coffee brewing or setting the table.

Another benefit of savory breakfast casseroles is their versatility. You can customize the recipe to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Want to add some spice? Throw in some diced jalapeños or red pepper flakes. Looking for a vegetarian option? Substitute the sausage with mushrooms or bell peppers. The possibilities are endless!

Recipe 1: Sausage and Potato Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 pound sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large skillet, sauté the sweet potatoes, onion, and garlic until the potatoes are tender.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, arrange half of the sweet potato mixture in the bottom of the dish.
  5. Top with half of the sausage, half of the cheddar cheese, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Pour half of the egg mixture over the top.
  7. Repeat the layers, starting with the remaining sweet potato mixture, sausage, cheese, and egg mixture.
  8. Sprinkle the parsley over the top and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the cheese is melted and golden brown.

Recipe 2: Mushroom and Spinach Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large skillet, sauté the mushrooms and garlic until the mushrooms are tender.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, arrange half of the mushroom mixture in the bottom of the dish.
  5. Top with half of the spinach, half of the Swiss cheese, and half of the feta cheese.
  6. Pour half of the egg mixture over the top.
  7. Repeat the layers, starting with the remaining mushroom mixture, spinach, cheese, and egg mixture.
  8. Sprinkle the parsley over the top and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the cheese is melted and golden brown.

Recipe 3: Bacon and Chive Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup chives, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large skillet, sauté the bacon and garlic until the bacon is crispy.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, arrange half of the bacon mixture in the bottom of the dish.
  5. Top with half of the chives, half of the cheddar cheese, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Pour half of the egg mixture over the top.
  7. Repeat the layers, starting with the remaining bacon mixture, chives, cheese, and egg mixture.
  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the cheese is melted and golden brown.

Conclusion

Savory breakfast casseroles are the perfect solution for feeding a crowd. With their ease of preparation, versatility, and crowd-pleasing appeal, you can’t go wrong. Whether you’re looking for a meat-based option or a vegetarian alternative, there’s a savory breakfast casserole recipe out there for you. So go ahead, get creative, and start cooking!
