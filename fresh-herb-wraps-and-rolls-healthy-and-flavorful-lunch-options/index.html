Fresh Herb Wraps and Rolls: Healthy and Flavorful Lunch Options


Are you tired of the same old boring sandwiches for lunch? If so, it’s time to give fresh herb wraps and rolls a try! Packed with vibrant flavors and nutritious ingredients, these wraps and rolls are not only delicious but also a healthy lunch option. Whether you’re looking for a light and refreshing meal or something more substantial, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, let’s dive in and discover some exciting new lunch ideas!

1. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cup mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup sliced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh mint leaves

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface and spread the hummus evenly over it.
  2. Layer the mixed greens, cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese on top of the hummus.
  3. Place a few fresh mint leaves on the filling.
  4. Roll the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Slice the wrap in half diagonally and secure with toothpicks if desired.
  6. Enjoy your Mediterranean veggie wrap as is or with a side of tzatziki sauce!

2. Thai Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped roasted peanuts
  • Butter lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, lime juice, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes until well combined.
  2. Add the shredded chicken, shredded carrots, chopped cilantro, and chopped peanuts to the bowl. Mix everything together until the chicken is coated in the sauce.
  3. Spoon the chicken mixture onto butter lettuce leaves, dividing it evenly among the leaves.
  4. Roll the lettuce leaves up, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Secure the wraps with toothpicks if needed.
  6. Serve your Thai chicken lettuce wraps with a side of sweet chili sauce for dipping.

3. Fresh Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • 1/2 cup cooked vermicelli noodles
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced cucumbers
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped roasted peanuts
  • Hoisin sauce, for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Fill a shallow dish with warm water.
  2. Dip one rice paper wrapper into the water, allowing it to soften for about 10 seconds.
  3. Place the softened wrapper on a clean surface.
  4. Arrange a small amount of vermicelli noodles, shredded carrots, sliced cucumbers, bean sprouts, basil, mint, cilantro, and roasted peanuts in a line down the center of the wrapper.
  5. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling, then tightly roll it up from bottom to top.
  6. Repeat steps 2-5 with the remaining rice paper wrappers and filling ingredients.
  7. Serve the fresh spring rolls with hoisin sauce for dipping.

Conclusion

Fresh herb wraps and rolls are not only a healthy and flavorful lunch option, but they also provide a great opportunity to experiment with different combinations of herbs, veggies, and proteins. Whether you’re a vegetarian or a meat lover, there’s a wrap or roll recipe out there to suit your taste buds. So, why settle for a mundane lunch when you can elevate your midday meal with these delicious and nutritious options? Give these recipes a try and discover a whole new world of lunchtime satisfaction!
