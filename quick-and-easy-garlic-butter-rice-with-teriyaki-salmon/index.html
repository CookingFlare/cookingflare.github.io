Quick and Easy Garlic Butter Rice with Teriyaki Salmon


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to prepare, then you’re in the right place. This recipe for Garlic Butter Rice with Teriyaki Salmon is a flavor-packed dish that will impress your family and friends without keeping you tied to the kitchen for hours. The combination of savory garlic butter rice and succulent teriyaki salmon creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will leave everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

For the Garlic Butter Rice:

  • 2 cups of jasmine rice
  • 3 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

For the Teriyaki Salmon:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/2 cup of teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Garlic Butter Rice:

    • Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
    • In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
    • Add the rice to the skillet and cook, stirring frequently, for 2-3 minutes.
    • Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
    • Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
    • Fluff the rice with a fork and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.

  2. Prepare the Teriyaki Salmon:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, honey, soy sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger.
    • Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the teriyaki marinade over them, ensuring they are evenly coated.
    • Marinate the salmon in the refrigerator for 30 minutes, flipping once halfway through.
    • Preheat the grill or broiler to medium-high heat.
    • Remove the salmon from the marinade and place it on the grill or under the broiler.
    • Cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the salmon is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
    • Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.

  3. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Divide the garlic butter rice among serving plates and top each portion with a teriyaki salmon fillet.
    • Garnish with additional chopped parsley and green onions, if desired.
    • Serve hot and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Conclusion

This Quick and Easy Garlic Butter Rice with Teriyaki Salmon recipe is a wonderful option for those busy weeknights when you want a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The rich, buttery flavor of the garlic rice perfectly complements the sweet and savory teriyaki salmon, creating a delightful dining experience for you and your loved ones. Give this recipe a try and savor the convenience and deliciousness it brings to your table!
