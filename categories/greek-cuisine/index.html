Mouthwatering Greek Moussaka Stuffed Peppers Recipe

If you’re a fan of Greek cuisine and love the rich flavors of moussaka, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering Greek moussaka stuffed peppers recipe. This dish combines the classic flavors of moussaka with the vibrant, colorful appeal of stuffed peppers, resulting in a delightful culinary experience that will tantalize your taste buds. Ingredients For the Stuffed Peppers: 6 large bell peppers (assorted colors for visual appeal) 1 pound ground lamb or beef 1 onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup cooked rice 1 can diced tomatoes 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste Fresh parsley for garnish For the Moussaka Filling: 1 large eggplant, diced 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/4 cup red wine 1 cup tomato sauce 1 cup crumbled feta cheese Bechamel sauce (see below) For the Bechamel Sauce: 3 tablespoons butter 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour 2 cups milk Salt and pepper to taste 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg 2 egg yolks Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Peppers Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes from the inside. Place the peppers in a baking dish and set aside. Step 2: Make the Moussaka Filling Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced eggplant and sauté until softened, about 5-7 minutes. Sprinkle the cinnamon and nutmeg over the eggplant and stir to combine. Pour in the red wine and cook until it evaporates. Stir in the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the crumbled feta cheese. Step 3: Prepare the Bechamel Sauce In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until golden. Gradually whisk in the milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Remove the saucepan from the heat and whisk in the egg yolks. Step 4: Assemble the Stuffed Peppers In a separate skillet, brown the ground lamb or beef with the chopped onion and garlic. Add the cooked rice, diced tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet and stir to combine. Fill each bell pepper with the meat and rice mixture, then spoon the moussaka filling on top. Pour the bechamel sauce over the moussaka filling, covering the tops of the peppers. Step 5: Bake and Serve Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the tops are golden. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving. The combination of savory meat, aromatic spices, and creamy bechamel sauce nestled within vibrant, roasted peppers makes for a truly unforgettable dining experience.
