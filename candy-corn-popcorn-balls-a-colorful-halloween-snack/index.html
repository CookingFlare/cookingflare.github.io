Candy Corn Popcorn Balls: A Colorful Halloween Snack


Halloween is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a fun and colorful treat like Candy Corn Popcorn Balls! These delightful snacks are not only easy to make but also add a festive touch to any Halloween gathering. Whether you’re hosting a spooky party or just looking for a fun activity to do with the kids, these Candy Corn Popcorn Balls are sure to be a hit. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 10 cups of popped popcorn
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/3 cup of light corn syrup
  • 1/3 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Yellow and orange food coloring

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Popcorn: Start by popping 10 cups of popcorn. You can use an air popper or stovetop method, whichever you prefer. Once the popcorn is ready, place it in a large mixing bowl and set it aside.

  2. Make the Candy Coating: In a saucepan, combine the sugar, light corn syrup, unsalted butter, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture comes to a boil. Once it reaches the boiling point, continue to cook for an additional 2 minutes without stirring.

  3. Add Flavor and Color: Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Divide the mixture into three separate bowls. Leave one bowl as is, add yellow food coloring to the second bowl, and orange food coloring to the third bowl. Stir each bowl until the color is evenly distributed.

  4. Coat the Popcorn: Working quickly, pour the white candy coating over the popcorn and gently toss to coat evenly. Repeat this process with the yellow and orange candy coatings, creating layers of color as you go.

  5. Shape the Popcorn Balls: Once the popcorn is evenly coated with the candy mixture, lightly grease your hands and begin shaping the mixture into balls, pressing firmly to ensure they hold together.

  6. Let Them Set: Place the shaped popcorn balls on a parchment-lined baking sheet to set. Allow them to cool and harden for at least 30 minutes before serving.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the Candy Corn Popcorn Balls have set, they are ready to be enjoyed! Serve them at your Halloween party or wrap them individually in plastic wrap for a fun and portable treat.

Tips and Tricks:

  • To prevent the candy coating from sticking to your hands, lightly grease them with cooking spray or butter before shaping the popcorn balls.
  • If you prefer a gooey texture, you can reduce the cooking time for the candy coating, resulting in a softer finish.

Conclusion:

Candy Corn Popcorn Balls are a whimsical and delicious addition to any Halloween celebration. Their vibrant colors and sweet flavor make them a hit with both kids and adults alike. So, why not add a dash of festive fun to your Halloween festivities with these delightful treats? Whether you’re making them for a party or just a fun family activity, Candy Corn Popcorn Balls are sure to bring a smile to everyone’s face. Happy Halloween!
