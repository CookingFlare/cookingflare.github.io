How to Make Soft and Chewy Sugar Cookies


Are you craving a classic sweet treat that’s soft, chewy, and utterly delicious? Look no further because we’ve got the perfect recipe for soft and chewy sugar cookies that will satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving for more. These cookies are incredibly easy to make, and they are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a cozy night in or a gathering with friends and family. So, let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to make these irresistible sugar cookies!

Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar for rolling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set the bowl aside.

  3. Cream the Butter and Sugar: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and 1 ½ cups of white sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy.

  4. Add the Egg and Vanilla: Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined.

  5. Incorporate the Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, mixing until the dough comes together.

  6. Form the Cookie Dough: Using a cookie scoop or your hands, roll the dough into 1-inch balls. Then, roll each ball in the ¼ cup of granulated sugar until coated.

  7. Bake the Cookies: Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.

  8. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, indulge in these soft and chewy sugar cookies!

Tips for Perfect Sugar Cookies

  • Don’t Overmix: When combining the wet and dry ingredients, be careful not to overmix the dough. Overmixing can result in tough cookies, and we want them to be soft and chewy!
  • Room Temperature Ingredients: Make sure your butter, egg, and even the flour are at room temperature. This will help the ingredients blend seamlessly and create a perfect cookie dough.
  • Adjust Baking Time: Keep an eye on the cookies while they’re in the oven. Depending on your oven, the baking time might vary, so adjust accordingly to achieve that perfect soft and chewy texture.

Conclusion

There you have it – a simple and delightful recipe for soft and chewy sugar cookies that will undoubtedly become a favorite in your household. These cookies are a timeless classic, and with this foolproof recipe, you can enjoy them anytime you want. Whether you’re an experienced baker or just starting, this recipe is sure to bring joy and sweetness to your kitchen. So, gather your ingredients and let’s bake up some irresistible soft and chewy sugar cookies!
