Comforting Chicken and Dumpling Casserole


There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting casserole to satisfy your hunger and soothe your soul. And when it comes to comfort food, few dishes can compare to a hearty Chicken and Dumpling Casserole. This classic dish is the perfect combination of tender chicken, fluffy dumplings, and rich, flavorful sauce. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the shredded chicken to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables. Season with dried thyme, dried parsley, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, chicken broth, and milk until well combined. Slowly pour the mixture into the skillet, stirring constantly. Continue cooking until the sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.
  5. Transfer the chicken and vegetable mixture to a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  6. In another bowl, combine the flour, salt, and baking powder to make the dumpling batter. Gradually add water, stirring until the batter forms a soft dough.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter onto the chicken and vegetable mixture in the baking dish.
  8. Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the dumplings are golden brown and cooked through.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This comforting Chicken and Dumpling Casserole is a complete meal in itself, but you can serve it with a side salad, steamed vegetables, or crusty bread to round out the meal. It’s the perfect dish to enjoy on a chilly evening or to bring to a potluck dinner.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add other vegetables like peas or corn for an extra burst of flavor and color.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can add a splash of heavy cream or sour cream to the chicken and vegetable mixture.
  • For an added touch of indulgence, sprinkle some shredded cheese on top of the casserole before baking.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave for a quick and delicious meal.

Conclusion

Chicken and Dumpling Casserole is a timeless classic that never fails to bring comfort and satisfaction. With its tender chicken, fluffy dumplings, and flavorful sauce, it’s a dish that will warm your heart and please your taste buds. So why not give this recipe a try and experience the joy of homemade comfort food? Your family and friends will thank you for it!

Remember, cooking is all about creativity and making the dish your own. Feel free to experiment with different herbs, spices, or even adding your favorite vegetables to customize the recipe to your liking. Enjoy!
