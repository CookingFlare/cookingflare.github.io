Mini Shepherd's Pies: Bite-Sized Comfort Food


There’s something undeniably comforting about digging into a warm, hearty pie. With their flaky crusts and savory fillings, pies have long been a favorite comfort food for many. But what if we told you that you can enjoy all the deliciousness of a classic shepherd’s pie in a bite-sized version? Introducing Mini Shepherd’s Pies – the perfect snack or appetizer that packs a punch of flavor and nostalgia.

The Perfect Combination of Meat and Vegetables

Traditional shepherd’s pie is known for its rich and flavorful combination of ground meat, vegetables, and mashed potatoes. This mini version captures all those elements in a single, perfectly portioned bite. Each mini pie is made with a deliciously seasoned ground meat filling, topped with a dollop of creamy mashed potatoes, and baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients:

For the filling:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the mashed potato topping:

  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a muffin tin and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.

  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Make sure to break up any clumps using a wooden spoon.

  4. Stir in the diced carrots, frozen peas, minced garlic, tomato paste, beef broth, and Worcestershire sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 10 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.

  5. While the filling is simmering, prepare the mashed potato topping. Boil the peeled and cubed potatoes in a large pot of salted water until tender. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot.

  6. Add the butter and milk to the pot with the potatoes. Mash them together until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Scoop a spoonful of the meat filling into each muffin cup, filling them about three-quarters of the way. Top each cup with a generous dollop of mashed potatoes, smoothing it out with the back of a spoon.

  8. Bake the mini pies for approximately 20 minutes, or until the mashed potato topping is golden and slightly crisp.

  9. Remove the muffin tin from the oven and let the pies cool for a few minutes before carefully removing them from the tin. Serve warm and enjoy!

Adding a Personal Touch

One of the great things about mini shepherd’s pies is that they are incredibly versatile. While the classic recipe is undeniably delicious, don’t be afraid to put your own spin on it. You can experiment with different fillings, such as lamb or turkey, or add extra vegetables like corn or green beans. You could even sprinkle some grated cheese on top of the mashed potato topping for an extra layer of indulgence.

Conclusion

Mini Shepherd’s Pies are the epitome of comfort food in bite-sized form. They are perfect for parties, gatherings, or simply as a delicious snack to enjoy on a cozy evening. With their flavorful filling and creamy mashed potato topping, these mini pies are bound to become a favorite in your household. So why not give them a try and experience the warm and comforting flavors for yourself?
