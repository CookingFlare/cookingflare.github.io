Sunday Roast: Classic Recipes for Two


Sunday roast, a traditional British meal, is a perfect choice for a cozy Sunday lunch or dinner. It consists of roasted meat, accompanied by various vegetables, potatoes, and Yorkshire pudding. While most Sunday roast recipes are designed to feed a crowd, here we present classic recipes tailored for two. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Classic Roast Beef

Ingredients

  • 500g beef joint (such as sirloin or ribeye)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Season the beef joint with salt and pepper, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear the beef joint on all sides until browned, about 2 minutes per side.
  5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and roast for 15 minutes for medium-rare or adjust the cooking time according to your preference.
  6. Remove the skillet from the oven and let the meat rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve the roast beef with roasted potatoes, steamed vegetables, and a rich gravy.

Herb-Roasted Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.5kg)
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, and thyme.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper, both inside and out.
  4. Gently loosen the skin of the chicken and spread the herb butter mixture evenly under the skin.
  5. Place the chicken in a roasting pan and roast for about 1 hour, or until the internal temperature reaches 75°C (165°F).
  6. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before carving.
  7. Serve the herb-roasted chicken with roasted vegetables and a side of creamy mashed potatoes.

Vegetarian Nut Roast

Ingredients

  • 200g mixed nuts (such as walnuts, almonds, and cashews), roughly chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 200g mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 100g breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Add the mushrooms and grated carrot, and cook for another 5 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the chopped nuts, breadcrumbs, parsley, thyme, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  6. Add the cooked vegetable mixture to the bowl and mix well.
  7. Transfer the mixture to a greased loaf tin and press it down firmly.
  8. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
  9. Allow the nut roast to cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  10. Serve the vegetarian nut roast with roasted root vegetables and a tangy cranberry sauce.

Enjoy these classic Sunday roast recipes for two, perfect for a special meal with your loved one. Whether you prefer a succulent roast beef, a herb-infused roasted chicken, or a vegetarian nut roast, these recipes are sure to impress. So set the table, light some candles, and savor the flavors of a traditional Sunday roast, made just for the two of you!
