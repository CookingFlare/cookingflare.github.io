Satisfying One-Pot Beef and Vegetable Stir-Fry: A Quick Meal


Are you looking for a quick and satisfying meal that’s packed with flavor and nutrients? Look no further than this delicious one-pot beef and vegetable stir-fry recipe. Not only is it easy to make, but it’s also a versatile dish that you can customize with your favorite ingredients. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe and get ready to elevate your culinary skills.

Ingredients

For the Stir-Fry Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup beef broth

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, brown sugar, cornstarch, and beef broth. Set aside.

  2. Cook the Beef: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the thinly sliced flank steak and cook for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Stir-Fry the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the red and yellow bell peppers, snow peas, broccoli, and carrots. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked beef to the skillet. Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything in the sauce. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  5. Finish and Serve: Add the sliced green onions to the stir-fry and stir to combine. Remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the beef and vegetable stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.

Tips for Success

  • Prep Ahead: You can slice the vegetables and beef in advance to make the cooking process even quicker.
  • Customization: Feel free to add your favorite vegetables or swap the beef for chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a different twist.
  • Serve with Style: Garnish your stir-fry with sesame seeds, chopped peanuts, or cilantro for an extra pop of flavor and texture.

Why This Recipe Rocks

This one-pot beef and vegetable stir-fry is a fantastic option for busy weeknights when you want a wholesome meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of tender beef, crunchy vegetables, and savory sauce creates a harmony of flavors and textures that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Plus, the simplicity of cooking everything in one pot means less cleanup and more time to enjoy your meal.

So, whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire. Give it a try and experience the joy of creating a delicious meal that’s as easy as it is satisfying.
