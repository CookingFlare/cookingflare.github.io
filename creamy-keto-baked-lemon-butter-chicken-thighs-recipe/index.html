Creamy Keto Baked Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs Recipe


If you’re looking for a flavor-packed, satisfying meal that fits perfectly into a ketogenic lifestyle, look no further! This Creamy Keto Baked Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs recipe is not only delicious but also simple to prepare. With its zesty lemon flavor and rich, creamy sauce, this dish will quickly become a favorite in your household. Let’s dive into how to make this mouthwatering meal that’s perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

To prepare this dish, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Chicken:

  • 4-6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Lemon Butter Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme (or 1 tablespoon fresh thyme)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Kitchen Tools Needed

  • Baking dish
  • Mixing bowl
  • Whisk
  • Skillet
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Knife and cutting board

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Thighs

Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). While the oven is heating, prepare the chicken thighs. Pat them dry with paper towels to remove any excess moisture, which helps achieve a crispy skin when baking. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Sear the Chicken

In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken thighs skin-side down. Sear them for about 5-7 minutes, or until the skin is golden brown and crispy. Flip the chicken thighs over and sear the other side for an additional 3-4 minutes. The goal here is to get a nice, crispy skin that will hold onto the flavors of the sauce.

Once seared, transfer the chicken thighs to a baking dish, skin-side up, and set them aside.

Step 3: Make the Creamy Lemon Butter Sauce

In the same skillet, reduce the heat to medium and add the unsalted butter. Allow it to melt, then add the minced garlic. Sauté the garlic for about 1 minute, until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.

Next, add the lemon zest and lemon juice to the skillet, stirring well to combine. This brightens up the sauce and infuses it with a vibrant, citrusy flavor. Allow the mixture to simmer for a couple of minutes to reduce slightly.

Now, slowly whisk in the heavy cream and Dijon mustard. Stir to combine, and let the sauce cook for another 3-4 minutes until it thickens slightly. Season the sauce with dried thyme, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper to taste. If you prefer more tanginess, feel free to adjust the lemon juice according to your preference.

Step 4: Combine and Bake

Once the sauce is ready, pour it over the chicken thighs in the baking dish, ensuring each piece is well-coated. This creamy lemon butter sauce is the star of the dish, and it will keep the chicken moist and flavorful as it bakes.

Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and place it in the preheated oven. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C). For an extra touch, remove the foil during the last 10 minutes of baking to allow the skin to crisp up even more.

Step 5: Serve

Once the chicken is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. This resting period allows the juices to redistribute, making the chicken even more succulent. Serve the chicken thighs with the creamy lemon butter sauce drizzled generously on top. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Pairing Suggestions

This Creamy Keto Baked Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs recipe pairs wonderfully with a variety of sides. Here are some suggestions to complete your meal:

  • Garlic Mashed Cauliflower: A low-carb alternative to traditional mashed potatoes, this creamy cauliflower dish complements the chicken perfectly.
  • Zucchini Noodles: Light and refreshing, spiralized zucchini tossed in olive oil and garlic makes for a great low-carb side.
  • Roasted Asparagus: The earthy flavors of roasted asparagus balance the richness of the chicken and add a vibrant touch to your plate.
  • Keto Salad: A fresh green salad with leafy greens, avocado, and a lemon vinaigrette will brighten up the meal.

Nutritional Information

One serving of Creamy Keto Baked Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs provides approximately:

  • Calories: 450
  • Fat: 36g
  • Protein: 30g
  • Total Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Fiber: 0g
  • Net Carbs: 4g

This recipe is not only delicious but also keeps your carb intake low, making it a perfect addition to your keto meal plan.

Tips for Success

  1. Use High-Quality Chicken: The quality of your chicken can significantly affect the flavor of your dish. Opt for organic, free-range chicken thighs if possible.
  2. Adjust the Creaminess: If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can simmer it longer to reduce it further. For a lighter option, use half-and-half instead of heavy cream.
  3. Flavor Variations: Feel free to experiment with different herbs such as rosemary or basil, or add a pinch of red pepper flakes for some heat.
  4. Make Ahead: This dish can be prepared in advance. Simply follow the recipe up to the baking step, cover, and refrigerate. When ready to bake, allow the dish to come to room temperature before placing it in the oven.

Conclusion

This Creamy Keto Baked Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs recipe is a delightful way to enjoy a wholesome, low-carb meal that’s brimming with flavor. The combination of tender chicken thighs, tangy lemon, and rich butter sauce creates a dish that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a comforting meal, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Try this recipe today and enjoy the creamy goodness of lemon butter chicken thighs that everyone will love! Happy cooking!
