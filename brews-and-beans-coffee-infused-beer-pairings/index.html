Brews and Beans: Coffee-Infused Beer Pairings


Sometimes a regular beer just won’t do. You’re craving something a little different, a little more robust, and a little more invigorating. That’s where coffee-infused beer comes in. Combining the rich aroma and taste of coffee with the smoothness of a good beer, coffee-infused beers can be an excellent way to satisfy your thirst while stimulating your senses. Pair these with the right foods, and you’ll have a culinary experience that’s out of this world.

Black and Tandy

One of the most popular coffee-infused beers is the Black and Tandy, a collaboration between Tandem Coffee Roasters and a local craft brewery. Here’s how you can recreate this delicious experience at home:

Ingredients:

  • 1 freshly brewed double shot espresso
  • 1 pint of porter or stout
  • Sweetener, to taste
  • Milk, cold

Instructions:

  1. Brew a double shot of espresso and let it cool.
  2. Pour the cold espresso into a pint glass filled with ice.
  3. Pour in the porter or stout.
  4. Add a dash of sweetener to taste.
  5. Top with cold milk, giving the drink a gentle stir.

Pair with: A classic chocolate brownie or a robust red wine like Cabernet Sauvignon.

Caffè Americano Lager

The Caffè Americano Lager takes its inspiration from the classic American lager, but with a twist of bold Italian espresso. The resulting brew is smooth, crisp, and perfectly balanced. Here’s how you can whip one up at home:

Ingredients:

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 pint of lager
  • Lemon twist, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Pull a shot of espresso.
  2. Chill the espresso.
  3. Pour the espresso into a chilled pint glass.
  4. Top with the lager.
  5. Garnish with a lemon twist.

Pair with: A lightly grilled chicken breast or a Caprese salad.

Flat White IPA

The Flat White IPA is a taste of Australia, blending a velvety flat white coffee with a hoppy India Pale Ale. Its aroma is as fresh as a beautifully brewed coffee, while its finish is long and satisfyingly bitter. Here’s how you can make your own Flat White IPA:

Ingredients:

  • 1 double shot of espresso
  • 1 pint of IPA
  • Lemon wedge, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Brew a double shot of espresso.
  2. Pour the espresso into a chilled pint glass.
  3. Slowly top with the IPA.
  4. Stir gently.
  5. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Pair with: A hearty steak or a plate of cheese and crackers.

Nitro Cold Brew and Cream Ale

Finally, consider treating your taste buds to the Nitro Cold Brew and Cream Ale. This beer is filtered through nitrogen, resulting in a velvety texture and a smooth coffee taste. Here’s how to make your own:

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter of cold brew coffee
  • 1 pint of cream ale
  • Cream, for topping

Instructions:

  1. Pour the cold brew coffee into a pitcher.
  2. Add the cream ale slowly, stirring gently to combine.
  3. Top with a dollop of cream.

Pair with: A slice of vanilla cake or a fruit tart.

Coffee-infused beers offer a unique, robust taste that pairs well with a variety of dishes. Try adding these beers to your next dinner, and experience a whole new level of culinary delight. Cheers!
