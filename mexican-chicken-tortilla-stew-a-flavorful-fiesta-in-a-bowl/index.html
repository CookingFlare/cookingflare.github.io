Mexican Chicken Tortilla Stew: A Flavorful Fiesta in a Bowl


Are you ready to embark on a culinary journey filled with bold flavors and vibrant colors? Look no further than this Mexican Chicken Tortilla Stew recipe! Bursting with the authentic taste of Mexico, this hearty and comforting dish will transport your taste buds to the streets of Mexico City.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breast, boneless and skinless
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the diced chicken breast to the pot and cook until browned on all sides. This will give the stew a rich flavor and add depth to the dish.

  3. Once the chicken is cooked, add the diced bell peppers, canned tomatoes, black beans, and corn kernels to the pot. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and add the chili powder, cumin, paprika, and oregano. These spices will infuse the stew with the irresistible Mexican flavors we all love.

  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to taste as you go and adjust the seasoning accordingly.

  6. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 30 minutes. This will allow all the flavors to meld together and create a harmonious blend.

  7. While the stew is simmering, prepare your garnishes. Chop some fresh cilantro and cut lime wedges for serving. These additions will elevate the flavors and add a refreshing touch to the stew.

  8. Once the stew is ready, ladle it into bowls and garnish with the chopped cilantro. Squeeze a wedge of lime over each serving to add a zesty kick.

  9. Serve the Mexican Chicken Tortilla Stew with a side of tortilla chips for dipping or crumbling on top for added crunch. This will give the dish an authentic Mexican twist.

  10. Gather your loved ones around the table and savor each spoonful of this flavorful fiesta in a bowl. Let the aromas and tastes transport you to the vibrant streets of Mexico, where every meal is a celebration of culture and tradition.

Final Thoughts

This Mexican Chicken Tortilla Stew is a perfect dish for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of Mexico, this recipe will satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. The combination of tender chicken, aromatic spices, and vibrant vegetables makes this stew a true delight for the senses.

So, gather your ingredients, put on some lively Mexican music, and let the flavors of this stew dance on your palate. It’s time to indulge in a flavorful fiesta in a bowl!
