Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe for Game Day Snacking


Are you looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer that will spice up your game day snacking? Look no further than this Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Dip recipe! Packed with flavor and heat, this dip is sure to be a hit at any gathering. Plus, it’s super easy to make in your Instant Pot, saving you time and effort in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Tortilla chips or celery sticks, for serving

Instructions

  1. In your Instant Pot, combine the shredded chicken, cream cheese, buffalo sauce, ranch dressing, and sour cream. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  2. Lock the lid in place and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural release of pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  3. Carefully remove the lid and give the dip a good stir to ensure all the ingredients are well incorporated. If you prefer a smoother consistency, use an immersion blender to blend the dip until smooth.

  4. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese and crumbled blue cheese until melted and creamy. The heat from the dip will help melt the cheese, but if needed, you can turn on the sauté function on your Instant Pot to help melt the cheese faster.

  5. Once the cheese is fully melted and the dip is smooth, transfer it to a serving dish. Sprinkle the sliced green onions on top for added freshness and color.

  6. Serve the Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Dip warm with tortilla chips or celery sticks for dipping. The combination of the spicy buffalo sauce, creamy cheese, and tender chicken will have your guests coming back for more.

Tips and Variations

  • If you like your dip extra spicy, feel free to add more buffalo sauce to taste. You can also add a dash of cayenne pepper or hot sauce for an extra kick.
  • For a milder version, reduce the amount of buffalo sauce or substitute it with your favorite barbecue sauce.
  • Don’t have an Instant Pot? No worries! You can easily make this dip on the stovetop. Simply combine all the ingredients in a saucepan and heat over medium heat until melted and creamy.
  • Want to make this dip ahead of time? Prepare the dip as instructed and store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator. When you’re ready to serve, reheat it in the Instant Pot or stovetop until warmed through.
  • Feel free to get creative with your toppings! Try adding some diced jalapeños, crumbled bacon, or extra blue cheese on top for added flavor and texture.

So, there you have it – a delicious and spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Dip recipe that will elevate your game day snacking to a whole new level. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply enjoying a cozy night in, this dip is a guaranteed winner. Give it a try and get ready to score big with your guests!
