Savory Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan Soup Recipe


When the weather turns chilly, there’s nothing quite as comforting as a warm bowl of soup. This Savory Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan Soup is a delightful twist on the classic Italian dish. Packed with flavor, this recipe is not only easy to prepare, but it also allows the slow cooker to do all the work for you. Let’s dive right into the ingredients, preparation, and tips that will help you create this mouthwatering soup that your entire family will love!

Ingredients

To make this scrumptious Chicken Parmesan Soup, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Chicken: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • Broth: 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • Tomatoes: 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • Onion: 1 medium onion, diced
  • Garlic: 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Carrots: 2 medium carrots, diced
  • Celery: 2 stalks celery, diced
  • Italian seasoning: 1 tablespoon dried Italian seasoning
  • Bay leaves: 2
  • Parmesan cheese: 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
  • Basil: Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Salt and pepper: To taste
  • Pasta: 1 cup small pasta (e.g., ditalini or elbow macaroni)

Preparation Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Base

Start by heating the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté the diced onion, carrots, and celery until they begin to soften, about 5-7 minutes. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute until fragrant. This step helps to develop a rich flavor base for your soup.

Step 2: Layer the Ingredients

In the slow cooker, add the sautéed vegetables, diced tomatoes (with their juices), and chicken broth. Stir in the Italian seasoning and bay leaves for an infusion of herby goodness. Next, gently place the chicken breasts or thighs into the broth mixture, ensuring they are submerged. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3: Slow Cook to Perfection

Cover your slow cooker and set it to low heat for 6-8 hours or high heat for 3-4 hours. Letting the chicken simmer slowly helps it become tender and allows the flavors to meld beautifully.

Step 4: Shred the Chicken

Once the cooking time is up, carefully remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. This will create bite-sized pieces that blend seamlessly into the soup. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

Step 5: Add the Pasta

About 30 minutes before serving, add the small pasta to the soup. Make sure to stir it in well. If you’re using a high setting, you can cook the pasta directly in the soup for about 15-20 minutes, or until al dente.

Step 6: Finish with Cheese

Once the pasta is cooked, stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. This will give the soup a creamy texture and a delicious cheesy flavor. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

Step 7: Serve and Garnish

Ladle the Chicken Parmesan Soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves and additional Parmesan cheese. Serve it hot with crusty bread or a side salad for a complete meal.

Tips for Making the Best Chicken Parmesan Soup

  • Use Quality Ingredients: The flavor of your soup will largely depend on the quality of your ingredients. Opt for fresh vegetables, good quality chicken, and authentic Parmesan cheese for the best results.

  • Customize the Veggies: Feel free to add other vegetables like zucchini or bell peppers to enhance the nutritional profile and flavor of your soup.

  • Spice it Up: If you enjoy a bit of heat, consider adding red pepper flakes or a splash of hot sauce to the soup for an extra kick.

  • Make it Ahead: This soup can be made a day in advance and stored in the refrigerator. The flavors will deepen as it sits, making it even more delicious when reheated.

  • Freezing Leftovers: If you have leftovers, this soup freezes well. Just make sure to leave out the pasta before freezing, as it may become mushy when thawed. You can always cook fresh pasta when you’re ready to enjoy the leftovers.

Nutritional Benefits

This Savory Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan Soup is not just delicious; it’s also packed with nutrients. Chicken is a great source of lean protein, while the vegetables add vitamins, minerals, and fiber. The addition of tomatoes brings lycopene to the table, a powerful antioxidant. Plus, the Parmesan cheese offers calcium and flavor without overwhelming the dish.

Conclusion

This Savory Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan Soup is a fantastic meal for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. It’s easy to prepare, requires minimal hands-on time, and delivers a hearty, satisfying dish that will warm your soul. Whether you serve it for dinner or pack it for lunch, this soup is sure to become a family favorite. So, dust off that slow cooker, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to this comforting bowl of goodness today!

Enjoy your cooking adventure, and don’t forget to share this recipe with friends and family who are looking to warm up their dinner table with something special. Happy cooking!
