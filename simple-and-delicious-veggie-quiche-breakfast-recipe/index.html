Simple and Delicious Veggie Quiche: Breakfast Recipe


Are you tired of the same old breakfast routine? Looking for a simple and delicious way to start your day? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be sharing a mouthwatering recipe for a veggie quiche that is sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you energized throughout the morning.

Ingredients

To make this scrumptious veggie quiche, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust (or you can make your own from scratch if you prefer)
  • 1 cup of chopped vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and spinach)
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese (cheddar or Swiss work well)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Start by preparing your pre-made pie crust. If you are using a frozen crust, make sure to thaw it according to the package instructions. If you prefer to make your own crust from scratch, now is the time to do so.

  3. Once your crust is ready, place it in a pie dish and set it aside.

  4. In a skillet, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add your chopped vegetables and sauté them until they are tender and slightly golden. This will take about 5-7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Spread the sautéed vegetables evenly over the pie crust.

  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  7. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables in the pie crust. Make sure it is evenly distributed.

  8. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on top of the egg mixture.

  9. Carefully transfer the quiche to the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the center is set.

  10. Once the quiche is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This veggie quiche is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve it warm alongside a fresh green salad for a light yet satisfying meal.
  • Cut it into bite-sized pieces and serve it as an appetizer at your next brunch gathering.
  • Pack a slice or two for a quick and nutritious on-the-go breakfast or lunch.

Final Thoughts

With its flaky crust, creamy egg filling, and a medley of savory vegetables, this simple and delicious veggie quiche is sure to become a breakfast favorite in your household. It’s a great way to incorporate more veggies into your diet and start your day on a healthy note.

So why not give this recipe a try? Gather your ingredients, follow the instructions, and enjoy the delightful flavors of this veggie quiche. Your taste buds will thank you!
