Satisfying Nut-Free Cabbage Roll Recipe


If you’re looking for a hearty and satisfying meal that’s also nut-free, this cabbage roll recipe is sure to hit the spot. Cabbage rolls are a classic comfort food, and this nut-free version is perfect for those with nut allergies or anyone looking for a delicious and wholesome meal. Packed with flavorful ingredients and a savory tomato sauce, these cabbage rolls are sure to become a new favorite in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Cabbage Rolls:

  • 1 head of green cabbage
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 14-ounce cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cabbage Leaves:

    • Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil.
    • Carefully remove the core from the cabbage head and place the whole head in the boiling water.
    • Let the cabbage cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the outer leaves are soft and pliable.
    • Remove the cabbage from the water and carefully separate the leaves. Set them aside to cool.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, cooked rice, chopped onion, minced garlic, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  3. Assemble the Cabbage Rolls:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F.
    • Place a cabbage leaf on a flat surface and spoon a portion of the filling onto the center of the leaf.
    • Fold the sides of the leaf over the filling, then roll it up from the bottom to enclose the filling. Repeat with the remaining cabbage leaves and filling.

  4. Make the Tomato Sauce:

    • In a saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add the crushed tomatoes, sugar, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes.

  5. Bake the Cabbage Rolls:

    • Pour a thin layer of the tomato sauce into the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Place the cabbage rolls seam side down in the dish.
    • Pour the remaining tomato sauce over the cabbage rolls.

  6. **Bake the cabbage rolls in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the cabbage is tender and the filling is cooked through.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the cabbage rolls are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
    • Serve the cabbage rolls with a spoonful of the tomato sauce and enjoy!

Final Thoughts

These nut-free cabbage rolls are a delicious and satisfying meal that’s perfect for a cozy family dinner or a gathering with friends. The flavorful filling and savory tomato sauce come together to create a dish that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re catering to nut allergies or just looking for a new twist on a classic recipe, these cabbage rolls are a must-try. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to enjoy this nut-free delight!
