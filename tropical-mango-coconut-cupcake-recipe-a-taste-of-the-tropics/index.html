Tropical Mango Coconut Cupcake Recipe: A Taste of the Tropics


Who doesn’t love the flavors of the tropics? With their vibrant colors and exotic tastes, tropical treats never fail to transport us to a paradise of sunshine and relaxation. In today’s recipe, we’ll be combining the luscious sweetness of mangoes with the creamy richness of coconut to create Tropical Mango Coconut Cupcakes that will make your taste buds dance with delight. So, put on your apron and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mango puree (about 2 ripe mangoes)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk

For the frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup mango puree
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the mango puree and coconut milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with the batter. Smooth the tops with a spoon or spatula.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

  8. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the frosting. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mango puree, coconut milk, and vanilla extract. Beat until light and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes.

  9. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them generously with the mango coconut frosting. For an extra touch of paradise, sprinkle some shredded coconut on top.

  10. Enjoy your Tropical Mango Coconut Cupcakes! These little delights are perfect for summer parties, picnics, or whenever you want to bring a taste of the tropics to your dessert table.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of mango flavor, you can add small chunks of fresh mango to the cupcake batter before baking.
  • If you prefer a lighter frosting, you can substitute whipped cream for the buttercream frosting.
  • For a more pronounced coconut taste, you can add a teaspoon of coconut extract to the cupcake batter and frosting.
  • Experiment with different decorations to make your cupcakes truly tropical. Consider using edible flowers, colorful sprinkles, or even a cocktail umbrella.

Now that you have the recipe for these delightful Tropical Mango Coconut Cupcakes, it’s time to gather your ingredients and let your kitchen be transformed into a tropical oasis. So, grab your mixing bowls, turn up the music, and let the flavors of the tropics take you on a sweet and delicious journey. Happy baking!
