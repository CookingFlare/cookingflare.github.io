Easy One Pot Chicken Fajita Pasta


If you’re looking for a delicious and convenient meal that’s perfect for busy weeknights, then this easy one pot chicken fajita pasta is the answer. Packed with flavors of tender chicken, vibrant bell peppers, and savory spices, this dish is a delightful fusion of Mexican and Italian cuisines. The best part? It all comes together in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to tantalize your taste buds with this delightful dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 1/2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 10 ounces of penne pasta
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes, drained
  • 2 teaspoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and cook until they are no longer pink. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic. Sauté the vegetables until they are tender-crisp.

  3. Add the chicken broth, heavy cream, penne pasta, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cover the skillet and let the pasta cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until it is tender, stirring occasionally.

  5. Once the pasta is cooked, return the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir to combine everything. Let the dish simmer for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Taste the pasta and adjust the seasoning if necessary. If you prefer a little kick, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes at this point.

  7. Once everything is well combined and seasoned to your liking, remove the skillet from the heat.

  8. Serve the chicken fajita pasta hot, garnished with fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness and color.

Enjoy!

This one pot chicken fajita pasta is a complete meal in itself, offering protein, vegetables, and pasta all in one dish. It’s a fantastic way to satisfy your cravings for both fajitas and pasta in a single, convenient recipe. The creamy texture of the dish combined with the bold flavors of the fajita seasoning will surely make it a hit with your family and friends.

The best part about this recipe is that it’s incredibly versatile. You can customize it to suit your preferences by adding ingredients like black beans, corn, or even a sprinkle of cheese on top. Additionally, it’s a great way to use up any leftover chicken or vegetables you have in your fridge.

Not only is this dish delicious, but it also saves you from a pile of dishes at the end of the night. With just one pot to clean, you’ll have more time to relax and enjoy your meal without worrying about a sink full of dirty dishes.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and flavorful meal without the hassle of a complicated cooking process, give this easy one pot chicken fajita pasta a try. It’s a delightful combination of simplicity and bold flavors that’s sure to become a regular in your dinner rotation.

Happy cooking and bon appétit!
