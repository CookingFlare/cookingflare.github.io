BBQ Shrimp Skewers: Easy and Flavorful Recipes for Summer


The summer season calls for outdoor gatherings, barbecues, and delicious grilled meals. And what better way to celebrate the warm weather than with BBQ shrimp skewers? Bursting with flavors and easy to make, these skewers are the perfect addition to any summer cookout. Whether you’re hosting a backyard party or simply enjoying a relaxing evening with your loved ones, these BBQ shrimp skewers are sure to impress. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the shrimp by placing them in a bowl.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, paprika, cayenne pepper, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Pour the marinade over the shrimp and toss gently to ensure each shrimp is coated evenly. Allow the shrimp to marinate for at least 15 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked wooden skewers, ensuring they are evenly spaced. This will help prevent them from sticking to the grill.

  5. Once the grill is hot, place the shrimp skewers on the grill grates. Cook for approximately 2-3 minutes per side or until the shrimp turn opaque and slightly charred.

  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro for an added pop of color.

  7. Serve the BBQ shrimp skewers hot and enjoy! They pair perfectly with a side of grilled vegetables, a fresh salad, or even some buttery corn on the cob.

Tips and Variations:

  • If you prefer a spicier kick, increase the amount of cayenne pepper in the marinade. Adjust the spice level according to your taste preferences.

  • For an extra burst of flavor, squeeze some additional lemon juice over the cooked shrimp skewers before serving.

  • Feel free to experiment with different seasonings and herbs. Adding a pinch of smoked paprika or some chopped fresh basil can take these BBQ shrimp skewers to the next level.

  • If you’re not a fan of shrimp, you can easily substitute it with chicken, beef, or even tofu. Just adjust the cooking time accordingly.

  • To ensure your wooden skewers don’t burn on the grill, remember to soak them in water for at least 30 minutes before threading the shrimp.

So there you have it – an easy and flavorful recipe for BBQ shrimp skewers that will elevate your summer cooking. These skewers are versatile, packed with delicious flavors, and sure to be a hit at any gathering. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and indulge in the taste of summer with these mouthwatering BBQ shrimp skewers. Enjoy!
