Oozing Cauldron Cakes: Alice In Wonderland Halloween Recipe


Embark on a journey down the rabbit hole with this magically delicious Alice In Wonderland-inspired recipe for Oozing Cauldron Cakes. This dark and eerie dessert will leave you and your guests bewitched by its scrumptious flavor. Prepare for a spellbinding feast with these uniquely crafted, devilishly delightful treats perfect for your Halloween party.

Ingredients for Cauldron Cakes

  • 1 1/2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 3/4 cup White Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons Buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon Distilled White Vinegar
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Melted Butter
  • 2 teaspoons Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 cup Milk Chocolate Chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon Ground Ginger
  • Edible Black Glitter or Black Sugar Pearls
  • Bee-shaped and Leaf-shaped Cookie Cutters
  • Melted Chocolate, for glue
  • Candy Eyes
  • Black Food Coloring
  • Coconut Whipped Cream or Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Edible Flowers or Fresh Mint Leaves (optional)

Instructions

  1. Begin by preparing your “witches” cauldrons. For this, you’ll need two sizes of cookie cutters - one for the base and another for the sides. The smaller one should be slightly wider at the bottom and taper off at the top, while the larger one should be wider at the top and gradually slim down towards the bottom. Trace and cut out these shapes from a double layer of thick cardstock or a sturdy craft paper. Then, use the melted chocolate to glue the base shape on top of the larger one, leaving a small opening for the cake to spill out from. Set them aside to dry.

  2. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and prepare your baking sheets with parchment paper.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt until well combined.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the buttermilk, vinegar, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Pour this wet mixture into the dry ingredients, stirring until it’s just mixed. Now, fold in the chocolate chips, cinnamon, and ginger.

  5. Spoon the batter into a piping bag fitted with a large round tip. Pipe blobs of batter into the base of each cauldron, then place the assembled cauldron shapes directly onto the lined baking sheet. Be sure not to overfill the cauldrons, or they’ll be difficult to handle when they’re done.

  6. Bake the cauldrons for 18-22 minutes or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean. Allow them to cool entirely on the baking sheet before handling.

  7. For the cauldron ooze, melt half of the chocolate chips in a double boiler or microwave at 20-second intervals, stirring between each interval until smooth. Add a few drops of black food coloring and let it cool down to a thicker consistency.

  8. Once the cakes have cooled, you can prepare the cauldron ’edge’ and the chocolate ooze. Fill the small opening at the top of each cauldron with some of the coconut whipped cream or ice cream, then top that off with some of the melted chocolate ooze.

  9. For added fun, you can add the candy eyes and edible glitter to your cauldron cakes. Use the melted chocolate to “glue” the candy eyes to the front of each cauldron, then sprinkle some of the edible glitter or black sugar pearls around them. If you’d like a more natural appearance, consider adding some fresh mint leaves and edible flowers on top of your whipped cream or ice cream.

  10. Display your eerie yet enchanting cauldron cakes and marvel at the coherent balance of frightful charm and delightful flavors.

The Oozing Cauldron Cakes are a devilishly delightful treat that’ll surely have your guests howling in delight. Though it may take a little bit of magic to transform these mouthwatering creations, the result is undoubtedly worth the spellbinding effort. Serve with a glass of apple cider and enjoy the spooktacular experience of Alice-inspired cruelty-free delights. Now, it’s time to blow the candles out and celebrate another hauntingly fun Halloween with your friends and family, and let the enchanting feast begin!
