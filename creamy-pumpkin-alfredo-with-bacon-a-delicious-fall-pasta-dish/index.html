Creamy Pumpkin Alfredo with Bacon: A Delicious Fall Pasta Dish


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in comforting, hearty dishes that warm the soul. This creamy pumpkin alfredo with bacon is a delightful twist on the classic alfredo pasta, infusing the rich flavors of pumpkin and the savory goodness of bacon. Whether you’re hosting a cozy dinner party or simply craving a taste of fall, this dish is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover the magic of this delectable fall pasta dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fettuccine pasta
  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of pumpkin puree
  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Fettuccine: In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving 1 cup of the pasta water.

  2. Cook the Bacon: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until crisp. Once done, transfer the bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess grease. Set aside.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Stir in the pumpkin puree and cook for another 2 minutes, stirring constantly.

  4. Add Cream and Cheese: Pour in the heavy cream, stirring to combine with the pumpkin mixture. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Allow the sauce to simmer gently for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  5. Combine the Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet with the pumpkin alfredo sauce. Toss the pasta gently to coat it evenly with the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add a small amount of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Divide the creamy pumpkin alfredo pasta among serving plates. Sprinkle the crispy bacon over the top of each serving and garnish with fresh parsley for a burst of color and flavor.

Tips for Success:

  • Use canned or homemade pumpkin puree for the best flavor.
  • Don’t skimp on the nutmeg – it adds a warm, aromatic note to the dish.
  • Crumble the bacon finely for an even distribution of flavor throughout the pasta.

Why You’ll Love This Dish:

The creamy pumpkin alfredo sauce creates a luscious, velvety coating for the fettuccine, while the addition of bacon lends a delightful crunch and smoky flavor. The marriage of savory and sweet, combined with the warm spices of fall, makes this dish a true celebration of the season. The aroma alone will transport you to a cozy autumn evening, gathered around the table with loved ones.

In Conclusion

Creamy pumpkin alfredo with bacon is a delightful fall pasta dish that captures the essence of the season. Whether you’re a fan of traditional alfredo or simply love exploring new flavor combinations, this recipe is a must-try. It’s a wonderful way to usher in the fall season and savor the rich, comforting flavors it has to offer. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of fall, and treat yourself to a bowl of this delicious pasta – you won’t be disappointed. Happy cooking!
