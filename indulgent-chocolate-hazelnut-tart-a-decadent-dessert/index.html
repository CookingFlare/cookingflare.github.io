Indulgent Chocolate Hazelnut Tart: A Decadent Dessert


Prepare yourself for a truly indulgent treat with this exquisite Chocolate Hazelnut Tart. Rich, creamy, and oh-so-decadent, this dessert is sure to impress even the most discerning chocolate lover. With a buttery hazelnut crust and a luscious chocolate ganache filling, every bite of this tart is pure bliss.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups crushed hazelnut cookies
  • ½ cup roasted hazelnuts, finely chopped
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

For the filling:

  • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
  • 12 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
  • ½ cup Nutella
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the crushed hazelnut cookies, chopped roasted hazelnuts, and melted butter. Mix well until the mixture resembles wet sand.

  3. Press the crust mixture evenly into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom. Use the back of a spoon or a measuring cup to ensure an even layer.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  5. While the crust is cooling, prepare the filling. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it starts to simmer. Remove from the heat and add the chopped dark chocolate, Nutella, unsalted butter, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Stir until the chocolate is fully melted and the mixture is smooth and glossy.

  6. Pour the chocolate ganache filling into the cooled crust, spreading it evenly with a spatula. Tap the tart pan gently on the counter to remove any air bubbles.

  7. Place the tart in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is set.

  8. Once the tart is chilled and set, remove it from the refrigerator. You can serve it as is, or garnish with some whipped cream, chopped hazelnuts, or a dusting of cocoa powder for an extra touch of elegance.

  9. Slice and savor each heavenly bite of this indulgent Chocolate Hazelnut Tart. The combination of the buttery hazelnut crust and the smooth, velvety chocolate ganache is simply irresistible.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra layer of flavor, you can add a teaspoon of espresso powder to the chocolate ganache filling. It will enhance the richness of the chocolate and add a subtle hint of coffee.

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can experiment with different types of cookies for the crust. Try using chocolate cookies, graham crackers, or even shortbread for a unique twist.

  • To make the tart gluten-free, simply substitute the hazelnut cookies with gluten-free cookies of your choice.

  • This tart can be made in advance and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Just make sure to cover it tightly with plastic wrap to prevent any odors from permeating the dessert.

  • Serve each slice of tart with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra touch of indulgence.

Conclusion

Indulge your senses with this mouthwatering Chocolate Hazelnut Tart. From its crunchy hazelnut crust to its velvety chocolate ganache filling, this dessert is a true masterpiece. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a decadent treat, this tart is sure to leave a lasting impression. So, go ahead and satisfy your sweet tooth with a slice of pure bliss!
