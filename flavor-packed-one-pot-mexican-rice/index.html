Flavor-packed One Pot Mexican Rice


One pot meals are a lifesaver when it comes to cooking delicious and convenient meals. They save time on both preparation and cleanup, making them perfect for busy weeknights. If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this flavor-packed One Pot Mexican Rice recipe. It’s a hearty and satisfying dish that will transport your taste buds straight to the vibrant streets of Mexico.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, diced red bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper. Saute for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are softened.

  2. Add the long-grain white rice to the pot and stir well to coat it with the oil and vegetables. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, black beans, frozen corn kernels, chili powder, cumin powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Once it comes to a boil, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a lid. Let the rice simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and all the liquid is absorbed.

  5. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for about 5 minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the rice to become even more flavorful.

  6. Fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with freshly chopped cilantro. Serve the flavor-packed One Pot Mexican Rice with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrusy freshness.

Tips and Variations:

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables such as diced zucchini, sliced mushrooms, or chopped spinach.
  • For an extra kick of spiciness, leave the seeds in the jalapeno pepper or add a dash of hot sauce.
  • If you prefer a protein-packed meal, you can add cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the rice during the simmering process.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop with a splash of vegetable broth to keep it moist.

There you have it, a flavor-packed One Pot Mexican Rice recipe that will satisfy your cravings for Mexican flavors in a convenient and delicious way. This dish is perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or meal prepping for the week ahead. Give it a try and let your taste buds embark on a culinary journey to Mexico! Enjoy!
