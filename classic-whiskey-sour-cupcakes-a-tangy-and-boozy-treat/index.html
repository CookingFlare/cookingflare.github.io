Classic Whiskey Sour Cupcakes: A Tangy and Boozy Treat


If you’re a fan of the classic cocktail, the whiskey sour, and adore the sweet indulgence of cupcakes, then you’re in for a treat with these Classic Whiskey Sour Cupcakes. This delightful fusion of tangy citrus and smooth whiskey flavor will elevate your baking game and impress your friends and family at any gathering.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons whiskey
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • Zest of 1 lemon and 1 lime

For the Whiskey Sour Buttercream:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons whiskey
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon and 1 lime

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.

  2. Make the Cupcake Batter:

    • In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
    • In a separate large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
    • Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then add the whiskey, lemon juice, and lime juice.
    • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk, and mix until just combined.
    • Fold in the lemon and lime zest.

  3. Bake:

    • Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about 3/4 full.
    • Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    • Allow the cupcakes to cool completely before frosting.

  4. Prepare the Whiskey Sour Buttercream:

    • In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until smooth.
    • Gradually add the powdered sugar, whiskey, lemon juice, lime juice, and zest, and beat until light and fluffy.

  5. Frost the Cupcakes:

    • Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, generously frost each cupcake with the whiskey sour buttercream.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Garnish with a twist of lemon or lime zest for an extra touch of citrus zestiness.
    • Serve and savor these tangy, boozy treats with your favorite whiskey sour cocktail for a perfect pairing.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra boozy kick, brush the cooled cupcakes with a light whiskey syrup before frosting.
  • You can adjust the amount of whiskey in the buttercream to suit your taste preferences.
  • Get creative with the presentation by adding a candied lemon or lime slice as a decorative topping.

These Classic Whiskey Sour Cupcakes are a wonderful combination of zesty citrus and the warmth of whiskey, making them a unique and indulgent dessert option for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a delightful treat, these cupcakes are sure to impress and satisfy.

So, gather your baking essentials, pour yourself a glass of whiskey, and let’s embark on a baking adventure to create these delightful, tangy, and boozy cupcakes that will leave everyone asking for more!
