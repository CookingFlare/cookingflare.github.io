Chinese Tomato and Egg Stir-Fry: A Simple and Satisfying Dish


Looking for a quick and delicious dish to satisfy your cravings? Look no further than Chinese Tomato and Egg Stir-Fry. This simple yet flavorful dish is a staple in Chinese cuisine, loved for its vibrant colors and delicate balance of flavors. Best of all, it’s incredibly easy to make, even for beginner cooks. So, let’s dive right into this tasty recipe!

Ingredients

To whip up this delightful dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 3 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the tomatoes. Cut them into wedges, discarding the tough core. Set aside.

  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl, season with salt and white pepper, and beat until well combined.

  3. Heat a wok or a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl it around to coat the surface evenly.

  4. Once the oil is hot, pour in the beaten eggs. Allow them to cook undisturbed for a few seconds until the edges start to set.

  5. Using a spatula, gently scramble the eggs, breaking them into small curds. Cook until they are just set but still slightly runny. Transfer the eggs to a plate and set aside.

  6. In the same wok, heat another tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the tomato wedges and sprinkle them with sugar. Stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until the tomatoes start to soften and release their juices.

  7. Return the scrambled eggs to the wok with the tomatoes. Gently stir everything together, allowing the flavors to meld for another minute or so. Be careful not to overcook the tomatoes; you want them to retain some of their shape and texture.

  8. Sprinkle the stir-fry with scallions and give it a final toss. Remove from heat.

  9. Transfer the tomato and egg stir-fry to a serving dish and garnish with a few more slices of fresh scallions.

  10. Serve hot with steamed rice or as a side dish alongside other Chinese favorites.

Tips and Variations

  • For added umami flavor, you can include a splash of soy sauce or a dash of oyster sauce during the stir-frying process.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, feel free to add a small amount of minced garlic or chili peppers when cooking the tomatoes.
  • To make this dish more substantial, you can incorporate other ingredients like diced bell peppers, mushrooms, or even thinly sliced beef or chicken.

The Perfect Blend of Sweet and Tangy

Chinese Tomato and Egg Stir-Fry is a delightful combination of sweet and tangy flavors. The juicy tomatoes provide a burst of freshness, while the fluffy scrambled eggs add a creamy texture. The subtle seasonings of salt, sugar, and white pepper tie everything together harmoniously, creating a dish that is satisfying and comforting.

This dish not only makes for a satisfying meal on its own but also pairs beautifully with other Chinese dishes. Its simplicity allows it to complement a variety of flavors, making it a versatile addition to any meal.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for something quick and delicious, give this Chinese Tomato and Egg Stir-Fry a try. You won’t be disappointed!
