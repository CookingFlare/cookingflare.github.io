Burrito Boned: Flavorful Fare for Weightless Wonders!


Ever found yourself craving a scrumptious, heartwarming meal that’s bursting with flavor but won’t weigh you down? Fear not, fellow food enthusiasts! Today, we’re diving into the magical realm of burrito-boned cuisine, a delightfully light and inventive approach to traditional burrito fare. Embrace your culinary adventures and prepare to indulge in these tantalizing treats!

To help you navigate this enticing menu, we’ve constructed a versatile, slimmed-down burrito-boned recipe that caters to all palates.

Ingredient Lineup

Our burrito-boned creation requires a variety of fresh, healthy, and flavorful components:

  • 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces (600 g)
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage (400 g)
  • 2 cups shredded carrots (400 g)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained (15 oz.)
  • ¾ cup quinoa
  • 1 avocado, cubed
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tsp. chili powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Homemade taco seasoning
  • Toppings: cheese, salsa, fresh cilantro, hot sauce

Assembly Guide

  1. Prep the ingredients: Diced the vegetables and shred the cabbage and carrots.
  2. Cook the quinoa: Combine the quinoa with 2 cups of water in a saucepan, bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Marinate the chicken: In a resealable plastic bag, combine the chicken pieces with lime juice, garlic powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Seal and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to an hour.
  4. Cook the chicken: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange the marinated chicken pieces on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 25 minutes.
  5. Season the chicken: While the chicken is roasting, prepare homemade taco seasoning by mixing 1 part cumin, 2 parts paprika, and 3 parts chili powder. Set aside.
  6. Master the method: Combine the cooked and seasoned chicken, cooked quinoa, shredded cabbage, shredded carrots, diced red bell pepper, and black beans in a large bowl. Gently toss to evenly distribute ingredients. You can adjust the measurements to your personal preference.
  7. Finish the masterpiece: Spoon a generous amount of the spicy mix onto a plate, scatter avocado cubes and toppings of your choice, and enjoy!

A Wholesome Feast

This burrito-boned delight is perfect for a satisfying and nutritious meal. The flavorsome combination of chicken, vegetables, and quinoa offers a light and adaptable alternative to conventional burritos. Serve this dish to family and friends, or pack it up for a quick, on-the-go meal. Don’t be shy about experimenting with your favorite additions or substitutions!

Embrace the flavors of traditional Mexican cuisine while honoring a healthier, lean lifestyle with our burrito-boned recipe. Indulge in a nourishing dish that’s anything but ordinary — and don’t forget to share your culinary creations in the comments below!
