Vegan Cheesecake Bars Recipe with a Shortbread Crust and a Creamy Filling


If you’re a vegan and love indulging in desserts, then this recipe for Vegan Cheesecake Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Creamy Filling is a must-try! These delicious bars are a perfect blend of sweet and tangy flavors, and they’re incredibly easy to make. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the Shortbread Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup of vegan butter, melted
  • ¼ cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the Creamy Filling:

  • 2 cups of vegan cream cheese
  • ⅔ cup of granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • 1 ½ teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • ½ cup of unsweetened plant-based milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the flour, melted vegan butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract for the shortbread crust. Mix until well combined and crumbly.

  3. Press the crust mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Use the back of a spoon or your fingers to press it down firmly.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 15 minutes or until lightly golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  5. Meanwhile, prepare the creamy filling by combining the vegan cream cheese, granulated sugar, cornstarch, vanilla extract, and plant-based milk in a mixing bowl. Use an electric mixer or whisk to beat the mixture until smooth and creamy.

  6. Pour the creamy filling over the cooled shortbread crust, spreading it out evenly.

  7. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are slightly golden.

  8. Once baked, remove from the oven and let the cheesecake bars cool completely in the baking dish.

  9. Once cooled, transfer the baking dish to the refrigerator and chill for at least 2 hours, or until the cheesecake bars are firm.

  10. When ready to serve, remove the cheesecake bars from the baking dish by lifting the edges of the parchment paper. Cut into squares or bars of your desired size.

  11. Optional: Garnish with fresh berries, a dusting of powdered sugar, or a drizzle of melted dairy-free chocolate for an extra touch of sweetness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a gluten-free version, you can replace the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend of your choice.

  • Feel free to experiment with different flavors for the creamy filling. You can add lemon zest, cocoa powder, or even fruit purees for a unique twist.

  • If you prefer a sweeter crust, you can add a tablespoon or two of maple syrup or agave nectar to the shortbread crust mixture.

  • These cheesecake bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. They also freeze well, so you can make a batch in advance and enjoy them whenever you crave a sweet treat.

  • Remember to let the cheesecake bars sit at room temperature for a few minutes before serving to allow them to soften slightly.

Now that you have the recipe for these delectable Vegan Cheesecake Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Creamy Filling, go ahead and give them a try! They’re sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your friends and family. Enjoy!
