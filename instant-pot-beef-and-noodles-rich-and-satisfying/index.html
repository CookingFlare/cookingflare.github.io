Instant Pot Beef and Noodles: Rich and Satisfying


If you’re looking for a hearty and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to make, Instant Pot beef and noodles is the answer. This comforting dish is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want something delicious without spending hours in the kitchen. The Instant Pot works its magic to tenderize the beef and infuse the noodles with rich, savory flavors, making this recipe a surefire hit with the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces wide egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Saute” function. Once hot, add the olive oil and brown the beef in batches. Remove the beef and set aside.

  2. Add the onion and garlic to the pot and sauté until fragrant. Add the carrots, celery, and mushrooms, and cook for a few minutes until slightly softened.

  3. Pour in the beef broth and water, then add the soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.

  4. Return the browned beef to the pot, secure the lid, and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” for 20 minutes.

  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully performing a quick pressure release.

  6. Open the lid and stir in the egg noodles. Select the “Saute” function again and cook the noodles until tender, stirring occasionally.

  7. Once the noodles are cooked, switch off the Instant Pot and let the beef and noodles sit for a few minutes to allow the sauce to thicken slightly.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot beef and noodles in deep bowls, garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a richer flavor, you can add a splash of red wine to the beef and broth mixture before pressure cooking.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, mix 2 tablespoons of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water and stir it into the beef and broth mixture before adding the noodles.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables to your liking. Peas, bell peppers, or even spinach would be great additions to this dish.

Why Instant Pot Beef and Noodles?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, and this beef and noodles recipe is a shining example of its versatility. The pressure cooking function tenderizes the beef in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional methods, while also infusing the noodles with the savory flavors of the broth and herbs.

This dish is not only convenient but also incredibly comforting. The tender beef, flavorful broth, and perfectly cooked noodles come together to create a rich and satisfying meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re feeding a crowd or simply craving a cozy dinner at home, Instant Pot beef and noodles is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe repertoire.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and fuss-free meal, give this Instant Pot beef and noodles recipe a try. It’s a delicious way to enjoy the rich flavors of a slow-cooked dish in a fraction of the time, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.
