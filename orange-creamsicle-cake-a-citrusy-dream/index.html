Orange Creamsicle Cake: A Citrusy Dream


Are you ready to embark on a delightful journey of citrusy flavors? Look no further than the Orange Creamsicle Cake! This delicious dessert is a dream come true for all the citrus lovers out there. With its vibrant orange hue and creamy texture, this cake is sure to impress both your taste buds and your guests.

Ingredients

To create this citrusy masterpiece, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • Zest of 2 oranges

For the Creamsicle Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Zest of 1 orange

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the orange juice and milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined. Fold in the orange zest.

  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula.

  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean. Remove from the oven and allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  7. While the cakes are cooling, prepare the Creamsicle Frosting. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, orange juice, heavy cream, vanilla extract, and orange zest. Beat until smooth and fluffy.

  8. Once the cakes have cooled, place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread a generous amount of Creamsicle Frosting evenly over the top. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the entire cake with the remaining Creamsicle Frosting.

  9. For an extra touch of citrusy goodness, garnish the cake with additional orange zest.

Serving and Storing

Now that your Orange Creamsicle Cake is ready, it’s time to enjoy it! This cake is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a birthday celebration or a casual get-together. Serve it chilled for a refreshing treat that will leave everyone craving for more.

To store the cake, cover it tightly with plastic wrap or place it in an airtight container. It can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, ensuring it maintains its freshness and flavor.

Conclusion

The Orange Creamsicle Cake is a true citrusy dream that will transport you to a world of vibrant flavors. With its moist and tender texture, paired with the creamy and tangy Creamsicle Frosting, this cake is a showstopper that will impress both your family and friends. So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients and let your taste buds embark on a citrusy adventure!
