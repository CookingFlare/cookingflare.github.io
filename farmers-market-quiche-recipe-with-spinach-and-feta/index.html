Farmers' Market Quiche Recipe with Spinach and Feta


If you’re looking for a delicious way to incorporate fresh produce from your local farmers’ market into a delightful dish, this Farmers’ Market Quiche with Spinach and Feta is the perfect choice. Packed with flavor, nutrients, and a hint of elegance, this quiche is bound to impress your family and friends. Let’s dive into the recipe and explore how you can create this culinary masterpiece in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup of red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C) to ensure it’s ready for baking later on.

  2. Prepare the Pie Crust: Take your pre-made pie crust and place it in a pie dish, ensuring it covers the bottom and sides evenly. Use a fork to prick the crust’s bottom to prevent it from puffing up while baking.

  3. Sauté the Vegetables: In a skillet over medium heat, add a drizzle of olive oil. Sauté the chopped red onion and minced garlic until they become translucent and fragrant. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until it wilts. Remove from heat and set aside.

  4. Whisk the Eggs and Milk: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

  5. Assemble the Quiche: Spread the sautéed vegetables evenly over the pie crust. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top. Pour the egg and milk mixture over the vegetables and cheese, ensuring it spreads evenly.

  6. Add the Final Touches: Place the halved cherry tomatoes on top of the quiche mixture for a pop of color and freshness.

  7. Bake to Perfection: Carefully transfer the quiche to the preheated oven. Bake for about 35-40 minutes or until the quiche is set and the top is lightly golden.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the quiche from the oven and let it cool slightly. Slice into wedges and serve warm. This quiche pairs well with a fresh green salad or a side of roasted vegetables.

Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply want to elevate your weekday meals, this Farmers’ Market Quiche with Spinach and Feta is a versatile and flavorful dish that celebrates the bounty of the season. Experiment with different vegetables and cheeses to customize the recipe to your liking. Remember, the key to a good quiche lies in using quality ingredients and infusing it with love and creativity in every step of the cooking process.

Enjoy the process of creating this delectable quiche, and savor the rewarding experience of indulging in a homemade dish that captures the essence of the farmers’ market freshness. Happy cooking!
