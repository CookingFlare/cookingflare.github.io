10 Refreshing Brunch Cocktails to Kickstart Your Weekend


Brunch is a beloved weekend ritual, a perfect blend of breakfast and lunch that often comes with the added delight of cocktails. When you think of brunch, images of sunny mornings, laughter, and delicious food often come to mind. But what elevates a brunch experience to the next level? That’s right, the perfect cocktail! Whether you’re hosting friends or enjoying a quiet morning at home, these 10 refreshing brunch cocktails will kickstart your weekend with a burst of flavor and creativity.

1. Classic Mimosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 part fresh orange juice
  • 2 parts chilled sparkling wine
  • Orange slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Pour the fresh orange juice into a champagne flute, filling it one-third of the way.
  2. Slowly top with chilled sparkling wine.
  3. Garnish with an orange slice on the rim.

The Classic Mimosa is a staple for a reason. Its light, bubbly nature combined with the fresh taste of orange juice makes it the perfect complement to any brunch spread.

2. Strawberry Basil Bellini

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 bottle chilled Prosecco
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, puree the strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice until smooth.
  2. Strain the mixture to remove seeds.
  3. Fill a flute one-third with the strawberry puree and top with Prosecco.
  4. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

This delightful twist on the classic Bellini combines the sweetness of strawberries with the aromatic notes of basil, creating a refreshing cocktail that’s perfect for summer brunches.

3. Cucumber Mint Cooler

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce elderflower liqueur
  • 1 ounce fresh lime juice
  • Club soda to top

Instructions:

  1. Muddle cucumber slices and mint leaves in a shaker.
  2. Add gin, elderflower liqueur, and lime juice.
  3. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Top with club soda and garnish with a cucumber slice.

The Cucumber Mint Cooler is refreshingly light, making it an ideal choice for a warm day. The combination of cucumber and mint is incredibly rejuvenating, ensuring you feel vibrant and energized.

4. Spicy Bloody Mary

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ ounces vodka
  • 3 ounces tomato juice
  • ½ ounce lemon juice
  • 2 dashes Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 dashes hot sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Celery stick and lemon wedge for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shake gently and pour into a tall glass filled with ice.
  3. Garnish with a celery stick and lemon wedge.

For those who enjoy a kick in their cocktails, the Spicy Bloody Mary is legendary. Its savory profile pairs beautifully with hearty brunch dishes, making it a favorite among many.

5. Peach Thyme Spritzer

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe peach, pureed
  • 1 ounce thyme-infused simple syrup
  • 1 ounce gin
  • 2 ounces sparkling water
  • Fresh thyme for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a glass, combine the peach puree, thyme-infused syrup, and gin.
  2. Stir well and fill the glass with ice.
  3. Top with sparkling water and garnish with fresh thyme.

The Peach Thyme Spritzer is a sweet and herbal combination that brings a unique flavor profile to your brunch table. The infusion of thyme adds depth that is both refreshing and aromatic.

6. Tropical Pina Colada

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces white rum
  • 1 ounce coconut cream
  • 1 ounce pineapple juice
  • Fresh pineapple wedge for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine rum, coconut cream, and pineapple juice with ice.
  2. Blend until smooth and pour into a chilled glass.
  3. Garnish with a fresh pineapple wedge.

Indulge in a mini-vacation with the Tropical Pina Colada. Its creamy texture and tropical flavor are sure to transport you to a beach paradise, making it a fun addition to any brunch gathering.

7. Lavender Lemonade Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 1 ounce lavender syrup
  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 2 ounces fresh lemonade
  • Club soda to top
  • Lavender sprigs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice and add lavender syrup, vodka, and lemonade.
  2. Stir gently and top with club soda.
  3. Garnish with lavender sprigs.

The Lavender Lemonade Fizz is a fragrant and refreshing cocktail that’s perfect for spring and summer brunches. The delicate floral notes, combined with zesty lemonade, create a wonderfully balanced drink.

8. Grapefruit Rosemary Collins

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ ounces gin
  • 2 ounces fresh grapefruit juice
  • 1 ounce rosemary simple syrup
  • Club soda to top
  • Rosemary sprig for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine gin, grapefruit juice, and rosemary syrup with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a glass filled with ice.
  3. Top with club soda and garnish with a rosemary sprig.

The Grapefruit Rosemary Collins is both tart and refreshing, with the rosemary adding an unexpected twist. This cocktail is an excellent way to wake up your taste buds!

9. Blackberry Lime Mojito

Ingredients:

  • 10 fresh blackberries
  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1 ½ ounces white rum
  • Club soda to top
  • Lime wedge and mint for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Muddle blackberries and mint leaves in a glass.
  2. Add lime juice and rum, then fill the glass with ice.
  3. Top with club soda and garnish with a lime wedge and mint.

The Blackberry Lime Mojito is a fruity take on the classic mojito. Its vibrant colors and flavors make it a visually stunning and delicious addition to your brunch menu.

10. Watermelon Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups seedless watermelon, pureed
  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce fresh lime juice
  • Club soda to top
  • Watermelon slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a glass, combine watermelon puree, vodka, and lime juice.
  2. Stir and fill the glass with ice.
  3. Top with club soda and garnish with a watermelon slice.

The Watermelon Fizz is a refreshing and hydrating cocktail that captures the essence of summer. Its sweet and tangy flavor profile pairs perfectly with lighter brunch offerings, such as salads and fresh fruits.

Tips for Hosting a Brunch Cocktail Party

  1. Preparation is Key: Prepare as much as you can in advance. Pre-make your cocktail mixes and store them in the fridge until guests arrive.

  2. Set Up a Cocktail Bar: Create a designated area for cocktails with all necessary ingredients, tools, and glassware. This allows guests to mix their own drinks and adds an interactive element.

  3. Garnishes Matter: Fresh herbs, sliced fruits, and edible flowers make for beautiful garnishes that enhance both the flavor and presentation of your cocktails.

  4. Offer Non-Alcoholic Options: Not everyone drinks alcohol, so provide delicious mocktail options to ensure all guests feel included.

  5. Pair with Delicious Foods: Coordinate your cocktails with your brunch menu. Light cocktails pair well with fresh salads, while richer drinks complement heartier dishes.

Final Thoughts

Brunch cocktails are an excellent way to elevate your weekend gatherings. With these 10 refreshing recipes, you can impress your friends and family while creating a festive atmosphere. Whether you prefer fruity, herbal, or classic flavors, there’s a cocktail on this list for everyone. So gather your ingredients, shake or stir up some drinks, and enjoy a delightful brunch experience. Cheers to a weekend filled with laughter, good food, and unforgettable cocktails!
