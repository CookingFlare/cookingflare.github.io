Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken: A Classic Comfort Combo


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful meal that will satisfy your cravings, look no further than Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken. This classic dish combines the richness of creamy tomato sauce with the fragrant aroma of fresh basil, creating a delightful flavor profile that is sure to impress your taste buds. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a hearty meal, this recipe is a must-try. Let’s dive into the details of creating this delectable dish!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 6-7 minutes per side. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and use a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. This will infuse the sauce with incredible flavor.

  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, and crushed red pepper flakes (if using). Let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream, stirring gently to combine with the tomato sauce.

  7. Return the cooked chicken breasts to the skillet, nestling them into the creamy tomato basil sauce. Let the chicken simmer in the sauce for an additional 10-15 minutes, allowing it to soak up all the flavors.

  8. Sprinkle the freshly grated Parmesan cheese over the chicken and sauce, letting it melt and create a luscious, creamy coating.

  9. Once the chicken is fully cooked and the sauce has thickened, remove the skillet from the heat.

  10. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a burst of aromatic freshness.

Serving Suggestions

Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken pairs beautifully with a variety of side dishes. Consider serving it over a bed of al dente pasta, such as fettuccine or penne, for a classic Italian twist. Alternatively, you can opt for a lighter option by accompanying it with a fresh green salad or steamed vegetables. Don’t forget a slice of crusty bread to soak up every last bit of the creamy tomato basil sauce.

Conclusion

Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken is a timeless combination that never fails to delight. With its rich and velvety sauce, complemented by the vibrant essence of basil, this dish is a true comfort food classic. Whether you’re preparing it for a cozy dinner at home or entertaining guests, this recipe is a surefire crowd-pleaser. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the heartwarming flavors of this culinary masterpiece. Bon appétit!
