Irresistible Pesto Parmesan Rolls: A Perfect Accompaniment


If you’re looking for a versatile and mouthwatering recipe that will elevate any meal, then look no further than these irresistible Pesto Parmesan Rolls. Whether you’re serving them as an appetizer, a side dish, or even as a standalone snack, these rolls are guaranteed to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup store-bought pesto
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well to ensure that the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the warm water and olive oil. Use a wooden spoon or your hands to mix the ingredients together until a sticky dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough back into the mixing bowl and cover it with a damp cloth. Allow it to rise in a warm place for approximately 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  6. Punch down the risen dough to release any air bubbles. Transfer it back onto the floured surface and roll it out into a rectangular shape, approximately 1/4 inch thick.

  7. Spread the pesto evenly over the surface of the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pesto.

  8. Starting from one of the longer sides, tightly roll the dough into a log shape. Use a sharp knife to cut the log into 1-inch slices.

  9. Place the slices onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving a small gap between each one to allow for expansion.

  10. Bake the rolls in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they turn golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

  11. Remove the rolls from the oven and allow them to cool slightly before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Pesto Parmesan Rolls are incredibly versatile and can be served in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Appetizer: Serve the rolls with a side of marinara sauce or a creamy herb dip for a delightful appetizer that will leave your guests wanting more.

  • Side Dish: Pair the rolls with a hearty soup or salad to create a satisfying and flavorful side dish that complements any meal.

  • Snack: These rolls are so delicious on their own that they can be enjoyed as a savory snack anytime. Pack them in lunchboxes or take them on picnics for a tasty treat on the go.

  • Brunch: Serve the rolls alongside scrambled eggs, bacon, and fresh fruit for a decadent brunch that will impress your friends and family.

Conclusion

These Irresistible Pesto Parmesan Rolls are a perfect accompaniment to any meal. With their soft and fluffy texture, flavorful pesto filling, and the cheesy goodness of Parmesan, these rolls are guaranteed to be a hit. So, why not try this recipe today and elevate your next meal with a touch of irresistible deliciousness? Enjoy!
