Instant Pot Chicken Tacos: A Crowd-Pleasing Favorite


Tacos are a beloved dish that never fails to impress. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply enjoying a weeknight dinner, Instant Pot Chicken Tacos are a crowd-pleasing favorite that will leave everyone asking for more. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have tender and flavorful chicken tacos ready in no time. So, let’s dive into this delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • Optional toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, sour cream, and lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and jalapeno pepper. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are softened.

  2. Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, and dried oregano. Add the seasoned chicken thighs to the Instant Pot and sauté for another 2-3 minutes until they are lightly browned.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth and give everything a good stir to combine. Place the lid on the Instant Pot and set the pressure valve to the sealing position.

  4. Select the “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer to 15 minutes. The Instant Pot will take a few minutes to come to pressure before the cooking time begins.

  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick release by turning the pressure valve to the venting position. Be cautious of the hot steam.

  6. Remove the chicken thighs from the Instant Pot and using two forks, shred the meat. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and give it a good mix, ensuring that the flavors are evenly distributed.

  7. Warm the corn tortillas either in a dry skillet or in the microwave. Fill each tortilla with a generous amount of the Instant Pot chicken filling.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Tacos with your favorite toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, sour cream, and a squeeze of lime juice.

Why Choose Instant Pot Chicken Tacos?

  • Time-Saving: The Instant Pot cuts down the cooking time significantly, allowing you to have a delicious meal on the table in no time.
  • Tender and Flavorful: The pressure cooking process infuses the chicken with all the spices, resulting in tender and flavorful meat that will satisfy your taste buds.
  • Crowd-Pleasing: Tacos are always a hit at gatherings, and this recipe is no exception. Your guests will love the combination of tender chicken and a variety of tasty toppings.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a crowd-pleasing favorite, give this Instant Pot Chicken Tacos recipe a try. It’s easy, delicious, and sure to become a staple in your meal rotation. Enjoy!
