Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Stew Recipe: A Flavorful and Healthy Option


Are you looking for a hearty, nutritious, and delicious meal that’s easy to prepare? Look no further than this Instant Pot lentil and vegetable stew recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and an array of vitamins and minerals, this stew is a perfect option for a wholesome dinner or a satisfying lunch. The best part? It can be made in a fraction of the time it takes to cook traditional stews, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.

Ingredients

To start, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by setting the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add a splash of olive oil, and once it’s hot, add the diced onion, garlic, carrots, celery, bell pepper, and zucchini. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  2. Next, add the rinsed lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, paprika, turmeric, salt, and pepper to the pot. Give everything a good stir to combine the flavors.

  3. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, making sure the valve is set to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 12 minutes.

  4. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then carefully switch the valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  5. Open the lid, and give the stew a final stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  6. Ladle the stew into bowls and garnish with freshly chopped parsley.

Serving Suggestions

This Instant Pot lentil and vegetable stew pairs beautifully with a slice of crusty bread or a side of fluffy rice. If you’re feeling adventurous, you can sprinkle some crumbled feta cheese on top for an extra burst of flavor. The stew also freezes well, so consider making a double batch and storing the leftovers for a quick and convenient meal option on busy days.

Health Benefits

Lentils are a nutritional powerhouse, rich in protein, fiber, and various essential nutrients. They are known for their heart-healthy properties and can aid in stabilizing blood sugar levels. The addition of colorful vegetables in this stew provides a spectrum of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making it a fantastic choice for anyone looking to boost their overall health.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot lentil and vegetable stew is not only a convenient and time-saving meal option but also a delightful way to incorporate more plant-based goodness into your diet. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your meal rotation. So, grab your Instant Pot and let’s get cooking!

With its rich blend of flavors and array of health benefits, this Instant Pot lentil and vegetable stew is a true winner for anyone seeking a wholesome and satisfying meal. Whether you’re a fan of hearty stews or looking for a simple way to add more plant-based nutrition to your diet, this recipe is a must-try. Happy cooking!
