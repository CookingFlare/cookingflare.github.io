Kitchen Sports: How to Make Indoor Picnics Enjoyable with Homemade Pizza and Flatbreads


Indoor picnics are the perfect way to mix up mealtime and bring a touch of novelty to your home cooking experiences. It’s a fantastic way to cheer up a rainy Saturday and escape the monotony of daily life. And what’s a picnic without some delicious, freshly made pizza and flatbreads? Today, we’ll transform our kitchens into a fun sports arena, where the prize is a scrumptious feast fit for the winners.

So, put on your apron and get ready for some kitchen sports!

The Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need for your homemade endeavors:

  • All-purpose flour (or, for gluten-free, opt for almond or buckwheat flour)
  • Warm water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Toppings of your choice (tomatoes, olives, spinach, sautéed mushrooms, etc.)
  • Pizza sauce or tomato puree
  • Grated cheese (mozzarella, parmesan, gorgonzola - whatever floats your boat!)

A Pitta Party: Homemade Flatbreads

These versatile creations can be used not only as pizza bases but also for wraps, sandwiches, and dips. Easy to make, they can be personalized in different shapes and with assorted toppings.

  1. In a large bowl, combine 2 cups (250g) of flour and 1 tsp of salt. Gradually mix in 1 cup of warm water, along with 1 tbsp of yeast, which has been dissolved in another 1/4 cup of warm water. Stir until a sticky dough forms; it should be wet but not too runny.
  2. Cover the bowl with a clean cloth and leave it in a warm place for about an hour, or until the dough has doubled in size.
  3. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  4. Once your dough has risen, divide it into golf ball-sized portions on a lightly floured surface. Knead each portion briefly and flatten it into a patty about 1.5 cm (1/2 inch) thick.
  5. Place your flattened patties on the prepared baking sheet. Using the bottom of a pot or any circular object, press down on each patty to create a flatbread. Repeat until you’ve shaped all of them.
  6. Brush the flatbreads with olive oil and bake them for 10-12 minutes, or until they are golden and puffed. Once done, remove them from the oven and prepare for toppings.

Pizza Pi: From Dough to Delight

Making your very own pizza is a fun activity that could turn into a family tradition. And who knew you could be a pizza chef on a lazy Sunday morning?

  1. Shape your risen dough into a smooth ball and place it in a lightly oiled bowl, covered, for about 20 minutes. This will give the dough time to relax, making it easier to work with.
  2. Meanwhile, preheat your oven to its highest temperature (usually between 240-260°C or 465-500°F). Make sure you’ve got a baking stone or steel ready.
  3. Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface until it reaches your desired thickness.
  4. Transfer the dough to a piece of parchment paper. Spread your pizza sauce evenly, leaving a small border around the edge. Add your preferred toppings, finishing with a generous sprinkle of grated cheese.
  5. Carefully slide your pizza (with the parchment paper) onto the preheated baking stone or steel. Bake it for 8-12 minutes, depending on the thickness of your base and the preheated temperature, until the cheese is bubbling, and the crust is crunchy.
  6. Once ready, use a large spatula to transfer your creation onto a pizza peel or cutting board. Allow it to cool slightly before diving in!

A Competitive Edge

An indoor picnic would not be complete without competing for the proud title of ‘Best Pizza Maker’. Create an event out of it by having a ‘hand toss’ contest, or a race to see who can top and bake their flatbread fastest. Remember, fun is the key ingredient in these kitchen games!

Also, this activity could be a fantastic way to bond with your kids – not only are they entertained, but they’ll also learn some cooking skills they can use for life. And the best part is that no matter who wins, everyone’s a winner when it comes to a meal that’s both tasty and fun to make.

So, put on your chefs’ hats, have fun, and enjoy your indoor picnic feast!
