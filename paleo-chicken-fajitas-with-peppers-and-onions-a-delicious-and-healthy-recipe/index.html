Paleo Chicken Fajitas with Peppers and Onions: A Delicious and Healthy Recipe


As the seasons change and the weather warms up, there’s nothing quite like firing up the grill and cooking up a hearty and flavorful meal that’s perfect for a family dinner or a gathering with friends. One of the most popular and delicious options for a summer meal is fajitas, and today we’re going to show you how to make a mouth-watering Paleo Chicken Fajitas with Peppers and Onions recipe that’s not only packed with flavor but also incredibly healthy.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need. For this recipe, you’ll need:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into thin strips
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4-6 Paleo tortillas (made from almond flour or coconut flour)
  • Optional toppings: avocado, salsa, cilantro, lime wedges

Instructions

Now that we have our ingredients, let’s get started with the recipe. Here’s how to make Paleo Chicken Fajitas with Peppers and Onions:

  1. Prepare the chicken: In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken strips and toss to coat. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours in the refrigerator.
  2. Grill the chicken: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and grill for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Transfer the chicken to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm.
  3. Sauté the peppers and onions: In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced onions and bell peppers and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5-7 minutes or until they’re tender and lightly charred.
  4. Add the garlic and chicken: Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Add the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir to combine with the peppers and onions.
  5. Warm the tortillas: Wrap the Paleo tortillas in a damp paper towel and microwave for 20-30 seconds to warm them up.
  6. Assemble the fajitas: Slice the chicken and peppers into thin strips. Place a portion of the chicken and peppers onto a warmed tortilla and add your desired toppings. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

Here are a few tips and variations to keep in mind:

  • Use different types of peppers: While bell peppers are classic in fajitas, you can also use jalapenos or Anaheim peppers for a spicy kick.
  • Add some heat: If you like a little spice, you can add some diced jalapenos or serrano peppers to the skillet with the onions and peppers.
  • Make it a meal prep: Cook the chicken and peppers ahead of time and store them in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat and assemble the fajitas when you’re ready.
  • Get creative with toppings: While avocado and salsa are delicious toppings, you can also try adding some diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, or crumbled queso fresco.

Conclusion

There you have it - a mouth-watering Paleo Chicken Fajitas with Peppers and Onions recipe that’s perfect for a summer meal. With its bold flavors and healthy ingredients, this recipe is sure to become a family favorite. So why not give it a try and see why fajitas are a staple of many a summer gathering?
