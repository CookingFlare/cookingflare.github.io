Mexican-Inspired Pizza: Adding a Spicy Kick


If you’re a fan of both Mexican cuisine and pizza, then you’re in for a treat. In this recipe, we’re going to take the classic pizza and infuse it with the bold and vibrant flavors of Mexican cooking. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with a fusion of two beloved culinary traditions. This Mexican-inspired pizza is not only delicious but also incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize it to suit your spice tolerance and flavor preferences.

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet of active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Spicy Tomato Sauce

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings

  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup sliced red onions
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Jalapeños (fresh or pickled) for extra heat
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

1. Prepare the Pizza Dough

In a large bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Allow the mixture to sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy. Add the olive oil, flour, and salt. Mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

2. Make the Spicy Tomato Sauce

In a small saucepan, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly. Adjust the seasoning to your taste preference.

3. Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to heat up.

4. Assemble the Pizza

Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired pizza shape and thickness. Transfer the dough to a piece of parchment paper. Spread the spicy tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle a handful of mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Layer on the cooked chicken, bell peppers, red onions, corn, and black beans. Top the pizza with the remaining mozzarella cheese and a few slices of jalapeños for an extra kick.

5. Bake the Pizza

Carefully slide the parchment paper with the assembled pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.

6. Garnish and Serve

Once out of the oven, sprinkle the freshly chopped cilantro over the hot, bubbling pizza. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve the Mexican-inspired pizza hot and enjoy the spicy, flavorful goodness.

Tips for Customization

  • Spice Level: If you can handle the heat, consider adding more jalapeños or even some sliced serrano peppers. For a milder option, you can omit the jalapeños altogether.
  • Protein Options: Feel free to swap the chicken for seasoned ground beef, shredded pork, or even grilled shrimp for a seafood twist.
  • Vegetarian Variation: To make this pizza vegetarian-friendly, simply omit the chicken and load up on extra veggies like zucchini, mushrooms, or diced tomatoes.

Final Thoughts

Mexican-inspired pizza offers a delightful blend of zesty and savory flavors, and the best part is that you can easily adjust the spice level and toppings to suit your personal preferences. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner party or simply craving a unique twist on pizza night, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your favorite Mexican-inspired ingredients, and get ready to savor every spicy, cheesy bite of this delectable fusion dish. Cheers to culinary creativity and the joy of sharing flavorful meals with loved ones!
