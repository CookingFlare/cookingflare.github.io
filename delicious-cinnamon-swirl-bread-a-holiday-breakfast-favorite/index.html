Delicious Cinnamon Swirl Bread: A Holiday Breakfast Favorite


There’s something magical about the aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through the house, especially during the holiday season. One of my all-time favorite holiday breakfast treats is a warm slice of delicious cinnamon swirl bread. The combination of sweet cinnamon and soft, pillowy bread is simply irresistible. In this post, I’ll share with you my cherished recipe for making this delectable treat at home.

Ingredients

For the Bread:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour

For the Cinnamon Swirl:

  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

Step 1: Activate the Yeast

In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until the yeast is activated and becomes frothy.

Step 2: Make the Dough

To the yeast mixture, add the sugar, softened butter, salt, eggs, and 2 cups of the flour. Mix until well combined. Gradually add the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until the dough begins to pull away from the sides of the bowl.

Step 3: Knead the Dough

Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Step 4: Prepare the Cinnamon Swirl

In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon for the swirl. Set aside.

Step 5: Shape the Bread

Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into a rectangle on a lightly floured surface. Brush the melted butter over the entire surface of the dough, then sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly over the butter.

Step 6: Roll and Bake

Starting from one of the longer sides, tightly roll the dough into a log. Place the rolled dough into a greased 9x5-inch loaf pan. Cover the pan with a kitchen towel and allow the dough to rise for an additional 30 minutes.

Step 7: Bake to Perfection

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Once the dough has risen, bake the cinnamon swirl bread for 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestion

Slice the cooled cinnamon swirl bread and serve it with a generous slather of softened butter or a dollop of whipped cream cheese. This bread also pairs beautifully with a hot cup of coffee or a steaming mug of spiced chai tea.

There you have it – a delightful recipe for making your very own cinnamon swirl bread at home. Whether you enjoy it as a holiday breakfast treat or a cozy afternoon snack, this bread is sure to become a household favorite. The sweet aroma and heavenly taste will have your loved ones gathering around the table in no time. Happy baking and happy holidays!
