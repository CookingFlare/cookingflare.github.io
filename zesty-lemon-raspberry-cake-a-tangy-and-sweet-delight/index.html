Zesty Lemon Raspberry Cake: A Tangy and Sweet Delight


If you’re looking for a delightful dessert that perfectly balances tangy and sweet flavors, then you’re in for a treat with this zesty lemon raspberry cake recipe. The combination of zesty lemon and juicy raspberries creates a burst of freshness in every bite, making this cake a perfect choice for any occasion, from casual gatherings to elegant dinner parties.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Raspberry Filling:

  • 2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the Lemon Glaze:

  • 1 1/2 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 3-4 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the softened butter and mix on low speed until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, lemon zest, and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until well combined and smooth.
  6. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and smooth the tops with a spatula.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 2: Making the Raspberry Filling

  1. In a saucepan, combine the raspberries, granulated sugar, water, and lemon juice.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the raspberries break down and the mixture thickens, about 10-15 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and let the raspberry filling cool to room temperature.

Step 3: Assembling the Cake

  1. Once the cakes have cooled, place one cake layer on a serving plate.
  2. Spread the raspberry filling evenly over the top of the cake layer.
  3. Carefully place the second cake layer on top of the raspberry filling.

Step 4: Making the Lemon Glaze

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the confectioners’ sugar and fresh lemon juice until smooth.
  2. Drizzle the lemon glaze over the top of the assembled cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.

Step 5: Serving the Cake

  1. Slice the zesty lemon raspberry cake and serve it with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  2. Enjoy the tangy and sweet flavors of this delightful dessert with family and friends.

Conclusion

This zesty lemon raspberry cake is a delightful combination of tangy lemon, sweet raspberries, and moist, tender cake layers. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a delicious treat, this cake is sure to impress your taste buds. With its bright flavors and beautiful presentation, this cake is a perfect addition to your baking repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in this tangy and sweet delight!
