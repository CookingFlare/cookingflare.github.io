Delicious and Easy-to-Make Soup Recipes for Beginners


If you’re new to cooking or looking for some simple yet delicious recipes to add to your repertoire, soup is an excellent place to start. Not only is soup versatile and comforting, but it’s also a great way to experiment with different flavors and ingredients. Whether you’re craving a hearty vegetable soup, a creamy chicken soup, or a spicy lentil soup, there’s a recipe out there for you. In this blog post, we’ll explore some easy-to-make soup recipes that are perfect for beginners. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup egg noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Add the chicken breasts, carrots, celery, onion, and garlic to the pot.
  3. Simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it using two forks.
  5. Return the shredded chicken to the pot and add the egg noodles.
  6. Cook for an additional 10 minutes or until the noodles are tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, then garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

2. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes and chicken broth to the pot.
  4. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and chopped basil.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.

3. Hearty Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and drain.
  2. In a large pot, sauté the diced onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until softened.
  3. Add the lentils, vegetable broth, cumin, and paprika to the pot.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Conclusion

There you have it – three delightful and straightforward soup recipes that are perfect for beginners. Whether you’re craving the comforting warmth of chicken noodle soup, the creamy richness of tomato basil soup, or the wholesome heartiness of lentil and vegetable soup, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings and impress your taste buds. So, don’t be afraid to dive into the world of soup-making and unleash your culinary creativity. Happy cooking!
