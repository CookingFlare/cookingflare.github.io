Irresistible Cajun Seafood Pasta: A Spicy Delight


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and crave a dish that will tantalize your taste buds, look no further than this Irresistible Cajun Seafood Pasta. Packed with a medley of fresh seafood, smothered in a rich and spicy sauce, this dish is guaranteed to leave you wanting more. So, roll up your sleeves, put on your chef’s hat, and let’s dive into this culinary adventure!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of linguine pasta
  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound of scallops
  • 1 pound of crab meat
  • 1 tablespoon of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the linguine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the shrimp, scallops, crab meat, Cajun seasoning, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper. Mix well to ensure the seafood is evenly coated with the spices.

  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onion, garlic, and bell peppers. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the seasoned seafood to the other side. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until the shrimp turns pink and the scallops are opaque.

  5. Remove the seafood from the skillet and set aside.

  6. In the same skillet, pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer. Let it reduce for a couple of minutes.

  7. Add the heavy cream to the skillet and stir well. Allow the sauce to simmer for another 5 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  8. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

  9. Return the cooked seafood to the skillet and toss gently to coat it with the creamy Cajun sauce.

  10. Add the cooked linguine pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until the pasta is well coated with the sauce.

  11. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley.

  12. Serve hot and enjoy the irresistible flavors of this Cajun Seafood Pasta!

Final Thoughts

The combination of tender shrimp, succulent scallops, and delicate crab meat, all enveloped in a creamy and spicy Cajun sauce, makes this pasta dish a true delight for seafood lovers. The Cajun seasoning adds an extra kick that elevates this dish to a whole new level of flavor.

Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving something indulgent, this Irresistible Cajun Seafood Pasta is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the boldness of Cajun cuisine, and treat yourself to a spicy delight that will leave you craving more. Bon appétit!
