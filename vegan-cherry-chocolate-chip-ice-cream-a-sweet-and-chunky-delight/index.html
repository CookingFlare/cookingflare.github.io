Vegan Cherry Chocolate Chip Ice Cream: A Sweet and Chunky Delight


Who doesn’t love a scoop of creamy, dreamy ice cream on a hot summer day? And when it’s vegan, cherry-infused, and studded with decadent chocolate chips, it’s a treat that’s hard to resist. In this recipe, I’ll show you how to make a delightful Vegan Cherry Chocolate Chip Ice Cream that’s not only dairy-free but also bursting with fruity flavor and rich chocolate goodness. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to indulge in this luscious frozen delight!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 cup of pitted cherries, fresh or frozen
  • 1/2 cup of maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of vegan dark chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Cherry Puree:

    • If using fresh cherries, pit them using a cherry pitter. If using frozen cherries, let them thaw slightly.
    • In a blender, combine the pitted cherries and maple syrup. Blend until smooth to create a luscious cherry puree.

  2. Mix the Base:

    • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, cherry puree, and vanilla extract until well combined. Make sure the mixture is smooth and creamy.

  3. Chill the Mixture:

    • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and place it in the refrigerator to chill for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight. This step is crucial for achieving a creamy ice cream texture.

  4. Churn the Ice Cream:

    • Once the mixture is thoroughly chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. As the mixture begins to freeze, it will transform into a velvety ice cream base.

  5. Add the Chocolate Chips:

    • When the ice cream is almost done churning, add the vegan dark chocolate chips into the ice cream maker. Let it churn for a few more minutes to evenly distribute the chocolate throughout the ice cream.

  6. Transfer and Freeze:

    • Transfer the churned ice cream into a freezer-safe container, smoothing the top with a spatula. Cover the container with a lid or plastic wrap and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until the ice cream is firm.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • When ready to serve, let the ice cream sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly. Scoop the Vegan Cherry Chocolate Chip Ice Cream into bowls or cones, and savor each spoonful of fruity, chocolatey bliss.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra crunch, add a handful of chopped almonds or walnuts to the ice cream along with the chocolate chips.
  • Experiment with different fruits such as raspberries, blackberries, or even peaches to create unique fruity variations of this vegan ice cream.
  • Drizzle a homemade chocolate or cherry sauce over the ice cream for an added burst of flavor and visual appeal.

There you have it – a delectable recipe for Vegan Cherry Chocolate Chip Ice Cream that’s sure to satisfy your sweet tooth while keeping things dairy-free and vegan-friendly. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply treating yourself to a delightful dessert, this homemade ice cream is a delightful way to indulge in the flavors of the season. So, gather your ingredients, whip up a batch of this sweet and chunky delight, and get ready to experience the sheer joy of a cool, creamy treat that’s as wholesome as it is delicious!
