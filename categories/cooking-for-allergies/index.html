Allergy-Friendly Trail Mix Cookies: A Crunchy and Nutritious Snack

Trail mix cookies are a delightful treat that combines the goodness of a classic cookie with the crunch and nutrition of a trail mix. What makes these cookies truly special is that they are allergy-friendly, making them suitable for people with various dietary restrictions. Whether you have food allergies or are looking for a healthier snack option, these allergy-friendly trail mix cookies are sure to satisfy your cravings. Ingredients 1 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted 1/2 cup coconut sugar 1/4 cup maple syrup 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup gluten-free rolled oats 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut 1/2 cup allergy-friendly chocolate chips 1/2 cup dried cranberries 1/2 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds, cashews, or walnuts) Instructions Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a medium bowl, whisk together the gluten-free all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside. In a large bowl, combine the melted coconut oil, coconut sugar, maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Fold in the gluten-free rolled oats, unsweetened shredded coconut, allergy-friendly chocolate chips, dried cranberries, and chopped nuts. Mix until all the ingredients are evenly distributed throughout the dough. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Gently flatten each cookie with the back of a spoon or the palm of your hand to ensure even baking. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges of the cookies turn golden brown. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. These allergy-friendly trail mix cookies are not only delicious but also packed with wholesome ingredients. The gluten-free all-purpose flour provides a light and airy texture, while the coconut oil adds a subtle hint of tropical flavor. The combination of gluten-free rolled oats, unsweetened shredded coconut, and chopped nuts gives these cookies a satisfying crunch. The allergy-friendly chocolate chips and dried cranberries add a touch of sweetness and chewiness, making every bite a delightful experience. ...
