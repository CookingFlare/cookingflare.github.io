Tantalizing Tomato Basil Penne with Mozzarella Balls


If you’re looking for a dish that’s bursting with flavor and simple to prepare, then look no further than this tantalizing tomato basil penne with mozzarella balls recipe. This dish combines the freshness of ripe tomatoes and fragrant basil with the creamy goodness of mozzarella, all served over perfectly cooked penne. It’s a delightful combination that will leave your taste buds singing.

Ingredients

For the Tomato Basil Sauce:

  • 8-10 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Mozzarella Balls:

  • 8 oz of fresh mozzarella, cut into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Other:

  • 1 lb of penne pasta
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tomato Basil Sauce:

    • In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
    • Add the chopped tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Stir well and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes until the tomatoes break down and the sauce thickens.
    • Stir in the chopped basil and let the sauce simmer for an additional 5 minutes. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

  2. Make the Mozzarella Balls:

    • Take each piece of mozzarella and form it into small, bite-sized balls. Roll the mozzarella balls in the flour, then dip them into the beaten eggs, and finally coat them in breadcrumbs, ensuring they are evenly coated.
    • In a deep pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the coated mozzarella balls in batches and fry until they are golden brown and crispy. Remove them from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

  3. Cook the Penne:

    • In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the penne according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

  4. Assemble the Dish:

    • Toss the cooked penne with the tomato basil sauce until well coated.
    • Serve the tomato basil penne in individual bowls, topping each serving with a few mozzarella balls.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated parmesan cheese, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This tantalizing tomato basil penne with mozzarella balls pairs beautifully with a fresh green salad and a glass of crisp white wine. It’s a perfect dish for a casual dinner with friends or a cozy night in with your loved ones.

Conclusion

This recipe for tomato basil penne with mozzarella balls is a celebration of simple, fresh ingredients that come together to create a dish that’s both comforting and sophisticated. The sweetness of the tomatoes, the brightness of the basil, and the creaminess of the mozzarella balls make this pasta dish a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to create a meal that will bring joy to everyone around your table. Cheers to good food and great company!
