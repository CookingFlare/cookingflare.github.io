Summer BBQ: Recipes with Seasonal Grilled Veggies


Summer is here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to fire up the grill and enjoy the warm weather. A summer BBQ is a classic way to spend time with family and friends, and what’s a BBQ without some delicious grilled veggies to accompany the meats? In this post, we’ll explore some mouth-watering recipes that showcase the best of seasonal grilled veggies.

Roasted Red Pepper Hummus

Start your summer BBQ off right with a refreshing dip that’s perfect for hot days. Roasted red peppers add a sweet and smoky flavor to this classic hummus recipe. Simply blend together 1 cup of cooked chickpeas, 1/4 cup of tahini, 1/4 cup of lemon juice, 2 cloves of garlic, and 1/2 cup of roasted red peppers. Serve with pita chips or vegetables for a healthy snack.

Grilled Asparagus with Lemon Aioli

Asparagus is in season during the summer months, and it’s a great addition to any BBQ. Simply brush the asparagus spears with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and grill until tender. Meanwhile, mix together 1/2 cup of mayonnaise, 2 tablespoons of lemon juice, and 1 minced clove of garlic for a tangy and creamy aioli. Serve the grilled asparagus with a dollop of aioli for a flavorful side dish.

Smoky Grilled Eggplant

Eggplant is another summer staple that’s perfect for grilling. Slice the eggplant into thick rounds, brush with olive oil, and season with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs. Grill until tender and slightly charred, then top with a tangy tomato sauce and melted mozzarella cheese for a vegetarian main dish.

Summer Squash and Zucchini Fritters

Summer squash and zucchini are abundant during the summer months, and they make a great addition to any BBQ. Simply grate 1 cup of summer squash and 1 cup of zucchini, then mix with 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 1 egg, and 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley. Form into patties and fry in a skillet until golden brown. Serve with a side of marinara sauce for dipping.

Grilled Corn with Herbs

Grilled corn is a summer classic, and it’s easy to elevate with some fresh herbs. Simply brush the corn with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and grill until slightly charred. Meanwhile, mix together 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley, 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh chives, and 1 tablespoon of grated lemon zest. Sprinkle the herb mixture over the grilled corn for a flavorful side dish.

Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burgers

Portobello mushrooms are a great vegetarian alternative to traditional burgers, and they’re perfect for a summer BBQ. Simply brush the mushroom caps with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and grill until tender. Meanwhile, mix together 1/4 cup of mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon of ketchup, and 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley. Spread the sauce on the mushroom caps, then top with your favorite toppings for a delicious vegetarian burger.

Summer BBQ Tips

Before we wrap up, here are some summer BBQ tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure to preheat your grill to the right temperature to prevent food from sticking.
  • Use a grill brush to clean the grates and prevent food from sticking.
  • Don’t overcrowd the grill, as this can prevent food from cooking evenly.
  • Keep a bowl of ice water nearby to cool off on hot days.
  • Don’t forget to have fun and enjoy the company of your friends and family!

Conclusion

Summer BBQs are the perfect way to spend time with loved ones, and with these recipes, you’ll be sure to impress your guests with some delicious and seasonal grilled veggies. Whether you’re a vegetarian or just looking for some healthy alternatives to traditional BBQ fare, these recipes are sure to become new favorites. So fire up the grill, grab some friends, and get ready for a summer BBQ to remember!
