Mouthwatering Instant Pot Shrimp Scampi Recipe


If you’re a seafood lover, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering Instant Pot Shrimp Scampi recipe. Shrimp scampi is a classic Italian-American dish known for its rich, garlicky, and buttery sauce that perfectly complements the tender shrimp. With the help of the Instant Pot, you can achieve the same luxurious flavors and textures in a fraction of the time. This recipe is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion when you want to impress your guests with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken or seafood broth
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces of linguine or spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly chopped parsley
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Season the shrimp with salt and black pepper, and set aside.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: Select the “Saute” function on the Instant Pot and melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and saute for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the chicken or seafood broth, white wine, lemon juice, lemon zest, and red pepper flakes. Stir well, scraping any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.

  4. Cook the Shrimp: Add the seasoned shrimp to the pot, making sure they are evenly distributed. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” on high for 1 minute. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release.

  5. Prepare the Pasta: While the shrimp is cooking, cook the linguine or spaghetti according to the package instructions in a separate pot.

  6. Combine the Dish: Once the pressure is released, carefully open the lid of the Instant Pot. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the cooked shrimp to a plate, leaving the sauce in the pot.

  7. Thicken the Sauce: Select the “Saute” function again and let the sauce simmer for a few minutes to thicken slightly.

  8. Serve: Serve the shrimp over the cooked pasta, spoon the sauce on top, and garnish with freshly chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese. Serve with lemon wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips for Success

  • Use fresh, high-quality shrimp for the best flavor and texture.
  • Adjust the amount of red pepper flakes according to your spice preference.
  • Feel free to add a splash of heavy cream to the sauce for a richer finish.
  • Serve the shrimp scampi with a side of crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot Shrimp Scampi recipe is a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that will elevate your dining experience. The convenience of using the Instant Pot ensures that you can enjoy a restaurant-quality meal in the comfort of your own home without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re cooking for your family or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to savor every bite of this irresistible shrimp scampi!
