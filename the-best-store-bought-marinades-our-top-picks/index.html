The Best Store-Bought Marinades: Our Top Picks


When it comes to elevating your meals with minimal effort, store-bought marinades are a game changer. They offer a quick and convenient way to infuse your proteins and vegetables with flavor, making them a staple in many kitchens. Whether you’re grilling, roasting, or sautéing, the right marinade can transform a simple dish into a culinary delight. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best store-bought marinades available, highlighting their unique flavors, uses, and what makes them stand out.

Why Use Store-Bought Marinades?

Store-bought marinades save time and effort, allowing you to enjoy delicious meals without the hassle of making your own from scratch. They are perfect for busy weeknights or when you want to impress guests without spending hours in the kitchen. Here are a few reasons to consider using store-bought marinades:

  1. Convenience: No need to gather ingredients or measure out spices. Just pour, mix, and marinate.
  2. Variety: With countless options available, you can easily switch up flavors and cuisines.
  3. Consistency: Store-bought marinades provide a reliable flavor profile every time.
  4. Accessibility: Most grocery stores carry a wide range of marinades, making them easy to find.

Our Top Picks for Store-Bought Marinades

1. Stubb’s Original Bar-B-Q Sauce

Stubb’s Original Bar-B-Q Sauce is a classic choice for those who love a smoky, tangy flavor. This marinade is perfect for ribs, chicken, and even vegetables. Made with a blend of tomatoes, vinegar, and spices, it offers a rich taste that enhances the natural flavors of your ingredients.

Best Uses: Ideal for grilling and slow-cooking. Marinate your meats for at least 30 minutes before cooking for the best results.

2. Annie’s Balsamic Vinaigrette

Annie’s Balsamic Vinaigrette is a versatile marinade that works wonders on salads, roasted vegetables, and grilled chicken. This organic option combines balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and a hint of sweetness, making it a healthier choice without sacrificing flavor.

Best Uses: Use it as a marinade for chicken or tofu, or drizzle it over roasted vegetables for a burst of flavor.

3. Kraft Italian Dressing

Kraft Italian Dressing is a staple in many households, and for good reason. This zesty marinade is packed with herbs and spices, making it perfect for marinating chicken, fish, or even vegetables. Its tangy flavor profile adds a refreshing twist to any dish.

Best Uses: Marinate chicken breasts for grilling or use it as a salad dressing for a quick and easy meal.

4. Lawry’s Teriyaki Marinade

For those who crave Asian-inspired flavors, Lawry’s Teriyaki Marinade is a must-try. This sweet and savory marinade features soy sauce, ginger, and garlic, making it perfect for stir-fries, grilled meats, and even as a dipping sauce.

Best Uses: Marinate beef or chicken for stir-fry dishes, or use it to glaze grilled salmon for a delicious dinner.

5. Frank’s RedHot Buffalo Wing Sauce

If you love a spicy kick, Frank’s RedHot Buffalo Wing Sauce is the way to go. This fiery marinade is perfect for chicken wings, but it can also be used to spice up burgers, tacos, and even roasted vegetables.

Best Uses: Marinate chicken wings for a classic buffalo flavor or drizzle it over roasted cauliflower for a spicy twist.

6. Tessemae’s Organic Lemon Garlic Dressing

Tessemae’s Organic Lemon Garlic Dressing is a fresh and zesty option that adds brightness to any dish. Made with organic ingredients, this marinade is perfect for those looking for a healthier alternative. The combination of lemon and garlic makes it ideal for seafood, chicken, and salads.

Best Uses: Use it to marinate shrimp for grilling or drizzle it over a fresh salad for added flavor.

7. Sweet Baby Ray’s Barbecue Sauce

Sweet Baby Ray’s Barbecue Sauce is a fan favorite for its sweet and tangy flavor. This marinade is perfect for ribs, chicken, and even grilled vegetables. Its thick consistency allows it to cling to your ingredients, ensuring maximum flavor.

Best Uses: Use it as a marinade for grilling or as a finishing sauce for baked chicken.

8. McCormick Grill Mates Montreal Steak Marinade

For steak lovers, McCormick Grill Mates Montreal Steak Marinade is a must-have. This robust marinade features a blend of garlic, onion, and black pepper, making it perfect for enhancing the flavor of beef.

Best Uses: Marinate steaks for at least an hour before grilling for a deliciously seasoned meal.
