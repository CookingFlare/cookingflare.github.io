Pulse the mixture until it forms a coarse paste. While the food processor is running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture becomes smooth and well combined. Step 3: Assemble the Muhammara Crostini Once the crostini are toasted, spread a generous amount of the prepared Muhammara on each slice. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.
