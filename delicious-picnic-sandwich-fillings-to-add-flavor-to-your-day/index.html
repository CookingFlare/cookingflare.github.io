Delicious Picnic Sandwich Fillings to Add Flavor to Your Day


A picnic is one of those timeless joys that never fail to bring a sense of relaxation, excitement, and connection with nature. Whether you’re enjoying a sunny day in the park, by the beach, or on a countryside hike, a picnic provides the perfect opportunity to indulge in good food while soaking up the outdoors. And no picnic would be complete without a variety of delicious, satisfying sandwiches.

But let’s face it, making sandwiches doesn’t have to be boring. Gone are the days of simply slapping together slices of bread, a bit of deli meat, and calling it a day. There are countless ways to elevate your picnic sandwiches to an entirely new level, ensuring that each bite bursts with flavor and variety.

In this blog post, we’ll explore a range of creative and tasty sandwich fillings that can transform your ordinary picnic lunch into an unforgettable feast. From vegetarian delights to hearty meat-based options, these fillings will keep you energized and satisfied throughout your outdoor adventure.

Classic but Upgraded: The Perfect Turkey and Cranberry

Let’s start with a classic combination that has stood the test of time – turkey and cranberry. However, we’re taking this picnic sandwich filling to new heights. By adding some fresh herbs, a slice of brie cheese, and a bit of spinach, you can turn this into a gourmet delight.

Ingredients:

  • Sliced turkey breast
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Cranberry sauce (preferably homemade)
  • Brie cheese slices
  • Whole grain or sourdough bread

Directions:

  1. Start with a hearty slice of bread, whether you choose whole grain or sourdough for a tangy touch.
  2. Layer on a generous portion of sliced turkey breast.
  3. Add a thin layer of brie cheese and melt slightly by placing the sandwich in a warm pan for a few minutes if you prefer a melted finish.
  4. Spread cranberry sauce over the turkey, and top it with fresh spinach leaves for a bit of crunch.
  5. Close the sandwich and enjoy the perfect balance of savory and sweet!

Mediterranean Bliss: Hummus and Veggies

For those seeking a vegetarian option that’s bursting with fresh flavors, a Mediterranean-inspired hummus and veggie sandwich is a fantastic choice. The creaminess of the hummus pairs perfectly with the crunchy vegetables, and it’s both refreshing and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • Hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Sliced red bell pepper
  • Sliced red onion
  • Feta cheese crumbles
  • Kalamata olives
  • Whole wheat or pita bread

Directions:

  1. Start with a base of hummus spread generously on both slices of bread. The creamy texture will keep everything together.
  2. Layer on the cucumber, red bell pepper, and red onion slices.
  3. Sprinkle feta cheese crumbles for that tangy, salty bite.
  4. Add a few Kalamata olives for that extra Mediterranean touch.
  5. Close the sandwich and serve, or cut into halves for easy sharing.

Classic Chicken Salad with a Twist

Chicken salad sandwiches have long been a staple of picnic lunches. But let’s give it a flavorful twist! By adding grapes, walnuts, and a bit of curry powder, you’ll elevate this classic into something truly special.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked, shredded chicken breast
  • Grapes (halved)
  • Walnuts (chopped)
  • Mayonnaise or Greek yogurt (or a combination of both)
  • Celery (chopped)
  • Curry powder
  • Fresh herbs (such as parsley or dill)
  • Croissant or soft roll

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mayonnaise (or Greek yogurt), and curry powder for a subtle, flavorful base.
  2. Add the chopped celery for crunch and the halved grapes for a pop of sweetness.
  3. Chop the walnuts and mix them into the salad for a nutty texture.
  4. Toss everything together, adding fresh herbs like parsley or dill for extra freshness.
  5. Spread the chicken salad generously on a croissant or soft roll. The flakiness of the croissant adds a rich contrast to the creamy salad.

The Ultimate BLT: Bacon, Lettuce, Tomato, and Avocado

A BLT is always a fan favorite, but let’s make it even more irresistible by adding creamy avocado. This simple addition enhances the flavor profile and adds a luscious texture to the sandwich. You can never go wrong with crispy bacon paired with creamy avocado!

Ingredients:

  • Crispy bacon strips
  • Lettuce (iceberg or romaine for crunch)
  • Sliced tomato
  • Sliced avocado
  • Mayonnaise
  • Toasted white or whole wheat bread

Directions:

  1. Start by crisping the bacon in a skillet until it’s golden brown and crunchy.
  2. Toast your bread until it’s golden and slightly crisped on the edges.
  3. Spread mayonnaise on both slices of the toasted bread. Add avocado slices on one side for creamy richness.
  4. Layer the crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, and sliced tomato.
  5. Close the sandwich, slice, and enjoy the delightful crunch and creamy bite.

A Veggie Delight: Roasted Vegetable and Goat Cheese

If you’re looking for a filling that’s both nutritious and incredibly flavorful, this roasted vegetable and goat cheese sandwich is a must-try. Roasting vegetables brings out their natural sweetness, and when paired with the tanginess of goat cheese, it’s nothing short of magical.

Ingredients:

  • Zucchini (sliced)
  • Eggplant (sliced)
  • Bell peppers (sliced)
  • Red onion (sliced)
  • Goat cheese (crumbled)
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Ciabatta or baguette

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven and roast the zucchini, eggplant, bell peppers, and red onion with olive oil until tender and slightly caramelized.
  2. Once the veggies are done, let them cool slightly.
  3. Slice the ciabatta or baguette into individual portions and spread goat cheese on both sides.
  4. Layer the roasted vegetables on one slice of bread, then sprinkle fresh basil leaves for an herbal kick.
  5. Close the sandwich and savor the warmth of the roasted veggies and the creamy goat cheese.

A Taste of the South: Pulled Pork with Coleslaw

Southern-style pulled pork is an iconic sandwich filling that’s both tender and full of flavor. When paired with tangy coleslaw, it creates a balance of savory and crunchy textures that’s perfect for a picnic.

Ingredients:

  • Pulled pork (slow-cooked or pre-cooked)
  • Coleslaw (store-bought or homemade)
  • BBQ sauce (your favorite variety)
  • Soft sandwich buns

Directions:

  1. If you’re using pre-cooked pulled pork, warm it up with some BBQ sauce for extra flavor.
  2. Prepare or grab a fresh batch of coleslaw. A mix of cabbage, carrots, and a tangy dressing works wonderfully.
  3. Spoon the pulled pork onto a soft sandwich bun, then top it with a generous amount of coleslaw.
  4. Close the sandwich and enjoy a delightful combination of smoky, tender meat and crunchy, tangy slaw.

Fresh and Light: Cucumber and Cream Cheese

For a light, refreshing sandwich filling, a cucumber and cream cheese sandwich is both simple and satisfying. This vegetarian option is perfect for a hot day when you want something cool and easy to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh cucumber (thinly sliced)
  • Cream cheese (spreadable or whipped)
  • Fresh dill (optional)
  • Rye or whole grain bread

Directions:

  1. Spread a layer of cream cheese on both slices of bread.
  2. Arrange the thin cucumber slices on one side of the bread.
  3. Sprinkle fresh dill over the cucumber for an aromatic touch.
  4. Close the sandwich and enjoy this light and refreshing bite.

Hearty and Flavorful: Roast Beef and Cheddar

For a robust and hearty sandwich filling, roast beef paired with sharp cheddar cheese is always a winner. The richness of the beef and the bold flavor of the cheddar cheese will make you feel like you’re indulging in a comforting, satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • Thinly sliced roast beef
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Horseradish sauce (optional)
  • Arugula or spinach
  • Toasted rye or French baguette

Directions:

  1. Toast your bread to a golden crisp, then spread horseradish sauce on one side (optional for an extra kick).
  2. Layer on the thinly sliced roast beef, followed by sharp cheddar cheese.
  3. Add a few leaves of arugula or spinach for a touch of green and freshness.
  4. Close the sandwich and enjoy the hearty flavors and satisfying textures.

Sweet and Savory: Bacon, Apple, and Brie

For a sandwich that combines both sweet and savory flavors, the combination of bacon, apple, and brie is a true winner. The crispness of the apple pairs wonderfully with the richness of brie and the salty crunch of bacon.

Ingredients:

  • Bacon strips (crispy)
  • Thinly sliced apple (preferably a sweet variety like Fuji or Honeycrisp)
  • Brie cheese (sliced)
  • Whole grain or French bread

Directions:

  1. Cook the bacon strips until crispy and golden.
  2. Slice the apple thinly to avoid overpowering the sandwich with too much sweetness.
  3. Spread a layer of brie cheese on your bread slices, then layer on the crispy bacon and apple slices.
  4. Close the sandwich and enjoy the perfect balance of sweet, savory, and creamy.

Conclusion

These picnic sandwich fillings are the perfect way to elevate your outdoor meals with a burst of flavor. Whether you’re in the mood for something light and refreshing or hearty and indulgent, there’s a filling here to suit every taste and dietary preference. So, the next time you’re planning a picnic, try out one of these delicious sandwich ideas and watch as your picnic meal becomes the talk of the day. With the right combination of ingredients and a little creativity, your sandwiches will be the highlight of any outdoor adventure!
