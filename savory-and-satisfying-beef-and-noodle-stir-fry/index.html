Savory and Satisfying: Beef and Noodle Stir-Fry


If there’s one dish that never fails to impress, it’s a delicious beef and noodle stir-fry. This savory and satisfying meal is a go-to for busy weeknights or when you’re craving something comforting yet flavorful. The combination of tender beef, crisp vegetables, and slurp-worthy noodles, all coated in a rich, savory sauce, makes this dish a winner every time. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create a mouthwatering beef and noodle stir-fry that will have your taste buds singing.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1 pound (450g) beef steak, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 8 oz (225g) noodles of your choice (such as udon, rice noodles, or egg noodles)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

For the Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 teaspoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup beef broth

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced beef with soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, sesame oil, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Toss to coat the beef evenly and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for maximum flavor.

  2. Prepare the Noodles: Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  3. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, brown sugar, and beef broth. Set aside.

  4. Stir-Fry the Beef: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the marinated beef in a single layer and let it cook without stirring for 1 minute to sear. Then, stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until the beef is browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  5. Stir-Fry the Vegetables: In the same skillet, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the sliced onion, red bell pepper, broccoli, and sugar snap peas. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  6. Combine and Serve: Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the vegetables. Add the cooked noodles and the prepared sauce. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.

  7. Garnish and Enjoy: Once everything is heated through, garnish the beef and noodle stir-fry with sesame seeds and sliced green onions. Serve hot and enjoy the savory and satisfying flavors!

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of chili oil to the stir-fry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences. Snow peas, carrots, or mushrooms would also work wonderfully in this dish.
  • To make this dish gluten-free, use tamari in place of soy sauce and ensure all other ingredients are gluten-free.

In Conclusion

Beef and noodle stir-fry is a versatile and satisfying dish that can be easily customized to suit your taste preferences. Whether you’re a fan of tender beef, slurp-worthy noodles, or vibrant, crisp vegetables, this recipe has something for everyone. So, the next time you’re craving a savory and satisfying meal, consider whipping up this delicious beef and noodle stir-fry. It’s a surefire way to elevate your home cooking game and delight your taste buds with every bite!
