Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese Recipe


Are you ready to take your mac and cheese game to the next level? If you’re a fan of bold flavors and comforting dishes, this Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese is the ultimate recipe for you. Combining the creamy, cheesy goodness of classic mac and cheese with the fiery kick of buffalo chicken, this dish is perfect for gatherings, game days, or cozy weeknight dinners. Let’s dive into this mouthwatering recipe!

Why Use a Slow Cooker?

Using a slow cooker for this buffalo chicken mac and cheese not only makes the cooking process easier but also enhances the flavors as everything simmers together for hours. The slow cooking method allows the chicken to become tender and soak up the spicy buffalo sauce, while the pasta cooks to perfection in a rich, cheesy sauce. Plus, you can set it and forget it, making meal prep a breeze!

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make this spicy slow cooker buffalo chicken mac and cheese, gather the following ingredients:

For the Buffalo Chicken:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts: The star of the dish, tender and juicy.
  • 1 cup buffalo wing sauce: This will add that signature spicy kick.
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder: For an aromatic flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder: To enhance the overall taste.
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper: A little heat to complement the buffalo sauce.

For the Mac and Cheese:

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni: The classic choice for mac and cheese.
  • 4 cups chicken broth: To cook the pasta and add flavor.
  • 3 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese: For that gooey, cheesy texture.
  • 1 cup cream cheese: To make the sauce creamy and rich.
  • 1 cup milk: Helps to achieve the perfect creamy consistency.
  • 1 teaspoon paprika: For a hint of smokiness.
  • Salt to taste: To balance the flavors.

Optional Toppings:

  • Chopped green onions: For a fresh crunch.
  • Crumbled blue cheese: To add a tangy flavor that pairs well with buffalo sauce.
  • Extra buffalo sauce: For those who want an extra kick.

Step-by-Step Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Place the Chicken in the Slow Cooker: Start by adding the boneless, skinless chicken breasts to the bottom of your slow cooker.

  2. Add the Buffalo Sauce: Pour the buffalo wing sauce over the chicken. Make sure all the pieces are well coated.

  3. Season: Sprinkle the garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper over the chicken.

  4. Cook: Cover the slow cooker and set it to low heat. Let it cook for about 6 to 8 hours, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.

Step 2: Shred the Chicken

  1. Shred the Chicken: Once the cooking time is up, carefully remove the chicken from the slow cooker. Use two forks to shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Return to the Slow Cooker: Place the shredded chicken back into the slow cooker, mixing it with the buffalo sauce to ensure it’s well coated.

Step 3: Cook the Macaroni

  1. Add the Chicken Broth: Pour the chicken broth into the slow cooker. This will be used to cook the macaroni.

  2. Stir in the Elbow Macaroni: Add the elbow macaroni to the slow cooker. Stir everything together to combine.

  3. Cover and Cook: Cover the slow cooker again and cook on high for about 30 minutes, or until the macaroni is tender. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

Step 4: Make It Cheesy

  1. Add the Cheeses: Once the macaroni is cooked, reduce the heat to low. Add the shredded sharp cheddar cheese, cream cheese, and milk to the slow cooker.

  2. Stir Until Smooth: Gently stir the mixture until all the cheeses melt and create a creamy sauce. If the mixture seems too thick, add a little more milk until you reach your desired consistency.

  3. Season to Taste: Taste your mac and cheese and add salt if needed. You might also want to stir in some paprika for extra flavor.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy!

  1. Garnish: Once everything is well mixed and creamy, it’s time to serve! Dish up the spicy buffalo chicken mac and cheese into bowls.

  2. Add Toppings: Sprinkle with chopped green onions, crumbled blue cheese, and a drizzle of extra buffalo sauce if desired.

  3. Dig In: Enjoy your hearty, flavorful meal that brings together the best of buffalo chicken and mac and cheese!

Tips for the Best Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese

  • Choose Your Sauce Wisely: The type of buffalo sauce you use can greatly affect the flavor. If you prefer a milder dish, opt for a mild buffalo sauce or mix it with a little ranch dressing.

  • Make It Your Own: Feel free to customize this recipe! Add in some veggies like diced bell peppers or spinach for added nutrition. You can also switch up the cheese with pepper jack for an extra spicy kick.

  • Storage: Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Reheat in the microwave, adding a splash of milk to maintain creaminess.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese is not just a meal; it’s an experience. The combination of spicy buffalo chicken and creamy, cheesy pasta creates a dish that is both comforting and exciting. It’s a crowd-pleaser that will keep everyone coming back for seconds (and thirds!). Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a cozy dinner, this recipe is sure to impress.

Final Thoughts

Cooking doesn’t have to be complicated to be delicious. With this Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese, you’ll have a flavorful, satisfying meal with minimal effort. The slow cooker does all the work, allowing you to enjoy the delightful aromas and the anticipation of a fantastic meal. So gather your ingredients, fire up your slow cooker, and get ready to indulge in this cheesy, spicy delight!

Now that you have the recipe, it’s time to get cooking! Enjoy your meal, and don’t forget to share this recipe with friends and family who would love to try this ultimate comfort food. Happy cooking!
