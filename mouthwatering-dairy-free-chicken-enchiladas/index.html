Mouthwatering Dairy-Free Chicken Enchiladas

3 min · 517 words

Enchiladas are a classic Mexican dish that everyone loves. They are usually made with a variety of cheeses, but today, we’re going to show you how to make mouthwatering dairy-free chicken enchiladas that are just as delicious and satisfying. Whether you’re lactose intolerant or simply looking for a healthier alternative, these enchiladas are sure to impress your taste buds. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • 1 cup dairy-free sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 10-12 corn tortillas

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and bell peppers. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the shredded chicken to the skillet and mix well with the vegetables. Sprinkle in the cumin, chili powder, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and cook for another 2 minutes.

  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and diced green chilies. Stir well to coat the chicken and vegetables with the sauce. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the chicken mixture is simmering, warm the corn tortillas. You can do this by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving them for 20-30 seconds, or by heating them on a dry skillet for a few seconds on each side.

  6. Take a tortilla and spoon some of the chicken mixture onto the center. Roll it up tightly and place it seam-side down in a baking dish. Repeat this process with the remaining tortillas and chicken mixture.

  7. Once all the tortillas are filled and rolled, spread the dairy-free sour cream evenly over the top of the enchiladas. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro.

  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the enchiladas are heated through and the edges are slightly crispy.

  9. Remove the enchiladas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with additional cilantro, if desired.

  10. Serve these mouthwatering dairy-free chicken enchiladas with a side of Mexican rice and guacamole for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

These dairy-free chicken enchiladas are a delicious alternative to traditional cheese-filled enchiladas. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful spices, and tangy tomato sauce will have your taste buds dancing with joy. Plus, the dairy-free sour cream adds a creamy and tangy element that perfectly complements the dish. Whether you’re lactose intolerant or simply looking to try something new, these enchiladas are sure to become a family favorite. So go ahead, give this recipe a try and enjoy a plate of mouthwatering dairy-free chicken enchiladas tonight!
