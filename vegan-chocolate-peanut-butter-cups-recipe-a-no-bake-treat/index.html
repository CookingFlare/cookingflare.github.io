Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups Recipe: A No-Bake Treat


Are you a fan of the classic combination of chocolate and peanut butter? If so, you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, I’m going to share with you a delicious recipe for Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups. Not only are these treats absolutely mouthwatering, but they’re also incredibly easy to make and require no baking at all. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to explore plant-based treats, these Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to whip up a batch of these delectable vegan treats:

  • For the Chocolate Shell:

    • 1 cup of dairy-free chocolate chips
    • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil

  • For the Peanut Butter Filling:

    • 3/4 cup of creamy peanut butter
    • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
    • 2 tablespoons of coconut flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
    • A pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chocolate Shell

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the dairy-free chocolate chips and coconut oil.
  2. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.

Step 2: Line the Muffin Tin

  1. Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper or silicone liners.

Step 3: Fill the Muffin Cups

  1. Spoon a small amount of the melted chocolate into the bottom of each muffin cup, using a spoon to spread it evenly and coat the bottom.
  2. Place the muffin tin in the freezer for 10 minutes to allow the chocolate to set.

Step 4: Prepare the Peanut Butter Filling

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the creamy peanut butter, powdered sugar, coconut flour, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir until smooth and well combined.

Step 5: Fill the Muffin Cups with Peanut Butter Filling

  1. Remove the muffin tin from the freezer and spoon a small amount of the peanut butter filling on top of the chilled chocolate layer in each cup, using a spoon to spread it evenly.

Step 6: Top with Chocolate

  1. Once the peanut butter filling is in place, spoon the remaining melted chocolate over the top of each cup, using a spoon to spread it and cover the peanut butter layer completely.

Step 7: Chill and Serve

  1. Return the muffin tin to the freezer for at least 30 minutes to allow the Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups to set completely.
  2. Once set, remove the paper or silicone liners from the muffin cups and enjoy your delicious no-bake vegan treats!

Tips and Variations

  • For a fun twist, sprinkle a pinch of sea salt on top of the chocolate before it sets for a sweet and salty flavor combination.
  • You can also experiment with different nut butters such as almond or cashew butter for a variety of flavors.
  • Store any leftover Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to two weeks.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups are a delightful combination of rich, creamy, and indulgent flavors, all without any animal products. Whether you’re making them for a special occasion or just as a sweet treat for yourself, these no-bake cups are sure to impress. They’re perfect for sharing with friends and family who may have dietary restrictions or simply for treating yourself to a guilt-free dessert. So, what are you waiting for? Get ready to indulge in these irresistible Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups and satisfy your sweet tooth without breaking a sweat!

I hope you enjoy making and savoring this delightful vegan treat. Happy no-bake cooking!
