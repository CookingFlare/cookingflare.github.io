Delicious Slow Cooker Creamy Chicken and Bacon Pasta Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting meal that practically cooks itself, then this Slow Cooker Creamy Chicken and Bacon Pasta recipe is just what you need. It’s the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings for something creamy, savory, and oh-so-indulgent. The best part? You can simply toss all the ingredients into your slow cooker and let it work its magic while you go about your day. Let’s dive right into this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 slices of bacon, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 oz. penne pasta
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Slow Cooker: Begin by spraying the inside of your slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray to prevent the pasta from sticking.

  2. Cook the Bacon: In a skillet over medium heat, cook the diced bacon until it’s nice and crispy. Once done, transfer the bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess fat.

  3. Sear the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and paprika. In the same skillet with the bacon fat, sear the chicken breasts until they’re golden brown on both sides. This step adds incredible flavor to the dish.

  4. Combine Ingredients in the Slow Cooker: Place the seared chicken breasts in the slow cooker. Add the cooked bacon, chopped onion, minced garlic, chicken broth, dried thyme, and dried oregano. Give everything a gentle stir to combine the flavors.

  5. Cook on Low: Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 4-5 hours, or until the chicken is tender and fully cooked.

  6. Shred the Chicken: Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker. This will allow the chicken to soak up all the delicious flavors in the sauce.

  7. Add the Pasta: Stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Then, add the uncooked penne pasta and gently push it down into the liquid.

  8. Cook Until Pasta is Tender: Cover the slow cooker and continue cooking on low for an additional 30-40 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked to al dente perfection.

  9. Serve and Enjoy: Once the pasta is cooked, give everything a final stir. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a splash of chicken broth to loosen it up. Serve the creamy chicken and bacon pasta in bowls, garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the sauce.
  • If you prefer a veggie-packed dish, throw in some spinach or broccoli during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  • This recipe can easily be customized with different types of pasta, such as fettuccine or rigatoni, depending on your preference.

Final Thoughts:

This Slow Cooker Creamy Chicken and Bacon Pasta recipe is a game-changer when it comes to easy yet satisfying meals. The combination of tender chicken, crispy bacon, and creamy sauce will have your taste buds singing with joy. Plus, the convenience of using a slow cooker makes it a perfect option for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Give this recipe a try and prepare to fall in love with the irresistible flavors. Happy cooking!
