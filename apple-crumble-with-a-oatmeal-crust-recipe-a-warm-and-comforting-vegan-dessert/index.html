Apple Crumble with a Oatmeal Crust Recipe: A Warm and Comforting Vegan Dessert


There’s something truly magical about the combination of sweet, tender apples and a crispy, buttery oatmeal crust. This vegan apple crumble recipe is a delightful twist on the classic dessert, offering a warm and comforting treat that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, craving a cozy dessert on a chilly evening, or simply want to indulge in some homemade goodness, this apple crumble with oatmeal crust is sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 6 large apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the Oatmeal Crust Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are evenly coated, then transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out into an even layer.

  3. In another bowl, combine the rolled oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Stir in the melted vegan butter until the mixture is crumbly and well combined.

  4. Sprinkle the oatmeal crust topping evenly over the apple filling, covering it completely.

  5. Bake the apple crumble in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apple filling is bubbling.

  6. Remove the apple crumble from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This delightful vegan apple crumble is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level with a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream or a dollop of coconut whipped cream. The contrast of warm, fragrant apple filling and cool, creamy accompaniments is simply irresistible.

About the Recipe

This vegan apple crumble with oatmeal crust is a wonderful way to enjoy a classic dessert while embracing a plant-based lifestyle. The tender, spiced apples offer a delightful contrast to the crisp, golden oatmeal crust, creating a symphony of flavors and textures in every bite.

The beauty of this recipe lies in its simplicity. By using wholesome ingredients and traditional baking techniques, you can recreate a beloved dessert without compromising on flavor or indulgence. Whether you’re vegan, dairy-free, or simply looking for a healthier dessert option, this apple crumble is a versatile and satisfying choice.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this apple crumble with oatmeal crust recipe is a testament to the fact that vegan desserts can be just as decadent and delicious as their traditional counterparts. By infusing this timeless dessert with a plant-based twist, you can savor the warmth and comfort of homemade apple crumble while honoring your dietary preferences.

So, go ahead and whip up a batch of this delightful vegan apple crumble, and let its irresistible aroma fill your kitchen as you embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the beauty of plant-based cooking.

Enjoy every delightful spoonful of this warm and comforting vegan dessert!
