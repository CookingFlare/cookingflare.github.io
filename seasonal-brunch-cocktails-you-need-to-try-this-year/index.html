Seasonal Brunch Cocktails You Need to Try This Year


Brunch is no longer just about the food. It’s about creating an experience—one where good company and great flavors come together in perfect harmony. While fluffy pancakes and savory egg dishes rightfully dominate the brunch table, the drinks deserve just as much attention. And this year, seasonal brunch cocktails are taking the spotlight. From bright and refreshing concoctions to rich and comforting sips, there’s a seasonal cocktail for every occasion. Here are some irresistible options that will elevate your next brunch gathering.

Spring Fizz with Elderflower and Citrus

As the frost thaws and vibrant blooms re-emerge, spring cocktails call for light, floral flavors that celebrate renewal. A Spring Fizz combines elderflower liqueur with freshly squeezed citrus juices for the perfect mixture of zesty and sweet. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 2 oz elderflower liqueur
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed orange juice
  • Sparkling water or soda water
  • Ice cubes
  • Garnish: Thin citrus slices or edible flowers

Directions

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine elderflower liqueur, lemon juice, and orange juice with ice cubes.
  2. Shake vigorously until chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled glass filled halfway with new ice cubes.
  4. Top off with sparkling water and stir gently.
  5. Garnish with a slice of citrus or a sprinkle of edible flowers.

Whether you’re brunching al fresco or warming up indoors, this cocktail symbolizes the refreshing energy of spring.

Summer Sangria Spritzer

Summer brunches are all about keeping cool while celebrating the abundance of seasonal fruits. This Sangria Spritzer is a lighter, sparkling twist on the classic sangria. Perfect for pairing with light salads and fresh pastries, it’s an ideal cocktail for making a pitcher in advance.

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle of chilled white wine (Sauvignon Blanc works beautifully)
  • ½ cup peach schnapps or apricot liqueur
  • 1 cup freshly diced seasonal fruits, such as peaches, strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ½ cup soda water or prosecco for a bubbly boost
  • Ice cubes

Directions

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the white wine, peach schnapps, orange juice, and diced fruits.
  2. Stir gently to allow the flavors to meld together.
  3. Let the pitcher refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, ideally 1–2 hours, to enhance infusion.
  4. Just before serving, add soda water or prosecco for extra fizz.
  5. Pour over ice in individual glasses and include some fresh fruit pieces in each glass.

This Sangria Spritzer is packed with summery vibes for those long, lazy brunches under the sun.

Autumn Apple Cider Mimosa

When crisp mornings signal the arrival of autumn, it’s time to turn to warm, spiced flavors. This Apple Cider Mimosa blends the comforting essence of apple cider with bubbly champagne, offering a seasonal spin on the brunch favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz apple cider (preferably spiced or fresh-pressed)
  • 1 oz spiced rum or bourbon (optional for an extra kick)
  • Champagne or sparkling wine
  • Garnish: Thin apple slices and a cinnamon sugar rim

Directions

  1. Optional: Prepare a cinnamon sugar rim by dipping the edge of each glass in a small dish of water, then pressing it into cinnamon sugar.
  2. Pour apple cider into the glass (with or without the rum or bourbon, depending on your preference).
  3. Top off with chilled champagne or sparkling wine.
  4. Garnish with a thin apple slice floating on top.

Pair this cocktail with seasonal comfort foods like pumpkin pancakes or a hearty quiche for a brunch that defines autumn indulgence.

Winter Spiced Cranberry Mule

Winter brunches often call for heartwarming cocktails that bring a bit of festive cheer. The Winter Spiced Cranberry Mule is a rich yet refreshing cocktail that combines the tartness of cranberry with ginger’s warmth—a true seasonal delight.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz cranberry vodka or regular vodka
  • 2 oz cranberry juice
  • ½ oz freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 3 oz ginger beer
  • Ice cubes
  • Garnish: Lime wheel, fresh cranberries, and a sprig of rosemary

Directions

  1. Fill a copper mug or glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add cranberry vodka, cranberry juice, and lime juice.
  3. Top with ginger beer and stir lightly to combine.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel, a few fresh cranberries, and a sprig of rosemary for a fragrant touch.

This cocktail delivers a cozy, festive vibe perfect for a fireside winter brunch.

All-Year Classic: Bloody Mary Garden Twist

No list of brunch cocktails would be complete without mentioning the Bloody Mary—a drink that can be customized to fit any season. The Garden Twist incorporates fresh, local ingredients that reflect the time of year, making it the quintessential all-season brunch beverage.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz fresh tomato juice (or seasonal heirloom tomato blend for extra flavor)
  • ½ oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Dash of Worcestershire sauce
  • Dash of hot sauce
  • Pinch of celery salt and black pepper
  • Garnish: Seasonal options like pickled vegetables, fresh herbs (basil or parsley), or even grilled asparagus spears

Directions

  1. In a shaker, combine vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, celery salt, and pepper with ice. Shake until chilled.
  2. Strain into a tall glass filled with fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a crisp, seasonal option—anything from garden-picked herbs to grilled vegetables.

The versatile and bold flavors shine whether you’re brunching in spring, summer, autumn, or winter.

Wrapping It Up: Elevate Your Brunch Experience

Seasonal brunch cocktails bring a creative edge to any gathering, offering flavors that celebrate the freshest ingredients each time of year. From the floral elegance of spring to the fruity joy of summer, the decadent warmth of autumn, and the festive cheer of winter, there’s no shortage of options to pair with your favorite meals.

When planning your brunch, think beyond mimosas and Bloody Marys. Get inspired by the season and let your creativity flow. These cocktails will not only impress your guests but make them eagerly anticipate your next brunch invite.

Cheers to seasonal sipping! Whether you’re hosting or attending, these vibrant creations will set the perfect tone for your gatherings all year long.
