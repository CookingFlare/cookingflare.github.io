Decadent Pumpkin Spice Donuts Recipe for a Thanksgiving Breakfast Treat


As the leaves turn golden and the air becomes crisp, it’s time to embrace the warm and comforting flavors of fall. What better way to celebrate the season than with a batch of decadent pumpkin spice donuts? These delightful treats are perfect for a Thanksgiving breakfast, adding a touch of indulgence to your holiday spread. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and let’s dive into the recipe for these irresistible pumpkin spice donuts!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a donut pan with butter or cooking spray.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and allspice.

  3. In another bowl, combine the melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar. Mix in the pumpkin puree, eggs, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

  5. Spoon the batter into a piping bag or a large resealable plastic bag with the corner snipped off. Pipe the batter into the prepared donut pan, filling each cavity about two-thirds full.

  6. Bake the donuts for 12-15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Allow the donuts to cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Glaze

For an extra touch of sweetness and flavor, consider glazing your pumpkin spice donuts with a simple cinnamon glaze or a luscious cream cheese glaze. To make the cinnamon glaze, whisk together powdered sugar, milk, and a dash of cinnamon until smooth. For the cream cheese glaze, beat together cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and a splash of milk until creamy and smooth. Drizzle the glaze over the cooled donuts and let them set before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These decadent pumpkin spice donuts are best enjoyed fresh, paired with a steaming cup of coffee or a glass of cold milk. They make a delightful addition to any Thanksgiving breakfast or brunch spread, adding a touch of warmth and sweetness to the table. Serve them alongside fresh fruit, yogurt, or scrambled eggs for a well-rounded morning meal that will leave everyone feeling grateful and satisfied.

Conclusion

With their irresistible aroma and cozy flavor, these pumpkin spice donuts are sure to become a favorite fall treat in your household. Whether you’re hosting a Thanksgiving gathering or simply craving a special breakfast, these decadent donuts will bring joy and indulgence to the occasion. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and treat yourself and your loved ones to the magic of homemade pumpkin spice donuts this Thanksgiving morning. Happy baking and happy Thanksgiving!

I hope you find this recipe for decadent pumpkin spice donuts as delightful as I do. Enjoy the warm, cozy flavors of fall with this indulgent treat. Happy Thanksgiving!
