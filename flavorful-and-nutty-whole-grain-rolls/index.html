Flavorful and Nutty Whole Grain Rolls


There’s something truly satisfying about biting into a warm, freshly baked roll. The softness, the aroma, and the burst of flavors make it an irresistible treat. And when it comes to rolls, nothing beats the goodness of whole grains. In this recipe, I’ll show you how to make Flavorful and Nutty Whole Grain Rolls that are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup flaxseed meal
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, rolled oats, flaxseed meal, honey, yeast, and salt. Mix well to combine all the dry ingredients.

  2. Gradually add the warm water to the dry mixture while stirring with a wooden spoon or a dough hook attachment on a stand mixer. Keep stirring until a sticky dough forms.

  3. Once the dough comes together, transfer it to a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If using a stand mixer, knead the dough on low speed for the same amount of time.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  5. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer it back to the floured surface and knead in the chopped walnuts and dried cranberries until they are evenly distributed.

  6. Divide the dough into 12 equal portions and shape each portion into a roll. Place the rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving some space between them.

  7. Cover the rolls with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  8. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  9. Once the rolls have risen for the second time, brush the tops with melted butter for a golden crust.

  10. Bake the rolls in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until they turn golden brown.

  11. Remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy Your Flavorful and Nutty Whole Grain Rolls!

These rolls are perfect for any occasion, from a family dinner to a picnic in the park. The combination of whole grains, walnuts, and cranberries adds a delightful nutty and slightly sweet flavor to every bite. Plus, the added nutrients from the whole wheat flour and flaxseed meal make them a healthier alternative to traditional rolls.

Serve these rolls warm with some butter or your favorite spread. You can also use them as a base for sandwiches or sliders. Get creative and experiment with different fillings like sliced turkey, cheese, or even avocado.

So go ahead and give this recipe a try. I promise you won’t be disappointed. Your kitchen will be filled with the aroma of freshly baked rolls, and your taste buds will be thanking you for the amazing flavors. Enjoy!
