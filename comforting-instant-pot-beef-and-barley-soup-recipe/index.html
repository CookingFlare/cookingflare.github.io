Comforting Instant Pot Beef and Barley Soup Recipe


Are you in need of a hearty and comforting meal that will warm you up on a chilly day? Look no further than this delicious Instant Pot Beef and Barley Soup! Packed with tender beef, wholesome barley, and flavorful vegetables, this soup is the perfect combination of comfort and nourishment.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup pearl barley
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the beef stew meat and cook until browned on all sides. This will help seal in the flavors and add depth to the soup.

  2. Once the beef is browned, add in the diced onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery, and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften and release their aromas.

  3. Next, add in the pearl barley, beef broth, water, bay leaf, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Secure the lid on your Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Select the “Soup” function and set the cooking time to 30 minutes.

  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes before carefully switching the pressure release valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  6. Remove the lid from the Instant Pot and give the soup a good stir. The beef should be tender and the barley perfectly cooked.

  7. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley for an extra burst of flavor. Pair it with some crusty bread or a side salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a slurry of cornstarch and water to the soup after it’s done cooking. Simply mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water, then stir it into the hot soup. Allow it to simmer for a few minutes until thickened.
  • Feel free to customize this soup by adding your favorite vegetables or herbs. Some great additions include mushrooms, green beans, or even a handful of spinach.
  • This soup is even better the next day, as the flavors have had time to develop. Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
  • For a gluten-free version of this soup, you can substitute the pearl barley with gluten-free grains like quinoa or rice.

Final Thoughts

This comforting Instant Pot Beef and Barley Soup is the ultimate meal for those looking for a warm and satisfying dish. With tender beef, wholesome barley, and a variety of vegetables, this soup is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Plus, with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have this soup on the table in no time. So go ahead and give this recipe a try - your taste buds will thank you!
