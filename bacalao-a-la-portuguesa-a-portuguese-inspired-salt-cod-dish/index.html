Bacalao a la Portuguesa: A Portuguese-Inspired Salt Cod Dish


If you’re a fan of seafood and bold flavors, then you’re in for a treat with Bacalao a la Portuguesa. This traditional Portuguese dish showcases the versatility and deliciousness of salt cod, a staple in Portuguese cuisine. With its rich history and unique preparation, Bacalao a la Portuguesa is a must-try for any adventurous food lover.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salt cod
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 red bell peppers, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Start by soaking the salt cod in cold water overnight. This will help to remove the excess salt. Change the water a couple of times during this process.

  2. Once the cod has been soaked, drain the water and rinse the fish under running water to remove any remaining salt. Pat it dry with a paper towel.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and cook until they become translucent. This should take around 5 minutes.

  4. Add the minced garlic and sliced red bell peppers to the skillet. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. Now it’s time to add the salt cod to the skillet. Break the fish into smaller pieces and carefully place them on top of the onion and pepper mixture.

  6. Pour in the diced tomatoes and white wine. Add the bay leaf, paprika, and black pepper. Give everything a gentle stir to combine the flavors.

  7. Cover the skillet and let the dish simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the cod is cooked through and tender.

  8. Remove the bay leaf and garnish with fresh parsley.

Serving Suggestions

Bacalao a la Portuguesa is a hearty and flavorful dish that pairs well with a variety of sides. Here are a few serving suggestions to enhance your culinary experience:

  • Serve the dish with a side of steamed vegetables, such as broccoli or green beans, to add a fresh and vibrant element to the meal.
  • Accompany the Bacalao a la Portuguesa with a simple salad dressed with lemon juice and olive oil. The acidity will complement the rich flavors of the dish.
  • For a more traditional Portuguese meal, serve the salt cod with boiled potatoes and olive oil. This classic combination will transport you to the streets of Lisbon.

Final Thoughts

Bacalao a la Portuguesa is a true celebration of Portuguese flavors. The combination of salt cod, onions, peppers, and tomatoes creates a harmonious blend of tastes that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re a seafood aficionado or looking to expand your culinary horizons, this dish is sure to impress. So get your ingredients ready, gather your loved ones, and embark on a delicious journey to Portugal with Bacalao a la Portuguesa.

Enjoy!
