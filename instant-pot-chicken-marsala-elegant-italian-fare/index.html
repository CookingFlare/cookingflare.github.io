Instant Pot Chicken Marsala: Elegant Italian Fare


If you’re looking to impress your dinner guests with a dish that exudes elegance and flavor, Instant Pot Chicken Marsala is the perfect choice. This classic Italian dish is a delightful combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and a rich Marsala wine sauce. The best part? With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, this gourmet meal can be prepared with ease and in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional cooking methods.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 ounces cremini or button mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Then, dredge the chicken in flour, shaking off any excess.

Step 2: Sauté the Chicken

Set your Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and melt 2 tablespoons of butter with the olive oil. Once hot, add the chicken breasts and brown them on both sides, working in batches if necessary. Transfer the browned chicken to a plate and set aside.

Step 3: Sauté the Mushrooms and Garlic

Add the remaining butter to the Instant Pot and sauté the mushrooms until they are golden brown. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds.

Step 4: Deglaze the Pot

Pour in the Marsala wine and chicken broth, using a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. This step is crucial for infusing the sauce with rich flavor.

Step 5: Pressure Cook

Return the chicken to the Instant Pot, lock the lid in place, and set the pot to cook at high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick pressure release.

Step 6: Thicken the Sauce

With the chicken removed from the pot, switch back to “Sauté” mode and stir in the heavy cream. Allow the sauce to simmer and thicken for a few minutes.

Step 7: Serve

Return the chicken to the pot, spooning the luscious sauce over the top. Garnish with chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Serving Suggestions

Instant Pot Chicken Marsala pairs beautifully with a variety of side dishes. Consider serving it over a bed of al dente pasta, creamy mashed potatoes, or alongside a simple green salad. A crusty loaf of Italian bread is perfect for soaking up every last bit of the decadent sauce.

Final Thoughts

With its luxurious flavors and sophisticated presentation, Instant Pot Chicken Marsala is a dish that is sure to elevate any occasion. Whether you’re preparing a romantic dinner for two or hosting a dinner party, this Italian classic is guaranteed to leave a lasting impression. Embrace the convenience of your Instant Pot and indulge in the indulgent flavors of this elegant Italian fare.
