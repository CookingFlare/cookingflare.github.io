Sheet Pan Honey Mustard Pork Chops with Potatoes: A Sweet and Savory Dinner


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy dinner recipe that will impress your family and friends, look no further than this sheet pan honey mustard pork chops with potatoes. This recipe is a perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors, and the best part is that it all comes together on one sheet pan, making clean-up a breeze. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons whole grain mustard
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that your pork chops and potatoes cook evenly and develop that beautiful golden crust.

  2. Prepare the honey mustard marinade: In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, whole grain mustard, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, olive oil, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. This flavorful marinade will be the key to infusing the pork chops and potatoes with a delightful mix of sweet and tangy flavors.

  3. Coat the pork chops and potatoes: Place the pork chops and diced potatoes on a large sheet pan. Drizzle the honey mustard marinade over the pork chops and potatoes, ensuring that they are evenly coated. Use your hands to gently massage the marinade into the pork chops and potatoes, allowing the flavors to penetrate the ingredients.

  4. Bake to perfection: Once the pork chops and potatoes are coated with the marinade, transfer the sheet pan to the preheated oven. Let the ingredients bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the pork chops are cooked through and the potatoes are tender, with crispy edges.

  5. Garnish and serve: Once the sheet pan honey mustard pork chops with potatoes are perfectly cooked, remove them from the oven. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the dish hot, and watch as your guests savor every bite of this delectable meal.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra caramelized finish, you can broil the pork chops and potatoes for 2-3 minutes at the end of the cooking time.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding seasonal vegetables such as carrots, Brussels sprouts, or green beans to the sheet pan.
  • If you’re a fan of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes into the honey mustard marinade for a spicy kick.

This sheet pan honey mustard pork chops with potatoes recipe is a true crowd-pleaser. The succulent pork chops paired with the golden, crispy potatoes create a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that will leave everyone at the table satisfied. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating your family to a special meal, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Give it a try and prepare to be amazed by the incredible flavors that come together with minimal effort. Cheers to sweet and savory perfection on a sheet pan!
