Beyond Beef: Creative Bison Burger Recipes to Impress

If you’re looking to add a unique and flavorful twist to your summer grilling menu, bison burgers are the way to go. Beyond Beef, known for its delicious plant-based products, offers a delectable ground bison option that’s perfect for creating mouthwatering burgers. In this blog post, we’ll explore some creative and impressive bison burger recipes that are sure to elevate your next cookout. 1. Classic Bison Burger with Caramelized Onions Ingredients: 1 pound Beyond Beef ground bison 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 large onion, thinly sliced Salt and pepper to taste 4 burger buns Your favorite burger toppings (lettuce, tomato, cheese, etc.) Instructions: Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat and add the sliced onions. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the onions are soft and golden brown. While the onions are caramelizing, divide the ground bison into four equal portions and form them into burger patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the bison patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute or two. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled bison patties on the toasted buns, topping them with the caramelized onions, and adding your favorite burger fixings. 2. Spicy Southwestern Bison Burger Ingredients: 1 pound Beyond Beef ground bison 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper Salt and pepper to taste 4 burger buns Pepper jack cheese, sliced Sliced avocado Salsa Instructions: In a bowl, combine the ground bison, chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix until the spices are evenly distributed throughout the meat. Form the seasoned bison into four patties and grill them over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes on each side. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of pepper jack cheese on each patty to melt. Toast the burger buns on the grill. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled bison patties on the buns, topping them with sliced avocado and a spoonful of salsa. 3. Teriyaki Pineapple Bison Burger Ingredients: 1 pound Beyond Beef ground bison 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce 4 pineapple slices 4 burger buns Red onion, thinly sliced Lettuce Instructions: Pour the teriyaki sauce over the ground bison and mix until well combined. Form the meat into four patties. Grill the bison patties over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes on each side. During the last minute of grilling, place a pineapple slice on each patty to caramelize. Toast the burger buns on the grill. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled bison patties on the buns, topping them with the caramelized pineapple slices, red onion, and lettuce. These creative bison burger recipes are not only impressive but also incredibly delicious. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a gourmet burger at home, Beyond Beef ground bison is a versatile and flavorful option that’s sure to satisfy. Experiment with these recipes and let your culinary creativity shine as you impress your friends and family with gourmet bison burgers. Enjoy! ...
