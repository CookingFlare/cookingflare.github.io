Top 14 Vietnamese Spring Roll Recipes


Vietnamese spring rolls, or “gỏi cuốn,” are a versatile and beloved dish that captures the essence of Vietnamese cuisine: fresh ingredients, simplicity, and balanced flavors. These wraps of goodness are perfect for any meal or occasion, combining vibrant colors and textures with a harmony of tastes. Whether you’re hosting a get-together or craving something light and healthy, these rolls offer endless possibilities to experiment with flavors while staying true to their roots.

If you’re ready to embark on a delicious culinary journey, these 14 Vietnamese spring roll recipes will bring creativity, excitement, and a burst of flavor into your cooking routine. Get ready to perfect your rolling technique and discover a variety of filling combinations that suit every palate!

1. Classic Pork and Shrimp Spring Rolls

The timeless recipe for Vietnamese spring rolls starts with tender slices of cooked pork belly and juicy shrimp nestled alongside noodles, fresh herbs, and crunchy vegetables. Wrapped in delicate rice paper, these rolls are paired with traditional peanut hoisin dipping sauce for an authentic taste of Vietnam. Perfected over generations, this recipe highlights simplicity and freshness.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked pork belly slices
  • Boiled shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro, and Thai basil)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Soak the rice paper in warm water just long enough to soften but not too long as it can tear easily. Roll tightly to ensure a firm bite.

2. Vegetarian Tofu Spring Rolls

For a plant-based version, tofu is the protein star. Pan-fried or baked tofu adds depth and richness to these rolls, complemented by crunchy vegetables, noodles, and fragrant herbs. Serve these with a zesty soy-lime dipping sauce for maximum flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Firm tofu, pan-fried or baked
  • Julienned carrots and cucumber
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Fresh herbs
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Marinate the tofu with soy sauce and garlic before frying for an extra layer of flavor.

3. Fried Spring Rolls (Cha Gio)

The traditional crispy Vietnamese fried spring rolls, known as cha gio, feature a savory filling of ground pork, shrimp, mushrooms, and vegetables wrapped in thin rice paper or wheat wrappers. These are deep-fried until golden and crunchy, then served with a tangy fish sauce dipping sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Ground pork
  • Chopped shrimp
  • Wood ear mushrooms
  • Grated carrots
  • Bean sprouts
  • Fish sauce
  • Egg to bind the filling
  • Spring roll wrappers (rice paper or wheat-based)

Tips:

Freeze your rolls for 15 minutes before frying to ensure they stay firm and maintain their shape.

4. Grilled Lemongrass Chicken Spring Rolls

Imagine mouthwatering marinated lemongrass chicken wrapped inside soft rice paper, paired with crisp vegetables and aromatic herbs. This recipe delivers smoky, savory, and slightly sweet flavors that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • Grilled lemongrass-marinated chicken
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Fresh herbs
  • Julienned zucchini and cucumber
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Grill the chicken on high heat to caramelize the lemongrass marinade for added smokiness.

5. Spicy Sriracha Shrimp Spring Rolls

If you’re looking for a fiery twist, this recipe pairs plump shrimp with a spicy sriracha mayonnaise, fresh herbs, and crunchy vegetables. These bold, vibrant spring rolls are ideal for heat lovers.

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp tossed in sriracha mayonnaise
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro and mint)
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Julienned bell peppers
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Adjust the sriracha mayo according to your heat tolerance for the perfect balance.

6. Vietnamese Beef Bulgogi Spring Rolls

Fusion meets tradition in this unique recipe. Marinated beef bulgogi adds a sweet, charred flavor to these rolls, complemented by fresh herbs, pickled radishes, and vermicelli noodles. It’s a delightful fusion of Korean and Vietnamese cuisines.

Ingredients:

  • Thinly sliced beef marinated in bulgogi sauce
  • Pickled daikon radish and carrots
  • Fresh herbs
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

To maximize flavor, grill the beef slices quickly over high heat.

7. Fresh Mango and Avocado Spring Rolls

This refreshing combination is perfect for summer. Sweet slices of mango and creamy avocado are paired with crispy vegetables and herbs, making these spring rolls light, healthy, and bursting with flavor. Dip into a tangy tamarind sauce to elevate the tropical experience.

Ingredients:

  • Ripe mango slices
  • Creamy avocado slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Fresh herbs
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Use slightly firm mangoes for easier rolling.

8. BBQ Pork Spring Rolls

Smoky and sweet flavored BBQ pork takes center stage in these fragrant spring rolls. Combined with crunchy lettuce, carrots, and an optional drizzle of hoisin sauce, these rolls are both comforting and crave-worthy.

Ingredients:

  • Grilled pork marinated in BBQ sauce
  • Julienned carrots and cucumber
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Fresh herbs
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Let the grilled pork rest before slicing to retain the juices.

9. Seafood Medley Spring Rolls

Perfect for seafood enthusiasts, this recipe features a mix of shrimp, crab meat, and scallops wrapped in rice paper with seasoned avocado, greens, and herbs.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked shrimp
  • Lump crab meat or imitation crab
  • Sear-grilled scallops (optional)
  • Fresh herbs
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Keep the fillings minimal for cleaner rolls and better presentation.

10. Vietnamese Rainbow Spring Rolls

Packed with colorful vegetables, these rolls are as visually striking as they are healthy. Layered with julienned cucumber, beetroot, bell peppers, and carrots, these rolls offer a stunning palette of freshness.

Ingredients:

  • Cucumber
  • Beetroot
  • Carrots
  • Red and yellow bell peppers
  • Fresh herbs
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Play with ingredient placement inside each roll for extra aesthetic appeal.

11. Spicy Peanut Chicken Spring Rolls

Here, shredded chicken tossed in a homemade spicy peanut sauce serves as the centerpiece of these uniquely flavorful spring rolls. The creamy peanut sauce pairs perfectly with fresh greens and rice noodles.

Ingredients:

  • Shredded cooked chicken
  • Homemade spicy peanut sauce
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Fresh greens
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Reserve some peanut sauce for dipping to maximize the taste.

12. Sweet Chili Salmon Spring Rolls

Salmon lovers rejoice! Sear-grilled salmon glazed with sweet chili sauce brings smoky, sweet, and spicy flavors to these fresh rolls. Add crisp cucumbers and mint to brighten these indulgent bites.

Ingredients:

  • Grilled salmon with sweet chili glaze
  • Cucumber slices
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Slice the salmon thinly for easier rolling and better texture.

13. Vietnamese Eggplant and Mushroom Spring Rolls

For a vegetarian option with depth of flavor, this recipe pairs roasted eggplant and sautéed mushrooms with fresh greens and herbs. A zingy lime-garlic dipping sauce adds the finishing touch.

Ingredients:

  • Roasted eggplant slices
  • Sautéed mushrooms (shiitake, oyster, or button)
  • Fresh herbs
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Roast the eggplant with a drizzle of sesame oil for added smokiness.

14. Vietnamese Dessert Spring Rolls

Imagine sweet spring rolls stuffed with coconut sticky rice, ripe mango slices, and a drizzle of condensed milk. These dessert rolls are an innovative twist that will satisfy your sweet tooth and bring a refreshing end to any meal.

Ingredients:

  • Coconut milk-infused sticky rice
  • Mango slices
  • Drizzle of condensed milk
  • Rice paper wrappers

Tips:

Wrap tightly and chill before serving for the perfect dessert texture.

Vietnamese spring rolls are a canvas for creativity, offering countless opportunities to mix and match ingredients while showcasing their signature freshness and vibrancy. Whether you’re drawn to classic recipes or adventurous variations, mastering the art of spring rolls will elevate your culinary repertoire while introducing a piece of Vietnamese culture to your table.

Roll, dip, and savor each creation—it’s time to bring Vietnam’s iconic flavors into your home! Try these recipes today and enjoy a burst of inspiration in every delicious bite.
