Fresh and Light Quinoa and Tomato Salad


Quinoa is renowned for its nutritional benefits and versatility in the kitchen. When paired with vibrant, juicy tomatoes and a medley of fresh herbs, it transforms into a delightful salad that’s perfect for summer or anytime you crave a light and nourishing meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed thoroughly
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar or lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

Cooking Quinoa

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa and water or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  2. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and has absorbed all the liquid.
  3. Remove from heat and let it sit covered for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and transfer to a large mixing bowl to cool.

Preparing the Salad

  1. While the quinoa is cooking, prepare the vegetables. Halve the cherry tomatoes, dice the cucumber, and finely chop the red onion.
  2. In the same mixing bowl with the cooled quinoa, add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, chopped parsley, basil, and mint.
  3. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar or lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Mixing and Serving

  1. Gently toss all the ingredients together until well combined and evenly coated with the dressing.
  2. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  3. Serve immediately, or refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together before serving.

Tips for the Perfect Quinoa Salad

  • Rinsing Quinoa: Always rinse quinoa thoroughly before cooking to remove its natural coating, which can make it taste bitter.
  • Vegetable Broth: Using vegetable broth instead of water adds extra flavor to the quinoa.
  • Fresh Herbs: Fresh herbs like parsley, basil, and mint bring a burst of freshness and aroma to the salad.
  • Make-Ahead: This salad can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Add fresh herbs just before serving to maintain their flavor.

Variations

  • Add Protein: Mix in grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu for added protein.
  • Cheese: Sprinkle feta or goat cheese over the salad for a creamy tangy touch.
  • Nuts and Seeds: Toasted pine nuts, almonds, or pumpkin seeds add crunch and extra nutrients.

Enjoy Your Quinoa and Tomato Salad!

This fresh and light quinoa salad is not only delicious but also packed with wholesome ingredients that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Whether you’re looking for a quick weekday lunch, a side dish for a barbecue, or a healthy potluck contribution, this salad fits the bill perfectly. Embrace the simplicity of fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors with every forkful. Cheers to good health and delightful eating!
