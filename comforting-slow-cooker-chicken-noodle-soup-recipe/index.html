Comforting Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe


There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of chicken noodle soup to soothe the soul and nourish the body. This comforting slow cooker chicken noodle soup recipe is the perfect way to bring warmth and flavor to your table with minimal effort. The slow cooker does all the work, allowing the flavors to meld together into a delicious and comforting dish that’s perfect for any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 4 medium carrots, sliced
  • 4 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces wide egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker.

  2. Add the chicken broth, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, bay leaves, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper to the slow cooker.

  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

  4. Remove the cooked chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  5. Add the egg noodles to the slow cooker, cover, and cook on high for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.

  6. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  7. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Tips for Success

  • For a richer flavor, you can use chicken thighs instead of chicken breasts.
  • Feel free to add your favorite herbs or spices to customize the flavor of the soup.
  • If you prefer a thicker broth, you can mix 2 tablespoons of cornstarch with ¼ cup of cold water and stir the mixture into the soup during the last 30 minutes of cooking.

Why Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup?

This slow cooker chicken noodle soup recipe is a must-have for busy weeknights, lazy weekends, or when you’re feeling under the weather. The convenience of the slow cooker allows you to simply set it and forget it, making it an ideal meal for those hectic days when you don’t have the time to stand over a stove.

The combination of tender chicken, flavorful vegetables, and hearty noodles creates a soup that’s both satisfying and nourishing. Plus, the aroma that fills your home as it simmers is simply irresistible.

Not only is this slow cooker chicken noodle soup a comforting and delicious meal, but it’s also a great way to use up any leftover chicken or vegetables you may have in your fridge. It’s a versatile recipe that can easily be adapted to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs.

In Conclusion

This comforting slow cooker chicken noodle soup recipe is a timeless classic that never fails to deliver on flavor and warmth. Whether you’re looking for an easy weeknight meal or a cozy dish to enjoy on a chilly day, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

So, dust off your slow cooker and give this recipe a try. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, it’s bound to become a family favorite in no time. Enjoy the heartwarming goodness of homemade slow cooker chicken noodle soup!
