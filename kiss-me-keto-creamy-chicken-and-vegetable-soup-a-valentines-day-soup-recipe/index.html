Kiss Me Keto Creamy Chicken and Vegetable Soup: A Valentine's Day Soup Recipe


Valentine’s Day is all about indulgence, romance, and showing your loved ones just how much you care. What better way to celebrate this special day than with a delicious and comforting bowl of Kiss Me Keto Creamy Chicken and Vegetable Soup? Packed with flavor, low in carbs, and oh-so-satisfying, this soup is the perfect dish to warm your heart and nourish your body.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 medium-sized carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 stalks of celery, diced
  • 1 medium-sized zucchini, diced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Begin by preparing the chicken breasts. Season them with salt and pepper, then heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook the chicken breasts until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside to cool.

  2. In the same pot, add another tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until they are fragrant and translucent, about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Add the sliced carrots, diced celery, and diced zucchini to the pot. Sauté the vegetables for another 5 minutes, allowing them to soften slightly.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the soup is simmering, shred the cooled chicken breasts using two forks. Once shredded, add the chicken to the pot.

  6. Stir in the heavy cream, dried thyme, and dried oregano. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Let the soup simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the flavors to fully develop and the soup to thicken slightly.

  8. Serve the Kiss Me Keto Creamy Chicken and Vegetable Soup hot, garnished with fresh chopped parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the soup.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable mix according to your preferences. Broccoli, cauliflower, or bell peppers would all work well in this recipe.
  • If you’re following a dairy-free or lactose-free diet, you can substitute the heavy cream with coconut cream or almond milk for a creamy alternative.
  • To make this soup even heartier, you can add cooked cauliflower rice or shirataki noodles to the pot during the last few minutes of simmering.

Conclusion

There you have it - a delightful and keto-friendly Valentine’s Day soup recipe that will have you and your loved ones swooning with each spoonful. The Kiss Me Keto Creamy Chicken and Vegetable Soup is not only delicious and satisfying, but it’s also incredibly easy to make. So why not treat yourself and your significant other to a cozy night in with this heartwarming soup? Happy Valentine’s Day!
