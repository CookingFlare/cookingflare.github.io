Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Lemon Glaze Recipe: A Tangy and Sweet Vegan Dessert

3 min · 492 words

Welcome to our vegan dessert recipe blog! Today, we have a delightful treat for you - Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Lemon Glaze. These tangy and sweet bars are not only vegan but also incredibly delicious. The combination of the buttery shortbread crust and the zesty lemon glaze creates a perfect balance of flavors. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

For the Shortbread Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Lemon Filling:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Zest of 2 lemons

For the Lemon Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8-inch baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, softened vegan butter, powdered sugar, and salt for the shortbread crust. Mix until the ingredients come together and form a crumbly dough.

  3. Press the dough evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Use the back of a spoon or your hands to ensure a smooth and compact crust.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes or until it turns golden brown.

  5. While the crust is baking, prepare the lemon filling. In a saucepan, whisk together the granulated sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually whisk in the freshly squeezed lemon juice, water, and lemon zest until well combined.

  6. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook the lemon filling, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a gentle boil. Continue cooking for another minute, then remove it from the heat.

  7. Pour the hot lemon filling over the baked shortbread crust, spreading it evenly. Allow the bars to cool completely at room temperature.

  8. Once the bars have cooled, prepare the lemon glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar and freshly squeezed lemon juice until smooth and creamy.

  9. Drizzle the lemon glaze over the cooled lemon bars, using a spoon or a piping bag. You can create patterns or simply cover the bars entirely with the glaze.

  10. Allow the glaze to set for about 30 minutes, or until it becomes firm. Once set, carefully lift the bars out of the baking dish using the parchment paper edges.

  11. Cut the bars into squares or rectangles of your desired size.

Serving and Storage

These Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Lemon Glaze are best served chilled. Place them in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving. They make a perfect dessert for any occasion, and their tangy and sweet flavors are sure to impress your guests.

You can store the bars in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. However, we doubt they will last that long!

Conclusion

We hope you enjoy making and savoring these Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Lemon Glaze. They are the perfect vegan dessert option for those who love a burst of tangy and sweet flavors. With their buttery crust and zesty glaze, these bars are sure to become a favorite in your household. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in this irresistible treat. Happy baking!

Note: This recipe has been adapted for a vegan diet. However, feel free to substitute any ingredients with their non-vegan counterparts if you prefer.
