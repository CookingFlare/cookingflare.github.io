Pasta with Broccoli and Garlic: A Simple and Healthy Pasta Option


If you’re looking for a quick, easy, and healthy pasta dish, look no further than pasta with broccoli and garlic. This simple yet flavorful recipe is a perfect option for a busy weeknight dinner or a leisurely weekend meal. Packed with nutrients and bursting with flavor, this dish is sure to become a staple in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces of pasta (such as penne or fusilli)
  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes, to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Before draining the pasta, reserve about 1/2 cup of the pasta water.

  2. Cook the Broccoli: While the pasta is cooking, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the broccoli florets and sauté for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender and lightly browned. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the broccoli from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Sauté the Garlic: In the same skillet, heat the remaining 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for 1-2 minutes, or until the garlic is fragrant and just beginning to brown.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the garlic and oil. Toss to coat the pasta evenly. If the pasta seems dry, add some of the reserved pasta water a little at a time until the desired consistency is reached.

  5. Add the Broccoli: Gently fold the sautéed broccoli into the pasta and garlic mixture, ensuring that the broccoli is evenly distributed throughout.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the pasta to serving plates, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley, and serve immediately.

Why It’s Great

  • Nutrient-Packed: Broccoli is a powerhouse of nutrients, including vitamins C and K, fiber, and antioxidants. Incorporating it into your pasta provides a healthy boost to your meal.

  • Flavorful and Aromatic: The combination of garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes creates a flavorful and aromatic base for the dish, elevating the overall taste.

  • Versatile: This recipe is incredibly versatile. You can add protein such as grilled chicken or shrimp for an extra boost, or swap the broccoli for other vegetables like spinach or cherry tomatoes.

  • Quick and Easy: With minimal ingredients and simple cooking techniques, this pasta dish comes together in no time, making it perfect for busy days.

Final Thoughts

Pasta with broccoli and garlic is a wonderful addition to any meal rotation. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this dish is sure to impress with its simplicity and bold flavors. Plus, it’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet without sacrificing taste. Give this recipe a try and savor the delicious combination of wholesome ingredients and delightful flavors!
