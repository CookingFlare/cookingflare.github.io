Satisfying Gluten-Free Stuffed Mushrooms: A Savory Appetizer


If you’re on the lookout for a delectable and gluten-free appetizer to impress your guests, look no further than these mouthwatering stuffed mushrooms. Bursting with savory flavors and easy to customize, these little bites are sure to be a hit at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a delicious snack, these gluten-free stuffed mushrooms are the perfect choice.

Ingredients

  • 24 large cremini or button mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup gluten-free breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Preparing the Mushrooms

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Gently wipe the mushrooms with a damp paper towel to remove any dirt. Carefully remove the stems and set them aside. Place the mushroom caps on the prepared baking sheet.

Making the Filling

  1. Finely chop the mushroom stems.
  2. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and chopped mushroom stems to the skillet. Cook for a few minutes until the mushrooms release their moisture.
  4. Stir in the gluten-free breadcrumbs and cook until they are lightly browned.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.

Stuffing the Mushrooms

  1. Using a small spoon, fill each mushroom cap with a generous amount of the prepared filling, mounding it slightly.
  2. Sprinkle the stuffed mushrooms with shredded mozzarella cheese.

Baking the Stuffed Mushrooms

  1. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the stuffed mushrooms for 18-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  2. Once done, remove the mushrooms from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley.

Serving

Arrange the gluten-free stuffed mushrooms on a platter and serve them warm to your eager guests. These savory bites are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, with their irresistible aroma filling the air.

Customization

Feel free to get creative with the filling for these stuffed mushrooms. You can add cooked sausage, chopped spinach, or even diced sun-dried tomatoes for a unique twist. The gluten-free breadcrumbs can also be substituted with almond meal or crushed gluten-free crackers for a different texture.

Conclusion

These satisfying gluten-free stuffed mushrooms are a delightful appetizer that will leave your guests coming back for more. With their savory flavors and easy preparation, they are a fantastic addition to any gathering. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply love delicious food, these stuffed mushrooms are a must-try. Enjoy the process of making them, savor every bite, and share the joy of this delightful appetizer with your loved ones.
