10 Decadent Vegan Dessert Recipes You Need to Try


Indulging in dessert doesn’t have to mean sacrificing your dietary choices or values. Vegan desserts can be just as rich, satisfying, and mouthwatering as their non-vegan counterparts. With an abundance of plant-based options available, there’s no shortage of ways to create decadent sweets that everyone can enjoy. Here, we present ten delightful vegan dessert recipes that are guaranteed to impress. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking to try something different, these recipes are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

1. Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Begin by halving the avocados and removing the pits. Scoop the flesh into a blender or food processor.
  2. Add the cocoa powder, maple syrup, almond milk, vanilla extract, and salt.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.
  4. Taste and adjust sweetness if necessary.
  5. Transfer the mousse to serving bowls and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving. Enjoy garnished with fresh berries or a sprinkle of cocoa powder.

2. Coconut Macaroons

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups shredded coconut (unsweetened)
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup dark vegan chocolate (for drizzling)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine shredded coconut, almond flour, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  3. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet, shaping them into small mounds.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  5. Allow cooling slightly before melting the dark chocolate and drizzling it over the macaroons. Let the chocolate set before serving.

3. Banana Bread Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups cubed day-old bread (preferably whole grain)
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup raisins or walnuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mashed bananas, almond milk, maple syrup, cinnamon, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Add the cubed bread, stirring gently to coat. If using, fold in raisins or walnuts.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and bake for 30-35 minutes or until the top is golden and the pudding is set.
  5. Serve warm, drizzled with additional maple syrup or a dollop of coconut whipped cream.

4. Raw Vegan Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup medjool dates, pitted
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1/2 cup raw cacao powder
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of sea salt

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the medjool dates and walnuts. Pulse until finely chopped and sticky.
  2. Add the raw cacao powder, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and sea salt. Process until the mixture is well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a lined cake pan, creating an even layer.
  4. Refrigerate for at least an hour to firm up before slicing into squares. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

5. Lemon Blueberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews, soaked for at least 4 hours
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup almond flour (for crust)
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted (for crust)

Instructions:

  1. Begin by preparing the crust. In a bowl, mix almond flour and melted coconut oil until crumbly. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
  2. Drain and rinse the soaked cashews, then place them in a blender or food processor.
  3. Add coconut cream, maple syrup, lemon juice, and zest. Blend until creamy and smooth.
  4. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries.
  5. Pour the filling over the crust and smooth the top. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until set.
  6. Slice and serve garnished with additional blueberries and lemon slices.

6. Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix together peanut butter, coconut sugar, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and baking soda until well combined.
  3. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them apart.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden. Allow to cool for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

7. Vegan Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 cup cashews, soaked for at least 4 hours
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup vegan ladyfingers or sponge cake
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine soaked cashews, coconut cream, maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Dip each ladyfinger quickly into the brewed coffee and layer them in a dish.
  3. Spread a layer of the cashew cream mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers, finishing with a layer of cashew cream.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours to set.
  5. Dust the top with cocoa powder before serving for an authentic tiramisu experience.

8. Chia Seed Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup chia seeds
  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruit and nuts for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together chia seeds, almond milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  2. Stir well to avoid clumping, then let it sit for about 10 minutes. Stir again to break up any clumps.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight to thicken.
  4. Serve chilled, topped with fresh fruit and your choice of nuts.

9. Strawberry Banana Nice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced and frozen
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and frozen
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup (optional)
  • A splash of almond milk (if needed)

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the frozen bananas and strawberries. Blend until creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.
  2. If the mixture is too thick, add a splash of almond milk or maple syrup to help it blend smoothly.
  3. Serve immediately for a soft-serve texture, or freeze for an hour for a firmer consistency. Enjoy garnished with fresh fruit.

10. Vegan S’mores Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows (vegan)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, almond flour, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup. Mix until crumbly.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan, creating an even layer.
  4. Sprinkle the chocolate chips and mini marshmallows over the crust.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the marshmallows are golden brown.
  6. Allow to cool before cutting into squares. These bars are perfect for a sweet summer treat!

Conclusion

These ten decadent vegan dessert recipes showcase the versatility and richness of plant-based ingredients. From creamy cheesecakes to rich chocolate treats, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Each recipe is designed to satisfy your sweet cravings while keeping your dietary choices in mind. So gather your ingredients, unleash your inner pastry chef, and treat yourself (and your loved ones) to these delightful vegan desserts. Happy baking!
