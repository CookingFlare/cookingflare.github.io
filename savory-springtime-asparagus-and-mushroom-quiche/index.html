Savory Springtime: Asparagus and Mushroom Quiche

3 min · 524 words

Welcome to my kitchen! Today, I’m excited to share with you a delightful springtime recipe that combines the freshness of asparagus with the earthiness of mushrooms – the Savory Springtime Asparagus and Mushroom Quiche. This quiche is a perfect dish to celebrate the arrival of spring and makes for a delicious breakfast, brunch, or even a light dinner option. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 bunch of fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup of shredded gruyere cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  3. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  4. While the mushrooms are cooking, blanch the asparagus in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then transfer them to an ice bath to stop the cooking process. This will help retain their vibrant green color.

  5. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Season with salt, pepper, and dried thyme.

  6. Place the pre-made pie crust in a pie dish, then spread the sautéed mushrooms evenly on the bottom.

  7. Arrange the blanched asparagus on top of the mushrooms in an attractive pattern.

  8. Pour the egg and milk mixture over the asparagus and mushrooms, ensuring that it covers everything evenly.

  9. Sprinkle the shredded gruyere cheese on top of the quiche.

  10. Carefully transfer the quiche to the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the filling is set.

  11. Once the quiche is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This savory springtime quiche can be enjoyed hot, warm, or even at room temperature. It pairs perfectly with a fresh green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette. You can also serve it alongside some crusty bread or as part of a larger brunch spread. The options are endless!

Final Thoughts

The Savory Springtime Asparagus and Mushroom Quiche is a versatile and flavorful dish that embodies the essence of spring. The combination of asparagus and mushrooms creates a delightful contrast of textures and flavors, while the creamy egg and cheese filling adds richness to every bite. Whether you’re hosting a springtime gathering or simply want to enjoy a delicious homemade meal, this quiche is sure to impress.

So, why not embrace the flavors of the season and give this recipe a try? Your taste buds will thank you!
