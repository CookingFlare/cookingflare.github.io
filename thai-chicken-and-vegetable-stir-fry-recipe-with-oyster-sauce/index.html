Thai Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe with Oyster Sauce


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and love the combination of savory flavors and vibrant colors, then this Thai Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry recipe with Oyster Sauce is a must-try. This mouthwatering dish is quick and easy to prepare, making it perfect for busy weeknights when you crave a delicious homemade meal. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken slices and stir fry until cooked through and lightly browned. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil and sauté the onion, bell peppers, broccoli, snow peas, and minced garlic. Stir fry for about 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  3. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, and red pepper flakes (if using).

  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables, and toss to coat everything evenly. Continue to cook for another 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  5. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve the Thai Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry over cooked jasmine rice.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables such as mushrooms, zucchini, or carrots.
  • For a vegetarian version, swap the chicken with tofu or tempeh and use vegetarian oyster sauce.
  • Adjust the spice level by adding more or less red pepper flakes according to your preference.
  • This dish pairs well with a side of Thai jasmine rice, but you can also serve it with noodles or quinoa for a healthier alternative.

Conclusion

This Thai Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry recipe with Oyster Sauce is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will surely satisfy your cravings. With its colorful array of vegetables, tender chicken, and savory oyster sauce, this dish is both healthy and delicious. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is easy to follow and will impress your family and friends. So, give it a try and indulge in the exotic flavors of Thailand right in your own kitchen!
