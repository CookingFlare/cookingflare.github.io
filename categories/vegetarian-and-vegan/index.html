10 Easy Vegan Stir-Fry Recipes for Busy Weeknights

If you’re someone who craves a quick, nutritious, and delicious meal after a long day, vegan stir-fries are the perfect solution. They are versatile, quick to prepare, and can be packed with a variety of vegetables and proteins, making them a fantastic option for busy weeknights. Here, we present ten easy vegan stir-fry recipes that will keep your weeknight dinners exciting and healthy. 1. Classic Vegetable Stir-Fry Ingredients: 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas) 1 block firm tofu, cubed 3 tablespoons soy sauce 2 tablespoons sesame oil 1 tablespoon ginger, minced 2 cloves garlic, minced Cooked rice or quinoa, for serving Instructions: Prepare the Tofu: Press the tofu to remove excess moisture, then cut it into cubes. Sauté the Tofu: In a large pan, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove and set aside. Cook the Vegetables: In the same pan, add ginger and garlic. Sauté for 1 minute, then add the mixed vegetables. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp. Combine: Return the tofu to the pan, pour in the soy sauce, and toss everything together. Serve over rice or quinoa. 2. Spicy Szechuan Noodles Ingredients: 8 oz. rice noodles 2 cups broccoli florets 1 bell pepper, sliced 1 cup snap peas 3 tablespoons Szechuan sauce 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Green onions, for garnish Instructions: Cook the Noodles: Prepare the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. Stir-Fry the Veggies: In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add broccoli, bell pepper, and snap peas, and sauté for 4-5 minutes. Combine with Noodles: Add the cooked noodles and Szechuan sauce to the pan, tossing until everything is well-coated. Cook for an additional 2 minutes. Serve: Garnish with sliced green onions before serving. 3. Chickpea and Spinach Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed 3 cups fresh spinach 1 onion, sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 tablespoon lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Sauté Onion and Garlic: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, cooking until translucent. Add Chickpeas: Stir in the chickpeas and cook for 3-4 minutes until heated through. Incorporate Spinach: Add spinach to the pan, cooking until wilted. Season with lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Serve: Serve warm over rice or in a wrap. 4. Peanut Butter Tofu Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 block firm tofu, cubed 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, zucchini) 3 tablespoons peanut butter 2 tablespoons soy sauce 1 tablespoon maple syrup 1 tablespoon vegetable oil Instructions: Cook Tofu: In a skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add cubed tofu and cook until golden. Prepare Sauce: In a bowl, mix peanut butter, soy sauce, and maple syrup until smooth. Stir-Fry Vegetables: Add mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for 5 minutes. Combine with Sauce: Pour the peanut sauce over the tofu and vegetables, mixing until well combined. Serve hot. 5. Sweet and Sour Tempeh Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 block tempeh, cubed 1 cup pineapple chunks 1 bell pepper, sliced 1 onion, sliced 3 tablespoons sweet and sour sauce 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Instructions: Cook Tempeh: Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add tempeh and cook until browned. Add Vegetables: Stir in onion and bell pepper, cooking for about 5 minutes. Incorporate Pineapple: Add pineapple chunks and sweet and sour sauce, cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes. Serve: Serve over rice or quinoa for a filling meal. 6. Garlic Mushroom and Asparagus Stir-Fry Ingredients: 2 cups mushrooms, sliced 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut 3 cloves garlic, minced 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Sauté Garlic: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute. Cook Mushrooms: Add sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown. Add Asparagus: Toss in the asparagus and cook for another 5 minutes. Season with balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Serve: Serve as a side dish or over grains for a complete meal. 7. Coconut Curry Vegetable Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 can coconut milk 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cauliflower) 2 tablespoons curry powder 1 tablespoon vegetable oil Fresh cilantro, for garnish Instructions: Cook Vegetables: Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mixed vegetables and sauté for 5 minutes. Add Coconut Milk: Pour in the coconut milk and stir in curry powder. Simmer for 10 minutes until the vegetables are tender. Garnish: Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro. 8. Lemon Basil Stir-Fry Ingredients: 2 cups broccoli florets 1 bell pepper, sliced 1 cup snap peas 1 tablespoon lemon juice Fresh basil, chopped 2 tablespoons olive oil Instructions: Sauté Vegetables: In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add broccoli, bell pepper, and snap peas. Sauté for 5 minutes. Add Lemon Juice: Stir in lemon juice and fresh basil, cooking for an additional 2 minutes. Serve: This stir-fry pairs beautifully with quinoa or rice. 9. Miso Eggplant Stir-Fry Ingredients: 2 eggplants, cubed 1 bell pepper, sliced 2 tablespoons miso paste 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 3 tablespoons water Green onions, for garnish Instructions: Cook Eggplant: Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add cubed eggplant and sauté until soft. Add Bell Pepper: Stir in bell pepper and cook for another 3-4 minutes. Make Miso Sauce: In a bowl, mix miso paste with water until smooth. Pour over the eggplant and bell pepper, cooking for an additional 2 minutes. Serve: Garnish with green onions and serve over rice. 10. Zucchini Noodle Stir-Fry Ingredients: 2 medium zucchinis, spiralized 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 bell pepper, sliced 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 tablespoon pesto Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Sauté Vegetables: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add cherry tomatoes and bell pepper, cooking for 3-4 minutes. Add Zucchini Noodles: Toss in the spiralized zucchini and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until just tender. Finish with Pesto: Stir in pesto, seasoning with salt and pepper. Serve immediately. These ten easy vegan stir-fry recipes are perfect for busy weeknights, providing a variety of flavors and nutrients in a short amount of time. Whether you prefer something classic, spicy, or creamy, there’s a stir-fry here for everyone. Get creative with your favorite vegetables and sauces, and enjoy the process of cooking a delicious and healthy meal! ...
