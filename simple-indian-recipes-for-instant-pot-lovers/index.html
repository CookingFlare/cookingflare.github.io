Simple Indian Recipes for Instant Pot Lovers


The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook. Gone are the days of waiting for hours for a slow-cooked dish to finish. With the Instant Pot, you can cook a wide variety of dishes in a fraction of the time. And when it comes to Indian cuisine, the Instant Pot is a godsend. You can cook tender lamb curries, fluffy basmati rice, and rich lentil soups in just a few minutes. In this post, we'll share some simple Indian recipes that are perfect for Instant Pot lovers. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced cook, these recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and impress your family and friends. Chana Masala: A North Indian Classic Chana Masala, a popular North Indian dish, is a staple of Indian cuisine. It's a flavorful stew made with chickpeas, onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices. Here's a simple recipe to make Chana Masala in your Instant Pot. Ingredients:

1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon ginger paste 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1 teaspoon garam masala powder 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper 1/2 teaspoon salt 2 tablespoons tomato puree 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 2 cups water Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

Press the "Saute" button on your Instant Pot and heat the oil. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger paste. Cook until the onion is translucent. Add the cumin, coriander, garam masala, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute. Add the tomato puree, chickpeas, salt, and water. Close the lid and set the valve to "Sealing". Press the "Manual" button and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Let the pressure release naturally. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with basmati rice or naan.

Palak paneer: Creamy Spinach Curry Palak paneer, a creamy spinach curry, is a favorite among Indian food lovers. It's made with paneer (Indian cheese), spinach puree, and a blend of spices. Here's a simple recipe to make Palak paneer in your Instant Pot. Ingredients:

1 cup paneer, cubed 2 cups fresh spinach leaves 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon ginger paste 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1 teaspoon garam masala powder 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper 1/2 teaspoon salt 2 tablespoons butter 2 tablespoons heavy cream Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

Press the "Saute" button on your Instant Pot and heat the butter. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger paste. Cook until the onion is translucent. Add the cumin, coriander, garam masala, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute. Add the spinach leaves and cook until wilted. Add the paneer, salt, and heavy cream. Close the lid and set the valve to "Sealing". Press the "Manual" button and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes. Let the pressure release naturally. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with basmati rice or naan.

Dal Makhani: Black Lentil Curry Dal Makhani, a popular Punjabi curry, is made with black lentils and kidney beans. It's a rich and creamy dish that's perfect for special occasions. Here's a simple recipe to make Dal Makhani in your Instant Pot. Ingredients:

1 cup black lentils 1 cup kidney beans 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon ginger paste 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1 teaspoon garam masala powder 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper 1/2 teaspoon salt 2 tablespoons butter 2 tablespoons heavy cream Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

Press the "Saute" button on your Instant Pot and heat the butter. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger paste. Cook until the onion is translucent. Add the cumin, coriander, garam masala, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute. Add the lentils, kidney beans, salt, and water. Close the lid and set the valve to "Sealing". Press the "Manual" button and cook on high pressure for 20 minutes. Let the pressure release naturally. Add the heavy cream and stir well. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with basmati rice or naan.

Chicken Tikka Masala Chicken Tikka Masala, a popular Indian dish, is made with marinated chicken cooked in a creamy tomato sauce. It's a loved dish among Indian food lovers. Here's a simple recipe to make Chicken Tikka Masala in your Instant Pot. Ingredients:

1 pound boneless chicken breast or thighs 1/2 cup plain yogurt 2 tablespoons lemon juice 2 teaspoons garam masala powder 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper 1/2 teaspoon salt 2 tablespoons butter 2 cups tomato puree 2 cups water Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

Press the "Saute" button on your Instant Pot and heat the butter. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger paste. Cook until the onion is translucent. Add the cumin, coriander, garam masala, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute. Add the tomato puree, salt, and water. In a separate bowl, mix together the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, garam masala, and cayenne pepper. Add the marinated chicken to the yogurt mixture and coat evenly. Add the chicken to the Instant Pot and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Let the pressure release naturally. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with basmati rice or naan.

Conclusion These simple Indian recipes are a great starting point for Instant Pot lovers. You can experiment with different spices and ingredients to create unique flavors. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced cook, the Instant Pot makes cooking Indian cuisine a breeze. Try these recipes and discover the magic of Instant Pot cooking. Tips and Variations:

For a thicker curry, add more tomatoes or heavy cream. For a spicier curry, add more cayenne pepper. For a vegetarian version, substitute chicken with paneer or tofu. For a vegan version with non-dairy alternatives.

Experiment with these recipes and find your own unique flavors. Happy cooking!
