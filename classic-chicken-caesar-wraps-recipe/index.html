Classic Chicken Caesar Wraps Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick, flavorful, and satisfying meal, a Chicken Caesar Wrap is the perfect choice. This classic wrap brings together tender grilled chicken, crisp lettuce, savory Caesar dressing, and Parmesan cheese, all wrapped in a soft tortilla. Whether you’re preparing a delicious lunch or a dinner that requires minimal effort, these wraps are ideal for busy weekdays or even weekend gatherings. Plus, they’re highly customizable, so you can make them exactly how you like.

In this recipe, we’ll guide you through every step to make your own homemade Chicken Caesar Wraps from scratch. We’ll also share tips on how to perfect the wrap and make it a standout meal. Let’s dive in!

Ingredients

For the Chicken Caesar Wraps, you’ll need a combination of fresh ingredients and some pantry staples. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Chicken

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon paprika (optional for a bit of spice)

For the Wraps

  • 4 large flour tortillas (or whole wheat, depending on your preference)
  • 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped (you can also use a pre-packaged Caesar salad mix for convenience)
  • ½ cup Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade – we’ll talk about making it from scratch below)
  • ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese (or more if you’re a cheese lover)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Optional Add-ins

  • Cherry tomatoes, halved (adds freshness and color)
  • Croutons (for an extra crunch)
  • Bacon bits (for that extra savory flavor)

Directions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by prepping your chicken breasts. Lightly pound them with a meat mallet or rolling pin to an even thickness, about ½ inch thick. This ensures the chicken cooks evenly and stays juicy.

Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat and add olive oil. Season both sides of the chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika. Once the pan is hot, place the chicken in and cook for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).

Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pan and let it rest for a few minutes. After resting, slice the chicken thinly against the grain. This makes the chicken more tender and easier to bite into.

Step 2: Prepare the Wraps

While the chicken is cooking, it’s time to prep the other ingredients. Start by washing and chopping the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces. If you’re using store-bought Caesar salad mix, simply open the package and use it as is.

In a small bowl, measure out the Caesar dressing. You can either use store-bought dressing or make your own at home. For a simple homemade dressing, combine mayonnaise, grated Parmesan cheese, anchovy paste (optional), garlic, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and a pinch of salt and pepper in a bowl. Stir well until everything is combined.

Step 3: Assemble the Wraps

Now that all your ingredients are ready, it’s time to assemble the wraps. Lay a tortilla flat on a clean surface. Start by adding a layer of romaine lettuce, ensuring it covers the entire center of the tortilla.

Next, add a generous amount of Caesar dressing on top of the lettuce. Be careful not to overdo it, as you want the dressing to coat everything lightly without making the wrap soggy. Sprinkle a bit of grated Parmesan cheese on top of the dressing for that extra cheesy goodness.

Place the sliced grilled chicken on top of the dressed lettuce, then fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly, ensuring the filling stays inside. Repeat the process with the remaining tortillas.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Once your wraps are assembled, you can slice each wrap in half for easy serving. Optionally, garnish with freshly chopped parsley and extra Parmesan cheese. If you like, you can also serve the wraps with a side of extra Caesar dressing for dipping, or even some homemade fries for a hearty meal.

These wraps are perfect for meal prep. They can be stored in the fridge for up to 2 days, making them a great option for quick lunches throughout the week. To keep them fresh, wrap them tightly in foil or parchment paper, or store them in an airtight container.

Tips for Making the Perfect Chicken Caesar Wrap

1. Grilled or Pan-Seared Chicken?

You can either grill the chicken or cook it in a pan, depending on your preference and available equipment. Grilling will add a smoky flavor to the chicken, while pan-searing allows you to achieve a crisp exterior. Both methods work well in this recipe, so feel free to choose based on what you have on hand.

2. Make It Your Own with Add-Ins

While the classic Chicken Caesar Wrap is delicious on its own, feel free to add extra ingredients to elevate the flavor. Cherry tomatoes add a burst of freshness, croutons bring extra crunch, and bacon bits introduce a savory element that pairs wonderfully with the Caesar dressing.

3. Use Fresh Tortillas

The texture of the tortilla makes a big difference in the wrap. Fresh, soft tortillas will hold up better to the fillings without tearing. If you prefer, you can even make your own tortillas at home for an extra special touch.

4. Control the Dressing

Store-bought Caesar dressing can be high in fat and calories, but you can control what goes into your dressing by making it yourself. Homemade dressing is easy to prepare and much healthier. Additionally, you can experiment with different dressings, such as a lighter yogurt-based version or a dairy-free alternative.

5. Add Protein Variety

While grilled chicken is the classic protein used in Caesar wraps, feel free to mix things up! You can use grilled shrimp, turkey slices, or even tofu for a vegetarian option. The Caesar dressing pairs well with almost any protein, making it versatile for various dietary preferences.

6. Toasting the Wrap

For extra flavor and texture, toast your wrap in a skillet for 1-2 minutes on each side after it’s been rolled. This adds a nice crispy layer to the tortilla and makes the wrap even more satisfying.

7. Perfect for Meal Prep

These Chicken Caesar Wraps are ideal for meal prep. Make a batch on Sunday and store them in the fridge for a quick lunch or dinner throughout the week. The flavors get even better as the wrap sits for a few hours, allowing the ingredients to marinate together.

Conclusion

Classic Chicken Caesar Wraps are a simple yet satisfying meal that can easily be customized to suit your tastes. With just a few ingredients, you can create a delicious wrap that’s perfect for lunch, dinner, or even a picnic. The combination of tender chicken, fresh lettuce, tangy Caesar dressing, and crunchy Parmesan cheese creates a delightful bite that everyone will love.

Whether you’re making these wraps for yourself or preparing them for a group, you’re sure to impress with this easy and tasty recipe. So, grab your tortillas and get ready to enjoy the perfect Chicken Caesar Wrap!
