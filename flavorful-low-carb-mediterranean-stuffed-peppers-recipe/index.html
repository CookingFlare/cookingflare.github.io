Flavorful Low Carb Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers Recipe


If you’re in the mood for a healthy and delicious meal that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors, then this recipe for low carb Mediterranean stuffed peppers is just what you need. Packed with nutritious ingredients and vibrant spices, these stuffed peppers are a delightful twist on a classic dish. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a flavorful and satisfying meal, these Mediterranean stuffed peppers are sure to become a new favorite in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound ground turkey (or chicken)
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Peppers

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
  • Place the peppers in a baking dish, cut-side up, and drizzle with a little olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Bake for 15 minutes to soften the peppers slightly.

2. Making the Filling

  • In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground turkey and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  • Add the minced garlic, cauliflower rice, dried oregano, and dried basil to the skillet. Cook for a few minutes until the cauliflower rice starts to soften.
  • Stir in the drained diced tomatoes, chopped Kalamata olives, and half of the chopped parsley. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

3. Stuffing the Peppers

  • Spoon the filling into the partially cooked bell peppers, dividing it equally among them. Press the filling down gently with the back of the spoon.
  • Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the tops of the stuffed peppers.

4. Baking

  • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake the stuffed peppers for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.

5. Serving

  • Once the stuffed peppers are cooked, remove them from the oven and sprinkle the remaining chopped parsley over the top.
  • Serve the Mediterranean stuffed peppers hot, garnished with an extra sprinkle of feta cheese and a drizzle of olive oil, if desired.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

The combination of ground turkey, cauliflower rice, and flavorful Mediterranean ingredients makes these stuffed peppers a wholesome and low-carb meal option. The vibrant colors and robust flavors of the Mediterranean-inspired filling create a tantalizing dish that’s perfect for a family dinner or for entertaining guests. This recipe is also versatile, allowing you to customize the ingredients to suit your taste preferences. You can easily make it vegetarian by swapping the ground turkey for chickpeas or lentils, making it suitable for a variety of dietary needs.

Final Thoughts:

These low carb Mediterranean stuffed peppers are a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients and bold flavors. They are a true crowd-pleaser and a fantastic way to incorporate the goodness of the Mediterranean diet into your meals. Whether you’re looking to cut back on carbs, increase your vegetable intake, or simply savor a delicious and nutritious dish, these stuffed peppers are a must-try. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor a taste of the Mediterranean with this delightful recipe!
