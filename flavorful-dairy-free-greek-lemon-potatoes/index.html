Flavorful Dairy-Free Greek Lemon Potatoes


Greek cuisine is known for its vibrant flavors and delicious combinations of ingredients. One classic Greek dish that never fails to impress is the flavorful dairy-free Greek lemon potatoes. These potatoes are not only packed with flavor but also make the perfect side dish for any meal. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make these delectable lemon potatoes without using any dairy products, ensuring that everyone can enjoy them.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 500 ml vegetable broth
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).

  2. In a large baking dish, add the peeled and quartered potatoes. Make sure they are evenly spread out.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.

  4. Pour the mixture over the potatoes, ensuring that they are coated evenly.

  5. Add the vegetable broth to the baking dish, making sure it covers about half of the potatoes.

  6. Cover the baking dish with foil and place it in the preheated oven. Bake for about 45 minutes.

  7. After 45 minutes, remove the foil and give the potatoes a gentle stir, making sure they are evenly coated with the cooking liquid.

  8. Increase the oven temperature to 230°C (450°F) and bake uncovered for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are golden and crispy.

  9. Once the potatoes are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  10. Serve the dairy-free Greek lemon potatoes garnished with fresh parsley. These potatoes are incredibly flavorful and pair well with a variety of main dishes.

Enjoy the Flavors of Greece!

These dairy-free Greek lemon potatoes are a delightful addition to any meal. The combination of the tangy lemon, aromatic garlic, and fragrant herbs creates a burst of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to elevate your weeknight dinner, these potatoes are the perfect choice.

Not only are these potatoes delicious, but they’re also dairy-free, making them suitable for individuals with lactose intolerance or those following a dairy-free diet. The absence of dairy products does not compromise the flavor or texture of the dish. In fact, the lemon and herbs provide a refreshing and zesty twist that takes these potatoes to a whole new level.

So, the next time you’re looking for a side dish that adds a touch of Mediterranean flair to your meal, give these flavorful dairy-free Greek lemon potatoes a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
