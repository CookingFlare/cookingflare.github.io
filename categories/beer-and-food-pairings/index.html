In this blog post, we will explore the art of creating the perfect homemade burgers and pairing them with the ideal beers to enhance your culinary journey. The Perfect Homemade Burger Ingredients: 1 pound of ground beef (80% lean) Salt and pepper 4 burger buns Your choice of cheese Lettuce Tomato slices Onion slices Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo) Pickles Optional: bacon, avocado Instructions: Start by shaping the ground beef into patties, ensuring they are slightly larger than the buns as they will shrink during cooking. Season the patties generously with salt and pepper on both sides. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side for a medium-rare doneness, adjusting the time based on your preference. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute until they are slightly charred. Assemble your burgers by placing a patty on the bottom bun, adding cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and any other toppings you desire. Spread condiments on the top bun and place it on top of the burger. Serve hot and enjoy the deliciousness!
