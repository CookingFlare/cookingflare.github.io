Flavorful Pesto Pasta with Roasted Cherry Tomatoes


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying pasta dish that combines the vibrant flavors of pesto and roasted cherry tomatoes? Look no further! This flavorful pesto pasta with roasted cherry tomatoes recipe is the perfect choice for a quick and easy weeknight dinner or a special occasion meal. With its blend of fresh ingredients and aromatic herbs, this dish is sure to impress both your taste buds and your dinner guests.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of your favorite pasta (linguine, spaghetti, or fusilli work well)
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the halved cherry tomatoes on a baking sheet, drizzle with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast the tomatoes in the oven for about 15 minutes or until they start to blister and release their juices. Set aside.

  2. While the cherry tomatoes are roasting, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.

  3. In a food processor or blender, combine the minced garlic, basil leaves, pine nuts, grated Parmesan cheese, and the remaining 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Process the ingredients until smooth and well combined. If the pesto is too thick, you can add a little more olive oil to achieve your desired consistency.

  4. In a large skillet, heat the extra-virgin olive oil over medium heat. Add the cooked pasta and roasted cherry tomatoes to the skillet, tossing gently to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the prepared pesto sauce until the pasta is evenly coated. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

  6. Serve the flavorful pesto pasta with roasted cherry tomatoes hot, garnished with some fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • If you want to add some protein to this dish, you can toss in cooked chicken breast or grilled shrimp.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the pasta before serving.
  • If you prefer a creamier pesto sauce, you can add a dollop of cream or a spoonful of Greek yogurt to the sauce while blending.
  • Substitute the pine nuts with walnuts or almonds for a different twist on the pesto sauce.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of pasta, such as whole wheat or gluten-free options, to suit your dietary preferences.

This flavorful pesto pasta with roasted cherry tomatoes is a versatile and satisfying dish that is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy weeknight meal or an impressive dish to serve at a dinner party, this recipe has got you covered. The combination of fresh basil, roasted cherry tomatoes, and fragrant pesto will transport your taste buds to a whole new level of culinary delight. So, grab your apron and get ready to enjoy this mouthwatering pasta dish!
