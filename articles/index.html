A Taste of the Tropics: Gluten-Free Mango Salsa Chicken

Are you ready to take your taste buds on a tropical getaway? This Gluten-Free Mango Salsa Chicken recipe is a burst of vibrant flavors that will transport you to a sunny beach with every bite. It’s not only delicious but also packed with healthy ingredients that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Ingredients For the Chicken: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 teaspoon paprika 1 teaspoon garlic powder Salt and pepper to taste For the Mango Salsa: 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 1 red bell pepper, diced 1 jalapeno, seeded and finely chopped 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped Juice of 2 limes Salt to taste For the Marinade: 1/4 cup olive oil Juice of 1 lime 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Chicken In a small bowl, mix the paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Rub the seasoning mixture evenly over the chicken breasts. Place the seasoned chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Step 2: Prepare the Marinade In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, minced garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure each breast is coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse. Step 3: Make the Mango Salsa In a medium bowl, combine the diced mangoes, red onion, red bell pepper, jalapeno, and cilantro. Squeeze the lime juice over the salsa and gently toss to combine. Add salt to taste and set aside. Step 4: Grill the Chicken Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Remove the marinated chicken from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for 10 minutes. Grill the chicken breasts for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through and have beautiful grill marks. Step 5: Serve Transfer the grilled chicken to a serving platter and spoon the vibrant mango salsa over the top. Garnish with additional fresh cilantro and lime wedges for an extra burst of flavor. Tips and Tricks For a touch of sweetness, you can add a drizzle of honey to the mango salsa. If you don’t have a grill, you can easily prepare the chicken in a skillet over medium-high heat. This recipe pairs perfectly with a side of coconut rice or a fresh green salad. The Tropical Experience Each bite of this Gluten-Free Mango Salsa Chicken is a celebration of tropical flavors. The juicy sweetness of ripe mangoes, the zing of lime juice, and the kick of jalapeno create a salsa that’s both refreshing and vibrant. Paired with perfectly grilled chicken, this dish is a harmonious blend of savory and sweet, making it an ideal choice for a summer gathering or a weeknight dinner. ...
