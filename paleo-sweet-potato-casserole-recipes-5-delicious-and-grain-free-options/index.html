Paleo Sweet Potato Casserole Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


If you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking for grain-free alternatives, sweet potato casseroles are a great option to satisfy your cravings. These recipes are not only delicious but also easy to make. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or just want a comforting meal, these grain-free sweet potato casseroles are sure to impress. So let’s dive right in and explore five mouthwatering recipes that will leave you wanting more.

1. Classic Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 cup pecans, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the sweet potatoes until tender, then drain and mash them in a large bowl.
  3. Add the melted coconut oil, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sea salt to the mashed sweet potatoes. Mix until well combined.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a greased casserole dish.
  5. Sprinkle the chopped pecans evenly over the top.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the casserole is heated through.

2. Maple Bacon Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/4 cup ghee, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the sweet potatoes until tender, then drain and mash them in a large bowl.
  3. Add the melted ghee, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, sea salt, and crumbled bacon to the mashed sweet potatoes. Mix until well combined.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a greased casserole dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is lightly browned and the casserole is heated through.

3. Pecan Streusel Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

For the pecan streusel topping:

  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup ghee, melted
  • 1/2 cup pecans, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the sweet potatoes until tender, then drain and mash them in a large bowl.
  3. Add the melted coconut oil, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sea salt to the mashed sweet potatoes. Mix until well combined.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a greased casserole dish.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the almond flour, coconut sugar, melted ghee, and chopped pecans. Mix until crumbly.
  6. Sprinkle the pecan streusel topping evenly over the sweet potato mixture.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the streusel is golden brown and the casserole is heated through.

4. Savory Herb Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ghee, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the sweet potatoes until tender, then drain and mash them in a large bowl.
  3. Add the melted ghee, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, sea salt, and black pepper to the mashed sweet potatoes. Mix until well combined.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a greased casserole dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is lightly browned and the casserole is heated through.

5. Coconut Curry Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the sweet potatoes until tender, then drain and mash them in a large bowl.
  3. Add the melted coconut oil, coconut milk, curry powder, turmeric, sea salt, and black pepper to the mashed sweet potatoes. Mix until well combined.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a greased casserole dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is lightly browned and the casserole is heated through.

Enjoy these delicious and grain-free sweet potato casseroles that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking for healthier alternatives, these recipes are sure to please your taste buds. Give them a try and let us know which one is your favorite!
