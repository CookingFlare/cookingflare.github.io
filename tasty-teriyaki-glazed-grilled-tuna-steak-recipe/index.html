Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Grilled Tuna Steak Recipe


Are you ready to take your grilling game to the next level? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to prepare a delicious teriyaki glazed grilled tuna steak that will leave your taste buds begging for more. The combination of succulent tuna, tangy teriyaki sauce, and a hint of smokiness from the grill is sure to impress your family and friends. So fire up the grill and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 4 tuna steaks (about 6-8 ounces each)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional garnish: sliced green onions and sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together soy sauce, mirin, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and sesame oil. This will be the delicious teriyaki glaze for your tuna steaks.

  2. Place the tuna steaks in a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag and pour the teriyaki glaze over them. Make sure the steaks are well coated. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for maximum flavor.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Make sure the grates are clean and lightly oiled to prevent sticking.

  4. Remove the tuna steaks from the marinade, allowing any excess glaze to drip off. Season both sides of the steaks with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Place the tuna steaks on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of the steaks. You want the tuna to be seared on the outside but still slightly pink in the center. Avoid overcooking as it can result in dry and tough fish.

  6. While the tuna is grilling, you can brush some of the leftover teriyaki glaze on the steaks for an extra burst of flavor. Be careful not to brush too much as the glaze can burn easily.

  7. Once the tuna steaks are cooked to your desired doneness, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in a tender and flavorful steak.

  8. Serve the grilled tuna steaks hot, garnished with sliced green onions and sesame seeds if desired. Pair them with steamed rice or a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of sriracha to the teriyaki glaze.
  • If you prefer a sweeter glaze, increase the amount of honey or add a tablespoon of brown sugar.
  • Experiment with different marinade times to find your preferred level of flavor. Just remember not to exceed 2 hours, as the acid in the marinade can start to break down the texture of the fish.
  • Don’t have a grill? No problem! You can also cook the tuna steaks in a hot skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat, following the same cooking times and instructions.

So there you have it – a tantalizing teriyaki glazed grilled tuna steak recipe that will make your next barbecue a true culinary success. The combination of flavors and textures in this dish is simply irresistible. Whether you’re a seafood lover or just looking to impress your guests, this recipe is a must-try. So fire up the grill, put on your apron, and get ready to enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal. Happy grilling!
