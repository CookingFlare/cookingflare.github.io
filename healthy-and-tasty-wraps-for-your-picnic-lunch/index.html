Healthy and Tasty Wraps for Your Picnic Lunch


Are you tired of the same old sandwiches for your picnic lunches? If you’re looking for a fresh and healthy alternative, then wraps are the way to go! Wraps are versatile, easy to make, and perfect for on-the-go meals. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious and nutritious wrap recipes that will make your picnic lunches a hit. So grab your picnic blanket and let’s get started!

1. Greek Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Whole wheat tortilla wraps

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken breast, diced cucumber, diced tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, Greek yogurt, chopped fresh dill, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly coated.
  3. Lay a whole wheat tortilla wrap flat on a clean surface.
  4. Spoon the chicken mixture onto the center of the tortilla.
  5. Fold the sides of the tortilla towards the center, then roll it up tightly.
  6. Slice the wrap in half and secure with toothpicks, if desired.
  7. Repeat with the remaining tortilla wraps and filling.
  8. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to pack for your picnic.

2. Veggie Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spinach or whole wheat tortilla wraps

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous amount of hummus onto a spinach or whole wheat tortilla wrap.
  2. Layer the shredded carrots, sliced bell peppers, sliced cucumbers, sliced red onions, chopped fresh parsley, salt, and pepper on top of the hummus.
  3. Roll up the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  4. Slice the wrap in half and secure with toothpicks, if desired.
  5. Repeat with the remaining tortilla wraps and filling.
  6. Enjoy immediately or pack for your picnic.

3. Buffalo Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Whole wheat tortilla wraps

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shredded chicken breast, buffalo sauce, ranch dressing, diced celery, and crumbled blue cheese.
  2. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly coated.
  3. Lay a whole wheat tortilla wrap flat on a clean surface.
  4. Spread a thin layer of shredded lettuce onto the tortilla.
  5. Spoon the chicken mixture onto the lettuce.
  6. Fold the sides of the tortilla towards the center, then roll it up tightly.
  7. Slice the wrap in half and secure with toothpicks, if desired.
  8. Repeat with the remaining tortilla wraps and filling.
  9. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to pack for your picnic.

Conclusion

These healthy and tasty wrap recipes are sure to elevate your picnic lunches. Whether you’re a meat lover or a vegetarian, there’s a wrap for everyone. The Greek Chicken Wrap offers a burst of Mediterranean flavors, while the Veggie Hummus Wrap is packed with fresh and crunchy vegetables. And if you’re a fan of spicy buffalo chicken, the Buffalo Chicken Wrap will satisfy your cravings. So next time you plan a picnic, think beyond sandwiches and try these delicious wraps instead. Your taste buds will thank you!
