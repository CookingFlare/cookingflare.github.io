Vegan Banana Pudding with Nilla Wafers and a Caramel Drizzle Recipe: A Creamy and Delicious Dessert


Are you craving a luscious, creamy dessert that’s completely vegan and bursting with banana flavor? Look no further! This Vegan Banana Pudding with Nilla Wafers and a Caramel Drizzle is a delightful twist on the classic southern dessert, offering a perfect combination of smooth, velvety pudding, crunchy Nilla wafers, and a decadent caramel drizzle. The best part? It’s entirely plant-based and cruelty-free, making it a guilt-free indulgence for all dessert lovers.

Ingredients

For the Pudding:

  • 4 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

For the Caramel Drizzle:

  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup coconut cream
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

For the Assembly:

  • 1 box of vegan Nilla wafers
  • Sliced bananas for layering

Instructions

1. Prepare the Pudding

  • In a saucepan, whisk together the mashed bananas, coconut milk, and almond milk over medium heat.
  • In a separate bowl, mix the cornstarch with a little water to create a slurry.
  • Add the cornstarch slurry, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt to the saucepan, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens into a pudding-like consistency.
  • Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

2. Make the Caramel Drizzle

  • In a small saucepan, combine the coconut sugar, coconut cream, and coconut oil over medium heat.
  • Stir continuously until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture begins to thicken.
  • Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt. Let it cool for a few minutes.

3. Assemble the Banana Pudding

  • In individual dessert cups or a large trifle dish, layer the vegan Nilla wafers, sliced bananas, and the creamy pudding.
  • Continue layering until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of pudding on top.

4. Add the Finishing Touch

  • Drizzle the caramel sauce over the top of the pudding, allowing it to cascade down the sides, creating a beautiful, delicious caramel masterpiece.

Serving and Enjoying

Once assembled, chill the vegan banana pudding in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld together and the pudding to set. When ready to serve, garnish with extra Nilla wafers and banana slices for a delightful presentation.

This Vegan Banana Pudding with Nilla Wafers and a Caramel Drizzle is perfect for any occasion, from casual family dinners to elegant gatherings. It’s a dessert that will satisfy both vegans and non-vegans alike, with its creamy texture, rich banana flavor, and indulgent caramel drizzle. Plus, it’s a great way to use up ripe bananas and impress your guests with a decadent, dairy-free treat.

So, if you’re looking for a show-stopping vegan dessert that’s easy to make and incredibly delicious, give this recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed!
