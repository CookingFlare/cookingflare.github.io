Greek Lemon Chicken Skewers: A Tangy and Protein-Packed Delight


Greek cuisine is known for its vibrant flavors and healthy ingredients, and these Greek Lemon Chicken Skewers are no exception. Bursting with tangy lemon, aromatic herbs, and tender chicken, this recipe is a true delight for both your taste buds and your health. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a flavorful meal, these skewers are the perfect choice.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup of extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.

  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss until they are well coated. Allow the chicken to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  3. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.

  4. Thread the marinated chicken onto the soaked wooden skewers, ensuring that each piece is secure.

  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char on the outside.

  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute and ensures maximum tenderness.

  7. Serve the Greek Lemon Chicken Skewers hot with a side of tzatziki sauce, lemon wedges, and a fresh Greek salad. The tangy flavor of the lemon-infused chicken pairs perfectly with the cool and creamy tzatziki, creating a harmonious balance of flavors.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a grill or grill pan, you can also cook these skewers in the oven. Simply preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and place the skewers on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add some sliced red onions and bell peppers to the skewers before grilling. The caramelized onions and charred peppers complement the lemony chicken beautifully.

  • If you’re following a low-carb or gluten-free diet, you can serve these skewers with a side of cauliflower rice or a Greek salad without the pita bread.

  • Feel free to adjust the amount of lemon juice and zest according to your personal taste preferences. If you prefer a milder lemon flavor, reduce the amount slightly. On the other hand, if you’re a lemon lover, you can add an extra squeeze of lemon juice for an even tangier result.

Final Thoughts

These Greek Lemon Chicken Skewers are a true taste of the Mediterranean. The combination of tangy lemon, fragrant herbs, and succulent chicken creates a dish that is not only delicious but also packed with protein. Whether you’re grilling them for a crowd or enjoying a quiet dinner at home, these skewers are sure to impress. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and savor the flavors of Greece with this delightful recipe. Opa!
