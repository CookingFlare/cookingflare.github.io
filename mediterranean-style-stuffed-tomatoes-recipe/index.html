Mediterranean Style Stuffed Tomatoes Recipe


The sweet, juicy flavor of fresh tomatoes is elevated in this Mediterranean Style Stuffed Tomatoes recipe. This mouth-watering dish is perfect for a flavorful addition to any summer table. Fresh herbs, succulent meat, and aromatic spices combine to create an explosion of flavors in every bite. Ingredients:

12 large, ripe tomatoes 1 pound ground lamb or beef 1/4 cup finely chopped onion 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup cooked rice 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint 2 tbsp olive oil 1 tsp salt 1 tsp black pepper 1/2 tsp ground cumin 1/2 tsp smoked paprika 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (optional)

Instructions:

Prepare the Filling: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minutes until fragrant. Add the chopped onion and cook for 3-4 minutes until it's translucent. Add the Meat and Spices: Add the ground lamb or beef and cook, breaking the meat into small pieces, until browned. Stir in salt, black pepper, cumin, smoked paprika, and red pepper flakes. Cook for 1 minute. Let the mixture cool completely. Stuffed Tomatoes: Cut the top from each tomato and carefully hollow out the inside, taking care not to pierce or break the flesh. Be mindful of the delicate nature of the tomato skin to avoid damaging it. Place the hollowed-out tomatoes on a baking sheet and sprinkle with salt. Mix the Filling: In a large bowl, combine the cooked meat mixture, cooked rice, chopped herbs, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper. Spoon the mixture evenly among the tomatoes until they're filled to the brim. If using, place a spoonful of shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the filling to melt beautifully in the oven. Bake in the Oven: Wrap the baking sheet with aluminum foil to keep the tops-of the tomatoes moist and let them bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and let the tomatoes bakes for an additional 30 minutes to caramelize the tops and allow the flavors to meld together. Serving: Serve with your choice of summer sides, such as grilled or sliced vegetables, salads, or a side of creamy feta sauce.

Nutrition Information:

Per serving (4 tomatoes): Calories: 220, Fat: 9g, Saturated Fat: 3g, Cholesterol: 40mg, Sodium: 280mg, Carbohydrates: 25g, Dietary Fiber: 3g, Sugars: 10g, Protein: 15g.
