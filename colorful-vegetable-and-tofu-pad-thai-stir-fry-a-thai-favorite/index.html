Colorful Vegetable and Tofu Pad Thai Stir Fry: A Thai Favorite


If you’re craving a burst of flavors and a rainbow of colors on your plate, then this Colorful Vegetable and Tofu Pad Thai Stir Fry is the perfect dish for you. This Thai favorite is a delightful combination of vibrant vegetables, protein-packed tofu, and the classic Pad Thai flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re a fan of Thai cuisine or simply looking to add more colorful and nutritious meals to your repertoire, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

To create this tantalizing dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 8 oz. rice noodles
  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded purple cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped peanuts
  • Lime wedges for garnish

For the Pad Thai Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha (or to taste)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Rice Noodles: Boil the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  2. Crispy Tofu: In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden and crispy on all sides. Once done, remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Pad Thai Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, tamarind paste, and sriracha. Set the sauce aside.

  4. Stir-Fry the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Sauté the minced garlic until fragrant. Add the sliced red and yellow bell peppers, shredded purple cabbage, and shredded carrots. Stir-fry the vegetables until they are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine the Ingredients: Add the cooked rice noodles, crispy tofu, bean sprouts, and the Pad Thai sauce to the skillet. Gently toss everything together until well combined and heated through.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle the chopped green onions and peanuts over the stir-fry. Serve the Colorful Vegetable and Tofu Pad Thai Stir Fry with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrusy flavor.

Why You’ll Love It

This Colorful Vegetable and Tofu Pad Thai Stir Fry is not only a feast for the eyes but also a nourishing and satisfying meal. The vibrant vegetables provide an array of essential vitamins and minerals, while the tofu adds a healthy dose of plant-based protein. The Pad Thai sauce brings a perfect balance of sweet, tangy, and spicy flavors, making each bite a delightful culinary experience.

This recipe is also incredibly versatile. Feel free to customize it with your favorite vegetables or add a protein of your choice, such as shrimp, chicken, or more tofu. You can also adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less sriracha to suit your taste preferences.

So, if you’re looking to add a burst of color and flavor to your dinner table, give this Colorful Vegetable and Tofu Pad Thai Stir Fry a try. It’s a celebration of wholesome ingredients and vibrant tastes that will leave you craving for more. Enjoy!
