Tangy Paleo Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers


Welcome to another delicious recipe on our cooking blog! Today, we’re bringing you a tangy and flavorful dish that is perfect for those following a paleo diet – Tangy Paleo Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers. These skewers are not only incredibly easy to make, but they are also packed with zesty flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional, for some extra heat)
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, dried oregano, sea salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir well to combine and create a tangy marinade for the shrimp.

  2. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the marinade and toss until each shrimp is well coated. Allow the shrimp to marinate for at least 10 minutes to let the flavors develop.

  3. While the shrimp is marinating, preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. This will ensure that the shrimp cook evenly and develop a nice char.

  4. Once the shrimp has marinated, thread them onto the soaked wooden skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each shrimp. This will allow for even cooking and easy flipping.

  5. Place the shrimp skewers on the preheated grill or grill pan and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp as they can become rubbery.

  6. Once the shrimp are cooked through, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a minute before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure maximum flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Tangy Paleo Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers are incredibly versatile and can be served in a variety of ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve the skewers as an appetizer at your next gathering. They are sure to be a hit!
  • Pair the shrimp skewers with a fresh green salad for a light and satisfying meal.
  • Serve the shrimp on a bed of cauliflower rice for a complete paleo-friendly dinner.
  • Use the grilled shrimp to make a delicious shrimp taco by wrapping them in lettuce leaves and topping them with your favorite salsa and guacamole.

Conclusion

There you have it – a simple and delicious recipe for Tangy Paleo Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers. This dish is not only packed with tangy flavors but is also paleo-friendly, making it a great option for those following a specific diet. So fire up your grill and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you! Enjoy!
