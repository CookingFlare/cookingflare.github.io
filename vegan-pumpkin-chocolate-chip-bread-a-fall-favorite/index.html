Vegan Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Bread: A Fall Favorite


As the leaves begin to change and the air turns crisp, there’s no better time to indulge in the warm, comforting flavors of fall. And what better way to embrace the season than with a slice of Vegan Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Bread? This delightful treat combines the earthy sweetness of pumpkin with the rich, decadent taste of chocolate, creating a perfect fusion of flavors that will satisfy your autumn cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves.

  3. In a separate large bowl, mix together the pumpkin puree, granulated sugar, brown sugar, applesauce, vegetable oil, almond milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Gently fold in the vegan chocolate chips, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra crunch, try adding a handful of chopped walnuts or pecans to the batter.
  • If you’re a fan of spice, you can increase the amount of cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves for a more robust flavor.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of chocolate chips, such as dark chocolate or even vegan white chocolate, to customize the bread to your liking.

Serving Suggestions:

This Vegan Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Bread is perfect for breakfast, a midday snack, or a cozy dessert. Pair it with a steaming cup of coffee, a dollop of dairy-free whipped cream, or a scoop of vegan vanilla ice cream for an indulgent treat that will warm your soul on a chilly autumn day.

Conclusion:

With its moist, tender crumb and irresistible combination of pumpkin and chocolate, this Vegan Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Bread is sure to become a seasonal staple in your kitchen. Whether you’re hosting a fall brunch, gathering with friends for a cozy evening in, or simply looking for a delightful way to celebrate the flavors of autumn, this bread is a must-try for any vegan or non-vegan alike. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let the aroma of warm spices and sweet pumpkin fill your home with the magic of the season.
