Springtime Brunch Bread: Homemade Recipes for a Memorable Morning

There’s something special about a leisurely springtime brunch. The sun is shining, the birds are singing, and the air is filled with the scent of blooming flowers. It’s the perfect time to gather with friends and family and indulge in a delicious spread of homemade treats. And what better way to elevate your springtime brunch than with a freshly baked, aromatic loaf of brunch bread? Lemon Blueberry Brunch Bread Ingredients: 2 cups all-purpose flour 1 cup granulated sugar 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted 2 large eggs 1 cup plain Greek yogurt Zest of 1 lemon 1 tablespoon lemon juice 1 cup fresh blueberries Instructions: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan and set aside. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In a separate bowl, mix the melted butter, eggs, Greek yogurt, lemon zest, and lemon juice until well combined. Gently fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, being careful not to overmix. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. The delightful combination of zesty lemon and juicy blueberries makes this brunch bread a perfect addition to any springtime gathering.
