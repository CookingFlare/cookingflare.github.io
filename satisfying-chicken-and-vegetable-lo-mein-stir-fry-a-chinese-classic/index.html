Satisfying Chicken and Vegetable Lo Mein Stir Fry: A Chinese Classic

3 min · 559 words

Are you craving a delicious and satisfying Chinese dish that you can make right at home? Look no further than this mouthwatering Chicken and Vegetable Lo Mein Stir Fry recipe! Packed with flavor and loaded with healthy ingredients, this classic Chinese dish is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of lo mein noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the lo mein noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large wok or skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken breast and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the cooked chicken from the wok and set aside.

  3. In the same wok, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir-fry for about 1 minute, until fragrant.

  4. Add the sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, broccoli florets, and shredded carrots to the wok. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the low-sodium soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, and chicken broth until well combined.

  6. Pour the sauce mixture into the wok with the vegetables. Stir well to coat the vegetables evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.

  7. Add the cooked lo mein noodles and cooked chicken back into the wok. Toss everything together until the noodles and chicken are coated with the sauce and heated through.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  9. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this delicious Chicken and Vegetable Lo Mein Stir Fry hot, straight from the wok. It makes a satisfying main course on its own, but you can also pair it with steamed rice or egg rolls for a complete Chinese feast.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite vegetables. Snow peas, bean sprouts, or baby corn would make great additions.
  • For a vegetarian version, omit the chicken and add tofu or extra vegetables.
  • If you prefer a spicier stir fry, add some red pepper flakes or a dash of Sriracha sauce.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in a skillet or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

In just a few simple steps, you can recreate the flavors of your favorite Chinese takeout dish right in your own kitchen. This Satisfying Chicken and Vegetable Lo Mein Stir Fry is a classic that never disappoints. With its vibrant colors, tender chicken, and crisp vegetables, it’s a dish that will satisfy both your taste buds and your hunger. So why wait? Gather your ingredients and get cooking!

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and making the recipe your own. Feel free to adjust the ingredients and seasonings according to your taste preferences. Enjoy this Chinese classic and savor every bite. Happy cooking!
