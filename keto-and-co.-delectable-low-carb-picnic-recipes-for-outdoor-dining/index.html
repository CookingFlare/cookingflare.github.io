Keto and Co.: Delectable Low-Carb Picnic Recipes for Outdoor Dining


Nothing quite beats a scrumptious picnic under the sun, and this summer, whether you are lounging in a park, enjoying a beach day, or exploring scenic trails, indulge in a guilt-free feast with irresistible low-carb picnic recipes that are perfect for the Keto and Co. lifestyle. Say goodbye to unsatisfying meal choices and hello to mouth-watering dishes that satisfy your cravings while helping you maintain a low-carb diet. Let’s dive into a picnic of flavors and variety that’ll make your outdoor dining experience one to remember!

1. Grilled Chicken Satay

wherever you are, this versatile dish will have you grilling up a storm. Marinate chicken skewers in a blend of almond flour, coconut aminos, ginger, and garlic for a tasty briefcase-ready appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 tbsp almond flour
  • 2 tbsp coconut aminos
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together almond flour, coconut aminos, ginger, garlic, lime juice, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring each piece is well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill on medium heat. Thread the chicken onto skewers, discarding any remaining marinade.
  4. Grill the chicken skewers for 8-10 minutes, turning frequently, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Let the skewers rest for a few minutes, then serve hot or at room temperature.

2. Caprese Salad in a Jar

A refreshing and hassle-free option for the picnic basket, the Caprese salad-in-a-jar makes for a delightful appetizer that showcases the simplicity and beauty of fresh ingredients. This colorful and low-carb meal captures the essence of a traditional Caprese salad in a convenient and easy-to-pack jar.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette
  • 4 (12-ounce) mason jars

Instructions:

  1. Divide the mozzarella cubes evenly among the four mason jars, pressing down slightly.
  2. Top the mozzarella layer with the cherry tomatoes, halved, and then layer the fresh basil leaves on top.
  3. Drizzle the salads with the balsamic vinaigrette, distributing it evenly between the four jars.
  4. Pack the jars in a cooler or insulated bag, ensuring they stay chilled until served. Enjoy the salad as is, or give it a gentle stir before taking a fork to the bottom.

3. Kale and Avocado Salad Wraps

Portion-control never tasted so delicious with these nutritious kale and avocado salad wraps. Hearty kale leaves are wrapped around a fresh, zesty salad, and then rolled tightly in slices of creamy avocado â€“ a tasty and satisfying meal packed with healthy fats and fiber.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large kale leaves
  • 1 avocado, sliced into 1/4 inch thickness
  • 1/2 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and the dressing, tossing well to coat.
  3. Place a kale leaf on a cutting board and carefully fold it in half. Place a small mound of the salad mixture at the base, and then layer a slice of avocado on top.
  4. Carefully roll the kale leaf tightly around the salad and avocado, ensuring that the “wrap” stays secure. Repeat with the remaining ingredients, and enjoy immediately. Store any extra kale leaves in a sealed plastic bag to preserve freshness.

4. Lemon-Dijon Grilled Shrimp Skewers

Nothing shouts summer like a picnic-friendly grilled shrimp skewer! Marinated in a zesty combination of lemon, Dijon mustard, and garlic, these succulent shrimp make for a flavorsome and satisfying low-carb option that’s sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together lemon juice, Dijon mustard, garlic, olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade, ensuring each piece is well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill on medium heat. Thread the shrimp onto skewers, discarding any remaining marinade.
  4. Grill the shrimp skewers for 2-3 minutes per side, until the shrimp turns pink and is cooked through. Allow the skewers to rest for a few minutes before serving.

5. Sugar-Free Lemonade

A refreshing libation to quench your thirst on a hot summer day, sugar-free lemonade makes for the perfect pairing with any of these low-carb picnic recipes. This zesty drink contains just two grams of carbohydrates per serving, making it an irresistible and guilt-free treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp granulated erythritol
  • 1 cup sparkling water

Instructions:

  1. In a pitcher, combine water and freshly squeezed lemon juice, stirring well.
  2. Add granulated erythritol and stir until completely dissolved.
  3. Top off the pitcher with sparkling water, stirring gently.
  4. Serve over ice, garnished with fresh lemon slices or any other low-carb garnishes of your choice.

There you have it, five scrumptious, low-carb recipes to bring a Keto-approved twist to your outdoor dining experience. These picnic-friendly dishes make it easy to enjoy delicious, wholesome meals without sacrificing flavor or variety. So pack your picnic basket, grab your trusty cooler, and head out for a satisfying and enjoyable low-carb dining adventure. Happy picnicking!
