Keto Zucchini and Tomato Tart: A Low-Carb and Healthy Main Course


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy main course that fits perfectly into your low-carb keto diet? Look no further! This Keto Zucchini and Tomato Tart is bursting with flavor and nutrients, making it the ideal dish for a satisfying and guilt-free meal. With a crispy almond flour crust, creamy cheese filling, and vibrant zucchini and tomato topping, this tart is sure to impress even the most discerning taste buds.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

For the filling:

  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the topping:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, thinly sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the almond flour, melted butter, salt, and garlic powder for the crust. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  3. Press the crust mixture into the bottom of a tart pan, making sure to evenly distribute it across the surface. Use your fingers or the back of a spoon to press the mixture firmly.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  5. While the crust is cooling, prepare the filling. In a separate bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, mayonnaise, dried Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined and smooth.

  6. Once the crust has cooled, spread the cream cheese filling evenly over the surface.

  7. Arrange the sliced zucchinis and tomatoes on top of the filling, alternating between the two to create an attractive pattern. Drizzle the olive oil over the vegetables, and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Place the tart in the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized.

  9. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor.

  10. Slice and serve the Keto Zucchini and Tomato Tart as a main course or alongside a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

This Keto Zucchini and Tomato Tart is a fantastic main course option for those following a low-carb keto diet. With its crispy almond flour crust, creamy cheese filling, and colorful vegetable topping, it’s a feast for both the eyes and the taste buds. Not only is it delicious, but it’s also packed with essential nutrients and fits perfectly into a healthy lifestyle. So why not give it a try and indulge in a guilt-free and flavorful meal?

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation, so feel free to customize this recipe to your liking. Add some extra herbs or spices to the filling, or even swap out the zucchinis and tomatoes for your favorite low-carb vegetables. The possibilities are endless! Enjoy your culinary adventure and savor every bite of this delectable Keto Zucchini and Tomato Tart.
