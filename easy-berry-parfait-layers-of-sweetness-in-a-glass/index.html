Easy Berry Parfait: Layers of Sweetness in a Glass


Indulge in the delightful combination of fresh berries, creamy yogurt, and crunchy granola with this easy and refreshing berry parfait recipe. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a healthy dessert, this colorful treat is a feast for the eyes and the taste buds. Let’s dive into the layers of sweetness in a glass!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup granola
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Berries: Wash the berries thoroughly and pat them dry with a paper towel. If using strawberries, remove the stems and slice them into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Mix the Yogurt: In a mixing bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, honey, and vanilla extract. Stir well until the mixture is smooth and well-combined. Set aside.

  3. Layer the Parfait: Grab your favorite glass or dessert dish and start layering! Begin with a spoonful of the yogurt mixture at the bottom of the glass, followed by a layer of mixed berries. Sprinkle a spoonful of granola on top of the berries, creating a delicious crunch. Repeat the layers until you reach the top of the glass, finishing with a dollop of yogurt and a sprinkle of granola.

  4. Garnish and Chill: For an extra touch of freshness, add a few mint leaves on top of the parfait. Cover the glass with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the parfait to chill.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once chilled, remove the parfait from the refrigerator and serve immediately. Grab a spoon, dig in, and savor every luscious bite of this Easy Berry Parfait. The contrasting textures of the creamy yogurt, juicy berries, and crunchy granola will leave you wanting more!

Tips and Variations

  • Customize with Your Favorite Berries: Feel free to use your favorite berries or a combination of different fruits. Blackberries, cherries, or even sliced bananas would make a delicious addition to this parfait.

  • Get Creative with Toppings: While this recipe calls for granola, you can experiment with other toppings such as chopped nuts, coconut flakes, or even a drizzle of melted chocolate for an indulgent twist.

  • Make it Vegan: If you prefer a dairy-free option, substitute the Greek yogurt with coconut or almond yogurt. Use maple syrup instead of honey as a vegan-friendly sweetener.

  • Layer it in a Jar: For a portable and Instagram-worthy presentation, layer the parfait in a mason jar. This makes it easy to grab and go for a quick and healthy snack on the move.

  • Try Different Flavors: Switch up the flavor profile by adding a teaspoon of lemon zest or a sprinkle of cinnamon to the yogurt mixture. These simple additions can elevate the taste and provide a unique twist to your Easy Berry Parfait.

Enjoy the burst of flavors and textures in every spoonful of this Easy Berry Parfait. It’s a delectable treat that can be enjoyed any time of the day. Whether you’re making it for yourself, your family, or to impress guests at a brunch gathering, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So, gather your ingredients, layer your parfait, and indulge in the layers of sweetness in a glass!
