Chai Spice Pull-Apart Bread: A Warm Treat


Elevate your snack time with this delightful Chai Spice Pull-Apart Bread that sets your taste buds alight with its warm and welcoming flavors. A culinary fusion of Indian chai spices and classic American soft bread, this scrumptious creation caters to both worlds, seamlessly merging the two flavors into a comforting and irresistible treat.

Why settle for mundane snacks when you can tantalize your taste buds with a scrumptious Chai Spice Pull-Apart Bread?

Ingredients You Will Need

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 Tbsp heavy cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 3/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 3/4 tsp ground cardamom
  • Vegetable oil for brushing

Preparation & Instructions

In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

Heat the milk, heavy cream, and melted butter in a small saucepan over low heat until the mixture is warm and the butter has melted. Stir in the cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cardamom – our chai spice blend. Allow the mixture to cool until it reaches room temperature.

Now, in a separate bowl, mix the eggs and then pour it into the flour mixture. Add the warm milk mixture and stir until well combined.

At this point, let the dough rest for about 10 minutes, as this will make the dough easier to work with later.

Next, in a lightly greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, layer dough by spreading a third of the dough mixture on the bottom. Then make little ridges to create “tunnels” for the bread to pull apart later.

Repeat this process until all the dough is used up.

At last, brush a thin layer of vegetable oil over the dough, cover the pan with a clean towel and put the pan in a warm place to rise. Allow the bread to rise for about 45 minutes or until doubled in size.

Preheat the oven to 350°F and bake the Chai Spice Pull-Apart Bread for approximately 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown.

Take the loaf out of the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, gently pull apart the individual pieces of bread.

Serving Suggestions

Enjoy the Chai Spice Pull-Apart Bread on its own, alongside a warm beverage, or get creative by incorporating it into a delicious spiced butter or caramel drizzle.
