Instant Pot Lasagna: A Quick Twist on a Classic Recipe


When it comes to comfort food, lasagna is undeniably a favorite in households worldwide. Its layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella feel like a warm hug on a plate. However, making traditional lasagna often requires significant time and effort – boiling the noodles, meticulously layering, and baking it to perfection. For many busy home cooks, this can be a daunting task. That’s where the Instant Pot steps in – simplifying the process without compromising flavor. This Instant Pot lasagna recipe is fast, easy, and just as satisfying as the oven-baked variety.

If you’re looking for a quicker way to make lasagna, while still delivering on taste and texture, you’re in the right place. Let’s dive into how to effortlessly create this quick twist on a traditional classic.

Why Make Lasagna in the Instant Pot?

You might be wondering why you should even consider making lasagna in an Instant Pot. After all, we’re used to the oven being the go-to method for this dish. The Instant Pot is perfect for those who want to streamline the process while keeping flavors intact. It offers:

  • Speed: Traditional lasagna can take well over an hour from start to finish, especially if you’re making meat sauce from scratch. The Instant Pot dramatically cuts down cooking time with its pressure-cooking capabilities.
  • Convenience: Building lasagna in the Instant Pot is incredibly easy, and there’s no need to pre-boil the pasta noodles.
  • Minimal Cleanup: Instead of using multiple pots, pans, and baking dishes, everything cooks right in the Instant Pot, making cleanup a breeze.
  • Consistent Results: The pressure-cooking environment ensures evenly cooked layers with perfectly melted cheese every time.

Ingredients You’ll Need

For the Meat Sauce:

  • Ground Meat: 1 lb of ground beef, pork, turkey, or a combination – your choice depends on personal preference or dietary needs.
  • Onion & Garlic: 1 onion and 3 cloves of garlic, finely minced. These aromatics build flavor in your sauce.
  • Tomato Sauce: 2 cups of tomato sauce or canned crushed tomatoes for a rich, hearty base.
  • Tomato Paste: 2 tablespoons for deeper flavor.
  • Seasonings: 1 teaspoon each of dried basil, oregano, and parsley; ½ teaspoon of red pepper flakes for a subtle kick (optional).
  • Salt & Pepper: Adjust to taste.

For the Ricotta Layer:

  • Ricotta Cheese: 1 cup, mixed until smooth. You can substitute cottage cheese for a lower-calorie option.
  • Egg: 1 large egg to bind the mixture together.
  • Parmesan Cheese: ¼ cup, freshly grated for added flavor.
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 1 cup, shredded and mixed in with the ricotta.

For the Layers:

  • Lasagna Noodles: 6-8 no-boil lasagna noodles.
  • Mozzarella Cheese: An additional cup to sprinkle on top.
  • Parsley: Fresh parsley for garnish (optional).

For Assembly:

  • Water: 1 to 1½ cups to create enough steam in the Instant Pot.
  • Cooking Spray or Oil: To prevent sticking.

Step-by-Step Instructions: Instant Pot Lasagna

Step 1: Prepare the Meat Sauce

Turn the Instant Pot to sauté mode and allow it to heat up. Add a tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the minced onion and garlic until fragrant, about 2-3 minutes. Add the ground meat and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spatula as it cooks. Drain excess grease if needed.

Next, stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, and seasonings. Taste and adjust the salt and pepper. Allow the mixture to come to a simmer. Once the sauce is fully combined and aromatic, turn off the sauté mode and set the sauce aside.

Step 2: Make the Ricotta Mixture

In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese. Stir until smooth. Fold in the shredded mozzarella to ensure an even distribution. This creamy mixture will add depth to your lasagna layers.

Step 3: Assemble the Lasagna

Start by spraying the Instant Pot with cooking spray or lightly brushing it with oil to prevent sticking. Place the trivet into the pot, but do not add water yet.

Begin layering the lasagna directly inside a round oven-safe dish that will fit snugly in the Instant Pot. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of the dish.
  2. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce, breaking them as necessary to cover the dish.
  3. Add a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  4. Spoon another layer of meat sauce over the ricotta, followed by another layer of noodles.

Repeat these steps until you reach the top, finishing with meat sauce and an even sprinkle of shredded mozzarella cheese.

Step 4: Prepare the Instant Pot

Pour 1 to 1½ cups of water into the Instant Pot, creating enough steam for pressure cooking. Place the dish with the assembled lasagna on the trivet. Cover the top of the dish loosely with aluminum foil to prevent water from dripping onto the lasagna during cooking.

Lock the Instant Pot lid into place, ensuring the pressure valve is sealed. Set to high pressure for 20 minutes.

Step 5: Cook and Release Pressure

Once the cooking cycle completes, allow the pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes. Afterward, carefully use quick release to vent any remaining pressure. Remove the lasagna from the Instant Pot and allow it to rest for 5-10 minutes, as this will help the layers firm up for slicing.

Serving and Storing Instant Pot Lasagna

Remove the lasagna from its oven-safe dish and plate individual slices. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added flavor. Serve with garlic bread or a crisp green salad for a complete meal.

Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container and refrigerated for up to three days. This dish also freezes well, making it perfect for meal prep.

To reheat, you can microwave portions or warm them gently in the oven, covered with foil to prevent drying out.

Pro Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Lasagna

  1. Use No-Boil Noodles: No-boil noodles are ideal for Instant Pot cooking, as they absorb just the right amount of liquid and cook evenly.
  2. Layer Strategically: Avoid adding too much ricotta mixture in each layer – keeping the layers thin ensures a good balance of flavors.
  3. Cheese Varieties: Feel free to experiment with other cheeses like provolone or sharp cheddar to switch up the flavor profile.
  4. Customize Your Sauce: Add veggies like bell peppers, mushrooms, or spinach to boost nutrition. Alternatively, use store-bought marinara sauce for ultra convenience.
  5. Use the Right Dish Size: Ensure your dish fits comfortably inside the Instant Pot, allowing the lid to close securely.

Why This Recipe Works

The Instant Pot method revolutionizes how we approach lasagna, providing speed and simplicity without skimping on the comforting qualities that make this dish a classic. The pressure cooking locks in moisture, ensuring that every layer—from the tender noodles to the creamy ricotta and melty mozzarella—perfectly melds together.

Making lasagna in the Instant Pot allows busy families and individuals to enjoy the familiar flavors of this Italian-inspired dish without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re looking to whip up a quick weeknight meal or impress guests with minimum effort, this recipe is sure to become a go-to in your repertoire.

So why wait? Grab those ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and discover the magic of quick and flavorful lasagna.
