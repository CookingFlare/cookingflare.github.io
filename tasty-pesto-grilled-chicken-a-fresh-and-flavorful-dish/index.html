Tasty Pesto Grilled Chicken: A Fresh and Flavorful Dish


Grilled chicken is a classic dish that never fails to impress. It’s easy to prepare, delicious, and versatile, allowing you to experiment with different flavors and seasonings. One of my favorite variations of grilled chicken is the Tasty Pesto Grilled Chicken. This dish combines the vibrant flavors of pesto with the smoky goodness of grilled chicken, creating a mouthwatering and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the pesto sauce.

  2. In a food processor or blender, combine the basil leaves, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and minced garlic. Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped.

  3. While the food processor is running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture forms a smooth and creamy sauce. Add the lemon juice, salt, and pepper, and pulse a few more times to combine.

  4. Set aside about 1/4 cup of the pesto sauce for serving, and use the rest to marinate the chicken breasts. Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish and pour the pesto sauce over them, making sure to coat each piece evenly. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight in the refrigerator.

  5. Once the chicken has marinated, remove it from the dish and discard any excess marinade. Place the chicken breasts on the preheated grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).

  6. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute and ensures a tender and juicy result.

  7. Serve the Tasty Pesto Grilled Chicken with the reserved pesto sauce on the side. You can also add a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook the chicken in a grill pan or bake it in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through.
  • Feel free to customize the pesto sauce by adding other herbs like parsley or cilantro, or by incorporating different types of nuts such as walnuts or almonds.
  • This dish pairs well with a variety of side dishes, such as grilled vegetables, roasted potatoes, or a fresh green salad.

So there you have it - Tasty Pesto Grilled Chicken, a fresh and flavorful dish that will surely become a favorite in your household. The combination of the aromatic pesto sauce and the charred smokiness of the grilled chicken creates a harmony of flavors that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply looking for a quick and tasty weeknight meal, this recipe is a winner. Give it a try and let your taste buds rejoice!
