Irresistible Strawberry Rhubarb Pie Recipe


If there’s one pie that captures the essence of summer, it’s the irresistible combination of sweet strawberries and tangy rhubarb baked into a flaky, golden crust. This classic pie is a celebration of seasonal produce at its finest, and its vibrant flavors and colors make it a standout dessert for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this strawberry rhubarb pie recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to indulge in the taste of summer.

Ingredients

For the Pie Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 3 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 3 cups fresh rhubarb, chopped
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • 1 tablespoon coarse sugar (for sprinkling)

Instructions

Making the Pie Crust:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add the cold, cubed butter to the flour mixture.
  3. Using a pastry cutter or your hands, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough begins to come together.
  5. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Preparing the Filling:

  1. In a large bowl, gently toss together the sliced strawberries, chopped rhubarb, granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until well combined.

Assembling and Baking the Pie:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disk of the chilled pie dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to a 9-inch pie dish, gently pressing it into the bottom and up the sides.
  4. Pour the prepared strawberry rhubarb filling into the pie crust, spreading it out evenly.
  5. Roll out the second disk of pie dough and place it over the filling. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  6. Brush the top crust with the beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse sugar.
  7. Using a sharp knife, make a few small vents in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  8. Place the pie on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes.
  9. Reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C) and continue baking for an additional 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  10. Allow the pie to cool completely before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This delectable strawberry rhubarb pie pairs beautifully with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of freshly whipped cream. The contrast of the warm, fruity filling against the cool, creamy accompaniment is a match made in dessert heaven. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of nostalgia, this pie is sure to impress your family and friends.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the irresistible strawberry rhubarb pie is a delightful ode to the flavors of summer. From its buttery, flaky crust to the luscious, sweet-tart filling, every bite is a symphony of textures and tastes. Whether you’re a fan of fruit pies or looking to expand your baking repertoire, this recipe is a surefire way to infuse your kitchen with the essence of the season. So, roll up your sleeves, embrace the abundance of summer produce, and treat yourself to a slice of this irresistible pie.

Feel free to try out this delightful recipe and share the joy of summer with your loved ones. The burst of freshness from the strawberries and the zing of rhubarb will make this pie a favorite at any gathering. Happy baking!
