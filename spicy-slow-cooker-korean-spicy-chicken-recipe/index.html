Spicy Slow Cooker Korean Spicy Chicken Recipe


Korean cuisine is known for its bold flavors and vibrant ingredients, and one dish that stands out is Spicy Korean Chicken. This recipe is not just about heat; it’s a perfect blend of spices, sweetness, and savory goodness. By using a slow cooker, you can easily infuse the chicken with flavor while allowing it to become tender and juicy. Get ready to impress your friends and family with this delightful dish that packs a punch!

Ingredients

To make the Spicy Slow Cooker Korean Spicy Chicken, you’ll need the following ingredients:

For the Chicken:

  • 2 lbs (about 900 grams) of chicken thighs (boneless and skinless)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (for browning)

For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey (or brown sugar)
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 inch ginger (grated)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • 2 green onions (sliced for garnish)

Optional Add-Ins:

  • 1 cup carrots (sliced)
  • 1 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1 cup onions (sliced)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Begin by trimming any excess fat from the chicken thighs. Pat them dry with a paper towel, then season both sides with salt and black pepper. This step is crucial as it builds the foundation of flavor for your dish.

Step 2: Sear the Chicken (Optional)

Although this step is optional, searing the chicken in a skillet enhances the flavor profile. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the chicken thighs and sear for about 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown. This process caramelizes the chicken and adds depth to the dish.

Step 3: Mix the Sauce

In a mixing bowl, combine the gochujang, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Whisk until the ingredients are well combined and smooth. The spicy, sweet, and savory notes in this sauce will elevate your chicken dish to new heights.

Step 4: Combine in the Slow Cooker

In your slow cooker, place the seared chicken thighs (or raw chicken if you skipped the searing step) at the bottom. If you’re using optional add-ins like carrots, bell peppers, and onions, layer them on top of the chicken. Pour the prepared sauce evenly over the chicken and vegetables.

Step 5: Slow Cook

Cover the slow cooker with its lid and set it to cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours. Cooking on low will yield the most tender chicken, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully. If you’re in a hurry, the high setting will still provide great results.

Step 6: Shred the Chicken

Once the cooking time is complete, carefully remove the chicken from the slow cooker. Using two forks, shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces. If you prefer a saucier dish, you can return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and mix it with the sauce.

Step 7: Serve

To serve, spoon the spicy chicken over a bed of steamed rice or noodles. Drizzle some of the cooking sauce over the top for added flavor. Garnish with sliced green onions and a sprinkle of sesame seeds for an appealing finish.

Tips for the Perfect Spicy Korean Chicken

  1. Adjust the Heat: If you’re sensitive to spice, start with half the amount of gochujang and gradually increase to your preferred heat level. You can also add a splash of water or chicken broth to dilute the spice.

  2. Make It Ahead: This dish is perfect for meal prep. You can prepare the chicken and sauce a day in advance and let it sit in the refrigerator overnight. The next day, simply dump it all in the slow cooker and let it do its magic.

  3. Pair it Right: This spicy chicken goes wonderfully with sides like kimchi, pickled vegetables, or a refreshing cucumber salad to balance the heat.

  4. Storage: Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop, adding a splash of water to keep it moist.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Spicy Slow Cooker Korean Chicken recipe is a game-changer for busy weeknights or gatherings with friends and family. The slow cooking process not only tenderizes the chicken but also allows the flavors to deepen and develop. It’s an effortless way to enjoy a restaurant-quality dish right at home without spending hours in the kitchen.

With its unique blend of spices and the convenience of a slow cooker, this dish offers versatility and satisfaction. Whether you’re a fan of Korean cuisine or looking to explore new flavors, this recipe is sure to please your palate.

Conclusion

There you have it—an easy and delicious Spicy Slow Cooker Korean Chicken recipe that will bring excitement to your dinner table. Make it your own by adjusting the spice levels and adding your favorite vegetables. This dish is not only a feast for the senses but also a wonderful way to explore the rich flavors of Korean cooking. So grab your slow cooker and get ready to savor the deliciousness that awaits!
