Treat Yourself with a Gluten-Free Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie


Indulge in the rich and decadent flavors of this gluten-free chocolate peanut butter pie. With its smooth and creamy filling, buttery crust, and irresistible combination of chocolate and peanut butter, this dessert is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply looking for a delicious treat, this pie is a must-try. So grab your apron and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups gluten-free graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped

For the chocolate ganache:

  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

For the topping:

  • Whipped cream
  • Chopped peanuts
  • Chocolate shavings

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the gluten-free graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated with the butter and sugar. Press the mixture into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie dish to form the crust. Place the crust in the refrigerator to chill while you prepare the filling.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the peanut butter, powdered sugar, softened butter, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. This may take a few minutes, so be patient. Once the mixture is well combined, gently fold in the whipped cream until fully incorporated.

  3. Remove the pie crust from the refrigerator and pour the peanut butter filling into the crust, spreading it evenly with a spatula. Smooth the top with the back of a spoon or an offset spatula. Return the pie to the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 2 hours, or until the filling is set.

  4. While the pie is chilling, prepare the chocolate ganache. In a microwave-safe bowl, heat the heavy cream until hot but not boiling. Pour the hot cream over the chocolate chips and let it sit for a minute to allow the chocolate to melt. Stir until the mixture is smooth and glossy.

  5. Remove the pie from the refrigerator and pour the chocolate ganache over the peanut butter filling. Use a spatula to spread the ganache evenly, covering the entire surface of the pie. Return the pie to the refrigerator and let it chill for another hour to allow the ganache to set.

  6. Once the pie is fully chilled and set, remove it from the refrigerator. Top with a generous dollop of whipped cream and sprinkle with chopped peanuts and chocolate shavings for added texture and decoration.

  7. Slice and serve this delightful gluten-free chocolate peanut butter pie to satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your guests. Enjoy the creamy and luscious combination of flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Note: This pie is best served chilled and can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Make sure to cover it tightly with plastic wrap to keep it fresh.

Conclusion

Indulge in the heavenly flavors of this gluten-free chocolate peanut butter pie. With its buttery crust, creamy filling, and luxurious chocolate ganache, this dessert is a true delight. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply treating yourself, this pie is the ultimate crowd-pleaser. So go ahead, take a slice, and savor every bite. You deserve it!
