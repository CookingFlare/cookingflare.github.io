Vegetarian Pad See Ew Noodle Dish


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and looking for a flavorful and satisfying vegetarian dish, then Vegetarian Pad See Ew Noodles should be at the top of your must-try list. This dish is a popular street food in Thailand known for its wide rice noodles, savory sauce, and crisp vegetables. The best part? It’s super easy to make at home and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Let’s dive into the world of Vegetarian Pad See Ew and learn how to create a mouthwatering version right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz wide rice noodles
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as broccoli, bell peppers, and snap peas)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crushed peanuts and lime wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Noodles: Start by soaking the wide rice noodles in hot water according to the package instructions until they are soft but still slightly firm. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and vegetable broth. This will be the flavorful sauce that ties the whole dish together.

  3. Stir-Fry the Vegetables: Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Toss in the mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender yet still crisp.

  4. Cook the Eggs: Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and pour the lightly beaten eggs into the empty space. Let them cook for a minute or until they start to set. Scramble the eggs gently until cooked through.

  5. Combine Everything: Add the soaked rice noodles to the pan along with the prepared sauce. Toss everything together until the noodles are well coated in the sauce and the vegetables are evenly distributed.

  6. Season and Serve: Season the Pad See Ew with salt and pepper to taste. Divide the noodles among serving plates and garnish with crushed peanuts and lime wedges for an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Enjoy: Your delicious Vegetarian Pad See Ew Noodles are now ready to be enjoyed. Serve hot and savor the wonderful blend of textures and flavors in every bite.

Tips for Success:

  • Customize Your Veggies: Feel free to use your favorite vegetables in this dish. Bok choy, mushrooms, and carrots are great additions.
  • Add Tofu: For an extra protein boost, consider adding cubed tofu to the stir-fry.
  • Spice it Up: If you like a bit of heat, sprinkle some chili flakes or add a dash of sriracha to your noodles.

Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting your culinary journey, this Vegetarian Pad See Ew Noodle recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more. Experiment with different ingredients, make it your own, and enjoy a taste of Thailand right in your own home. Happy cooking!

Remember, the key to a successful Vegetarian Pad See Ew lies in fresh ingredients, a hot wok, and a dash of creativity. Dive into the world of Thai cuisine and elevate your home cooking game with this delectable dish.
