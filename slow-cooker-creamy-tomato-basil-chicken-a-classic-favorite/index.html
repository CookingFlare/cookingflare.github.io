Slow Cooker Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken: A Classic Favorite


Are you looking for a comforting and delicious meal that practically cooks itself? Look no further than this Slow Cooker Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken recipe. It’s a classic favorite that never fails to impress with its rich flavors and creamy texture. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want to enjoy a home-cooked meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Slow Cooker: Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker and season with salt and pepper.

  2. Mix the Sauce: In a mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dried basil, and dried oregano. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Pour the Sauce Over the Chicken: Pour the creamy tomato basil sauce over the chicken in the slow cooker, making sure to coat the chicken evenly.

  4. Cook Slowly: Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 4-5 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Once the chicken is ready, serve it over cooked pasta or rice. Drizzle some of the creamy tomato basil sauce over the chicken and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a burst of color and flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For a thicker sauce, you can whisk together 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water and stir it into the sauce during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  • Feel free to add a splash of white wine to the sauce for an extra layer of flavor.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the chicken before serving.

Why Slow Cooker Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken?

This recipe is a crowd-pleaser for several reasons. Not only does it require minimal effort, but it also fills your kitchen with the irresistible aroma of tomatoes and basil as it simmers in the slow cooker. The creamy sauce, combined with the tender chicken, creates a comforting and satisfying dish that never fails to impress.

Conclusion

Slow Cooker Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken is a timeless recipe that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this dish is sure to be a hit. With its simple preparation and delectable flavors, it’s no wonder that this classic favorite continues to be a go-to recipe for home cooks everywhere.
