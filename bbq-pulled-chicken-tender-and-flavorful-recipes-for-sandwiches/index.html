BBQ Pulled Chicken: Tender and Flavorful Recipes for Sandwiches


If you’re a fan of barbecue flavors and love a good sandwich, then you’re in for a treat with these mouthwatering BBQ pulled chicken recipes. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout, planning a family picnic, or just craving a hearty and satisfying meal, these recipes are sure to hit the spot. From classic BBQ pulled chicken sandwiches to creative twists on the traditional favorite, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So, fire up your grill and get ready to savor the deliciousness of BBQ pulled chicken in every bite!

Classic BBQ Pulled Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hamburger buns or sandwich rolls

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the barbecue sauce, chicken broth, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Stir until well mixed.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the slow cooker and coat them with the sauce mixture.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  4. Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, mixing it with the sauce.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns or sandwich rolls, if desired, and spoon the BBQ pulled chicken onto the bottom half of each bun.
  6. Serve with your favorite coleslaw or pickles on the side and enjoy!

Spicy Chipotle BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup spicy barbecue sauce
  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 tablespoon adobo sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Slider buns
  • Sliced red onion and avocado for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the spicy barbecue sauce, minced chipotle pepper, adobo sauce, ketchup, apple cider vinegar, honey, and cumin.
  3. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper, then grill for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  4. Once the chicken is done, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
  5. Shred the chicken using two forks and toss it in the chipotle BBQ sauce mixture.
  6. Place a spoonful of the spicy chipotle BBQ pulled chicken onto each slider bun, and top with sliced red onion and avocado.
  7. Serve these spicy sliders at your next gathering and watch them disappear in no time!

Tangy Pineapple BBQ Pulled Chicken Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken tenders
  • 1 1/2 cups pineapple barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup diced pineapple
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce for assembly

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the chicken tenders in a baking dish and pour the pineapple barbecue sauce over them, making sure they are evenly coated.
  3. Scatter the diced pineapple, red bell pepper, and red onion over the chicken.
  4. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  6. Use two forks to shred the chicken and mix it with the pineapple and vegetables in the sauce.
  7. Warm the flour tortillas and assemble the wraps by placing a generous amount of the tangy pineapple BBQ pulled chicken in the center of each tortilla, topping with chopped cilantro and shredded lettuce, then folding in the sides and rolling them up.
  8. Slice the wraps in half and serve for a delightful lunch or dinner option.

Whether you prefer the classic BBQ flavor, a spicy kick, or a tropical twist, these BBQ pulled chicken recipes are guaranteed to elevate your sandwich game. So, gather your ingredients, get your grill ready, and treat yourself to the irresistible taste of tender and flavorful BBQ pulled chicken. Your taste buds will thank you!
