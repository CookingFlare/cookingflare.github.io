Quick and Easy Mini Sliders for Game Day


When it comes to game day, there’s nothing quite like a platter of delicious sliders to keep everyone fueled and satisfied. These mini burgers are not only quick and easy to make, but they are also a crowd-pleaser, making them the perfect addition to your game day spread. Whether you’re hosting a party at home or heading to a tailgate, these quick and easy mini sliders are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns
  • 6 slices of cheddar cheese, cut into quarters
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • Pickles for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Beef

Start by preheating a skillet over medium-high heat. While the skillet is heating up, season the ground beef with salt and pepper. Once the skillet is hot, form the seasoned ground beef into small patties, roughly the size of the slider buns. Cook the patties in the skillet for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness.

Step 2: Assemble the Sliders

While the beef patties are cooking, you can start preparing the slider buns. Cut the slider buns in half and place a quarter of a slice of cheddar cheese on the bottom half of each bun. Once the beef patties are cooked, place them on top of the cheese, allowing the residual heat to melt the cheese slightly.

Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

In a small bowl, mix together the barbecue sauce, mayonnaise, and Dijon mustard. This creamy and tangy sauce will add a burst of flavor to the sliders.

Step 4: Saute the Onions

In the same skillet used to cook the beef patties, add a tablespoon of olive oil and the sliced onions. Saute the onions over medium heat until they are caramelized and golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.

Step 5: Assemble the Sliders (Continued)

Once the onions are caramelized, it’s time to assemble the sliders. Spread a generous dollop of the prepared sauce on the top half of each slider bun. Place a spoonful of caramelized onions on top of the beef patties, followed by a slice of tomato and a handful of shredded lettuce. Finally, top each slider with the other half of the bun.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Arrange the prepared sliders on a platter and serve them with a side of pickles for an extra kick of flavor. These quick and easy mini sliders are best enjoyed fresh and hot, making them the perfect addition to any game day gathering.

Conclusion

With their juicy beef patties, gooey melted cheese, and flavorful toppings, these quick and easy mini sliders are a game day essential. Whether you’re cheering on your favorite team at home or tailgating with friends, these sliders are sure to be a hit. Plus, with their simple preparation and delicious flavors, you can spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying the game. So, next time you’re planning a game day get-together, consider adding these mini sliders to your menu for a winning combination of flavor and convenience.
