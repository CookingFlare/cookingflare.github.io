The Best Sous Vide Recipe for Flank Steak


Sous vide cooking has revolutionized the way we cook steak, allowing for unparalleled precision and consistency. Among the many cuts of steak that can be cooked sous vide, flank steak is a popular choice for its rich flavor and tender texture. In this recipe, we’ll explore the best sous vide recipe for flank steak, ensuring a juicy and flavorful dish that’s sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 flank steaks (depending on size and number of servings)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup beef broth
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)

Equipment

  • Sous vide machine
  • Cutting board
  • Knife
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Tray or plate for cooking

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Steak

Preheat your sous vide machine to 130°F (54°C). Trim any excess fat from the flank steak, if necessary. Season the steak with salt, black pepper, and thyme. Place the steak on a cutting board and drizzle with olive oil, ensuring the entire surface is coated.

Step 2: Create the Marinade

In a small bowl, whisk together garlic, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and beef broth. If using red wine, add it to the marinade and whisk until combined.

Step 3: Marinate the Steak

Place the marinated steak in a ziplock bag or airtight container. Seal the bag or cover the container with plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight, turning the steak occasionally to ensure even marination.

Step 4: Cook the Steak

Remove the steak from the refrigerator and place it in the sous vide machine. Cook for 1-2 hours, depending on the desired level of doneness. For medium-rare, cook for 1 hour and 15 minutes. For medium, cook for 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Step 5: Finish the Steak

Once the cooking time is complete, remove the steak from the sous vide machine. If desired, sear the steak in a hot skillet with a small amount of oil for 1-2 minutes per side, or until a nice crust forms.

Step 6: Serve

Slice the flank steak against the grain and serve immediately. You can serve it with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh salad.

Tips and Variations

  • For a more intense flavor, increase the marination time to 4-6 hours or overnight.
  • To add a smoky flavor, add 1-2 tablespoons of liquid smoke to the marinade.
  • For a more tender steak, cook it to 120°F (49°C) and then finish it with a high-heat sear.
  • Experiment with different seasonings and herbs, such as paprika, cumin, or rosemary, to create unique flavor profiles.

Conclusion

With this sous vide recipe for flank steak, you’ll be able to achieve a tender, juicy, and flavorful dish that’s sure to impress. By following these simple steps and tips, you can elevate your cooking game and create a steakhouse-quality meal at home. So go ahead, give it a try, and experience the magic of sous vide cooking for yourself.
