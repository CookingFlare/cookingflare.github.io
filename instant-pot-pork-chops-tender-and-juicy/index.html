Instant Pot Pork Chops: Tender and Juicy


If you’re looking for a quick and easy way to prepare tender and juicy pork chops, then look no further than your Instant Pot. This versatile kitchen appliance is a game-changer when it comes to cooking meats, and pork chops are no exception. In this recipe, I’ll show you how to use your Instant Pot to create mouthwatering pork chops that are sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the pork chops with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper. Make sure to coat the pork chops evenly with the seasoning.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to the “Saute” function and add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, sear the pork chops for 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a golden-brown crust. This step will lock in the juices and flavor of the pork chops.

  3. Once the pork chops are nicely seared, remove them from the Instant Pot and set them aside on a plate.

  4. Deglaze the Instant Pot by pouring the chicken broth into the pot and using a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom. This step is crucial for preventing the “Burn” notice during the pressure cooking.

  5. Return the pork chops to the Instant Pot, placing them on top of the liquid. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” on high for 8 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes, then carefully perform a quick pressure release.

  7. Remove the pork chops from the Instant Pot and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This resting period allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring that each bite is succulent and flavorful.

  8. Serve the pork chops with your favorite sides, such as mashed potatoes, steamed vegetables, or a fresh salad. The tender and juicy pork chops will be the star of the meal.

Tips for Success

  • Use bone-in pork chops for the best flavor and juiciness.
  • Searing the pork chops before pressure cooking adds depth of flavor and texture.
  • Adjust the seasoning to suit your taste preferences. You can add a little kick with some cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes.
  • For a thicker sauce, you can create a slurry of cornstarch and water and add it to the cooking liquid after the pressure cooking is complete. Simply set the Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and stir in the slurry until the sauce thickens.

By following this simple yet effective recipe, you can elevate pork chops to a whole new level. The Instant Pot makes it possible to achieve tender and juicy pork chops with minimal effort, allowing you to enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal in no time. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.
