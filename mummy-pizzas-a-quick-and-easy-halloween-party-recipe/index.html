Mummy Pizzas: A Quick and Easy Halloween Party Recipe


Halloween is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with some spooky and delicious treats? If you’re looking for a quick and easy recipe that will impress your guests at your Halloween party, look no further than Mummy Pizzas. These adorable and tasty bites are perfect for both kids and adults, and they’re a breeze to make. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • Mini pizza crusts or English muffins
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Black olives, sliced
  • Red bell pepper, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. If you’re using mini pizza crusts, place them on the baking sheet. If you’re using English muffins, split them in half and place them on the baking sheet.

  3. Spread a generous amount of pizza sauce on each crust or muffin half, leaving a small border around the edges.

  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, covering the entire surface.

  5. Now it’s time to create the mummy effect! Take the sliced black olives and place them strategically on the cheese to resemble the mummy’s eyes. You can get creative here and make different expressions for each mummy.

  6. Next, take the thinly sliced red bell pepper and arrange them over the cheese to resemble the mummy’s bandages. Crisscross them and leave some gaps to give it an authentic mummy look.

  7. Once you’ve finished assembling all the mummy pizzas, pop them in the oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the mummy pizzas cool for a few minutes before serving. This will prevent any burnt tongues!

  9. Serve your Mummy Pizzas on a spooky platter and watch as your guests devour them. Don’t forget to enjoy one or two yourself!

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize your Mummy Pizzas by adding different toppings like sliced mushrooms, diced bell peppers, or even mini pepperoni for some extra spookiness.

  • If you want to make your Mummy Pizzas healthier, you can use whole wheat mini pizza crusts or English muffins. You can also use low-fat cheese or skip the cheese altogether and focus on the veggie toppings.

  • To make your Mummy Pizzas even more festive, you can serve them with a side of “blood” (tomato) dipping sauce.

  • If you’re hosting a Halloween party, consider setting up a DIY Mummy Pizza station where guests can create their own mummy masterpieces. Provide a variety of toppings and let their creativity shine!

  • Don’t limit yourself to just Halloween – Mummy Pizzas are a fun and delicious treat that can be enjoyed all year round. They’re perfect for kids’ birthday parties, movie nights, or even as an after-school snack.

So there you have it, a quick and easy recipe for Mummy Pizzas that will impress your guests and make your Halloween party a memorable one. With just a few simple ingredients and some creativity, you can create these adorable and tasty treats in no time. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, these Mummy Pizzas are sure to be a hit. Happy Halloween and happy cooking!
