Healthy Nut-Free Energy Bites Recipe


If you’re on the lookout for a wholesome and energizing snack that’s both delicious and nut-free, then you’re in for a treat! These healthy nut-free energy bites are the perfect solution for those with nut allergies or simply looking for a change from the typical nut-filled energy ball recipes. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with flavor, these energy bites are a fantastic on-the-go snack or a quick pick-me-up during a busy day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seed butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, shredded coconut, and dried cranberries. Stir well to ensure an even distribution of all the ingredients.

  2. Mix the Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the honey, sunflower seed butter, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt until smooth and well combined.

  3. Combine the Mixtures: Pour the wet ingredients over the dry ingredients and mix thoroughly until everything is evenly coated and starts to stick together.

  4. Chill the Mixture: Place the mixture in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to allow it to firm up slightly, making it easier to shape into balls.

  5. Form the Energy Bites: Once the mixture has chilled, take it out of the refrigerator and begin shaping it into small, bite-sized balls using your hands. Roll each portion between your palms until you have a round and compact energy bite.

  6. Store and Enjoy: Place the energy bites on a parchment-lined tray and refrigerate for at least an hour to set. Once they are firm, transfer them to an airtight container and store in the refrigerator for up to two weeks. Enjoy them as a quick snack or a boost of energy whenever you need it!

Benefits of Nut-Free Energy Bites:

  • Allergy-Friendly: Perfect for individuals with nut allergies or those in nut-free environments such as schools or offices.
  • Nutritious Ingredients: Packed with fiber, protein, and healthy fats from the seeds and oats, offering a satisfying and nourishing snack.
  • Convenient and Portable: Ideal for busy lifestyles, travel, or as a quick energy boost during outdoor activities.

These nut-free energy bites are a fantastic addition to any meal prep routine, offering a convenient and healthy snack option that the whole family will love. Whether you’re looking for a nut-free alternative or simply want to try something new, these energy bites are sure to become a favorite go-to snack. Enjoy the delicious combination of flavors and the sustained energy they provide throughout your day!

So, why not whip up a batch of these delightful nut-free energy bites and have a delicious, wholesome snack ready whenever hunger strikes?

Remember, these energy bites are not only tasty but also a great way to fuel your body with essential nutrients and keep you going strong throughout the day. Happy snacking!
