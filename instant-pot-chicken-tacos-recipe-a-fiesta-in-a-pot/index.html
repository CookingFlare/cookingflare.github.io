Instant Pot Chicken Tacos Recipe: A Fiesta in a Pot


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy weeknight dinner that will transport your taste buds to a Mexican fiesta, look no further than this Instant Pot Chicken Tacos recipe. With just a few simple ingredients and the magic of the Instant Pot, you can have a flavorful and tender chicken filling ready in no time. So grab your sombrero and get ready for a fiesta in a pot!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of lime juice
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, chopped cilantro, sour cream, and avocado

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a spice blend.

  3. Add the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot and sprinkle the spice blend evenly over the chicken. Use tongs or a spoon to coat the chicken with the spices.

  4. Pour the chicken broth and lime juice into the Instant Pot, ensuring that the chicken is fully submerged in the liquid.

  5. Secure the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 5 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.

  7. Remove the chicken breasts from the Instant Pot and use two forks to shred the meat.

  8. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and mix it with the cooking liquid to ensure that it’s well-coated.

  9. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or directly on the stovetop until they become pliable.

  10. Assemble your tacos by placing a scoop of the shredded chicken onto each tortilla. Top with your desired toppings, such as diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, chopped cilantro, sour cream, and avocado.

  11. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Tacos immediately and enjoy the fiesta in a pot!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier flavor, you can add a diced jalapeño or a pinch of cayenne pepper to the spice blend.
  • For a smoky twist, you can substitute the paprika with smoked paprika.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use store-bought rotisserie chicken and simply mix it with the spice blend and cooking liquid in the Instant Pot.
  • These Instant Pot Chicken Tacos are versatile and can be customized to your liking. Feel free to add your favorite toppings or experiment with different types of tortillas.

So there you have it, a simple and delicious Instant Pot Chicken Tacos recipe that will bring the flavors of Mexico to your table. Whether you’re hosting a fiesta or just craving a flavorful meal, this recipe is a winner. So fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy a fiesta in a pot!
