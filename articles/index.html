The Secret to a Great Watermelon Salad To create the perfect Watermelon and Feta Salad, you need to start with ripe, juicy watermelon. The watermelon should be heavy for its size, as this is an indication of its sweetness. When ripe, the watermelon should be rich in lycopene, a powerful antioxidant responsible for its characteristic pink color. You can use either the round or the oval variety of watermelon, but smaller, seedless melons work best. Next, choose a good-quality feta cheese. For this recipe, you'll want to use crumbled feta made from sheep's milk and aged for at least six months. Feta with a higher sheeps milk content tends to have a richer, creamier flavor, a key component of this salad. Cut the feta into small pieces, so it oozes its creamy goodness when mixed with the watermelon.
