Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Meatball Soup: Hearty and Delicious


There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting bowl of soup, especially when it’s packed with hearty ingredients and bursting with flavor. If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine and love the convenience of slow cooker recipes, then this Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Meatball Soup is a must-try for your next family dinner or cozy gathering with friends. This classic soup combines the richness of Italian flavors with the ease of slow cooking, resulting in a dish that’s both satisfying and incredibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups chopped spinach
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup small pasta (such as acini di pepe or orzo)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Meatballs

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, egg, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.

Step 2: Cook the Meatballs

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with cooking spray or olive oil.
  2. Add the meatballs to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides, about 6-8 minutes.
  3. Once browned, transfer the meatballs to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess fat.

Step 3: Prepare the Soup

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the chicken broth, chopped spinach, diced carrots, diced celery, diced onion, small pasta, dried basil, dried thyme, dried rosemary, and dried parsley.
  2. Gently stir to combine the ingredients.
  3. Add the cooked meatballs to the slow cooker.

Step 4: Slow Cook

  1. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours, until the vegetables are tender and the meatballs are cooked through.
  2. Season with additional salt and pepper, if needed.

Step 5: Serve

  1. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
  2. Serve hot and enjoy the hearty and delicious flavors of this Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Meatball Soup.

Tips and Variations

  • For a lighter option, you can use ground turkey instead of ground beef for the meatballs.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a can of crushed tomatoes to the slow cooker.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables to suit your taste, such as adding diced zucchini or bell peppers.
  • This soup freezes well, so you can make a big batch and store the leftovers for future meals.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply want to enjoy a comforting meal at home, this Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Meatball Soup is a versatile and satisfying dish that’s sure to please. The combination of tender meatballs, flavorful broth, and wholesome vegetables makes it a go-to recipe for any occasion. Plus, the convenience of using a slow cooker means you can effortlessly prepare this soup while attending to other tasks. So, gather your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and get ready to savor the heartwarming flavors of this Italian-inspired soup. Buon appetito!
