Cooking for Two: 15 Flavorful Chicken Recipes


Cooking for two can sometimes feel like a challenge, but with the right recipes, it can be a fun and rewarding experience. Chicken, with its versatility and mild flavor, is perfect for creating a variety of meals that cater to different tastes and preferences. Whether you’re in the mood for something simple and comforting or looking to experiment with new flavors, chicken is always a great option. Below are 15 delicious and easy-to-make chicken recipes that are ideal for a cozy dinner for two.

1. Garlic Butter Chicken Breast

This simple yet flavorful recipe combines the richness of garlic butter with juicy chicken breasts. The garlic butter adds a savory depth, making the chicken succulent and tender. Serve this dish with a side of roasted vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and thyme.
  2. In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add the garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 6-7 minutes per side, or until golden brown and fully cooked.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. Pour any remaining garlic butter over the chicken and sprinkle with fresh parsley before serving.

2. Honey Mustard Chicken Thighs

Honey mustard is a classic sauce that pairs perfectly with chicken. This recipe uses chicken thighs, which are a bit more forgiving and tend to stay juicy. The combination of honey, mustard, and a touch of garlic creates a sweet, tangy, and savory glaze that will have you coming back for more.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together honey, Dijon mustard, olive oil, and minced garlic.
  4. Place the chicken thighs in a baking dish and pour the honey mustard sauce over them.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Serve with roasted potatoes or sautéed greens for a satisfying meal.

3. Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

Grilled chicken is a great option for a quick and flavorful dinner. The marinade made with lemon, garlic, and fresh herbs infuses the chicken with a burst of fresh flavors, making each bite juicy and tender.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, preferably a few hours.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked and juices run clear.
  5. Serve with a light salad or grilled vegetables for a refreshing meal.

4. Chicken Piccata

Chicken Piccata is a delicious Italian-inspired dish with a tangy lemon and caper sauce that’s perfect for a romantic dinner. The chicken is pan-fried to golden perfection and then drenched in a sauce made with white wine, lemon, and capers.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Dredge them lightly in flour, shaking off any excess.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, until golden brown.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add white wine, lemon juice, and capers. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet and cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the sauce to reduce.
  6. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve with pasta or roasted vegetables.

5. Creamy Tuscan Chicken

For a rich and indulgent meal, try this creamy Tuscan chicken recipe. The creamy sauce, made with garlic, sun-dried tomatoes, and spinach, envelops the chicken and creates a decadent flavor combination.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook them for 6-7 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add garlic and sun-dried tomatoes. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken broth and heavy cream, stirring to combine. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add the spinach and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until wilted.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet and cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.
  6. Serve with mashed potatoes or pasta for a rich and comforting meal.

6. Crispy Baked Chicken Tenders

If you’re craving something crunchy and comforting, these baked chicken tenders are the way to go. They’re crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, with a simple but flavorful seasoning mix.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a shallow dish, combine breadcrumbs, Parmesan, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip the chicken strips into the beaten egg, then coat with the breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Place the chicken tenders on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Serve with a side of honey mustard or ranch dressing for dipping.

7. Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Chicken Alfredo is a classic comfort food that combines tender chicken with a rich and creamy Alfredo sauce. This recipe is quick to prepare and perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook them in a skillet with butter over medium heat for 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. Slice the chicken into thin strips.
  4. In the same skillet, add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Add heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, stirring until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce.
  6. Top with the sliced chicken and serve immediately.

8. Balsamic Glazed Chicken

For a tangy and slightly sweet twist, try balsamic glazed chicken. The balsamic vinegar creates a rich glaze that perfectly complements the savory flavor of the chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, honey, and olive oil.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the chicken breasts for 6-7 minutes per side, or until golden brown and fully cooked.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, pour in the balsamic mixture and cook for 2-3 minutes, until it thickens into a glaze.
  6. Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the chicken and serve with roasted vegetables or a fresh salad.

9. Chicken Parmesan

Chicken Parmesan is a beloved dish, and it’s surprisingly easy to make at home. This version is baked rather than fried, making it a healthier option that still retains all the classic flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix the breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese in a shallow dish.
  3. Dredge the chicken breasts in the breadcrumb mixture, coating both sides evenly.
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the chicken breasts for 3-4 minutes per side until lightly golden brown.
  5. Transfer the chicken to a baking dish, top with marinara sauce, and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Serve with spaghetti or a green salad for a complete meal.

10. Spicy Honey Garlic Chicken Wings

If you’re in the mood for something with a bit of a kick, these spicy honey garlic chicken wings will hit the spot. They’re sticky, sweet, and spicy all at once.

Ingredients:

  • 10-12 chicken wings
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the chicken wings with salt and pepper, then place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, Sriracha, and garlic.
  4. Brush the chicken wings with the honey garlic mixture and bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  5. Serve hot with a side of celery and blue cheese dressing.

11. Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

For an easy and hands-off dinner, try slow cooker chicken tacos. The chicken becomes tender and flavorful, perfect for filling soft tortillas and topping with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 8 small tortillas
  • Toppings: lettuce, cheese, salsa, sour cream, etc.

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker. Sprinkle the taco seasoning over the top and add the chicken broth.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
  3. Once cooked, shred the chicken with two forks.
  4. Serve the shredded chicken in tortillas with your favorite taco toppings.

12. Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick stir-fry is the perfect meal when you’re looking for something light but flavorful. This recipe combines chicken with colorful vegetables in a savory stir-fry sauce that’s quick to prepare and absolutely delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced thinly
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced thinly
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken and cook for 5-6 minutes, or until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add the garlic, bell pepper, broccoli, and carrot. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch mixture. Stir to coat everything evenly.
  5. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve with steamed rice for a complete meal.

13. Chicken Shawarma

Bring the flavors of the Middle East to your kitchen with this chicken shawarma recipe. The chicken is marinated in a blend of spices and then cooked until golden and crispy, creating a delicious and flavorful dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, cumin, paprika, turmeric, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rub the mixture all over the chicken thighs and let marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill or skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook the chicken for 5-6 minutes per side, until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  5. Serve with pita bread, cucumber, tomato, and a drizzle of tahini sauce.

14. Chicken Caesar Salad

A light yet satisfying meal, Chicken Caesar Salad is perfect for a healthy dinner. The creamy Caesar dressing paired with crispy chicken and fresh greens makes for a delicious and balanced meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill or pan-sear the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through.
  2. Slice the chicken thinly.
  3. Toss the lettuce with Caesar dressing and Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  4. Top with sliced chicken and croutons.
  5. Serve with a lemon wedge for added freshness.

15. Chicken Stir-Fry with Peanut Sauce

This flavorful stir-fry is packed with protein and veggies, making it a wholesome choice for dinner. The peanut sauce adds a creamy, nutty element that complements the chicken and vegetables beautifully.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for 5-6 minutes until browned and cooked through.
  2. Add the snap peas, bell pepper, and carrot to the skillet, cooking for another 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, and rice vinegar.
  4. Pour the peanut sauce over the chicken and vegetables and stir to coat evenly.
  5. Serve with steamed rice or noodles for a complete meal.

With these 15 chicken recipes, cooking for two has never been easier or more delicious. Whether you’re in the mood for something light and healthy or rich and comforting, these dishes offer a variety of flavors and cooking methods to suit any occasion. Try them all and discover your new favorite dinner for two!
