Elegant Strawberry and Cream Tart: A Dessert Showstopper


As the weather warms up, there’s nothing quite like a sweet and refreshing dessert to satisfy your cravings. In this recipe, we’ll be creating an elegant Strawberry and Cream Tart that’s sure to impress your guests and satisfy your taste buds. This dessert is perfect for spring and summer gatherings, and can be easily customized to fit your personal style.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup ice-cold water

For the filling:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

Make the Crust

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, confectioners’ sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter and use a pastry blender or your fingers to work it into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add the ice-cold water, stirring with a fork until the dough comes together in a ball. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Roll Out the Crust

On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Transfer the dough to a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom. Trim the edges of the dough and press it into the corners of the pan.

Bake the Crust

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line the crust with parchment paper and fill with pie weights or dried beans. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment paper and pie weights or beans. Continue baking for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the crust is lightly golden.

Make the Filling

In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, granulated sugar, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture is hot but not boiling. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and vanilla extract. Gradually add the hot cream mixture to the egg yolks, whisking constantly to prevent the eggs from scrambling. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

Assemble the Tart

Arrange the sliced strawberries in a pattern on top of the baked crust. Pour the cream filling over the strawberries, spreading it evenly to cover the fruit. Sprinkle the cornstarch and lemon juice over the filling.

Chill the Tart

Refrigerate the tart for at least 2 hours or overnight to allow the filling to set. Just before serving, sprinkle the tart with confectioners’ sugar.

Serve and Enjoy

Slice the tart into wedges and serve chilled. This elegant dessert is perfect for spring and summer gatherings, and is sure to impress your guests with its sweet and refreshing flavors.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the tart more elegant, you can garnish it with fresh mint leaves or edible flowers.
  • If you prefer a stronger strawberry flavor, you can use 3 cups of sliced strawberries.
  • You can also use other types of fruit, such as blueberries or raspberries, in place of the strawberries.
  • To make the tart more decadent, you can drizzle it with chocolate sauce or whipped cream.

By following this recipe, you’ll be able to create an elegant Strawberry and Cream Tart that’s sure to impress your guests and satisfy your taste buds. With its sweet and refreshing flavors, this dessert is perfect for spring and summer gatherings. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the oohs and aahs from your guests!
