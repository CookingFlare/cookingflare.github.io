Flavorful Slow Cooker Indian Butter Chicken Recipe


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this Flavorful Slow Cooker Indian Butter Chicken Recipe. This classic dish is known for its rich and creamy tomato-based sauce, tender chicken, and fragrant spices. And the best part? You can make it all in your slow cooker, allowing the flavors to meld together beautifully while you go about your day. So let’s dive right in and discover how to make this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, minced garlic, grated ginger, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground turmeric, paprika, cayenne pepper, and salt. Mix well to form a marinade.

  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and coat them thoroughly. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for the best flavor.

  3. In a large skillet, melt the ghee over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.

  4. Transfer the onion to the slow cooker and add the marinated chicken along with any remaining marinade. Stir to combine.

  5. Pour the crushed tomatoes over the chicken and stir again to evenly distribute the sauce.

  6. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours, or on high heat for 3-4 hours. The chicken should be tender and cooked through.

  7. Stir in the heavy cream and let it cook for an additional 15 minutes to allow the flavors to blend together.

  8. Serve the Indian Butter Chicken over steamed basmati rice or with warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a pop of color and added freshness.

Tips:

  • For an extra layer of flavor, toast the ground cumin, ground coriander, and ground turmeric in a dry skillet for a few minutes before adding them to the marinade.

  • If you prefer a smoother sauce, you can use an immersion blender to blend the sauce in the slow cooker before adding the heavy cream.

  • Adjust the level of spiciness by increasing or decreasing the amount of cayenne pepper.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors will continue to meld together, making it even more delicious the next day.

So there you have it, a Flavorful Slow Cooker Indian Butter Chicken Recipe that will transport your taste buds to the streets of India. The tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices make this dish a true crowd-pleaser. Give it a try and experience the magic of Indian cuisine in the comfort of your own home. Happy cooking!
