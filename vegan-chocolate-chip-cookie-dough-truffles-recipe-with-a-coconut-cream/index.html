Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles Recipe with a Coconut Cream


If you’re a fan of cookie dough and truffles, then this recipe is a match made in heaven! These Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles are not only delicious but also made with wholesome ingredients. They are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without any guilt. And to make them even more decadent, we’ll be topping them off with a creamy coconut cream. So let’s jump right into it!

Ingredients

For the cookie dough truffles:

  • 1 cup of almond flour
  • 1/4 cup of coconut flour
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons of melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of vegan chocolate chips

For the coconut cream:

  • 1 can of full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, coconut flour, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well until a dough-like consistency forms.

  2. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  3. Using a tablespoon or a cookie scoop, portion out the dough and roll it into small truffle-sized balls. Place the truffles on a parchment-lined baking sheet and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to firm up.

  4. While the truffles are chilling, prepare the coconut cream. Open the can of refrigerated coconut milk and carefully scoop out the solid coconut cream that has separated from the liquid. Place the cream in a mixing bowl and discard the liquid or save it for another use.

  5. Using a hand mixer or a stand mixer, whip the coconut cream until it becomes light and fluffy. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract and continue to whip until well combined.

  6. Once the truffles have firmed up, remove them from the fridge. Using a toothpick or a fork, dip each truffle into the coconut cream, coating it evenly. Place the coated truffles back on the parchment-lined baking sheet.

  7. Return the truffles to the refrigerator for another 10-15 minutes to allow the coconut cream to set.

  8. Serve the Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles with Coconut Cream chilled and enjoy!

Tips

  • Make sure to refrigerate the can of coconut milk overnight for the best results in separating the cream from the liquid.
  • If you prefer a sweeter coconut cream, feel free to add more powdered sugar to taste.
  • You can customize these truffles by adding other mix-ins such as chopped nuts or dried fruit to the cookie dough.
  • Store any leftover truffles in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles with Coconut Cream are a delightful treat that will satisfy your cravings for something sweet and indulgent. The combination of the creamy cookie dough center and the luscious coconut cream topping creates a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. Whether you enjoy them as a dessert or a snack, these truffles are sure to be a hit with both vegans and non-vegans alike. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and let your taste buds dance with joy!
