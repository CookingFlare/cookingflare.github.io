Spicy Garlic Goodness: Homemade Garlic Chili Sauce Recipes


Welcome to our spicy kitchen adventure! Today, we’re diving into the world of homemade garlic chili sauce, where fiery heat meets the aromatic allure of garlic. Whether you’re a spice enthusiast or looking to add a kick to your dishes, these homemade recipes are sure to elevate your culinary creations. Let’s get started!

Classic Garlic Chili Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 10 cloves of garlic
  • 10 red chilies
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Peel and chop the garlic cloves and red chilies.
  2. In a blender, combine the garlic, chilies, vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a saucepan and simmer over low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Let the sauce cool before transferring it to a sterilized jar.
  6. Store in the refrigerator for up to two weeks.

This classic garlic chili sauce is perfect for adding a zesty punch to stir-fries, marinades, or even as a dipping sauce for spring rolls.

Roasted Garlic Chili Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of garlic
  • 8 dried red chilies
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cut the top off the head of garlic to expose the cloves.
  3. Place the garlic head on a piece of foil, drizzle with olive oil, and wrap it up.
  4. Roast the garlic in the oven for 30-40 minutes until soft.
  5. In a pan, toast the dried red chilies until fragrant.
  6. In a blender, combine the roasted garlic, toasted chilies, water, honey, and salt.
  7. Blend until smooth.
  8. Transfer the sauce to a saucepan and simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. Let cool and store in the refrigerator.

The roasted garlic chili sauce offers a smoky depth that pairs beautifully with grilled meats, roasted vegetables, or as a pizza drizzle.

Spicy Garlic Lime Chili Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • Zest and juice of 2 limes
  • 6 green chilies
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the garlic, lime zest, lime juice, green chilies, cilantro, honey, and salt.
  2. Blend until the mixture reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  4. Transfer to a jar and refrigerate.

This zesty garlic lime chili sauce is a versatile condiment that adds a burst of freshness to tacos, salads, or grilled seafood.
