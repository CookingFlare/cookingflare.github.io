Grilled Cheese: Melty Goodness on the BBQ


Who doesn’t love a classic grilled cheese sandwich? But have you ever thought about taking it to the next level by making it on the BBQ? Grilling your grilled cheese adds a delicious smoky flavor and a perfectly crispy exterior to the ooey-gooey cheesy interior. In this post, we’ll explore how to achieve grilled cheese perfection on the BBQ, along with some creative variations to elevate this comfort food to new heights.

Ingredients:

  • Sourdough bread
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Gouda cheese
  • Tomato slices
  • Bacon (optional)
  • Avocado (optional)
  • Pesto (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the BBQ: Start by preheating your BBQ to medium heat. You want to achieve a consistent temperature to ensure even grilling.

  2. Prepare the Ingredients: While the BBQ is heating up, it’s time to prepare the ingredients. Slice the sourdough bread into thick slices. Grate the cheddar, mozzarella, and gouda cheese. If you’re adding any optional ingredients like bacon, avocado, or tomato slices, make sure they are sliced and ready to go.

  3. Butter the Bread: Take one slice of sourdough bread and generously butter one side. This will help create a beautiful golden crust on the outside of the grilled cheese.

  4. Layer the Cheese: Place the buttered side of the bread down on a piece of aluminum foil. Layer the cheddar, mozzarella, and gouda cheese on top of the bread. If you’re adding any optional ingredients, now is the time to layer them on top of the cheese.

  5. Top it Off: Take another slice of sourdough bread and butter one side. Place it buttered side up on top of the cheese and optional ingredients.

  6. Wrap in Foil: Carefully wrap the assembled sandwich in the aluminum foil, ensuring it’s completely sealed.

  7. Grill the Sandwich: Place the foil-wrapped sandwich on the preheated BBQ. Grill for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown, and the cheese is melted to perfection.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Carefully unwrap the grilled cheese, slice it in half, and serve hot. The smoky flavor from the BBQ and the melty goodness of the cheese will surely delight your taste buds.

Creative Variations:

1. Caprese Grilled Cheese

  • Use sourdough bread, mozzarella, tomato slices, fresh basil leaves, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

2. Bacon Avocado Grilled Cheese

  • Add crispy bacon and sliced avocado to the classic grilled cheese for a delicious twist.

3. Pesto Grilled Cheese

  • Spread a layer of pesto on the inside of the bread before adding the cheese for a flavorful and vibrant grilled cheese experience.

Tips for Success:

  • Use good quality cheese for the best melting results.
  • Don’t skimp on the butter; it’s essential for achieving a golden, crispy crust.
  • Experiment with different bread varieties for unique flavor profiles.

Grilled cheese on the BBQ is a simple yet indulgent treat that is sure to be a hit at your next cookout. Whether you stick to the classic recipe or get creative with your ingredients, the smoky, cheesy goodness of a grilled cheese straight from the BBQ is an experience worth savoring. So fire up the grill and get ready to enjoy melty goodness like never before!
