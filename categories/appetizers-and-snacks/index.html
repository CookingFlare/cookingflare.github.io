Are you ready to elevate your game day spread with a crowd-pleasing appetizer that packs a fiery kick? Look no further than this Spicy Buffalo Chicken Dip recipe! Perfect for gatherings, parties, or simply indulging in a flavorful snack, this creamy and zesty dip will have everyone coming back for more. Ingredients 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken 8 oz cream cheese, softened 1/2 cup hot sauce 1/2 cup ranch dressing 1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1/4 cup chopped green onions 1/4 cup chopped celery 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tsp onion powder 1 tsp garlic powder Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cream cheese, hot sauce, ranch dressing, blue cheese crumbles, half of the shredded cheddar cheese, green onions, celery, minced garlic, onion powder, and garlic powder. Mix until well combined. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish, spreading it out evenly. Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheddar cheese on top of the mixture. Bake the dip in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are bubbly and the cheese is melted and golden brown. Once baked, remove the dip from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with additional green onions and blue cheese crumbles if desired. Serve the Spicy Buffalo Chicken Dip with tortilla chips, celery sticks, or your favorite dippers. Flavor Explosion This Spicy Buffalo Chicken Dip is a flavor explosion in every bite. The combination of tangy hot sauce, creamy ranch dressing, and savory chicken creates a mouthwatering experience that will leave your taste buds tingling.
