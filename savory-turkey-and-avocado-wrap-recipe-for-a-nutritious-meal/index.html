Savory Turkey and Avocado Wrap Recipe for a Nutritious Meal


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than this savory turkey and avocado wrap recipe. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and a medley of flavors, this wrap is perfect for a quick lunch or a light dinner. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 pound of turkey breast, sliced
  • 2 ripe avocados, pitted and sliced
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to create a flavorful dressing.

  2. Lay out the whole wheat tortillas on a clean surface. Divide the sliced turkey breast evenly among the tortillas, placing it in the center.

  3. Next, layer the sliced avocados, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and fresh cilantro on top of the turkey. Drizzle the dressing over the fillings.

  4. Gently fold the sides of the tortillas over the filling, then roll them tightly to create a wrap. Secure the wraps with toothpicks if necessary.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Place the wraps in the skillet, seam side down, and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are golden brown and the fillings are heated through.

  6. Once cooked, remove the wraps from the skillet and let them cool for a few minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve the savory turkey and avocado wraps warm, either as a whole or sliced into bite-sized pieces. Enjoy!

Nutritional Benefits

This savory turkey and avocado wrap recipe not only satisfies your taste buds but also provides numerous nutritional benefits. Let’s take a closer look at how each ingredient contributes to a healthy and balanced meal:

  • Turkey breast: High in protein, turkey breast helps build and repair tissues, supports muscle growth, and keeps you feeling full for longer.

  • Avocado: Packed with healthy fats and fiber, avocados promote heart health, aid in digestion, and provide a creamy texture to the wrap.

  • Whole wheat tortillas: Compared to their refined counterparts, whole wheat tortillas are higher in fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making them a healthier choice.

  • Cherry tomatoes: These vibrant red gems are rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, contributing to overall health and wellbeing.

  • Red onion: Besides adding a burst of flavor, red onions contain compounds that may help reduce inflammation and support a healthy immune system.

  • Cilantro: Known for its fresh and citrusy taste, cilantro adds a pop of flavor and provides small amounts of essential nutrients like vitamin K and vitamin C.

Final Thoughts

With its combination of lean turkey, creamy avocado, and a variety of fresh ingredients, this savory turkey and avocado wrap is a nutritious and satisfying meal option. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch to take on the go or a light dinner, this recipe has got you covered. Give it a try and enjoy a delicious and healthy meal that will leave you feeling energized and satisfied.
