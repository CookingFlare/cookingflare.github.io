Indulgent Chocolate Chip Pancake Recipes for a Decadent Brunch


Brunch, the most indulgent and delightful meal of the day! It’s a time to gather with friends and family, to savor delicious flavors and textures, and to treat yourself to something truly special. And what better way to do so than with a stack of fluffy, chocolatey pancakes? In this post, we’ll share two indulgent chocolate chip pancake recipes that are sure to impress even the most discerning palates.

Recipe 1: Classic Chocolate Chip Pancakes

These classic pancakes are a staple for a reason - they’re easy to make, deliciously fluffy, and packed with chocolatey goodness. The secret to their success lies in the combination of all-purpose flour, whole milk, and a generous helping of dark chocolate chips.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips (at least 60% cocoa solids)
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips and vanilla extract.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  6. Drop the batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto the skillet or griddle.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges start to dry.
  8. Flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until golden brown.

Recipe 2: Stuffed Chocolate Chip Pancakes with Caramelized Bananas

Take your pancake game to the next level with these stuffed pancakes, filled with caramelized bananas and a drizzle of honey. The combination of textures and flavors is truly decadent!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips (at least 60% cocoa solids)
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  6. Drop the batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto the skillet or griddle.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges start to dry.
  8. Flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until golden brown.
  9. While the pancakes are cooking, caramelize the bananas by cooking them in a pan with butter and honey over medium heat until golden brown.
  10. To assemble the pancakes, place a cooked pancake on a plate, top with a caramelized banana slice, and drizzle with additional honey.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra-decadent treat, top your pancakes with whipped cream, chopped nuts, or shaved chocolate.
  • Substitute the dark chocolate chips with milk chocolate or white chocolate for a different flavor profile.
  • Add a sprinkle of sea salt to balance out the sweetness of the pancakes.
  • Experiment with different types of milk, such as almond or soy milk, for a non-dairy version.

There you have it - two indulgent chocolate chip pancake recipes that are sure to impress your brunch guests. Whether you’re a classic pancake fan or prefer something a little more decadent, these recipes have got you covered. So go ahead, get creative, and indulge in the sweet, sweet taste of brunch perfection!
