Satisfying and Healthy Stir Fry Recipes with Tofu and Broccoli


If you’re looking for a delicious, nutritious, and satisfying meal, stir fry recipes with tofu and broccoli are an excellent choice. Not only are they packed with protein and fiber, but they also offer a burst of flavor and a delightful texture that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a stir fry aficionado or a newbie to this cooking technique, these recipes will surely become a staple in your kitchen.

Tantalizing Tofu and Broccoli Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of extra firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked brown rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of oil. Add the garlic and ginger, and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced onion and red bell pepper to the skillet, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Toss in the broccoli florets and continue to stir fry for another 3-4 minutes until the broccoli is tender-crisp.
  6. Return the cooked tofu to the skillet and pour the sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Stir gently to coat everything with the sauce and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the tofu and broccoli stir fry over cooked brown rice or quinoa.

Tempting Teriyaki Tofu and Broccoli Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of extra firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Cooked white or brown rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, honey (or maple syrup), and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, heat the sesame oil. Add the garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the julienned carrot, snap peas, and broccoli florets to the skillet, and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the cooked tofu to the skillet and pour the sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Stir gently to coat everything with the sauce and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve the teriyaki tofu and broccoli stir fry over cooked white or brown rice.

Conclusion

Stir fry recipes with tofu and broccoli are not only easy to prepare, but they also offer a healthy and satisfying meal option for any day of the week. With the right combination of sauces, spices, and fresh ingredients, you can create a variety of flavorful stir fry dishes that will tantalize your taste buds and keep you coming back for more. Whether you opt for the classic soy sauce-based stir fry or the sweet and savory teriyaki version, these recipes are sure to become a favorite in your home. Enjoy the delightful fusion of flavors and textures while nourishing your body with this wholesome and delicious meal option.
