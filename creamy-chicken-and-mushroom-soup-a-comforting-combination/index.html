Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Soup: A Comforting Combination


There’s something truly comforting about a warm bowl of soup, especially when it’s creamy, hearty, and packed with delicious flavors. If you’re a fan of creamy soups and looking for a new recipe to add to your collection, then you’re in for a treat. This Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Soup is a delightful combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, aromatic herbs, and a velvety smooth base that will warm you from the inside out. Whether you’re craving a cozy meal on a chilly evening or simply want to indulge in a bowl of homemade goodness, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Soup:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, diced
  • 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Garnish (Optional):

  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Extra drizzle of heavy cream
  • Crusty bread or garlic bread for serving

Instructions

  1. Sear the Chicken: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken thighs and season with salt and pepper. Sear the chicken until golden brown on all sides. Once done, remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: In the same pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Cook the Mushrooms: Add the sliced cremini mushrooms to the pot and cook until they are golden brown and have released their moisture. This will take about 8-10 minutes.

  4. Create the Base: Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and stir well to coat them. This will help thicken the soup. Pour in the chicken broth while stirring constantly to avoid any lumps. Add the dried thyme and rosemary, then bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  5. Simmer and Add Chicken: Once the soup base has thickened slightly, add the seared chicken back into the pot. Allow the soup to simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and the flavors have melded together.

  6. Finish with Cream: Pour in the heavy cream and stir gently to incorporate it into the soup. Let the soup simmer for a few more minutes until it reaches your desired consistency. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the creamy chicken and mushroom soup into bowls. If desired, drizzle a little extra heavy cream over the top and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Serve with a side of crusty bread or garlic bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a richer flavor, you can use a mixture of cremini and wild mushrooms.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a cornstarch slurry (cornstarch mixed with a little water) to the soup and simmer until thickened.
  • This soup can be made ahead of time and reheated gently on the stove, making it a convenient option for busy days.

In Conclusion

Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Soup is a classic comfort food that never fails to impress. With its creamy texture, savory chicken, and earthy mushrooms, it’s a delightful combination that will warm both body and soul. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a gathering, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So, next time you’re in the mood for a soul-soothing meal, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful flavors of homemade goodness.
