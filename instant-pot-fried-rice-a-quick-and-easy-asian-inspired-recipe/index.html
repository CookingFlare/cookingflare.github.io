Instant Pot Fried Rice: A Quick and Easy Asian-Inspired Recipe


If you’re searching for a simple yet flavorful dish that can be prepared swiftly and effortlessly, Instant Pot fried rice is here to save the day! Combining the timeless appeal of Asian-inspired flavors with the convenience of modern pressure cooking, this fried rice recipe makes it possible to whip up a restaurant-worthy dish right in your own kitchen. Perfect for busy weekdays and impressive enough for casual gatherings, this method will soon become your go-to when you’re craving authentic fried rice without the hassle of lengthy preparation.

Why Choose Instant Pot Fried Rice?

Traditional fried rice recipes often require cooking rice first, letting it cool, and then stir-frying it in a wok or pan—a process that requires time and patience. Fortunately, the Instant Pot simplifies everything. With its ability to cook rice perfectly and infuse flavors simultaneously, you’ll have a one-pot solution that cuts down on both prep time and cleanup. This recipe seamlessly blends convenience with bold, savory flavors that will satisfy your taste buds and save you hours in the kitchen.

Whether you’re a rice enthusiast or just looking for an easy way to reuse leftovers, this Instant Pot fried rice recipe is versatile enough to offer endless customization options. You can adjust the ingredients to fit your dietary preferences, flavor preferences, or even the contents of your refrigerator.

Ingredients You’ll Need

One of the great things about fried rice is its adaptability. While this recipe includes ingredients commonly found in Asian-inspired dishes, feel free to experiment based on what you have available. Here’s a list to get you started:

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups of jasmine rice: This fragrant variety is ideal for fried rice because of its slightly sticky texture. You can use long-grain white rice as a substitute.
  • 2 cups of water: Essential for cooking the rice in your Instant Pot.

For the Sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce: Adds a rich, salty umami flavor.
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce: A key ingredient for depth and traditional fried rice flavor.
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil: Imparts a nutty aroma and taste.
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar: Balances the savory and salty notes.

Mix-Ins:

  • 3 large eggs: Scrambled to perfection for that classic fried rice touch.
  • 1 cup of frozen peas and carrots: An easy way to incorporate vegetables.
  • 1 cup of diced cooked chicken or shrimp (optional): For added protein and heartiness.
  • 2 green onions: Sliced thin for garnishing and added freshness.
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced): Delivers bold flavor.

Optional Add-Ins:

  • Soy sauce paste: For extra umami.
  • Ginger (grated): For aromatic flair.
  • Chili flakes or sriracha: If you love spice.

Step-by-Step Instructions: Making Instant Pot Fried Rice

Here’s a detailed guide to making the best Instant Pot fried rice:

1. Prepare the Rice

Begin by rinsing your jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Rinsing removes excess starch, preventing your rice from becoming sticky or clumpy.

Instant Pot Method:

  • Add your rinsed rice to the Instant Pot along with an equal ratio of water (2 cups of rice to 2 cups of water).
  • Close the lid, set the valve to “Sealing,” and cook on high pressure for 3 minutes.
  • Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a 10-minute natural release, then carefully switch the valve to “Venting” to release any remaining pressure.

Once the rice is done, fluff it with a fork and transfer it to a bowl to cool. Allowing the rice to cool prevents it from becoming overly soft when stir-fried later.

2. Scramble the Eggs

  • Turn on the sauté function on your Instant Pot and wait until it heats up.
  • Add a drizzle of sesame oil, then crack the eggs directly into the pot and scramble them using a spatula.
  • Once cooked, remove the eggs and set them aside on a plate.

3. Sauté Vegetables and Protein

  • Add a splash of sesame oil to the Instant Pot and toss in your garlic, peas, carrots, and any protein (like chicken or shrimp, if desired).
  • Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender and fragrant, and the protein is heated through.

4. Incorporate the Rice and Sauce

  • Add the cooked rice back to the Instant Pot, breaking up any clumps and stirring it with the vegetables and protein.
  • In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and a touch more sesame oil to create your fried rice sauce. Pour this mixture over the rice and stir until evenly coated. The rice will soak up the hearty flavors, turning golden brown and mouthwateringly aromatic.

5. Finish with Eggs and Green Onions

  • Fold the scrambled eggs back into the rice mixture.
  • Sprinkle sliced green onions over the top for a fresh, vibrant garnish.

Tips for Success

  1. Use Day-Old Rice: While Instant Pot rice works wonderfully fresh, using day-old rice can create a more authentic texture. If you have leftovers on hand, it’s worth using them.
  2. Customize Ingredients: Swap out chicken for tofu or shrimp. Add more vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, or mushrooms for extra nutrients.
  3. Adjust Seasoning: Taste as you go, tweaking the soy sauce or oyster sauce amounts to match your preferences.

Why This Recipe Is Perfect for Meal Prep

Not only is Instant Pot fried rice quick to make, but it’s also an excellent option for meal prep. Cook a large batch, divide it into individual portions, and store in airtight containers in the refrigerator for up to four days. For longer storage, freeze it and reheat using a microwave or stovetop when ready to eat. The flavors deepen over time, making leftovers even more satisfying!

A Dish Everyone Will Love

Instant Pot fried rice is the ideal combination of simplicity, speed, and flavor. Whether you’re serving a busy family, hosting a casual dinner with friends, or preparing meals for the week, this recipe delivers every time. It’s a balanced dish that combines protein, carbs, and vegetables in one pot, satisfying hunger while delivering a taste of Asian-inspired comfort food.

Final Thoughts

Your Instant Pot is more than just a pressure cooker—it’s a key tool for bringing delicious, globally inspired dishes into your kitchen. This fried rice recipe proves how versatile it can be, helping you prepare flavorsome meals with minimal effort and maximum enjoyment.

Make your next meal memorable with this easy Instant Pot fried rice. Once you experience the blend of aromatic sesame oil, savory soy sauce, and perfectly cooked grains, you’ll wonder why you haven’t been making fried rice this way all along!
