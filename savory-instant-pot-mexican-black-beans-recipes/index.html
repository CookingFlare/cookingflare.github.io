Savory Instant Pot Mexican Black Beans Recipes


Are you looking for a delicious and hearty dish that’s packed with flavor? Look no further than these savory Instant Pot Mexican Black Beans Recipes. Whether you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine or simply love a good bowl of beans, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Recipe 1: Classic Mexican Black Beans

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound dried black beans, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups vegetable broth

Instructions:

  1. In your Instant Pot, add the rinsed black beans, diced onion, minced garlic, minced jalapeno pepper, cumin, chili powder, oregano, and salt.
  2. Pour in the vegetable broth and give everything a good stir.
  3. Close the lid of your Instant Pot and set it to manual mode on high pressure for 25 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  5. Give the beans a quick stir and serve hot. These beans are perfect as a side dish, in burritos, or as a topping for nachos.

Recipe 2: Spicy Chipotle Black Beans

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound dried black beans, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 tablespoon adobo sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups vegetable broth

Instructions:

  1. In your Instant Pot, add the rinsed black beans, diced onion, minced garlic, minced chipotle peppers, adobo sauce, cumin, paprika, and salt.
  2. Pour in the vegetable broth and give everything a good stir.
  3. Close the lid of your Instant Pot and set it to manual mode on high pressure for 30 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  5. Give the beans a quick stir and serve hot. These spicy chipotle black beans are perfect for adding a kick to tacos, enchiladas, or as a base for a delicious vegetarian chili.

Recipe 3: Smoky Bacon Black Beans

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound dried black beans, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups chicken broth

Instructions:

  1. In your Instant Pot, add the rinsed black beans, diced onion, minced garlic, crumbled bacon, smoked paprika, oregano, and salt.
  2. Pour in the chicken broth and give everything a good stir.
  3. Close the lid of your Instant Pot and set it to manual mode on high pressure for 25 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  5. Give the beans a quick stir and serve hot. These smoky bacon black beans are perfect as a side dish for grilled meats or as a filling for quesadillas.

Final Thoughts

These savory Instant Pot Mexican Black Beans Recipes are a fantastic addition to your recipe repertoire. They’re not only easy to make but also packed with flavor. Whether you prefer the classic version, a spicy kick with chipotle, or the smoky goodness of bacon, these recipes will surely impress your family and friends. So grab your Instant Pot and get cooking!
