Vegan Lemon Raspberry Bars: A Tangy and Fruity Treat


When life gives you lemons and raspberries, why not turn them into a delightful and tangy treat? These Vegan Lemon Raspberry Bars are the perfect balance of zesty lemon and sweet raspberry, all nestled in a buttery, crumbly crust. Whether you’re a vegan, a dessert enthusiast, or simply looking for a refreshing summer dessert, these bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, solid

For the filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan and line it with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. Make the crust: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, powdered sugar, and salt. Add the solid coconut oil and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until lightly golden.

  3. Prepare the filling: In a separate bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Stir in the lemon juice, coconut milk, applesauce, and lemon zest until smooth.

  4. Assemble the bars: Gently fold in the fresh raspberries into the lemon filling mixture. Pour the filling over the baked crust and spread it evenly.

  5. Bake the bars: Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the filling is set. The edges should be slightly golden, and the center should no longer jiggle when gently shaken.

  6. Chill and serve: Allow the bars to cool completely at room temperature, then transfer to the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours. Once chilled, lift the bars out of the pan using the parchment paper overhang, then slice into squares.

  7. Garnish and enjoy: Before serving, you can dust the bars with powdered sugar and garnish with fresh raspberries for an extra pop of color and flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For a gluten-free option, you can use a 1:1 gluten-free flour blend in place of all-purpose flour.
  • Feel free to experiment with other berry fillings such as blackberries or blueberries for a different flavor profile.
  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days, though they are best enjoyed fresh.

Conclusion

These Vegan Lemon Raspberry Bars are a delightful combination of tartness and sweetness, making them a perfect dessert for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a refreshing treat, these bars are sure to be a hit. With a buttery crust, zesty lemon filling, and bursts of fresh raspberries, this recipe is a celebration of vibrant flavors that are both vegan-friendly and irresistibly delicious. So go ahead, embrace the tangy and fruity goodness of these bars and treat yourself to a slice of pure bliss!
