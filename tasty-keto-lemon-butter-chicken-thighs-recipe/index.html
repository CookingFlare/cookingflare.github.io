Tasty Keto Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy keto-friendly meal, look no further than this Tasty Keto Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs recipe. Packed with flavor and low in carbs, this dish is perfect for anyone following a ketogenic diet or simply looking for a healthy and satisfying dinner option.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Season the Chicken Thighs: Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, dried oregano, and paprika, ensuring that both sides are evenly coated.

  3. Sear the Chicken Thighs: In a large oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the seasoned chicken thighs, skin side down. Sear for 3-4 minutes, or until the skin is golden brown and crispy. Flip the chicken thighs and sear for an additional 2-3 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Prepare the Lemon Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute, or until fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Stir in the unsalted butter until melted, creating a rich and fragrant lemon butter sauce.

  5. Bake in the Oven: Return the seared chicken thighs to the skillet, skin side up. Place the skillet in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once cooked, remove the skillet from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve the lemon butter chicken thighs hot, accompanied by your favorite keto-friendly sides or a crisp salad.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Tasty Keto Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs recipe is a must-try for several reasons:

  • Flavorful: The combination of zesty lemon, aromatic garlic, and savory chicken creates a burst of flavor in every bite.

  • Keto-Friendly: With minimal carbs and high-quality fats, this dish fits perfectly into a ketogenic lifestyle.

  • Easy and Quick: From stovetop to oven, this recipe comes together in no time, making it ideal for busy weeknights.

  • Versatile: You can customize this dish by adding your favorite herbs and spices, making it a versatile option for meal prep or family dinners.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply seeking a tasty and wholesome meal, these Tasty Keto Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs are sure to satisfy your cravings. With a perfect balance of tangy lemon and rich butter, this dish is a delightful addition to your recipe collection. Try it out and savor the delicious flavors of this low-carb, high-flavor chicken recipe.
