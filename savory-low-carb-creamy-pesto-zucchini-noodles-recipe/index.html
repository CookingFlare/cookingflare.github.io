Savory Low Carb Creamy Pesto Zucchini Noodles Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy low-carb meal, this savory creamy pesto zucchini noodles recipe is a must-try. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is a perfect way to enjoy a satisfying pasta-like meal without the guilt. Zucchini noodles, also known as “zoodles,” are a fantastic alternative to traditional pasta, offering a lighter and lower-carb option that doesn’t skimp on taste. Combined with a creamy pesto sauce, this recipe takes zucchini noodles to a whole new level of culinary delight.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup prepared pesto
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Zucchini Noodles:

    • Using a spiralizer, spiralize the zucchinis into long, thin noodles. If you don’t have a spiralizer, you can use a julienne peeler to create noodle-like strips. Once spiralized, set the zucchini noodles aside.

  2. Make the Creamy Pesto Sauce:

    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.
    • Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Let it cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    • Add the grated Parmesan cheese to the cream and stir until it’s fully melted and the sauce has thickened slightly.
    • Stir in the prepared pesto until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Cook the Zucchini Noodles:

    • Add the zucchini noodles to the skillet with the creamy pesto sauce. Toss the noodles gently to coat them evenly with the sauce.
    • Cook the zucchini noodles for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they are just tender but still have a slight crunch.

  4. Serve and Garnish:

    • Once the zucchini noodles are cooked to your desired consistency, remove the skillet from the heat.
    • Divide the creamy pesto zucchini noodles among serving plates. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor and a touch of elegance.

Tips and Variations

  • For a lighter version of this dish, you can use half-and-half instead of heavy cream, though the sauce may not be as rich and creamy.
  • Feel free to add cooked protein such as grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the zucchini noodles for a more substantial meal.
  • If you prefer a thinner sauce, you can add a splash of chicken or vegetable broth to the creamy pesto sauce.

Conclusion

This savory low-carb creamy pesto zucchini noodles recipe is a delightful way to enjoy a satisfying and flavorful meal while keeping your carb intake in check. Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into your diet, this dish is a winner. The creamy pesto sauce adds a luxurious touch to the zucchini noodles, making it a dish that’s sure to impress both your taste buds and your guests. Give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of creamy, savory, and healthy flavors!
