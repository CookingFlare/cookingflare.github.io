Easy and Tasty Chicken Tacos Recipe for Kids


Are you looking for a delicious and kid-friendly meal that the whole family will love? Look no further than this easy and tasty chicken tacos recipe! Packed with flavor and simple to make, these chicken tacos are sure to be a hit with even the pickiest eaters.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Toppings of your choice (shredded cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, salsa, sour cream, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a small bowl, combine the chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a spice rub.

  3. Rub the spice rub all over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  4. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are browned.

  5. Transfer the chicken breasts to a baking dish and place in the preheated oven. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center.

  6. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

  7. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side, or until they are soft and pliable.

  8. Fill each tortilla with a few slices of the cooked chicken and your choice of toppings. Fold the tortilla over the filling to create a taco.

  9. Serve the chicken tacos immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize these chicken tacos with your favorite toppings. Some popular options include shredded cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.

  • If your kids prefer a milder flavor, you can reduce the amount of chili powder and cumin in the spice rub.

  • To make this recipe even easier, you can use leftover cooked chicken instead of cooking it from scratch. Simply heat the chicken in a skillet with the spice rub until warmed through.

  • If you’re short on time, you can use store-bought rotisserie chicken instead of cooking the chicken breasts yourself. Just shred the chicken and toss it with the spice rub before filling the tortillas.

  • For added crunch, you can toast the tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for a few seconds on each side before filling them.

  • These chicken tacos are great for meal prep. You can make a big batch of the chicken and store it in the fridge for up to 3 days. Simply reheat the chicken before assembling the tacos.

Conclusion

With this easy and tasty chicken tacos recipe, you can whip up a delicious and satisfying meal for your kids in no time. The flavorful spice rub adds a kick of flavor to the tender chicken, while the variety of toppings allows each person to customize their taco to their liking. Whether it’s for a quick weeknight dinner or a fun weekend lunch, these chicken tacos are sure to be a favorite among both kids and adults. Give them a try and watch as your little ones devour every last bite!
