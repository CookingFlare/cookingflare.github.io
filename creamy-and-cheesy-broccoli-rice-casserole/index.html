Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Rice Casserole

3 min · 637 words

If you’re looking for a comforting and delicious dish that’s perfect for family gatherings or weeknight dinners, this Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Rice Casserole is sure to become a new favorite. Packed with flavor, creamy texture, and wholesome ingredients, this casserole is a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone asking for seconds. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak in some extra veggies for the picky eaters in your life. Let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.

  2. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the onion and garlic mixture, then stir to combine. Cook for a couple of minutes, stirring constantly, to create a roux.

  4. Slowly pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Allow the mixture to simmer and thicken for a few minutes.

  5. Stir in the sour cream, mayonnaise, and half of the grated cheddar and Parmesan cheese until everything is well combined and the cheese is melted.

  6. Add the cooked white rice and broccoli florets to the creamy cheese mixture, and gently fold everything together until the broccoli is evenly distributed.

  7. Season the mixture with paprika, salt, and pepper to taste, then transfer it to the prepared baking dish. Spread it out evenly with a spatula.

  8. Sprinkle the remaining cheddar and Parmesan cheese over the top of the casserole, then cover the dish with aluminum foil.

  9. Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

  10. Once the casserole is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

This Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Rice Casserole is a versatile dish that can be served as a main course or as a side dish alongside grilled chicken, roasted turkey, or your favorite protein. It also makes for a great potluck dish and can be prepared in advance, making it perfect for busy days.

Final Thoughts:

Creamy, cheesy, and packed with wholesome ingredients, this Broccoli Rice Casserole is a delightful comfort food that is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just for your family, this casserole is a delicious way to enjoy the classic combination of broccoli, cheese, and rice. Give this recipe a try and watch it become a new favorite at your dinner table!
