Easy Bibimbap Recipe: A Colorful Korean Rice Bowl


Bibimbap, a traditional Korean dish, is a vibrant and nutritious rice bowl that combines a variety of ingredients, making it not only a feast for the eyes but also a delight for the palate. This easy bibimbap recipe allows you to create a colorful and flavorful meal that can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this guide will walk you through the steps to prepare a delicious bibimbap that you can enjoy any day of the week.

What is Bibimbap?

Bibimbap translates to “mixed rice” in Korean, and it is a dish that typically consists of a bowl of warm rice topped with an assortment of sautéed vegetables, marinated meat, a fried egg, and a dollop of gochujang (Korean chili paste). The beauty of bibimbap lies in its versatility; you can use whatever vegetables and proteins you have on hand, making it a perfect dish for using up leftovers.

Ingredients for Easy Bibimbap

To make a delicious bibimbap, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Rice Base:

  • 2 cups of short-grain rice (or jasmine rice)
  • 2 ½ cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • Salt to taste

For the Vegetables:

  • 1 cup of spinach, blanched and squeezed dry
  • 1 cup of bean sprouts, blanched
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, julienned
  • 1 cup of shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Protein:

  • 1 cup of ground beef (or tofu for a vegetarian option)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of gochujang (optional)

For Topping:

  • 2 eggs
  • Gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Sesame seeds
  • Sliced green onions

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Rice

  1. Rinse the Rice: Start by rinsing the short-grain rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This helps remove excess starch and prevents the rice from becoming gummy.

  2. Cook the Rice: In a rice cooker or a pot, combine the rinsed rice and water. Cook according to your rice cooker’s instructions or bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 18-20 minutes until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed.

  3. Season the Rice: Once cooked, fluff the rice with a fork and stir in the sesame oil and a pinch of salt. Set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Vegetables

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  2. Cook the Mushrooms: Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms to the skillet. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Sauté for about 3-4 minutes until the mushrooms are tender. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Sauté the Carrots and Zucchini: In the same skillet, add the julienned carrots and zucchini. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes until they are slightly softened but still crisp. Season with salt and pepper, then remove from the skillet.

  4. Blanch the Spinach and Bean Sprouts: In a pot of boiling water, blanch the spinach for about 30 seconds, then transfer it to an ice bath to stop the cooking process. Squeeze out excess water and season with a pinch of salt. Repeat the process for the bean sprouts.

Step 3: Prepare the Protein

  1. Cook the Ground Beef: In the same skillet, add the ground beef. Cook over medium heat until browned, breaking it apart with a spatula. Drain excess fat if necessary.

  2. Season the Beef: Add soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, minced garlic, and gochujang (if using) to the beef. Stir well and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the flavors meld together. Remove from heat.

Step 4: Fry the Eggs

  1. Fry the Eggs: In a separate skillet, heat a little oil over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the skillet and fry until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny. You can cook them to your desired doneness.

Step 5: Assemble the Bibimbap
