Savory Beef and Potato Skillet: A Hearty One-Pan Meal


There’s nothing quite as satisfying as a hearty, one-pan meal that fills your kitchen with mouthwatering aromas. This Savory Beef and Potato Skillet recipe is a game-changer when it comes to convenience and flavor. With tender beef, crispy potatoes, and a medley of vegetables, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, cut into strips
  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef strips and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the diced potatoes and cook until golden brown and crispy. This will take about 10 minutes. Make sure to stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  3. Once the potatoes are cooked, add the sliced onion, minced garlic, and bell peppers to the skillet. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.

  4. Add the mushrooms to the skillet and cook for another 2 minutes, until they are slightly softened.

  5. Return the beef to the skillet and add the soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir everything together until well combined.

  6. Reduce the heat to low and cover the skillet. Allow the dish to simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the flavors meld together and the beef is cooked to your desired level of doneness.

  7. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure maximum flavor in every bite.

Serving Suggestions

This Savory Beef and Potato Skillet is a complete meal in itself, but you can elevate it further with some additional sides. Serve it with a side of steamed vegetables or a fresh green salad for a well-rounded dinner.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe based on your preferences. You can add other vegetables like carrots or zucchini for added flavor and nutrition.
  • If you’re looking to make this dish even heartier, you can serve it over a bed of fluffy mashed potatoes or with a side of crusty bread to soak up all the delicious juices.
  • For a spicy kick, add a dash of hot sauce or red pepper flakes to the skillet while cooking.
  • This recipe is also great for meal prep. Simply double the ingredients and divide the cooked dish into individual portions for easy grab-and-go meals throughout the week.

Final Thoughts

The Savory Beef and Potato Skillet is a versatile and satisfying one-pan meal that will please both your taste buds and your schedule. It’s perfect for busy weeknights when you want a wholesome dinner without spending hours in the kitchen. So gather your ingredients, fire up that skillet, and enjoy the deliciousness that awaits you!
