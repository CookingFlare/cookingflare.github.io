Delightful Italian Rainbow Cookies Recipe - Sweet Almond Treat


Looking to add a burst of color and flavor to your cookie jar? Look no further than these delightful Italian Rainbow Cookies! These sweet almond treats are not only visually stunning but also incredibly delicious. Perfect for any occasion, these cookies are sure to be a hit with both kids and adults alike. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and create a rainbow of flavors!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond paste
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs, separated
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Red, green, and yellow food coloring
  • 1/2 cup raspberry jam
  • 1/2 cup apricot jam
  • 8 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable shortening

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line three 9x9-inch baking pans with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, crumble the almond paste using your hands. Add the softened butter and granulated sugar. Mix well until the mixture is smooth and creamy.

  3. Separate the egg whites from the yolks. Add the egg yolks to the almond mixture. Mix until well combined.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Gradually add the flour mixture to the almond mixture, stirring until a smooth batter forms.

  5. Divide the batter equally into three separate bowls. Add a few drops of red food coloring to one bowl, green food coloring to another, and yellow food coloring to the third bowl. Mix each bowl until the colors are evenly distributed.

  6. Spread the red batter evenly into one of the prepared baking pans. Repeat with the green and yellow batters in the other pans.

  7. Bake the layers for about 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Remove from the oven and let cool completely.

  8. Once the layers have cooled, spread a thin layer of raspberry jam over the red layer and a thin layer of apricot jam over the green layer. Place the yellow layer on top of the green layer.

  9. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the semisweet chocolate and vegetable shortening together in 30-second intervals, stirring until smooth.

  10. Pour the melted chocolate over the stacked layers, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  11. Refrigerate the cookie stack for at least 1 hour or until the chocolate has hardened.

  12. Once the chocolate has set, remove the cookie stack from the refrigerator and trim the edges to create clean lines. Cut the stack into small rectangles or squares.

  13. Serve and enjoy these delightful Italian Rainbow Cookies with a cup of coffee or tea!

Conclusion

These Delightful Italian Rainbow Cookies are a true feast for the eyes and the taste buds. The combination of almond flavors, sweet jams, and rich chocolate creates a perfect balance of sweetness. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking to satisfy your sweet tooth, these cookies are a surefire way to impress. So, go ahead and indulge in the vibrant colors and delicious flavors of this sweet almond treat!
