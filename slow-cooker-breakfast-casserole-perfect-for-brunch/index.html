Slow Cooker Breakfast Casserole Perfect for Brunch


Are you looking for a dish that will impress your friends and family at your next brunch gathering? Look no further than this slow-cooker breakfast casserole recipe. Perfect for a large group, this delicious casserole is loaded with sausage, bacon, eggs, and cheese, making it a satisfying and filling breakfast option. Layers of flavors infuse this casserole, starting with the savory taste of crumbled sausage and crispy bacon. These meats add a smoky and meaty flavor that pairs perfectly with the richness of melted cheese. The eggs add creaminess and moisture, while the bread provides a satisfying crunch. What's more, assembling this casserole in the slow cooker makes it a breeze to prepare, allowing you to spend more time with your guests. Recipe Overview Ingredients:

1 pound of sweet Italian sausage 6 slices of bacon, diced 2 cups of milk 6 large eggs 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese 1/2 cup of chopped onion 3 cloves of garlic, minced 1/2 cup of chopped fresh parsley 1 loaf of bread, cut into 1-inch cubes Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

In the slow cooker, cook the sausage over medium heat, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks, until browned and no longer pink. Remove the cooked sausage from the slow cooker, leaving the drippings behind. Add the diced bacon to the slow cooker and cook until crispy, then remove from the slow cooker and set aside with the cooked sausage. In a large bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheese, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Grease a 6-quart slow cooker with cooking spray and arrange a layer of bread cubes on the bottom. Top the bread with a layer of sausage, followed by a layer of bacon, and then half of the egg mixture. Sprinkle with some of the onion and garlic over the top. Repeat the layers, starting with the bread, then the remaining egg mixture, sausage, bacon, onion, and garlic. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 2-1/2 to 3 hours, or on high for 1-2 hours. When the casserole is cooked, remove the cover and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. Slice into 6-8 servings and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

For an added layer of flavor, sprinkle some diced bell peppers, chopped ham, or chopped cooked sausage over the top of the casserole before adding the second layer of egg mixture. If you prefer a spicier casserole, use hot Italian sausage instead of sweet Italian sausage. To make the casserole more substantial, add some diced potatoes or chopped ham to the egg mixture.

Nutrition Information Per serving (assuming 8 servings):

Calories: 420 Fat: 25g Saturated Fat: 15g Cholesterol: 180mg Sodium: 450mg Carbohydrates: 25g Fiber: 1g Protein: 20g

This slow cooker breakfast casserole is the perfect dish for a large gathering, such as a family brunch or a group event. Its rich flavors and satisfying textures will keep your guests coming back for more.
