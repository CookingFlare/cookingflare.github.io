Slow Cooker Seafood Recipes for a Taste of the Ocean


Are you a seafood lover looking for new and exciting ways to enjoy your favorite ocean delicacies? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore a collection of mouth-watering slow cooker seafood recipes that will transport you straight to the coastal wonders of the ocean. From succulent shrimp to tender fish fillets, these recipes will not only tantalize your taste buds but also make your cooking experience effortless and convenient.

1. Slow Cooker Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic, melted butter, lemon juice, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the peeled and deveined shrimp in the slow cooker.
  3. Pour the garlic butter mixture over the shrimp and stir until the shrimp are evenly coated.
  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours or until the shrimp are cooked through.
  5. Serve the garlic butter shrimp over a bed of rice or with crusty bread. Garnish with fresh parsley for added freshness and color.

2. Slow Cooker Coconut Curry Fish

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of white fish fillets (such as cod or tilapia)
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the fish fillets in the slow cooker.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, curry powder, fish sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice.
  3. Pour the curry mixture over the fish fillets.
  4. Add the sliced red bell pepper and onion to the slow cooker.
  5. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours or until the fish is tender and flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Serve the coconut curry fish over steamed rice and garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of flavor.

3. Slow Cooker Clam Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of chopped clams, drained (reserve the clam juice)
  • 4 slices of bacon, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 stalks of celery, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of diced potatoes
  • 2 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook the diced bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, sauté the onion, celery, and garlic until softened.
  3. Transfer the cooked bacon, onion, celery, and garlic to the slow cooker.
  4. Add the diced potatoes, clam juice, and chicken or vegetable broth to the slow cooker.
  5. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or until the potatoes are tender.
  6. Stir in the drained clams and heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Cover the slow cooker again and cook for an additional 30 minutes.
  8. Serve the clam chowder hot, garnished with fresh chives for added flavor and visual appeal.

Conclusion

With these slow cooker seafood recipes, you can bring the taste of the ocean right into your kitchen. The convenience of using a slow cooker allows you to enjoy the flavors and textures of seafood without the hassle of extensive preparation and cooking time. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a delicious seafood meal, these recipes are sure to impress. So go ahead, dive into the wonderful world of slow cooker seafood and savor the flavors of the ocean!
