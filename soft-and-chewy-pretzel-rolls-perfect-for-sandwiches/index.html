Soft and Chewy Pretzel Rolls: Perfect for Sandwiches


Soft and chewy pretzel rolls are a game-changer for any sandwich lover. They add a delightful texture and flavor to your favorite fillings, making them a perfect addition to any meal. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make soft and chewy pretzel rolls from scratch, using a simple and easy-to-follow method.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of active dry yeast
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup of pretzel dough conditioner (optional)
  • Sesame seeds or pretzel salt for topping (optional)

Instructions

To start, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast in a large mixing bowl. Stir gently to dissolve the yeast, and let it sit for 5-10 minutes, or until the mixture becomes frothy.

Next, add the flour, salt, and melted butter to the bowl. Mix the dough until it comes together in a sticky ball. If using a stand mixer, use the dough hook attachment to mix the dough. If mixing by hand, use a wooden spoon or a silicone spatula to mix the dough.

Knead the dough on a floured surface for 10-15 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment to knead the dough.

Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.

Divide the dough into 8-10 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a ball and then flatten it slightly into a disk shape.

Place the disks onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving about 1 inch of space between each roll.

If desired, brush the tops of the rolls with egg wash (beaten egg mixed with a little water) and sprinkle with sesame seeds or pretzel salt.

Bake the rolls for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown.

Remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack for 5-10 minutes before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • To make soft and chewy pretzel rolls, it’s essential to not overbake them. They should be lightly browned and still slightly soft to the touch.
  • If you want to add some extra flavor to your rolls, you can mix in some grated cheese, chopped herbs, or minced garlic into the dough before kneading.
  • To make pretzel rolls ahead of time, you can shape the dough into disks and freeze them on a baking sheet. Then, bake them frozen for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  • You can also use pretzel rolls as a base for sandwiches. Simply place your favorite fillings on top of the roll and serve.

Conclusion

Soft and chewy pretzel rolls are a delicious and versatile addition to any meal. With this simple recipe, you can make soft and chewy pretzel rolls from scratch, perfect for sandwiches, snacks, or even as a side dish. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced baker, this recipe is easy to follow and requires minimal ingredients. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the soft and chewy goodness of homemade pretzel rolls!
