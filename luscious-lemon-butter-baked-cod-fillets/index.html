Luscious Lemon Butter Baked Cod Fillets


If you’re looking for a meal that’s quick, healthy, and bursting with flavor, look no further than luscious lemon butter baked cod fillets. This dish combines the delicate, flaky texture of cod with a rich, zesty lemon butter sauce that elevates it to gourmet status. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or an impressive dish to serve guests, this recipe is both simple and satisfying.

Why Choose Cod?

Cod is not only delicious but also a nutritious choice. Rich in protein, low in fat, and packed with essential vitamins and minerals, cod is a versatile fish that holds up well to various cooking methods. It’s mild in flavor, making it an ideal canvas for bold seasonings and sauces. When baked, cod retains its moisture, resulting in a tender and flaky texture that pairs beautifully with the lemon butter sauce.

Ingredients

Before diving into the cooking process, let’s gather all the ingredients needed for this delightful dish:

  • Cod Fillets: 4 pieces, skinless and boneless (about 6 ounces each)
  • Unsalted Butter: 1/2 cup, melted
  • Fresh Lemon Juice: 1/4 cup (about 2 lemons)
  • Lemon Zest: 1 teaspoon (from 1 lemon)
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Fresh Parsley: 2 tablespoons, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon (to taste)
  • Black Pepper: 1/2 teaspoon (to taste)
  • Paprika: 1/2 teaspoon (for a hint of color and flavor)
  • Optional: Lemon wedges for serving

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15-20 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). A hot oven is crucial for achieving that perfect flaky texture while ensuring the fish cooks evenly.

Step 2: Prepare the Baking Dish

Lightly grease a baking dish with cooking spray or a bit of olive oil to prevent the fish from sticking. This step is essential for easy cleanup and perfectly cooked fillets.

Step 3: Make the Lemon Butter Sauce

In a medium bowl, whisk together the melted unsalted butter, fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, and paprika. This luscious lemon butter sauce is the star of your dish, adding depth and richness to the cod fillets.

Step 4: Arrange the Cod Fillets

Place the cod fillets in the prepared baking dish in a single layer. Ensure they are not overcrowded to allow for even cooking. Pour the lemon butter sauce over the fillets, making sure each piece is well-coated. For an extra touch, you can reserve a little sauce to drizzle on top just before serving.

Step 5: Bake the Cod

Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, depending on the thickness of your fillets. The cod is done when it flakes easily with a fork and has reached an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C). Keep an eye on it to avoid overcooking, as cod can become dry if left in the oven too long.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Once the cod is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a couple of minutes. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and serve with lemon wedges on the side. This dish pairs beautifully with steamed vegetables, rice, or a fresh garden salad.

Tips for the Perfect Baked Cod

  • Choose Fresh Cod: Whenever possible, opt for fresh cod fillets. If using frozen, ensure they are thoroughly thawed before cooking.
  • Don’t Skip the Lemon Zest: The zest adds an aromatic quality that enhances the flavor profile of the dish.
  • Experiment with Herbs: If you want to add a personal touch, feel free to incorporate herbs like dill, thyme, or rosemary into the lemon butter mixture.
  • Monitor Cooking Time: Since every oven is different, check the cod a couple of minutes before the suggested cooking time to ensure it doesn’t overcook.

Pairing Suggestions

To create a complete meal, consider pairing your lemon butter baked cod fillets with:

  • Side Dishes: Roasted asparagus, garlic mashed potatoes, quinoa salad, or sautéed spinach with garlic.
  • Wine Pairing: A crisp white wine like Sauvignon Blanc or a light-bodied Chardonnay complements the flavors beautifully.
  • Dessert: Finish your meal with a light dessert such as lemon sorbet or a fruit salad to cleanse the palate.
