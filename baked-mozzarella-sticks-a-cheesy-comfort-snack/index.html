Baked Mozzarella Sticks: A Cheesy Comfort Snack


Who can resist the ooey-gooey deliciousness of mozzarella cheese? Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy appetizer or a tasty snack to enjoy on a cozy evening, these Baked Mozzarella Sticks are sure to hit the spot. With a crispy exterior and a warm, melty center, they’re the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 8 mozzarella string cheese sticks
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Marinara sauce, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a shallow dish, combine the bread crumbs, garlic powder, dried oregano, dried basil, paprika, and salt. Mix well.

  3. Place the all-purpose flour in another shallow dish.

  4. Dip each mozzarella stick into the flour, making sure it’s coated evenly. Shake off any excess flour.

  5. Next, dip the flour-coated mozzarella stick into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off.

  6. Roll the mozzarella stick in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the crumbs adhere to the cheese.

  7. Place the coated mozzarella stick onto the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process with the remaining cheese sticks.

  8. Bake the mozzarella sticks in the preheated oven for about 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the coating is golden brown and crispy.

  9. Remove the mozzarella sticks from the oven and let them cool for a couple of minutes.

  10. Serve the Baked Mozzarella Sticks warm with marinara sauce for dipping. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or chili powder to the breadcrumb mixture.

  • If you prefer a gluten-free option, use gluten-free breadcrumbs and flour.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese. Cheddar, Gouda, or Pepper Jack can be delicious alternatives to mozzarella.

  • To make the mozzarella sticks in advance, follow steps 1 to 7, then place the coated sticks on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and freeze until firm. Once frozen, transfer them to a freezer-safe bag and store for up to 2 months. When you’re ready to bake, simply preheat your oven and bake them for an additional 5-7 minutes.

  • These Baked Mozzarella Sticks are best enjoyed immediately after baking. However, if you have leftovers, you can reheat them in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for a few minutes until heated through.

Conclusion

Baked Mozzarella Sticks are a delightful treat that combines the irresistible taste of melted cheese with a crispy coating. Whether you’re hosting a party, looking for a quick snack, or just craving something cheesy and comforting, this recipe is a winner. With easy-to-find ingredients and simple steps, you can enjoy these homemade mozzarella sticks in no time. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the cheesy goodness of these Baked Mozzarella Sticks!
