Creative Holiday Wraps and Rolls: Quick Recipes


The holiday season is a time for celebration, family gatherings, and delicious food. As you plan your festive menus, consider adding some creative wraps and rolls to your repertoire. These dishes are not only quick to prepare but also versatile and visually appealing, making them perfect for holiday parties and family meals. Let’s dive into an array of delightful holiday wraps and rolls that will impress your guests and provide a memorable culinary experience.

1. Festive Turkey and Cranberry Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 4 oz sliced turkey breast
  • 2 tbsp cranberry sauce
  • 1/4 cup baby spinach
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 tbsp cream cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread the Cream Cheese: If using, spread the cream cheese evenly over the tortilla for added flavor and creaminess.
  2. Layer the Ingredients: Place the sliced turkey breast down the center of the tortilla. Add cranberry sauce, spinach, and shredded carrots on top.
  3. Season: Sprinkle with salt and pepper to enhance the flavor.
  4. Roll It Up: Starting from one end, roll the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go to keep the filling secure.
  5. Slice and Serve: Cut the wrap in half diagonally and serve immediately or wrap in foil for later.

This festive turkey and cranberry wrap is perfect for using up leftovers from your holiday feast or simply enjoying the classic flavors of the season.

2. Veggie and Hummus Roll-Ups

Ingredients:

  • 4 large collard green leaves (or spinach tortillas for a milder flavor)
  • 1/2 cup hummus (your favorite flavor)
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers (red, yellow, or orange)
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup grated zucchini
  • 1/4 cup shredded purple cabbage
  • Fresh herbs (parsley or cilantro) for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Leaves: If using collard greens, blanch them in boiling water for a few seconds to make them pliable. Rinse under cold water and pat dry.
  2. Spread the Hummus: Evenly spread a layer of hummus on each collard green leaf or tortilla.
  3. Add Vegetables: Layer the bell peppers, cucumber, zucchini, and purple cabbage on top of the hummus.
  4. Roll: Starting from one end, carefully roll the leaves or tortilla tightly, ensuring that the filling stays inside.
  5. Slice and Serve: Cut the rolls into bite-sized pieces and garnish with fresh herbs.

These veggie and hummus roll-ups are not only colorful but also packed with nutrients, making them an excellent option for health-conscious guests.

3. Spinach and Feta Stuffed Pita Pockets

Ingredients:

  • 2 whole wheat pita breads
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Filling: In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, feta cheese, cherry tomatoes, olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Warm the Pita: Lightly toast the pita breads in a toaster or on a skillet until warm.
  3. Stuff the Pita: Carefully cut a pocket in each pita bread and fill them generously with the spinach and feta mixture.
  4. Serve: Serve immediately while warm, or you can wrap them in foil for later.

This spinach and feta stuffed pita is a Mediterranean-inspired delight that will transport your taste buds to sun-soaked shores.

4. Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large spinach or herb tortilla
  • 4 oz smoked salmon
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup arugula
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Capers (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread the Cream Cheese: Spread the cream cheese evenly over the tortilla.
  2. Layer the Salmon: Place the smoked salmon on top of the cream cheese, followed by arugula and red onion slices.
  3. Add Capers: Scatter capers on top for extra flavor if desired.
  4. Roll It Up: Roll the tortilla tightly, securing the filling.
  5. Slice and Serve: Cut into bite-sized pieces for easy serving.

This wrap combines the rich flavors of smoked salmon and cream cheese, making it a sophisticated choice for your holiday spread.

5. Holiday Dessert Rolls: Nutella and Banana

Ingredients:

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup Nutella
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1/4 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1 tbsp crushed walnuts or pecans (optional)
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Spread Nutella: Spread a generous layer of Nutella over each tortilla.
  2. Add Fillings: Place banana slices, mini marshmallows, and crushed nuts (if using) on top of the Nutella.
  3. Roll It Up: Roll the tortillas tightly and slice them into pinwheels.
  4. Dust with Sugar: Lightly sprinkle powdered sugar over the top before serving.

These dessert rolls are a sweet way to end your holiday meal, appealing to both kids and adults alike.

6. Asian-Inspired Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded (rotisserie chicken works great)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup water chestnuts, chopped
  • 1 head of iceberg or butter lettuce

Instructions:

  1. Mix the Filling: In a bowl, combine the shredded chicken, soy sauce, sesame oil, green onions, shredded carrots, and water chestnuts.
  2. Prepare the Lettuce: Carefully separate lettuce leaves, washing and drying them.
  3. Fill the Lettuce: Spoon the chicken mixture into the center of each lettuce leaf.
  4. Serve: Serve cold or at room temperature as a refreshing holiday appetizer.

These Asian-inspired chicken lettuce wraps are light and flavorful, providing a satisfying crunch with every bite.

7. Caprese Salad Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 4 large basil leaves (or use a tortilla)
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced
  • 4 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Layer the Ingredients: If using basil leaves, layer the tomato and mozzarella on each leaf. If using a tortilla, lay down the tomato and mozzarella slices side-by-side.
  2. Drizzle: Drizzle balsamic glaze over the top and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Roll or Fold: Roll up the basil leaves or tortilla tightly.
  4. Slice and Serve: Cut as needed and enjoy as a fresh appetizer.

Caprese salad wraps are a classic combination of flavors that brighten up any holiday table.

8. Beef and Cheese Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb roast beef, sliced thin
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup pickles, sliced
  • 1 large tortilla

Instructions:

  1. Spread Cream Cheese: Spread cream cheese over the tortilla evenly.
  2. Layer Ingredients: Add slices of roast beef, Swiss cheese, and pickles on top.
  3. Roll It Up: Roll tightly, ensuring the filling is secure.
  4. Slice and Serve: Cut into pinwheels or halves to serve.

These beef and cheese wraps are hearty and satisfying, perfect for feeding a crowd during the holiday festivities.

Conclusion

Wraps and rolls are not only quick and easy to prepare, but they also offer endless combinations to suit all taste preferences. From festive turkey to vibrant veggie options, there’s a wrap for everyone at your holiday gathering. These dishes can be made ahead of time, allowing you to spend more time with loved ones and less time in the kitchen. So, get creative, mix and match ingredients, and enjoy the warmth and joy of the holiday season with these delightful wraps and rolls!
