To make this dish, start by marinating cubes of paneer in a mixture of yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Allow the paneer to soak in these flavors for at least 30 minutes. Then, grill or pan-fry the paneer until golden brown. In a separate pan, sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
