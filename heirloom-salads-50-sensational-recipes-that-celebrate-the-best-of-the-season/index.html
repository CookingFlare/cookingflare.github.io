Heirloom Salads: 50 Sensational Recipes that Celebrate the Best of the Season


Heirloom salads are on-trend and on our minds this season, as we appreciate local produce and unique flavors. With heirloom tomatoes, sweet corn, and tender greens at their peak, now is the perfect time to celebrate the best of the season with one of these 50 sensational heirloom salad recipes. These dishes are easy to make, delicious and perfect for any celebratory occasion or simple weeknight meal.

1. Heirloom Tomato Salad with Fresh Mozzarella and Basil A classic combination of juicy heirloom tomatoes, milky fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil, drizzled with a balsamic glaze.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs heirloom tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella spheres, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the heirloom tomatoes and fresh mozzarella on a serving platter, alternating slices and season with salt and black pepper.
  2. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  3. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  4. Serve immediately.

2. Summer Heirloom Tomato and Avocado Salad Sweet and juicy heirloom tomatoes, creamy avocado, and tangy red onions combined with a citrus vinaigrette.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs heirloom tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 large avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp honey

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, honey, salt, and pepper.
  2. Arrange the heirloom tomatoes, avocado, and sliced onion on a platter.
  3. Drizzle with the dressing and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately.

3. Herbed Quinoa Salad with Heirloom Tomatoes and Feta A refreshing salad with protein-rich quinoa, inviting herbs, feta cheese, and juicy heirloom tomatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 1/2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 cups heirloom cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a medium pot, bring vegetable broth, quinoa, olive oil, salt, and pepper to a boil over high heat.
  2. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the liquid is absorbed and quinoa is fluffy, about 15 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, heirloom cherry tomatoes, parsley, basil, mint, and feta cheese. Mix well to combine. Serve warm or chilled.

Those are just a few incredible heirloom salad recipes to enjoy the season’s best. With 50 more recipes to discover, your taste buds will be delighted and impressed each and every time. So go ahead, pick your favorite recipe from our list, and create an unforgettable, flavorsome salad that your family and friends will absolutely love. Don’t forget to swap the recipes regularly to experience the delight of new flavors and ingredients throughout the season. Happy cooking!
