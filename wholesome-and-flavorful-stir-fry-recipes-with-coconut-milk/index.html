Wholesome and Flavorful Stir Fry Recipes with Coconut Milk


Stir fry recipes are a fantastic way to create quick and delicious meals that are packed with flavor. By incorporating coconut milk into your stir fry, you can add a creamy and wholesome element that takes these dishes to a whole new level. In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering stir fry recipes that feature coconut milk as a star ingredient. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds and impress your friends and family with these delightful creations!

Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry with Coconut Milk

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup fresh Thai basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, snap peas, and mushrooms. Sauté for 3-4 minutes, until slightly softened.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, and brown sugar.
  7. Pour the coconut milk mixture into the skillet with the vegetables and bring to a simmer.
  8. Add the cooked chicken back to the skillet and stir to combine.
  9. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until everything is heated through.
  10. Remove from heat and stir in the fresh Thai basil leaves.
  11. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Spicy Shrimp and Vegetable Stir Fry with Coconut Milk

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until softened.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, carrots, and snow peas. Sauté for 4-5 minutes, until slightly tender.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, red curry paste, soy sauce, fish sauce, and lime juice.
  7. Pour the coconut milk mixture into the skillet with the vegetables and bring to a simmer.
  8. Add the cooked shrimp back to the skillet and stir to combine.
  9. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until everything is heated through.
  10. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  11. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Creamy Vegetable Stir Fry with Coconut Milk

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas, etc.)
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch, dissolved in 2 tablespoons water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until softened.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes, until slightly tender.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, soy sauce, fish sauce, lime juice, and cornstarch mixture.
  5. Pour the coconut milk mixture into the skillet with the vegetables and bring to a simmer.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Conclusion

Coconut milk adds a creamy and indulgent touch to stir fry recipes, elevating them to a whole new level of deliciousness. These wholesome and flavorful stir fry recipes are perfect for those looking to explore new flavors and create exciting meals that are both satisfying and nourishing. Whether you choose to make the Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry, the Spicy Shrimp and Vegetable Stir Fry, or the Creamy Vegetable Stir Fry, your taste buds are in for a treat. So, grab your skillet and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with these delectable coconut milk-infused stir fry recipes. Enjoy!
