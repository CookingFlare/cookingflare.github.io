Slow Cooker Chicken Alfredo Recipes for Creamy Italian Delights


Are you craving a delicious and creamy Italian dish but don’t have the time to spend hours in the kitchen? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering slow cooker chicken Alfredo recipes that will satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. These recipes are not only easy to make but also packed with rich flavors that will transport you straight to Italy. So, let’s dive in and discover the wonders of slow cooker chicken Alfredo!

Classic Slow Cooker Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound fettuccine, cooked according to package instructions

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides. Transfer the chicken to the slow cooker.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the skillet to release any browned bits. Pour the mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker.

  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 4-6 hours or on high for 2-3 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  4. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  5. Stir in the heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for an additional 30 minutes, until the sauce is creamy and heated through.

  6. Serve the chicken Alfredo over cooked fettuccine and garnish with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired. Enjoy!

Creamy Garlic and Mushroom Slow Cooker Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound linguine, cooked according to package instructions

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter and olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken thighs and cook until browned on both sides. Transfer the chicken to the slow cooker.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sliced mushrooms. Cook for about 5 minutes, until the mushrooms are softened and golden brown. Pour in the chicken broth and stir to scrape up any browned bits. Transfer the mixture to the slow cooker.

  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  4. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  5. Stir in the heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for an additional 30 minutes, until the sauce is creamy and heated through.

  6. Serve the chicken Alfredo over cooked linguine and garnish with fresh thyme leaves, if desired. Enjoy the creamy goodness!

Conclusion

With these slow cooker chicken Alfredo recipes, you can indulge in the flavors of Italy without spending hours in the kitchen. The convenience of the slow cooker allows you to set it and forget it, while still achieving a creamy and delicious meal. Whether you prefer the classic version or the addition of garlic and mushrooms, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings for creamy Italian delights. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to enjoy a taste of Italy in the comfort of your own home!
