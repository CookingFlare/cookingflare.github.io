Fall Baking: Delicious Recipes with Seasonal Apples


As the leaves begin to turn and the crisp autumn air sets in, our thoughts turn to the warmth and comfort of the kitchen. There’s something special about baking during the fall season, when the aroma of cinnamon and nutmeg wafts through the air, and the taste of fresh apples is at its peak. In this post, we’ll explore some of the most delicious fall baking recipes that showcase the star of the season: the humble apple.

Apple Cider Donuts

There’s nothing quite like the taste of a freshly baked donut, especially when it’s infused with the sweet, tangy flavor of apple cider. These donuts are a must-try for any fall baking enthusiast.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup apple cider
  • 1 egg
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and yeast.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until a smooth batter forms.
  4. Add the apple cider and mix until well combined.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the dough rise in a warm place for about an hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  6. Preheat the oil in a deep frying pan to 350째F.
  7. Once the oil is hot, use a cookie scoop or a spoon to drop the dough into the oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  8. Cook the donuts for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  9. Remove the donuts from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  10. Dust the donuts with confectioners’ sugar and serve warm.

Apple Crisp

There’s nothing quite like the classic combination of tender apples, crunchy oat topping, and sweet caramel sauce. This apple crisp recipe is a staple of fall baking, and for good reason.

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Mix until the apples are evenly coated with the dry ingredients.
  3. Transfer the apple mixture to a 9x9-inch baking dish and set aside.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the rolled oats, brown sugar, and chopped walnuts (if using).
  5. Add the unsalted butter to the oat mixture and mix until the mixture is crumbly.
  6. Spread the oat mixture evenly over the apple filling.
  7. Bake the apple crisp for 35-40 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apples are tender.
  8. Serve warm, topped with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream if desired.

Apple Cinnamon Swirl Bread

There’s something special about the combination of sweet, fluffy bread and the warm spices of fall. This apple cinnamon swirl bread recipe is a must-try for any bread enthusiast.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup chopped apples
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, sugar, and yeast.
  3. Add the warm water, melted butter, and egg to the dry ingredients and mix until a smooth dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about an hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375째F.
  7. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a rectangle, about 1/4-inch thick.
  8. In a small bowl, mix together the chopped apples, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon.
  9. Spread the apple mixture evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
  10. Roll the dough into a tight log, starting from the long side.
  11. Place the log seam-side down in a greased 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  12. Bake the bread for 35-40 minutes, or until it is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  13. Let the bread cool on a wire rack for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Apple Cheddar Scones

There’s something special about the combination of sweet, tender apples and sharp, tangy cheddar cheese. These apple cheddar scones are a must-try for any fall baking enthusiast.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped apples
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large egg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and cold butter. Use a pastry blender or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add the grated cheddar cheese, chopped apples, salt, baking powder, and baking soda to the flour mixture. Mix until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and egg.
  5. Add the cream and egg mixture to the flour mixture and mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead it for 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  7. Pat the dough into a circle, about 1-inch thick.
  8. Use a biscuit cutter or the rim of a glass to cut out scones.
  9. Place the scones on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  10. Brush the tops of the scones with heavy cream and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  11. Serve warm, with butter or clotted cream if desired.

Apple Cream Cheese Bars

There’s something special about the combination of sweet, tender apples and tangy, creamy cream cheese. These apple cream cheese bars are a must-try for any fall baking enthusiast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped apples
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and cold butter. Use a pastry blender or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
  5. Add the granulated sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract to the cream cheese and mix until well combined.
  6. Stir in the chopped apples and salt.
  7. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust.
  8. Bake the bars for 35-40 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the center is set.
  9. Let the bars cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares and serving.

These are just a few of the many delicious fall baking recipes that showcase the star of the season: the humble apple. Whether you’re looking for a sweet treat to satisfy your cravings or a savory dish to impress your guests, these recipes are sure to hit the spot. So go ahead, get baking, and enjoy the flavors of fall!
