Decadent Cookies and Cream Cake: A Sweet Tooth's Dream


If you’re a fan of cookies and cream, get ready to indulge in a dessert that will satisfy all your sweet cravings. This Decadent Cookies and Cream Cake is a dream come true for those with a serious sweet tooth. Each bite is a perfect combination of rich, moist cake and creamy, velvety frosting, all infused with the delightful crunch of chocolate cookies. Trust me, this cake is pure bliss!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup crushed chocolate cookies (Oreo or your favorite brand)

For the frosting:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup crushed chocolate cookies (for decoration)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.

  3. Add the softened butter to the dry ingredients. Mix on low speed until the butter is fully incorporated and the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, buttermilk, and vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture, beating on medium speed until the batter is smooth and well combined.

  6. Gently fold in the crushed chocolate cookies, ensuring that they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  7. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  9. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. While the cakes are cooling, prepare the frosting. In a chilled mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract. Continue to whip until stiff peaks form.

  11. Once the cakes have cooled completely, place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread a generous amount of the whipped cream frosting on top.

  12. Gently place the second cake layer on top of the frosting, making sure it is centered.

  13. Use the remaining whipped cream frosting to cover the top and sides of the cake. Smooth it out with a spatula for a polished finish.

  14. Sprinkle the crushed chocolate cookies over the top of the cake, pressing them gently into the frosting for decoration.

  15. For best results, refrigerate the cake for at least an hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

  16. Slice and serve this decadent Cookies and Cream Cake, and watch as your friends and family’s eyes light up with delight!

This cake is perfect for birthdays, special occasions, or simply when you’re in the mood for a seriously indulgent treat. It’s a guaranteed crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone wanting more. So go ahead, give in to your sweet tooth and enjoy every delectable bite of this Cookies and Cream Cake!
