Caramel Apple Cinnamon Rolls: A Sweet Morning Treat


There’s something magical about the combination of warm cinnamon, gooey caramel, and tender apples. When these ingredients come together in a soft, pillowy roll, the result is nothing short of heavenly. Caramel apple cinnamon rolls are the perfect indulgence for a cozy weekend morning or a special holiday brunch. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the steps to create these delightful treats from scratch. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 medium apples, finely chopped

For the Caramel Glaze:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. Warm the milk until it’s around 110°F. Sprinkle the yeast over the warm milk and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, softened butter, eggs, and salt. Add the yeast mixture and flour. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon to create a smooth paste.
  2. Roll out the dough into a large rectangle. Spread the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the chopped apples over the cinnamon sugar mixture.

Step 3: Roll and Cut the Cinnamon Rolls

  1. Starting with the long edge, tightly roll the dough into a log. Cut the log into 12 equal pieces.
  2. Place the rolls in a greased 9x13 inch baking pan, cover them, and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

Step 4: Bake the Cinnamon Rolls

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Once the rolls have risen, bake them for 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

Step 5: Prepare the Caramel Glaze

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the brown sugar, milk, and salt. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, stirring constantly, and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Let the caramel glaze cool for a few minutes.

Step 6: Glaze the Cinnamon Rolls

  1. Pour the warm caramel glaze over the baked cinnamon rolls, allowing it to seep into the crevices.

Serving

Once the caramel apple cinnamon rolls are glazed and slightly cooled, they are ready to be served. These rolls are best enjoyed fresh from the oven, with a steaming cup of coffee or a glass of cold milk. Their sweet, cinnamon-spiced aroma will fill your kitchen, creating an irresistible invitation to indulge in this delightful morning treat.

Conclusion

Caramel apple cinnamon rolls are a delightful twist on the classic cinnamon roll, combining the comforting flavors of caramel and apple with the warmth of cinnamon. Whether you’re treating your family to a special breakfast or impressing guests at a brunch, these sweet, gooey rolls are sure to be a hit. So, next time you’re in the mood for a baking adventure, give this recipe a try and savor the irresistible goodness of homemade caramel apple cinnamon rolls. Enjoy!
