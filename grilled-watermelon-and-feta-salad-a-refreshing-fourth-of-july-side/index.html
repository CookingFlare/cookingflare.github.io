Grilled Watermelon and Feta Salad: A Refreshing Fourth of July Side


Summer is in full swing, and nothing says Fourth of July quite like a refreshing and vibrant salad to accompany your grilled favorites. This Grilled Watermelon and Feta Salad is the perfect addition to your holiday spread. The combination of juicy watermelon, creamy feta cheese, and a tangy dressing creates a flavor explosion that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1 small seedless watermelon
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, torn
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Meanwhile, slice the watermelon into 1-inch thick rounds.

  2. Lightly brush both sides of the watermelon slices with olive oil. This will prevent sticking and add a subtle richness to the salad.

  3. Carefully place the watermelon slices on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear. The heat will intensify the sweetness of the watermelon and add a hint of smokiness to the dish.

  4. Remove the grilled watermelon slices from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes. Once cool, cut them into bite-sized cubes or wedges, whichever you prefer.

  5. In a large bowl, combine the grilled watermelon, crumbled feta cheese, and torn mint leaves. Gently toss the ingredients together to ensure even distribution.

  6. Drizzle the balsamic glaze and extra-virgin olive oil over the salad. Season with salt and pepper to taste. The combination of the sweet balsamic glaze and the salty feta cheese will elevate the flavors of the salad.

  7. Give the salad a final toss to coat all the ingredients in the dressing. The vibrant colors and contrasting textures make this salad a visual delight.

  8. Serve the Grilled Watermelon and Feta Salad immediately and watch as your guests’ eyes light up with delight. This salad is a refreshing and unexpected twist on a classic summer fruit, perfect for your Fourth of July celebration.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of freshness, add some arugula or baby spinach to the salad. The peppery greens will complement the sweet watermelon and tangy feta cheese.

  • If you don’t have access to a grill, you can achieve a similar effect by using a grill pan on your stovetop. Simply heat the grill pan over medium-high heat and follow the same instructions for grilling the watermelon slices.

  • Experiment with different herbs and dressings to customize the flavors of the salad. Basil, cilantro, or even a drizzle of honey can add exciting twists to this already delicious dish.

  • If you’re serving the salad at a later time, keep the dressing separate until you’re ready to serve. This will prevent the watermelon from becoming too watery and the flavors from getting diluted.

Conclusion

This Grilled Watermelon and Feta Salad is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will make your Fourth of July celebration truly memorable. The smoky sweetness of the grilled watermelon, the creamy and salty feta cheese, and the vibrant freshness of the mint leaves come together in a harmonious symphony of taste. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or attending a potluck, this salad is sure to be a hit. So fire up the grill, slice up some watermelon, and get ready to wow your guests with this refreshing and unique side dish. Happy Fourth of July!
