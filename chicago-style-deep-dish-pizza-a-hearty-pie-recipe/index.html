Chicago-Style Deep Dish Pizza: A Hearty Pie Recipe

3 min · 549 words

Chicago-style deep dish pizza is a beloved dish that originated in the Windy City. Known for its thick and buttery crust, generous layers of cheese, and chunky tomato sauce, this pizza is a true delight for pizza lovers. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of making your very own Chicago-style deep dish pizza from scratch. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to indulge in a slice of pizza heaven!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/8 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the sauce:

  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the filling:

  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 can (4 ounces) sliced black olives, drained

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt, and yeast. Mix well.

  3. Add the warm water and melted butter to the dry ingredients. Stir until a soft dough forms.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a well-floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Grease a deep-dish pizza pan (10-inch or 12-inch) with cooking spray or olive oil.

  6. Press the dough into the bottom and sides of the greased pan, making sure to create a thick crust.

  7. In a separate bowl, prepare the sauce by combining the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well incorporated.

  8. Spread half of the sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  9. Layer half of the mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, Italian sausage, green bell pepper, onion, and black olives over the sauce.

  10. Repeat the layers with the remaining ingredients, finishing with a final layer of sauce on top.

  11. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  12. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize your Chicago-style deep dish pizza by adding your favorite toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, or even spinach.
  • For a vegetarian version, omit the Italian sausage and add more veggies like zucchini or artichoke hearts.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the pizza before baking.
  • Serve your pizza with a side of marinara sauce or ranch dressing for dipping.

Enjoy this hearty and delicious Chicago-style deep dish pizza with your family and friends. Its thick crust, gooey cheese, and flavorful toppings will leave everyone craving for more. Make sure to share this recipe with your fellow pizza enthusiasts and let us know how it turned out for you. Happy baking!
