Budget-Friendly Instant Pot Creations


The Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking, providing a fast and efficient way to prepare meals without sacrificing flavor. Whether you’re a busy professional, a parent on-the-go, or simply someone looking to save time and money while cooking, the Instant Pot is your perfect kitchen companion. It’s an invaluable tool that can help create hearty, flavorful meals on a budget. In this post, we’ll explore some amazing Instant Pot recipes that are both budget-friendly and satisfying, proving that you don’t need to break the bank to eat well.

Why Choose the Instant Pot for Budget-Friendly Meals?

The Instant Pot combines multiple kitchen appliances into one, functioning as a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, yogurt maker, and even a sauté pan. The versatility of this kitchen gadget allows you to make everything from soups and stews to rice and meat dishes in record time.

But what really makes the Instant Pot perfect for budget-conscious cooks is its ability to tenderize tougher cuts of meat quickly. Instead of opting for expensive, premium cuts of beef or chicken, you can use more affordable options like chicken thighs, pork shoulder, and chuck roast. The pressure cooking function helps to break down the fibers in these cuts, resulting in tender, flavorful dishes without the high price tag.

Additionally, the Instant Pot uses a sealed cooking environment, trapping moisture and ensuring that your ingredients stay juicy and full of flavor, which makes even the simplest ingredients shine. Let’s dive into some tasty and budget-friendly Instant Pot recipes that won’t drain your wallet.

1. Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

Chicken and rice is a staple meal in many households, and it’s easy to make in the Instant Pot with minimal effort. This simple yet flavorful dish is full of protein and carbs, making it an ideal comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 bone-in chicken thighs (skin on or off, your preference)
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add olive oil and allow it to heat up.
  2. Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, paprika, thyme, and oregano.
  3. Brown the chicken thighs in the Instant Pot for about 4 minutes per side. Remove them and set aside.
  4. Add diced onion and garlic to the pot, sautéing for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Stir in rice, ensuring it’s coated with the onions and garlic.
  6. Add chicken broth and place the chicken thighs back on top of the rice mixture.
  7. Close the Instant Pot lid, set it to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 12 minutes.
  8. Once done, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before manually releasing the remaining pressure.
  9. Fluff the rice and serve. Enjoy!

This dish combines savory chicken with the comforting texture of rice, all cooked in one pot with minimal cleanup.

2. Instant Pot Beef Stew

Hearty beef stew is a comforting, filling meal that can feed a crowd without costing a fortune. The Instant Pot helps make this dish quickly, even with tougher cuts of meat like chuck roast, which is typically more affordable than other beef cuts.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 lbs chuck roast, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch (optional for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add olive oil.
  2. Season the beef cubes with salt and pepper, then brown them in batches, about 5 minutes per batch.
  3. After the beef is browned, remove it from the pot and set aside.
  4. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot, sautéing for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Stir in the tomato paste, allowing it to cook for 1 minute.
  6. Pour in the beef broth, scraping the bottom of the pot to deglaze.
  7. Return the beef to the pot along with carrots, potatoes, rosemary, and thyme. Stir to combine.
  8. Close the Instant Pot lid, set to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  9. Allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  10. If you prefer a thicker stew, mix cornstarch with a little water to make a slurry, then stir it into the stew. Let it cook on sauté mode for 3-4 minutes until thickened.
  11. Stir in frozen peas if desired and serve. Enjoy the hearty, comforting flavors!

This recipe is an excellent way to feed your family a rich, flavorful meal without expensive ingredients.

3. Instant Pot Lentil Soup

Lentils are one of the most affordable sources of protein and fiber, making them an excellent choice for budget-conscious meals. This Instant Pot lentil soup is nutritious, delicious, and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups dried lentils, rinsed
  • 6 cups vegetable broth (or water)
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add olive oil.
  2. Add the diced onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 5 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 1 minute.
  4. Add the lentils, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, and bay leaf. Stir to combine.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid, set it to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.
  6. Allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  7. Remove the bay leaf, stir in lemon juice for brightness (optional), and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve with a slice of crusty bread for a full meal.

This soup is perfect for those looking for a light, nutritious, and budget-friendly meal. It’s easy to make in the Instant Pot and provides plenty of leftovers for future meals.

4. Instant Pot Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish that’s both filling and affordable. With the Instant Pot, you can create this delicious meal in half the time of traditional methods, using inexpensive ground meat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef (or ground turkey for a leaner option)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 cups water or beef broth
  • 12 oz spaghetti (uncooked)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add olive oil.
  2. Brown the ground beef, breaking it apart as it cooks. Once browned, remove excess fat.
  3. Add the diced onion, garlic, carrot, and celery to the pot. Sauté for about 5 minutes until softened.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste, red wine (if using), oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes and water (or broth). Stir to combine.
  6. Break the uncooked spaghetti in half and add it to the pot. Press the pasta down into the sauce to ensure it’s submerged.
  7. Close the Instant Pot lid, set it to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  8. Once done, allow for a quick pressure release.
  9. Stir the pasta and sauce together. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.

This Instant Pot version of Spaghetti Bolognese is a weeknight wonder. It’s quick, satisfying, and budget-friendly, yet doesn’t skimp on flavor.

5. Instant Pot Vegetable Curry

A vegetable curry is a healthy and affordable way to enjoy a flavorful meal. By using a variety of vegetables and a simple curry sauce, you can create a vibrant and filling dish in your Instant Pot.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add coconut oil.
  2. Add diced onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Stir in curry powder, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 1 minute.
  4. Add the coconut milk, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Stir to combine.
  5. Add the potatoes, cauliflower, carrots, and green beans. Stir to mix.
  6. Close the Instant Pot lid, set to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  7. Once done, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  8. Stir in lemon juice and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

This vegetable curry is rich in flavors and textures, and it’s perfect for vegetarians or anyone seeking a meatless meal that’s both filling and affordable.

Conclusion

The Instant Pot is a game-changer for budget-conscious cooks looking to make quick, satisfying meals. By using affordable ingredients like chicken thighs, chuck roast, lentils, and potatoes, you can create a wide variety of delicious dishes that are perfect for busy weeknights or family dinners. These budget-friendly Instant Pot creations are not only simple to make but also full of flavor, ensuring you never have to sacrifice taste for savings. So, the next time you’re looking to whip up a hearty meal without breaking the bank, grab your Instant Pot and try out one of these recipes!
