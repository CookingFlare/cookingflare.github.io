Pine Nut and Basil Farro Salad: A Nutty and Fresh Grain Option


Are you looking for a healthy and flavorful grain option that will delight your taste buds? Look no further than this Pine Nut and Basil Farro Salad! Packed with nutty flavors, vibrant colors, and wholesome ingredients, this salad is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup farro
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the farro and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 20 minutes or until the farro is tender. Drain any excess liquid and set aside to cool.

  2. While the farro is cooking, heat a small skillet over medium heat. Add the pine nuts and toast them, stirring frequently, until they turn golden brown and become fragrant. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked farro, toasted pine nuts, cherry tomatoes, fresh basil leaves, red onion, and feta cheese. Toss gently to combine.

  4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss again to ensure everything is well coated.

  5. Serve the Pine Nut and Basil Farro Salad chilled or at room temperature. It’s a perfect accompaniment to grilled chicken or fish, or can be enjoyed on its own as a light and refreshing meal.

Why Choose Farro?

Farro, an ancient grain, is truly a nutritional powerhouse. It is rich in fiber, protein, and essential vitamins and minerals. This nutty and chewy grain adds a delightful texture to salads and other dishes. Unlike refined grains, farro retains its bran and germ, making it a whole grain that contributes to better digestion and overall health.

The Benefits of Pine Nuts

Pine nuts not only provide a delightful crunch to this salad, but they also offer numerous health benefits. They are an excellent source of heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, which help reduce bad cholesterol levels. Additionally, pine nuts are packed with essential minerals like magnesium, potassium, and iron, making them a valuable addition to any diet.

A Burst of Freshness with Basil

Fresh basil adds a burst of freshness and a fragrant aroma to this salad. It not only enhances the flavor profile but also offers a range of health benefits. Basil is known for its anti-inflammatory properties and is a rich source of antioxidants. It also supports digestion and can help alleviate stress, making it a perfect addition to your daily meals.

Final Thoughts

Incorporating nutritious and delicious ingredients into your meals doesn’t have to be complicated. This Pine Nut and Basil Farro Salad is a testament to that. With its wholesome grains, crunchy pine nuts, and vibrant basil, it’s a dish that satisfies both your taste buds and your nutritional needs. So go ahead, give this recipe a try and enjoy a nutty and fresh grain option that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.
