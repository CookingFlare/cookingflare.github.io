Crispy Sesame Ginger Shrimp: A Flavorful Asian Delight


If you’re a fan of Asian cuisine and are looking to add some pizzazz to your dinner repertoire, then you’re in for a treat with this Crispy Sesame Ginger Shrimp recipe. This dish is a delightful fusion of flavors, combining the crunch of crispy shrimp with the aromatic and zesty notes of sesame and ginger. Whether you’re planning a special dinner for guests or simply want to elevate your weeknight meal, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Shrimp:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Sesame Ginger Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water

For Garnish:

  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Shrimp

  1. Pat the shrimp dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  2. Place the cornstarch in a shallow dish.
  3. Dip each shrimp into the beaten eggs, then coat with the cornstarch, shaking off any excess.

Step 2: Fry the Shrimp

  1. In a large skillet, heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add the coated shrimp in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  3. Fry the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  4. Remove the shrimp from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 3: Make the Sesame Ginger Sauce

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water to create a slurry.
  4. Slowly pour the slurry into the saucepan, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
  5. Remove the sauce from the heat.

Step 4: Toss the Shrimp in the Sauce

  1. In a large bowl, toss the fried shrimp with the sesame ginger sauce until they are evenly coated.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

  1. Transfer the shrimp to a serving platter.
  2. Sprinkle with sliced green onions and sesame seeds for a pop of color and texture.
  3. Serve the Crispy Sesame Ginger Shrimp immediately, and enjoy the delightful combination of crispy, savory, and sweet flavors.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sesame ginger sauce.
  • Serve the shrimp over steamed jasmine rice or alongside stir-fried vegetables for a complete meal.
  • Substitute the shrimp with chicken for a delicious alternative.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Crispy Sesame Ginger Shrimp recipe is a perfect balance of crispy texture and vibrant Asian flavors. The combination of the golden-fried shrimp and the aromatic sesame ginger sauce creates a truly delightful dish that will impress your family and friends. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting to explore Asian cuisine, this recipe is a must-try. So, roll up your sleeves, get your wok ready, and embark on a culinary journey that will transport you to the bustling streets of Asia with every delectable bite.
