Delightful Belgian Waffles: 6 Recipes for Breakfast Bliss


Belgian waffles are a breakfast favorite, loved for their light and fluffy texture and deliciously crisp exterior. Whether you prefer them plain, topped with fresh fruits, or drizzled with syrup, there’s no denying the joy these waffles bring to the start of your day. In this post, we’ll explore six delightful Belgian waffle recipes that are sure to satisfy your breakfast cravings. So, let’s dive right in!

Classic Belgian Waffles

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 5 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs, separated
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. In another bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the beaten egg whites into the batter.
  5. Preheat your waffle iron according to its instructions. Once hot, pour the batter onto the iron and cook until golden brown and crisp.
  6. Serve the waffles warm with your favorite toppings, such as fresh berries, whipped cream, or maple syrup.
