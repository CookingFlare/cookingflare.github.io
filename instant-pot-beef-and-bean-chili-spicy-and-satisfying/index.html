Instant Pot Beef and Bean Chili: Spicy and Satisfying


If you’re looking for a hearty, flavorful, and easy-to-make meal, look no further than this Instant Pot beef and bean chili recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and a blend of spices, this chili is the perfect comfort food for any occasion. The best part? It can be ready to serve in under an hour, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of beef chuck, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Saute” function. Once hot, add the olive oil.

  2. Add the diced beef chuck to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  3. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced red bell pepper to the Instant Pot. Saute for another 3-5 minutes until the vegetables are softened.

  4. Stir in the tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, paprika, and oregano, coating the beef and vegetables evenly with the spices.

  5. Pour in the diced tomatoes and beef broth, then add the drained and rinsed kidney beans and black beans. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Bean/Chili” function and set the timer for 25 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes, then carefully perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Open the lid and give the chili a good stir. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  9. Serve the chili hot, garnished with your favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, sliced jalapenos, or fresh cilantro.

Tips and Variations

  • For a leaner option, you can use ground beef instead of beef chuck.
  • If you prefer a spicier chili, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a diced jalapeno during the cooking process.
  • This chili can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 4 days, making it a great option for meal prep.
  • To make it a complete meal, serve the chili over cooked rice or alongside a slice of warm cornbread.

Conclusion

With its rich and robust flavors, this Instant Pot beef and bean chili is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, feeding a hungry family, or simply craving a comforting bowl of chili, this recipe delivers on taste and convenience. The Instant Pot streamlines the cooking process, allowing you to enjoy a homemade chili without the long simmering time. So go ahead, grab your Instant Pot and ingredients, and treat yourself to a bowl of this spicy and satisfying beef and bean chili!
