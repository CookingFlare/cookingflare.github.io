Delicious Dessert Pizza Recipes: Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth


Who doesn’t love pizza? It’s a universally loved dish that comes in countless variations, from the classic Margherita to the more adventurous BBQ chicken. But have you ever considered using pizza as a canvas for a delectable dessert? That’s right, dessert pizzas are a thing, and they are a fantastic way to satisfy your sweet tooth in a unique and delightful manner. In this blog post, we’re going to explore some mouthwatering dessert pizza recipes that will surely become a hit at your next gathering or family dinner. So, let’s dive into the world of sweet, indulgent, and utterly delicious dessert pizzas!

Classic Fruit Dessert Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Assorted fresh fruits (strawberries, kiwi, blueberries, etc.)
  • 1/4 cup apricot preserves
  • 2 tbsp water
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown. Allow it to cool completely.
  3. In a bowl, mix the softened cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Spread the cream cheese mixture over the cooled pizza crust.
  5. Arrange the fresh fruits on top of the cream cheese layer.
  6. In a small saucepan, heat the apricot preserves and water until melted. Brush this glaze over the fruit.
  7. Garnish with mint leaves and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

This classic fruit dessert pizza is a delightful combination of creamy, sweet, and tangy flavors that will surely impress your guests.

Decadent Chocolate Lover’s Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1 cup chocolate-hazelnut spread
  • 1/2 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup chopped hazelnuts
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown. Allow it to cool slightly.
  3. Spread the chocolate-hazelnut spread over the cooled pizza crust.
  4. Sprinkle the mini marshmallows, chocolate chips, and chopped hazelnuts evenly over the chocolate spread.
  5. Place the pizza back in the oven for 5-7 minutes or until the marshmallows are golden and gooey.
  6. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before dusting with powdered sugar.

This indulgent chocolate lover’s pizza is a dream come true for anyone with a sweet tooth. It’s gooey, chocolatey, and utterly irresistible.

Caramel Apple Streusel Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 2 large apples, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup cold butter, cubed
  • Caramel sauce for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a parchment-lined baking sheet and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, toss the thinly sliced apples with granulated sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  4. Arrange the apple slices over the pizza dough.
  5. In another bowl, combine the flour, oats, and cubed butter, and mix until crumbly.
  6. Sprinkle the streusel mixture over the apple slices.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the streusel is golden brown and the apples are tender.
  8. Drizzle with caramel sauce before serving.

This caramel apple streusel pizza is a delightful marriage of warm, spiced apples, buttery streusel, and luscious caramel – a true taste of fall in every bite.

There you have it – three incredibly tempting dessert pizza recipes that will elevate your love for pizza to a whole new level. Whether you’re a fan of fruity flavors, a chocoholic, or a lover of all things caramel, there’s a dessert pizza here for you. So, the next time you’re craving something sweet and unique, why not whip up one of these delightful dessert pizzas? They’re sure to be a hit with kids and adults alike. Enjoy the sweet journey of dessert pizza creation!
