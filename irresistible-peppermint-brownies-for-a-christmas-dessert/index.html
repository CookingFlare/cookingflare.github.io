Irresistible Peppermint Brownies for a Christmas Dessert


The holiday season is upon us, and what better way to celebrate than with a batch of decadent peppermint brownies? These irresistible treats are perfect for Christmas dessert, combining the rich, fudgy goodness of brownies with the refreshing kick of peppermint. Whether you’re hosting a festive gathering or simply craving a cozy indulgence, these peppermint brownies are sure to delight your taste buds and spread the holiday cheer.

Ingredients

For the Brownie Batter:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

For the Peppermint Swirl:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon red food coloring

For the Topping:

  • Crushed candy canes
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and granulated sugar. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, and mix until well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.

  4. In another bowl, beat the cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth. Add the egg, peppermint extract, and red food coloring, and mix until fully incorporated.

  5. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it evenly. Drop spoonfuls of the peppermint swirl mixture on top of the brownie batter. Use a knife to create a swirl pattern by gently dragging it through the batter.

  6. Bake the brownies for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Be careful not to overbake.

  7. Once baked, remove the brownies from the oven and allow them to cool in the pan for 15 minutes. Sprinkle the crushed candy canes over the top, followed by a light dusting of powdered sugar.

  8. Once completely cooled, cut the brownies into squares and serve. Enjoy the delightful combination of rich chocolate, creamy peppermint, and satisfying crunch in every bite.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra festive touch, consider adding green food coloring to the peppermint swirl mixture.
  • Store any leftover brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Conclusion

With their luscious fudgy texture, enchanting swirls of peppermint, and a sprinkling of candy cane magic, these peppermint brownies are a delightful addition to your Christmas dessert spread. Whether you’re sharing them with loved ones or savoring them by the twinkling lights of the tree, these irresistible treats are a sweet reminder of the joy and warmth of the holiday season. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and let the aroma of peppermint and chocolate fill your home as you whip up a batch of these delectable peppermint brownies. Merry Christmas and happy baking!
