Thai Curries Made Easy: Quick and Delicious Recipes


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you know just how flavorful and aromatic Thai curries can be. The combination of spices, herbs, and creamy coconut milk creates a tantalizing dance of flavors that can transport you to the streets of Bangkok with just one bite. In this blog post, we will explore some quick and delicious Thai curry recipes that you can easily recreate in your own kitchen.

Green Curry with Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons green curry paste
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 500 grams chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • Fresh Thai basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and stir well to combine with the curry paste. Bring it to a gentle simmer.
  3. Add the chicken slices and cook until they are no longer pink in the center.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for another 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  5. Season the curry with fish sauce and palm sugar, adjusting the quantities to your taste.
  6. Garnish with fresh Thai basil leaves and serve hot with steamed rice.

Red Curry with Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 500 grams shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a medium-sized pot, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add the red curry paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and bring it to a gentle simmer, stirring well to combine with the curry paste.
  3. Add the shrimp and bamboo shoots, and cook until the shrimp turn pink and are cooked through.
  4. Season the curry with fish sauce and lime juice, adjusting the quantities to your taste.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with steamed rice.

Yellow Curry with Tofu and Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons yellow curry paste
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 200 grams firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, snap peas)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Fresh lime wedges, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the yellow curry paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and stir well to combine with the curry paste. Bring it to a gentle simmer.
  3. Add the tofu cubes and mixed vegetables, and cook for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Season the curry with soy sauce and honey, adjusting the quantities to your taste.
  5. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the curry before serving, and garnish with lime wedges.

Massaman Curry with Beef

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons massaman curry paste
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 500 grams beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add the massaman curry paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and bring it to a gentle simmer, stirring well to combine with the curry paste.
  3. Add the beef slices and cook until they are no longer pink in the center.
  4. Add the potatoes and roasted peanuts, and simmer until the potatoes are tender.
  5. Season the curry with tamarind paste and brown sugar, adjusting the quantities to your taste.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with steamed rice.

Final Thoughts

Thai curries are a fantastic way to explore the vibrant flavors of Thailand right in your own kitchen. Whether you prefer the fiery heat of a red curry or the fragrant blend of spices in a yellow curry, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, go ahead and give them a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
