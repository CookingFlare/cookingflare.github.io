Sous Vide Garlic Herb Pork Chops: A Juicy and Flavorful Recipe


Sous vide cooking has revolutionized the way we cook, allowing us to achieve precise temperature control and consistent results every time. In this recipe, we’ll be using the sous vide method to cook garlic herb pork chops to perfection, resulting in a juicy and flavorful dish that’s sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 4 pork chops (1-1.5 inches thick)
  • 4 cloves of garlic, peeled and minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Preheat the sous vide water bath: Set the sous vide machine to 130°F (54°C). While the machine is heating up, prepare the pork chops.
  2. Season the pork chops: In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic, olive oil, rosemary, thyme, salt, and black pepper. Rub the mixture all over the pork chops, making sure they’re evenly coated.
  3. Sear the pork chops: Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a small amount of oil. Sear the pork chops for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until they develop a nice brown crust. Remove the pork chops from the skillet and set them aside.
  4. Sous vide the pork chops: Place the seared pork chops in a sous vide bag or a zip-top plastic bag. Add the chicken broth to the bag and seal it, making sure to remove as much air as possible.
  5. Cook the pork chops: Place the sous vide bag in the preheated water bath and cook for 1-2 hours, or until the pork chops reach an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C).
  6. Finish with a blast of heat: After the pork chops have finished cooking, remove them from the sous vide bag and place them on a plate. Heat a skillet over high heat and add a small amount of oil. Sear the pork chops for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until they develop a nice crust.
  7. Serve: Slice the pork chops and serve immediately. You can serve them with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a salad.

Tips and Variations

  • To add an extra layer of flavor, you can add a few sprigs of fresh rosemary or thyme to the sous vide bag with the pork chops.
  • If you prefer a slightly charred crust on your pork chops, you can increase the cooking time in the skillet to 3-4 minutes on each side.
  • You can also add a splash of wine or beer to the sous vide bag for added depth of flavor.
  • For a spicy kick, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the garlic and herb mixture.

Conclusion

Sous vide cooking is a game-changer for anyone who loves cooking pork chops. By controlling the temperature and cooking time, you can achieve a perfectly cooked pork chop every time. This recipe for garlic herb pork chops is a great starting point, and you can experiment with different seasonings and ingredients to create your own unique flavor profiles. With a little practice, you’ll be cooking like a pro in no time!
