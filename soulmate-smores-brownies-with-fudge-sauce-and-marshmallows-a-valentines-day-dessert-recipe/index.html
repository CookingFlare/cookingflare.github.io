Soulmate S'mores Brownies with Fudge Sauce and Marshmallows: A Valentine's Day Dessert Recipe


Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to indulge in a delicious and decadent dessert that will make your special someone’s heart skip a beat. And what better way to celebrate love than with the heavenly combination of chocolate, marshmallows, and graham crackers? Introducing Soulmate S’mores Brownies with Fudge Sauce and Marshmallows – a dessert that will ignite fireworks of flavor and passion in your mouth.

Ingredients

For the brownie base:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the fudge sauce:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the topping:

  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 4 graham crackers, crushed into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and granulated sugar. Mix well until the sugar is dissolved.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly using a spatula.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  7. While the brownies are baking, prepare the fudge sauce. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it starts to simmer. Remove from heat and add the dark chocolate chips, butter, and vanilla extract. Stir until the chocolate and butter are melted and the sauce is smooth.

  8. Once the brownies are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for 10 minutes. Pour the fudge sauce over the brownies, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  9. Sprinkle the mini marshmallows on top of the fudge sauce, followed by the crushed graham crackers.

  10. Return the pan to the oven and broil on low for 2-3 minutes, or until the marshmallows are golden brown and toasted. Keep a close eye on them to prevent burning.

  11. Remove the pan from the oven and let the brownies cool completely before cutting them into squares.

  12. Serve these Soulmate S’mores Brownies with Fudge Sauce and Marshmallows to your loved one, and watch their face light up with delight. This irresistible dessert is the perfect way to show your affection and make this Valentine’s Day extra special.

Conclusion

Soulmate S’mores Brownies with Fudge Sauce and Marshmallows are a delightful treat that combines the classic flavors of s’mores with the richness of brownies. The gooey marshmallows, crunchy graham crackers, and luscious fudge sauce create a harmonious blend of textures and tastes that will leave you and your partner craving for more. This Valentine’s Day, surprise your soulmate with these indulgent brownies and let the magic of love unfold in every bite.
