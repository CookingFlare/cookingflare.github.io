Easy Pasta Recipes Ready in 20 Minutes or Less


Are you craving a delicious pasta dish but don’t have hours to spend in the kitchen? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ve gathered some easy pasta recipes that are ready in 20 minutes or less. These recipes are perfect for busy weeknights when you need a quick and satisfying meal. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz linguine pasta
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the linguine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the butter and olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  5. Pour the chicken broth into the skillet and bring it to a simmer. Cook for 2-3 minutes to reduce slightly.
  6. Add the cooked linguine pasta to the skillet and toss to coat it with the garlic-infused broth.
  7. Return the shrimp to the skillet and toss everything together.
  8. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the pasta and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

2. Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juice) to the skillet and bring to a simmer. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Add the cooked penne pasta to the skillet and toss to coat it with the creamy tomato sauce.
  6. Tear the fresh basil leaves and sprinkle them over the pasta.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

3. Lemon Garlic Chicken Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken pieces with salt, pepper, and lemon zest.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the seasoned chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes.
  5. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  6. Pour the chicken broth into the skillet and bring it to a simmer. Cook for 2-3 minutes to reduce slightly.
  7. Add the cooked spaghetti pasta to the skillet and toss to coat it with the lemon-infused broth.
  8. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss everything together.
  9. Squeeze the lemon juice over the pasta and sprinkle chopped fresh parsley on top.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy!

These easy pasta recipes are perfect for those days when you need a quick and delicious meal. They’re packed with flavor and can be made in just 20 minutes or less. Whether you’re a seafood lover or prefer a creamy tomato sauce, there’s something for everyone in this collection. So, why wait? Try these recipes and satisfy your pasta cravings in no time!
