Tasty and Healthy Chicken Stir-Fry Noodle Recipes for a Quick Dinner


Are you looking for a quick and delicious dinner option that’s both tasty and healthy? Look no further than these mouthwatering chicken stir-fry noodle recipes! Packed with flavor, protein, and wholesome ingredients, these recipes are perfect for busy weeknights when you want to whip up something satisfying without spending hours in the kitchen.

Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz (225g) of your favorite noodles (such as rice noodles or udon noodles)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed and sauté the garlic until fragrant. Add the broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot, and stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet. Add the cooked noodles, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the stir-fry noodles in bowls, garnished with sesame seeds and sliced green onions.

Spicy Chicken and Egg Noodle Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 oz (225g) egg noodles
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 red chilies, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed and sauté the garlic, ginger, and red chilies until fragrant. Add the snap peas and red onion, and stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the cooked chicken to the pan. Add the cooked egg noodles, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sriracha sauce. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
  5. Serve the spicy chicken and egg noodle stir-fry in bowls, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves.

These tasty and healthy chicken stir-fry noodle recipes are sure to become a favorite in your dinner rotation. They are not only quick and easy to make but also customizable with your favorite vegetables and sauces. Enjoy a satisfying meal without the hassle with these delightful recipes!
