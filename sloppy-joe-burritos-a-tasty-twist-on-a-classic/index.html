Sloppy Joe Burritos: A Tasty Twist on a Classic


If you love the comforting flavors of a classic Sloppy Joe and the convenience of a burrito, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful fusion recipe. Sloppy Joe Burritos combine the saucy, savory goodness of traditional Sloppy Joes with the portability and ease of a burrito. This recipe is a perfect way to shake up your weeknight dinner routine and introduce a fun twist on a beloved classic. Let’s dive into the details and learn how to create this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

For the Sloppy Joe Filling:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Burritos:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Cooked white rice
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Sour cream (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Sloppy Joe Filling

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked.
  2. Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, and garlic to the skillet, and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in the ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, salt, and pepper. Simmer the mixture for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the flavors meld together and the sauce thickens.

Step 2: Assemble the Burritos

  1. Lay out a large flour tortilla on a clean work surface.
  2. Spoon a generous portion of cooked white rice onto the center of the tortilla, leaving some space around the edges.
  3. Top the rice with a generous scoop of the Sloppy Joe filling, spreading it evenly over the rice.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded cheddar cheese over the filling.
  5. Add a sprinkle of chopped fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness.

Step 3: Roll the Burritos

  1. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up tightly from the bottom, tucking in the sides as you go.
  2. Place the burrito seam-side down on a plate, and repeat the assembly process with the remaining tortillas and filling.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. If desired, warm the assembled burritos in a skillet over medium heat for a few minutes on each side to lightly crisp the tortilla and melt the cheese.
  2. Serve the Sloppy Joe Burritos with a dollop of sour cream on the side, and enjoy the delicious fusion of flavors and textures.

Conclusion

Sloppy Joe Burritos are a fantastic way to elevate the classic Sloppy Joe into a handheld, flavor-packed meal that’s perfect for busy weeknights or casual entertaining. The combination of hearty Sloppy Joe filling, fluffy rice, gooey cheese, and fresh cilantro wrapped in a warm tortilla creates a delightful balance of savory, sweet, and creamy flavors. Whether you’re a fan of Sloppy Joes or simply love a good burrito, this recipe is sure to become a new household favorite. Give it a try and savor the delicious twist on this timeless classic!
