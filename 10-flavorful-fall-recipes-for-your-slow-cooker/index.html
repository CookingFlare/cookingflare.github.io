10. Slow Cooker Roasted Brussel Sprouts with Bacon

Fall isn’t just about sweet flavors—it’s also about the savory ones. These roasted Brussels sprouts, cooked with crispy bacon, will be the perfect side dish to any autumn meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 1/2 lb bacon, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the Brussels sprouts and bacon in the slow cooker.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours or high for 2 hours, until the Brussels sprouts are tender and the bacon is crispy.
  4. Serve as a savory side to any fall meal.
