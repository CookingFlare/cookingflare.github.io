Wholesome Instant Pot Vegetable Curry Recipes for Plant-Based Delights

Are you craving a delicious and nutritious plant-based meal that is quick and easy to prepare? Look no further than these wholesome Instant Pot vegetable curry recipes! Packed with vibrant flavors and nourishing ingredients, these recipes are perfect for those seeking a hearty and satisfying meal that celebrates the goodness of vegetables. Let’s dive right in! 1. Creamy Coconut Curry Ingredients: 1 can of coconut milk 2 cups of mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, peas, cauliflower) 1 onion, diced 3 garlic cloves, minced 1 tablespoon of curry powder 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder 1 teaspoon of cumin powder Salt, to taste Fresh cilantro, for garnish Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a splash of oil. Sauté the onions and garlic until they become translucent. Add the curry powder, turmeric powder, and cumin powder. Stir well to coat the onions and garlic. Pour in the coconut milk and mix everything together. Add the mixed vegetables and season with salt. Close the Instant Pot and set it to manual mode for 5 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the creamy coconut curry over steamed rice or with naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor. 2. Spicy Chickpea Curry Ingredients: 2 cups of chickpeas, cooked or canned 1 onion, diced 3 garlic cloves, minced 1 tablespoon of curry powder 1 teaspoon of paprika 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference) 1 can of diced tomatoes 1 cup of vegetable broth Salt, to taste Fresh parsley, for garnish Instructions: Sauté the onions and garlic in the Instant Pot until they become fragrant and golden. Add the curry powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Stir well to coat the onions and garlic with the spices. Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth. Mix everything together. Add the chickpeas and season with salt. Close the Instant Pot and set it to manual mode for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes, then do a quick release. Serve the spicy chickpea curry with rice or quinoa. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of freshness. 3. Fragrant Thai Green Curry Ingredients: 2 cups of mixed vegetables (zucchini, broccoli, snap peas, mushrooms) 1 onion, diced 3 garlic cloves, minced 2 tablespoons of Thai green curry paste 1 can of coconut milk 1 cup of vegetable broth 1 tablespoon of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of brown sugar Lime wedges, for serving Fresh basil leaves, for garnish Instructions: Sauté the onions and garlic in the Instant Pot until they become fragrant. Add the Thai green curry paste and stir well to coat the onions and garlic. Pour in the coconut milk, vegetable broth, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Mix everything together. Add the mixed vegetables and give it a good stir. Close the Instant Pot and set it to manual mode for 5 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the fragrant Thai green curry with steamed jasmine rice. Squeeze some lime juice over the curry and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a burst of flavor. Delicious Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas Recipe for Southwestern Flavor

Looking for a delicious and easy dinner recipe that will transport you to the flavors of the Southwest? Look no further than this mouthwatering Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas recipe. Packed with bold flavors and cooked to perfection in your trusty Instant Pot, these enchiladas will have your taste buds dancing with joy. So, put on your apron and let’s get cooking! Ingredients 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 small onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1 cup chicken broth 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 8 flour tortillas 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, sour cream Instructions Start by setting your Instant Pot to the “Saute” function. Add the olive oil and heat it up. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot and saute until the onion is translucent and fragrant. In a small bowl, combine the ground cumin, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well. Season the chicken breasts with half of the spice mixture, making sure to coat both sides. Add the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are browned. Pour the chicken broth, red enchilada sauce, and diced green chilies into the pot, along with the remaining spice mixture. Stir well to combine. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then carefully perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Take a tortilla and spoon some of the enchilada sauce mixture onto it. Add a portion of the shredded chicken and a sprinkle of shredded cheese. Roll the tortilla tightly and place it seam-side down in a baking dish. Repeat with the remaining tortillas. Pour any remaining enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas and sprinkle with the remaining shredded cheese. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. After 20 minutes, remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove the enchiladas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas with your favorite toppings, such as diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream. Enjoy the Flavors of the Southwest There you have it, a delicious Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas recipe that is sure to satisfy your cravings for Southwestern flavors. With its tender shredded chicken, flavorful sauce, and gooey melted cheese, these enchiladas are a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in this irresistible dish. Happy cooking! ...

Delicious Instant Pot Chocolate Lava Cake Recipes

If you’re a chocolate lover like me, then this blog post is a treat for you! Today, I’m going to share some mouthwatering recipes for delicious Instant Pot Chocolate Lava Cakes. These decadent desserts are perfect for any occasion and can be whipped up in no time using your trusty Instant Pot. So, let’s dive right in and satisfy our sweet tooth! Classic Chocolate Lava Cake Ingredients: 1/2 cup unsalted butter 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate, chopped 2 large eggs 2 large egg yolks 1/4 cup granulated sugar 1/4 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Pinch of salt Instructions: Start by melting the butter and chopped chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat in 30-second intervals, stirring after each interval until completely melted and smooth. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until well combined. Slowly pour the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture, whisking continuously. Add the flour, vanilla extract, and salt to the batter, and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix. Grease your ramekins or small oven-safe bowls with butter or cooking spray. Divide the batter evenly among the ramekins, filling them about 3/4 of the way full. Place a trivet inside your Instant Pot and add 1 cup of water. Carefully place the ramekins on the trivet, making sure they are not touching the sides of the Instant Pot. Close the lid, set the vent to the sealing position, and cook on high pressure for 6 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick pressure release and carefully remove the ramekins from the Instant Pot using oven mitts. Let the cakes cool for a few minutes before inverting them onto serving plates. Serve warm with a dusting of powdered sugar, a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or a dollop of whipped cream. Enjoy! Peanut Butter Chocolate Lava Cake Ingredients: 1/2 cup unsalted butter 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate, chopped 2 large eggs 2 large egg yolks 1/4 cup granulated sugar 1/4 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Pinch of salt 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter Instructions: Follow the same instructions as the Classic Chocolate Lava Cake recipe, but add the creamy peanut butter to the batter along with the flour, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix well to ensure the peanut butter is evenly distributed throughout the batter. Continue with the remaining steps of the recipe, cooking the cakes in the Instant Pot as directed. Serve warm and enjoy the irresistible combination of chocolate and peanut butter! Raspberry Chocolate Lava Cake Ingredients: 1/2 cup unsalted butter 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate, chopped 2 large eggs 2 large egg yolks 1/4 cup granulated sugar 1/4 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Pinch of salt 1/4 cup raspberry jam Instructions: Prepare the batter following the instructions for the Classic Chocolate Lava Cake recipe. After filling the ramekins with the batter, drop a teaspoon of raspberry jam into the center of each ramekin. Use a toothpick or a skewer to swirl the jam into the batter, creating a marbled effect. Cook the cakes in the Instant Pot as directed in the previous recipes. The burst of tangy raspberry flavor adds a delightful twist to the classic chocolate lava cake. Conclusion There you have it, three delicious variations of Instant Pot Chocolate Lava Cakes to satisfy your sweet cravings. Whether you prefer the classic version, the peanut butter twist, or the fruity raspberry variation, these recipes are guaranteed to impress your guests or simply indulge yourself.
