Creamy and Buttery Mashed Sweet Potatoes for Thanksgiving


As the holiday season approaches, it’s time to start planning the perfect Thanksgiving feast. While the turkey often takes center stage, let’s not forget about the delicious side dishes that complement the main course. One dish that never fails to impress is creamy and buttery mashed sweet potatoes. This classic Thanksgiving side dish is a crowd-pleaser, offering a delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors that perfectly complement the richness of the holiday meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sweet Potatoes: Start by peeling the sweet potatoes and cutting them into evenly sized cubes. This will ensure that they cook evenly and result in a smooth texture when mashed.

  2. Boil the Sweet Potatoes: Place the cubed sweet potatoes in a large pot and cover them with water. Bring the water to a boil and cook the sweet potatoes until they are fork-tender, which usually takes about 15-20 minutes.

  3. Drain and Mash: Once the sweet potatoes are cooked, drain them thoroughly and transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Using a potato masher, mash the sweet potatoes until they reach a smooth consistency.

  4. Add the Goodness: Now, it’s time to add the rich and creamy elements to the mashed sweet potatoes. Add the softened butter, heavy cream, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, salt, and freshly ground black pepper to the bowl with the mashed sweet potatoes.

  5. Mix and Blend: Using a hand mixer or a sturdy spoon, blend the ingredients together until the sweet potatoes are creamy and smooth. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a gummy texture.

  6. Taste and Adjust: Before serving, taste the mashed sweet potatoes and adjust the seasoning to your preference. You can add more butter, cream, or spices to achieve the perfect balance of flavors.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the creamy and buttery mashed sweet potatoes to a serving dish and garnish with a sprinkle of cinnamon or a pat of butter. These delectable mashed sweet potatoes are ready to take their place alongside the Thanksgiving turkey, delighting your guests with their rich, velvety texture and irresistible flavor.

Tips for Perfection:

  • For an extra indulgent twist, consider adding a splash of pure vanilla extract to the mashed sweet potatoes for an added layer of flavor.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute the heavy cream with low-fat milk or coconut milk for a dairy-free option.
  • To save time on Thanksgiving day, the sweet potatoes can be boiled and mashed a day in advance. Simply reheat them in the oven before serving, adding a pat of butter on top for a finishing touch.

Creamy and buttery mashed sweet potatoes are a timeless Thanksgiving classic that never fails to impress. With their luxurious texture and warm, comforting flavors, they are sure to become a cherished addition to your holiday table. Whether you’re hosting a large gathering or enjoying an intimate meal with loved ones, these mashed sweet potatoes are a delightful way to elevate your Thanksgiving feast. So, this holiday season, treat yourself and your guests to the indulgent delight of creamy and buttery mashed sweet potatoes – a true Thanksgiving masterpiece.
