Tasty Finger Foods for Your Summer Picnic Basket


As the sun shines brighter and the days grow longer, summer beckons us to embrace the great outdoors. What better way to enjoy the warm weather than with a delightful picnic? Whether you’re lounging in a park, by the beach, or in your backyard, having a basket filled with tasty finger foods can elevate your picnic experience. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of delicious and easy-to-make finger foods that are perfect for your summer picnic basket. From savory bites to sweet treats, these recipes will keep your guests satisfied and happy.

1. Mini Caprese Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Mozzarella balls
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper
  • Skewers or toothpicks

Instructions:

  1. Assemble the Skewers: On each skewer, thread a cherry tomato, a basil leaf, and a mozzarella ball. Repeat until the skewer is filled, leaving some space at the ends for easy handling.
  2. Season: Drizzle the assembled skewers with balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Serve: Arrange the skewers on a platter and enjoy the fresh flavors of summer.

These mini Caprese skewers are not only visually appealing but also packed with flavor. They are a refreshing option that pairs perfectly with a chilled glass of lemonade.

2. Savory Spinach and Feta Puff Pastry Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the Filling: In a bowl, mix the chopped spinach, feta cheese, cream cheese, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Cut the Pastry: Roll out the puff pastry on a floured surface and cut it into squares (about 3x3 inches).
  4. Fill and Fold: Place a spoonful of the spinach mixture in the center of each square. Fold the corners over to create a pocket and seal the edges with a fork.
  5. Brush with Egg Wash: Place the filled pastries on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the tops with the beaten egg.
  6. Bake: Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm or at room temperature.

These savory bites are flaky, cheesy, and packed with nutrients, making them a perfect addition to your picnic spread.

3. Zesty Hummus and Veggie Cups

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • Assorted fresh vegetables (carrots, cucumbers, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes)
  • Small cups or jars for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Veggies: Cut the vegetables into sticks or bite-sized pieces.
  2. Assemble the Cups: In each cup or jar, add a few tablespoons of hummus at the bottom.
  3. Add Veggies: Stand the vegetable sticks upright in the hummus, creating a colorful display.
  4. Serve: These cups are not only healthy but also easy to grab and enjoy.

The zesty hummus and fresh veggies provide a crunchy, satisfying snack that’s perfect for hot summer days.

4. Chicken Salad Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt or mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup celery, diced
  • 1/4 cup grapes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. Mix the Chicken Salad: In a bowl, combine the shredded chicken, Greek yogurt or mayonnaise, diced celery, halved grapes, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Prepare the Lettuce: Carefully separate the butter lettuce leaves, keeping them whole.
  3. Assemble the Wraps: Spoon a generous amount of chicken salad onto each lettuce leaf.
  4. Serve: Roll up the lettuce wraps and enjoy a light, refreshing meal.

These chicken salad lettuce wraps are a low-carb alternative that’s both filling and flavorful, making them a great choice for a summer picnic.
