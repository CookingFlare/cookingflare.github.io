Sweet & Savory Sandwiches: Insanely Fun & Filling Recipes for Busy Homeschool Days


Morning routines can turn chaotic when you’re juggling household chores, homeschooling, and keeping everyone nourished for a day of learning. Adding one more item to the breakfast list might sound unappealing, but a delicious and nutritious sandwich can do the trick! Not only will it give your family a satisfying meal, but it’ll also provide the energy needed to conquer the day. So, let’s dive into a selection of sweet and savory sandwich concoctions that you can whip up in no time. These crowd-pleasers are bound to keep you and your brood happy during your busy homeschool days.

1. Grilled Nutella Banana Sandwich

Start your day off with a sweet and delectable twist on the classic Nutella sandwich. This Nutella Banana Grilled Sandwich, a.k.a. “Francesinha” from Portugal, will give you that irresistible taste of childhood and that deliciously gooey texture to keep you energized.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 2 tablespoons Nutella
  • 1/2 banana (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • A pinch of cinnamon -1 tablespoon of unsalted butter

Directions:

  1. Slather one side of each bread slice with Nutella, making sure to cover evenly and generously.
  2. On the Nutella side of one bread slice, place the sliced bananas, creating a single layer.
  3. Drizzle a small amount of milk over the bananas, and sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon.
  4. Top the bananas with the other slice of bread (Nutella side down).
  5. Melting a tablespoon of unsalted butter in a large skillet, cook the sandwich over medium heat until golden brown on both sides, around 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Slice in half and serve immediately.

2. Caprese Grilled Cheese

Searching for a fresh and savory sandwich? Look no further than the classic Caprese Grilled Cheese. Inspired by the Italian Caprese Salad, this cheese-filled sandwich gives life to your usual grilled cheese, while keeping it home-cook friendly.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of rustic bread
  • 2 slices of mozzarella cheese
  • A few cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of balsamic glaze or reduction
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter

Directions:

  1. Fire up a skillet and melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Assemble the sandwich by placing one slice of mozzarella on one side of a bread slice. Follow with a layer of cherry tomatoes.
  3. Top with the second slice of mozzarella, a drizzle of balsamic glaze, a handful of fresh basil leaves, and finally, the other slice of bread (non-butter side up).
  4. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until the bread is crispy, and the cheese begins to melt (approximately 2 to 3 minutes).
  5. Flip the sandwich and cook for another 2 to 3 minutes, or until the second side is golden brown.
  6. Serve immediately and savor the delightful combination of gooey cheese, tangy tomatoes, and peppery basil in every bite.

3. Monte Carlo Sandwich

Don’t let the name fool you, there’s no gambling involved hereâ€”just a mouthwatering combination of flavors! The Monte Carlo sandwich features a smokey and savory Prosciutto, overflowing with melted cheese and fresh tomato goodness that’ll have you advertising the humble sandwich from your kitchen to your living room.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of rustic bread
  • 2 slices of provolone cheese
  • 1 ounce of Prosciutto
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Directions:

  1. Grab your skillet and heat it up over medium heat. Add the sliced tomatoes and a tablespoon of olive oil. Cook until the tomatoes are softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. While your tomatoes cook, lay one slice of bread on your work surface. Top with Prosciutto slices, followed by provolone cheese and fresh basil leaves.
  3. Spoon cooked tomatoes on top of the basil, then place the other slice of bread (non-butter side up) over the tomatoes.
  4. Cook for about 2 to 3 minutes on each side, or until the bread is crisp and the cheese is melted.
  5. Remove from heat and slice in half. Serve immediately to avoid an eagerly awaiting meltdown.

4. Peanut Butter Banana and Honey Sandwich

Why choose between sweet or savory when you can have both? Take a page from the classic Peanut Butter Banana Sandwich and upgrade it with drizzles of honey for a magical marriage of carbohydrates galore. This quick and simple sandwich is the perfect treat for impromptu homeschooling snacks.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 2 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1/2 banana (sliced)
  • A drizzle of honey
  • A pinch of salt

Directions:

  1. Lay out two slices of whole grain bread and spread peanut butter generously on one side of each slice.
  2. On one peanut butter-covered slice, arrange sliced bananas to create a single layer. Add a drizzle of honey and a pinch of salt over the bananas.
  3. Top the banana layer with the other bread slice, covering the bananas (peanut butter side down).
  4. Slice in half and serve immediately to fully enjoy the delightful fusion of flavors.

With these insanely fun and filling sandwich recipes by your side, navigating your busy homeschool days will be a breeze. Effortlessly combine sweet and savory flavors to make wholesome meals that keep your family satisfied and nourished throughout the day. So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients and start preparing a scrumptious and delectable array of sandwiches that are sure to captivate your taste buds. Happy sandwiching!
