Quick and Easy Stir Fry Recipes with Shrimp for Seafood Lovers


If you’re a seafood lover looking for a quick and delicious meal, look no further than these mouthwatering stir fry recipes with shrimp. Packed with flavor, nutrients, and the perfect balance of textures, these dishes are sure to become staples in your recipe collection. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out in the kitchen, these recipes are simple, satisfying, and perfect for any occasion.

Shrimp and Broccoli Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and broth. Set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and stir-fry until pink and cooked through. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Add the remaining tablespoon of oil to the skillet. Stir-fry the garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  4. Add the broccoli, bell pepper, and onion to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet. Pour the sauce over the shrimp and vegetables. Cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens and coats the shrimp and vegetables.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.

Spicy Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons chili flakes
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, and rice vinegar. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and stir-fry until pink and cooked through. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Add the sesame oil to the skillet. Stir-fry the garlic and chili flakes until fragrant.
  4. Add the bell peppers, sugar snap peas, and green onions to the skillet. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet. Pour the soy sauce mixture over the shrimp and vegetables. Cook, stirring constantly, for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve the spicy garlic shrimp stir-fry over cooked rice.

Conclusion

These quick and easy stir fry recipes with shrimp are perfect for seafood lovers looking for a delicious and satisfying meal. With simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can enjoy the flavors of the sea in the comfort of your own home. Experiment with different vegetables, sauces, and seasonings to customize these recipes to your taste. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a dinner party, these stir fry recipes are sure to impress. So, grab your skillet or wok and get ready to create a seafood feast that’s as delightful to cook as it is to eat!
