Turkey and Veggie Breakfast Sausage Patties


Are you tired of the same old breakfast options? If you’re looking to add some variety to your morning routine, these homemade turkey and veggie breakfast sausage patties are the perfect solution. Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, these patties are a healthier alternative to traditional sausage, and they’re incredibly easy to make. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create this delicious and nutritious breakfast option.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 cup finely chopped mixed vegetables (bell peppers, onions, and spinach work well)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground turkey, finely chopped mixed vegetables, garlic powder, onion powder, dried sage, dried thyme, paprika, black pepper, and salt. Using your hands, gently mix the ingredients until well combined.

  2. Once the ingredients are evenly mixed, divide the mixture into equal portions and shape them into patties. You can make them as thick or thin as you like, depending on your preference.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the patties in the skillet, ensuring they are not overcrowded. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  4. Once the patties are cooked, transfer them to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

  5. Serve the turkey and veggie breakfast sausage patties alongside your favorite breakfast sides, such as eggs, toast, or fresh fruit. Enjoy a wholesome and satisfying breakfast that will keep you fueled throughout the morning.

Tips and Variations:

  • Feel free to customize the vegetables used in the recipe based on your preferences. Finely chopped zucchini, carrots, or mushrooms can also be great additions to the mixture.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes or cayenne pepper to the patty mixture.
  • These sausage patties can be made in advance and stored in the refrigerator for a few days or frozen for longer-term storage. Simply reheat them in the microwave or on the stovetop for a quick and convenient breakfast option.

Final Thoughts:

Creating your own turkey and veggie breakfast sausage patties allows you to take control of the ingredients and flavors, ensuring a healthier and more satisfying breakfast experience. By incorporating nutrient-rich vegetables and lean turkey, you can enjoy a delicious morning meal without compromising on taste or nutrition. Whether you’re looking for a protein-packed breakfast option or simply want to switch up your morning routine, these homemade sausage patties are sure to become a new favorite at your breakfast table. Give this recipe a try and elevate your breakfast game with these wholesome and flavorful patties.
