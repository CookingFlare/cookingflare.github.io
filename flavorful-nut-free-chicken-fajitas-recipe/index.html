Flavorful Nut-Free Chicken Fajitas Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and nut-free recipe that will spice up your dinner routine? Look no further! These Flavorful Nut-Free Chicken Fajitas are the perfect solution. Packed with vibrant flavors, tender chicken, and a medley of colorful vegetables, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a dinner party, these fajitas are a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, salsa, shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken and cook until browned, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.

  3. While the vegetables are cooking, prepare the seasoning mixture. In a small bowl, combine the chili powder, paprika, cumin, oregano, salt, and black pepper.

  4. Once the vegetables are cooked, return the chicken to the skillet and sprinkle the seasoning mixture over the chicken and vegetables. Stir to coat evenly.

  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and continue cooking for an additional 5 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors have melded together.

  6. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave according to the package instructions.

  7. Serve the chicken and vegetable mixture in the warm tortillas, and top with your choice of optional toppings such as sour cream, guacamole, salsa, or shredded cheese.

  8. Enjoy your Flavorful Nut-Free Chicken Fajitas!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a few dashes of hot sauce to the seasoning mixture.
  • If you prefer a different protein, such as shrimp or beef, you can easily substitute it for the chicken.
  • Experiment with different toppings to customize your fajitas to your liking. Try adding fresh cilantro, diced tomatoes, or a squeeze of lime juice for an extra burst of flavor.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use pre-cut vegetables from the grocery store to save on prep work.

Conclusion

These Flavorful Nut-Free Chicken Fajitas are a versatile and satisfying meal that will leave your taste buds wanting more. With their vibrant colors, tender chicken, and robust seasoning, they are a feast for both the eyes and the palate. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. So gather your ingredients, get cooking, and savor the delicious flavors of these Nut-Free Chicken Fajitas.
