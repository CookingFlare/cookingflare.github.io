Delicious Ham and Cheese Sliders: Crowd-Pleasing Recipe

Are you in search of a simple yet crowd-pleasing recipe that will leave your guests asking for seconds? Look no further than these delicious ham and cheese sliders! Packed with flavors and easy to make, these sliders are the perfect choice for any gathering or even a cozy night in. Ingredients: 12 slider buns 12 slices of ham 12 slices of Swiss cheese 1/4 cup of melted butter 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon of poppy seeds 1 teaspoon of garlic powder 1 teaspoon of onion powder Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Slice the slider buns in half horizontally, keeping them connected. Place the bottom half of the buns in a baking dish. Layer the ham slices on top of the buns, followed by the Swiss cheese slices. Place the top half of the buns over the cheese, creating sliders. In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, poppy seeds, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined. Carefully pour the butter mixture over the sliders, ensuring each one is coated evenly. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the sliders are heated through. Remove the foil and let the sliders bake for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the tops to turn golden and slightly crispy. Once done, remove from the oven and let the sliders cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve and Enjoy: These delicious ham and cheese sliders are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a party, having a picnic, or simply craving a satisfying snack, these sliders will not disappoint.
