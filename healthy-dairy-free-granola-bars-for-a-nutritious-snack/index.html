Healthy Dairy-Free Granola Bars for a Nutritious Snack


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy snack that you can enjoy on the go? Look no further! These homemade dairy-free granola bars are the perfect solution. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with flavor, these granola bars are sure to become a staple in your snack rotation. Whether you’re heading to work, hitting the gym, or simply need a quick pick-me-up, these bars are the ideal choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup nuts (almonds, walnuts, or your choice)
  • 1/2 cup seeds (pumpkin, sunflower, or a mix)
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup dried fruits (raisins, cranberries, apricots, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. Spread the oats, nuts, and seeds on the prepared baking pan and toast them in the oven for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until fragrant and lightly golden.

  3. Transfer the toasted mixture to a large bowl and add the shredded coconut and dried fruits. Mix well and set aside.

  4. In a small saucepan, combine the honey (or maple syrup), coconut oil, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture is well combined and slightly thickened.

  5. Pour the honey mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until everything is well coated.

  6. Transfer the mixture to the lined baking pan and press it down firmly and evenly.

  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.

  8. Allow the granola bars to cool completely in the pan before cutting them into bars or squares.

  9. Store the bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, or in the refrigerator for longer shelf life.

Customization Options

Feel free to customize these granola bars to suit your preferences. You can swap out the nuts, seeds, or dried fruits for your favorites, or even add a sprinkle of chocolate chips for an indulgent twist.

Benefits of Dairy-Free Granola Bars

These dairy-free granola bars offer a range of nutritional benefits. They are rich in fiber from the oats, nuts, and seeds, which can help support digestive health and keep you feeling full and satisfied. The dried fruits provide natural sweetness and a dose of vitamins and minerals, while the coconut oil adds healthy fats to support overall well-being. Plus, the absence of dairy makes these bars suitable for individuals with lactose intolerance or those following a dairy-free diet.

Conclusion

In conclusion, these dairy-free granola bars are a simple, wholesome, and delicious snack option. By making them at home, you have full control over the ingredients, ensuring that you’re fueling your body with nourishing, real foods. Whip up a batch of these granola bars and have a convenient snack at your fingertips whenever hunger strikes.

Remember, the key to a successful snack is finding the balance between taste and nutrition, and these granola bars certainly deliver on both fronts. So, go ahead, get creative, and enjoy the goodness of these homemade dairy-free granola bars!
