Tasty One Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy-to-make dinner that’s packed with flavor, then this One Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry recipe is just what you need. This dish is not only quick to prepare, but it’s also a great way to get your daily dose of veggies and protein in one satisfying meal. Best of all, it’s all made in one pot, which means less cleanup and more time to enjoy your culinary creation.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of freshly grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Cooked white rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and cornstarch. Set aside.

  2. Cook the Beef: Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced flank steak and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. Cook the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the broccoli florets and sliced red bell pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and vegetables, stirring to coat everything evenly. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  5. Serve: Once the beef and broccoli stir fry is ready, remove it from the heat and garnish with sesame seeds. Serve the stir fry over cooked white rice and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the sauce.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables such as snap peas, mushrooms, or water chestnuts for added texture and flavor.
  • Substitute the flank steak with chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a different protein option.

Final Thoughts

This One Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry is a fantastic dish for busy weeknights when you want something tasty and satisfying without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of tender beef, crisp vegetables, and savory sauce makes this stir fry a crowd-pleaser for the whole family. Plus, with only one pot to clean up, you’ll have more time to relax and savor the flavors of this delicious meal. So, next time you’re craving Chinese takeout, consider making this homemade version instead – you won’t be disappointed. Enjoy!

In conclusion, this One Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry recipe is a delightful way to bring the flavors of a Chinese restaurant into your own kitchen. With its simple preparation and delicious results, it’s sure to become a regular in your dinner rotation. So, grab your skillet and get ready to whip up this tasty dish in no time!
