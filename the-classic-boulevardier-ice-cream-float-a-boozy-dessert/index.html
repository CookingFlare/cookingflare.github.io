The Classic Boulevardier Ice Cream Float: A Boozy Dessert


Indulge your taste buds with a delightful twist on a classic treat - the Boulevardier Ice Cream Float. This boozy dessert combines the rich flavors of a traditional cocktail with the creamy goodness of ice cream, creating a unique and satisfying dessert experience. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking to treat yourself, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 ounce of bourbon
  • 1 ounce of sweet vermouth
  • 1/2 ounce of Campari
  • A splash of soda water
  • Orange peel, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a tall glass, carefully place a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The creaminess of the ice cream will complement the bold flavors of the cocktail.

  2. In a separate mixing glass, combine the bourbon, sweet vermouth, and Campari. Stir gently to mix the ingredients together and allow the flavors to meld.

  3. Slowly pour the cocktail mixture over the ice cream, allowing it to cascade down the sides of the glass. The contrast between the cold ice cream and the warm cocktail adds a unique visual appeal to the dessert.

  4. Finish off the Boulevardier Ice Cream Float with a splash of soda water. This effervescent addition adds a refreshing touch to the dessert, balancing the richness of the ice cream and the intensity of the cocktail.

  5. Garnish the float with a twist of orange peel. The citrus notes from the peel complement the flavors of the cocktail, enhancing the overall experience.

  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your Boulevardier Ice Cream Float. Savor the rich, creamy texture of the ice cream as it melds with the complex flavors of the cocktail.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different ice cream flavors to customize your Boulevardier Ice Cream Float. Chocolate or coffee-flavored ice cream can add an extra layer of richness, while fruity flavors like strawberry or raspberry can provide a refreshing twist.

  • For a non-alcoholic version, simply omit the bourbon, sweet vermouth, and Campari. Replace them with your favorite non-alcoholic substitutes, such as flavored syrups or herbal infusions.

  • If you prefer a stronger cocktail flavor, increase the amount of bourbon, sweet vermouth, and Campari. Adjust the ratios to suit your personal taste preferences.

  • Consider adding a sprinkle of cinnamon or a drizzle of caramel sauce to elevate the flavors of the float even further.

  • Serve the Boulevardier Ice Cream Float alongside a plate of cookies or biscotti for a delightful dessert pairing.

Conclusion

The Classic Boulevardier Ice Cream Float is a decadent dessert that seamlessly blends the worlds of cocktails and ice cream. Its rich flavors and creamy texture make it a perfect treat for any occasion. Whether you’re a fan of classic cocktails or simply looking to try something new, this boozy dessert is sure to satisfy your cravings. So, gather your ingredients, grab a glass, and indulge in the delightful fusion of flavors that awaits you. Cheers!
