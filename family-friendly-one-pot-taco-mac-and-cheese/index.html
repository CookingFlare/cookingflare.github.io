Family-Friendly One Pot Taco Mac and Cheese


If you’re a fan of easy, delicious, and family-friendly meals, then this One Pot Taco Mac and Cheese is a recipe you’ll want to add to your collection. It’s a perfect combination of two beloved comfort foods – tacos and mac and cheese – all cooked together in one pot for minimal cleanup. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak in some extra veggies for the little ones without compromising on flavor. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to make this delightful dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or deep skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain any excess fat.

  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot with the ground beef. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Stir in the diced red bell pepper and corn kernels, cooking for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  4. Add the can of diced tomatoes (undrained) to the pot, along with the taco seasoning. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  6. Once the liquid is boiling, add the elbow macaroni to the pot. Stir well to ensure the macaroni is evenly distributed in the mixture.

  7. Reduce the heat to a simmer, cover the pot, and let it cook for about 12-15 minutes, or until the macaroni is tender and has absorbed most of the liquid. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  8. Once the macaroni is cooked, remove the pot from the heat and stir in the shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheese until melted and creamy.

  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a diced jalapeño or a pinch of red pepper flakes to the pot along with the other vegetables.
  • You can customize this recipe with your favorite taco toppings such as diced avocado, sour cream, or sliced black olives.
  • To make it vegetarian, simply substitute the ground beef with plant-based crumbles or extra veggies like zucchini and mushrooms.

Final Thoughts

This One Pot Taco Mac and Cheese is a surefire way to please both kids and adults alike. It’s a comforting, flavorful, and hassle-free meal that brings the best of two worlds together. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight dinner or hosting a casual gathering, this dish is bound to be a hit. Give it a try and watch it become a regular on your family’s menu!

So there you have it – a simple and satisfying recipe that ticks all the boxes for a family-friendly meal. We hope you enjoy making and savoring this One Pot Taco Mac and Cheese as much as we do! Cheers to good food and happy gatherings.
