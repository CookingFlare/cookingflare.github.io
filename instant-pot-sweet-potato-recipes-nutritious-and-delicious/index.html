Instant Pot Sweet Potato Recipes: Nutritious and Delicious


Sweet potatoes are a versatile and nutritious ingredient that can be used in a variety of dishes. They are packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber, making them an excellent choice for a healthy diet. The Instant Pot, a modern kitchen marvel, simplifies cooking sweet potatoes, ensuring they are perfectly cooked every time. In this blog post, we will explore several delightful and nutritious sweet potato recipes that you can easily prepare using your Instant Pot.

Why Choose Sweet Potatoes?

Sweet potatoes are not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. They are an excellent source of:

  • Vitamin A: Essential for maintaining good vision, immune function, and skin health.
  • Vitamin C: Important for immune support and overall health.
  • Fiber: Helps with digestion and maintaining a healthy gut.
  • Potassium: Regulates fluid balance and muscle contractions.
  • Manganese: Plays a role in metabolism and bone formation.

Their natural sweetness makes them a perfect ingredient for both savory and sweet dishes. Let’s dive into some mouthwatering sweet potato recipes you can create with your Instant Pot.

1. Instant Pot Sweet Potato Mash

Ingredients

  • 4 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: cinnamon and nutmeg for extra flavor

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sweet Potatoes: Peel and cube the sweet potatoes into even pieces to ensure uniform cooking.
  2. Add Water: Pour 1 cup of water into the Instant Pot. Place the sweet potatoes in a steamer basket or directly into the pot.
  3. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, perform a quick release of the pressure.
  4. Mash the Sweet Potatoes: Drain any excess water and return the sweet potatoes to the Instant Pot. Add the butter, milk, salt, and pepper. Use a masher or hand mixer to blend until smooth. For a twist, add a pinch of cinnamon and nutmeg.
  5. Serve: Scoop the sweet potato mash into a serving bowl and enjoy as a side dish or a base for other recipes.

2. Instant Pot Sweet Potato Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (optional for creaminess)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add a splash of oil, then sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add Sweet Potatoes and Spices: Add the diced sweet potatoes, ground cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. Pour in Broth: Add the vegetable broth, ensuring the sweet potatoes are fully submerged.
  4. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes. Allow for a natural release of pressure for about 10 minutes before performing a quick release.
  5. Blend the Soup: Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. For a creamier texture, stir in the coconut milk.
  6. Serve: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve warm.

3. Instant Pot Sweet Potato and Black Bean Chili

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Avocado, cilantro, and lime wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add a splash of oil, then sauté the chopped onion, garlic, and bell pepper until softened.
  2. Add the Sweet Potatoes and Spices: Add the cubed sweet potatoes, chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir to coat the vegetables in the spices.
  3. Combine Remaining Ingredients: Add the black beans, diced tomatoes (with their juices), and vegetable broth. Stir to combine.
  4. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 12 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release of pressure.
  5. Serve: Spoon the chili into bowls and top with avocado slices, fresh cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice. This hearty chili is perfect for a cozy meal.

4. Instant Pot Sweet Potato Breakfast Hash

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 eggs
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add a splash of oil, then sauté the chopped onion, garlic, and bell pepper until softened.
  2. Add Sweet Potatoes and Spices: Add the diced sweet potatoes, smoked paprika, ground cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 5 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release of pressure.
  4. Cook the Eggs: Make small wells in the sweet potato mixture and crack an egg into each well. Close the lid and let the residual heat cook the eggs to your desired doneness.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve the sweet potato hash warm. It’s a nutritious and filling breakfast option.

5. Instant Pot Sweet Potato Curry

Ingredients

  • 3 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Cooked rice for serving
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add a splash of oil, then sauté the chopped onion, garlic, and grated ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add Sweet Potatoes and Spices: Add the cubed sweet potatoes, red curry paste, turmeric powder, ground cumin, and salt. Stir to coat the sweet potatoes in the spices.
  3. Pour in Liquids: Add the coconut milk and vegetable broth, stirring to combine.
  4. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release of pressure.
  5. Finish with Spinach: Stir in the fresh spinach leaves until wilted.
  6. Serve: Ladle the sweet potato curry over cooked rice and garnish with fresh cilantro. This aromatic curry is a perfect balance of flavors and textures.

6. Instant Pot Sweet Potato Dessert: Sweet Potato Pudding

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt
  • Chopped nuts for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Sweet Potatoes: Add the cubed sweet potatoes and 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot. Secure the lid and set to high pressure for 8 minutes. Perform a quick release of pressure.
  2. Blend the Pudding: Drain any excess water from the sweet potatoes. Add the cooked sweet potatoes, coconut milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt to a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Chill: Transfer the sweet potato pudding to a bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld and the pudding to thicken.
  4. Serve: Spoon the pudding into serving dishes and top with chopped nuts for added texture. Enjoy this sweet and nutritious dessert.

Conclusion

The Instant Pot is a fantastic tool for creating a variety of sweet potato recipes that are both nutritious and delicious. From savory soups and chilis to sweet desserts and breakfast options, sweet potatoes can be transformed into a multitude of dishes that cater to different tastes and dietary preferences. Incorporating these recipes into your meal plan not only adds variety to your diet but also ensures that you are enjoying the numerous health benefits of sweet potatoes. So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in these delightful sweet potato creations!
