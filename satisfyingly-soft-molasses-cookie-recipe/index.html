Satisfyingly Soft Molasses Cookie Recipe


If you’re a fan of soft and chewy cookies, then this Satisfyingly Soft Molasses Cookie recipe is a must-try! These cookies are bursting with rich molasses flavor and have a delightful soft texture that will make your taste buds dance with joy. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make, requiring just a few simple ingredients that you probably already have in your pantry. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Additional granulated sugar for rolling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done using an electric mixer or by hand with a wooden spoon.

  3. Add in the molasses, eggs, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a soft dough forms. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Once the dough is ready, it’s time to shape the cookies. Take about 2 tablespoons of dough and roll it into a ball between your hands. Roll each ball in granulated sugar, coating it completely.

  6. Place the sugar-coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space between them for spreading. You may need to bake the cookies in batches depending on the size of your baking sheet.

  7. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until they are set around the edges but still slightly soft in the center. Remember, they will continue to firm up as they cool.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, these Satisfyingly Soft Molasses Cookies are ready to be enjoyed! They are perfect for a cozy afternoon treat or as a delightful addition to any holiday cookie platter. Serve them with a glass of milk or a hot cup of tea for the ultimate indulgence.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of sweetness, you can drizzle a simple glaze over the cooled cookies. Mix powdered sugar with a little milk or water until you achieve a smooth and pourable consistency. Drizzle the glaze over the cookies using a spoon or a piping bag.

  • Feel free to customize these cookies to your liking by adding your favorite mix-ins. Chopped nuts, raisins, or even chocolate chips would all be delicious additions to the dough.

  • If you prefer a spicier flavor, increase the amount of ground ginger, cloves, or nutmeg in the recipe.

  • These cookies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. They also freeze well, so you can make a batch ahead of time and thaw them whenever you’re craving a sweet treat.

Enjoy these Satisfyingly Soft Molasses Cookies and let their irresistible softness and molasses flavor transport you to cookie heaven. Whether you’re baking them for yourself or sharing them with loved ones, these cookies are sure to bring smiles and satisfy even the most discerning cookie lover. Happy baking!
