Quick and Easy: Gluten-Free Teriyaki Chicken Skewers


When it comes to hosting a gathering or simply preparing a delicious meal for your family, having a quick and easy recipe up your sleeve can make all the difference. These gluten-free teriyaki chicken skewers are not only incredibly flavorful but also simple to prepare, making them a perfect addition to your recipe collection. Whether you’re catering to gluten-free dietary needs or simply looking for a mouthwatering dish, these skewers are sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create these delectable gluten-free teriyaki chicken skewers in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the marinade:

  • 1/2 cup gluten-free soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the skewers:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks

Instructions

Preparing the Marinade

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the gluten-free soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, sesame oil, chopped green onions, and black pepper. Whisk the ingredients together until the honey is fully dissolved.

Marinating the Chicken

  1. Place the cubed chicken in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour half of the prepared marinade over the chicken, ensuring that each piece is well coated. Reserve the remaining marinade for basting.

  2. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse into the chicken.

Assembling the Skewers

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.

  2. Thread the marinated chicken, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and red onion onto skewers in an alternating pattern, leaving a little space between each piece.

Grilling the Skewers

  1. Place the assembled skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are slightly charred.

  2. While grilling, baste the skewers with the reserved marinade every 2-3 minutes to enhance the flavor and keep the chicken moist.

  3. Once cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and allow them to rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestion

  1. Garnish the gluten-free teriyaki chicken skewers with additional chopped green onions and sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor. Serve them with a side of steamed rice or a crisp, fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

In just a few simple steps, you can create a delightful dish that is not only gluten-free but also bursting with the irresistible flavors of teriyaki. These gluten-free teriyaki chicken skewers are a fantastic option for a quick and easy meal that doesn’t compromise on taste. Whether you’re firing up the grill for a summer barbecue or preparing a cozy indoor dinner, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Embrace the simplicity and deliciousness of these skewers, and treat yourself and your loved ones to a memorable dining experience. Enjoy!

This blog post aims to provide a comprehensive guide to preparing gluten-free teriyaki chicken skewers, from the marinade to the grilling process. The simple and clear instructions, combined with the enticing flavors of the dish, make it a must-try for anyone seeking a delightful gluten-free recipe.
