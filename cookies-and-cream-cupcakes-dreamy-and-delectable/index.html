Cookies and Cream Cupcakes: Dreamy and Delectable


Do you ever find yourself craving a delectable treat that combines the down-home comfort of a chocolate cookie with the luscious creaminess of vanilla ice cream? Well, look no further because I’ve got a recipe that will steal your heart away: Cookies and Cream Cupcakes! These dreamy little treats combine your favorite chocolate sandwich cookies with the smoothness of vanilla cupcake batter for a quite mouth-watering explosion of flavors. Let’s get started on whipping up a batch of these delightful confections right away.

Ingredients

For the Vanilla Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup of cookie crumbs

For the Creamy Cookie Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup of crushed cookies
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Vanilla Cupcakes

  1. Heat your oven to 350°F and line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well-combined.

  3. In another bowl, cream the softened butter using a hand mixer or stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment until it is light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract, making sure the ingredients are well-incorporated after each addition.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Mix until just combined, but do not overmix your batter!

  5. At this point, gently fold in the cookie crumbs using a rubber spatula or wooden spoon. Be gentle, as you don’t want to deflate the cupcakes’ air bubbles.

  6. Distribute the cupcake batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each approximately two-thirds of the way full.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Let the cupcakes cool completely on a wire rack before you frost them.

Step 2: Making the Creamy Cookie Frosting

  1. In a large bowl, combine the softened butter, powdered sugar, crushed cookies, heavy cream, and vanilla extract. Mix everything together until you achieve a smooth, creamy, and pipeable frosting consistency.

Step 3: Assembling the Cupcakes

  1. Once your cupcakes have cooled, you can get ready to generously frost them with your creamy cookie frosting. One easy way to do this is by using a piping bag fitted with a star tip, but feel free to use a butter knife or an off-set spatula if you prefer.

  2. Pipe or spread the frosting generously on top of each cupcake, intentionally swirling it a little for added effect.

  3. Return the frosted cupcakes to the refrigerator for approximately 15-20 minutes to allow the frosting to sufficiently set up.

  4. Remove the cupcakes from the refrigerator, grab some napkins or cute cupcake liners, and enjoy a scrumptious Cookies and Cream Cupcake!

Conclusion

Who wouldn’t want to indulge in the delightful world of these Cookies and Cream Cupcakes? These little gems are perfect for weekend breakfast, afternoon tea, or simply when your sweet tooth calls for some love. Trust me, these cupcakes will undoubtedly become a family favorite in no time. So, friends, grab your aprons, and embark on a baking adventure with this match made in heaven of a recipe. Enjoy!
