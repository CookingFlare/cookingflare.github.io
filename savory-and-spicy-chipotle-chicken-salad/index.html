Savory and Spicy Chipotle Chicken Salad


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and hearty salads, then this savory and spicy Chipotle Chicken Salad is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. This salad is a perfect blend of smoky chipotle, tender chicken, and crisp vegetables, making it a satisfying and delicious meal for any time of the year. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy weeknight dinner or a crowd-pleasing dish for your next gathering, this recipe has got you covered.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons plain Greek yogurt
  • 1-2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon adobo sauce (from the canned chipotle peppers)
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 6 cups mixed salad greens

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together 1 tablespoon of olive oil, ground cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Rub the spice mixture evenly over the chicken breasts.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until cooked through. Once done, set aside to cool.

Step 2: Make the Chipotle Dressing

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, chopped chipotle peppers, adobo sauce, honey, and lime juice until well combined. Set aside.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

  1. Once the chicken has cooled, slice it into thin strips or bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced chicken, red onion, red bell pepper, corn kernels, and cilantro.
  3. Pour the chipotle dressing over the chicken and vegetables, then gently toss to coat everything evenly.
  4. In a separate bowl, toss the mixed salad greens with the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil and a squeeze of lime juice.
  5. Divide the dressed greens among serving plates and top with the chipotle chicken mixture.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy!

Garnish the salad with extra cilantro or a sprinkle of crumbled queso fresco if desired. This Savory and Spicy Chipotle Chicken Salad is best served immediately, allowing all the flavors to meld together beautifully.

Conclusion

This recipe is a celebration of bold and zesty flavors, perfect for those who enjoy a bit of heat in their meals. The smokiness of the chipotle peppers combined with the tender grilled chicken and crisp vegetables create a delightful harmony of tastes and textures. Whether you’re enjoying this salad as a light lunch or a satisfying dinner, it’s sure to leave your taste buds tingling with delight. So, next time you’re craving a salad with a kick, give this Chipotle Chicken Salad a try and savor the delicious fusion of flavors!
