Focaccia Bread Pizza: A Rustic Italian Delight


If you’re a pizza lover like me, then you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m going to share with you a recipe for a mouthwatering Focaccia Bread Pizza. This rustic Italian delight is the perfect combination of crispy bread, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey cheese. It’s a simple yet satisfying dish that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To make this scrumptious Focaccia Bread Pizza, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (such as sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, and pepperoni)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well.

  2. Gradually add the warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Stir until a soft dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  5. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer it onto the prepared baking sheet and gently press it into a rectangular shape, about 1/2 inch thick.

  7. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the surface of the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  8. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce, ensuring it covers the entire surface.

  9. Now it’s time to get creative! Add your favorite toppings, such as sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, and pepperoni. Feel free to experiment with different combinations to suit your taste.

  10. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  11. Once the Focaccia Bread Pizza is ready, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle some fresh basil leaves on top for a burst of flavor.

  12. Slice the pizza into squares or rectangles and serve it warm. Enjoy the delightful combination of crispy bread, tangy tomato sauce, and melted cheese!

Conclusion

Focaccia Bread Pizza is an easy and delicious alternative to traditional pizza. The pillowy focaccia bread serves as the perfect base, providing a satisfying crunch with each bite. The tangy tomato sauce and gooey cheese add layers of flavor, while the toppings of your choice allow you to personalize your pizza to suit your preferences. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner party or simply craving a homemade pizza, this rustic Italian delight is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey that will transport your taste buds to the heart of Italy. Buon appetito!
