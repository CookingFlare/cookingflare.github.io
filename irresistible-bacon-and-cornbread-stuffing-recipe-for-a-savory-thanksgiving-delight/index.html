Irresistible Bacon and Cornbread Stuffing Recipe for a Savory Thanksgiving Delight


Thanksgiving is a time for indulgence and savoring the flavors of the season. And what better way to elevate your Thanksgiving feast than with an irresistible Bacon and Cornbread Stuffing? This savory delight combines the smoky goodness of bacon with the comforting taste of cornbread, creating a stuffing that will have your guests coming back for seconds (and maybe even thirds!). So, let’s get started with this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 3 celery stalks, finely diced
  • 4 cups crumbled cornbread
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish and set it aside.

  2. In a large skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside on a paper towel-lined plate to drain.

  3. In the same skillet with the bacon grease, add the diced onion and celery. Sauté them until they become tender and translucent, about 5 minutes. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the mixture cool slightly.

  4. In a large bowl, combine the crumbled cornbread, cooked bacon, sautéed onion and celery, beaten eggs, chopped parsley, and thyme leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix everything together until well combined.

  5. Gradually pour the chicken or vegetable broth over the cornbread mixture, stirring gently to moisten the stuffing evenly. The stuffing should be slightly moist but not soggy.

  6. Transfer the stuffing mixture to the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  7. Bake the stuffing in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and crispy.

  8. Once the stuffing is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Serve it warm alongside your Thanksgiving turkey or as a delicious side dish.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add chopped fresh herbs like sage or rosemary to the stuffing mixture.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can omit the bacon and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.
  • To save time on Thanksgiving day, you can prepare the stuffing in advance and refrigerate it overnight. Just remember to bring it to room temperature before baking.

Enjoy the irresistible combination of smoky bacon and flavorful cornbread in this savory stuffing recipe. It’s the perfect complement to your Thanksgiving feast and is sure to impress your family and friends. So, go ahead and add this delightful dish to your holiday menu, and get ready for a Thanksgiving feast that will be talked about for years to come!

Happy Thanksgiving and happy cooking!
