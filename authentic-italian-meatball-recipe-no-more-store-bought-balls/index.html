Authentic Italian Meatball Recipe: No More Store-Bought Balls


Boasting the perfect balance of aromatic, hearty flavors, these homemade Italian meatballs are so much better than store-bought balls. Traditional Italian ingredients create a dish that delighted your taste buds and welcomes your friends and family to your table with open arms. But, it’s your unique touch that truly sets this recipe apart.

We all know that store-bought meatballs can never compare to the taste and comfort of homemade ones. What if I told you today’s recipe also beautifully highlights the exquisite melding of flavors only carefully selected ingredients can provide? Yes, today we explore an unforgettable Authentic Italian Meatball Recipe!

Homemade Italian Meatball Recipe: Ingredients

Here is your essential shopping list to create mouth-watering meatballs. Trust me, they’re worth every minute spent in the kitchen:

  • 1 pound ground beef (80/20)
  • 1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh oregano, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated nutritional yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce of your choice

Homemade Italian Meatball Recipe: Steps

Making these delectable homemade Italian meatballs is a breeze! Follow the steps below, and you’ll have a delicious, from-scratch meal in no time.

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, Italian breadcrumbs, parmesan cheese, egg, and seasonings. Mix thoroughly ensuring an even distribution of ingredients. The blend of parsley, basil, oregano, garlic, salt, black pepper, and nutritional yeast adds layers of unparalleled flavor.
  2. Form golf ball-sized meatballs from the mixture. No need to be perfect – irregular shapes only add to the charm!
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Gently place the meatballs in the skillet, leaving enough space between them for even browning.
  4. Cook for approximately five minutes on each side, or until the meatballs are browned on all sides but not fully cooked through.
  5. Once browned, remove the meatballs from the skillet, and carefully place them into your saucepan. You can either use the same saucepan used for cooking the meatballs, or if you prepared the sauce separately, transfer the meatballs to your cooled saucepan. Either way, ensure the meatballs are nestled snugly in the sauce.
  6. Pour your tomato or marinara sauce over the meatballs, enough to cover each meatball generously. Simmer gently with the lid on for about 30 minutes.
  7. The sauce and residual heat take care of the rest, cooking the meatballs through and infusing the flavors into the sauce. Serve alongside pasta, or enjoy with a crusty loaf of Italian bread.

Experience a taste of Italy in your kitchen with this robust Authentic Italian Meatball Recipe. Enjoy the fruits of your labor with your loved ones at the dinner table. Rest assured; no store-bought meatball will ever come close to these mouthwatering delights!
