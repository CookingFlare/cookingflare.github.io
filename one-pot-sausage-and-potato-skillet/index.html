One Pot Sausage and Potato Skillet


If you’re looking for a quick, easy, and delicious meal that requires minimal clean-up, then this One Pot Sausage and Potato Skillet is the perfect recipe for you. This hearty dish is packed with flavor and is sure to become a family favorite. With just a few simple ingredients and a single skillet, you can have a satisfying meal on the table in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of smoked sausage, sliced
  • 4 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by slicing the smoked sausage, dicing the potatoes, chopping the onion, mincing the garlic, and slicing the bell pepper. Having all the ingredients prepped and ready to go will make the cooking process smooth and efficient.

  2. Sear the Sausage: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced sausage to the skillet and cook until it is nicely browned on all sides. This will help to bring out the rich flavor of the sausage and add a delicious depth to the dish.

  3. Add the Aromatics: Once the sausage is browned, add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and sliced bell pepper to the skillet. Sauté the vegetables until they are softened and aromatic, which will take about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Cook the Potatoes: Add the diced potatoes to the skillet, and season everything with paprika, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and ensure that the seasonings are evenly distributed.

  5. Simmer and Cover: Pour in a 1/2 cup of water or chicken broth into the skillet and bring it to a simmer. Cover the skillet with a lid and let the mixture cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the flavors have melded together.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the potatoes are cooked through, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the One Pot Sausage and Potato Skillet with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the dish hot and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a spicy kick, you can use spicy smoked sausage or add a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this dish based on your preferences. You can add zucchini, mushrooms, or spinach for extra color and nutrition.
  • This recipe is versatile and can be easily adapted to include different seasonings such as rosemary, cumin, or Italian herbs for a unique flavor profile.

With its simple preparation and delicious results, this One Pot Sausage and Potato Skillet is a fantastic option for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. It’s a wholesome, satisfying meal that brings together the comforting flavors of sausage and potatoes in a single skillet. Give this recipe a try and savor the convenience and richness it brings to your table!
