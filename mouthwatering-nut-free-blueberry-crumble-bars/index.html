Mouthwatering Nut-Free Blueberry Crumble Bars


If you’re a fan of blueberries and crave a delightful treat that’s both nut-free and bursting with fruity flavor, then these mouthwatering nut-free blueberry crumble bars are the perfect solution. With a buttery crumb base, a luscious blueberry filling, and a crispy crumble topping, these bars are a delightful combination of sweet and tangy flavors. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, packing a picnic, or simply looking to satisfy your sweet tooth, these blueberry crumble bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crust and Crumble:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1 egg

For the Blueberry Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust and Crumble

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt for the crust and crumble.
  3. Add the cold butter pieces to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Add the egg and mix until the dough comes together.

Step 2: Making the Blueberry Filling

  1. In a separate bowl, gently toss together the blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until the blueberries are evenly coated.

Step 3: Assembling the Bars

  1. Press two-thirds of the crust mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking pan to form an even layer.
  2. Spread the blueberry filling over the crust.
  3. Crumble the remaining crust mixture evenly over the blueberry filling.

Step 4: Baking and Serving

  1. Bake the blueberry crumble bars in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the blueberry filling is bubbling.
  2. Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares and serving.

Tips and Variations

  • For a citrus twist, add a teaspoon of lemon zest to the crust and crumble mixture.
  • Serve the blueberry crumble bars with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat.
  • If fresh blueberries are not in season, frozen blueberries can be used as a substitute.

Final Thoughts

These nut-free blueberry crumble bars are a delightful way to showcase the natural sweetness of blueberries. They make a perfect addition to any brunch, afternoon tea, or dessert spread. The buttery crust and crumble add a comforting touch to the juicy blueberry filling, creating a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is a crowd-pleaser that’s sure to become a staple in your baking repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and savor the delightful aroma of these mouthwatering blueberry crumble bars as they bake to perfection.
