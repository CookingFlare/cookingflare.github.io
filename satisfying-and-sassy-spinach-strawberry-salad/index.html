Satisfying and Sassy Spinach Strawberry Salad


If you’re in the mood for a refreshing and satisfying salad that’s bursting with flavors, look no further than this Satisfying and Sassy Spinach Strawberry Salad. This delightful dish combines the freshness of spinach, the sweetness of strawberries, and the nuttiness of almonds, all brought together with a zesty balsamic vinaigrette. Whether you’re looking for a quick and healthy lunch option or a vibrant side dish for a summer barbecue, this salad is sure to brighten up any meal.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 6 cups fresh baby spinach
  • 2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds

For the Balsamic Vinaigrette:

  • 1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Prepare the Ingredients

Start by rinsing the baby spinach thoroughly and patting it dry with a clean kitchen towel or paper towels. Ensure that the strawberries are fresh and ripe, then slice them into thin rounds. If you prefer, you can substitute the feta cheese with goat cheese, and the sliced almonds with chopped pecans or walnuts for a different flavor profile.

2. Make the Balsamic Vinaigrette

In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, extra virgin olive oil, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined. The honey adds a touch of sweetness to balance the tanginess of the vinegar, while the Dijon mustard helps to emulsify the dressing for a smooth consistency.

3. Assemble the Salad

In a large salad bowl, combine the fresh baby spinach, sliced strawberries, and crumbled feta cheese. Gently toss the ingredients together to distribute them evenly. Sprinkle the sliced almonds over the top of the salad to add a delightful crunch and nutty flavor.

4. Drizzle with the Balsamic Vinaigrette

Just before serving, drizzle the prepared balsamic vinaigrette over the salad. Use as much or as little dressing as you prefer, keeping any extra in a sealed container in the refrigerator for future use. Toss the salad again to ensure that each leaf of spinach and slice of strawberry is coated with the dressing.

Serving Suggestions

This Satisfying and Sassy Spinach Strawberry Salad is perfect for a light lunch or as a side dish alongside grilled chicken, salmon, or a juicy steak. The combination of flavors and textures makes it a versatile accompaniment to a wide range of main courses. It’s also a great option for picnics, potlucks, and other outdoor gatherings, as the spinach holds up well to the dressing without wilting too quickly.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant colors, delightful flavors, and healthy ingredients, this Satisfying and Sassy Spinach Strawberry Salad is a celebration of summer on a plate. Whether you’re looking to add more greens to your diet or simply want to enjoy the abundance of seasonal produce, this salad is a wonderful way to do so. So, gather your ingredients, toss them together, and savor the fresh, sassy goodness of this delightful salad. Cheers to good health and great taste!
