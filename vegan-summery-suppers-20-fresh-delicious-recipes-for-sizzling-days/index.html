Vegan Summery Suppers: 20 Fresh & Delicious Recipes for Sizzling Days


Summer’s here, and the time is right for some delightful vegan meals that are light, refreshing, and perfect for those balmy days. As the temperature rises, who wants to be chained to the kitchen stove? These 20 sumptuous vegan recipes are not only bursting with flavors, but also incredibly simple to make, ensuring you can still enjoy delicious home-cooked food without fuss.

1. Zucchini Noodles with Avocado Pesto

  • Ingredients:

    • 4 medium zucchinis
    • 2 avocados
    • 1/2 cup fresh basil
    • 1/4 cup pine nuts
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1/4 cup lemon juice
    • Salt to taste
    • Freshly ground black pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Use a spiralizer to create zucchini noodles and set them aside.
    2. In a blender or food processor, combine avocado, basil, pine nuts, garlic, and lemon juice. Blend until smooth, adding a little water if necessary. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    3. Toss the zucchini noodles in the avocado pesto, and serve with an extra squeeze of lemon juice and a sprinkle of chili flakes for some heat.

2. Quinoa Tabbouleh

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 cup quinoa
    • 2 cups water
    • 2 cups chopped cucumber
    • 1 cup chopped parsley
    • 1 cup chopped mint
    • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
    • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • Juice of 2 lemons
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Cook quinoa in water according to package instructions and let it cool.
    2. Combine the cooked quinoa with cucumber, parsley, mint, onion, and tomatoes in a large bowl.
    3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour over the quinoa mixture and toss to combine. Refrigerate before serving.

3. Grilled Portobello Steaks

  • Ingredients:

    • 8 large portobello mushrooms
    • 1/2 cup vegan Worcestershire sauce
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Preheat your grill or grill pan.
    2. In a bowl, mix together vegan Worcestershire sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
    3. Place the mushrooms gill-side up, brush them with the marinade, and grill for about 5-7 minutes on each side, until tender.

4. Chilled Avocado Soup

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 ripe avocados
    • 1/2 cup chopped cucumber
    • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
    • 1 cup vegetable broth
    • 1/2 cup plain unsweetened soy milk
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • Juice of 1/2 lemon
    • Salt to taste

  • Instructions:

    1. Combine all ingredients, excluding soy milk and lemon juice, in a blender and blend until smooth.
    2. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Chill the soup in the refrigerator for at least one hour.
    3. Stir in soy milk before serving, garnish with extra cucumber or bell pepper pieces, and a squeeze of lemon juice.

5. Couscous Watermelon Salad

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 cup couscous
    • 2 cups boiling water
    • 2 cups cubed watermelon
    • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
    • 1/4 cup chopped mint
    • 1/4 cup chopped basil
    • Olive oil and lemon juice

  • Instructions:

    1. Prepare couscous according to package directions, allowing it to cool.
    2. Combine the cooled couscous, watermelon, onion, mint, and basil in a large bowl.
    3. Dress with a blend of olive oil and lemon juice, gently tossed to combine. Chill before serving.

6. Spicy Chickpea Lettuce Wraps

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 cans chickpeas
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1-inch piece ginger
    • 1/2 tsp cumin
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric
    • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
    • Salt
    • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 heads lettuce

  • Instructions:

    1. Drain and rinse the chickpeas. In a food processor, blend together chickpeas, garlic, ginger, cumin, turmeric, cayenne pepper, and salt until almost smooth.
    2. Transfer to a large skillet and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes. Stir in the lime juice and olive oil and continue to cook for another 5 minutes.
    3. Serve in halved lettuce leaves, garnishing with extra lime juice, cilantro, or jalapenos, as desired.

7. Veggie Sushi Rolls

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 cups sushi rice
    • 1 cup water
    • 1/2 cup nori sheets
    • Assorted veggies (avocado, cucumber, carrots, etc.)
    • 1 block tofu (optional)

  • Instructions:

    1. Rinse the rice and cook in water according to package instructions. Allow to cool.
    2. Lay out a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, and spread the rice evenly on top.
    3. Place your chosen veggies and/or tofu in a line on the nori, at the edge nearest to you.
    4. Using the sushi mat, roll the nori tightly, sealing the sushi roll by moistening the edge of the nori with a bit of water.
    5. Slice the rolls into bite-sized pieces and serve with pickled ginger, wasabi, and soy sauce.

8. Chilled Coconut Corn Soup

  • Ingredients:

    • 4 cups corn kernels
    • 2 cups unsweetened coconut milk
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1/2 onion, diced
    • 2 cups vegetable broth
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. In a large pot, combine all ingredients. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes.
    2. Using an immersion blender, puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer to a blender and blend, then return to the pot.
    3. Strain the soup through a fine-mesh sieve. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.

9. Mushroom and Spinach Pasta Salad

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 lb pasta (choose your favorite shape)
    • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
    • 2 cups fresh spinach
    • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
    • 1/2 cup olive oil
    • Juice of 1 lemon
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and cool.
    2. In a large skillet, cook sliced mushrooms until tender. Add spinach and cook briefly until wilted.
    3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil and lemon juice, then dress the cooled pasta with this mixture.
    4. Combine the pasta, sautéed mushrooms and spinach, and sun-dried tomatoes in a large bowl. Toss well, taste, and season with salt and pepper as needed.

10. Cucumber Quinoa Salad

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 cup quinoa
    • 2 cups water
    • 4 english cucumbers
    • 1/2 red onion
    • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
    • Juice of 1 lemon
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Cook quinoa in water according to package directions. Allow to cool.
    2. Chop cucumbers, onion, and parsley. Combine these with the cooled quinoa in a large bowl.
    3. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Pour over the quinoa mixture and gently toss to combine. Chill before serving.

11. Black Bean and Tomato Salad

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 cans black beans
    • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
    • 1/2 red onion
    • 1/2 jalapeno
    • Juice of 2 limes
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Rinse black beans, then combine with chopped tomatoes, red onion, and jalapeno in a large bowl.
    2. Whisk lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl. Pour the dressing over the black bean mixture and toss gently to combine. Chill before serving.

12. Lentil Salad with Feta and Mint

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 cup dried lentils
    • 3 cups water
    • 1/2 cup crumbled feta
    • 1/2 cup chopped mint
    • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
    • Juice of 1 lemon
    • Olive oil
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Cook lentils in water according to package directions. Let them cool slightly.
    2. In a large bowl, mix the cooled lentils with crumbled feta, mint, and red onion.
    3. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour over the lentil mixture and gently toss to combine.

13. Vegan BLT Sandwich

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 slices sourdough bread
    • 1/2 avocado, mashed
    • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
    • 1/4 cup vegan mayonnaise
    • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
    • 1/2 cup sliced tomato
    • 1/2 cup cooked pancetta or a vegan alternative
    • Lettuce and sliced cucumber

  • Instructions:

    1. Mash avocado with nutritional yeast, vegan mayonnaise, and smoked paprika until smooth. Spread on one slice of bread.
    2. Layer tomato slices, pancetta or vegan alternative, and lettuce on top of the spread. Add cucumber slices.
    3. Put the second slice of bread on top and serve.

14. Sweet Potato and Pesto Salad

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 large sweet potatoes
    • 1/4 cup pesto
    • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
    2. Dice sweet potatoes into bite-sized pieces and toss with pesto, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
    3. Spread the sweet potatoes on the lined baking tray and roast for 20-25 minutes, until tender and golden.

15. Chickpea and Corn Tacos

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 cans chickpeas
    • 1 cup corn
    • 1/2 onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • Salt and pepper
    • 8 corn tortillas
    • Lime wedges, chopped cilantro for garnish

  • Instructions:

    1. Combine drained chickpeas and corn in a large skillet. Cook until chickpeas are heated through.
    2. Add diced onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion softens. Season with salt and pepper.
    3. Serve warm tortillas filled with the chickpea mixture. Garnish with lime wedges and chopped cilantro.

16. Tomato Basil Bruschetta

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 baguette
    • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
    • 1/2 cup chopped basil
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • Olive oil
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Slice baguette and toast lightly under a broiler.
    2. In a bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, basil, minced garlic, a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
    3. Top the toasted bread slices with the tomato mixture, and serve.

17. Vegan Sushi Rice Bowl (Donburi)

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 cups sushi rice
    • 2 cups water
    • 1 avocado, sliced
    • 1 cup cooked edamame
    • 1 cup cucumber, sliced
    • 1/2 cup carrot, julienned
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
    • Sriracha or wasabi for garnish

  • Instructions:

    1. Cook sushi rice in water according to package directions, then stir in rice vinegar.
    2. Divide the rice into two bowls, topping with avocado, edamame, cucumber, and carrot.
    3. Drizzle soy sauce over the top and garnish with a squeeze of sriracha or wasabi.

18. Grilled Eggplant Baba Ganoush

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 medium eggplants
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1/4 cup tahini
    • Juice of 1 lemon
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Grill or broil eggplants until soft and slightly charred. Let cool.
    2. Peel and mash the eggplants with the garlic in a bowl. Stir in tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
    3. Chill in the refrigerator for at least an hour before serving with pita bread or crackers.

19. Roasted Beet Salad

  • Ingredients:

    • 4 medium beets
    • 2 cups baby greens
    • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
    • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
    • 2 tbsp maple syrup
    • 1/2 cup olive oil
    • Salt and pepper

  • Instructions:

    1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Wrap beets individually in foil and roast for 1-1.5 hours, until tender. Let cool slightly, then peel and slice.
    2. In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, maple syrup, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
    3. Toss baby greens with the dressing, then top with sliced beets and chopped walnuts.

20. Vegan Pad Thai

  • Ingredients:

    • 8 oz rice noodles
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
    • 1 tbsp sriracha
    • 1/4 cup peanut butter
    • 1/4 cup lime juice
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 lb tofu or tempeh
    • 2 cups bean sprouts
    • 2 cups chopped cabbage

  • Instructions:

    1. Cook the rice noodles according to package directions and run under cold water.
    2. In a separate bowl, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, sriracha, peanut butter, and lime juice.
    3. Cook tofu or tempeh, then slice and add to the peanut sauce.
    4. Toss the cooked noodles with the peanut sauce, tofu, bean sprouts, and chopped cabbage. Serve immediately.

Now you can enjoy delightful and refreshing Vegan Summery Suppers, perfect for those hot days when you still want to treat yourself to a delicious homecooked meal. These easy, fresh, and delicious recipes will make you wish every day was summer.
