Egg-Free Mediterranean Quinoa Salad: A Fresh and Flavorful Dish


Looking for a light, refreshing, and egg-free dish that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than this delightful Mediterranean Quinoa Salad! Packed with wholesome ingredients and vibrant colors, this salad is not only a treat for the taste buds but also a feast for the eyes. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, packing a picnic, or simply craving a nutritious meal, this recipe is sure to delight. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/3 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Quinoa: In a medium saucepan, combine the quinoa and water or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the liquid is absorbed. Once done, fluff the quinoa with a fork and allow it to cool to room temperature.

  2. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Set the dressing aside.

  3. Assemble the Salad: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, diced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, sliced Kalamata olives, finely chopped red onion, fresh parsley, and fresh mint. Gently toss the ingredients together until well combined.

  4. Add the Dressing: Pour the prepared dressing over the quinoa and vegetable mixture. Toss the salad gently to ensure that the dressing coats the ingredients evenly.

  5. Final Touch: If using, sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the salad and gently toss once more to incorporate.

  6. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. When ready to serve, give the salad a final toss and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

Serving Suggestions

This Egg-Free Mediterranean Quinoa Salad is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own as a light lunch or paired with grilled chicken, fish, or tofu for a heartier meal. It also makes for a fantastic side dish at barbecues, potlucks, or alongside your favorite Mediterranean-inspired entrees. The vibrant colors and fresh flavors of this salad are sure to impress your family and friends.

Nutritional Benefits

Not only is this salad a feast for the senses, but it’s also packed with nutritional benefits. Quinoa is a complete protein, making it an excellent choice for vegetarians and vegans. Additionally, the fresh vegetables and herbs provide an array of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making this salad a powerhouse of nutrients.

Conclusion

With its blend of wholesome ingredients, zesty dressing, and Mediterranean flair, this Egg-Free Mediterranean Quinoa Salad is a must-try for anyone seeking a healthy and flavorful dish. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is simple to prepare and guaranteed to impress. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean, and savor every delightful bite of this fresh and flavorful salad!
