Baking with Kids: Simple and Delicious Cinnamon Rolls


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls wafting through the house. The warm, comforting scent signals a treat that’s sure to bring a smile to everyone’s face. Baking with kids can be a wonderful way to spend quality time together, and making cinnamon rolls is a perfect activity to involve them in. Not only is it a fun and interactive experience, but it also teaches them valuable skills and creates lasting memories. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through a simple and delicious cinnamon roll recipe that you can enjoy making with your little ones.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 1/2 cups of flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warm milk, melted butter, and eggs.
  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface, adding the remaining 1/2 cup of flour as needed, until the dough is smooth and elastic.

Step 2: Roll Out the Dough

  1. Roll the dough into a rectangle, about 16x21 inches in size.
  2. Spread the softened butter over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the buttered dough.

Step 3: Shape the Rolls

  1. Starting with the long edge, tightly roll up the dough into a log.
  2. Cut the log into 12 equal pieces.

Step 4: Bake the Cinnamon Rolls

  1. Place the rolls in a greased 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Cover the pan with a clean kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for about 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake the rolls for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

Step 5: Make the Cream Cheese Frosting

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat together the softened cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth.
  2. Spread the frosting over the warm cinnamon rolls and serve.

Tips for Baking with Kids

1. Get Them Involved

Let the kids help with measuring ingredients, mixing the dough, and spreading the butter and sugar mixture. It’s a great way for them to practice their coordination and learn about different ingredients.

2. Create a Fun Atmosphere

Put on some music, tell stories, or play games while you’re waiting for the dough to rise. Making it an enjoyable experience will keep them engaged and excited about the baking process.

3. Embrace the Mess

Baking with kids can be messy, but it’s all part of the fun. Embrace the flour on the countertop and the sticky fingers - those are the moments that make baking together memorable.

4. Teach Them Patience

Waiting for the dough to rise and the rolls to bake can be a lesson in patience for kids. Use this time to talk about the science of baking and how yeast makes the dough rise.

5. Celebrate the Results

Once the cinnamon rolls are ready, let the kids take pride in their creation. Praise their efforts and enjoy the delicious treats together.

Baking with kids is not just about the end result; it’s about the journey of creating something together. The memories made and the skills learned will last a lifetime. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to make some delightful cinnamon rolls with your little ones!
