Easy and Creamy Parmesan Polenta


If you’re looking for a comforting and versatile dish to add to your recipe repertoire, look no further than this easy and creamy Parmesan polenta. This Italian classic is a wonderful side dish that pairs beautifully with a variety of mains, from braised meats to roasted vegetables. It’s simple to make, incredibly satisfying, and sure to impress your family and friends. Let’s dive into the recipe and explore the magic of Parmesan polenta!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 cup of coarsely ground yellow cornmeal
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan, bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat.

  2. Gradually whisk in the cornmeal, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming.

  3. Reduce the heat to low and add the salt. Continue to cook the polenta, stirring frequently, for about 15-20 minutes or until it thickens and the cornmeal is tender.

  4. Once the polenta reaches the desired consistency, remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the butter until it’s fully melted.

  5. Next, add the grated Parmesan cheese to the polenta, stirring until it’s well incorporated and the mixture becomes creamy.

  6. Season the polenta with freshly ground black pepper to taste.

  7. Serve the Parmesan polenta immediately as a delicious side dish or as a base for your favorite toppings.

Tips for Success

  • Use coarsely ground yellow cornmeal for the best texture and flavor.
  • Stir the polenta regularly to prevent it from sticking to the bottom of the pan.
  • Experiment with different variations by adding herbs, garlic, or even a splash of cream for extra richness.

Serving Suggestions

This creamy Parmesan polenta is incredibly versatile and can be served in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to inspire your culinary creativity:

  • Creamy Polenta with Mushroom Ragù: Top the polenta with a hearty mushroom ragù for a vegetarian-friendly meal that’s bursting with earthy flavors.

  • Polenta with Braised Short Ribs: Pair the creamy polenta with succulent braised short ribs for a comforting and indulgent dinner.

  • Grilled Vegetables and Polenta: Grill an assortment of seasonal vegetables and serve them over the Parmesan polenta for a vibrant and wholesome dish.

  • Polenta Fries: Pour the cooked polenta into a shallow dish, refrigerate until firm, then cut into fries and bake until golden and crispy for a unique twist on a classic side.

In Conclusion

With its creamy texture, rich flavor, and versatility, Parmesan polenta is a delightful addition to any meal. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting dish, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Embrace the simplicity and elegance of this Italian staple, and let your culinary imagination soar with creative serving ideas. Enjoy the process of making this easy and creamy Parmesan polenta, and savor the comforting goodness it brings to your table.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients and get ready to experience the deliciousness of homemade Parmesan polenta!
