5 Delicious Spring Burger Recipes to Fire Up Your Grill


As the weather starts to warm up, there’s nothing quite like firing up the grill and cooking up some delicious burgers. But why stick to the same old recipes when you can try something new and exciting? Here are 5 delicious spring burger recipes that are sure to fire up your grill and impress your friends and family.

Recipe 1: Spring Onion and Feta Burger

This burger is a perfect representation of the flavors of spring, with caramelized spring onions and crumbled feta cheese adding a tangy and sweet twist to the classic burger.

Ingredients:

  • 85/15 ground beef
  • 1/2 cup caramelized spring onions
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and pickles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, caramelized spring onions, feta cheese, olive oil, garlic, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix well with your hands until just combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.
  4. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Assemble the burgers on the buns with lettuce, tomato, and pickles (if using).
