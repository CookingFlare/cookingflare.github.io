Family-Friendly BBQ Chicken Recipes for Outdoor Grilling


When it comes to outdoor grilling, BBQ chicken is a classic favorite that the whole family can enjoy. The smoky flavors, tender meat, and sticky sauce make it a perfect dish for a summer gathering or a cozy weekend dinner. In this blog post, we will explore three delicious and family-friendly BBQ chicken recipes that are guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds. So, fire up the grill and let’s get cooking!

Recipe 1: Honey Glazed BBQ Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine honey, ketchup, soy sauce, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Thread the chicken cubes onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  4. Brush the honey glaze mixture over the chicken skewers, making sure to coat them evenly.
  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the glaze is caramelized.
  6. Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve with your favorite side dishes.

Recipe 2: Tangy BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large ziplock bag and pour the BBQ sauce mixture over them. Seal the bag and marinate the drumsticks in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for better flavor.
  4. Remove the drumsticks from the marinade and place them on the preheated grill. Reserve the marinade for basting.
  5. Grill the drumsticks for about 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with the reserved marinade, until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve with a fresh salad or grilled vegetables.

Recipe 3: BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 store-bought pizza dough
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the BBQ sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded chicken, shredded mozzarella cheese, red onion, and cilantro leaves over the BBQ sauce.
  6. Carefully transfer the pizza onto the preheated grill and close the lid. Grill for about 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy!

These family-friendly BBQ chicken recipes are perfect for your next outdoor grilling session. Whether you prefer skewers, drumsticks, or a delicious pizza, these recipes are sure to please both kids and adults alike. So gather your loved ones, fire up the grill, and indulge in the irresistible flavors of BBQ chicken. Happy grilling!
