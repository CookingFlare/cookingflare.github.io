Savory and Smoky: BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Meatballs


Are you a fan of juicy meatballs infused with the smoky goodness of bacon? Look no further! Today, we’re bringing you a mouthwatering recipe for Savory and Smoky BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Meatballs that will leave you craving for more. These delectable bites are perfect for parties, game nights, or simply as a delicious appetizer to enjoy with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for a spicy kick)
  • 1 package bacon
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, milk, onion, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, oregano, basil, salt, black pepper, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using). Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Take about a tablespoon of the meat mixture and roll it into a small meatball. Repeat until all the mixture is used up, making approximately 20-24 meatballs.

  4. Cut the bacon slices in half. Take each half-slice of bacon and wrap it around a meatball, securing it with a toothpick. Place the bacon-wrapped meatballs on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each one.

  5. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the meatballs are cooked through.

  6. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the BBQ sauce. In a small saucepan, heat the barbecue sauce over low heat until warmed through.

  7. Once the meatballs are cooked, remove them from the oven and carefully remove the toothpicks. Using a brush, generously coat each meatball with the BBQ sauce.

  8. Return the meatballs to the oven and bake for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the sauce to caramelize slightly.

  9. Remove from the oven and let the meatballs cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve them as an appetizer with toothpicks or as a main dish with your favorite side.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a different type of meat, feel free to substitute the ground beef with ground chicken, turkey, or pork. The bacon wrapping will add a touch of smoky flavor to any meat you choose.
  • For a twist, try adding a small cube of cheese in the center of each meatball before wrapping it with bacon. The melted cheese will create an irresistible surprise when you take a bite.
  • Experiment with different types of barbecue sauce for a unique flavor profile. Whether you prefer a tangy, sweet, or spicy sauce, there’s no wrong choice when it comes to BBQ bacon-wrapped meatballs.

So there you have it – a tantalizing recipe for Savory and Smoky BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Meatballs that will make your taste buds dance with delight. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply looking to indulge in a mouthwatering treat, these meatballs are sure to impress. Give them a try and watch as they disappear from the plate in no time. Enjoy!
