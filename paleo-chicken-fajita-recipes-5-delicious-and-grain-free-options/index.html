Paleo Chicken Fajita Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


Are you following a Paleo lifestyle and looking for new and exciting recipes to spice up your mealtime? Look no further than these mouthwatering Paleo chicken fajita recipes. Packed with flavor and completely grain-free, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your healthy eating goals. Let’s dive right into the deliciousness!

1. Classic Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 bell peppers (red, green, or a mix), sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lettuce leaves (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken strips to the skillet and season with salt, pepper, chili powder, cumin, and paprika. Cook until the chicken is browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss with the vegetables to combine.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  7. Serve the chicken fajita mixture in lettuce leaves for a grain-free option.

2. Spicy Lime Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 bell peppers (yellow, orange, or a mix), sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sliced avocado (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the lime juice, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Add the sliced chicken thighs and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the coconut oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.
  6. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss with the vegetables to combine.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve the spicy lime chicken fajitas with sliced avocado for a creamy and flavorful addition.

3. Smoky Chipotle Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 bell peppers (red, yellow, or a mix), sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of avocado oil
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Guacamole (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the avocado oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken strips to the skillet and season with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and oregano. Cook until the chicken is browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, minced garlic, and minced chipotle peppers. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss with the vegetables to combine.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  7. Serve the smoky chipotle chicken fajitas with a side of guacamole for a creamy and spicy kick.

4. Zesty Lemon Herb Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 bell peppers (green, red, or a mix), sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of ghee
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sliced cucumbers (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Add the sliced chicken thighs and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the ghee in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.
  6. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss with the vegetables to combine.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley.
  8. Serve the zesty lemon herb chicken fajitas with sliced cucumbers for a refreshing and tangy twist.

5. Teriyaki Pineapple Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 bell peppers (red, orange, or a mix), sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut aminos
  • Juice of 1/2 a lime
  • 1/4 cup of pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup of chopped pineapple
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Shredded coconut (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the coconut aminos, lime juice, pineapple juice, chopped pineapple, salt, and pepper. Add the sliced chicken breasts and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.
  6. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss with the vegetables to combine.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve the teriyaki pineapple chicken fajitas with a sprinkle of shredded coconut for a tropical and sweet twist.

Enjoy these Paleo chicken fajita recipes, and let your taste buds savor the flavors of a healthy and delicious meal. Whether you choose the classic version or opt for a spicy or zesty twist, these grain-free options are sure to become a family favorite. Happy cooking!
