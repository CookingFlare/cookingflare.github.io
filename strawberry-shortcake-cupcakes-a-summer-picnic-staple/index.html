Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes: A Summer Picnic Staple


Summertime is the perfect season to enjoy picnics in the park, pool parties with friends, and a plethora of fresh seasonal fruits. This year, elevate your summer gatherings by incorporating the quintessential Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes to your dessert menu. These delightful treats will not only tantalize your taste buds but also bring a touch of nostalgia to your summer outings.

Ingredients • 2 cups all-purpose flour • 2 teaspoons baking powder • ½ teaspoon salt • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature • 2 cups granulated sugar • 4 large eggs • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract • 2/3 cup whole milk • 1 cup fresh strawberries, pureed • 1 cup strawberry jam • 2 cups powdered sugar • 3-4 cups fresh strawberries, chopped (for decorations) • Whipping cream (for decorations)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line cupcake tins with paper liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, to the butter-sugar mixture, making sure to fully incorporate each egg before adding the next.

  5. Add the vanilla extract and mix well.

  6. Combine the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients in three additions, alternating with the milk and strawberry puree, starting and ending with the dry ingredients.

  7. Fill each cupcake liner about 2/3 full with batter and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow cupcakes to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Once the cupcakes have cooled, make the frosting by combining the strawberry jam and powdered sugar in a bowl. Mix well until smooth.

  9. Using a pairing knife, create a small hole in the center of each cupcake and fill it with the strawberry-jam frosting. Fill the cupcake hole about ¾ full, making sure not to overfill it.

  10. For added elegance, you can also pipe the frosting onto the cupcakes. However, this step is optional.

  11. Decorate each cupcake with chopped strawberries and a dollop of whipped cream. You can also place one whole strawberry on top, with a few pieces of whipped cream around it, for an even fancier appearance.

  12. Chill the cupcakes in the refrigerator for approximately 30 minutes to allow the frosting to set and to enhance the flavor before serving.

Savoring the Flavors The delightful combination of vanilla-infused cupcakes and the lusciously sweet strawberry puree makes these cupcakes a summer staple. The freshness of the strawberries pulls inspiration from the classic dessert, while the creaminess of the frosting provides the perfect balance to the tart sweetness.

Perfect for shared moments and welcoming summertime get-togethers, these Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes bring your guests the nostalgic and delectable taste of a summer picnic in every bite.

Conclusion Whether you’re attendees of an outdoor wedding, a lazy Sunday brunch, or a simple picnic in the park, these dazzling Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes will be a hit with everyone in attendance. Take advantage of the warm weather and indulge in this scrumptious treat that brings summer flavors to life. Grab that picnic basket and enjoy!
