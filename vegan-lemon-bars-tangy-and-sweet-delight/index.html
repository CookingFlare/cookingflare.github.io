Vegan Lemon Bars: Tangy and Sweet Delight


If you’re a fan of tangy and sweet desserts, then these Vegan Lemon Bars are an absolute must-try! With a buttery crust and a zesty lemon filling, these bars are not only delicious but also vegan-friendly. They are perfect for any occasion, from a casual afternoon treat to a fancy dinner party dessert. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup of powdered sugar
  • ½ cup of vegan butter, softened
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt

For the lemon filling:

  • 1 ½ cups of granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup of all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • 1 ½ cups of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • ½ cup of plant-based milk (such as almond or soy milk)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, powdered sugar, vegan butter, and salt for the crust. Mix well until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Press the crust mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish, making sure to create an even layer. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust turns golden brown.

  4. While the crust is baking, prepare the lemon filling. In a large bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar, all-purpose flour, and cornstarch until well combined.

  5. Gradually add in the freshly squeezed lemon juice, lemon zest, and plant-based milk to the sugar mixture. Whisk until smooth and no lumps remain.

  6. Once the crust is baked, remove it from the oven and pour the lemon filling over the hot crust. Spread the filling evenly using a spatula.

  7. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges turn slightly golden.

  8. Remove the dish from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack. Once cooled, refrigerate the lemon bars for at least 2 hours, or until firm.

  9. Once chilled, remove the bars from the baking dish using the parchment paper. Cut them into squares or rectangles, depending on your preference.

  10. Serve the Vegan Lemon Bars chilled and dusted with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a gluten-free version, you can substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend.
  • Feel free to experiment with different citrus fruits for the filling. Orange or lime juice can be used as alternatives to lemon juice.
  • If you prefer a more intense lemon flavor, add a few drops of lemon extract to the filling mixture.
  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. They can also be frozen for longer storage.

Whether you’re a vegan or not, these Vegan Lemon Bars will surely impress your taste buds. The combination of the tangy lemon filling and the buttery crust creates a delightful contrast of flavors. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try. Your friends and family will be amazed by your baking skills!
