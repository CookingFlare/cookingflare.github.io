Creamy Tomato Basil Soup with Grilled Cheese: A Comforting Classic Combo


There’s something about the combination of creamy tomato basil soup and a warm, gooey grilled cheese sandwich that brings back memories of cozy evenings and childhood comfort. This classic combo never fails to satisfy, and it’s incredibly easy to make. So, if you’re looking for a comforting meal that will warm your heart and soul, look no further than this delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of your favorite cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or Swiss work great)
  • Butter for spreading

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.

  2. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth, and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth and creamy. Be careful when blending hot liquids, as they can splatter. If using a regular blender, blend in batches and return the soup to the pot.

  4. Stir in the heavy cream and fresh basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes to allow the flavors to combine.

  5. While the soup is simmering, it’s time to make the grilled cheese sandwiches. Butter one side of each bread slice.

  6. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Place two slices of bread, buttered side down, on the hot surface. Top each slice with a slice of cheese. Place the remaining bread slices on top, buttered side up.

  7. Cook the sandwiches for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted. Remove from the heat.

  8. Ladle the creamy tomato basil soup into bowls and serve alongside the grilled cheese sandwiches.

Tips and Variations

  • To add extra flavor to the soup, you can add a pinch of dried oregano or a splash of balsamic vinegar during the simmering process.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese for the grilled cheese sandwiches. Gouda, provolone, or even a combination of cheeses can take this classic sandwich to a whole new level.
  • If you’re looking to make the soup a bit healthier, you can substitute the heavy cream with coconut milk or almond milk. It will still be deliciously creamy but with a lighter touch.

Enjoy the Comfort and Nostalgia

Creamy tomato basil soup with grilled cheese is a timeless combination that brings comfort and warmth to any meal. Whether you’re enjoying it on a chilly winter evening or simply craving a taste of nostalgia, this classic combo is sure to hit the spot. So, gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and get ready to savor the comforting flavors of this delightful recipe.
