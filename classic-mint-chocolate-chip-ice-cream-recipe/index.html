Classic Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream Recipe


Mint chocolate chip ice cream is a classic favorite that never goes out of style. It’s the perfect combination of refreshing mint and rich chocolate, making it an irresistible treat on a hot summer day. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the steps to create your own homemade version of this beloved dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure mint extract
  • A few drops of green food coloring (optional)
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and salt. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir the mixture until the sugar has completely dissolved.

  2. Once the mixture is heated through, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes. Stir in the mint extract and green food coloring (if desired). The food coloring will give your ice cream that classic minty green hue.

  3. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until the mixture is completely chilled.

  4. Once the mixture is chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes around 20-25 minutes.

  5. While the ice cream is churning, chop the semisweet chocolate chips into small, bite-sized pieces.

  6. When the ice cream is almost done churning, add in the chopped chocolate chips. Allow the ice cream maker to continue churning until the chips are evenly distributed throughout the mixture.

  7. Transfer the freshly churned ice cream into an airtight container. Press a piece of plastic wrap directly onto the surface of the ice cream to prevent ice crystals from forming. Cover the container with a lid and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until the ice cream is firm.

  8. Once the ice cream has hardened, it’s ready to be scooped and enjoyed! Serve it in bowls or cones, and garnish with a sprig of fresh mint for an extra touch of elegance.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of mint flavor, you can add a handful of finely chopped fresh mint leaves to the mixture before refrigerating.

  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve before churning it in the ice cream maker. This will remove any small bits of mint leaves or other solids.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of chocolate. Dark chocolate or white chocolate can be used in place of the semisweet chocolate chips for a unique twist on the classic recipe.

  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can still make this recipe! Simply pour the chilled mixture into a shallow dish and place it in the freezer. Every 30 minutes, remove the dish from the freezer and stir the mixture vigorously with a fork to break up any ice crystals. Repeat this process until the ice cream reaches the desired consistency.

Enjoy your homemade classic mint chocolate chip ice cream! It’s the perfect treat to cool you down on a hot summer day or any time you’re craving a refreshing and indulgent dessert.
