Savory Bacon-Wrapped Dates Stuffed with Goat Cheese


If you’re in the mood for a delectable appetizer that combines sweet, savory, and creamy flavors in one irresistible bite, then look no further than these savory bacon-wrapped dates stuffed with goat cheese. This elegant yet easy-to-make dish is perfect for entertaining guests or simply treating yourself to a delightful culinary experience. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering treat.

Ingredients

  • 24 Medjool dates
  • 12 slices of bacon, cut in half
  • 4 oz goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lining a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Dates: Using a small knife, carefully make a lengthwise incision in each date and remove the pit. Be gentle to keep the date intact.

  3. Stuff with Goat Cheese: Take a small amount of goat cheese and stuff it into the cavity of each date. The creamy texture and tangy flavor of the goat cheese will beautifully complement the sweetness of the dates.

  4. Wrap with Bacon: Once the dates are stuffed, wrap each one with a half slice of bacon, securing it with a toothpick. This step adds a savory element that perfectly balances the sweetness of the dates and the creaminess of the goat cheese.

  5. Arrange on Baking Sheet: Place the bacon-wrapped dates on the prepared baking sheet, ensuring they are evenly spaced to allow for even cooking.

  6. Bake to Perfection: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the dates are caramelized.

  7. Prepare the Glaze: While the dates are baking, prepare the glaze by combining the honey and balsamic vinegar in a small saucepan over medium heat. Simmer the mixture for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

  8. Glaze the Dates: Once the bacon-wrapped dates are cooked, carefully brush each one with the honey-balsamic glaze, adding a delightful sweetness and tangy flavor that elevates the dish to a whole new level.

  9. Serve and Enjoy: Arrange the bacon-wrapped dates on a serving platter and watch as they disappear within moments. These delectable bites are best enjoyed warm, allowing the contrasting flavors and textures to tantalize your taste buds.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crunch, consider adding a small almond or walnut inside each date before stuffing it with goat cheese.
  • If you prefer a smoky flavor, you can use smoked bacon for an additional depth of taste.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as blue cheese or gorgonzola, for a unique twist on this classic appetizer.

Conclusion

Savory bacon-wrapped dates stuffed with goat cheese are a true delight for the senses, offering a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that will leave your guests craving for more. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply looking to indulge in a sophisticated treat, this recipe is a must-try. Embrace the simplicity and elegance of this dish, and let its irresistible appeal become a highlight of your culinary repertoire. Enjoy every savory, sweet, and creamy bite!
