Sweet and Tangy Orange Cranberry Bread Recipe


Are you looking for a delightful treat that perfectly balances sweetness and tanginess? Look no further than this delicious Sweet and Tangy Orange Cranberry Bread recipe. This easy-to-make bread is bursting with citrusy orange flavor and pops of juicy cranberries, making it a perfect choice for breakfast, snacks, or even dessert. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring a touch of citrusy goodness to your kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2/3 cup fresh orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons orange zest
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen cranberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well to ensure even distribution of the dry ingredients.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the vegetable oil, orange juice, orange zest, and eggs until well combined.

  4. Mix Batter: Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

  5. Fold in Cranberries: Gently fold in the cranberries until evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Transfer the bread to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions:

This Sweet and Tangy Orange Cranberry Bread is best enjoyed slightly warm or at room temperature. You can serve it plain, with a dollop of whipped cream, or a drizzle of orange glaze for an extra touch of sweetness. Pair a slice with a cup of hot tea or coffee for a delightful snack or breakfast.

Storage Tips:

To keep your Orange Cranberry Bread fresh, store it in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, wrap the bread tightly in plastic wrap and foil, then freeze for up to 3 months. Thaw overnight in the refrigerator before serving.

Final Thoughts:

This Sweet and Tangy Orange Cranberry Bread is a perfect blend of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds with every bite. Whether you’re baking it for yourself, family, or friends, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to enjoy a slice of citrusy heaven. Happy baking!

Remember, the key to a successful Orange Cranberry Bread lies in the balance of flavors and the freshness of the ingredients. So, don’t hesitate to experiment and make this recipe your own by adding nuts, spices, or even a hint of vanilla. Let your creativity shine in the kitchen, and savor the delicious results!
