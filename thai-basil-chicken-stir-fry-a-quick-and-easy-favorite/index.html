Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry: A Quick and Easy Favorite


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that can be whipped up in no time, look no further than Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry. This flavorful dish combines the fragrant aroma of basil with tender chicken and a medley of vegetables, creating a taste sensation that will leave you wanting more. Best of all, it’s incredibly easy to make, making it perfect for those busy weeknight dinners or when you’re craving something tasty and satisfying.

Ingredients

To make this Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 500 grams of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a large wok or skillet over high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and sliced red chili. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  2. Add the sliced chicken to the wok and stir-fry until it’s cooked through and no longer pink in the center. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes, depending on the thickness of the chicken slices.

  3. Push the chicken to one side of the wok and add the sliced onions and bell peppers. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened but still retain their crunch.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables in the wok. Stir everything together to coat the ingredients evenly.

  5. Add the fresh basil leaves to the wok and continue stir-frying for another minute or two until the basil leaves have wilted slightly.

  6. Taste the stir-fry and season with salt and pepper as desired. Remember, the sauces already contain salt, so be mindful not to overdo it.

  7. Once everything is well combined and heated through, remove the wok from the heat. Serve the Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry immediately with steamed rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, you can add additional chili or a sprinkle of chili flakes to the stir-fry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables to your liking. You can add mushrooms, carrots, or even snap peas for added texture and flavor.
  • If you’re vegetarian, you can replace the chicken with tofu or your favorite mock meat substitute.
  • Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry is a versatile dish that pairs well with steamed rice, jasmine rice, or noodles. Experiment with different combinations to find your favorite.

Conclusion

Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry is a quick and easy favorite that is sure to satisfy your taste buds. With its bold flavors and simple preparation, it’s a dish that everyone can enjoy. Whether you’re a busy professional or a home cook looking for a delicious meal, this recipe is a winner. So grab your wok, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a mouthwatering Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry that will leave you craving more.
