The Secret Life of Pesto: Creative Ways to Use Pesto in Your Pizza and Flatbread Creations


Pesto - that vibrant, green, herbaceous sauce - has found its way into countless kitchens since its humble beginnings in the heart of Italy. But what if you were to unlock the full potential of this versatile sauce? Ready to stir things up? Here are some creative ways to use pesto in your next pizza or flatbread adventure.

1. Pesto Base

Switch up your traditional red sauce base with a few swishes of pesto. It’s a fantastic way to instantly infuse your pizza with an intense burst of flavor. Drizzle it on sparingly, for a green tinge in each bite, or paint it on thick for a green overload with every slice.

2. Pesto Chicken Flatbread

Feeling simple yet impactful? Lay down a thin layer of pesto, followed by your favorite cooked chicken, sliced grape tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes. Pop it into a screaming hot oven until the cheese goes all melty. Slice and enjoy.

3. Potato Pesto Pizza

Love potatoes? Time for a twist! Start with a thin pizza crust, spread a small amount of pesto, then add thinly sliced roasted potatoes, crumbled bacon, shredded cheddar cheese, and a bit of chopped green onion. Bake until the cheese is bubbly and the potatoes are tender.

4. Veggie Power Pesto Pizza

Protein-packed, colorful vegetables loaded into a pesto-inspired pizza? Yes, please! Lay a thin layer of pesto as your base, then layer on the fresh goodness of grilled vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers, eggplant, or cherry tomatoes. Sprinkle mozzarella, bake to perfection, and let the deliciousness unravel.

5. Pesto Shrimp Flatbread

Fancy a seafood touch to your pizza? Create a pesto-infused base for a delightful, succulent shrimp flatbread. Add zesty slices of lemon, a sprinkle of red pepper flakes, and freshly grated Parmesan for a taste explosion. For a creamy touch, drizzle some dollops of mayo-garlic-sriracha sauce. Bake to bubbly glory.

6. Pesto, Artichoke, and Spinach Flatbread

Impress your guests with this elegant, savory creation. Start with a pesto base, scattered with marinated artichoke hearts and dollops of ricotta cheese. Top the ensemble with fresh baby spinach leaves, then bake until spinach wilts and cheese turns golden.

7. BBQ Pesto Chicken Pizza

BBQ fans, rejoice! Elevate your BBQ chicken days by swirring in a dollop of pesto into your red sauce, or ditch the red sauce for an all-out pesto rendition. Pile layers of juicy BBQ chicken, red onion rings, and some crumbled Blue cheese for an unexpected twist.

8. Mediterranean Mozzarella Flatbread

A Mediterranean-inspired creation? Yes, easily done with pesto! Brush it onto your pizza or flatbread, then layer slices of roasted red bell peppers, Kalamata olives, fresh mozzarella, and caramelized onions. Bake until the cheese is gooey and onions are soft.

9. Pesto Egg Pizza

Still hungry for more? This unconventional yet delightful blend of flavors is a must-try. Build your flatbread on a bed of pesto, add some crumbled cooked bacon, freshly grated Parmesan, and listen up - top it off with a perfectly poached egg. Bake gently, then cut into wedges to experience the gleeful yolk flow.

10. Vegan Power Pesto Pizza

Digging into plant-based goodness? A vegan-friendly pesto pizza is a dream come true! Craft your crust here, spread the pesto thinly, then add layers of your favorite roasted vegetables, tofu, then sprinkle with cashew cheese. Toss it into a hot oven and savor the colorful ensemble of flavors and textures.

So, ready to dip your toes into the endlessly exciting world of pesto-infused pizza and flatbreads? Your taste buds (and Instagram followers) will be thanking you. For more such culinary adventures, stay tuned to our blog for loads of unexpected, mouth-watering combinations. Grab your pesto, friends, and let’s start cooking up some major flavor surges!
