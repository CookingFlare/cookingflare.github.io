Salted Caramel Blondies: Sweet and Salty Perfection


Welcome to the world of irresistible desserts! In this blog post, we will be exploring a delectable treat that combines the sweetness of caramel with the savory touch of salt – Salted Caramel Blondies. Prepare to indulge your taste buds in a tantalizing blend of flavors and textures. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy your sweet tooth. So, grab your apron and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the Blondies:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Salted Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and light brown sugar until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Pour the blondie batter into the greased baking dish, spreading it evenly with a spatula. Bake for approximately 25-30 minutes or until the edges are golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  6. While the blondies are baking, let’s prepare the salted caramel sauce. In a medium saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water over medium heat. Stir gently until the sugar has dissolved, then increase the heat to high.

  7. Allow the mixture to come to a boil, swirling the pan occasionally. Cook until the mixture turns a deep amber color, which should take about 6-8 minutes.

  8. Remove the saucepan from the heat and carefully whisk in the heavy cream. Be cautious as the mixture will bubble up. Add the unsalted butter and sea salt, stirring until the sauce is smooth and well combined.

  9. Once the blondies are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool in the baking dish for about 10 minutes. Drizzle the salted caramel sauce over the top, spreading it evenly with a spoon or spatula.

  10. Allow the blondies to cool completely before cutting them into squares. The caramel sauce will firm up slightly as it cools, creating a perfect balance of sweetness and saltiness.

  11. Serve and enjoy! These Salted Caramel Blondies are best enjoyed with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a warm cup of coffee. Share them with friends and family or savor them all to yourself – they’re simply irresistible!

Conclusion

Congratulations! You have successfully created a batch of heavenly Salted Caramel Blondies. The combination of the rich, buttery blondies and the sweet-yet-salty caramel sauce is truly a match made in dessert heaven. Whether you’re looking for a delightful treat to satisfy your sweet cravings or a show-stopping dessert to impress guests, these blondies will not disappoint. So, go ahead and indulge in the perfect blend of sweet and salty perfection. Happy baking!
