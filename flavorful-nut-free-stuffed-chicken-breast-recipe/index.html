Flavorful Nut-Free Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe


If you’re a fan of chicken and looking for a delicious and nut-free recipe to try, then this flavorful stuffed chicken breast recipe is perfect for you! Packed with savory flavors and juicy chicken, this dish is sure to impress your family and friends. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make! So, let’s get started.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Place the chicken breasts on a cutting board and use a sharp knife to carefully slice them horizontally, creating a pocket in each breast. Be careful not to cut all the way through.

  3. In a bowl, combine the chopped spinach, feta cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  4. Spoon the filling mixture into each chicken breast pocket, dividing it equally among them. Press the chicken together to seal the pocket, and secure with toothpicks if necessary.

  5. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the stuffed chicken breasts in the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.

  6. Transfer the chicken breasts to a baking dish and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center.

  7. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the juices to redistribute.

  8. Serve the stuffed chicken breasts with your favorite side dishes, such as roasted vegetables or a fresh salad. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • To add some extra flavor, you can marinate the chicken breasts in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, and your favorite herbs and spices for 30 minutes to an hour before stuffing and cooking them.

  • If you prefer a different type of cheese, feel free to substitute the feta cheese with mozzarella, cheddar, or any other cheese that melts well.

  • For a gluten-free version of this recipe, make sure the feta cheese and sun-dried tomatoes you use are gluten-free certified.

  • If you have leftovers, you can slice the stuffed chicken breasts and use them in sandwiches, wraps, or salads for a quick and tasty meal.

Final Thoughts

This flavorful nut-free stuffed chicken breast recipe is a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone asking for seconds. The combination of spinach, feta cheese, and sun-dried tomatoes creates a burst of flavors that perfectly complement the juicy chicken. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just a weeknight dinner, this recipe is sure to impress. So, give it a try and enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal!
