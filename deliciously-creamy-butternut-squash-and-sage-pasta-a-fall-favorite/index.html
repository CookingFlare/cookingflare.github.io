Deliciously Creamy Butternut Squash and Sage Pasta: A Fall Favorite


As the leaves turn golden and the air gets crisp, it’s the perfect time to savor the flavors of fall. One dish that captures the essence of the season is the deliciously creamy butternut squash and sage pasta. This comforting and flavorful recipe is a celebration of autumn produce and will surely warm your heart and soul.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh sage leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 pound pasta of your choice
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss the butternut squash cubes with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Spread them in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and lightly caramelized.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the remaining olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped sage, and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the roasted butternut squash to the skillet and mash it with a fork or potato masher until it forms a creamy consistency.
  5. Pour in the vegetable broth and heavy cream, stirring to combine. Let the sauce simmer for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  6. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the cooking water.
  7. Toss the cooked pasta in the butternut squash sauce, adding a splash of the pasta cooking water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  8. Serve the creamy butternut squash and sage pasta in bowls, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and an extra sprinkle of chopped sage.

Why This Recipe Is Perfect for Fall

The creamy texture of the butternut squash sauce combined with the earthy aroma of sage creates a symphony of flavors that evokes the warmth and comfort of the fall season. This dish not only celebrates seasonal produce but also offers a satisfying and indulgent dining experience.

Butternut squash is a quintessential fall ingredient, known for its sweet and nutty flavor. When roasted, it develops a rich caramelization that adds depth to the sauce. The addition of sage, with its woodsy and peppery notes, complements the sweetness of the squash and lends a delightful herbal essence to the dish.

Moreover, the creamy sauce coats the pasta beautifully, ensuring that every bite is a harmonious blend of velvety richness and comforting flavors. This recipe is a wonderful way to embrace the spirit of fall and enjoy a heartwarming meal with loved ones.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering or simply craving a cozy night in, this deliciously creamy butternut squash and sage pasta is a must-try. Its vibrant colors, luscious texture, and captivating flavors make it a standout dish that captures the magic of the autumn season. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of fall, and savor every spoonful of this delectable pasta creation.
