Honey Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry: A Sticky and Sweet Seafood Treat


If you’re a seafood lover like me, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious and flavorful Honey Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry recipe. Packed with sticky and sweet goodness, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household. The combination of succulent shrimp, aromatic garlic, and the natural sweetness of honey creates a mouthwatering symphony of flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To make this delectable Honey Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley or green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, cornstarch, sesame oil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and slightly opaque.

  3. Once the shrimp are cooked, reduce the heat to medium and pour the sauce over them. Stir well to coat the shrimp evenly with the sticky and sweet sauce.

  4. Continue cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the shrimp are fully cooked.

  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the shrimp sit for a minute to absorb all the flavors.

  6. Serve the Honey Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry over a bed of steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley or green onions for added freshness and color.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add more red pepper flakes or a dash of Sriracha sauce to the sauce mixture.
  • Feel free to add your favorite vegetables to the stir fry for added crunch and nutrition. Bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas work particularly well with this recipe.
  • You can marinate the shrimp in the sauce for 15-20 minutes before cooking to enhance the flavors even more.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can increase the amount of cornstarch to achieve the desired consistency.
  • This Honey Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry is best enjoyed immediately after cooking, as the shrimp tend to become chewy when reheated.

Final Thoughts

With its sticky and sweet sauce and succulent shrimp, this Honey Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry is a seafood lover’s dream come true. The combination of flavors creates a delightful harmony that will make your taste buds dance with joy. Whether you’re preparing a quick weeknight dinner or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. So, go ahead and give it a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and making the dish your own. Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Enjoy your sticky and sweet seafood treat!
