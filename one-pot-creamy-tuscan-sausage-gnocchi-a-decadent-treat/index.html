One-Pot Creamy Tuscan Sausage Gnocchi: A Decadent Treat


If you’re craving a comforting and indulgent meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup, then you’re in for a treat! One-Pot Creamy Tuscan Sausage Gnocchi is a delightful combination of pillowy gnocchi, savory Italian sausage, and a luscious creamy sauce infused with the flavors of Tuscany. This dish is perfect for a cozy family dinner or when you want to impress your guests with minimal effort. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this decadent treat in just one pot!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 pound potato gnocchi
  • 3 cups fresh baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Sausage: Heat a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add the Italian sausage, breaking it into crumbles with a wooden spoon as it cooks. Sear the sausage until it’s browned and cooked through, then transfer it to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess grease.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add a tablespoon of olive oil and the chopped onion. Saute the onion until it becomes translucent, then add the minced garlic, Italian seasoning, and red pepper flakes if you’re using them. Stir the mixture for a minute or two until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Add the Sun-Dried Tomatoes and Gnocchi: Toss the sun-dried tomatoes into the skillet and stir them with the aromatics. Then, pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  4. Cook the Gnocchi: Once the liquid is simmering, add the potato gnocchi to the skillet. Give everything a gentle stir to ensure the gnocchi is submerged in the creamy sauce. Let the gnocchi simmer in the sauce, stirring occasionally, until they are cooked through and the sauce has thickened, about 5-7 minutes.

  5. Finish with Spinach and Cheese: When the gnocchi are tender, add the fresh baby spinach to the skillet. Toss it with the gnocchi and let it wilt in the creamy sauce. Then, sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the top and stir it in until it’s melted and the sauce is rich and creamy. Season the dish with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Garnish and Serve: To finish the dish, sprinkle some fresh basil over the top for a burst of herbal freshness. Spoon the creamy Tuscan sausage gnocchi into bowls and serve immediately.

Now that you’ve mastered the art of creating One-Pot Creamy Tuscan Sausage Gnocchi, it’s time to savor the fruits of your labor. The pillowy gnocchi, savory sausage, and creamy sauce infused with the flavors of Tuscany come together to create a truly decadent treat that will leave you and your loved ones coming back for more. Enjoy this dish with a glass of your favorite wine and revel in the satisfaction of creating a restaurant-worthy meal in the comfort of your own kitchen.

So, whether it’s a busy weeknight or a special occasion, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Embrace the simplicity and richness of flavors that One-Pot Creamy Tuscan Sausage Gnocchi has to offer, and delight in the joy of sharing a memorable meal with those you cherish. Cheers to good food, good company, and the convenience of one-pot wonders!
