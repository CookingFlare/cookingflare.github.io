Italian Chicken Marsala Recipe: A Creamy and Flavorful Dish


If you’re craving a taste of Italy right in your own kitchen, look no further than this delightful Italian Chicken Marsala recipe. This dish is a perfect blend of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and a rich, creamy Marsala wine sauce that will transport your taste buds to the heart of Tuscany. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just want to elevate your weeknight dinner, this recipe is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the art of creating this creamy and flavorful masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 oz cremini or baby bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 cup Marsala wine
  • 3/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Begin by pounding the chicken breasts to an even thickness, then season them generously with salt and black pepper. Dredge the chicken in flour, shaking off any excess.

  2. Sear the Chicken: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter with 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side until they are golden brown and cooked through. Once done, transfer the chicken to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm.

  3. Saute the Mushrooms: In the same skillet, melt the remaining butter and olive oil. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they are golden and all the liquid has evaporated, about 5-6 minutes.

  4. Create the Marsala Sauce: Pour the Marsala wine into the skillet, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Let the wine simmer and reduce by half, then add the chicken broth. Allow the sauce to simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

  5. Finish with Cream: Stir in the heavy cream and let the sauce simmer for another 2-3 minutes until it thickens to your desired consistency. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Return the chicken to the skillet, spoon the creamy Marsala sauce over the chicken, and let it simmer for a couple of minutes to heat through. Sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving.

Tips for Success

  • To ensure the chicken cooks evenly, use a meat mallet to pound the chicken breasts to an even thickness.
  • For an extra depth of flavor, consider adding a splash of chicken broth or water to the pan while cooking the chicken to create steam and prevent it from drying out.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, mix a small amount of cornstarch with water and stir it into the sauce during the final simmer.

Final Thoughts

Italian Chicken Marsala is a timeless classic that embodies the essence of Italian cuisine. The tender chicken, savory mushrooms, and luscious Marsala sauce come together to create a dish that’s both comforting and elegant. Whether you’re serving it with pasta, mashed potatoes, or crusty bread to soak up every last bit of the sauce, this recipe is sure to become a beloved favorite in your home. So, gather your ingredients, pour a glass of wine, and let the aromas of Italy fill your kitchen as you embark on a culinary journey with this creamy and flavorful delight.

Buon appetito!
