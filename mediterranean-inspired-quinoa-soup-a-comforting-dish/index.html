Mediterranean-Inspired Quinoa Soup: A Comforting Dish


Warm your soul with this delicious and hearty Mediterranean-inspired quinoa soup. Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavors, this comforting dish is perfect for those chilly evenings or when you simply crave a nourishing bowl of goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the quinoa: Rinse the quinoa under cold water until the water runs clear. In a medium saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the quinoa and reduce heat to low. Simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed. Set aside.

  2. Prepare the soup: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the vegetables and herbs: Add the carrots, celery, red bell pepper, dried oregano, dried basil, and dried thyme to the pot. Stir well to combine and cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Simmer: Pour in the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and water. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

  5. Add the quinoa and spinach: Stir in the cooked quinoa and chopped spinach into the pot. Cook for an additional 5 minutes to allow the flavors to blend and the spinach to wilt slightly.

  6. Season to taste: Season the soup with salt and pepper according to your preference. Remember to taste as you go and adjust the seasoning accordingly.

  7. Serve and enjoy: Ladle the Mediterranean-inspired quinoa soup into bowls and serve hot. You can garnish it with a sprinkle of fresh herbs, a drizzle of olive oil, or a squeeze of lemon for an extra burst of flavor.

Why choose Mediterranean-Inspired Quinoa Soup?

  • Healthy and nutritious: Quinoa is a fantastic source of protein and fiber, making this soup a great option for those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet. The Mediterranean-inspired flavors provide a wide array of antioxidants and vitamins.

  • Easy to make: This soup comes together effortlessly with simple ingredients and basic cooking techniques. It’s perfect for both beginner and experienced cooks alike.

  • Versatile: Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking. You can add your favorite vegetables, herbs, or even swap quinoa for another grain like barley or farro.

  • Satisfying and comforting: The combination of flavors in this soup creates a hearty and comforting dish that will warm you from the inside out. It’s the perfect meal to cozy up with on a cold day.

  • Perfect for meal prep: This soup can be made in advance and stored in the fridge for up to 4 days. It reheats beautifully, making it an ideal option for busy weekdays or for those who enjoy prepping meals ahead of time.

So why not give this Mediterranean-inspired quinoa soup a try? It’s a bowl of comfort that will transport you to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean with every spoonful. Enjoy the nourishing flavors and embrace the warmth it brings to your table. Bon appétit!
