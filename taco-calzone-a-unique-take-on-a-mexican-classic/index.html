Taco Calzone: A Unique Take on a Mexican Classic


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine and Italian flavors, then you’re in for a treat with this unique recipe for Taco Calzone. This fusion dish combines the best of both worlds, bringing together the bold and spicy flavors of a taco with the comforting and cheesy goodness of a calzone. Whether you’re looking to impress your family with a creative dinner idea or simply craving a new culinary adventure, this Taco Calzone recipe is sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

For the Filling:

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives

For the Toppings:

  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced avocado
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar. In a separate small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour mixture and stir until a dough forms. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or turkey over medium heat until browned. Add the diced onion and garlic, and cook until softened. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let the mixture cool slightly.

  3. Assemble the Calzone:

    • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Punch down the risen dough and divide it into 4 equal portions. Roll each portion into a 8-inch circle on a floured surface. Spoon the meat mixture onto one half of each dough circle, leaving a border around the edge. Sprinkle the cheddar and mozzarella cheeses over the meat, then top with diced tomatoes, cilantro, and black olives. Fold the other half of the dough over the filling and crimp the edges to seal.

  4. Bake and Serve:

    • Place the calzones on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown. Serve the Taco Calzones hot, topped with shredded lettuce, diced avocado, sour cream, and salsa.

Conclusion

With its delicious combination of Mexican and Italian flavors, Taco Calzone is a fun and inventive twist on traditional calzones and tacos. This recipe allows for creativity and personalization, making it a perfect choice for a family dinner or a casual get-together with friends. The next time you’re craving the flavors of both Mexico and Italy, give this Taco Calzone recipe a try and savor the best of both worlds in one delightful meal. Enjoy!
