Healthy Frittata Recipes Bursting with Veggies


Frittatas are a versatile and delicious dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or even dinner. Packed with protein, vitamins, and minerals, they are the perfect way to incorporate a variety of vegetables into your diet. In this blog post, we will explore some healthy frittata recipes that are bursting with veggies. Whether you are a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, these recipes are easy to follow and will leave you wanting more.

Recipe 1: Spinach and Mushroom Frittata

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a little bit of olive oil.
  4. Add the onions and bell peppers to the skillet and sauté until they become tender.
  5. Add the mushrooms and spinach to the skillet and cook until the spinach wilts.
  6. Pour the beaten eggs over the vegetables in the skillet and stir gently to distribute the ingredients evenly.
  7. Let the frittata cook for a few minutes, until the edges start to set.
  8. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on top (if using) and transfer the skillet to the preheated oven.
  9. Bake for about 15-20 minutes or until the frittata is set and slightly golden on top.
  10. Remove from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Recipe 2: Mediterranean Frittata

Ingredients:

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs until well combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a little bit of olive oil.
  4. Add the red onion to the skillet and sauté until it becomes translucent.
  5. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook until they start to soften.
  6. Stir in the black olives and cook for another minute.
  7. Pour the beaten eggs over the vegetables in the skillet and gently stir to combine.
  8. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and fresh basil on top.
  9. Let the frittata cook for a few minutes, until the edges start to set.
  10. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes or until the frittata is set in the center.
  11. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Recipe 3: Zucchini and Red Pepper Frittata

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a little bit of olive oil.
  4. Add the onions and sauté until they become translucent.
  5. Add the zucchini and red bell pepper to the skillet and cook until they start to soften.
  6. Pour the beaten eggs over the vegetables in the skillet and gently stir to combine.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese and fresh parsley on top.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Let the frittata cook for a few minutes, until the edges start to set.
  10. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes or until the frittata is set and slightly golden on top.
  11. Remove from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

These healthy frittata recipes bursting with veggies are not only delicious but also a fantastic way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. Feel free to experiment with different combinations of vegetables and herbs to create your own unique frittata creations. Enjoy these nutritious and flavorful dishes for any meal of the day!

Note: Remember to be mindful of your dietary restrictions and consult with a healthcare professional if needed.
