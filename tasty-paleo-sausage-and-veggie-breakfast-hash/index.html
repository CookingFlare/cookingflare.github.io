Tasty Paleo Sausage and Veggie Breakfast Hash


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy breakfast option that will keep you energized throughout the day? Look no further than this Tasty Paleo Sausage and Veggie Breakfast Hash recipe! Packed with flavorful ingredients and easy to make, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Plus, it’s paleo-friendly, making it perfect for those following a grain-free and dairy-free lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of paleo-friendly sausage, casings removed
  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced sweet potato and cook until it starts to soften, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the diced onion, bell peppers, and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender.

  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the sausage to the other side. Cook the sausage, breaking it up with a spoon, until it is browned and cooked through.

  4. Combine the sausage with the vegetables in the skillet. Sprinkle the dried thyme, salt, and pepper over the mixture. Stir to combine everything evenly.

  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the hash cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the hash with fresh chopped parsley.

  7. Serve the Tasty Paleo Sausage and Veggie Breakfast Hash hot and enjoy!

Why Choose Paleo?

The paleo diet focuses on consuming whole, unprocessed foods that were available to our ancestors. By eliminating grains, dairy, and processed sugars, this diet promotes better digestion, increased energy levels, and improved overall health. This Tasty Paleo Sausage and Veggie Breakfast Hash is a perfect example of a satisfying and nutritious paleo meal.

Health Benefits of the Ingredients

  • Sweet Potato: Packed with fiber, vitamins, and minerals, sweet potatoes are a nutrient-dense root vegetable that provide sustained energy and support gut health.

  • Bell Peppers: These colorful vegetables are rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber, promoting healthy digestion and bolstering the immune system.

  • Onion and Garlic: Both onions and garlic contain compounds that have been linked to numerous health benefits, including reducing inflammation and supporting heart health.

  • Paleo-friendly Sausage: When choosing sausage for this recipe, opt for a paleo-friendly option that is free of additives, preservatives, and artificial ingredients.

Final Thoughts

This Tasty Paleo Sausage and Veggie Breakfast Hash is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed any time of the day. It’s a fantastic option for breakfast or brunch, but it can also be served as a quick and satisfying dinner. The combination of flavors from the sausage and vegetables creates a hearty and delicious meal that will keep you feeling full and energized.

Give this recipe a try and savor the flavors of a healthy and tasty paleo breakfast hash. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
