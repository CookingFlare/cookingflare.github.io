Decadent Peanut Butter Cupcakes: A Must-Try for Peanut Butter Lovers


If you’re a peanut butter lover like me, then get ready to indulge in the most decadent treat you’ve ever tasted – Peanut Butter Cupcakes! These moist and fluffy cupcakes are infused with the rich, nutty flavor of peanut butter and topped with a creamy peanut butter frosting. Every bite is a heavenly explosion of peanut butter goodness that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To make these irresistible Peanut Butter Cupcakes, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Now that we have our ingredients ready, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of making these mouthwatering Peanut Butter Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Make sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl to incorporate all the ingredients.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  5. In a microwave-safe bowl, heat the milk for about 30 seconds until warm. Stir in the peanut butter until smooth and well combined.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter and sugar mixture, alternating with the peanut butter and milk mixture. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing well after each addition.

  7. Stir in the vanilla extract and mix until the batter is smooth and well combined.

  8. Spoon the batter into the prepared cupcake liners, filling each one about two-thirds full.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  10. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

Peanut Butter Frosting

No Peanut Butter Cupcake is complete without a luscious peanut butter frosting. Here’s how to make it:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  1. In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and peanut butter until smooth and creamy.

  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, mixing well after each addition.

  3. Add the vanilla extract and milk, starting with 2 tablespoons. Mix until the frosting is light and fluffy. Add more milk if necessary to achieve the desired consistency.

  4. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, generously frost each one with the peanut butter frosting using a piping bag or a spatula.

Enjoy the Decadence!

Now that you’ve completed all the steps, it’s time to sit back, relax, and savor the decadent delight that is the Peanut Butter Cupcake. The rich and creamy peanut butter flavor combined with the moist and fluffy texture of the cupcake is a match made in dessert heaven. Whether you’re treating yourself or sharing with friends and family, these cupcakes are sure to impress and satisfy even the most devoted peanut butter lovers.

So what are you waiting for? Head to your kitchen, gather your ingredients, and prepare yourself for a taste sensation like no other. These Decadent Peanut Butter Cupcakes are a must-try for all peanut butter enthusiasts out there. Trust me, once you try them, you’ll be hooked!

Note: This recipe makes approximately 12 cupcakes. Double the ingredients if you want to make a larger batch.
