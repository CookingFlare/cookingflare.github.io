How to Make Creamy Pistachio Ice Cream at Home


Are you ready to indulge in the rich, creamy goodness of homemade pistachio ice cream? Look no further because I’ve got a delightful recipe that will have you savoring every spoonful. This recipe combines the nutty flavor of pistachios with the luscious creaminess of ice cream, creating a treat that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shelled pistachios
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Pistachios

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Spread the shelled pistachios on a baking sheet and toast them in the oven for about 8-10 minutes until they become fragrant. Let them cool completely, then roughly chop the pistachios and set them aside.

Step 2: Infusing the Milk

In a saucepan, combine the whole milk and 1 cup of heavy cream. Heat the mixture over medium heat until it just begins to simmer. Add the toasted pistachios and stir gently. Remove the saucepan from the heat, cover it, and let the mixture steep for 1 hour to infuse the milk with the nutty flavor of the pistachios.

Step 3: Creating the Ice Cream Base

After the hour has passed, strain the milk and cream mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean saucepan. Press down on the pistachios to extract as much flavor as possible. Discard the pistachios.

In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and granulated sugar until the mixture is pale and slightly thick. Gradually pour the infused milk and cream mixture into the egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly. Return the combined mixture to the saucepan.

Step 4: Cooking the Custard

Cook the custard over low heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. Be careful not to let the mixture boil. Once thickened, remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the remaining heavy cream, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Allow the custard to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate it for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.

Step 5: Churning the Ice Cream

Once the custard is thoroughly chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. When the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, gently fold in the chopped pistachios.

Step 6: Freezing the Ice Cream

Transfer the churned ice cream to a freezer-safe container, smooth the top, and cover it with parchment paper directly touching the surface to prevent ice crystals from forming. Seal the container with a lid and freeze the pistachio ice cream for at least 4-6 hours, or until firm.

Serving Suggestion

Scoop the creamy pistachio ice cream into bowls or cones and garnish with a sprinkle of chopped pistachios for an extra crunch. Enjoy this delectable treat on a hot summer day or as a delightful dessert after a satisfying meal.

There you have it – a simple yet incredibly satisfying recipe for homemade creamy pistachio ice cream. With its rich, nutty flavor and velvety texture, this delightful treat is sure to impress your family and friends. So, go ahead and treat yourself to a scoop of this luscious pistachio ice cream – you won’t be disappointed!
