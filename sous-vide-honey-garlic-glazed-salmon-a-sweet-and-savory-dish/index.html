Sous Vide Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon: A Sweet and Savory Dish


Salmon is a versatile and delicious fish that can be prepared in countless ways. One method that stands out for its ability to create perfectly cooked, tender, and flavorful salmon is sous vide. By cooking the salmon in a water bath at a precise temperature, you can achieve amazing results every time. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to prepare a mouthwatering honey garlic glazed salmon using the sous vide technique.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets, skin on
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 130°F (54°C). This temperature will ensure a perfectly cooked salmon with a moist and tender texture.

  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper on both sides. Place each fillet in a separate vacuum-sealed bag, ensuring there is enough space for the marinade.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the minced garlic, honey, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, and olive oil until well combined. This flavorful glaze will add a perfect balance of sweetness and savory notes to the salmon.

  4. Pour the honey garlic glaze into each bag, making sure to coat the salmon evenly. Seal the bags using a vacuum sealer or the water displacement method, ensuring that all the air is removed.

  5. Gently place the sealed bags into the preheated water bath and cook for 45 minutes to 1 hour. This cooking time will allow the salmon to reach the desired internal temperature while retaining its moisture and tenderness.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully remove the bags from the water bath and transfer the salmon fillets to a plate. Pat them dry with a paper towel to remove any excess moisture.

  7. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Place the salmon fillets, skin side down, and sear for 1-2 minutes until the skin is crispy and golden brown. This step adds a delightful texture to the dish.

  8. Serve the sous vide honey garlic glazed salmon immediately, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. The salmon will be flaky, succulent, and infused with the delicious flavors of the glaze.

Serving Suggestions

This sous vide honey garlic glazed salmon pairs well with a variety of side dishes. Here are a few options to consider:

  • Steamed vegetables: Serve the salmon alongside a medley of steamed broccoli, carrots, and asparagus for a nutritious and colorful plate.

  • Lemon herb rice: Prepare a fragrant rice dish by cooking basmati rice with lemon zest, fresh herbs like dill or parsley, and a squeeze of lemon juice.

  • Roasted potatoes: Crispy roasted potatoes seasoned with salt, pepper, and herbs make a delightful accompaniment to the salmon.

  • Mixed greens salad: Create a refreshing salad with a mix of baby greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and a light vinaigrette dressing.

Final Thoughts

Sous vide cooking provides an incredible way to elevate the flavors and textures of salmon. With this recipe for sous vide honey garlic glazed salmon, you can enjoy a dish that combines the sweetness of honey, the savory notes of garlic, and the natural richness of salmon. Impress your family and friends with this restaurant-quality meal that is both easy to prepare and bursting with flavor. Give it a try and experience the magic of sous vide cooking!
