Once the butter melts, pour in the egg mixture and let it sit for a few seconds before gently stirring with a spatula. When the eggs are almost set, sprinkle in some shredded cheese, chopped tomatoes, and a handful of spinach. Continue to cook until the cheese melts and the spinach wilts.
