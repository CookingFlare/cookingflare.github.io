Delicious and Nutritious Chinese Steamed Chicken with Ginger and Green Onion

If you’re looking for a healthy, flavorful, and easy-to-make dish, Chinese Steamed Chicken with Ginger and Green Onion is a fantastic choice. This traditional Chinese recipe highlights the delicate flavors of ginger and green onion while keeping the chicken tender and succulent. Not only is it delicious, but it’s also a nutritious option for anyone seeking a wholesome meal. Ingredients 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 pounds) 3-4 inches of fresh ginger, peeled and sliced 4-5 green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces 2 tablespoons of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce 1 tablespoon of sesame oil 1 tablespoon of Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry) 1 teaspoon of sugar Salt, to taste White pepper, to taste 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil Instructions Prep the Chicken Start by rinsing the chicken under cold water and patting it dry with paper towels. Carefully remove any excess fat and innards from the cavity. Place the chicken on a deep plate or a shallow bowl that will fit inside your steamer. Season the Chicken In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, Shaoxing wine, sugar, salt, and white pepper. Rub this seasoning mixture all over the chicken, ensuring that it’s evenly coated inside and out. Place some of the ginger slices and green onion pieces inside the chicken cavity for added flavor. Prepare the Steamer Fill a wok or a large pot with water, making sure the water level is below the steaming rack. Bring the water to a boil over high heat. Steam the Chicken Once the water is boiling, carefully place the plate with the chicken into the steamer. Cover the steamer with a lid and steam the chicken over high heat for about 40-45 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked through. Check the chicken by inserting a knife into the thickest part of the meat. If the juices run clear, the chicken is done. Finish and Serve When the chicken is cooked, carefully remove it from the steamer. Heat the vegetable oil until it’s almost smoking, then pour it over the chicken to give it a glistening finish. Garnish with the remaining ginger slices and green onion pieces. Enjoy the Delightful Dish Once the chicken has been garnished, it’s ready to be served. This dish pairs wonderfully with steamed rice and a side of steamed vegetables. The tender and flavorful meat, infused with the aromatic ginger and green onion, makes for a delightful dining experience. Plus, the simplicity of the cooking method allows the natural flavors of the ingredients to shine through, creating a truly satisfying meal. ...
