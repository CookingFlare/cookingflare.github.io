Brunch Cocktails for Every Taste: Sweet, Sour, and Everything In Between

The Sweet Side of Brunch Cocktails

1. Peach Bellini

A classic choice for those who adore fruity flavors, the Peach Bellini is refreshing and effervescent.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe peaches, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 bottle of Prosecco (chilled)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, puree the peaches with sugar until smooth.
  2. Strain the mixture into a champagne flute, filling it about one-third full.
  3. Slowly top off with chilled Prosecco.
  4. Garnish with a mint leaf and serve immediately.

2. Strawberry Mimosa

A twist on the traditional mimosa, the Strawberry Mimosa adds a delightful sweetness that is perfect for brunch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 cup orange juice (freshly squeezed is best)
  • 1 bottle of Champagne or sparkling wine (chilled)
  • Strawberry slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Muddle the strawberries in a bowl until they form a chunky puree.
  2. In a pitcher, combine the strawberry puree with orange juice.
  3. Pour the mixture into flutes, filling them halfway.
  4. Top with Champagne and garnish with fresh strawberry slices.

3. Blueberry Lavender Fizz

For those who enjoy floral notes, this cocktail is both sweet and aromatic.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon culinary lavender
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 cup gin
  • 1 bottle tonic water (chilled)
  • Lemon slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine blueberries, lavender, and honey with a splash of water. Simmer until blueberries burst and the mixture thickens.
  2. Strain the mixture through a sieve to extract the syrup, discarding the solids.
  3. In a glass filled with ice, add 2 ounces of the blueberry lavender syrup and top with gin and tonic water.
  4. Stir gently and garnish with lemon slices.

The Sour Selections

4. Grapefruit Paloma

This cocktail offers a zesty and invigorating twist, perfect for those who love a tangy drink.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces tequila
  • 1 ounce fresh grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce agave syrup
  • Club soda
  • Salt for rimming the glass (optional)
  • Grapefruit wedge for garnish

Instructions:

  1. If desired, rim the glass with salt.
  2. In a shaker, combine tequila, grapefruit juice, lime juice, and agave syrup with ice. Shake well.
  3. Strain into a glass filled with ice and top with club soda.
  4. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge.

5. Cucumber Mint Cooler

A refreshing option that balances sour and herbal flavors, the Cucumber Mint Cooler is a must-try.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 ounce fresh lime juice
  • 1 ounce simple syrup
  • 2 ounces vodka
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Club soda

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, muddle cucumber slices and mint leaves.
  2. Add lime juice, simple syrup, and vodka. Fill with ice and shake well.
  3. Strain into a glass filled with ice and top with club soda.
  4. Garnish with cucumber slices and mint sprigs.

6. Spicy Bloody Mary

The Bloody Mary is a brunch staple that combines savory and sour elements, making it a great choice for those who enjoy bold flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 4 ounces tomato juice
  • 1/2 ounce lemon juice
  • 2 dashes Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 dashes hot sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Celery stick and lemon wedge for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Fill with ice and shake gently.
  3. Strain into a glass filled with ice and garnish with a celery stick and lemon wedge.

The In-Between Wonders

7. Hibiscus Spritzer

This cocktail straddles the line between sweet and sour, offering a beautiful color and a unique flavor profile.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried hibiscus flowers
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 bottle sparkling water (chilled)
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add dried hibiscus flowers and sugar, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Strain the mixture and let it cool.
  3. In a glass filled with ice, pour 2 ounces of the hibiscus syrup and top with sparkling water.
  4. Garnish with lime wedges.

9. Maple Bourbon Sour

A rich and complex drink, the Maple Bourbon Sour combines the warmth of bourbon with the sweetness of maple syrup.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces bourbon
  • 1 ounce fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 ounce maple syrup
  • Dash of Angostura bitters
  • Lemon twist for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker filled with ice, combine bourbon, lemon juice, maple syrup, and bitters.
  2. Shake well and strain into a rocks glass filled with ice.
  3. Garnish with a lemon twist.
