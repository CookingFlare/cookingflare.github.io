Aphrodisiac Aphrodisiac Valentine's Day Dinner: Oyster Recipes

4 min · 715 words

Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate this romantic occasion than with a sumptuous and sensual dinner? Oysters, known for their aphrodisiac properties, have long been associated with love and passion. Their luxurious, briny flavor and sensual texture make them the perfect ingredient for a Valentine’s Day feast. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of tantalizing oyster recipes that are sure to set the mood for a memorable evening with your loved one.

Oyster Rockefeller

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh oysters
  • 1/2 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. In a skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the spinach, parsley, green onions, celery, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in the breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, until the mixture is well combined.
  4. Shuck the oysters and place them on a baking sheet. Spoon the vegetable mixture over each oyster.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the oysters are cooked through and the topping is golden brown.
  6. Serve the Oyster Rockefeller with lemon wedges on the side.

Oyster Po’ Boy Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 24 fresh oysters
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup fine breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 soft sandwich rolls
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Sliced tomato
  • Remoulade sauce

Instructions

  1. In a shallow dish, combine the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Dredge the oysters in the seasoned flour, then dip them in the beaten eggs, and finally coat them in the breadcrumbs.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the oysters for 2-3 minutes on each side, until they are golden brown and crispy.
  4. Split the sandwich rolls and lightly toast them. Spread a generous amount of remoulade sauce on each roll.
  5. Arrange the fried oysters on the rolls, and top with shredded lettuce and sliced tomato.
  6. Serve the Oyster Po’ Boy Sandwich with extra remoulade sauce for dipping.

Champagne Mignonette Oysters

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh oysters
  • 1/4 cup champagne vinegar
  • 1 shallot, finely minced
  • 1 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper
  • Fresh chives, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the champagne vinegar, minced shallot, and black pepper.
  2. Shuck the oysters and arrange them on a bed of crushed ice.
  3. Spoon a small amount of the champagne mignonette over each oyster.
  4. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh chives.
  5. Serve the Champagne Mignonette Oysters immediately for a refreshing and elegant treat.

Conclusion

These delightful oyster recipes are the perfect way to add a touch of romance and sensuality to your Valentine’s Day dinner. Whether you choose to indulge in the rich and indulgent Oyster Rockefeller, the crispy and flavorful Oyster Po’ Boy Sandwich, or the elegant Champagne Mignonette Oysters, you’re sure to create a memorable dining experience that will set the stage for an unforgettable evening with your beloved. Pair these delectable dishes with a glass of champagne or a velvety red wine, and let the magic of oysters work its aphrodisiac charm. Cheers to love, romance, and the pleasures of a shared meal on this special day. Happy Valentine’s Day!
