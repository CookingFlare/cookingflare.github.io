Vegan Blueberry Pancakes: Fluffy and Bursting with Berries


Who doesn’t love waking up to a stack of fluffy, mouthwatering pancakes to start the day? And what’s even better is when those pancakes are not only delicious but also completely vegan! In this recipe, I’ll show you how to make the most scrumptious Vegan Blueberry Pancakes that are not only fluffy and bursting with berries but also incredibly easy to whip up. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups almond milk (or any plant-based milk of your choice)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Batter: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt until well combined.
  2. Add Wet Ingredients: Pour in the almond milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until the batter is smooth and free of lumps.
  3. Fold in the Blueberries: Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to overmix the batter as this can result in dense pancakes.
  4. Heat the Pan: Place a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil or cooking spray.
  5. Cook the Pancakes: Once the pan is hot, pour 1/4 cup of the batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake.
  6. Flip and Cook: Carefully flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the pancakes to a plate and serve them warm, topped with a drizzle of maple syrup and extra blueberries if desired.

Tips for Perfect Pancakes:

  • Don’t Overmix: Overmixing the batter can result in tough, dense pancakes. It’s okay if there are a few lumps in the batter.
  • Use Fresh Blueberries: While frozen blueberries can be used, fresh blueberries give the pancakes a burst of juicy flavor.
  • Adjust Heat as Needed: If the pancakes are browning too quickly, lower the heat to ensure they cook through evenly.

Why Choose Vegan Blueberry Pancakes?

Not only are these pancakes a delightful and satisfying breakfast option, but they are also a great way to incorporate more plant-based recipes into your diet. Vegan pancakes are not only kinder to the environment and animal-friendly, but they can also be just as delicious and indulgent as their non-vegan counterparts. By opting for vegan blueberry pancakes, you’re not only treating your taste buds but also making a positive impact on the planet.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Blueberry Pancakes are the perfect combination of fluffy, light, and bursting with juicy berries. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to add some plant-based goodness to your breakfast routine, this recipe is sure to become a go-to favorite. So, next time you’re craving a decadent breakfast treat, whip up a batch of these delightful pancakes and savor every fluffy, berry-filled bite!
