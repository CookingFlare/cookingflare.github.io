Coconut Cream Pie with a Graham Cracker Crust Recipe: A Creamy and Delicious Vegan Dessert


Are you craving a luscious and indulgent dessert that is not only vegan but also incredibly creamy and delicious? Look no further than this Coconut Cream Pie with a Graham Cracker Crust recipe! It’s the perfect dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your friends and family with its rich flavors and velvety texture.

Ingredients

For the Graham Cracker Crust:

  • 2 cups of crushed vegan graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup of melted coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • A pinch of salt

For the Coconut Cream Filling:

  • 1 can (13.5 oz) of full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

For the Whipped Topping:

  • 1 can (13.5 oz) of full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Graham Cracker Crust

  1. In a bowl, combine the crushed vegan graham crackers, melted coconut oil, granulated sugar, and a pinch of salt. Mix until well combined and the mixture resembles wet sand.

  2. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie dish, forming an even layer. Make sure to press the crust firmly to ensure it holds together.

  3. Place the crust in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to firm up.

Step 2: Making the Coconut Cream Filling

  1. In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, coconut cream, granulated sugar, cornstarch, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Whisk until the mixture is smooth and well combined.

  2. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a gentle boil. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  3. Once the mixture has thickened, remove it from heat and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, pour the filling into the prepared graham cracker crust.

  4. Smooth the top of the filling using a spatula and refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours, or until set.

Step 3: Whipping the Coconut Topping

  1. Open the can of refrigerated coconut milk and carefully scoop out the solidified coconut cream that has separated at the top. Place it in a mixing bowl, leaving behind any liquid.

  2. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the coconut cream. Using an electric mixer, beat the mixture on high speed until it becomes light and fluffy, resembling whipped cream.

Step 4: Assembling the Coconut Cream Pie

  1. Remove the chilled coconut cream pie from the refrigerator and spread the whipped coconut topping over the top of the pie, creating a beautiful and creamy layer.

  2. For an extra touch, you can garnish the pie with toasted coconut flakes or a sprinkle of cocoa powder.

  3. Slice and serve the Coconut Cream Pie with a Graham Cracker Crust, and enjoy the heavenly combination of flavors and textures.

Conclusion

This Coconut Cream Pie with a Graham Cracker Crust recipe is a must-try for any dessert lover, vegan or not. Its creamy coconut filling, combined with the crunchy graham cracker crust and fluffy coconut topping, creates a dessert experience that is truly unforgettable. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself, this recipe is guaranteed to impress. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to indulge in this delicious vegan dessert!
