Sizzling Mongolian Beef Stir Fry


If you’re a fan of Asian cuisine and enjoy the delightful combination of tender beef slices, savory sauce, and crisp vegetables, then this Mongolian Beef Stir Fry recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. The key to achieving that perfect balance of flavors and textures lies in the art of stir-frying, where ingredients are cooked quickly over high heat to seal in their natural goodness. Let’s dive into the steps of creating this sizzling dish that will tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Steamed rice, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Beef:

    • Start by slicing the flank steak thinly against the grain to ensure tenderness.

  2. Create the Sauce:

    • In a bowl, mix together soy sauce, water, brown sugar, and red pepper flakes. This will be the flavorful base of your dish.

  3. Coat the Beef:

    • Toss the sliced beef with cornstarch until evenly coated. This will help thicken the sauce later on.

  4. Stir-Fry:

    • Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over high heat. Add the garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
    • Add the beef slices to the pan in a single layer. Let them sear without stirring for about a minute, then stir-fry until browned but still pink inside.
    • Pour in the sauce mixture and stir to combine. Let it simmer until the sauce thickens and coats the beef beautifully.

  5. Finish and Serve:

    • Toss in the green onions for a pop of freshness and color.
    • Serve your sizzling Mongolian Beef Stir Fry over steamed rice and garnish with additional green onions if desired.

Tips for Success:

  • Hot Wok, Quick Cook: Ensure your wok or pan is hot before adding the ingredients for that signature stir-fry flavor.
  • Slice it Right: Cutting the beef thinly and against the grain guarantees a tender bite every time.
  • Customize to Taste: Feel free to adjust the level of spice or sweetness in the sauce to suit your preferences.

Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this Mongolian Beef Stir Fry recipe promises to deliver a burst of Asian-inspired flavors with every bite. Embrace the sizzle of the wok and the aroma of garlic and ginger as you create this delicious dish in your own kitchen. Enjoy the magic of stir-frying and treat yourself to a culinary adventure that celebrates the vibrant tastes of the East. Happy cooking!

Remember, the secret to a successful stir fry lies in the balance of flavors and textures, so don’t be afraid to get creative and make this recipe your own. Share it with friends and family to spread the joy of good food and great company. Cheers to many more sizzling stir-fry adventures in your culinary journey!
