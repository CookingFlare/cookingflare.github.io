Savory Slow Cooker Chicken and Sweet Potato Curry Recipe


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful dish that practically cooks itself, you’re in the right place! This savory slow cooker chicken and sweet potato curry recipe is the perfect blend of tender chicken, hearty sweet potatoes, and aromatic spices. The beauty of using a slow cooker for this dish is that it allows the flavors to meld together beautifully while you go about your day. By the time you’re ready to eat, you’ll be greeted with a fragrant, mouthwatering curry that’s sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan bread for serving

Instructions

  1. In the slow cooker, combine the chicken, sweet potatoes, onion, and garlic.

  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the coconut milk, diced tomatoes, chicken broth, curry powder, turmeric, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper.

  3. Pour the coconut milk mixture over the chicken and sweet potatoes in the slow cooker. Stir to ensure everything is well coated with the flavorful sauce.

  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, or until the chicken is tender and the sweet potatoes are cooked through.

  5. Once the cooking time is up, give the curry a gentle stir, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  6. Serve the savory slow cooker chicken and sweet potato curry over a bed of fluffy rice or with warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a diced jalapeño or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes to the curry mixture.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a splash of cold water and stir it into the curry during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  • Feel free to customize the veggies in this curry to your liking. Bell peppers, green beans, or spinach would all make excellent additions.

Final Thoughts

This savory slow cooker chicken and sweet potato curry is a fantastic way to enjoy a comforting and aromatic meal with minimal effort. The tender chicken, creamy sweet potatoes, and fragrant spices come together to create a dish that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this recipe is a winner. The beauty of using a slow cooker means you can simply set it and forget it, allowing you to come back to a house filled with the irresistible aroma of a home-cooked meal.

So, grab your slow cooker and give this recipe a try. It’s bound to become a beloved favorite in your culinary repertoire!
