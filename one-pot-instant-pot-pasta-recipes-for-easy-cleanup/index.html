One-Pot Instant Pot Pasta Recipes for Easy Cleanup


Cooking delicious pasta dishes doesn’t have to be a messy and time-consuming affair. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot and some clever one-pot recipes, you can whip up a flavorful meal without the hassle of multiple pots and pans. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering one-pot Instant Pot pasta recipes that not only save you time and effort but also make cleanup a breeze.

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

If you’re a fan of creamy, indulgent pasta dishes, this recipe is a must-try. Start by sautéing minced garlic and diced onions in your Instant Pot on the sauté function. Once they’re fragrant and golden, add your pasta of choice and enough vegetable or chicken broth to cover it. Close the lid, set the Instant Pot to manual mode, and cook for the recommended time for your pasta type. Once cooked, stir in grated Parmesan cheese and heavy cream to create a luscious, creamy sauce. Season with salt, pepper, and any herbs or spices you prefer. Serve piping hot, and prepare to be amazed by the burst of flavors in every bite.

Spicy Arrabbiata Pasta

If you crave a bit of heat in your pasta, this spicy arrabbiata recipe will satisfy your taste buds. Begin by browning some ground Italian sausage or ground beef in your Instant Pot using the sauté function. Once cooked, remove any excess grease and add diced onions and minced garlic. Sauté until the onions are translucent. Next, pour in a can of crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and a generous amount of red pepper flakes for that fiery kick. Stir in your pasta and enough water to cover it. Lock the lid, set the Instant Pot to manual mode, and cook for the recommended time. Once done, garnish with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese. This dish will surely leave you wanting more.

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

For seafood lovers, this lemon garlic shrimp pasta is a match made in culinary heaven. Start by sautéing minced garlic in olive oil using the sauté function of your Instant Pot. Once fragrant, add cleaned and deveined shrimp, cooking until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the Instant Pot and set them aside. In the same pot, add your pasta, chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Lock the lid, set the Instant Pot to manual mode, and cook for the recommended time. Once cooked, stir in the cooked shrimp, freshly chopped parsley, and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese. This dish is a refreshing and flavorful option for any occasion.

Cheesy Taco Pasta

If you’re craving a fusion of Mexican and Italian flavors, this cheesy taco pasta recipe is a winner. Begin by browning ground beef in your Instant Pot on the sauté function. Once cooked, drain any excess grease and add diced onions, minced garlic, and your favorite taco seasoning. Sauté until the onions are translucent and the spices are fragrant. Next, add your pasta, a can of diced tomatoes, and enough water or chicken broth to cover it. Lock the lid, set the Instant Pot to manual mode, and cook for the recommended time. Once cooked, stir in shredded cheddar cheese until it melts into a creamy, cheesy sauce. Serve with a dollop of sour cream, chopped green onions, and a sprinkle of shredded lettuce for that authentic taco experience.

In Conclusion

One-pot Instant Pot pasta recipes offer a convenient and efficient way to prepare delicious meals without the hassle of multiple pots and pans. Whether you prefer creamy, spicy, seafood, or fusion flavors, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, don’t let cleanup deter you from enjoying a homemade pasta dish. Grab your Instant Pot, gather the ingredients, and let your taste buds embark on a flavorful journey. Happy cooking!
