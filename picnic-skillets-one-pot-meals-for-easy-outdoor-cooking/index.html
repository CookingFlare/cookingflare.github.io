Picnic Skillets: One-Pot Meals for Easy Outdoor Cooking


Are you planning a picnic and looking for easy and delicious meals that require minimal preparation and cleanup? Look no further than picnic skillets! These one-pot wonders are perfect for outdoor cooking and will satisfy your hunger while keeping things simple and hassle-free. In this blog post, we will share some mouthwatering picnic skillet recipes that will elevate your outdoor dining experience.

1. Cheesy Chicken and Broccoli Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced chicken to the skillet and season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink and slightly browned.
  3. Add the broccoli florets to the skillet and cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender-crisp.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese evenly over the chicken and broccoli. Cover the skillet with a lid and let the cheese melt.
  5. Serve the cheesy chicken and broccoli skillet hot, straight from the pan. Enjoy!

2. Sausage and Vegetable Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced sausage to the skillet and cook until browned.
  3. Add the bell peppers, onion, and garlic to the skillet. Season with dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Serve the sausage and vegetable skillet hot, straight from the pan. Enjoy!

3. Mediterranean Shrimp and Rice Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque.
  4. Add the rice, chicken broth, diced tomatoes, black olives, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the shrimp and rice skillet. Cover the skillet with a lid and let the cheese melt.
  6. Serve the Mediterranean shrimp and rice skillet hot, straight from the pan. Enjoy!

Conclusion

Picnic skillets are a game-changer when it comes to outdoor cooking. These one-pot meals are not only delicious but also convenient, as they require minimal preparation and cleanup. Whether you’re grilling at a park or enjoying a day at the beach, these recipes will ensure that you have a satisfying and stress-free picnic experience. So grab your skillet, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress your fellow picnickers with these mouthwatering creations. Happy cooking and happy picnicking!
