Quick and Easy Keto Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy snack that is keto-friendly? Look no further! These Quick and Easy Keto Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries are the perfect guilt-free treat. Packed with flavor and crunch, these zucchini fries are sure to satisfy your cravings without derailing your diet. Plus, they’re a breeze to make! Let’s get started.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Wash the zucchinis thoroughly and cut them into long, thin strips, resembling the shape of fries. Make sure to remove any excess moisture from the zucchini strips by patting them dry with a paper towel.

  3. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten. In a separate bowl, combine the grated Parmesan cheese, almond flour, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to evenly distribute the seasonings.

  4. Take one zucchini strip at a time and dip it into the beaten eggs, making sure it is fully coated. Then, roll the zucchini strip in the Parmesan mixture, pressing gently to ensure the coating sticks.

  5. Place the coated zucchini strip onto the prepared baking sheet and repeat the process with the remaining zucchini strips.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until the zucchini fries are golden brown and crispy. Make sure to flip them halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  7. Once done, remove the zucchini fries from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. They are best enjoyed fresh and hot!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with different seasonings and spices to customize the flavor of your zucchini fries. Adding a pinch of cayenne pepper or smoked paprika can give them a spicy kick, while a sprinkle of dried herbs like oregano or thyme can add a Mediterranean twist.

  • For an extra crispy coating, you can place the zucchini fries on a wire rack set on top of the baking sheet. This allows air to circulate around the fries, resulting in a crunchier texture.

  • These zucchini fries are great on their own, but they also make a fantastic side dish. Serve them alongside your favorite keto-friendly dip, such as ranch dressing or marinara sauce, for a satisfying and well-rounded meal.

  • If you’re not following a keto diet, you can use regular all-purpose flour instead of almond flour. However, keep in mind that this will increase the carb content of the fries.

Final Thoughts

These Quick and Easy Keto Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries are a game-changer for anyone on a low-carb or keto diet. With their crispy exterior and tender interior, they provide the perfect balance of flavors and textures. Whether you’re looking for a delicious snack or a flavorful side dish, these zucchini fries are sure to impress. Give this recipe a try and let us know what you think. Enjoy!
