Irresistible Cinnamon Rolls for a Christmas Morning Treat


There’s something magical about waking up on Christmas morning to the aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls wafting through the house. The soft, pillowy dough, the gooey cinnamon filling, and the sweet icing on top make these treats truly irresistible. Whether you’re looking to create a traditional holiday breakfast or surprise your loved ones with a special treat, these cinnamon rolls are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk, warmed
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs

For the filling:

  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

For the icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warmed milk, melted butter, and eggs.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  6. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling by combining the brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.
  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.
  8. Spread the softened butter evenly over the dough, then sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture on top.
  9. Starting from the long edge, tightly roll up the dough into a log.
  10. Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 12 equal-sized rolls.
  11. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, cover with a kitchen towel, and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
  12. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  13. Bake the cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  14. While the cinnamon rolls are baking, prepare the icing by whisking together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  15. Once the cinnamon rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  16. Drizzle the icing over the warm cinnamon rolls.
  17. Serve the rolls while they are still warm and enjoy the heavenly combination of cinnamon, sugar, and icing.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra festive touch, add raisins, chopped nuts, or cranberries to the filling before rolling up the dough.
  • If you prefer a cream cheese icing, simply substitute the milk in the icing recipe with softened cream cheese.
  • To make the preparation easier on Christmas morning, you can prepare the dough and filling the night before and store them separately in the refrigerator. In the morning, simply roll out the dough, assemble the cinnamon rolls, and bake as directed.
  • Leftover cinnamon rolls can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. Simply reheat them in the microwave or oven before serving.

These irresistible cinnamon rolls are the perfect treat to make your Christmas morning extra special. The combination of warm, fragrant spices and sweet, gooey filling will have your loved ones begging for seconds. So go ahead, embrace the holiday spirit and indulge in the joy of homemade cinnamon rolls. Your family and friends will thank you!
