Instant Pot Recipes for the Holidays: Festive Meals Made Easy


The holiday season is a time for gathering with loved ones, sharing festive meals, and creating memories that will last a lifetime. However, preparing large holiday meals can be a daunting task, especially when you have multiple dishes to cook and limited time. Enter the Instant Pot — a magical kitchen tool that can significantly cut down on cooking time while delivering flavorful, tender meals. With the Instant Pot, you can effortlessly prepare everything from appetizers to main courses and even desserts. If you want to make your holiday cooking stress-free and enjoyable, these Instant Pot recipes will save you time, and make your festive meals a breeze.

Why Use an Instant Pot for Holiday Meals?

An Instant Pot is an all-in-one kitchen appliance that combines the functions of a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, sauté pan, and even yogurt maker. Its versatility makes it the perfect kitchen tool for preparing holiday meals quickly without sacrificing taste or quality. The pressure cooking function ensures that meats are tender and juicy, while the slow cook function allows you to prepare dishes in advance and have them ready when your guests arrive.

Here are a few reasons why the Instant Pot is a holiday game-changer:

  • Time-saving: It reduces cooking time by up to 70% compared to traditional cooking methods.
  • Hands-free cooking: Once the Instant Pot is set, it requires minimal attention, allowing you to focus on other tasks.
  • Consistent results: The pressure cooking method ensures that dishes are evenly cooked, with no need for constant stirring or monitoring.
  • Versatility: From soups and stews to roasts and desserts, the Instant Pot can handle it all.

Now, let’s explore some festive Instant Pot recipes that will make your holiday cooking a breeze.

Instant Pot Holiday Recipes

1. Instant Pot Roast Turkey

A perfectly roasted turkey is often the centerpiece of holiday meals, but cooking a large bird can take hours in the oven. The Instant Pot speeds up the process, ensuring your turkey is tender and flavorful in a fraction of the time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole turkey (3-4 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 lemon, quartered

Instructions:

  1. Prep the turkey: Rub the turkey with olive oil and season it generously with salt, pepper, thyme, and rosemary. Place the onion, garlic, and lemon inside the turkey cavity.
  2. Sauté: Set the Instant Pot to the “Sauté” function and add a bit of olive oil. Brown the turkey on all sides (about 4-5 minutes per side).
  3. Pressure cook: Add the chicken broth to the Instant Pot. Secure the lid, ensuring the valve is set to “Sealing.” Cook on “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” for 30 minutes per pound (e.g., for a 3-pound turkey, cook for 90 minutes).
  4. Release pressure: When the cooking time is up, perform a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  5. Rest and carve: Let the turkey rest for 10 minutes before carving.

This Instant Pot turkey recipe will yield a perfectly cooked bird with moist meat and crispy skin. Serve it with your favorite holiday sides for a stress-free feast.

2. Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes

Mashed potatoes are a classic holiday side dish, but boiling them on the stove and mashing them by hand can be a time-consuming process. The Instant Pot makes mashed potatoes easy by cooking them quickly and evenly.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh chives, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the potatoes: Place the potatoes in the Instant Pot with 1 cup of water. Secure the lid and set the valve to “Sealing.” Cook on “Pressure Cook” for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Release pressure: Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release.
  3. Mash the potatoes: Drain the water, then return the potatoes to the Instant Pot. Add the butter, heavy cream, salt, and pepper. Mash the potatoes until smooth and creamy.
  4. Garnish and serve: Top with fresh chives or parsley, if desired.

Instant Pot mashed potatoes are creamy, buttery, and full of flavor. They are the perfect side dish to pair with your holiday turkey or roast.

3. Instant Pot Stuffing

Stuffing is another holiday classic that can be challenging to prepare in a timely manner. The Instant Pot allows you to make delicious, flavorful stuffing without turning your kitchen into a mess.

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of French bread, cubed
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sage
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the vegetables: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” and add the butter. Once melted, sauté the onion, celery, and garlic for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  2. Add the bread: Add the cubed French bread to the Instant Pot and stir to combine with the vegetables. Season with sage, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the broth: Pour the chicken broth over the bread mixture, ensuring the bread is evenly coated.
  4. Pressure cook: Secure the lid and set the valve to “Sealing.” Cook on “Pressure Cook” for 10 minutes.
  5. Release pressure and serve: Once the cooking time is up, perform a quick release. Fluff the stuffing with a fork and garnish with fresh parsley.

This Instant Pot stuffing is full of savory flavors and has the perfect texture — it’s a holiday side dish that everyone will love.

4. Instant Pot Cranberry Sauce

No holiday meal is complete without cranberry sauce. The Instant Pot makes preparing this sweet and tangy sauce easy and quick, giving you more time to focus on other dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces fresh cranberries
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon allspice

Instructions:

  1. Combine the ingredients: Add the cranberries, orange juice, sugar, honey, cinnamon, and allspice to the Instant Pot.
  2. Pressure cook: Secure the lid and set the valve to “Sealing.” Cook on “Pressure Cook” for 5 minutes.
  3. Release pressure: Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release.
  4. Mash and serve: Stir the cranberry sauce and mash it to your desired consistency. Let it cool before serving.

This Instant Pot cranberry sauce is the perfect balance of sweet and tart, and it adds a beautiful splash of color to your holiday table.

5. Instant Pot Eggnog Cheesecake

What better way to finish your holiday feast than with a rich, creamy cheesecake? This Instant Pot eggnog cheesecake is the perfect dessert for the season, offering a festive twist on a classic favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 pounds cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup eggnog
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust: Mix the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.
  2. Make the filling: Beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add the eggs, one at a time, followed by the eggnog, vanilla extract, nutmeg, and cinnamon. Mix until fully combined.
  3. Pressure cook: Pour the cheesecake filling over the crust in the springform pan. Cover the pan with foil. Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot and place the pan on the trivet. Secure the lid and set the valve to “Sealing.” Cook on “Pressure Cook” for 35 minutes.
  4. Release pressure: Once the cooking time is up, perform a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  5. Chill and serve: Let the cheesecake cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.

This eggnog cheesecake is the perfect holiday dessert — creamy, decadent, and infused with the classic flavors of the season.

Conclusion

With the Instant Pot, you can spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying the holiday season with family and friends. These festive recipes — from turkey and mashed potatoes to stuffing, cranberry sauce, and cheesecake — are sure to impress your guests and make your holiday meal planning a breeze. The Instant Pot truly transforms holiday cooking by simplifying the process while delivering delicious results every time.

Whether you’re hosting a small gathering or a large family feast, these Instant Pot recipes will help you create memorable meals with ease. So, dust off your Instant Pot and get cooking — your holiday meal awaits!
