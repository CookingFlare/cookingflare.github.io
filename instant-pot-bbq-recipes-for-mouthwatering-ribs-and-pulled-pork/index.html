Instant Pot BBQ Recipes for Mouthwatering Ribs and Pulled Pork


If you’re a fan of tender, flavorful ribs and succulent pulled pork, then you’re in for a treat. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can create mouthwatering BBQ dishes that will have your family and friends coming back for more. In this blog post, we’ll explore two delicious recipes that are perfect for any BBQ lover – Instant Pot BBQ Ribs and Instant Pot Pulled Pork. Let’s dive into the world of savory, finger-licking goodness that will elevate your BBQ game to a whole new level.

Instant Pot BBQ Ribs

Ingredients:

  • 1 rack of pork baby back ribs
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup of chicken or beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Start by removing the membrane from the back of the ribs. This will help the flavors penetrate the meat more effectively.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Rub this seasoning all over the ribs, ensuring they are well coated.
  3. Pour the chicken or beef broth into the Instant Pot. Place the trivet inside and lay the seasoned ribs on top.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 25 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then carefully release the remaining pressure.
  6. Preheat your oven broiler. Carefully transfer the ribs to a baking sheet, brush them generously with BBQ sauce, and broil for 3-5 minutes until the sauce is caramelized and sticky.
  7. Slice the ribs and serve with your favorite sides. Enjoy the fall-off-the-bone tenderness and mouthwatering flavor!

Instant Pot Pulled Pork

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 pound pork shoulder or pork butt
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Trim excess fat from the pork shoulder and cut it into large chunks. Season the meat with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Pour the apple cider vinegar into the Instant Pot, then add the seasoned pork chunks.
  3. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 90 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then carefully release the remaining pressure.
  5. Remove the pork from the Instant Pot and shred it using two forks.
  6. Mix in the brown sugar and BBQ sauce, ensuring the pork is well coated.
  7. Serve the pulled pork on buns, sliders, or alongside your favorite BBQ sides. Get ready to savor the tender, juicy, and flavorful goodness that will have everyone asking for seconds!

With these Instant Pot BBQ recipes, you can transform humble ingredients into extraordinary dishes that will impress even the most discerning BBQ aficionados. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout, a family gathering, or simply craving some finger-licking goodness, these recipes are sure to satisfy your BBQ cravings. So, fire up your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to embark on a delicious culinary adventure that will elevate your BBQ game to new heights. Happy cooking and even happier eating!
