Grilled Fish Tacos with Mango Salsa Ingredients: 1 pound fresh white fish fillets 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder Salt and pepper to taste 2 cups shredded cabbage 1 ripe mango, diced 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 1 jalapeño, seeded and diced Juice of 2 limes Fresh cilantro, chopped Corn or flour tortillas Instructions: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Brush the fish fillets with this mixture. Grill the fish for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until it flakes easily with a fork. In a separate bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, diced mango, red onion, jalapeño, lime juice, and cilantro to make the salsa. Warm the tortillas on the grill for a few seconds on each side. Assemble the tacos by placing the grilled fish on the warm tortillas and topping with the mango salsa. These grilled fish tacos with mango salsa are a delightful combination of smoky, spicy, and sweet flavors.
