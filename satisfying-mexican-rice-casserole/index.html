Satisfying Mexican Rice Casserole


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying Mexican-inspired dish? Look no further than this mouthwatering Mexican Rice Casserole recipe! Packed with flavors, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to enjoy a comforting meal at home, this recipe is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can corn kernels, drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cook the rice according to the package instructions. Set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.

  4. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and bell peppers to the skillet. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.

  5. Stir in the black beans, corn kernels, and diced tomatoes. Season with chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  6. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread half of the cooked rice evenly on the bottom.

  7. Spoon the beef and vegetable mixture over the rice, spreading it out evenly.

  8. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheddar cheese over the beef and vegetable layer.

  9. Layer the remaining cooked rice on top, followed by the remaining shredded cheddar cheese.

  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  11. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.

  12. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  13. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.

Enjoy the Delightful Flavors

The combination of tender rice, flavorful ground beef, and vibrant vegetables makes this Mexican Rice Casserole a truly satisfying dish. The spices add a delightful kick, while the melted cheese creates a creamy and indulgent texture. The black beans and corn provide a burst of freshness, adding depth to every bite.

This casserole is not only delicious but also versatile. You can customize it to your liking by adding additional ingredients such as jalapeños for some heat or olives for a briny twist. Serve it with a side of guacamole, sour cream, or salsa for an extra burst of flavor.

Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or enjoying a cozy night in, this Mexican Rice Casserole is a fantastic choice. It’s easy to make, packed with flavor, and guaranteed to satisfy even the pickiest eaters. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a truly satisfying Mexican-inspired dish!
