Autumn Vegetable and Lentil Stew Recipe


As the crisp air settles in and the leaves turn vibrant shades of orange and gold, there’s nothing quite like the comforting embrace of a hearty stew. This Autumn Vegetable and Lentil Stew not only warms you from the inside out but also celebrates the rich bounty of the season. Packed with nourishing vegetables and protein-rich lentils, this dish is perfect for a cozy dinner or meal prep for the week ahead.

Ingredients

For the Stew

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 medium potatoes, diced (Yukon Gold or red potatoes work well)
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes, with juices
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups kale or spinach, chopped
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Optional Garnishes

  • A splash of lemon juice
  • Crusty bread or cornbread for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Lentils

Start by rinsing your lentils under cold water to remove any dirt or debris. Lentils are a fantastic source of protein and fiber, making them an excellent base for your stew.

Step 2: Sauté the Vegetables

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté for about 3-4 minutes until it becomes translucent. Stir in the minced garlic, followed by the sliced carrots and chopped celery. Cook for another 5 minutes until the vegetables soften.

Step 3: Add Remaining Vegetables

Now it’s time to add the red bell pepper, potatoes, zucchini, and butternut squash. Stir everything together and allow the mixture to cook for an additional 5 minutes. The colors of the vegetables will brighten the pot, signaling the heartiness that’s to come.

Step 4: Combine Lentils and Broth

Add the rinsed lentils to the pot, followed by the vegetable broth and the can of diced tomatoes. Stir in the dried thyme, rosemary, and paprika, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

Step 5: Simmer the Stew

Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes. This allows the flavors to meld beautifully while the lentils and vegetables cook through. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

Step 6: Add Greens

In the last 10 minutes of cooking, fold in the chopped kale or spinach. These greens will wilt nicely and add a burst of color and nutrition to your stew.

Step 7: Adjust Seasoning

After simmering, taste your stew and adjust the seasoning as needed. If you like a bit of acidity, a splash of lemon juice can brighten the flavors.

Step 8: Serve

Ladle the hearty stew into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Pair it with crusty bread or cornbread for a complete meal that captures the essence of autumn.

Tips for the Perfect Stew

Choose Seasonal Vegetables

Autumn is a wonderful time to experiment with seasonal produce. Feel free to swap in your favorites, such as parsnips, sweet potatoes, or turnips. Each adds its unique flavor and texture to the stew.

Meal Prep and Storage

This stew is perfect for meal prep. It stores well in the refrigerator for up to 5 days, and the flavors continue to develop over time. For longer storage, you can freeze it in airtight containers for up to 3 months. Just be sure to leave some space in the container, as the stew will expand when frozen.

Variations

  • Add Proteins: If you prefer a heartier meal, consider adding diced chicken or sausage to the mix. Brown the meat first, then follow the same steps for the vegetables.
  • Spice it Up: If you enjoy a little heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a diced jalapeño when sautéing the onions.

Nutritional Benefits

This Autumn Vegetable and Lentil Stew is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. Lentils provide an excellent source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential nutrients like iron and folate. The variety of vegetables adds vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that support overall health, making this stew a balanced meal option.

Conclusion

Embrace the spirit of the season with this delightful Autumn Vegetable and Lentil Stew. Its rich flavors and hearty texture make it a go-to recipe as the weather cools down. Not only is it a delicious way to use seasonal produce, but it also nourishes both body and soul. Whether you’re curling up at home or serving guests, this stew is sure to impress.

Get cozy in your kitchen this autumn, and let the comforting aromas of this stew fill your home. Enjoy the process, share with loved ones, and savor every warming spoonful. Happy cooking!
