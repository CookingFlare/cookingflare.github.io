Crispy Fried Green Tomatoes with Remoulade Sauce: A Southern-Inspired Snack


Are you looking for a delicious and unique snack that will transport you straight to the heart of the South? Look no further than Crispy Fried Green Tomatoes with Remoulade Sauce! This classic Southern dish combines the tangy flavors of green tomatoes with a crispy coating and a zesty remoulade sauce that will leave your taste buds begging for more. Whether you’re hosting a party, looking for a quick and easy appetizer, or simply craving a taste of Southern comfort, this recipe is sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

For the Fried Green Tomatoes:

  • 4 green tomatoes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

For the Remoulade Sauce:

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon capers, drained and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. To begin, slice the green tomatoes into 1/4-inch thick rounds. Set aside.

  2. In a shallow dish, combine the flour, cornmeal, salt, pepper, and paprika. Mix well to ensure all the spices are evenly distributed.

  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. The oil should be about 1/2-inch deep.

  4. Dip each tomato slice into the flour mixture, coating both sides evenly. Shake off any excess flour.

  5. Carefully place the coated tomato slices into the hot oil, being cautious not to overcrowd the skillet. Fry for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy. Remove from the oil and place on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

  6. While the tomatoes are frying, prepare the remoulade sauce. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, hot sauce, parsley, capers, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  7. Once the fried green tomatoes have cooled slightly, serve them with a dollop of the homemade remoulade sauce on top or as a dipping sauce on the side.

  8. Enjoy your Crispy Fried Green Tomatoes with Remoulade Sauce as a delightful snack or appetizer!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the flour mixture before coating the tomato slices.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can also bake the green tomatoes instead of frying them. Simply preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C), coat the tomato slices with cooking spray, and bake for about 20 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices in the remoulade sauce to suit your taste. Fresh dill, chives, or even a dash of Worcestershire sauce can add a unique twist.
  • Serve the Crispy Fried Green Tomatoes with Remoulade Sauce alongside other Southern favorites like fried chicken, collard greens, or cornbread for a complete Southern-inspired meal.

Conclusion

Crispy Fried Green Tomatoes with Remoulade Sauce is a delightful snack that brings a taste of the South right to your kitchen. The crispy coating perfectly complements the tangy green tomatoes, while the zesty remoulade sauce adds a burst of flavor. Whether you’re a fan of Southern cuisine or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. So grab some green tomatoes and get frying – you won’t be disappointed!
