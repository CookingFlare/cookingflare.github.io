Garlic Butter Shrimp Linguine: A Quick and Easy Seafood Pasta


If you’re a seafood lover looking for a simple yet delicious pasta dish, then you’re in for a treat! This Garlic Butter Shrimp Linguine recipe is a perfect blend of succulent shrimp, flavorful garlic, and al dente linguine, all coming together to create a delightful seafood pasta experience. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a satisfying homemade meal, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your loved ones.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to whip up this mouthwatering dish:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of linguine
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Linguine: Start by cooking the linguine according to the package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water. Once the pasta is al dente, drain it and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Shrimp: While the linguine is cooking, it’s time to prepare the star of the dish – the shrimp. In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and black pepper. Cook the shrimp for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once the shrimp is cooked, transfer it to a plate and set it aside.

  3. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant. Be careful not to brown the garlic as it can turn bitter.

  4. Bring It Together: Add the cooked linguine and cooked shrimp to the skillet with the garlic butter sauce. Toss everything together to ensure the linguine and shrimp are coated evenly with the flavorful sauce. Sprinkle the chopped parsley and drizzle the freshly squeezed lemon juice over the pasta, giving it a gentle toss to incorporate all the ingredients.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once everything is well combined, transfer the Garlic Butter Shrimp Linguine to a serving platter or individual plates. Garnish with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese and additional chopped parsley for an extra pop of flavor.

Final Thoughts

This Garlic Butter Shrimp Linguine recipe is a testament to the fact that simple ingredients can come together to create an extraordinary dish. The combination of succulent shrimp, aromatic garlic, and zesty lemon juice makes this seafood pasta a true culinary delight. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to elevate their pasta game with minimal effort.

So, the next time you find yourself craving a quick and easy seafood pasta, consider giving this recipe a whirl. Your taste buds will thank you for the burst of flavors, and your guests will marvel at your culinary prowess. Happy cooking!
