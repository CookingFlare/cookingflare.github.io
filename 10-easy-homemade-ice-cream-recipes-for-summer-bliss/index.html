10 Easy Homemade Ice Cream Recipes for Summer Bliss


Summer is the time to relax, soak in the sun, and indulge in sweet, cooling treats. Few things capture the joy of the season better than homemade ice cream. Whether you’re sharing scoops under the shade of a tree or ending a backyard barbecue with a decadent dessert, ice cream is the ultimate seasonal delight. Creating ice cream at home is surprisingly simple and offers endless possibilities for customization. These ten easy homemade recipes will have you churning out incredible flavors that everyone will love.

1. Classic Vanilla Ice Cream

Sometimes, simplicity is the key to perfection. This velvety vanilla ice cream is timeless and sets the foundation for endless creative toppings and mix-ins.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Whisk the cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract until combined and sugar is dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

2. Decadent Chocolate Ice Cream

Rich and indulgent, this chocolate ice cream will satisfy any sweet tooth and is perfect for those who can never resist chocolate.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Combine the cream, milk, sugar, and cocoa powder in a saucepan. Heat over medium heat until the sugar dissolves, whisking constantly.
  2. Remove from heat and let cool. Stir in vanilla extract.
  3. Pour into ice cream maker and churn until thickened, then freeze for 4-6 hours.

3. Refreshing Mint Chocolate Chip

Beat the heat with this cool, minty delight. The crunch of chocolate chips perfectly complements the creamy texture of homemade ice cream.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons peppermint extract
  • A few drops green food coloring (optional)
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together cream, milk, sugar, peppermint extract, and food coloring if desired.
  2. Pour into your ice cream maker and churn until thickened.
  3. Stir in chocolate chips before transferring to a container and freezing.

4. Strawberry Bliss Ice Cream

This fruity, vibrant ice cream captures the taste of sun-ripened strawberries and is a must-have for summertime.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk

Instructions:

  1. Blend strawberries with sugar in a blender or food processor until smooth.
  2. Mix in heavy cream and milk.
  3. Pour the strawberry mixture into your ice cream maker and churn as directed. Freeze before serving.

5. Homemade Cookies and Cream

Skip the store-bought version and make your own silky cookies and cream ice cream that’s packed with crushed Oreo cookies.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup crushed Oreo cookies

Instructions:

  1. Combine cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract until well mixed.
  2. Churn mixture in an ice cream maker. Add crushed Oreos during the last 5 minutes of churning. Freeze and serve.

6. Zesty Lemon Ice Cream

Perfect for citrus lovers, this tangy lemon ice cream adds a refreshing twist to the traditional summer treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 2 lemons

Instructions:

  1. Combine cream, milk, sugar, lemon zest, and lemon juice until well blended.
  2. Pour into an ice cream maker, churn until creamy, and freeze overnight.

7. Velvety Salted Caramel Ice Cream

Sweet and salty come together in this luxurious salted caramel recipe that feels like an indulgence with every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups heavy cream (additional for ice cream base)
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup sugar (for ice cream base)
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 cup of sugar in a saucepan over medium heat until melted into caramel. Stir in butter and ½ cup cream. Cool completely.
  2. Mix caramel with remaining cream, milk, and sugar until smooth. Add sea salt.
  3. Churn the mixture in the ice cream maker, freeze, and serve.

8. Tropical Mango Ice Cream

Transport your taste buds to an island paradise with this smooth and fruity mango ice cream.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk

Instructions:

  1. Puree mangoes and sugar in a blender until smooth.
  2. Mix with cream and milk, then churn in an ice cream maker.
  3. Freeze for 4 hours before enjoying.

9. Coffee Lover’s Ice Cream

This creamy coffee ice cream delivers a caffeine boost with the smooth richness of homemade dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 5 teaspoons instant coffee powder

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve coffee powder in warmed milk. Combine coffee-infused milk with cream and sugar.
  2. Churn mixture in an ice cream maker until ready. Freeze overnight for best flavor.

10. Pineapple Coconut Ice Cream

Satisfy your craving for tropical flavors with this Hawaiian-inspired creation that combines pineapple and creamy coconut.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together coconut milk, crushed pineapple, sugar, and heavy cream.
  2. Churn the mixture in an ice cream maker until thickened. Freeze for at least 4 hours.

These homemade ice cream recipes prove that delicious desserts don’t require fancy equipment or hard-to-find ingredients. Making ice cream in your own kitchen is an opportunity to experiment, embrace creativity, and share something special with loved ones. Whether you stick to classic flavors or venture into bold new combinations, there’s no limit to the cold, creamy enjoyment that awaits.
