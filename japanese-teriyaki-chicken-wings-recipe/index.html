Japanese Teriyaki Chicken Wings Recipe


If you’re a fan of Japanese cuisine, you’ve probably indulged in the delightful flavors of teriyaki at some point. The sweet and savory combination of soy sauce, mirin, and sugar creates a glaze that’s simply irresistible. Today, we’re going to explore a classic and crowd-pleasing dish: Japanese Teriyaki Chicken Wings. This recipe is perfect for gatherings, weeknight dinners, or even as a game-day snack. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced (for garnish)
  • Toasted sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions:

Step 1: Preparing the Chicken Wings

Start by thoroughly rinsing the chicken wings under cold water and patting them dry with paper towels. Trim off any excess skin or fat if desired. Place the wings in a large mixing bowl and set them aside.

Step 2: Making the Teriyaki Sauce

In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, mirin, sake, brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Stir the mixture over medium heat until the sugar has completely dissolved. Bring the sauce to a gentle simmer and let it cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until it has slightly thickened. Once done, remove the sauce from the heat and allow it to cool to room temperature.

Step 3: Marinating the Chicken Wings

Pour half of the teriyaki sauce over the chicken wings, ensuring that they are evenly coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight. Reserve the remaining teriyaki sauce for basting and serving.

Step 4: Preparing the Grill

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates to prevent the chicken wings from sticking.

Step 5: Grilling the Chicken Wings

Once the grill is ready, place the marinated chicken wings on the grates. Grill the wings for approximately 10-12 minutes per side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. During the last few minutes of grilling, baste the wings with the reserved teriyaki sauce, allowing it to caramelize and create a glossy glaze.

Step 6: Serving

Transfer the grilled teriyaki chicken wings to a serving platter and garnish with thinly sliced green onions and toasted sesame seeds for an extra burst of flavor and visual appeal. Serve the wings hot and watch them disappear within minutes!

Conclusion:

Japanese Teriyaki Chicken Wings are a delicious fusion of sweet and savory flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a comforting meal, these wings are sure to be a hit. Experiment with the marinade by adding a touch of heat with red pepper flakes or a hint of citrus with grated orange zest. Embrace the versatility of this recipe and make it your own. Enjoy the delightful experience of savoring these tender and flavorful wings, and share the joy of Japanese-inspired cooking with your loved ones.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a culinary adventure, whip up a batch of Japanese Teriyaki Chicken Wings and savor the delightful taste of Japan right in your own kitchen. Cheers to good food and memorable moments!
