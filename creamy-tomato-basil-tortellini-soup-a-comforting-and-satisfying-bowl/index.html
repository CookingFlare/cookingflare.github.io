Creamy Tomato Basil Tortellini Soup: A Comforting and Satisfying Bowl


Are you in the mood for a warm and comforting bowl of soup that will satisfy your cravings? Look no further than this Creamy Tomato Basil Tortellini Soup. Packed with flavors and textures, this soup is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy weeknight meal or a hearty dish to serve at a dinner party, this recipe has got you covered.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 package (20 ounces) refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

  2. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Stir in the dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  3. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth. If using a regular blender, be sure to let the soup cool slightly before blending and blend in batches if necessary.

  4. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream. Heat the soup over low heat, stirring occasionally, until it is warmed through.

  5. Meanwhile, cook the cheese tortellini according to the package instructions. Drain the cooked tortellini and add it to the pot of soup. Stir gently to combine.

  6. Ladle the creamy tomato basil tortellini soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a burst of flavor, add a handful of fresh baby spinach or kale to the soup just before adding the tortellini. The greens will wilt and add a nutritious twist to the dish.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a roux to the pot after pureeing the soup. To make a roux, melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a separate saucepan, then whisk in 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour until smooth. Slowly whisk in a ladle of the pureed soup, then pour the mixture back into the pot and stir to combine. Continue to heat the soup until it reaches your desired consistency.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of tortellini, such as spinach and ricotta or mushroom-filled tortellini, to add additional flavors to the soup.
  • This soup can be easily made ahead of time and reheated when ready to serve. Simply store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Conclusion

Creamy Tomato Basil Tortellini Soup is a versatile and delicious dish that is guaranteed to warm your soul. With its creamy texture, robust flavors, and tender tortellini, it’s the perfect comfort food for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or a crowd, this soup is sure to impress. So grab a bowl, cozy up, and savor every spoonful of this comforting and satisfying soup.
