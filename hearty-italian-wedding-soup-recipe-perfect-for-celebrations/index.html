Hearty Italian Wedding Soup Recipe - Perfect for Celebrations


When it comes to hearty and comforting dishes, Italian wedding soup is a classic that never fails to impress. This flavorful soup, packed with tender meatballs, hearty greens, and savory broth, is the perfect addition to any celebration. Whether you’re hosting a special gathering or simply craving a soul-warming meal, this Italian wedding soup recipe is sure to be a hit. Let’s dive into the details of creating this delectable dish that will leave your guests asking for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Soup:

  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups small pasta (such as orzo or acini di pepe)
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meatballs:

    • In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
    • Shape the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.

  2. Cook the Meatballs:

    • In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add the meatballs to the pot and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the meatballs and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Soup Base:

    • In the same pot, add the chopped onion, carrots, celery, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
    • Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.

  4. Add Pasta and Spinach:

    • Stir in the small pasta and let it cook until al dente, following the package instructions.
    • Once the pasta is cooked, add the chopped spinach and stir until wilted.

  5. Combine Meatballs and Soup:

    • Gently add the cooked meatballs back into the pot with the soup.
    • Let the soup simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Ladle the Italian wedding soup into bowls and serve hot, garnished with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes to the soup for a hint of heat.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can use ground turkey or chicken for the meatballs.
  • Make the soup ahead of time and let it sit for a few hours to intensify the flavors before reheating and serving.

Conclusion

Italian wedding soup is a timeless dish that brings warmth and comfort to any celebration. Its rich flavors and satisfying ingredients make it a crowd-pleaser that is guaranteed to leave a lasting impression. Whether you’re preparing it for a special occasion or simply craving a taste of Italy, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your loved ones, simmer a pot of this delightful soup, and let the festivities begin! Cheers to good food, good company, and cherished memories. Buon appetito!
