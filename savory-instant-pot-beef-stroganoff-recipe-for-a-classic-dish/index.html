Savory Instant Pot Beef Stroganoff Recipe for a Classic Dish


If you’re a fan of comfort food, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering Savory Instant Pot Beef Stroganoff recipe. This classic dish is a beloved favorite for its rich, creamy sauce and tender beef, and with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have this delicious meal on the table in no time. Let’s dive into the details of creating this hearty and satisfying dish that will surely become a regular in your recipe rotation.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of beef chuck, cut into strips
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces of cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound of egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the beef strips with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and add a little oil. Once the pot is hot, brown the beef in batches, then transfer it to a plate and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute. Incorporate the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the onions and mushrooms, stirring continuously for about a minute to create a roux. This will help to thicken the sauce later on.

  4. Pour in the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, and paprika, stirring well to combine all the ingredients. Return the browned beef to the pot, ensuring it’s submerged in the liquid.

  5. Close the Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 15 minutes at high pressure. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before performing a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  6. While the beef stroganoff is cooking, prepare the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Once the noodles are cooked, drain them and set them aside.

  7. After releasing the pressure from the Instant Pot, open the lid and stir in the sour cream until it’s well incorporated into the sauce. Taste the stroganoff and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  8. Serve the savory beef stroganoff over a bed of the cooked egg noodles, garnishing with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Conclusion

This Savory Instant Pot Beef Stroganoff recipe offers a delightful twist on a classic dish, with the convenience of the Instant Pot making it a breeze to prepare. The tender beef, rich and creamy sauce, and earthy mushrooms come together to create a comforting and satisfying meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this beef stroganoff is sure to impress. So, fire up your Instant Pot and treat yourself to a bowl of this scrumptious comfort food!
