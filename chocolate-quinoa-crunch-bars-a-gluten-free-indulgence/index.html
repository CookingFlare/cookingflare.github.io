Chocolate Quinoa Crunch Bars: A Gluten-Free Indulgence


If you’re in the mood for a gluten-free treat that’s both indulgent and nutritious, look no further than these Chocolate Quinoa Crunch Bars. Packed with the goodness of quinoa, the richness of chocolate, and a satisfying crunch, these bars are sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply looking for a delicious snack, these bars are a must-try.

Ingredients

For the Base:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

For the Chocolate Layer:

  • 1 1/2 cups dark chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Crunchy Topping:

  • 1/2 cup crispy rice cereal
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds or pecans
  • 2 tablespoons shredded coconut

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Base

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a square baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, almond flour, honey or maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. Allow it to cool while you prepare the chocolate layer.

Step 2: Make the Chocolate Layer

  1. In a heatproof bowl set over a pot of simmering water, melt the dark chocolate chips and coconut oil, stirring until smooth.
  2. Once melted, remove the bowl from the heat and stir in the almond butter, honey or maple syrup, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Pour the chocolate mixture over the cooled quinoa base, spreading it out evenly. Place the dish in the refrigerator to set while you make the crunchy topping.

Step 3: Add the Crunchy Topping

  1. In a small bowl, combine the crispy rice cereal, chopped almonds or pecans, and shredded coconut.
  2. Sprinkle the crunchy topping over the chocolate layer, gently pressing it down to adhere.
  3. Return the dish to the refrigerator and chill for at least 2 hours, or until the chocolate is set.

Step 4: Cut and Serve

  1. Once the bars are fully set, use the parchment paper to lift them out of the baking dish and onto a cutting board.
  2. Using a sharp knife, cut the bars into squares or rectangles of your desired size.
  3. Serve and enjoy! Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

Nutritional Benefits

These Chocolate Quinoa Crunch Bars not only satisfy your sweet tooth but also offer a nutritional punch. Quinoa is a complete protein, rich in fiber, and contains various vitamins and minerals. Dark chocolate is packed with antioxidants, and the nuts and coconut provide healthy fats and additional nutrients. This gluten-free treat is a guilt-free indulgence that you can feel good about enjoying.

Conclusion

Whether you’re hosting a gathering, need a mid-afternoon pick-me-up, or simply want to treat yourself, these Chocolate Quinoa Crunch Bars are the perfect solution. With their delightful combination of flavors and textures, they are bound to become a staple in your repertoire of gluten-free treats. So, gather your ingredients and give this recipe a try - your taste buds will thank you!
