Asian Inspiration: Sweet and Sour Pineapple Chicken Stir-Fry


When it comes to Asian-inspired dishes, the combination of sweet and sour flavors is a classic favorite. This Sweet and Sour Pineapple Chicken Stir-Fry recipe brings together the tanginess of pineapple and the savory goodness of tender chicken in a delightful stir-fry that will tantalize your taste buds. With a perfect balance of flavors and textures, this dish is sure to become a regular feature on your dinner table.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup (240ml) pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) ketchup
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, ketchup, pineapple juice, brown sugar, and cornstarch. Set the sauce aside.

  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and season with salt and pepper. Cook until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the sliced onions, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, garlic, and grated ginger. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sweet and sour sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything in the sauce.

  5. Add the pineapple chunks to the skillet and gently stir to combine with the chicken and vegetables. Allow the stir-fry to simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens and the pineapple is heated through.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency and the chicken and vegetables are well coated, remove the skillet from the heat.

  7. Serve the Sweet and Sour Pineapple Chicken Stir-Fry over a bed of cooked rice and garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor and visual appeal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian version, substitute the chicken with firm tofu or your favorite meat alternative.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir-fry based on your preferences. Snow peas, broccoli florets, or water chestnuts would make excellent additions.
  • Adjust the sweetness and tanginess of the sauce to your liking by altering the amount of brown sugar and rice vinegar.

Conclusion

This Sweet and Sour Pineapple Chicken Stir-Fry is a delightful way to bring the vibrant flavors of Asian cuisine to your dinner table. The combination of tender chicken, juicy pineapple, and crisp vegetables, all coated in a luscious sweet and sour sauce, creates a dish that is both comforting and exciting. Whether you’re a fan of stir-fries or looking to explore new flavors, this recipe is a must-try. Enjoy the delightful interplay of sweet and sour notes in every delectable bite!
