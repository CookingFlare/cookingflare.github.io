Fluffy Blueberry Pancake Recipes for a Burst of Flavor

3 min · 567 words

Who doesn’t love waking up to a stack of fluffy pancakes? And when those pancakes are bursting with the sweet and tangy flavors of blueberries, it takes breakfast to a whole new level. In this post, we’ll explore some delicious and easy-to-make blueberry pancake recipes that are sure to delight your taste buds and impress your loved ones. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Classic Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Do not overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  5. Heat a lightly greased skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with maple syrup and extra blueberries on top.

Fluffy Blueberry Pancakes with Lemon Zest

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, vanilla extract, and lemon zest.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Again, do not overmix.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  5. Heat a lightly greased skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with a sprinkle of powdered sugar and a drizzle of lemon juice.

Vegan Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, apple cider vinegar, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  5. Heat a lightly greased skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite vegan butter and maple syrup.

Blueberry Pancake Tips and Tricks

  • For extra fluffiness, separate the egg and beat the egg white until stiff peaks form. Fold it into the batter just before cooking the pancakes.
  • If you’re using frozen blueberries, thaw them first and pat them dry before adding them to the batter.
  • To prevent the blueberries from sinking to the bottom of the pancake, toss them in a little flour before folding them into the batter.
  • Feel free to add a sprinkle of cinnamon or a dash of nutmeg to the batter for some added warmth and flavor.
  • To keep the pancakes warm while you cook the rest, place them on a baking sheet in a preheated oven at 200°F (93°C).

So there you have it, three mouthwatering blueberry pancake recipes that are guaranteed to brighten up your breakfast table. Whether you prefer the classic version, a zesty twist, or a vegan alternative, these fluffy pancakes will surely satisfy your cravings. So go ahead, whip up a stack, and enjoy a burst of flavor with every bite. Happy cooking!
