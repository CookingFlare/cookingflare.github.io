Grilling Peaches: Sweet and Juicy Recipes for a Summery BBQ Dessert


Summer is in full swing, and what better way to celebrate the season than with a sweet and juicy grilled peach dessert? Grilling peaches not only brings out their natural sweetness but also adds a smoky and caramelized flavor that will leave your taste buds wanting more. In this article, we will explore different recipes that showcase the versatility of grilled peaches, from simple and elegant to decadent and indulgent. So fire up your grill and get ready to create some mouthwatering desserts that will impress your family and friends at your next BBQ!

Grilled Peaches with Honey and Cinnamon

This classic recipe is a crowd-pleaser and a perfect introduction to the world of grilled peaches. The sweetness of the honey and the warmth of the cinnamon complement the natural flavors of the fruit, creating a delightful combination.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh peaches
  • Honey
  • Ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the peaches in half and remove the pits.
  3. Place the peach halves on the grill, cut side down, and cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until grill marks appear.
  4. Flip the peaches over and drizzle the cut side with honey and sprinkle with cinnamon.
  5. Grill for an additional 3-4 minutes, or until the peaches are tender and caramelized.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them cool slightly before serving.

Serve these grilled peaches on their own as a light and refreshing dessert, or pair them with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence.

Grilled Peach and Mascarpone Crostini

If you’re looking to impress your guests with an elegant and sophisticated dessert, look no further than these grilled peach and mascarpone crostini. The combination of the creamy mascarpone, the sweet grilled peaches, and the crunchy crostini creates a harmonious blend of flavors and textures.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh peaches
  • Baguette, sliced
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Honey
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the peaches into thin slices.
  3. Brush the baguette slices with olive oil and grill until toasted and lightly charred.
  4. Remove the baguette slices from the grill and spread a generous amount of mascarpone cheese on each slice.
  5. Top each crostini with a few slices of grilled peaches.
  6. Drizzle with honey and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

These grilled peach and mascarpone crostini are not only delicious but also visually appealing, making them the perfect dessert for a summer dinner party or special occasion.

Grilled Peach and Blueberry Crumble

For those who love a warm and comforting dessert, this grilled peach and blueberry crumble is a must-try. The combination of the sweet grilled peaches, the burst of tangy blueberries, and the crunchy crumble topping creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will satisfy any sweet tooth.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh peaches
  • Fresh blueberries
  • All-purpose flour
  • Rolled oats
  • Brown sugar
  • Butter, cold and cubed
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla ice cream, for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the peaches into wedges and place them in a grill basket.
  3. Add the blueberries to the grill basket and gently toss them with the peaches.
  4. Grill the peaches and blueberries for about 5-6 minutes, or until they are slightly softened and caramelized.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, rolled oats, brown sugar, butter, and cinnamon. Use your fingers to rub the butter into the dry ingredients until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  6. Transfer the grilled peaches and blueberries to a baking dish and sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the fruit.
  7. Place the dish on the grill and close the lid. Grill for about 15-20 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the fruit is bubbling.
  8. Remove from the grill and let it cool slightly before serving.
  9. Serve the grilled peach and blueberry crumble warm, with a scoop of vanilla ice cream if desired.

This grilled peach and blueberry crumble is the ultimate comfort dessert that will warm your soul and satisfy your sweet cravings. It’s perfect for cozy summer nights or any occasion where you want to indulge in a delicious treat.

In conclusion, grilling peaches opens up a world of possibilities when it comes to creating sweet and juicy desserts. From simple and elegant to decadent and indulgent, there is a grilled peach recipe for every taste and occasion. So fire up your grill, grab some fresh peaches, and get ready to impress your loved ones with these mouthwatering summer treats. Enjoy the flavors of the season and embrace the sweetness of grilled peaches!
