Homemade Key Lime Bars: A Refreshing Summer Dessert


There’s nothing quite like a refreshing dessert to beat the summer heat, and these Homemade Key Lime Bars are the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth. Bursting with tangy lime flavor and a buttery crust, these bars are easy to make and even easier to devour. So, grab your apron and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the filling:

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup fresh key lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons key lime zest

For the whipped cream topping:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and melted butter. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated.
  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking dish, creating an even layer. Use the back of a spoon or the bottom of a measuring cup to firmly press the mixture down.
  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until it is lightly golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, and key lime zest until well combined.
  6. Pour the filling mixture over the cooled crust, spreading it out evenly.
  7. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the filling is set and no longer jiggles when gently shaken.
  8. Remove from the oven and let the bars cool to room temperature. Then, refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until chilled and firm.
  9. While the bars are chilling, prepare the whipped cream topping. In a mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  10. Once the bars are fully chilled, remove them from the refrigerator and cut them into squares or rectangles.
  11. Serve each bar with a dollop of whipped cream on top, and if desired, garnish with a sprinkle of lime zest for an extra burst of flavor.
  12. Enjoy the creamy, tangy goodness of these Homemade Key Lime Bars!

Tips and Variations

  • If you can’t find fresh key limes, you can use regular limes instead. The flavor will be slightly different, but still delicious.
  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try adding a teaspoon of grated ginger to the filling mixture for a hint of warmth and spice.
  • If you prefer a more tart flavor, increase the amount of key lime juice by an additional 1/4 cup.
  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Just be sure to let them come to room temperature before serving for the best texture and flavor.

In Conclusion

Homemade Key Lime Bars are the ultimate summer dessert, offering a refreshing burst of citrusy flavor in every bite. With a buttery graham cracker crust and a creamy key lime filling, these bars are a crowd-pleaser that will leave your guests asking for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a taste of summer, these bars are the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth. So, go ahead and whip up a batch of these Homemade Key Lime Bars and indulge in the flavors of summer!
