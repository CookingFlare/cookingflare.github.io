Irresistible Cinnamon Rolls: A Breakfast Classic


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls wafting through the kitchen on a lazy weekend morning. These sweet, soft, and gooey treats are a breakfast classic that never fails to delight. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, making your own irresistible cinnamon rolls is a rewarding experience that’s sure to impress your family and friends. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through a step-by-step recipe for creating the perfect batch of cinnamon rolls, along with tips and tricks to ensure they turn out absolutely divine.

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 large egg

For the filling:

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 cups of flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
    • In a separate microwave-safe bowl, heat the milk and butter until the mixture reaches 120-130°F. Add this to the dry ingredients, along with the egg.
    • Stir until a soft dough forms, then gradually add the remaining cup of flour until the dough is smooth and elastic.
    • Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, then place it in a greased bowl. Cover and let it rise for about an hour, or until doubled in size.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon for the filling.
    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a 16x21-inch rectangle.
    • Spread the softened butter over the dough, then sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly on top.

  3. Roll and Cut:

    • Starting with the long edge, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Pinch the seam to seal.
    • Cut the rolled dough into 12 equal pieces and place them in a greased 9x13 inch baking pan. Cover and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  4. Bake:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake the cinnamon rolls for 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown.

  5. Prepare the Frosting:

    • While the rolls are baking, prepare the cream cheese frosting by beating together the cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth and creamy.

  6. Enjoy:

    • Once the cinnamon rolls are out of the oven and slightly cooled, generously spread the cream cheese frosting over the top.

Tips for Success:

  • To speed up the rising process, preheat your oven to the lowest temperature, then turn it off and place the covered dough inside to rise.
  • For a twist, add chopped nuts or raisins to the filling for extra texture and flavor.
  • Leftover cinnamon rolls can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week. Simply reheat them in the microwave for a few seconds before serving.

There you have it – a foolproof recipe for creating your own batch of irresistible cinnamon rolls that are sure to become a family favorite. Whether you enjoy them with a steaming cup of coffee or as a special treat for brunch, these homemade cinnamon rolls are bound to bring joy to your kitchen and your taste buds. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a delicious baking adventure!
