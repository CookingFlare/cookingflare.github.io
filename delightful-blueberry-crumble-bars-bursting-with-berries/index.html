Delightful Blueberry Crumble Bars: Bursting with Berries


If you’re a fan of blueberries, then you’re in for a treat! These Delightful Blueberry Crumble Bars are the perfect balance of sweet, tangy, and buttery goodness. Packed with juicy blueberries, these bars are bursting with flavor and are sure to be a hit with your family and friends. So, without further ado, let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust and crumble topping:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 large egg

For the blueberry filling:

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt until well combined. Add the cold, cubed butter to the bowl and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Remove 1 cup of the crumb mixture and set it aside for later use as the crumble topping.

  4. To the remaining crumb mixture, add the egg and mix until the dough comes together. Press the dough evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan, creating a firm and even crust.

  5. In a separate bowl, combine the blueberries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Gently toss the mixture until the blueberries are evenly coated.

  6. Spread the blueberry filling evenly over the crust in the baking pan. Sprinkle the reserved crumble topping over the blueberry filling, ensuring an even distribution.

  7. Bake the bars in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust and crumble topping are golden brown, and the blueberry filling is bubbling and thickened.

  8. Once baked, remove the pan from the oven and allow it to cool completely on a wire rack. This will ensure that the bars set properly and are easier to cut.

  9. Once cooled, lift the bars out of the pan using the parchment paper as handles. Transfer them to a cutting board and slice into squares or rectangles of your desired size.

  10. Serve these Delightful Blueberry Crumble Bars as a delicious snack, dessert, or even as a breakfast treat. They can be enjoyed on their own or paired with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence.

Storage

These Blueberry Crumble Bars can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. For longer storage, keep them in the refrigerator for up to a week. They can also be frozen for up to three months. Just make sure to wrap them tightly in plastic wrap or place them in a freezer-safe container to maintain their freshness.

So, why wait? Gather your ingredients and get ready to bake these Delightful Blueberry Crumble Bars that are bursting with berries. With their buttery crust, sweet blueberry filling, and crumbly topping, these bars are sure to become a favorite in your household. Enjoy!
