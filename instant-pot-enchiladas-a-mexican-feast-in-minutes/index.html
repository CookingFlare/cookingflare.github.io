Instant Pot Enchiladas: A Mexican Feast in Minutes


If you’re craving a flavorful, savory Mexican meal but don’t have hours to spend in the kitchen, you’re in the right place. Instant Pot Enchiladas bring all the bold flavors of traditional enchiladas into your home without the hassle of lengthy cooking times. The Instant Pot makes this classic Mexican dish quicker and easier than ever before, without sacrificing taste or texture. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or meal prepping for the week, this recipe is sure to become a favorite.

What Are Enchiladas?

Enchiladas are a staple in Mexican cuisine, consisting of corn tortillas stuffed with a variety of fillings, rolled up, and smothered in a rich, flavorful sauce. The fillings can range from shredded meats like chicken or beef to beans, cheese, or even vegetables. The sauce—typically a red or green chili sauce—adds a depth of flavor that ties everything together. The beauty of enchiladas is their versatility, making them easy to customize to suit your taste preferences or dietary needs.

Why Use an Instant Pot for Enchiladas?

The Instant Pot is a kitchen appliance that has revolutionized home cooking by combining multiple cooking functions into one device. It acts as a pressure cooker, slow cooker, sauté pan, and even a rice cooker. When it comes to making enchiladas, the Instant Pot can streamline the process by cooking the fillings and sauce quickly and evenly, ensuring a delicious result in a fraction of the time. The pressure cooking function seals in the flavors and makes the ingredients tender, while the sautéing option allows you to brown your meat and vegetables, adding depth to the overall taste.

The Ingredients for Instant Pot Enchiladas

The beauty of Instant Pot Enchiladas lies in how simple the ingredients are. You likely already have most of them in your pantry, making this dish an accessible and convenient option for any day of the week.

For the Enchiladas:

  • 2 lbs of chicken breasts or thighs (for a quicker version, you can use rotisserie chicken)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (4 oz) of diced green chilies
  • 1 can (10 oz) of enchilada sauce (choose red or green, depending on your preference)
  • 12 small corn tortillas (or flour, if you prefer)
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Mexican blend, or your favorite cheese)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Seasoning:

  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for heat)

How to Make Instant Pot Enchiladas

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

The first step is to prepare the chicken, which will serve as the filling for your enchiladas. If you’re using fresh chicken breasts or thighs, season them with a pinch of salt and pepper, then set them aside. If you’re using rotisserie chicken, you can skip this step and simply shred the meat later.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

Set your Instant Pot to the ‘Sauté’ function and allow it to heat up. Add a tablespoon of oil to the pot and let it warm for a minute or two. Then, add the chopped onion and garlic to the pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes until they soften and become fragrant.

Step 3: Add the Chicken and Seasonings

If you’re using fresh chicken, add the chicken breasts or thighs to the Instant Pot with the onions and garlic. Sprinkle in your seasoning mix, which includes chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, and cayenne pepper. Stir everything together, ensuring that the chicken is coated in the seasonings and onions. Let it sauté for another 2 minutes to allow the spices to toast slightly.

Step 4: Pressure Cook the Chicken

Once the chicken is nicely seasoned and the onions have softened, add 1 cup of water or chicken broth to the Instant Pot to create steam for pressure cooking. Close the Instant Pot lid and make sure the valve is set to ‘Sealing.’ Set the Instant Pot to ‘Pressure Cook’ or ‘Manual’ on high pressure for 10 minutes. If you are using rotisserie chicken, skip this step and proceed directly to shredding the chicken later.

Step 5: Release the Pressure and Shred the Chicken

Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release by carefully turning the valve to ‘Venting.’ Open the lid and check the chicken to ensure it’s cooked through (the internal temperature should be 165°F). Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and mix it with the onions, garlic, and seasonings.

Step 6: Assemble the Enchiladas

Now it’s time to assemble your enchiladas! Begin by lightly warming the corn tortillas to make them pliable. You can do this by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds. Once warmed, spread a small amount of enchilada sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish or the Instant Pot insert if you want to cook everything together.

Take each tortilla and spoon some of the shredded chicken mixture onto the center. Roll the tortilla tightly around the filling and place it seam-side down in the baking dish. Repeat until all the tortillas are filled and arranged in the dish.

Step 7: Add the Sauce and Cheese

Once your tortillas are all rolled up, pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the top, making sure each tortilla is covered in sauce. Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly on top of the sauce. If you like your cheese extra bubbly and browned, you can add a few extra handfuls of cheese for that perfect melt.

Step 8: Final Cooking in the Instant Pot

To cook the enchiladas in the Instant Pot, use the ‘Sauté’ function again and set the pot to the ‘Keep Warm’ setting. Cover the pot with the lid, and let it warm for 5-10 minutes, allowing the cheese to melt and the sauce to soak into the tortillas. If you’re using a baking dish, you can also bake the enchiladas in a preheated 350°F oven for 15-20 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

Step 9: Serve and Enjoy

Once your Instant Pot Enchiladas are ready, serve them up hot with your favorite toppings. Some great options include sour cream, avocado, cilantro, sliced jalapeños, or a squeeze of fresh lime juice. For a complete meal, pair them with Mexican rice, refried beans, or a crisp salad.

Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Enchiladas

  • Use Corn Tortillas for Authentic Flavor: Corn tortillas add an authentic Mexican touch to your enchiladas. If you prefer flour tortillas, you can certainly use those, but corn tortillas hold the sauce better and add a more traditional flavor.

  • Customize the Filling: While this recipe uses chicken, you can swap it out for beef, pork, or even vegetarian options like beans, cheese, or roasted vegetables. Feel free to get creative with the fillings based on your preferences or dietary needs.

  • Sauce Options: If you prefer a spicier flavor, opt for a red enchilada sauce with chipotle peppers or add some extra cayenne pepper. For a milder flavor, green enchilada sauce is a great choice.

  • Make it Ahead: Enchiladas are a great make-ahead dish. You can assemble them a day or two before you plan to cook them, store them in the fridge, and then pop them in the Instant Pot or oven when you’re ready to eat.

  • Extra Cheese: Don’t skimp on the cheese! A generous amount of melted cheese on top of the enchiladas adds richness and depth to the dish, making each bite even more satisfying.

Why You’ll Love These Instant Pot Enchiladas

The Instant Pot truly transforms the way we cook, making it possible to enjoy a homemade Mexican feast in a fraction of the time. These enchiladas are quick, easy, and packed with flavor. You can throw together the ingredients in just a few minutes, set the Instant Pot to cook, and walk away. In no time, you’ll have a delicious, satisfying meal that tastes like it’s been simmering for hours. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, these Instant Pot Enchiladas are sure to impress your taste buds and simplify your cooking routine.

Conclusion

Instant Pot Enchiladas are the perfect solution for busy weeknights or when you’re craving a homemade Mexican dish without the long cooking times. With the right ingredients and just a few easy steps, you can bring the flavors of Mexico to your dinner table in no time. So, dust off your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a meal that’s as delicious as it is convenient. Whether you’re making them for a family dinner, hosting friends, or meal prepping for the week, these enchiladas will quickly become a go-to recipe in your rotation. Enjoy!
