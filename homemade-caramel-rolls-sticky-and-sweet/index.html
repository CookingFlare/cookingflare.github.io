Homemade Caramel Rolls: Sticky and Sweet


There’s something undeniably comforting about the aroma of freshly baked caramel rolls wafting through the kitchen. These homemade treats are not only sticky and sweet but also a labor of love. From mixing the dough to drizzling the luscious caramel sauce, making caramel rolls from scratch is a rewarding experience that yields irresistible results. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a delectable journey of baking these indulgent treats in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Caramel Sauce

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup light corn syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the Filling

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened

Instructions

Making the Dough

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm milk and let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add sugar, melted butter, eggs, flour, and salt to the yeast mixture. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Preparing the Caramel Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the brown sugar, heavy cream, and corn syrup. Cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture comes to a boil.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract and salt. Set aside to cool slightly.

Assembling the Rolls

  1. Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a 16x21-inch rectangle.
  2. Spread the softened butter over the rolled-out dough.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon for the filling. Sprinkle the mixture evenly over the dough.
  4. Starting from the long edge, tightly roll up the dough into a log and pinch the seam to seal.
  5. Cut the rolled dough into 12 equal pieces.

Baking the Rolls

  1. Pour the prepared caramel sauce into the bottom of a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Place the cut rolls in the dish, spacing them evenly apart.
  3. Cover the dish with a clean kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Bake the rolls for 25-30 minutes or until they turn golden brown.

Serving and Enjoying

Once out of the oven, let the caramel rolls cool in the dish for a few minutes before inverting them onto a serving platter. The gooey caramel sauce will coat the rolls, making them irresistibly sticky and sweet. These delightful treats are best enjoyed warm, with a steaming cup of coffee or a cold glass of milk.

Conclusion

Homemade caramel rolls are a delightful indulgence that brings warmth and comfort to any occasion. Whether you’re baking them for a leisurely weekend breakfast or as a special treat for your loved ones, the process of making these sticky and sweet delights is as rewarding as the taste itself. So, gather your ingredients, channel your inner baker, and savor the heavenly aroma of freshly baked caramel rolls filling your home. After all, there’s nothing quite like the magic of homemade goodness.

Happy baking!
