Savory Roasted Vegetable Quiche: A Brunch Delight


If you’re looking for a delicious and versatile dish to serve at your next brunch gathering, this Savory Roasted Vegetable Quiche is sure to impress your guests. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this quiche makes for a satisfying and visually appealing addition to any brunch spread. The combination of savory roasted vegetables and creamy egg custard nestled in a flaky crust creates a harmonious medley of textures and tastes that will leave everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups mixed roasted vegetables (such as bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, and red onions)
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (such as Gruyère or cheddar)
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  2. Add the cold butter cubes to the bowl and use a pastry cutter or your hands to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overwork the dough.
  4. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Roast the Vegetables

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss the mixed vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized.
  4. Remove from the oven and let the vegetables cool slightly.

Step 3: Assemble the Quiche

  1. Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish.
  2. Gently press the dough into the pie dish, trimming any excess overhang.
  3. Arrange the roasted vegetables and shredded cheese in the pie crust.

Step 4: Prepare the Custard Filling

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, milk, salt, pepper, thyme, and oregano until well combined.
  2. Pour the custard mixture over the roasted vegetables and cheese in the pie crust.

Step 5: Bake the Quiche

  1. Place the quiche on a baking sheet to catch any spills and bake in the preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 35-40 minutes or until the custard is set and the top is golden brown.
  2. Remove the quiche from the oven and let it cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Savory Roasted Vegetable Quiche pairs beautifully with a fresh green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette, making it a complete and satisfying brunch dish. You can also serve it alongside some crusty bread and fresh fruit for a well-rounded brunch spread. The quiche can be made ahead of time and reheated before serving, making it a convenient option for entertaining.

Whether you’re hosting a casual brunch with friends or preparing a special meal for your family, this Savory Roasted Vegetable Quiche is a delightful option that is sure to please everyone’s taste buds. The combination of tender roasted vegetables, creamy custard, and flaky crust creates a flavor experience that will have your guests asking for the recipe. Try it out and elevate your brunch game with this delectable quiche!
