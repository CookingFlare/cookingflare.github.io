BBQ Chicken Lettuce Wraps: Light and Flavorful Recipes


When it comes to summer grilling, there’s nothing quite like the smoky, charred flavors of barbecue chicken. But if you’re looking for a lighter and healthier alternative to the traditional BBQ chicken sandwich, look no further than these delicious BBQ Chicken Lettuce Wraps. Packed with flavor and wrapped in crisp lettuce leaves, these wraps are the perfect combination of light and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 large lettuce leaves (such as romaine or butter lettuce)
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, sliced red onions, shredded cheese, avocado slices

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the chicken by seasoning it with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.

  2. In a small bowl, mix together the barbecue sauce and olive oil. This will create a flavorful marinade for the chicken.

  3. Place the chicken breasts on the grill and brush them generously with the barbecue sauce mixture. Cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure a juicy and tender final result.

  5. While the chicken is resting, prepare the lettuce leaves by gently washing and drying them. Lay them out on a clean surface and get ready to assemble your wraps.

  6. Slice the grilled chicken into thin strips, and divide the strips evenly among the lettuce leaves. You can customize your wraps by adding your favorite toppings such as diced tomatoes, sliced red onions, shredded cheese, or avocado slices.

  7. Once you’ve added your desired toppings, carefully roll up each lettuce leaf, creating a tight wrap. Secure the wraps with toothpicks if necessary.

  8. Serve your BBQ Chicken Lettuce Wraps immediately, and enjoy the burst of flavors and textures in every bite. These wraps are perfect for a light lunch, a refreshing dinner, or even as a party appetizer.

  9. If you have any leftovers, you can store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Just be sure to remove the toothpicks before storing.

Conclusion

BBQ Chicken Lettuce Wraps are a fantastic way to enjoy the flavors of barbecue chicken while keeping things light and healthy. The combination of tender grilled chicken, tangy barbecue sauce, and crisp lettuce leaves creates a delightful contrast of textures and tastes. Feel free to get creative with your toppings and add your personal touch to these wraps. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply looking for a quick and satisfying meal, these BBQ Chicken Lettuce Wraps are sure to be a hit. So fire up the grill, grab some lettuce leaves, and get ready to indulge in these light and flavorful creations.
