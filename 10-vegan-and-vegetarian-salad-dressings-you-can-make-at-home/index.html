10 Vegan and Vegetarian Salad Dressings You Can Make at Home


Salad dressings can elevate even the simplest salad into a memorable dish. Creating dressings at home not only allows you to control the quality of the ingredients but also empowers you to experiment with flavors and textures. Whether you’re chasing bold flavors or light, refreshing options, these 10 vegan and vegetarian salad dressings are easy to make, delicious, and designed to complement a variety of salads.

1. Classic Creamy Tahini Dressing

Tahini-based dressings are a go-to for their rich, nutty flavor and smooth texture. This simple recipe blends the natural earthiness of tahini with bright citrus tones for a well-rounded flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons water (adjust for desired consistency)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together tahini and lemon juice until smooth.
  2. Add olive oil, minced garlic, and water, continuing to whisk until fully blended.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then taste and adjust as needed.

2. Zesty Lime Cilantro Dressing

This dressing bursts with the vibrant flavors of lime and cilantro, making it ideal for Mexican-inspired salads or dishes featuring beans and avocado.

Ingredients:

  • Juice of 2 limes
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro leaves, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons maple syrup or agave
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine lime juice, olive oil, maple syrup, and garlic in a jar or bowl and mix well.
  2. Fold in chopped cilantro, stirring until incorporated.
  3. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper before serving.

3. Spicy Cashew Cream Dressing

For salads that need a rich, creamy touch with a kick of spice, this cashew-based dressing provides protein and bold flavor without dairy.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup cashews (soaked for 4 hours)
  • ½ cup water
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 small clove garlic
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Drain soaked cashews and blend them with water, lemon juice, paprika, cayenne, garlic, and salt until smooth.
  2. Taste and adjust seasonings for desired heat and flavor balance.

4. Maple Dijon Vinaigrette

Sweet and tangy with a hint of spice, this vinaigrette pairs beautifully with spinach, kale, and roasted vegetable salads.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix Dijon mustard and maple syrup in a bowl or jar.
  2. Slowly add olive oil while whisking to create an emulsified base.
  3. Stir in apple cider vinegar and season with salt and pepper.

5. Creamy Hemp Seed Dressing

Packed with nutrients, this creamy dressing features hemp seeds for a protein boost while being completely plant-based.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons hulled hemp seeds
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Blend hemp seeds, lemon juice, water, garlic, and apple cider vinegar until creamy.
  2. Add salt and pepper to taste, and adjust the water for preferred consistency.

6. Vegan Pesto Dressing

This twist on traditional pesto uses nutritional yeast instead of cheese, making it fully vegan while still maintaining rich, herby flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • ¼ cup pine nuts (or walnuts)
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 clove garlic
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Blend basil, nuts, garlic, nutritional yeast, and olive oil until smooth.
  2. Add lemon juice and salt, adjusting flavors to taste.

7. Smoky Chipotle Dressing

For salads that need a punch of bold, smoky flavor, this chipotle dressing delivers heat and depth that works well on taco bowls or grain salads.

Ingredients:

  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • 1 small clove garlic
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Blend chipotle pepper, olive oil, lime juice, maple syrup, garlic, and salt until smooth.
  2. Taste and adjust seasoning to achieve your preferred balance of smokiness and spice.

8. Avocado Lime Dressing

Creamy yet light, this avocado-based dressing is perfect for those seeking a fresh, nutrient-dense option.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mash avocado and blend with lime juice, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  2. Thin with water or additional lime juice if desired.

9. Sweet and Savory Ginger Dressing

This ginger-forward dressing adds a touch of warm spice and sweetness, making it a great choice for Asian-inspired salads or soba noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce or tamari
  • 2 teaspoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and whisk until well blended.
  2. Taste and adjust for sweetness, acidity, or saltiness as needed.

10. Herby Lemon Garlic Dressing

Bright and flavorful, this herby dressing complements fresh greens and roasted vegetables alike.

Ingredients:

  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine lemon juice, olive oil, and garlic in a jar or bowl, whisking to blend.
  2. Stir in parsley and dill, ensuring the herbs are evenly distributed.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Making your own vegan and vegetarian salad dressings not only demonstrates culinary creativity but also ensures you’re consuming high-quality and health-conscious ingredients. Whether you prefer classic flavors or bold, adventurous profiles, these simple recipes will inspire you to craft dressings tailored to your tastes. Experiment with ingredients, adjust to suit your salads, and enjoy the satisfaction of homemade deliciousness.
