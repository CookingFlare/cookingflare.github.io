Summer Italian Fare: Refreshing Recipes to Beat the Heat


Summer is in full swing, and what better way to celebrate the season than with some delicious Italian dishes that perfectly balance the heat and the flavors? This post will dive into refreshing and scrumptious recipes to keep you and your loved ones energized during the warm months.

1. Watermelon and Feta Salad

The most refreshing salad you’ll ever taste! Perfect for those hot days when all you want is something light, refreshing, and full of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Watermelon (half for the salad, half for slicing)
  • 1/2 Cup Feta Cheese (cubed)
  • 1/4 Cup Fresh Mint Leaves
  • 1/4 Cup Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 2-3 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Grab your cleaver or large knife and chop the watermelon half into bite-size chunks, discarding the rind.

  2. In a large bowl, combine watermelon chunks, crumbled feta, mint, and basil.

  3. Drizzle with olive oil and give it a gentle toss. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Serve and enjoy this refreshing summer salad!

2. Zucchini and Mint Rolls

Light and refreshing, these zucchini rolls are a vegetarian’s dream. Filled with fresh mint and ricotta cheese, you’ll enjoy an Italian-inspired appetizer that screams summer.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Medium Zucchini
  • 1 Cup Fresh Mint Leaves
  • 1 Cup Ricotta Cheese
  • 2 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Slice the zucchinis lengthwise into thin strips, 1/8" – 1/4" thick. Arrange the strips on the baking sheet.

  3. In a food processor or blender, combine mint leaves and ricotta cheese. Process until smooth.

  4. Lay each zucchini strip horizontally and spread a thin layer of the ricotta mixture. Roll up tightly and secure with a toothpick.

  5. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until zucchini becomes tender.

  6. Servings of 4

3. Tomato and Mozzarella Caprese Skewers

Classic Italian dish taken to the next level - a delicious bite of summer that will transport you straight to Italy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Large Tomato (cubed)
  • 1 Large Mozzarella Ball (cubed)
  • 10-12 Basil Leaves
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil and Balsamic Vinegar for drizzling
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.

  2. Thread tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil leaves onto skewers, alternating between each ingredient.

  3. Lightly brush the skewers with olive oil and grill for 2-3 minutes on each side, until the cheese is golden and slightly charred.

  4. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar, season with salt and pepper.

  5. Servings of 4

4. Cucumber, Mint, and Lemon Granita

The perfect palate cleanser, or a cool treat that’s a refreshing departure from ordinary desserts.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Large Cucumbers (peeled, seeded, and chopped)
  • 1/2 Cup Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Juice of 2 Lemons
  • 1 Cup Sugar

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine cucumbers, mint leaves, lemon juice, and sugar.

  2. Blend until smooth, then transfer the mixture into a shallow pan. Freeze for 1 hour, stirring with a fork every 20 minutes to break up ice crystals.

  3. Return the granita to the freezer and repeat the stirring process every 20 minutes for a total of 5 hours or until fully frozen.

  4. Scoop into bowls or glasses and serve.

  5. Servings of 8

Embrace the warm Italian sun with this selection of refreshing, delightful dishes. Mix and match them, or indulge in all five – your taste buds will thank you! Enjoy your summer and stay cool while feasting on this scrumptious Italian fare. Buon Appetito!
