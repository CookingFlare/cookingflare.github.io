Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipes

Are you craving a delicious and comforting meal that can be prepared in a flash? Look no further than these quick and easy Instant Pot chicken tortilla soup recipes. Packed with flavor and ready to eat in no time, these recipes are perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Recipe 1: Classic Chicken Tortilla Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 small onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 4 cups chicken broth 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup frozen corn 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed Juice of 1 lime Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish Tortilla chips, crushed, for garnish Sour cream, for garnish Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the diced tomatoes, green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Place the chicken breasts on top of the mixture, ensuring they are fully submerged. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release to release the pressure. Carefully remove the chicken breasts and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot, along with the frozen corn and black beans. Stir well to combine. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and simmer the soup for an additional 5 minutes to heat through. Stir in the lime juice and adjust the seasonings to taste. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, crushed tortilla chips, and a dollop of sour cream, if desired. Recipe 2: Creamy Chicken Tortilla Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 small onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes with green chilies 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 4 cups chicken broth 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup frozen corn 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed 1 cup heavy cream Juice of 1 lime Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish Tortilla chips, crushed, for garnish Sliced avocado, for garnish Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the diced tomatoes with green chilies, green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Place the chicken thighs on top of the mixture, ensuring they are fully submerged. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 15 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release to release the pressure. Carefully remove the chicken thighs and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot, along with the frozen corn and black beans. Stir well to combine. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and simmer the soup for an additional 5 minutes to heat through. Stir in the heavy cream and lime juice. Adjust the seasonings to taste. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, crushed tortilla chips, and sliced avocado. Recipe 3: Spicy Chicken Tortilla Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken tenders 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 small onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 2 jalapeños, seeded and diced 1 can (14.5 ounces) fire-roasted diced tomatoes 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 4 cups chicken broth 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup frozen corn 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed Juice of 1 lime Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish Tortilla chips, crushed, for garnish Shredded cheese, for garnish Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced jalapeños. Sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the fire-roasted diced tomatoes, green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Place the chicken tenders on top of the mixture, ensuring they are fully submerged. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release to release the pressure. Carefully remove the chicken tenders and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot, along with the frozen corn and black beans. Stir well to combine. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and simmer the soup for an additional 5 minutes to heat through. Stir in the lime juice and adjust the seasonings to taste. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, crushed tortilla chips, and shredded cheese. There you have it - three delicious and easy Instant Pot chicken tortilla soup recipes. Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew Recipe: A Hearty and Flavorful Option

There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting stew to satisfy your cravings on a chilly day. And when it comes to convenience and flavor, it’s hard to beat the Instant Pot. This versatile kitchen appliance allows you to create delicious meals in a fraction of the time, and today, we have a recipe that will surely become a favorite in your household: Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew. Ingredients 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 large onions, thinly sliced 3 cloves garlic, minced 4 carrots, peeled and sliced 3 celery stalks, diced 1 pound potatoes, cubed 4 cups beef broth 1 cup red wine (optional) 2 tablespoons tomato paste 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon dried thyme 1 tablespoon dried rosemary Salt and pepper, to taste 2 tablespoons olive oil Instructions Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the olive oil and allow it to heat up. Once the oil is hot, add the beef stew meat and brown it on all sides. This step will help seal in the flavor and keep the meat tender. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and set it aside. Add the onions and garlic to the pot and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant. Next, add the carrots, celery, and potatoes to the pot, and give them a quick stir to combine everything. Return the beef to the Instant Pot, along with any accumulated juices. Pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using), and stir in the tomato paste and Worcestershire sauce. Add the dried thyme and rosemary, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Give everything a good stir to ensure the flavors are well-distributed. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, making sure the valve is set to the sealing position. Select the “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 30 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes, and then carefully perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure. Give the stew a gentle stir, and if desired, you can thicken the sauce by selecting the “Sauté” function and simmering the stew for a few minutes until it reaches your desired consistency. Serve the Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. Pair it with crusty bread or fluffy mashed potatoes for a complete and satisfying meal. Why Choose Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew? There are several reasons why this Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew recipe deserves a spot in your regular meal rotation.
