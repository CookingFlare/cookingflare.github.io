Chicken and Rice Recipes for a Simple Dinner


Chicken and rice are two of the most beloved ingredients in the world, and they pair perfectly together to create hearty, flavorful meals that are easy to prepare. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a more elaborate weekend meal, chicken and rice can be customized in countless ways to suit your tastes. These recipes offer something for everyone, from savory, comforting dishes to zesty, flavorful options. If you’re craving a satisfying dinner with minimal effort, these chicken and rice recipes are sure to be a hit in your kitchen.

Why Chicken and Rice?

The combination of chicken and rice is not only delicious but also incredibly versatile. Chicken provides a lean source of protein, while rice offers carbohydrates that are perfect for filling you up. Together, they create a balanced meal that can be adapted to almost any cuisine or flavor profile. Plus, both ingredients are widely available, affordable, and can be prepared in a variety of ways, making them a perfect choice for a simple dinner.

Additionally, both chicken and rice are easy to cook in large quantities, making them ideal for meal prepping. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or feeding a family, you can scale the recipes to your needs. And because these dishes are often even more flavorful the next day, they make excellent leftovers. Now, let’s dive into some of the best chicken and rice recipes that will help you create simple, yet satisfying dinners.

1. Classic Chicken and Rice Casserole

This is the ultimate comfort food dish. A creamy, flavorful casserole that’s easy to assemble and bake, perfect for busy days or when you want a hassle-free dinner. This recipe combines tender chicken with creamy soup and seasoned rice to create a hearty, delicious meal that will leave you coming back for seconds.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 4 chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
  • 1 can of cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the chicken breasts over medium heat until golden and cooked through, about 7-10 minutes per side. Remove from heat and shred the chicken using two forks.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the chopped onion and garlic until softened and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked rice, shredded chicken, sautéed onion and garlic, cream of chicken soup, chicken broth, thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir until everything is well incorporated.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a greased 9x13-inch casserole dish. Top with shredded cheddar cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

This casserole is a great option for a quick dinner with minimal preparation, and it’s packed with rich, comforting flavors.

2. Chicken and Rice Stir-Fry

For those who love a bit of a kick, this chicken and rice stir-fry is a flavorful, vibrant option. The stir-fry method allows you to cook everything in one pan, making cleanup a breeze. The chicken is stir-fried with colorful vegetables and served with rice, creating a dish that’s both filling and nutritious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice (cold rice works best)
  • 1 red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil and sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced chicken to the skillet and stir-fry until browned and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the bell peppers, carrot, and peas to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Stir in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil, making sure the rice is well-coated.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat everything through.
  7. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds before serving.

This stir-fry is a delicious and quick dinner option that can be customized with any vegetables you have on hand. Plus, it’s a great way to use up leftover rice!

3. One-Pot Chicken and Rice

For a one-pot meal that requires minimal effort and cleanup, this one-pot chicken and rice recipe is perfect. The chicken is seared and then simmered with seasoned rice in one pan, allowing the flavors to meld together and create a satisfying meal with little fuss.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, paprika, oregano, and turmeric.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in the pot and sear them for about 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the diced onion and garlic. Sauté for 3-4 minutes until the onion becomes soft and translucent.
  4. Add the rice to the pot, stirring to coat it with the onion and garlic mixture.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. Place the chicken thighs back into the pot, nestling them on top of the rice.
  6. Cover the pot, reduce the heat to low, and cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and the rice is tender.
  7. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for an additional 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh cilantro before serving.

This recipe is a true one-pot wonder, combining tender chicken with fragrant rice and spices for a delicious, no-fuss meal.

4. Lemon Garlic Chicken and Rice

If you prefer a light and fresh flavor profile, this lemon garlic chicken and rice recipe is a fantastic choice. The zesty lemon and aromatic garlic pair perfectly with the savory chicken, creating a bright and refreshing dish that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, and cook them in the skillet for 6-8 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the garlic for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the rice to the skillet and stir it for a minute to coat the grains in the oil and garlic.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Return the chicken breasts to the skillet, placing them on top of the rice. Cover the skillet and cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.

This dish combines bright, citrusy flavors with savory chicken and fluffy rice, making it an ideal choice for a light yet satisfying dinner.

5. Chicken and Rice Soup

If you’re in the mood for something warm and comforting, chicken and rice soup is a perfect choice. This hearty, soothing soup is packed with tender chicken, rice, and vegetables in a savory broth, making it an ideal option for chilly evenings or when you’re feeling under the weather.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup celery, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery, and sauté for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.
  2. Pour in the chicken broth and add the bay leaf and thyme. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add the cooked rice and shredded chicken to the pot. Stir to combine and simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, until the soup is heated through and the flavors meld together.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and remove the bay leaf.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

This chicken and rice soup is a comforting, nutritious meal that’s perfect for warming up on a cold day.

These chicken and rice recipes offer a variety of options that can be easily adapted to suit your preferences and the ingredients you have on hand. From comforting casseroles to fresh and zesty stir-fries, there’s a recipe here for everyone. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight or preparing a leisurely weekend dinner, these dishes will help you create satisfying, flavorful meals with minimal effort. So grab your chicken and rice, and get cooking!
