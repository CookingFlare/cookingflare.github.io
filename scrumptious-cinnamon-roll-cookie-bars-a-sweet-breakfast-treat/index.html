Scrumptious Cinnamon Roll Cookie Bars: A Sweet Breakfast Treat


If you’re a fan of cinnamon rolls and cookies, then you’re in for a treat with these scrumptious cinnamon roll cookie bars! Imagine the delightful combination of soft, chewy cookie dough infused with the warm, comforting flavors of cinnamon and brown sugar, all topped with a luscious cream cheese frosting. These bars are the perfect sweet indulgence for breakfast, brunch, or anytime you’re craving a delectable treat.

Ingredients

For the Cookie Bars:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk

For the Cinnamon Swirl:

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.

  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk, and mix until well combined.

  4. In a small bowl, prepare the cinnamon swirl by mixing together the melted butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon.

  5. Spread two-thirds of the cookie dough into the prepared baking pan, pressing it down evenly. Drizzle the cinnamon swirl mixture over the dough, then drop spoonfuls of the remaining cookie dough on top. Use a knife to swirl the dough and cinnamon mixture together.

  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.

  7. While the bars are cooling, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a medium bowl, beat together the softened cream cheese and butter until smooth. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar and vanilla extract, beating until the frosting is creamy and spreadable.

  8. Once the bars have cooled, spread the cream cheese frosting over the top. For easier slicing, refrigerate the bars for about 30 minutes to allow the frosting to set.

  9. Cut into squares and serve these delightful cinnamon roll cookie bars for a sweet and satisfying breakfast or brunch.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, sprinkle chopped pecans or walnuts over the cream cheese frosting.
  • You can also add a hint of citrus by including a teaspoon of orange or lemon zest to the cookie dough.
  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, but they are best enjoyed fresh.

Enjoy the delightful blend of cinnamon, sugar, and cream cheese in every bite of these scrumptious cinnamon roll cookie bars. Whether you’re treating yourself to a lazy weekend morning or sharing them with loved ones, these bars are sure to become a sweet breakfast favorite. Indulge in the comforting flavors of a classic cinnamon roll in a convenient, handheld cookie bar form – it’s a delightful treat you won’t want to miss!
