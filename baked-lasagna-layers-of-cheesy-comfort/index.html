Baked Lasagna: Layers of Cheesy Comfort


Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is loved by people all over the world. There’s something truly comforting about the layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, and oozing cheese. Baked to perfection, this dish is a crowd-pleaser and perfect for any occasion. In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps to create your very own delicious and comforting baked lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon of dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 3 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and the beef is fully cooked.

  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and half of the grated Parmesan cheese. Mix well to create a creamy mixture.

  5. Now it’s time to assemble the lasagna. Start by spreading a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping them.

  6. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture evenly over the noodles. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese on top. Repeat this layering process, using up all the ingredients. Finish with a layer of meat sauce and sprinkle the remaining grated Parmesan cheese on top.

  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and place it in the preheated oven. Bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  8. Once the lasagna is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. This will allow the lasagna to set and make it easier to cut into neat slices.

  9. Serve the baked lasagna with a sprinkle of fresh basil leaves on top, if desired. This adds a touch of freshness and a burst of flavor.

Enjoy the Cheesy Comfort!

There you have it – a delicious and comforting baked lasagna recipe! The layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese come together to create a dish that is both satisfying and indulgent. Whether you’re serving it for a family dinner or bringing it to a potluck, this lasagna is sure to impress.

Feel free to get creative with this recipe by adding your own twist. You can incorporate vegetables like spinach or mushrooms into the sauce, or experiment with different types of cheese. The possibilities are endless!

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to dive into a world of cheesy comfort. Baked lasagna is a timeless classic that never fails to satisfy. Enjoy!
