Greek-style Lemon Garlic Grilled Chicken: A Juicy and Flavorful Recipe


Grilled chicken is a classic dish that never fails to impress. And when you combine the bold flavors of Greek cuisine with the tangy zest of lemon and garlic, you get a mouthwatering dish that will leave your taste buds begging for more. That’s exactly what you can expect from this Greek-style Lemon Garlic Grilled Chicken recipe. Juicy, flavorful, and incredibly satisfying, this recipe is a must-try for any chicken lover.

Ingredients

To make this delicious Greek-style Lemon Garlic Grilled Chicken, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.

  2. Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure it coats each piece evenly. Cover the dish or seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight if possible. This will allow the flavors to meld together and infuse the chicken.

  3. When you’re ready to cook the chicken, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using a charcoal grill, make sure the coals are hot and glowing.

  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade, allowing any excess marinade to drip off. Discard the remaining marinade.

  5. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). This will ensure that the chicken is cooked through and safe to eat.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in a moist and tender chicken.

  7. Serve the Greek-style Lemon Garlic Grilled Chicken with your favorite side dishes, such as a fresh Greek salad, grilled vegetables, or warm pita bread. The bold flavors of the marinade will complement any side dish and create a truly memorable meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can add some fresh herbs, such as chopped parsley or dill, to the marinade.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add a pinch of crushed red pepper flakes to the marinade.
  • This recipe works well with other cuts of chicken, such as chicken thighs or drumsticks. Just adjust the cooking time accordingly.
  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook the chicken in a grill pan or even bake it in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through.

Conclusion

Greek-style Lemon Garlic Grilled Chicken is a recipe that brings together the best of Greek flavors and the simplicity of grilled chicken. With its juicy texture, tangy lemon, and aromatic garlic, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re hosting a summer BBQ or simply looking for a delicious and healthy meal, this recipe is a winner. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every bite of this flavorful Greek-style Lemon Garlic Grilled Chicken. Enjoy!
