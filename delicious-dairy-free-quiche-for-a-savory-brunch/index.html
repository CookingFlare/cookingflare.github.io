Delicious Dairy-Free Quiche for a Savory Brunch


When it comes to brunch, there’s nothing quite like a delicious quiche to bring a touch of elegance to the table. However, for those with dairy allergies or dietary preferences, traditional quiche recipes can be off-limits due to their heavy reliance on milk, cream, and cheese. But fear not, because I’ve got a fantastic dairy-free quiche recipe that’s sure to impress even the most discerning brunch guests.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup tapioca flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon cold water

For the Filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chopped mixed vegetables (such as spinach, bell peppers, and mushrooms)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the almond flour, tapioca flour, melted coconut oil, and salt. Mix until the dough comes together.
  3. Add the cold water and continue mixing until a smooth dough forms.
  4. Press the dough into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish. Use a fork to prick the bottom of the crust to prevent air bubbles. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until lightly golden. Set aside to cool.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  2. Add the mixed vegetables, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 3: Assemble the Quiche

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, almond milk, nutritional yeast, and Dijon mustard until well combined.
  2. Spread the sautéed vegetable mixture evenly over the pre-baked crust.
  3. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables, ensuring it’s evenly distributed.

Step 4: Bake and Serve

  1. Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the center is set and the top is lightly golden.
  2. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize the vegetable filling based on your preferences or what’s in season.
  • If you’re not a fan of almond flour, you can use a gluten-free all-purpose flour for the crust.
  • For an extra flavor boost, consider adding a sprinkle of dairy-free cheese on top of the quiche before baking.

The Perfect Dairy-Free Brunch Option

This dairy-free quiche is a true crowd-pleaser, offering a flavorful and satisfying option for those avoiding dairy. With a golden, flaky crust and a hearty, veggie-packed filling, it’s a dish that’s sure to become a brunch favorite. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a special weekend meal, this dairy-free quiche is a savory delight that everyone can enjoy.

So, go ahead and whip up this delightful quiche for your next brunch gathering, and watch as it disappears in no time, earning you compliments and requests for the recipe. Happy brunching!
