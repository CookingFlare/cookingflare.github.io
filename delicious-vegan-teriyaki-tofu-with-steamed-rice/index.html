Delicious Vegan Teriyaki Tofu with Steamed Rice


If you’re looking for a flavorful and satisfying vegan meal, look no further than this delicious Vegan Teriyaki Tofu with Steamed Rice recipe. It’s a perfect combination of savory and sweet flavors that will leave you craving for more. Tofu, often known as the “meat of the vegetable world,” is an incredibly versatile ingredient that absorbs flavors like a sponge, making it the perfect star of this dish. Paired with steamed rice, this dish is a complete and wholesome meal that’s easy to prepare and guaranteed to impress even the most discerning of palates.

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 1 cup of uncooked jasmine rice
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce (or tamari for a gluten-free option)
  • 1/4 cup of rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Tofu:

  • Begin by pressing the block of tofu to remove excess water. Once pressed, cut the tofu into cubes or slices, according to your preference.

2. Cooking the Rice:

  • Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the rinsed rice with 1 1/2 cups of water. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the water has been absorbed.

3. Making the Teriyaki Sauce:

  • In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce (or tamari), rice vinegar, maple syrup, minced garlic, and grated ginger. In a separate small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water until the cornstarch is fully dissolved.

4. Cooking the Tofu:

  • In a large non-stick skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes or slices to the skillet and cook until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Once the tofu is cooked, pour the teriyaki sauce over the tofu and stir gently to coat the tofu evenly. Allow the sauce to simmer and thicken for a few minutes.

5. Serving:

  • Fluff the steamed rice with a fork and divide it among serving plates. Top the rice with the teriyaki tofu, then garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.

Enjoy your delicious Vegan Teriyaki Tofu with Steamed Rice! This dish is perfect for a cozy night in or for impressing your friends and family with a delightful vegan meal. The rich flavors of the teriyaki sauce combined with the tender tofu and fluffy steamed rice make this dish a true winner in both taste and presentation.

This Vegan Teriyaki Tofu with Steamed Rice recipe is a testament to the fact that vegan cooking can be both nutritious and utterly delicious. With its simple preparation and extraordinary flavors, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your ingredients, and get ready to create a culinary masterpiece that will delight vegans and non-vegans alike.
