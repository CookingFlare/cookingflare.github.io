Savory Cheddar and Chive Breakfast Biscuits


Breakfast is often hailed as the most important meal of the day, and for good reason! It sets the tone for the rest of your day, providing the energy and nutrients you need to tackle whatever lies ahead. If you’re looking to elevate your breakfast game, these Savory Cheddar and Chive Breakfast Biscuits are a perfect choice. Fluffy, buttery, and bursting with flavor, these biscuits are sure to become a staple in your morning routine.

Ingredients

To create these delightful biscuits, you’ll need the following ingredients:

For the Biscuits:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • ¼ cup fresh chives, chopped
  • ¾ cup buttermilk (or regular milk with 1 tablespoon vinegar added)

For the Topping:

  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons additional shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped chives

Equipment Needed

  • Mixing bowls
  • Whisk
  • Pastry cutter or fork
  • Baking sheet
  • Parchment paper (optional)
  • Rolling pin (optional)
  • Biscuit cutter or a round glass

Preparation Time

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30-35 minutes

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 425°F (220°C). This high temperature is crucial for achieving those deliciously flaky layers in your biscuits.

Step 2: Combine Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. This step is essential as it ensures that the leavening agents are evenly distributed throughout the flour.

Step 3: Cut in the Butter

Add the chilled, cubed butter into the flour mixture. Using a pastry cutter or a fork, cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. You want to see some small, pea-sized pieces of butter remaining, as this will help create those flaky layers.

Step 4: Add Cheese and Chives

Next, fold in the shredded cheddar cheese and chopped chives. Make sure they are evenly distributed throughout the mixture. The cheddar adds a rich, savory flavor, while the chives bring a fresh, onion-like taste that complements the cheese beautifully.

Step 5: Incorporate Wet Ingredients

Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the buttermilk. Use a wooden spoon or spatula to gently mix the ingredients until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are perfectly fine. The dough should be slightly sticky but manageable.

Step 6: Shape the Biscuits

Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface. Gently knead it a few times until it comes together. Pat the dough into a rectangle about 1 inch thick. If you prefer a more rustic look, you can simply cut the dough into squares. For a traditional biscuit shape, use a biscuit cutter or a round glass to cut out your biscuits.

Step 7: Arrange and Bake

Place the cut biscuits onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving about an inch of space between each one. For an extra touch, brush the tops of the biscuits with melted butter and sprinkle with additional cheddar cheese and chopped chives.

Step 8: Bake to Perfection

Bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the biscuits are golden brown on top and cooked through. The aroma that fills your kitchen will be irresistible!

Step 9: Cool and Serve

Once baked, remove the biscuits from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes. Serve warm, and enjoy them fresh out of the oven. These biscuits are delicious on their own or can be paired with butter, honey, or your favorite jam.

Tips for Perfect Biscuits

  • Keep Ingredients Cold: Make sure your butter and buttermilk are cold to create the best texture in your biscuits. This helps in achieving that tender, flaky quality.
  • Don’t Overwork the Dough: Mix until just combined. Overworking the dough can lead to tough biscuits.
  • Experiment with Add-ins: Feel free to customize your biscuits with additional ingredients. Diced ham, cooked bacon, or even sun-dried tomatoes can add unique flavors.

Nutritional Benefits

These Savory Cheddar and Chive Breakfast Biscuits not only taste delicious but also offer several nutritional benefits:

  • Cheddar Cheese: A great source of calcium and protein, cheddar cheese adds creaminess and depth of flavor.
  • Chives: These green herbs are low in calories and rich in vitamins A and C, making them a nutritious addition.
  • Whole Grain Option: For a healthier twist, consider substituting half of the all-purpose flour with whole wheat flour for added fiber.

Storage Tips

If you have leftovers (which is rare, but possible!), store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to two days. To enjoy them later, reheat the biscuits in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 5-10 minutes until warmed through. These biscuits also freeze well. Simply wrap them tightly and store in the freezer for up to a month. When you’re ready to enjoy them, thaw and reheat as described above.

Conclusion

Savory Cheddar and Chive Breakfast Biscuits are a delightful way to start your day. With their fluffy texture and rich flavors, they’re sure to impress your family and friends. Whether served alongside eggs, as part of a breakfast spread, or enjoyed on their own, these biscuits are both versatile and satisfying. Easy to make and even easier to devour, they are a must-try for any breakfast lover. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to bake your way to a fantastic breakfast experience!

Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences, and remember: baking is all about having fun in the kitchen. Enjoy your savory creations!
