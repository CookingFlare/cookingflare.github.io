Savory Thai Peanut Noodles: A Nutty Indulgence


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this delectable recipe for Savory Thai Peanut Noodles. Bursting with bold flavors and a delightful nutty twist, this dish is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings and transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Thailand. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Peanut Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/4 cup warm water

For the Noodles:

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together all the ingredients for the peanut sauce until smooth and well combined. Set aside.

  2. Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced bell pepper, julienned carrot, shredded cabbage, and bean sprouts. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Add the cooked rice noodles to the skillet and pour the peanut sauce over them. Toss well to coat the noodles and vegetables evenly with the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat everything through.

  5. Remove the skillet from heat and garnish the noodles with chopped cilantro and peanuts. Give it a final squeeze of lime juice for an extra zing of flavor.

  6. Serve the Savory Thai Peanut Noodles warm, with lime wedges on the side for an added burst of freshness. Enjoy it as a main course or as a side dish alongside your favorite Thai curry or grilled meat.

A Nutty Indulgence

The combination of creamy peanut butter, tangy soy sauce, and aromatic sesame oil creates a rich and luscious sauce that clings to every strand of the rice noodles. The medley of colorful vegetables adds a refreshing crunch, while the hint of lime juice brightens up the entire dish. The chopped peanuts sprinkled on top provide a satisfying crunch and a burst of nuttiness in every bite.

Whether you’re a seasoned Thai food enthusiast or looking to explore new flavors, these Savory Thai Peanut Noodles are a must-try. It’s a dish that effortlessly balances sweet, savory, and tangy flavors, creating a harmonious symphony in your mouth.

So, gather your ingredients and embark on a culinary adventure with this recipe. Indulge in the nutty flavors of Thailand and let your taste buds dance with delight. You won’t be disappointed!
