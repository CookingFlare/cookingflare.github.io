5 Simple and Affordable Vegetable Side Dish Recipes


Are you looking to add more vegetables to your meals without breaking the bank? Look no further! Here are 5 simple and affordable vegetable side dish recipes that will elevate your dining experience and keep your wallet happy.

1. Garlic Parmesan Roasted Broccoli

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, toss the broccoli florets with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the broccoli on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and slightly crispy.
  4. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with grated parmesan cheese before serving.

This simple and flavorful dish is a perfect accompaniment to any main course.

2. Honey Glazed Carrots

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound baby carrots
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil and add the baby carrots. Cook for 5-7 minutes or until slightly tender.
  2. Drain the carrots and return them to the pot.
  3. Add honey, butter, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt to the pot with the carrots.
  4. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, for 5-7 minutes until the carrots are glazed and tender.

These honey glazed carrots are a delightful combination of sweet and savory, making them a crowd-pleaser at any gathering.

3. Zucchini Parmesan Crisps

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, sliced into rounds
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine parmesan cheese, breadcrumbs, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip each zucchini round into the cheese and breadcrumb mixture, ensuring it is coated evenly on both sides.
  4. Place the coated zucchini rounds on the prepared baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchinis are golden and crispy.

These zucchini parmesan crisps are a delightful alternative to traditional potato chips and make for a crunchy and satisfying side dish.

4. Lemon Garlic Roasted Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the asparagus on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Sprinkle minced garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper over the asparagus.
  3. Toss the asparagus to ensure even coating, then spread them out in a single layer.
  4. Roast for 12-15 minutes, or until the asparagus is tender but still crisp.

This lemon garlic roasted asparagus is a bright and refreshing addition to any meal.

5. Skillet Corn with Chili and Lime

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, melt the butter.
  2. Add the corn kernels and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the corn is tender and slightly charred.
  3. Sprinkle chili powder over the corn and stir to combine.
  4. Squeeze the lime juice over the corn and toss to coat evenly.

This skillet corn with chili and lime is a zesty and vibrant side dish that pairs perfectly with grilled meats or tacos.

These 5 simple and affordable vegetable side dish recipes are not only budget-friendly but also packed with flavor and nutrients. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a gathering, these dishes are sure to impress. Enjoy experimenting with these recipes and making them your own!
