Delicious Instant Pot Pork Recipes to Satisfy Your Cravings


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying pork dish? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore a variety of mouth-watering Instant Pot pork recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. The Instant Pot is a versatile kitchen appliance that allows you to cook meals quickly and easily, making it the perfect tool for creating flavorful pork dishes. So, dust off your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in these delectable recipes!

1. Pulled Pork Tacos

First up, we have pulled pork tacos, a crowd-pleasing favorite. To make this savory dish, start by seasoning a pork shoulder with a blend of spices such as paprika, cumin, garlic powder, and salt. Place the seasoned pork shoulder in the Instant Pot along with some chicken broth and cook on high pressure for about 90 minutes. Once the pork is tender and easily shreds, remove it from the Instant Pot and shred it using two forks. Serve the pulled pork in warm tortillas, and garnish with your favorite toppings like salsa, avocado, and cilantro. These tacos are sure to be a hit at your next gathering!

2. Honey Garlic Pork Chops

Next on our list is honey garlic pork chops, a sweet and savory delight. Begin by searing the pork chops on both sides in the Instant Pot using the sauté function. Once the chops are nicely browned, remove them from the pot and set them aside. In the same pot, combine honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, and a pinch of red pepper flakes. Return the pork chops to the Instant Pot and cook on high pressure for about 10 minutes. The result? Tender, juicy pork chops with a sticky and flavorful glaze. Serve these mouth-watering chops with steamed vegetables and rice for a complete meal.

3. BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

If you’re a fan of BBQ, then you’ll love these BBQ pulled pork sliders. Start by seasoning a pork loin with salt, pepper, and your favorite BBQ rub. Place the seasoned pork loin in the Instant Pot along with some BBQ sauce and chicken broth. Cook on high pressure for about 60 minutes until the pork is tender and easily shreds. Once cooked, remove the pork from the Instant Pot and shred it using two forks. Toss the shredded pork in additional BBQ sauce for extra flavor. Serve the pulled pork on small slider buns, and top with coleslaw for a refreshing crunch. These sliders are perfect for game day or any casual gathering.

4. Asian-Inspired Pork Stir-Fry

For those craving a taste of the East, this Asian-inspired pork stir-fry is a must-try! Start by sautéing sliced pork tenderloin in the Instant Pot until browned. Then, add a mix of colorful vegetables such as bell peppers, snow peas, and carrots. In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and a touch of honey. Pour the sauce over the pork and vegetables in the Instant Pot. Cook on high pressure for a few minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender and the pork is cooked through. Serve this flavorful stir-fry over steamed rice or noodles for a satisfying meal.

5. Cuban Mojo Pork

Last but certainly not least, we have Cuban mojo pork, a dish bursting with zesty flavors. Begin by marinating a pork shoulder in a mixture of orange juice, lime juice, garlic, cumin, and oregano for at least 4 hours or overnight. Place the marinated pork shoulder in the Instant Pot along with the marinade and cook on high pressure for about 90 minutes. Once cooked, remove the pork from the Instant Pot and shred it using two forks. Serve the succulent mojo pork with black beans, rice, and plantains for a traditional Cuban feast.

Now that you have a variety of delicious Instant Pot pork recipes to choose from, it’s time to get cooking! Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a satisfying meal, these recipes are sure to impress. So, grab your Instant Pot and let the flavors unfold. Bon appétit!
