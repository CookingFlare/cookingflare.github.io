Instant Pot Honey Mustard Chicken: Sweet and Tangy


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy weeknight dinner, look no further than this Instant Pot Honey Mustard Chicken recipe. The combination of sweet and tangy flavors makes this dish a crowd-pleaser, and the best part is that it can be ready in no time thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. This simple step ensures that the chicken is flavorful from the inside out.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, and paprika. This luscious mixture will be the star of the show, infusing the chicken with its irresistible flavor.

  3. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and add a drizzle of olive oil. Once the pot is hot, add the seasoned chicken breasts and sear them for 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a golden brown crust. This step not only locks in the juices but also adds a depth of flavor to the dish.

  4. After the chicken is golden brown, pour in the chicken broth to deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. This step ensures that no flavor goes to waste and adds richness to the sauce.

  5. Pour the honey mustard mixture over the chicken, ensuring that each piece is generously coated. The aroma that fills the kitchen at this point is simply irresistible.

  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Pressure cook on high for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes before performing a quick pressure release.

  7. Carefully remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and set it aside. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Set the Instant Pot to the sauté function and stir in the slurry. Allow the sauce to thicken, stirring constantly for 2-3 minutes.

  8. Return the chicken to the Instant Pot, coating it with the luscious honey mustard sauce. This step ensures that each bite is filled with the delightful flavors of the dish.

  9. Serve the Instant Pot Honey Mustard Chicken over cooked rice, quinoa, or with a side of roasted vegetables. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and a hint of freshness.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot Honey Mustard Chicken recipe is a game-changer for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. The sweet and tangy flavors of the honey mustard sauce combined with the tender, juicy chicken create a palate-pleasing experience that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Plus, the ease of preparation and the quick cooking time make this dish a go-to option for any occasion. Give this recipe a try, and prepare to be amazed by the delightful flavors that unfold with each bite.

Remember, the key to a successful Instant Pot Honey Mustard Chicken lies in the quality of the ingredients and the love you put into the cooking process. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor a meal that’s as easy to make as it is delicious to eat. Cheers to creating unforgettable culinary moments with this delightful recipe!
