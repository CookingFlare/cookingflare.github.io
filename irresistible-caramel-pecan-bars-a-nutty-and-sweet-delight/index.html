Irresistible Caramel Pecan Bars: A Nutty and Sweet Delight


If you’re a fan of decadent, nutty, and sweet treats, then you’re in for a real treat with this recipe for Irresistible Caramel Pecan Bars. These bars are the perfect combination of buttery shortbread, rich caramel, and crunchy pecans, creating a mouthwatering dessert that will have everyone coming back for more. Whether you’re looking for a delicious addition to your dessert table or a sweet indulgence for yourself, these bars are sure to impress. So, let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to indulge in this delightful treat.

Ingredients:

For the Shortbread Crust:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Caramel Layer:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup light corn syrup
  • 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Pecan Topping:

  • 2 cups pecan halves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan and line it with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. Make the Shortbread Crust: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the flour and salt, and mix until the dough comes together. Press the dough evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.

  3. Prepare the Caramel Layer: In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter, brown sugar, and corn syrup, stirring constantly. Once the mixture is smooth, add the sweetened condensed milk and continue to cook, stirring constantly, until the caramel thickens and turns a light golden color, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract and salt. Pour the caramel over the cooled shortbread crust, spreading it into an even layer.

  4. Add the Pecan Topping: Sprinkle the pecan halves evenly over the caramel layer, gently pressing them into the caramel.

  5. Bake and Cool: Return the pan to the oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the caramel is bubbly and the pecans are lightly toasted. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once completely cooled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the bars out of the pan. Place them on a cutting board and cut into squares or bars. These Irresistible Caramel Pecan Bars are ready to be enjoyed!

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra indulgent twist, drizzle melted chocolate over the cooled bars before cutting them into squares.
  • You can substitute pecans with other nuts like walnuts or almonds for a different flavor profile.
  • Store the bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 1 week, or in the refrigerator for longer shelf life.

Final Thoughts:

Irresistible Caramel Pecan Bars are a delightful combination of sweet, buttery, and nutty flavors that make them a perfect choice for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, celebrating a special milestone, or simply craving a delicious homemade treat, these bars are a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. With a buttery shortbread crust, luscious caramel layer, and crunchy pecan topping, these bars are a true indulgence that will leave everyone asking for more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the irresistible delight of these Caramel Pecan Bars. Enjoy!
