Tahini-lating: 6 Sesame Seed-based Juice & Smoothie Recipes


If you’ve never ventured into the world of tahini, you’re missing out on one of life’s greatest joys. Sesame seeds are tiny but mighty, packing big flavor in their small size. These nutty, earthy seeds make for a delightful addition to juices and smoothies, lending depth, texture, and a slight kick to your favorite beverages. Today, we’re diving headfirst into six enticing sesame seed-based concoctions that’ll tantalize your taste buds and have you hooked on tahini. So, grab your blender, juicer, and taste buds, and let’s get started.

1. The Zen Smoothie

Move over, matcha, because the new kid in town is the Zen Smoothie. Combining tahini with banana and avocado guarantees a silky smooth texture and a boost of omega-3 fatty acids, while spinach adds a freshness that soothes the soul.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • ½ cup spinach
  • ½ avocado
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • ½ cup non-dairy milk (almond, soy, or oat)
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to your blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, adding more non-dairy milk if needed for consistency.
  3. Optional: For added sweetness, drizzle in a teaspoon of honey.

2. Gingered Tahini Glow Juice

Tired of the same old green juices? Try a powerful and exhilarating blend of pineapple, ginger, and tahini that’ll leave you shining like the sun. This juice is a worthy burst of nutrients, revitalizing your body and spirit.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pineapple
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 cup spinach
  • ½ cup kale
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • ¼ cup water

Instructions:

  1. Juice pineapple, ginger, spinach, and kale, reserving the juices separately.
  2. Transfer the juices into a blender.
  3. Add tahini and water, and blend until smooth.
  4. Pour and savor your Gingered Tahini Glow Juice.

3. Creamsicle Cooler Smoothie

Who says creamsicles are just for kids? Dive into this grown-up twist combining velvety tahini, creamy coconut milk, and refreshing oranges and pineapple. This dreamy concoction will transport you to your happy place.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 oranges, peeled
  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • ½ cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add ingredients to your blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed.
  3. Pour and indulge in this Creamsicle Cooler Smoothie.

4. Green Goddess Smoothie Bowl

Get your green on with this Green Goddess Smoothie Bowl. Rich in protein and healthy fats, this bowl features tahini alongside a powerhouse mix of kale, avocado, and coconut milk, all topped with tropical fruits and crunchy seeds. Your new breakfast bestie is here.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup kale
  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • ½ avocado
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon hemp seeds
  • ½ banana
  • ¼ cup assorted tropical fruits (mango, kiwi, pineapple)

Instructions:

  1. Add kale, coconut milk, avocado, tahini, chia seeds, hemp seeds, and half of the banana to your blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a bowl, top with remaining banana and tropical fruits, and sprinkle with seeds.

5. Tahini Turmeric Tonic

Embrace the golden paste, turmeric, which combines beautifully with tahini to create a blissful immune and anti-inflammatory tonic. This smooth, creamy, and warming drink will become your go-to remedy for those chilly winter days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • 1-inch piece of ginger (peeled)
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Add almond milk, turmeric, ginger, tahini, and honey to a small saucepan.
  2. Heat gently over low heat, stirring well to combine and melt the honey.
  3. Transfer mixture to a blender for a frothy tonic.
  4. Pour and sip your healing, comforting Tahini Turmeric Tonic.

6. The Sesame Spin

Feeling adventurous and craving a burst of Southeast Asian flavors? Look no further than The Sesame Spin - a delicious fusion of sesame seeds, tahini, and antioxidant-rich berries. This wholesome smoothie is a testament to the versatility of sesame seeds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, strawberries)
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon raw honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add mixed berries, almond milk, tahini, sesame seeds, and honey to your blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, adding ice cubes if desired.
  3. Pour and sip this luscious, antioxidant-rich The Sesame Spin smoothie.

With these six tahini-infused recipes, your taste buds have a lot to look forward to. Creamy, nutty, and earthy, tahini has stolen our hearts and will undoubtedly steal yours, too. So, don’t shy away from that jar of tahini in your pantry. Embrace its glory and enjoy the growth of your culinary repertoire. Happy blending!
