Homemade Chocolate Chip Banana Bread


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting treat, look no further than Homemade Chocolate Chip Banana Bread. This classic recipe combines the sweetness of ripe bananas with the richness of chocolate chips, resulting in a moist and flavorful loaf that is perfect for breakfast, dessert, or anytime in between.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring each egg is fully incorporated before adding the next.
  5. Add the mashed bananas, milk, and vanilla extract to the mixing bowl. Mix until well combined.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until the flour disappears.
  7. Gently fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it evenly.
  9. Bake for 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Allow the banana bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a teaspoon of cinnamon or a pinch of nutmeg to the dry ingredients.
  • If you prefer a nutty texture, fold in 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts or pecans along with the chocolate chips.
  • To make this recipe vegan-friendly, substitute the butter with coconut oil and use a flaxseed egg (1 tablespoon ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons water) in place of the eggs.
  • This banana bread can be stored at room temperature in an airtight container for up to 3 days, or refrigerated for up to a week.

Why Homemade Chocolate Chip Banana Bread?

There’s something truly special about the combination of ripe bananas and chocolate chips. The sweetness of the bananas adds a natural and comforting element to the bread, while the chocolate chips provide bursts of rich, indulgent flavor. This recipe is a great way to use up those overripe bananas sitting on your kitchen counter, transforming them into a delicious treat that the whole family will love.

Moreover, this Homemade Chocolate Chip Banana Bread is incredibly versatile. It can be enjoyed as a satisfying breakfast option, served with a cup of coffee or tea, or as a delightful dessert, accompanied by a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Its moist and tender texture, along with the melty chocolate chips, make it an irresistible treat for any time of the day.

So, if you’re craving a comforting and delicious baked good, give this Homemade Chocolate Chip Banana Bread a try. With its simple ingredients and easy-to-follow instructions, you’ll have a mouthwatering loaf of bread in no time. Enjoy!
