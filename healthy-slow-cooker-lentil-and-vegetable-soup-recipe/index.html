Healthy Slow Cooker Lentil and Vegetable Soup Recipe


If you’re looking for a hearty and nutritious meal that practically cooks itself, look no further than this Healthy Slow Cooker Lentil and Vegetable Soup recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and an array of vitamins and minerals, this soup is not only delicious but also incredibly good for you. Plus, it’s a breeze to prepare, making it the perfect option for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups chopped kale or spinach
  • Fresh lemon juice, for serving
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prep the Ingredients: Start by rinsing the lentils under cold water and draining them. Chop the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic.

  2. Combine Ingredients in the Slow Cooker: In the slow cooker, combine the lentils, chopped onion, carrots, celery, garlic, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, ground cumin, ground coriander, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.

  3. Cook: Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, or until the lentils and vegetables are tender.

  4. Add Greens: About 30 minutes before serving, stir in the chopped kale or spinach and let it cook until wilted.

  5. Serve: Once the soup is ready, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Squeeze a bit of fresh lemon juice over each serving and garnish with fresh parsley.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For added protein, you can stir in a can of chickpeas or cannellini beans during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on what you have on hand or what’s in season. Bell peppers, zucchini, or even sweet potatoes would all be great additions.
  • This soup freezes well, so consider making a double batch and freezing individual portions for quick and easy meals later on.

Health Benefits:

This Healthy Slow Cooker Lentil and Vegetable Soup is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. Lentils are an excellent source of plant-based protein and fiber, which can help with digestion and promote a feeling of fullness. The array of vegetables in this soup provides a wide range of vitamins and minerals, making it a great way to boost your immune system and overall health.

Conclusion:

This Healthy Slow Cooker Lentil and Vegetable Soup recipe is a must-try for anyone looking for a wholesome, satisfying meal that’s easy to prepare. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your collection. Plus, it’s a fantastic way to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet without sacrificing flavor or satisfaction. Give it a try and enjoy a warm, comforting bowl of goodness!
