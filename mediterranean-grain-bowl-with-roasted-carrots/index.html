Mediterranean Grain Bowl with Roasted Carrots


As the seasons change and winter begins to set in, we start to crave heartwarming, comforting dishes that nourish both body and soul. One of my favorite recipes that fits the bill is this Mediterranean Grain Bowl with Roasted Carrots, a flavorful and nutritious bowl that's perfect for a chilly evening or a quick, easy lunch. The Recipe This recipe serves 4-6 people and can be easily scaled up or down depending on your needs. For the Quinoa and Grain Mixture:

1 cup of quinoa, rinsed and drained 1 cup of brown rice 1 cup of bulgur 2 tablespoons of olive oil 1 teaspoon of ground cumin 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika Salt and pepper to taste

For the Roasted Carrots:

4-5 large carrots, peeled and chopped into 1-inch pieces 2 tablespoons of olive oil 1 teaspoon of ground cumin 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika Salt and pepper to taste 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese (optional)

For the Topping:

1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese (optional) 1/4 cup of chopped scallions 1/4 cup of chopped fresh mint 2 tablespoons of lemon juice

Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Spread the chopped carrots on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat evenly and roast in the oven for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and caramelized. Cook the quinoa, brown rice, and bulgur according to package instructions. Typically, this will involve combining the grains with water in a saucepan, bringing to a boil, covering, and simmering until the liquid is absorbed and the grains are cooked. Alternatively, you can cook the grains in a rice cooker or instant pot for added convenience. Fluff the cooked quinoa, brown rice, and bulgur with a fork. In a large bowl, combine the fluffed grains and stir in olive oil, ground cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Assemble the bowls. Divide the quinoa-grain mixture among four to six bowls. Top each bowl with a generous portion of roasted carrots, parsley, feta cheese (if using), and a sprinkle of scallions. Finish with a drizzle of lemon juice. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top of each bowl and garnish with chopped mint.

The Story Behind the Recipe I first discovered this recipe on a visit to the Mediterranean coast, where street food vendors offered a hearty, flavorful bowl of quinoa, roasted vegetables, and tangy cheese. It was love at first bite! The combination of smoky spices, bright citrus, and satisfying textures had me hooked. I set out to recreate this dish at home, experimenting with different grain combinations and roasted vegetables until I landed on this recipe. Why This Recipe Works This Mediterranean Grain Bowl with Roasted Carrots is an excellent option for several reasons:

It's nutritious. Quinoa, brown rice, and bulgur provide a complete protein, while the carrots offer a boost of vitamins A and K. Feta cheese adds a tangy touch of calcium and protein. It's easy to customize. Feel free to swap out the carrots for your favorite roasted vegetables, or add some sautéed spinach or kale for added nutrition. It's perfect for meal prep. Cook the quinoa, rice, and bulgur in advance and refrigerate or freeze for up to three days. Simply reheat and top with roasted carrots and fresh herbs when you're ready.

Variations and Substitutions

Swap out the carrots for parsnips, sweet potatoes, or Brussels sprouts for added flavor and texture. Use chopped almonds or pistachios instead of scallions for added crunch. Substitute goat cheese or feta with a vegan alternative for a dairy-free option. Add some heat with a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of harissa.

The Bottom Line This Mediterranean Grain Bowl with Roasted Carrots is a hearty, satisfying dish that's perfect for the cooler months. With its rich flavors and satisfying texture, it's sure to become a new favorite in your household. Experiment with different grains, roasted vegetables, and toppings to make this recipe your own, and enjoy the benefits of a balanced, wholesome bowl that will keep you coming back for more.
