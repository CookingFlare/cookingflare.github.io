Luscious Strawberry Cheesecake Cupcakes: Creamy and Fruity


If you’re a fan of strawberries and cheesecake, then these luscious strawberry cheesecake cupcakes are the perfect treat for you. With a creamy cheesecake filling and a burst of fruity strawberry flavor, these cupcakes are sure to satisfy your dessert cravings.

Ingredients

For the cupcake batter:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk

For the cheesecake filling:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

For the strawberry topping:

  • 1 ½ cups fresh strawberries, diced
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Mix until just combined, being careful not to overmix.

  5. In another bowl, prepare the cheesecake filling by beating the softened cream cheese, granulated sugar, egg, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  6. Fill each cupcake liner halfway with the cupcake batter. Then, spoon a dollop of the cheesecake filling on top of the batter in each liner.

  7. Place the cupcakes in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Remove from the oven and let cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the strawberry topping. In a small bowl, combine the diced strawberries and granulated sugar. Stir until the sugar has dissolved and the strawberries are coated. Let the mixture sit for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  9. Once the cupcakes have cooled, spoon a generous amount of the strawberry topping onto each cupcake. You can also garnish with additional fresh strawberries if desired.

  10. Serve and enjoy these luscious strawberry cheesecake cupcakes! They are best enjoyed on the day they are made, but can also be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Conclusion

These luscious strawberry cheesecake cupcakes are a delightful combination of creamy cheesecake and fruity strawberries. The moist and fluffy cupcake base provides the perfect canvas for the creamy cheesecake filling, while the fresh strawberry topping adds a burst of sweetness and tanginess. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply indulging in a sweet treat, these cupcakes are sure to impress. So grab your apron and get ready to enjoy the blissful combination of flavors in these delightful strawberry cheesecake cupcakes!
