Flavorful Instant Pot Beef Stew Recipe for Busy Weeknights


Are you looking for a hearty and delicious meal to warm you up on a busy weeknight? Look no further than this Flavorful Instant Pot Beef Stew recipe. Packed with tender beef, hearty vegetables, and savory flavors, this stew is the perfect comfort food to enjoy with your family after a long day. The best part? It’s made in the Instant Pot, which means you can have a wholesome homemade meal on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 carrots, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Beef: Set the Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and heat the olive oil. Add the beef cubes and sear until browned on all sides. This step adds depth of flavor to the stew.

  2. Saute Aromatics: Add the minced garlic and diced onion to the pot and saute until fragrant, about 2 minutes.

  3. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the red wine (if using) and scrape the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. This step enhances the richness of the stew.

  4. Add Remaining Ingredients: Stir in the carrots, potatoes, celery, beef broth, tomato paste, bay leaves, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  5. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” setting and set the timer for 35 minutes on high pressure.

  6. Natural Release: Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 15 minutes. Then carefully perform a quick pressure release.

  7. Thicken the Stew: In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Set the Instant Pot to “Saute” mode again and stir in the slurry. Let the stew simmer for a few minutes until it thickens.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Discard the bay leaves and ladle the flavorful beef stew into bowls. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra boost of flavor, you can add a splash of Worcestershire sauce or balsamic vinegar to the stew before pressure cooking.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences. You can add mushrooms, parsnips, or even butternut squash for a unique twist.
  • This stew tastes even better the next day, so don’t hesitate to make a big batch for leftovers.

Final Thoughts

This Flavorful Instant Pot Beef Stew is a game-changer for busy weeknights. It’s a one-pot wonder that delivers on both flavor and convenience. The tender beef, savory broth, and hearty vegetables come together to create a soul-warming dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Plus, the Instant Pot makes the cooking process a breeze, allowing you to spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying a wholesome meal with your loved ones. So, the next time you’re craving a comforting dinner without the fuss, give this recipe a try and savor every spoonful of this delightful stew.
