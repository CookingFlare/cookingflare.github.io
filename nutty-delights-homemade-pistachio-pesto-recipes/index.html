Nutty Delights: Homemade Pistachio Pesto Recipes


Pesto, a versatile and delectable sauce that originates from Genoa, Italy, has certainly made its mark on culinary scenes around the world. With a few key ingredients, you can whip up homemade pistachio pesto to elevate your dishes and tantalize your taste buds. This nutty delight offers a unique twist on the classic_configs basil pesto, perfect for those craving something extraordinary. Let’s dive into a couple of delightful pistachio pesto recipes that will fill your kitchen with aromatic goodness.

Creamy Pistachio Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of fresh shelled pistachios
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of walnuts
  • 1/2 cup of freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 3 cups of fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic (about 2 cloves)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Gather all your ingredients and prepare a large mixing bowl for the pesto.
  2. In a food processor, pulse pistachios, walnuts, garlic, basil leaves and grated cheese until coarsely ground. Set aside.
  3. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and cook pasta according to package instructions.
  4. Meanwhile, slowly add olive oil and water into the nut mixture while processing until smooth.
  5. Once pasta is cooked, drain and return it to the large pot.
  6. Add the nutty pesto to the pasta and toss to coat thoroughly.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper for added flavor and serve while warm.

Pistachio Pesto Stuffed Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves *1 cup dry bread crumbs *1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese *1/4 cup fresh Italian herbs (basil, parsley, oregano) *1/4 cup freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese *1/4 cup of homemade pistachio pesto *Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. With a sharp knife, create a horizontal cut in the chicken breast, stopping just shy of through the other side.
  2. Gently open the chicken breast and stuff about 2 teaspoons of pistachio pesto into the pocket.
  3. To secure, place a toothpick through the chicken to hold contents in place. Repeat with remaining chicken breasts.
  4. In a shallow dish or bowl, whisk together bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, and Italian herbs.
  5. Season chicken with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  6. Dip the stuffed chicken breasts into the breadcrumb mixture and press the mixture onto the chicken, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  7. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and prepare to cook the chicken in a light oil or butter.
  8. Cook the chicken until cooked through and golden brown, approximately 15 minutes per side.
  9. Remove the toothpicks, let the chicken rest for a minute, then serve and enjoy!

Don’t forget to portion out some extra pesto for future culinary creations or simply enjoy with fresh crusty bread. Happy cooking!
