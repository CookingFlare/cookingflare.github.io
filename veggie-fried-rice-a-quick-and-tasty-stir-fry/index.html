Veggie Fried Rice: A Quick and Tasty Stir-Fry


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy dish that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than Veggie Fried Rice! This quick and tasty stir-fry is packed with colorful vegetables, aromatic flavors, and a perfect balance of textures. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably jasmine or basmati)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup diced zucchini
  • 1 cup diced broccoli florets
  • 1 cup diced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
  • Optional: chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the diced carrots, bell peppers, zucchini, broccoli, mushrooms, and frozen peas to the skillet. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the cooked rice to the empty space. Use a spatula to break up any clumps and stir-fry the rice for 2-3 minutes until heated through.

  5. Drizzle the soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice and vegetables. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Stir everything together until well combined.

  6. Remove the skillet from heat and garnish with chopped green onions, if desired.

  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a protein-packed version, you can add tofu, tempeh, or cooked edamame to the stir-fry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables according to your preference. You can include snap peas, corn, or even thinly sliced cabbage.
  • To make it gluten-free, use tamari or coconut aminos instead of soy sauce.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a dash of sriracha or red pepper flakes to the stir-fry.
  • Leftovers can be refrigerated for up to 3 days and reheated in a skillet or microwave.

Why Veggie Fried Rice?

Not only is Veggie Fried Rice incredibly delicious, but it also offers a wide range of health benefits. By using an assortment of colorful vegetables, you’ll be getting an array of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. This dish is high in fiber, which aids in digestion and promotes a healthy gut. Additionally, it’s a fantastic way to reduce food waste by utilizing leftover rice and vegetables. It’s an affordable and versatile recipe that can be enjoyed as a main course or a side dish.

Final Thoughts

Veggie Fried Rice is a go-to recipe for busy weeknights when you want a quick and satisfying meal. It’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet and experiment with different flavors. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, family, or friends, this dish is sure to impress. Give it a try and let your taste buds dance with joy!

Note: This recipe serves 4 people. Adjust the measurements accordingly based on your needs.
