Bountiful Bowls: Spring Quinoa Salad with Honey-Lemon Vinaigrette


Spring is the perfect time to enjoy light and refreshing salads that showcase the vibrant flavors of the season. If you’re looking for a healthy and satisfying dish, look no further than our Bountiful Bowls: Spring Quinoa Salad with Honey-Lemon Vinaigrette. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with fresh flavors, this salad is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled

Honey-Lemon Vinaigrette

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. In a saucepan, bring the water to a boil and add the quinoa. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, spinach, red onion, parsley, and mint. Toss gently to combine.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, honey, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Drizzle the vinaigrette over the quinoa salad and toss until well coated.

  4. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top of the salad. Give it a final toss to incorporate the cheese.

  5. Serve the Spring Quinoa Salad immediately as a light lunch or as a side dish. It’s also perfect for picnics or potlucks.

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize this salad by adding or substituting your favorite vegetables. Try adding sliced radishes, grated carrots, or diced avocado for extra flavor and texture.
  • For a protein boost, add grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu to make it a complete meal.
  • If you prefer a vegan version, omit the feta cheese or substitute it with a vegan cheese alternative.
  • This salad can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to three days. Just wait to add the vinaigrette until right before serving to keep the salad fresh and crisp.

Final Thoughts

Our Bountiful Bowls: Spring Quinoa Salad with Honey-Lemon Vinaigrette is a refreshing and nutritious way to celebrate the flavors of spring. The combination of quinoa, fresh vegetables, and the tangy-sweet vinaigrette creates a harmonious medley of flavors that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Whether you enjoy it as a light lunch or a side dish, this salad is a perfect addition to any springtime meal. Try it today and embrace the bountiful flavors of the season!
