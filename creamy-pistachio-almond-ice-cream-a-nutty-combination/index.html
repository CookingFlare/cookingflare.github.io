Creamy Pistachio Almond Ice Cream: A Nutty Combination


If you’re a fan of creamy, nutty treats, then you’re in for a delightful surprise with this homemade Creamy Pistachio Almond Ice Cream recipe. This frozen dessert is a perfect combination of rich, velvety texture and the delightful crunch of pistachios and almonds. It’s a refreshing and indulgent treat that’s perfect for summer days or any time you’re craving a cool, satisfying dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 cup shelled pistachios, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup slivered almonds

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the whole milk, heavy cream, sugar, and salt. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir occasionally until the mixture is hot but not boiling.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until they are slightly thickened. Slowly pour about 1/2 cup of the hot milk mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly. This step helps to temper the eggs, preventing them from scrambling when added to the hot mixture.

  3. Pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining milk mixture. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon (about 5-7 minutes).

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla and almond extracts. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl to remove any cooked egg bits.

  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, pressing it directly onto the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or until completely cooled.

  6. Once the custard is chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. When the ice cream is almost fully churned, add the chopped pistachios and almonds and continue churning until they are evenly distributed throughout the ice cream.

  7. Transfer the churned ice cream to a freezer-safe container, smoothing the top with a spatula. Cover the surface of the ice cream with parchment paper or plastic wrap, then cover with a lid. Freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

  8. When ready to serve, allow the ice cream to sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly. Scoop the Creamy Pistachio Almond Ice Cream into bowls or cones, and garnish with extra chopped pistachios and almonds if desired.

Nutritional Information:

  • Saturated Fat: 8g
  • Cholesterol: 125mg
  • Sodium: 75mg

Conclusion:

Creamy Pistachio Almond Ice Cream is a delightful fusion of creamy, nutty flavors that will satisfy your sweet cravings and cool you down on a hot day. This homemade treat is a fantastic way to enjoy the rich, luxurious taste of pistachios and almonds in a refreshing frozen dessert. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply treating yourself to a special indulgence, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So, gather your ingredients, churn up a batch of this delightful ice cream, and indulge in the nutty goodness of this frozen delight!
