Mediterranean Comfort Food: Greek Style Baked Pasta


If you’re looking for a hearty and delicious comfort food dish that combines the flavors of the Mediterranean, then look no further than Greek Style Baked Pasta. This mouthwatering recipe is a perfect blend of rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, tender pasta, and a sprinkle of cheese on top. It’s a crowd-pleasing dish that will transport your taste buds straight to the sunny shores of Greece.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a large baking dish.

  2. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned and fully cooked. Break up any large chunks of meat with a wooden spoon as it cooks.

  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. While the sauce is simmering, prepare the béchamel sauce. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour until it forms a smooth paste.

  7. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to prevent any lumps from forming. Continue to cook and whisk until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

  8. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the ground nutmeg and half of the grated Parmesan cheese. Set aside.

  9. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked penne pasta and the tomato meat sauce. Toss until the pasta is well coated.

  10. Transfer the pasta mixture to the greased baking dish. Pour the béchamel sauce over the top and spread it evenly with a spatula.

  11. In a small bowl, mix together the remaining grated Parmesan cheese and breadcrumbs. Sprinkle this mixture over the top of the béchamel sauce.

  12. Bake the Greek Style Baked Pasta in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the sauce is bubbly.

  13. Remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together even more.

  14. Serve the Greek Style Baked Pasta hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. It pairs perfectly with a crisp green salad and a glass of red wine.

Enjoy a Taste of Greece

With its comforting flavors and satisfying textures, Greek Style Baked Pasta is a dish that will transport you to the sun-drenched shores of the Mediterranean. The combination of the rich tomato meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and perfectly cooked pasta creates a harmony of flavors that will leave you craving for more. So gather your loved ones, set the table, and indulge in this Mediterranean comfort food that will warm both your heart and your stomach. Opa!
