Tasty and Easy Mac and Cheese Recipe for Kids


Mac and cheese is a classic dish loved by both kids and adults. Its creamy and cheesy goodness is hard to resist, making it a go-to comfort food for many. If you’re looking for a tasty and easy mac and cheese recipe that will please even the pickiest eaters, you’ve come to the right place!

Ingredients

To make this delicious mac and cheese, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions. Make sure to cook it al dente, as it will continue to cook when mixed with the cheese sauce later on. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the flour and whisk constantly for about 1 minute. This will help cook out the raw flour taste.

  3. Slowly pour in the milk while continuing to whisk. Cook the mixture, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a simmer. This should take about 5 minutes.

  4. Once the mixture has thickened, reduce the heat to low and add the shredded cheddar cheese. Stir until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir until all the macaroni is coated evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Remove the mac and cheese from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to thicken slightly. This will help the sauce cling to the macaroni better.

  7. Serve the mac and cheese hot, straight from the saucepan. You can also transfer it to a baking dish, sprinkle some extra shredded cheese on top, and broil it for a few minutes until golden and bubbly.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, try adding cooked bacon bits or diced ham to the mac and cheese before serving.
  • If your kids enjoy vegetables, you can mix in some steamed broccoli, peas, or carrots to make it a more nutritious meal.
  • To make the mac and cheese extra creamy, you can substitute some of the milk with heavy cream or evaporated milk.
  • If you want to make the dish ahead of time, simply prepare the mac and cheese as instructed, but stop before baking it. Store it in the refrigerator and when ready to serve, bring it to room temperature and bake as directed.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat it in the microwave or on the stovetop with a splash of milk to restore its creaminess.

Conclusion

This tasty and easy mac and cheese recipe is sure to be a hit with your kids. The creamy cheese sauce paired with perfectly cooked macaroni will have them asking for seconds! Feel free to get creative with additional ingredients and variations to suit your family’s preferences. Enjoy this classic comfort food dish and create lasting memories around the dinner table.
