Crunchy and Nutty Whole Grain Bread Recipe


When you’re in the mood for some homemade, wholesome bread, this Crunchy and Nutty Whole Grain Bread Recipe is sure to hit the spot. With a delicious combination of grains and nuts, you’ll have a satisfying and nutritious loaves of bread for your family to enjoy. So grab your mixing bowl and let’s get started.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup chopped mixed nuts ( WALNUTS,PINE NUTS, ALMONDS)
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 ½ teaspoons rapid-rise active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 cups warm water

Instructions

Step 1: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, rolled oats, chopped mixed nuts, honey, yeast, and salt together. Mix well and evenly until everything is fully incorporated.

Step 2: Add Warm Water

Add the warm water to the dry ingredients and mix everything together. The dough should be sticky at first, but continue to combine until the ingredients come together into a cohesive dough. If you find the dough is too dry, add more warm water one tablespoon at a time.

Step 3: Knead the Dough

Knead the dough for about 10 minutes. This can be done by hand or with a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook attachment. The dough should become smooth and elastic. You can add a little more flour if needed.

Step 4: First Rise

Lightly oil a large bowl and transfer the kneaded dough to the bowl. Cover the bowl with a towel and let it rise in a warm area for about 1 to 1 ½ hours, or until it has doubled in size.

Step 5: Shape the Dough

Once risen, punch down the dough and transfer it to a floured surface. Divide the dough into two equal portions and shape into round loaves.

Step 6: Second Rise

Place the shaped loaves onto a parchment-lined baking sheet or into greased loaf pans. Cover again with a towel and let the dough rise for an additional 30 minutes to 1 hour, or until it has nearly doubled in size.

Step 7: Bake

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the loaves for about 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the loaves sound hollow when tapped.

Step 8: Cool and Serve

Remove the bread from the oven and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes before slicing. Slice and serve your homemade Crunchy and Nutty Whole Grain Bread while warm with butter or your favorite spread.

Tips

  • Be sure to use a mix of nuts to add a variety of flavors and textures to your bread. Almonds, walnuts, and pine nuts are a delicious choice.
  • For a gluten-free version, try replacing up to half of the whole wheat flour with coconut flour or rice flour.
  • You can also add additional grains such as quinoa flakes or buckwheat flour for added nutrition and different texture.

This recipe will add a touch of crunch and a delicious earthy flavor to your bread basket. Enjoy with a slice of cheese, some homemade jam, or served alongside a hearty bowl of soup. Whether you’re looking for a healthy breakfast option or a satisfying snack, this Crunchy and Nutty Whole Grain Bread is sure to satisfy.
