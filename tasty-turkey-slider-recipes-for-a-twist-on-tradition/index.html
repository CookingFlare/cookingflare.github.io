Tasty Turkey Slider Recipes for a Twist on Tradition


Turkey sliders are a delicious and versatile alternative to traditional burgers. These mini sandwiches are perfect for parties, game days, or even a quick weeknight dinner. With their juicy turkey patties and a variety of flavorful toppings, they are sure to please everyone’s taste buds. In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering turkey slider recipes that put a unique twist on tradition.

1. Spicy Buffalo Turkey Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and red onion for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground turkey, breadcrumbs, hot sauce, blue cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  2. Divide the mixture into small patties, about the size of your slider buns.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the turkey patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F.
  4. Toast the slider buns lightly on the grill or in the oven.
  5. Assemble the sliders by placing a turkey patty on each bun. Top with lettuce, tomato, and red onion.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy the spicy kick of these Buffalo-inspired turkey sliders!

2. Mediterranean Turkey Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Tzatziki sauce, cucumber slices, and red bell pepper for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine ground turkey, parsley, feta cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly distributed.
  2. Shape the mixture into small patties, slightly larger than the size of your slider buns.
  3. Heat a grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the turkey patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F.
  4. Lightly toast the slider buns.
  5. Spread a generous amount of tzatziki sauce on the bottom bun. Place a turkey patty on top and add cucumber slices and red bell pepper.
  6. Serve these Mediterranean-inspired turkey sliders for a burst of fresh and tangy flavors!

3. BBQ Bacon Ranch Turkey Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ranch dressing mix
  • 4 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Cheddar cheese, lettuce, and tomato for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground turkey, breadcrumbs, BBQ sauce, ranch dressing mix, crumbled bacon, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly until all ingredients are well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into small patties, slightly larger than the slider buns.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the turkey patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F.
  4. Toast the slider buns until lightly golden.
  5. Place a turkey patty on each bun. Top with cheddar cheese, lettuce, and tomato.
  6. These BBQ Bacon Ranch Turkey Sliders are a perfect combination of smoky, creamy, and savory flavors!

Conclusion

Turkey sliders offer a delicious twist on traditional burgers, and these three recipes are sure to elevate your slider game. Whether you prefer a spicy kick, a Mediterranean flair, or a barbecue bacon ranch indulgence, these tasty turkey sliders will satisfy your cravings. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to impress your friends and family with a delightful twist on tradition!

Remember to get creative with toppings and sauces to make these sliders your own. Enjoy this fun and flavorful twist on a classic favorite!
