Creamy Pistachio Cherry Ice Cream: A Nutty and Fruity Delight


There’s nothing quite like the creamy indulgence of homemade ice cream, especially when it’s infused with the delightful flavors of pistachio and cherry. This creamy pistachio cherry ice cream recipe is a perfect blend of nuttiness and fruity sweetness, creating a delightful frozen treat that’s sure to impress your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a unique dessert, this recipe is bound to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shelled pistachios
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen cherries, pitted and chopped

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pistachios: Begin by toasting the shelled pistachios in a dry skillet over medium heat for 3-4 minutes, or until fragrant. Be sure to stir them frequently to prevent burning. Once toasted, allow the pistachios to cool completely.

  2. Infuse the Milk and Cream: In a saucepan, combine the whole milk and heavy cream. Heat the mixture over medium heat until it’s just about to simmer. Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the cooled pistachios. Let the mixture steep for 1-2 hours to infuse the flavors.

  3. Blend the Mixture: After the pistachio-infused milk and cream mixture has steeped, strain it through a fine-mesh sieve into a large bowl. Press down on the pistachios to extract as much flavor as possible. Discard the pistachios.

  4. Add the Flavorings: To the strained mixture, add the granulated sugar, pure vanilla extract, and almond extract. Whisk until the sugar is fully dissolved.

  5. Chill the Mixture: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the mixture for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight. This will allow the flavors to meld and the mixture to chill thoroughly.

  6. Churn the Ice Cream: Once the mixture is well-chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. During the last few minutes of churning, add the chopped cherries to the ice cream and let the machine incorporate them evenly.

  7. Freeze the Ice Cream: Transfer the churned ice cream with the cherries into a freezer-safe container. Smooth the top with a spatula, then cover the surface directly with a piece of parchment paper or wax paper to prevent ice crystals from forming. Seal the container with a tight-fitting lid and freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: When you’re ready to serve, scoop the creamy pistachio cherry ice cream into bowls or cones. Garnish with additional chopped pistachios or whole cherries for a beautiful presentation.

Conclusion

Creamy pistachio cherry ice cream is a delightful combination of nutty, creamy, and fruity flavors that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a fan of homemade ice cream or simply looking for a unique dessert to impress your guests, this recipe is a must-try. The rich, creamy texture of the ice cream combined with the crunch of pistachios and the burst of sweet cherries makes for a truly indulgent treat. So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to this nutty and fruity delight?
