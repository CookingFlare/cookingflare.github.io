Grilled Veggie Wraps with Hummus: Healthy and Delicious


Are you looking for a quick, delicious, and healthy meal that’s perfect for lunch or dinner? Look no further than these Grilled Veggie Wraps with Hummus! Packed with nutrient-rich vegetables and flavorful hummus, these wraps are not only satisfying but also incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

To make these mouthwatering Grilled Veggie Wraps with Hummus, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 whole-wheat or gluten-free tortillas
  • 1 cup hummus
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Optional: hot sauce or tahini for extra flavor

Instructions

  1. Prep the Veggies: Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. In a large bowl, toss the zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper.

  2. Grill the Veggies: Place the seasoned veggies on the grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender and have beautiful grill marks. Make sure to turn them occasionally for even cooking.

  3. Warm the Tortillas: While the veggies are grilling, warm up the tortillas on the grill for about 1 minute on each side, until they are soft and pliable.

  4. Assemble the Wraps: Spread a generous amount of hummus on each tortilla. Top with a handful of fresh spinach leaves, followed by a generous portion of the grilled veggies.

  5. Add Extra Flavors (Optional): For an extra kick of flavor, drizzle some hot sauce or tahini over the veggies before wrapping up the tortillas.

  6. Wrap and Serve: Fold the sides of the tortillas over the filling, then roll them up tightly. Cut the wraps in half diagonally and serve immediately.

Health Benefits

Not only are these Grilled Veggie Wraps with Hummus bursting with flavor, but they also offer a range of health benefits. The colorful array of vegetables provides essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, while the hummus adds plant-based protein and fiber. The whole-wheat or gluten-free tortillas ensure a good source of complex carbohydrates for sustained energy. This meal is not only delicious but also a great way to nourish your body with wholesome, natural ingredients.

Serving Suggestions

These wraps are perfect for a light lunch, a quick dinner, or even as a portable meal for picnics or work lunches. Pair them with a side of fresh fruit, a simple green salad, or some baked sweet potato fries for a complete and balanced meal. They are also great for meal prep – simply grill the veggies ahead of time and assemble the wraps when ready to eat.

Conclusion

Grilled Veggie Wraps with Hummus are a versatile, nutritious, and flavorful option for anyone looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet. They are easy to make, customizable to your taste preferences, and perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, these wraps are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

So, what are you waiting for? Fire up the grill and get ready to enjoy these delightful Grilled Veggie Wraps with Hummus – a healthy and delicious meal that’s guaranteed to become a favorite in your recipe collection!
