Savory Instant Pot Mexican Shredded Chicken Recipes


Are you craving a delicious Mexican-inspired meal that’s easy to make and bursting with flavor? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of savory Instant Pot Mexican shredded chicken recipes that will satisfy your taste buds and impress your family and friends. From tacos to enchiladas, these recipes are versatile and can be customized to suit your preferences. So dust off your Instant Pot and let’s get cooking!

Recipe 1: Instant Pot Mexican Shredded Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortillas, for serving
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, sour cream, etc.

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the Instant Pot and add the diced onion, minced garlic, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Close the lid, set the valve to “Sealing,” and select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” setting. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.
  4. Remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and mix it with the cooking liquid to enhance the flavor.
  5. Serve the shredded chicken in warm tortillas, and top with your favorite toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, and sour cream.

Recipe 2: Instant Pot Mexican Shredded Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) enchilada sauce
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8-10 flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheese, for topping
  • Chopped cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken thighs in the Instant Pot and add the diced onion, minced garlic, enchilada sauce, diced green chilies, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Close the lid, set the valve to “Sealing,” and select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” setting. Cook on high pressure for 20 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.
  4. Remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and mix it with the cooking liquid to ensure it’s moist and flavorful.
  5. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Place a spoonful of the shredded chicken mixture onto each flour tortilla, roll them up, and place them in a baking dish.
  7. Pour any remaining cooking liquid from the Instant Pot over the enchiladas, and sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Garnish with chopped cilantro before serving.

Recipe 3: Instant Pot Mexican Shredded Chicken Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 ounces) corn, drained
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or quinoa, for serving
  • Guacamole, salsa, or your favorite toppings

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken thighs in the Instant Pot and add the diced onion, minced garlic, black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Close the lid, set the valve to “Sealing,” and select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” setting. Cook on high pressure for 20 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.
  4. Remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and mix it with the cooking liquid and vegetables.
  5. Serve the shredded chicken over cooked rice or quinoa, and top with guacamole, salsa, or your favorite toppings.

Conclusion

These savory Instant Pot Mexican shredded chicken recipes are a fantastic addition to your recipe repertoire. Whether you prefer tacos, enchiladas, or a hearty bowl, these recipes offer a delicious and convenient way to enjoy the flavors of Mexico in the comfort of your own home. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to savor the mouthwatering goodness of these Mexican-inspired dishes. ¡Buen provecho!
