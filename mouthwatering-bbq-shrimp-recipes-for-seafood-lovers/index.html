Mouthwatering BBQ Shrimp: Recipes for Seafood Lovers


If you’re a seafood lover, you know there’s nothing quite like the succulent, juicy flavor of perfectly cooked shrimp. And when it comes to grilling, BBQ shrimp is an absolute showstopper. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving a taste of the sea, these mouthwatering BBQ shrimp recipes will elevate your culinary game and leave your taste buds singing.

Classic BBQ Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat evenly. Marinate for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked skewers.
  5. Grill the shrimp skewers for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they are opaque and slightly charred.
  6. Serve the classic BBQ shrimp skewers with a side of your favorite dipping sauce and enjoy!

Cajun-Style BBQ Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds jumbo shrimp, shells on
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic, Worcestershire sauce, Cajun seasoning, hot sauce, and lemon juice to the melted butter. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Place the jumbo shrimp directly on the grill grates and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, until the shells are pink and the shrimp is opaque.
  5. Brush the Cajun-style BBQ shrimp with the butter sauce while grilling.
  6. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the grill and garnish with fresh parsley.
  7. Serve the shrimp with crusty bread for dipping in the flavorful butter sauce.

Asian-Inspired BBQ Shrimp Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and let it marinate for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  4. Add the marinated shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, until pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, stir-fry the red bell pepper and snap peas until they are tender-crisp.
  6. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss everything together to coat in the flavorful sauce.
  7. Serve the Asian-inspired BBQ shrimp stir-fry over a bed of fluffy rice and savor the fusion of sweet and savory flavors.

Conclusion

Whether you prefer the classic smoky flavor of BBQ shrimp skewers, the bold and spicy kick of Cajun-style shrimp, or the exotic twist of an Asian-inspired stir-fry, these recipes are sure to delight your palate and impress your guests. Embrace the versatility of shrimp and the magic of the grill to create unforgettable dishes that celebrate the ocean’s bounty. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible delight of BBQ shrimp that will leave you craving for more!
