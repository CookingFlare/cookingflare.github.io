Delicious and Nutritious Whole Wheat Pizza Crust Recipes


Are you tired of the same old pizza crust? Looking for a healthier alternative that doesn’t compromise on taste? Look no further! In this article, we will explore some delicious and nutritious whole wheat pizza crust recipes that will satisfy your cravings while providing you with the goodness of whole grains.

1. Classic Whole Wheat Pizza Crust

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, dissolve the yeast and honey in warm water. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the whole wheat flour and salt.
  3. Add the yeast mixture to the flour and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  6. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  7. Punch down the dough and divide it into two equal portions.
  8. Roll out each portion into a round shape, about ¼ inch thick.
  9. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  10. Add your favorite toppings and bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

2. Herbed Whole Wheat Pizza Crust

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon dried Italian herbs (such as basil, oregano, and thyme)
  • 1 cup warm water

Instructions:

  1. Follow steps 1-5 from the Classic Whole Wheat Pizza Crust recipe.
  2. Add the dried Italian herbs to the flour and salt mixture before adding the yeast mixture.
  3. Proceed with steps 6-10 from the Classic Whole Wheat Pizza Crust recipe.

3. Garlic and Parmesan Whole Wheat Pizza Crust

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup warm water

Instructions:

  1. Follow steps 1-5 from the Classic Whole Wheat Pizza Crust recipe.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated Parmesan cheese to the flour and salt mixture before adding the yeast mixture.
  3. Proceed with steps 6-10 from the Classic Whole Wheat Pizza Crust recipe.

4. Veggie Whole Wheat Pizza Crust

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ½ cup grated carrots
  • ½ cup chopped spinach
  • ½ cup diced bell peppers

Instructions:

  1. Follow steps 1-5 from the Classic Whole Wheat Pizza Crust recipe.
  2. Add the grated carrots, chopped spinach, and diced bell peppers to the flour and salt mixture before adding the yeast mixture.
  3. Proceed with steps 6-10 from the Classic Whole Wheat Pizza Crust recipe.

Conclusion

With these delicious and nutritious whole wheat pizza crust recipes, you can enjoy your favorite pizza guilt-free. Whole wheat flour adds fiber, vitamins, and minerals to your crust, making it a healthier alternative to refined flour. So, go ahead and experiment with these recipes, add your favorite toppings, and indulge in a wholesome and tasty pizza night! Bon appétit!
