Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Kabobs with Lemon and Herbs


If you’re in the mood for a delicious and healthy meal that’s bursting with flavors, look no further than these Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs with lemon and herbs. This recipe is perfect for a summer barbecue or a cozy indoor dinner. The combination of tender chicken, vibrant vegetables, and zesty lemon and herbs will transport your taste buds straight to Italy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 bell peppers, any color, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 8 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 zucchini, cut into thick slices
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chopped basil, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss until they are well coated. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.

  4. Thread the marinated chicken, bell peppers, red onion, cherry tomatoes, zucchini, and lemon slices onto skewers, alternating between ingredients. Make sure to leave some space between each piece to ensure even cooking.

  5. Place the kabobs on the preheated grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.

  6. Remove the kabobs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy chicken.

  7. Serve the Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs with lemon and herbs hot off the grill. They pair perfectly with a side of herbed rice or a fresh salad.

  8. Enjoy the explosion of flavors in every bite, from the succulent chicken to the smoky vegetables, complemented by the bright and tangy notes of lemon and herbs.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook these kabobs in the oven. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and place the kabobs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.

  • Feel free to customize the vegetable selection based on your preferences. You can add mushrooms, cherry tomatoes, or even chunks of pineapple for a touch of sweetness.

  • Experiment with different herbs to create unique flavor profiles. Try adding rosemary or oregano to give the kabobs a Mediterranean twist.

  • For an extra burst of flavor, squeeze some fresh lemon juice over the kabobs just before serving.

  • If you’re vegetarian, you can substitute the chicken with tofu or tempeh for a delicious plant-based version.

These Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs with lemon and herbs are a delightful and healthy option for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a flavorful meal, this recipe will not disappoint. So fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in a taste of Italy right in your own backyard. Buon appetito!
