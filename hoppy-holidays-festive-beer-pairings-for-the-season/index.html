Hoppy Holidays: Festive Beer Pairings for the Season


‘Tis the season to be jolly with friends and family! This holiday season, warm up your senses with a delightful array of beer pairings that will make your festivities unforgettable. Beer, a versatile beverage enjoyed across the globe, is more than just a drink. When paired appropriately, it can elevate your culinary adventures to new heights.

If you’re a foodie or a beer enthusiast, read on to discover how you can create a harmonious union of flavors between your favorite brews and scrumptious dishes.

1. Christmas Ale & Cookie Exchange

One traditional holiday custom that brings people together is the cookie exchange. When hosting this event, Christmas Ale is the perfect pairing to bring smiles to everyone’s faces. Its subtle notes of cinnamon and nutmeg enhance the flavors of homemade cookies while providing a comforting warmth.

For an extra special treat, try brewing a seasonal ale with ingredients like orange peel, honey, and spices for added zest and sweetness. Pair with chocolate-based cookies orruit-cake for a perfect medley of flavors.

2. Specialty IPAs & Charcuterie Board

A charcuterie board never goes out of style, and when elevated by a Specialty IPA, it becomes the life of the party. IPAs often carry flavors of citrus and tropical fruit, which perfectly complement the savory and salty flavors of charcuterie board favorites like cheeses, cured meats, and olives.

When choosing your IPA, opt for a double or triple IPA with heightened ABV. This intensity will naturally pair well with the smoky flavors of prosciutto or chorizo and the tanginess of aged gouda and MANCHEGO. Don’t forget to add a few comfortable snacks for enhanced flavors.

3. Hard Cider & Roast Turkey

Hard cider tends to be an underrated choice during the holidays, but when combined with the timeless dish of roast turkey, it proves to be a match made in culinary heaven. Tart apples offer a refreshing contrast, cutting through the richness of the meat. When choosing between sweet and dry hard cider, opt for dry varieties. These offer a crisp taste that complements the subtle and complex flavors of turkey gravy and stuffing.

For a remarkable taste experience, incorporate apple brandy into the turkey’s gravy for additional zest. This will pair excellently with your hard cider and contribute to a balanced, warm atmosphere.

4. Traditional Pilsner & Eggnog

Eggnog is an icon of the holiday season, but its richness can be overwhelming. A Traditional Pilsner, with its crisp and clean taste, is a suitable, balanced pairing. Pilsners are the perfect choice for washing away the richness of the eggnog, preserving each sip’s drinkability.

Liven up your eggnog with nutmeg and a hint of cinnamon, which complements the crisp Fischer nose of your Pilsner. For complete satisfaction, mix bourbon or rum into your eggnog to create a sophisticated twist that perfectly accompanies your chosen beer.

5. Dark Ale & Gingerbread

Gingerbread has long been a festive treat, and when served alongside a robust Dark Ale, your taste buds are in for a heavenly experience. The gingerbread’s spices meld with the dark elements of the ale, creating a comforting, indulgent treat.

To enhance the pairing, add molasses or brown sugar to your Dark Ale for a richer, boozier concoction. Envelop your gingerbread with brandy butter for an extra layer of celebration.

In conclusion, using beer to pair with your holiday meals enhances the flavor and creates a richer culinary experience. Whether you choose a Christmas Ale or a hard cider, the key is to experiment and enjoy the versatility of beer. Give your festivity an extra kick and partake in these delightful beer pairings this holiday season. Cheers!
