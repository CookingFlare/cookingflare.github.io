Fragrant Instant Pot Thai Green Curry Recipe for Exotic Flavors


There’s something magical about the fragrant blend of herbs, spices, and creamy coconut milk that makes Thai green curry an irresistible dish. With the help of an Instant Pot, you can enjoy this exotic Thai delicacy in the comfort of your own home. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the steps to create a mouthwatering Fragrant Instant Pot Thai Green Curry that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 pound chicken breast, sliced into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons Thai green curry paste
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup sliced bamboo shoots
  • 1 cup sliced zucchini
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Cooked jasmine rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the vegetable oil. Add the chicken breast pieces and cook until lightly browned, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Add the Thai green curry paste to the Instant Pot and sauté for another minute to release the flavors and aromas.

  3. Pour in the coconut milk, chicken broth, fish sauce, and palm sugar. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  4. Add the sliced bell peppers, bamboo shoots, and zucchini to the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set the valve to the sealing position.

  5. Select the manual/pressure cook function and set the timer for 5 minutes on high pressure.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release of the pressure by carefully turning the valve to the venting position.

  7. Open the lid of the Instant Pot and stir in the Thai basil leaves. The residual heat will wilt the basil leaves and infuse the curry with their aromatic flavor.

  8. Serve the Fragrant Instant Pot Thai Green Curry over a bed of jasmine rice. Garnish with lime wedges for a burst of citrusy freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or protein. Shrimp, tofu, or even a medley of vegetables make delicious alternatives to chicken.

  • If you prefer a spicier curry, increase the amount of Thai green curry paste according to your taste.

  • For a vegetarian version, simply omit the meat and increase the amount of vegetables. You can also substitute the fish sauce with soy sauce or tamari for a vegetarian-friendly alternative.

  • To make this recipe even more authentic, serve the curry with traditional Thai condiments such as sliced red chili peppers, chopped cilantro, or Thai chili sauce.

Enjoy the explosion of exotic flavors that this Fragrant Instant Pot Thai Green Curry has to offer. With its vibrant colors, tantalizing aroma, and creamy texture, it’s sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, grab your Instant Pot and let’s embark on a culinary adventure to Thailand’s bustling streets, one spoonful at a time.
