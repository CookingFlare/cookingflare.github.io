Hearty Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Soup


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious soup that will warm your soul? Look no further than this hearty Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Soup. Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavors, this soup is the perfect comfort food for those chilly evenings or when you’re in need of a nourishing meal. Best of all, it’s Paleo-friendly, which means it’s free from grains, dairy, and processed ingredients. Let’s dive into the recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the sliced carrots, chopped celery, diced red bell pepper, and diced zucchini to the pot. Stir well to combine and sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the pot and add the chicken pieces to the other side. Cook the chicken until it’s no longer pink in the center, stirring occasionally.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, pour in the chicken broth. Add the dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Give it a good stir to combine all the flavors.

  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.

  6. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley for an extra burst of flavor and a touch of freshness.

Why Choose Paleo?

The Paleo diet focuses on consuming whole, unprocessed foods that our ancestors would have eaten during the Paleolithic era. By following a Paleo lifestyle, you’re nourishing your body with nutrient-dense foods, while avoiding grains, dairy, and refined sugars. This Chicken and Vegetable Soup fits perfectly into the Paleo framework, as it’s made with real ingredients and avoids any additives or preservatives.

Nutritional Benefits

This Hearty Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Soup provides a wide range of nutrients to support your health and well-being. The chicken offers a lean source of protein, while the vegetables add an array of vitamins, minerals, and fiber. The use of olive oil provides healthy fats that are beneficial for heart health. By incorporating this soup into your diet, you’re giving your body the nourishment it needs to thrive.

Make It Your Own

Feel free to customize this soup to your liking. If you prefer a thicker consistency, you can add some arrowroot powder or coconut flour to the broth. You can also experiment with different vegetables or herbs to suit your taste buds. This recipe is versatile and can easily be adapted to accommodate your preferences.

Final Thoughts

There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of soup to comfort your body and soul. This Hearty Paleo Chicken and Vegetable Soup ticks all the boxes when it comes to taste, nutrition, and simplicity. Whether you’re following a Paleo lifestyle or simply looking for a nourishing meal, this soup is a winner. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and let’s get cooking! Your taste buds will thank you.
