Grilled Watermelon Salad with Feta: A Refreshing Fourth of July Dish


The Fourth of July is all about celebrating with friends and family, and what better way to do so than with a refreshing and vibrant dish that captures the essence of summer? Grilled watermelon salad with feta is a delightful combination of smoky sweetness, tangy feta, and crisp greens that will elevate any Independence Day gathering. This simple yet impressive salad is a feast for the eyes and the taste buds, making it the perfect addition to your holiday spread.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 small seedless watermelon, cut into wedges
  • 6 ounces of feta cheese, crumbled
  • 4 cups of arugula
  • 1/4 cup of fresh mint leaves, torn
  • 1/4 cup of balsamic glaze
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Balsamic Glaze:

  • 1 cup of balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of honey

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Balsamic Glaze:

    • In a small saucepan, combine the balsamic vinegar and honey. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil over medium heat.
    • Reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until the glaze coats the back of a spoon. Set aside to cool.

  2. Grill the Watermelon:

    • Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
    • Lightly brush each watermelon wedge with olive oil and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    • Grill the watermelon for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks form. Remove from the grill and let cool.

  3. Assemble the Salad:

    • On a large serving platter, arrange the grilled watermelon wedges.
    • Scatter the arugula and torn mint leaves over the watermelon.
    • Crumble the feta cheese on top of the salad.
    • Drizzle the balsamic glaze and extra virgin olive oil over the salad.
    • Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • This salad is best enjoyed immediately to savor the contrast of flavors and textures.

Why Grilled Watermelon Salad?

Grilling the watermelon caramelizes its natural sugars and adds a subtle smokiness that perfectly complements the salty feta and peppery arugula. The burst of juicy sweetness from the watermelon combined with the tangy balsamic glaze creates a harmonious blend of flavors that is both refreshing and satisfying. Additionally, the vibrant colors of this salad make it a stunning addition to any Fourth of July spread, adding a pop of red and green to the table.

Conclusion

Grilled watermelon salad with feta is a celebration of summer flavors and a delightful way to honor the spirit of Independence Day. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or attending a potluck, this salad is sure to impress and refresh your guests. So, fire up the grill, embrace the summer season, and treat yourself and your loved ones to this irresistible dish.
