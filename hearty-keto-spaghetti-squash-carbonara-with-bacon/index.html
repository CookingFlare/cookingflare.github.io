Hearty Keto Spaghetti Squash Carbonara with Bacon


If you’re on a keto diet or just looking for a delicious low-carb meal, this Hearty Keto Spaghetti Squash Carbonara with Bacon is a game-changer. It’s a flavorful twist on the classic carbonara dish, using spaghetti squash as a healthy, keto-friendly alternative to traditional pasta. The combination of creamy sauce, crispy bacon, and tender spaghetti squash makes this dish a comforting and satisfying option for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium spaghetti squash
  • 6 slices of thick-cut bacon, chopped
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Spaghetti Squash

  • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Carefully cut the spaghetti squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds and stringy bits.
  • Place the squash halves on a baking sheet, cut side down, and roast for 40-45 minutes, or until the flesh is tender and easily shreds into spaghetti-like strands with a fork. Set aside to cool.

Step 2: Cook the Bacon

  • In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until it’s crispy and the fat has rendered. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the bacon to a plate lined with paper towels, leaving the bacon fat in the skillet.

Step 3: Make the Carbonara Sauce

  • In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and heavy cream. Set aside.
  • Using the same skillet with the bacon fat over medium heat, add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add the cooked spaghetti squash to the skillet and toss to combine with the garlic.
  • Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the spaghetti squash and quickly toss everything together until the sauce thickens and coats the strands. Be careful not to scramble the eggs.

Step 4: Assemble the Dish

  • Add the crispy bacon back into the skillet, reserving some for garnish.
  • Season the dish with salt and black pepper to taste, and toss to combine.
  • Serve the Hearty Keto Spaghetti Squash Carbonara with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and the remaining crispy bacon on top.

Enjoy Your Hearty Keto Meal

This Hearty Keto Spaghetti Squash Carbonara with Bacon is a delightful dish that’s rich, creamy, and packed with flavor. The use of spaghetti squash provides a satisfying “al dente” texture while keeping the dish low in carbohydrates. It’s a perfect way to indulge in a comforting pasta-like meal without compromising your keto lifestyle.

Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. The creamy carbonara sauce, savory bacon, and tender spaghetti squash create a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that will leave everyone wanting more. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak in some extra veggies while enjoying a classic Italian-inspired dish.

So, next time you’re craving a hearty and indulgent meal, give this Hearty Keto Spaghetti Squash Carbonara with Bacon a try. It’s a simple yet impressive recipe that’s bound to become a staple in your keto cooking repertoire. Enjoy every forkful of this delightful, low-carb creation!
