The Perfect Garlic Knots: A Savory Addition to Your Pizza Night


Whether you’re having a casual movie night or hosting a gathering with friends, pizza is always a crowd-pleaser. And what’s better than a delicious slice of pizza? A slice accompanied by the perfect garlic knots! These savory, buttery bites are the ultimate complement to any pizza night. In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the steps to create the most irresistible garlic knots that will have everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

To make approximately 20 garlic knots, you’ll need:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt, for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar. Create a well in the center.

  2. In a separate small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes foamy.

  3. Pour the yeast mixture into the well of the dry ingredients. Add the olive oil.

  4. Mix the dough until it comes together. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a damp cloth. Allow it to rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.

  6. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  7. Punch down the dough and transfer it to a floured surface. Divide it into 20 equal pieces.

  8. Roll each piece into a rope about 6 inches long. Tie the rope into a loose knot, tucking the ends underneath. Repeat with the remaining dough.

  9. Place the garlic knots on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each knot.

  10. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic and melted butter. Brush the garlic butter mixture generously over the knots.

  11. Sprinkle the knots with chopped parsley and a pinch of salt.

  12. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the knots are golden brown.

  13. Remove from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These garlic knots are perfect on their own as an appetizer or side dish. However, they also make a great addition to your pizza night. Serve them alongside your favorite pizza, allowing your guests to savor the combination of flavors. The soft, buttery knots with a hint of garlic will take your pizza experience to the next level.

Tips and Variations

  • For a cheesy twist, sprinkle grated Parmesan or mozzarella cheese over the garlic knots before baking.
  • If you prefer a spicier flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the garlic butter mixture.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of your knots. Rosemary, oregano, or even a touch of paprika can add a unique twist.

Conclusion

Garlic knots are a simple yet irresistible addition to any pizza night. With their soft, buttery texture and aromatic garlic flavor, they elevate the entire pizza experience. Whether you’re sharing them with loved ones or enjoying them on your own, these garlic knots are sure to become a favorite. So, the next time you’re planning a pizza night, don’t forget to whip up a batch of these perfect garlic knots. Your taste buds will thank you!
