10 Refreshing Brunch Cocktails to Start Your Weekend Right


Brunch is the perfect excuse to gather with friends and family, indulge in a delicious meal, and toast to the weekend. But what’s a weekend brunch without a refreshing cocktail to accompany it? In this post, we’ll explore 10 refreshing brunch cocktails that are sure to start your weekend off right.

1. Bellini’s Bloody Mary

Inspired by the classic Bellini cocktail, this Bloody Mary variation adds a twist of citrus and spice to the traditional recipe. Simply combine 1 1/2 ounces of vodka, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed grapefruit juice, 1/2 ounce of grenadine, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice, and a dash of cayenne pepper in a shaker filled with ice. Strain into a glass filled with ice and garnish with a slice of grapefruit and a sprig of rosemary.

2. Mimosa’s Cousin

This refreshing cocktail is perfect for those who prefer a lighter take on the classic mimosa. Combine 1 1/2 ounces of prosecco, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed orange juice, and a splash of Grand Marnier in a champagne flute. Garnish with a slice of orange and a sprig of rosemary.

3. Summer Sunrise

This fruity and bubbly cocktail is perfect for a warm summer morning. Combine 1 1/2 ounces of vodka, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed pineapple juice, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed orange juice, and a splash of grenadine in a shaker filled with ice. Strain into a glass filled with ice and garnish with a slice of pineapple and a cherry.

4. Brunch Bellini

This variation of the classic Bellini cocktail adds a splash of citrus to the traditional recipe. Combine 1 1/2 ounces of peach puree, 1/2 ounce of prosecco, and a splash of freshly squeezed lemon juice in a champagne flute. Garnish with a slice of peach and a sprig of rosemary.

5. Cucumber Gimlet

This refreshing cocktail is perfect for a hot summer day. Combine 1 1/2 ounces of gin, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice, and a splash of cucumber syrup in a shaker filled with ice. Strain into a glass filled with ice and garnish with a slice of cucumber and a lime wheel.

6. Spicy Mango Margarita

This spicy and fruity cocktail is perfect for those who like a little heat in their drink. Combine 2 ounces of tequila, 1 ounce of mango puree, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice, and a dash of cayenne pepper in a shaker filled with ice. Strain into a glass filled with ice and garnish with a slice of mango and a sprig of cilantro.

7. Lavender Collins

This floral and refreshing cocktail is perfect for a spring or summer brunch. Combine 1 1/2 ounces of gin, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed lemon juice, 1/2 ounce of lavender syrup, and a splash of soda water in a Collins glass filled with ice. Garnish with a sprig of lavender and a lemon wheel.

8. Blackberry Bourbon Smash

This fruity and refreshing cocktail is perfect for a warm summer evening. Combine 2 ounces of bourbon, 1 ounce of blackberry puree, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed lemon juice, and a splash of soda water in a shaker filled with ice. Strain into a glass filled with ice and garnish with a sprig of mint and a blackberry.

9. Cranberry Orange Spritzer

This refreshing cocktail is perfect for a fall or winter brunch. Combine 1 1/2 ounces of prosecco, 1/2 ounce of cranberry juice, and a splash of freshly squeezed orange juice in a champagne flute. Garnish with a slice of orange and a sprig of rosemary.

10. Ginger Peach Fizz

This spicy and fruity cocktail is perfect for a warm summer evening. Combine 1 1/2 ounces of ginger liqueur, 1/2 ounce of peach puree, 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice, and a splash of soda water in a shaker filled with ice. Strain into a glass filled with ice and garnish with a slice of peach and a sprig of rosemary.

These refreshing brunch cocktails are sure to start your weekend off right. Whether you’re in the mood for something classic and simple or something fruity and adventurous, there’s a cocktail on this list for everyone. So gather your friends and family, grab your favorite cocktail, and toast to the weekend!
