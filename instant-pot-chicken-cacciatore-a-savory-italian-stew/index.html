Instant Pot Chicken Cacciatore: A Savory Italian Stew


If you’re looking for a hearty, flavorful dish that combines rich Italian tradition with the modern convenience of an Instant Pot, look no further than Chicken Cacciatore. This classic Italian stew, often made with chicken braised in tomatoes, wine, and aromatic herbs, comes together in a fraction of the time with the help of your Instant Pot. The result? A tender, juicy chicken with a flavorful, savory sauce that’s perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. Let’s dive into how you can create this mouthwatering dish using the Instant Pot.

What Is Chicken Cacciatore?

Chicken Cacciatore, or “Pollo alla Cacciatora” in Italian, is a rustic, traditional Italian dish known for its rich, savory flavor profile. The term “cacciatore” means “hunter” in Italian, and the dish was historically made by hunters using ingredients that could be easily found in the countryside, such as chicken, tomatoes, onions, and herbs.

Traditionally, Chicken Cacciatore is a braised dish, meaning the chicken is cooked slowly in a pot with liquid to allow the flavors to meld. The addition of mushrooms, olives, and red wine gives the stew its signature deep, umami flavor.

While Chicken Cacciatore is usually cooked slowly in a pot or Dutch oven, the Instant Pot makes this process much quicker, maintaining the traditional flavors while significantly reducing cooking time.

Why Use the Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to cooking stews and braised dishes like Chicken Cacciatore. Traditional methods can take hours to achieve that perfect, fall-off-the-bone tenderness. But with the Instant Pot, you can have a similar result in just about 30 minutes. The high-pressure cooking locks in all the flavors and keeps the chicken incredibly moist.

Additionally, the Instant Pot’s versatility allows you to sauté, slow cook, and pressure cook all in one device, making it perfect for recipes like Chicken Cacciatore that require a multi-step cooking process.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make this savory Instant Pot Chicken Cacciatore, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skinless chicken thighs (you can also use boneless thighs, but bone-in adds more flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup dry red wine (or chicken broth if you prefer a non-alcoholic version)
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional for a little heat)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup pitted olives (optional)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Why These Ingredients?

  • Chicken thighs: Bone-in, skinless chicken thighs are perfect for this recipe as they stay tender and juicy when cooked under pressure. You can use boneless chicken thighs if preferred, but bone-in adds an extra depth of flavor.
  • Red wine: The dry red wine adds richness to the sauce. It also helps tenderize the chicken while infusing the dish with complex flavors. If you prefer not to use wine, you can substitute it with more chicken broth.
  • Olives: The olives add a subtle briny contrast to the rich sauce. They’re not a must but definitely enhance the overall taste. If you’re not a fan of olives, feel free to leave them out.
  • Herbs and spices: The oregano, basil, rosemary, and red pepper flakes (if using) bring the unmistakable flavors of Italy to the dish, making it as flavorful as possible.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Instant Pot Chicken Cacciatore

1. Sauté the Chicken

To begin, turn the Instant Pot to the “Sauté” mode and add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper and add them to the pot. Sear each side of the chicken thighs for about 3-4 minutes, until golden brown. This step helps to lock in the flavor and gives the chicken a nice texture. Once browned, remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

2. Sauté the Vegetables

In the same pot, add the sliced onion, red bell pepper, and garlic. Sauté for about 3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables soften and become fragrant. The residual oil and browned bits left in the pot from the chicken will help flavor the vegetables.

Next, add the sliced mushrooms and continue to sauté for another 3 minutes until the mushrooms start to release their moisture.

3. Deglaze the Pot

Add the dry red wine to the pot, scraping up any browned bits on the bottom of the Instant Pot with a wooden spoon. This step is essential for ensuring all those flavorful bits are incorporated into the sauce, contributing to the overall richness of the dish.

4. Add the Remaining Ingredients

Now that the vegetables are softened and the wine has deglazed the pot, it’s time to add the other ingredients. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, rosemary, and red pepper flakes (if using). Mix well to combine.

Return the chicken thighs to the pot, nestling them into the sauce. If you’re using olives, add them now. Make sure the chicken is mostly submerged in the sauce for even cooking.

5. Pressure Cook

Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to “Sealing.” Set the Instant Pot to cook on “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” on high pressure for 12 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, let the pressure release naturally for about 10 minutes, and then carefully switch the valve to “Venting” to release any remaining pressure.

6. Serve and Garnish

After the pressure has been released, open the Instant Pot lid, and you’ll find the chicken is tender, juicy, and cooked to perfection. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning with more salt, pepper, or herbs as needed.

To serve, place the chicken thighs on a plate and spoon the rich sauce over the top. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and a burst of freshness.

7. What to Serve With Chicken Cacciatore

Chicken Cacciatore is versatile in terms of side dishes. The rich, savory sauce pairs wonderfully with a variety of options:

  • Crusty bread: Perfect for sopping up the flavorful sauce.
  • Pasta: Serve it over a bed of spaghetti, penne, or rigatoni for a classic Italian meal.
  • Rice: A bed of fluffy rice also works wonderfully with this dish, as it absorbs the savory sauce.
  • Mashed potatoes: For a comforting and hearty option, mashed potatoes provide a creamy base that complements the robust stew.

Tips for the Perfect Instant Pot Chicken Cacciatore

  • Use bone-in chicken thighs: While boneless chicken thighs work, bone-in thighs provide more flavor and tenderness, which is important for a dish like Chicken Cacciatore.
  • Sear the chicken first: Don’t skip the searing step! This helps develop a deeper flavor in the chicken and the sauce.
  • Customize the sauce: Feel free to adjust the seasonings to suit your tastes. Add more red pepper flakes if you like heat or experiment with other herbs like thyme or bay leaves.
  • Wine: If you’re concerned about using alcohol, you can substitute the wine with additional chicken broth for a milder flavor.

How to Store and Reheat Leftovers

If you have leftovers, you’re in for a treat! The flavors of Chicken Cacciatore actually get better after sitting for a day or two. To store, place the chicken and sauce in an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 3 days.

To reheat, simply warm it up in a pot on the stove or in the microwave. If you find the sauce has thickened too much, add a splash of chicken broth to loosen it up before reheating.

Conclusion

Instant Pot Chicken Cacciatore is an easy and delicious way to bring a taste of Italy into your home without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of tender chicken, rich tomato sauce, and fragrant herbs makes this dish a satisfying, comforting meal for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting to explore the world of pressure cooking, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who loves hearty, flavorful stews. So grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and enjoy a delightful Italian meal in no time!
