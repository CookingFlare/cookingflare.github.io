A Delicious Italian-Style Prosciutto-Wrapped Chicken Recipe


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine and looking to add a touch of elegance to your dinner table, then this Italian-style prosciutto-wrapped chicken recipe is a must-try. Combining the rich flavors of prosciutto, fresh herbs, and tender chicken breast, this dish is sure to impress your family and friends. The best part? It’s surprisingly simple to make!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 slices of prosciutto
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Take each chicken breast and season it with salt and pepper on both sides. This will enhance the flavor of the chicken as it cooks.

  3. Lay two slices of prosciutto side by side on a clean surface. Place one chicken breast on top of the prosciutto slices.

  4. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, and olive oil. This will create a fragrant herb-infused oil.

  5. Spoon a generous amount of the herb-infused oil over the chicken breast, ensuring it is evenly coated.

  6. Carefully wrap the prosciutto slices around the chicken breast, sealing in the moisture and flavor.

  7. Repeat the process with the remaining chicken breasts.

  8. Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the wrapped chicken breasts to the skillet and sear them for approximately 2 minutes on each side, or until the prosciutto is crispy and golden.

  9. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and let the chicken cook for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

  10. Remove the skillet from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to distribute evenly throughout the meat.

  11. Slice the chicken breasts diagonally to showcase the beautiful prosciutto wrapping.

  12. Serve your Italian-style prosciutto-wrapped chicken with a side of roasted vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider stuffing the chicken breasts with some mozzarella cheese or sun-dried tomatoes before wrapping them in prosciutto.
  • If you don’t have fresh herbs on hand, you can use dried herbs instead. Just remember to use approximately half the amount called for in the recipe.
  • To ensure your prosciutto stays crispy, avoid covering the skillet while the chicken is cooking in the oven.
  • If you prefer a sauce to accompany your chicken, you can create a simple pan sauce using the drippings from the skillet. Just deglaze the skillet with some white wine or chicken broth, simmer it until reduced, and then whisk in a tablespoon of butter to create a silky sauce.

With this delicious Italian-style prosciutto-wrapped chicken recipe, you can bring a taste of Italy to your dinner table. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply want to elevate your weeknight meals, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. The combination of tender chicken, savory prosciutto, and fragrant herbs creates a dish that is both comforting and sophisticated. So, gather your ingredients, put on some Italian music, and get ready to enjoy a truly memorable meal!
