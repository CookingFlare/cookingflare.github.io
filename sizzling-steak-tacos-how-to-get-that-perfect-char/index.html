Sizzling Steak Tacos: How to Get That Perfect Char


Taco lovers rejoice! Today, we’re diving into the world of sizzling steak tacos and uncovering the secrets to achieving that perfect char on your meat. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a delicious homemade meal, these steak tacos are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, grab your apron, fire up the grill, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of skirt steak
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Corn tortillas
  • Toppings: diced onions, chopped cilantro, lime wedges, salsa, and guacamole (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to high heat. While the grill is heating up, let’s prepare the steak.

  2. Start by seasoning the skirt steak with salt and pepper to taste. Make sure to season both sides generously, as this will enhance the flavor of the meat.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, smoked paprika, cumin, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  4. Brush the marinade onto both sides of the steak, ensuring an even coating. Allow the steak to marinate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors penetrate the meat.

  5. Once the grill is hot, place the steak directly onto the grates. Cook for approximately 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time according to your desired level of doneness.

  6. While the steak is grilling, warm up the corn tortillas on the grill for a minute or two on each side. This step will add a delightful smoky flavor to your tacos.

  7. Once the steak is cooked to your liking, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. This step is crucial as it allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a tender and juicy steak.

  8. Slice the steak against the grain into thin strips. This technique ensures that each bite of your taco is tender and easy to chew.

  9. Heat up the tortillas on the grill for a few seconds on each side, then transfer them to a plate.

  10. Assemble your sizzling steak tacos by placing a few strips of steak onto each tortilla. Top with diced onions, chopped cilantro, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice. For an extra kick, add salsa and guacamole to your liking.

  11. Serve immediately and enjoy the sizzling goodness of your homemade steak tacos!

Tips for the Perfect Char

  • Make sure your grill is preheated to high heat before cooking the steak. This will create a beautiful sear and char on the meat.

  • Season the steak generously with salt and pepper. Don’t be afraid to be liberal with your seasoning, as it will enhance the flavor of the meat.

  • Let the steak rest after grilling. This step allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more tender and flavorful steak.

  • Slice the steak against the grain. This technique ensures that each bite of your taco is tender and easy to chew.

  • Warm up the tortillas on the grill before assembling your tacos. This step adds a smoky flavor and enhances the overall taste experience.

Now that you have the secrets to achieving that perfect char on your steak, it’s time to gather your ingredients and start grilling! These sizzling steak tacos are guaranteed to be a hit at your next gathering or a satisfying meal for a cozy night in. So, fire up the grill and get ready to savor the deliciousness of these mouthwatering tacos. Bon appétit!
