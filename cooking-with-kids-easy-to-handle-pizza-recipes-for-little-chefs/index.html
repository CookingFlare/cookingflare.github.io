Cooking with Kids: Easy-to-Handle Pizza Recipes for Little Chefs


Pizza-making is a fun and entertaining activity that can bring your family closer. Including your little ones in the kitchen for family meal preparation can be an excellent opportunity to bond and teach them essential life skills. In this cooking with kids blog post, we have some easy-to-handle pizza recipes that will allow your little chefs to showcase their creativity and enthusiasm while enjoying the process of eating a delightful dinner.

For these recipes, we have taken special care to select safe, simple, and non-hazardous cooking tasks that your children can do independently or with minimal adult supervision. Embrace the mess and encourage creativity, as this experience is all about discovering different flavors and textures! Let’s dive into these scrumptious and fantastic recipes.

1. Build-Your-Own Mini Vegan Pizzas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made vegan pizza base (homemade or store-bought)
  • Vegan tomato sauce
  • Vegan cheese (grated)
  • Selection of veggies (e.g., bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, cherry tomatoes)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Bamboo skewers

Instructions:

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 200°C (400°F).

  2. Prepare the pizza bases by brushing them lightly with olive oil. This will prevent the crust from getting soggy.

  3. Bake the bases for 5-7 minutes or until they are slightly crispy.

  4. Remove the bottoms from the oven and spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly all over the surface.

  5. Let your little chefs choose their favorite garnishes like veggies and vegan cheese.

  6. Return the pizzas to the oven and bake for another 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.

  7. Carefully remove the pizzas from the oven and let them cool for 2-3 minutes.

  8. To make it easier for the kids to assemble their own mini vegan pizzas, use bamboo skewers to slide the desired toppings onto each base.

  9. Sit down, enjoy, and savor the smiles on their faces as they taste the fantastic pizzas they built themselves!

2. Three-Cheese Veggie Stuffed Crust Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Vegan tomato sauce
  • Vegan mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Vegan parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Vegan cream cheese
  • Selection of veggies (e.g., bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, cherry tomatoes)
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).

  2. Take the pizza dough and shape it into a circle, larger than your usual pizza base.

  3. Pinch and fold the outer edges of the dough inwards, creating a raised border.

  4. With your fingertips, weave the stretched dough’s extra edge around the base, forming a crust.

  5. Brush the olive oil evenly on the dough’s outer rim. This step adds a tasteful crunch to the crust.

  6. Spread the tomato sauce generously on the pizza dough, leaving a margin around the crust.

  7. Layer the cheese (mozzarella, parmesan, and cream cheese) and arrange the veggies on top of the cheese.

  8. Place the pizza in the oven and bake at 200°C (400°F) for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden brown.

  9. Remove from the oven, allow the pizza to cool for a few minutes, and cut into slices.

  10. Serve with excitement and relish your incredible creation that brings together everyone’s favorite food – pizza!

Now that you have some fantastic DIY pizza recipes for your little chefs to embrace, make sure to set aside some quality family time in the kitchen. Easy-to-handle recipes help convert indecisive kids into eager young culinary artists. Bon appétit, and don’t worry about making a mess – the real magic is in the memories you’ll create!
