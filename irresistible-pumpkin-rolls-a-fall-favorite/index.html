Irresistible Pumpkin Rolls: A Fall Favorite


As the leaves start to turn and the air becomes crisp, it’s time to indulge in the warm, comforting flavors of fall. One classic treat that never fails to delight during this season is the irresistible pumpkin roll. This delectable dessert is a perfect blend of moist, spiced cake and creamy, luscious filling, making it a favorite for autumn gatherings, holiday parties, or simply cozy nights at home. In this blog post, I’ll share with you my tried and true recipe for creating the most irresistible pumpkin rolls that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

To begin, here are the ingredients you’ll need to gather:

For the Pumpkin Cake:

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup pumpkin puree
  • Confectioners’ sugar (for dusting)

For the Cream Cheese Filling:

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Optional Garnish:

  • Additional confectioners’ sugar for dusting

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pumpkin Cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 10x15-inch jelly roll pan and line it with parchment paper, then grease the parchment paper as well.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and salt until well combined.

  3. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and granulated sugar together until thick and pale. Add the pumpkin puree and mix until fully combined.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  6. Bake for 13-15 minutes or until the cake springs back when lightly touched.

  7. While the cake is baking, lay a clean kitchen towel flat on the counter and dust it with confectioners’ sugar.

  8. Once the cake is done, immediately invert it onto the prepared towel. Carefully peel off the parchment paper, then starting from a short end, roll the cake and the towel together. Place the rolled cake on a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 2: Prepare the Cream Cheese Filling

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the cream cheese, confectioners’ sugar, butter, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  2. Once the cake has cooled, carefully unroll it and spread the cream cheese filling evenly over the cake, leaving a small border around the edges.

  3. Gently re-roll the cake without the towel, starting from the same short end, and place it seam side down on a serving platter.

Step 3: Optional Garnish

If desired, dust the pumpkin roll with additional confectioners’ sugar for a decorative touch.

Serving and Storage

Once assembled, the pumpkin roll can be served immediately, or refrigerated for up to 3 days. To serve, slice the roll into rounds and enjoy with a warm cup of coffee or tea.

Conclusion

There you have it - a delightful recipe for creating irresistible pumpkin rolls that will surely become a fall favorite in your home. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a taste of autumn, these pumpkin rolls are a delicious way to celebrate the season. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the warm, inviting flavors of fall with every delightful bite of these irresistible pumpkin rolls!
