Quick and Easy Garlic Butter Shrimp Recipe


If you’re a fan of seafood and are looking for a quick and easy recipe to satisfy your cravings, then you’re in for a treat! This garlic butter shrimp recipe is not only incredibly delicious but also a breeze to whip up, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. The combination of succulent shrimp, aromatic garlic, and rich butter creates a flavor explosion that is sure to impress your taste buds. Let’s dive right into the details of this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients

To get started, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Prep the Shrimp: Ensure that the shrimp is thoroughly cleaned, peeled, and deveined. Pat them dry with a paper towel and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  2. Sauté the Garlic: In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter and olive oil together. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.

  3. Cook the Shrimp: Once the garlic is aromatic, add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet in a single layer. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Avoid overcooking the shrimp to maintain their tender texture.

  4. Season and Garnish: Sprinkle the paprika and red pepper flakes over the shrimp, giving them a gentle toss to ensure even coating. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Finish off by garnishing with freshly chopped parsley.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the garlic butter shrimp to a serving platter, squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top, and serve immediately while still hot. This dish pairs wonderfully with crusty bread, steamed rice, or a simple green salad.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra depth of flavor, consider adding a splash of white wine or a squeeze of lemon juice to the garlic butter sauce.
  • If you prefer a creamier texture, you can incorporate a touch of heavy cream into the pan sauce just before serving.
  • Feel free to customize the level of spice by adjusting the amount of red pepper flakes to suit your taste buds.
  • Experiment with different herbs such as thyme, oregano, or basil to elevate the flavor profile of the dish.

In Conclusion

With minimal effort and a handful of ingredients, you can whip up a gourmet-worthy dish that is guaranteed to impress. This quick and easy garlic butter shrimp recipe is a testament to the fact that sometimes the simplest recipes pack the most flavor. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your skillet, and get ready to indulge in a culinary delight that will have everyone asking for seconds!
