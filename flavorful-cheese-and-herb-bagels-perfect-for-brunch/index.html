Flavorful Cheese and Herb Bagels: Perfect for Brunch


Bagels are a beloved staple in many households, but if you’re looking to take your bagel game to the next level, then these flavorful cheese and herb bagels are a must-try. Perfect for brunch or any time you’re craving a savory treat, these bagels are a delightful blend of comforting flavors and aromatic herbs. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and elevate your brunch experience.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons of instant yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons of salt
  • 1 ½ cups of warm water
  • 1 ½ cups of shredded cheese (such as cheddar or Gruyère)
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh herbs (such as rosemary, thyme, or chives), finely chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Gradually add the warm water to the dry ingredients, mixing until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

Step 2: Add the Cheese and Herbs

  1. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air.
  2. Gently knead in the shredded cheese and finely chopped herbs until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

Step 3: Shape the Bagels

  1. Divide the dough into 8 equal portions.
  2. Roll each portion into a ball, then use your thumb to poke a hole through the center, stretching the dough to form a bagel shape.
  3. Place the shaped bagels on a parchment-lined baking sheet, cover them with a kitchen towel, and let them rest for 15 minutes.

Step 4: Boil and Bake

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a simmer.
  3. Gently place the bagels, two at a time, into the simmering water for 1 minute, flipping them halfway through.
  4. Remove the bagels from the water using a slotted spoon and place them back on the baking sheet.
  5. Brush the tops of the bagels with the beaten egg wash.
  6. Bake the bagels for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and have a slightly crispy exterior.

Step 5: Enjoy!

  1. Allow the bagels to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  2. These cheese and herb bagels are delicious on their own or with a schmear of cream cheese. They also make a delightful base for savory breakfast sandwiches or as an accompaniment to a brunch spread.

Conclusion

These flavorful cheese and herb bagels are a delightful twist on the classic bagel, boasting a savory and aromatic profile that’s perfect for brunch or any occasion. The combination of gooey melted cheese and fragrant herbs will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more. Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply treating yourself to a special homemade breakfast, these bagels are sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the irresistible flavors of these cheese and herb bagels.
