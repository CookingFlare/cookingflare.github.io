Nut-Free Nutritionist: 55 Wholesome Nut-Free Recipes for a Healthier Lifestyle


Welcome to our Nut-Free Nutritionist series, where we explore the world of delicious, nourishing meals without the use of nuts. Whether you have an allergy, a sensitivity, or simply prefer to avoid nuts, this collection of 55 recipes will cater to your every cravings. With a variety of dishes inspired by cuisines from around the globe, you’re sure to find something new and exciting to try. Let’s dive in and see what treats await!

1. Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed and drained
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups assorted vegetables, chopped (e.g., bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, red onion)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried herbs (e.g., thyme, basil, or oregano)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine quinoa and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes or until water is absorbed.
  3. In a baking dish, spread vegetables and drizzle with olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes or until tender.
  4. Mix quinoa and roasted vegetables together before serving.

2. Chickpea and Spinach Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • Lemon juice to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs for garnish, if desired

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and cook until softened.
  2. Stir in garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute before adding curry powder. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, chickpeas, and vegetable broth. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add spinach and simmer until wilted, about 5 minutes. Season with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Serve and garnish with fresh herbs, if desired.

3. Sriracha Tofu and Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 2 tablespoons tamari (or soy sauce)
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (divided)
  • 2 cups assorted vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas), sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cut tofu into 1-inch cubes and toss with tamari and sriracha sauce. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together 1 tablespoon cornstarch with water to create a slurry.
  3. In a large pan or wok, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil over medium-high heat. Add tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, heat the remaining olive oil and add vegetables. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender-crisp.
  5. Stir in the tofu and pour the cornstarch slurry over the mixture. Cook for 2 more minutes, until the sauce has thickened. Season with salt and pepper.

4. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized sweet potatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and chili powder to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425째F (220째C). Pierce sweet potatoes with a fork and microwave for 5 minutes before roasting for 30 minutes or until tender.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add black beans, corn, and onion. Season with salt, pepper, and chili powder. Cook for 10 minutes until warmed through.
  3. Cut sweet potatoes in half and remove flesh, leaving a thin layer for structure.
  4. Fill the sweet potato shells with the black bean and corn mixture, top with sliced avocado, and serve.

5. Strawberry-Banana Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen ripe banana
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup coconut water
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • Toppings (e.g., granola, chia seeds, sliced almonds, berries, sliced banana)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine banana, strawberries, Greek yogurt, and coconut water. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add rolled oats and blend again, until well incorporated.
  3. Pour the smoothie mixture into a bowl and top with desired toppings.
