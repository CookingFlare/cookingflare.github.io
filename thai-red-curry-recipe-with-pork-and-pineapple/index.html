Thai Red Curry Recipe with Pork and Pineapple


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat today! In this blog post, we’ll be exploring the delicious flavors of Thai red curry with a twist – pork and pineapple. This combination of tender pork, sweet pineapple, and aromatic spices creates a mouthwatering dish that will transport your taste buds straight to the bustling streets of Thailand. So, grab your apron, sharpen your knife, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 500g pork tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  2. Add the red curry paste to the pan and stir-fry for another minute, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the sliced pork to the pan. Stir-fry until the pork is cooked through and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.

  4. Add the sliced onion and red bell pepper to the pan and continue stir-frying for another 3 minutes, until the vegetables are slightly softened.

  5. Now it’s time to add the star ingredient – pineapple chunks! Add them to the pan and gently stir to combine with the other ingredients.

  6. Pour in the coconut milk, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir well to ensure everything is evenly coated in the creamy coconut sauce.

  7. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the curry for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to develop and the pork to become tender.

  8. While the curry is simmering, prepare the rice according to the package instructions. Fluffy, fragrant rice is the perfect accompaniment to this spicy dish.

  9. Once the curry is done simmering, remove it from the heat and let it cool slightly. This will allow the flavors to further infuse, resulting in a more complex and delicious curry.

  10. Serve the Thai red curry with pork and pineapple over a bed of steamed rice. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an added burst of flavor and freshness.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You’ve successfully cooked up a tantalizing Thai red curry with pork and pineapple. The combination of tender pork, sweet pineapple, and aromatic spices creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a seasoned Thai food lover or a curious beginner, this recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your loved ones, set the table, and enjoy a taste of Thailand from the comfort of your own home. Bon appétit!

Note: Feel free to adjust the amount of red curry paste according to your spice tolerance. You can also add other vegetables such as bamboo shoots or baby corn for additional texture and flavor.
