Gluten-Free Holiday Treats for Festive Celebrations


The holiday season is a time for joy, laughter, and delicious treats! If you or your loved ones follow a gluten-free diet, you don’t have to miss out on the festive indulgences. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of mouthwatering gluten-free holiday treats that are perfect for celebrating the most wonderful time of the year.

1. Peppermint Chocolate Brownie Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp peppermint extract
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and grease a mini muffin tin.
  2. In a bowl, mix almond flour, cocoa powder, and coconut sugar.
  3. Add coconut oil, eggs, and peppermint extract. Stir until well combined.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop the batter into the muffin tin and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Let them cool before enjoying these fudgy brownie bites.

2. Cinnamon Sugar Pecan Pie Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and line a baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. Mix almond flour, coconut flour, coconut oil, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and cinnamon until a dough forms.
  3. Press the dough into the baking dish and sprinkle chopped pecans on top.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Let it cool, then slice into bars to enjoy the rich flavors of pecan pie.

3. Gingerbread Cookie Cutouts

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cloves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and spices.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream coconut sugar, molasses, and coconut oil.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients to form a dough.
  5. Roll out the dough, cut out shapes, and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Once cooled, decorate the gingerbread cookies for a festive touch.

4. Cranberry Orange Bliss Balls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almonds
  • 1 cup dates
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Shredded coconut (for rolling)

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, blend almonds until finely ground.
  2. Add dates, cranberries, orange zest, and vanilla. Blend until a sticky mixture forms.
  3. Roll the mixture into balls and coat with shredded coconut.
  4. Chill in the fridge before serving these sweet and tangy bliss balls.

Conclusion

With these delectable gluten-free holiday treats, you can savor the festive season without compromising on taste or dietary preferences. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply treating yourself, these recipes are sure to add a touch of sweetness to your celebrations. Enjoy the magic of the holidays with these flavorful and wholesome delights!

Remember, the holidays are about sharing love and joy through every bite, so whip up these gluten-free treats and spread the cheer this festive season!
