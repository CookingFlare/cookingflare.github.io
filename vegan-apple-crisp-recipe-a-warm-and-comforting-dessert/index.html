Vegan Apple Crisp Recipe: A Warm and Comforting Dessert

3 min · 492 words

There’s something undeniably comforting about a warm apple crisp straight out of the oven. The sweet aroma of baked apples, the crunchy and buttery topping, and the hint of cinnamon create a dessert that is both rustic and delightful. And the best part? You can enjoy all of this deliciousness without any animal products! That’s right, this Vegan Apple Crisp recipe is the perfect dessert for those following a plant-based lifestyle or simply looking to incorporate more vegan options into their diet.

Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

For the Crispy Topping:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, maple syrup, lemon juice, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Toss until the apples are well coated with the mixture.

  3. Transfer the apple mixture to a baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  4. In another bowl, mix together the rolled oats, all-purpose flour, almond flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Stir until well combined.

  5. Pour the melted vegan butter over the oat mixture and mix until the butter is evenly distributed and the mixture becomes crumbly.

  6. Sprinkle the oat topping over the apples, covering them completely.

  7. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apples are tender.

  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  9. Serve the Vegan Apple Crisp warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vegan vanilla ice cream for an extra treat.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different types of apples to find your favorite combination. Granny Smith apples add a tartness that pairs well with the sweetness of the crisp topping, while Honeycrisp or Gala apples offer a sweeter flavor.
  • Add a handful of chopped walnuts, pecans, or almonds to the topping for an extra crunch.
  • If you prefer a gluten-free version of this recipe, simply replace the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend.
  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try adding a sprinkle of cardamom or ginger to the apple filling.

Conclusion

There you have it: a Vegan Apple Crisp recipe that is sure to satisfy your cravings for a warm and comforting dessert. The combination of tender apples, sweet maple syrup, and a crispy oat topping creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. With its simple ingredients and easy preparation, this recipe is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a cozy family dinner or a holiday gathering. So go ahead, indulge in this delightful vegan dessert and discover the joy of guilt-free indulgence.
