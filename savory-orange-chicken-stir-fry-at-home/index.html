Savory Orange Chicken Stir Fry at Home


Are you craving a delicious and savory dish that will tantalize your taste buds? Look no further than this amazing Savory Orange Chicken Stir Fry recipe that you can easily whip up in the comfort of your own kitchen! This vibrant and flavorful stir fry combines the tangy zest of oranges with tender chicken and a medley of fresh vegetables, creating a dish that is both satisfying and bursting with citrusy goodness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken:

    • In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with soy sauce and cornstarch. Mix well to coat the chicken evenly. Let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes.

  2. Stir Fry the Chicken:

    • Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir fry until it is browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.

  3. Prepare the Vegetables:

    • In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Stir fry the bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, garlic, and ginger until the vegetables are slightly tender yet still crisp.

  4. Combine the Flavors:

    • Return the cooked chicken to the pan with the vegetables. Pour in the orange juice, orange zest, hoisin sauce, and rice vinegar. Stir well to combine all the flavors. Allow the sauce to thicken slightly.

  5. Season and Serve:

    • Season the stir fry with salt and pepper according to your taste. Once everything is well-cooked and the sauce has thickened, remove the pan from heat.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Serve the Savory Orange Chicken Stir Fry hot over a bed of steamed rice. Garnish with additional orange zest or sliced green onions for an extra pop of freshness.

Tips:

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce.
  • You can customize this recipe by adding other vegetables like water chestnuts, baby corn, or mushrooms.
  • Adjust the sweetness and tanginess of the sauce by tweaking the amount of orange juice and hoisin sauce to suit your preference.

There you have it! A mouthwatering Savory Orange Chicken Stir Fry that will take your home-cooking game to the next level. This dish is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a flavorful weekend meal. Impress your family and friends with this easy-to-make yet impressive recipe that delivers on taste and freshness. Get your ingredients ready and start cooking up a storm in your kitchen today!

Remember, the key to a great stir fry is high heat, fresh ingredients, and a touch of creativity. Enjoy every bite of this savory and citrusy delight!
