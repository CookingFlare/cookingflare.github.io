Satisfy Your Cravings: 5 Mouthwatering Shrimp Wrap Recipes


Are you a seafood lover looking for a delicious and satisfying meal? Look no further! In this post, we will be sharing five mouthwatering shrimp wrap recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. These wraps are not only packed with flavors but also incredibly versatile, making them perfect for any occasion. So, let’s dive right in and discover these amazing recipes!

1. Spicy Shrimp Lettuce Wraps

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Lettuce leaves for wrapping
  • Optional toppings: chopped peanuts, cilantro, lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the chili flakes, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and honey. Pour the sauce over the cooked shrimp and toss until coated.
  4. To assemble the wraps, place a spoonful of the shrimp mixture onto a lettuce leaf. Top with optional toppings if desired.
  5. Roll up the lettuce leaf, tucking in the sides as you go. Serve immediately and enjoy!

2. Cajun Shrimp and Avocado Wraps

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Tortillas for wrapping

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, toss the shrimp with Cajun seasoning until well coated.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the seasoned shrimp and cook until pink and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the avocado, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet until pliable. Place a spoonful of the shrimp mixture onto each tortilla, followed by the avocado and tomato mixture.
  5. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Serve immediately and enjoy!

3. Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Wraps

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup sliced red bell pepper
  • Tortillas for wrapping

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, marinate the shrimp in teriyaki sauce for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the marinated shrimp and cook until pink and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the pineapple chunks, shredded lettuce, and sliced red bell pepper.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet until pliable. Place a spoonful of the shrimp mixture onto each tortilla, followed by the pineapple and lettuce mixture.
  5. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Serve immediately and enjoy!

4. Greek Shrimp Wrap

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Tortillas for wrapping

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shrimp, olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried dill, salt, and black pepper. Toss until the shrimp are well coated.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add the shrimp and cook until pink and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet until pliable. Place a spoonful of the shrimp mixture onto each tortilla, followed by the Greek salad mixture.
  5. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Serve immediately and enjoy!

5. Buffalo Shrimp Wraps

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup sliced celery
  • Tortillas for wrapping

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shrimp, buffalo sauce, and melted butter. Toss until the shrimp are well coated.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add the shrimp and cook until pink and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the ranch dressing, shredded lettuce, and sliced celery.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet until pliable. Place a spoonful of the shrimp mixture onto each tortilla, followed by the lettuce and celery mixture.
  5. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Serve immediately and enjoy!

There you have it - five mouthwatering shrimp wrap recipes that will surely satisfy your cravings! These wraps are packed with flavors, easy to make, and perfect for any mealtime. Whether you prefer spicy, tangy, or refreshing flavors, there’s a shrimp wrap recipe for everyone. So, gather your ingredients and start cooking these delightful wraps today!
