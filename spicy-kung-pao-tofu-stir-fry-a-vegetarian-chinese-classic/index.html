Spicy Kung Pao Tofu Stir Fry: A Vegetarian Chinese Classic


If you’re a fan of Chinese cuisine and crave a flavorful and spicy dish, look no further than Spicy Kung Pao Tofu Stir Fry. This vegetarian twist on a Chinese classic is packed with bold flavors and a satisfying crunch that will leave you coming back for more. Made with tofu, peanuts, and a medley of vegetables, this dish is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and get started!

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted peanuts
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup diced zucchini
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch, mixed with 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until golden brown and crispy on all sides. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the peanuts and toast them for a few minutes until they become fragrant. Remove the peanuts from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the diced onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic and ginger are fragrant.

  4. Add the diced bell peppers, celery, carrots, and zucchini to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and rice vinegar. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the skillet and stir to coat evenly.

  6. Return the tofu and peanuts to the skillet and toss everything together. Cook for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Stir in the cornstarch mixture to thicken the sauce. Continue stirring until the sauce has thickened and coats the tofu and vegetables.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If you prefer a spicier dish, you can add a dash of chili flakes or Sriracha sauce.

  9. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro, if desired.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Serve the Spicy Kung Pao Tofu Stir Fry over a bed of steamed rice or noodles for a complete meal.
  • Pair it with a side of steamed broccoli or bok choy for an extra dose of greens.
  • Top it off with a sprinkle of sesame seeds for added texture and flavor.

Final Thoughts:

Spicy Kung Pao Tofu Stir Fry is a versatile and satisfying dish that will surely become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe offers a delicious and nutritious option that doesn’t compromise on taste. The combination of crispy tofu, crunchy peanuts, and vibrant vegetables tossed in a spicy and savory sauce creates a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing. So, gather your ingredients and give this vegetarian Chinese classic a try – you won’t be disappointed!
