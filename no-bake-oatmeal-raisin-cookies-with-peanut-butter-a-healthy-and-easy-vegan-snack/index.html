No-Bake Oatmeal Raisin Cookies with Peanut Butter: A Healthy and Easy Vegan Snack


Are you looking for a delicious and guilt-free treat that requires no baking? Look no further! These no-bake oatmeal raisin cookies with peanut butter are not only incredibly tasty but also vegan and packed with wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re vegan or simply looking for a healthier snack option, these cookies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Plus, they’re so easy to make that even novice bakers can whip them up in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, peanut butter, maple syrup, raisins, chopped nuts (if using), vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt. Stir well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Once the mixture is well combined, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Chilling the mixture will make it easier to handle and shape into cookies.

  3. After the mixture has chilled, remove it from the refrigerator. Using your hands or a cookie scoop, portion out about 1 1/2 tablespoons of the mixture and roll it into a ball. Place the ball on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Repeat this step until you have used up all the mixture, spacing the cookies about 1 inch apart.

  4. Once all the cookies are shaped, gently press down on each one with the back of a fork to flatten them slightly. This step will give them the classic cookie appearance.

  5. Transfer the baking sheet with the cookies back to the refrigerator and let them chill for another 30 minutes to set.

  6. After the cookies have set, they are ready to be enjoyed! Store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week.

Why Choose No-Bake Oatmeal Raisin Cookies with Peanut Butter?

These no-bake oatmeal raisin cookies with peanut butter are not only delicious but also offer several health benefits. Let’s take a closer look at why you should include these cookies in your snacking routine:

  • Vegan-friendly: These cookies are completely plant-based, making them suitable for vegans and those following a dairy-free lifestyle. They are a great alternative to traditional cookies that often contain eggs and butter.

  • Wholesome ingredients: Made with rolled oats, natural peanut butter, and raisins, these cookies are packed with wholesome ingredients. Rolled oats are an excellent source of fiber and can help promote healthy digestion. Peanut butter provides a good dose of healthy fats and protein, while raisins add natural sweetness and a touch of chewiness.

  • No baking required: The beauty of these cookies lies in their simplicity. With no need for baking, you can satisfy your cookie cravings without heating up the oven. This also means you can enjoy these treats all year round, even during the hot summer months.

  • Quick and easy: With just a few simple steps, these cookies come together in no time. Whether you’re in a hurry or looking for a fun activity to do with kids, these no-bake oatmeal raisin cookies with peanut butter are the perfect choice.

Final Thoughts

No-bake oatmeal raisin cookies with peanut butter are the ultimate healthy and easy vegan snack. They are bursting with flavor, require minimal effort, and are made with wholesome ingredients. Whether you enjoy them as an afternoon pick-me-up, a post-workout snack, or a sweet treat after dinner, these cookies will surely become a staple in your kitchen. So go ahead, give them a try, and indulge guilt-free!
