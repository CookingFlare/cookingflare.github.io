Spicy Szechuan Beef and Vegetable Stir Fry: A Fiery Delight


Are you a fan of bold and spicy flavors? Look no further! In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps of creating a mouthwatering Spicy Szechuan Beef and Vegetable Stir Fry. Prepare your taste buds for an explosion of fiery delight!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons Szechuan peppercorns, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon chili paste (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the sliced beef and stir fry until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  3. Add the sliced bell peppers, julienned carrot, snap peas, and broccoli florets to the skillet. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the crushed Szechuan peppercorns, soy sauce, rice vinegar, hoisin sauce, brown sugar, and chili paste. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the skillet and toss to coat evenly.

  5. Return the browned beef to the skillet and stir fry for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the beef is cooked through and the flavors are well combined.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Be cautious with the salt as the soy sauce and hoisin sauce already provide a salty flavor.

  7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.

  8. Serve the Spicy Szechuan Beef and Vegetable Stir Fry hot with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy the fiery flavors and the satisfying crunch of the vegetables!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add some dried red chili flakes or fresh red chilies to the stir fry.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, simply omit the beef and increase the amount of vegetables. You can also substitute the beef with tofu or seitan for a protein-packed alternative.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables according to your preference. Snow peas, bok choy, or baby corn can be great additions.
  • To save time, you can pre-cut the vegetables and marinate the beef in the sauce ahead of time. This way, you can whip up this delicious stir fry in no time.

Gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and get ready to create a meal that will excite your taste buds and impress your loved ones. Happy cooking!
