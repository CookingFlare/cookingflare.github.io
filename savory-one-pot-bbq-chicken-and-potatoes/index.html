Savory One Pot BBQ Chicken and Potatoes


Are you looking for a simple yet delicious meal that requires minimal effort and cleanup? Look no further than this savory one pot BBQ chicken and potatoes recipe. Packed with flavor and easy to prepare, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this mouthwatering meal in just one pot.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, cubed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by seasoning the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, and paprika. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Place the chicken thighs skin side down and cook until golden brown, then flip and brown the other side. Once the chicken is nicely browned, remove it from the pot and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Onions and Garlic: In the same pot, add the sliced onions and minced garlic. Sauté until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Add the Potatoes and BBQ Sauce: Stir in the cubed potatoes, coating them in the flavorful mixture of onions and garlic. Pour the BBQ sauce over the potatoes and stir to combine.

  4. Return the Chicken: Nestle the browned chicken thighs back into the pot, arranging them on top of the potato and BBQ sauce mixture.

  5. Simmer to Perfection: Cover the pot with a lid and let the chicken and potatoes simmer over medium heat for about 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the potatoes are tender.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken and potatoes are done, remove the pot from the heat. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the savory one pot BBQ chicken and potatoes directly from the pot, allowing everyone to dig in and enjoy the delicious flavors.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, consider adding a pinch of cayenne pepper or a splash of hot sauce to the BBQ sauce mixture.
  • If you’re a fan of crispy chicken skin, you can finish the dish under the broiler for a few minutes after it’s done simmering to achieve that perfect crispy texture.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite BBQ sauce flavors, whether it’s smoky, tangy, or sweet.

Conclusion

This savory one pot BBQ chicken and potatoes recipe is a game-changer for anyone who loves a hearty, flavorful meal without the hassle of using multiple pots and pans. With just a single pot and a handful of simple ingredients, you can create a satisfying dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Give this recipe a try and savor the delightful flavors of tender chicken and perfectly cooked potatoes, all infused with the rich taste of BBQ sauce. It’s a surefire way to elevate your weeknight dinner game and impress your family and friends with minimal effort.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, grab your trusty pot, and get ready to whip up a delectable one pot BBQ chicken and potatoes feast that will leave everyone asking for the recipe. Happy cooking!
