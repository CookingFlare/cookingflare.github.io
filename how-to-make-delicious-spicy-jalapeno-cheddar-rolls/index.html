How to Make Delicious Spicy Jalapeno Cheddar Rolls


If you’re craving something that combines the rich flavors of cheese, the kick of spice, and the satisfying softness of freshly baked bread, then these spicy jalapeno cheddar rolls are the perfect solution. Whether you’re planning a family dinner, hosting a casual gathering, or just want a unique snack, these rolls will leave everyone coming back for more. With the perfect balance of heat and cheesy goodness, these rolls are an irresistible treat.

In this post, we’ll guide you through the entire process of making jalapeno cheddar rolls from scratch. From preparing the dough to the final golden, cheesy product, you’ll discover tips and tricks to ensure your rolls turn out perfectly every time.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make these spicy jalapeno cheddar rolls, you’ll need the following ingredients:

Dough Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour – The base for your dough. You can also substitute a portion with whole wheat flour for a more hearty texture.
  • 1 tablespoon sugar – Adds a touch of sweetness to balance the spicy heat.
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast – This will help your dough rise.
  • 1 teaspoon salt – To enhance all the flavors and season the bread.
  • 1 cup warm milk – Milk provides moisture and richness to the dough.
  • 1/4 cup melted butter – Butter gives the rolls a rich flavor and tender texture.
  • 1 egg – To bind the dough and help it rise.
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil – Helps in greasing and adds flavor.

Filling Ingredients:

  • 2-3 fresh jalapenos – Finely diced. The amount depends on how spicy you want the rolls to be. Remove the seeds for less heat.
  • 1 ½ cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese – Sharp cheddar has a bold flavor that pairs well with the heat of the jalapenos.
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder – Adds depth and a savory undertone to the filling.
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro – Optional, but cilantro adds a fresh and zesty flavor that complements the heat of the jalapenos.

Topping (Optional):

  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese – For a cheesy topping that melts beautifully over the rolls.
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter – Brushed over the rolls to give them a golden, glossy finish.

How to Make Spicy Jalapeno Cheddar Rolls

Step 1: Activating the Yeast

The first step in making the dough is activating the yeast. This is an essential part of the process, as it ensures your rolls rise properly.

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm milk and sugar. Make sure the milk is not too hot (about 110°F or 43°C). If it’s too hot, it could kill the yeast, preventing your rolls from rising.
  2. Sprinkle the active dry yeast over the milk and sugar mixture. Stir gently and let it sit for 5-10 minutes. You should see the yeast start to bubble and foam, indicating it’s alive and active.

Step 2: Preparing the Dough

Once the yeast is activated, it’s time to prepare the dough.

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Stir to mix evenly.
  2. Add the activated yeast mixture, melted butter, and egg to the dry ingredients. Mix everything together with a wooden spoon or your hands until it forms a rough dough.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 8-10 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook to knead the dough.
  4. Once the dough is kneaded, place it in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Let the dough rise for about 1-1.5 hours or until it has doubled in size.

Step 3: Preparing the Filling

While the dough is rising, you can prepare the spicy jalapeno cheddar filling.

  1. Finely chop the fresh jalapenos. Remember, the more seeds you include, the spicier the rolls will be. For a milder version, remove most or all of the seeds.
  2. In a bowl, mix the chopped jalapenos, shredded cheddar cheese, garlic powder, and fresh cilantro (if using). Set this mixture aside.

Step 4: Shaping the Rolls

After the dough has doubled in size, it’s time to shape the rolls.

  1. Punch down the risen dough to release the air.
  2. Roll the dough out on a floured surface into a rectangle about 12 inches by 18 inches (30 cm by 45 cm).
  3. Spread the jalapeno cheddar mixture evenly over the dough. Be generous but leave a small border around the edges.
  4. Starting from one long edge, roll the dough tightly into a log. Pinch the seams together to seal the roll.
  5. Slice the rolled dough into 12 equal pieces. You can use a sharp knife or dental floss to ensure clean cuts.

Step 5: Baking the Rolls

Now that your rolls are shaped and filled, it’s time to bake them.

  1. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Arrange the rolls in the prepared dish, making sure they are close but not touching. This allows them to rise together and bake into soft, pull-apart rolls.
  3. Cover the baking dish with a towel and let the rolls rise for another 30-45 minutes, or until they have puffed up.
  4. While the rolls are rising, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Once the rolls have risen, brush the tops with melted butter for a golden finish and optional extra flavor.
  6. Bake the rolls in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the tops are golden brown and the rolls sound hollow when tapped.

Step 6: Adding the Final Touches

If you want extra cheesy goodness, sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese on top of the rolls about 5 minutes before they finish baking. Let the cheese melt and turn bubbly and golden for a delicious, cheesy finish.

Once the rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool in the dish for a few minutes. Serve warm, and enjoy the spicy, cheesy goodness.

Tips for Perfect Spicy Jalapeno Cheddar Rolls

1. Adjust the Spice Level

If you love spice, add more jalapenos or keep some seeds in the mixture. For a milder version, remove the seeds and ribs of the peppers or use only one pepper. You can also try using pickled jalapenos for a different flavor profile.

2. Use Fresh Cheese

Freshly shredded sharp cheddar cheese melts better and has a more robust flavor than pre-shredded cheese. Shred the cheese yourself for the best results.

3. Don’t Overproof the Dough

While it’s important to let the dough rise, don’t let it overproof. If the dough rises too long, it can become too airy and lose its structure, affecting the texture of your rolls.

4. Let the Rolls Cool Slightly

These rolls are best served warm, but be careful not to burn yourself with the molten cheese! Let the rolls cool for a few minutes before diving in.

Variations of Spicy Jalapeno Cheddar Rolls

While the original recipe is already a hit, you can customize these rolls to suit your taste preferences. Here are a few ideas to inspire you:

1. Bacon Jalapeno Cheddar Rolls

Add crispy bacon bits to the filling for an extra savory element. The smoky flavor of bacon pairs wonderfully with the spice of the jalapenos.

2. Herb-Infused Rolls

Add fresh herbs like rosemary or thyme to the dough for an additional layer of flavor. These herbs will complement the spiciness of the jalapenos and add a savory note to each bite.

3. Vegan Version

You can easily make these rolls vegan by substituting the milk with almond milk or oat milk, the butter with plant-based butter, and the cheese with vegan cheddar. There are plenty of great vegan cheeses available that will melt and provide that delicious cheesy texture.

4. Cheesy Jalapeno Breadsticks

For a fun twist, shape the dough into long breadsticks instead of rolls. You can still fill them with the jalapeno cheddar mixture, and they’ll bake up into crispy, cheesy sticks perfect for dipping.

How to Store and Reheat

These spicy jalapeno cheddar rolls are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, but you can store them for later.

  1. Storage: Place leftover rolls in an airtight container or wrap them in foil. Store them at room temperature for up to 2-3 days. For longer storage, you can freeze the rolls.

  2. Reheating: To reheat, wrap the rolls in foil and place them in a 350°F (175°C) oven for about 10 minutes. For a quicker option, you can microwave them for about 20 seconds, but the texture may not be as soft as when freshly baked.

Conclusion

Making spicy jalapeno cheddar rolls from scratch is a rewarding experience that results in an incredibly flavorful and comforting dish. With the perfect balance of heat from the jalapenos and creaminess from the cheddar cheese, these rolls are sure to become a favorite in your household.

Whether you’re serving them as a side to a main dish, as part of a bread basket, or as a standalone snack, these rolls will impress your friends and family. Enjoy the process, experiment with variations, and most importantly, savor the delicious, spicy goodness of homemade jalapeno cheddar rolls.

Happy baking!
