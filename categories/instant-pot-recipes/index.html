Instant Pot Lentil Soup Recipe: A Nutritious and Filling Option

Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than this Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe. Packed with nutrients and bursting with flavor, this soup is the perfect option for a quick and nutritious meal. Ingredients 1 cup dried lentils 1 onion, diced 2 carrots, chopped 2 celery stalks, diced 3 cloves of garlic, minced 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1 bay leaf 4 cups vegetable broth Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Rinse the lentils under cold water and set them aside. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the sauté function. Add a drizzle of olive oil and sauté the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until they become tender and fragrant. Add the cumin, paprika, and bay leaf to the Instant Pot and stir well to combine the flavors. Pour in the vegetable broth and add the rinsed lentils. Give it a good stir and season with salt and pepper to taste. Secure the lid of the Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Select the manual or pressure cook function and set the timer for 15 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes. Then, carefully turn the pressure release valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure. Open the Instant Pot lid and give the soup a stir. The lentils should be tender and the flavors well combined. Serve the soup hot and garnish with fresh herbs, such as parsley or cilantro, if desired. Why Choose Instant Pot Lentil Soup? Not only is this Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe incredibly easy to make, but it also offers a host of health benefits. Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup Recipe for Vegetarian Comfort

There’s nothing quite as comforting as a warm bowl of soup on a chilly day. And when it comes to vegetarian comfort food, this Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup is a winner. Packed with flavor, protein, and nutrients, this recipe will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make in your Instant Pot, saving you time and effort in the kitchen. Ingredients 1 pound dried black beans, rinsed and soaked overnight 1 onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 bell pepper, diced 2 carrots, diced 2 stalks celery, diced 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced 1 tablespoon cumin 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon smoked paprika 1 teaspoon oregano 4 cups vegetable broth 2 cups water Salt and pepper, to taste Optional toppings: chopped cilantro, diced avocado, sour cream, lime wedges Instructions Drain and rinse the soaked black beans. Set aside. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and heat a tablespoon of oil. Add the diced onion, garlic, bell pepper, carrots, celery, and jalapeno pepper. Sauté for 5 minutes until the vegetables are softened. Add the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and oregano to the Instant Pot. Stir to coat the vegetables in the spices. Pour in the vegetable broth and water, followed by the soaked black beans. Give everything a good stir. Secure the lid on your Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 25 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes, then carefully quick-release any remaining pressure. Use an immersion blender to partially blend the soup, leaving some whole beans for texture. If you don’t have an immersion blender, carefully transfer a portion of the soup to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot. Taste the soup and season with salt and pepper as needed. Ladle the soup into bowls and top with your favorite garnishes, such as chopped cilantro, diced avocado, sour cream, and a squeeze of lime juice. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors of this Instant Pot Black Bean Soup! Why We Love This Recipe This Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup is a vegetarian’s dream come true. Not only does it offer a hearty and filling meal, but it’s also packed with plant-based protein from the black beans. The combination of spices, including cumin, chili powder, and smoked paprika, adds a depth of flavor that will keep you coming back for more. ...

Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee Recipe: A French-Inspired Dish

French cuisine is renowned for its rich flavors, intricate preparations, and exquisite presentation. Today, we’re going to bring a taste of France to your table with a classic dish that’s both elegant and effortless: Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee. This heavenly recipe is a perfect blend of tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices, all cooked to perfection in the Instant Pot. Ingredients 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces 2 tablespoons butter 1 medium onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup chicken broth 1 cup heavy cream 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper, to taste Fresh parsley, chopped (optional) Instructions Press the “Saute” button on the Instant Pot and melt the butter. Add the diced onion and cook until it’s translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they’re browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside. Add the flour to the pot and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. The mixture should form a smooth paste. Gradually add the chicken broth and heavy cream, whisking continuously to prevent lumps. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until it thickens slightly. Add the thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine. Return the chicken to the pot and stir to coat with the sauce. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to “Sealing”. Press the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” button and set the cooking time to 10 minutes at high pressure. When the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure. Open the lid and check the chicken for tenderness. If it’s not cooked to your liking, close the lid and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve the Chicken Fricassee hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired. Tips and Variations For a richer sauce, use 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream or add 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. To add some color and texture, stir in 1 cup of frozen peas during the last 2 minutes of cooking. For a spicy kick, add 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes to the sauce. This recipe freezes well, so feel free to make a batch and store it in the freezer for up to 3 months. Simply thaw and reheat when needed. Conclusion Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee is a show-stopping dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends. With its tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices, it’s a perfect representation of French cuisine. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make and requires minimal effort, thanks to the Instant Pot. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and experience the magic of French cooking in the comfort of your own home.
