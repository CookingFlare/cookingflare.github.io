Rustic Vegetable and Bean Soup: Wholesome and Satisfying


There’s something truly comforting about a bowl of hearty soup, especially when it’s packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor. Rustic Vegetable and Bean Soup is a classic dish that never fails to satisfy, offering a delightful blend of vegetables, beans, and aromatic herbs. This recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious, making it a perfect choice for a wholesome and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (15 oz) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.

  2. Add the diced carrots, celery, red bell pepper, zucchini, and green beans to the pot. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth. Stir in the cannellini beans and kidney beans. Season with dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.

  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Once the soup is ready, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Ladle the hot soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra depth of flavor, consider adding a Parmesan rind to the soup while it simmers. Remove it before serving.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can mash some of the beans against the side of the pot to create a creamier texture.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on what’s in season or what you have on hand. This soup is versatile and can accommodate various vegetable combinations.

Health Benefits

Rustic Vegetable and Bean Soup is not only a delight for the taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse. Packed with fiber, vitamins, and minerals from the colorful array of vegetables and beans, this soup is a great way to boost your daily nutrient intake. The combination of beans and vegetables provides a healthy dose of protein, making it a satisfying and wholesome option for vegetarians and meat-eaters alike.

Serving Suggestions

This soup is a complete meal on its own, but you can elevate the experience by serving it with a side of crusty bread or a simple green salad. The leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, allowing the flavors to develop even further.

Conclusion

Rustic Vegetable and Bean Soup is a timeless classic that embodies the essence of comfort and nourishment. With its vibrant colors, robust flavors, and nutritional benefits, this soup is a celebration of wholesome cooking. Whether enjoyed on a chilly evening or as a light lunch, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Try it out, savor the goodness, and share the joy of this wholesome and satisfying dish with your loved ones.
