Healthy Instant Pot Lentil Soup Recipe for a Hearty Meal


Are you looking for a comforting and nutritious meal that’s easy to make? Look no further than this Healthy Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe! Packed with protein, fiber, and a variety of vitamins and minerals, this hearty soup is not only delicious but also incredibly good for you. The best part? It can be prepared in a fraction of the time thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by setting the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add a splash of olive oil. Once the pot is hot, add the diced onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  2. Add the minced garlic, ground cumin, turmeric, paprika, and coriander to the pot. Stir well to coat the vegetables and let the spices toast for a minute or two, releasing their aromatic flavors.

  3. Pour in the rinsed lentils, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, bay leaves, and season with salt and pepper. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  4. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, set the valve to the sealing position, and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.

  5. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick release of any remaining pressure.

  6. Open the lid and give the soup a good stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. The lentils should be tender, and the flavors should be beautifully melded together.

  7. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot. Optionally, you can squeeze a bit of lemon juice over the soup for a bright, citrusy finish.

Benefits of Lentil Soup

Lentils are a nutritional powerhouse, and this soup is a fantastic way to incorporate them into your diet. They are rich in protein, fiber, and various essential nutrients such as folate, iron, and manganese. This makes lentils a great choice for anyone looking to boost their overall health and well-being.

The vegetables in this soup also contribute to its nutritional value. Carrots are packed with beta-carotene, while celery adds crunch and a refreshing flavor. The combination of these ingredients makes for a well-rounded and satisfying meal that will keep you full and energized.

Serving Suggestions

This Healthy Instant Pot Lentil Soup pairs beautifully with a slice of crusty whole-grain bread or a simple green salad. It’s also a great make-ahead meal that can be stored in the refrigerator for several days, making it perfect for meal prep or busy weeknights.

For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a dollop of Greek yogurt or a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top. You can also customize the soup by adding other vegetables such as spinach, kale, or even sweet potatoes for a unique twist.

Final Thoughts

There’s something undeniably comforting about a warm bowl of soup, and this Healthy Instant Pot Lentil Soup certainly delivers on comfort and flavor. Whether you’re in need of a quick and nourishing meal or simply want to add more plant-based goodness to your diet, this recipe is a winner.

So, dust off your Instant Pot and give this recipe a try. It’s a simple, satisfying, and healthy meal that is sure to become a staple in your cooking repertoire. Enjoy!
