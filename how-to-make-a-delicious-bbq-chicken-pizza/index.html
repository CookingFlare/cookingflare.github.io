How to Make a Delicious BBQ Chicken Pizza


Few dishes evoke the perfect combination of sweet, savory, and smoky flavors like a homemade BBQ chicken pizza. Whether you’re hosting a backyard dinner party, entertaining the family, or simply craving something indulgent and comforting, this recipe is guaranteed to bring a slice of happiness into your kitchen. Creating BBQ chicken pizza from scratch ensures every bite bursts with freshness. From the tender, marinated chicken to the crispy crust and gooey cheese, making it at home is more rewarding—and tastier—than ordering from your favorite pizza spot.

Let’s dive step-by-step into creating a BBQ chicken pizza so delicious, you’ll want to make it again and again.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Before getting started, gather all of your ingredients. Prepping everything beforehand makes the process more seamless. Here’s what you’ll need to whip up the ultimate BBQ chicken pizza:

For the Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour (plus extra for dusting)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 packet active dry yeast (7g)
  • ¾ cup (180ml) warm water (around 110°F)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

For the BBQ Sauce (if making from scratch):

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Toppings:

  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast (shredded or cubed)
  • ½ cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup shredded smoked gouda cheese
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Step 1: Preparing the Dough

A solid crust is the foundation of any great pizza. To make the perfect dough:

  1. Activate the yeast: In a small bowl, combine warm water and sugar. Stir in the yeast and let it sit for 5–10 minutes until frothy. This step is crucial for light and airy pizza dough.
  2. Combine dry ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and salt.
  3. Form the dough: Slowly pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until the dough begins to come together. Knead it on a floured surface for about 8–10 minutes, or until you achieve a smooth, elastic texture.
  4. Let it rise: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it with a damp kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1–2 hours or until doubled in size.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

For the star of this pizza—BBQ chicken—you’ll want it perfectly seasoned and tender:

  1. Season and grill: Rub the chicken breast with salt, pepper, and a touch of smoked paprika. Grill or pan-sear the chicken until cooked through (approximately 6–7 minutes per side depending on thickness).
  2. Shred or dice: Once the chicken has cooled slightly, shred or dice it into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Mix with BBQ sauce: In a bowl, combine the cooked chicken with half a cup of BBQ sauce. Coat evenly to infuse smoky flavors into the meat.

Step 3: Making Homemade BBQ Sauce (Optional)

Whether you’re a fan of tangy or sweet BBQ flavors, making your sauce ensures it’s tailored to your preference. To make it:

  1. Combine ingredients: In a saucepan over medium heat, whisk together ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, smoked paprika, and garlic powder.
  2. Simmer: Let the sauce cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring frequently, until thickened. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and adjust the sweetness or tanginess as desired.
  3. Cool: Remove from heat and let it cool slightly before using as one of your pizza toppings.

Step 4: Assemble the Pizza

With all the components ready, it’s time to bring them together into a masterpiece:

  1. Preheat your oven: Set your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat.
  2. Roll out the dough: On a lightly floured surface, roll out the risen dough into your desired thickness and shape—round is traditional, but rectangular pizzas work well, too.
  3. Transfer to parchment paper: Carefully place the rolled-out dough onto parchment paper to make handling and transferring easier.
  4. Add the sauce: Spread a generous layer of BBQ sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  5. Layer the toppings: Distribute the BBQ chicken evenly across the pizza, followed by a sprinkle of red onion for crunch and flavor. Add the mozzarella and smoked gouda cheeses, ensuring optimal coverage for gooey goodness.
  6. Optional spice: If you love some heat, sprinkle chili flakes over the toppings before baking.

Step 5: Bake to Perfection

Crust texture is critical when achieving that restaurant-worthy pizza at home. To bake:

  1. Transfer and bake: Carefully slide the parchment paper onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden brown.
  2. Check doneness: Peek under the crust—it should have a nice, crisp base without being overcooked.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Fresh garnishes can elevate your BBQ chicken pizza. Scatter freshly chopped cilantro across the pizza for a pop of color and herbal freshness. Slice it up into portions and serve hot. The marriage of smoky BBQ flavors, melted cheese, and tender chicken will make each bite unforgettable.

Tips for Success

  • Customize your cheese: Smoked gouda adds a unique depth to this pizza, but you can experiment with cheddar, Fontina, or even a sprinkle of Parmesan for extra layers of flavor.
  • Make it extra smoky: Consider adding a touch of liquid smoke to your BBQ sauce or topping your pizza with smoked meats like bacon.
  • Perfect the crust: Not a fan of thin crust? Roll the dough thicker or stuff it with cheese along the edges!
  • Get creative with toppings: Add bell peppers, jalapeños, or even pineapple chunks for a tropical twist.

Why Homemade BBQ Chicken Pizza Always Wins

There’s something special about crafting pizza in your own kitchen. You control the quality of ingredients, the richness of the sauce, and every element of the cooking process. Homemade BBQ chicken pizza isn’t just a recipe—it’s an experience. Whether you’re sharing slices with loved ones or indulging solo, the combination of crispy dough, smoky chicken, and melted cheese is unbeatable.

This easy-to-follow recipe is perfect for beginners and seasoned cooks alike. With practice, you’ll start finding your rhythm and tweaking ingredients based on your preferences. Soon, making this pizza will become a cherished tradition in your kitchen.

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to dig into a slice of barbecue bliss!
