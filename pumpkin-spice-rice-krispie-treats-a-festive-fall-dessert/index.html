Pumpkin Spice Rice Krispie Treats: A Festive Fall Dessert


As the leaves begin to change and the air turns crisp, it’s time to embrace all things cozy and comforting. What better way to celebrate the arrival of fall than with a delightful treat that captures the essence of the season? Pumpkin Spice Rice Krispie Treats are the perfect fusion of traditional Rice Krispie Treats with the warm, aromatic flavors of pumpkin spice. Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering, looking for a lunchbox surprise, or simply craving a cozy dessert, these treats are sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of crispy rice cereal
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 package (10 ounces) of marshmallows
  • 1/3 cup of pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon of pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pan: Begin by greasing a 9x13 inch baking pan with butter or non-stick cooking spray. This will prevent the treats from sticking to the pan and make for easy removal.

  2. Melt the Butter: In a large saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Once melted, add the marshmallows and stir continuously until they have completely melted and combined with the butter.

  3. Add the Flavor: Once the marshmallows have melted, stir in the pumpkin puree, pumpkin pie spice, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix until all the ingredients are well combined and the mixture takes on a beautiful, warm orange hue.

  4. Incorporate the Cereal: Remove the saucepan from the heat and gently fold in the crispy rice cereal. Stir until the cereal is evenly coated with the marshmallow and pumpkin mixture.

  5. Press into the Pan: Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking pan. Using a lightly greased spatula or wax paper, press the mixture evenly into the pan, ensuring a smooth and compact surface.

  6. Set and Serve: Allow the treats to cool at room temperature for about 30 minutes, or until they have set. Once set, cut into squares or fun shapes of your choice. Serve and enjoy the delightful blend of pumpkin spice and crispy sweetness.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra layer of indulgence, consider drizzling melted white chocolate over the cooled treats and sprinkle with a dash of cinnamon or nutmeg.
  • If you prefer a more pronounced pumpkin flavor, you can adjust the amount of pumpkin puree to suit your taste.
  • These treats can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, but we doubt they’ll last that long!

Embrace the Flavors of Fall

Pumpkin Spice Rice Krispie Treats are a delightful way to usher in the fall season. Whether you’re a pumpkin spice fanatic or simply looking to add a touch of warmth and flavor to your dessert repertoire, these treats are a must-try. With their irresistible blend of crispy, gooey, and spiced goodness, they’re sure to become a seasonal favorite. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the aromas of fall, and treat yourself to a taste of the season with these delightful Pumpkin Spice Rice Krispie Treats.
