Vegan Chilled Delights: 20 Refreshing Recipes to Beat the Heat in a Snap


Life at its hottest days can often leave you craving something cooling, quick, and entirely satisfying. Enter vegan chilled delights! These mouthwatering recipes not only cater to your taste buds but also to your wellness needs, being plant-based, refreshing, and easy to prepare. Let’s dive into this delightful world of chilled treats that beat the heat in a snap!

1. Minty Avocado Gazpacho

Silky avocados are made for cool dips, and here’s a refreshing twist on the classic. Puree avocado, cucumber, bell pepper, and garlic in a blender, then chill before stirring in vibrant fresh mint, lime, and a splash of olive oil. Garnish with a mint sprig and crunchy seeds.

2. Zucchini Noodles with Edamame Pesto

Whip up a cool vegan pesto with edamame, garlic, pine nuts, and a generous squeeze of lemon. Toss it with soft, chilled zucchini noodles for an enticing pasta salad that’s as light as it is satisfying.

3. Watermelon Strawberry Slush

For a naturally sweet summer cooler, blend chunks of ripe watermelon and strawberries in a blender. Sweeten to taste with a touch of agave nectar, and let the blender work its magic. Voila! A slush that will keep you refreshed.

4. Chickpea and Herb Salad Wraps

Make a Revitalising Chickpea and Herb Salad by combining cooked chickpeas with finely sliced red onions, chopped parsley, dill, and capers. Add a dash of lemon juice and olive oil, then fold everything into flatbread or lettuce leaves.

5. Lemon Meringue Bliss Bowls

For dessert, the option is endless – from ice cream bowls to smoothie bowls. Top chilled lemon sorbet or lemon ice with fluffy vegan meringue kisses and a sprinkle of fresh mint.

6. Coconut Lime Cooler

Blend ripe bananas, coconut milk, and a splash of lime juice until smooth. Sweeten with a touch of agave syrup and chill. This creamy, refreshing treat is an instant hit on warm days.

7. Cucumber & Avocado Gazpacho Cups

Choose the best quality ingredients for this stylish gazpacho. Scoop a refreshing gazpacho into chilled cucumber cups and garnish with thinly sliced avocado and a drizzle of olive oil.

8. Peach Rosemary Sorbet Floats

Bring a jar of peaches in rosemary-infused syrup to a simmer, then transfer to a blender and puree until smooth. Chilled and served over coconut ice cream – the simplest and yummiest dessert ever!

9. Creamy Mango Lassi

Blend fresh mangoes, a splash of almond milk, and a dollop of coconut yogurt until creamy. Sweeten to taste and serve chilled; this vegan delight is an unmatched summer clooer.

10. Chilled Cantaloupe Soup

Sauté shallots and garlic, blend with chunks of ripe cantaloupe, then strain to create a velvety soup. Serve chilled and enjoy this delicate, elegant summer favorite.

11. Avocado & Lime Chess Pie

Pre-bake (or buy from a local bakery) a vegan chess pie. Mix together ripe avocado with lime zest and juice, turmeric and a bit of mayo. Dollop the mixture onto the pie and swirl for an indulgent, zesty treat.

12. Cold Sesame Noodle Salad

Toss cooked and cooled noodles (soba, rice, or zucchini) with a sesame dressing made with tahini, freshly squeezed lime juice, maple syrup, and thin rice vinegar. Add finely sliced veggies for crunch, and you have a cool, fresh, and satisfying meal.

13. Mango & Chili Popsicles

Blend ripe mango chunks with a touch of honey or agave, and a pinch of red chili for a flavor twist. Pour into popsicle molds and freeze for a dragon-fruit-inspired, spicy-sweet delight.

14. Chickpea, Fennel & Orange Salad

Massage cooked chickpeas with orange juice, zest, fresh mint, slivered fennel, and a touch of maple syrup. Serve over our favorite bed of baby spinach leaves.

15. Chilled Avocado Banana Smoothie

For a heart healthy and satisfying chill-down, blend ripe avocados with bananas, almond milk, a touch of agave, and a scoop of vegan protein powder for a cool breakfast of champions.

16. Red Bean Quinoa Salad

Cook and chill quinoa while you prep a can of rinsed red beans and your favorite assortment of finely chopped veggies. Toss with a tangy dressing made with olive oil, fresh lime juice, and a dash of agave.

17. Watermelon & Feta Salad Skewers

Thread cubes of cool, juicy watermelon onto skewers and top with crumbled feta and fresh mint. For a spicy twist, add a drizzle of balsamic reduction and sesame seeds.

18. Veggie Patch Gazpacho

Add variety to your gazpacho by incorporating a variety of veggies like tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, and radish. Blend until smooth and let chill in the fridge for a few hours before serving.

19. Mango Sago Pudding

Soak sago pearls in water and simmer until clear and transparent. Blend ripe mangoes with agave syrup, lime juice, and coconut cream, then fold it into the cooked sago to create a cooling dessert that’s popular in Southeast Asia.

20. Coconut Chia Pudding

In a jar, combine chia seeds, coconut milk, maple syrup, and a dash of vanilla extract. Seal and refrigerate overnight, resulting in a magical, gelatinous concoction that’s as delightful as it is nutritious. Serve with fruit, nuts, and a drizzle of honey.

Nothing like a range of refreshingly cool and delightful dishes to pull you out of sticky heat. Keep them chilled and close at hand if the mercury soars. Try these vegan chilled delights and discover new favorites that not only meet your needs for a plant-based diet but also sate the most ardent cravings for all things sweet, tangy and oh-so-refreshing. Happy cooking!
