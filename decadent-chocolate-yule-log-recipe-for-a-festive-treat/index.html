Decadent Chocolate Yule Log Recipe for a Festive Treat


The holiday season is upon us, and what better way to spread joy and indulge in some festive cheer than with a decadent chocolate yule log? This classic dessert is not only a feast for the eyes but also a delight for the taste buds. In this blog post, we will take you through a step-by-step guide on how to create your very own chocolate yule log, guaranteed to impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 65g plain flour
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

For the filling:

  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 300ml double cream
  • 2 tablespoons icing sugar

For the ganache:

  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 200ml double cream

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a baking tray and line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk the eggs and caster sugar together until pale and fluffy.
  3. Sift in the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the egg mixture until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter onto the prepared baking tray, spreading it evenly with a spatula.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the cake springs back when touched lightly. Remove from the oven and allow it to cool completely.

Step 2: Preparing the Filling

  1. In a heatproof bowl, melt the dark chocolate over a pan of simmering water. Stir until smooth and set aside to cool slightly.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip the double cream and icing sugar until soft peaks form.
  3. Gradually fold the whipped cream into the melted chocolate until well combined.

Step 3: Assembling the Yule Log

  1. Carefully unroll the cooled cake and spread the chocolate filling evenly over the surface.
  2. Gently roll the cake back up, starting from one of the long sides. Place the log seam-side down on a serving platter.

Step 4: Preparing the Ganache

  1. In a heatproof bowl, melt the dark chocolate for the ganache over a pan of simmering water. Stir until smooth.
  2. Remove from heat and gradually whisk in the double cream until well combined and glossy.

Step 5: Decorating the Yule Log

  1. Pour the ganache over the rolled cake, spreading it evenly with a spatula to cover the entire surface.
  2. Use a fork or a decorating comb to create the bark-like texture on the ganache.
  3. Optional: Decorate the yule log with icing sugar, fresh berries, or edible holly leaves to give it a festive touch.

Step 6: Serving and Enjoying

  1. Refrigerate the assembled yule log for at least 2 hours, or overnight, to allow the flavors to meld together and the ganache to set.
  2. Slice the log into servings and serve chilled. This delightful treat is perfect for sharing with loved ones during the holiday season.

Conclusion

Creating a decadent chocolate yule log is a labor of love that will bring joy and delight to your festive celebrations. With its rich chocolate flavor and festive appearance, this dessert is sure to become a holiday favorite. So gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and let the magic of the season unfold in every bite. Happy baking and happy holidays!
