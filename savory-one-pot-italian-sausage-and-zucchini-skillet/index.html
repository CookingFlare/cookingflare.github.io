Savory One Pot Italian Sausage and Zucchini Skillet


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy one-pot meal that’s bursting with Italian flavors, then this Savory One Pot Italian Sausage and Zucchini Skillet is the perfect recipe for you. This dish is not only flavorful and satisfying but also incredibly simple to prepare, making it an ideal option for busy weeknights when you want a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 2 medium zucchinis, sliced into half moons
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or pan, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks, until it’s browned and no longer pink. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  2. Once the sausage is cooked, add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the onion is soft and translucent.

  3. Add the sliced zucchinis to the skillet and sauté for 3-4 minutes, allowing them to soften slightly.

  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes and chicken broth, then sprinkle in the dried oregano and basil. Stir everything together and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  5. Let the skillet simmer for 10-12 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the zucchini to fully cook. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

  6. Once the zucchini is tender and the flavors have combined, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  7. Serve the savory Italian sausage and zucchini skillet directly from the pan, or transfer it to a serving dish. This dish pairs wonderfully with a side of crusty bread or a simple green salad.

Why You’ll Love It

This one-pot Italian sausage and zucchini skillet is a fantastic option for anyone looking to enjoy a hearty and flavorful meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of savory Italian sausage, tender zucchini, and aromatic herbs creates a dish that’s both comforting and satisfying. Plus, the fact that it all comes together in just one skillet means minimal cleanup, making it a win-win for busy home cooks.

The Italian sausage infuses the entire dish with its rich and robust flavor, while the zucchini adds a fresh and slightly crisp texture that perfectly complements the sausage. The addition of diced tomatoes and aromatic herbs elevates the overall taste, giving it that unmistakable Italian flair.

Not only is this meal delicious, but it’s also quite versatile. You can easily customize it by adding other vegetables such as bell peppers, mushrooms, or spinach, depending on your preferences and what’s in season. It’s a great way to use up any surplus vegetables you may have on hand.

In addition to being incredibly tasty, this dish is also a great source of protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, making it a wholesome option for the whole family. It’s a fantastic way to sneak in some extra veggies, especially for those who may not be the biggest fans of zucchini.

Another wonderful aspect of this recipe is its simplicity. With just a handful of ingredients and minimal prep work, you can have a wholesome and satisfying meal on the table in no time. It’s perfect for those days when you want something homemade but don’t have the energy for an elaborate cooking session.

Whether you’re a fan of Italian cuisine or simply enjoy a good, hearty meal, this savory one-pot Italian sausage and zucchini skillet is sure to become a favorite in your recipe rotation. So, grab your skillet and get ready to whip up a meal that’s as easy to make as it is delicious to eat!
