Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe: A Soft and Chewy Treat


Who doesn’t love a warm, freshly baked chocolate chip cookie? The aroma that fills the kitchen, the soft and chewy texture, and the burst of chocolatey goodness with every bite—it’s a treat that brings joy to both kids and adults. And guess what? You can enjoy this classic dessert even if you follow a vegan lifestyle. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make delicious vegan chocolate chip cookies that are soft, chewy, and completely plant-based.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup refined coconut oil, melted
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, whisk together the melted coconut oil, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until well combined. The mixture should be smooth and slightly thick.

  4. Add the almond milk and vanilla extract to the sugar mixture and whisk until fully incorporated.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring with a wooden spoon or spatula until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. You can flatten the dough slightly with the back of a spoon if desired.

  8. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until they are golden brown around the edges. The centers will still be slightly soft.

  9. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cookies cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once cooled, store the vegan chocolate chip cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. Enjoy them with a glass of almond milk or your favorite dairy-free beverage.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add ½ cup of chopped nuts (such as walnuts or pecans) to the dough along with the chocolate chips.
  • If you prefer a thinner and crispier cookie, you can flatten the dough more before baking. Just keep an eye on the baking time, as thinner cookies may cook faster.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of vegan chocolate chips, such as dark chocolate, semi-sweet chocolate, or even white chocolate.

Now you can satisfy your sweet tooth with these delightful vegan chocolate chip cookies. Whether you follow a vegan lifestyle or simply want to try a plant-based dessert, these cookies are sure to impress. They are soft, chewy, and packed with chocolatey goodness. So grab your apron, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a delicious treat that everyone will love. Happy baking!

Note: This recipe makes approximately 24 cookies, depending on the size of your cookie scoop or tablespoon.
