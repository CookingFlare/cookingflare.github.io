1. Enchilada Quinoa Salad

First, let’s transport ourselves straight to the heart of Mexico, by blending two absolute legends of the food world—the classic enchilada and the superfood, quinoa. This dish might just be your ultimate heartwarming comfort food that does wonders for your waistline!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa (uncooked)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup black beans (canned, rinsed, and drained)
  • 1 small red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small avocado, diced
  • ½ cup corn (canned, drained, and rinsed)
  • Juice of ½ lime
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles, drained
  • 1/3 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Optional toppings include feta cheese and fresh jalapeño for a kick!

Directions:

  1. Bring quinoa, vegetable broth, and water to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to maintain a simmer, cover, and cook until quinoa is tender and water has been absorbed, 10 to 15 minutes. Set aside to let it cool.

  2. Once the quinoa has cooled, transfer it to a large bowl. Drizzle olive oil and mix until well incorporated.

  3. Add cherry tomatoes, red onion, black beans, red bell pepper, avocado, and corn to the bowl. Toss gently to combine.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, cumin, garlic powder, paprika, and diced tomatoes and green chiles.

  5. Pour the lime dressing over the quinoa salad, mix well, then gently fold in fresh cilantro. Top with feta cheese and fresh jalapeño, if desired.

Voilà! Your Enchilada Quinoa Salad is ready to steal the show! Your friends and family won’t be able to resist this amazing twist on a classic.

2. Super Kale Salad Bowl

Next up! If you’re someone who’s always looking for ways to get more greens in your diet, the Super Kale Salad Bowl is about to become your new, absolute favorite go-to. Combining the power of kale with its perfect counterparts, this dish will not only make you feel like a superstar but will also keep you glowing from within!

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of kale (tough stems removed and torn into bite-sized pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (rinsed and drained)
  • 1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges (drained)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/3 cup pumpkin seeds
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ cup chopped dried cranberries

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix kale, olive oil, lemon juice, and salt. Ensuring each leaf is well coated, massage the kale for two to three minutes to tenderize it.

  2. Add chickpeas, mandarin oranges, cherry tomatoes, red onion, pumpkin seeds, parsley, and cranberries to the kale. Mix well.

  3. Devour this refreshing and healthy kale salad bowl to fuel your body with nutritious нnergy!

3. Detoxifying Quinoa and Avocado Salad

When you’ve had a slightly taxing week and are craving a refreshing, palate-cleansing salad, look no further! With ingredients packed with essential nutrients to detoxify and revitalize, this Quinoa and Avocado Salad is perfect for washing away the chaos of everyday life.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa (uncooked)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 avocado, chopped
  • ½ English cucumber, chopped
  • ½ red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small zucchini, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • ¼ cup ripped fresh mint leaves
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Directions:

  1. Cook quinoa as per the previous recipe’s instructions, cooled, and set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, avocado, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, zucchini, parsley, and mint.

  3. Drizzle lemon juice and olive oil over the salad, and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Gently mix all the ingredients until well-combined. Let the loyalty of fiber, antioxidants, and healthy fats from this salad nourish your mind and body.
