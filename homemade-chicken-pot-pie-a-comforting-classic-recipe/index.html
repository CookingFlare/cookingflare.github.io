Homemade Chicken Pot Pie: A Comforting Classic Recipe


There’s something undeniably comforting about a homemade chicken pot pie. The flaky crust, the creamy filling, and the savory aroma that fills the kitchen as it bakes – it’s a classic dish that never fails to warm the soul. Whether you’re looking for a hearty family meal or a comforting dish to share with friends, this homemade chicken pot pie recipe is sure to become a favorite.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 3 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup celery, diced
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or two forks until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix gently until the dough holds together. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the carrots, celery, and peas, and cook until the vegetables are slightly softened.
    • Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and thyme until well combined. Gradually stir in the chicken broth and milk, cooking and stirring until the mixture comes to a boil and thickens.
    • Add the diced chicken to the saucepan, stirring to combine. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. Assemble the Pot Pie:

    • Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
    • Roll out one of the chilled dough disks on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Place the rolled-out dough into the pie dish.
    • Pour the chicken filling into the pie crust.
    • Roll out the second dough disk and place it over the filling. Trim and crimp the edges, then cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.

  4. Bake the Pot Pie:

    • Place the pot pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips, and bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Allow the chicken pot pie to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy the comforting flavors and the heartwarming aroma of this classic homemade dish.

Final Thoughts

Homemade chicken pot pie is a timeless classic that brings warmth and comfort to any table. Whether it’s a cozy family dinner or a gathering with loved ones, this comforting dish is sure to be a hit. The flaky crust, the creamy filling, and the delicious blend of savory flavors make it a true crowd-pleaser. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and prepare to delight your senses with the heartwarming goodness of homemade chicken pot pie.
