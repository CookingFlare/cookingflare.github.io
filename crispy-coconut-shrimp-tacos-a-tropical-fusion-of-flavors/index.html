Crispy Coconut Shrimp Tacos: A Tropical Fusion of Flavors


Tired of the same old taco night? Say aloha to a tropical twist on a classic favorite with these Crispy Coconut Shrimp Tacos! This recipe takes the traditional shrimp taco to a whole new level with the delightful crunch of coconut and a burst of exotic flavors. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving a taste of the tropics, these tacos are sure to transport your taste buds to a sun-kissed paradise.

Ingredients

For the Crispy Coconut Shrimp:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil for frying

For the Pineapple Salsa:

  • 1 cup diced pineapple
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt to taste

For the Lime Crema:

  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest
  • Salt to taste

Additional:

  • 8 small flour or corn tortillas
  • Shredded cabbage or lettuce for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crispy Coconut Shrimp

  1. In three separate shallow dishes, place the flour in one, beaten eggs in another, and a mixture of shredded coconut, Panko breadcrumbs, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in the third.
  2. Dredge each shrimp in the flour, then dip into the beaten eggs, and finally coat in the coconut breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the cooking oil over medium-high heat. Fry the shrimp in batches for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

Step 2: Prepare the Pineapple Salsa

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the diced pineapple, red bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, and salt. Toss gently to mix, then refrigerate until ready to use.

Step 3: Prepare the Lime Crema

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, lime zest, and salt until smooth and creamy.

Step 4: Assemble the Tacos

  1. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until soft and pliable.
  2. To assemble each taco, place a layer of shredded cabbage or lettuce on a tortilla, followed by a few crispy coconut shrimp. Top with a generous spoonful of pineapple salsa and a drizzle of lime crema.
  3. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side.

Serving Suggestions

These Crispy Coconut Shrimp Tacos are best enjoyed fresh, with a side of rice and beans or a refreshing tropical salad. Pair them with a cold glass of pineapple-infused iced tea or a fruity margarita for the ultimate island-inspired feast.

So, whether you’re dreaming of a beach getaway or simply looking to spice up your dinner routine, these Crispy Coconut Shrimp Tacos are a surefire way to bring a taste of the tropics to your table. Embrace the fusion of crispy, coconutty goodness and zesty, tangy flavors with every bite. Get ready to embark on a culinary adventure and savor the irresistible charm of these tropical-inspired tacos!
