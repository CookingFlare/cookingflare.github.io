Healthy Veggie Breakfast Burrito Recipes for a Nutrient-Packed Start

Breakfast is often hailed as the most important meal of the day, and for good reason. It provides us with the energy and nutrients we need to kickstart our day. If you’re looking for a healthy and delicious way to power up your mornings, then look no further than these nutrient-packed veggie breakfast burrito recipes. Spinach and Mushroom Breakfast Burrito Ingredients: 1 whole wheat tortilla 2 eggs, beaten 1 cup spinach, chopped 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced 1/4 cup red bell pepper, diced 2 tablespoons feta cheese, crumbled Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with cooking spray. Add the mushrooms and red bell pepper to the skillet and sauté for 3-4 minutes until softened. Add the spinach to the skillet and cook for an additional 2 minutes until wilted. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and scramble until fully cooked. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Warm the tortilla in a separate skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make it pliable. Place the scrambled eggs and vegetable mixture onto the tortilla. Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Slice in half diagonally and serve warm. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Breakfast Burrito Ingredients: 1 whole wheat tortilla 1 small sweet potato, diced 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained 1/4 cup red onion, diced 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped 1/2 teaspoon cumin 1/4 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the diced sweet potato with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Spread the sweet potato evenly on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender. In a separate skillet, sauté the black beans and red onion until heated through. Warm the tortilla in a separate skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make it pliable. Place the roasted sweet potato, black bean mixture, and fresh cilantro onto the tortilla. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go. Slice in half diagonally and serve warm. Tofu and Veggie Breakfast Burrito Ingredients: 1 whole wheat tortilla 1/2 cup firm tofu, crumbled 1/4 cup red bell pepper, diced 1/4 cup zucchini, diced 1/4 cup yellow squash, diced 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast 1/2 teaspoon turmeric 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with cooking spray. Add the crumbled tofu, red bell pepper, zucchini, and yellow squash to the skillet and sauté for 5-6 minutes until vegetables are tender. Sprinkle turmeric, garlic powder, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper over the mixture and stir well to combine. Warm the tortilla in a separate skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make it pliable. Place the tofu and veggie mixture onto the tortilla. Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the sides as you go.
