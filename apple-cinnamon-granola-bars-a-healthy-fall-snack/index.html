Apple Cinnamon Granola Bars: A Healthy Fall Snack


Fall is here, and what better way to embrace the flavors of the season than with a batch of delicious and nutritious Apple Cinnamon Granola Bars? Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with the warm, comforting flavors of apples and cinnamon, these homemade granola bars are the perfect on-the-go snack for those crisp autumn days.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 cup dried apples, chopped
  • 1/2 cup almonds, chopped
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, dried apples, and chopped almonds. Mix well to distribute the ingredients evenly.

  3. In a separate microwave-safe bowl, combine the honey, almond butter, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and salt. Microwave the mixture for about 30 seconds, or until the ingredients are melted and well combined. Stir to ensure everything is blended together.

  4. Pour the wet mixture over the dry ingredients in the mixing bowl. Use a spatula or your hands to thoroughly mix everything together, making sure all the oats, apples, and almonds are coated with the sweet and sticky mixture.

  5. Transfer the mixture into the prepared baking dish and press it down firmly using the back of a spoon or your hands. Make sure to create an even layer throughout the dish.

  6. Bake the granola bars in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they turn golden brown around the edges. Keep an eye on them to prevent burning.

  7. Once the granola bars are done baking, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool completely in the baking dish. This will help them set and hold their shape.

  8. Once cooled, use a sharp knife to cut the granola mixture into bars of your desired size. Whether you prefer bite-sized snacks or larger bars, it’s up to you.

  9. Store the Apple Cinnamon Granola Bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week. If you want to keep them fresh for longer, you can refrigerate them for up to two weeks.

Enjoy the Flavors of Fall!

These Apple Cinnamon Granola Bars are a delightful treat that captures the essence of the autumn season. Whether you enjoy them as a quick breakfast on the go, a midday pick-me-up, or a post-workout snack, these bars are sure to satisfy your cravings for something sweet and wholesome.

The combination of chewy dried apples, crunchy almonds, and the warmth of cinnamon will transport your taste buds to a cozy apple orchard on a cool fall day. Plus, the natural sweetness from honey and dried fruit means you can indulge without any guilt.

So, whip up a batch of these Apple Cinnamon Granola Bars and embrace the flavors of fall. They are not only a tasty and convenient snack but also a healthier alternative to store-bought granola bars. Enjoy the season and savor every bite!
