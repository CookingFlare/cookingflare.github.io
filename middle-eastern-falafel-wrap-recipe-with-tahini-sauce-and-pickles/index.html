Middle Eastern Falafel Wrap Recipe with Tahini Sauce and Pickles


If you’re craving a taste of the Middle East, look no further than this delicious falafel wrap recipe with creamy tahini sauce and zesty pickles. Falafel, made from chickpeas or fava beans, is a popular Middle Eastern street food known for its crispy exterior and moist, flavorful interior. When combined with the tangy tahini sauce and crunchy pickles, it creates a symphony of flavors that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of the Middle East.

Ingredients

For the Falafel:

  • 2 cups dried chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 1 small onion, roughly chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Tahini Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste

For the Wraps:

  • 4 large pita bread or flatbreads
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced red onions
  • Pickles

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Falafel

  1. Drain the soaked chickpeas and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  2. In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, onion, garlic, parsley, cilantro, cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper, baking powder, and salt. Pulse the mixture until it forms a coarse paste.
  3. Transfer the falafel mixture to a bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  4. Shape the falafel mixture into small patties, about 1 inch in diameter.
  5. In a deep skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Fry the falafel in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

Step 2: Make the Tahini Sauce

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, water, lemon juice, minced garlic, and salt until smooth and creamy. Adjust the consistency with more water if needed.

Step 3: Assemble the Wraps

  1. Warm the pita bread or flatbreads in a dry skillet or microwave.
  2. Spread a generous amount of tahini sauce on each pita bread.
  3. Place 3-4 falafel patties on top of the tahini sauce.
  4. Add sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and pickles.
  5. Fold the sides of the pita bread over the filling and roll it up tightly.

Serving Suggestions

Serve the falafel wraps with extra tahini sauce on the side for dipping and a side of crispy fries or a fresh salad for a complete Middle Eastern meal experience.

Final Thoughts

This Middle Eastern falafel wrap recipe is a celebration of vibrant flavors and textures. The crispy falafel, creamy tahini sauce, and tangy pickles come together to create a meal that’s both satisfying and deeply satisfying. Whether you’re a fan of Middle Eastern cuisine or looking to expand your culinary horizons, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Enjoy the experience of crafting these wraps and savoring the delicious flavors of the Middle East in every bite.
