Let’s dive into the recipe and explore the wonderful combination of ingredients that make this salad a standout dish. Ingredients For the Chicken: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon paprika 1 teaspoon garlic powder Salt and pepper to taste For the Salad: 6 cups fresh spinach leaves 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cucumber, sliced 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced 1/4 cup sliced almonds For the Dressing: 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard 1 teaspoon honey Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. In a skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil and place the seasoned chicken breasts. Cook for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice the chicken into thin strips. Step 2: Prepare the Salad In a large bowl, combine the fresh spinach, cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumber, red onion, and sliced almonds. Toss the salad gently to mix the ingredients evenly. Step 3: Prepare the Dressing In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper until well combined. Step 4: Assemble the Salad Place the sliced chicken on top of the prepared salad. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and chicken. Serving Suggestion Once the salad is assembled, it’s ready to be served. This Flavorful Paleo Chicken and Spinach Salad makes for a satisfying and wholesome meal on its own.
