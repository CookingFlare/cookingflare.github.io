Vegan Orange Chocolate Chip Cookies: A Citrusy and Chocolatey Treat


If you’re in the mood for a sweet and zesty treat, look no further than these Vegan Orange Chocolate Chip Cookies. Combining the bright, citrusy flavor of oranges with the rich indulgence of chocolate chips, these cookies are a delightful fusion of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Best of all, they are completely vegan, making them a guilt-free indulgence for everyone to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Cream the Wet Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the melted coconut oil, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth and creamy.

  4. Add Orange Flavor: Add the freshly squeezed orange juice, orange zest, and vanilla extract to the wet ingredients. Mix until well combined.

  5. Incorporate Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a dough forms.

  6. Fold in Chocolate Chips: Gently fold in the dairy-free chocolate chips, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Form Cookie Dough: Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, scoop out portions of the dough and place them on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.

  8. Bake to Perfection: Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.

  9. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

These Vegan Orange Chocolate Chip Cookies are best enjoyed with a glass of almond milk or a cup of hot tea. The combination of citrus and chocolate flavors makes them a delightful addition to any afternoon snack or dessert spread. They also make a lovely homemade gift for friends and family who appreciate the goodness of vegan baking.

The Perfect Blend of Flavors

The bright, tangy notes of fresh orange zest and juice add a refreshing twist to the classic chocolate chip cookie, creating a unique flavor profile that’s both comforting and invigorating. The use of coconut oil keeps these cookies vegan-friendly while providing a subtle hint of tropical sweetness to complement the citrus and chocolate. The dairy-free chocolate chips add a luscious richness to every bite, making each cookie a decadent treat.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Orange Chocolate Chip Cookies are a delightful combination of citrusy and chocolatey flavors, making them a standout treat for vegans and non-vegans alike. Whether you’re looking to satisfy a sweet craving or impress guests with a unique dessert, these cookies are sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible charm of these delightful cookies.
