Zesty Gluten-Free Lemon Bars: A Tangy Sweet Treat


Are you a fan of tangy and sweet desserts? Look no further than these Zesty Gluten-Free Lemon Bars. Packed with a burst of citrus flavor, these delightful treats are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer picnic or simply craving a refreshing dessert, these lemon bars will surely satisfy your taste buds. And the best part? They are completely gluten-free, making them a great option for those with dietary restrictions. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups gluten-free flour
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ¼ cup powdered sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

For the filling:

  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup gluten-free flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the gluten-free flour, melted butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix until a crumbly dough forms.

  3. Press the dough into the prepared baking dish, ensuring an even layer. Bake the crust for 15 minutes or until lightly golden.

  4. While the crust is baking, prepare the filling. In a separate bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar and gluten-free flour until well combined.

  5. Add the eggs, lemon juice, and lemon zest to the sugar mixture. Whisk until smooth and creamy.

  6. Once the crust is done baking, pour the lemon filling over the hot crust. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set.

  7. Remove the lemon bars from the oven and let them cool completely in the baking dish.

  8. Once cooled, refrigerate the bars for at least 2 hours to allow them to firm up.

  9. Once chilled, remove the bars from the baking dish using the parchment paper and transfer them to a cutting board.

  10. Using a sharp knife, cut the bars into squares or rectangles of your desired size.

  11. Dust the bars with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness, if desired.

  12. Serve and enjoy these zesty gluten-free lemon bars!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a teaspoon of lemon extract to the crust mixture.

  • If you prefer a less tangy taste, reduce the amount of lemon juice used in the filling by half.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of gluten-free flour blends to find the one that works best for you.

  • These lemon bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Conclusion

There you have it — a delicious recipe for zesty gluten-free lemon bars. With their tangy and sweet flavors, these bars are sure to become a new favorite in your dessert repertoire. Whether you’re gluten-free or simply looking for a refreshing treat, these lemon bars are a must-try. So, don’t wait any longer! Gather your ingredients and start baking these delightful zesty gluten-free lemon bars today. Enjoy!
