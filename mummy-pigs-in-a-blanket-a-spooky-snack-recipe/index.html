Mummy Pigs in a Blanket: A Spooky Snack Recipe


If you’re looking for a fun and spooky snack to serve at your next Halloween party or movie night, look no further than these adorable and delicious Mummy Pigs in a Blanket. This creative twist on a classic appetizer will delight both kids and adults with its cute and slightly eerie presentation. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make, so you can spend more time enjoying the festivities and less time in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Mini cocktail sausages or hot dogs
  • Puff pastry
  • Mustard or ketchup (optional, for decoration)
  • Egg wash (1 egg beaten with a splash of water)

Instructions

  1. Preparation: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Cutting the Puff Pastry: Roll out the puff pastry on a lightly floured surface. Using a sharp knife or pizza cutter, cut the puff pastry into thin strips, about 1/4 inch wide.

  3. Wrapping the Sausages: Take one strip of puff pastry and wrap it around a mini sausage, leaving a small space at one end to create the look of mummy bandages. Repeat this process for all the sausages, laying them on the prepared baking sheet as you go.

  4. Creating the Mummy Look: Once all the sausages are wrapped, brush the puff pastry with the egg wash. This will give them a golden color as they bake. You can also dot two small eyes on each mummy using mustard or ketchup for a fun and spooky effect.

  5. Baking: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the puff pastry is golden brown and the sausages are cooked through.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the mummy pigs in a blanket from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. Arrange them on a platter with a small bowl of ketchup or mustard for dipping, and watch as your guests marvel at these adorable and delicious Halloween treats.

Tips and Variations

  • Cheesy Mummies: For an extra gooey and delicious twist, you can add a thin strip of cheese along with the sausage before wrapping it in the puff pastry. The melted cheese will ooze out of the mummy bandages, adding a delightful surprise to every bite.

  • Spooky Sauce: Serve these mummy pigs in a blanket with a side of “bloody” ketchup or mustard for a truly spooky presentation. Simply mix a few drops of red food coloring into the condiment of your choice for a fun and eerie effect.

  • Make-Ahead: You can prepare the mummy pigs in a blanket ahead of time and store them in the refrigerator until you’re ready to bake. This makes them a convenient option for entertaining, allowing you to focus on other party preparations.

Conclusion

Mummy Pigs in a Blanket are a playful and easy-to-make snack that’s perfect for Halloween or any spooky-themed event. With just a few simple ingredients and a dash of creativity, you can transform a classic appetizer into a festive and charming treat that will delight guests of all ages. Whether you’re hosting a costume party or a family movie night, these adorable mummies are sure to steal the show and add a touch of whimsy to your celebration. So, gather your ingredients, get creative, and enjoy making these delightful Mummy Pigs in a Blanket for your next gathering. Happy snacking!
