On a Budget? A Knack for Creating Irresistible Pizzas Using Basic Ingredients


Who says you need a hefty budget to enjoy delicious, homemade pizzas? Embrace your inner pizza artisan and strive for creativity and resourcefulness, while still delivering pizza perfection to tickle your taste buds. Here, we’ve compiled a range of scrumptious pizza recipes using basic ingredients that will enable you to satisfy those pizza cravings without breaking the bank. Give these mouthwatering recipes a go and see how far your culinary skills can take you!

1. Classic Margherita Pizza

Ah, the classic Margherita. It’s loved for its simplicity and zest. This pizza takes you back to the origins of pizza-making and showcases how fabulous simple ingredients can taste when combined masterfully.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the flour, instant yeast, salt, and sugar.
  2. Gradually add the warm water and olive oil, stirring until a soft dough forms. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to your desired thickness.
  6. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  7. Scatter shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add fresh basil leaves.
  8. Slide your masterpiece onto a preheated pizza stone or an inverted baking tray, and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly golden.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it sit for a few minutes before slicing. Bon appétit!

2. Spinach and Feta Pizza

If you want something a bit different but still delectable, this spinach and feta pizza recipe is a must-try. Create a new favorite that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 cup cooked and drained spinach
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Prepare your dough as shown in the previous recipe.
  2. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  3. Roll out the dough to your desired thickness.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine cooked spinach, crumbled feta cheese, and shredded mozzarella. Mix well.
  5. Spread the spinach mixture evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  6. Slide your creation into the oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly golden.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Enjoy this fusion of Mediterranean flavors!

3. BBQ Chicken and Pineapple Pizza

This iconic combination will bring a delightful twist to your usual pizza night. A flare of sweetness from the pineapple against smoky barbecue sauce and succulent chicken is a perfect balance of flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup canned pineapple chunks, drained

Instructions:

  1. Prepare your dough as shown in the previous recipes.
  2. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  3. Roll out the dough to your desired thickness.
  4. Spread barbecue sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Add shredded mozzarella, cooked chicken, and drained pineapple chunks over the sauce.
  6. Slide your scrumptious pizza into the oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly golden.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Experience the harmonizing flavors that make this a crowd-pleaser!

There you have it - three incredibly delicious pizza recipes that don’t require breaking the bank. Pizza nights can now be a regular occasion without guilt or regrets. Keep experimenting with different combinations of ingredients, and don’t forget to have fun while creating your personal pizza masterpieces!
