Hearty Paleo Baked Chicken Thighs with Herbs


When it comes to delicious and healthy meals, Hearty Paleo Baked Chicken Thighs with Herbs is a recipe that ticks all the boxes. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is perfect for those following a paleo lifestyle or simply looking to enjoy a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

To make Hearty Paleo Baked Chicken Thighs with Herbs, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Place the chicken thighs in a baking dish, skin side up. Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring each piece is coated evenly.

  4. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.

  5. Once cooked, remove the baking dish from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute and ensures moist and tender chicken.

  6. Serve the Hearty Paleo Baked Chicken Thighs with Herbs with your favorite side dishes, such as roasted vegetables or a fresh salad. The herby flavors of the chicken pair perfectly with a variety of sides, making it a versatile and satisfying meal.

Why Choose Hearty Paleo Baked Chicken Thighs with Herbs?

There are several reasons why this recipe should be on your regular meal rotation:

  • Paleo-friendly: This recipe is fully compliant with the paleo diet, which emphasizes whole, unprocessed foods. It excludes grains, dairy, and refined sugars, making it a healthy choice for those following this dietary approach.

  • Flavorful: The combination of herbs, garlic, and lemon juice creates a burst of flavors that complements the natural taste of the chicken. Each bite is a delightful blend of savory and tangy notes.

  • Easy to make: With just a few simple steps, you can have a delicious meal on the table in no time. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want something tasty without spending hours in the kitchen.

  • Nutrient-rich: Chicken thighs are packed with protein and essential vitamins and minerals. By opting for bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs, you also add extra flavor and healthy fats to your meal.

  • Versatile: Hearty Paleo Baked Chicken Thighs with Herbs can be paired with various side dishes to create a complete and satisfying meal. Whether you prefer roasted vegetables, cauliflower rice, or a fresh salad, this dish can be customized to suit your preferences.

Final Thoughts

Hearty Paleo Baked Chicken Thighs with Herbs is a recipe that combines simplicity, flavor, and nutrition. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking for a wholesome meal, this dish is sure to please your taste buds. Give it a try and discover the joy of a hearty and healthy chicken dinner.
