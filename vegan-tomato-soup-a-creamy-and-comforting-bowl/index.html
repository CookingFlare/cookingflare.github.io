Vegan Tomato Soup: A Creamy and Comforting Bowl


There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of tomato soup to soothe the soul and satisfy the taste buds. And when it’s vegan, it becomes a guilt-free indulgence that everyone can enjoy. This recipe for Vegan Tomato Soup is not only delicious, but it’s also incredibly easy to make. So, grab your apron and let’s dive into this creamy and comforting bowl of goodness!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk (or any other non-dairy milk)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, diced carrot, and celery stalk. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, dried basil, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully blend the soup until smooth and creamy. Be cautious when blending hot liquids to avoid any accidents.

  5. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the unsweetened coconut milk. This will add a creamy texture and a hint of sweetness to the soup.

  6. Taste the soup and adjust the seasonings according to your preference. You can add more salt, pepper, or herbs to enhance the flavor.

  7. Once the soup is heated through, it’s ready to be served. Ladle it into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a pop of freshness and added aroma.

  8. Serve the Vegan Tomato Soup with a side of crusty bread or a simple green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the soup.
  • To make the soup even heartier, you can add cooked pasta or rice before serving.
  • If you prefer a chunkier texture, you can leave some of the vegetables unblended or add diced tomatoes for added texture.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile of the soup.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat on the stovetop or in the microwave before serving.

Final Thoughts

This Vegan Tomato Soup is the epitome of comfort food. Its velvety texture, rich tomato flavor, and aromatic herbs make it the perfect dish for a cozy night in or a quick lunch. Plus, it’s packed with nutrients from the fresh vegetables and is completely free of animal products. So, whether you’re a vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe will surely become a staple in your kitchen. Give it a try and let the flavors of this creamy and comforting bowl of soup warm your heart and nourish your soul.
