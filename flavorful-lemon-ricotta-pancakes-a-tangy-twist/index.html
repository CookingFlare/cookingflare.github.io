Flavorful Lemon Ricotta Pancakes: A Tangy Twist


If you’re looking to start your day with a burst of tangy flavor, look no further than these delicious Lemon Ricotta Pancakes. With their fluffy texture and zesty taste, these pancakes are sure to become a new favorite in your breakfast rotation. Whether you’re hosting a brunch or simply treating yourself to a special morning treat, these pancakes are a delightful choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  2. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, milk, eggs, melted butter, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Whisk until well combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently fold together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense pancakes.

  4. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with cooking spray or a small amount of butter.

  5. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, pour the pancake batter onto the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake.

  6. Flip the pancake and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through. Transfer the cooked pancake to a plate and repeat with the remaining batter.

  7. Serve the Lemon Ricotta Pancakes warm, with your favorite toppings such as fresh berries, powdered sugar, or a drizzle of maple syrup.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of lemon flavor, you can add a few drops of lemon extract to the batter.
  • If you prefer a lighter texture, you can separate the egg whites and beat them until stiff peaks form. Fold the beaten egg whites into the batter just before cooking.
  • Feel free to get creative with your toppings! Sliced bananas, chopped nuts, or a dollop of whipped cream can all add a delicious twist to these pancakes.

Why Lemon Ricotta Pancakes?

The combination of lemon and ricotta cheese may seem unconventional for pancakes, but it’s a match made in breakfast heaven. The tanginess of the lemon pairs perfectly with the creamy richness of the ricotta, resulting in a pancake that is light, fluffy, and bursting with flavor.

Ricotta cheese adds a unique texture to these pancakes, making them incredibly moist and tender. The addition of lemon zest and juice adds a refreshing citrusy tang that brightens up the entire dish. The result is a pancake that is not only delicious but also visually appealing with its vibrant yellow color.

Whether you’re looking to impress your brunch guests or simply treat yourself to a special breakfast, these Lemon Ricotta Pancakes are a must-try. With their tangy twist and fluffy texture, they are sure to become a favorite in your breakfast repertoire. So go ahead, whip up a batch, and indulge in the delightful flavors of these flavorful pancakes.
