Tasty Chicken Parmesan: A Family Favorite


If there’s one dish that never fails to bring smiles to the faces of the whole family, it’s the classic Chicken Parmesan. This hearty, flavorful Italian-American dish is a real crowd-pleaser, and it’s no wonder why – crispy breaded chicken, tangy marinara sauce, and melted cheese come together to create a symphony of flavors that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

For the Sauce:

  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Assembly:

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the chicken breasts between two sheets of plastic wrap and pound them to an even thickness.
  3. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  4. Set up a breading station with three shallow dishes: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with a mixture of breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, then dip it in the beaten eggs, and finally coat it in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

  1. Heat a generous amount of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Fry the breaded chicken breasts for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and crispy.
  3. Transfer the chicken to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the marinara sauce over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the dried oregano, dried basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Let the sauce simmer for 5-7 minutes, then remove it from the heat.

Step 4: Assemble the Chicken Parmesan

  1. Spread a thin layer of the marinara sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  2. Arrange the fried chicken breasts in the dish.
  3. Spoon the remaining marinara sauce over the chicken.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the top.

Step 5: Bake the Chicken Parmesan

  1. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  2. Remove the dish from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Garnish the Chicken Parmesan with fresh basil leaves before serving.
  2. Serve it hot with your choice of pasta, a crisp green salad, or some garlic bread on the side.

Conclusion

Tasty Chicken Parmesan is a timeless family favorite that never fails to satisfy. Whether it’s for a cozy weeknight dinner or a special gathering with loved ones, this dish is sure to bring joy to the table. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create a memorable meal that will have everyone asking for seconds!
