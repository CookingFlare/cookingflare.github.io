Delightful Nut-Free Cherry Crisp for a Sweet Dessert


Who doesn’t love a warm, fruity dessert that’s both comforting and indulgent? This delicious nut-free cherry crisp is the perfect treat for any occasion, and it’s a great way to make use of fresh, juicy cherries when they’re in season. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a cozy dessert at home, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your collection.

Ingredients

For the Cherry Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh cherries, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract (optional, for a hint of almond flavor without the nuts)
  • Pinch of salt

For the Crisp Topping:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease an 8x8 inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Cherry Filling: In a large bowl, combine the pitted and halved cherries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, vanilla extract, almond extract (if using), and a pinch of salt. Toss the mixture gently to ensure the cherries are well coated. Transfer the cherry filling to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  3. Make the Crisp Topping: In another bowl, mix together the rolled oats, all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, ground cinnamon, and salt. Once combined, pour in the melted butter and stir until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Assemble and Bake: Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the cherry filling in the baking dish. Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the filling is bubbly and the top is golden brown.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Allow the cherry crisp to cool for a few minutes before serving. This delightful dessert is best enjoyed warm, either on its own or with a scoop of creamy vanilla ice cream.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This nut-free cherry crisp is a wonderful alternative for those with nut allergies or for anyone looking to enjoy a classic dessert without the addition of nuts. The combination of sweet, tart cherries with a buttery, crispy topping is simply irresistible. Plus, the addition of a hint of almond extract provides a delightful aroma without the need for actual nuts, making this dessert both flavorful and allergy-friendly.

This recipe is also incredibly versatile. While fresh cherries are ideal, you can also use frozen cherries when they’re not in season. Additionally, feel free to adjust the sweetness to suit your taste, and consider adding a splash of lemon juice to the cherry filling for a touch of brightness.

Whether you’re preparing this cherry crisp for a family gathering, a potluck, or simply as a weeknight treat, its simplicity and crowd-pleasing flavors make it a fantastic go-to dessert. The warm, bubbling cherries paired with the crisp, golden topping are a match made in dessert heaven.

So, next time you find yourself with an abundance of cherries, give this nut-free cherry crisp a try. It’s a delightful, comforting dessert that’s sure to leave everyone asking for seconds!
