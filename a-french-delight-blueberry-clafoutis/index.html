A French Delight: Blueberry Clafoutis


Bonjour, food enthusiasts! Today, we’re delving into the delightful world of French cuisine with a classic treat that’s sure to tantalize your taste buds – Blueberry Clafoutis. This traditional French dessert is a marriage of simplicity and sophistication, showcasing the sweet and juicy blueberries in a custardy, flan-like batter. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a passionate foodie looking to expand your culinary repertoire, this easy-to-follow Blueberry Clafoutis recipe is bound to become a staple in your dessert arsenal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Confectioners’ sugar for dusting

Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat and Prepare

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round baking dish with butter and sprinkle a light coating of granulated sugar to create a delicate crust.

Step 2: Prepare the Blueberries

Rinse the fresh blueberries under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel. Spread the blueberries evenly in the bottom of the prepared baking dish.

Step 3: Prepare the Batter

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until the mixture becomes pale and slightly thickened. Gradually add the flour and salt, whisking until smooth. Slowly pour in the whole milk while whisking continuously until the batter is well combined and free of lumps.

Step 4: Assemble and Bake

Carefully pour the batter over the blueberries in the baking dish, ensuring an even distribution. Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the clafoutis is puffed and golden brown on the edges, and the center is set but still slightly jiggly.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Remove the Blueberry Clafoutis from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Dust the top with a generous sprinkling of confectioners’ sugar before serving. This dessert can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature, and it pairs beautifully with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

The Charm of Blueberry Clafoutis

The beauty of Blueberry Clafoutis lies in its versatility. It can be served as a comforting ending to a lavish dinner party, or as a simple yet elegant treat for a cozy night in. The burst of sweet, juicy blueberries nestled in the creamy custard is a celebration of seasonal fruits and French culinary finesse.
