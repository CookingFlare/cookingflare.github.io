Sous Vide Desserts: Indulge in Sweet Sous Vide Treats


Are you a dessert lover looking to take your sweet tooth on a culinary adventure? Look no further than sous vide desserts. Sous vide cooking has gained popularity in recent years for its ability to create perfectly cooked and flavorful dishes, and desserts are no exception. In this post, we will explore the world of sous vide desserts and share some indulgent recipes that will satisfy even the most discerning dessert connoisseurs.

What is Sous Vide Cooking?

Sous vide, which means “under vacuum” in French, is a cooking technique that involves sealing food in airtight bags and cooking it in a water bath at a precise temperature. This method ensures that the food is cooked evenly and retains its moisture, resulting in tender and flavorful dishes. While sous vide is commonly associated with cooking meats and vegetables, it can also be used to create delectable desserts.

The Benefits of Sous Vide Desserts

Sous vide desserts offer a unique twist to traditional sweet treats. The precise temperature control of sous vide cooking allows for a level of precision that is difficult to achieve with other cooking methods. This precision ensures that desserts are cooked perfectly every time, with a consistently smooth and silky texture.

Additionally, sous vide desserts require minimal effort and supervision. Once you have set the temperature and cooking time, you can simply let the sous vide machine do its magic while you focus on other tasks. This makes sous vide desserts perfect for busy individuals who still want to indulge in homemade sweets.

Indulgent Sous Vide Dessert Recipes

Now that you’re familiar with sous vide cooking and its benefits, let’s dive into some mouthwatering dessert recipes that will elevate your culinary skills and impress your friends and family.

1. Sous Vide Crème Brûlée

Ingredients:

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Extra sugar for caramelizing

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar until well combined.
  2. Add the heavy cream, vanilla extract, and salt to the egg mixture. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Pour the mixture into individual sous vide bags, ensuring that they are sealed tightly.
  4. Preheat the water bath to 176°F (80°C) and place the bags in the water bath.
  5. Cook the crème brûlée for 1 hour.
  6. Once cooked, remove the bags from the water bath and let them cool in an ice bath for 10 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle a thin layer of sugar on top of each crème brûlée and use a kitchen torch to caramelize the sugar.
  8. Serve and enjoy!

2. Sous Vide Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the dark chocolate and butter together until smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, powdered sugar, eggs, egg yolks, vanilla extract, and salt.
  3. Gradually add the melted chocolate mixture to the egg mixture, whisking until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter into individual sous vide jars or ramekins, leaving some space at the top for expansion.
  5. Preheat the water bath to 176°F (80°C) and place the jars/ramekins in the water bath.
  6. Cook the lava cakes for 45 minutes.
  7. Once cooked, remove the jars/ramekins from the water bath and let them cool for a few minutes.
  8. Carefully invert the jars/ramekins onto serving plates to release the lava cakes.
  9. Serve warm and enjoy the ooey-gooey goodness!

3. Sous Vide Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, flour, and salt until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  4. Preheat the water bath to 176°F (80°C) and place the springform pan in the water bath.
  5. Cook the cheesecake for 2 hours.
  6. Once cooked, remove the springform pan from the water bath and let the cheesecake cool to room temperature.
  7. Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to set.
  8. Slice and serve chilled, and savor the creamy goodness!

Whether you choose to try one of the recipes mentioned or experiment with your own, sous vide desserts are sure to elevate your dessert game to new heights. So go ahead, indulge in these sweet sous vide treats and treat yourself to a culinary adventure like no other!
