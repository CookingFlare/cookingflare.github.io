Flavorful Bacon-Wrapped Cream Cheese Stuffed Peppers


If you’re looking for a show-stopping appetizer or a delicious side dish that’s sure to impress, then look no further than these flavorful bacon-wrapped cream cheese stuffed peppers. This tantalizing dish combines the smoky richness of bacon with the creamy goodness of cream cheese, all enveloped in the spicy kick of fresh peppers. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a savory treat, these stuffed peppers are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 12 fresh jalapeño peppers
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Peppers

Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). While the oven is heating up, slice the jalapeño peppers in half lengthwise. Carefully remove the seeds and membranes from each pepper to reduce their heat level. If you prefer a spicier dish, you can leave some of the seeds and membranes intact.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix the ingredients thoroughly until they are well combined and smooth.

Step 3: Stuff the Peppers

Using a spoon or a piping bag, generously fill each jalapeño half with the cream cheese mixture. Ensure that each pepper is evenly filled to create a satisfying bite of creamy goodness.

Step 4: Wrap with Bacon

Next, take a slice of bacon and wrap it around each cream cheese-filled pepper, securing it with a toothpick if necessary. The smoky flavor of the bacon will infuse the peppers with an irresistible richness as they cook.

Step 5: Bake to Perfection

Place the bacon-wrapped stuffed peppers on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, ensuring that they are evenly spaced. Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake the peppers for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the peppers are tender.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the bacon-wrapped cream cheese stuffed peppers are golden brown and irresistible, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Serve these delectable treats on a platter and watch as your guests delight in the flavorful combination of creamy cheese, spicy peppers, and savory bacon.

Conclusion

These bacon-wrapped cream cheese stuffed peppers are a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that are sure to elevate any occasion. Whether you’re a fan of spicy dishes or simply enjoy indulging in delicious appetizers, these stuffed peppers are a must-try. With their irresistible combination of creaminess, smokiness, and heat, these peppers are bound to become a new favorite in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the delightful marriage of bacon, cream cheese, and fresh peppers in every bite.
