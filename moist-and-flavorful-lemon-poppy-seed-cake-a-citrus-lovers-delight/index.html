Moist and Flavorful Lemon Poppy Seed Cake: A Citrus Lover's Delight


If you’re a fan of citrusy desserts, then this Moist and Flavorful Lemon Poppy Seed Cake is a must-try for you. Bursting with zesty lemon flavor and speckled with tiny poppy seeds, this delightful cake is the perfect treat for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, celebrating a birthday, or simply craving a delicious dessert, this recipe is sure to impress. So, let’s dive into the world of citrusy goodness and learn how to create this mouthwatering Lemon Poppy Seed Cake.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and poppy seeds.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the sour cream, fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. While the cake is baking, prepare the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, fresh lemon juice, and lemon zest until smooth.

  9. Once the cake is done, let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Drizzle the lemon glaze over the cooled cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.

Serving Suggestions

This Moist and Flavorful Lemon Poppy Seed Cake pairs beautifully with a cup of hot tea or coffee. It’s also a lovely dessert to serve at a springtime gathering or as a sweet ending to a family dinner. The bright, citrusy flavor of the cake is perfectly complemented by the subtle crunch of the poppy seeds, creating a delightful texture in every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra pop of lemon flavor, add a few drops of lemon extract to the batter.
  • Feel free to experiment with different glazes, such as a lemon cream cheese glaze or a simple lemon syrup.
  • This recipe can also be adapted to make lemon poppy seed muffins by pouring the batter into a muffin tin and adjusting the baking time accordingly.

So there you have it – a simple yet irresistible recipe for Moist and Flavorful Lemon Poppy Seed Cake. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this cake is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire. The bright, citrusy flavors and tender crumb make it a standout dessert that’s perfect for any occasion. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a slice of citrusy heaven!
