Fresh Strawberry Cupcakes: A Summery Twist to Traditional Flavors


As the warm summer days roll in, we all crave something fresh, fruity, and delightful to indulge in. And what could be more perfect than fresh strawberry cupcakes? These little treats are a delightful twist on the traditional flavors, bringing a burst of summery sweetness to your taste buds. So, let’s dive into the recipe for these delectable Fresh Strawberry Cupcakes that are bound to become a favorite at your next gathering.

Ingredients:

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, finely chopped

For the Strawberry Buttercream Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/4 cup strawberry puree (made from fresh strawberries)
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. Prepare the Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Cream the Butter and Sugar: In a separate bowl, cream the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Incorporate the Wet Ingredients: Add the eggs, one at a time, to the butter and sugar mixture. Then, add the vanilla extract and mix until fully incorporated.

  5. Combine Dry and Wet Mixtures: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with the milk, and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Fold in the Fresh Strawberries: Gently fold in the finely chopped fresh strawberries into the batter.

  7. Fill the Cupcake Liners: Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  8. Bake the Cupcakes: Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely before frosting.

  9. Prepare the Strawberry Buttercream Frosting: In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar, strawberry puree, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Beat until smooth and creamy.

  10. Frost the Cupcakes: Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them with the strawberry buttercream frosting using a piping bag or a spatula.

  11. Garnish and Serve: Garnish the cupcakes with a fresh strawberry slice or a sprinkle of chopped strawberries. Serve and enjoy!

These Fresh Strawberry Cupcakes are a delightful treat that perfectly captures the essence of summer. The burst of fresh strawberry flavor combined with the light, fluffy texture of the cupcakes and the creamy strawberry buttercream frosting creates a truly irresistible combination. Whether you’re hosting a summer garden party, a birthday celebration, or simply craving a taste of summer, these cupcakes are sure to impress.
