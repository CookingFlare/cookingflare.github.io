Zesty Thai Shrimp Stir-Fry: A Quick Weeknight Dinner


Are you tired of the same old weeknight dinner routine? Look no further than this zesty Thai shrimp stir-fry recipe to add some excitement to your kitchen! Packed with vibrant flavors, this dish is a quick and easy way to transport your taste buds to the streets of Bangkok without leaving the comfort of your home. Let’s dive into the delightful world of Thai cuisine and whip up a delicious meal together.

Ingredients

To start off, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly grated ginger
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prep Work

Before you start cooking, make sure to prepare all the ingredients. Mince the garlic, grate the ginger, and slice the bell peppers, snap peas, and carrots. Having everything ready to go will make the cooking process smooth and efficient.

Step 2: Searing the Shrimp

In a wok or a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the shrimp in a single layer and let them sear for about 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the wok and set them aside on a plate.

Step 3: Stir-Fry Magic

Using the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed and toss in the minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté them for about 30 seconds until they become fragrant. Next, add the sliced bell peppers, snap peas, and carrots to the wok, stirring constantly for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender yet still crisp.

Step 4: Flavor Infusion

Now, it’s time to infuse the stir-fry with the incredible flavors of Thai cuisine. Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and brown sugar, stirring to combine. Sprinkle in the chili flakes for a hint of heat and continue tossing the ingredients together until everything is well coated in the luscious sauce.

Step 5: Bringing It All Together

Return the seared shrimp to the wok and gently toss them with the vegetable mixture, ensuring that each shrimp is coated in the flavorful sauce. Let everything cook together for another 2-3 minutes so that the shrimp absorb all the delicious Thai-inspired flavors.

Step 6: Finishing Touches

Once the shrimp and vegetables are perfectly combined, remove the wok from the heat. Garnish the dish with fresh cilantro for a burst of herbaceous aroma. Serve the zesty Thai shrimp stir-fry over a bed of fluffy jasmine rice and get ready to savor the delightful flavors of this quick and easy weeknight dinner.

Final Thoughts

In just a few simple steps, you’ve created a mouthwatering Thai-inspired dish that’s perfect for those busy weeknights when you crave something bold and flavorful. The zesty Thai shrimp stir-fry is a celebration of fresh ingredients, vibrant colors, and an explosion of tastes that will leave you craving more. So, why not spice up your dinner routine with this delightful recipe and treat yourself to a taste of Thailand right in your own kitchen? Cheers to the joy of cooking and the pleasure of savoring every bite!
