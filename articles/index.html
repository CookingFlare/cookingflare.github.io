Chocolate Oreo Cheesecake: A Cookie Lover's Dream

If you’re a fan of cookies and cheesecake, then you’re in for a real treat with this Chocolate Oreo Cheesecake recipe. This indulgent dessert combines the rich, creamy goodness of cheesecake with the irresistible crunch of Oreo cookies. It’s a match made in dessert heaven, and it’s perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a dinner party or just treating yourself to a decadent sweet treat. Ingredients For the Oreo Crust: 24 Oreo cookies 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted For the Cheesecake Filling: 32 oz cream cheese, at room temperature 1 cup granulated sugar 1 tsp vanilla extract 4 large eggs 1 cup sour cream 1/2 cup heavy cream 8 Oreo cookies, coarsely chopped For the Chocolate Ganache: 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips 1/2 cup heavy cream For the Topping: Additional Oreo cookies for garnish Instructions Step 1: Preparing the Oreo Crust Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Place the Oreo cookies in a food processor and pulse until they are finely ground. Transfer the cookie crumbs to a bowl and stir in the melted butter until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, forming an even layer. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside to cool. Step 2: Making the Cheesecake Filling In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy. Add the vanilla extract and mix until combined. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed. Stir in the sour cream and heavy cream until the mixture is smooth and well combined. Gently fold in the chopped Oreo cookies. Pour the filling over the cooled Oreo crust and spread it into an even layer. Step 3: Baking the Cheesecake Place the springform pan in a large roasting pan and fill the roasting pan with enough hot water to reach halfway up the sides of the springform pan. Bake the cheesecake for 55-60 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside with the door slightly ajar for 1 hour. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and run a knife around the edge of the pan to loosen the cheesecake. Allow it to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight. Step 4: Making the Chocolate Ganache In a heatproof bowl, combine the chocolate chips and heavy cream. Microwave the mixture in 30-second intervals, stirring after each interval, until the chocolate is completely melted and the ganache is smooth. Allow the ganache to cool slightly, then pour it over the chilled cheesecake, spreading it into an even layer. Step 5: Adding the Final Touches Garnish the cheesecake with additional Oreo cookies, if desired. Slice and serve the Chocolate Oreo Cheesecake, and enjoy the heavenly combination of rich chocolate, creamy cheesecake, and delightful cookie crunch. Conclusion This Chocolate Oreo Cheesecake is a show-stopping dessert that will impress any cookie and cheesecake lover. With its velvety smooth texture, decadent chocolate flavor, and irresistible Oreo crunch, it’s sure to become a favorite in your dessert repertoire.
