Tangy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry


Are you craving a dish that bursts with zesty flavors and offers a perfect balance of tanginess and garlic goodness? Look no further than this delightful Tangy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry recipe. This easy-to-make dish is a surefire way to impress your taste buds and elevate your culinary skills to new heights. Let’s dive right into the details of creating this flavorful masterpiece!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 1/4 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Shrimp:

    • In a bowl, combine the shrimp with minced garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  2. Prepare the Sauce:

    • In a separate bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, and lemon juice. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Stir-Fry the Ingredients:

    • Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
    • Add the marinated shrimp to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the pan and set them aside.

  4. Cook the Vegetables:

    • In the same pan, add the sliced bell peppers and snap peas. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender yet still crisp.

  5. Combine Everything:

    • Return the cooked shrimp to the pan with the vegetables.
    • Pour the prepared sauce over the shrimp and vegetables. Toss everything together to ensure the sauce coats all the ingredients evenly.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Garnish the Tangy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry with chopped green onions for a pop of color and freshness.
    • Serve the dish hot over a bed of steamed rice or noodles.

Tips:

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the dish.
  • Feel free to customize the stir-fry with your favorite vegetables like broccoli, carrots, or mushrooms.
  • Adjust the level of sweetness by increasing or decreasing the amount of honey in the sauce.

Conclusion:

This Tangy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry recipe is a quick and flavorful meal option that is perfect for busy weeknights or special occasions. The combination of tangy lemon, savory garlic, and succulent shrimp creates a harmony of flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. Give this recipe a try and experience a burst of freshness in every bite!
