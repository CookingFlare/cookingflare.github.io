One Pot Cheesy Taco Pasta


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further than this One Pot Cheesy Taco Pasta recipe! Packed with flavorful spices, tender pasta, and gooey cheese, it’s a dish that will have your taste buds dancing with joy. And the best part? You only need one pot to make it, which means less time spent on cleanup and more time enjoying your meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 8 ounces rotini pasta
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, chopped cilantro, sliced jalapeños

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain any excess grease.

  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot with the cooked ground beef. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes, until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Sprinkle the taco seasoning over the beef mixture and stir well to coat. Let it cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and chicken broth. Stir everything together until well combined.

  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Add the rotini pasta to the pot and give it a good stir. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked to your desired tenderness.

  6. Once the pasta is cooked, remove the pot from the heat. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the top, and cover the pot again. Let it sit for a few minutes, allowing the cheese to melt.

  7. Give the cheesy taco pasta a final stir to incorporate the melted cheese throughout the dish. Serve hot, and garnish with your favorite toppings such as sour cream, chopped cilantro, or sliced jalapeños.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking! Add some diced bell peppers or corn kernels for extra color and flavor. You can also swap out the ground beef for ground turkey or chicken if you prefer a leaner protein option.

  • If you like your pasta on the spicier side, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the pot before simmering.

  • This recipe makes generous servings, but if you have leftovers, they can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop for a quick and easy meal.

  • If you’re feeding a crowd or want to make this dish for a potluck, you can easily double or triple the recipe. Just make sure you have a large enough pot to accommodate the additional ingredients.

Enjoy the deliciousness of this One Pot Cheesy Taco Pasta! It’s a crowd-pleasing dish that will satisfy your cravings for a hearty and flavorful meal. Plus, with just one pot to clean, it’s a recipe that’s as convenient as it is tasty. So gather your ingredients, get cooking, and prepare to indulge in a dish that’s sure to become a family favorite.
