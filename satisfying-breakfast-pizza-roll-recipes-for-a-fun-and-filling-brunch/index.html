Satisfying Breakfast Pizza Roll Recipes for a Fun and Filling Brunch


Are you tired of the same old breakfast options? Do you want to impress your family and friends with a delicious and satisfying brunch dish? Look no further than these delightful breakfast pizza roll recipes! Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply want to elevate your weekend breakfast routine, these recipes are sure to be a hit. From classic combinations to unique flavor twists, there’s something here for everyone to enjoy.

Classic Breakfast Pizza Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked breakfast sausage
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a rectangle, then spread the marinara sauce evenly over the surface.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, leaving a border around the edges.
  4. Evenly distribute the cooked breakfast sausage, bell peppers, and onions over the cheese.
  5. Carefully crack the eggs onto the pizza, spacing them out evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then gently roll the pizza into a log, starting from one of the longer sides.
  7. Place the rolled pizza on the prepared baking sheet, seam side down, and brush the top with a little olive oil.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  9. Let the pizza roll rest for a few minutes before slicing it into rounds. Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm.

Bacon and Cheese Breakfast Pizza Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound refrigerated pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup creamy cheese sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup diced red onions
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Roll out the refrigerated pizza dough into a rectangle, then spread the creamy cheese sauce evenly over the surface.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the sauce, leaving a border around the edges.
  4. Evenly distribute the cooked bacon and diced red onions over the cheese.
  5. Carefully crack the eggs onto the pizza, spacing them out evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then gently roll the pizza into a log, starting from one of the longer sides.
  7. Place the rolled pizza on the prepared baking sheet, seam side down, and brush the top with a little olive oil.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  9. Let the pizza roll rest for a few minutes before slicing it into rounds. Garnish with chopped chives and serve warm.

Whether you’re a fan of classic breakfast flavors, a lover of all things veggie, or a bacon enthusiast, these breakfast pizza roll recipes offer a delicious and satisfying twist on traditional brunch fare. With their versatile ingredients and simple preparation, these recipes are perfect for any occasion.
