BBQ Chicken Skewers: Flavorful Recipes for Grilled Kabobs


When it comes to summer grilling, BBQ chicken skewers are a classic choice. These flavorful recipes for grilled kabobs will take your outdoor cooking game to the next level. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply enjoying a sunny day with your family, these mouthwatering chicken skewers will surely impress your guests. So fire up the grill, grab your skewers, and let’s get cooking!

1. Tangy Teriyaki Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if desired).
  2. Thread the chicken pieces onto the soaked wooden skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.
  3. Place the skewers in a shallow dish and pour the teriyaki marinade over them, ensuring each skewer is well coated. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Remove the skewers from the marinade and discard any leftover marinade.
  6. Grill the skewers for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds, if desired.

2. Spicy Honey Lime Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, honey, lime juice, hot sauce, minced garlic, and chili powder.
  2. Thread the chicken pieces onto the soaked wooden skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.
  3. Place the skewers in a shallow dish and pour the honey lime marinade over them, ensuring each skewer is well coated. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Remove the skewers from the marinade and discard any leftover marinade.
  6. Grill the skewers for about 4-6 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and nicely charred.
  7. Serve hot and drizzle with extra honey and lime juice, if desired.

3. Mediterranean Lemon Herb Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, basil, or oregano)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped fresh herbs, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Thread the chicken pieces onto the soaked wooden skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.
  3. Place the skewers in a shallow dish and pour the lemon herb marinade over them, ensuring each skewer is well coated. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Remove the skewers from the marinade and discard any leftover marinade.
  6. Grill the skewers for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with extra fresh herbs, if desired.

Conclusion

These BBQ chicken skewers are the perfect addition to any summer cookout. With their flavorful marinades, they are sure to become a crowd favorite. Whether you prefer a tangy teriyaki, a spicy honey lime, or a Mediterranean lemon herb flavor, these recipes will satisfy your cravings. So fire up the grill, gather your family and friends, and enjoy these mouthwatering grilled kabobs. Happy grilling!
