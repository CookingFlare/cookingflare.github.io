One-Pot Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Recipes for Effortless Cooking


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen, juggling multiple pots and pans to prepare a delicious meal? Look no further! One-pot Instant Pot chicken and rice recipes are here to save the day. With the convenience of the Instant Pot and the simplicity of one-pot cooking, you can create flavorful and satisfying meals with minimal effort. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of mouthwatering recipes that will revolutionize your weeknight dinners.

Creamy Lemon Chicken and Rice

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • ¼ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In the Instant Pot, combine the rice, chicken broth, lemon juice, garlic, thyme, and oregano.
  3. Place the seasoned chicken breasts on top of the rice mixture.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set the vent to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is complete, quick release the pressure.
  6. Remove the chicken breasts from the Instant Pot and set aside.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream into the rice mixture until well combined.
  8. Shred the chicken using two forks and return it to the Instant Pot.
  9. Serve the creamy lemon chicken and rice hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Teriyaki Chicken and Pineapple Rice

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 ¼ cups water
  • ½ cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Green onions, sliced, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cut the chicken thighs into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In the Instant Pot, combine the jasmine rice, water, teriyaki sauce, pineapple chunks, and sliced red bell pepper.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the Instant Pot.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set the vent to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is complete, quick release the pressure.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water to create a slurry.
  7. Stir the slurry into the chicken and rice mixture in the Instant Pot.
  8. Select the “saute” function on the Instant Pot and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce thickens.
  9. Serve the teriyaki chicken and pineapple rice hot, garnished with sesame seeds and sliced green onions.

Mediterranean Chicken and Brown Rice

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 ¾ cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup pitted Kalamata olives
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, dried oregano, and dried basil.
  2. In the Instant Pot, combine the brown rice, chicken broth, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Nestle the seasoned chicken thighs into the rice mixture.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set the vent to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 20 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is complete, natural release the pressure for 10 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.
  6. Remove the chicken thighs from the Instant Pot and set aside.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork, then stir in the crumbled feta cheese and chopped fresh parsley.
  8. Serve the Mediterranean chicken and brown rice hot, with the chicken thighs on top.

Conclusion

With these one-pot Instant Pot chicken and rice recipes, you can elevate your cooking game while simplifying your meal preparation. Whether you crave a creamy lemon-infused dish, a sweet and savory teriyaki creation, or a Mediterranean-inspired feast, these recipes have got you covered. So, grab your Instant Pot and give these effortless recipes a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Note: The cooking times mentioned in the recipes are based on an 8-quart Instant Pot. Adjust the cooking times accordingly if you are using a different size.
