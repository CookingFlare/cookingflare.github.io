Allergy-Free Mexican Pasta: Flavorful and Satisfying


Are you craving a delicious and allergy-friendly Mexican dish? Look no further! This Allergy-Free Mexican Pasta recipe is packed with flavor and will leave you feeling fully satisfied. Whether you have dietary restrictions or simply want to try something new, this recipe is perfect for a cozy night in or a gathering with friends. Let’s dive into the ingredients and steps to create this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of gluten-free pasta (choose your favorite shape)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup of corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pasta: Cook the gluten-free pasta according to the package instructions. Once al dente, drain and set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent. Then, add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Veggies: To the skillet, add the diced red and yellow bell peppers, black beans, and corn kernels. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.

  4. Season the Dish: Sprinkle the ground cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and dried oregano over the vegetable mixture. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices.

  5. Incorporate the Tomatoes: Pour the can of diced tomatoes into the skillet and stir to combine. Allow the mixture to simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Combine with Pasta: Add the cooked gluten-free pasta to the skillet and gently toss everything together until the pasta is well coated with the flavorful vegetable and bean mixture.

  7. Serve and Garnish: Once everything is combined, transfer the Allergy-Free Mexican Pasta to a serving dish. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness and color.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a diced jalapeño to the dish.
  • If you’re a fan of avocado, consider adding sliced avocado on top before serving for a creamy and nutritious addition.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable selection based on your preferences. Zucchini, mushrooms, or spinach would be delightful additions to this dish.

Final Thoughts

This Allergy-Free Mexican Pasta recipe is a delightful blend of vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, making it a perfect option for those with dietary restrictions or anyone looking for a nourishing and satisfying meal. Enjoy the bold and comforting flavors of Mexican cuisine without worrying about allergens. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or sharing with loved ones, this dish is sure to impress and satisfy.

We hope you give this recipe a try and discover the joy of allergy-friendly Mexican cuisine right in your own kitchen. Buena provecho!
