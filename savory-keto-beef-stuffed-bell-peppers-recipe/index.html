Savory Keto Beef Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb meal, this Savory Keto Beef Stuffed Bell Peppers recipe is just what you need. Packed with flavors and nutrients, these stuffed bell peppers make for a hearty and wholesome dish that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized. Plus, they’re keto-friendly, making them perfect for those following a low-carb lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking dish with parchment paper or lightly grease it with cooking spray.

  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the peppers upright in the prepared baking dish and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef until no longer pink. Drain any excess fat from the skillet.

  4. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the ground beef. Cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.

  5. Stir in the cauliflower rice, dried oregano, dried basil, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the cauliflower rice is tender.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the mixture cool slightly.

  7. Spoon the beef and cauliflower rice mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers, filling them to the top.

  8. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the stuffed bell peppers.

  9. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the bell peppers are tender and the cheese is melted and golden brown.

  10. Remove the stuffed bell peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  11. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Savory Keto Beef Stuffed Bell Peppers can be enjoyed on their own as a complete meal. However, if you’d like to add some extra elements to your plate, here are a few serving suggestions:

  • Serve alongside a fresh green salad for a lighter option.
  • Pair with cauliflower mash for a comforting and satisfying meal.
  • Top with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of chopped green onions for added flavor.

Conclusion

This Savory Keto Beef Stuffed Bell Peppers recipe is a fantastic choice for anyone looking for a flavorful and low-carb meal option. The combination of ground beef, cauliflower rice, and spices creates a delicious filling that is perfectly complemented by the tender bell pepper shells. Plus, the melted mozzarella cheese adds a creamy and indulgent touch.

Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking to incorporate more low-carb meals into your routine, these stuffed bell peppers are a must-try. They are easy to make, packed with nutrients, and incredibly satisfying. So, give this recipe a go and enjoy a delicious and healthy meal that will leave you wanting more.
