Healthy Indian Salads: Fresh Recipes for Light Meals


As the temperatures rise, the craving for light, healthy meals becomes overwhelming. While salads may be a staple in Western cuisine, India offers an amazing variety of its own, blending the vibrant colors and bold flavors of the country's diverse regions. In this blog post, we'll take you on a culinary journey across India, exploring the unique and refreshing flavors of its salads. Classic Raita Salad (Cucumber and Yogurt Salad) North Indian cuisine offers a refreshing twist on salads with the Creamy Raita Salad. The combination of cucumber, onion, cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper, and yogurt will quench your thirst for a cooling snack.

1 large cucumber 1 large onion 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon coriander 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper 1/2 cup of plain yogurt Salt to taste Cilantro for garnish

Combine the grated cucumber and onion, chopped fresh cilantro, salt, cumin, coriander, and cayenne pepper in a bowl. In a separate bowl, mash the plain yogurt, then combine it with the vegetable mixture. Stir well and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving. Tale of Three Ways: Tomato Salad with Mango Cilantro Dressing This tropical salad comes straight from the cuisines of Southern India. Sweet, juicy tomatoes combined with a delectable mango cilantro dressing will draw you instantly. Pair this salad with whole wheat bread for an addiction filled snack.

3 medium tomatoes 1 ripe mango 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro 2 tablespoons of olive oil 2 tablespoons of lemon juice 1/4 cup of plain yogurt Salt to taste

Grate the tomatoes, while blending the mango, cilantro, olive oil, and lemon juice to create the dressing. Combine the two to create a work of art, or a sheer delight on the plate. Adding a dollop of yogurt over the dish tops it off.
