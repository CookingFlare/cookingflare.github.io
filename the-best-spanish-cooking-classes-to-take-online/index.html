The Best Spanish Cooking Classes to Take Online


Spanish cuisine is renowned for its rich history, vibrant flavors, and diverse regional dishes. From the world-famous paella to the lesser-known but equally delicious tapas, Spain’s food culture is deeply intertwined with its history, traditions, and local ingredients. If you’re eager to learn the art of Spanish cooking, taking an online cooking class can be a perfect way to immerse yourself in this culinary world from the comfort of your home.

In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best online Spanish cooking classes that can help you master everything from iconic dishes to regional specialties, making you feel like you’re in a Spanish kitchen no matter where you are. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned cook, there’s a course for you. So, let’s dive into the world of Spanish cooking and discover the best online classes available to help you get started.

1. Spanish Cooking with José Andrés (MasterClass)

When it comes to Spanish cuisine, few names are as iconic as José Andrés. A celebrated chef, author, and restaurateur, José Andrés is often credited with introducing authentic Spanish dishes to the United States. His online Spanish cooking class on MasterClass offers an exceptional opportunity to learn from a true master of the craft.

Course Overview:

  • Instructor: José Andrés
  • Duration: Approximately 3 hours
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Course Structure: The class is broken down into short video lessons that cover key aspects of Spanish cooking. You’ll learn how to make traditional Spanish dishes like paella, gazpacho, and tortilla española, along with tips for perfecting your knife skills and cooking techniques. The lessons are infused with José’s charismatic teaching style, making the process engaging and fun.

Why Take This Course?

José Andrés brings his passion for Spanish food to every lesson, giving you an insider’s view into the importance of ingredients, technique, and cultural influences behind Spanish cooking. His emphasis on creating authentic dishes while using accessible ingredients is perfect for those who want to recreate the essence of Spain in their own kitchens.

2. The Spanish Cooking School (Online Cooking School)

The Spanish Cooking School offers a unique and comprehensive way to learn Spanish cuisine online. Based in Spain, this school provides live, interactive online cooking classes taught by professional chefs who specialize in Spanish food. Their courses are designed to give you a hands-on experience that mimics what you would learn in a traditional cooking class in Spain.

Course Overview:

  • Instructor: Various chefs from Spain
  • Duration: Varies by course
  • Level: Beginner to Advanced
  • Course Structure: The school offers a range of online courses that cover different aspects of Spanish cuisine. You can choose from specific dishes, like paella, or opt for more advanced courses focused on Spanish tapas or Spanish desserts. All classes are interactive, and you can ask questions and receive feedback during the class, making the experience feel personal and tailored to your needs.

Why Take This Course?

What sets The Spanish Cooking School apart is its emphasis on traditional Spanish ingredients, techniques, and flavors. They offer live classes, which means you get the benefit of direct interaction with expert chefs. Additionally, they provide a wide range of course options, allowing you to learn everything from basic Spanish cooking techniques to complex dishes, ensuring that you can tailor your learning experience to your interests and skill level.

3. Spain on a Fork (YouTube Channel and Online Courses)

If you prefer learning through free content, Spain on a Fork is an excellent resource. This YouTube channel, run by Spanish chef Albert Bevia, is dedicated to teaching people how to cook traditional Spanish dishes with easy-to-follow recipes and step-by-step instructions. The channel also offers more structured online cooking courses for those who want to dive deeper into Spanish cuisine.

Course Overview:

  • Instructor: Albert Bevia
  • Duration: Varies
  • Level: Beginner
  • Course Structure: Spain on a Fork’s YouTube channel provides a plethora of free cooking videos, many of which focus on classic Spanish recipes. The channel also features in-depth online cooking courses that focus on specific Spanish dishes or types of food, such as Spanish tapas or Mediterranean cooking. Courses are broken down into clear, easy-to-follow videos that walk you through each step.

Why Take This Course?

Spain on a Fork is great for those who want to start with easy, accessible recipes before progressing to more complex dishes. Albert’s down-to-earth teaching style makes Spanish cooking approachable, even for beginners. Additionally, the channel’s free content makes it an affordable option for those on a budget.

4. The Chef & The Dish (Private Virtual Cooking Classes)

For those looking for a more personalized and interactive Spanish cooking experience, The Chef & The Dish offers private virtual cooking lessons with chefs based in Spain. This platform connects you with professional chefs for one-on-one cooking sessions in the comfort of your own kitchen, allowing you to ask questions and get tailored feedback in real time.

Course Overview:

  • Instructor: Various chefs from Spain
  • Duration: 2 hours per session
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Course Structure: Each class is designed to be interactive and customized to your preferences. You can choose from a variety of Spanish dishes to learn, such as paella, croquetas, or even more advanced dishes like fideuà (a noodle-based seafood dish). The chef will guide you through the process in real time, offering hands-on instruction and tips to help you perfect your technique.

Why Take This Course?

The main appeal of The Chef & The Dish is the opportunity to have a private cooking class with a Spanish chef who can provide personal guidance. Whether you’re looking to master paella or learn how to make the perfect Spanish omelet, the one-on-one setting ensures you get tailored advice and feedback. This is perfect for anyone who values a more intimate and custom learning experience.

5. Cookly (Spanish Cooking Classes Online)

Cookly offers a wide range of virtual cooking experiences, including Spanish cuisine. With live, interactive classes taught by expert chefs from Spain, Cookly lets you learn Spanish cooking in a fun and engaging way, all from the comfort of your home.

Course Overview:

  • Instructor: Various chefs from Spain
  • Duration: 1.5–2 hours
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Course Structure: Cookly provides live virtual cooking classes that focus on Spanish classics like paella, gazpacho, and more. The classes are live, so you can interact with the chef, ask questions, and get tips on how to improve your cooking skills. Cookly’s chefs are passionate about Spanish food and offer a thorough understanding of the history and cultural significance behind each dish.

Why Take This Course?

Cookly offers a variety of options when it comes to online Spanish cooking classes. The live sessions allow for real-time interaction, making them feel more personal and engaging than pre-recorded tutorials. Plus, Cookly’s chefs are knowledgeable and passionate about Spanish cuisine, making the experience both educational and enjoyable.

6. Udemy: Spanish Cooking Courses

Udemy is a well-known online learning platform that offers a variety of cooking classes, including Spanish cuisine. You can find a range of courses that cover everything from beginner-level dishes to more advanced Spanish cooking techniques. With pre-recorded lessons and lifetime access to course materials, Udemy allows you to learn at your own pace.

Course Overview:

  • Instructor: Various instructors
  • Duration: Varies by course
  • Level: Beginner to Advanced
  • Course Structure: Udemy features numerous Spanish cooking courses, each with a different focus. Some courses focus on individual dishes, like paella, while others provide a comprehensive overview of Spanish cuisine, covering everything from tapas to regional specialties. Each course includes video lessons, downloadable materials, and lifetime access.

Why Take This Course?

Udemy’s Spanish cooking courses are ideal for those who prefer to learn at their own pace. The courses are affordable, and you can choose the one that best fits your interests. Additionally, the lifetime access to course materials ensures that you can revisit lessons whenever you need a refresher.

7. La Tienda (Spanish Cooking Classes and Recipe Videos)

La Tienda, a popular online marketplace for Spanish products, also offers cooking classes and recipe videos on their website. They specialize in authentic Spanish ingredients, which they often use in their cooking lessons to ensure you’re using the right products for each dish.

Course Overview:

  • Instructor: Various chefs
  • Duration: Varies
  • Level: Beginner
  • Course Structure: La Tienda offers both free recipe videos and paid cooking classes that cover a range of Spanish dishes. The classes focus on regional specialties, including dishes from Andalusia, Catalonia, and Galicia. The recipe videos are step-by-step guides that teach you how to make classic Spanish dishes using traditional ingredients.

Why Take This Course?

La Tienda is a great option if you want to learn how to cook authentic Spanish dishes while using high-quality, Spanish-sourced ingredients. The focus on regional Spanish cuisine provides a deeper understanding of the diverse culinary traditions found throughout Spain.

Conclusion

Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned home cook, there’s a Spanish cooking class out there to suit your needs. From the charismatic José Andrés on MasterClass to personalized one-on-one lessons with chefs from Spain, the options are abundant. Online classes make it easy to explore the vibrant and diverse world of Spanish cuisine, and they provide a perfect opportunity to hone your skills and impress your friends and family with your culinary creations.

Learning Spanish cooking can open up a world of flavors, traditions, and techniques that will not only make your meals more exciting but also give you a deeper appreciation for Spain’s rich culinary heritage. So, grab your apron, choose the best online class for you, and start your journey into the world of Spanish food today!
