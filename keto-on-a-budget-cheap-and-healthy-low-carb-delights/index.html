Keto on a Budget: Cheap and Healthy Low-Carb Delights


Looking for scrumptious low-carb meals without breaking the bank? You’ve come to the right place! In this blog post, we’ll explore the world of cheap and healthy keto-friendly recipes. Get ready to amaze your taste buds while keeping a tight grip on your wallet.

Welcome to Keto on a Budget

Some people think that going keto means bidding farewell to affordable meals. But fear not, my friends! Keto doesn’t have to be an expensive culinary journey. By incorporating budget-friendly ingredients and planning ahead, you can indulge in these delightful low-carb dishes without hurting your wallet.

Below, I’ve gathered eight flavorful keto meals that won’t drain your bank account. Say goodbye to mundane keto dinners and hello to vibrant, mouthwatering dishes!

1. Cabbage Chicken Stir-Fry

Delicious, nutritious, and budget-friendly – this Cabbage Chicken Stir-Fry is worth a try. Loaded with protein, fiber, and flavor, this recipe is perfect for busy days when you need a quick-fix dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 medium head of cabbage, chopped
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce or coconut aminos
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: 1 tbsp sesame seeds or chopped green onions for garnish

Directions

1.Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 2.Add the chicken pieces and cook until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes. Remove and set aside. 3.In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if necessary, and sauté the onions and garlic until fragrant, around 2 minutes. 4.Toss in the cabbage and cook until it starts to wilt, about 3-4 minutes. 5.Return the chicken to the skillet, add soy sauce or coconut aminos, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until the cabbage is tender and the chicken is well-coated with the sauce. If desired, garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions before serving.

2. Cauliflower Mac and Cheese

Who says you have to give up indulgent meals on a keto diet? This low-carb version of a classic dish will satisfy your mac and cheese cravings, without loading you up with carbs!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cauliflower florets, chopped
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup milk (whole or 2%)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: paprika for garnish

Directions

1.Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). 2.Place chopped cauliflower in a steamer basket over a pot with an inch of water. Cover and steam until tender, about 8-10 minutes. 3.In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add the heavy cream, milk, and shredded cheddar cheese. Stir until the cheese is melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 4.Place the steamed cauliflower in a baking dish and pour the cheese sauce over it. Mix well to combine. 5.Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the top is golden brown. Sprinkle with paprika for an additional touch of color if desired.

3. Zucchini Noodles with Avocado Pesto

Perfect for when you want something light, fresh, and flavorful, these zucchini noodles with avocado pesto will transport you straight to an Italian countryside eatery.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis, spiralized
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

1.Place the avocado, basil, Parmesan, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a food processor. Pulse until a smooth, creamy texture forms. 2.Toss the zucchini noodles in the avocado pesto and serve immediately.

4. Breakfast Egg Muffins

Fuel your day with these simple, portable, and delicious breakfast egg muffins. With only a handful of ingredients, you can make a batch and enjoy them throughout the week!

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or milk (whole or 2%)
  • 1/2 cup diced vegetables (bell pepper, onion, zucchini, spinach, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup cheese, shredded (cheddar, pepper jack, or mozzarella)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: chopped scallions for garnish

Directions

1.Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 12-cup muffin tin. 2.In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, heavy cream or milk, salt, and pepper. 3.Vegetables and cheese. Divide the mixture evenly among the muffin cups. 4.Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the muffins are set. 5.Let the muffins cool for a few minutes before removing them from the tin. Serve with garnished with chopped scallions.

5. Avocado Toast with Fried Egg

Bring some positive vibes to your morning routine with this low-carb version of the much-loved avocado toast with a fried egg. Fresh and satisfying, it’s a meal you won’t regret.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 slices of light bread (rye, sourdough, or any keto-compatible option)
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 2 eggs
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, lemon juice, or chopped cilantro for garnish

Directions

1.To assemble the avocado toast, mash the avocado with salt and pepper, to taste, on the slices of bread. 2.Add a fried egg on top of the avocado toast, and garnish with red pepper flakes, a squeeze of lemon juice, or chopped cilantro.

6. Tuna Lettuce Wraps

Need a fast and Easy lunch? These tuna lettuce wraps are your answer. Fresh, low-carb, and packed with protein – what more could you ask for?

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 oz) can of tuna packed in water
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lettuce leaves (romaine, Boston, or butter)
  • Optional: thinly sliced celery, capers, or red onion for extra crunch

Directions

1.In a small bowl, combine the tuna, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. 2.Place the lettuce leaves on a plate, spoon the tuna mixture on the center of each leaf, and wrap them up like you’re creating a little burrito. 3.Top with thinly sliced celery, capers, or red onion if you desire.

7. Keto Chicken Alfredo

Enjoy the heavenly combination of chicken and Alfredo sauce with this low-carb adaptation. Creamy and satisfying, you’ll hardly miss the pasta!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Chopped parsley or chives for garnish

Directions

1.Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 2.Season the chicken with salt and pepper, then cook for about 5-7 minutes per side or until cooked through. Set aside. 3.In the same skillet, add the heavy cream, Parmesan, garlic, salt, and pepper. Cook on low heat, stirring until the sauce is smooth and bubbly. 4.Slice the chicken and serve with the creamy Alfredo sauce. Garnish with chopped parsley or chives.

8. Cabbage and Egg Fried Rice

Loving fried rice without the carbs? This cabbage and egg fried rice will be your new go-to dish! Quick and tasty, you’ll want to make it over and over again.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of cabbage, cut into small pieces
  • Olive oil
  • 8 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Soy sauce or coconut aminos, to taste

Directions

1.In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and garlic. Cook until aromatic. 2.Add cabbage and cook until the leaves are slightly wilted. 3.Pour the beaten eggs over the cabbage and mix gently so that the egg scrambles and incorporates with the cabbage. 4.Add salt, pepper, and soy sauce or coconut aminos to taste. Mix well and serve immediately.

There you go! With these budget-friendly and scrumptious keto meals, you can enjoy awesome low-carb dishes without breaking the bank. Pick your favorite and give them a try. Remember, happy and healthy cooking is about enjoying meals without feeling guilty – and that’s something we all deserve. So, next time you’re shopping or meal prepping, consider these cost-effective and handy ingredients to make your keto journey even more exciting.
