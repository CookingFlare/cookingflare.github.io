Homemade Chicken Noodle Casserole: A Family Favorite


There’s something incredibly comforting about a warm and hearty casserole, especially when it’s a homemade chicken noodle casserole. This classic dish is not only delicious, but it’s also a favorite among families all around the world. With its creamy sauce, tender chicken, and perfectly cooked noodles, it’s no wonder why this recipe is a go-to for many home cooks. In this blog post, I’ll be sharing my own version of the homemade chicken noodle casserole recipe, along with some tips and tricks to make it even more flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 8 ounces egg noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots, thawed
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cooked egg noodles, thawed peas and carrots, diced onion, diced celery, and minced garlic. Mix well to combine.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the cream of chicken soup, sour cream, mayonnaise, chicken broth, dried thyme, dried parsley, salt, and pepper. Pour this mixture over the chicken and noodle mixture and stir until everything is well coated.

  4. Transfer the mixture to the greased baking dish and spread it out evenly. Top with shredded cheddar cheese.

  5. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Once the casserole is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and make it easier to handle.

  7. Serve the homemade chicken noodle casserole hot and enjoy the comforting flavors it brings to your table. It pairs perfectly with a side salad or some crusty bread.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add other vegetables to the casserole, such as mushrooms or bell peppers, to customize it to your liking.
  • If you prefer a crispy topping, sprinkle some breadcrumbs mixed with melted butter on top of the casserole before baking.
  • This casserole can be made ahead of time and refrigerated for up to 24 hours. Just make sure to bring it to room temperature before baking.
  • For a healthier version, you can use reduced-fat or Greek yogurt instead of sour cream and mayonnaise.

Conclusion

Homemade chicken noodle casserole is a classic dish that never fails to bring comfort and joy to the dinner table. With its creamy sauce, tender chicken, and perfectly cooked noodles, it’s a family favorite for a reason. Give this recipe a try and watch as your loved ones gather around for seconds. The best part? It’s easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste. So go ahead, whip up a batch of this delicious casserole and create memories that will last a lifetime.
