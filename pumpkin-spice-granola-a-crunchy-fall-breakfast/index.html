Pumpkin Spice Granola: A Crunchy Fall Breakfast


Fall is just around the corner, and what better way to welcome the season than with a delicious and nutritious breakfast option? Pumpkin Spice Granola is the perfect crunchy, flavorful treat to kick off your autumn mornings. Packed with the warm, comforting flavors of pumpkin spice, this granola is not only a delightful breakfast option but also a versatile snack throughout the day. Let’s dive into the recipe and explore the delightful world of Pumpkin Spice Granola!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raw pecans, chopped
  • 1 cup raw almonds, chopped
  • 1 cup raw pepitas (pumpkin seeds)
  • 1/2 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, chopped pecans, almonds, and pepitas. Mix well to evenly distribute the ingredients.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the maple syrup, melted coconut oil, pumpkin puree, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, ground ginger, ground nutmeg, ground cloves, and salt until well combined.

  4. Pour the wet mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until everything is well coated.

  5. Spread the granola mixture in an even layer on the prepared baking sheet.

  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, stirring once halfway through, until the granola is golden brown and crisp.

  7. Remove the granola from the oven and allow it to cool completely on the baking sheet. It will continue to crisp up as it cools.

  8. Once cooled, you can mix in the dried cranberries for an extra burst of sweetness, if desired.

  9. Store the Pumpkin Spice Granola in an airtight container at room temperature for up to two weeks. Enjoy it with milk or yogurt for breakfast, or simply grab a handful for a satisfying snack.

Why Pumpkin Spice Granola?

Pumpkin Spice Granola is more than just a delicious breakfast option; it’s also a wholesome and nutritious choice for starting your day. Rolled oats provide a good source of fiber, which can help keep you feeling full and satisfied until your next meal. The nuts and seeds in this granola offer healthy fats, protein, and essential nutrients, making it a well-rounded addition to your morning routine.

The warm spices like cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves not only create the signature pumpkin spice flavor but also bring their own health benefits. Cinnamon, for example, is known for its antioxidant properties and potential to help regulate blood sugar levels. Ginger is often praised for its anti-inflammatory and digestion-aiding properties. Nutmeg and cloves also contain various beneficial compounds that contribute to overall wellness.

Versatility and Adaptability

One of the best things about Pumpkin Spice Granola is its versatility. While it’s a delightful breakfast option with milk or yogurt, you can also get creative with how you enjoy it. Sprinkle it over a bowl of oatmeal for added crunch and flavor, or use it as a topping for your favorite fall-inspired desserts, such as pumpkin spice parfaits or apple crisps. You can even pack it as a convenient on-the-go snack for those busy autumn days.

Moreover, this recipe is highly adaptable. Feel free to customize it based on your preferences. If you’re not a fan of pecans or almonds, swap them out for your favorite nuts. You can also adjust the sweetness by adding more or less maple syrup, depending on your taste. The possibilities are endless, allowing you to tailor this granola to suit your individual preferences.

In Conclusion

Pumpkin Spice Granola is a delightful and nutritious way to embrace the flavors of fall. Whether you’re a fan of pumpkin spice or simply looking for a wholesome breakfast option, this crunchy granola is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. With its warm spices, hearty oats, and wholesome nuts and seeds, it’s a perfect way to start your day on a comforting note. So, as the leaves begin to change and the air becomes crisper, treat yourself to a delicious bowl of Pumpkin Spice Granola and savor the flavors of the season.
