Quick and Easy Gluten-Free Chicken Stir Fry Recipe


Are you in the mood for a delicious and healthy meal that won’t take hours to prepare? Look no further than this Quick and Easy Gluten-Free Chicken Stir Fry Recipe. Packed with flavor and ready in a flash, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want something satisfying without the fuss.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons gluten-free soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons gluten-free oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 orange bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped green onions, for garnish
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken with the gluten-free soy sauce and oyster sauce. Toss to coat the chicken evenly and set aside to marinate for at least 10 minutes.

  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sesame oil and vegetable oil, and swirl to coat the pan.

  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan, and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and stir-fry for 5-6 minutes until cooked through and slightly browned.

  5. Push the chicken to one side of the pan and add the sliced bell peppers, onion, broccoli florets, and snap peas. Stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  6. Season the stir fry with salt and pepper to taste. Give it a good toss to combine all the flavors.

  7. Remove the pan from the heat and garnish with chopped green onions.

  8. Serve the gluten-free chicken stir fry over cooked rice or noodles for a complete meal.

Why Choose Gluten-Free?

If you’re following a gluten-free diet or have dietary restrictions, this recipe is a perfect choice. By using gluten-free soy sauce and oyster sauce, you can enjoy a delicious stir fry without worrying about gluten sneaking into your meal. Plus, the vibrant mix of colorful vegetables adds not only flavor but also essential nutrients to your plate.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to your taste. You can add more vegetables like mushrooms, carrots, or zucchini for extra color and texture.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, add some red pepper flakes or sriracha sauce to the stir fry.
  • For a vegetarian version, swap the chicken with tofu or tempeh and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.

In Conclusion

With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, this Quick and Easy Gluten-Free Chicken Stir Fry Recipe is a winner for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or feeding a crowd, it’s a versatile dish that satisfies both your taste buds and your desire for a healthy meal. So why wait? Get your wok ready and start making this delicious stir fry tonight!
