Savory Garlic Shrimp and Broccoli Stir Fry: A Healthy Option


If you’re looking for a quick and healthy meal that’s packed with flavor, then this savory garlic shrimp and broccoli stir fry recipe is just what you need. This dish is not only delicious but also a great option for those looking to incorporate more seafood and vegetables into their diet. The combination of succulent shrimp, crisp broccoli, and aromatic garlic makes for a satisfying and nourishing meal that can be prepared in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, sugar, and red pepper flakes. Set the sauce aside.

  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the broccoli florets and sauté for 3-4 minutes until they are bright green and slightly tender. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant.

  4. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and pour the prepared sauce over the shrimp and broccoli. Stir the mixture well to ensure that the shrimp and broccoli are coated evenly with the sauce. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the sauce has thickened and the shrimp and broccoli are heated through.

  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and serve the savory garlic shrimp and broccoli stir fry over cooked rice or noodles.

This savory garlic shrimp and broccoli stir fry is a fantastic option for a quick and healthy meal. The combination of tender shrimp, crisp broccoli, and aromatic garlic creates a dish that is not only delicious but also nutritious. Additionally, the ease and speed of preparation make it a perfect choice for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Whether you’re a seafood lover or just looking to add more variety to your meals, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

So, the next time you’re craving something flavorful and wholesome, give this savory garlic shrimp and broccoli stir fry a try. It’s a simple yet satisfying dish that will leave you feeling nourished and satisfied, without spending hours in the kitchen. Enjoy the delightful flavors and the health benefits of this delicious stir fry!
