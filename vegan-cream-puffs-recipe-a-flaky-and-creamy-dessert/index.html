Vegan Cream Puffs Recipe: A Flaky and Creamy Dessert


Who says vegans can’t enjoy delicious and indulgent desserts? In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make vegan cream puffs that are just as flaky and creamy as their non-vegan counterparts. These delightful treats are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a special celebration or a simple afternoon snack. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the choux pastry:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter
  • 4 tablespoons aquafaba (chickpea brine)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the vegan cream filling:

  • 1 can coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Choux Pastry

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the water, vegan butter, and salt. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally until the butter has melted completely.

  2. Reduce the heat to low and add the flour all at once. Stir vigorously with a wooden spoon until the mixture forms a ball and pulls away from the sides of the saucepan. This process should take about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the mixture cool for a few minutes. Transfer the dough to a mixing bowl.

  4. Add the aquafaba to the bowl and beat the mixture using an electric mixer on low speed until well combined and smooth.

Step 2: Baking the Pastry

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Using a piping bag fitted with a round tip, pipe small mounds of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Alternatively, you can use a spoon to drop dollops of dough onto the baking sheet.

  3. Bake the cream puffs in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until they are puffed up and golden brown. Avoid opening the oven door during the baking process, as it may cause the cream puffs to deflate.

  4. Once baked, remove the cream puffs from the oven and let them cool completely on a wire rack.

Step 3: Making the Vegan Cream Filling

  1. Open the can of refrigerated coconut milk and carefully scoop out the solidified coconut cream, leaving behind the coconut water. Transfer the cream to a mixing bowl.

  2. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the bowl. Using an electric mixer on medium-high speed, beat the mixture until light and fluffy, about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Once the cream puffs have cooled, use a sharp knife or a pastry bag fitted with a small tip to make a small hole in the bottom of each puff.

  4. Fill the cream puffs with the vegan cream filling by either piping it into the holes or spooning it in.

Step 4: Serving and Enjoying

  1. Serve the vegan cream puffs immediately or refrigerate them for a couple of hours to allow the filling to set.

  2. Dust the tops of the cream puffs with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness, if desired.

  3. Enjoy these flaky and creamy vegan cream puffs as a delightful dessert or a special treat with your loved ones!

Conclusion

With this vegan cream puffs recipe, you can satisfy your sweet tooth while staying true to your vegan lifestyle. These flaky and creamy delights are a testament to the fact that vegan desserts can be just as indulgent and delicious as their non-vegan counterparts. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try – you won’t be disappointed!
