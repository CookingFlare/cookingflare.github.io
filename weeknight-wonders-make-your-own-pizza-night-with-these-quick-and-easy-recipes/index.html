Weeknight Wonders: Make Your Own Pizza Night with These Quick and Easy Recipes


Ah, pizza night – an ever-exciting event that brings family and friends together over warm slices and cheerful laughter. The magic of pizza never fails to delight our taste buds, whether with familiar favorites or daring combinations of flavors. But who says you can only enjoy this culinary delight on weekends? Revamp your weeknight dinners with these quick and easy pizza recipes that combine convenience with mouthwatering satisfaction. Turn common ingredients into gourmet delights straight from your kitchen oven!

1. 15-Minute Skillet Pizza

When you’re pressed for time but craving a slice of heaven, look no further than the skillet pizza. This flavorful, under 30-minute recipe delivers the perfect blend of crust, sauce, and toppings to quench any craving.

Ingredients:

  • 1 10-inch cast-iron skillet
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice (recommendations: sliced chicken, bell peppers, mushrooms, and olives)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and dried basil in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add warm water and olive oil to the dry mix while gradually stirring in a circular motion until the dough forms.
  4. Transfer the dough onto your greased cast-iron skillet, ensuring it evenly covers the entire surface.
  5. Spread the marinara sauce over the dough, leaving space for the crust.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese, followed by your toppings.
  7. Cook the skillet pizza for about 15-20 minutes, or until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese melts beautifully.
  8. Remove from oven, let it cool for a couple of minutes, then slice and serve.

2. No-Knead Pizza Dough

This no-knead pizza dough guarantees convenience and consistency every time. Just mix, wait, and bake for fresh homemade pizza every weeknight!

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour, salt, instant yeast, and sugar in a large bowl.
  2. Gradually pour warm water and olive oil into the dry mixture while stirring.
  3. Combine until the dough forms, making sure to scrape any remaining dry ingredients from the sides of the bowl.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth or plastic wrap and set in a warm, draft-free area for about 1-2 hours, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Punch down the dough a few times, then transfer it onto a floured surface and shape it into a ball.
  6. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  7. Roll out the dough thinly for flatbreads, or curl and shape into a thicker disk for more traditional pizza crusts.
  8. Top as desired, then bake for 12-16 minutes, depending on the thickness of your crust.

3. Speedy Veggie Pizza

Those with more adventurous taste buds will surely appreciate this speedy veggie-packed pizza that’ll keep you satisfied without breaking the weeknight dinner pace.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made refrigerated or frozen pizza crust (2 cups of homemade pizza dough can also work)
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as red onions, spinach, cherry tomatoes, mushrooms, and black olives)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Follow the package instructions for warming the pizza crust or bake homemade dough at 450°F (230°C) for about 6 minutes.
  2. Spread the marinara sauce evenly, sprinkle the shredded mozzarella over the sauce, and add the mixed vegetables.
  3. Sprinkle feta cheese and olive oil on top.
  4. Bake for another 8-10 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and the cheese starts to turn golden.
  5. Slice and serve immediately.

There you have it: six weeknight wonders that are guaranteed to bring joy and zest to your Made at Home pizzas! With less fuss and more flavor, you’ll wonder why you didn’t try these sooner. Try them out and witness the magic that transforms your weeknights into feel-good, family-friendly, delectable pizza nights.
