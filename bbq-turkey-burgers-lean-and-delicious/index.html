BBQ Turkey Burgers: Lean and Delicious


Summer barbecues are all aboutunpacking your inner-chef and bringing a collection of tantalizing flavors together, out in the open air. This year, impress your guests by adding a juicy, mouth-watering BBQ turkey burger to your menu. Turkey burgers are as versatile as they are delicious, packed with lean protein that’s perfect for a healthy summer meal. As the sizzling smoke curls upward, gather your ingredients and let’s flip up our stock of healthy and flavor-filled BBQ turkey burgers that you won’t soon forget!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. ground turkey (preferably lean)
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup finely diced green bell pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • BBQ sauce and toppings of your choice, for serving

Instructions:

Prepare the fire for a medium-hot barbecue heat. If you’re opting for a gas grill, adjust the temperature accordingly.

  1. First, handle the ground turkey and season it with a bit of salt and black pepper. See, it’s all about balance here - that gentle seasoning will keep your BBQ turkey burgers lean and let those other flavors shine through.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the seasoned ground turkey with chopped red onion, finely diced green bell pepper, minced garlic, and finely chopped fresh cilantro. You’ll find this mix perfect for a light hand as it will just infuse the turkey with an interesting flavor profile.

  3. Next, gently mix in the barbecue sauce and bread crumbs. Fold in the egg, breaking it up evenly throughout.

  4. Since turkey burgers are notorious for being tender and slightly fragile, make a slight indentation in the center of each formed patty to prevent curling up while it grills.

  5. Grill your patties for roughly five minutes on each side or until the meat’s cooked to an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C). This signals that the turkey burgers are cooked through and safe to enjoy.

  6. When your turkey burgers are cooked, transfer them off the grill and allow them to rest for a couple of minutes. This step is important, as it lets the juices redistribute throughout the burger.

  7. Finish your BBQ turkey burgers to your preference. Add more barbecue sauce on top, let others spruce their burgers with a little heat from jalapeno slices, or maybe even a slice of melted cheese. There’s no right or wrong way to top your turkey burger, so cater to your crowd’s tastes.

Final Thoughts:

It’s no secret how these BBQ turkey burgers deliver a rich, smoky, and savory experience that never disappoints. Turkey’s lean profile makes this burger a perfect addition to any summer barbecue. Keep experimenting with flavors and toppings to find your own preferred variation of this delightful and healthy burger alternative. So, light up your grill, gather your loved ones, and make this summer a memorable one with these scrumptious BBQ turkey burgers.
