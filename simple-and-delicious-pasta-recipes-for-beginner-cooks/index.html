Simple and Delicious Pasta Recipes for Beginner Cooks


Are you a beginner cook looking to impress your friends and family with a delicious and satisfying meal? Look no further than these simple and delicious pasta recipes. Pasta is a versatile ingredient that can be transformed into a variety of mouthwatering dishes. Whether you prefer a creamy sauce or a light and refreshing pasta salad, there’s a recipe here for everyone. So put on your apron and let’s get cooking!

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream to the skillet. Stir gently until the cream starts to bubble.
  4. Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet, stirring constantly until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet and toss until the pasta is evenly coated with the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce.
  7. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Caprese Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of bowtie pasta
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup of fresh mozzarella balls, halved
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the bowtie pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water to cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella balls, and torn basil leaves.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Refrigerate the pasta salad for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve the pasta salad chilled as a refreshing side dish or a light lunch.

One-Pot Tomato Basil Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of spaghetti
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes (including the juice) and vegetable broth to the pot. Stir to combine.
  3. Break the spaghetti in half and add it to the pot, ensuring that the pasta is fully submerged in the liquid.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Simmer for about 10-12 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked to your desired level of tenderness, stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the torn basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the tomato basil pasta hot, garnished with additional torn basil leaves if desired.

These simple and delicious pasta recipes are perfect for beginner cooks who want to create impressive meals without spending hours in the kitchen. From the creamy garlic Parmesan pasta to the refreshing caprese pasta salad, there’s something here for every pasta lover. So grab your chef’s hat and start cooking up a storm!
