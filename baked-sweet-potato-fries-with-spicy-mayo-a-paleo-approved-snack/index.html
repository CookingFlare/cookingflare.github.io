Baked Sweet Potato Fries with Spicy Mayo: A Paleo-Approved Snack


Sweet potato fries have become a popular snack in recent years, and for good reason. They’re a delicious and healthier alternative to regular potato fries, with a sweet and nutty flavor that’s hard to resist. But what if you could take your sweet potato fries to the next level by adding a spicy kick? That’s exactly what we’re going to do in this recipe, by pairing our baked sweet potato fries with a creamy and spicy mayo.

Why Sweet Potato Fries?

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s talk about why sweet potato fries are a great choice. For one, sweet potatoes are a much healthier option than regular potatoes. They’re higher in fiber, vitamins, and minerals, and lower in calories and fat. They’re also a great source of antioxidants, which can help protect against chronic diseases like heart disease and cancer.

Another benefit of sweet potato fries is that they’re incredibly versatile. You can season them with a wide range of herbs and spices, from classic salt and pepper to more adventurous options like curry powder or smoked paprika. And with this recipe, we’re going to add a spicy kick to our fries with a creamy and tangy mayo.

The Recipe

To make our baked sweet potato fries, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2-3 large sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Chopped fresh cilantro or scallions for garnish (optional)

First, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). While the oven is heating up, slice the sweet potatoes into long, thin strips. Place the strips in a bowl and drizzle with coconut oil, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Toss the fries to coat them evenly with the seasonings and oil.

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and arrange the sweet potato fries in a single layer. Bake the fries for 20-25 minutes, or until they’re crispy and golden brown. Flip the fries halfway through the cooking time to ensure even cooking.

While the fries are baking, prepare the spicy mayo. In a bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, sriracha sauce, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and garlic powder until smooth and creamy.

Once the fries are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Then, toss them with the spicy mayo until they’re evenly coated.

Tips and Variations

Here are a few tips and variations to keep in mind when making this recipe:

  • To make the fries extra crispy, try baking them for an additional 5-10 minutes. Just keep an eye on them to make sure they don’t burn.
  • If you prefer a milder mayonnaise, you can reduce the amount of sriracha sauce or omit it altogether.
  • You can also add other seasonings to the fries, such as dried herbs or grated cheese.
  • To make the fries more substantial, you can serve them with a side of grilled chicken or salmon.

Conclusion

Baked sweet potato fries with spicy mayo are a delicious and addictive snack that’s perfect for game day, movie night, or any other occasion when you need a tasty and satisfying treat. With their sweet and nutty flavor, crispy texture, and spicy kick, these fries are sure to become a new favorite in your household. So go ahead, give them a try, and enjoy the perfect combination of sweet and heat!
