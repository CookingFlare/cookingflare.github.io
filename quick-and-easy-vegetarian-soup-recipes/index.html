Quick and Easy Vegetarian Soup Recipes


Looking for some delicious and nutritious vegetarian soup recipes that are quick and easy to prepare? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share with you three mouthwatering soup recipes that are perfect for those busy weeknights or lazy weekends. These recipes are not only packed with flavor but also loaded with vitamins and minerals, making them a healthy choice for you and your family. So, let’s dive in and get cooking!

1. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream (or coconut cream for a vegan option)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes.
  3. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream (or coconut cream). Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Simmer the soup for an additional 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. Enjoy!

2. Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the dried lentils, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30 minutes or until the lentils are cooked through.
  3. Adjust the seasonings according to your taste preferences.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy this hearty and nutritious lentil and vegetable soup!

3. Coconut Curry Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the coconut oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the cubed butternut squash, coconut milk, vegetable broth, curry powder, turmeric powder, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes or until the squash is tender.
  3. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth and creamy.
  4. Adjust the seasonings according to your taste preferences.
  5. Serve hot and savor the wonderful flavors of this coconut curry butternut squash soup!

These three quick and easy vegetarian soup recipes are perfect for any time of the year. Whether you’re looking for a light and refreshing option or a hearty and comforting meal, these soups have got you covered. So, grab your ingredients, get cooking, and enjoy a warm and satisfying bowl of vegetarian goodness. Happy cooking!
