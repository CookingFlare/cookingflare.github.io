Vegan Apple Cinnamon Muffins Recipe with a Crunchy Oat Topping


Welcome to your new favorite baking adventure! Today, we’re diving into a delightful recipe for Vegan Apple Cinnamon Muffins topped with a crunchy oat topping that will make your taste buds dance. These muffins are not only delicious but also packed with wholesome ingredients that are perfect for breakfast, snacks, or even dessert. Let’s get baking!

Why Choose Vegan Apple Cinnamon Muffins?

Baking vegan muffins is a fantastic way to enjoy a treat that’s kinder to animals and often lighter on the planet. These muffins are made without any animal products, using plant-based ingredients that are both nutritious and satisfying. The combination of sweet apples and warm cinnamon creates a comforting flavor profile, while the oat topping adds a satisfying crunch.

Health Benefits of the Ingredients

  • Apples: Rich in fiber and vitamin C, apples contribute to heart health and can aid in digestion. Their natural sweetness reduces the need for added sugars.
  • Cinnamon: Known for its anti-inflammatory properties, cinnamon can help regulate blood sugar levels and adds a warm, comforting flavor.
  • Oats: A great source of whole grains, oats provide essential nutrients and can help lower cholesterol levels, making them a perfect addition to the topping.
  • Plant-Based Milk: Using almond, soy, or oat milk makes these muffins dairy-free while still keeping them moist and delicious.

Ingredients

For the Muffins

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup (or agave syrup)
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup diced apples (about 2 medium apples)

For the Crunchy Oat Topping

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar (or coconut sugar)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil (melted) or vegan butter
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat Your Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease it lightly with coconut oil. This will ensure your muffins come out easily and have a lovely texture.

Step 2: Prepare the Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, rolled oats, baking powder, baking soda, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Ensure that all the dry ingredients are well combined, as this will help your muffins rise evenly.

Step 3: Mix the Wet Ingredients

In another bowl, combine the unsweetened applesauce, maple syrup, almond milk, and vanilla extract. Whisk these ingredients until they are smooth and well-blended. The applesauce acts as a binding agent and adds moisture to the muffins.

Step 4: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to dense muffins. Gently fold in the diced apples, ensuring they’re evenly distributed throughout the batter.

Step 5: Prepare the Crunchy Oat Topping

In a small bowl, combine the rolled oats, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, melted coconut oil, and a pinch of salt. Mix until the oats are well-coated and crumbly. This topping will create a deliciously crunchy texture on top of your muffins.

Step 6: Fill the Muffin Tin

Spoon the muffin batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about two-thirds full. This allows enough room for the muffins to rise without overflowing. Once filled, sprinkle the crunchy oat topping generously over each muffin.

Step 7: Bake to Perfection

Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 18 to 22 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The aroma of cinnamon and apples will fill your kitchen, making it hard to wait!

Step 8: Cool and Enjoy

Once baked, remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool in the tin for about 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy these muffins warm or at room temperature. They are perfect for breakfast, an afternoon snack, or even a late-night treat!

Storage Tips

These Vegan Apple Cinnamon Muffins can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer freshness, you can refrigerate them for up to a week. If you want to keep them for an extended period, consider freezing them. Just wrap each muffin individually in plastic wrap and place them in a freezer-safe bag. They can be thawed overnight in the fridge or warmed in the microwave for a quick snack.

Variations to Try

One of the best things about this recipe is its versatility. Here are a few variations you might consider trying:

  • Add Nuts: For extra crunch and protein, add chopped walnuts or pecans to the batter.
  • Dried Fruits: Incorporate raisins or dried cranberries for a touch of sweetness.
  • Spices: Experiment with other spices like ginger or allspice for a unique flavor twist.
  • Chocolate Chips: For a decadent treat, fold in some dairy-free chocolate chips.

Conclusion

There you have it! A delicious recipe for Vegan Apple Cinnamon Muffins with a crunchy oat topping that is sure to become a staple in your baking repertoire. These muffins are not only easy to make but also healthy and satisfying. Share them with friends and family, or keep them all to yourself—no judgment here!

Get creative in the kitchen, and enjoy the wonderful flavors of these vegan muffins. Happy baking!
