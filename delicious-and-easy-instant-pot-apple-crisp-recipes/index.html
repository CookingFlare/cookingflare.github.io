Delicious and Easy Instant Pot Apple Crisp Recipes


If you’re a fan of warm, comforting desserts, then you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m going to share with you two delicious and easy Instant Pot apple crisp recipes that will satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you wanting more. The best part? These recipes can be made in a fraction of the time it would take to bake them in the oven. So, grab your Instant Pot and let’s get cooking!

Recipe 1: Classic Instant Pot Apple Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of peeled, cored, and sliced apples
  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the rolled oats, all-purpose flour, brown sugar, melted butter, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Mix well until the ingredients are evenly combined.

  2. Place the sliced apples in the Instant Pot and sprinkle the oat mixture on top of the apples.

  3. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 8 minutes on high pressure.

  4. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.

  5. Carefully remove the lid and give the apple crisp a gentle stir. The apples should be tender, and the oat topping should be golden and crispy.

  6. Serve the apple crisp warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence.

Recipe 2: Caramel Pecan Instant Pot Apple Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of peeled, cored, and sliced apples
  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup of caramel sauce
  • 1/2 cup of chopped pecans

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as Recipe 1 to prepare the apple crisp base.

  2. After sprinkling the oat mixture on top of the apples, drizzle the caramel sauce over the oat topping, and sprinkle the chopped pecans on top.

  3. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 8 minutes on high pressure.

  4. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.

  5. Carefully remove the lid and give the apple crisp a gentle stir. The apples should be tender, and the oat topping should be golden and crispy, with a delicious caramel and pecan flavor.

  6. Serve the caramel pecan apple crisp warm, and for an extra touch, top it with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Both of these Instant Pot apple crisp recipes are quick, easy, and incredibly delicious. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can enjoy a warm and comforting dessert in no time. So, go ahead and give these recipes a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember to experiment with different types of apples and toppings to personalize your apple crisp. Whether you prefer a classic version or a caramel pecan twist, these recipes are sure to become a family favorite. Enjoy!
