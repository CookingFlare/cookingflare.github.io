Creamy Roasted Red Pepper Pasta: A Vibrant and Flavorful Dish


Are you tired of the same old pasta recipes? Looking for something vibrant and flavorful to spice up your dinner routine? Look no further than this delightful Creamy Roasted Red Pepper Pasta recipe. It’s a dish that combines the rich and tangy flavors of roasted red peppers with the creamy goodness of pasta. With its bold taste and beautiful color, it’s sure to impress both your taste buds and your dinner guests.

Ingredients

  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 pound pasta of your choice (linguine or fettuccine work well)
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. Begin by roasting the red bell peppers. Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and drizzle with olive oil. Roast in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the skin is charred and blistered.

  3. Remove the peppers from the oven and transfer them to a bowl. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let them steam for about 10 minutes. This will make it easier to peel off the skin.

  4. Once the peppers have cooled slightly, remove the skin, seeds, and stems. Cut the roasted peppers into small pieces and set aside.

  5. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.

  6. Add the roasted red peppers to the skillet and stir to combine with the onion and garlic. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Transfer the mixture to a blender or food processor and blend until smooth. If you prefer a chunkier sauce, you can pulse the mixture a few times instead of blending it completely.

  8. Return the blended red pepper mixture to the skillet and add the vegetable broth and heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer the sauce over low heat for about 10 minutes, or until it has thickened slightly.

  9. While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the skillet with the red pepper sauce. Toss the pasta in the sauce until it is well coated.

  10. Serve the creamy roasted red pepper pasta hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves for an extra touch of freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy twist, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce while it’s simmering.
  • Want to make it more substantial? Add sautéed mushrooms, grilled chicken, or shrimp to the dish.
  • If you’re looking for a vegan option, you can substitute the heavy cream with coconut milk or cashew cream for a creamy, plant-based pasta dish.

So there you have it, a vibrant and flavorful Creamy Roasted Red Pepper Pasta recipe that will surely become a favorite in your household. Its creamy texture, bold flavors, and beautiful color make it an impressive dish for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to something special, this recipe is a winner. Give it a try and let your taste buds rejoice!
