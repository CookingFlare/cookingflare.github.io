Wholesome Nut-Free Lentil Salad Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious, nutritious, and allergy-friendly dish, this Wholesome Nut-Free Lentil Salad Recipe is the perfect choice. Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of flavors, this salad is not only satisfying but also incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the lentils, water, and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the lentils are tender but still hold their shape. Drain any excess water and let the lentils cool.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked lentils, red onion, red bell pepper, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, parsley, and mint.

  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.

  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can add more salt, pepper, or a splash of balsamic vinegar according to your preference.

  6. Once the salad is well mixed and seasoned to your liking, cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve chilled and enjoy the wholesome goodness of this nut-free lentil salad!

Nutritional Benefits

This Wholesome Nut-Free Lentil Salad is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a powerhouse of nutrients. Lentils are rich in protein, fiber, and various essential vitamins and minerals. They are also low in fat and high in folate, iron, and potassium, making them a fantastic addition to a balanced diet. The fresh vegetables in the salad provide an array of vitamins and antioxidants, while the olive oil in the dressing offers heart-healthy monounsaturated fats.

Serving Suggestions

This versatile salad can be enjoyed on its own as a light lunch or dinner, or served as a side dish alongside grilled chicken, fish, or tofu. It’s also a great option for potlucks, picnics, and barbecues, as it can be made ahead of time and travels well.

Conclusion

With its simple preparation, vibrant colors, and robust flavors, this Wholesome Nut-Free Lentil Salad Recipe is a must-try for anyone seeking a nutritious and satisfying meal. Whether you’re catering to specific dietary needs or simply want to add more plant-based goodness to your diet, this salad is a winner. Give it a try and savor the wholesome goodness in every bite!
