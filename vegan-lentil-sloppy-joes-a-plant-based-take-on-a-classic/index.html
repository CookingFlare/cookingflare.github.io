Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes: A Plant-Based Take on a Classic


If you’re looking for a hearty, satisfying, and entirely plant-based meal that will please even the most devoted meat-eaters, then look no further than these Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes. This recipe puts a delicious and nutritious spin on the classic sloppy joe, using wholesome ingredients and bold flavors to create a dish that’s perfect for weeknight dinners, potlucks, or any occasion that calls for good food and good company.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of dried green or brown lentils
  • 3 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 hamburger buns

Instructions

Cooking the Lentils

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and drain them.
  2. In a medium saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil.
  3. Add the lentils to the saucepan, reduce the heat, and let them simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender but still hold their shape.
  4. Once the lentils are cooked, drain any excess liquid and set them aside.

Making the Sloppy Joe Filling

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the diced bell pepper and grated carrot to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, maple syrup, apple cider vinegar, soy sauce, smoked paprika, cumin, and chili powder. Stir to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Add the cooked lentils to the skillet and stir until everything is well combined.
  7. Let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Assembling the Sloppy Joes

  1. Toast the hamburger buns until they are golden brown and slightly crispy.
  2. Spoon a generous amount of the lentil sloppy joe filling onto the bottom half of each bun.
  3. Place the top half of the bun over the filling, and serve the sloppy joes with your favorite side dish or a fresh salad.

Why Choose Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes

These Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes are not only delicious but also packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. Lentils are an excellent source of plant-based protein, making them a great alternative to ground meat in traditional sloppy joe recipes. Additionally, this dish is loaded with vegetables, providing a wide array of vitamins and minerals to support a healthy lifestyle.

By opting for this plant-based version, you’re not only treating your taste buds to a burst of flavor but also contributing to environmental sustainability. Embracing more plant-based meals in your diet can have a positive impact on the planet, making Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes a win-win choice for both your health and the environment.

In Conclusion

Whether you’re a committed vegan, a flexitarian, or someone who simply enjoys exploring new culinary horizons, these Vegan Lentil Sloppy Joes are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. They offer a delightful combination of textures and tastes, all while being a wholesome and compassionate choice for you and the planet. Give this recipe a try and savor the goodness of a plant-based take on a classic comfort food.
