Decadent Vegan Chocolate Cake Recipe: Indulge in Guilt-Free Sweetness

If you’re craving a sinfully delicious chocolate cake but want to stick to your vegan lifestyle, you’ve come to the right place. This Decadent Vegan Chocolate Cake is the epitome of indulgence without the guilt. Moist, rich, and oh-so-chocolatey, this cake will satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving for more. Ingredients 2 cups all-purpose flour 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder 2 tsp baking powder 1 1/2 tsp baking soda 1 tsp salt 1 cup unsweetened almond milk 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce 1/2 cup vegetable oil 2 tsp pure vanilla extract 1 cup hot water Vegan chocolate chips (optional, for extra indulgence) Instructions Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking pan. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined. In a separate bowl, mix the almond milk, applesauce, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix. Pour in the hot water and continue to stir until the batter is smooth. If you’re feeling extra indulgent, fold in a handful of vegan chocolate chips at this stage. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
