Sous Vide Lemon Garlic Shrimp: A Tangy and Tasty Dish


If you’re a seafood enthusiast looking for a mouthwatering dish that bursts with flavor, then you’re in for a treat. This sous vide lemon garlic shrimp recipe is a perfect blend of tangy and savory, creating a delightful medley of tastes in every bite. The sous vide cooking method ensures that the shrimp are perfectly tender and infused with the zesty flavors of lemon and garlic. So, let’s dive into the culinary adventure and explore how to create this delectable dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

To begin this gastronomic journey, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Sous Vide Bath

  1. Fill a large pot or container with water and attach the sous vide precision cooker. Set the temperature to 135°F (57°C) for perfectly tender shrimp.

Step 2: Seasoning the Shrimp

  1. In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated with the flavorful marinade.

Step 3: Vacuum Sealing

  1. Transfer the seasoned shrimp and marinade into a vacuum-sealable bag, ensuring the shrimp are in a single layer for even cooking.

Step 4: Sous Vide Cooking

  1. Seal the bag using a vacuum sealer to remove the air and create a tight seal.
  2. Gently submerge the bag into the preheated water bath, ensuring the shrimp are fully immersed.
  3. Cook the shrimp for 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse and the shrimp to reach the perfect texture.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

  1. Once the shrimp are cooked, carefully remove the bag from the water bath and transfer the shrimp to a serving dish.
  2. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a vibrant pop of color and an added layer of flavor.

Serving Suggestions

This sous vide lemon garlic shrimp pairs wonderfully with a variety of accompaniments. Consider serving it over a bed of fluffy couscous, alongside a crisp garden salad, or with a side of buttery garlic bread to soak up the delectable juices. The versatility of this dish allows you to explore different serving options to suit your personal preferences.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this sous vide lemon garlic shrimp recipe is a delightful fusion of tangy citrus and robust garlic, creating a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. The precision of the sous vide cooking method ensures that the shrimp are tender and infused with the aromatic essence of lemon and garlic. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a restaurant-quality meal at home, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure that promises to deliver a burst of tangy and tasty delight with every bite. Enjoy!

With this sous vide lemon garlic shrimp recipe in your culinary repertoire, you’ll have a show-stopping dish that elevates any dining experience. The marriage of tangy citrus, savory garlic, and perfectly cooked shrimp is a testament to the exquisite flavors that can be achieved through the art of sous vide cooking. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your precision cooker, and prepare to indulge in a culinary masterpiece that is as delightful to prepare as it is to savor. Cheers to creating unforgettable dining experiences right in the heart of your own kitchen!
