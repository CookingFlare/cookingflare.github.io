Crispy Paleo Chicken Tenders with Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce: A Kid-Friendly Recipe


Are you tired of the same old boring chicken tenders? Do you want to serve your family a delicious and healthy meal that everyone will love? Look no further! Today, we’re going to share with you a recipe for crispy paleo chicken tenders with honey mustard dipping sauce that’s perfect for kids and adults alike.

The Magic of Paleo

Paleo, short for paleolithic, is a diet that focuses on whole, unprocessed foods that our ancestors would have eaten during the Paleolithic era. The idea is to eliminate grains, dairy, and processed foods from your diet and focus on lean proteins, fruits, vegetables, and healthy fats. By adopting a paleo lifestyle, you can improve your overall health, increase your energy levels, and even lose weight.

The Recipe

To make these crispy paleo chicken tenders, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • Honey mustard dipping sauce (recipe below)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a shallow dish, mix together the coconut flour, almond flour, unsweetened shredded coconut, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. In a separate dish, whisk together the eggs.
  4. Dip each chicken breast into the eggs, coating completely, then roll in the coconut flour mixture to coat. Place the coated chicken on a plate or tray.
  5. Drizzle the coconut oil over the chicken tenders, making sure they’re evenly coated.
  6. Bake the chicken tenders in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through and crispy.
  7. While the chicken tenders are baking, prepare the honey mustard dipping sauce (recipe below).
  8. Serve the crispy paleo chicken tenders with the honey mustard dipping sauce and enjoy!

Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce

To make the honey mustard dipping sauce, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, honey, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, salt, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Refrigerate the sauce for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  3. Serve the honey mustard dipping sauce chilled alongside the crispy paleo chicken tenders.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the chicken tenders extra crispy, try baking them for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  • If you prefer a spicier dipping sauce, add a dash of hot sauce, such as sriracha or hot sauce of your choice.
  • For a sweet and tangy dipping sauce, try substituting the honey with maple syrup and the Dijon mustard with whole-grain mustard.
  • To make the recipe more substantial, serve the crispy paleo chicken tenders with roasted vegetables, such as broccoli or sweet potatoes.

Conclusion

We hope you enjoy this recipe for crispy paleo chicken tenders with honey mustard dipping sauce! It’s a kid-friendly recipe that’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions. By adopting a paleo lifestyle and incorporating healthy fats, lean proteins, and whole grains into your diet, you can improve your overall health and well-being. Happy cooking!
