Savory Herb and Cheese Pull-Apart Bread


Are you tired of the routine sandwich or slice of pizza? Introducing a uniquely scrumptious treat to spice up your palate – Savory Herb and Cheese Pull-Apart Bread. This delectable bread is perfect for snacking, lunch, or dinner. Not only will it tantalize your taste buds, but it will also leave you feeling satisfied and vibrant!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup rosemary, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup salted or unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

Firstly, measure and combine whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, yeast, sugar, salt, and olive oil in a mixing bowl. Add warm water, ensuring the consistency is slightly sticky, but not overly wet. Cover the dough and allow it to sit in a warm place for approximately 45 minutes.

While the dough rises, gather your chopped herbs - parsley, basil, and rosemary. It’s essential to create a delicious mix; prepare your herbs in advance. In a separate bowl, mix together the shredded mozzarella cheese and grated parmesan cheese.

Once the dough is adequately risen, turn it out onto a floured surface. Roll or pat the dough into a 10-foot rope shape. Sprinkle half the cheese and herb mixture down the center lengthwise. Fold both ends of the rope to meet in the center, creating a usual bread texture.

Divide the combined dough rope into 10-12 equal pieces. Place these pieces into a greased or parchment-lined 9x13-inch baking dish. They will expand slightly, filling up the tray, so give them enough space.

Brush the tops of your pieces with melted butter and sprinkle the remaining cheese and herb mixture over the top. Cover the dish with aluminium foil and leave it in a warm place to rise for an additional 30 minutes.

After the rising period, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Remove the aluminum foil and bake the bread in the oven for about 25 minutes. Until golden brown.

As soon as the baking process is done, allow your bread to cool slightly prior to serving. The tempting flavours of the herbs, combined with the gooey cheese pulling away from the crust, make this pull-apart bread an addictive and delightful experience.

Conclusion

What a delightful journey making and consuming this amazing Savory Herb and Cheese Pull-Apart Bread has been! Not only is this bread delicious, but it also has a healthier twist on the classic comfort foods we all know and love.

So why not try out this mouthwatering recipe the next time you are in the mood for something beyond a sandwich or slice of pizza. Whether it’s for a quick snack or an end-of-the-week feast, the Savory Herb and Cheese Pull-Apart Bread will surely satisfy those cravings.

With that, the oven timer should be going off shortly. Time to pull apart your bread!
