Homemade Peppermint Bark Brownies: A Decadent Holiday Treat


‘Tis the season for indulgence, and what better way to celebrate the holidays than with a batch of homemade peppermint bark brownies? These rich, fudgy brownies are elevated to a whole new level with the addition of luscious layers of peppermint bark, making them a perfect treat for festive gatherings, cozy nights by the fire, or as a delightful gift for friends and family.

Ingredients:

For the Brownie Layer:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt

For the Peppermint Bark Layer:

  • 12 ounces high-quality white chocolate, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 3–4 candy canes, crushed

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 6 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • ¼ teaspoon peppermint extract

Instructions:

Making the Brownie Layer:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan and line it with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in 30-second intervals until completely melted. Stir in the sugar until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared pan and spread it into an even layer. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Allow the brownies to cool completely.

Creating the Peppermint Bark Layer:

  1. In a heatproof bowl set over a pan of simmering water, melt the white chocolate, stirring occasionally until smooth. Stir in the peppermint extract.

  2. Pour the melted white chocolate over the cooled brownies, spreading it into an even layer. Sprinkle the crushed candy canes on top, gently pressing them into the chocolate. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes or until set.

Preparing the Chocolate Ganache:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to simmer. Remove from the heat and add the chopped semi-sweet chocolate, letting it sit for a minute to soften.

  2. Gently stir the mixture until the chocolate is completely melted and the ganache is smooth. Stir in the peppermint extract.

  3. Pour the ganache over the peppermint bark layer, spreading it evenly. Return the brownies to the refrigerator for another 30 minutes to set the ganache.

Serving and Storing:

  • Once fully set, use the parchment overhang to lift the brownies out of the pan. Place them on a cutting board and slice into squares using a sharp knife.

  • These peppermint bark brownies can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. For longer storage, they can be frozen for up to 2 months.

Conclusion:

Indulge in the magic of the holiday season with these homemade peppermint bark brownies. The combination of rich, fudgy brownies, creamy white chocolate peppermint bark, and decadent chocolate ganache makes for a truly irresistible treat that is sure to delight your taste buds and bring joy to those around you. Whether you’re hosting a festive gathering or simply looking to spread some holiday cheer, these brownies are the perfect way to sweeten the season. Enjoy the warmth and sweetness of the holidays with every delectable bite of these peppermint bark brownies. Happy baking!
