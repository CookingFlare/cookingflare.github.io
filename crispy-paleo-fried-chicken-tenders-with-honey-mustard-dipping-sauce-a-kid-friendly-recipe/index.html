Crispy Paleo Fried Chicken Tenders with Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce: A Kid-Friendly Recipe


When it comes to satisfying the taste buds of picky eaters, nothing beats the classic appeal of chicken tenders. This recipe for crispy Paleo fried chicken tenders is a game-changer, combining the familiar crunch and flavor that kids love with wholesome, nutritious ingredients that parents can feel good about. Paired with a delightful honey mustard dipping sauce, this dish is not only kid-friendly but also perfect for family dinners, lunchboxes, or even as a fun snack.

Why Go Paleo?

The Paleo diet emphasizes whole, unprocessed foods that our ancestors would have eaten, such as lean meats, fish, fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds. By avoiding grains, legumes, and processed sugars, this approach to eating can help promote better health and well-being. This recipe embraces the Paleo philosophy, ensuring that you can enjoy comfort food without the guilt.

Ingredients

For the Crispy Paleo Fried Chicken Tenders:

  • 1 pound chicken breast (cut into tenders)
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk (or almond milk)
  • Coconut oil or avocado oil (for frying)

For the Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup raw honey
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Tenders

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Start by placing the cut chicken tenders in a bowl. Pour in the coconut milk and beaten eggs, ensuring that the chicken is well-coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to enhance flavor and tenderness.

  2. Create the Coating Mixture: In a separate bowl, combine the almond flour, coconut flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure all the dry ingredients are evenly distributed.

Step 2: Coat the Chicken

  1. Coat the Tenders: Once the chicken has marinated, remove each tender from the liquid mixture, allowing any excess to drip off. Dredge it in the flour mixture, pressing gently to ensure an even coating. Place the coated tenders on a plate and repeat until all tenders are coated.

Step 3: Fry the Chicken Tenders

  1. Heat the Oil: In a large skillet, heat about 1/4 inch of coconut oil or avocado oil over medium-high heat. To test if the oil is hot enough, drop a small amount of the coating mixture into the oil; if it sizzles, you’re ready to fry.

  2. Fry the Tenders: Carefully add the chicken tenders to the skillet in batches, ensuring not to overcrowd the pan. Fry for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cooked through. Use a meat thermometer to check that the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).

  3. Drain the Tenders: Once cooked, remove the tenders from the skillet and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb any excess oil.

Step 4: Make the Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a small bowl, mix together the raw honey, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper. Whisk until smooth and well combined.

  2. Taste and Adjust: Feel free to adjust the sweetness or tanginess by adding more honey or mustard according to your preference.

Step 5: Serve

Serve the crispy Paleo fried chicken tenders hot with the honey mustard dipping sauce on the side. This dish pairs wonderfully with fresh vegetable sticks or a side salad for a complete meal.

Tips for Perfect Chicken Tenders

  1. Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh chicken will yield the best flavor and texture. Look for organic or free-range options if possible.

  2. Customize the Coating: Feel free to experiment with different seasonings in the coating mixture. Adding Italian herbs or a bit of cayenne pepper can give your chicken a unique twist.

  3. Oven-Baked Option: If you prefer a healthier alternative, you can bake the chicken tenders at 400°F (200°C) for about 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and cooked through.

  4. Meal Prep: These chicken tenders can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to three days. Reheat them in the oven to maintain their crispiness.

Nutritional Benefits

Not only are these crispy Paleo fried chicken tenders delicious, but they also offer several nutritional benefits:

  • High in Protein: Chicken is an excellent source of lean protein, which is essential for muscle growth and repair.

  • Healthy Fats: Using almond and coconut flour provides healthy fats and fiber, making this dish more satiating.

  • Natural Sweeteners: The honey mustard dipping sauce uses raw honey, which has antioxidant properties and can be a healthier alternative to processed sugars.

Conclusion

Crispy Paleo fried chicken tenders with honey mustard dipping sauce are a fantastic way to please the entire family. This kid-friendly recipe is not only easy to prepare but also packed with flavor and nutrition. Whether you’re serving it for dinner or packing it for lunch, these chicken tenders are sure to become a family favorite. Enjoy the satisfaction of watching your kids devour a wholesome meal that you can feel good about serving. Happy cooking!
