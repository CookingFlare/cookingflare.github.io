Tasty Bacon-Wrapped Tater Tot Skewers


If you’re a fan of bacon and tater tots, then you’re in for a treat with these delicious bacon-wrapped tater tot skewers. This recipe combines the crispy, smoky flavor of bacon with the comforting goodness of tater tots, resulting in a mouthwatering appetizer or snack that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

To make these tasty bacon-wrapped tater tot skewers, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 package of bacon
  • 1 bag of frozen tater tots
  • Wooden skewers

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. Take a slice of bacon and wrap it tightly around a tater tot. Secure the bacon in place by inserting a wooden skewer through the center of the tater tot. Repeat this process until all the tater tots are wrapped in bacon and skewered.

  3. Place the bacon-wrapped tater tot skewers onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them evenly apart. Make sure the bacon ends are tucked underneath the tater tots to prevent them from unraveling while baking.

  4. Bake the skewers in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and cooked to your desired level of crispiness.

  5. Once the bacon is cooked, remove the skewers from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes before serving. The tater tots will be piping hot, so be careful when taking a bite!

  6. Serve the tasty bacon-wrapped tater tot skewers as an appetizer at your next gathering or as a fun snack for movie night. You can also pair them with your favorite dipping sauces like ketchup, ranch dressing, or barbecue sauce for an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Enjoy the irresistible combination of crispy bacon and tender tater tots in every bite. These skewers are sure to be a hit with friends and family!

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try adding a sprinkle of shredded cheese or a dash of your favorite spices to the tater tots before wrapping them in bacon. This will add an extra layer of flavor and make the skewers even more irresistible.

  • If you prefer a healthier option, you can use turkey bacon instead of regular bacon. It will still provide that delicious smoky flavor while being lower in fat.

  • Experiment with different dipping sauces to find your favorite combination. From tangy mustard to sweet and spicy sriracha, there are endless possibilities to complement the flavors of the bacon-wrapped tater tot skewers.

  • If you’re serving a crowd, double or triple the recipe to ensure there’s enough for everyone. These skewers are always a hit and tend to disappear quickly!

Conclusion

These tasty bacon-wrapped tater tot skewers are a crowd-pleasing appetizer that combines the irresistible flavors of crispy bacon and tender tater tots. With their simple preparation and delicious results, they’re perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a savory snack, give this recipe a try and prepare to be delighted by the mouthwatering combination of bacon and tater tots. Enjoy!
