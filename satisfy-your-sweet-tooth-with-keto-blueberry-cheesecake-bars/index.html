Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Keto Blueberry Cheesecake Bars


If you’re a fan of cheesecake and looking for a guilt-free, low-carb dessert option, these keto blueberry cheesecake bars are a must-try! Packed with the luscious flavors of creamy cheesecake and tangy blueberries, these bars are the perfect way to indulge your sweet tooth while staying on track with your keto lifestyle.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup powdered erythritol (or your preferred keto-friendly sweetener)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup melted butter

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 2/3 cup powdered erythritol
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang for easy removal.

  2. Prepare the Crust: In a bowl, combine the almond flour, powdered erythritol, salt, and melted butter. Mix until the ingredients are well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking pan, creating an even layer. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is lightly golden. Remove from the oven and let it cool while you prepare the filling.

  3. Make the Cheesecake Filling: In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and powdered erythritol until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the sour cream and vanilla extract until fully incorporated.

  4. Assemble the Cheesecake Bars: Gently fold the fresh blueberries into the cheesecake filling, being careful not to break the berries. Pour the filling over the cooled crust, spreading it into an even layer.

  5. Bake and Chill: Bake the cheesecake bars for 30-35 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly. Once done, remove the bars from the oven and let them cool at room temperature. Then, transfer the pan to the refrigerator and chill the bars for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

  6. Slice and Serve: Once the cheesecake bars are thoroughly chilled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift them out of the pan. Cut into squares, and serve chilled. Enjoy the delightful combination of creamy cheesecake, bursting blueberries, and nutty crust!

Nutritional Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 240
  • Total Fat: 22g
  • Saturated Fat: 11g
  • Cholesterol: 77mg
  • Sodium: 160mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 5g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 6g

Final Thoughts

These keto blueberry cheesecake bars are a fantastic way to indulge in a decadent dessert while sticking to your low-carb goals. With their rich, creamy texture and delightful fruity burst, they are sure to become a favorite in your dessert rotation. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a satisfying sweet treat, these bars are a delicious option for any occasion. Treat yourself to a slice (or two) and savor the goodness without any guilt!

So, go ahead and whip up a batch of these delightful keto blueberry cheesecake bars to satisfy your sweet cravings the low-carb way. Your taste buds and your body will thank you for it!
