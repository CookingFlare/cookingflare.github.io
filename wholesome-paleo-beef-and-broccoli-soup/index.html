Wholesome Paleo Beef and Broccoli Soup


As the seasons change and the weather grows colder, there’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting bowl of soup to soothe the soul. But not just any soup will do - we want something that’s not only delicious, but also nourishing and wholesome. That’s why we’re excited to share this recipe for Paleo Beef and Broccoli Soup, a hearty and flavorful dish that’s perfect for a chilly winter’s night.

The Benefits of Paleo

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s talk about the benefits of the Paleo diet. In a nutshell, Paleo is all about eating foods that our ancestors would have eaten during the Paleolithic era - think lean meats, fresh fruits and vegetables, nuts and seeds, and healthy fats. By cutting out grains, dairy, and processed foods, we can reduce our risk of chronic diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and obesity, and improve our overall health and well-being.

The Recipe

Now that we’ve talked about the benefits of Paleo, let’s get to the recipe! This Beef and Broccoli Soup is a classic comfort food dish that’s been adapted to fit the Paleo diet. It’s made with tender chunks of grass-fed beef, fresh broccoli, and a rich, savory broth.

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 pounds grass-fed beef, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

To make the soup, start by heating the coconut oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the beef and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.

Next, add the chopped onion to the pot and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

Add the broccoli, beef broth, and thyme to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.

Add the browned beef back to the pot and season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer the soup for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the flavors have melded together and the beef is tender.

Serving Suggestions

This Paleo Beef and Broccoli Soup is perfect for a chilly winter’s night, served hot with a side of crusty bread or a green salad. You can also serve it as a main course, topped with chopped fresh parsley and a dollop of coconut cream.

Tips and Variations

If you like a little extra flavor in your soup, try adding a teaspoon of red pepper flakes or a pinch of paprika. You can also customize the recipe to your taste by using different types of broth or adding other vegetables like carrots or potatoes.

Conclusion

This Paleo Beef and Broccoli Soup is a hearty and comforting dish that’s perfect for a chilly winter’s night. By using grass-fed beef and fresh broccoli, we can ensure that we’re getting the nutrients we need to stay healthy and energized. And with its rich, savory broth and tender chunks of beef, this soup is sure to become a new favorite. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the warmth and nourishment of a delicious bowl of soup!
