Slow Cooker Mediterranean Shrimp Recipes for a Seafood Feast


If you’re a seafood lover looking for a delicious and hassle-free way to prepare shrimp, then this blog post is for you! Today, we’re diving into the world of Slow Cooker Mediterranean Shrimp Recipes that are guaranteed to impress your taste buds and satisfy your cravings for a seafood feast. With the convenience of a slow cooker, you can create mouthwatering shrimp dishes that are bursting with flavors from the Mediterranean region. So, let’s get cooking!

1. Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Place the shrimp in the slow cooker and pour the lemon garlic mixture over them. Toss to ensure the shrimp are coated evenly.
  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours or until the shrimp are cooked through.
  4. Serve the Lemon Garlic Shrimp over a bed of steamed rice or with crusty bread. Garnish with fresh parsley.

2. Slow Cooker Mediterranean Shrimp Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine the chopped onion, minced garlic, diced bell peppers, diced tomatoes (with their juice), vegetable broth, dried basil, dried oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Add the shrimp to the slow cooker and stir to combine with the other ingredients.
  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 3-4 hours or until the shrimp are cooked through and the flavors are well blended.
  4. Serve the Mediterranean Shrimp Stew in bowls, garnished with fresh basil. Enjoy with crusty bread or over cooked pasta.

3. Slow Cooker Spicy Mediterranean Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat them in the marinade. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  4. Place the shrimp skewers in the slow cooker and pour any remaining marinade over them.
  5. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 1-2 hours or until the shrimp are cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve the Spicy Mediterranean Shrimp Skewers as an appetizer or as a main course with a side of couscous or a fresh salad.

Enjoy these Slow Cooker Mediterranean Shrimp Recipes that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean. With their rich flavors and tender shrimp, these dishes are perfect for a seafood feast or any occasion where you want to impress your guests. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to indulge in the vibrant and delicious world of Mediterranean cuisine!
