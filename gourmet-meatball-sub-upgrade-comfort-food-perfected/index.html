Gourmet Meatball Sub Upgrade: Comfort Food Perfected


There’s something undeniably comforting about sinking your teeth into a hearty meatball sub. The combination of tender meatballs, savory tomato sauce, and melted cheese nestled within a warm, crusty roll is a true classic. However, if you’re looking to take this beloved comfort food to the next level, we’ve got just the recipe for you. Our gourmet meatball sub upgrade promises to elevate this traditional favorite into a culinary masterpiece that will have your taste buds singing with delight.

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Additional Ingredients:

  • 4 to 6 hoagie rolls
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Making the Meatballs:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, garlic, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter.
  3. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet and bake in a preheated 375°F oven for 25-30 minutes, or until cooked through.

Preparing the Sauce:

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the chopped fresh basil and remove the sauce from the heat.

Assembling the Meatball Subs:

  1. Slice the hoagie rolls in half lengthwise and place them on a baking sheet.
  2. Place a slice of fresh mozzarella on the bottom half of each roll.
  3. Arrange the cooked meatballs on top of the cheese.
  4. Spoon the tomato sauce over the meatballs.
  5. Top with additional slices of mozzarella.
  6. Place the assembled subs under the broiler for 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve hot.

Tips for Perfection

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes to the tomato sauce.
  • If you prefer a lighter option, you can use turkey or chicken mince instead of beef and pork for the meatballs.
  • Don’t skimp on the fresh basil – it adds a bright, aromatic touch to the finished subs.

Elevate Your Comfort Food Experience

By following this gourmet meatball sub upgrade recipe, you’ll transform a beloved classic into a sophisticated and utterly delicious dish that’s perfect for a cozy night in or a casual gathering with friends. The combination of tender, flavorful meatballs, rich tomato sauce, and gooey melted mozzarella will undoubtedly make this upgraded meatball sub a new favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to savor the ultimate comfort food perfected.
