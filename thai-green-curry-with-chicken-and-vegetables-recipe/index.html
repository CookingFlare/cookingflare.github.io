Thai Green Curry with Chicken and Vegetables Recipe


Are you a fan of Thai cuisine? If so, you’re in for a treat with this authentic and flavorful Thai Green Curry with Chicken and Vegetables recipe. This dish is a delightful combination of tender chicken, crisp vegetables, and aromatic herbs, all brought together in a creamy and fragrant green curry sauce. It’s a perfect balance of heat, sweetness, and savory goodness that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand. Let’s dive into the details of this tantalizing recipe and bring the exotic flavors of Thailand to your very own kitchen.

Ingredients:

For the Green Curry Paste:

  • 3-4 fresh green Thai chilies
  • 2 shallots, peeled and chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass, thinly sliced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of galangal, peeled and sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of shrimp paste
  • A handful of fresh cilantro roots and stems
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce

For the Curry:

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 400g (14oz) of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 can (400ml) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon of palm sugar
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 small eggplant, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of bamboo shoots
  • 1 cup of Thai basil leaves
  • Fish sauce, to taste
  • Fresh lime juice, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Green Curry Paste:

    • In a mortar and pestle or a food processor, combine the green Thai chilies, shallots, garlic, lemongrass, galangal, coriander seeds, cumin, shrimp paste, cilantro roots and stems, and lime zest. Grind or process the ingredients into a smooth paste. Add the fish sauce and mix well. Set the green curry paste aside.

  2. Cook the Chicken:

    • In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and sauté for a few minutes until fragrant. Add the sliced chicken and cook until it’s no longer pink on the outside.

  3. Simmer with Coconut Milk:

    • Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir in the palm sugar and add the kaffir lime leaves. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, allowing the flavors to meld together for about 10 minutes.

  4. Add Vegetables:

    • Add the eggplant, red bell pepper, and bamboo shoots to the curry. Let the vegetables simmer until they are tender but still slightly crisp.

  5. Finish and Serve:

    • Stir in the Thai basil leaves and season the curry with fish sauce and a splash of fresh lime juice to balance the flavors. Remove the kaffir lime leaves before serving.

Serving Suggestion:

This aromatic and creamy Thai Green Curry with Chicken and Vegetables is best enjoyed with steamed jasmine rice, allowing the fragrant curry to mingle with the fluffy grains. Garnish the curry with additional fresh basil leaves and a wedge of lime for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion:

With its vibrant colors, aromatic blend of herbs, and a perfect balance of flavors, Thai Green Curry with Chicken and Vegetables is a culinary masterpiece that is sure to impress family and friends. The rich and creamy curry sauce, combined with tender chicken and crisp vegetables, makes this dish a standout in Thai cuisine. So, why not embark on a culinary adventure and savor the exotic tastes of Thailand with this delightful recipe? Your taste buds will thank you for it!
