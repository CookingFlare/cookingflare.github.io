Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Stir-Fry: Quick and Easy


If you’re looking for a quick, flavorful, and healthy meal that’s ready in no time, then this Instant Pot beef and vegetable stir-fry recipe is just what you need. With the magic of the Instant Pot, you can have a delicious stir-fry on the table in no time, making it perfect for busy weeknights or whenever you’re craving a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Begin by slicing the flank steak thinly against the grain to ensure tenderness. Slice the onion, red bell pepper, and carrots, and prepare the broccoli florets and snap peas. Mince the garlic and grate the ginger.

  2. Sear the Beef: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add the vegetable oil. Once hot, add the sliced flank steak and sear until browned on all sides. This will help to lock in the flavors and juices of the beef.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. This flavorful sauce will be the key to the delicious taste of the stir-fry.

  4. Add Vegetables and Sauce: Once the beef is seared, add the sliced onion, red bell pepper, broccoli, snap peas, and carrots to the Instant Pot. Pour the prepared sauce over the ingredients and mix well to ensure everything is coated.

  5. Pressure Cook: Pour the beef broth into the Instant Pot and give everything a good stir. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” mode for 5 minutes. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes before carefully performing a quick pressure release.

  6. Thicken the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with water to create a slurry. Set the Instant Pot back to “Sauté” mode and add the slurry to the beef and vegetable mixture. Stir gently until the sauce thickens, which will only take a few minutes.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the sauce has thickened, your Instant Pot beef and vegetable stir-fry is ready to be served. You can pair it with cooked rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this stir-fry recipe with your favorite vegetables such as mushrooms, snow peas, or water chestnuts.
  • For a spicier kick, add a dash of chili flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce to the sauce mixture.
  • If you prefer a different protein, you can easily swap the beef for chicken, shrimp, or tofu.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot beef and vegetable stir-fry recipe is a game-changer when it comes to convenient and delicious meals. The Instant Pot allows for quick and efficient cooking, while the combination of tender beef and vibrant vegetables in a savory sauce creates a dish that’s both satisfying and wholesome. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or friends, this recipe is sure to impress with its bold flavors and minimal effort. So, the next time you’re craving a stir-fry, look no further than your trusty Instant Pot for a meal that’s quick, easy, and utterly delicious!
