Tasty Keto Cheese and Bacon Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and low-carb appetizer or snack, then you’re in for a treat with this Tasty Keto Cheese and Bacon Stuffed Mushrooms recipe. These savory bites are packed with flavor and are the perfect addition to any meal or gathering. Not only are they incredibly delicious, but they are also keto-friendly, making them suitable for those following a low-carb lifestyle. Let’s dive into the recipe and explore how you can make these mouthwatering stuffed mushrooms in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 12 large mushrooms, stems removed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Clean the mushrooms using a damp paper towel to remove any dirt. Carefully remove the stems and set them aside for later use.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil and chopped bacon. Cook until the bacon is crispy, then add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute. Remove the bacon mixture from the skillet and set it aside on a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess grease.
  4. In the same skillet, add the mushroom stems and cook for a few minutes until they are tender. Remove from the heat and allow them to cool slightly.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  6. Add the cooked bacon and mushroom stems to the cheese mixture and stir to incorporate all the ingredients.
  7. Using a spoon, generously fill each mushroom cap with the cheese and bacon mixture, mounding it slightly.
  8. Place the stuffed mushrooms on the prepared baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is golden and bubbly.
  9. Once done, remove the mushrooms from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestion

These Tasty Keto Cheese and Bacon Stuffed Mushrooms are best served warm, allowing the flavors to meld together beautifully. They can be enjoyed as an appetizer at a dinner party or even as a satisfying snack for a cozy night in. Pair them with a crisp salad or some roasted vegetables for a complete and satisfying keto-friendly meal.

Conclusion

This Tasty Keto Cheese and Bacon Stuffed Mushrooms recipe is a delightful way to enjoy the rich and savory flavors of cheese and bacon while staying true to your keto lifestyle. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a flavorful snack, these stuffed mushrooms are sure to impress. Give this recipe a try and savor the deliciousness of these mouthwatering keto-friendly bites!
