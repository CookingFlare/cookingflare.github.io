Savory Italian Stuffed Artichokes Recipe - Mediterranean Sensation


Are you looking for a flavorful and impressive dish to impress your dinner guests? Look no further than this Savory Italian Stuffed Artichokes recipe! Bursting with Mediterranean flavors, this dish will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Italy. The combination of tender artichoke hearts, savory breadcrumbs, and aromatic herbs is sure to make this recipe a crowd-pleaser. So, let’s dive into the recipe and create a Mediterranean sensation in your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 4 large artichokes
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Start by preparing the artichokes. Trim the top of each artichoke and remove any tough outer leaves. Cut off the stem, leaving about an inch attached to the base. Use a pair of kitchen shears to snip off the sharp tips of the remaining leaves.

  3. Next, gently spread the leaves apart to expose the artichoke heart. Use a spoon to scoop out the fuzzy choke in the center, making sure to remove it entirely.

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, minced garlic, sun-dried tomatoes, Kalamata olives, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  5. Now it’s time to stuff the artichokes. Take a generous spoonful of the breadcrumb mixture and press it into the center of each artichoke, filling the cavity where the choke was removed. Continue to fill all the artichokes with the breadcrumb mixture.

  6. Place the stuffed artichokes in a baking dish. Drizzle them with a little olive oil and sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.

  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes, or until the artichokes are tender and the breadcrumbs are golden brown.

  8. Once cooked, remove the foil and let the artichokes cool for a few minutes before serving. The stuffed artichokes can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

These Savory Italian Stuffed Artichokes can be served as an appetizer or as a side dish. They pair perfectly with a fresh green salad or a bowl of creamy risotto. For a complete Mediterranean meal, serve them alongside grilled fish or roasted chicken. The artichokes can also be enjoyed on their own as a light and satisfying vegetarian main course.

Final Thoughts

With their delightful combination of flavors and textures, these Savory Italian Stuffed Artichokes are a true Mediterranean sensation. The tender artichoke hearts, combined with the savory breadcrumb filling, create a dish that is both comforting and elegant. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking to elevate your weeknight meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the taste of Italy with every bite!
