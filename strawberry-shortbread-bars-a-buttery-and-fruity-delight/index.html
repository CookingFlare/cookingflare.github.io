Strawberry Shortbread Bars: A Buttery and Fruity Delight


There’s something undeniably delightful about the sweet, tangy flavor of fresh strawberries paired with the rich, buttery goodness of shortbread. If you’re a fan of this classic combination, then you’re in for a treat with our recipe for Strawberry Shortbread Bars. These delectable bars are perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a summer picnic, a cozy brunch, or simply craving a homemade sweet treat.

Ingredients

For the Shortbread Base:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed

For the Strawberry Layer:

  • 3 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Streusel Topping:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan and set aside.

  2. Make the Shortbread Base: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt for the shortbread base. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan.

  3. Prepare the Strawberry Layer: In another bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Gently toss the strawberries until they are evenly coated with the sugar mixture. Spread the strawberry mixture over the shortbread base.

  4. Create the Streusel Topping: To make the streusel topping, mix the flour, oats, and brown sugar in a bowl. Drizzle the melted butter over the mixture and stir until it forms clumps. Sprinkle the streusel evenly over the strawberry layer.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Place the baking pan in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the strawberry filling is bubbling.

  6. Cool and Serve: Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack. Once cooled, cut into bars and serve.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist, try using a combination of fresh berries such as raspberries, blueberries, or blackberries in place of the strawberries.
  • Serve the bars with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat.
  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Conclusion

These Strawberry Shortbread Bars are a delightful balance of buttery, crumbly shortbread, and luscious, sweet-tart strawberry filling. They are a perfect way to showcase the vibrant flavors of fresh strawberries and make a stunning addition to any dessert spread. Whether you’re an experienced baker or just starting out, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your collection. Enjoy the process of making these bars and savor every delicious bite!
