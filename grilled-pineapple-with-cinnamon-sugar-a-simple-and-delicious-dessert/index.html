Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon Sugar: A Simple and Delicious Dessert


If you’re looking for a quick and easy dessert that will satisfy your sweet tooth, look no further than grilled pineapple with cinnamon sugar. This simple yet delicious treat is bursting with flavor and is sure to impress your family and friends. Plus, it’s a great way to make use of those ripe pineapples that are sitting on your kitchen counter. So, fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in this tropical delight.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe pineapple
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. This will ensure that the pineapple gets nice grill marks without becoming too soft.
  2. While the grill is heating up, prepare the pineapple by removing the outer skin and cutting it into slices. Make sure to remove the core as well, as it can be tough and fibrous.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon. This will create a sweet and fragrant coating for the pineapple slices.
  4. Once the grill is hot, place the pineapple slices directly on the grates. Let them cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until they have nice grill marks and have softened slightly.
  5. While the pineapple is grilling, sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture on both sides of the slices. This will create a caramelized crust that adds a delightful crunch to each bite.
  6. After the pineapple slices have been grilled to perfection, remove them from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes. This will allow the sugars to caramelize and the flavors to meld together.
  7. Serve the grilled pineapple slices on a platter, garnished with fresh mint leaves if desired. The mint adds a refreshing touch to the sweet and tangy flavors of the pineapple.
  8. Enjoy your grilled pineapple with cinnamon sugar straight from the grill, or pair it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat. Either way, it’s bound to be a hit!

Tips and Variations

  • To add an extra kick of flavor, you can sprinkle a pinch of chili powder or cayenne pepper on the pineapple slices before grilling. This will give them a spicy-sweet twist that is sure to tantalize your taste buds.
  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also prepare this dessert on a stovetop grill pan or even in a regular frying pan. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.
  • For a tropical twist, you can serve the grilled pineapple with a dollop of coconut cream or a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes. This will transport your taste buds straight to a sandy beach in the Caribbean.
  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can experiment with different spices and seasonings. Try adding a pinch of nutmeg or ginger to the cinnamon sugar mixture for a unique flavor profile.

Grilled pineapple with cinnamon sugar is a delightful dessert that is both simple to make and bursting with tropical flavors. It’s the perfect way to end a summer barbecue or to satisfy your sweet tooth any time of the year. So, grab a pineapple and fire up the grill – it’s time to indulge in this delicious treat!
