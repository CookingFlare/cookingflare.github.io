Wholesome Keto Baked Chicken Parmesan Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious, healthy alternative to the classic Italian dish? Look no further! This Wholesome Keto Baked Chicken Parmesan recipe is not only low in carbs but also packed with flavor, making it an ideal choice for anyone following a ketogenic diet. With its crispy coating and rich tomato sauce, this dish will satisfy your cravings without derailing your dietary goals. Let’s dive into this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs

For the Sauce:

  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For Topping:

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Prepare the Chicken

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). This will ensure that your chicken cooks evenly and becomes beautifully golden brown.

  2. Set Up Your Breading Station: In one bowl, whisk the eggs. In another bowl, combine the almond flour, grated Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. This mixture will create a flavorful and crunchy coating for your chicken.

  3. Coat the Chicken: Dip each chicken breast into the egg mixture, allowing any excess to drip off. Then, coat the chicken thoroughly in the almond flour and Parmesan mixture, pressing gently to adhere. Place the coated chicken breasts on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

Make the Sauce

  1. Cook the Sauce: In a saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Then, pour in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Allow the sauce to simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. This will enhance the flavors and create a rich tomato sauce.

Bake the Chicken

  1. Bake the Chicken: Once the chicken is coated, place the baking sheet in the preheated oven. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the coating is golden brown. The internal temperature of the chicken should reach 165°F (74°C).

Assemble the Dish

  1. Add the Sauce and Cheese: Once the chicken is baked, remove it from the oven. Spoon the prepared sauce generously over each chicken breast, then sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese. Return the dish to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Serve

  1. Garnish and Enjoy: Once the cheese is melted to perfection, take the chicken out of the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving. Pair this delicious dish with a side of zucchini noodles or a fresh garden salad to complete your meal.

Nutritional Benefits

This Wholesome Keto Baked Chicken Parmesan is not only delicious but also packed with nutritional benefits:

  • High in Protein: Chicken breast is an excellent source of lean protein, which is essential for muscle repair and overall health.
  • Low in Carbs: By using almond flour instead of traditional breadcrumbs, this recipe keeps the carb count low, making it suitable for a ketogenic diet.
  • Rich in Healthy Fats: The addition of olive oil and cheese provides healthy fats that are important for energy and nutrient absorption.
  • Packed with Vitamins: The tomato sauce is rich in vitamins A and C, while the garlic adds antioxidants that support immune health.

Tips for Success

  • Pound the Chicken: For even cooking, consider pounding the chicken breasts to an even thickness before breading them. This will help them cook more uniformly and avoid dry spots.
  • Customize Your Seasoning: Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices in the breading mix to suit your taste. Paprika, cayenne pepper, or even a sprinkle of lemon zest can add a unique twist.
  • Make It Ahead: This dish is perfect for meal prep! You can prepare the chicken and sauce ahead of time, then bake when you’re ready to eat. Store the cooked chicken in an airtight container in the fridge for up to three days.

Conclusion

This Wholesome Keto Baked Chicken Parmesan is a fantastic way to enjoy a classic dish while sticking to your keto lifestyle. It’s simple to prepare, packed with flavor, and sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or hosting a dinner, this dish is sure to impress.

Give it a try, and savor the delightful combination of crispy chicken, rich tomato sauce, and gooey cheese. Your taste buds will thank you, and your body will appreciate the wholesome ingredients. Enjoy your culinary adventure and indulge without guilt!
