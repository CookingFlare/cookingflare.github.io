Whip Up a Dairy-Free Coconut Cream Pie for Dessert


Coconut cream pie is a classic dessert that never fails to delight with its creamy texture and tropical flavor. However, for those who are lactose intolerant or follow a dairy-free diet, enjoying this delectable treat can be a challenge. But fear not! With this simple and delicious recipe, you can whip up a dairy-free coconut cream pie that will satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch dairy-free pie crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 can (14 ounces) full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons dairy-free butter substitute
  • 1 cup dairy-free whipped topping (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).

  2. Place the dairy-free pie crust in a 9-inch pie dish. If using a store-bought crust, follow the instructions on the package for blind baking. If making a homemade crust, blind bake it for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Set aside to cool.

  3. In a medium saucepan, combine the coconut milk, unsweetened coconut flakes, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Whisk the mixture until the cornstarch is fully dissolved and there are no lumps.

  4. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a gentle boil. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  5. Once the mixture has thickened, remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract and dairy-free butter substitute until well combined.

  6. Pour the coconut cream filling into the cooled pie crust and smooth the top with a spatula. Cover the pie with plastic wrap, making sure it touches the surface of the filling to prevent a skin from forming. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or until set.

  7. Before serving, spread the dairy-free whipped topping over the chilled coconut cream filling. You can pipe it on for an elegant touch or simply spread it with a spatula.

  8. Optional: Sprinkle some toasted coconut flakes on top for extra flavor and decoration.

  9. Slice and serve your dairy-free coconut cream pie chilled. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra coconutty flavor, toast the coconut flakes before adding them to the filling. Simply spread the flakes on a baking sheet and toast in a preheated oven at 325°F (165°C) for 5-7 minutes, or until golden brown.

  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can strain the coconut cream filling through a fine-mesh sieve before pouring it into the pie crust.

  • Feel free to experiment with different dairy-free whipped toppings or make your own at home using coconut cream and a sweetener of your choice.

  • If you’re not a fan of coconut flakes, you can omit them from the filling or substitute them with chopped nuts for added crunch.

Now that you have this fantastic recipe for a dairy-free coconut cream pie, you can indulge in a slice of tropical paradise without worrying about lactose or dairy. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply treating yourself, this delicious dessert is sure to impress. So go ahead, whip up this delightful pie, and savor every creamy, coconutty bite. Happy baking!
