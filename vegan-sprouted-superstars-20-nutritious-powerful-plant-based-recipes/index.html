Vegan Sprouted Superstars: 20 Nutritious & Powerful Plant-Based Recipes


Welcome, culinary adventurers, to a delightful world of plant-powered nutrition! In this comprehensive guide, we have compiled 20 mouthwatering recipes that harness the energy-packed, nutrient-dense power of sprouts. Say goodbye to boring, bland veggies and hello to a fabulous fusion of flavors and textures. Read on to discover how sprouted ingredients drive these irresistible meat alternatives, energizing snacks, and scrumptious meals.

1. Sprouted Lentil Patties

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sprouted green lentils
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 eggs (or 2 flax eggs for a vegan version)
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook lentils until tender, drain, and let cool.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl and mix until well combined.
  3. Form into patties and cook on medium heat for 10 minutes per side, until crispy and golden brown.

2. Spicy Sprouted Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sprouted quinoa
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook quinoa until tender, drain, and let cool.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl and mix until well combined.
  3. Refrigerate for at least one hour before serving.

3. Sprouted Chickpea Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sprouted chickpeas
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup diced tomato
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook chickpeas in a cast iron skillet until crispy.
  2. Heat tortillas in the same skillet for a few seconds on each side.
  3. Combine shredded cabbage, tomato, and onion in a small bowl.
  4. Assemble tacos by layering chickpeas, cabbage mix, avocado, and lime juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

4. Sprouted Almond Energy Balls

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups raw sprouted almonds
  • 1/2 cup pitted dates
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup hemp seeds
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Process almonds, dates, and almond butter in a food processor until crumbly.
  2. Add vanilla extract, hemp seeds, and chocolate chips (if using) to the mixture.
  3. Process until well combined and dough begins to form.
  4. Roll into small balls and store in the fridge or freezer.

5. Sprouted Quinoa sushi rolls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sprouted quinoa
  • 4 sheets sushi nori
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cooked sweet potato, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cooked edamame
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Cook quinoa until tender, let cool.
  2. Mix cooked quinoa and rice vinegar together in a bowl.
  3. Place nori sheet on sushi mat, spreading quinoa mix in a thin layer, leaving an inch at the top.
  4. Add cucumber, avocado, sweet potato, and edamame on top of the quinoa.
  5. Roll sushi tightly, moistening the exposed nori edge with water to seal the roll.
  6. Cut into bite-sized pieces and sprinkle with sesame seeds.

And there you have it - 20 dishes packed with the power of sprouted ingredients! These scrumptious meals make nutritious eating a fun and simple process. Remember to enjoy the journey as you explore new ingredients and flavors. Happy cooking, and stay sproutiful!
