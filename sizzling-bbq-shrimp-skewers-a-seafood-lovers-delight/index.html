Sizzling BBQ Shrimp Skewers: A Seafood Lover's Delight


BBQ shrimp skewers are a scrumptious and refreshing dish that no seafood lover should miss out on. As summertime approaches, this delightful recipe offers an excellent reason to gather friends and family around the grill. In this post, I’ll guide you step by step through the process of preparing these sizzling shrimp skewers, perfect for serving at your next barbecue party.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 red bell pepper, deseeded and sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup bbq sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Begin by slicing the red and yellow onion, bell pepper, zucchini, orange, and lemon into even pieces. Cut the shrimp in half crosswise to ensure that they’ll be more manageable on the skewers.

  2. Assemble the skewers: Thread the vegetables onto skewers, starting and ending with one piece of each vegetable to keep the shrimp in the middle. Then, add one-half of the shrimp to the skewer, followed by another piece of a vegetable and continue the sequence. You can use wooden or metal skewers, but I recommend using metal skewers, as they aren’t as prone to burning on the grill.

  3. Create the BBQ marinade: In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, bbq sauce, honey, garlic, paprika, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. This marinade brings together a symphony of flavors on your shrimp skewers.

  4. Marinade the skewers: Pour the marinade over your skewers, making sure they are evenly coated. Place them in a dish or a large resealable plastic bag and marinate them in the fridge for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for more flavor.

  5. Prepare the grill: While your skewers are marinating, heat your outdoor grill to medium-high heat. If using a gas grill, preheat it to about 350-400°F.

  6. Grill the skewers: With your skewers sufficiently marinated and the grill preheated, it’s time to let the flavor development begin. Place the skewers on the grate, ensuring maximum surface contact for the best grill marks. Cook each skewer for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the shrimp turns pink and the vegetables become tender but not mushy. The key to obtaining a nice char on your skewers is to resist the urge to move them around on the grill – let them develop those attractive, deep grill marks.

  7. Serve and enjoy: Once your sizzling BBQ shrimp skewers are cooked to perfection, remove them from the grill and serve immediately. You can set them up on a bed of your favorite rice dish, or individual serving plates to showcase the beautiful colors from the vegetables. Garnish with a wedge of lemon or lime for a zesty finish.

BBQ shrimp skewers showcase the versatility of seafood and are a fun, unique way to serve shrimp at a party or gathering. You’ll be amazed by the flavor explosion that occurs when you combine smoky and sweet barbecue with a seafood favorite – an irresistible combination that will quickly become an all-time hit at your summer parties. Give this recipe a try and savor every last scrumptious bite of these Sizzling BBQ Shrimp Skewers!
