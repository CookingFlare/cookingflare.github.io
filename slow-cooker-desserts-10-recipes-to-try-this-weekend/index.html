Slow Cooker Desserts: 10 Recipes to Try This Weekend


Are you looking for a unique twist on traditional dessert making? Do you want to come home to a delicious treat without slaving away in the kitchen? Look no further than slow cooker desserts! These easy-to-make treats are perfect for busy home cooks who want to enjoy a sweet escape without sacrificing time. In this post, we'll explore 10 mouth-watering slow cooker dessert recipes that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether you're in the mood for a classic comfort treat or something more adventurous, we've got you covered.

  1. Apple Cinnamon Cobbler What's more comforting than a warm, crumbly cobbler fresh from the slow cooker? Our Apple Cinnamon Cobbler recipe combines the flavors of tender apples, warm cinnamon, and a hint of vanilla to create a dessert that's sure to become a family favorite. Simply layer sliced apples, brown sugar, and cinnamon in your slow cooker, top with a mixture of flour, sugar, and melted butter, and cook on low for 6-8 hours. When you get home, serve warm with a dollop of whipped cream or vanilla ice cream. Ingredients:

6-8 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced 1/2 cup brown sugar 2 tsp ground cinnamon 1 tsp vanilla extract 1 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 cup granulated sugar 1/2 cup melted butter

  1. Chocolate Lava Cake Who says you can't have a rich, decadent lava cake in the slow cooker? Our Chocolate Lava Cake recipe is a game-changer for anyone with a sweet tooth. Simply mix together melted chocolate, heavy cream, eggs, sugar, and flour, then pour the mixture into individual ramekins or cupcake liners. Place the liners in your slow cooker and cook on low for 2-3 hours. When you remove them, the centers will still be gooey and rich â€“ perfect for serving warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Ingredients:

1 cup unsalted chocolate chips 1 cup heavy cream 2 large eggs 1 cup granulated sugar 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

  1. Peach Cream Pie Summer just got a whole lot sweeter with our Peach Cream Pie recipe. This slow cooker dessert combines the flavors of juicy peaches, creamy vanilla pudding, and a crumbly graham cracker crust to create a treat that's sure to impress. Simply layer sliced peaches, sugar, and cornstarch in your slow cooker, then cook on low for 6-8 hours. Meanwhile, mix together graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter to create the crust. When you're ready to serve, top the peaches with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon. Ingredients:

4-6 medium-sized peaches, sliced 1 cup granulated sugar 2 tbsp cornstarch 1 cup graham cracker crumbs 1/4 cup granulated sugar 1/4 cup melted butter

  1. Lemon Bars The tang of lemon is a perfect contrast to the sweetness of sugar in our Lemon Bars recipe. These slow cooker treats combine the flavors of tart lemon curd, crumbly shortbread crust, and a hint of vanilla to create a dessert that's sure to become a classic. Simply mix together lemon juice, sugar, eggs, and flour, then top with crumbly shortbread mix and cook on low for 4-5 hours. When you're ready to serve, sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve warm. Ingredients:

2 lemons, juiced 1 cup granulated sugar 4 large eggs 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup shortbread mix 1/4 cup powdered sugar

  1. Banana Pudding Who wouldn't love a creamy, dreamy banana pudding straight from the slow cooker? Our Banana Pudding recipe combines the flavors of ripe bananas, creamy vanilla pudding, and a hint of nutmeg to create a dessert that's sure to become a staple. Simply mix together sliced bananas, sugar, eggs, and flour, then cook on low for 6-8 hours. Meanwhile, whip together heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla to create the whipped cream topping. When you're ready to serve, sprinkle with nutmeg and serve warm. Ingredients:

4-6 ripe bananas 1 cup granulated sugar 4 large eggs 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup heavy cream 1 tsp vanilla extract 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

  1. Strawberry Shortcake Spring just got a whole lot sweeter with our Strawberry Shortcake recipe. This slow cooker dessert combines the flavors of juicy strawberries, sweet shortcake, and a hint of vanilla to create a treat that's sure to become a favorite. Simply layer sliced strawberries, sugar, and shortcake in your slow cooker, then cook on low for 6-8 hours. Meanwhile, whip together heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla to create the whipped cream topping. When you're ready to serve, top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of sugar. Ingredients:

4-6 cups sliced strawberries 1 cup granulated sugar 4 cups shortcake 1 cup heavy cream 2 tbsp granulated sugar 1 tsp vanilla extract

  1. Caramel Pecan Cheesecake Who says cheesecake can't be made in the slow cooker? Our Caramel Pecan Cheesecake recipe is a game-changer for anyone with a sweet tooth. Simply mix together cream cheese, eggs, sugar, and flour, then pour the mixture into individual ramekins or cupcake liners. Place the liners in your slow cooker and cook on low for 2-3 hours. Meanwhile, mix together caramel sauce and chopped pecans to create the topping. When you remove the cheesecake, drizzle with caramel and sprinkle with pecans. Ingredients:

1 cup cream cheese 4 large eggs 1 cup granulated sugar 1/2 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup caramel sauce 1 cup chopped pecans

  1. Chocolate Peanut Butter Parfait Who wouldn't love a rich, decadent chocolate peanut butter parfait straight from the slow cooker? Our Chocolate Peanut Butter Parfait recipe combines the flavors of creamy peanut butter, melted chocolate, and crunchy peanuts to create a dessert that's sure to become a favorite. Simply layer peanut butter, chocolate chips, and peanuts in your slow cooker, then cook on low for 6-8 hours. When you're ready to serve, sprinkle with chocolate shavings and serve warm. Ingredients:

1 cup creamy peanut butter 1 cup chocolate chips 1 cup peanuts 1/4 cup chocolate shavings

  1. Panna Cotta Why settle for a boring old casserole dish when you can have a creamy, dreamy panna cotta straight from the slow cooker? Our Panna Cotta recipe combines the flavors of sweet cream, creamy ricotta cheese, and a hint of vanilla to create a dessert that's sure to impress. Simply mix together cream, ricotta cheese, sugar, and vanilla, then pour the mixture into individual ramekins or cupcake liners. Place the liners in your slow cooker and cook on low for 2-3 hours. When you remove the panna cotta, drizzle with honey and serve warm. Ingredients:

1 cup heavy cream 1 cup ricotta cheese 1 cup granulated sugar 1 tsp vanilla extract 1 tbsp honey

  1. Lemon Chiffon Cake Who wouldn't love a light, fluffy lemon chiffon cake straight from the slow cooker? Our Lemon Chiffon Cake recipe combines the flavors of tart lemon juice, sweet sugar, and creamy lemon zest to create a dessert that's sure to become a favorite. Simply mix together lemon juice, sugar, eggs, and flour, then cook on low for 6-8 hours. Meanwhile, whip together egg whites, sugar, and lemon zest to create the meringue topping. When you're ready to serve, sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve warm. Ingredients:

2 lemons, juiced 1 cup granulated sugar 4 large eggs 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup egg whites 1/2 cup granulated sugar 1 tsp lemon zest

In conclusion, slow cooker desserts are a game-changer for anyone with a sweet tooth. With these 10 delicious recipes, you'll be able to whip up a delicious treat without sacrificing time or effort. Whether you're in the mood for a classic comfort treat or something more adventurous, we've got you covered. So go ahead, get creative, and indulge in the sweet life with these stunning slow cooker desserts!
