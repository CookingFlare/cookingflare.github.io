Quick and Easy Stir Fry Recipes with Ramen Noodles


Stir fry recipes are a fantastic option for busy individuals who want a delicious and satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. And when you combine the convenience of stir fry with the affordability and versatility of ramen noodles, you have a winning combination that will surely become a go-to meal in your household. In this blog post, we’ll explore some quick and easy stir fry recipes that feature ramen noodles as the star ingredient.

Vegetable Stir Fry with Ramen Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of ramen noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sliced mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, etc.)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir fry for 3-4 minutes until they are crisp-tender.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using).
  6. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the cooked ramen noodles to the other side.
  7. Pour the sauce over the noodles and toss everything together until well-coated.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.

Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry with Ramen Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of ramen noodles
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the sliced chicken breasts to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  5. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  6. Add the broccoli florets to the skillet and stir fry for 2-3 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and honey.
  8. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and pour the sauce over the chicken and broccoli.
  9. Toss everything together until well-coated and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve hot.

Shrimp and Mushroom Stir Fry with Ramen Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of ramen noodles
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink and are cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  5. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  6. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and stir fry for 2-3 minutes until they are tender.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil.
  8. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and pour the sauce over the shrimp and mushrooms.
  9. Toss everything together until well-coated and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.

These quick and easy stir fry recipes with ramen noodles are perfect for those nights when you want a delicious and satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The best part is that you can customize them to suit your taste by adding your favorite vegetables or protein. So go ahead, give these recipes a try and enjoy a flavorful stir fry that will leave you craving more!
