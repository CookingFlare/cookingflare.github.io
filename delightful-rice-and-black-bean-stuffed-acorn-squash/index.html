Delightful Rice and Black Bean Stuffed Acorn Squash

3 min · 637 words

Are you looking for a hearty and delicious vegetarian dish that will satisfy your taste buds? Look no further than this delightful recipe for Rice and Black Bean Stuffed Acorn Squash. Packed with flavors and nutrients, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 2 acorn squash
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut the acorn squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds. Place the squash halves on a baking sheet, cut side up, and roast in the oven for about 30-40 minutes, or until the flesh is tender when pierced with a fork.

  2. While the squash is roasting, prepare the filling. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and red bell pepper, and sauté until they become tender and slightly caramelized, about 5-7 minutes.

  3. Add the minced garlic, cumin, paprika, and chili powder to the skillet, and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

  4. Stir in the cooked brown rice and black beans, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Once the acorn squash halves are done roasting, remove them from the oven. Carefully fill each squash half with the rice and black bean mixture, dividing it evenly between the four halves.

  6. Return the stuffed acorn squash to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the filling is heated through and the squash is golden brown on top.

  7. Remove the stuffed acorn squash from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve warm.

Serving Suggestions

This Rice and Black Bean Stuffed Acorn Squash is a complete meal on its own, but you can also serve it with a side salad or some steamed vegetables for added freshness. The combination of the sweet and nutty acorn squash, the flavorful rice and black bean filling, and the vibrant garnish of fresh cilantro creates a harmonious and satisfying dish.

Nutritional Benefits

Acorn squash is a great source of vitamins A and C, as well as dietary fiber. The black beans provide protein and fiber, while the brown rice adds complex carbohydrates for sustained energy. This dish is not only delicious but also nutritious, making it a perfect choice for those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet.

Conclusion

Delight your taste buds and nourish your body with this flavorful and nutritious recipe for Rice and Black Bean Stuffed Acorn Squash. It’s a satisfying vegetarian dish that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized. So why wait? Give this recipe a try and enjoy a delightful meal that’s as good for you as it is tasty!
