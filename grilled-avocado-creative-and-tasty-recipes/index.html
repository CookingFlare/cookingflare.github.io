Grilled Avocado: Creative and Tasty Recipes


Avocados are a versatile and delicious fruit that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. While they are commonly used in salads, sandwiches, and dips, grilling avocados can take their flavor and texture to a whole new level. The gentle heat of the grill brings out the natural creaminess of the avocado and adds a smoky, charred flavor that is simply irresistible. In this blog post, we will explore some creative and tasty recipes for grilled avocados that will elevate your culinary repertoire and impress your guests.

Grilled Avocado with Salsa

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits.
  3. Brush the cut sides of the avocados with a little olive oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Grill the avocados cut side down for 2-3 minutes, until grill marks appear.
  5. In a bowl, combine the diced tomato, red onion, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper to make the salsa.
  6. Remove the avocados from the grill and spoon the salsa into the well of each avocado half.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the creamy, smoky goodness!

Grilled Avocado and Shrimp Salad

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts
  • Balsamic vinaigrette

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits.
  3. Brush the cut sides of the avocados with a little olive oil.
  4. Season the shrimp with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  5. Grill the avocados cut side down for 2-3 minutes, until grill marks appear.
  6. Grill the seasoned shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.
  7. In a large bowl, toss the mixed greens with balsamic vinaigrette.
  8. Arrange the grilled avocados and shrimp on top of the greens.
  9. Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese and toasted pine nuts.
  10. Serve immediately and savor the combination of creamy avocado, succulent shrimp, and vibrant greens.

Grilled Avocado and Black Bean Tacos

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits.
  3. Brush the cut sides of the avocados with a little olive oil.
  4. Grill the avocados cut side down for 2-3 minutes, until grill marks appear.
  5. In a small saucepan, heat the black beans with cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  6. Warm the corn tortillas on the grill for 1-2 minutes on each side.
  7. Scoop the seasoned black beans into the grilled avocados.
  8. Top with diced red onion and chopped cilantro.
  9. Serve the stuffed avocados in warm tortillas with lime wedges on the side.

Grilled avocados are a delightful addition to any meal, whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply looking to experiment with new flavors in the kitchen. The smoky, creamy goodness of grilled avocados will leave a lasting impression on your taste buds. Try out these creative and tasty recipes and elevate your love for avocados to a whole new level!
