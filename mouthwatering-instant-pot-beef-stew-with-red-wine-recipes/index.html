Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef Stew with Red Wine Recipes


Instant Pot is an incredibly versatile cooking tool that can help you prepare a wide range of delicious dishes, from tender and juicy meats to flavorful soups and stews. One of the most popular and beloved Instant Pot recipes is undoubtedly the mouthwatering beef stew with red wine. In this article, we’ll explore the secrets of making the perfect Instant Pot beef stew with red wine, and share a simple and easy-to-follow recipe that’s sure to become a family favorite.

Why Instant Pot Beef Stew with Red Wine?

Beef stew with red wine is a classic dish that’s been enjoyed for centuries, and for good reason. The rich flavors of the beef, combined with the bold and fruity notes of the red wine, create a truly unforgettable culinary experience. When cooked in an Instant Pot, the tender and fall-apart beef is perfectly complemented by the rich and savory sauce, making for a truly comforting and satisfying meal.

The Secret to Success: Choosing the Right Ingredients

While it’s tempting to grab the first pack of beef and red wine you see, choosing the right ingredients is crucial to making a truly exceptional Instant Pot beef stew with red wine. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Beef: Look for a cut of beef that’s well-marbled and has a good balance of fat and lean meat. This will help keep the beef tender and juicy, even after hours of cooking. Some popular options include chuck roast, round steak, and brisket.
  • Red wine: Choose a red wine that’s full-bodied and has a rich, fruity flavor. Some popular options include Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, and Pinot Noir.
  • Vegetables: While it’s tempting to add a wide range of vegetables to your stew, it’s best to stick with a few simple options that will hold their flavor and texture throughout the cooking process. Some popular options include onions, carrots, and potatoes.

The Recipe: Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef Stew with Red Wine

Now that we’ve covered the importance of choosing the right ingredients, it’s time to dive into the recipe. Here’s a simple and easy-to-follow recipe that’s sure to become a family favorite:

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef (chuck roast, round steak, or brisket), cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup red wine (Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, or Pinot Noir)
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Press the “Saute” button on the Instant Pot and heat the butter until melted. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add the beef cubes and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the chopped carrots and potatoes, and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Add the red wine, beef broth, thyme, and rosemary. Stir to combine, then season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to “Sealing”. Press the “Meat/Stew” button and cook for 30 minutes at high pressure.
  7. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  8. Stir in the flour to thicken the sauce, then serve hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

While this recipe is sure to become a family favorite, there are a few tips and variations to keep in mind:

  • Add some heat: If you like a little spice in your stew, add some red pepper flakes or diced jalapenos to the pot.
  • Try different vegetables: While onions, carrots, and potatoes are classic stew vegetables, you can also try adding other options like bell peppers, mushrooms, or zucchini.
  • Use different types of beef: While chuck roast, round steak, and brisket are all great options, you can also try using other types of beef like short ribs or shank.

Conclusion

Instant Pot beef stew with red wine is a classic dish that’s sure to become a family favorite. With its tender and fall-apart beef, rich and savory sauce, and comforting and satisfying flavor, it’s the perfect meal for a cold winter’s night. By following the simple and easy-to-follow recipe outlined above, you’ll be able to create a truly unforgettable culinary experience that’s sure to impress even the most discerning palates. So why not give it a try? Your taste buds will thank you!
