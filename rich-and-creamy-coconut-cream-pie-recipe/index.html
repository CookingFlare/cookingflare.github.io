Rich and Creamy Coconut Cream Pie Recipe


If you’re a fan of tropical flavors and silky textures, you’re in for a treat with this indulgent coconut cream pie recipe. This dessert is a perfect blend of rich coconut, velvety cream, and a buttery crust that will transport your taste buds to a paradise of flavor. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a decadent dessert, this coconut cream pie is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients:

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

For the Coconut Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

For the Whipped Cream Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Coconut Filling

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the coconut milk, whole milk, and sweetened shredded coconut. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar, cornstarch, salt, and egg yolks until smooth.
  3. Gradually pour the hot milk mixture into the egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the butter and vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the filling into the cooled pie crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  7. Place a piece of plastic wrap directly onto the surface of the filling to prevent a skin from forming.
  8. Refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is set.

Step 3: Prepare the Whipped Cream Topping

  1. In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream, confectioners’ sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  2. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled coconut filling.
  3. Optional: Garnish with toasted coconut flakes or shaved chocolate for an extra touch of elegance.

Serving and Storage:

This rich and creamy coconut cream pie is best served chilled. It makes for a delightful dessert after a flavorful dinner. Store any leftovers in the refrigerator, covered, for up to 3 days. The flavors will meld together even more, making each bite even more irresistible.

Conclusion:

Indulge in the tropical flavors and luxurious texture of this rich and creamy coconut cream pie. Its velvety coconut filling and buttery crust will surely captivate your senses and leave you craving for more. Whether it’s a special occasion or a casual gathering, this decadent dessert is a delightful finale to any meal. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to a slice of paradise with this irresistible coconut cream pie.
