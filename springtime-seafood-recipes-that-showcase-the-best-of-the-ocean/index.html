Springtime Seafood: Recipes That Showcase the Best of the Ocean


As the weather warms and the days grow longer, springtime brings with it a bounty of delicious seafood. From succulent shrimp to delicate scallops, the ocean offers an abundance of flavors that are perfect for the season. Whether you’re planning a light lunch or an elegant dinner party, these springtime seafood recipes are sure to impress your guests and tantalize your taste buds.

Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the garlic, olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the shrimp onto skewers and grill over medium heat for 2-3 minutes per side, or until pink and opaque.
  4. Serve the grilled shrimp skewers with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and enjoy!

Seared Scallops with Asparagus Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound sea scallops
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, melt 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Add the shallot and cook until softened.
  2. Add the Arborio rice to the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Pour in the white wine and cook until the liquid is absorbed.
  4. Gradually add the broth to the skillet, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring frequently until the rice is tender and creamy.
  5. In another skillet, melt the remaining butter over high heat. Pat the scallops dry and season with salt and pepper. Sear the scallops for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and opaque.
  6. In a pot of boiling water, blanch the asparagus for 2 minutes, then drain and add to the risotto along with the Parmesan cheese.
  7. Serve the seared scallops on a bed of asparagus risotto and enjoy this elegant springtime dish.

Citrus Glazed Grilled Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the orange juice, lime juice, honey, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the citrus glaze over them. Marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Grill the salmon for 5-6 minutes per side, or until it flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Serve the citrus glazed grilled salmon with a side of steamed vegetables for a light and refreshing springtime meal.

These springtime seafood recipes are perfect for ushering in the season with fresh, vibrant flavors. Whether you’re craving the smoky char of grilled shrimp, the delicate sweetness of seared scallops, or the zesty tang of citrus glazed salmon, these dishes are sure to delight your senses and elevate your springtime dining experience. So, head to your local fish market, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the best the ocean has to offer this spring!
