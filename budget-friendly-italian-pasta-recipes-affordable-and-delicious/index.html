Budget-Friendly Italian Pasta Recipes: Affordable and Delicious


It’s no secret that Italian cuisine is renowned for its rich flavors, aromas, and hearty portions. However, many people assume that cooking Italian dishes requires a hefty budget and a lot of time. But what if I told you that you can enjoy authentic Italian pasta recipes without breaking the bank or sacrificing flavor? In this post, we’ll explore some budget-friendly Italian pasta recipes that are both affordable and delicious.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

This classic Italian pasta dish is a staple in many Italian households. Spaghetti Aglio e Olio, which translates to “spaghetti with garlic and oil,” is a simple yet flavorful recipe that’s perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-low heat. Add the thinly sliced garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, for 4-5 minutes or until the garlic is golden brown and fragrant.
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet, tossing to combine with the garlic and oil.
  4. Season with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped parsley.

Cost: $1.50 per serving

Fettuccine with Tomato and Basil

This creamy and comforting pasta dish is a staple in many Italian restaurants. Fettuccine with Tomato and Basil is a simple yet impressive recipe that’s perfect for a special occasion or a cozy night in.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz fettuccine
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5-7 minutes or until the tomatoes start to release their juices.
  3. Add the chopped basil leaves to the skillet and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet, tossing to combine with the tomato and basil mixture.
  5. Season with salt, black pepper, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with additional basil leaves if desired.

Cost: $2.50 per serving

Penne with Mushrooms and Spinach

This hearty and flavorful pasta dish is a staple in many Italian households. Penne with Mushrooms and Spinach is a simple yet impressive recipe that’s perfect for a cozy night in or a special occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz penne
  • 1 cup mixed mushrooms (button, cremini, shiitake), sliced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the penne according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5-7 minutes or until the mushrooms release their moisture and start to brown.
  3. Add the fresh spinach leaves to the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, until wilted.
  4. Add the cooked penne to the skillet, tossing to combine with the mushroom and spinach mixture.
  5. Season with salt, black pepper, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with additional spinach leaves if desired.

Cost: $2.25 per serving

Conclusion

Italian pasta recipes don’t have to break the bank or require a lot of time. With these budget-friendly Italian pasta recipes, you can enjoy authentic and delicious Italian cuisine without sacrificing flavor or quality. Whether you’re a busy professional or a stay-at-home parent, these recipes are perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion. So go ahead, get creative, and start cooking up some delicious Italian pasta recipes!
