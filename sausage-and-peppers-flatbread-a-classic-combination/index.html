Sausage and Peppers Flatbread: A Classic Combination


There’s something undeniably satisfying about the combination of savory sausage, sweet bell peppers, and gooey melted cheese. This classic trio comes together in a delightful marriage of flavors and textures in the form of a Sausage and Peppers Flatbread. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal, a crowd-pleasing appetizer, or a tasty game day snack, this recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

For the Flatbread

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet instant yeast

For the Toppings

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 2 bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Flatbread Dough

    • In a large bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and instant yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    • Add the olive oil, flour, and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir until a dough forms.
    • Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  2. Cook the Sausage and Peppers

    • In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the Italian sausage, breaking it apart with a wooden spoon, until browned and cooked through. Remove the sausage from the skillet and set it aside.
    • In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and onions. Cook until softened and slightly caramelized, about 10 minutes.
    • Add the minced garlic, dried oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  3. Assemble and Bake the Flatbread

    • Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal portions.
    • Roll out each portion of dough into a rough rectangle or oval shape on a floured surface.
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet.
    • Spread half of the shredded mozzarella cheese over each piece of dough.
    • Top the cheese with the cooked sausage and pepper mixture.
    • Bake the flatbreads in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
    • Remove the flatbreads from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before slicing.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, use hot Italian sausage or add extra red pepper flakes.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese such as fontina, provolone, or gouda.
  • Add a drizzle of balsamic glaze or a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese before serving for extra flavor.

Enjoy the Classic Combination

This Sausage and Peppers Flatbread recipe offers a perfect balance of flavors and textures, making it a versatile and satisfying dish for any occasion. Whether you’re preparing it for a casual dinner at home or as a party appetizer, the combination of savory sausage, sweet peppers, and melted cheese on a crispy flatbread crust is sure to be a hit. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy this classic combination in a whole new way!
