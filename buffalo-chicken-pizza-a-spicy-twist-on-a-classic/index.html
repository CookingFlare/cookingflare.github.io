Buffalo Chicken Pizza: A Spicy Twist on a Classic


If you’re a fan of both pizza and spicy flavors, then Buffalo Chicken Pizza is a must-try dish that combines the best of both worlds. This delicious recipe takes the tangy heat of Buffalo chicken wings and transforms it into a mouthwatering pizza topping. Whether you’re hosting a game night, craving a hearty dinner, or simply want to spice up your pizza night, this recipe is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 1 package (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

For the Toppings:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup Buffalo sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/3 cup ranch or blue cheese dressing
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Cornmeal or flour, for dusting

Instructions

1. Prepare the Pizza Dough

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Stir the dough until it comes together, then turn it out onto a lightly floured surface.
  4. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.

2. Prepare the Buffalo Chicken

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the chicken breasts for 5-6 minutes per side until cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes before shredding it using two forks.
  5. In a bowl, toss the shredded chicken with Buffalo sauce until evenly coated.

3. Assemble and Bake the Pizza

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to heat.
  2. Punch down the pizza dough and divide it into two equal portions. Roll out each portion into a circle on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Sprinkle cornmeal or flour onto a pizza peel or the back of a baking sheet. Transfer the pizza dough onto the peel.
  4. Spread half of the ranch or blue cheese dressing evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella and cheddar cheeses over the dressing.
  6. Scatter half of the Buffalo chicken evenly over the cheese.
  7. Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before garnishing with sliced green onions and chopped cilantro, if desired.
  10. Repeat steps 3-9 with the remaining pizza dough and toppings.

4. Serve and Enjoy!

Buffalo Chicken Pizza is best enjoyed hot and fresh out of the oven. The combination of spicy Buffalo sauce, creamy dressing, and gooey melted cheese atop a crispy pizza crust is irresistible. Serve slices of this pizza with extra ranch or blue cheese dressing on the side for dipping, along with a side salad or celery sticks for a complete meal.

Tips for Success

  • Pizza Dough Consistency: Ensure your pizza dough is well-kneaded and properly risen for a light and airy crust.
  • Chicken Cooking: Grill the chicken until just cooked through to retain moisture and flavor.
  • Buffalo Sauce: Adjust the amount of Buffalo sauce to suit your heat preference—from mild to extra spicy.
  • Cheese Melting: Use a combination of mozzarella and cheddar cheeses for the perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Conclusion

Buffalo Chicken Pizza offers a delightful twist on the traditional pizza recipe, bringing together the zesty flavors of Buffalo chicken wings with the comfort of pizza. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or simply craving something different for dinner, this recipe is sure to satisfy. Experiment with different toppings or spice levels to personalize your pizza experience. Enjoy the process of creating this homemade pizza masterpiece and indulge in every spicy, cheesy bite!
