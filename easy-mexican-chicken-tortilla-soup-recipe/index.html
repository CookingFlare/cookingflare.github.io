Easy Mexican Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful soup that’s easy to make, look no further than this delicious Mexican Chicken Tortilla Soup recipe. Packed with traditional Mexican flavors and wholesome ingredients, this soup is perfect for a cozy night in or for entertaining guests. The best part? It’s simple to prepare and can be ready in no time. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this hearty and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish
  • Avocado, sliced, for garnish
  • Tortilla strips or crushed tortilla chips, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic, ground cumin, and chili powder to the pot, and stir for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes and chicken broth, and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  4. Add the chicken breasts to the pot and let them cook in the simmering liquid for about 20 minutes, or until they are fully cooked through.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  6. Stir in the black beans and frozen corn, and let the soup simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with chopped cilantro, sliced avocado, and tortilla strips or crushed tortilla chips.
  9. Serve the soup with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the top.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a diced jalapeno pepper along with the onion.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a small amount of crushed tortilla chips directly into the soup as it simmers.
  • This soup can easily be made in a slow cooker. Simply follow the recipe up to step 4, then transfer everything to a slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  • Feel free to customize the toppings based on your preferences. Sour cream, shredded cheese, or even a dollop of guacamole would make excellent additions.

Final Thoughts

This Easy Mexican Chicken Tortilla Soup is a fantastic meal option for busy weeknights or casual dinner parties. Its rich flavors and vibrant colors make it a feast for the senses, and the simplicity of its preparation ensures that anyone can enjoy it. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, get cooking, and savor the delightful taste of Mexico in a comforting bowl of soup. Enjoy!
