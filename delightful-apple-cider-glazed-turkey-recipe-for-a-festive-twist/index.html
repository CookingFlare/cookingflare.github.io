Delightful Apple Cider Glazed Turkey Recipe for a Festive Twist


The holiday season is the perfect time to indulge in a delicious and festive meal, and what better way to celebrate than with a beautifully glazed turkey? This delightful apple cider glazed turkey recipe is a showstopper that will leave your guests raving. The combination of succulent turkey meat and a sweet and tangy apple cider glaze creates a dish that is both comforting and elegant. Whether you’re hosting a festive dinner party or simply want to elevate your holiday meal, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Turkey:

  • 1 whole turkey (12-14 pounds)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 carrots, roughly chopped
  • 2 stalks of celery, roughly chopped
  • 4 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 4 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • 4 sprigs of fresh sage
  • 1 lemon, quartered
  • 1 head of garlic, halved

For the Glaze:

  • 2 cups apple cider
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prep the Turkey

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Remove the giblets from the turkey cavity and pat dry with paper towels.
  3. Season the turkey generously with salt and pepper, both inside and out.
  4. Stuff the turkey with the quartered onion, chopped carrots, celery, thyme, rosemary, sage, lemon, and garlic.

Step 2: Roast the Turkey

  1. Place the turkey on a rack in a roasting pan, breast side up.
  2. Roast the turkey in the preheated oven for 2 hours, then tent it with foil to prevent over-browning.
  3. Continue roasting for another 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) in the thickest part of the thigh.

Step 3: Make the Glaze

  1. While the turkey is roasting, prepare the glaze.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the apple cider, maple syrup, apple cider vinegar, and Dijon mustard.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the glaze has thickened.
  4. Stir in the butter and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Glaze the Turkey

  1. During the last 30 minutes of roasting, begin brushing the turkey with the apple cider glaze every 10 minutes.
  2. Continue glazing until the turkey is beautifully caramelized and the glaze is sticky.

Step 5: Rest and Serve

  1. Once the turkey reaches the desired temperature, remove it from the oven and tent it with foil.
  2. Allow the turkey to rest for at least 30 minutes before carving.
  3. Serve the apple cider glazed turkey with the remaining glaze on the side for extra flavor.

Tips for Success

  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure the turkey is cooked to perfection.
  • Basting the turkey with the glaze regularly will result in a beautifully lacquered finish.
  • Letting the turkey rest before carving will help the juices redistribute, ensuring a moist and flavorful bird.

Final Thoughts

This apple cider glazed turkey recipe is a delightful way to add a festive twist to your holiday meal. The combination of flavors from the apple cider, maple syrup, and Dijon mustard creates a glaze that perfectly complements the succulent turkey meat. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice cook, this recipe is sure to impress your guests and become a holiday favorite. So, this holiday season, elevate your dinner table with this delightful apple cider glazed turkey and create unforgettable culinary memories.
