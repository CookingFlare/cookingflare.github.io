Creepy Crawly Surprise Marshmallow Pops: Halloween Candy Making


Are you ready to take your Halloween treats to another level of excitement and creativity? Whether you’re a kid or a kid at heart, the Creepy Crawly Surprise Marshmallow Pops will bring a fun twist to the typical Halloween candy classics. Prepare to dive into a world of bug-inspired, marshmallow-filled fun!

The Ingredients:

To make these delightful creepy crawly creations, gather these key ingredients because you’ll want the treats to be spectacular:

  • Licorice twists or black string licorice (for legs)
  • Mini marshmallows
  • White or dark chocolate melted (depending on how brave you want to get with your decorations)
  • Lollipop sticks
  • Bug-shaped cookie cutters
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Finely crushed Halloween candy or cookies (optional, for additional crunch)

How to Make It:

Step 1: Marshmallow Pops

  1. Allow a grown-up to help you melt the chocolate using a double boiler or microwave, stirring frequently to prevent burning. Chill in the fridge for a few minutes.

  2. Meanwhile, cut a bug shape into a marshmallow. To prevent the marshmallow pieces from becoming too sticky, lightly grease the cookie cutter with butter or oil first.

  3. Thread each mini marshmallow onto a lollipop stick, taking care not to overcrowd the stick.

  4. If you wish, give some pops a colorful surprise by adding food coloring to the melted chocolate. Stir it in until it reaches the desired hue.

Step 2: The Legs

  1. Help the grown-up prepare a dipping sauce for the licorice legs by mixing equal parts melted butter and white sugar. This will help the licorice stick to the marshmallow pops.

  2. Cut licorice into small, inch-long pieces for the legs,sticks or use black string licorice in smaller sections.

  3. Dip each piece of licorice into the sugar and butter mixture, and then quickly attach it to the marshmallow pop. Repeat until your pop has the desired number of legs.

Step 3: Chocolate Coating

  1. Let the grown-up coat each marshmallow pop in chocolate, starting with a quick thin layer to set the base.

  2. Once the first layer sets, a second thicker layer of chocolate will be applied for a smooth exterior.

  3. For the less adventurous, use white chocolate and for the more daring, get a bit wild with dark chocolate – either variety is entirely up to you!

Step 4: Decorating

Give free reign to your imagination as you create the perfect scary spider, creepy bug, or unearthly monster for your Creepy Crawly Surprise Marshmallow Pops. The kids will love decorating them, and a variety of crushed Halloween candies or finely crushed cookies can be added for added texture and color.

Step 5: Chill Time

To ensure your creations harden properly and are ready for snacking, let them sit in the refrigerator.

Serving:

After the chilling time has passed, it’s time to enjoy your Creepy Crawly Surprise Marshmallow Pops! With a few final fluctuations of eye-catching decorations, these autumn-spooky delicacies will become your Halloween party’s star attraction.

These marshmallow pops are a treat everyone will remember, with innocent marshmallows disguised as creepy crawlers sure to send delight shivers down spines. Happy Halloween candy making and enjoy the wickedly sweet treats you conjure up!
