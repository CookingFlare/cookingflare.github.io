Divine Chocolate Cherry Scones: A Fruity Morning Indulgence


There’s something truly divine about starting your morning with a delectable treat, and these chocolate cherry scones are the perfect indulgence. Imagine sinking your teeth into a warm, buttery scone filled with pockets of rich chocolate and bursts of sweet, juicy cherries. It’s a delightful way to kickstart your day, and the best part is, you can easily whip up a batch in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Scones:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen cherries, pitted and halved

For the Glaze (Optional):

  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. Incorporate Butter: Add the cold butter pieces to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, egg, and vanilla extract.

  5. Bring It Together: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Add Chocolate and Cherries: Gently fold in the chocolate chips and cherry halves until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Shape the Scones: Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a circle, about 1-inch thick. Using a sharp knife, cut the circle into 8 triangular wedges.

  8. Bake: Place the scones onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 15-18 minutes, or until they are golden brown.

  9. Glaze (Optional): If desired, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract to create a simple glaze. Drizzle it over the cooled scones for an extra touch of sweetness.

Tips and Tricks

  • Keep it Cold: It’s crucial to use cold butter and cream to achieve that perfect flaky texture in your scones.
  • Don’t Overwork the Dough: Overmixing the dough can result in tough scones, so handle it gently.
  • Customize: Feel free to swap the cherries for other fruits like blueberries or raspberries, and switch up the chocolate for white chocolate if you prefer.

Conclusion

These divine chocolate cherry scones are a delightful way to elevate your breakfast or brunch spread. Whether enjoyed with a cup of hot coffee or a refreshing glass of milk, the combination of rich chocolate and sweet cherries is a match made in scone heaven. So, the next time you’re craving a fruity morning indulgence, consider treating yourself to the simple pleasure of freshly baked scones straight from your own oven.

With just a few basic ingredients and a little bit of love, you can create a morning treat that’s truly special. So, why not give these divine chocolate cherry scones a try and make your mornings a bit more indulgent?
