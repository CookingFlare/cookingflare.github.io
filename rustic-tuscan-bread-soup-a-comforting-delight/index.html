Rustic Tuscan Bread Soup: A Comforting Delight


There’s nothing quite like a hearty bowl of soup to warm your soul and satisfy your hunger. And when it comes to comfort food, Rustic Tuscan Bread Soup is an absolute winner. This traditional Italian dish is a delightful blend of simple ingredients that come together to create a truly satisfying and flavorful meal. So, if you’re ready to embark on a culinary adventure, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups day-old Italian bread, torn into small pieces
  • 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juice) to the pot and cook for an additional 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 15 minutes.

  4. Stir in the dried thyme and rosemary, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Allow the soup to simmer for another 5 minutes, allowing the herbs to infuse their flavors into the broth.

  5. Add the torn bread pieces to the pot and stir well to combine. Let the soup simmer for an additional 10 minutes, or until the bread has softened and absorbed some of the broth.

  6. Remove the pot from the heat and let the soup cool slightly before serving. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with torn basil leaves for an extra burst of freshness.

Serving Suggestions

Rustic Tuscan Bread Soup is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a variety of accompaniments. Here are a few serving suggestions to enhance your culinary experience:

  • Drizzle a bit of extra-virgin olive oil over the soup just before serving for an added richness and depth of flavor.
  • Sprinkle freshly grated Parmesan cheese on top of each bowl for a salty and nutty kick.
  • Serve the soup alongside a fresh green salad or a crusty baguette for a complete and satisfying meal.
  • For a heartier version, add cooked Italian sausage or diced pancetta to the soup during the cooking process.

Conclusion

Rustic Tuscan Bread Soup is a true delight for the senses. With its comforting flavors, simple ingredients, and ease of preparation, it’s no wonder why this dish has become a staple in Tuscan cuisine. So, the next time you’re craving a warm and satisfying meal, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation and personalization. Feel free to tweak this recipe to suit your preferences and dietary restrictions. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, Rustic Tuscan Bread Soup is a recipe that anyone can master. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of this delightful soup fill your home. Bon appétit!
