10 Quick and Easy Pasta Recipes for Busy Weeknights


When life gets busy, finding quick and easy dinner ideas becomes essential. Pasta is one of the most versatile ingredients to work with, and it can be transformed into countless delicious dishes in no time. If you’re looking for weeknight dinner inspiration that doesn’t compromise on flavor or nutrition, you’ve come to the right place. Here are 10 quick and easy pasta recipes that will help you whip up tasty meals with minimal effort.

1. One-Pot Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Garlic, butter, and Parmesan cheese—what’s not to love? This one-pot garlic Parmesan pasta recipe is not only simple but also packed with flavor. Perfect for a weeknight meal, it requires just a few basic ingredients and minimal cleanup.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine work best)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cook the pasta according to the package instructions, but reduce the cooking time by 2 minutes. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  2. In the same pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring to a simmer. Let it cook for 3-4 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine.
  4. Add the cream, then stir in the Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the cooked pasta back into the pot and toss it to coat with the sauce. Continue cooking for another 1-2 minutes, until the pasta absorbs some of the sauce.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.

Why it’s great: This dish combines creamy, cheesy goodness in under 20 minutes, making it an ideal choice for busy nights.

2. Pasta Primavera

Pasta Primavera is a colorful, fresh, and light dish that’s perfect for a quick weeknight meal. Packed with veggies and tossed in a simple olive oil and garlic sauce, this recipe is both healthy and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (penne or spaghetti works well)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the zucchini, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, and broccoli. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the garlic and Italian seasoning, then cook for another minute.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta into the pan with the vegetables. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan.

Why it’s great: It’s a healthy, veggie-packed dish that’s quick and light, making it perfect for a nutritious weeknight meal.

3. Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta

Rich, creamy, and comforting, this tomato basil pasta is a crowd-pleaser. The tangy tomatoes, fresh basil, and creamy sauce create a delicious harmony that’s perfect for any busy evening.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package directions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for another minute, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Lower the heat and pour in the heavy cream. Stir to combine and let it simmer for another 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add the chopped basil, salt, and pepper. Toss the pasta into the sauce and stir to coat evenly.
  7. Serve with grated Parmesan.

Why it’s great: The creamy tomato basil sauce is rich yet balanced, making it a flavorful, comforting dish you can make in just 20 minutes.

4. Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

For seafood lovers, this lemon garlic shrimp pasta is an easy and flavorful dish that’s light, fresh, and tangy. The garlic butter sauce pairs beautifully with shrimp and pasta, making it a delicious meal in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (linguine or spaghetti)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and opaque.
  3. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add the lemon juice, zest, and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Toss the pasta into the skillet and mix to coat in the sauce. Add the shrimp back in and toss everything together.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.

Why it’s great: This dish is bursting with fresh flavors, and the shrimp cooks quickly, making it ideal for a weeknight dinner.

5. Pasta with Pesto and Cherry Tomatoes

Pesto is a classic Italian sauce made with fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan, and olive oil. Tossing it with pasta and sweet cherry tomatoes results in a simple, satisfying dish that’s perfect for busy nights.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (spaghetti or farfalle)
  • 1 cup fresh basil pesto (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package directions, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the pasta with the pesto sauce until evenly coated.
  3. Add the halved cherry tomatoes and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Why it’s great: Pesto sauce brings a burst of fresh, vibrant flavor to the pasta, and the whole dish is ready in less than 15 minutes.

6. Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Aglio e Olio is a classic Italian dish made with spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes. It’s simple but packed with flavor, making it a perfect option for a quick, no-fuss dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Grated Parmesan for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic slices and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the red pepper flakes and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat in the oil and garlic.
  5. Season with salt, then garnish with fresh parsley and Parmesan if desired.

Why it’s great: This dish is quick, simple, and delicious, with a perfect balance of garlic and spice.

7. Baked Ziti with Sausage

Baked ziti is the ultimate comfort food. This version is made with Italian sausage and a rich tomato sauce, baked with melted mozzarella cheese for a hearty, satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz ziti pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb Italian sausage (mild or spicy)
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 ½ cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ziti pasta according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up into smaller pieces as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the marinara sauce and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, combine the cooked ziti with the sausage sauce. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Why it’s great: Baked ziti is a classic family favorite, and it’s an easy way to prepare a filling dinner with minimal effort.

8. Carbonara Pasta

Carbonara is a rich and creamy pasta dish made with eggs, Parmesan cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It’s a quick, comforting meal that only takes a few minutes to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 4 oz pancetta, diced
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package directions, then drain, reserving ½ cup of pasta water.
  2. In a skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the skillet with the pancetta, then remove from heat. Add the egg mixture, and stir quickly to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed to adjust the sauce consistency.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.

Why it’s great: This creamy, rich pasta dish is perfect for when you’re craving something indulgent but need a quick meal.

9. Pasta with Spinach and Ricotta

This light yet satisfying pasta is made with fresh spinach and ricotta cheese. It’s a healthy, delicious way to get some greens into your meal while enjoying a creamy pasta dish.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (penne or rotini)
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, sauté the garlic in olive oil for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the spinach and cook until wilted, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Stir in the ricotta cheese and Parmesan cheese, and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta into the skillet and mix everything together. Season with salt and pepper.

Why it’s great: This dish is light yet creamy and packed with nutritious spinach, making it perfect for a quick, healthy weeknight dinner.

10. Pasta alla Caprese

Pasta alla Caprese is inspired by the classic Italian salad, combining pasta with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. It’s a refreshing and easy dish, perfect for those hot summer nights when you want something light and flavorful.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (fusilli or rotini)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella, cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, combine the cooked pasta with the cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and fresh basil.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss everything together and serve immediately.

Why it’s great: This dish is light, fresh, and full of vibrant flavors, making it an ideal choice for a quick, satisfying meal.

These 10 quick and easy pasta recipes are perfect for busy weeknights when you need to get dinner on the table fast. Whether you’re craving creamy sauces, fresh veggies, or rich, comforting dishes, there’s something here for everyone. With just a few ingredients and a little time, you can create delicious, satisfying meals that the whole family will love. Happy cooking!
