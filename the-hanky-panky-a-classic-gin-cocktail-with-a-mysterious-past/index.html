The Hanky Panky: A Classic Gin Cocktail with a Mysterious Past


If there’s one cocktail that embodies the intrigue and allure of the classic cocktail era, it’s the Hanky Panky. This enigmatic drink has a mysterious past, full of twists and turns that have contributed to its legendary status in the world of mixology. Join me as we delve into the history, ingredients, and art of crafting the perfect Hanky Panky cocktail.

Unveiling the Origins

The origins of the Hanky Panky cocktail are shrouded in mystery, adding an air of mystique to this classic concoction. It is believed that the cocktail was created by Ada Coleman, the head bartender at the American Bar in the Savoy Hotel, London, during the early 20th century. Ada, known for her pioneering role as a female bartender in a male-dominated industry, crafted the Hanky Panky as a tribute to her friend and fellow bartender, Sir Charles Hawtrey.

The Intriguing Ingredients

To recreate the magic of the Hanky Panky, you’ll need to gather a few essential ingredients. The star of the show is gin, a spirit that brings depth and complexity to the cocktail. Vermouth, particularly sweet vermouth, adds a luscious sweetness that balances the botanical notes of the gin. Lastly, a few dashes of Fernet-Branca, an aromatic bitter liqueur, provide the cocktail with its distinctive and complex flavor profile.

Crafting the Perfect Hanky Panky

Now, let’s embark on the journey of crafting the perfect Hanky Panky cocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • 1 1/2 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Fernet-Branca
  • Orange peel, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Add the gin, sweet vermouth, and Fernet-Branca to the mixing glass.
  3. Stir the mixture well to chill the ingredients and achieve proper dilution.
  4. Strain the cocktail into a chilled coupe glass.
  5. Express the oils from an orange peel over the cocktail and use it as a garnish.

By following these simple steps, you can create a Hanky Panky cocktail that pays homage to its intriguing past, while delighting your taste buds with its complex and delightful flavors.

The Enigma of the Hanky Panky

The Hanky Panky cocktail continues to captivate cocktail enthusiasts and mixologists alike, thanks to its mysterious origins and alluring flavor profile. Its timeless appeal serves as a testament to the artistry and creativity that characterize the world of cocktail crafting.

Next time you find yourself in the mood for a classic cocktail with a touch of enigma, consider mixing up a Hanky Panky. As you savor each sip, you’ll be transported to a bygone era, where the clinking of glasses and the art of mixology reigned supreme.

In conclusion, the Hanky Panky is more than just a cocktail; it’s a tribute to the golden age of mixology and a testament to the enduring allure of classic cocktails with a mysterious past. So, raise your glass and toast to the timeless elegance of the Hanky Panky cocktail!
