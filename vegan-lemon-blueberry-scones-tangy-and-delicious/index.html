Vegan Lemon Blueberry Scones: Tangy and Delicious


Are you ready to elevate your vegan baking game with a delightful treat that combines the zesty freshness of lemon with the sweet burst of blueberries? Look no further than these Vegan Lemon Blueberry Scones! Whether you’re a seasoned vegan baker or just dipping your toes into the world of plant-based desserts, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil (solid, not melted)
  • 1/2 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C) and lining a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Whisk the ingredients together until well combined.

  3. Incorporate Coconut Oil: Add the solid coconut oil to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, work the coconut oil into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Add Wet Ingredients: Pour in the full-fat coconut milk, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Gently stir the mixture until a soft dough forms.

  5. Fold in Blueberries: Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to crush them and distribute them evenly throughout the dough.

  6. Shape and Cut: Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a circle about 1-inch thick. Use a sharp knife to cut the circle into 8 equal wedges.

  7. Bake to Perfection: Carefully transfer the scones to the prepared baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until the scones are golden brown and cooked through.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the scones from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes. Serve the scones warm or at room temperature, and savor every tangy, blueberry-filled bite!

Why You’ll Love These Scones:

  • Vibrant Flavors: The combination of zesty lemon and juicy blueberries creates a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.

  • Tender Texture: These scones boast a tender crumb that is both satisfying and comforting, making them a perfect treat for any time of day.

  • Vegan-Friendly: By using plant-based ingredients, these scones are not only delicious but also align with a vegan lifestyle, allowing you to indulge guilt-free.

  • Versatile: Whether enjoyed with a cup of tea in the morning or as a sweet afternoon pick-me-up, these scones are versatile and suitable for any occasion.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and embark on a vegan baking adventure with these delectable Vegan Lemon Blueberry Scones. Your taste buds will thank you, and your friends and family will be begging for the recipe!
