Quick and Easy Fourth of July BBQ Recipe Ideas for Busy Cooks


Are you a busy cook looking for quick and easy Fourth of July BBQ recipe ideas? Look no further! We’ve got you covered with these simple and delicious recipes that are sure to impress your guests. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout or attending a potluck, these recipes will make your Fourth of July celebration a hit. So fire up the grill and get ready to impress with these mouthwatering dishes!

Grilled BBQ Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces and thread them onto the skewers.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the BBQ sauce mixture onto the chicken skewers, coating them evenly.
  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Grilled Corn on the Cob

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Peel back the husks of the corn, but leave them attached at the base.
  3. Remove the silk from the corn and discard.
  4. Spread the softened butter evenly over the corn.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Pull the husks back up over the corn, covering it completely.
  7. Place the corn on the preheated grill and cook for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the corn is tender and slightly charred.
  8. Remove from the grill, let cool for a few minutes, then peel back the husks and serve.

Grilled Watermelon Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of watermelon, about 1-inch thick
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Balsamic glaze, for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the watermelon slices directly on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, until grill marks appear.
  3. Remove the watermelon from the grill and let cool for a few minutes.
  4. Cut the grilled watermelon into bite-sized pieces and place in a large serving bowl.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and chopped mint leaves over the watermelon.
  6. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  7. Toss gently to combine and serve immediately.

Grilled Pineapple Sundaes

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of pineapple, about 1-inch thick
  • 4 scoops of vanilla ice cream
  • Caramel sauce, for drizzling
  • Toasted coconut flakes, for garnish (optional)
  • Maraschino cherries, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the pineapple slices directly on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, until grill marks appear.
  3. Remove the pineapple from the grill and let cool for a few minutes.
  4. Place a scoop of vanilla ice cream on each grilled pineapple slice.
  5. Drizzle with caramel sauce.
  6. Sprinkle with toasted coconut flakes and top with a maraschino cherry, if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

These quick and easy Fourth of July BBQ recipe ideas are perfect for busy cooks who want to spend more time enjoying the festivities and less time in the kitchen. With minimal preparation and maximum flavor, these dishes are sure to be a hit at your Fourth of July celebration. So fire up the grill and get ready to impress your guests with these delicious recipes. Happy Fourth of July!
