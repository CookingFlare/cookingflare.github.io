Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese


Are you looking for a comforting and delicious twist on traditional mac and cheese? Look no further! This Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese recipe is the perfect combination of creamy, cheesy goodness with a hint of sweetness from the butternut squash. It’s a fantastic way to sneak in some extra veggies while indulging in a classic favorite. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound macaroni pasta
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs (optional for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Roast the Butternut Squash: Place the diced butternut squash on the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and roast in the oven for about 25-30 minutes or until tender. Once done, remove from the oven and set aside.

  3. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the macaroni pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  4. Prepare the Cheese Sauce: In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour to create a roux, cooking for about 2 minutes. Slowly pour in the milk while whisking continuously until the sauce thickens.

  5. Add the Cheeses and Seasonings: Stir in the cheddar and mozzarella cheeses until melted and smooth. Add the garlic powder, paprika, nutmeg, and roasted butternut squash. Mix until well combined.

  6. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the cheese sauce, stirring until the pasta is coated evenly.

  7. Bake the Mac and Cheese: Transfer the mixture to a baking dish. If desired, sprinkle breadcrumbs on top for a crunchy topping. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once out of the oven, let it cool slightly before serving. Garnish with a sprinkle of paprika or fresh herbs if desired. Enjoy the creamy and flavorful Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese!

Conclusion:

This Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese recipe is a delightful way to elevate a classic dish with a nutritious twist. The creamy texture, cheesy goodness, and subtle sweetness of the butternut squash come together to create a memorable and satisfying meal for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this recipe is sure to impress. Give it a try and savor the delicious flavors in every bite!

Remember, cooking is all about creativity, so feel free to adjust the seasonings or cheese types to suit your taste preferences. Dive into the world of culinary delights with this Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese recipe and watch it become a new favorite at your dining table!
