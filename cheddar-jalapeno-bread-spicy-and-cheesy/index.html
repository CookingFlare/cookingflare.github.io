Cheddar Jalapeno Bread: Spicy and Cheesy


Are you ready to elevate your bread game with a tantalizing combination of sharp cheddar and fiery jalapenos? Look no further than this Cheddar Jalapeno Bread recipe that promises to bring a perfect balance of spiciness and cheesiness to your table.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 2-3 jalapenos, finely chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and greasing a loaf pan with butter or cooking spray.

  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Mix well to ensure even distribution of the ingredients.

  3. Add wet ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the melted butter, milk, and egg until well combined.

  4. Combine the mixtures: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

  5. Fold in the cheese and jalapenos: Gently fold in the shredded cheddar cheese and chopped jalapenos into the batter. The combination of sharp cheese and spicy jalapenos will add a kick to your bread.

  6. Bake the bread: Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for about 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Cool and serve: Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes. Then transfer the bread to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Tips:

  • For an extra spicy kick, leave the jalapeno seeds in while chopping.
  • You can customize the bread by adding herbs like cilantro or green onions for more flavor.
  • This bread pairs perfectly with soups, salads, or as a delicious snack on its own.

With its golden crust, oozing melted cheese, and a hint of heat from the jalapenos, this Cheddar Jalapeno Bread is sure to be a hit at your next gathering. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is straightforward and yields impressive results every time. Give it a try and savor the irresistible combination of spicy and cheesy flavors in every bite!

Remember, the key to a successful Cheddar Jalapeno Bread lies in the quality of your ingredients and the love you put into baking it. Enjoy the process, experiment with variations, and share this delightful recipe with your friends and family. Happy baking!
