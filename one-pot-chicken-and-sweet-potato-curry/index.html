One Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Curry


Curries are a staple in many households, and for good reason. They are flavorful, versatile, and can be customized to suit different tastes and preferences. If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free curry recipe, then you’re in luck! Today, I’m going to share with you my recipe for One Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Curry. This recipe is not only easy to make, but it’s also packed with nutrients and bursting with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the curry powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Mix well to create a spice blend.

  3. Add the chicken to the pot and sprinkle the spice blend over it. Stir well to coat the chicken pieces with the spices.

  4. Cook the chicken until lightly browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. This will help seal in the flavors and give the curry a rich taste.

  5. Add the cubed sweet potatoes, coconut milk, and chicken broth to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let the curry simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sweet potatoes are tender.

  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If you prefer a spicier curry, you can add more chili powder or a dash of hot sauce.

  8. Serve the One Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Curry hot, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves. This curry pairs perfectly with steamed rice or naan bread.

Final Thoughts

There you have it - a simple and delicious One Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Curry recipe that will surely satisfy your taste buds. The combination of tender chicken, sweet potatoes, and aromatic spices creates a comforting and flavorful dish that is perfect for any occasion.

Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking. You can add vegetables like bell peppers, peas, or spinach to make it even more nutritious. Additionally, if you prefer a creamier curry, you can substitute some of the chicken broth with more coconut milk.

This one-pot curry is not only a time-saver but also a crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. So why not give it a try and let the aromatic flavors transport you to a culinary paradise? Happy cooking!
