Vegan 'Chicken' Frenzy: 15 Mouthwatering Fakeaway Recipes to Satisfy Your Cravings


Are you a fan of the classic taste of chicken but looking to break free from consuming animal products? Dive into this vegan ‘chicken’ frenzy with an assortment of comforting and satisfying recipes that are sure to tease your taste buds. No longer do you need to limit your culinary adventures!

In this collection, we’ll explore 15 scrumptious fakeaway dishes that can bring that much-needed twist to your beloved chicken favorites. Lose yourself in a world of mouthwatering flavors and tantalizing textures as we share these vegan ‘chicken’ trends that are bound to be a hit whether you’re a flexitarian, a committed vegan, or simply someone with a curious palate.

Without further ado, let’s jump right into our magical vegan ‘chicken’ experience!

1. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Nuggets

There’s something truly nostalgic about chicken nuggets. Why not recreate that feeling without the chicken? These crispy vegan nuggets are made from seitan, seasoned to perfection, and coated in a pleasantly crunchy breadcrumb mixture. Delight your taste buds with a healthy twist on an old classic.

2. Vegan ‘Chicken’ and Waffles

The perfect combination of sweet and savory, vegan ‘chicken’ and waffles are incredibly indulgent yet surprisingly simple to whip up. Our crispy, spiced vegan ‘chicken’ goes swimmingly with a mouthwatering glaze, glazed on top of pillowy waffles. Indulge!

3. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Caesar Salad

Classic, refreshing, and packed with nutrients, this salad is made even more irresistible with the addition of our homemade vegan ‘chicken.’ Let crunchy romaine lettuce, zesty croutons, and creamy avocado be the vessels for this umami-packed fakeaway delight.

4. ‘Chik’n’ Parmesan

Hearty, comforting, and bursting with flavor, this vegan ‘chicken’ parmesan is quite simply divine. Who says comfort is off-limits? Dive into a warming bowl of pasta, drowning in our juicy ‘chicken’ pieces, covered generously in tangy tomato sauce and gooey, melted vegan cheese.

5. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Tacos

Tacos are the ultimate fakeaway, with endless combinations and adaptability. Opt for our vegan ‘chicken’ that is spiced traditionally and topped with a zesty pico de gallo, avocado, and tangy cashew cheese crema. Excite your senses with every bite!

6. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Curry

A popular dish in many households, our vegan ‘chicken’ curry features a tender and flavorful heart, nestled in an aromatic coconut-based sauce. Authentic Indian spices and an abundance of fresh vegetables create a flavorsome combination that leaves you wanting more.

7. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Salad Wraps

For a light and healthy midweek meal, our vegan ‘chicken’ salad wraps are an ideal choice. Featuring nutty freekeh or quinoa, copious amounts of veggies, and seasoned with herbs and spices, this is a filling, refreshing alternative for those hot summer days.

8. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Pot Pie

If comfort food is what you’re after, our vegan ‘chicken’ pot pie is sure to hit the spot. Hearty and satisfying, this dish features our meaty vegan ‘chicken’ bathing in a lusciously creamy sauce, encased in flaky, buttery vegan pastry.

9. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Scramble

For breakfast enthusiasts, a delicious vegan ‘chicken’ scramble is sure to tantalize your taste buds. Featuring savory tempeh, seasoned to mimic the taste and texture of scrambled eggs, complete this dish with your preferred vegetables and a slice of velvety vegan cheese. Bon app√©tit!

10. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Noodle Soup

When it’s been a long day, or you’re simply in need of coziness, our vegan ‘chicken’ noodle soup is a go-to remedy. Rich, comforting, and packed with wholesome ingredients, this heartwarming dish is perfect for sharing with your loved ones.

11. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Alfredo

Indulge in a luxurious vegan ‘chicken’ Alfredo that is as delightful to the eyes as it is to the palate. The combination of al dente pasta, velvety ‘chicken,’ and silken vegan cream sauce is sure to make your taste buds swoon.

12. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Fajitas

Head to your nearest grill and experience a smoky, sizzling, and zesty vegan ‘chicken’ fajita. Flavorful and packed with fresh ingredients, these sizzling fajitas are sure to become a favorite weeknight meal or delicious weekend celebration, shared amongst friends and family.

13. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Caesar Dressing

Recreate your favorite restaurant salad dressing at home with this Vegan ‘Chicken’ Caesar Dressing recipe. Packed with flavor and rich in texture, use it to give your romaine, croutons, and parmesan a wholesome twist. It’s as simple as it is delish!

14. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Satay Skewers

Bring the flavors of Southeast Asia to your very own kitchen with these spicy and fragrant vegan ‘chicken’ satay skewers. Serve them up with a irresistible peanut sauce for a show-stopping dish that’s both nutritious and flavorful. Barbecue season awaits!

15. Vegan ‘Chicken’ Soup Dumplings (Jiaozi)

Embrace Asian cuisine in all its vegan glory with these delicious Vegan ‘Chicken’ Soup Dumplings. A delightful combination of homemade vegan ‘chicken,’ tender dumpling wrappers, and an aromatic broth, these authentic dumplings are bound to become your new go-to dumpling recipe.

In conclusion, finding plant-based alternatives that cater to those serious about their vegan lifestyle or simply those who want a break from meat has never been easier. From classic comfort food to bold global flavors, these vegan ‘chicken’ recipes are designed to exceed expectations. Indulge guilt-free and keep coming back for more. Happy cooking!
