Moist and Fluffy Vanilla Cupcakes: The Perfect Base for Any Frosting


If you’re a fan of classic, timeless desserts, then vanilla cupcakes are likely a staple in your baking repertoire. These delightful treats offer a perfect canvas for a variety of frostings, making them a versatile and beloved choice for any occasion. In this recipe, we’ll dive into the art of creating moist and fluffy vanilla cupcakes that serve as the ideal base for any frosting you desire.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¼ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup whole milk

For the Frosting (Optional):

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3-4 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2-3 tablespoons heavy cream or milk

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cupcake Batter

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Combine the sour cream and milk in a separate bowl or measuring cup.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture, alternating with the sour cream and milk mixture. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

Step 2: Baking the Cupcakes

  1. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.
  2. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  3. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and allow them to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 3: Optional - Preparing the Frosting

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar, one cup at a time, beating well after each addition.
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract and heavy cream or milk, adjusting the consistency as needed.
  4. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them using a piping bag or offset spatula.

Tips for Success

  • Ensure that your butter, eggs, and sour cream are at room temperature for the best incorporation and texture.
  • Be mindful not to overmix the cupcake batter, as this can result in a dense and tough texture.
  • Experiment with different frosting flavors and decorations to customize your cupcakes for any occasion.

Conclusion

With their tender crumb and delicate vanilla flavor, these moist and fluffy cupcakes are the perfect foundation for a myriad of frosting creations. Whether you opt for a classic buttercream, a tangy cream cheese frosting, or a rich chocolate ganache, these cupcakes will provide a delightful base for your culinary imagination. Embrace the versatility of this timeless treat and delight your friends and family with these heavenly vanilla cupcakes.

So, go ahead and whip up a batch of these delightful vanilla cupcakes and let your creativity run wild with the frosting. After all, the perfect cupcake is just a recipe away!
