Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Kabobs Recipe


If you’re in the mood for a taste of Italy with a twist, then these Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs are just the ticket. This recipe brings together the flavors of Italian cuisine with the fun and flair of kabobs, making it a perfect choice for a summer barbecue or a cozy indoor meal. The combination of tender chicken, colorful veggies, and zesty Italian seasoning creates a dish that’s bursting with flavor and visual appeal. Plus, the convenience of cooking kabobs makes this recipe a winner for busy weeknights or laid-back weekend gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 8 ounces cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken chunks to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated, and let them marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.
  3. While the chicken is marinating, prepare the vegetables by chopping the bell peppers, red onion, and zucchini into chunks and slicing the cherry tomatoes in half.
  4. Once the chicken has marinated, thread the marinated chicken, bell peppers, red onion, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes onto the skewers in an alternating pattern, creating colorful and appetizing kabobs.
  5. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. If using wooden skewers, remember to soak them in water for at least 30 minutes before threading the ingredients to prevent them from burning on the grill.
  6. Place the assembled kabobs on the preheated grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  7. Once the kabobs are cooked, transfer them to a serving platter and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs are delicious on their own, but they can also be served with a variety of accompaniments to complete the meal. Consider serving them over a bed of fluffy couscous, alongside a vibrant green salad, or with a side of creamy garlic mashed potatoes for a hearty and satisfying feast. A dollop of tangy tzatziki or a drizzle of zesty chimichurri sauce can add an extra kick of flavor to these kabobs, elevating them to a gourmet dining experience.

Final Thoughts

With their vibrant colors, tantalizing aromas, and mouthwatering flavors, these Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs are a delightful addition to any dining table. Whether you’re hosting a summer soirée, planning a cozy family dinner, or simply craving a taste of Italy with a modern twist, these kabobs are sure to impress. They’re versatile, easy to prepare, and a feast for the senses, making them a must-try recipe for anyone who appreciates the art of flavorful and visually stunning cuisine.

So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and let the culinary magic unfold with these Italian-style chicken and vegetable kabobs. Buon appetito!
