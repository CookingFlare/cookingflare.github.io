Mediterranean-Inspired Wraps and Sandwiches: Portable and Delicious


When it comes to a quick, flavorful, and portable meal, Mediterranean-inspired wraps and sandwiches are hard to beat. Packed with vibrant colors, bold flavors, and wholesome ingredients, these portable delights are perfect for picnics, lunch on the go, or a light and refreshing dinner. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering recipes for Mediterranean-inspired wraps and sandwiches that are not only delicious but also easy to make.

Classic Greek Gyro Wrap

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 30 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. thinly sliced lamb or chicken
  • 4 pita bread or flatbreads
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the Greek yogurt with the minced garlic, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt to make the tzatziki sauce.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat and cook the sliced lamb or chicken until browned and cooked through.
  3. Warm the pita bread or flatbreads in a dry skillet or oven.
  4. Assemble the wraps by spreading a generous amount of tzatziki sauce on each bread, then layering the cooked meat, diced cucumber, sliced onion, tomato, and chopped parsley.
  5. Roll up the wraps and secure them with parchment paper or foil for easy handling.

Veggie Hummus Wrap

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup sliced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper to make a simple vinaigrette.
  2. Lay out the tortillas and spread a generous amount of hummus on each.
  3. In the center of each tortilla, layer the mixed greens, red bell pepper, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and Kalamata olives.
  4. Drizzle the vinaigrette over the fillings.
  5. Fold the sides of the tortillas inward, then roll them up tightly.

Mediterranean Chicken Salad Sandwich

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 35 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 cup arugula

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, basil, parsley, red onion, celery, pine nuts, sun-dried tomatoes, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shredded chicken to the bowl and toss until well combined.
  3. Toast the whole grain bread slices lightly.
  4. Divide the chicken salad evenly among 4 slices of bread, then top with a handful of arugula.
  5. Place the remaining bread slices on top to complete the sandwiches.

Conclusion

Mediterranean-inspired wraps and sandwiches offer a delightful combination of flavors and textures that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re craving a classic gyro wrap, a veggie-packed hummus wrap, or a hearty chicken salad sandwich, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you coming back for more. Try them out for your next outdoor gathering or a satisfying meal on the go, and experience the transportive flavors of the Mediterranean right in the palm of your hand.

I hope you find these recipes inspiring and delicious. Feel free to experiment with different fillings and sauces to create your own Mediterranean-inspired wraps and sandwiches!
