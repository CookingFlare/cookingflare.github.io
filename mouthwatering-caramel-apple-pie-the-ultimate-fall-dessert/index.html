Mouthwatering Caramel Apple Pie: The Ultimate Fall Dessert


Are you ready to indulge in a slice of heaven? Look no further than this mouthwatering caramel apple pie recipe that will have your taste buds dancing with delight. With its perfect combination of sweet and tart flavors, this pie is the ultimate fall dessert that will warm your heart and soul. So grab your apron and get ready to bake up a storm!

Ingredients

  • 2 pre-made pie crusts
  • 6 cups of thinly sliced apples (use a mix of tart and sweet apples for the best flavor)
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of caramel sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place one of the pie crusts into a 9-inch pie dish, pressing it gently against the sides and bottom. Set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well until the apples are evenly coated with the sugar mixture.

  3. Pour the caramel sauce over the apple mixture and stir until all the apples are coated with the caramel goodness. Don’t be afraid to be generous with the caramel!

  4. Transfer the apple filling into the prepared pie crust, spreading it out evenly. Dot the top of the filling with the small pieces of unsalted butter. This will help create a rich and luscious caramel sauce as the pie bakes.

  5. Place the second pie crust on top of the apple filling. You can choose to leave it as a whole crust or create a lattice pattern by cutting strips and weaving them over the filling. Crimp the edges of the pie crust to seal in the deliciousness.

  6. Brush the top crust with the beaten egg wash. This will give the pie a beautiful golden-brown color as it bakes.

  7. Place the pie in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly. If the crust starts to brown too quickly, cover it loosely with foil.

  8. Once baked, remove the pie from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for at least 2 hours. This will allow the caramel to set and the flavors to meld together.

  9. Serve your mouthwatering caramel apple pie warm or at room temperature, and don’t forget to top it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for that extra indulgence!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra special touch, sprinkle some flaky sea salt on top of the pie just before serving. It adds a delightful contrast to the sweet caramel and enhances the overall flavor profile.
  • Feel free to add some chopped nuts, such as pecans or walnuts, to the apple filling for added crunch and depth of flavor.
  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can even make your own caramel sauce from scratch. Just be sure to let it cool slightly before pouring it over the apple filling.

Now that you have the ultimate fall dessert recipe in your hands, it’s time to gather your ingredients and bake up a storm. Your friends and family will be in awe of your culinary skills when they take a bite of this mouthwatering caramel apple pie. So go ahead, embrace the flavors of fall and treat yourself to a slice of pure deliciousness!
