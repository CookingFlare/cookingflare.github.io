Soft and Flaky Crescent Rolls: Perfect for Any Occasion


If you’re looking for a versatile and delicious bread recipe that will impress your family and friends, look no further than these Soft and Flaky Crescent Rolls. These golden beauties are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a casual family dinner, a special holiday feast, or even just a lazy Sunday brunch. With their buttery, flaky texture and irresistible aroma, these crescent rolls are sure to become a staple in your baking repertoire.

Ingredients

To make these delectable crescent rolls, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 and 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast. Mix well to ensure all the dry ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warm milk, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract. Make sure the milk is warm but not hot, as high temperatures can kill the yeast.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Using a wooden spoon or a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment, mix until a soft and slightly sticky dough forms.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough feels too sticky, you can add a little more flour, but be careful not to add too much, as it can result in dry rolls.

  5. Shape the dough into a ball and place it in a greased bowl. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1 to 2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

  6. Once the dough has risen, gently punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer it back to the floured surface and divide it into 12 equal portions.

  7. Working with one portion at a time, roll it into a ball and then roll it out into a thin circle, about 6 inches in diameter. Using a sharp knife or a pizza cutter, cut the circle into 4 equal triangles.

  8. Starting from the wide end of each triangle, roll it up tightly towards the pointed end, forming a crescent shape. Repeat this process with the remaining portions of dough.

  9. Place the shaped crescent rolls onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving some space between each roll for expansion. Cover them loosely with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 to 45 minutes, or until they have puffed up slightly.

  10. While the rolls are rising, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Once the rolls have finished rising, brush them gently with melted butter for a beautiful golden finish.

  11. Bake the crescent rolls in the preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes, or until they turn golden brown. Keep a close eye on them as baking times may vary depending on your oven.

  12. Once baked, remove the rolls from the oven and transfer them to a wire rack to cool slightly. Serve them warm or at room temperature, and enjoy their soft and flaky goodness!

Conclusion

These Soft and Flaky Crescent Rolls are a true delight to bake and enjoy. Their buttery flavor, tender texture, and elegant shape make them a perfect addition to any meal or gathering. Whether you’re serving them alongside a bowl of soup, using them as a base for mini sandwiches, or simply indulging in their warm goodness on their own, these crescent rolls are guaranteed to be a hit. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and let the aroma of freshly baked bread fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
