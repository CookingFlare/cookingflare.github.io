Spicy Sriracha Chicken Lettuce Wraps for a Light Snack

3 min · 563 words

Are you looking for a quick and delicious snack that won’t weigh you down? Look no further than these Spicy Sriracha Chicken Lettuce Wraps! Packed with bold flavors and fresh ingredients, these wraps are the perfect light option for any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup finely diced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup finely diced carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Large lettuce leaves, such as Bibb or Boston lettuce

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the marinade for the chicken. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, Sriracha sauce, lime juice, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Set aside.

  2. Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips, about 1/4-inch thick. Place the chicken strips in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Make sure the chicken is well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per side. Make sure the chicken is cooked to an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C). Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the diced red bell pepper and carrots. Cook for about 2-3 minutes until they start to soften. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and toss it with the vegetables. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to ensure everything is heated through.

  6. Remove the skillet from heat. Stir in the chopped green onions and fresh cilantro for an added burst of freshness.

  7. To assemble the lettuce wraps, take a large lettuce leaf and spoon a generous amount of the spicy chicken and vegetable mixture onto the center. Fold the sides of the lettuce leaf inward, then roll it up tightly. Repeat with the remaining lettuce leaves and filling.

  8. Serve the Spicy Sriracha Chicken Lettuce Wraps as a light snack or appetizer. They are best enjoyed immediately while the lettuce is crisp and the flavors are at their peak.

Final Thoughts

These Spicy Sriracha Chicken Lettuce Wraps are a fantastic choice for a light yet satisfying snack. The combination of succulent chicken, crunchy vegetables, and the kick of Sriracha sauce creates a flavor explosion in every bite. The crisp lettuce leaves provide a refreshing and healthy base for this delicious treat.

Feel free to customize these wraps to your taste by adding extra vegetables or adjusting the level of spice. You can even serve them with a side of sweet chili sauce or a squeeze of lime for an extra tangy twist.

Whether you’re preparing a quick snack for yourself or entertaining guests, these Spicy Sriracha Chicken Lettuce Wraps are sure to impress. So go ahead, indulge in this tasty delight and enjoy a guilt-free treat!
