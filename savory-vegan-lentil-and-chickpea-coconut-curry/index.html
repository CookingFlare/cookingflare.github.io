Savory Vegan Lentil and Chickpea Coconut Curry


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious vegan curry recipe that will satisfy your taste buds? Look no further! This savory vegan lentil and chickpea coconut curry is packed with flavor and will leave you wanting more. Made with wholesome ingredients and bursting with aromatic spices, this curry is a perfect weeknight dinner option. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the lentils. Rinse them thoroughly and then place them in a saucepan with 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let them simmer for about 20 minutes or until tender. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet and cook for another minute, stirring constantly to avoid burning the garlic.

  4. Next, add the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well to coat the onions, garlic, and ginger with the spices.

  5. Pour in the coconut milk and stir to combine everything. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Add the cooked lentils and chickpeas to the skillet and stir to coat them with the coconut curry sauce. Simmer for another 10 minutes to allow the flavors to develop further.

  7. Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning if needed. If you prefer a spicier curry, you can add some red pepper flakes or chili powder at this point.

  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the curry sit for a few minutes to thicken slightly.

  9. Serve the savory vegan lentil and chickpea coconut curry over a bed of fluffy basmati rice or with warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra pop of freshness.

Conclusion

This savory vegan lentil and chickpea coconut curry is a wholesome and flavorful dish that will impress even the most skeptical of eaters. Packed with protein from the lentils and chickpeas, this curry is not only delicious but also highly nutritious. The aromatic spices and creamy coconut milk create a harmonious blend of flavors that will transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise.

Whether you’re a dedicated vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this curry is a must-try. It’s easy to make, budget-friendly, and perfect for meal prepping. So go ahead, grab your apron, and get ready to indulge in a bowl of this mouthwatering savory vegan lentil and chickpea coconut curry. Your taste buds will thank you!
