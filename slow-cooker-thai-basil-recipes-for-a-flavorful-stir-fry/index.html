Slow Cooker Thai Basil Recipes for a Flavorful Stir-Fry


Are you a fan of Thai cuisine? Do you love the vibrant flavors of basil, garlic, and chili peppers? If so, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, I will share with you some delicious slow cooker Thai basil recipes that will take your taste buds on a flavorful journey.

The Beauty of Slow Cooking

One of the best things about using a slow cooker is that it allows the flavors of the ingredients to meld together over time, creating a rich and aromatic dish. Thai cuisine is known for its bold and spicy flavors, and these slow cooker recipes are no exception. By using a slow cooker, you can infuse the dish with the fragrant aroma of Thai basil and other herbs and spices, resulting in a mouthwatering stir-fry that will leave you craving for more.

Slow Cooker Thai Basil Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the chicken, onion, garlic, soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and crushed red pepper flakes. Mix well to ensure the chicken is evenly coated with the sauce.
  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 4-6 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  3. Just before serving, stir in the Thai basil leaves and let them wilt in the heat of the dish.
  4. Serve the slow cooker Thai basil chicken over steamed rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

Slow Cooker Thai Basil Pork

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork shoulder, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the pork shoulder, bell peppers, garlic, soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and crushed red pepper flakes. Mix well to ensure the pork is evenly coated with the sauce.
  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours, or until the pork is tender and easily shreds apart.
  3. Just before serving, stir in the Thai basil leaves and let them wilt in the heat of the dish.
  4. Serve the slow cooker Thai basil pork over steamed rice or with a side of jasmine rice for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can substitute tofu or your favorite vegetables for the meat in these recipes. Just adjust the cooking time accordingly.
  • Feel free to experiment with the spice level by adding more or less crushed red pepper flakes. Thai cuisine is known for its heat, but you can always adjust it to your taste.
  • Thai basil has a distinct flavor that sets it apart from other varieties of basil. If you can’t find Thai basil, you can use regular basil, but the taste may be slightly different.
  • You can garnish the dish with a sprinkle of chopped peanuts or a squeeze of fresh lime juice for added flavor and texture.

In Conclusion

These slow cooker Thai basil recipes are a fantastic way to bring the authentic flavors of Thai cuisine to your home. The slow cooking process allows the ingredients to develop their flavors, resulting in a mouthwatering stir-fry that will transport you to the bustling streets of Thailand. Whether you choose chicken or pork, these recipes are sure to impress your family and friends. So, fire up your slow cooker and get ready to savor the bold and aromatic flavors of Thai basil. Enjoy!
