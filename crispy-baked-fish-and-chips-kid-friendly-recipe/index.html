Crispy Baked Fish and Chips: Kid-Friendly Recipe


Are you tired of the same old fish sticks and frozen French fries? Why not try making your own delicious and healthier version of fish and chips at home? This crispy baked fish and chips recipe is not only kid-friendly but also easy to make. It’s a perfect dish for a family dinner or a weekend treat. So, let’s dive right into it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound white fish fillets (such as cod or haddock)
  • 4 large potatoes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Olive oil, for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with olive oil.

  2. Start by preparing the potatoes. Peel them and cut them into thick, even-sized wedges. Place the wedges in a large bowl of cold water to remove excess starch.

  3. In another bowl, combine the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to evenly distribute the spices.

  4. Drain the potato wedges and pat them dry with a clean kitchen towel or paper towels. Toss the wedges in the flour mixture until they are well coated.

  5. Arrange the coated potato wedges on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer. Drizzle them with olive oil to ensure they crisp up nicely in the oven.

  6. Bake the potatoes for about 25-30 minutes, flipping them halfway through, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

  7. While the potatoes are baking, prepare the fish. Rinse the fish fillets under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels. Cut the fillets into smaller, kid-friendly portions.

  8. Dip each piece of fish into the beaten eggs, making sure it is fully coated. Then, transfer the fish to the flour mixture, pressing it gently to ensure the flour sticks to the fish.

  9. Place the coated fish on a separate baking sheet lined with parchment paper or greased with olive oil. Drizzle a little more olive oil over the fish to help it brown and become crispy.

  10. Bake the fish for 12-15 minutes, or until it is cooked through and golden brown.

  11. Serve the crispy baked fish and chips together, along with your favorite dipping sauces such as tartar sauce or ketchup. You can also add a side of coleslaw or a fresh garden salad for a complete meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a healthier twist, you can use sweet potatoes instead of regular potatoes.
  • Experiment with different spices and herbs in the flour mixture to add more flavor to the fish and chips.
  • If you want an extra crispy coating, you can double-dip the fish in the egg and flour mixture.
  • Feel free to use different types of white fish, such as tilapia or halibut, depending on your preference.

Now you have a delicious and healthier alternative to the classic fish and chips that your kids will love. The crispy baked fish and chips are not only easy to make but also a fun way to involve your little ones in the kitchen. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try. Your family will thank you for it!
