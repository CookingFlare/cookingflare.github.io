Mouthwatering Oatmeal Raisin Cookies: A Perfect Breakfast Treat


If you’re a fan of cookies for breakfast, you’re going to love this recipe for mouthwatering oatmeal raisin cookies. These delightful cookies are not only delicious but also nutritious, making them a perfect morning treat to kick-start your day. Packed with wholesome oats and sweet raisins, these cookies are an excellent source of fiber and energy, making them a great choice for a satisfying breakfast on the go. Plus, they’re incredibly easy to make, so you can whip up a batch in no time and enjoy them throughout the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 cups raisins

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet mixture and mix until well combined.
  5. Fold in the rolled oats and raisins until evenly distributed throughout the dough.
  6. Using a cookie scoop or a spoon, drop rounded balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space between each cookie for spreading.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

These mouthwatering oatmeal raisin cookies are best enjoyed with a glass of cold milk or a hot cup of coffee. The combination of the chewy texture and the sweet burst of raisins makes them a delightful addition to any breakfast spread. You can also pack them as a mid-morning snack to keep you fueled throughout the day.

Health Benefits

Oats are known for their high fiber content, which can help with digestion and provide a lasting feeling of fullness. Raisins, on the other hand, are a good source of natural sugars, iron, and antioxidants. By incorporating these wholesome ingredients into a delicious cookie, you’re not only treating your taste buds but also nourishing your body with essential nutrients.

Conclusion

Mouthwatering oatmeal raisin cookies are a delightful and nutritious breakfast treat that will surely become a favorite in your household. Whether you enjoy them fresh out of the oven or save them for a quick grab-and-go breakfast, these cookies are a versatile and wholesome option for starting your day on a sweet note. So, why not give this delicious recipe a try and indulge in the comforting flavors of oats and raisins in every bite? Your mornings will thank you for it!
