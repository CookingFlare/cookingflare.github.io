Delightfully Cheesy Baked Ziti with Spinach and Ricotta


Baked ziti is a classic comfort dish that brings together layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, and plenty of gooey cheese. Adding spinach and creamy ricotta takes this beloved recipe to a whole new level, adding a fresh twist and extra nutrients to an already hearty meal. Perfect for feeding a crowd or simply enjoying leftovers throughout the week, this recipe strikes a balance between simplicity and indulgence. Here’s how to create your own delightfully cheesy baked ziti with spinach and ricotta at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ziti pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 10 ounces (300g) fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 15 ounces (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Pasta

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the ziti according to package instructions until just al dente. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Make the Tomato Sauce

  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, and red pepper flakes (if using). Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.

Step 3: Prepare the Spinach and Ricotta Mixture

  1. In a separate bowl, combine the chopped spinach, ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese. Mix until well combined.

Step 4: Assemble the Baked Ziti

  1. In a large mixing bowl, toss the cooked ziti with the tomato sauce until well coated.
  2. Spread half of the ziti mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Top evenly with half of the spinach and ricotta mixture.
  3. Add the remaining ziti mixture on top, followed by the remaining spinach and ricotta mixture.
  4. Sprinkle the remaining 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese over the top.

Step 5: Bake and Serve

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  3. Let the baked ziti cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.

Tips for Success

  • Choose Your Pasta: Ziti works wonderfully in this dish due to its tubular shape that holds the sauce and cheese well. Penne or rigatoni can be substituted if needed.
  • Fresh vs. Frozen Spinach: Fresh spinach adds a vibrant color and texture to the dish, but frozen spinach can be used as a convenient alternative. Thaw and drain it well before mixing with ricotta.
  • Make-Ahead Option: Assemble the baked ziti up to 24 hours in advance, cover tightly with foil, and refrigerate. Bake as directed when ready to serve, adding a few extra minutes to the covered baking time if starting from cold.

Serving Suggestions

Serve your delightfully cheesy baked ziti with a crisp green salad dressed with balsamic vinaigrette and crusty garlic bread. It’s also a great dish to bring to potlucks or family gatherings, as it satisfies both kids and adults alike with its comforting flavors and cheesy goodness.

Conclusion

This recipe for baked ziti with spinach and ricotta is sure to become a staple in your home cooking repertoire. It’s easy to prepare, packed with flavor, and perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a comforting meal, this dish delivers on all fronts. Enjoy the creamy richness of the ricotta cheese, the freshness of the spinach, and the gooey goodness of melted mozzarella—all baked together to perfection. Treat yourself and your loved ones to this delightful twist on a classic Italian-American favorite tonight!
