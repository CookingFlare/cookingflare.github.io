Irresistible Allergy-Friendly Oatmeal Raisin Cookies for a Sweet Treat


Looking for a delicious and allergy-friendly treat? Look no further! These irresistible oatmeal raisin cookies are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth while accommodating dietary restrictions. Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, these cookies are sure to become your new favorite go-to recipe. So grab your apron and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gluten-free oats
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free oats, almond flour, raisins, ground cinnamon, and salt. Mix well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the unsweetened applesauce, melted coconut oil, pure maple syrup, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until a thick dough forms. If the dough seems too dry, you can add a tablespoon of water to help bind it together.
  5. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Flatten the dough slightly with the back of a spoon or your fingers.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden brown. The cookies may still feel slightly soft, but they will firm up as they cool.
  7. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a sweeter cookie, you can add 1/4 cup of coconut sugar or brown sugar to the wet ingredients.
  • Feel free to customize your cookies by adding other mix-ins such as chopped nuts, dried cranberries, or chocolate chips.
  • These cookies are also great for making ice cream sandwiches. Simply sandwich a scoop of your favorite dairy-free ice cream between two cookies for a delightful summer treat.

Allergy-Friendly and Nutritional Information

  • Gluten-Free
  • Dairy-Free
  • Vegan
  • Refined Sugar-Free
  • High in Fiber
  • Contains Healthy Fats from Almond Flour and Coconut Oil

Note: Always check the labels of your ingredients to ensure they are free from allergens or cross-contamination.

Conclusion

These irresistible allergy-friendly oatmeal raisin cookies are the perfect treat for anyone with dietary restrictions or food allergies. Not only are they delicious, but they are also packed with wholesome ingredients and easy to make. Whether you’re looking for a sweet snack or a dessert to impress your friends, these cookies are sure to hit the spot. So why wait? Gather your ingredients and start baking these mouthwatering treats today!
