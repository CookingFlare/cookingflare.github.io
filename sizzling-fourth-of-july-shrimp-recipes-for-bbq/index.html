Sizzling Fourth of July Shrimp Recipes for BBQ


The Fourth of July is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a sizzling BBQ featuring mouthwatering shrimp recipes? Whether you’re hosting a backyard bash or attending a potluck, these shrimp recipes are sure to impress your guests and elevate your Fourth of July feast. From zesty grilled shrimp skewers to smoky bacon-wrapped shrimp, these recipes will add a burst of flavor to your holiday spread. So, fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and get ready to indulge in some unforgettable sizzling shrimp dishes.

Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto skewers, leaving a little space between each shrimp.
  5. Grill the shrimp skewers for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are pink and opaque.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

These grilled lemon garlic shrimp skewers are a perfect combination of tangy and savory flavors, making them a delightful addition to your Fourth of July BBQ spread.

Smoky Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound thin-cut bacon slices
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Toothpicks

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the brown sugar, smoked paprika, and garlic powder.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  3. Wrap each shrimp with a slice of bacon and secure it with a toothpick.
  4. Sprinkle the brown sugar mixture over the bacon-wrapped shrimp.
  5. Place the shrimp on the grill and cook for 5-7 minutes on each side, or until the bacon is crispy and the shrimp is cooked through.

The combination of succulent shrimp, smoky bacon, and sweet-savory seasoning makes these bacon-wrapped shrimp a crowd-pleaser at any Fourth of July celebration.

Spicy Cajun Shrimp Foil Packets

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon wedges

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the shrimp, bell pepper, and onion with melted butter, Cajun seasoning, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Divide the shrimp and vegetable mixture into foil packets and seal them tightly.
  4. Place the foil packets on the grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, or until the shrimp is pink and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve the spicy Cajun shrimp foil packets with fresh lemon wedges for a burst of citrus flavor.

These spicy Cajun shrimp foil packets are not only delicious but also a convenient and mess-free option for a Fourth of July BBQ, allowing you to spend more time enjoying the festivities and less time cleaning up.

Conclusion

This Fourth of July, treat your family and friends to a sensational BBQ experience with these sizzling shrimp recipes. From the zesty grilled lemon garlic shrimp skewers to the irresistible smoky bacon-wrapped shrimp and the convenient spicy Cajun shrimp foil packets, these recipes are guaranteed to make your celebration unforgettable. So, fire up the grill, sizzle some shrimp, and let the flavors of summer elevate your Fourth of July feast. Happy Independence Day!
