Easy and Flavorful Dairy-Free Chicken Curry


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting meal that’s both easy to make and dairy-free, then this Dairy-Free Chicken Curry recipe is just what you need. Packed with aromatic spices and rich flavors, this curry is sure to become a favorite in your household. The best part? It’s simple enough for a weeknight dinner yet impressive enough to serve to guests. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan, for serving

Instructions

  1. Sear the Chicken: In a large skillet or pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and sear them until they are golden brown on all sides. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Spices: Sprinkle the ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, paprika, garam masala, and cayenne pepper into the skillet with the onion mixture. Stir the spices for about 30 seconds to release their flavors.

  4. Simmer with Coconut Milk and Tomatoes: Pour in the coconut milk and diced tomatoes, and stir well to combine. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  5. Return the Chicken to the Skillet: Once the sauce is simmering, return the seared chicken pieces to the skillet. Stir everything together, ensuring that the chicken is well-coated with the flavorful sauce.

  6. Simmer Until Chicken is Cooked: Lower the heat to medium-low and let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and the flavors have melded together.

  7. Season and Garnish: Taste the curry and add salt as needed. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the Dairy-Free Chicken Curry over a bed of cooked rice or with warm naan bread. Enjoy the delightful blend of spices and the creamy texture of the coconut milk.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add some sliced green chilies or a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in the curry by adding bell peppers, peas, or spinach.
  • To make this dish even more indulgent, stir in a few tablespoons of dairy-free yogurt just before serving.

Final Thoughts

This Dairy-Free Chicken Curry recipe offers a perfect combination of convenience and flavor, making it an ideal choice for anyone looking to enjoy a satisfying meal without dairy. Whether you’re a curry enthusiast or trying it for the first time, this recipe is bound to impress with its vibrant spices and rich, creamy sauce. Give it a try and savor the comforting warmth of this delightful dish.
