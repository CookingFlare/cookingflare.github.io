Homemade Tzatziki Hummus for a Mediterranean-inspired Appetizer

If you’re looking for a refreshing and flavorful appetizer that captures the essence of the Mediterranean, homemade tzatziki hummus is the perfect choice. This fusion of two beloved Mediterranean dips – tzatziki and hummus – creates a creamy, tangy, and herbaceous spread that will transport your taste buds to the shores of the Aegean Sea. Ingredients For the Tzatziki: 1 cup Greek yogurt 1 cucumber, grated and drained 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil 1 tablespoon fresh dill, finely chopped 1 tablespoon fresh mint, finely chopped Salt and pepper to taste For the Hummus: 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed 2 tablespoons tahini 2 tablespoons lemon juice 2 cloves garlic, minced 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil Salt to taste For Serving: Extra virgin olive oil Chopped fresh parsley Kalamata olives Warm pita bread or pita chips Instructions Tzatziki Preparation: In a bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, grated and drained cucumber, minced garlic, extra virgin olive oil, fresh dill, and fresh mint. Season the mixture with salt and pepper, and stir until well combined. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Hummus Preparation: In a food processor, combine the drained and rinsed chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, minced garlic, ground cumin, and smoked paprika. While blending, drizzle in the extra virgin olive oil and continue to process until the hummus is smooth and creamy. Add salt to taste and blend again. Assembling the Tzatziki Hummus: Spread a generous layer of the tzatziki on a serving platter, creating a shallow well in the center. Spoon the hummus into the center of the tzatziki, allowing the tzatziki to show around the edges. Drizzle the entire spread with a swirl of extra virgin olive oil and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley. Garnish with Kalamata olives for a burst of briny flavor. Serving Suggestions: Serve the homemade tzatziki hummus with warm pita bread or pita chips for dipping. Pair with fresh vegetables such as cucumber slices, cherry tomatoes, and bell pepper strips for a colorful and healthy presentation. Why Homemade Tzatziki Hummus? This delightful fusion of flavors brings together the cool, refreshing taste of tzatziki with the creamy richness of hummus, creating a harmonious balance that is irresistible. The vibrant green of the tzatziki against the golden beige of the hummus makes for a visually stunning dish that will impress your guests. ...
