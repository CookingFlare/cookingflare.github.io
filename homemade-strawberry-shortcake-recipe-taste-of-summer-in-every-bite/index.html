Homemade Strawberry Shortcake Recipe: Taste of Summer in Every Bite


There’s nothing quite like the taste of a homemade strawberry shortcake on a warm summer day. The combination of sweet, juicy strawberries, fluffy whipped cream, and tender shortcake is a true taste of the season. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, planning a picnic in the park, or simply craving a delightful dessert, this classic recipe is sure to please. So, let’s dive into creating this timeless treat that captures the essence of summer in every luscious bite.

Ingredients

For the Shortcake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 egg, for egg wash

For the Strawberry Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon

For the Whipped Cream:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

Making the Shortcake:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold butter to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the milk and vanilla extract. Gradually add the milk mixture to the flour mixture, stirring until the dough comes together.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it holds together. Pat the dough into a 1-inch thick round.
  6. Use a biscuit cutter to cut out rounds of dough and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Brush the tops with egg wash.
  7. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until the shortcakes are golden brown. Allow them to cool on a wire rack.

Preparing the Strawberry Filling:

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, granulated sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Toss gently to coat the strawberries and let them macerate for about 30 minutes at room temperature.

Whipping the Cream:

  1. In a chilled mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, confectioners’ sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.

Assembling the Strawberry Shortcake:

  1. Once the shortcakes have cooled, carefully slice them in half horizontally.
  2. Spoon a generous amount of the macerated strawberries onto the bottom half of each shortcake.
  3. Top the strawberries with a dollop of whipped cream.
  4. Place the top half of the shortcake over the whipped cream and serve immediately.

Tips for a Perfect Strawberry Shortcake:

  • For an extra indulgence, consider drizzling a bit of strawberry syrup over the strawberries before adding the whipped cream.
  • To enhance the flavor of the shortcakes, you can add a sprinkle of sugar on top before baking for a slightly sweet and crunchy crust.
  • Ensure the heavy cream is well chilled before whipping to achieve the perfect consistency for the whipped cream.

Enjoy the Taste of Summer

There you have it, a delightful homemade strawberry shortcake that captures the essence of summer in every bite. Whether you’re enjoying it at a family gathering, a picnic, or simply as a sweet treat at home, this classic dessert is sure to bring joy with its vibrant flavors and luscious textures. So, gather your ripe strawberries, roll up your sleeves, and indulge in the delightful process of creating this timeless summer delight. Let the taste of summer come alive in every luscious bite of this homemade strawberry shortcake.
