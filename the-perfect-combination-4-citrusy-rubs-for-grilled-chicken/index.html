The Perfect Combination: 4 Citrusy Rubs for Grilled Chicken


Grilled chicken is a versatile and delicious dish that can be enjoyed year-round, but sometimes it’s nice to shake things up with a new flavor profile. That’s where citrusy rubs come in. These zesty combinations of herbs, spices, and citrus zest can elevate your grilled chicken to a whole new level. In this blog post, we’ll explore four different citrusy rubs that are perfect for grilled chicken, each bringing its own unique twist to the table.

1. Lemon Pepper Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the lemon zest, black pepper, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and dried thyme.
  2. Rub the mixture evenly over the surface of the chicken pieces, ensuring they are well coated.
  3. Grill the chicken over medium heat until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F, flipping once halfway through.

The bright, zingy flavor of the lemon zest combined with the warmth of the black pepper makes this rub a classic choice for grilled chicken.

2. Orange Ginger Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons orange zest
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the orange zest, grated ginger, soy sauce, honey, ground coriander, and paprika.
  2. Rub the mixture onto the chicken, making sure to coat it evenly.
  3. Grill the chicken over medium-high heat until it is cooked through, with a slightly charred exterior.

The combination of citrusy sweetness from the orange zest and the warm, spicy kick of the ginger makes this rub a standout choice for those looking for a more exotic flavor.

3. Lime Chili Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons lime zest
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the lime zest, chili powder, cumin, brown sugar, smoked paprika, and salt.
  2. Rub the mixture onto the chicken, ensuring it is thoroughly coated.
  3. Grill the chicken over medium-high heat until it is cooked through and has a slightly caramelized exterior.

The tangy lime zest combined with the smoky sweetness of the chili powder and brown sugar creates a vibrant and flavorful rub that is sure to tantalize the taste buds.

4. Grapefruit Herb Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons grapefruit zest
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the grapefruit zest, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Rub the mixture onto the chicken, ensuring it is well coated.
  3. Grill the chicken over medium heat until it is cooked through, with a fragrant, herb-infused aroma.

The floral, slightly bitter notes of the grapefruit zest combined with the earthy freshness of the herbs make this rub a sophisticated and elegant choice for grilled chicken.

Each of these citrusy rubs offers a different flavor profile, allowing you to experiment and find the perfect combination for your grilled chicken. Whether you’re craving a zesty kick, a sweet and spicy twist, or an herby, aromatic infusion, these citrusy rubs have got you covered. So, fire up the grill, grab your favorite citrus fruits, and get ready to elevate your grilled chicken game to new heights!
