Thai Red Curry with Tofu: A Vegetarian Delight


If you’re looking for a flavorful and satisfying vegetarian dish, Thai Red Curry with Tofu is an excellent choice. This aromatic and creamy curry is a staple in Thai cuisine, known for its rich and complex flavors. The combination of creamy coconut milk, spicy red curry paste, and tender tofu makes this dish a delightful experience for your taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

For the Curry Paste:

  • 2-3 red chilies
  • 2 shallots
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 lemongrass stalk
  • 1-inch piece of galangal or ginger
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste

For the Curry:

  • 14 oz (400g) firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 small carrot, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2-3 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Curry Paste:

    • In a food processor, combine the red chilies, shallots, garlic, lemongrass, galangal (or ginger), coriander seeds, cumin seeds, paprika, salt, and tomato paste. Blend until a smooth paste forms.

  2. Cook the Tofu:

    • In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the pan and set aside.

  3. Make the Curry:

    • In the same pan, add the prepared curry paste and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly to release the flavors. Pour in the coconut milk and stir until the curry paste is fully incorporated.

  4. Add the Vegetables:

    • Add the red bell pepper, broccoli, and carrot to the pan. Stir to coat the vegetables with the curry sauce. Let the vegetables simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender but still crisp.

  5. Season the Curry:

    • Stir in the soy sauce and brown sugar, adjusting the quantities to taste. Add the kaffir lime leaves to the curry for a citrusy aroma.

  6. Finish and Serve:

    • Return the cooked tofu to the pan and gently stir to combine with the vegetables and sauce. Let the curry simmer for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Garnish and Enjoy:

    • Once the curry reaches your desired consistency, remove it from the heat. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. This Thai Red Curry with Tofu pairs perfectly with steamed jasmine rice or rice noodles.

Tips for Success

  • For a spicier curry, increase the number of red chilies in the curry paste.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables such as snow peas, baby corn, or bamboo shoots for variety.
  • If you can’t find kaffir lime leaves, a squeeze of fresh lime juice can be used as a substitute.
  • Adjust the consistency of the curry by adding more or less coconut milk according to your preference.

Final Thoughts

Thai Red Curry with Tofu is a versatile and comforting dish that can be customized to suit your taste. The creamy texture of the coconut milk complements the bold flavors of the curry paste, creating a harmonious blend of sweet, spicy, and savory notes. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a nourishing meal, this vegetarian delight is bound to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Enjoy the exquisite flavors of Thai cuisine in the comfort of your own home with this tantalizing recipe.
