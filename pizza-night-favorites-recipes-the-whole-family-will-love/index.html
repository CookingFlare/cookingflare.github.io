Pizza Night Favorites: Recipes the Whole Family Will Love


Pizza night is a beloved tradition in many households. It’s a time for family and friends to come together, share a delicious meal, and create memories that will last a lifetime. But, let’s be honest, after a while, the same old pizza recipe can get a bit repetitive. That’s why we’ve put together a collection of pizza night favorites that are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters.

Classic Cheese Pizza

There’s a reason why classic cheese pizza is a timeless favorite. It’s simple, yet satisfying, and can be customized to suit any taste. Here’s a recipe that serves 4-6 people:

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-7 minutes until yeast is activated.
  3. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the bowl. Mix until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until it has doubled in size.
  6. Punch down the dough and shape it into a circle or rectangle.
  7. Place the dough on a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
  8. Spread the mozzarella and parmesan cheese evenly over the dough.
  9. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Sprinkle with chopped fresh basil leaves, if desired.

White Pizza with Garlic and Spinach

For a change of pace, try this white pizza recipe that’s packed with garlic and spinach. It’s a great option for those who prefer a lighter, more delicate flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • Fresh parsley leaves, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-7 minutes until yeast is activated.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until it has doubled in size.
  6. Punch down the dough and shape it into a circle or rectangle.
  7. Place the dough on a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
  8. Spread the ricotta and mozzarella cheese evenly over the dough.
  9. Top with minced garlic and fresh spinach leaves.
  10. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley leaves, if desired.
