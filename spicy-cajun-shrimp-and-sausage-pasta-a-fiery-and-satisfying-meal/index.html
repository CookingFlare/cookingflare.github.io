Spicy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta: A Fiery and Satisfying Meal


If you’re a fan of bold and zesty flavors, then this Spicy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. This dish combines succulent shrimp, savory sausage, and a medley of vibrant spices to create a mouthwatering and satisfying meal that will please even the most discerning palate.

Ingredients

For the Cajun Seasoning:

  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper

For the Pasta:

  • 12 oz. penne pasta
  • 1 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 oz. andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cajun Seasoning:

    • In a small bowl, combine the paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, dried oregano, dried thyme, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and white pepper. Mix well and set aside.

  2. Cook the Pasta:

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the penne pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.

  3. Sear the Sausage and Shrimp:

    • In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced andouille sausage and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove the sausage from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the shrimp for 2-3 minutes until pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Saute the Vegetables:

    • In the same skillet, add the diced onion and bell peppers. Cook until the vegetables are soft, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  5. Bring it All Together:

    • Return the cooked sausage and shrimp to the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Sprinkle the Cajun seasoning over the mixture and stir to coat evenly. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow the sauce to thicken slightly, about 5-7 minutes.

  6. Combine with Pasta:

    • Add the cooked penne pasta to the skillet with the shrimp and sausage mixture. Toss to combine, ensuring that the pasta is coated in the flavorful sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Transfer the Spicy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta to a serving dish and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot and savor the irresistible combination of flavors in every bite.

Conclusion

This Spicy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta is a celebration of vibrant and robust flavors, making it a perfect choice for a special dinner or a comforting weeknight meal. The harmonious blend of spices, tender shrimp, and smoky sausage creates a dish that is both fiery and satisfying. Whether you’re a fan of Cajun cuisine or simply love indulging in a delicious pasta dish, this recipe is sure to impress and delight. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the heat, and treat yourself to a culinary experience that will leave you craving more. Cheers to savoring the bold and fiery flavors of this remarkable dish!
