Dairy-Free Cake and Cupcake Recipes for Special Occasions


Are you looking for delicious and dairy-free cake and cupcake recipes to impress your guests on special occasions? Look no further! In this blog post, we have compiled a list of mouthwatering recipes that are not only dairy-free but also perfect for those with dietary restrictions or lactose intolerance. So, let’s dive right into these delectable treats!

1. Vegan Chocolate Cake

Preparation time: 20 minutes Cooking time: 30 minutes Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add almond milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix well until the batter is smooth.
  4. Stir in the boiling water slowly, while continuing to mix. The batter will be thin, but that’s normal.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and bake for about 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cake to cool completely before frosting.

2. Coconut Raspberry Cupcakes

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 20 minutes Servings: 12

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup fresh raspberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. Add coconut milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until the batter is smooth.
  4. Gently fold in the raspberries.
  5. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners.
  6. Bake for about 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let the cupcakes cool completely before frosting.

3. Lemon Blueberry Bundt Cake

Preparation time: 20 minutes Cooking time: 45 minutes Servings: 10

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest
  • 1 ½ cups fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a bundt pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar.
  3. Add vegetable oil, almond milk, lemon juice, and lemon zest to the dry ingredients. Mix until the batter is well combined.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared bundt pan and bake for about 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Conclusion

With these dairy-free cake and cupcake recipes, you can delight your guests with decadent desserts that cater to their dietary needs. Whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, or any other special occasion, these treats will impress everyone, regardless of their dietary restrictions. So, go ahead and indulge in these dairy-free delights and make your next celebration a memorable one!
