Vegan Lemon Blueberry Pancakes: Fluffy and Bursting with Berries


Are you ready to elevate your breakfast game with a delightful twist? Look no further than these Vegan Lemon Blueberry Pancakes! This recipe combines the zesty freshness of lemon with the sweet, juicy bursts of blueberries to create pancakes that are not only fluffy and delicious but also completely plant-based. Whether you’re a committed vegan, looking to reduce your consumption of animal products, or simply curious about trying something new, these pancakes are a delightful treat for everyone to enjoy.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • 2 cups unsweetened almond milk (or any plant-based milk of your choice)
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Batter: In a large mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients – flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In another bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients – almond milk, lemon juice, lemon zest, vanilla extract, and melted coconut oil.

  2. Combine and Fold: Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour the wet mixture into it. Gently fold the ingredients together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps in the batter are perfectly okay.

  3. Add Blueberries: Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to crush them. The bursts of blueberry goodness in every bite will make these pancakes a true delight.

  4. Cook the Pancakes: Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with coconut oil or cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake and cook until bubbles form on the surface. Flip the pancakes and cook until golden brown on the other side.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Stack the pancakes on a plate, drizzle with a touch of maple syrup, and garnish with extra blueberries and a sprinkle of lemon zest for a beautiful presentation.

Tips for Success

  • For extra fluffy pancakes, let the batter rest for a few minutes before cooking.
  • If you prefer a smoother pancake, you can blend the wet ingredients, excluding the blueberries, in a blender before adding them to the dry ingredients.
  • Feel free to customize these pancakes by adding a handful of chopped nuts or a sprinkle of shredded coconut to the batter for added texture and flavor.

Why Choose Vegan Lemon Blueberry Pancakes?

These Vegan Lemon Blueberry Pancakes are not only a delicious and indulgent breakfast option, but they also offer a range of health benefits. Blueberries are packed with antioxidants and vitamins, while lemons provide a refreshing burst of vitamin C. By using plant-based ingredients, you’re also reducing your impact on the environment and contributing to a more sustainable food system.

So whether you’re serving these pancakes for a leisurely weekend brunch, a special occasion, or simply as a weekday treat, they are sure to impress vegans and non-vegans alike. The combination of tangy lemon, sweet blueberries, and fluffy pancake goodness makes this recipe a winner for any occasion.

Final Thoughts

Vegan Lemon Blueberry Pancakes are the perfect way to start your day with a burst of flavor and a touch of indulgence. By choosing plant-based ingredients, you can enjoy a delicious breakfast while also making a positive impact on your health and the planet. So go ahead, whip up a batch of these delightful pancakes and savor every fluffy, berry-filled bite!
