Classic American Pancake Breakfast Casserole: A Crowd-Pleasing Dish


Are you looking for a breakfast recipe that will impress your family and friends? Look no further than the Classic American Pancake Breakfast Casserole! This delicious dish combines the best of both worlds – fluffy pancakes and savory breakfast favorites – into one crowd-pleasing meal. Whether you’re hosting a brunch or simply want to treat yourself to a special breakfast, this casserole is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup cooked bacon, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced green onions
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, eggs, and melted butter. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; some lumps are okay.

  3. Pour half of the pancake batter into the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle the cooked bacon, shredded cheddar cheese, and diced green onions evenly over the batter. Top with the remaining pancake batter, spreading it out gently with a spatula.

  4. Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  5. Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice it into squares or rectangles, just like you would a traditional casserole.

  6. Serve the Classic American Pancake Breakfast Casserole warm, with maple syrup on the side for drizzling. You can also add a dollop of whipped cream or fresh fruit for extra flavor and presentation.

Variations

  • For a sweet twist, you can add a layer of sliced bananas or blueberries between the pancake batter and the toppings. This will give the casserole a fruity burst of flavor.
  • If you prefer a meatless version, you can omit the bacon and replace it with sautéed mushrooms or roasted vegetables. This will give the casserole a delicious, savory taste.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese, such as mozzarella or pepper jack, to give the casserole a unique flavor profile.

Tips and Tricks

  • Make sure to let the casserole cool slightly before slicing it. This will help it hold its shape and prevent it from crumbling.
  • If you want to make the casserole ahead of time, you can assemble it the night before and refrigerate it overnight. Just pop it in the oven the next morning and you’ll have a hot and fresh breakfast in no time.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in the microwave or oven before serving.

Enjoy the Classic American Pancake Breakfast Casserole with your loved ones and savor the delicious combination of fluffy pancakes, savory bacon, and gooey cheese. This dish is guaranteed to become a staple in your breakfast repertoire and leave everyone asking for seconds. So go ahead, give it a try and indulge in a truly crowd-pleasing breakfast experience!
