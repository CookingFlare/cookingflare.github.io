Quick and Flavorful Mediterranean Shrimp Tacos


Get ready to level up your taco game with this mouth-watering Mediterranean Shrimp Tacos recipe! Inspired by the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean, this dish is a perfect fusion of crispy, chewy, and tangy textures that will leave you craving for more. What You Need: For the marinade:

1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Mediterranean Tacos Filling:

1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint 1/4 cup chopped red onion 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped 2 tablespoons lemon juice 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon garlic powder Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Taco Assembly:

8-10 corn tortillas Mediterranean Shrimp Filling (above) Crumbled feta cheese Chopped fresh parsley and mint Sliced red onion Crispy pita chips Lemon wedges

Step 1: Marinate the Shrimp In a medium bowl, whisk together the marinade ingredients until well combined. Add the shrimp and toss to coat evenly. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours. Step 2: Grill the Shrimp Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Remove the shrimp from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they're pink and cooked through. Transfer the shrimp to a plate and set aside. Step 3: Prepare the Mediterranean Tacos Filling In a small bowl, mix together the feta cheese, parsley, mint, red onion, jalapeno pepper, lemon juice, olive oil, and garlic powder until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Step 4: Assemble the Tacos Warm the corn tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds. Place a grilled shrimp on each tortilla, followed by a spoonful of the Mediterranean Tacos Filling. Top with crumbled feta cheese, chopped fresh herbs, and sliced red onion. Garnish with crispy pita chips and a squeeze of lemon juice. Tips and Variations: To add some extra flavor, top the tacos with a drizzle of tahini sauce or a sprinkle of sumac. If you prefer a spicier taco, add some diced serrano peppers or red pepper flakes to the Mediterranean Tacos Filling. Feel free to customize the toppings to your liking – sliced avocado, crumbled queso fresco, or roasted sweet potatoes would all be amazing additions. Why You'll Love This Recipe: This Mediterranean Shrimp Tacos recipe is a quick and flavorful twist on traditional tacos. With the addition of feta cheese, parsley, and mint, these tacos pack a punch of Mediterranean flavor in every bite. Plus, they're ready in just 30 minutes, making them perfect for a weeknight dinner or a quick and delicious meal prep option. Get Ready to Spice Up Your Taco Game: This Mediterranean Shrimp Tacos recipe is the perfect way to add some excitement to your taco routine. With its bold flavors and textures, you'll be hooked from the very first bite. So, what are you waiting for? Get cooking and discover the magic of Mediterranean-inspired tacos!
