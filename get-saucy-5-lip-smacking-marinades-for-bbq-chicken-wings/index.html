Get Saucy: 5 Lip-Smacking Marinades for BBQ Chicken Wings


Who doesn’t love a plate of delicious, sticky, and finger-licking BBQ chicken wings? These little bites of heaven are perfect for any gathering or simply as a mouthwatering treat for yourself. And what takes these wings to a whole new level of flavor? It’s the marinade, of course! In this blog post, we’re going to share with you five lip-smacking marinades that will make your BBQ chicken wings the star of the show. So, fire up that grill and get ready to get saucy!

1. Classic BBQ Marinade

Let’s start with a classic that never disappoints. The Classic BBQ Marinade is a timeless favorite that combines the perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl until well combined. Place your chicken wings in a resealable bag or a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Make sure each wing is coated evenly. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight. Grill the wings over medium-high heat until they are cooked through and have a beautiful caramelized glaze. Serve with a side of extra BBQ sauce for dipping.

2. Honey Mustard Marinade

For a touch of sweetness and a hint of tangy goodness, try our Honey Mustard Marinade. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

In a bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined. Place your chicken wings in a resealable bag or a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Give the wings a good toss to ensure they are fully coated. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour. Grill the wings over medium heat until they are golden brown and cooked through. Serve with extra honey mustard sauce for dipping.

3. Teriyaki Marinade

If you’re in the mood for a taste of the East, our Teriyaki Marinade is the way to go. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, grated ginger, minced garlic, and red pepper flakes (if desired). Place your chicken wings in a resealable bag or a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Make sure each wing is coated evenly. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour. Grill the wings over medium-high heat until they are nicely charred and cooked through. Serve with a sprinkle of sesame seeds for added crunch.

4. Spicy Buffalo Marinade

If you like it hot and spicy, our Spicy Buffalo Marinade is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

In a bowl, whisk together the hot sauce, melted butter, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined. Place your chicken wings in a resealable bag or a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Give the wings a good toss to ensure they are fully coated. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour. Grill the wings over medium heat until they are crispy and cooked through. Serve with a side of ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping.

5. Asian Sesame Marinade

For an explosion of Asian flavors, try our Asian Sesame Marinade. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (for garnish)

In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if desired). Place your chicken wings in a resealable bag or a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Make sure each wing is coated evenly. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours. Grill the wings over medium-high heat until they are nicely caramelized and cooked through. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving for an extra burst of flavor and texture.
