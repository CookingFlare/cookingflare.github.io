Italian-Style Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta with Garlic Shrimp


If you’re a fan of indulgent pasta dishes with a touch of elegance, then this Italian-style chicken and mushroom creamy pasta with garlic shrimp is a must-try. This dish combines the richness of creamy pasta with the savory flavors of chicken, earthy mushrooms, and succulent garlic shrimp, creating a harmonious medley of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a comforting yet sophisticated meal, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the pasta:

  • 12 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 ounces of cremini or white mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

For the garlic shrimp:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta: In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the garlic shrimp: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and opaque. Season with salt, pepper, and crushed red pepper flakes if desired. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Sauté the chicken and mushrooms: In the same skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the sliced mushrooms until they are golden brown and tender. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  4. Make the creamy sauce: Pour the chicken broth into the skillet with the mushrooms and garlic, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Bring the broth to a simmer and let it cook for 2-3 minutes. Reduce the heat to medium-low and stir in the heavy cream. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce has slightly thickened. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until it is fully melted and the sauce is smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Combine the pasta, chicken, and sauce: Add the cooked fettuccine and sautéed chicken to the skillet with the creamy sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta is well coated with the sauce and the chicken is evenly distributed.

  6. Serve: Divide the creamy pasta into individual serving bowls and top each portion with the garlic shrimp. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley.

Enjoy!

This Italian-style chicken and mushroom creamy pasta with garlic shrimp is a delightful blend of flavors and textures that will transport you to the heart of Italy with every bite. The creamy sauce envelops the tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and perfectly cooked pasta, while the garlic shrimp adds a delightful pop of seafood flavor. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a gourmet meal at home, this dish is a true crowd-pleaser.

So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and embark on a culinary journey to create this Italian-inspired masterpiece. Your taste buds will thank you, and your guests will be asking for seconds! Buon appetito!
