Vegan Marinade Marvels: 20 Zesty & Flavor-Packed Recipes for Ultimate Plant-Based Grilling


Grilling season is upon us, and what better way to celebrate than by trying out some mouth-watering vegan grilling recipes? Plant-based meals are not only delicious but they are a fantastic way to experiment with different flavors and techniques. In this post, we bring you twenty zesty and flavor-packed vegan marinade recipes that are sure to tickle your taste buds. So, letâ€™s sizzle our veggies, tofu, tempeh, and other meat alternatives into smoky perfection!

  1. Lemon-Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice from 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: Combine all ingredients in a mason jar. Seal the lid tightly and shake vigorously. Shake again before marinating your favorite veggies or protein.

  1. Sweet & Tangy BBQ Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegan BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: In a bowl, whisk together all ingredients until completely combined. Let your food marinate for at least one hour to fully absorb the flavors.

  1. Spicy Asian Fusion Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce (optional)

Instructions: Combine all ingredients in a bowl and stir well to mix. This marinade pairs exceptionally well with tofu, tempeh, or any stir-fried veggies.

  1. Cumin-Coriander Southwestern Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)

Instructions: Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Marinate your ingredients for at least 4 hours to let the flavors penetrate your food.

  1. Zesty Balsamic Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped parsley
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions: In a bowl, whisk all ingredients together until emulsified. Let your veggies or tofu soak up the goodness for at least 2 hours before grilling.

  1. Citrus-Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped basil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: Combine all ingredients in a jar and shake well. Allow your choice of plant-based proteins or veggies to marinate for at least 2 hours.

  1. Savory-Sweet Teriyaki Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup coconut aminos
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger powder
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a medium bowl until completely combined. Your workhorse marinade pairs well with so many ingredients, including tofu, mushrooms, and bell peppers.

  1. Mint-Avocado Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions: Put all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Marinate your veggies or tofu for at least one hour to absorb the rich flavors.

  1. Apple Cider Bourbon Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons vegan Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon bourbon
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: In a mason jar, combine all ingredients and shake well. This marinade is a definite crowd-pleaser that pairs wonderfully with firm tofu, tempeh, or even Beyond Meat products.

  1. Turmeric-Spiced Yogurt Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup vegan yogurt (coconut or almond)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions: Whisk together all ingredients in a bowl until well-combined. This cooling, flavorful marinade washes your taste buds with a fresh burst of spices, pairing perfectly with falafel patties or grilled vegetables.

  1. Orange-Cardamom Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cardamom pods
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely grated orange zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions: Place orange juice and cardamom pods in a saucepan, bring to a boil, and let simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat, let cool, and strain the mixture. Mix in remaining ingredients. Marinate your dish for at least two hours to really appreciate the melding of spices.

  1. Black Bean Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup cooked black beans
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: Using an immersion blender or food processor, puree the ingredients until smooth. This marinade works wonders for Mexican-inspired dishes like grilled portobello mushroom tacos or black bean burgers.

  1. Simple yet Bold Tahini Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoonsmaple syrup
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: Whisk ingredients together in a bowl until smooth and creamy. This delightful marinade sings with flavor whether you’re marinating tofu, tempeh, cauliflower steaks, or any other vegetable of choice.

  1. Southwestern Spinach Pesto Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: Using a food processor, blend all ingredients until a smooth paste forms. This punchy marinade is perfect for chicken-less ribs, tempeh, or quinoa-stuffed bell peppers.

  1. Mango-Ginger Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup diced mango
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped red onion
  • Fresh chopped cilantro, to garnish

Instructions: Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. The sweetness of the mango paired with the heat from the ginger creates an irresistible marinade perfect for veggies, tofu, or any of your favorite plant-based proteins.

  1. French Oregano & Mustard Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped oregano
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a mason jar, then shake vigorously. This classic marinade is perfect for marinating tofu, tempeh, or veggies before grilling.

  1. Sweet & Tangy Mango Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup diced mango
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon fresh squeezed lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • Fresh chopped cilantro, for garnish

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Wrap your tofu, tempeh, portobello mushrooms, or any vegetables in this fruity, tangy mix for a truly refreshing meal.

  1. Citrus & Fennel Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Fresh chopped parsley, for garnish

Instructions: Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Let your veggies or protein marinate for at least one hour before cooking to really let the flavors meld.

  1. Maple Pecan Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup pecans
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions: Using a food processor, blend all ingredients except salt until smooth. This decadent marinade is perfect for tofu, tempeh, or even a hearty vegetable skewer.

  1. Ginger-Lime Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: Whisk all ingredients together in a bowl. Marinate your ingredients for at least one hour to let the flavors bake in, creating a divine and fiery symphony of taste.

Conclusion:

Grilling season is the perfect time to explore and indulge in the flavors of plant-based cuisine. These twenty vegan marinade recipes provide a lively alternative to traditional protein and vegetable marinades. Impress your friends, delight your taste buds, and appreciate the depth of flavors in these amazing vegan marinades. So, grab your veggies, tofu, tempeh, or any of your favorite plant-based proteins and let’s get grilling!
