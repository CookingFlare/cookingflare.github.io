Classic Spiced Hot Apple Cider

Ingredients:

  • 1 gallon of apple cider
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 1 teaspoon whole cloves
  • 1 teaspoon whole allspice
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, pour the apple cider and add the orange slices.
  2. Place the cinnamon sticks, cloves, and allspice in a piece of cheesecloth and tie it with kitchen twine to create a spice sachet.
  3. Add the spice sachet to the pot and stir in the brown sugar.
  4. Bring the cider to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once simmering, reduce the heat to low and let the cider steep for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Remove the spice sachet and orange slices before serving.
  7. Ladle the hot apple cider into mugs and garnish with a cinnamon stick for stirring.

This classic spiced hot apple cider recipe is a timeless favorite that captures the essence of fall in every sip. The combination of citrus, warm spices, and sweet apple cider creates a symphony of flavors that will envelop you in warmth and comfort.

Mulled Hot Apple Cider with a Twist

Ingredients:

  • 1 gallon of apple cider
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 star anise
  • 1 vanilla bean, split lengthwise
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorns
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup bourbon (optional for a spirited twist)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, pour the apple cider and add the lemon slices.
  2. Add the star anise, split vanilla bean, and peppercorns to the pot.
  3. Stir in the honey and bourbon (if using).
  4. Bring the cider to a gentle simmer over medium heat, allowing the flavors to meld for about 20-30 minutes.
  5. Once the cider is infused with the aromatic flavors, remove the pot from the heat.
  6. Discard the lemon slices, star anise, and vanilla bean before serving.
  7. Pour the mulled hot apple cider into mugs and garnish with a lemon twist for an extra touch of citrus.

This mulled hot apple cider with a twist offers a delightful departure from the traditional recipe. The addition of vanilla and a hint of bourbon elevates the drink to a whole new level of indulgence, making it a sophisticated choice for cozy evenings and intimate gatherings.

Spiced Cranberry Hot Apple Cider

Ingredients:

  • 1 gallon of apple cider
  • 2 cups cranberry juice
  • 1 orange, zested and juiced
  • 2 teaspoons whole cloves
  • 2 teaspoons whole allspice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the apple cider and cranberry juice.
  2. Add the orange zest and juice to the pot.
  3. Place the cloves and allspice in a spice infuser or tie them in a piece of cheesecloth.
  4. Add the spice infuser to the pot and stir in the granulated sugar.
  5. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer over medium heat, allowing it to infuse for 20-30 minutes.
  6. Remove the spice infuser and orange zest before serving.
  7. Ladle the spiced cranberry hot apple cider into mugs and garnish with a twist of orange peel for a pop of color.

This spiced cranberry hot apple cider is a vibrant and flavorful twist on the classic recipe. The addition of cranberry juice and citrus infuses the cider with a bright, tangy complexity that’s perfect for those who crave a more adventurous flavor profile.

So, gather your loved ones, curl up with a blanket, and savor the magic of warm and spiced hot apple cider as you create cherished memories that will last a lifetime. Cheers to cozy evenings filled with the simple pleasures of life!
