Decadent S'mores Dip: A Crowd-Pleasing Dessert


Who doesn’t love the classic combination of gooey marshmallows, creamy chocolate, and crunchy graham crackers? S’mores have been a beloved treat for generations, but what if we told you there’s a way to enjoy all the deliciousness of s’mores without the need for a campfire? Introducing Decadent S’mores Dip – a crowd-pleasing dessert that will satisfy your sweet tooth and become the star of any gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Place a medium-sized oven-safe skillet or baking dish in the oven to warm up.

  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the chocolate chips and heavy cream. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring after each interval, until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth.

  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the melted butter, brown sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.

  4. Take the warm skillet out of the oven and pour the butter and sugar mixture into it. Spread it evenly across the bottom.

  5. Next, pour the melted chocolate mixture over the butter and sugar layer. Use a spatula to spread it out, ensuring it covers the entire surface.

  6. Sprinkle the mini marshmallows evenly over the melted chocolate layer. Be generous with the marshmallows, as they will melt and spread out as the dip bakes.

  7. Finally, sprinkle the crushed graham crackers on top of the marshmallows, creating a deliciously crunchy crust.

  8. Place the skillet back in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the marshmallows are golden brown and toasted to your liking.

  9. Remove the skillet from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. The dip will be very hot, so exercise caution.

  10. Serve the Decadent S’mores Dip warm with a side of additional graham crackers for dipping. Enjoy the gooey, chocolatey, marshmallow goodness!

Tips and Variations:

  • Dipping Options: While graham crackers are the classic choice for dipping, you can get creative and offer a variety of options. Try serving the dip with pretzels, vanilla wafers, or even fresh fruit for a unique twist.

  • Toppings: Add some extra pizzazz to your S’mores Dip by sprinkling it with toppings like crushed nuts, shredded coconut, or even a drizzle of caramel or chocolate sauce.

  • Grilled S’mores: If you have access to a grill, you can also make this dip by placing the skillet on a preheated grill instead of baking it in the oven. The smoky flavor from the grill will take this dessert to a whole new level.

  • Mini S’mores Dip: Want to serve individual portions? Divide the dip into smaller oven-safe ramekins or baking dishes before baking. This way, everyone can have their own personal S’mores Dip experience.

Decadent S’mores Dip is a dessert that brings people together. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a sweet indulgence, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So gather your loved ones, grab your favorite dippers, and get ready to dive into a world of chocolatey, marshmallowy deliciousness!
