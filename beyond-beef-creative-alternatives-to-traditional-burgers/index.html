Beyond Beef: Creative Alternatives to Traditional Burgers


When it comes to burgers, the traditional beef patty has long been the star of the show. However, with the rise of plant-based alternatives like Beyond Beef, there’s a whole new world of burger possibilities to explore. Whether you’re a committed vegetarian, looking to reduce your meat intake, or just curious to try something new, there are countless ways to get creative with Beyond Beef and reinvent the classic burger. In this blog post, we’ll dive into some innovative and delicious recipes that will elevate your burger game and leave you craving more.

1. Spicy Southwest Beyond Beef Burger

Ingredients:

  • Beyond Beef patties
  • Taco seasoning
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Jalapenos, sliced
  • Pepper jack cheese
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Chipotle mayo

Instructions:

  1. Season the Beyond Beef patties with taco seasoning for an extra kick.
  2. Grill the patties to your desired level of doneness.
  3. Toast the burger buns on the grill until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the burgers by layering lettuce, tomato, red onion, jalapenos, avocado, and a slice of pepper jack cheese on each patty.
  5. Spread a generous amount of chipotle mayo on the top bun for a creamy and spicy finish.
  6. Serve with a side of sweet potato fries for a complete Southwest-inspired meal.

2. Mediterranean Beyond Beef Gyro Burger

Ingredients:

  • Beyond Beef patties
  • Greek seasoning
  • Tzatziki sauce
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Cucumber, thinly sliced
  • Feta cheese
  • Burger buns or pita bread
  • Lettuce

Instructions:

  1. Season the Beyond Beef patties with classic Greek seasoning for an authentic Mediterranean flavor.
  2. Cook the patties on the grill until they are perfectly juicy and flavorful.
  3. Warm the burger buns or pita bread on the grill for a touch of smokiness.
  4. Assemble the burgers by layering lettuce, red onion, cucumber, and crumbled feta cheese on each patty.
  5. Drizzle a generous amount of tzatziki sauce over the top for a refreshing and tangy finish.
  6. Serve with a side of Greek salad for a complete taste of the Mediterranean.

3. Umami Beyond Beef Mushroom Swiss Burger

Ingredients:

  • Beyond Beef patties
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • Swiss cheese
  • Burger buns
  • Arugula
  • Dijon mustard

Instructions:

  1. Season the Beyond Beef patties with salt and pepper to enhance their natural umami flavor.
  2. Sauté the cremini mushrooms until they are golden and fragrant.
  3. Grill the patties to perfection, ensuring they are juicy and irresistible.
  4. Melt a slice of Swiss cheese on top of each patty for a creamy and nutty addition.
  5. Assemble the burgers by layering arugula and sautéed mushrooms on each patty.
  6. Spread a dollop of Dijon mustard on the bottom bun for a sharp and zesty kick.
  7. Serve with a side of truffle fries for a luxurious and indulgent meal.

With these creative and mouthwatering Beyond Beef burger recipes, you’ll never look at traditional burgers the same way again. Whether you’re craving bold and spicy flavors, a taste of the Mediterranean, or a rich and savory umami experience, Beyond Beef opens up a world of possibilities for culinary exploration. So, fire up the grill, gather your favorite ingredients, and get ready to savor the incredible flavors of these innovative burger creations. Your taste buds will thank you!
