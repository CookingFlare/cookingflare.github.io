One-Pot Creamy Lemon Garlic Chicken and Asparagus Pasta


Are you looking for a delicious and easy one-pot meal to add to your recipe collection? Look no further than this One-Pot Creamy Lemon Garlic Chicken and Asparagus Pasta! This dish is a perfect combination of tender chicken, vibrant asparagus, and flavorful pasta, all brought together in a creamy lemon garlic sauce. Not only is it a feast for the taste buds, but it’s also a breeze to prepare and requires minimal cleanup. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1 pound asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 12 ounces fettuccine or any pasta of your choice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 lemon (zest and juice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by cutting the chicken into cubes and trimming the asparagus. Mince the garlic, grate the Parmesan cheese, and gather the remaining ingredients for easy access.

  2. Sear the Chicken: Heat the olive oil in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat. Season the chicken cubes with salt and pepper, then add them to the skillet. Sear the chicken until it’s golden brown on all sides, then transfer it to a plate and set it aside.

  3. Cook the Asparagus and Pasta: In the same skillet, add the asparagus and cook for a few minutes until it’s slightly tender. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute. Next, pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Add the pasta to the skillet, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  4. Simmer and Finish: Allow the pasta to cook in the creamy sauce, stirring occasionally, until it’s al dente and has absorbed most of the liquid. This should take about 10-12 minutes. Once the pasta is cooked, return the seared chicken to the skillet. Add the grated Parmesan cheese, butter, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Stir everything together until the sauce is creamy and well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Once the dish is ready, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the One-Pot Creamy Lemon Garlic Chicken and Asparagus Pasta with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  6. Enjoy: Serve the creamy pasta and chicken hot, and savor every bite of this delightful one-pot meal.

Tips and Variations

  • To add an extra kick of flavor, consider sprinkling red pepper flakes over the finished dish.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute half-and-half for the heavy cream.
  • Experiment with different types of pasta, such as penne or linguine, to change up the texture of the dish.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this One-Pot Creamy Lemon Garlic Chicken and Asparagus Pasta is a versatile and satisfying meal that’s perfect for both busy weeknights and special occasions. With its creamy texture, bright citrusy notes, and tender chicken, it’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. Plus, the convenience of cooking everything in one pot means less time spent on cleanup and more time enjoying the company of your loved ones. Give this recipe a try and watch it become a staple in your kitchen!
