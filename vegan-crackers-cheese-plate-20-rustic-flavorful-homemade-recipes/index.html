Vegan Crackers & Cheese Plate: 20 Rustic & Flavorful Homemade Recipes


Are you searching for some mouthwatering vegan cracker and cheese plate recipes? Have you been desiring a healthier variation of your traditional crackers and cheese? Look no more! In this blog post, we have curated a list of 20 rustic and flavorful homemade vegan cracker and cheese plate recipes that will make it easier for you to treat your taste buds without sacrificing your healthy lifestyle. Get ready to whip up these delightful and scrumptious concoctions by amping up your vegan game using a combination of zesty ingredients, flavorful spices, and artistic presentation techniques.

  1. Rosemary Almond Crackers Begin your vegan journey with these crunchy rosemary almond crackers that offer both a delicious and nutritious punch. Mix together almond flour, whole wheat flour, rosemary, salt, and water to create a smooth and elastic dough. Flatten the dough between two sheets of parchment paper and transfer it to the freezer for 15 minutes. Slice the dough into thin squares, sprinkle with more rosemary, and bake until golden and crisp.

  2. Sesame Ginger Crackers Give a zesty twist to your traditional cracker recipes with a combination of sesame seeds and ginger to create a beautifully blend of flavors. Combine whole wheat pastry flour, rolled oats, sesame seeds, ginger, salt, and olive oil to form a firm dough. Roll out the dough and cut it into desired shapes, then bake each cracker until it becomes crisp and golden. Serve with vegetable crudité for a delightful appetizer.

  3. Quinoa Crackers with Thyme Create a delectable blend of quinoa with fresh thyme for a gluten-free cracker option. Mix cooked quinoa flakes, thyme, salt, and water to form a moist dough. Chill the dough for at least an hour, then roll it out and cut into cracker shapes. Bake until crispy and perfectly seasoned, allowing the earthy flavors of quinoa and herbs to shine.

  4. Spicy Chickpea Crackers Spice up your cracker game with this satisfying spicy chickpea cracker recipe. Process cooked chickpeas, whole wheat flour, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and olive oil in a food processor to create a soft dough. Spread the dough into a thin layer and let it dry to fully develop the flavors. Once crisped, enjoy with a cool and creamy vegan dip for the ultimate texture contrast.

  5. Kale Pesto Crackers Transform leftover kale pesto into something extraordinary with these savory kale pesto crackers. Pulse together whole wheat flour, olive oil, water, and kale pesto in a food processor until a dough forms. Chill for an hour, then roll out the dough and cut into cracker shapes. Bake until golden brown, then serve alongside a cheese board or enjoy on their own.

  6. Garlic & Herb Crackers Elaborate on your basic cracker recipe with the aromatic blend of garlic and fresh herbs. Whisk together whole wheat flour, quinoa flakes, salt, garlic powder, and dried thyme. Gradually add in olive oil until the mixture reaches a a dough-like consistency. Roll the dough flat and cut into cracker-size pieces, then bake to crispy perfection.

  7. Walnut Crackers with Roasted Garlic Drench your taste buds in rich, earthy flavors with these walnut crackers studded with roasted garlic. Pulse walnuts, almond flour, roasted garlic, salt, and Italian seasoning in a food processor until finely ground. Slowly add in water until the dough comes together, then refrigerate for an hour. Roll the dough thin and cut into cracker shapes before baking to a caramelized finish.

  8. Smoked Paprika Crackers Tantalize your taste buds with the smoky depth of these smoked paprika crackers. Combine almond flour, whole wheat pastry flour, smoked paprika, salt, and olive oil in a bowl, then knead to form a cohesive dough. Roll the dough flat and slice into cracker shapes, then bake to crunchy golden-brown perfection.

  9. Beet Bio Crackers Blend a vibrant touch of red with these “bio” beetroot crackers, made with a procaryotic ingredient, beets. Whisk together whole wheat flour, cooked beets, salt, and water to create a dough. Chill for an hour to allow the color to fully develop, then roll out and bake until crisp.

  10. Olivenöl-Schnitzecken (in German: Olive Oil Crackers) Embrace your inner gourmet with these olive oil crackers that combine a basic recipe with high-quality ingredients. Combine whole wheat flour, coarse salt, and enough olive oil to create a desirable dough. Form the dough into a log, wrap it tightly in plastic wrap, and let it sit overnight in the refrigerator. Slice the dough into thin pieces, then bake until just crispy.

  11. Black and White Sesame Crackers Can’t decide between sesame or black sesame seeds? Try them both! Mix together quinoa flakes, sesame seeds, black sesame seeds, salt, and olive oil to create a delightful black and white speckled cracker. Roll out the dough and score the crackers before baking for a perfect finish.

  12. Everything Bagel Crackers Celebrate New York City’s most infamous bagel with these everything bagel-inspired crackers. Sprinkle pumpernickel bagel seasoning generously on top of quinoa-crust crackers before baking - you won’t be disappointed!

  13. Curry & Cashew Crackers Take your tastebuds on a flavor journey with the subtle warmth of curry and crunchy cashews in these unique crackers. Grind together roasted cashews, whole wheat flour, curry powder, salt, and olive oil to create a spicy-sweet crust. Top with additional cashews for an added crunch.

  14. Macadamia Rosemary Crackers Experience the decadence of macadamia nuts in these luxe rosemary macadamia crackers. Pulse together macadamia nuts, almond flour, rosemary, salt, and olive oil in a food processor until coarse crumbs form. Slowly mix in water, then chill the dough for an hour. Roll out the dough and cut into cracker shapes, then bake to a crispy golden deliciousness.

  15. Fig and Walnut Crackers Incorporate California’s finest with these fig and walnut cracker that celebrate one of the state’s most beloved products. Pulse together whole wheat flour, walnut pieces, dried figs, salt, and olive oil until crumbly, then slowly mix in water to form a dough. Flatten the dough, top with additional walnuts or figs, then bake into a delicious crispy treat.

  16. Lavender Almond Crackers Delightfully fragrant, this lavender almond cracker recipe is inspired by the relaxing scent of lavender fields. Combine almond flour, whole wheat pastry flour, lavender, salt, and olive oil to create a flavorful dough. Roll the dough thin and score the crackers, then bake to a perfect crisp.

  17. Seaweed Crackers Satisfy your umami cravings with these seasoned seaweed crackers. Mix together whole wheat flour, sesame seeds, kelp powder, salt, and olive oil, then refrigerate for an hour. Roll out the dough thinly and bake to a perfectly crisp cracker that is packed with nutrition and flavor.

  18. Avocado Oil Crackers Lean into the healthy fats of avocado oil with these crisp and light crackers. Combine whole wheat flour, avocado oil, salt, and water until a smooth dough is formed. Roll out the dough thin and score the crackers before baking, for a delectable cracker with zero compromise.

  19. Pumpkin Seed Crackers Harness the power of the pumpkin in these protein-packed pumpkin seed crackers. Pulse together pumpkin seeds, almond flour, whole wheat flour, salt, and olive oil until crumbly, then slowly add in water to bring the dough together. Chill for an hour, then roll out the dough and slice into desired shapes before baking.

  20. Caramelized Onion and Thyme Crackers Unleash the sweet and savory flavor of caramelized onions with these incredible thyme-infused crackers. Cook down a large onion until caramelized, then mix it into a dough of whole wheat flour, thyme, salt, and olive oil. Roll the dough flat and cut into cracker shapes, then bake until golden and bubbly.

A delicious vegan cracker and cheese plate reflects the creativity and dedication one puts into preparing a healthy and satisfying meal. With these 20 rustic and flavorful homemade recipes, there are endless possibilities of delicious combinations of ingredients you can use to craft your favorite cracker and cheese plate. Have fun experimenting and enjoy the mouthwatering culinary journey!
