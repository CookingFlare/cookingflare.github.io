Satisfying and Spicy Mexican Street Tacos for Couples


Are you and your partner craving a flavorful and exciting meal that will transport you to the bustling streets of Mexico? Look no further than these satisfying and spicy Mexican street tacos! This recipe is perfect for couples who want to spice up their dinner routine with a fun and interactive cooking experience. From preparing the succulent meat to assembling the tacos with an array of fresh and zesty toppings, this recipe promises to ignite your taste buds and bring you closer together in the kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1 pound of skirt steak
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Tacos:

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 white onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Salsa verde and hot sauce for topping
  • Optional: sliced radishes, avocado, and crumbled queso fresco

Instructions

  1. Marinating the Steak:

    • In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, ground cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, dried oregano, chopped cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.
    • Place the skirt steak in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it, making sure to coat the meat evenly. Cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to infuse the steak.

  2. Grilling the Steak:

    • Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Remove the marinated steak from the refrigerator and let it come to room temperature.
    • Grill the steak for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or longer if desired. Once cooked to your preference, transfer the steak to a cutting board and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing it against the grain into thin strips.

  3. Preparing the Toppings:

    • While the steak is resting, warm the corn tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side, until they are soft and pliable.
    • Place the chopped onion, cilantro, lime wedges, salsa verde, hot sauce, and any additional toppings in separate bowls for easy assembly.

  4. Assembling the Tacos:

    • To assemble the tacos, place a few slices of grilled steak on each tortilla. Top the steak with a sprinkle of chopped onion and cilantro. Squeeze a wedge of lime over the filling and add a drizzle of salsa verde and hot sauce.
    • Serve the tacos with your choice of sliced radishes, avocado, and crumbled queso fresco on the side for added freshness and creaminess.

Tips for a Romantic Taco Night

  • Create a cozy and inviting atmosphere by setting up a taco bar with all the toppings laid out beautifully on the table. Light some candles and play some Mexican music to set the mood for a delightful evening together.
  • Pair these spicy tacos with a refreshing margarita or a cold Mexican beer to complement the bold flavors and add an extra element of fun to your meal.

Final Thoughts

These satisfying and spicy Mexican street tacos are not only a delicious culinary adventure but also a fantastic way to bond with your significant other over a shared love of good food. The combination of tender, flavorful steak and vibrant, zesty toppings will surely make this meal a memorable one. So, roll up your sleeves, get cooking, and savor the joy of creating a delightful meal together with your loved one. Cheers to a wonderful taco night for two!
