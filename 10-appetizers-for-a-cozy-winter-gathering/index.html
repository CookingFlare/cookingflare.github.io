10 Appetizers for a Cozy Winter Gathering


Winter is here, and it’s the perfect time to gather with friends and family to share warmth, laughter, and delicious food. A cozy winter gathering is all about creating a welcoming atmosphere, and that starts with the perfect appetizers. In this article, we’ll explore 10 mouth-watering appetizer recipes that are sure to impress your guests and keep them warm and cozy throughout the evening.

Spiced Cranberry and Brie Crostini

This sweet and savory appetizer is a perfect blend of flavors and textures. Start by spreading a layer of creamy brie cheese on toasted baguette slices, then top with a spoonful of spiced cranberry compote. The combination of tangy cheese, sweet cranberries, and crunchy bread is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8 oz) wheel of brie cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup spiced cranberry compote
  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Roasted Butternut Squash and Apple Soup Shooters

These bite-sized soup shooters are a delicious and comforting way to warm up on a chilly winter evening. Simply puree roasted butternut squash and apple in a blender, then ladle into small cups and garnish with a sprinkle of crumbled crackers and a dollop of sour cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 apple, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup crumbled crackers
  • Sour cream, for serving

Maple Bacon Wrapped Dates

These sweet and savory dates are a perfect combination of flavors and textures. Simply wrap dates with maple bacon, then bake until crispy. Serve warm and enjoy the oohs and aahs from your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of dates, pitted
  • 6 slices of maple bacon
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Dip

This classic dip is a crowd-pleaser, and it’s easy to make in large quantities. Simply combine cooked spinach, artichoke hearts, cream cheese, and Parmesan cheese in a bowl, then serve with crudités or crackers.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (14 oz) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1 package frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup crudités or crackers, for serving

Mini Beef Wellingtons

These bite-sized Wellingtons are a fun twist on the classic dish. Simply wrap mini puff pastry sheets around beef and mushroom duxelles, then bake until golden brown. Serve warm and enjoy the oohs and aahs from your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of puff pastry, thawed
  • 1/2 cup beef and mushroom duxelles
  • 1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Spicy Shrimp Ceviche

This refreshing appetizer is perfect for a winter gathering. Simply combine cooked shrimp, lime juice, and diced mango in a bowl, then serve with tortilla chips or toasted bread.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/2 cup diced mango
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 bag tortilla chips or 1 baguette, sliced

Mini Quiches Lorraine

These bite-sized quiches are a perfect appetizer for a winter gathering. Simply combine eggs, cream, and grated cheese in a bowl, then pour into mini muffin tin cups and bake until golden brown. Serve warm and enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 dozen eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced ham
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package of mini muffin tin cups

Bacon Wrapped Scallops

These sweet and savory scallops are a perfect combination of flavors and textures. Simply wrap scallops with bacon, then bake until crispy. Serve warm and enjoy the oohs and aahs from your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound scallops
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Creamy Broccoli and Cheddar Fritters

These crispy fritters are a delicious and comforting way to warm up on a chilly winter evening. Simply combine cooked broccoli, cream cheese, and grated cheddar cheese in a bowl, then pour into mini muffin tin cups and bake until golden brown. Serve warm and enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of broccoli, steamed and chopped
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package of mini muffin tin cups

Mini Chicken Tacos

These bite-sized tacos are a fun twist on the classic dish. Simply combine cooked chicken, diced tomatoes, and shredded lettuce in a bowl, then serve in mini taco shells with crumbled cheese and sour cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 package of mini taco shells
  • Salt and pepper to taste

These 10 appetizers are sure to impress your guests and keep them warm and cozy throughout the evening. Whether you’re hosting a holiday party or just want to spend a cozy night in with friends and family, these recipes are sure to be a hit. So go ahead, gather ‘round, and enjoy the warmth and comfort of a cozy winter gathering.
