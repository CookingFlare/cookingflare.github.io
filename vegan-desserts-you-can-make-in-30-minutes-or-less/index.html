Vegan Desserts You Can Make in 30 Minutes or Less


When it comes to satisfying your sweet tooth, vegan desserts can be both delicious and quick to prepare. Whether you’re a full-time vegan, a curious foodie, or just someone looking to whip up a sweet treat on a busy day, these vegan desserts can be made in 30 minutes or less! Get ready to explore a variety of delectable options that are not only easy to make but also kind to your body and the planet.

1. Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup (or agave syrup)
  • 1/4 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Avocados: Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a blender or food processor.
  2. Blend Ingredients: Add cocoa powder, maple syrup, almond milk, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and Adjust: Taste the mousse and adjust sweetness if necessary by adding more maple syrup.
  4. Chill and Serve: Transfer the mousse to serving bowls and refrigerate for about 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy this rich and creamy dessert!

Tips:

  • For an extra touch, top with fresh berries or shredded coconut before serving.
  • This mousse can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.

2. Banana Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup almond butter (or any nut butter)
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips (vegan)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mash Bananas: In a large bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Add rolled oats, almond butter, chocolate chips, cinnamon, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  4. Form Cookies: Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of the mixture onto a lined baking sheet, flattening them slightly.
  5. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Allow to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Tips:

  • Feel free to add nuts, dried fruit, or seeds to the cookie mixture for added texture and flavor.
  • These cookies are perfect for breakfast or a quick snack!

3. Vegan Chocolate Chip Mug Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons dark chocolate chips (vegan)

Instructions:

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add Wet Ingredients: Add maple syrup, almond milk, and melted coconut oil. Stir until smooth.
  3. Fold in Chocolate Chips: Gently fold in the dark chocolate chips.
  4. Microwave: Microwave on high for 1-1.5 minutes, checking for doneness. The cake should rise and look set.
  5. Cool and Enjoy: Allow to cool for a minute before digging in.

Tips:

  • Top with a dollop of vegan ice cream or whipped coconut cream for an indulgent treat.
  • This mug cake is perfect for satisfying a late-night craving!

4. No-Bake Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips (vegan)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, combine rolled oats, peanut butter, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  2. Press into Pan: Line an 8x8 inch baking dish with parchment paper. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of the dish.
  3. Melt Chocolate: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the dark chocolate chips until smooth, stirring every 30 seconds.
  4. Top Bars: Pour the melted chocolate over the pressed mixture, spreading it evenly.
  5. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes until set. Once firm, cut into bars and enjoy!

Tips:

  • Store these bars in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.
  • Feel free to substitute almond butter or sunflower seed butter for a different flavor.

5. Coconut Chia Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup coconut milk (canned or carton)
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruit for topping (mango, berries, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together chia seeds, coconut milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until combined.
  2. Let Sit: Allow the mixture to sit for about 5 minutes, then whisk again to prevent clumping.
  3. Refrigerate: Cover and refrigerate for at least 20 minutes or until it thickens to a pudding-like consistency.
  4. Serve: Once thickened, divide into serving bowls and top with fresh fruit.

Tips:

  • For added texture, sprinkle with nuts or granola before serving.
  • This pudding can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for a quick breakfast or snack.

6. Raspberry Almond Energy Balls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pitted dates
  • 1/2 cup almonds
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh or frozen raspberries
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Blend Ingredients: In a food processor, combine dates, almonds, oats, coconut flakes, raspberries, and salt. Blend until the mixture sticks together.
  2. Form Balls: Scoop out small portions and roll them into bite-sized balls.
  3. Chill: Place the energy balls in the refrigerator for about 10 minutes before serving.

Tips:

  • These energy balls are perfect for a quick snack on the go.
  • Experiment with different fruits and nuts to customize your energy bites.

7. Strawberry Banana Nice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, frozen
  • 1 cup strawberries, frozen
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup (optional)
  • A splash of almond milk (if needed)

Instructions:

  1. Blend Ingredients: In a high-speed blender or food processor, combine frozen bananas and strawberries. Blend until creamy and smooth.
  2. Adjust Consistency: If necessary, add a splash of almond milk to help the blending process.
  3. Serve Immediately: Scoop the nice cream into bowls and enjoy immediately, or freeze for later.

Tips:

  • Top with nuts, chocolate chips, or granola for added crunch.
  • This nice cream is a fun and healthy alternative to traditional ice cream!

Conclusion

These vegan desserts are not only quick and easy to prepare but also incredibly delicious. Whether you’re craving something rich and chocolatey or light and fruity, there’s a dessert here for everyone. Perfect for busy weeknights or impromptu gatherings, these recipes will have you creating delightful treats in no time. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and indulge in these scrumptious vegan desserts that are sure to impress! Enjoy your sweet creations guilt-free!
