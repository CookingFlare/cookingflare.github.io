Sous Vide Honey Glazed Carrots: A Sweet and Savory Side

3 min · 616 words

Carrots are a versatile and nutritious vegetable that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. One of the most delicious and unique methods of preparing carrots is through the sous vide cooking technique. Sous vide allows the carrots to cook gently and evenly, resulting in a tender and flavorful dish. In this recipe, we will explore how to create Sous Vide Honey Glazed Carrots – a dish that combines the natural sweetness of carrots with the rich flavor of honey.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of carrots, peeled and cut into even-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 185°F (85°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the carrots, butter, honey, salt, and black pepper. Toss until the carrots are evenly coated.

  3. Transfer the carrots to a vacuum-sealed bag or a ziplock bag, making sure to spread them out in a single layer. Remove as much air as possible from the bag before sealing it.

  4. Gently lower the bag into the preheated water bath, ensuring that the carrots are fully submerged. Cook for 1 hour, allowing the flavors to meld and the carrots to become tender.

  5. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully remove the bag from the water bath. Open the bag and transfer the carrots to a serving dish, reserving the cooking liquid.

  6. In a small saucepan, bring the reserved cooking liquid to a boil over medium heat. Allow the liquid to reduce until it becomes a thick glaze, approximately 5-7 minutes.

  7. Pour the glaze over the carrots, ensuring that they are evenly coated. Toss gently to combine.

  8. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired, and serve immediately.

Serving Suggestions

Sous Vide Honey Glazed Carrots make a perfect side dish for a variety of meals. They pair well with roasted meats, such as chicken or beef, and can add a touch of sweetness to any meal. These carrots also make a great addition to salads or grain bowls, adding both flavor and color.

Tips and Tricks

  • To enhance the natural sweetness of the carrots, choose fresh and vibrant ones from your local market.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of honey to add unique flavors to the dish. For a milder taste, use clover honey, while a darker honey, such as buckwheat honey, will provide a more robust flavor.
  • If you don’t have a sous vide machine, you can still achieve similar results by cooking the carrots in a pot of simmering water on the stove. Just make sure to monitor the temperature and cooking time carefully.

In Conclusion

Sous Vide Honey Glazed Carrots are a delightful side dish that combines the earthy sweetness of carrots with the richness of honey. The sous vide cooking technique ensures that the carrots are perfectly cooked and infused with flavor. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking for a way to elevate your weeknight meals, this recipe is sure to impress. Give it a try and savor the sweet and savory flavors of these delicious glazed carrots.
