Easy as Pie: 5-Ingredient Cherry Tart Recipe


Looking for a delightful dessert that’s as easy to make as it is delicious? Look no further than this 5-ingredient cherry tart recipe! Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet treat, this tart is sure to impress with its simplicity and vibrant flavors. Let’s dive right into how you can create this mouthwatering dessert in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry: This serves as the flaky, buttery base of our tart. You can find puff pastry in the frozen section of most grocery stores, making it a convenient option for quick desserts.

  • 1 can of cherry pie filling: Opt for a good-quality cherry pie filling to ensure rich, fruity flavors. Alternatively, you can make your own filling using fresh cherries if they’re in season.

  • 1 tablespoon of sugar: A touch of sweetness enhances the tartness of the cherries and balances the flavors perfectly.

  • 1 egg: This will be used for an egg wash to give the pastry a golden, shiny finish.

  • Powdered sugar for dusting: A final sprinkle of powdered sugar adds a touch of elegance and sweetness to the finished tart.

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Puff Pastry

Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). While the oven is heating up, take your sheet of puff pastry out of the freezer and let it thaw slightly at room temperature. Puff pastry is easier to work with when it’s just slightly softened but still cold.

Step 2: Roll Out the Pastry

On a lightly floured surface, gently roll out the puff pastry sheet into a rectangle or circle, depending on your preference and the shape of your baking dish. Aim for a thickness of about 1/8 inch (3 mm).

Step 3: Transfer to Baking Dish

Carefully transfer the rolled-out puff pastry onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or into a tart pan if you have one. If using a baking sheet, you can leave the edges of the pastry slightly raised to create a rustic tart look.

Step 4: Add the Cherry Filling

Spread the cherry pie filling evenly over the puff pastry, leaving about a 1-inch border around the edges. If you’re feeling adventurous, you can dot a few fresh cherries on top of the filling for an extra burst of flavor and visual appeal.

Step 5: Fold and Seal

Gently fold the edges of the puff pastry over the filling, creating a rustic crust that partially covers the cherries. Press lightly to seal any loose corners or edges, ensuring the filling stays contained as it bakes.

Step 6: Egg Wash

In a small bowl, beat the egg with a tablespoon of water to create an egg wash. Brush the edges of the puff pastry with the egg wash using a pastry brush. This will give the tart a beautiful golden color as it bakes.

Step 7: Bake

Place the assembled tart in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the pastry is puffed and golden brown. Keep an eye on it towards the end of the baking time to prevent over-browning.

Step 8: Cool and Dust

Once baked, remove the cherry tart from the oven and let it cool slightly on a wire rack. Before serving, dust the top of the tart with powdered sugar for a finishing touch of sweetness and elegance.

Serving Suggestions:

This 5-ingredient cherry tart is delicious served warm or at room temperature. It pairs wonderfully with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat. The combination of flaky pastry and sweet-tart cherries makes it a versatile dessert that’s perfect for any occasion, from casual gatherings to formal dinners.

Tips for Success:

  • Use Quality Ingredients: Since this recipe relies on minimal ingredients, using high-quality puff pastry and cherry pie filling will elevate the final result.

  • Adjust Sweetness: Depending on your preference and the sweetness of your cherries, you can adjust the amount of sugar added to the filling.

  • Experiment with Variations: Feel free to experiment with different fruit fillings such as blueberries, peaches, or apples to create your own unique tart flavors.

Conclusion:

With just 5 simple ingredients and a few easy steps, you can create a stunning cherry tart that looks and tastes like it came from a fancy bakery. Whether you’re a novice in the kitchen or an experienced baker looking for a quick dessert fix, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. Enjoy the buttery crispness of the pastry, the burst of fruity cherry flavor, and the joy of sharing this delightful treat with family and friends. Happy baking!
