Satisfying Mediterranean Beef Kofta Recipe


In the world of Middle Eastern cuisine, koftas are a staple dish that never fails to impress. These juicy, flavorful meatballs are a popular street food in many Mediterranean countries, including Turkey, Greece, and Lebanon. Today, we're taking a trip to the Mediterranean coast and bringing back a mouth-watering recipe that will transport your taste buds to the sun-kissed beaches and charming markets of the region. The Mediterranean Beef Kofta recipe we're sharing today is a delicious twist on the classic dish, featuring succulent beef mixed with aromatic spices, herbs, and a hint of tanginess. This recipe is perfect for a casual dinner party, a family gathering, or a romantic evening in with your loved ones. Ingredients:

1 pound ground beef 1 onion, finely chopped 2 cloves of garlic, minced 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar 1 teaspoon paprika 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon coriander 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional) 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese 20 bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

Preheat your grill or grill pan: Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. If using a grill, make sure to oil the grates to prevent the koftas from sticking. Prepare the kofta mixture: In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, chopped onion, minced garlic, olive oil, white wine vinegar, paprika, cumin, coriander, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using). Mix everything together with your hands or a wooden spoon until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the mixture, as this can make the koftas dense and heavy. Add the herbs and feta: Stir in chopped parsley, mint, and crumbled feta cheese. Mix until well combined. Shape the koftas: Use your hands to shape the mixture into small oblong shapes, about 1 1/2 inches long and 1 inch in diameter. You should end up with around 20-25 koftas. Thread the koftas: Thread a kofta onto each bamboo skewer, leaving a small space between each one. Grill the koftas: Place the koftas on the preheated grill or grill pan and cook for 5-7 minutes per side, or until they're nicely charred and cooked through. Serve and enjoy: Remove the koftas from the grill and serve hot with your favorite sides, such as hummus, tabbouleh, or a simple green salad.

Tips and Variations:

To add some extra flavor, try marinating the kofta mixture in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, and chopped herbs for at least 30 minutes before grilling. For a vegetarian or vegan version, substitute the beef with ground lamb, turkey, or a combination of vegetables. Experiment with different types of cheese, such as ricotta or goat cheese, for an unique flavor profile. Try using a mixture of ground meats, such as beef and lamb, for a richer flavor.

Nutritional Information:

Per serving (2 koftas): Calories: 220 Protein: 22g Fat: 14g Saturated fat: 4g Cholesterol: 60mg Sodium: 250mg Carbohydrates: 6g Fiber: 1g Sugar: 1g

Conclusion: This Mediterranean Beef Kofta recipe is a hearty and flavorful dish that's perfect for any occasion. With its bold flavors and vibrant colors, it's sure to impress your friends and family. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a kitchen novice, this recipe is easy to follow and requires minimal ingredients. So, fire up your grill and get ready to transport yourself to the Mediterranean with this delicious and mouth-watering kofta recipe! Timeline:

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 15-20 minutes Total Time: 35-40 minutes

Difficulty Level:

Easy to medium

Servings:

Serves 4-6 people

Note: Enjoy this recipe with a side of pita bread, hummus, or a simple green salad for a well-rounded and satisfying meal.
