Fourth of July BBQ Pizza Recipes for a Festive Twist


The Fourth of July is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with some delicious BBQ pizza recipes? This year, let’s take your backyard BBQ to the next level with these mouthwatering creations that combine the smoky flavors of barbecue with the cheesy goodness of pizza. Get ready to impress your friends and family with these festive twists on a classic favorite!

1. Grilled BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Cooked chicken, shredded
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Brush one side of the dough with barbecue sauce.
  4. Place the dough, sauce side down, on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom is lightly charred.
  5. Flip the dough over and spread a layer of barbecue sauce on the cooked side.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded chicken, sliced red onion, and mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  7. Close the grill lid and cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the grill and sprinkle with chopped cilantro.
  9. Slice and serve hot.

2. Smoked Brisket Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Smoked brisket, thinly sliced
  • Red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Cheddar cheese, shredded

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or smoker to 250°F (121°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Brush one side of the dough with barbecue sauce.
  4. Place the dough, sauce side down, on a pizza stone or directly on the grill grate.
  5. Close the lid and cook for about 5 minutes, or until the bottom is lightly browned.
  6. Flip the dough over and spread a layer of barbecue sauce on the cooked side.
  7. Arrange the smoked brisket, red bell pepper, and red onion evenly over the sauce.
  8. Sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese.
  9. Close the lid and cook for another 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove the pizza from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

3. Grilled Veggie Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • Zucchini, sliced
  • Yellow squash, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Brush one side of the dough with olive oil.
  4. Place the dough, oiled side down, on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom is lightly charred.
  5. Flip the dough over and brush the cooked side with olive oil.
  6. Arrange the sliced zucchini, yellow squash, red onion, bell peppers, and cherry tomatoes evenly over the dough.
  7. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Close the grill lid and cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the grill and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  10. Slice and serve warm.

Conclusion

These Fourth of July BBQ pizza recipes are sure to be a hit at your festive celebration. Whether you prefer the tangy flavors of grilled BBQ chicken, the smoky goodness of smoked brisket, or the vibrant colors of grilled veggies, there’s a pizza here for everyone. So fire up your grill, gather your loved ones, and enjoy these delicious creations that combine the best of both worlds. Happy Fourth of July!

Note: Remember to adjust cooking times and temperatures according to your specific grill or smoker.
