Savory Keto BBQ Pulled Pork: A Low-Carb Twist on a Classic


Are you craving the delicious taste of BBQ pulled pork, but don’t want to derail your low-carb keto diet? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a savory and satisfying keto-friendly version of this classic dish. Get ready to indulge in tender, flavorful pulled pork without the guilt!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds pork shoulder roast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup sugar-free BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for some heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepping the Pork: Begin by trimming any excess fat from the pork shoulder roast. Season the roast generously with salt and pepper, ensuring that all sides are coated.

  2. Searing the Pork: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the pork roast and sear it on all sides until browned. This process helps to lock in the flavors and create a delicious crust.

  3. Slow Cooker Magic: Transfer the seared pork roast to a slow cooker. Add the chicken broth, sugar-free BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, ground cumin, and cayenne pepper (if using). Gently mix the ingredients together, ensuring that the pork is evenly coated with the flavorful sauce.

  4. The Low and Slow Cook: Set your slow cooker to low heat and cook the pork for 8-10 hours. Slow cooking allows the flavors to meld together and results in incredibly tender meat that falls apart effortlessly.

  5. Shredding the Pork: Once the cooking time is up, remove the pork from the slow cooker and transfer it to a cutting board. Using two forks, shred the pork into bite-sized pieces. The meat should be so tender that it practically falls apart!

  6. The Finishing Touch: Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and mix it with the remaining sauce. Let it cook for an additional 15-30 minutes on low heat to allow the flavors to infuse the meat even more.

  7. Serving Suggestions: Serve your savory keto BBQ pulled pork on a bed of crisp lettuce leaves for a low-carb option. You can also enjoy it on cloud bread or low-carb buns if you prefer a more traditional sandwich experience. Feel free to top it with some sliced avocado or a dollop of sugar-free coleslaw for added freshness and texture.

  8. Storing and Reheating: Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. To reheat, simply place the desired amount of pulled pork in a microwave-safe dish and heat on medium power for 1-2 minutes, or until heated through.

Conclusion

With this savory keto BBQ pulled pork recipe, you can enjoy the smoky, tangy flavors of a classic favorite while staying true to your low-carb lifestyle. The tender, melt-in-your-mouth pork paired with the delicious sugar-free BBQ sauce is a match made in keto heaven. So gather your ingredients, fire up your slow cooker, and get ready to savor every bite of this guilt-free indulgence!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation, so feel free to adjust the seasonings and spices to suit your taste buds. This recipe is not only keto-friendly but also versatile enough to be enjoyed by the whole family. So why wait? Dive into this low-carb twist on a classic and experience the mouthwatering satisfaction firsthand. Happy cooking!
