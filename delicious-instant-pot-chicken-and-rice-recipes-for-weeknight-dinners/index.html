Delicious Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Recipes for Weeknight Dinners


When it comes to weeknight dinners, finding a meal that is both quick to prepare and satisfying can often be a challenge. Fortunately, the Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking, allowing you to whip up delicious meals in a fraction of the time. One of the most beloved combinations for a hearty dinner is chicken and rice. This versatile duo can be flavored in myriad ways, making it perfect for any palate. In this post, we’ll explore several Instant Pot chicken and rice recipes that are sure to become staples in your weeknight rotation.

The Benefits of Cooking with an Instant Pot

The Instant Pot is not just a trendy kitchen gadget; it’s a powerful tool that can help you save time and energy in the kitchen. Here are a few reasons to embrace this handy appliance:

  • Speed: The pressure cooking function significantly reduces cooking time. What might take hours on the stovetop can be done in minutes.
  • Flavor: The sealed environment traps steam and flavors, resulting in tender, juicy chicken and perfectly cooked rice.
  • Versatility: From hearty stews to delicate rice dishes, the Instant Pot can handle it all.
  • Ease of Use: With just a few buttons to press, even novice cooks can create impressive meals.

Now that we’ve established the benefits of the Instant Pot, let’s dive into some mouthwatering chicken and rice recipes that will elevate your weeknight dinners.

Classic Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs chicken thighs (boneless, skinless)
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Turn the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add olive oil and let it heat. Once hot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Brown the Chicken: Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, paprika, and Italian seasoning. Add the chicken to the pot and brown on both sides for about 5 minutes.

  3. Add Rice and Broth: Pour in the rice, making sure it is evenly distributed. Then, add the chicken broth. Stir gently to combine.

  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and ensure the valve is set to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural release for 10 minutes before performing a quick release.

  5. Fluff and Serve: Open the lid and fluff the rice with a fork. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm.

Lemon Garlic Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon (juice and zest)
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Lemon slices, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Base: Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot. Melt the butter and add the chopped onion and garlic. Cook until the onion is soft, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Cook the Chicken: Add the diced chicken breast to the pot and season with salt, pepper, and thyme. Sauté until the chicken is lightly browned, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add Rice and Liquid: Stir in jasmine rice, lemon juice, and zest. Pour in the chicken stock and mix well.

  4. Pressure Cooking: Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes. Allow for a natural release for 5 minutes before releasing any remaining pressure.

  5. Serve: Fluff the rice and serve with lemon slices for a refreshing touch.

Spicy Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs chicken legs (thighs and drumsticks)
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14 oz)
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Onion and Garlic: Using the sauté mode, heat olive oil in the Instant Pot. Add chopped onion and garlic. Cook until fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Brown the Chicken: Season the chicken with salt, pepper, chili powder, and cumin. Add to the pot and brown on all sides.

  3. Combine Ingredients: Pour in the diced tomatoes (with juices) and chicken broth. Stir to combine, making sure to scrape the bottom of the pot to prevent burning.

  4. Add Rice: Sprinkle the basmati rice over the chicken mixture without stirring it in.

  5. Pressure Cook: Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 12 minutes. Allow for a natural release for 10 minutes, then perform a quick release.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Fluff the rice and garnish with fresh cilantro. This dish pairs well with a side of avocado or a simple salad.

Creamy Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breasts
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: On the sauté setting, heat olive oil and add diced onion and garlic. Cook until onion is translucent, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Brown the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and oregano. Add them to the pot and brown for about 5 minutes.

  3. Add Rice and Broth: Stir in arborio rice and pour in chicken broth. Mix to combine.

  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Allow for a natural release for 5 minutes, then release any remaining pressure.

  5. Finish with Cream and Peas: Open the lid, stir in the heavy cream and frozen peas. Let it sit for a few minutes to warm the peas through.

  6. Serve: Spoon into bowls and top with grated Parmesan cheese for a rich, creamy finish.

Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

  • Adjust Cooking Times: Different cuts of chicken and types of rice may require slight adjustments in cooking times. Always refer to the Instant Pot guidelines for best results.
  • Use Quality Broth: The flavor of your dish greatly depends on the quality of chicken broth you use. Opt for low-sodium options if you’re watching your salt intake.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Don’t be afraid to mix in different spices, herbs, or vegetables to create a dish that suits your taste preferences.
  • Rest Before Serving: Allowing the dish to rest for a few minutes after cooking helps the flavors meld and improves the overall texture.

Conclusion

With these delicious Instant Pot chicken and rice recipes, weeknight dinners can be a breeze. Each recipe offers a unique twist on the classic combination, ensuring that you never get bored. The Instant Pot not only saves time but also infuses your meals with rich flavors that your family will love. Don’t hesitate to experiment and make these recipes your own. Happy cooking!
