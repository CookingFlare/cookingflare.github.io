Grilled Sausage Party: Brats, Hot Dogs, and More


Summer is the season of outdoor gatherings, barbecues, and, of course, delicious grilled sausages. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ, a tailgate party, or simply enjoying a cozy evening with friends, there’s something undeniably satisfying about the sizzle of sausages on the grill. In this post, we’ll dive into the world of grilled sausages, focusing on brats, hot dogs, and an array of tantalizing toppings and sides that will make your sausage party a hit!

The Basics of Grilling Sausages

Grilling sausages might appear straightforward, but there are techniques and tips that can elevate your sausage experience. Here’s a quick guide to getting started:

Choosing Your Sausages

The first step to a successful sausage party is selecting the right sausages. Here are some popular options:

  • Bratwurst: A German favorite, brats are typically made from pork and seasoned with a blend of spices. They’re juicy and flavorful, perfect for grilling.
  • Hot Dogs: The classic American staple, hot dogs are versatile and come in various types, including beef, pork, turkey, and even plant-based options.
  • Italian Sausages: These sausages are seasoned with fennel and other herbs, offering a distinct flavor that pairs well with peppers and onions.
  • Chorizo: A spicy, flavorful sausage that can add a kick to your grill party. It’s often made from pork and seasoned with smoked paprika.
  • Kielbasa: A Polish sausage that is hearty and flavorful, perfect for grilling and serving with mustard and sauerkraut.

Preparing the Grill

Before you start grilling, it’s essential to prepare your grill correctly. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Clean the Grill Grates: Ensure your grill grates are clean to prevent sticking. Use a grill brush to scrub away any residue from previous cookouts.
  2. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. This helps achieve that perfect char while cooking the sausages evenly.
  3. Oil the Grates: Lightly oil the grill grates with a paper towel dipped in vegetable oil to reduce sticking.

Grilling Techniques

To achieve perfectly grilled sausages, follow these techniques:

  • Direct Heat: For most sausages, grilling them over direct heat is ideal. Place them directly over the flames for that perfect sear.
  • Avoid Piercing: Don’t pierce the sausages with a fork. This allows the juices to escape, leading to dry sausages.
  • Turn Regularly: Rotate the sausages every few minutes to ensure even cooking and prevent burning.

Delicious Sausage Recipes

Now that you have the basics down, let’s explore some mouthwatering recipes that are sure to impress your guests.

Classic Bratwurst with Sauerkraut

Ingredients:

  • 6 bratwursts
  • 2 cups sauerkraut
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup beer (optional)
  • Mustard for serving
  • Fresh buns

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté the onion until translucent. Add sauerkraut and beer (if using), and let simmer for about 10 minutes.
  3. Grill the bratwursts for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are nicely browned and cooked through.
  4. Serve the bratwursts in fresh buns topped with the warm sauerkraut and a drizzle of mustard.

Gourmet Hot Dogs

Ingredients:

  • 6 gourmet hot dogs (beef, turkey, or plant-based)
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup pico de gallo
  • ½ cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Hot sauce (optional)
  • Fresh buns

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the hot dogs for 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally until heated through and grill marks appear.
  3. Assemble the hot dogs in buns and top with diced avocado, pico de gallo, shredded cheese, and a sprinkle of cilantro. Drizzle with hot sauce if desired.

Spicy Chorizo and Pepper Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chorizo sausage, cut into chunks
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), cut into squares
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Skewers (soaked in water if wooden)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Thread the chorizo, bell pepper, and onion onto the skewers, alternating between the ingredients.
  3. Brush with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally until the chorizo is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

Italian Sausage with Peppers and Onions

Ingredients:

  • 6 Italian sausages
  • 2 bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh Italian rolls

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, toss the sliced peppers and onions with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the sausages for about 15-20 minutes, turning regularly.
  4. In a grill basket, add the peppers and onions and grill until they are tender and slightly charred.
  5. Serve the sausages in Italian rolls topped with the grilled peppers and onions.

Sizzling Sides and Toppings

A sausage party wouldn’t be complete without delicious sides and toppings to complement your main dish. Here are some ideas:

Grilled Corn on the Cob

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: chili powder, lime juice, cotija cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the corn for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally until charred and tender.
  3. Brush with butter and season with salt and pepper. For a Mexican twist, add chili powder, lime juice, and crumbled cotija cheese.

Classic Potato Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds potatoes, diced
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil the potatoes in salted water until tender. Drain and let cool.
  2. In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, mustard, celery, onion, salt, and pepper.
  3. Fold in the cooled potatoes and toss to combine. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.

Refreshing Coleslaw

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shredded cabbage and carrots, tossing to coat.
  3. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Drinks to Pair with Your Sausage Feast

When it comes to beverages, consider offering a selection that complements the flavors of your grilled sausages. Here are some suggestions:

  • Craft Beer: A variety of craft beers can enhance your sausage experience. Consider local brews or lagers that pair well with grilled foods.
  • Refreshing Lemonade: A classic summer drink, lemonade is a refreshing option that balances the savory flavors of grilled sausages.
  • Iced Tea: Offer sweetened and unsweetened options for guests to enjoy with their meal.

Conclusion

Hosting a grilled sausage party is a fantastic way to enjoy the summer with friends and family. With a variety of sausages like brats and hot dogs, complemented by delicious sides and refreshing drinks, your gathering will undoubtedly be memorable. Remember, the key to a successful sausage party lies in the quality of your ingredients, the preparation of your grill, and the joy of sharing delicious food with loved ones. So fire up that grill and get ready for a feast of flavors that will have everyone coming back for seconds! Enjoy your grilling adventure!
