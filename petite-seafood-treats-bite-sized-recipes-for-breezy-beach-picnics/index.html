Petite Seafood Treats: Bite-Sized Recipes for Breezy Beach Picnics


The beach is all about relaxation, sun, and fun – but let’s not forget the essential component that binds all these together: delicious food! But finding the perfect beach picnic recipes doesn’t have to be a hassle. We’ve curated a list of bite-sized, petite seafood treats to satisfy your cravings on your breezy beach day. So, put on your flip flops, pack your cooler, and let’s dive into some scrumptious and easy-to-make beach picnic ideas!

  1. Mini Shrimp Ceviche Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of juicy shrimp, small size, peeled, and deveined
  • 1/2 white onion, finely diced
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1 avocado, diced into mini cubes
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, finely diced
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the shrimp by cooking them in boiling water for 2 minutes and then quickly chilling in ice water to preserve that fresh flavor.

  2. Shell the shrimp and cut them into tiny, bite-sized pieces.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the shrimp with the onion, lime juice, diced avocado, bell pepper, and cilantro. Mix gently but thoroughly to incorporate all the ingredients.

  4. Season with salt and pepper to finalize your tasty ceviche bites.

  5. Grab some endive leaves to hold these tasty morsels and serve.

  6. Dainty Smoked Salmon Pinwheels

Ingredients:

  • Cream cheese, softened
  • 1 package of reduced-fat cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup of finely chopped dill
  • Lemon pepper seasoning
  • 1 package of smoked salmon (make sure it’s of good quality)
  • Sliced cucumber and carrots for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Combine the softened cream cheese and dill in a mixing bowl.

  2. Season the cream cheese mixture with lemon pepper seasoning.

  3. Spread a thin layer of the dill cream cheese mixture onto the smoked salmon.

  4. Roll up the salmon and cream cheese into small pinwheels and chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to set.

  5. Cut the chilled pinwheels into bite-sized pieces and garnish the cold smoked salmon appetizers with sliced cucumber and carrots.

  6. Petite Crab Cake Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of pasteurized in the shell crab meat
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped red onion
  • 3 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 tablespoons of spicy mustard
  • 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Panko breadcrumbs

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the crab meat with the bell pepper, onion, parsley, mustard, mayonnaise, Worcestershire sauce, egg, salt, and pepper.

  2. Portion and shape the crab mixture into small bite-sized patties, then coat in panko breadcrumbs.

  3. Place the patties on a baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.

  4. Serve these savory crab cake bites warm alongside your other picnic treats.

  5. Mini Seafood Gazpacho Shot Glasses

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper (mix of colors), chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cubed baguette bread
  • 2 pounds of peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 1 small jalapeño, seeded and chopped
  • 2 cups of good quality broth (chicken or seafood)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Blend the cucumber, tomatoes, bell pepper, red onion, baguette cubes, shrimp, jalapeño, broth, salt, and pepper in a blender until smooth.
  2. Chill the gazpacho mixture in the refrigerator for 1-2 hours.
  3. In shot glasses, pour the chilled gazpacho and garnish with chopped chives.

These delightful petite seafood treats will bring the ocean right to your breezy beach picnic! Don’t forget to pair these dishes with some refreshing drinks, and prepare to have an unforgettable day by the shore! Just imagine the satisfied smiles from your family and friends after taking a bite from these delectable appetizers. Happy beaching!
