Healthy Instant Pot Cauliflower Buffalo Wings Recipes for a Spicy Snack


Are you craving a spicy and satisfying snack? Look no further than these Healthy Instant Pot Cauliflower Buffalo Wings! These delicious bites are a healthier alternative to traditional buffalo wings, made with tender cauliflower florets and a spicy buffalo sauce. Thanks to the Instant Pot, they are quick and easy to make, perfect for a game day appetizer or a tasty snack any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (or gluten-free flour for a gluten-free version)
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 tablespoons melted vegan butter (or regular butter if not following a vegan diet)
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing, for dipping (optional)
  • Celery sticks, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, almond milk, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper until smooth. This will be the batter for coating the cauliflower.

  2. Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, ensuring it is completely coated. Shake off any excess batter and place the coated florets onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  3. Preheat your Instant Pot by selecting the “Saute” function. Once hot, add the buffalo sauce and melted butter to the pot. Stir well to combine.

  4. Transfer the coated cauliflower florets into the Instant Pot, gently tossing them in the buffalo sauce mixture to coat evenly.

  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 3 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.

  7. Carefully remove the cauliflower buffalo wings from the Instant Pot and transfer them back to the parchment-lined baking sheet.

  8. Preheat your oven to broil mode. Place the baking sheet with the cauliflower wings on the top rack of the oven and broil for 2-3 minutes, or until they are golden and slightly crispy.

  9. Serve the cauliflower buffalo wings hot with ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping, and celery sticks on the side, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a few dashes of hot sauce to the buffalo sauce mixture.
  • If you don’t have an Instant Pot, you can also bake the cauliflower buffalo wings in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  • Feel free to experiment with different spices or seasonings in the batter to suit your taste preferences.
  • To make the buffalo sauce from scratch, combine equal parts hot sauce and melted butter, adjusting the ratio to your desired level of spiciness.

These Healthy Instant Pot Cauliflower Buffalo Wings are the perfect guilt-free snack for any occasion. They are packed with flavor, easy to make, and a great way to enjoy the classic buffalo wing taste without the added calories. So go ahead and spice up your snacking routine with these delicious cauliflower wings – you won’t be disappointed!
