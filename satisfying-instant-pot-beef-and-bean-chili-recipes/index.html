Satisfying Instant Pot Beef and Bean Chili Recipes


Are you in the mood for a hearty and comforting meal that’s packed with flavor? Look no further than these satisfying Instant Pot beef and bean chili recipes! Whether you’re hosting a game day gathering, feeding a hungry family, or simply craving a delicious dinner, these recipes are sure to please. The magic of the Instant Pot allows you to achieve rich, complex flavors in a fraction of the time it would take with traditional cooking methods. Let’s dive into the world of savory chili and discover two delightful variations that will become staples in your recipe collection.

Classic Beef and Bean Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds of beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions, diced avocado

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef cubes and cook until browned on all sides.
  2. Add the diced onion, garlic, and red bell pepper to the pot, and sauté until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, beef broth, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” mode for 25 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully perform a quick release.
  6. Give the chili a good stir, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve the chili hot, topped with your choice of shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions, or diced avocado.

Smoky Chipotle Beef and Bean Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds of beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups of beef stock
  • 2 teaspoons of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: crumbled queso fresco, chopped cilantro, lime wedges, sliced jalapeños

Instructions:

  1. Select the “Sauté” function on the Instant Pot and heat the olive oil. Add the beef stew meat and brown it on all sides.
  2. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and chipotle peppers to the pot, and sauté until the onion is translucent.
  3. Pour in the fire-roasted diced tomatoes, pinto beans, cannellini beans, beef stock, coriander, oregano, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  4. Lock the lid in place and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” mode for 30 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes before performing a quick release.
  6. Give the chili a good stir, and adjust the seasoning to your liking.
  7. Ladle the chili into bowls and garnish with crumbled queso fresco, chopped cilantro, lime wedges, and sliced jalapeños for an extra kick of flavor.

Whether you opt for the classic beef and bean chili or the smoky chipotle variation, these Instant Pot recipes are guaranteed to fill your home with irresistible aromas and your taste buds with satisfaction. Feel free to customize the level of heat and add your favorite toppings to make these dishes your own. Embrace the convenience and delicious results that the Instant Pot brings to your kitchen, and get ready to savor every spoonful of these comforting beef and bean chili creations.

So, fire up your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a bowl of chili that’s as comforting as it is flavorful. Happy cooking!
