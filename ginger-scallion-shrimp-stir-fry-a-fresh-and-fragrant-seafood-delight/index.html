Ginger Scallion Shrimp Stir Fry: A Fresh and Fragrant Seafood Delight


If you’re looking for a quick and flavorful seafood dish, look no further than Ginger Scallion Shrimp Stir Fry. This dish combines the delicate flavors of shrimp with the aromatic punch of ginger and scallions, resulting in a mouthwatering experience that is both fresh and fragrant. With a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, this stir fry is the perfect choice for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of minced ginger
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 scallions, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Stir well to dissolve the cornstarch completely. This will be our sauce for the stir fry.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced ginger and garlic, and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.

  4. Push the shrimp to one side of the skillet and add the sliced scallions and bell peppers. Stir fry for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  5. Give the sauce mixture a quick stir and pour it into the skillet. Toss everything together to coat the shrimp and vegetables evenly with the sauce. Continue cooking for another minute until the sauce thickens slightly.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the stir fry sit for a minute to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve the Ginger Scallion Shrimp Stir Fry over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with additional sliced scallions, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • To add a touch of heat to the dish, you can include a sliced red chili pepper or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir fry according to your preference. Broccoli, snap peas, or mushrooms would be great additions.
  • For a gluten-free version, use tamari instead of soy sauce and ensure the oyster sauce you use is gluten-free.
  • Consider marinating the shrimp in a mixture of soy sauce, ginger, and garlic for 15-20 minutes before cooking to infuse even more flavor into the dish.

Final Thoughts

Ginger Scallion Shrimp Stir Fry is a delightful seafood dish that brings together the vibrant flavors of ginger and scallions with the succulent shrimp. The combination of aromatics and the simplicity of the stir fry technique make this dish a winner in terms of both taste and ease of preparation. Whether you’re a fan of seafood or just looking to add some variety to your weeknight meals, this recipe is sure to impress. Give it a try and savor the freshness and fragrance of this delightful seafood delight!
