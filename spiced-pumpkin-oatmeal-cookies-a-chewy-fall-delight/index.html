Spiced Pumpkin Oatmeal Cookies: A Chewy Fall Delight


As the air turns crisp and the leaves begin to change, there’s no better way to embrace the cozy spirit of fall than by indulging in the warm, comforting flavors of spiced pumpkin oatmeal cookies. These chewy delights are the perfect blend of autumnal spices and wholesome oats, making them an irresistible treat for the season. Whether you’re looking for a sweet snack to accompany your hot cup of tea or a homemade gift to share with friends and family, these spiced pumpkin oatmeal cookies are sure to delight your taste buds and warm your heart.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the pumpkin puree, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and softened butter until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the pumpkin mixture, and beat until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the oats, flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a thick cookie dough forms.
  6. If desired, fold in the chopped walnuts or pecans for an added crunch and nutty flavor.
  7. Using a cookie scoop or spoon, drop rounded tablespoons of the cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie for spreading.
  8. Gently flatten each cookie with the back of a spoon or your fingertips.
  9. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers are set.
  10. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving and Storage

These spiced pumpkin oatmeal cookies are best enjoyed fresh from the oven, when their warm, fragrant aroma fills the air. However, if you have leftovers (which is unlikely!), store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. To reheat and revive the cookies’ chewy texture, simply pop them in the microwave for a few seconds before serving.

The Perfect Pairing

Pair these spiced pumpkin oatmeal cookies with a steaming mug of chai tea or a velvety pumpkin spice latte for the ultimate fall indulgence. The combination of spicy, aromatic flavors will transport you to a cozy autumn day, wrapped in a blanket with a good book or in the company of loved ones.

Embrace Fall with Spiced Pumpkin Oatmeal Cookies

In conclusion, these spiced pumpkin oatmeal cookies are a delightful embodiment of fall flavors, offering a chewy, comforting, and satisfying treat for all occasions. Whether you’re baking them for yourself or sharing them with others, these cookies are sure to bring joy and warmth to your autumn days. So, embrace the season and savor the flavors of fall with every delicious bite of these spiced pumpkin oatmeal cookies.
