Satisfying Italian Wedding Soup Recipe - Hearty and Healthy


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful soup that’s both hearty and healthy, Italian wedding soup is the perfect choice. This classic Italian dish is a delicious combination of savory meatballs, tender vegetables, and delicate pasta, all simmered together in a flavorful broth. Whether you’re serving it as a starter or a main course, this Italian wedding soup is sure to satisfy your cravings and warm your soul.

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Soup:

  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup small pasta (such as orzo or acini de pepe)
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meatballs:

    • In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
    • Shape the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.
    • Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and refrigerate for 30 minutes to help them hold their shape.

  2. Make the Soup:

    • In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a simmer over medium heat.
    • Add the carrots, celery, and onion to the pot and cook for about 5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
    • Gently drop the meatballs into the simmering broth and let them cook for 10 minutes, or until they are cooked through.
    • Add the pasta to the pot and cook for the time indicated on the package, usually around 8-10 minutes.
    • Stir in the chopped spinach and cook for an additional 2 minutes until wilted.
    • Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Ladle the Italian wedding soup into bowls and garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
    • Serve hot and enjoy the heartwarming flavors of this satisfying and healthy Italian wedding soup.

Tips for Success

  • For a leaner option, you can use ground turkey or chicken instead of beef for the meatballs.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables such as diced zucchini or chopped kale to boost the nutritional value of the soup.
  • Make a big batch of this soup and store it in the fridge for a quick and nourishing meal throughout the week.

Final Thoughts

Italian wedding soup is a timeless classic that brings together the wholesome goodness of homemade meatballs, nutritious vegetables, and comforting broth. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a bowl of something warm and nourishing, this recipe is a winner. The combination of flavors and textures in each spoonful will leave you feeling satisfied and content. So, next time you’re in the mood for a hearty and healthy soup, give this Italian wedding soup recipe a try and let its heartwarming goodness delight your taste buds. Buon appetito!
