Easy Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious, comforting meal that requires minimal effort, look no further than this Easy Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan recipe. This dish combines tender, juicy chicken with a flavorful marinara sauce and gooey melted cheese, all cooked to perfection in your slow cooker. Whether you’re feeding a family or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress without requiring hours of your time in the kitchen.

Ingredients

To make this hearty Chicken Parmesan, gather the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup marinara sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup Italian breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish, optional)

Equipment Needed

Before we dive into the cooking process, ensure you have the following equipment:

  • A slow cooker
  • A mixing bowl
  • A measuring cup
  • A spoon for mixing
  • Tongs or a spatula for serving

Preparation Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by patting the chicken breasts dry with paper towels. This step is crucial as it helps the seasoning stick better to the chicken. Season both sides of each chicken breast with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.

Step 2: Create the Sauce Mixture

In a mixing bowl, combine the marinara sauce with dried oregano. This simple mix adds an extra layer of flavor to your dish. If you’re feeling adventurous, you can also add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a bit of heat.

Step 3: Layer the Ingredients in the Slow Cooker

In your slow cooker, pour a small amount of the marinara sauce mixture to cover the bottom. This prevents the chicken from sticking and ensures even cooking. Next, place the seasoned chicken breasts on top of the sauce.

Once the chicken is in place, pour the remaining marinara sauce over the chicken. This helps to keep the chicken moist and infuses it with flavor as it cooks. Finally, sprinkle the Italian breadcrumbs evenly over the top of the sauce. This will create a deliciously crispy topping as it cooks.

Step 4: Add the Cheese

The final touch before the slow cooker works its magic is to sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese on top of the breadcrumbs. The combination of these two cheeses will create a rich, gooey topping that is the hallmark of a good Chicken Parmesan.

Step 5: Cook

Cover your slow cooker and set it to low heat. Allow the Chicken Parmesan to cook for 4-6 hours or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender. If you’re in a hurry, you can also set it to high heat and cook for 2-3 hours, but low and slow is the way to go for maximum flavor.

Serving Your Chicken Parmesan

Once your slow cooker beeps to signal that cooking is complete, carefully remove the lid. The aroma of the bubbling sauce and melted cheese will be irresistible! Using tongs or a spatula, gently lift the chicken out of the slow cooker, allowing any excess sauce to drip off.

Serve your Easy Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan over a bed of cooked pasta, such as spaghetti or fettuccine, for a classic Italian meal. You can also pair it with a side of garlic bread and a fresh green salad for a well-rounded dinner.

Optional Garnishes

For a touch of freshness, garnish your Chicken Parmesan with torn basil leaves just before serving. This not only enhances the visual appeal of the dish but also adds a delightful herbal note that complements the flavors beautifully.

Tips for the Best Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan

1. Use Quality Ingredients

The key to any great dish lies in the quality of its ingredients. Opt for fresh, high-quality chicken breasts and a good marinara sauce. If you have the time, making your marinara sauce from scratch can elevate this dish even further!

2. Donâ€™t Overcook

While slow cooking allows for a more forgiving cooking process, it’s still important to monitor the time. Overcooking the chicken can lead to dryness. Check for doneness around the 4-hour mark if cooking on low.

3. Customize the Recipe

Feel free to customize this Chicken Parmesan recipe to your liking! You can add veggies like spinach or mushrooms to the sauce, switch up the cheese for a unique flavor, or even use chicken thighs for a richer taste.

4. Leftover Magic

If you happen to have leftovers, they make for a fabulous next-day meal. Simply reheat the Chicken Parmesan and serve it in a sub roll for a delicious sandwich, or toss it into a salad for a protein-packed lunch.

Conclusion

This Easy Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan recipe is the perfect solution for busy individuals and families alike. It combines the classic flavors of Italian cuisine with the convenience of a slow cooker, resulting in a dish that’s both comforting and satisfying. Whether it’s a weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering, this Chicken Parmesan is sure to become a staple in your meal rotation.

So, dust off your slow cooker and get ready for a culinary experience that will leave you and your loved ones wanting more. Enjoy the simple pleasure of a home-cooked meal, made easy with this delightful recipe!
