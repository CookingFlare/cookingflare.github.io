No-Sew Pizza Pockets: The Easiest Way to Create Hand-held Pizza Treats


Who can resist the mouthwatering aroma of a warm, cheesy pizza pocket? These handheld delights are messy and oh so indulgent, just like your favorite pizza. But did you know that you can now enjoy these delicious treats without having to spend hours in the kitchen or dealing with complicated recipes? Introducing the No-Sew Pizza Pockets – an easy, fun and delicious way to make your favorite pizza-on-the-go.

No-Sew Pizza Pockets are the perfect meal or snack for a busy family or hungry friends. They take very little time and can be served and eaten immediately after baking. Plus, neither you nor the kids have to worry about the messy sewing process. You’ll quickly become a master chef with your new technique!

Ingredients

  1. 1 package of pizza crust dough (homemade or store-bought)
  2. ½ lb ground Italian sausage (or your preferred protein)
  3. 1 cup marinara sauce
  4. 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  5. Toppings of your choice (pepperoni slices, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, etc.)
  6. 1 egg (beaten)
  7. 2 tbsp olive oil
  8. Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat Oven and Prepare Baking Sheet Preheat your oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit (220 degrees Celsius) and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper. This is optional but will make clean-up much more manageable.

Step 2: Cook the Protein In a large skillet over medium heat, cook your chosen protein (we used Italian sausage) until fully cooked and browned. Drain any excess oil/fat.

Step 3: Assemble the Pizza Pockets Now for the fun part! On a lightly floured surface or using a pizza stone, roll out your pizza dough into a large rectangle or chose your desired shape/sizes for the pockets.

Take a spoonful of cooked protein and spread it evenly over the dough, leaving about an inch around the edges. Add a thin layer of marinara sauce, then sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese. Place your desired pizza toppings on and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Fold and Seal the Pizza Pockets Take one end of the dough and fold it over the filling, pressing gently to seal the edges. To ensure a nice and compact seal, use a fork to crimp the edges or simply press the edges firmly with your fingers.

Step 5: Brush with Egg Wash and Bake In a small bowl, beat the egg to create an egg wash. Brush the egg wash over the tops and edges of the pizza pockets. Drizzle with a small amount of olive oil for added crispiness.

Place the pizza pockets onto your prepared baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and crispy and the cheese is bubbly and golden. Once baked, let them cool slightly before moving onto a wire rack or plate.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy Serve your freshly baked No-Sew Pizza Pockets immediately with your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy the delicious flavors of a conventional pizza in a fun, handheld treat!

Conclusion

No-Sew Pizza Pockets are an excellent dinner idea that everyone in the family will love. It is a fun and easier way to make those scrumptious handheld treats all year long. Plus, with limitless toppings to choose from, the only limitation is your creativity. Give it a try this week and indulge in some delicious No-Sew Pizza Pocket goodness!
