What You’ll Need: 1 can (15 oz) unsweetened coconut milk 2 tablespoons lime juice 1 spoonful Liquid Monk Fruit Sweetener (or honey, to taste) Splash of club soda or sparkling water Ice cubes Optional: fresh lime wedges or a sprig of mint for garnish Preparation Combine the unsweetened coconut milk, lime juice, and sweetener in a cocktail shaker or a lidded jar, and shake well to mix. Alternatively, you can mix the ingredients in a large pitcher for serving a crowd. Fill a highball glass with ice cubes to just below the rim. Pour the coconut-lime mixture over the ice. Optional: garnish with lime wedges or a sprig of mint for a pop of color and extra flavor.
