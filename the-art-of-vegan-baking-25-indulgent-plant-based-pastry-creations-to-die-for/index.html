1. Vegan Chocolate Cake

Rich, velvety and simply irresistibly decadent, this chocolate cake transcending all expectations will leave you yearning for more sans the guilt.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup (65g) unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (200g) cane sugar
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) hot water
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp white or apple cider vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line cake pans. Combine the flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  2. Make a well in the center of your dry mixture. Add the wet mixture and stir to combine until there are no lumps.
  3. Divide batter among prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Allow to cool completely before frosting.

2. Vegan Vanilla Cupcakes

A simple yet satisfying burst of vanilla fudge tops these scrumptious vegan vanilla cupcakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick/115g) vegan butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 vegan eggs
  • 2 tbsp white or apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line cupcake pans with paper liners. In a small bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the non-dairy milk, vanilla, and vinegar, mixing until fully incorporated.
  3. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture gradually, whisking after each addition. Mix until just combined.
  4. Distribute batter evenly among cupcake liners, filling 2/3 of the way full.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool on wire rack before frosting.
