Cheesy Baked Ziti: A Crowd-Pleasing Comfort Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting dish to satisfy your cravings, look no further than Cheesy Baked Ziti. This crowd-pleasing recipe is a perfect combination of tender pasta, rich tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese. It’s the ultimate comfort food that will leave everyone at the table asking for seconds.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering Cheesy Baked Ziti, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound ziti pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.

  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the ziti pasta according to the package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet, breaking it up with a wooden spoon. Cook until browned and no longer pink. Drain any excess grease.

  5. Season the meat with dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine.

  6. Pour the marinara sauce into the skillet with the meat mixture, and stir until everything is well coated. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for about 5 minutes.

  7. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and half of the shredded mozzarella cheese. Mix well until smooth and creamy.

  8. Add the cooked ziti pasta to the skillet with the meat sauce. Stir gently until the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce.

  9. Transfer half of the ziti mixture to the prepared baking dish. Spread it out evenly.

  10. Dollop spoonfuls of the ricotta cheese mixture over the ziti in the baking dish. Use a spatula to spread it out evenly.

  11. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the ricotta layer.

  12. Top with the remaining ziti mixture, spreading it out evenly once again.

  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.

  14. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese on top is golden and bubbly.

  15. Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  16. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired, and serve hot.

Conclusion

Cheesy Baked Ziti is a crowd-pleasing comfort dish that brings together the timeless combination of pasta, sauce, and cheese. With its rich flavors and gooey texture, it’s the perfect meal to satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. This recipe is easy to make and can be prepared in advance for a stress-free dinner. So why wait? Gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in a bowl of cheesy goodness!
