Satisfying Vegetable Lo Mein Stir Fry


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying dish that is packed with flavors and healthy vegetables? Look no further than this amazing Vegetable Lo Mein Stir Fry recipe! This dish is not only easy to make but also customizable to suit your taste preferences. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create this mouth-watering dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz of Lo Mein noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sriracha sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, sliced (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Lo Mein Noodles: In a large pot of boiling water, cook the Lo Mein noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and rinse the noodles under cold water. Set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and sriracha sauce. Set the sauce aside for later.

  3. Stir Fry the Vegetables: In a large wok or skillet, heat the sesame oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sliced onion, and sauté until fragrant.

  4. Add the Vegetables: Add the julienned bell peppers, broccoli florets, julienned carrot, and snap peas to the wok. Stir-fry the vegetables until they are crisp-tender.

  5. Combine Everything: Add the cooked Lo Mein noodles to the wok with the stir-fried vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the noodles and vegetables.

  6. Stir Fry Together: Toss everything together until the noodles are well coated with the sauce and the vegetables are evenly distributed.

  7. Season and Garnish: Season the Vegetable Lo Mein Stir Fry with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds for an extra burst of flavor and texture.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the Vegetable Lo Mein Stir Fry to a serving dish and serve hot. This dish is best enjoyed fresh and can be paired with your favorite protein for a complete meal.

Customization Tips:

  • Feel free to add in other vegetables such as mushrooms, baby corn, or water chestnuts.
  • For a protein boost, you can incorporate tofu, chicken, shrimp, or beef into the stir fry.
  • Adjust the level of spiciness by increasing or decreasing the amount of sriracha sauce used in the recipe.

Final Thoughts:

This Vegetable Lo Mein Stir Fry recipe is a perfect balance of flavors, textures, and colors that will surely satisfy your taste buds. Whether you are a fan of Asian cuisine or looking to try something new, this dish is a great choice for a quick and healthy meal. So, grab your ingredients, fire up your wok, and get ready to enjoy a delicious homemade stir fry that will leave you wanting more!

Remember, cooking is all about creativity, so don’t be afraid to experiment and make this recipe your own. Happy cooking!
