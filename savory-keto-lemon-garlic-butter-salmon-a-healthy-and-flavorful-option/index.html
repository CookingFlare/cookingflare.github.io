Savory Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon: A Healthy and Flavorful Option


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy dinner option, look no further than this savory keto lemon garlic butter salmon recipe. Not only is salmon a fantastic source of protein and healthy fats, but it’s also incredibly versatile and easy to prepare. By infusing it with the bright flavors of lemon and garlic butter, you’ll create a dish that’s bursting with flavor and perfect for a keto-friendly lifestyle.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that your salmon cooks evenly and develops a beautiful golden crust.

  2. Prepare the Salmon: Pat the salmon fillets dry with a paper towel and season them with salt and pepper. This simple seasoning will enhance the natural flavors of the salmon without overpowering them.

  3. Make the Lemon Garlic Butter: In a small saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the lemon zest and fresh lemon juice. This aromatic and tangy butter sauce will be the star of the dish.

  4. Coat the Salmon: Place the seasoned salmon fillets on a lined baking sheet. Pour the lemon garlic butter over the fillets, ensuring they are evenly coated. The butter will keep the salmon moist and infuse it with rich flavors as it bakes.

  5. Bake the Salmon: Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until it flakes easily with a fork. Be careful not to overcook the salmon, as it can become dry.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once the salmon is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. The vibrant green parsley adds a pop of color and a hint of freshness to the dish. Serve the lemon garlic butter salmon hot and enjoy!

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 34g
  • Fat: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 2g
  • Fiber: 0g
