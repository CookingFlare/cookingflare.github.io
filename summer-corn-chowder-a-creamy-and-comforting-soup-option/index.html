Summer Corn Chowder: A Creamy and Comforting Soup Option


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in the delicious flavors of fresh corn, and what better way to savor this seasonal favorite than with a hearty and creamy corn chowder? This comforting soup is a delightful blend of sweet corn, savory bacon, and creamy broth that will warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a comforting meal, this summer corn chowder is sure to be a hit. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful dish in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 6 ears of fresh corn, husked and kernels removed
  • 4 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Bacon: In a large pot over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until it becomes crispy and golden brown. Once the bacon is cooked, remove it from the pot and set it aside, leaving the bacon fat in the pot.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot with the bacon fat. Sauté the aromatics until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the Corn and Potatoes: Stir in the fresh corn kernels and diced potatoes, and cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Pour in the Broth: Add the chicken or vegetable broth to the pot, ensuring that the corn and potatoes are fully submerged. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.

  5. Blend the Soup: Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until it reaches your desired consistency. If you prefer a chunkier chowder, you can leave some of the corn and potatoes whole.

  6. Add the Cream and Seasonings: Pour in the heavy cream, sprinkle in the thyme, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir the soup well to incorporate the cream and seasonings.

  7. Simmer and Serve: Let the chowder simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Once ready, ladle the creamy summer corn chowder into bowls, garnish with the crispy bacon, and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley.

Tips for Serving

  • Pair this creamy corn chowder with a crusty loaf of bread or a side salad for a complete and satisfying meal.
  • Consider adding a sprinkle of shredded cheddar cheese on top for an extra layer of richness and flavor.
  • For a lighter version, you can substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half or whole milk.

Final Thoughts

This summer corn chowder is a true celebration of the season’s bounty, offering a creamy and comforting soup option that is both satisfying and versatile. With the natural sweetness of fresh corn and the savory richness of bacon, this chowder embodies the essence of summer in every spoonful. Whether enjoyed as a standalone meal or as part of a larger spread, this delightful dish is sure to become a favorite in your summer recipe repertoire. Embrace the flavors of the season and treat yourself to a bowl of this creamy and comforting summer corn chowder – your taste buds will thank you!
