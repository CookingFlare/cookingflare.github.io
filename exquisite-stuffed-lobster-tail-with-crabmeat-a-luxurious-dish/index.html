Exquisite Stuffed Lobster Tail with Crabmeat: A Luxurious Dish


If you’re looking to impress your dinner guests with a luxurious and exquisite dish, look no further than the Stuffed Lobster Tail with Crabmeat. This indulgent recipe combines the delicate flavor of lobster with the richness of crabmeat, creating a dish that is sure to leave a lasting impression. Whether you’re hosting a special celebration or simply want to treat yourself to something extraordinary, this recipe is the perfect choice.

Ingredients

  • 4 lobster tails
  • 1 pound lump crabmeat
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup fresh breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Lobster Tails: Begin by preparing the lobster tails. Using kitchen shears, cut the top of the shell lengthwise and remove the vein. Gently lift the lobster meat from the shell, keeping it attached at the base. Place the meat on top of the shell and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Filling: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion, celery, red bell pepper, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are soft and fragrant. Remove from heat and transfer to a large bowl.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: To the bowl of cooked vegetables, add the lump crabmeat, fresh breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and Old Bay seasoning. Gently fold the ingredients together until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Stuff the Lobster Tails: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Divide the crabmeat mixture evenly among the prepared lobster tails, mounding it on top of the lobster meat.

  5. Bake the Lobster Tails: Place the stuffed lobster tails on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the lobster meat is opaque and the crabmeat is golden brown.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the lobster tails are cooked to perfection, carefully transfer them to serving plates. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and a wedge of lemon. Serve alongside your favorite side dishes and enjoy this exquisite and luxurious dish with your loved ones.

Tips for Success

  • When selecting lobster tails, look for ones that are bright and firm, with no discoloration or strong odor.
  • Be gentle when removing the lobster meat from the shell to ensure it stays intact for stuffing.
  • For added flavor, consider incorporating additional herbs or spices to the crabmeat filling, such as tarragon or paprika.

This Stuffed Lobster Tail with Crabmeat recipe is truly a showstopper, elevating any occasion with its opulent flavors and elegant presentation. Whether you’re celebrating a special milestone or simply want to indulge in a memorable dining experience, this dish is sure to leave a lasting impression.

So, go ahead and treat yourself and your loved ones to this exquisite culinary delight, and savor every luxurious bite!
