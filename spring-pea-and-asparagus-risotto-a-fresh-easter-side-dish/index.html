Spring Pea and Asparagus Risotto: A Fresh Easter Side Dish


Risotto is a classic Italian dish that is beloved for its creamy texture and versatility. This Spring Pea and Asparagus Risotto recipe is the perfect addition to your Easter dinner table. The vibrant green color and fresh flavors of the peas and asparagus make this dish a delightful celebration of spring. Whether you’re hosting a festive Easter gathering or simply looking for a delicious way to welcome the season, this risotto is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon zest for garnish
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Begin by heating the vegetable or chicken broth in a pot and keeping it warm over low heat. In a separate pan, blanch the asparagus and peas in boiling water for 2 minutes, then transfer them to an ice water bath to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large, deep skillet, heat the olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Add the finely chopped shallot and minced garlic, and sauté until translucent and fragrant.

  3. Toast the Rice: Add the Arborio rice to the skillet and stir to coat the grains with the oil and butter. Cook for a few minutes until the edges of the rice become translucent.

  4. Deglaze with Wine: Pour in the dry white wine and stir the rice constantly until the liquid is absorbed.

  5. Add the Broth: Begin adding the warm broth to the rice, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly and allowing the liquid to be absorbed before adding more. This gradual process helps release the starch from the rice, resulting in the creamy texture of the risotto.

  6. Incorporate the Vegetables: Once the rice is almost al dente, stir in the blanched asparagus and peas. Continue adding broth and stirring until the rice is cooked to your desired consistency and the vegetables are heated through.

  7. Finish with Cheese and Butter: Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and remaining butter. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Serve and Garnish: Spoon the Spring Pea and Asparagus Risotto onto serving plates. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh lemon zest and chopped parsley for a pop of color and brightness.

Tips for Success

  • Use high-quality Arborio rice for the best creamy texture.
  • Keep the broth warm as you add it to the rice to maintain a consistent temperature throughout the cooking process.
  • Stir the risotto constantly to encourage the release of starch and achieve the desired creaminess.

Enjoy the Fresh Flavors of Spring

This Spring Pea and Asparagus Risotto is a delightful way to embrace the seasonal produce and elevate your Easter feast. The tender asparagus and sweet peas perfectly complement the creamy rice, while the hint of lemon zest adds a refreshing zing to each bite. Whether you’re serving this dish alongside roasted lamb, glazed ham, or as the star of a vegetarian Easter spread, it’s sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Embrace the vibrant flavors of spring with this fresh and satisfying risotto recipe. Happy cooking and happy Easter!
