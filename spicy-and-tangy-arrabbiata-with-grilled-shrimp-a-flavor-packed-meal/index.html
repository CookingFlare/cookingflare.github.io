Spicy and Tangy Arrabbiata with Grilled Shrimp: A Flavor-Packed Meal


Are you ready to elevate your dinner game with a dish that combines bold flavors and succulent grilled shrimp? Look no further than this Spicy and Tangy Arrabbiata with Grilled Shrimp recipe. This mouthwatering dish is a delightful fusion of tangy tomato sauce, fiery red pepper flakes, and perfectly grilled shrimp. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a restaurant-quality meal at home, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

Ingredients

To create this tantalizing dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil, chopped
  • 12 ounces of spaghetti or your pasta of choice
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Grilled Shrimp

  • In a bowl, toss the shrimp with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  • Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are pink and opaque.
  • Remove the shrimp from the grill and set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Arrabbiata Sauce

  • In a large skillet, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat.
  • Add the red pepper flakes and sauté for 1 minute to infuse the oil with heat.
  • Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Pour in the crushed tomatoes and sugar, then season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.
  • Stir in the chopped basil just before serving.

Step 3: Cook the Pasta

  • While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  • Drain the pasta and set aside.

Step 4: Bring It All Together

  • Toss the cooked pasta with the arrabbiata sauce until it is well coated.
  • Divide the pasta among serving plates and top with the grilled shrimp.
  • Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and additional chopped basil, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This Spicy and Tangy Arrabbiata with Grilled Shrimp is best enjoyed fresh and hot. Pair it with a crisp green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette to balance the bold flavors of the dish. A glass of chilled white wine or sparkling water with a squeeze of lemon will complement the meal beautifully.

Final Thoughts

With its zesty tomato sauce, fiery kick of red pepper flakes, and succulent grilled shrimp, this Spicy and Tangy Arrabbiata with Grilled Shrimp is a culinary masterpiece that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or simply looking to add some excitement to your dinner routine, this dish is a must-try. So gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and get ready to savor every mouthful of this flavor-packed meal.

What are you waiting for? It’s time to bring a taste of the Mediterranean to your dinner table with this delectable arrabbiata recipe!
