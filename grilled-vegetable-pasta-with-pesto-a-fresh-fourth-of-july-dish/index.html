Grilled Vegetable Pasta with Pesto: A Fresh Fourth of July Dish


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to mark the occasion than with a fresh and vibrant dish that captures the essence of summer? This grilled vegetable pasta with pesto is a perfect addition to your Fourth of July menu. It’s bursting with flavors, colors, and wholesome ingredients that will delight your guests and leave them craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Grilled Vegetables:

  • 2 zucchinis, sliced lengthwise
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and quartered
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and quartered
  • 1 red onion, cut into thick slices
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pesto:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves, packed
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pasta:

  • 1 pound pasta of your choice
  • Salt for boiling water

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating, toss the sliced zucchinis, bell peppers, and red onion with olive oil, salt, and pepper.

  2. Grill the Vegetables: Place the prepared vegetables on the grill and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are nicely charred and tender. Once grilled, transfer the vegetables to a cutting board and let them cool slightly.

  3. Cook the Pasta: While the vegetables are grilling, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  4. Prepare the Pesto: In a food processor, combine the basil, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, garlic, salt, and pepper. Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped. With the processor running, slowly add the olive oil until the pesto is smooth and well combined.

  5. Assemble the Dish: In a large bowl, toss the cooked pasta with the prepared pesto until it’s well coated. Then, add the grilled vegetables and gently toss to combine.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Garnish the dish with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves. Serve the grilled vegetable pasta with pesto at your Fourth of July gathering and watch as it becomes an instant crowd-pleaser.

The Perfect Fourth of July Dish

This grilled vegetable pasta with pesto is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a vibrant celebration of summer produce. Its colorful and inviting presentation is perfect for a Fourth of July gathering, adding a touch of freshness and elegance to your table. The smoky flavor from the grilled vegetables combined with the aromatic basil pesto creates a harmonious symphony of flavors that will leave your guests impressed.

So, this Fourth of July, embrace the flavors of the season and treat your loved ones to a dish that embodies the essence of summer. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or a casual get-together, this grilled vegetable pasta with pesto is sure to be the star of the show. Cheers to good food, great company, and memorable moments this Independence Day!
