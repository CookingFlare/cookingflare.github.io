Flatbread Pizzas for Vegetarians: Creative Meatless Options


Are you a vegetarian looking for a delicious and creative meatless option for your next pizza night? Look no further! In this post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering flatbread pizza recipes that are perfect for vegetarians. These recipes are not only easy to make but also bursting with flavors that will satisfy even the pickiest eaters. So, let’s get started!

Recipe 1: Mediterranean Veggie Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 flatbread crust
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the flatbread crust on a baking sheet and brush it with olive oil.
  3. Sprinkle minced garlic evenly over the crust.
  4. Arrange cherry tomatoes and black olives on top.
  5. Crumble feta cheese over the vegetables.
  6. Sprinkle chopped fresh basil, salt, and pepper.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Slice and serve your Mediterranean veggie flatbread pizza!

Recipe 2: BBQ Chickpea Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 flatbread crust
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Spread BBQ sauce evenly over the flatbread crust.
  3. Scatter chickpeas, red onion, and bell peppers on top.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the vegetables.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and sprinkle chopped fresh cilantro.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve your BBQ chickpea flatbread pizza!

Recipe 3: Pesto and Roasted Vegetable Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 flatbread crust
  • 2 tablespoons pesto sauce
  • 1/2 cup sliced zucchini
  • 1/2 cup sliced yellow squash
  • 1/2 cup sliced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Spread pesto sauce evenly over the flatbread crust.
  3. Arrange zucchini, yellow squash, red bell pepper, and red onion on top.
  4. Sprinkle crumbled goat cheese and pine nuts over the vegetables.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is crispy and the vegetables are tender.
  6. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  7. Slice and serve your pesto and roasted vegetable flatbread pizza!

Recipe 4: Margherita Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 flatbread crust
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the flatbread crust.
  3. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Tear fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cheese.
  5. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the pizza.
  6. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Slice and serve your margherita flatbread pizza!

Conclusion

These flatbread pizza recipes are not only delicious but also provide creative and meatless options for vegetarians. From the Mediterranean veggie pizza to the BBQ chickpea pizza, there’s something for everyone. So, next time you’re craving pizza, give these recipes a try and enjoy a tasty and satisfying meal. Bon appétit!
