Wholesome and Tasty Breakfast Burrito Recipes for Kids


Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, especially for growing kids. It provides them with the energy and nutrients they need to start their day on the right foot. One delicious and nutritious breakfast option that kids love is breakfast burritos. Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavors, breakfast burritos are a great way to fuel your little ones for the day ahead. In this blog post, we will share three wholesome and tasty breakfast burrito recipes that are sure to become a hit with your kids.

1. Veggie-Packed Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced zucchini
  • 1/2 cup diced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced bell peppers, onion, zucchini, and mushrooms. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and season with salt and pepper. Pour the eggs into the skillet with the sautéed vegetables and cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are scrambled.
  3. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave.
  4. Divide the scrambled eggs and vegetable mixture among the tortillas. Sprinkle each burrito with a generous amount of shredded cheddar cheese.
  5. Roll up the burritos, folding in the sides as you go.
  6. Serve the veggie-packed breakfast burritos warm, and watch your kids enjoy a nutritious and delicious start to their day!

2. Protein-Packed Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced avocado
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large whole wheat tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and season with salt and pepper. Cook the scrambled eggs in a skillet until done.
  2. Warm the whole wheat tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave.
  3. Divide the cooked quinoa, black beans, scrambled eggs, diced tomatoes, diced avocado, diced red onion, and chopped fresh cilantro among the tortillas.
  4. Roll up the burritos, folding in the sides as you go.
  5. Serve the protein-packed breakfast burritos warm, and watch your kids enjoy a hearty and nutritious meal to keep them fueled throughout the day!

3. Sweet and Nutty Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup sliced bananas
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 4 large whole wheat tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs. Cook the scrambled eggs in a skillet until done.
  2. Warm the whole wheat tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave.
  3. Spread a tablespoon of almond butter on each tortilla.
  4. Drizzle a teaspoon of honey over the almond butter on each tortilla.
  5. Divide the scrambled eggs, sliced bananas, and granola among the tortillas.
  6. Roll up the burritos, folding in the sides as you go.
  7. Serve the sweet and nutty breakfast burritos warm, and watch your kids indulge in a delicious and nutritious treat to kickstart their day!

Conclusion:

Breakfast burritos are a versatile and delicious option for kids’ breakfast. These three recipes provide a variety of flavors and ingredients to suit different tastes and dietary preferences. Whether you opt for a veggie-packed, protein-packed, or sweet and nutty burrito, these wholesome and tasty breakfast options will ensure your kids start their day with a nutritious and satisfying meal. Get creative in the kitchen and enjoy the smiles on your little ones’ faces as they devour these scrumptious breakfast burritos!
