Baked Parmesan Zucchini Rounds: A Healthy and Delicious Snack


Are you looking for a healthy and delicious snack to satisfy your cravings? Look no further than these Baked Parmesan Zucchini Rounds! This simple yet flavorful recipe is the perfect way to enjoy the goodness of zucchini while indulging in a crunchy and cheesy treat. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply want a wholesome snack for yourself, these zucchini rounds are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchinis, sliced into rounds
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a shallow bowl, combine the grated Parmesan cheese, breadcrumbs, garlic powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix the ingredients well to ensure a uniform flavor in each bite.

  3. Dip each zucchini round into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off. Then, coat the zucchini round in the Parmesan breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere the coating to the zucchini.

  4. Place the coated zucchini rounds in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Lightly spray the rounds with cooking spray to help them achieve a crispy texture during baking.

  5. Bake the zucchini rounds in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the coating is golden brown and the zucchini is tender.

  6. Once baked, remove the zucchini rounds from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

  7. Enjoy these Baked Parmesan Zucchini Rounds on their own or with a side of marinara sauce for dipping.

Health Benefits

Zucchinis are a fantastic source of nutrients, including vitamin C, vitamin A, potassium, and dietary fiber. By baking the zucchini rounds instead of frying them, you can indulge in a satisfying snack without the guilt associated with traditional fried snacks. The addition of Parmesan cheese adds a savory and indulgent flavor, making this snack a crowd-pleaser for both kids and adults.

Serving Suggestions

These Baked Parmesan Zucchini Rounds are versatile and can be served in various ways. They make a fantastic appetizer for parties, a wholesome after-school snack for children, or a flavorful side dish for a family dinner. You can also get creative with the seasoning by adding a pinch of cayenne pepper for a spicy kick or a sprinkle of fresh parsley for a burst of freshness.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Baked Parmesan Zucchini Rounds are a delightful way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet while treating yourself to a satisfying snack. This recipe is not only easy to prepare but also offers a healthier alternative to traditional fried snacks. With the perfect balance of crunchiness and cheesy goodness, these zucchini rounds are bound to become a favorite in your household. So, next time you’re craving a snack, give this recipe a try and savor the deliciousness of these Baked Parmesan Zucchini Rounds!

Feel free to share your feedback and any creative twists you add to this recipe. Happy snacking!
