10 Family-Friendly Recipes for Holiday Gatherings


Holiday gatherings are the perfect opportunity to bond with loved ones over delicious food. But finding dishes that strike the right balance between crowd-pleasing and festive can sometimes be a challenge. The following recipes will simplify your holiday menu planning while delighting guests of all ages. These family-friendly dishes are easy to prepare, full of flavor, and sure to become seasonal favorites that will keep everyone coming back for seconds.

1. Honey-Glazed Ham with Dijon Mustard

A holiday centerpiece that never fails to impress, this honey-glazed ham is both simple and elegant.

Ingredients:

  • Bone-in cured ham
  • 1 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F. Place the ham in a large roasting pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together honey, Dijon mustard, ground cloves, and cinnamon. Brush half the mixture generously over the ham.
  3. Cover with foil and bake for 1 to 1.5 hours, depending on the size of your ham. Halfway through, brush the remaining glaze over the ham.
  4. Remove the foil for the last 10 minutes to achieve a caramelized finish.

2. Cheesy Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Creamy and comforting, mashed potatoes are a must-have on any holiday table.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large russet potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil potatoes until fork-tender, about 15 minutes. Drain and return to the pot.
  2. Mash the potatoes while adding butter, cream, and garlic. Stir in shredded cheddar cheese.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot!

3. Holiday Cranberry Pecan Salad

Bright and flavorful, this refreshing salad is a lighter option for your holiday spread.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, and kale)
  • ½ cup dried cranberries
  • ½ cup candied pecans
  • ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and freshly cracked pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine greens, cranberries, pecans, and feta.
  2. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar on top. Toss everything together lightly.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then serve immediately!

4. Mini Chicken Pot Pies

These individual-sized pot pies are perfect for kids and adults alike.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked diced chicken breast
  • 1 cup frozen vegetables (peas, carrots, green beans, etc.)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Refrigerated pie crusts
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F. In a skillet, cook the chicken, frozen veggies, chicken broth, cream, flour, and thyme until thickened.
  2. Cut pie crusts into circles to fit muffin tin cups. Place crusts in the tin, fill with the chicken mixture, and cover with another layer of dough. Crimp the edges together.
  3. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

5. Green Bean Almondine

Elevate humble green beans with a nutty twist that pairs well with almost any main dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • ¼ cup sliced almonds
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil the green beans for 4 minutes, then drain and immediately transfer them to an ice bath to retain their crispness.
  2. Sauté the garlic in butter before adding the green beans. Toss in sliced almonds and lemon juice.
  3. Season with salt and serve warm.

6. Classic Herb Roast Turkey

Nothing says holiday feast like a beautifully roasted turkey infused with classic herb flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole turkey (12-14 pounds)
  • ½ cup softened butter
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, whole

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Carefully loosen the turkey skin. Rub butter and herbs underneath the skin.
  2. Stuff the cavity with lemon slices and garlic cloves. Tie the legs together with string.
  3. Roast the turkey for about 3 to 3.5 hours, basting occasionally with juices.

7. Sweet Potato Casserole with Marshmallows

Endlessly popular at holiday gatherings, this recipe is a blend of rich sweetness and crunch.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and boiled
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • ½ cup chopped pecans

Instructions:

  1. Mash the cooked sweet potatoes with butter, brown sugar, and vanilla extract. Spread into a casserole dish.
  2. Top with chopped pecans and mini marshmallows.
  3. Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes, or until marshmallows are golden brown.

8. Baked Macaroni and Cheese

This creamy, cheesy classic will be an instant hit with kids—and adults, too!

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz elbow macaroni, cooked
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Melt butter in a skillet and whisk in flour to make a roux. Gradually add milk, stirring continuously until thickened.
  2. Stir in cheddar cheese, then combine the cheese sauce with cooked macaroni. Spread into a baking dish.
  3. Bake at 375°F for 15-20 minutes.

9. Spiced Apple Cider

Warm and aromatic, this festive drink is ideal for cooler holiday weather.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups apple cider
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 5 whole cloves
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • ½ teaspoon allspice

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine apple cider, cinnamon sticks, cloves, allspice, and orange slices.
  2. Simmer on low heat for 15 minutes. Strain and serve warm.

10. Chocolate Peppermint Brownies

End the meal with a decadent dessert infused with holiday flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box brownie mix (prepared following package instructions)
  • ½ teaspoon peppermint extract
  • ½ cup crushed candy canes
  • ½ cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the brownie batter, then stir in peppermint extract.
  2. Spread the batter into a pan and sprinkle with chocolate chips. Bake according to package instructions.
  3. Once cool, top with crushed candy canes for a festive touch.

These recipes are designed to please palates and spark joy around the holiday table. Whether you’re hosting a grand celebration or a small family dinner, these family-friendly dishes will ensure your gathering is memorable and filled with delectable flavors. Try them out this season and make every bite count.
