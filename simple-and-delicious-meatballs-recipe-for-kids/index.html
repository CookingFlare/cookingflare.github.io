Simple and Delicious Meatballs Recipe for Kids


Who doesn’t love meatballs? These little balls of goodness are not only delicious but also incredibly versatile. Whether you serve them with spaghetti or as a tasty snack, meatballs are always a hit with kids. In this blog post, I will share a simple and mouthwatering meatball recipe that your little ones will adore.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, milk, onion, garlic, egg, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Once the mixture is well combined, shape it into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. You can use your hands or a small cookie scoop to make the process easier.

  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Place the meatballs in the skillet, making sure to leave some space between them. Cook the meatballs for about 8-10 minutes, turning them occasionally, until they are browned on all sides and cooked through.

  4. If you prefer to bake the meatballs, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  5. Once the meatballs are cooked, you can serve them with your favorite sauce or as a standalone snack. They are perfect for dipping into marinara sauce, sweet and sour sauce, or even barbecue sauce.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the meatballs even more kid-friendly, you can add finely chopped vegetables like carrots or zucchini to the mixture. This is a great way to sneak in some extra nutrients while keeping the meatballs moist and flavorful.

  • If you’re short on time, you can prepare the meatball mixture in advance and store it in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. This allows the flavors to meld together, resulting in even tastier meatballs.

  • For a fun twist, you can stuff the meatballs with a small cube of cheese before shaping them. When cooked, the cheese will melt and create a delicious surprise in the middle of each meatball.

  • Leftover meatballs can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. They can also be frozen for up to 3 months. To reheat, simply place them in a microwave-safe dish and heat for a minute or two, or until heated through.

Conclusion

These simple and delicious meatballs are sure to become a favorite in your household. They are easy to make, packed with flavor, and perfect for little hands. Whether you serve them as a main dish or a fun snack, these meatballs will bring smiles to your kids’ faces. So go ahead and give this recipe a try, and watch as your little ones gobble up every bite. Enjoy!
