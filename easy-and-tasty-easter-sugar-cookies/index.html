Easy and Tasty Easter Sugar Cookies


Springtime is in the air, and what better way to celebrate the season than with delicious Easter sugar cookies? These delightful treats are not only easy to make but also a fun activity for the whole family. With their vibrant colors and delightful shapes, these cookies are sure to bring joy to any Easter celebration. So, let’s get started and create a batch of these easy and tasty Easter sugar cookies!

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Food coloring (various colors)
  • Sprinkles (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. This will ensure that your cookies don’t stick and will make cleanup a breeze.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set this aside for now.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. This will take about 3-4 minutes with an electric mixer, or you can do it by hand using a wooden spoon.

  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the butter and sugar mixture and mix until well combined. This will give your cookies a rich and flavorful taste.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing well after each addition. The dough should come together and be slightly sticky.

  6. Divide the dough into separate bowls, depending on how many colors you want to use for your cookies. Add a few drops of food coloring to each bowl and mix until the color is evenly distributed.

  7. Now it’s time to shape your cookies! Roll out each colored dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness. Use your favorite Easter-themed cookie cutters to cut out shapes from the dough.

  8. Carefully transfer the cut-out cookies to the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each cookie. If desired, you can sprinkle some colorful sprinkles on top of the cookies to add an extra touch of sweetness and decoration.

  9. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. Keep a close eye on them, as they can quickly go from golden to burnt.

  10. Once baked, remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  11. Once the cookies are completely cooled, you can get creative with some icing or frosting to decorate them. Use different colors and designs to make each cookie unique and eye-catching.

  12. Enjoy your delicious Easter sugar cookies with a cup of tea or share them with family and friends. They make a wonderful addition to any Easter gathering or can be enjoyed as a special treat throughout the season.

These easy and tasty Easter sugar cookies are a delightful way to celebrate the holiday and welcome the arrival of spring. From mixing the dough to decorating the final cookies, this recipe is a fun and creative activity that can be enjoyed by all ages. So gather your loved ones and create beautiful memories while indulging in these delicious treats. Happy baking and a joyful Easter!
