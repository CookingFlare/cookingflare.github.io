Crunchy Low Carb Cauliflower Tots

Are you looking for a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional tater tots? Look no further! These Crunchy Low Carb Cauliflower Tots are the perfect guilt-free snack or side dish. Packed with flavor and nutrients, they are sure to satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your low carb lifestyle. Ingredients 1 medium head of cauliflower, grated 2 eggs, beaten 1/4 cup almond flour 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon onion powder 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Grate the cauliflower using a box grater or pulse it in a food processor until it resembles rice. Place the grated cauliflower in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave on high for 5 minutes. This will help soften the cauliflower and remove excess moisture. Allow the cauliflower to cool slightly, then transfer it to a clean kitchen towel or cheesecloth. Squeeze out as much liquid as possible. This step is crucial to achieving a crispy texture. In a large mixing bowl, combine the grated cauliflower, beaten eggs, almond flour, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, dried parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated. Take about 1 tablespoon of the cauliflower mixture and shape it into a tot-like shape. Place it on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat until all the mixture is used, leaving some space between each tot. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the tots are golden brown and crispy. Flip them halfway through baking to ensure even browning. Remove the tots from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. They are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, but you can also store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. To reheat, simply pop them in the oven or air fryer until heated through and crispy again. Serving Suggestions Serve these Crunchy Low Carb Cauliflower Tots as a tasty appetizer with your favorite dipping sauce, such as marinara, ranch, or guacamole. Enjoy them as a side dish alongside grilled chicken or fish for a complete and satisfying meal. Add them to a salad for an extra crunch and burst of flavor. Light and Fresh Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad

Are you in the mood for a light and refreshing meal that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than this delightful Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and vibrant vegetables, this salad is not only delicious but also perfect for those following a low-carb lifestyle. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this culinary masterpiece in your own kitchen. Ingredients For the Chicken: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste For the Salad: 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cucumber, diced 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped For the Dressing: 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar 1 clove garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Rub this mixture evenly over the chicken breasts. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Once done, set the chicken aside to cool. Step 2: Assemble the Salad In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh parsley. Once the chicken has cooled, slice it into bite-sized pieces and add it to the bowl with the salad ingredients. Step 3: Prepare the Dressing In a small jar, combine the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Shake the jar vigorously until the dressing is well combined. Step 4: Toss and Serve Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss everything together until the salad is evenly coated with the dressing. Divide the salad into individual serving bowls and garnish with an extra sprinkle of feta cheese and a few fresh parsley leaves. Why You’ll Love This Salad This Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients that come together to create a symphony of flavors and textures. The tender grilled chicken pairs beautifully with the crisp vegetables, briny olives, and tangy feta cheese, all brought together by the vibrant and zesty dressing.
