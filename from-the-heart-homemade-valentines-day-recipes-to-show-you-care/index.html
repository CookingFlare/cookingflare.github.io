From the Heart: Homemade Valentine's Day Recipes to Show You Care


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to show your loved ones you care than with a homemade gift from the heart? Cooking a delicious meal or baking some sweet treats is a thoughtful way to express your feelings and make the day even more special. In this post, we’ll share some of our favorite homemade Valentine’s Day recipes that are sure to impress.

Breakfast in Bed

Start the day off right with a romantic breakfast in bed. Our Strawberry Waffles with Fresh Whipped Cream recipe is a sweet and indulgent treat that’s sure to delight. Made with fresh strawberries and whipped cream, these waffles are the perfect way to start a romantic morning.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup hulled and sliced strawberries
  • Fresh whipped cream, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat waffle iron to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, and melted butter.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in sliced strawberries.
  6. Cook waffles according to manufacturer’s instructions.
  7. Serve with fresh whipped cream and a sprinkle of sugar.

Sweet Treats

No Valentine’s Day would be complete without some sweet treats. Our Chocolate-Dipped Shortbread Cookies recipe is a classic favorite that’s easy to make and always a hit. Made with melted chocolate and crunchy shortbread cookies, these treats are the perfect way to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped pecans (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour and salt.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until a dough forms.
  5. Roll out dough to 1/4 inch thickness.
  6. Cut into desired shapes using a cookie cutter.
  7. Place on prepared baking sheet and bake for 18-20 minutes or until lightly golden.
  8. Allow to cool completely.
  9. Melt chocolate chips in a double boiler or in the microwave in 30-second increments, stirring between each interval until smooth.
  10. Dip cooled cookies in melted chocolate and sprinkle with confectioners’ sugar and chopped pecans (if using).

Dinner for Two

For a romantic dinner for two, our Pan-Seared Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction recipe is a sophisticated and delicious choice. Made with tender filet mignon and a rich red wine reduction, this dish is sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 2 filet mignon steaks (6 oz each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season filet mignon with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear filet mignon for 2-3 minutes per side or until browned.
  4. Transfer skillet to preheated oven and cook for 8-10 minutes or until cooked to desired level of doneness.
  5. While filet mignon is cooking, reduce red wine and beef broth in a small saucepan over medium heat until almost syrupy.
  6. Stir in butter and thyme until melted.
  7. Serve filet mignon with red wine reduction spooned over the top.

These homemade Valentine’s Day recipes are sure to show your loved ones how much you care. Whether you’re cooking a romantic breakfast, baking some sweet treats, or making a delicious dinner, these recipes are the perfect way to make the day special. So go ahead, get cooking, and show your love and appreciation in the most delicious way possible.
