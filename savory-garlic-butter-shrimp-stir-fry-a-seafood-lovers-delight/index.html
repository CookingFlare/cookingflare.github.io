Savory Garlic Butter Shrimp Stir Fry: A Seafood Lover's Delight


If you’re a seafood enthusiast looking for a quick and flavorful dish, then this savory garlic butter shrimp stir fry is a must-try. Bursting with the aroma of garlic and the rich flavor of butter, this recipe is a delightful fusion of bold flavors and succulent shrimp. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a gourmet meal at home, this dish is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of sugar snap peas
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked white rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Pat the shrimp dry with paper towels and season with a pinch of salt and black pepper.

  2. Heat the Butter: In a large skillet or wok, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Once the butter is melted and bubbling, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds, or until fragrant.

  3. Add the Shrimp: Carefully add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn pink and opaque. Transfer the cooked shrimp to a plate and set aside.

  4. Sauté the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the sliced red and green bell peppers along with the sugar snap peas. Sauté the vegetables for 3-4 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine the Flavors: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and drizzle the soy sauce and sesame oil over the ingredients. Toss everything together to ensure that the shrimp and vegetables are coated evenly with the flavorful buttery sauce.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Once the shrimp and vegetables are well combined, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the stir fry with fresh cilantro for a pop of color and an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Enjoy: Serve the savory garlic butter shrimp stir fry over a bed of steamed white rice and savor every mouthwatering bite.

Tips for Success:

  • To ensure the shrimp cook evenly and stay tender, avoid overcrowding the skillet while cooking.
  • Feel free to customize the stir fry by adding your favorite vegetables such as broccoli, mushrooms, or carrots.
  • For a touch of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the finished dish.

Conclusion:

This savory garlic butter shrimp stir fry is a celebration of fresh ingredients and bold flavors. The succulent shrimp, vibrant vegetables, and aromatic garlic butter sauce come together to create a dish that is both elegant and comforting. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a cooking novice, this recipe is a delightful way to elevate your seafood culinary skills and indulge in a gourmet dining experience at home. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your skillet, and treat yourself to this seafood lover’s delight!
