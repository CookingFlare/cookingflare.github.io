Quick and Easy Nut-Free Quiche Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dish to serve at your next brunch or lunch gathering? Look no further than this Quick and Easy Nut-Free Quiche recipe! Quiche is a versatile and savory dish that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. Whether you’re hosting a weekend brunch or preparing a light dinner, this recipe is sure to become a go-to favorite. The best part? It’s nut-free, making it an ideal option for those with nut allergies or dietary restrictions.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup diced ham or cooked bacon
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

1. Prepare the Crust

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Add the chilled, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or fork to blend the mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  • Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overwork the dough.
  • Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disc. Wrap the disc in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

2. Preheat the Oven

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) while the dough is chilling.

3. Prepare the Filling

  • In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced bell peppers and onions, and sauté until they are tender. Set aside to cool.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, whole milk, and heavy cream. Season the mixture with salt and pepper according to your taste.

4. Assemble the Quiche

  • Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Gently press the dough into the dish, trimming any excess around the edges.
  • Spread the cooked bell peppers, onions, diced ham or bacon, and shredded cheddar cheese evenly over the crust.
  • Carefully pour the egg and cream mixture over the filling ingredients.

5. Bake the Quiche

  • Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the center is set and the crust is golden brown.
  • Once baked, remove the quiche from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different fillings such as spinach, mushrooms, or sundried tomatoes to create your own unique quiche flavors.
  • For a lighter version, you can use a mixture of whole eggs and egg whites, and substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half.
  • This quiche can be made ahead of time and refrigerated, making it a convenient option for meal prepping or entertaining.

With this simple and nut-free quiche recipe, you can effortlessly whip up a delightful dish that is bound to impress. Whether you’re catering to guests with dietary restrictions or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is a winner. Serve it alongside a fresh green salad or a bowl of soup for a satisfying and wholesome meal. Enjoy the ease and versatility of this Quick and Easy Nut-Free Quiche recipe for any occasion!
