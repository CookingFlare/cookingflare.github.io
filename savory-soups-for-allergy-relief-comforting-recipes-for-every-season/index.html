Savory Soups for Allergy Relief: Comforting Recipes for Every Season


Allergies can be a real struggle, especially when it comes to finding delicious and comforting meals that won’t trigger any unpleasant reactions. That’s why we’ve put together a collection of savory soup recipes that are not only allergy-friendly but also incredibly satisfying. Whether you’re dealing with seasonal allergies or have specific dietary restrictions, these recipes are sure to bring you comfort and relief, no matter the season.

Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Total Time: 40 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (14 ounces each) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until translucent and fragrant.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and vegetable broth to the pot. Stir well and bring to a simmer.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Add the chopped basil and coconut milk to the pot. Stir well and let the soup simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, blend the soup until smooth and creamy.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired. Enjoy!

Hearty Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes Total Time: 55 minutes Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 cup green lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, diced carrots, and diced celery. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the green lentils, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, dried thyme, and dried rosemary to the pot. Stir well and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 30 minutes, or until the lentils are cooked through and tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy this hearty and nutritious soup!

Spicy Thai Coconut Curry Soup

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Total Time: 40 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, and mushrooms)
  • 1 cup cooked rice noodles
  • Fresh cilantro and lime wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Sauté until fragrant and the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the red curry paste to the pot and stir well to combine with the onion mixture.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables to the pot and let the soup simmer for about 10 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked to your liking.
  5. Add the cooked rice noodles to the pot and stir well to combine.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with fresh cilantro and lime wedges. Enjoy the bold flavors of this Thai-inspired soup!

These savory soup recipes are the perfect way to find comfort and relief from allergies while still enjoying delicious meals. Whether you’re craving a creamy tomato basil soup, a hearty lentil and vegetable soup, or a spicy Thai coconut curry soup, these recipes have got you covered. So grab a bowl, cozy up, and savor the flavors that will bring you comfort all year round.
